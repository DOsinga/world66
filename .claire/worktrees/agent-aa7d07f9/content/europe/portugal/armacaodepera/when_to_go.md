---
title: "When to Go"
type: section
---

The Algarve has one of Europe's sunniest climates. July and August are hot and dry, averaging 28°C, with virtually no rain. This is peak season: beaches are busy, prices are highest, and the resort is at full capacity. June and September offer similar temperatures with smaller crowds and better prices.

May and October are good shoulder-season choices: warm enough for swimming, quieter, and cheaper. Winter (December to February) is quiet and mild — rarely below 12°C — but many seasonal businesses close. Rainfall peaks in December and January.
