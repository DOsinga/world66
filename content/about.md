World66.com is one of the most complete travel resource in the internet, thanks to more than 10.000 articles on destinations all over the world.

The company was founded by Douwe and Richard Osinga in 1999, went through the wild years of the Internet hype and came out with its soul untouched. Indeed, after a few years as a rather closed content project without a proper licence, it has now joined the open content community publishing its artilces under the [Gnu Free Documentation Licence](/world/contributing/copyright).

This because we think that travellers are the most reliable source of information for other travellers.

Richard and Douwe Osinga have been joined by Giorgio Baresi and Hans-Peter Harmsen and together they form the [management team](/world/about/people) of the company
