World66 is an open content travel guide. That means that with some restrictions you can use our content on your own site.

If you are interested in displaying our world travel guide on your
website we have an easy to use solution using javascript.

Step 1: Copy the script:

the travel guide you write - This content is licensed under the [Creative Commons Attribution-ShareAlike 1.0 Licence](http://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-sa/1.0/)

Step 2: Customize. Change the name of the destination (africa in the example script) to get any destination you want. When using destinations that consist of more than one word (like New York), write 'new york' with an empty space. Also please fill in your email address.
Step 3: Paste the javascript into your HTML code.

The javascript will show up to 10,000 page views / month on one IP address. If you have a website that has a lot of traffic or if
are looking for a more sophisticated or simply need more information, please, **[contact
us](mailto:business@world66.com)**.
