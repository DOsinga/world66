### Not-Open Content Images

**When we have no Open Content image** we show images an Internet spider produces for certain keywords. We check if robots.txt forbids us to show the images and if that's not the case, we show them.

If you see an image we should not use please use our [image block list](copyright_images) to prevent World66 from showing this image.

### If you find a copyright infringement

If you find a copyright infringement, please, [contact us](mailto:copyright@world66.com) immediatly. The situation will be examined together and if a page really contains a copyright infringement its content will be deleted.

Before giving a copyright "alarm" consider that:

- if the contributor was in fact the author of the text that is published elsewhere under different terms, that does not affect his right to post it here too;
- sometimes you could find text elsewhere on the web that was copied from World66;
