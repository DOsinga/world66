Africa




Africa is where life begun with Black Eve, Africa is where civilization first developed on the Banks of the river Nile. From the old Arab cities of North-Africa, through the enormous desert of the Sahara, the thick of the jungle, to the great Game Parks, Africa offers a huge diversity of sights to the traveller, but before all, it’s the people that make a visit unforgettable.


Down here you find some highlights of Africa. There are many more, and the choice here is the editor's choice. We invite you to disagree.


**Morocco**
Oasis lined by ancient Casbahs, Snowcapped mountains (you can ski) and immense Medinas and Souqs. 
Morocco has it all. 

**Egypt**
The history of 
Egypt goes as far back as history can go. Many sights testify of this. Absolutely a must see.

**Great Game**
Many African countries have great Game parks. The ones in 
Kenya, 
Tanzania and 
South Africa are probably the best, but by no means the only ones.
