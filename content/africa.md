Africa is where life begun with Black Eve, Africa is where civilization first developed on the Banks of the river Nile. From the old Arab cities of North Africa, through the enormous desert of the Sahara, from the thick of the jungle to the great Game Parks, Africa offers a huge diversity of sights to the traveller. But above all, getting in contact with the native inhabitants makes it an unforgettable visit. 



Going through Africa from the North to the South, you will first encounter six arab-berber countries: [Morocco](/africa/morocco) , [Algeria](/africa/algeria) , [Tunisia](/africa/tunisia) , [Libya](/africa/libya) , [Egypt](/africa/egypt) and [Mauritania](/africa/mauritania) . The inhabitants of these countries are almost 100 percent muslims. Among the highlights of this part of the continent we would like to mention [Cairo](/africa/egypt/cairo) with its pyramids, [Luxor](/africa/egypt/luxor) with its valley of Kings, the medieval cities of Morocco such as [Marrakesh](/africa/morocco/marrakesh) and [Fez](/africa/morocco/fez) and the cities of [Tangier](/africa/morocco/tangier) , [Rabat](/africa/morocco/rabat) and [Casablanca](/africa/morocco/casablanca) . 

Below, you will find the Sahel region. The countries are predominantly Muslim, but African heritage with its influence dominates. [Senegal](/africa/senegal) including the famous [Dakar](/africa/senegal/dakar) and [Mali](/africa/mali) with places like [Timbuktu](/africa/mali/timbuktu) , [Segou](/africa/mali/segou) and [Mopti](/africa/mali/mopti) are among the most visited countries here. 

West Africa sees relatively few tourists which in some cases may be due to political turmoil. [Ghana](/africa/ghana) has good beaches and nice forts. [Togo](/africa/togo) is a gem under the West African countries and so is [Benin](/africa/benin) and the [Ivory Coast](/africa/ivorycoast) . [Nigeria](/africa/nigeria) has many sights but travelling there can be time- and nerveracking. 

Central Africa too, has had its fair share of trouble in recent years, which is the reason why many travellers deleted countries such as the [Central African Republic](/africa/centralafricanrepublic) , [Rwanda](/africa/rwanda) and [Congo Kinshasa](/africa/congokinshasa) (formerly known as Zaire) from their to do and to see list. 

East Africa however is a real treat. [Ethiopia](/africa/ethiopia) with its ancient Christan civilization and spectacular mountain scenery and [Kenya](/africa/kenya) and [Tanzania](/africa/tanzania) with their great game wildlife parks are a must for travellers. 

The south is getting more popular since it is Political Correct to visit [South Africa](/africa/southafrica) . This marvellous country definitely deserves a visit and travel there is easy as numerous airlines offer flights on a regular basis. A trip there can be combined with [Botswana](/africa/botswana) , [Zimbabwe](/africa/zimbabwe) and [Namibia](/africa/namibia) which are equally fascinating destinations.
