An impressive collection of indigenous artefacts from across Algeria, housed in a beautiful Moorish-style mansion. The museum covers prehistory, ethnography, regional jewellery, costumes, leatherwork and weaponry. The prehistoric rock drawings and ancient weapons are particularly worth seeing.

3 Avenue Franklin Roosevelt, at the top of Rue Didouche Mourad. Open 9am-12 noon and 2pm-5pm.
