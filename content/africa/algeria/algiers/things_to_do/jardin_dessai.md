One of the most important botanical gardens in the Mediterranean, the Jardin d'Essai du Hamma covers about 32 hectares and contains over 2,500 plant species. The French-style gardens with rows of towering palms and Ficus trees are genuinely impressive. It is a welcome escape from the noise and heat of the city.
