Oran is the most important city of the west of Algeria. With [Tlemcen](/africa/algeria/tlemcen) it is probably the most Moroccan of all Algerian cities. Oran's mahor claim to fame is the fact that it is the home of Rai music. Almost all great singers (Khaled, Cheb Mami) are from Oran.


 
 The town was founded by Andalusian seamen in AD937 and flourished under Zianid rule, developing strong trading ties with Spain, which eventually led to Spanish occupation from 1509 to 1708 when Oran was captured by Ottoman forces. The Spanish recaptured the city two years later but withdrew after an earthquake destroyed most of the city in 1790.


 Nowadays Oran is a very busy port town. There are quite a few things to do and see in town.
