One of Oran's more upmarket dining options, located in the Miramar neighbourhood near the coast. The restaurant serves Algerian and French-influenced cuisine in a polished setting. The staff are welcoming and attentive, and the food is well prepared. Worth the trip if you want a proper sit-down meal away from the city centre.
