title: "Eating Out in Setif"
type: section
---

There's a new restaurant that opened here in our friendly city of Setif. Serving Syrian cuisine. It's wonderful. The food is great! Great atmosphere, very clean, and excellent service. And very affordable. You can enjoy a very nice meal for two for under 1500 DZD. It's name is Sham Nights and is located on the main route, to the right of the old hospital.
