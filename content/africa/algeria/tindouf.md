Tindouf used to be a small town in the westernmost part of Algeria. Since the outbreak of the conflict in the Western Sahara, it has seen many people from the WS flocking to the city. Most of them live in camps that have the same names as the cities they left behind: Smara, el Ayoune, Dakhla.

Tindouf is rarely visited by tourists. In principle it is on one of the routes crossing the Sahara, since it should be possible to cross from here to [Zouerate](/africa/mauritania/zouerate) in [Mauritania](/africa/mauritania) but I have not heard of anyone doing this. Even when the borders are open, the route itself is hard enough.
