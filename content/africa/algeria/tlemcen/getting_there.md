---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

Tlemcen has a small airport (Zenata-Messali El Hadj Airport) with flights to [Algiers](/africa/algeria/algiers) and some connections to France. Buses and trains connect the city to [Oran](/africa/algeria/oran) (about two hours), Algiers, and other Algerian cities. The road from Oran follows the coast before turning inland through the hills.
