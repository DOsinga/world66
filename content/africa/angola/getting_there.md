**Points of Interest**

**By air**

Angola's national airline is TAAG Angola Airlines International airport. [Luanda](/africa/angola/luanda) is located 4 km (2.5 miles) from the city. There are no taxis: visitors must be met by their sponsors. Airport facilities include a restaurant bar post office curr [more..](/africa/angola/gettingthere/byair)

**By boat**

The main ports are Cabinda Lobito Luanda and Namibe. 

**By rail and road**

All land frontiers are currently open. Plans to re-open the Benguela railway seem unlikely to achieve fruition until a greater degree of peace is achieved.
