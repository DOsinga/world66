The Iona National Park, located about 200 kilometers from [Namibe](/africa/angola/namibe), was an animal paradise, rich in big game. Unfortunately the fauna has been reduced as a result of illegal hunting and poaching. Efforts are now underway to replace some of Iona's lost wildlife.
