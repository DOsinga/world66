---
title: "Day Trips"
type: section
---

Porto Novo makes a good base for day trips into southern Benin.

**Abomey**, about 150 km northwest, was the capital of the powerful Dahomey kingdom. The Royal Palace complex is a UNESCO World Heritage Site with a historical museum, royal sepulchres, and artisans at work in the town market making bronze and copper artefacts.

**Cotonou**, just 30 km west, is worth visiting for the Dantokpa Market — one of the largest open-air markets in West Africa — and the artisans' quarter.

**Ouidah**, about 100 km west, is the spiritual centre of West African voodoo. Highlights include the Temple of the Sacred Python, the Voodoo Museum, and the Route des Esclaves (Slave Route) ending at the Gate of No Return on the coast — a deeply affecting site.

**Pendjari National Park**, in the far northwest near the border with Burkina Faso, is one of the best wildlife reserves in West Africa, with lions, elephants, buffalo, and over 250 bird species.
