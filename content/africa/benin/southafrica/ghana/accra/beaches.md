### [Elmina Beach](/africa/ghana/accra/beaches/elmina_beach)

The Elmina Beach is a walking distance from the Elmina Beach Resort. It is one of Central Region's cleanest and quietest beaches. The ocean view is amazing and often see fishermen bringing in the catch of the day. Want to have a picnic? No worries. This beach offers a nice, relaxing place for you to have either an intimate picnic with a love one or a big family outing. Whatever it is, Elmina Beach will be perfect for it.

### [Labadi Beach](/africa/ghana/accra/beaches/labadibeach)

East from Labadi Beach Hotel. It's a very popular beach and many times there are shows in the weekend. Too bad the entrance fee is actually quiet high, unless you are staying in one of the closest hotels.

### [Kokrobite](/africa/ghana/accra/beaches/kokrobite)

The first beach behind Accra you pass on the highway to Cape Coast. Nothing spectacular, but a good place to have a beer.
