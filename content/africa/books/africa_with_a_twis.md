A book for exploring Africa and for expats old and new and others travelling to Africa or wanting an insight into living and working in Africa. From Uganda and the mountain Gorillas to Madagascar, Malawi, Liberia, Nigeria and many more. David henry takes you there for his many experiences, some with twists of places, people and wildlife, covering 30 years on this great continent.
