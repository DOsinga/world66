Francistown, the "old lady of Botswana" is the countru's second largest municipality. It is said to be the oldest urban settlement in Botswana.


 Located over 400 km north of [Gaborone](/africa/botswana/gaborone), the city may not have much to offer as a tourist spot. There is a small museum and there is an interesting market. More importantly its convenient location makes Francistwon it an ideal gateway to the [Okavango delta](/africa/botswana/okavangodelta), [Moremi Game Reserve](/africa/botswana/moremigamereserve), the [Chobe national park](/africa/botswana/chobenationalpark) and the borders of [Zambia](/africa/zambia) and [Zimbabwe](/africa/zimbabwe).
