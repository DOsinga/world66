The mountain resort of Dschang is the Cameroon version of Baden Baden. The town is set at an altitude of 1400 meters and with an average temperature of 22 degrees Celsius.


 A visit to Dschang can be combined easily with a trip to [Foumban](/africa/cameroon/foumban) and hiking in the [Bamenda](/africa/cameroon/bamenda) area.
