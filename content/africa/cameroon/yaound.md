Yaoundé is the capital of [Cameroon](/africa/cameroon), a sprawling city of hills and greenery set at around 750 metres altitude in the south-central part of the country. It is quieter and more administrative than the economic capital [Douala](/africa/cameroon/douala), with a calmer pace and a more agreeable climate thanks to the elevation.

The city has few conventional tourist sights, but the Musée National and the Benedictine Monastery of Mont Fébé are worth a visit. The lively Mokolo Market is one of the largest in Central Africa. Yaoundé is primarily a stopping point — a place to organise permits, arrange transport and stock up before heading to the rest of the country.
