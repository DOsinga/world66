Santiago island was first settled and it's still the major island of Cape Verde. Attractions on the island include [Praia](/africa/capeverde/saotiago/praia) the laid back capital of the country, Cidade Velha the oldest settlement of the country and the beaches of Tarrafal.

 The inland is rugged and mountainous and great for hiking.
