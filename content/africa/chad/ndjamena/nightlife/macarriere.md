---
title: "Ma Carriere"
type: poi
address: "N'Djamena, Chad"
---

One of N'Djamena's better-known nightlife venues, Ma Carriere draws a mixed crowd of Chadians, expats, and aid workers. Live music features on some weekends, typically Chadian and Congolese styles. The atmosphere is lively and welcoming. Arrange your transport before heading out, as taxis can be scarce late at night.
