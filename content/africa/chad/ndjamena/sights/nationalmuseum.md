The National Museum was looted during Chad's civil wars but has since reopened with a reduced collection. Displays include artifacts from the Sao civilization, which flourished around [Lake Chad](/africa/chad/lakechad) from the 6th century, along with traditional crafts and archaeological finds from the Saharan north. It is a small museum and won't take more than an hour, but it provides useful background before heading into the country.
