Lake Asal and the relatively young volcano of Arboukoba (people still remember the eruption of the 8th November 1978) offer stunnigly beautiful views. See the salt flats and the seismological probes. They are pretty close to [Tadjoura](/africa/djibouti/tadjoura) as well as to [Djibouti city](/africa/djibouti/djibouticity) so it is realtively easy to arrange a trip here.
