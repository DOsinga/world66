title: Small Temple of Hathor and Nefertari
type: poi
---

A hundred metres north of the Great Temple, Ramses built a smaller one for his chief wife Nefertari and the goddess Hathor. Six standing colossi — four of Ramses, two of Nefertari — front the temple, and unusually for Egyptian art they are all exactly the same height, a striking public gesture of Nefertari's importance. Inside, Hathoric columns and reliefs of the queen making offerings replace the martial scenes of her husband's temple next door.
