**An excellent experience with Egypt last minute!**

I would like to thank everyone in Egypt last minute company for helping me to customize my itinerary and know such helpful information in short time during my last minutes booking
to Egypt. I contacted several companies, after correspondence and
thinking. I chose egypt last minute for the clarity of the program in
details, explain the sights and reasonable price..

I had a conference for engineering in **Cairo**, and then
stayed 9 days more with my husband and 5 people from that conference. We
were in Egypt a total of 9 days this is not enough time to see
everything, we had plans to visit Cairo and *Giza*, went after to
see Aswan and Abu Simbel, then to join our cruise ship for the Nile
cruise to visit Luxor, Kom Ombo and Edfu, and then went for a brief
visit to Red sea (Hurghada).

We chose egypt last minute itinerary for hotels and transport, they
worked through every item and attempt to obtain the best possible deals
and prices of hotels to stay at and which methods of transport to use to
get around in our tour and helped us make more informed decisions. They
were also very reasonably priced.

We met Mr. George the tour manager of our tours, had a little discussion
about our itinerary. He gave us Egyptian mobile phone with some credits
to able to contact someone if we needed or wanted to check something on
our itinerary or if we have any problem during our trip.

Next morning we were picked up at 0730 by our guide, to visit Giza
Pyramids. Climbed inside The Great Pyramid not much to look at but was
amazing. We also went inside one of the private tombs, with amazing
reliefs telling the story of their daily life. We visited the solar boat
museum, the viewpoint, The Sphinx and the mummification temple. The bad
thing that some vendors are pushing you to buy some statues and tourist
cards in an inappropriate manner, but the guide helped us a lot of
friction to prevent these. Then Egyptian Museum, was fabulous

Then had a flight to Aswan, We stayed at Jamila Nile Cruise, the staff
were nice and friendly, food was very good. I will never forget our trip
to Abu Simbel; the car was comfortable and the driver is friendly; the
van carried a large ice-box and snacks for the afternoon meal. Abu
Simbel was more than beautiful and interesting place,

The ship made a couple of stops on the way: Kom Ombo and Edfu.. This was
arranged very efficiently, two temples are right along the Nile. The
Temple of Horus at Edfu was a lot larger and a lot more crowded than Kom
, there was difficulty in reaching the temple because of the carriages
and the bad behavior in dealing with people and try to pressure to take
more and for tipping, Luxor was wonderful place with nice weather than
Cairo, the only thing there don’t go with any man on the street, or with
carriages with yourself, they will try to stop at some shops and
pushing to buy ant tourist staff with high price, Just say NO. or tell
your agent to organize it.

We had a relaxed time in Hurghada at Marriott resort, nice room with sea view, food is very tasty and excellent room service.

We had an excellent experience with you guys and very pleased with all that we saw.
