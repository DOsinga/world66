A small fishing town east of Alexandria where you can sunbathe, fish, swim, and eat fresh seafood. Abu Qir has an old-world charm -- simple cafes, fish huts on the beach, and a pace of life that feels like Alexandria's past. The seafood restaurants here are among the best in the region.
