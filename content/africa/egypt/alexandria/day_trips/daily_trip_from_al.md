**Itinerary:-**
(Day Trip To Giza Pyramids & The Nile From Alexandria Port)

Our representative will pick you up from Alexandria Port to enjoy a shore excursion & day trip to Giza Pyramids & the Nile from Alexandria port, approximately 3 hours by an air-conditioned vehicle. Get excited by viewing the enigmatic Pyramids of Giza of King Cheops , Chefren , Mykernuis , Sphinx , & Valley temple which are the most famous monuments of ancient Egypt, you can feel the mysteries surrounding their symbolism , design & purpose have inspired passionate debate, it’s likely that many of these mysteries will never be solved, Then enjoy a relaxing lunch cruise on the Nile for about 02 hours sailing. Enjoy a sumptuous buffet lunch as you glide past Cairo's waterfront landmarks and are entertained by a folkloric presentation. Before beginning your return trip to Alexandria, you will stop at a Papyrus Institute where you will have the opportunity to shop for souvenirs of Cairo & Egypt.
