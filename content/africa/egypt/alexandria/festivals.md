---
title: 'Festivals'
type: section
---

Alexandria hosts a handful of events worth timing a visit around.

**Alexandria Film Festival** -- Running since 1979, this is the city's most internationally recognized cultural event. Held annually in September, the festival is organized by the Egyptian Association of Film Writers and Critics and focuses on Mediterranean cinema, though international productions are also screened. It draws filmmakers, critics, and audiences from across the region.

**Alexandrias of the World Festival** -- A curious and charming event held in mid-October: all the cities in the world named Alexandria (there are more than 40) celebrate together. Alexandria, Egypt hosts cultural events, exhibitions, and performances over four days as part of this global gathering.

**Religious festivals** -- Islamic holidays, particularly Eid al-Fitr (marking the end of Ramadan) and Eid al-Adha, bring the city to life with family gatherings, special meals, and a festive atmosphere. Coptic Christian celebrations, including Christmas (January 7) and Easter, are also observed by Alexandria's significant Christian minority.
