---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment in Alexandria"
type: section
---

Experience the exciting **Alexandria nightlife** by enjoying
cocktails and good music in one of Alexandria’s bars and clubs. You can
choose from a number of remarkable bars where you can unwind and party
all night. Here are a few recommendations for you.

**San Giovanni Night Club**

Located right inside San Giovanni Hotel, the San Giovanni Night Club
provides various entertainments from belly dancers, live music, and
well-known singers to world-renowned show entertainers. This nightclub
welcomes you to a world of entertainment in Alexandria. Nights at this
club start from 10:30 p.m. and go to 4:00 a.m. This is one of the
popular nightclubs in Alexandria.

**Stingers Disco**

Found inside the Borg El Arab Resort Hotel, this party place is open
every day and it is known for offering the most popular western music
and incredible light effects. This has made Stingers Disco the most
partied place in Alexandria. It is best if you make reservations before
going there, as it is always full, especially during the weekends. You
will surely have a great experience and enjoy your night out in
Alexandria.

**Aquarious Discotheque**

Located right inside the prestigious Montazah Sheraton Hotel, the
Aquarious Discotheque is the most decent nightclub in Alexandria. Aside
from its great wine, Egyptian cuisine and a la carte menu, you can
appreciate the excellent view of the Mediterranean Sea and feel
comfortable in the nightclub’s marine-themed decor. Moreover, you can
work up a sweat on its dance floor, enjoying the latest Arabic and
international music hits.

**Al-Fouad Bar**

Opening as early as 11:00 a.m., the Al-Fouad Bar is situated right
inside the El-Salamlek Palace Hotel. Aside from the bar’s alcoholic and
non-alcoholic drinks, you can also appreciate the kind service and
hospitality it offers. While it is open during the daytime for you to
appreciate its good piano music, you can also visit it at night for its
partying scene. You can dance until dawn as you sample their cool
cocktails and drinks. The bar is open until 2:00 a.m.

**Qatr El-Nada Garden**

Situated inside the El-Salamlek Palace Hotel, Qatr El-Nada Garden is
known for its live music, its view of the sea, and its wonderful garden
breeze during nighttime. You can enjoy the party along with one of the
incredible cocktails there. The club opens as early as 11:00 a.m. to
bring you a great lunch, and closes at 3:00 a.m. to cater your partying
needs.
