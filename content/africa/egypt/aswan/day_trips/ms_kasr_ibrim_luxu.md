Itinerary:-
Itinerary A :Aswan - Abo Simbel / 4 Nights Every Saturday

Day 1: Arrive Aswan
Meet and assit by our travel REP upon your arrival at Aswan Airport / train station. Then you will be transferred to board your Lake Nasser Cruise ship. Lunch meal is served on board followed by a guided tour visit to Kalabsha Temple, the Kiosk of Kertassi and Beit El Wali. Return to the cruise for free time at leisure and afternoon tea served in the lounge. Dinner and overnight stay.

Day 2: Wadi El Seboua
Early Sailing to Wadi El Seboua. Enjoy breakfast on board and a cocktail when passing the Tropic of Cancer. Lunch and dinner meals served on board, and overnight stay.

Day 3: Wadi El Seboua / Amada
Enjoy breakfast a board before a n early visit to Wadi El Seboua, the Temple of Dakka and the Temple of Meharakka. back to the cruise ship for lunch on board while sailing to Amada. in arrival, visit the Temples of Amada and Derr as well as the Tomb of Penout. Dinner and overnight stay on board.

Day 4: Kasr Ibrim / Abu Simbel
Enjoy breakfast during sailing to Kasr Ibrim. in arrival, a view from the ship's sun deck will be conducted, as entrance to the site is not permitted. Depart, and sail to Abu Simbel. After lunch on board, visit the Temples of Abu Simbel. back to the cruise for a candlelight dinner. Overnight on board.

Day 5: Disembarkation
Disembarkation after breakfast a board the cruise

Itinerary B :Abo Simbel - Aswan / 3 Nights Every Wednesday
Day 1: Arrive Abu Simbel
Meet and assist by our REP in your arrival in Abu Simbel airport / abu simbel Hotels and transfer to board the Lake Nasser Cruise Ship. Lunch meal served on board before then you will visit the famous Temples of Abu Simbel with your tour guide. Return to the nile cruise for dinner. Overnight on board.

Day 2: Kasr Ibrim
Breakfast meal is served during sailing to Kasr Ibrim. Upon arrival, enjoy a tour conducted from the ship's sun deck as entrance to the site is not permitted by the Government. Lunch is served while sailing to Amada. Visit the temples of Amada and Derr as well as the Tomb of Penout, then return to the cruise for a relaxing and calm sailing to Wadi El Seboua. Dinner and overnight stay.

Day 3: Wadi El Seboua
Enjoy a morning visit to Wadi El Seboua and the Temples of Dakka and Meharakka. Return to the ship for lunch and sail to Aswan. Dinner on board and overnight stay.

Day 4: Aswan sightseeing - Departure
Visit the Kalabsha Temple, Beit El Wali, and the Kiosk of Kertassi. Depart the nile cruise ship after breakfast.
