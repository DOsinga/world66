**Tour Itinerary:**

**Day 1: Alexandria port to Cairo, famous
Pyramids of Giza, Memphis, Sakkara****

**Our **Cairo sightseeing tour**
will start with pick up service from your ship terminal in **Alexandria port** , where our REP will meet you with
aboard reflecting your name, then you will be transferred by air-conditioned
vehicle to **Cairo**. In **Cairo** you will be accompanied by **private** Egyptologist guide and have first step of
your **Cairo sightseeing** **tour** to **famous Pyramids** of **Giza**, one of the Seven Wonders of the **Ancient** World, the tombs of pharaohs constructed
around 5000 years ago and **great Sphinx** - guard of
those tombs, with body of lion and human head. Lunch meal will be served at a
good quality local restaurant in **Cairo**. Next **sightseeing** of your **Cairo**
**tour** is Memphis Necropolis, the
oldest capital of Egypt and the legendary city of King Menes, who united Upper
and Lower Egypt. There you will visit huge cemetery of **ancient** Memphis, which was an active burial ground
for more than 3500 years, final resting place for deceased pharaohs and their
families, and is Egypt’s largest archaeological site. Also you will visit
during **Cairo sightseeing** **tour** Sakkara - one section of the great necropolis
of Memphis, best known for the Step Pyramid, the oldest known of Egypt **pyramids**, built for King Zoser. Free time for
photos and camel ride. Then you will be transferred to your hotel in **Cairo** to have rest and overnight stay. You can
attend light and sound show at **famous Pyramids** of **Giza** or enjoy dinner cruise with belly dance show
in the Nile River in **Cairo**, as optional **tour**.

**Day 2: Egyptian Museum, Citadel, Alabaster
Mosque, Old Cairo, return to Alexandria port****

**Have breakfast at your hotel in **Cairo** and continue the
second day of your **Cairo sightseeing** **tour** with your guide to visit the **Egyptian Museum** with extensive collection of 120000
items of **ancient** **Egyptian** antiquities, **famous** by artefacts of the child king Tutankhamen's
tomb, which was found mostly intact. Lunch meal will be served at a good
quality local restaurant in **Cairo**. Next **sightseeing** of your **tour**
in **Cairo** will be Citadel.
Originally, it is a fortification and was built on a promontory beneath of Muqattam
hills, that make a amazing view of **Cairo**. This is one of the
oldest castles in the world for centuries reflected the invasion of the
conquerors. For seven centuries the Citadel in **Cairo**
was the seat of the rulers of Egypt. One of the most striking **sightseeing** of the Citadel in **Cairo**, of course, the Alabaster mosque of Muhammad
Ali. Architecture of the mosque - a mixture of many different styles and it's
in sharp contrast with the strict structures of Mamluk era. Mosque - square in
plan, the length of each side of 41 m. At the western facade of tower two
cylindrical minarets in the Ottoman style, each 84 meters in height. In the
inner space of the mosque is dominated Turkish Rococo style and interior wins
by its dizzying volumes. Afterwards in your **Cairo sightseeing** **tour**
you visit Old **Cairo** and see Coptic
Monastery with Hanging Church. The area called "Complex of Three
Religions", because there also set the mosque of Amr Ibn Al Asa, erected
in the year 642, and Ben Ezra Synagogue, dating from the tenth century. Free
time in **Cairo**, then you will be driven
to **Alexandria port** back to your
ship terminal.
