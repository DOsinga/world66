**Travel Package Itinerary: **

**Day 1 : Arrival Cairo international airport**

Meet & assist by our representative, who will help you in handling all your
procedures through passport control, customs formalities & luggage area,
then transfer you to your hotel via air conditioned modern vehicle &
overnight in **Cairo**.

**Day 2 : Pyramids – Egyptian Museum ****

**Breakfast at your hotel in **Cairo**, enjoy a full day **tour**
to visit the enigmatic Pyramids of Giza of King Cheops, Chefren, Mykernuis,
Sphinx & Valley temple, which are the most famous monuments of
ancient **Egypt**, you can feel the mysteries surrounding their
symbolism, design & purpose have inspired passionate debate, it’s
likely that many of these mysteries will never be solved, then proceed your
fantastic **tour** to visit the Egyptian antiquities museum
commonly known as the Egyptian Museum, which located in **Egypt**
capital city of **Cairo**, where you can see the most extensive
collection of ancient antiquities & the famous golden mask of Tutankhamun
among other amazing pharaonic artifacts, then back to your hotel &
overnight in **Cairo**.

**Day 3: Cairo - Aswan – Nile Cruise Holiday**

Breakfast at your hotel in **Cairo**, transfer to **Cairo**
airport for departure to **Aswan** by flight, arrival **Aswan**
airport, meet & assist by our representative, transfer to your **Nile
Cruise** boat for embarkation before lunch. In the afternoon, you will
discover the gigantic High Dam; the unfinished Obelisk and Philae temple
dedicated to the goddess ISIS. Dinner and overnight on board **Nile
cruise** in **Aswan**.

**Day 4: Kom Ombo - Edfu - Nile Cruise Holiday**

In the early morning, your ship will sail to Kom Ombo where you will visit the
unusual double temple dedicated to the crocodile god Sobek and the god
Haroeris. Lunch on board **Nile cruise**. Admire the beautiful
scenery of the **Nile** while sailing to Edfu, where you will
discover the huge temple dedicated to the God Horus by horse carriage and **tour**
it with your guide. Visit the colorful local bazaars on your way where you can
buy your souvenirs. Dinner and overnight on board **Nile cruise**.

**Day 5: Luxor - Nile Cruise Holiday ****

**After breakfast it is time to focus on the West Bank of **Luxor**:
explore the great valleys of the Kings and Queens where generations of Pharaohs
and Nobles were buried in crypts cut into the cliffs and guarded by the two
huge Colossi of Memnon. You will then visit the temple of Queen Hatshepsut.
Back to the **cruise** for lunch. Enjoy the afternoon in the East
Bank of **Luxor**: walk among the pillars of the temple dedicated
to the god Amun Ra and along the avenue of the Sphinxes at Karnak and **Luxor**
temples, an experience which you will treasure for the rest of your life.
Dinner and overnight in **Luxor**.

**Day 6: Luxor - Hurghada - Beach Holiday****

**Check out after breakfast, drive by air-conditioned modern vehicle to **Hurghada**
the Egyptian beautiful city based on the Red Sea, it’s distinctive coastal
harbor-city that’s scattered with sandy beaches, coral reefs, aquatic life,
night clubs & bars along with prospects for outdoor recreational
activities, **Hurghada** is dotted with several beaches where the
warm water is home to rare fishes & coral reefs that can be glanced through
the glass bottom boats, overnight in **Hurghada**.

**Day 7: Hurghada Beach Holiday****

**Breakfast at your hotel in **Hurghada**, enjoy with the sunny
& sandy beach where all of water sports are available such as diving ,
snorkeling & windsurfing , **Hurghada** is the most for
tourist resort on the Red Sea coast & an international center for all kind
of aquatic, its unique underwater coral gardens are the best in the world, you
can have a day trip to Giftun Island for snorkeling & fishing or view the
Marine life through a glass bottomed, when you’re not in the sea you can go for
shopping or have a safari trip by jeep to the red sea mountains (all water sports
available on request)

**Day 8 : Hurghada Beach Holiday****

**Free time at your own at the beach for snorkeling - diving - sunbathing

**Day 9 : Hurghada Beach Holiday**

Free time at your own at the beach for snorkeling - diving - sunbathing

**Day 10: Hurghada - Cairo****

**After having your breakfast, drive via air-conditioned modern vehicle back
to **Cairo** & overnight.

**Day 11: Cairo - Fly Home****

**After having your breakfast, transfer to **Cairo**
international airport for final departure, assistance through formalities by
our representative.
