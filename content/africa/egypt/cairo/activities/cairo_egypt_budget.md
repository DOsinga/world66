For those who haven’t much time, the Cairo Egypt Budget Tour is extensive and stimulating, offering the best of Egypt to participants.

Itinerary: Cairo Budget Tour, 4 Days

Day 1: Arrive in Egypt

Upon arrival on the Cairo, Egypt Budget Tour you will be met by our representative when you arrive at the Cairo International Airport. You will then enjoy an air-conditioned vehicle on the route to your hotel, where you will spend the night.

Day 2: Giza Pyramids/Saqqara

After enjoying breakfast at your hotel, you will discover the exquisite Giza pyramids of Cheops, Chefren and Mykerinus. You wilt then visit the Great Sphinx, as well as the Valley Temple.

Next on this days sightseeing program is Memphis, the capital of ancient Egypt. Finally, you will proceed to Djoser's Step Pyramid in Saqqara. You will return to your hotel to spend the night.

Day 3: Cairo Sightseeing

After you finish breakfast, you will visit the Egyptian Museum. Here, you will enjoy more than 250,000 unique pharaonic artifacts, as well as the treasures of Tutankhamen.

You will then visit Coptic Cairo, where you will see the Hanging Church, the Church of Abu Sirga and Ben Ezra Synagogue. You will then return to your hotel for the night.

Day 4: Final departure

Immediately after breakfast on the final day of your Cairo, Egypt Budget Tour you will be transferred to the Cairo International Airport, where you will catch your departure flight.
