Several operators in Cairo run jeep safaris into the Western Desert. If you prefer something more independent, hire horses from one of the Pyramids Road stables and ride out into the desert for a barbecue or overnight stay. Spending a night in the desert outside Cairo — with clear skies and total silence after the day's chaos — is an experience that stays with you.
