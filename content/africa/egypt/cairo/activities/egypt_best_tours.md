Day 1: Arrival Cairo, visit Egypt
Our Egypt Best Tour Package start upon your arrival at the airport where you will be met and assisted by our travel & tour representative who will escort to your hotel in Cairo for check in and relax. In the evening an optional dinner cruise with belly dance show, then back to the hotel and overnightstay.

Day 2: visit Egypt, pyramids
After Breakfast, start your first day by a sightseeing tour to visit Egypt, to visit the great pyramids of Egypt, the Sphinx and ValleyTemple. Lunch is served during the tour. Optional tour sound and light show at the Pyramids. Then visit Papyrus institute and Perfume Factory if you wish to visit it. Transfer back to the hotel and overnight.

Day 3: Cairo Sightseeing, visit Cairo, sleeper Train to Aswan
Breakfast at hotel in Cairo followed by sightseeing tour in Cairo to visit the Egyptian Museum, tour to visit Mohamed Ali Alabaster Mosque and Saladin Citadel, and the Coptic churches in Old Cairo as well followed by a shopping tour to the famous Khan El Khalili bazaars. Lunch is served during the tours, from there you will be taken to the train station to board on the first class sleeping train to Aswan. Overnight aboard the train.

Day 4: Aswan Sightseeing, Nile cruise
Breakfast aboard the train before reaching Aswan, Upon arrival our travel REP will be waiting for you to escort you in a guided tour to the sights in Aswan to visit the high dam, the unfinished obelisk and the Philae Temple, then transfer to the cruise to check in and lunch aboard the cruise. In the afternoon enjoy a felucca ride around the rocky island of the Nile. Transfer to the cruise, dinner and entertainment on board. Sail to Kom ombo and overnight stay onboard .

Day 5: Kom Ombo – Edfu tours - Nile cruise
Breakfast, arrival to Kom Ombo and visit the temple of Kom Ombo. The temple is dedicated to the crocodile god Sobek and the falcon god Haroeris. Continue sailing to Edfu, lunch on board. After lunch a sightseeing tour to visit Edfu temple, the major Ptolemaic temple and the best preserved in Egypt. The temple is dedicated to the falcon god Horus and was built over a 180 years period from 237 BC to 57 BC. Continue sailing to Esna to cross the lock. Dinner followed by a galabiya party, sail to Luxor and overnight aboard.
