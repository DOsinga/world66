The turquoise-colored water and soft, sandy beaches of El
Agami and Hannoville lie about 12 miles west of downtown Alexandria
along the Mediterranean Sea in northern Egypt. Tourists as well as
locals flock to these beaches, also known as the Egyptian Saint-Tropez.
The Agami and Hannoville beaches became famous in the mid-1940s, when
British officers stationed in Egypt headed to the area for recreation
during their time off from war activities.
Activities
Swimming and sunbathing are the most popular
activities at the Agami and Hannoville beaches, especially in the
summer. The beaches are within walking distance of each other. You also
can hire a bicycle or jump on an open-sided bus to take you between the
beaches, which are about a mile apart. Pick up local foods at the nearby
markets and bring them to the beach to enjoy a snack or a picnic lunch.
Vendors lined up along the beach sell hot food, snacks and cold
beverages. A trip to the Sidi Kreir beach, located just beyond
Hannoville, gives you access to 1.8 miles of private beach accessible
for a fee. The area includes a tourist village and a casino.
Planning
The dress code on Agami and Hannoville beaches
remains conservative, making one-piece bathing suits ideal. You may wear
bikinis on semi-private beaches, but they are not recommended on public
beaches. Plan to wear clothing that covers your bathing suit on your
way to and from the beach. Cotton clothing works well in the humid
landscape. Bring bottled water to keep hydrated. Sunglasses with high UV
ratings and sunscreen make the effects of the sun less harmful. Unless
you borrow chairs and an umbrella from your hotel, plan to bring a beach
towel to spread out on the sand.
Climate
The hottest time of the year occurs from May to
October, when the beaches get very busy with locals and tourists alike.
Temperatures average from 62 to 73 degrees Fahrenheit thanks to the
city’s location in northern Egypt, where it stays cooler than the
desert-like sections of southern Egypt. If you prefer to walk the beach
with fewer people around or just want to enjoy some solitude, the
mild-to-cool weather that occurs from November to April makes it an
optimal time to visit. Come prepared for the occasional rain that falls
during the winter.
