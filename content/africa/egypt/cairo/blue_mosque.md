The Blue Mosque of Cairo can't compete with Istanbul's, but it's still worth a stop. The tiled interior and peaceful courtyard offer a brief escape from the city's noise, and the surrounding neighbourhood — Bab Zuweila — is one of the most atmospheric parts of medieval Cairo.
