Egypt Adventure tour Package Itinerary:-
Day 1: Siwa and Alamein
Our Sunray Tours representative will take you from your hotel in Cairo in the early morning and bring you to Alamein, where you will visit the WW II Museum and the Cemetery. Proceed your Egypt Adventure Tour Package with the amazing bay of Marsa Matrouh, which is located on the Mediterranean Coast and the capital of Matrouh Governorate in Egypt. Then move on to Siwa. Take your dinner and spend the night there.

Day 2: Sightseeing Tour Siwa
After you take your breakfast at the hotel, you will start the second day of your Egypt and Oasis Trip with Shali, the Old City of Siwa. Continue with Aghurmi Village, where you will see the Temple of Alexander, which was constructed for God Amon and after this discover the Amun Ra Temple in Um Ubeyda. Continue with the famous Spring of Cleopatra. End the tour for this day with the Siwa House Museum, where you will have the opportunity for unforgettable photos. Later we will bring you Fitnas Island, where you will enjoy an overwhelmed view of the sunset. Take your dinner in your hotel in Siwa and spend the night there.

Day 3: Shiata Lake and Bir Wahed
Take your breakfast at the hotel, then start your tour with Maraki Village, which is located at the Siwa Dessert. Cross the dessert, to see the archeology sign at the Great Siwa Mountain. Here you will see the first human food step, which is from the pre-history area. The next place will be the fossils area and the rocky coral reefs. Proceed with the Shiata Lake in the middle of the dessert. You will have the chance to see the Flamenco Birds and to swim relaxed in the cold spring in the dessert atmosphere.
