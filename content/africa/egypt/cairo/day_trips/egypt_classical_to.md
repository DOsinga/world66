Itinerary:-
DAY 1: Arrive Cairo, Egypt
Upon your arrival at Cairo Airport, our representative will be waiting to assist you through airport formalities and escort you to the hotel in Cairo for check in and overnight stay in Cairo.

DAY 2:Cairo sightseeing tours - Train to Aswan
Check out after breakfast. Depart for a full day city tour of Cairo. Visit the famous Egyptian Museum. After discovering the pyramids of Giza and the famous Sphinx, you will visit the Citadel of Saladdin and Mohamed Ali Mosque. Your tour will end with a visit to Khan El Khalili, the oldest bazaar in Cairo, where you can shop for souvenirs. After a full day in Cairo, you will be escorted to Giza railway station to board the sleeper train to Aswan. Dinner and overnight on board.

DAY 3: Arrive Aswan - Nile Cruise Holiday
Morning arrival at the Aswan Train Station, where you will be met by our representative for transfer to your Nile cruise, which will be your home for the next three nights. Check in. Embarkation and check in before lunch on board. In the afternoon, you will visit the High Dam, unfinished obelisk, and beautiful Temple of Philae. Dinner and overnight on board.

DAY 4:Kom Ombo - Edfu - Nile Cruise Holiday
Early morning sail to Kom Ombo. In Kom Ombo, visit the temples of Sobek and Horus. Lunch on board. Relax and admire the beautiful scenery along the Nile while sailing to Edfu. In Edfu, take a tour by horse-drawn carriage to visit the Temple of Horus. Back on board and sail to Luxor. Dinner and overnight on board.

DAY 5: Luxor - Nile Cruise Holiday
Following breakfast on board; depart for visits to the sites on the West Bank, including the Colossi of Memnon, Temple of Queen Hatchepsut, and three tombs in the Valley of the Kings. Lunch on board. Spend the afternoon visiting the Karnak and Luxor Temples on the East Bank. Dinner and overnight on board in Luxor.

DAY 6: Luxor free day - Cairo
Disembark the cruise after breakfast; free time in Luxor for shopping and optional tours ( optional tour to visit Dandara Temple - or Luxor Museum) then transfer to Luxor train terminal to board the sleeper train to Cairo.

DAY 7: Cairo - Bahariya Oasis - White Desert
In arrival at Cairo you will start our 360 km journey to Bahariya Oasis. Lunch then visit The black Desert and the dune area. We start our climb of a 50 meter dune and in the route to Farafra we visit the Crystal Mountain, the area of the wonders with the desert roses then we head to the White Desert. We start exploring the formations and wind carved sculptor, in the evening, we have a barbeque dinner and sleep in camps under the stars
