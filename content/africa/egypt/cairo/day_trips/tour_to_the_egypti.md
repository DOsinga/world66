Pick up from your hotel in Cairo , enjoy an excursion to visit the Egyptian Museum where you can see the collection of Tutankhamun among other splendid pharaonic artifacts.The museum exhibits over 120000 objects, some of the important groups of these objects are : Artifacts from the tombs of kings and members of the royal families . After that visit Salah El-Din Citadel that rises on The Hill of Al-Moqattam, in one of the highest areas in Cairo. It’s about 800 years old, but still firm and grand and there you will visit also Mohammed Ali’s Mosque. Then onto the Coptic area of Cairo where the Romans constructed the fortress of Babylon. Built over the ruins of two towers of the fortress the church of St Mary or more commonly know as the Hanging Church then onto St Sergius where it is said to be the resting place for the holy family.Transfer back to your hotel in Cairo .
