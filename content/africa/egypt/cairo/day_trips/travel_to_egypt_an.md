Egypt and Morocco Tour Package Itinerary:-
Day 1: Arrival in Cairo
After you arrived Cairo airport, our Sunray Tours representative will welcome you there and help you with the arrival papers. Then he will bring you to the hotel in which you will stay in this night.

Day 2: Giza Plateau and Egyptian Museum
Start this day of your Egypt and Morocco Holidays with a breakfast and after this the first place will be the Giza Plateau, where you will discover the Giza Pyramids, the Wonder of the Ancient World and the gigantic Sphinx, which was carved out of a solid rock, has the head of a human and the body of a lion to show the strength of the Pharaohs. Visit the Valley Temple and proceed your trip with the Egyptian Museum in Cairo, which displays you thousand of statues, jewels, the Dead Mask of king Tut-Ankh-Amun and artifacts older than 5000 years. The last stop for this day will be the Khan El Khalili Market, which is the oldest in the Middle East. Then we transfer you to the train station in Cairo, there you will take the train to Aswan and spend the night in this sleeper train.

Day 3: Sightseeing Tour Aswan
After your breakfast on the train we will pick you up and bring you to the 5 stars Nile Cruise, where you will take your lunch.
