**Tour Itinerary:**

Our **Cairo tour** will start
with pick up service from your hotel in **Marsa Alam**, where our REP
will meet you with aboard reflecting your name, then from **Marsa Alam** you will be transferred by
air-conditioned vehicle to **Hurghada** airport for 1
hour flight to **Cairo**. In **Cairo** you will be accompanied by private
Egyptologist guide and have first step of your **Cairo
tour **to **great** **Pyramids of Giza**, one of the Seven Wonders of the **Ancient** World, the tombs of pharaohs constructed
around 5000 years ago and **great Sphinx** - guard of
those tombs, with body of lion and human head. Lunch meal will be served during
