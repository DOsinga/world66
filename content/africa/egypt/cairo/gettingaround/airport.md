Cairo International Airport is 23 km northeast of the city centre. A taxi to Downtown takes around 30 minutes outside rush hour; agree a price before you get in (100–150 Egyptian pounds is reasonable). An air-conditioned bus also runs from the far side of the parking lot to a stop behind the Egyptian Museum for a fraction of the cost. International check-in closes 90 minutes before departure; domestic, 30 minutes.
