A shared shuttle service running between Cairo International Airport and Downtown. Seven-seater air-conditioned vans; a group of seven pays around 70 Egyptian pounds total. Recommended for avoiding the taxi negotiation on arrival. Book by phone and look for the shuttle desk at the airport.
