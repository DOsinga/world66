Turn left off Pyramids Road about a kilometre before the Pyramids and you'll find a row of stables — "AB Stables", "EuroStables" and several others. The horses here are well-kept, better quality than the ones rented right at the pyramid gates. Rates run 20–40 Egyptian pounds per hour. The stables sit at the very edge of the desert; beyond them is nothing but sand. Sunset is the best time to ride, and the view of all three pyramids from the top of the dunes is hard to forget.
