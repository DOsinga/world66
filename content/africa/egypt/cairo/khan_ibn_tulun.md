A small, high-quality crafts shop directly behind the Ibn Tulun Mosque, run by an Egyptian-Swiss couple. Pottery, Bedouin jewellery, hand-woven textiles, and decorative objects — all of it several notches above the souvenir-market norm. Worth seeking out if you want something that will actually last.
