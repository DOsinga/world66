One of Cairo's long-running Indian restaurants, located in the Maadi neighbourhood that has always been home to much of the city's expat community. The menu covers the expected subcontinental range — tandoori dishes, curries, biryanis — in a setting that errs on the comfortable side. Not cutting-edge, but reliable when the craving hits and the alternatives are limited.
