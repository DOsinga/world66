The Great Pyramids sit at the edge of the desert in Giza, a short taxi ride from central Cairo. Plan at least half a day — go inside the Cheops Pyramid if you can bear the steep, narrow descent, and be sure to visit the Sphinx nearby. The hawkers around the site are relentless; a firm "no thank you" and the confidence to walk away are your best tools.
