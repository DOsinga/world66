title: "City of Death"
type: poi
---

You might also be interested in visiting the City of the Dead, a cemetery larger than 1 sq mi/2 sq km. It looks as if it’s part of the city —it’s filled with buildings laid out on a grid of streets— but each building holds only a tomb. The size of the building reflects the wealth of the person buried there. A population of squatters has taken up residence in the cemetery and it’s not a safe place to visit after dark.
