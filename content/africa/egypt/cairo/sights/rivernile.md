The river Nile flows majestically through the city. Although the water is really polluted and dangerous to swim in, the presence of the river gives the town something extra.
 It’s possible to take a trip on the Nile for just a few pennies (the river runs right through the city and commuter boats shuttle between the shores) or you might want to take a lunch or dinner cruise.
