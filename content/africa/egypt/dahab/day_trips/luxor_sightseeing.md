Overday **trip** to **Luxor** from **Dahab** by flight to the sightseeing in East bank of
Nile River, West bank and Colossi of Memnon with free time in **Luxor**. Private vehicle and included during **Luxor** **tour**.

**Tour Itinerary:**

Our **Luxor tour** will start with pick
up service from your hotel in **Dahab**, where our REP will
meet you with aboard reflecting your name, then from **Dahab** you will be transferred by air-conditioned
vehicle to Sharm El Sheikh airport for 1 hour flight to **Luxor**. In **Luxor** you will be
accompanied by private Egyptologist guide and have first step of your **Luxor tour** to **Valley
of the Kings** - burial place of pharaohs, hidden in the rocks,
decorated with scences and funerary rituals of the period for afterlife of
pharaohs. **Valley of Kings** is famous for
Tutankhamen tomb and its treasures, discovered in 1922 by Howard Carter. Next
sight of your visiting **tour** in **Luxor** will be **Hatshepsut
temple** at El Deir Al Bahari, dedicated to the **queen Hatshepsut** - famous female pharaoh. **Hatshepsut temple** with three layered terraces
connected by long ramps which were once surrounded by gardens. Inside **Hatshepsut temple** you can see text and picture of
an expedition to exotic country calls Land of Punt. Proceed **Luxor tour** to **Colossi
of Memnon**, two colossal statues, was stayed guard at entrance
of Amenhotep III temple in **ancient** times, and only
remaining nowadays. Name '**Colossi of Memnon**' means 'Ruler
of the Dawn' and was probably given, because of 'singing' at dawn one of the
statue. Lunch meal will be served during **Luxor tour** at a good
quality restaurant in **Luxor**. Continue **tour** in **Luxor** to **Karnak temple**, dedicated to the God Amon-Ra, his
wife Mut and their son Khonsu. In **Karnak temple** each
pharaoh tried to perpetuate his name. One of the famous area in **Karnak temple** - Hypostyle hall with 134 massive
columns with over three meters in diameter. Free time in **Luxor**, then you will be driven to **Luxor** airport to fly to Sharm El Sheikh, with
direct transfer to your hotel in **Dahab**.
