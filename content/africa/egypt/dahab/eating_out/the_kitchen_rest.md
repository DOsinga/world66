The Kitchen Restaurant is located in the heart of Dahab, offering
some of the most impressive sea and mountain views our beautiful town
has to offer. It is arranged over 3 floors, the ground floor is
designated non smoking, while as the 2 upper floors are semi and
entirely open air, smoking is permitted.

Whether you’d like to eat inside, outside, upstairs,
downstairs, in a smoking area, or non smoking, in the air conditioned
area, out in the sea breeze, you’d like your food to takeaway or to be
delivered, you can be sure your needs will be well provided for.

If you decide to order takeaway food, enjoy your short wait by sinking into one of our comfy sofas and
plug into the free wifi while your food is being prepared.

Each of specialist chefs is highly trained and has
previously been working in either 5* hotels or other top restaurants.
Not only is the food they serve absolutely delicious, it’s always
beautifully presented.

Hygiene issues are one of the key drivers of restaurant
choice for most customers. This is why it is top of out list of
priorities to ensure all health and hygiene standards are met. All new
staff employed by The Kitchen are fully trained to a high standard in
our health and hygiene course, and continuously trained throughout
their employment with us, to ensure the high standards are adhered to.
We have an open kitchen so all customers can see how clean the
facilities are. Mineral water is always used in the food preparation
and to make the ice. Toilet facilities are monitored every hour to
ensure cleanliness levels are kept high.
