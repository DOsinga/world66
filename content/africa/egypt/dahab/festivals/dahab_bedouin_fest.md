The Dahab Bedouin Festival group was set up in February 2011 inspired by the new found freedom in Egypt. Our aim is to promote Bedouin culture and Bedouin in the tourism industry. Last year we organised the first Bedouin Festival in Dahab and have been organising regular events on a monthly basis since then. Our other objectives are to create awareness in Dahab concerning the environment and ethics towards our foreign guests.

This year the festival will be taking place between the 1st and 7th of April. We hope people visiting Dahab and local residents take this opportunity to come and visit the festival in the flood plain, by the bridge in Dahab. It is an ideal time for cultural exchange and communication. We hope to gather people for future charity and environmental work for the benefit of the Bedouin and the entire community.

We welcome everyone without exception to come and have a welcome tea and spend some relaxing and educational time with the Bedouin of Sinai at the Dahab Bedouin Festival.
