Arrival in **Aswan**, and start the day by visiting the Aswan **Highdam**, whose 364-feet-high walls hold back 300 mile-long Lake Nasser, world’s largest reservoir.

We take a boat to Philae Island to see the distinctive **Temple of Isis** and gain an insight in the quarrying techniques of ancients at the Unfinished Obelisk. Sail on the Nile by felucca around **Kitchener’s island and Elephantine**.

Early morning sail to **Kom Ombo** and visit the **Temple of Sobek**, the crocodile-headed god. Located on the edge of the Nile, and contains many fine reliefs.

Approaching **Edfu**, and visit the temple of Horus, its menacing black stone statue of the falcon god. Built in the time of Cleopatra about 2,000 years ago. Back to our cruiser, start sail to Esna. Then cross the lock and continue sailing to Luxor.

The cruiser approaches **Luxor**. Cross the Nile to the west bank, where the sun sets over a backdrop of arid mountains and where pharaoh chose to bury the dead at **Valley of Kings & Valley of Queens**. We will descend into some of the finest tombs.

**Hatshepsut temple** at El Deir El Bahari, build in honor of the only woman to ever region Egypt as Pharaoh. And then the **Colossi of Memnon**, an enormous pair of enthroned statues that once guarded the entrance to a long lost temple.

Disembarkation from your lovely Cruise. Visit to **Luxor temple**, a majestic piece of Egyptian architecture, the temple was dedicated to sun god Amun, with its gigantic statues of Ramses II

Three miles away is the temple of **Karnak**, a vast and awe-inspiring complex of pillared Halls, towering pylons, scared lakes and obelisks.
