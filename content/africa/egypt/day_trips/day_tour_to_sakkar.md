Pick up from your hotel in Cairo to enjoy an excursion to visit the step Pyramid of Sakkarra is considered as an evolution in the concept of pyramids, from the simple mastaba to the pyramids in its final form in the region in Sakkara. It was built as step pyramid, its height is 60 meters and consists of 6 steps on top of each other, each is smaller than the one below . After that transfer to the south to visit the Pyramids ( the Red Pyramid and Bent Pyramid ) of Dahshour that always evoke in me a great part of the history of Ancient Egypt. The Southern (Bent) Pyramid of Dashur was built of local limestone and cased with fine, Turah limestone. Back to your hotel in Cairo .
