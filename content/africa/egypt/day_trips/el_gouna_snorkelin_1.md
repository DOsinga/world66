El Gouna Snorkeling and Fishing Trip by boat From Hurghada

Tour Type:Snorkeling & Relaxation
Tour Length:9 Hours
City Tour:El Gouna, Egypt
Pick up Point:Your hotel In Hurghada
Drop off Point:Your hotel In Hurghada

Tour Itinerary : -

Pick you up from your hotel in Hurghada by air-conditioned vehicle for a full day trip to El Gouna by boat for snorkeling and swimming with the dolphins and fishing is also available on board the boat. Continue sailing to El Gouna to board the shuttle boat from our big boat and sailing in the Laguna between the hotels in El Gouna ( sailing among the hotels in the small canals connected to the Sea). Then go to aquarium Museum in El Gouna to discover the undersea life and know some information about the Marine life in the Red Sea. Start sailing back to the Big boat and then continue sailing back to Hurghada, in the way back you will stop for snorkeling again, then proceed sailing to Hurghada with direct transfer to your hotel in Hurghada.

Tour Includes : -

Pick up and drop off services from your hotel in Hurghada
All transfers by Air-conditioned vehicles
Snorkeling Equipment aboard the Boat
Lunch meal aboard the Boat
Mineral water and soft drink aboard the boat
Entrance fees to the aquarium Museum in El Gouna
Multi Lingual Guide aboard the boat

Tour Excludes : -

Personal Expenses
