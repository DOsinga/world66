***Tour Package Itinerary:
*Day # 1: Arrive Hurghada, Egypt**
Meet and assist upon arrival at Hurghada international airport by our travel REP and then you will be transferred to your hotel for check in and free time to relax and overnight stay.

**Day # 2: Hurghada City Tour
**Enjoy your Breakfast at your hotel with leisurely free time at to enjoy the sandy beach. In the afternoon you will be accompanied by your private English speaking guide and exclusive deluxe vehicle for a city tour of Hurghada to visit the Aquarium Museum, followed by a visit to the Old Mosque and the Orthodox Church of St. Mary Church, the fishermen village as well as the port where the small ships being constructed and renovated. Enjoy Free time Shopping in the local bazaars where you can find your own Egyptian treasures that can be purchased!. Finally a short visit to our projects in Hurghada From there you will be transferred back to the hotel for free time and an enjoyable overnight stay at your hotel in Hurghada.

**Day # 3: Luxor Sightseeing tours**
After your relaxing early breakfast at the hotel you will be picked up by our English Egyptologist expert guide to leave Hurghada for Luxor to visit Karnak Temple, the largest place of worship that was ever built! Its ancient name Ipet-isut means “the most sacred of places.” The temple, or, more correctly, the complex of temples, was built more than two thousand years ago by generation after generation of pharaohs. Within the complex, the great “Hypostyle Hall” is an incredible forest of giant pillars . You will be served lunch at the beautiful specious restaurant of Mercure hotel in Luxor .Next you will cross the Nile River by a relaxing Sail on the Felucca to see the two colossal statues of Amenhotep III (Colossi of Memnon), and then you will enjoy visiting the Valley of the Kings, where the magnificent tombs were discovered, those tombs were carved deep into the desert rock, richly decorated and filled with treasures for the afterlife of the pharaohs, you will the chance to visit 3 tombs. Also you visit the Hatshipsut temple at El Deir El Bahary, this impressive temple was dedicated to Queen Hatshipsut the only female pharaoh who rises out of the desert plain in a series of terraces and merges with the sheer limestone cliffs that surround it . Then you will return back to your hotel in Hurghada for a wonderful dinner and overnight stay

**Day # 4: Sunset Desert Safari Tour by 4X4**
Enjoy Breakfast at the hotel with leisurely free time on the sandy beach and enjoy the hotel’s unique facilities. In the afternoon you will be taken into the fascinating desert of Hurghada for an unforgettable Jeep or quad bike sunset desert safari tour. Making memories that will last you a lifetime! Enjoy a unique camel ride and Join the Bedouins for a barbeque dinner including all your beverages. At 2:00 PM our expert English speaking desert guide and driver will take you through the bizarre landscapes and drying valleys into the desert to a Bedouin Village with the possibility for photographing the impressing desert landscape and the mountains and then a Bedouin Village tour to learn about the their habits, traditions, lifestyle and the daily life affairs, And how their women prepare the tasty flatbread. Next you will ride a camel in the desert to discover the area with fascinating sunset view from the top of the surrounding hills that will be captured your mind for years to come, and the enjoy a barbecue dinner that will be served in Bedouins style under an unforgettable starlit sky and romantic candlelight .the trip ending with a Bedouin show, Bedouin dance and folkloric songs. Then you will return back to your hotel for some free time and overnight stay

**Day # 5: Snorkeling Trip by Boat**
Buffet breakfast at the hotel. After breakfast transfer to the Marina and embark the ship for a fantastic snorkeling trip. On the Desert Island free time for swimming and play with dolphins, great opportunity for different kind of water sports activities. Lunch aboard including water and soft drinks. In the afternoon sail back to the marina and transfer to your hotel for free time at leisure and overnight.

**Day # 6: Cairo Sightseeing Tours
**You will have an Early morning breakfast and then you will be accompanied by your guide for a full day tour in Giza and Cairo to see the ONE of the original Seven Wonders of the ancient world! The Great Pyramids of Cheops, Chephren and Micerinus and the almighty sphinx, the royal human head on a lion's body! Then you will visit the valley temple, the awesome megalithic temple, known as the Sphinx Temple, and the Valley Temple traditionally attributed to the Pharaoh Chephren that it is an absolute wonder of engineering. Lunch meal will be served at a local restaurant of high quality food close to the Pyramids. then you will be visiting the Egyptian Museum ( there may be an extra charge added for these extra tour activities ) of antiques that was built in the neo-classical style by the French architect Marcel Dourgenon the museum housing over 120000 objects from different Pharaonic eras especially those of the child king Tutankhamen .Then you will Proceed to the citadel of Saladin and Mohammed Ali`s Alabaster Mosque. Then enjoy a relaxing drive to the Old Cairo to visit the old Coptic churches and the fortress of Babylon where the holy family took a refuge Leaves the Church of St Barbara and St. St. George's church to the Islamic Cairo and Khan El Khalili bazaars and Old Market with free time to walk around and discover the amazing area in your own way then back to your hotel in Hurghada for overnight stay.

**Day # 7: Hurghada /1001 Night Arabian Show
**Enjoy your Breakfast at the hotel with some leisurely free time in Hurghada, In the evening Take a fantastic evening tour of Alf Leila WA Leila fantasia show, including sound and Light show, belly dancer, horse show and folkloric songs and oriental show That are the best in Hurghada, Alf Leila WA Leila show is the biggest entertainment and banqueting center in the Middle East, a rare combination of oriental Andalusia architecture of Royal Gates, Domes, Towers and the never ending fantasy of Egyptian charm. A place where you can enjoy the life of Pharaohs, the Bedouins, customs and traditions of the region Alf Leila WA Leila is unique for its spacious, splendid and marvelous theatre of 2500 audience who are comfortably offered a breathtaking spectacle. Live music performances and folkloric dances in the tents, the horse show of twenty Bedouin knights presenting individual and group acrobatic games, as well as other shows performed in a very professional artistic talent. Then you will return to your hotel in Hurghada for a relaxing overnight stay

**Day # 8: Hurghada fly back home
**Enjoy your Breakfast at the hotel with free time at your own and then you will be transferred to Hurghada airport for your flight back home.
