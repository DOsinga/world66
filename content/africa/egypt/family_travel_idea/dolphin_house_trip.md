**Tour Itinerary:**

Our **trip** to **Dolphin House** will start with pick up service from
your hotel in **Marsa Alam**, where our REP
will meet you with aboard reflecting your name, then you will be transferred by
air conditioned bus to the port of **Marsa Alam**,where you
board a boat for the 45-minute **trip** to **Dolphin House**. At the site, we transfer to zodiac
boats and it's off to visit the **dolphins**.

After **snorkeling** for about 45 minutes
you return to the boat for lunch and cool drinks before relaxing on board. You
can then snorkel again in the area for a while before we head back to **Marsa Alam** port and transfer you to your hotel in **Marsa Alam**.
