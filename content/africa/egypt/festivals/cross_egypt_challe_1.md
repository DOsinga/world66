Cross Egypt Challenge is a series of endurance cross-country rallies conducted
throughout the most challenging roads and tracks in Egypt. This
breathtaking challenge is fascinating for spectators and riders alike.
Through combining the best of adventure travel and extreme sports.

Adventure riders from the four corners of the globe came to Egypt to
take part in Cross Egypt Challenge in a bid to
promote tourism and give the world a glimpse of Egyptian hospitality.
