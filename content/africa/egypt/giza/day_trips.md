---
title: "Day Trips"
type: section
---

The pyramid fields of **Saqqara** and **Dahshur** lie 30 and 40 km south and are the essential day trip from Giza. Saqqara holds the **Step Pyramid of Djoser**, the oldest monumental stone structure on earth (c. 2670 BC), and the astonishing reliefs of the **Mastaba of Mereruka**. Dahshur has the **Bent Pyramid** and the **Red Pyramid**, the first true smooth-sided pyramid ever built. A taxi for the day from Cairo or Giza, with time for both sites, is the sensible way to see them.

Further afield, **Alexandria** is 220 km north and doable as a long day trip by train — the Library of Alexandria, the Roman catacombs and a seafood lunch on the corniche — though an overnight is better.
