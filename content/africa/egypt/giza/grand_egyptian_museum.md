The largest archaeological museum in the world, built specifically to house the Egyptian collection previously split between the old Egyptian Museum in Tahrir Square and dozens of other sites. Its centrepiece is the complete treasure of Tutankhamun — finally shown together in one place — and the giant statue of Ramses II in the entrance hall. A kilometre and a half from the pyramids; plan a full day for the two sites combined.
