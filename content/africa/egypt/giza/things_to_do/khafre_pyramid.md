title: "Pyramid of Khafre"
type: poi
category: "Sight"
latitude: 29.9761

---

The second pyramid, built by Khufu's son Khafre, looks taller than his father's because it sits on higher ground and still wears a cap of its original polished casing stones at the top. The only pyramid at Giza to retain any of that smooth outer skin, which once covered all three. Its valley temple at the foot of the plateau is worth the short walk over.
