title: "Pyramid of Menkaure"

The smallest of the three Great Pyramids — just 65 metres high, less than half Khufu's — built around 2510 BC for pharaoh Menkaure. Three even smaller "Queens' Pyramids" stand alongside it. The interior is open to visitors and, because most tourists stop at Khufu, you will often have it almost to yourself.
