A family-run Irish bar in the heart of Sekalla, PJ's keeps things unpretentious: cheap drinks, a big screen for football and sport, pub quizzes, and karaoke nights. It fills up with a mix of expats and tourists looking for a relaxed evening. The food is decent pub grub. If you need a break from Egyptian cuisine and fancy a pint, this does the job.
