The world's only Hed Kandi Beach Bar opened in 2007 on Papas Beach and built a loyal following among house and electronic music fans. Weekly themed nights run the gamut from Serve Chilled to Disco Kandi, and monthly special events bring in UK-based Hed Kandi residents alongside live saxophone from resident Craig Hume. The crowd is clubber-friendly; arrive after midnight for the best atmosphere.
