A large open-air shopping and entertainment complex on the waterfront, with international brands (Nike, Adidas, Levi's, Timberland) alongside a souk-style market section selling spices, papyrus, cotton goods, and souvenirs. The marina setting makes it a pleasant place to wander in the evening. Expect firm haggling in the market sections; fixed-price stores are clearly marked.
