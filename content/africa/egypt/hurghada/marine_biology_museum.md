title: Marine Biology Museum
type: poi

A small but worthwhile museum in the northern part of town, run as part of a marine biology research station. The highlight is the walk-through aquarium showcasing Red Sea species — coral formations, tropical fish, sea turtles, and sharks — that non-divers rarely get to see up close. Opens daily 8am–8pm; admission is modest.
