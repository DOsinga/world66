The world's only beachside Ministry of Sound venue, this 2,500-capacity club sits right on the sand near Sakkala with a raised VIP area, a circular dance floor, and a boat-shaped bar looking out to sea. International guest DJs — Judge Jules, Brandon Block, Marco V — rotate through regularly, covering house, techno, and R&B. If you're in Hurghada and want a serious night out, this is the place.
