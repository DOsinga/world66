Hurghada has a great Nightlife,

People can go enjoy their night out at the world reknown club "Ministry Of Sound" which has worldwide dj's visiting all year long or go to a very fun and exiting pub/bar/club known as Papas Bar. Other great clubs should be visited such as Calypso or Ambassador.
