The world's first and only **Hed Kandi Beach Bar**, opened in March 2007 to incredible acclaim...

With award-winning decor, chic cuisine and tons of fabulous fun to be had, this is certainly a must for any clubber visiting [Hurghada](/africa/egypt/hurghada).

The weekly programme of themes runs along the lines of the successful compilation albums from Serve Chilled, to Disco Kandi and Twisted Disco...

With two monthly-ish 'special events' Hed Kandi LIVE (usually on the second Saturday of the month) flies in a UK Hed Kandi resident and then Full Moon Party with Hed Kandi Egypt residents - both events are accompanied by Craig Hume on LIVE saxophone!
