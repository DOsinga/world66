Two days trip to **Luxor** from **port Safaga **to visit famous sightseeing of West and East banks of Nile River with private vehicle and private guide in** Luxor**.

Tour Itinerary:
**Day 1: Visit Karnak and Luxor temples (East Bank)**
Our **Luxor tour** will start with pick up service from your ship terminal in **Safaga port**, where our REP will meet you with aboard reflecting your name, then from** Safaga port** you will be transferred to **Luxor** by air-conditioned vehicle with your private Egyptologist guide. The way to** Luxor** takes 3,5 hours driving with majestic view of mountains of the western desert. In arrival to **Luxor** you will visit** Karnak temple**, dedicated to the God Amon-Ra, his wife Mut and their son Khonsu. In **Karnak temple** each pharaoh tried to perpetuate his name. One of the famous area in **Karnak temple** - Hypostyle hall with 134 massive columns with over three meters in diameter. Next **sightseeing** of your **Luxor tour** will be** Luxor temple** built by Amenhotep III with grandeur intention and have great number of pillars. **Luxor temple** was connected by 3 kilometers paved alley of sphinxes with **Karnak Temple**. Then you will be transferred to your hotel in** Luxor** for rest and overnight stay. You can attend the light and sound show in **Karnak temple**, as optional** tour**.

**Day 2: Visit Valley of the Kings, Hatshepsut temple (West Bank)**
After your breakfast at the hotel in **Luxor** you will continue second day of your** Luxor tour** with your Egyptologist guide to West Bank of the Nile River. You will start by visiting the **Colossi of Memnon**, two colossal statues, was stayed guard at entrance of Amenhotep III temple in ancient times, and only remaining nowadays. Name '**Colossi of Memnon**' means 'Ruler of the Dawn' and was probably given, because of 'singing' at dawn one of the statue. Next **sightseeing** of your visits in **Luxor** will be **Valley of the Kings **- burial place of pharaohs, hidden in the rocks, decorated with scences and funerary rituals of the period for afterlife of pharaohs. **Valley of Kings** is famous for the child king Tutankhamen tomb and its treasures, discovered in 1922 by Howard Carter. Proceed your** tour** in **Luxor** to **Hatshepsut temple** at El Deir Al Bahari, dedicated to the **queen Hatshepsut** - famous female pharaoh. **Hatshepsut temple** with three layered terraces connected by long ramps which were once surrounded by gardens. Inside **Hatshepsut temple** you can see text and picture of an expedition to exotic country calls Land of Punt. Free time in **Luxor**, and then you return address: Hurghada, Egypt
