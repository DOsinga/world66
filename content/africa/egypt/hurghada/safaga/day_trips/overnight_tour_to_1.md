Overnight **tour** from **Safaga port** to biggest cities of Egypt -** Cairo**, capital of Egypt with famous in the world Giza Pyramids and other ancient places, and **Alexandria**, city of Alexander Macedonean famous by Alexandrian library and Pharos lighthouse.

Tour Itinerary:
**Day 1: Transfer to Cairo, Cairo sightseeing **
Our **Cairo** and **Alexandria tours** will start with pick up service from your ship terminal in **Safaga port**, where our REP will meet you with aboard reflecting your name, then from **Safaga port** you will be transferred by air-conditioned vehicle to Hurghada airport for 1 hour flight to **Cairo**.
