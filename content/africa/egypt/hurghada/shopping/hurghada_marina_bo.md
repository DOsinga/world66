The new Hurghada Marina Boulevard boasts anything up 100 fresh and exciting retail outlets for the city, big brand names like Nike, Adidas, Timberland, Levi's and Dockers are all there, a couple of designer glasses shops show Gucci, Armani and Oakley's in their windows...

Alongside the more high-end shops, you can also find an authentic Souk Marina selling all the goods you can find in the high street, just without so many bizarre boys and/or taxis harassing you! This is the place to head for bargains!
