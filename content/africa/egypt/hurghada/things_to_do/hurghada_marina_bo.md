The newly opened Hurghada Marina Boulevard boasts over 80 shops (including Nike, Adidas, Timberland, Levi's and Dockers), 15 restaurants (including Indian, Thai, Sushi, Mediterranean and even Fish & Chips!), and 8 bars...

The picturesque surroundings are both quiet, tranquil whilst oozing a certain air of excitement around the place. Their team of 10 European professional entertainers keep people amused and amazed with juggling, fire-shows, stilt walking, breakdancing, and the on-site ex BMX World Champion performing some jaw-dropping tricks!

Live music every Friday witnesses talent flying in from abroad or popping down from Cairo to wow the crowds gathering around the music stages...
