Itinerary:-
Itinerary A :Luxor - Aswan 4 Nights Every Saturday
Day 1, Saturday
Embarkation luxury Dahabiya in Luxor.Lunch will be served on board, then after you will visit Luxor and Karnak Temples. Dinner and overnight on Dahabiya board on the
West bank of Nile River.

Day 2, Sunday
After breakfast you will visit sights of the West Bank - Valley of the Kings, Temple of Hatshepsut, Colossi of Memnon. After lunch on board - start sailing to Esna. Dinner will be during sailing. Overnight on board Dahabiya beside an Island.

Day 3, Monday
Breakfast while crossing Esna lock. Sailing to Edfu. After lunch on board visiting Edfu Temple. Sailing to Al Ramady. Dinner will be on island in Nile River. Overnight beside Al Ramady Village.

Day 4, Tuesday
Early morning sail to Kom Ombo. Visit Temple of Kom Ombo (noon time). Start sail to Aswan. Lunch will be on board while sailing. Overnight in Aswan.

Day 5, Wednesday
Breakfast on board. Disembarkation after breakfast in Aswan.

Itinerary B :Aswan - Luxor 3 Nights Every Wedneday
Day 1, Wednesday
Embarkation luxury Dahabiya in Aswan. Lunch will be on board. Then you will have free time at Aswan market. Overnight in Aswan on the West bank of Nile River.

Day 2, Thursday
Breafast on board and after visit Temple of Philae and High Dam. Lunch will be on board, then sailing to Kom Ombo. In arrival visit Kom Ombo Temple and sail to Al Mansouriya Village. Dinner on board and overnight beside Al Mansouriya.

Day 3, Friday
Early morning sail to Edfu while breakfast. Visit Edfu Temple, lunch will be while sailing to Esna and crossing Esna lock. Sail to Luxor, dinner and overnight in Luxor.

Day 4, Saturday
Breakfast on board. Disembarkation after breakfast in Luxor.
