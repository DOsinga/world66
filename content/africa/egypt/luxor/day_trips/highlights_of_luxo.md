Luxor **'World's Greatest Open Air Museum'** as it contains a major amount of Monuments of the world which remain till today.

This
City is located 680 km south of Cairo on the East Bank of the River
Nile. The ancient name of Luxor is Ta-Pe/Ta-Opet,which means The Holy
Land. This name becomes Thebes during the Greek reign in Egypt then the
Arabs come and call it Luxor which is an Arabic word means palaces in
English.

The Importance of Luxor comes from its position as it is
the Capital of Ancient Egypt for years, it's the main cult center of
the main God of Egypt, and in it there is the largest temple in Egypt
which is Karnak Temple.
