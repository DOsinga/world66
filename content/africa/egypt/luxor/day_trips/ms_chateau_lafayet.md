MS Iberotel Chateau Lafayette Nile Cruise Itinerary:-
Cruise Itinerary A
MS Iberotel Chateau Lafayette the deluxe five-star cruise ship operating at Nile River, measures approximately 75 meters in length and providing impeccable service, in comfort and style onboard cruise. This deluxe Nile cruise ship creates the perfect environment to which you can retreat after shore excursions. The main and upper deck cabins have large panoramic windows with view of Nile River.
MS Iberotel Chateau Lafayette cruise offers 33 cabins and 22 suites. The main restaurant accommodates all passengers in one seating and offers a mix of local and international buffet style cuisine. Accommodation is on a full-board basis with daily tea time service.
MS Iberotel Chateau Lafayette facilities:
24 hours reception, Large sun deck with swimming pool and pool bar, Gift shop, Beauty salon, Meeting space, International telephone and fax service, Fitness Studio, sauna, Jacuzzi and steam bath, Laundry service.
MS Iberotel Chateau Lafayette cabins:
All cabins are fully air-conditioned (individually controlled) and furnished with marine satellite TV and in-house video channels, mini bar, safety box, music system and private W/C with shower and hairdryer.
Upper deck 2: 14 suites
Upper deck 1: 15 double cabins and 2 suites
Main deck: 18 double cabins
Lower deck: 6 suite
