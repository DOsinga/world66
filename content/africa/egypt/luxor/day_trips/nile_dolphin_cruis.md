MS Nile Dolphin Nile Cruise Itinerary:-
MS Nile Dolphin is a brand new 5-star deluxe floating hotel cruising on the Nile commenced in February 2007 and provides luxurious accommodation. Its interieur with all the comfort and technical facilities of a modern hotel, simplified to satisfy contemporary taste.
MS Nile Dolphin has 63 deluxe double cabins and 2 suites, cabins decorated in Empire style and have mahogany furniture with brass art decorations.
Facilities MS Nile Dolphin:
Restaurant, panorama lounge, bars, ‘silent area’ adjacent to lounge with panorama terrace, sun deck with pool, Jacuzzi and bar, business center with internet access, boutique, gift shop.
Cabins MS Nile Dolphin:
All cabins have large, panorama windows that can be opened — so you can enjoy the view of the Nile. Each cabin has mini bar, satellite TV, a music and video system, mini-safe. All cabins have en-suite, pre-fabricated bathrooms with marble floors and wall decorations, full glass shower cabins with jet panels, toiletries, hairdryer, and loudspeaker so you can watch TV or listen to music even when showering. Suites have their own terrace. All cabins are airconditioned with individual controls.
