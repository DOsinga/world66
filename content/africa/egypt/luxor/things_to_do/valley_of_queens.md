Burial site for the wives and children of the New Kingdom pharaohs. The tomb of Nefertari — wife of Ramses II — is considered one of the finest in all of Egypt, its colors still luminous after 3,000 years. Access to Nefertari's tomb requires a separate, pricey ticket, but it is worth every pound.
