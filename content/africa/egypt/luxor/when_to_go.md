---
title: "When to Go"
type: section
---

October through April is the best time to visit — temperatures are manageable, typically 15–30°C, and you can actually walk around the temples without suffering. November to February is peak season, which means more tourists but also cooler mornings that make early starts rewarding.

Summer (June–August) is brutal, with temperatures regularly above 40°C. It's survivable if you stick to early mornings and air-conditioned museums, but there's no good reason to choose it unless the cheaper prices are essential. Avoid April if you can — sandstorms are a real risk.
