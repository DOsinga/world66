---
title: "Eating Out"
type: section
---

The lively **Gam'a** district around Mansoura University has the city's biggest concentration of restaurants and cafes — student prices, English sometimes spoken, a mix of Egyptian standards and cheap international food. For classic Delta fare, try the seafood places along the Nile corniche; grilled mullet and grey mullet roe (**fesikh**) are local specialities.
