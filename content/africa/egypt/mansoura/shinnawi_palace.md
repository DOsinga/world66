title: Shinnawi Palace
type: poi

A flamboyant 1928 mansion designed by an Italian architect for a wealthy Egyptian cotton merchant — marble floors, frescoed ceilings, mirrored halls, the works. Said to be the finest building of its style outside Italy. Ask at the door about visiting hours.
