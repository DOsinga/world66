**Enjoy Mansoura&rsquo;s Beaches Year Round
**

If you are planning a trip to **Mansoura, when to go** is an issue to consider before reserving the trip. The typical desert climate is perfect for taking advantage of the city's beaches at any time of the year. However, the Nile River affects the humidity levels, and sometimes the combination of hot weather and high humidity causes some discomfort. Desert storms are also frequent, especially in the winter.

#### **Summer**

The summer lasts from May to October and is characterized by hot weather and high temperatures. The hottest months are June and May, with the temperatures reaching peak levels of nearly 40 degrees Celsius or 104 degrees Fahrenheit. At night, temperatures fall about 20 degrees Celsius, but they are still high enough for the weather to be warm and suitable for having a night out or dining at a nice restaurant. Keep in mind that no rain falls in the summer, which increases the feeling of heat. Be careful not to overdo the sunbathing as the sun is strong.

#### **Winter**

The winter lasts from November to April. It is mild. The coolest months are December and January, with the temperatures rarely reaching a minimum of 8 degrees Celsius or 46 degrees Fahrenheit. Rains are possible in the winter, but the amount is insignificant per month. April and March can be very raw, since desert storms often occur.
