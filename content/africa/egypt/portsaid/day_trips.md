Port Said's position at the northern end of the Suez Canal makes it a natural base for day trips into the Egyptian heartland. [Cairo](/africa/egypt/cairo) and the Pyramids of Giza are about two and a half hours away by road — the most popular excursion, especially for cruise ship passengers with a day in port. [Alexandria](/africa/egypt/alexandria) is a similar distance to the west along the Mediterranean coast.
