Travel Guide

Safaga is situated on the coast of the Red Sea. It is a working port, serving both the tourist and trade industries. Located on the western flank of the Red Sea, Safaga Port is also a port for ferries to and from Saudi Arabia.

Safaga Port has undergone major refurbishment and as a tourist destination in and of itself, cruise passengers sometimes choose to stay in Safaga, especially if they really enjoy diving or windsurfing. There are a range of hotels to serve tourists.
