Being a luxury resort city, Sharm el Sheikh has developed a great reputation for fantastic, one-of-a-kind bars and cafes. Most places will wrap you in over-the-top extravagance, similar to that which Las Vegas is known for. Colored lights, excellent music and other stylistic elements directly related to the theme of the bar will make you feel like you are in another world. From cozy traditional British pubs, founded by the ex-pats of Britain in Sharm el Sheikh, to large chic cafes with cool colors and ambient lights, you will find a bar or cafe to fit your style.

**Prices and Hours**

In Sharm el Sheikh, unlike most areas, the alcohol is actually more expensive than the food. Being in Egypt, however, the overall price of food and drinks is quite inexpensive compared to the prices in other countries. A bottle of beer at a bar will cost you about 2.5 euros and a cocktail will cost around 3 euros. A single bottle of wine can cost you 10 euros or more. Prices of alcohol in a supermarket are much lower.

Most cafes during the day turn into bars at night, while most daytime bars change into clubs for the evening hours.
