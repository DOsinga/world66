The Middle East’s first Ice Bar!

Truly the coolest place to have a drink. The walls, Bar, the seats the tables and even the glasses are made from Ice. Entry price includes your choice or drink – Vodka shooters the way they were meant to be served, or your choice from our full bar range.

For those that need it, cold weather clothing is included, as entering a room running at -5 degree or below, when its 35 degree outside, can shock even Siberian residents.
