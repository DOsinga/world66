Sharm el-Sheikh was originally established as a fishing village and
has now metamorphosed into a bustling beach resort that is soaring in
popularity. The ** Sharm el-Sheikh Beaches **guide gives visitors information on the fantastic beach hotspots that adorn the land of this charming sea-side retreat.

**Orientation**

Sharm el-Shiekh boasts of a super sunny weather and perfect scuba
diving waters that are frequented by diving aficionados from all over
the world. The beaches of the region are pulsating tourist hubs that
are packed with the young party brigade and families alike. The
breathtakingly beautiful landscape overlooks the Strait of Tiran and is
bordered by expansive dessert plains and auburn mountains. The
sun-kissed beaches of the region have besotted several revelers, who
throng here to watch the spectacular sunset in the backdrop of the
tangerine sky. The beaches of the region also have a fascinating range
of coral reefs and a rich marine ecological heritage. Apart from scuba
diving, there are a range of other water sports activities like
windsurfing, canoeing and parasailing. The waters here remain crystal
clear and refreshingly warm all around the year. Visitors can also go
boating on the pristine waters of the region.

**The Red Sea Beach Resort**

The Red Sea Beach Resort is a terrific destination for a string of
sporting activities like scuba diving, snorkeling and windsurfing apart
from offering a range of options for family picnics. The resort is
touted to have the best diving facilities in the world. Red Sea Beach
Resort is flanked by a lovely dessert landscape and features some of
the best hotels and eateries in town.

**Namma Bay**

Namma Bay is a beautiful stretch
of coastal beauty and one of the most throbbing nightspots of the
region for some heavy-duty boisterous revelry. The promenade features
several glitzy restaurants and cafes for an action-packed night out.
There are several swanky beach resorts like the Hilton Sharks Bay
Resort that features a one of the most famous diving clubs in the
region-The Sinai Dive Club.

**Terrazzina Beach **

Terrazzina Beach is a perfect getaway for a
tranquil holiday. Spread out over the warm sunny sands of the beach and
revel under the tender glow of the sun for an unruffled vacation. The
beach has soothing music being played in the background and
occasionally also features a few activities like parasailing.

Sharm el-Shiekh has bountiful natural coastal elegance and clear
blue waters that are superb for diving and other sports activities.
