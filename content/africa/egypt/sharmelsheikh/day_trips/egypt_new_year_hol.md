Christmas Holiday Tours to Egypt & Jordan Itinerary:-
Day 1: Arrival Cairo

Our Sunray Tours representative will meet you at Cairo airport, after you arrived and assist you with the arrival matters. He will bring you in a modern and air conditioned van to the hotel in which you will stay in. You will spend the first night of your Egypt Christmas Holiday in Cairo.

Day 2: Pyramids of Giza & Egyptian Museum

After you enjoyed your breakfast at the hotel you will start the second day of your Egypt Travel Package with a visit at the great Pyramids of Giza. This Pyramids are big tombs of the Pharaohs Cheops, Chaperon and Mykerinos and they constructed it for their mummified bodies in the afterlife. Furthermore you will explore the giant Sphinx of Giza, which is built from a natural. It has the head of a human and the body of a lion and should show the strength of the Pharaohs. Included in this tour is the entrance ticket to the Valley Temple, which you also will explore. Your lunch will be taken at a local restaurant with view to the Pyramids. The Classic Tour Package will continue with the Egyptian Museum, where you will find thousand of statues, jewels and ancient treasures. The date of some of these things goes back to 5000 years. It will show you also the treasure of King Tut-Ankh-Amun, which will be an unforgettable adventure. The last stop for this day will be the Khan el Khalili Market, which is the oldest one in Middle East and you will feel the charm of it. Than we will transfer you to the train station and you will sleep and take your dinner in the train to Aswan.

Day 3: Aswan Sightseeing Tour

After your breakfast you will be transferred by our representative to the 5 star Nile cruise where you will take your lunch. Then start your Egypt Holiday Package at this day with the Aswan Sightseeing Tour. You will discover the High Dam, which is very important for the Egyptian culture and economy and the Philae Temple. End your tour with the unfinished Obelisk, which is very famous and take later your dinner on board. Overnight Aswan.
