**Tour itinerary:**

**Day 1: Visit Karnak and Luxor temples (East
Bank)****

**Our **Luxor tour** will start with pick
up service from your hotel in **Sharm El Sheikh**, where
our REP will meet you with aboard reflecting your name, then you will be
transferred by air-conditioned vehicle to **Sharm El
Sheikh** airport for 1 hour flight to **Luxor**. In **Luxor** you will be
accompanied by private Egyptologist guide and you will start your **Luxor tour** from visit **Karnak temple**, dedicated to the God Amon-Ra, his
wife Mut and their son Khonsu. In **Karnak temple** each
pharaoh tried to perpetuate his name. One of the famous area in **Karnak temple** - Hypostyle hall with 134 massive
columns with over three meters in diameter. Next **sightseeing** of your **Luxor
tour** will be **Luxor temple** built by
Amenhotep III with grandeur intention and have great number of pillars. It was
connected by 3 kilometers paved alley of sphinxes with **Karnak Temple**. Then you will be transferred to your
hotel in **Luxor** for rest and **overnight** stay. You can attend the light and sound
show in **Karnak temple**, as optional **tour**.

**Day 2: Visit Valley of the Kings, Hatshepsut
temple (West Bank)**

After your breakfast at the hotel in **Luxor** you will continue
second day of your **Luxor tour** with your
Egyptologist guide to West Bank of the Nile River. You will start **Luxor tour** by visiting the Colossi of Memnon, two
colossal statues, was stayed guard at entrance of Amenhotep III temple in
ancient times, and only remaining nowadays. Name means 'Ruler of the Dawn' and
was probably given, because of 'singing' at dawn one of the statue. Next **sightseeing** of your visit in **Luxor **will be **Valley
of the Kings** - burial place of pharaohs, hidden in the rocks,
decorated with scences and funerary rituals of the period for afterlife of
pharaohs. **Valley of Kings** is famous for
the child king Tutankhamen tomb and its treasures, discovered in 1922 by Howard
Carter. Proceed your **Luxor tour** to **Hatshepsut temple** at El Deir Al Bahari, dedicated
to the queen **Hatshepsut** - famous
female pharaoh, **Hatshepsut temple** with
three layered terraces connected by long ramps which were once surrounded by
gardens. Inside **Hatshepsut temple** you can
see text and picture of an expedition to exotic country calls Land of Punt.
Free time in **Luxor**, and then you will
be driven to **Luxor** airport to fly back
to **Sharm El Sheikh** with following
transfer to your hotel in **Sharm El Sheikh**.
