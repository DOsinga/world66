We will pick you from your hotel in Sharm El Sheikh at 10:30 p.m., drive towards **St. Catherine** for about 3 hours, arrive to St. Catherine Monastery start to climb **Moses Mountain**
where Moses received the Ten Commandments over 3000 years ago. After 3
hours climbing we will reach the top of 2295 meters, where you will
experience the fantastic sunrise, with the first hour of Sunlight, we
will descent towards **St.Catherine Monastery** for **Breakfast**
and then a journey on to St. Catherine Monastery and it’s amazing
collection of icons, the monastery’s gardens, the Chapel of the Burning
Bush. After this breathtaking trip, you will ride the bus back to the
Sharm El Sheikh by lunchtime.
