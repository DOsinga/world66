---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

The Sinai combines desert trekking, world-class diving, and ancient pilgrimage sites in a compact area. The main draws are in the south: Ras Mohammed for reef diving, Mount Sinai for the dawn climb, and St Katherine's Monastery for its extraordinary history. Inland, the Coloured Canyon and the high desert around St Katherine offer excellent hiking through some of Egypt's most dramatic scenery.
