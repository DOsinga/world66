Less than 50 km from teh capital [Malabo](/africa/equatorialguinea/malabo), Luba is the second largest town of Bioko. It's a good base for visiting some of the nicest beaches in the country, including the beautiful Arena Blanca, as well as several small fishing villages and two hiking trails.
