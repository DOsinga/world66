**Assab** is a port city in the far south of [Eritrea](/africa/eritrea), close to the border with [Djibouti](/africa/djibouti). It sits on the Red Sea coast in a hot, arid stretch of land and is one of the most remote cities in the country.

The port was once a major trade gateway for landlocked [Ethiopia](/africa/ethiopia), but traffic dropped sharply after the Eritrean-Ethiopian war. The city feels quiet and isolated compared to [Asmara](/africa/eritrea/asmara), with a dry, dusty character and intense heat for most of the year. There is little in the way of tourism infrastructure, but the setting on the Red Sea has its own stark appeal.
