---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

Keren is about 90 km northwest of [Asmara](/africa/eritrea/asmara) along a good asphalt road. Buses run regularly between the two cities and take around two hours. Taxis and shared minibuses are also an option for the journey.

There is no rail service to Keren — the old Italian-era railway never extended this far.
