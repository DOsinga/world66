A rare hybrid of art gallery and Italian restaurant in the Bole area, Makush serves homemade pasta, thin-crust pizza, and espresso in a space hung with work by Ethiopian painters and sculptors. The combination of good food and rotating art exhibitions makes it a reliable favourite among expats and travellers. The lasagne is particularly good. Prices are reasonable by international standards.
