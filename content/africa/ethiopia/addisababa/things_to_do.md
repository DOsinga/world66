---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Addis Ababa packs in a surprising amount for a city that most travellers treat as a transit stop. The museums are genuinely world-class, the Mercato is an experience in itself, and the churches offer a window into one of the oldest Christian traditions on earth. Most of the key sights are spread across the city, so plan on using taxis or the light rail to get between them.
