The largest Ethiopian Orthodox cathedral in the city, Holy Trinity was built after the liberation from Italian occupation in 1941. Emperor Haile Selassie and Empress Menen are buried here. The stained glass windows by Afewerk Tekle, one of Ethiopia's most celebrated artists, are stunning. The grounds also contain the graves of resistance fighters and a small museum.
