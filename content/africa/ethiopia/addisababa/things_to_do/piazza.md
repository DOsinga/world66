A legacy of the Italian occupation of the 1930s, the Piazza district has a distinctly Mediterranean feel unusual for an African capital. The area is full of Italian restaurants, cafes, tailors, and small shops. It is worth wandering here for the atmosphere alone, and the pizza genuinely is some of the best on the continent.
