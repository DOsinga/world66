The Gambia is an excellent place to go on a *fishing*, *birding *or *beach* trip.

It's relative proximity to Europe makes it ideal for a getaway in the **sun** and it is very rich in bird and aquatic life.

Unfortunately many of the larger game species have long since
dissapeared but you will still find crocodile and hippos in the rivers
and monkeys in the trees.

And the beaches are excellent although there is a continuing problem
with sea erosion which is being addressed by the Gambian government by
investment in preventative measures.
