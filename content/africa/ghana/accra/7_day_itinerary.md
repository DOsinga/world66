Shilpa Mudiganti

Accra is Ghana’s sea side capital with nearly two million residents.
Accra has plenty to offer visitors and will provide you with a
memorable experience. It is a mixture of big city business and laidback
African ease. The people are warm and friendly. For a first timer, it
is preferable to go on one of the package tours which are
comprehensive, informative and value for money. You can choose from the
different types of tours being offered from wildlife safaris, to nature
trails, to the slave route.

**Day 1: Culture and Shopping

**

The traditional welcome is followed by an orientation of the
tour. Post lunch, the sightseeing would commence with a drive to Accra.
The 128 year old city is a blend of colonial and modern architecture.
Visit the W.E.B. Dubois Centre for Pan African Culture, Independence /
Black Star Square and Kwame Nkrumah Memorial Park (mausoleum of Ghana’s
first president). At the Arts Centre, bargain and shop at the open air
market for artifacts.

**Day 2: Kakum National Park

**

Visit the Kakum National Park. Kakum is home to Africa’s only aerial walkway through the tree canopy.

**Day 3: Owabi Wildlife Sanctuary

**

The Owabi Wildlife Sanctuary is home to many types of
butterflies, over 140 species of birds, monkeys, and more reclusive
bushpig, bushbuck, and antelope. Stay overnight at Kumasi.

**Day 4: Kintampo Falls**

The 80ft Kintampo falls are
breathtaking with a small swimming area below. Boabeng Fiema is where
the sacred monkeys live in harmony with the community. Stay overnight
at Kumasi.

**Day 5: Bobiri Forest Reserve**

The Bobiri Forest Reserve
has over 400 types of butterflies, and white nosed, green and black n
white colobus monkeys. After lunch at Aburi, visit the Botanical
gardens that overlook the coastal plain at a height of 1200 to 1500ft
above sea level.

**Day 6: Sacred Monkeys**

At the Shai Hills forest reserve,
you will get to see the most commonly found olive baboons and 170
species of birds, including the grey hornbill and Senegal parrot.
Continue to Tafi Atome Monkey Sanctuary, home to sacred monkeys.
Complete the exciting trip with visit to Wli (Agumatsa) Falls and then
finally return to Accra.

**Day 7: Kick Back and Relax**

Enjoy the last day of the
trip at leisure. Indulge in some shopping, or soaking in the warmth of
the hospitality or just reveling in the laid back comfort of the place
before the holiday ends!
