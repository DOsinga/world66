Travel Guide

If you will be traveling to Accra, the 4th Edition Brandt
Travel Guide (ISBN: 1841622052) is your ultimate companion. This is by
far the best travel guide to this area on the market, and you will be
happy to have it with you, especially if you will be doing any
additional off the beaten path traveling while in Ghana.

This guide to Ghana is exceptionally well written and very easy to
follow. Not only does it provide you with places to see and where to
lodge, it includes countless amounts of practical information such as cultural taboos and general information about how to
get around and navigate through Ghana.

Using this guidebook makes traveling through a country as vast and
diverse as Ghana all the more enjoyable. As one of the only guidebooks
solely focusing on Ghana and not just West Africa, this book is a great
resource. Because of inflation, rapid price fluctuations and a quickly
developing economy, many of the prices, restaurants, nightspots and
hotels change frequently, and may not be current even with the updated
version just completed. Though this book provides many tools for your
upcoming trip to Accra, it would be recommended to cross reference
important information before making solid travel plans and keeping your
budget flexible.
