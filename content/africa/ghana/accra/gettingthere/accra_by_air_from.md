From most places in the world it is easyest to get to Ghana by air.
Some companies who fly on Accra are: KLM, Lufthansa, Alitalia, British Airways, South-african airlines, Egypt air, Afriquiya & Apstar (the replacement of Ghana Airways).

The airport where you'll land in Accra is Kotoka International. It is not yet that big, but still expanding, and you'll find solutions to all your basic needs. At the airport side you'll see restaurants, drinking spots, a callcenter/internetcafé, taxi's,... and the offices of most airlines where you might wanna confirm your flight back.
