The expat-and-elite eating-and-drinking strip, centred on Cantonments Road (still universally known as Oxford Street). Restaurants of every cuisine, bars, late-night chop bars, supermarkets, and a steady throng of street vendors fill the pavements. It's not where you come for the deep Accra experience, but it's where most travellers end up at some point, and after a sweaty day it's a relief.
