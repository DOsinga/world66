---
title: "Shopping"
type: section
---

Accra shopping divides cleanly between the markets, which are essential and overwhelming, and the air-conditioned malls, which are mostly useful for stocking up on familiar brands. For souvenirs head for the Arts Centre near Independence Square; for fabrics, beads, and the full sensory market experience, Makola is unmissable. Bargain everywhere except in supermarkets and at fixed-price stalls.
