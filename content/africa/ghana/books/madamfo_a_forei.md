Written by a British teacher with eleven years' experience in Ghana, this is a valuable book for those who want to appreciate Ghana and really get to know the people, not those who just want to get drunk on the beach.
It deals with how and why to learn the Twi language, the country's 'lingua franca', and also deals with the culture, food, music, proverbs, etc.
The author lives in Ghana and teaches very enjoyable Twi lessons to visitors.
