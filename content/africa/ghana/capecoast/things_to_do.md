title: "Things to Do"

---

Cape Coast Castle is the essential visit — a guided tour through the slave dungeons, the governor's quarters, and out through the Door of No Return is devastating and unforgettable. Allow at least 90 minutes. [Elmina](/africa/ghana/elmina) and its castle are just 15 kilometres west and can easily be combined in the same day.

Kakum National Park, 30 minutes north of town, is one of the best-accessible rainforest reserves in West Africa. The canopy walkway — a series of rope bridges strung 40 metres above the forest floor — is the main draw, though the park also protects forest elephants, bongo antelopes, and over 250 bird species. Early morning is best for wildlife.
