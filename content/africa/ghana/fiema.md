In Brong Ahafo Region, in between Sunyani and Kintampo, there's avillage where people and monkey's live happely together. The people there believe the monkey's have the spirit's of there ancestors, so they'll give them food, drinks (yeah, even beer if they'd want it) etc. 

You can get there by taxi or trotro, but getting back will be more difficult. 

Yet it's really interesting visiting Boabeng-Fiema Monkey Resort and get the monkey's posing for the camera and eating out of your hand. (visit is with a guide so you'll not get lost in the woods around there)
