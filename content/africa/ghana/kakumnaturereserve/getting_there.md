---
title: "Getting There in Kakum nature reserve"
type: section
---

There are many trotro's to kakum national park leaving from Cape Coast. But some taxidrivers shall also want to take you there (offcourse you'll pay more if you take a taxi)

Getting back to [Cape coast](/africa/ghana/capecoast) after you visited the parc might be a bigger problem if you want to go by trotro. You'll have to walk to the roadside and wait until a trotro passes wich is going in the right direction and still has enough space to take you and your fellowtravellers. How long it will take you to get back to cape Coast will depend on your luck...
