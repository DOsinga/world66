Kokrobite is the closest beach escape from [Accra](/africa/ghana/accra), about 30 km west of the capital. It is a laid-back fishing village popular with backpackers, weekend visitors, and anyone looking for drumming and dancing on the sand.

The Academy of African Music and Arts (AAMA), founded by the late master drummer Mustapha Tettey Addy, puts on drumming and dance workshops and regular performances. Big Milly's Backyard, right on the beach, has long been the social hub of the village. The beach itself is wide and sandy, though the currents can be strong -- swim with caution.

Kokrobite is easy to reach by tro-tro from Kaneshie station in Accra. It works as a day trip or an overnight stay.
