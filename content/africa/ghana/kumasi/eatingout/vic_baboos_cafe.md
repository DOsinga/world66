Vic baboo's cafe is in the heart of the city of Kumasi .

We see most of the backpackers & tourists meeting there.

It has a freindly & cordial atmosphere with an internet cafe attached.

It has the largest menu seen so far in Ghana which includes continental , chinese & indian meals & snacks not forgetting a large variety of vegetarian dishes .

It's probably the only place in Kumasi that serves a healthy variety of cocktails apart from great milkshakes & lassi. 

The vic baboo's also manage a budget accomodation called Guestline lodge near the STC transport. It's clean , affordable and has all the basic services. 

You can also ask for their other services .
