Also the city has Kiravi Night Club, Fox Trap, Genesis Night Club, Cafe Masserati, Yegoala etc. Kiravi Night Club has won the number night club Ghana awards in Ghana for three consecutive years. Aside night Clubs, the City has about 23 FM stations, and 2 cable TV Stations - Fontomfrom and Crystal tv, Hotels, etc.
