title: "Kejetia Market"
type: poi
category: Sight
latitude: 6.6985

---

One of the largest open-air markets in West Africa, Kejetia is a sprawling maze of stalls selling everything from yams and kente cloth to car parts and herbal medicines. It is chaotic and colourful in the best possible way. Give yourself at least an hour and keep your valuables secure.
