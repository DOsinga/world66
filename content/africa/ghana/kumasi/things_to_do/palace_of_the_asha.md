The old palace of the Asantehene has been turned into a museum packed with historical objects, paintings, and photographs of the Ashanti rulers. A guided tour gives a fascinating insight into the power and traditions of the Ashanti kingdom. The current Asantehene still holds court in the adjacent modern palace, and the Akwasidae ceremony held every six weeks is a spectacular display of traditional authority.
