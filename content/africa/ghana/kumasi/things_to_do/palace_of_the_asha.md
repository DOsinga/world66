The old palace of the Ashanti kings has been converted into a museum and is the main tourist attraction in Kumasi. It is stuffed with historical objects, paintings, and photographs of the Ashanti rulers. A guided tour gives a fascinating insight into the power and traditions of the Ashanti kingdom. The current Asantehene still holds court in the adjacent modern palace — if you time your visit to coincide with the Akwasidae ceremony, held every six weeks, you may see the king in full regalia.
