The best resource for sights, hotels, restaurants, bars, what to do and see

In this rather small village at the boarder with Burkina Faso, you'll find some very intersting tourist sites.

The most famous one is the crocodile pond. If you pay a certain amount of cedi's you can see & touch the crocodile, plus feed it with a chicken.
Right accross the pond is a small museum in witch is shown the way houses were traditionally put up in the north. They also show & sell some old craftworks.
