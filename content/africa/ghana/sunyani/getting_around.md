---
title: "Getting Around"
type: section
---

Sunyani's town centre is compact enough to walk around comfortably. When you need a taxi, you have two options common across Ghana: a "dropping" (shared taxi following a set route, very cheap) or a "chartered" taxi where you hire the vehicle for yourself and tell the driver where to go. Chartered taxis cost more but are still inexpensive by international standards. Agree on the fare before you get in.
