Call it "tah-mah-LAY" not "ta-mal-EY." The latter is a cornmeal-based food product from Mexico. The former is a dry, dusty city in the north of Ghana. It's your likely transportation hub from which you can go to Mole Nature Reserve and the picturesque city of [Larabanga](/africa/ghana/larabanja).
