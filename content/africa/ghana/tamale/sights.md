### [Mole National Park](/africa/ghana/tamale/sights/mole_national_park)

Mole National Park is about 100 km(60 mi.) away from the busy city of Tamale. This park inhabits over 94 different species which include the elephant, lions, antelopes, buffalo, monkeys, leopards, and the list goes on. When you visit, trained guides will guide you through this park on "Foot Safaris".
