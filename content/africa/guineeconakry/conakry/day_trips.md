The most obvious day trip from Conakry is the [Iles de Los](/africa/guineeconakry/ilesdelos), a group of small islands sitting just offshore in the Atlantic. Boats leave from the beach near the old Novotel hotel. The islands offer beaches, calm water, and a welcome escape from the city's congestion.
