title: Conakry Grand Mosque
type: poi
---

One of the largest mosques in West Africa, a dominant presence on the peninsula's skyline. The white facade and twin minarets are impressive from the street, and the surrounding square fills with worshippers on Fridays. Non-Muslim visitors should observe from outside.
