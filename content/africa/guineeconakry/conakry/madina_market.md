title: Madina Market
type: poi

The largest and most chaotic market in Conakry, sprawling across the Madina district well north of the old center. Everything is sold here — fabric, produce, electronics, household goods, live animals. It is intense and crowded, and you should keep a close hold on your belongings. But it is also genuinely alive in a way that sanitized markets rarely are.
