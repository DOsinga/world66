The markets in Labe are quite lively. The main market is located in the
city center, and is the center of daily life in Labe. The main
artisan products are the famous indigo cloths and leather goods.
There are also large sections of foods, from the butchers selling fresh
beef to fruits and vegetables, such mangos(depending on the season),
bananas, oranges, peppers, and many others available. For most goods,
especially non food items bargaining and haggling are expected.
