Find out about **Guinee Conakry** **when to go** for a summer holiday.
See beautiful, sandy beaches, basking in the sun and travel through Guinea
Conakry lush forests. Rich with bright colors, fruit and vegetables ripened in
the sun, and a natural heritage with exceptional charm is what you can expect
from Guinea Conakry. Situated in Africa, discover Guinee Conakry when to go and
see a rich cultural and artistic heritage.

**Snow in Guinee Conakry**

Different heritages and cultures celebrate life with dances, traditions,
artists, and so much more to fascinate you. Visit Guinee Conakry when the
country is filled with snow, covering the beautiful land and blanketing the
forests. Awaiting the snow during this time of the year is the best time to go
visit Guinee Conakry.

**Rainy Season Guinee Conakry**

Look to travel to Guinee Conakry all throughout the year. Rainy seasons in
Guinee Conakry may prevent you from seeing a lot of the outdoors when visiting.
Plan to go around this season from June to November. Travel Guinee Conakry
during the rainy season can see high monsoonal type rains.

**Dry Season Guinee Conakry**

Journey to Guinee Conakry during the dry season (from December to May) for
the best visit possible. Beaches lay out to enjoy during the visit through the
dry season. Enjoy the sun and paths to bike upon throughout the land. See the
natural wonders that Guinee Conakry has to offer with mountains, rivers, lush
forests, and ancient buildings to explore.

**Festival of Nimba**

March is the time you would want to visit Guine Conakry during the festival
of Nimba, also known as the Goddess of plenty. During the harvest season when
sowing and harvesting, a festival is held when Nimba’s mask is paraded
throughout the country. Even though there is an influence of Christianity and
Islam, the people of Baga respect the ritualism and traditions of the sacred
forest.

**Road Travel Times and Markets**

Biking the roads of Guinee Conakry is recommended. The roads can be jerky if
you are planning to travel by vehicle. Unless you are adventurous and don't mind
bumping along, car rentals are available. Rent a Peugeot 504 with 11 passengers
(not counting children!) in the area. Visit the village markets especially the
one at Koundara. Fruits and vegetables heighten the visit by tasting what Guinee
Conakry has to offer.

Hiking up to Mount Nimba is a day's journey. Tour guides are available to
help you. Hiking in the dry season is recommended. Seeing the rolling green
hills and flat lands of Guinee Conakry is when to go during this season.
