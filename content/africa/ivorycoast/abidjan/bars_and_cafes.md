---
title: "Bars and Cafes"
type: section
---

Abidjan has the most active nightlife in West Africa. Zone 4 and Treichville are the main entertainment districts. The Butterfly Lounge near Marseille Boulevard in Zone 4 has live jazz in its garden on Thursday nights. Jamaica City, also in Zone 4, is the place for live reggae. Jaguar Bar on Boulevard Delafosse in Treichville has live music and happy hours. The Bar des Sports is popular with French expats.
