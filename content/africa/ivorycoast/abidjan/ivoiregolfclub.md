The Ivoire Golf Club sits on the grounds of the landmark Hôtel Ivoire in Cocody, one of Abidjan's upscale districts. The 27-hole course is one of the few international-standard layouts in West Africa, stretching across green, hilly terrain with views over the Ébrié Lagoon. It's a popular stop for visiting golfers and business travellers staying at the hotel. Green fees are reasonable by international standards, and club hire is available on site.
