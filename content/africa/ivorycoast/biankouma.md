About 40 km north of [Man](/africa/ivorycoast/man) on the road to Touba, Biankouma is best visited on Sundays, when you can see the acrobatic Goua dancers perform. The old town is attractive in its own right. Before entering you should ask permission from the village chief, and before taking a photograph make sure you are not photographing a fetish object, which is considered bad luck.

There is a hotel here (Hotel du Mont Sangbé), but you may want to base yourself in [Gouessesso](/africa/ivorycoast/gouessesso) instead.
