Bingerville is about 15 km east of [Abidjan](/africa/ivorycoast/abidjan), a charming town on a plateau overlooking the lagoon. Until the 1930s it served as the capital of Côte d'Ivoire.

The major attractions include the old botanical gardens, the governor's palace, and the Atelier d'Art. From Bingerville, a 25 km dirt road takes you to [Grand Bassam](/africa/ivorycoast/grandbassam), where the French first set up their colonial capital.
