Fakobly is a small village 9 km from [Tieni-Siabli](/africa/ivorycoast/tienisiabli) and about 25 km from [Man](/africa/ivorycoast/man), in the far west of [Ivory Coast](/africa/ivorycoast). It lies on the banks of a tributary of the Sassandra River, where hippos and crocodiles can be spotted — you can hire a pirogue for a short trip on the water. The local Wobé girls perform an attractive dance called the temate, though it is often hard to find out in advance when there will be a performance.
