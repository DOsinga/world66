Fakobly is a small village in western Ivory Coast, about 9 km from [Tieni-Siabli](/africa/ivorycoast/tienisiabli) and 25 km from [Man](/africa/ivorycoast/man). It sits on the banks of a tributary of the Sassandra River, in a region of forested hills close to the Liberian border.

The river is the main draw. Hippos and crocodiles live along this stretch, and you can hire a pirogue for a small tour on the water — a memorable way to see the wildlife at close range. The local Wobe people are known for the temate, an elaborate masked dance performed by young women. Performances are irregular and not easy to predict in advance, so ask around in the village when you arrive.
