Meru is a fairly big town northeast of Mt Kenya. It’s
 home to the Meru people, close relatives of the Kikuyu
 and Embu. There’s little reason travelers would stay
 in Meru, but if you do, it can be a fun place. Meru’s
 famous for its *miraa,* twigs of a plant that has
 stimulant properties. It is grown legally in large
 amounts here, so you won’t get fresher miraa anywhere.
 After chewing the bark of the twigs for an hour or
 so, you’ll feel like you’ve drank two or three strong
 cups of coffee. Stay at the *Milimani Hotel* on
 the junction north of town. It has nice
 singles/doubles for KSh 600/800 including breakfast
 and the bar turns into a disco on the weekends. 
 There’s a museum in town if you have any free time.
