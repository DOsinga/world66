---
title: "Lake Elmenteita"
type: location
---

Lake Elmenteita is a shallow soda lake near Lake Nakuru. The lake is frequented by flamingos, but you won’t see as many here as Lake Nakuru or Lake Bogoria. This is not a National Park, so you can walk around the lake, although there is a whole lot of wildlife.  If you stay, the only hotel is *Lake Elmenteita Lodge* (# (0367) 5040), which has singles/doubles for US$ 40/80 in the low season, and US$ 110/140 inthe high season.
