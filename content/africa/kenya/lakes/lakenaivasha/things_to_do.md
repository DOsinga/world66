title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

The lake and its surroundings offer a mix of wildlife viewing, walking, and history.

Crater Lake, on the western side, sits inside a small game sanctuary — a peaceful spot with colobus monkeys and good birdwatching. Crescent Island, reachable by boat from the lakeshore, is a private wildlife sanctuary where you can walk freely among zebras, giraffes, and wildebeest with no fences.

Elsamere, the former home of Joy Adamson (author of *Born Free*), is now a conservation centre about 2 km past Fisherman's Camp on the Lake Road. It is open to the public for afternoon tea and has a small museum about Adamson's work with lions and cheetahs.
