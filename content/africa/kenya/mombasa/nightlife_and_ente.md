---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment in Mombasa"
type: section
---

**Casablanca Club**-Perhaps Mombasa's most popular night spot.Open 24 hours and attracting a mixture of sailors,tourists,locals and lots of girls.Can get very packed particularly on weekends.Always has a good crowd throughout.There's an exotic dance bar with VIP section on the ground floor.Right in the heart of Mombasa's downtown so transport is never a problem.

This club is a must see if you'r e in Mombasa.Some of the ladies can be a bother particularly with tourists...but good all around fun.
