**A slumtour in Nairobi**
*Kibera the friendliest slum in the world*

If you'd like to see something different, see a side of Kenya unseen by most tourists:
Join a tour to the Nairobi slum Kibera with Kiberatours.

Kibera is the friendliest slum in the world, also one of the largest in Africa. It is located in the southern part of Nairobi and over 1 million people are living here.

When you join this tour of Kiberatours you will see how the people in Kibera live. You will visit their houses, streets and businesses. Kiberatours will also take you to several projects in Kibera that will show you how the people in Kibera try to improve their lives. This will include an orphanage, a biogas center and a youth club.

The tour guides of Kiberatours are born and living in Kibera and know all the ins and outs. Also a safety guard will join the tour.

The groups will be small, maximal 6 guests at the same time.

More information can be found at **www.kiberatours.com**
