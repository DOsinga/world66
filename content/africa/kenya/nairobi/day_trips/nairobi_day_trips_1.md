Nairobi Excursions (GCS:1)

Nairobi City Tour - 3 hours.

The
tour covers the modern city centre, the colorful city market,
parliament building , the Railway station and the renowned National
Museum where there spell binding displays of the early man, tribal
regalia and the Flora and Fauna of Kenya. A visit to the adjacent Snake
park is also included.***Price US $ 50 per person.** *

Nairobi National Park - a full day

The unique wildlife sanctuary is just a few kilometers from Nairobi's bustling city center but

despite its proximity over 50 species of mammals can be found there, including four of the

Big
Five. The tour also includes a visit to the animal orphanage at the park gate. The tour

returns to
the city center by sundown. ***Price US $ 105 per person.** *
Karen Blixen and Giraffe Center - 3 hours

Departs
in the afternoon or in the morning. Drive towards the Ngong Village to
the Karen Blixen museum, for many years this was her home. Later,
proceed to the Giraffe center to see and feed the famous Rothchild
giraffes. ***Price US $ 70 per person.***
Carnivore Experience - 2 hours

Guaranteed start daily at 12.30 p.m. and 7.00 p.m. Nairobi has many restaurants offering varieties

of cuisine. We recommend a dinner or lunch at the Carnivore, which is famous for the succulent

stew and barbecue dishes of game meat as an excellent compliment to a holiday in Kenya;

especially as an excellent welcome or farewell dinner. ***Price US $ 45 per person.***
Bomas of Kenya - 3 hours

A
Boma is a homestead and just a few kilometers from city , numerous
homesteads reflecting Kenya's cocktail of cultures have been faithfully
recreated for visitors to see traditional village life. But the
greatest excitement during the afternoon is a visit to the display of
traditional dancing music and folklore songs performed in splendid
arena.*** Price US $ 60 per person.***

**Lost Paradise - 3 hours **

This is a unique excursion, where by you go visit the Lost Paradise in
the morning or afternoon. The drive takes you to Kiambu to visit the
coffee estate with a tour of the factory, followed by a visit to the
estate's century-old stone caves. Later, enjoy feeding the Ostrich,
riding camels and boating among other activities. Enjoy Tea before
returning to Nairobi. ***Price US $ 60 per person.******
