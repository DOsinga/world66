Nairobi is a natural and fun-filled family holiday destination that
offers several opportunities for wildlife spotting and mystical
cultural discoveries. Here are a few ideas to make the most of
your family vacation in this wild Kenyan hotspot.

**Nairobi**** National Park**** **

Hire a jeep and go game viewing and mammal spotting at Nairobi’s
most illustrious wildlife park. Children will enjoy viewing some of
most magnificent species of the big cats along with the wild
buffaloes, rhinos, giraffes, zebras, warthogs and a host of other
animals and birds in their natural surroundings. Visitors can also hire
an experienced guide and take a nature walking trip at the Education
Center. The park houses an Animal Orphanage that tends to sick and
