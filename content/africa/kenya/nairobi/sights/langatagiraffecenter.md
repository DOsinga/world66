title: "Langata Giraffe Center"
type: poi
---

The Giraffe Center is on Gogo Falls Road about 1 km from the Langata shopping center. Adult and young giraffes can be fed by visitors and it’s an excellent opportunity to have a closer look of these beautiful animals. Admission is KSh 250, 70 for children.

 ** Lee:** I visited there in May of 2002. It was fantastic! The giraffes are kept behind a fence in a large field, but there is a feeding building where you can stand on the balcony and they come right up to you. Admission was a little pricey, but worth it. The gift shop was very nice as well. Expect to spend 1 - 2 hours at the center. It's best if you can go there with someone who has already been. I can't wait to go again!** Hilda:** going there
