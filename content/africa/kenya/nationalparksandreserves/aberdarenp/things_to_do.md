---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

The Ark is the park's most famous experience — a lodge built like Noah's Ark, positioned beside a floodlit waterhole where elephants, rhinos, buffalo, hyenas and lion come to drink day and night. Guests watch from covered decks or through ground-level bunkers. It is expensive, but the nighttime wildlife viewing is unlike anything you get on a standard game drive.
