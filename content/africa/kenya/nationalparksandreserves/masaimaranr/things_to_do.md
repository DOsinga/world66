3 Days Masai Mara Safaris.****TWB 109:
3 Days | 2 Nights:
Masai Mara Game Reserve.**Day 1. Masai Mara Game Reserve****.**

Depart Nairobi in the morning and drive through the floor of the rift
valley climbing the western rim and cross the loita plains to arrive
Mara in time for lunch. Afternoon game drive inside the park. Dinner and
overnight at Lodge.**Day 2. Masai Mara.**

Breakfast and leave with picnic lunches for a full day game drive
inside the Park. Search for Wild game black manned lion for which Mara
is famous for, cheetah, buffalo, wildebeest, zebra, leopard, grants
gazelle are quite common, even the rare rhino might come out of the
thicket for you, hyenas are plentiful, Extend your drive to the hippo
point where schools of hippo submerge on the approach of vehicles only
to surface seconds later to shot and gamble their displeasure as the
crocodiles sunbathe on the river banks mouths agape. Between meals there
is optional visit to the maasai villages for an insight to their
culture if clients so wish. Meals and overnight at Lodge.**Day 3. Nairobi **
Morning game drive and return for breakfast. Depart with game viewing en route arriving Nairobi in the afternoon.
