---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Game drives are the heart of any Mara visit, typically two per day: an early-morning drive to catch predators on the hunt, and a late-afternoon drive as the animals stir again after the midday heat. Beyond the usual safari routine, two splurges are worth it: a sunrise **hot-air balloon ride** over the plains (about 450 USD per person, ending in a champagne breakfast on the savanna), and a visit to a **Maasai village** to meet the community whose land surrounds the reserve. During the July–October migration months, spend as much time as you can near the Mara river and its famous crossing points.
