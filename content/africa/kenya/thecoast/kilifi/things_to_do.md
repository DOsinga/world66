title: "Things to do"
type: section
---

Ask at Mnarani Club for Captain Issa or Max and arrange for them to take you out on their traditional dhow for a day of snorkelling/swimming, and fabulous seafood lunch on a beautiful unspoilt beach. It's great fun, fascinating watching them operate the sails (there is a motor) and the crew are a lovely bunch of lads. You'll enjoy the whole experience!
