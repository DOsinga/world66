---
title: "Eating Out"
type: section
---

During the month of Ramadhan, most restaurants will be closed all day until sundown. The more expensive restaurants catering to tourists, however, will remain open all day.

  For cheap but good Swahili food, try the rice pilau or beans at the *New Star Restaurant.* *Hapa Hapa* and the *Bush Gardens* on the waterfront serve good food for about KSh 200-400. The restaurant on top of *Petley's Inn* have consistently good food for a bit more. The *Barbecue Grill* at Peponi Hotel is open to non-guests and is a bit expensive, but also good.
