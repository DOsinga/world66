---
title: "Beaches"
type: section
---

Malindi's beaches line a bay sheltered by coral reefs, with calm clear water suitable for swimming. The protected marine national park at the southern end of the bay offers excellent snorkelling over coral gardens. For deep-sea fishing, the waters off Malindi are known for marlin, sail fish, and sharks. Boat trips — including glass-bottomed boats over the reef — can be arranged through most hotels or directly on the beach.
