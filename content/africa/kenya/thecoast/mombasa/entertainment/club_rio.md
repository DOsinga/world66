This is a bar and grill during the day(excellent food by the way-better price) and becomes a nightclub after dark when they have full blast music.Popular with locals particularly the bueracrats and bankers from the surrounding business district.Has the feel of a sports bar with all the screens adorning the walls and showing the latest sporting events.Was full to overflowing during an english premier league game while we were there.Very tidy.

Nice surprise was the rooms in the back.This is actually a hotel.Rooms started at $15 with continental breakfast included.

Not crazy or anything like that but lot's of fun.Worth a visit.
