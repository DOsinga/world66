Our 2nd April event is an Easter special! Join us in Nyali at the Coast for an epic weekend.

Blankets & Wine is the premier Afro- based music festival in East Africa designed to showcase genres of Afro fusion music

Blankets& Wine is a picnic style music festival in which you are actively encouraged to drive down to the venue. In Nairobi, this event takes place monthly. This year, Blankets & Wine is expanding into Mombasa, Nanyuki, Diani, Dar es Salaam, Kampala, and Kigali.

On the 8th join us from 3:00pm - Midnight at Nyali International Beach for the 1st edition of Blankets & Wine Mombasa!
