I prefer to stay in the Eldoret Club. It is has old colonial feel about it. It has a great golf course, swimming pool, gym etc. It is tucked away just outside the main town center. It is very safe, good for women alone. Rates are around 2300 for a room, breakfast extra. South African sat TV (sometimes). Jim Bedford
