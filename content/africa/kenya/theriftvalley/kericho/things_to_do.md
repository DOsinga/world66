---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Merely driving towards Kericho from any direction will give one breath-taking views of the vast tea plantations that are the economic backbone of the local economy. Scattered among the tea plantations are neatly arranged housing complexes for the tea pluckers, who every two to four weeks pick the tea from every square metre of the expansive plantations.

For some peace and quiet, consider spending a little time at the arboretum at Chagaik, about 10km from Kericho on the Nakuru Road. There is a small dam at the bottom of the valley.
