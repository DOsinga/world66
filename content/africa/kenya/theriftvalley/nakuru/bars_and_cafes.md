---
title: "Bars and Cafes"
type: section
---

Nakuru's drinking scene is a mix of busy local sports bars, hotel lounges, and a handful of decent cafes serving fresh coffee from the surrounding highlands. The town centre — particularly along Kenyatta Avenue and Moi Road — is where most of the action is, with places that double as restaurants by day and dance floors by night. Tusker is the local beer of choice; nyama choma (grilled meat) is the standard bar food.
