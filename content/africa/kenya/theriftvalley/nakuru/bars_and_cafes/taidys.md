A three-storey bar and restaurant with a wraparound balcony — one of the best-known spots in town. Football is on the screens most evenings, the Tusker is cold, and the food (grilled meats, ugali, pilau) is decent. After dark it turns into one of Nakuru's main dance floors, with a friendly crowd of locals and the occasional traveller passing through.
