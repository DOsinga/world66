On the corner of Moi Road and Kenyatta Avenue, this small cafe is one of the best places in central Nakuru for a proper breakfast and freshly ground Kenyan coffee. The pastries are baked in-house — the lemon tart, naturally, is the one to try. Open 8 a.m. to 5 p.m., with light snacks through the day.
