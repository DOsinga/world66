Gilani's Restaurant in Nakuru situated within the Gilani's Supermarket Shopping Complex, is the Largest, Finest and most reasonably priced fully licensed restaurant in Nakuru.

The Cafeteria Section sits 250 persons and serves Fresh Juices, Snacks, Pastries, Indian Curries and a large variety of Continental Dishes - *Servings are huge!*

The Magic Carpet Section is a posh 5 Star Restaurant - Fully Carpeted, Air Conditioned and Tastefully Decorated sits 60 persons. The menu has over 250 items ranging from Steaks, Chicken, Fish to Italian, Indian and plenty of Chinese Cuisine to choose from - *The Servings are Generous and Very Reasonably Priced.*

The Supermarket Downstairs is the largest in Town and Guarantees to be the Cheapest in Town stocking a huge variety of household needs.
