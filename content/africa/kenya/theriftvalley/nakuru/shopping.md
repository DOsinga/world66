---
title: "Shopping"
type: section
---

Nakuru is a regional trading centre, and the streets around the **Nakumatt Westside** and the old market are crowded with dukas selling everything from Kenyan tea and coffee to baskets, beads, and wooden carvings. Look for **kiondos** (handwoven sisal bags), soapstone carvings from nearby Kisii, and Maasai jewellery; the area's furniture-makers also sell solid hardwood pieces, though they're hard to ship. **Gilani's Supermarket** on Club Road, opposite the Rift Valley Sports Club, is a useful one-stop for groceries and supplies before heading into the parks.
