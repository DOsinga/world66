  **Menengai Crater:**

 Menengai is an extinct volcano 2490 m high, lying about 7 km north of Nakuru town. You can drive right to the edge if you have a vehicle, but if you don’t, it isn’t a bad walk. Take Menengai Drive at the eastern end of town, off of Showground Rd. The crater is not so spectacular, but the view is beautiful and it’s a nice hike to make if you want to buy some time in Nakuru.
