**Discover Africa** off the beaten track with **Bike the Coast**: local people in their settlements and traditional African villages, palm- and mango-tree forests, different climate zones, fantastic views on Creeks and the Mombasa North Coast Area, bush along the Indian Ocean...

Bike the Coast, **[Mombasa](/africa/kenya/thecoast)** offers guided half day **Bush-Tours** on safe unpaved roads with no traffic.

With the mountain-bike you come closer to the local life than with any other mean.

All the equipment provided.

We also offer individual full day, 2 or 3 days tours. Please inquire.
Under Swiss Management, we speak English, German and French.

Africa begins right behind your hotel - discover it.
