About Kisii Hotel

Situated
close to the centre of town, the Kisii Hotel is an old established business.

Built in the 1920s, it retains a quaint colonial style, but offers all
the amenities that the modern traveller expects, catering for families,
backpackers and campers in the new campsite.

Accommodation is clean if a little worn, but this is more than reflected
in the very attractive prices.
