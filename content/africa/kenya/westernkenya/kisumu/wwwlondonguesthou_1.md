***London Guest House*** is conveniently situated in the Kondele area of **Kisumu town, Kenya**. Family run and owned, the hotel carries an English themed decor whilst offering traditional Kenyan hospitality.

The hotel has 15 clean and comfortable rooms all en-suite, reception area, breakfast/meeting area and plenty of parking.

You can rest securely knowing there are always four professional security guards on the premises and alarm system - all to give you peace of mind for the duration of your stay.

All guests receive a free daily breakfast with compliments of London Guest House.

The Hotel is in an ideal location with easy airport transfer, ensuring that from the moment you step foot in this hotel, you will feel relaxed, rejuvenated and fully re-charged as you wake up each morning, allowing you to function smoothly during the day, whether you are completing your business or touring the area.
