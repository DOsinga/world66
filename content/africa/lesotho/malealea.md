Don't miss the pony treks in the village of Malealea, the definite highlight of our Lesotho experience. These are best organized by the Malealea Lodge (and they offer fantastic dinners!) You enter the village via the spectacular entrance through the Gates of Paradise Pass. Don't forget to bring warm clothes and your rain gear.
