---
title: "Getting Around in Monrovia"
type: section
---

**Motorcycle taxis** (known locally as "pen-pen") are the quickest and cheapest way to get around the city. Agree on the fare before you get on — ask a local what the going rate should be, as drivers sometimes quote higher prices to unfamiliar visitors.

**Taxis** are more comfortable and are the better choice for longer journeys or at night. Shared taxis run fixed routes at fixed prices; private hire costs more but is straightforward to negotiate.

**Buses** are cheap but very crowded and not well suited to visitors unfamiliar with the routes.
