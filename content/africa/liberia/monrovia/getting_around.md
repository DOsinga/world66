---
title: "Getting Around in Monrovia"
type: section
---

**Monrovia**** transportation** services allow you to reach your destination in and out of Monrovia. Monrovia is the capital city of the West African nation of Liberia.

**Motorcycle**

Motorcycle is a good option for traveling around the city. You must choose a trustworthy driver when you choose a motorcycle. There are drivers at times who take advantage of the costumer, especially if they know that you are a foreigner. Be sure that before you ride in the motorcycle, you know how much you are going to pay when you reach your destination. You can verify this fare with the local residents who are around there. If the fare given by the driver is too high, you have the choice to either bid for a lower fare or look for another motorcycle and driver.

**Taxi**

Taxis are available in Monrovia but are quite expensive. However, if you are looking for a relaxing ride, you can pick this mode of transportation. These taxis are the only comfortable and safe means of transportation in Monrovia. There are taxis in the city that are shared and charge a fixed price. If you do not want to waste money, you should be sure that the fare you pay is fair to you and to the driver.

**Bus**

There are buses available in the city. However, it is not recommended that tourists use this form of transportation since you may be thrown out of the bus when it is crowded. Also, there is a chance that you might lose your purse, wallet, and other valuables.
