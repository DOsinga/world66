Al Khums (Arabic: الخمس) is a coastal town in Al Murgub District, about 130 km east of Tripoli. It serves as the nearest town to [Leptis Magna](/africa/libya/leptismagna), the Roman city just 3 km to the east, and is the usual base for visitors to the ruins. There is modest accommodation here and a small harbour.
