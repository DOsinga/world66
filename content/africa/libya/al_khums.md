Al Khums (Arabic: الخمس ‎) is a town in Al Murgub District on the Mediterranean coast of Libya.[[1]](/africa/libya#cite_note-0) Between 1983 and 1995 it was the administrative center of the Al Khums District.

Approximately 3.2 km (2 miles) to the east is the ancient Roman city of Leptis .
