The Jebel Akhdar, or "Green Mountain" is the hilly region near [Benghazi](/africa/libya/benghazi). For the last couple of years the region has been somehwat unsafe due to small groups of people trying to fight the regime. Thier attempts have not really posed a threat so far, but did make this region of limits.
