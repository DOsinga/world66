Misurata City is situated on the location of an old trade centers that the Phoenicians founded in the tenth century BC . It is some 200 km East of [Tripoli](/africa/libya/tripoli) and 825 km west of [Benghazi](/africa/libya/benghazi).

Misurata city is the third largest of the Libyan cities with a population of 300,000.
