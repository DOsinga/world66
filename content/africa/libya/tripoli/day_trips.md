---
title: "Day Trips in Tripoli"
type: section
---

Tripoli’s surrounding regions are blessed with stunning
natural beauty and a rich archeological heritage offering many day
trip options. Here are some of the best day trip ideas to make the most of their exciting Libyan sojourn.

**Sabratha**

This is classic ancient archeological site that features
tinctures of the power of the Numidian Kingdom and the Roman
Empire. The site dates back to thousands of years when the
region was discovered by the Phoenicians during the 3 rd
century AD. The abandoned archeological ruins and monuments are replete
with tales of the glory and downfall of the Byzantines and the
Carthaginians. View fascinating previews of the ostentatious Roman
lifestyle in the form of public baths, impressive fountains and
avant-garde mosaics. The destination also features a heritage theater
that is used is showcase live performances and a museum chronicling
events and items from the Romanian era. The heritage site is located at
approximately 40 miles from the western border of Tripoli and completes
an enchanting trilogy with Leptis Magna and Oea.

**Wadi Metkhandoush**

Wadi Metkahndoush in the Tadrart Acacus is a nice place to visit if
you have an affinity for prehistoric cave paintings and ancient
engravings. The destination is filled with illustrations of the grand
artistic legacy of an earlier era in the form of aesthetic rock
carvings, charmingly rustic stone paintings and intricate symmetrical
sculpture patterns.

**Beach Fun**

Firefight the desert heat with a dip in the lush waters of Farwa or
Zwara. The beautiful twin beaches are idyllic spots for a peaceful day
trip in the midst of Libya’s coastal magnificence.

**Murzuq Sand Dunes**

One of the most exciting drives that you will take in Libya will be
through the intimidating sand dunes of Murzuq. Hire a SUV and buckle up
for a thrilling ride in the vast desert of the region for some
breathtaking sites and a generous dose on undiluted adventure.
Definitely not for the faint hearted.

**Fezzan Desert **

Fezzan Desert features striking lakes that look like glistening gems
in the background on the humongous desert. A true depiction of the
fabled oasis, the destination is one of the most beautiful sights in
Libya and is located a few kilometers from the heart of Tripoli.

Like Tripoli, its neighbouring regions are rich reserves of heritage
architectural glory and natural beauty and provide innumerable day trip
options.
