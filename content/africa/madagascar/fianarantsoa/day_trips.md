The area around Fianarantsoa has two destinations worth a dedicated excursion. Ranomafana National Park, about 60 km to the northwest, is one of the best places in Madagascar to see lemurs in dense montane rainforest. The town of Ambalavao, 50 km to the south, is known for its Antaimoro paper factory and the Anja Community Reserve — a small but rewarding lemur sanctuary just outside town.
