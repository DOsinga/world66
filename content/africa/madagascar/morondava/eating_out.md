### [Le Masoandro Restaurant / Chez Maggie Hotel](/africa/madagascar/morondava/eatingout/le_masoandro_resta)

**Le Masoandro Restaurant / Chez Maggie Hotel**

I ate here my first night in Morondava and still sometimes wake in the
night craving the crabs sautéed in butter and garlic that are equaled
nowhere else in Morondava, at the very least. The restaurant does set
the menus at the high end of Morondava’s expense range, but it’s often
worth it. However the ambience is one of the best to be had if you are
looking for gently bulging sofas amongst rustling palms.
