**The Blue Elephant - Bar & Restuarant - Blantyre**

Tel local 09955242 and speak to Vero

This is a first class Pub that puts on local bands every Wednesday and Saturday nights, from 8pm till very late. They also have a disco every Saturday and Happy Hours every Monday and Tuesday from 5pm to 6.30pm.

It's a really great place and the atmoshere is warm and friendly. It's run by this local couple and they really do a good job. If you are in Blantyre, the "Blue" is a must both for the visitor and locals alike. Oh! They also do food, all day and night, but no accomodation!!

Ask anybody in Blantyre where it is.
