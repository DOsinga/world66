Hiking in Malawi is very rewarding. Zomba Mt. is the easiest to reach with no special planning required. Mulanje Mt., at 3000m high, requires some planning, preparation and bookings for mountain huts, however, access is not difficult.

 Nyika Plateau offers splendid opportunities for long hikes at 2000m, plus the addition of safe 'on foot' game viewing. A 5hr drive or 40min flight from [Mzuzu](/africa/malawi/mzuzu) puts you into Chelinda Camp.
