---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

Djenné is most easily reached from [Mopti](/africa/mali/mopti) (about 130 km) or from [Bamako](/africa/mali/bamako) (about 575 km via Ségou). Transport is more frequent on Mondays — market day — when bush taxis and minibuses run to and from Mopti. On other days you may need to arrange private transport or wait for a shared vehicle. The road from the main highway crosses a seasonal floodplain that can be impassable during the rainy season (July to October).
