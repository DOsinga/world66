Djiguibombo is easy to reach from [Bandiagara](/africa/mali/dogoncountry/bandiagara) and makes an excellent base for exploring the escarpment villages of Ende, Teli, and Kani Kombole. All of these have basic guesthouses where you can spend the night, as does Djiguibombo itself. To get here from Bandiagara, hire a mobylette or walk down the escarpment.
