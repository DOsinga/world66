Kita is a charming little town with a lively market halfway between [Kayes](/africa/mali/kayes) and [Bamako](/africa/mali/bamako).


 The best way to get here is by train. For a place to stay your best option is Hotel Relais Touristique. It has a swimming pool, something you will nedd in this part of the world.
