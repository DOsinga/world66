A pirogue (dugout canoe) trip on the Niger and Bani rivers is one of the great pleasures of Mopti. Short trips from the port visit nearby villages at dusk when the light is spectacular. Head to the Bar Bozo on the waterfront to find boat owners willing to arrange a trip. Longer journeys toward Djenné or Timbuktu can also be arranged.
