---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

Nouakchott is the main entry point for Mauritania. The international airport, Oumtounsy International Airport (NKC), lies about 25 km north-east of the city centre. There are direct flights from Paris, Casablanca, Dakar, Las Palmas, and a handful of other African hubs.

Overland, the main crossing from Senegal is at Rosso on the Senegal River, though the crossing can be slow and chaotic — the Diama Dam crossing further west is often faster and less hassle. From Morocco, the crossing at Guerguerat south of Dakhla connects to the northern Mauritanian town of Nouadhibou; from there, the iron-ore train or a shared taxi south will eventually get you to Nouakchott, roughly 500 km away.
