Flacq (also written as Centre de Flacq) is the main town on the east coast. It hosts the island's largest outdoor market on Sundays and Wednesdays — a genuine local market rather than a tourist one, good for cheap produce, street food, and household goods. The nearby Belle Mare beach is one of the finest on the island, and Ile aux Cerfs is reached by boat from Trou d'Eau Douce, a short drive south.
