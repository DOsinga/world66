title: "Mont Choisi"
type: location
---

Mont Choisi is a small beach resort a few kilometres south of [Grand Baie](/africa/mauritius/grandbaie) on the north coast. The beach here is wide, sheltered, and lined with casuarina trees — slightly quieter than Grand Baie itself, with a more residential feel. It's a good base for exploring the north while avoiding the busiest stretch of the tourist strip.
