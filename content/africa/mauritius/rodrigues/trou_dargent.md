Consistently rated among the best beaches in Rodrigues, and genuinely exceptional — a crescent of pale sand tucked between low headlands on the east coast, with turquoise water and good snorkelling just offshore. It takes a walk to reach from the nearest road, which means you'll often have it to yourself.
