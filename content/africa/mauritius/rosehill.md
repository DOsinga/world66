Rose-Hill is one of the larger towns in the central plateau, merging with Beau Bassin to form a significant urban area. It's primarily a residential and commercial centre for Mauritians rather than a tourist destination. The Plaza Theatre is the island's main cultural venue for drama and music. The town is well connected by bus to Port Louis and Curepipe, and makes a reasonable base if you want to avoid resort prices while exploring the island interior.
