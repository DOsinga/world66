There are some local cafes and restaurants as follows:

Rochester Falls Restaurant, Souillac Bridge, Surinam/Souillac.

Suresh Babaji's restaurant, Arcade Ramtohul, Surinam.

Sunrise Restaurant, Cemetary Road, Trois Bras, Surinam.

Many local grocery shops also sell snacks such as onion bajees, samosas, fried noodles/chow mein.
