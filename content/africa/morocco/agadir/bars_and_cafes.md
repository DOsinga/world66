---
title: "Bars and Cafes"
type: section
---

Agadir is one of the more permissive cities in Morocco for alcohol, and bars are found throughout the hotel zone along the beach boulevard and in the Talborjt neighbourhood. The beach boulevard has the greatest concentration of tourist-oriented bars. For a more local atmosphere, the cafes around the Nouvelle Talborjt neighbourhood serve mint tea and coffee to a mixed crowd. Most bars are open until midnight; clubs run later.
