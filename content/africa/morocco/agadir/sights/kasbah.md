The Kasbah is a bit out of the center, about 8 km. The sight from the Kasbah over the coast is great. The construction itself is not grand. It has an inscription in Dutch, saying that the Netherlands founded a trading post here in 1746.** samir:** An excellent view of Agadir and Anza . A historic site for all tourists , and is a must see for everyone . A reminder of old Agadir but not much is left . The view alone is worth the trip .
