title: Souq El Had
type: poi

Agadir's main souq on Rue Ben Brahim is a large walled market with dozens of stalls selling clothing, spices, leather goods, and household items. Prices are generally better here than in the Marché Municipal, and the atmosphere is more authentically Moroccan. Go in the morning when it is busiest.
