---
title: "When to Go"
type: section
---

Agadir has one of the most reliably mild climates in Morocco, with over 300 sunny days a year. Summer temperatures sit in the low-to-mid 20s Celsius, moderated by Atlantic trade winds, making it comfortable even in July and August. Spring and autumn are ideal for beach visits without peak-season crowds. Winter is mild by European standards — rarely below 13°C — but can bring light rain between October and March, and beach conditions are not reliable. The resort fills with northern Europeans escaping winter, so December to February is busy despite the weather.
