---
title: "Asilah"
type: location
---

Asilah feels and looks a lot like a Spanish town in Andalucia. It is a lovely, old, very clean white-washed town on the Atlantic.   The town has a very quiet feel, and makes for a good stop.  The ramparts and towers of the town give you the impression time has stood still here, and if you walk down the small alleys at lunch time you can't help but wonder at the tranquillity.
