title: "Day Trips"
type: section
---

The **Monoliths of M'Soura**, 25 km south-east of Asilah, form one of the world's few neolithic stone circles outside Britain — arranged in a perfect ellipse in an otherwise desolate landscape. You will need a local guide and a reliable vehicle to reach them.

The Roman ruins of **Lixus**, one of the Phoenicians' most successful settlements in [Morocco](/africa/morocco), lie 5 km north of [Larache](/africa/morocco/larache). Less excavated and far less visited than [Volubilis](/africa/morocco/volubilis), Lixus has an atmospheric, slightly wild feel. A grand taxi from Asilah or Larache will get you there for a reasonable fare.
