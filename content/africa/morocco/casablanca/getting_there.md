title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

Casa is the transport hub of the country, so you shouldn't have any difficulty getting there and away. Going to Rabat, Marrakech, Fes, or Meknes the train is a wise choice. Moroccan trains are reliable, clean and fast.For a time-tabel see:  ONCF.

 For destinations down the coast (Azemmour, el-Jadida, Safi Essaouira) there are many buses at the bus station in the centre of town. Count about half an hour to get out of Casa and the 2 hours for every 100 km.
