type: poi

A small rough medina wedged between the port and the European city. Not on the scale of Fes or Marrakesh — a morning covers it — but a useful antidote to the rest of Casablanca's colonial grid. The Koubba of Sidi Bousmara, the 16th-century shrine at the centre, is the oldest piece of the old city.
