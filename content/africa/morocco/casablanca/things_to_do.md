---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Casablanca is a working city, not a tourist one — the great Hassan II Mosque aside, a half-day covers most of what most visitors want to see. Walk **Boulevard Mohammed V** for the weathered French-era Mauresque buildings; dip into the **Old Medina** for a small, rough version of what Fes and Marrakesh do better; then head out to **Ain Diab** for the beach and the seafood restaurants along the Corniche.
