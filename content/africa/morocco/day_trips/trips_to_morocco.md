**1st day :Marrakech
- Ait Benhaddo - Ouarzazate- Skoura- El Kelaa M'gouna – Valley des
roses-Boumalene Dades.**

After breakfast, we pick up
you at the hotel in Marrakech, departure via Ouarzazate over Tizi-n-Tichka passing
by the col 2260m while enjoying some attractive views of the High Atlas
Mountains also the Berber villages. Then visit the UNESCO World Heritage Site:
the exotic Kasbah of Aït Benhaddou then have lunch. In the entrance to
Ouarzazate you can see the site of the Atlas studios which host the largest
movie studios in the world such as Lawrence of Arabia, The Mummy and The Man
Who Would Be King, passing by Kasbah of Taourirt, then continue the road to El
Kelaa M’gouna (the valley of roses) then to Boumlen Dades . Overnight include
Dinner and breakfast in the Riad .

**2nd day: Boumalen
Dades – Todra gorges-Tinjdad- Arfoud -Merzouga .**

After breakfast, heading
toward Merzouga, passing by Todra gorges which worth a visit and a walk around,
then continue the road by crossing the Todra valley toward Tinjdad, where you
can have lunch. After that we drive you via Arfoud which is known by fossils
and marbles then a Visit to a Carrier. Later will continue to Merzouga where
the caravans of desert will be waiting for you to have an adventure by riding
camels in Erg Chebbi dunes. It will take you to spend the night in a bivouac
(camp in the heart of the desert), enjoying Berber food, listening to the drums
in a starry night and sleeping in a traditional nomad tent (camel hair tent) in
calmness and tranquility.
