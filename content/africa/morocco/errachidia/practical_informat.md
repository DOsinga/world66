There are banks to change your money, restaurants, hotels - even Berber tents to stay in or camping grounds. You can find English speaking tourist guides who can help you in many ways - especially since the people are friendly and sociable.

### [Location of Errachidia](/africa/morocco/errachidia/practical_informat/location_of_errach)

Errachidia or Er Rachidia is located south of the Middle Atlas Mountains and north of the Sahara desert. To the north of Errachidia are the Gorges du Ziz, a lake made by a dam and a tunnel made by the French. All the palm trees are mainly to the south.
