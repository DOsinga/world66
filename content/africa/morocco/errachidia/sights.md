### [Lizards, Mice, Toads and Frogs](/africa/morocco/errachidia/sights/lizards_mice_toa)

Some people like Birdwatching and other people like to see the flora and the fauna. It is very interesting to see what kind of reptiles can survive in this area. Some of the animals besides the dromadaries are lizards, mice, toads and frogs. Hey! There's something for everyone! Kids love them and so we should all be a little bit a child at heart.
