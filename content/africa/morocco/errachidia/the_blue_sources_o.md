About 21 km south of Errachidia, a spring gushes from a rocky hillside and fills a vivid blue pool surrounded by date palms. The contrast against the dry, ochre landscape is striking. Locals and travelers swim in the cool water, and it makes a natural stopping point on the route toward the Ziz Gorges and the Sahara beyond.

A small campsite sits near the source, and there are basic facilities. The site gets busy on weekends with families from Errachidia. Best visited in the morning before tour groups arrive.
