Seafood! With its fishermen galore, Essaouira offers excellent seafood. Try Chalet de la Plage on the seafront and Chez Sam at the end of the harbor. 

**all**

**Petit Pearl Restaurant**

A wonderful Moroccan style Restaurant with comfortable seating and low candlelit tables. Marvelous food and very nice staff. It is found be entering the old city via the Bab Saba and walking to the left hand far corner of the small square.
