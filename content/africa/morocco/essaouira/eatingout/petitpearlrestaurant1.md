---
title: "Petit Pearl Restaurant"
type: poi
---

A wonderful Moroccan style Restaurant with comfortable seating and low candlelit tables. Marvelous food and very nice staff. It is found be entering the old city via the Bab Saba and walking to the left hand far corner of the small square. The Petit Pearl Restaurant is located in the passageway that leads to the main shopping street.  Enjoy!
