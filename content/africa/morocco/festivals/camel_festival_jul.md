---
title: "Tan-Tan Moussem"
type: poi
address: "Tan-Tan, southern Morocco"
---

The Tan-Tan Moussem (officially listed as UNESCO Intangible Cultural Heritage) is a gathering of nomadic and semi-nomadic Saharan tribes held every two years in Tan-Tan, near Goulimine in the deep south. Camel trading, traditional music, the hypnotic Guedra dance, and fantasia horseback displays make it one of the most authentic cultural events in the country. Dates vary — check locally before planning a visit around it.
