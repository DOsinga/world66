---
title: "Getting Around"
type: section
---

Within the old town: walk all you can. Walk until you feet can no longer bear you. It's best way to see what you need to see. Within the old city there are no other means of transport except for donkeys (and there are streets with signs 'donkeys not allowed'), so you don't have a lot of choice, anyway.

 To get from the Ville Nouvelle to the old town, you can find the normal cabs, with a meter the driver is unwilling to use. There are also buses, but they tend to be crowded.
