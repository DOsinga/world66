---
title: "Getting Around"
type: section
---

Getting around in this mountainous region means bringing enough cash for food and transport — both are more expensive than in lowland Morocco — and knowing when market days are. On market days it is much easier to find transport on a lorry. Lorries and Renault 4s are the most common form of transport, after the donkey.

Key market days in the region: Ait Hani on Thursday, Imilchil on Saturday, Rich on Sunday.
