Camel Trek in Atlas Mountains:
You will be picked up Marrakech in the morning. The drive takes 45 minutes from Marrakech. You will meet your camel and be given some basic instructions before starting the trek from a hamlet, Moulay Brahim; it follows a route through juniper and pine forest, passing many villages near Asni, with fine views of Mount Toubkal (4,167m), the highest peak in North Africa. Lunches may be served in a Berber house or near the river or in the forest. Overall a 5 hour trek. A longer trek can be arranged. After the camel trek, you would spend the night in one of the Berber villages and on the 2nd day walk for two or more hours near Asni in the foothills of the High Atlas Mountains. It’s easy trek to Moderate
