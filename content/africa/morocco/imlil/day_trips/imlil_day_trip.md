Drive for 1h10 from Marrakech to Imlil (60km), walk from the centre of Imlil, crossing the beautiful village of Aroumd and up to the waterfalls. Here we have a picnic lunch and come back down, crossing the juniper forest of the National park of Toubkal

- After ice breaking session with Berber tea, your guide will take you to visit around Berber villages and cross Ait Mizan valley to go to the traditional gite(guesthouse) for a home cooked lunch. You can witness beautiful view of green terrace of barley, the still quietness of the valley and the therapeutic sound of the flowing river which is something the city dwellers would want to remember forever.

You will have the chance to see the highest peak in the North of Africa Jbel Toubkal (4167meters).

In the evening our driver will drive you back to Marrakech.
