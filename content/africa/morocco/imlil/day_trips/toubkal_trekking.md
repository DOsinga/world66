Tizi n'Tamaterte (2279m of Altitude) Half or Full Day -
A gentel walk to the est of Dar Achain towards Tizi n’Tamaterte (2279m) will take you to see the most typical valley in the high atlas mountains, which Imnane valley, several berber villages and also a spectavulaire view of the high atlas mountains, lunch on the pass & a break before going back to the house.

Tizi Mzik (2684m of Altitude) Half or Full Day -
West of Dar Achain is also beautiful and atractive, 2h50 walk uphill to Tizi Mzik to get a nice view above Imlil valley & Azzaden valley, lunch on the pass & a break before going back to the house.

Sidi Chamharouch (2310m of Altitude) Half or Full Day-
Take the path leading to Toubkal Hut following the mule track until the Marabout sidi Chamharouch, where you will find your lunch ready, back by the same way.

Tachdirte valley 2 days & 1 night -
Around 9.00, walk with a guide from the house towards Tachdirte through the pass Tizi n’Tamaterte (2279m) stuning views above the berber valleys & the atlas mountains, overnight & dinner at the berber house in Tachdirte village. The next day walk along Imnane valley & cross many berber villages (Tmaguiste, Tinghrine, Ikkis…) walk up to cross Tizi n’Ougerssioual (1

900m) join Imlil valley & back

Azzaden Valley 2 days & 1 night -
Around 9.00, walk with a guide from the house towards Azzaden valley via Tizi Mzik 2684m, stunning view above Azzaden & Ouirgane vallay, continue to the village Tizi Oussem, overnight & dinner at the Berber house. 5h walking.
The next day walk back to Imlil via Tizi N’oudid & Mattat village. 4h walking to the house.
