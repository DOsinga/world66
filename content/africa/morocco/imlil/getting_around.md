---
title: "Getting Around"
type: section
---

Imlil is a small village and everything is on foot. Mules can be hired to carry luggage on treks into the mountains — an essential option for anyone attempting Toubkal with camping gear. The valley paths are well-worn and signposted at major junctions. For travel between villages in the wider valley, local guides are the most reliable option for route-finding on lesser paths.
