The Club Alpin Français refuge in the centre of Imlil village functions as the main information point for trekkers. Its noticeboard lists registered guides, current trail conditions, and accommodation options up the valley. Guides can be hired here directly; they are licensed by the Royal Moroccan Federation of Skiing and Mountaineering. The refuge also has basic dormitory accommodation and is the starting point for almost all treks into the Toubkal massif.
