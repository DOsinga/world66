---
title: "Things to do in Khamlia"
type: section
---

It is a nice place for relaxing. To forget stress, only enjoy. Take a camel trek into the sand dunes or stroll around by foot. Take a cup of nus-nus (half and half - coffee with milk) or Berber tea and talk with the local people. To listen to Gnawa music just in village. Look at children when they are playing or go to the school. The local people help you and advice you what you can do of more things here.
Be only careful by driving, many children are jumping in the village and around. One year ago was happen one terrible accident.
