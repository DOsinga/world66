**Dar Achain** is a small & comfortable guest house in a serene mountain setting in the Toubkal National Park it's owned by a Berber family living for several years on the heart of the high atlas Mountains of Morocco.

The house offers a comfortable & cheap accommodation and restaurant in a traditional Moroccan style with panoramic views of the atlas mountains including MT Toubkal, the North Africa's highest peak. Perched on the hillside within the Berber villages and only at10 minutes walking up the hill from Imlil.
