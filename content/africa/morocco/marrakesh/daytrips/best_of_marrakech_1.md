Marrakech is a great starting point to visit its red hamara city and other regions of Morocco. The Best of Marrakech & Excursions to Waterfalls, Mountains or the Sahara Desert Region of Ouarzazate are available daily.

When visiting Marrakech, one can discover the best through an entertaining and exciting historical tour with a licensed tour guide and visit places like the Majorelle Gardens, the Saadian Tombs, Koutoubia Mosque, Tiskwin Museum & the Contemporary Museum of Marrakech.

Most exciting is also the option to take a one or two day Sahara Desert excursion from Marrakech to the Erg Chebbi Dunes of Merzouga or the M'hamid Sahara Dunes. Other options from one day excursions from Marrakech also include the opportunity to visit the the majestic Cascades D'Ouzoud Waterfalls, the seaside town of Essaouria, the pottery capital of Safi, hike in Imlil up towards Djbel Toukbal or venture to the Ourika Valley, climb the Setti Fatima's 7 waterfalls, visit an Argan Cooperative and have tea with a Berber family.

Other options for those who want to extend their journey outside the region of Marrakech and beyond is a two or three day tour to the Sahara Desert. Travel Exploration Morocco offers a wide range of excellent journeys to M'hamid often referred to as the real Sahara Desert, to the Erg Chebbi Dunes of Merzouga, to the region of the Dades Valley and Todra Gorge with stunning landscapes along with the option to visit Bougthgrar's Valley of Nomads. Have lunch with a Berber, Moroccan family and discover Ouarzaztae's great Kasbahs: Ait Benhaddou Kasbahs, Kasbah Taouirirt, Kasbah Tifoltilte and the Oasis of Fint.
