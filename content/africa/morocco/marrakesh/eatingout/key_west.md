A huge brand new restaurant that boasts the best cuisine and concept in my opinion, it has French/Thai fusion cuisine at a very resonable price(cheaper than most places). Enveloped in a great decor with an atmosphere that is just simply amazing( belly dancers, great music and professional-friendly service). Its in Gueliz next to the Diesel shop, off avenue mohammedV. I highly recommend it to anyone visiting marrakech, it will certainly be a talking point for when you come back.
