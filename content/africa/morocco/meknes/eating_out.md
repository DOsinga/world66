---
title: "Eating Out"
type: section
---

Most restaurants in the Ville Nouvelle are around the Place Administrative, serving French and Moroccan food. In the medina, simple meals are available at good prices at the small restaurants near the souks.

Collier de la Colombe (67 Rue Driba) is considered the best option in the medina — set in an old mansion near the Mausoleum, follow signs from Place Lalla Aouda. La Coupole (Av. Hassan II / Rue du Ghana) is a reliable choice for Moroccan food in the Ville Nouvelle. Restaurant Merhaba (23 Av. Mohammed V) offers decent meals at low prices.
