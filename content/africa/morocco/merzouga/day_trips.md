title: "Day Trips"
type: section
---

Spending one or two nights under a nomad tent in the dunes or going for a dromedary ride with camelman is one of the best things to experience when traveling to Merzouga desert. Undergo the splendour of sunrises and sunsets, visit the oasis and the berber villages in the surrounding, spend a night with a nomad family under black tent where you will get in touch with commun life of the nomads and original nature.You can also drive or rent 4x4 to visit the serji lake,different villages and do the tour around all the erg chebbi sand dunes to drink thea with one nomad family and visit different oasis and fossile's carrieres...
