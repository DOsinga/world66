**Visiting Merzouga with your Family**

Here are some **Merzouga family travel** tips you can use for your
vacation. Merzouga is equipped with many little hotels, restaurants and
shops, so families can feel comfortable while staying here. Merzouga is
close to a couple of interesting desert sites. Visiting them will
guarantee for a memorable family travel experience.

**Safety **

When visiting Merzouga with your whole family, you should take
certain measures for the safety of your children. Stomach problems are
the most common thing children experience here. To avoid these, don't
give your children tap water to drink. The locals usually offer it, but
try to get bottled water instead. When going on a day trip, always apply
sunscreen with a high UVA/UVB factor to all exposed skin areas and
encourage your children to drink more water. Dehydration, heat
exhaustion and sunburns are the most common problems here.

**Family Fun **

For a day filled with family fun, you should visit either the famous
Erg Cherbi, one of the world's largest sand dune chains, or the fossil
site on the road to Erfoud. Both trips can be interesting, especially if
you choose to ride on camels. Camel rides are a popular option for
families. You will experience these exciting desert adventures at their
fullest. The whole family will be able to climb, roll and slide on the
sand at the erg, and if you have some luck you may discover some bones
and teeth at the fossil site.
