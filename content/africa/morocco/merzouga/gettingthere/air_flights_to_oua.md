There are daily flights from casablanca to Ouarzazte arriving at midnight. Best spend the night in Ouarzazate and travel 5 hours to Merzouga. Of course, this is for those who have little time- as you miss the spectacular views of the High Atlas Mountains on the way by car from Marrakech.

Great to have private transport so you can see Todra Grge and dades valley on the way after you leave Ouarzazate.

There are also weekly flights from Errachadia which is 2 hours away but then you must arrange transport as onr does not rent a car in Errachadia. There are public taxis- but they are crowded and slow.
