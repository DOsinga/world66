The best resource for sights, hotels, restaurants, bars, what to do and see

Nkob is a small village located 160km south of Ouarzazate, on the
national road 108 heading Rissani in Sahara Desert, after passing
Tazzarine and Alnif.

This incredible village has around 45
beautiful and well preserved kasbahs and just recently the local
authorities were able to send Nkob to be inscribed for UNESCO world
heritage list.

Nkob is surrounded by the Saghrou Mountains and by
2 main oasis and palm grove.

This is a special place that is
worth passing by after Ouarzazate and on the way to Sahara Desert in Erg
Chebbi Dunes.
