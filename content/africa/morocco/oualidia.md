---

Oualida is a spectacular resort with a lagoon beach on the Atlantic coast between [Safi](/africa/morocco/safi) and [El Jadida](/africa/morocco/eljadida). It is a wonderful place to relax, eat delicious seafood, swim, surf, wind-surf and walk on the beach. It’s not easy to get there, so there are not many tourist. If you have your own car, it’s an easy drive from El Jadida. It’s possible to take a grand taxi from El Jadida.
