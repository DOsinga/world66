---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

The main draw of Ouarzazate is the surrounding landscape and its famous film history. Kasbah Taourirt in town is the best-preserved of the great Glaoui fortresses. Ait Benhaddou, 32 km northwest, is a UNESCO-listed ksar that has appeared in more films than almost any other location on earth. The Atlas Film Studios on the edge of town offer an unusual look at how Hollywood uses the Moroccan desert as a backdrop.
