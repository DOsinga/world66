---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

Oujda is connected to the rest of Morocco by road and rail from [Fes](/africa/morocco/fes), with the train taking around six hours. It is the main transport hub for eastern Morocco — if you are heading to or from Berkane, Nador, Bouarfa or Figuig, you will likely change here. Oujda-Angads Airport has domestic flights and seasonal connections to Europe.
