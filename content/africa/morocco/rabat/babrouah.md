One of the most beautiful Almohad city gates in Morocco — the "Gate of the Winds" on the southwestern walls, near the Royal Palace. The carved stone surround frames the gate; inside, the old gate chambers are now a small gallery running rotating shows of contemporary Moroccan art. Free.
