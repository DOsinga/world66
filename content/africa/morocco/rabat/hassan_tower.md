---

The unfinished 12th-century minaret that would have been 60 metres tall above the world's largest mosque. Around its base, the hundreds of stubs of columns from the mosque floor stand in neat rows. On the opposite side of the plaza is the **Mausoleum of Mohammed V**, a mid-20th-century Alaouite masterwork by Vietnamese architect Eric Vo Toan. Open daily; free.
