**Rabat nightlife** presents a great mix of bars, live music,
cafes and disco clubs throughout the city. While much of the bar scene
is largely patronized by foreigners, a mix of foreigners and young
Moroccans populate the open-air cafes and lively disco clubs centralized
in their own areas of Rabat.

**Rabat Bars**

Though drinking alcohol is still considered taboo throughout much of
Morocco, in Rabat consumption is legal and there are many nice bars to
enjoy. Most of the bars that are visited by Westerners tend to be
located in the more upscale hotels like Sofitel Diwan or the Rabat
Hilton. The Golden Tulip and La Tour Hassan are 2 other popular bars
in Rabat.

Many hotel bars also feature live music as part of their lineup. The Hilton's Hamilton
Traveler's Bar is well-known for its entertainment lineup and live
music, so be sure to check out what is happening there while you are in
town if you want to catch some live music.**
**

**Rabat Nightlife**

Along Mohammed V and Allal Ben Abdallah Avenues there are many cafes
featuring a full selection of non-alcoholic beverages and food as well
as some great ambiance. Disco clubs are a popular non-alcoholic choice
for people who enjoy dancing and socializing late into the night.
Around the Rue Patric Lumumba and the Place de Melilla streets are the
most clubs.

For cinema, the best place to catch a movie is the
downtown Cinema Renaissance on Avenue Mohamed V though most movies shown
are presented in only Arabic and French. In addition to regular
show times, cultural centers often put on film weeks throughout the year.
Cultural centers also put together many music festivals in Rabat
throughout the year. Inquire at your hotel if any cultural events are
taking place at the time of your visit.

The Dawlitz Complex has a 4-lane bowling alley and there are pool
tables at Amnesia on Rue Monastir for anyone interested in a little
gaming while out. Both venues offer specials throughout the week.

There is plenty to do when night falls in Rabat. The ample
selection of bars, cafes, clubs and other entertainment make this city a
popular location for leisure. Explore the possibilities and create a
truly memorable trip to Morocco.
