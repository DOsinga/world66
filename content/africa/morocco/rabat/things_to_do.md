---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Rabat is compact and easy. Spend a morning at the **Kasbah des Oudaya**, the walled 12th-century fort at the mouth of the Bou Regreg with blue-and-white lanes and a famous andalusian garden. Climb the **Hassan Tower**, the unfinished minaret of a mosque that would have been the world's largest in the 12th century. And take a taxi out to **Chellah**, the Roman-and-Merinid ruins in a garden full of storks, for the best single archaeological site in northern Morocco.
