The Trekking-Toubkal Team is an authentic and a reliable trekking group of very experienced Mountain Guides . All of our team members are familiar with the nature and culture of Morocco with most having grown up in the High Atlas mountains. We strive to instill respect for these mountains and take pride in doing our part to preserve their natural beauty, the way of life of its people, and its rich cultural heritage.

We offer a wide range of services including airport transfers, hotel bookings, short and easy hikes, customized trekking, sightseeing and educational tours, simple peak climbing, and complex expeditions; all according to your interest, time and choice. Whether arranging trips to meet the needs of individual clients or to satisfy the requirements of groups with special areas of interest, our accomplished team always endeavors to make every trip a tremendous one.
