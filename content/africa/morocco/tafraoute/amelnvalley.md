title: Ameln Valley
type: poi
---

A broad valley stretching northeast of Tafraoute, dotted with 26 Berber villages built into and around enormous granite boulders. The houses and mosques are brightly painted, and the people are welcoming. You could spend days exploring on foot or by mountain bike — each village has its own character, and the landscape shifts constantly between palm groves, terraced fields, and bare rock.
