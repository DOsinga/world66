A small, no-pressure gift shop near Hotel Salama, run by Houssain Laroussi. More useful as a local information hub than a shopping destination — Houssain knows the area well and can connect you with reliable walking and trekking guides. He also knows the best independent belgha (slipper) makers in town. A welcome change from the high-pressure carpet shops.
