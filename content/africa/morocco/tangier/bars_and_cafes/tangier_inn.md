type: poi
latitude: 35.7790

Small but full of character, the Tangier Inn was a favourite of the Beat writers: Burroughs, Kerouac, and Ginsberg all drank here. The atmosphere and heritage are first-class. Recommended by locals and travellers alike as a good place to sit and have a drink when the bustle of the city gets to you. Located on Rue Magellan.
