---
title: "Forbes Museum of Military Miniatures"
type: poi
---

Millionaire and press tycoon Malcolm Forbes (1919-1990) purchased the ancient palace of the mendoub in 1970. From 1978, Forbes decided to set in this palace his collection of military miniatures which he had started as a child. Today, the collection contains over 120 000 miniatures, arranged to feature famous battles of history, and was bequeathed to the city of Tangier. The garden offers an exceptional view of the Gibraltar Straits and Spanish coast.
