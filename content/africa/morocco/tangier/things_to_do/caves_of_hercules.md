---
title: "Caves of Hercules"
type: poi
category: "Sight"
latitude: 35.7613
longitude: -5.9381
---

About 14 km west of Tangier, these partly natural, partly man-made caves are the city's most popular excursion. According to legend, Hercules rested here after separating Europe from Africa. The sea-facing opening is shaped remarkably like the outline of the African continent. At high tide, waves crash into the lower chambers. Guides are available at the entrance to walk you through.
