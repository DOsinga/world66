An advantage of shopping in Taza is the fact, that it is not as overrun by tourists as the larger cities in Morocco. You can take your time and enjoy the calm and the ambiance of small town life.

At the Medina you can get what most tourists take home as souvenirs: arts and crafts or the typical Moroccan "babouches" and clothing. The main shopping streets in the Ville Nouvelle are the *Avenue Mohammed V* and the *Rue Allal Ben Abdullah*. Here you can get everything, that you need for everyday life. At the corner of the *Place d'Indépendance* there is a bookstore.
