Tiznit is a pretty town with ochre walls around the city center. Although it used to have a reputation for its whorehouses there is not much to do nowadays. 



The market is nice (the buses stop on the square opposite the market), there is an old well you can visit, but that's about it. The people are friendly enough though and the town is pretty relaxed. 

It is a stop on your way south from [Agadir](/africa/morocco/agadir) on to [Tafraoute](/africa/morocco/tafraoute) , or further south to the West-Sahara.
