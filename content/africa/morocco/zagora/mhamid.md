A small village at the edge of the Sahara, population around 4,000. The end of the road for most travellers heading south, and the main launching point for camel treks and 4×4 trips to the dunes of Erg Chigaga and Erg Lehoudi. The Sunday market is a meeting point for nomadic Sahrawi families from the surrounding desert.
