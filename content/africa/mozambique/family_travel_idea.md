title: "Family Travel Ideas in Mozambique"
type: section
---

It is a republic in southeast Africa that offers the perfect **Mozambique** **family travel**.
It’s the gateway vacation for families. With its untouched
beaches as well as pure coral reefs, this holiday destination is truly
a heaven on earth that is sure to give you the relaxation that is
desired. This country is bursting with a rich culture and heritage that
is sure to spark one's interest and intrigue others with its great architect and
marvelous sites.

**Where to Visit**

The best place to start off the family travel idea is the Ilha de
Mocambique which has been named a world heritage treasure. This architectural
treasure is located in the Nampula province which is on the northern
part of Mozambique, where you will also find numerous prehistoric
buildings that are still intact. This place is also a good place to buy
a souvenir to take back to friends who have never visited the country.
Another family travel idea is to visit the Museu Nacional de Arte as
well as Maputo, all this enhances the family day travel to this
beautiful country but it’s highly advisable to be careful as crime is
rampant in the city of Maputo.

Mozambique family travel is one of the best ways for bird viewing,
especially in a place like the Bazaruto Archipelago and also where you
want to view whales.

**Traveling There**

Families can fly into Pemba from various corners of the world. From
the airport you can travel to Mozambique Island as you leave Pemba
while passing through Nampula. The distance covered by this road is
about 400 km. For those who don’t like
using taxis there is a car rental located just a few miles from the
airport. But for those who have a tight budget for the holiday there is
always the local buses called chapas which will take you anywhere.

**Things to Do**

Ensure that you and your family get the chance to sample some for
the local cuisine and for the kids most of the local bakeries have
sweet tasting snacks that will keep them asking for more.

Mozambique has breath taking beaches that are filled with white sand
offering the perfect relaxation spot for the entire family. Water
sports such as scuba diving and water skiing are just a few of the fun
activities the whole family can do together. This is one Mozambique
family travel idea that you will never forget.
