Flights to Mozambique

Maputo:
LAM- Mozambique Airline, the national carrier and biggest airline in the country link Maputo with Johannesburg, Nairobi, Dar es Salaam, Durban and Lisbon.

Nampula / Pemba:
LAM operates 3x weekly flights to Nampula, Pemba from Dar es Salaam (Tanzania) and 2x weekly flights from Nairobi (Kenya). Flights from DAR operate every Tue, Thu and Sat and from NAIROBI operate every Tue and Sat.

The best convenient options to get to Mozambique include flying to Nairobi or Dar es Salaam and connecting with LAM flights to Mozambique.
