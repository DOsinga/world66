Maputo, formerly known as Lourenco Marques, after a portuguese explorer who landed here in 1544, is the capital of Mozambique and located all the way in th south of the country. The city is easily reached from [Swaziland](/africa/swaziland) and South Africa.


 The city was founded in the 18-th century but not much from that period remains today. The biggest historic attraction in town in the Portuguese fortress, built in 1787. A more recent landmark is the Mousinho de Albuquerque Square in the centre of town.
