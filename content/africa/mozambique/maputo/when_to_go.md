This **Maputo when to go** advisory gives you important information on Maputo&rsquo;s seasons. Knowing when to go to this Indian Ocean waterfront city will assure you a very exciting vacation. You can enjoy snorkeling and diving to explore its beautiful, lush corral reefs. You can opt to go Sports Fishing to catch your king mackerel or yellow fin tuna and kingfish. Although the best time to go to Maputo is April to October, you can still visit this place anytime of the year.

**Summer Season**

Summer time in Maputo offers the warmest and the wettest months. During summer months, the city receives 4 inches of rain, making the city hot and humid. Summer starts from December to March, with maximum temperatures during the month of December. Sometimes, the temperature reaches 41 degrees Celsius or approximately 106 degrees Fahrenheit. If you prefer hot and humid weather conditions, then you can visit and enjoy Maputo in summer.

**Winter Season**

Winter Season in Maputo is mild beginning on the months of April through September. July being the coldest month, registers a high temperature of 70 degrees Fahrenheit or 21 degrees Celsius and low temperatures of 50 degrees Fahrenheit or 10 degrees Celsius. This is the best time to visit this capital city of Mozambique. The cool nights are ideal for nightlife and the days to enjoy swimming in its beaches, and snorkeling, diving and shopping. You can also visit some of its attractions like The Fortress and the Natural Historical Museum.
