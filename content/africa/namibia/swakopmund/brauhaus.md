title: Swakopmund Brauhaus
type: poi

A proper German-style pub in the centre of town, serving Namibian lager and a menu of schnitzel, sausage, and grilled fish that hits the right note after a day on the dunes. Lively in the evenings, particularly on weekends when the Windhoek crowd is in town. The Brauhaus is where you go when you want a beer and a meal and don't need a sea view — it is good value and reliably busy.
