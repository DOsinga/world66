The place to eat Namibian food rather than seafood or German fare. Hafeni serves traditional dishes — oshifima (stiff maize porridge), kapana (grilled meat), and various stews — in a straightforward setting aimed at local diners as much as tourists. It is unpretentious and good value, and gives you a sense of what people in Swakopmund actually eat when they are not performing for visitors.
