Travel Guide

The beautiful garden town of Tsumeb is your gateway to the Etosha National Park and the north of Namibia. Places of interest are the Tsumeb Museum with artifacts from the German occupation until 1910, and the Oshikoto Lake approximately 15 km out of Tsumeb.

Enjoy traditional Namibian cuisine at a variety of Hotels, B&B's and Restaurants.

DSL Internet access in a secure climate controlled environment is available at iLogix in the center of town.
