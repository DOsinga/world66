---
title: "Tours"
type: section
---

Desert and mountain tours are the main reason most travelers come to Agadez. You can book before you leave home through specialist agencies in France or elsewhere, or arrange things locally once you arrive — local operators are generally cheaper and more flexible.

Whatever you do, don't attempt the Air Mountains or the Tenere alone. You need a guide, reliable 4WDs, proper desert equipment, and government permits. The distances are vast and rescue, if needed, is not guaranteed.

Most tours include the Air Mountains (Timia, Iférouane), the Tenere dune fields, and often time in a Tuareg camp overnight. Duration ranges from 5 to 10 days depending on how far into the desert you want to go. Camel journeys are also available for those who prefer a slower pace.

Check current security conditions before booking any desert tour. Parts of the north have seen armed activity in recent years and tour operators will know which areas are currently accessible.
