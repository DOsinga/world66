Arlit has quite some options for both food and accommodation. In most cases they are the same. You may try out any of the following addresses.

**all**

**Hotel Auberge la Caravane**

In the centre just west of the main street, this hotel ofers rooms for about 7000 CFA and a place to sleep on the terrace for 2000 CFA. Tel. 45 22 78

**Hotel Tamesna**

Just off the main street, quite a good option. Sleep on the roof for about 2000 CFA, or take a room fior about 4 times that. Tel. 45 23 30. The terrace is great and they serve cold drinks there.
