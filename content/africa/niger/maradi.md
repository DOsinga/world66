Maradi is located in the south of Niger on the main road from [Niamey](/africa/niger/niamey) to [Zinder](/africa/niger/zinder) . It's the third largest city of the country with some 60,000 inhabitants. It's mainly an agricutural centre. 







The market is the closest thing to a real tourist attraction. It's a good place for buying cheap Nigerian stuff smuggeld in. The Maison des Chefs is a good example of Hausa architecure, but if that's waht you are looking for head for [Zinder](/africa/niger/zinder) straight away.
