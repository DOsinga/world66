---
title: "Bars and Cafes"
type: section
---

Niamey's social life revolves around the Corniche de Gamkalle, a strip of open-air bars and restaurant terraces along the Niger River. The expat and NGO crowd mixes with locals here most evenings, and the atmosphere is relaxed and welcoming. Elsewhere in town, hotel bars and the Franco-Nigerien Cultural Centre round out the options.
