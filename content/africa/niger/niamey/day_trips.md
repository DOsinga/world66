---
title: "Day Trips"
type: section
---

For an interesting day trip go to the market town of [Baleyara](/africa/niger/baleyara) about 60 mi/100 km northeast of Niamey on a good paved road.

Another interesting side trip is to [Koure](/africa/niger/koure) a small town about 30 mi/50 km southeast of Niamey. Although it’s not found on many maps it is important as the center of an area roamed by a herd of giraffes.
