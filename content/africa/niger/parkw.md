The Park W is located in three countries: Niger, [Burkina Faso](/africa/burkinafaso) and [Benin](/africa/benin) . It is most easily accesed form Niger however. Tours can be arranged in [Niamey](/africa/niger/niamey) . The Park du Double Vé is one of the best wildlife parks of West Africa. Lions, giraffes and a lot more. The best time to come is March-May at the end of the dry season, when the animals have to look around for watering holes.
