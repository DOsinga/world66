The Ténéré is 500 km north of [Agadez](/africa/niger/agadez) and about 300 km north of the [Aïr Mountains](/africa/niger/airmountains). It has incredibly beautiful sand dune areas, and anyone disappointed by their first desert experiences should absolutely come here — this is the real thing.

This is genuinely remote territory, so plan on at least a week starting from [Agadez](/africa/niger/agadez). Travel in a convoy of cars and hire at least one guide. The road to Bilma — not always a road in the western sense of the word — crosses the Ténéré from west to east and offers the best opportunities to explore this stunning region.
