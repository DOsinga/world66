Tillaberi is located on the road from [Niamey](/africa/niger/niamey) to [Gao](/africa/mali/gao). Most travelers stopping here are probably hurrying from one to the other. There are not many reasons to stay longer than necessary here. The main attraction is the possibility of spotting wild giraffes on the river banks south of town — though the main West African giraffe herd has shifted to the area around [Koure](/africa/niger/koure) further south. Ask locally whether any have been spotted nearby recently.


 Tillaberi is also one of Nigers biggest rice growing areas. Sunday is the market day and if you happen to pass by on that day it is worthwhile to stay and have a look.
