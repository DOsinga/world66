A reliable Indian restaurant in Garki with a good-value lunch buffet and an a la carte dinner menu. The tandoori dishes are the kitchen's strength. A solid option if you have been eating Nigerian food all week and want something different, or if you simply want a predictable meal in a part of town where restaurant quality can be uneven.
