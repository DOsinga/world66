title: "Eagle Square"
type: poi
category: Sight
latitude: 9.0579
longitude: 7.4891
---

Nigeria's main outdoor ceremonial space, used for presidential inaugurations and national celebrations. The square itself is a large open plaza flanked by government buildings, and while there's not much to do here on a quiet day, it's the symbolic heart of the capital and worth a brief visit if you're in the Central District.
