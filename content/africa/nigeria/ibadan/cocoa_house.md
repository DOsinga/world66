The first skyscraper built in Africa and a distinctive feature of Ibadan's skyline. Once the tallest building in tropical Africa, it remains a symbol of the city's ambition. The building houses several restaurants and the Cocoa Dome, which serves American and European cuisine. Worth a visit for the history and the views.
