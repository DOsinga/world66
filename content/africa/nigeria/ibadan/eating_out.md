---
title: "Eating Out"
type: section
---

Ibadan has a good range of places to eat, from fast food chains to local bukas (inexpensive eateries serving home-style Nigerian food). The local cuisine leans heavily on Yoruba staples like amala, pounded yam, and soups.

#### Mr. Biggs

A popular Nigerian fast food chain with several locations in Ibadan. Good for quick meals: meat pies, donuts, chicken dishes, and local options like asaro (yam porridge) and fried plantain. Affordable and reliable.

#### Big Treats

Two locations in Ibadan, one on Alaafin Avenue in Oluyo Estate and another in Bodija. Serves both local and international dishes including pasta, sandwiches, burgers, pizza, and seafood.

#### Tantalizers

Another well-known Nigerian chain with seven branches in Ibadan. A favorite hangout among locals, serving salads, chips, chicken dishes, and burgers. Casual and inexpensive.

#### Bisi Restaurant

A step up from the fast food options, located in Kakanfo Inn on Nihinlola Street, Ring Road. Serves Indian, European, American, and Nigerian cuisine in a more formal sit-down setting.
