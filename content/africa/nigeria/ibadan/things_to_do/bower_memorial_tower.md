---
title: "Bower Memorial Tower"
type: poi
category: "Sight"
latitude: 7.3876
longitude: 3.8878
---

The highest monument in Ibadan, visible from across the city. Climb to the top for the best panoramic view of the sprawling cityscape. The tower was raised in honor of British Captain Robert Bower, who served as Resident and Traveling Commissioner for the area and earned considerable local respect. Open during the day all week.
