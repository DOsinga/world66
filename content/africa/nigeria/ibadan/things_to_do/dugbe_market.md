With more than 200 stalls, Dugbe is Ibadan's great market experience. Each line of stalls sells a different commodity, from food and traditional medicine to clothes and household goods. The smells of cooking spices and the sound of spirited bargaining can be overwhelming at first, but the color and energy make it essential. Go in the morning for the best atmosphere.
