title: Shere Hills
type: poi

The Shere Hills rise on the southern edge of Jos — rounded granite outcrops typical of the plateau landscape. The walking isn't strenuous, but the views across the surrounding country are good and the streams and small dams make it an agreeable place for a picnic. A half-day is plenty. Go in the morning before it gets warm.
