The busiest (although sometimes quite rowdy) nightclub in Kaduna is The Stallion, located above the main grandstand of the Kaduna racetrack. It is a very friendly place - full of VERY obliging local ladies. A good time is guaranteed.

### [NAF Club](/africa/nigeria/kaduna/nightlife_and_ente/naf_club)

The **NAF club** is located along Rabah Road of Ali Alkali Road.

The Club offers good accomodation, a lively Night Club, outside and inside bars, with two giant TV screens in the outside bar.

At weekends the place rocks and rolls with live bands, giving out a uniquely African beat, the ladies are obliging & friendly.

Be careful when you leave, as it has ben reported that car thieves sometimes target late night revellers coming out of the club with their cars.

### [Uncle Sams Place](/africa/nigeria/kaduna/nightlife_and_ente/uncle_sams_place)

Situated in the heart of kaduna,just behind the New Nigerian Newspaper Building,in a quiet and clean environment ,open air garden bar with smooth music, bar-b-que fish,chicken and all sorts of food to accompany a large variety of alchoholic and non-alchoholic beverages.This is where you need to hang out after a hectic day at the office.....or entertaining down-to-earth guests,who love nature.
