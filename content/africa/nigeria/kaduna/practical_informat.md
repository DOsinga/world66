**Sports facilities**

**Kaduna Rugby Union Football Club**, is situated off Waff Road (renamed Buhari Road) on Murtalla Square near to the Kaduna Club and the Crocodile Club.

The Club has an active Rugby team, that plyas several times a month according to the national fixtures.

The members are mixed expatriates (mostly British & Europeans) and Nigerians.

The club dates back to 1956.

The Club offers bar and restaurant facilities, visitor cab be signed in by members and can become temporay members for the period of their visit.

Club has satellite television, Pool table, table tennis, and is open from 5pm on weekdays sand 12 noon at weekends and public holidays.

**Kaduna Polo Club** is situated on the opposite side of Murtalla Square, and offers Polo Players playing facilities also has bar and restaurant.

It is one of the most pestigious Clubs in Northern Nigeria, and during competitions Polo Players from all over the world come over to play, members include past presidents of Nigeria.
