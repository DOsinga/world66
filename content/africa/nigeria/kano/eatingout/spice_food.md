Located in the area of the tourist center, it provides easy access
to good food while staying over in Kano. It in also one of the best
places to eat out. The food is clean, tasty and the service is friendly
and efficient. It serves mainly one of the best South Asian cooking in
Northern Nigeria.

Open from 10 am till midnight, it is one of the few places in Kano where you can order dinner at a late hour.

Ask any taxi, or kabo kabo (motorcycle taxis) to take you to "tourist camp" in Bompai for a quick trip to good eating.
