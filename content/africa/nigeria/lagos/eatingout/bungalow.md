BUNGALOW RESTAURANT is a unique category of restaurants, offering higher-quality service and food than existing chain or independent restaurants. We believe that this niche is a dynamic growth area within the restaurant industry that offers significant growth opportunities for BUNGALOW RESTAURANT . We believe that our restaurants are uniquely positioned as "destination restaurants" that attract a very loyal clientele.
BUNGALOW RESTAURANT owns and operates a chain of mid-priced, full-service, casual dining restaurants that serve mesquite grilled steaks, ribs, chicken, and fish. Our restaurants embrace a western-style concept featuring Western Modern artefacts and music. The exciting and vibrant atmosphere created by the Bungalow ambiance is enhanced by specially selected upbeat country western music and neon signs.
The decor includes planked wooden floors, "dim lighting," Western memorabilia, all of which enhance the casual dining experience and establish a distinct identity for our concept. BUNGALOW RESTAURANT is further distinguished by its high quality, choice-graded steaks which are hand-cut fresh daily at each restaurant and mesquite grilled to order, meals are served in generous "Western sized" portions under the watchful eyes of our chef Cook Mikel as for your drink treat, the full taste and the real feeling of the best show a bartender can create in Nigeria , mixed with the best choice of liquor and bar service, is available behind our best loaded bar carfully handled by our expatriate bartender MARK
BUNGALOW RESTAURANT will start operating its first restaurant in October 2004 in Victoria Island Lagos Nigeria. In 2006, Bungalow Restaurant will become a public company with 8 chain restaurant opened nationwide.
BUNGALOW RESTAURANT believes that excellence in operations and quality of food and service, ambiance, location and price-value relationship are keys to success in the restaurant industry. We believe that we differentiate our restaurants by emphasizing the following strategic elements:

Positioning in the mid-priced, full service casual dining segment of the restaurant industry.

Nigerian's consumed an estimated 64.4 pounds of beef per person on a per capita basis in 2002 and steak continues to be one of the most frequently ordered dinner entrees at restaurants.

We believe that a limited menu, concentrating primarily on high quality choice-grade steaks, distinguishes our restaurants at a time when other chains are expanding their menus and losing their distinctiveness.

The popular brand of BUNGALOW RESTAURANT provides a unique and enduring attraction to a broad and diverse demographic and social-economic mix of guests in the 15 and over age groups.

Generous "Bungalow-sized" portions offered at moderate prices.

High quality and attentive service with each waitperson generally being assigned to no more than three tables at dinner to ensure guest satisfaction.

Consistent high quality products through careful ingredient selection, food preparation and aging of steaks.
