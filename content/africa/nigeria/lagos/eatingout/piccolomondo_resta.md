Piccolo Mondo is a celebration of international cuisines from different cultures and regions of the world. Offering an elegant décor in luxurious comfort, with excellent service and a charming setting. Our upstairs lounge is the ideal gathering place for friends or business associate with a full service bar including a gorgeous selection of Italian and French wines.

The menu is filled with a grand selection of authentic delectable dishes that will take you on fabulous culinary journey to a far away land. From the delicious healthy salads of the Far East to the spicy, robust flavors of India to the hearty pastas of Italy to the wonderful, rich traditional Lebanese dishes to the delicate delights of French deserts. It is but a dream to be able to savor such dishes in one place

Our upstairs menu has a selection of delicious appetizers, incredible sandwiches/burgers and scrumptious crispy salads for the perfect lunch with the gang.

Each day for lunch, our professional chefs prepare a full course “plat du jour” to temp the limits of every palette.

Catering, take away and delivery available

Open 7 Days a week.

(Kitchen closes at around 11:00) Reservation advisable
