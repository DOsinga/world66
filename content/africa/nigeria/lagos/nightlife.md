**Place to go outs**

**The Motherlan'**

Showcase of an African variation of mid tempo jazz. Owned by a Nigherian Artist called Lagbaja, a brilliant saxophonist known for performing on stage with local masquerade style masks, this is a brilliant place to go and is frequented by foriegners.

address: Opebi Road, Ikeja

openinghours: Every Last Friday Nite of the Month

**Afrika Shrine**

Run by Femi Kuti (son of Fela), this is a great rock temple. The best night to go there is sunday. When Femi is outside of the country the place can be rather empty, however, so you might want to check if he is around. The Shrine is located in a quiet street in Ikeja.

**Bar Beach**
