title: Things to Do
type: section
---

Sightseeing is not why you come to Lagos. Most travellers end up doing a mix: a half-day at the **Nigerian National Museum** on Lagos Island, a morning wander through **Freedom Park** (the former colonial prison now a cultural centre), a ferry out to **Tarkwa Bay** or a lazy afternoon at the **Lekki Conservation Centre** canopy walk — and then they spend the evenings on what Lagos really does, which is live music, clubs, and food. Ask where the night is happening; it changes.
