### [Ifeanyi Anadu](/africa/nigeria/nnewi/getting_around/ifeanyi_anadu)

With the good network of roads embarked upon by the current state administration, private investors are now into car hire service for easy movement in addition to state sponsored yellow taxis. You have the popular motorcycle taxi (OKADA) for very brisk movement. Seventy percent of the populace in the town have one form of transpotation.
