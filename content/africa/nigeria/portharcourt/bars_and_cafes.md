---
title: "Bars and Cafes in Port Harcourt"
type: section
---

While you travel to Nigeria you will find that the **Port Harcourt Bars and** **Cafes** are the most exciting and entertaining the country has to offer. This city is a primary resort area in Nigeria and the exquisite bars here can attest to that. Tourists that come here have the opportunity to relax and kick back while having a few drinks with friends or business associates in some special establishments. No matter what your tastes in bars or cafes are here in Port Harcourt there is a place to satisfy you.
**

Bars and Cafes**
**Cheers Bar
**
Located in the center of town, this famous bar is a real attraction. The size of the establishment is humongous and has bars inside and outside as well. Cheers is owned by a Britain which is why it has such a U.K. atmosphere to it. There are various ways you can be entertained here, from the girls to the pool tables and a disco. The business is open twenty-four hours a day, seven days a week so you can go anytime you desire.
**

Liquid Lounge and Bar
**
This well known establishment was started as a place where distinguished people with flair for upper style and dress could come together. It is a great place for visitors to check out while they have a drink with the local folk. The atmosphere is really laid-back and is especially friendly to tourists.
**

Night Box
**
Although the Night Box is a newly built bar, it has acquired a steady stream of patrons since its opening at the beginning of 2010. If you are into a more high-intensity atmosphere to have a drink, this is the place for you. There is always a live DJ playing modern tunes and it has a second story that allows for a more intimate interaction.

**Other Predominate Bars and Cafes
**
In addition to those bars mentioned above, you can check out the following bars to have a drink after a hard day at work or play. The Goodfellas is a drinking hole designated mostly for the rowdy type of crowd, or you can see for yourself why The Stellars Bar seems to be a favorite among tourists. And the Toby’s Jug is certain to offer an exciting time for the adventurous. A bar that is a preferred choice for the really mellow drinker is the Blues Café, located in the Blues Café Hotel.
