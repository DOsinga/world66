**Port Harcourt family travel **will ensure that the whole family
enjoys themselves during their holiday.

**Hotel Novotel **

This hotel is the ideal place to stay while in Port Harcourt. Your
family will be provided with all the amenities required during your
stay. Free bed and breakfast for your 2 children aged below 16 years is
one of the offers available. Some of the facilities and services offered
are: family rooms, balanced diet, child safe playing areas, welcome
gift and outdoor amusement park.

**Zoological Garden**

Port Harcourt family travel should include visiting this vast natural
wildlife habitat. Here you will find different species of apes,
elephants, giraffes and many other exotic birds and animals. This is the
ideal place to take those memorable family and nature pictures.

**Port Harcourt Tourist Beach**

This beach offers your family the chance to have an enjoyable day.
Take a swim in the ocean or indulge in the various water sports
available at this beach. You can also opt to get a suntan or take a walk
along the beach. Various establishments located nearby offer cold
drinks, food and snacks.

**Isaac Boro Park**

The park is located in the heart of Port Harcourt city. Here you can
enjoy a picnic with your family in these quiet and beautiful
surroundings. It is also considered a consecrated area by the locals.
Make sure you carry some binoculars for some bird viewing.

**Bonny Island**

A visit to this island which is among the places with the most oil in
Nigeria, is a worthwhile experience. Here you will find most of the
multinational oil companies have set up their refineries. The
expatriates who work at these companies also have favorite beaches that are ideal places to take your family. The island's small town also has restaurants, fast food joints
and supermarkets.
