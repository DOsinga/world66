title: "Getting Around in Port Harcourt"
type: section
---

**Port Harcourt transportation** is readily available for any
traveler touring this Nigerian oil town.

**Buses**

This is the main Port Harcourt transportation that
is readily available. Bus companies operating include ABC Transport,
Agofure Transport, Chisco Transport, Ekene Dili Chukwu Transport,
Greenerline Transport, Cross Country Transport, ITC Transport and RTC
Transport. It will depend if you want to use the local services or hire a
bus for private tours. The local tourist office will direct you to
their various offices.

**Motorcycle Taxis**

This country
is famous for its motorcycle taxis that can be found nearly everywhere
and charge very fair prices to get one to their destination.

**Taxis**

These
are readily available and they also charge reasonably but make sure you
ask the local tourist office about the fares. Women taxi drivers (ESI
Lady Cabbies) have also started operating and they could be your better
option when it comes to hiring a taxi.

**Ferries**

Ferry
services provide cruises to places near the city. The Port Harcourt -
Bakana Cruise is one of them. There is also the Port Harcourt to Boony
Cruise, Port Harcourt to Buguma Cruise and Port Harcourt to Okirika
Cruise.

**Car Hire**

Hiring a car is another good Port
Harcourt transportation mode that you should consider. Many reliable
rental agencies can be found in the city. Europcar have their offices at
Port Harcourt Novotel on 2 Lord Emmanuel Drive. Avis Car Hire and Hertz
also have offices in the city. You can get all kinds of makes and
models to suit your requirements.Port Harcourt transportation
depends on your destination and budget.
