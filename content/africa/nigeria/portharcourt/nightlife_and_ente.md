You will get a lot of entertainment in **Port Harcourt nightlife.**
There are many nightspots to choose from depending on your taste of
music and crowd.

**D's Place**

This is the place to spend a nice evening if you like having wine.
The place has a cool atmosphere and not very noisy. It is the place to
start before you hit the night clubs.

**Night Box Nightclub**

This fairly new nightclub has good music with modern equipment. The
decor and lighting match well and it has an upper floor where you can
dance the night away. The various bar counters available serve a variety
of spirits and beers (Local or international). If you feel hungry, try
out the Nigerian cuisine available in the restaurant. Also try out the
Shawarma sandwich which is an Middle East delicacy. This club is on King
Perekuele Road.

**Cheers Bar**

Port Harcourt nightlife to this bar is an exciting and fun thing to
do. Owned by a British expatriate, you will the setting very unique and
quite spacious. There are 3 bars plus an outdoor and indoor dance floor.
The restaurant serves a variety of dishes and you will get all kinds of
beers and cocktails available. They also have friendly and helpful
staff. You will also get to meet new friends.

**Casablanca**

A fun Port Harcourt nightlife is making sure you visit this place.
Here you will get very modern and good music and the clientele really
enjoy themselves. There is a dance floor with numerous bars available.
The resident DJ does not disappoint.

**Wine Bar**

It is another place that is classy and neat. You can have your drinks
here where it is not crowded. You will find nearly all kinds of brands
available here. Try out this nice joint.

**Protea Hotel **

If you are looking for a classy place to
spend your evening make sure you head to this fine hotel located on
PH/ABA Express road. You will find a nice restaurant which will have
very tasty and various cuisines to choose from. The place also has two
elegant bars to choose from. You can enjoy your night at the bar
listening to the cool soothing music.

**Barracuda Restaurant
& Bar **Another** **Port Harcourt nightlife place to
visit is this bar & grill which offers an atmosphere of fun and good
music. The crowd at this place is fantastic. This place sometimes hosts
live shows by local artists so you may be lucky to find a night when it
is happening. It is located at GRA phase 2.
