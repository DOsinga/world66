---
title: "Shopping in Port Harcourt"
type: section
---

**Port Harcourt**** shopping** will allow you to get many nice and memorable things.

**Supermarkets**

Most of these establishments open at 8.00 a.m. and close at 10.00
p.m. There are numerous supermarkets to choose from. Park N Shop,
Choice Supermarket and Chanrai Supermarket are located on Aba Road.
Others include the Mall Shopping Complex found just opposite Shell's Residential Complex. At Rumuola Junction you will find De Giant
Supermarket, and Nik Nak Super Mart. If you are in G.R.A Phase 2 you
can go to Everyday Supermarket or Well Done Supermarket. These
supermarkets have many items all under a single roof.

**Fruit/Garden Market**

Shopping in an open air market is an experience that
you should try. Located on Kaduna Street, you will get all kinds of
fresh fruits and vegetables of your choice. It is usually open at 8.00
a.m. and closes at 7.00 p.m.

**Mr. Bigg's**

Here you will get various varieties of fast foods available to your
taste. The main establishment is on Azikiwe Road and the other branches
can be found at Rumuola Junction and the Shell Residential Complex.

**Bonny Island**

While in Bonny Island, Port Harcourt shopping can be done at various
establishments. Joneks Superstores, found on Hospital Road, offers all
kinds of items and also has a pharmacy. At Best Way Supermarket, you
can purchase wine and all kinds of provisions. For frozen food and
packed food, try Dammy Supermarket found on Pepple Road. Joyful Variety
Store also has a wide range of products on sale including cocktail
drinks, wines and beer.

**Open Air Markets**

Port Harcourt shopping is a must in these open air markets. You will
be able to get items like the traditional Nigerian African wear which
is very popular around the world. Mile 1 Market is a place where you
may get various African attire. It is open from 6.00 a.m. up to 9.00
p.m. Another place is Creek Road Market and Mile 3 market
where you can get fruits and vegetables.

**Electronics**

Port Harcourt shopping for electronics items is easy as there are
various shops available dealing with all makes and models. Bhojson
Electronics & Co., located at Azikiwe road, has various electronic
goods available such as rechargeable batteries for your digital camera.
Adamas Digital will have anything to do with computer-related
electronics and is located at Aba Road.

**Shoes**

One Port Harcourt shopping experience not to be missed is getting
yourself those Nigerian sandals. Visit Chonotex NIG Ltd on Rumuola Road
to find your perfect pair.
