**Taking a Family Trip to Warri**

You will be pleasantly surprised to discover that a good number of travelers select **Warri ****family travel** options. Although it is primarily known for its major commercial seaport and well-developed palm oil industry, Warri does offer some nice family travel possibilities. You should consider taking advantage of them. Along with all the hotels and restaurants offering comfort to families, here are some of the attraction you may find interesting for a family visit.

#### **Visit the ****Abraka**** ****Beach**

The whole family will enjoy having a trip to the nearby Abraka Beach. Abraka is a small town located just a 30-minute drive away from Warri. Its beach is famous particularly for its natural flowing spring water. It offers many fun outdoor activities. These include fishing, canoeing, swimming and picnicking. The beach is visited by many tourists as well as by the younger part of the local residents. They are friendly, so the whole family will feel comfortable while relaxing here.

#### **Effurun**** ****Garden**** ****Park**

The Effurun Garden Park is another nice place you can visit with your family while staying at Warri. The park is kept in perfect condition. The green areas are mowed well and covered by an abundance of lovely palm trees. Several short trails are available for walks with your family. Here, you can all have a long and relaxing walk in an undisturbed environment and enjoy many wonderful moments together.
