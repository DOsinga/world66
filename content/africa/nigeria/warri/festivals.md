---
title: "Festivals"
type: section
---

The Niger Delta communities surrounding Warri have a rich tradition of festivals. The Okiroro (Okere) Festival, celebrated by the Okere Urhobo people in July or August, is a major ancestral festival featuring masquerades and communal gathering. Boat regatta festivals on the delta waterways are a regular feature of the region's calendar, with decorated canoes, racing, and music drawing participants from across the communities. Dates vary; asking at your hotel or the local tourism office is the best way to find out what is happening during your visit.
