---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

Warri is served by Osubi Airport (Warri Airport), with domestic flights connecting to Lagos, Abuja, and Port Harcourt. The city is on the main road between Lagos (about 400 km west) and Port Harcourt (about 200 km east), and is reachable by long-distance bus. Warri also has a river port and is connected to other Delta communities by speedboat — the river routes are how much of the local population moves around.
