---
title: "Shopping"
type: section
---

Warri's main markets are the best place to shop. Igbudu Market has tailors and seamstresses producing custom clothing on request, along with leather goods and fabric. Ogunu Market is a produce market with a good range of local ingredients. The Ibo Market sells clothing, shoes, and household goods. Petty theft is a concern in crowded market areas; keep valuables secure and be aware of your surroundings.
