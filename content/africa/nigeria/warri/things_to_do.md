In **Warri, things to do **range from catching a soccer match to exploring the Nigerian delta. Warri is river town that has become famous for its oil reserves, which attract ex-pats from all over the world work to work in the Nigerian oil industry.

**Sports**

In Warri there are several different soccer teams in that play in the national leagues. The city's stadium that has hosted such prestigious events as the International Women&rsquo;s Football Tournaments. Sport enthusiasts can join the locals for a game and a beer while visiting Warri.

**Beaches**

Warri is a river town at the edge of the Atlantic Ocean. There are number of beautiful white sand beaches to explore, top among them being Abraka Beach, a popular destination for both tourists and residents. The beach is unique in that it features nature flowing spring water. Canoeing and hiking are two of the many activities in this area that are encouraged by local tour guides.

**City Tours**

The city of Warri has a number of different sites that every visitor will want to take in. Just roaming the city on foot is a great experience. Start with a cup of coffee at the Uwie Modern Market and continue to the Palace of Olu of Warri. To cover more ground quicker, a motor tour is a great option and can be arranged by a hotel guide.
