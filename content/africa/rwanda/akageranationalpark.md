Akagera National Park is located in the east of [Rwanda](/africa/rwanda). Kibungu is the city that is nearest to the park and the best starting point. 

The park covers over 2500 sq km of savannah west of the Kagera River, which denotes the frontier with [Tanzania](/africa/tanzania). The park has a variety of wildlife and is a habitat for over 500 different species of birds. There are accommodation facilities on the edge of the park at Gabiro, 100km (60 miles) to the north. It is best not to visit the park in the rainy season (December, March and April) since many of the routes become impassable.
