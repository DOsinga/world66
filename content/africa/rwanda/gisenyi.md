Gisenye is located in the west of the country and van be considered the main centre for excursions in the [Volcanoes national park](/africa/rwanda/volcanoesnationalpark). Plane trips can be made from here to view the craters. Situated on the north of Lake Kivu, it also offers many opportunities for water sports or for excursions on the lake.
