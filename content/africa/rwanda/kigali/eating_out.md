Kigali offers 2 types of eateries: international restaurants catering mostly to foreigners and local establishments serving brochettes and buffets. Street food is non existent.

Kigali's restaurants are some of the best in the region, but you will have to pay for the pleasure as most places charge 10 USD (in 2011) per main dish. Luckily local places are fairly cheap, but expect to wait more than one hour to get your food. Lunch buffets are plentiful and fast in downtown Kigali and cost around 3 USD (in 2011).

### [Beijing Tangren Restaurant](/africa/rwanda/kigali/eating_out/beijing_tangren_re)

This place seems to be the most popular Chinese restaurant in town. Service is also some of the fastest in Kigali. It's on the airport road in Remera, just beside RDB.

### [Flamingo Restaurant](/africa/rwanda/kigali/eating_out/flamingo_restauran)

This Chinese restaurant doesn't attract the local Chinese expat community, but it is a pleasant setting for a quiet dinner. It's just near Africa Bite Restaurant up from Papyrus in Kimihurura.

type: Chinese



address: Kimihurura

PriceOfMenu: 6,000

### [Royal Garden Restaurant](/africa/rwanda/kigali/eating_out/royal_garden_resta)

This Chinese restaurant is probably the cheapest in Kigali, offering Chinese and Indian dishes. It is also the only Chinese restaurant downtown (in 2011) and does delivery at lunch if you are stuck at the office. Although the chef is Chinese, this place is often empty.

type: Chinese



tel: 250-5805

address: Kiyovu, opposite Banque National Rwanda

PriceOfMenu: 4,500

### [Lalibela](/africa/rwanda/kigali/eating_out/lalibela)

Lalibela offers an excellent Ethiopian fare at a decent price. It's best to go with another person so you can share a platter. It is located five minutes walk from Papyrus in Kimihurura. Go up the road from Papyrus and take your first right and then your first left.

type: Ethiopian



address: Kimihurura

PriceOfMenu: 5,000

### [Khana Khazana](/africa/rwanda/kigali/eating_out/khana_khazana)

Khana Khazana has the reputation as the best Indian restaurant in Kigali. It is more expensive than the others, but the food is awesome. It is located in Kiovu, 5 minute walk from Milles Collines hotel. Go towards Banque National Rwanda, and it will be the second left.

type: Indian and Pakistani



address: Kiovu

PriceOfMenu: 7,000

### [Zaafran](/africa/rwanda/kigali/eating_out/zaafran)

Zaafran has excellent Indian food at cheaper prices than Khana Khazana, and both are in Kiyovu. Indian food is one of the fastest options for eating out in Kigali, so it makes for a good choice at lunch. Zaafran is at the beginning of the Canadian Embassy road near town.

type: Indian and Pakistani



address: Rue de L'Akegera, Kiyovu

PriceOfMenu: 5,000

### [Sol e Luna](/africa/rwanda/kigali/eating_out/sol_e_luna)

There is some dispute over which pizza is the best in Kigali. Sol e Luna is one of the contenders. It has a great view, and great service. Try going on Monday nights for quiz night at 8:30. You can join all of Kigali's anglophone community trying to win free dinner. Ask to join any table that has less than 7 players. Sol e Luna is on the road to the Airport just before the Remera roundabout.

type: Italian



address: Gishushu near RDB Offices

PriceOfMenu: 6,000

### [Papyrus](/africa/rwanda/kigali/eating_out/papyrus)

UPDATE: Sadly this place has closed but the owner has opened another restaurant and bar around the corner.

This is one of the best pizza's in town. And has the added bonus of turning into a dance bar on the weekend. Ask any moto taxi and they will be able to take you to Papyrus. Service is friendly and fairly quick.

type: Italian



address: Kimihurura

PriceOfMenu: 5,500
