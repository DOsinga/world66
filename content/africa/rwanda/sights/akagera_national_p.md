Head East from Kigali to find one of the cheapest and less well-known wildlife parks of East Africa. Not quite the big five, but if you're lucky and go early in the day, you could see Elephants, Hippo, Zebra, Giraffe, Antelope, Baboon, etc. Worth a visit in dry season.
