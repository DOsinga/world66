Muslim feast commemorating Abraham' s willingness to sacrifice his son in obedience to God. Each Muslim head of family sacrifices a sheep.

June **

  Magal. Muslim pilgrimage to Touba, the birthplace of Muridism, a Senegalese Muslim Sufi brotherhood.   

**
