The Senegalese capital has some of West Africa’s best nightlife. Lively discos and nightclubs are jammed after 11 pm most evenings. The French Cultural Center off the Ponty is a good source of information for events held at the center and elsewhere throughout the city. 

**Recommendations**

**Keur Samba**

A jazzy atmosphere and rather popular. Address: 13, rue Jules Ferry.

**Ozio**
