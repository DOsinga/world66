### [WorldzAfrica](/africa/senegal/getting_around/worldzafrica)

Taxi's are always a good cheap way to get around. You can go easily from town to town for about 2,000-3,000 cfa ($4-6) but you have to barter. The drivers will start at a high price (about $10 for us) but you can usually get it down pretty quickly.

If you want a real cheap and very African way to get around; try a rapid. They are big colorful buses that the locals will cram into. They stop at several key areas around each town as well as travel between.
