### [Historical museum](/africa/senegal/iledegoree/sights/historical_museum)

### [Le castel](/africa/senegal/iledegoree/sights/le_castel)

Sitting up on the only hill this fortified complex dominates Ile de
Goree. A number of fortifications from different time periods, le
Castel offers magnificent views of dakar's skyline. The bunkers of the
fortress are now inhabited by a religious sect.

type: Castles



[[rate it]](/africa/senegal/iledegoree/sights/le_castel)

### [Musée des esclaves](/africa/senegal/iledegoree/sights/muse_des_esclave)

This museum is dedicated to all the slaves that were shiped trough Ile de Gorée to the new world.

type: Memorials
