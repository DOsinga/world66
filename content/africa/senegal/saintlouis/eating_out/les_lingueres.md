A popular spot near the northern tip of the island with a riverside terrace looking out toward the Pont Faidherbe. The menu leans toward French-Senegalese fusion — fish dishes done with a lighter touch than the heavier rice-based local cooking. A bit more expensive than the local options, but the setting is hard to beat in the evening when the bridge is lit up.
