Touba is located some 200 km inland from [Dakar](/africa/senegal/dakar). It's a pretty stange city. The first thing that strikes you is the number of Mercedeses you see driving up and down the streets. Touba is the centre of the Mourid Sect, the most powerful islamic brotherhood of Senegal. This brotherhood runs one of the best organized business imperiums of the whole of Africa. They control a number of plants and factories in Senegal; but the Senegalese guys in the street who sell (fake) Rolexes you find in [New York](/northamerica/unitedstates/newyorkstate/newyork), [Paris](/europe/france/paris) or [Rome](/europe/italy/lazio/rome) are also part of their network. A percentage of the money they make flows back to Touba. And then the leadres of the brotherhood buy a new Mercedes.


 The main attraction of town is Touba’s Grand Mosque. It is the largest in the country and has minarets over 80 m tall, with wonderful views of the town and countryside. 


 You might want to avoid Touba during Magal, the religious pilgrimage that draws hordes of people to the mosque. It is pretty spectacular though, but getting a room is close to impossible and getting rest in completely impossible. Prepare for the worst. And then some.
