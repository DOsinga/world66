This restaurant is in an old Creole house on the outskirts of Victoria, on Mahe. Try their delicious family-style Creole meal of parrotfish, tuna steak, barracuda, fish stew, chicken curry, battered aubergine, salad and rice. Over coffee, Mama and her Sri Lankan born daughter may come and sit with you for an interesting chat about food, travel, and world politics*. (Contributed by Howard Banwell) *
