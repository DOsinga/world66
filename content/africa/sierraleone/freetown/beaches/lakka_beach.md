title: "Lakka Beach"
type: poi
latitude: 8.3915
longitude: -13.2639
---

Halfway down the peninsula toward River No. 2, Lakka Beach is a good compromise — less remote than the beaches further south, but quieter than Lumley and reachable in under an hour from Freetown. The sand is clean, the water is warm, and there are a few simple places to eat nearby.
