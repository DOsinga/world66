---

Air

The national airline is *Somali Airlines.*

Approximate flight time

From Mogadishu to *London *is 15 hours flying first to Dubai and then onto London with a stopover in Djibouti.

International airport

*Mogadishu (MGQ)* is 6km (4 miles) west of the city. There is a taxi service to the city centre.

Departure tax

The equivalent of US$20. Transit passengers and children under two years are exempt.

Sea

The principal ports are Mogadishu Kismayu Berbera and Marka. The *Norwegian American Line* operates a passenger service to Mogadishu.

Road

There are routes to Somalia from Djibouti and Kenya. There is no border crossing with Ethiopia at present. Roads are underdeveloped and travel requires suitable 4-wheel-drive desert vehicles.
