Lasaod International
Airport

The
city is home to Lasanod International Airport, with flights
to Addis Ababa, Djibouti City, Dubai and many other cities across Africa, Somalia and all around the world . All
foreigners are required to exchange 50 US Dollars to local currency (which is the Somali Shilling.
(1 USD=4000 Somali Shillings in Open market as of Nov. 2008, Official rate is
almost half of the open market). There is a bus service in lasanod.
