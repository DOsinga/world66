Dacarbudhuq is the largest town on the road between [Hargeisa](/africa/somaliland/hargeisa) and [Berbera](/africa/somaliland/berbera), sitting on the main highway that connects Somaliland's capital to its principal port. Most travellers pass through without stopping, but the town has a certain significance — a monument to Omar Kujoog commemorates his defence of Dacarbudhuq against the Italian advance towards Hargeisa during the colonial era.
