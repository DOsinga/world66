Travel Guide

Arabsiyo is a beautiful city in between Hargeisa and Gabiley. Arab is a location with a lot of farming where various fruit and vegetables grow. Honeymoon makers go there to take photos of themselves in its dry valley. It is a tourism area for Somaliland as Camp David is to America. There is a restaurant, Dalxiis by name ('Dalxiis' is a Somali word which means tourism). Your visit there once will never slip of your mind. you would remember the sweet fruit and vegetables, the beautiful scenery, the photos you have taken, the hospitable people you met, the fresh air you inhaled, the neatly-organised gardens you strolled through, and so on and so forth. Make Arabsiyo your favourite city.
