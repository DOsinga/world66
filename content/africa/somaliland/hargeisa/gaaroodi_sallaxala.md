Travel Guide

** caasimada gobalka gaaroodi ee sallaxlay waa maagaalo dagan jawi **

**nasasho leh waxaaxyna hawdka ka xigtaa casimada somaliland ee **

**hargiesa waa magaalo dadka dagani is jecelyahiin intbadana ku dadaalna **

**sidii ay hormar u gaadhi lahayeen taas oo ka muuqata bilicda maagada oo **

**imika leh soo jiidasho xad dhaafa magaaaladu waxay gancsi leedahay **

**magaalyin ka mida itoobiya sidaa daroor iyo gobalada ogadeenka ee **

**aadka u fog fog si kastaba ha ahaada magaalada salaxalay waa magaalo **

**ku talaabsaday hormar balaadhan mudooyinkan u danbeeyay**
