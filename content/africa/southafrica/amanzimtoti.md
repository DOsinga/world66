The Amanzimtoti region runs along the Sapphire Coast in the province of Kwazulu Natal.

The beaches are great for swimming, walking, surfing, kite surfing, wind surfing and fishing.

The town in the area are Umbogintwini, Amanzintoti, Doonside, Warner Beach, Astra Park , Winkle spruit and Illovo Beach.
