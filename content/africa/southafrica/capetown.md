title: "Cape Town"
type: location
---

Cape Town has the most beautiful of settings, on the coast with the flat top Tafelberg behind the city. Combine that with a very nice climate and some pretty good sight seeing opportunities and you have a perfect travel destination.

 Established as a European settlement in the 17th century, Cape Town has a rich history.   The city is a sparkling mixture of old and new, high culture and popular entertainment, civelization and wilderness.

 You can shop for African souvenirs at the outdoor market or shop for designer clothes at the huge Victoria and Albert Waterfront shopping centre. At the Waterfront you can also catch a boat for Robben Island, where Nelson Mandela was held prisoner before he became president, or visit the Two Oceans Aquarium. Be sure not to miss the Bo Kaap, and the Castle of Good Hope - the oldest building in South Africa.

 Near Cape Town are some good beaches -some of them you will have to share with bathing pinguins. If you have the winter blues in Europe or the USA, remember that it is sunny and warm on the Cape!

 If you are into hiking and walking the region around Cape Town offers great outdoor opportunities. The Table Mountain and the Cape Peninsula National Park are two great areas to go to.
