Paranga has the kind of feel of an uber-glam restaurant/bar that wouldn't look out of place along Miami's South Beach. The decor wows you as you enter, as does the exotic selection of cocktails. The menu concentrates on fresh fish and sushi which proved deliciously fresh and tasty. Puddings came in small portions and were not particularly original (ice cream and hot chocolate sauce)

A lot can be said of the waiters and staff, some would call them pompous and arrogant, others may find their incessant yapping about the food/wine/weather a tad annoying.

Besides this, there is no more a beautiful place to watch the people go by, in one of the most breathtaking parts of this magnificent city.
