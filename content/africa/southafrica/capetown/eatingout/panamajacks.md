**Panama Jacks** is an exellent sea food restaurant, and a well kept secret. Located deep in the docks the decor is dominated by flags pinned to the ceiling and water tanks full of live **lobster** and **abalone** (pick your own!). Lively and down to earth. Be sure to book a table, it's popular.
