Conveniently situated in the Best Western Cape Suites Hotel at the foot of Table Mountain. The Village Cafe restaurant is open daily for a breakfast buffet from 06:30 until 10:30. Light lunches such as Toasted sandwiches, Tramezini’s and a full salad buffet can be enjoyed while relaxing on the Pool deck. There is a spacious Terrace outside the Village Café that enables diners to enjoy a pre-dinner drink while the Village Café Restaurant provides a soft but elegant atmosphere in the evenings and are open for a la carte dining from 18:30 until 22:30.
 
The menu provides exceptionally good value for money and offers a selection of wholesome dishes including fresh line fish daily as well as vegetarian options. Local specialties such as Bobotie and Curries give overseas tourists the chance to sample some spicy Cape fare. The daily changing menu offers a variety of Game, Poultry and different Steaks that are all immensely popular main course dishes. All dishes are served with vegetables in season and potatoes/rice.
We also organize Sunday luncheons buffet or braai buffet 
 
The Village Pub offers a Vibe atmosphere where guests can enjoy a refreshing drink while watching their favorite sports on the big screen.  Pub lunches and dinners can be enjoyed from 14:00 until 22:30. The Village Pub terrace also provides a private braai area witch is perfect for smaller functions such as Birthdays and Year End functions.
Secure parking available
