Cape Town's only surviving water mill built in 1818.
 The enormous cast iron wheel was built in 1840 by Jacob Letterstedt.
 Ground flour and biscuits are for sale.
 Open Mon-Fri 10h00 - 13h00 and 14h00 - 16h00.
 Guided tours daily at 10h15, 11h30 & 14h15.
