title: "Giants Castle"

Giants Casle offers some of the most wonderful hiking oppertunities available in the Central Drakensbeg. Dont expect to find mass tourism here infact its quite possible to see nobeoy except the game guard at the park entrance. The Bushman rock art at the main caves is very well known but check out what Kamberg offers too as the rock art there is infinatly better preserved.
