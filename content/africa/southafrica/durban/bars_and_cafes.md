---
title: "Bars and Cafes"
type: section
---

Most of Durban's drinking and dancing happens around **Florida Road** and **Windermere Road** in Morningside, with a string of bars, restaurants and clubs within walking distance of one another. The crowd is mixed and the vibe is laid-back compared to Johannesburg or Cape Town. Things start late and run very late — places that close at 2am are the early ones. Take a taxi back to your hotel; the suburbs are safe but the routes between them less so after dark.
