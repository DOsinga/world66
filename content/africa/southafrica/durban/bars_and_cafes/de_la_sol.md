A relaxed Florida Road hangout — deep-blue walls, velvet stools, mosaic tile behind the bar. Light meals and cocktails early, DJs and dancing later, with a soundtrack that runs from '80s and '90s pop to house and hip-hop depending on the night. Tuesday is ladies' night with half-price cocktails. Free entry except for special events.
