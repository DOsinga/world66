A very versatile place - a leisurely breakfast, a late-night cup of Lavazza coffee or three-course meal (and don't forget the small but well-selected wine list), they serve it all. Just about everything on the menu is available as a takeout as well. The diverse menu utilises unlikely combinations to delicious effect and covers everything from Bovril toast to sushi, from game bangers to soup.
