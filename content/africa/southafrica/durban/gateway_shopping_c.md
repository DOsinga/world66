One of the largest shopping malls in the southern hemisphere, in Umhlanga north of Durban. Hundreds of shops, cinemas, restaurants and a long list of in-mall attractions including a free-standing artificial surfing wave (the Flowrider), a multi-storey climbing wall, and the Barnyard Theatre. Useful even if you hate malls — there's almost always something on for kids, and the food court is a reliable lunch.
