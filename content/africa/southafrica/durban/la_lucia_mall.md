A relaxed suburban shopping centre on the northern edge of Durban, in La Lucia. Smaller and quieter than Gateway just up the road, with the usual selection of South African chains, a couple of supermarkets and some good cafes. Worth a stop if you're staying in Umhlanga and don't want to brave the bigger mall.
