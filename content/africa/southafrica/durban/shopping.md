---
title: "Shopping"
type: section
---

The most distinctive shopping in Durban is at the **Victoria Street Market** in the old Indian quarter — two floors of spice stalls, fabric, brassware and souvenirs, with the freshest curry powders in the city. For a more conventional experience, head to the big malls: **Gateway** in Umhlanga is one of the largest in the southern hemisphere and has a surfable wave pool inside, while **The Pavilion** in Westville is the local favourite. Look out for Zulu beadwork, basketry and woodcarving, sold both at the markets and at craft stalls along the beachfront.
