Gateway shopping centre is situated in Umhlanga, near the Sharks board. As well as a selection of shops, cinemas and a variety of entertainment it also accomodates a variety of restaurants, your choice to sit outside and enjoy the entertainment or inside. Supposed to be one of the largest malls in the Southern Hemisphere. It has it's own Wavepool with a free standing surfing wave! Were you can learn how to surf! There is a rock climbing wall were you can learn how to rock climb. There are plenty of clothes shops which sell really good clothes. One of which is called Zip code which sells really good clothes and I would really recomend going there.
