Durbanville is an upmarket residential suburb situated in the heart of
the vibrant industrial and business hub of Tygerberg. There are three
major shopping malls and a wide range of restaurants, retail stores,
medical, banking, sporting and entertainment facilities. As the retail
heart of the Cape Town Metro, Durbanville caters for the professional
businessman and corporate executives as well as local and international
tourists and its accommodation reflects this range.
