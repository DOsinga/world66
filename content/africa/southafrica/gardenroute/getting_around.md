The most unique experience is treading on secluded private beaches
where hardly ever another human walks. Accessable from the property run
by Tsitsikamma on Sea (www.tsitsikammaonsea.com) in the heart of the
Tsitsikamma National Forest, this billionaire’s
estate features a luxurious, 4-star, Boutique Hotel/Luxury Lodge,
nestled 100m
above 3kms of rugged Tsitsikamma coastline and Nature Reserve, remote,
private
and exclusive, borders on the Tsitsikamma National Forest and boasts
spectacular
million dollar ocean and mountain views.

Surrounded by rolling
green hills amid dense indigenous vegetation, early morning game and abundant
bird life, the Lodge presents six elegantly appointed suites with sweeping
ocean views and private balconies for intimate, romantic dining.

Watch whales and dolphins from the overlook deck,
majestically perched above boisterous breaking waves. Experience bass fishing, horse riding, guided nature walks amid
lush, dense green forest to secluded beaches below, or adventures like bungi jumping, hangliding ,golf and a Big-5 game reserve, nearby .

Authentic South African
gourmet dinners and breakfast buffets are served in the panoramic view lounge,
open-air patio, or your own private balcony, on request. For special occasions, try our traditional
South African BBQ’s (“Braai’s”), on the patio or he overlook.

A fully licensed bar with championship snooker table flanks
a wine cellar for every collector's dream, featuring some of the finest,
award winning South African vintage wines.
