Hout Bay ("Wood Bay") is a fishing village about 20 km south of [Cape Town](/africa/southafrica/capetown) city centre, tucked into a dramatic valley between the mountains of the Cape Peninsula. The harbour is active — fishing boats, a seal colony on Duiker Island just offshore, and the chaotic bustle of the waterfront market.

From Hout Bay, the road over Chapman's Peak to Noordhoek is one of the most spectacular coastal drives in South Africa. On a clear day the views across the Atlantic are extraordinary.
