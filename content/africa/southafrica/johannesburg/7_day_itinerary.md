---
title: "7 Day Itinerary in Johannesburg"
type: section
---

A **Johannesburg itinerary** will take you on a tour around South Africa's
largest city. This city is full of beautiful places and site worth your
visit and relaxation.

**Day 1 - Johannesburg Botanical Garden**

This beautiful amusement park is found in Emmarentia and should be your first
place to visit on your Johannesburg itinerary. Here you will have the opportunity to see a dam
inside the garden and thousands of roses of every kind and color. There
are many places within the garden that you will enjoy like. The garden has an island
with birds, picnic space, a pet walking area, a tree and flower nursery,
an herbal garden and an environment center.

**Day 2 - Johannesburg
Museums**

Make a point of visiting the various museums in the
city. Johannesburg Art Gallery is the place to see artists' work. There
is also the Hector Pieterson Museum right next to Nelson Mandela's
former house which is also a museum. From here you can go to Museum
Africa which has a lot of art made from rock and exhibits explaining the city's history.

**Day
3 - Gold Reef City**

Another Johannesburg visit should
include Gold Reef City, an amazing park where you will find so many things to
do. Visit the gold mining museum to learn about mining. Go for
exhilarating rides on roller coasters. The Apartheid Museum will give a tour about the apartheid era. There are game rooms, a 4D movie
theater and a casino. You will wonder how the day ended so fast.

**Day
4 - Hartbeespoort Dam**

This park will give you the opportunity
to see all kinds of reptiles found in the south of Africa. There are
live shows performed by seals and snakes. Various species of wild
animals are also found in the zoo. You will see many types of the cat
family here. Endangered species are also reared here.

**Day 5 -
Cradle of Humankind **

Found in Gauteng province, these caves
have fossils of all kinds of the great apes and plants. Here you will be
able to learn how man evolved. You will be able to visit numerous caves
like Stekfontein cave which also has a museum with ancient remains.
Swartkrans cave, one of many caves in the area, exhibits tools made from stones and bones.

**Day 6 - Montecasino**

A Johannesburg
itinerary without fun is not an option. You will wonder if you are in
Las Vegas because casinos, shopping and amusement centers are all around the city.
There is also a replica Tuscan village there. After having some fun, go
to Pietor Toerien Theater and enjoy a show.

**Day 7 - Shopping**

You
should not leave Johannesburg without purchasing some souvenirs
like handicrafts and South African art from Bruma Lake Market. Head to
Bryanston organic market and get all kinds of food and fresh produce.
From here, go to Khinoor to get a collection of African songs. Johannesburg
itinerary will leave you yearning for more of this beautiful city.
