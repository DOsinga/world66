---
title: "Day Trips in Johannesburg"
type: section
---

Johannesburg, South Africa is an area rich in resources and a great
place to visit. It’s a good idea to see other sites of interest around
the area during your visit.

**Roodeplaat**

Nearby Pretoria is the home of Roodeplaat, a nature reserve and
dam. There are over 250 species of birds in this reserve along with
zebra, impala, wildebeest, and other wild life. The dam is a popular
site for fishing and camping. There is a wonderful hiking trail that
will take you take you through the most beautiful countryside in South
Africa.

**National Zoological Gardens of South**

In Pretoria there is the National Zoological Gardens of South Africa
established in 1899. Here they breed endangered species including
white rhino and Arabian Oryx. There is an aquarium and a reptile park
in the gardens and a primate and carnivore section. Also in Pretoria
is the Voortrekker Monument that memorializes the Battle of Blood River
in 1838.

**Suikerbosran Nature Reserve****
**

Nearby is the Suikerbosran Nature Reserve, which is also near the
airport. This reserve is 115 square kilometers and has hiking trails
but it might be better traveled by mountain bike. There is a museum
and visitor's center on the site.

**Magaliesburg**

Visit the nearby village of Magaliesburg by steam train called the
Magaliesburg Express. It goes from Park Station to the Magaliesburg
Country Hotel. During the weekend the town is a jumping place for
families and individuals alike. There are hiking and rock climbing
opportunities and you can rent mountain bikes and go out on the
countryside.

**Sun City**

Two hours out of Johannesburg is Sun City, a popular resort
featuring the Lost City and Valley of the Waves. There are artificial
beaches, volcanoes, and the ever present slot machines.

**Cradle of Humankind World Heritage Site**

The Cradle of Humankind World Heritage Site is an absolute must do
while in the area. This is a dozen or so limestone caves which were
coral reefs 2.3 billion years ago. The Wonder Cave is a 60 meter chasm
that is 1.5 million years old. Many hominid fossils have been found in
the area.

**Cullinan**

Take a 45 minute drive to Cullinan to see the mining town where the
largest diamond every to be found had its beginning. This diamond is
part of the current crown jewels in England. The town has tried to
keep the turn of the century charm.

Make sure to take the time to see these areas outside of Johannesburg and take in the ambiance of the area.
