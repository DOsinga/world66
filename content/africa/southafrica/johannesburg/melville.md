The trendy suburb of melville is situated just west of Johannesburg central and features a wide variety of nightlife. Situated close to two universities (Rand Afrikaans University (RAU for short, but name will be changed to university of Johannesburg in Jan 2005) and University of the Witwatersrand), It is also a meeting point and general hangout for many students. There are trendy restaurants, clubs, pubs, etc
