---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Johannesburg's sights are mostly tied to the history of apartheid and, behind it, to gold. The **Apartheid Museum** and **Constitution Hill** are essential — not cheerful, but essential — and a guided tour of **Soweto** is the standard afternoon. Further out, the **Cradle of Humankind** is an excellent half-day escape to the fossil caves that gave the world the Australopithecines.
