---
title: "Top 5 Must Do's in Johannesburg"
type: section
---

Johannesburg is a popular tourist destination that boasts of an
eclectic line-up of attractions in the backdrop of breathtaking natural
scenic beauty. Here are some ideas for things to do and see in this striking South African region.

**1. Wild Encounters**

Visit The Lion Park, a sprawling reserve that offers visitors an
opportunity to get up close and personal with a large number of
majestic beasts and an assortment of predators. Watch the indigenous
herbivores and an incredibly rich natural vegetation that adorn the
park. Visitors can spot the rare white lion along with more than 80
lions and other wild beasts like cheetahs, hyenas, wild dogs, wolves,
black jackals, giraffes and several species of antelopes that roam
around unrestricted through the confines on the park. The Cub World
area is an amusing place to interact with some animals and feed the
giraffes by climbing a giraffe feeding platform that puts you at eye
level with the tame herbivores. The park is open from 8:30am to 5pm on
weekdays and 8:30am to 6pm on weekends.

**2. Museum Mania**

Johannesburg is filled with stunning specialty and general history
museums like the Mandela Family Museum, the African National Museum of
Military History, James Hall Museum of Transport and the popular
Apartheid Museum which is a fascinating montage of South Africa’s
tumulus history. It houses brilliant memorabilia including pictures,
videos, documentaries and hundreds of exhibits that resonate with the the land’s struggle for freedom. The museum is open from
10.00am to 5.00pm Tue to Sun and is closed on Mondays, Christmas Day
and Good Friday.

**3. Botanical Gardens**

Visit the Walter Sisulu Botanical Gardens at Malcolm Road. The place
is laden with lush green vegetation and several species of endangered
birds that offer a spectacular glimpse of South Africa’s wild flora and
fauna. Spot the rare Black Eagle in the wilderness of the garden that
also houses a restaurant and picnic area.

**4. ****Gold Reef City**

Visit the Gold Reef City, located off Xavier’s Road - a thrilling
interactive mining center that lets visitors experience theme rides,
games and spectacular mining shows. A stunning collage of colors,
special effects and sounds that offer a glimpse into the legendary
mining activities in the region.

**5. Bunny Park**

One of the best places in Johannesburg if you have young children.
Watch a bevy of cute rabbits, geese, ducks, beautiful swans and rare
turtles wandering about the park. There are also fun pony rides for
kids and a nice park where visitors can feed the harmless bunnies.

Johannesburg presents a fantastic mesh of things to do and an array
of entertaining leisure options that include theme parks, wildlife
reserves, botanical gardens and museums.
