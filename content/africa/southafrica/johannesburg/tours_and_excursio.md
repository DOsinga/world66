It is one of Johannesburg's frustrations that visitors landing at the airport rarely ever want to visit or see the city but mainly come to visit a touristic thriller nearby : Kruger National Park. There used to be a time that safaris were for the very wealthy , but over the past decades with the cheaper airline tickets and the tours whereby a minivan is shared by 5 to 9 passengers, as well as the tented camps , have reduced the costs to an absolute minimum - see the 4 or 5 or 6 day budget tours offered in www.safaritravelguide.com. Participants are even picked up and dropped off from their hotel in Johannesburg and all they need to bring is a sleeping bag and a relaxed attitude.

### Kruger Budget Tours
