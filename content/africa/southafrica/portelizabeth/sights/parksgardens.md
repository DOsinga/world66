Port Elizabeth is a good place for viewing a few Game reserves.

  Located only 72 km from Port Elizabeth, the exclusive [Tibet](/asia/northeastasia/china/tibet) is the only private reserve in the Eastern Cape boasting the "Big Five" (elephant, black rhino, buffalo, lion and leopard). Early morning and late afternoon game drives through the wilderness in a specially equipped game viewing vehicle, together with an armed game-ranger as escort, are provided. 

 The notable [Tibet](/asia/northeastasia/china/tibet) was originally proclaimed in 1931 to support and save the last vestiges of Eastern Cape Elephant and Cape Buffalo from extinction. Various other notable private game reserves such as Schotia Safaris, Kariega Park and Shimba Safaris provide wildlife enthusiasts and nature lovers with exciting and memorable wildlife experiences.
