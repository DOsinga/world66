---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Pretoria's main sights are concentrated in two clusters. In the **city centre**, Church Square is the historic heart — flanked by the Palace of Justice and the statue of Paul Kruger — with the **Kruger House**, **Melrose House**, and the **Transvaal Museum** all within walking distance. A short drive east, the **Union Buildings** sit on Meintjieskop hill above the city with Nelson Mandela's enormous bronze statue looking out over the amphitheatre lawns. The **Pretoria National Botanical Garden** (out in Brummeria) holds 50 hectares of indigenous South African flora and is a good retreat from the city. South of the centre, the **Voortrekker Monument** on Monument Hill is a loaded but unmissable piece of Afrikaner heritage, with sweeping views from the dome. For families, the **National Zoological Gardens** on Boom Street is one of the best zoos on the continent.
