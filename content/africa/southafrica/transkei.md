The Transkei region is a gorgeous region in the Eastern Cape, extending from roughly Durban to East London. Rolling hills, beautiful coast line and picutresque farmlands provide for a delighful drive through this area. Within the Transkei, the Wild Coast offers destinations such as Cintza and Coffee Bay, both popular with the backpacking crowd. 

A practical note: Mthatha (formerly Umtata), the main town on the N2, has a poor reputation for safety and is worth passing through rather than stopping in. Drive through during daylight, don't leave valuables visible in the car, and you should have no trouble. The wild coast backpacker lodges at Cintsa, Coffee Bay, and Wavecrest are established and safe bases for exploring the area.
