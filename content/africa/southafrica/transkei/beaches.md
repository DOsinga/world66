---
title: "Beaches"
type: section
---

The Wild Coast runs for about 250 km and almost all of it is beach. The big names are **Coffee Bay**, with its famous Hole in the Wall arch; **Port St Johns**, tucked into the mouth of the Mzimvubu river; **Mazeppa Bay**, a fishing village with a suspension bridge to its offshore island; and **Morgan's Bay**, a gentler family spot at the southern end. Between them are dozens of unnamed coves where you can swim, surf, and walk for hours without seeing another person.
