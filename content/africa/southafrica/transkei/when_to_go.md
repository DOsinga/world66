---
title: "When to Go"
type: section
---

The Wild Coast has mild weather year-round — summers (November to February) are warm, subtropical and good for swimming but very busy over South African school holidays in December. Spring and autumn are probably the best compromise: thinner crowds, lower prices, comfortable temperatures. Winter (May to August) is dry and much quieter, with cooler evenings, and is the best season for whale-watching as southern rights migrate up the coast.
