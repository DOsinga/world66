Sudan is a large country in Northern Africa, bordering the Red Sea, between [Egypt](/africa/egypt) and [Eritrea](/africa/eritrea). It has very different climatical zones, ranging from dry desserts to fertile mountain slopes and the Nile valley. Interesting places to visit are [Khartoum](/africa/sudan/khartoum) and [Port Sudan](/africa/sudan/portsudan).
