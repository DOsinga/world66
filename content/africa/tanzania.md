Tanzania is a big country in East Africa: it is a prime destination for people interested in big game and wildlife. 



















To the north of Tanzania you find [Kenya](/africa/kenya), [Lake Victoria](/africa/kenya/lakes/lakevictoria) and [Uganda](/africa/uganda), in the west [Rwanda](/africa/rwanda), [Burundi](/africa/burundi) , Kongo Kinshasha and [Zambia](/africa/zambia) . In the south [Malawi](/africa/malawi) and [Mozambique](/africa/mozambique) . The East is occupied by the Indian Ocean. 

Highlights of Tanzania are the Kilimanjaro , the many national parks with excellent opportunities for viewing wildlife, [Serengeti national park](/africa/tanzania/serengetinationalpark) being probably the best known, and the island of [Zanzibar](/africa/tanzania/zanzibar) with its fascinating blend of Arab and African culture. The capital city [Dar es salaam](/africa/tanzania/daressalaam) is a very lively city, with good markets and a great place to meet real Africans. It is also the central travel hub from where you can explore all the country's sights.
