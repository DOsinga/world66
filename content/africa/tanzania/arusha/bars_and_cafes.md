---
title: "Bars and Cafes"
type: section
---

Via Via in the Natural History Museum gardens is the main traveller bar — lively, social, and with information boards about upcoming events. Africafe on Old Boma Road is the best coffee stop, with excellent pastries and reliable wi-fi. The Greek Club on the corner of Old Moshi and Serengeti Roads is the main expat hangout, with cheap draught beer and sport on screen at weekends.
