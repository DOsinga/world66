---
title: "Day Trips"
type: section
---

**Arusha National Park** is 25km east of town — a manageable half-day trip with game drives among the Momela crater lakes, or a guided walk on the lower slopes of Mount Meru. Not as spectacular as the main safari parks, but good for smaller mammals, birds, and close views of Meru's summit.

**Tengeru market**, 25 minutes east on the Moshi road, is held on Wednesdays and Saturdays: produce, clothing, local crafts, and a chaotic but enjoyable atmosphere. The lake at Doluti crater, just nearby, can be visited by canoe.

**Longido**, 80km north near the Kenyan border, is a Maasai settlement accessible on organised half-day trips. It gives a genuine introduction to Maasai culture and livestock farming. The walk up Longido Mountain, with a local guide, takes around four hours and rewards with views across both Tanzania and Kenya.

**Ng'iresi Village**, 7km from town in the foothills of Mount Meru, offers a community-run cultural tour: coffee and banana plantations, traditional farming methods, and views of the Arusha district. **Nkoaranga**, 40 minutes out at 1,650m elevation, is similar and on a clear day gives sightlines to Kilimanjaro.
