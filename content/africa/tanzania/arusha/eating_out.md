---
title: "Eating Out"
type: section
---

Arusha has a range of eating options for a Tanzanian city. The expat and safari crowd supports several international restaurants around Boma Road. Indian food is excellent — reflecting Tanzania's South Asian population — with curries, samosas, and chapati available at several places around Mosque Street and India Street at very low prices. Via Via in the gardens behind the Natural History Museum is reliable for both local and European dishes, and doubles as the main social hub for travellers. For local food at near-nothing prices, stalls near the bus station serve ugali, rice, and grilled meat.
