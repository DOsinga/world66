---
title: "Getting Around"
type: section
---

Within the city centre, walking is fine during daylight hours, though take care with bags at busy spots. Daladala minibuses cover the main routes cheaply. Taxis are available but should be negotiated before you get in. For safaris and day trips to the national parks, your tour operator will arrange transport. Renting a car with a driver is easier than driving yourself and is the standard approach.
