The TrueAfrica Safari Company was founded by a Dutch man who married a Masai woman. His love for Africa is apparent in their safari iternaries: camping in the midst of the parks, visiting the real Masai life. Far away from the spoilt tourist traps. 

TrueAfrica organizes it´s tours with a personal touch and a great eye for details. Everything has been taken care of, you can dedicate yourself to spotting the game and taking pictures.
