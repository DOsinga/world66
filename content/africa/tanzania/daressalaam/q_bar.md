A long-running Oyster Bay institution that combines a bar, restaurant and small guesthouse. Live music or sport on the big screens most evenings, and a reliable kitchen turning out burgers, pizzas and Indian. Tuesdays are sambuca night, Thursdays the bucket-of-beer special. Loud, friendly, popular with the expat crowd.
