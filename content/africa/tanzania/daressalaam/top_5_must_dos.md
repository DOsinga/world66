This travel writer has frequented Dar es Salaam three times in six months, and
finds no shortage of activities in this vibrant coastal Tanzanian city. A
quieter and less touristy alternative to nearby Zanzibar, this multicultural
fishing town, influenced by African, Arab and European tastes, has numerous
local and worldly activities, shops, markets and restaurants for the
high-spirited traveler.
