---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

Several daily buses run from Dar es Salaam, and there are daily connections from Mbeya, Songea, [Njombe](/africa/tanzania/njombe), and Dodoma. The road from Dar es Salaam via Morogoro is tarmac and good quality, as is the continuation to Mbeya. The Dodoma road requires a four-wheel drive. Iringa has an airport but no regular scheduled flights.
