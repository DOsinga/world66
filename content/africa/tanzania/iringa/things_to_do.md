---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

**Neema Crafts** in town employs disabled people to make handicrafts — woven goods, elephant-dung paper products, recycled glass beads. Take a workshop tour and eat in the gallery cafe. Excellent coffee and ice cream.

**Gangilonga Rock** is a prominent boulder on the edge of town with good views. Worth the short climb, but go in a group.

**Isimila Stone Age Site** (20 km south) — a remarkable Palaeolithic site in an eroded gully with tools and hand axes up to 100,000 years old. A museum on site provides context.
