**Lushoto** is a small town in the Usambara Mountains of northeastern [Tanzania](/africa/tanzania), set in a green, hilly landscape at about 1,400 metres. It makes a refreshing break from the heat of the coast — the air is cool, the scenery lush, and the hiking excellent.

The Western Usambaras around Lushoto offer walks through montane rainforest, past waterfalls, and through villages where people farm the steep hillsides. Several local guides run ecological tours that combine hiking with community visits. Lushoto is about an hour north of Mombo, roughly halfway between [Dar es Salaam](/africa/tanzania/daressalaam) and the northern safari circuit.
