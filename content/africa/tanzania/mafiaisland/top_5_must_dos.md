CHOLE FOXES LODGE

Welcome
to Chole Foxes Lodge. Situated on the beautiful island
of Chole, this is the only place in
the Mafia Island Marine
Park where you can enjoy
a relaxing holiday at a reasonable price.

All our rooms are self contained, with twin beds, bucket showers and
toilet. Situated on an unmodified beach, nestled amongst the mangroves, this is
a fabulous place to relax and watch the sun set over neighbouring Mafia Island.
The natural break in the mangroves provides direct access to a secluded beach,
from which it is possible to enjoy a cooling dip at high tide.

During
the evenings, why not enjoy a moonlight barbeque of freshly caught fish under
the stars.
