---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Most visitors to Moshi are here for Kilimanjaro, but the town and its surroundings offer more than just the mountain. Day walks through the Chagga villages on the lower slopes pass through coffee and banana plantations, with waterfalls and viewpoints along the way. Several operators in town can arrange guided cultural walks.

Moshi is also a base for safari trips to the northern parks — Tarangire, Lake Manyara, the Serengeti, and Ngorongoro Crater. Multi-day safaris typically depart from either Moshi or neighbouring Arusha.
