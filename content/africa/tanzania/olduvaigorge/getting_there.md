---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

Olduvai Gorge sits between Ngorongoro and the Serengeti, so most visitors stop here as part of a safari itinerary. You need your own vehicle or a tour — there is no public transport. From the Ngorongoro Crater area, continue down into the plains toward the Serengeti. A signposted turnoff on the right leads to the museum, which you reach after a short drive.
