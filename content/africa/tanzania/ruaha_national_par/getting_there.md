---
title: "Getting There in Ruaha national park"
type: section
---

Buses from Iringa run to Tungamalenga, 20 km from the gate. There is no public transport to the park itself. It is therefore necessary to hire a vehicle, which will anyway be mandatory inside the park.
It is also possible to hire “all-inclusive packages” through the lodges in the park, all of which are, however, extremely expensive. Less wealthy visitors are recommended to stay the night in Tungamalenga.
There are regular flights to the park airstrip.
