Serengeti is the best known game park of Tanzania and probably also in the world. The park measures almost 15,000 square km. In the park you find almost all the animals you wnat to have in your photobook in a perfect surrounding. On the Serengeti plains there are literally millions of hoofed animals. They're constantly on the move in search of grassland and are watched and preyed upon by a varied parade of predators.
