An easy 45 kilometer ride from [Lome](/africa/togo/lome) brings you to Aneho. Aneho was the capital of Togo until 1920. It is located very close to the border with [Benin](/africa/benin) and if you are going form Lome to Benin it is an ideal place to stop.
