Kara enjoys a lot of restaurants, in different price ranges.

### [Restaurant Le Jardin](/africa/togo/kara/eating_out/restaurant_le_jard)

The restaurant, which is attached to the hotel with the same name, is set in the lush garden and very calm and quiet. The food is very good and the meals are either French, African or Asian.

### [Restaurant Le Chateau](/africa/togo/kara/eating_out/restaurant_le_chat)

This is a great place to sit on the terras and watch the world go by while having lunch, dinner or a drink.
The hotel serves pizzas and European and local meals.

type: Local
