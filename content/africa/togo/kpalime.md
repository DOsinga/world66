---
title: "Kpalime"
type: location
---

Located in the coffee growing area of Togo, Kpalime is one of the best spots for seeing some incredibly beautiful scenery just two hours from  [Lome](/africa/togo/lome) . The area is ideal for hiking. Togo's highest mountain, Mt Agou is just 20 km away.  

 While your in town and not out hiking the hills, check out the market on the Rue du Marche and the Centre Artisanal about 5 km north of the centre on the road to Klouto.
