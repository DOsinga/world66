Lome is the capital of Togo and a very nice place to be. You can stroll from the city center to some great white sandy beaches, there are colourfull markets, a good museum and exciting nightlife.


 From Lome you can make many daytrips to Davie, Lake Togo, [Togoville](../togoville) or even [Aneho](../aneho)
