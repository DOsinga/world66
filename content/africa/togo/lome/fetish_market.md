The Fetish Market (Marché des Féticheurs) in Lomé is one of the largest voodoo markets in West Africa. Stalls are piled with animal skulls, dried chameleons, porcupine skins, and all manner of ingredients used in traditional ceremonies and healing. The vendors are used to tourists and will ask for a fee to let you walk around. One of them will act as your guide, explaining the purpose of the various items and their role in voodoo practice. It is strange, pungent, and utterly fascinating.
