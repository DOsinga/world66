---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Lomé is not packed with formal tourist attractions, but what it has is genuinely interesting. The Fetish Market is the main draw for visitors, a window into voodoo traditions that is unlike anything you will find elsewhere in West Africa. The National Museum offers a quieter look at Togolese culture and history. Beyond that, much of the pleasure in Lomé comes from simply walking the streets, browsing the Grand Marché, and watching life unfold along the beachfront boulevard.
