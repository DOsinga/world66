The Grand Marché is the commercial heart of Lomé, a vast multi-story market run predominantly by the famous Nana Benz — the market women who built fortunes trading textiles. You will find Dutch wax fabrics, spices, household goods, and food stalls crammed into a labyrinth of alleys. It is loud, colourful, and overwhelming in the best possible way. Go in the morning when the energy is highest.
