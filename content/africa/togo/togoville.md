On the northern banks of Lake Togo, Togoville is interesting for its history. Slaves from Togoville where transported to [Haiti](/northamerica/haiti) and brought Voodoo to that island. Voodoo is still very big here.


 The main sights are the Artisanal, where you can see artisans at work, the chucrh nearby with great glass stained windows and the Maison Royal. It's not a lot, but the the location on the lake is great.
