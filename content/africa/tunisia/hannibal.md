A concise biography of Hannibal Barca, the Carthaginian general who crossed the Alps with elephants and came within reach of destroying Rome. Bradford writes with military clarity and a genuine feel for the Mediterranean world. Good background reading before visiting [Carthage](/africa/tunisia/carthage).
