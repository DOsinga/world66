---
title: "Sousse"
type: location
---

Sousse is a prime tourist destination on the coast. It receives a lot of direct flights full of tourists from Europe. Most of the tourist accommodations are a few kilometers north of the old town.

 The beaches here are good and the climate is great. The old town is nice, although it is not very big. There are walls around and there is a small castle.

 From Sousse you can make easy daytrips, it is even possible to visit [Kairouan](/africa/tunisia/kairouan) and [Tunis](/africa/tunisia/tunis) as daytrips if you make Sousse your basis for exploring the country. Other good daytrips include [Mahdia](/africa/tunisia/mahdia) (old fisher town) and [Monastir](/africa/tunisia/monastir).
