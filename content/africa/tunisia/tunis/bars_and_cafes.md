---
title: "Bars and Cafes"
type: section
---

Tunis has a lively cafe culture. The Ville Nouvelle is lined with sidewalk terraces where locals sit for hours over espresso or mint tea. In the medina, traditional Moorish cafes serve sweet tea and chicha in tiled interiors. Alcohol is available in hotel bars and a handful of standalone venues, though the scene quiets down noticeably during Ramadan.
