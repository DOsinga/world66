Tunis is the hub of northern Tunisia, and the country is small enough that many destinations make excellent day trips. [Carthage](/africa/tunisia/carthage) and [Sidi Bou Said](/africa/tunisia/sidibousaid) are practically suburbs, reachable by the TGM train in under 30 minutes. Further afield, the Roman ruins at Dougga are among the best-preserved in Africa, and the resort towns of [Hammamet](/africa/tunisia/hammamet) and [Nabeul](/africa/tunisia/nabeul) are about an hour south by car or bus.
