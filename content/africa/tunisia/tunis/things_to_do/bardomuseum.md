Housed in a former palace in the Le Bardo suburb, this is one of the most important museums in the Mediterranean. Its collection of Roman mosaics is the largest in the world, covering three floors of rooms. The Punic and Roman archaeological finds are impressive, and the palace architecture adds to the experience. Closed Mondays.
