**chez habib**

ask the cab driver for "Habib restaurant", close to the "pirate" hotel - it's the best one in Zarzis, even tho it looks cheap and empty, you're guaranteed to eat fresh fish (fished from the very morning) and the nicest "spaghetti crevette" (spaghetti with shrimps and tomato sauce with spices - beware if you're not used to it, it's a bit hot) in all Tunisia. iza [more..](/africa/tunisia/zarzis/eatingout/chez_habib)
