Uganda is landlocked, bordering Rwanda and Tanzania to the south, Kenya to the east, Sudan to the north and Zaire to the west. Oral tradition describes several African Kingdoms in the area. Uganda has magnificent scenery, including snowcapped mountain, thick tropical forests and semidesert areas. Lakes cover more 
 than a sixth of Uganda. The capital is [Kampala](/africa/uganda/kampala) which is well worth a visit. Other interesting places to visit are Ssese Islands, Kasese, Entebbe and Kabale. Highly recommmended is also the Bwindi National Park.
