---
title: "Bars and Cafes"
type: section
---

Jinja's bar scene is low-key but lively, particularly around the backpacker lodges near the river. Nile Special — Uganda's ubiquitous lager, brewed since the 1950s and named for the river — is what most people are drinking. After a day on the rapids, a cold one by the Nile is one of those simple pleasures that travel is made of.
