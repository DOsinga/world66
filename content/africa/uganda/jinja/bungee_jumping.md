A 44-metre bungee jump over the Nile at Bujagali, run by Adrift. You jump from a purpose-built platform and swing out over the river — it's one of the few bungees in Africa where the setting genuinely adds to the experience. The operation is well-run and the equipment is reputable. It costs around USD 115, which is steep by local standards but in line with the adrenaline market here.
