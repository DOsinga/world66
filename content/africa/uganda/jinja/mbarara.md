Travel Guide

This quiet little town is a couple of hundred miles from Kampala. It is not far from the Source of the Nile, or the Equator I found the town to be full of wonderful, friendly people. The Lake View Hotel was just one of many there. At the Lake View we had excelent service and also the food was wonderful. This is a town you don't want to miss as you travel to see gorillas, or just to travel there to see the town itself.
