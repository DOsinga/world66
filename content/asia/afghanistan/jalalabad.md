Jalalabad is located in the east of Afghanistan on the main road from [Kabul](/asia/afghanistan/kabul) the [Khyber Pass](/asia/pakistan/khyberpass) and on to [Pakistan](/asia/pakistan). It is the capital of Nangarhar province and has a population of over 100,000

The city dominates the entrances to the Laghman and Kunar valleys and used to be a leading trading center with India and Pakistan. Before the turmoil in the country the city enjoyed some popualrity as a summer resort because of its climate. It was a popular stop over in the 70-ies for overlanders going from [London](/europe/unitedkingdom/england/london) to [Kathmandu](/asia/nepal/kathmandu).
