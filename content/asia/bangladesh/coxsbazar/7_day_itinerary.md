Cox's Bazaar is the tourist capital of Bangladesh and home of the world's longest natural beach. However, there is more to this ancient city and lots to see. If you are planning a trip to Bangladesh, consider this **Cox's Bazaar itinerary.**

**Day 1
**

The Aggameda Khyang Monastery is not far from the entrance to the town of Cox's Bazaar. A Buddhist monastery, it is a place of tranquil beauty. The sanctuary rests on a series of timber columns along with the assembly hall and prayer chamber. There are a collection of bronze Buddha statues that complete the serenity.

**Day 2
**

Sonadia Island is a short distance from Cox's Bazaar. Walk along the western seashore and look at all the different varieties of shells. On the northern side of the island there are beds of oysters.
