---
title: "Beaches in Cox's bazar"
type: section
---

Cox's Bazar is popular among locals and international tourists. It is
famous for its beautiful beaches that stretch for miles. The longest
sandy beach in the world is located in **Cox's Bazar. Beaches** are set against the natural beauty of green
hills and forests. On colder days
tourists can enjoy a cup of tea along the beach while admiring the
magnificent natural beauty that surrounds them.

**Laboni Beach **

Laboni Beach is the major tourist beach in Cox's Bazar. It is situated
by the town area. There are shops at Laboni beach that sell souvenirs
and beach wear.

**Himchari**

Himchari beach is situated 18 km to the south of Cox's Bazar. The
drive to this beach is scenic. This beautiful beach is a good place to
take photos. A suggestion for lunch is to take a picnic to Himchari
beach. There is a waterfall at Himchori. The waterfall is small during the dry
season but during rainy season it is an amazing site. Another site
seeing attraction in this area is the "Broken Hills."

**Enanai B****each**

This beach is situated 35 km to the south of Cox's Bazaar in Ukhia
Thana. Enani beach can be reached by taking a jeep ride along the
beach from Cox's Bazzar. It takes approximately 30 minutes to get to
Enani beach. The sea water is perfect for taking a refreshing swim
after sun bathing, and although this beach is farther away it is worth the
drive to get away from the crowds that frequent the closer beaches. At
Enani beach you can see coral and rock boulders, and it is the ideal spot
for a picnic.

**

Maheskahali Island**

Maheskahali is an island near Cox's Bazar. On this island you can enjoy
the beaches, mangrove jungles and hills. There is also a shrine in
honor of Shiva. Another attraction is a Buddhist Pagoda.

**Sonadia Island **

Sonadia Island is located 7 km from Cox's Bazar. It has sandy beaches
and there is a variety of shells to be seen on this beach. During
winter there are many fisherman along the island.

**Tenkaf**

Tenkaf is the the end point of the beach area. It is located along the
Naaf river. A suggested outing for the day is to take a river boat to Tenaf beach. The scenery along the river is breathtaking, making this
an enjoyable trip. There are also many wild animals and birds to see
along the way to the beach.
