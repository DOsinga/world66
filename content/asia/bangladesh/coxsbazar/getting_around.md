---
title: "Getting Around in Cox's bazar"
type: section
---

There are many transportation options available to you when visiting
the sites of Bangladesh. You can get to the wonderful area via flight.
The flights arrive at a domestic airport and then you have the option
of entering the city either by bus, cycle-rickshaws, which are the most
common, or rental cars with a local driver.

Car rentals here are not very common unless you are renting a car
with the driver. You are unable to rent a vehicle for your own
operation due to being unfamiliar with the region and driving cultures.
For instance driving is on the left side of the road and the roads are
mostly in poor condition making for a bit more aggressive drivers.

The Ferry service is another option and is located about 5
kilometers from the northern part of Teknaf and proceeds to Cox’s Bazar.

Another popular mode of transportation is the cycle-rickshaw. This
is a tricycle-looking bike in which you have a driver to peddle and two
occupants behind him in a seat. This is a quiet and clean ride and
enables you to enjoy the sights at a comfortable speed. Be sure to know
exactly where you want to go and clearly understand the fees so there
are not any surprises. There are many different types and styles of
these unique bikes. Some are equipped with an umbrella to keep you
somewhat dry and some are motorized but environmentally friendly and
quiet.

For whichever mode of transportation you chose always remember to
negotiate your price for the destination before accepting the
transport. There are many beautiful sights to enjoy especially along
the white sandy beaches!
