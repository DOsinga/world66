title: Maheshkhali Island
type: poi
---

A tidal island a short boat ride from Cox's Bazar, with mangrove forests, fishing villages, and a hilltop that holds both a Buddhist pagoda and the Adinath Shiva shrine side by side. The combination of religious sites and the working fishing community makes for an interesting half-day trip.
