---
title: "Shopping"
type: section
---

The Hotel Motel Zone near the beach is the main tourist shopping area, selling seashells, shawls, T-shirts, and souvenirs. Prices are negotiable. The Burmese Market caters to the area's Burmese community and is a good source of handwoven textiles, sandalwood products, and household goods. The village of Ramu, 10 km away, is known for handmade cigars and woodcarvings.
