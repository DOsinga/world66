Himchari, is really a hotspot for visitors coming to Cox'sbazar, be
it local or foriegner. This location is situated 6KM from the Kalatali
beach by the hill. The magnificent waterfall leading to the beach
is a rare scene to enjoy. This place is ideal for a day picnic,
sunbathing and relaxing. Do watch out for the locals staring at you
while sunbathing. Its not a practice in Bangladesh to
