Bangladesh's only coral island, lying 9 km south of the Teknaf peninsula in the Bay of Bengal. Small, calm, and very different from the mainland — the island is fringed with coral reef and coconut palms. Local name is Narical Gingira (Coconut Island). Boats run daily from Teknaf during the tourist season (November–March); the journey takes two to three hours.
