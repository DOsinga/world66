title: "Himchari"
type: poi

latitude: 21.3535

---

A scenic headland 18 km south of Cox's Bazar town, with a small waterfall that drops through forest to the beach below. Best visited early morning when the light is good and crowds are thin. Reachable by beach jeep along the shoreline or by road. Good viewpoints over the Bay of Bengal from the hillside.
