A Buddhist village 10 km from Cox's Bazar, known for its monasteries, pagodas, and a large bronze Buddha statue. The village also produces handmade cigars, a local craft tradition. Woodcarving workshops and weavers can be found along the main street. A half-hour bus ride from town.
