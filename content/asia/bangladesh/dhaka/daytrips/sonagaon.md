---
title: "Sonargaon"
type: poi
---

Although all but a few remnants of the ancient capital of Bengal are long-since gone, this is a town well worth visiting.

Sonagaon can be found just to the south east of Dhaka off the
Chittagong highway. After the crowds of Dhaka it feels like a peaceful
haven. Long abandoned homes of rich Hindus and an old bridge and older
mosque add a mysterious quality to the town.

Sonagaon is also the home of a folk museum and local craft items may be purchased.
