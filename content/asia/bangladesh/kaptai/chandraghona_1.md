Travel Guide

Chandraghona is a nice area between Kaptai and Ranguni. It is 24 km from Bahaddarhut bus station.It is famous for KARNAFULLY PAPER MILL which is situated bank of karnafully. The 2nd largest crop field of Bangladesh"Gumai Jheel" is here. The natural sceens of chandraghona is very nice. Green hills are covered it and the Karnfully river
flows beside it.You can meet here Chakma,Marma and many kinds of culture.Many Old buddist temple is available here. Really you will enjoy if u visit it.
