### [Moymonshingher Itihash O Moymonsingher Biboron](/asia/bangladesh/mymensingh/books/moymonshingher_iti)

Name of the book in English is: "A History of the Moymonsingh District of Bangladesh and it's Description". The recent edition of the book was published on February, 2005. Publisher is Anandadhara. Here the total history of Mymensingh would be found.
