Travel Guide

Located in Bumthang district in east-central part of the country this small town is
surrounded by heavenly apple groves (the juice is fantastic). Jakar
is one of the most beautiful places in Bhutan. It has very beautiful
undulating landscapes with many fruit trees and temples. Bumthang is in
the area where Buddhism first took hold in Bhutan. The town has several
temples including Kuje Lhakhang (dating from the 8th century) Jampey
Lhakhang (associated with Guru Rimpoche who brought Tantric Buddhism to
Bhutan and Tibet) and Jakar Dzong (which alone makes Bumthang worth a
one-night stop). There are two interesting festivals—Tamshing Phala and
Thangbi Mani—in late September. It is located to the east of Thimphu.
It will take about 9 - 10 hours from Thimphu by car. It is about 250 km
from Thimphu.
