A small, unpretentious restaurant and bar popular with NGO workers and Thimphu locals, particularly busy on Friday evenings. The BBQ ribs are a local favourite; the momos (Tibetan dumplings) are good but slow. Also serves french fries. A good place to get a sense of Thimphu's expat community.
