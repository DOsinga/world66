Benez is a tiny little place which has a very interesting clientele.
Some of Thimphu's more colorful locals mix with the many foreign NGO
workers living in Thimphu. Friday night is always packed and the tiny
bar is jammed. The BBQ ribs are the best in town, not that anyone else
ever has them. They tend to be fairly spicy. They also make excellent
mo mos but it takes a long time. Their Fench Fries are the best.
