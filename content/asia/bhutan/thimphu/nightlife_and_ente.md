---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment"
type: section
---

Thimphu has a surprisingly active bar and restaurant scene for a small Himalayan capital. The Zone, above the Changlimithang stadium, is one of the main expat and local meeting points — it serves burgers, pizzas, and steak alongside Bhutanese food, and screens major football and tennis matches. OM bar on Changlam comes alive after 9pm on Fridays and Saturdays, drawing a mix of expats and locals.
