---
title: "Beaches in Bandar Seri Begawan"
type: section
---

Bandar Seri Begawan is the capital of Brunei. This area has had human settlements at least as far back as the 7th century. Located on the banks of the Brunei River, there are many beaches in the area as well. Most of them overlook the South China Sea or the Gulf of Brunei.

**Manikar Beach
**

This beach overlooks the South China Sea. There is also a resort nearby. Go there only if you can afford the car trip, as it is quite far away from the city center. Enjoy the beach and the blue waters of the South China Sea. The resort is expensive and service is world-class.

**Layang-layangan Beach
**

This one also overlooks the South China Sea. It is located on the Jalan Pancur Hitam. It is a peaceful and serene place that will let you relax and rejuvenate at your pace.

**Pancur Hitam Beach
**

This is a beautiful beach – with plenty of sun, sand, tropical palms and water. Visitors will find it an unruffled place with a serene ambiance.

**Pohon Batu Beach
**

Yet again located on the shores of the South China Sea, this beach is ideal if you want to relax and have a laid back holiday. There is not much to do besides sunbathe and swim.

If you are keen on water sports and would love to try those, try one of the beach resorts. These cost a bit more than the free beaches, but you do get to do what you want. You can even play golf right next to the beach. Almost all the resorts are on the South China Sea. Pick one of your choice, and have fun. They make a good base for you to relax in or entertain business colleagues at. Most of the resorts have fully equipped business centers as well. You will be able to enjoy the lagoon in some of them. They have facilities for all the imaginable water sports. If you're looking for a more adventurous holiday, a resort is definitely what you should opt for.

The beaches on the South China Sea are all clear blue waters and clean sand. The waters are calm and clear. Make your stay in Bandar Seri Begawan a memorable one by choosing to stay at one of the many resorts near the beaches.
