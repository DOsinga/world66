Brunei is a small country, but public transport is limited and a car makes life significantly easier if you want to explore beyond [Bandar Seri Begawan](/asia/brunei/bandarseribegawan).

## Buses

The Franchise Bus system in BSB operates six color-coded lines, running from around 6am to 8pm. Fares are a flat BND1 per ride. Routes cover the main areas of the capital and extend to Gadong, the airport, and Muara. Outside BSB, bus services are sparse and unreliable -- buses leave when full rather than on a fixed schedule, and most stop running by late afternoon. The TransportBN app (iOS and Android) has route maps and schedules.

## Taxis and Ride-hailing

Taxis are metered, starting at BND3.50 for the first kilometer. An airport surcharge of BND3 applies, and inter-district trips incur an extra BND8 charge. Taxis can be hard to flag down on the street -- it is best to call ahead or ask your hotel to arrange one. Grab and Uber are not available in Brunei, but Dart is a local ride-hailing app that works similarly and is often cheaper than traditional taxis.

## Car Rental

Renting a car is the most practical option for getting around. Roads are excellent, traffic is light, petrol is extremely cheap (around BND0.53 per liter), and driving is on the left. Rental cars cost BND60-100 per day. You will need a valid international driving permit along with your national license.

## Water Taxis

Water taxis are an essential part of getting around BSB, particularly for reaching Kampong Ayer and for the river crossing to the Temburong District. Short rides across the river cost BND2-5 (negotiable). Speedboats to [Bangar](/asia/brunei/bangar) in the Temburong District take about 45 minutes and cost around BND6.

## Between Districts

The Temburong District is physically separated from the rest of Brunei by a strip of Sarawak. You can reach it by speedboat from BSB (the traditional route) or via the Temburong Bridge, a 30-km sea-crossing bridge that opened in 2020 and cut the journey from over an hour to about 20 minutes by car.
