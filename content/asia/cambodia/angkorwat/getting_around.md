By helicopter

Get the chance to see the most interesting sites in the Siem Reap area in the comfort of a modern turbine helicopter.   The helicopter is available for charter on an hourly basis from the airport in Siem Reap. Any charter to areas other than the immediate Siem Reap province is available upon request.   Seats: 5 persons in total.
