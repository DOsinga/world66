---
title: "Getting Around"
type: section
---

The Angkor complex is spread over a large area, so you will need transport between temples. Most visitors hire a tuk-tuk driver for the day from Siem Reap — expect to pay around $15–20 for the standard circuit. Bicycles are another popular option for the closer temples, though the heat can be punishing. A rented motorbike or car gives more flexibility for reaching outlying sites like Banteay Srei.
