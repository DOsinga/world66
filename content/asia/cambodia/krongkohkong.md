**Krong Koh Kong** is a coastal village in the south-west corner of Cambodia. To the north are the Cardaman mountains. The island is only 80 km away from Thailand's island
[Koh chang](/asia/thailand/kohchang). This part of Cambodia is still relatively undeveloped for tourism but the potential is enormous. Koh Kong Island of the coast is a undiscovered paradise and the Government of Cambodia hope to build infrastructure to attract overseas tourists in the future. There are flights from
[Phnom penh](/asia/cambodia/phnompenh) and boats from
[Kampong Cham](/asia/cambodia/kampongcham) Som.
