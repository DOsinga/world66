---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Phnom Penh's main sights cluster along the riverfront and can be covered on foot in a day or two. The Royal Palace, Silver Pagoda, and National Museum sit within a few blocks of each other near the Tonle Sap. The Tuol Sleng Museum is a short tuk-tuk ride south.
