**Cambodia Cooking Class**

Khmer cuisine is not as renowned as the Thai kitchen. Some people even say Cambodian food is nothing to write home about. Cambodia Cooking Class lets people discover the opposite. In one day travellers can learn the basic art of preparing traditional Khmer food. English-speaking instructor and every participant has his or her own cooking station.

The class is located on a breezy terrace in a Khmer-style villa. Included in the price: transportation to and from central Phnom Penh, a colour recipe booklet and a market tour.

Address: 14 Street 285 (or via frizz restaurant, 335 Sisowath Quay)

Website: www.cambodia-cooking-class.com

Hours: 9am - 5pm

Tel: +855(0)23-882314

Course price: US$ 20
