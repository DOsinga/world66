We are a specialized tour agency which offers day tour and overnight stay tour around Siem Reap. With our deep experiences in setting the tour programs, we sensitively set up and select the tour areas where are not influenced by tourism activities.

Buffalo Trails offers various tour programs to temples and forest and villages, buffalo cart and horse cart trek, to Kompong Khleang stilt village, and bird sanctuary in Prek Toal core area on Tonle Sap. Tours can arrange for half day, one days, overnight stay and 3 to 4 days program specializing locally around Siem Reap.

Buffalo Trails is a tour agent that practises responsible tourism in order to contribute a positive impact environmentally, and economically to local families.

If you wish to get more details on programs and prices, please have a look at our website.
