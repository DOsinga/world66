Central Asia consists of the seven countries, all of which are landlocked. Five are Soviet Union republics: [Kazakhstan](/asia/centralasia/kazakhstan), [Uzbekistan](/asia/centralasia/uzbekistan), [Kyrgyzstan](/asia/centralasia/kyrgyzstan), [Turkmenistan](/asia/centralasia/turkmenistan) and Tajikstan. [Afghanistan](/asia/centralasia/afghanistan) and [Mongolia](/asia/centralasia/mongolia) kept their indepenence although both have had a (short) period of Communist rule.


 With the exeption of [Mongolia](/asia/centralasia/mongolia) all of these countries are predominanlty muslim. Mongolia is one of the few Buddhist countries in the world.


 Herding has been a key element in the lifesyles of all the people of Central Asia. The traditional Yurt (nomadic tent) can be still be seen even in big cities.
