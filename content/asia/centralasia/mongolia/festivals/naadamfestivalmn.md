The funniest and happiest time for Mongolians is being during Naadam.
It is organizes between 11-13th of July in every year throughout all of
soums, provinces and cities at the same time and includes competition
of the fastest horses’ racing, wrestling of powerful wrestlers and
archery that named as Eriin Gurvan Naadam.
