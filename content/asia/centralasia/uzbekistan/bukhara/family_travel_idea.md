---
title: "Family Travel Ideas in Bukhara"
type: section
---

Bukhara is a charming trading town nestled on the historic Silk Road, an assortment of trade routes
stretching from China to Europe. Here are some fantastic family
vacation ideas in Bukhara.

**The Old Town Center**

The old town region is the epicenter of the cultural and historical
splendor of Bukhara and has been appropriately marked as a World
Heritage Site by UNESCO. Here you'll find fascinating old minarets, impressive
mosques that are a blend of Middle Eastern and European architecture
and several other monuments that date back to more than 2500 years.
Some attractions worth viewing are the exquisite Kalian Minaret, the
beautiful Kos Madras and the striking Samani Mausoleum-one of the most
ancient structures of the city that was erected by the Samanid rulers.
Watch the stunning embellishments of the mausoleum when
the natural light changes. The erstwhile Fortress Ark Citadel now acts
as a museum.

**English-Speaking Puppet Theater
**

This one is guaranteed to amuse the children and adults alike. Watch
outstanding and captivating performances by a bevy of brightly-dressed
and vivacious puppets. The skillful narration and superb dialogue
delivery combined with the exuberance with which the characters are
essayed makes this a must visit destination for family outings in
Bukhara.

**Carpet Hunting**

Visit the old town center to view some of the finest pieces of
Turkoman carpets made by the local tribes of Bukhara. The intricate
designs, stunning motifs and artistic embellishments are hard to find
elsewhere except probably in Turkey or Egypt. The rugs are dunked in
dyes that are manufactured from natural and hand-picked
ingredients. Visitors can catch a glimpse of the dye making process and
also witness the locals weave the carpets.

**Lyab-iHauz**

The Lyab iHauz is a region of immense historical and natural beauty. The area was once filled with ponds in the 17 th century, but now, only a few remain. This quarter of the town is known for its splendid architectural assemble and houses the Kukeldash Madrasah.

Bukhara has several historical sites and beguiling markets along with a puppet theater to keep the family travelers enthralled.
