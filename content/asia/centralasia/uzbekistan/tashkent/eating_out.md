title: "Eating Out"
type: section
---

My favorite restaurant in Tashkent would have to be Tblisi's. It's on the second floor overlooking the Amir Burger right across the street. They serve a very good shaslik - barbecue and with a side of fries makes for a good meal. Wash it down with a cold beer.

As mentioned, across the street is the Amir Burger. Uzbek fast food, cheap and a large selection. Different counters selling different foods. Very good, quick eats.
