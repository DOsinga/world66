Travel Guide

Chuxiong City is located Yunnan province of China, it's a country level city. The place is a wonderful and a unique city where there are lots of tourist attraction. One of the place where you can go in the place is the Zixi Mountain, where those nature lover spend there leisure time with there friends. Hua Fo Mountain is also one of the attraction in the place where you can unwind with your love ones during weekends. White Pogada is also located in the city. Oceania Hotel Yunnan is one of the most visited hotel in the city. You can also be relax in Yunnan, the place with a nice ambiance where you can spend time with your family.
