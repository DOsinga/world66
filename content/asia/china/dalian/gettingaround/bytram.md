If you’re not in a hurry, take the tram which provides you the charming
of old times. Three lines are in operation, with a flat rate of 1 yuan.
Trams glide around the city, and run until 11 pm.

The new line is #202. This runs from XinGong Street all the way down South past the Hi Tech Park.
