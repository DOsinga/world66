**Shops**

**Dalian Friendship Shopping Center **

The Dalian Friendship Shopping Center (No.6-8 Renmin Lu) is a modern department store provides everything includes traditional Chinese arts and crafts. 

**Tianjin Jie Market **

A more atmospheric shopping experience can be had at Tianjin Jie Market next to the Holiday Inn, near the train station on Changjiang Lu. The market has a good collection of stalls ideal for browsing. lkmqwlekmlqwkmelqkwm

**Tiyuchang Outdoor Market (Xigang district)**

For more modern goods such as clothes and electronics, check out Tiyuchang Outdoor Market (Xigang district), which teems with people on the weekends. 

**Rongsheng Underground Market **

Rongsheng Underground Market is the bustling market gives you some idea of the sheer scale and variety of seafood around Dalian. Good for anyone who doubts the freshness of fish in local restaurants. It locates in Jiefang Lu, in front of Laodong Park by TV tower. Open daily 9am.-6pm.
