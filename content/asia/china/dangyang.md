Travel Guide

The Hua Sha Hotel is known for it's luxury suites in Dangyang. The Dongyang Xuanyu Hotel is known for it's spectacular service. Both the Hua Sha Hotel and the Dongyang Xuanyu Hotel are fine examples of luxury Dangyang hotels. The Dongyang Xuanyu Hotel is located within the city of Dangyang. The Hua Sha Hotel is located within miles of the international airport. The Goethe Hotel is a great stay for it's great price. The Landison Plaza Hotel provides great amenities for it's budget price. Both the Goethe Hotel and Landison Plaza Hotel is located within miles of the international airport.
