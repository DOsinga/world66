Day 1: We meet you at Liangjian international airport and transfer you to your hotel. Free at leisure or arrange your time on your own. It is a good choice to see the night scenery in Gulin.

Day 2 : We will drive about 1 hour to the Zhujiang Pier for boarding the Li River Cruise boat and taking about 4 hours' cruise to Yangshuo. The picturesque Li River has been descriptions as "a grand silk ribbon with hills like jade hairpins' and is ideal for exploration on a boat. The meandering 65km cruise last 5 hours and takes you past bamboo groves, sleepy villages, endless peaks, and fisherman on rickety bamboo rafts fishing with cormorant birds.
