Great Name! The Paddy Field is a welcome addition to Guangzhou's bar scene. Great food, friendly staff and reasonably priced for an Irish bar. The Paddy Field gets better everytime you enter. You really can leave the hustle and bustle of the city behind you and enjoy a pint of Guinness or Kilkenny. Situated behind Garden Hotel.
