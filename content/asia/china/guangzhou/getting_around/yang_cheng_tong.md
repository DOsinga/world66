There is a card named "Yang Cheng Tong" which allows you to ride all major transportations (including subway, bus, and taxi) without using cash. the card also works in low cost facilities, such as convinient stores, supermarket, parking, and selected parks. 30 yuan deposit, with 500 yuan maximum value.
