South China's largest zoo, spread across 430,000 square metres on Xianlie Road. The collection runs to over 2,000 animals across 200 species, with a strong emphasis on animals native to China -- giant pandas, golden snub-nosed monkeys, red-crowned cranes, and South China tigers among them. The grounds are well-shaded and walkable, and there is a decent aquarium within the complex. It is a popular weekend outing for families, so expect crowds on Saturdays.
