**Restaurants**

**Jiulong Restaurant**

Opposite the Osmanthus Hotel, this large restaurant is quite well priced, with the main restaurants downstairs and bar upstairs  

**Yueyalou Restaurant**

A nice little restaurant in picturesque setting, set amongst small karsts in Seven Star Park. Cuisine includes local and regional dishes.  

**Yiyuan Restaurant**

This place offers Sichuanese food in a cozy little atmosphere, and tasteful all-wood decor. No credit cards.  

address: Nanhuan Lu

openinghours: Open till 9.30pm.

**Tailian Hotel**

The Cantonese restaurant here still offers the best dim sum in town, though as in every dim sum restaurant the world over you have to get there indecently soon after the end of breakfast.
