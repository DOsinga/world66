**Points of Interest**

**Magic night of Li River**

Magic lighting light up the Elephant Trunk Hill, the Fubo Hill and the Folded Brocade Hill along the Li River. Taste the local ethnic culture while the cormorants fishing at the light of lanterns hanged at the front of rafts.  

**The Folkway Garden **

The Folkway garden located in the Peach-blossom Villa, in which is a place folk dancing and singing performances are held every day. Performers of the Miao, the Dai, the Zhuang, the Dong and the Mosuo sing songs of their nationality and dance in their traditional way very enthusiastically to welc [more..](/asia/china/guilin/nightlife/thefolkwaygarden)

**Hoover Palace**

Hoover Palace was originally the provincial Art Studio of Guangxi, set up by famous opera entertainer Mr.Ouyang Yuqian in 1944. It is now a first-class entertaining center. 

**The Li River Folk Custom Centre**

Being a galaxy of folk custom of the Miao, the Yao, the Dong and the Zhuang, where you can not only appreciate the exquisite architecture of these ethnic groups, but also can enjoy their exotic arts and dietetic culture
