Dadonghai is a compact beach resort about 3km south-east of [Sanya](/asia/china/hainanisland/sanya) — ten minutes by minibus, or a pleasant hour's walk along the coast. It draws a younger, more budget-conscious crowd than Yalong Bay, and has a scrappier, more lived-in feel for it.

The beach is the draw: warm, clear water with decent coral not far offshore. Snorkelling and diving are well-organised here, with professional instructors willing to take first-timers under the water. Beach umbrellas rent for around 50 yuan for a half day. The seafood restaurants lining the streets just back from the sand are the obvious place to eat — pick from the day's catch straight out of the nets.

There's a low-key bar scene, including at least one place with live blues music and a proper cocktail list. For a beach resort this compact, it punches above its weight for a good night out.
