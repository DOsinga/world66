---
title: "Day Trips in Hainan island"
type: section
---

Hainan Island, which is located on the southern tip of China, is considered to be the "Hawaii of China." It is famous because of its good climate, deep blue sea and its beautiful beaches. The unspoiled beauty of the island continues to charm visitors and it has evolved into a prime destination for tourists who want to have a relaxing vacation. Golf courses and new hotels are being built to accommodate the increasing number of tourists who want to visit the place. The **Hainan day trips** guide lists interesting tourist spots near Hainan Island.

**Huguangyan National Scenic Spot**

This scenic area which can be found in Zhanjiang is a must-see destination for tourists. It consists of a volcanic lake which scientists assessed as a maar, a crater formed after a volcanic eruption. Basalt rocks are also found around the lake. Much of the attention the volcanic lake gets from visitors is due to its interesting formation and history dating back thousands of years.

**Cunjin Park**

The Cunjin Bridge Park is known for its famous scenery in Zhanjiang. The lush greenery and the complimenting beauty of the lake and mountain adds to the charm of the place. Nature lovers will have a great time in Cunjin Park, with its flower garden and the beautiful species of birds. At the entrance, tourists will awe at the beauty of the stone carving, which represents a farmer from the Qing Dynasty. While enjoying the beauty of the surroundings, one can learn a lot from the history of an era.

**Donghai Island**

Considered to be a favorite among beach lovers, Donghai Island has 28 kilometers of sand beach, clear blue waters and an aquatic facility for the whole family. Time is slow in this island, where the wind and waves meet. Visitors can camp around the beach and abundant fish are available for cooking. There is also a fishing village, where one can mingle with local fisherman and simply observe the life of the island people. The villas and houses are available to rent for a reasonable price and several tiny restaurants serve fresh sea-food around the island.

**Naozhau Island**Naozhau Island was made from volcanic rock thousands of years ago and is considered China's biggest volcanic island. The island is filled with corals, seaweeds and a rich marine life, thus making fishing the prime source of income for the locals. It is also rich in the political history of the Song Dynasty, having served as a station for the royal government. Naozhau Island is also home to one of the last crystal-built lighthouses in China, making it a famous landmark in Zhanjiang.

Hainan Island is a beautiful place worth visiting, with its pristine waters and beaches, and its surrounding parks and islands are great for day trips.
