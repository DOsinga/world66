Air: There are five flights from Haikou to [Beijing](/asia/china/beijing) (20 weekly, 4 hours), [Guangzhou](/asia/china/guangzhou) (8 daily, 50 minutes), Shenzhen (5 daily, 1 hour), [Hong Kong](/asia/china/hongkong) every week (1 hour), as well as a weekly connection to [Bangkok](/asia/thailand/bangkok).

 A ferry service also runs to [Guangzhou](/asia/china/guangzhou). Haikou Melian Airport (open on 25 May 1999) is 25km from Haikou. You will pay 10-15 yuan by taxi.

 Phoenix is the name of the airport of Sanya, with flights to Beijing and Shanghai twice a week and Guangzhou 15 times a week.
