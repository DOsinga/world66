If you are going to be exploring **Sanya bars and cafes**, you will find there are different varieties of cuisine and tastes you can have at each place.

#### Pirates Restaurant and Bar

The Pirates Restaurant and Bar is located near the beach and offers plenty of drink and cuisine choices. You can enjoy any of the house favorites and the variety of cocktails. You can relax with the beautiful views of the beach and the city. The bar serves seafood dishes along with other light meals. You can enjoy the look of the bar and the lively crowd.

#### Roma Italian

This bar and caf&eacute; is located on the beach, and you can enjoy the variety of drinks while you taste the Italian cuisine which is served in large portions. You can also try some of the wines from the wine list which features wines from all over the world. If you are looking for a place to relax and enjoy quiet music, great food and drinks, this is one of the Sanya bars and cafes you should visit.

#### Seawave Bar and Caf&eacute;

This bar is found on Dadonghai Beach in Sanya and offers a variety of cocktails, beers and wine. You can also try any of the fun and light meals served in the bar. There is plenty to enjoy about the bar including the live music and the beautiful scenery all around the beach.
