---
title: "Beaches"
type: section
---

Sanya has several distinct beaches, each with a different atmosphere. Yalong Bay is the most upmarket — a crescent of fine sand lined with international resort hotels, relatively uncrowded, with individual resort beach fronts. Dadonghai, 2 km from downtown, has more local character and livelier surroundings. Sanya Bay is the longest and most accessible, stretching 15 km along the city's western edge. Shimei Bay, further along the coast, has clearer water and is good for snorkelling.
