---
title: "Eating Out"
type: section
---

Sanya's seafood is among the best reasons to visit. The Dadonghai area is the main eating district, with numerous restaurants serving fresh catches at reasonable prices. Dongjiao Yelin is one of the most popular multi-floor restaurants here — the menu runs to over a hundred dishes, described in English, with Dongshan lamb and harmony crab (in honey and chili sauce) as standouts. The market at Sanya sells tropical fruit, live seafood, and coconut products.
