title: "Eating Out in Sanya"
type: section
---

The **Sanya restaurants** reflect the colorful local lifestyle. Sanya is compared to Hawaii and is located in the southern part of China in the Hainan Islands. There are several restaurants, cafes and bars that cater to the visitors. Don't let the unending choices confuse you; eat at the below mentioned restaurant to experience the true flavor of Sanya.
**
Dongjiao Yelin**

Walk or take the local transport to the Dadonghai area in Sanya, and eat at Dongjiao Yelin. The popularity of Dongjiao will automatically pull you towards the restaurant. Located right at the center, the over bearing structure cannot be missed. Dining here is a complete bliss as the chef presents you will a gamut of local dishes. Buzzing with tourists, this dining experience cannot be substituted. Seating is spread across three floors. You can avoid the crowd by going straight to the third floor.

**The Menu**

The best part about the menu is that each of its 100 plus dishes are explained in English. Even though you are venturing into the unknown, you will have control over what you are ordering. The must try dish at this restaurant is the Dongshan lamb dish. This dish is red in color and tempts and teases all your senses. The tender meat of the lamb melts in your mouth. The harmony crab is cooked in a honey and chili sauce with a mix of local herbs. Dig into the crab and enjoy the juicy meat. It won't be a surprise if you end up having all your meals at Dongjiao Yelin.
