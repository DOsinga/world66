---
title: "Festivals in Sanya"
type: section
---

**Sanya festivals** are more distinct than any festivals celebrated in other parts of the world. These festivals will make your visit in the city a memorable and colorful one.

**Tianya Haijiao International Wedding Festival**

Tianya Haijiao International Wedding Festival is a rare festival that is held every 7th day of July 7th (traditional Chinese calendar). It is a big wedding celebration wherein the couples from around the world come to Sanya for their wedding ceremony or honeymoon.

**Dragon–boaters Festival (DuanWu Festival) **
Dragon-boaters, or DuanWu Festival, is a festival in Sanya that is celebrated every 5th day of May in the Chinese Lunar Calendar. During this event, the dragon boaters from all over the place gather in Sanya to take part in this momentous festival.

**End of the Earth Moon Happy Festival**

The End of the Earth Moon Happy Festival** **is a festival that takes place every August 15 in the Chinese Lunar Calendar. In this event, they organize a party that includes a folk singing competition, folk dance, and a lantern show at the end of the earth. This festival is held at the End of the Earth Sightseeing Zone in Sanya.

**China**** Nanshan Longevity Culture Festival**

China Nanshan Longevity Culture Festival is a Sanya festival that happens every September 9 in the Chinese Lunar Calendar. The longest life expectancy in China is found in Sanya. To celebrate this, a cultural entertainment show takes place in the Nanshan Cultural Tourism Area. The main event in this festival is the mountain hiking of Sanya people aged 100 years above.
