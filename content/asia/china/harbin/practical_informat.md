title: "Practical Information in Harbin"
type: section
---

For the best in luxury in Harbin, China the Heilongjiang Kunlun Hotel and the Harbin LongMen Hotel offers some of the best amenities that this city has to offer, these hotels are also great with features and unique design that highlight the city. Harbin LongMen Hotel is located near the downtown areas of Harbin which offers the best in attraction from various resources. However, this hotel attraction is the Hongbo Square commercial center. The Heilongjiang Kunlun Hotel is also located close by in the downtown area with attractions which include Sun Island and Unit 731 Museum. If you are looking for just a good safe place to sleep and do not care about amenities then the Jin Gu Hotel and the Splendor Century Hotel are two hotels that are more budget friendly. The Jin Gu Hotel is located near Zhongyang walk street which offers attractions and wonderful views of the Song Hua River. The Splendor Century Hotel is located near the downtown area which features attractions including the Unit 731 Museum
