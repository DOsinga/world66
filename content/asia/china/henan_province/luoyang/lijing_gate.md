The imposing gate tower marks the entrance to Luoyang's old town district. Climb to the top for views over the surrounding streets, then wander the old town area stretching south, where narrow lanes hold small shops, street food vendors, and a sense of how Luoyang looked before the modern city took over. The area around Lijing Gate is the best place in Luoyang for an evening stroll and casual street food.
