The island's shopping heartland and probably the most crowded square kilometre on Hong Kong Island — Times Square, Sogo, and the pedestrian streets around Fashion Walk pull relentless weekend traffic. Beyond the malls, Causeway Bay has a strong Japanese character, a long-running tradition of cheap Cantonese restaurants, and Victoria Park (the island's largest open space, and the former site of the annual June 4th candlelight vigil). The daily Noonday Gun, fired since the 1860s, still sounds at the waterfront. Causeway Bay MTR.
