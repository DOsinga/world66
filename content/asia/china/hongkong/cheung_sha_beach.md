The longest beach in Hong Kong stretches along Lantau's south coast, split into upper and lower sections. Lower Cheung Sha has a couple of beachside restaurants and is the more popular stretch; Upper Cheung Sha is quieter and backed by trees. The water is clean and lifeguards are on duty in summer. Take bus 1 or 2 from Mui Wo, or bus 11 from Tung Chung.
