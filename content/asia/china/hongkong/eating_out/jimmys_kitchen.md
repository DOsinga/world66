[Don't let name turn you off!](/asia/china/hongkong/eating_out)

Having dined at this restaurant since 1962(it's been here since 1928)- this is the place to begin your Hong Kong experience- the menu includes something for every taste and will not break the bank. Last time with the three of us having drinks, appetizers, main course and dessert the bill was about $200(USD). I enjoyed the Steak Diane and my gal who ordinarily will pass up the meat dishes for fish, etc loved the taste of my steak although she ordered fish for herself.

The ambiance is classy modern and smart and the service was perfect.

Don't show up without reservations!

The location is Wyndham Street in the central district- all the cab drivers know exactly where it is.
