A large, upmarket shopping mall directly connected to Admiralty MTR station, with over 160 shops including Lane Crawford (the territory's premier department store), Harvey Nichols, and international luxury brands. Also has a food court, several restaurants, and cinemas. Useful as a rain shelter and air-conditioned walkway through Admiralty regardless of whether you plan to shop.
