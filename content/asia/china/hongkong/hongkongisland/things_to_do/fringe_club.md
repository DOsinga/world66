A Hong Kong arts institution housed in a Victorian brick building from 1913 that served as the dairy farm cold storage. The Fringe Club hosts live music, theatre, exhibitions, and comedy in a relaxed venue that's a genuine alternative to the corporate entertainment options in Central. There's a rooftop bar and a restaurant. The building is a heritage landmark and visually distinctive among the glass towers around it.
