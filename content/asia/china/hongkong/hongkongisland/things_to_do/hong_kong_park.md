A well-designed urban park sandwiched between skyscrapers in Central, with a large walk-in aviary housing over 150 bird species among tropical vegetation, a visual arts centre, a conservatory, and the Museum of Tea Ware in the Flagstaff House — the oldest surviving colonial building in Hong Kong. A good place to slow down mid-city. Two minutes' walk from Admiralty MTR via the Pacific Place mall. Open daily, free admission.
