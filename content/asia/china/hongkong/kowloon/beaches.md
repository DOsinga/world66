---
title: "Beaches"
type: section
---

Kowloon itself has no beaches — the peninsula faces Victoria Harbour, which is not for swimming. The nearest beaches are on Hong Kong Island's south side (Repulse Bay, Deep Water Bay, Shek O, Stanley), all accessible by bus from Central in 30–40 minutes. For beaches with more space and cleaner water, the outlying islands (Lantau's Cheung Sha, Lamma Island, Cheung Chau) are the better option and easily reached by ferry from the Central Piers.

For swimmers staying in Kowloon, Kowloon Park has a public swimming pool complex.
