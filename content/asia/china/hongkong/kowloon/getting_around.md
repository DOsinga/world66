---
title: "Getting Around"
type: section
---

There is no tram like on Hong Kong Island to get you around town. Kowloon's town centre, however, can be explored easily by foot. The train station, where the trains for China and the New Territories depart, is located at Canton Road. West of this train station is the Cross-Harbour Tunnel that links Kowloon to Hong Kong island. Nearby is also the Hung Hom Ferry Pier, which offers services to Central, Wan Chai, and North Point.
