---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

Kowloon is connected to the rest of Hong Kong and the mainland by MTR, ferry, and bus. See the main [Hong Kong Getting There](/asia/china/hongkong/getting_there) page for information on arriving in Hong Kong from overseas.

From the airport on Lantau, the Airport Express stops at Kowloon Station in Jordan (20 minutes from the airport). From Kowloon Station, the MTR Tung Chung Line and various buses reach Tsim Sha Tsui and other parts of Kowloon.

From Hong Kong Island, the Star Ferry connects Tsim Sha Tsui to Central and Wan Chai (7 minutes; ferries run every few minutes). The MTR cross-harbour tunnels (Tsuen Wan Line, Kwun Tong Line) provide faster connections. The Cross-Harbour Tunnel carries buses and taxis between Causeway Bay and Kowloon.

From the mainland, the East Rail Line terminates at Hung Hom, and the high-speed rail link arrives at West Kowloon terminus from Guangzhou, Shenzhen, and beyond.
