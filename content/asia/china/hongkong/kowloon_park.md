A substantial park in the middle of Tsim Sha Tsui, entered via Nathan Road or the Haiphong Road end. Has a good-sized swimming pool complex (open in summer), a Sculpture Walk with large outdoor pieces, a bird lake with flamingos, and lawns that fill with people on Sunday afternoons. A useful place to escape the noise and shopping of the surrounding streets. MTR Tsim Sha Tsui Station, Exit A1.
