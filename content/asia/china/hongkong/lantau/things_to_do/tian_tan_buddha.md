The 34-metre bronze seated Buddha at Ngong Ping is visible from miles away and has become the symbol of Lantau. Climb the 268 steps for close-up views and a panorama of the surrounding hills. The statue was completed in 1993 and sits above Po Lin Monastery. Entry to the base is free; a meal ticket for the monastery's vegetarian restaurant gets you into the exhibition halls beneath the statue.
