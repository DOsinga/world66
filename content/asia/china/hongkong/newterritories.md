The mainland north of Kowloon, including some small islands, is known as the Northern Territories. Although the cities are expanding rapidly, this still is the area where travellers can see old villages, duck farms, rice paddies and mountains. The Kam Tim walled villages are quite touristy but at the same time offer a good insight into how life used to be in the 16th century. The China viewpoint is not far from these villages. However, less people make the effort to climb to the top to catch a glimpse of China, since it became more easily accessible recently. It would be a shame to rush through the NT in order to arrive in China as quickly as possible. Not in the least because the NT area offers some excellent hiking possibilites.
