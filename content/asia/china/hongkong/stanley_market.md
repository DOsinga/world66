A covered market on the south side of Hong Kong Island, selling silks, linens, Chinese antiques, clothing, and souvenirs. Atmospheric in a relaxed way, with the Stanley waterfront nearby for lunch or a drink after browsing. Open daily from about 10:30am to 6:30pm. Take bus 6, 6X, or 260 from Exchange Square in Central; the ride takes about 40 minutes along the scenic south coast road.
