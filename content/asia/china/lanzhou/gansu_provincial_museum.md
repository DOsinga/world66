One of the best museums in northwest China, with an exceptional Silk Road collection. The star exhibit is the Flying Horse of Gansu (also called the Galloping Horse Treading on a Swallow), a small Han Dynasty bronze horse in mid-gallop with one hoof resting on a flying swallow — an object of extraordinary elegance and confidence. The Neolithic painted pottery from Dadiwan and the Han Dynasty wooden documents are also well worth your time. Xijin Dong Lu, near the Friendship Hotel. Open Tuesday–Sunday.
