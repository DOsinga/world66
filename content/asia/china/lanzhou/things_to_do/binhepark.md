A long riverside park following the northern bank of the Yellow River through the city, known locally as the Green Travel Corridor. A popular place for early morning walkers, tai chi practitioners, and people flying kites. Sculptures along the route reference Silk Road history — the Camel Team on the Silk Road and the Mother of Yellow River statue are the most photographed. Free to enter.
