---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Luoyang's main attractions are its Buddhist heritage sites and the famous peony festival. The Longmen Grottoes, a UNESCO World Heritage Site south of the city, contain over 100,000 carved Buddhist statues in caves along the Yi River. The White Horse Temple, east of the city, is considered the first Buddhist temple built in China. Every April, the Peony Festival draws visitors from across the country to see the city's gardens in bloom.
