---

Besides winning a million in one of the casinos (or being bankrupt for the rest of your life), Macau offers some other events that usually claim the content of your wallet as well. The Macau Jockey Club organizes horseracing contests. If you’re more interested in dog-racing, go to the Macau Canidrome, where the greyhounds run for their lives (or rather, for their owners'). Once a year, the Macau Grand Prix takes place. It lasts for two days and the cars and motorcycles follow the track throughout town.
