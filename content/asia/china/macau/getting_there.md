---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

The easy way to arrive is by **ferry from Hong Kong** — TurboJet and Cotai Water Jet run boats every 15 to 30 minutes to Macau's Outer Harbour Ferry Terminal and to the Taipa terminal near the airport, a one-hour crossing. The 55 km **Hong Kong–Zhuhai–Macau Bridge**, the world's longest sea crossing, opened in 2018 and now offers a faster bus link. **Macau International Airport** on Taipa handles flights from across East and Southeast Asia. Overland, you can walk or bus across the Barrier Gate (Portas do Cerco) from Zhuhai in mainland China.
