Want to have a shot at it? Well, then you've come to just the right spot. Macau's casinos will definitely keep you awake. Whenever you feel like spending your well-earned money on something else, go to a neighbouring bar or nightclub or sing 'Money' at a Karaoke bar. Check out the casinos (especially the floating one), the clubs and the rest of the caboodle.
