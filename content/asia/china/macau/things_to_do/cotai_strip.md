Macau's Vegas — a reclaimed spit of land between the old islands of Taipa and Coloane now lined with enormous themed casinos: The Venetian Macao (with its full-sized Grand Canal and gondolas), the Wynn, MGM Cotai, Galaxy, Studio City, City of Dreams. The combined gambling revenue of the strip is several times that of the Las Vegas Strip. Even teetotal non-gamblers will find the architectural spectacle worth an evening walk, and several of the resorts host world-class concerts and Cirque du Soleil-style shows.
