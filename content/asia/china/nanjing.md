Nanjing is one of China's more attractive major cities. As the capital of Jiangsu province, Nanjing is the political, economic and cultural centers in the province a city full of antiquity of China. It is on the southern bank of the Yangtze River, and the junction close to the Great Canal, which made it a transport hub for centuries. Historically, there have been ten dynasties that established their capitals here, so Nanjing hence the fame of "the Capital of Ten Dynasties". With the great historical background, Nanjian now is a famous tourism city in China. The ten-li Qinhuai River, especially the Confucius Temple area, both culture and trade have been most thriving.
