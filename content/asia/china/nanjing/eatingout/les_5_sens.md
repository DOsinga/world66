Les 5 Sens is the oldest French restaurant in Nanjing, opened for more than 3 years, and the only one with a French Chef.

It proposes a large range of traditional and family homemade French dishes such as:
- Burgundy snails
- Foie Gras
- Burgundy Beef
- Quiche Lorraine
- Confit de Canard
- Terrine de Campagne
- Crème Brûlée etc.
There are also some special creations from its French Chef.

The price quality/ratio is one of the best in the city in a cozy and friendly ambience.

It is also well located in the middle of the University Quarter between Nanshida and Nanda, 100 meters from Shanghai Lu.
