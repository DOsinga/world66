Les 5 Sens is the oldest French Restaurant in the city, opened for more than 3 years now and the only one with a French Chef.

Located in the old University Quarter between Nanda and Nanshida (100 meters from Shanghai Lu), the restaurant offers a cosy and friendly ambiance with its 3 rooms of 10 persons each.

The French Chef proposes several traditionnal and family dishes such as:
- Burgundy snails
- Burgundy Beef
- Quiche Lorraine
- Foie Gras
- Crème Brûlée
- Profiterolles etc...
but also some special dishes created by our French Chef always with an excellent ratio price quality.
