**Salsa Mexican Cantina**

**
**This authentic Mexican restaurant offers your usual Mexican dishes and some interesting twists to dazzle your taste buds. Dishes include Pork Chili, Chicken Mole, and Enchiladas. Breakfast is fantastic and great value at around 10 rmb; lunch is just 26 rmb and includes a free beer or cola, and the evening meals are huge and fantastic value for money. I would definitely recommend it!
