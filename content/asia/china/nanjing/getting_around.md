---
title: "Getting around"
type: section
---

Taxi: Not expensive and really the best way to get around town. 

  Bus: The bus network is comprehensive but the system really overloads during rush hour and the all-Chinese signs are confusing for foreigners. However, English-language maps with bus routes are available from larger hotels. 

  Bicycle: Bicycle rental is available from some of the smaller hotels. The cost is 15 yuan per day or 1 yuan per hour.
