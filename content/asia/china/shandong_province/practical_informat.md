type: section

If you are used to luxury, you should consider The Fuwah Hotel, a 5 star hotel situated in the Weifang high-tech development zone, right next to the Fuwah Amusement Park. Another good choice is The Weifang International Financial Tower, a 4 star hotel located in the heart of the Weifang Financial District. You will never get bored if you chose to stay in the Fuwah Hotel - The Fuwah Amusement Park, featuring a first-class Waterworld and many other attractions, is just a few minutes walk. The Weifang International Financial Tower has everything that a 4-star hotel should have to offer, including facilities for tourists both on business, or pleasure trips. You can also sample local or Western dishes in several of the hotel's restaurants. If you're tight on budget, pick from the Homeinns Weifang, a 2 star hotel in the Weicheng District, and Home Inn - Weifang Dongfeng East Street, a 2 star hotel located near the Taihua Commercial Plaza. Homeinns Weifang has a strategic location adjacent to several popular attractions: the Museum, People's Park, and Bailanghe Square. Home Inn - Weifang Dongfeng East Street Branch is an ideal place to stay with its excellent location, convenient transportation, and good service. Weifang is known as the "Kite Capital of the World".
