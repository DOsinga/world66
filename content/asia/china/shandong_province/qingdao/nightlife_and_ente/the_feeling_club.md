type: poi

---

This is a favorite of young Chinese in Qingdao. Located on Hong
Kong Lu near Jusco (but on the other side of the road), this club has
techno music all the time and a crowded hydraulic dance floor every
night of the week. It's best to hold off until at least 12 to go,
because you don't want to be early. Drinks are a bit pricy, but
you can always buy one and make a dash for the dance floor.
