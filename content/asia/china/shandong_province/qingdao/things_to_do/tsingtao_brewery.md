The brewery founded by a joint German-British venture in 1903, now the second-largest beer producer in China and one of the best-known Chinese exports worldwide. The factory runs guided tours that walk through the history of the brewery and production process; free samples of filtered and unfiltered beer are served at the end, including a dark ale. Take bus No. 25 to the brewery. English-speaking guides available.
