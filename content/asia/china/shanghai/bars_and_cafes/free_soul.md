A relaxed neighbourhood bar at the Yanan Xi Lu end of Fahuazhen Lu, with a home-away-from-home vibe. Customers take over the music system from an eclectic CD selection, and the English-speaking staff chat with guests like friends. Ground floor has a foosball table; upstairs, sofas and opium-style couches surround a pool table. Drinks reasonably priced — shots and cocktails around 40 RMB, beers from 20 RMB. A surprisingly broad coffee menu makes it work for non-drinkers too.
