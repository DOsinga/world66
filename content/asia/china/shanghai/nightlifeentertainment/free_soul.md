A bar on the more respectable Yanan Xi Lu end of Fahuazhen Lu, Free Soul aims for a home-away-from-home atmosphere. Customers take over the music system from an eclectic CD selection, and the English-speaking staff chat with guests like friends. The ground-floor bar area has a foosball table; upstairs, comfortable sofas and opium-style couches surround a pool table. Drinks are reasonably priced, with shots and cocktails around 40 RMB and beers at 20 RMB. A surprisingly broad coffee menu makes it a place for drinkers and non-drinkers alike.
