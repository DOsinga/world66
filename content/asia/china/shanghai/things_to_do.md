title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Shanghai's headline sights are a mix of the obvious and the addictive. Start with the Bund and the Pudong skyline opposite, then walk Yu Garden and the surrounding Old Town bazaar, lose an afternoon in the French Concession, and devote at least half a day to the Shanghai Museum on People's Square. Beyond that, the city rewards aimless wandering: every neighbourhood has its own texture, from the Art Deco villas of the former concession to the canal-front lanes around Qibao.
