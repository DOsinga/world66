---
title: "Bars and Cafes"
type: section
---

Chengdu is famous across China for its teahouse culture, and an afternoon spent drinking jasmine tea, eating sunflower seeds and watching old men play mahjong is one of the city's signature experiences. The People's Park teahouse and the courtyards around Wenshu Monastery are classic spots. For Western-style nightlife, head to the bars and clubs around Jiuyanqiao and the Lan Kwai Fong development on the south bank of the Jinjiang River.
