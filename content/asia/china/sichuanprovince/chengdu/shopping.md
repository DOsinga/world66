The **Chengdu shopping** experience offers everything that a visitor could seek, including street markets and massive shopping malls. In fact, Chengdu is quickly becoming a sought after shopping destination, drawing comparisons with Hong Kong. However, Chengdu offers a more traditional flavor among its shopping locales. In many places, the shops still exude the ancient heritage of the Shu Empire.

**Shopping for Chinese Brocade**

The traditional Shu Brocade is still popular across Asia and Chengdu. Silkworm breeding is prevalent across Chengdu and it acts as a major, wholesale location for silk. Thus, travelers can look forward to getting some serious discounts on silk items. The Shu Brocade is treasured across China. It is also picked up by visitors as a souvenir. Similarly, the Shu form of embroidery presents brilliant, detailed craftsmanship. Visitors can choose from a wide range of items that are sold with Shu embroidery including pillow-covers, quilts clothes and canvas shoes. Recommended locations to buy Chinese brocade in Chengdu include:

- Shu Brocade Factory, Caotang East Road
- Sichuan Arts & Crafts Store, Sichuan Exhibition Hall
- Hehuachi Distribution Center, Erhuan
