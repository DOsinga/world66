Chengdu's must-see is the **Chengdu Research Base of Giant Panda Breeding** on the city's northern edge, where you can watch dozens of pandas at much closer range than at any zoo. Beyond the pandas, the city's pleasures are unhurried: the temple-and-tomb complex of Wuhou, the Tang-era poet's retreat at Du Fu Thatched Cottage, the Buddhist calm of Wenshu Monastery, and the lantern-lit lanes of Jinli and Kuanzhai Xiangzi.
