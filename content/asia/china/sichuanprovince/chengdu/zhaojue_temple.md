A Tang dynasty Chan (Zen) Buddhist temple in the north of the city, next door to Chengdu Zoo. It was an important seat of meditation practice for centuries and counts the famous Yuanwu Keqin among its abbots. Heavily damaged in the Cultural Revolution and rebuilt since, it remains an active monastery with a working community of monks.
