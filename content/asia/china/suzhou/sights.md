### [Garden of the Master of the Nets](/asia/china/suzhou/sights/garden_of_the_mast)

It's the smallest of the gardens, but probably the most popular.
Proof? It was used as a model for the Ming Hall at the
Metropolitan Museum of Art (New York City, USA).

The garden takes it name from its previous resident, a retired
government official who was said to have declared that he'd rather be a
fisherman.

type: Parks, Gardens and Zoos



openingHours: 8am to 5pm

### [Tiger Hill (Hu Qiu)](/asia/china/suzhou/sights/tiger_hill_hu_qiu)

Pisa in Italy has a leaning tower, and Suzhou has a leaning pagoda. Set
aloft a small hill, the tilt of the pagoda was "corrected", and is an
unusual sight.

type: Landmarks



### [Spend a great day in Suzhou!](/asia/china/suzhou/sights/spend_a_great_day)

Start the day off with brunch at the Sheraton Hotel downtown. Then walk out of the hotel to the rear and visit the Panmen Gate Park. It is a great afternoon and a wonderful way to walk off all the calories from the brunch!

type: Hotspots
