---

On a low hill northwest of the old town stands the Yunyan Pagoda, a seven-storey brick tower from the 10th century that leans noticeably — Suzhou's answer to Pisa, though rather less touristed. The pagoda has been stabilised and is no longer at risk of falling, but the lean is still visible and gives the whole site a slightly surreal quality. The wooded hill is pleasant to walk regardless.
