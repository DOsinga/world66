---
title: "Shopping"
type: section
---

**Binjiang Dao** and **Heping Lu** form Tianjin's main central shopping district — a pedestrianised strip of department stores and brand shops. **Ancient Culture Street** near the drum tower is the place for calligraphy brushes, Yangliuqing new-year prints, clay figurines and shadow-puppet souvenirs. For antiques and jade, try the weekend market at **Shenyang Dao**.
