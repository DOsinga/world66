### [Heping Foreign Foods Market](/asia/china/tianjin/shopping/heping_foreign_foo)

Heping is the best foreign foods market in central Tianjin. They offer just about everything: spaghetti, pasta, sauce, mac n' cheese, pancake stuff, good veggies, tons of different cheeses, cereals, snacks, caviar, bacon. Wow! They got a lot. Also they offer specialty cuts of meat that they deliver to your door!

Tim Tams, Cheese, Caviar, Bacon, Pancake Mix, Wine, French Bread, Cream Cheese, Chips & Salsa... All the hard to find stuff!
