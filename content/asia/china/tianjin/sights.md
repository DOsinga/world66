Tianjin's a great place. I recommend a walk around the Concession
Areas. There's numerous districts where Europeans lived during
occupation at the start of the 20th Century (approx!) - these include:
British, American, Italian, French and Russian. TJ's architecture has a
great European influence as a result, and a lot of the banks etc. used
to be official concession buildings.

### [Tianjin Radio and TV Tower](/asia/china/tianjin/sights/tianjin_radio_and)

Formerly the tallest tower in Asia and the third tallest in the world, the tower is situated near Nankai University and can be seen for miles around. It is also very close to Alibabas bar and restaurant. Admission is 50 Yuan and a map, GPS grid and pictures can be found on the website.
