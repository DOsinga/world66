The Potala Palace dominates the Lhasa skyline and also a symbolic for Tibetan. It is a 13 stories and 1000 rooms palace and once winter residence of the Dalai Lama. The construction of the present structure began during the reign of the fifth Dalai Lama in 1645 and took more than 50 years to complete
