**Xeger** (also spelled Shelkar or New Tingri) is a small town at 4,350 metres in southern Tibet, built at the foot of the ruins of Xegar Dzong, an old hilltop fortress. It sits just off the Friendship Highway between [Shigatse](/asia/china/tibet/shigatse) and the Nepalese border.

The town is primarily a stopping point for expeditions heading to Mount Everest — the turnoff to Everest Base Camp is nearby. There is not much to Xeger itself beyond basic guesthouses and a few restaurants, but the dzong ruins above the town are worth the climb for the views across the high plateau. At this altitude, take it easy — the thin air is felt immediately.
