Wuxi, China has hotels for both those on a budget, and those who are looking for some luxury. The Super 8 Wuxi Ai Di and Wuxi Cts Grand Hotel are both conveniently located within minutes of the airport and famous parks, while also offering basic amenities. For a more discerning traveler, the Hotel One Suzhou and the Traders Fudu Hotel Changzhoi offers a bit of luxury. The Hotel One Suzhou has unique amenities such as a spa tub and a pool with wave lounge chairs. It is conveniently located in the city's New District economic development area. The Traders Fudu Hotel Changzhou is ideal for business travelers and has 15 meeting rooms and two ballrooms to meet with clients, and is located in the Xinbei high-technology development zone of the city.
