There are many nice, expensive and yet popular Chinese resaurants around Nan Er Huan. Sichuan food and Guangdong Food are most popular. Hot Pot restaurants can be found almost everywhere. Little Sheep seems to be quiet good. Tasting local snacks in the Muslim Quarters is quiet a treat. Cafe shops are mainly located in De Fu Xiang.

**Wuyi Fandian**

A small canteen outside this hotel serves dumplings and steamed buns (mantou). The Wuyi also has a smarter third-floor restaurant specializing in Anhui and Jiangsu cuisine.  

address: 351 Dong Da Jie

**Dong Da Jie**

A highly decorated restaurant famous for dumpling banquets, but it tends to cater for larger groups so it might be difficult to sneak in as an individual or couple.
