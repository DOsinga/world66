title: Great Mosque
type: poi

One of the largest and oldest mosques in China, founded in 742 AD, tucked into the alleyways of the Muslim Quarter. The architecture is unusual — Chinese pavilion style rather than domed Islamic. Non-Muslims can visit the gardens but not the prayer hall. Worth an hour alongside a walk through the surrounding food market.
