Drifters, on Yangshuo's busy West Street, has been turning out what many regulars call the best apple crumble in China for years. It became a rite of passage among the town's expat and backpacker crowd -- the kind of dish people specifically recommend before you even ask about food.

The crumble is generous, served warm with a crumbly, buttery topping. Pair it with a coffee or a cold beer and watch the West Street foot traffic go by. Drifters also does reasonable Western and Chinese dishes, but the apple crumble is the reason you are here.
