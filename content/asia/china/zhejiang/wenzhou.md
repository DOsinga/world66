Travel Guide

Wenzhou is the third city of Zhejiang Province and one of the economic
power houses of the region. Famed for its businessmen and women (and
the *Wenzhou model* of business), Wenzhou is frequently used by
Western media as a barometer of the Chinese export economy; among other
things, Wenzhou manufactures around 90% of the world's spectacles and
massive quantities of shoes. Wenzhou is not on many China tour
itineraries and despite being a Chinese cultural hotpot, it is not the
most foreigner friendly of cities. However, there are plenty of things to do and see should you find
yourself with time on your hands in Wenzhou. The city has a resident
foreigner community that is below average for the city's size (100-200)
although foreign businessmen often pass through.
