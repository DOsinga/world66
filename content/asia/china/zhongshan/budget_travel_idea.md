title: "Budget Travel Ideas in Zhong Shan"
type: section
---

If you are looking for some real **Zhong Shan Budget Travel, **then
these top 5 budget ideas in this Chinese City will make your stay fun
and worthwhile even on a tight budget. You can eat delicious dim sum or
roasted pigeon for less than $10, and enter the famous Zhong Shan Park
for free.

**Inexpensive Hotels**

There are approximately 86 hotels in Zhong Shan, but only a few of
them are affordable enough for travellers. One of these is the Green
Tree Inn, where in the check-in rate is only $21. You can stay in the
Ibis Hotel located at the downtown for $29. These two offer lower rates
compared to the other hotels, but still provide good services and rooms.

**Getting Around the City**

There are three means of transportation to get around Zhong Shan:
taxis, buses and motorbike taxis. You can bargain for your fare when
you ride on taxis, but you can just tell the driver to turn on the
meter if you’re not good at it. Buses and motorbike taxis are also
cheap and fast. However, you must know that taking motorbike taxis
follows extra safety. Either one of these can take you to the different
sights and attractions in the city in no time.

**Sightseeing For Free**

Zhong Shan Park can be visited without paying any entrance fee. Here
you can get to see the 5-coloured altar located at the heart of the
park. The Forbidden City can also be accessed from this park by means
of riding boats. Breathtaking views await the tourists who would want
to go for a peaceful natural tour. This is a good site since you will
have fun looking at the variety of plants even if you don’t spend
anything here.

**Eating Out**

If you want to taste the good food offered by the city for only less
than $10, the Fuhua Lobby Cake Shop is a must. This restaurant has the
roasted Shiqi Pigeon, a dish that is famous among the tourists because
of its delicious taste for a cheap price. Fuhua King Hall restaurant is
another bistro that tenders yummy dim sum for only $5 to 10.

**Cheap Fun Dazzle for Kids**

The Zhong Shan Park has a great fun dazzle where kids can play on
the huge slide and swim through the pool filled with plastic balls. For
just about $3.50 (25 RMB), you can let your kids enjoy playing
hide-and-seek in the giant connected tunnels in the area.
