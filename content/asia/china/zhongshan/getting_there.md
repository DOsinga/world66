There are some great places to visit in **Zhong Shan. Getting** there is fairly easy. There are different means of transportation that can help tourists reach the city. Tourists can fly, drive, ride the bus or even reach Zhong Shan on a boat. There are different freeways that connect the city with the rest of the country. People can visit Zhong Zhang to enjoy the different nature parks. **Traveling to Zhong Shan by air
**
This airport is one of the most visited airports in China. The International Airport serves Zhong Shan. Getting there is easy once people reach the airport. This airport is not within the boundaries of the city of Zhong Shan, but it is the closest airport and tourists can take the bus or the highway to reach the actual city.

**Traveling to Zhong Shang by bus**

There are a total of 15 bus lines that connect Zhong Zhan to the rest of the country. This makes it easier to reach Zhong Zhan and getting there because most buses run every 15 minutes on their way to Zhong Zhan. Reaching Zhong Zhan by riding one of the buses is easier because of the buses frequency. **

Traveling to Zhong Shan by water**

People that are in Hong Kong can reach Zhong Zhan using a boat or a ferry. Zhong Zhang is 50 miles away from Hong Kong and the trip does not take a long time. Many ferries are luxurious and they can transport people from Hong Kong, and Macau directly into Zhong Zhan. The trips can take as much as 30 minutes so reaching Zhong Zhan. Getting there is not a problem.
