There are different things that people can learn about **Zhong Shan. Pactical information ** ranks amongst them. The city of Zhong Shan was named after a famous doctor from China-Doctor Sun Zhong Shan. Many people consider him to be the person who carried China into the Modern Era. The town was also an important center of operations for the Japanese Army during World War II. There was a secret base were humans were used to perform experiments. The Zhong Shang University was the head quarters for these experiments.There are many parks in Zhong Shan and there are different places where people can walk and look at the different monuments. Most people consider the city pretty quiet compared to other cities around the area. There are different factories around the city of Zhong Shan. Some practical information is that this is mostly a working district, many people choose this city to settle down, work in one of the factories and raise a family.

**Museums in Zhong Shan**

The museums in Zhong Shan show the rich culture of China. There are pots made out of bronze and porcelain that are also decorated with Chinese Calligraphy. The museums offer tours for tourists and they are frequently visited by tourists and locals alike.

The Zhong Shan's old town is full of buildings that played an important part in the history of Zhong Shan. It is fair to note that most important roads or parks bear the name of Zhon Zhang, because of the fame of this notable character. So even if there is a Zhong Shan Road or a Zhong Shan Park that does not necessarily mean that is located in the city by the same name.**Shopping in Zhong Shan**

There are many different towns that form part of Zhong Shan and where people can acquire anything from furniture to clothing. This manufacturing towns make most of the merchandise that is sold around the world by many different companies. The government tends to encourage companies and individuals in the research and innovation of new products. **

Transportation in Zhong Shan**

Besides the regular bus lines there are different ferries that are able to transport people in and outside Zhong Shan. This practical information about transportation will help people avoid delays. The ferries offer speedy service because Zhong Shan is located on a coastal region and the city is very small compared to other Chinese cities.
