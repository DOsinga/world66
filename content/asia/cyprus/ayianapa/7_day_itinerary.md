Just off the tip of the island of Cyprus are the
famous beaches of Ayia Napa. Here is a 7-Day Itinerary for a trip around
what was originally a small fisherman’s village that has grown into the
Middle East’s tourist resort capital.

**Day 1-Nissi Beach**

A great way to start off your stay in Cyprus is at the famous Nissi
beach. Here you can sit back and relax, soaking in the sun, or take a
dip in the tropical Mediterranean waters. After lying in the sun and
taking a dip there are also well-kept city sand volleyball courts and
small beach-side vendors who sell snacks.

**Day 2-Free Tour**

For the second day, a great idea is a free-guided tour of Cyprus.
The Cyprus Tourism Organization offers free tours a few times a week.
The area is known for its great hospitality and this is just one of the
unique services they provide to ensure a great vacation in Ayia Napa.

**Day 3-Boat Safari**

One of the best ways to get a feel for Ayia Napa’s beautiful
coastline is by a boat tour. Both half and
full day safari tour trips up and down the coast. While on the tour you
will see the beautiful coastal caves, historic land markings, and the
beautiful marine life living in the Mediterranean waters.

**Day 4-Waterworld Waterpark**

The Waterworld Waterpark is a great place to spend the day in the
water and sunshine with a slight change of scenery from the beach. A
day pass is around $20 a person and is well worth it for a daylong
adventure through over 18 water attractions and slides.
