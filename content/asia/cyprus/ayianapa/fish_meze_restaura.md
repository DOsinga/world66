A fish meze specialist on the harbour beach, right on the east side of the traditional harbour. The meze runs to over 20 courses, including standout ouzo mussels and traditional Cypriot desserts. The beachfront setting is the real draw -- on a clear evening with the sun going down, it is hard to beat. Go for dinner rather than lunch.
