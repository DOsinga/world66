The Live Lounge Venue is a new bar in the heart of Ayia Napa and is Napa's only live band music venue playing live indie music 7 nights a week. The bar is new for the 2009 summer season and has already attracted considerable interest due to the success of similar venues in other hot spots like Ibiza. The Live Lounge Venue has several resident bands playing favourite covers and original music including the brilliant Loud Hailers (with former members of the Godhead Number) fresh from their winter tour of Europe. Also look out for some very special guests playing this summer. The bar will offer an alternative to many of the other venues due to the live bands and indie music theme. As this bar is unique to Ayia Napa it is expected to become one of the most popular venues in town. For all information please check out the website [www.liveloungevenue.com](/asia/cyprus/ayianapa/nightlife_and_ente) and if you visit this summer why not let us know what you thought.
