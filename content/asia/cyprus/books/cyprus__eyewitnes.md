The DK Eyewitness guide to Cyprus covers the Republic of Cyprus thoroughly, with good maps, photographs of archaeological sites, and practical information on museums, sights, and hotels. Coverage of Northern Cyprus is limited but present. A useful companion for navigating Paphos, Kourion, and the Troodos painted churches.
