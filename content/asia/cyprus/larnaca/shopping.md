** **There are 4 items you must add to your Larnaca Shopping list – handmade lace, silverware, pottery,and designer clothes and accessories. Here are some of the best shopping spots in and around Larnaca.

**Sunday Open-Air Market **
Larnaca

This market, held every Sunday in a parking lot at Ermou Street. is open from 10:00 a.m. to 1:00 p.m.. You’ll find all kinds of goodies at bargain prices, such as clothes, local crafts,souvenirs, CDs and DVDs, fashion accessories, electrical equipment, snacks, fruits, vegetables and flowers. The best buys in this market are the souvenirs, fresh fruits and crafts, and you won’t get them cheaper anywhere else in the city.

**Zenonos Kitieos Street**
Larnaca

Probably the most stylish shopping area in Larnaca, this street is lined with shops selling a variety of items such as clothes, silver and copper goods. The copper pots, planters and saucepans of Larnaca are known for their quality and are great buys here. Larnaca is a also a good place to buy cheap eye glasses and lens.

You’ll find the Fendi store on this street, offering fashionable apparel and accessories for men and women. If you’re looking for classy jewels and accessories, there’s Joakim Jewelry, featuring goodies from Roberto Cavalli, Versace and others.

**Ak Deniz Street**
Larnaca

Pottery is a must-buy in Larnaca and you’ll find some beautiful pieces at Stavrou Stavrakis or Symeou Efthymios on Ak Deniz Street. Or you could try Emira Pottery on Mehmet Ali Street. For the famous hand-made lace and silverware from Leftkara, you can visit Michael S. Michael Antiques on Mihail Paridi Street.

**Kornos**
27 km from Larnaca

If you have a weakness for beautiful pottery, make a short trip to Kornos, known internationally for this craft. You can buy urns, flower pots, censers, wine jars and more made from the red soil of the village. The village is also famous for its honey.
**
Ermou Street**
Larnaca

This street has some fashionable boutiques selling famous designer labels. There’s Infolio Donna with women’s apparel and accessories from designers like Roberto Cavalli, Kenzo, and Valentino. And there’ s Infolio Uomo with clothes and accessories for men from leading designer houses such as DKNY, Dolce & Gabbana, and Cavalli Class.

Jango has some wonderful clothes for men and women from fashion houses in Italy, Denmark and Greece. The store also has Spanish and Italian footwear.

The 34the Shop has casual name-branded clothes from more than 20 labels like Pepe, Nolita, Paul Smith, Ed Hardy, and Rare.

**Leftkara Village**
25 miles from Larnaca

Finally, if you want to go to the very source of the famous hand-made lace and silverware and get them at the cheapest rates, you could go on a day trip to the delightful village of Leftkara.
