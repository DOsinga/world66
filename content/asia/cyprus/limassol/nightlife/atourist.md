This is a scam- once you get the girls to your hotel (or prior to that, since we were not interested and lucky enough to find out), they charge you additional money which the club manager does not inform you about. So if you choose not to avail of the services of the girls, you lose your money for the 'extra' xxx drinks as well as the tips to the girls.

Be aware of this place- not an honest place- but then what are we expecting anyway?
