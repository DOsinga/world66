This restored area of the old city is the pride of all Nicosians and the center of the capital's life. Restoration began in 1981, and included renewal and reconstruction of all structures within the target area. Now the streets are lined with small art-and-craft shops, boutiques and galleries. The project won 1988 the "Pomme d'Or", a prestigious biennial international prize.
