---
title: "When to Go in Nicosia"
type: section
---

The stunning capital of Cyprus, Nicosia, really is an amazing place to visit
anytime of the year. Its mythic beauty and comfortable, Mediterranean climate
make it the perfect place for both indoor and outdoor activities. Most of the
days of the year are blessed with bright blue skies and not a cloud in sight.
Precipitation is rare as well, making Nicosia a great tourist destination. The tourist season is from the end of June until early October, and the city
can get quite crowded during that time, it is best to avoid taking your
vacation in **Nicosia. When to go** depends not only on the tourists, but on
the weather as well. In general, the weather is amazing in Cyprus, however, you
still must take into consideration that despite being mild, winters can still
have chilly temperatures and some rain, which is not exactly ideal when visiting
tourist attractions. Basically, the best time to visit Nicosia is in the spring
or autumn because the city is not packed with people, prices are low,
temperatures are comfortable, and there is little rain.

**The Seasons**

Summer is from June to September and is characterized by clear skies, hot
temperatures, and dry winds off the coast. This season is also the tourist
season, so prices for attractions, food, and hotels go up due to the higher
demand, and everything becomes more crowded. Average temperatures during the
summer season range from 32 to 35 degrees C. Spring and autumn are very
short, but are the best times to visit Nicosia. Temperatures are warm still,
precipitation is not common, and attractions are not overcrowded with tourists.
They are the perfect seasons for traveling, visiting archaeological sites,
wandering through museums, hiking through the hills, and basically any other
activity you would like to do. The average temperatures during these seasons are
in the low 20s. Winters in Nicosia, which are from November to March are very
mild and very long. Temperatures range from about 6 to 15 degrees C, which
can seem quite chilly if you are planning on doing a lot of outdoor activities.
Rain is possible during this time of year, and there is often snow in the nearby
Troodos Mountains. Winter is still a reasonable time to vacation in Nicosia, as
prices are low and the weather is not that bad.
