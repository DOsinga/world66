This restaurant offers not one, but two menus! It's basically two restaurants (Fat Mama's and Willy's Rib Ranch) put together and the mix creates an extensive choice of very reasonably priced meals that are large enough to fill even the most capacious belly!

Top service is complimented by numerous coreographed dances put on by the waitresses in celebration of birthdays and weddings (make sure you tell them early) culminating in a round of "Happy birthday" or an announcement.

Although large, the restaurant is very popular and there are always folk sitting in the waiting area with a cool drink, so make sure you either go early (before 7:30 to be on the safe side) or if you have a large group, book the day before to avoid dissapointment.

Personal recommendation: Fillet steak followed by The Godfather of chocolate cake or if you are feeling hungry try the ribs, you wont be disappointed!!
