---
title: "Festivals in Troodos mountains"
type: section
---

**Troodos Mountains festivals **are exciting, boasting of wine, music and Greek drama. They are sure to delight the entire family. Here, a festival is nothing rare or out of the ordinary. They are integrated into the daily lives of the people in Cyprus. Listed below are a few festivals that might be worth the experience.

**Limassol Carnival**

This colorful 11 day long festival takes place in February. It is a world wide festival, celebrated on the entire island with traditional floats and parades.

**Limassol International Children&rsquo;s Festival**

This exciting festival is for the young and the young at heart. Taking place in May, it offers face painting, trampolines, clowns, song and dance, a puppet show, table games, etc. It is a child's delight.

**Anthestiria**

This particular festival, taking place in May, is known as the spring flower festival. It is a gorgeous site to behold because during this festival, the island is filled with flowers. The flower-covered floats all have a focus on Ancient Greece.

**Greek Orthodox Easter**

This religious festival is maybe the most coveted of the Greek calendar. It begins on Good Friday to mark the death and burial of Christ. Saturday, there is a midnight mass to celebrate the resurrection of Christ.

**Epiphany**

Taking place on January 6th, this festival is a favorite of children who get to dive into the cold sea to retrieve a cross that priests throw into it in order to bless the sea.
