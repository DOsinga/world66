**Visit the Indulgence Restaurant when in ****Ipoh******

There are so many **Ipoh**** restaurants** around the city that it may be really hard for you to choose exactly which one to eat at. If you have trouble making your decision, go and visit the renowned Indulgence Restaurant on Jalan Raja Dihilir 14. This restaurant is surely one of the best eating out spots in Ipoh. Indulgence Restaurant can be visited every Wednesday to Sunday from 9 a.m. to 11.30 p.m.

**The Atmosphere**

As you go inside the restaurant, you will enter a dim lit room, decorated with various paintings, plants and antiques. Small 2-seat wooden tables are located in the center of the dining room, while larger tables with comfortable couches are located near the walls. In addition, 3 private dining rooms are available if you want to escape the crowd and dine in an intimate atmosphere. The whole restaurant creates of feeling of relief and calmness. You can sit down and have a drink while chilling with the sounds of soft jazz music.

**The Menu**

All the delicious meals at the restaurant are prepared by the award-winning chef Julie Song. Whether it is Malaysian, Chinese or Western cuisine, Indulgence has it all and everything is prepared by Julie Song herself. Some of the must-tries are the cherry tomato bread, the smoked duck or the hand-made saffron spaghetti with seafood. Desserts are one of the best parts of the menu. You should try some of the Tiramisu or the various fruit trifles. Some excellent wines and cocktails are served as well.

### [New Delhi Indian Restaurant](/asia/eating_out/new_delhi_indian_r)

A Great Indian Restaurant
The **New Delhi Indian Restaurant** in** Siem Reap**, has excellent food, and is very reasonably priced. The Chicken Masala (a kind of chicken curry) I tried was one of the best I’ve ever had, with big chunks of chicken, and it was not too spicy. The rice was nice and fluffy, and the Pakoda (which I had not tried before) was very tasty. In fact the entire meal was quite good, and with drink only cost about $5. They also serve vegetarian food.
