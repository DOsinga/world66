A Great Indian Restaurant
The **New Delhi Indian Restaurant** in** Siem Reap**, has excellent food, and is very reasonably priced. The Chicken Masala (a kind of chicken curry) I tried was one of the best I’ve ever had, with big chunks of chicken, and it was not too spicy. The rice was nice and fluffy, and the Pakoda (which I had not tried before) was very tasty. In fact the entire meal was quite good, and with drink only cost about $5. They also serve vegetarian food.
The service was good, but as for the atmosphere, well, it is typical of a small Indian restaurant. All in all though, I would give it an outstanding rating for food. For lovers of good Indian food, it is worth checking out.
The New Delhi Indian Restaurant is located across from the provincial hospital just north of Pub Street. The hours are from 8am to 11pm.
