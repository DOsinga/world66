It is now possible to travel around Asia on board budget flights.

At present, there are 6 budget airways that travel between cities in Malaysia, Singapore, Thailand, Vietnam, Indonesia and India.

1) **Berjaya Air**
http://www.berjaya-air.com
Travels from and to [Koh Samui](/asia/gettingaround), Pangkor Island, Tioman Island, Kuala Lumpur and Singapore

2) **Air Asia**
http://www.airasia.com
Travels between Malaysia, Mainland China, Hong Kong, Viet Nam, The Philippines, Indonesia and Thailand

3) **ValuAir**
http://www.valuair.com.sg/
Travels between Jakarta, Surabaya and Singapore.

4) **Tiger Airways**
http://www.tigerairways.com
Travels from and to Singapore, Bangkok, Chiang Mai, Hat Yai, Phuket, and Krabi. As well as from and to Macau and Clark Air Base in the Philippines

5) **JetStar Asia
** http://www.jetstarasia.com/
Travels to and from Bangkok, Hong Kong, Manila, Singapore, Taipei, Kolkata, Phuket, Yangon

6) **NokAir
** http://www.nokair.com
Travels to and from Bangkok, Hat Yai, Chiang Mai, Udonthani, Phuket, and Nakhonsi Thammarat.

Remember that most flights are usually fully booked so buy your tickets well ahead of time.
