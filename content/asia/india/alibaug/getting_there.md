Ways of reaching Alibaug - By Road, Sea by ST Bus.
An easy guiding map to get here by your vehicle is very well exhibited on a website of Nirmal Cottages, Zirad which is a very famous and a popular resort in Alibaug.

You can also reach Alibaug Through Sea as well. Catch a Ferry / Boat from Gateway of India for Mandwa. After you reach Mandwa jetty which takes about an hour the same ferry operators take you in the same fare by their own bus up to Alibaug. For your ready reference timings and other details are listed on the website of Nirmal Cottages, Zirad.

Third option to reach Alibaug is by ST (State Transport) Bus. Almost from all the locations in Maharashtra and Mumbai, Buses are frequently available to go to Alibaug.
