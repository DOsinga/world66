Radhanagar Beach

Incase you love the sea, its a must go. Crystal clear waters , white sands, and absolutely no one else on the beach. It was rated 7th best in the world as per one rating few years ago.
The beach is on the Havelock island, which has government as well as private guest houses. Trips to havelock are arranged as 1.5 day excursions from port blair. The boat trip takes around 1/ 1.5 hours.
