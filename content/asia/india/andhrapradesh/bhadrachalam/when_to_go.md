---
title: "When to Go"
type: section
---

October through February is the most comfortable time to visit — dry weather, temperatures between 12°C and 25°C, and low humidity. The most significant time to visit is Rama Navami (March–April), when the temple hosts a 10-day festival celebrating the marriage of Rama and Sita; the event draws enormous crowds and accommodation should be booked well in advance. Summer (March–June) is very hot with temperatures up to 40°C. The monsoon (June–September) brings heavy rain and can flood roads to some riverside attractions.
