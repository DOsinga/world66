A scholarly study tracing the military architecture of Andhra Pradesh from its earliest known fortifications through the 16th century. N. S. Ramachandra Murthy catalogues dozens of forts across the Deccan, covering their construction, strategic roles, and the dynasties that built them — from the Satavahanas and Kakatiyas to the Vijayanagara empire. Useful background reading for anyone planning to visit Golconda, Warangal, or the lesser-known hilltop forts scattered through the region.
