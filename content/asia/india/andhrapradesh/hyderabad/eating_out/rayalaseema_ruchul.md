Rayalaseema Ruchulu (literarily means "The Tastes of Rayalaseema") was founded in 2003 by a team of young entrepreneurs originating from the region with a mission to;

"Introduce the connoisseurs of cosmopolitan Hyderabad to the authentic and spicy culinary delights of the Rayalaseema cuisine which has its own distinctive taste, taking all necessary steps to ensure strong presence of home touch."

Over the years Rayalaseema Ruchulu has grown into a chain comprising of 3 restaurants at Hyderabad. We serve ethnic culinary delights of Rayalaseema to gourmets at Masab Tank, Lakdi ka pul and Dilsuknagar. Each restaurant has a capacity to serve over 75 guests at any point in time with add-on services in tastefully decorated air-conditioned environment that depicts the culture of the region.

**We have recently been given an award in the "Best Andhra" category by The Times Of India Group, Times Food Award - 2009, Hyderabad.**
