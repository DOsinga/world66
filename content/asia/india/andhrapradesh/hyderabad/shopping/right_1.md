The Golden Mile in Hyderabad for shopping having Malls, Designer Boutiques and top end shops and show rooms - Starting from Lifestyle near the Begumpet Flyover and ending at Nagarjuna Circle in Banjara Hills.

The mile will soon (in 2007/2008) get extended to maybe 2 or 3 miles as more and more swanky malls are getting added.
