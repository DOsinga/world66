The bangle alley running west from Charminar — a narrow street densely packed with shops selling lacquer bangles studded with coloured stones, pearls (Hyderabad has been a pearl-trading centre for centuries), silks, and wedding finery. Best early evening, when the lights come on and the shops fill with brides-to-be shopping for their weddings.
