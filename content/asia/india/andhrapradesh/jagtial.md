Jagtial is a Mandal or Taluka in Karimnagar District of Andhra Pradesh, which is in 5 hours travel distance from the State Capital Hyderabad. 

In Karimnagar Dist., Jagtial is hub for commercial as well as Entertainment Activities. This town consists good educational Institutions, starting from Primary Education to Engineering & Agricultaral Studies. 

Having all types of facilities like good number of Government as well as Private Hospitals, Play Grounds, Lots of Cinema Theatres, Gymnasiums, and traditional Schools. 

Politically this town has significant recognisation in Karimnagar District. Basically itz a Assembly constituency . Many of MLAs of Jagtial worked as Cabinet Ministers in State Assembly too.. 

As a part of Telangana Region which is ruled by Nizams in the history..it have lot of old monuments and places to visit.Jagtial "KILLA" is a very old rulers place which is still existing...Itz the place of 'Samantha Raja's' who ruled in the history. 

Jagtial division has lot of well known temples too..'Dharma Puri' and 'Konda Gattu' are two temple towns very near to this town. Dharmapuri is famous for Narasimha Swamy Temple on the bank of Godavari River. Kondagattu is a HighLand and very famous for Anjaneya Swamy Temple. 

The Youth of Jagtial are very determined and they achieved a lot and trying their level best in all the fields..and they are distributed all over the World.
