Kothagudem is a an iota of cosmopolitan town in Khammam district . It is better known as coal town of South India, as it heads the Singareni Colleries Company Limited chair. Kothagudem and Palvancha are the twin towns where in they are endowed with rich forests, industries, landscapes etceteras. The railway station is known as Bhadrachalam Road, as this place reaches you to the place of Ramadasa's Lord Rama, BHADRACHALAM. You find a very pleasant and pro city atmosphere in Kothagudem.
