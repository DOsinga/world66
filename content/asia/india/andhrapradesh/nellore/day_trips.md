You can visit famous temples like Ranganayukala swamy temple which is adjacent to river penna.This is one of the oldest temples located on the banks of river penna

You can visitRajarajeshwari devi temple, Jonnavada Kamashamma temple. These temples are one of the oldest temples in and around nellore city.

Many other beautiful temples like Ayyappa temple, Kumaraswamy temple, Saibabatemple are in heart of city. Nearly 15 Kms away there is famous Venkaiah Swamy Temple.

Near the Nellore Tank there is famous Barashahid Darga and at Kasumur there is famous Masthanaiah Darga nearly 15 kms away from Nellore.

Nearly 20 Kms away from Nellore on the seashore at Kotha Koduru there is famous Velankani Church and in the heart of Nellore there are more than Century old Downie Memorial Church and Lone Star Telugu Baptist Church.

There is worth seeing, enjoyable and pleasant Children's Park in the town.
