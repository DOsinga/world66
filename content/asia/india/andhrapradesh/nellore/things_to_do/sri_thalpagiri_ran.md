title: "Sri Thalpagiri Ranganath Swami Temple"
type: poi
category: "Sight"
address: "Ranganayakula Peta, Nellore"
opening_hours: "All days"
---

One of Nellore's most revered temples, dedicated to Lord Ranganath Swami and set directly on the banks of the Pennar River. The temple is old — the exact founding date is debated — and the riverside location gives it a calm, unhurried atmosphere. It draws pilgrims from across the district and is the natural starting point for exploring the old town.
