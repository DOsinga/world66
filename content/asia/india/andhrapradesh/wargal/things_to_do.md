---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Wargal is a pilgrimage destination, and the Saraswati Temple on the hill is the reason visitors come. The temple complex also includes shrines to Vigneshwara (Ganesha) and Saniswara, and a Veda Pathasala where children can be seen reciting ancient texts. The Nachagirigutta Temple with its Narasimha and Hanuman shrines is about 15 km away and can be combined with a visit.
