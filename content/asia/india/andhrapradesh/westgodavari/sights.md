### [Pattisima

It is famous for the temple of Sri Veerabhadra (Shiva), which is
picturasquely located on a hill known as Devakuta Parvatha in the
middle of the Godavari river like an Island. Considered to be one of
the Panchkasi Kshetrams of the Shivaites, the temple has a beautiful
Nandi Vigarha.Carved images of dancing people adorn the walls while an
awe-inspiring life-size sculpture of Devi Bhadrakali with lot of
ornaments & a sword, catches the eye. A temple dedicated to Sri
Bhavanarayana Swamy can also be visited.
