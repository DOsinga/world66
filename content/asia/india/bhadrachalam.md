Travel Guide

Bhadrachalam is one of
the most mystical places of India. Most of
India’s history mingles with religion. Most important cities or places were
built around places of worship like temples and those places bear the names of
important shrines, the names of saints and of Hindu deities. Bhadrachalam is
one of this places full of mysticism. This place is geographically located on
the banks of the Godavari River of India. This site is a prime destination for
those people that want to embark on a journey and to see the glory and the
architecture of India’s earliest inhabitants.

**Transportation Methods
to Reach Bhadrachalam**

The most important
place is Bhadrachalam, the holy site that is believed to be the holy home of
Lord Rama. This makes this place one of the most significant destinations of
India. While the place is hard to reach you can always find many inexpensive
ways to visit this place without spending that much money. You can use a boat
or a ferry to reach this pilgrimage site. You can also reach this place
traveling by train. There is a train route, the Machilipatnam/Manuguru Express
the number of this train is 7050.You can also take different bus routes to
travel to Bhadrachalam.

When traveling by road
you can use one of the many buses provided by the tourism department. The good
thing about this buses that is that they will not stop along the way. They make
a direct nonstop trip to the pilgrimage site; however you have to be ready for
a long 7 hour ride.

**When to visit
Bhadrachalam**

During the months of
October and November there are many different celebrations when pilgrims take a
dip on the cold waters of the Godavari River. The celebrations are made mainly
in the temple’s grounds and many people flock for this holydays. There are many
different accommodation that the temple and the government offer to the
pilgrims and travelers alike.

**Where to stay when
visiting**

There are many places
where travelers could find lodging that is virtually inexpensive. It should be
noted that it’s best to visit this location during the week. There are a lot of
people visiting during the weekends that it is virtually impossible to find
lodging during the weekends. The prices of the lodgings vary from RP$ 20 to
RP$200. Of course the price of the room varies according to the style and the
commodity of the place.
