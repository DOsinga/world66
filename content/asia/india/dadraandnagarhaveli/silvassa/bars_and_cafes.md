---
title: "Bars and Cafes"
type: section
---

Silvassa is a small town, and nightlife options are limited to hotel bars and resort lounges. That said, Dadra and Nagar Haveli has relatively relaxed alcohol regulations, making drinks cheaper here than in neighbouring Gujarat.

**The Banyan Tree Lounge and Bar (Ras Resorts)**

A pleasant bar with outdoor seating under a banyan tree said to be over 150 years old. Good selection of spirits and multi-cuisine food. The outdoor arrangement is the main draw.

**Junk Resto Lounge (Khanvel Resort)**

The name is literal: the decor is built from repurposed drums, barrels, and other industrial salvage. There is a small discotheque with decent sound and lighting. The food and drink menu is broad. A good option if you want something livelier than a hotel bar.

**Hotel Woodland Regency**

On MG Road in the centre of town, this hotel bar has an in-house DJ playing Eastern and Western music. Kingfisher beer is cheap (about 100 rupees for a 650ml bottle). The attached restaurant serves Indian, Continental, and Chinese food at communal tables. There is also a separate vegetarian restaurant, Aman Tran, open until 10:30pm.
