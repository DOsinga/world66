---
title: "Family Travel Ideas in Silvassa"
type: section
---

Silvassa is a well known destination for family vacation in India.
The city is very friendly and safe to travel. **Silvassa Family Travel**
offers great attractions which every member can enjoy.

**Family Accommodation **

There are several hotels in Silvassa which offer weekdays discounts.
You can take the benefit of these offers in your family trip here.
Khanvel Resort is well known for its hospitality in the city. It offers
swimming pool, children play area, water sports facilities along with
family rooms. Lotus Riverside Resort located on the banks of the river
Daman Ganga and is a good hotel for family travel. It offers children
play area, indoor games, swimming pool along with restaurant and bar
facility.

**Sight seeing and activities **

**Tribal Culture Museum **

The museum is located just near the Tourist Information Center in
the city and is a must see place to understand the culture and lifestyle
of the tribal in the region. The museum showcases life size statues of
various tribes in the region in their day to activity. They give good
impression of the lifestyle of the tribal and children enjoy it too.
This is also great venue to know about the lives of the tribal here.

**Hirwa Van Garden **

Dedicated to the tribal Goddess “Hirwa”, the goddess of the forest,
this beautiful garden attracts lots of tourists every year. Water play
is the theme here. You can find cascading waterfalls surrounded by lush
green trees and carpet of green lawns bordered by exotic and colorful
flowebeds. It is an ideal picnic spot for the locals. The garden also
has a children play section with lots of rides to enjoy.

**Our Lady of Piety Church **

This church is a living example of Portuguese architecture in India.
Built in 1889 the church still holds the appreciation of the visitors
with its spectacular exteriors and interiors. It is a must visit in
Silvassa trip.

**Van Ganga Lake and Island Garden **

The lake is situated about 6 km from Silvassa town and has a very
picturesque setting. The lake is surrounded by Island Garden which has
numerous wooden bridges, colorful flowers and walking trails. The lake
area is great fun to hang around with family. You can enjoy paddle
boating in the calm water while watch the scenic beauty surrounding it.
And if you are tired and hungry then you can grab a bite from the
numerous eateries on the shore.
