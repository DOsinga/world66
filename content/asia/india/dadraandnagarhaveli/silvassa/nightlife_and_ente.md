---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment in Silvassa"
type: section
---

Being a small town, **Silvassa Nightlife** and entertainment does
not have much to offer. However, with lots of renowned industries
setting up their base here many resorts and hotels have come up in the
area and opened new doors to tourism here. These hotels offer bars and
lounges for relaxation to the visitors.

**The Banyan Tree Lounge and Bar **

It is part of the Ras Resorts in Silvassa and is a perfect place to
unwind after a hectic day schedule. The lounge offers excellent choice
of liquor and Multi-cuisine food to enjoy along with. It also has an
outdoor sitting arrangement which is under Banyan Tree believed to be
over 150 years old. You can have great time here.

**Junk Resto Lounge **

This lounge is part of Khanvel Resort in Silvassa. As the name
suggests this lounge has used junk beautifully and utilized it
perfectly. Even the discotheque is made of rejected drums and barrels.
The lighting and sound effects are incredible here. This is perfect
place to shake your legs if you are in a mood. The bar offers excellent
choice of liquors and the food is excellent with lots of variety.

**Daman Ganga Valley Resort **

This resort offers mini bar in each room so that you can enjoy your
drink in the comfort of your room whenever you feel like. Also the big
private balcony overlooking the garden area and the river gives awesome
view. You can sit there and enjoy the view along with your drink.

**During Festivals**

If your Silvassa trip is colliding with a festival in Silvassa then
there is much to do for entertainment in the evenings. Most of the
festivals hosts events like folk dance, cultural programs and other
events which are full of excitement and fun. During these festivals you
can have great evenings in Silvassa which will always be a memorable
experience for your trip here. You can know more about it from **Silvassa
Festivals**.
