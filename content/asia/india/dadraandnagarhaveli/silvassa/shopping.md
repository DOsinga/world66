---
title: "Shopping"
type: section
---

Kilvani Road is the main shopping strip, lined with small shops on both sides. Do not expect malls or big retail here. The best things to buy are local crafts tied to the region's tribal heritage.

**Warli Paintings**

Silvassa is a hub for Warli painting, an ancient folk art tradition named after the Warli tribe. The paintings use white pigment on earthen-coloured backgrounds, with geometric patterns depicting harvest seasons, weddings, and daily life. Available at art and handicraft shops around the market. Prices are generally fixed; check at the Tourist Information Centre for current rates.

**Carved Smoking Pipes**

Tribal craftsmen carve decorative smoking pipes from local wood. Even non-smokers buy them as ornamental pieces. They are handmade and each one is different.

**Recycled Paper Bags**

The local administration promotes eco-friendly practices, and many shops use recycled paper bags and bags made from raagi flour. These make interesting and inexpensive souvenirs.
