---
title: "Eating Out"
type: section
---

Delhi's restaurant scene is improving at very fast pace. Numerous restaurants open up every months. These days even fantastic Italian or Thai restaurants have started to the relief of the big diplomatic crowd. Besides that, Delhi's vendor stalls offer all sorts of food, ranging from deep-fried poppadums to fresh fruits. Besides that, there is always a local restaurant where you can try the curries or thali's. The meals won't cost you much at these places, but be aware of the possible amount of spices that might make you gasp for breath or give you severe stomach problems. The same goes for meat at the vendor stalls: some people already fall ill by simply looking at it, while others can eat to their heart's content. It is quite common for both locals and travellers, to eat at hotels. They often have quite good restaurants with a lot of traditional (and international) dishes.
