The Lotus Temple is a spectacular and colourful sight. It is the main temple of the Bahai's in Delhi. Located in Kalkaji in the south of Delhi, it is lotus shaped and has rightly been given the name. It is open to all faiths and is an ideal place for meditation and obtaining peace and tranqillity.
 **Justin :** I visited the Lotus temple as part of a bus tour through town and I can't say I found it very 'genuine'. It is pretty colourful though...
