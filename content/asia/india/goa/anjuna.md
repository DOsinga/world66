title: "Anjuna"
type: location
---

Anjuna is one of the most popular beach resorts. All night long, beach parties with Rave music attract a versatile crowd with people from all over the planet. The wednesday flea market is a good reason to leave the beach for a while and to look around in the town. The best time to go is very early, so either you are still awake or you have to get up at 7:00 for a change.  The Albuqurque Mansion is also worth a visit. It was built in the 1920s and modelled after the palace of the Sultan of [Zanzibar](/africa/tanzania/zanzibar).
