---
title: Bars and Cafes
type: section
---

There are so many good cafe's to be found in Anjuna. A favourite for those who like to sit and watch the sun sink into the Arabian sea, is Zoorie's, an Israeli run outdoor restaurant perched on the cliff. There's even a double bed that you can recline on as you sip an iced coffee and wait for your pasta. For those who like to watch a big screen tv, share chillum and choose from an extensive and affordable menu, there's the popular Munchies, which is on the road to Chapora, a minutes walk from Starco junction.
The German bakery and Mango Shade, both on the way to South Anjuna, are good places to breakfast in the shade. The German bakery has a relaxed vibe, and Mango shade does great lassi.
