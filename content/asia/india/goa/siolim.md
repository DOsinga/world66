Siolim is a village about 7 kms from [Mapusa](/asia/india/goa/mapusa). It is situated around Chapora River. Wednesday market at the old Ferry Terminal is worth a visit. St. Anthony's Church and Sateri Temple are good for a visit. A heritage hotel called "Siolim House" is popular among tourists. Most North Goa beaches are within short driving distance.
