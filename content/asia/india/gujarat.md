Gujarat was once the most prosperous state in India. However many businesses have left the state. However there are many merchant businesses present there. Although media reports of the riots paint Gujarat as a very dangerous place, it is not so. Gujarat is well known for its culture, General, beaches, palaces, temples, mosques and deserts. The west coast, with its 1666 kms long coastline, is one of the busiest tourist attractions. Political leaders, such as Sardar Vallabhai Patel and Mahatma Gandhi who build the nation, are only two of the famous people that originated from Gujarat. Ahmedabad, the state's capital in the past (the capital is now Gandhinagar), is the perfect spot for tourists to start their onward journey. This can be to Patan, located northerly of Gujarat, [Kutch](/asia/india/gujarat/kutch), the General at Modhera or the General. There are also some excellent Wildlife in this area.
