---
title: 'Getting There'
type: section
---

**By Air:** Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel International Airport has flights to all major Indian cities and international connections to the Middle East, Southeast Asia, and London. The airport is about 9 km north of the city centre.

**By Rail:** Ahmedabad Junction is one of western India's busiest railway stations, with frequent trains to Mumbai (7-9 hours), Delhi (12-15 hours), and [Vadodara](/asia/india/gujarat/vadodara) (2 hours). Multiple daily express and mail trains connect to destinations across Gujarat and beyond. Kalupur is the main station.

**By Road:** Ahmedabad is well connected by the national highway network. The Ahmedabad-Vadodara Expressway makes the 100 km journey to [Vadodara](/asia/india/gujarat/vadodara) fast and comfortable. State transport and private buses run frequently to all major cities in Gujarat and to Mumbai.
