A combination eatery and art shop near C.G. Road with a Buddhist-themed interior and lounging space for book lovers. The menu covers a wide range -- Punjabi, Gujarati, Mexican, and Italian fare, from soups to desserts. Open all day for snacks including wraps, sandwiches, pizza, quesadillas, and dosa, plus mocktails and coffees.
