---
title: 'Museums'
type: section
---

**Junagadh Museum** is inside the zoo grounds and has an interesting collection of art, furniture, stuffed animals, ancient coins, and Persian and Sanskrit inscriptions.

**Science Museum** is Gujarat's first private science museum, with over 60 interactive science exhibits that visitors can operate. There is a small aquarium attached.

**Darbar Hall Museum** occupies the hall once used by the Nawabs of Junagadh to hold their court. The collection includes a picture gallery, textiles, and an arms gallery.
