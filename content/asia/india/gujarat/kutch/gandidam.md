Gandhidham is a planned city in [Kutch](/asia/india/gujarat/kutch), built in the 1950s to resettle refugees from Sindh after Partition. It is the main commercial centre of Kutch and the railway junction for the region, though most travellers pass through on their way to [Bhuj](/asia/india/gujarat/kutch/bhuj) or [Kandla](/asia/india/gujarat/kutch/kandla).
