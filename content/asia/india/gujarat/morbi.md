Morbi is a small city 64 km from [Rajkot](/asia/india/gujarat/rajkot), once a princely state and now known as India's ceramics capital -- along with being a major centre for clock manufacturing (Ajanta Quartz, the world's largest clock manufacturer, is based here on the Morbi-Rajkot road).

The city was devastated by a catastrophic flood in 1979 that destroyed most of its historical monuments, but the **Mani Mandir**, the former palace of the Morbi kings, survives and is worth seeing. The **Hanging Bridge** over the Machchu river is the city's most famous landmark. Victoria Garden, opposite Mani Mandir, is a pleasant place for a walk.

You can reach Morbi within two hours from Rajkot by ST bus. Hotels are available near the New Bus Stand.
