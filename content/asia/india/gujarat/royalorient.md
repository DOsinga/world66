**ROYAL ORIENT**

 If you want to be treated like a Maharaja, if you want to feel the royal experience and the royal way of hospitality, then the Royal Orient is an absolute must for you.  **THE ROYAL ORIENT** Train is a joint venture of the Gujarat Tourism Corporation and the Indian Railways and will keep you on the rails with its programmed journey of seven days/nights. This train starts from Delhi passing through Chittaurgarh, Udaipur, Modhera, Patan, Ahmedabad, SasanGir, Ahmedpur-Mandvi, Diu and Palitana, the pink city of Jaipur and returning
