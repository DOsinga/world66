---
title: 'Nightlife and Entertainment'
type: section
---

Like the rest of Gujarat, Vadodara is a dry state with no bars or clubs. Entertainment revolves around cinema -- the city has several modern multiplexes including **INOX** at Ellora Park on Racecourse Circuit Road and **PVR** on Old Chhani Road, both offering comfortable seating and modern sound systems along with food courts.

The **Navratri** celebrations in October are when Vadodara truly comes alive, with enormous Garba and Raas dance events that go through the night.
