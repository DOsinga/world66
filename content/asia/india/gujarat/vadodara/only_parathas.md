Over 150 varieties of paratha -- the stuffed Indian flatbread -- make this a small institution for flatbread lovers. The menu is entirely vegetarian and runs from familiar aloo and paneer fillings into more inventive territory (cheese chocolate, anyone?). The ambience is pleasant and prices are low.
