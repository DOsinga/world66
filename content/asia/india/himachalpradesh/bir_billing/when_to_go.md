---
title: 'When to Go'
type: section
---

The best season for paragliding runs from the first week of October through the end of November. After that, snow arrives and lasts until late February. Spring starts in early March and runs through mid-May, though after mid-April conditions become too bumpy for paragliding.

If you're interested in lama dances and visiting monasteries, October and March are ideal. Sherab Ling monastery holds teachings sometime between late March and April.
