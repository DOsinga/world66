---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

As usual, there are three options.

 **By Air:** The nearest airport is at Gaggal, Kangra.

 **By Rail: ** The nearest railway station is at Pathankot at a distance of 80 kms and from there you will have to take bus or private taxi.

 **By Road:** Bus services run between Dalhousie and Chamba. You can even get a bus from Delhi and Chandigarh.
