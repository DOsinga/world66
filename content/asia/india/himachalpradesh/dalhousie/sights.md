---
title: "Sights"
type: section
---

The most famous places to visit here are the famous old churches, Panchpulla (the 'Five Bridges', at distance of just 3 kms), Subhash Baudi, Bara Patthar (at a 4 kms' distance) and Kalatope (at a 11 kms' distance) which is a wildlife sanctuary. You can also visit the Satdhara and Barkota Hills.
