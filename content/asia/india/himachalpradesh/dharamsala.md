---
title: "Dharamsala"
type: location
---

A beautiful little hill resort, Dharamshala is 90 kms from from Pathankot, located on the other side of Chamba. It is the headquarters of Kangra district. Dharamsala is probably best known as the place where the Tibetan government in exile is seated. The Dalai Lama has lived here since he fled from Tibet 1959. There are also many of his followers in the town.

  The town is built on a spur of Dhauladhar range and altitudes vary between 180 and 1250 meters. The waterfall of Bhagsunath is within a walking distance. Dharamshala is split in to two parts, i.e. the upper side and the lower side. The upperside is usually known as Mclodganj. Because of the presence of Dalai Lama, this is a popular place and Holywood stars, like Steven Seagull and Richard Gere, visit this place frequently.
