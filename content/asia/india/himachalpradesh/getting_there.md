Here there is some information about how to get to Himachal Pradesh.

### By Air

Gaggal is the nearest airport, 15 kms from Dharamsala and 24 kms from
Mcleodganj. IA /JA flies thrice a week from Delhi on Monday, Wednesday
and Friday to Gaggal Apt.. The other airports are Amritsar (210kms)
Jammu 200 kms and Chandigarh 240 kms.
