Linked by road to all major towns nearby,KANGRAis just ON the NH 20. The 88 km distance from Pathankot to KANGRA can be covered by taxi. or deluxe bus. Bus terminus: is on the main highway and is the normal arrival / passing point for luxury and deluxe buses from Manali and Delhi. Jammu Shimla

Direct & regular bus services link Dharamshala 18 kms, Chandigarh 222kms, Delhi 510 kms, Dalhousie 140kms, , Jwalamukhi38 kms, Jammu 200kms, , Kullu 210 km, Manali 250 km, Mandi 140 km, Palampur 35 kms Chamba 190 kms and Shimla 240 kms. Local Transport: Buses and taxis (JEEPS /TOYOTA QUALIS AND VANS )available.
