---
title: 'Eating Out'
type: section
---

Keylong's eateries, called *dhabas*, are scattered throughout town and serve hearty, affordable meals. One worth seeking out is *Fauji da Dhaba* (Soldier's Eatery), owned by an ex-soldier. Tucked in a narrow street overlooking the village and surrounding fields, it serves delicious *parathas* and vegetable dishes at very reasonable prices.
