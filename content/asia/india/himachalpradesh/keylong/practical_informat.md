title: "Practical Information"
type: section
---

Keylong is a very beautiful town, inhabited by friendly, simple,
helping, hospitable and honest people (really!!). You can catch a good
meal at any of the eateries, called *dhabas*, throught the town. One that the author recommends is *Fauji da dhaba*
(Soldier's eatery). It is called so because it's owned by an
ex-soldier. Located in a narrow street, it overlooks the village and
beautiful fields. It serves delicious *parathas* and veggies for very reasonable price.
