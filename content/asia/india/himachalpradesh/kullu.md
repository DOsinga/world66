---
title: "Kullu"
type: location
---

One of the loveliest spot in the Himalayas, the Kulu Valley is known as the *Valley of Gods*. It is thus hardly surprising that you will find numerous temples here. The vally offers a variety of scenic charms, from wooded glades to barren rocks with snow above the tree line. Kullu is famous for its apple orchards, wooden temples, folk music and dances and their handsome hill folk. Kullu is a great place for trekking.
