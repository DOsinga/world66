title: "Budget Travel Ideas in Mcleodganj"
type: section

Visiting the small Himalayan Town of Mcleodganj is an awesome
experience. The calm and relaxed environment will relax not
only your body but your mind, also. Touring McLeodganj is a memorable
experience for all--be it luxury traveler or a budget traveler. These
few tips can help you enjoy more by spending less.

**Reaching McLeodganj **

The town is well connected with all major cities in India. However
it is recommended to take the train as it will be cheaper and more
comfortable as compared to other modes of transportation. The nearest
international airport is New Delhi. You can easily take a train from
Delhi to Pathankot, the nearest broad gauge railways station, and then
to Kangra--the nearest meter gauge station. From Kangra you can take a
taxi to McLeodganj which will only take about 20 to 30 minutes.
There is frequent bus service also that you can take. This
route might take more time but, considering the picturesque setting of
mountains and valleys, this is worth taking.

**Budget Accommodations **

You can easily find a hotel or rest house with all the basic
amenities at a minimum cost. However, before choosing one, make sure it
is within walking distance from your area of interest. For example, if
you are here on a pilgrimage then it is better to stay in and around
the temple area rather than in a hotel on the outskirts. Mentioned
below are names of few budget hotels that might help you in your stay
in McLeodganj.

**Anupam Resort **

This hotel is located nearby Dal Lake and is in a beautiful setting. It
is around 4 km from McLeodganj and provides guests with all the basic
amenities like laundry, TV car rental.

**Glenmoor Cottages **

Situated on Mall Road in McLeodganj, this facility provides guests with fully-equipped
kitchenettes in its cottages so the guests can prepare their own
food. It also provides other basic amenities.
**Snow Crest Inn
**

This is a newly built budget hotel in Naadi Village, fully equipped
with all the basic amenities. The inn has a fine multi-cuisine restaurant.

**Moving Around **

The center of attraction within the town is, walkable and if you
want, you can take the internal bus service that plies between lower
Dharmashala and McLeodganj. Though it might take longer time, it is
a cheaper mode of transport when compared to hiring a taxi.
