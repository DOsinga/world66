Indian Airlines, Jagson and recently Deccan Airlines have their regular flights from
Chandigarh, Delhi, Dharamshala and Kullu. You will be arriving at
"Jubber Hatti" a small airport which is at a
distance of 28 Km from the town area. It would take you another hour by road to reach Shimla town from Jubber Hatti.
