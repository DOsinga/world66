Sirmour is the southernmost district of Himachal Pradesh, tucked into the Shivalik Hills where the mountains begin their descent toward the Indo-Gangetic plain. The district headquarters is Nahan, a pleasant hill town with a colonial-era layout and a genuine lack of tourist crowds. Other worthwhile stops include Paonta Sahib, a Sikh pilgrimage town on the Yamuna River, and Renuka Lake, the largest natural lake in Himachal Pradesh, sacred to the goddess Renuka.
