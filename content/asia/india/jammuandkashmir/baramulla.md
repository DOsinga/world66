Baramulla is a town in the western part of the Kashmir Valley, about 60 km from Srinagar on the Jhelum river. Historically it was an important trade town on the route between Kashmir and the rest of the subcontinent. Today it is mainly of interest as a base for the areas surrounding it, including Gulmarg (35 km to the south).
