Travel Guide

Bhaderwah also know as “Nagon ki bhoomi” means ‘ land of Snakes '. The history is very little known to general masses. The [Bhaderwah](/asia/india/jammuandkashmir/bhaderwah) Town ( Nagar Bhaderwah ) was known as Hettary Nagar and prior to that, there were two other towns namely Donga nagar and Udho Nagar. Both the Nagar was situated around villages Mondha which is about 3 Kms. In the East of present Bhaderwah town.
