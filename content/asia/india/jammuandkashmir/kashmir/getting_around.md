---
title: "Getting Around"
type: section
---

Within Srinagar, auto-rickshaws and cycle-rickshaws handle short city trips. Shikaras cross the lake and navigate the water channels — essential for reaching the floating gardens and more remote parts of Dal Lake.

For the wider valley, shared Sumo jeeps depart from fixed points in Srinagar on regular routes to Gulmarg, Pahalgam, and other valley destinations. They fill up and leave when full; mornings are the best time to find them. Hiring a private taxi for the day is the most practical option for visiting multiple sites — Srinagar to Gulmarg and back, for example, takes the better part of a day and a shared taxi would be awkward.

Distances in the valley are not large but road conditions and security checkpoints add to journey times. Srinagar to Gulmarg is 55 km (about 1.5 hours). Srinagar to Pahalgam is 96 km (around 2 hours).
