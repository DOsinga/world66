The Gulmarg Gondola climbs in two stages from the meadow at 2,690 m to Afarwat Peak at 3,980 m — one of the highest cable cars in the world. The views from the top across the Kashmir Valley and toward the distant mass of Nanga Parbat (8,126 m, across the Line of Control in Pakistan) are extraordinary. In winter the upper slopes provide India's best skiing; the season runs roughly December to March. In summer the ride is still worth it for the views, the cool air at altitude, and the short walk to Alpather Lake, which stays frozen well into June. [Gulmarg](/asia/india/jammuandkashmir/gulmarg) is 55 km from Srinagar by road.
