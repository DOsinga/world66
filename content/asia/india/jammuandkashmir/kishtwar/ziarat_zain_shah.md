title: Ziarat Zain-Shah-Sahib
type: poi

The most venerated Sufi shrine in the Kishtwar area, dedicated to the saint Zain Shah Sahib. The ziarat draws pilgrims from across the region and is a quiet, atmospheric place to visit outside of the main festival days. Remove footwear before entering.
