The famous village in kulgam like **kadder** is known for the finest apples produced from this village. the village is known for the introduction of apples orchids in that area and now produces huge quantities of apple fruits yearly. Another important thing is that apples of kadder are famous for their quality i.e., taste ,colour and variety etc. so to taste such apples one has to visit the particular village which is just 6 km form main kulgam town.
