Leh is best visited during Summer
months of June to August. Leh in winters is mostly deserted. One can
reach there by road or by air.

**Contributors**
August 12, 2005

### Indian Airlines, Jet Airways

Both the airlines offer direct connection between New Delhi and Leh.
Frequency of flights die down during winters. Average cost of one way
ticket is around INR 6.8k. There are usually no apex (early tickets)
fares.
