type: poi

---

Shey, built in 1645 as a summer residence for the Kings of Ladakh, is one of the oldest palaces in the region. The gompa houses a gilded copper two-storey image of the seated Buddha — one of the largest such images in Ladakh. Located 15 km south-east of Leh, easily combined with Thiksey Monastery on the same day.
