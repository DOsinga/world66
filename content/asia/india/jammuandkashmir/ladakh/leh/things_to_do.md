---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Leh's main sights are the palace and monasteries that line the Indus Valley. The Leh Palace rising above the old town is the defining monument. The Shanti Stupa on its ridge above the tourist village of Changspa gives the best panoramic views and is particularly beautiful at sunset. For the monastery circuit, Thiksey, Hemis, and Alchi Gompa can be visited as day trips from Leh by taxi or hired motorcycle.
