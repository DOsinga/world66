---
title: "Getting There in Srinagar"
type: section
---

One of the must-see hill destinations in India is the capital city of Jammu and Kashmir state, **Srinagar. Getting there** is quite easy as the city is well-connected with all the major cities of the country by air, train and road services.

**Srinagar by Air**

The city has its own airport, Srinagar Airport, which offers
domestic air services. Both private and public carriers operate from
Srinagar to major cities like Chandigarh, Delhi, Mumbai and Shimla on
a regular basis. The nearest international airport is in Delhi. From
Delhi domestic carriers like GoAir and Spicejet offers daily services
to Srinagar.

**Srinagar by Train **

The nearest railway station to reach Srinagar is Jammu which is well-connected with all the major cities of the country like Delhi, Mumbai,
Chennai, Bangalore and Kolkata. Jammu is at a distance of 290 km from
Srinagar. Both bus and taxi services operate on a regular basis from
Jammu to Srinagar.

**Srinagar by Road **
The road network of Srinagar is excellent and connects the city to
all the major cities within the state and cities of Northern India like
Leh, Delhi and Chandigarh. Many government and tourist services operate
daily service in deluxe buses on this route. Cab services are also
offered by many tour operators, however hiring a cab can be very
expensive.
