**Registration**

Foreign nationals should register with the Foreigners Regional Registration Office (FRRO), Canal Road, Srinagar. Check current requirements as rules on registration periods change.

**Money**

ATMs are available throughout central Srinagar near Lal Chowk and Dal Gate. Most guesthouses and houseboats are cash-only; larger hotels accept cards. Banks and authorised money changers on Residency Road handle currency exchange.

**Getting Around**

Auto-rickshaws and cycle-rickshaws handle short trips within the city. For destinations around the valley, shared Sumos (jeeps) depart from fixed points on regular routes. Hiring a private taxi for a full day is the most practical way to visit the Mughal Gardens, Shankaracharya Temple, and other sights spread around the lake.

**Tourist Information**

The Tourist Reception Centre (TRC) near the main bus stand is the official first stop for maps, current conditions, and houseboat bookings. The Houseboat Owners Association office is directly outside.
