Travel Guide

Meerut is a Metro City. Located nearby Delhi, only just 65 Km.s from Capital of India.
Meerut is also known as Gold City of India, where Gold Jewellery Trading occurs on large scale. Meerut is also the Birth place of the war against Britishers.
