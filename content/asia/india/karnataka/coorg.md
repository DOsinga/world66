Travel Guide

Coorg region is famous for its beautiful coffee plantations. Also worth visiting is Buddhist Monastry of Kushalnagar. There are many nice places to stay in coorg. Apart from famous resorts like Orange County & club Mahindra resort there are many privates houses of coffee plantation owners, where homestay is offered.
