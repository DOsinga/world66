title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

Gokarna lies in the district of Uttara Kannada, and is well connected
by road. A number of buses ply to Gokarna from Goa, Bangalore, Kumta,
and other nearby towns.

By Road:

Kumta: 32 kms

Karwar: 60 kms
[Bangalore](/asia/india/karnataka/bangalore): 470 kms

Nearest rail-head:

Kumta 32 kms

Nearest airport:

Dabolim ([Goa](/asia/india/goa)): 185 kms
