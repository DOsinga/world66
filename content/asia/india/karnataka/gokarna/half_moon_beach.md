A small crescent of sand separated from Om Beach by a rocky cliff, reached by a 20-minute walk along the cliff path. Far more secluded than Om or Kudle, with minimal facilities — one or two shacks serve basic meals and chai. In the monsoon season the shack area reverts to a paddy field and the beach is largely deserted.

Basic bamboo huts with well water are available if you want to stay the night. Bring a flashlight for the walk back after dark — the trail is unlit and uneven.
