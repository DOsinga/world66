The annual Urs festival, held at the tomb of Khwaja Bande Nawaz to
commemorate his death anniversary, is an important festival of
Gulbarga. Thousands of pilgrims throng Gulbarga during the Urs
festival.

### [Pavan BS](/asia/india/karnataka/gulbarga/festivals/pavan_bs)

All major Indian festivals are celebrated here with pomp and gaiety, namely:

Holi (festival of Colors)

Deepavali (festival of Lights)

Raksha Bandhan (Festival of Brotherhood)

Sankranti (Festival of Harvesting)

Dusshera (Celebration of triumph of Good over Evil)
