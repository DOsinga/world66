Gulbarga does not have an airport of its own.

You can get to Gulbarga from [Hyderabad](/asia/india/andhrapradesh/hyderabad) which is nearly 5 hrs journey by train/ bus and which is also the nearest airport. You can take trains bound to Mumbai i.e the Hussain Sagar Express originating from Hyderabad.

You can also make it to [Gulbarga](/asia/india/karnataka/gulbarga) from Bangalore by train or bus. The distance from Bangalore is around 623 kms and takes around 12hours by train and 10hrs by bus.

You can take trains bound to [Delhi](/asia/india/delhi) i.e the Karnataka Express originating from Bangalore City or alternatively you could also take trains bound to Mumbai i.e the Udyan Express or the Lokmanya Tilak - Dadar Express from [Bangalore](/asia/india/karnataka/bangalore) city.

Many KSRTC (Govt.) and Private Ltd. buses ply to connect Gulbarga to all different parts of Karnataka, Andhra Pradesh, Maharashtra.

### [By Bus](/asia/india/karnataka/gulbarga/getting_there/by_bus)

As Gulbarga is well connected by road from all over the state and from hyderabad, since last year 2005 there are number of good Volvo bus service has started, about 5 daily volvo buses semi sleepers and two sleeper A/C runs between Gulabraga and bangalore, journey time is about 9-10hrs,they run in the night time,distance is about 610 kms.
