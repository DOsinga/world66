---
title: "Under The Mango Tree"
type: poi
---

About 400 meter to the west from the bazar, this little cafe is actually under a big mangoo tree. Getting there involves a nice walk along the river and then under the banana trees. 

The food is excellent and the service is also great, but being there is what this place is about. The view over the river is great and the quiet is a nice break from the postcard sellers near the temples.
