title: Elephant Stables
type: poi

An elegant row of 11 domed chambers that housed the royal elephants of the Vijayanagara empire. The structure blends Hindu and Islamic architectural elements — unusual for the period. Part of the royal enclosure complex along with the Lotus Mahal.
