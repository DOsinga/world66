latitude: 15.3203

---

A two-storey royal pavilion shaped like a lotus from above, built for the queens of the Vijayanagara court. The interlocking arches and corbelled design kept the interior cool — an early form of air conditioning. The geometric precision of the structure is remarkable given that it was built in the 15th century.
