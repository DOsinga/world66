The spiritual heart of Hampi, a living temple dedicated to Shiva that has been in continuous use since the 7th century. The towering gopuram at the entrance is visible from most of the site. The resident elephant Lakshmi blesses visitors in the morning. The bazaar street leading to the temple is lined with ruined market stalls that were once the richest commercial district in India.
