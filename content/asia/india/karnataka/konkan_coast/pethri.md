Travel Guide

Small town, can be considered as the capital of Cherkady distrcit.

Educational Information:

Primary schools in 5 Kms radius: Apprx 5 - 7

High school: 2

Pre University: NA (nearest in Brahmavar 10 Kms)

Religion: Majority (80%) are Hindus and rest Christians.

Nearest lodge: 10 Kms (Brahmavar)

Locations to Visit: Madi River bridge, Kannar (Durga) temple.

Main road of pethri is both residential and commercial. People in this main road (Pethri) are well educated and well known families in the region compared to some interior parts.
