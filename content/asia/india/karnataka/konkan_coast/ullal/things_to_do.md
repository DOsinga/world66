---
title: "Things to do in Ullal"
type: section
---

1. Cycling (hiring of cycles available in the local village) along the country side & along the back waters & lagoons.
2. Boating in the rivers, back waters & lagoons.
3. Fishing in the river.
4. Sailing or canoeing in the river.
5. Cockle picking off the river bed at the Islands in the river.
6. Picnic to islands in the river / moon lit picnic to the islands.
7. A sojourn with the fishermen on their fishing trips in the sea (sometimes at night) / Shark fishing.
8. Picnic to the Someshwara Rock, and Temple (2km. along the beach to the south of Summer Sands Beach resort).
9. Trip to the bar (where the two rivers Nethravathi & Gurpur meet). Here we are on a tongue of land with the sea on one side, the river on the other side with Mangalore city beyond, and the Western Ghats as a back drop.
10. A walk along the single winding street through the village of Ullal where there is a small market place and general shopping.
11. Marine processing unit.
12. Boat Building / Repairing.
13. Lime kilns, dry fish & fish oil extraction.
14. Visit the orphanage with handicrafts center (cutwork embroidery) and old age home at the Nirmala Convent.
15. Adoption services at the Nirmala Convent. Ph 2465692
16. Cashew Research Centre at Thokkotu.
17. Rope making at Kallappu.
18. Nirmala convent out-patient/Maternity center, a home for destitute children.
a. S.K Hospital, Syyed Madani Charitable Hospital and Pandyaraja Ballal Hospital, barely a 7 minute walk away.
19. Yellappa Nethaji Hospital about 2km. on the NH 17 at Thokkotu. ( 24 hr drug store)
20. Medical Drug Store at Ullal , a 5 minute walk.
21. Ullal Railway Station at a distance of 2km. Ph. 2466426
22. Three Wheeler auto, taxi cabs and regular local and express bus service just outside the Resort.
23. Internet Surfing Parlours & STD/ISD booths at the Ullal Town, a 7 minute walk from the Resort.
24. Eco-trips for experiencing nature, photography or painting / drawing along the coast, rivers, lagoons, backwaters or the countryside.
25. Professional photographer on call. Mr. Prashanth 2465106, 98451 56580. Also photo developing available .it is a 5 minutes walk.
26. Photograph processing 2 kms away at Thokottu
27. Cashew research Center at Thokottu 2 kms away ph : 2466249 8am – 5 pm, sat 8 am – 1 pm
28. Petrol Diesel at a distance of 2 km at Thokottu.More supermarket 1 km away
