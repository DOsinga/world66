This lovely city will cover you up under a spell of sandelwood as soon as you set foot in it. Besides that, it is also the paradise for incense lovers. Whatever sort of scent you're looking for, there is a vendor stall thats sells it. The city offers many markets, the Maharadja's and the Jaganmoham Palace (with an excellent painting section) and some good museums. It might be a nice option to spend a morning or afternoon climbing up Chamundi Hill. Famous rock cuttings and temples along the way make this climb even worth more the effort.
