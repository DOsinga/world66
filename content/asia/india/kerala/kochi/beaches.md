### [Kuzhupilly Beach](/asia/india/kerala/kochi/beaches/kuzhupilly_beach)

T he beach is 26km from Cochin
International Airport , 4.5 km
down the Cherai coast and 24km away from Ernakulam. Very unique with backwaters, shrimpfarms and lagoons just 100mt away frm beach. This fishing village has amazingly friendly people. A good alternative for travellers looking for a non commercial place to live, close to paradise.
