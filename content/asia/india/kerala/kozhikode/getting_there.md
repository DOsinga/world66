**By Air**

Air-India has, since December 15, 1993, been operating 12 flights per week jointly with Indian Airlines - four each to Dubai, Abu Dhabi and Muscat. The flights are operated with Airbus A320 aircraft.  The Airport is located roughly 30 kms. away from the city office. It has both domestic and international terminals. A Cargo Terminal is getting ready soon. Air India has an office in the new International Terminal. Tel No: 0493-722 401. Air India does not have any special lounge for Executive Class passengers. Apart from the normal Rs. 500/- Foreign Travel Tax, the user`s fee for the development of Airport is levied per passenger @ Rs. 500/-.   [more..](/asia/india/kerala/kozhikode/gettingthere/byair)
