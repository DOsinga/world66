Travel Guide

Many find that this is the most peaceful and beautiful town in Kerala.
Situated on the banks of the holy River Achankoil, Mavelikara is considered as the cultural center of central Travancore; the place boats of some of the oldest English Medium Schools; the last King Sri Chithira Thirunal Maharaja's Mother's palace; the famed Budha Statue; borat races; home town of several famed journalists and writers, catoonists;politicians;artists....and place of cultural integration...being once the capital of the famous kind Mahabali..Mavelikara is really endearing place to live..
