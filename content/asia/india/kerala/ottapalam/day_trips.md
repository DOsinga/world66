type: section
---

5 km away from Ottapalam main town, placed called Varode - on the foot steps of famouns Ananganmala - one of the best mountains in the region. In mythology, it is said that, when Lord Hanuman carried the mountain with Mruthasanjeevini, and while passing through Varode, a piece of the mountain fell which is now called as ananganmala.

A wonderful treking place for whole day. Located in a village. Can expect good hotels and there are few refreshment cafes available. Can travel by taxi, bus or autos from Ottapalam to Varode.

June to Sept to be avoided due to rain.
