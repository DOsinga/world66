---
title: "Bars and Cafes"
type: section
---

Varkala's nightlife is low-key. The cliff-top cafes serve drinks alongside food, and a few places have live music in season. Kerala's alcohol laws are stricter than Goa's — some restaurants serve beer and wine discreetly, while others stick to juices and lassis. The atmosphere is sunset-watching and conversation, not clubbing.
