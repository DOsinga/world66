---
title: 'Bars and Cafes'
type: section
---

The cliffside path in Varkala is lined with small, friendly cafes and bars, most with ocean views and a laid-back atmosphere.

**The Funky Art Cafe** is one of the most popular spots, with live music most evenings, good seafood and a social atmosphere. Alcohol is served.

**The Quaint Sunshine Cafe** has a wonderful view of the sea. Try the Arabian salad and Jeera rice with papaya juice.

**The Juice Shack** is not on the cliffs but worth finding. Everything is freshly made, with a great vegetarian menu of salads, juices and rice dishes. A good breakfast spot, especially if you are doing yoga.

**Cafe Del Mar** serves a mix of Indian, Italian and loosely Mexican food. The fresh fish and seafood are the best choices.

**The Rock and Roll Cafe** has the best Indian food on the cliff, with live music in the evenings. A good place to settle in for the night.

**Dolphin Bay** has friendly service, music and a pleasant interior. They tend to give small gifts to diners at the end of the meal.
