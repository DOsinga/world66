---
title: "Shopping"
type: section
---

The cliff path doubles as Varkala's shopping strip. Small shops sell clothing, jewelry, spices, and souvenirs — Tibetan shops with silver jewelry sit alongside Kashmiri handicraft stalls and local stores selling coir products and carved horns. Prices are aimed at tourists, so bargaining is expected. The Khadi Store near town is worth a visit for locally made clothing, oils, and honey at fixed prices.
