**Mandu**

Known as "city of joy", Mandu was founded as a fortress and retreat
by Raja Bhoj in the 10th century. However, Mandu's golden age
began when
Dilawar Khan, the governor of Malwa , set up his own little Kingdom in
the 15th century. Later, Mandu was added to the Mugghal empire by Akbar.

Mandu showcases some of the finest examples of Afghan architecture in India.
