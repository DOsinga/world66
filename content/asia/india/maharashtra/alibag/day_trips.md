**Best of Alibag Day Trips: Visiting Chaul
**

**Alibag day trips** present an excellent way to understand the
cultural heritage of Alibag, a historical city located in the state of
Maharashtra, India. One of the perennial favorites among Alibag day
trips is visiting Chaul.
**Reaching Chaul**

Chaul is located just about 15 kilometers or 30 minutes from central
Alibag. It is well-connected and most residents can easily provide you
with directions. Visitors can rent cabs provided by the numerous hotels
in Alibag. The residents speak a strange dialect which is surprisingly
unknown to residents of Maharashtra itself. It is a rare combination of
the local language, Marathi and Portuguese. This dialect is now referred
to as Kristi.
