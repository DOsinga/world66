Dombivli used to be strictly Common Man's town sometime back, and hence the eating habits also were little different than Hotel Culture. Dombivliates still prefer to eat food on Tapri, or stand alone stalls (don't worry, it's higinic in many places). Trimurti Vada pav is the most famous spot. there are lot of Chat shops opened now a days, serving variety of Chat items, believe me they are very tasty, shichever shop you select. Dombivliates drink sugarcane juice a lot. you will find these juice centers every 5 min. walk on any road. the unique food center in Dombivli is Poli Bhaji Centers. again Trimurty leads here, however remaining all are equally compitemt. they prepare Home Like Food, but it's carry home only.

now a days there are lot of good and big hotels which serve quality Global food (Indian, Chinese, Continential etc.).
