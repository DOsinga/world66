**Gudi Padwa**

A day before gudi padwa , we welcome the marathi new year with spectacular firecrackers and amazing Rangolies.

We erect Gudi's in every house and a huge procession embracing maharashtrian culture is on display. Various eminent personalities also visit dombivli on this day.
