---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

Getting to Dombivli is not much of a problem. Yes, the city hasn't had a fair share of highways, but a State Highway connecting Kalyan, Ambarnath and Ulhasnagar to Mumbai passes through the outskirts of the city.

By Air :
Mumbai is the nearest domestic and international airport, about 50kms from Dombivli.

By Rail :
Dombivli is one of the most important suburban railway stations on the Mumbai Suburban ( Central Railway ) line. However, long-distance trains do not halt at the station.
The nearest major train station is Kalyan Junction, 6 kms from Dombivli or Thane ( district HQ ) - 15 kms. All suburban(fast and slow) trains from Mumbai CST marked as DL, K, A, BL, TL, S, KP, AN, N halt at Dombivli. There are special Dombivli trains from Mumbai CST and Dadar.

By Road :
The Kalyan Shil Road connects Dombivli to the rest of the places. KDMT ( Kalyan Dombivli Municipal Transport ) provides Government bus services to Kalyan. NMMT ( NaviMumbai Municipal Transport ) provides bus services to various suburbs of Navi - Mumbai.
