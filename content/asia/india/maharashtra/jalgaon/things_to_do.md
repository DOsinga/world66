---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Jalgaon's own sights are modest; the caves in the surrounding region are the real draw. The Omkareshwar Temple in the city centre is worth a quick visit, and the Shirsoli Lake 6 km out on the Pachora road makes a pleasant early-morning stop. But most visitors spend their time here planning excursions rather than exploring the town itself.
