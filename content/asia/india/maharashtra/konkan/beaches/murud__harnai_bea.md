A relatively undeveloped stretch of sand near Dapoli on the Konkan coast, about 200 km from Pune and 250 km from Mumbai. The beach is long and uncrowded, backed by casuarina trees and fishing villages. Dolphins are sometimes visible offshore, especially in the early morning. Harnai port, at the southern end, has a small fish market where the day's catch comes in around noon.
