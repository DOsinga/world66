A narrow beach 6km south of Malvan with clear water and white sand — considered one of the best beaches on the Konkan coast for water clarity. The area is also known for scuba diving and snorkelling in the coral reefs offshore. Accessible by bus from Kudal station on the Konkan Railway; Malvan is the nearest town with accommodation.
