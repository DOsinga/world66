A Shiva temple set on the shore about 12 km north of Deogad town. The beach here is quiet and clean, and the temple complex is well maintained. The drive up from Deogad passes through lush coastal countryside. Pilgrims come year-round, but it is particularly busy during the monsoon when the sea is dramatic.
