title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

The Konkan coast is shaped by its geography: long stretches of relatively undeveloped beach, Maratha sea-forts rising from the water, and a fishing culture that feeds one of India's most distinctive regional cuisines. The forts at Janjira, Sindhudurg, and Vijaydurg are all worth visiting for their history and their settings above the sea.
