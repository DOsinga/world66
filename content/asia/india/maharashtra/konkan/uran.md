Travel Guide

Located near Bombay, Maharashtra, Home of the famous Tungekar's of Uran.
Their famous ancestor Mohammad Ibrahim Mohammad Jafar Tungekar was famous for his acts of Philanthropy.
The Tungekar families summer vacation home built aproximately 200 years ago is located in Pirwari, near Uran.
