Travel Guide

**WANIVADE** is one of village of Deogad taluka of sindhudurga district, This village is famous for Alphanso Mango. The Occupation of the people in this village is farming and fishing. This village is located on near by mond-baparde.

**Pavnaidevi **temple is also famous in Deogad taluka. **Bhairaidevi** Mandir @ Mond. **Kavaladevi **Mandir at Tembavali. Peoples going to native place in vacation were also visit to these temples.
