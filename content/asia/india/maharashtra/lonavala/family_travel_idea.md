Family travelers headed towards Lonavala will find the following **Lonavala family travel**
ideas very useful. Lonavala is a small hill-station of Maharashtra.
Located within the Sahyadri Mountains, it enjoys pleasant weather
throughout the year. En route to Lonavala, visitors can spend some time
at the Rajmachi Point or the Sunset Point that offers a postcard-like
sunset view. There is vast garden spread out here, where small children
can play with parents feeling relaxed about the safety of the
play-rides.

**Exploring Lonavala Lakes & Waterfalls**

Lonavala is popularly referred to as the lake district of
Maharashtra. It is sprinkled with numerous artificial lakes. The most
famous ones are the Bushy, Pawna and Tungarli lakes. Visitors can rent
fishing equipment and fish by the lakeside. Visiting Lonavala
post-monsoon provides an opportunity to see the small waterfalls in full
bloom.

**Exploring Lonavala Forts**

Lonavala is among India’s noted historic spots, apart from being a
vacationing option. The forts of the famous Maratha warrior, Chhatrapati
Shivaji, are considered sacred by the Maharashtrians. Other forts worth
exploring include the Lohagad fort and those of Rajmachi and Visapur.
Fort visits are usually scheduled in the form of short daytime trips
that are ideal for a family visit.
