---
title: "Things to do in Lonavala"
type: section
---

Visit the ancient Karla & Bhaja caves, built in 1 st century A.D. and 6th century A.D. respectively, paragliding, rappelling, treks to old and romantic forts of the Maratha warriors perched on mountain tops, yoga ashrams and in the monsoon, enjoy the waterfalls that the area is famous for.
