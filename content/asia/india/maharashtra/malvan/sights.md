### [Tarkarli](/asia/india/maharashtra/malvan/sights/tarkarli)

7km north of Malvan Beach, is Tarkarli... pristine beach...all the privacy one can need; a great getaway... Further north about another 6-7km, is the Tarkarli creek and a lovely estuary...boat ride on the Tarkarli creek totally recommended; takes you a little out in the sea and on a lucky day, you might spot some dolphins too! Lovely accomodation at MTDC cottages on the beach.

### [Sindhudurg Fort](/asia/india/maharashtra/malvan/sights/sindhudurg_fort)

Constructed by Shivaji in 1664, at a site personally selected by him.
The construction of a sea fort is a stupendous task, and at Sindhudurg
no efforts were spared. Over 2000 khandis (4000 mounds) of iron were
used for casting and the foundation stones were laid down firmly in
lead.

Even today, as one approaches the fort past a rocky
reef, navigable through a narrow channel, one marvels at the
transportation of such heavy material through such choppy waters.
Within its precincts are temples holding the shrines of Maruti,
Bhavani, Mahadeo, Jarimai, Mahapurush and also of [more..](/asia/india/maharashtra/malvan/sights/sindhudurg_fort)

### [Malvan Beach](/asia/india/maharashtra/malvan/sights/malvan_beach)

This is one of the most commercially active beaches in the Konkan. It is buzzing with life at the jetty where most of the fishermen anchor their boats of various dimensions and colors. There are a few bars and restaurants on the beach. The government has proposed the marine park and rock garden since 2000 but nothing done as of now.

The launch service to Sindhudurg Fort is available at Malvan beach.
