'[Be the Local](/asia/india/maharashtra/mumbai/travelagents)' offers walking tours of the Dharavi
slum, led by lifelong residents of the slum fluent in English. Unlike
other tour companies, 'Be the Local' is the only tour service fully owned and
operated by Dharavi residents, not foreigners.

Three times daily, the tours range from 1-3.5 hours. The Dharavi slum is a
breathtaking, overwhelming experience at the heart of central Mumbai, with a
massive recycling industry, ultra-dense housing, and both poverty and wealth
existing in close proximity. Seeing the slum firsthand is safer and easier
under the auspices of a guide from Dharavi.

The longer tours are only slightly more expensive and worth the extra time,
traveling through the slum's factories, bakeries, dwellings and schools, and
interacting with local residents.

Tours are offered at 9:30am, 10:15am, 12:00nn, 2:30pm, 3:15pm. Prices are Rs 350 for a 1-hr tour, Rs 400 for a 2.5 hr tour, Rs 500 for a 3.5 hour tour. 'Be the Local' can be reached at +91 9664376369 anytime or visit http://www.bethelocaltoursandtravels.com/ .
