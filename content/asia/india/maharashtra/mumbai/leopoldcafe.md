One of Mumbai's oldest and most famous bars, open since 1871 and a long-time gathering point for travellers and Mumbaikars alike on Colaba Causeway. The menu spans Indian and Western food; the beer is cold and the atmosphere reliably lively. Featured prominently in Gregory David Roberts' novel *Shantaram*. The cafe was targeted in the 2008 Mumbai terrorist attacks and has since been rebuilt.
