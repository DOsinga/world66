The town hall is designed by Col. Thomas Cowper and was completed in 1833. It is especially known for its neo-classical architecture.** juiee:** This neo-classical design is due to Colonel Thomas Cowper of the Bombay Engineers. The building is 200 feet long and 100 feet deep. The long  facade with three porticos features fluted Doric columns. Gradually the town hall came to be closely associated with the growth and development of Bombay
