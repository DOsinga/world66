---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Mumbai's historic core is in the south — the Fort area, Colaba, and the waterfront around the Gateway of India — where the colonial-era monuments, museums, and bazaars are concentrated. Further afield, the Elephanta Caves on their island and the Buddhist cave complex at Kanheri in Sanjay Gandhi National Park are both worth a half-day or more.
