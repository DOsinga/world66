---
title: "Things to do in Mumbai"
type: section
---

There are lots of activities for all age group in **Mumbai Things to
Do. **This metro is one of the most happening places in India and a
trip here is always full of memorable experience.

**Night life**

Mumbai is better known as “The City Never Sleeps” because of its very
active nightlife. The city has several nightclubs, discotheques,
lounges and pubs where people love to spend time in the evenings. These
are the best places to relax after a hectic day schedule. The music,
liquor and food in a great relaxing atmosphere makes one forget all
stress and make merry.

**Shopping**

With sprawling malls and shopping centers, Mumbai is a great place
for the shoppers.Some of the well known traditional markets are Zaveri
Bazzar known for all kinds of jewelry and Chor Bazar known for antiques
and wooden carvings. The malls are best place for branded items.

**Bollywood**

A trip to Mumbai is incomplete without visiting Film City. One of the
biggest industries in the country, Bollywood is what Hollywood is in
the West. Watching the film shooting is one of the favorite activities
of many tourists visiting the city. Cinemas have played an important
role in almost everyone’s life and getting a chance to see it in real
life should not be missed.

**Amusement Park**

There are several amusement parks in the city like the Essel World
and Water Kingdom. Spending a whole day with family and friends in these
parks is one of the memorable experiences of the trip here. The parks
offer rides which members of all age group can enjoy.

**Paragliding**

If you are the adventurous type then indulge yourself in paragliding
during your stay in Mumbai. You need not be a professional to do this
activity. The trainers can help you learn the basics so that you enjoy
the activity in no time. The Western Paragliding Association hosts the
paragliding event.

**Camel Ride in Juhu Beach**

Juhu Beach is the most happening place in Mumbai. It is an excellent
place to spend the evening with your family during your Mumbai trip.
Children as well as adults love to enjoy the camel ride here. Enjoy
playing with the waves or just sit and relax in the sand. Try excellent
snacks from the food stalls when tired.
