Parsi Point and Sydney Point are alike. Parsi Point lies in between Mahabaleshwar and Sydney, Sydney Point is along the route of Wai. From Parsi Point, you can see the beautiful view of the Koyna Valley and from Sydney Point, you can catch a glimpse of the Wai Village. For 5Rs, you you can also have a look through the binoculars at the famous Ganpati Temple at Wai.
