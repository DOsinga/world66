The Rajpuri Caves are located at a distance from 7 kms from Pachgani and the trip takes at least 30 to 35 minutes. Local taxis are available at a nominal charge of 5Rs. Passing through villages and finally descending by brick steps, you will reach the Rajpuri Caves. The Kartik Swami Temple is in one constructed out of cave-sand and has several water kunds, like the Triveni Sangam temple at Prayag. The story goes that by taking bath in this Kund, you'll be cured of all your diseases. According to the Vedshastra, the river Ganges also floats into these kunds, most of them being named after gods, such as the 'Shri Dutt Kund', 'Shri Malikarjun Kund' and 'Shri Vishnu Kund'.
