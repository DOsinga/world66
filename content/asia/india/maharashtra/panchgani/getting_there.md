---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

Panchgani has no railway station of its own — the nearest is Wathar, about 45 km away, or (much better served) Pune, 100 km northeast. Most visitors drive up from **Pune** (about 2.5 hours via Wai) or from **Mumbai** (about 5 hours via the Mumbai–Pune Expressway and Wai). State transport buses run from both cities and from nearby Satara. There is no airport; Pune Airport is the closest major hub.
