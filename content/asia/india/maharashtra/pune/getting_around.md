**Pune Forts:**

Pune, the land of forts
and the sea forts stand in testimony of the glory of **Raja Shivaji** till
date. Historically speaking Pune was handed over to the Peshwa family,
whose Maratha power rose to be a major political force. In 1818, Pune
was taken over by the East India Company. Though the capital city of
Maharashtra, Mumbai is attracts larger number of tourists, Pune is a
popular tourist destination being the cultural capital of Maharashtra.
Now, Pune has even gained fame as an important commercial and
educational centre as well.
