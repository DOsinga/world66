Ukhrul is the district headquarters of Ukhrul district in eastern Manipur, about 80 km from Imphal at an altitude of around 1,830 metres in the Naga Hills. It is the highest hill station in Manipur and the cultural centre of the Tangkhul Naga people. The surrounding hills are covered in forests and the area is known for the Shirui lily — a rare lily found only on Shirui peak and celebrated at the annual Shirui Lily Festival. The hills offer trekking and a cooler climate than the valley below.
