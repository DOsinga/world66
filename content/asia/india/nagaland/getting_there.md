---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

**By air:** Dimapur Airport has flights to Kolkata, Delhi, Guwahati, and Imphal. The airport is small and connections are limited; book ahead, especially around the Hornbill Festival. From Dimapur, Kohima is about 3 hours by road (74 km on mountain roads).

**By train:** Dimapur is the only railway station in Nagaland, connected to Guwahati and the broader northeast rail network. The overnight train from Guwahati is a reasonable option.

**By road:** State buses and shared taxis connect Dimapur and Kohima. Guwahati in Assam is the main regional hub; buses run between the two cities.

**Permits:** Non-Naga Indian citizens need an Inner Line Permit (ILP), obtainable online through the Nagaland government portal or at entry points including Dimapur airport and railway station. Foreign nationals need a Protected Area Permit (PAP), also available online and at state checkpoints. Both are straightforward to get but don't skip them — checks are real.
