---
title: "Dzukou Valley"
type: poi
category: Sight
latitude: 25.5417
longitude: 94.0750
---

A high valley at around 2,440 metres behind Japfu Peak, about 30 km south of Kohima. The valley is known for its seasonal blooms — particularly the Dzukou lily, which flowers in July and August — and for being genuinely peaceful. The main trailhead is at Viswema village; the walk up takes two to three hours. Basic huts in the valley allow overnight stays. Go in summer for flowers, or in winter for frost and solitude.
