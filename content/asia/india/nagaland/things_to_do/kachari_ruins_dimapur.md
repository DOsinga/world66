---
title: "Kachari Ruins, Dimapur"
type: poi
category: Sight
latitude: 25.9065
longitude: 93.7264
---

Scattered through the outskirts of Dimapur, these stone mushroom-shaped pillars are the remains of the medieval Kachari kingdom, which controlled much of the Brahmaputra valley before the Ahom expansion in the 16th century. The main cluster is in a small park. Low-key and easy to miss, but a good reminder that the region's pre-colonial history runs deep.
