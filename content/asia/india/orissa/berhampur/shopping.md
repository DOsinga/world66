---
title: 'Shopping'
type: section
---

Berhampur is the biggest commercial hub in southern Odisha, and the star purchase is Berhampuri silk sarees with their distinctive designs. The main shopping areas are Annapurna Market, Urban Bank Road, SNT Road, Ram Lingam Tank Road, Giri Market, and Chena Bazar. You can find everything from ethnic products to electronics, but the silk sarees are what make a trip to the markets worthwhile.
