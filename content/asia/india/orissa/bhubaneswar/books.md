---
title: 'Books'
type: section
---

Bhubaneswar has a handful of bookshops clustered around Market Building and the city centre. Granthalaya, Utkal Bharati, and Gyana Trushna are all in or near the Market Building complex. Modern Book Shop at Master Canteen Square carries a good range, and Tech Book Shop on Janpath stocks technical titles. For books about Odisha's temples, history, and culture, these local shops often have titles you will not find elsewhere.
