---
title: 'Eating Out'
type: section
---

Bhubaneswar has a lively food scene ranging from street stalls to upmarket hotel restaurants. For North Indian food, try Dawat on Janpath. Ashirwad in Bapuji Nagar is popular for its cosy atmosphere and both vegetarian and non-vegetarian dishes. South Indian food fans should head to the South Indian Hotel or Suruchi, both near Market Building. Venus Inn in Bapuji Nagar does good Chinese. For the real taste of the city, seek out the street food around Market Building, the famous Chit-Chat stall in Bapuji Nagar, and the gupchup vendors on Mahatma Gandhi Road.
