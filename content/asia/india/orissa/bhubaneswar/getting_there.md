---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

Bhubaneswar is connected by daily flights from New Delhi, Bombay and Calcutta .It is also connected by air to Hyderabad , Raipur and Banaras . Bhubaneswar has 3 daily express trains to Delhi, a weekly Rajdhani to Delhi, a daily express train to Bombay, several daily trains to Calcutta , and has daily express trains to Madras and Hyderabad .
