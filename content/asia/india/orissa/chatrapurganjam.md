Chatrapur (also spelled Chhatrapur) is the headquarters of Ganjam District in southern Odisha. It is a small town with two railway stations on the Chennai-Howrah main line. The town sits on NH-5, making it easy to reach. The Thakurani Yatra festival, celebrated once every two years, is the most eagerly awaited local event and draws large crowds.
