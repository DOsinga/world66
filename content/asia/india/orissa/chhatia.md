Popularly known as Chhatiabata, Chhatia is a sacred place of pilgrimage about 30 km from Cuttack. The shrine of Lord Jagannath here is a fine example of modern Orissan temple architecture. Nearby lie the archaeological remains of Amravati-Kataka, one of the five important forts of the Chodaganga Deva dynasty. The hill called Dhania provides an attractive backdrop to the ruins.
