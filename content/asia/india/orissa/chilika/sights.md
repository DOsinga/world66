### [Nalabana Island (island of reeds)](/asia/india/orissa/chilika/sights/nalabana_island_i)

The island of Nalabana, 8 Kms. in circumference occupies a unique place
in the vast expanse of Chilka Lake as it happens to be the central
point for the birds. Nearest Access point to this wonderful island is
from Barkul a place from where Orissa tourism runs Boats and where
Orissa toursm have accomodation facilities too.

### [Kalijai Island](/asia/india/orissa/chilika/sights/kalijai_island)

The island of Kalijai is famous as a centre of religious worship due to
the temple of Goddess Kalijai where a big fair is held on the occasion
of Makar Sankranti that falls on January 14th every year. There is an
epic like story behind the place which is centered around an innocent
girl called "Kalijai" who got married and was going by a boat along
with her in-laws through this lake to the in-laws place. In between,
heavy storms came and the boat sank. Everybody including Kalijai died
in that storm. The place where the temple stands now is exactly the
place where the boat sank.

### [Satpada](/asia/india/orissa/chilika/sights/satpada)

This popular sight of Chilika lake is near to Bhubaneshwar the Capital
of the State. Chilika Developemnt Authourity is running a musuem here
which explains the phenomenon called chilika and wetland biodiversity.
Here you can find famous Irrawady Dolphins.

### [Dr Hrudananda Jena](/asia/india/orissa/chilika/sights/malud_parikud)

Malud-Parikud

Malud and Parikud are the main islands inside Chilika lake. The eastern part of these islands is the Bay of Bengal and are full of natural sceneries. These are potential sites for water sking and water sports. These have potential for tourist attraction. Malud is 22 km from national highway-5 (NH-5). Malud is wel connected by road to Berhampur. Tourists travelling from southern India can reach Satpada and Puri through this route. Malud and Parikud are pollution free calm and beautiful places for building island resorts and holidat homes.
