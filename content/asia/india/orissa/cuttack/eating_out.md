---
title: 'Eating Out'
type: section
---

Cuttack is famous for its street food. The city's dahi bara and aaloo dum, available from countless street vendors, are genuinely excellent. You will find the best stalls in front of Barabati Stadium. Gupchup (pani puri) is equally popular. For sit-down meals, Akbari Restaurant serves good Indian food, and Dwaraka in the Brindaban Cinema complex is a reliable option.
