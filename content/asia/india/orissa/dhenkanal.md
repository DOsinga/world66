Dhenkanal is a district town in central Odisha known for its Kapilash Temple, a Shiva shrine atop a hill surrounded by forest. The Satakosia Gorge on the Mahanadi River, partly within Dhenkanal district, is one of Odisha's most dramatic natural landscapes. The district is also known for its dhokra metal castings, a traditional tribal craft made using the lost-wax method.
