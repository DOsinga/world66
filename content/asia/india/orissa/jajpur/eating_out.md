---
title: "Eating Out in Jajpur"
type: section
---

When it comes to eating, Jajpur is one of the best places for people who love food. You can get really good east indian spicy foods at a very reasonable price in Jajpur. For both veg and non veg foods the hotels to look out are Tarinee hotel, Swasti and Trupti, Yashoda hotel.
