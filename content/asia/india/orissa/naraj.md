Naraj sits at the point where the Mahanadi River splits into the Kathajodi, about 12 km from [Cuttack](/asia/india/orissa/cuttack). It is a scenic spot with a long history — once a centre of Buddhist learning, now a quiet riverside settlement. The barrage across the Mahanadi here controls the flow into the delta and makes for a pleasant place to watch the water, especially in the evening.
