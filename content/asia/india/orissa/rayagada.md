Rayagada is a small district town in southern Odisha, primarily of interest as a base for visiting the weekly tribal markets in the surrounding hills. The Dongria Kondh and other tribal communities hold colourful haats (markets) in the area, though visiting these responsibly and with a local guide is strongly recommended.
