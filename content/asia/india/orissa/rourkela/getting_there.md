---
title: 'Getting There'
type: section
---

Rourkela is well connected by rail, with direct trains to Kolkata (8 hours), Bhubaneswar (overnight), Mumbai (26 hours), Delhi (30 hours), and Chennai (32 hours). There are also flights connecting Rourkela to Bhubaneswar. By road, Rourkela is 350 km from Bhubaneswar. Both state-run and private buses operate on major routes, with overnight luxury services being the most comfortable option.
