### [Ghoghar](/asia/india/orissa/rourkela/sights/ghoghar)

A gorge on the river Ib, extending over 5 Km, it is popular as a picnic spot. A naturally formed Siva Lingam of black granite worshipped by the local people, is an additional attraction.

### [Khandadhar](/asia/india/orissa/rourkela/sights/khandadhar)

244 metres or almost 800 feets in height, the waterfall at Khandadhar is said to be the loveliest and is the highest waterfall in Orissa and it is a nice picnic spot. Khandadhar means the sharpness of a sword in the reginal lanaguage Oriya, which is also the state language.

### [Vedavyas](/asia/india/orissa/rourkela/sights/vedavyas)

Situated on the confluence of the rivers Sankha and Koel, Vedavyas is not only a place of natural beauty but also considered a ‘Tirtha’,a place of religious sanctity. A vedic Ashram and a school on the lines of Gurukula Ashram are the added attractions of the place.

Tradition has it that Vyasadeva, the reputed sage lived here and composed the great epic, Mahabarata. There are three temples here, two dedicated to Lord Shiva and one to Rama. A vedic ashram and a school on the lines of gurukul are added attractions of the place. An ideal picnic spot, it is 5 kms from [more..](/asia/india/orissa/rourkela/sights/vedavyas)
