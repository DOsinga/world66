---
title: 'Shopping'
type: section
---

Odisha's craft traditions run deep, and shopping here means buying directly from artisans or their cooperatives. The standout purchases are Sambalpuri ikat textiles (tie-dyed silk and cotton sarees from the weavers of Sambalpur and Sonepur), silver filigree work from Cuttack, and pata chitra paintings from the artists' village of Raghurajpur near Puri.

Applique work from Pipli, bell metal castings in the dhokra tradition, stone carvings from Puri's Pathuria Sahi, and palm-leaf etchings are all widely available. The Utkalika State Emporium in Bhubaneswar carries a curated selection of most crafts and can arrange shipping. For the full experience, visit the artisan villages themselves, where you can watch the work being done and buy directly.
