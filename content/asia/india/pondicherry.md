Travel Guide

Pondicherry or Where God comes to take a break !

A quiet, spiritual sea-side town, a few hours drive from Chennai. Centered around the Ashram of Sri Aurobindo, this old french colony offers authentic accomodation such as the Aurodhan Guest House, where you stay over an art gallery and lear about the town of Pondi, the people, the ashram and the experimental town of Auroville from the fascinating owner, Lalit Verma.

A stroll on the sea side, dinner at the roof-top restaurants overlooking the bay of bengal, a visit to the Matrimandir in Auroville, so many things to do in Pondicherry.
