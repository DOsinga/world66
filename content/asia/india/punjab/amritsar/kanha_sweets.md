A long-standing fixture near the Golden Temple, Kanha Sweets is one of the better places to try amritsari kulcha. The stuffed flatbreads come with chickpea curry, pickled onions, and a wedge of butter. Order the aloo-paneer version. Cheap, quick, and usually packed with locals on their way to or from the temple.
