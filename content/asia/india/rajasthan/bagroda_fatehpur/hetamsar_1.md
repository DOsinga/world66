Travel Guide

Hetamsar is a city in the State of Rajasthan with a population of
approximately 2700. The closest tourism destination to Hetamsar is
Shekhawati. Other close by tourism destinations include Jhunjhunu,
Dundlod and Mandawa. The nearest major railway station to Hetamsar is
Jhunjhunun (JJN) which is at a distance of 15.5 kilometres. The nearest
airport is at Jaipur which is at a distance of 149 kilometres. Lying at
an elevation of 1059 m, which makes it a high-altitude city
