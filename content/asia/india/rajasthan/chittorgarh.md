Chittorgarh has many sights: old city walls and ruined palaces.

Western railways connect Chittorgarh on the metre guage with [Ajmer](/asia/india/rajasthan/ajmer), [Delhi](/asia/india/delhi), [Jaipur](/asia/india/rajasthan/jaipur), Ratlam and [Udaipur](/asia/india/rajasthan/udaipur).
