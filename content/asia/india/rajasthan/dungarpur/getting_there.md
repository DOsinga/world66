---
title: 'Getting There'
type: section
---

Dungarpur has no airport or major railway station. The nearest airport is at Udaipur (110 km) and the nearest major railhead is at Ratlam in Madhya Pradesh (80 km). Buses run regularly from Udaipur, taking about two and a half hours. Cycle rickshaws and auto-rickshaws are the main transport within town.
