---
title: 'Getting There'
type: section
---

Jaipur has good connections by air, rail, and road. Jaipur International Airport (JAI) has direct flights to Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore, Hyderabad, and several other Indian cities, plus some international routes to Dubai and Bangkok.

By rail, Jaipur is well connected to Delhi (4.5-5.5 hours by express train), Agra (4 hours), Jodhpur (5-6 hours), and Udaipur (8-9 hours). The main station is Jaipur Junction in the city center. By road, it is 260 km from Delhi (4-5 hours), 230 km from Agra, and 340 km from Jaisalmer.
