The grandest fort in the Jaipur area, Amber sits on a hillside 11 km north of the city. The architecture blends Rajput and Mughal styles beautifully, with the Sheesh Mahal (Mirror Palace) as the standout room. You can walk up from Maota Lake or take a jeep. The elephant rides that were once popular here have drawn animal welfare criticism and are best avoided.
