---
title: 'Getting There'
type: section
---

Jaisalmer is remote, which is part of its appeal. The nearest major city is [Jodhpur](/asia/india/rajasthan/jodhpur), about 290 km east.

By rail, a daily overnight train connects Jaisalmer to Jodhpur (5-6 hours) and onward to [Jaipur](/asia/india/rajasthan/jaipur) and Delhi. By road, Jaisalmer is connected to Jodhpur (290 km), Jaipur (560 km), Bikaner (330 km), and Delhi (800 km) via state buses and private operators. Jaisalmer has a small airport with seasonal flights to Delhi and Jaipur, though services are not always reliable.
