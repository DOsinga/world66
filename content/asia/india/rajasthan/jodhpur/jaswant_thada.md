A white marble cenotaph built in 1899 in memory of Maharaja Jaswant Singh II, set on a small lake just below Mehrangarh. The marble is so finely carved it glows from within when the sun is right. Quieter and far less crowded than the fort, it makes a good pause on the walk down. Family memorials of subsequent rulers stand in the surrounding garden.
