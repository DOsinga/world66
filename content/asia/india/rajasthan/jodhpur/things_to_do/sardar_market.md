The clock tower (Ghanta Ghar) anchors the old city's main bazaar, a dense warren of stalls selling spices, bandhani textiles, brass, lacquer bangles, and silver. This is the heart of Jodhpur's daily life and the best place to try mirchi vada from a street stall. Come in the late afternoon when the light is good and the heat has eased; the lanes radiating off the square reward aimless wandering.
