---
title: 'Eating Out'
type: section
---

Sri Ganganagar's food has a strong Punjabi influence, with hearty dhabas serving dal, paneer dishes, and fresh roti. Cheap restaurants cluster around the main market area and Gol Bazaar. The city is known locally for its mango bars and jeera (cumin) drinks -- look for stalls selling chilled jeera water and nimbu pani in summer.

For a sit-down meal, Hotel Shagun in the Old Grain Market serves reliable vegetarian Indian food.
