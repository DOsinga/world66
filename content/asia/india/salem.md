Travel Guide

Known for its mangoes and its steel plant, this city gets its name from being surrounded by hills. It is situated at the base of the Yercaud Hills and a hour long drive up these hills from Salem is a treat for the eyes, be it in the daytime or night when the citylights shines like a bejewelled ornament. Its proximity to bigger cities like Chennai, Bangalore, Coimbatore and Madurai and its comparatively equable climate makes it a very favourable city to live in.
