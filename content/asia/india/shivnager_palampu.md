Travel Guide

Shivnager Palampur is a green hill station and council in the Kangra Distt in the Indian state of Himachal Pradesh. Shivnager is a special interest place. Abundance of water and proximity to the mountains has endowed it with mild climate.The town has derived its name from the Old Lord Shiva Mandir (Local Word for Lord Shiva Sehnalia). In the backdrop of this beautiful town stands the Dhauladhar mountain range, which remains snow-covered for most part of the year.
