Like the San Thome Church, Luz Church is also one of the oldest churches in
[Chennai](/asia/india/tamilnadu/chennai). This church was built by Portugese, in the 12th Century, and was later renovated during British rule."Luz" in Portugese means light. When some Portugese sailors were sailing in a storm, a light appeared in the land and they followed it to the shore. They erected a church in the same place where they saw the light. A beautiful church to visit.-Ram Shridhar
