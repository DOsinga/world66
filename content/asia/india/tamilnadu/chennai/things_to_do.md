---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Chennai's sights spread across the city, but most visitors start with the old colonial strip along the waterfront. **Fort St. George** is the oldest surviving English fort in India. A short drive south, **Marina Beach** runs for thirteen kilometres — the second-longest urban beach in the world, and where Chennai walks in the evenings. For Hindu Chennai, head to **Mylapore**, the city's oldest neighbourhood, and the **Kapaleeswarar Temple**. The **Government Museum** in Egmore has the best collection of Chola bronzes anywhere.
