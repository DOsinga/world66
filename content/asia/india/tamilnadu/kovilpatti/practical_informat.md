---
title: "Practical Information in Kovilpatti"
type: section
---

Kovilpatti sits on NH-7 (now NH-44), the main north-south highway connecting Varanasi to Kanyakumari. This gives it decent road connections to the major cities of southern Tamil Nadu.

The railway station is 2 km from the town centre. Trains connect to Chennai, Madurai, and Thiruvananthapuram. The nearest airport is at Madurai, about 95 km away.

The town is easily reached by bus from Tirunelveli (55 km) or Thoothukudi (50 km), with frequent services throughout the day.
