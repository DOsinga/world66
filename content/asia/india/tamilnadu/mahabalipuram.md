Who doesn’t want to send a postcard to his family and friends from ‘Mahabalipuram’? It’s a small village near the sea with excellent beaches. Some people say that Mahabalipuram is becoming the Goa of the south. Undoubtedly, you’ll bump into a lot of travellers who just want to relax before heading for other cities in Tamil Nadu. Mahabalipuram is also known, next to its relaxed atmosphere, for its vast stone shore temples. Nearby are more temples and crafts villages where you can have a closer look at the craft of stone carving.
