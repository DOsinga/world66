Athiyuthu is an agriculture based village . It is the largest area about near 12,000hectares when compared to the nearest surroundings . It is the village panchayat of three villages namely athiyuthu , iraniyanvalasai and kovilkarai . A man from this village is working as a doctor in US . Most of the people from athiyuthu are working in malaysia and saudi .
