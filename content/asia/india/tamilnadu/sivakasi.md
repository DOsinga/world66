Sivakasi , renowned the world over for its printing, Litho Presses,
Safety Matches and Fire Crackers industry and now for its Educational
Institutions. Sivakasi town is known for its fireworks manufacturing
reputation and offset printing machines of which is has the second
largest number in the world, next to Guthenburg, a city in Sweden.
