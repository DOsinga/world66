---
title: "Beaches in Tirunelveli"
type: section
---

**Tirunelveli Beach **offer a joyous and
fun-filled holiday retreat to visitors. Tirunelveli, a town in Tamil
Nadu, is famous for the rich culture, beautiful landscapes and exotic
waterfalls. Colorful festivals and temples also draw tourists here. They
are attracted by the beauty of temples and love to spend their vacation
in Tirunelveli. In this wonderful city with a beautiful beach famous
for its charm and serenity. Crystal clear water draws the attention of
visitors, beach lovers and water sports enthusiasts from all over the
world. If you are planning a vacation in South India, Tirunelveli is an
ideal spot for you. The panoramic view of the sun-kissed beach will
create excitement and make your tour memorable and pleasant.

**Fun Filled Beach Activities**

Tourists can spend quality time on the beach and enjoy swimming and
sunbathing. A boat ride is good option to discover the beauty here. Hire
a boat if you are with your family. This way you can venture into the
sea and coastline. Your guide in the boat will brief you about things to
be seen. Water sports facilities are also available. The beach in
Tirunelveli is ideal for surfing, especially on a bright sunny day.
Sailing is another attraction. Tourists can also go fishing. Rent bait
and hooks and try catching fish on the beach. If you have children
accompanying you, let them watch dolphins and crocodiles in their
natural habitats.

**Water Skiing**

Water skiing is best for adventure freaks and the beach in
Tirunelveli is ideal for skiing. Water skiing is a great way to unwind
and have fun at the same time. Altogether it will give you a thrilling
experience.

**Scuba Diving**

Go scuba diving and discover countless marine creatures. If you are
little scared, go for trials before taking up scuba diving. Those who
want to feel safer in the water can go snorkeling.

**Jet Skiing**

Jet skiing is an ideal activity for speed lovers. Tourists must practice jet skiing only under the supervision of an instructor.

**Best Time to Visit**

The best season for enjoying water sports activities in Tirunelveli
is between October and May. Tirunelveli makes an ideal spot to enjoy
thrilling water sports on the beach. You can get the chance to surf and
swim across the seas to freshen up. Nature lovers can find innumerable
entertainment options. They can visit wildlife parks, sanctuaries,
temples and waterfalls and many other options during their visit.
