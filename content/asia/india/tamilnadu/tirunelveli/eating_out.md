Tirunelveli is famous for its Wheat Halwa.. You can get fresh, hot and yummy wheat halwa from a famous Shop called 'Iruttukadai', just opposite to Nellaiappar Temple.. But the thing is sales starts only in the evening and will get over very soon.. Luck is yours tasting a very yummy halwa.. No need to worry, you can get same kind of Halwa in famous shops like Shanti Sweets and Lakshmi Sweets near Tirunelveli Railway Juntion.. Enjoy the taste of Tirunelveli Halwa... :)

### [SETHUPILLAI HOTEL, Pettai, Tirunelveli](/asia/india/tamilnadu/tirunelveli/eating_out/sethupillai_hotel)

Are you a vegetarian food lover? Don't worry !!! This family owned small business hotel located at Pettai (a near small suburb of Tirunelveli, located about 5 miles from Tiruneveli Town area) is pleased to serve your food needs. This restaurant serves** tasty and spicy Vada**, soft and yummy **Idli sambar**,** Dosa** varieties for your breakfast/ dinner needs and different **special rice varieties** for your lunch needs.
