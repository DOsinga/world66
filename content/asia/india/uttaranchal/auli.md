Travel Guide

Auli features a ski resort run by the state-owned Garwahl Mandal Vikas Nigam, which has what is arguably some of the cheapest skiing in the world. It is connected by road to [Haridwar](/asia/india/uttaranchal/haridwar), a day's bus journey away.
