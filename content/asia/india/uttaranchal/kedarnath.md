Travel Guide

The village of Kedarnath has a shiv temple that is one of the holiest places in India. It is one of the Char dham yatras.

The temple is reached through a 14 km trek from he village of Gaurikund. It remains open till the first week for Nov, when it shuts down for the 6 months for the winter.
