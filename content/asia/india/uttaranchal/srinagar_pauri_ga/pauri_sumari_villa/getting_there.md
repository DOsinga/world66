---
title: "Getting There in pauri sumari village"
type: section
---

Sumari is 22 km away from Srinagar town via road and 24 km from district town of Pauri Garhwal. There are two motorable road to Sumari from Srinagar - one via Khola and Kwisyu and second via Khanda. From Pauri it is approachable via Khanda. One road from Sumari leads to Nagrasaini temple close via CP Kala's house at Thapul dhar from Palsaryun.

Earlier people walked down to Srinagar via trails, which still exists. This sort cut trail is about 7 km. Long back during the time of Panthya in 1790s, there was one more route to reach Sumari via Shoudu and Junpathyali. This route was scaled a quite steep slopes.

There is a small hally pad at Palsaryun, which is used to land for helicopters.
