The Agra fort is of great historical importance, so don't miss out on it. Whereas it had strict military functions in the early days, it became the palace where great decisions considering the city's future were made and the residence of Shah Jahan. Many courtyards, lady bazar, hall of public hearing, the Moti Masjid mosque and a good view of the Taj Mahal. 
 

address: Bank of Yamuna River
