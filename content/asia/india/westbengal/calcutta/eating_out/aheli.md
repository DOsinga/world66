One of Kolkata's best spots for traditional Bengali cuisine, set inside the Peerless Inn with an ambiance that evokes old-world Bengal. The paturi (fish steamed in banana leaf) and luchi (deep-fried puffed bread) are the dishes to order. Service is warm and unhurried, and the restaurant draws more locals than tourists, which is always a good sign.
