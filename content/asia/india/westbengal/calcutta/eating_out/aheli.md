Aheli at the Peerless Inn is one of the most reliable options for authentic Bengali food in a hotel setting. Signature dishes include paturi (fish marinated in mustard and steamed in banana leaf), luchi with alur tarkari, and prawn malaikari. The ambiance has a genuine Bengali hospitality feel rather than the generic hotel dining room style. A good choice if you're staying in the Chowringhee area and want proper Bengali cooking without venturing far.
