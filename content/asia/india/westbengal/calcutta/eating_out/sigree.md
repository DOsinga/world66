Situated on the Bypass near the ITC Sonar Bangla, Sigree specialises in food cooked over charcoal — the sigree is a traditional iron vessel used for slow cooking. The Sikandari Raan (slow-cooked whole leg of lamb) is the house speciality and worth ordering ahead. The interiors are designed around the concept of the charcoal fire, with an atmosphere that feels more Lahore than Kolkata. Good for a special evening meal if you're in the south-east of the city.
