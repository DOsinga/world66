Modeled after a British pub, Someplace Else is widely regarded as Kolkata's best live music venue. Located inside The Park Hotel on Park Street, it draws a loyal crowd with a rotating lineup of bands playing everything from rock to Bollywood covers. The vibe is relaxed and welcoming to visitors. Happy hour runs until about 8 pm, so arrive early for cheaper drinks.
