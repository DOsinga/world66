---
title: 'When to Go'
type: section
---

The best time to visit Kolkata is between November and February, when daytime temperatures are warm but manageable and nights are pleasantly cool. December and January are ideal -- little rain, plenty of sunshine, and comfortable walking weather.

From April through June the heat becomes fierce and sightseeing during the day is exhausting. The monsoon arrives in June and lasts through September, bringing heavy rains that regularly flood streets and snarl traffic. October can still be steamy, but it also brings **Durga Puja**, the city's biggest and most spectacular festival. If you can handle the heat, timing your visit for Durga Puja (usually late September or October) is an unforgettable experience -- the streets come alive with elaborate pandals, music, and enormous crowds dressed in their finest.
