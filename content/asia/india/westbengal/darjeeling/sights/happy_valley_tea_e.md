Happy Valley Tea Estate is a tea estate in Darjeeling district in the Indian state of West Bengal.

The estate was established in 1854.

Happy Valley Tea Estate offers a magnificent view of the Mt. Kanchenjunga, provided the day is sunny and you are lucky. The estate stretches to many kilometers and you can hardly see its end. The estate produces world class Darjeeling Tea. It is one of the oldest and most renowned tea estate in Darjeeling. Tea picking, manufacturing and processing of tea can be seen here. Do not forget to buy a pack of tea from here on your next visit to this lovely estate.It's the nearest tea estate that you can visit frm the main darjeeling town.
