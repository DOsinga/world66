---
title: "Practical Information in Durgapur"
type: section
---

Durgapur is a planned industrial city, comfortable and functional, with better infrastructure than most West Bengal cities outside Kolkata.

**Getting there:** Frequent trains from Howrah and Sealdah in Kolkata. The Shatabdi Express (6:05am departure) is the fastest, reaching Durgapur non-stop in about 1 hour 45 minutes. The Durgapur Expressway (NH2) from Kolkata takes under 2 hours by car at off-peak times.

**Getting around:** Shared minibuses, auto-rickshaws, and taxis cover most of the city. The city is large in area — don't plan to walk between neighbourhoods. Car hire with driver (around Rs 50/hr) is the most practical option for visitors.

**Money:** ATMs are plentiful in City Center and Benachity.

**When to go:** October to March is most comfortable. April–June is hot. The monsoon months (July–September) are rainy.
