*Asia > India > West Bengal > Shankarpur*

Shankarpur is a small fishing village and emerging beach resort 14 km east of Digha on the Bay of Bengal coast. It has most of what Digha offers — wide beach, calm sea, casuarina groves — without the weekend crowds. The beach here is largely unspoiled and the sea quiet enough for safe swimming. A functioning fishing harbour adds activity in the early mornings when the boats come in.

Development is gradual; accommodation is mostly simple lodges and a few mid-range beach resorts. There is no town centre worth speaking of. It is a place to come for a quiet couple of days at the sea rather than as a base for exploration.

From Shankarpur you can reach [Digha](/asia/india/westbengal/digha) in about 20 minutes by road and [Chandaneshwar](/asia/india/westbengal/chandaneshwar) with its Shiva temple across the Odisha border.
