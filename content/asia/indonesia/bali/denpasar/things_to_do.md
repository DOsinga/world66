---
title: "Things to do in Denpasar"
type: section
---

The long list of **Denpasar Things to Do** makes the capital of
Bali a must-visit for travelers looking for some variety in their
vacations. Denpasar, apart from being the most important city of the
country, holds a lot of attractions and natural beauty that is sure to
impress the visitors. The popular Ngurah Rai Airport of Bali is located
here which makes this place a gateway to the country. You can go out for
the local sightseeing tour or indulge in shopping during the sojourn at
Denpasar. Most of the tourist attractions of Denpasar are located at
the Gajah Mada Street. Foodies will love the idea to taste around the
local delicacies of Indonesia at the finest restaurants of Denpasar.

**Pura Masopahit**

It is an ancient temple located at the heart of Denpasar, and Candi
Rengat and Candi Kusuma are two memorable shrines in this temple. They
are the gateways which separate the outer and inner sanctums of this
temple. It is worth a visit during your Denpasar sojourn.

**Sightseeing at Kuta**

The town of Kuta is a must-visit for tourists planning a trip to
Denpasar. It is close to the famous Ngurah Rai Airport. You can find a
number of luxury hotels, lavish shopping arcades and posh restaurants at
this place. Most of the travelers love to spend time shopping, dining
or simply strolling across the roads of Kuta,

**Beach Activities**

You will cherish the visit to Denpasar for the lovely beaches it
features. The sun-kissed beaches filled with white fine sand have earned
worldwide acclaim for their gorgeous beauty. Let your spirit flow with
the interesting beach activities. Surfing, swimming and sun-bathing are a
few of the commonest activities at the beaches. Some of the
most-coveted beaches known for surfing are Canggu Beach, Legian Beach
and Kuta Beach. The blue waters stretch up to horizons and will
mesmerize you with their vast azure color.

**Shopping at Denpasar**

Shopaholics will find Denpasar a great destination indeed. If you
adore the handcrafted items, Denpasar is the right place to buy them.
The splendid Balinese handicrafts are a major shopping item here. The
local shops house beautiful pottery, textile fabrics, silver jewelry and
utensils, artworks and many more. Denpasar is famous for the Batik
clothes. It is a local made item that comes in a wide variety of colors
and materials. They are brightly colored and made from pure cotton. You
can get them at a cheap rate if you know how to bargain!

**Jewelry District**

Do not forget to visit the Jewelry District of Celuk at Denpasar. It
is just a 30-minute drive from Kuta. There are rows of goldsmith and
silversmith shops selling great ornaments at a low prices.
