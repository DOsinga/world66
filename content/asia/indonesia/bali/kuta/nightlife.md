It would probably be a bit over the edge, saying that there are as many bars and pubs in the Kuta area as there are grains of sand on the Kuta beach. However, the difference won't be that big. Especially along Jalan Legian and Jalan Raya Seminyak is a high concentration of anything that will keep you out of your bed until the sun rises again. After your tiring day of shopping, sunbathing and swimming, you can always relax with a refreshing cool drink at one of the beach clubs. Afterwards, check out one of the neighbouring clubs or enjoy the beach in the moonlight.
