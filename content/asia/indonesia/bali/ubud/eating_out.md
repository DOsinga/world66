---
title: "Eating Out"
type: section
---

Ubud has a strong restaurant scene for its size, ranging from cheap warungs serving nasi goreng to upscale places with valley views. Jalan Raya and Jalan Monkey Forest have the highest concentration. Bebek Bengil on Jalan Padang Tegal is famous for its crispy duck, and Murni's Warung at the Campuhan Bridge has been a fixture for decades.
