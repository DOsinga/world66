Travel Guide

**Welcome to Puri Rai Hotel Padangbai - Bali**

Puri Rai Hotel Padangbai warmly welcomes you with the comforts of private, intimate and romantic location, natural warmth, and friendly staff. They will make it hard to leave Puri Rai Hotel Padangbai, where comfort, nature cool beauty and tradition combine.

Puri Rai Hotel Padangbai is the only hotel in town with a swimming pool. Puri Rai Hotel Padangbai is a Melati accommodation with 1 star hotel services. Puri Rai Hotel Padangbai invites you to experience and enjoy the thorough magic of Bali in your home away from home at the eastern part of Bali.

**Location**: Puri Rai Hotel Padangbai is located on Jl. Silayukti Padangbai, eastern part of Bali. Padangbai predominantly a fishing village and still has enough tourism aspirations to qualify as a great holiday alternative in Bali. The small town is the main gate for Bali and East Nusa Tenggara connection through ferry boats that criss cross Bali strait every 2 hours.
