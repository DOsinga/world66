, taken from Sanskrit language, literally means *monkey* [wenara] *forest* [wana]. A greenery hills with monkeys inhabitat. In there also located 3 temples and temporary cemetery for Balinese before these dead bodies get *Ngaben *[crematory] by their families. Since Ngaben take lots of money and the people who left behind need to save it first before arrange a special ceremony called Ngaben.
