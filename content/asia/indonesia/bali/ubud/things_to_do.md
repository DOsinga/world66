---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Ubud's main draws are its dance performances, museums, and the surrounding countryside. The Royal Palace stages traditional Balinese dance most evenings — the Legong and Barong dances are particularly striking. Shadow puppet performances are also worth seeking out. Four museums display Balinese painting and sculpture spanning centuries, with Museum Neka the standout for its range. The Monkey Forest on the south side of town combines three temples with a walk through dense tropical woodland full of long-tailed macaques. Rent a bicycle to explore the craft villages and rice terraces beyond town.
