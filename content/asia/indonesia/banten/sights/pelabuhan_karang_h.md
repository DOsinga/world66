Pelabuhan Karang Hantu — "Ghost Rock Harbor" in English — is a small working fishing port on the coast of Banten province. The harbor offers an unvarnished look at daily life for local fishermen: boats coming and going, catches being sorted and sold on the quayside, nets being mended in the shade. It is not a tourist attraction in any polished sense, but visitors who wander down in the early morning will find the scene genuinely interesting.
