---
title: "Beaches"
type: section
---

Don't expect Bali. Most of Batam's coast is industrial or mangrove, and the strips closest to Nagoya — including the much-touted Waterfront City — are often dirty. The decent beaches are on the northern side around **Nongsa**, where a cluster of resorts (Turi Beach Resort, Batam View, Nongsa Point Marina) maintains private stretches of sand with swimming, parasailing and snorkelling. To the south, the coastline along the **Barelang Highway** has quieter, less developed beaches that you reach by car. Daytrippers from Singapore mostly stick to the northern resorts.
