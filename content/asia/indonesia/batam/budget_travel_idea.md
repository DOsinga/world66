Being a major industrial hub of Indonesia,
Batam has ample options for the budget travelers. This is because besides
tourists most of the visitors here are on regular business trips and they
usually look for budget lodging. So if you are working on a trip to Batam on a tight
budget then here are few tips that can help you have complete fun in this
beautiful island without worrying much about your pocket.

**Getting There**

If you are planning your trip here by air then you can checkout
one of these airlines. Mandala Air, Batavia Air, Sriwijaya Air, Kartika
Airlines, Airasia, Lion Air, Firefly, Garuda and Riau Airlines. Mostly of these
airlines offer discounted tickets if booked well in advance. So all you have to
do is plan early and book your tickets. These flights can be availed from major
cities of Indonesia ,
Malaysia and Singapore.

Amazingly this small island is well connected by ferries
with Singapore
and Johor Bahru in Malaysia.
A ferry trip from Singapore
is just 45 minutes and will cost you merely $25. Penguin Ferry Services has
good reviews and you can get it from World Trade Center Ferry Terminal in Singapore.
However you should avail the ferry services only if you are not short of time.

**Affordable Accommodations **

Most of the hotels in Batam are located in Nagoya.
Most of the budget hotels here are not listed on the internet therefore you
will have no information on them while surfing the net. Therefore after
reaching here it is better to look for it yourself. You can easily find a
budget hotel in Nagoya. This is the
center of the island and has plenty of shopping centers, restaurants, discos,
music lounges perfect for enjoying nightlife. While looking for a budget hotel
there are certain things that you should always bear in mind other than money.
Here are few tips that can help you decide on your budget hotel.

The accommodation should have all the basic amenities that
you require otherwise you will have difficulty adjusting to it. If it is just
lodging that you are opting for then make sure you have good dining places
nearby otherwise it will be hectic for you to catch your meals. And
lastly, never forget that you are here to enjoy. So the location of your hotel
should be at a walking distance from the center of attractions of the city. This
will help you save money on the transportation for sightseeing. And the best
part is you can walk down there whenever you feel like and don’t have to bother
about catching up taxis.

For more information on the accommodation in Batam you can
always checkout [Batam Budget Hotels](/asia/indonesia/batam/accommodation)
