Indonesia isn't a common destination for people with children, but for
those who desire a more exotic vacation than Disneyland, the island of **Batam** offers **family**-friendly **travel** options, if you know where to look.

**Head to the Beach**
What
good is an island vacation if you don't spend at least part of it
soaking up the sun next to the ocean? Bring buckets, sunscreen and a
picnic lunch, and take the kids to one of Batam's world-famous sandy
beaches. Older children and teens can join you for boogie boarding or
surfing, while the younger set can build sandcastles or spend
supervised time in the warmth of the Pacific.

**Rent Bicycles**
Batam
is a small island, and it's easy to navigate on bike, which can be
rented for a small fee at any number of shops. If your children are old
enough, they can have their own set of wheels, while younger kids can
be strapped into child seats on yours. Don't forget helmets!

**Get a Babysitter**
If
you want to experience Batam's active social night scene, you'll need
to leave the kids behind. Many hotels and resorts offer childcare, or
can refer you to services that do, or you can bring along a responsible
teenaged family member willing to trade an exotic vacation for
providing au pair services.

**Visit an open-air Market**
Colors,
sounds, smells, foods, people, souveniers and a learning experience:
visit one of Batam's many open-air markets, and you'll get all that and
more. Forgo lunch at a restaurant and dine instead on local fruits and
breads.

**Do what the Locals Do**
It's an easy concept to
forget when traveling, but keep in mind that anywhere you go, people
live there. And where people live, people raise children. Ask your
concierge what Batanese parents do when they want to do something
special with their children and try that.

**Take a Daytrip**
If
you want to see and experience other areas of Indonesia and its
neighboring countries, the local geography makes that easy. Ferries
take travelers to the other Riau Islands, Singapore, Sumatra, and other
ports. Inexpensive plane tickets to Jakarta are also available.

**Go West, Adventurous Family**
Located
on Batam's western side, Waterfront City offers a flying fox, a cable
ski park, a kart racetrack and other attractions that are likely to
appeal to even the surliest teenagers.
