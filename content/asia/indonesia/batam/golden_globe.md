Known to locals as GG (or "GeeGee"), this is the largest two-storey pub in Nagoya. Live bands — most of them brought over from Bandung — alternate with DJs running everything from R&B to house. Reliably busy most nights and one of the more energetic spots in the district, with VIP karaoke rooms upstairs.
