---
title: "Shopping"
type: section
---

Batam is a Free Trade Zone, which keeps prices on imported goods, electronics and clothes well below Singapore — one of the main reasons day-trippers come over. Most of the action is in **Nagoya**: **Nagoya Hill Mall** is the biggest and busiest, **DC Mall** trades on cheap branded clothing, and **Lucky Plaza** is the place for electronics, CDs and DVDs (always bargain). For something less polished, the **Robinson & Ramayana** mall is where locals shop and a good place for inexpensive Indonesian clothing. Outside Nagoya, **Panbil Mall** in Muka Kuning serves the industrial estates and has the most authentic local prices.
