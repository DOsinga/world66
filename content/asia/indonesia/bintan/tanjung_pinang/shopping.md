---
title: "Shopping in Tanjung Pinang"
type: section
---

**Tanjung Pinang shopping **is a lively and fun with many great deals to
be had. When you travel to the largest city and shopping hub for the
entire Bintan island, you can be sure to pick up some great handicrafts,
as well as other necessities for low prices. Shop around and explore
the commerce of this city while visiting to purchase some great items
that will remind you of your time here for years to come.

**

Tanjung Pinang Souvenirs **

The most famous handicrafts made in this area are wooden handmade items
and textiles with stunning Indonesian designs. Be sure to shop around
before you buy anything and bargain over the prices to ensure you are
getting the best deal. Many items may initially look the same, but upon
further inspection, some are obviously better made than others. Be sure
to check around at several places before making a purchase, especially
if it is a large, expensive one.

Tea and coffee are both popular souvenirs from a trip to Tanjung Pinang.
Teh Prendjak by PT. Panca Rasa Pratama is a popular brand of tea that
many tourists like to purchase as a gift. Its unique flavor is
refreshing and it is hard to find this brand outside of the area, making
it a great gift for tea lovers at home.

Kaya, a spread made from egg and coconut, is another specialty of the
area and makes a great gift for those visitors who enjoy its
hyper-sweet taste.

**Tanjung Pinang Merchandise**

As in much of Southeast Asia, pirated merchandise such as DVDs and music
CDs are widely available throughout the city. If you are buying these
goods, be sure to test them before buying, as the quality will vary
greatly in some cases.

There is a large electronics market in Tanjung Pinang. Most electronics
are at very nice prices, often with a saving of 10 percent or more over
European prices. A note of warning is to be sure to check the voltage of
the products that you are buying with that of your home country before you buy to
ensure that your purchase will work at home. If you do not
have the same voltage in your country, in most cases converters are
available to allow you to continue to use your purchase at home.

Be
sure to spend some times shopping while in Tanjung Pinang so that you
have some great items from the area to remember your trip with.
