Tip for budget minded travelers: We are now staying in Green Garden B&B in Yogyakarta and as we travel on a tight budget this place is right for us because apart from the included breakfast, all coffee and tea, mineral water and (fast) internet is all included in the room rate. And because we stayed 3 nights they even picked us up from the airport for free!
