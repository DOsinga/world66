Travel Guide

Irian Jaya has, again, changed its name. The Province is since 2001 now called Papua, with Jayapura as its capital. In 2003 a new province, Irian Jaya Barat, was split from Papua with as its capital Manokwari. This province has recently been renamed Papua Barat.
