Jakarta is considered the “melting pot” of Indonesia, and is a great place to visit. This **Jakarta Itinerary** will
help you organize your trip if you do decide to venture into Jakarta

**Day 1 - Jakarta History Museum
**

The
Jakarta History Museum, known as **Taman Fatahillah,** is located in Old Jakarta. This museum displays relics from many stages of Jakarta's history, including prehistoric times, the city's first founding 1527, its colonization by the Dutch in the 16th century, andm finally, Indonesia’s independence in 1945.

**Day 2 - Marine Tourism**

The best way to enjoy the mystical archipelago of Indonesia is
through its beautiful and crystal clear waters. You can choose from
these wonderful marine tourism destinations all around Jakarta: Thousand
Islands Marine Resort, or the islands of Bidadari, Ayer, Matahari,
Putri, Pelangi, Sepa, Kotok, Bira Besar and Untung Jawa.

**Day 3 - Art and Culture**

It’s nice to explore and discover the culture of the place you're
visiting. You can set aside a whole day to visit the following art and culture
centers: Setu Babakan, Jakarta’s Art Center, the International, Usmar
Ismail Hall, the** **Sekar Gallery and the Twilight
Orchestra Jakarta.
