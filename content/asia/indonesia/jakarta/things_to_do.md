---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Jakarta's sights are spread across a large city, but the two most rewarding clusters are the colonial old city of Kota in the north and the monumental civic precinct around Merdeka Square in the centre. Kota rewards a half-day of wandering; the Merdeka area holds the best national museums and is anchored by the National Monument. Allow a full day to cover both.
