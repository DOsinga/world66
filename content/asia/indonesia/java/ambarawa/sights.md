### [Rawa Pening Lake](/asia/indonesia/java/ambarawa/sights/rawa_pening_lake)

Rawa Pening lake is a good object for those who like photography. Sunrise comes out from the other side of the lake and can be seen a simple daily activity from the locals. Like fishermen going out with their boats to the heart of the lake to catch the fish.

Believe it or not, the hills where visitors can see the sunrise named as *Bukit Cinta* [Love Hills].

type: Parks, Gardens and Zoos

### [Ambarawa Train Museum](/asia/indonesia/java/ambarawa/sights/ambarawa_train_mus)

Here can be found old steam locomotives and carriages which remains memories nowadays. Beside the locos and carriages, also available old equipments that used in the station with formerly named *Koening Willem I* [opening 1873]. The visitors can try nostalgic trip using wooden carriages and old loco made-in 1907 enroute ambarawa-ngampin-bedono-jambu vice versa, passing through beautiful landscape.

type: Memorials



openingHours: 08 am - 05 pm
