Old steam locomotives and carriages from the colonial era are on display at this station, formerly known as *Koening Willem I* (opened 1873). Visitors can take a nostalgic ride in wooden carriages pulled by a 1907 locomotive along the scenic Ambarawa-Bedono route through beautiful mountain landscape.
