title: 'Rawa Pening Lake'
type: poi
category: 'Sight'
address: 'Rawa Pening, Ambarawa'
latitude: -7.2878

---

A scenic lake popular with photographers. Sunrise is the best time to visit -- the light comes up over the far shore as fishermen head out in their boats. The hills overlooking the lake are known locally as *Bukit Cinta* (Love Hills).
