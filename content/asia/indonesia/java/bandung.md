Indonesia's third-largest city and capital of West Java, Bandung sits at 750 meters elevation and has noticeably cooler air than [Jakarta](/asia/indonesia/java/jakarta). The Dutch colonials used it as a summer retreat, and they left behind some of Java's finest colonial architecture. This is Sundanese country, culturally distinct from the Javanese heartland to the east.

Bandung is a bustling city and high-tech center with good universities, a lively cafe culture, and factory outlet shopping. The main draw for visitors is the volcanic landscape nearby -- Tangkuban Perahu, an active volcano with accessible craters, is an easy half-day trip from the city.
