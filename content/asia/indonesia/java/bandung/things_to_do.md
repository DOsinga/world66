---
title: 'Things to Do'
type: section
---

Bandung's main sights divide neatly between its Art Deco colonial heritage in the city centre and the volcanic landscape on its northern and southern fringes. The Gedung Sate and its surrounding civic buildings are a short walk from Braga Street, the old colonial promenade now lined with cafes and boutiques. Geology and history sit side by side at the Geological Museum, which explains why the city sits where it does — inside an ancient caldera.
