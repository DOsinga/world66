A satellite city between [Jakarta](/asia/indonesia/java/jakarta) and [Bogor](/asia/indonesia/java/bogor), Depok is mainly known as the home of the University of Indonesia. Most travelers pass through on the commuter train between the capital and Bogor's Botanical Gardens.
