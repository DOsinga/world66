One of Kemang's most popular hangouts for expatriates and Indonesians alike. The atmospheric front bar has a darts corner and 9ft billiards table, plus free Wi-Fi. The restaurant serves British and Indian curries -- the Chicken Tikka Masala is a must -- along with fresh homemade meat pies and full breakfasts. The beer garden out back is one of the few enjoyable outdoor terraces in Jakarta, with live jazz every Wednesday and rock bands on Friday and Saturday.
