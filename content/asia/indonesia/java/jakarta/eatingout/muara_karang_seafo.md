While enjoy a day trip to the old harbour Sunda Kelapa, visit Muara Karang Seafood corners will be nice and becomes new experience.

Don't think the guests will have a seat and order, but more than that. Please go to the fresh market nearby and choose your own seafood fave. From squid to prawn, from red snapper to crabs and others.

Then bring all of your fave seafood to the one of the restaurants there and start order; grilled or a'la Chinese seafood is up to you. They'll served with hot steam rice and manykind of sambal [chili sauce]. From sambal belacan to mango sambal. Plus some fresh veggies.
