Various Indonesian Foods. But its specialization is Satay. Chicken satay or lamb satay. With peanut sauce or red onioned soybean sauce. Also try the "nasi goreng" or fried rice and "tahu telur" or tofu egg. Very nice place, good quality of food and cleanliness. Located in several place. One of them is in Menteng. Close to Keris Gallery. All price in Rupiah.
