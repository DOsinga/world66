A large upscale mall on Jalan Thamrin in central Jakarta, connected to the Grand Hyatt and Plaza Indonesia by a skybridge. The ground floors are high-end retail; upper levels have a cinema, gym, and event spaces that host live music. On weekends it stays open until 3am, making it one of the few places in the city where you can shop, eat, and catch a show well past midnight.
