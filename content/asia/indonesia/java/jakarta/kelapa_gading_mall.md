One of the biggest mall complexes in Jakarta, located in the upscale Kelapa Gading area in north Jakarta. Four interconnected buildings with branded shops, three cinemas, a supermarket with imported goods, and endless food options. Nearby Gading Food City has a huge range of Chinese, Indonesian, and Western restaurants. About 45 minutes from Soekarno-Hatta Airport.
