Near the old harbour of Sunda Kelapa, this cluster of seafood restaurants works differently from most. You visit the fresh market nearby and choose your own seafood -- squid, prawns, red snapper, crabs -- then bring it to one of the restaurants to be cooked however you like: grilled or Chinese-style, served with steamed rice, various sambal (chili sauces), and fresh vegetables.
