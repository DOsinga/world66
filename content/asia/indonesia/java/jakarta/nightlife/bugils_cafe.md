BuGils cafe is located centrally in the Taman Ria Senayan Complex, close to TJI Friday's and Bebek Bali. This Dutch style "brown cafe" mostly caters for expatriates. The restaurant is managed by KOI and the Eurasian menu features Dutch snacks as well. Every Saturday there is live music through a classy jazz / blues style piano player. Every Monday is singles night with happy hour beers for all and free margaritas for ladies. Ladies drink for free (beer, wine, house pourings) all night long on Tuesday.
