A top-end boutique restaurant in a former house of the famous Indonesian painter Raden Saleh. Several of his masterpieces hang on the walls alongside wooden statues and old ceramic porcelain. The rijstaffel, served by twelve maids bringing platters of delicacies to your table, is the must-order experience -- though they require a minimum of three guests. Live Batak folk songs accompany the meal.
