In the heart of busy Jakarta, this green park offers a welcome respite. A small fountain sits among statues created by ASEAN artists. The surrounding Menteng neighborhood, known as the lungs of Jakarta, still has several old Dutch buildings from the area once called Burgemeester Bishopplein.
