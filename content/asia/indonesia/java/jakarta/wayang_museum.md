Housed in a building originally constructed as a warehouse in 1908, the Wayang Museum contains a collection of various kinds of wayang (shadow puppets) from across Indonesia and beyond. Open Sunday, Tuesday to Thursday 9:00-15:00, Friday 9:00-11:30, Saturday 9:00-13:00.
