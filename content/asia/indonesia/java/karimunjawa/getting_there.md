---
title: 'Getting There'
type: section
---

There are two entry points to Karimunjawa. From **[Semarang](/asia/indonesia/java/semarang)** (Tanjung Mas harbour), a fast boat takes about 3 hours. From **Jepara** (Pantai Kartini harbour), the slower ferry takes about 5 hours. Schedules change without notice, so check in advance before you travel. Fast boats are more expensive but much more comfortable.
