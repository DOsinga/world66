A wooden boardwalk threads through a dense mangrove forest on the southern edge of the main island. The walk takes around thirty minutes at a gentle pace and passes through stands of mature mangroves — good habitat for kingfishers and the occasional monitor lizard. It is shaded and relatively cool even in the middle of the day.
