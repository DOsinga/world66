A highland city in East Java that offers cooler temperatures and a more relaxed pace than [Surabaya](/asia/indonesia/java/surabaya), about two hours to the north. Malang has attractive Dutch colonial architecture, a compact center good for walking, and serves as the gateway to some of East Java's best volcanic trekking including Mount Semeru and Mount Bromo via the southern approach.
