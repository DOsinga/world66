[Salatiga](/asia/indonesia/java/salatiga) is located about 40 kms south of [Semarang](/asia/indonesia/java/semarang) to [Solo](/asia/indonesia/java/solo) under Merbabu mountain. A city of students and retirement, it has been known since the Dutch occupation era to be a city of relaxation because of its cool relaxing temperature. About 40% of its 150,000 residences are students of three private universities in the city.
