---
title: 'Eating Out'
type: section
---

Semarang's food scene revolves around Simpang Lima, the busy central square. **Shabu Shabu House** on Gajah Mada Street is an upscale Japanese-style shabu-shabu restaurant with house-made goma and ponzu sauces (Ruko Murni Block C & D, Gajah Mada Street 144, tel: +62 24 3584020). For local food, Semarang is known for its lumpia (spring rolls) and tahu gimbal (tofu with shrimp fritters).
