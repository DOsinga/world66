**Shabu Shabu House** is an upscale restaurant in a relaxed setting. We pride ourself on the proprietary "*Goma*" and "*Ponzu*" sauces. Our chef, Mr Taka, has perfected those sauces through a long period of time while he was a chef in "Shabu Shabu House" and "California Shabu" in California. S2H is situated on Ruko Murni Block C & D, Gajah Mada Street 144, near "Simpang Lima" the heart of Semarang. For reservation call: **+62 24 3584020**.
