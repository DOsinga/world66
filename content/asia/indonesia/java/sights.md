Volcano Viewing
Nature Lovers Discover One of Java’s Best Keep Secrets.

1. Mount Merapi (2968 meter) Sleman Regency, one of the Indonesia active and dangerous volcanoes,tours range from a few hours walk to observation points to pre-sunrise climbs to the summit (conditions permitting) to see lava flows. Observation points at Jurang Boyong, Plawangan, Kaliadem and Babadan. At Jurang Boyong, there are trails for mountain climbers, sunrise treks for Merapi lava-watching also begin from Ratu Boko of the ruins of King Boko palace with MountSindoro, Sumbing and Lawu as backdrops.
2. Mount Merbabu (3150 meter), South of Solo, breathtaking views of MountMerbabu and Merapi on Selo pass, is an excellent route to Dieng Plateau from Solo.
3. Mount Sindoro (3225 meter) and Mount Sumbing, Magelang, Kledung pass provide awesome views, as the road to and from Dieng Plateau passes between the two volcano
And, many more.
