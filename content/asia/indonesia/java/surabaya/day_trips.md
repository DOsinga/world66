title: 'Day Trips'
type: section
---

Surabaya is a good base for exploring East Java. All destinations below are reachable by bus from Bungurasih terminal or by train from Gubeng Station.

**Madura** is the closest excursion -- a ferry ride across the strait from Tanjungperak. The Jalesveva Jayamahe Monument at the naval base is visible from the boat. **[Malang](/asia/indonesia/java/malang)** is a pleasant retreat from Surabaya's heat, with colonial-era architecture, Hindu temples, and the hill resort of Selecta. **Sidoarjo** is the place for East Javanese street food and cheap leather goods. **Pasuruan** and **Probolinggo** are the entry points for Bromo-Tengger-Semeru National Park and the spectacular Madakaripura waterfall.
