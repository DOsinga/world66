---
title: "Getting Around"
type: section
---

Unfortunately for getting around in Surabaya is not very easy. That's all just because of many Surabayans are not speak English. Basically the easiest way to getting around the city is by taking a taxi. Beware, otherwise you may getting ripped off. Other mode of public transportations that currently available in Surabaya are Bemo (minibus), becak (ricksaw), public bus (not recommended), and train (lack of route).
