A must-see on any trip to Java. Located about 42km from Yogyakarta, [Borobudur](/asia/indonesia/java/borobudur) is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and the world's largest Buddhist monument. The name derives from an expression meaning 'Mountain of accumulation of merits of the ten states of Bodhisattva.' Though commonly thought of as Buddhist, its initial construction was planned and executed by Hindu builders around 775 AD. Allow a full morning for the visit.
