title: Taru Martani Cigar Factory

Java is famous for cigar production, and Taru Martani has been making them since 1918. Factory visits can be arranged on weekdays (Monday to Friday, 8am-2pm) by appointment with the management. A guide walks you through the 2-hectare facility explaining the production process. The factory produces 14 brands including the legendary Senator and Mundi Victor labels. Free admission.
