Kesuma Restaurant

Gang Sartono (Jalan Parangtritis - Opposite side Market Prawirotaman) | MJ III - 827, [Yogyakarta](/asia/indonesia/java/yogyakarta) 55143, ☎ +62 274 8245027. A cozy and pleasant place where you can find an excellent local home cooking. The menu is exclusively native of Yogya or Solo. There is a small garden where guests can settle in a Gasebo and the main room is in a Limassan, traditional Javanese wooden house. 6PM-11PM. Rp 20,000-50,000. Lunch only available for Reservations.
