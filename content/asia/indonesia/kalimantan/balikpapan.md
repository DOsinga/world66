Balikpapan is East Kalimantan's main port and air hub, a prosperous oil city with better infrastructure than most of the island. Most travelers pass through on the way to [Samarinda](/asia/indonesia/kalimantan/samarinda) and the Mahakam River, but the city itself is a comfortable place to rest and restock before heading into the interior.
