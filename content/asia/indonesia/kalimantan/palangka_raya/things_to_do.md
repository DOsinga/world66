---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

The main reason to come to Palangka Raya is the natural environment. The Sebangau National Park, a vast expanse of peat swamp forest and black-water rivers, begins just south of the city and is one of the best places in Borneo to see wild orangutans. The Kahayan River running through town is also worth exploring by klotok (traditional motorised canoe).
