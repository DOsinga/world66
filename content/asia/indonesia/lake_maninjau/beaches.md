These are not real beaches, but some of places to stay in Maninjau make artificial beaches using white sand and maintenance them, added with kept well garden and coconut trees. So it feels like a freshwater beach.

One of the bungalows that have this beach is Arlen's Bungalow at Bayur. marker: the signboard Arlen's on the left side of the road [from direction Jalan Haji Udin Rahmani at Maninjau to direction Bayur].
