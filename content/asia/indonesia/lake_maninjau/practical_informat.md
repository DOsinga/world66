title: "Practical Information in Lake Maninjau"
type: section
---

There are several accommodation type in Maninjau. From big hotel into budget-backpacker types. But by the time writing, places around Maninjau seems quite noisy. Better to go further away, about 2 km to Bayur. Passing through the rice [padi] fields and you'll find the tranquility of Lake Maninjau.
