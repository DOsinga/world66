### [Jujuran, a traditional ceremony](/asia/indonesia/loksado/sights/jujuran_a_traditi)

Jujuran is about pre-wedding procession where the sides of bride and groom dealing about the expenses of the wedding. And the intermission of this event is dancing, singing and playing music with *in-trance* conditions. Held in communal house.
