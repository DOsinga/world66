Travel Guide

**Cakranegara** or shorted as Cakra is the part city of Mataram [the provincional city of Nusa Tenggara Barat] that got Balinese influences. This condition can be tracking down since Cakra was the place where King of Karangasem [from Bali] took rules in the past centuries.

Here can be found several monasteries and monuments which reflecting the power of King Karangasem. Such as *Pura Meru* [Meru monastery, built 1720], *Mayura High Court and Water Castle* and *Narmada Water Park* as miniature of the sacred mountain Rinjani. Said that .... once you take the water for drink or washing your face, you'll be look forever young!
