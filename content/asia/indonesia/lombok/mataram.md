Mataram is the capital of [Lombok](/asia/indonesia/lombok), though in practice it is four towns that have merged into one sprawling conurbation: Ampenan, Mataram, Cakranegara (Cakra) and Sweta. Each has its own character.

Ampenan, the old port district, has most of the town's sights, including the Negeri Nusa Tenggara Barat museum, which gives a good overview of the culture and history of Lombok. Cakra is the liveliest area — the best place for eating, shopping and accommodation. It is also home to the Water Palace and the Pura Meru, Lombok's largest Hindu temple. Mataram proper is the administrative centre, and Sweta is the island's main transport hub.
