Tucked away in remote South-East Maluku, the Kei Islands are famous for having the very finest unspoilt white-sand beaches in all Indonesia. There is limited accomodation on Pasir Panjang, the best of these beaches. The regional capital Tual is well connected to the rest of Indonesia via Ambon, but has no attractions apart from its better facilities.
