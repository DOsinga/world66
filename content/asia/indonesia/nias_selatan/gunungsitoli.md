Gunungsitoli is the main town and capital of Nias Island. It suffered badly in the March 2005 earthquake but has since been rebuilt. The town itself holds limited tourist interest, but it serves as the gateway to the island — the airport and main ferry terminal are here. From Gunungsitoli, roads head south to the surf beaches and traditional villages of South Nias.
