Travel Guide

It is located on the northwest border of [Yogyakarta Special Region](/wiki/Yogyakarta_Special_Region), between [Yogyakarta](/wiki/Yogyakarta) region and [Magelang](/wiki/Magelang) region. [Mount Merapi](/wiki/Mount_Merapi) is located on the northeastern border of the regency. It also covers some parts of Yogyakarta-[Surakarta](/wiki/Surakarta) inter-city road to the east, thereby having the [Adisucipto International Airport](/wiki/Adisucipto_International_Airport) within its boundaries.
