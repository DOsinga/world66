Manado is the capital of North Sulawesi and the launching point for Bunaken National Marine Park, one of the richest coral reef systems on earth. Most visitors come to dive — the underwater walls off Bunaken drop hundreds of metres and teem with turtles, sharks and an astounding variety of reef fish. The city itself is busy and unpretentious, with a predominantly Christian Minahasa population and a cuisine that is unapologetically hot and adventurous.
