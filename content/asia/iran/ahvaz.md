Ahvaz is one of the most boring places in Iran. Located in the south near the Gulf it is mainly a commercial city. It was heavily bombed during the Iran-Iraq War and reconstruction has not made the place more attractive. 
 
 

The main reason to come here is the city's proximity to several historic sites: Choga Zambil (Elamite ruins and well-preserved ziggerat), Haft Tappe (ruins) and Shush.
