Travel Guide

Besnegerd is a village in
mountainous areas of Iran, 75 kilometers to the west of the city of qom and 35
kilometers south of Saveh and 20 kilometers east of Tafresh city.

The climate is freezing
cold in autumn and winter but temperate in spring and summer.

There are a number of
archaeological sites, castles and other historical monuments near the village. Recently, the graves of Iron Age around 3000
BC have been found in this village.

Beautiful natural
sceneries and unique style of architecture have turned Besnegerd into a cozy
tourist spot.
