The obvious day trip from Shiraz is [Persepolis](/asia/iran/persepolis), the ceremonial capital of the Achaemenid Empire, about 60 kilometres to the northeast. It is one of the great archaeological sites of the ancient world — a vast complex of terraced palaces, bas-relief friezes, and monumental staircases. Taxis from Shiraz will take you there and back for a negotiated price; the drive takes about an hour.
