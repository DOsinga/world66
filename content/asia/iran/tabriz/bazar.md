Tabriz Bazaar

 Strolling in the center of Tabriz, one is reminded very forcibly that it is a commercial city; one cannot miss its very large and 15th-century covered bazaar.

 It is already much diminished in its variety of goods, but still a great place for getting hopelessly lost amid its dusty architectural splendors. Its architectural style, numerous carvanserais, mosques , and schools have added further beauty and glory to this complex.
