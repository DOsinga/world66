title: "Festivals in Tehran"
type: section
---

Planning your trip to Tebran when there are events to see, including** Tehran festivals**, is a good way to learn more about the culture and to have fun. You can choose the best time to go for the festival you would like to see.

#### Tehran Book Fair

The Tehran Book Fair is held in June and is known to be one of the largest publishing events held in Asia and the Middle East. Publishers can go to the fair and sell their new books, and those attending the festival can see some of the newest books to be released and get to see some of the authors from all over the world.

#### Tehran Puppet Theatre Festival

This festival is held in July and is a great place to see some of the best puppeteers in the area and also enjoy the different performances which take place during the festival. You can see the arts center and all of the performers who take their best puppets and shows to the stage during the fun festival. If you are a fan of watching different puppet shows, this is one of the Tehran festivals you may want to attend.

#### Tehran Short Film Festival

The Tehran Short Film Festival is held in November and is a fun way to see some of the new film makers show their first movies. It is also a place to see some of the best short films that are made and meet the directors who make them. The event has plenty of shows to attend during the day.
