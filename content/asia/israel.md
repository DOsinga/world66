Israel is a treat for travelers. The country has beautiful old cities, good beaches and more culture, history and holy places than any other country.

 Israel is bordered by Egypt in the south, Lebanon in the North and Jordan and Syria in the East.

 Jerusalem will be high on any list of favourite destinations and with good reason: in the old city there is a church or convent on every corner, there are beautiful mosques and many other holy places. Tel Aviv is the place to party. It has good beaches and excellent nightlife, probably the best to be found within a circle of a few thousand kilometers.

 The north of Israel is worth to explore: the lake of Tiberias, Nazareth, the Israeli admistered Golan Heights and the towns of Acre (aka Akko) and Haifa are the most important stops on your itinerary to the North.

 The south offers the enormous Negev desert and the Dead Sea. In the southernmost point of Israel, the beach resort of Eilat is a touristic hot spot.

  Within the state of Israel you will find the [Palestinian Authority](/asia/palestinianauthority) which has some form of autonomy. Cities like [Nablus](/asia/palestinianauthority/nablus), [Jenin](/asia/palestinianauthority/jenin), [Hebron](/asia/palestinianauthority/hebron) (in arabic called al Khalil), [Bethlehem](/asia/palestinianauthority/bethlehem) and [Ramallah](/asia/palestinianauthority/ramallah) are dealt with in the guide about the Palestinian Authority.
