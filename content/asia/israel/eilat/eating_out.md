---
title: "Eating Out"
type: section
---

Eilat's restaurant scene leans toward the tourist-friendly end — seafood, steakhouses, and Asian restaurants dominate the promenade and hotel strips. The city is duty-free, which keeps prices lower than the rest of Israel, though seafront locations still charge a premium. A few good options have persisted across the years: the marina area has solid kosher choices, while the Coral Beach strip further south has quieter dining away from the main crowds.
