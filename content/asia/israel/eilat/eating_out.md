### [Ginger](/asia/israel/eilat/eating_out/ginger)

Completely renovated and very modern - it's a very classy restaurant with a serene atmosphere. I noticed that my Mai Thai (the restaurant once in this place) favorites were still on the menu along with a lot of new dishes.

We booked by telephone, carefully confirming it was Ginger, formerly My Thai, for the same evening for four at 7p.m. Arrived and were told the booking was 'lost' and there were no free tables. There were empty tables but we were told they were booked from7:30p.m. so we left.

Very high quality service and food. No probs booking, returned [more..](/asia/israel/eilat/eating_out/ginger)
