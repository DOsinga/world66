---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Eilat's main attractions cluster around the water and the desert. The coral reefs along the southern beaches are the city's best asset — snorkeling and diving here is world-class, with visibility often exceeding 20 meters. On land, the Eilat Mountains nature reserve offers desert hiking and jeep tracks through dramatic sandstone terrain, best explored on a half-day or full-day guided hike rather than the short tourist loop drives.
