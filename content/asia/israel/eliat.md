Eilat is the place to come to swim, sun and tan. Except for the beaches, the area around Eilat doesn't offer a lot to do and see. You can sit on a camel, snorkel etc, but for a cultural holiday you need to go north to Jersusalem or [Acre](/asia/israel/acre).

At night there are quite a few things going on in Eilat. The nightlife is lively and there are many disco's and bars where you can meet fellow travelers.
