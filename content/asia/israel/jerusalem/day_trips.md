---
title: "Day Trips"
type: section
---

**Bethlehem** is ten minutes south across the West Bank checkpoint — the Church of the Nativity and Banksy's "Walled Off Hotel" are both here. **Jericho**, the world's oldest continuously inhabited city, is 40 minutes east across the Judean Desert. **Masada** and the **Dead Sea** are a long half-day out — float, then climb the Snake Path at sunrise. **Ein Kerem**, a quiet hillside town believed to be the birthplace of John the Baptist, is technically still within Jerusalem and reachable on bus 28; churches, monasteries, cafés. The monastery wineries around **Latrun**, **Deir Rafat** and **Beit Jamal** run free tastings and are a good afternoon with a car.
