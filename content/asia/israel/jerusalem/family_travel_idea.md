The Western Wall**

The most sacred place for the Jewish people, it's also known as the Wailing Wall. This wall is part of a temple that was built in
20 BC. Jewish people place
written wishes in the cracks of the wall and say prayers. It is a popular place
to see a bar or bat mitzvah. Some Jews come to pray here every day. People of all
religions are welcome to visit.
