Christianity's holiest site, marking what tradition identifies as the hill of Crucifixion (Golgotha) and Christ's tomb. Shared, uneasily, by six Christian denominations under a centuries-old Status Quo — a Muslim family has kept the keys since 1187 to stop them fighting over it. Go early or late to avoid the tour groups.
