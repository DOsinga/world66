A Bedouin-run desert camp between Sde Boker and Mitzpe Ramon, built around an authentic community tent of woven black goat hair. The camp offers camel rides, off-road jeep tours, mountain biking, and traditional Bedouin hospitality — tea brewed over a fire, flatbread baked in the embers, and stories told under a sky thick with stars. Even a day visit gives you a taste of Negev Bedouin culture that's hard to find elsewhere in Israel.
