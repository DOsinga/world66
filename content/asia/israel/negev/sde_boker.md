title: Sde Boker and Ben Gurion's Tomb
type: poi

The kibbutz where David Ben Gurion chose to spend his retirement, and where he and his wife Paula are buried on a bluff above the Zin valley. The grave site is simple — two flat stones on a terrace overlooking a vast sweep of desert — and unexpectedly moving. Nearby Midreshet Ben Gurion (also called Midreshet Sde Boker) is a campus housing the Ben Gurion Heritage Institute, the Institute for Desert Research, and the trailhead for Ein Avdat. The field school here can advise on hiking and jeep tours in the area.
