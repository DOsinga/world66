title: "Chan HaShayarot (Caravan Inn)"
type: poi
category: Sight
latitude: 30.6700
longitude: 34.8000
---

A Bedouin-run desert camp between Sde Boker and Mitzpe Ramon, built around an authentic community tent of woven black goat hair. The camp offers camel rides, off-road jeep tours, mountain biking, and traditional Bedouin hospitality. An overnight here — with dinner in the tent and the Negev sky filling with stars — is a genuinely different experience from anything else in Israel.
