Tel Aviv has restaurants in almost every style imaginable — Jewish and Middle Eastern food alongside South American, European, and Asian cuisines. If Israel is a culinary heavyweight, Tel Aviv is where it shows. Falafel stands sit a block away from restaurants that earn serious French-guide recognition.

**Pronto** — The only Italian restaurant officially acknowledged by the Italian embassy. Not kosher, so meat and dairy both appear. Nachmani 26. Tel: 03-566 0915.
