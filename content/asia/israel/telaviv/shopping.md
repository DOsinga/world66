---
title: "Shopping"
type: section
---

Tel Aviv is one of the better cities in the Middle East for fashion and design. Sheinkin Street is the heart of it — boutiques, cafes, and the neighbouring Shuk HaCarmel market, which runs daily on Carmel Street and sells everything from produce to clothing at market prices. Nahalat Binyamin, nearby, is a pedestrian street with art stalls on weekends. For upscale brands, Kikar HaMedina (Medina Circle) is where you'll find the international names. The Azrieli Center and Ramat Aviv mall cover all other shopping needs.
