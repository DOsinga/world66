WAND TANGUnagi or eel is a real Japanese delicacy: to the extent that there are many restaurants which specialise in serving it. Commonly unagi is basted with a rice wine/soy sauce mixture, grilled and then served with rice. Culinary daredevils can also try eel liver soup as a side order.
