title: "Shousuke Don"
type: poi

A well-known watering hole on one of Sapporo's main roads at S4W1, right where the Susukino and Odori areas meet. Shousuke Don is open to the street and always friendly and welcoming. It is a good place to have a drink, watch the neighbourhood go by, and meet locals. Next door is the popular Sinner Cafe. Both have English menus.
