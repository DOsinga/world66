Housed in the atmospheric red-brick buildings of the original Sapporo brewery, this is one of the city's most enjoyable visits. The free self-guided tour walks through the history of beer in Japan, from the Meiji-era founding through to the present day. The real draw is the tasting hall at the end, where you can sample the museum-exclusive Kaitakushi Beer alongside the regular Sapporo range. The adjacent Sapporo Beer Garden serves Genghis Khan (jingisukan) lamb barbecue, a Hokkaido speciality, in a grand brick hall.
