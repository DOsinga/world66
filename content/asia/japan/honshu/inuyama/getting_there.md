---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

Inuyama can be accessed by taking the Meitetsu (private) line to Inuyama-yuen station.

Alternatively, those with JR Rail Passes, can take the train from
Nagoya to Gifu, and then the JR Takayama line train to Unuma, and then
walking over to Inuyama (about 20 minutes)
