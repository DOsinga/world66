title: "Tokugawa Museum"
type: poi
---

This fascinating museum provides an excellent introduction to Japanese arts and culture. It is filled with artifacts from the inheritance of the great Edo Period shogun Tokugawa Ieyasu and his clan, such as swords and armor, Noh drama costumes, tea ceremony items, and more. Its most precious possession is the original scroll of the 12th century epic poem 'The Tale of Genji'.
