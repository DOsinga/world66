Located in the west of Honshu, Osaka offers many sights and is worth a visit. It can easily be combined with a visit to [Kobe](/asia/japan/honshu/kobe) and [Kyoto](/asia/japan/honshu/kyoto) which are nearby. Kobe is not very interesting but Kyoto and Osaka are on your to do and to see list.
