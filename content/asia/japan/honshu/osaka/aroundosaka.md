---
title: "Around Osaka"
type: section
---

The area around Osaka is dense with worthwhile destinations. Kyoto is 15 minutes by shinkansen — enough said. Nara, with its deer park and giant bronze Buddha, is 45 minutes by train. Kobe is 30 minutes west by JR or Hankyu. Himeji Castle (Japan's finest) is 90 minutes from Osaka by shinkansen.
