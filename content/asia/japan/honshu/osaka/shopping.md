---
title: "Shopping"
type: section
---

Osaka divides into two main shopping zones. Umeda in the north has the big department stores and the Hankyu railway complex; Namba in the south has the covered Shinsaibashi shopping arcade and the Dotonbori restaurant and entertainment strip. Between them, the Amerika-mura district caters to younger shoppers with streetwear and second-hand clothing. For electronics and manga, Den Den Town (Nipponbashi) is the Osaka equivalent of Tokyo's Akihabara.
