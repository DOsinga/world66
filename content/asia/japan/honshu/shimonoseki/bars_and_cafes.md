---
title: "Bars and Cafes"
type: section
---

Shimonoseki has the low-key drinking culture typical of Japanese port cities — small, welcoming places where the bar staff often know most of their regulars. Near the waterfront, **Baghdad Cafe** (1-6 Akama-cho, 3rd floor; closed Thursdays) is a quirky little spot named for the 1987 film, run by an English-speaking owner who has filled it with curiosities. Coffee and light meals until 17:30, then bar hours until 23:00. The view of the Kanmon Strait from the front tables makes it worth the narrow staircase climb.
