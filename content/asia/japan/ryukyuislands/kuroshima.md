title: "Kuroshima"
type: location
---

Located between [Iriomote Island](/asia/japan/ryukyuislands/iriomoteisland) and [Ishigaki Island](/asia/japan/ryukyuislands/ishigakiisland), Kuroshima is known for its cattle, and the turtles that come to spawn upon its shores every year. Watch out for the cattle, the locals tend to herd them by chasing them with trucks, causing the cattle to run over everything in their path.
