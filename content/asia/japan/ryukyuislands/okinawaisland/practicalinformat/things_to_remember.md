Although Okinawa has a large amount of U.S. military personnel, it is
important to remember that not everyone there speaks English. Before you visit, learn some basic phrases that will help you in your travels:

**Greetings:**

**Ohaiyo Gozaimasu** (pronounced: OHIO GO-Zai-masu)=Good Morning

**Konnichi Wa** (KO-NEE-CHI-WA): Good Afternoon

**Konbanwa **(Khon-bahn-wah): Good Evening

**Counting:**

**Ichi** = 1

**Ni ** = 2

**San ** = 3

**Yon ** = 4

**Go** = 5

**Roku ** = 6

**Nana** = 7

**Hachi** = 8

**Kyu** = 9

**Ju** = 10

**Hyaku** = 100

**Sin** = 1000

**Maan** = 10000

These are the basic 13 you need to remember for counting because all
you have to do after you remember these basic numbers you just add to
get the value you want. (for example: 1575 =
Sin-Go-hyaku-Nana-Ju-Go)
