---
title: "Eating Out"
type: section
---

Kōchi has some of the best food in Shikoku, centred on the extraordinary local bonito. *Katsuo no tataki* — bonito seared briefly over a straw fire then served sliced with garlic, ginger, and ponzu — is the dish you must eat here. The city's *izakayas* are lively and inexpensive, and the local *yuzu* (citrus) flavours everything from dressings to sake.

Hirome Ichiba (listed under Things to Do) is the easiest and most atmospheric place to eat, but the smaller *izakayas* around the covered **Obiyamachi** arcade are worth exploring in the evening.
