---
title: "Ninomaru Shiseki Teien"
type: poi
category: Sight
latitude: 33.8448
longitude: 132.7628
---

A Japanese garden on the western slope of castle hill, laid out on the grounds of the former Ninomaru (outer palace). Tranquil and well-maintained, with a teahouse where you can sit with a bowl of matcha and a sweet for ¥300. A good stop on the way down from the castle.
