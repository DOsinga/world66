---
title: "Shopping"
type: section
---

Walk Chuo Dori up and down a few times and let the shops come to you — much of the merchandise is displayed on the pavement, and unlike most of Tokyo there is some room to negotiate on price. The streets just off Chuo Dori, including Yasukuni Dori, have a more eclectic mix of hobby shops, sporting goods, and specialist stores.
