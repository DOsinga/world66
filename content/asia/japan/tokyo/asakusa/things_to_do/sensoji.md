Tokyo's oldest temple, founded in 628 AD, and still one of the city's most visited sites. The main hall is dedicated to Kannon, the goddess of mercy. The grounds include the striking five-storey pagoda and numerous smaller halls and gardens. Incense smoke drifts across the courtyard, where visitors wave it over themselves for good health.
