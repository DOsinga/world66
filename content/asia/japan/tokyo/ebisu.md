Ebisu is a district located on the just south of Shibuya on the Yamanote Line, the JR (Japan Rail) railway. The name Ebisu, pronounced (ebis) is named after the fishing God per Japanese Shinto religion. Ebisu is hip happening place known for fine shopping and dining. The main attraction is Ebisu Garden Place Plaza, with a movie theter, the Mitsukoshii shopping center and the Sapporo Brewery and Museum.
