The JR Rail Pass is a special ticket that is available

only to travelers visiting Japan from foreign countries for

sight-seeing.

With the Rail Pass you can freely board almost any JR train around the country.

There are two types of JR Rail Pass: Green (for superior-class
Green cars), and Ordinary. Each of these types is available
as a 7-day, 14-day, or 21-day pass.
