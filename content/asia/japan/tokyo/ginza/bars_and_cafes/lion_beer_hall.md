---
title: "Lion Sapporo Beer Hall"
type: poi
address: "7-9-20 Ginza, Chuo-ku"
latitude: 35.6693
longitude: 139.7630
---

A grand old beer hall on Chuo-dori that has been serving Sapporo draught since 1934. The interior is decorated with striking mosaic murals and has the feel of a European brasserie. A good place to rest your feet after walking Ginza, with reliable beer and simple food.
