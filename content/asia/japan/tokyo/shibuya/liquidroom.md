One of Tokyo's best mid-sized live music venues, Liquidroom sits in Ebisu and books a strong mix of Japanese and international acts across electronic, rock, hip-hop, and everything in between. The sound system is excellent, and the standing-room layout keeps the energy high regardless of crowd size.

Originally located in Kabukicho in Shinjuku, the club relocated to its current Ebisu home and has built an even stronger reputation since. Tickets for popular shows sell out quickly — check the schedule and book ahead at Ticket Pia or e+ if you have a target act in mind. The venue is a short walk from Ebisu Station on the JR Yamanote Line.
