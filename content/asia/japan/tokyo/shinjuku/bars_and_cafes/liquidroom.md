A mid-sized live music venue that books a strong mix of Japanese and international acts across electronic, rock, and hip-hop. The sound system is excellent and the standing-room layout keeps the energy high. Originally in Kabukicho, now relocated to Ebisu but still a Shinjuku-area institution.
