Tokyo's largest entertainment district stretches east of Shinjuku station, packed with restaurants, bars, karaoke halls, and arcades stacked high in narrow buildings. The area has a somewhat seedy reputation but is largely safe for visitors and fascinating to walk through, especially after dark when the neon takes over. The Robot Restaurant and the towering Godzilla head on the Toho Cinema building are among the more surreal sights.
