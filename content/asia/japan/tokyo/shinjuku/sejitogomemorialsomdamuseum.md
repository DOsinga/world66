Housed on the 42nd floor of the Sompo Japan Building, this museum is best known for owning one of Van Gogh's Sunflowers paintings. The collection also includes works by Cezanne, Renoir, and Gauguin alongside a substantial collection of paintings by Seiji Togo. The views from this height are a bonus.
