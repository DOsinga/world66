title: Shinjuku Park Tower
type: poi

A 52-storey postmodern tower designed by Kenzo Tange, recognizable by its three stepped granite segments. The lower floors house design showrooms and the upper floors contain the Park Hyatt Tokyo, made famous by the film Lost in Translation. The New York Bar on the 52nd floor has some of the best views in Shinjuku.
