---
title: "Shopping"
type: section
---

The Ameyoko market is the main draw — a boisterous outdoor market running alongside the JR train tracks between Ueno and Okachimachi stations. Originally a post-war black market, it now sells fresh fish, spices, coffee, tea, dried fruits, and snacks at competitive prices. The shops east of and under the tracks sell clothing, shoes, and luggage. It is at its liveliest in the days before New Year.
