---
title: "Day Trips"
type: section
---

Amman is the best base in the country for day trips. **Jerash**, an hour north, holds the most complete provincial Roman city in the Middle East — colonnaded streets, oval forum, two theatres, temples of Artemis and Zeus. The **Dead Sea**, 45 minutes west and 430 m below sea level, has half a dozen public beaches and spa hotels where you can float in the most saline water on earth. Further east, the **Desert Castles** — Qasr Amra with its 8th-century frescoes, Qasr Kharana, Qasr al-Hallabat — can be strung together in a loop. For a longer day, the Byzantine mosaics of **Madaba** and the viewpoint from **Mount Nebo**, where Moses is said to have looked over the Promised Land, are under an hour south.
