---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Amman's headline sights — the Citadel, the Roman Theatre, and the Jordan Museum — all cluster around the downtown area and can be covered in a day on foot, linked by the steep staircases that run between the hills. Allow an extra half-day for the souks and cafes of Al Balad and an evening in the modern Rainbow Street area of Jabal Amman.
