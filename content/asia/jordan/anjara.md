Anjara is a small town in the hills of northern [Jordan](/asia/jordan), about 8 kilometres from [Ajlun](/asia/jordan/ajlun). It is a Christian pilgrimage site — tradition holds that Jesus and his disciples rested in a cave here during a journey between Jerusalem and Galilee.

The Our Lady of the Mountain shrine, built around that cave, is the main attraction. A Roman Catholic church houses a life-size wooden statue of the Virgin Mary holding the infant Jesus, and the grotto below has been developed as a place of prayer. The site features on most biblical tours of Jordan and draws pilgrims from across the Middle East. The town is quiet and can be combined with a visit to Ajlun Castle nearby.
