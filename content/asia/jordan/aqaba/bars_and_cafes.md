**Aqaba bars and cafes** dot this important seaport of Jordan. It is a coastal town in the southern portion of Jordan. Being a very important seaport in the country of Jordan, this area is filled with luxurious hotels, beaches, bars and cafes to keep its tourists more than satisfied.

**Twist Pub and Restaurant**

This is an attractive pub with a lounge bar and a conservatory. You can also opt to stay al fresco at its terrace, with tables overlooking the red sea. Twist is renowned for its good food, drinks and weekly entertainment; its the party place to be. When in town, you need to groove at the Twist. It has plenty of amenities that keep people coming back for more: free Wi-Fi, pets are allowed, smoking and non-smoking areas, no dress code, and they accept cash and credit cards. So, if you are here on vacation or business, you might want to stop here and have a good time.

**Diwan Al Basha Restaurant & Caf&eacute;**

This place offers high quality food and a fine waterfront dining experience overlooking the magnificent view of the Aqaba beach line and harbor. While you sip and drink champagne, or any beverage for that matter, surrounded by the caf&eacute;&rsquo;s wall to wall glass windows, you can savor the glorious and unparalleled view. The place is decorated with state of the art lighting and sound system to enjoy the fun-filled night. If you are looking to enjoy a night out, this could be the place for you.
