---
title: "Shopping"
type: section
---

Aqaba is a duty-free zone under the Aqaba Special Economic Zone (ASEZ), which makes imported goods cheaper here than elsewhere in Jordan. Electronics, alcohol, and cigarettes are notably discounted. The duty-free advantage draws cross-border shoppers from Saudi Arabia and day visitors from Eilat.

The Noor Al-Hussein Foundation shop, near the Archaeological Museum, sells quality Jordanian crafts: hand-embroidered textiles, ceramics, jewellery, and woven goods. Prices are fixed and support artisan communities. The souk area around the city centre has more casual shopping — clothing, spices, and everyday goods. Dream Mall in the downtown area serves local retail needs.
