---
title: "When to Go"
type: section
---

Spring (March to May) and autumn (October to November) are the best times to visit. Temperatures are comfortable for diving, hiking, and sightseeing. Winter is mild by regional standards — daytime temperatures typically reach 20°C — making Aqaba a popular winter sun destination. Summer (June to September) is very hot, with temperatures regularly above 40°C; the water is warm but the heat onshore is intense. Ramadan affects restaurant and bar hours.
