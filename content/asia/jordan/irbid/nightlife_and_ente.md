---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment"
type: section
---

Irbid offers a very good nightlife through lots of nightclubs, partiesvellages, theatres, and high class cafes.

For example, if you are looking for oriental dance/music, you can get it until the morning in some nightclubs like Meramar, al qanater (South of the City). On the other hand, you can have a very nice western night with classic or hip hop, Jazz music in some clubs like Al joud, or Hejazi, Umaia, Pravesta, Mango... etc.

However, when you want a very private night you can get it through rent a hall with complete service until the morning.
