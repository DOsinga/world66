Hello and welcome to Irbid

It is true that there are not too many things to do in the city of Irbid itself, but there are many places to visit around Irbid and withing 30 minutes drive from it. The most intersting would be Um Qais with its ruins,history and if you go south west of the city and you will have the greatest view of the Jordan Valley and Palastine. Then you have the hot water springs, there is one in north Shuna which I do not advise you to visit but the one in Mokhaibah is great and you will enjoy it.

if you want a place to see nature and have a BBQ just go to Bergesh.

enjoy your visit
