Madaba is just 30km south of [Amman](/asia/jordan/amman). It's a perfect daytrip. Madaba has some stunninly beautiful Byzantine-era mosaics, including the 'Madaba map', a 6th-century mosaic map of Palestine. 

You can see this mosaic in the Greek Orthodox St George's Church. 

Most of Madaba is now a carefully restored Archaeological Park. The park is well worth a visit. It includes the churches of the Virgin and the Prophet Elias, that date
