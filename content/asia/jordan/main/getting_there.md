---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

Ma'in is most easily reached from [Madaba](/asia/jordan/madaba), about 30 km away. Taxis from Madaba's town centre take around 30 minutes and the fare is negotiable — agree on a round-trip price that includes waiting time if you want the driver to bring you back. Expect to pay around 15–20 JD for a round trip with an hour or two of waiting.

From Amman, the drive is roughly an hour via the Desert Highway and then down into the gorge. Some visitors combine Ma'in with the Dead Sea on the same day, as the two are close to each other along the rift valley. Public transport to Ma'in itself does not run regularly; a taxi or private car is the practical option.
