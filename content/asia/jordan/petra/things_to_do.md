PLACES OF INTEREST

SHOPS

Aqaba not only offers a great selection of hand-crafted souvenirs, such as the traditional

Bedouin jewellery, sand bottles, etc., but also excellent modern and traditional jewellery

in gold and silver, at exceptionally good prices. Most of the shops open from

( 09h30 – 14h00 ) & from ( 17h00 – 22h00 ).

The Queen Noor Hussein Foundation, which supports local craftspeople, offers a

selection of handmade clothing, carpets, cushion covers, wall-hangings, pottery &

glassware. Located in front of the fort ( 08h00 – 16h00 ).
