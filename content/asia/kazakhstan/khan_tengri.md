Travel Guide

This Mountain, called "Celestial Emperor", on the border between Kazakhstan and Kyrgistan, near their respective borders with China. One of the most famous of the peaks in the Tien Shan, Khan Tengri is reported to be 6,995 or 7.010 meters by different sources. It is an ideal destination for those who are addict to extreme summits. From time to time, an international speed climbing festival is held to honour the mountain.
