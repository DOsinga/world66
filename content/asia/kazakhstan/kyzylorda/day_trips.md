[Turkestan](/asia/kazakhstan/turkestan) is within day-trip range of Kyzylorda. The city is home to the Mausoleum of Khoja Ahmed Yasawi, one of the most important pilgrimage sites in Central Asia -- three visits to the mosque there are said to count for one visit to Mecca.
