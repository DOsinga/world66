---
title: "Sights"
type: section
---

The city has a beautiful new plaza near city hall, and a new river walk along the river. In addition, the train station is noted by Lonely Planet as the best in Central Asia. Finally, one city park has all the old Lenin statues rounded up and in one place. Also interesting are the old Soviet murals at the factories on the way to the airport, between the river and the Petro-Kazakhstan oil worker residences.
