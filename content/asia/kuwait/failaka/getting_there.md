---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

Ferries to Failaka depart from the Ras Salmiya terminal in Kuwait City. Check current schedules with the Kuwait Public Transport Company, as sailing times vary by season. The crossing takes roughly 45 minutes. The ferry is the only public access to the island; there is no airport or bridge. Go early — the island is best explored in the morning before the heat builds, and the last ferry back is typically in the afternoon.
