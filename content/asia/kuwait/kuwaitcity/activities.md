**Aqua Park** -- The first and largest water park in the Gulf, located next to the Kuwait Towers on Arab Gulf Street. Pools, water slides, a man-made river, go-karts, and paintball. Open April to October. A great way to cool down in the summer months. [More...](/asia/kuwait/kuwaitcity/activities/aqua_park)

**Ice skating** -- Kuwait has two ice rinks, the first of their kind in the Gulf. The main rink is Olympic-sized with seating for 1,600 and is used by the Kuwait Falcons ice hockey team. A smaller rink seats 600. Skating lessons are available. A fun novelty in one of the hottest countries on earth.

**Scuba diving** -- One of the fastest-growing water sports in Kuwait. The Gulf waters offer good visibility and interesting marine life. Contact the Al Boom Marine Centre for organised diving trips.

**Desert camping** -- In the cooler months (November-March), Kuwaitis head to the desert for weekend camping trips. Some tour operators offer desert excursions for visitors, including barbecues and traditional Bedouin-style camps.

**Dhow cruises** -- Traditional sailing vessel trips along the coast offer views of the Kuwait City skyline from the water. Some include dinner. A pleasant evening activity.

**Golf** -- The Sahara Golf Resort offers an 18-hole course in the desert. Not cheap, but a surreal experience -- green fairways surrounded by nothing but sand.
