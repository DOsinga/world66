---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment"
type: section
---

Salmiya has the liveliest evening scene in Kuwait, though "nightlife" here means cafes, restaurants, and entertainment venues rather than clubs and bars.

**Hard Rock Cafe** in the Marina Mall's glass pyramid is one of the most popular spots. Rock memorabilia, American food, and sea views. Open until midnight, seven days a week.

**Sultan Center** is a sprawling shopping complex open late, with some stores operating 24 hours. Everything from clothing and electronics to restaurants and groceries.

**Cozmo Bowling** is a well-equipped entertainment centre with modern bowling lanes, billiards, darts, football games, and a large video arcade. Open from 11 AM to 1 AM. The atmosphere is warm and relaxed -- a popular hangout for families and groups of friends.

**Al-Shaab Gardens** (Shaab Leisure Park) is a large amusement park spread over 14,000 square metres. Rides, ice skating, adventure sports, roller skating, restaurants, landscaped gardens, and a multipurpose theatre. Entertainment for the whole family, open until midnight.

**Marina Crescent** is the waterfront promenade lined with restaurants and gift shops. Pleasant for an evening stroll, especially when the weather is cool. Some of the best restaurants in Kuwait are along this stretch.

Salmiya's evening atmosphere is relaxed and social. Families stroll, friends gather at cafes for shisha and coffee, and the pace is unhurried. Most places close by midnight or 1 AM.
