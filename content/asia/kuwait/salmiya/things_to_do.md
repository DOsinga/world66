---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Salmiya's sights are modest compared to Kuwait City, but a handful are worth the trip. The **Kuwait Scientific Centre** is the standout — a waterfront complex housing the largest aquarium in the Middle East, a discovery centre, and an IMAX cinema, all behind a striking facade decorated in Arabic calligraphy. Allow half a day. **Salem Al-Mubarak Street** is the oldest and largest shopping district in the country, and still retains something of the old souk character among the glass-fronted stores — gold and fabric shops tucked between the chains. **Marina World**, with its glass-pyramid mall and the waterfront Marina Crescent promenade, is where Salmiya's evening social life happens. The **Salmiya Beach** along Marina Bay is particularly pretty at sunset.
