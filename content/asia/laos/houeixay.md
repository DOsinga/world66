**Houeixay** is in the north-west corner of Laos on the border with [Thailand](/asia/thailand) and only kilometres from [Myanmar](/asia/myanmar). The Thai/Chinese influence is evident in the local architecture and culture.  This area of [Laos](/asia/laos) is not widely travelled by tourists because of the connection with the Golden Triangle.   Those who venture to the area will be impressed with the natural beauty and the friendliness of the locals.
