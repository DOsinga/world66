Patong is a small village on the road between [Vientiane](/asia/laos/vientiane) and [Luang Prabang](/asia/laos/luangprabang), north of [Vang Vieng](/asia/laos/vangvieng). Not to be confused with the beach town in Thailand, this Patong is a quiet roadside stop in the Lao mountains.
