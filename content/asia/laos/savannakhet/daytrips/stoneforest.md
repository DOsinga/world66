A new destination for nature lovers in Laos is “Hinboun” or “Stone Forest”, located half way between [Vientiane](/asia/laos/vientiane) and Savannakhet, which takes around 5-6 hours from each town. Excursion can be made by boat, by bicycle or trekking to nearby caves and villages.
