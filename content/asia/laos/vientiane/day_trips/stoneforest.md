The Hinboun area, sometimes called the Stone Forest, lies roughly halfway between Vientiane and [Savannakhet](/asia/laos/savannakhet), about 5-6 hours' drive from either city. The landscape of dramatic limestone karst formations is threaded with rivers and caves. Excursions by boat, bicycle, or on foot can be arranged locally. The Tham Kong Lo cave, a 7-kilometre river cave you travel through by boat in near-darkness, is the standout attraction in the area.
