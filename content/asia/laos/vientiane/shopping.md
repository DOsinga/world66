---
title: 'Shopping in Vientiane'
type: section
---

Vientiane has surprisingly good shopping, particularly for textiles and handicrafts. Bargaining is expected at markets and can bring prices down by up to 50 percent.

**Talat Sao (Morning Market)** is the city's main covered market. Despite the name, it runs all day from around 7am to 4pm. You will find Lao silk, handicrafts, electronics, jewelry, and just about everything else. A modern shopping mall is attached. It is also a reliable place to change money.

**Ban Anou Night Market** opens in the late afternoon and runs until midnight. It is smaller and cleaner than Talat Sao, with good street food, flowers, and handicraft souvenirs.

**Talat Thong Khan Kham** is an all-day market with local food and handicrafts. More merchants are present in the early morning hours.

**Kanchana Boutique** has the widest selection of Lao silk in Vientiane. The boutique has won international awards for its fabric quality. You can also arrange a visit to the adjacent Lao Textile Museum, open from 10am to 4pm.
