---
title: "Shopping in Vientiane"
type: section
---

The **Vientiane Shopping **options are endless. The city markets and shops offer a wide variety of wares – antiques, jewelry, clothing and many more. Don&rsquo;t forget to bargain – it is possible here and you can lower the price of the wares by up to 50 percent. Let&rsquo;s look at some of the interesting markets.**
**

**Talat Sao**

Talat Sao is also known as The Morning Market. It is a large covered market where you can find absolutely everything you can think of – from the finest Lao silk to motor bikes and musical instruments. Talat Sao usually opens at 7 a.m., although most of the merchants arrive at 9 a.m., and it closes at 4 p.m. Connected to the market is a modern four-storey shopping mall – an incarnation of the trendy Western shopping mall culture.

**Ban Anou**

Don&rsquo;t worry if your time isn&rsquo;t enough to view all the goods at Talat Sao, as there is Ban Anou – the Night Market, which opens in the late afternoon. It is known as the cleanest market in the city. Here, you will have the chance to try some of the delicious local food. You can also buy some beautiful flowers and interesting handicraft souvenirs. The market closes at midnight and is much smaller than Talat Sao, so you'll have more than enough time to browse it.

**Talat Thong Khan Kham**

Talat Thong Khan Kham is another place where you can find some good local food and handicrafts. Unlike the previous two, this is an all-day market, opening up in the early morning and closing in the evening. However, there are more merchants and goods in the early hours. The market is relatively big and worth visiting.

**Kanchana Boutique**

The Kanchana Boutique is very popular among the connoisseurs of Lao silk, as it offers the widest selection of silk in Vientiane. The boutique has won many awards at numerous worldwide festivals for the high quality of the silk fabrics. Certain royal families shop here. The boutique works from 8 a.m. to 9 p.m. and you can also arrange a visit to the local Lao Textile Museum - open from 10 a.m. to 4 p.m.

**Saigon**** Bijoux
**

Saigon Bijoux offers the best in silver and gold jewelry, as well as a wide variety of gems and pearls. But there&rsquo;s more – an extensive selection of handbags and both modern and antique art can be found here too. In addition, you must know that all the jewels here are hand-made and jewel repairs are also possible.
