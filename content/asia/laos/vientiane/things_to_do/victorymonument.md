Vientiane's victory monument sits at the north end of Lane Xang Avenue and is often compared to the Arc de Triomphe in Paris -- the Lao themselves call it 'the vertical runway' because it was built with cement donated by the US for an airport. The monument can be climbed for an excellent panoramic view of the city. A pleasant park with fountains surrounds it.
