---
title: "Festivals"
type: section
---

The Harum Manis Mango Festival in May celebrates the harvest of the Harum Manis mango — a variety specific to this region of Johor and highly regarded across Malaysia for its sweetness and aroma. The festival at Bukit Harumanis draws crowds from surrounding towns for mango tasting, cultural performances, and agricultural exhibits. The full range of Malaysian public holidays — Hari Raya, Chinese New Year, Deepavali — are all observed in this multi-ethnic town.
