The main mosque of Batu Pahat, on Jalan Kluang, is one of the town's most visible landmarks. Its architecture reflects the regional Johor style. Non-Muslim visitors are welcome outside of prayer times provided they dress modestly. The Masjid Jamek on Jalan Ampuan is a second historic mosque worth visiting.
