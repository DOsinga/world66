---
title: "When to Go"
type: section
---

Batu Pahat has a tropical climate with no distinct dry season. The southwest monsoon brings heavier rain from May to September; the northeast monsoon from November to January brings the heaviest rainfall and occasional flooding in low-lying areas. Year-round temperatures are 28–33°C during the day. The Harum Manis Mango Festival in May is the main reason to time a visit to a particular month.
