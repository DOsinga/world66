type: poi

A small butterfly breeding centre in the Cameron Highlands, housing dozens of tropical species in a netted enclosure. You'll find common birdwings, rajah Brooke's birdwings, and various Papilio species fluttering around the lush plantings. The centre also keeps some other insects and small animals. It's a brief stop — an hour is plenty — but pleasant if you have children or a passing interest in entomology. Check opening times locally as hours vary.
