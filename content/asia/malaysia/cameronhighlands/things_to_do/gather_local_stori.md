title: "Gather local stories"
type: poi
type: "Other"
---

Talk to the locals (particularly Taxi drivers) to find out about local stories and urban myths.

Gather stories about the famous celebrities who have visited the place
in the seventies, or about the American tourists who went into the
forest and were never found, or about the haunted hotel rooms in town.

Most of the locals will be more than eager to share these stories with you.
