Upscale pub atmosphere with a large centrally located donut-shaped bar surrounded by tables, and off to one side a darker, more intimate seating arrangement reminiscent of a lounge bar. This is a watering hole patronised by the local white-collar crowd. Live entertainment by a Filipino band starts at 9pm Tuesday to Sunday and things get lively by the small hours. House pours and beers cost around MYR 9.20 per glass. Happy hour runs from 6pm to 9pm daily and all night on Mondays.
