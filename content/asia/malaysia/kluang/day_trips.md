title: Day Trips
type: section
---

If a day is all you have in the Malaysian state of Johor, then why not choose from a host of **Kluang Daytrips**. Kluang is filled with greenery of different kinds, has a number of interesting places to visit and is rich in delicious food. Let's take a look at some possible ways to spend a day trip in Kluang.**
**

**Scaling Heights**

Gunung Lambak is Kluang's most famous mountain, whose peak is scalable in around 2 hours flat with not much stress involved. It's a good idea to begin early in the morning so as to avoid the mid-day sun. Before setting out on the ascent of this mountain, a must see is the playground at the bottom. While you're at it, you should drop into the nearby resort for a nourishing breakfast that will provide just the right amount of energy to climb the mountain. From the mountain peak, expect a great view by the picturesque town of Kluang as well as an abundance flora and fauna.**
**

**Fresh and Organic**

Spend a day at Kluang's Zenxin Organic Park, where you'll find everything grown organically. Once here, you can choose to go on a walking or bicycle tour around the green farm area, where you will see a flower nursery and other vegetables and fruits grown without any chemicals. Look out for Kluang's famous produce - the dragonfruit! The park also has a café for refreshments.**
**

**Gunung Belumut**

If you plan to take in the beauty of Kluang, head to the Gunung Belumut Recreational Forest, which is an experience not to be missed. Take in the stunning views of breathtaking waterfalls and exotic flora in this green zone. It's a good idea to come here well equipped with a picnic basket so that you can truly enjoy a meal in the midst of lush greenery.**
**

**Tropical Village**

If you have a day to spare in Kluang and you happen to be traveling with your young children, spend a day at Tropical Village, a theme park in Kluang. The park is divided into different sections such as Playground, Leisure Corner, Landmarks and Agriculture. The miniature replicas of the Taj Mahal, The Statue of Liberty and other wonders of the world are very realistic.**
**

**Wild Thing**

If adventure is what you are looking for, head to the Tanjung Piai National Park for a day filled with bird watching. You will be sure to spot beautiful bird species like the sandpiper and the greenshank. Since the park is in the vicinity of the sea, you can enjoy a good seafood meal before heading back home.
