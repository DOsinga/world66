A family-owned business since 1964. High grade genuine Ceylon tea, homemade cakes, pies, puffs, and buns reminiscent of the colonial era of English tea time, alongside local delights: nyonya kuih, crispy fritters, nasi lemak, mee siam, noodles, and fried radish cake (chai tow kuih). Sandwiches and kaya toast prepared fresh, warm and crispy. Located opposite Wisma Bunga, Honeyland is a classic kopitiam experience with a genuine muhibbah atmosphere.
