---
title: "Sights in Kluang"
type: section
---

**Kluang sights** attract a variety of tourists every year. Kluang is part of Johor state. It is situated inland so there are no beaches but there are many other natural attractions. Kluang is popular among trekkers as there are mountains in the vicinity and other adventure activities.
**
Shopping**

Kluang has a variety of shopping options including shopping malls and markets. BCB Plaza, Kluang Shopping Mall and Kluang Parade are different options. Roadside stalls and night markets are
places to mingle with the locals and buy handmade products. Markets are
a good place to sample traditional cuisine.
**
Gunung Lambak**

Gunung Lambak is a mountain where tourists can go trekking and explore the beautiful natural surroundings. Gunung Lambak is situated 5 km outside the town. It takes approximately
2 hours to reach the top of the mountain. You may be able to spot fruit
bats during your hike. There are trails and bridges to make the hike
easier. Natural pools of water provide places to swim. At the bottom
of the mountain is a play area where children can keep themselves
entertained. The surrounding forest is a good picnic spot which can be
a fun family activity. There are a variety of amenities including
toilets and benches.
**
Zenxin Organic Park**

Zenxin Organic Park is located on the way to Air Hitam.
At the park there are many things for visitors to do including walking
trails and bicycle tours through the interesting scenery. This is a
good place to go to get some fresh air and take in your surroundings.
There is also educational information that offers insight into the
different processes used on farms. An on-site cafe sells
refreshments. There is a shop selling fresh produce and plants.
Functions can be held at this venue. There is no entrance fee.

**Gunung Belumut**

Mount Belemut
is 1,010 meters above sea level. It takes 1 or 2 days to climb,
depending on the climber's experience and fitness level. Just before
the summit is a place where climbers can camp under suitable weather
conditions. At the summit is a rock called Crown Rock. The mountain can
be reached by taxi from Kluang. It is 25 km from the city.
There is a recreational area at the bottom of the mountain with a
playground and food stalls. There are waterfalls located on this
mountain, including Sg Yong Falls which has refreshing clear water.
