---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Kluang's natural assets are its main draw for visitors. Gunung Lambak and Gunung Belumut, both within easy reach of the town, are designated recreational forests with jungle hiking trails. Tasik Mardi is another recreational area near the town. The Kluang Railway Station, in service since 1915, is the town's most photogenic structure — and the railway canteen on Jalan Omar is famous throughout Malaysia for its kopi (local coffee served strong and sweet), toasted bread with kaya coconut jam, and half-boiled eggs. Breakfast there is a local institution. The station cuts the town in two, and when trains pass, the road across the tracks closes and the town, as locals say, briefly stands still.
