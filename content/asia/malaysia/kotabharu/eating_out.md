---
title: "Eating Out in Kota bharu"
type: section
---

The several **Kota Bharu Restaurants** provide visitors a chance
to enjoy the unique Malaysian cuisine while on holiday. The restaurants
specialize in authentic Chinese, Thai, Malaysian and Indian dishes, all
at affordable rates. Here are some restaurants you might just want to
try.

**Noodle Station**

This is a small chic café near the popular Hotel Sentosa. Noodle
Station offers different variations of noodle dishes, as well as other
western dishes, snacks, kid’s meals, desserts and great coffee. This is a
well-known restaurant for locals and tourists who want delicious,
mouthwatering food. Noodle Station’s must-try dishes are Tom Yam, Kuey
Teow, Fried Wanton Noodles and Station Smoothie. All the dishes are very
reasonably priced at this restaurant.

**Four Seasons Restaurant**

The Four Seasons Restaurant is a Chinese restaurant situated in the
eastern part of the city. The menu includes a wide range of Chinese
cuisine as well as Thai and Malaysian dishes. The restaurant also
offers a la carte menu for groups of 8 to 10 diners, as well as lunch
and dinner buffets. Some of the restaurant popular dishes are: kampung
chicken, oyster pancakes, curries, buttered prawns and steamed fish.

**Meena Curry House**

If you are craving Indian cuisine, Meena Curry House is the best
place to go. This Indian restaurant is located right in the heart of
Kota Bharu. The restaurant offers wide array of Indian curries, rotis
and more. Meena Curry House is one of the popular restaurants for both
tourists and locals alike. The restaurant is open for lunch and dinner
and also serves pre-cooked food displayed in covered food racks.

**Medan Selera MPKB (Night Market)**

The best, most delicious and cheapest Malaysian foods can be found at
the street stalls, particularly at the Night Market. Every night a huge
number of food stalls are set up along the street of Jln Pintu Pong in the town center. Here you can find wide selection of regional delicacies
like ayam percik, nasi kerabu, grilled squid, blue rice and banana
murtabak.

**Medan Selera Kebun Sultan Food Court**

The Medan Selera Kebun Sultan Food Court is a huge area in the town
center where you will find different food stalls. If you would like to
eat Chinese, Indian, Thai or Malaysian dishes, this is the right place
to come and enjoy some delicious food. This food court is always packed
with diners, both locals and tourists.
