title: "Medan Selera Night Market"
type: poi
latitude: 6.1270
longitude: 102.2438
address: "Jalan Pintu Pong"
---

Every evening, food stalls line Jalan Pintu Pong in the town centre. This is the best and cheapest eating in Kota Bharu. Look for ayam percik, nasi kerabu, grilled squid, blue rice, and banana murtabak. Come hungry and try a bit of everything.
