A Chinese restaurant in the eastern part of the city serving Chinese, Thai, and Malaysian dishes. Good for groups, with an a la carte menu for 8 to 10 diners and regular buffets. The kampung chicken, oyster pancakes, and steamed fish are reliable choices.
