The Museum of Royal Traditions, housed in a handsome 1887 wooden palace near Padang Merdeka. The building itself is the main attraction, with intricately carved wooden panels throughout. Inside you will find displays on Kelantanese royal customs, wedding ceremonies, and traditional crafts. Entry is around RM 2.
