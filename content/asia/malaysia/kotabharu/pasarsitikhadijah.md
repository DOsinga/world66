Kota Bharu's famous central market is a four-storey landmark and the social hub of the city. The ground floor is a wet market selling fresh fish, meat, fruits, and vegetables. Upper floors are devoted to dried fish, spices, batik, and Kelantan's famous lekor crackers. Most of the vendors are women, a local tradition that has fuelled the (unfair) reputation of Kelantanese men as lazy. Come early in the morning for the best atmosphere.
