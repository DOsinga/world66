A striking Thai temple at Kampung Jambu, just outside Kota Bharu, home to a 40-metre reclining Buddha that ranks among the largest in Southeast Asia. The temple is a vivid reminder of Kelantan's Thai-Buddhist minority and makes for a peaceful half-day visit.
