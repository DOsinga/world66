---
title: "Eating Out"
type: section
---

Seafood is the reason to eat well in KK. The Filipino Market at the waterfront is the most atmospheric option — pick your fish and shellfish from the stalls, then have it grilled to order at one of the adjacent tables. Gaya Street has a string of kopitiam coffee shops good for cheap noodles and dim sum breakfasts, while the Waterfront Esplanade has smarter restaurants with views across the bay.
