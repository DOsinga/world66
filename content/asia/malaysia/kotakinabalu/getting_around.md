---
title: "Getting Around"
type: section
---

The city center is compact enough to walk. Minibuses and taxis cover most destinations within the urban area, and Grab (the regional ride-hailing app) works well here. For day trips to the islands, speedboats leave from Jesselton Point Ferry Terminal throughout the morning. Renting a car is useful for reaching Lok Kawi, Kundasang, or other out-of-town destinations on your own schedule.
