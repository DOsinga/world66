---
title: "Shopping"
type: section
---

The Sunday morning market on Gaya Street is the city's best weekly event — fresh produce, local snacks, and craftwork spread out along several blocks from around 6am to 1pm. The Handicraft Market near the waterfront has batik, pearls, and Sabahan basketry at negotiable prices. For air-conditioned shopping, Centre Point and Imago Mall carry international brands alongside a solid food court.
