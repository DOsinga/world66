---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Kota Kinabalu punches well above its weight for outdoor activities. The five islands of Tunku Abdul Rahman Park are reachable by speedboat in 15 minutes for snorkeling and beach time, while the Lok Kawi Wildlife Park offers close encounters with pygmy elephants and proboscis monkeys. Serious hikers come from across Asia to climb Mount Kinabalu, with most doing the two-day ascent from Kinabalu Park.
