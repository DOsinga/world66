Sabah's premier cultural institution occupies a sprawling hilltop complex near Queen Elizabeth Hospital. The main building houses tribal artifacts, ritual objects, and Borneo's natural history across six galleries, while a heritage village on the grounds reconstructs traditional dwellings from the state's main ethnic groups. The botanical garden and Science and Technology Center round out a visit that easily fills half a day.
