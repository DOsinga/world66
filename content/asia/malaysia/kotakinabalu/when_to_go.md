---
title: "When to Go in Kota Kinabalu"
type: section
---

Kota Kinabalu, though not as widely known and revered as other
Malaysian tourist hotspots is fast acquiring the status of its more
illustrious peers with its national parks, breathtaking mountains and a
bustling commercial town center. Here is a guide to the best time to
visit Kota Kinabaslu to enjoy its natural beauty.

**Weather **

Kota Kinabalu sports a primarily equatorial climate with relatively
uniform temperatures and a high level of humidity owing to a perennial
rainfall in the region. The best time to visit the city is from January
to March, when the climate is cool and dry and makes for less messy and
comfortable sightseeing sessions and outdoor activities. October sees
torrential strokes of rain in Kota Kinabalu and it would do visitors
good to avoid making a trip during this period. The months of April and
May are extremely hot and humid and hence not the best time to make a
trip to the region.

**Festivals**

The period from November to mid January is best avoided due to the
fact that the festival season sees hordes of Catholics and members of
the Christian community flock to the city. Kota Kinabalu is
uncomfortably crowded during the festivals and it is a daunting task to
find decent accommodation options and go for city sightseeing sessions.
Late January and February are a good time to enjoy the weather and a
relatively peaceful vacation in Kota Kinabalu.

**Sabah Fest**

For visitors who don’t mind the sultry weather in their quest to
discover the gems of the Sabah State culture, the Sabah fest held
annually in the month of May is a good time to catch colorful displays
of the heritage of the region with customary rituals, statewide
celebrations and lectures on the land’s ethnography and civilization.

**Mount Kinabalu International Climbathon**

The exciting annual mountain climbing event sees hordes of climbing
purists and enthusiasts from all over the world vying for the coveted
top mountain runner’s title. The race covers 4096 meters over a
cumulative distance of over 21 kilometers.

**Malaysia Mega Sale Carnival**

Shopaholics from all over the world converge in the town during the
annual mega sale carnival held in the region from June to September.
Visitors can bag some of the best deals and special discounts on a
range of items from designer apparel and accessories to electronic
gadgets and local artifacts.

Kota Kinabalu is a year round attractive destination depending on
what visitors come for. Climatically January and February are the best
months but for a cultural odyssey of the town, the May Sabah fest is
the best time to visit. Compulsive shoppers will not be able to resist
travelling here between June to September during the annual carnival
sale.
