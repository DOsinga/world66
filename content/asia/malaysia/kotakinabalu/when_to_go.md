---
title: "When to Go"
type: section
---

January through March offers the most comfortable conditions — temperatures are warm but not oppressive, and rainfall is lighter than the rest of the year. October brings the heaviest rains and is best avoided. May hosts the Sabah Fest, a worthwhile celebration of the state's indigenous cultures, and the Kinabalu International Climbathon takes place in late August or September for those who want to watch elite mountain runners tackle the peak.
