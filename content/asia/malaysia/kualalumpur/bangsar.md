Bangsar is one of KL's most popular upscale neighborhoods, centered on two modern shopping complexes and a warren of boutiques selling antiques, furniture, textiles, and cosmetics. The real draw is the dining and nightlife — Telawi Street and the surrounding lanes fill up every evening with a mix of international restaurants, wine bars, and late-night mamak stalls. It's where KL's expat and young professional crowd gravitates after dark.
