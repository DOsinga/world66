Kuala Lumpur's busiest shopping and entertainment district, centered on Jalan Bukit Bintang. The strip runs from Pavilion KL at the western end through Lot 10, Fahrenheit 88, and Sungei Wang Plaza — a reliable progression from international luxury brands down to affordable local goods and electronics.

The street-level action is just as interesting: hawker stalls set up in the evening along Jalan Alor (one block north), and the side streets fill with massage parlors, bars, and late-night restaurants. Changkat Bukit Bintang is the main bar street, lively every night of the week. The whole area is well connected by monorail (Bukit Bintang station) and MRT.
