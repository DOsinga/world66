Most buses in KL operate from the hot and noisy Pduraya bus station on Jalan Pulujust of Chinatown. There are departures to most places throughout the day and at night to main towns. 

Typical fares from KL: RM 23 to Singapore, RM 16.50 to Johor Bahru, RM 8 to Melaka, RM 12.50 to Lumut, RM 11 to Cameron Highlands, RM 21 to Penange and RM 15 to Kuantan.



Buses to Kelang and Port Kelang No.793)and Shah Alam  (Nos.337/338) leave from the Kelang bus station at the end of Jalan Hang Kasturi in Chinatown.

 buses to Kuala Lipis and Jerantut operate from the Pekeliling bus station in the north of the city on Jalan Tun Razak. At Putra bus station - opposite the World Trade Centre -express coaches leave for Kuantan, Kuala Terengganu and Kota Bharu.
