KL is the hub of the railway system. From the historic old KL train station there are daily departures for Butterworth and Singapore.

 For the east-coast line to Jeranut serving Taman Nekara and Kota bharu it is more complicated as you have to take a southbound train to Gemas and then catch another connection. So both places are easier to reach by bus.
