title: "Money"
type: poi
---

Foreign currency and travellers cheques may be exchanged in all commercial banks during banking hours:  Mondays to Fridays - 10.00 am to 3.00 pm  Saturdays - 9.30 am to 11.30 am.  Licensed money changers at the airport, in international hotels and the city sometimes offer a better exchange rate.  Travellers Cheques are also accepted by hotels and major department stores. Generally, banks give the best rates.
