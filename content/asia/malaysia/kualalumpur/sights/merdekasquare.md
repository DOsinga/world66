Merdeka Square is in the heart of downtown KL. It is also the site of the proclamation of independence in 1957. Surrounding the square are many interesting and buildings of historical interest, including the Royal Selangor Club where KL's elite meet, the Sultan Abdul Samad building, the National History Museum and and the modern Dayabumi Complex.
