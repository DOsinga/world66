---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Cherating is all about the water. The sheltered lagoon and open coastline between them offer something for every level, from first-time windsurfers to experienced kayakers, and the mangrove river adds a quieter alternative when you want to get off the beach.

Windsurfing on the lagoon is the classic Cherating activity, with flat water and steady breezes that make it a forgiving place to learn. Board and kayak rental is available from outfits along the beach. During the monsoon season from November to March, the open sea produces consistent three-to-five-foot waves for surfing, with a sandy bottom that keeps things safe for beginners.

River boat trips up the mangrove are well worth the couple of hours. A guide poles you through the channels where you can spot water monitors, macaques, otters, and a good variety of birds. The evening version takes you out to see fireflies blinking along the riverbanks, a genuinely magical sight.
