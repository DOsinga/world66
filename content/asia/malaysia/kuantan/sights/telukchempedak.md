Teluk Chempedak is an excellent beach for water sports activities like sailing, surfing, skiing and sunbathing. The beach is located 5 km from the city centre. Most visitors stay in one of the 5 star hotels. Check out {accommodation). You can choose between the Hyatt Kuantan and the Merlin Inn Resort.
