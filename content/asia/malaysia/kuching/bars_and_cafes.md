---
title: "Bars and Cafes"
type: section
---

The waterfront area and the Bukit Mata neighbourhood have the highest concentration of bars and cafes. Open-air coffee shops serving Chinese-style kopi (local coffee) and light food are found throughout the city and are the cheapest and most authentic option. The evening hawker stalls opposite Electra House Shopping Centre are popular for supper. Bars cluster around Bukit Mata; the Travillion Pub Centre in Petanak has multiple bars in one complex for those who want to stay out late.
