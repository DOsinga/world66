Sharing Planet is an uniquely designed wooden built restaurant in Kuching, you can enjoy western food anywhere else, but dinning here will definitely be one of your unforgetable experience!

Cosy, quiet environment, and you can hear the natural symphony of insects choirs in the night time, yes, the choirs are the real insects singing!

This planet serves western food, pasta and a wide variety of drinks selection, located about 4KM away from downtown Kuching, getting a cab is the only way to it (usually cost RM15 one way), but be rest assured it is a place worth visiting during your stay in Kuching.

Closes on every Wednesday.

The contact of the managers are 016 8635151 (Kenneth) and 016 864 6864 (Kent).
