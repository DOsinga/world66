The esplanade along the south bank of the Sarawak River is the social heart of Kuching. A landscaped promenade runs for about a kilometre past food stalls, benches, and viewpoints looking across to the Astana — the former palace of the White Rajahs — and Fort Margherita on the opposite bank. Bumboats cross to the north bank on request. The Main Bazaar runs parallel to the waterfront and has the best souvenir and antique shops in the city.
