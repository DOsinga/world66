---
title: "7 Day Itinerary in Langkawi"
type: section
---

A **Langkwai Itinerary** is great for tourists who have a week to spend, as there are many things to do amongst the islands
of Langkwai and your week is going to fly by. Here is the best
way to spend your week.

**Day 1: Relax**

Lay back, relax, and enjoy the beautiful beaches. The main reason
most tourists go to Langkwai is to see their world renowned beaches.
For a real relaxing treat try Pantai Tengah beach. Separated from the
popular Cenang beach by a small cape, Pantai Tengah offers visitors a
tranquil experience, while still located within walking distance to
restaurants, bars and shops.

**Day 2: Pulau Payar Marine Park Excursion**

After a nice day or relaxing, day 2 will crank up the excitement.
Pulau Payar Marine Park offers the best view of marine life in all of
Malaysia. The day will offer snorkeling, beach time, and swimming in
crystal clear water. This excursion includes transportation, equipment,
simple lunch and water. This tour takes approximately 7 hours.

**Day 3: Stardust Adventure Day Cruise**

Day three will be spent on a classic wooden sail board, exploring
many of the small islands, islets and bays. See all that the ecosystem
has to offer, including exploring the caving system, sting ray feeding,
and swimming with dolphins. This tour includes transfers, 12 course buffet
lunch, and open bar.

**Day 4: Caves and Mangroves**

The fourth day will be spent exploring the inner island ecosystem. A
day away from the beaches, this tour will take you around Kilim Nature
Park. Kilim offers stunning mangrove forests, hidden blue lagoons and
plenty of flora and fauna, which your tour guide can explain in
detail. Schedule about 5 hours for this tour.

**Day 5: Island Hopping**

Take a day to explore the many islands in the area. Traveling by
boat you will see many different islands, islets, lagoons, rainforests,
eagle feedings, and more. Mid day you will find yourself at an animal
sanctuary, offering home to many indigenous animals, some of whom have
been rescued and relocated to the sanctuary.

**Day 6: Langkwai Island Discovery Package A**

The sixth day of your tour will offer you a glimpse into the rich
tapestry of traditions and customs. The first stop will be at Atma Alam
Batik Art Village and will provide you opportunity to purchase
souvenirs made from a painting and dying technique typical to the area.
Next is Eagle Square, a 19 acre landmark featuring a large bronze eagle
statue. Next on the list is Mahsuri's Tomb, where local legend has it
that after Mahsuri was conemed to death for adultery she took her own
life and laid a curse upon the island. The last stop on the tour is
shopping in Kuah Town and then to the Underwater World, the largest
fresh water aquarium in Malaysia featuring a 15 meter long underwater
viewing tunnel.

**Day 7: Sunset Dinner Cruise**

With the morning free to do any remaining shopping or beach laying,
the night will take you on a beautiful sunset dinner cruise. The cruise
will feature meal and drinks (both alcoholic and non alcoholic). After
dinner enjoy a soak in the saltwater jacuzzi to finish off a busy week.
