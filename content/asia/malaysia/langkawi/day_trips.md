As a tropical Island, Langkawi blessed with ancient nature and spectacular sights and activities. Langkawi has some of Asia's best beaches and virgin rainforest thriving with wildlife.

The tropical island is a beautiful
tranquil retreat from the real world and ideal for those who want to
unwind in peace. Langkawi has an array of attractions for beach lovers
to nature lovers to adventure lovers.
Langkawi is home to approximately 200 species of birds, of which
about one third are migrants who winter here between mid-December and
mid-April.

In Some parts of Langkawi one can notice the age-old limestone outcrops rise from
the sea. Go to a pristine stretch known as Tanjung Rhu north of the
island.
