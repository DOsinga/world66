---
title: "Day Trips"
type: section
---

The archipelago itself is the main day-trip destination. Island-hopping tours from Pantai Cenang loop to **Pulau Dayang Bunting** — where the freshwater Lake of the Pregnant Maiden sits inside a ring of limestone — and stop at small beaches en route. **Pulau Payar Marine Park**, an hour south by speedboat, has the best snorkelling in the region.

From mid-December to mid-April, **Koh Lipe** in southern Thailand is a direct speedboat ride away, and the immigration formalities are handled on arrival. This is one of the easiest Thailand-Malaysia border crossings in either direction.
