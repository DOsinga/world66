Hankering for authentic Thai and seafood when in town? Look no further as there's one located in the town of Kuah. Just opened recently and this is their second outlet in Langkawi, with the first outlet, Islandish Seafood Restaurant is located in Pantai Chenang.

Their signature dishes includes Tom Yum Lobster, Tom
Kha Khai (Coconut Tom Yum), Otak Otak Fish, ABC Fish (3 Flavoured Fish),
Thai Green and Red Curry, Sizzling Tiger Prawns, Thailandish Fish (Pek
Sak), Deep Fried Squid, Thai Style Fried Rice and Tom Yum Steamboat.

They also offer full bar service which includes beer on draft, wine,
cocktails, liquor and juices with alfresco and in house dining with soothing
jazz and light and easy music. They also cater for in house function. Service is excellent with friendly staff.

You can check them out at http://www.facebook.com/ThailandishSeafoodRestaurant
