A UNESCO-listed mangrove and karst landscape on the northeast coast, best seen by boat. Three-hour tours run from Kilim Jetty through the mangroves to a bat cave, a crocodile cave, and a fish farm — brahminy kites and sea eagles wheel overhead, diving for fish scraps thrown from the boat. Go early; by mid-morning there are a lot of boats.
