---
title: "7 Day Itinerary in Melaka"
type: section
---

Melaka is considered as
Malaysia’s historical state; that means you have lot of things to incorporate
in your **Melaka Itinerary** list. Melaka’s
rich history and culture has made it one of Asia’s best places to explore.
Melaka is also one of the UNESCO’s World Heritage Sites.

**Day
1 - Melaka Sultanate Palace Museum **

Melaka has several historical
places you can visit, but the Melaka Sultanate Palace Museum is the most
important of all. It is an exact replica of the Malay Annals Sultanate Palace
and it is made of wood. The palace is located at the foot of St. Paul’s Hill.

**Day
2 - Melaka Day and Night Zoo**

The largest zoo in
Malaysia, the Melaka Zoo offers different activities for their day and night
visitors. During the day you can visit their open air zoo that features 200
species of rare animals like the Malayan sun bears, tigers, Sumatran rhinoceros
and more. If you choose to visit the zoo at night, you can take the tram ride and
you can see nocturnal animals and African wildlife scenario.

**Day
3 - Gastronomic Adventure**

Melaka’s cuisine is one of
Southeast Asia’s best. Melaka is famous for its spicy baba and nyonya cooking
style. Some of the most popular dishes are the foods cooked in rich coconut
milk, fresh seafood, satays and more. There are several fine restaurants in
Melaka where you can enjoy Melaka’s unique cuisine.

**Day 4 - A’Famosa Water World **

A’Famosa Water World is the
largest resort and the most modernized hot spot in Melaka. It houses a water-based theme park, safari world, horse riding, golf resort, cowboy town,
children’s theme park and micro-light flying club. You will need to spend the
whole day here to enjoy all the attraction of the resort.

**Day
5 - Tropical Fruit World **

D-Paradise Tropical Fruit
World and Aboriginal Native Village has the world’s largest collection of
tropical, continental and sub-tropical fruits. It covers 168 acres of well
maintained garden and houses a unique aboriginal native village. Take a trip to
this village and enjoy the day trips offered at the village.

**Day
6 - Tanjung Bidara Beach **

If you are looking for great
[beaches](/asia/malaysia/melaka/beaches), look no farther because Melaka has it all. Popular beaches here are
Tanjung Bidara Beach and Pengkalan Balak Beach, both of which offer great
amenities, picnic grounds, surau and great scenic views.

**Day
7 - Shopping**

Jonker Walk is a huge street
that has a great number of stores that offer ready-made clothing, jewelry,
antiques, glassware, fiber, food and more.
This is Melaka’s best [shopping](/asia/malaysia/melaka/shopping) paradise where you can find great
bargains and interesting items.
