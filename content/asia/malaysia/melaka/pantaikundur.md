Pantai Kundur is a small fishing village about seventeen kilometres from Melaka. The beach is a favorite spot for swimming and picnicking, but it is perhaps best known for its graceful form of night-fishing. Fishermen suspend their nets from poles near their kelongs — houses built on stilts over the water — and periodically lower them to net a passing shoal. If you can time a visit for the evening, watching the lanterns come on over the water is worth the trip.
