The entire **Miri 7 day itinerary** usually starts at the break of day, so wake up early enough. It's always good to familiarize
yourself with the town's history first.

**Day 1 – Canada Hill/Crocodile Farm**

Start your day by touring the city of Miri and a drive to Canada
Hill, which is an excellent lookout point over the whole city and its
coastline. Malaysia's first oil well, discovered in 1910 and now known
as The Grand old Lady, sits as a relic on Canada Hill. On Kuala Baram
