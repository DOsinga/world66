**Miri budget travel **is for those travelers who want a nice day
out in the city with the family and have fun while at the same time
only spend a small amount of money.

**Taman**** Awam Miri
**

Miri public park (Taman Awam Miri) is the place to go, only 3km from
the city center along Miri-Bintulu road. Filled with flower gardens,
man-made waterfalls and stone carving, the park gives you the ideal
place to have a picnic as well as relax. Having an observatory
platform, you get the perfect aerial view of the park and the
surrounding landscape.

**Park Hotel**

Located where the old city of Miri used to be, this hotel offers a
wide range of services such as car parking, wireless internet for
guests and fax. With the price just a bit over the average, its ideal
as it gives easy access to the city center. All the staff speak English
as well as Malay.

**Miri Hotel**

It’s situated right at the centre of the city. This hotel has a
higher price charge as compared to the others as it has services such
as baby cot, daily housekeeping, arranged car hire, laundry and
ironing, as well as foreign currency exchange. All these services make
your traveling experience relaxing and convenient. All the staff
working there speak more than three language like English,
Chinese-Cantonese and Bahasa Melayu.

**Marriott Miri resort and spa**

With Miri budget travel, experience luxury in a whole new level by
staying at the Marriott Miri resort and spa. Located in the Borneo
Island the resort offers guest with a full service complete with a
wonderful view of the gardens overlooking the South China Sea.

**Miri Reef**

This
coral reef is one of the most recent discovered diving location. It has
a variety of coral and marine life that rivals the best anywhere in
Borneo. The patches of reefs are from 7 to 30 meters deep. Make a point
of visiting this beautiful place.

**Miri Nightlife**

Miri
budget travel isn't complete without you sampling its nightlife. Catch
a taxi and you are sure to find exciting places to visit for drinks or
supper.You will find clubs and pubs offering western food and finger
food. There is also places where you can get karaoke, dancing,
drinking, clubbing e.t.c. You can try places like Club Infinity and
Karaoke located at Boulevard Commercial Centre, Cherries Berries, and
Balcony located at Yakin Commercial Centre are among the few. **
