---
title: "Sights in Miri"
type: section
---

**Miri Sights **will
offer you an unforgettable vacation filled with warm and fond
memories of this small fishing town which hit gold with the discovery
of oil.

**
**
**
The Miri Waterfront**

Observe
the old and the new parts of Miri on the Miri waterfront. The
fishermen's village will give you a peek into traditional Miri
culture and lifestyle. On the other hand, the newly developed
commercial area has several shopping spots. Take a romantic stroll on
the waterfront and dine in any of the seafood restaurants.**
**
**
The Crocodile Farm**

The
famous crocodile farm is located on the outskirts of Miri. It is a 45-minute car drive to this location. You will find various
types of crocodiles resting in their oases. You will see several crocodile sculptures
scattered around the farm. Other animals like snakes, monkeys and
birds also inhabit this farm. All in all it will be a fun experience.

**
Canada Hill**

Bukit
Kanaan or the Canada Hill offers a panoramic view of the beautiful
city. It has an observation deck which is located on a hill park.
This spot also has the distinction of being the first location where oil
was discovered in Miri--the oil that changed the fortunes of the
city for the better.

**
Public Park**

The
public park in Miri is nestled 3 km away from the town's
center. It is situated on the road that goes to the airport. It is a
well maintained park and has majestic gardens and beautiful fountains. The
hanging bridge and sculptures are the major attractions of this park.
It also goes by the name Taman Awam. The observatory deck is designed in the form of an oil rig. The food court offers a variety of food
to choose from.

**
Taman Selera**

Located
on Brighton Beach, this is the best park for recreation in Miri. The star attraction is an open air food court filled with stalls and hawkers selling mouth watering food. The aroma of
the food just pulls you towards this market. From chicken wings in
BBQ sauce to noodles and local delicacies like chicken sataya and ice
kacang, you will have a wide variety to choose from. The park also
has a great play area for kids, equipped with swings and
slides, enough to keep the kids occupied. The park is great place to watch the
setting sun and to lounge around.
