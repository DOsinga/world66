---
title: "When to Go in Miri"
type: section
---

If you are getting ready to plan a vacation to **Miri, When to Go** is going to be the most important decision that you make. To make this decision you will need to know what is going on in Miri, to allow you to plan your vacation according to the things that you want to do. One of the great things about Miri is that the weather is not really a problem because Miri does not have 4 seasons; the weather is the same throughout the year.

**August to September**

Even though the weather is not a problem in Miri, you may not want to visit Miri during the months of August and September. The main reason that you want to stay away during these months is the smoky haze that blankets the city, which is mainly caused by forest fires. The clearing in Kalimantan can also contribute to the smoky haze that blankets the city.

**March to July**

If you enjoy diving, you will want to make sure that you visit Miri in March because you can experience some of the best dives. During the month of March, you can have visibility of up to 30 meters, which allows you to see quite a lot underwater. During the months of August and September, you can still experience the perfect diving conditions, but the haze is a huge factor.

**September to February**

These months are considered the rainy months; it is actually monsoon season, so many of the outdoor activities are not going to be as nice. For example, diving during monsoon season means you are going to have less visibility, it goes down to about 10 meters, but it can be less. Having that low of visibility, you will not experience as much of the beautiful conditions underwater.

**Weather**

The weather in Miri is normally warm and sunny throughout the year. However, you may also experience rain showers and sunny skies all in one day. All of the activities in Miri are outdoor activities, including exploring caves and visiting the public parks, the nice weather makes doing these activities year round easy.

**Tourist Season**

Since Miri is a place that you can visit year round, the busiest times of year is going to be when children are out of school, mostly the summer months in the Americas and Europe. If you don’t mind the crowds you can go during the months of May, June and July, but if you are looking for a more peaceful vacation you might want to wait until October or November.
