Sintok is a small town in Kedah state, near the Thai-Malaysian border. It is home to Universiti Utara Malaysia, the country's management university, and most visitors are here on campus business rather than tourism. The nearby town of Changlun is known locally for good Thai food, a reflection of the border's proximity.
