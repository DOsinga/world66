---
title: "Bars and Cafes"
type: section
---

Sitiawan's nightlife is low-key. Beer prices in Malaysia are high and the culture leans conservative, so don't expect a wild bar scene. The town's evening energy centers on the late-night food courts and a handful of cafes. Bread Forest Bakery on Jalan Sejahtera is a local favorite, with a quirky upper-floor cafe decorated with rope swings and good cakes.
