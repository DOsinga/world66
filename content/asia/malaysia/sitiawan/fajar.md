Fajar was Sitiawan's first proper shopping mall and for years served as the town's main retail and social hub. The complex houses a mix of local shops, a supermarket, and food outlets where you can grab a quick meal between errands. While newer malls have appeared as the area has grown, Fajar still draws a steady crowd of locals and remains a handy stop for everyday essentials.
