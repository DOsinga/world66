This is the Atoll in which the captial city ([Male](/asia/maldives/male)) and international airport reside. It is the most densely populated atoll and boast many tourist resorts. As busy as this sounds, even within viewing distance of [Male](/asia/maldives/male), there is plenty of peace and quiet to be found.
