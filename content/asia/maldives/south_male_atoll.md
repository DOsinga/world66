South Malé Atoll (Kaafu South, or simply South Malé) sits directly south of [Malé Atoll](/asia/maldives/male_atoll), separated by a channel. It's quieter than the north, with fewer resorts and a more relaxed atmosphere, but still within easy speedboat distance of the airport — most transfers run 45 minutes to an hour.

The atoll has some well-regarded dive sites and good snorkelling. It's a reasonable choice if you want proximity to Malé without being in the middle of the most trafficked part of the country.
