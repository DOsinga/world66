**Museums**

**New Museum**

Drop in at the blocky New Museum (modern art gallery) which has a good display of modern art. 

**Heritage Center (in the old law courts) which has exhibits on daily life pearl diving and falconry. **



**Bait al Quran**

Book lovers will enjoy the Bait al Quran with many early illuminated Koranic texts on display.  

**Museum of Pearl Diving**

The 'Museum of Pearl Diving' building is regarded as one of the most important and historic buildings in Bahrain. Its importance derives from being the first official center for the Bahrain Courts
