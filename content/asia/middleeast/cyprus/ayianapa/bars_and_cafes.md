---
title: "Bars and Cafes in Ayia napa"
type: section
---

Ayia Napa is known throughout the region as the creme de la creme of party towns. With more than 20 bars scattered throughout the town and along the coastlines, the variety of bars and cafes will keep any party goer from getting bored. Not only offering a diverse selection of clubs and bars, cafes are also numerous through Ayia Napa, making this town a perfect spot to have a cup of coffee after a long night partying or a quick luncheon with friends. Wherever you travel, Ayia Napa bars and cafes litter the streets, providing something for everyone. **

Bedrock Inn**

The Bedrock Inn is the place to go if you are seeking diversity. Ideal for a dinner with the kids (what kid wouldn't like to be served dinner by Fred Flintstone?), a night out dancing with the girls or an evening strutting your stuff on stage, Bedrock Inn is the most all-inclusive bar in Ayia-Napa. Karaoke is offered all night long, and even the kids can partake if it's prior to 11pm. More than four thousand songs are available to listen to, dance to and sing to at Bedrock Inn and drinks are never a worry with all of the experienced B-A-R-M-E-N on staff. Overall, a night at Bedrock Inn is not one you will want to miss. **

Starsky's **

Located across the street from Bedrock Inn is another favorite place to visit on "the strip." Starsky's is a favorite among locals and tourists alike, offering a large bar and dance floor holding more than 1,000 clubbers. The perfect club in Ayia Napa for dancing to commercially-based music, Starsky's is open until 5am and is definitely a place you will want to hit up before your night on the town is over. Starsky's (formerly known as Starsky and Hutch) is a perfect spot for the younger party crowd, as it's action packed and lets you dance all night long.**

Cafe Central**

Situated within the walls of the Napa Plaza Hotel, Cafe Central is a modern cafe that combines the cafe, bar and restaurant experience all in one inviting and sensual environment. Open year round, this cafe offers a huge variety of both savory and sweet dishes, ice cream, waffles and crepes and of course, the ever popular cup of coffee. With such a large variety of coffee beverages and special cold drinks, there is something to please everyone at Cafe Central. More suited for couples and adult groups, Cafe Central is the perfect spot to end the evening out on the town with an espresso and dessert.
