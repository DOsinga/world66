title: "Beaches"
type: section
---

Ayia Napa beaches are characterised by white or golden beaches and turquise waters. While most get packed in summer, you will still find some that are not too congested even then (eg. Ayia Thekla). You could combine your stay here with a bit of walking (in summer early in the morning or late in the afternoon) especially towards Cape Greko Park, the ragged coastline and the collection of coves and sea caves.

You can find in depth information on Ayia Napa beaches, as well as other natural attractions (trails, sea caves) on www.natureofcyprus.org.
