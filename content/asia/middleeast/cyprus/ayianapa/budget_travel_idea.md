Staying on a budget is important when going on a vacation, especially in an area that you're new to. In Ayia Nappa, budget travel is easy to stick to as this small town offers a variety of activities that cost little or no money but do not require you to scrimp on enjoyment. With a surprisingly large list of things to do for such a small town, Ayia Napa offers something for the young and old alike that will not cause you to break the bank

**Accommodations

**In Ayia Napa, there is a huge variety of first-class hotels situated right on the beach but unfortunately, these are not very budget-friendly. However, there are a few hotels that give you an amazing view of the sea costing at or under $100 a night. These hotels include the Tasia Maris Beach Hotel, the Grecian Park Hotel, Lotusiana Hotel Apartments, and the Alexia Hotel Apartments. Ayia Napa allows you to stay in clean, good quality accommodations with great service--all with an amazing view--at a decent price.

**Dining**
**
**There are a number of restaurants in Ayia Napa that give you a full taste of the local cuisine without costing an arm and a leg. Esperia offers amazing Middle Eastern food as well as fresh seafood. A great place for variety, a full meal costs an average of $10 to $20.
**
Things to Do
**

Waterworld is located on the outskirts of Ayia Napa and is a must if
traveling to this region. An award-winning water park featuring
countless heart-stopping rides, relaxing tubs, a river ride and a wave
pool, this attraction gives families a day packed full of fun all
without the need to travel around. Centrally located are 6 different
restaurants offering everything from fish and chips to pizza and
burgers. Affordable and fun, this adventure is one you will need to
experience for yourself.

If you're looking for a relaxing escape to a secluded beach, Palaces is the place to visit. Named such for it's uniquely shaped rock formations, Palaces is located in a quite cove that can only be reached by boat. Unique among beaches as it offers the privacy other public beaches do not offer and is the place to go for a free and stress-free afternoon.
