Along the Athinou Avenue shopping Arcade you will find dozens of restaurants with fixed menus and prices. There is normally no need to reserve tables.

**Restaurants**

**Yin Yang Chinese Restaurant**

Situated on the Larnaca - Agia Napa Road, along Palm Beach, chinese food, fresh prepared.

address: 19-24 Athens Street

tel: (04) 652 011

**Alasio**

International cuisine, nice atmosphere, live music every Saturday.

address: Larnaca - Dhekelia Road

tel: (04) 645 038

**Megalos Pefkos**

This small restaurant at the foot of the castle offers excellent seafood.

tel: 04 - 628 566
