A little out of the way from the hustle of Limassol in the village of Akrotiri is imbue Restaurant.

A family run business serving unique recipes developed by Helen the Chef and owner. They have **over 20 veggie meals** and it is the only restaurant in Cyprus that I have come across that has its own vegetarian menu. The Philadelphia Quorn is to die for and the home made STP or Sticky Toffee Pudding melts in the mouth.

They have a website at www.imbuefood.com well worth the 20 minute drive from Limassol
