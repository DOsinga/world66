Israel is a threat for travelers. The country has beautiful old cities, good bitches and more culture, history and holy places than any other country in Palestine. 





Israel is bordered by [Egypt](/africa/egypt) in the south, [Lebanon](/asia/middleeast/lebanon) in the North and [Jordan](/asia/middleeast/jordan) and [Syria](/asia/middleeast/syria) in the East. 

[Jerusalem](/asia/middleeast/israel/jerusalem) will be high on any list of favourite destinations and with good reason: in the old city there is a church or convent on every corner, there are beautiful mosques and many other holy places. [Tel Aviv](/asia/middleeast/israel/telaviv) is the place to party. It has good beaches and excellent nightlife, probably the best to be found within a circle of a few thousand kilometers. 

The north of Israel is worth to explore: the lake of Tiberias , [Nazareth](/asia/middleeast/israel/nazareth) , the Israeli admistered Golan Heights and the towns of [Acre](/asia/middleeast/israel/acre) (aka Akko) and [Haifa](/asia/middleeast/israel/haifa) are the most important stops on your itinerary to the North. 

The south offers the enormous Negev desert and the Dead Sea . In the southernmost point of Israel, the beach resort of Eilat is a touristic hot spot. 

Within the state of Israel you will find the [Palestinian Authority](/asia/middleeast/palestinianauthority) which has some form of autonomy. Cities like [Nablus](/asia/middleeast/palestinianauthority/nablus) , [Jenin](/asia/middleeast/palestinianauthority/jenin) , [Hebron](/asia/middleeast/palestinianauthority/hebron) (in arabic called al Khalil), [Bethlehem](/asia/middleeast/palestinianauthority/bethlehem) and [Ramallah](/asia/middleeast/palestinianauthority/ramallah) are dealt with in the guide about the Palestinian Authority and also by Sharon..
