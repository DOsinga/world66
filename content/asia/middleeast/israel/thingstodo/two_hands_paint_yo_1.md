Two Hands is a full-service Paint Your Own Pottery Studio, located in Pardes Chana-Karkur, that offers visitors to Israel the chance to design their own souvenirs and personalized gifts for taking home to friends and family (help with Hebrew spelling is always available). The studio stocks a wide range of Judaica and other ready-made pottery items for visitors to choose from and uses only quality, non-toxic materials. After firing, delivery of the finished items can be arranged either in Israel or abroad.

The studio is located in Karkur, just east of Caesarea and south of Zichron Ya'akov, and can accommodate groups up to 30-35 people. The large studio has lots of natural light and is air conditioned, and can host many types of events such as birthday parties, anniversaries and bridal showers and workplace team building events. Cindy, the owner, originally from Spring Valley, NY, has been in Israel since 1982 and speaks English and Hebrew. She and her staff keeps up-to-date with all the latest pottery decorating techniques and accessories. In addition to painting pottery, the studio also offers clay handbuilding workshops and has two pottery wheels. Parking is available in the lot next to the building. The studio's hours vary from season to season so it is best to call ahead and reserve a space.
