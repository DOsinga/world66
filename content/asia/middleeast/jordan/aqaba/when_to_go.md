title: "When to Go in Aqaba"
type: section
---

Jordan is a country rich in culture and history.** Aqaba when to go** is crucial to making your holiday everything you want it to be. The weather conditions in Aqaba might be different than what your used to so plan your trip accordingly.

**Spring**

The spring months offer the best weather. The climate is warm and very comfortable. April is the best month to travel if you want to see all the flowers in bloom. March can be a little cold and rainy but still a beautiful time to visit. You will also find several festivals going on in Aqaba during the spring months.

**Autumn**

August through October is another good time to visit. You will find the temperatures to be warm but not unpleasant. The Autumn months also offer a wide selection of travel discounts. Planing your trip in advance will offer you the best discounts.

**Winter**

The winter months are very cold in Aqaba and snow is not unheard of. If you do decide to travel during the winter months make sure to bring plenty of warm clothing. Winter is not a popular time to travel but it can afford you excellent travel discounts.

**Summer **

The summer months are extremely hot and very humid. If you decide to travel during the summer months bring plenty of sunscreen and clothes that will protect you from the sun. Keep in mind that you will need to stay extremely hydrated during your stay in Aqaba. The summer months do offer the most festivals such as the Jerash Festival.
