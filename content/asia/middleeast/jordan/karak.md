title: "Karak"
type: location
---

Karak is the a crusader castle and a museum. Built about 1 km above sea level the castle boasts an impressive view as well as a moat lookouts and chapel. All of these are in quite good condition. Karak is 90 km south of [Amman](/asia/middleeast/jordan/amman) and can be visited on the way to [Petra](/asia/middleeast/jordan/petra).
