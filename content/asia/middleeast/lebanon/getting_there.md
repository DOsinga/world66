Air

The national airline is *Middle East Airlines (MEA). *

Approximate flight time

From London to Beirut is 4 hours 40 minutes.

International Airport:

Beirut International Airport is 16km (10 miles) south of the city (travel time – 20 minutes). A bus service operates to the city centre (07:00 - 20:00) leaving every 30 minutes. Of course, taxis are also available. Airport facilities includes a tourist information desk, duty-free shops, post office (07:00 - 20:00) restaurants and bank/bureau de change (24-hour). The airport has recently been redeveloped.

Sea

Main international ports are Beirut, Tripoli, Jounieh, Tyre and Sidon. Cruise ships are available from Jounieh. The sea connection between the Cypriot port of Larnaca and Jounieh in Lebanon is often closed and travellers considering that route are advised to check with The Ministry of Tourism or the Embassy.

Rail

There are no passenger services operating at present.

Road

Best international routes are via Turkey and Aleppo–Homs and Lattakia in Syria along the north–south coastal road and also the Beirut–Damascus trunk road. Bus services are available from Europe. For further details check The Ministry of Tourism.
