The best resource for sights, hotels, restaurants, bars, what to do and see

Ibra is a growing provincial capital. There are plenty of collapsing "burj" towers on the surrounding mountaintops. They can be rewarding hikes in the winter

To its north is the small town of Al Yahmadi, which has a renovated fort with apparently irregular hours. Al Yahmadi also has impressive date palm groves to walk among, which are watered by the "falaj" water channels.
