---
title: "Budget Travel Ideas in Masirah Island"
type: section
---

A small island located of the coast of Oman, Masirah Island doesn’t offer the typical tourist attractions, such a cruises in the Gulf, safaris and shopping in the larger cities, a visitor to Oman would usually expect. Being so isolated, this island makes the ideal vacation spot for some but may be a bore for others. If you are seeking a quiet getaway to relax and enjoy the scenery this is the perfect spot for you and your family. If seeking an exciting, action packed adventure, activities on the mainland would be better suited for you. Whatever you chose to do, activities on Masirah Island are cheap, if not free, and are bound to entertain.

**Surfing on Masirah Island

**

Any sports enthusiast can appreciate this coastline for its amazing swells ranging from 3 to 5 feet. Tourists, as well as the sports fanatic, can appreciate this incredible display of 'man versus nature.' Surfing is completely free if you bring your own surf or body board, and surfing on Masirah Island offers tourists the chance to partake in or just sit back and appreciate this impressive sport. If you don’t own a board of your own, there are surf shops in Muscat where you can rent one prior to taking the ferry over to Masirah Island.

**Camping on Masirah Island
**

Another free activity that anyone can participate in while visiting Masirah Island is the ever popular pastime of camping. Roughing it is by far the best way of exploring the beautiful beaches, serene deserts and stunning rocky cliffs of Masirah Island. This allows tourists the closest connection with nature whether watching the sunset or fishing in the Gulf for dinner. If seeking an experience of solitude and a way to “get away from it all” camping anywhere on Masirah Island is the vacation for you!

**Affordable Accommodations

**

Masirah Island offers tourists 4 hotels, 3 of which are very affordable if you are trying to stretch a buck. The Danat-Al-Khaleej Hotel, Serabis Hotel and Masirah Hotel all offer standard amenities at a reasonable nightly rate. Located at different spots along the beach, all 3 hotels are located within walking distance of activities the island has to offer.

**Sea-Turtle Viewing

**

Also a free activity, you can view 4 species of sea turtles such as the loggerhead and green sea turtle come to the beaches of Masirah Island to lay their eggs. About all year there is something to see on the beach, whether it be these magnificent creatures nesting or the tiny turtles hatching and scurrying towards the surf. An amazing natural phenomenon, it is an experience you won’t want to miss.
