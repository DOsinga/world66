---
title: "Top 5 Must Do's in Musandam peninsula"
type: section
---

Musandam Peninsula is a geographical enclave of Oman that is cut off from the main part of the country by the UAE. It can be reached from Muscat by a ferry that's said to be the world's fastest. Here are the top 5 must do's for those who visit this enchanting place.

**1. Take a Drive**

There is a highway running the length of the western part of the peninsula. Take a drive through it to see the scenic mountains on one side and the beautiful sea and fjords on the other.

**2. Take a Cruise**

Hire a dhow (traditional fishing boat) and take a cruise along the northern end of the peninsula to view the splendid fjords from the best possible angle. Also, keep an eye out for dolphins that are attracted to the dhows. The dhows offer a leisurely way of exploring the fjords. For a more luxurious and exclusive experience, visitors can hire the Charlotte, which promises increased privacy and pampering on your exploratory pursuits.

**3. Go Snorkeling**

Take your snorkeling gear along and snorkel off the cruise boats to see the great underwater treasures of this peninsula. There is nothing compared to the beauty of the serene waters in this area. Visitors can discover the rich ecology of the region and view its spectacular marine life.

**4. Dive**

You can also take your diving gear with you and see the underwater sights that the sea off the peninsula has to offer. There is great looking marine life to be spotted in the sea off the peninsula. If you are a novice at diving, take a guided tour that's on offer. Visitors will enjoy the thrilling experience.

**5. Have a Campfire**

Stop the dhow at one of the remote and unspoiled beaches and have a relaxing campfire amidst the breathtaking coast of the region. The night sky provides the perfect backdrop for an adventure-packed campfire, where visitors can even cook their meals under the huge canopy of the stars.

Apart from these must do's you should try to visit a small fishing village. The population is very friendly and you will get to see their lifestyle and experience their culture from up close. If you're in Oman, a visit to the Musandam Peninsula is a must. The entire trip can be extremely pleasant and relaxing.
