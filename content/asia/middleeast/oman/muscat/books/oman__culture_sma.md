This book is an essential guide to
enjoy the beauty of the city in a much better way with a clear
understanding of the country, its culture and values. The book covers
almost each and every aspect of the history and rich cultural values of
the country over a journey of 168 pages. The book is published by the
Kuperard publishing house and the latest revised edition was published
in the year 2009 on March 24, so the book contains the latest
information about different places and all the historical facts and
statistics are up to date.

The dimensions of the book are very compact
(6.5 x 4.4 x 0.6 inches) as compared to the features and descriptions
of the tourist spots carried by the book inside it. The book provides
important information about the culture, history, politics and social
life of the country.

The best part of the book is the information
about the things to do and things not to do while visiting Oman, which
enables the visitor to make his stay in the country even more pleasant
and colorful without any hassles. The major tourist places are
described briefly in the book, enabling the user to find the places of
interest before visiting the country. The book provides illuminating
insight into the life style, dining and living habits of the natives of
the nation.

If you are visiting for a business purpose or just to
add new dimensions to your world travel, this book can really help you
in achieving a better understanding of the nation. Knowing a place
before visiting it is the best way to connect to the place and its
natives and this book is really capable of doing the same.
