This **Sohar travel guide** offers information about local attractions such as parks and castles. Sohar is a developed city which provides tourists with all the amenities necessary to have a comfortable stay. It is situated 200 kilometers from Dubai and it was once the capital of Oman. It began as a fishing town, and Sohar's Industrial Port has resulted in the growth of the city into an economic hub. Copper also makes up a large part of the economy and has contributed to the wealth and development of the city. Entwined with its seafaring history it is believed that the legendary Sinbad was born in Sohar. Over the years, Sohar has also been referred to as Majid and Omana.

**Attractions**

Escape the busy city and relax in one of Sohar's parks. There is a park situated in Al Humbar and another in Sallan. Take a stroll through the park to enjoy the scenery and to get some exercise.

Sohar has a variety of shopping options including traditional markets. Going to a traditional market is a memorable experience and is a must do when visiting the area. There is a market in the heart of the town which has many stalls to explore. Another market can be found near Sohar Castle. Shopping malls sell a variety of products and two options are Safeer Mall and Centerpoint. These shopping malls sell a variety of brands and there are restaurants where you can stop for refreshments.

Sohar Regional Sports Complex has a stadium where you can watch football, and the Sohar Club runs from here. Sohar Plaza Cinema has good entertainment and screens a variety of movies. Sohar Castle is a prominent tourist attraction. Construction on the castle began in the 13th century. Portuguese rulers were driven from the castle and it became an administrative center. Inside the castle is a museum which gives visitors more information on Sohar.

**Accommodations**

Accommodation options include Crowne Plaza Sohar, Sohar Beach hotel, Al Wadi Hotel and green oasis Hotel. An alternative to hotels are apartments that cater to tourists. These modern hotels offer all the facilities to make visitors comfortable, and they make a good base from which you can explore the city.

**Food and Drink**

If you want an alcoholic drink, head to Sohar Beach Hotel. For a good meal there are a variety of restaurants to choose from, serving traditional local dishes. Try a shwarma at one of the local restaurants. It is possible to buy international food such as Indian, Lebanese or American, and there are chains of KFC and Pizza Hut.
