The Palestinians are slowly working their way to an independent state. Right now they have a series of towns on the West-Bank that are under Palestinian control, such as [Ramallah](/asia/middleeast/palestinianauthority/ramallah), [Jenin](/asia/middleeast/palestinianauthority/jenin), [Nablus](/asia/middleeast/palestinianauthority/nablus) and Jericho as well as towns under joint Israeli-Palestinian control. Furthermore the Gaza strip is almost totally under PNA control.

 These different cities offer quite a few tourist attractions, as well as the possibility to feel part of World Politics. The things you see when traveling here are exactly the images you know from CNN: Israeli soldiers, heavily armed, Orthodox settlers, angry young Palestinians. 

 If you have the chance to speak to the Palestinians you have a unique opportunity to find out about the people who are part of this struggle, who suffer from the quarrels between politicians and the radical ideas of extremists. Recently hope grew, but right now things are looking worse again. A longtime solution is not within sight.
