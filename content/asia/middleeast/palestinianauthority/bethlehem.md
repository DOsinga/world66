---

Bethlehem is a major attraction within the Palestinian Authority. People flock in to see the Nativity Church, the Milk Grotto and other places that have to do with the fact that Jesus was born here.

For the year 2000 a lot of money was invested to make the town of Bethlehem look good. European donor countries invested heavily in the overhaul.

Bethelehem is located halfway between [Jerusalem](/asia/middleeast/israel/jerusalem) and [Hebron](/asia/middleeast/palestinianauthority/hebron) and can be seen as a daytrip from either.
