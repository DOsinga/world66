This museum is located in a old (i.e. early twentieth century) restored house with a traditional wind tower, which was used as a natural way of cooling buildings in the days before air conditioning. The exhibits are largely composed of folk items and artefacts from Qatar's pre-oil days. Admission is QR 1. Telephone 436008 for more details on opening times etc.
