A great book, written by an American Paramedic that was on the personal medical team for King Abdullah. This book is a rare insight into the country. It is well writen and is a cannot put down type of book.

The best book about Saudi Arabia I have ever read. A must for anyone interested in the medical field or Saudi Arabia.

NOTE: This book is banned by the ministry of information in Saudi Arabia.
