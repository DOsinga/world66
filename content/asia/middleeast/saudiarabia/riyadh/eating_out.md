**Assaraya Turkish Restaurant**

For full-bodied Turkish fare, visit Assaraya Turkish Restaurant located at Olaya Road. The restaurant is one of the most popular eateries for Turkish food and has been serving delighted customers for the last 20 years. The food is interesting and the service is good on weekdays but on weekends the packed eatery leaves little room for personalized service. Try the lip-smacking clay pot lamb or the tasty meat kebabs with soft pita bread. The restaurant has a good vegetarian menu featuring dishes made with chickpea and wheat. All dishes are reasonably priced and an average meal will cost between $30 and $40.

**Golden Royal Chinese Restaurant**

The sprawling and up-market Chinese eatery is located on the King Fahd Road and sports a glitzy Asian decor, complete with golden wall papers, embossed dragon motifs and aquariums. The food is good although slightly overpriced. Visitors flock here more for the dining atmosphere than the cuisine. A "must try," however, is the tantalizingly flavored terriyaki chicken. Fish and chips and dim sums are also recommended. An average meal per person costs approximately SR 100.

**Mosaic Mediterranean Restaurant**

This fine-dining restaurant located in the Marriott on Al Matther Street, serves an array of grilled and Mediterranean foods in an intimate, dimly lit and tastefully decorated facility. There are over 25 appetizers and several grilled main course dishes to select from. The sea food and salmon entrees are quite good as are the shrimp salad and hot dessert puddings. The buffet is priced at approximately $50 per person.

Riyadh features a variety of restaurants serving good food at reasonable prices--for an expensive city.
