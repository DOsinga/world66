** Itinerary**

** Ephesus Daily Tour / By Plane **

** 06:00 ** Pick up from your hotel early in the morning
** 07:45 ** Fly from Istanbul to Izmir
** 08:15 ** Arrive in Izmir and airport transfer
** 09:30 ** Full Day Ephesus Tour

** Visiting:**
The ruins of the Temple of Artemis,one of the seven ancient wonders of the world. Then on, to visit Ephesus Ancient City, the best preserved Greco-Roman city in the world. Here you will explore the Agora, Odeon, Curettes Street, public toilets, Celsius Library, Hadrian Temple, Roman Baths, Grand Theatre, Marble Street. After a lunch break depart for the House of Virgin Mary and visit the chapel where she is said to have spent her last day. End of the tour.
** 18:00 ** Airport transfer
** 20:00 ** Fly from Izmir to Istanbul
** 20:45 ** Arrive in Istanbul and transfer back to your hotel.
