**Duration: **04 Days / 03 Nights
**Run: **Everyday

**DAY 01 – Arrival in Istanbul
**Upon clearing customs at Istanbul Airport, you will be met by one of our representatives staff who will arrange the transfer to your hotel. You will have the rest of the afternoon/evening to spend at your leisure exploring the sights of Istanbul.

**DAY 02 – Bosphorus Cruise and Dolmabahce Palace Tour**
Your day will start with journey along the Golden Horn, the arm of the Bosphorus peninsula which separates the old and new cities of Istanbul. Follow the traces of history in Balat at the Fener Orthodox Patriarchate, proceeding on to visit the Metal Church of St Stephen the Bulgarian, Ahrida Synagouge, Eyup Sultan Mosque and see the 21km long City Walls of Istanbul. Next stop, the Egyptian Market (Spice Bazaar), with different aromas of various spices From here, join a cruise along the Bosphorus on one of the iconic ferries of Istanbul giving you the chance to see the two bridges that link Europe and Asia. Disembark the ferry and enjoy a trip over the Intercontinental Bosphorus Bridge to Camlica Hill, the highest hill in Asiatic Istanbul to enjoy breathtaking views of both shores. Travel back to Europe with a stop at Dolmabahce Palace which was the residence of the late royal Ottoman family with 285 rooms, 46 halls, 6 baths and 68 lavatories.

**DAY 03 – Ottoman and Byzantine Relics Tour
**Start the day with a visit to Hagia Sophia basilica, built during the reign of Emperor Theodosius and burned down in the fire of Nika Revolt in 532 A.D., during the reign of Justinian. Continue to the elegant Sultan Ahmet Mosque (with its heavenly six minarets) and the ancient Byzantine Hippodrome (once the scene of chariot races and the centre of Byzantine civic life), where only three remain: The Obelisk of Theodosius, the bronze Serpentine Column, and the Column of Constantine. Following on, a visit to Istanbul’s bustling Grand Bazaar, exploring a labyrinth of streets and passages housing more than 4,000 shops. After lunch, proceed to Topkapi Palace, the maze of opulent buildings that was the center of the Ottoman Empire between the 15th and 19th centuries. Finally complete your tour with a visit to the Rustem Pasha Mosque, famous for its exquisite Iznik tiles, set in beautiful floral and geometric designs. Overnight in Istanbul.

**DAY 04 – Transfer to Istanbul International Airport
**After check-out from your hotel, we will arrange for the transfer to Istanbul International Airport for your return or onward flight
