One of the main reasons I like this place is because they have a little room set aside for non-smokers. The food is very good too, and service is fast and friendly. Despite the fact that pictures of Mecca line the walls, the guys here are very nice. If you have time, I can thoroughly recommend the Iskender Kebap; if not, a half bread doner with home-made tomato sauce and pickles will fill the gap. It’s not expensive at all, and it’s one of the most popular ‘cheapie’ places in Beyoglu to eat out at night as well.
