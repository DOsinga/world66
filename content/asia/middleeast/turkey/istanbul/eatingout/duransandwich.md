There’s no waiting here at all, the sandwiches at Duran are all pre-prepared and displayed in the window so you can choose what you want before you even walk in the door. Everything is always fresh and the turnover of food out of this place at lunchtime is incredible! There are two tiny tables to sit down and eat if you want to, but it does get cramped in there when it’s busy. Service is fast and friendly, though often the staff are so busy serving that they barely have time to look at the customers. It’s fast, good food with lots to choose from and it won’t cost you an arm and a leg either.
