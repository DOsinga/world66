Polonezkoy is a touristic Polish village in
[Istanbul](/asia/middleeast/turkey/istanbul/festivals). Polonezkoy maintains the friendship and hospitality between Turkey
and Poland for more than 160 years. **Traditional Polonezkoy Cherry Festival** is
organized annually in this heaven town every 1st & 2nd weekedd fo June. During the festival, Polish folk
dancers coming from Poland preform shows. Resitals and concerts are being held
by the popular artists and many activities take place at the town. All Istanbul
residents and travelers are invited. Participation is free of charge.
