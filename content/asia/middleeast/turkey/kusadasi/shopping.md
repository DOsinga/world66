Gallery Elit was born in the city of Kayseri, oldest capital of Cappadocia region in Central Anatolia, which is recognized worldwide for its quality of handmade carpets. We pride ourselves in providing largest collection of unique authentic Anatolian handmade carpets & kilims now also in our Kusadasi showroom.
The Gallery Elit is a family business, dating back third generations. We expert on finest quality new and antique hand woven authentic Anatolian carpets and kilims made in the finest traditions, using lustrous hand spun wools, rich natural colors and a myriad of intricate memorised designs and individual artistry.
Anatolian carpets and kilims are most valuable collections of museums and collectors in the world. Today, world museum’s exhibit as their most important and valuable works of art the carpets woven in Anatolia, beginning from the Seljuk period and continuing with the Ottoman Empire.
Dreams of the Anatolian women combines with fine quality wool, with natural colors from vine leaves, wild mint, walnut shell, berries, bushes, roots, bark and minerals with the traditional vegetable dyes and coloring using the techniques known only to the generational tribes. Each carpet and kilim is a direct expression of the unique individual skill and artistry of the weaver. It is impossible to find an exact copy of the particular carpet or kilim. Nomadic females explaining their desires, happiness, fears and dreams woven into carpets and kilim's which the legacy of Anatolian past.
