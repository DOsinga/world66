### [Jackies Bistro Cafe Bar](/asia/middleeast/turkey/marmaris/eating_out/jackies_bistro_caf)

Behind Cheers bar (which is on the sea front) you'll find Jackies Bistro Cafe Bar, they do the best breakfast in Marmaris. You can get a full english belly busting breakfast for 5 1/2 liras and a half breakfast for 4 1/2.

The main menu has a fantastic mix of Turkish and English dishes, although the meatballs and lamb kebabs are to die for!!

They have high chairs and a childrens menu for people with families.

The restuarant is only small, but what it lacks in size it make up for in atmosphere, the staff are really friendly, getting up and dancing, keeping us [more..](/asia/middleeast/turkey/marmaris/eating_out/jackies_bistro_caf)
