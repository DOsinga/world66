For fantastic food and great entertainment check out the Happy Brothers Restaurant and Fun Pub.

Inside the Club Portobello complex in Marmaris the food is top quality. Excellent presentation and amazing taste.

The Turkish dishes are awesome especially the Ottoman Kebab and Mix Grill , but they also do a large range of Top Quality Steaks including Rokfort, Pepper and Plank steaks, there is a Chinese Menu of nearly 100 dishes plus English, Mexican and a Kids Menu!

The staff are always smiling and friendly and the entertainment top quality. Billy, Casper and Gino are legends amongst the Portobello guests!

Get in a taxi anywhere in Marmaris and ask for Club Portobello, as soon as you walk through Reception you will see the Restaurant
