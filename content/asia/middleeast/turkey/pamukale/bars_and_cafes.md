In a city popular for its hot springs, the **Pamukale bars and cafes **are known for their hospitality and amazing food. The city of Pamukale, located in the southwest part of Turkey, is historically known at the &ldquo;cotton castle.&rdquo; Visitors looking for a bite or a drink should stay by Mehmet Akif Ersoy Cd, a road by the main drag of the city. Many of the cafes and bars are located by this road.

#### **Enjoying Bars in Pamukale**

****The bars in Pamukale serve Ayran, the national drink of Turkey. Ayran is made of yogurt, water and some spices. It is popular in the Middle East. While not an alcoholic drink, visitors should try it to get a flavor of Turkey. Turkey is also developing a wine industry. To bring business to the area, the bars are pushing wine as a way to attract visitors to area. Some of the spas now have bars featuring local vintages. The wine has received awards and other recognition.

#### **Cafes Pamukale Style**

Cafes in Pamukale are the traditional places for many of the residents. They are places for people to unwind and catch up with each other. Most of the cafes are family-owned, so there is a lack of westernized chains. While getting a cup of a coffee, cafes are a great place for customers to check their email and make their plans. Most of the cafes now have Wi-Fi for visitors. The cafes in the area sell borek, the traditional Turkish pancakes.
