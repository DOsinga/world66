**Minibuses and Taxis
**

However, they are not widely used as a form of transportation throughout the city, as Pamukale is truly very small—about 600m by 800m. **Walking
**

Walking is definitely the best transportation option in Pamukale. The town is really small, and the accommodations and restaurants are concentrated in its center, so you really won&rsquo;t have to walk distances longer than 50m to 100m. The streets are safe enough, the people are friendly, and you have no reasons to be afraid to travel by foot even when it is late at night.
