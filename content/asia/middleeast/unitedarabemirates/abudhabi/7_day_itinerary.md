title: "7 Day Itinerary in Abu dhabi"
type: section
---

Abu Dhabi has a mysterious air surrounding it. There is so much to see and do that time always feels like a constraint. Read on and get ready to indulge in a week long love affair with Abu Dhabi by using this **Abu Dhabi itinerary**.

**Day 1 – It's all about the food
**

Abu Dhabi is a food lovers paradise. Not just local cuisine, but also for cuisine from around the world. The Arabian hospitality is reflected in the way the food is served and the effort put into it. Dive into a sea of options, ranging from seafood to steaks, Indian curries to Arabian meat preparations. Alcohol is forbidden in most of the Muslim countries, but not in Abu Dhabi. You can hit the various pubs or bars and soak in the nightlife that Abu Dhabi has to offer.

**Day 2 – Outdoor Activities
**

Enjoy the mornings of Abu Dhabi by playing golf. You can charter boats and indulge in some on board fishing activity. Follow it up by visiting the cultural landmarks in the city. The downtown area houses Qasr Al Hosn. The Cultural foundation is also located there. The heritage village at the Breakwater is a great place to buy souvenirs. Unwind over dinner in any of the restaurants in that area.

**Day 3 – The Mountains
**

Head off to the Hajar mountains. Enjoy the majestic view of the Emirates from the top most point of Jebel Hafeet located in the Hajar mountains. Once you are finished on the mountains you can head straight to the National museum in Al Ain on your way back.

**Day 4 – Desert Safari
**

Experience the true desert life by taking a desert safari. This is going to be an extremely adventurous event. You will be treated to authentic Arabic cuisine and belly dancing.

**Day 5 – The Beach
**

Head off to the beach to enjoy your day in the water. Dedicate the day to experiencing the beach life in Abu Dhabi. You can head over to the Lulu island or swim away in the Corniche. There are free boat rides available.

**Day 6 – Shopping
**

Abu Dhabi has some good shopping malls and you will find almost everything you need here. From the top brands to some unique handcrafted stuff, you will be able to get some great bargains at most shopping centers. The Marina Mall is a popular spot for designer wear.

**Day 7 - Gold
**

Buy some gold jewelry from the Gold Souks. Gold in Abu Dhabi is cheaper than in most western countries, so use the opportunity to pick up some exquisite jewelry here.
