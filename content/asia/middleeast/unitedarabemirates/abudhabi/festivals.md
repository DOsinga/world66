---
title: "Festivals in Abu dhabi"
type: section
---

The number of lavish **Abu Dhabi Festivals** that are held every
year is just unbelievable. The wealthy and incredibly luxurious city in
the United Arab Emirates is always trying to promote tourism and
business, and holding spectacular festivals, especially those promoting
the arts or the buying and selling of goods, is 1 of the many ways
that Abu Dhabi draws in the international crowd. If you travel to Abu
Dhabi during 1 of these lavish festivals, you will certainly enjoy yourself.

**Abu Dhabi International Motor Show**

This December festival that occurs every other year and is probably the
most popular in Abu Dhabi. It draws in massive crowds from not only all
over the Middle East, but from all over the world. This extravagant
event features every kind of vehicle from heavy motor vehicles to
go-karts. It also displays all sorts of accessories, high tech gadgets,
lubricants, and garages for the cars. Hundreds of manufacturers and
marketing agents from various parts of the globe attend the motor show,
so it is a great place not just for car lovers but for people who want
to do some networking as well. Tickets are 10 Dirhams each; it is
highly recommended to get tickets as early as possible.

**Abu Dhabi Shopping Festival**

The month of March brings about Abu Dhabi's huge festival dedicated
to shopping. This is one of the most popular activities for tourists in the
city. Throughout all of the area's comfortable, air conditioned malls
are festive decorations to entice shoppers in to look at all of the
unique products on sale. Discounts are offered and prizes can be won
during the extent of the festival. It is the perfect time to buy
clothing, perfumes, electronics, carpets, tools, wallets and much more.

**Other Festivals**

The Abu Dhabi International Jewelry and Watch Show takes place in
September or October, depending on the year. The Al Ain International
Aerobatic Show at the end of January features a world class display of
flying and stunts. For a taste of the arts, travel to Abu Dhabi during
the Middle East International Film Festival, the Al Ain International
Music Festival in March that features classical music, the International
Fine Art and Antiques Fair or the Abu Dhabi Salsa and Jazz Festival.
Lovers of literature will enjoy the Abu Dhabi International Book Fair
while those who prefer a more traditional look at the city will
appreciate the annual camel races. This modern city really has a little
something for everyone when it comes to festivals.
