---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment in Al Ain"
type: section
---

Al Ain is the Garden City of the Gulf and has a steady stream of visitors. As such, it has many places where one can eat out and enjoy oneself to the hilt.

**Horse & Jockey
**

This is the only pub in town and is a typical sports bar. It serves standard bar food. It is a casual place and good for a beer and a quick bite. You can take your friends and colleagues here for a quick snack or meal.

**Casa Romana
**

Go here if you are in the mood for Italian food. The food is good, and the menu offers all the classics. They also have a vegetarian platter. Ideal if you are entertaining expatriates.

**Min Zaman
**

If you want to taste authentic Arabic Lebanese food, visit this place. They offer large and delightful portions. Moreover, there is onstage entertainment with talented belly dancers. You must experience at least one dinner at this wonderful place. You and your guests will enjoy yourselves at Min Zaman. It's a true reflection of Arab culture. The array of hard drinks and liquor is fairly good, but it’s the entire ambiance and experience that scores.

**Bravo Tapas Bar
**

Bravo Tapas Bar is inspired by the Spanish and serves light tapas and drinks. The bar offers a very comfortable atmosphere for casual drinks. It has a classic menu of tapas that are done very well. The tapas are made from imported Spanish ingredients. Bravo is a great place to spend time with friends or business colleagues. It is an informal place and one can relax in the laid back atmosphere of this bar.

**Paco's Bar
**

Paco's Bar provides classic Tex-Mex dishes such as fajitas and burritos. The bar is well-stocked with a variety of tequilas and other drinks. Besides, Paco's offers live entertainment and huge plasma screens that play live sports events. It stays open until 1 am daily. You can also have lunch or dinner there apart from a drink. It's best to dress smart-casual. This is a great place to take business colleagues to.

**
Leisure Chill Out Cafe
**

Leisure Chill Out Café is a great place for sandwiches, breakfast, coffee or drinks. There is a fantastic outdoor patio for dining. The ambiance is very laid back You can meet up with friends here and try their coffee and shisha.

Apart from these forms of entertainment, one can also take in a desert safari. This is a great way to explore the area around Al Ain.
