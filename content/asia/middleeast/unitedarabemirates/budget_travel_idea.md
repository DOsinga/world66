title: "Budget Travel Ideas in United Arab Emirates"
type: section
---

The **United Arab Emirates Budget Travel** guide will help you
take advantage of all the sights and scenes that the country has to
offer. This luxurious land of the Arabs is a must see destination. Even
though it might seem like an expensive affair, you will be surprised
with the things you can do in the UAE on a shoestring budget.

**Getting There**

Getting to United Arab Emirates is now an easy task. Several
airlines fly to this destination. There are several tour operators that
offer discount tickets to the UAE. Etihad Airlines connects to all the
major cities in UAE and offers good deals. In case you are traveling
from Saudi Arabia or Oman you can travel by road to the UAE. From any
other country in the Middle East you can avail the inexpensive bus
service to the UAE.

**Stay**

Dubai is the star attraction in the UAE. This city is compared to
the Las Vegas of The United States of America. Everything about this
city is expensive. Among all the splendor you will find budget hotels. Al Reem
is one such hotel that will give you a chance to experience Dubai and
other attractions in UAE at an affordable price. The hotel is well-maintained and is tourist-friendly. Similarly all major cities in the
UAE have budget class hotels and hostels.

**Eating Out**

For a budget traveler, UAE offers several eating out options. The
street food, be it the mighty chicken roll called Shawarma or the fried
chicken, is a treat to the pallet and easy on the wallet. UAE is filled
with such fast food joints that offer delicious food at affordable
rates.

**Getting Around**

The taxi service in the UAE is fairly inexpensive. You can tour the
UAE in the local taxi. You can save a lot as the local cab drivers
prove to be enthusiastic guides as well. Renting a car, so that you can
drive yourself around, also proves to be economical. The routes are
well marked; hence the chances of getting lost are minimal. UAE
also has a fleet of low cost airlines that connect to all the major
cities.

**Sightseeing**

The UAE is filled with tour operators fighting to get business. As a
result, you will be able to find an operator that gives you the best
value for your money. Unlike other tourists spots, availing the service
of a tour operator in UAE will not cause much damage to your wallet.
