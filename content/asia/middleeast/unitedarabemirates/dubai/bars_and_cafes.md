title: "Bars and Cafes in Dubai"
type: section
---

Dubai is one of the seven emirates that unite to form United
Arab Emirates. Dubai is fast developing, and is a completely
independent city-state. It has gained popularity for its trade sector and
especially tourism. If you are looking for a destination that is full of
partying, sunbathing, sporting events and shopping, then Dubai is a perfect
place to visit. This partying destination is just 5 hours from Europe, 3 hours from
most parts of Middle East, sub-continent of India and the near East. Here's a look at various **Dubai bars and cafes**.

**Chelsea Arms Pub******

Chelsea Arms Pub serves you the best, refreshing serving of
vegetarian and non-vegetarian salads that tantalize your taste buds. You will
find everything you could expect in this traditional English Pub. Champagne,
pale ale, gin and tonic, whiskey and more are available for your parched
and sand-swept thirst. There are many appetizers that are meant to quench your
hunger, such as buffalo tenders, salad, sandwiches, nachos and chicken Satays.
It's guaranteed that nobody leaves hungry from Chelsea Arms Pub.

**Café Arabesque******

Park Hyatt's Café Arabesque has the wide variety of Middle
Eastern cuisine to serve. It has the traditional taste in a spacious café, with
perfect Arabic patterns dominating bright interiors that welcome you. You will
feel spoiled with five bullet displays and additional menus for dinner and
lunch. You must not miss out on the mouth watering kebabs that are cooked in a wood
fire.

**Leaves******

Leaves is a
styled tearoom that is located in Grosvenor House West Marina Beach by Le
Meridien. You can enjoy a relaxed atmosphere in an oriental décor with some amazing
tea. Enjoy your sips of tea on the terrace while having a
breathtaking view of the city.

**Deli Café******

Deli Café is located right in the middle of the shopping
court of Grosvenor House West Marina Beach by le Meridien. You can relax at
this café if you get tired while shopping. The coffee and sweets are delicious, and there are several snacks and beverages to choose from at this fine cafe.

**Lobby Lounge ******

Lobby Lounge at The Taj Palace is popular for its exclusive
cakes and coffee. You can also find some snacks and some light in between
meals. You will be amazed to see the best in anything that is related to caffeine. This is popular among tired tourists for its great service and settings.
