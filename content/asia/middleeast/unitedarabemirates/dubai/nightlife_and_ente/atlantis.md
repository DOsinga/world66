Located right next to the famous Hard Rock Care, is a
popular, modern disco with a lively atmosphere. Music ranges from
techno to trance to dance, and there are also many performances by drum
masters, dancing girls and more. The scene is modern, young and hip,
and you'll find a good mix of expats and locals! Ladies night Sunday,
Tuesday and Thursday, and a pint of Foster's costs around 18Dhs.
