---
title: "Getting Around in Fujairah"
type: section
---

When you're safely settled in Fujairah, there are multiple modes of transportation that can shuttle you and your family safely around this city. Buses and taxis are the primary methods of transportation in the city but walking or riding a bike are nearly unheard of.

**Traveling by Bus

**Traveling by bus in Fujairah is ideal for tourists as it is convenient and versatile. Buses offer longer trips for traveling from 1 emirate to another as well as to and from different locations within Fujairah. Traveling by bus is the cheapest mode of transportation in Fujariah and the most reliable as the bus schedules are constant and predictable.
**
Traveling by Taxi

**A bit more expensive than traveling by bus, traveling by taxi is also easy and readily available. Tourists generally rely on traveling by taxi to get from 1 part of the city to another as they are more available instantly than waiting around for a scheduled bus.

**Traveling by Car

**Rental cars are also readily available throughout Fujairah and the neighboring emirates. Argus Car Hire is the largest rental car business in Fujairah and offers tourists a wide variety of automobiles to chose from. Offering competitive low rates, renting a car is the best type of transportation to chose if traveling with a family or planning on doing some sightseeing while visiting the UAE.
