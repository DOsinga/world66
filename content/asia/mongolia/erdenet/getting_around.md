**Taxi and Bus
**There are official marked taxis and casual taxis. Any car may function as a taxi. Just extend your arm toward the street and wiggle your hand. You can direct them by pointing and you pay by the kilometer, not by time elapsed. Be sure to check the going rate from someone with nothing to gain.
Buses run reglarly between the train station and city center, but within Erdenet itself no vehicular transportation is necessary. You can walk across the entire city in 20 minutes at a brisk pace.
