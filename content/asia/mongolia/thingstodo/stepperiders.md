Stepperiders is located just outside of [Ulaanbaatar](/asia/mongolia/ulaanbaatar), great if you don't have the time to travel further into the countryside.
There are a variety of 1 or 2 day horse treks to choose from, all based in and around the Bogdkhan Uul National Park.
Basic accommodation is offered in a traditional Mongolian ger.
Stepperiders is a English-Mongolian joint venture, so language won't be a problem.
