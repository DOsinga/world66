---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

Bago is about two hours from Yangon by road or rail, making it an easy day trip or a stopover on the way to Kyaiktiyo.

**By train:** Regular services run from Yangon to Bago. The journey is cheap and scenic, though not always punctual.

**By car:** Hiring a driver from Yangon is straightforward and gives you the flexibility to combine Bago with a trip to the Golden Rock in a single long day.

**By bus:** Buses between Yangon and points east pass through Bago frequently.
