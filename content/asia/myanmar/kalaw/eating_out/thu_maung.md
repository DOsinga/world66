A no-frills restaurant serving traditional Burmese meals at very low prices. Order a curry — chicken or mutton — and the green tomato salad, and leave the rest to them. Side dishes arrive steadily: balachaung (dried shrimp relish), pickled bamboo shoots, soup, and fried vegetables. At the end comes fruit, pickled tea salad, Shan tea, and pieces of palm sugar. Around $2 per person.

A great place for a traditional Burmese meal. Order any of the curries — chicken, mutton, or whatever is available — and the green tomato salad, then leave the rest to the kitchen. Side dishes arrive in waves: balachaung, pickled bamboo shoots, soup, fried vegetables, and rice. They will continuously refill the plates until you are stuffed. After the main course comes sliced fruit, pickled tea salad, Shan tea, and nuggets of palm sugar. The whole thing costs almost nothing.
