** Ngapali Beach** is surely one of the most beautiful beaches in Myanmar with unspoilt white sand fringed with coconut groves as far as the eye can see. Still, you find primarily peace and quiet, lonely beaches for relaxing or excursions to some islands and fishing villages. Ngapali is easily and quickly accessible by airplane from [Yangon](/asia/myanmar/yangon) to Thandue and a car transfer of about 30 minutes.
