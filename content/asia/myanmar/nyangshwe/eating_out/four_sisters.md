title: "Four Sisters"
type: poi

A beloved Nyangshwe institution with attractive decor, warm hospitality, and a menu of local Intha food. The Four Sisters operate a pay-what-you-want policy and occasionally host live music. The spicing is toned down for foreign visitors, but the food and the atmosphere more than make up for it.
