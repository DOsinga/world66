Good Chinese food in a reliable setting on the main road in Nyangshwe. The hot and sour fish is worth ordering, and cold beer is available — something not to be taken for granted in smaller Myanmar towns. A solid option after a day on [Inle Lake](/asia/myanmar/inlelake).
