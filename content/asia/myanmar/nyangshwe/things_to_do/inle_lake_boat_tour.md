A full-day boat tour is the essential Nyangshwe experience. Arrange one through your guesthouse — expect to pay around $6–8 for a boat and guide. The standard circuit covers the floating market at Ywama, a silver workshop, a longyi weaving factory, and several stilt villages. The famous one-legged fishermen are out in force in the early morning, standing at the prow and rowing with one leg wrapped around the oar.
