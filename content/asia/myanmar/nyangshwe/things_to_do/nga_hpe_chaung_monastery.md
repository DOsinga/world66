title: "Nga Hpe Chaung Monastery"
type: poi
category: Sight
latitude: 20.5833
longitude: 96.8667
---

Also known as the Jumping Cat Monastery, this atmospheric old wooden monastery sits on stilts over the lake. The resident monks once trained their cats to leap through hoops — a quirk that drew visitors for decades. The monastery itself, with its collection of Buddha images and aged teak construction, is worth the visit regardless of any feline acrobatics.
