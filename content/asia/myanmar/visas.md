title: "Visas"
type: location
---

Visa fee: Subject to present visa fee at Myanmese embassy of your country. 

 Processing Time: Approximately 1 working day, excluding postal delivery time 

 Requirements: 

 1 - Two visa application forms

 2 - Three passport size photographs

 3 - Valid passport

 4 - Bank cheque for visa fee (cashiers cheque or similar)

 5 - Self-addressed, stamped envelope (registered mail)

 6 - Entry form

 Additional requirements for business visas: Confirmation letter from the employer or the Myanmar partner company showing reason and duration of stay 

 Confirmation of onward travel (photo-copy of flight ticket or similar) 

 Procedure:   The above documents should be sent to the embassy of Myanmar in your country (by registered mail). Once the visa is approved by the authorities, the passport is posted
