**Weather**

 Best time to travel to Myanmar is the dry season from November till February, with December probably being the best. March and April are dry but very hot. It's not adviseable to go during the monsoon season. It rains heavily several times a day and the sky is grey.  If you want to know what the weather will be in Myanmar for the next 4 days, go to the CNN page and click on the city you want to go to http://www.cnn.com/WEATHER/cities/asiapcf.html
