---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Yangon's centrepiece is the Shwedagon Pagoda, which should take up most of a morning or afternoon. Beyond the pagoda, the downtown colonial core rewards walking: the crumbling British-era buildings along Strand Road and the lanes around Bogyoke Market are among the best-preserved examples of colonial streetscapes in Southeast Asia. Allow a full day for the city.
