The most elaborate carved window in Bhaktapur, tucked in a narrow alley off Dattatreya Square. This second-floor window of an ordinary building is an extraordinary example of Newari woodcarving. A master woodcarver's workshop operates directly across the alley, selling replicas and original pieces.
