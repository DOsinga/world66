Birgunj is Nepal's main border crossing with India, sitting opposite the Indian town of Raxaul across the Gandak River. Most travellers pass through rather than stopping, but if you're crossing the border or need to spend a night, the town has serviceable accommodation and services.

The border itself is open around the clock. Buses to [Kathmandu](/asia/nepal/kathmandu) and [Pokhara](/asia/nepal/pokhara) depart from the bus station north of the clock tower (Ghanthaghar). The journey to Kathmandu takes about six to seven hours.

For a comfortable stay, Hotel Vishuwa has a pool and good restaurant. For cheaper options, the Hotel Kailash near the clock tower area is reliable. There are internet cafes and money exchange offices concentrated around the same area.
