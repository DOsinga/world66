---
title: "Eating Out"
type: section
---

Dining options in Birgunj are limited but functional. Brindavans is a popular vegetarian restaurant with friendly staff and good food. The hotel restaurants — particularly at the Vishuwa and Heera Plaza — are reliable options for a meal. Street food stalls near the bus park and clock tower area serve Nepali and Indian staples: dal bhat, momos, samosas, and chai.
