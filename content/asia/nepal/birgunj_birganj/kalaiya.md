Travel Guide

Kalaiya Municipality was established in 2039. The Only one municipality and headquarter of bara District. 306 k.m far from kathmandu and 12 k.m east of Birgunj. At the its established Kalaiya, Bhawanipur, Bairiya, Baghwan, and Rampur VDC’s were Included. At first it was divided in 11 wards later its area was broadened in 14 Wards in 2047 Including some more VDC’s Bhatauda, Brahampuri, and Biswambharpur. Its area is 3223.5 hector and known as well productive land. This spot is suitable for tourist spot because of having the greatest fair of GADHIMAI in Asia famous from sacrifice.
