Dhulikhel is an ancient Newar town perched on the eastern rim of the Kathmandu Valley, about 30 km from the capital. On a clear day, the panorama of Himalayan peaks from Langtang to Everest is one of the best viewpoints in [Nepal](/asia/nepal) — and far quieter than Nagarkot.

The town itself has handsome old brick houses, a handful of temples, and comfortable lodges that make it a good base for longer stays outside Kathmandu. Mountain bikers come for the classic loop: ride up to Dhulikhel, continue to the hilltop stupa at Namobuddha, then descend to the medieval town of Panauti — a full day's ride through terraced hills and small villages.
