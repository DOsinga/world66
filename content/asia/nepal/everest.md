Travel Guide

It is a famous challenging and popular trekking destination located at Everest (Khumbu) region. EBC Trek is a life inspiring experience that will introduce your soul and naked eye to stunning views of the highest mountain in the Himalayas and the world. It ranks highly among the classic treks of the world with awesome beautiful mountain sceneries including Everest and Ama Dablam (the most technically difficult mountain to climb). Since the Everest Summit conquest in 1953 by Sir Edmund Hillary and Sherpa Tenzing Norgay, thousands of trekkers have visited The Land of the Sherpa to be amazed and pay homage to this historic extraordinary achievement.
