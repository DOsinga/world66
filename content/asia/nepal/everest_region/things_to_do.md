---
title: 'Things to Do'
type: section
---

Trekking is the reason to come here. The Everest Base Camp trek (12-14 days round trip from Lukla) is the classic route, passing through Namche Bazaar, Tengboche, and Dingboche before reaching base camp at 5,364m. The Gokyo Lakes trek is a quieter alternative with stunning turquoise lakes. The Three Passes route is a demanding 18-20 day circuit for experienced trekkers. For a shorter trip, the Everest Panorama trek reaches Tengboche monastery and its views of Everest in about a week. Peak climbing on Island Peak (6,189m) and Mera Peak (6,476m) is available for those with mountaineering ambitions.
