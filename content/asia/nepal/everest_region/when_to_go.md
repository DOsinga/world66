---
title: 'When to Go'
type: section
---

The best trekking seasons are pre-monsoon (March to May) and post-monsoon (September to November). Autumn offers the clearest skies and most stable weather, making it the peak season -- trails and teahouses can be crowded. Spring brings wildflowers at lower elevations and longer days, though clouds tend to build in the afternoons. Winter (December to February) is cold but quiet, with teahouses still open on the main routes. Avoid June through August when the monsoon brings rain, poor visibility, and leeches to the lower trails.
