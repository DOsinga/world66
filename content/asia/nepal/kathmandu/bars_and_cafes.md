type: section

Thamel is the centre of Kathmandu's nightlife. The neighbourhood's alleyways fill up after dark with duelling sound systems from competing bars, bands of travellers bar-hopping between venues, and rickshaw drivers waiting for closing time. It can feel hectic and a little relentless — but it's also genuinely lively, and easy to find your footing. Most places stay open late and cater to a mixed crowd of backpackers, expats, and Nepali city dwellers.
