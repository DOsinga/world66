One of the most popular attractions of **Nagarkot sights** is the exhilarating view of Mt. Everest and other major peaks of Himalaya at sunrise. Nagarkot, a most popular tourist destination of Nepal and the favorite weekend getaway, is about 2,175 meters above sea level, in Bhaktapur district, 32 km east of Kathmandu. With terraced hillsides, long mountain walls of snow capped peaks, stunning sunrises and sunsets, colourful valleys and lush green forest, Nagarkot is an idyllic dream of peace and tranquility.

**Sunrise**

The spectacular and awe-inspiring sunrise view of the Himalayas - stretching from Dhaulagiri in the west past Everest to Kanchenjunga in the east - is just breathtaking and unforgettable. Visitors travel to Nagarkot to spend the night so that they can view the majestic Himalayas at sunrise. The sight is best seen from the viewing tower. Sunset is also great to view from places like cafe Dumont.

**Indravaty River Valley**

An excellent place to enjoy the aesthetic views of Indravaty River Valley to the east.

**Temples and Trekkings**

Visit the nearby villages like Tamang village, Sankhu to the right and Bhaktapur to the right. Vajrayogini Temple and Changu Narayana Temple can be reached via popular walks along a ridge down from Nagarkot. These are Kathmandu Valley's most ancient temples.

The trips or hikes to these temples can be done as one-way hikes utilizing taxis or buses for returning to Nagarkot. It is a 13-km hike and takes you through a stunningly beautiful forest and little hamlets. The temple and the medieval town Sankhu built around it are both fascinating and frequented tourist attractions. Short treks and picnics are very popular. Jungle walks and pony riding are other options.

Another popular trekking trail is to south to Nala and Banepa en route to Tibet. Lush green forests offer a great opportunity for bird-watching. Himalaya range and the Indravaty River valley offer great opportunity to enjoy the verdant lush forests, colourful flower meadows, unusual flora and fauna and unique rock formations. The Fort has an organic garden and traditional architecture.

Local guides are available for trail walks just around Nagarkot, where beauty abounds year round.

Nagarkot is a place to relax and enjoy nature at its best - the majestic Himalayas and the stunning Indravati River Valleys. Do a lot of hiking, trekking and mountain biking along with just beauty and silence as companions.
