Travel Guide

**BANDIPUR **is a pearl of an historic mountain town between Kathmandu, Pokhara and the Chitwan National Park. This village should be on everyone's 'must do' list! Whether it's the eagle-nest location, its remoteness, its lazy walks through the idyllic farms and orange groves, the locals' friendliness, or the breathtaking views of the Himalayas...you'll love this place.

**The Old Inn** in Bandipur is a unique building uniquely sited in the traffic-free bazaar with stunning views from its bedrooms and its mountain-facing terrace, where many meals are taken.
