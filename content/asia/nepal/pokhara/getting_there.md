---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

Pokhara is 200 km west of Kathmandu — nominally a seven-hour bus ride, often longer on the winding Prithvi Highway. The comfortable tourist buses (Greenline, Jagadamba) leave both cities every morning. **Pokhara International Airport** (PKR) opened in 2023 and handles domestic flights from Kathmandu (30 minutes) plus a few Chinese connections; domestic flights from Kathmandu are frequent and cheap.
