Pokhara's emerald heart — Nepal's second-largest lake, with the Annapurnas reflected in its water on clear mornings. Hire a brightly painted **doongas** (wooden rowboat) at Lakeside for an hour and row yourself out to the small island shrine of **Tal Barahi** in the middle. A paved footpath lets you walk the eastern shore end to end.
