Travel Guide

Poon Hill situated at an elevation of 3200m is famous place in Annapurna region offers an opportunity to have incredible views of Mountain above 8000m like Annapurna, (8093m) Mt. Dhaulagiri (8167m), Nilgiri, Mt. Fistail (Machhapuchhare), Lamjung Himal, Tukuche Peak and several other mountains. It is the most interesting place for sunrise view over Annapurna range.

The village Ghorepani near by Poon Hill offer the unique experience of Nepalese life. Beside these scenery of mountains Poon hill trail also offers remarkable experience passing through ethnic patchwork valleys, dense mossy rhododendron forests and past icy waterfalls.
