Several rafting programs are available most ranging from five to 14 days in length (some even include trekking). Two of the more popular rivers are the Trisuli (can be done as a day trip from Kathmandu) and the Sun Kosi (travels east-west). These programs are a fun exciting way to see another part of this scenic country. Most rafting is done October-May when the rivers run the swiftest.
