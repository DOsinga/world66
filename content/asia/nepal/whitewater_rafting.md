Travel Guide

Rapidrunner Expeditions, based in Pokhara, offers world class rafting adventures on all of Nepals rivers, with superb quality and safety. The Nepali/British husband and wife owners give a warm welcome to everyone and make sure that all their trips are special experiences. Trips vary from a half day to 12 days long, but if you have the time, try to spend at least one night at their remote river beach camps. Sitting next to a drift wood fire under a canopy of the stars - a truly magical experience!
