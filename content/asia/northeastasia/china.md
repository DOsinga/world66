China is incredible in many ways. The list of things to do and see in China is just too long. From the barren highland of [Tibet](/asia/northeastasia/china/tibet) , via the silk road to the forbidden city in Bejing , the great wall, the army of [Xian](/asia/northeastasia/china/xian) , the skycrapers of [Shanghai](/asia/northeastasia/china/shanghai) , the beauty of [Yunnan Province](/asia/northeastasia/china/yunnanprovince) and the incredible city of [Hong Kong](/asia/northeastasia/china/hongkong) , it's all there and there is much, much more.
