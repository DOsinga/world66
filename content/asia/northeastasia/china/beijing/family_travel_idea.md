Beijing Imperial Courtyard

Imperial Courtyard a Chinese traditional Courtyard hotel is
situated in Beijing, a city with a unique history and a magical aura about it.

Imperial Courtyard, an 18th Century courtyard, having been
totally renovated to the highest standards, now combines the beauty of the past
with the comforts of today and offers the perfect environment for its guests
relaxation and is the ideal accommodation for a "get away from it
all" visit to historical and traditional Beijing.

The Neoclassical "feel" is apparent in all 16 of
the Imperial Courtyard’s luxurious and hospitable rooms. In fact one of the
Imperial Courtyard rooms "the Executive Suite" seems to have glided
effortlessly and had landed on the roof of the Traditional Hotel Imperial
Courtyard.

As soon as someone enters the Imperial Courtyard, the
feeling of familiarity combined with the unique and luxurious setting, becomes
aware that the Imperial Courtyard had been renovated with its guests comfort in
mind. you will be charmed and delighted with the simple pleasures of
traditional Chinese living coupled with all of the modern amenities and
services with the warmth of Chinese hospitality.

Rooms:

A wonderful reception and relaxation lounge, welcome you at
the Imperial Courtyard, providing the ideal vacation environment in Beijing
within classical and luxurious surroundings.

Dining:

Enjoy Chinese Traditional Cuisine for both lunch and dinner
in elegant surroundings. Imperial Traveler Café Cuisine Restaurant features a
great selection of your favourite dishes with its original flavour.
International and other Asian menu also available and served throughout the
day.

Tours and Travel:

We offer tours to all of Beijing's famous attractions, such
as the Great Wall, the Forbidden City and the Summer Palace. If there are any
other locations/venues you would like to visit, we can arrange custom tours for
you. We recommend seeing Chinese acrobatics, Shaolin Kung Fu and Beijing opera.

We can also help you book domestic and international
flights, train tickets and hotels throughout China.

Location:

Hotel is located in the bustling downtown commercial street,
and convenient transportation. Andingmen close to Metro Line 2 station,
surrounded the bus station (special 2,44,800) Road. Surrounding attractions are
the Temple of Earth Park, Drum Tower, Bell Tower, Lama Temple, Imperial
College, Hou Hai Bar Street, Nan Luo Gu Xiang, and five camps Culture Street.
From the Workers Stadium, the International Exhibition Centre, the embassy district,
Tiananmen Square, the Olympic Games only 10 minutes. Close to 15 minutes by
subway or bus to reach the Oriental Plaza, Wangfujing Street, Tiananmen Square,
Forbidden City, Poly Plaza, Workers Stadium and other places. Only a few stops
away from Beijing Railway Station, the car 15 minutes. From the airport only 20
minutes.
