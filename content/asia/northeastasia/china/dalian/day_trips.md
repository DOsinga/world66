**Day Trips**

**Lushun (Port Arthur)**

    The attractions in Lushun are Coast Park, Baiyushan Tower, Dongjiguan Fort, Snake Island and Huangjinshan Outdoor Bathing Place.     

**Ocean World Aquarium**

    Walk through a 100-meter long transparent tunnel, you'll observe kinds of marine wildlife in Xinghai Park in Huanghe Jie.    Open 8:30am-5pm. Entry fee: 35 yuan
