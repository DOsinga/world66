Club Spark is the most popular HipHop bar in Dalian. Clean and good atmosphere. They have 5 foreign djs that plays really good musics. dj cowboy (philippines) dj yas (japan) dj ken (japan) dj oscar (spain) and dj joon (korea).. 80% of the crowd are foreigners, so easy to find or meet new friends. It is located on the small street between Shangrila Hotel and Furama Hotel..
