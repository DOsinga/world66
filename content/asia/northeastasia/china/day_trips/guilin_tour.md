Day 1: We meet you at Liangjian international airport and transfer you to your hotel. Free at leisure or arrange your time on your own. It is a good choice to see the night scenery in Gulin.

Day 2 : We will drive about 1 hour to the Zhujiang Pier for boarding the Li River Cruise boat and taking about 4 hours' cruise to Yangshuo. The picturesque Li River has been descriptions as "a grand silk ribbon with hills like jade hairpins' and is ideal for exploration on a boat. The meandering 65km cruise last 5 hours and takes you past bamboo groves, sleepy villages, endless peaks, and fisherman on rickety bamboo rafts fishing with cormorant birds. In the late day, drive back to Guilin. (For the optional, you may stay in Yangshuo tonight and enjoy the amazing night life there. Just tell us if you prefer.)

Day 3: After breakfast, pay a visit the Reed Flute Cave, which is a garish but nonetheless impressive grotto housing multicolored lighting and fantastic stalactites and stalagmites. Then visit the Elephant Trunk Hill, which is the symbolic of Guilin and looks like an elephant, dipping its snout into the Li River, hence its name.

You will be escorted to the airport for your flight to your next destination in the afternoon.
