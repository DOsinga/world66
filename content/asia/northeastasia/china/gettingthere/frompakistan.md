The Karakoram Highway takes you from
[Kashgar](/asia/northeastasia/china/xinjiangprovince/kashgar) to
[Gilgit](/asia/southasia/pakistan/gilgit) via
Tashkurgan,
[Hunza valley](/asia/southasia/pakistan/hunzavalley) and
[Passu](/asia/southasia/pakistan/passu) or vice versa. The
[Khunjerab pass](/asia/northeastasia/china/xinjiangprovince/khunjerabpass) is some 4800 meters high and is only open in summer. Border formalities are not very strict. There is one bus daily in eevry direction that takes two days to go from the border to
Kasghar - you spend the night in
Tashkurgan. In Pakistan it is pretty easy to find onward transport.
