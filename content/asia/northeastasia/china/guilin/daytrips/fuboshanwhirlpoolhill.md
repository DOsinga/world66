---
title: "Fubo Shan (Whirlpool Hill)"
type: poi
---

Situated on the edge of the Li River on the site of a temple dedicated to a general back to the Tang Dynasty, Whirlpool Hill offers an impressive view of the surrounding area. At its base are a vast bell and the Qing-era Thousand Man Vessel (Qian Ren Gang). The 10-foot stalactite in Huanzhu Dong (The Cave of the Returned Pearl) is a place should not be missed on the south side of the hill.
