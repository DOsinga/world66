the Fubo Hill stands an ancient temple bell (a popular place for photos), this bell was made inside Guilin's Fubo Park in Qing Dynasty with a series exquisite inscriptions etched onto its surface.Fubo Hill's famous Thousand Buddhas Cave is nationally renowned from its rich array of ancient Buddhist carvings, they are really beautiful. Some are so old and worn that you can just see the basic shape, however the cave still echoes with the sounds of ancient scholars.

In the summer months, Fubo hill attracts a lot of locals as sit rests on the banks of the Li river, and sweltering locals bring their radios, swimmers and umbrellas and enjoy an afternoon of relaxing swimming and popsicles.

From the Pedestrian street we strolled north along the river and ended up at Fubo Hill. The park is very nice and well kept. The climb up the hill had many steps, but there were a few benches along the way to stop to catch your breath and admire the view on the ascend up.
