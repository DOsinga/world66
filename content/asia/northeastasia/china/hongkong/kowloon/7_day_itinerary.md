title: "7 Day Itinerary in Kowloon"
type: section
---

Have the complete urban experience in Hong Kong by preparing the best **Kowloon Itinerary**. Kowloon, an area bordered by 8 great mountains and is situated at the northern part of Hong Kong, is popular among tourists who want to get a fill of Asia with a little modern twist. Here is a 7-day Itinerary suggestion for you.
**
Day 1 – Experience the Waterfront**

What better way to enjoy your first day than to experience the Kowloon waterfront. You must not miss the view this place has to offer for most travelers consider it a wonderful spectacle of Hong Kong’s shore and skyline. Be sure to take photos while you walk.
**
Day 2 – Visit the Temple**

If you want to have some quiet and soul- relieving time, take a visit to the Tai Sin Temple. You may enter the place free of charge and ask for some divine guidance through contemplation. Make donations if you want to.

**Day 3 – Learn Their History
**
Don’t forget to pass by the Hong Kong Museum of History located in Tsim Sha Tsui. Dedicate this day of your travel to knowing more of Hong Kong’s origins and you’ll sure to be enjoying much than you expect to.
**
Day 4 – Visit Kowloon Park**

If you think a park’s all about sight-seeing, then you must know Kowloon Park also offers water leisure activities to locals and tourists alike. Be sure to savor the wonderful natural resources, wildlife, and recreational areas Kowloon has to offer.
**
Day 5 – Practice Tai Chi**

Shake up some muscles and stretch with this Asian exercise. Tai Chi is popular for the physical and psychological reliefs it’s given people. Take a free lesson when you get to visit the Avenue of the Stars. The lessons, though, are scheduled Monday, Wednesday and Friday between 8 and 9 in the morning.
**
Day 6 – Try Some Seafood and Watch A Light Show
**
Give this day your best shot by doing 2 grand activities you’ll remember the most. Go to the Temple Street and experience Kowloon’s famous seafood and other street food choices while watching the world go by. When the night comes, see the Symphony of Lights at 8 p.m. atop harbor buildings.

**Day 7 – Go Shopping**

Whether you are buying on a restricted budget or not, Kowloon is the place to be. Shopping malls offer the best prices for almost all items and they are practically everywhere. Go to Harbour City or Elements for some good buys. Also, remember not to miss your flight.
