The Ten Thousand Buddhas Monastery is a major attraction here. More than 400 steps lead up the hillside to the temple, so strong legs and lungs are essential. Over 10,000 Buddha statues are stacked on shelves filling the walls of the main temple. Actually, there are now close to 13,000 images, all of a similar height but rendered in slightly different poses
