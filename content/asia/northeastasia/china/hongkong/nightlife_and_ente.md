---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment"
type: section
---

Hong Kong is a Mecca for pubs, bars, casinos, karaoke,
and nightclubs once the sun has gone down in the sky. The skyline is
lit up and beacons all to come and play after a hard days work.

**Entertainment Districts**

There are three entertainment districts in Hong King. Lan Kwai Fong
is in central Hong Kong and is where most of the locals go after work.
Places here are trendy and fun. Wan Chai near Causeway Bay has many
bars and discos that are open until dawn. Tsim Tsa Tsui in Kowloon
caters to mostly locals.

**Bars and Clubs**

Bars and Pubs can be found all over Hong Kong and most have a great
variety of beers and other alcoholic drinks from all over the world.
They are open 24 hours a day and will serve you when ever you want, but
they come to life in the evening. There are English pubs, fancy
lounges, and the less attractive types of bars in town.

**Different Types of Clubs**

Nearly all the hotels in town have night clubs where DJ’s regale
guests with the greatest hits. There are two types of clubs in Honk
Kong; the Western clubs and the Chinese clubs. If you are a tourist it
might be better to go to a Western Club. Be sure you know the dress
code before you go or you may not get in. It is also best to call and
get your name on the reservation list before you go. Some are easy to
get into while others it is impossible unless you know someone while
others are accessible as long as you get there early.

**Karaoke**

The Karaoke craze hit Hong Kong and never left. There are many
hotels, clubs, bars, and restaurants that have Karaoke night or they
may have a private room where you can enjoy Karaoke. Often times the
establishment will hire a professional singer to sing along with
customers.

Many hotels and restaurants have live music from a piano bar to
local rock bands. You can hear oldies and punk rock in the same
neighborhood at different establishments and the variety goes on and
on.

**The Night Park**

The Night Park in the Tsim Sha Tsui district is a popular place to
go at night. This park is decorated with thousands of lights and many
of them are neon. You have to see it to believe it. This is a good
place to take an evening stroll and look at the sights.
