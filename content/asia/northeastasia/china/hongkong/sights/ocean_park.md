Hong Kong is a a playground for teenagers and adults, and luckily for children, there are two great options--Ocean Park and Water World. Ocean
Park, and its neighbor, Water World, are natural attractions for children
who are tired from a long journey, hotel rooms or shopping in hot humid
weather. Ocean Park, spectacular and fun, is a combination marine land and amusement
park. **

Getting There

**Both Ocean Park and Water World are easily accessible, even though they are on the south side of Hong
Kong Island, near Aberdeen and Kowloon. It is a good idea to combine a
trip to Ocean Park with a trip to Aberdeen, the floating restaurants or Stanley Market. Water World is only open from May through October but both venues are
served by special bus transports from Central Station and tickets can
be purchased in the Mass Transit Railway stations. Ticket prices include the
round trip bus fare. Some of the buses are topless double deckers which
make the trip a nice sight seeing tour as well. If you've rented a car,
there's plenty of parking available at Ocean Park.

**Ocean Park**

The
Ocean Park is divided between two levels and it is on the headland,
connected to the lowland by a 0.93 miles (1.5 kilometers) cable car
system. Here you will find the Ocean Theater, a 3,000 seat aquatic
theater for performing dolphins and a killer whale. Occasionally,
there might be some high diving acts. The Wave Cove,
with its rocky shore line that is home to a variety to seals, penguins and
birds, is also at this level. Viewing stations are available above and below the water line.
The Atoll Reef is a 3-level aquarium, housing more than 30,000 fish and
sharks that are hand fed!**

Rides **

The
amusement park section of Ocean Park is amazing! The Dragon roller coaster, one of
the longest and most spectacular in the world covers 2,772 feet or 840 meters. An escalator takes you to yet another section which, according to the Guiness Book of Records, is the
longest in the world. On the lower level of the amusement park section,
there is a childrens' play area and a petting zoo and the children will
also find dolphin feeding pools, trained bird shows and several garden
areas.**
