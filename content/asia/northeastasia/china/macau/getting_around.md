---
title: "Getting Around"
type: section
---

Get around by bus. Frequent services which go all over the territory at a fixed rate (30 US cents around the peninsular to 60 US cents to get down to Hac Sa beach from the ferry terminal)

Taxis are cheap but the drivers usually only speak Cantonese. Show them on a map where you want to go and it will cost a maximum of 10 US$
