title: "Nightlife"
type: section
---

Nanjing has an active nightlife, with a range of bars, pubs and discos to choose from.  

**Points of Interest**

**Casablanca Disco**

A club that attracts mixed crowd for drinking and dancing. 

address: Xuanwu Hotel

tel: 025-3358888

**Zhongshan Paradise**

Mainly Chinese punters at this down-to-earth dance club, filling up with foreigners on a Friday night (free for foreign students). 

address: Corner of Zhongshan Lu and Zhujian Lu

tel: 025-3361888

**Manhattan**

Largely local well-to-do crowd here. 

address: 5 Yanliang Xiang

tel: 025-5508888
