**Sights**

**Confucius Temple**

The Temple of Confucius is located on the Qinhuaihe River. The central Temple of Confucius itself resembles a Disney Confucian theme park inside, complete with mannequins in fancy dress.  

**Qinhuaihe River**

The Qinhuaihe River is a bustling commercial district with a dense population during the Six Dynasties Period and the downtown area in the Ming and Qing dynasties.Today it has became a tourist attraction and a commercial center with a unique ancient style.  

**The Sun Yat-sen Mausoleum **

The Sun Yat-sen Mausoleum is the memorial to the father of the modern China, the great revolutionary forerunner, Dr. SunYat-sen (1866-1925). The temple itself is divided into two chambers, the memorial ceremony chamber and the coffin chamber.  

**The Ming City Walls **

with 33 km, The Ming City Walls are the longest city wall in the world. And it took over 200,000 people build it during 1366 - 1386. Bricks from five provinces were used and each brick has been stamped with the origin, the overseer's name and rank, the bricklayer's name, and sometimes the date.  [more..](/asia/northeastasia/china/nanjing/sights/themingcitywalls)

**Mochou lake **

Mochou lake got the name from a beautiful legend about Mochou, a girl from Luoyang. Places of interest on the lake are the Chess Tower, Moon-embracing Tower and the Cemetery for Generals.
