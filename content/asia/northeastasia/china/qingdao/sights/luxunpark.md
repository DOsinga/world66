---
title: "Lu Xun Park"
type: poi
---

Lying on the south of the Laiyang Lu, Lu Xun Park is the most different park near the sea. Near to the Little Qingdao in the west, next to the No.2 beach in the east, the whole park is attractively decorated with the red riff, blue wave, green pine, quiet pass and lush hill.   The park was first built in 1929 and named "Ruoyu Park'at that time. Later it was renamed twice. In 1950, the park was renamed as "Lu Xun Park" to commemorate Mr. Lu Xun, a great proletariat writer.
