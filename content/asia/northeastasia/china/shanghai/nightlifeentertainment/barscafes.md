**Place to go outs**

**New York, New York **

Huqiu Lu Road. Popular bar, crowded with Chinese as well as young expats. 

**Malone's **

Tongren Lu Canadian bar showing recreational sports events on the TV screens. Occasional live music, plus weekly film night on Wed.

**Top of the World **

A sports bar overlooking Shanghai Stadium. You can drink and watch games as well as play pool, football, air hockey, pinball and lots of nightly specials. Tel: 64266888 

**D&K Bar**

  Cool music and great atmosphere, this bar is a good place to hang out with relaxed service and comfortable atmosphere. Tel: 62473943 

**Mandy's**

  Great bar and patio. The kitchen serves great BBQ and a live band plays Thursday through Sunday. Friday is Ladies Night with one free drink for women. Tel: 64746628 

**Noble Single **

Comfortable, friendly surroundings with a long bar and good staff. Nice and cheap neighborhood bar. Tel: 65486676 

**O'Richard's Bar & Restaurant **

  A real traveler's post, this bar has info on travelling around China, internet services and free pool. Also has very cheap beer and food. Tel: 63246388 

**Peace Hotel Bar **

  Dark and cavernous pub with original 1930's decor, legendary for its eight-piece dance band. Come here and down some expensive drinks is to see the band of old hipsters who've been playing together since the roaring 30s. Tel: 63216888
