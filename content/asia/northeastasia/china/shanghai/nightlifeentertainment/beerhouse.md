  Irish pub off Hengshan Road, with large beer garden, a pint and live music imported from Ireland. Tel: 64370667

**No.1 Beer City **

Their specialty is, you guessed it, beer. It's four floors with a piano bar, karaoke room, and disco floor. Tel: 63535988
