Here are a few museums you can visit in Pinyao.

### [Tianchengkui Farming Museum](/asia/northeastasia/china/shanxiprovince/pingyao/museums/tianchengkui_farmi)

This museum specializes in the collection and display of farming
implements. There is a collection of over 300 items on display in seven
exhibition rooms. The exhibits present a vivid portrayal of the
development of farming techniques and productivity and their effect on
the social economy during different periods of time.****
