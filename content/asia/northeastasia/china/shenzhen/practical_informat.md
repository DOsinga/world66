Lonely Writers Publishing, a Hong Kong based publishing company, published in 07/2008 a Shenzhen guidebook, "Shopping in Shenzhen", RMB98/HK$128, 276 pages.
The guide comprehends 4 sections, "Shopping in Shenzhen", "Shenzhen's
Finest Cuisine", "Shenzhen's Best Hotels", and "A Day in Shenzhen."
In 2009, "Living in Shenzhen" is to be published. Checkout http://www.szcityguide.com.
