**Sights**

**Wuhou Shrine**

Wuhou might be translated as "Minister of War", and was the title given to Zhuge Liang, a famous military strategist of the Three Kingdom Period (220-280AD) immortalized in one of the classics of Chinese literature, the Tale of the Three Kingdom. Entry fee is 15 yuan. 

**Wenshu Monastery (Wenshu Yuan)**

Dating back to the Tang Dynasty but largely reconstructed in the Qing Dynasty, this monastery was originally known as the Xinxiang temple. It was renamed after a Buddhist monk who resided here in the 17th century.
