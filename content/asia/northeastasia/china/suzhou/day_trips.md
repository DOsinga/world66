title: "Day Trips"
type: section
---

The Water Village of Zhou Zhuang is a good day-trip from Suzhou, or can
be taken in on the way to Shanghai. Though commercialized, it is an
attempt to freeze a moment in time, for tourists to see what little
towns and villages were like before industrialization. It also gives
you an idea of what the setting of Suzhou was like many years ago.
