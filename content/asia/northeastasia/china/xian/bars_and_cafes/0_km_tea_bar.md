A tea bar provide several kinds of perfect green tea in Shaanxi.

A website with more than 5,000 destination info in Western China.

A club with several partners along Qingzang Plateau and Silkroad.

It's located in the 12th Floor, 1248 Room, Yulang International Office Building, Jiefang Road, Xi'an, only 1200 meters south of the Xi'an railway station, about 20 minutes' walking distance.

Actually, it's the office of a travel website, here you could taste the latest green tea produced in south of Shaanxi province, which enjoys a high fame for its good quality. Besides, free travel info is provided here, including Xi'an, Chengdu, Lhasa , Xining , Dunhuang, Urumqi , Kashgar , etc..
