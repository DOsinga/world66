There are many different things to do in one of the **Zhong Shan Day Trips**. Dalian's commercial center and the coastline make up Zhong Shan's District and it is famous for having Haizhiyun Square and Zhong Shan Square. There are also different places where people can shop, such as: Tianjin Square and Times Square.**Tiger Beach**

Zhong Shan Day Trips can start at Tiger Beach. People can take a bus from the center of the district that can take them straight to Tiger Beach. Tiger beach is home to the largest polar aquarium in Asia. Most tourists will be amazed by the sculpted Tigers and by the majestic blue sea.

Tiger Beach is a popular site for people who love to watch aquatic shows. Besides the aquatic sports there is a coral exhibition and even performances by dolphins. This beach is also home to Singing Birds Woods. There is a cage that is 7 feet in height and it houses exotic birds.**Boating to Bang Shui Island**

To make this one of the most memorable Zhong Shan Day trips tourists can take a boat from Tiger Beach to the island of Bang Shui and enjoy looking at the mountains and swimming on the beach. This island is home to politicians and celebrities. It costs around $40 Yuan to take a boat to this island. Century City Food Court

The food court offers different kinds of food like Korean, Japanese and Chinese. The specialty of the place is Malatang that is to say to numb the mouth because of the blend of hot and spicy ingredients. People are allowed to custom make their own noodless and soups by simple choosing the ingredients. **The Friendship Centre**

People can visit the Friendship Centre and shop for some of the most exclusive products in China. People flock to this center to buy designer clothing and high-end apparel. There are many different shops in the area and most of the locales of this venue and very luxurious.

**Tin Whistle**

For those people who like to drink there are different bars that cater to different crowds of people. Zhong Shan Day trips can end with style at one of the most upscale bars in Zhong Shan. The Tin Whistle serves both domestic and foreign beverages like Guinness. The bar showcase sports and its a popular place for tourists and locals alike.
