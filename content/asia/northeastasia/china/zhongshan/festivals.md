**Zhongshan Festivals** exude the cultural richness of the people of Zhongshan. Zhongshan is not well known to tourists, but the Chinese love it because it is the home of Dr. Sun Zhongshan (or Dr. Sun Yat-Sen) who is considered the father of mModern China. Zhongshan is found in the province of Guangdong, China. It is at the center of the Pearl River Delta, a waterway that connects Guangzhou with Hongkong and Macau.

**Lingnan**** Water Town**** Tourism and Cultural Festival**

Zhongshan reflects the way of life of their people. Being near to the Pearl River Delta, the province of Guangdong holds its Lingnan Water Town Tourism and Cultural Festival in Zhongshan. This festival happens on the month of November. During this festival, a series of events are followed. The first event is an Opening Ceremony followed by the Exhibition of Agricultural Products of Zhongshan City. There is also a Water Town Sports Meeting and a Documentary Photography Contest. To complete the fun of this festival, there is an animal show and a one-day Travel in the Water Town.

This water town festival combines amusement, sightseeing, and celebration. It also shows a glimpse of the people&rsquo;s culture and the rural essence in the water town. Moreover, it is also supplied with entertainment services like a water park and other recreational facilities. This festival is not restricted to Lingnan Water Town. It has also celebrated in beautiful spots like the Agricultural Products Trading Center, Fengben Known Fruit Garden, Mingcai Fruit Garden, and Dashatian, Duobao Pet Park.
