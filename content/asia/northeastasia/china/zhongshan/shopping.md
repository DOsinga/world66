title: "Shopping in Zhong Shan"
type: section
---

Zhongshan City, town of the great Dr. Sun Yat-Sen, is situated in
the Pearl River Delta. The beautiful city of Zhongshan not only offers
gorgeous natural views to its tourists, but fabulous **Zhong Shan shopping** as well.
Although Zhong Shan has a population of over 1.3 million people, it is
not as large as its neighboring cities. This factor and because its
location is by the waterways, can offer Zhong Shan shopping at
incredibly lower prices than other larger cities, such as Hong Kong.
There are numerous shopping destinations to choose from. One can find
the delicious popular almond cakes and fresh juicy pineapples here, and
of course popular international import and domestic items.
**Victoria Plaza Shopping Center

**This mall is one of the
largest indoor malls in China, and offers over 400 specialty stores and
restaurants. This is a great place to find a bargain. It boasts a
3-storey underground shopping center.**

Taipei Zhongshan Underground Metro Shopping Mall

**This
beautiful underground mall is one of the largest underground malls in
the world. It offers numerous stores along with views of gardens and
pools of water for relaxation as you shop.**

Friendship Shopping Center

**There are lots of imported goods here with items of brand names. It is located along the area of Renmin Lu and Zhongshan Square.**

Renmin Lu Street Area

**Stores with many high end items, such as Gucci and Armani can
be found here. The Times Square shopping area is
along this street and carries more upscale goods. Bus transportation is
offered here and goes down this street and another popular shopping
street of Renmin Dajie.

**Zhong Shan Sanxiang Guhe Area

**There is an antique furniture
area here that prices its pieces quite high, but bargaining is
certainly an option. Do keep in mind that many of these pieces are
actually just new replicas of originals.**

Tianjin Street

**This street runs from Victoria Square to
nearby Zhong Shan Square. Here you will find shopping centers and
department stores, along with several little stalls all along the road,
selling a variety of unique items.**

Metro Mall

**The Metro Mall is located in the Shiqi district.
It hosts the largest shopping mall in downtown Zhong Shan, with its 7
floors of stores and specialty shops, restaurants and hotels.**

Qingniwaqiao

**This is one of the largest retailer areas in
Northeast China, and is situated on Youhao Jie. Qingniwaqiao shopping
area boasts department stores and specialty shops with designer
clothing.
