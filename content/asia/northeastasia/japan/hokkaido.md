Hokkaido is the northernmost of the four big islands that make up Japan. It is really a great place if you are an outdoor enthusiast. There are many many hiking possibilities in the great national parks such as [Daisetsuzan](/asia/northeastasia/japan/hokkaido/daisetsuzan) and there are some good skiing slopes (Sapporo hosted the winter olympics once).

 [Sapporo](/asia/northeastasia/japan/hokkaido/sapporo) is the capital and one of the most lively cities of Japan. It has a great beer drinking culture. The city is an ideal place to arrange everything for a trip to some of the most remote parts of Japan.

## Destinations
