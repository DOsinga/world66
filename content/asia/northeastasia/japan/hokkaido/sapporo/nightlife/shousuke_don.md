This well known watering hole located on one of Sapporo's main roads(S4W1) that intersects the Susukino and Odori areas is always friendly and welcoming to all. It is open to the street and has a great atmosphere- a good place to have a drink and meet the locals.Located next door is the popular Sinner Cafe, for details please see sappororestaurant.jp website guide. Both have English menus.
