Japan has a lot of small cafes and bars and some that double as both depending on the time of day.

### [Baghdad Cafe](/asia/northeastasia/japan/honshu/shimonoseki/bars_and_cafes/baghdad_cafe)

Quirky, warm and friendly little cafe/bar down near the Kaikyo Straits...named for the movie and run by a fun guy who likes to practice his English. Great coffee and light meals during cafe hours (11:00-5:30) and a nice cozy atmosphere during bar hours (5:30-11:00) Wonderful view of the Kaikyo Straits from the front tables.
Closed Thursdays.
