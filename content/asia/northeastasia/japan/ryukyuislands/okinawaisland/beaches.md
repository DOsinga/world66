---

There are many beaches on Okinawa. Some quite popular and easy to find, some secluded and hard to reach. They can run the gambit from fine white sand, to coral and shells, to large boulders. Some require a vehicle with high clearance, while others have paid/paved parking. 

All are interesting. The beaches of Okinawa are an ideal place to view many different types of fascinating geologic formations.
