**Restaurants**

**Hantei**

A charming old wooden building with lots of charm. They serve kushi-age sticks which are brought to you even if you do not order them. After 12 sticks they ask if you want more. 

address: 2-12-15 Nezu

**Sasa-no-Yuki**

One of the better vegetarian restaurants. You will need to reserve. 

address: 2-22-15 Negishi

tel: 03-38731145

**Honte Ponta**

A good place to go for Japanized western food. Quite inexpensive.

addres: 3-32-2 Ueno
