**Shops**

**Ameyoko Outdoor Market**

The best place to go shooping in Ueno is Ameyoko market. A boisterous place, Ameyoko started as a black market after World War II. The outdoor markets runs alogn side the train tracks and you will find fish, spishes, coffee, tea and a whole lot of other things here at very competitive prices (at [more..](/asia/northeastasia/japan/tokyo/ueno/shopping/ameyokooutdoormarket)

**Ameyoko Shops**

The shops are east and under the tracks. They mostly sell clothing, shoes and suitcases.
