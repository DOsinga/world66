If you're looking for luxury in Busan, South Korea, check out the following two hotels: The Westin Chosun and The Royal Kingdom Hotel. The Westin Chosun offers world-class menus and exceptional personalized service. The Royal Kingdom Hotel has a modern exterior and an excellent service. The Westin Chosun is located at 737 Woo 1-Dong in Busan, South Korea. The Royal Kingdom Hotel is located at 1509-2 Joong-Dong Haeundae-Gu, Busan. You can also check out the more affordable options, such as The Haeundae Riviera hotel in Busan and The Lord Beach Hotel, one of the best 4-star hotels in Busan. The Haeundae Riviera can be found at 1380-14 Jung1-dong Haeundae-gu. The Lord Beach Hotel is located near the beach at 544-7 Woo-dong Haeundae-gu Pusan South Korea.
