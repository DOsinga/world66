**By train**

From [Seoul](/asia/northeastasia/southkorea/seoul) Station, trains leave 24 times a day on a four and a half hour trip, while buses leave from the Gangnam Express Bus Terminal 70 times a day, traveling 5 hours and 20 minutes to reach their destination!
