---
title: "Top 5 Must Do's in Pusan"
type: section
---

**Pusan must do's **are varied and exciting, whether you're
visiting the city with friends or family. There are plenty of sights and
sounds that will capture your attention, but here are 5 Pusan must do's
that you won't want to miss.

**1. Haedong Yonggung Temple**

Situated among the rocks with only the sea for company, Haedong
Yonggung Temple definitely deserves a spot on the Top 5 Pusan Must Do's.
Visit at sunrise to start your day with an awesome view. Walk up the
108 steps to rub the belly and nose of the jolly statue of Deungnambul
and ask for a son. Throw a coin into the wishing pond, or ask for a
miracle from the healing Buddha.

**2. Shinsegae Centum City**

The fantastic complex that is Shinsegae Centum City houses within its
walls a group of facilities that will entertain every member of your
family for an entire day. Spaland offers hot saunas and treatments for
anyone looking for a bit of relaxation. Kids will love the indoor ice
rink and the cinema, and those looking for a spot of culture will enjoy
the concert hall and the art gallery. There's even a driving range and
an outdoor park, and you can intersperse the day with bouts of shopping
at the department store and snacks at the food court.

**3. Haeundae Beach**

The most popular beach in Korea has naturally made the list of Pusan
must do's: HaeundaeBeach. Lounge on the white sand or go for a dip in
the sea, all while enjoying the view of Dongbaek Island. Sunrise visits
are extremely popular, and in the summer, Folk Square is in full swing
with the opportunity to try your hand at Korean wrestling, tug-of-war,
or even arrow throwing. The boardwalk offers plenty of food, and the
nightlife is crazy.

**4. Pusan Aquarium**

Over 35,000 marine animals ranging from penguins to piranhas are on
display at Pusan Aquarium. You can watch them all through the standard
tanks or head into the tunnels to feel like you're a part of the
underwater life. The touch tank is popular with children, and if you
really want a hands-on experience, you can schedule a dive with the
sharks.

**5. Jagalchi Fish Market**

Famous throughout Korea for its fresh seafood is the Jagalchi Fish
Market. Buy fresh fish or watch it being expertly prepared for the ice
boxes and long trips around the globe. Whether you're after squid,
shellfish, monkfish, or more, you're sure to find it fresh (sometimes
even alive) in Jagalchi Fish Market. Head upstairs to the restaurants to
sample some of the fresh caught wares.
