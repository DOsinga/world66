For some pretty authentic Mexican food, try Choi's Tacos. They have a pretty extensive menu with tacos, burritos, fajitas, quesadillas, nachos etc.

Go to Sinchon (신촌) Subway Station Exit 3 and head up toward Yonsei University and turn right at the first big intersection. It is a little bit past Krispy Kreme on your right. Look for the orange store playing Mexican music.
