---
title: "Getting Around"
type: section
---

Sorry no taxi's here. If you want to get around Kenting and see some sights it will be necessary to rent a scooter or a car. Scooter rentals are available just about anywhere so this shouldn't be a worry.

Buses run between Kenting and Kaoshiung regularly for those coming and going.
