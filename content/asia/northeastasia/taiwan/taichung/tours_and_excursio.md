### [Green Island Taiwan

If you are looking to get away from the hustle and bustle of Taiwan,
Green Island is nature-lover's paradise. This island is
sparsely-populated and can be circumnavigated in 30 minutes by scooter.
Except for a small town on the southern part of the island the island
has been kept free from commercial development. This offers the visitor
unspoiled natural beauty both on and offshore. The climate on Green
Island is tropical and the best time to visit is between April and
September. The activities on Green Island include diving, snorkeling,
hiking, salt water hot springs more..](/asia/northeastasia/taiwan/taichung/tours_and_excursio/green_island)
