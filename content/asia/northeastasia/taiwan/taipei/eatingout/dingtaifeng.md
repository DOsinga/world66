Listed as one of the top ten restaurants in the world by the New York
Times, Din Tai Fung specialises in steamed dumplings.

There are two
branches in Taipei, plus several others around the world, with the Xinyi shop in
Taipei being the original, it is the most popular in the Din Tai Fung chain. Booking is required--or
be prepared for a lengthy wait.

Opens
Monday to Friday 10:00-21:00, Saturday and public holidays 09:00-21:00.
