title: "Electricity and water"
type: location
type: "Tourist information"
---

Taiwan uses electric current of 110 volts at 60 cycles. Many buildings have sockets
with 220 volts especially for the use of air conditioners.

Tap water is drinkable, but drinking unboiled water is not recommended. Most hotels
provide boiled drinking water. Bottled water is easily available at convenience
stores and supermarkets.
