---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

Buraimi is accessible by road from Muscat (about three hours) and from Al Ain on the UAE side. The border crossing between Buraimi and Al Ain requires a valid visa for whichever country you are entering — check current requirements before travelling, as border policies have changed several times in recent years. If you hold a UAE residence visa, confirm that it permits multiple entries before crossing into Oman.
