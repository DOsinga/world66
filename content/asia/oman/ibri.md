Located halfway between [Muscat](/asia/oman/muscat) and the border with the Emirates, Ibri is an old caravan town with a restored fort and a lively souq. It is a good base for exploring the western desert region of Oman, including the Bat tombs (a UNESCO World Heritage Site dating to the 3rd millennium BC) and the dramatic landscape of Jebel Shams and the Grand Canyon of Oman to the east.
