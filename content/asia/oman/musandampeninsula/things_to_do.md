---
title: "Things to do in Musandam peninsula"
type: section
---

Musandam Peninsula is a geographical enclave of Oman. It is a place of great natural beauty and should be visited by all those who go to Oman.

**
Hire a Dhow
**

Hire a dhow and view the fjords of Musandam from the sea. Go on a day trip or a three day cruise. Either way you will see the magnificent fjords from the best vantage point. Unlike in the case of a motor boat, a dhow attracts dolphins, so keep a sharp eye out for these friendly and lovable creatures.

**
Drive on the Coast
**

There is a good highway all along the western part of the peninsula. Drive down the coastline and enjoy the scenic beauty of the mountains and the sea.

**Visit a Fishing Village**

Either by a dhow or from the car, visit a fishing village. The people are friendly and will welcome you whole-heartedly.

**
Snorkel
**

Go snorkeling from the dhow. You will see some fantastic underwater life in this area. There is a very rich marine life under this sea. Take your own gear along if you plan to go snorkeling. Or you could hire the gear in Muscat, the capital of Oman.

**Dive
**

Again from the dhow, one can go diving. You will see a spectacular variety of marine life. Take your own gear along if you plan to dive.

**
Light a Campfire
**

Ask your dhow’s captain to set you down on an isolated beach and have a campfire going. Everyone, both adults and children, will enjoy this experience by the sea. There are many small beaches along the coast where you can safely light a campfire.

**Go Trekking
**

Go trekking in the interior hills. The view from the lofty peaks is fabulous and well worth the effort. You will be awarded with a splendid vista of the sea from some of the hills in the area.

**Charlotte Anne
**

If you love to sightsee in style, hire the Charlotte Anne and see the fjords. This is a tourist ship that will take you on a cruise in style.

As the Musandam Peninsula is known for its natural beauty and splendor, all the activities you can do here are based on the outdoors. If you are a nature lover, you will love visiting this spot for its pristine beauty. There is not much else to do in this peninsula other than take in the natural wonders of the place.
