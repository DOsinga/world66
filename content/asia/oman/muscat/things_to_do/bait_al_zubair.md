A private museum in Old Muscat built around the Zubair family's collection of Omani heritage — *khanjars*, jewellery, costumes, household items, and a reconstructed traditional mud-brick village in the grounds. Well-curated and compact; an hour or two is enough. Open Saturday to Thursday.
