Located in the Cultural Center, this is the main museum covering Dhofar's geography, history, and frankincense trade. Exhibits include ancient coins, clay pottery, manuscripts, and replicas of the old trade routes and caravans that connected Salalah to the wider Arab world. Free admission; open Saturday to Wednesday, 7:30am–2:30pm.
