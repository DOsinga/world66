Traveling in Sohar Oman means that you need to find the best **Sohar transportation** that will allow you to get where you are going and find what you are looking for. There are a few ways that you can find to get around in the city including cars, taxis, and busses.

#### Buses

The city bus system has daily bus services that you can take in Sohar to go to other cities in Oman and you can also get around in the city. It is very affordable to take the city buses and you can also find that it is easier than trying to drive in the area if you are not familiar with it. There can be a lot of people who are riding on the buses and if you are not sure what the routes are or if there is a bus near where you are traveling, you can find the routes and information at your hotel to see where you can get on the bus, and when you will get to your stops.

#### Taxis

Taxi services in Sohar are called taxi services because you can call them first to come and get you where you are. They are affordable, safe, and dependable. If you want to find a good way to get around by Sohar transportation, taxis are a good choice. You can also ask the taxi driver to have private service, or they may stop and pick up on others during your ride so if you do not want other people riding with you, you need to request it.

#### Rental Cars

You can find rental cars that you are able to use to get around in the city and you can find that they are reasonable in price. It may take you longer to get around if you are not familiar with the area, and you need to pay for the distance that you are traveling, so be sure that you find out where you are going to be traveling first.

#### Walk

Depending on where you are traveling in Sohar, you may find that it is a nice place to walk and be able to stop to eat, shop, and see the attractions along the way. You can find a detailed map of the area that you are going to be traveling in and decide what would be the best Sohar transportation for you to take. If you do choose to walk, you want to make sure that you have proper foot attire and clothing.
