Travel Guide

Rahi Village is located in Union council Phallah. It is Three Kilometer from Lora. Rahi is Very beautiful village of Abbottabad. Kotlaya is Mahalla of Rahi Village. Kotlaya is very beautiful place which is located center of the hills. From Rahi to kotlaya ten minutes distance and its come before Khuya. All around of Kotlaya a top hills and cowered by a woods and jangle. The People of Rahi Kotlaya are very honest and well settled. They use to help each other.
