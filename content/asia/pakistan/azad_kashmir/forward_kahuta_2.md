Travel Guide

Forward Kahuta, Bagh Azad Kashmir: almost 170km away from Islamabad/Rawalpindi, located in District Bagh on the foot of Pirpunjal Region. Temperate climate during summer and cold during winter. Hilly area woven by a net of streams and rivers. If you ever visit this place, you will find that God has bestowed it with extremely unique climate. People are very much hospitable and friendly.
