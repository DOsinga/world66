Travel Guide

It is a very beautiful small city of kotli situated among the mountains.

There are beautful mountains on all sides of the city and at the top there is plan area in which there is the city so called "Charhoi".

All the basic facilities of life are available in the city.Now it is the Tehsil of kotli.Most of the people of charhoi are settled in foregin countries.
