type: section
---

local Suzuki Vans & minibuses are availabe from the chowk near Hamid Pur Colony which goes past the village of Barwan on the way to Chakswari shopping centre.

On the outskirts of Chakswari as you pass Barwan on the Road to Dadyal - you will see the Mosque with a very tall minaret in the Village of Hamid Pur colony. If you local they wiil give your directions towards a famous tree above Hamid Pur Colony. This Tree (Jand) was revered as a holy place - where a unkown Fakir/dervish lived.

Also, when you pass through Chakswari and head towards the Mangla Reservoir - if it is around February/March time the waters recede and the area in bone dry. Try to visit the tomb of 'Zinda Peer' (living holy man) near the old Gorah Domal area. You will need to pass the old "Racha-Rama" well - once pass adha-ne-palye.
