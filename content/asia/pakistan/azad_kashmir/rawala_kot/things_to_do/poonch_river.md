title: "Poonch River Valley"
type: poi

The Hajira-Kotli road follows the Poonch River for much of its length, about 35 kilometers from Rawalakot. Several spots along the route offer views of the wide river passage cutting through the hills. The water is broad enough in places that it has real presence in the landscape.

There is no single destination here — it is more of a scenic drive with places to pull over and take in the river views. The valley floor sits at around 900 meters, significantly lower and warmer than Rawalakot itself.
