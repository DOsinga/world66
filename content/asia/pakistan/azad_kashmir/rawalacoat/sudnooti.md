Sudhnoti is a district of Azad Kashmir immediately to the south of Poonch. Its headquarters is Pallandri, a town at lower elevation than Rawalakot with a warmer climate and more agricultural landscape. The district's hill areas — including the villages around Gorah — connect up through forested roads to the Rawalakot valley.
