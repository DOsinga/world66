Travel Guide

Toli Pir, a hilltop 30 kilometres from Rawalakot, It is located at the drive of 45 minute from city.

Take a glance at the panoramic view around and you will realize why Iqbal had gone overjoyed: “If paradise can be on earth, it is this same place.” And why had the emperor Jahangir on his deathbed said he desired “Nothing but Kashmir.”
