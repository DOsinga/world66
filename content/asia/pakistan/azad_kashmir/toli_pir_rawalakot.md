Toli Pir is a hill station about 30 kilometres from Rawalakot in Azad Kashmir, reached by a winding mountain road that takes around 45 minutes. At roughly 3,000 metres, the summit offers sweeping views across the Kashmir valleys on a clear day. It is a popular day trip from Rawalakot, particularly in summer when the cooler air provides relief from the heat below.
