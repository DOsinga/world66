Bahawalpur was one home to several Nawabs (rulers) and hence is referred to as the Princely state of Pakistan. Situated in the Punjab province, there are several places of interest in and around Bahawalpur for tourists. Several **Bahawalpur**** day trips** can be planned from this area.

**Uch Sarif**

Uch Sharif is believed to the seat of ancient civilization and is situated 75 kilometers from Bahawalpur. You can hire a car or a mini bus from Bahawalpur and visit Uch Sarif. Tour operators in Pakistan will be able to help you with the travel arrangements. It is believed that the civilization at Uch existed since 500 BC. Today Uch Sarif is famous for its ancient historical monuments, breathtaking shrines and tombs. The city of Uch is divided into three parts and named after three famous personalities.

On your visit to Uch, plan your day before you travel, as there are several places of interest. Covering all of them in a day trip may not be possible. The must-visit places at Uch Sarif are the tomb of Bibi Jawinidi, which is a beautiful monument covered with several tiny blue tiles all over, presenting a spectacular effect in the sunlight.

Another must-visit place is the Shire of Jalaluddin Bukhari, who was a popular saint. There are several festivals that take place in Uch in the honor of the Sufi saint Jalaluddin Bukhari in September and October every year. These festivals are a different experience and a must visit if you are in Uch during these months.

**Lal Suhanra National Park**

The Lal Suhanra National Park is situated 36 kilometers from Bahawalpur and sprawls over more than 77,480 acres on both the sides of the Bahawalpur canal. This park is an ideal tourist site if you want to just relax and enjoy the nature and also have some adventure. The park has tourist huts, camping grounds and rest houses. There are treks arranged for interested tourists and nature lovers. On your visit to Lal Suhanra National Park you can get to see ravine deer, black bucks, nilgai, jackals, larks, hawks and several endangered species. The Lal Suhanra National Park is a must-visit day trip spot on your visit to Bahawalpur. Tour operators in Bahawalpur can help you arrange for transport to reach Lal Suhanra National Park.
