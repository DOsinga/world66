A massive 18th-century fortification rising from the Cholistan Desert, with 40 circular bastions and walls 30 metres high. The interior is closed but the exterior, the adjacent Abbasi Mosque, and the royal tombs are accessible. Around 100km south of Bahawalpur; requires a 4WD or desert-capable vehicle.
