---
title: "Eating Out in Bahawalpur"
type: section
---

Bahawalpur is located in the province of Punjab and is one of the largest cities in Pakistan. Travelers head to the city of Bahawalpur to visit the famous palaces and immerse themselves in its culture. In the city you can find many bazaars and shopping centers such as the Shahi Bazaar. Apart from visiting the sites Bahawalpur has to offer, you can experience its rich culture by eating out. Bahawalpur is a city that offers restaurants of various cuisines at different price ranges. Here are some **Bahawalpur Restaurants.**

You can start your day with popular Pakistani food found in Bahawalpur, such as the roti or naan*.* Maybe you are looking for something with a kick like a lamb curry or some spicy barbecued meat. Many street vendors offer delicious drinks that go well with a hearty meal, such as chai (tea) or lassi (yogurt drink). Another dessert unique to this area is the sohn halwa, a mix of dry fruits and nuts. To find out about the food that Bahawalpur has to offer you can ask the residents, because they are the best source of information to help you.

**Desert Grill Tandoori Restaurant**

Going to Bahawalpur is not complete without trying this restaurant, which serves local food and delicacies. This restaurant is popular with tourists and locals alike. Specialties of Desert Grill include the boneless chicken achari handi and fish tikka.

**Four Seasons **

This popular restaurant located on Railway Road offers an array of cuisines ranging from Pakistani and Mexican to Chinese and Continental food. This Bahawalpur food establishment also offers a soft drinks and has a bar built to look like a classy wine bar.

**La Taska Village Restaurant**

This is also a popular food destination. Specialties of this Bahawalpur restaurant include dishes such as balochi chicken karhai, served with roti and raaita.

**Panda**

Eating out in Bahawalpur** **should include a meal at this Chinese restaurant. Also located on Railway Road, this places serves a piping hot Szechuan Beef, among other Chinese dishes.

**Pan Pizza**

If you are craving a more familiar taste, then Pan Pizza** **is the place for your late night meal. This fast food restaurant in Bahawalpur serves locals and tourists pizza with various toppings to agree with the spicy palette of the region.

**Disneyland Sweets**

To complete your experience eating out in Bahawalpur, try this familiar sounding sweets shop. Disneyland Sweets serves up ice cream with many flavors, which is a delicious treat - especially during the summer months.
