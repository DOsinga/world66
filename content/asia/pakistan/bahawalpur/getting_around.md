Getting around in Bahawalpur is getting easier all the time. **Bahawalpur transportation** still isn't as reliable as that found in other countries, but you will be able to get to and from your destination without too much hassle.

**Rail Network**

The rail network in Pakistan is very extensive. Main rail lines run throughout major cities in Pakistan, and are fairly easy use. When traveling on the rail network, you should try to sit in an air-conditioned compartment, as the train can get very hot and crowded. Children under three years old travel for free, and children eleven years and younger travel for half the fare. Students traveling on the rail network also receive a fifty percent discount.

**Driving**

When driving in Pakistan, all traffic drives on the left. You have to be at least eighteen to drive in Pakistan. The roads are not maintained well, and it is not advisable for tourists to drive. Driving during the evening is also not advised, as the roads are poorly lit. Drivers should keep their doors and windows locked at all times. If you are considering driving while in Bahawalpur, make sure to have your international driving permit or national license with you at all times. One or the other is required while driving in Pakistan.

**Bus**

There is an extensive bus service in Bahawalpur. The bus is an inexpensive way to travel, and is usually very reliable. The buses travel throughout the whole city. They tend to get very crowded, and finding a spot to sit can sometimes be a challenge. Make sure to carry exact change with you, as the bus drivers do not give out change.

**Taxis**

Taxis are the most efficient way to get around while in Pakistan. Taxis are reasonably priced and are a safe method of transportation. Taxis are normally metered, but you should still ask the price before getting in. Trying to find a taxi after sunset during Ramadan can be difficult. If you are traveling during Ramadan, arrange other means of transportation.

**Private Driver**

If you are looking to get around without the hassle of public transportation, hiring a driver is a great method of transportation. Hotels and travel agencies can normally arrange a driver. Hiring a driver is an expensive way to get around, but a very safe and reliable method.
