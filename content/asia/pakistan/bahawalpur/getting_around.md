---
title: "Getting Around"
type: section
---

Rickshaws and local minibuses cover the city centre and inner suburbs cheaply. Taxis are available for longer trips and can be hired by the day for excursions to Derawar Fort or Lal Suhanra — negotiate a price before setting out. For Derawar Fort, a 4WD vehicle is advisable for the final desert track. Bahawalpur is connected to Lahore, Karachi, and Multan by Pakistan Railways, with the station in the city centre.
