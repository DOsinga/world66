---
title: "Shopping in Bahawalpur"
type: section
---

Bahawalpur is the city in the north of Pakistan located just next to the border with India and on the edge of Cholistan desert. **Bahawalpur shopping** is quite interesting experience. Next to the high class glamorous things selling in shops all around the city you can find shops with amazing local handicrafts from the Cholistan desert, probably never seen before. Some of Cholistani handicrafts are: chungirs which are actually bread plates made from date palm leaves*, *weavings of cotton and camel hair, used as carpets, wall hangings, etcrillis which are all-purpose cotton blankets, handmade dupattas (long scarves), kurtas and khalitis (ladies' handmade purses).

**Aziz Plaza**

This is a great place to buy imported Thai goods. It is located just a bit to the left from Farid gate; it just takes 500m of walking. Tourists can get mobile phones and all kind of tech goods in this plaza but the prices here are slightly higher than in other malls.

**Bobby Plaza**

Bobby Plaza is just meters away from Aziz Plaza and this is excellent place for buying clothes, shoes and imported goods from Europe. Tourists who are fans of designer clothes can get them here for low prices but they should be aware that most of these things are not genuine. The prices, on the other hand give the impression of genuine so it is quite often to see people bargain for the price of goods that has Gucci sign on them.

**Dubai Plaza**

Dubai Plaza is on the circular road just on the other side to Quaid-e-Azam Medical College mosque. This plaza is well-known because tourists can buy almost every kind of mobile phone here. DVD&rsquo;s, TV&rsquo;s, all kind of house supplies are available here. Tourists can find quite cheap goods and they can always bargain for the price.

**Bahawalpur Trade Center **

This building has probably everything that a person needs. The building has six huge shopping floors and big parking lot in the basement. This is not just a shopping center but a business building so you can find offices of companies and tourist agencies there. Restaurants are everywhere so there is no need of leaving this place at all during the day. There is even a sports center, spa and sauna.

**Local Bazaars of Bahawalpur**

Probably these are the most interesting places to shop and they are all around the city in the small alleys. The local goods from the desert can be found here--also clothes, jewelry and tech goods. Just beware of pickpockets.
