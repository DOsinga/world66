Burewala is a very important city & Tehsil of District Vehari (Punjab, Pakistan ) .On the map you can find it at Latitude = 30.16°N and Longitude = 72.67°E .It is 35 Km east of District Capital Vehari on main Multan-Delhi Road. Its population in 1998 was about 150000 which increased to about 174700 in 2004 (statistics from The World Gazetteer ) .Burewala is 31st biggest city of Pakistan with respect to population ....
