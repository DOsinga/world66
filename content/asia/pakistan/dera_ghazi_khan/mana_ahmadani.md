Travel Guide

Mana Ahmadani ( Urdu : احمدانی منا ), is a town and union council of Dera Ghazi Khan District in the Punjab province of Pakistan . [1] It is located at 29°47'58N 70°35'3E and has an altitude of 111 metres (367 feet). [2]

Mana Ahmedani has been named for Mana Khan Baloch, who was tribal chief of Ahmadani tribe. The prominent tribe of the town is Ahmadani Baloch. It is the last union council of DG Khan district bordering Rajanpur District .
