Travel Guide

This one is the biggest Mohallah of Taunsa city.It is basically old taunsa.there r a lot of castes in this Mohallah but Buzdar r dominated in this Mihallah.Here some one named Mr.Jaffar is land lord and is owner of many plots.
He is selling plots in very much high rates.He is thinking Mohallah Nizam abad as Defence of Taunsa City.
Now a days there are Nutkani,Malghani,Qaisrani,Rind and other castes r coming to live in this Mohallah.
Many people r migrating from villages to Taunsa City for their children to study.
