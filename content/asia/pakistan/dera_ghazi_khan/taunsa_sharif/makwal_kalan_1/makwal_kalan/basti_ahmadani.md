Travel Guide

Basti Ahmadani Is Last Village of the Tehsil D.G khan at the border of the Tehsil D.g Khan And Tehsil Taunsa. 80% of the population of this village is educated. The Only tribe here is Ahmadani Balooch. this village was named "ahmadani" due to ahmad khan numberdar.who was founder of this village.
