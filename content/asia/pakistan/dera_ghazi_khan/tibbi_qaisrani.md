Tibbi Qaisrani (Tibbi Kaura Khan Qaisrani) is a growing union council of tehsil Taunsa in the Dera Ghazi Khan District of Punjab, Pakistan, with a population of approximately 30,000. The area is predominantly agricultural.

## History

The town is named after Sardar Kaura Khan Qaisrani, a local hero who fought against the British during the colonial era. Originally known as Tibbi Kaura Khan, the town was later renamed Tibbi Qaisrani. PTV (Pakistan Television) produced a play called "Kaura Khan" as part of its "Wafa Kay Paikar" series to honour his legacy.
