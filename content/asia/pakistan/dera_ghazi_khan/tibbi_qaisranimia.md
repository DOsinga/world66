Travel Guide

*Tibbi* Miana (qaisrani) is a developing city of Tehseel Taunsa Sharif in the district of D G Khan. Its population consists of nearly 30,000 people. The concept of education is mounting among the people.

Political awareness is developing in the people. Basically it is an agricultural zone so the main occupation of the region is farming. But due to advancement of the world, the new generation is going towards the education.

The sports and games often and mostly played in this area include kabaddy, cricket, volleyball, and rarely, football.
