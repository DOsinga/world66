Travel Guide

Yaroo Khosa is very important town in Distt D G Khan in Tuman Khosa. It is located at distance of 15 Km in the north of DG Khan city. This town has major vote bank of Khosa tribe but Khosa Sardars never put any attention to problems of this region. The majors problems include basic necessities of life like education, health and electricity.
