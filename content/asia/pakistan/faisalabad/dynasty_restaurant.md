A well-known restaurant at Kohinoor Chowk in the heart of Faisalabad, Dynasty serves a broad menu of Pakistani and North Indian dishes — biryanis, karahis, grilled meats, and a solid selection of vegetarian options. The location near the city's famous clock tower bazaar area makes it a convenient stop while exploring Faisalabad's commercial centre. Portions are generous and prices reasonable by local standards.
