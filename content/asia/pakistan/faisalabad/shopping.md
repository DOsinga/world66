---
title: "Shopping in Faisalabad"
type: section
---

**Faisalabad Shopping** opportunities abound. If you find yourself in Faisalabad for business or simply a leisurely family getaway, the amount of shopping available is sure to amaze. No matter what you are in the market for, you will probably be able to find it in one of Faisalabad's numerous shops and marketplaces.

**Bazaars**

There are several Bazaars located in Faisalabad like those in Ghenta Ghar, one of the city's oldest shopping areas. Here you will find traditional Indian crafts like hand loomed clothing. You will also find some of the many products that have made Faisalabad famous, such as the textiles that are a major part of the industry in this large business city. The bazaars are open air markets that contain a wide variety of objects ranging from antiques to homemade goods, to every day needs.

**Shopping Malls**

There are many shopping malls in Faisalabad and more popping up every day. Malls like D-Ground, Rex City and Kohinoor One offer a wide variety of modern merchandise for the enjoyment of every visitor.

Rex City is a large computer factory outlet where you can purchase goods like software, hardware and CDs. There are several shops within Rex City, each offering different types of computer oriented merchandise at discount prices.

The Hassan Shopping Mall is much like the shopping outlets you would find in any industrialized nation. You'll find merchandise at discount prices in addition to food and fun. This is a great place to get some new clothes or shoes, a music CD or a sound system. Window shopping is also a great way to spend the evening.

**Roadside Stalls and Trendy Boutiques**

If you are looking for something a little more out of the ordinary you should be sure to visit some of Faisalabad's many roadside stalls or specialized boutiques. These places offer the best of what Faisalabad has to offer. You will find specialized fabrics, indigenous crafts, homemade goods, authentic treats and much more. Many of the boutiques and small shops are located within walking distance of the larger city shopping malls. This makes it extremely easy for visitors to make a day out of shopping during their time in Faisalabad.

Though the town may be known for its business and industrial trade, there are few places in the world that offer this magnitude of shopping. No matter what you are looking for you should be able to find it in one of Faisalabad's numerous shops.
