Travel Guide

Ganish Khun is popular place in Hunza. Ganish Khun is awarded by UNESCO in the year 2002 for its tradition, the 800 years old mosques and battle fort is it's popularity, especially the Tamurkutz Battle Tower. But this fort is under use of some invaders and these invaders are using this fort for their own purpose. the people of Ganish Khun want to see this battle tower free of invaders.
