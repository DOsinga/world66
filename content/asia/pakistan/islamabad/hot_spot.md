A homegrown Islamabad institution that started as a simple ice-cream parlour and grew into a small chain with branches in F-7, F-6 Hill Park, E-11, and Saidpur Village. The menu now covers milkshakes, sandwiches, pies, and light meals, but ice cream remains the draw. The decor has a quirky, artistic feel that sets it apart from the city's more generic cafes. A good stop for a cold treat on a hot Islamabad afternoon.
