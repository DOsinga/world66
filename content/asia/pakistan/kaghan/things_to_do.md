title: "Things to Do in Kaghan"
type: section
---

You may find that a list of **Kaghan Valley things to do** may be quite long. There are many destinations and hidden sights that you can discover in this popular summer holiday spot in Pakistan. If you are into the outdoors or would like some tranquility in your vacation, then Kaghan Valley is highly recommended.

#### Lake Saiful Muluk

When you get to Kaghan Valley, make it a point to get to Lake Saiful Muluk, which is the most popular attraction in the valley. This bowl-like lake is about 10 kilometers from the beautiful city of Naran. You can get to Lake Saiful Muluk by jeep or if you dare, you can always trek up the 8-kilometer hiking trail. If trekking is not your thing, then you can always enjoy a pony ride, which you can hire in Naran, along this legendary lake.

A romantic legend behind Lake Saiful Muluk brings many tourists to the lake, and with the crystal blue waters and picturesque views, it is no wonder that many people do visit the lake.

#### Shogran

A visit to Kaghan Valley is not complete without a trip to Shogran. This beautiful plateau is 2,362 meters above sea level and about 33 kilometers from Balakot. You can choose from several hiking paths in this area like the Dona Meadows or the Sri Paya. Hiking to Sri Paya will take you about 2 hours and 30 minutes, but the trek is well worth it, with lush, green meadows and beautiful lakes at the end of the trip. For the more experienced hiker, Dona Meadows is recommended, since you have to cross a huge glacier and two waterfalls to get there.

There are also several nature resorts in this area that provide many outdoor activities. Most of these resorts or hotels are surrounded by green mountains and pine forest, so you can take your pick.

#### Trout Fishing

If you enjoy trout fishing, then you will truly enjoy your trip to Kaghan Valley. There are several lakes in the valley where you can fish for brown or rainbow trout. You can also go to Kunhar River, which is one of the more popular fishing areas in the valley. Lake Dudipat Sar is another favorite trout fishing spot, which can be reached by foot from Basel. The middle of June is the best time to visit Lake Dudipat Sar, since the weather is more pleasant for walking and fishing.

You do need a fishing license before you can cast your pole. Fishing permits are available from the Fisheries Department located in Shinu or at Naran.
