title: "Gadani Beach"
type: poi
latitude: 24.977307
longitude: 66.705958
---

Two hours away from karachi main city. White sand beach, perfect for a swim. Its best to with a group of friends or families to go for the whole day and enjoy swimming. It is not crowded or polluted which makes it more fun to swim. The tides are high usually, but not surfable. There are no lifeguards there, although locals are helpful.
