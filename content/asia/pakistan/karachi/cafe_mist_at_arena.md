Cafe Mist sits inside the Arena entertainment center on Karsaz Road, a large family recreation complex that includes bowling, ice skating, and a gym. The cafe itself focuses on mocktails, desserts, and light bites in a modern setting. It's a reasonable pit stop if you're already at Arena with family, though it's more of an entertainment-complex food court experience than a standalone cafe destination. The complex stays open late (until 1am on weekdays, 2am on weekends).
