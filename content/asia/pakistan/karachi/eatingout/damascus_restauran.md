Damascus Restaurant founded in 1999 has been one of Karachi's favorites ever since. Serving a large variety of Authentic Arabian delights comprising of Shawarmas, Furrouge (Flame Grilled Chicken), Pizza (Crust-filled, & thin crust with optional toppings), Falafel, Pita Sandwiches, and now one of the world's finest beef or chicken Burgers.
Located in Karachi's posh Clifton locality, Damascus Restaurant offers casual seating arrangement for gentlemen and another separate one for families, groups of friends, & couples to enjoy their meals or sheesha (dozens of fruity sheesha flavors to choose from) under the open sky comfortably.
With Damascus came the trends of Crust Filled Pizzas, Flame Grilled Chicken (no added fat), Sheesha (also hookah or narghile), fusion of Arabic & world cuisines like Pita Sandwiches, Mal Batata, & Mufalfal to Karachi.
If you're looking for a nice time out without having to deal with cramped up seating at most fine-dining restaurants or the stuffy atmosphere at sheesha cafes, Damascus is the place to be.
Open for deliveries & take aways during lunch seven days a week. Seating available around sunset up to the wee hours of the morning.
