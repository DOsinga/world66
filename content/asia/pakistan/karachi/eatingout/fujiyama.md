The first thing guests notice when arriving at the newly refurbished Fujiyama Japanese restaurant, perched on top of the Avari Towers iconic skyscraping hotel, (tallest building in Karachi) are the magnificent and breathtaking 360 degrees panoramic views of the city, now greatly enhanced by floor to ceiling windows which have recently been installed. This being just one of the impressive architectural features of the new Fujiyama.
