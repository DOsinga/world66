A local restaurant with a vast menu. Everything on the menu sounds familiar but tastes vaguely like the named version. The localization of international cuisine is in full swing here. The restaurant isn't too big on presentation and lacks finesse. Still worth the visit if you're in the neighborhood but it has to be noted there are several much better alternatives available in the city.
