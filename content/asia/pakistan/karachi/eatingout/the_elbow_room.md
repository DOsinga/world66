The ambiance of The Elbow
Room is that of an older era. Its antique decor allows the
mind to wander into the times gone by. Its inviting atmosphere
is a perfect getaway for hearts and minds that want a break
from the tiring, and sometimes, monotonous professional life. And
best of all, now you don't have to go a long way to get away.

The
Elbow Room's in-house menu mainly
comprises of two different cuisines, Continental &
Pakistani. The specialty being the mouthwatering steaks and
juicy chicken dishes, made with the most fresh of ingredients
and presented with an art. They also have a wide variety of
cocktails which cannot be found anywhere else in the city.

By popular request, The Elbow
Room is now open for dinner and will assist you with your entertaining in the evenings.
The Elbow Room, with its exciting evening ambiance and unique specialty
dishes, is the perfect locale to host your dinner parties
and small celebrations. You can reserve The entire restaurant
for your private
parties and events and they can assist you in arranging for live
entertaining as well. There are different packages available
to suit all of your requirements. Come and discuss it over a cup of tea.
