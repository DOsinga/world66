title: Sandspit Beach
type: poi

A long sandy spit southwest of the city, calmer than Clifton and good for swimming from October to March. Sandspit is also a nesting ground for green and olive ridley sea turtles, with the WWF's wetland centre managing conservation work here. Horse and camel rides available.
