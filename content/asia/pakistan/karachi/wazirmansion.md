title: Wazir Mansion
type: poi

The birthplace of Muhammad Ali Jinnah, the founder of Pakistan, in the Kharadar neighbourhood. The three-storey balconied house has been preserved as a national monument and contains artefacts relating to Jinnah's life and the independence movement.
