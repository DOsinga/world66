Originally called Bhinwar Fort, this Cholistan desert stronghold was built by Rawal Bhim Singh in the early 17th century. A Babri-script inscription on the gate records its founding date. The fort is deep in the desert, about 80 kilometers southeast of Khanpur, and in a dilapidated state. Getting there requires a vehicle suited to sand tracks, but the isolation is part of the appeal.
