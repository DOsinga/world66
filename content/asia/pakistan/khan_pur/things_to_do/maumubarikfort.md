---

One of the older fortifications in Cholistan, Mau Mubarik Fort was built by Raj Hans Karar as a residence for his mother (mau means mother in the local language). It was captured by Shah Arghun in 1525. The fort had 20 bastions and towers, with ramparts about 550 meters in circumference. Inside the ruins stands the shrine of Sheikh Hakim, still visited by locals.
