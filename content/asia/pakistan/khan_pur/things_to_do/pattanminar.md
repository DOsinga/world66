The ruins of Pattan Minar lie about 8 kilometers southeast of Rahim Yar Khan. The site has been variously identified as an Ashokan-era structure from the 3rd century BC or a Buddhist monastery. Remains of a fort, a mosque, and tunnels are visible around the minar. The site was partially excavated in the 19th century by a British political agent but the work was never completed, leaving much of its history still buried.
