#### **Traveling from Abroad**

You can reach the town of Kohat in Pakistan via air transport. As a rule, reserving your room before the scheduled date and paying in full are advisable for discount travelers. There are hotels which do not accept foreigners, so confirm your intentions directly with the hotel before booking. Since it is a developing town, do not expect luxurious hotel rooms and high-quality services. Lodging in chain hotels like Holiday Inn and Pearl Continental, which is in nearby Peshawar (31 miles away), is another good option. If you are on a tight budget, you can opt for the small hotel rooms, like Green Hill Hotel.

The wildlife and lush vegetation of Kohat are worth a tour, too. The beautiful Indus River and a number of wonderful tributaries are inexpensive places to visit and enjoy.
