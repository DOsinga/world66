The heart of Pakistan, Lahore is a great place to travel on a budget. Its many sights and variety of attractions make stretching your dollar easy to do without cutting out the fun and attractions.

**Travel and Accommodations**

Clearly the cheapest way to get around would be by foot but also, Lahore taxis can get you to any attraction at a reasonable price. Saving money on transportation costs is easiest if you make taxi appointments ahead of time and negotiate a price to your destination. As far as accommodations, the budget friendly hotels are located conveniently downtown near the train station. This location is beneficial also because it is close to most of the downtown attractions.
