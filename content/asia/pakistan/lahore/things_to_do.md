---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Lahore's sights concentrate in and around the **Walled City** at the north-east edge of the modern city. The Badshahi Mosque and Lahore Fort, facing each other across the Hazuri Bagh, are the set piece — together one of the most impressive Mughal ensembles in South Asia. The Wazir Khan Mosque, the Walled City gates, and the bazaars along the way repay a slow day on foot. The Shalimar Gardens east of the centre are the other unmissable Mughal site; Minar-e-Pakistan in Iqbal Park is where modern Pakistan was declared.
