Travel Guide

Isak khel is a famouse village of district lakki marwat. it is situated in north of lakki marwat city at a distance of 12 Km drive. all basic facilities of life are available here. One degree college for boys, high school for boys, 4 primery schools for boys, one middle and one primery school for girls are working. bhu, ptcl exchange, mobile network, drinking water supply line to every house is available.
