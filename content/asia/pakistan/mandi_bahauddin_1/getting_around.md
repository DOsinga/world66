**KASHMIR STREET**

Kashmir street is a very famous street of sufi pura and whole city mandi bahauddin. Kashmir street is somehow seperate from other muhallah as sufi pura is in two parts one is in the north of canal and other is in the south of canal so kashmir street is in the southern part. People of kashmir street are very hardworking and highly qualified. Almost 6 lawyers 2 doctors, police officers, mobile engineers and lecturers belong to this specific street.More than 10 students are studying abroad in different parts of the world like Europe,USA,UK and Australia. Many good players of cricket (like Mudassar Shah, Raza Amin, Khurram, Usman, Hassan,Waleed, Tahir) and football who are representing mandi bahauddin in pakistan belong to kashmir street.
