**Visit Islamabad, the Capital of Pakistan
**

One of the best **Murree day trips **you can take is to Islamabad, the capital of Pakistan. Islamabad is located approximately 30 kilometers southwest of Murree and is easily accessible by car via the Murree Road. As the capital city, Islamabad has the best infrastructure in Pakistan, and it is also one of the greenest cities in the region. Despite its role as the capital, Islamabad is only the tenth largest city in Pakistan, which makes it a relatively peaceful place to visit. The city is home to the Faisal Mosque and the Margalla Hills National Park, which are two of the major attractions.

#### **Faisal Mosque
**

The famous Faisal mosque is the largest one in South Asia and the sixth largest mosque in the whole world. It was constructed by the famous Turkish architect, Vedat Dalokay. The mosque covers an area of five square kilometers, with a total capacity of up to 300,000 worshippers and has four impressive minarets at a height of 80 meters each. The architecture is very modern and does not correspond to the traditional South Asian Islamic style, which resulted in some criticism from more conservative Muslims.**
**

#### **Margalla**** ****Hills**** ****National Park****
**

Right behind the Faisal Mosque is the Margalla Hills National Park. It is protected by numerous non-governmental organizations, which care for the preservation of the local flora and fauna. Margalla Hills is covered by a lovely pine and oak forest. The park has some excellent hiking trails and is a must for every visitor to Islamabad.
