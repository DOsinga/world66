Murree, being one of the most popular summer destinations of people from Islamabad as well as other neighboring countries, is famous for its great number of **Murree restaurants**. People simply can&rsquo;t resist hopping from one diner to another since each restaurant has something to offer.

Murree is a resort town in Pakistan which boasts of historic landmarks, churches and fun nightlife. There are also very affordable shopping centers around where you can buy great finds to take home and show to your friends. Here are some of the most visited Murree restaurants you may check out for yourself:

**Murree Tabbaq Hotel and Restaurant**

A hotel and restaurant rolled into one, this is a top place for tourists who prefer to spend their vacation comfortably. The hotel rooms are cozy while the in-house restaurant serves different menus from breakfast to dinner. It is also open to guests from early in the morning to midnight.

**Red Onion **

If you prefer to dine in a place where you can try the flavors of America, India, China and native delicacies, this is the place to be. The atmosphere of this restaurant is varied. You can stay in a corner where you can chat the night away with friends while there&rsquo;s also a place for those who value their privacy.

**Saffron**

The breathtaking view of the hills and valleys can make your dining experience in Saffron memorable. They serve continental and native dishes like chicken karahi, chicken makhani and many more. You may also opt to dine outside since they have a patio where you can enjoy your meals under the stars.

### [Red Onion](/asia/pakistan/murree/eating_out/red_onion)

Chain of Restaurants situated on the main mall road, stands one of the most modern restaurant in Murree. Established in 1993.

this restaurant offers a wide range of cuisines not offered anywhere else in the vicinity i.e Mexican, Italian, Chinese, Pakistani & Continental. Prices are very moderate, ambiance is modern & customer service is very friendly!

type: International



address: Opposite TDCP Tourist Information Centre Mall Road

### [Saffron](/asia/pakistan/murree/eating_out/saffron)

An elegantly decorated restaurant with stunning views of the surrounding hills and valleys. Saffron offered the ultimate personalized culinary experience. The menu is a blend of continental and local favorites including our very own rendition of the ever-popular chicken karahi and chicken makhani. Saffron lies overlooking crisscrossing paths, beautiful hills and valleys.

type: Local



address: Shangrila Resort Hotel
