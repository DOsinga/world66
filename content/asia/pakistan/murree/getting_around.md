---
title: "Getting Around"
type: section
---

Most of Murree is walkable along the main ridge road. To get here from Islamabad, hire a car from Faiz Abad Bus Stand in Rawalpindi (around 350-700 Rs depending on your negotiating) or take the Daewoo Coach from the Rawalpindi Peshawar Road terminal (about 100 Rs, ninety minutes). Non-air-conditioned buses run for 35-60 Rs. For trips to Nathia Gali and the Galyat area, ask your hotel to arrange a driver who can double as a guide.
