title: Lawrence College & Church
type: poi

A Gothic-revival boarding school founded in 1860 during the British era, set among pine trees on the Murree ridge. The attached church is one of several colonial-era churches in town. The grounds are not always open to visitors, but the buildings are visible from the road and worth a look for their Victorian stonework.
