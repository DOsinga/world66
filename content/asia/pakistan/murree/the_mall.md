The pedestrian road connecting Kashmir Point and Pindi Point, and the social heart of Murree. Shops, restaurants, and street food vendors line both sides. It fills up in the evenings when families come out to walk, browse, and eat corn on the cob. The GPO Chowk area in the centre is the busiest stretch.
