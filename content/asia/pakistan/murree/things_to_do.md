This guide will discuss **Murree things to do**,** **as there are plenty of activities you can indulge in. Murree is a beautiful town located in Pakistan, built in 1853. #### Shop at Mall Road

Mall Road is one of the most visited places in Murree. People go there to browse the markets, souvenir shops and restaurants. The road is closed off from traffic and is often more crowded during the evening.

#### Explore Kashmir Point

Kashmir point is loved and cherished by locals and tourists alike. Many people come and visit Kashmir point during the day, to experience everything the point has to offer. It&rsquo;s a great location to take pictures of the area, to hike or picnic.
