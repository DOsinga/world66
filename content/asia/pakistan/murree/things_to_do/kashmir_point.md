title: "Kashmir Point"
type: poi

latitude: 33.9141
longitude: 73.4047
---

The viewpoint at the northeast end of Murree's ridge, looking across the Jhelum Valley into Azad Kashmir. It is a favourite spot for families, photographers, and horse riders during the day, and at night the twinkling lights on the surrounding mountains draw evening strollers. Tea stalls and snack vendors line the approach.
