---
title: "Eating Out"
type: section
---

Nushki has a modest but lively strip of local eateries along Jinnah Road, with tikka houses and chaat stalls doing the main business. A few options:

- Sana Ullah Tikka and Chaat House, Jinnah Road
- Seeth Chaat and Samosa, near Chand Medical, Jinnah Road
- National Hotel and Restaurant, Masjid Road
- Sajjad Hotel, Masjid Road
