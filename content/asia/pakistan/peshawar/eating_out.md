---
title: "Eating Out in Peshawar"
type: section
---

There are many places to choose to eat at when you are looking for **Peshawar restaurants**. You can choose from a number of local and custom dishes that are served in the city. You will find that the variety of Pakistan dishes and other cultural favorites offer you a lot of variety to choose from.

#### Habibi Restaurant

The Habibi restaurant is one of the great places to go to if you want to eat some of the best local food and cuisine. You can enjoy the flatbreads and other foods that are served in the restaurant. The colorful decorations and the unique look to the restaurant are complimented by the excellent staff and service. The restaurant is affordable and offers a variety of different flavors.

#### Shiraz Restaurant

The Shiraz restaurant is a great place to enjoy some of the traditional Pakistan food and also enjoy some of the Chinese favorites that are served. The restaurant has great service, and you can enjoy the unique look to the building. There are not a lot of dishes to choose from on the menu, but the dishes that are served offer a lot of great tastes.

#### Balana Restaurant

The Balana restaurant is a great place to enjoy the cuisine in the city. You can enjoy the affordable prices and choices that are available on the menu. If you are looking for a great place to get some of the best Pakistan food and try some of the local dishes, you can choose this as one of the Peshawar restaurants to eat at.

#### Pearl Barbeque Continental

The Pear Barbeque Continental restaurant is located on the top floor of the Continental hotel that overlooks the city. You can enjoy some of the best views of the city and enjoy trying some of the different menu items that are available. If you like to enjoy a quiet and relaxing meal on top of the roof and look out across the city, you can find this to be one of the Peshawar restaurants to choose while you are in the city.

#### Spring Rose

The Spring Rose restaurant serves a variety of dishes including many different cuisines from Pakistan and some continental choices are also available. You can enjoy the decorations at the restaurant while you relax and savor the menu items that are available.
