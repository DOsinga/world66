title: "Getting Around in Peshawar"
type: section
---

Finding the best way to enjoy the Pakistan area can be easier when you find the best **Peshawar transportation**. You can experience some unique ways to travel around the Peshawar area, including taking a camel to get through some of the terrains.

#### Bus

The city bus does offer good service around the city and is very affordable. The buses are confusing to figure out when you are first using them because there are four stations that are held in the town, and all of the buses leave and enter the stations going in different directions. If you are able to figure out the schedule and how the buses work, it can be a very good way to get around in the city.

#### Taxis

There are taxis available in the city, and they are very affordable. They are a little more than you will spend on the buses, but you can also get exactly where you want to go. You can find that there are many taxis to catch throughout the city.

#### Horses or Camels

You can get around the outskirts of the city by taking a horse or camel. They are very affordable and easy ways to travel outside of the city. Many people find that they can explore more of the Pakistan area and see what the culture is about when they are traveling by a camel or a horse. They are available to use for different day trips and are very affordable. It is a great way to see how the culture uses the animals to transport themselves and goods.

#### Trucks and Cars

The city has a number of unique trucks and cars that the local citizens use to attract people. You will be able to enjoy the decorated trucks and cars that travel around the city and can take you to where you need to go. If you are looking for a fun and unique way for Peshawar transportation, you may want to try finding one of the colorful trucks or cars to get where you want to go.

#### Walking

You can get to see more of the sights in Peshawar if you travel by foot. Because of the climate and desert areas, you will want to wear light layers of clothing and good shoes. You can walk around the town and catch a ride from one of the local services available if you want to relax. Many people choose walking as one of the Peshawar transportation methods to use.
