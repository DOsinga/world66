title: "Nightlife and Entertainment in Peshawar"
type: section
---

The city of Peshawar is recognized as one of the oldest cities in Asia. **Peshawar**** nightlife **consists of clubs, exploring the city and restaurants. It is recommended by the local government that visitors interested in nightlife outside their hotels should travel in groups as there are a number of refugees in Peshawar.

**The Picture House**

** **The Picture House is a must-do in Peshawar. For people interested in spending a day as Peshawar native, they need to catch a movie at the Picture House. The building is one of the oldest in the city and located in former residence. Movie bluffs will enjoy the old fashion movie experience. The movies are shown in Urdi and have English subtitles. The cinema is famous for the billboards around the city advertising the Picture House. Large and colorful, the billboards and posters will make just as much an impression on the visitor as the local culture does.

**Khan&rsquo;s Club**

Khan&rsquo;s Club is rated as one of the 10 best restaurants in Pakistan and is also a boutique hotel. The hotel and restaurant were an original private home that was converted in 1995 to the Khan&rsquo;s Club. The owner, Mr. Davis, spent 13 years redesigning the house into the current hotel. This restaurant is famous for the world class Afghani cuisine featured on the menu. People looking for a true Pakistani experience need to stop by the Khan club. Walking into the club, the patron will be amazed by the d&eacute;cor. Khan&rsquo;s Club is decorated in a theme right out of Arabian nights. There are no chairs--people sit on cushions and there is live music playing in the background. A number of ex-pats come to this restaurant to socialize.

**Pearl Continental Hotel**

The Pearl Continental is a western hotel near the center of the city. The location of this hotel is great for people who spend the day exploring and want a quiet place to spend the evening. There are several restaurants in this hotel that provide live music for customers. There are number of different foods featured from local to western dishes. Visitors can also use the pool at night. With 24-hour security, people feel right home in this small piece of the city. The gazebo is a nice way to enjoy watching Peshawar. The bar invites travelers to come down and socialize. For people craving romantic night, the hotel offers a nice way for them enjoy it without having to join a group. It has been recommended that visitors go out in groups and that single people and couples are more vulnerable.
