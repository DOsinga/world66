The old Storytellers' Bazaar at the heart of Peshawar — for centuries the place where caravans from Central Asia offloaded, told their tales, and exchanged goods. Still a working market: dried fruit, nuts, brass, carpets, and the city's famous *chapli kebabs* grilling on charcoal. Go on foot in the cool of the morning.
