---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Peshawar's core is the old city — a warren of narrow lanes in and around **Qissa Khwani Bazaar**, best seen on foot in the cool of early morning. Climb to the terrace of **Bala Hisar Fort** for the skyline view; visit the **Mahabat Khan Mosque**, one of the finest Mughal mosques outside India. The **Peshawar Museum** is the city's one essential museum — the Gandharan Buddhist sculpture collection is unmatched anywhere.
