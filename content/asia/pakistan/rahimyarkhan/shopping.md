There are many things to see and do in the Pakistani city of Rahim Yar Khan, and here are some **Rahim Yar Khan shopping **places:

**Clothing**

With cotton and embroidery being one of the primary cottage industries of the town, it is no surprise that the clothing here is of a very high quality. There are two main services for the average tourists – traditional ethnic clothing and tailor made clothes. The former is available at most of the marketplaces, and generally is a result of the work of the women who stay at home to weave and embroider the costumes that come out of the weaving. These costumes are reflective of the traditional ones that are worn in Pakistan, even today, and consist of colorful cotton dress and flowing shawls or scarves. The latter service, on the other hand, is generally provided by the men – tailors who run shops in the market square.

Tailor made clothes are cheap, so be sure to get some new suits, shirts, trousers or skirts while in Rahim Yar Khan.
