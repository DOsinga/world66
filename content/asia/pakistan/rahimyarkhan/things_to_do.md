Among the favorite **Rahim Yar Khan Things to Do** are a Desert Safari, visiting the mosques, mausoleums or lighthouses. Rahim Yar Khan is a major city located in the southern part of Pakistan and also the capital of Rahim Yar Khan District. If you are planning to travel to this city, there are many beautiful places to visit and things to do.

#### Take a Desert Safari

This self-guided safari through the desert of Cholistan offers a chance to view a variety of wildlife. With special permission, one can also go hunting for wildcats, Chinkara deer, a variety of pigs or jackals.

#### Visit Bhong Mosque

Located approximately 40km from the city, is the Bhong Mosque. This mosque considered as the most beautiful mosque in the country. It was constructed by Rias Ghazi Muhammad and has many beautiful designs as well as many marble stones. This mosque, with its array of chandeliers is a work of architecture.

#### Visit Pattan Minara

This ancient lighthouse is located on the bank of the river Hakra or Ghagra. Although most of the lighthouse is in ruin, the tower still stands. It is said to have remains of a monastery. Also nearby are a tunnel and the remains of an old fort. Colonel Minchin began excavating the tunnels more than 100 years ago but discontinued it for some unknown reason. Fazal Halani destroyed it in the 18th century and used the materials to construct two forts.

#### Explore the Many Mausoleums

Many saints are buried within the main district of the city and visiting these mausoleums is a unique experience. The buildings themselves have so much architectural character and it is definitely a must-do while you here.

#### Enjoy Walking through the Garden of Heaven

This beautiful garden was built by Meer Syed Abid Hussain, Esq. After his death, his son Meer Syed Abid Hussain, Esq. reconstructed it
