---
title: "Top 5 Must Do's in Rahim Yar Khan"
type: section
---

When you are traveling to Pakistan, the **Rahim Yar Khan Must Do&rsquo;s **are going on your own personal desert safari, visiting the Bhong Mosque and the Pattan Minara lighthouse and exploring the many mausoleums and the renowned Garden of Heaven.

#### 1. Experience Your Own Desert Safari

If you have ever wanted to go on a safari, Rahim Yar Khan offers one of the most interesting, self-guided safaris there is. You can observe many of the variety of wild cats, pigs, jackals as well as many other exotic animals. If you wish, with special permission, you can also hunt many of the wild animals roaming the Cholistan Desert.

#### 2. Visit the Bhong Mosque

Mosques have always been an important part of the Islamic culture. The Bhong Mosque is one of the most beautifully decorated structures in the area, making use of many materials such as marble, teak and ivory throughout the mosque. Built in 1932, this beautiful mosque houses prayer rooms as well as a library. The mosque and main compound took over 50 years to build using the expertise of craftsmen from Pakistan and India.

#### 3. Explore the Pattan Minara Lighthouse

One of the places you may want to explore is the remains of the Pattan Minara lighthouse. This lighthouse was considered to be the shell of a monastery built by the Buddhist monks. Also nearby are the ruins of tunnels, a mosque and a fort. More than 100 years ago, the tunnels had been excavated but the process was stopped for a reason that is still unknown.

#### 4. Discover the Mausoleums

In and around Rahim Yar Khan are numerous mausoleums to explore. Mausoleums were built to bury the saints in the district and are visited by tourists from all over the world. These mausoleums are beautiful works of architecture and a definite must-see while you are in Rahim Yar Khan.

#### 5. Walk around the Garden of Heaven (Bagh-I-Bahisht)

This 75 acre garden, with its manicured lawns, huge flower gardens and tended roads is a beautiful place to visit while in Rahim Yar Khan. With flowers and plants from all over the world, the Garden of Heaven is easily one of the most popular tourist stops in Rahim Yar Khan. Originally built by Meer Syad Abid Hussain, this lush garden was reconstructed by his son. Meer Syad Abid Hussain&rsquo;s grandson now upholds the garden&rsquo;s original beauty. Although the garden is privately owned, it is open to the public on specific days.
