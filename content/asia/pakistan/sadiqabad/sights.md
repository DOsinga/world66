Sadiqabad is a city located in punjab, Pakistan. Its name after the ameer Sadiq who is the nawab of bahawalpur state.

Geography

The most prominent Tehsil of District Rahim Yar Khan is inaugurated in 1948. The geographical cordinates of Sadiqabad are 28 18 0 North 70 8 0 East.

Sadiqabad is the last city of punjab province. It is in between Sindh and Punjab border, while traveling from Lahore to Karachi by rail or road. This Tehsil stands as transitional area between the two provinces where sindhi and punjabi cultures survive together, along with Sindhi culture on its western boundry like in kot-sabzal, sanjar pur or Ghotki District of Sindh and on its northern side Rajanpur District of Punjab.The Indian border is about 25km on the southern side Sadiqabad.

Sadiqabad is the largest Tehsil of the District Rahim yar khan.

Most of the people of this city are punjabi and seraiki speaking. Pathan, sindhi, Blochi and urdu speaking also have residency.

total Population 760,769 which is according to survey of Mr. Rana Amjad Naseem

**Transportation**

In the field of transport, this city is very prominent due to the fact that it has got double story railway station for the stops of almost all express trains. As for the road transport air-conditioned and non-air-conditioned buses and coaches connect the city with all the major cities of Pakistan.
