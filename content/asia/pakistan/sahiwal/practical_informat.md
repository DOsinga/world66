Heaven Fonder

Domestic Tour Operator

First time in history of Sahiwal, in private sector we are operating Domestic Tours For all over Pakistan and especially for Northern Areas of Pakistan. It's a non profit organization working to promote the domestic tourism. They have a qualified staff fully devoted towards promoting the tourism at their best.

This organization starts to provide information from 2004 at a domestic level.The managing Director of this Organisation is attached with a Govt Registered Tour Operators in Northern Areas of Pakistan. It only focused on providing information to the people of Sahiwal for nothing. Information of the areas, Maps, Literatures, Pictures, Movies of the areas.

The aim of this organization is only to give awareness to the people of Sahiwal about this industry and make their trips more unforgettable.
