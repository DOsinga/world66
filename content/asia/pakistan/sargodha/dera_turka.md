Travel Guide

Dera Turka is situated in the north east of Sargodha district near Kot Awan (Chak 35 NB). In this village, more than 75% Awan cast present from last century. Awan migrated from Khushab due to allotment in Sargodha region. Malik Ghulam Muhammad Awan Turka is the most popular personality of the region. Malik Allah Yar Khan Bagha is very much found of OX. He is also a big name of 35 NB.
