Travel Guide

Shahpur is a historical city situated in district Sargodha, Pakistan. Once this city was an important cantounment and district in the era of British empire. In 1935 due to floody area its recognition as district was changed to Tehsil. There in Shahpur city remains of a fort are present. On these remains people has built their houses. Now this city consis of population of above 70,000.
