***Iqbal's Birth Place , Iqbal Manzil*** is situated on Iqbal Road in the old city.

1800 years old ***Sialkot Fort*** is in the center of the city on Fort Road .

***Imam Ali ul Haq shrine*** is on Imam Sahib Road .

Sikh Shrine (***Bari Sahib***) is located on Zafarwal Road .

***Jinnah Stadium*** (International Cricket Test Centre) is located on Circular Road .

***Sialkot cathedral*** - This 150 years old cathedral is located in cantonment area.

**Clock Tower** is the most famous landmark of Sialkot , situated in Saddar Bazar.

**Puran Bhagat** and **Hamza Ghaus** are is another historical site situated near cantonment area.

***Local Industry*** - Visit Sialkot Chamber of Commerce to see local products. You can ask them to arrange visit local manufacturing units (Sports goods, garments, leather, surgical instruments, music instruments).

**Gulsan i Iqbal Park** , **Sialkot Park, Garrison Park, Alladin Fun House** , ***Company bagh***
