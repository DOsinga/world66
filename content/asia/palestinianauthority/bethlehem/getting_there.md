---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

Bethlehem is just 10 km south of [Jerusalem](/asia/israel/jerusalem). Arab shared taxis and buses run frequently from the Damascus Gate area in Jerusalem's Old City. You will need to cross the Israeli checkpoint at the separation wall — on busy days this can take time. From the Palestinian side, regular transport also connects Bethlehem to [Hebron](/asia/palestinianauthority/hebron) and other West Bank towns.
