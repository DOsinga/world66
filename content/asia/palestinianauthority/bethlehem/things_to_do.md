**Murad Tourist Resort **http://www.muradtouristresort.com

This beautifully landscaped resort, on the outskirts of Bethlehem, has four large sized swimming pools and a children’s pool with playground, they provide unique Turkish caves that accommodate to all your spa needs, including Jacuzzis, saunas, Turkish baths, and physiotherapy rooms. Relax amidst natural surroundings with an amazingly priced spa package and accommodations in one of 20 Chalets. With a beautiful restaurant to top off all your Mediterranean Cuisine needs.
