Ramallah is the capital of the Palestinian Authority and also the place where most things happen. For an evening out, eating or dancing, this is probably the best place in the West-Bank.

 The town itself does not have many sights and things to do. It is hardly more than a big suburb of [Jerusalem](/asia/israel/jerusalem). There are many administrive buildings of the Palestinian Authority but you are not very likely to be interested in those.
