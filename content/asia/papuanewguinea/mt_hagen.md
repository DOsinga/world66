Travel Guide

Growing up as a kid in MT Hagen some 40 years ago proved to be an adventure. We and the local District Commissioner had the only two 4 wheel drive landrovers in the town. Other modes of transportation were tractors, which were flown in by DC 3's. I haven't lived there since 1971 but would welcome any updates.
