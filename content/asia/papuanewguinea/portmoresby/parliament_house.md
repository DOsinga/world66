A striking 1984 building in the Waigani district designed to echo the "haus tambaran" spirit houses of the Sepik river, its soaring facade inlaid with traditional motifs. Visitors can sit in on debates when parliament is in session — worth it for the unique atmosphere of a Melanesian democracy at work.
