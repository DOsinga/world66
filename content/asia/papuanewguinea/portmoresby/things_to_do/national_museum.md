The national collection of Melanesian art and ethnography, housed in a modernist building on Independence Hill next to Parliament. Sepik masks, Asaro mud masks, canoe prows, ancestor figures — an excellent grounding for anyone heading on to the Sepik or the Highlands. Closed Fridays and Saturdays.
