With its lagoons, tiny islands, and north coast drive through vast coconut plantations, Madang lives up to its reputation as the prettiest town in the Pacific. The waterfront is lush with parks, ponds, and waterways, and the diving and snorkelling here rank among the best in Papua New Guinea.

The islands around Madang are worth the boat trip. Kranket Island has traditional villages where you can watch daily life unfold, while Long Island draws birdwatchers with its abundant species. Several of the volcanic islands offshore are still active, adding a dramatic backdrop to an already stunning setting.
