---
title: "7 Day Itinerary in Angeles City"
type: section
---

This** Angeles City itinerary** will help you plan your holiday
and enjoy the best Angeles City has to offer. From historical places to
beautiful scenery, Angeles City has it all.

**Day 1 - Old Pamintuan Residence**

Old Pamintuan Residence has been the pride of Angeles City for many
years. The building served as the previous sanctuary of Gen. Emilio
Aguinaldo at the time of his revolutionary government. This is also the place where the first anniversary of Philippine Independence Day
took place.

**Day 2 - Fort Stotsenburg**

This is the perfect destination if you want to know more about World
War II. Being a historical sight, Fort Stotsenburg speaks volumes about
World War II. Fort Stotsenburg is situated in Barrio Sapang Bato,
approximately 80 km from the north of Manila. This is one of the
fundamental landmarks in the city where the training on coastal artillery was conducted under the National Defense Act of 1935.

**Day 3 - Kapampangan Museum **

The Kapampangan Museum is a popular tourist spot within the city.
Also known as the Clark Museum, this museum displays numerous artifacts
that are indispensable to Philippine history and culture. Many
travelers visit the museum for the showcased relics that reflect the
lives of the natives during World War II.

**Day 4 - Mount Pinatubo**

A once-in-a-life time exciting tour to Mt. Pinatubo will surely be a
great experience for the whole family. Spend time with your buddies
while hiking its different trudge points and going toward the peak,
passing through the sandy cliffs. Take in the fantastic surroundings and
enjoy swimming in the crystal blue waters around the crater.

**Day 5 - Nayong Pilipino**

Being the only cultural park in the country, Nayong Filipino is
globally famous for presenting the whole Philippines in a “miniature
view.” Nayong Pilipino provides tourists a taste of the country's
ever famed landmarks with a showcase of the rich Filipino culture.

**Day 6 - Salakot Arch**

Visit the Salakot Arch, a monument that is viewed as the native hat
which is a symbol of the 1978 Philippine-American contract to institute
Philippine independence over the different bases of U.S. in the country.
This led to the formation of the famous Clark Air Base Command (CABCOM).

**Day 7 - Bayanihan Park (Formerly Astro Park)**

The Bayanihan Park is a mini-amusement park where you and your kids
can enjoy some recreational activities like playing basketball or
volleyball on the large courts. You can also take a walk or jog and
enjoy the beautiful scenery.
