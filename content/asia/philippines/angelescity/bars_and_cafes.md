title: "Bars and Cafes in Angeles City"
type: section
---

**Angeles City bars and cafes** have a notorious reputation for being sex tourism spots and even if you just go to a regular bar you may have to be prepared for the fact that you might be hassled by sex workers.

**Carousel Bar**

First opening in 2007, the Carousel features a rotating stage, 110 dancers and 40 waitresses. Patrons can sit inside and enjoy the entertainment but be wary that brownouts (electrical power-outs) can be a regular problem here. There is however a generator in case of such incidents. The opening hours are 6 p.m. to 2 a.m. This venue is located at 14-4 Fields Avenue, Balibago and is well worth the visit.

**Oasis Pub**

For those wishing to avoid the sleazy side of Angeles City bars and cafes, this is truly an 'oasis'. You can relax with a stiff drink and some excellent local live music laid on by a truly broad selection of acts. The bar is fully stocked with all the international favorites: wine, beer, spirits, liquers. Monday is jazz night, Tuesday is more 1980s and retro-oriented, Wednesday is light jazz, and Thursday is where fans of acoustic, R&B and alternative music can enjoy themselves. There is also a great value happy hour (or happy 2 hours) from 6 p.m. to 8 p.m. every night when all local beers are half price.

**Dencio's**

A little further out, in the Clark Economic Zone is this fantastic eaterie-cum-bar. A full range of drinks is served alongside mouthwatering Filipino delicacies such as lechon kawali and a plethora of barbecued and sizzling plates.
