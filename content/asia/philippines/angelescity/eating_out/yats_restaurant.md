Where to eat, eating out, wine and dine in Angeles Philippines

Yats Restaurant and Wine Lounge located in Clark Philippines, near Angeles City is open 7 days a week for lunch and dinner. Yats Restaurant serves French and Italian Mediterranean cuisine. Compared to many fine-dining restaurants in Manila and hotel-based fine-dining outlets, prices at Yats Restaurant are very reasonable.

Quality of food is second to none and the menu contains both familiar fares done to perfection and exquisite gourmet dishes involving ingredients flown in from all over the world.

The wine list of this fine-dining restaurant is legendary not only for its collection of rare labels but more for its eclectic selection of over 2000 labels of aged vintage wines available at mid-range (US$50 to $200) prices. In the cellars of Yats Restaurant, you will find aged vintages from all regions, including new-world wines at remarkably low prices.
