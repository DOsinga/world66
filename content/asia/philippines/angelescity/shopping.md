---
title: "Shopping"
type: section
---

Angeles City has a good spread of malls and markets, from the big Philippine chains to duty-free outlets in the Clark Freeport Zone. Haggling is expected at street markets and smaller shops.

**SM City Clark**

The local branch of the Philippines' biggest mall chain. Clothing, jewelry, handcrafted items, restaurants, cinemas, and a children's amusement center — everything under one roof.

**Robinsons Place**

A newer mall near the downtown area and the airport. Good for imported products at low prices, local brands, souvenirs, and a spacious supermarket with fresh produce.

**AsiaLuxe**

Located in the Clark Special Economic Zone, AsiaLuxe specializes in Philippine arts and crafts, particularly handcrafted pearl jewelry — necklaces, bracelets, earrings, and jewelry boxes adorned with Tahitian Mother of Pearl.

**Duty-Free Shopping Mall**

Also in the Clark zone along MacArthur Highway. Imported groceries, chocolates, snacks, and canned goods at duty-free prices. A popular stop for stocking up.

**Street Markets**

The city's street markets are where the real bargains are. Locally made clothing, shoes, crafts, toys, jewelry, and electronics — bring your haggling skills.
