---
title: "Top 5 Must Do's in Angeles City"
type: section
---

Get to know the **Angeles City Must Do’s** if you are planning to travel to the entertainment capital of the Philippines. Set in Pampanga, Angeles City is known for its luscious cuisine and its surprisingly vibrant nightlife. If you are set to discover more of the city, be sure you don’t miss out on these Top 5 Must Do’s.

**1. Bar Hopping**

Angeles City is known for its abundance of such establishments. Dance to the loud and modern music and spend the night away at the city’s best clubs and bars. Dollhouse and La Pasha are just some of them. However, this is not advisable for families who want a nice bonding night.

**2. Food Trip**

Angeles City is also popular for its expertise in culinary arts. That is why a trip to this city is incomplete if one forgets to do some food explorations. Yes, a trip to the restaurants is quite an adventure you must not overlook. You also have a choice over local and Italian cuisine. C Italian Dining, Sombrero Snacks and Bretto’s are only some of them. Watch your diet, though.

**3. Park Strolling**

Since Angeles City is all about life and fun, strolling at parks near Roxas Highway is another must-do. Do some walking while you enjoy the culture-rich sites offered to you by the best parks in the city. The Astro Park is one that offers an access to the Juliana Theater Lounge and other commercial establishments. This is perfect for families who want to talk and bond while walking.

**4. Shopping along Fields Avenue**

Of course, you have to get some memoirs of the city for yourself. Certainly, shopping for the best buys is necessary and is not hard at all, especially if you do some of it at outlets along Fields Avenue. Get some clothes at Ocean Shirts at reasonable prices. More stores are also present in the area.

**5. Trekking or Riding Planes**

If you think you have almost done everything in this wonderful city, you might want to consider doing some trekking to complete your vacation. Most tourists also visit Angeles City to get a view of the scenic sites that surround it. You can either trek or ride a plane if you want to. Plane tours allow you the opportunity of seeing the Crater Lake and Mt. Pinatubo’s wider scope. Tour rates start at $180.
