Shopping in Binan ranges from a modern mall to the weekly local market where you can find handmade crafts and regional food products.

#### The Pavilion Mall

Binan's only proper shopping mall, right off the Old National Highway. Two buildings house over 150 shops: retail and apparel in one, food chains and a cinema in the other. The mall's arrow-shaped design is a local landmark.

#### Local Market

The weekly market is where locals sell handmade goods, native bags, wooden instruments, longganisa (native sausage), dried fish, dried mangos, coffee, rice cakes, fruit candies, and more. This is the best place to find authentic local products and to see everyday Binan life in action.

#### Resort Boutiques

The various resorts around Binan have their own boutiques carrying higher-end goods. You do not need to be a guest to browse, but prices reflect the resort setting.

If Binan does not have what you need, Manila is a short trip north and has everything.
