---
title: "Eating Out"
type: section
---

Tagbilaran has a modest but growing restaurant scene, centred mostly around the downtown area and the road to Panglao. Street food stalls and local eateries serve Filipino staples at low prices, and a few places stand out for fresh fruit shakes and grilled seafood.

**Garden Cafe** on JS Toralba Street is a long-standing local favourite, known for its fresh fruit shakes.
