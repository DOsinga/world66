A quiet little cove on the northwestern side of the island, far removed from the bustle of White Beach. You can drive in by tricycle, but it is much nicer to rent a small boat and sail around the coast. There is a small resort here with a handful of cliffside cottages and a restaurant perched at the top — if you don't fancy the steps, your food and drinks come down the cliff in a basket. The water is calm, the snorkelling is good, and you won't be hassled by vendors.
