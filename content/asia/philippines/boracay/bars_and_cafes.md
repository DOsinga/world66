---
title: "Bars and Cafes"
type: section
---

Boracay's nightlife is its other defining feature. White Beach turns into a long open-air bar after sunset, with everything from quiet beachfront cafes serving cold San Miguel and rum cocktails to thumping clubs around Station 2 that go until dawn. The party concentrates in the middle of the beach; the southern end (Station 3, around Angol) is where the more relaxed, reggae-leaning bars live. Beachfront fire dancers are a nightly ritual.
