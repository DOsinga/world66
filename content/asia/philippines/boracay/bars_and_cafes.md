---
title: "Bars and Cafes in Boracay"
type: section
---

Boracay, an island under the administration of the government of Aklan province, has been recognized as having the best beaches in the world, and **Boracay bars and cafes** will rescue you from lonely nights. These bars and cafes are there to entertain you and make sure that the nights you spend in Boracay are unforgettable.

**Pier One Grill and Bar**

Pier One Grill and Caf&eacute; is a well-known bar in Boracay that will give you, your family and friends a sumptuous meal. It is overlooking the White Beach, giving you a more relaxing atmosphere.

**Summer Place Bar and Restaurant**

Summer Place Bar and Restaurant is one of the places in Boracay that stays open late at night. If you want to have fun at night to the fullest, this bar is the one you are looking for. Many tourists come to dance as the music is played. In addition to that, beer, cocktails and snacks are available.

**Bom Bom**

Bom Bom is a small bar in the island that plays reggae music. In this bar, the young and old tourists meet to take pleasure in sitting on the wooden and bamboo seats while the ethnic music is played. They also have live jam sessions at times, while the tourists have fun sipping their beer and cocktails.

Boracay has a proliferation of little bars and cafes to counteract the lively party scene, some of the most popular being Cafe del Mar, Bom Bom Bar, Pat's Creek Bar, Red Pirates, Lemon Cafe and Bamboo Lounge.
