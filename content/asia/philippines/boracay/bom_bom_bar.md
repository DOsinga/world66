If you're tired of the loud hip hop and R&B that dominates much of White Beach, Bom Bom is a refreshing alternative. The funky decor pulls in an arty, musical crowd from all over the world; they play a mix of reggae and ethnic sounds with live music most nights. The bar itself is tiny but there's a large garden out back with chairs and tables knocked together from driftwood.
