A bright, lemon-coloured cafe in the middle of D'Mall, and a welcome refuge after a hot morning's shopping. The menu runs to breakfasts (eggs benedict, pancakes), inventive sandwiches like the Thai lemon chicken ciabatta, and lighter mains such as grilled mahi-mahi on warm potato salad. The fresh fruit juices — the mint and pineapple crush in particular — are worth the visit on their own.
