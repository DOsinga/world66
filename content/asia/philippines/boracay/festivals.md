---
title: "Festivals in Boracay"
type: section
---

Boracay Island is the number one tourist destination in the Philippines, and there are many fun **Boracay festivals**. Its crystal clear water and white sand beaches make the place a paradise. Many tourists do not only visit the island because of its wonderful beaches, they also come to the place for the festivals:**
**

**Ati-Atihan Festival**

Ati-Atihan is a famous festival not only in Boracay Island, but also in the whole Philippines. This is an annual festival celebrated on the third week of January in honor of their patron saint Sto. Nino. To commemorate this, the people put charcoal all over their body and dress up with colorful and attractive costumes.

**Food Festival**

The Food Festival is a grand food exhibition in Boracay. Everyone flocks to witness this festival to have a taste of a variety of Filipino food delicacies.

**Beach Reggae Festival**

Every May 11 to 14, Boracay holds the Beach Reggae Festival. The reggae music is enjoyed by the tourists while having fun partying.

**Dragon Boat Festival**

Experience the exciting adventure that the Dragon Boat Festival can give you! It is a sports competition, and the crowd can enjoy watching the colorful boats as they go across the water. After the competition, a series of beach parties and shows follow.

**Boracay**** International Beach**** Festival**

Boracay International Beach Festival is the most popular festival in Boracay. Thus, you should not miss the opportunity to witness this event. This is celebrated in the months of October, November and December. The Boracay residents and guests unite as one to commemorate this festival.
