title: "Getting Around in Boracay"
type: section
---

Boracay Island is just a small island, and if you are planning to see the beauty of the whole island, some **Boracay transportation** is available for rent. You can rent a motorcycle, tricycle or a bicycle to tour around the place and see the magnificent views.

**Bicycle**

Most of the resorts have a bicycle for rent. You need to pay a rental fee of 50 pesos for an hour of use. For safety reasons, you need to check the brakes and the pedals of the bicycle. Be sure that both the brakes and the pedals are working properly so no accidents will happen. Always be alert with your surrounding as you start to bike. Be sure that you are in the right lane while biking, and don&rsquo;t forget to be vigilant of the tricycles that might bump you.

**Motorcycle**

The rental fee for a motorcycle is 150 pesos per hour. You can ask the personnel in the resort where you are staying if you want to rent this mode of transportation. However, the use of a motorcycle is not recommendable for foreigners. Philippines&rsquo; road conditions and driving rules might be different from your country. If you really want to get around the island by riding a motorcycle, then you must have an international driver&rsquo;s license.

**Tricycle**

A tricycle is one of the most famous modes of transportation in the country. It is a motorcycle with a side car. The tricycle can enter the White Beach for a fare of 5 to 20 pesos. If you want to rent it, an hour of using it costs 150 pesos.
