---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

Boracay has no airport of its own. You fly into one of two airports on Panay — **Caticlan** (MPH), a fifteen-minute drive from the jetty, or **Kalibo** (KLO), a two-hour bus or van ride away. Caticlan is the easier hop but the airport is small and prone to weather delays; Kalibo handles bigger jets and most international flights, including direct services from Korea, China and Hong Kong.

From the jetty at Caticlan, a short *banca* boat ride (about fifteen minutes) drops you at one of three jetty ports on Boracay depending on the season and the wind. There is an environmental fee, a terminal fee, and a boat fare to pay — keep small notes handy. Most resorts will arrange a porter and a tricycle to your room if you have booked ahead.
