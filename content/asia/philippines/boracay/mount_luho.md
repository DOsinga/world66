Mount Luho is the highest point on Boracay at around 100 metres, and the viewpoint at the top gives a sweeping panorama over the whole island and the surrounding sea — White Beach to the west, Bulabog to the east, and the green hills running north towards Puka. You can drive most of the way up by tricycle and walk the last stretch, or hire a quad bike from one of the rental outfits near the road. Best at sunrise or late afternoon, when the light is kinder.
