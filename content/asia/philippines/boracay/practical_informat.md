**Basic Things to Know About Traveling to Boracay**

This particular **Boracay practical information** will be useful if you are going to travel to this small island paradise in the Philippines. Boracay is a famous destination for locals and foreign tourists alike because of the fact that it is blessed with a long stretch of beaches. Recently, Boracay was voted as one of the best beaches in the world. For visitors, it is important to be able to learn the basic information about the island, such as its language and climate.

**A Multi-Lingual Island **

The original language of most people from Boracay is a dialect native to the region called Bisaya*.** ***This is a mix of Malayan, Spanish and American, which makes it difficult for most foreign tourists to communicate with the locals. However, there are a lot of tourists living in the island. Thus, Boracay is slowly emerging as a multi-lingual island in the Philippines. A lot of locals can speak good English as well as understand some Japanese and Korean, so you will be able to communicate with the locals effectively.

**Boracay Has Two Seasons**

Boracay basically has two seasons, which are the wet and dry seasons. The summer in Boracay occurs from March to June, while the wet season sets in from July to February, which also happens to be Boracay&rsquo;s off season. Although this is the case, there are still a lot of tourists who visit the island during July to August and December to January because of the discounted hotel rates.
