**BUTUAN CITY GENERAL INFORMATION **

**BY Melfrank A. Lugod, Bit-os, Butuan **

**“In the beginning, there was no Philippines but there was BUTUAN”**

Located at the Northeastern part of Agusan Valley sprawling across the Agusan River is Butuan City, known for its colorful history and culture. BUTUAN is a term believed to have originated from the sour fruit “Batuan”. Others opined, it came from a certain “Datu Buntuan”, a chieftain who once ruled Butuan. Scholars believed, it came from the word “But-an”, which literally means a person who has sound and discerning disposition.
