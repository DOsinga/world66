title: "Beaches in Butuan"
type: section
---

**Butuan Beaches **are famous in the world for their beauty. Here are some of the beaches that can't be missed while on your Philippines vacation:

**Cubi**** Cubi Beach******

The Cubi Cubi Beach is the perfect spot for a family outing, or for couples who simply would like to spend quality time with each other. This is a very popular tourist destination, and during the peak seasons, it can get very crowded with people. However, the popularity of the place simply means that transportation also comes much easier, and that buses come to this beach more frequently than others. The waters can be classified as &ldquo;G-Rated&rdquo;, considering how the waves are far gentler and the waters are warmer during the non-monsoon season. Restaurants and bars line the side of the beach in a slightly Miami fashion, and resorts are everywhere in the area, providing a good choice of accommodations for the visitors to this area.

**Tinago**** Beach******

Also in Butuan City is the Tinago Beach, where everything is marketed as being trendy and swanky. The Tinago Beach is truly a city beach, where stylish hotels and resorts can be found, and where the swankiest bars in town are located by the beachside. The place provides a sharp contrast where the traditional nipa huts are found against the backdrop of such a trendy area, and there are the best spots to stay for backpackers looking to experience the true culture of the Philippines. In the day, the beach is known for its serene waters that are popular amongst visitors bringing along their children. The fine white sand is the true highlight of the beach, and provides a good contrast to the clear blue waters.

**Vinafor**** Beach******

The Vinafor Beach is easily the beach diving site in Butuan, and according to the Tourism Board, is one of the top 5 beaches in the city. This is the place for the adrenaline junkies who enjoy a thrill ride above everything else. The activities are endless, from water skiing to snorkeling, but the true star is the diving here. Experienced divers will enjoy the deep waters that are simply teeming with marine life, while rookies will enjoy the comprehensive diving lessons that are provided by some of the agencies around. The night view of the beach is spectacular as well, with the light hitting the water and creating pretty patterns that dance in the moonlight. It is the perfect place to end the day with a slow stroll by the water.
