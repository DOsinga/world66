---
title: "Day Trips in Butuan"
type: section
---

**Butuan day trips** can be enjoyed while visiting this city in the Philippines. Butuan is found at the northeastern part of Agusan Valley. It is among the first developed cities in Mindanao. Recently, Butuan is becoming a quickly advanced city.

**Jurassic Resort**

To enjoy your Butuan Day Trip, visit the Jurassic Resort in Carmen. Jurassic is at the south part of Butuan. You can reach this resort by hiring a van or a jeepney that costs 1500 pesos. You can get cheaper transportation if you negotiate outside the hotel. At the Jurassic Resort, you can order food and beverages, and have it served to you in your own nipa hut that floats over the water. Jurassic Resort also offers snorkel and swim adventures. There are small bancas, as well, that are offered in the resort. This banca will take you for a tour up or down the coast for P20 or less. You can stay for a night in one of their cottages and avail of the facilities that they offer.

**Butuan**** National Museum**

Butuan National Museum is where you can find the historical and cultural materials and artifacts. This museum has two exhibit galleries: the Archaeological Hall and the Ethnological Hall. Specimens of stone and metal crafts, woodcrafts, burial coffins and other archaeological diggings are exhibited at the Archaeological Hall. On the other hand, the contemporary cultural materials of the people of Butuan are shown at the Ethnological Hall.
