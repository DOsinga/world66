---
title: "Eating Out"
type: section
---

Seafood is plentiful given the bay and river; grilled fish, kinilaw (raw fish cured in vinegar), and shrimp dishes are common. Weegol Grillhaus on Montilla Boulevard serves grilled chicken (chicken inasal) and Filipino food on banana leaf — the local way to eat. Jonies Flavored Chicken inside Gaisano Mall is popular for halal chicken dishes.
