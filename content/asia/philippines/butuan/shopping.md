---
title: "Shopping"
type: section
---

Gaisano Mall and Otis Metro Mall on JC Aquino Avenue are the main shopping centres. Madyaw Kadyaw sells locally made handicrafts, Manobo textiles, and hand-made jewellery at reasonable prices. The Langihan Public Market on Satore Street is the best place for fresh produce, seafood, and cheap handicrafts. Pasalubong centres near the city centre and at terminals stock local sweets and the region's Laksoy rice wine.
