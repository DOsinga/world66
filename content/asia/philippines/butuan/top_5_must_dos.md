Here are some of the top **Butuan must do&rsquo;s** while visiting this popular tourist hot spot in the island of Mindanao. Here are a few suggestions to help you enjoy your stay here in Butuan.

**Mount Mayapay**

Take a trip to Mount Mayapay which is one of the highest peaks in Butuan City and the most amazing natural landmark in the whole CARAGA Region. Mount Mayapay is located in the southwestern district of Butuan, near the Agusan Valley. This 700-meter tall mountain is the most-favored destination for mountain climbers and adventurers. You will truly enjoy vast greenery, rich flora and fauna and diverse wildlife of the mountain.
