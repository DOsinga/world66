Located thirty minutes ride to Oquendo from Calbayog plus 15-minute hike to the Spring.

As the name suggests, “mapaso” literally means hot. Fresh crustacean (pokot) abounds. They are naturally pinkish resembling scalded shrimps due to the high temperature.

try to visit there and just relaxing..
