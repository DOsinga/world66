---
title: "Festivals"
type: section
---

The Lanzones Festival in the third week of October is Camiguin's biggest celebration, marking the harvest of the sweet tropical fruit that grows across the island's hillsides. The week includes street dancing, a food fair at Mambajao, and the chance to join the harvest on farms around the island. The San Juan Hibok-Hibokan festival on 24 June, honouring St John the Baptist, is celebrated on the north coast beaches with boat races and water sports.
