Volcanic hot springs channelled into a series of pools in the hills above Mambajao. The water emerges at around 40°C and cools as it flows downstream, so the lower pools are more comfortable for soaking. The setting — lush forest, the sound of the river, cooler mountain air — makes this the most pleasant of Camiguin's several hot spring sites. Open day and night; most pleasant in the evening.
