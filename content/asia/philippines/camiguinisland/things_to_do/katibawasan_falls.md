latitude: 9.1578

---

The most accessible of Camiguin's waterfalls, Katibawasan drops about 70 metres into a cold emerald pool surrounded by dense forest, five kilometres southeast of Mambajao. The pool is safe for swimming. A small entrance fee is charged at the park gate. Tricycles from Mambajao reach the trailhead, from which it is a short walk through jungle.
