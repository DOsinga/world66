---
title: "When to Go"
type: section
---

The dry season from March to May is the most reliable time for beach activities and diving. The Lanzones Festival in October draws visitors to a normally quiet island, and is a good reason to visit in the shoulder season. Typhoons can affect Camiguin from June through November, with August and September the most active months — though the island's position south of the main typhoon belt means it is less exposed than Luzon or the Visayas. Year-round temperatures are warm and humid; the cooler months from November to February are generally pleasant for hiking.
