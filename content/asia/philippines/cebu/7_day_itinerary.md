---
title: "7 Day Itinerary in Cebu"
type: section
---

If you are fortunate enough to stay in Cebu City for a week you can enjoy many things if you follow this 7-day itinerary of Cebu City.

**Day One-Santo Niño Basilica**

People who have the luxury of staying in a place for more than a day should get a real feel of the area. They should get immersed in the traditions and the folklore of the place. This is the reason why the first day of your trip in the city of Cebu should be spent visiting the Santo Niño Basilica. The relic of the Holy Child was a gift from Magellan himself to Queen Juana.

**Day Two-Cebu Country Club**

For those families that want to be in a foreign land but like the luxury of a state of the art recreational complex, the Cebu Country Club could offer that and more. There are great golf courses for those people who want to practice their swing. For the teenagers there are swimming pools where they can swim and just relax. There is also a food court and a badminton court to play in. The whole day can be full of activity and rest.

**Day Three-Tahusan Beach**

A 7-day travel itinerary of Cebu City cannot be complete without a trip to Tahusan Beach. The drive to Tahusan beach is a heartwarming experience in itself. On your drive on the way to Hinunangan you could see the rice fields and you can also look at the coast as it unveils right before you.

**Day Four-Crocolandia**

The whole family will be amazed when visiting Crocolandia, a nature reserve found in Cebu City. The reserve has crocodiles and one of the most majestic ones is Lapu Lapu who some people think may have been named after the Pilipino national hero.

**Day Five-SuperCat Fast Ferry**

This is one of the most amazing ways to travel. The ferry has tourist guides that take you around to visit the sites most important to the country in that area. People can visit the Loboc River on a cruise that has a mixture of festivity and food with live entertainment. You cannot complete your day without buying souvenirs at the gift shop.

**Day Six-Rizaliana Museum**

The museum is a space devoted to learning. Here people can enjoy a wide display of paintings and art by native Cebuanos. The museum is found at Salinas Drive. Kids and parents need to fit in this amazing place in their 7-day itinerary.

**Day Seven-Sightseeing**

Cebu is full of surprises and people who hire a local tourist guide can visit interesting places such as Magellan’s cross and The Last Supper Chapel in Mandaue.
