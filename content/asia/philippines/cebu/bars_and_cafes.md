---
title: "Bars and Cafes in Cebu"
type: section
---

The beautiful island Cebu, nestled in the center of Philippines of
Southeast Asia, is an ideal tropical tourist attraction. Part of its
appeal is its exquisite cafes, bars and nightlife. With the large
selection to choose from while vacationing in Cebu, this list will
guide you through the top bars and cafes you should hit.
Slabadu Café & Bistro

Located on Fortuna Street in the Mandaue area of Cebu is Slabadu
Café & Bistro. Slabadu is known for its live music and superb
cocktails. This is one of the top upscale bars you will find in Cebu,
and regular home to the popular local band, Firelights.
El Gecko Bar & Grill

The Crossroads Mall is home to many popular bars. Among these is El
Gecko Bar & Grill. This is the perfect venue to start out your
night. It has a casual feel, with fabulous appetizers and happy hour
menu.
Club Vudu

Also located along the strip of Crossroads Mall is one of Cebu’s
most well known clubs, Club Vudu. This is the club to go to for a
fantastic time dancing. They offer cheap drinks, with daily specials
and different music genres every night. Friday and Saturday, this is
the spot in Cebu to be. Club Vudu is also very popular among tourists,
and most of the staff speaks English.
Blue Zydeco Bayou

The Blue Zydeco Bayou, also known as BZB Bar & Grill is one more
must-visit while bar hopping along the Crossroads Mall. It's a
delightful bar to go to if you're looking for some quality American
food. Their menu features traditional New Orleans and Southern dishes.
They offer ample sized portions at low prices. BZB is a lovely spot to
bring the family by day, or kick back with your friends and mingle by
night.
Anzani

If you're look for a fine dining in an upscale lounge, then Anzani
is where you want to go. This luxury nightclub and lounge offers a
spectacular view over Cebu from its location on the Lahug Nivel Hills.
They serve novelty dishes at a reasonable price and have even been
featured in Circuit Magazine for their excellence.

The bars and cafés that Cebu has to offer will fit the needs of any
traveler. Their variety from bistros to nightclubs and fine dining
attracts both tourists and locals alike.
