This place has
been around for more than two decades ,and yet when I recently visited
it last February of 2005 it was still packed and still serving the same
old great dishes. Must try the lechon (barbeque pork) either in whole
or chop-up for two. Warning: might be grosteque to see
whole pig in your table... the seafood, rice, Pinabet and Halo-Halo
dessert are great too.
