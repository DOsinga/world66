Cebu dining means eating at a "sugbaan", meaning "grilled". A.S. Fortuna Avenue in Cebu City is home to some of the province's best "sugbaan" and Mr. AAs is the name to beat. Part of the fun is choosing from the variety of fresh meats and seafoods available for cooking in the large grilling stations. With a plateful of "puso" the local rice, and bottles of beer, it is as good and as local as it gets.
