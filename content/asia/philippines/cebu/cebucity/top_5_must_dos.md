Known as the Queen City of the South, Cebu City on Cebu Island offers a plethora of enticing attractions. The following list of **Cebu City Must Do’s** might come in handy as you design your trip.

**1. Beaches**

[Cebu City](/asia/philippines/cebu/cebucity/museums) has a host of magnificent sandy beaches, ideal for a family holiday. The beaches are lined with some world-class resorts equipped with every possible facility to entertain the tourists. Barili Beaches and Alcoy Beaches are 2 popular beaches of Cebu City. Two of the main beaches of Barili are the Candugay Beach and the Sayaw Beach. Alcoy boasts of the largest dolomite mine in the Philippines. Aside from the mine, Alcoy is home to some splendid beaches, a mangrove forest and coral reefs. Both Barili and Alcoy are blessed with breathtaking natural beauty.

**2. Historical Monuments**

Cebu City is a major historical landmark of the island Cebu. Cebu City is replete with several monuments and sculptures dating the mountains of Barangay, Matutinao and Badian are blessed with 3 beautiful cascading falls popularly known as Kawasan Falls. The first waterfall is the largest and the most crowded one. Trekking for 15 minutes more will take you to the second fall and then third. The cool, refreshing water of the falls will definitely set your mood for more fun.

**5. Shopping**

Shopping in Cebu City is real fun as the city has plenty of shopping malls and stores all the across the city. To name a few, SM City Cebu and Ayela Mall are 2 upscale shopping malls with an array of shopping stores, restaurants, and a Cineplex to satisfy your every need. Aside from these 2 malls, there are Robinson’s Mall, e-Mall, Cebu Parkmall and Mango Sqaure which will give you a fascinating shopping experience.
