By air 

 Fly direct to Mactan International Airport from Singapore (via Singapore Airlines), or from Narita (via Philippine Airlines). Or, fly to Manila and connect to Cebu. If you come from Manila, connect to Cebu via Philippine Airlines. This airline also has regular flights between Cebu and major cities in the Philippines.

 By Sea

 From within the country  There are regular scheduled trips to/from Cebu from/to Manila, Butuan, Davao, Cagayan de Oro, Ozamis and Iligan.
