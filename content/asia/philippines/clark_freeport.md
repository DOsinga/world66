Travel Guide

Clark Freeport is located in province of Pampanga of the Philippines. It adjoins Angeles City and Mabalacat. Clark Freeport is a special Freeport with near zero crime rate and very low pollution and traffic. It is complete with its own international airport named DMIA. Inside Clark Freeport one can find tourist and leisure establishments such as the Mimosa Golf Course and the American-suburban-styled country park, Clearwater Resort and Country Club, a 13ha lake resort that is a favorate of all visitors to Clark Freeport. Other attractions include the famed Yats Restaurant and Wine Bar, Michelin class fine dining restaurant located next to the Mimosa Casino. For more information about Clark Philippines, visit www.ClarkPhilippines.com
