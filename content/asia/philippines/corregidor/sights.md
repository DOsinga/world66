### [Pacific War Memorials](/asia/philippines/corregidor/sights/pacific_war_memori)

Remind the visitors to Pearl Harbour Memorials in Hawaii. Here, US and Philippines governments built the memorial to commemorate US-Filipino armies in the World War II. Especially who took battle in Corregidor. In front can be found *Brother in Arms* monument and at the top of the hills can be seen the *eternal flame* monument.

type: Memorials



[[rate it]](/asia/philippines/corregidor/sights/pacific_war_memori)

### [Filipino Memorials and Museum](/asia/philippines/corregidor/sights/filipino_memorials)

This is a historical Filipinos [Pinoy] memorials and museum. In front can be found the monument of **President Quezon** with the flag. Further afield can be found the museum of Filipinos.

Completed with old pictures of the Filipinos heroes. Such as **Jose Rizal** and Norman Reyes. Norman Reyes or **Normando Ildefonso Reyes** was a journalist and radioman who supported the morals of the Filipinos during World War II by broadcast live from Corregidor. He's among the heroes who stayed and take defense in this [more..](/asia/philippines/corregidor/sights/filipino_memorials)

type: Memorials



[[rate it]](/asia/philippines/corregidor/sights/filipino_memorials)

### [Malinta Tunnel](/asia/philippines/corregidor/sights/malinta_tunnel)

Malinta tunnel is a lateral tunnel where the soldiers take defense during World War II. It's about 1,500 personels in the tunnel and alleys. To enjoy the tunnel, can join *Light and Show of Malinta Tunnel* during the trip to Corregidor.

type: Landmarks
