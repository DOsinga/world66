The Malinta Tunnel is a network of lateral underground tunnels blasted through the rock in the 1930s. During the Japanese siege of 1942, it served as General MacArthur's headquarters and a hospital, housing some 1,500 personnel in its main corridor and lateral branches. A sound-and-light show recreates the wartime conditions for visitors.
