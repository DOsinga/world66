Built jointly by the US and Philippine governments to commemorate the Allied forces who fought and died here in 1942, the Pacific War Memorial sits on the highest point of Corregidor island. The circular memorial building, the eternal flame, and the surrounding park offer panoramic views of Manila Bay. The "Brother in Arms" monument stands at the entrance.
