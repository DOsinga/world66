YATS Clearwater Resort in Angeles Clark Philippines is exactly what Clark is about – American suburban lifestyle of leisure and laid-back quality living. Special picnic packages are now available which includes a delectable lunch prepared by fine-dining Yats Restaurant.

Clark Freeport has a great day-tour venue called Clearwater Resort & Country Club. Clark Freeport offers an ideal crime-free environment for family to enjoy a day free of pollution, noise and congestion. Children can roam freely within the 13ha property of Clearwater Resort and Country Club which was designed specifically for this purpose.

Lake, beach, 13ha of trees and green, safe, secure, clean environment for kids to roam around free are some of the things that enable the whole family to really enjoy spending a few quality nights together at YATS Clearwater Resort and Country Club. This is a good rendition of the lifestyle that the Americans left behind after they left the Clark Airbase. A good short description of this lifestyle is suburban laid-back slow-paced clean living.

YATS Leisure Philippines, a developer and operator of clubs, resorts and high-class restaurants and wine outlets in Clark Angeles Philippines has announced a special full-board package for YATS resort hotel in Clark Philippines called Clearwater Resort and Country Club, just 5 minutes from Angeles City.

Yats Restaurant (www.YatsRestaurant.com) specially designed delectable picnic meal boxes to make the picnic even more memorable for the whole family. Picnic boxes must be purchased in advance as food is prepared in high-standard kitchens of the fine-dining Yats Restaurant and Wine Bar.

Getting there is quite simple: after entering Clark Freeport from Dau and Angeles City, proceed straight along the main highway MA Roxas, passing the stand-along wine shop called Clark Wine Center on your right, continue to bear right making no turns at all, go past Mimosa Leisure Estate on the opposite side of the road, there’ll be a major intersection. Go straight and the road becomes Creekside Road. Y A T S Clearwater Resort and Country Club is on the right just 200m down.
