---
title: "Eating Out"
type: section
---

Dumaguete has a wide range of restaurants for a provincial city, from Filipino seafood grills to Chinese restaurants and the occasional international option. Chin Loong on the Boulevard serves Chinese food and Filipino barbecue and is popular with locals. Mc Neloy's La Cavitena (Silliman Ave.) is known for grilled seafood and Filipino barbecue at low prices. The city has cafes and fast food around the university area.
