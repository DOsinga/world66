A small restaurant serving Indian and Persian dishes — kebabs, hummus, curries — at affordable prices. Good food and a calm atmosphere. Located beside La Residencia Al-Mar along Rizal Boulevard. The kebabs and hummus are the strongest items on the menu, and portions are generous for the price.
