A small volcanic island 30 km south of Dumaguete, surrounded by one of the best-protected coral reefs in the Philippines. The Apo Island Marine Reserve, managed by the local community, has exceptional visibility and fish density. Sea turtles are commonly seen year-round. Snorkelling is possible from the beach; dive operators from Dumaguete run day trips. The island has basic accommodation for those who want to stay overnight and dive across multiple days.
