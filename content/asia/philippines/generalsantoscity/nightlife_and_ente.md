---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment in General Santos City"
type: section
---

General Santos City, or Gensan, offers several opportunities to a variety of travelers who want to explore its coastal beauty at night. It has a great nightlife and entertainment profile that includes sea food restaurants, cafes and beaches. Here are some super ideas to enjoy the nightlife in the city.

[Vanessa](/asia/philippines/generalsantoscity/eating_out_1/vanessa)

Here you will get great seafood, served in a friendly atmosphere. The rooftop restaurant lets you admire the city’s panoramic view as you eat grilled and sizzling seafood.

**Eat Fish
**

If you are a lover of seafood, this is the place to be in. The tuna here is famous, and all eateries take pride in serving the freshest seafood. They also serve Chinese and Mediterranean style cuisines. Every palate is catered to here.

**Relax at the Beach
**

The city is surrounded by beaches. You can relax at a beach with your family or a friend and take in the rustic charm.

**Enjoy Authentic Filipino Cuisine at Nana Beth
**

Nana Beth Fast Food House is a great institution that serves tasty and wholesome local grub. The menu has a host of local dishes that are affordably priced. Be sure to try their seafood preparations, especially the absolutely tasty fresh tuna salad. You can eat here in casual clothes. This is a great place for going out with friends or family.

Besides these, there are many small restaurants that serve such delicacies as charcoal-grilled dayana, tula soup and kinilaw, fresh fish in vinegar and shallots. You must sample the food at a couple of these places.

**
Lemlunay Dive Resor
**

Lemlunay should be on your must visit list. It is well-maintained and fully equipped. Here you can witness the marine ecosystem of the region while taking enjoyable diving sessions. Even the non-divers in your group will enjoy the trip, as they savor the moist breeze and relax on the palm-fringed, sandy beach. The food at the resort is out of this world. They offer regular diving courses and follow this up with a guided diving session for novices.

**Internet Cafes
**

For the business traveler, the city is sprinkled with Internet cafes. Here you can stay connected with your work while enjoying a quiet cup of coffee. They can also serve as a place for you to take your business colleagues to for a refreshing cup of coffee and a quick talk about work.
Take a tricycle and they will charge you very little to drop you off wherever you want to go. You can also take a taxicab to work – though it is not as much fun.
