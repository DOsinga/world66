Gen. Trias is a city in Cavite province, just south of Metro Manila on the Cavite mainland. It sits along the main road south from the capital and has grown rapidly as part of the wider Metro Manila urban sprawl. The city has a mix of residential subdivisions, commercial areas, and some light industrial zones. For travelers, Gen. Trias is primarily a pass-through point on the way to Tagaytay or other Cavite destinations, though the nearby Imus and Kawit areas have more colonial-era heritage to explore.
