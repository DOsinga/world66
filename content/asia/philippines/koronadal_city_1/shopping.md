---
title: "Shopping"
type: section
---

Koronadal's shopping is concentrated in a handful of large malls and the public market. **KCC Mall** on General Santos Drive was the first, and still the busiest — clothes, electronics, a cinema, fast food. **Fitmart Mall**, next to NDMU-IBED, is larger and a little more modern. The **Koronadal public market** is the place for fresh fruit, fish, and locally made crafts; it is unusually clean and well-organised by Philippine standards, and you can pick up T'boli beadwork and woven textiles at *pasalubong* (souvenir) stalls for a fraction of what you would pay in Manila.
