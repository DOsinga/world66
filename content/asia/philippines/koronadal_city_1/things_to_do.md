---
title: "Things to do in Koronadal City"
type: section
---

**Koronadal City things to do** are many. Koronadal City is
located in the southern Mindanao region of the Philippines and is also
known by the name of "Marbel." This alternative name has been derived as
a result of the fact that some of the water in and around this area is,
by Filipino standards, not particularly clear. Marbel means "murky
waters."

**Arrange to Stay in the Marvella Plaza hotel**

The rooms which are provided in this particular hotel complex are
generally considered to be among the best that Koronadal City is able to
offer. Admittedly, the options which are available in this city are
actually quite limited and there is not a great deal of competition.
**Visit the Cultural Center Museum**

While this is quite a small museum, it is an example of a place where
information can be found about the local T’boli culture. The admission
price is cheap and it allows the tourist to obtain an accurate feel of
the ethos of the area and the people and customs which are prevalent
there.
**Go on a Shopping Trip**

Koronadal City is home to quite a large number of malls. Most of the
popular brands of food and clothing are available in this city.
**Eat Out in One of the Many Restaurants**

In common with many other towns and cities located in the
Philippines, there is a wide variety of different bars and restaurants.
These offer a selection of foods which are traditional to the area of
Southeast Asia but there are also many different types of western food
available too. By western standards, prices are extremely cheap.
**Visit El Gawel Zoo**

This is a good place to visit in order to view some of the wildlife
available in the Philippines, albeit in a captive environment.
**Visit the Sarabia Falls**

This is quite a pleasant waterfall which provides a picturesque
environment. This is a natural water fall.
**Visit the Mambucal Hot Spring**

Another notable one of the Koronadal City things to do is to make a
visit to this hot spring, which has been created from water moving
within the earth’s center. This is warmed because of its original
proximity to the hot core.

**Enjoy at Rizal Park**

Here you are able to view the Jose Rizal monument. Rizal was a famous
nationalist hero, executed by a Spanish firing squad at the end of the
19th century.
