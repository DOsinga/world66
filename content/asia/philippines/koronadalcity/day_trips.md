title: "Day Trips in Koronadal City"
type: section
---

The capital of the South Cotabato province in Philippines is **Koronadal City**: **day trips** from this area are numerous in number and include many popular Philippines destinations and those farther afield as well. Before you arrive, plan out your trip carefully so that you'll be able to maximize your time exploring while you're in Koronadal City.

**Philippines Day Trips**

Cotabato city is one of the most popular destinations for travelers staying in Koronadal City. Cotabato City is larger than Koronadal and features a number of additional amenities and attractions that aren't available in Koronadal City. It's possible to also go farther in a single day by means of a car or boat; Ormoc and Santiago are also major metropolitan areas in the Philippines that make for good day trips.

**Extended Trips**

If you wish to extend your trip to two or three days, it's possible to visit other islands and further sites. Singapore is the closest country, accessible by flight or by cruise ship. Bali is a popular destination for tourists as well; this western island in Indonesia is easily reached by a short flight and makes for a great travel destination for a couple of days as well. Manila is the largest city in the Philippines, and it is a third popular destination. This is the cheapest of the options listed here, although the city is somewhat dangerous so it's best to exercise caution when visiting.

Consult with a representative at your local tourism agency for additional information about planning the trips from Koronadal City.
