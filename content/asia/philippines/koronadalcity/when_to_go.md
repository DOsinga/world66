---
title: "When to Go"
type: section
---

Koronadal is typhoon-free, which sets it apart from much of the Philippines. The dry season runs from November to May, with the hottest months in March and April. The rainy season from July to October brings reliable afternoon rains but rarely severe weather. The Tinalak Festival in July and the Charter Anniversary celebrations in October are the best times to experience local culture.
