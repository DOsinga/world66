---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment in Las Pinas City"
type: section
---

The** Las Pinas City Nightlife** is worth sampling. The nightlife is a true
reflection of the soul of a city. Experience the essence of Las Pinas
by indulging in its nightlife. It is not the usual, in-your-face kind
of an atmosphere; the night spots are scattered around the city. Read on
to find the local favourites and the best places to chill in Las Pinas.

**Dencio Bar and Grill

**A great joint to have a hearty meal with an ambiance that will add
color to your night in Las Pinas. Dencio specializes in Filipino food.
Enjoy the authentic dishes ranging from sausages to fried pork legs, the
famous peanut sauce, seafood and beef. The grilled seafood and meat
are cooked to perfection and the flavors are one of a kind. The music at
the bar is extraordinary. All this at a very economical rate. It is
housed in the Las Pinas famous Star Mall.

**

Z-Bar

**
Located on the well known Alabang Zapote road, this fancy bar is a
party animal's retreat. Working way past midnight, it sets the nightlife
in Las Pinas ablaze. The working hours are between 6 p.m. and 2 a.m.

**Cowboy
Grill

**This bar truly stands by its name. A western cowboy haven is the
theme of this Grill bar. The concept is simple: if you're looking to
have a good time with a mug of cold beer and some great grilled food,
this is the place to be. The live music and entertainment rates this
as the most preferred spot for the youth in Las Pinas.

**Barrio
Fiesta

**This restaurant offers traditional Filipino food served in the most
authentic manner. The food is not overly spiced and there is a whole
gamut of items to choose from. Food is served in traditional dishes
like the earthen pot. The success and popularity of this joint is
exhibited in the fact that it has opened up a new branch in Southmall.

**

Cuba
Libre

**A great bar filled with activities and food that will leave you
asking for more. Not only does this place serve food and drinks, but it also
offers billiards and pool tables. If you want to sing your night
away, then go crazy with the Karaoke, available in Cuba Libre. The staff
are extremely friendly and you will definitely leave the restaurant in
high spirits.
