Ormoc is a chartered city on the west coast of [Leyte](/asia/philippines/leyteisland), known as the sugar capital of the Eastern Visayas. The surrounding countryside is lush with sugarcane fields and the city serves as a gateway to Lake Danao National Park in the highlands above town.

The city was devastated by Typhoon Haiyan in 2013 but has rebuilt with determination. Today it is a functional, friendly stop for travellers heading between Cebu and the Leyte interior — ferries run regularly from Ormoc to Cebu City.
