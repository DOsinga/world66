Mandaluyong is one of the sixteen cities of **Metro Manila**, a wedge of skyscrapers and high-rise condominiums sandwiched between Pasig and the financial centre of [Makati](/asia/philippines/makati). For most of the twentieth century it was a quiet residential district known mainly as the home of the country's largest psychiatric hospital, the **National Center for Mental Health**, but the boom of the 1990s turned its central avenues into one of Manila's principal shopping and business districts. The vast **SM Megamall** on Edsa is one of the largest malls in the Philippines, and the **Ortigas Center** financial district straddles the boundary with neighbouring Pasig. Most travellers will pass through here without realising it — Mandaluyong has no headline sights of its own — but if you are staying in Ortigas, this is where you actually are.
