Champagne/Wine Brunch in Manila Philippines is something that wine lovers have looked forward to for a while now. Yats Wine Cellars (www.YatsWineCellars.com) wine brunch will take place in the second floor of ShangrilLa Mall in Ortigas Center, Manila Philippines on Sunday 18th October, 2009 from 10am to 2pm. The brunch is a sumptuous affair prepared by Chef Jacqie of Cafe Provencal and here is the wine line-up to help wash everything down:

Sparkling wine
NV Ch. De Montgueret Rose Sec,Saumur France
Cava Artelatino Brut Metodo Tradicional Spain

Full-bodied Red wine from California
2007 Pinot Noir Parducci Mendocino

Medium-bodied Red wine from Italy
1999 Dolcetto D'Alba, Rossana Ceretto, Piedmonte

Dry white wine from France
2000 Chablis, Lamblin et Fils

Off-dry white wine from Germany
2006 Riesling J B Wagoner, Rheingau

Ticket price is p1,500 per person. Children are also welcome. Children breakfast can be arranged by advanced booking. As a matter of fact, there is a charming carousel right next to the venue. For mom, there is plenty of shopping, again right next to the restaurant also. Everybody have fun.

For tickets please call (632) 637-5019 ask for Chay or Rea or email Wine@Yats-International.com. Only 30 tickets for this event, so it is best to purchase them now.
