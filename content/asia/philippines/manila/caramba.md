A Latin-themed bar in the Bonifacio Global City development (formerly Fort Bonifacio), south of the Makati business district. Live bands play salsa and Latin pop most nights, and there's a small dance floor that fills up after 10 PM. The crowd skews young professional -- BGC is where Manila's office workers come to unwind. Drinks are reasonably priced by the area's standards.
