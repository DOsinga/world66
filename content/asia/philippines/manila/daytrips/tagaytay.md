An hour or so ride away from Manila is Tagaytay, a picturesque city that surrounds (or almost surrounds) a lake that has a volcano that has a lake inside it. There are several ways to take advantage of this gift of nature. You can either have lunch or dinner (with a panoramic view of the volcano and lake) at one of the restaurants on the ridge and enjoy the view from there, or you can travel down the ridge to the lakeside, take a small boat to the volcano in the center. From there, you have two options, either hike up on foot (not recommended during the summer unless you have a couple of liters of water with you) or take a small horse.

Both methods will get you to the same place, to the top of the volcano where you can look down into the lake below. If you are lucky, you may even reward yourself with fresh coconut being sold right by the ridge.
