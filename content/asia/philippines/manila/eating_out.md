### [L'Opera](/asia/philippines/manila/eating_out/lopera)

Patrons dine on fresh homemade pasta, lobster, carpaccio, veal, duck-filled ravioli, an impressive array of italian cheeses and a staggering collections of wine that would compete with some of the best enoteca bars in town. Regional Tuscan cuisine at its best in Manila.

### [Alda's](/asia/philippines/manila/eating_out/aldas)

A quaint little restaurant with dim lighting, checkered tablecloth and
decent prices. Half-orders of pasta are welcome. Sometimes
they serve free dessert (usually at dinner time).
