El Cirkulo is an upscale lounge and bar located in the Milky Way Building at 900 Pasay Road, corner of Paseo de Roxas in Makati. The venue draws a well-dressed crowd with its sleek interior and strong cocktail menu. It sits in the heart of Makati's nightlife district, making it easy to combine with dinner or club-hopping nearby. Expect a slightly higher price point than the average Manila bar, but the atmosphere delivers.
