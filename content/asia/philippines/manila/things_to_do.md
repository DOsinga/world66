---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Manila rewards those who accept it on its own terms. The old walled city of **Intramuros** — bombed flat in 1945 and only partially rebuilt — is where you start, with San Agustin Church, Fort Santiago, and the cathedral all within a fifteen-minute walk. The **Ayala Museum** in Makati is the best single museum. For the stranger side of the city, take the jeepney to the **Chinese Cemetery**, where the tombs are bungalows with air conditioning.
