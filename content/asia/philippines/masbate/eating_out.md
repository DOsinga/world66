**Experience Good Local Food in Baywalk Restaurant in Masbate, Philippines**

Be sure to dine in one of the many **Masbate restaurants** that are found all over Masbate. Masbate is an island province of the Philippines, which is located in the southeastern end of the country. It is famous for its scenic beaches, cowboys and lush forests. Aside from nature, Masbate is also famous for its food; thus, if you want to have an enjoyable gastronomic experience, then the best place to go to is the Baywalk Restaurant.

**The Baywalk Restaurant**

Located in Ibingay, the Baywalk Garden Restaurant is one of Masbate&rsquo;s famous restaurants. It is not only popular in serving Filipino dishes, but the chefs in the restaurant can also serve Chinese, American and Italian cuisines. Moreover, what makes this restaurant popular is its scenic ocean view. The restaurant has both air-conditioned and alfresco setting. If you truly want to enjoy your food and the scenes of the ocean, then it is best to dine alfresco in Baywalk Garden Restaurant. The restaurant is open for lunch and dinner only.

**Gastronomic Delights in the Baywalk Restaurant**

For a unique gastronomic experience, then you ought to try the Filipino cuisines served in the restaurant. For starters, you can feast on grilled seafood such as lobsters, oysters and giant crabs. You can also try the restaurant&rsquo;s seaweed salad which is heavenly and for the main course, you can pair your rice with beef steak or if you are up for some adventure, you can try the specialty of the house which is grilled horse meat. For desserts you can munch on carmelados, which are a type of candy derived from Carabao&rsquo;s milk.
