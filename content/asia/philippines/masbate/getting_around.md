---
title: "Getting Around in Masbate"
type: section
---

Finding the options for **Masbate transportation** can help you plan your trip and give you the best options to see the city and explore the area. There are a few different ways you can enjoy seeing everything the city has to offer.

#### Ferries

Ferries are a great choice to use for traveling in the area. You can use the air conditioned ferries from Montenegro Lines and follow the lines of Masbate, Pilar and Sorsogon. The departures from Masbate leave at different times throughout the day, so you are able to catch the ferry when you want and still be able to see what you want.

#### Boats

If you do not mind taking a longer ride, you can take one of the boats that departs Masbate and goes to Pilar. The fares are cheaper than the ferries, but it will take you about three hours to get between the two cities. The boats can be docked twice a day, so you will want to be sure and check when they are leaving the city and when they will be arriving. This is a more affordable but slower option for travel.

#### Buses

There are also city RORO buses you can take to get around in the city. They are smaller but are still able to accommodate a lot of people. You will want to learn what routes go where and also when they travel. They are affordable and stop at many of the popular streets that you may be interested in seeing in the city.
