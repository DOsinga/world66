---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment in Masbate"
type: section
---

If you think Masbate is all about clear waters and underground rivers, then you might want to check out what more is there in the **Masbate nightlife**. Set in the middle of the Philippines, this province is known for its wonderful marine scenery, greenery and unexplored caves. However, exploration is most likely done by day. But then, here are some of the best spots you must not miss out on when the day is over:

#### Victoria Mall

Take some time to unwind at the Victoria Mall with your friends or family. This spot is most popular among young locals and tourists alike for it offers a break from all their daytime escapades. Experience shopping at this province or eat some snacks while you go and see a movie. It surely is a complete getaway from a full day of activities.

#### Masbate Coast Line

Looking for an inexpensive spot to hang out? Well then, the Masbate Coast Line is free of charge. Bring your car and drive up to this place. Indeed, it is an experience you should not dare miss. Turn on some music and relax as you gaze at the breathtaking night sky. This would be best if you bring along friends or close loved ones.

#### Baywalk Restaurant

Whether you are staying at the Baywalk Hotel or not, you ought to eat at the Baywalk Restaurant at least once during your vacation in Masbate. Not only does it offer a wide array of food and drinks, this place is also known for being inexpensive. You may even watch projected concerts and films while eating. The Baywalk Restaurant is certain to serve you to your heart&rsquo;s content.

#### Amperez Bar and Grill

Masbate Nightlife is never complete without the bar experience. So, chill out at one of the best spots in town and see what the city&rsquo;s got to offer tourists other than day tours and excursions. The Amperez Bar and Grill is a dining spot well renowned for its good food and relaxing atmosphere. Drink some beer and discuss with your friends what has passed during the day.

#### Floating Restaurant

Have you heard of the Floating Restaurant before? In any case, you must go and see it for yourself. With an astonishing view of the bay, this restaurant is listed as a must-not-miss due to the unforgettable dining experience it has to offer. If you are searching for live entertainment, this is definitely the place to be. Enjoy eating while listening to good music. Enjoy watching as good music is played.
