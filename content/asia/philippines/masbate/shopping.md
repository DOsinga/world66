Masbate is a province in the Philippines, and there are some great **Masbate shopping** spots to be found here. Here are some** **places, featuring some of the provinces best known and best loved items.

**Jewelry Shopping
**

Masbate has been termed a &ldquo;first class province&rdquo; due to its richness in minerals and mines, resulting it in being one of the largest providers of ores to the rest of the cities and provinces in the Philippines. The sheer volume of these natural resources has allowed Masbate to produce gold and silver jewelry cheaply, and the people of the province have become very skilled at creating jewelry pieces from the wonderful raw materials that are native to their land. Walk along the city streets and be amazed at the number of little jewelry shops that can be found within the city itself. Stepping into one of them, the traditional designs can be seen – anything from indigenous designs and art, to Catholic crucifixes, all in good quality gold or silver. The Victoria Mall at the pier of Masbate is the best place to source for these offers and designs.

**Handicrafts
**

Masbate is also the province known for its artisans and carpenters, and the handicrafts here are good souvenirs to be bought home as a keepsake. Those looking for something larger and more prominent can explore the possibility of buying some of their furniture pieces and shipping it home – many of the larger shops here provide the service of transporting pieces
