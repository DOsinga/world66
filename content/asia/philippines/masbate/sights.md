Adventure-seeking travelers are attracted with the unique **Masbate Sights **found in the Masbate Island. You will love the beaches, natural waterfalls and the abundant marine life. Discover the unique way of life of the local people through traditional festivals while traveling around the province. Experience and explore the wonders of its unspoiled beauty.****

**Limestone Hills**

Limestone Hills is famous for its ancient burial sites. The Hills serves as the home of the indigenous tribe of the province, the Kalanay and they regard the place with reverence. An excavation mission was done here and remains of the earlier people found inside the jars, proves that the tribe practices jar burials.

**Underwater Cave**

The underwater cave is another famous burial site in Masbate. When visiting this place, you need to take a short dive to reach the cave. Be amazed with the sealed human caskets, made of solid wood that has human remains dating back 500 years. All caskets have the same measurements, 24 by 12 inches. You need to bring your own diving gear and a good source of light when exploring the cave.

**Pawa Mangrove System and Wildlife Park**

The Pawa Mangrove System and Wildlife Park is a good spot for bird watching. Bird lovers will love the different species of birds easily found on the mangrove system and park. Pay attention to unique varieties like the rhabdornises whose heads are decorated with stripes. The mangrove system and park is not only good for fishing but also a good place to spend the afternoon for family picnics. Explore the park and do not forget to check the km-long wooden bridge connecting the two barangays in Masbate.

**Ticao**** Pass**

Ticao Pass is well-known among divers because of its abundant marine life, and its Manta Bowl which became a gathering place for manta rays during the summer. They gather here to get some food and some cleaning. This lovely sight usually occurs during the months of December to May. Aside from the large manta rays, the pass also attracts throngs of whale sharks, thresher sharks and hammerheads. This is not only a good spot for diving but also a good place to see the rich marine life and exotic groups of fish. You can bring your own diving equipment or rent from one of the diving centers.

**Catandayagan**** Falls**** **

These two famous waterfalls are must-sees for adventure travelers. Catandayagan Falls, one of the most beautiful falls in the province, has a height of 60 feet. The clear cold water cascades from the top to a lovely lagoon full of multi-colored fish. You can either trek to the top of the falls or hire a motorboat to reach the base of the waterfalls.
