title: "Top 5 Must Do's in Masbate"
type: section
---

Experience several famous **Masbate must do&rsquo;s** when you plan your vacation in this island province in the Philippines. Masbate is home to remarkable tourist destinations that offer fun activities fit for every vacationer.

#### 1. Dive into the ocean

Masbate is surrounded by huge bodies of water; this explains the abundance of pristine white sand beaches and resorts. Many of these resorts offer affordable services and amenities for ocean activities, especially snorkeling and scuba diving.

Masbate offers diving enthusiasts one of the most diverse marine environments around. You will find schools of colorful fish across a long and wide stretch of coral reefs, all in the clear and calm waters of Masbate beaches. Rent provisions to take pictures underwater and capture every moment of your ocean adventure. There are also safe sites designated for observing the natural way of life of gentle whale sharks, stingrays, and other wild sealife. This adventure is perfect for everyone from diving enthusiasts to thrill-seekers.

#### 2. Catch the Rodeo Masbateno

The province has the reputation of being the &ldquo;Wild West&rdquo; of the Philippines. The Rodeo Masbateno is one of the biggest annual events in the island. It is held every April, which is perfect because you can really enjoy the beaches during the summer heat. It features action-packed bull-riding events, and bullfighting enthusiasts will have a great time watching the local rodeos compete in various categories. This is certainly one of the unique Masbate must-do&rsquo;s.

#### 3. Sail Along the Pristine Beach

Colorful vintas and sailboats are available for tourists who want to enjoy the breeze and the ocean while observing the sunset in the horizon. You should watch out for picture-perfect moments or occasional sightings of dolphins and whales.

#### 4. Tour with the Horses

If you think that walking along the landscapes and shoreline of Masbate is a tedious activity, then horseback riding is the perfect way for you to explore the beauty of the island. There are a lot of local businesses that offer such activities. Pick your own horse and start appreciating the amazing views amidst the fresh air. Small ponies are also available for your kids. This is an ideal activity that the whole family can enjoy.

#### 5. Shop for Native Items

Street bazaars and flea markets are available for tourists who are looking for great finds at affordable prices. Most of the products you&rsquo;ll find are intricate, handmade crafts like woven bags, blankets, and accessories. Flatter your friends with great gift items from the island&rsquo;s museum shops, where you can enjoy art exhibits and buy local products like native pottery and accessories made from local gold and silver.

These are some of the Masbate must-do&rsquo;s that you should try when you set foot in the beautiful island of Masbate.
