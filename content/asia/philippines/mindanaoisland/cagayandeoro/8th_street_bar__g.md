A neighborhood bar at the corner of 8th Street and Tomas Saco in the Nazareth area. 8th Street Bar & Grill draws a local crowd for acoustic sessions on Wednesday, Friday, and Saturday nights. The food menu covers standard Filipino bar fare, and the atmosphere is relaxed and unpretentious. Easy to reach by jeepney or motorela from the city center.
