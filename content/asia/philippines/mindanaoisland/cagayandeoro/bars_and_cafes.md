This modern city in the Phillipines has a vibrant nightlife and attracts hordes of tourists. Here are some Cagayan de Oro Cafes and Bars for your pleasure.

**Night Café**
Plaza Divisoria
Tirso Neri Street
Cagayan de Oro

This café is probably the most famous in Cagyan de Oro. Food and beer is cheap, and so are the goodies you can buy in the night market nearby. This open air café is basically a bunch of stalls with tables and chairs set out on the empty street every Friday and Saturday evening. Live music adds to the atmosphere.

**Tubby's Gay Bar**
Rizal Gomez Street
Cagayan de Oro City, Mindanao 9000

A night out in this famous gay bar may turn out to be unforgettable. It’s well known for its great shows and dancing, with DJs churning out the music.

**Picasso Bar**
6th Floor, Dynasty Court Hotel
Tiano Corner Hayes Streets, Cagayan de Oro,
Mindanao 9000

Picasso Bar has live music every evening and is a favorite hangout for the young.

**Cafe Alessandria Restaurant and Music Lounge**
Southwinds Hotel
Capt. Vicente Roa Extension
Cagayan De Oro City, Mindanao 9000

Besides good food, Cafe Alessandria serves alcoholic beverages and coffee. Try the delicious Lobster Thermidore, Beef Pot Roast, juicy steaks and the cakes.

**Spooks**
Tiano-Pacana Streets Cagayan de Oro City
852-40-21

One of the most hygienic and famous bars in the city, Spooks is reputed to pay special attention to its patrons. What’s more, the drinks are not expensive and there are no hidden costs.
**
Bigby’s Café**
SM City Cagayan de Oro,
Upper Carmen, Misamis Oriental
(088) 859-1358

Feeling homesick for American food and coffee? Centrally located Bigby’s Café is the place to head to. The comfortable café has a relaxing atmosphere for a leisurely beer and you can choose from a variety of dishes. Bigby’s is great for a weekend hangout.

**Vienna Kaffeehaus**
1st Level East Concourse, Limketkai Mall,
Cagayan de Oro City

If it’s European cuisine you’re longing for, try Vienna Kaffeehaus downtown. The café is considered as one of the best choices for European cuisine and is popular with tourists. It offers authentic European cuisine and the service is excellent. And of course, the coffee is a must.
**
Fu Club and Bar**
Corrales Corner J. Ebarle Streets,
Cagayan de Oro City

This club and bar has comfortable brown leather couches and features some of the best DJs in the Philippines.

**Brew Berry Cafe**
Velez-Chavez Streets, Cagayan de Oro City
(08822) 72-52-91

You’ll find traditional western-style dishes here, such as chicken cordon bleau, spaghetti and meatballs, and fettuccine with mushrooms. You can finish your excellent meal with a big chunk of mouth-watering blueberry cake. They have an area for smokers and their cappuccino is as good as Starbuck’s’.
