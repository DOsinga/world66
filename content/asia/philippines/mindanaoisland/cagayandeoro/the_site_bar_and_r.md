A long-running bar and restaurant on Cagayan de Oro's Rosario Strip, the commercial arcade near Limketkai Center. The Site serves Filipino and international dishes alongside cold San Miguel in a casual, open-air setting. It draws a mix of office workers and families, especially on weekends. The strip around it has filled up with coffee shops and pizzerias over the years, but The Site remains a reliable spot for a meal and a drink.
