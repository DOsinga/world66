### [Bar - Clubbing](/asia/philippines/mindanaoisland/cagayandeoro/things_to_do/bar__clubbing)

For fans of NightLife, if you like the House party music, CDO has FU Bar (OIC), Volume (SM) and 1150. If you prefere R&B, there are Cafe Macumba (Limketkai) and also 1150. For people who loves singing in cabines, there is rhe cafe-club M2 (Limketkai). During the evening, the city presents NightCafe at the Divisoria where you can buy anything to the lowest price, eat and taste or just listen a music band on a stage.
