---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Whitewater rafting on the Cagayan River is the defining activity: the commercial stretch begins about 20 minutes from the city centre and runs through Class II and III rapids accessible to beginners. Several operators run daily trips with guides and gear. Kayaking is available on the same stretch for the more experienced. Beyond the river, the Macajalar Bay waterfront is a pleasant place to walk, and the Divisoria night market — open Friday and Saturday evenings on Tirso Neri Street — is a lively combination of food stalls and live music.
