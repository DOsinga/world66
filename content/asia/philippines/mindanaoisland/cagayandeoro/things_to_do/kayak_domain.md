**Whitewater Kayaking in Cagayan de Oro.
**
The most convenient and easy to access whitewater kayaking and rafting destination in the Philippines.
The Cagayan River passes through Cagayan de Oro city itself and empty into the Macajalar bay.
The commercial kayaking and rafting runs is just 20 minutes drive from the city. Thus, you can do whitewater kayaking and/or rafting every day. Limited only to your fitness and capability.
The water level is ideal for kayaking all year round unlike other kayaking/rafting destinations in the Philippines. The kayaking take-out point is at the Old Carmen Bridge which only a stone-throw from DV Soria. You can go straight for a coffee, tea, or a meal after a whitewater kayaking run.
