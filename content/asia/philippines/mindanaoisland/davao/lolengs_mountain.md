A nature resort in the hills of Toril district, about an hour south of Davao's airport. The main draw is the free-flowing spring water swimming pool and the panoramic views over metropolitan Davao below. The grounds include a playground, chapel, and restaurant, all set in a cool mountain climate that's a welcome break from the city heat. Entrance is around P50 unless you're booked for an overnight stay.
