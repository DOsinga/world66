A large, well-maintained public park in downtown Davao, built on the former PTA Grounds. Landscaped waterfalls, tropical gardens featuring plants from across the Philippines, traditional cottages, and a playground fill the grounds. It draws a cross-section of Davao life — families, students, joggers — and is flanked by restaurants on almost every side. A pleasant spot to spend an hour and see how cosmopolitan Davao has become.
