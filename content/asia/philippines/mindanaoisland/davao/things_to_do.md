---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

You might want to add these things to your **Davao things to do**
list. Davao is the largest, safest and the most beautiful tourist
destination on the island of Mindanao. There are several great things to
do here, so you don’t have to worry about being bored with nothing to
do while on holiday in Davao.

**Kadayawan Festival**

This is the most celebrated and grandiose event in the southern most
part of the Philippines. Kadayawan Festival is a week-long celebration
in Davao City which showcases the colorful culture and abundant harvest
of Dabawenyos. The festival is celebrated during the third week of
August. There are several interesting events that take place during
these festivities like fashion shows, street dancing, a float parade,
music and dance competitions, as well as free concerts and Davao’s major
beauty pageant. If you are in Davao during the month of August, you
might want to join the fun and celebrations.

**Mountain Climbing or Biking**

If you are up for physical activities then you will surely enjoy
biking and/or climbing the great trail of Mount Apo. This is the highest
peak in the Philippines. You will also be amazed to see the wide
collection of flora and fauna as well as the immaculate rainforest and
wildlife. This is definitely a one-of-a-kind experience you shouldn’t
miss.

**Zip City**

Traveling on a 320-meter zip line is definitely an extraordinary
adventure you should try. Zip City is located on the hilltop of Barangay
Langub in Davao City and has easy access to the main road and different
establishments like Dencio’s Kainan Restaurant. There’s nothing to
worry about as all the facilities and equipment in Zip City are checked
and approved by the International Mountaineering and Climbing Federation
(UIAA). This is one of the most exciting things to do in Davao.

**Philippine Eagle Conservation Center**

Davao is known for the endangered monkey-eating-eagle that can only
be found in Philippines. So if you are in Davao you must visit the
Philippine Eagle Conservation Center situated in Malagos. Here you can
see the largest and rarest eagles in the whole world as well as other
wildlife creatures like warthogs, monkeys, owls and deer.

**Whitewater Tubing**

Another interesting thing to do in Davao is whitewater tubing. Some
of the powerful and wild water rapids are at the tubing hot spots like
Santa Cruz, Davao River and Sibulan River. Whitewater tubing is a water
adventure comparable to whitewater rafting but instead of rafts you will
ride on a tube. You don’t have to be worried as these rivers are
shallow and all activities are monitored and guided by professionals to
ensure that every visitor is safe and comfortable.
