This shrine located at the top of Matina Hill is an open air shrine
with replicas of the Infant Jesus of Prague and Our Lady of Fatima. A
nice and quiet place to reflect and

worship. It also commands very nice views of Davao City, Davao Gulf, and Samal Island.
