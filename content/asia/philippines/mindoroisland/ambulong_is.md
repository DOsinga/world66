Travel Guide

Ambulong Island ( Brgy . Ambulong , Ambulong Island ). The 3,000-hectare Ambulong Island is one of the three islands found in San Jose , Occidental Mindoro . It is characterized by white sand beaches, cliffs along pillars jutting out of the water and underwater caves. Don't need foreign investor to develop the island
