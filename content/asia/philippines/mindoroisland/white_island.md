Travel Guide

***White island ( Manadi , Bo. Ilin , San Jose ). Is a 3 acre beach with smooth and powdery white sand. The tranquil and peaceful island is an ideal place for swimming, scuba diving, and fishing. Turtle eggs are occasionally found buried in the sand.***

***Peaceful place..Not a 5 star but you can see million of stars at night....clean..very private...inexpensive- 150php ($4.00) for cottage and 30php ($1.00 or less) for entrance..bring your own food and drinks..picking corals /shells not allowed. Do not forget to be generous to the caretaker, she is the one that cleans the island and the banka guy...very nice and helpful!***
