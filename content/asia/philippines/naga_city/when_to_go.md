---
title: "When to Go"
type: section
---

The dry season runs from November through April, making it the most comfortable time to visit. The wet season (May–October) brings heavy rain but also the city's biggest event.

The **Peñafrancia Festival** takes place over nine days in the third week of September. Book accommodation months in advance — the city fills completely during this period. If you can tolerate the crowds (and the rain), the atmosphere is extraordinary: street processions, candlelit vigils, and the final fluvial parade make for one of the Philippines' most memorable travel experiences.
