A classic pala-pala (seafood market) experience. Pick your seafood fresh and have it cooked however you like. They also serve grilled chicken inasal, barbecue pork, and spare ribs. Turo-turo counters display ready-cooked Bacolod delicacies for those in a hurry. Very affordable and very local.
