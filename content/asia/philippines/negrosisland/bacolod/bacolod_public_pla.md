title: 'Bacolod Public Plaza'
type: poi
category: 'Sight'
address: 'Rizal Street, Bacolod City'
latitude: 10.664645
longitude: 122.958562
---

The heart of old Bacolod, shaded by mature trees and anchored by a Spanish-era gazebo. Fountains and statues dot the grounds, and it fills with families in the late afternoon. The plaza sits in front of the provincial capitol and hosts festivals and public performances throughout the year.
