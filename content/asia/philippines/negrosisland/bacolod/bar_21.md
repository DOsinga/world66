Bar 21 is one of Bacolod's more polished dining and drinking spots, set on Lacson Street in the heart of the city. The menu runs from Filipino comfort food to international dishes, and the cocktail list is a step above the usual local bars. The interior is sleek by Bacolod standards. A solid option if you want a quieter, air-conditioned evening after a day exploring the sugar capital of the Philippines.
