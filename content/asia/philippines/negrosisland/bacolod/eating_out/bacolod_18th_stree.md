Bacolod 18th Street PalaPala
Serves freshest seafoods the palapala (seafood market way) and cooked anyway you want it. Also served grilled chicken inasal, barbeque pork and spare ribs. Turo-turo or ready-cooked Bacolod delicacies and meals are also on display for those who want quick meals or just want that mouth-watering Bacolod food. Ambiance is very typical of Bacolod. Price is very affordable.
