### [Masskara Festival](/asia/philippines/negrosisland/bacolod/festivals/masskara_festival)

The show of masks, dances, food, and lots of fun. This showcases a madigra of how Bacolodnons adapt to even drastic changes of the situation of the local status.

### [Pana-ad Sang Negros Festival](/asia/philippines/negrosisland/bacolod/festivals/panaad_sang_negro)

A nine day of celebration of the "**Mother of all Festivals of Negros Occidental**".
Showcasing all of what the 19 towns and 13 cities has to offer in terms of trade, tourism and culture.
