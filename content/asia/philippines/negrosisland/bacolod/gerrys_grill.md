A branch of the popular Filipino chain known for grilled dishes and Filipino comfort food. The Lacson Street location is convenient if you're in the city center. The menu covers the usual hits — sisig, grilled liempo, sinigang — at prices that won't stretch a budget. Nothing remarkable, but consistently decent and a safe choice when you're not sure where to eat. The air-conditioned dining room is a welcome break from the heat.
