---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

From **Manila**, you can access **Bacolod City** by land, sea and air.
Through land is by using the Roll on Roll off port from island to island.
By sea there are two known Shipping Companies that access Bacood althrough out, these were **WG&A Super Ferry** and **Negros Navigation**.
By air, there flights from Manila direct to bacolod everyday, just contact the **Philippine Airlines**, **Cebu Pacific**, and **Air Philippines**, for more info.
