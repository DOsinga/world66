Bacolod's biggest event and one of the Philippines' most famous festivals. Dancers in elaborate smiling masks fill the streets for weeks in October, with competitions, street parties, and food everywhere. Started in 1980 as a response to economic hardship, it has become a joyful celebration of Bacolod's resilient spirit.
