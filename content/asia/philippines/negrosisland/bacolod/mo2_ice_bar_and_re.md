A long-running nightlife venue on Lacson Street in Mandalagan, MO2 draws a mixed crowd of students and young professionals for live bands, DJs, and affordable drinks. The place is large enough to absorb big weekend crowds without feeling impossibly packed. Food is standard Filipino bar fare — sisig, pulutan platters, buffalo wings — decent enough to soak up the San Miguel. Gets going around 9 pm on weekends.
