Mushu is an Asian fusion restaurant on 20th Lacson Street, the creation of Bacolod-born chef JP Anglo. The menu mixes Southeast Asian and Filipino flavors — standouts include the Laksa, Batchoy Ramen, and a Lechon wrapped in dough skin. It is a popular gathering spot among locals, as much a bar and lounge as a restaurant.
