---
title: "Day Trips in Negros island"
type: section
---

Make your travel to the Philippines unforgettable by including the most popular **Negros Island Day Trips** in your itinerary. Known as the 3rd largest island in the country, Negros consists of some of the most beautiful spots which tourists never fail to visit. It is also identified for its rich culture and history. Have a taste of these day trips that are available for anybody who wants a great vacation.

**Mt. Kanlaon Climb**

It's worth all the sweat when you reach the crater of Mount Kanlaon, an active volcano situated on Negros Island. No need to worry, though, because it's safe to climb this mountain unless it is declared by the authorities as otherwise. The trek duration is usually 3 hours. However, a complete round lasts 3 days.

**Cave Explorations**

There are a lot of caves to be found on Negros Island. It surely will be a miss if you don't get to visit any of them. Take in the Cayaso Cave in Mabinay that leads to an underground river, in which the water is drinkable. Also try exploring the Pandalihan Cave in the same area. It is accessible by foot and contains beautiful formations of stalactites and stalagmites. Tours can be arranged with nearby resorts.

**Underwater Adventures**

Love to get a first-hand view of what the marine environment is like? Whether you are already a marine expert or a newbie at this field, the underwater scenery at Mina's Undersea World in Zamboanquita is sure to have you awed for the rest of the day. Go snorkeling and swimming and get to see amazing marine creatures such as white sea turtles and many more.

**Bird Sanctuary and Mangrove Park**

Negros Island also boasts of its abundance of natural resources and wildlife. If you want a bird-watching trip added to your list of activities, the Bird Sanctuary and Mangrove Park in Bais City is the place to be. With a large collection of different species of birds and other wildlife, this park offers tourist a great view of the city's natural wealth.

**Bais Bay Cruises**

Want to catch a glimpse of dolphins and pygmy sperm whales jumping in and out of the water during the day? You not only get a glimpse if you ride on one of Bais City Hall's cruising services. A trip to the Tañon Strait avails you a chance to watch these wonderful water creatures play under the sun. They appear more often, though, from May to October.
