---
title: "Getting Around in Negros Island"
type: section
---

**Negros Island transportation** can help you to have a wonderful and safe travel around the place. Negros Island is divided into two parts: Negros Occidental and Negros Oriental.

#### **Long Distance Travel**

Jeepney is the national mode of transportation in the Philippines and the main form of transportation on Negros Island. Many of the residents of Negros Island do not have their own vehicles, so they rely on public transport. These jeepneys are controlled by private owners, and they link the many towns of Negros Island. The fare is based on the distance you are going to travel. Visitors may find it advisable to ask the local residents how much it will cost to travel to your destination. Pay the fare exactly so you will not worry about your change.

#### **Short Distance Travel**

Motorized tricycles, or pedicabs, are the form of transportation used for short distance travel. There are motorized tricycles available in every corner of the area. Motorized tricycles can be found parking in popular areas such as the market, mall or church. There are also moving tricycles that look around to find passengers. Tricycles are just about everywhere, so you can reach your destination quickly and at any hour. The island can be noisy at times with all of the motorized pedicabs.
