A sheltered bay just north of Sipalay with white sand beaches and excellent diving. The horseshoe shape keeps the water calm, and the entrance is a wide coral garden teeming with reef fish. The bay is also good for kayaking, and the surrounding coastline has several small resorts catering to divers. It's quieter than the better-known beaches of the Visayas and worth the trip if you're looking for good reefs without the crowds.
