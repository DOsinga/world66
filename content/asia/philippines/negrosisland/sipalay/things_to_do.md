DIVING!!!!
DIVE SPOTS

The coastline has excellent areas for snorkeling while scuba diving thrives in 42 diving spots. Some are identified as follows:

**MS JOJO**
Freighter from the 80's. Excellent condition. Topmost level at 20 m. Max. depths. 33 meters. Plenty of big lion fish to be seen.

**SS PANAY**
Exiting wreck from the second world war. Very good condition with 33 m at topmost level. This war ship was hit by Japanese torpedo's in March 1942. Still gas mask in the stock rooms.

**ELCO Wreck**
Nice small wreck at Obon Pt with nice corals and plenty of never seen big stag horn corals with plenty of fish inside.

**Juliens Wreck**
Wreck dive with some current at 6 meters. The quiet big wreck lies on a large area. You can dive or snorkel on it, beautiful coral coverage.

**Cathedral Tower**
Deep dive down to 40 meters on an excellent reef. A tower goes up to 22 meters.

**Turtle Island South Pt.**
South of Turtle Island , we have a beautiful reef which is full of soft and hard corals. Diving is best at 6 to 18 meters.

**Mystery Wall**
Beautiful Drop Off starts at 35 meters down to 70 meters. Only for Deep Divers. Large Turtles can be seen.

**Eva's Point**
Drop off wall from 4 to 33 meters straight down. Just fantastic.

**Eva's Pt. Extension**
Wall dive toward Campomanes Bay with a max depth of 26 meters. Plenty of table corals.

**Habok Habok Reef**
A very nice Wall with several big cracks and Canyons in it. Depth from 1 to 16m.

**Villamor**
A special dive site near Hinoba-an. Entry at 5 meters going through channels. If current comes in , the dive site changes to a drift dive.
Most beautiful soft corals on reef top.

**Kevin's Reef**
Excellent house reef with an old big anchor at 9-meter depths. Max. depths 24 meters.

**A- Rock**
Nice coral rocks surrounded by white sand. Depths 12 to 18 meters.

**Great Canyons**
Really great canyons to dive through like a labyrinth. All at 8 to 17 meters.

**Toscana Reef**
An other very nice house reef like a drop off from 11 meter down to 28 meters.

**Disneyland**
Very exiting dive site with coral blocks and white sand. Plenty of lions fish. 6 to 15 meters. Excellent for night diving with lots of night active marine life.

**Leonardo Reef**
Healthy coral reef with hard and soft corals. Max. depths at 15 m.

**MA - ASIN Island**
Most beautiful big table corals ever seen. Great coral garden from 8 to 28 meters. Mostly good visibility.

**MA-ASIN South Wall 1**
Nice coral walls starts from 5 down to 30 meters.

**MA-ASIN South Wall 2**
An other wall full of nice corals starts from 5 m down to 30 meters.

**Black Rock**
Steep wall dive along the front reef of Obon Pt. From 5 to 25 meters with slightly current.

**Black Rock I**
Similar extension of Black Rock, 5m to 18 meters.

**Black Rock II**
Nice Dive starts at 4 meters with small drop of down to 12 meters. Several channels along the wall.

**Spiral Drops**
Straight down wall from 5 meters down to 27 meters, with a Coral formation like a spiral.

**Catmon Pt. I**
Nice coral garden near Hinoba an with canyons and walls.

**Catmon Pt. II**
North of Catmon Pt. is an other dive site from from 5 to 18 meters. Excellent for day trips.

**Matatindoc Pt.**
Simple wall dive along the front reef of Nataasan Resort.
From 5 to 18 meters with slightly current.

**Enzian Reef**
Reef / Wall dive with a very nice plateau at 28 meters, max. 40 meters.

**Sunken Island**
Beautiful big sunken island with plenty of fish and very nice table corals. 8 m down to 30. For many divers one of the best dive site.

**Clara Reef**
The reef starts at 11 meters down to 30 on a exiting slightly dropping wall. Plenty of Butterfly Fish among others and with the best chance of good visibility.

**Ayers Rock**
Fantastic sunken island with wonderful corals and plenty of fish at 18 to 35 meters depths.

**Marco Polo**
Negative entry down to a fantastic coral reef with hundreds of big fish down to 30 meters. Just amazing and very unique. There are sharks passing by

**Da West**
Negative entry down to a fantastic coral reef with hundreds of big fish like Barracuda, Tuna, etc. down to 34 meters. Just amazing and very unique. There are Sting Rays and Sharks passing by. Perfect for underwater video and photography.Just in front of your cottage.

**Miami Beach**
Straight down wall to 30 meters. The coral top is on 4 to 5 meters. Nice corals and a lot of colorful coral fish

**Blue Wall**
Deep blue negative entry down to 45 metes. Only for experienced divers.

**Fritzantino Wall**
Very nice drop off down to 40 meters. The coral wall
goes up to 15 meters. You even can reach the shallow area at 5 meters. Very exiting dive experience.

**Marcello Reef**
A very nice underwater world for divers with canyons. And different cracks. Depths from 5 to 28 meters.

**Buluguisan Pt.**
A coral plateau of about 5 meters starts the dive.A very nice drop off goes down to 22 meters to an entrance to a big cave. Excellent corals and gorgonians.
