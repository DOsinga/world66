---
title: "Things to do in Negros island"
type: section
---

The list of **Negros Island things to do** has many activities.
The Philippines are a group of Southeast Asian islands which
contain beautiful landscapes, friendly people and immaculate beaches.
Negros Island is one of the popular tourist destinations within this
group of islands.
**Take a Trip Up Mount Kanlaon**

This area has national-park status and, because of its landscape, is a
popular destination for hikers. It contains an active volcano which
means that some areas can be difficult to access. However, the national
park is sufficiently large to accommodate these people safely and with
ease. It also contains dense woodlands and a number of notable birds and
animals. For example, you might see the spotted deer or bleeding heart
pigeon.
**Visit the Pleasant Town of Dumaguete**

The fact that many of the larger towns and cities of the Philippines
are notoriously cramped, smelly and unhygienic means that a visit to
this rare example of a charming town is definitely one of the important
Negros Island things to do. The waterfront promenade is particularly
nice. It is also a university town and, because of this, is one of the
trendier places in which to hang out.
**Go SCUBA diving**

The Philippines contain a number of particularly nice places for
diving. The clear waters and hot summer environment are conducive to
this activity. Some of the best SCUBA diving on the island is found in
Sipalay which is on the southwest side of the island. The dive sites are
located at Punta Ballo and the calm waters make it a good place for
learners.
**Go Sunbathing on Sugar Beach**

Sugar Beach contains some of the white sand beaches for which the
Philippines are famous. Here, the sand is clean and the water is crystal
clear. It is an ideal spot for swimming or simply spending a lazy few
hours in the sun.
**Visit Twin Lakes National Park**

This is another part of the island which is particularly conducive to
wildlife and hiking. The scenery is extremely impressive and the region
is also noted as being home to the indigenous Bukidnon people. It is
possible to arrange for an experienced guide to show tourists around the
area. By Western standards, the prices tend to be extremely
competitive. There are a number of rare orchids here.
