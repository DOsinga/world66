Ozamiz City sits at the western tip of Misamis Occidental province on the shores of Panguil Bay in northern Mindanao. Known locally for its busy market and as a regional commercial hub, the city has a relaxed pace typical of provincial Philippine cities. The bay views are pleasant, and the city serves as a jumping-off point for the surrounding municipalities. Fort Santiago, a Spanish-era fortification, is a historical point of interest in the older part of town.
