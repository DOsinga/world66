---
title: "Family Travel Ideas in Quezon Province"
type: section
---

If you are traveling with your family to Quezon Province, these **Quezon
Province family travel** tips will give you an idea of things you can
do as a family. Quezon Province has several attractions that are just
fight for families. Here are a few recommendations.

**Bird Island–Polillo Island**

The Bird Island is a beautiful biodiversity situated in the Island of
Polillo, Quezon. The Bird Island started out as several cluster of
corals that later on became a huge coral formation and eventually became
a rough island. It now serves as a sanctuary to several species of
birds like the Philippine duck, the Philippine cockatoo, the Philippines
dwarf-kingfisher, the Chinese egret and much more. Today, Polillo Bird
Island is protected by the DENR and IBA. A visit to the Bird Island will
be a real treat for the whole family.

**Lopez Submarine Garden-Lopez, Quezon**

The Lopez Submarine Garden is a perfect place for families to enjoy
water sports like snorkeling and scuba diving. This place has a varied
collection of marine life and coral reefs. The submarine garden is
located in Brgy Hondagua in Lopez, Quezon.

**Quezon National Park**

The Quezon National Park covers an area of more than 9-million
hectares. This huge park features natural waterfalls, limestone
formations and caves. A visit to this park with your family will give
them the opportunity to enjoy the vast greenery and wide collection
flora and fauna, as well as some animals such as monkeys, snakes, birds
and butterflies. Well known hot spots that are located within the Quezon
National Park are Sapang Ulo, Sapinit Malaki, Sapinit Maliit, Amao and
many others. This park is located along the Maharlika Highway.

**Kwebang Lampas**

Kwebang Lampas is a wonderful rock formation in the waters of
Pagbilao Bay in Brgy Ibabang Polo in Pagbilao, Quezon. The cave itself
is a picturesque spot. You will be captivated by the wonderful white
sand beaches and crystal clear sea water, as well as the diverse marine
life and coral reefs. This is a must-visit spot for families visiting
Quezon.

**Bantakay Falls and Cave**

The Bantakay falls and cave are located in Atimonan, Quezon. You and
your family can start your journey with an hour of walking and trekking
along the Maharlika Highway. You can enjoy a dip in the cold water at
the beautiful waterfalls of Bantakay. The Bantakay cave is located just a
few meters away from the falls. This is a nice place to enjoy a trek
and a picnic with your family.
