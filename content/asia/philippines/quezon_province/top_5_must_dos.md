---
title: "Top 5 Must Do's in Quezon Province"
type: section
---

While holidaying in Quezon Province, you would not want to miss out
on these **Quezon Province must do’s**. Here are the top 5 things you
need to do while in Quezon Province.

**Beach Hopping- Pagbilao Grande**

The Pagbilao Grande coastal area has several beautiful beaches,
fishing grounds and wonderful scenery. You can go to the beach and go island
hopping here and explore the magnificent spots nature has to offer.
Popular places you can explore are the Puting Buhangin beach, Pagbilao
bay, Borawan beach and Patayan Island to name just a few. Besides island
hopping, you can also engage in water sports like snorkeling and
diving.

**Nature Tripping-Mount Banahaw**

Mount Banahaw is a popular spot for trekking here in Quezon. This
mountain has different access points from different towns in Quezon.
Besides the vast forestry, there are also some amazing hidden nature
spots in this majestic mountain. A few of these must-visit spots are the
Sta Lucia Falls, the San Jacob’s underground cave and the Kinabuhayan
complex.

**Cultural Tour-All over Quezon**

A visit to Quezon must include a culture tour. The tour includes a
visit to the towns of Sariaya, Tayabas, Lucban, Mauban and Gumaca. You
will be able to see Spanish-inspired ancient architecture like the
Malagunlong Bridge, the San Diego Fort, the Caprichosa Park and the San
Miguel de Archangel Basilica.

**Merry Month of May**

The best time to visit Quezon is during the month of May. This is
because most of the town fiestas take place during this month. Enjoy
yourself at the Agawan festival in Sariaya and then move on to the
Pahiays festival in Lucban. Follow these two up by taking part in the
Mayohan festival in Tayabas and the Pasayahan sa Lucena in Lucena.

**Quezon Kulinarya Tour**

The Quezon Kulinarya Tour is a tour that features the province
culture, colors and flavors. This tour starts in Lucban, Quezon where
you will taste the infamous pansit habhab, boras and longanisang Lucban.
You will also get a chance to experience a bit of the Pahiyas festival
and buy local souvenirs. After this, the tour will take you to Kamayan sa
Palaisdaan where you will be able to in the floating bamboo rafts and
feast on local cuisines like ginataang tilapia and more. You will also
be able to watch how Lambanog is made in Calle Rizal, Sariaya. Finally,
you will be able to explore Ugu Bigyan Potter’s Garden. This is also
where this famous local potter showcases his works.
