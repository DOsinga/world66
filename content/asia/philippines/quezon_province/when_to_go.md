---
title: "When to Go in Quezon Province"
type: section
---

If you are planning a holiday in **Quezon Province, when
to go** is probably the question on your mind. Quezon Province is one
of the largest agricultural and coastal provinces in the Philippines.
Here are a few suggestions to help you decide when to go.

**Enjoy the Sun**

You don’t need any special reason to go and have fun in Quezon
Province. This huge province in Region IV-A has lots of beautiful
beaches and natural resources that will definitely keep you busy all
throughout your vacation. If you plan your holiday any time between
March and July, you can be sure to enjoy the beautiful beaches to the
fullest.

**Quezonarya Month**

March is one of the nicest months to visit Quezon. Not only because
it's spring, but because March is Quezonarya month. Quezonarya is a
culinary festival organized by the provincial government and all of the
towns and city within Quezon participate in this event. You can watch
and enjoy as different teams compete in different categories. The best
part is in tasting amazing creations afterward.

**Lenten Season-April**

If you want to experience an extraordinary Lenten season, plan your
holiday to Quezon during lent. Quezon celebrates the Lenten season in an
extraordinary manner. If you are looking to spend some time in quiet
reflection and spiritual renewal, then plan your holiday in Quezon
during lent.

**Merry Month of May**

May is the best month to visit Quezon. Most of the town fiestas take
place during May to celebrate the feast of San Isidro
Labrador, the patron saint of farmers. The most famous festival in
Quezon is the Pahiyas Festival in Lucban, where locals decorate their
homes with rice, vegetables, fruits and the infamous kiping, longanisang
lucaban and pansit habhab.

**Christmas **

Although December is one of the coldest months in Quezon, you will
still feel welcome and loved. The locals in Quezon are known
for their hospitality and love for families, which is why Christmas is
also one of the best times to visit Quezon. During the Christmas season
there are lots of bazaars, carnivals and events on the street throughout the
whole province. You don’t want to miss the solemn simbang gabi, the
luscious puto bungbong and bibingka. You will definitely have the best
Christmas in Quezon, ever. If you want to have a unique Christmas, plan
your visit to Quezon in December.
