MOGWAI CAFÉ
Unit 62 & 63 Cubao Expo, Gen. Romulo Ave., Cubao

Located in Cubao Ex (formerly Marikina Shoe Expo), owned by two of the most prestigious film and advertising directors in Philippines, Erik Matti and Lyle Sacris.

A place to relax and to have a good conversation with friends or colleagues about creative ideas, life or any random thoughts.

Homey and yet artsy ambiance of rustic interiors combined with good food and drinks. The best sellers are Sole Fillet, Twice Cooked Fish, Broccoli Soup, Dynamite, home made Potato Chips, Lava Cake and Alamid Coffee.

Mogwai has a small cinema on the second floor with wide selection of both local and international films.

Open from 6pm to 2am, time may be adjusted for private parties. Price range is about 150php - 300php ($4 to $8 US) per person for drinks and chips only and 280php – 500php ($7 to $13 US) per person for food and drinks. For tickets and how to be part of Mogwai Cinematheque, send email to mogwaifilm@yahoo.com.
