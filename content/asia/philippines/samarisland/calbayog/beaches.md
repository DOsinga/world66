---
title: 'Beaches'
type: section
---

Calbayog's beaches are not postcard-perfect white sand, but they have genuine charm. Malajog Beach, about 45 minutes from the city centre, is the most popular -- 200 metres of dark grey sand backed by towering rocks, with floating cottages available for rent. Naga Beach is just 10 minutes from downtown and good for a quiet morning swim, best suited to families with young children. Bagacay Beach is also close to town and convenient when you want to combine beach time with shopping at nearby stalls.
