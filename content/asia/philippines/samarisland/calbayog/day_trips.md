---
title: 'Day Trips'
type: section
---

From Calbayog you can visit Mapaso Hot Springs in the Oquendo District -- a 30-minute ride plus a short hike to natural hot pools where pink crustaceans scuttle about in the warm water. The Guinogo-an Caves in the same district offer an atmospheric underground trek. For a more substantial outing, the Marble Mountains in the Tinambacan District combine hiking with geological curiosity. Binaliw Isle makes a good island-hopping target with marine life visible at low tide.
