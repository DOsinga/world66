---
title: 'Eating Out'
type: section
---

Calbayog has a mix of local Filipino eateries and simple restaurants. Kamayan sa Carayman is popular for fresh seafood, clam soup, and chili crab served al fresco. Jollibee on Gomez Street covers the fast-food basics for under two dollars a meal. For something more adventurous, the makeshift carinderias (street-side food stalls) found throughout the city serve cheap home-cooked Filipino dishes. Marcial's Grille in Brgy. Matobato does good baby back ribs.
