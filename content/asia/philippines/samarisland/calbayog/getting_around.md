---
title: 'Getting Around'
type: section
---

Tricycles (pedicabs) are the cheapest way to get around Calbayog at about 7 pesos per trip. Habal-habals -- modified motorcycles seating up to four passengers under a small roof -- cover longer distances and rougher roads. Jeepneys run set routes around and between districts. For day trips outside the city, hiring a tricycle or habal-habal for the day is usually the most practical option.
