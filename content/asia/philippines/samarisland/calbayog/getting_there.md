---
title: 'Getting There'
type: section
---

Calbayog Airport, about 7 km from the city centre, has domestic flights from Manila several times a week. Overnight ferries from Cebu dock at Calbayog City Pier, taking roughly 12 hours. From elsewhere on Samar, jeepneys and buses connect via Allen in the north. Tricycles are available from the airport and pier into town.
