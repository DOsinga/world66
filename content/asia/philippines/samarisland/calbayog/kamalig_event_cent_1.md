A casual dining spot in Barangay Rawis that uses local ingredients to create dishes inspired by recipes from around the world. The ambiance is relaxed and the staff friendly. Kamalig also hosts events -- weddings, birthdays, and parties -- so the space can feel either intimate or festive depending on the night. Budget around 200 pesos per person for a meal.
