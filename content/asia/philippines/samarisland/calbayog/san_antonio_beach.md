San Antonio is a small islet off the northern tip of Samar, close to Capul Island and the Biri Rock formations. The beach is a quiet strip of white sand with clear water, far from any tourist infrastructure. Access is by motorized banca from San Isidro or Victoria on the mainland -- the ride takes about twenty minutes. From Calbayog airport, Victoria is roughly an hour by road.
