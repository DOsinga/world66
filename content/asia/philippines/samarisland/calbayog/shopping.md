---
title: 'Shopping'
type: section
---

The Calbayog Public Market is the main shopping area for fresh produce, seafood, locally made clothing, and crafts. For the freshest catch, head to Pier One before dawn when fishing vessels unload their haul. Pasalubong stores downtown sell local delicacies like calamay, bibingka, and pili nut candies -- good gifts to bring home.
