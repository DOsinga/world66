---
title: 'Things to Do'
type: section
---

Calbayog's attractions are all about nature. Beach-hop between Malajog, Lo-ok, and Bagacay beaches. Trek to any of the city's many waterfalls -- Bangon-Bugtong Falls has the best swimming pool at its base. Slide down the Mawacat natural water slide, a 50-metre mossy rock chute into cool water. Explore the Guinogo-an and Danao caves if you do not mind the dark (and bats). Visit the grand old Sts. Peter and Paul Cathedral in the city centre, one of the oldest and largest churches on Samar.
