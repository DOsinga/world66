---
title: 'When to Go'
type: section
---

Calbayog has a tropical climate with rain distributed year-round, so there is no sharp wet and dry season. The driest months tend to be March through May, which is the most comfortable time for beach visits and waterfall treks. Always carry a light rain jacket or umbrella. Check monsoon conditions before heading to the beach, as the Pacific swells can be unpredictable.
