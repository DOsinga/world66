---
title: "Bars and Cafes"
type: section
---

San Pablo is a provincial city rather than a nightlife destination. Dub City Cafe is the most distinctively alternative bar in town — live and DJed music, eclectic décor, and a mixed Filipino-international crowd; also serves tea and coffee during the day. Sulyap Gallery Cafe in a restored colonial house offers antiques, art, and Filipino home cooking in a historic setting. Clydenjosh, built from bamboo overlooking Sampaloc Lake, is a relaxed café and eating spot with a memorable setting.
