title: Pandin and Yambo Lakes
type: poi

Twin volcanic crater lakes 10km north of San Pablo centre, more secluded than Sampaloc. Bamboo raft tours (30 minutes, organised by the local lakeside community) are the main draw — guides pole the rafts across both lakes. A ridge trail connects the two. Accessible by jeepney from the cathedral area.
