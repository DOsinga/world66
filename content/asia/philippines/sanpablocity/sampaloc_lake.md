---

The largest of San Pablo's seven volcanic crater lakes, covering 104 hectares. A concrete path circles the entire shore, popular for early morning walks and bike rides. Freshwater fish farms line the eastern shore. Lakeside restaurants serve tilapia and catfish caught in the lake.
