---

Your vacation wouldn&rsquo;t be complete without experiencing the numerous **San Pablo City shopping** destinations around the city. Here are a few ideas to help you enjoy your shopping experience.

**SM City **

SM Prime Holdings is one of the leading mall operators in the Philippines, and it has recently opened a branch in San Pablo City. SM City San Pablo is located in the Riverina Subdivision in San Rafael. The mall has around 200 shops and boutiques that sell local and international clothing brands like Bench, Girbaud, Penshoppe, and Human. The mall also houses cinemas, supermarkets, department stores, and food courts. Here you can pick up some amazing bargains and shop till you drop! There are also caf&eacute;s and restaurants available so you can take a break from your shopping and enjoy a good snack or meal. You might be interested in taking a look at the several stores selling electronic goods as well.

**Ultimart Shopping Plaza**

This is one of the major shopping complexes within the city of San Pablo. The mall has several clothing stores that sell top international brands. If you are looking to pick up some hand crafted items, this shopping complex is the right place to shop. To give you a pleasurable shopping experience, there are some caf&eacute;s and restaurants that serve delicious food.

**San Pablo City Shopping Center**

The San Pablo City Shopping Center is located in the corner of Bonifacio Street and Rizal Avenue. This shopping center houses different stores and boutiques that sell local clothing brands, locally made shoes and slippers from the nearby town Liliw, Laguna, and accessories and jewelry made from indigenous materials. You will also be able to pick up some souvenirs to take home.

**Centro Park Department Store and Supermarket**

This is a department store and supermarket rolled into one. If you are looking to pick up some basic necessities, this store is the right place for you. The store offers locally made clothes for both men and women, as well as shoes, bags, hats, and other fashion accessories. You will be able to pick up things in this store at much cheaper prices than at other places.

**Pasalubong Centers**

There are many Pasalubongs around the city and at the main transportation terminals. The Pasalubongs are places where you can buy local delicacies like Buko Pie, coconut-based sweets and budin, as well as other local handicrafts like slippers, shoes and native jewelry. Check out one of these if you're in need of a great local souvenir.
