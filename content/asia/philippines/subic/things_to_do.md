---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Subic Bay’s mix of tropical jungle, wartime history, and marine life makes for varied outings. The old-growth forest within the freeport zone is home to wild monkeys and good birdwatching trails. Wreck diving on World War II ships in the bay is a major draw for divers. Family attractions like Zoobic Safari and Ocean Adventure draw weekend crowds from Manila.
