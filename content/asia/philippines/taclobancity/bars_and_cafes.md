### ['ayo Cafe](/asia/philippines/taclobancity/bars_and_cafes/ayo_cafe)

The establishment offers special sandwiches, pastas, salads, and set meals. Also included in their menu are desserts like cheesecakes and yogurt mousse pies. They also accept in-house catering. Wi-Fi is provided by Blast (Bayantel); cards available from the counter.
