---
title: "Bars and Cafes"
type: section
---

Tacloban's drinking scene runs from quiet coffee bars on Juan Luna Street to live music venues around Sto. Niño and Burgos. Calle Zaragosa (Calle Z) on Independencia is the long-running favourite — open until late, with grilled food and music — while Topline Music and Bar near the corner of Burgos has staked its claim as the city's best small live-music room. Beer is cheap and the pace is unhurried; most places get going only after dinner.
