The establishment offers special sandwiches, pastas, salads, and set meals. Also included in their menu are desserts like cheesecakes and yogurt mousse pies. Most ingredients are sourced locally, making use of fresh and seasonal products. Flavors of dishes are attuned to the local taste but food preparation methods and presentation are carried out with outmost consideration to quality and safety. They also accept in-house catering. Vegetarian requests can happily be accommodated.
