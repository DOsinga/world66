A great chance to eat spicy Indian food in Tacloban.

The **Fil-India** restaurant is located on the Coca Cola rotunda near the new Robinsons shopping mall just outside Tacloban City.

The menu offers Mutton, Beef, Chicken, Fish, or Vegetable curries cooked in the following tasty styles:

- Curry - Coconut based curry

- Madras - Tomato based curry

- Bhuna - Yoghurt based curry

- Biryani - Rice based dish

To complement the curries Biryani rice or Java rice are available by the
cup, and various Indian breads such as Nan bread are available.
