This popular little restaurant is located in the garden of the Rosvenil Pensione hotel, on P.Burgos street, close to its junction with Real street

A pleasant, brightly lit, covered outdoor area is well furnished with sturdy wooden tables and chairs and can get very busy at times.

The local menu features a selection of grilled meat and seafood dishes such as grilled Tuna, as well as the usual local favourites such as Pork Sisig and Sinigang.

A good place to sit on a warm evening.
