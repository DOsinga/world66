**Topline Music and
Bar** is
located at Sto Niño Street near the corner of Burgos Street, Tacloban City - a
once famous residential neighborhood to the Encarnado’s,, Larraga’s, Tismo’s
and many more prominent Taclobanons . Sto. Niño Street and the Tacloban
itself has recently experienced an explosion of business including bars,
venues, art galleries, eateries and an eclectic assortment of shops and cafes. Topline
Music and Bar, finds itself in the center of this revival as one of the best
local bars and the streets premier music venue capable of hosting rock-n-roll
shows that can parade talent in front of many people.

The space has been fully
renovated and altered to provide a superior music venue and complete DJ system
with a warm, welcoming front room that offers all the fun conveniences to the
delight of our customers.

Topline Music and Bar offers
daily and nightly specials, serving frost-chilled cocktails and drinks all
night long to the tune of the music of live band performers, standing comedians
and DJs to entertain everyone.

What is most remembered
about Topline Music and Bar though is its impressive staged venue. Completely
rebuilt and drastically improved, Topline has turned itself into a band
friendly, high quality music venue. Already home to rock shows that will go
down in Tacloban live music lore, Topline has a driving primary goal, to have a
great sounding room that is booked with great bands. So far we have had an
extremely positive response from the public both local and visitor and an
excited reaction from the bands that have already come through.

If you haven’t experiencde
us yet, just tell us and we’ll treat you with lots of surprises.
