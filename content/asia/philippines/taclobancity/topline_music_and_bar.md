A small live-music bar on Sto. Niño Street near the corner of Burgos, in a stretch of downtown Tacloban that has filled up with cafes, galleries, and eateries in the years since Yolanda. The room is set up around a proper stage with a working DJ rig, and the booking leans towards local rock bands and the occasional stand-up night. Cold cocktails, late hours, and a friendly door — one of the better places in town to spend a long evening.
