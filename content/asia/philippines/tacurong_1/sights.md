Well Tacurong City is not just a little city with a bunch of Internet Cafe's, but yes they do pop out out like mushrooms in every corner of the City. I would just like to make a point to the previous entry. No, I can basically attest that not all Tacurongnon's are not ashamed of their hometown and in fact are proud to be citizens of the city. I myself, can attest to this. If you possess an iron liver, then this City is a mecca for you, Red Horse beer is abundant and cheap and there a lot of roadside bars and taverns to be in if you are planning to know what it feels like to have a malfunctioning liver. After a nights out of being drunk and almost losing your conscoiusness, call someone else to hail a tricycle (the local means of public transportation) and have your 8 pesos ready, just tell the driver to head straight to Beldent Star Hotel or Edjin Lodge (cost: just a hefty 750 for a days stay) and sleep all your booz till Father Sunrise wakes you up. If your budget constraints from having comfortable amenities then opt for the cheaper Motels that would cost you from 150Php to 300Php. If you'd like to dine out, then head to VE-JR and at Exit for an exotic adventure of Catfish and other delicacies. Well, I'm not planning to make a comprehensive travel tips for you, I'm just passing by.
