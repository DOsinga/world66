A small beach resort at the mouth of the Liboganon River, where it meets the Davao Gulf. Popular with locals from Tagum City as a weekend escape — low-key by international standards, but the setting where the river meets the sea gives it a pleasant, unhurried character. Good for swimming and picnicking. Bring your own food and drink, as facilities are basic.
