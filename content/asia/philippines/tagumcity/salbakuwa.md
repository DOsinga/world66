A local favourite known for its bulalo (beef bone marrow soup), balbacua (slow-cooked ox feet stew), and other traditional Filipino dishes. The portions are generous and the prices are low, making it a popular spot for families and groups. Located in front of Ursua Village in the Mangga area of Tagum.
