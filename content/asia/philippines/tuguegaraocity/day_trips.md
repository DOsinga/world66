**Tuguegarao City day trips** let you indulge yourself in natural activities. Tuguegarao, as part of a highland place, is blessed with lush mountains, wonderful caves and a clean river. Your day trips can include exploring all of these magnificent places.

#### **Cave Exploration in Penablanca**

Penablanca is a place in Cagayan famous for its numerous caves. It is formerly known as Barrio de Bubug or the Village of Fire Tree. There are more than 400 caves and only 20 percent of these caves have been discovered and explored. Callao Cave is the most popular caves in Penablanca because of its natural skylights. When you get inside the, you will see seven chambers of limestone formation and a chapel. The cave entrance is accessible by climbing 187 steps. Outside the Callao Caves, you will see a lot of unexplored caves and a rich forest. The caves are located 25 kilometers from Tuguegarao.

#### **Kayaking on the Pinacanauan River**

Pinacanauan River is a great place for water adventures like kayaking and rafting. You can also go for swimming and boating in the river. The river is clean, so it is safe to dive and swim. When you look around the Pinacanauan River, it is surrounded by mountains. You can opt for hiking and mountain climbing if you tire of your water adventures. While you are visiting the Callao Caves, you may hire a boat to go on the river. Or you can hire a boat for a separate trip.
