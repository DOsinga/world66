---
title: "Eating Out"
type: section
---

Tuguegarao is known for Pancit Batil Patung — a local noodle dish made with egg noodles, vegetables, meat, and a fried egg on top, served with an egg soup. Gretchen's on Magallanes Street is the most established place to try it, and the version here is what the city's annual Pancit Eating Contest at the Pav-vurulun festival uses as its standard. Kainang Pinoy in Brgy. Leonarda is another good option for local cooking, including crispy pata, dinengdeng, and fresh river shrimp. Crown Bakeshop next to Hotel Roma does good pastries and sandwiches for breakfast.
