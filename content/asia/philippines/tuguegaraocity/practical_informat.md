**Tuguegarao City practical information** is the basic thing you must research before you plan your trip. The City of Tuguegarao is the capital of the province of Cagayan, Philippines and the regional capital of Region II.

**Language**

The dialects used by the people of Tuguegarao are Ytawes, Ybanag and Ilokano. You do not need to worry if you do not know these local dialects. The people of Tuguegarao can speak Tagalog. You can also communicate with the people of Tuguegarao using the English language. In the Philippines, foreigners can travel anywhere in the country because even the drivers know English.

**Money Exchange**

Tuguegarao use the Philippine peso as their currency. One US dollar is equivalent to around 46 pesos. Most of the money changers are found in the airport and mall. It is better to have your money exchanged before you leave the airport.

**Safety**

Tuguegarao City is a safe place for travelers. This city is so peaceful, and the people are friendly. The only thing that you must be wary of is when taking trips to the caves and river. However, there are guides whose job is to ensure that you are safe.
