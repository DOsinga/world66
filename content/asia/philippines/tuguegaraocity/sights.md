First-time visitors to Tuguegarao might find it to be too chaotic, particularly when compared to the metropolitan culture of Manila. However, beyond its choked markets and busy commercial buildings, **Tuguegarao City sights **have lots to offer. Tuguegarao is also the capital of Cagayan—a major, commercial province of Philippines.
