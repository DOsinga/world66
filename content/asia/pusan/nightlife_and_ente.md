---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment in Pusan"
type: section
---

There are a lot of activities that tourists can do to enjoy **Pusan nightlife.** Considered the second largest city in South Korea, Pusan is a very populous city and has diverse entertainment districts where tourists can enjoy their night away. Here are some of the bars and clubs in Pusan.

**Rock n Roll House**

This bar offers a good view of Haeundae Beach. The bar is famous for their daily drink special, and is frequented by both Koreans and westerners alike. The staff are great and can understand basic english. Rock and Roll music is also offered here, and several pool tables and ping pong tables are also situated in every corner of the bar. The entrance, drink and food in this bar cost from $10 to $20.

**Basement**

Located in the PNU area in Pusan, the bar has a very small area but offers a cozy atmosphere to its customers. It has a nice interior, comfortable chairs, and subtle music. This bar is a great place to drink with old friends or meet new ones, and offers live music every Friday and Saturday. Drinks at this place cost around $10 to $20.

**Westin Chosun Bar**

The Westin Chosun Bar is located inside the Westin Chosun Hotel. It offers live entertainment as well as a bar area where customers can relax and drink any type of beverage that they want. The bar has a competent bartender who can understand and speak English as well as prepare interesting cocktails. Drinks in the bar cost from $10 to $15.

**Vinyl Underground**

This bar plays upbeat music. It does not offer live bands, but it has its own DJ who plays upbeat Korean and International music. The bar is a bit small, but it has a small dance floor that is always jam packed with excited locals and foreigners dancing to the beat. Drinks are served at the bar by friendly waiters, and usually cost from $10 to $15.

**Star Face**

This bar is frequented by a great deal of locals and young professionals because it has great drink specials like all you can drink Jim Beam, gin, beer, or vodka. The bar also plays music from local bands and foreign expatriates living in Korea. They also have several pool tables and plenty of seats for everyone.
