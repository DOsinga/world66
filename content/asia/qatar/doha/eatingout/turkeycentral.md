Great value Turkish food, plus superb and very reasonable carry outs.

I know Turkish food very well, and I must say that they mixed Turkish and Arabic food, and the result is not exciting. Just one example: "fried liver" is absolutely a different dish than the original Turkish one.

Extremely good value, and, if one is new to the wide and varied food-types available in Doha, this is certainly a tasty introduction to some "home-cooked" breads, meats, and salads from Arabia. The takeout is especially good.
