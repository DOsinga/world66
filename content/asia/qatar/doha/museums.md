**POST OFFICE MUSEUM **

Housed near the Souq Post Office, this museum covers the early days of Qatar's postal services and has some quaint Victorian artefacts, including old scales, post boxes, and stamps. It is open from 4pm - 6pm daily, except on Fridays.
