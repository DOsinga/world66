title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Doha in the last twenty years has gone from a dusty Gulf port to an unapologetically ambitious capital. I.M. Pei's **Museum of Islamic Art** on its private island was the opening shot; the slate-tiled **National Museum of Qatar** by Jean Nouvel — meant to evoke a desert rose — is the follow-up. Between them, walk the **Corniche**, the seven-kilometre promenade along Doha Bay. **Souq Waqif**, the restored traditional market, is where the city socialises at night.
