There is a new Amazon Kindle book called "May You Have an Interesting Wife!". The third chapter tells the story of an American expat, Michael, who lived in Jeddah and Yanbu for 2 years. The overall book is about two people, Michael and Bonnie. Bonnie is a great world traveler until she falls down a Mayan pyramid in Mexico. Michael has also traveled the world as well as having lived in Saudi Arabia. Michael and Bonnie meet and marry 5 years after Bonnie's injury, again traveling the world.
