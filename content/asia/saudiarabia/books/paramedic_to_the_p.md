A memoir by an American paramedic who served on the personal medical team for King Abdullah. It offers an unusual insider view of the royal household and day-to-day life in the kingdom that is difficult to find elsewhere. Well written and genuinely revealing. The book is banned inside Saudi Arabia by the Ministry of Information — read it before you arrive.
