The Dammam Corniche is a long seaside promenade along the Gulf waterfront, lined with parks, walking paths, and outdoor seating areas. It is the main public gathering space in the city, particularly lively in the evenings when families come to enjoy the sea breeze. The views extend across the Gulf and on clear days the causeway to Bahrain is visible. The corniche connects the city's main hotel district to the fishing harbour and Coral Island.
