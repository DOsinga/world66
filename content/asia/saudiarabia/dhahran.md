Dhahran is the headquarters of the Saudi oil company ARAMCO. Most of the people living in the city are employees or relatives of employees of ARAMCO, with the exception of some contract workers who work for ARAMCO.  Dhahran is a compound or contained town much like a military compound. It is a fenced in, access controlled area. Dhahran is totally self contained with its own recreation facilities (pools, tennis courts, raquetball courts, weight rooms, golf course, arcade, movie theatre playing out of date movies, putt-putt golf, incredible SCUBA in the Red Sea, private beach, BMX track, youth center and tons of desert for motorcycle riding). 

 Dhahran is split into two sections, main camp and the Rolling Hills. Main camp is the older portion of Dhahran built in the 1950's while the Hills was built during the 1980's when ARAMCO was hiring like crazy. Actually, Dhahran looks a lot like parts of [Arizona](/northamerica/unitedstates/arizona). 

 Outside of Dhahran lies the Saudi city of [Khobar](/asia/saudiarabia/khobar) where you can experience some Saudi culture, but American fast food places and stores have popped up everywhere there too.
