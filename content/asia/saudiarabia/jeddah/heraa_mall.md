Heraa Mall holds the distinction of being the first purpose-built shopping mall in Jeddah. Spread across several floors on Heraa Street, it hosts over 240 international and local retailers, a food court, and a family entertainment zone. It is not the newest or flashiest mall in the city — that competition is fierce in Jeddah — but it remains a popular and central shopping destination, particularly for everyday needs and mid-range fashion.
