This is a great cocktail bar located in the garden come courtyard of an old villa. The bar belongs to the French restaurant "Le Pont De Vie" at 42 Waterloo Street, so fine wines can be had but most come here for the great cocktails and chill-out music and ambience of drinking cocktails under the stars. Although the bar is in the downtown area (just off Bras Basah Rd) you have the feeling of being in the middle of the country, it's so relaxing. The cocktails are great with the classics, but also some newer ones, and the service is so friendly. Why go out to Rochester when this pleasurable experience is almost on your door step?
