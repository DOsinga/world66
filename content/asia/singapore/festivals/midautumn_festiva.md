Once again Chinatown has been dressed up for the Mid-Autumn Festival.It
was a fantastic evening, with a street procession, dragon and lion
dances, and even a spectacular fireworks display during the opening
ceremony. To experience the grand opening, take a visual tour at
singaporesights.com for the coverage.
