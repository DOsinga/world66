---

Legend has it that a magical turtle turned itself into an island to save two shipwrecked sailors — one Malay, one Chinese. A Malay shrine (keramat) and a Chinese Taoist temple (Tua Pek Kong) now stand on the island as reminders of that legend. Each year in the ninth lunar month, thousands of pilgrims make their way to Kusu for the annual pilgrimage, throwing coins into the Wishing Well. Outside the pilgrimage season, Kusu is a peaceful spot with a sheltered lagoon and sandy beach.
