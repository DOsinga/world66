Take a bumboat from Changi Point Ferry Terminal for the short crossing to Pulau Ubin — one of the last places in Singapore where kampong life survives. The island is about 10km in circumference and almost entirely free of cars; bicycles are cheap to rent at the main pier and the cycling is easy and enjoyable. The beaches are quiet and private, the forest paths are good for birdwatching, and the restaurants at the pier serve excellent fresh fish and Mee Goreng at prices that feel like another era.
