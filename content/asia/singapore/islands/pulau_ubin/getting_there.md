---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

Take a bumboat from **Changi Point Ferry Terminal**, which is served by bus 2 from Tanah Merah MRT. The bumboats leave when they have twelve passengers — you never wait more than a few minutes on a weekend, a little longer on quiet weekdays. The crossing takes about ten minutes. The fare is S$4 each way (cash only). The last boat back to the mainland departs when the last passenger wants to leave, but in practice you should aim to return before 7pm to be safe.

There is no advance booking. You simply turn up, pay the boatman, and go.
