title: Ubin Village
type: poi
---

The small kampong at the main pier is the social and practical centre of the island. Bicycle hire shops, a few seafood restaurants, a Chinese temple (Fo Shan Ting Da Bo Gong), and a Malay shrine are all clustered here. The village has been in continuous use since the quarrying era and retains a worn, authentic character entirely unlike anything on the mainland. Wander the backstreets behind the pier to see the remaining wooden houses and vegetable plots.
