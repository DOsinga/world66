The Red Dot Design Museum occupied a striking red-painted heritage building at 28 Maxwell Road, near Tanjong Pagar. It showcased award-winning product and communication design from more than 20 countries, rotating exhibitions drawn from the international Red Dot Design Award. The building itself — a former traffic police headquarters — was as much a draw as the collection, its bold red facade a deliberate contrast to the grey towers of the CBD.

The museum closed permanently in 2020. The building remains a landmark worth passing by if you're in the area.
