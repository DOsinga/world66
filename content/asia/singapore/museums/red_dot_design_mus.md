Preview how our future will evolve with an exhibition of award winning
design ideas from more than 20 nations at the red dot design museum.

The museum is located at red dot traffic, a heritage building given a new
lease of life with clever space transformation to become Singapore’s de
facto creative hub. Its red façade is an iconic landmark in Singapore and
a contrary to grey skyscrapers in the central business district.
