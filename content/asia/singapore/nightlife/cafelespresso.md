Cafe L'Espresso sits in the Goodwood Park Hotel on Scotts Road, one of Singapore's grand colonial-era hotels. The cafe is best known for its afternoon high tea and its pastry counter, which draws locals as much as hotel guests. The setting is polished but not stuffy, with seating spilling out onto a terrace overlooking the hotel's gardens.

The durian pastries are a local favourite if you are feeling adventurous. A pleasant stop after shopping along nearby Orchard Road.
