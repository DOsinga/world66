Part of the Traders Hotel on Cuscaden Road, this cafe serves a generous buffet spread covering both local and Western dishes. Popular with hotel guests and nearby office workers at lunch. Nothing destination-worthy on its own, but a reliable option if you're in the Orchard Road area and want variety without decision fatigue.
