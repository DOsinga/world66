Queensway Shopping Centre is Singapore's go-to destination for discounted sports gear. Spread across two floors near the Queenstown MRT, the mall is packed with small shops selling shoes, rackets, jerseys, and gym equipment at prices well below retail. Bargaining is expected and part of the fun — start at about 30% off the asking price and work from there. The selection of running shoes and football boots is particularly strong.
