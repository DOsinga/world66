One of Singapore's oldest golf clubs, founded in 1946 on a former RAF airfield near Changi. The 18-hole course is flat and breezy, with views of the Straits of Johor from several holes. Green fees for visitors are reasonable by Singapore standards, and the clubhouse has a relaxed, no-frills atmosphere compared to the island's more exclusive private clubs. The course sits next to the Changi Chapel and Museum, making it easy to combine a round with a bit of wartime history.
