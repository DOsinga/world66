A low-key pub tucked off North Bridge Road in a converted two-storey shophouse. The space has a relaxed feel — good for a quiet drink early on, livelier later when the karaoke kicks in around 8:30pm. Pool and electronic darts round out the entertainment. Nothing fancy, just an unpretentious local with reasonable beer prices.
