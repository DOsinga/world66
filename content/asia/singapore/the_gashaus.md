A long-running live music venue and bar on Middle Road in Bugis, known as a hub of Singapore's indie music scene. The Gashaus hosts an eclectic range of acts — punk, metal, jazz, reggae, J-rock — and rents out jamming studios by the hour. The space doubles as a casual hangout with pool tables, a full bar, and simple food.
