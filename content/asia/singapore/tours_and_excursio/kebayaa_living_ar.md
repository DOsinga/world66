There is an exhibition on nyonya kebaya held in Asian Civilisations Museum at Empress Place from 9/3/05 to 29/5/05. It features the best private collection of nyonya kebaya from Datin Paduka Seri Endon Mahmood of Malaysia.

What is Kebaya? It is a long-sleeved, figure-hugging blouse favoured by the Peranakans women . It is usually paired off with a batik sarong. It is very elegance design like the one wear by the Singapore Girl, SIA. Do take a journey into www.singaporesights.com and discover the splendid fashion in the early 20th century till now.
