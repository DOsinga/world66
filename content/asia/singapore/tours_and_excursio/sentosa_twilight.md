A small island just off Singapore's southern shore, connected to the mainland by a bridge, monorail, and cable car. Once a British military base, it has been developed into a resort destination with beaches, theme parks, and the massive Resorts World complex.

The cable car ride from Mount Faber offers good views over the harbour and container port. S.E.A. Aquarium houses a large collection of marine life. Universal Studios Singapore is here too, along with quieter stretches of beach if you want to escape the crowds.

Most visitors come as a day trip from the city centre, though there are hotels if you want to stay.
