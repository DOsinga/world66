The famous Kampung Batu Pahat Well in Minyank Beku is one of **Batu Pahat sights**. Batu Pahat, the second largest town in Johor State, district capital of Batu Pahat, also known as Bandar Pennagaram, is now developing as a major textile center with a booming economy. This is a city located on the west coast, in Johor State, in Malaysia. This city has earned the sobriquet of "shopping paradise." Not only tourists, but people from surrounding neighboring towns throng to Batu Pahat to enjoy the shopping experience in the shopping malls.

**The Sights:**

There are few amazing sights at Batru Pahat that are hard to resist.

**The Batu Pahat City Square**

This holds the &lsquo;The Chisel and Rock Monument.&rsquo; Batu Pahat means "Carved Rock" or "Chiselled Stone." The city is also called "Dataran Pennangaram." This city has celebrated its centenary in the 1990s. At that time, the government renovated the square. This Chisel and Rock monument is built in the center of the square and is a popular place for the locals to enjoy a cool evening out.

**Kampung Minyak Beku**

The ancient well that was chiseled by Siamese can still be seen in Batu Pahat in Kampung Minyak Beku.

**Tasik Y**

This is a lake which is a favourite place for the local people. Most of them can be found here early morning briskly walking or doing exercises.

**BP Walk**

The walk is on Jalan Rahmat, this is also popular locally.

**Two Famous Mosques**

There are two mosques in Batu Pahat. One is on Jalan Kluang. It is called Masjid Batu Pahat. The other mosque is called Jamek. This is at Jalan Ampuan. All tourists visit these mosques.

**Taman Parit Labis**

This is an ideal place for bird-watching. The Lesser Adjutant storks find a sanctuary in Batu Pahat. These birds are called locally as bald birds. The local name for them is burung botak. These birds are decreasing in number and considered endangered. These are migratory birds. Nature lovers flock to see these giant birds which are as tall as human beings.

The Summit Batu Pahat, Square One Mall, Batu Pahat Mall, and Carrefour Batu Pahat are the local malls that attract locals and tourists as well. A lighthouse at Cape Segenting is an added attraction.

**Bukit Banang Golf and Country Club **

This golf course is carved out of an old rubber estate.

**Pasar Malam**

These are local night markets set up by petty traders and are held in various localities.
