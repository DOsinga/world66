---
title: "Shopping in Kuala Kubu Baru"
type: section
---

If shopping is your pleasure, then there are plenty of places to find **Kuala Kubu Baru shopping** that will blow your mind. Not only can you find things like mall shops and outlet stores, but there are many places you can find local delights that you can only find within the city of Kuala Kubu Baru. Everything you could ever need during your trip can be found within the town, which by itself is actually quite small. You could easily tour the entire city within about 40 minutes or so. The city itself holds a certain rustic appeal that many find simply irresistible. Many people fall in love with it's old fashioned and still unaltered shop houses. If you happen to take the Kuala Kubu Baru Shuttle Route, then one of the first things you will see is the main shopping center that is located within Lot G.

**Selayang Mall**

This mall offers a shopping experience unlike any other found in any other mall. Not only are there many different varieties of stores that sell things like fashion, shoes, electronics, handbags and household items but there are also places for recreation and fun. Some of these places include a 24-lane bowling alley, shuttle games on an impressively big and clean 24-court badminton hall which is located on the rooftop level of the mall, a cinema hall movie center and a Fun Centre with many other fun things to enjoy and occupy some time. There are also many special offers and certain time only promotions held within this mall.

**Bukit Baru Area**

Within the Bukit Baru area, also know to the locals as New Hill, you will find both the Jusco Shopping Center as well as the Kota Mas Shopping Center. Both of these wonderful shopping centers are popular places to shop that include many different kinds of things that you can buy. They also happen to house many different popular restaurants that serve traditional as well as other cuisine. These are also great places to wander around in your free time, shopping for the things you need as well as all those little things you simply must have once you see them.

**Bangsar Shopping Centre**

This exciting place to shop has over 200 fantastic shopping outlets within it's walls that offer everything you could possibly need from the best brand names, fashion and accessories to food, toys, music and a magical environment in which to enjoy all of these things in.
