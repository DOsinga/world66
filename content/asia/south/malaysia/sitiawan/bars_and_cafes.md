title: "Bars and Cafes in Sitiawan"
type: section
---

There are many great aspects to **Sitiawan; bars and cafes** are some of the most favored things to explore in this city. Below are some of the establishments that create tapestry of the leisurely culture of Malaysia.

#### Son Hon Siong

This restaurant has a great bar where tourists can experience the best of local culture. Sun Hon Siong has been established for over 50 years and is known for its traditional drinks. Located at Main Road, Ayer tawar, this Mom and Pop shop allows you to eat for under $10 US dollars.

#### Hooha Caf&eacute;

Sitiwan bars and cafes are varied in this part of town. One of the most revered restaurants to dine at is Hooha Caf&eacute;. At Hooha Caf&eacute; you can experience cuisines and take a trip across the world. Western cuisine as well as seafood and Chinese cuisine are some of the cultures represented here. It is at Hooha Caf&eacute; that you can try their award-winning beef tenderloin that is imported from New Zealand. Travelers can experience this fine dining for under $40 US dollars per person.

#### Mood Ladder Bar and Bistro

Sitiawan pubs and karaoke bars are also a great way to go when looking for nightlife. People can be seen and heard drinking and singing their favorite tunes at such places, where the night gets better after midnight. There is the Moon Ladder Bar and Bistro, which has a private and quiet outdoor ambience. Located at 30 Labrador Villa Road, this fine dining can be experienced for under $45 US per person.
