There are many interesting places that can be seen in **Sitiawan Day Trips**. Below are a few ideas for you to consider.

#### Berjaya Time Square

Berjaya Time Square, located 3 hours south of Sitiawan in Kuala Lumpur, is the fifth largest building in the world. Berjaya Time Square offers great shopping, a large indoor theme park, a bowling alley, and a movie theater. Prices vary depending on which activities you partake in. The Bejaya Time Square adds a cosmopolitan air to any of the different Sitiawan day trips.

#### Aquaria KLCC

Another popular attraction in Kuala Lumpur is the Aquaria KLCC. This beautiful aquarium offers a walk-through tunnel that surrounds you with swimming sharks, a &ldquo;touch the sharks&rdquo; pool, and a &ldquo;swim with the fish&rdquo; tour, which consists of swimming with sharks, sand tigers, sting rays, turtles, and more.The general aquarium price, not including the &ldquo;swim with the fish&rdquo; tour, will cost you around RM100, or 30 USD.

#### Sunway Lagoon

The complex Sunway Lagoon theme park is located 3 hours South East of Sitiawan, in Petling Jaya, Malaysia. This complex theme park is made up of five themed sections: Xtreme Park, Amusement Park, Water Park, Wildlife Park, and Scream Park. A package allowing you to have access to all of the parks will cost you RM 100, or $30 USD. Xtreme Park consists of paint balling, rock climbing, and thrill rides; Amusement Park offers a carnival-style park, Water Park has water slides and wave pools; the Wildlife Park section is a large zoo; and the Scream Park hosts a three-dimensional horrow theater and haunted mazes.
