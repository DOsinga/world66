This **Sitiawan practical information **guide is very useful for learning what to expect if you are visiting Sitiawan. Sitiawan is a region located in Perak, Malaysia. Other nationalities which include Dutch and Irish, no visa is required for them to enter Sitiawan. The allowed time to stay in Sitiawan without a visa is normally for one month so if you are going to stay in Sitiawan beyond a month, it is important to apply for a visa.

**Entry Tips In Sitiawan**

Not all people can enter Sitiawan. In fact, there are a lot of exceptions on people with special conditions. For instance, women who are at least 6 months pregnant are denied entry to Sitiawan. Visitors also need to show proof that they have enough funds to allow them to return to their countries of origin. It is important that you take note of these tips in order to avoid any problems with their government.
