---
title: "Shopping in Binan Laguna"
type: section
---

**Binan Laguna shopping** is varied, as you can find handicraft stores as well as larger shopping malls. Where you go will depend on what you want to buy and what your spending limit is. Many times, the country is the best place for those true handicraft goods. However, the larger department stores also carry more commercial versions of many of these traditional objects as well.

#### The Pavilion Mall

This is the most modern and only shopping mall in Binan. It is right off of the Old National Highway. This mall was designed as a strategic landmark, as the surrounding area is under development. The Mall has 2 buildings. The majority of the shops and retail stores are in one building, while food stores are in the 2nd building. There are more than 150 shops within the mall, which include retail stores, food chains, and apparel stores. There is a cinema in the food building and an area for events. The mall itself has an interesting shape, as it was designed to represent an arrow.

#### The Resorts

There are many resorts in Binan, and they all will have their own boutiques. If you are looking for high-end goods, then your best chance of finding them are at the resorts. You do not have to be staying at the resort to visit the boutiques. If you have an opportunity to visit the many different resorts, you should definitely browse through the boutiques. Just make sure your wallet can take the hit if you plan on making a purchase.

#### Local Market

If you are looking for handmade items, then stop in at the weekly market. This is the place where the locals set up shop with their goods. You can find beautiful handmade crafts, local produce, and much more. This is also a great way to see some of the local culture, as everything goes on at the markets. Some of the items you can expect to find include lamps, native bags, wooden instruments, longganisa or native sausages, dried fish, milk products, coffee, dried mangos, pastries, fruit candies, rice cakes, and much more.

If you do not find what you are looking for in Binan City then you are just a short trip to Manila. This is the largest city in the Phillipines and you will be able to find exactly what you are looking for.
