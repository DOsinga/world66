---
title: "Top 5 Must Do's in Butuan"
type: section
---

Here are some of the top **Butuan must do&rsquo;s** while visiting this popular tourist hot spot in the island of Mindanao. Here are a few suggestions to help you enjoy your stay here in Butuan.

**Mount Mayapay**

Take a trip to Mount Mayapay which is one of the highest peaks in Butuan City and the most amazing natural landmark in the whole CARAGA Region. Mount Mayapay is located in the southwestern district of Butuan, near the Agusan Valley. This 700-meter tall mountain is the most-favored destination for mountain climbers and adventurers. You will truly enjoy vast greenery, rich flora and fauna and diverse wildlife of the mountain.

**Butuan National Museum**

Visit the Butuan National Museum to see the Balangay and the Golden Tara. Both these artifacts were unearthed in the historic Agusan River and date back to the fourth century. The Balangay is an ancient marine vessel used in the Philippines, while the Golden Tara is a four-pound figurine of a sitting woman made of 21k gold. Visiting the museum is a must while you are in Butuan.

**Agusan River**

Agusan River is another natural tourist destination you shouldn&rsquo;t miss. This river is the longest and largest of its kind in the entire Mindanao Island. The river is also the third longest river in the Philippines. Take a boat ride on the river and you will be able to enjoy the beautiful flora and fauna of the region. The river is also a wildlife sanctuary. If you happen to visit Butuan during the last Sunday of July and the first week of August, you can experience the famous Abayan Festival.

**Diving in Butuan**

If you love the water you will surely enjoy scuba diving at Butuan&rsquo;s wonderful diving sites. Butuan is also known for its beautiful and diverse marine life and coral reefs. There are several diving sites around Butuan such as Carmen, Vinapor, Poblacion, Jabonga and Tagcatong. So get your diving gear ready and go take a dip and enjoy yourself.

**Beach Hopping**

Butuan is also known for its pristine and exotic beaches. One of the things you must do while in Butuan is experience these beautiful beaches. All of these beaches here offer serene daytime atmosphere and vibrant nightlife. There are several resorts and cottages for rent at the beaches. Take one beach at a time to be able to completely enjoy the unique beach experience in Butuan.
