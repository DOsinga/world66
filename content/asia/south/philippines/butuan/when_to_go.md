**Butuan When to Go **is the very first thing in mind you should ask to yourself before embarking for a fun-filled trip. Butuan is in Mindanao part of the Philippines.
**
Witness Abayan Festival in the Month of July**

July is the best time to visit Butuan. On the last Sunday of the month, the people of Butuan celebrate the Abayan Festival, which is among the top reasons to visit the place. This festival is done to honor St. Anne, the patron saint of the Agusan River. St. Anne is believed to be the protector of the local residents who cross the river. There is a fluvial procession that takes place during this celebration. a fluvial procession is a parade in the river where the figure of the saint is placed in a well-decorated big boat and it is surrounded by small boats. All these boats and bancas will sail together in the river up to their destination. The Abayan Festival is known in the country for its colorful boat race.

**Extraordinary January**

January is also a good season for you to visit Butuan. In this month, the people in the city celebrate the Kahimunan Festival. This festival is the time when the Butuan people spend their thanksgiving celebration to their patron saint, Sto. Nino. They show reverence to the patron for giving them bountiful harvests and blessings for the entire year. During the Kahimunan Festival, there are performances of exotic dances and activities coming from the well-dressed participants all over Butuan and neighboring provinces.
