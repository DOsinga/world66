title: "Getting Around in Camiguin Island"
type: section
---

Camiguin Island is known for its splendid beaches and wonderful nature. Many tourists come to the island because of the wonders that it can offer to the visitors. Not only it has a beautiful place to visit, but the people there as well are nice and hospitable. Delicious and exotic foods are available in the island to give you enough energy as you explore its wonders.

Certain **Camiguin**** Island**** transportation** forms are available for you so that you can see the out of the ordinary splendor of this place. Though there is only a limited mode of transportation in the island, you can be assured that this will really take you to your destination list.

**Multi-cab**

Multi-cab is a small means of transportation that will take you around the island. It can carry more than ten passengers. You can hire this transportation for a day or two so that you can enjoy your tour to the fullest. There is an available multi-cab for hire that covers the service of fetching you at the airport, taking you to the place where you are going to stay and touring you around the place. The fee for this multi-cab is 2000 pesos, and that includes the wait of the driver as you soak your body in the water and enjoying some more adventures.

**Tricycle**

Tricycle is known in the place as Motorela. These motorelas are used for eastbound and westbound routes. Be reminded that the eastbound route motorela is not allowed to take the westbound route and vice versa. Before taking the motorela, you should know where you are heading to so you will not get lost. The minimum fare for this is six pesos. You can also rent this for a day tour. It will cost you 1000 to 1800 pesos to rent this transportation.
