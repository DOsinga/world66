---
title: "Shopping in Cebu"
type: section
---

**Cebu Shopping** presents a wide range of options, catered to the taste of every visitor. Sandwiched between Mandaue and the Mactan Island, Cebu City is a beautiful island destination. The Cebu shopping experience is essentially about exploring the best and the biggest of globally-themed malls and buying highly-discounted, branded merchandise. Some of the most recommended Cebu shopping sites are listed below:

**SM City
**

The SM City chain of malls has been created by one of Philippines&rsquo; biggest retailers—the SM Group. The SM City Mall is a typical super-mall, at par with the biggest of European or American shopping malls. Visitors can find nearly every known international brand across this massive stretch of retail space. Within the mall, there is a small space dedicated to discounted spa services. Visitors can take a break from their shopping sessions and seek a cheap massage here. Visitors can hitch free rides on the shuttle service that is run by the mall authorities.

**Ayala Center**

Another reputed mall is the Ayala Center. However, this is a themed mall with an emphasis on selling home-entertainment and home-d&eacute;cor items. The Ayala Center too runs its shuttle service for a token charge. Ayala is recommended for the more casual shoppers. The numerous boutique-type shops here aren&rsquo;t much about impressing the customers with upmarket ambiences. Visitors at the Ayala can dine at the Terraces restaurant. Another recommended option is shopping at the Marina Mall near Mactan.

**Unique Cebu Shopping Options**

From the outside, the Banilad looks like just another mall, but it has something unique-to-offer. For starters, the mall opens very late in the day and most visitors head here during the evening time. Somehow this mall has become a favorite place for some late-night lounging and buying souvenirs. Visitors at the Banilad can feast at the famous, Crossroads Arcade. Another unique shop in Cebu is the Alegre Guitar Factory. This shop has been able to get great customer reviews over the past decade. The shop repairs, manufactures and sells local musical instruments like the mandolins and ukulele. More than the retail aspect, the Alegre emphasizes upon educating visitors about the musical heritage of the Philippines people.

**Discounted Cebu Shopping**

Located in the southern part of Cebu, Minglanilla is a favored shopping destination among the island&rsquo;s natives. It is just about 15 km from the main part of the Cebu City. Visitors can look forth to getting huge discounts on branded goods here, including the second&rsquo;s sales that are very common over the weekends. Visitors headed to Minglanilla should seek directions for the markets of Bohol Strait or markets near the city of Toledo.
