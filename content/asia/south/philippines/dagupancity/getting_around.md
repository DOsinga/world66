---
title: "Getting Around in Dagupan City"
type: section
---

**Dagupan City transportation** on the ground consists primarily of tricycles and pedicabs. Both are reliable, but the tricycle is a faster option. Both can carry you wherever you need to go in Daugpan.

#### **Tricycle**

Tricycle is a good vehicle option for you to reach your destination. This transportation is a motorcycle with a sidecar. At most, 5 passengers can fit in the tricycle. If you are going to ride in a tricycle to a certain destination within the city, the fare is not more than 10 pesos. However, if you are going to hire the tricycle, it has a different fare rule. You are going to pay a fixed fare set by the driver. The fare is based on the distance of the travel. Logically, the further the distance, the higher the fare. There are a lot of tricycles in the city, so you do not need to worry if you do not like the higher bid of one driver. You can scout for another driver to give you a lower fare.

#### **Pedicab**

Pedicab is the cheapest transportation in Dagupan to take you anywhere in the city. Pedicab is a bicycle with a sidecar. Only 2 to 3 passengers can fit in the pedicab, and each person pays for at least 5 pesos. If you are in a hurry, choose another kind of transportation. This is quite slow because it takes manual pedaling.
