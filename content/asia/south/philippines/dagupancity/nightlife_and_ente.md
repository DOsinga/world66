---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment in Dagupan City"
type: section
---

You might want to check out the **Dagupan City nightlife **venues while you are on a vacation or a business visit. The city is a small, but vibrant town located 200 km north of Manila. Being the acknowledged financial, trading and information technology center in the northern Philippines, you can expect Dagupan City to be lively, even at night. There are activities for the young and old alike.

**Videoke Bars **

Belt out some tunes with your friends or family in the KTV rooms of the Star Plaza Hotel located in the busiest street of the city, AB Fernandez Street. The place has an extensive and updated lists of songs that make socializing with friends a fun and memorable experience.

**Bangusan Street Party**

If you happen to be in Dagupan City around the end of April, then you can join biggest street part in the country–the annual Bangusan Street Party, which marks the end of the two-week Bangus (milkfish) Festival. Organizers close the Jose de Venecia Expressway Extension to vehicular traffic to give way for a huge crowd–numbering up to 700,000 people. You could set up a night picnic using grills and fish sold at the party, and listen to the various bands that play mostly rock music. Or, you could dance with the younger crowds who are used to livelier activities.

**Music Warehouse**

People in this city love dancing. The most popular dance club, Music Warehouse, is open till the wee hours of the morning. Most of these places are for adults only, as some dancing could involve showing more skin than usual. If you want to drink alcohol while dancing, these are the just the right venues for you.

**City Plaza**

This wide a tree-lined area is ideal for family groups. It offers a calesa (mini-horse-carriage) ride, where tourists can have an overview of the happenings in the city at night. The 3 km stretch of the ride–from the plaza to the downtown area and back–costs about $1 per head.

**Ragtime**

The city offers bars and restaurants, which offer live music ranging from Jazz to rock. Ragtime, located at the corner of Perez and MH del Pilar Streets, offers nightly live jazz and rhythm and blues music. This is one of the popular places in the city and is a great place to enjoy the night.
