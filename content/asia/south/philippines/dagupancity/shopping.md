title: "Shopping in Dagupan City"
type: section
---

There are a variety of **Dagupan City shopping** hotspots to choose from depending on your preferences. This city is located about 200 km north of Manila and is considered the fish trading capital in the northern Philippines. Its access to Lingayen Gulf, and vicinity to other provinces, makes it accessible via land and sea. Dagupan City is also the home of the famous Bunoan Bangus, said to be the tastiest variety of milkfish.

**CSI City Mall**

CSI owns two malls in Dagupan City. One is the CSI City Mall in Lucao, and the other is CSI Market Square in the downtown area. The City Mall is the biggest mall and has many specialty shops and 2 cinemas, but its main attraction is the hypermarket, where you can buy anything from clothes to toys and gift items, as well as grocery items. As with the other malls, prices are fixed so haggling will be useless.

**Nepo Mall**

The Nepo Mall is a 3-story building and is the newest of the 3 malls. It is built on a 3-hectare prime lot at the city center. The Nepo mall tries to offer a complete package to consumers, and thus it houses a supermarket, an appliance center, 4 cinemas, various restaurants, a cake shop, a salon, boutiques and specialty shops. Parking spaces are available.

**Magic Centerpoint**

Magic Centerpoint is near the major city landmarks like St. John's Cathedral, public market, fire station, city hall, city plaza and the city museum. It houses retail outlets that offer products such as shoes, ready-to-wear items, appliances, groceries and gift items. This is a fine place to do some shopping for the entire family.

**Tinday Labi**

Tinday Labi is a type of flea market that operates only during Christmas season. This night market set up along AB Fernandez Street opens at 4:00 p.m. and closes past midnight every Friday and Saturday. The numerous stalls lined up along the street sell all sorts of goods such as clothes, fruits, toys and gifts. Hungry shoppers could expect to find food and beverage stalls. Bargain hunters looking for unique and innovative gift items will definitely love this place. You&rsquo;re free to haggle anytime.

**Souvenir Center**

The souvenir shop is located at the City Hall Complex. It sells shirts, key chains, fans, necklaces and bracelets that bear the city&rsquo;s name and emblem. This is a nice place to pick up some souvenirs for your family and friends back home.
