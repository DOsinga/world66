---
title: "Shopping in Davao"
type: section
---

You might want to check out the **Davao shopping** scene while you are in this city. Davao City is the largest city in Mindanao and takes pride in not only its tourist spots such as the majestic mountain of Mt. Apo and the white beaches of Samal Island but also in being one of the cities wherein peace and order is the main priority of its government officials. Because of that, not only has tourism progressed but also the city's business and development. This can be evident in many parts of the city where shopping has become a great treat for many locals and visitors for its unique high quality products and competitive prices.

**Uyanguren Shopping District**

Situated in China Town, there are many shops mostly owned by Filipino-Chinese traders that sell fashionable clothes, accessories, baking and cooking items and just about anything you can imagine. They are sold cheaply compared to the items sold at the shopping malls and you can even haggle for a price. You can get wholesale items for a good discounted price.

**Chimes Shopping Mall**

The luxurious shopping mall is the place to go if you have money to spend. The mall specializes in international branded items that range from clothes, shoes and accessories to children's items and house needs. However, the mall closes early at around 8:00 p.m. There are several restaurants just outside the mall where you can sit down and enjoy a good meal after your shopping spree.

**Aldevinco Shopping Center**

If you are looking for the perfect souvenirs to take back to your loved ones, you can be sure to find them at Aldevinco Shopping Center. The shopping center specializes on ethnic dresses and accessories. The ethnic dresses boast of colorful and unique designs that will make you look Asian or ethnic all over.

**SM Shopping Mall**

The country's premiere shopping mall is one of the popular places to shop. The SM Davao Shopping Mall is ideally located for residents in the nearby subdivisions. It has free Wi-Fi connection and has local and international shops like People are People and Lacoste where you will be able to find quality products. There are several restaurants on the ground floor where you can sit back and enjoy a quick bite or a good meal.

**Gaisano Mall**

Gaisano Mall has been around for many years already and is a popular shopping center for Dabawenyos. Its grocery department also offers imported items apart from the regular locally produced items. You might want to check the 3rd floor, as there are several cell phone shops that offer new phones and also exchanges.
