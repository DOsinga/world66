**Wonderful Masbate Day Trips **

You might be interested in taking one of the interesting **Masbate day trips** to any of the beautiful islands in the Visayas, Philippines. Masbate City, the capital of Masbate, is a quaint city where the Visayan culture is reflected in its stores, food and way of life. A good day trip when in Masbate is a trip to Boracay Beach, a world-renowned beach.

#### **Boracay Beach**

From Masbate, you can travel through land and then sea to reach the island of Boracay. The fine, white sandy beaches of Boracay and the crystal clear waters are good places to swim, windsurf, kiteboard, sunbath, snorkel and scuba dive. It is a peaceful place where you can de stress and relax. The Asian windsurfing competition has been held in this small, beautiful, tropical Island. You can even play golf in one of its golf course.

#### **Going There**

From Masbate, you can travel by air and sea to Boracay. If you have a private plane, traveling directly to the island is recommended because it will only take about an hour at the most. It is just an island hop from Masbate. If you travel by land, it would take you longer especially during tourists&rsquo; peak season. But once there, you can enjoy the whole day romping in the wonderful beach and have fun trips with. The whole trip can cost you anywhere from $80 to $400 depending on whether you would rent a small Cessna plane or not for your day&rsquo;s trip.
