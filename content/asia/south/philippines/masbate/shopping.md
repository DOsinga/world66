---

Masbate is a province in the Philippines, and there are some great **Masbate shopping** spots to be found here. Here are some** **places, featuring some of the provinces best known and best loved items.

**Jewelry Shopping
**

Masbate has been termed a &ldquo;first class province&rdquo; due to its richness in minerals and mines, resulting it in being one of the largest providers of ores to the rest of the cities and provinces in the Philippines. The sheer volume of these natural resources has allowed Masbate to produce gold and silver jewelry cheaply, and the people of the province have become very skilled at creating jewelry pieces from the wonderful raw materials that are native to their land. Walk along the city streets and be amazed at the number of little jewelry shops that can be found within the city itself. Stepping into one of them, the traditional designs can be seen – anything from indigenous designs and art, to Catholic crucifixes, all in good quality gold or silver. The Victoria Mall at the pier of Masbate is the best place to source for these offers and designs.

**Handicrafts
**

Masbate is also the province known for its artisans and carpenters, and the handicrafts here are good souvenirs to be bought home as a keepsake. Those looking for something larger and more prominent can explore the possibility of buying some of their furniture pieces and shipping it home – many of the larger shops here provide the service of transporting pieces back to your hotel where arrangements can be made for shipping. Cane, teak and rattan are familiar materials here in the furniture, as well as in many of the household items in Masbate. Those looking for something to fit in your suitcase can find little woven handicrafts in the form of key rings, bangles and fans to be brought home as momentos or gifts. These items are generally available at most of the hot tourist spots, including Catandayagan Falls and the Kalanay limestone hills.

**Foodstuffs**

Tourists can walk along the markets to find very fresh produce that is a signature of the Philippines and most of its provinces. Those looking to buy a little taste of the Philippines home can purchase little cookies that come in sealed packets. For those with adventurous taste buds, have a taste of the salted fish or the liver sauce that comes in bottles that line the shelves of the dried goods stores. Feeling hungry? Be sure to pick up some of the fresh produce, and have the shops found behind the markets to cook them into authentic, delicious Filipino dishes that cannot be found anywhere else in the world. Markets are scattered throughout Masbate city, and can be conveniently found from your hotel.
