This **Masbate travel guide **will surely guide you while you are staying in Masbate. Located in the Philippines, Masbate is an island province in the Bicol region. The island is blessed with a lot of scenic rural views. Thus, if you are in Masbate, be sure to include these things in your travel guide.

**Accessibility **

The island is very accessible, as you can get there from the mainland by riding the ferry boat from the port of Pilar. Once you get to the island, you can ride motorcycles for hire or the local jeepneys to get around the island. Travelers can finish traversing the coastal road of Masbate within just a day, thus it might be advantageous if you own or rent a car for your Masbate travels.

**Attractions for Nature Lovers**

Most attractions in Masbate involve the natural beaches and its pristine islands. Thus, Masbate is perfect for people who love nature. While you are there, make sure to stop by at Matangtubig Spring located in the town of Montreal or look at the entire view of Masbate on the Cagpating Island. The island also holds its annual Rodeo Masbateno during the month of May, which is a competition of local cowboys in the Philippines.

**Food
**

Food in Masbate is unlike any you have ever tasted in other parts of the Philippines. Food in the Bicol region usually involves chili peppers and coconut milk. You can sample local cuisines such as laing and other foods for as low as $10. Dining in Masbate is very cheap as the island is abundant with farm products. You can also see plenty of restaurants in the island, so finding food are not a problem. Just do not forget to drink lots of water since food in the Bicol Region is notorious for its spicy flavor. If you are not up for this gastronomic adventure, you can always dine at Jollibee, which is the local version of McDonald&rsquo;s in the Philippines.

**Cheap Accommodations**

Masbate is a perfect place for travelers who are in a tight budget. In fact, accommodations in hotels can cost as low as $20 and is inclusive of air conditioned rooms, hot and cold showers, as well as cable TV. The best place to stay in Masbate is the Sea Men's Hotel located in Danao Street in the heart of Masbate City.
