---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment in San Pablo City"
type: section
---

The **San Pablo City nightlife** and Entertainment choices are varied. People can visit cafes and even music bars that will entertain them during their stay in this city.

**Dub City Cafe**

Dub City Cafe is an alternative venue in San Pablo City. There is a variety of music played here including: vintage 70's rock, classic jazz, 80's punk and new wave. It is open from 5 P.M. onward into the night. This town house is painted with bright colors and decorated in album covers. This nightlife hot spot includes an alcohol bar and serves coffee, food and souvenirs as well. This cafe adds a great musical delight to San Pablo City nightlife and entertaiment.

Dub City Cafe is a great place to socialize and have a good time without the formality of a usual restaurant. It is located on Dub City Cafe 39S Mabini St. San Pablo City, Philippines 4000 and costs about $15 USD PAX

**La Pizzeria Total Gas Station**

La Pizzeria Total Gas Station is a popular nightlife hangout that is located at a 24 hour gas station. It is has native style decor, including wooden chairs and tables surrounded by palm plants and Bermuda grasses. Not only is this a great place to stop and get authentic Italian food served all night long, which is very hard to find in the Philippines, but it is an affordable place to fill up on gas, and it also includes free WIFI. This is perfect for travelers with laptops who desire to keep in touch with their family, friends or even business partners.

The Italian menu includes pizza, pasta, and lasagna. A thin crust, medium size, six slice pizza will run a guest about $5.50 USD. Ice cold beer is $.55 USD. Smoking is allowed here. This popular local nightlife spot is located on Barangay San Rafael, San Pablo City, Laguna Province, 4000, PH.

**Trio**

Trio is a popular nighttime hangout, which has a sports bar decor feel to it. It is the home of the infamous Trong vodka mix. Trio is known for its delicious yet afordable food and beer. The house specialties are Trio's Chicken, Sisig and Crispy Pasta. Trio is a great place to have a party or just stop by for dinner. Only cash is accepted at this location, and there is street parking available.

Their hours are from 5:00 p.m. until midnight Tuesday through Thursday, and 5:00 p.m. to 1:00 a.m. Friday through Sunday. They are closed on Mondays. Trio is located on M. Brion St. San Pablo City, PH and costs about $15 USD PAX per person.
