---
title: "Eating Out in Tacloban City"
type: section
---

**Tacloban City restaurants** are full of flavor. They offer sweet food and other delicacies which will delight any traveler lucky enough to travel to this city in the Philipines.

**Zaibatsu Cafe Bar**

Zaibatsu Cafe Bar is a small bistro and one of the Tacloban City restaurants that serve sweets, coffee, tea, cocktails, and smoothies. It has a coffee shop atmosphere, with light music playing while you dine, study, or do both. The Cafe is well spaced out and allows guests to have a sense of privacy. WIFI access is available here for travelers with laptops. It is located on #29 Senator Enage Street, Tacloban City. Prices are under $10 USD PAX.

**'Ayo Cafe**

'Ayo Cafe is a small restaurant that serves international and Japanese cuisine, including sandwiches, pastas, salads, and entrees. Dessert is also available here, including sweets, such as mousse pies and cheesecake.

'Ayo Cafe uses local produce and spices to create an authentic taste. They serve orange ribs and a tasty Spanish-style bangus. Vegetarian diets can be accommodated here.'Ayo Cafe's address is JCO Number 5 on Arcade Apitong Road Tacloban City. Dishes start at $5 USD PAX.

**Calle Zaragosa**

Calle Zaragosa is know by Calle Z by the locals. This a favorite nighttime hangout. This restaurant includes both local Filipino and international cuisine. If you want to dine outside, just tell your host that you want to dine al fresco.

Calle Z is known for its grilled dishes and laid back atmosphere. Travelers can eat here for $5 USD PAX. The restaurant is located at 39 Independencia Street in Tacloban City. This is one of the most visited Tacloban City Restaurants.
