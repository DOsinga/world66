---
title: "Getting Around in Tacloban City"
type: section
---

Tacloban City is the home of Pintados Festival. It is the capital of Leyte and is located in the northeastern part of the Philippines. To get around the city, certain **Tacloban**** City**** transportation** is available. There is a variety of transportation that is offered for use.

**Jeepney**

Jeepney is the national transportation of the Philippines. It is the main mode of transportation in the country specifically in Tacloban City. It will cost you only eight pesos to get around the city. However at first, you will encounter some problems in riding a jeepney. Because you are unfamiliar with the places and its names, you have the chance to get lost. To avoid this dilemma, do not be shy to tell the local passenger, the driver or the fare collector to stop the jeepney on the place where you are heading. The local residents are usually nice and friendly. They are always pleasure to help the tourists who come to visit their place.

**Tricycle**

Tricycle is a mode of transportation in Tacloban that has a minimum fare of five pesos if you are going to transfer from a place to another place within the city. However, you are going to pay a fee of 100 pesos if you are going to use the tricycle to tour around the Tacloban City. There are tricycle drivers who are willing to drive you around so that you will see the best places in the city. Usually, these drivers will take you to five or six wonderful sites in this place.
