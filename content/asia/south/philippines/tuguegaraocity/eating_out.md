**Tuguegarao City restaurants** serve excellent local and national dishes. Two restaurants which highlight local specialties are Gretchen's and Kainang Pinoy.

#### **Gretchen's**

Gretchen&rsquo;s is a restaurant serving tasty food at the corner of Magallanes Street in Tuguegarao. The main specialty of the restaurant is the Pancit Batil Patung, a local noodle dish. This pancit is what they use in the Pancit Eating Contest during the Pav-vurulun festival. The ingredients of the dish include vegetables, meat and egg that are placed on top of the egg noodles. Gretchen&rsquo;s also serves the Gretchen&rsquo;s Special Pancit, which is similar to Pancit Batil Patung. The dish comes together with an egg soup for only 60 to 70 pesos.

**Kainang Pinoy
**

Kainang Pinoy is a restaurant in Tuguegarao City that serves a variety of local and national dishes. It is located at Brgy. Leonarda. Dining at the Kainang Pinoy restaurant is the best gift you can give yourself after a tiring trip. If you want to try Filipino dishes, you can order for a palatable crispy pata and delicious sizzling kangkong. Kainang Pinoy has the best crispy pata in the city. Of course to complete your Tuguegarao trip, you must taste their local cuisines. Some of the local food they serve are Dinengdeng, Halabas na Hipon, and Dinakdakan. You will have a good meal here with price ranges from 11 to 20 USD in 2010. It is expensive, but it is all worth it.
