#### Exploring the Shopping Hot Spots of Tuguegarao City

One of the things that tourists in Tuguegarao get to experience is the **Tuguegarao City shopping** scene, and it&rsquo;s often a highlight for most visitors to the city. Discover the best places to go shopping in Tuguegarao City.

**Shopping Around Tuguegarao City**

There are quite a few superstores and food markets in Tuguegarao City that you can explore. In the downtown area, you will find the Candice Megamart, which stocks the regular range of imported goods, including foods and household products. If you are looking for something a little more adventurous, you can explore the malls in the center of town, where the main shopping buzz is located. You can visit places such as Brickstone Mall in Del Rossario Street or you can head down to Rizali Street and visit Pesso Realle Mall, opposite the hospital, which has many different shops that you can browse through. Downtown, in the central district, you will also find most Western conveniences. If you are not willing to walk the streets or drive around, you can always catch a local taxi to get to all the main shopping areas. Most of the streets are lined with interesting stalls that have everything, from cigarettes to sketch artists who can whip up a portrait of you in a matter of minutes.

#### Fashions of the Tuguegarao City Shopping Scene

Fashions are colorful and fun in Tuguegarao City, with a variety of unique clothing boutiques that sell hand crafted clothing, as well as clothes markets and more modern clothing stores in the malls. If you are planning on going to the central district, take the time to visit Tuguegarao Public Market, located just outside town on the Pan-Philippine highway, where you can get the most out of the cultures of the city and barter for unique clothing and accessories, including handmade necklaces, handbags and more. Aside from clothing, some other merchandise available also include those of home wares, including interior d&eacute;cor and household goods. There is no limit to the home d&eacute;cor, electronic and furniture stores that you can find in the central district or the markets, so you will always find some interesting items to take back home as gifts for family and friends.

The most happening and bustling part of the Tuguegarao City shopping scene is in the central district, and because of this, there are always lots of people. When shopping and spending money, it is advisable to take extra care of your personal belongings, as you would in any busy city centre, but the busy atmosphere creates a wonderfully vibrant setting for a great shopping experience.
