---
title: "Top 5 Must Do's in Tuguegarao City"
type: section
---

You can choose from a long list of **Tuguegarao City must do's**, from visiting historical churches to taking daytrips around the city. The capital of Cagayan, Tuguegarao is a haven for colonial buildings and churches, as well as several natural destinations that can cater to various types of travelers.

#### 1. Visit the Basilica Minore of Our Lady of Piat

This historical church houses the 365 year old Nuestra Se&ntilde;ora del Santissimo Rosario (Our Lady of the Rosary), which is said to give miraculous cures and grant wishes. Due to this, the church has become a famous pilgrimage site of the region where thousands of devotees flock every year.

Beside the church, you can visit the Basilica Museum which houses an extensive collection of religious items as well as the different vestments and accessories used for Our Lady of the Rosary.

#### 2. Explore Saints Peter and Paul Cathedral

This impressive church was built in 1700s by the Dominican friars and is one of the most visited churches in Tuguegarao. Said to be the biggest Spanish church built in the Philippines, Saints Peter and Paul Cathedral is also considered to be one of the most beautiful churches in the country.

The exterior or fa&ccedil;ade of the church is made from bricks, but the interior is quite simple. This famous church also has a picture at the Basilica in Rome.

#### 3. Walk along Buntun Bridge

Travel 2.5 kilometers from Tuguegarao City proper and visit Buntun Bridge, which is the second longest bridge in the country. Walk along the bridge and get an unobstructed view of the Cagayan River. This 1.098 kilometer bridge is also the main connection between the city and a couple of districts of Cagayan and Apayao Provinces.

#### 4. Explore the Iguig Calvary Hills

In this eleven hectare rolling hills, explore the larger-than-life size statues depicting the 14 Stations of the Cross. The concrete-made statues stand on the hillside overlooking the Cagayan River. The statues are scattered behind the church building on the hilltop, which is said to be a century old.

#### 5. Visit the Cagayan Museum and Historical Research Center

Situated at the Cagayan Capitol Compound is the Cagayan Museum and Historical Research Center. This museum features a massive collection of artifacts, antiques and artwork. The museum also showcases liturgical and ethnographic works of the province of Cagayan.

You can also find on display fossils of animals that used to roam the province, as well as heirloom pieces of the region.
