**Pattaya Budget Travel**

Traveling to most countries on a budget can prove difficult, but **Pattaya budget travel **offers so much choice for all ages and at prices that are sometimes unbelievably cheap. Thailand is a favorite destination for every type of tourist, from backpacker to holiday-maker.

#### **Value For Money**

For starters, there are many different price levels of accommodation. Standards vary, but everything is available from basic to comfortable. No matter what size of budget you are on, you will never go hungry or thirsty. Thai food is offered everywhere in Pattaya at reasonable prices and in ample and delicious portions. Cheap bars are also dotted around the city. The value for the money is guaranteed in Pattaya.

#### **Pack Your Spirit Of Adventure **

Maintaining a spirit of adventure will go a long way in Pattaya, as you choose from a seemingly never-ending list of things to do, and most will fit in with your budget plan. Budget-conscious travelers and adventurers can take advantage of reasonable and inexpensive entrance charges at most regular tourist attractions. Boat trips to nearby islands from Pattaya Beach are a little more costly but provide you with lasting memories of this tropical paradise.

Budget pleasers include old favorites like beach sports, fishing trips, submarine trips, elephant park visits and an all-time favorite, the Pattaya Crocodile Farm. Local tour operators can put together affordable and interesting trips around Pattaya, providing a local insight into many attractions you might otherwise not see. Do not forget to visit the markets for a cheap selection of quality clothing, delicious food items, souvenirs and knick knacks. Most temples are free to enter on your own, but donations are always welcome. If the beach is not for you, there is plenty to see and do in the many parks in and around Pattaya, with the Nong Nuch Botanical Gardens being the most popular.

**A Note on All-Inclusive Tours
**

If you decide to take a boat trip to one of the islands, take a packed lunch or something to eat with you. Sometimes the organizers will offer an all-inclusive trip with lunch or dinner, but if you embark on a trip-only visit and have to fend for yourself when you want a bite to eat, you might find most island restaurant menus will break the bank.
