---
title: "Eating Out in Pattaya"
type: section
---

**Pattaya restaurants** found in Thailand are exotic and feature menus from all around the world. If you are interested in a nice experience at dinner time, you can choose from many different kinds of foods including Italian, Thai, Japanese, Romanian, Indian, American and even Russian. Many of the meals served in Pattaya restaurants are done so family style, meaning each dish is served on a big serving platter than is shared by the group at the table. You can also find affordable buffets and diners spread throughout.

**Sunday Markets**

On Sundays, you can find fresh fruits and vegetables to eat at the Sunday market or at the various stands set up all along the Pattaya beach road. These stands will cook your fish right there in front of you, proving just how fresh it is. You can even shop at the night market for fresh foods to eat the next morning for breakfast. When you are in the mood for a coffee break and a snack, consider visiting Boncafe where you can get fresh brewed mugs of steaming hot coffee specials, and delicate treats like cinnamon buns.

**Hotel Restaurants**

Fancier meals can be sought at the local hotels where most usually feature their own high-class restaurants. Some even offer more than one type of restaurant to choose from, making eating out convenient in just about every part of Pattaya. With the many parks and outdoor areas to relax in, grabbing a sandwich at the local grocery store and spending some time under a tree for lunch is also a great option.
