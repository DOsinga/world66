---
title: "Festivals in Pattaya"
type: section
---

Festivals are an inseparable component of the Thai society, and there are many fun **Pattaya festivals **offered. Pattaya – acknowledged as the &lsquo;Fun City&rsquo; – is a holiday haven that is situated on the Eastern Thailand&rsquo;s North Gulf Coast, about 150 kilometers south-east of the capital city of the country, Bangkok. Apart from boasting of stupendous beaches, incomparable tourist spots like the Million Years Stone Park and Pattaya Crocodile Farm, exceptional shopping facilities, and an exuberant nightlife, Pattaya also serves as a venue for a number of interesting festivals and events. Discussed below are the most prominent among the Pattaya festivals and events.

**Pattaya Music Festival **

This is perhaps the most momentous of all events in Pattaya. It takes place during the month of March, and is participated by both local and international musicians from countries like Australia, China, Hong Kong, Laos, Malaysia and Vietnam. No matter if your favorite music is Jazz, Pop, Hip Hop or Rock, this three-day long festival enthralls all music lovers!

**Wan Lai Festival **

Also known as Songkran Festival, Wan Lai Festival** **is celebrated in** **mid April every year, and it marks the beginning of the Thai New Year. The focal point of this event is the water pouring activities. ****

**Chonburi Province Annual Festival**

Chonburi Province Annual Festival has been organized here since 1932. It is conducted by the people of Chonburi Province in April to primarily preserve the incredible cultural heritage of the place, in addition to promoting and inculcating its importance to people of a young generation.

**Ancestral Spirits Ceremony Festival **

An annual festival that is organized in Sai Racha district from April 19th to 21st, Ancestral Spirits Ceremony Festival is a colorful festival that is accompanied by a number of amazing folk performances. Further, a sumptuous array of local cuisines can be tasted during this event.

Above mentioned are just few to mention among many of the festivals that are conducted in Pattaya, and the events celebrated here also include Pattaya Marathon organized in July and Chonburi Buffalo Race held during October.
