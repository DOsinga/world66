This **Pattaya - When to Go** guide will aid you in planning a trip to this Southeastern part of Thailand. Pattaya is situated in a tropical country, where wet and dry climates are divided into three seasons: warm and dry, hot and humid, and hot and rainy. Although it is mostly hot all throughout the year with an average peak temperature of 29 degrees Celsius, Pattaya is subject to monsoons annually.

**Warm and Dry
**

November to February is normally warm and dry, and is the most favorable time to visit Pattaya. You can experience very little rain and moderate temperatures ranging from 21 to 31 degrees Celsius. Though it is a bit warm, this is when you can really enjoy your stay and visit the different places here in Pattaya.

**Hot and Humid**

March to May is summer time in Pattaya, and the temperature is normally between 25 degrees Celsius and 33 degrees Celsius. You will need to be prepared for a sticky, hot weather if you visit during these months. Though the sea breeze helps in alleviating the heat, the humidity cannot be disregarded.

**Rainy Season**

June to October is the rainy season and experiences brief but heavy downpours, thus, this is the off- peak season. The rainy season is also the mosquito breeding season. If you are planning your trip during this period, it is best to come prepared. You may experience temperatures of 30 degrees Celsius during the day and 25 to 27 degrees Celsius during the night. The rainy season is also a good time to visit since the beaches and bars are not crowded and the hotel rates are lower.
