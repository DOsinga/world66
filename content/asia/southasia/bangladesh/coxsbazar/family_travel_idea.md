type: section

Bangladesh proves to be a precious pearl within Asia because of Cox’s Bazar. This town is a small port, which is famous for its long, sandy beaches and impressive shoreline. A panoramic view of Cox’s Bazar offers visitors a portrait-like scene of still waters and blue-colored skies as well as huge expanses of greenery and forests from a distance. The **Cox’s Bazar family travel** guide lists various ideas for families to experience an enjoyable vacation in this Asian subcontinent hotspot.

**Laboni Beach**

Being famous for its clear waters and blue skies, Cox’s Bazar offers kids and adults a wonderful place to rest, play and unwind. Considered the main beach in Cox’s Bazar, Laboni is closest to the town center. Kids can build sandcastles and swim under the sun. Adults, on the other hand, can relax and pamper themselves with some fresh drinks and a massage. Small shops and stalls are also present, offering tourists some fabulous gift ideas.

**Himchari**

Himchari is another famous spot. In fact, Himchari ranks as one of the most favorite tourist destinations near Cox’s Bazar. Known as a perfect picnic spot, this place boasts of picturesque waterfalls and lush greenery, making it an instant favorite among nature lovers.

**Dulhazra Safari Park**

Dulhazra Safari Park is the most appropriate place for travelers seeking a high dose of adventure and a glimpse of the region’s wildlife. Located along Chittagong-Cox’s Bazar Road, this sanctuary takes families through an exciting journey of viewing some of the most interesting creatures on the subcontinent. Spend some time with domesticated elephants and rhinos. See bears, Bengal tigers and lions along with several monkeys and crocodiles. The sight of the animals playing and interacting in their natural habitats is worth experiencing.

**St. Martin’s Island**

Situated at the northeast part of Bengal, St. Martin’s Island is one of the main attractions near Cox’s Bazar. In fact, it is the only coral island in Bangladesh where you can see turtles and dead corals. Moreover, day trips are scheduled regularly for both tourists and locals.

**Enani Beach**

About 35 km south of Cox’s Bazar is the popularly known Enani Beach. Famous for its white sand and shark-free waters, Enani Beach is ideal for family trips and getaways. Kids can swim in the safe waters and play. Bring a good book and find a perfect spot to relax on the shores of Enani. Take a nap while lying on its golden sand or hearing the calming sounds of its waves.

Cox’s Bazar is not an archetypical vacation destination but is nevertheless a clean and quiet summer beach retreat for families.
