Surfing The Nations Presents the Aloha Surf Classic Surfing Competition

This is a must see event. International surfing group Surfing The Nations hosts this surfing competition to see which surfer of the Bangladesh surf club is #1

Held in the middle of September you can catch a glimpse of real surf history and meet the first ever surfers from Bangladesh. Event is held at the Kalotoly Beach

It's a fun family day, with games, food, international media coverage and award ceremony to wrap up the day. if you want more details please e-mail surfingbangladesh@yahoo.com or go@surfingthenations.com www.surfingthenations.com
