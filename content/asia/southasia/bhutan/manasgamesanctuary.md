---
title: "Manas game sanctuary"
type: location
---

This fascinating jungle game reserve is not often visited because it’s hard to get to and requires a special visa. Moreover there is some fear about the militants of Assam who are fighting against India for Independent Assam.

 Most who do go there are interested in seeing the once-endangered golden langur (a monkey that is actually white during the summer) but there are also elephants, buffalos, tigers rhinos and a variety of birds. 65 mi/105 km southeast of Thimphu.
