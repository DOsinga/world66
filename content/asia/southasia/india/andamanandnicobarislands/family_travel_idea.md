One of the renowned places in India for underwater marine life and
beaches is the **Andaman and ****Nicobar Islands****. Family
Travel** is highly recommended here as the area is very friendly and
less crowded unlike other tourist destination in the country.

**Family Accommodations

**

There are many hotels in Port Blair city to choose from; however, while
traveling with family there are certain things you must always take
care of before selecting one. First, the hotel environment should be
family-friendly and second, family rooms should be available or the
rooms should be spacious enough to accommodate the whole family. This
will ensure that you are not bothered about the well-being of your
family members.

Hotel Sun Sea Resort at Middle Point, Port Blair, offers family-room
accommodations. Located just 3 km away from airport this hotel offers
facilities like telephones in all rooms, an elevator and multi-cuisine restaurant and bar. It is a nice place to stay with your family.

**Family Sightseeing Places **

Andaman and Nicobar islands
have many sightseeing places and fun-filled activities which everyone in
the family can enjoy.**
**

**Mahatma Gandhi National Park **

Located just 29 km from Port Blair. this park is a must visit on an
Andaman and Nicobar Islands tour. One can enjoy the rare beauty of the
underwater marine life and corals by snorkeling, scuba diving or glass
bottom boat rides.

**Water Sports Complex**

This is ideal place for water activities. Enjoy motor boat rides to
the Ross Island or enjoy in the calm seawater swimming pool. The complex
is open everyday from 2:00 p.m. to 5:00 p.m.

**Marina Park **

It is the children's amusement park which has several rides for both
children and adults. You can also see the botanical garden here which is
home to rare species of flora on the islands. The park is adjacent to
Water Sports Complex.

**Mini Zoo **

This is situated at Haddo and is home to numerous rare species of
birds and animals found in the islands.

**Gandhi Park **

This is another well-known tourist place in Port Blair. It has an
amusement park for children, water sports and a nature trail as well as the Deer
and Bird Park. It’s an ideal place to hang around with family in the
evening. The park also has a restaurant and garden when you can
rest if you are tired.
