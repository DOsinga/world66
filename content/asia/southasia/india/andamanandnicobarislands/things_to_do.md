**Andaman and Nicobar Islands Things to Do** has many activities
for both adventure lovers and nature lovers. Vacationing in the islands
is such a thrilling experience that you will end up asking for more time
to spend here.

**Things to Do for Adventure Lovers **

With small islands, exotic wild and the sea, Andaman and Nicobar
Islands is paradise for the adventure freak. Even if you are not one of
them you will still find an adrenaline rush in your body watching the
excitement of others.
**Scuba Diving
**
One of the most fascinating worlds of under-water marine life can be
enjoyed in the Andaman and Nicobar Islands. The sea water is crystal
clear and the area is one of the biggest abode of coral reef ecosystems in the
world which is yet to be ventured into by the humans. While scuba diving, you
will come across colorful fishes, a variety of sharks and even sunken
ships. Mahatma Gandhi Marine National Park is a well-known scuba
diving location here. Also, there are many private locations.

**
Snorkeling

**This is another very popular activity of most of the tourists.
Snorkeling gives you a wonderful opportunity to enjoy the under-water marine
life and some of the rarest variety of corals. Islands like North
Bay, Red Skin, Havelock and Jolly Bouy are some of the popular
destinations for snorkeling. Regular ferry services to these islands are
easily reached from Port Blair.
**

Water Sports
**
Those interested in water sports like water-skiing, windsurfing, para
sailing, speed boats and paddle boats can enjoy them at the Water Sports
Complex close to the renowned cellular jail in Port Blair. You can also
enjoy a boat ride to Ross Island from here.

**Things to Do for Nature Lover **
**Trekking
**
Those interested in trekking can enjoy the nature trail from Mt.
Harriet to Madhuban where one can come across rare flora and fauna.
Other trekking routes are also available.
**

Island Camping
**
This is an ideal activity for those who love to be close to nature
away from the maddening crowds. Island camping in Andaman and Nicobar
Islands offers a relaxing and rejuvenating atmosphere. These trips are
mostly organized by local tour operators and provide with modern
amenities like Swiss tents. This is a perfect activity for those who wish
to enjoy the sea, beaches and the natural beauty.
