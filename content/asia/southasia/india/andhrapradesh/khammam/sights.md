Khammam Fort was constructed in the year 950 AD. With the active
involvement of the Army Chiefs Velma Reddy and Laksham Reddy.
Subsequently, Golconda Sultans took over the regime from Kakatiya
Administration and strived hard for development of Khammam Fort and
other parts of the region.
