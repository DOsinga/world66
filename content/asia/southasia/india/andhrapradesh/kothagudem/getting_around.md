---
title: "Getting Around in Kothagudem"
type: section
---

City buses run by [APSRTC](/asia/southasia/india/andhrapradesh/kothagudem), Auto Rickshaws and Taxicabs are available for getting around.

Buses ply on all the main routes and are the cheapest mode of transportation.

Auto rickshaws can take you anywhere and are available almost everywhere and you can wave them to take you. You can ride alone or you can share with others. Sharing is possible only if you are travelling on the main routes.

Taxicabs can be booked and are available at the gate of the APSRTC bus stand.
