The best resource for sights, hotels, restaurants, bars, what to do and see

Dirang, a nice place in between Tawang and Bomdila, is the centre for KIWI production and YAK Research Centre. It also has abundant production of apple. The sheep breeding farm at Sangti is just a few kilometers away from Dirang. Also, Sangti is the place where you can enjoy the migratory birds from Siberia.
