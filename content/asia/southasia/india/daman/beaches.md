---
title: "Beaches"
type: section
---

There are two Beaches -The Jampore Beach and the Devka Beach. The Jampore Beach is rather secluded while the Devka beach has a children's park where there are swings and slides to keep the children busy. The beach is not much to write home about, just good enough to take long walks.
The Devka Beach is more popular as most of the hotels are situated here and so is the shopping promenade.
