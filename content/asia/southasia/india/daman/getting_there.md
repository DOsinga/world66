title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

**By Road**:Daman is about 15kms from Vapi.
Vapi is around 175km north of Mumbai. It lies on the Western Express Highway that connects Mumbai to Ahmedabad (NH8). Now with plans to convert this Highway into an Expressway, the roads are widened, multilaned and it sure is a smooth drive all the way.

**By Air**:From Vapi, Gujarat Airways has
three flights a week to Mumbai.

**By Rail**:The
closest railway station to Daman is **Vapi** at a distance of about 10kms.
You can take a shared taxi from the station for about Rs 15 per head to Daman, bus
(Rs 3), or taxi (Rs 75).

Vapi
is on the main railway line between Mumbai and Ahmedabad. There are
trains to Mumbai Central (3-4 hr, nine daily), Ahmedabad (7 hr, 5 expresses
daily), Vadodara (5 hr, 8 daily), and the overnight Saurashtra Janata
Exp #9017 goes to Rajkot.
