Kashmir tourism industry has seen tremendous growth from last couple of years. Kashmir valley which is also known as ‘Paradise on Earth’ is famous for its natural beauty. Valley is the most perfect tourist destination to enjoy your vocation with your family and friends. Tourist from across the globe visit valley frequently and enjoy great environment of the valley. Kashmir is all blessed with natural beauty of beautiful valleys, breathtaking views of Mountains, hill stations, adventurous & winter sports, gorgeous Lakes and many more.
Most of the people choose Kashmir Tour Packages to enjoy their different categories of tour packages including honeymoon packages, adventurous packages, family tour packages, leisure packages and so on. There are so many beautiful things to explore in the valley. Lakes and valleys in Kashmir are wonderful places to enjoy your voyage. The most famous Valley in Kashmir is Pahalgam, also known as Valley of Shepherds. Pahalgam is the awesome place to beat the heat. The temperature of Pahalgam Valley is very cold during summers as well. Many tourists visit the valley to beat the summer heat. The valley is located on the banks of river Leddar where you can enjoy river rafting as well.
Beautiful lakes, houseboat stay and Shikara ride are also the famous attractions of Kashmir Tours. You can explore Dal Lake which is located in the heart of the city. In Dal Lake one also gets to enjoy the most famous Shikara ride and stay in the houseboat. Dal Lake and Mughal gardens are major attraction of Srinagar city. Here in Srinagar- the summer capital of J&K, you can shop for different varieties of handicraft products. The famous handicrafts in the valley include of Paper- Mache, Pashmina shawls and many more.
