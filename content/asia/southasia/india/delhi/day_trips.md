**Badkhal **

Located on Delhi Mathura Road you can visit this nice picnic spot. You can do boating, fishing and other water sports. The places is 32 km from Delhi. 

**{Debradun}**

The capital of the Uttaranchal state is located in a beautiful valley and is known as a famous summer resort. 

**{Alwar}**

In 1775 A.D. is an interesting historic city. Alwar is sourrounded by hills, valleys, exotic wildlife and lakes. The city is famous for the City Palace Museum, forts, Vinay Vilas Palace and more interesting historic sights.
