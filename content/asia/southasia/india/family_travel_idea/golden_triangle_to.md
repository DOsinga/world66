Taj Mahal Tour is one of the most revered tours in the world, our Golden Triangle Taj Mahal Tours include two itineraries namely Agra Overnight Tour, Agra Same day tour. Those of who want to explore Taj in Agra can embark on this Taj Mahal tour. One will be thrilled to explore the marble symphony of Taj Mahal. This great monument of love attracts tourists from all over the world. It is more emotive than any other monuments in the world as it is made for love by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his beloved wife Mumtaj Mahal.

Three golden triangle cities-Delhi, Agra and Jaipur tell history of past and present. Delhi, the capital of India is the face of true India, flamboyant and extravagant in one side and starving in other side. The mughal monuments of Delhi are revered as major attractions due to mastery of Mughal art and the temples of past and present symbolize the Hindu beliefs. There are religious centers of Jain, Muslims and Sikhs in Delhi. Delhi, a secular city houses lots of entertainment zones, markets and eating joints. The Mughal monuments of Agra are Agra Fort, It-mad-Daula’s Tomb, and on the route to Agra and Jaipur are Tomb of Emperor Akbar at Sikandra and Fatehpur Sikri respectively.

Jaipur, the pink city of Rajasthan is home to monuments of Rajput rulers and various gardens, parks and markets of art and handicrafts. One will be mesmerized to see the pink beauty of this vibrant city. Traveling this city embarking on an India Golden Triangle Tours one can explore the colorful folk dance.
