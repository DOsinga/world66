### [SanFrancisco Restaurant](/asia/southasia/india/goa/anjuna/eatingout/sanfrancisco_resta)

SanFrancisco Restaurant on Anjuna beach is one whacky place to eat. It used to be called William's Chai Shop in the 70s. William was a jolly Goan and I was a pranky teenager. He used to let lots of travellers sleep in the small courtyard if they didn't have a place to stay~ and we left our backpacks and guitars there too!

Mary used to be a little kid back then with pigtails and she dished out endless cups of FREE TEA for me.

Now, this place is SanFrancisco Restaurant. Awesome international cuisine; some Goan specialities, good portions and very reasonable [more..](/asia/southasia/india/goa/anjuna/eatingout/sanfrancisco_resta)
