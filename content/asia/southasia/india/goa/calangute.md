---
title: "Calangute"
type: location
---

Calangute is the busiest beach resort. It has many small hotels and guesthouses. In the weekends it gets a bit too crowded so we may suggest you use the weekends to visit [Old Goa](/asia/southasia/india/goa/oldgoa) or daytrip to another nice place. The beaches are good: white sand, cocotrees, the works. North of Calangute you find Baga and [Anjuna](/asia/southasia/india/goa/anjuna).

Cows often roam around the beaches. The water is warm but not clear. Beware of drum sellers!
