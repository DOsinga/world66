Nr the Canara Bank, candolim A choice of continental and Indian. A restaurant with real good service, ask for willy the waiter and tell him that I sent you. He is always smiles and can help you with menu. Priced a bit higher than the others this place is a favourate for the steaks and the cauliflower sauce. Casa Sea-Shell, a second contributor), has been one of my choice restaurants in Candolim for many years. The atmoshere created by the soft music, the fountain and garden-like surroundings are relaxing and can lead you to staying there for a couple of hours or more. The staff are well trained, smartly smartly fitted out and can relate to customers of all nationalities.The restuarant is run by Sameer who goes out of his way to give all a cheery, warm welcome. Casa Sea-Shell, located at the junction of the main road and Panjim road, is a sister property of the Sea Shell Inn, located further along the main road towards the Beach Road junction. I highly recommend both restaurants for discerning guests who like to relax and enjoy good food and good service.
