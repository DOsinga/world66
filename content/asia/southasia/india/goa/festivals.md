The Carnival is held in Goa around Feb-March every year with a lot of song and dance and a parade that you just cannot miss..

### [Three Kings Feast](/asia/southasia/india/goa/festivals/three_kings_feast)

The Three Kings Feast is held at Cansaulim every year. There is a wonderful spread of Goan food in every house at Cansaulim on this day..
