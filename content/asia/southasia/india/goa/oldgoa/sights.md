**Holy Hill**

Holy Hill is full of chapels and churches. Visit the Chapel of Our Lady of the Rosary, the Royal Chapel of St. Anthony and the Convent of St Monica. 

**Basilica of Bom Jesus**

Great church, a world heritage site. 

**Archeological Musuem and Portrait Gallery**

Best Museum in town. Don't miss it. Entry is free. 

**Se Cathedral**

The largest church in Goa, built in 1562-1623. 

**Palace of the Inquisition**

16000 cases were heard here between 1561 and 1774. There are dungeons below the palace you can visit.
