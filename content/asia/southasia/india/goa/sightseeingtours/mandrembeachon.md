Highlights of the Tour    Pick up from Hotels by mini Coach and stop Vagator top.  Carry on drive through in roads along Chopdem River to Siolim Market.  Cross the ferry from Siolim across the Chopdem River onto Morjim.  The drive carries on to Terekhol Fort with another ferry crossing. The view from Terekhol Fort is just breathtaking.  We drive back to Mandrem, which is in between Morjim and Terekhol fort to your accommodation.  The rooms are simple Huts made of Bamboo mats with basic amenities. The toilet/bath is common to the 6 huts. The setting is overlooking the beach in the midst of a coconut grove.  After you are well settled into your rooms you can walk along the nearly deserted beach and wait for the spectacular sunset. You are given some time after which a local entertainment program will be staged for you. The buffet dinner is served  After you are well fed you can sit by the bonfire and let the guide tell you some stories under the beautiful sky until you retire to bed.  The following morning after breakfast we drive on to a ‘Choir Community’. This is a group of people all in their traditional way making rope out of the coconut. One of the villagers will be most obliged to show you what they are actually up to. Here the guests are welcome in the traditional Hindu Goan way and are taken around their simple house.  After a stop to buy some sweets the group is taken to a village primary school where they can distribute the candy and have the children sing a song in appreciation.  We return to the accommodation where the guests are just left on their own to relax in the sun until the lunch is served.  A short while after lunch the group will head back to their hotels.
