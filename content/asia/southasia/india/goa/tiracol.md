Tiracol is in the northernmost tip of Goa. The reason to come here is the fort -now a hotel- with its great views. If you can try to stay a night or two in the hotel.  The fort was built by the Maratha, but it was captured by the Portuguese in 1776 who built a church in the fort.

  Nearby are the unsploit beaches of Redi Beach in (Maharashtra).
