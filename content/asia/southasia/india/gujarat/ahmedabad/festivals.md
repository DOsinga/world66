Here are the festivals held in Ahmedabad.

### Navratri

For nine nights, Amdavadis enjoy traditional dances of Garba and Ras.
Superb folk music, often mixed with pop rhythems, and colorful ethnic
dresses combine to create an enchanting experience. Navratri is
celebrated during first nine days of the month Aso in the lunar
calender, which typically falls in the month of October.
