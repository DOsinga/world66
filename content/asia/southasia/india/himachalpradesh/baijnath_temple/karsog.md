The best resource for sights, hotels, restaurants, bars, what to do and see

Karsog is in Mandi District. It is about 110 Km. from Distt. Headquarters, it is a tehsil. It is a beautiful valley . One can reach here by bus from Shimla Via Tattapani or from Mandi Via Sundar Nagar or Via Chail Chowk.There is a Govt Degree College also. Temples famous here are Mummeleshwar at Mummel and at Kamaksha Devi temple at Kao , Mahunag Temple at Mahunaag about 26Kms from Karsog, Shikari Devi which is highest in the area,Kamrunaag Lake on the way in between the Mandi to Karsog.there is Mahamaya temple at Pangna.
