Air: Kangra has its own domestic airport at Gaggal, which is 7km away from
[Kangra](/asia/southasia/india/himachalpradesh/kangra) town and about 40 km from Palampur. There are straight flights from Delhi to Guggal. 

Rail :
[Pathankot](/asia/southasia/india/punjab/pathankot) is the nearest railhead. There is an overnight train from
[Delhi](/asia/southasia/india/delhi) to Pathankot. From Pathankot Palampur is connected by narrow guage railway. One can also take a bus or taxi to Bir from Pathankot. 

Road : There are ordinary, semi deluxe or deluxe buses available from Delhi and other major towns of Himachal Pradesh to
[Dharamsala](/asia/southasia/india/himachalpradesh/dharamsala),
[Palampur](/asia/southasia/india/himachalpradesh/palampur),
Baijnath,
[Dadra and Nagar Haveli](/asia/southasia/india/dadraandnagarhaveli) or
[Mandi](/asia/southasia/india/himachalpradesh/mandi). All
Kulu-
[Manali](/asia/southasia/india/himachalpradesh/manali)buses go via Mandi. Bir can be reached easily from all these towns by buses or taxi.
