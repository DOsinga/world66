You will find it easy to get either a
taxi or auto-rickshaw. (Auto-rickshaws are incredibly impractical,
however, because of the engine-killing gradient of the town). The taxi
union is very strong, so if you'd like to avoid the bureaucracy, as the
only vehicle in no is sent you cant have the choice of driver /
vehicle, contact your travel agent or a private operator who is
reliable and
can offer a good deal.
