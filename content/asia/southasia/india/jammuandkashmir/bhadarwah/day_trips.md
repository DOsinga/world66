If you enjoy the nature, natural spings, flowing rivers, wish to
have bath in natural springs and rivers, enjoy easy treks, villege life,
walk in dense forests of deodar and pine trees, watching mountains to
the height of 16000 ft., then Bhadarwah is the destination for you.
With
the base camp at Bhadarwah, plenty of day tours can be aranged with the
help of the local friendly persons.

For a listing of possible destinations, please check the Sights section.
