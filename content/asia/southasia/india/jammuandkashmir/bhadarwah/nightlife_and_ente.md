---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment"
type: section
---

There is no nightlife and entertainment in Bhadarwah except to watch cable T.V. in your hotel room. However if you wish Camp Fire can be arranged with music, dance
including local music and local cultural dances with dinner at hotel
surya. The bagpiper music with local traditional band is a thrilling
experience. The people of Bhadarwah are very hospitable and helpful in the time of any emergency.
