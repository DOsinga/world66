Mata Vaishno Devi is located at 5200 feet in the north of Jammu on the Mountain Trikuta. Every year, there are millions of devotees visiting the three deities (Pindies) of the temple, being i.e Maha-Kali, Maha-Lakshmi and Maha-Saraswati. Buses leave at the railway station and take about two hours.
