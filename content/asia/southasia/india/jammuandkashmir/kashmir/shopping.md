---
title: "Shopping in Kashmir"
type: section
---

The Kashmir shopping scene will open the door to an exotic land where shopping is taken to a whole new level. Kashmir is a tourist destination in India. Read on to find out more.

Dal Lake

Dal Lake is famous for its floating market. Located in Srinagar in Kashmir, this is the only floating market in India. This is predominantly a vegetable market. You will be taken on the traditional Kashmiri boat called the shikaras. The boat will take you on a tour of the market that floats on the Dal Lake. You will find vendors selling vegetables and fresh fruits. These vendors are local farmers who load their shikaras with produce and sell them till late nights. Even if you do not wish to buy anything, go to this market to experience the energetic and the colorful surroundings.

Lal Chowk

Lal Chowk is the heart of Kashmir. It is a major business and commercial center. Business here starts early in the morning and goes on till sundown. This block is filled with shopping areas, restaurants and other tourist attractions. Lal Chowk has several shops that specialize in various traditional Kashmiri products. You can buy handicrafts as well as handmade carpets. The Papier Mache that is an important part of Kashmir's artistic life is available here as well.

Polo View

This is another picturesque market area in Kashmir. It is considered to be Srinagar's most beautiful market, as it is surrounded by breathtaking views. At this market, you can browse through a range of Kashmir's finest shawls. The shawls made in Kashmir are popular worldwide. They come in various designs and are made with either wool, pashmina or shahtoosh. You can also buy several handicrafts and carpet here, and there is no need to bargain as the prices are fixed and accurate.

Badshah Market

Also located in Srinagar, the Badshah market is a treat to the eyes. This market is known for its outdoor stalls that display stacks of colorful spices. The world famous saffron and Kashmir's pride is sold in this market. Also, the stores here sell items that are carved out of wood. This is another Kashmiri specialty that showcases the skill of the local craftsmen.

J and K Handicrafts

This is the pride of the Kashmir valley. This market is a government run establishment and features all items related to the handicraft in Kashmir. You will find various items carved out off wood that are high in utility and look exquisite. Located near the police control, this market is truly one of a kind. You can also buy Papier Mache products like chests, vases and wall hangings here.
