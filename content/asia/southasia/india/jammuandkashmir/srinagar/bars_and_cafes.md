title: "Bars and Cafes in Srinagar"
type: section
---

**Srinagar****, Bars and Cafes** are the best place to hangout in the evenings while holidaying here. However, due to security concerns, most of the bars in the city are limited to the hotel restaurants only which offer excellent service.

**Hotel Broadway Srinagar**
****(Maulana Azad Road, Srinagar). The hotel is located in the heart of the Srinagar city and gives excellent view of the valley. You can taste excellent wines and cocktails in the bar here while enjoying the view. The bar also serves beer and foreign liquor. After drinks you can enjoy great Kashmiri cuisine here. The restaurant also serves Indian, Chinese and Continental food. **

Hotel Lake Isle

**

(Nagin Lake, Srinagar). The mini bar of the hotel serves excellent liquor amid the picturesque backdrop of Lake Nagin. You can enjoy your drink watching the beauty of the lake at night away from the maddening crowd of the city. The restaurant also offers Indian, Chinese and Continental dishes. Kashmiri cuisine is a must try here.**Hotel Zamrud
**
(Boulevard Road, Srinagar). The hotel has a mini bar where you can enjoy your choicest liquor watching the immense beauty of Dal Lake. The restaurant offers excellent delicacies from Kashmiri, Indian and Continental cuisines.** ****

Hotel Grand InterContinental

**

(Gupkar Road, Srinagar). This is a perfect place for those who want to enjoy the palace ambiance. The hotel is based in the Grand Palace adjacent to the Governor house and the bar here serves excellent wine. You can enjoy watching the magnificent view of the snow clad Himalayas and the Dal Lake while relaxing in the bar. Do not miss to taste the Kashmiri cuisines here. They also serve excellent Indian and Continentaldishes.
**Hotel Centaur Lake View

**

(Chasmesahi, Srinagar). The bar has ample stock of liquor of all choice and is a perfect place to enjoy the beauty of Dal Lake along with your drink. You can also enjoy some of the local cuisines here along with other dishes. The restaurant also has the facility of barbecue.**Café Coffee Day

**

(Hotel Akbar, Dalgate, Gate No. 01, Srinagar). Now you can enjoy world class flavor of coffee during your trip to Srinagar at Café Coffee Day. This is the first outlet of the joint in the Kashmir valley. More outlets are opening soon in the neighboring areas of the valley.**Coffee Arabica

**

(Maulana Azad Road, Srinagar). It is a perfect place to stop for a chat over a cup of coffee. In between your talks you can also enjoy kebabs. Located in the heart of the city, this café offers everything that you need to enjoy with the flavors of coffee.
