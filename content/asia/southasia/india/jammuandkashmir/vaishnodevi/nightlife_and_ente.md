type: section

Being one of the great pilgrimage destinations in India, there is as such no **Vaishnodevi** **nightlife**.
For nightlife and entertainment one has to reach Jammu, which is the
nearest big city in the region situated at a distance of 50 kilometers
from Katra.

**Tavern Bar**

The bar is a part of Hotel Asia, Jammu. Situated opposite to pool
area, Tavern Bar has great stock of liquor and relaxing ambiance. The
open terrace adjoining the bar area is best for enjoying your drink
overlooking the beauty of city night light.

**Baron Bar**

To enjoy an exotic drink in a relaxing atmosphere, head out for Baron
Bar situated in K.C. Residency Hotel in Jammu. This place is well known
for its soothing music, welcoming and friendly interior and great
choice of exotic drinks ranging from cocktails to wines.

**Nagin Bar**

The bar is part of Hotel Jammu Ashok, Jammu. Nagin Bar is well known
for its stock of beer and foreign cocktails. It is an ideal place to
enjoy the evening after a hectic day. The atmosphere is very soothing
and you can also enjoy great Indian and continental dishes from the
multi-cuisine restaurant here.

**Polo Bar**

The bar is part of Heritage Hotel, Hari Niwas Palace in Jammu and
features the charm and era of the gracious past. The interior of the bar
is splendidly done with woodwork and artifacts from a bygone era. The
bar has a great stock of old wines, cigars and cocktails. It is one of
the best places to enjoy in the evenings.

**Amar Singh Club**

This is one of the renowned clubs of Jammu open for non-members. The
club is well equipped with all the facilities required for great
entertainment and nightlife in Jammu. The club has a billiards room,
cards room and the Royal Retreat Bar which is the perfect place to hang
out in the evenings. The bar has a good stock of wines and beers.

**Jammu Club**

One of the prestigious clubs open for non-members is the Jammu Club,
situated near Tawi bridge. The club is fully equipped with all sorts of
entertainment and relaxation facilities. It has a card room and bar
where you can spend the evenings. The bar has great stock of liquor.

**Vaishnodevi Nightlife** is all about spiritualism and getting
closer to The Divine Goddess. The aura in the region fills you with
devotion and love for The Goddess. This is one place where the pilgrims
open up all their grievances in front of the divine mother and find
peace inside.

**Join the Aarti Ceremony**

This is a very sacred ceremony which is performed twice every
day,during early morning at sunrise and just after the sunset. Devotees
consider it very auspicious to attend the ceremony. This is the only
time when the devotees can hear the pujaris chant the 108 names of The
Goddess offering prayer. The divine light is also offered to the
devotees during this ceremony.

**Get Enchanted With the Bajans**

Devotees gather in groups with musical instruments to sing the
Bhajan, devotional songs of The Mother, during evenings. These songs and
music fills the soul with devotion and peace. No matter how bad you
sing you can participate with the group by singing along with them or
just follow the beats by clapping your hands. Many shops sell music CDs
of these bajans which you can buy as souvenir of your trip.

**Read Devotional Books**

Devotinal books of The Goddess is found almost everywhere in the
place. You can learn a lot of things about The Goddess in these books.
Starting from the legends, history, puja offering, tour information,
these book give all information about The Divine Goddess.

**Hear the Sadhus Speak
**

Vaishnodevi is a very famous Hindu-pilgrimage center in India and
therefore many sadhus come to visit here from far and wide. A trip to
Vaishnodevi is a great chance of meeting these learned men from
different parts of the country. Hearing them speak about the way of life
and how to purify the lifestyle to make a better living is a great way
of learning about good things in life. Sometimes you also get to hear
mythological stories. One should not miss an opportunity to hear them.

**Meditate**

With spiritualism in the atmosphere meditation cannot be missed. The
entire process in this calm surroundings fills you with so much peace
and completeness which you have never experienced before.

**Take a stroll**

Evening is the best time to take a leisure stroll during your
Vaishnodevi trip. Listen to the bajans or chanting of Vedas from the
distant as you walk along the nature trail. The calm surroundings will
energize and revitalize you.
