Sita River Nature Camp, or *Sita Nadi Nisargha Dhama*
if English doesn't help, are a 30min bus ride away from Agumbe, 4km
before the town of Hebri. Any bus heading towards the Hebri will drop
you there for Rs. 15. Expect a leisurely boat ride more than a white
water rafting run. The river course is pretty, however. People at
www.acepaddlers.com take care of arranging all activities
there. They also offer a trek to Koodlu Theertha falls.
