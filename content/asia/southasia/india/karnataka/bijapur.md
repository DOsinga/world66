Following is a trial posting of a brief account of a great historical city in Madhya Pradesh; Bijapur. 

This city of 150 000 has many interesting Islamic monuments. See the Gol Gumbaz, a huge domed tomb with an amazing whispering gallery (any slight noise on one side of the dome can be heard perfectly on the opposite side—and can even echo up to a dozen times). Visit the museum the Ibrahim Roza tomb the citadel and some of the dozens of other mosques and Muslim monuments.**
