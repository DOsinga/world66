Jevargi, 40 km from Gulbarga, is an important religious place on the
banks of the Bhima River. It has important Jain temples. The Jaladurga
Falls near Narayanpura Dam is 120 km from Gulbarga on the Krishna
River. The historic town of Bidar is 110 km northeast of Gulbarga,
while Bijapur is 150 km southwest of Gulbarga.
