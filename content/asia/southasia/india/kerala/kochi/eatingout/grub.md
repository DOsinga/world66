One of the finest examples of local cuisine at rates that are really cheap. Duck and Pork dishes can be made to order and parties are catered for. Grub is the brainchild of Susan and Paul James, entrepreneurs with a genuine liking for sticking to authentic regional recipes. Appam and Rice dishes appear to be their specialities.They will cook to order to accomodate your taste in spices. You can have a sit down meal but their takeaway and home delivery service is excellent.

The GRUB, is located a stones throw from the Manorama Building. Manorama is the most popular local newspaper, so everyone knows their office.
