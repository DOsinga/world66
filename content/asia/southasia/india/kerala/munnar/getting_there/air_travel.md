[Madurai](/asia/southasia/india/tamilnadu/madurai) and [Kochi](/asia/southasia/india/kerala/kochi) (Ernakulam) are the two closest airports to [Munnar](/asia/southasia/india/kerala/munnar). Once you land in the airport, next option is to arrange a road transport to reach [Munnar](/asia/southasia/india/kerala/munnar).

[Munnar](/asia/southasia/india/kerala/munnar) is around 110kms away from [Kochi](/asia/southasia/india/kerala/kochi) ([cochin](/asia/southasia/india/kerala/cochin)) International airport and around 160kms away from [Madurai](/asia/southasia/india/tamilnadu/madurai) airport.
