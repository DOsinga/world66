Cherumbattakavu is one of the famous temple at ottpalam area. It is situated in anangannadi pachayath, around 15 km away from Ottapalam. Cherumbattakavu Pooram is on March / april months and one of the famoums poorams of Kerala. All the traditional forms of anciant art like Enakkala, Kutira,Poothn, Thira, naayadi, Karivela are taking part in this festival. You can see the tallast "Teru" in kerala in this festival and the most famous elephents in kerala. Around 50 elephents are participatin in this festival. The main paticipants ( called as Deshams) in this pooram are Pavukkonam desam, chalavara desam, puliyanmkunnu, kuttikode, taruvakkonam, kotakurssi etc...
