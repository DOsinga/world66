By Air

Currently, 24 flights operate through Thiruvananthapuram every week. Air-India is the only airline to operate direct flights to the Gulf, the main market for Kerala. The Airport is situated approximately 10 kms away from the main city. It has two terminals - one domestic and the other international. There are special check-in counters for Executive Class passengers, who can also tele check-in on Tel no. 501 1426 / 501 1281.  The Airport Tax is Rs 500 ( Rupees Five Hundred )
