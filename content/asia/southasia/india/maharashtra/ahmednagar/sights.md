### [NarendraTamboli](/asia/southasia/india/maharashtra/ahmednagar/sights/narendratamboli)

In the heart of the Ahmednagar City in Manik Chowk there is aTemple of Lord Dattaraya .The Ekmukhi Dattamandir.
The Temple was built by late Dhondopant Tamboli famous Saraf and Savkar in 1860 The owner of the Temple Late Govindrao Tamboli an orthodox Brahmin and landlord of opend the Temple for Harijans in 1936 Late Gangutai Tamboli arranged HaldiKumkum ceremony for Harijan ladies.
This is the first Temple in India opened to the Harijans
At that time orthodox Brahmins of the town outcasted Tamboli family for opening the Temple for [more..](/asia/southasia/india/maharashtra/ahmednagar/sights/narendratamboli)
