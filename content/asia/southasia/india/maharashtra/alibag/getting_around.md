---
title: "Getting Around in Alibag"
type: section
---

If you are looking for the best **Alibag transportation**, the ways most people use include taking the ferry, riding the bus and using a taxi.
Ferry

You can take the ferry to get around to some of the other cities near
Alibag, and you can go to see Revas and Mandwa which are about 20
minutes away. Board the ferry at the port in Alibag and take it across
the waters to have a fun and easy way to get to another city.
Bus

The city bus is affordable and will take you where you want to go.
The buses have a few different routes and make many stops, but they can
be full. If you do not mind the crowds, they are easy to use and you can
get anywhere in town. A lot of people choose to take the buses as
Alibag transportation. There are some areas the buses are not able to
travel, so you want to check first before you plan your travels.
Taxi

There are taxis available in Alibag to take if you do not want to
ride the bus. They mainly service the hotels and center of the town, but
there are some other services available to take you to other places. If
you want to go outside of Alibag, you can take one of the off-road
taxis to the areas without paved streets.
