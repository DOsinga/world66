### [Dombivli Eating habits](/asia/southasia/india/maharashtra/dombivli/eating_out/dombivli_eating_ha)

Dombivli used to be strictly Common Man's town sometime back, and hence the eating habits also were little different than Hotel Culture. Dombivliates still prefer to eat food on Tapri, or stand alone stalls (don't worry, it's higinic in many places). Trimurti Vada pav is the most famous spot. there are lot of Chat shops opened now a days, serving variety of Chat items, believe me they are very tasty, shichever shop you select.
