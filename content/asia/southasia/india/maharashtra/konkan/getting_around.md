title: "Getting Around in Konkan"
type: section
---

Railways are an important part of **Konkan Transportation**. There are other means of transportation too, however they are not always comfortable.

**By Train**

The entire Konkan region is well connected with Kokan Railways. The
railway network covers parts of state of Maharashtra (Raigad), Goa and
Karnataka (Mangalore). Several tourist places like the Raigad Fort,
Mahabaleshwar and Marleshwar in the region are easily reachable by
train. Konkan Railway Tours are the best means of exploring the entire
region.

**By Bus**

State Public Bus Transport also provides good service for getting
around the Konkan region. It is best if you are traveling from 1
district to another within the Konkan region. However, commuting by bus
within a particular town in the region is not advisable as they are
mostly crowded by locals. Some private bus services also operate but
they are limited to a particular area only.

**Other Means**

Auto rickshaw is another means of getting around in the region. They
are a cheaper mode of transportation and readily available, too. You can hire
an auto rickshaw for visiting tourist attractions within a
city or town in the region. However it is advisable to bargain for
rates before boarding the auto rickshaw so that you do not have trouble
later.The 6-seater tempo is another mode of public
transportation here. It is easily found in the region. However, the
journey becomes quite uncomfortable as it is overcrowded by locals. It
is advisable to hire a taxi or private car for moving around within the
region for a comfortable journey.
