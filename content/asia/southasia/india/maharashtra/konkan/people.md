---
title: "People"
type: section
---

People living in Konkan are artless and friendly. Their love for celebration is deeply ingrained in their culture and it finds expression through the various occasions. In Mumbai, they speak Marathi, as it goes towards Goa, the language changes every 4 kms. People in Goa speak Konkani language. There are few people near the areas of cost of Sindhudurg district who speak "Malvani". Konkani and Malvani are very much similar. They live in deep jungles of Sahyadris without worrying about anything outside their small world. Konkan is blessed with lots of water and greenary. Most of them are farmers and fishermen.
