Airport code: PNQ Pune is a military airport, be aware that airforce jet training might delay civil flights.

(2005): Two daily flights (Jet Airways) from / to Mumbai. Other flights to / from Delhi, Bangalore, Goa, Chennai.

One-way ticket BOM - PNQ about Rs 2,600 (2500). Flight time 20 minutes.
