---
title: "Lohagad"
type: poi
---

Lohagad is 3400 feet high and is very wide spread fort. There are 4
doors to Lohagad from the nearby village -Lohgadwadi. i.e. ‘Ganesh
Darwaja’ , ‘Narayan Darwaja’ , ‘Hanuman Darwaja’ and ‘Maha Darwaja’.
Some sculptures can also be seen on Maha Darwaja. Lohagad is not so
spreaded and can be easily seen in few hours . The famous spot on
Lohagad is ‘Vinchu Kata’ - a range of hills that looks like Capricorn.

Amazing view of Pawana Dam is also visible from the back side of
Lohagad. The wealth from Surat was brought here under the leadership of
Netaji Palkar and kept here for some period.
