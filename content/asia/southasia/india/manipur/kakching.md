**[Kakching](/asia/southasia/india/manipur/kakching) is **situated in the southeastern part of Manipur in Thoubal district. The town is around 44 km far away from the capital of Manipur, Imphal and around 70 km far from the border of India and Burma. Kakching is the second largest town of manipur with a well structured streets and landscapes .
