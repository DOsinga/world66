Bhubaneswar has a zillion number of good places to eat a variety of foods. Apart from the famous multicuisine restaurants in the hotels listed in the accomodation section, here are a list of other places:  Dawat, 1 Janpath, Bhubaneswar

*Good place to eat Non-vegetarian Indian food, mainly North Indian food.*   Ashirwad, Bapuji Nagar, Bhubaneswar

*Famous for its cosy ambience....a perfect place to spend a romantic evening... good vegetarian and non-vegetarian food.*   South Indian Hotel, Bapuji Nagar, Bhubaneswar

*As the name suggests, this is the place to go for good South Indian food.*   Suruchi, Market Building, Bhubaneswar.

*Again, real good South Indian food....nice to go here for some snacks....*   Venus Inn, Bapuji Nagar, Bhubaneswar

*Nice Chinese food. *   If you are really interested in finding out the real taste of Bhubaneswar, don't miss out the various monger shops in the streets of Market Building, the famous CHIT-CHAT in Bapuji Nagar, and the gup-chup (gol gappa) shops in Mahatma Gandhi Road.
