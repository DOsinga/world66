The nearest airport is Bhubaneswar. Puri has a railway station and is connected by the South East (SE) railways. Refer to Railway TimeTable to see the latest timings of trains to Puri. Apart from that, Puri is well-connected by buses from all the major parts of the state. From Bhubaneswar, the state bus normally takes around three to four hours to reach Puri.
