244 metres or almost 800 feets in height, the waterfall at Khandadhar is said to be the loveliest and is the highest waterfall in Orissa and it is a nice picnic spot. Khandadhar means the sharpness of a sword in the reginal lanaguage Oriya, which is also the state language.
