**Pink City**

Pink City Jaipur has got many places of tourist attractions. Most famour are Hawa Mahal or Palaces of the Winds, City Palace - showing glimpses of India's glorious past, Jantar Mantar - the 18th century observatory and Birla Temple.

Places of interest arround Jaipur are Forts of Amber, Nahargarh, Jaigarh and Jal Mahal. Amber being the most favourite amongst tourists.

### City Palace

A must see in Jaipur. The palace dates back to 17th century and a mixture of Rajput and Mughal architecture.

It houses Museums and part of palace is still used by the Jaipurs Royal Family.
