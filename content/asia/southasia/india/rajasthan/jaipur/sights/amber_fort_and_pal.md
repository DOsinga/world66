Amber, the ancient capital of Jaipur state is a magnificient piece of architecture with perfect blend of Mughal and Rajput style.

The fort is situated on top of hill overlooking Dil-e-Aaram Garden. Best way to reach fort is climb from the garden side or Delhi Road or take a elephant ride in true royal style.
