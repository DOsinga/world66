Take a Sky Tour and treat your eyes with an awesome view of the Pink City. [SkyWaltz](/asia/southasia/india/rajasthan/jaipur) now offers Hot Air Balloons Flights over Jaipur & Samode. One can watch the forts, palaces, sand dunes & villages with a birds eye view. It surely is an experience of a life time.
