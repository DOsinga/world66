The Bhairon Haveli is a landmark building in Bikaner This property is ideally situated near the Junagarh fort. All the places worth visiting are at a convenient distance from here. The Bhairon Vilas hotel consists of 18 well decorated rooms. Each room has its own unique heritage style and highlights the legendary history of the Bikaner. The rooms have old wooden jarokhas or carved windows still existing from older days and some of the rooms have glided leaf work.
