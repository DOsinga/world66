Most travellers to Sikkim go by Jeep taxi. Frequent service runs between Gangtok and Darjeeling; service to other points is also possible. Prices and departure times are are variable by season.

There is a daily 5-Seater Helicopter Service (SIKKIM HELIKOPTER SERVICE) between Ganktok and Bagdogra connecting all civil flights to and from Bagdogra. The trip takes half an hour and the fare is Rs1500. 

Dep Gangtok - 12:30 hrs 

Dep Bagdogra - 14:30 hrs.
