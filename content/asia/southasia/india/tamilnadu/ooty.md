[Ooty](/asia/southasia/india/tamilnadu/ooty) popualrly known as the "Queen of Hill Stations", is located about 90kms from [Coimbatore](/asia/southasia/india/tamilnadu/coimbatore),which is also the nearest airport, one of the big cities in Tamilnadu. With a beautiful, breath-taking lake and lush green scenery with Silver Oak and Teak trees bordering the skyline. Nearby destinations are Coonoor, Dhodda Betta and Kotha Giri. [Ooty](/asia/southasia/india/tamilnadu/ooty) can be visited during any time of the year, but the best time is between Mar-July.-Ram Shridhar
