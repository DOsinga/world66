Pondicherry is where the French tried to setup their own Indian empire. It didn't really work, though, but the present day town still has a few reminders of those times. Technically, The Union Territory of Pondicherry includes 4 enclaves located in three states of South India. It includes the coastal towns of Pondicherry and Kerala in Tamil Nadu, Yanam in [Andhra Pradesh](/asia/southasia/india/andhrapradesh) and [Mahe](/africa/seychelles/mahe) in Kerala.

Present day Pondi is mostly interested as the site of the Ashram of Aurobindo. Pondi also has a superb beach.
