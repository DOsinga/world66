'Thrissivaperror' was the first name of Thrissur. In city there are three G odsiva. Thrissur is a the cultural city of Kerala. Thrissur is very famous for 'Trichur pooram' (april-may). The city has own rich culture, heritage & archeological wealth. The cultural centers like Kerala sahithya academy, Kerala lalithakala academy & Kerala sangeetha nataka academy are in Thrissur. The city has also known as 'Festivals place' & 'Temples place'. Famous Vadakkumnathan temple, Paramekkavu temple & Thiruvambady temple are in city only. Thrissur is the center district of Kerala. So, the city has connecting more rails & more roads. Nearest International is Airport Nedumbassery-Kochi.
