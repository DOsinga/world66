---
title: "Sights in pauri sumari village"
type: section
---

Sumari is nestled among some of the beautiful landscapes and rolling mountain terrain. It has some finest terraced agricultural fields dotted with pine trees. The scenic view from the mountain top of Sumari is so attractive that one does not want to go off from it. The view of Alaknanda river and the landscape in which it flows is worth to see again and again. The snow clad peaks of Chawkhamba and its associated Himalayan mountains mesmerize the viewers.

Indra gaman, Nagrasaini, Nagraja dhar, Pitrodi dhar, Kanda top, and Thapul dhar are all eye catching places around this village.
