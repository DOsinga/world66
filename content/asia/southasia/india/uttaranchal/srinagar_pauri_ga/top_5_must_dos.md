---
title: "Top 5 Must Do's in Srinagar (Pauri Garhwal)"
type: section
---

While visiting Srinagar, you might want to add these things to your **Srinagar must do's**
list. Srinagar is the capital of the northern state of Jammu and
Kashmir, near India. Tourism plays a very important role in Srinagar.
Thus, if you are visiting Srinagar for the first time, here are the top 5
things that you should do.

**1. Visit the Houseboats in Dal Lake**

Srinagar is highly regarded for its lake. One of the most famous
lakes in the area is Dal Lake. The first thing that you should do in
this city is to see the houseboats that are numerous in the many lakes
of the city. You can also ask your local guide to arrange for you to go
aboard one of these houseboats on the lake.

**2. Climb the Takht-e-Sulaiman **

The second thing that you should do is to climb the Takht-e-Sulaiman.
This is a famous hill where the Shankaracharya Temple, an ancient Shiva
Temple, rests. This hill rises one thousand feet above the sea level,
but you should only climb the first few hundred feet towards the temple
to visit the shrine and get a good panoramic view of the city at the
same time.

**3. Hike in the Trails of Dachigam National Park**

This natural park is located in the outskirts of the city, and has
several trails that lead to the interior of the forest, where you can
see the natural flora and fauna of the country. However, while walking
the trail, you need to observe cleanliness. Do not throw your litter
anywhere, as this park is maintained by the government to protect the
drinking water supply of the city.

**4. Ride the Horse Carriages**

The fourth thing that you should do in Srinagar is to ride the horse
carriages, which are the main mode of transportation in the city. What
is great about riding horse carriages is that it is a cheaper
alternative to hiring taxis, which exist in few numbers in Srinagar.

**5. Bargain Shopping In the Streets of Srinagar**

Never leave Srinagar without experiencing how to shop like the locals
do. Thus, your top 5 must do’s in Srinagar should include bargain
shopping. The streets of Srinagar have a lot of peddlers selling their
wares to locals and tourists. Shopping in Srinagar is a great way for
you to practice your haggling skills.
