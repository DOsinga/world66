Aligarh is a nice exscursion from [Agra](/asia/southasia/india/uttarpradesh/agra) or [Lucknow](/asia/southasia/india/uttarpradesh/lucknow). It is a student town dominated by the red brick Moorish-style buildings of the Aligarh Muslim University. Aligarh is also an industrial centre known for its lock and hardware manufacturing industry.
