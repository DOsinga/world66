DUBRAN is a beautiful village of District Abbottabad, it is situated 35 Kms in Southeast of Haripur town on the Lora-Ghora Gali road.

the village is basically green and fertile but Employment is the main source of income. However, 30% people,
mostly illiterate, now the literacy rate is raising day by day. there is one Private English Medium School also in Dubran. Old and women are engaged in agro-pastoral activities. Land
is partially owned by community. Livestock subsistence agriculture,
rural women mostly carry out fuel wood, fodder and water collection etc. while the men are engaged in jobs, mostly are in Pak Army, School Teacher and also a big no. in foreign countries.
