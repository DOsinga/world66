Sherwan is a beautiful Village near Abbottabad. The population of Sherwan Town is over 30000. The local language is Hindko while Urdu is also understood and spoken by local people. The main tribes of Sherwan are Tanolis (Labial, Sarganal and Sadial), Awans (Marjan or Kutab Shahi), Syed and Mughals. Sherwan is divided into many parts in which the main and big parts are Sherwan Kalan, Sherwan Khurd, Patheel and thora.
