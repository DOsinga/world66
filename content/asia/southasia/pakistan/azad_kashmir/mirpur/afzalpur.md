**Afzalpur** (In past known as Gobindpur) is located at the bank of Jhelum Canal. It is about 6 miles away from Mirpur City. If you are traveling from Mirpur to Jhelum via Chechiyan (Darbar Baba Peir-E-Shah Ghazi and The Tomb of Mian Mohammad Bakhash). You will find it in your way from Chechiyan to on word. It is located in the middle of Chechiyan and Jatlan towns. It is a well knwon place in all over the Azad Jammu & Kashmir because of it's Government Teachers training collage, Government Degree collages for Boys and Girls, Government High schools for Boys and for Girls, and as well as Private schools. Police station, Post office, and Union council,s Offices and much more. It is the place in area to visit.
