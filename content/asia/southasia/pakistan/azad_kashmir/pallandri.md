It presents a ravishing scenery and alluring view to the visitors. The valley consists of numberless evergreen pine and cedar trees, transparent spring, musical water-falls and sky-high mountains. The climate is pleasant throughout the year. Temperature in summer is almost 20 to 35 °C and in winter -5 to 25 °C. In winter, one may witness the snow-fall on the nearby mountains.
