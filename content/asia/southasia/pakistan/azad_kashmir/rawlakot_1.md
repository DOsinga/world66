**Rawlakot**

Rawlakot, the district headquarter, is situated in the heart of district
Poonch. The altitude beautiful Saucer–Shaped Valley IS 1615 Meters. Situated at a distance of 76 Kilometers from Kohala is also linked with Rawalpindi/Islamabad via Azad Pattan and Dhalkot and with Muzafarabad Kohala by black top roads. The construction of Guoien Nullah road between Rawlakot Azad Pattan has considerably reduced the distance and journey time.
