Khairpur Tamewali Tehsil is an administrative subdivision (tehsil) of Bahawalpur District in the Punjab province of Pakistan. The city of Khairpur Tamewali is the headquarters of the tehsil which is administratively subdivided into 8 Union Councils.Khairpur Tamewali was named as Tamewali on a name of an English person Tommy. which became Tamewali with the passage of time. Khairpur Tamewali is under control of Tehsil Munciple Administration headed by Tehsil Nazim while city Nazim is Also available to minitor local government, All other Nazims of Union Councils work under Both City, and Tehsil Nazim.
