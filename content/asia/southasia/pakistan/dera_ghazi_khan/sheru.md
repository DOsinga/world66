The village Sheru is located 40km away from Dera Ghazi Khan in south-east direction.
It is a historic village,twice engulfed by river Indus. The current village was established in 1956. It is 5 km west of river indus.
It is a hub of Chishti family which is known for its good manners and ethics around the area. Among other familis are shah, qazi, malana, maril, somra and arain...
