---
title: "Family Travel Ideas in Faisalabad"
type: section
---

There is no shortage of opportunities for **Faisalabad family travel**. When you come to Pakistan's second largest biggest city you will be greeted with a bevy of fun activities for all ages. This is a great place for family travel. You shouldn't hesitate to bring your kids along as they will have plenty to keep them busy as you work the day away.

**Sindbad Amusement Park**

This awesome amusement park is a must do for families with children. The park is located near Iqbal stadium, and offers a huge indoor bumper car arena as well as a gaming arcade that caters to all ages. The park tends to open in the evening during the summer and early during the winter. There is no need to buy tickets in advance; they are readily available at the gate. There are food service establishments both in and outside of Sindbad. Make sure to come early during the weekends, when this place can get packed in a hurry.

**Iqbal Stadium**

Iqbal Stadium is a great place to spend some quality family time. This stadium hosts both national and international cricket competitions each year. This large stadium has a capacity for 25,000 people and on the right day it will be full. If you are lucky enough to be in Faisalabad during a cricket tournament, this is a must see for any family.

**Aqua Land Water Park**

Aqua Land Water Park is one of several amusement parks located in Faisalabad. What sets Aqua Land Water Park apart from the pack is the fact that it is both the largest and the newest water park in Faisalabad. It is in fact one of the largest in the entire region. When the weather is hot, this is a fantastic place to soak up some sun and splash in the cool water. This is perfect for family members of all ages.

**Khewra Salt Mines**

Located just outside of Faisalabad are the Khewra Salt Mines. This ancient mine is the second largest salt mine in the world. Kids and adults will enjoy the fantastic size of the Khewra Salt Mines while taking one of the daily guided tours. Here you will see crystalline walls made completely of salt, as well as an underground mosque built of salt, brick by brick.

There is no limit to the variety of things to do in Faisalabad when it comes to family entertainment. The shopping, amusement parks and other attractions can't be beat. There is so much family entertainment in Faisalabad that you will have to book a second trip just to see it all.
