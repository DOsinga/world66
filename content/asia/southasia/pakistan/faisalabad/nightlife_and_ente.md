---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment in Faisalabad"
type: section
---

**Faisalabad Nightlife** offers the right atmosphere for just about anyone. Though Islam prohibits drinking, you will find that many of the hotels have bars for the non-Islamic tourists to indulge themselves with a bit of alcohol, which is allowed until 7 PM. Despite the lack of alcohol pouring bars on every corner, there is still plenty of fun and entertainment in Faisalabad after the sun goes down. Many of the same activities available in the day time are still open and going strong at night.

**The Cinema**

There are several theaters located within Faisalabad. Stop by one of these during your stay to see nightly showings of some of the best movies in India. The beauty and detail of the Indian cinema has attracted international renown. Nothing is more exciting than viewing a fantastic Indian film inside of an authentic Indian theater.

**Restaurants**

A great way to spend your time after dark in Faisalabad is sampling some of the country's many options for cuisine. The restaurants in this area range from classy and luxurious to run-of-the-mill, with a food variety to suit every taste.

In Faisalabad you will find many restaurants like Dynasty or Lasania that offer local fare. If you are looking for something western, you can enjoy fast food from places like KFC or McDonalds. If you want something Chinese, there are a multitude of choices including Pearl Garden, China City and Exotica, all of which offer authentic Chinese cuisine.

**D-Ground**

D-Ground is a popular evening retreat for locals and tourists alike. This shopping center offers a wide variety of entertainment and many people come out to D-Ground in the evening just to have something to do. People watch, peruse the multitude of shops and stop to get some local food. This fabulous market area is a great place to spend an evening.

Though Faisalabad may not be particularly known for its nightlife, this doesn't mean that there's not plenty to do in this fabulous business community. After a long day of work, many guests and locals spend the evening enjoying some of the activities that abound in Faisalabad, even after the sun goes down.

Whether you want to spend your evening watching an India feature film, dining on exquisite Indian cuisine at some of the finest restaurants in the region, shopping for anything your heart desires or having a great time splashing at one of Faisalabad’s many water parks, you should have no problem finding something to entertain you in this wonderful city.
