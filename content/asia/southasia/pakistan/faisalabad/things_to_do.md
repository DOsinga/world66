---
title: "Things to do in Faisalabad"
type: section
---

There is no limit to the amount of **Faisalabad things to do**. No matter your age or preference you are most certain to find something in Pakistan's third largest city to interest you. Whether you are there for business or leisure, enjoy your time in Faisalabad by visiting some of the following spots.

**Enjoy Amtex Square**

In an attempt to beautify the city, the Amtex Company created a unique architectural development near Jaranwala Road known as Amtex Square. What makes this place so fascinating is the man-made waterfall at the center of the square. This amazing piece of human achievement is definitely something you should check out before leaving Faisalabad.

**Have Fun at Canal Park**

One of Faisalabad's many parks, Canal Park is a great place to spend some quality time with your family or just relax in solitude. It can be found on the Rakh Branch Canal, hence the name.

**Faisalabad Hockey Stadium**

For those who are into sports, visit Faisalabad Hockey Stadium to catch a fun field hockey game. When the game is over, enjoy the many shops and dining areas that are located in and around the stadium.

**Religious Architecture**

Be sure to check out the many still visible relics of the various religions that have come to set claim in Faisalabad over the years. There are many temples, monasteries, gurudwaras and mandirs from the past standing in the city. Due to the major increase in population, some of these religious structures have been converted into schools, museums and other establishments, so be sure to purchase a guide if you are going to be checking out these amazing structures.

**Eat at Various Restaurants**

No matter what kind of food you are in the mood for, you should have no problem finding it at one of Faisalabad's many dining establishments. There are many popular western establishments like McDonald's and KFC available in the city, as well as traditional Indian and Chinese places. Be sure to try a Chatani Samosa while in Faisalabad, as the Samosas here are different from any you will find elsewhere. Also be sure to try some of the local drinks, often available from street side vendors. Some drinks of note are rabri, lassi, sugar cane rusk, limo pani and the ever-sour khatta.

You are certain to find something to do in Faisalabad to meet your interests. Whether visiting some of the ancient architecture, one of the many parks, or eating some of the fine cuisine, you will enjoy your visit to Faisalabad.
