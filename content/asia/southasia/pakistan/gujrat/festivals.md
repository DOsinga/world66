### [Summer fairs in Gujrat

Two famous summer fairs take place in Gujrat every year. One fair is called ' MAILA SHAH JEHANGIR' which means the festival of the King Jehangir. It takes place in May or June every year. According to a local tradition, the famous King Jehangir of India, died when his caravan was passing by Gujrat. His internal organs were taken out and buried in a grave near Gujrat and his body was taken to Lahore and buried there. Somehow the site of his burial place became the focus of an annual village fair in Gujrat.
