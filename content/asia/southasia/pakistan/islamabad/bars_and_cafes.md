### [Civil Junction](/asia/southasia/pakistan/islamabad/bars_and_cafes/civil_junction)

Civil Junction is a great addition to Isloo's popular food spots. It is located in Sector F-7/3's famous Gol Market.

This is the place which offer you real good coffee and drinks of exclusive variety. Light snacks are also offered and they are really too good. Prices are very reasonable and service n food is excellent. Their menu card is itself a piece of art and you would not stop your smile while having a look at items being offered. You may enjoy Musharaf's Expresso ,Vajpai's Cappuccino or other such items.

Every Saturday night a local band Corduroy also performs for [more..](/asia/southasia/pakistan/islamabad/bars_and_cafes/civil_junction)



tel: (51)-2274436, (51)-2274378

address: Shop# 6, Gol Market, Street 8, Sector F-7/3

### [Hot Spot](/asia/southasia/pakistan/islamabad/bars_and_cafes/hot_spot)

One of the few places that Islamabad can claim as theirs first. Offers a great ice-cream place. Though the menu has now increased from just ice-cream to milkshakes, pies, sandwiches and plenty more, the place still has the feel of an ice-cream joint. With a unique, rather artistic decor, Hot Spot is a must visit for any first time traveler to Islamabad.
