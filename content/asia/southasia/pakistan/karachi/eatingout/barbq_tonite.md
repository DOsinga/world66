Without a doubt, Karachi's most popular restaurant. My own estimate is that it serves at least 2,500 - 3000 people every night !! The restaurant is built over 3 storeys, with the rooftop being the ideal spot to enjoy your tikkas, kebabs, ribs and naans hot off the oven. Food quality has remained consistently excellent for over 15 years and prices have always been very reasonable. Word has spread far and wide and Karachiites descend enmasse to devour their delicacies every single night of the week. TIP : go early, preferably before 8 p.m. to avoid the rush and get a good table. This restaurant never disappoints.
