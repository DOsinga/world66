This is definitely one of the best fast food restaurants in Karachi. The food has superb taste with a wholesome variety. My favorite is the Club sandwich - it's simply amazing. McANZS is situated in Bahadurabad but I hear there may be another one opening soon in Defence.

A must try for all who love good food with plenty of taste.
