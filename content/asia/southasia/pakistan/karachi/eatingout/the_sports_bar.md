At The Sports
Bar, you won't find waitresses in orange shorts serving you
beer... now, wait a minute, don't be disappointed. After
all, this is Pakistan, right?

What
you will find is an upscale sports bar theme which caters to refined
tastes and those who appreciate an enjoyable atmosphere where they can
hang out with friends, watch sports throughout the season, listen to
live music, come to movie night, dance salsa, sing karaoke... and order
some not-so-typical sports bar food.

Moving
away from the typical café trends, The Sports Bar wanted to create a
space where the youth and the young at heart could sit and enjoy the
sports they love in an environment that’s both hip and trendy without
losing its elegant touch. The oversized LCD’s, digital satellite
receivers and surround sound ensure an exciting sports viewing
experience. Private VIP rooms are also available for those groups who
really want to..., well, let's just say, have an extra good time.

The
Sports Bar really knows the meaning of top-class customer service.
You'll find an attentive and caring staff that will ensure that you
always have a great experience. Hygiene and food handling practices are
extra important at The Sports Bar and the management is very particular
about the cleanliness of the restaurant. Non-smoking areas are
available and air purifiers are in place to maintain a refined ambiance
for smokers and non-smokers alike.

On the menu, you'll find an array of tasty choices, many recipes
influenced by the chef's extensive culinary experience from abroad,
including the USA and Dubai. High quality ingredients are used, many of
which are imported. The salads at The Sports Bar that are both healthy
and fresh and you'll find a wide range of sandwiches, steaks, burgers,
wraps and pastas that are prepared from the best ingredients. Hot and
cold coffees, mocktails, smoothies, Italian sodas and some special
signature drinks are also on the menu. The Sports Bar is the only
eatery in Karachi that serves the super premium Häagen-Dazs® ice cream,
and the desserts come freshly baked by the best bakers in town.

So come by The Sports Bar, open for lunch and dinner and everything in between.
Good Times, Great Food.
