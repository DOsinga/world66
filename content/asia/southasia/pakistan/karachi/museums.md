**National Museum.address=Ziauddin Ahmed Rd.**

The National Museum has an important collectins of items relating to Pakistan`s ancient heritage. Well arranged galleries display Indus Civilization, Gandhara sculptures, Islamic art Miniatur paintings ancient coins and manuscripts documenting Pakistan`s political history. There is also an interesting ethnological gallery.   [more..](/asia/southasia/pakistan/karachi/museums/nationalmuseumaddressziauddinahmedrd)

**Empress Market**

Built by the British to commemorate the Silver Jubille of Queen Victoria`s reign, this historic market is designed in the domestic Gothic style. From its centre rises a tall clock tower.
