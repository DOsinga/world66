Shah Bandar is located in Thatta District, Sindh, Pakistan.
Shah Bandar is a Tehsil of District Thatta. history is that Shah Bandar was a prominent trading port of Sindh during the Soomra and Kalhora dynasties. The British East India Company established a trading factory in Shah bandar in 1758 during the Kalhora Dynast.
the majority of the population of Shah bandar is Jalbani, Jamali, Jatt, Kutchi and other casts settled in Shah bandar. The population is predominantly Muslim with a small Hindu minority.
the big city of tehsil shah bandar is Chuhar Jamali.
