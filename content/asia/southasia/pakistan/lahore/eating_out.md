Lahore is city with an extremely rich food culture. People from Lahore are famous all over the country for their love for food. The city offers a vast variety of options when it comes to gastronomy.*
*

*Gosht Karahi (*Chicken or mutton cooked in Spices) is a speciality of Lahore. There are no equals to Lahore Resturants, even in Pakistan. As far as this food item is concerned, hub of such resturants is Royal Park area (*Lakshmi Chowk).* Some of the famous names are Pakasia, Butt and Nirala Butt.

*Dal Gosht *(Meat and pulses*)* of the canteen situated inside the premesis of Regal Cinema, Mall Road, has a unique taste.

*Murgh Channy* form Manna, Tooba and Shaihi are classical local food, all of these resturants are situated at Abbot Road. Lso known in this catagory is Ghulam Rasool Resturant, Nila Gumbad, near Bank Square Lahore.

Tabbaq Resturant, Lakshmi Chowk and Paradise canteen, State Life Building, Mall road, are hud of declicious Mutton Roast and *Murgh Musallam* (Chicken cooked with rice and dry fruits stuffed inside) .

*Seekh Kababs* (minced meat rolls) of inside Mochi Gate, bear the speciy taste of local Lahore food. There is also a cult following for the *Seekh Kababs* from Bhayya Kabab Shop, situated in C-Block, Model Town. Do not go by the looks of the small shop, they are known all over the place for their unique taste.

The best *Gol Gappas* in lahore are available at Nisbat Road, near Dayal Sing Library. There is also a good *thaila* (wood cart) serving nice *Gol Gappas* near the entrance of the Lawrance Garden. If you are in for a change, try the *Gol Gappas* filled with *Dahi Bhallay*.

Bundu Khan Resturants at Mall Road, Liberty Market Gulberg and Moon Market, Allama Iqbal Town is know for its spicy gravey dishes.

Haji's Nihar inside Lohari Gate and Amritsar Harrisa at Nisbat Road are two places for special local foods but only person having healty stomach may eat there as these contain pecices densly.

Food Street at *Gawalmandi* and at *Purani Anarkali* are two good junctions of local food dishes.

M M Alam Road, Gulberg and H-Block Defence Housing Authority (DHA) are the two areas heavily populated with resturants serving modern Chinese and exotic food dishes.

Copper Kettle Main Boulevard Gulberg and Gun Smoke Resturant at MM Allam Road Gulberg are two interesting places for novel foods.

*Freddy's Cafe* is a restaurant which has jumped up the slots to snatch a top position in the best places for food in town. Situated on M. M. Alam road, the restaurant offers an amiable atmosphere and a variety of Mexican, Italian, Afghani and Continental dishes.
