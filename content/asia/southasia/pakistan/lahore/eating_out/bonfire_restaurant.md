Bonfire Restaurant aims to provide a variety of high quality food at economical prices in a pleasant environment. Soon after its opening, Bonfire was able to attract and retain a large clientele of loyal customers. The use of finest ingredients and exotic recipes has enabled Bonfire to hold a distinct position among the dining places of Lahore .

Bonfire has always lived up to its reputation of serving fresh and quality food at affordable prices. Bonfire offers a vast menu including BBQ, traditional Pakistani dishes, Pizzas, Chinese, Fast Food and Sea Food dishes to make the customer’s experience a memorable one.

Bonfire operates two restaurants for the moment in Allama Iqbal Town and Johar Town . Soon it is planning to expand operations in other areas of Lahore
