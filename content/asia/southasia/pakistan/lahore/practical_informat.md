For history, you may visit Lahore Fort, Shalimar Gardens, walled City, Lawrance Garden, old campus, Lahore Musemum, Noor Jehan's Tomb or Qutub-ud-din Aibak'sTomb. For modern day Lahore, you may go to Pace shopping Mall, Siddique Trade Centre, City Tower Gulberg, Liberty Market or Anarkali. There are a whole lot of eating places of local fame.
