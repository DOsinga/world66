The **National College of Arts Lahore** , usually referred to by its acronym **NCA**, is a famous old college in Lahore, Pakistan.

Like all old institutions, the National College of Arts, has a
historical tradition. Much of its present tone was set long ago when it
was known as the **Mayo School of Arts**. Gazetteers written about the city of Lahore in 1915 describe the work being done at the school as one of the cultural highlights of the urban centre.
