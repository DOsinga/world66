### [Red Onion](/asia/southasia/pakistan/murree/eating_out/red_onion)

Chain of Restaurants situated on the main mall road, stands one of the most modern restaurant in Murree. Established in 1993.

this restaurant offers a wide range of cuisines not offered anywhere else in the vicinity i.e Mexican, Italian, Chinese, Pakistani & Continental. Prices are very moderate, ambiance is modern & customer service is very friendly!

### [Saffron](/asia/southasia/pakistan/murree/eating_out/saffron)

An elegantly decorated restaurant with stunning views of the surrounding hills and valleys. Saffron offered the ultimate personalized culinary experience. The menu is a blend of continental and local favorites including our very own rendition of the ever-popular chicken karahi and chicken makhani. Saffron lies overlooking crisscrossing paths, beautiful hills and valleys.

type: Local



address: Shangrila Resort Hotel

The grill is located adjacent to the Huqqa Pani lounge and follows the same earthen traditional theme. So after relexing in the lounge, you can come and watch our chefs prepare a variety of mountwatering barbeque dishes.
