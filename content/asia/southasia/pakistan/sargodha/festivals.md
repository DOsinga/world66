**The City of Festivals:**

** Sargodha **Division is famous for its festivals and different other programmes are called in local language Mela's. **Sargodha **itself hosts a very big festival called Mela Maveshian O Aspan or Mela Mandi. Related Territories specially a village **Saliqa** is very famous for its match competitions. Volly Ball and Kabaddi are the mostly like games of the population. **Saliqa** has given the District top players of Volly Ball and Kabaddi. **Malik Muhammad Ahmad Hayyat Lumbardar** is the best player of Kabaddi and Volly Ball as well. He made the District proud at National level games.
