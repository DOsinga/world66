### [Kataragama Devale](/asia/southasia/srilanka/the_south/day_trips/kataragama_devale_3)

This is major site of pilgrimage for Hindus, Buddhists and Muslims in Sri Lanka. I is said to contain the trident or spear of the Hindu warlord Skanda in his incarnation as the Kord Kataragama. The site is located by the Manik Ganga and is associated with a major festival when pilgrims show their devotion by fire walking
