A very well respected tour company based in Siem Reap is well worth a mention here. Peace of Angkor Tours have been operating in Cambodia since 2003.

They specialize in photo-orientated tours of Ankor Wat, Angkor Thom, Bayon and other Angkor Kingdom temples.

Peace of Angkor Tours also operate regular one-day tours from Siem Reap; with no minimum numbers of passengers, these small-group tours are ideal for independent travellers - because no single supplement is payable. Live tour availability and prices can be found on their website
