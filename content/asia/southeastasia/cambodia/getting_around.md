### [Tuk-Tuk, Cyclo and Moto](/asia/southeastasia/cambodia/getting_around/tuktuk_cyclo_and)

In modern Cambodia, most people own cars and this is how they travel. But some still travel by foot or **tuk-tuk**, perhaps **cyclo **or **moto. **Cyclo is cheap, you can travel long distances with very little money, but it takes more time as the person on the back has to pedal, turn and stop with his own two legs. Many people can fit on a cyclo, but no one considers the pedaler and how much weight he has to carry.
