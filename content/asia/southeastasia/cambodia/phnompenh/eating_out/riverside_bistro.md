T he Riverside Bistro is located in the heart of Phnom Penh's riverfront, With its spectacular riverfront
(Sisowath Quay) setting which it has been for over 10 years. The
Riverside Bistro is set in an old colonial style building with
comfortable outdoor dining and brilliant views of the Tonle Sap (The
river Sap), the Riverside Bistro is your ideal stop for a relaxing
rest, a drink outside or at one of our two bars inside, dine on our
extensive selection of Western & Asian food, shoot some pool, score
on our Foosball table or simply relax....

H aving already the perfect location, we also wanted to make sure the whole experience is in perfect balance;
to be something extraordinary...
Great location, with comfortable & cosy outdoor seating, and superb riverfront view. Excellent Western & Asian food, with open charcoal BBQ. A broad selection of cocktails, beers, wine, single malt and liqeuers. Western-trained attractive personnel; attentive yet unobtrusive. An international and multi-cultural clientele of both foreigners and Khmers. Tourists & business people mix easily. Two large pool tables, with good lighting and ventilation. A large (2x2m) projector screen, showing live sports and concerts. Three 40" Plasma Screens
A wide range of international magazines and daily newspapers. Wonderful hand-painted murals on the walls. The most beautiful Oriental antiques on display, and for sale. Excellent security to ensure your time at the Riverside, in one of the safest cities in South East Asia, is stress free.
