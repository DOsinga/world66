Equinox is located in the buzzing st 278, in an old-style house.
Downstairs a gaming room with **pool table**, darts and the only 'Bonzini' foosball table in town !
Upstairs a **painting gallery** and a **clothing shop** presenting the work of local artist Soumey
Finally on the balcony, a nice bar with a large variety of original **cocktails**, imported and local boozes and beers and a computer with **free internet** and loads of **good music**.
The place regularly organize parties on friday night, so don't be surprised is the place is packed when you come to visit !
