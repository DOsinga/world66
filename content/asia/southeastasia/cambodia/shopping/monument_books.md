**Monument Books **is a bookstore located in Phnom Penh on Norodom Boulevard (just nearby the Independence Monument), International Airport and Siem Reap (Airport and National Museum) as well as on the internet on [www.monument-books.com.](/asia/southeastasia/cambodia/shopping)

From 7.30 am to 9 p.m visitors can browse through a large selection of cambodian and foreign books. They cooperate with the famous Java Cafe and opened a Java Tea Room in the book store - so its convenient to sit down, take some coffee and cookies and preview some books. For the kids the just opened the Monument Toys section, where they can spend a lot of time - and parents a lot of money.

Monument Books supports the local publishing industry with special cambodian books sections in the store an on the website.
