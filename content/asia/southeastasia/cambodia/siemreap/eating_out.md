**Restaurants**

**Cafe D'Angkor**

This fine restaurant serves European and Asian food - breakfast, lunch and dinner. All major credit cards are taken. 

address: Grand Hotel D'Angkor

tel: +63-963888

**Chivit Thai**

Thai restaurant located in a Khmer-style wood house. Reasonable priced.

address: Wat Bo Road

tel: +63-15-630059

**Continental Cafe**

This restaurant is open from 7 am until midnight. It serves Pasta and Pizza and other moderately priced food. You can also play billiard here. 

address: 353 Pokambor Avenue

tel: +63-15-8503212
