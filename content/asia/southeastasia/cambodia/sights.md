### [Angkor

The celebrated temples of Angkor are Cambodia's greatest tourist attraction. The 100 or so temples are the sacred remains of what was once a much larger administrative and religious centre, and were built between the 9th and 13th centuries to glorify a succession of Khmer kings. The three most magnificent temples are Bayon, Ta Prohm and the immense Angkor wat](http://www2.world66.com/asia/southeastasia/cambodia/sights/angkor). Most of Angkor was abandoned in the 15th century and the temples were gradually cloaked by forest.
