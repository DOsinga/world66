**Banking:**

The following banks are in Dili: ANZ Banking Group, Bank Mandiri and the Banco Nacional Ultramarino.

ANZ Bank has an ATM in Dili, one at the Corner of Av. Presidente Nicolau Lobato & Rua Belarmino Lobo, Bidau Lecidere, Dili, Timor Leste, and one at the Leader Store, Comoro, on the way to the airport. Open during business hours Monday to Sunday.

In addition, Western Union also provides a money transfer service.

**Internet:**

There are a number of internet cafes in town while this type of pleasure is certainly not cheap.

**Medical facilities:**

Medical facilities are limited and evacuation may be required in cases of serious illness or accident. Generally, expatriates attend Dili National Hospital for treatment. Dental facilities are extremely limited. Usually, expatriates travel to Bali or Darwin for emergencies and other treatments.
