** Pete:** The Bali Hard Rock is unremarkably like every other Hard Rock Cafe around the world. It's a good place to go if you're feeling homesick or longing for some Western-style food. It's one of the few places in Kuta where you can eat beef and be reasonably confident you won't be parting with it a couple of hours later. It also has the most over the top service of any restaurant in Kuta - they won't leave you alone!
