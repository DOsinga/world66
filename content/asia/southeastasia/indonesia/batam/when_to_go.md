---
title: "When to Go"
type: section
---

Batam Island, Indonesia, is an enjoyable city to visit year round,
especially since the shopping is all duty free and dirt cheap. You can
attend the DC Mall, Batam Centre Mall and the Plaza Mall for designer
clothes, international books and electronics for half of what you’d
pay back home. If you want local goods, head to the Batam Crafts
Market in the city centre.

Here's a look at some of the annual events on Batam Island, to help you plan on when to go.

**DECEMBER EVENTS**

**Parade Budaya Nusantara**

One of Batam’s best parades attended by locals and travelers alike,
here you can listen to music, buy souvenirs, enjoy the local food and
stroll the streets of the city centre. A great hit with children.

**Batam Batik Fair**

Showcasing an array of Batik artwork, crafts, design, fabric and
performances, the annual Batam Batik Fair is a delightful way to spend a
day away from the beach and buy gifts to send back home.

**Pageant Fair in Batam**

A newly created event, the Pageant Fair in Batam features women
contestants who discuss the history of factories in Batam. What better
way to get a glimpse into Batam history and tradition? Watch the
contestants engage in friendly debate while snacking on a delicious
chicken wrap.

**Batam New Year’s Eve Festival**

Just head to the city centre to see Batam’s annual fireworks display.
Food and drink will be on sale in the streets, and you can sit at any
of the parks, the beach or bring your own chairs.

**JANUARY EVENTS**

**Batam Regatta**

Every year the tourism office holds a regatta between Singapore
boaters and Batam’s local club at Nongsa Point Mariana. Sailors will
love this event! Food and drink available at the marina. Entry is free.

**SUMMER EVENTS**

**Sea Eagle Boat Race**

The Sea Eagle Boat Race is another huge and popular event with locals and
tourists. Open to both local and international boaters, this is another
annual event not worth missing. Boaters and non-boaters will delight in
this friendly competition.

**FALL EVENTS**

**Marine Expedition**

If you head to Batam in November, don’t
miss the famous Marine Expedition. Here you can view ancient Batam
nautical displays, ships and tools. You can also read about the first
sailors that came into this Indonesian port. A hit with the entire
family!
