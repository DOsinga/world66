---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment in Tanjung Pinang"
type: section
---

**Tanjung Pinang nightlife** can be just as exciting as daytime
events, provided you know where to look. Tanjung Pinang is located on
the Island of Bintan. With over 150,000 residents, the city is growing
everyday and has become known for its tourism and trading. Nightlife in
the area has been limited to hotel bars and entertainment.
**Local Nightlife

**

Local nightlife is often limited to hotel entertainment. There are a
few places to check out during your stay. The Santai Lounge is located
in the Comfort Hotel & Resort, and offers a great atmosphere where
you can wind down and enjoy a cocktail. The Panorama bar is located in
the Panorama Hotel. There’s a café on the 4th floor that guests can
enjoy.

Be sure to visit the karaoke lounge, located in the Bintan Lagoon
Resort. Although located an hour away from Tanjung Pinang, its well
worth the trip due to all the great activities you can enjoy. You can
also relax at a lounge located in the Bintan Agro Beach Resort &
SPA. Enjoy your drinks poolside, or opt in for a stress-relieving
massage.

For a unique nightlife experience, check out an open-air bar and
disco, located right on the beach at La Luna Beach Club. Enjoy drinking
and mingling with guests as you take in the sights and sounds of the
ocean. This bar is open Friday through Sunday night.
**Nightlife in Surrounding Areas

**

Silk is a nightclub located in Bintan at the Bintan Lagoon resort.
With a modern décor and younger crowd, this is the place to be for most
tourists and locals looking for a good time. The club has an urban feel,
and live entertainment is offered most nights of the week.

You can also check out Nirwana Gardens. It has 3 different locations,
found at different Bintan Resorts. The Calypso Bar features reggae
music with a Caribbean theme, and it is located near Indra Maya Villa.
Another of the clubs is located at Nirwana Resort and it is set up as a
karaoke lounge. The final club is located near Nirwana beach and has a
disco theme

Whether your nightlife consists of relaxing at your resort or
traveling to nearby areas, make it worthwhile by planning ahead. Make
sure to book your hotel in advance so that you don’t lose out on the
opportunity to stay at one of these grand resorts. If you only get one
shot at visiting Tanjung Pinang, make it a trip that you’ll never
forget.
