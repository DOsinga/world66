**Festivals**

**National Film Day **

This festival takes place on 30 March and is celebrated by the country's film community. New films produced in the country are shown in various cinemas. A carnival is held along the main streets of Jakarta. 

**Jakarta Fair **

Jakarta Fair (usually from mid-June) is the annual seven-week trade, tourism and cultural fair. Many regions of Indonesia, ASEAN countries and international firms participate.
