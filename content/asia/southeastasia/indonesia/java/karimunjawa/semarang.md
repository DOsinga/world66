The best resource for sights, hotels, restaurants, bars, what to do and see

Another entry point to Karimunjawa archipelago by hovercraft from Tanjung Emas harbour.

One of the point of interests in Semarang is the old quarter of the city, known as **Kota Lama** [the Old City]. Where several historical old buildings standing still nowadays. Such as **Gereja Blenduk** [the Dome Church, Saint Emanuel] and **Marba** Building and the old bridge. And not to be missed are the famous **Lawang Sewu** [the Thousand Doors] and Chinese temple named Candi Gedung Batu **Sam Po Kong**. Those are becomes the landmarks of Semarang.
