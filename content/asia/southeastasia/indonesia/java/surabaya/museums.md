type: section
---

There are several museums can be visited in Surabaya. Two of them are:

- Museum Mpu Tantular. Named after the famous ancient Majapahit author, situated at Sidoarjo about 18 km from downtown Surabaya. Exhibit some of skull and skeleton of *homo sapiens* figure from Brantas delta and also homo mojokertensis. And several ancient treasure from Majapahit era [Majapahit is the last great kingdom in east Java].

- Monkasel a.k.a Monumen Kapal Selam or Submarine Monument. Exhibit old Russian submarine Yankee class plus several of diorama and documentary movies about Indonesian navy from Surabaya base.
