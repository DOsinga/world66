---
title: "Things to do in Java"
type: section
---

Indonesia is rich in culture, one of those places is Java. Central Java especially, has a variety of things to do, some of them are:

1. Gamelan,Yogyakarta Keraton, the orchestra that accompanies dance and puppet shows, singers voice blend with
instruments.

2. Jathilan, Trance dance accompanied by simple gamelan and mostly perform out door in the villages of Java. Jathilan
also well known as a kuda lumping dance.

3. Ramayana Ballet Prambanan, the story of Ramayana and shinta told in traditional in Javanese. Performed at the
open air theatre at Prambanan on full moon nights as well as other venues.

4. Wayang Kulit, Yogyakarta Keraton, leather shadow puppet show, at the Jogyakarta Keraton and other enues.

5. Wayang Orang, classical Javanese dance based on the Hindu Ramayana and Mahabrata epics, performed at the Yogyakarta Keraton every Sunday by recognized artists and students, also at Sriwedari Amusement Park in Solo.

6. Uyon-Uyon Hadiluhung, Yogyakarta Keraton, Javanese slow-rhythm song.
Throughout Java, traditional as well as regional performances are offered year’s round, to encourage the continuation of traditional art form, the royal palaces Jogyakarta and Solo offer regularly scheduled performance that are open to the public.
