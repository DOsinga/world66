Kalimantan is one of the least visited parts of Indonesia. The main reason for this is that it is pretty hard and expensive to get there. It's a long way from Java or Bali. The good thing about the distance is that Kalimantan has done a great job preserving its unique culture. 



[Banjamarsin](/asia/southeastasia/indonesia/kalimantan/banjamarsin) is the main tourist destination. It is also the ideal place to start exploring the rest of the island. [Samarinda](/asia/southeastasia/indonesia/kalimantan/samarinda) has very interesting traditional settlements.

## Destinations

- [Banjamarsin](/asia/southeastasia/indonesia/kalimantan/banjamarsin)
- [Samarinda](/asia/southeastasia/indonesia/kalimantan/samarinda)
