The best resource for sights, hotels, restaurants, bars, what to do and see

This area is known for silver crafts, located along the road until fork ex-Cinema Palace. In addition there are also Tomb of previous Mataram Kings, example Panembahan Senopati tomb (the founder of Mataram). But then the tomb of the Kings of Mataram moved to the area by Sultan Agung Imogiri Hanyokrokusumo (king of Mataram which attacked Batavia).

The atmosphere was still very traditional in this city, for example, look at the Grand Mosque complex Kotagede still feels like the Palace environment, where complete with a stone fence around the mosque berelief, spacious courtyard where there are several kecik sapodilla tree, and a large Bedug age very old, as old as the Great Mosque Kotagede own.
