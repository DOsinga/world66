---
title: "Lake Toba"
type: location
---

Lake Toba is on most itineraries of tarvelers going to Sumatra. The lake is set in the caldera of a giant volcano and it's the largest lake of South East Asia. Right in the middle there is an island Pulau Samosir, which is about the size of [Singapore](/asia/southeastasia/singapore).

 The climate is pleasant and the setting is cool as well. This makes Lake Toba a favourite get away for people living in [Medan](/asia/southeastasia/indonesia/sumatra/medan). Medan is less than 200 km away and many buses make the trip every day.
