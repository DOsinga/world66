---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment in Lake Toba"
type: section
---

The **Lake Toba Nightlife** and entertainment is full of
refreshment and excitement due to the environment and the beautiful
scenery there. There are many bars, cafes, beaches, resorts, shopping
centres, and restaurants to enjoy. The night life is not only enjoyable
for the local residents, but also for the tourists who come away with
unforgettable memories.
Batak Cultural Center

There is a cultural centre in Prapat (Parapat) that is famous for
its live music entertainment, singing and dancing. The lovely singing
events, dances and music performances are held at this cultural centre
every Saturday night, and entrance for tourists is free.

There is an annual event held in Batak Cultural center in June which
is called Festival, and many of the people near Lake Toba wait
throughout the year for this traditional occasion. Surrounding the
Batak Cultural Center in Prapat, there are a lot of restaurants,
hotels, shops, and bars that serve refreshments day and night. This
cultural center shows the history of the place while adding to Lake
Toba's nightlife and entertainment.
Danau Toba International Hotel Bar

The majority of Lake Toba nightlife and entertainment takes place in
the bars inside the hotels. One of the great bars is within Danau Toba
International Hotel. This is the best place for night time enjoyment,
with beautiful landscapes of the lake and a variety of drinks being
served.
Tabo Cottages

These cottages are situated at Tuk-Tuk Lake, near Lake Toba, and
people go there to get relaxation, good food, and enjoyment. This place
is beautiful due to the presence of Lake Toba, with a great view that
is enjoyed by most of the people who visit here. People find it a great
resort for stay and enjoy good quality, tasty food.
Cotney Restaurant

This is also a peculiar food spot of Samosir Resort. A lot of
fantastic dishes are served here. Their specialties include curries.
This restaurant also provides free Internet service to its visitors.
Jenny’s Restaurant

This is also a very good restaurant that serves a variety of food
items including fresh fish, which is served either steamed or grilled,
and other delicious Western and Easern dishes. Desserts such as fresh
papaya or golden brown banana with chocolate sauce and coconut are also
well loved specialities. This is marvelous place due to its location
near the lake, which provides exciting and memorable moments to
tourists.
