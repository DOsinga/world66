title: "Day Trips"
type: section
---

From Padang, travelers can proceed to several destination, such as: Bukittinggi [clock tower or known as Jam Gadang], Lake Maninjau, Harau Valley, Batusangkar [coffee mills], Bonjol, Kerinci Seblat National Park, Lake Singkarak and Danau di Atas - Danau di Bawah and West Coast (Sikuai Island, Pagang Island and Cubadak Island).
