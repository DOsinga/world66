**DALAM LOKA (OLD PALACE)**

This ancient palace made of wood that was built during the reign of Sultan Muhammad Shah Jalaludin III (c. 1885 BC. Currently being used / exploited as a "Regional Museum Sumbawa" a place to store things Sumbawa Regency historical objects. The palace is two twin buildings supported by columns as many as 99 large wooden pieces, according to the nature of God in Al - Qur `an (Asma` ul Husna). At Dalam Loka Palace we can see the typical motifs carved Sumbawa, as an ornament on the wooden structure.
