title: "Bars and Cafes in Cameron highlands"
type: section
---

In **Cameron Highlands Bars and Cafes** are sometimes some of the best places to go and eat. Most people find the bars and cafes great places to eat because you are able to truly experience the local culture. Not to mention that the authentic food that is served at the local bars and cafes is hard to find in bigger restaurants.

**Strawberry Moment Dessert Café**

Based on the name of the café you would assume that they only served desserts, but while desserts are a specialty of the café they also offer other choices. If you are looking for something nice and cool to drink on a warm summer day, you need to try a Chocoberry & Strawberry juice. If you are looking for something a little more filling, the Chicken Spaghetti is good. As far as desserts, you will have a choice between various strudels, but you can also try out a mousse cake.

**Starbucks Coffee**

If you are simply looking for a taste that you can find back home, Starbucks is going to be your best choice. Starbucks in Cameron Heights offers the same choices they do back home; everything is imported into the country from the United States. If you are not familiar with Starbucks, inside you will find a variety of hot drinks, including coffee and tea. You can also find snacks, coffee mugs and even whole coffee beans to purchase as a souvenir.

**Jasmine CafeHouse**

The Jasmine CafeHouse comes highly recommended. Inside you will find a variety of food to choose from. The choices of food at the Jasmine CafeHouse range from local selections to Chinese, and Western selections, which makes it easy for somebody to find something that they enjoy. One dish that comes highly recommended is the rijstafel.

**T-Café**

Despite the focus on desserts, you can find a variety of different foods at the T-Café. On the sign that hangs outside the establishment, scones and apple pie are advertised, but once you come inside you will be able to browse the entire menu. If you are looking for a light meal, be sure to try one of T-Café’s homemade soups. If you are looking for a drink, the Mango Lassi is highly recommended.

**Travellers Bistro**

If you are looking for a great place that serves Western food, you want to go to the Travellers Bistro, where you can also find authentic Malaysian food. Not only do they have a great choice in food, but they also have a great wine selection. Fruit juices come recommended with most everything that you order.
