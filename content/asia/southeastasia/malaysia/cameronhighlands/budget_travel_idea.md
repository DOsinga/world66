When you are visiting Cameron Highlands, one of the best things that you can do to get more out of your vacation is to find out about the different **Cameron Highlands Budget Travel** ideas. Getting as many ideas as possible will ensure that you can budget your money through all avenues of your trip, not just the main parts, such as airfare and hotels.

**Accommodations**

To get the best rates on hotel rooms what you will want to do is book your hotel room along with your airfare. If you book your hotel stay at the same time you book your airfare you can get discounts as high as 40 percent off your hotel stay, so be sure to look around for the best deals. You can also look into vacation rentals and hostels instead of hotel rooms. If you are going to be traveling with a group of people the vacation rental can often be the cheaper avenue because you can split the rent between all parties.

**Budget Airfare**

Airfare is one of the biggest expenses when going on vacation. Booking your own airfare through the Internet is going to be cheaper than going through a travel agent. The main travel websites will get you to Malaysia, but after that you will need to book through Malaysia Airlines to get to Cameron Highlands.

**Attractions**

When visiting Cameron Highlands be sure to stop at some of the local farms, including the butterfly farm and the strawberry farm. There are numerous things to see in Cameron Highlands and sightseeing on your own is a great way to save money. The temples are also very interesting to visit, and they are free.

**Transportation**

The best mode of transportation in Cameron Highlands also happens to be the cheapest mode of transportation. Rather than taking a taxicab anywhere throughout the city, you will want to take a bus. The best things about the buses is that they are relatively fast, which means you won’t spend hours trying to get from 1 place to another, unless you are traveling long distances.

**Dining**

Dining out is going to be another big expense while on vacation. To help save money on dining out one of the best things to do is to eat a bigger meal at lunchtime, and then have a smaller meal for dinner. You can even go to a grocery store to pick up snacks to help save on food expenses.
