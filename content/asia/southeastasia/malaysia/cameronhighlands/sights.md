**Boh Tea plantation**

**Bharat Tea Estate**

Located along the main road from Ringlet to Tanah Rata, you can have a great view of the tea estate.  

**Butterfly Garden**

A breeding place for butterflies. Many rare varieties of butterflies can be found here.

**Gunung Brinchang**

The highest peak in Cameron Highlands. Those who like hiking should not give it a miss. Satisfaction awaits the conquerer of this nostalgic peak.
