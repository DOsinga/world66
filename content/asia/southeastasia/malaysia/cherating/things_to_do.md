Windsurfing. The lagoon at Cherating is a great place to learn or improve your skills. Cost to hire equipment is RM25 per hour and instruction for an extra RM5. 

Surfing is available during monsoon months (November-March). Average wave heigh 3-5 feet. Sand under foot so very safe for beginners. Cost to hire board is Rm15 per hour and RM60 per day. Boogie boards and wave skis also available. 

Kayaking - sea and river kayaking available. Two person kayaks cost RM 15 per hour, Rm 60 per day ; single person kayaks cost RM10 per hour, Rm 40 per day. 

River Trip by Boat. Very nice relaxing trip up the mangrove river to see wildlife with the help of your friendly guide. Chance to see snakes, water monitor lizzards, otters, monkeys, birds, turtles and many more creatures of the jungle. Cost Rm15 per person for the 2 hour trip. 

Fire-fly Boat Trip at Night. Trip up the mangrove river at nght to see the lovely display of insects flashing on and off like christmas lights. Cost Rm15 per person. 

ALL OF THE ABOVE CAN BE ARRANGED THROUGH:- 

Kelaut Watersports, The Beach, Rhana Pippin Resort and at Payung Guest House, Cherating.
