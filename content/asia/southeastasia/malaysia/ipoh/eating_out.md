Ipoh is famous for its food, which Ipoh-nites claim to be the result of the "hard" water due to the geology around Ipoh. Places to eat is never hard to find here, as it seems like feasting takes place around the clock.

For a list of famous Ipoh dishes, see Wikipedia at http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ipoh_cuisine.

Another place which lists places to eat can be seen at http://www.journeymalaysia.com/MC_ipohfood.htm
