Johor Bahru is a popular destination in Malaysia. The city
has a multi-cultural population and is filled with popular attractions and
things to do. Rich in tradition and culture, Johor Bahru offers its visitors a
chance to experience first-hand its hospitality through its bars and cafés. The
task of choosing the best cafés and bars is not easy with the endless choice that
is available. However, the following 5 bars and cafés are a few among the best
that are available in the city.

Caesar Karaoke and Musical Lounge

88 Jalan Ibrahim Sultan, Stulang Laut

2nd Deck, Eden Floating Palace

Johor Bahru, 80300

Phone: +60 7 221 0333

Fax: +60 7 221 0222

This is a karaoke bar on floating barge; the bar includes a seafood
restaurant a deck below. The bar is a great place for singing and merrymaking. The entrance to the bar leads directly into a common
sitting area, where huge sofas are arranged for the singing guests. The bar
provides a variety of drinks including the standard drinks on the menu. There
is a permanent happy hour including discounted beer.

Dome Cafe

Lot J1-09

Level 1 City Square

Johor Bahru, 80000

Phone: +60 7 226 1055

http://www.domecafe.com.my/

The Dome Café is a great Italian place, set in a warm and
cozy atmosphere, the cafe is popular among many who like to come and relax and
enjoy an informal lunch. There is a wide range of dishes to choose from. The
coffee’s are of the best quality and are served either hot or cold.

Newsroom Cafe (The)

Jalan Abdullah Ibrahim

The Puteri Pacific Johor Bahru

Johor Bahru, 80730

Phone: +60 7 219 9999

Fax: +60 7 219 9998

The Newsroom Café is an informal place for business or
friendly meetings. The place is enveloped in full length glass panels with chrome
colored railings. The café serves a mix of international and local dishes. A
variety of dishes including hot and cold beverages are available.

TGI Friday’s

2 Jalan Ayer Molek

Unit L2-01 Wisma Jotic

Johor Bahru, 80100

Phone: +60 7 221 3380

Fax: +60 3 221 3814

http://www.fridays.com.my/

The popular Friday’s chain is another favorite for many in
Johor Bahru. This bar is set to the Country and Western feel and Mexican and
American dishes dominate the menu. The bar provides a variety of drinks which
sometimes includes heavy drinking. All popular brands of beer are available.

Palm Bistro

88 Jalan Ibrahim Sultan

The ZON Regency Hotel by the Sea

Johor Bahru, 80300

Phone: +60 7 221 9999

The Palm Bistro is located at one of the most beautiful
locations in Johor Bahru. You can come here for a tranquil time to relax and
have a drink. The bar serves both hot and cold beverages including beer, wine
and cocktails. The bar also provides a small variety of snacks and pastries.
