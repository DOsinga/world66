** **Johor Bahru family travel can be memorable with the unique experiences the city offers. There’s something here for the entire family.

**Kota Tinggi Fireflies Tour **
65, Jalan Bahagia,
Bandar Penawar,
Kota Tinggi, Johor
012-759 3468/ 013-755 8640

This 45-minute night boat tour will be a cherished memory for your family when they see thousands of fireflies lighting up the night over the submerged mangroves. One by one, the bushes on the banks of the Lebam River light up with tiny lights, and are best seen on a moonless night. They are more numerous after a shower.

Locals will tell you that the fireflies are a dying breed, and this is one place that conserves these insects. Travelers suggest that you time your visit during the two weekends after Chinese New Year when the area is not so crowded with tourists, who flock here to see this magical sight.

**Desaru Fruit Farm **
P.O. Box 26, 81930 Bandar Penawar,
Kota Tinggi, Johor
07-8222 333

If any of your family members enjoy gardening, or like the idea of eating wild honey and fruit plucked fresh from the tree, this award-winning fruit farm with more than 100 species of tropical fruit is a must-see. The guided tour available on site will provide you with interesting information about unusual fruits like bread fruit, passion fruit, durian and dragon fruit grown on this 100-acre farm.

The kids will enjoy petting the goats, rabbits, monkeys, ostriches, chickens and turtles in the mini zoo. They can also feed the fish in the many ponds on the farm. In fact, “fish therapy” is growing in popularity, as the gently nibbling fish are known to exfoliate and rejuvenate skin.
**
Sultan Abu Bakar Royal Palace Museum**

This 19th century opulent palace museum has some spectacular exhibits such as chairs carved from crystal, beautiful Chinese pottery and silver-plated ceramics to marvel at. Also on display are gold coins and Johor’s first postal stamps. The Sultan of Johor still holds important state ceremonies here.

**Dasaru Beach**

Also known as Pulai Dasaru Beach, this is one of the most famous in Johor and is referred to as the “Last Unspoiled Corner of Southeast Asia.” Apart from 26 km of clean sands bordered by casuarina trees, the beach is great for a swim and offers top class accommodation with fun water sports.

**Pulau Tinggi Island**

On this island with its white sands, transparent blue waters and lush tropical forests, you will feel refreshed after the hectic life of the city. Its enchanting coral reefs have a wealth of marine life, and make for fantastic snorkeling and diving. Locals believe that the spirit of the Malay hero Hang Tuah guards the island. You can spend some time on this captivating island, as there’s some good accommodation available.
