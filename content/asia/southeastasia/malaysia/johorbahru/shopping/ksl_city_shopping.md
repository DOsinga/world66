[KSL City Shopping Mal](/asia/southeastasia/malaysia/johorbahru/shopping)l opened in December'2010. It is the latest shopping mall to be located near to the Johor Bahru City and Malaysia/Singapore Immigration Checkpoint(CIQ).

It is easily accessible by cars and public transport. It cost around RM10/- in taxi fare from CIQ. There's direct public bus from CIQ to the front entrance of KSL City Shopping Mall. Parking fees cost RM1/per hour.

The main tenants are Tesco, MBO cineplex and K-Box karaoke. Infamous F&B outlets include KFC, Pizza Hut, Bread Talks, Seasons, Kim Gary, Asahi and etc. Others are FOS, Watson, Giordano, Nokia, Samsung, Maxis, Celcom & etc. You could also find pharmacy, bank, money changer, massage palour, beauty & hair salon, IT shops and etc under the same roof. There's also a kids corner for your kids to have fun while you enjoy your shopping. Last but not least, there will be a hotel by the name of KSL Resorts due to open soon.
