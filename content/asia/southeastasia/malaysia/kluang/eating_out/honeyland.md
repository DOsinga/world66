A family-owned business since 1964

**Tantalise YOUR TASTE **buds at Honeyland. High grade genuine ceylon tea, delicious homemade cakes, pies, puffs, buns reminiscent of the colonial era of English tea time, and local delights nyonya kuih, crispy fritters, nasi lemak, mee siam, noodles, fried radish cake (chai tow kuih) are all on the offering. Sandwiches and kaya toasts prepared a la minute, warm and crispy. Honeyland is located at No. 54 Jalan Lambak. opposite Wisma Bunga. A visit to Honeyland is a unique kopitiam experience. Come to enjoy a meal and soak in the muhibbah ambience of Kluang town.
