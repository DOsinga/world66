All the about the food and where to go, well I've stayed in Kluang most of my life. Its a pretty small town with the exceptional of Kluang Rail Coffee. Maybe people would not even know where the heck is Kluang. The food here is great if you know where to go. Here is a list of places worth going to.
1) Chuan Mei Restaurant - Chinese Food- Operates during lunch and dinner till around 11pm - Food is tasty 8/10 - Speciality Seafood. Some dishes are pricey though (for kluang standard)
2) Cin Cin Restaurant - Chinese Food - Operates during lunch and dinner till around 10pm - Food is tasty 8/10
- Speciality Wedding as most of the time is booked for this. Most of the food is pricey.
3) Lerk Foo Restaurant Lian Seng - Chinese Food - Operates 8pm till 3 am. Reasonable price and good food. 7/10. A good place to have supper/chinese food after a few rounds...
4) I cant remember the name of the restaurant, but its in Taman Intan opposite Prohealth Pharmacy. - Chinese Food - Food is good and cheap 8/10. Open for dinner.
5)Roundabour Restaurant - Chinese Food - Well the name speaks about where it is. beside a roundabout in Yap Tau Sah. Been there twice, some people like it some people dont. Not to saty the food is bad, but i think there are better places to go. 6.5/10. Prices are reasonable.

Now for hawkers stores/kopitiams. Well I frankly feel that Kluang Rail is OVERATED just because they have a wider market and famous name. Its not bad but there are better places than this in this little town.
1) For mamak food, you must try Warung Kas Esah in Taman Intan, recommend the mee goreng and roti... when i say roti is roti prata.. Hands down the best Mamak shop in town. Open from 7-2 am. Another mamak place worth mentioning is at Kluang Baru at the cross junction along old "Paris Massage Parlout". The Nasi lemak served in Anika Hotel is not too bad also. Worth trying.
2) FOR Nasi lemak, you should try Bob's, he have 2 branches or more like stall one in Taman intan opposite 711 and the other in Kluang Baru oppsite Myone. Quite good.
3) Kopitiam... Kopitiam plenty around other than Kluang Rail, Old Town in Klaung Parade and Klaung Mall are quite ok. The one near Rex Cinema is also ok.
4) Curry mees... Well botak have 2 outlet one china town and the other is at Kluang Baru. Both are good, another one is Yong He along Jalan Niyor.
5) Western Food, theres Barneys and there Ritz, both are below par. Barneys is more expensive and the food is slightly better then Ritz, however nothing great to shout about as this is the only 2 places that you can go for western. You dont reallly have a choice as there is no where else to go, heck i think i can cook better...if not for the cleaning up. get what i mean..

Ok guys and gurls... mine drink is nearly empty and i gotta go. so to sum it up...

Chinese Food- As above depends on price
Mamak - Kak Esah

Cheers
