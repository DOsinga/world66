---
title: "Things to do in Kluang"
type: section
---

If you are going to be traveling to the city of Kluang and are
looking for the best places to see and fun activities to enjoy, there
are many **Kluang things to do**. If you are interested in relaxing
and enjoying a great meal with live music, there are a number of fine
restaurants to choose from. If you enjoy shopping, there are a lot of
modern and unique shops and centers to find everything that you are
looking for. There are also many places to enjoy nature and learn more
about the heritage and history of the area.
**Shopping and Dining**

If you enjoy shopping, there are many great places in Kluang that
offer modern shopping facilities and also unique shops where you can
find anything that you want. There is the Kluang Mall that offers the
newest stores. There are also local shops and places to buy arts and
crafts that the locals have created. If you like to try out new cuisine,
there are plenty of restaurants and bars in the area that offer local
and traditional menus.
**Nature and Theme Parks**

If you enjoy spending time in nature, you will find a number of
landmarks and attractions in the area. The Tropical Village is a theme
park that offers fun and excitement for anyone. There are also a number
of recreational forests to see and explore on vacation in Kluang
including the Gunung Lambak Recreational forest, the Gunung Belumut
Recreational forest and the Tasik Mardi recreational forest. All of the
local theme parks and recreational areas offer tourists facilities to
poke around in and places to relax and enjoy the day in nature. Tourists
can also take in the heritage of the area and learn more about the
cultures.
**Sports and Leisure Activities**

Those who enjoy catching some sporting action and fun can find what
they are looking for at the many country clubs and stadium that are in
the area. Some of the best country clubs to see include the Kluang Golf
and Country Club, the Guthrie Golf and Country Club, the Kompleks Sukan
MPK and the Pamol Golf Club. These clubs offer fun and leisure
activities to enjoy while on vacation and have plenty of fun activities
for anyone.
