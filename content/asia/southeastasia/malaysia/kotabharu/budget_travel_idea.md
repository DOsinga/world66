If you are planning a holiday in Kotabharu, these **Kotabharu Budget
Travel** ideas will help you find the best offers and help you save
some money for other things.

**Budget Travel**

Getter there can be quite costly. That is why it is always to make
use of the budget travel options that are available. You can book a
flight with Firefly, a subsidiary of Malaysia Airlines, which offers
travel convenience for as low as RM 45.95, exclusive of tax and
surcharges. You can take a train from the Wakaf Bharu station which is 5 km away from the city; this will cost you from RM15.00 to RM20.00.

**Budget Accommodations**

Enjoy your stay in the culture-rich city of Kota Bharu with some of
best and most reasonably priced hotels and establishments. You now have
to worry no more on spending a lot on accommodations since there are
hotels that offer packages at very low rates. Try out some guesthouses
such as Cerana, Ideal Traveler’s and Lee. Staying here will cost you
anywhere from RM15.00 to RM 45.00; prices include breakfast and free
toiletries. You may also want to stay at the Hotel Rebana, which offers a
convenient location and comfortable rooms with rates ranging from $25
to $28 (in U.S. dollars.)

**Museum Hopping**

If you want to know more of the Malay culture, you can always go to
museums instead of spending too much on transportation media around the
city. Entrance fees to these places of recreation are priced from RM1.00
to RM2.00, depending on the type of museum you’re entering. This is
one of the best ways you can enjoy Kota Bharu without spending a fortune.

**Budget Shopping**

You can take a walk downtown and explore the shopping areas. You do
not have to ride a cab or a bus while exploring this place, so it’s
going to cost you nothing. It is best that you walk so that you don’t
miss out on good and cheap items. Remember to bargain to get the best
prices. There are also some cheap goodies available in souvenir stores
near beaches in Kota Bharu.

**Beach **

Head out for the beach, this again is a place where you do not have
to pay to enjoy yourself. Pack a picnic lunch and you are all set to
enjoy the whole day at the beach. Most hotels and guesthouses will
provide you a full picnic basket on request, or you can pick up some
food from the several road-side restaurants for you picnic. These don’t
cost much and are delicious.
