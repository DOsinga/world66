**Sights**

**Padang Merdeka (Independence Square)**

A rather uninteresting places, but a number of museums can be explored.

**Wat Photiwihan**

A nice Thai temple with a 40m reclining Buddha. It is located out of town at Kampung Jambu. 

address: Kampung Jambu
