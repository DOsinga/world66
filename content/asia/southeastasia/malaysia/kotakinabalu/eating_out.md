**Anjappar Chettinad Restaurant** - *Indian Specialty Cuisine *

Anjappar is an Indian franchise restaurant offering authentic South and North Indian food in Kota Kinabalu. It is right across the road from the popular shopping mall Centrepoint. The outlet in Kota Kinabalu is the Chennai, India-based group's 23rd.

Anjappar offers vegetarian and non-vegetarian set meals called Thali meals with steaming white rice with accompaniments. The restaurant specialises in Chettinad food, which is known to be one of India's most aromatic for its distinctive blends of spices. Chettinad Lamb Biriyani, Lamb Sukka Varuval (Fried Lamb), Crab Roast, and Pepper Chicken Masala are among the Chettinad favourites served in the restaurant. North Indian dishes on the menu include naans, rotis, and tandoor baked items. The restaurant is open from 11 am to 10.30 pm. Price ranges from Ringgit Malaysia 3.50 to 30.

### [@mosphere restaurant](/asia/southeastasia/malaysia/kotakinabalu/eating_out/mosphere_restaura)

@mosphere is a revolving restaurant and bar situated in Borneo's tallest building, the Menara Tun Mustapha.

The restaurant is located on the 18th floor and gives 360 degree views of Kota Kinabalu, the South China Sea and Crocker Range.

@mosphere specialises in pacific rim cuisine as well as a local Malay menu and is open for lunch, afternoon tea and dinner daily.
