---
title: "Family Travel Ideas"
type: section
---

Kuala Lumpur is a nice family holiday destination that features an
eclectic range of attractions and activities for all ages. There are
parks, malls, flea markets, entertainment centers, monuments, spas and
a whole lot of other exciting destinations to keep tourists on their
toes. Here is a super guide to have an enjoyable family vacation in the
Malaysian Capital.

**Kanhcing Rainforest Waterfall**

To modify a famous adage, a family that treks together stays
together. Get your tribe’s trekking attire in place and go for an
exciting trail through the road that leads to Keopang and Rwang on a
discovery of the lush rainforest of Kuala Lumpur situated adjacent to
the Templers Park. There are some pretty-looking waterfalls along the
walking trail along with a few rare botanical species. The parking area
has a number of snack joints including a Kentucky Fried Chicken Outlet.
Make sure you carry an extra set of clothes for a post water-play
change along with plenty of water and food. Entrance is RM 1 for adults
and an extra RM2 for car parks.

**Kuala Lumpur Bird Park**

This one scores majorly with the kids. The largest walk-in aviary in
the world features some of the most vibrant and stunningly colored
birds flying around in a natural and peaceful environment. The setting
is complete with added frills and enhancements like ponds and
waterfalls along with flower gardens and artificially created springs.
The experience of birds flying all over your head and sitting at your
feet is memorable. Visitors can indulge in a nice bird feeding session
and view a variety of birds including the mammoth ostrich.

**Suria KLCC Mall**

The swanky shopping mall housed in the basement of the Petronas
Towers is a great family recreation center that features several up
market boutiques and a well-maintained and beautifully designed
aquarium along with a games and entertainment center for children. The
sprawling food court houses a number of international fast-food
outlets. Suria KLCC is a good idea for shopping and an evening out with
the kids.

**Lake Gardens Park**

The expansive play grounds of the park coupled with a nice boathouse
featuring a range of fruit, drinks and ice-cream stalls are a hit with
the kiddies and their parents as well. The Lake Gardens Park is a nice
place to take the children for an early morning stroll.

Kuala Lumpur is a quintessential family holiday destination that
features a number of interesting attractions that cater to diverse age
groups.
