Getting to Kuala Lumpur is no problem as over 40 international airlines, including Malaysia Airlines, serve the city's new Kuala Lumpur International Airport (KLIA) at Sepang. Domestic routes are served by Singpore Airlines, Air Asia, Pelangi Airand Royal Brunei Airlines, in addition to Malaysia Airlines. Flight time from Europe is 12-13 hours.
