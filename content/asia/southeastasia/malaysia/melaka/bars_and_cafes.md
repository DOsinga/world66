title: "Bars and Cafes in Melaka"
type: section
---

**Melaka bars and cafés** provide visitors a chance to experience the perfect
combination of sumptuous food and good beer. Experience this unique side of
Melaka at one or more of the bars and cafes here. There are some bars and cafes
around the historic port of Malaysia that are popular with tourists and locals
alike. You can go bar hopping and enjoy yourself in any of these bars and cafés
in Melaka.

**Aloha
Dance Club and Bistro**

This is an international
renowned bistro that showcases Hawaii’s traditional style, food and
entertainment. Aloha Dance Club and Bistro is Melaka’s most sought after and
popular spot for having good times with friends. Aloha Dance Club and Bistro is
a modern lounge, mega dance club, bistro and restaurant rolled into one. The
club has special promos like Hula nights, Pizza Madness and Happy Hour. There
are also live bands that play every night to heat up the atmosphere.

**Green
World Bistro**

Green World is a must-visit
hot spot for the younger crowd. The place has a simple look to it and is a
great place to unwind and enjoy some green tea. The menu includes a wide range
of meals, light snacks, sandwiches, coffee, milkshakes and teas. Green World
Bistro has an inviting and comfy indoor ambiance.

**Botanical
Café **

Botanical Café is a Halal
certified restaurant located at the Melaka Botanical Garden. The café offers
local and International cuisine, one of which is the famous and mouth-watering
fish head curry. The menu includes fish sambals,
Thai crispy chicken, prawn sambals, nasi lemak, mango shake, botanical delight
and more. The interior of Botanical Café is changed every month to suit the
particular month’s festivities. The café also has private VIP rooms and is a
great place to enjoy an evening.

**Geographer
Café**

Geographer Café is located
at the center of Melaka’s Old Chinatown. Geographer Café serves as an “ice
breaker” among the historical houses of Melaka’s Old Chinatown. Geographer Café
has kept the warmth and history of the place and is considered a “living museum.” If you are looking for a unique café, Geographer Café is the right
place for you. The café is open daily and is a nice place to relax and enjoy
some authentic Malaysian cuisine.

**Famosa
Lounge**

Located in the Renaissance
Melaka Hotel, Famosa Lounge offers a luxurious and laid back atmosphere. The bar
includes a wide range of drinks like draft beers, cocktails, light snacks, international
wines and more. The Famosa lounge, just like the hotel, is lavishly decorated
with comfy couches, a grand piano and antique bookshelves. If you are looking
for a classy place to enjoy the night, Famosa is the right place to be.
