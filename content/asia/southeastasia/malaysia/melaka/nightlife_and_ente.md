Honky Tonk Haven Cafe
Nice little pub at Jalan Lorong Hang Jebat (turn left off Jonker Walk towards the river), has tables with a view onto the Melaka river, run by a musician New Zealander and his companion.

Live music (specialising in jazz and also other types of music on request). As of 9 pm every night, good pub food.
