---
title: "Shopping in Miri"
type: section
---

If you are visiting Miri, then you need to indulge yourself in the unique **Miri Shopping **experience. There is something for everyone here in this awesome destination. Here are a few ideas that you can use.
**Bintang Megamall
**

You cannot miss this mall as it is strategically located on the
highway at the city center of Miri. A great place to buy designer wear
and accessories, you will find international brands like Fila, Vincci
and The Body Shop here. The mall has a book store and is a great place to buy
cosmetics. If all the shopping has left you tired, you can relax over
lunch or dinner in the food court. It also has a pharmacy and a
department store.
**Heritage Center
**

Miri is famous for its handicrafts and traditional methods of
producing fine pieces of art. Head over to the Heritage Village, and you will find you
need not travel too far as it is located in the heart of the town.
These are several stalls that sell traditional vases, the intricately
designed Pua fabric and scarfs, skirts and tees with batik prints. This is
a great and inexpensive place to buy souvenirs for your friends back
home. While you are shopping you can also enjoy live performances and
watch batik painting artists at work.
**The Boulevard Shopping Complex
**

This shopping complex holds around 80 shops, hypermarkets and
department stores. This complex has all the fittings of a high class
mall, from designer clothing to accessories to sport apparel to
cosmetics. Plenty of eateries are evenly spread out and seating
arrangements add bliss to the whole shopping experience. The ambiance
in the complex is of opulence, with water fountains and other attractive
décor items.
**The Weekend Market **

This is a typical local market and is great place to soak in the local
culture--a shopper's gateway to heaven. This market is run during
the night and starts after noon. In contrast to the malls. this local
market at the Sarbekas center is apt to sell fashion items and
accessories. It is also famous for the toys and handicrafts. More than
170 stalls are available for you to explore. The air carries the aromas
of the BBQ chicken stands and other food stalls.
**Wisma Pelita Tunku
**

The very first shopping area in Miri is still popular amongst the
locals. It is located close to the old town. A visit to this oldest
shopping center will take you back in time, to shopping before the
advent of the whole mall concept. Even though it might not look too
appealing, you can definitely grab a few good deals here.
