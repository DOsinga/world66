---
title: "Bars and Cafes in Penang"
type: section
---

When you visit Penang, one of the things you should do is to check
out the various **Penang bars and cafes**. There are several fine
bars and cafes in Penang, which means there is no shortage of places to
sit back and enjoy a drink. Here are a few recommendations for you.

**Orange Cafe and Bar**

The Orange Bar and Cafe is one of the most popular bars in Penang.
This bar offers you a fresh approach to modernity with a bit of vintage
thrown in for style. The Orange Cafe and Bar is a great place to enjoy
an evening in Penang. You can choose to sit back and enjoy a few drinks
or you can eat, drink and dance through the night.

**Shock Egypt**

Shock Egypt is another hotspot for the younger crowd in Penang.
Shock Egypt is a techno club that offers good music and a lively
atmosphere. Shock Egypt is a great place to come with your friends; you
can enjoy a few drinks and dance till you drop! The bar has a wide range
of alcoholic drinks including some of the best beers.

**Cheers@32**

If you fancy some good whiskey, then you shouldn’t overlook this bar.
Cheers@32 is the top whiskey bar in Penang and it offers more than 100
whiskey varieties from different parts of the world. Step into this bar
and you will be floored with the choice that is available. If whiskey,
music and dancing are your thing, Cheers@32 is the place to enjoy your
night.

**20th Leith Street**

20th Leith Street is a bar that will cause your jaw to drop in
amazement. The architecture of the bar is just fantastic. The bar is a
place where you can enjoy some of your favorite drinks and some amazing
bar food. You can choose a on the terrace and relax and enjoy your
evening with a view of the Cheong Fatt Tze Mansion.

**Louis Cafe**

Louis Cafe is located in George Town, Penang. The cafe is well-known
for its friendly atmosphere and excellent food. Louis Cafe is the perfect
spot to stop by with your family. The cafe offers western cuisine, so if
you are longing for some western food, this is the place for you. You
can also enjoy a nice time of karaoke with your friends or family at
Louis Cafe.
