This is a new staging point in the state of Pahang between Endau and
Rompin and it's about 30 minutes north of Mersing on the coast. All
buses from KL to Mersing will pass this area, just ask the bus driver
to let you off at Tanjung Gemuk jetty. If you are coming from KL,
instead of exiting the North-South Expressway at Ayer Hitam and
following the Kluang route, exit Seremban instead, follow Kuala Pilah,
Muadzam Shah and along the coastal road.

There is only one ferry operating from Tanjung Gemuk. However, the
ferry only takes between 1.5 - 2 hours and they leave on time. The
ferries are comfortable and air-conditioned.

To book, contact Fast Ferry at +60 9 4131998. The return rate is RM60 for adult and RM40 for child between 3 - 12 years old.
