---
title: "Eating Out"
type: location
---

The home cooking of the Great Star hotel is recommended (+/- 1 US$). The Hupin has quite good Chinese food (try the hot and sour fish) and cold beer (7/99). The popular Four sisters has attractive decor, wonderful people, a pay-what-you-want policy, occasional live music and local Intha food. They do tone the spices down for the tourists (7/99). The Big Drum is to be avoided (7/99). Almost all tourist hotels or guesthouses offer meals. At the mingala market you can find fresh food and vegetables. There are also bakeries around the market area.
