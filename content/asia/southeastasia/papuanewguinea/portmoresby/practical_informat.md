title: "Practical Information in Port Moresby"
type: section
---

When vacationing in Papau New Guinea, this** Port Moresby practical
information** will be helpful as you get accustomed to the area and
should help you not to have any problems while on your trip. Still not booming with
the tourist trade, this small country is beautiful and unique, and armed
with this valuable information, you should be able to confidently
travel throughout the area with relatively no problems.

**Money in Port Moresby**

It will be necessary for you to change money while in Port Moresby.
The easiest places to do that are at Westpac and ANZ, both of which are
much more efficient than Bank South Pacific which is known for long
waits in line. Be sure to ask for the international desk if you plan on
exchanging cash or traveller's cheques to expedite the process.

**Safety in Port Moresby**

Port Moresby gets a reputation as a dangerous place, however, if you
practice using your common sense while on your trip, you should have no
problem. Situations can change quickly, but in most cases if you ask
locals when you arrive if some particular place is safe to travel to,
you generally can trust their estimation. It is perfectly safe to walk
around the town and Boroko throughout the day, though outside of these
places, you should walk with a local. Any secluded urban areas should
avoided at all times.

There are great views from Paga Hill,
though you should only visit there with a local. The same goes for any
of the settlements, including Hanuabada, Sabama, Kila Kila
and Six Mile. After dark, you should not walk anywhere.

**Health in Port Moresby**

There are decent services in Port Moresby if you are hurt during your
trip to the area. The Paradise Private Clinic and the Port Moresby
General Hospital offer the best services in the city. If you are having
an emergency, the Port Moresby Medical Centre is a great place for
emergency care and can arrange for a medivac if needed.

Using
common sense and speaking with the locals is an easy way to ensure that
you have a safe and enjoyable trip to Port Moresby. With little tourist
trade here, you may have to work a little harder to arrange a vacation
that is ideal for you, but it will be worth your while in the wonderful
reward of unique and memorable travel.
