title: "Budget Travel Ideas in Angeles City"
type: section
---

Here are some **Angeles City budget travel** ideas that
you can use while vacationing here. Angeles City is a booming city in
Pampanga, and it is packed with the perfect blend of rich history, urban living,
exhilarating nightlife and majestic natural adventures.

**Transportation**

Angeles City is accessible by air through the Diosdado Macapagal
Airport. However, if you want more adventure and an affordable means of
transportation, take a bus. You can either go for the ordinary or
the air-conditioned buses. It is 1 1/2-hour drive from Metro
Manila, (if there’s no traffic). To get around the city, you can ride
the Jeepney or tricycle. These are colorful vehicles that you can find
all over the Philippines. However, be careful not to loose your
belongings, especially your wallets.

**Affordable Hotels**

There are lots of hotels around Angeles City. If you are on a tight
budget, you can choose to stay at some of the budget hotels and guest
houses. Fields Avenue is one place where you can find several cheap
accommodations. Since Angeles City is a tourist destination, you will be able to find
accommodations for almost any kind of budget.

**Enjoy for Free**

Visit Angeles City during the Philippines International Hot-Air Balloon
Fiesta that takes place during the months of January and February. This
is the biggest aviation sports event in the Philippines. You can watch
hundreds of multi-colored hot-air balloons soar and race into the clear
blue skies of Pampanga. There is no entry fee to witness this
spectacular event.

**Shopping**

There are 5 major department stores in Angeles City, namely
Robinson’s Place, SM City Clark, Jenra Grand Mall, Savers Mall and Nepo
Mall. All of these stores offer great bargains. Walk around and check
the prices of things before buying. If you bargain, you are sure to get
a good price. The street markets are also good places to get bargains.

**Eating Out**

One of the best ways to save money while traveling is by not eating
at the restaurants. If you are
staying at a guesthouse, chances are that you might be able to cook your
own food. This will save you a lot of money which you can use for
something else. You can also try the food available at the street-side
restaurants. Here’s a tip, if you are going to eat food anywhere
outside, try to eat food that is freshly cooked.
