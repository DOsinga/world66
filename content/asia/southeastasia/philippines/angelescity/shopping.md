---
title: "Shopping in Angeles City"
type: section
---

This highly urbanized city in Northern
Philippines has several things in store for business and leisure
travelers alike, but one thing you absolutely must not miss out on are the **Angeles City Shopping** havens.
If you want to take home some souvenirs and memoirs of your magnificent
stay in Angeles City, Pampanga, you can visit these great shopping
hot-spots within the city.

**SM City Clark**

This is a popular shopping-mall chain that can be found all over the
Philippines. SM City Clark in Angeles City is a one-stop shop that
offers a host of things, from clothing to jewelry to handcrafted items.
It houses different boutiques as well as restaurants, an amusement
center for children, and cinemas. You can find all you need at this
shopping mall.

**Robinsons Place**

Robinsons Place is one of the newest shopping malls in the downtown
area of Angeles City, near the airport. This mall is where you
can pick up imported products with super-low price tags, as well as
local brands, gifts, and souvenirs. Robinson’s Place also houses
international and local food chains that serve Filipino, American and
European cuisines. The mall has a spacious supermarket that sells fresh
produce, meats, dry goods, and other necessities.

**AsiaLuxe**

If you are looking for great Philippines arts and crafts,
particularly pearls, take a trip to AsiaLuxe. This wonderful craft
store is located in the Clark Special Economic Zone. Some of the things
you will see displayed here include: handcrafted jewelry, jewelry
boxes, bags, trays, bracelets, necklaces, and earrings adorned with
Tahitian Mother of Pearls. You can choose from their catalog
offerings or their ready-made displays. This is a great place to pick up a gift
for your loved one if you have an anniversary coming up.

**Duty-Free Shopping Mall**

The Clark Special Economic Zone, located along MacArthur Highway, is
the place to head over to if you're looking for duty-free products. The
duty-free mall stocks several imported items and groceries like
candy bars, chocolates, canned goods, breakfast cereals, and snacks, all
at very low prices. Duty-Free Shopping Mall is also a great place to
satisfy your American fast food cravings for burgers, hot dogs and more.

**Street Markets**

One of the places not to be missed are Angeles's street markets. If you're
looking for a good bargain, these markets are the best places for you. You will find locally made products like clothing, shoes, crafts,
toys, jewelry, electronic goods, and more. This is one place you're sure
to get a good bargain, especially if you're good at haggling.
