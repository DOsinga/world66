The humble beginning as a hobby, after five years of great service and hospitality of these fascinating party people, PATs Creek Bar now turns to a MUST visit place in Boracay!

PATs Creek Bar is the only Reggae Bar in Boracay!
It is ideally located at the heart of the famous Boracay Island coastline.
We offer wide ranges of alcoholic drinks, cocktails of international standard and a wide variety of Acoustic Reggae Music by the beach!

Feel the sea breeze blowing through your face, stare at the dreadlock peeps around and let Pat's Power fuel you to party all night (u can jam with the band!).

For travelers sick of the usual crowded and suffocating city bars, this is the place to go. Sit on drift wood chairs. And if you like to lazy 'round the clear white beach of Boracay and just forget about the pressures in life; grab some iced cold beers, or fresh smelling fruit shakes and intoxicating cocktails will be served right in-front of you.

Simply lie down at the beachfront of PATs…while worshiping the sun as it sets!
