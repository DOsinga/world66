These **Camiguin Island Family
Travel ideas** should help you enjoy your holiday to the fullest. This
pear-shape island is located in the Visayan region and is considered the second smallest island in the Philippines. The island paradise is
surrounded by great beaches and immense forest vegetation that offers
wide array of activities that are family and kid friendly.

**Lansones Festival**

If you want to explore Camiguin Island, never miss out the Lansones
Festival that happens every third week of October. This is to celebrate
the harvest season of the local fruit known as Lansones. This is a
well-known celebration that draws tourists and locals alike. Your
children will enjoy the colorful parades, street dancing competitions and the beauty pageant during the festival. This is one event
that the whole family can enjoy while holidaying in Camiguin Island.

**Katibawasan Falls**

Take a trip together to the slopes of Mt
Hibok-Hibok and enjoy the majestic Katibawasan Falls afterward. This
magnificent 250-foot waterfall is a spectacular sight worth enjoying with
your family. You can have a family picnic here and also make use of the
cookout facilities and rock tables.

**Ardent Hot Springs**

For a whole new type of adventure, visit the Ardent Hot Spring right
in the heart of Mambajao. The hot springs are great for calming your
nerves and comforting your tired body and senses. The kids can enjoy
themselves at the Ardent Hot Spring because water temperature is only 40
degrees centigrade. There are picnic facilities, clean rest rooms and
dormitories so you'll be comfortable to stay for one whole day or night.

**White Island**

White Island is a place where you can enjoy the natural beauty of the
island. This Island offers you a clean white sand beach and a majestic
view of Mt Hibok-Hibok and Old Vulcan. Take a trip to White Island with
your family to relax and enjoy the beauty of the place. White Island is
located 2 km away from the coast of Agoho, Mambajao. You and your family
can enjoy swimming, snorkeling and sunbathing in a beautiful environment.

**Catarman Church Ruins**

Catarman Church Ruins is a place for your family to unwind and learn
Camiguin history together. Located in Catarman, this old Spanish church
was ruined when a volcano erupted in 1871. The walls, belfry and ruins
of the church are silent witnesses of the fury of nature.
