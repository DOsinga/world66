---
title: "Budget Travel Ideas in Cebu"
type: section
---

If you want to explore the
Queen City of the South, you don’t need to splurge because there are simple
ways to enjoy **Cebu Budget Travel**. Being
one of the best tourist destinations in the Philippines and in Southeast Asia,
Cebu boasts of its booming metropolitan district, rich culture and majestic
natural assets. If you are clueless on how you could start your exploration
here are few ideas:

**Budget
Airlines**

There are several budget
airlines that operate flights to the Philippines every day. Budget Airlines, Cebu
Pacific, Air Asia and Tiger Airways are some of the airlines that operate in
the region. Check with the airline office to get the latest discount rates.

**Passenger Ferries**

You
can also take the passenger ferries or medium size RORO (roll on/roll off)
vessels and explore the vast, open sea surrounding Cebu. This may take longer
but you will surely enjoy mingling with different people; plus it is much
cheaper than flying.

**Getting
Around in Cebu**

Once you have reached the
Cebu’s domestic port, going around the city is no big deal. You can ride the
Jeepney or tricycle (public transportation that can only be seen in the
Philippines) for only Php 10. If you are to travel a longer route you can opt
for the bus or taxi, which will cost you Php 100.

**Budget
Accommodations**

There are lots of budget
accommodations around Cebu. Normal rates per person can be as low as Php 300 to
as much as Php 1000 per night. Sugbutel Bed and Bath is one of the lowest
hostel accommodations in Cebu. The hostel charges Php300/person for a 20-hour stay. Every room has air-conditioning and Wi-Fi access.

**Eating**

Eating out is the biggest
adventure when visiting different places and Cebu is no exception. There are several
restaurants and eateries around Cebu and if you are on a budget you can stop by
any of these restaurants and enjoy a good meal. Some of the local delicacies to
try are the Adobo, Inihaw, Kare-kare and many for as low as Php50. Some of the
restaurants worth stopping by are Neo-Neo, Goodah-Gud, Tsibog and Larsian
Barbeque. Must- try foods are Cebu’s Lechon and Halo-Halo.

**Budget
Destinations**

If you are on a budget,
visiting the following places will cost you nothing. Take a trip to the different
museums and historical places like the Basilica de Sto Nino, Magellan’s Cross, Taoist
Temple and Lapu-Lapu’s Monument. There are also lots of beautiful spots in Cebu
like the Santa Rosa Falls, Mt. Manghilaw and the Olango Wildlife sanctuary that do not charge for admission.
