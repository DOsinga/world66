---
title: "Family Travel Ideas in Cebu"
type: section
---

There are many great family
travel ideas in Cebu City. Cebu is full of modernity and its one of the most up
and coming cities in the Philippines. Thanks to its location, Cebu City is a
meeting ground for business, industry and fun. Many people from other places of
the Philippines and abroad flock to this city to transact business and to
relax and take in the cultural variety this place can offer.

**Casinos**

There are many different
casinos in Cebu City. The casinos are state-of-the-art and a nice blend of
different ethnicities. The crowd is made up of Western, Malay and other south East
Asian people. While the casinos are not a place for children, adults can have
a blast enjoying their favorite games and life entertainment. The casinos often
have live music and dancers. Some of the shows showcase traditional Filipino
song and dance. This is a good place to see the folklore of this nation while
enjoying games and drinks.

**Ayala
Center Shopping**

There are many different shopping
centers tha are elegantly built. This shopping center is situated
in the outskirts of Manila and it was inaugurated in 1998. The Terraces
at the Ayala Center is a true architectural feat. This is one of the greatest
and most elegant food courts in the world. While moms could shop around the
kids and fathers can walk along the garden like structure of the landscaping.

**Casa Gorordo Museum**

Another of the
many family travel ideas in Cebu City is visiting the Casa Gorordo Museum. Here
families can catch a small glimpse of how the life was from 1860 to 1920. The
museum has a courtyard and the façade shows early colonial style influence by
Spaniard colonial era architecture. There are many different artifacts and
antiques found in this museum that was the home of the first Filipino Bishop of
Cebu City.

**Jumalon Butterfly
Sanctuary**

This place has many different kinds of rare
butterfly species. Some of these butterflies can only be seen in this place of
the Philippines. The sanctuary is a nature reserve and it is found on Macopa
Street in Cebu City. This is one of the greatest family travel ideas in
Cebu City because children as well as adults can learn to appreciate nature and
all its wonders.
