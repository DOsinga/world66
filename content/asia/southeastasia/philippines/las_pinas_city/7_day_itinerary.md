---
title: "7 Day Itinerary in Las Pinas City"
type: section
---

With its rich history and
culture, this city has a lot of things to offer that you can include on your **Las Piñas City Itinerary** list. Las Piñas is located northwest of Manila Bay
and south of Imus, Cavite. Here is an itinerary to help you make the most of
your visit to Las Piñas.

**Day
1 - The Bamboo Organ**

Visit the world renowned Bamboo
Organ at the parish of St. Joseph in Las Piñas City. This city was highly associated
with The Bamboo Organ and that’s why the local government created The Bamboo Organ
Festival, celebrated every February.

**Day 2 - Sarao Jeepney Factory**

You will see
several Jeepneys around the city. Take a trip to the Jeepney factory and you will be
able to see where and how they are made. You can visit their assembly factory
along Padre Diego Cera Avenue, Las Piñas City.

**Day
3 - Las Piñas Salt Beds**

Las Piñas was dubbed as the
“Salt Center of Metro Manila” because of the vast salt beds along the Manila
Bay and the coastal area. Although this
spot was demolished due to the construction of a new road; it was restored with
the help of the Las Piñas City government. Now you can visit this spot to learn
and experience the traditional salt-making.

**Day 4 - The Battle of Zapote Bridge**

The biggest battle of the
Philippine-American War was the Battle of Zapote. This took place in the year 1899
at the Zapote Bridge. The ruins of the Zapote Bridge still stand along the Gen.
Emilio Aguinaldo Highway close to the Manila Bay. Just a few steps away you will
find the Las Piñas Corridors; one of the city’s historical landmarks.

**Day
5 - Eating Out and Bar Hopping**

Just like the other places
in Asia; Las Piñas has a lot of gastronomical offerings that will surely
satisfy your cravings. There are lots of restaurant abound the city; the most
popular spots are Ted’s La Paz Batchoy and Barrio Fiesta. When dusk falls you
can hop around from one bar to the other.

**Day
6 - Nature Tripping**

Las Piñas has been
recognized as Manila’s cleanest and greenest city. There are several beautiful
places to visit in Las Piñas. Take a trip to the 4000 square meter mango orchard and
tropical garden of the Las Piñas Nature Church.

**Day
7 - Shopping Galore**

One of the largest malls in
Las Piñas is the SM Southmall. This mall covers an area of 205,120 square meters. You can find readymade and brand name clothing
lines, jewelry, shoes, bags and more. Use 1 day to indulge in some serious
shopping here in Las Piñas.
