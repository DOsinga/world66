---
title: "Bars and Cafes in Las Pinas City"
type: section
---

Las Pinas is a city that is located in the National Capital Region in the Philippines, and is a popular tourist destination. If you are looking for a place to go and get a drink while listening to live music or catching up with the latest news on the Internet, there are numerous **Las Pinas City Bars and Cafes** you can choose from.

**22nd Street Music Cafe**

This bar is located along the Alabang-Zapote Road in Las Pinas. If you are looking for a place to get a drink while enjoying some live entertainment this is one spot to check out. This café offers both a music bar and a comedy bar.

**Dencio's Bar and Grill**

This bar and grill is located inside of the Star Mall in Las Pinas; you can find it on the second level of the mall. In addition to the numerous drinks that they have to offer, you also have the choice of ordering some traditional Filipino dishes or even ordering grilled seafood, all while sitting back and listening to musical entertainment.

**Magic Wand**

If you want to get to the Magic Wand, your best choice is to hail a cab and tell your driver where you want to go; every cab driver in Las Pinas knows how to get there because of its popularity. Once you have reached your destination, you can try any one of their delicious pastries or breads, while sitting back in the relaxing dining room. If you are looking for something to drink you can give their smoothies or shakes a try.

**Mark's Sports Bar**

This bar is located inside the Star Mall, on the second level, and is a local hotspot. Inside the bar, you can play billiards or relax in a private lounge while listening to music and enjoying your drinks. If you come in on the weekend, you will also be offered the chance of listening to live entertainment.

**Cowboy Grill**

This popular bar is located along Alabang-Zapote Road. One of the main attractions to this bar is that it is modeled after bars from the Western world. Inside the bar, you can find good food and cold beer, but you also get the chance to listen to live bands every night you come in, unlike other bars that only have live entertainment on the weekend.
