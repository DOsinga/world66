**Las Pinas City is a budget travel** destination with its
inexpensive lodging, food and entertainment. This city, that neighbors
the country's capital of Manila, has retained its own character and
charm even as Manila's urban sprawl has threatened to swallow the small
city up. Enjoy an inexpensive trip to Las Pinas and experience the
uniquely Filipino attractions and sights here.

**Budget Activities in Las Pinas**

One of the prized draws of tourists to the city of Las Pinas is the
Bamboo Organ. Constructed by St. Joseph's first priest in 1824, the
magnificent organ is still in use today and is the centerpiece of the
Bamboo Organ Festival that takes place annually in February. Stop by the
church for a look at the great organ and experience its essence by
dropping in on a mass to truly experience the unique sound it emanates
nearly 2 centuries after its creation.

Las Pinas is also the production center for the country's ubiquitous
jeepney and here you can tour the vehicle assembly plant for a look into
how these unique vehicles are constructed. Located along Padre Diego
Cera Avenue, the production center is a must-see when visiting Las Pinas
and suitable for travelers of any budget.

Another great budget attraction is the Salt Beds of Las Pinas. These
restored salt beds allow visitors to see how salt was traditionally made
and harvested for over 2 centuries in Las Pinas and throughout the
country.

**

Budget Dining in Las Pinas**

There are countless budget restaurants throughout Las Pinas that will be
suitable for any budget conscious traveler. For the best deals, stick
with the Filipino food and restaurants. Some popular and inexpensive
restaurants include Barrio Fiesta Restaurant and Ted's La Paz Batchoy.

**

Budget Lodging in Las Pinas**

There are many reasonably priced accommodations in the city. With B&B
style and modern hotel accommodations starting with rates of just $50,
visitors can find guest houses and alternative lodging for less than
half that a night. Be sure to inspect your room before agreeing to the
charge, especially at budget accommodations, so as to ensure that the
room meets your standards.

Traveling to Las Pinas can be inexpensive and fun. A nice escape from
the overwhelming city life of Manila, Las Pinas should be included on
any trip to Luzon.
