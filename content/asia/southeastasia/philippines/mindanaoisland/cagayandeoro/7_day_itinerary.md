---
title: "7 Day Itinerary in Cagayan de Oro"
type: section
---

Cagayan de Oro is located in the North of Mindanao and is a
popular tourist destination; it is also called the "City of Golden Friendship." There
are several things that you can do in Cagayan de Oro; the following is 7 day itinerary for a possible trip here.

**Day 1****
White Water Rafting**

One of the most popular attractions in Cagayan de Oro is its
white water rafting. Spend a day white water rafting at the Cagayan River. The
river has several whirlpools, twists and under currents that make it an excellent
place to enjoy white water rafting.**

Day 2****
Mapawa River Trek**

Enjoy a day trekking at the Mapawa River; this will normally
take the whole day so be prepared. The activity involves a lot of physical
action but it's worth the effort. The trek includes a truck ride up and down a
mountain, a 30 foot high rock jump, rappelling down a cliff wall, a trek up a
waterfall and more. You will need to make reservations beforehand.**

Day 3****
Museums**

Visit the two museums in Cagayan de Oro: the Museum of Three
Cultures and the Museo del Oro. The Museo del Oro is where you can find
traditional folk culture on display, including an assortment of artifacts. The
Museum of Three Cultures is also filled with several interesting pieces of art
and sculptures.**

Day 4****
Opol Beach**

Only 30 minutes from the city, this beach is one of the
finest places to enjoy a day swimming and relaxing. It's a great spot for a
day picnic with your family or friends. You can swim, wade or snorkel in the
pristine waters of Opol beach.**

Day 5****
Macahumbus Adventure
Park**

Spend a day at this wonderful adventure park. One of the
most popular activities are the Sky Bridge and Zipline. It's an adventure that
will leave you spellbound. The park also has various other activities.**

Day 6****
Malasag Eco-Tourism
Village**

Experience the beauty and freshness of this eco-tourism
village. You can spend a day taking a walk in this village, admiring the beauty
of the unique flora and fauna, including the ancient architecture of Cagayan de
Oro.**

Day 7****
Crocodile and
Ostrich Farm**

Don’t leave Cagayan de Oro without visiting the Ostrich and
Crocodile farm. The farm is located on the outskirts of the city and is a great
place to visit with your family. You can also buy an ostrich egg and enjoy an omelet
at the farm.
