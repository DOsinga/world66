---
title: "Eating Out in Cagayan de Oro"
type: section
---

Cagayan de Oro is situated in the North of Mindanao, one of
the most beautiful places on the earth. It is famous for its attractions and is
commonly called “City of Golden Friendship”. There are several restaurants in
Cagayan de Oro offering excellent local and international food. Much of the
local cuisine consists of seafood, so if you love seafood- you are going to
enjoy it here. The following are some of the restaurants you can check out,
they offer excellent food and great service, giving you value for money.

**La Vetta Wine Bar
& Restaurant**

The Vetta Wine & Bar Restaurant is the first restaurant
to offer French and Mediterranean cuisine. French cuisine is one of the best in
the world and Mediterranean cuisine is the healthiest in the world. With this
awesome combination this is one of the most sought out restaurants in Cagayan
de Oro. The restaurant is located in the Limketkai Center as is easy to reach.
One visit to this restaurant will leave you wanting to come back for more.

**Dynasty Chinese
Restaurants**

Chinese cuisine is popular among many and is easily
available in many restaurants. The Dynasty Chinese Restaurant is ideally
located in the center of the city, making it easily accessible any time of the
day or night. The restaurant serves excellent Chinese dishes; however not much
drink is available at the restaurant. Some of the dishes to try are the fresh
seafood dishes.

**La Tegola Cucina
Italina**

This is a premier Italian restaurant specializes in Pizza,
Spaghetti, Pasta and almost all Italian dishes. The restaurant is well known
for its sumptuous food and excellent service and has a reputation for
delivering more than what you expect. The restaurant is situated in the Juan
Sia Building.

**Bucher’s Best
Barbeque**

If the smell of fresh meat being grilled is what satisfies
you, Bucher’s Best Barbeque is the place for you to treat yourself to a great
meal. The restaurant is famous for serving excellent grilled food. Try the
chopped pig’s head – it is an excellent dish. Both the Bucher’s outlets are
famous and offer great service.

**Max’s Restaurant**

Want to try special Filipino and Asian cuisine? Head over to
Max’s Restaurant – the place to get the best Sinigang Hipon, Kare-Kare and much
more. Max’s Restaurant is one of the popular chain-restaurants in Cagayan de
Oro and they have branches all over the Philippines. Give your taste buds a
delightsome treat at Max’s Restaurant.
