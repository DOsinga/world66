From Manila take the early plane to San Jose, Occ. Mindoro

its about 40 minutes ride by plane. Once your'e there take a tricycle and ask the driver to take you to Pag-Asa by the Barangay Hall and ask when you get there. Every local knows how to get there and who to contact.
