title: "Naga City"
type: location
---

Naga City is located at the Province of Camarines in the Bicol Region.
It is an independent city and is widely known as a model of local
governance.

Along with this, Naga City is also famous for Peñafrancia Festival, a
celebration held annually every September in honor of Bicol's
patroness, Our Lady of Peñafrancia.
