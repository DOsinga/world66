Wen you talk about Bacolod its not only sugar that the city can be famous for, besides the festivals and the sights, the city is also famous when it comes to dining. from sweet delicacies to very fine dining, the city does not only showcase international food from other cities in the world but also the authentic negros food like the Chicken Inasal, the different menus from Pala-Pala. When your in the City, just ask around where is a nice place to dine, sure you can find one just around the corner.
