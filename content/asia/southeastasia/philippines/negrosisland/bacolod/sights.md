### [Mariano Ramos Ancenstral House](/asia/southeastasia/philippines/negrosisland/bacolod/sights/mariano_ramos_ance)

The Mariano Ramos Ancestral House, was the the home of the first el Presidente del Municipal of Bacolod.

During World War 2, this house was occupied by the Japaneses and became the foothold for command and coordination since it had a 3-storey octagonal tower which gave a full view of Bacolod City.

Today the Mariano Ramos Ancestral House stands as historical site in Bacolod City.

type: Castles
