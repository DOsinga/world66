---
title: "Family Travel Ideas in Negros island"
type: section
---

Negros Island is made
up of 2 main provinces. One of them is the Central Visayas region and the
other is Negros Occidental and Negros Oriental. There are many good family travel
ideas for Negros Island. Here the family can enjoy the nice weather and many different activities associated with the sea and the warm sun.

**Aquino Freedom Park**

Freedom Park was named
after the great Ninoy Aquino of the Philippines. The park is situated in the
front of the main capitol building of Negros Island. It is common to see
performances and festivals that are sponsored by the local government. In the
afternoons families flock to the grounds of this park and they have picnics.
Children play various games and often school children perform their dance or
sports routines in this park. People should note that because Aquino Freedom Park
is a family-oriented place alcoholic beverages are not permitted.

**Antulang**

Now, for those lovers
of adventures the southernmost part of Negros Island has lots to offer. Here the family can
go deep sea diving. Kids (if they are fond of the water and dolphins, of course)
can swim with the dolphins. The older kids and parents can enjoy a great
horseback ride. Now, for the very adventurous kayaking is a possibility. It should be noted that while this activities are tons of fun, safety
should be the number 1 priority.

**Silliman University**

Silliman University is
a higher education institute of the Philippines. It has 3 museums, the Museum of Anthropology, the Marine Mammal Museum and the Gonzalez Biology Museum. The museum has different exhibits that are used to show the prominence
of indigenous tribes in the Philippines. The museums are a way of promoting the
cultural identity of the Philippines. Here children can learn about nature, the
marine life of the species found in the Philippines and the culture of
this country.

**Mount Talinis**

This place is also
referred as being the horns for Negros Island. There are many lakes in the
basin of these peaks. There are also 2 twin crater lakes in Balinsasayao. One
of the best family travel ideas in Negros Island is visiting Mount Taglines
and climbing the nature trails that start in Aplong and Valencia.
