---
title: "Beaches in Olongapo City"
type: section
---

The best way to enjoy the
calm and clear waters connected to South China Sea is through **Olongapo City beaches**. Located in the western coast of Central
Luzon, Olongapo City boasts of its beautiful and grandiose bodies of water. The
beaches’ clear, turquoise water surrounded by clean white sand and lush
tropical forest is a perfect summer hide away for locals and tourists alike.

**Subic
Bay **

It may be well known for the
ex-US naval base (now Subic Bay Freeport Zones) but Subic Bay has a wide
stretch of beautiful white sand beaches along its shore. Popular beaches and
resorts within the bay are White Rock Beach Resort, Camayan Beach Resort,
Arizona International Hotel and Resort, and Johan’s Beach Resort. All these
beach resorts are popular for their special activities.

**Half
Moon Beach**

Half Moon Beach is located
along the National Highway. It has number of bamboo cottages brilliantly
located along the shore lines. The great thing about this is that you can enjoy
this white side beach without spending much for entrance fees because it’s for
free. This is a perfect place if you are looking for some good budget ideas in
Olongapo City.

**Dungaree
Beach**

Dungaree Beach is another
popular beach in Olongapo City. The beach is a popular spot for group events
and parties. The beach can accommodate 500 people and can cater to beach
parties and retreats. They charge Php150/person for entrance fee. You do not have
to worry if this is a good place to enjoy a retreat or party. The authorities
make sure that the beach is clean so that guests can enjoy themselves here.

**Driftwood
Beach**

If you are looking for a place
to enjoy your holiday on the beach, Driftwood beach is a good option. Driftwood
beach is popular with tourists and locals alike. The beach has several cottages
for rent behind the seawalls. Driftwood Beach is located across the 164 PNP
Camp along the National Highway.

**All
Hands Beach**

All Hands Beach is also
known as “everybody’s beach”. Earlier it was a popular get away for navy
cadets. Today, the beach is open to all and can accommodate 1,500 people. There
are also several colorful cottages by the shore. If you are looking for a place
to enjoy a beach picnic, this is the right spot to be. You can also pack your
own picnic basket or you can ask your hotel to prepare a nice picnic basket for
you.
