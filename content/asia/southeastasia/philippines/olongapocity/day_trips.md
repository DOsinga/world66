title: "Day Trips in Olongapo City"
type: section
---

Olongapo City is one of
Philippines’ top tourist destinations; it has different must-see and must-try
offerings especially for **Olongapo City
day trips**. Situated north of Manila, it is one of the youngest
municipalities in the country but has become highly urbanized and well-developed in a short span of time. If you are planning to visit Olongapo City
don’t miss out on these must-visit spots that will keep you busy and occupied
all day long.

**Mt.
Pinatubo Trail**

The huge eruption that occurred
in 1991 has created a majestic and breath-taking scenery and highly vegetated
land. The Mt. Pinatubo trail is a 2-hour hike from the foot of the mountain
to its magnificent crater. Within the crater you can explore the Nature Park
and the lake in the middle of the Mt. Pinatubo crater against the huge volcanic
rock backdrop. A great experience for those who want to see the 2 different
faces of Mother Nature; beauty and destruction. Afterwards, you can try the hot
spring spa and Mt. Pinatubo mud spa therapy.

**Tree
Top Adventure**

If you are brave enough you
might want to try the Tree Top Adventure or the “Superman Ride.” This adventure
starts with a canopy ride to reach the top, over 100 feet high. You will be required
to wear body gear and a helmet for protection. Then you will “fly” through the
zip-line face first just like Superman, hence its name. You can choose to continue
your nature trip with the basic jungle survival training with the native Aetas.

**Zoobic
Safari **

Take a trip to the most
popular wildlife destinations in Olongapo City, the Zoobic Safari. The Zoobic
Safari has vast collection of exotic and wild animals like tigers, snakes,
crocodiles and lizards. Sturdy, enclosed safari Jeepnies are used to transport
visitors around Zoobic.

**Ocean
Adventure **

Spend a day enjoying the Ocean
Adventure Park. The park showcases marine animals like dolphins, whales and
seals. These marine animals perform different acts to entertain the crowd. Another
main attraction in this theme park is the Ocean Discovery Aquarium that has a
huge display of mysterious and astounding marine environment and creatures.
Ocean Adventure is the first and only open–water Marine Park in Southeast Asia.

**Pundaquit
**

Pundaquit is one of the best
water sport destination in Olongapo; it is located in San Antonio. Other
popular destinations include Crystal Beach Resort, River Mouth Break, Capones
Island and the hidden resort of Punta de Uian. If you are into diving, some of
the best locations are the El Capitan wreck, Barges wreck and Lighthouse Reef.
