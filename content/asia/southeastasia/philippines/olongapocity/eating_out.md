title: "Eating Out in Olongapo City"
type: section
---

The **Olongapo City restaurants**
provide visitors a chance to enjoy some of the best cuisine in the region.
There are several fine restaurants in Olongapo City, all of them reflecting the rich culture of the land and giving their visitors something to
remember for a long time. Here are a few of the best restaurants in Olongapo
City.

**SIBIL**

Take a trip down to this restaurant located at the beachfront
on Pier One. This restaurant specializes in local cuisine and is a popular
hang-out for tourists and locals alike. The restaurant offers a spectacular
view of the beachfront and is a great place to relax and enjoy the evening over
a few drinks and a sumptuous dinner.

**Seafront Restaurant**

Located in Subic Bay, this restaurant is one of the best in
the area. The restaurant has a 5-star ambience to it and provides a
breathtaking view of the Grande Island. The Restaurant, as its name suggests,
serves high quality seafood. Allow yourself to be treated like royalty at this
restaurant.

**Dryden Restaurant**

This restaurant is owned by Leah Dryden and her husband Thomas
Dryden. This is one of the best restaurants in Olongapo City and is very reasonably
priced. While you are here, try the fillet mignon, you will be glad you did because it
is very tasty and prepared to perfection. The food is cooked by Thomas himself
and he takes pleasure in making sure his guests are completely satisfied.

**Pier One**

If you are looking for a restaurant with live music, Pier
One is the right place to be. The restaurant has a live band that performs and
you can request any particular song you like--they will be glad to play it
for you. The restaurant is a great place to enjoy the night with live music
over a tasty dinner. The food in the restaurant is priced between $21 and $30.

**Mango’s Seaside Grill
& Restaurant**

The Mango’s Seaside Grill & Restaurant is very popular
among the tourists in Olongapo City. The restaurant serves some tasty fresh
fish. Try the tamarind soup with prawns and fresh vegetables. You can also go
in for the grilled chicken, ham and cheese with vegetables. The restaurant is a
vibrant place that is filled with excitement over dinner. You can enjoy your
dinner in a lively atmosphere at the Mango’s Seaside Grill & Restaurant.
