---
title: "Getting There in Olongapo City"
type: section
---

If you are traveling to **Olongapo City, getting there **can be
accomplished in a number of ways. With transport available by air, bus
or sea, you have many options for your travel to this great destination.
Manila is the gateway for travel internationally to Ologapo City, and
flights are available daily from a number of international destinations
and airlines.

**Getting there by Air
**

There are limited domestic flights from Manila to Subic Bay
International Airport, which serve Olongapo City. There are also charter
flights and seaplane flights available with pickup generally arranged
in Manila, though service from other islands and cities is available.
Travel agencies at the airport can arrange this transport for you when
you arrive or you can prearrange a flight from home.

**Getting there by Bus
**

There are two bus lines that provide daily service to Ologapo City.
Saulog Transit and Victory Liner bus companies both provide transport on
a hourly basis from early morning to the afternoon on an hourly basis
from the Manila area. Take Saulog Transit from Cavite City or Victory
Liner from Pasay City, Sampaloc, Cubao or Kalookan.

**Getting there by Sea
**

There is ferry service connecting Manila's CCP bay terminal to Orion,
Bataan. Contact Interisland Tours and Travel to book a land transfer
before taking this ferry service to ensure transport to Ologapo City.
The ferry is air conditioned and provides a nice view of the seaside on
the trip.

With so many options, there is a way to easily get to Ologapo City
that fits your budget so that you can begin enjoying your time there on
the beautiful beaches.
