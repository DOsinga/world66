---
title: "Sights in Olongapo City"
type: section
---

**Olongapo City sights** include a range of interesting attractions
such as beaches, jungles, museums and aquariums. Olongapo is a modern city with a population of over 227,270 people. It was
occupied by the United States navy before gaining independence in 1959.
The city has implemented crime and cleanliness programs which are
internationally renowned. The city's development and management programs have won
it many awards, making it a pleasant tourist destination to visit.

**Beaches**

There are many beautiful beaches to visit in Olongapo and with the
city's programs in place the beaches are kept clean. Baloy is a note
worthy beach that is popular among tourists. A suggested activity is to
take a picnic and watch the breath taking sunset at Baloy Beach.

**Jungle Tours**

The jungle boasts an abundance of sites that are a must see for
visitors to Olongapo. Guides offer information about the jungle and the
animals that live there to increase your knowledge of the surrounding
area. Tours that teach mountaineering and survival techniques are also
available. Pamulaklakin Nature Park has jungle tours where
the Aeta tribe share jungle survival skills with visitors. On your trip at Pamulaklakin Nature Park you will be taught
necessary skills such as how to find a safe place to sleep and how to find
food to eat in the jungle.

**Aquarium**

Visit the aquarium to see a variety of fish species and observe their
habits in their natural environment. At the aquarium there are shows
performed with dolphins, which is a good activity for children. It is
possible to see whales and sharks at this aquarium.

**El Kabayo Equestrian Center**

The El Kabayo Equestrian Center is located in Subic Bay Freeport. The stable has many top quality horses for professional riders as well
as trail rides for tourists. The trail rides offer a good way to
explore the jungle and there are rides to Kabayo Falls. Rides typically
last for 1 hour. There are rides available for different age groups
and different skill levels.

**Friendship Park**

Friendship Park is located near the Subic Bay Freeport and Perimeter
Road. This park is a good place to relax and get away from the hustle
and bustle of the city. There is also a playground for children located
at this park.

**Olongapo City Museum**

Olongapo City Museum was founded in 2003. The artifacts on display at
the museum offer more information on the history of the city. There are
exhibitions of beautiful artwork that illustrate the local culture.
When entering the museum visitors can see the Ulo Ng Apo statue.
