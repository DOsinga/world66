There are many sites to see and activities to enjoy in **Olongapo City. When to go**
depends on the weather conditions and events that are being held in the
local area. Olongapa originated as a fishing village and during the war
it served as a naval base. Over the years since Olangapo gained it's
independence the area has grown in popularity among tourists.

**Climate**

Olongapo has a tropical climate. The temperatures do not vary greatly
from season to season. Temperature averages range from 25 C to 28 C.
November until February is the cool season when mild temperatures are
experienced in the area. Temperatures at this time are normally in the
range of 25 C and there is a minimal amount of rain. The
weather from March until May is warm and temperatures are in the 28 C
range. The wet season is from June until October. During the wet season
there is a high level of rain and monsoons may occur. There are often floods during this season. The rain and floods can make it difficult to travel and you may
have trouble getting around Olongapo City if there is excessive
rainfall. The rain also hinders sightseeing activities and activities
such as the Tree Top Adventure and Zoobic Safari. The surrounding
mountainous area is good for hiking and outdoor activities which are
not possible when there are heavy rains. Scuba diving conditions are
also not ideal during this time. It is therefore best to visit Olongapo
City from November until February when the weather becomes mild and dry.

**Events**

Olongo City celebrates many festivals. Festivals give tourists a better
understanding of the local culture and traditions. The vibrant
festivals are a fun to join in with and it may be worth your while to incorporate a festival into your trip. Sto Nino festival is held in
January and centers around Jesus. The feature of this festival is
parades where hundreds of pictures of Jesus are displayed. Sto Nino festival includes activities which do not center around religion. There is a significant Chinese population in Olongapo City and
therefore Chinese New Year is celebrated in the area. This is held in February. The festival lasts for a few days featuring colorful dances,
parades and delicious food. If you
want to know more about Olongapo's history, June would be a good month
to visit as Independence day falls in this month. There are many
parades including military processions. The end of September hosts a tourism
week which celebrates the local culture. During this week there are promotions for
tourists and a variety of activities are arranged.
