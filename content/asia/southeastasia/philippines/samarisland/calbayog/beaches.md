---
title: "Beaches in Calbayog"
type: section
---

Along the northern curve of Samar
Island is Calbayog.
This provincial city is sought by travelers each year, all wanting to
indulge in natural beauty and easy escapes. Forests, mineral waters and
white shores all blur; and they offer an adventure that is truly one of a
kind. Follow **Calbayog beaches** and discover a world captured in
the past... and still reveling in it.

**Naga Beach**

Waiting
a mere 10 minutes from the city center is Naga Beach. This charming
stretch of coast is ideal for quiet mornings. It offers grand views of
the island and its many attractions.
Spend the day in solitude--hike, swim and enjoy. It’s close to the city
center and it’s relaxing. It must be noted, though, that this is not
the right destination for those seeking sports such as surfing or
diving. It is tailored best for families, especially those with young
children. Go to Naga for a languid experience, not a thrilling one.

**Malajog
Beach**

Discover the South Sea with
Malajog. Of all Calbayog beaches, this is perhaps the most famous
because it is frequented by the rich and playful. Its long sprawl of
dark sand makes it unique and a city favorite. With mazes of stone and
trees spaced along the waves, it becomes an obvious treat. It is located
not quite an hour from town. Islands
sit just beyond, offering perfect excuses for sailing. Caves provide
secrets and opportunities for exploration. Guests craving a more
challenging day will think this is the best beach. There is much to do
and much to appreciate. Crowds will be heavy during the entire year.
Plan well.

**Bagacay Beach**

Stroll to this
resort-flavored coastline and experience the season. With picturesque
scenery, Bagacay Beach is a true paradise. Spend your morning
accompanied by cool breezes and slow waves; and then, when the evening
comes, make the simple transition from sand to streets. Because those
not content to devote their entire day to touring will find this a
convenient alternative. Nearby shops and vendor stalls should appeal to
shoppers. Bagacay is best for those in need of diversity.

Calbayog
beaches offer a rare gift--the chance to satisfy all. No individual
will be without a thrill, no family will be without an adventure. There
is instead a shoreline for everyone, and each is certain to delight.
