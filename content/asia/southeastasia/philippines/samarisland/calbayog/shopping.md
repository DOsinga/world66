---
title: "Shopping in Calbayog"
type: section
---

There are not many **Calbayog Shopping** centers in Calbayog.
However, you will be able to get almost all that you need including some
items to take home with you after your holiday. Here’s where you can do
some shopping.

**Public Market**

The Calbayog Public Market is the major shopping area in the city.
You will be able to find several different kinds of goods here at the
market. The market is the main place for fresh fruits and vegetables, as
well as meats, fish and poultry. On the other side of the market you
will find locally made clothing, jewelry, accessories, shoes and bags.
There is also a part dedicated for crafts and artworks made by local
artists that you can bring home as souvenirs. This is one place you can
use your bargaining skills to get the best price.

**Pier One**

Pier one is located within the main port of Calbayog City, which is
accessible by a “trike-ride”. You can see different sizes of fishing
vessels unloading their catch from as early as 2 a.m. until the sun
rises. This is the place you can buy the freshest seafood like prawns,
crabs, squids, oysters and fishes like sardines and mackerel caught
straight from Samar Sea. You can then ask your host (in hotel or resort)
to cook it for you the Calbayoganon way.

**Pasalubong Stores**

There are lots of Pasalubong Stores in downtown Calbayog’s, most of
which don’t have particular names. This is the place where you can buy
local delicacies such as calamay, bibingka, puto-bungbong, biluko,
tinapa and candies made of jackfruit and pili nuts. All these products
do not cost much and can be given as gifts to your friends and families
back home.

**PCRx Computing Solutions**

This is an electronic retail store situated right in the city center
of Calbayog. PCRx Computing Solutions sell and repair electronic
gadgets, computers and its peripherals. This store is the place where
you can pick up some computer parts at half the price you would normally
pay back home. So if you want to buy affordable electronics gadgets for
yourself or your family, PCRx is the best place to go if you are in
Calbayog City.

**Analyn Fuenteblanca Mushroom House**

For a one-of-a-kind shopping experience, head over to Analyn
Fuenteblanca’s Mushroom House. This shop is located in Baranggay
Capacuhan in Calbayog City. The store does not just sell mature
mushrooms but also sells fruiting bags and offers seminar and trainings
for those who wants to venture into mushroom production. If you are a
foodie or you simply love mushrooms, Analyn Fuenteblanca’s Mushroom
House will serve as a haven for you.
