This cool new French restaurant offers the finest cuisine in a
fantastic ambience. As well as canapes, the restaurant offers four,
five and six course menu's as well as a la carte dining. Lunch menu's
are also available. As well as delicious food, the restaurant boasts a
large selection of fine wines from all over the world. The cosy yet
stylish atmosphere is matched by excellent, friendly service. As well
as indoor dining, there is an outdoor chill out area ideal for
cocktails and canapes or for catered events. The restaurant will move premises to 26 Kandahar Street at the beginning of August and will feature new menus and a new style of dining experience.
