Relatively expensive but it is a one stop food festival for your taste buds. Ranging from sweet potato to **ostrich meat** this restaurant has it all and to further enhance yoru experiance at Mama Africa Restaurant and Wine Bar, the decor consists of the unpolished timber floors, hand-painted walls, woven basket lampshades, and recycled tables and chairs - add to the ambience and help you get into the African beat.
