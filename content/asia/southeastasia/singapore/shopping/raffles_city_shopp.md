Just take the subway to City Hall station where you can shop till you drop. Robinson Deparmental Store and Mark & Spencer are located or you you like to read, get books from MPH bookstore, one of the oldest bookstore . If you are hungry, just head to the 3rd floor, where you can savour local delights like chicken rice, laksa..... Take the city link from the station to Esplanade, Theatres on The Bay.
