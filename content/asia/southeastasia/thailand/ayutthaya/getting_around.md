title: "Getting Around"
type: section
---

The best way around is on one of the many tuk-tuks or songteaws. You
can even get them to do a full-day package. You can also cycle around,
since the terrain is rather flat. Once in the historic centre, you can
even walk from site to site.

### [Elephant Ride](/asia/southeastasia/thailand/ayutthaya/getting_around/elephant_ride)

You can take an elephant ride around the ruins. This gives you a
different vantage point, and how it must have been like when Ayuthaya
was capital.
