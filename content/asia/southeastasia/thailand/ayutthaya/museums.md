**Chao Sam Phraya National Museum**

The museum is located on Rochana Road, opposite the city wall. It houses various antique bronze Buddha ifnages and famous carved panels.A receptacle at the Thai Paviflion contains relics of Lord Buddha and objects of art more that 500 years old.
