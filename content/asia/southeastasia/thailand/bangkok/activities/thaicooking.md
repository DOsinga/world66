There are quite a large number of courses to attend. Probably the best, but rather expensive is the Oriental Hotel Thai Cooking School. They offer five-day courses under the direction of a well-known chef.n Phone: 439-7587, Soi Oriental, Thanon Charoen Krung. Also the Landmark Hotel offers a five-day course. Phone 254-0404 ext. 48. For alternative possibilies contact a travel agency.
