In its own exclusive space on the Ground Floor of Siam Paragon Center, Belguardo, with Creative Cuisine by [Marco Cammarata](/asia/southeastasia/thailand/bangkok/thechef.html), lives up to its name - a "beautiful vista" where food, ambience and service offer discerning guests a distinctive dining experience for lunch or dinner.

Glass and space give Belguardo an airy openness, where [diners](/asia/southeastasia/thailand/bangkok/restaurant_dining.html) can relax and enjoy Chef Marco's expert culinary creations. Chef Marco himself presides over the open kitchen, where he and his well-trained staff prepare his dishes "a la minute" with the finest and freshest of ingredients.

On the mezzanine, is Belguardo's [Wine Lounge](/asia/southeastasia/thailand/bangkok/restaurant_winelounge.html), serviced by an extensive [wine cellar](/asia/southeastasia/thailand/bangkok/winecellar.html) featuring a range of labels to suit every preference, as well as a [list of wines available](/asia/southeastasia/thailand/bangkok/wine_list_menu1.html) by the glass.
