Patong Beach Phuket

Patong Beach Phuket Island. Patong Beach is Phuket's liveliest beach area. Patong beach has a long and wide stretch of beach that fronts this popular and busy beach resort area that offers swimming, water sports, shopping, restaurants, entertainment and nightlife. Beches to the south of Patong beach include Karaon beach and Kata beach. Phuket Island has a wide choice of beaches.
