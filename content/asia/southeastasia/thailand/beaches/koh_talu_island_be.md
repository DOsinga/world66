Koh Talu is a small and natural island in Thailand off the coast near [Bang Saphan](/asia/southeastasia/thailand/bang_saphan) and Ban Krut on the Thai Sunrise Coast of the Gulf of Thailand. No nightlife or shopping, this a a quiet and natural island for lazing on the beach, snorkeling, beach games, kayak and great Thai seafood. [Koh Talu](/asia/southeastasia/thailand/koh_talu_island/koh_talu) Island is an ideal island if beach hopping or island hopping down the coast between [Bangkok](/asia/southeastasia/thailand/bangkok) or [Hua Hin](/asia/southeastasia/thailand/huahin) and the Gulf of Thailand island of Koh Tao, [Koh pha-ngan](/asia/southeastasia/thailand/kohphangan) and [Koh Samui](/asia/southeastasia/thailand/kohsamui). Excellent for a get-away-from-it-all small Thai island.
