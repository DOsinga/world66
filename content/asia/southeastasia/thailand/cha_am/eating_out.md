**Crawfords Restaurant and Irish bar**

What a find! Food is fantastic, staff friendly and music very Irish! Have no idea how they can produce such large portions at such low prices!

The owner is an Irishman who has been in catering for over 30 years so standards are VERY high… especially in the Guinness department!!

The atmosphere was very relaxed. Suitable for all the family!!! They even have a free internet access!

They offer a free taxi from your hotel and back again at the end of the night! All you have to do is call 032471774.
