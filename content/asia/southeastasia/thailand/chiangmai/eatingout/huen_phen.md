Huen Phen is a good, authentic northern Thai restaurant serving local Cuisine. . They of course have the famous kao soi, Chiang Mai's yellow noodle curry. It serves great good sai ooua - Chiang Mai sausage, sii kroung moo - barbecued ribs, gang huang la - pork and garlic curry. Sticky rice is of course the rice of choice there. It is open in the mid day for lunch and the more romantic garden area is open in the evening for dinning the fare are the same.
