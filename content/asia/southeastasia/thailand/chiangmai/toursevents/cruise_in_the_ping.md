The source of the Ping
River is in the mountain
chains in Chiang Dao district of Chiang Mai province.

There were many records written by foreigners referring to journeys along the
Ping River In the second half of the 19th century, for example, journeys in
1863 and 1866 by Daniel Mcgilvary, a missionary who had a very important role
in Lanna Kingdom.

If you want to know more about a brief history of Ping River while you are cruising,Scorpion Tailed Boat is suitable river cruise.

There have a guide in the boat to show you the old picture and describe the place which located near the river bank.Some building used to be the palace of Chiang Mai's King but you will not know that it used to be the important place of Chiang Mai.Because of the location and the body of the building.

The fresh wind blow to your face while cruising.It peaceful and relax.Slowly float in the Ping River will make you stuck on it.

Cruise last for approximately 1 and half hours.After finish the trip.There have a yellow mango and sticky rice for the customer at Scorpion Tailed Boat Village.You can find some herb and fruit at the resort.It's very interesting place.Over more there have a local toys which made by wood,bamboo and coconut.

Transportation car is prepared for the customer.They will send you to your hotel after finish the trip of pick you up at your hotel.Scorpion Tailed Boat River Cruise is the best for historical retrospect on The Ping River.
