title: "Getting Around"
type: section
---

**Best ways to travel inside the cities:** By *tuk-tuk* bus - in cities like Pattaya - By *Taxi-meter* in Bangkok, because of "not so fresh" air

The best way to travel (if possible) is by water. Many types of boat, and fresh air makes travel marvelous, especially in the hot day.

Renting a car is not recommended.
