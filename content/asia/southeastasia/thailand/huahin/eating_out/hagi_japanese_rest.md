Japanese food at it's best in the
**Hagi Restaurant** (located on the corner of Damnernkasem Rd. and
Naresdamri Rd.)

The **Hagi Japanese Restaurant** features daily performances of
traditional and modern Japanese cuisine. Located on one of Hua Hin’s busiest
street corners, it affords nice views of the bustling Hua Hin nightlife.
Japanese Chef Wuttichai prepares sushi to perfection, and the sashimi is
both fresh and delectable. Signature dishes include the Beef Teppanyaki Live
Cooking Station and the town’s favorite sukiyaki Japanese hot pot.
Terrace Air conditioning Non smoking area Days closed : Midday : Monday,Tuesday,Wednesday,Thursday,Friday,Saturday,Sunday Opening hours : 15:00-23:00
