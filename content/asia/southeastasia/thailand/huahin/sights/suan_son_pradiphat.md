Fringed with pine trees, Suan Son Pradiphat is a peaceful, lovely beach, where you can get away from it all.
Wanna get away from the fast pace of life? Get rest at the tranquil beach of Suan Son Pradiphat or Sea Pine Tree Garden. Though it is not as popular as Cha Am or Hua Hin, the beauty of the garden is comparable, talking of its stretching white-sand beach amid tranquil atmosphere.
Shaded with lots of pine trees, the seaside destination was named Sea Pine Tree Garden or Suan Son Pradiphat. This beachside area belongs to the Army Welfare Office, originally established to be the retreat for soldiers and veterans. The beach is less crowded and in secluded location, comparing to other beaches in the neighborhood. Visitors can take some rests and relax under the shade of pine trees, and let the cool, steady breeze blow away all stress, while listening to the sound of the waves.
Sea Pine Tree Garden is at Km. 241, some 8 kilometres south of Hua Hin on Highway 4.
