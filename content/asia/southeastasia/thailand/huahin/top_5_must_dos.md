---
title: "Top 5 Must Do's"
type: section
---

Hua Hin is one of the best destinations for relaxing vacations. Days
can be spent lazily sitting on the beach or in a spa. The charming atmosphere
of the town is infectious and will help you relax and
have fun. It may be hard for you to decide what you should do when
you’re in Hua Hin. This is a list of the “Top 5 Must Dos” that will go
over all of the most exciting and worthwhile activities so you can take
the most out of your trip.

**1. Hua Hin Beach**

The beach is probably the number one attraction of the area. With
quite places that are made for relaxing on the sand and listening to
the gentle waves to packed crowded places that are great for sports.
Some sports include windsurfing, para-sailing, banana boat, and speed
scooter. You might also enjoy a nice horse ride on the beach. Hua Hin
Beach is a great place for families and sports. There are lots of
places to enjoy beach games like volleyball or other ball type games.
It’s also good for children to be able to spread out without getting
lost.

**2. Spas**

There are many spas in Hua Hin that attract a lot of people. Some
luxury hotels and resorts have spas on the premises that they open up
to the public but there are also some great private spas that you can
enjoy. Take some time for a facial treatment or massage therapy. You
can also get beauty treatments like hair treatments and manicures or
pedicures. One of the best spas in the area is Chiva-Som Spa which is
in the Chiva-Som hotel. It’s a very well renowned spa with great
service, food and spa therapists.

**3. Seafood Restaurants**

When eating in Hua Hin you have to try some of the seafood
restaurant. These can generally be found near the beach. Some of them
hang out over the water supported by stilts that shoot up from shallow
waters. The fish is fresh every day brought in from the fish catchers
in the morning. They offer fantastic views and romantic settings but
also delicious food. The best place to look for seafood restaurants is
on Naresdamri Road.

**4. Other Attractions**

Other good places to visit are the Night Market which is a good
place to get good food and Golf is very popular here with 7
professional golf courses.
