---
title: "Sri Nakharin Dam"
type: location
---

Situated not very far from Erwan waterfall, a huge dam was built for a hydroelectric powerplant on the river Kwai. The traveller to this area can spend a night on the beautiful lake nearby which has some quiet resorts, raft houses and floating houses. A great place to watch the fishing activities in the villages while enjoying a healthy breakfast in the middle of the lake.
