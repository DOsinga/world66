---
title: "Koh pha-ngan"
type: location
---

This island just north of Koh Samui in the Gulf of Thailand is being highly developed though it lags a few years behind Koh Samui or Phuket—it’s actually still possible to chance upon a deserted beach there. Reef snorkeling is popular and you can make a day trip to Ang Thong National Marine Park for snorkeling and relaxation amid beautiful scenery. On the northeast end of the island Bottle Beach offers a quiet place to stay and trekking to a beautiful waterfall. On the south part of the island things can get quite a bit more crowded—every full moon brings a full moon festival and thousands of visitors. 280 mi/450 km south of Bangkok.
