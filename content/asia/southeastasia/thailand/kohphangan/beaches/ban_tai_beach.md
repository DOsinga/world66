title: "Ban Tai Beach"
type: poi
---

**Ban Tai** beach is one of the longest stretches of golden sand on Koh Phangan. Located just south of the port of Thonsala the beach offers miles of weaving coastline that can be walked in almost its entirety at low tide joining up with Ban Kai and in the very far distance the Haad Rin Peninsula.

There are many places to stay at Ban Kai and some of the finest are **[Rung Arun Resort](/asia/southeastasia/thailand/kohphangan/accommodation/rung_arun_resort)**which offers large grounds, excellent bungalow design and prime beach front location at an affordable price.
