**Sri Thanu** is a volcanic peninsula that is positioned on the west coast of Koh Phangan within 5 minutes of Thongsala. There is a coral reef offshore that is easily accessible for swimmers. The bay is pictureesque and offers a range of accommodation including [**Loyfa Bungalows.**](/asia/southeastasia/thailand/kohphangan/accommodation/loyfa_bungalows)
