---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment in Koh Samui"
type: section
---

Most of the **Koh Samui nightlife** hot spots are highly
concentrated in the island’s entertainment metropolis known as Chaweng.
Here you will find a great numbers of bars, discos and clubs that offer
great entertainment until 2:00 in the morning.

**Green Mango Club**

Green Mango Club is one of the biggest and most popular disco clubs
on the island of Koh Samui. It is located in Soi Green Mango which is
the center of the vibrant Chaweng Beach district. Green Mango Club has a
unique tropical tree house design that features an open-air dance floor
and multiple bars. Here you can enjoy sumptuous food, great music, a
big screen TV and non-stop partying. The Green Mango Club also serves as
a venue for huge parties and events on Koh Samui Island.

**Reggae Pub**

Reggae Pub is one of the biggest and most established clubs on Koh
Samui. It is situated at Chaweng Lake. This pub has traditional Thai
architecture with a contemporary classical twist. Every night local DJs
play solid reggae music and by midnight the famous Italian DJ Sergio
takes over. Here you can relive the old days, drink some beers and party
all night long. There is also a small souvenir shop and cinema within
the pub’s premises.

**Tropical Murphy's**

Tropical Murphy’s is an Irish pub situated at Chaweng Beach Road. The
pub is a great place to enjoy live band performances every night and
live sports on TV. You can also play billiards and feast on delicious
Irish dishes and draught beer. Tropical Murphy’s definitely delivers
Irish-style night entertainment on the tropical Island of Koh Samui.

**Q-Bar Samui**

Q-Bar is a sophisticated and trendy nightclub situated on the slopes
of northern Chaweng. This is a place where you can enjoy the magnificent
night view of Chaweng Lake together with some amazing night
entertainment. Q-Bar serves sumptuous seafood dishes, homemade pizza,
Angus beef and other high-class cuisine. There are also wide selection
of fine wines, cocktails and ice cold beer. Q-Bar also hosts special
events and parties.

**Zico's Brazilian Grill and Bar**

If you are up for an ultimate Brazilian entertainment, then Zico’s
Brazilian Grill and Bar is the best place to go. It is located in the
southern part of Chaweng, right next to Soi Calibri. At Zico’s you can
enjoy the unique Latin American flai-- from the mouth-watering foods to
the lively music and dance shows as well as the vibrant interiors. This
is one hot-spot not to be missed.
