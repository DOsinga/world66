title: "Hin Lad Waterfall"
type: poi
---

The Waterfall is located two km south of Nathon Town. It is approx. a 3-4 kilometer walk down a country foot walk. In evergreen, rain forest setting with palms and creepers, the waterfall have several levels with a cool pool for a fresh water swim.
