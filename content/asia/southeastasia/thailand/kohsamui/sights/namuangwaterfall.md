---
title: "Na Muang Waterfall"
type: poi
---

Na Muang Waterfall is situated 10 km south from Nathon at Ban Thurian. There are actually two waterfalls. Na Muang one is 18 meter high and can be reached by vehicle. Na Muang two is about 80 meters high and can only be reached by a 30 minute walk. These waterfalls are rated the most beautiful on Ko Samui island.
