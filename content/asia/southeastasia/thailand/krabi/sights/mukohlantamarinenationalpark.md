Located in the southern most point in Krabi province, this park covers the greater area of Lanta Island and numerous surrounding islands.
[Koh Lanta](/asia/southeastasia/thailand/kohlanta) Yai - the main island - is the National Park's headquarters and is the home to several communities of the Chaolay or Sea Gypsies.
