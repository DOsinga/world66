---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

Thai Airways flies to Mae Hong Son from Chiang Mai three to five times daily, depending on the season. The fare is about US$25 and the flight takes half an hour. Some prefer to fly least one-way as it is a very winding road and an eight hour trip by bus to Mae Hong Son town. The mountain scenery and viewpoints are beautiful, and a self-drive car hire is a good option.
