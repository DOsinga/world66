**Phu Klon Country Club Health Mud Spa**

Located 10 minutes out of **Mae Hong Son** town on the
road to **Pai,** a natural spring and mud source was discovered here. The
health club offers a variety of beauty and skin treatments using naturally
sourced mud rich in minerals. Here you can have a mud facial, mud body mask,
mineral bath, Thai massage and for gold enthusiasts there is also a golf range.
