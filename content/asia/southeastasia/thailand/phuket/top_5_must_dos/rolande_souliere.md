Just recently been to Phuket and my recommendations would be:

1. Stay at Kata Beach

2. Eat at Mom Tri's Kitchen-excellent contemporary Thai food. A bit pricey but food and view are fantastic. Suggest booking a reservation for best water views.

3. Night time markets in Phuket town. Be sure to eat there as the food is super cheap and excellent freshness due to the high turnover. Shopping is pretty fantastic. Book a tuk tuk return otherwise its may be difficult and more expensive to return to Kata beach or thereabouts.

4. Go on an all ATV through the rain forest. It's a lot of fun and they are pretty easy vehicle to ride. You may even see a monkey or two.

5. Have a pedicure with those flesh eating little fish. Its the weirdest sensation ever.
