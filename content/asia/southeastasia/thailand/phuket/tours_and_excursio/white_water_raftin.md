Snorkeling is something everybody, young and old, can do and enjoy. As a family activity it’s outstanding! All you need are fins and a mask allowing even the beginner a glimpse at the amazing undersea world. It's possible to snorkel around some of Phuket's beaches, but the best reefs are found around the offshore islands. Charter a boat or join a trip to find the best spots. Snorkelling guides will give you basic instruction on how to use the equipment, and take you through another world...
