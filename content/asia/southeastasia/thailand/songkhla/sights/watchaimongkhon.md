---
title: "Wat Chai Mongkhon"
type: poi
---

Wat Chai Mongkhon is located on the corner of Petch Mongkhon and Chai Mongkhon Roads. A pagoda enshrines a highly revered Buddha bone relic that was brought to the temple by a Buddhist monk called, Na Issaro. The monk visited Sri Lanka in 1892 and was presented the relic by a rich merchant.
