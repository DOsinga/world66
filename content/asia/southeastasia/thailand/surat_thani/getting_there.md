---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

Surat Thani Airport is served by domestic flights from Bangkok by Thai Airways, One-2-Go and other airlines.

Surat Thani also has a rail station with frequent trains North to Chumphon, prachuap Khiri Khan, [Hua Hin](/asia/southeastasia/thailand/huahin) and Bangkok. Trains operate also to the south to [Hat Yai](/asia/southeastasia/thailand/hatyai) and onwards to Malaysia.

Surat Thani is located close to the main highway, in addition to the highway North and South this is where there is also a road to krabi and Phuket.
