**BA BE LAKE** **Code: N BB 01A **
(3 days/2 nights) Daily departure
**Day 1: Hanoi - Ba Be Lake**
AM.: 7:30 Depart for BaBe Lake, an mountain area, which is nearly 250 km far from Hanoi. Arrive on afternoon. Check in hotel. A short visit around. Overnight at Hotel in BaBe.

**Day 2: Ba Be Lake**
AM: Boat trip and trek to Visit BaBe : Nang river, grotto Puong, waterfall DauDang. Visit ethnic village, namely PacNgoi, where visitors can get to know about Tay People. Enjoy daily meals of ethnic minorities. Overnight in Hotel.
**Day 3: Ba Be - Hanoi :
**7h30 Back to Hanoi. More stops on the way, visit more minority village. Stop in ThaiNguyen for visiting the Vietnam Minority People Museum. Arrive Hanoi at 15h00. End tour.
