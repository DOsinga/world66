---
title: "Cuc Phuong"
type: location
---

**Cuc Phuong** is the largest national park in Vietnam. It is home to flora and fauna now unique to Vietnam. This area of fragile beauty is protected by local authorities with assistance from organisations like the Zoological Society of Frankfurt.  A guest house built in the park and special excursions under expert guidence help preserve the habitat while enabling limited numbers of visitors the opportunity to discover this tropical jungle refuge.
