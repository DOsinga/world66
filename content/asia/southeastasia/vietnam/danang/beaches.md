---
title: "Beaches in Da nang"
type: section
---

Vietnam offers
some exceptionally good beaches of romantic, picture postcard quality with
clean white sand and warm, pleasant water that is great for swimming. This
guide will inform you all about the best **Da Nang beaches** you can access.**

China Beach**

Just 10km from the city of Da Nang is China Beach, located near a former
US army base where American soldiers fighting in the Vietnam conflict would
enjoy their R & R. There are two splendid restaurants: Sandy Beach Resort and
Hoa's Place, where rooms are available from US$5 and the beer is cheap and cold.
Backpackers often comment that Hoa's is easily the best accommodation in all of
central Vietnam. Near China Beach is the impressive Marble Mountain, which makes
a great half-day guided tour.**

Han Nam Beach**

Perhaps the most chilled out of Da Nang Beaches, this stretch of lovely sand
is located conveniently close to the coast road. If solitary relaxation is your
thing, then this is the place to be: you can often get 3 or 4km of beach all to
yourself. The Nam Hai Resort is the standout place to stay here, with rooms
ranging from the very cheap and basic to deluxe villas that cost $300 per
night.**

An Bahn Beach**

Be prepared to travel a little further and enjoy the busier and more
happening beach of An Bahn. Eat delicious Vietnamese food at the bamboo
restaurants, such as Tuyet and La Plage, and check out the authenticity of a
genuine traditional Vietnamese fishing village nearby. At Tuyet Resort you can
use deck chairs for free and drink beer in the sun all day long. La Plage also
plays movies, and has yoga and fundraising events, so you'll never get bored here. In the morning you can watch the locals enjoying
a pre-work swim. In the late afternoon when the sun is going down, you can
participate in a game of volleyball.**

Cua Dai Beach**

Another of the great Da Nang beaches; you'll need to travel 25km or so from
the city centre to enjoy this one, but it's really worth the trip. This is the
beach for watersports, with perfect waves and conditions for body boarding,
windsurfing and just plain old surfing. Surfboards and body boards can be
rented at Palm Garden Beach Resort, which also serves delicious local food such
as Pho soup, the national dish.
