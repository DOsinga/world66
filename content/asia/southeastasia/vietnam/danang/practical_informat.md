If you are planning on visiting Da Nang, there are some facts you should know in order to make the most of your vacation. Below is some **Da Nang practical information** about the city's currency, language, population, culture, geography, and climate.

**Population**

The population of Da Nang, Vietnam is about 887,069, according to the census of 2009. It is expected to reach up to one million by the year 2014.

**Geography**

Da Nang is the third largest city in Vietnam, and one of the most important ports in the country. It is surrounded by mountains on one side and the East Sea on the other side, and is famous for its natural beauty and historical sites.

**Climate**

Da Nang's climate is tropical, with occasional monsoons. There are two seasons, one wet and one dry. The wet season starts in August and goes through December, and the dry season lasts from January to July. Humidity is usually about 84 percent in this tropical city. The highest temperatures are June (28-30 degrees Celcius), and the lowest are in December, January and February (18-23 degrees Celcius). The average rainfall is about 2500mm in October and November.

**Currency**

The official currency for Da Nang in Vietnam is *dong,* which is used throughout the country. The United States dollar is equal to 14,000 dong. Travelers can exchange US dollars and other currencies for dong in banks.

**Languages**

The official language for Da Nang is Vietnamese. It is the mother tongue for their people. English is the second language which is taught in schools. There are also other languages like Chinese, French, Russian, and tribal languages like Kradai, Cham, and Malayo-Polynesian spoken by some people.

**Economy**

Da Nang is an industrial zone and has a port facility. Its economy relies upon industries such as seafood, furniture, household goods, clothing, and tourism.
