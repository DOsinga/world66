**Halong Bay 01 Day - Daily ****code: HL 01B **

World Heritages in Vietnam and recognized by UNESCO in 1994. There are thousands of stone islands with marvelous shapes floated on the surface of the sea. You can take a boat trip to see landscape and visit some beautiful caves.

**AM: 8:00 **Our bus Pick you up at your Hotel or our office. leave for Halong.

Short break on the way (20 mins). Arrive in Halong at 11:30. Get on boat, lunch on boat. Start cruising to visit landscape of Ha Long Bay (5 hours boat trip). Stop on the bay to get inside and visit Thien Cung and Dau Go caves.

**PM:** Enjoy sceneries on Ha Long bay. You will see floating fishing villages to know more about local people’’s life. Swimming and taking photo. Back to harbour at 16:00. Get on bus to return to Ha Noi. Arrive in Ha Noi at 19:30. Tour ends.

**Hoa Lu – Tam Coc Code: NB G01 **

**1 day tour - Everyday **

In Ninh Binh province, Hoa Lu Ancient Capital (in Truong Yen Commune, Hoa Lu District now) was chosen as the first capital of Vietnam feudal centralism regime and the Dai Co Viet ’s political, economic and cultural centre with the name of Kings Dinh Tien Hoang, Le Dai Hanh and Ly Thai To in 10th century. In 1010, King Ly Thai To moved the capital from Hoa Lu to Thang Long (Hanoi now), opening a new era for Vietnamese people.

Ninh Binh owns beautiful Tam Coc, Bich Dong, Dich Long, Hoa Son, Tien caves, Van Trinh Grotto and other special landscapes

**Tour Programme**
**AM**: 7h45 Bus Pick up at your hotel in Hanoi. Depart for Hoa Lu (Ninh Binh – 2 hours on bus), enjoying the rural scenery along the road. Stop to Visit Hoa Lu old citadel with Ding & Le king temple. Keep going to Tam Coc. Lunch in a restaurant.
**13:00** **PM **take a rowing boat along the tiny canal among the rice fields to visit Tam Coc (Three Grottoes – for 2.5 hourss). Boat trip back to harbour at 15:30 PM. Bus return to Hanoi. Arrive at 18:00 PM. Ends tour.
