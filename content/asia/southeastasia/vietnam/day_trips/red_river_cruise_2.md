After breakfast, we drive to the Chuong Duong Do Wharf for embarking on the boat. Start your cruise along Red River where you'll fall in love with the romantic scenery on both banks. At 9:30, disembark to visit Dam & Dai Lo temple, and then continue our cruise toward Chu Dong Tu Temple where you will feel very happy to hear a very interesting fate love story about a poor fisherman named Chu Dong Tu and the princess of Hung King Dynasty named Tien Dung (Devine Beauty). After happy hours at Chu Dong Tu Temple, we'll get back to the boat for another cruise up to the Red River. Lunch will be served on the boat. After lunch, our guide will take you to visit one of the oldest traditional ceramic village named Bat Trang. Finally we get the last cruise back to Hanoi. Arrive in Hanoi at 16:30, enjoy the cyclo touring around the Old Quarter and Hoan Kiem Lake with a stop at Thang Long water puppet theatre for your attendance at this unique art performance. After the show, it's your free time to walk around the city for sightseeing and shopping. Overnight in Hanoi.
