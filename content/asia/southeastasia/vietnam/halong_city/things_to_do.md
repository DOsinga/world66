**HL-KA 03 : HA LONG KAYAKING** ( 2 days/ 1 night )
**Overnight in Halong city**

****

**Day 1: Hanoi -Halong Kayaking** (** L, D)
**AM : 8h00 pick up from hotel. Depart to Halong Bay by Bus. Arrive at 11h30,Get on boat for cruising.Lunch on boat.
PM : Cruising on the bay, Stop for visiting ThienCung, Daugo and Luon caves. Relaxing at a desert beach for swimming and Kayaking. Back to the hotel in the evening. **Overnight in HaLong**.

**Day 2: Halong Kayaking - Hanoi (B,L)**
8h00 : Have breakfast at the hotel. transfer to visit Tuan Chau island by bus. Swim at the beach, lunch at the restaurant.
1:30 PM: Return to Ha Noi. Arrive Hanoi about 16h00. Ends.
