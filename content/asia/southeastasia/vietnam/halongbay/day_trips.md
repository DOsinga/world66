**Halong Bay Day Trip From Hanoi ** **Itinerary :**
We start at 08 00 - 08:30 am for our drive to Halong City,
approximately 3 hours away. Once at Halong City you will board our
private boat for a four-hour cruise of this stunning area, stopping
along the way to visit Thien Cung & Dau Go caves.

As we cruise our boat staff will serve you a sumptuous seafood lunch on boat.

Continue our cruise, we pass several floating villages. Upon request,
we can stop the boat in the middle of the bay for some swimming.
