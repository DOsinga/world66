Arrive Surprise Cave and explore the by bay kayak, paddle to Luon cave, this cave is a natural tunnel thrusts through a mountain’’s foot and leads to an exotic lagoon surrounded by mountains covered by dense jungle. 12.00 Return to Halong wharf, get on land and transfer back to Hanoi. End the trip.
