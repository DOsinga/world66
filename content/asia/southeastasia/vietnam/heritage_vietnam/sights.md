WORLD HERITAGE ROAD OF VIETNAM
*********
HUE- PHONG NHA- DA NANG- HOI AN-DA NANG
(4days-3nights)

Day 1 : Hue Arrival (L,D)

Meet at Phu Bai Airprot and transfer to the hotel. We will then visit Dong Ba market and the Imperial Citadel from where the Nguyen Dynasty ruled between 1802 to 1945. Enjoy dinner and listen to Hue Court music on Perfume River.
Overnight at the hotel in Hue City.

Day 2 : Hue - Phong Nha - Hue (B,L,D)

Today, we drive to Quang Binh Province. En route we will stop to visit Hien Luong Bridge, Ben Hai River, the 17th parallel. After lunch, we will take a boat cruise on the Son River to discover Phong Nha Cave. Return to Hue City for overnight stay.

Day 3: Hue – Da Nang – Hoi An (B, L, D).

After breakfast, we take a morning boat ride on the Perfume River to visit a Sampan Village,Thien Mu Pagoda, Minh Mang Tomb. After lunch, we will leave Hue for Hoi An and stop on the way to visit Lang Co Beach, the spectacular Hai Van Pass, Cham Museum, home to Vietnam’s largest collection of Cham treasure, and the Marble Mountains. Arrive in Hoi An for check into the hotel. Have dinner and rest. Overnight in Hoi An Town.

Day 4: Hoi An – Da Nang Airport (B, L).

In the morning, we will do a walking tour to visit Hoi An Ancient Town, visit the Japanese Covered Bridge, the Pottery Museum, Tan Ky old house, the Cantanese congregation, silk street in Hoi An, the temples and pagodas of Chinese appearance. Free for shopping until transfer to Danang Airport for next destination. Tour end here.

HUE-BACH MA NATIONAL PARK- LANG CO-HUE
(2 days - 1 night)
Day 1: Hue-Bach Ma (L, D)
We will pick you up at your hotel. Ride to the top of the national park for check in at the hotel. Trekking to the summit of Bach Ma, we will see Hai Vong Dai (Sea-view tower) with the beautiful panorama of mountains, lagoons and East Sea. Then walk to the Five lakes and swim in the cool stream water. Enjoy lunch at the garden in the park. In the afternoon, we will continue on Rhododendron and Pheasant trails. Go back to hotel and have dinner.
Overnight at the hotel in Bach Ma Park
Day 2: Bach Ma-Lang Co-Hue (B, L)

In the early morning we will depart from the hotel. Transfer to Lang Co Beach which has clear water, pristine sands, beautiful scenery and is an interesting place for sun bathing. Have lunch with fresh seafood.
In the afternoon, we will return to Hue City and drop off at the hotel.
