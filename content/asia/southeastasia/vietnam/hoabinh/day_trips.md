**MAI CHAU TOURISM INFORMATION**
In search of beauty
By Mary Anne Ostrom
Mai Chau has long been a destination for travelers in search of beauty. The French soldiers, or so the story goes, would make special trips from their outpost at Dien Bien Phu in the far northwest reaches of Vietnam to the village ome 200 miles away. They didn ’t come to take advantage of the breathtaking scenery of the surrounding mountains, but rather the beautiful women of the Tai hill tribe who inhabited the serene valley.

**MAI CHAU - HOA BINH Code: MC G02
(2 days/1 night) – Depart from Hanoi**
**Day 1 : Hanoi - MaiChau ( L,D)
**Am : Departure from your hotel in Hanoi at 8h00. Stop on the way for esight seeing and taking picture. 11h30 arrive Maichau. Lunch at the Thai village. Leave Man Duc to Muong village where you can do a short walk for 2 hours. then drive on to Mai chau with few stops on the way for taking pictures.
Go around the valley before having dinner at a stilt house of Thai minority village. After dinner, enjoy the traditional music show performed by local artists. sleep on stilt house at Lac village.

**Day 2 : Mai Chau - Hanoi (B,L)
**Am : 8h00 Breakfast. Short walk along the valley for 3 km to visit several Thai and Muong villages. Luch with Vietnamese food and return to Hanoi . Stop on the way to take the photos at the Hydro Electronic Central in HoaBinh. 17h00 PM Arrive Hanoi. Ends tour.

**Hoa Binh - Mai Chau Trekking & Home Stay Code: MC G03
3 Days / 2 Nights – Depart from Hanoi**
**Day 1:Hanoi –Maichau (L,D) **
8:00 AM Pick up at your hotel in Hanoi. Drive to Hoa binh( about 2 hours). Short stop for break and coffee. Drive on to Man Duc for lunch. Leave Man Duc to Muong village where you can do a short walk for 2 hours.
then drive on to Mai chau with few stops on the way for taking pictures.
Go around the valley before having dinner at a stilt house of Thai minority village. Night in the village. Clean and comfortable matress, blanket and pillow are provided.

**Day 2 Maichau Trekking (B,L,D) **
Whole day trek. 8:00 AM Transfer by bus to Sam khoe village, some 20 km further from Mai Chau. Start trekking through villages. Enjoy beautiful, peaceful scenery along the path. Picnic lunch en route. The trek takes about 6 hours to get back to Mai Chau with a lot of stops on the way. Dinner and night in the village.

**Day 3 Maichau -Hanoi (B,L)**
After breakfast drive back to Hanoi. Tour ends at your hotel around 12:00.
