There is much for the tourist and traveller to discover in Hue and the surrounding countryside. The imperial walled city know as "The Forbidden Purple City" is a major landmark and quite unique in Indochina.   The Citadel and Thien Mu Pagoda are of special architectural note. Many tourists enjoy a boat trip down the Perfumed River which includes visits to imperial tombs.
