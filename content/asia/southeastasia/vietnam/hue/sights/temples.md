**Sights**

**Thien Mu Pagoda. **

This pagoda overlooks the south-west bank of the Perfume River, around 4 km south of the railway bridge crossing. This was the home of Thich Quang Duc, made famous by the photo of him burning to death in protest to the Diem regime.
