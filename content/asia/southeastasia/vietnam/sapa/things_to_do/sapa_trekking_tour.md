**SAPA TOURS Code: SP G02 **
**2 Days/ 3 Nights** **– tour with a group
Depart from Hanoi / Daily**
**Night 1: HA NOI – LAO CAI **
20h30 ( Depart for Sapa tour ) our guide will come to pick you up at your hotel for the night train depart at 22h00 to Sapa. Overnight on train.

**Day 1 (B,L,D): LAO CAI – SAPA (B,L,D) **
Arrive Lao Cai train station **at 6:00 Am. **
**OPT 1:**
Our guide picks you up and transfer by bus to Sapa town ( 1.5 hours ). 8:00 Am check in Hotel. Breakfast and free at leisure. After lunch Our guide takes you to visit Sapa town and have a trekking 5 km to visit Catcat Village. Meet and talk with the local people to learn more about their daily life. Free in the evening. Overnight in Hotel.
**Day 2: SAPA - LAOCAI -HANOI ( B,L ) **
After Breakfast. Have a long trek 15kms through tropical forests. Follow the small paths to visit ethnic minority villages- Y ning ho, Lao chai & Ta van. Meet and talk with the Local people – H’ mong, Dzay, Dao…Take in the stunning scenery combined by rice terrace fields along Muong hoa stream where the ethnic minority is living. Have picnic lunch on the way. 2:00 Pm Our Jeep take you back Sapa town . Free at leisure to relax and discover Sapa town by your self. 4:00 Pm Bus take you back Lao cai train station for the night train back to Hanoi.
**OPT 2**
**Day 1 (B,L,D): **
Arrive Lao cai train station at 6:00 Am. Transfer by Bus to Sapa town. Breakfast. 10:00 Am start discovering Sapa by a long trek (15 Km) through tropical forests & the villages of the local ethnic minorities, watching their daily life, meet and talk with the local people. Stay overnight in Tavan village at a Dzay family (Home-stay). Picnic lunch on the way and dinner in the village.
Next morning – Day 2 (B,L,D): After breakfast, the Guide leads you to discover more culture & landscapes before our Jeep take you back Sapa town at noon. Have lunch and free at leisure. 16:00 Pm transfer by bus back to Lao Cai station for the night trai back to Hanoi.
**Night 3:** Overight on the Train, Arrive Hanoi train station at 5:00 Am. Trip ends.

**SAPA – BAC HA TOUR Code: SPB G05
**Visit Sapa Town - Cat cat Village – Lao chai - Tavan - Giang ta chai – Bac ha Market
**3 days / 4 Nights – Depart from Hanoi by Night Train**

**Night 1: Hanoi- Lao cai – Sapa**
20:30 Pm pick you up at your hotel or our office by taxi to Ha Noi railway station for the night train to Lao cai town.

**Day 1( B,L,D ): Visit 4 villages by trekking ( 12 kms ) **
6:00Am arrive Lao cai train station. Take a Bus trip for 1.5 hours (40 kms ) to Sapa town at around 8:00Am. Take a rest, Breakfast. 10:00 Am start trekking to the Black H’ Mong ( Cat cat ), Walk around, visiting and seeing the daily life of the local people in the village & hydro electric power station ( built by the french in the old days ).follow the paths along the Valleys between mountains to get to Lao chai Village. Enjoy the overall pictures of the Village among terraces. Visit H’Mong’s Living in wooden & bamboo houses. Keek going to Tavan village to visit Dzay minority. Stay overnight in Tavan village at a Dzay Family. Picnic lunch on the way and dinner at the Village.

**Days 2 ( B,L, D ): Visit 3 villages by 5 hours trekking ( 7 kms )**
Get up early in the morning to enjoy the fresh air of the moutainuos area and watch the interesting daily life of the ethnic minorities in the village. Have breakfast. Follow the road to Giang Ta Chai village of the Red Dzao minority via the paths between terraces and through bamboo forests. Visit silver waterfall & suspention, rattan bridge. Meet & talk with the people in the village and then cross Muong hoa stream up to the moutain to visit the last village of trekking – Supan village of the H’mong. Get back to Sapa by jeep. Enjoy visiting the acient stone circles on the way back where stones are decorated by carvings. Check in Hotel and Overnight in Sapa Town.

**Day 3 (B,L,D): Sapa – Bac ha - Hanoi **
Breakfast, 6:00 AM bus take you to Bac Ha Market. Enjoy beautiful landscape on the way with 2 hours on Bus. Walk around and visit colorful market of Bac Ha all day (only on Sunday). Lunch in a restaurant. 4:00 Pm Bus back to Lao Cai train station for the night train back to Ha noi.

**Day 4: Arrive Hanoi station at 5:00 Am. Trip ends. **
