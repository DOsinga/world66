title: "Practical Information in Anyang"
type: section
---

Anyang Hotel and An Cai Hotel are two of the best known luxury Anyang hotels. Anyang Hotel is in No.1 Youyi Road, Anyang, Henan Province. It is located on a busy road, has seventeen floors, not including its two-story high basement. There is also a business center, a shop, a tea bar, a dining hall, and a beauty center. An Cai Hotel is in Middle section of Zhongzhou Road, Anyang, Henan, China. It is only 2km from the birthplace of Oracle Bone Characters. Anyang Feiying Hotel is a budget hotel that you can find in Anyang. Anyang Feiying Hotel is in No.119 Renmin Avenue, Beiguan District, Anyang, Henan, China. There are eleven restaurants near this hotel.
