---
title: Getting There
type: section
---

Jeju is well connected by air and sea. Gimpo Airport in Seoul has near-hourly flights (about one hour); most other Korean cities also have direct flights. International flights arrive at Jeju International Airport. Overnight ferries from Busan take about 14 hours; ferries also run from Mokpo (about 5 hours) and Wando. The ferry terminal is in Jeju City.

On the island, buses cover the main roads but run infrequently to more remote areas. Rental cars, scooters, and bicycles are widely available and are the best way to explore at your own pace.
