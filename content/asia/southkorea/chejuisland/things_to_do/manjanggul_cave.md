---

One of the longest and best-preserved lava tube systems in the world, stretching about 13.4 km. A 1-km section is open to visitors, passing through dramatically shaped underground chambers. At the far end stands a 7.6-metre lava column, one of the largest in the world. Part of the UNESCO World Natural Heritage Site. Open daily; modest entrance fee.
