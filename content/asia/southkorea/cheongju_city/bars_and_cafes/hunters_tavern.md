Hunter's is a big bar on the 2nd floor right in the downtown core of Cheongju. It's got a pool table, a big screen TV with a playstation and loads of space to sit if you don't fancy a sit at the giant bar. They have all manner of cocktails, and their bar staff puts on a nightly cocktail show on the weekends. Be sure to order your drinks before the show starts, though.

To get to Hunter's take a taxi to Shinae (downtown) YMCA and keep your eyes peeled. It's just up the street from the Y, and the two are separated by a large-ish park.
