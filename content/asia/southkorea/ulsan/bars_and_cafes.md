---
title: 'Bars and Cafes'
type: section
---

Ulsan has a surprisingly good cafe and bar scene spread across its main districts. Seongnamdong is the foreigner hub -- Holly's Coffee almost always has a familiar face on the veranda, and J&J Coffee down a side street near Shigaetop serves the best drinks in town in beautifully designed premises. In Samsandong, the Lotte and Hyundai department stores have decent cafes on their upper floors, and Be-Bop Jazz offers live music. Mugeodong, near the university, is packed with student-friendly options worth exploring at random. For bars, the foreigner-operated Tombstone and Benchwarmers are popular in Seongnamdong. In Bangeojin, the Korean Union and the Elephant and Castle serve as locals for British engineers working at the shipyards.
