---
title: 'By Road'
type: poi
---

Korea's well-developed expressway network makes driving to Ulsan straightforward. From Seoul, take the Gyeongbu Expressway south toward Busan and exit at Eonyang junction. From Gwangju, the Namhae Expressway connects via Wonyang junction. Express buses from Samsandong terminal run frequently to most major cities, with Seoul about six hours away in good traffic.
