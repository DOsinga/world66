A frozen yoghurt chain with two locations in Ulsan -- one on the main covered shopping street in Seongnamdong, another near Ulsan University in Mugeodong. The yoghurt is low-fat and topped with fresh fruit, with portions running 7,000-10,000 won. The pastel-coloured interiors make a pleasant place to cool off in the Korean summer heat. Not ice cream, as the many wall slogans insist -- it is fruit yoghurt, and it is tasty.
