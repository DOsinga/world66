---
title: 'Things to Do'
type: section
---

Ulsan spreads its activities across widely separated districts, so plan around where you are staying. Sports fans can catch the Ulsan Hyundai FC at the World Cup-era Munsu Stadium, or watch the Ulsan Mobis basketball team. Ulsan Grand Park is excellent for cycling, rollerblading, and a visit to the butterfly house and petting zoo. In Bangeojin, the Hyundai Arts Centre hosts touring theatre and opera, and there is subsidised ice skating and bowling nearby. For hikers, the Yeongnam Alps offer Mt. Gajisan, Mt. Sinbulsan, and Paraeso Waterfall.
