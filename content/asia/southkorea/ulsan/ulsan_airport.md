Ulsan Airport handles only domestic flights -- Seoul Gimpo and Jeju-do. There are no international services. From Incheon, transfer to Gimpo via the limousine bus (about 35 minutes, 6,000 won), then fly to Ulsan in roughly an hour. Korean Air and Asiana operate the Seoul route, with weekday fares starting around 62,000 won. The terminal is small but modern, with food outlets, a coffee bar, a chemist, and car rental. The tourist information booth on the ground floor has good maps and leaflets. A direct bus to Gyeongju runs four times daily from the airport.
