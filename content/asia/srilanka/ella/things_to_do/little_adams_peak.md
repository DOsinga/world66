latitude: 6.8630

---

An easier walk than Ella Rock and just as rewarding. The trail winds through tea estates to a grassy ridge with views in every direction. The hike takes about 45 minutes each way and is straightforward enough for anyone reasonably fit. Popular at sunrise, but the views are good at any time of day.
