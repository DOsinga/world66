Travel Guide

**Jaffna** is the cultural town of the Tamil people of Sri Lanka.
Jaffna District, the northernmost region of the Island of Sri Lanka, is
one of the oldest habitation sites in Lower South Asia, populated by
Tamil speaking people. Jaffna is situated within ten degrees of latitude
to the north of the equator. It is in close proximity to the
sub-continent of India and separated from it by the Palk Strait and the
Bay of Bengal.

Jaffna is the northern capital and has an airport based in Palali. Due
to the current circumstances there are no international flights to
Palali. To get to Jaffna one must fly into Colombo the southern capital
and get a domestic flight to Palali.
