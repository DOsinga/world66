Location
About 10Km to the South East of Colombo.

Major roads
High Level Road, Stanley Thilakarathne Mawatha, Pagoda Road, Old Kesbawa Road.

How to get there
From Colombo by bus with route number 138 or by train. catch a taxi, or a three wheeler.

Shopping
Nugegoda Super Market: A shopping complex with shops & selling a large range of items ranging from Cloths, Electronic goods, toys, etc. Sarasavi Book Shop, Sammana book shop in the town itself and Vijitha Yapa book shop towards to Kuhuwela. Cargill’s and Keels Supermarkets within the city, Modern style air-conditioned grocery/consumables shopping centre. Sausiri shpos: Modern style air-conditioned shopping centre. Nolimit, Debug and many more Clothing shop. Fashionable shoes from Bangkok in various types. Also there are multitudes of other small/medium/large shops to cater for any shopping need.

Nearest Police Station
Near the intersection of Stanley Thilakarathne Mawatha, Kohuwela, small police post Near the railway Station and Kirulapona town u may find Police Stations. Closest major Police Station is Mirihana which is about 4Km away from Nugegoda town Centre.

Places of Interrest
Kotte Naga Viharaya - A Buddhist temple about 5Km away from Nugegoda town Centre. Roman Catholic Church - Stanley Thilakarathne Mawatha. Markets - Station Lane off Stanley Thilakarathne Mawatha. Banks: Peoples Bank, Bank of Ceylon, Commercial Bank, Sampath Bank and HSBC Bank Brach is in Nawala Road, 4 k.m. way from the main city. HNB and Bank of Ceylon is just near the BOO TREE in the Nugegoda Junction. "Collanka Internet Cafe" is also near the junction. High Level Road, Just Oposite old Peoples Bank City Branch.

Languages Spoken
Mainly Sinhalese, English and also few Tamils.

Places to eat
FAB, Devon, KFC, Macdonld, Pitza-Hut, (air-conditioned),Enrich Hotel, Kosgaha Yata food outlet and many more. (for English type food, snaks and typical Sri Lankan food Rice & Curry, Kottu Rotti, Hoppers, String Hoppers etc), Also you will find lots of small cafe's selling short-eats (Chinese Rolls, Patis, Cutlets etc).
