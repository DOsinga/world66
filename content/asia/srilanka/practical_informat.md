**Currency**

The local currency is the Sri Lanka Rupee (LKR)

### Travel Information Centre

The main tourist office in Columbo is near Crescat Boulevard a
westernized mall where you can eat in food courts and purchase items in an air
conditioned mall.

There you can obtain the Travel Lanka book which contains most of the information you need to travel around Sir Lanka.
