Unawatuna is a sheltered cove on the southwest coast, a few kilometres east of [Galle](/asia/srilanka/galle). The bay curves around a calm, sandy beach backed by a dagoba on the headland, and it is considerably more relaxed than the busier resort town of Hikkaduwa to the north. The water is generally safe for swimming and snorkelling. Accommodation ranges from simple beach guesthouses to small boutique places, and there are plenty of straightforward restaurants along the beach. Take the train to [Galle](/asia/srilanka/galle) and then a tuk-tuk south to reach it.
