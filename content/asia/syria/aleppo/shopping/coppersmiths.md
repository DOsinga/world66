---
title: "Coppersmiths"
type: poi
---

Due north of the Citadel, at right angles to the road which encircles the mound, the street al Kawakbi leads to a quarter where craftsmen abound and the coppersmiths’ hammers can be heard all day. Great sheets of copper are transformed into jugs and pans by their well-aimed blows; a rough outline drawn on the wall is their only pattern. They sell their goods by weight. At the end of this street of copper a beautifully carved entrance leads into an ordinary warehouse.  A little covered souk brings us
