title: Mount Kassioun
type: poi

The 1,115-metre summit overlooking Damascus from the northwest. Take a taxi up for a panoramic view of the city and the green Ghouta stretching away to the east. Best at sunset, when the city lights begin to appear. There are cafes with terraces near the top.
