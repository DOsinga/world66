---
title: "Hammam Nureddin"
type: poi
category: "Sight"
latitude: 33.5120
longitude: 36.3095
---

The most famous of Damascus's traditional bathhouses, roughly 800 years old. It can feel a bit touristy, but the experience of a proper steam, scrub and massage in a vaulted stone chamber is hard to beat. There are other hammams in the Old City if you want something less visited.
