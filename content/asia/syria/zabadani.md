A summer resort some 45 kilometres north of Damascus, 1175 metres above sea level. It overlooks the plain of Zabadani, a fertile land with thousands of fruit trees bearing apples, cherries, plums, peaches, and pears. The source of the river Barada rises in this plain and supplies Damascus with water, irrigating the Ghouta around the city. The river's source forms a small lake surrounded by cafes and restaurants.
