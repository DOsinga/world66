Taiwan is as island off the southern coast of China. It is famous for its **Taiwan**** day trips** among other things. Taiwan is one of the fastest-growing economies in the world. The culture of Taiwan is a hybrid of several sources. China has a lot to offer to tourists like its ancient monuments, beaches and much more, making it one of the must see destinations of Asia.

#### **Enjoy the Beauty of Taiwan with the Taipei City tour**

Taipei is the largest city of Taiwan and is situated at the tip of the island. The tour of the city takes place in the morning and afternoon. This three-hour trip aims to cover the most famous tourist attractions of Taipei like the Chiang Kai-Sek memorial hall, the Martyr's shrine, the National palace museum, Chinese temples and the Handicraft center. The morning tour commences at 8 a.m., and the afternoon tour starts at 1 p.m. with a pick-up from the hotel lobby. In 2010, the tour costs $25 USD for adults and $20 USD for children. This is a great tour if you are visiting Taipei for the first time.

#### **Discover the Scenery on Your Northern Coast Tour**

The Northern coast tour commences in the morning at 8 a.m. with a pick-up from the hotel lobby. This tour takes you across the Northern coast which is known for its scenic beauty. Keelung City, the international seaport of Taiwan, and Yehliu Park, which is famous for its natural rock formation, are the tourist attractions covered by the tour. This four-hour tour costs $28 USD for adults and $22 USD for children.
