---
title: "Practical Information"
type: section
---

There are no money-changers in Kenting and, interestingly, no banks
either. There are a couple of ATMs which can be used. The two 5-star
hotels change money only for their guests. Although some local vendors
might agree to change money, it's unusual. Make sure that you have
enough money for the duration of your stay.

There is a bank right next to the bus-station in the nearby town of Hengchun. But it's closed on Sundays and public holidays.
