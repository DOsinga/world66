There are three main shopping areas in Taichung including: Chung-Kang Road shopping area, Chung-You shopping area, Taichung railway station shopping area.

**Chung-Kang Road Shopping Area**

1. Shin-Kong Mistukoshi Department Store
2. Idee Department Store
3. Tiger City Shopping Mall
4. Warner Village Cinema
5. KS SOGO Department Store

**Chung-You Shopping Area**

1. Chung You Department Store
2. Body Shop
3. Camper

**Taichung Railway Station Shopping Area**

1. First Plaza Shopping Mall
2. Elsite Bookstore & Department Store
