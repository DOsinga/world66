---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

**Taiwan Taoyuan International Airport (TPE)**, about 40 km west of the city, is Taiwan's main international gateway. The **Taoyuan Airport MRT** links it directly to Taipei Main Station in about 40 minutes on the express service. **Taipei Songshan Airport (TSA)**, right inside the city, handles some mainland-China, Japanese and Korean flights — much more convenient if you can fly into it. The **high-speed rail** (HSR) runs down the west coast from Taipei to Kaohsiung in 90 minutes, with frequent trains throughout the day. Conventional **TRA trains** cover the rest of the island, including the slow, scenic east coast to Hualien and Taitung.
