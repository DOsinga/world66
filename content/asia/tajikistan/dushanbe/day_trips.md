The hills and valleys around Dushanbe are worth at least a day's exploration. Hisar Fortress, 30 km west, is the most visited excursion — an 18th-century stronghold that was once the seat of Turko-Mongol rulers, now a pleasant ruin with good views. The Varzob Gorge north of the city offers cooler temperatures, a Soviet-era health resort, and thermal springs if you feel adventurous. Ramit Nature Reserve is further out but rewards the effort with dramatic canyon scenery.
