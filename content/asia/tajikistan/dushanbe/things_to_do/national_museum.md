The country's main museum covers Tajik history from ancient times through Soviet rule. The archaeology section is the highlight — Buddhist-era finds, Sogdian artifacts, and a large reclining Buddha statue from Ajina-Tepe, one of the finest pieces of Buddhist art found in Central Asia. The post-independence exhibition is heavy on national pride but worth a look to understand the country's self-image.
