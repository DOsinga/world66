**Nurek is in Tadjikistan, Central Asia, some 70 km from Dushanbe, capital city of Tajikistan. Nurek is a town with about 40000 population is surrounded by mountains and is famous with a hydropower station build there during Soviets. The dam of the station is of the highest in the world (300m)... Vakhsh river divides the city into to parts... Nurek is a nice place to visit for couple days... there are nice restaurants and very friendly and nice people... taxi to Nurek from Dushanbe would cost you about 15-20 $US...**
