---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

The most common way to come here is form [Samarkand](/asia/uzbekistan/samarkand). It is only 60 km and there are buses to the border, as well as taxi's. Neither of those actually cross the border so you should arrange to be dropped off at the border and find onward transport there. This shouldn't cause too much problems, except maybe when you arrive at the end of the day.
