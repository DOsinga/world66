Located on New Road between Silom and Sathon roads, this market offers a large selection of cut flowers and orchids. Jasmine garlands are the specialty — sold by the strand and used as offerings at spirit houses and temples across the city. It's busiest in the early morning when vendors stock up. A good spot to pick up something beautiful and fragrant for very little money.
