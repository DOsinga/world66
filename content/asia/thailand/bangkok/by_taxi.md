Metered taxis are plentiful and cheap. Always insist the driver uses the meter -- if they refuse, get out and flag another one. Tuk-tuks are fun for the experience but negotiate the fare before you get in and never agree to stop at shops or restaurants they suggest along the way, as these are commission scams. Be wary of drivers eager to take you somewhere other than your destination.
