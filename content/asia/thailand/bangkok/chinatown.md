Bangkok's Chinatown covers a large area around Yaowarat Road and Charoen Krung Road, with narrow streets and alleys full of shops selling everything imaginable. Nearby Phahurat Market is one of the city's best cloth centres. The area has been a trading hub for the Chinese community for over 200 years.
