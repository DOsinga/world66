---
title: 'Day Trips'
type: section
---

Bangkok makes a good base for excursions into the surrounding countryside. The express boat up the Chao Phraya to Nonthaburi is an easy half-day trip, while Safari World and the Crocodile Farm appeal to families. For something further afield, the ancient capital of Ayutthaya is about 80 km north and makes a rewarding full-day excursion.
