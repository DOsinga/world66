---
title: 'Getting There'
type: section
---

Most visitors arrive at Suvarnabhumi Airport, Bangkok's main international hub. Budget airlines often use the older Don Mueang Airport north of the city. The Airport Rail Link connects Suvarnabhumi to central Bangkok in about 30 minutes. From either airport, metered taxis into the city centre cost 300-400 baht plus expressway tolls. Bangkok is also well connected by rail and bus to other Thai cities and neighbouring countries.
