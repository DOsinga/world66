Bangkok's older airport, north of the city, now used primarily by budget carriers including AirAsia, Nok Air, and Thai Lion Air. A free shuttle bus connects Don Mueang to Suvarnabhumi if you are transferring. Taxis to central Bangkok take 30-60 minutes depending on traffic.
