Many of the 5-star hotels in Bangkok maintain discos such as Spasso in Grand Hyatt Erawan, Sparks at the Emerald Hotel and The Salsa Club at the Pathumwan Princes Hotel. There are also the huge halls with the latest in lighting and audio, and thousands of dancers, such as Phuture and Dance Fever on Ratchadaphisek Road. Sukhumvit Road is a good place with its ever-popular Taurus and Discovery as is the trendy area off Silom Road with the Factory, Lucifer and Freeman.  

**Place to go outs**

**Lucifer**

The Lucifer takes the look and feel of the inferno and brings it to the dance floor. With its blend of Trace Techno Dance and Underground, it is certainly one of the hottest locations among the bars and clubs in the Patpong area. A good mix of Thai and Western customers.

**Narcissus **

Narcissus is a huge place with an aimed at an upscale and dressed-up clientele. It has a terrific light and sound system and occasional guest DJs. It is very popular with the foreign expatriates. Narcissus opens daily from 9.00 pm to 2.00 am. The admission is 500 baht (price includes two drinks) [more..](/asia/thailand/bangkok/nightlife/discodancing/narcissus)

**NASA Spacedrome**

One of Bangkok's flashy discos which can cater for more than 2000 visitors. At midnight, a spaceship descends to the floor amid smoke, flashing lights, and the theme song from 2001: A Space Odyssey. Located at 999 Ramkamheng Rd., 100 meters north of New Petchburi Rd. and therefore rather far away [more..](/asia/thailand/bangkok/nightlife/discodancing/nasaspacedrome)

**Paradise Music Hall**

This giant disco is located on the other side of the Chao Phraya river in Thornburi. It is actually not far away from Banglampoo and therefore a convenient place to go for people staying in this area. The Paradise is on Arun Amarin Road.
