**Place to go outs**

**The Babylon**

This is the most famous sauna catering for Thai-Foreigner couples. It has recently moved (november 2000) to Soi Nantha, off Sathorn Soi 1 (also known as Soi Atthakarnprasit 1). Phone: +66-2-2132108. 

**The Balcony**

This bar got an award of the well-known magazine METRO as one of the best gay bars in town. It is located at Soi 4, off Silom Rd. and is very popular with foreigners. It serves only OK food, but it has a balcony to watch the action on Soi 4 on Friday and Saturday. ** mungorn:** Balco [more..](/asia/thailand/bangkok/nightlife/gayscene/thebalcony)

**DJ Station**

Bangkok's most known gay disco. It is always crowded. There is a daily show at midnight. Located at Soi 2 at Silom Street.   

**Freeman Dance Area**

Busy gay disco. Open daily until 2.30 am. Located at 60/18-21 Silom Rd.,in subsoi opposite Silom complex. Check out their website for more information at: www.freemanclub.com
