---
title: 'Shopping'
type: section
---

Shopping in Bangkok is an experience in itself. The city's countless markets offer the widest variety of goods at prices that invite haggling -- bargaining is expected and part of the fun. For sheer range, do not miss the Chatuchak Weekend Market, open Saturdays and Sundays near the BTS Mo Chit station. For air-conditioned comfort and international brands, head to Siam Square's cluster of malls including MBK and Siam Paragon. Markets generally operate from early morning until evening, while malls keep hours from 10 am to 9 pm.
