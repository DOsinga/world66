Run by the Thai Red Cross as a centre for collecting venom and producing antitoxin, the Snake Farm is surprisingly entertaining. You can watch handlers milk poison from cobras and other snakes during the daily shows at 10:30 am and 2 pm. Monday to Friday 8:30 am to 4:30 pm, weekends 8:30 am to noon.
