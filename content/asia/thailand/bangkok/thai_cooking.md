Bangkok offers dozens of Thai cooking classes ranging from half-day market tours with hands-on cooking to multi-day professional courses. The Oriental Hotel Thai Cooking School is one of the most prestigious, with five-day courses under an expert chef. Many guesthouses in the Khao San Road area can arrange more affordable options. A cooking class is one of the best souvenirs you can take home from Thailand.
