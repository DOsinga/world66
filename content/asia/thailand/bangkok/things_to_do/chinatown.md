Bangkok's Chinatown sprawls east of Hua Lamphong Station around Yaowarat Road. It is a wonderful place to wander for a couple of hours, with temples, traditional shop-houses, and narrow alleys specializing in everything from gold to shoes. Come during the day for the energy and street food; at night, the area empties out surprisingly fast. The street food along Yaowarat Road after dark is some of the best in the city.
