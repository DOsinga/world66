Bangkok's oldest and largest temple, located just south of the Grand Palace. Wat Pho is famous for its enormous reclining Buddha and as the birthplace of traditional Thai massage. The temple's massage school on the eastern side of the compound is the real draw for many visitors -- you can get a proper Thai massage for a fraction of what you would pay at a hotel spa, or even sign up for a multi-day course. Admission 200 baht.
