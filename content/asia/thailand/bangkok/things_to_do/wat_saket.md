The Golden Mount is an artificial hill topped by a gilded pagoda, rising 80 metres above the old city. Built during the reigns of Rama III and IV, it remains one of Bangkok's most recognizable landmarks. The climb to the top rewards you with a panoramic view across the rooftops. Admission 50 baht.
