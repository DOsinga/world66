The Temple of Dawn sits on the west bank of the Chao Phraya River, its 79-metre central pagoda decorated with ceramic tiles and fragments of multi-coloured porcelain. The most beautiful view is from the east bank at sunset. Take a small ferry from Tha Tien Pier near Wat Pho -- the crossing costs just a few baht. Climbing part way up the steep central prang is possible and offers good river views. Admission 100 baht.
