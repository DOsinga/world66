Situated within the Grand Palace compound, Wat Phra Kaew is Thailand's most sacred temple and home to the Emerald Buddha, the country's most revered Buddha image. The temple grounds are a treasure house of Thai art, featuring the Golden Stupa, the Phra Mondop library, and the Royal Pantheon. The 178-section mural paintings depicting the Ramayana are stunning. Open daily 8:30 am to 3:30 pm, included in the Grand Palace admission.
