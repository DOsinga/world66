Thailand has a wide choice of beaches including picturesque white sandy beaches washed by clear azure waters. Beaches and Islands in Southern Thailand include Phuket island, Krabi, Phi Phi islands, Koh Lanta on the South West Coast, Koh Samui, Koh Tao and Koh Phangan in Southern Thailand in the Gulf of Thailand. Koh Chang and Koh Samet off the South Eastern Seaboard of Thailand in the Gulf of Thailand. mainland beaches in the Gulf of Thailand close to bangkok include Pattaya and Jomtien beach are and the Hua Hin area including Cha Am, Khao Takiab, Pak Nam Pran and Pranburi. Beaches also stretch between pattaya and Trat on the South Eastern Seaboard and also south from Prachuap Khiri Khan to Bang Saphan and Chumphon on the coast south of Bangkok.

Khao Takiab Beach and Bay

Khao Takiab beach and Khao Takiab Bay in the Hua Hin area offer a choice of beaches, swimmings, sailing, bopat trips and fishing at offshore islands. Also temples, elephant rides, horse cart rides, off orad all terrain rides and nearby golf. The busy town of Hua hin is just 10 minutes for restaurants and entertainment plus markets and shopping. All just over 2 hrs 30 mins from Bangkok by highway.
