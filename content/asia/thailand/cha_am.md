Cha Am is a quiet coastal town on the Gulf of Thailand, about 160 kilometres south of Bangkok and 20 kilometres north of the busier resort of [Hua Hin](/asia/thailand/hua_hin). It is popular with Bangkok weekenders — the beach fills up on Saturdays and empties again by Monday — but during the week it has a sleepy, unhurried feel.

The beach is long and wide, lined with seafood vendors and deckchairs for hire. There is not a great deal to do in town beyond the beach, which is part of the appeal. For shopping, restaurants, and nightlife, Hua Hin is a 20-minute drive south on a modern highway.
