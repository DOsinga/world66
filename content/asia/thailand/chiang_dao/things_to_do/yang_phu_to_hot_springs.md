---
title: "Yang Phu To Hot Springs"
type: poi
category: Sight
latitude: 19.4108
longitude: 98.9432
---

Natural mineral springs about seven kilometres from town, set in jungle alongside a stream. The public pools are free; private cabanas run 50 THB per hour. A Karen hill tribe village sits just next to the springs. It's a pleasant half-day trip, especially good for cyclists — the road there is flat and easy. Go early morning on a weekday to have it mostly to yourself.
