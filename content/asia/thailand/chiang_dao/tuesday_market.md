title: Tuesday Market
type: poi
---

Every Tuesday morning, a market sets up near the bus station drawing hill tribe vendors from the surrounding valleys — Lisu, Karen, Hmong — selling produce, textiles, and prepared food. It's genuinely local and not oriented toward tourists. Get there early; it winds down by midday. Thursday evening has a smaller night market in town.
