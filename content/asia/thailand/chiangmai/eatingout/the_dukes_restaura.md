The Duke's is small yet popular place with westerners and Thai clientele that enjoy good old fashioned American style meal. It is good value with western size portions be it the imported steaks or the delicious pasta dishes.
Dave, the manager is an American restaurateur who has returned again to the CNX restaurant scene after a far too long an absence, along with his wife and family who help out in the place, all having been living stateside for some time.

The pizza is a true American style pizza that has found a following among true pizza lovers of Chiang Mai that are tired of the "Thai fusion Pizza" served by many chain pizzerias as genuine pizza. My personal favorite being a real nice Caesar salad served with piping hot bread. With Nightly specials being offered to supplement the menu for the many regular customers they have. Be sure not to miss the Tuesday Mexican food specials. Any of the meals served could easily be mistaken for great meal from your favorite restaurant back home. The decor however is not American but a blend of English tavern meets Thai rustic Chic. That is not to say that it is has snob appeal because here the atmosphere warm and friendly. One has been know to see famous actors and actresses eating there while filming the latest "Vietnam" movie around Chiang Mai, so check it out.
