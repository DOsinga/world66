Wat Ram Poeng, also known as Tapotaram, is a forested temple on the southern edge of Chiang Mai famous for its 26-day silent vipassana meditation retreats. The temple welcomes foreigners and provides basic accommodation and vegetarian meals during the course, making it one of the most accessible places in northern Thailand to experience traditional Buddhist meditation practice.

The grounds are quiet and shaded by large trees, a world away from the busy old city. Even if you are not signing up for the full retreat, shorter courses are sometimes available and the temple is worth a visit for its peaceful atmosphere and distinctive Lanna-style architecture.
