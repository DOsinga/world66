**Sights**

**Golden Triangle**

The most famous attraction are the common borders of three countries; Thailand, Laos and Myanmar. It is about 9 kilometers north of Chiang Saen. From this place, Laotian and Myanmar border can be seen through Mae Khong and Ruak River respectively.

**Phrathat Doi Tung**

Phrathat Doi Tung is the holy relic that draws devout Buddhists from as far as Laos and Myanmar for annual worshipping. The reliquary is atop the highest Chiang Rai mountain at Doi Tung, elevation 2,000 meters above sea-level.
