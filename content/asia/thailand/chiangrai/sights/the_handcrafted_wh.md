This temple is commonly known as the White temple but to the Thais it is named as Wat Ron Khon. It is situated less than 10 kilometer south of the Chiangrai city. Most of the mystical designs are hand made with pieces of mirror glue to its outline. From a far it is like a fairy land in snow white color and sparkling jewels. You will have to see it for yourself to experience it.
