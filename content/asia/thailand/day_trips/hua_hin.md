Day trips from Bangkok to Hua Hin offer the visitor the chance to swim or relax on the beach, enjoy amazing Thai seafood ( Hua Hin is famous in Thailand for excellent seafood ), Elephant Ride, Off Road all Terrain rides, Elephant Rides, fishing trips, a large number of amazing temples including the temple area of Khao takiab and Khao Khailat. Alos visit the Old Palace and see the famous Hua Hin Railway Station, one of the most photographed rail stations in all Asia. Enjoy Afternoon Tea at the world famous Sofitel Hotel which was built in the 1920's as the original rail station hotel. Journey time from bangkok about 3 hours.
