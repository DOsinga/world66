**Golf Courses in Thailand** 


 THAILAND is known as a major golfing destination. There are more than 200 golf courses in the Kingdom, many of them are up to international tournament standard. 


 The differences between these golf courses are the range of facilities and services. Many golf courses are located on hillsides, near the seas, and forests. Club houses facilities are designed like five star hotels. 


 Many of them have refreshment stands about every third hole, while caddies have knowledge of the game and cannot only help to choose clubs, but also offer advice at some tricky holes.
