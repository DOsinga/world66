DK Publishing
offers another fascinating and factual travel guide, this time for
visitors to Thailand. The stunning photographs live up to DK
Publishing’s reputation for showing the areas they review. This guide
book is comprehensive, interesting, well organized and jam packed with
information.

The author covers not only the history and culture of Hua
Hin, but local festivals, celebrations and sights that are begging to
be seen. Three easily referenced sections break the information into
bite sized chunks. With a section on history, a section for tourist
information and a survival guide, this guide book offers the best of
all worlds.

This guide is remarkably detailed and offers impressive
cutaway diagrams of museums to provide readers with a visual reference
for the places they will be visiting. Packed with photographs and
artistic representations of the area, this guide book is a pictorial
presentation of the local colors and textures. With detailed street
maps, this guide proves itself to be functional as well as beautiful.

For those looking for concrete information, this guide to Hua Hin also
supplies detailed information on lodgings, restaurants, local
festivals, museums, wildlife, crafts, beaches, temples and markets. An
entire section devoted to the practical aspects such as cost, hours of
operation and location, offers tourists the ability to plan their trip
wisely. A handy survival guide section offers insights on local public
transportation, public restrooms, customs and climate. A phrase book
with a basic pronunciation guide is a convenient assistant to
interactions with the natives.
