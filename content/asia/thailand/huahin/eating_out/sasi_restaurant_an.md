**"A First for Hua Hin"**

Visitors to Hua-Hin will have the chance to enjoy a traditional 2 hour Thai Classical Dance Show and Thai Cuisine.

The show includes a Demonstration of Dance Language, The Golden and Silver Brance Dance, a Ram Sat Chatri, The Ballet of Horses, Thai Classical Mask Dance or **"Khon"**, a Long Drum Dance and an impressive display of Thai Martial Arts.

If you want to experience **a taste of real Thai culture and tradition**, then this show is well worth a visit.

Priced at 750 Baht per person , it includes of fine Thai Cuisine and comes with a warm, friendly welcome. Drinks are not included in the price. Kid 5-10 years old 600 Baht per person. Vegetarian is welcome.

Hua-Hin's First Thai Classical Dance Show is performed everyday
