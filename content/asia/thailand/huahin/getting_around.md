---
title: "Getting Around"
type: section
---

Getting around Hua hin is very easy. Many of the main beach hotels and beach resorts are right on the beach, both north, south and in the center of Hua Hin. Most resorts outside of the center operate their own shuttles to and from Hua Hin. Local taxis and small 'Tuk-Tuks' are readily available and cheap. You can also enjoy a ride on a Samlor ( Bicycle Rickshaw ) thought they are really only suitable for short journeys.
