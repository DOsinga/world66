---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

SGA Aeo operates 4 daily flights between Bangkok and Hua Hin. USD 135 return.

Air-con buses leave Bangkok's Southern Bus Terminal more than 36 times daily between 4.20 a.m.. and 4.20 p.m. (40 minute intervals). Non-air-conditioned coaches leave from Bangkok's Southern Bus terminal on Petkasem Road 19 times daily. 

Trains depart daily from Bangkok for Cha-Am, Hua Hin. Express Diesel Railcar leaves Bangkok daily at 9.15 p.m. Express trains leave Bangkok 5 times daily at 2.00 p.m., 3.15 p.m., 5.05 p.m.,7.20p.m.and 10.35 p.m. 

Rapid trains leave Bangkok 4 times daily at 12.35 p.m., 4 p.m., 5.45 p.m., and 6.30 p.m. 

Diesel Railcars leave Bangkok twice vice daily at 9.00 a.m. and 1.40 p.m. 

Taxis and Limousine Services operate from Bangkok Airport and Bangkok City to Hua Hin. Rates vary from about Thb 1,600 to Thb 2,850 each way.
