The first thing you notice is the classical music in the background, and the décor. Oh the décor, magnifico!

It's cozy, romantic, trendy and offers probably the best service you will find outside the big hotels. Popular with the local community, tourists and Bangkokians alike, it seems to have grown from strength to strength the last years. Pasta Factory Hua Hin with Nut and Hannes has also featured on TV Channel 11 and in many international magazines. Inside the restroom area are a few visitor's book, and judging from the many comments written in it, the restroom is one of the talking points of Pasta Factory, along with the food of course. The food (Roasted breads, salads, soups, pasta, pizza, Thai and international selection, seafood specialities) is extremely reasonable and very good.

Discover Pasta Factory Hua Hin, here on holiday. or down for the weekend from Bangkok check out this special restaurant (and the restroom) by yourselfes.

Nut & Hannes, Pasta Factory Hua Hin - Thailand
