Turn right out of the ***Hilton Hotel*** on ***Naresdamri Rd.*** Take a 3-4 minute walk towards the main ***fishing pier*** (300 metres), **Ye Olde** **Buffalo Tavern** is on your left hand side next door to seven eleven.

**The Buffalo Tavern restaurant **and **pub** has full A/C with 5 LCD flat TV screens with all current global sporting events and world news. Now with family run luxurious 5th floor rooftop terraced harbour view **studio apartments** with elevator access, B&B wecome.

***Open daily from 8am*** for breakfast, Danish bacon, English pork bangers and the best Eggs Benedict, fresh Scottish Kippers flown in daily, several ***healthy alternatives available.***

**The Buffalo Tavern** is under new management still having the reputation for giving good food at reasonable prices. Whether it be prime ***Australian Ribeye**, ****Sirloin* **or ***Rump steaks***, ***Australian lamb chops***, ***English Fish and chips*** served with home crinckle cut chips, mushy peas and tartar sauce and of course their signature dish of ***dinosaur size pork*** ***spare ribs*** with their very special ***BBQ sauce***. Fresh local sea food is available daily and probably the best ***Thai food*** in town with no MSG (***monosodium glutamate***)

The huge bar area is very well stocked with the coolest beers and Ciders, Draft ***Chang***, ***Strongbow ***and ***John Smiths,*** bottled ***Magners ***and ***Bulmers Ciders*** coming soon ***Kronenbourg 1664*** on draft. You can find **The Buffalo Tavern** on the corner of ***Chomsin** and **Naresdamri Rd*** , opposite the entrance to the fishing pier where the fishing trawlers come in. Free Wi Fi for customers also takeaway service, phone/fax ***032-530087*** mobile ***+668-5291-8685*** or ***+668-6603-5335***

***Check out the quiz nights and live music***

***PS. Try the spare ribs and chocolate Brownies, they're awesome!!! By royal appointment as trays of Brownies go to the Palace every month!!***
