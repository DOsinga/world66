---
title: "Getting Around"
type: section
---

Songthaews -- the converted pickup trucks that serve as shared minibuses -- run constantly along the beach road and connect Jomtien to [Pattaya](/asia/thailand/pattaya) for 10 to 20 baht. They operate around the clock, though frequency drops after midnight. Motorbike taxis cluster at the main junctions and are the fastest way to cover short distances. Many visitors rent motorbikes or bicycles for the duration of their stay; the beach road is flat and manageable, though traffic can be chaotic at rush hour.
