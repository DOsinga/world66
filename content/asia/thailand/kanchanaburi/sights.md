**Wat Tham Khao Poon**

The Wat is located one kilometer southwest of the Chung-Kai War Cemetery. The Buddhist temple is locally renowned for a cave containing stalactites and stalagmites, and many beautiful Buddha images.      

**River Kwai bridge**

Thanks to the famous movie the bridge became the most known tourist attraction in the small city of Kanchanaburi. The iron bridge was brought from Indonesia by the Japanese army and reassembled by by Allied prisoner-of-war laborers as part of the 'Death Railway' linking Thailand with Burma.
