title: "Wat Tham Khao Poon"
type: poi
category: Sight
latitude: 13.9952
longitude: 99.5239
---

A Buddhist cave temple 1 kilometre southwest of the Chong Kai War Cemetery. The cave contains stalactites and stalagmites alongside a series of beautiful Buddha images. One of the more atmospheric temple sites in the area, combining natural cave formations with religious iconography.
