---
title: "Eating Out"
type: section
---

Seafood is the thing to eat along Khao Takiab Bay Road. Small Thai restaurants line the road behind the beach, ranging from budget street-food stalls to sit-down places with tables on the sand. Madame Green Seafood, right on the beach in Khao Takiab village, is a local favourite for fresh catches cooked to order.
