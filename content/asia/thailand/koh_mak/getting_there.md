By speedboat:

There is a twice daily speedboat connection between the Krom Luang pier in Laem Ngop near Trat and the Makathanee Resort pier on Koh Mak, operated by Leelawadee Speedboats. Departures are currently at 10:30 and 14:00 and the fare from the mainland to [Koh Mak](/asia/thailand/koh_mak) is 450 baht. More information is available on Koh Mak Speedboats.

### [Koh Mak Speedboat](/asia/thailand/koh_mak/getting_there/koh_mak_speedboat)

Enjoy the excitement of travelling by fast speedboat when you transfer from the picturesque Krom Luang pier in Laem Ngop to the Makathanee Resort pier on the South West of Koh Mak island in only 45-60 minutes. Koh Mak Speedboat offer you this speed boat transfer service to Ko Maak and Ko Waai on behalf of the well respected and often praised Leelawadee Speedboats company which is based on [Koh Mak](/asia/thailand/koh_mak) island.
