Travel Guide

Koh Talu is an island, quite small, limited to accommodation near the beach, a short boat ride from [Bang Saphan](/asia/thailand/bang_saphan) which can be reached by road or rail from Bangkok or from the south of Thailand. Situated along the coast between [Hua Hin](/asia/thailand/huahin) and Chumphon, trains also operate north from Hat yai and Surat Thani. There are express buses from Bangkok and Hua Hin and also from Surat Thani.
