A small, flat island between Koh Chang and Koh Kood, Koh Mak is marketed as a family-friendly alternative to the bigger islands. It is quiet, with a handful of resorts spread along its beaches. There are no cars to speak of, and you can cycle around the island in an hour or two.

Speedboats from Koh Chang and from the mainland reach Koh Mak in about 45 minutes.
