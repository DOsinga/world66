---
title: "Klong Dao Beach"
type: poi
latitude: 7.6350
longitude: 99.0310
---

The most popular beach on Koh Lanta and the first you reach heading south from Ban Sala Dan. Three kilometres of wide, gently sloping sand with calm, shallow water that makes it ideal for swimming and families. At low tide the beach stretches out enormously, good for volleyball or a morning run. The most developed beach on the island, with a mix of upscale resorts and budget bungalows, but it never feels overcrowded.
