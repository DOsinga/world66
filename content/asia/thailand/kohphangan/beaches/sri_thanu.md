Sri Thanu is a small volcanic peninsula on the west coast, just five minutes from Thong Sala. A coral reef sits just offshore, within easy swimming distance from the beach. The bay is picturesque and quiet, with a handful of bungalow operations. Its proximity to the ferry port makes it a convenient base.
