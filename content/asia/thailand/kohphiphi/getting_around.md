---
title: "Getting Around"
type: section
---

Phi Phi Don is car-free — no tuk-tuks, no motorbike taxis, no buses. Everything in the village is within walking distance, and even the far end of the island is under an hour on foot.

For the more remote beaches (Long Beach, Laem Tong), you can hire a longtail boat taxi from the pier. Expect to haggle — prices are not fixed and go up after dark.
