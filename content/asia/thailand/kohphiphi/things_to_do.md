---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

The main attraction on Phi Phi is the water — snorkelling, diving, and island-hopping by longtail boat. On land, the hike up to the viewpoint is the one must-do. It is a steep climb, but the panorama over the isthmus and both bays is spectacular. Bring mosquito repellent and go late afternoon for the best light.

Boat trips run to Phi Phi Leh, where Viking Cave shelters the nests of swiftlets harvested for bird's nest soup. Maya Bay on the same island — the *Beach* filming location — reopened to visitors with daily limits after years of closure for reef recovery.
