---
title: "Day Trips"
type: section
---

Samui sits in the middle of an archipelago, and the best days out are boat trips rather than inland drives. **Ang Thong Marine Park** — forty-two limestone islands in a protected reserve — is the headline excursion, with kayaking, snorkelling, and the emerald saltwater lake on Koh Mae Ko that inspired *The Beach*. **Koh Phangan**, a half-hour ferry ride north, is where the monthly **Full Moon Party** happens at Haad Rin; go if that's your thing, or skip it and stay the day for the quieter beaches on the west side. **Koh Tao**, another hour on, is one of the world's cheapest places to get open-water scuba certified.
