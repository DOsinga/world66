**TOPCATS FISHING RESORT**

Most anglers will have never seen such a diverse selection of large fish in one place.There are over 4000 fish and over 20 different species to catch including 5 potential world records.

The lake is set on 4.35 acres,and have been designed and manicured to sit naturally within their own spectacular natural enviroment.Our specimen lake has depths upto 27 feet,and a diverse selection of gigantic fish up to 120 kg.

Due to the perfect enviroment,the natural food chain we have created and the high protein balanced diet we have created,the weight gains have to be seen to be believed.

Includes **red tail catfish,giant siamese carp,brazilian ripsaw niger catfish,thai giant mekong catfish,red tail tigers,alligator gar,amazonian arapaima gigas,red tail leopards,julliens price carp,indian rahu carp,chao phraya catfish and african sharptooth catfish.**

At topcats we also provide a fully stocked bar of wine spirits and beer,and a open plan restaurant thats menu includes authentic Thai food and most european dishes.

Within the bar we have big screen satellite tvs,showing all the sport,pool tables and a dart board.And if the kids are bored we even have a children play room !!!!!
