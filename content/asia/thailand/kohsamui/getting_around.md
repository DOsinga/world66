---
title: "Getting Around in Kohsamui"
type: section
---

Traveling in Koh Samui offers you different forms of **Koh Samui transportation** that you can choose to get around the city and see the many sights that you are going to visit on vacation. Some of the best ways to get around the city and to the Islands include ferries, boats, taxis, and rental cars.

#### Boats and Ferries

You can take boats and ferries around the town to get to where you are going and enjoy the relaxing waters while you are able to take in all of the views. You can find a few different ferries to travel including the Na Thon Pier, P &O Cruises, Koh Samui Tender, and the Catamaran. You can also find boats that will take you to the islands and nearby cities if you are going to be going on an Island trip.

#### Taxis

There are great taxi services in the city that you can find to take from your hotel to anywhere in the city. The Tut Tut taxi is one of the services that you can find and the fares are affordable. You can use the taxis if you are not familiar with the area and prefer having someone drive you around. You can get taxi service to travel around round trip to where you are going, or you can get taxi service to go one way if you are going to be taking a ferry or walking.

#### Rental Cars

If you would like to experience the city by driving, you can find rental cars to use that you can take wherever you are going. You can find local companies that offer car rentals that you can take to the islands or around the city. The rental cars are available to rent for the day, or for the length of your vacation, but you may have to pay for extra mileage if you are going to be going longer distances and to the islands. Some people prefer the rental cars for Koh Samui transportation.

#### Walking

You can also walk around the city to get to where you are going, and to explore the areas outside of the city. There are paths and trails that you can find and it can be a great way to enjoy the views, stop at the tourist spots, and find the best shopping and restaurants. You can choose this form of Koh Samui transportation if you prefer to get some exercise and see the fun places to stop along your trip.
