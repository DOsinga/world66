title: Hin Lad Waterfall
type: poi

Two kilometres south of Nathon, a three-kilometre walk up through rain forest to a multi-tiered waterfall with swimmable pools at the base. Quieter than Na Muang, which gets busier with tour buses. Go early to beat the heat.
