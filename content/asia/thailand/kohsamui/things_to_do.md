---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Beyond the beaches, Samui is best taken slowly — a morning climbing to **Hin Lad** or **Na Muang** waterfalls in the interior, an afternoon at the **Big Buddha** temple on the north coast, and a day out to **Ang Thong Marine Park** for the sea kayaking and snorkelling that anyone who's read *The Beach* will recognise.
