type: poi

The 12-metre gold-plated Buddha that sits on a tiny tidal island on the north coast — visible from arriving flights. Climb the naga-lined staircase to the platform for a view back across the lagoon. A small temple and market stalls at the base. Free to enter; cover your shoulders and knees.
