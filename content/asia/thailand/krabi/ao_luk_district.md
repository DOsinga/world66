About 50 km north of Krabi Town, the Ao Luk district holds some of the most spectacular cave scenery in the province. The **Tham Lod** sea caves — Tham Lod Neua and Tham Lod Tai — are long, low limestone tunnels that you can paddle through at low tide in a sea kayak, dripping with stalactites and emerging into hidden mangrove lagoons on the other side. Nearby **Tham Phi Hua To** holds prehistoric cave paintings estimated to be 3,000 years old. The easiest way to visit is on one of the guided kayak tours run daily from Krabi and Ao Nang.
