A modest but pretty limestone cave at Ban Nong Kok, about 7 km from Krabi Town along Highway 4034, full of stalactites and stalagmites. The future King Rama VI visited in 1909, which earned the cave its name — *sadet* is the Thai verb used for royal comings and goings.
