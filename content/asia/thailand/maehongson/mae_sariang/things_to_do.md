Mae Sariang has a handful of Burmese-influenced temples worth a look, their whitewashed chedis and tiered roofs reflecting the town's border-country heritage.

**Wat Phrathat Chom Chaeng** — Located at the intersection of SH108 and SH105, this temple blends northern Lanna art with Burmese architectural elements.

**Wat Phrathat Chom Thong** — Perched on a hill above town beside the large hilltop Buddha, with views over Mae Sariang and the Yuam River valley below.
