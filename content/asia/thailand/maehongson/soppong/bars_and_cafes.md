---
title: "Bars and Cafes"
type: section
---

The Seven Elephants Cafe offers indoor or outdoor dining in a tropical garden amongst the palms and heliconias. The menu includes dishes from all over SE Asia, including some local Shan dishes, plus some western selections should you need a break from rice. There is some nice music during the day and at dinner, and after dinner you can select from our extensive selection of DVDs should you wish to take in a movie. There is a coffee bar and espresso machine if you would enjoy a good, real coffee, cappucino, or even a Thai iced coffee made with a shot of espresso instead of the usual upcountry instant Nescafe. The cafe offers an interesting menu of house cocktails, including our own Bloody Mali and the Thaijito - a Mojito made with Similan Rum. You can also select from several different bottles of imported red wines. Drinks, snacks, and Western or Thai breakfast is also offered on the deck overlooking the river. Located at the Soppong River Inn, 150 metres west of the Soppong market along the main road to Mae Hong Son.
