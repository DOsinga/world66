Travel Guide

**Nakhon Pathom** is one of the central provinces ( changwat ) of Thailand . Neighboring provinces are (from north clockwise)Suphan Buri, Ayutthaya, Nonthaburi, Bangkok, Samut Sakhon, Rathaburi and Kanchanaburi. Nakhon Pathom is a small province located just 56 km from Bangkok. It
is located in the alluvial plain of central Thailand. It is drained by
the Tha Chin River (also called Nakhon Chaisi river), a distributary of the Chao Phraya river. The urban area of Bangkok has already grown until the provincial borders to Nakhon Pathom.
