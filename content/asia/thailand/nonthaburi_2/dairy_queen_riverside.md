At the foot of Phra Nang Klao Bridge on Rattanathibet Road (27/4 Mu 3), this riverside spot runs evening dinner cruises departing at 8pm from Phra Nangklao Bridge to Sang Hi Bridge. Fares around 90–100 baht per person (higher on weekends). Tel: 0 2921 8670.
