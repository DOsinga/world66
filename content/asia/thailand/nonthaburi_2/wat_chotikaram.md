The highlight of Wat Chotikaram is the Viharn Song Rong, a pavilion whose walls, floor, and ceiling are entirely covered in mural paintings — an unusually complete decorative scheme. The paintings depict scenes from Buddhist cosmology and are among the more intact examples of traditional Thai temple murals in the Nonthaburi area.
