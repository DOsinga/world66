---
title: "When to Go"
type: section
---

The cool season from November to February is the most comfortable time to visit. From April to June the province celebrates its famous fruit harvest with the **Nonthaburi Fruits and Fairs** fair, held in front of the old city hall, where local durian, mangosteen, and mango are sold alongside flowers and plants.

April also brings the **Mon Songkran Festival** on Ko Kret, held one week after the national Songkran date (April 13), with a traditional Mon procession and musical performances. November is the time for the **Tham Bun Tak Bat Phra Roi Paet** ceremony in Amphoe Bang Kruai — a long-running riverside tradition in which monks from multiple temples along Khlong Bangkok Noi receive offerings simultaneously by boat.
