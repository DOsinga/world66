---
title: "Day Trips in Pattaya"
type: section
---

Known for its beaches and resorts, tourists often don&rsquo;t realize just how many fascinating** Pattaya day trips** are available to them.
**
Half-Day Nong Nooch Village Tour**

This four-hour tour takes tourists to an indigenous village that is known for its tropical gardens, elephant and monkey shows, and traditional culture performances. Thai boxing and dancing are often the highlights of the trip. There is free pick-up and drop-off by an air conditioned vehicle to and from any of the hotels or resorts in Pattaya. Tours are 8:30 to 12:30pm and 1:30pm to 5:30pm. The day trip costs 27 US dollars.

**Half-Day Jungle Hike and Elephant Ride**

This four-and-a-half-hour day trip provides a one-hour elephant ride, a 30-minute hike through the jungle, a rafting trip around a tranquil lake, and a ride in an ox-cart. Two tours are available in the morning or afternoon, and lunch or supper is provided before tourists return to their hotels. It costs 67 US dollars.
**
Koh Larn Coral Island Trip**

This full-day trip is popular in Pattaya, as it provides a chance to get away from the resorts. Tourists leave in the morning by speed boat to the nearby Coral Island, which takes less than half an hour. Once at the island, they can participate in water sports or relax on the beach. A glass bottom boat to view the coral while staying dry is also available. A seafood lunch is included on the day trip. It costs 29 US dollars for the entire trip.
