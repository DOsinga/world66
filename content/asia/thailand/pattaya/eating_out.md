---
title: "Eating Out"
type: section
---

Pattaya has an impressive range of food for a beach resort. Beach Road and the sois running inland have cheap Thai noodle shops alongside German-style beer halls and international restaurants. Seafood restaurants along the waterfront serve fresh fish, prawns, and crab. For authentic Thai food at local prices, eat inland or at the markets. The Thepprasit Night Market (Thursday to Sunday evenings) is excellent for street food at very low prices.
