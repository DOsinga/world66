---
title: "Shopping"
type: section
---

Pattaya has extensive shopping: the Thepprasit Night Market (Thursday–Sunday evenings) is the best for cheap clothing, street food, and local products. Central Festival Pattaya Beach is the largest mall on the eastern seaboard, with international brands and a large food hall. The local Pattaya market near North Pattaya Road is primarily a fish and produce market, liveliest in the early morning. Bargaining is expected at street markets and smaller shops.
