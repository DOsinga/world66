Pattaya is working very hard on cleaning up its bad reputation as a sex resort. The message is now loud and clear that such behavior is no longer acceptable and anyone foolish enough to think come and get away with is mistaken. Nevertheless Pattaya is still a party city for people looking for legal fun. It is like an a la carte menu, each visitor can choose those items that suit their taste. And there is a lot to do in Pattaya both in the daytime and at night.

 Apart from the obvious choice of going to the beach to relax and swim why not try some of the entertainment park in and around the City.  

**Sights**

**Khao Kheaw Open Zoo**

  Animals include bears, elephants, buffaloes, monkeys, snakes. The key attraction is the huge aviary. This structure covers over 100 acres and houses more than 100 different kinds of birds 

**Sri Racha Tiger Zoo**

The zoo is home ot numerous interesting animals. The highlight might be the many Bengal tigers. The zoo seems to provide a safe home for the endangered tigers with over 100 staff to care for them. The Bengal Tiger is pretty sensible and don't like hot weather. They love to lie in the shade, swim [more..](/asia/thailand/pattaya/sights/srirachatigerzoo)

address: 341 Moo 3, Nongham, Sri Racha, Chonburi

**Nong Noch Tropical Garden**

Nong Noch Tropical Gardens is actually known for its "Cultural Show". There are three daily shows. What is not so widely known is that the primary business of Nong Noch is Architectural Landscaping and a Tropical Nursery. Tel: +66-38-709358-8, Fax: +66-38- 238160  

address: 163 Sukhumvit Rd, Sattahip, Chonburi

**Elephant Village**

The Elephant Village is a place where you can go elephant riding and see elephants at work. Tel: +66-38-428645-8, 428158, Fax: 66-38+423031.  

address: 54/1 Moo 2, Nongprue, Banglamung, Pattaya

**Ripley's World of Entertainment**

This amusement park might offer you a nice change from hanging around at the beach and its good fun for kids. Address: Tel: 66-38-710485-7, Fax: 66-38- 412319.    

address: 218 Moo 10 Beach Rd, Pattaya, Chonburi
