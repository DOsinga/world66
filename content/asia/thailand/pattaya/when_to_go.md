---
title: "When to Go"
type: section
---

The best time to visit is November to February: warm, dry, and relatively cool (21–31°C). March to May is hot and humid. The monsoon season runs from June to October, with heavy but brief daily downpours; the beaches are quieter and prices are lower, but expect some grey days. Pattaya is on the western side of the Gulf and is less affected by the October–November northeast monsoon than the eastern islands like Koh Samui.
