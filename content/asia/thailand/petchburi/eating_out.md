---
title: "Eating Out"
type: section
---

Phetchaburi is famous across Thailand for its sweets, especially khanom mo kaeng, a rich egg custard baked with palm sugar and coconut. You will find it everywhere, but the city also has solid Thai food at very local prices.

**Roi VIP Kuai Tiao Pla** (2/82 Mu 1, Tambon Rai Som; tel: 0 3242 4129) specialises in fish and pork noodles. Open 9am to 4.30pm. **Ban Mueang Phet** (20/2-4 Ratchawithi Road; tel: 0 3241 8286) serves good Thai food in a pleasant setting. Open 10am to 9.30pm. **Ban Rim Nam** (22 Ratchadamri Road; tel: 0 3240 2277) sits by the river and serves Thai food until 11pm.
