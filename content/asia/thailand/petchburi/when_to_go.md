---
title: "When to Go"
type: section
---

Phetchaburi has a tropical climate with a distinct wet season from May to October. The coolest and driest months, November through February, are the most comfortable for sightseeing.

The **Phra Nakhon Khiri Fair**, held over five days in late February at the Khao Wang compound, is the city's biggest event. It features a historical procession re-enacting the arrival of monarchs who ruled Phetchaburi, exhibitions on local history and archaeology, and cooking demonstrations of the province's famous sweets. The **Thai Song Dam Festival** on April 18 in Khao Yoi district celebrates the culture and cuisine of the Thai Song Dam community, with villagers wearing traditional dress.
