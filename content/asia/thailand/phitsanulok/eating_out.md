---
title: "Eating Out in Phitsanulok"
type: section
---

Phitsanulok has a lively evening food scene centred on the riverfront night market along the Nan River, where raft restaurants and street-food stalls serve local dishes. The city is known for its flying vegetables (phak bung fai daeng), a theatrical dish in which cooks toss morning glory through the air, which is worth seeking out.
