---
title: "Bars and Cafes"
type: section
---

**Patong's Bangla Road** is the famously chaotic centre of Phuket nightlife — a pedestrianised strip of go-go bars, beer bars, ladyboy cabarets and dance clubs that gets going around 9pm and keeps going until dawn. Soi Easy and Soi Tiger are dense with pop-up bars; the Bangla Boxing Stadium runs Muay Thai bouts most nights. It is loud, neon-lit, and not to everyone's taste.

For a calmer drink, **Kata** and **Karon** have lower-key beach bars and resort lounges; **Phuket Old Town** has the best independent bar scene, with craft cocktail and craft beer spots in restored shophouses around Thalang Road and Soi Romanee. Rooftop bars at the larger resorts in Surin and Kamala are popular for sunset.

The big organised entertainment shows — **Simon Cabaret** at Patong (a long-running ladyboy revue) and **Phuket FantaSea** at Kamala (a Vegas-style themed dinner show) — are touristy but professional, often included in package itineraries.
