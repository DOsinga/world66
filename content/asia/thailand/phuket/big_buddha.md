title: Big Buddha of Phuket
type: poi
---

A 45-metre seated Buddha clad in white Burmese marble, perched on Nakkerd Hill above Chalong with sweeping views over Kata, Karon and Phang Nga Bay. The statue was completed in 2014 and is largely funded by visitor donations. Free to visit; modest dress required (sarongs lent at the entrance). Best at sunrise or late afternoon.
