Kamala sits about 10 km north of Patong on Phuket's west coast, and the difference in atmosphere is pronounced. The beach itself is a long, curving strip of sand backed by casuarina trees rather than the wall of bars and hotels you find to the south. It attracts a mix of longer-stay visitors and families looking for something calmer. A small village at the southern end has restaurants and shops, and the sea is swimmable outside of monsoon season. The Phuket FantaSea entertainment complex on the northern edge adds a Vegas-style note, but it's easy enough to ignore.
