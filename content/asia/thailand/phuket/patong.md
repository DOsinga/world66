Patong is [Phuket](/asia/thailand/phuket)’s most developed tourist paradise with long white sandy beaches facing the blue Andaman sea and plenty of restaurants, shops and hotels. Many hotels offer all sorts of daytrips and day activities, such as parasailing and scuba diving. The nightlife has something to offer for everyone, ranging from cabaret, discos and bars to films in local restaurants.
