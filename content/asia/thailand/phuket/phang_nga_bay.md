The dramatic limestone-karst seascape east of Phuket — hundreds of jagged towers rising vertically from the shallow turquoise sea, riddled with sea caves and hidden lagoons. Famous as the location of the James Bond film *The Man with the Golden Gun*; the cone-shaped islet that featured prominently is now known to everyone as **James Bond Island** (Khao Phing Kan). Day trips by speedboat or longtail leave from several Phuket piers, typically combining the Bond island with sea-cave kayaking inside hidden hongs and a stop at the stilted Muslim fishing village of Koh Panyee.
