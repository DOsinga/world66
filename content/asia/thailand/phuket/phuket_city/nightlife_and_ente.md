---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment"
type: section
---

Most popular bar/club in Phuket city is Jammin' , in this cool place there is live (rock/reggae) music alternated with a DJ playing 'happy house' music.

If you're looking for a place that stays open after 2 'o clock you should check out the Sentavee Hotel, they have a club inside that stays open till late.

Further there are numerous beergardens with often live music and cheap drinks and food.

When you're too drunk to dance you might wanna check out a karaoke bar where you can sit down and lauch with/at all the people that try to sing a song.

The Timberhut is popular among locals and expats and provides a nice mix between dj's and live music.
