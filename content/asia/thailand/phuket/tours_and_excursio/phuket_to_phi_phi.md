Phi Phi Islands are one of the best beautiful island the world. There are two islands, Phi Phi Don and Phi Phi Ley. The larger and inhabited, Phi Phi Don attracts hundreds of visitors to stay on its lovely shores. The best places for relax and Snorkeling are Maya Bay, Pileh Island, Monkey Bay, Bamboo island and much more.
