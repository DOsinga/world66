Prachin Buri is located some 134 kilometers from Bangkok. It occupies an area 
 of 11,957.5 square kilometers and is administratively divided into seven Amphoes : 
 Muang, Ban Sang, Kabin Buri, Na Di, Prachantakham, Si Maha Phot and 
 Si Mahosot.
