SISAKET PROVINCE
Somdet Sri Nakharin Park, is a large public park inside an Agricultural college 2 kms from the provincial hall. At the park one can see flowering shrubs called Lamduan that are the official plants of the province. Lamduan is a Thai flower that is at its most enchanting when it blooms from March to Apric as its sweet fragrance covers the whole area.
