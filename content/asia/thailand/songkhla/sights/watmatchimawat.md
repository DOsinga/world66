Wat Matchimawat is situated on Saiburi Rd. It is the largest temple in Songkhla and over 400 years old. Inside the bot are some fine frescoes depicting the history of Songkhla and Buddhist philosophy. A museum within the compound has an interesting collection of ancient art objects found around the province.
