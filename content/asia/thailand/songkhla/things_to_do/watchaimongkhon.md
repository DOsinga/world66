title: "Wat Chai Mongkhon"

latitude: 7.1994

---

Standing at the corner of Petch Mongkhon and Chai Mongkhon Roads, this temple's centrepiece is a pagoda enshrining a revered Buddha bone relic. The relic was brought back from Sri Lanka in 1892 by the monk Na Issaro, who received it as a gift from a merchant during his travels there.
