Songkhla's largest and oldest temple, founded over 400 years ago on Saiburi Road. The bot is decorated with vivid frescoes depicting the history of Songkhla and scenes from Buddhist philosophy. Within the compound a small museum displays ancient art objects recovered from around the province.
