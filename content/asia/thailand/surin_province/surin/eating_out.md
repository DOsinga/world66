---
title: "Eating Out"
type: section
---

Surin's food scene is quintessential Isan — spicy, herbaceous, and built around sticky rice. The night market near the train station is the go-to spot for visitors, with stalls selling som tam, grilled chicken, and larb. For a sit-down meal, several small restaurants around the centre serve reliable Thai and Isan dishes at local prices.
