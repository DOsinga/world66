---
title: "Eating Out"
type: section
---

For those just visiting Surin for the first time an important stop could be the "Farang Connection".Located behind the local Bus Station and 500 m from the train station. A full service resturant and Sports Bar with a broadband access internet cafe. The English owner and the locals regulars are full of interesting information about the area and are pleased to help with advice and directions. Many languages spoken.
