---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

The headline attraction is Ban Chiang, an easy day trip from the city, but Udon Thani itself has a handful of rewarding spots. The lakeside park at Nong Prachak is the city's social centre, while the Pu-ya Shrine offers a glimpse of the local Chinese-Thai religious culture that flourishes in Isan's market towns.
