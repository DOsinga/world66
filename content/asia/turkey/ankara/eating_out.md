---
title: "Eating Out in Ankara"
type: section
---

Ankara is the capital of Turkey and is the second largest
city there. Restaurants in the city are scattered all over offer many different flavors. There are places that serve
Turkish food alongside places that specialize in Chinese or
Mediterranean food. Almost all of them include some sort of seafood in
their menus since that is a main staple in Turkish cooking. The
restaurants here are very diverse but still maintain the flavors of the region.

**Turkish Restaurants**

Turkish food is filled with lots of vegetables and subtle flavoring.
Most of
the vegetable dishes include some sort of fish or seafood element. Most Turkish dishes aren't heavily seasoned so that natural flavors can be discovered.
This cuisine is characterized by the use of herbs and spices like thyme, mint and cinnamon. Some
places to enjoy these Turkish flavors include Tike Kavaklidere which is
an beautiful restaurant that serves both Turkish and international cuisine at a fair price. Recep Usta is another restaurant that offers
delicious variations of Turkish dishes that average in prices around
$20. Other places to sample Turkish flavors are Uludag Kebapcisi, Kuki
House and Ballk Pisirme Evi.

**Seafood**

Restaurants all over Ankara serve seafood dishes but there are
always places that specialize in seafood and do it well. One of the
best places for seafood is Trilye Restaurant. This is an upper level
fine dining restaurant with dishes that cost around $50 to $80. It's a
beautiful atmosphere that would be the perfect place for a romantic
evening out. Another fine seafood restaurant is Kalbur which has a
great menu that offers dishes like stuffed calamari. If you want to eat
here it's a good idea to get a reservation and credit cards are not
accepted.

**Other Dining**

There are lots of restaurants in Ankara that serve a mix of
different cuisines. One place to try is the PepperMiLL. This place has
a menu that covers many different nationality's dishes. Num Num is a
restaurant that serves American Italian foods. Big Chefs is an
international restaurant that serves western foods with Turkish twists.
The average price of a meal here is around $15.
