---
title: "Bars and Cafes"
type: section
---

Bodrum's nightlife has been famous across Europe since the 1980s. The waterfront bar street — known locally as Barlar Sokağı — is where most of the action concentrates. Halikarnas, the legendary open-air disco, set the template for the town's clubbing scene, though venues come and go with the seasons. The scene runs late, with many places not filling up until midnight.
