---
title: "Eating Out"
type: section
---

Bodrum has restaurants for every budget, from simple kebab stands to waterfront fish restaurants. Restaurant Alley near the harbour is packed with options — Ibo is a local favourite for Turkish meze and dishes like hünkar beğendi, tender lamb on a puréed aubergine and cheese sauce. For the best seafood, take the short trip to the fishing village of Gümüslük, where restaurants line the waterfront and the fish is as fresh as it gets.
