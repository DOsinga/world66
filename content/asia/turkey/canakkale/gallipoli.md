The World War One battlefields known as Gallipoli are just 15 km from Çanakkale.

For eight and a half months in 1915, men from across the world fought here in an effort to open the Dardanelles, which had been closed when the Ottoman Empire entered the war on the side of Germany.

A visit to the battlefields has become a pilgrimage for Australians and New Zealanders visiting Turkey. Daily tours of the battlefields operate from Çanakkale.
