Tribal Collections Carpets and Kilims is one of the most reputable rug
shops in Turkey. It's located in Goreme and had an wonderful
collection of antique and new rugs, kilims and fascinating tribal
pieces. Ruth Lockwood,a Kiwi who has lived in Turkey for 16
years is one of the owners and extremely knowledgeable about all
rugweaving traditions and the culture in general. They have fixed
prices and an impressive client list and some of the most beautiful
rugs I saw in Turkey!
