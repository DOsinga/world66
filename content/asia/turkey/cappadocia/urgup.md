Urgup is one of the main base towns for exploring [Cappadocia](/asia/turkey/cappadocia), along with Goreme and Avanos. It has a slightly more upmarket feel than Goreme — the old stone houses on the hillside have been converted into boutique hotels, and the town centre has good restaurants and a lively evening atmosphere.

The fairy chimneys, underground cities, and rock-cut churches that define Cappadocia are all within easy reach. Many tour operators run day trips from Urgup covering the main valleys, though renting a car or even walking gives you more freedom. The hot air balloon flights that have become synonymous with the region launch at dawn from fields nearby.
