---
title: "Dogubeyazit"
type: location
---

Dogubeyazit, or jocularly referred to as "Doggy biscuit" is a small but pleasant town on the road to Iran. Situated in the far East of Turkey at the side of sweeping grass plains that run all the way up to Mount Ararat (supposed restingplace of Noah's Ark) you will enjoy the small size of the town, the unexpected friendlyness and be able to talk to Iranians spending their holiday abroad.
