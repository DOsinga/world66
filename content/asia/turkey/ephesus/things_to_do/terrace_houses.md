The Terrace Houses — two blocks of multi-storey Roman townhouses on the hillside above the main street — are among the most remarkable residential remains in the ancient world. Extraordinarily well preserved under collapse debris, they still have mosaic floors, frescoed walls, and marble panelling intact. An additional entrance fee applies, but the detail and the ongoing excavation work make them essential. English-language audio guides are available.
