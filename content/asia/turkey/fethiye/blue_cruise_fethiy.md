A four-day gulet cruise from Fethiye Harbour south to Olympos, taking in Butterfly Valley, Oludeniz (the Blue Lagoon), St Nicholas Island, Kas, the sunken city of Kekova, the fishing village of Simena, and the ruins of Myra before a bus transfer to the tree houses at Olympos. All meals, harbour fees, and snorkelling equipment included.
