The classic Turkish Blue Cruise runs from Fethiye harbour south along the Lycian coast to Olympos, typically over three or four days aboard a traditional wooden gulet. The route takes in Butterfly Valley, the Blue Lagoon at Oludeniz, St Nicholas Island, the sunken city of Kekova (where you peer down at submerged Lycian ruins from the deck), and the fishing village of Simena with its hilltop castle.

Dozens of operators run this route from Fethiye; prices vary widely depending on the boat, season, and group size. Budget boats pack in backpackers and run loud music; pricier charters offer smaller groups and quieter anchorages. Most include all meals and snorkelling gear. The trip ends with a bus transfer to the treehouse camps at Olympos. Book from the harbour or through your guesthouse — comparing a few options in person is worthwhile, since the boat condition matters more than the brochure.
