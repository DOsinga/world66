The Arena Restaurant is situated overlooking Palm Island and the marina
in Fethiye. It has a upper open terrace gallery which offers wonderful
views for alfresco dining and a stylish main dining room. The menu
offers beautiful fresh fish and sea food, meat and poultry in
traditional Turkish and International styles. The staff are really
friendly and whilst I would suggest it should be the highlight to your
holiday dining experience, the prices do not reflect this as it offers
really good value. I would recommend this restaurant to anyone, from a
guest of Marina View Apartment in Karagozlar Fethiye.
