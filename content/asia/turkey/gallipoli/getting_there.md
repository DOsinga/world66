---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

Most visitors come to Gallipoli on a day tour from **Çanakkale** on the Asian side of the Dardanelles. Frequent **car and passenger ferries** cross the strait in about 20 minutes, and there are ferry terminals at Eceabat and Kilitbahir on the European side. Çanakkale itself is a six-hour bus ride from Istanbul (around 320 km) or a short hop from İzmir or Bursa. On 25 April (Anzac Day) tour buses run direct all-night services from Istanbul to the dawn service at ANZAC Cove; book months in advance. From the ferry at Eceabat, the main battlefield sites are scattered over 20–30 km of coast road; without your own vehicle, you will want a guided tour.
