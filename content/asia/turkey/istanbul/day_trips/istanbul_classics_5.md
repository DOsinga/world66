**DAY 01 – Arrival in Istanbul
**Upon clearing customs at Istanbul Airport, you will be met by one of our representatives staff who will arrange the transfer to your hotel. You will have the rest of the afternoon/evening to spend at your leisure exploring the sights of Istanbul.

**DAY 02 – Bosphorus Cruise and Dolmabahce Palace Tour**
Your day will start with journey along the Golden Horn, the arm of the Bosphorus peninsula which separates the old and new cities of Istanbul. Follow the traces of history in Balat at the Fener Orthodox Patriarchate, proceeding on to visit the Metal Church of St Stephen the Bulgarian, Ahrida Synagouge, Eyup Sultan Mosque and see the 21km long City Walls of Istanbul. Next stop, the Egyptian Market (Spice Bazaar), with different aromas of various spices From here, join a cruise along the Bosphorus on one of the iconic ferries of Istanbul giving you the chance to see the two bridges that link Europe and Asia. Disembark the ferry and enjoy a trip over the Intercontinental Bosphorus Bridge to Camlica Hill, the highest hill in Asiatic Istanbul to enjoy breathtaking views of both shores.
