Steps from the Chora Church, Asitane specialises in Ottoman court cuisine — dishes researched from historical sources and rarely found elsewhere. It's a serious restaurant with serious prices, but the food is genuinely unlike anything you'll eat in the tourist centre. Worth combining with a visit to Kariye Camii.
