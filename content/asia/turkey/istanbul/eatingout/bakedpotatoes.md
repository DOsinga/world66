There used to be lots of guys here selling baked potatoes (‘baket potatoes’ by thier reckoning!), but now there’s only one left and he does a nice filled potato too! If you’re vegetarian or don’t like processed meat, just ask for the vegetarian or "etsiz" version of filled spud. It costs around $2.50-3 for a huge potato filled to overflowing with all kinds of salad, olives and chillie sauce with lashings of butter and salt. You can choose whatever you want or you can let the guys choose for you.
