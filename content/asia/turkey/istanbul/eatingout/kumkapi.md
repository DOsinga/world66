Kumkapi is a neighbourhood on the back of the Sultanahmet hill packed with fish restaurants spilling onto the street. Every taxi driver knows it, and in summer the outdoor tables are packed. Prices are higher than the tourist traps of Sultanahmet proper but the fish is fresh. For a splurge, Tarabya further up the Bosphorus European shore is even better — take the ferry up and a bus back.
