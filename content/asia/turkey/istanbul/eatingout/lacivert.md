Lacivert Restaurant is furnishing services since 1999 in a historical waterside mansion at the Bosphorus, at a spectacular quay during summer months, in cosy saloons with windows that hold the scene with their new design durin the winter months. Lacivert Restaurant is located right under one of two Bosphorus bridges. You can get here by boat (the restaurant has a boat and will take you from European side to the restaurant in Asian side) and is free of charge or you can go by car on bridge. They serve great seafood (it is not a cheap restaurant) and the view is amazing! Afiyet olsun (bon appetit)!
