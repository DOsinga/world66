On the Bosphorus waterfront directly below the Fatih Sultan Mehmet Bridge, Lacivert has been serving excellent seafood since 1999. The restaurant will send a complimentary boat from the European side to collect you. Not cheap, but the setting — historic waterside mansion, bridge overhead, Bosphorus view — justifies the price.
