A quiet vegetarian-leaning restaurant tucked off Istiklal on a tiny side street near the Fitas cinema. The menu is short but well-considered: stuffed cabbage, pasta, chicken curry, good salads. Warm provincial atmosphere, reasonable prices, and a reprieve from the noise outside. Open daily 11am–11:30pm.
