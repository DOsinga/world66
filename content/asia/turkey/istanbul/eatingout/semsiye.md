A small, calm restaurant in the Tünel neighbourhood run by two friends with a daily-changing menu of vegetarian-leaning Turkish food. Artichokes stuffed with rice, soya dishes, fresh soups and salads. The menu is written on paper each morning; dinner for two without drinks costs around €15. A good pick before heading out for the evening.
