Turks like to be entertained when they eat. At home, it'll be the family and friends, outside it'll be the shows - if there are any. [Sultana's is an Istanbul belly dancing and dinner venue](/asia/turkey/istanbul/eatingout/sultanas_dinner_a)
with other shows such as Turkish folk dancing and singing, and is attended by locals and foreign visitors to Istanbul.

Meze, kebabs and fish are served, as well as vegetarian dishes. On stage, Turkish music is sung live as well.

Sultana's is located near major Istanbul hotels, in Taksim.
