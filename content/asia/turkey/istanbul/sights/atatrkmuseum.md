The Atatürk Museum is the house where Ataturk lived and worked in before the War of Independence. Atatürk (literally meaning Father of Turks) is the founder of the modern Turkish stated and held in great esteem in modern Turkey.

 *Open daily between 9:30-4:00 p.m. except Thursdays and Sundays.*
