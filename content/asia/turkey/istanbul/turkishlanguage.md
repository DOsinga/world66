Learning Turkish as a "yabanci" [foreigner] is not really easy, but the people - as everywhere - really appreciate if you make an effort. If you want to learn basic words and sentences buy a Turkish language course which is available in every book shop selling English language books. There are quite a few on the Istiklal Caddesi between Tünnel and Taksim.

  Turkish is a very complicated language to learn with some special letters in the alphabet. Pronunciation is not the problem, but grammar is. To master a few words is not difficult however and will be greatly appreciated by the locals: you will find out, it is really rewarding!  Here are some essential words. For more information on the Turkish language go to Travlang.  Yes = evet  No = hayir Thank you = tesekkürler; tesekkür ederim  Thank you very much = cok tesekür ederim  You're welcome = Bir sey de-gil  Please = lütfen  Excuse me = özur dilerim, pardon  Hello = merhaba; selam  What's up = ne haber? Goodbye = güle güle, alahsmaladik  So long = Görüsürüz  Good morning = günaydin  Good afternoon = iyi gunler, iyi ö-glenler  Good evening = iyi aksamlar  Good night = iyi geceler  I do not understand = anlamiyorum How do you say this in =Bu [Türkce] nasil soylenir? [Turkish]?  Do you speak ... = ... biliyormusunuz;  English = Ingilizce French = Fransizca  German = Almanca Spanish = Ispanyolca Chinese = Cince
