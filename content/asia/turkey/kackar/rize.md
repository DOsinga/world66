Rize sits on the Black Sea coast at the foot of the Kaçkar Mountains, about 70 km east of Trabzon airport and 90 km from the Georgian border. It is the heart of Turkey's tea country — the steep green hillsides above the town are covered in tea plantations, and the drink is a matter of local pride. Rize serves as the main gateway to the northern approaches of the Kaçkar range.
