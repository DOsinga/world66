title: "Bars and Cafes in Side"
type: section
---

**Side Bars and Cafes** are scattered throughout this well-known resort town and classic Turkish site. Side, Turkey, located on the Mediterranean coast, sees an influx of tourists each year who come to bask in the sun and swim in the beautiful waters of this resort town. In recent years, Side had a seen a boom in tourism, and it is no wonder with it's modern architecture, boutique shops, great restaurants and picturesque town among the ruins of the ancient city. Here World66 will highlight some of Side's best bars and cafes:

**The Royal Castle Pub**

Located behind the Aphrodite Restaurant, this pub is within walking distance from the harbor. The Royal Castle Pub can be classified as a typical Irish pub with lively crowds, loud music and friendly faces. The Royal Castle is a live music venue, so it is a good idea to check the calendar of events if you are looking to catch a live music act while visiting Side. The pub also caters to Europeans by offering Sky sports channels, and a number of televisions for prime football watching. Many locals, as well as visitors, come to the Royal Castle to watch their favorite teams play in rivalry football matches. The pub serves a limited menu with decent food, and a wide range of cocktails, beers, wines and spirits. All prices at the Royal Castle are listed in euro, so it can be a little pricey.

**Appolonic Cafe**

A quaint, romantic spot, this cafe is especially great for watching the beautiful Side sunsets. Appolonic is located on the harbour, on the east side. If you are heading to the Apollo Temple, you are sure to pass right by the Appolonic Cafe. The cafe has a broad menu with local seafood, sandwiches and traditional Turkish dishes. Opened for lunch and dinner, Appolonic cafe is a nice quiet cafe with light music and great sea views of the harbor. Prices at Appolonic are moderate.

**Mehmet's Cafe - Bar **

A family owned and operated quality establishment, Mehmet's Cafe - Bar offers guests excellent service, great views, superb music--all at a reasonable price. The menu is simple and on the basic side, but what they do, they do well. Mehmet's has an excellent view of the main beach and harbor, on the eastern side of the antique town. Mehmet's is a great place to relax and unwind after a long day of sightseeing and shopping. Mehmet's serves lunch and dinner
