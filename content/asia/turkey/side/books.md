**Stationery Shop**

There is one stationery shop in Side, if you are entering Side on the main street if you take the second left which is called gul street than take the first right it s on the left handside. This shop offers a small selection of Turkish and German very poor selection of other languages. But offers a good selection of stationery items. Some of the pensions have books that can be borrowed. There s a place called Kiss Bar at the end of mosque street which has quite a good selection of books that can be borrowed. If you are looking for newspapers several of the markets in Side offer newspapers from various countries. Should you wish to buy a book the nearest town Manavgat can offer better selection.
