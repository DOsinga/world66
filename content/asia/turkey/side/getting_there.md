---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

Antalya Airport is the nearest airport, about 65km west. Most package tourists arrive on charter flights from European cities. From the airport, minibuses (dolmus) connect to the Side junction on the main coast road, from where local minibuses or taxis cover the final stretch into town. A taxi from Antalya Airport takes about 50 minutes.

From Antalya city, regular dolmus services run along the coast to Side throughout the day.
