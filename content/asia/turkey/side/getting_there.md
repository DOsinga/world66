When you have decided to visit the classical town of** Side,** **Getting There** can be a very easy endeavor. Still you want a stress free vacation so a little bit of information will help you out a lot. It is important to determine what mode of transportation you will take. It may be that you are interested in comfort or in just getting there as fast as you can. Another very important aspect is that passports, visas and other documentation may be needed to enter a country.

**Ways of Entering Side **

There is only one way to get to this antiquated city and that is by car. First you can arrange a flight into the international airport at Antalya. They serve airlines coming and going out of the United Kingdom year round. You will find it easy to rent a car right at the airport. They are inexpensive and will give you the flexibility to go where you want on your trip. The ride to Side from the airport is approximately 50 minutes.

**Passport and Visas**

It is vitally important to understand some information on the documentation you will need before entering Turkey. All visitors must have a passport on them at all times. Visas are going to be required by most visitors and especially by people from the United Kingdom. The fastest and less frustrating way to do this is to approach the separate sales clerk. Get that taken care of before you go to the passport control clerk. It is suggested that you get a visa from a Turkish Consulate if available before coming here on your wonderful trip to Side.
