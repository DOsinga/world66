---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Side packs an unusual number of well-preserved ancient sites into a small area. The theatre, the Temple of Apollo, the agora, and the museum are all within easy walking distance of each other in the old town. Outside the historic core, the beaches, boat trips to the Manavgat waterfall, and river rafting on the Köprülü Canyon are popular activities.
