---
title: "When to Go"
type: section
---

May, June, and September are the best months to visit Side. The weather is warm and sunny, the sea is swimable, and the crowds are manageable. July and August are peak season: hot (35°C+), extremely busy, and expensive. The archaeological sites are best visited in the morning before the heat builds.

October is pleasant for sightseeing if not swimming. Side is essentially closed from November to March as it is a seasonal resort.
