Chardzhou — now officially known as Türkmenabat — is Turkmenistan's second city, sitting on the Amu Darya river close to the Uzbekistan border. It is primarily a transit point for travelers crossing between Turkmenistan and Uzbekistan. The Trans-Caspian Railway passes through here on its way to Tashkent.
