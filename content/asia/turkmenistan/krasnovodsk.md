Krasnovodsk is the former Soviet-era name for the Caspian port city now called [Turkmenbashi](/asia/turkmenistan/turkmenbashi). The city was renamed in 1993 after the president gave it his own title. See [Turkmenbashi](/asia/turkmenistan/turkmenbashi) for current information.
