---
title: "Bars and Cafes"
type: section
---

Alcohol is available in Abu Dhabi but only in licensed hotels and clubs — not standalone bars. The better hotels along the Corniche and on Yas Island have well-stocked bars. Arabian-style cafes serving strong coffee and shisha are far more common and stay open late. The Corniche area has several coffee shops with outdoor seating looking out toward the Gulf.
