**Introduction**

Abu Dhabi is truly blessed with over 400 km of coastline boasting some
gorgeous beaches that will not disappoint even the most experienced
traveler. These **Abu Dhabi beaches **are equipped with the latest
amenities such as barbecue and picnic facilities and you can indulge in
fun activities on them such as water sports. As with many such tourist
areas, it's better to avoid the high season which, typically, is when
the weather cools down rather than when it heats up.

**Corniche**

The most appealing aspect of Abu Dhabi
is the Corniche which is a redeveloped leisure zone running parallel to
the sea offering an entertainment park, shopping malls and excellent
restaurants. Its walkway allows one to roller skate, cycle and jog.
Nearby are beautifully designed picnic grounds and parks that attract
many visitors during high season but are still very pleasant places to
spend your time. Many people, Abu Dhabi residents and visitors alike,
are intensely proud of the Corniche and cite it as a real move forward
in the history of the city.**
**

**New Beach**

Abu Dhabi beaches just got better with
the establishment of the beach attached to the Corniche. The beach
opened in summer 2008 and offers a stunning range of attractions from
designated safe swimming areas to luxury eateries to pathways, gardens
and volleyball pitches. Kids can take pleasure in the specialized areas
reserved for them and there are many conveniences. A new central building on the Corniche is under
construction which will house a mosque, changing rooms and eating areas.
The venue can be rented for parties, functions and family get-togethers
and serves very many useful purposes. Benches have been provided by
the local council allowing visitors to relax, catch some excellent
sights and sunbathe on this stand-out example of the best among Abu
Dhabi beaches.

**For Your Safety**
On the beach, lifeguards
overlook swimmers from 7 surveillance towers specially constructed to
afford the best views of the surroundings. Visitors are restricted to
swimming off the beach only at certain times of the day for their own
safety. There are also almost 200 umbrellas to stop you from catching the
sun too seriously. For added protection there are floating fences that
stretch 50 meters into the sea which stop people from swimming too far
out of their depth. You can also take peace of mind from the police
station that has been newly built nearby this very best of the Abu Dhabi
beaches.
