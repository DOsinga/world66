---
title: "Day Trips"
type: section
---

Al Ain, about 140km east of Abu Dhabi, is the UAE's fourth-largest city and a UNESCO World Heritage site for its ancient falaj (irrigation) system and oasis gardens. The Al Ain Zoo is one of the best in the region. The drive through the dunes approaching the city is itself part of the appeal.

The Liwa Oasis, about 200km south, sits at the edge of the Empty Quarter and is surrounded by some of the highest sand dunes in the world. Moreeb Dune rises 300 metres. The drive down is dramatic and the oasis has date plantations and a handful of places to stay.
