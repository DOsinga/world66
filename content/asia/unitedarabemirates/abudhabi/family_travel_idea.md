---
title: "Family Travel Ideas in Abu dhabi"
type: section
---

Your** Abu Dhabi Family Travel **itinerary** **is sure to have
something to please every member of your family. Abu Dhabi is a great
vacationing spot. It is fun and excitement personified. It offers all
around entertainment and activities for the young as well as the old. Be
it the museums or nightlife, Abu Dhabi has it all. Here are a few
family travel ideas you can use to make the most of your visit to Abu
Dhabi.

**Unleash the Wild Side**

The sand dunes and the desert safari constitute a great family
activity. From riding the dessert in a powerful 4-wheel drive, to
camel rides this will be a highly adventurous day. Relax in the
traditional Arabian tents and enjoy Arabian delicacies around a
bonfire. You can also treat yourself to some Hookah and belly dancing
by the locals.

**Hili Fun City**

This is a great park for the whole family, especially the children.
Hili Fun City is the most renowned amusement park in the whole of the
Gulf region. Equipped with roller coasters, theater and around forty
rides, this is a haven for those seeking fun. It has a carnival like
feel with food stalls and entertainment.

**The Heritage Village**

There is no better way to explore and understand the history and
essence of Abu Dhabi, than visiting the Heritage Village. Located close
to the Breakwater, this spot is a true tribute to the soul of Abu
Dhabi. Peek into the Bedouin lifestyle of the past and buy souvenirs
and other handicraft items.

**The Corniche**

Check out this spectacular waterfront, which has been newly
redeveloped. It offers a breathtaking view of the Abu Dhabi skyline.
The boulevard-like appearance is a great place to take a romantic
stroll, ride a bicycle or go skating. The entertainment quotient is
always high due to the numerous cafes, amusements spots and top-notch
restaurants. The park is a great place to have a family picnic. It is
also the known as the Mecca for shopping.

**Pampering Personified**

Escape the hustle and bustle of Abu Dhabi and retire with your
partner into one of the many top spas in the city. Most of the spas
have monitored play areas for children. Check out the famous Anantara Spa located in
the luxurious Emirates Palace Hotel. Known for its Thai, Ayurvedic and
Swedish massages, this place is the ultimate relaxation spot.
Rejuvenate your tired self and go back with fond memories of Abu Dhabi.
