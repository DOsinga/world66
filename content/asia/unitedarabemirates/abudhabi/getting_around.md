As one of the richest and most modern cities in the world, **Abu Dhabi transportation **is just as wonderful as its facilities and infrastructure. No matter where you want to go, you can find appropriate transportation that is reasonably priced, comfortable and easy to use, as well.

**Taxis **

The white and gold taxis are the primary means for local transport in this emirate, especially if you do not have your own car. Hiring a taxi can take you anywhere within the emirate or even from here to another emirate. You would also have to use another taxi for traveling
