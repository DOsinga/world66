---
title: "Getting Around"
type: section
---

Abu Dhabi is primarily a driving city, and most visitors rely on taxis or ride-hailing apps (Careem, Uber). The white-and-gold taxis are metered and generally reliable. Public buses cover the main routes, though services are less frequent than in Dubai. The city is walkable along the Corniche, but distances between attractions are often too large to cover entirely on foot.
