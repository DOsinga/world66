---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment in Abu dhabi"
type: section
---

The **Abu Dhabi Nightlife **and Entertainment will take you by
surprise, with its wide range of offerings. From night clubs and
lounges to restaurants and cafes, Abu Dhabi has it all. Read on to find
out more.**
**

**Nightclubs**

The night clubs in Abu Dhabi add spunk to the nightlife. To cater
to the tourists and the expats, major initiatives have been taken to
give the clubbing scene a face lift. The result is a plethora of
English bars, European wine bars, discotheques and lounges. The best
among the lot is the Zenith bar, the pride of Hilton, Abu Dhabi. The mood is set with high energy music and exotic cocktails, paired
with delicious finger food. Ladies Nights and Happy Hours are a popular
trend in this bar.

**Cafes**

The Arabian style cafe runs till the wee hours of the morning. Coffee is an
integral factor in the social life of Abu Dhabi. The cafes are a great
place to unwind after a day’s work or sightseeing. Shisha, the
traditional Arabian smoking pot is available in most of the cafes
around Abu Dhabi. Select from a wide variety of flavors. The snacks are
made fresh and most of the cafes feature both a la carte as well as
buffets.

**Restaurants**

The Finz restaurant is the ultimate spot to enjoy seafood in Abu
Dhabi. It gives and image of a floating restaurant as it is a shack-like structure built over the Arabian Gulf water. The fish is fresh and
calling the dishes delicious would be an understatement. There is also
meat and chicken to choose from. Ask for the day’s specials and the
chef's recommendations in case you are not sure what to order.

**Shows**

Laugh your night away by going to one of the comedy shows The Crown
Plaza hotel in Abu Dhabi hosts. These shows are highly entertaining and
are totally worth visiting. Standup comedy is the theme there, and is a
hit amongst the British population in Abu Dhabi.**
**

**Cinema**

The world of cinema plays a very important role in the cultural life
of Abu Dhabi. Film festivals, both national and international, are held
here. Spend the night out with family by watching a Hollywood flick at
the Grand Abu Dhabi mall. They have a great ambiance and show the
latest movies.
