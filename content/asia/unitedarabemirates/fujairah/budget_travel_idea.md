---
title: "Budget Travel Ideas in Fujairah"
type: section
---

Traveling on a budget is always a smart thing to do. Luckily, in Fujairah, it is easy to do as there are many accommodations, methods of transportation and things to do that are either free or very budget friendly.

**Accommodations

**There is a large variety of hotels and resorts in Fujairah. With the majority of the the accommodations along the beach, hotel rates in Fujairah arn't necessarily cheap but there are some things to keep in mind to save money. Beach front or ocean front rooms are much more expensive so book a room without a view to save nearly $100 a night.

**Transportation

**The cheapest way to get around Fujairah is by bus. Much cheaper than taking a taxi or renting a car, buses are available on a daily basis to transport you and your family from your hotel to your destination, whether it be across town or to a neighboring emirate. Walking or riding a bike is not recommended in the city of Fujairah because the city center is very commercialized and is primarily all roads and buildings, leaving little sidewalk space.
**
Things To Do

**A visit to the Fujairah Fort and the surrounding Fujairah Heritage Village is a free experience that will interest everyone in your party. The Fujariah Fort is an ancient fortress that was built over 300 years ago. Surrounding this castle are numerous houses equipped with tools, pottery and attire from this period that make up the Fujairah Heritage Village. All tours are free and will give you great insight into the culture of this region.

As Fujairah is situated along the Persian Gulf, an afternoon spent basking in the sun at one of the many beaches in Fujairah is a must. If staying at a hotel or resort on the beach, the private beach owned by the hotel will be yours to use. If not staying at one of these accommodations, visit a public beach such as Sandy Beach for a free afternoon of relaxation.

Hiking in the Hajjar mountain range surrounding Fujairah is another free experience that you will not want to pass on. Free to the public, you can find a parking spot along the coastal highway and from there, follow one of the countless trails into the rocky hillsides to experience the beautiful scenery this region has to offer.
