title: "Sights in Fujairah"
type: section
---

Fujairah has many different sites to explore that are both
interesting and fun to visit. This area is currently developing into a
resort beach area in the United Arab Emerits and has many sites to see
and things to do.

**Le Meridien Al Aqah Beach Resort**

Le Meridien Al Aqah Beach Resort is near a reef where many different
corals grow. If you are a certified diver you can take advantage of
the equipment they have to rent and dive to see these corals and
several old wrecks in the Indian Ocean.

**Ain Al Madhab Gardens**

Ain Al Madhab Gardens is a beautiful spot near the Hajar Mountains. There are many mineral spa springs in this area and you can get rid of
tired muscles here. That isn’t the only thing to do in the Gardens. There is a small zoo, an outdoor theater, and a heritage village where
you can explore some of the traditional homes of the area and see
displays of pottery, cookware, fishing boats and also explore the ruins
of the old Fujairah fort. This fort is currently being renovated after
being severely damaged by the British in the 20th Century.

**Bull Fighting**

A site one must see in Fujairah is Bull Fighting, which is very
popular here. It is a little different than what one would expect and
not bloody at all. Most bullfighting takes place between a bull and a
bull fighter, but not in Fujairah. Instead they put two bulls together
and watch as they struggle together to see who has the most strength. It is unique.
