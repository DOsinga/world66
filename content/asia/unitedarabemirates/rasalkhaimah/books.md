### [Spectrum Guide to the United Arab Emirates](/asia/unitedarabemirates/rasalkhaimah/books/spectrum_guide_to)

This book will really help you if you are planning to visit the United Arab Emirates. A journey spread over 364 pages with beautiful
illustrations and detailed description are the main features of the
book. The dimensions of the book are 8.6 x 5.8 x 0.9 inches and the
overall weight of the book is also very light, making it easy to carry.

The book is published by Interlink Publishing Group. The book covers
all the seven states of UAE and provides information about the history
and economic boom of the country. The business and the economy are
