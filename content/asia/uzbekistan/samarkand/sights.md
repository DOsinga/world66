**Shah i Zinda**

This small street with tombs has some remarkable examples of the rich decorative tiles. Some of the finest you will ever see. Great colors. 

**Registan**

Three enormous coranic schools around a square. All three are richly decorated in a special style. Don't miss it or you'll be sorry for the rest of your days.
