Getting around Tashkent is very easy, as long as you know what type of **Tashkent transportation **you should hire. Tashkent is the capital of Uzbekistan. This particular city has a population of 2.1 million, thereby making this city quite populous. Transportation options in Tashkent are numerous, but here are some of the most common means that people and tourists take.

**Tashkent Metro System**

The fastest way to get around the city is by riding the underground railway system in Tashkent. The great thing about this particular transportation option is that it is cheap, fast and efficient. The railway station is also very clean and organized, which makes traveling amongst tourists very easy. Moreover, the railway system is really easy to understand, as there are maps that are located in each subway station. In addition, the stations are decorated with marbles and other intricate architectural features, which makes this particular transportation option very popular among tourists. Train tickets in the Tashkent Metro System cost between $5 and $10.

**Tashkent Bus **

Another way of getting around Tashkent is by riding the bus, which takes travelers anywhere in the city. The buses in Tashkent are painted green; thus, it is really not difficult to miss them. The bus stops on designated posts only, so it is important that tourists should wait on designated posts in order to get in the bus. Bus fares cost between $2 and $5, depending on the destination.
