**CUC PHUONG NATIONAL PARK Code: CP G01 ****
****1 Day Tour** **- Daily Departure from Hanoi**

**AM**: 7:45 Pick up from your hotel in Hanoi by bus. Depart to Ninh Binh. 3 hrs road trip with stop-over in Ninh Binh (Hotel in Ninhbinh). 10h30 Visit the Endangered Primate Rescue Centre, See wild Animals and Birds. Picnic lunch and relax in the Park.
**PM**: 13:00 Trekking (5km) to the Park Centre , Get deep inside the forest to come closer with the wild nature. On the way visit the "Cave of Early Man", admire the primary forest with "1000 year Old Tree",
