**Truc Lam Buddhist Pagoda**
***Location: Dalat, Vietnam***

Truc Lam Buddhist Pagoda is located on Phuong Hoang Mountain, by Tuyen Lam Lake in Ward 3, Dalat City, Lam Dong Province.
Truc Lam Buddhist Pagoda is located amidst beautiful natural scenery. This is the biggest pagoda in Dalat City.

The pagoda is covering an area of about 24ha with a Buddhist institute and a 22ha garden.The pagoda was inaugurated in 1994.

The
Bonze Superior Thich Thanh Tu, who built and still manages this pagoda,
is also the Buddhist dignitary of Thuong Chieu Buddhist Pagoda in Dong
Nai.
