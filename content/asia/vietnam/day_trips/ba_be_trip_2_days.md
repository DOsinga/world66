Overview

Explore this beautiful natural Lake
located in Ba Be National Park, where there is dramatic landscape and authentic
culture, it is only 280 km from Hanoi. After careful review, Viet Vision Travel
would like to offer a 2 day trip to Ba Be Lake for those who love exploring
nature combined with local culture with a schedule that does not rush you.
Within 2 days you will be able to enjoy sightseeing to beautiful ethnic
villages in the region around the national park; this will be via an easy bike
ride from the local village where you are having your home stay. There will
also be a tranquil boat trip on Ba Be Lake and the River Nang to view romantic
and imposing landscape.

**DAY
1 : HANOI- BA BE LAKE (B,D)**

Our trip should depart Hanoi at 7.30 AM, heading straight to center
north of Vietnam. After about 2 hour drive, we will have coffee-break at **Cho
Lu (Lu Market),** then start to enter the mountainous region where
beautiful dramatic natural landscape alternates with ethnic villages located at
the foot of the mountains. The drive will be slow-paced and peaceful, we will
have a chance to stop to take photos, enjoy the views...Upon arrival, we enjoy
the well-established home-stay in **Pac Ngoi**, with the**
Tay people**. Around 1 PM, we will spend a couple of hours to enjoy slow
biking along village lanes to explore neighborhoods. **Overnight at the
hotel in Ba Be**.
