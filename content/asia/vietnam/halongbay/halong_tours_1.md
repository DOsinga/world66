**Day 1** **Hanoi -Halong**

8h00 Depart from Hanoi by private car.

12h30: Board Hai Au Junk, check-in registration

13h00: Have lunch with welcome wine.

13h30: A fantastic cruise in Ha Long bay to visit Thien Cung cave , Dau Go cave, round over Tuan Chau island, Dinh Huong island, Ga Choi island, Dog island, Sail island and a swim at Titov beach. Enjoy dinner on board while watching sunset. Fishing with seine, drinking Vietnamese wine, and spending a night on junk.

**Day 2** **Halong - Hanoi**

7h00 : Have breakfast.

7h30 : A morning cruise to visit Man’s head island, Tortoise island, Bai Tu Long bay, Toad island, Bai tho mountain.

11h00 : Return to Halong city and have lunch on board.
