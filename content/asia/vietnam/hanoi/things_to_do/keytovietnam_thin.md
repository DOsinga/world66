About The Things To Do In Vietnam

You will be of course making visits to the various Vietnam popular destinations on your Vietnam holidays. But do take a moment out of your whirlwind round of sightseeing and head out for shopping in Vietnam. Vietnam may not be a retail haven like Bangkok or Paris, but it is by no means a wet blanket either. Souvenirs are found here by the cartful and so are traditional Vietnamese handicrafts, mostly lacquer ware. Vietnamese jewelry is renowned for its exquisite workmanship and it is a good idea to stock up on a few of these pieces while you are browsing in the shops.
Things to do in Vietnam give you choices aplenty, especially for the shopaholics. Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam and Hanoi, Vietnam are the places to head for if you are scouting for trendy clothing and real antique pieces respectively. Tourists in Vietnam also prefer the Vietnam War- Army watches and military clothing to take away with them as souvenirs.
Flushed from your shopping success, now it's time to head out for the other entertainment spots in Vietnam. Entertainment in Vietnam and especially the nightlife in Vietnam is nothing to be scoffed at. Take a look at your Vietnam travel guide and you will be quite surprised to discover that beer, pool and discos are not hard to come by, even in the far-flung and remote mountainous regions like Sapa. Karaoke bars are very popular amongst the various nightspots of Vietnam.

Vietnam has arrived, man! There are numerous bars and nightclubs in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam, the most popular ones amongst them being Apocalypse Now, Q Bar, Underground Bar and Grill and Carmen Bar. Nha Trang too offers an array of choices with regards to bars and nightclubs. With these options, you certainly wouldn't need to worry about the things to do in Vietnam after dusk sets in.
