---
title: "When to Go"
type: section
---

Hanoi has two seasons and a long shoulder between them. **October–November** is the best time — dry, mild, blue-skied, with temperatures around 20–25°C. **March–April** is the other sweet spot, though slightly warmer and more humid. **May–September** is the southern monsoon: hot, humid, sticky, with heavy afternoon rain. **December–February** is surprisingly cold — it can dip to 10°C, grey skies for weeks on end, and Hanoians layer up in parkas they seem to own just for these months.

Avoid **Tet** (Lunar New Year, late January or early February) unless you want to experience it: prices spike, most shops and many restaurants close for a week, and the city empties as people go home to family villages.
