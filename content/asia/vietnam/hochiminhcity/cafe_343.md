A popular local hangout on Nguyen Trai in District 1, Cafe 343 spreads across two air-conditioned levels with an outdoor area centred around a fountain. Screens throughout play music videos, movies, and sports, giving the place an energetic buzz without being overwhelming. The menu is in both Vietnamese and English, with the usual range of Vietnamese coffees, juices, and light bites. It is a good spot to cool off and people-watch after exploring the busy streets of Cholon nearby.
