Cu Chi Tunnels

It is an educational tour. However, should you wish to enter the Cu Chi Tunnel to experience what the Vietcongs went through, please be informed that it is extremely narrow and one has to either "duck-walk" or crawl. It is in total darkness and has very little ventilation. People with heart, respiratory and back problems should avoid entering the tunnel. There are no safetly measures in place and can be very traumatic for some.
