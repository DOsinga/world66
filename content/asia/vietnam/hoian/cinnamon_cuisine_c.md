A sunset cruise on the Thu Bon River lasting nearly three hours, with cocktails and a spread of Vietnamese dishes. Highlights include pork and egg with rice and Vietnamese spring rolls with a light crispy batter and prawn filling. Recommended for those wanting a relaxed evening on the water.
