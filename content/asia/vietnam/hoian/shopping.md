One of the attractions to Hoi An now is shopping with a very cheap price to have a very nice products. While walking around the town to enjoy the ancient life there, you can drop by a shop to have some things you like with the price that any one can jealious of. Hoi An is now famous for their tailors there. They can have your suits, dresses, gowns, skirts ect made quickly in just half a day to give you the nice pieces of clothes you want. MEKONG Tailor 72(342new) Nguyen Duy Hieu st is the one I experienced thanks to advices from my friends who had been there many times. I do have some really nice suits made there with my full satisfaction. Travelling to Hoi An, do not miss this attraction!
