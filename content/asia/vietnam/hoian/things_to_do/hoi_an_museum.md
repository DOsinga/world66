Housed in a 17th-century pagoda on Nguyen Hue Street, this museum traces Hoi An's development from prehistoric Sa Huynh culture through the Cham period to its peak as a trading port. The collection includes ceramics, coins, and trade goods recovered from shipwrecks in the area.
