One of the best-preserved merchant houses in the Old Town, dating from the late 18th century. The architecture blends Vietnamese, Chinese, and Japanese elements — a carved wooden interior, Japanese roof supports, and a Chinese-style courtyard. The family that built it still lives there and offers tours.
