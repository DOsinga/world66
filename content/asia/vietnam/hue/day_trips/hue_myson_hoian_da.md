**Day 1 : Arrival in Hue. ( D )**
Arrive at PhuBai airport in Hue. Pick up then transfer to your hotel in Hue. Bus trip to visit Hue City and King’’s tombs (KhaiDinh). Dinner in Hue with Hue specialty ( Com CungDinh). Overnight in Hue

**Day 2 : Hue (B, L) **
AM: Boat trip on Perfume River to visit handicraft village: making conical hat and bronze casting. Visit Thien Mu Pagoda considered as Royal Pagoda of the Nguyen Dynasty , TuDuc and KhaiDinh Tombs. Lunch at the garden restaurant.
Visit Imperal Citadel in the afternoon then DongBa market. Diner at the restaurant. Overnight at hotel in Hue.

**Day 3 : HUE - MY SON - HOI AN (B, L)**
Am : Depart to Hoian. Relax at Lang Co Beach. stop on Hai Van Mountain Pass of 30 km length. Drive to visit My Son, a ancient religion center of the Cham people in the past, 40 km to the South West of Hoi An town, another World’’s Cultural Heritage.
P.M: Tranfer to Hoian. Dinner and overnight at hotel in Hoian.

**Day 4: Hoian (B,L)
**Am : breakfast at hotel. 8h30 Visit Hoian - an old trading town, some 30km from Danang. Visit Hoian: Japanese Covered Bridge, TanKy Old house, Assembly Hall Chinese. PhucKien Pagoda, Hoian Museum and Market. Lunch and dinner at restaurant.
Pm : free time. You can take a bike to CuaDai beach , 5 km from the Hoian Town centre. Overnight in Hoian.

**Day 5 : Hoian -Danang Departure (B )** **
**Am : Breakfast at hotel. Free time till transfer from Hoian to Danang airport for departure flight to Hanoi or Saigon.
