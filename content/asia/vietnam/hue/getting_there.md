---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

Phu Bai International Airport is about 15 kilometres south of the city centre, with domestic flights from Hanoi and Ho Chi Minh City. Hue is on the Reunification Express railway — the journey from Hanoi takes about 13 hours, from Ho Chi Minh City about 20. Open tour buses connect Hue with [Hoi An](/asia/vietnam/hoian) (about 4 hours via the Hai Van Pass), [Da Nang](/asia/vietnam/danang) (2-3 hours), and other destinations along the coast.
