**SAPA TOURS Code: SP G02 **
**2 Days/ 3 Nights** **– tour with a group
Depart from Hanoi / Daily**
**Night 1: HA NOI – LAO CAI **
20h30 ( Depart for Sapa tour ) our guide will come to pick you up at your hotel for the night train depart at 22h00 to Sapa. Overnight on train.

**Day 1 (B,L,D): LAO CAI – SAPA (B,L,D) **
Arrive Lao Cai train station **at 6:00 Am. **
**OPT 1:**
Our guide picks you up and transfer by bus to Sapa town ( 1.5 hours ). 8:00 Am check in Hotel. Breakfast and free at leisure. After lunch Our guide takes you to visit Sapa town and have a trekking 5 km to visit Catcat Village. Meet and talk with the local people to learn more about their daily life. Free in the evening. Overnight in Hotel.
**Day 2: SAPA - LAOCAI -HANOI ( B,L ) **
After Breakfast. Have a long trek 15kms through tropical forests. Follow the small paths to visit ethnic minority villages- Y ning ho, Lao chai & Ta van. Meet and talk with the Local people – H’ mong, Dzay, Dao…Take in the stunning scenery combined by rice terrace fields along Muong hoa stream where the ethnic minority is living. Have picnic lunch on the way. 2:00 Pm Our Jeep take you back Sapa town . Free at leisure to relax and discover Sapa town by your self. 4:00 Pm Bus take you back Lao cai train station for the night train address: 28 Hang Mam-Hanoi
