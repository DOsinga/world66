**Tay Ninh** is home to one of the largest and most unusual temples in Vietnam. The Cao Dai religion is a mixture of Christian, Buddhist, Islamic and European humanist elements. It has more than one million followers in Vietnam and the temple in Tay Ninh is the the centre. 
 A visit to Tay Ninh is usually twinned with the [Cu Chi](/asia/vietnam/cuchi) tunnels excursion as both sites are close together. 
 Tay Ninh is one hour to the west of [Ho Chi Minh City](/asia/vietnam/hochiminhcity).
