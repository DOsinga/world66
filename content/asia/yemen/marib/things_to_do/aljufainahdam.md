title: "Al-Jufainah Dam"
type: poi
category: Sight
latitude: 15.3880
longitude: 45.2700
---

The ancient Al-Jufainah dam was part of the Great Marib Dam's irrigation network. Located 8 km south-west of the centre of Marib, it served as a secondary structure managing water distribution across the agricultural lands of the valley.
