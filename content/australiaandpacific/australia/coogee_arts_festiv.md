The Coogee Arts Festival is the largest arts festival in the eastern suburbs of Sydney. Launched in 2004 with a production of Shakespeare's *Twelfth Night*, the festival has grown into a month-long programme spanning theatre, music, film, comedy, and community events every February. Most performances and exhibitions take place in the parks and on the beaches around Coogee, giving the whole thing a relaxed, seaside vibe that sets it apart from Sydney's bigger cultural events.
