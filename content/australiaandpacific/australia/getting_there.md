Air

The national airline is *Qantas (QF).*

For free advice on air travel from the UK call the *Air Travel Advisory Bureau* on (0171) 636 5000 (London) *or* (0161) 832 2000 (Manchester).

Qantas offers the 'South Pacific Pass' with a saving of up to 50% on economy fares between Sydney Brisbane Melbourne Fiji and Christchurch.

Approximate flight times

From *London* to Adelaide is 23 hours 55 minutes to Brisbane is 23 hours 55 minutes to Cairns is 25 hours 45 minutes to Darwin is 21 hours 50 minutes to Melbourne is 24 hours 25 minutes to Perth is 21 hours 40 minutes to Sydney is 21 hours 45 minutes and to Townsville is 26 hours 5 minutes.

From *Los Angeles* to Sydney is 14 hours 30 minutes.

From *New York* to Perth is 27 hours 35 minutes and to Sydney is 21 hours and 5 minutes.

From *Singapore* to Sydney is 7 hours 30 minutes and to Perth is 5 hours.

Approximately 30 international airlines fly to Australia.

International airports

Sydney Adelaide Melbourne Perth Darwin Brisbane Hobart Townsville and Cairns. All airports have a duty-free shop bank/bureau de change and car hire; these will almost always be available on arrival and departure of international flights. Departure tax is not levied on users of the airports listed below.

*Canberra Airport (CBR) *is 9km (5.5 miles) east of the city. Transport into the city is available by bus taxi or rental car (travel time - 15 minutes). Canberra is a domestic airport. International passengers wishing to go to Canberra should fly to Sydney and connect with a domestic flight.

*Sydney* *Airport* *(SYD) *(Kingsford Smith) is 8km (5 miles) south of the city (travel time - 15 minutes). Coaches meet all incoming international and domestic flights. The international terminal is separate to the domestic terminal. Passengers may be set down at city airline terminals and city hotels motels and guest-houses on request. There are also buses and taxis. Airport facilities include a duty-free shop banks restaurant (1200-1430 and 1730-2000) car hire car park and a tourist information desk.

*Adelaide* *Airport* *(ADL)* is 6km (4 miles) west of the city (travel time - 15 minutes). Coaches meet all international and domestic flights. Buses and taxis are available to the city and hotels. Airport facilities include a duty-free shop restaurant bar and car hire.

*Melbourne* *Airport* *(MEL) *(Tullamarine) (Web site: http://www.melair.com.au) is 21km (13 miles) northwest of the city (travel time - 30 minutes). Skybus Coach or taxis are available to the city centre. Airport facilities include restaurants bars banks post office car park and duty-free shop.

*Perth* *Airport* *(PER)* is 10km (6 miles) northeast of the city (travel time - 25 minutes). There are separate international and domestic terminals. Airport buses run 0500-2100 and meet international and domestic flights. Taxis are available. Airport facilities include banks duty-free shop 24-hour restaurant bar car park garage and gift shop (access from public area 0500-2400; access from transit/departure lounge 2400-0500).

*Brisbane* *Airport* *(BNE)* is 13km (8 miles) northeast of the city (travel time - 35 minutes). Coach services are available to the city Gold Coast Sunshine Coast and major hotels. Coaches meet all international flights. Taxis are also available. Airport facilities include a car park duty-free shop and a bar-buffet.

    *Darwin* *Airport* *(DRW) *is 13km (8 miles) from the city (travel time - 20 minutes). Coaches and taxis meet all incoming international daytime flights. Airport facilities include a duty-free shop and a bar.

*Hobart* *Airport* *(HBA)* is 16km (10 miles) northeast of the city (travel time - 25 minutes). Coaches meet all incoming flights. Buses and taxis are available to the city. Airport facilities include a restaurant and bar (1000 to last flight in domestic terminal).

*Cairns* *Airport* *(CNS)* (Queensland) is 8km (5 miles) from the city (travel time - 10 minutes). Coaches meet all incoming flights plus airport bus limousines car rental taxis to city and other areas by arrangement. Airport facilities include a duty-free shop restaurant and bar (open 30 minutes before first flight to 30 minutes after last flight).

*Townsville* *Airport* *(TSV) *(Queensland) is 5km (3 miles) from the city (travel time - 10 minutes). Coaches meet all incoming flights operated by* Qantas *and *Ansett.* The first coach leaves at 0735 and the last at 1949. Buses and taxis are available to city and hotels.

All other State capital cities are served by connections from the above international airports.

Sea

Cruise liners dock at Sydney Melbourne Hobart Perth (Port of Fremantle) Adelaide and Brisbane.
