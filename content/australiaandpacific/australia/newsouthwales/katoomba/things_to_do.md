title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Katoomba is the gateway to the Blue Mountains, and the main draw is the spectacular scenery. Echo Point, a short walk from the town centre, gives the classic view across the Jamison Valley to the Three Sisters sandstone formation. From Echo Point, walking trails descend into the valley via the Giant Stairway. The Scenic Railway and Scenic Skyway offer easier access to the valley floor and canopy level respectively.
