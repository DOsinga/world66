---
title: "Bars and Cafes"
type: section
---

Bondi's cafe culture runs strong — Campbell Parade and the surrounding streets are lined with brunch spots, specialty coffee roasters, and juice bars that cater to the post-surf and post-yoga crowds. As the sun goes down, the scene shifts to rooftop bars, wine bars, and pubs with ocean views. The vibe is relaxed and beachy rather than dressy, and you can comfortably spend an evening hopping between a few spots along the parade.
