---
title: "Bars and Cafes in Bondi Beach"
type: section
---

For an amazing night on the town BB's Bar Bondi is the place to go. Offering live music and great food, BB's is a perfect place to start your evening. Established in 1959 BB's is a renowned as a Blues n Roots, Rock n Roll venue. Originally on curlewis st Bondi, BB's recently relocated to 78 Camble parade. With a larger venue BB's opened Beach Bistro and now caters top quality food in huge portions at a very affordable price, perfect for any traveler an a budget.
