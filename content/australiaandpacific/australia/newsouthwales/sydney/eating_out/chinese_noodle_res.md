A tiny hand-pulled noodle place with fantastic food. Featured in Cheap Eats and the Good Food Guide multiple times. Northern Chinese cooking, quite different from standard Chinese restaurant menus. The vegetarian fried noodles and the eggplant, potato and capsicum stir-fry are both excellent. Two dishes will cost around $20 and you will likely leave with leftovers. Open daily 10am to 11pm.
