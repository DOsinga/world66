---
title: 'Getting There'
type: section
---

Most international visitors arrive at Sydney Kingsford Smith Airport, located about 8 kilometres south of the CBD in Mascot. The Airport Link train reaches the city centre in around 15 minutes. Taxis and rideshares to the CBD cost roughly AUD $45-55. Interstate travellers can also arrive by train from Melbourne or Brisbane, or by car via the Pacific Highway from the north or the Hume Highway from the south.
