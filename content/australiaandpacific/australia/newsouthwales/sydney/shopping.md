---
title: 'Shopping'
type: section
---

Sydney's shopping ranges from flagship department stores in the CBD to quirky independent boutiques in the inner suburbs. The Pitt Street Mall area is the commercial heart, with the QVB (Queen Victoria Building) offering a grand heritage setting. Paddington's Oxford Street and Surry Hills' Crown Street have the best independent fashion. For markets, try the Rocks Markets on weekends or Glebe Markets on Saturdays.
