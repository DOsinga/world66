Wonderland Sydney has something for everyone.
 Walk through the gates into a magical land full of thrilling rides and colourful shows all presented with a unique Australian flavour. Just a short drive from Sydney, Wonderland is Australia's number one centre for fun, thrills and entertainment. Set upon 219 hectares of beautifully landscaped parkland, Wonderland Sydney is the largest theme park in the southern hemisphere.
