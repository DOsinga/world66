---
title: 'Things to Do'
type: section
---

Sydney rewards the curious. Beyond the obvious harbour icons, the city is full of world-class museums, sprawling parks, coastal walks and wildlife encounters. The Bondi to Coogee coastal walk is free and unforgettable. Taronga Zoo offers Australian wildlife with harbour views. And the historic Rocks district, where Australia's European settlement began, is worth a slow afternoon of wandering.
