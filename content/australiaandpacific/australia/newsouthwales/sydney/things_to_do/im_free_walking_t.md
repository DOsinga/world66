Free tip-based walking tours of Sydney led by young local guides. Just turn up at the anchor beside Sydney Town Hall at 10:30am or 2:30pm any day. No booking needed -- look for the guide in a bright green t-shirt. A solid introduction to the city's history and landmarks.
