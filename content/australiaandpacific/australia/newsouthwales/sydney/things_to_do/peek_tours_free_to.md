A two-hour guided pub crawl through The Rocks, visiting three of Sydney's oldest pubs while hearing stories of convicts, larrikin gangs and the Rum Rebellion. Runs Tuesday, Thursday and Saturday at 6:30pm. Tour includes a drink at each pub. Bookings essential.
