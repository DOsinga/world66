The site of Sydney's first European settlement, The Rocks is a compact historic neighbourhood of cobbled lanes, sandstone warehouses and colonial-era buildings. Many of the original warehouses now house restaurants, galleries and shops. The weekend Rocks Markets are popular and the area is a pleasant place to wander, with harbour views at every turn.
