---
title: "Bars and Cafes"
type: section
---

The café scene is concentrated on Todd Mall and the surrounding streets. Bar Doppio is the best-known coffee specialist. Bojangles Saloon on Todd Street is the most characterful bar — a long-running institution with a bush theme and a menu of game meats and light meals alongside the drinks. The town's pub culture is mixed; some venues have a rougher character associated with alcohol problems in the local Aboriginal community.
