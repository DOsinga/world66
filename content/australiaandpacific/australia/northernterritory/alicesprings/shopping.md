---
title: "Shopping"
type: section
---

Aboriginal art is the main reason to shop in Alice Springs. Todd Mall has numerous galleries selling Western Desert dot paintings, bark paintings, carved wooden objects, and jewellery — quality and authenticity vary, so buy from galleries that can document the artist's community and provenance. The mall also has general tourist shops. Yeperenye Shopping Centre at Bath and Hartley Streets is the main indoor mall for everyday needs.
