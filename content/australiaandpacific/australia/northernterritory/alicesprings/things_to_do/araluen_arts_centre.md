Alice Springs's main arts venue, with four galleries of Aboriginal and contemporary Australian art, a theatre for performing arts, and significant collections of dot paintings from the Western Desert tradition. The Albert Namatjira Gallery holds the work of the most famous Aboriginal watercolourist. Located on Larapinta Drive in the Araluen Cultural Precinct, which also includes the Museum of Central Australia.
