---
title: "When to Go"
type: section
---

April to October is the main season — warm, dry days with clear skies, cool nights (cold in winter, with temperatures dropping near freezing in June and July). This is when the parks and gorges are at their best and the Uluru area is most accessible. Summer (November to March) is harsh: temperatures regularly exceed 40°C, and isolated thunderstorms can close tracks and make some areas inaccessible. The shoulder months of April and September offer the best balance of comfortable temperatures and relatively fewer tourists.
