A casual bar on the corner of Mitchell and Daly Streets in Darwin's CBD. Beachcombers pulls a mixed crowd of locals and backpackers with cold beers, basic pub food, and the kind of no-fuss atmosphere that suits Darwin's permanent wet-season heat. Nothing fancy, but a reliable spot for a drink in the city center.
