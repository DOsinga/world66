Furlanos is a well-established Italian restaurant in Darwin's Fannie Bay neighbourhood, not far from the waterfront. It has been serving locals and visitors for years with a straightforward menu of pasta, pizza, and grilled meats. The atmosphere is relaxed and unpretentious, fitting for Darwin's casual tropical vibe. A solid choice when you want a reliable meal without fuss.
