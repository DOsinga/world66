A casual cafe on Smith Street in the Darwin CBD, known for solid breakfasts and decent coffee. The menu runs through the usual Australian cafe staples — eggs done every way, smashed avo, wraps and burgers — with generous portions. Friendly service and a relaxed vibe that suits the Top End pace.
