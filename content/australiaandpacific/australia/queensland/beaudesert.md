Beaudesert is a country town in the **Scenic Rim**, an hour or so south of [Brisbane](/australiaandpacific/australia/queensland/brisbane) where the suburban sprawl of South-East Queensland gives way to dairy paddocks and the green ridges of the McPherson Range. The town itself is a working agricultural centre — saleyards, a Saturday country market, the regional showgrounds — but for travellers it is mostly a base or a refuelling stop on the way to the surrounding national parks. The basalt walls and rainforest of **Lamington National Park** lie to the south-east; **Mount Barney** and the spectacular ridge country of **Mount Lindesay** rise immediately to the south. There is a small museum on the main street with displays on the area's logging and dairying history if you have an hour to fill before the drive into the hills.
