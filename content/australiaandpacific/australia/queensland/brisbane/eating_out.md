Brisbane's restaurant and cafe scene has blossomed in recent years and you'll find that there is no shortage of good eateries. Many of these places have taken advantage of the balmy climate and provide outdoor eating areas. Away from the city centre, there are clusters of restaurants in Paddington, Fortitude Valley and New Farm, on the South Bank and in the West End
