title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Brisbane's main cultural institutions — the Queensland Museum, Queensland Art Gallery, Gallery of Modern Art, and State Library — are clustered together at the South Bank Cultural Precinct, south of the river. The South Bank Parklands alongside offer a riverfront beach, gardens, and a pleasant strip of cafes and restaurants. The historic buildings of the city centre, including the Old Windmill and Newstead House, give some sense of Brisbane's convict origins and colonial past.
