**Holiday Houses**

Renting a Holiday House can be the best option for Family Travel . Accommodation choice is an important factor in your decision to take a well earned break and truly enjoy your holiday with your family.
Holiday Houses provide space, comforts of home, flexibility, privacy and are usually a more cost effective option to several motel or hotel rooms. Wilks House, managed by Cairns Holiday Homes, in the inner city suburb of Bungalow is located five minutes from the Cairns CBC.
Facilities include: All linen, well equipped kitchen, Austar TV, Broadband wirless internet, free local calls, airconditioning to all bedrooms and upper sitting/dining room, off street covered parking, fully fenced front and back tropical garden.
Wilks House provides an ideal base to explore the many attractions the Cairns area has to offer such as trips to the Great Barrier Reef, day outings to Port Douglas or the Atherton Tablelands, free family fun at the wonderful Esplanade Lagoon and children's waterpark.
