Gladstone is an industrial port city about 6 hours drive north of [Brisbane](/australiaandpacific/australia/queensland/brisbane).It is a good entry point to the southern end of the great barrier reef which extends about 1000 kilometers all the way up to [Cairns](/australiaandpacific/australia/queensland/cairns).Check out the Marina and hit the pubs at night. Its worth a look if your travelling north.
