Mackay is home to a great number of the beaches of Queensland, as well as some beautiful nature walks and trails. It is a good launching pad for the rest of Queensland, and here are some **Mackay Hotels**.

**Comfort Inn The Rose**

The Comfort Inn The Rose is a typical Australian-style hotel that can be found tucked away in a corner of the city. The hotel on the outset can look unappealing, but stepping inside reveals a comfortable, homey atmosphere to the place. The hotel is located close to most amenities, and can be found only a two-minute drive away from the Mackay airport. Rooms are fully air-conditioned – and this can prove to be truly a godsend, particularly in the summertime when the Australian sun is most intense. Have your meals served in your rooms, or have them at the fully-licensed restaurant found on the ground floor of the hotel. Internet services can be provided upon request, and each of the 28 rooms in the hotel is clean and comfortable. Rooms begin at about US$100.

**Clarion Hotel**

The Clarion Hotel can be a dazzling display of lights in the quiet Mackay neighborhood. This establishment is unmistakable, and tourists can be impressed by it the moment it appears before their eyes. Located in the Mackay Harbour neighborhood where most of Mackay&rsquo;s 31 beaches are found, the hotel stands out as one of the snazzy, modern looking buildings that are beginning to become common in the area. Those tired of the beach and sand, but still looking forward to a day in the sun, can opt for the hotel&rsquo;s private pool. The hotel bar serves up a good choice of drinks, and can be welcoming on a warm summer evening. Rooms start at US$200.

**Dolphin Heads Resort**

Providing a form of accommodation for beach lovers, the Dolphin Heads Resort is located in the Dolphin Heads neighborhood. The beaches are situated close by, and unlike the Mackay Harbour area, Dolphin Heads is slightly more rugged and more popular among the locals. Surfers staying here will not be disappointed, given the staff&rsquo;s excellent knowledge about the winds and waves of the beaches around. Those looking to try out some of the water activities here will find that this is a good place to be, since equipment and tours can be arranged at the desk during check in.
