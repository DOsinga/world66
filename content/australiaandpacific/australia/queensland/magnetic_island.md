Magnetic Island, is located approximately 8 kms north east off the coast of Townsville in Tropical North, in the north of Queensland.

It's a real tourist detsination with a wide range of water sport activities, 9 hole golf course, a variety of restaurants, 23kms of National Park Walking Tracks, chemist, video shop, boutiques, gift shops,bank, post office, cafe's, taverns, medical centre and much more.
