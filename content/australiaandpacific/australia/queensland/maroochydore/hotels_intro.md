Maroochydore is found in the Sunshine Coast hinterland, in the State of Queensland in Australia, and here are some **Maroochydore hotels **to consider during your stay:

**Beachfront**** Towers**

The Beachfront Towers, as its name suggests, are a set of apartments located at the beach of Mooloolaba. Famous for its fine white sun, warm Australian sun and its myriad of water activities, the Mooloolaba beach has become more popular in the recent years with locals and tourists, rising as a strong competitor to its more famous counterpart in the form of the Noosa Heads (located further up North). The Beachfront Towers are a good place for those who want to enjoy the full glory of the beach – found just a 2 minute walk away from the beach itself, and among all the shops. Bodyboarding has always been the favorite, with no surprise, given that the waves at the beach are just perfect for that. With this hotel, bring along a sense of adventure and thrill, as the hotel staff are all too willing to suggest some of the most exciting activities to take part in. Prices start from about US$118 a night.

**Horton Apartments**

The Horton Apartments are an establishment located on the Sunshine Coast itself, off the city center of Maroochydore. The apartments are a comfortable, well-equipped facility with a focus on families and young couples who are on the coast for a honeymoon. With the view of the parks that have been well kept and structured by the architects of the place, the Horton Apartments can offer a view that is beautiful from the hotel room, without beach views. Be sure to use the facilities of the place – an outdoor pool, and a chance to arrange transportation to the beach areas with the hotel reception desk. Prices begin from US$125 a night.

**The Sebel**

Offering apartment-styled hotel rooms, The Sebel provides the perfect place for business people on a visit to Maroochydore. Providing a wonderful ambience, with bright, cheery looking rooms and contemporary furniture, The Sebel is located in a prime spot of the city, with the main business area located close by. What is great about the hotel for those busy with work in Maroochydore is the wonderful view that the hotel provides, as well as the facilities that are available. The rooms face the beautiful beaches that the town is famous for, and is well equipped with a gym, a full length swimming pool and a business center. An array of good food choices, from Italian to Oriental, can be found a mere five minute walk away, and the staff will be happy to make reservations on your behalf. Families are not left out either, with the option to choose between two to three bedroom suites. Rooms begin at US$140 a night.
