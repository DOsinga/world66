Travel Guide

Mena Creek is a quiet township one and a half hours south of Cairns. Paronella Park is situated here. This park has a fascinating history and captures the imagination with it's colourful history. The park has undergone recent renovation and is a credit to its current owners. A visit to this park is a must for travellers visiting the area.
