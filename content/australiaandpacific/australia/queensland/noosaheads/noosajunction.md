Noosa Junction is the commercial hub serving Noosa Heads, Sunshine Beach, and Sunrise Beach. Nearly everything practical is on Sunshine Beach Road: shops, a cinema, a handful of restaurants, and the obligatory fast-food outlets. The parallel street behind it has a Coles supermarket and two medical centres.
