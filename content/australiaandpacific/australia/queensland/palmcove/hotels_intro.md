Palm Cove is a perfect beach destination for the family, with lots of **Palm Cove Hotels** to choose. You will have a great time walking on the beach, exploring the shops around the beach villages and dining in one of the finest restaurants with a sumptuous seafood buffet. Palm Cove offers the best diving site, the Great Barrier Reef, or hiking on the rainforest. Book in one of these fine hotels after a tiring day.

**The Perfect Hotel for Couples**

The Sea Temple Resort & Spa is a fabulous five-star hotel in Palm Cove, perfect for a romantic getaway. The Buddha heads and water cascading decorations at the hotel entrance welcomes travelers from all over the world. Walk into the beautiful garden and relax on a comfortable sofa with your favorite drink. Enjoy swimming in a lagoon-style swimming pool or enjoy pampering in a health spa. You will love the simple shades of the room decor and the balcony and deck offers magnificent views of the gardens and pools. All hotel rooms have cable TV, minibars, jetted tubs, and hair dyers. You can spend the night here for less than $180.

**Best Value for a Personalized Service**

If you want a great value-hotel during your Palm Cove vacation, then The Melaleuca Resort will be perfect for you and your loved ones. The owner is very accommodating and hands on; you feel her sincerity in giving you the best service from the time you arrive until you depart. The apartments have big air-conditioned rooms, with a kitchen, bathroom and washer/dryer. Barbecue grills are available for use and a day spent at the pool means fun times at the resort. The staff makes it a point keep the hotel very clean. You can book your tours and excursions through the hotel. Rates start at $100 per night.

**A Hotel for Sports Enthusiasts**

The Novotel Rockford Hotel boasts of a golf course, a tennis court and gym for the family. This is a nice hotel conveniently located near the beach and has complete amenities like satellite TV, Internet access, sofa beds and desks, kitchen and dining areas. The staff is courteous and very helpful, and offers babysitting services. Your kids will love the pool and if you want to go on day trips, the activity manager will gladly help. Explore the various shops near the hotel and stop by at one of the restaurants for delicious authentic Australian cuisine. Expect to pay $150 to $200 per night.
