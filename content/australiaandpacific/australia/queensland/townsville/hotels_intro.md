**Townsville Hotels** in Townsville, Queensland provide quality lodging to tourists who want to visit the Great Barrier Reefs and the tropics for some sunshine and reef exploration. Nature tripping is one popular activity for tourists, which can serve as a refreshing respite from the metropolitan daily grind.

**Holiday Inn Townsville **

Centrally located at the center of Townsville&rsquo;s Business and shopping districts, Holiday Inn is just a few minutes&rsquo; walk to popular attractions like the Reef HQ Aquarium and the Magnetic Island ferry terminal. Newly refurbished, this hotel offers 24-hour front desk and bellman services with a 24-hour airline desk service. The hotel provides child-minding services not available in other hotels, smoke free or non-smoking floors, business facilities, parking, dry cleaning, pools and currency exchange. Price ranges from $320 to $420 per night.

**Oaks Gateway on Palmer**

This waterfront hotel offers a resort –type accommodation and caters to both leisure and business travelers. They have modern luxurious apartments. You will be impressed with the location as it is accessible to all the local attractions. They have an impressive gym complete with exercise equipment. The hotel also has an on-site Spa and Sauna, and a swimming pool. Room rates are from $315 to $482

**Oaks Hotel and Resort or Hotel M on Palmer**

Ideally located in the center of the cosmopolitan community, on Palmer Street, this brand new 4- star hotel offers all modern facilities and excellent amenities. There are 11 Floors of modern, self-contained apartments. Guests can both benefit in its location and facilities. You can use the business conference room for conferences or business meetings. Price: $297 to $387.

**Hotel Ibis Townsville**

Found in Palmer Street, Ibis is the prime dining area of Townsville and a few minutes from the Townsville airport. Ibis offers modern facilities, with each room equipped with sound systems, individual climate control and speed broadband connections. They have also conference rooms that can accommodate 40 or 80 people. The rooms and bathrooms are wheelchair accessible. If you are traveling with senior adults, you should consider this extra service at Ibis. Room rates are $120 to $130.

**Mercure Inn Townsville**

This environmentally friendly facility stands in the center of a lush tropical garden and is near an 18-acre lake. It has 2 tennis courts, 201 spacious, well-appointed rooms that overlook breathtaking views of the lake and the lush tropical surroundings. Mercure is just 1.24 miles from the airport, a few minutes ride from Castle Town Shopping Center and the fast food outlets. Rates are from $147 to $228.
