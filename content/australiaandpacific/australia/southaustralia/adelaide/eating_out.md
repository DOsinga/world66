Some Australians claim that Adelaide is a city that has the most restaurants per person.  You’ll find all sorts of restaurants, ranging from cheap snack bars (concentrated around Central Market) to exclusive Japanese restaurants. There is also a big bunch of cafes that don’t only offer a nice refreshing drink after shopping at East End, but also serve some good meals.
