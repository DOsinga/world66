---
title: "Eating Out"
type: section
---

Adelaide punches well above its weight for food. The **Central Market** on Gouger Street is the anchor of a whole restaurant district — Gouger itself is Chinatown's main strip. **Hutt Street** and the **East End** around Rundle Street are the other main eating areas, with everything from pub classics to fine dining. Further out, the seaside suburbs — **Henley Beach** and **Glenelg** — do excellent fish and chips. Don't miss the South Australian specialties: Coffin Bay oysters, Kangaroo Island marron, and a pie floater (meat pie sunk in pea soup) if you can still find one.
