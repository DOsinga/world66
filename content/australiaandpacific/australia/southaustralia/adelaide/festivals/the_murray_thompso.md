---
title: "The Murray Thompson Appreciation Festival"
type: poi
---

This festival celebrates the short bald man in all his glory. Named after its mascot - Mr Murray "Hrrrrrrrr" Thompson - the festival is held every December and comprises a week long celebration. Events include competitions such as the Shortest Man, the Man with the Least Hair, the Man Who Most Resembles a Monkey and the Loudest "Hrrrr" Call.

Hrrr.
