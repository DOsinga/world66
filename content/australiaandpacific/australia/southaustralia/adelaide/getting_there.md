---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

**By plane** — Adelaide Airport (ADL) is 7 km west of the CBD, with domestic flights from all Australian capitals and limited international routes, mostly to Asia and New Zealand. The JetBus 200-series runs into town.

**By train** — The **Overland** runs between Adelaide and Melbourne; the **Indian Pacific** crosses the continent from Perth to Sydney and stops here. Both are occasional scenic services rather than practical transport.

**By road** — Adelaide is about 8 hours from Melbourne and 7 from the Flinders Ranges.
