---
title: "Nightlife"
type: section
---

**Adelaide** is, amongst other things, known for its café-culture. Especially around East End, you’ll find streets packed with cafés and restaurants. At night, these cafés turn into perfect spots to meet either other travellers or locals. When you’ve tasted the wine from the nearby region, and are ready to face the real nightlife in Adelaide, try one of the many other bars or set off for dancing in a nightclub.
