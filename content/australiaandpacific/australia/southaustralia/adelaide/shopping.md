---
title: "Shopping"
type: section
---

**Rundle Mall** is the main pedestrianised shopping strip in the CBD — the big department stores (David Jones, Myer) and the high-street chains. For something less generic, the side lanes off Rundle Street East have boutiques and record shops. **Harbour Town Outlet Centre** near the airport is the place for discounted brands. Nothing beats the **Central Market** for food shopping.
