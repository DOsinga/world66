---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Adelaide's cultural quarter is North Terrace — a single street lined with the **Art Gallery of South Australia**, the **South Australian Museum**, the **State Library** and the **Migration Museum**, all free. The **Adelaide Botanic Garden** is at the east end. Cross the river and you're at the **Adelaide Oval**, with stadium tours and a roof climb. A short tram ride out, **Glenelg** gives you a beach; a short drive up, the **Adelaide Hills** give you wine, Hahndorf's German bakeries and Mount Lofty for the view.
