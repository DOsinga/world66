Travel Guide

If you happen to be in Adelaide in october and not in Germany for
the Octoberfest then Handorf in the Adelaide Hills is the place to be.

For 3 days the local oval is awash with great food wine and of
course, BEER. You can eat and drink until your heart is content, and
then do it some more.

Handorf is nestled on top of the Adleaide Hills, and is an easy
drive from the CBD of approx 25 to 30 minutes. On the way you will get
to see the sights of the Hills area and see how close the bush comes to
being part of Adelaide itself.

You will find heaps of local produce and some of the most beautiful
bed and breakfast homes and should spend a night or two and become
aquainted with the town.
