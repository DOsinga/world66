title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

The **Tamar Valley** is a wine-growing region producing some of Tasmania's best-known wines. Many wineries have restaurants and picturesque settings — highlights include the Bay of Fires winery, Pipers Brook Vineyard with its cloistered garden, Rosevears overlooking the Tamar Valley, and Daniel Alps at Strathlynn by the water. Most wineries are open for tastings. A local winery map is available from the tourist office.
