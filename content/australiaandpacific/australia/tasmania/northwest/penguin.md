Travel Guide

15 minutes easy drive west from the Bass Strait ferry terminus [Devonport](/australiaandpacific/newzealand/auckland_region/devonport), the town of Penguin is fast becoming an increasingly desirable retirement and tourist destination with a magnificently forested hinterland, regular Sunday market, cafe studded beachside and artistic community.
