title: 'Things to Do'
type: section
---

Ballarat's main draw is its gold rush heritage. Sovereign Hill is an outstanding living museum that recreates an 1850s mining township, and the Eureka Centre tells the story of Australia's only armed civil uprising. Beyond the history, the Ballarat Fine Art Gallery is the oldest provincial gallery in the country, and the botanical gardens beside Lake Wendouree are lovely for a stroll.
