A charming volunteer-run museum that operates vintage trams along a 1.3-kilometre section of the original Ballarat tramway, running through the Botanical Gardens on the shore of Lake Wendouree. The fleet includes Ballarat's original horse tram from 1887 and several Melbourne trams from the early 1900s. Trams run on weekends, public holidays, and school holidays from 11am to 5pm.
