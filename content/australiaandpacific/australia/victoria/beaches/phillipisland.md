title: 'Phillip Island'
type: poi

---

Phillip Island's south coast has some of the best surf beaches in Victoria. Woolamai is the pick for experienced surfers with its powerful swells, while Smiths Beach is more beginner-friendly. Cat Bay offers a wilder, less crowded alternative. Keep an eye out for penguins at dusk.
