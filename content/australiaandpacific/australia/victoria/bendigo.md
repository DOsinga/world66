Bendigo is one of Victoria's great gold rush towns, about two hours northwest of Melbourne. Like [Ballarat](/australiaandpacific/australia/victoria/ballarat), it boomed in the 1850s and the wealth still shows in the elaborate Victorian architecture along the main streets. The Central Deborah Gold Mine offers underground tours, and the Golden Dragon Museum tells the story of the Chinese miners who shaped the town's character.
