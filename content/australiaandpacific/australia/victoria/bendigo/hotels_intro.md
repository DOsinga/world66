**Bendigo hotels **in** **Victoria, Australia provide comfortable accommodations for both business and leisure travelers. One of the most important parts of planning a business trip or a vacation is finding the right hotels to stay. If you get a place that is comfortable and has all modern amenities, you can be sure you will have a good vacation or business trip. Hotels are easily accessible within the area. Here are a few recommendations.

**Best Western Crystal Inn Hotel **

Along the Mclvor Highway stands the newest 4-Star hotel in Bendigo, Best Western. This hotel has soundproof rooms. Guests can laze in heated pools or relax in the old world elegance of spacious rooms. They can also enjoy quiet moments in the covered courtyards. The hotel has executive suites, honeymoon suites, family suites and disabled suites. Prices range from $145 to $175 a night.

**Cathedral Motor Inn **

The Cathedral Motor Inn is wonderfully equipped to meet the needs of the traveler. The hotel offer spacious deluxe rooms. All rooms come with queen sized beds, room service and free TV. Guests can take advantage of the traditional breakfast delivered to the room. The room rate per night is $131.

**Quest Hotel Bendigo**

The Quest Bendigo offers rooms and apartments for either short or long stays. The hotel is open 24 hours. You can use the safe-deposit boxes at the front desk to keep your packages or documents safe. Within the structure is a shopping center and swimming pool to meet customers&rsquo; needs and wants. The average price is $153 per night.

**Tea House Motor Inn**

Considered the number one Inn in Bendigo, Tea House Motor Inn offers cozy rooms for businessmen and tourists. The Inn has comfortable rooms, a parking lot and a swimming pool. Guests can also enjoy a great barbecue at the hotel&rsquo;s Barbecue area. Room rates are $122 a night.

**Quality Resort All Seasons**

This hotel offers the comfort of home and the sophistication of high standard amenities. This multi-awarded resort can provide rooms for any type of traveler. The air-conditioned rooms have TVs, DVD players and refrigerators. Health conscious guests can take advantage of their mini-gym and spa. Guests with kids can bring their children to the playground where they can play freely. They can also do business at the convention center, equipped with fax machines and teleconferencing equipment. Rooms cost $126 per night.
