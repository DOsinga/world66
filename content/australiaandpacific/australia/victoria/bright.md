A picturesque alpine town in northeast Victoria, Bright sits in the Ovens Valley surrounded by mountains that blaze with autumn colour each April. It is a popular base for skiing at Mt Hotham and Falls Creek in winter, and for cycling, bushwalking, and paragliding the rest of the year.
