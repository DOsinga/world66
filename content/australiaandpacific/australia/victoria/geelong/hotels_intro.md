You may consider staying at one of the **Geelong Hotels** located near the Eastern Beach in Geelong, since this is the most attractive place in the city, especially popular with its shark-proof enclosure. Don&rsquo;t get confused, since many buildings labeled as hotels are actually pubs and don&rsquo;t accept guests. Here are some of the hotels around the Eastern Beach area.

**Four Points by Sheraton ****Geelong******

Located right on the waterfront, this is the leading hotel in Geelong, preferred mainly by businessmen. Four Points is extremely luxurious. It has 109 rooms and studios, with prices ranging $160 from to $330. Several rooms have a direct view of the beach. You may experience some trouble finding a parking space within the busy hotel parking area, but you can always park on the street in front.

**Vailima Waterfront Apartment**

Located in a historical Edwardian home built in 1908, the Valima Waterfront Apartment is an excellent choice to spend a romantic holiday with your partner. The interior is very modern and corresponds to the latest trends. Each apartment consists of a living room, kitchen, bathroom and a king-sized bedroom. The price is $330 for couples nightly.

**Comfort Inn**

There are two Comfort Inns located near the waterfront of Geelong – Bay City and Eastern Sands. Both of them are small, neat and comfortable, with acceptable prices for budget travelers. Eastern Sands has 25 rooms at a price of $85 per night and Bay City has 18 at $90. Each Comfort Inn has a guarded parking and a special restaurant – the Thai Elephant at Bay City and the Fish Bowl at Eastern Sands. Both offer delicious food and are worth checking out.

**Mercure Hotel**

Located in the center Geelong, yet close enough to the Eastern Beach, the Mercure Hotel offers 138 luxury rooms and suites, starting at about $110 and up to $180 per night. All rooms are modernly decorated and very comfortable. A swimming pool is available, just in case you&rsquo;re feeling that the beach is too far away.

**Rose Garden Motel
**

Finally, even deeper in the city, there is the Rose Garden Motel. It offers 15 rooms in four-star category, with prices ranging from $105 to $150. The motel has a luxurious spa and is especially suitable for families – there is a nice playground for kids and a family barbeque area. The whole building is surrounded by a beautiful rose garden.
