Tucked down a little alley in the CBD, Bennett's Lane is Melbourne's most celebrated jazz venue. It has won best jazz club in Australia twice, and the intimate room draws both local musicians and international acts. The bar is good, the vibe is relaxed, and the music is consistently excellent. Check the website for the nightly lineup.
