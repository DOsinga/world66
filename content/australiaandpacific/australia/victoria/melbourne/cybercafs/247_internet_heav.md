Internet Heaven is the world's first 24/7 unmanned internet café and provides an on-demand high-speed ADSL2+ service that allows the customer to use our self serve internet kiosks 24 hours 7 days of the week.

The internet cafe caters to a wide variety of customers such as students, tourists, and general day to day users.

Customers pay for services via an automated payment station which accepts coins or notes until the desired credit is reached & then uses the touch screen to print out a PINcode. This printed PINcode is used to log into an internet kiosk terminal and also print. Any balance credit is retained for unto 6 months, therefore customers only pay for the service they use.

Services:
1. Internet
2. Email
3. Skype
4. Instant Messaging
5. Printing
6. Wifi available within the store and within 50m of the storefront.
7. Lounge facilities within the store cater to customers who are looking to spend some quiet time or looking to use their wifi enabled devices.

Charges:
* Internet Usage - $4/hr, $2/0.5hrs
* Printing - 30c/page for both black&white + color prints
* Wifi charges are the same as using the internet kiosk. Accepted forms of payment for wifi include Credit Card and Paypal
