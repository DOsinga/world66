Get here after six and you will have to queue. The Chinese food is tasty and cheap, the atmosphere lively and unpretentious. Skip the standards and go for the seafood (the scallops are great), the Lo Hun vegetables, or the deliciously spicy deep-fried tofu and beans in chilli salt. Bring your own wine from the bottle shop across the street. Open until very late, it is a popular spot for a feed after the pub.
