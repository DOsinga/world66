Get here after six and you'll have to stand in line. There's a good reason why: the Chinese food here is tasty and cheap and the atmosphere is very lively. Skip the standards and go for seafood (scallops are great), or the Lo Hun vegetables or deliciously spicy deep fried tofu and beans in chilli salt. Bring your own from the bottle shop across the street, as the wine here is hideous. Open til very late, it's a popular spot for a quick feed after the pub and before heading home.
