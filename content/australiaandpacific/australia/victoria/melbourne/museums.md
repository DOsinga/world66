### [Immigration Museum](/australiaandpacific/australia/victoria/melbourne/museums/immigrationmuseumhellenicantiquitiesmuseum)

The Immigration Museum is a vibrant and inclusive living cultural centre that explores Australia ’s immigration history, from the 1800s to the present day. Providing a global and personal insight into the migrant experience, the Museum presents an engaging, entertaining and emotional experience that contributes to a greater understanding of Australia ’s cultural diversity.

type: Museums



address: 400 Flinders St

accessibility: full disabled access

tel: 03 9927 2700

openingHours: 10am to 5pm

url: [museumvictoria.com.au](http://museumvictoria.com.au/immigrationmuseum)
