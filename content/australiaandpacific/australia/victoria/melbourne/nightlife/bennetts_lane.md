Like most good things in Melbourne, Bennett's Lane is at the back of a
little alley hidden in the city. The name of this alley?
You guessed it, Bennett's Lane. Offering up a eclectic selection
of Jazz every night of the week, this cozy venue is an experience not
to miss. Twice winner of the best Jazz club in Australia, be sure
to get smashed at the bar and help contribute to the vibrant Melbourne
Jazz scene.
