About two hours southeast of Melbourne, Phillip Island is best known for its nightly Penguin Parade, where little penguins waddle up the beach at dusk to their burrows in the dunes. Beyond the penguins, there are koalas at the Conservation Centre, rugged coastal walks at the Nobbies and Cape Woolamai, and excellent surf beaches on the south coast. The island also hosts the Australian Motorcycle Grand Prix. A full day trip, or better yet an overnight stay.
