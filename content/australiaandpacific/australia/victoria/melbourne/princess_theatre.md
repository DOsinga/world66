One of Melbourne's grandest Victorian-era theatres, the Princess Theatre has been staging performances since 1854. The ornate Second Empire facade and lavish interior make it worth seeing even if you are not catching a show. It is reputedly haunted by the ghost of a baritone who died on stage in 1888.
