Melbourne's biggest park at 180 hectares, Royal Park is a favourite with joggers, dog walkers, and cyclists. Hot air balloons sometimes launch from here on calm mornings, creating a striking scene against the city skyline. The Melbourne Zoo sits within the park and deserves at least half a day. The Burke and Wills memorial marks the starting point of their ill-fated expedition to the centre of Australia.
