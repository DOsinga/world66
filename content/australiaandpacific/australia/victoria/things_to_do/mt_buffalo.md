Mt Buffalo is one of Victoria's most scenic alpine destinations, offering skiing and snowboarding in winter and excellent bushwalking, rock climbing, and hang gliding in the warmer months. The plateau sits within Mt Buffalo National Park, with granite tors, waterfalls, and wildflower meadows.
