A former coal-mining town in South Gippsland, Wonthaggi is the main service centre for the Bass Coast region. The State Coal Mine heritage site offers underground tours of the old workings. The town is a practical base for visiting nearby Phillip Island and the beautiful Cape Paterson coastline.
