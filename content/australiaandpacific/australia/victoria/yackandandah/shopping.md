I went to Yackandandah in North Eastern Victoria and what a great place it turned out to be. Everyone there was so friendly with a hello or g'day with every passing local. Can't get over how friendly that place was. Met some really nice people in a shop there called A bears old wares I think it mainly sold antiques but saw the best range of buddha statues that i have ever come across. I bought one and can recommend anyone if you are looking for buddhas then this is the place to go such a big range of huge ones too. I bought a medium size about 18 inches tall for only $150 can't remember the address and don't have a phone number but got their web address it's www.abearsoldwares.com Anyway get down to Yackandandah so friendly. i can't wait to get back. Jeremy
