Fremantle is famous for its old heritage buildings and impressive beaches and there are plenty of **Fremantle Hotels **to accommodate visitors. The port city of Fremantle offers various recreational activities and one of them is jet boat ride. Various other water sports activities can be enjoyed during stay. The city is closed to Perth and has no dearth of hotels and other accommodation facilities.

**Five-star Hotels**

Make your next holiday trip wonderful by choosing five-star hotels in Fremantle. Your vacation will be delightful as these hotels provide tourists with all sorts of ultra-modern amenities and recreation opportunities. These luxurious accommodations feature friendly and warm atmospheres along with professional services. Your fun is doubled with the outdoor and indoor spas, sauna, swimming pools and fitness centers. Hotel Esplanade, Hyatt Regency Perth, Sheraton Perth are few of the most popular in Fremantle. You can expect the best rooms and ultra-modern amenities to maximize your comfort. Price varies from $250 to $500.

**Three-star hotels**

The three-star hotels are situated at nice locations close to the heart of the city, many of them overlooking the view of River Swan. Most of them have modern amenities like swimming pool, spa, tennis courts, and an ample parking facility. Tourists can have a Continental breakfast and rooms include queen bedrooms, spacious bathrooms and living areas. Some well-known three-star hotels include Girton House, Fremantle Hotel, Norfolk** **Hotel** **and** **Flying Angel Hotel. Tariffs start from $100 and above.

**Resorts**

Fremantle Sunset Suites, Fremantle Beach Street Resort and Quest Harbour Village Resort offer you wonderful stays at Fremantle. They provide marvelous services for comfortable stay. All the rooms in these resorts are well-furnished and equipped with modern amenities. The rooms are designed stylishly. Those who prefer to eat outside can try restaurants close to the resorts. Rooms are spacious and pleasant.

**Home Rentals**

Home rentals fit the needs of many who are vacationing in Fremantle. These homes are equipped with beautifully decorated rooms and attached bathrooms. They are suitable for big families and visitors who are looking for less expensive alternatives. Hampton House, Fremantle Short Stay, Limestone at the South Beach and Lefroy Cottage are just some of the options.

**Bed and Breakfasts **

Budget accommodations are also available. Backpackers Inn offers single rooms at $35 per night and dormitory accommodations at $18 a night. Port City Backpackers and Old Firestation Backpackers are two of the places in this genre.
