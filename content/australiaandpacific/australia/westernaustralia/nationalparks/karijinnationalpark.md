---
title: "Karijin National Park"
type: location
---

Karijin National Park is abundant with spectacular gorges with varied colours and impressive rock formations. There are several great walks you can take on your own, or in an organised tour. The visitors centre in the south-east of the park (junction of Juna Downs, Joffre Falls and Yampire Gorge tel.: 014-511 1285) can give you all the information you will need.
