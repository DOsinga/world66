As already has been mentioned, Perth is not the place to go to when you are looking for cultural nourishment. There is only one museum, the Western Australia museum at Francis St. (+61 8 9328 4411). The London Court, the Tudor Style shopping arcade, is worth a visit as well. Kings Park offers a nice overview of the town and when you want to see an astonishing beach (the way you’ve always dreamt of it), go to Rottnest Island. It’s only one ferry ride away.
