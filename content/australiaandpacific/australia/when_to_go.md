---
title: "When to Go in Australia"
type: section
---

If you are thinking of visiting **Australia, when to go** is
probably what’s on your mind. There are several times during the year
when it would be considered perfect to visit Australia. Here are a few
recommendations.

**The Melbourne Food and Wine Festival, Victoria**

One of the best times to visit Australia is during the month of
March, because the Melbourne Food and Wine Festival is one of the major crowd
pullers. The festival takes place in different areas in the Queen
Victoria Market. You might want to stop at Chinatown for the “World’s
Longest Lunch.” The festival also includes an event known as the “Master
Class,” which showcases several world-renowned chefs and wine makers.

**Australian Grand Prix, Melbourne**

If the sound of roaring engines and the smell of burning rubber
excite you, you might want to visit Australia during the month of March
for the Australian Grand Prix. This grand event takes place in Albert
Park in Melbourne, Australia. This is the place to see popular cars like
Ferrari, Aston Martin, Lotus, Lamborghini and Porsche in action. After
the Grand Prix you can also enjoy live band performances and carnivals.

**Canberra Balloon Fiesta, Canberra**

This is a multicolored celebration that you don’t want to miss. The
Canberra Balloon Fiesta serves as an opening ceremony for the annual
Canberra Day celebration. You will be able to witness the spectacular
sight of several brightly-colored hot-air balloons in the sky. This is
an event that you will enjoy together with your family. After the
hot-air balloon show, you can kayak or canoe along Lake Burley Griffin or
explore the magnificent scenery within the city’s parkland.

**Tasting Australia, Adelaide**

This is another festivity that focuses on the gastronomical treasures
of Australia. Tasting Australia is an 8-day celebration that takes
place during the months of April and May. If you are visiting Australia
during this time, you will get a chance to participate in this great
event. Some of the events of this celebration are wine tasting, cooking
classes, food tasting, wine auction and more. However, the main idea of
this event is to showcase the gastronomical treasures of Australia,
particularly Southern Australia.

**The Cooly Classic, Gold Coast**

This celebration showcases Australia’s beach culture. The Cooly
Classic takes place during the month of April in the open water of the
Gold Coast. This beach carnival has several challenges for everyone. If
you are looking to have an extraordinary holiday, you might want to
consider visiting Australia during the month of April for the Cooly
Classic.
