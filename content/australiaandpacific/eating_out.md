If you are going to be traveling to Ile des Pins, there are a lot of **Ile des Pins restaurants** you can find. There are a variety of different cuisines to enjoy, and you can find a lot of local favorites. There are local huts which serve the cuisine where you can sit and see the views of the beaches and enjoy the fresh air.

#### Local Gite Restaurant

The Local Gite restaurant is located near the beach and is open to enjoy the weather and views. You can try some of the local dishes that include seafood and dishes that are different and you can experience the culture of the area. This is one of the hut restaurants that you can find near the beaches and swimming pool and enjoy watching the food being cooked over the rocks.

#### Kodjeue Hotel Restaurant

The Kodjeue hotel restaurant is a great place to try some of the local dishes and enjoy the fresh air and nearby beaches. The restaurant serves both the local cuisine and the traditional dishes, you can find what you want to eat. If you are going to be staying in the city, this is one of the Ile des Pins restaurants that you may want to try.
