---
title: "Bars and Cafes"
type: section
---

Nadi's nightlife is low-key and local. The bars in the town centre are basic, and most travelers end up at the spots along Queens Road north of town toward the airport. Drinks are cheap by international standards — expect to pay around FJ$5 for a spirit and mixer. There is also a cinema near the supermarket that screens a mix of Hollywood and Bollywood films.
