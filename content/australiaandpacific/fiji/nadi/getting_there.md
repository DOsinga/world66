---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

Nadi International Airport is the main entry point for Fiji, with direct flights from Australia, New Zealand, and several Pacific Rim cities. The town is about fifteen minutes south of the airport.

A taxi from the airport into Nadi costs around FJ$10. If you want to save, walk 200 metres to the main highway and flag down a public minibus heading south — the fare is under a dollar. From Denarau Island marina, a taxi into town runs about FJ$6.
