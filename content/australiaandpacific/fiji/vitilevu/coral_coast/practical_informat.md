ACCOMMODATION

For simple self-contained bungalow accommodation at very reasonable prices, check out Tubakula Beach Bungalows located in the seaside community of Korotogo.

"... Tubakula is a quiet, do-your own-thing kind of a place.... Seated on your terrace watching the sky turn orange and purple behind the black silhouettes of the palms along the beach, a bucket of cold Fiji Bitter stubbies close at hand, you’d swear this is paradise! One of the most popular backpacker resorts in Fiji and w ell worth a couple of nights" (David Stanley, *Fiji Handbook*, Moon Publications, 2004)
