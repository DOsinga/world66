**Nad's Handicraft**, located on the northern end of nadi town or the first on the right from the airport end, specializes in traditional fijian handicrafts, souveniers, clothing, fijian art gallery and fine jewellery. Stores located in *Nadi, Suva (MHCC), Crusoe's Retreat (Manfriday) *and* Capricorn International Hotel*. Nad's also has an exclusive fine jewellery store at the *Sofitel Fiji Resort (Denerau Island)* trading as **Inspired by nads**.

For a truely fijian atmosphere, the best quality products at the best prices, visit any of our stores and be greeted with a big smile. Our main store in Nadi which is a two level luxurious shopping complex, is the only store in the country that has a traditionally dressed fijian warrior waiting to welcome you. You will also enjoy complimentry tropical drinks while you take pictures with our warrior and shop in a hassle free, secured environment.

For your convinience, we also pack and post worldwide.
