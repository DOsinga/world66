When it comes to watersports, Fiji is one of the worlds true paradises. Jetski, diving, snorkeling are just a few of the available options. Not to forget the world famous cruises starting at Denarau Marina near Nadi International Airport.

### [Yasawa Island Cruises](/australiaandpacific/fiji/vitilevu/nadi/tours_and_excursio/yasawa_island_crui)

Discover the real Fiji in Captain Cook’s way. Glorious days and romantic nights in the Mamanuca and Yasawa Islands aboard the cruise ship Reef Escape. Enjoy magnificent cuisine and personalised service as you cruise to pristine beaches and remote islands with palm fringed villages. Bask in the warm lagoons, savour to the bounty of the lovo feast, dance to the drums of the meke and sleep to the sound of surf on a distant reef.
