Suva is the capital of [Fiji](/australiaandpacific/fiji) and the largest city in the South Pacific outside of Australia and New Zealand. Set on a hilly peninsula on the southeast coast of Viti Levu, it is a more cosmopolitan and urban experience than the resort islands — a working city where you can get a real feel for everyday Fijian life.

The town has old shops and bustling markets selling fresh produce, seafood, kava, and handicrafts. The Fiji Museum in the lush Thurston Gardens is worth a visit for its collection of cannibal-era artefacts and double-hulled canoes. Nearby stand the old Parliament Buildings and Government House. Suva is also a practical base for arranging onward travel to the outer islands and the Coral Coast.
