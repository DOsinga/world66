Suva is the capital of Fiji. It is a pretty relaxed town and a good place to arrange things for the rest of your stay.

The town has many old shops and markets with various foods, artefacts, handicrafts and especially seafood. Places of historic interest include the National Museum, situated in the lush surrounds of Thurston Gardens next to Government House and the old Parliament Buildings.
