The Fiji Museum is a must-see for anyone spending time in Suva. The collection covers Fiji's history from its earliest Lapita settlers through the cannibal era, the colonial period, and independence. Highlights include a sacred priest's house, war clubs, and the rudder from HMS Bounty.

The museum is located in Thurston Gardens on Victoria Parade, a short walk from the centre of Suva.
