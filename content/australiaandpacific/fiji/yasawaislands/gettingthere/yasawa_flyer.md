The Yasawa Flyer is a privately owned catamaran that runs from Port
Denarau to the Yasawa islands daily. It leaves port Denarau and travels
up north during the morning and returns to the port in the evening.

If you are staying for a longer time in the Yasawas, you might find one
of the island hopping packages more affordable. They come in "bula
passes" of 7, 14 and 21 days. One trip normally costs around 60-70
Fijian dollars. The Yasawa Flyer is operated by the Awesome Adventures
Fiji company.
