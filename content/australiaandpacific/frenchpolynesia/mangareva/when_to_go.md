---
title: "When to Go"
type: section
---

The Gambier Islands have a warm, humid climate year-round, moderated slightly by their southern latitude compared to Tahiti. The drier, cooler season runs from May to October, with temperatures around 22–28°C — this is the most comfortable time to visit. The wet season from November to April brings heavier rainfall and the occasional cyclone, though severe storms are relatively rare this far south. The lagoon is diveable throughout the year.

Flight availability is the bigger constraint. Check Air Tahiti's schedule well in advance regardless of when you plan to travel.
