The APICA, an association for the promotion of images, creation, and the
arts, based in French Polynesia, under the guidance of the Ministry of
Tourism, Ecology, Culture, Territorial Amenities and of Air transport
and of the youth and sports institut, in partnership with Air Tahiti
nui, Media Polynésie, Tahiti Nui Television, Tahiti Tourism, , Tahizea,
Le Méridien, la ville de Papeete, l’Agence française de développement
(AFD), le centre européen de formation à la production de film, le
conservatoire artistique de la Polynésie française, le centre Vaima and
Te fare tauhiti nui – Maison de la culture, organizes the International Festival of Short
Fiction Films from the Islands of the World: Courts des îles.

### [International Festival of Short Fiction Films from the Islands of the World: Courts des îles](/australiaandpacific/frenchpolynesia/tahiti/papeete/festivals/international_fest)

**The perspective of island directors at the heart of the “Courts des îles” Festival
**

The APICA wishes to highlight island sensitivities from all over the
world through their audiovisual and cinematic creations, by gathering
them around a unique event: The International Festival of Short Fiction
Films from the Islands of the World, Courts des îles.

From Java to Madagascar, from Iceland to Corsica, from the Bahamas to
Guadeloupe, from Hawaii to Tasmania… Courts des îles hopes to be an
