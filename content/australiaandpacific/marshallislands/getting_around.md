---
title: "Getting Around"
type: section
---

Domestic flights service 25 airstrips on 22 inhabited atolls. Transportation between islands is also available by sea. Taxis are available during normal work hours in [Majuro](/australiaandpacific/marshallislands/majuro) and public transportation is exceedingly limited. Rental cars are available but there are only 152 km. (95 mi.) of paved roads in the Marshalls (mostly on Majuro and Ebeye).
