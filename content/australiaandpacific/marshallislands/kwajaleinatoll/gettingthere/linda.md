Aloha Airlines and Continental Micronesia have regularly scheduled flights from Honolulu. Air Marshall Islands has flights from Majuro.

You must have written military orders from the U.S. Army to visit Kwajalein. Most visitors can be sponsored by a friend or relative residing on Kwajalein, which takes approximately one month for the paperwork. There are dive companies and hotels on Ebeye that you can stay at without these papers, but you will not be allowed to visit Kwajalein Island or many outer islands.
