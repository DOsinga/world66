The Alele Museum showcases both the traditional and colonial history of the Marshall Islands. Its features include authentic tools and artifacts, traditional canoe displays, original photographs from the Joachim de Brum collection, a geological history model of the islands, and photographs from the German, Japanese, and American colonial eras. The newest attraction is an elaborate shell collection from Mili Atoll.
