---
title: "Pic N'Ga"
type: poi
tags: [things_to_do]
snippet: Island high point with panoramic lagoon views
latitude: -22.5900
longitude: 167.4983
---

The island's high point at 262 metres. The hike to the summit takes about an hour each way on a well-marked trail. Not technically demanding, but wear proper footwear — it's steep in places. The payoff is a panoramic view over the entire island, the surrounding lagoon, and on clear days back towards Grande Terre. Go in the morning before haze builds up.
