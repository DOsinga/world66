One of the better snorkeling sites in the Pacific. The protected bay on Lifou's west coast has excellent visibility, healthy coral, and over 2,000 recorded fish species. You don't need to go far from shore to see serious reef life. Snorkeling gear can be hired locally or brought from Nouméa.
