title: Jokin Cliffs
type: poi
---

The northeast coast of Lifou drops away in sheer limestone walls up to 60 meters high — layers of fossilized coral exposed as the island gradually rose from the sea. The viewpoint at Jokin gives you the full panorama: cliffs stretching south, turquoise water below, and the occasional sea turtle visible from above. It's the most striking landscape on the island and worth the drive from Wé.
