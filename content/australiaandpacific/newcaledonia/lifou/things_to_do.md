---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Lifou's main draws are natural: cliffs, caves, coral, and beaches. The Jokin Cliffs on the northeast coast are the most dramatic sight on the island — fossilized coral formations rising up to 60 meters above the sea, with views down the coast that are hard to beat. The protected Jinek Bay Marine Reserve, on the west coast, is widely regarded as one of the best snorkeling sites in the Pacific.
