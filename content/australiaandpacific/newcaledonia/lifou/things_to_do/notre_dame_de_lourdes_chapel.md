title: "Notre Dame de Lourdes Chapel"
type: poi
category: Sight
latitude: -20.9139
longitude: 167.0541
---

A small Catholic chapel perched on a headland above Santal Bay, reached by a short walk from Easo beach. The chapel itself is modest, but the location is exceptional — a 180-degree view over the bay and out toward the open Pacific. It's a five-minute walk from the beach and worth combining with a swim.
