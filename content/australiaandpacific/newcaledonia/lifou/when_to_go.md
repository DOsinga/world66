---
title: "When to Go"
type: section
---

The best time to visit is from April to November, during the dry season. Temperatures stay comfortable — typically 22–28°C — and the trade winds keep conditions pleasant. The sea is calm and clear, which matters a lot if you're coming for snorkeling or diving.

December through March is cyclone season. Rain is heavier, humidity climbs, and while direct cyclone hits are relatively rare, conditions can be rough and some services operate on reduced schedules. It's not impossible to visit in this period, but the dry season is notably better.

July and August are the coolest months and the most popular with French visitors on school holidays — Lifou gets busier than usual, particularly in the first two weeks of August.
