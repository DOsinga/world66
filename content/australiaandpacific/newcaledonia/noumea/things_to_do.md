### Yachting

Noumea Yacht Charter offers a variety of deals. These deals include charter (with or without skipper), group, theme cruises, or cruises by cabin. Noumea Yachts have a world class quality fleet to ensure you have the best possible time you can.

Noumea Yacht Charter is located in the heart of Noumea in Port Moselle.
