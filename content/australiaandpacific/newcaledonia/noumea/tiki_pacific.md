A small boutique on rue de Verdun in central Noumea specialising in locally designed beachwear and resort clothing with Pacific island motifs. The prints draw on Melanesian and Polynesian patterns, and the shop carries swimwear, sarongs, and casual wear for men and women. A good spot to pick up something distinctly New Caledonian rather than generic resort wear.
