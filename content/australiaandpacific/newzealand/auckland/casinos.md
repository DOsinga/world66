New Zealand has two casinos of international standard - one in Auckland and one in Christchurch. The largest of the two is Auckland's Sky City, easily located by its Sky Tower - one of the tallest in the world, at 328m. The Casino offers all the usual tables and games, and includes 85 gaming tables, over 1000 gaming machines and an 80-seat keno lounge.
