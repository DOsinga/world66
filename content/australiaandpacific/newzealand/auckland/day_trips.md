---
title: "Day Trips"
type: section
---

The Hauraki Gulf is the obvious day trip from Auckland. Waiheke Island is a thirty-minute ferry ride from downtown and has beaches, vineyards, and olive groves that feel a world away from the city. Rangitoto Island, even closer, is a volcanic cone rising straight out of the water with a good summit track through lava fields and pohutukawa forest. Further afield, the Waitakere Ranges to the west have bush walks and wild surf beaches, and the Coromandel Peninsula is reachable in a long day if you leave early.
