---
title: "Eating Out"
type: section
---

Auckland's food scene draws on the city's Pacific and Asian communities as much as its European roots. The Viaduct Basin and Wynyard Quarter have the waterfront dining, Ponsonby Road has the brunch spots and neighbourhood restaurants, and Dominion Road is a long strip of Chinese, Indian, and Korean places that locals swear by. Seafood is excellent here, especially green-lipped mussels and fresh fish from the Hauraki Gulf.
