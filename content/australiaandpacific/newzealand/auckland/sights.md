A diversity of landscape provides an inviting playground for the adventure seeker. A multi-cultured city, Auckland offers all the amenities and attractions associated with a sophisticated cosmopolitan city, including galleries, museums, theatre and concerts. All of these have the distinctive blend of Polynesian and European themes. 

Many historic places, important scenic spots and some of New Zealand's modern buildings have gained a special status as cultural icons. Among them are the Auckland Harbour Bridge and theSky City Tower. Two distinctive buildings that have become enshrined in "Kiwi culture" are the Parliament Buildings that New Zealanders affectionately refer to as The Beehive, and Te Papa, New Zealand's leading edge, interactive national museum.
