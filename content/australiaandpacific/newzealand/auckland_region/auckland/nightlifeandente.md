The nightlife in Auckland is mainly in three different neighbourhoods: Parnell, Ponsonby and Viaduct Harbour.

Parnell is the most poshest area of those three. If you want to go out
on Wednesday go to Parnell. This is one of the 'rules' I learned during
my stay in Auckland. The rest of the week Parnell is empty.

An other place I can recommend is High Street. High St is near
Britomart (Queen St). I found there one of the best Jazz Bars
(Deschlers Jazz Bar). There are several bars and clubs as well. If you
like house music go to The Met in High St.

### [Parnell](/australiaandpacific/newzealand/auckland_region/auckland/nightlifeandente/miss_carla_p)

Parnell is a very happening place to be late on a wednesday evening. Best club to date is the Vanilla Bar. Drink specials, superb RnB music to move to. Open 9pm till 3am. Not too shabby! Bullfish was once the place to be, George Bar was good but now also closed.
