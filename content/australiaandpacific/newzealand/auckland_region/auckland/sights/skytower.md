The Sky Tower is the tallest tower in the Southern Hemisphere. You get an overview over whole Auckland.

Admission is $18.00 for adullts (Senior Citizens $14.00; Students (with ID) $13.00) and $8.00 for childrens (5-14). Children under 5 are free. Family passes (2 adults and 2 children) are $44.00, and $6.80 for each additional child. There is also a Sun & Stars pass that allows 1 day visit and 1 night visit for $24.00. The Sky Deck is an additional $3.00.
