Coast to Coast Tours is an eco-tour of the west Auckland region. It is operated by New Zealander's where they show you this area; (-*it's where their family has lived since 1922, and you also visit their own sheep farm and have afternoon tea inside their historic Homestead!*)

This is a one day tour, (with transport and lunch is included in the price), as well as going to Auckland's rainforest area (the Waitakere Ranges), craft-shopping, wine-tasting, honey-sampling, and seeing the gannet sea-bird colony at Muriwai Beach.

You'll also get the chance to buy and plant your own Carbon Credit native tree to offset the carbon emissions from the tour.

There's a lot to do on this tour, and it gets you out of the City.
