---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

If you find yourself in Rotorua, check out the Whakarewarewa Thermal
Village. A unique experience of living Maori culture and volcanic
activity. Caution, though, the high sulphur content of the volcanic mud
pits makes for a strong odor. Other sights include:

**Mud Pools** - Watch mud bubble from the ground and feel its intense heat.

**Geysers** - There is a geyser in Roturia that erupts periodically every few hours!

**Polynesian Spa** - Here
you can choose from a range of temperatures and mineral compositions in a
beautifully landscaped natural environment

**History** - Learn about the Polynesian culture that still
thrives in New Zealand to this day. Observe craftsmen carving in
the same manner that their ancestors did, see what an actual tribal
town would have looked like.

**Kiwi Birds** - There is a place where one can observe kiwi birds hatch and mature.

**Farm Shows** - There are a few shows where one can observe
the ways of a New Zealand farmer. They are very family
friendly. Activities range from observing a sheep actually being
sheared to demonstrating the abilities of a sheep dog.
