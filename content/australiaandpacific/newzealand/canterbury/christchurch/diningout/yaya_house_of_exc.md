The teahouse offers a range of mainly organic and/or fair-trade loose leaf tea. Teas are served traditionally in the tea house and all teas are sold for home consumption.
Light meals (crepes, oatcakes, etc.) are served to accompany the teas and home-made European-style hot chocolates are offered as an alternative to tea.
**Ya-Ya Teahouse** offers regular events like tea-tastings or yoga.
