### [Air Force Museum

Let the **Air Force Museum** take you on a unique journey of discovery and adventure, with 28 amazing aircraft, captivating displays and fun, hands-on entertainment. One of Christchurch’s “must see” attractions, the Air Force Museum is a sensational family day out with a difference! 

Close and convenient, the **Air Force Museum** is located just 15 minutes drive from Christchurch city center and on 3 major bus routes. There’s free parking for 200 cars and lots of space for motor homes and buses. 

Open 7 days (except more..](http://www2.world66.com/australiaandpacific/newzealand/canterbury/christchurch/museums/air_force_museum)
