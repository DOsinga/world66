title: "Cathedral Cove"
type: poi
category: Sight
latitude: -36.8294
longitude: 175.7842
---

A sea arch on the eastern coast near Hahei that connects two small beaches. The natural arch in the white-rock cliff is tall enough to walk through at low tide. The 45-minute walk down from the Hahei car park is steep in places but worth it. Water taxis run from Hahei beach in summer if the walk is not appealing. Cathedral Cove Marine Reserve surrounds the area — snorkelling in the clear water is excellent when conditions allow.
