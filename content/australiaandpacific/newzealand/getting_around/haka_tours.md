Haka Tours is a relatively young company specialising in adventure tours around New Zealand. Kiwi owned and operated, they offer a number of options from 7 to 26 days of North, South and both islands.

Tours include accommodation, transport, breakfast and a host of activities in each location they visit. In addition to this, Haka offers a selection of extreme activities, including bungy jumping, sky diving, surfing, skiing and snowboarding. In January 2008 they launched three NZ Snow Tours.

Tour members are generally aged between 18 and 40 and tours depart every month.
