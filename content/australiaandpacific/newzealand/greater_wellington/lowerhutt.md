Lower Hutt is just 15 minutes drive northeast from
[Wellington](/australiaandpacific/newzealand/greater_wellington/wellington). It lies on the banks of the Hutt river and the hills and plain of the lower Hutt valley. There are swimming beaches in the seaside suburbs of Petone and Eastbourne.

The suburb of Petone is where the first settlers from Britain landed in the 1840s and there is a museum dedicated to their arrival on the foreshore.
