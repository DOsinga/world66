Upper Hutt is part of
[Greater Wellington](/australiaandpacific/newzealand/greater_wellington). It lies on the banks of the Hutt river in the hills northeast of
[Wellington](/australiaandpacific/newzealand/greater_wellington/wellington) and
[Lower Hutt](/australiaandpacific/newzealand/greater_wellington/lowerhutt). Just half an hour's drive from downtown Wellington, the city has one of the last remaining stands of Podocarp Forest in the Wellington area at Bartons Bush.
