### [Bars/Clubs in Wellington](/australiaandpacific/newzealand/greater_wellington/wellington/bars_and_cafes/barsclubs_in_well)

There are other bars than Blend Bar (own by Nomads) in Wellington worth visiting, trust me!

*Sandwiches*-the one and only club for international performances/djs within house/ electronica/drum'n base etc. The cost is about $20, but worth it big time. Check out their homepage for updated events and guests. End of Courtneyplace next to the Embassy Theatre. Open until 5 am.
