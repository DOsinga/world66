Courtenay Place is Wellington's main nightlife strip. Havana on upper Cuba Street is good for salsa nights and live jazz — they also roast their own coffee, and you can smell it during the day. Matterhorn on Cuba Street is a relaxed bar during the week that gets busier on weekends, with an outdoor terrace and a solid cocktail list. Good Luck Bar is the one with no sign on the door — a speakeasy-style basement bar that stays mellow and friendly.
