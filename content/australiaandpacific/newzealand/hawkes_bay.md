The **Hawkes Bay** lies on the east coast of the
[North Island](/australiaandpacific/newzealand/north_island) of
[New Zealand](/australiaandpacific/newzealand). It is supposedly named for the large bay it lies in, *Hawke Bay*, but an *s* was added. A colonial spelling mistake?

Places in the Hawkes Bay include [Napier](/australiaandpacific/newzealand/hawkes_bay/napier) and [Wairoa](/australiaandpacific/newzealand/hawkes_bay/wairoa). [
