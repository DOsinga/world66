type: section
---

The Auckland Region offers a mix of outdoor landscapes, harbour activities, and suburban day trips. The 21 Regional Parks scattered around the region each have their own character — from the wild surf beaches of the Waitakere Ranges to the calm swimming spots of Long Bay. The Hauraki Gulf Maritime Park, accessible by ferry from downtown Auckland, provides easy access to volcanic islands and open water.
