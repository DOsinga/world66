title: Rainbow Springs Kiwi Wildlife Park
type: poi

One of the more reliable places in New Zealand to actually see a kiwi. Rainbow Springs operates a kiwi hatching and rearing programme, and the nocturnal house lets you observe the birds in low red light — they're surprisingly active once you adjust to the dim setting. The park also has trout-filled natural springs, native birds, and tuatara. It's particularly good for families, and the kiwi hatchery gives the visit some genuine conservation weight beyond the usual zoo experience.
