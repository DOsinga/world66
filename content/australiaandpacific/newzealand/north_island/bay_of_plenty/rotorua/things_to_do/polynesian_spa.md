On the edge of Lake Rotorua, the Polynesian Spa draws from two separate mineral springs — one alkaline (good for skin), one acidic (said to help with arthritis) — and offers a range of outdoor pools at different temperatures. The lake views are pleasant, and on a cool evening with steam rising off the water it's a genuinely relaxing place. There's a range of pool areas from public to more private; the public pools are fine and considerably cheaper. Open daily from 8am.
