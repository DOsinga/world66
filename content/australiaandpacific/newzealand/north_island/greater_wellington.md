The Greater Wellington region covers the southern tip of the [North Island](/australiaandpacific/newzealand/north_island), from the [Kapiti Coast](/australiaandpacific/newzealand/north_island/greater_wellington/kapiti_coast) in the north to the harbour and hills of [Wellington](/australiaandpacific/newzealand/north_island/greater_wellington/wellington) city at the bottom. The region includes the Hutt Valley cities of [Lower Hutt](/australiaandpacific/newzealand/north_island/greater_wellington/lowerhutt) and [Upper Hutt](/australiaandpacific/newzealand/north_island/greater_wellington/upperhutt), [Porirua](/australiaandpacific/newzealand/north_island/greater_wellington/porirua) to the north, and the [Wairarapa](/australiaandpacific/newzealand/north_island/greater_wellington/wairarapa) on the eastern side of the Remutaka Range — a wine-growing region with a very different character from the urban western side.
