**Taranaki** lies on the western coast of the [North Island](/australiaandpacific/newzealand/north_island) of [New Zealand](/australiaandpacific/newzealand). It is named for its mountain, the volcano *Taranaki,* which used to be called *Mount Egmont*. (See [Egmont national park](/australiaandpacific/newzealand/egmontnationalpark)) 

The largest city is [New Plymouth](/australiaandpacific/newzealand/newplymouth)
