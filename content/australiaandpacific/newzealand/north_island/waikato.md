The **Waikato** is named for its mighty river. It runs 425 km from Lake Taupo to the Tasman Sea, making it New Zealand's longest river. The region occupies the broad lowland plains south of Auckland, and includes the Waitomo Caves, Lake Taupo, and the farming heartland of the Waikato basin.

The largest city is [Hamilton](/australiaandpacific/newzealand/hamilton).
