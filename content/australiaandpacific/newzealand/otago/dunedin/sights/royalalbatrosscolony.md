The world's only mainland albatross colony. Originally this was a Maori pa, but after fortification in the 1880s, Taiaroa Head is occupied by the Northern Royal Albatross, a colossal bird that can reach a wingspan of up to three meters! . Experienced guides from the Royal Albatross Centre can tell you all about this majestic bird.
 Also dont miss the historic Fort Taiaroa.
