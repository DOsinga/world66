title: Things to Do
type: section
---

Dunedin punches well above its weight for a city of its size. The Otago Peninsula just outside the city is home to remarkable wildlife — albatross, yellow-eyed penguins, fur seals, and sea lions — all within a short drive of the centre. The city itself rewards exploration on foot, with a concentration of Victorian and Edwardian architecture that rivals anything in the Southern Hemisphere.
