THE backpacker bar in Queenstown. It´s part of the Discovery Lodge hostel so you´ll find a lot of backpackers here and a good atmosphere. It can get tacky sometimes, but hey, what do you expect in a backpackers bar?
Every night they have nice things to win in backpacker games. (You know, those games where you have to make a complete fool out of yourself for a bit of booze)
So if you want a big party, nice atmosphere and easy pickings, then come here.
