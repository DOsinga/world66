The **road to Milford Sound**. Worth an afternoon on it's own merits. This 100km stretch from Te Anua has some sights and sounds, smells available nowhere else on the planet.

**Milford Sound Underwater** centre. Not like Loch-ness, Milford sound is brimming with wildlife below the surface. Pay your $10 fee and watch the multiples of fish and wildlife perform for the public. Feeding time is at 10am and 3pm. Don't miss it.

**Mitre Peak**. Pure granite slab marking the end of the Routeburn trail, New Zealands most popular walk.
