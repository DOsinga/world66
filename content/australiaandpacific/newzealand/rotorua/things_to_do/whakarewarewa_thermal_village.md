---
title: "Whakarewarewa Thermal Village"
type: poi
category: "Sight"
latitude: -38.1620
longitude: 176.2535
---

A living Maori village built among active geothermal vents. Residents of the Te Arawa iwi still use the hot pools for cooking and bathing. Guided tours explain the culture and history while you walk past steaming vents and bubbling mud. The sulphur smell is strong — you get used to it.
