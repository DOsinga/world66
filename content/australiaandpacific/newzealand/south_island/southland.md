What can you call the southern part of the [South Island](/australiaandpacific/newzealand/south_island) that has fiords, mountains, lakes, plains, rolling hills, rare birds that were believed extinct, a high rainfall on one side of the mountain range and near drought on the other and rugged valleys that people have never set foot in? How about **Southland**?

The largest city in Southland is [Invercargill](/australiaandpacific/newzealand/invercargill).
