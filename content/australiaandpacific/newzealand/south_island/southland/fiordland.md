**Fiordland** lies in the southwest corner of the [South Island](/australiaandpacific/newzealand/south_island). Most of it is part of the *Fiordland National Park*, one of the most wild and untouched regions of [New Zealand](/australiaandpacific/newzealand). There are places here where man has never set foot, only flown over. 

Places in Fiordland include:

[Milford sound](/australiaandpacific/newzealand/milfordsound)
