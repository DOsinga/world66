The **West Coast** is *only* the *West Coast* of the [South Island](/australiaandpacific/newzealand/south_island), not [New Zealand](/australiaandpacific/newzealand). The Maori first came here for Greenstone, then the Pakeha for gold, then coal, then they cut down trees to farm the land. But there was still plenty left over to see.... Glaciers, Native Forests, High Mountains, Lakes and Rivers and, of course, ***Rain.***
