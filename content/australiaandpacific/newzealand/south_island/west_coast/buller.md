The **Buller** region of [New Zealand](/australiaandpacific/newzealand) is named for its river. Together with [Westland](/australiaandpacific/newzealand/south_island/west_coast/westland) it makes up the [West Coast](/australiaandpacific/newzealand/south_island/west_coast) of the [South Island](/australiaandpacific/newzealand/south_island).
