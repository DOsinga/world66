Wellington's beaches are not tropical but are popular with locals for jogging, picnicking, and summer swimming. Oriental Bay, a short walk from the central city, is the most used. The [Kapiti Coast](/australiaandpacific/newzealand/kapiti_coast) to the north has better open-water swimming beaches.
=======
Wellington's harbour beaches are small but well-loved. [Oriental Bay](/australiaandpacific/newzealand/wellington/beaches/oriental_bay), just fifteen minutes' walk from the city centre, is the most popular — locals jog along its promenade and swim on warm summer days. For wilder surf beaches, head south to Lyall Bay or over the Rimutaka Range to the Wairarapa coast.
