Just fifteen minutes' walk from the central city, Oriental Bay is the closest thing Wellington has to a classic beach. Wellingtonians jog the promenade, eat lunch on the grass, and — on warmer summer days — actually swim. The views back across the harbour to the hills make it worth the walk even when the weather is less cooperative.
