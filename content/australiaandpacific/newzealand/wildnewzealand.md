A richly illustrated coffee-table book pairing Gerald Cubitt's photographs of New Zealand's landscapes, forests, coastlines and wildlife with Les Molloy's knowledgeable text on the country's natural history and conservation efforts. Covers everything from the volcanic plateau to the fiords of the south.
