Saipan International Airport (Francisco C. Ada/Saipan International Airport) receives direct flights from Tokyo, Osaka, Seoul, Taipei, Manila, and Guam, as well as connections via Guam from the US mainland. The island is a US territory and passport rules follow US requirements — US citizens do not need a passport, but most other nationals do. A taxi or rental car is the best way around the island, as public transport is limited.
