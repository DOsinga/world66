---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment"
type: section
---

Many restaurants have small bars and several are located along the waterfront are popular watering holes. Visit the Pacific Royale Hotel bar or upstairs at the Nuku'alofa Hotel. The International Dateline Hotel has a delightful pool side bar and open air restaurant.. The main bar is located in the reception area. The International Dateline Hotel has a good floor-show and special buffet nights. Details are available at the front desk. There are several good disco clubs around Nuku'alofa which are popular with younger persons. Some nightclubs such as the Ambassador are located a short taxi ride from the town center.
