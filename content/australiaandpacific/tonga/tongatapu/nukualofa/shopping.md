---
title: "Shopping"
type: section
---

The Langa Fonua Handicraft Centre and the FIMCO shop sell locally made baskets, woven mats, and tapa cloth. At the vegetable market, jewellers display carvings in cow bone and black coral — tiny dolphins and Polynesian motifs that make good souvenirs. Along the main street you will find silk-screen prints, hand-painted lavalava wraps, and casual South Pacific fashion.
