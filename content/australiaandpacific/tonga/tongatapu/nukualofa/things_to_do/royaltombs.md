title: "Royal Tombs"
type: poi
category: "Sight"
latitude: -21.1359
longitude: -175.2041
---

The burial site of Tongan royalty since 1893, set in a fenced enclosure in the centre of Nuku'alofa near the Basilica of St. Anthony of Padua. The tombs are not open to the public, but you can view them from outside the fence.
