Home to many rare and interesting birds found in Tonga. The center has been so successful with its breeding program that surplus birds are released. The birds congregate in the surrounding trees filling the air with their songs. The Wildlife Center is open to the public daily from 9 am to 4 pm and Sundays until 6 pm. The rare Malau bird of Niuafo'ou is being bred here
