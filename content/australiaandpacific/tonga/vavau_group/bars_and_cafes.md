---
title: "Bars and Cafes"
type: section
---

Neiafu has a small but sociable bar scene, mostly catering to the yachting crowd and visiting divers. The Mermaid has long had a reputation as the best bar in the South Pacific — or at least it will tell you so. Down the hill from the main town, there is a local bar that operates more like a honky-tonk disco in an old warehouse. It is where the Tongans go, and it has more character than anything on the ridge.

Be warned: the local beer has a mixed reputation at best.
