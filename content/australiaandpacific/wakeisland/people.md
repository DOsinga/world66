---
title: "People"
type: section
---

There is no civilian population on Wake Island and never has been. The atoll has no indigenous inhabitants.

Today a small rotating contingent of U.S. Air Force personnel and civilian contractors operates the base — typically a handful of active-duty airmen and around 100 contractors at any given time. They maintain the airfield, support missile defense operations, and keep the facilities running. It is not a posting anyone volunteers for enthusiastically.

Access is restricted to those with official military or government business. There is no tourism, no transit, no exceptions.
