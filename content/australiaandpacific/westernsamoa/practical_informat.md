The new 18 hole par 72 championship Penina golf course (http://www.peninaresortandgolfclub.com) is now open at Faleolo. The course is built on a former US military base used in World War II and is adjacent to the airport and Aggie Grey’s resort.

The course can play from 6300 to 7500 yards and will provide a challenge for all. The course is a genuine par 72 course with real sand bunkers. Players will be treated to beautiful views of the coast line, mountain, and Savaii island.

The course features all modern conveniences: driving range, new Club Car golf carts, new Cobra rental clubs.

**Currency**

The local currency is the Somoa Tala (WST)
