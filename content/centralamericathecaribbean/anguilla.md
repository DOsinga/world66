Still a bit off the beaten track, Anguilla is one of the most paradise like islands in the Caribbean. The northernmost of the Leeward Islands, 145 miles east of [Puerto Rico](/centralamericathecaribbean/puertorico) , the island is 16 miles long and 3 miles wide. It has a population of only 10,000 souls. 



Anguilla is mostly flat, so there are no mountains to climbs or hikes to be made during your stay here. This can be a very relaxing thought. There is no reason not to enjoy the flawless beaches with their pure sand and neon sea. 

The Valley is the capital of the island, and is located right in the center. Sandy Grove is one of the major resorts and is located on the Wets Coast. Most ferries arrive at Blowing Point which is some 8 miles south of the capital. Since the island is very small, the beach is never far away.
