**Authentic Greek Dining at Zorbas in Freeport, Bahamas**

**Freeport restaurants** offer a wide variety of local and international menus. Freeport is located on the island of Grand Bahama and is a free trade zone. It is just 100 miles from Fort Lauderdale, Florida.

#### **Zorbas**

Zorba&rsquo;s restaurant boasts of authentic Greek cuisine and some Bahamian dishes. It is located at Port Lucaya, Freeport. The place is open daily from 7 a.m. until 10:30 p.m. It is always filled with diners, no matter the time of day. The restaurant has a casual atmosphere and has a large outdoor patio where you can dine al fresco.

#### **Menu**

Zorbas is a must when in the Freeport City. You know that it&rsquo;s worth eating there because early in the morning you will find a line of locals waiting for their breakfast order of any of the following: chicken souse, corned beef and grits, pancakes and waffles plus omelets.

For lunch or dinner, you can enjoy the restaurant&rsquo;s freshly made Greek salad, gyro, burger, souvlakia, moussaka, grilled chicken or any pasta dish. For dessert, never miss their baklava, a sweet, nut-filled pastry. Food here is simply delicious and satisfying. In summer 2010, Zorba's is also priced reasonably at a rate of $4 to $15 per order or $13 to $29 for entrees which include rice and a side dish.
