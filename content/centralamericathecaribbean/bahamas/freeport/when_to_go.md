**Planning your trip to Freeport**: **The Perfect Time to Go**

This **Freeport when to go** guide offers various attractions that can make your vacation exciting. Originally a fishing and farming village, Freeport today is a bustling tourist city. The tropical summers allow you to enjoy swimming and sunbathing at its many beaches, or fishing and other water sports at Deep Water Cay and golfing at the Fortune Hills Golf and Country Club. Nightlife in Freeport is also as exciting as the daytime activities. You can listen to Bahamian music and dance the calypso all night long.

**Freeport&rsquo;s Peak Season**

The pleasant, sunny weather attracts tourist from all over the world to enjoy the city&rsquo;s many attractions. The average temperature during these months is 75 degrees Fahrenheit or 24 degrees Celsius. When you visit Freeport in the months of December and January bring warm clothes, as nights are cold, especially if you are planning to enjoy nightlife entertainment. Dry season is from September to May. Many tourists prefer Freeport for romantic getaways and nature tripping. Shopping during this season is one activity worth trying. You can purchase native garments and souvenirs from the many local shops around the city.

**The Off Season**

During the rainy season, Freeport hotels are almost empty so they offer prices at half its peak season price. There are fewer crowds during these months as the island city is visited by tropical cyclones, and storms. The unpredictable weather condition during rainy seasons make these months not tourist friendly. You may prefer to visit Freeport during the dry season that starts from June through early September.
