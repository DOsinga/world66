All Beaches are public, but not all ways to get to the beaches are public. Please, ladies, wear a top (and bottom) on the beach. There are no nude or semi-nude beaches in Barbados.

Use your head when on the island. While want you to have a great time, don't get caught up in the "romance" of being on a caribbean island.
