---
title: "Bars and Cafes in Belize"
type: section
---

**Belize City bars and cafes** are lots of fun and always provide an enjoyable experience. The nightlife in Belize City is something that should not be missed while on your vacation.

**Waluco's**

Waluco's is a great place to enjoy some authentic Belize nightlife. Most nights you will find local drummers and locals dancing to a style called "punta." Waluco's is a must if you are seeking excellent nightlife with a twist of Belize culture.

**Big Daddy's**

Big Daddy's is a bar and disco in Belize City. The music consists of local music to mainstream top 40. The dance floor is always packed with people dancing the night away. The drinks are good but there is normally a wait to be served. There is also an outside bar which is a great place to enjoy the cool evenings.

**Hideaway Hotel & Bar**

Hideaway Hotel & Bar is a relaxed bar where you can enjoy some tasty local drinks. The bartenders are friendly and actually take the time to get to know you. If are seeking some nightlife without the crowds then this is the place to go!

**The Pier Lounge**

The Pier Lounge is a fun place to go and enjoy some good entertainment. There are always drink specials going on and The Pier Lounge has theme nights throughout the week.

**Wet Willies Cantina Bar and Grill**

Wet Willies is a great place to enjoy delicious food and drinks. Some of the favorites on the menu are Belizean BBQ items and local cocktails. While the bar is only up till 11pm it is still a top place to start your evening off. Wet Willies has a vibrant atmosphere that most will enjoy.

**Cheers Sports Bar and Grill**

Cheers is the place to be if you love sports. You can watch your favorite team while enjoying a tasty dinner and cocktails. Cheers has several televisions throughout the restaurant that feature sporting games. There are also plenty of different beers on tap, making this bar a hit! Cheers is open for breakfast, lunch, dinner and late evening drinks.

**Tipsy Tuna Sports Bar**

Tipsy Tuna Sports Bar has it all. You will find pool tables, dancing, high-definition televisions and karaoke. Sunday through Wednesday the bar is open till 11pm and Thursday through Saturday until 2am. While the food isn't the best, the nightlife is very entertaining.
