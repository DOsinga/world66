Regular, frequent and reliable air service is available from Belize
City (Philip W. Goldson International Airport - BZE) to Dangriga, on
either of the Belize-flagged carriers, Maya Island Air, and Tropic
Air. Both carriers rely almost exclusively on the well-regarded
Cessna 208 Caravan, a rugged and comfortable single-engined turboprop.

Pilots are skilled, and you'll appreciate that when approaching the
airfield at Dangriga- the runway is but 2,000 feet long, and not too
smooth, but the Caravan is well-crafted for this mission.

Be sure that you have arranged for pickup by the operator of your lodging , as there is no taxi service at Dangriga.

See: http://www.tropicair.com and http://mayaairways.com
