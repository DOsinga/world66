When you go, though, especially if you go in the summer, be sure to bring lots of very strong insect repellent, the higher the DEET percentage the better. We went through a can a day. When the mosquitos are bad, you cannot be outside for even a minute with any uncovered skin, and they do bite through clothes. If you're prepared for that, though, it's a beautiful place.
