San Ignacio is about 100 miles West of Belize City, and it
can be reached through the Western
Highway , one of the few fully paved roadways in
the country, in about 1 hour and a half. The bus used to be the favorite mean
of transportation: from Belize City , the fare
used to be about US$7 to catch an express bus that would take you to San
Ignacio (called Cayo by the locals) via Belmopan
in about two and a half hours. However, recently the bus service has become
more sporadic and less reliable, with many runs canceled and very few ways of
getting information on schedule and expected trip duration More and more people
have hence decided to rely on car rental, which is fairly pricey in Belize , or
taxi. a taxi will take you from Belize
City to Cayo or vice-versa for around US$60 per car:
if you can find travel companion it will be more convenient to use this mean of
transportation.
