---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment in Hamilton (Capital City)"
type: section
---

**Hamilton nightlife** is lively and fun in this tropical
destination. After your dinner plates are cleared, waste no time in
stepping out for some evening entertainment to ensure you get the
complete Caribbean experience on your trip to Hamilton.

**Nightlife in Hamilton
******

The Deep Lounge and Nightclub, located just outside the city is a
great option to start your night. This high energy lounge has a
state-of-the-art sound system, a new dance floor and HDTV for watching
all the premiere sporting events. Stop here for drinks, snacks and some
dancing to cap off a great evening.

Fresco's Restaurant and Wine Bar offers the largest selection of wines
in Bermuda in a laid back atmosphere, while Opus is also a fine
selection for a sophisticated night out enjoying a glass of wine or
espresso.

Barracuda Grill is a nice chic location to enjoy martinis or wine. If
you love martinis though, you should also check out The Pickled Onion, a
martini bar and popular local hangout with live entertainment 7
nights a week during the beach season.

Cafe Cairo offers some great food selections from Egypt, Morocco and
Lebanon, along with dancing until 3 a.m. to music from the regions. Belly
dancers, sheesha pipes and Turkish coffee complete the atmosphere here.

**Entertainment in Hamilton**
****

If you love to catch live music, Bermuda Bistro is a nice place to
stop during the high season to catch a party and dance. Open until 3 a.m.
every day, this venue also doubles as a great place to catch American
and European sports action.

During beach season, be sure to stop by the Reggae Beach Party at
Snorkel Park from 10 p.m. to 2 a.m. Take a ferry from Hamilton and enjoy
dancing the night away. Bone Fish also offers some great salsa dancing
during the beach season from 7 p.m. to 12 a.m.

The Champion Sports Club is home to Chewstick's Neo-Griot Lounge Night,
an open-mic-like event featuring artists, spoken word, singers and
musicians. This is one of the premier live entertainment spots in
Bermuda and draws some of the biggest names in the business. The event
is weekly from May to September and bi-weekly from October through
April.

Be sure to spend some time exploring the nightlife and entertainment
when visiting Bermuda to ensure that you get the complete experience of
this lively and unique area.
