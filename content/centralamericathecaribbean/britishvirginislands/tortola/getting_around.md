---
title: "Getting Around"
type: section
---

If you like to explore, you should rent a car. The cars are small, but if you plan on walking around, the hills are steep, and you won't want to walk it. A couple people have biked over the mountians, but there are blind turns, and that's not very safe. If you don't want to rent a car, you can call for a taxi(or bus). Also, sometimes locals hitch-hike around the island.
