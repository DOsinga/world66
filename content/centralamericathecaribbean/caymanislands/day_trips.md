If you should get tired of lazing around the Cayman Islands the
following are some suggestions of where you can visit while you are
there.

**Short Flight to Cuba**

The Caymans aren't too far from Cuba and if you are up to a trip you
can find a short flight that will accommodate you. Island Air will
transport you on a day trip to Havana and it will only take about 1.5 hours. Once you get there you board an air conditioned tour bus
for a trip around the city. You will eat in a restaurant partaking of
native Cuban food. The restaurant/bar is the same that Ernest Hemingway
used to go to. After lunch you will walk around Old Havana and explore
the Cathedral, castle and museums. There are also street vendors that
