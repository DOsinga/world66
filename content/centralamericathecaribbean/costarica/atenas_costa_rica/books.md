rolling countryside, with its bountiful fruit orchards and sweeping
valley vistas, magically transforms as you ascend the winding mountain
road that brings you to the vibrant celestial mosaic that surrounds
[Vista Atenas B&B](/centralamericathecaribbean/costarica/atenas_costa_rica).

Here you will find an oasis of tranquility and beauty, where the cool evening breeze invigorates your senses.

Where nature´s harmonic rhythms are reflected in every glistening plant and every shimmering star.

personalized services - a guided tour to the nearby volcano, a walk
through the coffee plantations…
