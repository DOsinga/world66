---
title: "Practical Information"
type: section
---

Manuel Antonio is limited as to the amount of visitors that are allowed in per day, so it can be nicely secluded. That said, the beaches are popular and can get busy. there aren't many picnic tables, so bring a beach blanket! You cannot buy ANYTHING in the park, so remember to bring plenty of water and whatever else you think you will need. You can buy most "beach" stuff along the "strip" that runs through the town just before you get to the park.

Don't plan to buy any fresh produce along the "strip" however - it isn't available (although you can get as many exotic-wood hash pipes as you think you will need...) Decent groceries must be purchased at the superercado back towards Quepos.
