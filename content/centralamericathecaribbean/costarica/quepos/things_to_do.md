**World Class Sportfishing**

Hundreds of anglers flock to Quepos each year to be part of world class fishing. Sailfish season is hottest from December through April, the Marlin bite is strong November and April. Snapper, tuna, dorado, and roosterfish can be caught every month of the year.

Whether you are an accomplished angler, or just someone looking for a new adventure, you should not miss the chance to fish in Costa Rica. Quepos offers some of the best fishing in the world.

Large boats like Caribsea, Estrella de Mar, and Alhombram charter for between $950 and $1500 per day, while a smaller boat can be chartered for as little as $600 per day.

You should book your charter in advance of your trip. Trips fill up fast in the high season. For your best price, book direct with boat owners as they pay commissions to hotels and booking agents making your price higher.
