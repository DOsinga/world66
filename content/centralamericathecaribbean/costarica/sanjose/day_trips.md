**Points of Interest**

**Monumento Nacional Guayabo**

The major archaeological site in Costa Rica is the Monumento Nacional Guayabo, which is about 85 kms east of the capital of San José. Guayabo is currently under investigation and is thought to have been inhabited from about 500 B.C. to 1400 A.D.
