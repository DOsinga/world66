**Points of Interest**

**Costa Rican Art Museum**

At the entry of downtown San Jose you can visit this paint museum. Rotating selections from the permanent collection together with temporary exhibitions showcase the artwork of Costa Rican painters and sculptors in a building that was once an airport terminal. The Sabana Metropolitan Park which s [more..](/centralamericathecaribbean/costarica/sanjose/museums/costaricanartmuseum)

address: Central ave, 42 street Parque Metropolitano, La Sabana

**Museo de Arte Y Diseno Contemporaneo **

The emergence of this new cultural institution has provided a space devoted to develop and expand the main tendencies of contemporary art and design in its diverse manifestations, both nationally and internationally.   

address: Street 15-17, Ave 3

**Children's Museum**

This museum is located in a former prison. It brings science, culture and history to life (or at least nearly so through the use of robotized Costa Rican personalities). Phone: 223-7003.  

address: Calle 4

openinghours: 9:00 a.m. to noon and from 2:00 p.m. to 5:00 p.m., Tuesday through Friday, and from 10:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. on Saturday and Sunday

**Jade Museum**

This museum exhibits a good Jade Collection. Also ceramics, stone and some gold miniatures are on display.  

address: National Insurance Institute

openinghours: Mon - Fri, 9:00 to 3:00 pm

**National Museum**

The National Museum that was founded in 1897 and got itself a permanent home in which was the "Cuartel Bellavista". Phone: +50-6-2214429  

address: Between first and second ave in 17 street

openinghours: Mon a Sat, 8.30 a.m. - 16.30 p.m.. - Sundays 9 a.m. - 16.30 p.m

**Gold Museum**

This museum houses an extensive collection of pre-Columbian gold in which the level of artistry achieved by native craftsman working with this precious metal is easily appreciated. Phone: 233-4233.  

address: Calle 5

openinghours: 10:00 a.m. to 4:30 p.m., Tuesday through Sunday

**Entomological Museum**

This museum belongs to the University of Costa Rica. It exhibits the insect life of Costa Rica. It is located in the basement of the School of Music.

address: University Campus in San Pedro de Montes de Oca.

openinghours: Mon - Fri, 1:00 pm to 5:00 pm

**Museum of Natural Sciences**

The collection shown on this museum is know to be among the most comprehensive in the world and it has been divided in four areas: Paleontology, Archaeology, Zoology and Malancology (mollusks). The museum is located southwest of La Sabana Metropolitan Park.

openinghours: Mon-Fri, 8:00 to 3:00 pm

**Legal Science Museum**

This museum exhibits portraits and items related to the Legal Sciences and the Supreme Court. 

openinghours: Mon-Fri, 7:00 and to 11:00 am & from 1:00 pm to 5:30 pm
