The Children’s Museum, formerly a prison, is a huge facility with exhibits for all ages and interests. In the courtyard of the museum you can walkthrough an actual train, an airplane and a helicopter. Inside there’s everything from a giant mouth with huge teeth to can play on to a music studio where you can sing and experience what’s it’s like to record a CD. You won’t find “Do Not Touch” signs here, as everything is made to be touched, grabbed, pulled, tried on or thrown. And, teenage and college age employees are available throughout the museum to teach you more about the exhibits and help out as you experience the fun.
