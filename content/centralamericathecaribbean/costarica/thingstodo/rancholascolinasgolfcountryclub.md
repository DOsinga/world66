Situated in Playa Grande on the Northwest Pacific coast, this Ron Garl (Las Vegas Country Club, Palm Beach Polo and Country Club) designed course is a challenging but fair golf test. A round golf here is a lot of fun, especially the early part of the year when the winds of the Pacific make each shot more exciting. When a slope is determined look for about a 130. 

 Par: 72, Yardage: 6,721, Slope: n/a,  Architect: Ron Garl  Yardage, par, and slope from Championship tees.
