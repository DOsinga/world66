Remedios is a small town. You do not need much time to explore it's wonders.

### [Iglesia de San Juan Bautista](/centralamericathecaribbean/cuba/remedios/sights/iglesia_de_san_jua)

This small church is in fact a real cathedral! It is one of the most important churches in all of Cuba. It features a great altar and a beautiful wooden roof.
