---
title: "Shopping in Varadero"
type: section
---

A holiday in Varadero is not complete without visiting the several** Varadero
Shopping** hot spots. These places have great bargains and great
items that you can buy and take back home.

**Plaza Americas**

This is one of Cuba’s largest shopping malls. Plaza Americas is
located right in the center of Peninsula de Hicacos. If you are looking
for brands like Mango, United Colors of Benetton and others, this is the
place to do your shopping. There are shops that deal exclusively in
clothes, bags, shoes and belts, so this is one place you can shop till
you drop. Plaza Americas also houses boutiques, cigar stores, music
shops, bar, restaurants, a post office, banks, car rental agencies and a
supermarket.

**Taller de Cerámica Artística**

This ceramic store is located right next to the Galleria de Arte
Varadero. Taller de Cerámica Artística showcases and sells great
artworks from local artists and Cuban potters. All items in this shop
are of high quality and perfect as souvenirs for your family or for
yourself.

**Casa Del Habano**

If you are looking for exotic Cuban cigars, then Casa Del Habano
is a nice place to go. This store is located right in the heart of
Varadero and offers the best quality cigars in the city. Casa Del Habano
is the most popular cigar bar in Cuba. Besides the high quality and
authentic merchandise, this store also boasts of its friendly
atmosphere, great costumer services and electrifying crowd. If you want
to take some for souvenirs you can buy the 7-cigar variety pack which
cost 40pesos.

**Bazar Cuba**

Bazaars are the best places to shop in Varadero; this is because of
the variety of merchandise and the affordable price tags that come with
it. One of the most popular bazaars within the city is the Bazar Cuba.
Here, you can find great souvenirs and gift items like: beach clothes,
books, crafts, jewelry and more. Bazar Cuba is situated in Central
Varadero and it showcases locally produced items that you cannot find
elsewhere.

**Bazar Varadero Publicigraf**

Another popular bazaar in Varadero is the Bazar Varadero Publicigraf,
which is located in Parque Central. You will find an amazing display of
ceramic items here on display. All these are made by the locals. Bazar
Varadero Publicigraf also features different souvenir shops that offer
unique items like: reproduction of popular paintings, dolls, postcards,
books, clothes and wall decorations. In every piece you can see Cuban
culture, traditions and flair.
