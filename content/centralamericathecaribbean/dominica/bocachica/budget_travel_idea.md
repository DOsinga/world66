There are a lot of **Boca Chica budget travel **ideas to consider if you want to travel to this little city located in the western portion of Panama. Boca Chica is known for its white, sandy beaches and great diving and snorkeling sites. The city is very small and you can travel the entire area within a day. You do not need to break your bank if you want to travel to Boca Chica. In fact, a budget traveler can still enjoy his stay in this city without spending a lot of money.

**Walk Around **

Boca Chica&rsquo;s area is very small, thus the best way to get around the town is by walking. The roads in the city are paved, but there are some parts particularly in the outskirts that are still dirt roads, so when traveling around Boca Chica, you need to make sure that you wear a reliable pair of sneakers or sandals in order to protect your feet.

**The White Sandy Beaches **

Boca Chica is blessed with white sandy beaches. The best way to enjoy yourself in the area is to lounge in the many beaches that span the area. What is great about this activity is that the beaches in Boca Chica are free. Moreover, you can also snorkel or take a dip in the azure waters of the beach and see for yourself the rich marine sanctuary that Boca Chica has.

**Dominican Bay Hotel**

Accommodations in Boca Chica are very few, but if you want to get the cheapest yet good service from any inns or hotels in the city, then you ought to check in at the Dominican Bay Hotel. This hotel lies near the beach and has a price range for its accommodations of $75 to $150. If you really want to save a lot, another option for your accommodation is to go camping along the public beach in Boca Chica, but this is not advisable if you are traveling alone. But, camping with a group along the beaches in Boca Chica is pretty safe.

**Eating in Boca Chica**

You can eat at restaurants in the hotels in Boca Chica. However, if you want to save, you can sample local foods that are sold in the food stalls in the market of Boca Chica.
