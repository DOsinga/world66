title: "Things to Do in Sosua"
type: section
---

You might want to add some activities to your **Sosua things to do** list if you&rsquo;re planning a trip to this lovely little beach resort located on the North Coast of the Dominican Republic. It surely has plenty to offer.

**Surfing**

The fine white sands and crystal clear waters make Sosua Beach an ideal place for surfing. The surf spot is a bit small, but you can definitely catch some good waves. For your comfort, a large number of local shops and bars are set up right on the beach line. However, be careful with the locals, since they are a bit short-tempered, and remember to be polite with them to avoid trouble.

**Diving and Snorkeling**

Sosua is a real paradise for diving and snorkeling. Snorkelers can easily reach the coral reef from the beach. The shallow depth allows beginners to get comfortable with diving. Rich amounts of tropical fish make the reef a good place to take some underwater pictures, and if you&rsquo;re lucky enough, you might even see some whale sharks, dolphins or manta rays. You can ask your diving guide to take you to some shipwreck sites or underwater caves. Advanced divers have the opportunity to take their diving certification exams in Sosua.

**Golfing**

Golfing is also very popular in the area with a number of golf courses around. Playa Grande is said to be the best one and is praised by the professionals – 10 of the 18 holes of this golf course interact directly with the Atlantic Ocean. Worth mentioning are also Playa Dorada (another well-kept 18-hole golf course) and Costa Azul (a nice 9-hole golf course).

Having fun in one of the two nearby aqua parks is a very good idea, especially if you&rsquo;re on a vacation with your family. One of them is the Columbus Water Park - the biggest aqua park in the Caribbean. It has swimming pools, bars, restaurants, many different shops and a wide variety of water slides. The other one is the Ocean World Adventure Park, which is unique with its dolphin lagoon, shark interaction pool and has an incredible sea lion attraction.

**Botanical Gardens**

The Botanical Gardens are located atop Isabela de Torres Mountain, very close to Sosua. You can either travel by car or go to Puerto Plata and take the cable car. Except the big number of interesting plant species, the Gardens offer a spectacular view to the whole surrounding area.
