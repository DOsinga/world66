Antigua Guatemala, commonly known as just "La Antigua," or "Antigua," is a famous city situated in the center highlands of Guatemala. It is famous for its **Antigua Guatemala Bars and Cafes**. Apart from this, Antigua is also famous for its rich Spanish heritage with its spectacular ruins of the colonial churches and the Baroque architecture that is visible all over the city. The city is also a world heritage site. It has a lot to offer terms of sight seeing, experiences, cuisine, and shopping. The city has several restaurants, cafes and bars, serving some of the finest local and international delicacies. Below are just some of the Antigua Guatemala bars and cafes.

**Cafe Condesa**

Cafe Condesa, which means "The Countess's Cafe," is situated on Antigua&rsquo;s Central park, huddled in the Count's house known as La Casa Del Conde. On entering the Casa, the strong aroma of freshly brewed coffee and freshly baked bread will lead you right to the Cafe Condesa. Cafe Condesa offers its guest a great choice of breakfast, lunch and dinner. Their never-ending supply of coffee is an Antiguan treat to all the guests. This is a great place to unwind and relax after a long day out exploring the city. Their desserts are a must-have. Do not miss it on your visit to Cafe Condesa.

**Rainbow Cafe**

Rainbow cafe is one of the oldest cafes in the city of Antigua Guatemala, offering its guests great food and drinks at really affordable prices. Every evening you can relax in their welcoming atmosphere and enjoy live music. Be it coffee during the day or dancing and drinks during the night, this is the perfect place to be to experience real Antigua hospitality. The bar at the cafe serves a great variety of exotic cocktails, freshly prepared right in front of you.

**Fernando's Kaffee**

Situated close to the Posas La Merced hotel, Fernando's Kaffee offers some of the best coffee and breakfast choices to its guests. The owner of the establishment, Fernanado, is a jolly fellow who is friendly and eager to help. You can enjoy your breakfast and coffee on their courtyard. This is a perfect spot for you to relax and refuel before you start off with your tours of the city.

**El Muro Pub**

The El Muro Pub is a friendly neighborhood pub in the city of Antigua Guatemala that serves great drinks and beer and wonderful local delicacies. There are several live performances that are organized in El Muro for their guests, making it one of the best places to hang out in Antigua.
