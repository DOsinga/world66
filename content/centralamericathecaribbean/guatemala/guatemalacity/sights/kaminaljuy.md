Located in the western section of Guatemala City (zone 7), Kaminal Juyú is one of the most important sites of the pre-Hispanic Period. Although the city's growth has destroyed a great deal of what once was a large archaeological site, it still has outstanding features, such as ceramics, sculptures and architecture.
