Travel Guide

Santa Cruz Spanish School is located on Lake Atitlán, one of the most beautiful lakes in the world, you can study in a relaxed, peaceful atmosphere surrounded by nature (in the paradise). Take a bout or (lancha), 15 minutes from panajachel.

Pedro Juan Solis, Director, ESL and Spanish teacher he will endeavor to provide exactly what you need, in this case the ability to speak Spanish well.
