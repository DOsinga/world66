**Sights**

**National History Museum**

The museum Is located on the south side of the park, in another neoclassical building called the Casa de Cultura de Occidente. Items on display include pottery from the Pacific lowland area, Precolumbian ceramic and jade, native costumes and stuffed animals and birds.  

openinghours: Tuesday through Saturday, from 9:00 a.m. to noon and from 2:00 p.m. to 5:00 p.m.
