Jamaica is one of the bigger islands in the Caribbean. When you say Jamaica you immediately think of Bob Marley, Rum and Bobsleighing.

 [Kingston](/centralamericathecaribbean/jamaica/kingston) the capital city is the first place many travelers arrive. It is located on the South coast of the island. The city offers world class business hotels, upscale shopping and top entertainment and dining.

 The main tourist areas are on the northwest of the island. [Montego bay](/centralamericathecaribbean/jamaica/montegobay) is probably the best known beach resort, further to the west [Negril](/centralamericathecaribbean/jamaica/negril) is also a good option with low rise buildings and high rise palm trees.

 [Ocho rios](/centralamericathecaribbean/jamaica/ochorios) and [Port antonio](/centralamericathecaribbean/jamaica/portantonio) offer great opportunities to go hiking in the hills. If you want to go even higher you should head for [Mandeville](/centralamericathecaribbean/jamaica/mandeville), located at 2000 feat above sea level.
