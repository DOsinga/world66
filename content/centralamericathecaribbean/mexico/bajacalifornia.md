Baja Califonia is that 1300 kilometer long arm that dangles down from the US border between the Pacific and the Gulf of California. The whole of the peninsula consists of rugged and almost inhabited mountains.

 The desertlike landscape are fascinating and the scenery is extremely beautiful. Most travelers find Baja a magical place of blue skies, fresh air and solitude. A great get away form the crazy land of California.

Tijuana, a city with a population of over 1 million is located on the Pacific and is the most important border crossing. The town boomed in the 1920s when hollywood stars came here for a drink and it is still a great place for nighlife.

 Ensenada is the third city of Baja and is a leading seaport. It is a great city, beautiful and fully equiped to serve tourists and travelers.

 BC is the capital of Baja California Sur. It is a fast growing city, but still a quite relaxed place to stay. It is an excellent base for exploring the sourth of the peninsula. [Cabo san lucas](/centralamericathecaribbean/mexico/cabosanlucas) is a big resort with many tourist facilities in the south.
