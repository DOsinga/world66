---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment"
type: section
---

Cabo San Lucas is a playground for adults when the sun goes down.
The lights go up and all kinds of clubs and bars and you can have a
wonderful time. This is a party town so make sure you party hardy. It
is considered Party Central for the whole world. Here you can find any
kind of music you want, any type of beverage you desire, and the food
is out of this world.

**Caborey Luxury Dinner Cruiser**

Try the Caborey Luxury Dinner Cruiser leaving from Cabo in the upper
deck of a catamaran. The food is gourmet including Chateaubriand,
Shrimp with White Wine Cream Sauce, Sea Bass filet.

**Caliente Puerto Paraiso**

You can do a little horse racing at Caliente Puerto Paraiso. Horse
race or wager on sporting events and keep track of them on the multiple
TV’s.

**The Giggling Marlin Bar and Grill**

You will have a fabulous time at The Giggling Marlin Bar and Grill. Outside you will see a man hanging upside down next to a giggling
marlin. Start at the espresso bar then make your way to the floor show
where you could become a performer. The food is great and includes
Mexican dishes and fresh seafood.

**Harley – Davidson Bar and Grill**

If you like motorcycling you will love the Harley – Davidson Bar and
Grill. They boast having the best hamburgers in town and you can rent
a motorcycle to go about town.

**Las Varitas Bar and Nightclub**

Las Varitas Bar and Nightclub features local acts including rock
bands and Latin rock. This is a place to see the real Cabo San Lucas
and it is advised that you take someone from the area with you.

**Mambo Cafe**

MamboCafe is a Caribbean disco that specializes in Latin Music. The tropical atmosphere is luscious and exciting.

**Passion**

Want to have a luxurious experience. Go to Passion where you find a
refined atmosphere with interesting cocktails. Dance to International
music mixed by DJ’s.

Don’t leave Cabo until you visit Poncho’s and Blondies for a good
time. They have an open air dance floor with dance poles, if you wish
to try them. You can play pool on the professional pool table or try
Karaoke in Spanish.

**The Zoo Bar and Dance**

The Zoo Bar and Dance has an African Safari-like atmosphere and you
will always have fun there. Dance to an incredible collection of music
and go wild.

Party from dusk til dawn in Cabo San Lucas. You won’t believe what you can do in just one night.
