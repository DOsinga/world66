**Cuidad Juarez Day Trips** include the trip of the guests to the neighboring places. Ciudad Juarez is known as a business hub in Mexico. There are only little of beautiful sites that you can visit here. If you are done touring the whole Ciudad Juarez, then you can have an escape to its adjacent areas.

**An escape to the border**

El Paso is the border of Texas from Mexico. It is connected by a bridge to Ciudad Juarez. Due to its proximity, many of the tourists escape to El Paso to have a day trip from Ciudad Juarez. There are many tall buildings, theaters, museums, cathedrals, and many other beautiful sites that you can explore in El Paso. Wells Fargo Plaza is the tallest building in the place that you should not dare to miss. This building is well known for its 13 horizontal lights that gives the place a sparkling light at night.

**Run off to the east**

Piedras Negras is the city of Coahuila that is located at the east side of Ciudad Juarez. A lot of historical places are found in this city. One of these is the famous El Santuario de Nuestra Se&ntilde;ora de Guadalupe, which was built in 1859. It was renovated in the year 1934 because of the battle and explosion that ruined the church. You can also find other tourist attractions here such as The Macro Plaza, The Ruins of San Bernardo Mission, The Handicrafts Museum, The Culture House, The Hunting and Fishing Club, The De La Ventana Caverns, and many other magnificent places that will captivate you.
