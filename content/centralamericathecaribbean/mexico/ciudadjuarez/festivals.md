Ciudad Juarez, formerly known as El Paso del Notre, is famous for **Ciudad Juarez**** festivals**. The city is in the state of Chuihuahua in Mexico and is one of the fastest growing cities in Mexico. It is well connected to the world by all means of transport. Given its history and culture, there are several festivals that are celebrated in Ciudad Juarez. Given below are some of the Ciudad Juarez festivals.

**The Tequila Festival**

Organized by the National Chamber of commerce and the tourism department of Ciudad Juarez, this festival which has slowly grown to become a family event where people gather not only to celebrate and enjoy the tequilas, but also to socialize and enjoy the ambiance and have a good time. There are several tequila tasting competitions and song and dance performances. This is a perfect place for fun, and is one festival that you cannot miss in Mexico. Only adults are given an opportunity to taste the tequila, while the entire family can take part in the festivities and enjoy themselves. This is a perfect place where you can witness the true vibrant colors and life in Mexico.

**Festival Internacional Chihuahua**

The festival Internacional, which is conducted every year in Ciudad Juarez at the Centro Civico Paso del Notre, is a cultural and artistic festival of music. There are several activities held indoors and outdoors at Ciudad Juarez and several artists from all over the world participate in this festival and regale the audiences in Ciudad Juarez with their performances. This is a great festival, especially for music lovers.
