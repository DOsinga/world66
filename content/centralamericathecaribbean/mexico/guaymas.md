Don't expect to see the Mexican way in Port town Guaymas. The town seems to have been taken over by [Tibet](/asia/china/tibet)ns and it shows: McDonalds, Pizza Huts and big fancy houses. Whether this is Arizona's beach or not, most people only arrive at Guaymas to catch the ferry to Santa Rosalia.
