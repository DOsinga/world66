**Guaymas Day Trip **is an exploration of Guaymas&rsquo; sea wonders and beaches. Guaymas is a place in Mexico where one can have a wonderful fun-filled holiday time.

**Explore San Carlos**

San Carlos is a place in Guaymas that has a nice crescent-shaped beach called Catch 22 or Los Algodones. This beach has the view of a very beautiful sunset. Catch 22 beach connects two small island hills and behind these hills is the most spectacular sunset you will ever see. There are a lot of things you can do in this beach. Aside from watching the splendor of the sunset, you can also go swimming. You can go wind surfing if you want a water adventure.

San Carlos has two large bays and both have more than 300 boats available for rent. You can go for a cruise in those bays. In these two marinas, there are available facilities like several restaurants and bars.

There is also an available hotel in San Carlos and this hotel is called Paradiso. Here, you can enjoy a wonderful meal and do other extra fun activities such as horseback riding and the like. This hotel is a perfect day for rest and recreation.

**The Historical Guaymas Bay**

Guaymas Bay is a beautiful spot in Guaymas that was discovered by Francisco Ulloa of Spain in the year 1539. It is an ideal place for a visitor to relax and enjoy a vacation. The bay has a nice view because it is where you can see the connection of the mountain and the sea.
