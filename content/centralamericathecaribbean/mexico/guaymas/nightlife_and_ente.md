---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment in Guaymas"
type: section
---

**Guaymas Nightlife** is something which you should not try to
compare with the Mardi Gras atmosphere. If you expect that atmosphere to
be present in the Guaymas nightlife, you could be slightly
disappointed. But having said that, you should know that you can have a
lot of fun with nightlife if you know where to look in the city.

So what is there in Guaymas night life which you can look forward
too? Well you certainly have the usual music, pool and dancing. There
are lounges and night clubs. Night clubs are usually open only on the
weekends. There are special drinks which you can try, like Bacanora. It
is from Sonora and somewhat similar to Mescal. In Guaymas, you are not
very far from San Carlos, so a visit there could add a few more
options. You could try Ranas Ranas, whose claim to fame is that John
Travolta slept there.
**Charly’s Rock

**

If you are in the center of Guaymas at night, this is the place to
go. They have a great collection of drinks. A very good selection of
cocktails is also present over there. But the main reason why Charly’s
Rock is so famous is its cuisine. Now, you might have something against
going to a bar to eat, but if you keep an open mind you could be
pleasantly surprised. Charly’s Rock has managed to combine a good bar
and a restaurant that is really worth trying.
**Charly’s Baby

**

Well, you say, I like Charly’s Rock , the food is really good there,
but I need to get my foot loose. I need to dance and I need to sing and,
of course, I need to drink. Ok, we can take care of that. Stop by
Charly’s Baby. They have DJs who play great dance music non stop. And,
if you need to dance, you can dance all night and they will let you. And
they, too, have a great collection of drinks like the other Charly’s.
**Restaurante Bar Mexico Gourmet

**

This is a dignified bar that has a great selection of drinks with
some really good local cuisine, and the music here is much softer. So if
your head banging days are over and you are happily married and are
here with your partner, this is the place to go. Again, the focus here
is some great food.
