**Tips to Help You Plan Your Visit to Guaymas**

**Guaymas practical information** is something that will come handy when planning a trip to this municipality of the Sonoran State. Sonora is in Mexico, and Guaymas is a city inside Sonora. Guaymas is basically an industrial port and it's well known for its century-old annual carnival attraction.

**Visa**

In order to enter Guaymas, Mexico you need to apply for a visa. However, there are certain countries that are exempted from getting a Mexican visa. Examples are the USA, Canada, UK, Spain and several more. You can check on the Internet to find out which countries are exempted from applying for a tourist Visa for a trip to Guaymas.

The cost of applying for a visa varies on the nationality of the applicant, and ranges from $17 to $30 US. An average of two days up to three weeks is the needed time to process a visa application, and the visa is valid for up to six months. If you need to extend your stay in Guaymas, the processing only takes a few minutes provided you meet all the requirements. You will be given an extended stay of up to 180 days.

**Currency**

The currency used in Guaymas is the Mexican Peso (MXN). One Mexican Peso is equivalent to US$0.08; this rate is as of June 23, 2010. The Mexican peso is generally not legal tender in places outside of Mexico.
