---
title: "Shopping in Guaymas"
type: section
---

One thing to keep in mind about the **Guaymas Shopping** availability is that the city has not always been a popular tourist attraction. There is not a huge shopping district, but that is starting to change. As more and more people are making their way to Guaymas, the shopping choices are starting to improve.

**Perlas Del Mar De Cortez**

This is one of the best stores that Guaymas has to offer its tourists, especially people who love pearls. At Perlas Del Mar De Cortez, you will be able to find a variety of pearls to purchase, but the best part is you can get them at a great price. The reason for the great price is you are able to purchase them from a direct source rather than having to go through a middleman. They also carry gold and silver as well as other types of gifts.

**The Mercado**

This is also known as the common market in Guaymas. If you are looking for an authentic pi&ntilde;ata, this is probably the best place to come. You can also find a large variety of sugar candies that you can use to fill the pi&ntilde;ata or just munch on as a snack. There are local shops inside the common market that sell handmade wooden toys, which are a great treasure to take home to a child or grandchild.

**Jugetes y Mas**

If you happen to be in Guaymas during the Christmas holidays you must stop in this store, but it is even worth a brief visit during the rest of the year. During the Christmas season you're going to find a large variety of Christmas decorations and little trinkets for the holiday. Other items you can find inside the store include music boxes and talking dolls.

**Plaza Ley El Vigia**

This is similar to what Americans would call a strip mall, but it is located in what is considered downtown. In this little shopping center you will be able to find a large variety of stores, including jewelry and clothing stores.

**Kiamy's**

Although this shop is located in San Carlos, about 10 minutes away from Guaymas, it is one shop that you don't want to miss. You will find a huge selection of Mexican Handicrafts, but one thing that you might not find elsewhere is the handcrafted figures in palo fierro.
