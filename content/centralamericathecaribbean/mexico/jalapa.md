Jalapa is the capital of Veracruz state. The city issituated in a fertile valley surrounded by some of the highest mountains in the Sierra Madre including the "Pico de Orizaba". An extremely high annual rainfall contributes to the importance of this agricultural and commercial center. Coffee, vanilla and other tropical crops are found in abundance throughout the area.
