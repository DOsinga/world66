For 51 weeks out of the year, the main tourist attraction in Janitzio is the statue at the summit of the island.

During the other week it is the Dia De Los Muertos celebration.

### [Jose Maria Morelos Statue](/centralamericathecaribbean/mexico/janitzio/sights/jose_maria_morelos)

At forty meters tall, the statue of Jose Maria Morelos towers over the island from it's perch on the summit.
