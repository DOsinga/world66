If you can believe it, Merida has seen an explosion in the number of sushi restaurants lately. Sushi in the land of the Maya, you ask? Yes, sushi.

The best option for fresh fish, rice dishes and all the other Japanese food you have come to know and love, is Campay. What makes the difference is the consistently attentive service, freshness of the raw fish and availability of ice-cold Kirin or Sapporo beer. Also, Yucatecans seem to love cream cheese in all their sushi rolls, and Campay will not flinch if you ask them to keep the cloying, artery-clogging Philadelphia out.
