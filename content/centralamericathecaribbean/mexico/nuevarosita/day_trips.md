title: "Day Trips in Nueva Rosita"
type: section
---

**Nueva Rosita day trips **include visiting its coal mines and seeing its historical sites. Nueva Rosita is a city in Coahuila, Mexico. Despite of becoming a blooming city, Nueva Rosita has preserved its culture and history. It has a rich deposit of coal and a lot of historical sites visitors should not miss.

#### **Coal Mines
**

A large deposit of coal has been discovered in Nueva Rosita. In fact, coal produced in Nueva Rosita comprises 6.5 percent of the national production. Among the popular coal mines to visit is the Nueva Rosita OP Coal Mine. This is an operating pit mine. You need to ask and inform them ahead if you want to visit so they can prepare. If Nueva Rosita OP Coal Mine cannot accept visitors that day, you can explore other coal mines in the place.

#### **Historical Monuments**

Nueva Rosita has numerous historical monuments you can visit. Some of these monuments are the Municipal Pantheon, The Church of San Juan and Ignacio Zaragoza School. Municipal Pantheon is also called as Museum of Mummies. It is the preservation of mummified human corpses. Ignacio Zaragoza School is a school built in the nineteenth century that is named after Ignacio Zaragoza, the Mexican hero of military victory over the French in 1862.
