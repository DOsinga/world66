**Nueva Rosita**** restaurants** provides you the most delectable Mexican food. Nueva Rosita is a town in the northeastern part of the State of Coahuila in Northern Mexico.

**A Small White Food Truck**

Taqueria Nueva Rosita is an eating place in Nueva Rosita, Mexico. It is a tiny white food truck in a convenience store that serves different tacos and freshly made tortillas. Their taco is very cheap, amounting only to $1.5. Aside from tacos and tortillas, they also provide vegetarian dishes. Beverages are not served in Taqueria Nueva Rosita. If you want, you can buy from the convenience store. There is a wide selection of beverages and drinks offered in there for the customers of the restaurant. Alcohol is not available here. You can order for take out in this small eating place. This is a simple and small dining area, you will surely leave the place very full and satisfied with what you eat.

**Mexican Dishes Available**

Restaurant Bar La Puntada is a another great restaurant in Nueva Rosita. It is a popular eating place that serves a variety of Mexican dishes. They also prepare the yummiest T-bone steak. It has a nice and elegant ambiance that may contribute to a good appetite. This restaurant offers affordable food prices. The menu value only ranges from US$5 to US$10. With the tasty Mexican food that they offer plus a great ambiance, the little money you spend is all worth it.
