---
title: "Sights in Nuevo Casas Grandes"
type: section
---

**Nuevo Casas Grandes sights** will give you the true feeling of Mexican countryside. The following is a list of the best attractions to consider visiting during your trip to Nuevo Casas Grandes.

**Archaeological Zone of Paquime**

Among Nuevo Casas Grandes sights, this place is very important, and was even declared a World Heritage Site by UNESCO. Tour these ruins, where prehistoric architecture can be seen. The area is quite large, and there is a museum that houses most of what was found during the excavation. You will find pots, works of art, and religious objects on display. There is also a model reconstruction of the original city to give you an idea of how it looked years ago. On the way to the ruins, you will find locals selling souvenir pots at reasonable prices.

**Galeria Casa Grandes**

This** **is a studio where people are taught how to make local pottery. You will be able to get a chance to learn and see a step-by-step exhibition of how to make one. Some of work is for sale, so make a point of purchasing a piece. You will find that this establishment is the city center.

**Cueva de la Olla**

This is another of Nuevo Casas Grandes sights where you will see ancient artwork and tombs. It is an archaeological site and a museum as well. Here you can take very memorable pictures and buy some souvenirs to take home.

**Casas Grandes River**

This river is a great place where you can enjoy some quiet fishing time. Rent or purchase some fishing gear, pack a picnic basket, and head to this river, where you will really enjoy your day. There are various dams along the water where you can fish if you so desire. The scenery is breathtaking, so don't forget to carry your camera.

**Sierra Madre Occidental**

This** **mountain range, located near the city, is another tourist attraction that you should visit. You will find a forest full of pine and oak, and a few small wild animals roaming about in these mountains.

**Academia Juarez**

This Mormon colony is found near the Paquime. Here they have a beautiful compound where you can experience the kind of life they live. They usually grow many kinds of fruits in their orchards. Apart from fruits, various trees are also grown. Other activities include cattle rearing and wheat farming.

**Mata Ortiz**

Another famous of Nuevo Casas Grandes sights is this tiny village where the tradition of pot making goes back in time. Here you will find very beautiful designs that are hand made using the potter's wheel. You will be able to get a chance to see how this pots are created from start to finish.
