**

The Little Mexican Cooking School in Puerto Morelos**
Opens on January 5, 2010 and will be supplying twice weekly 'hands-on' cooking classes for tourists, in traditional and contemporary Mexican food. Bookings are in advance call 52 998 1598890.
The school offers an alternative cultural experience and provides a souvenir apron and recipe book as well as a late lunch

**National Maritime Park Reef** - Diving Snorkelling Fishing
The reef in front of Puerto Morelos provids hours of fun for divers and snorkellers, it is a protected reef so no motorised sports allowed. There are several dive shops to look after your needs, Dive in Puerto Morelos, Aquanauts, Almost Heaven to name just three. They can help with all your equipment needs and your snorkel or dive trips. There are several fishing companies including Diveing Dog Toiurs and Chucho's Don Pepe boats.

**Horse Back Riding and lessons at the Polo Club**, **four wheelers and more**
Puerto Morelos sports the only full size polo green in this region of Mexico at the El Rey Polo Country Club. Some of their beautiful horses ar available for trail riding through the jungle surrounded tracks of the grounds, for children and adults. There are also 4 wheeler tours to our beautiful cenotes where you can swim in their cool clear waters

**Want to Learn Polo, play or watch?**
World class polo is played at the El Rey Polo Country Club, you can pick up some very  fairly priced lessons, stick and ball OR come along on a Saturday and watch a game of polo. There are several tournaments through out the year and players come from all over to participate

**Yaax Che Botanic Garden**
Just outside Puerto Morelos is th beautiful  quiet lush botanic garden where you can learn a lot about the local flora and fauna. Come and support the many volunteers who have made this unique garden possible.

**Ruta de Cenotes - the world famous cenotes**
Puerto Morelos has several unique cenotes along the roda called Ruta de Cenotes, you cna get there by fourwheeler form the polo club or  with a hire car. Green Star, Siete Bocas, Boca de Puma, Kin Ha are amongst those possible to visit.
