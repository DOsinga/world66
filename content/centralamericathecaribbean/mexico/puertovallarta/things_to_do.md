### [Marigalante](/centralamericathecaribbean/mexico/puertovallarta/things_to_do/marigalante)

Set sail aboard a Pirate Ship!

The ship is a reproduction of an old ship, the Marigalante, a vessel
more famous under its second name, the Santa Maria. It is crewed
by a raucous band of pirates who do their best to ensure that a good
time is had by all. There are two different excursions offered:

**Day Trip

**Come aboard and enjoy a hot breakfast before the ship sets out for
the Majahuitas Islands for snorkeling, kayaking, swimming, or lying on
the beach. Lunch served on the return trip, with entertainment
