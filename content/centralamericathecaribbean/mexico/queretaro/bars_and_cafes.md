title: "Bars and Cafes in Queretaro"
type: section
---

**Queretaro bars and cafes **provide good places to wind down and to eat. Some bars like La Viejoteca have a specific theme, while others like Cafe Crayola offers great food for tourists and locals around.

**La Viejoteca**

La Vijoteca is one of the few Queretaro bars and cafes that is a themed bar. The theme revolves around the music that was heard in Mexico from 1970 to 1990. The decorations and pictures inside the bar depict important singers from that era.

The bar counter itself is made out of several counters that were used in pharmacies and other stores in the last century. The counters were restored and assembled for use as a bar counter, which is more than 20 meters long. The bar counter is used to hold and showcase some 400 products.

This bar plays live music, and they also offer a comedy show. The bar's address is Andador 5, Numero 39 in Queretaro Mexico.

**Bar Las Calandrias**

This bar is located inside Hotel Hacienda Jurica. The bar has nice leather couches and a table game area. Tourists and patrons can order their favorite drink while they watch their favorite sport. The average tab for a single person is around $15.00 USD.

This bar is located on the corner of Paseo Jurica and Paseo del Meson, in Queretaro's Historic Center.

**Cafe Crayola**

This is one of the Queretaro bars and cafes that offer a great menu for tourists who want to eat breakfast. Tourist can design their own omelets or breakfast burritos. Customers can chose to eat outside or inside the establishment.

Tourists can order a hot cup of coffee with cinnamon, tea or milk. Cafe Crayola also serves soda and juice.

The address for cafe crayola is Calzada de Aurora Number 40 in Queretaro. The average meal per person costs $7.00 USD.
