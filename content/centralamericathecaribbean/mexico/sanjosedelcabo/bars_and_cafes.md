---
title: "Bars and Cafes in San Jose del Cabo"
type: section
---

San Jose del Cabo is one of the popular tourist destinations
in Mexico. San Jose del Cabo is located in Baja California. There are a few
bars and cafes in town where you can enjoy drinks over lunch or dinner. These
are places that offer their visitors a chance to experience the warmth of their hospitality.

**El Moro**

This bar is located in the center of the gallery district in
San Jose del Cabo. The bar is housed in an old brick building. The owner of the
bar, Dana Lieb, opened this place for locals as a place where they can come and
enjoy their drink, “let off steam” and have a “swell time.”

**Baja Brewing **

You will find Baja Brewing is bar with a difference. The bar
serves international pub fare and cold Cerveza including India Pale Ale, Golden
Ale and Hefeweizen among others. If you are looking for a unique experience,
take a trip down to Baja’s. The bar also has a good range of food available.
MasterCard and Visa are accepted forms of payment.

**Cactus Jack’s**

Cactus Jack’s is a karaoke bar where you can come and enjoy
the singing or you can sing a few songs of your own while enjoying your drink.
The bar has a happy mood and is popular with the fun loving young crowd. There
are several large TV screens in the bar which makes it popular with the
American crowd in this part of town.

**El Spicy Cooper’s**

If Italian is what you want, El Spicy Cooper’s is the best
place for you in San Jose del Cabo. This is an Italian bar popular for its music. They have a regular band that plays here. Enjoy Salsa, Rumba, Bolero and
Flamenco as you sip on your drink and sample some of the bar’s interesting food. The bar is located in the art district in San Jose
del Cabo and is a great place to enjoy the evening.

**Red Lounge**

As the name suggests, Red Lounge is red. You cannot miss the
red square lounge located right across Desire Resort. This is martini bar that
caters to the young trendy crowd. The bar also serves a variety of delicious international
food. The bar is a popular hangout for locals and visitors alike. You can enjoy
an evening of some martinis and good music at this trendy bar.
