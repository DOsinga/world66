---
title: "Things to do in San Jose del Cabo"
type: section
---

San Jose del Cabo is a small, friendly resort town on the Baja
Peninsula. It is known for its incredibly warm, crystal clear waters
which are perfect for all sorts of water sports including swimming,
scuba diving, snorkeling, jet skiing, and kayaking. Founded as a
mission, the historic center of San Jose del Cabo is also a sight to
see, with its old, colonial style buildings including the Parroquia San
Jose Church. A great thing to do in this town is to simply walk around,
observing the architecture and the contrast between the old and the new.

**Beaches**

The beaches are popular places to go while on vacation, the most
popular being Costa Azul. It is lined with vacation condos, but still
is a nice stretch of sand to relax and sunbathe on. Unlike many resort
towns, the beaches of San Jose del Cabo are not directly in the main
part of town. They are somewhat out of the way, which makes them much
more peaceful and less chaotic. If you fish, La Playita is the perfect
place to go fishing. In the past, it was a basically unknown spot to
tourists, and was frequented only by locals. Today, it is seeing
more and more tourists. Near the beaches there are also many golf clubs
that are both gorgeous and reasonably priced.

**Natural Areas**

A trip to San Jose's Estuary and Bird Sanctuary is an entertaining
activity, especially as a family or as a couple. You can see more
than 200 species of interesting birds as well as fish, turtles, and
other marine animals. The place is always open and sunrise and sunset
are the most beautiful times. You can also have a nice picnic in
the estuary. Admission is free. Cabo Pulmo National Park is another
stunning natural area that you can check out. It is also a national marine preserve. The water is perfectly clear and you can see all of
the coral reefs. Snorkeling and scuba diving are popular there.

**Other Things to Do**

Of course, shopping is always a fun activity. Be sure to check out
Necri, a shop that sells pewter items and Talevera ceramics. For a very
upscale experience, go to Las Tiendas de Palmilla, a luxurious shopping
center featuring jewelry, women's clothing, and furniture. The spas of
San Jose del Cabo are also luxurious. The Hive Spa specializes in the
Mohito Sugar Cane Scrub, while the Spa at Las Ventanas provides
holistic treatments and healing with the 4 elements--earth, water,
fire and air.
