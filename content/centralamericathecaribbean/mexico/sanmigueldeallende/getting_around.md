There are a lot of options for getting around in the city of San Miguel de Allende, and if you are looking for the different types of **San Miguel de Allende transportation** that are available, you can find many to choose from including taxi services, taking the bus, renting a car and walking.

#### Traveling by Taxi

The taxi services in the city are easy to use, and you can find a taxi wherever you are in the city. You can have them pick you up at the hotel that you are staying at, or take you wherever you are going. The fares are reasonable, and you can find that the service is easy to use when you are in San Miguel de Allende. There are a number of places that you can catch a taxi in the city and get to where you are going.

#### Riding the Bus

You can also take the city buses wherever you are going in the city, and enjoy the many stops that you can make. The buses are affordable, and there can be a lot of stops. If you are not looking for a fast way to travel, you'll be fine with the buses, but if you need to be somewhere, you are probably better off calling a taxi. The bus service is easy to use.

#### Renting a Car

You can find a lot of great car rental services that you can use to have a car while you are in the city. The streets are well marked, so you'll be able to get around easily. Renting a car may cost more money, but if you like to go different places, you can find that it is a good choice.

#### Walking in the City

Taking a walk to get where you want to go is also a good way to get around in the city. The paths are paved and make it easy to find plenty of great places to walk. A lot of people walk around in the city and find that it is a fun way to stop and shop or eat whenever they want (and enjoy the views of the city). You can also choose walking as a mode of transportation to get to the bus station or to find a bus.
