---
title: "When to Go in San Miguel de Allende"
type: section
---

Going to Mexico for a vacation can be a great trip and if you know the **San Miguel de Allende when to go** options, you can enjoy the trip even more. There are busier times in the city, and you can choose to go then or at a time when things slow down.

**Traveling in April and May**

If you choose to travel during these months, you will be able to celebrate many of the Mexican festivals that are going on, and you will also enjoy pleasant weather. It is one of the quieter times to visit the city because it is not the tourist season yet, and you can still enjoy all of the activities that are going on without the crowds. You may find that you can save more money traveling during these months, when hotels and resorts are not as expensive.

**Traveling September to December**

You can also enjoy traveling in these months because it's not the busiest season. While there are still Mexican festivals taking place in the city, it's not a busy tourist time and you can enjoy the slower pace. Many people choose to travel here at this time because it's during the off tourist season.

**Traveling in the Winter**

If you travel to the city during the winter, you are likely going to see the most tourists and the biggest crowds. Most people who are trying to get away to a warm spot for the winter go to the city during this time. If you enjoy being around a lot of people and the activities that are going on in the city, you can have a good trip during these months. It may be more expensive to stay at some of the hotels and resorts, and you may have to plan where you're going to stay early to make sure that you can get a room.

**Traveling in the Summer**

When you travel to the city in the summer months, you will find few tourists, but you will also see Texans who often frequent the city at this time. It will be much hotter this time of year, and there will be more rooms available. You can also usually save money on the resorts and hotels when you travel to the city during the summer.
