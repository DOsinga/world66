Mexican Home Cooking School is located just outside the city of Tlaxcala, surrounded by three majestic volcanos. Estela Salas Silva offers classes in “traditional” cuisine. Far beyond what is usually understood to be Mexican food. Course is in English, spans the pre-Hispanic era to present day and vegetarian cusine. Classes include demonstrations, techniques, the use of ingredients, hands-on participation with usually a maximum of 4 students. Accommodations in “hacienda style” home with spacious double rooms, private baths and fireplaces.
