Cabo San lucas is located in the south of [Baja California](/centralamericathecaribbean/mexico/bajacalifornia). The town has grown rapidly ove rthe recent years. From as small village with just over a 1000 inhabitants in 1970 to a bustling international beach resort.

 There are trailer parks, campings, luxury hotels, discos, gift shops and a marina to cater for the flood of North Americans who come to fish here or just relax a bit.

 The town forms a small harbour facing the rocky peninsula that is the southern most tip of Baja California. The Cape was first rounded in 1539, when people still believed California to be a separate island.
