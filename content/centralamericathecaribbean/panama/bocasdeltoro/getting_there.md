---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

From Panama City, there are several direct flights daily from Panama City to Bocas Del Toro (one hour or less), or you can make the Panama-David-Bocas connection. By highway in a bus or rental car from Panama City, (or from San Jose, Costa Rica) it takes 8 - 10 hours to drive to the port town of Almirante where you can catch the regular Water Taxis to the town of Bocas del Toro on Isla Colon. Leave your rental car in Almirante everything in Bocas town is within easy walking distance and taxis around town are only 50 cents per person.
