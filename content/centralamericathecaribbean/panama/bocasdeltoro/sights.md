---
title: "Sights in Bocas del toro"
type: section
---

Bocas del toro is a top Panamanian tourist attraction. The Caribbean
archipelago on the northern coast of the country holds historical and
ecological wonders like no other place in the world.

**Sitio Drago**

Visitors can take a tour of Sitio Drago, where Christopher Columbus
first passed through on his way to the mainland of Central America.
Sitio Drago is located on the northern shore of Isla Colon in Bocas del
toro, and most hotels provide convenient tour arrangements. Artifacts
are still being found to this day extending
