The arid island of Culebra is located off the east coast of the mainland of Puerto Rico and can be reached by ferry from Playa Fajardo or by plane from [San juan](/centralamericathecaribbean/puertorico/sanjuan). Besides the fact that the flight is not very expensive, you'll have some breathtaking views before arriving at the airport of Culebra.

  Coral reefs, beaches, mountains and little villages set the tone on this island. A good option for exploring the island is by bike. It is a pleasant ride from Dewey to the unbelievable white Flamenco Beach, an excellent snorkelling spot.
