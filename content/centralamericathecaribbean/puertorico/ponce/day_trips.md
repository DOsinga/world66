title: "Day Trips in Ponce"
type: section
---

Caribbean Images Tours, 787 244-6283, operates 1/2 day guided snorkeling excursions to the nearby nature reserve island, Caja de Muertos. The island is located 8 miles off the coast of Ponce and its reef is teeming with an abundance of beautiful tropical fish. Nurse sharks, spotted eagle rays and schools of blue tang are common sights. info@caribbeanimagespr.com
