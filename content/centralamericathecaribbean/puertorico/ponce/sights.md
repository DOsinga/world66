**Plaza las Delicias**

The main square Plaza las Delicias is one of the loveliest in Puerto Rico with its lighted Fountain of the Lions.  

**Parque de Bombas**

Parque de Bombas (a red-and-black firehouse built in 1883)   

**Lady of Guadelupe**

Our Lady of Guadalupe Cathedral 

**Serrales Castle**

Serralles Castle a Spanish Revival mansion built in the 1930s by the island’s oldest rum-making family. You can tour the mansion and its grounds. If you have extra time walk to Vigia Cross a concrete structure that commemorates an old Spanish watchtower.
