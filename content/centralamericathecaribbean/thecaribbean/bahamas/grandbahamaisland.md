Grand Bahama is located in the North of the Bahamas. Contrary to what the name suggest it's not the biggest island, that's [Andros](/centralamericathecaribbean/bahamas/andros). Teh island is the home of Freeport, aka Lucaya, the second biggest city of the Bahamas. Freeport has a faster pace and a more cosmopolitan atmosphere than the old-world sophistication of [Nassau](/centralamericathecaribbean/bahamas/nassau). 

Freeport is a sportsman's paradise, with many golf, tennis, scuba diving, pan handler dodging, and fishing facilities. The city was set up completely as a tourist resort. Forty years ago Freeport didn't exist; today, it's "a fantasy vacation made real on the shores of Grand Bahama".
