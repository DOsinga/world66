title: "Bridgetown"
type: location
---

Bridgetown is the capital city of Barbados. The city's historic centre is well worth a visit. The main attractions in town include Trafalgar Square with a Nelson monument that predates the one in [London](/europe/unitedkingdom/england/london) by almost 40 years. Just east of here you find St. Michaels Cathedral built between 1784 and 1786. The Synagogue is a good 100 years older and is now a National Trust Property.

 Other interesting sights include the Garrison, the Barbados Art Gallery and the Barbados Museum.
