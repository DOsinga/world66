Christchurch is situited along the scenic south coast of Barbados. Popular is St. Lawrence Gap, where you will find a convention centre, a variety of shopping, dining and entertaining night life.

The long, white sandy beach washed by the warm blue of the Caribbean Sea.

It beckons guests to a leisurely stroll or an energetic jog along the picturesque shoreline.
