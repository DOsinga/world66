This tiny island
is the subject of author Robb White's novel, "Two on the Isle". White
and his wife Rodie bought Marina Cay in 1937 and proceeded to build
their home perched atop the peak, an idyllic setting to be sure.
Through many struggles, they managed to complete their task ... which
was no mean feat in those days. A set of unfortunate circumstances,
World War II and an uncooperative BVI government brought their dream to
a disappointing end.

Two
on the isle is a good read which I highly recommend for anyone who
loves the British Virgin Islands or may be considering building a life
here. Today, Robb & Rodie's original home serves as a reading
lounge/book swap for Pusser's hotel & villa guests. I'm sure Robb
White himself would approve!

Charles Tobias, owner of Pusser's, purchased Marina Cay
then set about building a very small and intimate resort, gift shop and
another Pusser's Restaurant on this charming, 8 acre island.
