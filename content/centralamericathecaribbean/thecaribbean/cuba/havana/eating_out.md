### La Bodeguita del Medio

Havana's La Bodeguita del Medio has long been known as a wonderful gathering place for cocktails, cigars, and the local cuisine.

It used to be the place where Hemmingway sat daily and sipped his mojito's. Now it's crammed with tourists wanting to taste the same mojito's. If you like paying thrice the normal mojito price for seeing a couple of Hemmingway photo's, this is just the place for you.
