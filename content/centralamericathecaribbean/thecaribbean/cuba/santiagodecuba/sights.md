---
title: "Sights"
type: section
---

There are many interesting sights: the Metropolitan Cathedral, the Municipal Palace, the House of the Young Diego Velázquez, the Town Hall, the Museo del Carnaval, the Casa de la Trova, the Bacardi Museum, Padre Pico, the Museo de la Lucha Clandestina, the Cuartel Moncada and so on.
