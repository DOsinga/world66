type: section
---

Traveling to Varadero, Cuba should be the trip of a lifetime. We have the top 5 **Varadero must do's** in Cuba. Cuba is known for beautiful beaches and rich history.There is so much to do in Cuba that you will never find yourself bored. We have a list of the top 5 must do's while staying in Varadero, Cuba

**The Beach**

The beaches in Varadero are breathtaking and should be a must on your to do list. With white sand beaches and crystal clear waters you can swim, snorkel, sunbath, or try any other water sport your heart desires. If you really want to take in the underwater delight that Cuba has to offer, try scuba diving.

**Casa de la Cultura**

If you want to see the local culture then Casa de la Cultura should be on your list of things to do. You will find rumba dances, local art museums, and poetry at Casa de la Cultura. If you want add some spice to your time at Casa de la Cultura try learning a new dance, maybe the rumba or salsa.

**Visit the Market**

The market in Varadero is one of the biggest around. You can find everything from handmade crafts and leather to musical instruments. Even if you are not up for shopping, the market can still be a joy. Spending the day in this lovely open air market will give you a real feel for Cuba.

**Visit Havana**

You simply can not go all the way to Cuba without visiting Havana. Hire a driver and spend the day touring Havana. Make sure you take a tour of Old Havana and take a walk along the Malecon. Old Havana is rich in history and has extraordinary architecture. Do not forget to take a break from the sight seeing and enjoy a traditional Cuban lunch or dinner. There are several restaurants to choose from in Old Havana.

**Swim with the Dolphins**

Swimming with dolphins in Varadero is a sure way to add some excitement to your vacation. You can take a catamaran cruise that includes drinks, a buffet lunch, dessert, peso cigars, and a dolphin swim encounter. You can't go wrong with ride on a catamaran ride and a relaxing swim with the dolphins.

No matter how you decide to spend your time in Cuba you are going to enjoy yourself. Spending a day at the beach, shopping, touring Old Havana, or taking in the local art you will find Cuba to be an extraordinary country.
