type: section
---

Vinales is about 40 minutes by car from Pinar del Rio. If you come from Havana you can either take the 3 hours highway route via
[Pinar del Rio](/centralamericathecaribbean/thecaribbean/cuba/pinardelrio) or take the 6 hour scenic route along the Cuban north coast.
