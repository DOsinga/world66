title: "Portsmouth"
type: location
---

Portsmouth is an excellent base for visiting Cabrits National Park where you find Fort Shirley. Other attractions in the area include a trip to the mangrove lagoons.

 The road between Portsmouth and [Roseau](/centralamericathecaribbean/thecaribbean/dominica/roseau) is pretty scenic. Many small villages, restaurants and overlooks on the way. The Macoucherie Rum factory with its old waterwheel near Salisbury is a nice place to visit.
