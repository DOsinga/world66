title: "Bars and Cafes in Sosua"
type: section
---

If you are going to Sosua, you probably want to find the best **Sosua bars and cafes** to stop at along the way. There are a variety of places you can choose.

#### Cabarete

This is a fun and energetic bar for anyone to enjoy. It is a great place to enjoy music and entertainment and to try some of the island cocktails. If you want to enjoy the beaches and being outside, this is a great choice. There are local favorites for interesting new cocktails that you can try, and you can either relax or get on the dance floor.

#### Pasha

The Pasha bar is a fun place to go if you like music and dancing. Enjoy the many different kinds of cocktails, and the music gets everybody on the floor and dancing. You can also relax outside and get the fresh air from the beach and enjoy the lights and sounds of the city.

#### Ono&rsquo;s and Bambu

The Ono&rsquo;s and Bambu club is a fun place to go and celebrate. You can enjoy dancing, music and some of the best local drinks. If you enjoy the energy and fun of a club and still want to relax with a drink and enjoy the beautiful islands, you can choose this Sosua bar.
