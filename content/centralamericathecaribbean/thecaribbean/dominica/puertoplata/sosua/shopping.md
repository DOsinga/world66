Sosua, a small quaint beach town in Puerto Plata, Dominican Republic offers its tourists the greatest pleasures of **Sosua shopping**. Situated just four minutes from the Puerto Plata international airport, Sosua is a destination easily accessible to tourists. Several beaches in Sosua with their clear waters and coral formations attract divers, snorkeling enthusiasts and fishing enthusiasts from all over the world to pursue their hobbies here. Given the large expatriate community in Sosua, you are sure to feel at home. Sousa also has a lot to offer to shoppers. Given below are some details on Sosua shopping.

**Shopping on the Beaches of Sosua**

Shopping in Sosua can be done in the line of shops on the beach or in the malls available around the town. Sosua beach offers its tourists a plethora of shopping options. Situated to the east of the main beach, there are several shops selling curios, collectibles and souvenirs. You can find some exotic handmade local produce, handicrafts, jewelry and paintings. Some of the popular shopping at Sosua includes Larimar Jewelry, the blue semi-precious stone, which is the native gem stone of the Caribbean, colorful art, Leon Jimenez cigars, Caribbean apparel and sweet Dominican Brugal Rum. Look out for the Larimar stone set in silver, which looks quite exquisite and is sold at reasonable rates. On your visit to Sosua you should also look out for Amber jewelry as the Amber from Dominican Republic is considered to be the best in the world. This is a great place to shop for gifts and souvenirs for your loved ones back at home. Be sure that you are ready to haggle for the best price.

**Shopping malls**

If you rather prefer shopping in malls to open stores, then Sosua offers you great options of shopping malls too. The Playa Dorada Plaza is one such mall where you can find exquisite stuff. You can shop from some of the best stores in the plaza which offers you great choices in handicrafts, Dominican cigars, fine art, heavenly Dominican coffee, great beachwear, t-shirts and other colorful apparel, beadworks, Dominican rum, pottery and great jewelry. All this situated just five minutes away from the Playa Dorada beach. You can shop all day at the Plaza and enjoy a nice night at the Playa Dorado beach. Walking is safe during the day and night in this area as there are several hotels situated close to one another all over this place.
