Two of the friendliest, as well as efficient barmaids, are Maxine and Shawn at the bar/cafe called Firefly Pancake House. This small bar is located right on the beach south of Margaritaville, and a little south of Chances.

Known mostly just as Firefly bar, it is becoming well known for the pancakes with various fruits, and a special one, 'Debbie's Vermont Pancakes', based on the personal recipe of Debbie who has stayed in one of the[Firefly](/centralamericathecaribbean/thecaribbean/jamaica/negril/accommodation/firefly_beach_cott) cottages every year since the 80's.

Make a point of calling in when on the beach and buying a very cold Red Stripe lager, or better still, a fruit punch - just see the fruit they pile into each drink!

There's a real friendly group at the bar and you'll soon get into the conversation (if you so wish). The guests come from many countries and cover all age groups.

Shawn was one of the ladies on Firefly's team for the Reggae Marathon and Half Marathon for the last three years, and in 2007 this was the fastest team in the race.

Soft music will not drown the sound of the waves, or the conversation and the place is open from 7am to 10 pm Christmas to the end of April, and 8 to 6 pm at other times.
