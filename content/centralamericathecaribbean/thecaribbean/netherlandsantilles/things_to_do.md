**Kitesurfing in Bonaire**

Due to the very steady wind and high water temperature Bonaire is a very popular kitesurfing destination.

At the far-west side of the island Roan and his team provide kitesurfing lessons for both beginners and the more advanced *boarders*.

Due to the laid-back atmosphere inherent to the surf-lifestyle the kitebeach Atlantis is a meeting point and hangout spot for fellow boarders and locals.

For more information see their website at:

www.kiteboardingbonaire.com
