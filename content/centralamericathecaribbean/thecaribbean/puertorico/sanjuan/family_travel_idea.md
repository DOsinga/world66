**San Juan family travel **offers many activities, accommodations
and attractions geared towards family entertainment. Services here are
geared towards families and children, with numerous hotels supplying
pool inflatables, rental car companies offering car seats and
restaurants geared towards children, making it a great place to travel
whether you have small children or teenagers.

**Family Friendly Lodging in San Juan**

There are countless family friendly hotels and resorts in San Juan. Two
particularly good hotels for family travel are the El Convento Hotel and the
Holiday Inn San Juan. The El Convento Hotel is centrally located,
making it a great place to stay if you want to explore the Old San Juan
and its many cultural attractions. The Holiday Inn is located close to
the beach, perfect for families that will be spending a lot of time in
the water and sun. Both of these hotels, along with many others in the
city, offer great services for planning family day trips.

**Family Friendly Activities in San Juan**

A great activity for families with young children is to visit the Museo
del Nino, or the Children's Museum. There are many exhibits here that
are both educational and interactive that will interest children of a
wide range of ages. Nearby the museum is the Luis Munoz Marin Park with a
great playground for children to explore.

There are many hidden underground passages surrounding Old San Juan's
fortress that make for an exciting tour. Kids can sign up for free
guided tours or the whole family can explore the fortress and
surrounding area together. Though the best way to see the Old San Juan
area is by exploring on foot, young children with tired legs may prefer
viewing the sights from a horse drawn carriage instead.

Many other water activities and day trips can be arranged through your
hotel as well. Additionally, many hotels offer daycare services and
group activities for older children. These services can be really nice
to allow parents to relax and enjoy some time alone together. Look into
these type of services before booking your accommodation to ensure that
they are available.

Older children and adults will enjoy the many outlet and designer stores
lining Cristo, Fortaleza and San Francisco streets. There is also
plenty of shopping for handmade items throughout the city that may
interest teenagers, as well as adults.

Find a hotel that will
suit your family's needs and begin planning your trip to this family
friendly city from there.
