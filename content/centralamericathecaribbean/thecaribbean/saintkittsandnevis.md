This lush tropical paradise could easily be in the South Pacific rather than the Caribbean. With lush forests and a dormant volcano, St Kitts and it's sister island [Nevis](/centralamericathecaribbean/thecaribbean/saintkittsandnevis/nevis) are often called 'the secret Caribbean.'   St Kitts was an British colony for hundreds of years, and the legacy lingers with afternoon tea and Cricket.
