---
title: "Things to do in Saint-Martin"
type: section
---

If you're planning a trip to St. Martin there are many things you can do
on a day trip or for nightlife. No trip would be complete without a
little bit of time on the sand and in the water. This guide will ensure you have a whole day filled with activities.

**Family Activities**

The flyzone on the French side is a zip line adventure course for both
kids and adults. How can you go wrong zipping through the island air?
For full day sailing and snorkeling trips head on up to Aqua Mania.
Their ferries from St. Martin are great way to see all the beautiful
islands surrounding the island. Another great idea for children is the
play-station. Playstation is a playground on the water. No matter what
you choose you and your family will thoroughly enjoy your day. These
trips are not offered on a daily basis, so check with your concierge
before starting your day.

**Nightlife**

If nightlife is what you're looking for, St. Martin has plenty. The
Soggy Dollar Bar is great for parties and its indoor and outdoor
setting. Bliss is one of the hottest club on the island. Bliss is
located in an outdoor setting and you will dance until dawn. For a more
island feel try Sunset Beach Bar. With the reggae and steelpan music
you will be able to really enjoy the island atmosphere. Sunset Beach is
also the best place to watch the sunset.

**The Beach **

For the cheapest day or night activity hit the beach with towels, an
umbrella, and a picnic basket. If you time your trip to the beach right
you will be able to relax without to many other tourist crowding the
beach. Pack yourself a great meal, spread your blanket out, and watch
the waves roll in a setting you can not beat.
