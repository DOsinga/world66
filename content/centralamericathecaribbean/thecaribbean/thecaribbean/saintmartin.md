This is the french side of the island of St Martin (french) / [St Maarten](/centralamericathecaribbean/thecaribbean/netherlandsantilles/stmaarten) (dutch). 

Situated in the North Eastern Caribbean, between Anguilla, Saint Barths, Statia and Saba, it is administratively a commune of Guadeloupe... the smallest territory in the world shared by two countries and the only one divided without a frontier...
