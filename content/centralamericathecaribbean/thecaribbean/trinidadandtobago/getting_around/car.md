If your time is limited and if you wish to explore the islands in depth, then the best option is renting a car.
There are several international car rental companies as well as local ones. Prices start at around 35 USD per day for a small car but will be higher during the peak season (and, obviously, for larger or more luxurious vehicles). Daily rates are reduced for periods of one week or longer (prices for one week start at around 225 USD). These prices may or may not include CDW (collision damage waiver). It should also be noted that drivers under the age of 25 are not permitted by law to rent in Trinidad and Tobago. Renting a car in Tobago is usually a bit pricier than in Trinidad . Rental cars have **R number plates**.
