World66 is a collaborative project to create an open content travel guide.

Have you been on a holiday recently? Do you know some great travel destination? World66 is for you if you want to share tourist and travel information.

If you:

- Have just returned from a fun trip to [Ibiza](/world/europe/spain/balearicislands/ibiza).
- Have trekked across the mountains of [Papua New Guinea](/world/asia/papuanewguinea/).
- Have visited this little-known but fascinating museum.
- Know some interesting places for travellers in your home town.
- Want to warn other travellers of things that went wrong during your recent trip.
- And lots more...

Then why don't you write about it? No matter if you like to think of yourself
as a modern-day explorer, or prefer all-inclusive package deals (or anything
in between), we'd like to know about it! destination you want to write about, click the [edit] button and you're set.

Perhaps you're not really interested in writing... but you know people who
are. Tell them about World66 - they might be really interested!

All the contents of this site are "Open" (for more information read World66 [Copyright
Policy](/world/contributing/copyright)).
