## Images and copyright

We invite every user to upload images to world66.

When we have no image we show images the google spider produces for certain keywords. We check if robots.txt forbids us to show the images and if that's not the case, we show them.

Tell us if you see an image we should not use - on the [change image] page you can find out how to do this.
