---
title: "Visitor Information"
type: section
---

The park is open daily from 8 am to dusk. Allow around three hours for a thorough visit. Entry fees are 700 leke for foreign visitors (500 leke for groups of ten or more) and 200 leke for Albanians. An English-language guidebook to the monuments is available at the ticket office or directly from the Butrint Foundation.

The park administration is based in Saranda: R. Skenderbeu Lagja 1, Godina e Muzeut Etnografik, Saranda. Phone: +355 852 4600.
