You should allow approx. three hours to visit the Park. An
English language guidebook to the monuments of Butrint is on sale at
the Park ticket office, or can be purchased directly from the Butrint
Foundation.

The Park is open daily, from 8 am to dusk.

**Entry fees:*** *

700 leke for foreigners

500 leke for foreigners in groups over ten

200 leke for Albanians.
