Albania has several ports with regular ferry connections to Italy and Greece. [Durres](/europe/albania/durres) is the main port, with frequent services to Bari and Ancona in Italy — the crossing takes roughly 8 to 10 hours overnight. [Vlora](/europe/albania/vlore) also has connections to Brindisi.

In the south, [Saranda](/europe/albania/saranda) has a short ferry hop to and from Corfu in Greece, taking about 30 minutes. This is a popular route for day-trippers in both directions. Shengjin, further north, handles some seasonal services but is less commonly used by travelers.

Booking in advance is advisable in summer, particularly on the Saranda-Corfu route and the overnight Italy crossings. Several operators compete on the main routes, so it is worth comparing prices.
