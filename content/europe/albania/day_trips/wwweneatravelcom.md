**First Tour Package**

**Day 1:**
Arrival at the Tirana International Airport(Mother Theresa),and after
lunch comes the accommodation at a comfortable hotel. In the afternoon
there will be a promenade round the city, to be introduced to its
beauty and also to learn something about it. Dinner will be at a
restaurant with live music.

**Day 2:**
After breakfast around 8 o’clock begins the journey to the beautiful
beach of Shengjin, where you will spend some wonderful hours among pine
trees and immense sand, and after a lunch with sea food, in the
afternoon there will be a visit at the castle of Lezha and also on the
grave of Gjergj Kastrioti(Scanderbeg) and finally the accommodation at
a hotel neat the sea in Shengjin.

**Day 3:**
After breakfast in the restaurant we continue our journey to Shkoder,
we will see the beautiful beach of Velipoja and after a traditional
lunch we will visit the castle of Rozafa. A castle of many generations,
rebuilt various times and with a particular history. We will see also
relics of this city. The accommodation at a luxurious hotel will relax
us and prepare us for the following day.

**Day 4:**
In the morning we will leave for Kruja to visit the castle of the city
and also the Historical Museum, we will visit also the Castle of Preza,
which is of the same epoch. We return in Tirana, and during the night
entertainment in the Pubs and Bars of the metropolis.

**Day 5:**
The destination of the day will be the city of Durres, where after the
beach we will visit what used to be the biggest city of the Adriatic,
Dyrrah. At night you can enjoy the diversity of the Albanian tourism.

**Day 6:**
We return to Tirana to visit the Castle of Petrela, where the Albanian
tourism presents one of its surprises, a traditional and at the same
time contemporary restaurant. After that we will visit the Cave of
Pellumbas which has been inhabited for many centuries.

Here ends the first tour package.
