latitude: 41.1118

The 17th-century hamam inside the castle walls is one of the better-preserved Ottoman baths in Albania. The domed structure is largely intact from the outside; the interior has been partially restored. Not in use as a bath, but worth a look as a piece of the Ottoman streetscape. It stands near the bazaar and is easy to find on a walk through the old town.
