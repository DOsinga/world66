Fier is a mid-sized city in southwestern Albania, best known to travellers as the nearest town to the ancient ruins of [Apolonia](/europe/albania/apolonia) (about 12 km away) and the Monastery of [Ardenica](/europe/albania/ardenica). The city itself is industrial and not a destination in its own right, but it is a practical base if you're combining visits to those two sites.

Buses and furgons connect Fier to Tirana (about 2 hours), [Vlore](/europe/albania/vlore), and [Berat](/europe/albania/berat).
