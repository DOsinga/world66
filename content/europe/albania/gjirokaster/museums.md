**The National Museum of Weapons**

The National Museum of Weapons at Gjirokastra has a USAF Lockheed T-33A which became lost over the Adriatic in the 1950s and was forced to land in Albania.

Ethnographic Museum is the house where Enver Hoxha was borned.
