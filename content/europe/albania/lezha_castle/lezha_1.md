Travel Guide

Lezha is located in the north western part of Albania. Located only 7 km away from the Adriatic Sea, it is a great destination in the summer. Lezha is a historic city, being the site where the "League of Lezha" was created and home to Skanderbeg's grave. Don't miss the chance to take a small hike up to the Lezha castle where you will be greeted with a breathtaking view of Lezha as well as Shengjin and the Adriatic Sea. While in Lezha, don't pass up an opportunity to try the seafood. It is fresh and delicious. There's so many reasons to come to Lezha - have you planned your trip to Lezha yet?
