Travel Guide

Kelcyra gorge is created by river Vjosa that runs here between Trebeshine and Dhembel mountains.
The national road Gjirokaster-Permet goes side by side with the river offering great views of the gorge.
Most probably you will not see the sun while visiting the gorge as you will be sorrounded by high mountains.

There are two restaurants in the place shown in the picture serving fresh trout and good wine.
