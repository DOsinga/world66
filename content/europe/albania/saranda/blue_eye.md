title: Blue Eye (Syri i Kaltër)
type: poi

A natural spring 25 kilometres east of Saranda where water pushes up through an apparently bottomless hole. The inner circle is a deep sapphire, the outer rim turquoise — hence the name. Divers have reached 50 metres without touching the bottom. Small entry fee; swimming is not permitted. Best visited on the way to or from Gjirokastër.
