Krorëz is a remote beach south of Saranda, reachable only by boat from Kakome or Saranda's harbour. The water is strikingly deep and shifts between green and blue depending on the light. A strip of clean sand backs onto rocks, and the remains of an old monastery sit above the shore. Because there is no road access, it stays uncrowded even in high summer — one of the last genuinely quiet stretches on the Albanian Riviera. Boat trips from Saranda typically combine it with other beaches along the coast.
