---
title: "Bars and Cafes in Tirana"
type: section
---

Tirana bars and cafes are quickly gaining a reputation for being the best in Eastern Europe. From first-rate sausage cafes to excellent local beers, even the most demanding traveler will enjoy him or herself.

**New Berlin Cafe**

If you want to sample the Albanina delicacy of sausages, then this excellent grill cafe is the place for you. You can also enjoy some excellent desserts such as creme caramel. Also on the menu are some of the biggest beers available in the city in 1-liter glasses.

**The French Bakery**

This is like a little corner of Paris in the middle of Tirana bars and cafes. The owner is well loved for his charm and amiability, and the atmosphere is said to be great. The macaroons stand out as a favorite dish, and the desserts are truly mouth watering.

**Era**

Perhaps Tirana's best late night dining/drinking option, Era serves delicious pizza and equally delicious beers at a time when most other bars and cafes are shutting down for the night.

**Biloku**

Many people's favorite hangout, Biloku is an atmospheric spot that serves cold beers and a selection of wines, spirits and soft drinks. The low-hanging lanterns, arched ceiling and long bar make for a great bohemian vibe.

**Flares Karaoke Bar**

One of the most flamboyant Tirana bars and cafes, you'll find Albanian locals belting out their favorite tunes over the P.A. The decor is very attractive and the beer of choice is ice cold Corona.

**Jatar**

If al fresco drinking and eating is your thing on sunny days then check out this superb cafe-bar.
