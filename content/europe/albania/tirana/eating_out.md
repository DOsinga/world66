---
title: "Eating Out in Tirana"
type: section
---

**Tirana restaurants** serve a blend of European, Balkan and Turkish cuisines. Most places remain open the whole day and serve food at inexpensive prices. There are different restaurants which serve seafood and exotic dishes such as kangaroo meat.

**Serendipity International**

This particular restaurant is well-known for its artwork and style statement. Serendipity is open from 10 a.m. to 11 p.m. The food is not too expensive. Even the main courses cost less compared to other restaurants.

Serendipity Restaurant is at the address Rr. Deshmoret e 4 Shkurtit, 21, and it is next to Hotel President.

#### **Rozafa Restaurant**

Rozafa is one of the Tirana restaurants known for its delicacies and seafood specialties. It is one of the most preferred restaurant in Tirana. The restaurant claims to use fresh seafood from the local market.

The address of the restaurant is Rr. Luigj Gurakuqi 2.

#### **Apollonia Restaurant**

This is one of the most luxurious Tirana restaurants. Apollonia Restaurant not only specializes in Italian, Greek and Albanian Cuisine but also in the making of kangaroo meat. Entertaining the guests with soft music, the restaurant has a garden with chairs for seating. The prices are more expensive than some other local restaurants.

Appolonia Restaurant is located on Bulevardi Deshmoret e Kombit.

#### **Era Restaurant**

Era Restaurant is located at the center of the city and mainly serves specialized Italian cuisine and other traditional Albanian food. It does not open until 11 a.m but is open until 11:30 p.m.

The address is Ismail Qemali (Sami Frasheri).
