**Tirana Festivals** display the passion in arts and music of the people in Tirana. Visiting the largest city in Albania is priceless. These festivals give satisfaction and happiness to all visitors.

**Tirana International Film Festival**

Tirana International Film Festival (TIFF) is the international film festival in Albania that is celebrated annually in the month of December. This began in the year 2003. Tirana International Film Festival is a cultural event has been successful from then on because of the sponsorship of the Albanian National Center for Cinematography. The ultimate goal of this film festival is to create a harmonious relationship among all the filmmakers and cinema aficionados from Albania and the whole world. These cinema artists and aficionados meet together to share their expertise and passion in art films.

**Tirana Jazz Festival**

Tirana Jazz Festival is a creative event in Tirana that began in the year 2000. The objective of this festival is to promote a stronger communication and collaboration between the local and international artists. The Tirana Jazz Festival has already invited more than a hundred artists both local and international to entertain the audience with their music. The Moving Culture, International Culture Center initiated the planning of this festival and it was supported by the Ministry of Culture, Youth and Sports. By celebrating the Tirana Jazz Festival, the residents and music artists of Tirana are getting acquainted with the various music styles from different countries. In this way, the Albanian music artists get an idea of how to enhance more their musical tastes and styles.
