An Ottoman mosque on Skanderbeg Square, completed in 1821 and notable for its interior frescoes of trees, waterfalls, and bridges — unusual in Islamic religious art. One of the few religious buildings in Tirana to survive the communist-era demolitions. Open to non-Muslim visitors outside prayer times.
