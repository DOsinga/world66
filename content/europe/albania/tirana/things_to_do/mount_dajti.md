---
title: "Mount Dajti"
type: poi
category: Sight
latitude: 41.3714
longitude: 19.9019
---

A mountain at 1,613 metres rising above the eastern edge of Tirana, part of a national park. Reached by a cable car (gondola) from the city outskirts — a 15-minute ride with views over the city and surrounding countryside. Good hiking trails and restaurants at the top. Clear days give views west to the Adriatic coast.
