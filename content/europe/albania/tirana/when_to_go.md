---
title: "When to Go"
type: section
---

Spring (April to June) and autumn (September to October) are the best times to visit. Temperatures are comfortable for sightseeing, the city is at its most lively, and the surrounding countryside is at its most attractive. Summer (July–August) is hot and dry — temperatures regularly reach 35°C or above — but the city remains functional and evenings are still pleasant. Winter is mild by Balkan standards, with temperatures rarely below 5°C, but rainfall is heavy and some days are grey; the city is less crowded and prices drop.
