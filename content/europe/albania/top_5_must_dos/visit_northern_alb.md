Explore Northern Albania. Unspoiled nature of the Albanian Alps will surprise you with its splendid views. You can either camp in the nature or go to one of the local guesthouses. For only 25 eur a night full board you can experience local hospitality, try delicious Albanian cuisine, and stay at a traditional house (http://www.tourism-in-albania.com/hotels-in-thethi).
