Vlore is located in southwestern Albania, on the coast of the Adriatic. It is the second major sea port after Durres. 

In ancient times, Vlore was known as Aulon. The oldest traces of civilization in the area of Vlora date back to the 6th century B.C. Nowadays it is a busy industrial city. 

There are some good beaches close to Vlore, although the onse closest to town tend to be a bit dirty and quite crowded in summer.
