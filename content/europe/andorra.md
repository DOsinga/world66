Andorra is a tiny ministate between [France](/europe/france) and [Spain](/europe/spain). High up in the Pyrenee mountains it is a unique place to visit. In winter time Andorra has some fine ski slopes to explore. In summer it is an enjoyable stop when going from Spain to France. The capital, [Andorra la vella](/europe/andorra/andorralavella) is a nice town, with a few good museums and a relaxed atmosphere.


 Andorra's shopping is known all over the world. Everything is prepared to give the customer the leading role. The privileged tax system allows very competitive prices. In the Principality you will also find [Caldea](/europe/andorra/caldea), the largest fun spa centre in the south of Europe, a water palace situated at an altitude of 1000 m, with an architecture and amenities which make it a unique building.
