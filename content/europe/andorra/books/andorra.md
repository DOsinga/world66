Peter Cameron's novel uses "Andorra" as a setting only loosely inspired by the real country — in his version it has an ocean coastline, which the actual Andorra emphatically does not. The story follows a man fleeing a mysterious past who takes refuge in this invented principality, and the dark comedy unfolds from there. A quick read with a genuinely enigmatic ending.
