A small museum dedicated to Andorra's postal history, housed in the Casa de la Vall area in Ordino. The collection traces the country's stamps from the earliest issues onward, alongside postal equipment, letter boxes, and related artifacts. Andorra's dual postal system — one French, one Spanish — makes its philatelic history unusually interesting. Worth a quick stop if you are already exploring Ordino's cluster of small museums.
