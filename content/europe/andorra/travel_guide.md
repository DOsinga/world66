Andorra la Vella is located in the Pyrenees Mountain Range and is the capital of Andorra. Andorra la Vella relies heavily on the tourism industry, and with all of the outdoor activities that the Pyrenees Mountains provide it is easy to understand why.

**Accommodations**

Being that Andorra la Vella relies heavily on tourism there are plenty of hotels in the area to choose from. With this many hotels it can be difficult finding the perfect hotel for your stay. If you are looking for a decent hotel for a reasonable price you will want to check out the Acta Art Hotel where rooms start at $94 a night. The Andorra Park Hotel is a bit pricier because rooms start around $214 a night.

**Things To Do**

While staying in Andorra la Vella one of the obvious activities is skiing or hiking, depending on which time of year you are visiting. Aside from that, one of the things that you simply can&rsquo;t miss is a trip to Caldea. Caldea is the largest and most popular spa in Europe, so when you visit make sure you go one day during the week. Three hours inside the spa is going to cost $42, but you can stay longer if you wish to pay an extra $2 for each 15 minutes.
