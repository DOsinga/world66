---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Armavir Marz's main draws are concentrated around the city of Vagharshapat (also called Echmiadzin), which holds three UNESCO World Heritage churches and the mother church of the Armenian Apostolic faith. Allow at least a half-day for the cathedral compound alone. The Sardarapat battle memorial and ethnographic museum, in the west of the region, is Armenia's most moving modern monument. The archaeological sites of ancient Armavir and Metsamor round out a full day's touring.
