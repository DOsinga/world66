The first comprehensive English-language travel guide to Armenia, covering the capital [Yerevan](/europe/armenia/yerevan) and the country's medieval monasteries, fortresses, and stone carvings in detail. Nicholas Holding gives practical information on day trips from Yerevan, the territory of [Nagorno Karabagh](/europe/azerbaijan/nagornokarabakh), and the history of the first country to officially adopt Christianity. Includes 24 maps and sections on architecture, wildlife, religion, and culture.
