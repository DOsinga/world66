A thorough and well-photographed guidebook covering Armenia and Nagorno-Karabagh, written by Matthew Karanian and Robert Kurkjian. First published in the early 2000s and updated since, it remains one of the best English-language travel guides to the region. The book combines practical travel information with historical context and strong photography, making it useful both as a planning tool and a companion on the ground.
