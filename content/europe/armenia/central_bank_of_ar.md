The Central Bank of Armenia on Vazgen Sargsyan Street in Yerevan sets the official exchange rate for the Armenian dram (AMD). For travelers, the practical value is checking the day's rate at cba.am before hitting the exchange offices scattered around the city center. ATMs are widely available in Yerevan and dispense both drams and US dollars. Outside the capital, carry cash — card acceptance drops off quickly in smaller towns.
