Cheers disco-pub is the only real western-style bar in Yerevan. The bar
is open everynight, with great music and drinks and very energetic
and "live" staff. You can find all the major drinks and cocktails of the
world here, including a big variety of beers. People who visit the bar
are expats, tourists, local and diasporan armenians. The place is open
everyday at 7:00 pm till 3:00 4:00 or even till 5:00 am sometimes. It's
the only disco-bar that stays late, where other places are closed.
Friday and saturday night are always packed with people and are not effected by other
parties as they have their own crowd.
