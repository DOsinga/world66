The waterfall that defines Bad Gastein — an 85-metre cascade that plunges in three leaps through the very centre of town, framed on both sides by Belle Époque hotels. There are viewing platforms at several levels, and the footbridge across the top gives a dizzying close-up view. At night it is floodlit. Completely free, utterly unmissable.
