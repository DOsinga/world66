title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

Austria is connected by all kinds of communications. Generally all travelers make base to Vienna which is connected by most of the world’s top destinations. Later, travelers who have their budget tight, use the medium of road for traveling. While other uses sky as their medium which is fast and easily accessible to other cities of Austria. There are five other international airports in Austria at Linz, Graz, Innsbruck, Salzburg and Klengenfurt beside Vienna.

Austria has excellent rail connections to all major European destinations. They are probably the most comfortable and environmentally responsible way to travel overland.

Buses are generally slower and less comfortable than trains, but they are cheaper and they go to all major European countries.
