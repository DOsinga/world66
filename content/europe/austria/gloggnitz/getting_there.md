---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

By car drive to Vienna and then take the Sud-Autobahn, the motorway from Vienna heading south to Graz / Slovenia. By air-plane head either for Swechat-Vienna Airport or to Graz. Recently high-speed train-link service has been established between germany and Vienna which allows you to travel in reasonable time without the hassle of traffic-jams. Also night train links are available trhoughout Germany and neigbouring countries.
