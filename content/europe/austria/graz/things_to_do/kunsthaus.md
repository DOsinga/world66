Designed by Peter Cook and Colin Fournier and opened in 2003 when Graz was European Capital of Culture, the Kunsthaus is immediately recognisable by its blob-like blue shell — the architects called it the "Friendly Alien." It stages ambitious exhibitions of contemporary art from the 1960s to the present. The building itself generates more conversation than some of the shows inside, but that is not necessarily a bad thing.

Lendkai 1. Open Tue–Sun 10am–5pm.
