This traditional, family-run Tirolean inn in the city centre is open throughout the year. Cosy restaurants and lounges tempt visitors to linger. The innkeeper himself caters for your culinary needs! Comfortable rooms with shower/WC and TV; beer garden, car park. Perfect for holidays with all the family. The family Bradic is looking forward to seeing you. Authentic specialties of the region and vegetarian dishes. main course between 8 - 13 Euro.
