Set in tranquil surroundings at the heart of the Tirolean Gurgltal valley. Marvellous view! Perfect base for various outings to the most beautiful valleys in the Tirolean Oberland region. This is the ideal resort for a relaxed holiday in central location. 50 beds, 1 banqueting hall, dining room, lounges (one with open fire-place), day room, bar, sauna, steam bath, tanning beds, gym, swimming pool, garden, sunbathing lawn. Dietary cuisine/Light foods, typical regional food, international specialities, authentic specialties of the region, vegetarian dishes.
