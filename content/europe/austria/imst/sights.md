**Imst - a Gem in Tirol!**  Imst has plenty to offer for the culture buff: interesting architectural buildings, museums, art, ....

**general**

**Carnival museum - Fasnachtsmuseum**

The recently opened carnival museum gives its visitors impressions of the "Imster Schemenlaufen" procession and "Imster Buabefasnacht". Guided tours in German, English and French available, entrance fee/person 1 - 4 €.

email: info@imst.at

address: Streleweg/Gafiailgasse, A-6460 Imst

tel: +43 5412 6910

url: [www.fasnacht.at](http://www.fasnacht.at)

**Museum of local heritage - Imster Heimatmuseum**

The museum was founded in 1909 and features items connected with the history of Imst, like prehistory, the Romans, textile industry. There are also religious exhibits, sculptures and tools from the mining era. Open all year.

In 2003 the Museum was reopened as "Museum im Ballhaus".

address: Ballgasse 1, A-6460 Imst

tel: +43 5412 64927

email: ballhaus.imst@cni.at

url: [www.imst.tirol.gv.at](http://www.imst.tirol.gv.at)

**King's chapel**

Opened in August 1855 after King Friedrich's ox Saxonias fatal accident.  

address: Brennbichl, A-6460 Imst

**St. Michael's chapel with tomb**

Small chapel next to the parish church; war memorial with 4 frescos by painters Kopp, Stimpfl, Wachter and Weißenbach.  

address: A-6460 Imst

**Imst farm museum - Imster Bauernmuseum**

Collection of farm tools and devices demonstrating traditional farm work which has almost been forgotten due to increasing mechanization. Free admission. Open September - June  

address: Meraner Straße, A-6460 Imst

tel: +43 5412 66346

**Church of the Holy Angels**

Designed by architect Norbert Heltschl, this church contains works by artists Gustav Stimpfl (glass windows, tabernacle), Elmar Kopp (stations of the Cross) and Andreas Weißenbach (St. Michael mosaic). In 1993 the angel fountain, also designed by Norbert Heltschl, and built by students of the Imst technical school, was consecrated. The angel statue was made by Werner Abraham.   [more..](/europe/austria/imst/sights/churchoftheholyangels)

address: Brennbichl, A-6460 Imst

**Maria Himmelfahrt parish church **

An impressive building from the 15th century with the highest spire in Tirol (84.5 m), frescos on the outside and a modern altar by sculptor Elmar Kopp from Imst.  

address: Thomas Walch Straße, A-6460 Imst

**Plague chapel -Pestkapelle **

Erected in remembrance of the 17th century's plague epidemic, which killed almost one fifth of Imst's population.  

address: Am Bergl, A-6460 Imst

**Rosengarten gorge**

Only a few minutes from the town center you will find on of the most beautiful biotopes, the Rosengarten gorge.  

address: A-6460 Imst

**SOS Children's Village**

In 1949 the first SOS Children's Village was founded by Hermann Gmeiner in Imst. Guided tours: Mon - Sat 9:00am - 11:00am  

address: Hermann-Gmeiner-Str. Weinberg, A-6460 Imst

tel: +43 5412 61493
