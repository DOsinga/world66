title: "Church of the Holy Angels"
type: poi
---

Designed by architect Norbert Heltschl, this church contains works by artists Gustav Stimpfl (glass windows, tabernacle), Elmar Kopp (stations of the Cross) and Andreas Weißenbach (St. Michael mosaic). In 1993 the angel fountain, also designed by Norbert Heltschl, and built by students of the Imst technical school, was consecrated. The angel statue was made by Werner Abraham.  

address: Brennbichl, A-6460 Imst
