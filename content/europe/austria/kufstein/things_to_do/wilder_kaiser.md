The Wilder Kaiser range — a jagged wall of limestone peaks rising south of Kufstein — is one of the most dramatic mountain landscapes in Tyrol. In summer it offers excellent hiking, from easy valley trails to demanding ridge traverses; in winter, the SkiWelt Wilder Kaiser-Brixental is one of the largest ski areas in Austria. The trailhead villages of Ellmau and Going are about twenty minutes by car from Kufstein and well connected by bus.
