Linz punches above its weight for a city this size. The Ars Electronica and Lentos sit side by side on the north bank of the Danube, the Hauptplatz anchors the Old Town, and the Pöstlingberg tram is one of those experiences that's worth doing even if viewpoints aren't usually your thing.
