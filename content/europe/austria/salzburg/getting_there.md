---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

**By train**, Salzburg is exceptionally well-connected: the main station (Hauptbahnhof) is on the main east–west rail line and has fast connections to Vienna (2.5 hours), Munich (1.5 hours), and Innsbruck (1.5 hours), plus direct long-distance services to Zurich, Frankfurt, Prague and Budapest. **By air**, Salzburg Airport (W.A. Mozart), 5 km from the centre, has mostly European regional and low-cost flights; for intercontinental connections, Munich is a better bet and can be reached in 90 minutes by train. **By car**, Salzburg is on the A1 Vienna–Munich motorway. The old town is pedestrianised and parking garages on the edge are well-marked.
