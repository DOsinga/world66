---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment in Schladming"
type: section
---

When it comes to the town of **Schladming**,** nightlife** is pretty much an afterthought. Most of the activities that the town is known for take place during the day, such as skiing and shopping. However, after a long day of skiing, there is nothing better than finding a nice quiet pub to sit back and relax in.

**Marias
**

If you are looking for a nice quiet place to sit back and relax after a day out on the town, Marias is the best choice around. Marias is one of the quieter bars in town, which allows you and your friends to sit and talk without any music blaring in the background. Marias is also the best bar in town to grab a glass of wine because they have a large variety of wines to choose from.

**Sonderbar**

If you are interested in dancing the night away, you will want to visit the Sonderbar, which is also referred to as the Cult Club. The Sonderbar is one of the most popular discos in the area, so no matter what night you stop in, you are sure to be entertained. Not only can you spend an evening dancing, but there are lounge areas inside the club that make sitting back and having a drink from of the numerous bars on site possible.

**Bazi&rsquo;s SportCafe Bar**

If you happen to enjoy bowling, you will want to check out Bazi&rsquo;s because they feature the only bowling area in the town of Schladming. Bazi&rsquo;s also offers an Internet corner with wireless Internet, which you can use to update your friends and family about your trip. Perhaps pool is more to your liking than bowling; if this is the case, the bar features four pool tables, plus a snooker table for guests to use.

**Hanglbar**

If you are looking for a bar that has the best music selection in town, you must stop in at the Hanglbar. Not only do they have the best music, they are also very big on tradition, which is important seeing as they are the oldest bar in town. You won&rsquo;t find many food choices at the Hanglbar, but there are plenty of snacks on the menu, along with a decent sized collection of cocktails. The bar opens daily at 8:00 p.m.

**Niederl&rsquo;s Caf&eacute; + Bar**

This bar is located in the center of Schladming, but it is more than just a bar. This is one of the best spots in town to stop in after a hard day of skiing. Although the caf&eacute; serves fresh coffee and homemade pastries, you can also find a good selection of wine. At Niederl&rsquo;s, not only can you warm up after a day of skiing, but you can also catch up with your friends about what happened out on the slopes.
