While visiting **Schladming, things to do** are going to include sightseeing tours, shopping and many outdoor activities. If you enjoy outdoor activities the one thing that you need to keep in mind is that what you can do is going to depend on the weather and the time of year that you choose to visit.

**Skiing**

If you are an avid snow skier or snow boarder, Schladming offers you some of the best snow around for skiing or boarding. The best part is you can ski or board year round because of how high up the ski resorts are in the surrounding mountains. The town has two mountains and both of them offer you some of the longest ski runs that you will ever see. The best part about the ski runs is that they are all uninterrupted. Dachstein Glacier is one of the best places to go because here you can try your hand at one of the fastest ski runs around. Dachstein Glacier provides activities year round, so if you are visiting in any other season be sure to check them out.
