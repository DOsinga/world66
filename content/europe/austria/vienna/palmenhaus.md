A beautiful bar inside a Jugendstil greenhouse from around 1900, set in the Burggarten. The main space is a restaurant, but a separate bar area to the left of the entrance lets you sit under high glass domes between large potted plants. Dozens of wines and spirits available. One of the more atmospheric spots in Vienna for a drink. U-Bahn: Karlsplatz.
