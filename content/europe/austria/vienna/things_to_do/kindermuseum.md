This place is amazing. There is different rooms with different topics, according to different ages. Everything from 0-14y. A Lab for multimedia (mixing, 3D, cartoons, etc.), a studio for the artists (paint, build), an Ocean room for adventures for the youngests. Just one thing: the prices are different for the different exhibitions and children pay more than adults. This place is in the middle of the Museumquartier.
