**by car:**

 from Passau: motorway A8

 from Graz: motorway A9 - exit Sattledt

 from Salzburg: motorway A1 - exit Sattledt

 from Vienna/Linz: motorway A1 local private transport

  **by train:**

 Wels is located right at the Westbahn train track. The Wels train station you wil find at Bahnhofstraße 33, A-4600 Wels, Tel. +43 (0)5-1717

  **by plane:**

 Airport Linz/Hörsching, ca. 25 km far from the city
