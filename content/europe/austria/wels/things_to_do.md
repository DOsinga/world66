---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

The heart of Wels is the long Stadtplatz, anchored at its western end by the Ledererturm, the last surviving medieval gate tower. Along the square you'll find the town hall, the Kaiserliche Burg (Imperial Castle, where Emperor Maximilian I died in 1519, now a museum), and a string of baroque and Renaissance burgher houses. The Stadtmuseum in the Burg covers local history, and the Minoritenkloster houses the interesting Österreichisches Zuckermuseum, devoted to sugar and chocolate.

A few kilometres north of town, Schmiding Zoo and Bird Park is one of the largest zoos in Austria and a popular outing with children.
