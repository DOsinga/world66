---
title: "Shopping in Zell am See"
type: section
---

The **Zell am See shopping** experience provides visitors a chance
to pick up some high quality products while on holiday. There are
several stores that offer some excellent items that you will want to buy
and take back home.

**Intersport City Xpress**

If you are in Zell am See to enjoy a skiing holiday, this is a great
place to start your shopping. This store stocks all that you need for
skiing and snowboarding. Besides sportswear and gear, you will also be
able to pick up some high quality casual wear, shoes, boots and bags
for men, women and children. Intersport City Express is located in
Schmittenstraße, right in the heart of the town center.

**Gazelle/Huber Shop**

This is one of the largest Austrian stores that offer high quality
lingerie and undergarments. This is a great place to shop for women’s
underwear, lingerie, slips, loungewear and undershirts. Huber also
offers shorts, loungewear and undershirts for men. You will be
able to choose from a wide range of international brands at this fine
store.

**Trend Maker **

This is s chic fashion company that offers hip, young and fashionable
clothing and costumes. Trend Maker has 12 stores around Vienna but the
store in Zell am See is the newest of all. You will definitely enjoy the
wide range of designs available at the store. The store has modern
interiors and the staff is highly skilled and very helpful. All the
creations of Trend Maker’s are crafted to perfection and come with an
attractive price tag.

**GEOX/Stiefelkönig**

If you would like to pick up some fine shoes, step into
GEOX/Stiefelkönig. This is a great place where you will find a wide
collection of shoes, boots and bags. All of the products available in
GEOX/Stiefelkönig are marked in US and European sizes so you don’t need to
worry about converting the shoe size. This store offers casual,
professional, sporty and formal shoes for men, women and children. The
store also stocks some super trendy designer bags and accessories.
GEOX/Stiefelkönig is dubbed as the “Boots King” of Zell am See.

**Pagro Diskont **

Pagro Diskont is a store that offers more than 9000 products like
office supplies, paper products, books and school supplies, computers,
household tools and much more. The store is more or less a
one-stop-shop for all your needs. You might find some interesting items
to buy and take home with you. Everything here at Pagro Diskont comes
with a reasonable price tag.
