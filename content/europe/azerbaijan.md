Azerbaijan is a small country on the Caspian Sea. Until 1991 it was part of the Soviet Union. It borders on [Armenia](/europe/armenia), [Georgia](/northamerica/unitedstates/georgia), [Russia](/europe/russia) and [Iran](/asia/iran), but culturally speaking it probably has the closest ties with Turkey. The Azeri language is a lot like an archaic form of Turkish. Iran has a large Azeri Community as well.


 [Baku](/europe/azerbaijan/baku) the capital is the best place to start exploring the country. The city has a strange kind of charm. It is old, a bit run down and dirty (the beaches are very dirty actually) but once you accept this you will surley love it.


 There are a few good beaches on the Caspian coast, especially in the direction of the Iranian border.


 The hinterland of Baku is mountainous. To cross for Baku to [Tbilisi](/europe/georgia/tbilisi) in Georgia is a great experience. It would take you over high passes and through gorgeous valleys.
