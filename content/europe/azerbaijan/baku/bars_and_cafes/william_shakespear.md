A basement pub on A. Alizade Street, wedged between Finnegan's Irish Pub and Adams. The same owner has kept standards high through at least one relocation. It draws heavily from the oil and gas crowd, who come for decent beer, reliable food, and a lively atmosphere. Big screens cover major sports — rugby, football, Formula 1 — and live music runs on Friday nights. Staff are friendly but the place gets busy enough that you may need to chase your order.
