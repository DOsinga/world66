Situated in the Old City / Icheri Sheher ( centre of Baku, Azerbaijan
), The Oilman's Inn is a wonderful fusion of a Curry House & Pub.
It is just 2 minutes walk from the famous Maiden's Tower. They mainly
cater to the expat community living in Baku.

They offer a quite extensive a la carte menu that includes British,
Indian, Chinese, Continental and international cuisine. Curries like
Balti, Tikka Masala and Korma are their specialities. Though their
traditional British & European food like Steaks, Pork & Lamb
Chops, Fish 'n' Chips etc. are equally delicious. They offer set lunch
menu too. Prices are quite reasonable.

Their bar is open till quite late and they offer a wide range of beers, spirits & wines etc.

Though opened recently, they have become one of the most popular
restaurants & pubs in Baku because of their high quality food
& services.
