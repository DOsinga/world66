August 2009 - Shakespeares has relocated and is now nestled between Finnegans Irish Pub and Adams on A. Alizade Street. It is run by the same owner so the overall standard remains high.

The pub is located in the basement of the building while Maharaja's Indian restaraunt is upstairs in the same building. The pub is a great place to for a beer, good food and a lively atmosphere. Frequented these days mainly by the Oil and Gas community who enjoy their Nights at the Round Table. Supposedly, there are tables for smokers and non smokers, but from experience this does not work.

Live music on Friday nights. Big screens show all major sports events including Rugby, F!, Premier and Scottish Football, International championships, etc. Celtic FC are well represented and alas, so is cricket. Staff are hard working and friendly but expect to have to chase your orders!
