---
title: "Getting There in Nakhchivan City"
type: section
---

Nakhchivan city is served by an airport and theoretically has good
road and rail links, however the Nagorno-Karabakh conflict has made
access more difficult contributing to a greater isolation. The best way
to get to Nakhchivan is to fly from Baku to Jevlach airport,
south-east of the city (airport code: EVL). From Jevlach you can also
fly to Moscow with Samara airlines. If you are arriving from Baku there
is no passport check and you can simply walk out of the terminal. There
are buses and taxis to the city, which is only 3km away. From the city,
buses to the airport leave from the Shabuz bus station on the NW part
of the city.

Several buses a day depart for Igdir and Erzerum in eastern
Turkey , allowing easy connections to the rest of Turkey. There are
train services to Ordubad and Sharur. Inside the city there are buses
and trolley-buses.
