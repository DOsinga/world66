---
title: "Practical Information in Nakhchivan City"
type: section
---

Nakhichevan radio operated as a station in 1932 and since 1936 it has been broadcasted on the territory of the Autonomous Republic.

Television has been broadcasted as an independent channel on the territory of the Autonomous republic since 1936.

Nakhichevan accounts for the state committee for radio and television programs and Channel-25 within the center of Radio and Television Transmission under the Ministry of Communication and Informational Technologies.

The channel-35 established on July 14, 2004 broadcasts its scientific and publicistic programs on the territory of the Autonomous Republic.

Radio ?The voice of Nakhichevan? that started operating on September 19, 2001 is transmitted on the 100.6 FM wave within 18 hours on the territory of the Autonomous republic.

The sociopolitical newspaper Sharg Gapysy started operating in 1921. It was founded by the Supreme Assembly of the Autonomous Republic of Nakhichevan and the Cabinet of Ministers.

The executive body of Nakhichevan Nuh Yurdu newspaper started operating in 1996. The newspaper plays an important role in the sociopolitical life of Nakhichevan.

Moreover, the programs of Azerbaijani television and radio, Lider, Space and ANS channels as well as Turkish TRT and Russian ORT channels are broadcasted in the city.

The official newspapers of the Republic of Azerbaijan Yeni Azerbayjan, Khalg other publicistic bodies (Iki sahil, Express, Zaman, 525-dji gazet and others) are distributed among the subscribers.
