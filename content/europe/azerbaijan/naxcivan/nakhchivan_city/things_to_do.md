title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Nakhchivan City's main attractions are its medieval monuments, particularly the extraordinary 12th-century mausoleums designed by the master architect Ajami. The Momine Khatum Mausoleum is the finest example of medieval Azerbaijani architecture. The State History Museum and the Carpet Museum in the historic Khan's House give context to the region's long history.
