The region accounts for over 100 historical and architectural as well as archeological monuments differing in their sizes. Over 30 of them are protected by the state.

These are the Sarshahar Mosque, Anbars Mosque, Gaysariyya, Cuma Mosque, Mingis Mosque, Takeshiyi Mosque dating
