---
title: "Sights in Sadarak"
type: section
---

An *ancient habitation* of the eneolit period and early bronze age locates in the south of Sadarak.

*Sadarak habitation* of the middle ages locates on the left bank of Araz.

*Sadarak tower* of the late Bronze and early Iron Ages locate at the peak of Vali dag in south-east of Sadarak.

*Sadarak cave* and *Div horan* (Giant built ) of the Bronze Age are also among ancient historical monuments.

One of the habitations of the Middle Ages is *Girmizi tora* that locates in north-east of Sadarak village.

The *Mosque of Sadarak* is a monument of the 18th-19th centuries.

The *ancient habitation of the 2nd millennium and graveyard* locate in the south-west of the village Kerki.

*Chaparag tumulus* (burial monument) locate on Vali dag chain.

The history of the temple of *Ag oglan* has ancient history as well.

Built memorial monument dedicated to those died in the Great
Patriotic War and the alley of martyrs fell in struggle for the
independence of our lands.
