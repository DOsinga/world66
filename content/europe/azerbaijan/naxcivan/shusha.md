Shusha is a Azerbaijani city in the Karabakh region. The city is famous for the fact that alot of talented artists were born and raised here. Shusha has some of the most ancient Christian churches built by the ancestors of Azerbaijanis, the Caucasian Albanians.

Shusha is now under occupation by Armenians armed forced due the Karabakh conflict.
