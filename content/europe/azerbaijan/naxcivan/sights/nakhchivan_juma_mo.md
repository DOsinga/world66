Nakhchivan Juma Mosque – this monument belongs to Middle Ages as well, and it was also constructed by architect Ajami Nakhchivani son of Abubakr, and is one the monuments of Atabeyler architecture complex. It was conserved in the destroyed condition by XIX century Kohnegala – the Middle Ages settlement far from Shapurgala, near to Shahbuzkend in Shahbuz region. It was registered in 1990. Area is more than 10 ha. It is surrounded with precipitous cliffs. During the archeological exploration the clay pots pieces were discovered. The stonewall pieces in the southeast part of Kohnegala may be observed far from 150 m. The surface materials are iron, lion-off and lion earthenware pots and pieces of glass dishes. The settlements are supposed to belong to XIV-XVIII centuries.
