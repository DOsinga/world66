**Kish** (Kiş) - this town is about 5km from Sheki (about AZN4 by taxi). It's a bumpy ride on the narrow cobblestone roads, but well worth it to visit the beautiful Albanian church, fabled to have been started by the first century apostle Eliseus, who converted the site from a pagan temple to an apostolic church.
