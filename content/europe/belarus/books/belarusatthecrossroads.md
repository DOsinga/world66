title: "Belarus at the Crossroads"
type: poi
---

Belarus is little understood, and has been neglected by many in the West. "Belarus at the Crossroads" attempts to explain the importance of Belarus not only to its neighbors, but to all of Europe. It is a collection of essays by authors from many different countries, such as; Russia, Poland, Lithuania, United States, and Belarus. Each author gives a description of how Belarus is viewed from their country and analyzes the relationship between their own country and Belarus. Each author examines the role that Alexander Lukashenko plays in the development of Belarus, and how their country relates to his control of Belarus.
