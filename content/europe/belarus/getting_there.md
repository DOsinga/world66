Citizens of most Europian countries, Canada and the Unites States require a visa to travel to Belarus. Starting April 1st, 2006 original invitation is required to apply for Belarus visa.

For citizens of the Unites States, Canada, Japan and EU countries it is possible to buy visa in the airport Minsk-2.
