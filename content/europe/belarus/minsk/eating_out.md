If you've been traveling around Belarus, you probably haven't found much variety in your diet. Minsk is the only place in Belarus that has a large number of different restaurants to choose from. 

**Restaurants**

**Uzbekistan**

This is my personal favorite. Here you can get some of the greasiest food to be found anywhere. If you are hungry order the Plov, a meat and rice dish. The soups are also good. The service is decent and sometimes after 7 pm there is live entertainment. A good filling meal is about US$4.

**McDonalds**

Same McDonalds as everywhere else. In the one across the street from Patio Pizza there is a plaque, telling you that Belarus was the one hundreth country that McDonalds has opened in.  

Many locations around Minsk 

**El Rincon Espanol**

Minsk's only Spanish restaurant usually has good food, but is often overpriced. The waiters serve you dressed in Spanish attire.  

address: Skoriny 22

tel: (+375-17) 227 23 31

openings: 12:00 - 24:00 (licensed until 03:00).

**Blinnaya**

A Belarusian version of crepes - some are so-so, some are delicious. Very friendly staff of mostly young girls eager to please (something rarely found in old-style establishments).
