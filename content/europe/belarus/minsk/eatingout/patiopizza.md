Openings=12:00 - 24:00.  A great stop for anyone from out of town as well as the few locals who can afford the mouth-watering pizzas, well-stocked salad bar, excellent cocktails and tasty pastas on offer. Brick walls and red-checkered tableclothes make for a warm family atmosphere. If you really want it is possible to convice the cooks to give your pizza a double thick crust, that is if you like thick American pizza crusts. A full meal with a drink should not run you more than US$10
