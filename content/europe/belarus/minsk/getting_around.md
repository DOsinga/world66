There are many ways to travel around Minsk. The fastest and easiest type of public transport is the Metro(underground subway). There are also trollys, buses, and trams. Taxis are also a good way to get around the town, but they can be expensive. Beats the price of NYC taxi though - you can cross the city for $5. Most state taxis (yellow "Volgas") have an electronic counter - just make sure driver zeroe's it before you leave. Negotiation is encouraged prior to boarding the private taxi. Negotiate down, and then tip a guy - they are always very appreciative.
