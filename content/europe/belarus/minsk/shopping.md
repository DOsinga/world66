Don't be afraid to go out and shop, if you have money you can buy almost anything in Minsk.



**Shops**

**Antiques**

Skoriny 43, tel. (+375-17) 284 86 17. Open 10:00 - 14:00, 15:00 - 19:00. Closed Sat, Sun.

**TSUM (Central Department Store)**

Skoriny 54, tel. (+375-17) 284 21 64. Open 09:00 - 21:00, Sun 11:00 - 18:00.

 Here you can buy clothes, food, souvenirs, etc.

**GUM (State Department Store)**

Skoriny 23, tel. (+375-17) 227 88 76. Open 09:00 - 21:00, Sun 09:00 - 21:00. Sun 11.00 - 18.00. Often crowded, but has a good selection of many items. Also there is a decent cafeteria on the top floor.

**Komarovsky Market**

Very Khoruzhey 6, tel. 231 79 00. Open 08:00 - 19:00. Closed Mon.

 All kinds of food, you can even get yourself a whole pig, or a barely alive carp. Outside you can buy all kinds of seasonal produce.

**Zhdanovichi Market**

10 minutes’ drive on the highway extending from Masherova tel. 296 72 25.

**Artists Market**

Lenina, a short walk from McDonalds, it is halfway between the Metro stops Kupalovskaya and Nemiga.

 Here you can barter for handcrafted items, paintings, *matroshka*(nesting dolls), etc. If you are staying a while it is possible to get an artist to make a set of nesting dolls [more..](/europe/belarus/minsk/shopping/artistsmarket)
