One of the first and most comprehensive World War II museums in Europe, opened before the war even ended. Belarus lost a quarter of its population — the highest per capita loss of any country in the war. The exhibits cover the full occupation and liberation, including a detailed model of the Maly Trostenets death camp. Open 10am–5pm, closed Monday.
