Groenplaats is a large square in the heart of Antwerp, right next to the Grote Markt and the Cathedral of Our Lady. It was built over the cathedral's old cemetery — the name means "green square" — and serves as one of the city's main meeting points.

A statue of Rubens stands at the centre, surrounded by café terraces that fill up whenever the sun appears. The square is a good starting point for exploring the old town: the cathedral is steps away, the Grote Markt is just north, and the Meir shopping street begins nearby.
