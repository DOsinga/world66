Art nouveau street, unique architecture from around 1920. The
street is located outside of the city centre, near the Berchem train
station (where the international train Brussels-Amsterdam stops).

There aren't any buildings you can go in, but it is great to stroll
through the street and the side streets (Waterloo street, Transvaal
street) where you will find many great buildings and villas.

There are some nice bars and restaurants in the neighborhood too.
