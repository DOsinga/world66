Bastogne is located in the South-east of Belgium, close to the border with [Germany](/europe/germany) and Luxembourg . The region is very beautiful with rolling hills, small rivers and great outdoor opportunities. Many old castles and mansions are to be found in the area. Bastogne is an ideal place for exploring the area.
