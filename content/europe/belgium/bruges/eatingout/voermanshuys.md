A real treat is the Voermanshuys. Located in a 'gezellige' cellar the food is really great and the service very friendly. The cook even won the 'Best cook of Flanders' award in 1998.

Price? €50 for a menu Marché (4 course). €70,- including pre-dinner drink, wines at every course, mineral water and coffee or tea.

The restaurant is located not far from Market square but keep in mind: the address is 'Oude Burg' and not just plain 'Burg'.
