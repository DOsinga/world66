Brussels, home port of the national airline SN Brussels Airlines, is an important junction in the international air traffic.

Bruges is within easy reach (ca 1 hour).

By road : E 40 highway, direction Ostend (exit nr. 8).

By train: regular trains (every half hour) between the airport and railway stations Brussels Centraal or Brussels Noord, from where several trains per hour to Bruges.
