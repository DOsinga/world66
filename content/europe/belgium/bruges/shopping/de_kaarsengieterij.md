De Kaarsengieterij is the oldest, and the one and only, candle-shop in the centre of Brugge.

Only a few footsteps away from the marketsquare you find exclusive, high quality candles. All the candles are designed and hand made in Brugge by the shopowners. A wide variety from garden candles to interior candles, all in unique colours.

Do you stay two or more days in Brugge, do not hesitate to order a hand painted candle for whatever occasion (wedding, birthday, ...). Kalligraphique or modern fonts, the choice is yours. Concerning these personal gifts, our device: measure to pleasure.

A nice range of candle-holders and t-lights make the shop to a jewel.
