**Caves**

There are several caves in the area of Dinant. The most beautiful one is 'La Merveilleuse' (the wonderful). Visit the magnificent waterfalls and stalactites. The entrance is about 500 m from the Dinant Railway station. 

openings: from April till 15 Noember daily from 11am to 5pm (In July/August till 6pm).
