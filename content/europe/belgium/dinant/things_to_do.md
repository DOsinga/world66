---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

The citadel is the main sight — take the cable car from beside the church or climb the 420 steps for the exercise. Inside there is an arms museum and views over the Meuse valley. Summer boat trips run from the quay along the river to Freyr, Hastière, and even across the French border to Givet. The cave of La Merveilleuse, 500 metres from the railway station, has impressive stalactites and underground waterfalls.
