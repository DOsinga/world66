Great service, with an owner who creates a familiar atmosphere. Like in most places in Liege the staff only speaks French, but you get by with pointing at the English menu.

The steak here is excellent -- extremely tender, with an interesting range of dishes and combinations on offer. On weekends there is a live band and the place fills up.

Dinner runs anywhere between $15 and $30.
