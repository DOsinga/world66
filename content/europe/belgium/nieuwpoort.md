Nieuwpoort is a fishing harbour by the North Sea, and is very well-known for his shrimps.Nieuwpoort is the greatest belgian harbour for sailing-boats and yachts.It lies at the mouth of the river Yser. This river was during the Worldwar I and II a fighting place against the Germans.
