One of the great motor racing circuits in the world, Eau Rouge alone worth the journey for any motorsport fan. The track cuts through the Ardennes forest about 7 kilometres from Spa town, and on race weekends — particularly the Belgian Grand Prix in late August — the entire region fills with fans. Outside race season, the circuit offers driving experiences, guided tours of the paddock, and a small museum. The setting, among pine-forested hills, makes it more dramatic than almost any other track in the calendar.
