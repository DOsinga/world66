title: Things to Do
type: section
---

Ypres and the surrounding Flanders Fields are one of the most important World War I memorial landscapes in the world. The In Flanders Fields Museum in the rebuilt Lakenhalle is the place to start — it covers the entire Ypres Salient through personal stories and interactive exhibits. Beyond the town, dozens of cemeteries, preserved trenches, and monuments are scattered across the countryside.
