Europe is an
Eyewitness Travel Guide published by DK Travel. The Eyewitness series
is one of the most comprehensive and respected travel guide series on
the market today. Europe is 8.6 x 5.1 x 1.4 inches, 800 pages and 2.3
pounds of travel tips covering the countries of Great Britain, France,
Ireland, Belgium, the Netherlands, Luxembourg, Spain, Italy, Greece,
Portugal, Germany, Switzerland, Austria, Sweden, Denmark, Norway,
Finland, Hungary, the Czech Republic and Poland.

All of the major
cities of Europe are covered in exquisite detail with eye-popping
magazine quality photographs of historical sites and tourist
attractions that will make you refer to this guide over and over, even
long after your trip. The guide helps you plan a perfect trip, is a
wonderful tool to help you complete the trip once you’ve set out, and
serves as a beautiful memento of the trip once you return home.

The
guide gives pointers on finding the best restaurants and tells you what
to expect on the menus. It lists pros and cons for a selection of
hotels, suggests routes for traveling from one iconic location to
another – it even provides 3-D maps and floor plans for many of the
sites so that you know where to park, how to enter, etc…

The Eyewitness
Travel Guide for Europe provides thousands of cultural details that will
allow you to move seamlessly from one cultural hot spot to the next, traveling confidently and secure in the knowledge of where you can
stay, what to expect when you get there, what to order from the menu
and how much to tip the waiter. The book is simply an incomparable
compendium of relevant customs and facts for anyone traveling through
Europe.
