A collection of poems by Beatrice Lao, born between the Alps and the Tyrrhenian Sea. The writing captures the light, landscapes, and moods of the Mediterranean with a quiet intensity. Worth picking up if you're drawn to European poetry that feels rooted in specific places rather than abstractions.
