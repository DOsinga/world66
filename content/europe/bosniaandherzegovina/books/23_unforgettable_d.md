A collection of over 700 photographs taken on journeys through Bosnia and Herzegovina, portraying the natural beauty and versatile landscapes of the country. The photographs are grouped into 23 sections representing 23 days of travel, each with a map and suggestions for outings.

The itinerary covers Mostar, Blagaj, Stolac, the wine road, rafting on the Neretva, Sarajevo and its surroundings, Bjelasnica, Prokosko Lake, the Upper Drina region including Sutjeska National Park, old fortress towns like Doboj and Travnik, Jajce, the Una National Park, and more. Bilingual in English and Bosnian.
