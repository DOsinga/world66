---
title: "Apparition Hill (Podbrdo)"
type: poi
category: "Sight"
latitude: 43.1883
longitude: 17.6842
---

The rocky hillside in the neighbouring hamlet of Bijakovići where six local teenagers reported the first visions of the Virgin Mary on 24 June 1981. A rough, stony track climbs up past bronze plaques at each station, to the spot where the visions were said to occur. Wear proper shoes — the path is steep and uneven.
