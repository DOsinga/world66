Sandstone and conglomerate formations rising up to 200 metres, shaped by millions of years of erosion into towers, pillars, and figures. Local legend has given names to the most distinctive shapes -- "The Madonna", "The Monks", "The Bear", "The Schoolgirl". The rock park has marked paths that wind between the formations, and the views back towards the fortress perched among the cliffs are striking.

The Belogradchik Fortress, built into the rocks themselves, dates back to Roman times and was expanded by the Ottomans. The combination of natural rock formations and man-made fortifications blending into the landscape is unlike anything else in the Balkans.
