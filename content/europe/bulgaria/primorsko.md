Travel Guide

Town of Primorsko
is picturesquely situated on a small Peninsula between the beautiful
Stamopolu Bay and Devil’s Bay, situated 50 km south of Burgas. The
climate is on the continental influence from the north and west, the
Black Sea influence from the east and the Mediterranean influence from
the south. The weather in the summer is sunny and warm with fresh day
winds and comparatively high night temperatures. Material and technical
foundation – the town of Primorsko has about 75 000 beds in hotels,
motels, family boarding-houses, camping sites, holiday houses, villas,
houses and individual rooms, it has constructed complete hotel
complexes, including restaurants, swimming pools, sport hails, parking
lots, parking areas, children playgrounds, and others. In the
entertainment and food – establishments one may taste dishes both from
the Bulgarian traditional cuisine and the international one. The new
"Primorsko" Sports Complex disposes of a sports centre and an
aqua-park, stretched on 30 decares. The complex is unique on the
territory to the south of Burgas and offers entertainments for sports
amateurs and professionals. The soft climate of Primorsko, a
combination of continental and Mediterranean climate, gives the
opportunity for professional sports trainings during the whole year.
The pearl of the complex is Primorsko Sports Hall. It offers
multi-functional covered football, basketball, volleyball playgrounds,
a SPA centre, a fitness centre, a solarium, four open-air tennis
courts, a restaurant and a coffee-confectionery during the whole year.
The hall is also modified for concerts and other entertainment and
corporative events. The Aqua Park includes water coast, Rolba, Cobra,
Elephant, Octpus, Clowm, Black-Hole, Kamikadze, Family slide, Tsunami,
Centrifugee, Slow River, Bar "Children's World", Ice Cream Bar, Bar
"The Island", Restaurant "Primorsko"
