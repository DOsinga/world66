title: "Budget Travel Ideas in Sofia"
type: section
---

Bulgaria is an exotic destination in Eastern Europe with a rich cultural history and heritage. The capital city of Sofia presents an eclectic mix of natural beauty and historical wealth that deserves to be explored. Sofia budget travel is a good possibility if you are keen to save pennies and yet enjoy your travel through the length and breadth of this wonderful city.

#### **Choose Budget Airlines**

As a first step, you should compare the online rates of different airlines to take you to Sofia on a budget. Some airlines may offer a slightly longer journey with a break in between at some other destination, but it may be worthwhile if it saves you some precious cash.

#### **Travel in Non-Peak Season**

June to September is usually the peak summer season in Sofia when there is a large influx of tourists. The airfares as well as accommodation costs will be high during this period. Winters are an ideal time for Sofia budget travel when you can enjoy less crowded destinations, plenty of scenic beauty and good opportunities for skiing in the snow. The Christmas season should be avoided, because it is also a popular time for tourists to visit Sofia.

#### **Public Buses for Local Travel**

Sophia has an extensive network of public buses to take you to the popular tourist destinations such as Boyana Church, Alexander Neviski Church, Central Sofia Synagogue, the Rotunda of St. George, Soviet Army Monument and the famous Vitosha Mountain. Buses are faster and cheaper than the local train network. Even if you are traveling out of Sofia on a day trip, check out the option of a public bus instead of expensive car rentals. Car rentals can be risky because of the poor road conditions. All the leading bus service operators in Sofia provide English service so you cannot have a language problem while traveling.

#### **Budget Accommodation**

In Sofia you can find a number of hostels and other budget accommodations to make your stay cheap. There are budget accommodation guides on the Internet to give a vast range of options for cheap places to stay in Sofia. Hostels are an excellent option in the whole of Bulgaria for people who need an affordable accommodations.

Adventurous travelers also like to undertake backpacking trips in the city. If you are keen on Sofia budget travel, there are plenty of options for you all through the year.
