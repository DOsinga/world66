---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Sofia's essentials cluster around the central square. Start at the gilded **Alexander Nevsky Cathedral**, drop into the older and much smaller **St. Sofia Basilica** that gave the city its name, then walk south through the **Tsar Osvoboditel Boulevard** past the Russian Church to the **Palace of Culture**. A half-day covers the centre; the **National History Museum** in the old communist-era residence on the outskirts is worth a second half-day for the Thracian gold alone.
