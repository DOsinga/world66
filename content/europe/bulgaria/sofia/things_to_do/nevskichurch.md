The gilded Eastern Orthodox cathedral at the heart of Sofia — completed in 1912 in honour of the 200,000 Russian soldiers who died freeing Bulgaria from five centuries of Ottoman rule in 1877–78. Italian marble, Egyptian alabaster, Brazilian onyx, and a crypt containing the best icon collection in Bulgaria. Free to enter the church; modest fee for the crypt.
