Having a good meal in Vratza is not problem. In the city there are wide varieties of places to dine.

If you prefer **Fast Food**, try the **"CITY Grill House"**, **McBrothers**, and **"4etvorkata"**

**Bulgarian cuisine** is also available in many of the restaurants in the city. Try the "**Vrachanska sreshta", "Nashentsi", "1001 nights", "Gerana"**

**Pizza** restaurants in the city who i recommended are **"Napoli", "Bohemi", "Milevi"**
