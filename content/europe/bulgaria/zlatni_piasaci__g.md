---
title: "Zlatni Piasaci / Golden Sands"
type: location
---

Holiday Resort situated on the Black Sea, 18 kilometres north of Varna (taxi to Varna costs from 12-20 Leva). 

Lots of nightlife, Bonkers, ZigZag, Pr. , Malibu and Roxy are the biggest clubs, the visitors in the clubs are varying from day to day.

Prices are double of Varna prices.
