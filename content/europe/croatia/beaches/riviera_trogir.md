**Beach Medena** is situated in Seget, 4km from Trogir Center. It is 3km long gravel beach surrounded by old pine-woods. Recreation facilities: tennis courts, mini golf, football court, a volleyball court, a basketball court and a beach volleyball court. For children there is variety of offers at the children's playground.
**Beach Milicevo** in Okrug Gornji (Island Ciovo) – Two kilometers long gravel beach is also called Copacabana because of its popularity. There is a water-sport center with complete water sports offer for your active summer day: motor scooters, parachute flight...
There are many beach bars and restaurants on the beach.
Traditionally there are few days in the top of the season when bars organize beach party. Famous D.J. names, cocktails, Sun and sea makes this party unforgettable.
Some of the bars are: Flash Back, Aquarius, Papaya, etc.
There is also taxi boat on the beach which drives to Trogir every 30 min.
Price of the ticket is about 2 €, so there is no need to drink and drive. :-)
