---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Beyond the beach at Zlatni Rat, Brač has several worthwhile sights.

**Blaca Hermitage** — A monastery dramatically built into a cliff face above the sea. The walk in takes about 45 minutes from the nearest parking area and is steep in places, but the setting is extraordinary.

**Vidova Gora** — At 778 metres, the highest point on any Adriatic island. The view down to Zlatni Rat and across to Hvar is one of the best panoramas in Dalmatia. You can drive or hike up.

**Skrip** — The oldest settlement on the island, with a small museum of Brač heritage housed in the Radojković Tower.

**Lovrecina Bay** — A sandy beach (rare in Dalmatia) on the north coast near Postira, with the ruins of a 5th-century basilica behind the shore.
