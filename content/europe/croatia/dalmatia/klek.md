Travel Guide

[**Klek**](/europe/croatia/dalmatia/klek) ,a little **Adriatic** sea town ,situated in splendid cove by the road between **Split** and **[Dubrovnik](/europe/croatia/dubrovnik)**. This history landed area used to belong to the famous republic of **Dubrovnik**. Nowadays, it has some 200 inhabitants who live off fruit, vegetables, olive growing, as well as fishing and tourism.
