title: "Day Trips"
type: section
---

Dubrovnik sits near the southern tip of Croatia, close to the borders with [Montenegro](/europe/montenegro) and [Bosnia and Herzegovina](/europe/bosnia_and_herzego). The Elafiti islands are reachable by regular ferry from Gruz harbour, with Lopud the most popular for its sandy beach and 15th-century architecture. The Peljesac peninsula to the north is wine country, known for its robust Dingac and Postup reds. Mostar in Bosnia, with its rebuilt Ottoman bridge, makes a long but rewarding full-day trip. Closer to home, the Arboretum at Trsteno and the medieval salt town of Ston are both easy half-day outings.
