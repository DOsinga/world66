**War Photo Limited** is an exhibition center of war and conflict photography, just off the main street in Dubrovnik’s old town. This center displays stunning images of modern day conflicts by world re-known photojournalist. The exhibitions change during the season but the subject remains the same. No visitor leaves without a better understanding of the consequences of war.

War Photo Limited is open from the 1st of May till the 30th of October
