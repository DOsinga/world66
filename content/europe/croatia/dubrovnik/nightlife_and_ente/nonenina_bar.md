Nonenina is a bar is the ideal place to rest
after a visit to the rich cultural and historical inheritage of
Dubrovnik. As the daylight smoothens, Nonenina becomes a spot for an
evening visit. A lounge bar, chill out music, professional service, the
view at Dubrovnik’s exceptional architecture, comfortable seats are
only a part of the athmosphere that you will experience in Nonenina.

An offer of over 180 different cocktails, a choice
of wine that can satisfy even the finest palatals, huge selection of
international cognacs, whiskeys, liquors, champagnes and other
alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks. For the tobacco-lovers we offer a
large choice of cigarettes, Cuban Havanas cigars of world-known
producers.

We are located no Dubrovnik’s
main street, it’s easy to find us and with our offer and ambiente we
have, we became a preferred place to many, including famous people who
choose every year Dubrovnik as their destination for vacation. So you
shouldn’t be surprised if next to you seats the soccer-tycoon
Abramovich, the beautiful nightlife loving Tara Reid, the fashion guru
Valentino or the king of the big screen Tom Cruise.

From the terracce of our bar you
can enjoy of a view to the Rectors Palace in which atrium take place
chamber-musical concerts of the world famous pianist Julian Rahlin and
friends. With some planning and luck you might assist the openings of
Dubrovnik’s summer festival games.
