---
title: "Shopping"
type: section
---

The old town shops lean heavily on souvenirs, but there are worthwhile finds if you know where to look. Dubrovnik is known for its olive oil, fruit preserves, handmade lace, and local wines. The daily market at Gunduliceva Poljana sells fresh produce, honey, and handmade goods. For wine, try Vinoteka Dubrovnik near the clock tower. The Ronchi Hat Factory, a family-run shop since 1858, is a local institution.
