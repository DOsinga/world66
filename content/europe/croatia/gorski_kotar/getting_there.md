---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

Gorski Kotar lies on the main motorway between [Zagreb](/europe/croatia/zagreb) and [Rijeka](/europe/croatia/rijeka) — about an hour's drive from either city. The A6 motorway passes through the region, making it an easy stop on the way to or from the coast. The town of Delnice is the main hub, with bus connections to both Zagreb and Rijeka.
