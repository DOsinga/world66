Travel Guide

Komarna is little village located on the Adriatic coast, 70 km north of Dubrovnik. It is located in Mali Ston Bay overlooking Peljesac peninsula and small islands.

There is only one little hotel in Komarna, the rest are privat houses (many of them for renting). In Komarna there is a mini market, a bar, a pizzeria, and two restaurants.

Tourist resort Klek is 3 km far from Komarna, and Neum (the only sea side town in Bosnia and Herzegovina) is 8 km south of Komarna. In Neum there are several supermarkets, fish markets, and restaurants. The closest bigger town is Metković, located 25 km north of Komarna.

Komarna surrounding offers many sightseeing spots. Old city Dubrovnik is only 1h drive from Komarna. Ston (30 min drive), with its 5.5 km long walls, is particulary famous for its oysters. Mostar with its Unesco listed Old Bridge is 65 km from Komarna.
