Friendly atmosphere, great affordable food that isn’t pasta or pizza, free book exchange, and cheap beer. Finally a place in Korcula that caters to budget travelers! Fresh serves a tasty takeaway menu of vegetarian and non-vegetarian wraps & real fruit smoothies for under 5$. Located across from Korcula Town's marina & bus station next to the half-buried pirate ship.
