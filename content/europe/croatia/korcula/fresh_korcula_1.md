Budget-friendly takeaway spot across from Korčula Town's marina and bus station. Serves vegetarian and non-vegetarian wraps, real fruit smoothies, and cheap beer — a welcome alternative to the pasta-and-pizza menus that dominate the old town. There is a free book exchange for travellers.
