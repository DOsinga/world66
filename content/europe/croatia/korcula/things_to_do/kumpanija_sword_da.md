A traditional sword dance performed in the island's western villages of Vela Luka, Blato, Smokvica, Čara and Pupnat. The performance in Vela Luka takes place in front of the St Joseph church. Check with local tourist offices for dates — performances are usually tied to the summer season and to saint's days.
