title: "Krka National Park"
type: location

The Krka National Park is located near Šibnik and is about 10o square kilometers big. It is located along the
Krka River. Many waterfalls here. It's definetly a "must see" when your
travelling the Croatin Coastline. It's
fantastic to swim around in the fresh and cold water of these
Waterfalls. Many Families are travelling there. The Entree Fee is
around 10 Dollars but its worth it! Take a waterproof Camera with you.
