Ferries and catamarans are the main way to reach Croatia's islands. Jadrolinija operates most of the routes along the coast, with major hubs in Split, Rijeka, Dubrovnik, and Zadar. The busiest connection is Split to Supetar on Brac, running four or five times daily. In summer, book car ferries in advance — foot passengers can usually walk on. Catamarans are faster but don't carry vehicles. Timetables shift between summer and winter schedules, so check ahead.
