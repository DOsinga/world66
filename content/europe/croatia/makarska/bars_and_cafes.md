---
title: "Bars and Cafes"
type: section
---

The Riva is lined with cafes and restaurants and is the centre of evening life — the Friday "Fisherman's Night" in summer brings live music, performers, and food stalls to the promenade. At the far end of the Riva, several cocktail bars play electronic music well into the night. Club Deep is set into a natural cave by the sea; Peter Pan (formerly Opera) is an open-air club with capacity for 1,500.
