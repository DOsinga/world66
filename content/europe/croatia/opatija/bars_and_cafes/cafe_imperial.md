The terrace of the Imperial Hotel is the classic Opatija coffee stop — wicker chairs, a view of the Kvarner Gulf, and an atmosphere that connects pleasingly with the town's aristocratic past. Coffee and cake in the morning, Aperol spritz in the late afternoon. The prices are slightly elevated but you are paying for the setting, and the setting delivers.
