---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

The Lungomare promenade ties the town together, but Opatija's real pleasures are in the details: the iron railings of a Habsburg-era villa, a park bench overlooking the Kvarner islands, the statue of a woman carrying a seagull that has stood on its rock since 1956. The town is compact enough to walk in a morning, and the surrounding hills and coastline reward a longer stay.
