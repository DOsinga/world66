Mrljane is a small fishing village on the eastern shore of Pašman Island in the Zadar archipelago. Sandy beaches and clear Adriatic water have made it increasingly popular with nautical tourists, and the village's residents — traditionally skilled navigators — now run much of the local boat traffic. A hill above the village affords a fine view across the Pašman Channel toward the mainland.
