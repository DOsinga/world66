title: Pentagonal Tower
type: poi

At the entrance to the old town, where the Decumanus begins, this 15th-century Gothic tower once guarded the city gates. A Venetian lion relief is carved into the stone. Until the Napoleonic era, the Great City Doors were still preserved here.
