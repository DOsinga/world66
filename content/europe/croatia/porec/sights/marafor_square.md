Remains of the temple of Mars and Neptune can be seen on Marafor
square. This is the place very near the old town core of Porec located
practically on the very end of the peninsula.
The name of the square originates in two words – “Mars” and
“forum” (square). The present Marafor is significantly smaller than the
previous square, and you can still see the remains of the ancient
pavement.
