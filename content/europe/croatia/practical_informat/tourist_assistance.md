Tourist Assistance Card provides safeness when you are in a foreign
country, do not speak Croatian language, you are not conversant with
legal regulations, you do not know where is the nearest pharmacy or
hospital, you need road assistance or you do not avail Croatian
language and letter and you need an interpreter service.

Tourist Assistance Card is intended for Swedish, German and Czech speaking tourists who plan to visit Croatia.
