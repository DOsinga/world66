---
title: "When to Go"
type: section
---

Summer is the peak season, with warm seas and long days from June to September. July and August are busiest — book accommodation well ahead.

The **Rab Summer Fiera** (25–27 July) is a medieval festival held in the old town's Kaldanac quarter, with costumed processions, traditional crafts, and a crossbow tournament. It is the highlight of the island's cultural calendar.
