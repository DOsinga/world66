Common day trips include the towns of Trogir or Omisby bus, or the islands of Brac, Hvar or Solta by ferry.

**all**

**Brac**

the island of [Brac](/europe/croatia/brac) is easily reachable by ferry to Supetar or a catamaran to Bol in the peak season

**Omis**

To get to [Omis](/europe/croatia/omis) you need to take Bus 60. A one way ticket is 19 Kuna and a return is 29.

**Trogir**

To get to [Trogir](/europe/croatia/trogir) you need to take bus number 37. A one way ticket is 19 kn, and a return in 29 kn
