---
title: "Eating Out in Zagreb"
type: section
---

**Nova**

Located on Ilica Street you will find a restaurant called Nova. It
is one of the few restaurants in Zagreb that try to cater to
vegetarians and vegans, but meat eaters will enjoy the fair here too.

**The Regent Esplanade**

Right next to the Zagreb train station (Glavni Kododvor), one of the
stops on the Orient Express, you will find The Regent Esplanade. The
Regent Esplanade is a 5 star hotel rich in history and tradition, but
also home to Zinfandel's, one of the 4 gourmet restaurants located in
within the hotel. Zinfandel's is where California meets the
Mediterranean. The dishes here are just as rich as the Croatian
culture. This restaurants is one of the finest and most expensive in
Zagreb, but if you are looking for the perfect dining experience - look
no farther.

**Boban**

owned by the former AC-Milan football legend Zvonimir Boban, Enjoy a
drink upstairs in the bar area, or head to the basement for superb
pasta, cave-like atmosphere, or to rub shoulders with Zagreb's finest.
The Italian food is good, service is so so, and qualities are large.
Some may find this spot a little on the pretentious side. Fans will
enjoy however.

**Pinguin Sandwich Bar**

If you are looking for a quick bite, or a fantastic sandwich for lunch try Pinguin
Sandwich Bar located on Nikola Tesle 7. The Sandwich bar is extremely
busy, but worth the wait. The menu is entirely in Croatian, but the
staff do speak some English. The sandwiches are made to order, but my
advise is to ask what is good and take a chance. The subs can be messy
with all the condiments, but delicious when accompanied with a beer.

**Pod Grickim Todom**

You will find traditional Croatian fare at Pod Grickim Todom** **on
Zakmardijeve stube 5. The locations is quite nice, and the setting is
intimate. In this traditional style restaurant you have your choice of
Croatian pate, blitva, shrimp risotto, or fresh grilled seafood of the
season. The menu changes from time to time, but the execution remains
on par with the finest restaurants in Zagreb. The food is amazing, but
the price matches.
