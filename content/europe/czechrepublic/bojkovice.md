Bojkovice is a small town in the Zlín Region of eastern Moravia, known primarily for Nový Světlov Castle — a neo-Gothic château perched above the town that now operates as a hotel and cultural center. The surrounding countryside is pleasant and typical of the rolling Moravian hills.
