address: "Rooseveltova, just across the bridge from the town square"
---

A delightful restaurant built into what was once a gunpowder room in the old town's defensive wall. Small and cosy, the Barbakon serves a nice assortment of very reasonably priced dinners cooked in a wood stove only a few feet from your table.
