---
title: "Barbakon"
type: poi
---

A delightful restaurant built into what was once a gunpowder room in the old town's defenisve wall, the Barbakon is a must-see in Cesky Krumlov. Small and cozy, the Barbakon serves a nice assortment of very reasonably priced dinners cooked in a wood stove only a few feet from your table. This was the best meal I had in all of Cesky Krumlov and Prague!

address: Rooseveltova - Just across the brigde from the town square
