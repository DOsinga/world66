Many EuroCity, InterCity and Express trains starting in various European destinations travel to (or through) the Czech Republic. It is a common way to travel in/out/through this country.

They are operated by Czech Railways (CD), German Railways (DB), Austrian Railways (ÖBB), Polish Railways (PKP), Slovakian Railways (ŽSSK) and Hungarian Railways (MÁV).
