### [Colours of Ostrava](/europe/czechrepublic/ostrava/festivals/colours_of_ostrava)

Colours of Ostrava is the biggest world music festival in the Czech Republic.**
**
Colours of Ostrava is an innovative and multi-genre
festival, aimed not only at the young generation. Fans of all music
styles can get their fill of world and dance music, ethnic music, and
a host of well-known names from the Czech music scene, including
alternative groups and artists. The diversity of styles is
well-reflected and expressed through the festival’s name - Colours
of Ostrava.
