**Restaurants**

**RED HOT + BLUES **

Open: Daily. 9.00 - 23.00. Mainly expats who come for the Mexican-style food. They have a courtyard if it's hot. (AMEX, EC, MC, V) 
 

address: Jakubska 12, Praha 1

tel: +42 (0)02 231 4639

**KOJAK'S**

Open: Mon.-Fri. 11.30 - 24.00, Sat., Sun. 15.00 - 24.00. Needless to say, there is a lot of Kojak involved, as well as other American themes. Good selection of reasonably cheap meals and drinks.
 

address: Anny Letenske 16/916, Praha 2

tel: +42 (0)02 254 581

**JAMA**

Open: Mon.-Fri., 11.00 - 01.00, Sat., Sun., 13.00 - 01.00. Lots of noise and music, good beers, shared tables and a wide variety of 
 dishes on the menu. 
 

address: V jame 7, Praha 1

tel: +42 (0)02 9000 0412

**POTOMAC - The Renaissance Prague Hotel **

Open: Mon.- Sat. 18.30 - 22.30. Well-prepared food from an interesting menu. The beef dishes are excellent. (AMEX, DC, JCB, MC, V, Renaissance Plus) 
 

address: V celnici 7, Praha 1

tel: +42 (0)02 2182 2100

**WESTERN CAFE **

Open: Daily. 12.00 - 01.00. As close as you can get to an American deli in Prague. American breakfasts during the week, as well as sandwiches, salads and snacks. Brunch at weekends. 
 

address: Elisky Krasnohorske 5, Praha 1

tel: +42 (0)02 231 7694

**RADOST FX CAFE **

Open: 11.30 - 04.00. Mainly vegetarian. A laid-back sort of place that verges on the scruffy. Good food.
 

address: Belehradska 120, Praha 2

**OSCAR'S**

Open: Daily. 11.00 - 01.00. Revolves around a movies theme. Averagely cooked and presented meals. Lots of ribs. (AMEX, MC, V) 
 

address: Tyn 1, Praha 1

tel: +42 (0)02 2489 5403

**VICTORIA SALOON **

Open: Daily. 11.00-24.00. Very good steaks in a cowboy setting. Good drinks list. (AMEX, V, MC, EC)
 

address: Seifertova 44, Praha 3

tel: +42 (0)02 270 581

**AVALON, Bar + Grill **

Open: Daily 11.00 - 01.00. Nice atmosphere with a really interesting menu and good, if slightly unreliable, food. The utensils are so clean that the beer often comes in hot glasses. 
 

address: Malostranske namesti 12, Praha 1

tel: +42 (0)02 53 02 76, 53 02 63
