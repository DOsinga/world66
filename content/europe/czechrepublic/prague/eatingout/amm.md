‘U Zeleneho Stromu’, the place seems to be famous for the locals. The good traditional Czech specialties, such as peceno vepreno koleno (roasted pork knee), pecena kacana (roasted duck), zebra (pork ribs) are available at low prices. I paid USD 10 for my one kg of spare rips. Please be aware that they don’t accept cards, all meals are paid in cash.
