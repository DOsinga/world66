**Restaurants**

**OTTOMAN (turkish) **

Open: Daily. 12.00-01.00. Good starters menu. Reasonable food. (AMEX, MC, V)  

address: U luzickeho seminare 23, Praha 1

**DIOGONES**

Open: Mon.-Sat. 11.00 - 23.00, Sun. 12.00 - 23.00. (AMEX, V, MC, EC)  

address: Gorazdova 22, Praha 2

tel: +42 (0)02 296 395

**ZLATA ULICKA **

Open: Daily. 10.00 - 24.00. Tiny restaurant but with very good Yugoslav dishes. (AMEX)  

address: Masna 9, Praha 1

tel: +42 (0)02 232 0884

**ANATOLIA**

Open: Daily. 09.00-23.00. Mainly Turkish cuisine featuring meats grilled and skewered in various ways (and sometimes they have virtually nothing at all.)  

address: Ujezd 5/598, Praha 5

tel: +42 (0)02 534 857

**DOLLY BELL **

Open: Daily. 14.00 - 24.00. Weird, fun atmosphere with tables on the ceiling, etc. Interesting menu with well prepared meals. (AMEX, DC, V)  

address: Neklanova 20, Praha 2

tel: +42 (0)02 231 4639

**FAROS**

Open: Daily. 12.00 - 23.00. Can be very pleasant on a warm day. Reasonable food and laid-back atmosphere. (AMEX, EC, MC, V)  

address: Sporkova 5, Praha 1

tel: +42 (0)02 533 482

**THRAKIA **

Open: Daily. 11.00 - 23.00. Fairly simple menu featuring several baked pepper dishes plus other Bulgarian staples.  

address: Rubesova 12, Praha 2

tel: +42 (0)02 2421 7135
