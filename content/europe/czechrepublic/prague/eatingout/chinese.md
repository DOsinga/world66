**Restaurants**

**CINSKE ZATISI **

Open: Daily. 11.30 - 22.30. One of the better Chinese restaurants in Prague. Doesn't look desperately promising from the outside as it is in a sort of housing complex. Brave this and you will enjoy your meal. 
 

address: Batelovska 120-5, Praha 4

tel: +42 (0)02 6121 8088

**THANH LONG **

Open: Daily. 11.30 - 15.00, 17.00 - 23.30. A well established restaurant that is well frequented by Czech dignitaries. Professional service with reasonable, if slightly pricey food. (AMEX, V)
 

address: Ostrovni 23, Praha 1

tel: +42 (0)02 2491 2318

**PEKING**

Open: Daily. 11.30 - 23.00. One of the older Chinese restaurants. Quite an atmospheric cellar with average food. (AMEX, MC, DC, V)
 

address: Legerova 64, Praha 2

tel: +42 (0)02 293 531

**SUNSHINE**

Open: Daily. 11.30 - 15.30, 17.30 - 23.00. This is a large restaurant with a menu to match. The meal is very good and the cost is not exorbitant. 
 

address: Dejvicka 40, Praha 6

tel: +42 (0)02 322 888

**KONGZI**

Open: Daily. 11.00 - 15.00, 18.00 - 23.00. Despite the extraordinarily seedy exterior, the food is actually quite good here. The noodles and duck dishes are best.
 

address: Seifertova 18, Praha 2

**GLOBUS **

Open: Daily. 11.00 - 24.00. Has a long Czech history, and this shows a bit in the Czech-style menu. Fairly cosy, always busy, very cheap and well-prepared food. 
 

address: Dejvicka 27, Praha 6

tel: +42 (0)02 325 177

**DONG DO **

Open: Mon.-Sat. 11.30 - 15.00, 17.00 - 23.00. Extended and interesting menu. Nearly everything is well cooked and presented. 
 

address: Vitezne namesti 4, Praha 6

tel: +42 (0)02 342 323

**MALY BUDDHA **

Open: Tues.-Sun. 14.00 - 22.30. Lovely restaurant with an interesting menu. Especially good on vegetarian dishes. 
 

address: Uvoz 46, Praha 1

tel: +42 (0)02 2051 3894

**FENIX**

Open: Daily. 11.30 - 15.00, 17.30 - 23.00. Has its ups and downs, but fortunately more ups. The spring rolls and noodles are usually excellent. Slightly lacking in atmosphere. 
 

address: Vinohradska 88, Praha 3

tel: +42 (0)02 250 364
