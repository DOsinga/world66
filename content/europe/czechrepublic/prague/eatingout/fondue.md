Open: Daily. 11.30 - 01.00. Don't turn around and walk back out into the street when you see the smoky dive that is their street-level bar. Make your way downstairs. It is roomy and air-conditioned. The service is quick and the meals are great. I love fiddling around with fondues and all their attendant sauces and besides, where else can you get the chance to cook your own crocodile. (AMEX, V, EC)
