**Restaurants**

**CORNUCOPIA**

Open: Mon.-Thurs. 09.30 - 23.00, Fri.-Sun. 10.00 - 20.00

As close as you can get to an American deli in Prague. American breakfasts during the week, as well as sandwiches, salads and snacks. Brunch at weekends. 

address: Jungmannova 10, Praha 1

tel: +42 (0)02 2422 0950

**RADOST FX CAFE **

Open: 11.30 - 04.00

Mainly vegetarian. A laid-back sort of place that verges on the scruffy. Good food.  

address: Belehradska 120, Praha 2

**POTOMAC - An 'All American Restaurant'**

Open: Mon.- Sat. 18.30 - 22.30

Well-prepared food from an interesting menu. The beef dishes are excellent. (AMEX, DC, JCB, MC, V, Renaissance Plus) 

address: V celnici 7, Praha 1

tel: +42 (0)02 2182 2100

**RED HOT + BLUES **

Open: Daily. 9.00 - 23.00

Mainly expats who come for the Mexican-style food. They have a courtyard if it's hot. (AMEX, EC, MC, V) 

address: Jakubska 12, Praha 1

tel: +42 (0)02 231 4639

**BARRACUDA CAFE **

Open: Mon.-Fri. 11.30 - 23.30, Sat., Sun., 17.00 - 23.30

Good value Tex-Mex. 

address: Krymska 2, Praha 10

tel: +42 (0)02 746 881

**AVALON, Bar + Grill**

Open: Daily 11.00 - 01.00

Nice atmosphere with a really interesting menu and good, if slightly unreliable, food. The utensils are so clean that the beer often comes in hot glasses. (AMEX, EC, MC, V) 

address: Malostranske namesti 12, Praha 1

tel: +42 (0)02 53 02 76, 53 02 63

**OSCAR'S **

Open: Daily. 11.00 - 01.00

Revolves around a movies theme. Averagely cooked and presented meals. Lots of ribs. (AMEX, MC, V) 

address: Tyn 1, Praha 1

tel: +42 (0)02 2489 5403

**KOJAK'S**

Open: Mon.-Fri. 11.30 - 24.00, Sat., Sun. 15.00 - 24.00

Needless to say, there is a lot of Kojak involved, as well as other American themes. Good selection of reasonably cheap meals and drinks.  

address: Anny Letenske 16/916, Praha 2

tel: +42 (0)02 254 581

**SPORT BAR **
