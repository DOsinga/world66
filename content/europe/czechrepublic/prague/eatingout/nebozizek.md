Open: 11.00 - 23.00. This place is a nightmare to find unless you take the vertical tram that goes up through the Petrin Hill park from Mala Strana. But persevere because the view of the castle from the terraces is spectacular. The food is a mix of good Czech and International, all set in a sort of decayed 'Art Deco' atmosphere. (AMEX, V, MC)
