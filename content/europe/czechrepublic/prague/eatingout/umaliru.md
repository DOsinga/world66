Open: Daily. 19.00 - 22.00. Find yourself transported to London or Paris, price-wise, anyway. A restaurant to use if you have an unlimited expense account and desperately need to impress a client. Wonderful atmosphere and service. Very good food and wines, although something in me rebels at paying more for a meal for two than the average Czech might earn in a month. (AMEX. V, EC, MC)
