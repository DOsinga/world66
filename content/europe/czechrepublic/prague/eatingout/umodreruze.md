Open: Mon.-Sat. 11.30 - 23.30, Sun., 18.00 - 23.30. This is a beautiful, vaulted cellar that has been nicely converted into its present use. The menu doesn't really appeal to the serious diner as it seems to consist of mainly Ostriches, Rattlesnakes and Alligators. However it is also possible to eat a humble fish if you have the inclination. Nice atmosphere and service.(AMEX, V, EC, MC)
