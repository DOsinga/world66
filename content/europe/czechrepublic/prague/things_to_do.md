If you feel like making a morning or afternoon excursion to get the best strudel in Prague, I have a suggestion.

This strudel bakery was recently featured on the Samantha Brown show, and a lot of interest has been expressed by viewers, and requests made for the location of the bakery.

The small bakery is located in Praha (address- Jeseniova 29, Praha 3). It is a residential community, and the bakery is about a 10 or 15 minute walk from the Flora metro station, but is easy to find.

After getting off the metro, just go north on Jicinska, which will turn into Prokopova. (street names tend to change a lot in Prague)

Continue north on Prokopova, and turn right on Jeseniova. The bakery will be about 3 blocks on your left.

You will get a 12-13 inch strudel for about 3 dollars, and it is worth the trip!
