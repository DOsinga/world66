---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Prague's sights cluster tightly. The **Castle** on the hill, the **Charles Bridge** spanning the Vltava, and the **Old Town Square** make the classic walk; any two-day visit revolves around them. Beyond the obvious, Prague rewards wandering: the Jewish Quarter of Josefov, the Art Nouveau details of Nové Město, the baroque gardens of Vyšehrad, and the sprawling Strahov and Břevnov monasteries on the western hills.
