---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

Aarhus Airport is located about 40 kilometres northeast of the city, with coach connections to the central bus station (about 50 minutes). Billund Airport, served by more international routes, is about 100 kilometres west. Copenhagen is roughly three hours by train via the Great Belt Bridge, with frequent departures. Ferries connect Aarhus to Odden on Zealand (about one hour) for a faster route to Copenhagen by car.
