---
title: "Bars and Cafes in Billund"
type: section
---

Although this part of Denmark may be a small and picturesque village, there are quite a few decent **Billund Bars and Cafes** to choose from.

**Propellen**

A trendy and classy establishment where the cool people go to be seen, Propellen is located at Nordmarksvej and can be contacted on +45 (0) 75 33 81 33.

**Svanen**

A major hotel that also offers a great bars serving a broad international selection of beers, wines, spirits, teas and coffees, Svanen is situated at Nordmarksvej and is contactable on +45 (0) 75 33 28 33.

**Bredal Kro**

If you don't mind going a little further afield to nearby Velje, you can sample a broad selection of local Danish beers and traditional Danish food. The address is: Horsensvej 581, Vejle.

**Jelling Kro**

The architecture of this great example of Billund bars and cafes will blow you away before you can even get inside and order a drink. Jelling Kro, on Gormsgade 16, Jelling, is housed inside a gorgeous old Danish building and it's a perfect place to unwind after a visit to the World Heritage Site and museum in Billund.

**Evergreen**

Located on Munkebjergvej 125, Vejle, this late-night venue is the place to listen to live music which happens almost every night. You can call the venue on +45 (0) 76 42 85 00.

**Vaeksthuset i Torvehallerne**

Easier to get to than to pronounce, this place is also a great live music venue where some of Scandinavia's best bands perform every weekend.

**Crazy Daisy**

If you like your Billund bars and cafes to stay open a little bit later then this great spot offers a wide variety of music.
