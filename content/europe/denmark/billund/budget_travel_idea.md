---
title: "Budget Travel Ideas in Billund"
type: section
---

Billund is situated on the western part of the Danish mainland. Having been surrounded by a beautiful countryside, Billund possess a number of tourist attractions such as Legoland amusement park, a destination of 25 million visitors per year and Center Mobilium, which comprises of three fascinating museums namely Aviation, Motor and Falck museums. It’s quite expensive to visit and stay in Billund but making educated decisions when selecting flights, hotels and accommodations may greatly lessen the expenses and will offer tourists an enjoyable time even on a modest budget.

**Economical Accommodation and Eating**

Some economical accommodation options in Billund can be found in suburbs around Vejle and Fredericia. One of the cheapest places to stay is Munkebjery Bed and Breakfast - situated in an old farm house. Budget hotels in Billund include Zleep Hotel Billund, Hovborg Kro and Hotel Svanen. The cheapest price per night starts from approximately $ 110 after adding taxes and fees. Reasonably priced foods and beverages are also available at these hotels.

Carbinn Esbjerg and Billund Kro along with a few other restaurants in the old town region, offer generous portions of standard local fare at cheap rates.

**Budget Shopping Areas**

Billund is a lively city with a number of shops offering a variety of products - ranging from local items to quality fashion wear, although one has to look out for the cheap shops among them. Visitors can get a 20% discount by buying from the duty-free shops at the airport. Shoppers can also visit Vejle or Kolding areas where a lot of cheap shops can be found along with a newly opened pedestrian shopping street. The finest beers such as Havnegade 37 can be bought in the Vejle area within a reasonable price. Moreover, a large mall in the Kolding neighborhood houses some low-priced designer merchandise.

**Cheap Sightseeing**

Center Mobilium provides an interesting environment with three different museums. Billund’s famous Legoland, with its theme rides, live shows, 3-D films and special effects studios attracts hordes of tourists, especially children. For cutting costs on the entrance tickets, buy a family ticket that costs about € 133 rather than buying individual tickets. The family ticket allows access to 2 adults and 2 children. Billund Kirke, a modern church, is another good place to visit for its unusual construction pattern replete with a theater, a modern café and a well-stocked library. Givskud Zoo is a great idea to spend an enjoyable day discovering several varieties of flowers and a rich collection of wildlife.
