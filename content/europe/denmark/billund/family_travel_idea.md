title: "Family Travel Ideas in Billund"
type: section
---

Billund offers an array of attractions and activities to keep the entire family entertained on their Danish vacation. There are loads of things to do in the kid-friendly city, which include fishing, golfing, amusement-park hopping and of course, taking sightseeing flights. Also, there are lots of other family-oriented pursuits that make Billund a famous vacation destination. The **Billun family travel **guide offers visitors information on the best places to visit when traveling with kids in tow.

**Legoland**

This place was opened to public in 1968 and from then on, it developed into a foremost tourist attraction. One of the remarkable spectacles in Legoland is the lego imitations of popular edifices from all over the world. There are over 50 attractions in Legoland. These include the Wat Pra Keo Palace, Miniland and Amalienborg Palace. There is also a Duploland area within the complex that allows small children to play in a safe environment.

**Lalandia**

This is a family resort which boasts of a lot of activities. There is a Mony Tonky Land that offers a range of activities for keeping the children entertained. These activities consist of creating Lego buildings, getting your face painted and climbing up jungle gyms. Also, there is a unique play room intended for children to test their wits through some brain-challenging games. There is a bowling facility and a small indoor golf course which houses 18 holes, and is equipped with different obstacles and bunkers. If bowling is not your thing, opt for tennis, archery or badminton instead.

**Sightseeing Flights**

Sightseeing flights in Billund provide flights to other attractions in Denmark as well as in Legoland. You can plan a trip across the main entrance of Legoland. Some flights opt to go over Givskud Zoo and Legoland. You can also plan to mix both in your sighseeing flight plan. There are different routes available and it is possible for you to book your own airplane.

All airplanes are colored yellow and every passenger gets the chance to sit near the window in order to enjoy the breath-taking Danish scenery. The maximum number of passengers allowed is 3 adults and a child while the least is 2. Sighteeing flights conform to the same safety measures followed by other airlines in Denmark, so you don’t have to worry about safety.

Billund is truly a remarkable destination for family vacations due to its fantastic mix of state of the art amusement parks, sprawling indoor play areas and well-equipped gaming centers.
