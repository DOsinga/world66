---
title: "Getting Around"
type: section
---

Billund is small enough to walk across in 20 minutes. Legoland, Lalandia, the Lego House, and the airport are all within a short walk or a few minutes by taxi of each other. Car hire is available at the airport and is the best way to explore the wider region (Jelling, Vejle, Givskud Zoo). Taxis are available in town. Billund has no local public transport network worth mentioning.
