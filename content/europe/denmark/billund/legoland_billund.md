The original Legoland park, opened in 1968 in the hometown of the Lego Group. Miniland is the heart of the park — a sprawling scale model of European cities, airports, and landmarks constructed from around 20 million Lego bricks. Beyond Miniland, the park has over 50 rides and attractions across themed areas including Duplo Land (small children), Pirate Land, Knight's Kingdom, and Ninjago World. Average visit time is around six hours. Season runs from late March to late October; pre-booking tickets online is advisable in peak summer.
