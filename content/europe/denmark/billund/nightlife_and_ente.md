---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment in Billund"
type: section
---

Billund is essentially a small town that has attained cult status among the Scandinavian nations for its Legoland. Being a relatively tiny region, most of its bars and nightclubs are located in the confines of chic tourist hotels. For the party animals, the Vejle district is a happening neighborhood that houses several famous nightclubs and the Crazy Daisy complex. The **Billund nightlife** and entertainment guide offers travelers handy information on the party and entertainment scene in this quaint, yet modern, Danish town.

**Crazy Daisy**

This is an iconic nightclub located at Noerregade 65, Vejle and has earned huge popularity ratings from both tourists and locals. The venue plays a wide genre of music that includes contemporary rages and vintage hits along with classic tunes and hits from the 60s and 70s. The ambiance is hip, kitsch and informal and this is 1 of the best places to visit if you are looking to rub shoulders with the crème de la crème of Billund.

**Jelling Kro**

Nestled in a charming antique Danish building at Gormsgade 16 Jelling, Jelling Kro is often frequented by tourists for its old-fashioned setting and casual atmosphere. A lot of travelers come here after a hectic sightseeing day at the museum and the famous World Heritage site in Jelling.

**Vaeksthuset i Torvehallerne**

This is a place mostly populated by the younger crowd that dances to some of the best Scandinavian schagerf music and the live band renditions on weekends. The club is trendy and spacious and also hosts some entertaining gigs on weekdays. The collection of wine and beer is also impressive along with a good mix of local and international appetizers. Vaeksthuset i Torvehallerne is located at Kirketorvet 10-16, Vejle.

**Bredal Karo**

Bredal Karo, tucked away at Horsensvej 581, Vejle, boasts of a superb collection of fine Danish beer and also serves delicious local cuisine at reasonable prices. This bar, with its relaxed setting, is ideal for a quiet evening out with friends and fellow travelers.

**Evergreen Nightclub**

The unimaginatively christened Evergreen Nightclub at Munkebjergvej 125 plays live music on almost all evenings. The genres of music played are diverse and the collection of local and international beverages here is quite extensive. This is a relatively expensive club housed in a swanky hotel in the entertainment district of Vejle.

**Svanen**

Housed inside the Svanen Hotel, this bar is where most tourists wind up after a frenzied sightseeing itinerary. The place is classically furnished and features a ritzy interior complete with wooden tables and Italian furnishings. Though the rates are slightly on the higher side, Svanen has a good mix of fine local and international wine and spirits. The bar is located at Nordmarksvej 8.

Billund, though a small settlement famous for its Legoland, has several bars and nightclubs in upmarket hotels and entertainment districts located in the neighboring regions.
