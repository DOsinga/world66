---
title: "Shopping"
type: section
---

Legoland's shops are the main draw — a wide range of Lego sets, Legoland merchandise, and branded souvenirs. The Lego House also has a well-stocked shop. Outside the parks, Billund town itself has minimal retail. Vejle (30km east) has a proper shopping centre and pedestrian streets for everyday shopping. The airport has duty-free.
