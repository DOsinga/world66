**Billund Sights Intro **

There are numerous **Billund sights** that will keep you and your
family entertained during your trip to this beautiful Danish town.
Outside of the obvious and traffic-drawing attraction of Legoland, there
are some great museums, outdoor activities and historical sights
scattered throughout the city to explore during your trip.

**Outdoor
Sights in Billund**

There are some great outdoor attractions to
explore when in Billund that are a great change from crowded theme parks
and shopping. The Torskind Gravel Pit is a fun stop that features an
array of sculptures created by the Danish sculptor Robert Jacobsen. The
sculptures were designed to interact with the landscape and its
lighting, making this walking the park a great experience.

The
Billund Sculpture Park is also a great attraction that continually
renews itself with new sculptures. Stretching between the Billund Civic
Center and Hotel Propellen, this park makes another great walk and
provokes thought among viewers of all ages.

The Uhre Fuchsia
Garden is also a beautiful stop on your tour of the city. With over 500
varieties on the grounds, this is a great spot for an impromptu family
picnic.
**

Museums and Monuments in Billund**

There are many interesting
museums to check out when in Billund, including Karensminde, where
visitors can experience life in the countryside of the 1930s and 40s,
the Gl Blahaj Mead Brewery, where visitors can view the world's oldest
brewing traditions, and the Billund Museum where there are numerous
local cultural exhibits.

The Viking informational center of
Royal Jelling is a fun stop to learn some Viking trivia in a historic
setting. There is a Viking long ship on Lake Farup as well that provides
some perspective on the size and legacy of the Vikings.

There are
numerous great churches to tour in the area, including one (Vorbasse
Church) that was originally built in the 12th century and countless
others built in the early 1900s that offer a unique perspective into the
culture and history of Billund and the surrounding community. Other
notable churches worth visiting on your trip to Billund include Grene
Church, Filskov Church and Grindsted Church.

While you may have planned your trip to Billund to visit the Legoland
theme park, be sure to check out some of these other local gems while
you are there and get the full experience of this beautiful Danish town.
