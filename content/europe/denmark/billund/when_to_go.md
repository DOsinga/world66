---
title: "When to Go"
type: section
---

Legoland season runs from late March to late October, so visits outside this window make little sense unless you are staying at Lalandia (which is open year-round). Peak summer (mid-July to mid-August) is extremely busy — Danish school holidays and European charter season bring large crowds and long queues in the park. Late May, June, and early September offer the best balance: Legoland is open, temperatures are pleasant (15–22°C), and the crowds are significantly thinner.

Danish winters are cold (often below freezing) and dark; Billund has little to offer outside Legoland season.
