---
title: "Bars and Cafes"
type: section
---

Copenhagen's drinking scene splits neatly between cosy, candle-lit bars and late-night clubs that keep going until dawn. Jazz has deep roots here, with several dedicated venues, while the Vesterbro and Norrebro neighbourhoods are where newer cocktail bars and music venues cluster. Most clubs don't fill up until midnight, so start with a bar and work your way up.
