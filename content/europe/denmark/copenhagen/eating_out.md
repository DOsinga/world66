---
title: "Eating Out"
type: section
---

Copenhagen's food scene ranges from traditional Danish open sandwiches (smorrebrod) to some of the most inventive cooking in Europe. Budget options include the many shawarma shops scattered through the centre and the all-you-can-eat buffets that several restaurants offer. For something more local, look for the lunch spots serving classic smorrebrod with herring, roast pork, or egg and shrimp.
