**Open hours:** 11am to midnight Thursday-Saturday; 11am to 11pm Sunday to Wednesday **

English Speaking:** Yes

** On offer: **

Skildpadden offers open sandwiches and burgers. Each are made to order, so you can let your appetite dictate the choice of fillings. Skildpadden ordering process is an interesting one, but it just maybe a Danish style. It all starts at the counter. You are asked to choose the type of bread, then the basic contents; up to two different meats, with the selection including: salmon, turkey, Spanish sausage, Italian pastrami. There is also the choice of a number of different types of cheeses. Moving onto the next person, you select drinks and pay.

From here you proceed to the stand with vegetables, salads, dressings and other interesting produce. You can then elect to toast your sandwich creation. Now you can eat your creation.

**Decor: **

Whilst being a little on the small size, Skildpadden has a real sense of tradition, which is shown through its décor and atmosphere. Old wooden tables lit by candlelight. Walls decorated with Danish paraphernalia. The atmosphere is friendly and local.

**Reservation:**

Not necessary. However, Skildpadden is quite popular, so you may have to wait, but it is well worth the wait.

**Seating:**

Between basement and upstairs seating, Skilpadden accommodates around 60. Noise level The space is quite small in the basement, so the noise is amplified. This rather adds to the atmosphere than subtracts, as you feel a real sense of goodwill.

**Price:**

Good quality produce, coupled with generous sized portions mean the prices are more than reasonable.

**Payment:** Cash, Check, Dankort, Eurocard, MasterCard, VISA
