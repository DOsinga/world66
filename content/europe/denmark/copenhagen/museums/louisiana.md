Also a visit to this museum is an experience in itself. Louisiana has grown in a circle at the coast of Øresund. Pavilions in different stiles are bound together like a necklace to shelter a unique collection of modern art. Especially the Giacometti is a site in itself. When weather allows you can have a picnic in the central clearing. But even when rain pours down it is a place of interesting harmonics and contrasts.
