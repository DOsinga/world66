Denmark's national museum of cultural history, housed in a 17th century prince's mansion. The collection spans over 10,000 years, from Stone Age finds to medieval treasures, with Egyptian, Greek, and Roman antiquities as well. The Danish prehistory galleries, with their Viking weapons and Bronze Age sun chariots, are the highlight.
