Christiania is a 'free state' inside Copenhagen. Originally a
military base, hippy squaters moved into the area when the militray
abandoned it and formed a community based on cooperative ideals.

Part commune, part social experiment, Christiania is home to an array
of uniquely designed houses and market stalls selling all maner of
good. The open sale of hash on pusher street, however is probably
the communities most famous sight.

Booming land prices are now the biggest threat to the free state.
The government seems convinced to reclaim the site to develop upmarket
apartments and has used the sale of soft drugs as an excuse to
continually send in the police. See it while it lasts
