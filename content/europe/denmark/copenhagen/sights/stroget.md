Commonly referred to as "The Walking Street", this area is full of people, shopping, cafes, restaurants and tons of ice cream! This is the place to be at any hour of the day... The shops close at around 6 PM, so if you are interested in shopping get there early... If your looking for a cheap place to eat-- there are plenty of Shwarma restaurants, etc..
