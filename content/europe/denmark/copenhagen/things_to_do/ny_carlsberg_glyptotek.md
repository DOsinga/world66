One of Copenhagen's finest museums, built around the art collection of brewer Carl Jacobsen. The Glyptotek holds over thirty works by Rodin, the largest collection outside France, alongside sculptures by Degas and an outstanding collection of French Impressionist paintings. The winter garden with its palm trees and fountain is worth the visit alone.

address: Dantes Plads 7
