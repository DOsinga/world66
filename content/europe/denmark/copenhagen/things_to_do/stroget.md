One of Europe's longest pedestrian streets, stretching from City Hall Square to Kongens Nytorv. Created in 1962, it is lined with shops ranging from international brands to small Danish boutiques, plus buskers, ice cream vendors, and pavement cafes. The side streets are often more interesting than the main drag.
