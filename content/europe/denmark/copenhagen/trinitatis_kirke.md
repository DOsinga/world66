Built in 1656, this church is best known for the Round Tower attached to it, which served as an astronomical observatory. The spiral ramp inside, wide enough for a horse and carriage, leads to a rooftop platform with panoramic views over the old city. The church itself was rebuilt after a devastating fire in 1728.
