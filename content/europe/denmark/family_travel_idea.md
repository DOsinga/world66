**Denmark family travel** offers numerous possibilities for a wonderful holiday for the entire family. Here are some interesting ideas when traveling with your family on a holiday to Denmark.

#### **Mobile Holiday **

One of the best ways to discover Denmark is to be constantly on the move. The geography of the region makes it easy to explore the country by private vehicle or public transport. Try biking through the scenic locales of Denmark along the comfortable designated routes for bikers. Consider an exploratory trip of Denmark along its stunning waterways. Canoeing, kayaking and ferrying through the islands can make an experience of a lifetime for the whole family. In Copenhagen, for instance, you can make a trip to Kastrup Sobad by train or car. There are great opportunities here to sunbathe, indulge in a variety of water sports and take a cool dip in the Baltic Sea.

#### **Rural Farm Vacation **

If your idea is to introduce the splendors of country life, a trip to the Danish countryside for a farmhouse vacation may be a wonderful idea. Kids are bound to love the experience of playing with pet farm in tranquil surroundings, away from city life. Milking cows and tilling vegetable gardens are things the entire family can do. An interesting option is to visit the Viking Age farm settlements near the towns of Saedding and Vorbasse. Amid these tranquil, ancient farming communities a wonderful vacation awaits you and your family.
