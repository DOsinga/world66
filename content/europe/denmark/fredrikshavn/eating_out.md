---
title: "Eating Out"
type: section
---

Fish is the staple — the harbour produces fresh cod, plaice, and shrimp. Katfizk serves traditional Danish food including frikadeller (meatballs), open sandwiches at lunch, and hearty fish dinners. Restaurant Frank's at the harbour in nearby Sæby does a popular summer fish buffet with fresh fillets, shrimp, and calamari. Karma Sushi on Lødsgate is a local favourite for quick, fresh sushi.
