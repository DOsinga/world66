---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment in Fredrikshavn"
type: section
---

A walk around the town will lead you to some excellent pubs and bars, all of which serve superb food and drinks. One of the many reasons tourists frequent this area is for the **Fredrikshavn Nightlife** scene. Here are the top places to enjoy a good night out in Fredrikshavn.

**Cafe Staehr**

Cafe Staehr, which can be found in the northern zone of Fredrikshavn, has been decorated in a classic way, straight out of a Charles Dickens novel. The cafe serves delicious food and well made coffee, but most come here to enjoy an alcoholic drink or two. The cafe's wine list is extensive and a meal for two including wine would set you back $70.

**Miscellaneous Pubs**

If a cheap beer is what you are looking for, head straight for the town's many nondescript local pubs. You will be able to find a whole lot of such pubs near the ferry port of Fredrikshavn, close to the main shopping area. Here you can enjoy downing all sorts of beer at extremely low prices.

**John Bull Pub**

John Bull Pub can be found in Hotel Jutlandia in the Havnepladsen area. It can best be described as a warm pub with decor that is much like one of the many cozy pubs in England. The pub offers a wide selection of beers from all over the world. Every Saturday, live bands entertain the crowds. Interestingly, a traditional pub quiz takes place on the last Tuesday every month. John Bull Pub also attracts crowds during the football season, when the pub airs live matches on its big screen.

**Hotel Scandic Stena Line**

Head to Hotel Scandic Stena Line, located in Tordenskjoldsgade. The hotel's bar is known for its well-made cocktails. One can also enjoy draught beer and other alcoholic beverages here. The hotel houses a nightclub which is frequented by a vibrant set of people, including a good number of locals. The nightclub itself has a maritime theme and usually plays good music, turning the dance floor into a lively zone.

**The Jolly Butcher**

The Jolly Butcher is one of the most famous Irish pubs in Fredrikshavn. In fact, tourists from all over the world make it a point to visit this pub at least once during their holiday in Fredrikshavn. The pub is open from 12:00 p.m. to 12:00 a.m. on Monday through Thursday, and from 12:00 p.m. to 2:00 a.m. on Friday and Saturday. However, The Jolly Butcher is only open from 02:00 p.m. to 08:00 p.m. on a Sunday. You can enjoy a game of billiards or karaoke while downing your drink. Plus, the pub houses a smoking room.
