---
title: "Things to Do in Fredrikshavn"
type: section
---

In the tiny Danish town of Fredrikshavn, tourists will find many **Fredrikshavn things to do.** It is a very important port city and fishing town. Interestingly, Fredrikshavn translates as "Fredrik's Harbour." Further, Fredrikshavn is also known as the "Land of Light," as a good many artists come here to try and create the same glow of light as seen over this town. Here is a list of things to do when visiting this Danish town.

**Museums**

Fredrikshavn houses a number of interesting museums. The Bangsbo Museum displays warships and other historical items from the resistance movement, while the Fredrikshavn Kunst Museum has a good collection of nice books and art. If World War II is a topic you would like to learn more about, then head straight for the Bunkermuseum, which happens to be the only German bunker that has remained intact after passing of many years. The Craftsman Museum is superb to have a look at arts and crafts of old times.

**Beaches**

Fredrikshavn plays host to over 30 beautiful beaches. Palm Beach seems to be the most popular of the lot and mainly attracts tourists who come to this palm tree-dotted beach to play sports, swim, eat and lounge around. In the northern area of Skagen lies a lovely beach which is known for the most enticing sunset. If you happen to find a Blue Flag on a particular beach, it simply means that it is safe to swim.

**Cycling**

Explore the charming fishing town of Fredrikshavn on a bicycle. Interestingly, you can make use of the town's free cycles which can be picked up from an area near any tourist office. Fredrikshavn has nine bike trails for those who wish to go the natural way.

**Festivals**

Fredrikshavn organizes a good number of fun festivals every year. The two-day "Days of Tordenskold" Festival sees thousands of attendees who come here to better understand the history of the Great Northern War in Scandinavia. The Light Visions Festival however, is held every two years and runs for around 17 days.

**Fishing**

Fishing is a leisurely activity as well as an occupation and is an integral part of Fredrikshavn. Fishing here can be done in streams and even in the deep sea. One will be able to fish for a grand variety of fishes including sea trout, cod, garfish and eel. However, you must always read the board that lists the rules about fishing in a particular area.
