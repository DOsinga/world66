This **Gentofte Travel Guide** lists all the important things you need to know about this Danish municipality in Copenhagen. There are quite a good number of interesting places to visit, eat at, and stay in, and all of the are great reasons to visit Gentotfte.

**Accommodation**

There are many types of accommodation available in Gentofte. These range from reasonably priced bed and breakfasts and 4-star hotels to the more expensive 5-star hotels. The Gentofte Hotel is a good option in the luxury bracket, while the Pea Blossom and Villa Plana are known for their hospitality. Room rates at bed and breakfasts cost around $60 a night.

**Places of Interest**

There are many good places to visit if you happen to be vacationing in Gentofte. The Dyrehavsbakken is an amusement park that lies in the countryside. The Eksperimentarium is a fun museum to visit to learn more science-related things in a practical manner, while the Open Air Museum is a live display of an authentically built village, complete with a workshop in full swing. Oregaard Museum is another must-visit. The Gentofte Church lies in the central part of the city and it happens to be the second oldest church in Denmark.

**Celebrity Attractions
**

Gestofte played host to many famous people, such as Lars Ulrich of Metallica, and many well-known writers, actors and models. The city also houses a first-of-its-kind luxury cinema that was built in 1938, the seats of which are ultra-comfortable and the sound and screen are top notch.

**Things to do**

Once can never get bored in Gentofte, because it offers a whole range of fun activities, such as boat rides on one of Gentofte&rsquo;s many lakes and small rivers. A visit to the Bernstorff Park offers you a good chance to be surrounded by nature in a park that was once the royal family&rsquo;s summer residence. One can also go swimming in the Kildeskovshallen swimming pool.

**Gastronomy**

Gentofte offers a good choice of places to dine at. Most of these restaurants serve local delights. Le Patron is a good choice for local delicacies. There are pizza parlors like Vangede Pizza Grill and Costa Smerelda Pizzarias. Fast food like Mac Donald&rsquo;s is also available here. Besides restaurants, Gentofte also has many cute cafes and some bars. You will also find a good number of pancake houses in Gentofte.
