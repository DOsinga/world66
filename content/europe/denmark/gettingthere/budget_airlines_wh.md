Use the European Low Cost Airline Guide search engine ( http://www.low-cost-airline.info) to find out which budget airlines fly into Denmark airports (Mainly Copenhagen, but some also serve Aarhus, Altenburg, Baden-Karlsruhe and Esbjerg)

Often you can save many $/£/whatever by using these airlines instead of the main carriers.
