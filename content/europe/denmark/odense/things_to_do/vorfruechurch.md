title: "Vor Frue Kirke"
type: poi

latitude: 55.3961

---

The oldest church in Odense, dating from the end of the 13th century. A wooden church stood on this site as early as the 11th century, later replaced by one in granite. Next to the church lies Odense's oldest secular building, built around 1300, which formerly served as a grammar school and mortuary.
