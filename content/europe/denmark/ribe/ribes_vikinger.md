Ribes Vikinger is the main museum for the Viking era and the Middle Ages in Ribe, covering the town's history from its founding around 700 AD through the medieval period. The exhibits include artefacts from the original Viking market, grave goods, and reconstructed environments. A separate outdoor living history park — the Ribe Viking Centre, a short distance outside town — brings the period to life with craftspeople and re-enactors.
