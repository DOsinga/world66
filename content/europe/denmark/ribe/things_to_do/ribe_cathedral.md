Ribe Cathedral — the Church of Our Lady — is the only five-aisled church in Denmark, with mosaics and fresco paintings by the Cobra artist Carl Henning Pedersen. Climb the Commoner's Tower (52m, 248 steps) for a panoramic view of the flat Jutland marshland and the Wadden Sea extending 30–35km in every direction on a clear day.
