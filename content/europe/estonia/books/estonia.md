Neil Taylor's guide to Estonia, published by Bradt, is one of the most thorough English-language travel guides to the country. Taylor, who first visited Estonia in 1994, covers Tallinn's Old Town in detail as well as lesser-visited destinations like Saaremaa, Hiiumaa, and the towns of southern Estonia. The book includes practical transport information and historical context that helps make sense of Estonia's rapid post-Soviet transformation.

Particularly strong on architecture, local history, and off-the-beaten-path towns. A solid companion for anyone spending more than a weekend in the country.
