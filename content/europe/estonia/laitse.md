Travel Guide

*RACING WITH REAL RALLY CARS: You have an opportunity to race on Estonians biggest 2,3 km long double track with real BMW 318 or Lada 2105 / 06 rally cars.
*TRICKY CAR DRIVING: drive with the car, that goes the opposite way.
*VISITING UNIQUE CARS COLLECTION: LaitseRallyPark owner has a very exclusive and unique car collection. There are about 12 cars and we offer our visitors an opportunity to see the entire collection! In summer, it is also possible to rent the cars for fun-drives or weddings, but only with our own driver.
* CO-DRIVE NEXT TO PROFESSIONAL PILOT: 6-time Estonian Champion Einar Laipaik. It is very exciting to sit next to Einar Laipaik (his racing car is BMW M3) and feel the real speed.
*SAUNA COTTAGES: we have 2 cottages (both fit maximum 15 persons, so we can offer it
maximum up to 30 participants).
