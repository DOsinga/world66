Travel Guide

**Laulasmaa** is a small coastal village in North Estonia. It's situated 20km west from Paldiski and ~40km east from Tallinn.

It got its name from the singing sands on the Laulasmaa beach ("laulev maa" - the land that sings).

Laulasmaa has been a well-known holiday place since the 1930s.

Laulasmaa can be accessed from the nearby **Lohusalu marina**.
**More information **about Laulasmaa can be found on the official tourist
information website of Estonia: http://www.visitestonia.com/en/articles/destinations/the-coastline/lohusalu-laulasmaa
