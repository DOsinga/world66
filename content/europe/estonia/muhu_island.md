Travel Guide

Sleepy fishing villages, working windmills, thatched cottages, plenty
of deer, moose and birds plus a more secluded location help define Muhu
as profoundly Estonian.

MSt. Catherine’s church has pagan tombstones and
is one of the oldest in Estonia.

**More information **about Muhu Island can be found on the official
tourist
information website of Estonia
(http://www.visitestonia.com/en/articles/destinations/the-islands/muhu)
and Muhu Tourism Association Website (http://www.muhu.info/).
