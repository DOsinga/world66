Noarootsi is a parish just north of [Haapsalu](/europe/estonia/haapsalu) on the Estonian west coast. Its Swedish name is Nuckö and up to the early 1940s the population predominantly consisted of a Swedish-speaking minority. Most of these fled to Sweden when the Soviet army occupied Estonia . After independence from Russia the links with Sweden have been resumed and are today rather intensive.
