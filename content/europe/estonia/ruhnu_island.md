Travel Guide

The most southern island and closer to Latvia than Estonia, Ruhnu is
referred to as the "pearl of the Baltic".

If you go there, ask about
the giant chocolate bear statue and check the ancient wooden church.
Both are intriguing.
**
More information **about Ruhnu Island can be found on the official
tourist
information website of Estonia
(http://www.visitestonia.com/en/articles/destinations/the-islands/ruhnu).
