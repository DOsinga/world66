Eiði is a village in the north-west of Eysturoy, set on a narrow isthmus with the sea on both sides. The sea stacks of Risin and Kellingin — a troll and a witch turned to stone, legend says — rise from the water just offshore, and a free public telescope near the road is aimed directly at them.

The mountain road from Eiði south to [Gjogv](/europe/faroeislands/eysturoy/gjogv) is one of the finest in the Faroes. There is also a small local museum in the village.
