Travel Guide

Norðoyggjar is the collective name for the
six north-eastern islands of the Færoes: Bordoy, Fugloy, Kalsoy, Kunoy,
Svinoy and Vidoy.

Svinoy and Fugloy are only reachable by ferry, often running in fierce Atlantic conditions, from Havassund on Vidoy.

The other four will be covered under their own names.
