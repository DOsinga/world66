The best resource for sights, hotels, restaurants, bars, what to do and see

Skopun is the first village on Sandoy, which you will arrive to, if you take the ferry from Gamlarætt, near Tórshavn. A buss is driving to the other villages: Sandur, the main village, and the three villages on the east coast: Húsavík, Skálavík and Dalur. There is another village, a small village called Skarvanes, but the busses doesn't go there. In Skopun there are a few shops and a church. The population of Skopun is around 500.
