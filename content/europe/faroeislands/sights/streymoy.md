[Streymoy](/europe/faroeislands/streymoy) is the largest island and contains the capital [Tórshavn](/europe/faroeislands/torshavn). Its highlights are spread across the island: the medieval site at Kirkjubøur in the south, the bird cliffs at Vestmanna in the west, the remote villages of Saksun and Tjørnuvík in the north. The road to Tjørnuvík offers the best views of the sea stacks Risin og Kellingin, standing just offshore from northern Eysturoy.
