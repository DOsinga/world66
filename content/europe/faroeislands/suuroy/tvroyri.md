The best resource for sights, hotels, restaurants, bars, what to do and see

Tvøroyri is one of the largest towns in Suðuroy. The ferry Smyril arrives to the port of Krambatangi, which is situated on the opposite side of the fjord Trongisvágsfjørður. The municipality of Tvøroyri consists of Tvøroyri, Trongisvágur, Froðba and Øravík. The population of the municipality is around 1780.
