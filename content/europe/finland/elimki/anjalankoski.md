Travel Guide

**Anjalankoski** is a beautiful Finnish Industrial Community. It situates between **Kotka** and **Kouvola**. There are three railway stations in Anjalankoski **Inkeroinen**, **Kaipiainen** and **Myllykoski**. Anjankoski is part of Kouvola City.
