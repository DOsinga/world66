Helsinki makes an easy base for day trips. Porvoo, 50 km east, is a small, pastel-painted wooden town — Finland's second-oldest. Espoo on the western edge of the metro area holds Nuuksio National Park. Hämeenlinna, an hour north by train, has a medieval castle and was Sibelius's birthplace. Turku, two hours west, is the old capital and the gateway to the Archipelago Sea.
