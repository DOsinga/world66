A nice and cosy restaurant with a maritime atmosphere. Konstan Molja hounours old finnish traditions, and offers hearty home-style finnish fare.

### [Kiala Manor/Distillery](/europe/finland/helsinki/eating_out/kiala_manordistil)

The Kiala Manor/Distillery is located about 30 miles outside of Helsinki near Porvoo. The actual restaurant is situated in the former cellars of the distillery. The food is typical Finish type foods. It is a popular lunch stop for tour buses bringing in tourists from cruise ships.
