Helsinki's museum of contemporary art, housed in Steven Holl's swooping 1998 building near the main railway station. The collection focuses on contemporary Finnish and Nordic artists, with a rolling programme of temporary exhibitions. The building itself — a curved steel volume slicing light through the interior — is a key piece of late-twentieth-century Helsinki architecture.
