Finland's 1938 functionalist landmark, built to host the Olympics of 1940 — cancelled by the war, eventually held here in 1952. The stadium reopened in 2020 after a major renovation that kept the elegant 72-metre tower intact. Take the lift up for one of the best views over Helsinki and its offshore islands.
