Helsinki is good place to shop. In center of Helsinki is a shoppingcenter, Forum, witch is mostly full of popular youth's clothing shops like Backstreet, Lindex, H&M, Seppälä, KappAhl, O.N.L.Y, and many, many more. Forum includes few restaurants and then places like Mc Donalds, Arnolds, and some finnish companys like Hesburger, Café Picnic and pizzerias. Also for older people is shops too like Marimekko, Idea, Pierre Cavallo, Tiimari, Sokos (like local Sears) and few shops more where i haven't time to visit. Close Forum is Stockmann (like Macy´s/Saks Fifth Avenue). In Stockmann is allkind of stuff; clothes, shoes, make-up, cosmetics and many more. Stockmann includes also restaurant. Close from Stockmann and Forum is Central Railway Station witch is beatyfull building. I can recommend some restaurants: Café Picnic is really good when wants to eat like sandwitch,coffee etc. and pizzerias in downtown are really good. When is in Helsinki longer i would say it would be cheaper to buy some food from foodshops like S-Market, K-Market, Alepa, Ruokavarasto, Stockmann Delicates,Siwa etc. But I *dont* recommend foodshops witch are running by foreigners, because the food is coming for places like Burundi, India, Ethiopia and Kenya so the quality isn't so good. Food where is the *Swan*-mark on it is 100% finnish, so it's good quality. Also *Itäkeskus* is big (biggest shopping center in Nordic Countries) and good place to shop. In Itäkeskus is many shops. Like Tarjoustalo (cheap) Euron Ihme (in English one euros mirical, and everything pays 1€) Anttila (another 'like Sears' shop) and the youths shops (H&M, Seppälä, Lindex, Vero Moda, O.N.L.Y, KappAhl, In Model etc). And giftshops: Idea,Indiska.... Bookshops: Suomalainen Kirjakauppa, Akateeminen Kirjakauppa etc. Computer shops: Veikon Kone,ON/OFF.... And shops for men: DressMan and some more i didn't pay attention to mens cloth-shops and shops for womens were many,many shops. Helsinki and towns close Helsinki are full of shopping centers; Jumbo (Vantaa) Myyrmanni (Vantaa) Forum (Helsinki) Itäkeskus (HKI) Malmin Nova (HKI) Iso-Omena (Espoo) Lippulaiva (Espoo).
