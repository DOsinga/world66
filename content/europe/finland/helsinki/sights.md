**General** 

The Helsinki Tourist Information Centre hands out excellent books of walks [in English] with good maps. They also issue a multi-lingual guide on what to look out for from the 3T tram, a figure of eight route that passes many points of interest. 

**Sibelius memorial** 

This fabulous sight can easily be combined with a visit to the outdoor museum and/or included in one of the walks. Alternatively it can be reached by no 4 tram or bus 24. The coastal and inland scenery all around is extremely attractive.
