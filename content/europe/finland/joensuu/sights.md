**Butterfly-Botania**

Botania, the **Botanical Garden** of the University of Joensuu, offers year-round experiences for the senses − see, hear and touch. Travel from an arid desert to a melodic rain forest and see a thousand exotic species, carnivorous and tactile plants, lianas and bamboo.
