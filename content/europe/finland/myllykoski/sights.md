type: section
---

Myllykoski has got its name of **Myllykoski Falls** which are in **Kymmene River**. It is one of great rivers of Finland. Myllykoski used hto have a mill (mylly) on riverbank. Now there are **Myllykoski Papermills**. In Myllykoski there is **Anjalankoski FC Stadium**, homestadium of **MYPA FC**. Near stadium and Myllykoski station there is a traditional Finnish **Inn Rauhala**. There is also hicking cross country skiing tracks near rhe stadium.
