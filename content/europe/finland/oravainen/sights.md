Sights in Oravainen

### [Kimo Iron Works](/europe/finland/oravainen/sights/kimo_iron_works)

The iron Works in Kimo was founded by Petter Heijke in the year
1703. Petter Heijke chose the place because in Oravais was found an
enough deep harbour, waterpower and wood for making charcoal. The iron
works in Kimo was in 1792 the most productive of the iron works in
Finland. Towards the end of the nineteenth century the production of
ironobjects, however, became unprofitable, partly because other
forgingmethods and stonecoal took over the market. The forging was shut
down in the year 1891.
