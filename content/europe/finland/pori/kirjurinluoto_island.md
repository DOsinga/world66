A leafy island in the Kokemäenjoki river, Kirjurinluoto is Pori's green lung and the beating heart of the jazz festival every July. Outside festival season it is a peaceful park with walking paths, a pond, a children's playground, and an outdoor summer theatre. The riverside setting makes it a fine place to spend a summer evening.
