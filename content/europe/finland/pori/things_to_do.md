---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Pori's cultural life punches above its weight for a city of its size. The Satakunta region's history fills the museum on Hallituskatu, while the art museum on the waterfront hosts serious international contemporary shows. The Juselius Mausoleum in the Käppärä Cemetery is one of Finland's more moving architectural curiosities, and Kirjurinluoto Island — the heart of the jazz festival — is a lovely park for a summer afternoon even when the music is not playing.
