**The Home of J. L. Runeberg**

The Finnish national poet J.L Runeberg lived in this house togetether with his wife until his death in 1877. 

address: Aleksanterinkatu 3

openinghours: Mon-Fri 10am-4pm, Sun 11am-5pm.

tel: (019) 581 330

**Kiiala Manor**

One of the old historical mansions in Parvoo.

address: Kiialantie 22

openinghours: Tue-Thu 10am-5pm. Fri 10am-6pm.

tel: (019) 580 074

**Studio Museum**

Museum dedicated to the life and work of Albert Edelfeltin. Most of his paintings were painted in this building. 

address: Edelfeltinpolku 3

openinghours: Tue-Sun 10am-2pm.

tel: (019) 577 414

**Historical Museum **

Display of old cultural and historical items from historic Porvoo, housed in the old city hall. 

address: Välikatu 11

tel: (019) 574 7589

**Porvoo Museum of Natural History**

The museum of national history has a permanent exhibition on Finnish animals in their natural surroundings.

address: Kaivokatu 40

openinghours: Mon-Fri 8am-4pm. Sun 11am-4pm.

tel: (019) 523 1700

**Doll and Toy Museum of Porvoo**

Display of a large private collection with toys and dolls from both the 19th and the 20th century.

address: Jokikatu 14

openinghours: Mon-Sat 11am-3:30pm.

tel: (019) 582 941
