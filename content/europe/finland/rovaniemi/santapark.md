An underground theme park built inside a natural cave on the Arctic Circle, billed as the official cave of Santa Claus. The complex includes an elf training school, gingerbread bakery, ice gallery, and various winter-themed experiences. It operates in winter only (typically November through January), with the peak around Christmas. Combined tickets with the Santa Claus Village next door are available.
