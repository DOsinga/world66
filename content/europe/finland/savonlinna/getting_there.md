---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

The distance from [Helsinki](/europe/finland/helsinki) to Savonlinna by road is 335 km and there are daily express coach services. An express train journey from Helsinki to Savonlinna takes less than 5 hours, whereas a flight from Helsinki takes between 40-50 minutes. In summer scheduled boat services operate from [Kuopio](/europe/finland/kuopio), Punkaharju and boaters from farther afield have access to the region via the Saimaa Canal. The Savonlinna region has good public transport and well-maintained roads. You can explore the region also by touring with your own car or bike, or even by boat or on a cruise aboard a genuine steamboat.

Train schedules are available from VR, the Finnish national railway operator.
