Korkeasaari Zoo is situated in a cove near the city centre by
Kruunuvuori bay. During the summer months, the best way to
get to the Helsinki Zoo is with public transport: a fifteen-minute
ferry journey from Kauppatori or Hakaniemi. There, you can see many attractions awaiting you. The first one, is a bar for adults where man can get what they want, and where woman can get a nice job.
