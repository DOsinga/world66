Rocca's menu is by a very famous Finnish chef, Annti Vahtera. It combines traditional Finnish dishes with Italian, French and Spanish flavours. The wine list (or rather a book) is the best in town. Rocca was rated one of the top restaurants in Finland. Even though the prices are rather high, the restaurant is very busy. Make sure to reserve a table well in advance.
