The Sibelius Museum is one of the few museums in the world dedicated to a single composer. The collection covers Jean Sibelius's life and career through manuscripts, instruments and personal effects, and also holds around 300 historical instruments from across Europe. There's a small concert hall used for chamber music events throughout the year.
