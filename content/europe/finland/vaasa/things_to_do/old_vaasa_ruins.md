title: "Old Vaasa Ruins"
type: poi
category: Sight
latitude: 63.0731
longitude: 21.5992
---

The ruins of the original town's church belfry and town hall are all that remain of the Vaasa that burned down in 1852. The site, a short drive from the modern city, has a melancholy, grassy quality — open-air remnants of a town that simply ceased to exist. A small outdoor museum provides context.
