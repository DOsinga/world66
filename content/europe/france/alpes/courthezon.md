Located in the river Rhone valley between [Orange](/europe/france/midi/provence/orange) and [Avignon](/europe/france/midi/provence/avignon), Courthezon is a nice starting point for a wine journey towards Chateauneuf du pape, Gigondas or Vacqueras. The village itself isn't a real cultural highlight if you compare it with Avignon or Carpentras, but it is worth a short stop (just 2 min away from the highway exit), especially for a delicious lunch break at eating out.
