Grenoble is a thriving mélange of modern hustle and bustle on the one hand and historical treasures on the other hand. As many other town in the region, such as [Annecy](/europe/france/alpes/annecy), the city can be divided in the modern part, prepared for and meeting the demands of the visitor when it comes down to accommodation and entertainment facilities, and the historic Old Town.
