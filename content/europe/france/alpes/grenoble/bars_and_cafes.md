---
title: 'Bars and Cafes'
type: section
---

Grenoble's cafe culture centres on the squares and lanes of the old town, where terraces fill up from late afternoon onwards. Place Grenette and Place Saint-Andre are the main gathering spots, with everything from traditional French cafes to more eclectic wine bars. The student population keeps prices reasonable and the atmosphere lively, especially around Rue Chenoise and Rue Brocherie.

Cafe Le Dix Vins on Avenue Felix Viallet is a good bistro-bar with a full wine list and outdoor terrace. Bar Phenomen on Rue Vauban draws a younger crowd with its urban-art decor and cheap shooters. For a quieter afternoon, Librairie Cafe Decitre on Grande Rue combines a bookshop with a riverside cafe.
