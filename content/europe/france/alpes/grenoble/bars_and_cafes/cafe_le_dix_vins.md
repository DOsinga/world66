A French bistro with a full wine list (the name is a pun — "dix vins" sounds like "divin"). The bar section has an outdoor patio, and the cafe side serves lemon tart, chocolate mousse, and lavender French toast. There is also a full restaurant menu with seafood and steak. Most cafe items run under ten euros.
