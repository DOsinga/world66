title: Fontaine du Lion et du Serpent
type: poi
---

This striking fountain symbolises the meeting of Grenoble's two rivers. The white limestone lion represents the powerful, flood-prone Drac; the bronze serpent represents the quieter Isere. It sits in the Jardin de Ville, making it an easy stop on any walk through the old town.
