Grenoble's largest central park, built on the site of the 1925 International Exhibition. The standout landmark is the Perret Tower — a reinforced-concrete observation tower that is especially striking when lit up at night. The park has playgrounds, a roller skating rink, and plenty of space for a picnic with mountain views.
