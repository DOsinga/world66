---
title: "Shopping"
type: section
---

The pedestrianised streets around Place Grenette and Grande Rue are the main shopping area, with a mix of French chains and independent boutiques. For local food products, the Halle Sainte-Claire covered market is hard to beat — cheeses, charcuterie, wines, and fresh produce from the surrounding valleys. The Saturday morning market on Place aux Herbes is also worth a visit.
