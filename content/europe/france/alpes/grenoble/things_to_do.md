---
title: 'Things to Do'
type: section
---

Grenoble rewards both cultural exploration and outdoor adventure, often in the same day.

The Bastille fortress is the essential Grenoble experience. Ride the cable cars up for panoramic views, then explore the tunnels and ramparts of this 19th-century defensive network. The Musee de Grenoble is one of France's finest regional art museums, with works spanning from Egyptian antiquities to modern masters. The Fontaine du Lion et du Serpent, representing the powerful Drac and gentle Isere rivers, is a striking landmark in the old centre.

For food lovers, the Halle Sainte Claire indoor market is a feast of local produce, cheeses, and charcuterie. The Parc Paul Mistral offers green space in the city centre with a roller skating rink and the 1925 Perret Tower, which is particularly striking when lit up at night.
