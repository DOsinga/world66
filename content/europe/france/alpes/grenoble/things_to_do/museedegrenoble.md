One of France's most important art museums outside Paris, with a collection spanning the 13th century to the present day. Works by Matisse, Monet, Rubens, Chagall, Picasso, and Warhol hang alongside Egyptian antiquities and Greek and Roman artifacts. The modern building on Place de Lavalette has generous natural light. Closed Tuesdays.
