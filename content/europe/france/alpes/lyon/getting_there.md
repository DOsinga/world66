---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

Lyon is on the main TGV line and fast trains put **Paris** 2 hours away, **Marseille** 1 hour 40 minutes, **Geneva** 2 hours and **London** about 5 hours (with a change in Lille). **Lyon-Saint-Exupéry Airport** (LYS), 25 km east of the city, handles flights from across Europe and some intercontinental destinations; the Rhônexpress tram connects it to central Lyon in about 30 minutes. Drivers will find Lyon on the A6/A7 autoroutes between Paris and Marseille.
