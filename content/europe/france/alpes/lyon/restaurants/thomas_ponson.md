A young and talented kitchen at Thomas Ponson. Native of Cornas, in Ardeche, Thomas is a true craftsman of the taste, formed in excellent houses, like the Inn of Isle or Chanteclerc (Restaurant of the Negresco hotel in Nice). In 2002, Thomas opens his restaurant in the district of Ainay. Increasingly many and faithful customers related on the quality of the products, the choice of the suppliers, the impeccable service of Maxime, the simple and harmonious kitchen with the triads of Thomas. extract of the current chart (10-2006) : “Egg bio calf, breaded and fried, vegetables of autumn out of leek vinaigrette and crusty, or Large ravioli with fresh boletus and dry ham, Parmesan chips and rocket, then Steaklet of quill roasted, turnips frozen and white onion stuffed with one compotée of thighs, or Red mullet stuffed with fennel, carrots and mushrooms, mashed potaties with the tapenade and butters white acidulous. Daring, Thomas proposes one Tuesday out of two, a dinner with topic on Italy, the Niçois country or of season, Gibier, Truffes, etc… A **confidential address** to give to her friends, Thomas is essential naturally like a future **big chief** !
