---
title: "Sights in St. Etienne"
type: section
---

St Etienne is due West of Lyon, on the wester side of the Rhone Valley. It is the home of the ASSE ( les verts), one of France's great Soccer team that had it's hey days in the mid 70's.

The inhabitants are called the Stefanois(es). It is a thriving and bustling city with strong economy particularly in the industrial sector. It has a very good University and pleasant surroundings and quiet suburban type of environment.

Worth the short detour if you are visiting [Lyon](/europe/france/alpes/lyon) or doing the wine route and are on your way to [Macon](/northamerica/unitedstates/georgia/macon) a great wine tasting town located north east of St Etienne.
