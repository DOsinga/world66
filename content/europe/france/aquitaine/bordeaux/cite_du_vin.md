Bordeaux's striking modern wine museum on the riverfront, opened in 2016. The permanent exhibition traces the history and culture of wine worldwide through interactive displays. The visit ends with a tasting on the panoramic 8th-floor belvedere with views over the city and the Garonne. Allow two to three hours.
