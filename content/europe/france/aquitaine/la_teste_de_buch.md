La Teste-de-Buch is a commune on the southern shore of the Bassin d'Arcachon, about 10 km from Arcachon town. It sits at the foot of the Dune du Pilat — the tallest sand dune in Europe at over 100 metres — which draws visitors who climb it for sweeping views across the bay and the Atlantic pine forest. The town itself is a quiet residential base with good transport connections to Bordeaux by train.
