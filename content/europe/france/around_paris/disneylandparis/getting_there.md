Disneyland Paris is well connected by road and rail. The **RER A** line runs directly from central Paris to Marne-la-Vallée/Chessy station, right at the park entrance — the journey takes about 35 minutes from Châtelet-Les Halles.

By car from Paris, take the **A4 (Autoroute de l'Est)** and exit at junction 14 ("Val d'Europe, Parc Disneyland"). From **Roissy-Charles de Gaulle Airport**, follow the A104 to the A4 toward Metz/Nancy — exit 14 is about 40 km from the airport. From **Orly Airport**, head for Créteil on the A86, then join the A4 toward Metz/Nancy — exit 14 is about 30 km.

**Eurostar** trains from London run directly to Marne-la-Vallée during peak season.
