---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

The easiest way from Paris is the RER A train, which runs directly to Marne-la-Vallée/Chessy station right at the park entrance. Journey time from central Paris is about 35 minutes.

By car from Paris, take the A4 motorway east and exit at junction 14 (Val d'Europe, Parc Disneyland). From Charles de Gaulle airport, follow signs for Marne-la-Vallée on the A104, then join the A4. From Orly, head toward Créteil on the A86 and join the A4.

TGV trains call at Marne-la-Vallée/Chessy directly, connecting the park to several French cities and to London via Eurostar.
