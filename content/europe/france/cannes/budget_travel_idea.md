---
title: Budget Travel Ideas
type: section
---

Cannes has a reputation for being expensive, but there are ways to visit without breaking the bank. The public beaches are free (only the private hotel beaches charge entrance fees), and the old town of Le Suquet offers cheaper dining than the Croisette. The Marché Forville market is a good spot for picnic supplies, and the Lérins Islands ferry is an affordable day out.

For airport transfers and getting around, try the Bus Azur network — much cheaper than taxis.
