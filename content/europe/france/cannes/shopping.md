---
title: Shopping
type: section
---

Shopping in Cannes is all about big name designers — and you will find every high-end designer you can think of here from Gucci (17 Boulevard Croisette) to Sonia Rykiel (52 Boulevard Croisette). Boulevard Croisette, also known as the Promenade de la Croisette, is home to the vast majority of designer boutiques as well as many upscale restaurants, cafes, and nightclubs. If Dior (38 Promenade de la Croisette) is out of your price range, you can also find several department stores, big flea markets, and a less expensive shopping street — rue d'Antibes. Books, antiques, and chocolate stores are fun places to shop here as well.
