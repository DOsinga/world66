Shopping in Cannes is all about big name designers -- and you will definitely find every high-end designer you can think of here from Gucci (17 Boulevard Croisette) to Sonia Rykiel (52 Boulevard Croisette). If you haven't guessed by now, Boulevard Croisette, also P*romenade de la Croisette,* is home to the vast majority of designer boutiques as well as a many upscale restaurants, cafes, and nightclubs. If Dior (38 Promenade de la Croisette) is out of your price range, you can also find several department stores here, several big flea markets, and a less expensive shopping street -- rue d'Antibes. Books, antiques, and chocolate stores are fun places to shop here as well. Continue reading through the listings to find out where to shop on any given day of the week in Cannes.
