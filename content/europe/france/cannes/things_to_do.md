Cannes enjoys a Mediterranean climate year round, so there are plenty of indoor and outdoor activities every day of the year.

### Port Pierre Canto

Interested in sailing or taking a cruise around the harbour to enjoy views across the Bay of Cannes and the Estérel Mountains? Contact Port Pierre Canto for information about boating and cruising opportunities. Boulevard de la Croisette. Tel: +33 04 92 18 84 84.

### ASLM Tennis Municipal de la Bastide

Cannes has several municipal tennis courts: ASLM Tennis Municipal de la Bastide at 220 av. Francis Tonner, and ASLM Tennis Municipal Aérodrome at Aérodrome de Cannes Mandelieu. Courts are either synthetic or clay-topped. Hourly rates depend on court type and time of day. Tel: +33 04 93 47 29 33.

### Country Club de Cannes-Mougins

This internationally recognised golf course has been around since 1926. The 6,312m, par-72 course features English architecture, two forests, olive trees and cypresses, rivers, and several water hazards. On site are a bar, cafeteria, tearoom, and restaurant. Proof of handicap and reservations required. 175 Avenue du Golf. Tel: +33 04 93 75 79 13.

### Cycles Daniel

Rent a mountain bike and ride the easy paths between Cannes and nearby resorts such as the Château de la Napoule in Mandelieu, only 10 minutes away by car. Day rental costs around €20. 2 rue de Pont Romain. Tel: +33 04 93 99 90 30.

### La Suquet

La Suquet is the Old Town of Cannes, built on the site of a Roman military camp and the highest point in the city. Here you will find the 14th-century Tour de Suquet ("Lord's Tower") and the 12th-century Chapel of St. Anne. La Suquet offers spectacular views of La Croisette and is the perfect place for a stroll on a sunny day.
