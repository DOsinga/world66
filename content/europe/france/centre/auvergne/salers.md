Salers, situated north and east of Aurillac, is an ancient town at the top of a hill. Visitors park below and walk up — certainly no hardship for the majority.

The town dates from the 11th century but most of what you see is 15th or 16th century. Many towns make exaggerated claims about their beauty, but this surely has to be one of the best — even though there are elements of tourist trap about it.
