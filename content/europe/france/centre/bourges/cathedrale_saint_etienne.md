A UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of the great Gothic cathedrals of France. The west facade is unique — five portals instead of the usual three — and the 13th-century stained glass windows are among the finest in the country, rivaling those of [Chartres](/europe/france/centre/chartres). Open daily, roughly 8am to 6pm.
