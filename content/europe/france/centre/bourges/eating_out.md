---
title: "Eating Out"
type: section
---

Berry cuisine is hearty and unfashionable — galettes de pommes de terre (potato pancakes), lentils from nearby Berry, and goat cheeses like Chavignol (Crottin de Chavignol comes from just up the road). The Place Gordaine, a medieval square in the old town, has several restaurant terraces and is a good spot for lunch.
