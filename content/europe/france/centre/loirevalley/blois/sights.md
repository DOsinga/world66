**Maison de la Magie**

Dedicated to Houdin - Houdini was named after him - a famous French magician, diplomat and inventor. Even if you don't go in it is worthwhile to wait outside until the dragon comes out of the window.

address: accross from the Castle

**Castle**

The first castle in Blois was built in the 10th century but the oldest remains date from the 13-th century: the Salle des Etats was constructed by the Chatillon family.
