Situated some 15 km south of [Blois](/europe/france/centre/loirevalley/blois), at the edge of the park of the [Chambord](/europe/france/centre/loirevalley/chambord) domain, Bracieux is an ideal base for seeing both the best Loire chateaux and for discovering the secrets of the Sologne.

 The old market hall is probably the closest thing to a sight in this charming town. But with tow rivers flowing through the centre and many small lakes around the reguion is ideal for walking and trekking.
