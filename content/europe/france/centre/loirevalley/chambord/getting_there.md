---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

Chambord is easy to reach from [Blois](/europe/france/centre/loirevalley/blois). From may to september there are buses and even special trains for the castle. Both buses and trains have irregular schedules.

 It's best to avoid taking a special tour bus - if you are fit enough you would be much better of renting a bike for a day - the ride is pretty and you have the chance to see some of the forested **domaine de Chambord**.
