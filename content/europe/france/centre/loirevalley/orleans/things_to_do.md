---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Orléans rewards a day of walking. The cathedral and Joan of Arc sites form the core of any visit, but the old quarter and the Loire riverfront are worth time on their own terms.

**Parc Floral de la Source** is a large park on the southern edge of the city, built around the spring that marks the source of the Loiret, a short tributary of the Loire. It is particularly worth visiting in spring and early summer when the flower collections are at their best.
