The Cadre Noir is France's national equestrian academy, founded in 1814 and still one of the most prestigious riding institutions in the world. It is housed in an impressive complex on the edge of town and stages public performances — the Carrousel de Saumur each July is the most spectacular, a formal riding display in full dress uniform that draws crowds from across France. The academy is open for guided visits outside of performance periods.
