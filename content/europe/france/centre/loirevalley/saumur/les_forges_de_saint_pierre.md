A dependable choice on Place Saint-Pierre, one of the nicer squares in Saumur's old town. The menu leans traditional French — duck, steak, fish — at prices that feel fair for the setting. The terrace is the main draw: on a warm evening it is a fine spot to watch the square while working through a carafe of local Saumur-Champigny. Service is steady rather than polished, and portions are generous. Not a destination restaurant, but a solid everyday option if you are staying in town.
