---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Saumur's main sights are compact and walkable. The château commands the skyline, the Cadre Noir riding academy is one of the great equestrian institutions in the world, and the tuffeau cave cellars scattered around the hillsides make for an unusual afternoon. Wine tastings are easy to arrange at nearby Saumur-Champigny and Saumur Mousseux producers.
