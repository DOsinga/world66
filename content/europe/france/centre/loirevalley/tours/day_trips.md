Tours is a good base for seeing some of the chateaux in the neighborhood.

**all**

**Villandry**

[Villandry](/europe/france/centre/loirevalley/villandry) is 13 km west of Tours. Extraordinary renaissance gardens and a chateau of course.

**Chenonceau**

[Chenonceau](/europe/france/centre/loirevalley/chenonceau) is the most elegant of all Loire valley chateaux. Only 15 km south of Tours. 2 buses daily.

**Loches**

[Loches](/europe/france/centre/loirevalley/loches) is one hour by train from Tours. The castle here is a real fortress with ramparts.
