**Cathedral**

The Cathedral of Tours is well worth a visit. It is located in the eastern part of the centre at the end of the Rue Colbert.

address: Place Francois Sicard

**Place St.Pierre le Puellier**

Many halftimbered houses in the old town. The place st.Pierre is the centre of the old town and the most beautiful part - but there are many great things in the small streets around it as well.

address: Place St.Pierre le Puellier
