---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Tours rewards slow walking. The medieval quarter around Place Plumereau and Place Saint-Pierre-le-Puellier is the most atmospheric part of the city, with half-timbered houses lining narrow lanes that beg to be explored without a plan. The cathedral anchors the eastern end of the old centre, and several good museums fill the gaps between sightseeing and lunch.
