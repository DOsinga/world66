Travel Guide

ideal city of 1630's of Richelieu for Cardinal Richelieu. A classical, symmetrically designed village with its majestic mansions behind great oak gates is must-see in the Loire tour.

The Chateau 1000 acres gardens is now the elegant public park of tree-lined avenues, canals to row in and rose gardens. One of France's guarded secret places.
