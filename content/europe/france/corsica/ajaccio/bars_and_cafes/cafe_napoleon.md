title: "Café Napoléon"
type: poi
address: "Place Foch, Ajaccio"
latitude: 41.9200
longitude: 8.7368
---

The most central café on Place Foch, with a terrace facing the statue. It trades on the Napoleon connection in its name but is otherwise a straightforward French café: good coffee, cold beer, and a view of the square. Useful for a morning croissant before the museums open.
