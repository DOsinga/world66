Although the Strasbourg airport is great, it is very expensive. For cheap flights on discount airlines, try Baden Karlsruhe Airpark. It is located in Baden Baden, Germany. A short train ride will get you to the airport for cheap. Ryanair also offers a shuttle bus between Strasbourg and the Baden Airpark.
