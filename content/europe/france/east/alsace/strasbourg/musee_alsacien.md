type: poi

A museum of Alsatian folk life spread across three connected 17th-century townhouses on the quay. The collection covers domestic life from the 18th and 19th centuries: period furniture, costumes, pottery, toys, religious objects, and painted interiors. One of the better regional museums in France — specific enough to be genuinely informative rather than generic.

address: 23–25 Quai Saint-Nicolas
