---
title: "Shopping"
type: section
---

Strasbourg's main shopping streets fan out from Place Kléber: Rue des Grandes Arcades is the pedestrianised high street, while Rue de la Mesange and Rue du 22 Novembre carry more mid-range chains. For local Alsatian products — ceramics, gingerbread, Munster cheese, local wines — the covered market at Place du Marché-Gayot and the Christmas market stalls around the cathedral are the best options.
