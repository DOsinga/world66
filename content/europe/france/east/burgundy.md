Burgundy is not just a region in France. It stands for a lifestyle: the good life! Drink a glass of wine, eat some delicious food and take your time to sit back and relax. If this kind of life is appealing to you, there is no better place to experience it than in Burgundy itself.

 [Dijon](/europe/france/east/burgundy/dijon) is the capital of Burgundy and the best place to start exploring the region. In the city you will find many monuments that date back to the period when Burgundy was one of the more powerful states in Central Europe.

 [Beaune](/europe/france/east/burgundy/beaune) is a bit further to the south and is mostly famous for its hospice. One of the oldest hospitals in Europe and built in the characteristic style of the region.
