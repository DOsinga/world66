Mailly-le-Chateau is a village at the top of a cliff above the river Yonne. It just had to be the site for a chateau. Behind the buildings there is a very pleasant open area with seats and a fabulous view over the river. The views are equally good in reverse and the secod photo on http://www.highonadventure.com/Hoa02dec/Burgundy/gallery.htm gives a good impression.
