---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

The WWI battlefield sites north of Verdun are the main draw. The **Ossuaire de Douaumont** (Douaumont Ossuary) contains the remains of over 130,000 unidentified soldiers from both sides. Nearby, **Fort de Douaumont**, the largest of the ring of forts defending Verdun, can be explored — its dark corridors and casemates give a visceral sense of the conditions soldiers endured. The surrounding landscape of cratered hillsides and destroyed villages, now reclaimed by forest, is haunting.
