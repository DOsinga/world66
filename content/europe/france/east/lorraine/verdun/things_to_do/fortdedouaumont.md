The largest and highest of the ring of 19 forts built to defend Verdun, Fort de Douaumont was captured by the Germans early in the 1916 battle and became a symbol of the struggle. The fort can be explored — its dark, damp corridors, gun turrets, and casemates convey the claustrophobic conditions of trench warfare better than any museum.
