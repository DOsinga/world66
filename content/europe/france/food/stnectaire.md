This is a semi-soft, disc-shaped cheese from Auvergne, made from milk of Salers cows that feed on the very rich and perfumed volcanic pasture lands of the uplands of the Auvergne region of France. The Saint Nectaire is a fermier (farm) cheese easily recognized by its green label. The aging process (affinage) is carried out in old vine cellars. At maturity it cured on a bed of straw for eight weeks and seems to absorb some of its earthy, pastoral aroma.
