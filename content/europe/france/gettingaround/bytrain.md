France has the fastest trains in Europe. The TGV (Train a Grande Vitesse i.e. Very Fast Train) take syou from [Paris](/europe/france/paris) to most major cities at an average speed of some 200 km an hour. [Nantes](/europe/france/normandybrittany/nantes), [Lyon](/europe/france/alpes/lyon), [Marseille](/europe/france/midi/cotedazur/marseille), [Lille](/europe/france/nord/lille), [Brussels](/europe/belgium/brussels) and [London](/europe/unitedkingdom/england/london) are all very close to Paris by TGV. There is no point in taking the plane if you can fly by train.
