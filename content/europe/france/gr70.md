The GR 70 traces the route Robert Louis Stevenson walked with his donkey Modestine in 1878, later described in *Travels with a Donkey in the Cévennes*. The trail runs about 270 km from Le Puy-en-Velay south through the Gévaudan and the granite highlands of Mont Lozère into the chestnut forests of the Cévennes, finishing near Alès.

Most walkers take ten to twelve days. The landscape shifts dramatically — volcanic plateaux, deep river gorges, Protestant hill villages, and vast empty uplands where you may not see another person all day. Donkey hire is still available for those who want the full Stevenson experience.
