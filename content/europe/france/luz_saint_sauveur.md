Capital of the 3 vallées of "Pays Toy".. Luz Saint Sauveur is a cute little town (1 000 perm res) high in the French Pyrénées. Situated between Lourdes and Gavarnie, on one of the main roads of Tour de France.

In winter you will appreciate the easy access to three of the best downhill ski stations in the Pyrenees, and in summer, it is just magic.
