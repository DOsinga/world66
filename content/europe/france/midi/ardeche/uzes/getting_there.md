---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

Uzes is easily accessible, by car, train, or bicycle.  By air:  Fly to Montpellier, rent a car and drive from there.  By train:  Take the train to Avignon, rent a car and drive from there.  By car:  Follow the autoroute to Avignon, then take the N7 west to Uzes (about 20 km)  By bicycle:  The terrain around Uzes is moderately hilly, but with many flat stretches too, and lots of country roads with little traffic. Cycling along the N7 is possible but it's busy, with lots of trucks and buses.
