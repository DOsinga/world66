### [Newquay](/europe/france/midi/basqueregion/biarritz/nightlife_and_ente/newquay)

Newquay is an Irish pub situated in the absloute centre of Biarritz, close to the famous Galleries de la Fayette. Newquay is a nice and genuine place with very nice staff and a great selection of food, drinks, beer, wine, coffee, juice, milkshakes ect.

Newquay is often crowded in the summer, and not a place for those who wants a quiet drink in the evening, but more a place to go to meet your friends and listen to great Live music.

Newquay , is not an Irish pub , it is an ordinary , run of the mill pub that you would find in any resort , it is styled towards [more..](/europe/france/midi/basqueregion/biarritz/nightlife_and_ente/newquay)
