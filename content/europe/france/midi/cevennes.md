The Cevennes are a beautiful region in the south of France. The Parc National des Cevennes offers endless oppportunities for outdoor activities.

 If you want to go hiking, Grande Randonee no 70 is reight here for you. You can take 1 day, 3 day of 7 day walks and follow the footsteps of Stevenson (and his donkey).

 The starting point in in [Le Puy en Velay](/europe/france/midi/cevennes/lepuyenvelay) and the road ends in [Ales](/europe/france/midi/cevennes/ales).
