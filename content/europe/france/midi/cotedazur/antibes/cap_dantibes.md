The headland between Antibes and Juan-les-Pins is one of the better walking spots on the Côte d'Azur. The Sentier du Littoral runs along the rocky western shore for about five kilometers, with views back across the Baie des Anges to Nice and the Alps behind it. The path is mostly flat and accessible; stretches close in rough weather. The interior of the cap is private villa territory and largely inaccessible, but the coastal path is public and well-maintained.
