Argelès-sur-Mer is a beach town at the southern end of the Languedoc coast, just north of the Spanish border near Perpignan. It has one of the largest sandy beaches in the region and fills up with campsite-holidaymakers in summer. The old village inland is quieter and more characterful than the beachfront strip.
