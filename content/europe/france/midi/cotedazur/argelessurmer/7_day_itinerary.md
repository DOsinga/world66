type: section

Avoid spoiling your grand vacation in the southern village of France by preparing your **Argeles Sur Mer Itinerary**. A trip to Argeles Sur Mer is sure one getaway that gives you a lot of options due to its diverse features. So, get ready as we dive and climb in to a 7-day Itinerary suggestion made just for you.

**Day 1 – Go Swimming and Sunbathing**

Enjoy your first day in Argeles Sur Mer swimming and bathing under the sun. The place offers clear waters and a wide stretch of fine sand. Scuba diving and other water-sports are among the activity options.

**Day 2 – Go for Go-Karts**

Establishments in Argeles Sur Mer also offer kart riding services to kids and adults alike. Different kart sizes are offered to different ages. A track is also made available to complete the experience.

**Day 3 – Pay a Visit to any Ranch**

If you are craving for variation in your travel, you must not miss out on horse riding. Ranches are everywhere and almost all offer horse and pony riding services at reasonable rates. Bring your boots and give this one a try.

**Day 4 – Participate in an Excursion**

Many organizations offer excursions in Argeles Sur Mer. If you want to get a clearer view of the Alberes mountains or you simply want to go hiking, you can always participate in an excursion. Excursions are best done for the whole day to maximize the fun and learning.

**Day 5 - Party the Night Away**

After you have enjoyed the beaches during the day, you should experience a taste of what nightlife is in Argeles Sur Mer. Le Playa and Le Potchic are just some of the clubs and bars you may go to. Bring your best party outfit and dance to the music all night long.

**Day 6 – Explore their Art**

Never overlook on your itinerary the planned visit to museum Casa de les Albères. Not only is Argeles Sur Mer rich in natural resources, the village also has something to offer when it comes to art and history. See their ancient resources and architecture by visiting this museum.

**Day 7 – Buy your Mementos**

Make the most out of your last day by buying things that will remind you of your vacation in Argeles Sur Mer. The Craft Market is one that offers different items created by artisans. Although the store is only open from June to September other markets are open the whole year.
