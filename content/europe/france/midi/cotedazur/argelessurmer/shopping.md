---
title: "Shopping"
type: section
---

Argeles-sur-Mer has good markets and a scattering of artisan shops. The best finds tend to be at the open-air markets, which run on different schedules through the week and season.

### Argeles Village Market

Open year-round on Wednesdays and Saturdays, 07:00 to 13:00, in the centre of town. About 60 local vendors sell produce, cheese, seafood, and other goods -- everything locally made, grown, or caught.

### Argeles Plage Shops

Over 100 shops line the beachfront strip. From June to September a traditional market runs on Monday, Wednesday, and Friday mornings (07:00 to 13:00).

### Evening Craft Market

From mid-June to mid-September, artisans set up along Argeles Plage every evening from 17:00 to midnight. Around 40 artists display handmade goods -- a good place to find something to take home.

### Port Docks Market

Along the waterfront, about 50 stands sell paintings, jewellery, and earthenware. Open June through August on Tuesdays and Fridays, 17:00 to midnight.

### Palau del Vidre

A glassblowing workshop where you can watch demonstrations and buy hand-blown pieces. Open daily 09:00 to 12:00 and 14:00 to 19:00.
