Cagnes-sur-Mer sits on a hillside between Nice and Antibes, split between a medieval hilltop village (Haut-de-Cagnes) and a modern beach strip below. The Château-Musée Grimaldi dominates the old town, and the area has long attracted artists — Renoir spent his final years here and his house is now a museum.
