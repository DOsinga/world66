Cagnes-sur-Mer sits between Nice and Antibes, split between a medieval hilltop village (Haut-de-Cagnes) and a beach resort below. The Château Grimaldi houses a museum of modern art, and Renoir spent his final years here — his house and garden are preserved as a museum on the edge of the old town. The lower town is unremarkable, but Haut-de-Cagnes rewards an afternoon's wandering.
