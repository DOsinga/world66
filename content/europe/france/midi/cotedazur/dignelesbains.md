Digne-les-Bains is the quiet capital of the Alpes-de-Haute-Provence, tucked into a valley well north of the coast. It's known for its thermal baths, a lavender festival in August, and as the terminus of the narrow-gauge Train des Pignes that winds down to Nice. A good base for walking in the pre-Alps with none of the coastal crowds.
