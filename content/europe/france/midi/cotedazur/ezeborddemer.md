Eze-bord-de-mer is the lower, coastal part of the perched village of Eze — a small stretch of shore along the Grande Corniche between Nice and Monaco. It's quieter than its hilltop counterpart, with a pebble beach and direct train access on the scenic Côte d'Azur line.
