Gréolières is a small hilltop village in the Alpes-Maritimes, sitting at around 800 metres and just 40 minutes from Nice airport. Its position between the coast and the mountains gives it a completely different feel from the Riviera below — stone streets, perched houses, and big views across the pre-Alps. In winter, the nearby ski station Gréolières-les-Neiges is one of the most accessible in the region.
