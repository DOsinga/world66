Traverse St-Paul, Le Merlan (13e) – Tel : 04 91 02 29 55 – Buses 32, 33
 
 The museum is housed in an old flour-mill, a fine example of industrial architecture at the end of the 19th century, and shows how engineering methods and attitudes to engineering have changed from 1898 to the present.
