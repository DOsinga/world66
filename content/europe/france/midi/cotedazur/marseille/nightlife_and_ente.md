---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment in Marseille"
type: section
---

Part of going to Marseille is taking in the nightlife, which is best shared without kids. The **Marseille Nightlife** has a variety of things to do, what you can find is really going to depend on what you are looking for. If you are a young adult or perhaps a young couple nightclubs or bars might be more to your liking. Older adults might not enjoy the nightclubs, but the pubs and even the theaters have plenty to offer.

**Docks des Suds**

Docks des Suds is the city’s best venue for entertainment and exhibitions. Docks des Suds has a 3,000-seat auditorium where you can go to attend numerous concerts. The Docks des Suds is located in the middle of the Marseille docklands, but despite the location, it is still very pleasant to visit. If you are in Marseille during the month of October, you need to attend the Fiesta des Suds.

**Shamrock Irish Pub**

This cute little pub is located in the Vieux-Port area of Marseille. It is a great place to go if you are looking for a nice cold beer; their beer is imported straight from Ireland. You can come visit the bar during Happy Hour, where you will be entertained with a variety of Celtic myths or you can come later in the evening where you will be treated to musical entertainment.

**Zoo Bar**

One thing that really makes this bar stand out from the rest is the décor, which includes animal skins that have been stretched out along the walls and the cane seats. This bar is a great place to go if you are looking for a place to drink and dance the night away, since they are open until dawn. This bar is not for the occasional drinker, but more for the serious party person.

**Cochino**

If sitting back and drinking a glass of wine is more along your lines of nightly entertainment, then Cochino is the place to go. At this wine bar you can order wine by the bottle or the glass, you can also order a plate or 2 of cold pork and various cheeses. Quietly in the background, you will hear some of the best jazz music around, just loud enough to listen to without disturbing your conversation.

**Quai 9**

If disco is your thing, this is the place to be with its special “Saturday Night Fever” events. This club is for the trendier and young crowd than the older crowd and it is considered a place to be seen although it is not one of the hottest spots in town. Here you can find just about any beer or alcoholic drink that you are looking for.
