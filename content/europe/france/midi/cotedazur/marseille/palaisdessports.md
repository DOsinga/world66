Marseille's principal indoor arena, located in the southern Rond-Point du Prado area. Built in 1988 and designed by architect Roland Letellier, it seats between 4,300 and 7,300 depending on the configuration. The venue hosts basketball (home of local clubs), handball, boxing events, and touring concert acts. Not architecturally remarkable, but it fills a gap if you want to catch live music or a match while in town.
