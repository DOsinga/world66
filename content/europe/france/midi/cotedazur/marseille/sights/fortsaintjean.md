---
title: "Fort Saint-Jean"
type: poi
---

The origin of the fort goes back to the 12th century, with the Commandery of the Hospitallers of Saint-John of Jerusalem. The square Tower built on the orders of King René dates from the 15th century. Louis XIV had a watchtower, the Tour du Fanal, built by the Chevalier de Clerville around 1670 and also had the building fortified.
