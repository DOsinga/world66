Stade Vélodrome, the cathedral of Marseilles football fans was built in 1937 by the architect Ploquin. The stadium has been renovated by J.-P. Buffi and can now hold 60,000 spectators. Its elliptical shape is still a reminder of its beginnings as a cycle-racing track. It is home to the Olympique de Marseille team, wich will celebrate is centennial in 1999.
