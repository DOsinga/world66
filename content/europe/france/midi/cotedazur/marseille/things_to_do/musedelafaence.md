Château Pastré – 157 avenue de Montredon (8e) – Tel : 04 91 72 43 47
 
 Situated between the sea and the hills in the parc de Montredon, the Château Pastré (19th century) contains over 1200 marseillais, regional , french and european ceramic objects, from the Neolithic period to the present day.
