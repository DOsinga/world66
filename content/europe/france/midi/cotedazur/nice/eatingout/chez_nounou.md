This restaurant has exceptional bouillabaisse** **and a fabulous beachfront location near Nice. If you have never experienced bouillabaisse and you are in Nice, this is the place to go. Skip the appetizers and avoid the bread, because you have some serious work to do with the quantities of fish and soup you will soon be delivered.

If you've never done bouillabaisse before, have no fear. If requested, the staff will show you how to do it. Once you have received the lesson, the delightful soup and accoutrements blend together into a mixture of flavors that you are unilkely to find anywhere else in the world. You might even find that you have difficulty finding this quality of bouillabaisse elsewhere in France.

The restaurant is decorated in a colorful tent-like motif and has a wine selection should meet most wine-drinker's needs. This is an opportunity not to be missed.
