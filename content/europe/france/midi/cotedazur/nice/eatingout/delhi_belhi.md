**Delhi Belhi** is an award winning family owned and operated Indian restaurant tucked away in Vieux Nice in a small street behind the popular Cours Saleya Market. They serve really good Indian Curries and Tandoori specialties in a very welcoming and authentic ambience.

Head for this hidden jewel of the riviera once you tire of the commonly available “seafood” and “nicoise” cuisine… and enjoy some exotic and aromatic Indian delicacies to the beat of contemporary bollywood music in the background.

Delhi Belhi is the only Indian restaurant on the entire French Riviera to be listed in the prestigeous Gault-Millau guides of the Top 5000 restaurants in France.

Since the Indian couple from Punjab who own this restaurant also run it on a day to day basis, you are bound to get consistently great food and attentive service everyday. The place is very popular with tourists and locals alike, and reservations are advised, though not required.

The owners and staff are bilingual - Fluent French and English are spoken here.
