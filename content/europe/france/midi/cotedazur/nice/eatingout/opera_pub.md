The Opera Pub serves first rate bistro fare at value for money prices. The roast chicken and frittes is second to none - truly the best in Nice and believe me my wife an I have tried them all. Also superb is the charcoute garni (basically pork sausages, chops and sauerkraut). The beer is cold, the service friendly (no seriously it is) and the location as convenient as can be - right at the end of the row of restaurants in the Old Town. I don't want to say too many good things about this place because my wife and I go there all the time and I don't want it to fill up!!!
