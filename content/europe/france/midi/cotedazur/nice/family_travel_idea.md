---
title: "Family Travel Ideas in Nice"
type: section
---

The presence of fun-filled activities and favorable climate has added to the popularity of **Nice family travel**. Some of the activities and tourist spots that can be enjoyed by the entire family are as follows:

**Colline du Chateau**

This is one of the most popular family tourist spots in Nice. You can enjoy a spectacular view of the entire city from the top of the castle. You will have to climb the stairs to reach the platform 90 meters above. You can also take the elevator if you have any older members in your family. Remember that the park closes around sunset.

**Skates**

You can explore the entire city on skates provided that all the members of the family are fit and healthy. Rent skates from the Fun N Roll, situated on 13, rue Cassini 06300.

**Beaches**

Spend a quiet time on the beach with your family. The light blue colored beaches are suitable for swimming. Enjoy on a private beach with various services such as restaurants and bars along with rental towels and lounge chairs. The beaches in towns such as Villefranche-sur-Mer and Cannes are quite popular with tourists. Villefranche is a good choice if you are traveling with children. Ensure that you carry towels or mats and wear sandals as most of the beaches contain large flat stones devoid of much sand.

**Hiking**

You can also embark on a hiking trip with your family. The hiking trail begins from La Turbie. Those who love to ride bicycles can opt for a guided bicycle tour which covers all the historical landmarks. Each trip lasts for 3 hours.
