Toulon is a working naval city on the western edge of the Côte d'Azur, less glitzy than Nice or Cannes but more authentic. The old port is lively and scruffy in the right way, and the cable car up Mont Faron gives sweeping views over the bay and the fleet anchored below. It's often used as a base for exploring the Var interior.
