One of the most respected restaurants in the region, run by Fabien Lefebvre, who was named Meilleur Ouvrier de France (Best Craftsman in France) for cooking in 2004. The cuisine is creative modern French, rooted in local Languedoc ingredients. It sits just off the allées Paul Riquet, easy to find and worth booking ahead.
