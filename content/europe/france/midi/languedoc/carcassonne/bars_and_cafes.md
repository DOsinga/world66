### [La Fiesta Bodega](/europe/france/midi/languedoc/carcassonne/nightlife/lafiestabodega)

Spanish-style bar that also does decent tapas. Address: 49 ave Henri Gout. Tel: 04 68 71 39 42.

### [Le Broadway](/europe/france/midi/languedoc/carcassonne/nightlife/lebroadway)

Karaoke bar in the centre. Address: 17 blvd Omer Sarraut. Tel: 04 68 47 53 53.

### [La Bulle](/europe/france/midi/languedoc/carcassonne/nightlife/labulle)

Discotheque in the centre, open Wednesday to Sunday. Address: 115 rue Barbacane. Tel: 04 68 72 47 70.
