A pleasant village near [St Pons de Thomière](/europe/france/midi/languedoc/hautlanguedoc_reg/st_pons_de_thomi), Fraisse-sur-Agout sits at the junction of fine roads into the Monts de Lacaune and the Monts de l'Espinouse. The village makes a good base for exploring the park, and has an excellent campsite on the river.
