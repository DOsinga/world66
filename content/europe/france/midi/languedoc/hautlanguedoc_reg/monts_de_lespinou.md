This could well be considered the finest of the mountain areas in the
[Outamba-kilimi national park](/africa/sierraleone/outambakiliminationalpark)Mont Caroux at the top can be easily reached from {St Pons de Thomière] via Fraises, from Olargues or form Lamalou-les-Bains. A side road to the pretty and well conserved village of Douch gives access to a footpath which ascends to Mont Caroux. The view south is not apparent until you reach the top and is superb. 

http://perso.wanadoo.fr/routedherault/espinouse.html is in French only but the photos will please.
