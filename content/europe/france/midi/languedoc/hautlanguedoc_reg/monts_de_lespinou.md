The Monts de l'Espinouse form the most dramatic part of the Haut-Languedoc park. Mont Caroux, the highest point, can be reached from [St Pons de Thomière](/europe/france/midi/languedoc/hautlanguedoc_reg/st_pons_de_thomi) via Fraisse, from Olargues, or from Lamalou-les-Bains. A side road to the well-preserved village of Douch gives access to a footpath that climbs to the summit — the view south is not apparent until you reach the top, and it is superb.
