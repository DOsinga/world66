---
title: "Bars and Cafes in Montpellier"
type: section
---

Montpellier is one of the most developed cities in the southern part of [France](/europe/france), and it has a wide variety of entertainment and amenities for tourists. When you need to find a place to get refreshments or unwind over a few drinks, there are many bars and cafes in the city that you can visit. Here are some of the most popular bars in the city of Montpellier.

**Grand Café Riche**

The Grand Café Richie is situated on a very popular square in [Montpellier](/europe/france/midi/languedoc/montpellier), which is the [Place de la Comedie](/europe/france/midi/languedoc/montpellier/castles). It was established in the year 1893, and it is presently one of the favorite cafes among locals and tourists. The café is the perfect place to have a coffee and enjoy the human scenery, and its wonderful décor provides an ideal environment for relaxation. It serves delicious snacks, coffee, lunch and tea, and the service is very professional. The prices of food and beverages in this café are a little higher compared to other cafes in the square.

**Apollo Jazz Café**

The Apollo Jazz Café in Montpellier is modeled after the famous Apollo Theater in Harlem that featured jazz greats such as Billie Holiday and Ella Fitzgerald. This place serves a great selection of authentic soul [food](/europe/france/midi/languedoc/montpellier/eatingout) and grilled meat items, and it has a bar that offers a wide variety of unique beverages, including a few popular ones that contain Cuban rum. The Apollo Jazz Café is one of the best places in Montpellier to listen to live music. The musicians will perform various types of music, including blues, rock and salsa, and there is also a dance floor in the café. This café is located on Avenue de Palavas.

**The Circus**

One of the main reasons why people visit this bar is because of its great mix of music. You will get the chance to listen to a wide variety of songs that are seldom played elsewhere, including tunes from relatively unknown classic rock, soul, R&B and blues artists. The bar features a circus-themed décor which is cozy and interesting, and it has very comfortable leather sofas for those who wish to relax while listening to music. It also has a patio that is open in the summer. The Circus is also known for its great bar service and selection of fine cocktails. It is situated in Rue Collot, right in the downtown area of Montpellier.
