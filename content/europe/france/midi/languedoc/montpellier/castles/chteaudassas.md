This listed summer house was built by architect Giral. It features Regency wrought irons, a vast oval music hall decorated with paintings by Lajoue,and a collection of keyboard music instruments, among which a wonderful French 18th century harpsichord. French Formal Gardens.  

Location : 1, route de St Vincent à Assas (12 km Nord de Montpellier sur la D109). main entrance on the square of the church of Saint Martial.

Tel : 04 67 59 62 45

Visits on Sundays and Holidays from 14:30 to 18:00 from May 1st to November 2.  Group visits upon request. Concerts during Fall.
