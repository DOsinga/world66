17th century building, 17thand 18th century furniture, works of art, gorgeous formal French gardens. This listed castle, that has belonged to the family since 1696, is located in the middle of the "Côteaux de la Méjanelle" vineyard (Cellar with wine-testing sessions).

 Location : 1744, av. Albert Einstein – Le Millénaire

Tel : 04 99 52 66 37

Visits in July and August every afternoon from 14:30 to 18:00. Closed : Mondays.During the rest of the years, visits upon request only. 

The Cellar and Park are open daily in July and August from 9:00 to 12:30 and from 14:30 to 19:00. During, the rest of the years, the opening hours remain the same except on Sundays and Legal Holidays.
