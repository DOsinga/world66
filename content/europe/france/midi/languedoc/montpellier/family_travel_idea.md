title: "Family Travel Ideas in Montpellier"
type: section
---

Montpellier offers many different enjoyable things for a family to do while visiting. Attractions will appeal to both kids and parents and there are several places to take the kids where they can play in the square and watch the fountains.

**Place de la Comedie**

The Place de la Comedie is in the heart of Montpellier and is a huge pedestrian area-- so you don’t have to worry about competing with traffic unless it has two legs. There is a large fountain in the center of the square which is lined with cafes and little shops and an old fashioned carousel with painted animals on which the kids can take a ride.

**Tourist Tram**

A small blue and white tram travels a loop around the city, taking visitors to the area where the museums are and to the water tower and promenade.
