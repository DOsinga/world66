Since its creation in 1981, Montpellier Dance Festival has been one of those very special places where the most famous choregraphers in the world like to meet on a regular basis. Montpellier has already hosted more than 300 dance companies from all the world thanks to this festival, a logical extension of the rich dance season that attracts a more and more numerous public to our city.
