Every summer, in July, Montpellier opens its buildings and gardens to almost one hundred daily concerts : operas, symphonic concerts, chamber music, young soloist recitals, jazz, concerts in the districts and hospitals, France Culture Debates... Music spreads its wings in all kinds of settings : the warm light of the sunset in the Ursulines courtyard, the cicadas in the Pétrarque gardens, the welcome air-conditioning in Pasteur and Einstein halls, the exceptional acoustics in the Corum's Berlioz Opera...
