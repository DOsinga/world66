**MUSEUM OF PHARMACY (MUSEE DE LA PHARMACIE)**

Location : Faculté de Pharmacie - 15 av. Charles Flahault 

Tel : (33) 04 67 54 80 62 

Visits Tuesdays and Fridays from 10:00 to 12:00.
