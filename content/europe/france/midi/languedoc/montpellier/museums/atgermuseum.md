It contains a wonderful collection of drawings from the Flemish, Italian, Dutch, German and French  schools of painting, patiently collected by art lover Xavier Atger who bequeathed it to the Faculty of  Medicine in the 19th century. The library of this Faculty also houses numerous invaluable manuscripts  from the 8th to the 19th centuries and a remarkable collection of Flemish local masters' drawings  dating from the Renaissance to the 19th century. 

Location : Faculté de Médecine, 2, rue école de Médecine 

Tel : (33) 04 67 66 27 77

 Opening Hours : Mondays, Wednesdays and Fridays from 13:30 to 17:45.  Closed : During Christmas Holidays and in August.  Group Visits upon request only.
