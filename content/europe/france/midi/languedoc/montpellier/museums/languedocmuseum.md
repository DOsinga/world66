Located in a former private mansion, it houses very interesting prehistoric, protohistoric, Gallo- Roman, Roman, Greek, Etruscan, Egyptian, Medieval (Romanesque sculptures) and classical  (ceramics)collections.

 Location : 7, rue Jacques Coeur

 Tel : (33) 04 67 52 93 03

 Opening Hours : From Monday to Saturday from 14:00 to 17:00 and from 15:00 to 18:00 from July  to September. Closed on Sundays and Holidays.
