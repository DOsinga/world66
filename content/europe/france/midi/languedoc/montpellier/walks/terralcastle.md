Location : Terral district in Saint-Jean de Védas
 Opening Hours : Saturdays and Sundays from 14:00 to 18:00. Upon request during the week. 
 
 Its 19th century garden is planted with numerous tree species : Lambat cypresses, European hackberry elms, scholar-trees, and Chusan palms. It also has a pleasant "Scent Garden" and gathers over 1,000 plant species on a 4 ha surface.
