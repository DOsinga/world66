Created in 1593 by Henry the IVth, to make it easier for medicine students to lead their researchs, the Plant Garden is France's oldest botanical garden. It includes tropical greenhouses, a 19th century orangery, a systematics school and a gorgeous English Garden, covering a 4,5 ha surface;  Location : Boulevard Henri IV Tel : 04 67 63 43 22 Opening Hours : Daily from 10:00 to 19:00 from April 1st to September 30 and from 10:00 to 17:00 from October 1st to March 3. Closed : Mondays.
