---
title: "THE PEYROU ROYAL PROMENADE"
type: location
---

In the outskirts of the "Ecusson" (a name derived from the historic centre's shield-like shape), the Peyrou promenade, outfitted for the equestrian statue of Henry the IVth to be erected, is one of the favorite places of Montpellier inhabitants. The Arch of Triumph and the Water Tower, in the continuation of which is located the Aqueduc, are all classical architecture models.
