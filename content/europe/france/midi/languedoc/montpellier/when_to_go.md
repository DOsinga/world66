Following is a **Montpellier when to go** guide that will help tourists to decide the best time of the year to visit this French city.

Due to its location on the Mediterranean Sea, Montpellier has a pleasant temperate climate. Montpellier witnesses more sunny days as compared to most other French cities. This ensures that no matter which part of the year you visit Montpellier, you will most likely get some good weather.

**Spring

** The spring season in Montpellier usually lasts from March to May. This is probably the best time to visit the city as the weather is very pleasant. Temperatures during this time of the year range from about 16 degrees to 23 degrees Celsius during the day. Sometimes temperatures can even drop to about 9 degrees on a minimum. While the temperatures may not be all that high, it is ideal for relaxing on Montpellier ’s beaches or even taking a swim in the Mediterranean Sea . The floral displays all over the city are the most memorable sights during this time of the year.

**Summer
**
Montpellier ’s summer season is from June to August. Sometimes, summer can last right up to October. During this time, Montpellier becomes quite warm with average temperatures reaching about 26 degrees. On certain days in summer, temperatures can also reach 30 degrees. It is advisable for tourists to take some time out from sightseeing, especially in the afternoon as it can get quite hot. Sea temperatures can go quite high and this is perfect for those who like swimming. Evening temperatures are pleasant to sit outdoors in any of the city’s restaurants and cafes.

**Autumn
**
Autumn months in Montpellier are usually very short, usually lasting from late September to November. Most tourists will not notice the difference between the weather in summer and autumn, but it does become slightly cooler at this time of the year. On an average, temperatures are about 20 degrees Celsius. While this is still pleasant enough to take a swim, the weather is quite balmy.

**Winter
**
The winter months in Montpellier last from December to February. The average temperature at this time of the year is about 11 degrees. The skies are still clear blue and the breeze is crisp. This is ideal for enjoying some lunch and coffee in outdoor restaurants.

Most visitors prefer to visit Montpellier during the summer months. But visiting this French city during other months is also a good idea. This is because temperatures at quite pleasant for sightseeing. In addition to this, the city is also less crowded during off season.
