### [Private Footsteps of the Romans at Pont du Gard Tour](/europe/france/midi/languedoc/nmes/sights/private_footsteps)

The Romans chose the Eure in Uzes to supply the town of Nimes with
water and they built a thirty mile aqueduct and the bridge to achieve
it. Today you can learn more about the history and heritage of the city
of Nimes and see the many Roman remains. There's also free time to
explore the site of le Pont du Gard.
