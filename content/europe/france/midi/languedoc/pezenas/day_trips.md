**Pezenas Day Trips **truly celebrate nature and the outdoors in ways that most travelers wouldn't anticipate from France. Each day trip has something unique to offer its visitors and are worth the short separation from charming Pezenas.

**Aqualand**

There are several Aqualand locations in France. This particular Aqualand is located in Cap d'Agde, which is about 30 minutes south of Pezenas. It's accessible by personal vehicle or taxi. It's a wonderful way to see a different side of France and escape the heat of the summer months. The water park has slides for any type of traveler and range from enclosed slides to multi-person slides. There is even a slide shaped like a half-pipe. Aqualand also has a lazy river and an area for lounging. There is a small fee for renting a lounge chair. Families with children will especially love Aqualand. This addition to Pezenas Day Trips is open July through September and admission is $23.50 euros per adult. Remember to bring a dry change of clothes for your trip
