This **Pezenas Family Travel **guide will help you in deciding where to stay and what to do when visiting Pezenas with family or a large group.

**Lodging**

Pezenas offers several different options for lodging with a larger group or with family. The most economical is to camp out, with prices starting around 10 € per day. If sleeping in a tent is not your thing, the Parc Loisir Vacances Castelsec offers bungalows sleeping up to 5 people with prices starting at 67 € per night or 398 €
per week. Another option is to rent an apartment. The prices start around 500 € per week for apartments in town and around 340 € for those just outside of town and they offer all amenities.

**Dining**

There are several restaurants in town. If you are looking for traditional specialties, Apres le Deluge offers 130 selections on their menus and also offer a children menu. The Creperie le Gelase is a smaller restaurant offering several different types of crepes. L'assiette du Boucher is a grill specializing in meats and fresh fish, and they offer a children's menu. For local specialties, you should try La Tartine. This is a smaller restaurant offering local dishes and the prices are quite affordable.
**
Les Attelages du Picpoul**

Take a ride back in time with this stable that offers horse drawn buggy rides to discover the area and town, as well as visits to some of the "chateaux" with wine tastings. Prices are 30 € per person for the day and they offer pricing for children.

**Bikes and ATV Rental**

If you would like to explore the town and surroundings on your own terms, you can rent bicycles from Planete Velos. If you prefer exploring the countryside, you can rent ATVs from Base de Bessilles. They offer 6 km to 35 km trails and have rates for children.

**Forest Adventure**

Located in a pine tree forest, La Foret d'acrobate offers 4 acrobatic paths accessible to all. You can try ziplining, walking on suspended bridges or rope bridges, crawling through suspended tubes, keeping your balance on the zig zag bridge or try the Tarzan jump. They also offer a kids path', Parcours Lilliputien. An amazing experience for the entire family.
