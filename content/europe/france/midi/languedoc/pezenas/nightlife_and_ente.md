---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment in Pezenas"
type: section
---

Discover the history-rich city of Southern France in a different way by discovering the **Pezenas nightlife.** If you think this place is all about antique findings, then you might actually be mistaken. Pezenas is also known among tourists as a haven for fun night activities. Here are some of what the city’s got to offer.

**Au Bon Coin**

If you are looking to spend some chill night at Pezenas, then you must include Au Bon Coin in your list of bars to visit. A hint of modern is adapted to its style. Drink beer all you want and spend the night away with your friends or your partner. You may also eat out and get new acquaintances.
**
La Pomme d'Amour**

Or the Love Apple. This is a small spot in the city of Pezenas that is most famous for its traditional style of cooking. Due to its size, it can only accommodate as much as 22 people. Better make early reservations so you can enjoy a romantic eat out with your partner and an excellent dish to compliment your stay. Prices start at $11. Also, it’s closed on Mondays and Tuesdays.

**Mirondella des Arts**

Don’t miss out on the Saturday late night craft market which offers only the best of Pezenas’ art. Be familiar with the rich history of this commune and keep some mementos by purchasing for yourself some of their local products. Spend the night shopping with your daughter or with your fiancé. Don’t drag too much crowd, though, as the place can get real busy.

**Old Town Center**

Who says the Old Town is just for day trips and day amusements? Well, you can always stroll along this historical spot at your leisure, even if the day is almost over. Take a walk in the beautiful cobbled streets of Pezenas’ preserved old town. Get a glimpse of its rich medieval and renaissance history as you take your fill of its architecture in dim lights.

**Moliere Theater**

If you think you have already completed your stay by visiting old sites and trying out this French city’s menu, you might want to rethink that and pay a visit to the Moliere Theater. Not only is Pezenas popular among tourists for its old accounts, but also of the old traditions it has kept to place. Sit back and enjoy some good performances the rest of your night.
