---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Walking is the primary activity in Pézenas. The tourist office arranges guided tours of the old town for around €5 — there are themed walks covering the architecture, the Molière connection, and local food and wine. The Vulliod Saint-Germain Museum, in a donated hôtel particulier, has antique furniture, porcelain, and local history exhibits. The Maison des Métiers d'Art, in the former consular building, displays crafts and art for free. Outside town, several Coteaux du Languedoc wine estates offer cellar tours and tasting.
