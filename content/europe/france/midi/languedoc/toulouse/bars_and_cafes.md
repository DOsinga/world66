---
title: "Bars and Cafes in Toulouse"
type: section
---

There are several **Toulouse bars and cafes** in this French city located in the southwest of France. Toulouse is scenic, so it's a favorite place among a lot of tourists who want to relax and have fun at the same time. Here are some of the best bars and caf&eacute;s in Toulouse.

**The Frog & Rostbif**

This is an English style bar but it is also influenced with a bit of French flavor. The bar is frequented by a lot of young people. It plays upbeat music and dancing is a favorite. The dance floor can accommodate a huge number of dancers but during Fridays and weekends, the bar can be jam-packed with many partygoers. The good thing about this bar is that it does not have any prescribed attire. In fact, anyone can go inside the bar wearing casual clothes. The price of drinks in the bar cost from $10 to $15.

**The Mulligans**

This is a typical Irish pub which is situated in the end of the town. Although this bar is an Irish pub, it draws a lot of French crowds as well as tourists. You can get a good Irish beer and watch football. The bar does not offer dancing but that is okay considering that it offers delicious food. During the weekends, the bar offers live band music which is performed by local entertainers. Cost of drinks at this bar range between $10 and $20.
