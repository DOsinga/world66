A masterpiece of southern Gothic architecture built by the Dominican order in the 13th century. The nave is divided by a single row of columns that branch out into extraordinary palm-tree vaults at the roof — one of the most beautiful interior spaces in France. The remains of Thomas Aquinas are kept here. Small admission charge.
