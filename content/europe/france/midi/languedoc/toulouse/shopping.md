---
title: "Shopping in Toulouse"
type: section
---

Since traveling goes hand-in-hand with souvenir buying, you cannot miss out the must-haves from these **Toulouse Shopping**
centers. From department stores to the many flea markets around the city, Toulouse is a great place where you can enjoy purchasing European
clothes, shoes, and customary foods and specialties for memories’ sake.

**Galeries Lafayette**

Being an elegant department store, the Galeries Lafayette can be
found in the shopping district of the Old Town, also known as Place du
Capitol. Here you can buy branded clothes from the boutiques of top
French designers. You can also go shopping for jewelry, perfumes,
plates, cups, and other household bits and pieces to serve as your
memorabilia of Toulouse.

**Toulouse Flea Markets**

Several flea markets are open on Wednesdays and Sundays. The
Place du Capitol is filled up with markets that are only available on
Wednesdays. These bazaars sell various items including French clothing
and household goods. Some of them vend products that were
imported from countries of other continents, such as Asia, South
America, and Africa. On Sundays, the market of Place Saint-Aubin drives
in many tourists to buy souvenir items. The paths at the basilica of
San-Aubin are congested because of the many people buying traditional
foods and delicacies, home equipment, and authentic accessories.

**St. Sernin Market **

Located in the Old Town, the Sernin Church is the haven of the
famous St. Sernin Market. This market sells all kinds of stuff,
including clothes, accessories, jewelry, antiques, and even junk
objects. This is one of the good places in Toulouse where bargaining is
very welcome. All though you will definitely see here the souvenir you
are looking for, the market is only available on a weekly basis, so
you need to buy the item you want as immediately as possible. Otherwise, someone else
might get it before you do because of the many people shopping
in the area.

**Rue de Rome**

Rue de Rome is the most popular area in Toulouse where you can buy
shoes and clothes from different stores. Being the main shopping
street, it is one of the very few places where second-hand jewelries
are on sale. Here you can also find unique designer clothes from
boutiques.

**La Maison de la Violette**

The violet, Toulouse’s traditional flower, is the main theme of La Maison de la
Violette. This is a specialty store where you can buy everything
created from violet, including teas, candles, soaps, and even sweets.
