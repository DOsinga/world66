Housed in a 14th-century Augustinian monastery whose Gothic cloisters are themselves a highlight, this museum contains one of France's best collections of medieval sculpture — Romanesque capitals from demolished abbeys, Gothic funerary monuments — alongside European paintings from the Renaissance to the 19th century. The medieval collections are exceptional.
