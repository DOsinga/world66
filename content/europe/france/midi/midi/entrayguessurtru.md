Such is the beauty of the Truyère gorges and the Olt [upper Lot] valley that there is no decent website on Entaygues which is situated at their junction. Even so it makes a splendid sight as you descend through forests from far above and is well endowed with places t eat and stay -warmly recommended as a stop in transit or for a few days.
