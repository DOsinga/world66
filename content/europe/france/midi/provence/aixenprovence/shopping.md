---
title: "Shopping"
type: section
---

Aix is a lively market town. The big markets take place three times a week (Tuesday, Thursday, Saturday) in the city centre squares: Place des Precheurs and Place de la Madeleine and the surrounding neighbourhoods. Saturday is the busiest day.

Under the plane trees of the little Place Richelme you will find a charming produce market that draws housekeepers, restaurant chefs, and tourists alike. The stalls are piled with Provencal olives, goat cheese, lavender honey, and seasonal fruit.
