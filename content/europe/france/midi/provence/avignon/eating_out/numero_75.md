Casual restaurant near the main fruit and vegetable market, housed in the former home of distiller Jules Pernod. The dining room opens onto a garden shaded by century-old trees. Chef Robert Brunel keeps a short, market-driven menu that changes with the seasons — grilled fish, grilled lamb with stewed peppers, good crème brûlée. Reasonable prices for the quality. 75 rue Guillaume Puy.
