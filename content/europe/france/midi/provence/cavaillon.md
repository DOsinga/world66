Cavaillon is a market town in the Luberon known above all for its melons. The sweet Cavaillon melon has been celebrated here since at least the 16th century, and the Monday morning market is one of the largest in Provence. Beyond the market, the old town has a small synagogue and Jewish museum, remnants of one of the oldest Jewish communities in France.
