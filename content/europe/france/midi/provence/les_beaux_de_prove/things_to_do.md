---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

The main attraction is the ruined Chateau des Baux, which sprawls across two thirds of the rock plateau above the village. The castle grounds include a keep, fortified towers, and reconstructed medieval siege engines. From the ramparts the views over the patchwork of olive groves and vineyards below are magnificent. The village itself, with its galleries and craft shops tucked into stone buildings, is worth an hour of wandering.
