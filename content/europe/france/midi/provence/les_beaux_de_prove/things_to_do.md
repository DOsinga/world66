CHATEAU DES BAUX DE PROVENCE, PROVENCE
Towering over Provence right down to the sea, Château des Baux is built in one of the most beautiful locations in France . Explore it , with a fascinating audio guide: the keep and its fortified towers, siege machines, courtly love and the history of Provence . An unforgettable visit for young and old!
