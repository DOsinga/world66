---
title: "When to Go"
type: section
---

April, May, and October are the best months. The light is good, the temperatures are pleasant for walking, and the village hasn't yet filled with coaches. July and August are very crowded — the site is one of the most visited in Provence, and the narrow lanes can feel like a bottleneck on summer weekends. Winter is quiet and atmospheric, though some shops and attractions reduce their hours or close.

Les Baux sits at altitude and can be cold and windy in winter. The mistral blows hard here — dramatic in its way, but bring a windproof layer even in spring.
