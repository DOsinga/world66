ROMAN THEATRE AND MUSEUM OF ORANGE , PROVENCE
Go back in time to discover, intact in the heart of Provence , one of the most beautiful theatres of the Roman Empire . Thanks to the film and with the help of an audio guide, you'll be able imagine 10,000 spectators in the gardens, actors, dancers, jugglers coming on stage in front of a sumptuously decorated marble wall. Discover the history of this magnificent place, the splendour of the Empire at the Chorégies.
