The departement of the Hautes-Pyrenees, or High Pyrenees offers just that - lots of high mountains. In winter it's great for skiing, with a sun a bit hotter than in the [Alpes](/europe/france/alpes) and snow just as white. In summer it's great for hiking and other outdoor activities. The [Pic du Midi](/europe/france/midi/pyrenees/picdumidi) is the highest mountain, with great trekking and skiing opportunities. Other famous resorts include [Luz Ardiden](/europe/france/midi/pyrenees/luzardiden) and [Val Louron](/europe/france/midi/pyrenees/vallouron).


 If you are not able to hike, bike, ski or walk, head for [Lourdes](/europe/france/midi/pyrenees/lourdes) the major tourist city in the departement. Since the virgin Mary appeared to a few peasant gilrs here, miracles have been plenty...
