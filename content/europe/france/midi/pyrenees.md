The departement of the Hautes-Pyrenees, or High Pyrenees offers just that - lots of high mountains. In winter it's great for skiing, with a sun a bit hotter than in the 
Alpes and snow just as white. In summer it's great for hiking and other outdoor activities. The 
Pic du Midi is the highest mountain, with great trekking and skiing opportunities. Other famous resorts include 
Luz Ardiden and 
Val Louron. 
 

If you are not able to hike, bike, ski or walk, head for 
[Lourdes](/europe/france/midi/pyrenees/lourdes) the major tourist city in the departement. Since the virgin Mary appeared to a few peasant gilrs here, miracles have been plenty...
