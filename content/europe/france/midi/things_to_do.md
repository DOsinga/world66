---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Southern France rewards the active traveller. The gorges are made for kayaking, the mountains beg to be hiked, and the coast offers everything from scuba diving among sunken wrecks to surfing Atlantic swells near Biarritz. Inland, you can cycle Tour de France climbs like Mont Ventoux or spend a lazy afternoon floating down the Ardèche beneath limestone arches.
