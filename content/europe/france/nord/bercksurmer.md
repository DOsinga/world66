For a beach holiday in the north of France, Berck sur Mer offers you everything you could want - except maybe the hot mediterranean sun of the Midi. The sandy beaches are great, there is a casino and there are many activities in and around Berck. The ideal place for an active beach holiday.


 The towns of [Boulogne](/europe/france/nord/boulogne), [Arras](/europe/france/nord/arras) and [Lille](/europe/france/nord/lille) are not far and even [Paris](/europe/france/paris) is within reach for a day trip.


 For thos looking for a bit of culture, Berck has a museum and a few churches worth visiting. See the berck sur mer,sights section for more details.
