title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Berck-sur-Mer is built for activity holidays. The enormous beach and the nearby Authie Bay estuary offer sand sailing, horse riding, fishing, and water sports, while the town itself has a small museum and a couple of churches worth a look.

**Musee de Berck-sur-Mer**, 60 Rue de l'Imperatrice, tel: +33 (0)3 21 84 07 80. The town museum covers local history, maritime life, and the artists who were drawn to the Opal Coast light.

**Eglise Notre Dame des Sables**, Rue de l'Imperatrice, tel: +33 (0)3 21 09 01 03.

**Eglise Saint Jean Baptiste**, Avenue Quettier, tel: +33 (0)3 21 09 09 49.

**Sand Sailing** at the Ecole de Char a Voile, Esplanade Parmentier, tel: +33 (0)3 21 89 87 00. Two-hour sessions; bring boots or old trainers, waterproofs, and gloves. Helmets provided.

**Horseback Riding** near the Authie Bay, Chemin du Paragon, tel: +33 (0)3 21 89 87 92. Long rides along the estuary on firm sand. Open 9am-12pm and 2pm-6pm.

**Water Sports** at Club Nautique Berckois, Chemin au Raisins, tel: +33 (0)3 21 09 23 29. Catamaran, windsurfing, kayak, and jet ski.

**Fishing** with Les Sternes, tel: +33 (0)3 21 84 43 48. Sea fishing trips from the harbour.
