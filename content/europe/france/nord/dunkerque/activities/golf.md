The golf of Dunkerque is situated on a historic zone: the Fort Vallières. You can come to practise golf, whether you are a beginner or an expert, everything is provided for you to have good time. 

The golf of Fort Vallières proposes to you a very technical course of 18 holes by 71 (H 5710 m, D 4827 m). The wind will be with you on this course shit where the region is windy enough. Between the first 9 holes where the course is hilly and the last ones where stretches ofwater come to decorate your course, you will find there all the pleasures of the golfer.
