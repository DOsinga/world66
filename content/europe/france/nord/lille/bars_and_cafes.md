---
title: "Bars and Cafes in Lille"
type: section
---

Take your holiday experience to a new level by stopping by at these
great **Lille Bars and Cafes. **There are several bars and cafés all
over the city that offer good beers and great times. Here are a few
recommendations for you.

**Café Oz**

Café Oz is an Australian bar situated right in the center of the
city. Café Oz has many branches all over Europe, but the one located
in Lille is very popular with the younger crowd. Watch your favorite sport
games while you enjoy some tasty Australian food along with a few beers.
If beer is not your thing, try the excellent coffee at Café Oz; you
will be glad you did.

**La Guapa Bar **

If you have "Salsa fever” then La Guapa Bar is the
perfect place for you. This Latino-themed bar is located right in the
busy area of Place du Nouvelle Adventure. You can sit at the terrace,
drink some beer or coffee and just enjoy yourself with your friends. You
can also sit inside or underground and mingle with the spirited crowd.
The bar has an open-stage every Thursday and a live-band and DJ during
weekends. This bar serves some of the best local and international beers
and wines.

**La Pirogue**

La Pirogue is a famous hot spot in Lille. It is always jam packed
with the energetic, young crowd. This restaurant / bar serves a wide
selection of ice cold draught beers, wines and cocktails, as well as
French dishes and light snacks. La Pirogue is located in Jean Jacques
Rousseau Street. You might want to check this place out while holidaying
in Lille.

**Le Palais de la Biere**

Le Palais de la Biere literally means “Beer Palace." It is named that
way because the owners claim that they serve the best selection of beers
all over France. The selection includes Belgian, German, exotic French
beers and various international beers. All of which are priced
reasonably. You can also play pool in the hall for only €10 per hour.

**Le Decale**

Le Decale is a friendly place that plays Indie pop and rock music. It
also serves simple French dishes, light snacks, appetizers and
interesting variations of pickled aperitifs. Le Decale also serves great
cocktails, wines and ice cold beers. Plus, you can watch your favorite
sports on the wide screen LCD TV.
