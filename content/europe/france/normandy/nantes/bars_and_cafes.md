There are many **Nantes bars and cafes** you can enjoy while you are traveling in the city.

#### La Nota Bene

The La Nota Bene caf&eacute; and bar offers a great way to experience the Nantes area. There are traditional meals and drinks, and you can also try any of the local drinks which are full of flavor. The bar and caf&eacute; offers casual dining and a bar area to relax and enjoy the music played at night, or you can find a nice place to relax and sip on your cocktails while enjoying one of the many dishes.

#### Havana Caf&eacute;

The Havana caf&eacute; is a beautiful little caf&eacute; surrounded by plants and ivy. You can enter the caf&eacute; and enjoy the decorations and relax at one of the tables to have a meal, or you can go to the bar area and enjoy a drinks. You can have wine imported from all over, or enjoy one of the local drinks. If you are looking for a fun place to go, try this one of the Nantes bars and cafes.

#### Taverne la Gargouille

The Taverne la Gargouille is well-known for its delicious menu items and also for its selection of wines and drinks. You can pair any of the many wines with one of the traditional meals. There is a relaxing bar area to unwind and enjoy your drinks and quiet music to help you to enjoy your time on vacation.
