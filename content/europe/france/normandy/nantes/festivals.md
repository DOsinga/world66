---
title: "Festivals in Nantes"
type: section
---

**Nantes Festivals** are a popular attraction among the locals and tourists alike. A number of festivals and exhibitions are organized in the city throughout the year.

**The Three Continents Film Festival**

This is one of the most extraordinary festivals. It is held in November. The festival celebrates the work of directors from Asia, Latin America and Africa and provides the visitors an opportunity to see some unique films. Every year between 15 and 20 films are screened over the course of the festival, which also holds discussions and talks on the films and their directors. The festival offers a fabulous opportunity to visitors to widen their cultural horizons.

**Les Allumees**

This is a well known festival that presents the cultural life of a different foreign city each year. It offers an interesting opportunity to learn everything about a new land, and appreciate its history, culture, art and music.

**Nantes International Science-Fiction Festival**

This is a multimedia festival featuring complete sections devoted to literature, comics, science fiction, video games and films. It was founded in 1998. This is the most important science fiction cultural event in Europe and is one of the biggest science fiction writers' conventions in the world. This event is a great way to discover the most recent and exciting productions in science fiction.

**Nantes Electro Festival**

Nantes has recently embarked on a new cultural adventure with this festival. Nantes Electro Festival crosses the threshold of electronic music festivals with its rich programming artists such as Electro Trash, Dirty, Fidget House and Techno.
