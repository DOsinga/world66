A neoclassical château designed by Nicolas Ledoux in the 1770s, considered one of the finest examples of his work. The building now functions as a maternity hospital but is open to visitors during temporary exhibitions. The exterior and formal grounds are worth a look even when the interior is closed. A striking contrast to the military landscape a few hundred metres away.
