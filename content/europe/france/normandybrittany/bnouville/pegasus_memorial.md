The museum dedicated to the British 6th Airborne Division's role in Operation Overlord. Exhibits trace the planning and execution of the glider assault on Pegasus Bridge and the Ranville area, with personal equipment, weapons, uniforms, and oral history accounts from veterans. The original Pegasus Bridge spans a shallow pool in the museum grounds — visitors can walk across it. Open March to November; closed December and January. Tel: +33 (0)2 31 78 19 44.
