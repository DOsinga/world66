A 24-hectare landscaped park on the edge of town, also known as the Domaine des Enclos. It has been owned by the city since 1973 and is planted with rare varieties of trees and shrubs that make it worth a visit in any season. Open July and August; check with the tourism office for current hours. A good option for a shaded afternoon walk away from the crowds.
