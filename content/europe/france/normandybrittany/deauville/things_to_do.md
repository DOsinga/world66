---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Deauville punches above its weight for a small resort town. The horse racing at Clairefontaine and La Touques is genuinely world-class, the casino is a spectacle worth walking through even if you don't gamble, and the surrounding park and gardens reward a slower afternoon. Active visitors will find golf, sailing, and thalassotherapy all within easy reach.
