---
title: "Mont-Canisy"
type: poi
category: "Sight"
latitude: 49.3472
longitude: 0.0917
---

A hill rising 110 metres above sea level on the edge of Deauville, offering views over the coast and a peaceful nature walk. The 13-hectare site also holds remnants of Second World War fortifications. Walking tours are available through the tourism office.
