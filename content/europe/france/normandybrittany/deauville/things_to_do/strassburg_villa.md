type: poi

latitude: 49.3563

---

The most emblematic example of Deauville's extravagant Norman-revival architecture. Built at the turn of the twentieth century, the villa now belongs to the city and is open to visitors. Its half-timbered facade and elaborate gardens capture the Belle Epoque ambition that made Deauville fashionable.
