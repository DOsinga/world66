### [Musée André Lemaitre](/europe/france/normandybrittany/falaise/museums/muse_andr_lema)

Boulevard de la libération, 14700 Falaise.

This museum houses 90 of the works of the painter André Lemaitre, born in Falaise in 1909. Greatly influenced by 20th century French painting, his work can be divided in four periods. 



[[rate it]](/europe/france/normandybrittany/falaise/museums/muse_andr_lema)

### [Août 44](/europe/france/normandybrittany/falaise/museums/aot_44)

chemin des roches, 14700 Falaise. Tel: 02.31.90.37.19.

This museum retraces the famous battles of the "Falaise pocket", using a range of memorabilia unique in Normandy.



### [Automates Avenue](/europe/france/normandybrittany/falaise/museums/automates_avenue)

A museum which retraces the history of animated shop window displays
presented in the big Parisian shops between 1920 and 1960. Ten large
animated scenes, 300 automatons from the Roullet-Decamps collection, a
few displays of old toys and a shop.



tel: 02.31.90.02.43

address: Boulevard de la libération, 14700 Falaise
