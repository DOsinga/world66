A tropical butterfly garden housed in a large greenhouse near the old harbour. Step inside and you're in a warm, humid jungle where hundreds of free-flying butterflies land on your arms and shoulders while parrots and other exotic birds call from the canopy above. The collection includes species from Central America, Southeast Asia, and Africa.

Allow about an hour. Children love it, and it makes a good rainy-day alternative to Honfleur's outdoor sights.
