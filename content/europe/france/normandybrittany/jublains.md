---
title: "Jublains"
type: location
---

Known in Roman times as **Noviodunum**, Jublains is now a quiet little (mainly agricultural) town. Renowned for its ruins of the ancient fortress, theatre and *thermes*, a museum was founded in 1969 and upgraded in 1995.
