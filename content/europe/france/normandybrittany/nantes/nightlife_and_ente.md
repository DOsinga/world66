---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment in Nantes"
type: section
---

**Nantes Nightlife** is as classic as the
historical sights found within the city and as modern as today’s
nightclubs around the world. You can dine in luxury and dance the night
away or you can take in a show or spend the night on the wild side.
Festivals and music concerts can always be found in the city for the
price of a ticket.
Dinner on the Move

There is nothing like seeing Nantes from the river. You will start
your cruise when the captain calls to cast off. Live music is provided
as you begin your meal as you are cruising down the river. You will be
treated to fine foods specially prepared by the on-board chef--duck,
shrimp, wonderfully rich soups, and fish. You will have your choice of
the entrees and desserts. The magic begins with the first note from the
band and continues on throughout the cruise and on into the night.
Nightclubs

Nightclubs and pubs are the hottest spots around. You can find
yourself rubbing elbows with people you have never met before. Enjoy
lively music while you dance the night away or enjoy the intimacy of the
many pubs that can be found. You can even find a nightclub in buildings
that used make great biscuits. You can find nightclubs doubling as art
galleries and live concerts venues. You will have a great time making
new friends and dancing or simply enjoying the more laid-back feel of
the pubs. You can even find a few after-hours' clubs to party in through
the night.
On the Wild Side

Nantes nightlife can get pretty wild with an overnight safari at the
Planete Sauvage. After the zoo closes for the day, things get heated up
for those who spend the night in a bivouac camp under the stars. You
will spend the night participating in bringing the animals in at night,
and getting you up close and personal with the wild beasts of nature.
You will camp among the zebras and antelopes and share a meal and
stories by the camp fire. In the morning you will get to eat breakfast
and head back on in to the park.
Music Festival

The Jazz Festival at the end of August is a great time to spend an
evening or two with the locals, enjoying good music in a friendly,
laid-back atmosphere. The Endre River comes to life for three days and
it is a grand party. The scenery is beautiful and the party is
happening. It is a great way to spend the evening.
