---
title: "Shopping in Nantes"
type: section
---

You cannot end your trip without experiencing the thrill of **Nantes shopping**.
From sweet treats to reading the written word, you can find what you
are looking for in the city of Nantes. The streets of Nantes hold many
delights as you explore the world of retail, fine fashion, small elegant
boutiques, and malls. Bargain hunters will delight with flea markets
and small shops.
Rue Jean Jaures

Let the hunt begin! Rue Jean Jaures is a district packed with
antiques, knick-knacks, and art. You can find craftsmen plying their
trade as you stroll the shops looking for great treasures at bargain
prices. On Saturdays, you can go through the stalls of the flea market
and find those items that you simply must have. If it is a bargain
you're hunting for, you are sure to find it here.
Malls

Mall Madness, large and small mall type shopping centers, can be
found within the city that include movie theaters and major stores as
well as a grocery store. You can find anything you are looking for in
the major shopping centers and malls and spend a whole day in one. From
designer clothes to luggage, there is something for every member of the
group. It is a great way to spend a day and people-watch at the same
time.
Wine, Books, and Sweets

Few things in this world can compare to curling up with a nice glass
of wine, a good book, and maybe a chocolate or two. Regional wines can
be found at any of the wine shops available in the city. While you’re
cruising the wine shops, don't forget those great little book boutiques.
You can pick up a good book at any of the book stores in town. Nantes
also delights in the sweeter side of life with special candy and
chocolate shops that are worth visiting.
Shopping Streets

The main shopping streets have many great shops full of the latest
fashions, artwork, jewelry, and specialty shops that sell mouth-watering
butter caramels and salt for seasoning. Rue Crebillon may be small but
it is a great delight to wander in and it opens up to Passage Pommeraye
where original boutiques can be found and explored. You may also find
great guides for touring the city and post cards to send home.
Museum Shops

Tour the museums and you will find more than tombs, exhibits, and
elegance, you can actually find shops. You can pick up replicas of
exhibits, mementos of the trip, apparel, and great little gifts for
family and friends back home. Museum shops are always fun to browse and
you can find great treasures hidden away on the shelves.
