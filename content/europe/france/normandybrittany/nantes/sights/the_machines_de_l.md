The ***Machine Gallery*** is the dreamland to take the controls of numerous "strange creatures" or of the "flying flea" at a speed of more than 100 kilometres per hour, and to discover all work-in-progress phases of the future creations: some elements of the ***Marine Worlds*** and the prototype of the ***Heron Tree***. The ***Marine Worlds Carrousel*** will soon be standing on the banks of the *Loire River*. In a few years, visitors will also be able to climb onto the back or the wings of the birds in the ***Heron Tree*** for a breathtaking circular flight above the suspended gardens in the air.

Have you ever seen a giant elephant taking a stroll in the city? The ***Great Elephant*** (12 metres high - 8 metres wide - 45 tons of weight) takes passengers on its back and in its stomach for a journey on the *Ile de Nantes*. On-board travellers can take a glimpse of the city from the 4 storey high moving animal.
