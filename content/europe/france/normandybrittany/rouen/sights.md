**general**

**The cathedral of Our-Lady **

Built over several centuries (from XIIe to XIXe), one can follow all the periods of the Gothic style there. Remarkable canopies from XIIIe, XIVe, XVe and XVIe century.  

**Abbey Saint-Ouen **

Built building of XIVe in XVIe century, one of the most beautiful Gothic monuments of France.  

**The Holy Church Jeanne d' Arc **

Splendid stained glasses of XVIe century coming from the Saint-Vincent church, destroyed during the last war.  

**The Saint-Maclou Church **

Blazing jewel of the Gothic art (XVe century), it has a famous gate with 5 porches whose carved wood gates go back to 1552.  

**Aitre Saint-Maclou **

Old parochial cemetery in the shape of cloister now occupied by the school of Beautiful Arts.  

**The Large Clock **

The large-Clock is composed of a belfry and a stone arch of Renaissance style spanning the street and surmounted of a house in wood sides supporting of a each with dimensions polychrome dial.  

**The Jewish monument **

Monument dating from the end of XIe century, testifying of the Jewish presence to the Middle Ages in Rouen.  

**The hotel of Bourgthe roulde **

Beautiful Gothic hotel of XVe century having a gallery Rebirth comprising in low-relief a representation of the interview of "the camp of the gold cloth " between Henri VIII and François Ist  

**The tower Jeanne d' Arc **

Place where Jeanne d' Arc was threatened of torture in the presence of her judges.
