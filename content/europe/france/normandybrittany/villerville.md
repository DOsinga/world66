Small, charming town halfway between [Honfleur](/europe/france/normandybrittany/honfleur) and [Deauville](/europe/france/normandybrittany/deauville)/ [Trouville](/europe/france/normandybrittany/trouville). It has a nice but small beach, especially at high tide when it all but disappears.
