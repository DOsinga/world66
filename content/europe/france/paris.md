Paris is known as *The City of Light* and absolutely deserves this name. But it is much more than just the City of Light. Paris is one of the best travel destinations. It has everything a traveler could possibly wish for: good Eating Out (both expensive and cheap), good Accommodation at moderate prices, some of the best museums in the world, as well as a couple of Landmarks you must have seen at least once in your life. 



A stay in Paris should at least include a visit to the Louvre, one of the best Museums in the world, a climb of the Landmarks , a day of Shopping frenzy, Nightlife and time to relax to sit in a parks or visit one of the Squares . 

In the neighborhood of Paris you can visit, [Fontainebleau](/europe/france/paris/fontainebleau) , [Versailles](/europe/france/paris/versailles) with its magnificent palace of Louis XIV and [Disneyland Paris](/europe/france/paris/disneylandparis) . All of these make perfect daytrips.
