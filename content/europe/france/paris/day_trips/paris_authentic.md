Versailles can be reached by car, train, or via guided tour. Attractions include Chateau de Versailles, Parc de Versailles, Petit Trianon, and Musee des Carosses (Carriage Museum), to name a few. The town of Versailles also offers an assortment of historic structures such as the baroque church of Notre-Dame, built from 1684-1686, and a lively morning market at place du Marche. Just follow rue de la Paroisse – the street right in front of Notre-Dame, to get to the market. Guided Versailles tours are offered through Paris Vision ( www.parisvision.com ) and Cityrama ( www.cityrama.com ). For trusted information about visiting Versailles, visit the Versailles Office du Tourisme: 2bis, avenue de Paris 78000 Versailles, 01-39-24-88-88, www.versailles-tourisme.com .
