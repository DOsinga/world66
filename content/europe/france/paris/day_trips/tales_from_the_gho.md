Welcome to **P&egrave;re Lachaise**, **&ldquo;*La Cit&eacute; des Morts*&rdquo;**, the City of the Dead. Perhaps the most visited cemetery in the world, P&egrave;re Lachaise is the eternal resting place of some of the world&rsquo;s most talented and intriguing people.

On your journey through this hauntingly beautiful place you&rsquo;ll see many elaborate chapels, stunning monuments and simple unadorned tombs - each grave with a story to tell. Some are of brilliant young lives tragically cut short, others of celebrated French national heroes and heroines. You will learn a little about these fascinating characters, and hopefully leave P&egrave;re Lachaise with a new appreciation for the lives and works of these extraordinary people.
