Disneyland Paris is the very popular with children all over Europe. It is a big them park with many, many attractions. We will not describe all of them in detail - whatever we say about them your children will have a better idea of what it is like, anyway.

 In Disneyland Park, there are 2 playgrounds, perfect for small children, in Frontierland and Adventureland, and one indoor playground at "Buzz Lightyear's Pizza Planet Restaurant" in Discoveryland. 

 To make the most of a visit with your children it is useful to know which attractions are advised for what age category.
