title: Île de la Cité
type: poi
---

The island in the Seine where Paris began. Notre-Dame and the Sainte-Chapelle share this sliver of land with the Conciergerie, the Palais de Justice, and the oldest hospital in Paris. Most visitors rush through to the cathedral, but the island rewards a slower pace: the flower market on Place Louis Lépine, the quiet triangular park at the western tip (Square du Vert-Galant), and the views from the bridges on either side are among the best in the city.
