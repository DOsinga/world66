Paris has more sights than can be visited in one trip. Here we show a menu of all the different possibilities. Check out [Museums](/europe/france/paris/sights/museums) - in any case be sure not to miss the Louvre, but the centre Pompidou, the Musee Rodin and the Musee d'Orsay also deserve special attention. 

The beautiful [Churches](/europe/france/paris/sights/churches) among them the Notre Dame and the Sacre Coeur with its great views over the city should be on every itinerary. 

The famous [Landmarks](/europe/france/paris/sights/landmarks) such as the Eiffel Tower or the Arc de Triomphe are absolute highlights npo traveler would want to miss. 

If you are a bit tired of all that you can relax in one of the many parks .
