Akhalkalaki is one of the bigger cities in southern Georgia. It is something of a travel hub, with connections to [Tbilisi](/europe/georgia/tbilisi) and [Akhaltsikhe](/europe/georgia/alkhatsikhe). It is not ideal to visit [Vardzia](/europe/georgia/vardzia) from here, but is is possible. The easiest way is probably to take a marshutka minibus to Khertvisi and try to catch one there to [Vardzia](/europe/georgia/vardzia).

From Akhalkalaki you can cross into [Armenia](/europe/armenia) to Gyumri. There are minibuses and taxis. A taxi to [Gyumri](/europe/armenia/gyumri) should cost about 20 dollars.
