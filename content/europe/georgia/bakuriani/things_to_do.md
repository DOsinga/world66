---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Bakuriani is primarily a skiing and snowboarding destination in winter, with a handful of runs served by lifts on the slopes above the village. The resort is unpretentious and affordable by any standard. In summer, the surrounding Trialeti forests open up for hiking and mountain walks, and the cooler altitude makes Bakuriani a popular escape from the Georgian lowland heat.
