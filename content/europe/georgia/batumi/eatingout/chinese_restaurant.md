Chinese Restaurant in Batumi is located in very nice place. Near the sea in the middle of lake, actually on the island, The food is really delicious and the main thing is that you really feel China, because the restaurant itself is build with Chinese style, like Chinese Pagoda.

Service is really good, and the prices are fair.

At night you can see dancing fountains in the lake. which is very spectacular too. Or after dinner you can take a walk to seafront promenade.
