The museum was established in 1998, it houses paintings of well-known Georgian and foreign artists (Niko Pirosmani, D. Kakabadze, L. Gudiashvili, E. Akhvlediani, S. Bakalovich, A. Zommer, A. Tern, N. Ignatov, A. Zankovski, etc.). Graphic works and sculpture, as well as samples of decorative art (ceramics, woodcut, glasswork, engravings, tapestry).

The Museum collection: 500 items
