The State Art Museum of Adjara, established in 1998, holds works by major Georgian painters including Niko Pirosmani, Lado Gudiashvili, and Elena Akhvlediani, alongside foreign artists. The collection of around 500 items spans paintings, graphic works, sculpture, and decorative arts — ceramics, woodcut, glasswork, engravings, and tapestry. It is the main art gallery in the region.
