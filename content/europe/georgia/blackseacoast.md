---
title: "Black sea coast"
type: location
---

The black sea coast of Georgia deserves the name Riviera of the Black Sea. There are palm trees, goof sandy beaches and the wheather is always good. 

In the northern part of Georgia the political situation is not very inviting to tourists. It may take some time before this becomes a true tourist destination. 

In the south however, near [Batumi](/europe/georgia/batumi) all is clear and the good news is this is the best part of the coast. 19-th century architecture, boulevards, good beaches and pleasant temperatures.
