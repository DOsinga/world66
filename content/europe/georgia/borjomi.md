You will hear the name Borjomi everywhere in Georgia. It is the name of the mineral water and like Spa has become synonymous with mineral water.

Borjomi has a few sights especially the mineral waters park is worth a visit. It is also the place to go when visiting [Bakuriani](/europe/georgia/bakuriani) the skiing resort.
