---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

Gori is reachable from [Tbilisi](/europe/georgia/tbilisi) by bus, minibus and train. Both busses and minibusses leave frequently from the main station. The trip by minibus costs US$ 1.5 and takes slightly more than an hour. The busses are a bit cheaper but also take a little more time. Trains are irregular and extremely slow, but also very cheap.
