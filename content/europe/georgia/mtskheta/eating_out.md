Mtskheta is not big but there are a few options to get a decent meal within walking distance of the cathedral.

**Restaurants**

**Guga**

Nice restaurant, with different rooms, some with fireplace, where you can eat and drink. The menu doesn't have prices so you need to agree on this first. They do speak some english.

address: Mamulashvilis Kucha

PriceOfMenu: 5 US$
