1 or 2 days spent in this mountainous region will make a great
impression on you. These two brilliant complexes built in the middle
centuries and earlier are considered unique in the world by archaeologists and historians.
You
will also be impressed with the breathtaking mountain views. Your
palate also won't suffer, either. Here you can taste delicious trout,
prepared especially for you by our friend - an owner of one of the
local hotels.

The tour can be a daytrip but if you wish you can stay in one of Shatili's hotels and explore more of this breathtaking region.
