***Marco Polo*** consists of 4 floors: Banquet hall, restaurant, bar and tavern.

Diverse menu of Georgian, Oriental and European cuisines. Special menu for vegetarians. Variety of drinks and a really chilled atmosphere. Service is great too!!

They organize banquets, fourchettes, different group events, business meetings, lunches and celebration parties. They also have safe parking.
