A favourite place of Tbilisi citizens and foreign guests. You can rent a private room for 30 dollars for one hour (Royal Bath e.g.) or go to one of the public bath where a ticket is just one Lari. Massage sets you back a further 2,5 dollar. You can also rent towels for a small charge.

The baths are located at the foot of the Narikala, near the Mosque. Ask for Abunatobani.
