---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Tbilisi's sights mostly cluster in the **Old Town** (Kala), where a handful of surviving medieval churches sit between the Narikala Fortress on the hill and the sulphur baths at the foot. The main set-piece walk goes from Freedom Square, down Rustaveli Avenue past the parliament and the opera house, onto Metekhi Bridge, and into the warren of lanes in Kala itself.
