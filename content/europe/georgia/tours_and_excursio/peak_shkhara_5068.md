**Day 1.** Our guide will meet you in Tbilisi Airport with poster: "IRIDIUM TRAVEL". Luggage carriers will be at your service at Airport. Formalities at the Airport (visas, passport control, luggage delivery). Transfer Airport-Hotel (15 km) by comfortable minibus. Hotel. Light lunch during check-in (cool or hot drinks, sandwiches). Rest. Small sightseeing tour to Tbilisi. Supper.

* The plan of the 1st day may be changed, according to the time of your arrival. The Company will undertake all costs for extra cultural measures and meals

**Day 2.** Breakfast at the hotel. Check-out. Set out at 9 a.m. in comfortable minibus. Travel to the province Svaneti, north-west Georgia. Tbilisi-Mestia (480 km, 9-10 hour). Dinner in the road. Arrival to the capital of province Svaneti, Mestia, Dislocation in guesthouse. Supper.

**Day 3.** Breakfast. Set out. Mestia-Ushguli (45 km, 2-3 hour). Village Ushguli. The highest populated point in Europe (2200 m). Dislocation in guesthouse. Dinner. Inventory of equipment and food, preparation, instructions. Supper.

**Day 4.** Breakfast. Set out to Camp 1 (2800 m). Rest. Dinner. Arrival to Camp 1. Bottom of the peak. Camp break. Supper. Stay for a night in a camp.

**Day 5.** Breakfast. Set out. Stay for a night at the bottom of a peak. Camp 2. Thorough meal according to the favorable situation.

**Day 6.** Continuation of a route. Camp 3. Stay for a night on the plateau.

**Day 7.** Continuation of a route. Camp 4. Stay for a night on the plateau.

**Day 8.** Ascend to the mountain. Descend. Stay for a night in camp 4.

**Day 9.** Descend. Camp 4 – camp 1.

**Day 10.** Descend to Ushguli. Supper. Night in guesthouse.

**Day 11.** Wake up at 11 a.m. Breakfast. Sightseeing to Ushguli. Departure to Mestia, Dinner. Stay for a night in Mestia

**Day 12.** Wake up at 4 p.m. Breakfast. Mestia-Tbilisi (480 km, 9-10 hour). Dinner in the road. Tbilisi. Visit to sulfur baths, the legend about foundation of Tbilisi is connected with. Dislocation in hotel. Farewell supper.

**Day 13.** Reserve. In case the reserve day is not used during ascend to the peak, the Company will offer you historical-informational tour.

**Day 14.** Breakfast in hotel. Check-out. Transfer Hotel-Airport.

* The plans for this day might be changed, according to the time of flight. The company id responsible for arranging additional events and covering food expenses.
