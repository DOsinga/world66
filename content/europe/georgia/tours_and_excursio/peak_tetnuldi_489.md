Alpine Tour for None Professionals

The Great Caucasus. Georgia. Province Svaneti.

Peak Tetnuldi. 4894m. Category 2A.
Season July-August 2005. 10 days.

**Day 1.** Our guide will meet you in Tbilisi Airport with poster: "IRIDIUM TRAVEL". Luggage carriers will be at your service at Airport. Formalities at the Airport (visas, passport control, luggage delivery). Transfer Airport-Hotel (15 km) by comfortable minibus. Hotel. Light lunch during check-in (cool or hot drinks, sandwiches). Rest. Small sightseeing tour to Tbilisi. Supper.

* The plan of the 1st day may be changed, according to the time of your arrival. The Company will undertake all costs for extra cultural measures and meals

**Day 2.** Breakfast at the hotel. Check-out. Set out at 9 a.m. in comfortable minibus. Travel to the province Svanei, north-west Georgia. Tbilisi-Mestia (480 km, 9-10 hour). Dinner in the road. Arrival to the capital of province Svaneti -Mestia. Dislocation in guesthouse. Supper.

**Day 3.** Breakfast. Inventory of equipment and food. Preparation, instructions. Lunch. Start to the mountain foot. Camp 1. Camp break. Supper, Night in tents.

**Day 4.** Breakfast. Road to Camp 2. Rest. Dinner. Arrival to Camp 2. Plateau. Camp break.. Supper. Night in tents.

**Day 5.** Breakfast. Continuation of a route. Camp 3. Thorough meal according to situation.

**Day 6.** Ascend to the mountain. Descend. Stay for a night in camp 2.

**Day 7.** Descend to Mestia. Supper. Stay for a night in guest-house.

**Day 8.** Wake up at 4 a.m. Breakfast. Departure by minibus. Mestia-Tbilisi (480 km, 9-10 hour). Dinner in the road. Tbilisi. Visit to sulfur baths, the legend about foundation of Tbilisi is connected with. Dislocation in hotel. Farewell supper.

**Day 9.** Reserve. In case the reserve day is not used during ascend to the peak, the Company will offer you historical-informational tour.

**Day 10.** Breakfast in hotel. Check-out. Transfer Hotel-Airport.

• The plans for this day might be changed, according to the time of flight. The company id responsible for arranging additional events and covering food expenses.
