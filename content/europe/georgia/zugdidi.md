Zugdidi is the main city of the Samegrelo region in western [Georgia](/europe/georgia), historically the capital of the Mingrelian principality. The star attraction is the Dadiani Palace, now a museum housing an eclectic collection that includes one of Napoleon's three death masks — a relic of the marriage between a Dadiani prince and Napoleon's niece.

The city is the usual jumping-off point for trips to Mestia and the Svaneti highlands, and sits close to the Enguri Dam and the crossing point into Abkhazia. Zugdidi itself is a low-key, friendly place with a large botanical garden surrounding the palace.
