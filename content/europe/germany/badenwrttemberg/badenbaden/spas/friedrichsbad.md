A magnificent nineteenth-century bathhouse with a strict 17-step programme that takes about three hours. You leave everything in a locker and proceed through dry saunas, a soap-and-brush scrub, steam rooms, and a series of thermal pools — the centrepiece is a warm pool under a beautiful painted dome. Some sections are single-sex, others mixed; all are textile-free. Regimented but extraordinary. You leave feeling like a new person.
