Travel Guide

Baden-Baden is a little spa town in Germany near the western
foothills of the Black Forest that was built on a thermal springs.
There are many things to do in this little town but while you are there
it is worth it to check out some of the places you can travel to in a
day.

**Schwarzwaldhochstraße**

The Schwarzwaldhochstraße is the road that winds 65 km through the
Black Forest. Make sure to have your camera ready because there are more
picturesque scenes behind every bend than you can imagine.

**Strasbourg**

Strasbourg is nearby and has a famous historic old town and a
beautiful cathedral. You might feel like you are in France because
there is a definite French flavor to this town. This is about 55 km
and only 1 hour 30 minutes by train. The Ruins of All Saint’s Abbey is
in Wurttemberg which was a monastery built around 1192 is nearby about
50 km from Baden Baden. Fires hit the abbey in the 1500’s and the final
one that forces all inhabitants out was in 1804. There are some pretty
impressive waterfalls under the abbey.

**Rastatt**

Rastatt, (15 km can be reached by bus) has two palaces that can be
visited as well as a military museum and in Pforzheim you will find a
jewelry museum. Don’t miss the historic market place in Freudenstadt
(30 km can take bus) and in Karlsruhe 40 km can take a bus) visit
another palace, museum and a zoo.

**Heidelberg and Freiburg**

Heidelberg and Freiburg are about 100 km away and you can take a
train to get there. In Heidelberg there is a working castle to visit
(Schloss Heidelberg). Building of the castle started in the 1300’s and
it hasn’t stopped yet. You get a guided tour and might be able to
participate in wine tasting. Visit the Freiburg Münster or Cathedral of
Freiburg to climb the tower and get a grand view of the city as well as
seeing architecture from 3 different eras in history.

**Europa Park**

Europa Park is 77 km away and takes you through the history of
Germany in an informative and fun way. It is great for the whole family
and has a few rides, a castle, gardens, fountains, German antique fair,
and much more.

There is certainly a lot you can do just a short way from Baden
Baden. Visit all the castles, drive through the Black Forest and see
all the sites in the region.
