---
title: "Top 5 Must Do's in Black Forest"
type: section
---

The mysterious Black Forest offers many activities to those who
visit. Here are the top five must do’s while in the Black Forest:

**Hiking Tours**

One thing travelers must do in the Black Forest is hike. There are many hiking
tours available that can last anywhere from a few hours to many hours or
days. Hiking in the forest has been going on for over 100 years and
that is evident as the trails are well marked. One place to visit is
the Gutach River where water slips and slides over 600 feet of seven
steps that were created naturally.

**Baths Near Baden-Baden**

After that hike you may want to indulge in the baths near Baden-Baden. These are spas that have been around for about 125 years. There are 16
stages to go through from air baths, steam baths, whirlpool baths, and
cold baths all designed to refresh your body and mind. In most baths
no clothing is worn, but everything seems very non sexual.There are several that will allow you to wear a bathing suit. The experience runs about €30 but can be less in some cases.

**German Clock Museum**

This is cuckoo clock territory and you will find them in almost
every shop. Visit the German Clock museum (Deutches Uhrenmuseum) in
Furtwangen. Here you will see the world’s largest historical clock
collection and on the hour there is quite a cacophony of sound coming
from all those clocks.

**Open Air Mueseum**

Another must do in the Black Forest is the Open Air Museum
(Freilichtmuseum Vogtsbauernhof). Visitors can visit actual Black
Forest farmhouses and see local artisans busy at work doing the jobs of
yesterday. The buildings are built from the wood of the forest and are
old style with the living room in the middle of the house to symbolize
the center of the family. Kitchens have a tiled stoves across from
which is the Hergottswinkle or "Place of God" with a small altar. Hand
carved saints, along with the Virgin Mary, are usually placed there. These homes are handed down from generation to generation.

**Freiburg Cathedral**

Freiburg Cathedral is a definite must do in the Black Forest. Outside the spire is most beautiful looking like hand carved lace. Inside is just as memorable with many sculptures in wood and stone,
paintings, and stained glass. The Cathedral was built in the middle
ages and there are several chapels behind the high carved altar and
there are four pipe organs.

Visit this place of mystery and beauty and don’t miss the above attractions and activities.
