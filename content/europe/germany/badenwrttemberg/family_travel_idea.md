**Baden-Wurttemberg family travel** can be full of adventure and fun. Here are some great Baden-Wurttemberg family travel ideas that will keep all members of the family entertained. With festivals, theme parks and family-friendly lodging available, coming up with Baden-Wurttemberg family travel ideas will not be a difficult thing to do

**African Summer Festival**

This summer festival will delight the entire family. Shopping for handcrafted African rugs and jewelry, the shoppers will find plenty to feast their eyes on while the kids visit their own little corner of the market. The children's area of the festival is an experience that the kids will talk about from crafts they made themselves to learning a little bit of music. This festival is the place to visit for the entire family to enjoy musical and dance performances and maybe get involved in a drumming work shop.

**Black Forest Open Air Museum**

With real houses, workshops and various other buildings to wander through, this activity is fun for all ages. Children will enjoy making their way through the old farmhouses, seeing the bedrooms of the children who lived there previously and delight in the artifacts that adorn each building. Hike the many trails through the Black Forest and maybe acquire a cuckoo clock along the way. All members of the family can have hands-on experience with this delightful family venture.

**Theme Parks**

Roller coasters, fast thrilling rides and games are always a favorite among all members of the family. With approximately 7 theme parks to choose from, the family should have no problem spending an entire day with adventures through a Jurassic theme to an aquatic theme. The only question would be which theme park to visit on which day. Always check to make sure if the theme park you want to visit is open.

**Mercedes Benz Museum Stuttgart**

Explore the great world of luxury cars and thrilling racing while you tour this fantastic museum. The building itself is a sight worth looking at. This building is something out of the future with all of the glass that surrounds the odd shaped building. The kids will think they are entering into a space ship filled with cars. They might even get a kick out of the horse that is on display among many fine automobiles. It is definitely worth the visit as a family.
