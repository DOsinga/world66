**general**

**Munsterplatz**

Some old buildings remain on Munsterplatz and around the town hall. Here one of the most diverting markets is hold on a daily basis.

**Munster**

For a fantastic view climb the tall spire of the Munster tower right in the center of town. 

**Cathedral**

Beautiful Gothic cathedral with pretty stained-glass windows inside.  

**Schwaben Tor**

One of the two surviving towers of the medieval fortifications. 

**Kaufhaus**

This blood-red building from the 16th century dominates the south side of the square and served once as a merchants’ hall. 

**University**

The city’s university dates from the 17th century and there are more than 20 000 students in town.
