---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

The market square and the Stadtkirche are worth an hour on arrival; after that, point yourself at the forest. The Parkwald nature preserve begins at the edge of town and offers trails for all levels, from gentle valley walks to longer ridge routes. The Schwarzwald Hochstrasse north toward Baden-Baden is one of the finest drives in Germany and gives good access to trailheads along the way.
