Getting around in Heidelberg is not difficult, distances are mostly small, for example you can easily walk between most sights in Old Town.

**all**

**Public Transport**

All parts of the city are well served by public transport. It is managed by VRN, a regional traffic organisation. At the two major stations Hauptbahnhof (railway station) and Bismarckplatz there are offices to get directions or general assistance. **Taxi**

Many taxi stations throughout the city make this the easiest transportation. To call a taxi, dial 06221/302030.
