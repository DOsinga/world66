There is an excellent and detailed city map available, which has so far only description in German. 

Nevertheless, it is easy to use by clicking through it. Explanation: Nord - North, Ost - East, Süd - South, and West - West. 



If you are looking for a particular street, you can click on the letters at the top of the page, like "u" for Untere Straße. Then you will see a listing of all streets beginning with u. Click on the desired street name, and you will find the appropriate section of the map.
