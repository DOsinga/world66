Karlsruhe is a major tech and industry town. Owing to its even architectural features, Karlsruhe reflects the spirit of rationalism. Founded as a Grand-Ducal residential city, Karlsruhe accommodates the oldest technological university of Germany. Nowadays, the city is a "Residence of Law" that comprises the supreme German courts of justice.

Karlsruhe can be used as a basis for exploring the [Black Forest](/europe/germany/badenwrttemberg/blackforest).
