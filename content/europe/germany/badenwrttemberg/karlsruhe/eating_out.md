---
title: "Eating Out"
type: section
---

Karlsruhe offers good value eating, particularly around the university district. The Kaiserstraße and its side streets have a mix of cafes, cheap lunch spots, and sit-down restaurants.

**Kippe** (Gottesaurstraße 34) — a student favourite serving good, cheap meals in the university neighbourhood.

**Cafe Emaille** (Kaiserstraße, near Kaiserpassage) — a reliable cafe on the main shopping street, good for a coffee and a break from sightseeing.
