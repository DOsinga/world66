Karlsruhe is Germany's "Residence of Law." The Federal Constitutional Court (Bundesverfassungsgericht) and the Federal Court of Justice (Bundesgerichtshof) are both headquartered here — their imposing buildings in the western city centre reflect the city's unusual role as a judicial capital. The courts are working institutions, not museums, but the buildings are worth passing on a walk through the neighbourhood.
