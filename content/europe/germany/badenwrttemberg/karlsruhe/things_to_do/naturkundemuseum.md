At Friedrichsplatz on Erbprinzenstraße 13, the Naturkundemuseum — the State Museum of Natural History — holds an extensive collection of flora and fauna specimens alongside a large range of prehistoric objects. A solid choice for families and anyone interested in the region's natural environment.
