Housed in a Neo-Renaissance building constructed between 1843 and 1846, the Staatliche Kunsthalle holds one of Baden's finest art collections, focused on German and Dutch painting from the 16th to 19th centuries. Grünewald's famous *Crucifixion* (1523) is the headline work — one of the most striking late-Gothic paintings in Germany.
