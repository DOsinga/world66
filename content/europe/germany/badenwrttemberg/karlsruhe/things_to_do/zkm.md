The Zentrum für Kunst und Medientechnologie is widely regarded as Europe's leading museum for media art. Housed in a converted munitions factory, it combines a permanent exhibition on the history of media art with constantly changing temporary shows — always worth checking the programme before you visit. The approach is hands-on: visitors become part of the art, filmed live and inserted into video works. The interactive format makes it as engaging for children as for adults, with discounts widely available.
