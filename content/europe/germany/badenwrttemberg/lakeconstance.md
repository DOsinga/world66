The Lake Constance - "Bodensee" is the German word - is located between Germany and Switzerland. On the Swiss side are the famous Rhine falls, best reached from Schaffhausen. The lake area has one of the warmest summer microclimates in the country. 

There are many outdoor activities that one can do including water sports sailing and hiking. 

There are lots of pretty resort towns along its shores notably Meersburg which is surrounded by vineyards and which has half-timbered houses and the oldest castle in Germany. 

You can alos visit the island of Mainau (lush tropical vegetation) at the northern end of the lake or Lindau at the southern end if you have a chance. There is ferry service across the lake from Friedrichshafen into Switzerland -- End of General Section
