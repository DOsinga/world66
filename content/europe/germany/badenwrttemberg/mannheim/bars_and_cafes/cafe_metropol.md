A central café that opens early and stays open late, good for breakfast through to a late glass of wine. The interior is generous and slightly worn in a comfortable way. On weekday mornings it fills with a mix of students, freelancers, and people reading newspapers — a reliable indicator that the coffee is worth having. Occasional live music in the evenings.
