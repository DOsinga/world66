---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

**By train** is the most practical option for most visitors. Mannheim Hauptbahnhof is a major junction on the Frankfurt–Basel and Frankfurt–Paris lines, with frequent ICE services. Frankfurt is 35 minutes away; Stuttgart is 35 minutes; Paris is about 3 hours on the TGV via the Strasbourg route. Heidelberg is a 12-minute S-Bahn ride away.

**By air** the nearest major airports are Frankfurt (FRA), about 70 km north, and Stuttgart (STR), about 90 km south. Both are well connected to Mannheim by train. Karlsruhe/Baden-Baden Airport is smaller and closer (about 50 km) but has fewer connections.

**By car** Mannheim sits at the intersection of the A6 (east–west) and A656 motorways, with good connections in all directions. Parking in the city centre is metered and can be expensive; several multi-storey car parks operate near the main station and the palace.
