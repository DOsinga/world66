A traditional Pfälzer wine tavern in the old market area, one of the more established options for regional cooking. Saumagen (the local stuffed stomach dish that was Helmut Kohl's favourite) appears on the menu, as do Flammkuchen and various seasonal specials. The wine list focuses on Palatinate whites — Riesling and Grauburgunder — which pair well with the food. Busy on weekend evenings; worth booking.
