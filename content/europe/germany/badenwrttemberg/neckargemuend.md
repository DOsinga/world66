Neckargemünd is a small town on the Neckar river, about 12 kilometres upstream from Heidelberg. It makes a pleasant half-day excursion — the old town is compact enough to walk in an hour, and the riverside setting is quietly attractive. The easiest way to arrive is by boat from Heidelberg (the tour takes around two hours) or by public bus number 35.
