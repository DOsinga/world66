title: "Schonwald im Schwarzwald"
type: location
---

Enjoy the beauty of nature, the romantic villages, and top European
culture. All in the Black Forest (Schwarzwald) - South West Germany.

The little town of Schonwald is a German health resort (Kur-Ort) ;
well known for alpine & cross-country in the winter, as well as
excellent hiking & mountain biking in the warm summers. The area is
also dotted with health spas, saunas and wellness centers. Golf,
Horse-back riding, Tennis, etc. all near-by.

A beautiful place to relax for you and your family. Away from it
all, but still close to the action. Enjoy the active outdoors in Summer
and Winter, and the old cities of Freiburg (Germany), Strassbourg
(France) and Zurich & Basel (Switzerland), with histories dating
back to 950 AD.

Day trip opportunities include ; the village of Triberg with the
highest waterfall in Germany, Europark (family fun-park), Cuckoo-clock
shops, Museums, The wine area of the Upper Rhine Valley / Alsace (and
only 2 hrs from the Munich Oktober Fest). Nearby crystal-clear lakes;
such as TitiSee, SchluchtSee and BodenSee, Flowerpark Mainau, The
source of the river Donau (Danube), Old steam train rides...and many
more. This is also a place for good food and drink, including the
famous "Schwarzwalder Kirschtorte" (Black Forest cake), and a vast
choice of local beers and wines ; Enjoy our favourite Gasthof Rossle in
the valley ; they have it all.
