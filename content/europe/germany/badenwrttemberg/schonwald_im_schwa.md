Enjoy the beauty of nature, the romantic villages, and top European
culture. All in the Black Forest (Schwarzwald) - South West Germany.

The little town of Schonwald is a German health resort (Kur-Ort) ;
well known for alpine & cross-country in the winter, as well as
excellent hiking & mountain biking in the warm summers. The area is
also dotted with health spas, saunas and wellness centers. Golf,
Horse-back riding, Tennis, etc. all near-by.
