Amadeus is a long-running bar and restaurant right on Charlottenplatz in Stuttgart-Mitte, within easy walking distance of the Altes Schloss and the main shopping streets. The interior leans into a classic European cafe style with dark wood and warm lighting. It draws an after-work crowd during the week and fills up on weekend evenings. The menu covers solid German and international bistro fare alongside a full cocktail list.
