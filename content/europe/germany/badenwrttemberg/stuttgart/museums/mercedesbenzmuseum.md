The museum islocated in the large factory-area, from the "Cannstatter Tor" (Entrance from Bad Cannstatt)near the Busstop "Martin-Schrenk-Weg" and you can take a free busride to the Museum and back The Mercedes-Benz Museum in Stuttgart, Germany is a major attraction to visitors from all over the world. It displays the complete history of the automobile from the very first car to the latest high-tech vehicle.
