Stuttgart's thermal bath complex in the Berg neighbourhood, fed by the city's natural mineral springs. The Berg bath is the oldest and most traditional of Stuttgart's three public mineral baths, with indoor and outdoor pools using natural spring water. A classic Stuttgart institution.
