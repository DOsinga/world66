A fixture on the Konigstrasse, Zenit is a cocktail bar that draws a mixed crowd of after-work locals and weekend revelers. The location right on Stuttgart's main pedestrian shopping street makes it easy to find, and the drink list is solid without being pretentious. A reliable spot for an evening out in the city center.
