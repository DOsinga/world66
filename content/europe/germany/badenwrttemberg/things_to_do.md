**Baden-Wurttemberg things to do** offer the traveler a variety of activities, festivals and forests to hike. Shopping is never in short supply and neither is the beer or food. In Baden-Wurttemberg things to do include:

**Cannstatter Wassen Beer Festivals**

The Cannstatter Volkfest is held every year for 2 weeks at the Cannstatter Wasen festival area. It is held 2 times a year,in autumn, 1 week after Oktoberfest in Munich and a smaller festival in the spring. This unofficial beer fest started out as a trade fair and has evolved into good beer, great food and fun to be had by all.
