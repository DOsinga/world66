---
title: "Top 5 Must Do's in Baden-Wurttemberg"
type: section
---

**Baden-Wurttemberg Must do's** will not only keep the traveler entertained and active, they will keep the visitor there until the very last moment. The traveler will vie for Baden-Wurttemberg must do's at the beginning of their journey and will want to end the holiday making sure they see every last possible thing there is to see.

**Black Forest Open Air Museum**

Baden-Wurttemberg offers many wonderful museums and at the top of the list should be the Black Forest Open Air Museum. This museum boasts of 20 authentic buildings that you can wander through and get a sense of what it was like to live in that time period. Visitors from all ages enjoy the authentic artifacts and wandering through the buildings. The Black Forest Open Air Museum is definitely a must.

**The Black Forest**

A trip to Baden-Wurttemberg is never complete without traipsing through the Black Forest. Here you will find lush greenery, smells of the woods and ferns and sites of cuckoo clocks. Take an adventure through the lovely forest taking paths that will lead the visitor to quiet areas, small food stalls and even cuckoo clocks. It is worth every minute the traveler spends in this wonderful mysterious forest.

**Cannstatter Wassen Beer Festivals**

If the traveler finds themselves in the neighborhood during Oktoberfest, pop on over the border and experience the Cannstatter Wassen Beer Festival. Originally started as a agricultural trade fair, this beer festival has evolved into a good time for all. Cannstatter Wassen Beer Festival is held twice a year. A smaller version in the spring time and the full onslaught in the winter as Baden-Wurttemberg gear up for the Christmas Market.

**Freiburg**

Munsterplatz is the largest square in Freiburg and is home to a fabulous farmer's market that is open 6 days a week and packed with true German culture. The traveler can dine on authentic German sausages and beer and a view of the Munster. One cannot miss this grand building, with its red sandstone structure, as its aggressive stature announces its presence to the square.

**Stuttgart **

Who can resist a visit to this grand city? With Mercedes-Benz museum located here, this is a great place to visit. The Mercedes-Benz Museum building looms out like something from the future. With its glass and silver layers, this museum holds the great cars of its past and present. While in Stuttgart, take in the shopping on Konigstrasse or fun parks like the Das Grune U get giddy as the joyous spirit of this city works its magic.
