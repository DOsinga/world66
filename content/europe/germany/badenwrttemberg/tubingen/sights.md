**St. Georg**

The Gothic Church of St. Georg is located on the Holzamrkt ans has a very beautiful interior. The chancel has stunning stained-glass windows. You can climb the tower with the 2 DM ticket.

**Markt**

The Markt is the old heart of Tubingen. The houses around it are excellent. The fountain of Neptune in the middle is the centre around which markets are held on mondays, wednesdays and fridays.

**Schloss**

On the place of a medieval castle dating back to the 11-th century, now stand the Renaissance Schloss Hohentubingen. It contains a museum (Schausammlung der Universitat) you can visit for 4 DM and there are guided tours.** gabriella volpi:** 

**Burgsteige**

The Burgsteige is a small street leading from the Markt to the Schloss Hohentubingen. Very handsome, with many nice renaissance houses and mansions.
