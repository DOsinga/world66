title: "Eating Out in Ulm"
type: section

Ulm is a quaint city situated on the River Danube offering its tourists rich history and great **Ulm**** restaurants**.
Situated in the German Bundesland of Baden-Wuttemberg, Ulm is a famous
tourist destination. It is known for its historical monuments,
universities, and the church with the tallest steeple in the world. It
is also the birth place of Albert Einstein. Given these attractions and
the many tourists who visit Ulm every year, there are several
restaurants in Ulm to cater to their needs.

**Brewery Barfuber Ulm**

The Brewery Barfuber is located in the heart of the city of Ulm.
This is a great brewery restaurant offering amazing German cuisine and
a great choice of beer and other drinks. The restaurant is warm and
welcoming and the staff is friendly. Do try out their selection of
brewed beer, which is something that you cannot afford to miss.

**Bella Vista**

Another great restaurant, located in the heart of the city, Bella
vista offers its guests an amazing choice of international cuisine.
Situated on the 4th floor, the restaurant offers its guests amazing an
view of the Ulm cathedral. The restaurant has a Mediterranean flair to
it and is well lit and warm. This is a perfect place to be for that
special dinner or meal with a loved one. You can choose to dine on
their terrace, even during winters. Their ingenious heating technology
keeps you warm. You can also enjoy your drink or tapas at their bar
which has a very cozy atmosphere.

If you are visiting Ulm, stopping by some of the fine **Ulm restaurants** is highly recommended.
Ulm is located on the River Danube, at the point where River Iller and River
Blau meet the Danube. With its long history, Ulm is the perfect spot for a holiday.
Visit some of these restaurants while staying at Ulm to experience firsthand
the unique cuisine and hospitality. Here are a few recommendations for you.

**Alexandre**

Alexandre occupies the Old Town Hall in the city center and
is a restaurant that is popular with many people. The restaurant serves a
variety of food and is open for breakfast, lunch and dinner. The restaurant is
not extravagant but serves quality food and is in fact crowded most of the
time. The restaurant is a good place to sit back and enjoy a few glasses of
beer over lunch or dinner. Alexandre is open 9:00 a.m. to 1:00 a.m. during the
week and until 2:00 a.m. during the weekends.

**Ristorante Bei
Salvatore**

If you prefer Italian
food while holidaying in Ulm, don’t worry. Ristorante Bei Salvatore will come
to your rescue. This is an Italian restaurant that serves quality Italian
cuisine in Ulm. The pizzas here are amazingly delicious. The restaurant is
reasonably priced and is a nice place to enjoy some Italian food over Italian
music.

**Barfusser**

Barfusser is located on the banks of the River Danube. This
restaurant specializes in serving authentic German food. The restaurant, apart
from serving tasty dishes, also has a wide range of beers. You will have to
place your order at the counter and pay there for the order, when it is ready it will
be delivered to your table. The restaurant is a great place to enjoy a fine
German meal over a few glasses of beer.

**Drei Kannen**

This is another restaurant that serves authentic German food.
If you enjoy the outdoors, you will like this place. The restaurant has a neat
backyard where you can sit and enjoy your lunch and a few glasses of fine beer.
The restaurant is part of a Bavarian brewery.
This is a great place to enjoy a meal with your family and take in the
beautiful scenery.

**Weinkrüger**

If you are looking for a place that has a wider range of
dishes at a reasonable price, Weinkrüger is the place for you. The restaurant
serves large portions and the food is cooked to perfections. The restaurant
offers quality service to its guests and the staff is very friendly. The restaurant
is located in Fischerviertel – a popular area in Ulm.
