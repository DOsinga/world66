This **Ulm Festivals** guide will help you decide when to go so you wont miss any of these great events.
**
Ulmer Zelt (Ulm Tent Festival)**

Held from May 19 until July 3, this festival offers national and international rock, jazz, blues and pop bands as well as comedy, vaudeville and cabaret acts, all under a huge circus tent that can accommodate 1000 people. You can also visit a gastronomy tent with a beer garden, and there is a great children program. Tickets need to be booked in advance.

**Serenade of Lights**

On July 17th, thousands of candles are floated along the river accompanied by soft music and tall water columns to create a romantic scenery.

**Schworfeier and Nabada Celebrations**

The Schworfeier (Oath Celebrations takes place in the morning of July
19th). The Ulm Mayor speaks about the past year and offers his prognostic
of the year to come and he swears an oath to uphold the town's
