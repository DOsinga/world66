**Ulm must do's** include visiting both religious and historical sites. Ulm is located on the Danube River, and has a rich history and is
a center for science and technology. Albert Einstein was born here in
1879. Ulm has a commercial river port which has contributed to the
areas wealth. In Ulm the old fuses with the new and historical
monuments can be viewed in conjunction with modern constructions.
Visitors can lose themselves down quaint alleys or go for a walk down
the modern promenades along the riverbank.

**Ulm Cathedral**

This magnificent cathedral is a popular tourist attraction in Ulm. The construction of the cathedral was started in 1377 and
completed in 1891. Ulm Cathedral has the highest steeple in the world reaching a height of 161.53 meters. To reach the top you have to climb 768 steps. The
towers, top story and spire offer different views of the cathedral
where you can admire the intricacy of the cathedral design form a
different angle. The spires also offer magnificent views of the city. Inside the
church you can see stained glass windows, choir loft carvings and
