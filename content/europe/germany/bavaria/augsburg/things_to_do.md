title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Augsburg's highlights cluster in the compact Altstadt, easily covered on foot in a day. The Maximilianstrasse and its Renaissance fountains set the stage, but the real draws are the Fuggerei, the cathedral, and a clutch of museums spanning Roman archaeology, Mozart, Brecht, and Bavarian art.
