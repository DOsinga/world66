**Enjoy a drink at Cafe Bar Holzk&auml;fer in Berchtesgaden
**

Of all **Berchtesgaden Bars and Cafes**, Cafe Bar Holzk&auml;fer is definitely an establishment worth visiting. The bar is located on Buchenh&ouml;he Street, very close to the Berchtesgaden Asthma Treatment Center. It is opened every day (except Tuesday) from 2 p.m. and works up to the late evening hours. The place is ideal for having a snack in the afternoon or a drink in the evening.

**The Atmosphere
**

Caf&eacute; Bar Holzk&auml;fer has a really warm and friendly atmosphere that attracts the attention of many tourists and locals. The bar is situated inside a lovely mountain hut. Wooden walls and posts, stone floor, a fireplace and many traditional decorations, create a feeling for authentic Bavarian folklore among the visitors. During the summer, you can sit at a little table on the bar's beautiful terrace, surrounded by many lovely flowers and having the chance to admire some breath-taking mountain views. The terrace gives you the chance to enjoy some fascinating summer sunsets. Unlikely for a typical bar in the mountain, Caf&eacute; Bar Holzk&auml;fer is equipped with an excellent baby-changing room.

**The Menu
**

Cafe Bar Holzk&auml;fer serves some excellent wine, beer and schnapps (a strong local alcohol), which are an excellent choice for an evening drink. In the afternoon, you can try some well-prepared tea or coffee. To complement them, you should certainly try one of the delicious home-made cakes. Some great ice specialties are available in the summer as well.
