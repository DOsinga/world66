---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment in Berchtesgaden"
type: section
---

**Berchtesgaden nightlife** consists of bars, restaurants and going to the theater. The municipality of Berchtesgaden is located in the Bavarian Alps of Germany. This small and charming town is full of history, and travelers visiting the place will often soak in the different natural wonders of the place. However, there is more to Berchtesgaden than mountain climbing and nature tours.

At night, the municipality of Berchtesgaden comes to life featuring different bars, clubs and night entertainment that caters to different people.

#### Nightclubs and Bars

There are a few nightclubs in Berchtesgaden suitable for travelers looking for a night of music and dancing. One can also find several good bars in some of the hotels. The Watzmann Bar in Alpenhotel serves a wide range of wines and beers. The hotel also houses the Bauernstube, that is more causal featuring a tiled oven.

#### Local Restaurants

Travelers can find several restaurants in Berchtesgaden serving traditional and international cuisine. However, when visiting this picturesque city, it is highly recommended that you try their local restaurants and have a taste of their local cuisine. The Gasthof Goldener B&auml;r is one restaurant that travelers should visit. This restaurant serves Bavarian cuisine and is a quaint restaurant that only has a seating capacity for 68 persons.

#### Cafes

After dining out, one can sit and relax at one of the cafes scattered around the city. There are several authentic Berchtesgaden cafes serving coffee, as well as Italian coffee paired with German cake and pastries. Caf&eacute;-Forstner is a local caf&eacute; that serves affordable food items. For a relaxing night, grab a chair at their outdoor seating and have a cup of good coffee. Caf&eacute; Spiesberger is another location known for the different types of chocolate candies they serve.

#### Bistros

There are also several bistros in the area that have a casual setting. These bistros often provide some local drinks, particularly Wei&szlig;bier which is a wheat beer that is often served with sweet mustard and Wei&szlig;wurst.

Gasthof Bier Adma is local favorite which serves Bavarian cuisine. The Gasthof Bier Adma, just like most of the bistros in the area, closes at around 1a.m. There are a few that operate later.

#### Theaters

For a little bit of art and culture, travelers can go to the local theaters. Although some are spoken with the local dialect, seeing the local plays is a fun experience in itself. Visit Kurlichtspiele or Berchtesgadener Bauerntheater, and enjoy a night at the opera.
