---
title: "Shopping in Berchtesgaden"
type: section
---

**Berchtesgaden shopping** includes shops that carry great gifts, unique toys and clothes. With out-of-the-way places to popular tourist attractions, finding a shopping experience can be exciting. Berchtesdgaden shopping can be done at the following places.

**Eagles Nest**

There is a great little gift shop here where you have great shopping opportunities to buy a book or 2 for friends at home, badges for the kids or stickers as well as other trinkets. It is a small building and rather cozy, so round out your visit to the Eagles Nest with a purchase or 2 from this delightful gift shop.

**The Kiosk**

As well as post cards and guide books, t-shirts and beer steins, this shop also carries a video about the history of the Eagles Nest. Things cost about a euro more here, so if you don't mind paying a little extra, this shop has what you may be looking for.

**Hitler’s Old Dining Room**

This shop carries a variety of books and trinkets for the visitor to Berchtesgaden. With reasonable prices you can pick up a nice book for relaxing in the courtyard behind the Dom. Enjoy German history through the very informative books available at this great little gift shop.

**MaBanfertigung**

Famous brand names and traditional dress can be found in this unique store with jewelry, handbags and shoes with famous brand names. Traditional dress can be purchased here as well. With dirndl for the ladies and ledenhosen for the gentlemen, explore and enjoy the experience. Some brand name items can be a little expensive, but this store has plenty of merchandise the shopper can acquire for just a few euros.

**Kugelmuehle: Marbles**

Take a tour or simply wander around and look at the variety of marbles. This mill produces marbles and is only open in the summer season. Water is used to make these wonderful little objects. Learn how they make the marbles and how they get the stone with which to make the marbles. While the travelers are shopping for marbles they can also appreciate the building and its history.

**Black Forest**

The main trails in the Black Forest have little gift shops and cuckoo clock stalls. While the visitors are hiking through the woods they can shop for friends and family at home. They can also stop by any of these little shops and pick an authentic cuckoo clock.
