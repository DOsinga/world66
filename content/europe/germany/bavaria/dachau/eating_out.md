---
title: "Eating Out"
type: section
---

Dachau's dining scene is modest but solid Bavarian fare, with a few international options mixed in. The old town around Konrad-Adenauer-Strasse has the best concentration of restaurants.

**Zieglerbräu** is a local brewery and Bavarian restaurant on Konrad-Adenauer-Strasse 8, the obvious choice for traditional food and house-brewed beer. For Italian, **Bassano** on Jakob-Schmid-Strasse 44 serves reliable pizza and pasta. **Bräustüberl** on Schlossstrasse 8, near the castle, is another good spot for Bavarian classics in a traditional setting.
