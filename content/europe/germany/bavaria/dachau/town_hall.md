The current town hall complex dates from 1974, combining the old town hall with the facade of the historic Lebzelterhaus (gingerbread baker's house). The integration of the old facades into the modern block is worth a look, with stone sculptures by Reinhold Grübl decorating the exterior. On Konrad-Adenauer-Strasse 2-6, right in the centre of the old town.
