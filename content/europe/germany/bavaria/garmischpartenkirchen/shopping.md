---
title: "Shopping in Garmisch-partenkirchen"
type: section
---

Along with the many activities that can be enjoyed by the visitor walking the
streets of Garmisch-Partenkirchen is shopping. However, shopping in
Garmisch-Partenkirchen is not defined or reduced to a mall experience or being
exposed to the traditional shops that one is accustomed to. That is because a
shopping experience in Garmisch-Partenkirchen is an experience unto itself.

Specifically, Garmisch-Partenkirchen allures shoppers because the
town combines the flavors of the old Bavarian culture along with the contemporary
shopping experience that one can indulge in any city around the globe. Therefore, a hybrid of shopping experience exists and can be enjoyed
in this specific geographical location.

One of the venues of shopping is the streets and lanes of this city. A traveler will be
immediately struck with the special atmosphere due to the various
antique shops that populate the city and their offerings. Every traveler would want to possess these items or share
these items with loved ones at home as memories of a memorable excursion.

In addition, there are modern galleries that exhibit the contemporary art that can be described as sheer elegance.

Another shopping district is known as “historic Ludwigstrasse.” This
particular shopping area has its roots in trading that occurred over
1,000 years ago because it was a prominent trading center
and part of the merchandising route that dates back to the days of the Roman
Empire.

Examples of treasures that can be viewed, touched and purchased include
jewelry, footwear and clothing. In addition, traditional Bavarian wear can be
purchased new or gently used. In both cases, the craftsmanship is
sterling and a wonderful reflection of the skill and beauty of a rich and proud
culture.

The shopping areas have a number of quaint restaurants and
inns, some of which are historic, that can be enjoyed by all.
