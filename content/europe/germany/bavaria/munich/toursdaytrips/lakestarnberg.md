Munich's nearest lake is about 20 km long and 5 km wide. It is a popular place for day excursions. 20 km of the 49 km shoreline are reserved for recreational purposes only. Four ships sail on the lake. Starnberg can be reached from [Munich](/europe/germany/bavaria/munich) by public transport ("S-Bahn") within 45 minutes from the central station.
