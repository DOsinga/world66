---
title: "Bars and Cafes"
type: section
---

Nuremberg has a good spread of student pubs (Studentenkneipen) serving food and drink at reasonable prices, concentrated in the streets south and west of the old town. The old city itself picks up in the evening around the Hauptmarkt and along Karolinenstrasse. The city also has a strong live music scene, with several dedicated venues for rock and alternative music.
