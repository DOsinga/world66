A consistently popular bar in the Gostenhof neighbourhood west of the old town. The name translates roughly as "disturbance of the peace," and the atmosphere delivers on that promise most evenings. Gostenhof is Nuremberg's most alternative quarter, and Ruhestörung fits right in — expect a mixed crowd, reasonable drink prices, and a volume level that rises steadily as the night goes on.
