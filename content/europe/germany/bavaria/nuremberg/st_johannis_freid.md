One of the best-preserved Renaissance cemeteries in Germany, established in 1518. Albrecht Dürer is buried here, as is the sculptor Veit Stoss and the painter Anselm Feuerbach. The sandstone epitaphs and grave markers are works of art in their own right — it is worth walking the paths slowly.
