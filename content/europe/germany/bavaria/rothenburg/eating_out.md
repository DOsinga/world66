**general**

**Gasthof Butz**

Plain German food and traditional franconian dishes, cosy beer garden. If you drink too much, stay overnight in one of the nice hotel rooms. 

address: Kapellenplatz 4

tel: +49 9861 - 2201

zipcode: 91541 Rothenburg o.d.T.

**Gasthof Goldener Greifen**

Historical restaurant with 600 years tradition, plain German cooking. A charming small family hotel offers also nice rooms. 

address: Obere Schmiedgasse 5

tel: +49 9861 - 2281

zipcode: 91541 Rothenburg o.d.T.

**Goldener Hirsch**

Well known restaurant with blue terrace overlooking the Tauber valley. 

address: Untere Schmiedgasse 16/25

tel: +49 9861 - 7080

zipcode: 91541 Rothenburg o.d.T.

**Gasthof Linde**

Culinary specialties out of the Franconian kitchen. 

address: Vorm Wörzburger Tor 12

tel: +49 9861 - 7444

zipcode: 91541 Rothenburg o.d.T.

**Weinstube Pöbel**

Small wine restaurant with 24 seats, really cosy. They also have 3 hotel rooms. 

address: Kobolzellersteig 10

tel: +49 9861 - 1350

zipcode: 91541 Rothenburg o.d.T.

**Gasthof Schwarzes Lamm**

Romantic restaurant (and small hotel) run by the Friedle family, nice beer garden. 

address: Detwang 21, Rothenburg-Detwang

tel: +49 9861 - 6727

zipcode: 91541 Rothenburg o.d.T.

**Café Restaurant Spätzle Schwab**

Famous for their "Spätzle" (kind of egg noodles) and other regional specialties, especially cakes and pastry. 

address: Milchmarkt 6

tel: +49 9861 - 6068

zipcode: 91541 Rothenburg o.d.T.

**Gasthof Rödertor**

Franconian hospitality outside the old town (5 min walk to the market square), nice beer garden. Try the potatoe dishes. 

address: Ansbacher Strasse 7

tel: +49 9861 - 2022

zipcode: 91541 Rothenburg o.d.T.

**Gasthof Zum Ochsen**

Traditional restaurant with Franconian cooking (and hotel rooms as well) 

address: Galgengasse 26

tel: +49 9861 - 6760

zipcode: 91541 Rothenburg o.d.T.
