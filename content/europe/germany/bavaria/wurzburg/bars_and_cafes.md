**Enjoy an Evening in One of the Wurzburg Bars and Caf&eacute;s **

There are many **Wurzburg bars and caf&eacute;s** where you can enjoy the evening or the night out. Wurzburg is a city located in Bavaria, Germany and most of the bars and caf&eacute;s offer guests authentic German drinks and bar food. If you are here on vacation, you must take some time to try the drinks and delicious food in the bars and caf&eacute;s in this city. During the evenings, you will find several bars and caf&eacute;s filled with young people having a good time. Here is one place where you can enjoy the night out with your friends – or even make new friends.

**Loma**

Loma is a cocktail bar located in Sanderstra&szlig;e, Wurzburg. The bar was started by 5 friends; Carsten, Jochen, Lappa, Hobbo and Pippo and soon became a popular place for young people to hang-out and enjoy the night with their friends. The bar has a wide range of cocktails to choose from and has a nice comfortable atmosphere where you can feel at home and party late into the night.

**Specials**

The bar has popular DJs playing great music; most of the action takes place during the weekends. Saturday nights also have a special &ldquo;Cocktail Happy Hour.&rdquo; A great time to go to this bar is when one of the football (soccer) games is on. You will be able to enjoy the game over a few drinks – the action in the bar is very lively during these times.
