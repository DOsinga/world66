---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment in Wurzburg"
type: section
---

The majority of **Wurzburg nightlife** occurs in the wine cellars, known as Weinstuben. Wurzburg has plenty to do because it is the gateway for the romantic highway to Bavaria. There is something for everyone as you can enjoy a drink at a bar or club, go to concerts or see a movie. To see what is going on check out pamphlets and posters near the university or stop in at the tourist information.

**Music in the Evenings**

If you like jazz then you should go to Omnibus, the oldest jazz club in the city. This club is in the cellar and you can enjoy a drink and listen to excellent live jazz. It is open until 1 a.m. Mondays to Thursday and until 3 a.m. on Fridays and Saturdays. Standard is another club that has a live band on the weekends. You can get a cheap meal and something to drink. Gehrings offers famous potato-based dishes and they also live music. Stop by for potato cakes topped with cheese and veggies.

**Wine and Local Cuisine**

The most popular wine bar of them all is Weingut Juliusspital. The wine bar is open throughout the year and has one of the best wine collections in the city. This wine bar offers excellent regional cuisine and the best Franconian wine. The central point of the entire facility is the wine bar and if you are looking for fine food and drink then this is your best spot.

**Opera and Theater in Wurzburg**

If you love opera then you should stop in Wurzburg. The city has an annual Mozart festival and the opera house produces operas and even premier operas. The city has also produced several famous opera singers. Mainfrqanketheater has been putting on opera, musicals, ballets, plays and concerts for more than 200 years.

Chambinzky is a theater that has open air performances in the summer. This theater is famous because it not only offers great performances but it also serves food and drink during the performance. Bockshorn has puts on concerts, musicals, cabarets and plays. If you like dance performances, then see what is playing at the tanzSpeicher.

**Alternative Nightlife**

For those looking for the alternative scene check out the AKW, or autonomies kulturzentrum wuerzburg. This is held in an old brewery and you will find a great mix of people, business students, and the older generation looking for some fun and those that can be classified as weekend punks. They get some very interesting acts. In the summer there is a beer garden and an outdoor cinema. On Thursdays there are no entrance fees.
