---
title: "Shopping in Wurzburg"
type: section
---

**Wurzburg shopping** is something you shouldn’t miss when you are in this city. This Franconia city is one of the most important shopping centers in the entire region. Following are some of the main streets where you can find some good shops and deals.

**Schönbornstrasse and Domstrasse**

These are two extensive pedestrian zones where tourists will find large departmental stores that offer a wide range of products. From furniture to clothes, toys to accessories, you can find a variety of high-end shops on these two Wurzburg streets. If you are looking for the latest in fashion, you will find many options to choose from. This is because there are a number of fashion outlets on both streets that can meet your every need. Whether you are looking for clothes, shoes or accessories, you can be sure to find them in the Domstrasse or Schönbornstrasse zones. However, shoppers should be aware that these streets are not meant for budget shopping. They are exquisite shops where you will have to spend large amounts of money on your shopping.

**Juliuspromenade**

Visitors looking for a cheaper shopping experience need not be disappointed. The Juliuspromenade Street has numerous smaller shops that sell a variety of articles. While you do find many designer boutiques and other shops on this street, they are not as extravagant and costly as those found on the Domstrasse or Schönbornstrasse. This is one of the things that make shopping at Juliuspromenade great for budget-minded tourists. Many of the shops located on this street have their own range of items. You might come across something really interesting to buy while browsing through these shops.

**Malls**

Besides the two streets mentioned above, there are a few malls that you can find in the city. The stores in these malls boast of local and international brands of clothing, shoes and other items. There are a number of wine shops around the town where tourists can buy some of the best wines. The Haus des Frankenweins is one of the best places to visit for wines. Known as the House of Franconian Wine, it organizes wine tastings especially for tourists. In addition to wines, visitors can also buy different kinds of wine accessories from here.
