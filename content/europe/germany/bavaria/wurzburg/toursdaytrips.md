**{Romantic Road}**

Würzburg is the starting point of the Romantic Road, the most famous German tourist route.  From April to October there is a daily coach service between Würzburg and Füssen linking all cities and villages on the Romantic Road. 

tel: +49 (0)931 - 37 24 36 (Tourist information)
