A specialist shop under the railway arches near Zoologischer Garten, stocking African cosmetics, wigs, hairpieces, and fabrics. The range covers products that can be hard to find elsewhere in Berlin. Handy for the U+S Zoologischer Garten stop. Open Mon–Fri 10am–6:30pm, Sat 10am–2:30pm.
