---
title: "Bars and Cafes"
type: section
---

Mitte's bar scene is dense around Oranienburger Straße and the Hackesche Höfe, though these areas skew touristy. The better spots are a few streets back: Rosenthaler Platz and Zionskirchplatz have quieter neighbourhood bars that fill up with locals on weekday evenings. The area around Hackescher Markt is good for café culture during the day.
