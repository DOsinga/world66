---
title: "Bars and Cafes in Berlin Mitte"
type: section
---

Mitte is the first borough of Berlin and encompasses the historic core of
the city. It is one of the most popular tourist sites of Berlin. Mitte was part of East
Berlin with the Berlin Wall to its west, south and north. Mitte is a place that
is filled with excitement and a variety of attractions. With a vibrant
nightlife, Mitte is one of the most popular stops for tourists visiting Berlin.
The area is filled with some of the best bars and cafes that Berlin has to
offer. The following are a few of the top clubs and bars in Mitte.

**Opernpalais**

The Opernpalais cafe is situated right next to the Staatsoper unter den
Linden. The building that houses the cafe was built between 1733 and 1737 by
F.W.Diterichs and is one of the oldest buildings in Mitte. Today the building
houses a café, providing customers a great opportunity to enjoy the design and
luxury of the 19th century while having cake and coffee. The café serves
fantastic coffee, cakes and pastries and is open Monday to Sunday from 8 a.m.
to 12 p.m.; however, reservations are recommended.

**Ossena**

Ossena is another premier bar/café in Mitte where the quality of the food is
excellent and the service is just great. Ossena is a great place to relax and
have a drink. The café also has restaurant facilities that provides excellent
dishes. Ossena is located at 42, Rosenthaler Straße and is open Monday to
Saturday between 9 a.m. and 1 a.m. Prior booking is advisable.

**Jubinal**

Located at 34, Tucholskystraße, Jubinal is a recent addition to the bars in
Mitte. The bar caters to an upscale clientele and is very popular during summer
where much of the activity takes place on the terrace. You can come here if you
want to avoid the other crowed bars and cafés.

**The Pips**

The Pips is a bar that lets you drift back and enjoy the style and music of
the 70s. Though its music is not the style that goes with the modern day, Pips is
popular for its cocktail parties. Pips is located at 84, Auguststraße.

**Café Orange**

Café Orange is one of the hottest spots in Mitte. This is the
perfect place to relax during the summer or in the afternoon and enjoy the
special ‘Orange Coffee’. The café is situated at 32, Oranienburger Straße and
includes a bar and a restaurant. It is open Monday to Thursday 9 a.m. to 1 a.m.
and Friday to Sunday frin 9 a.m. to 2 a.m.
