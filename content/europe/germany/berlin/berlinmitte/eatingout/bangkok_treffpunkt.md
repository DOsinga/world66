Bangkok Treffpunk is reputedly one of the most authentic Thai Restaurants in Berlin. In addition, the food is very inexpensive, you are served within 10 minutes of ordering, and the staff is very friendly. Very small and very popular, so you might have to wait for a table. (Take the M2 to Marienstrasse)
