This big new café restaurant is named after the socially-critical magazine edited by Tucholsky and Ossietzky that was published during the Weimar Republic till it was banned by the Nazis. The international press café with its 50 seats is a vivid reminder of this. Test out its progressive aim and its commitment to tradition -at one of the events in the Weltbar (world bar), for instance.
