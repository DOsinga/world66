Mr. Vuong is utterly charming and his Vietnamese food attracts the
scene like a magnet. Despite the low prices (dishes are under 8 EUR),
the food is always elegantly prepared: just choose one of the two main
dishes of the day written on the blackboard to get what you really want
and sit back and people watch. The homoerotic posters and sexy waiters
are also a big draw too.
