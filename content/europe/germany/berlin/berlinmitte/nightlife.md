---
title: "Nightlife"
type: section
---

Mitte has some of Berlin's best theatre and cabaret alongside its clubs. The techno scene that defined 1990s Berlin started in basement venues near Tresor and Wertheim; the current club map changes fast, but the energy is still here. For theatre, Bar jeder Vernunft — a mirrored tent venue in nearby Wilmersdorf — is one of Germany's finest cabaret spaces.
