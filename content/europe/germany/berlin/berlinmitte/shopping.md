Across the border from Kreuzberg in the green district of Treptow is Germany's biggest covered antique market. Next door is Europe's largest hall with cantilevered iron beams, the Arena, with lots of different kinds of events. At the end of Puschkinallee you come to Treptower Park. Here you find the Insel - not to be missed. It really is an island in the River Spree. Parties, cinema screenings and concerts are held in and around the clubhouse. On the terrace you can just relax and have a drink.
