---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Mitte is where Berlin's greatest density of monuments and museums sits. Museum Island alone — Pergamon, Bode, Neues Museum with the Nefertiti bust — could fill a full day. Beyond the island, the Brandenburg Gate, the Jewish Memorial, Checkpoint Charlie, and the Reichstag are all within walking distance of each other. The key is to pick a few things and engage with them properly, rather than sprinting from site to site.
