---
title: "Bars and Cafes"
type: section
---

Charlottenburg's bar scene clusters around Savignyplatz and the S-Bahn arches, with a more upmarket, older crowd than the eastern districts. The Kantstraße-Grolmannstraße area is more varied; the Ku'damm end has institution-type cafés. Schwarzes Café on Kantstraße is a Berlin institution — open around the clock, it's the last stop of the night and the first stop of the morning. For nightlife, Quasimodo (jazz club under Café Kranzler) is the best jazz venue in western Berlin. Bar jeder Vernunft, just south in Wilmersdorf, is one of Germany's finest cabaret venues in an authentic mirrored tent.
