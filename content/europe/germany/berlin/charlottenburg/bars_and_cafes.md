---
title: "Bars and Cafes"
type: section
---

Charlottenburg's bar scene clusters around Savignyplatz and the S-Bahn arches (S-Bahn-Bögen), with a more upmarket, older crowd than the eastern districts. The Kurfürstendamm end has institution-type cafés; the Kantstraße-Grolmannstraße area is more varied. Schwarzes Café (open around the clock) on Kantstraße is a Berlin institution — the last stop of the night and the first stop of the morning.
