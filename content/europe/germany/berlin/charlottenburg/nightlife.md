---
title: "Nightlife"
type: section
---

Charlottenburg's nightlife is more grown-up and less frantic than the eastern districts. The clubs around Savignyplatz and the Kurfürstendamm cater to a mixed crowd, from long-established discos on the Ku'damm to jazz at Quasimodo. Bar jeder Vernunft, just south in Wilmersdorf, is one of Germany's finest cabaret venues — first-class performers in an authentic mirrored tent.
