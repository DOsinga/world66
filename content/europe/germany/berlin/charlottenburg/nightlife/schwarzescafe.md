Cult cafe close to Zoo station - for many people the first or the last stop on their Berlin trip. Open round the clock, so you can spend the whole night here if you've got nowhere to sleep, and you won't get bored unless you're boring yourself. Weird dishes as well as good food. In the daytime you can eat outside in the courtyard.
