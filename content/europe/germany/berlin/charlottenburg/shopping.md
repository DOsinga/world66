**general**

**Stilwerk**

Close to Kerfürstendamm on the corner of Uhlandstr. is a new complex specialising in interior decor and design, with 48 shops on 5 floors covering an area of 19,000m2. The restaurant Stil, the bar Remington and the sushi bar Heiku offer culinary delights and "The Forum" is open for events and ex [more..](/europe/germany/berlin/charlottenburg/shopping/stilwerk)

connection: U+S Zoologischer Garten, S Savignyplatz,

address: Kantstr. 17

openings: Mo-Fr 10am -8pm, Sa 10am -4pm, Su noon -6pm

**S-Bahnbögen Savignyplatz**

Smart boutiques, galleries and restaurants under the S-Bahn arches between Uhlandstr. and Bleibtreustr. Scout around for what takes your fancy.

address: Savignyplatz
