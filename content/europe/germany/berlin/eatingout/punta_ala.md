Very, very far off the beaten path, in Berlin-Steglitz, you will find the "*Punta Ala*", a nice Italian restaurant. It's nothing very extraordinary, but the food tastes yummy, the prices are low and the service is very friendly.

You can get a lasagne for 3 Euro, a pizza-salame for 2,50 Euro and a rumpsteak for 5 Euro. The "*Carciofini al Forno*" (artichokes au gratin) are delicious.
(By the way: the prices on the *Punta Ala* website are not up-to-date, there has been a slight raise. But it's still quite low priced compared to other Italian restaurants in Berlin.)

**Opening hours:
**Everyday from 11 a.m. to midnight

**Location:
**As the restaurant is quite out of range - a 20 min. walk from the S-Bahn station "Lichterfelde-Ost" - it just makes sense to go there, if you are in this area anyway. You can also take the bus 186 from Rathaus Steglitz and get out at "Gärtnerstrasse". The bus stop is right in front of the restaurant.
