In Germany, dial 112 for ambulance and fire, and 110 for police. These work from any phone, including mobiles without a SIM card. Most operators speak some English.

For non-life-threatening medical issues, the on-call doctor service ("Ärztlicher Bereitschaftsdienst") is reachable at 116 117, available evenings and weekends. Many doctors in Berlin speak English. If you lose your passport, file a report with the police and then contact your embassy.
