---
title: "Bars and Cafes"
type: section
---

Simon-Dach-Straße and the surrounding streets — Wühlischstraße, Grünberger Straße — form the densest bar district in Friedrichshain. It's cheap, loud, and full of students; the prices are the best in the city. The Volkspark Friedrichshain beer gardens are the daytime alternative. For breakfast, most of the cafés on the Simon-Dach strip serve substantial late-morning spreads.
