---
title: "Eating Out"
type: section
---

Eating in Friedrichshain is cheap and good. The Simon-Dach-Straße area has the densest concentration of café-restaurants, many serving substantial brunches on weekends. Oberbaumbrücke end of Mühlenstraße has a few larger venue-restaurants. The neighbourhood's student demographic keeps prices low across the board — you can eat well here for very little.
