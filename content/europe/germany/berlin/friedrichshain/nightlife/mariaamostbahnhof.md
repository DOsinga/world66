A must for nightclubbers and sound maniacs and a big attraction way beyond Berlin's borders for fans of electro and experimental music (concerts and live DJs). You can relax in the post-socialist atmosphere here -on Wednesdays at the floosy bar of the legendary Lassie Singers you can look out at Ostbahnhof train station and be filled with yearning for fraraway places.
