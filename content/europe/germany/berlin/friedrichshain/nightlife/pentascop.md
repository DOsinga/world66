Located ona patch of street that prides itself on space and grace, you can still feel a touch of nostalgia for communist times in this pub with its tough types and drinks to match. Get someone to explain the ideas behind the interior decor on the spot. Maybe you'll stumble on a party in the back room, which can be rented by outside organizers.
