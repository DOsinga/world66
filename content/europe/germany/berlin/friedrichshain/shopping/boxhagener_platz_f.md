This is the freakier flea market in Berlin. Every Sunday the punks and deliquent 30somethings in platform shoes come here to sell their wares. Retro furniture, antiques, record collections, hand screened political t-shirts, junk, goa hippie bags: they've got it all! Certainly one of the more interesting people watching activites in the city.

This flea market is generally regarded as actually one of the more yuppy, hipster, overpriced flea markets in Berlin, for the oh-so-chic and ironic young couple working in advertising. For cooler, more "punk rock" fleamarkets go to the irregular ones at TEK or SO36 in Kreuzberg Oranienstrasse, or in the part of Friedrichshain ABOVE Frankfurter Allee
