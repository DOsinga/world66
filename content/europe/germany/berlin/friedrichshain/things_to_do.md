---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Friedrichshain's most important sight is the East Side Gallery — 1.3 km of the Berlin Wall covered with murals by artists from around the world, running along Mühlenstraße between Warschauer Brücke and the Ostbahnhof. Karl-Marx-Allee, the Stalinist boulevard to the north, is an extraordinary piece of Soviet-era urban planning worth walking end to end. The neighbourhood's charm is otherwise less about specific attractions and more about the density of bars, cheap restaurants, and the energy of a genuinely young, international crowd.
