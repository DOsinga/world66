Use the European Low Cost Airline Guide search engine ( http://www.low-cost-airline.info) to find out which budget airlines fly into the 3 Berlin airports (Schonefeld and Tegel are the 2 airports best served by cheap flights)

Often you can save many $/£/whatever by using airlines such as Easyjet and Ryanair compared to the main carriers.
