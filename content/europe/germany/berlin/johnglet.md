A Kreuzberg institution selling sturdy workwear — overalls, boiler suits, heavy-duty jackets — that Berlin's club and rave crowd long ago adopted as streetwear staples. The selection is no-nonsense and the prices are fair. Open Monday to Thursday 8 am–6 pm, Friday 8 am–2 pm, Saturday 9 am–1 pm. Right by U Mehringdamm.
