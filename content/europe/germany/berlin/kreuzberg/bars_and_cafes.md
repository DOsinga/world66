---
title: "Bars and Cafes"
type: section
---

Kreuzberg's bar culture is centred on Oranienstraße and Bergmannstraße, with quieter side-street spots tucked in between. The neighbourhood has a long tradition of honest, unpretentious Kneipen — places where you pay very little for a beer and no one tries to impress you. The canal-side cafés on Paul-Lincke-Ufer are the best spots for a sunny afternoon drink.
