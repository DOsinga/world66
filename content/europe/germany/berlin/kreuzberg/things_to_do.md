---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Kreuzberg's must-sees cluster around two very different registers: the sobering and the convivial. The Jewish Museum — Libeskind's extraordinary building on Lindenstraße — is one of the finest museum experiences in Europe. Viktoriapark offers the city's best elevated view. And simply wandering Oranienstraße or Bergmannstraße gives you the real feel of the neighbourhood — Turkish food, vinyl shops, and the remnants of Berlin's punk and alternative past.
