Berlin's only proper hill (61 metres), with good views from the top and a Schinkel monument to the Liberation Wars of 1813–15 at the summit. The park has a waterfall, a small vineyard, and the Golgatha beer garden at the foot of the hill — a classic Berlin Sunday afternoon spot when the weather cooperates.
