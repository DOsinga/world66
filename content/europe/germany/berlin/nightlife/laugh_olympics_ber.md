Laugh
Olympics Berlin is a comedy improv show performed by an energetic cast of comedians
with a true passion for creativity and improvisation. This talented group come
from a variety of English speaking nations including New Zealand, Canada, USA
and England.

They never cease to amaze their audiences with the ability to
conjure up comedy sketches and songs based on audience suggestions provided
only seconds earlier. It´s a perfect choice for ex-pats, tourists, people learning English, and those who need an alternative to Berlin´s serious theatre-scene.
