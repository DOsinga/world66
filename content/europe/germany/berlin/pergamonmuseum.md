The undisputed highlight of Museum Island and one of the great museums anywhere in the world. Built between 1912 and 1930 to house objects too large for conventional display cases, the Pergamon contains the reconstructed Pergamon Altar (c. 160 BC), the Market Gate of Miletus, and the jaw-dropping Processional Way and Ishtar Gate from Babylon. Allow at least two hours — longer if you want to do justice to the Islamic Art collection as well.
