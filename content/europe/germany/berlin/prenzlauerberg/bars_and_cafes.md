---
title: "Bars and Cafes"
type: section
---

Prenzlauer Berg's café culture is the densest in Berlin. Kastanienallee ("Casting Allee" to locals) is the central strip, but the side streets around Kollwitzplatz and Knaackstraße are where the better neighbourhood spots cluster. The Kulturbrauerei complex is worth knowing about: a former brewery with multiple bars and a Sunday flea market. Konnopke's Imbiss under the Eberswalder Straße U-Bahn tracks is Berlin's most famous currywurst stand.
