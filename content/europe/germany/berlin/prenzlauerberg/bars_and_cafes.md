---
title: "Bars and Cafes in Prenzlauer Berg"
type: section
---

One of the exciting cities within a city is the district known as
Prenzlauer Berg. This bustling area within Berlin has a
reputation for a number of offerings. Some of those unique offerings
include the individuality expressed by people in this specific region.**
**

**KulturBrauerei**

The most popular bars and cafes in Prenzlauer Berg are located in a
region known as KulturBrauerei. There is a complex of restaurants, cafes
and bars housed in this renovated brewery that boast of the most
cutting edge dining and drinking opportunities that the world has to
offer. One can dine on a fine meal consisting of luscious insects or
attend movies that offer different and unique genres for the movie
buff. Another authentic offering within this old brewery is the
Kesselhaus Concert House and its various associated stores that offer
the finest musical instruments.**
**

**Helmholtzplatz**

Helmholtzplatz provides a wonderful background for enjoying the
outdoors, as well as cafes situated along the edges so that people can
refresh their bodies as well as having their spirits tuned to the
sounds of nature.

**Kollwitzplatz**

Another park in the Prenzlauer Berg area is known as
Kollwitzplatz. This quaint little park has various cafes that can
provide refreshment for the individual while enjoying nature. There is also a marketplace here. The Saturday market is a
veritable display of trinkets, crafts and good food, all designed to
entice others to part with their money.

**Konnopkes Imbiss**

This is a house of authentic Austrian food which is quite popular
with the locals. Located in Schonhauser Allee and under the tracks of
Eberswalder Strasse, this place is famous for local delicacies such as
curry mitner schrippe. A real treat would be the currywurst from this
place, something that the place specializes in.

**Malzcafe**

Malzcafe is located in Knaackstr. It is a popular food joint for
people who have vegetarian food choices. The place serves authentic
German fare for very cheap rates.

**Morgenrot**

The Morgenrot, located in Kastanienalle, is a place that offers
different varieties of food. This bar is known for its health-conscious
food. There are several choices here for vegetarian and vegan food
eaters.

**Café and Restaurant Berlin**

This is one of the main points of the city, located in Gleimstrasse.
The place is more renowned for its breakfast than its regular fare. The
café offers simple food which is priced cheaply and carries a highly
memorable taste.
