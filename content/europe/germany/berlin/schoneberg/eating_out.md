---
title: "Eating Out"
type: section
---

Schöneberg is known for relaxed Sunday brunches and a solid selection of neighbourhood restaurants. The area around Winterfeldtplatz has a weekend market and several good café-restaurants around it. Goltzstraße is lined with international options — Turkish, Italian, and German alongside each other. The gay-friendly venues around Nollendorfplatz tend to be unpretentious and good value.
