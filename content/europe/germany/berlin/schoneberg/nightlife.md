---
title: "Nightlife"
type: section
---

Schöneberg is Berlin's historic gay quarter — Nollendorfplatz has been the centre of gay life here since the 1920s. Today it's more mixed but still LGBTQ+-friendly throughout. Kit Kat Club is one of the city's most famous fetish/techno venues, at the Nollendorfplatz building. The area around Winterfeldtplatz has a cluster of relaxed bars popular with a slightly older crowd.
