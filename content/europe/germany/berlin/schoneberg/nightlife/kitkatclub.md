One of Berlin's most famous and most polarising nightclubs — an institution since 1994. The dress code is strict (fetish, kink, or fantasy; nothing casual), the door is selective, and the atmosphere inside is overtly sexual. Techno music, no photography, and no judgement. Genuinely unlike anywhere else. Friday and Saturday nights from late.
