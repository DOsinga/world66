Only 100 meters from Checkpoint Charlie you will find a small but very interesting shopping place, the boxoffberlin, or just bob. It's a **shop, Cafe and gallery in one**. The shop offers **extraordinary souvenirs and cool gifts made by local designers** (shirts, bags, comics, music, films, games, post cards, toys, books...). The Cafe has coffee specialties based on organically grown and fair traded Espresso and an individual selection of soft drinks (e.g. Fritz Kola, Bionade) and beer (e.g. Berliner Weisse, Palast-Bier) and the gallery shows changing exhibitions of contemporary art, films and more from Berlin artists.
