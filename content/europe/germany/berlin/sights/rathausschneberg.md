(Schöneberg Town Hall)
 From 1948 to 1991 Rathaus Schöneberg was the seat of West Berlin's regional
 parliament and government. The bell tower houses the famous Freedom Bell, a
 gift from the American people to mark the end of the Soviet blockade of West
 Berlin. The Town Hall also contains the permanent exhibition "Fighting for
 Freedom" -Willy Brandt 1913 -1992"
