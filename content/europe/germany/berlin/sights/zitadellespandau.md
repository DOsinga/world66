(Spandau Citadel)
 Today the once-proud water castle of the Askanians houses a museum on the
 history of the fortress and the citadel. After you've seen the exhibition
 you can dine here in medieval style or browse in the local history museum.
 In winter at 7pm and 8pm you can take a guided tour to the place where lots
 of bats go into hibernation in the citadel.
