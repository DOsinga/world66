What would Kreuzberg be without SO 36? Considerably worse off. The regular programme ranges from "Electric Ballroom" on Mondays for techno fans to the standard dance events on Sundays for gays and friends. In tune with the old fringe politics, nobody's excluded. Varying opening times (usually from 10pm or 11pm) admission: 7-10DM, more for special events.
