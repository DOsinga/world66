A five-floor complex near Kurfürstendamm devoted to interior design and upscale home furnishings, with around 48 shops spread across 19,000 square metres. Even if you're not in the market for a new sofa, the building itself is worth a walk through — sleek, airy, and a good snapshot of Berlin's design-conscious side. Several restaurants and bars inside make it easy to combine browsing with a meal.
