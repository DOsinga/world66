A former infantry barracks that became one of Berlin's most elegant small museums. The Bröhan houses an extensive private collection of art nouveau and art deco objects — furniture, glass, ceramics, and paintings — including works by Max Liebermann, Lovis Corinth, and Henry van de Velde. It sits directly across from Charlottenburg Palace, making it a natural companion visit.
