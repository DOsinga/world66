A genuine urban oasis on the hill that gives Kreuzberg its name. On a clear day the views across the city from the top are excellent. The park has a waterfall, a small beer garden (Golgatha) at the base, and wide lawns for lazing or kicking a ball around. The multicultural summer festival brings live music and performances to the open-air stages.
