---
title: "Tours and Excursions"
type: section
---

Berlin repays walking — the city is compact enough that self-guided routes between the major sights in Mitte take less than a day, and most of the interesting neighbourhood exploration is done on foot or by bike. For structured tours, walking tours in English run daily from the main tourist areas; bike tours are an excellent way to cover the former east-west border. The Spree is navigable by boat for a different view of the city.
