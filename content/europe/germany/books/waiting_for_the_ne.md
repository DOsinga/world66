This book is a brilliant analysis of today's Germany's political aim and long term strategy. It describes in great detail how Germany finally achieved by economic means what Hitler failed to achieve by military terrorism. It also analyses the US-EU relationship and tensions between the two blocks. The author is always referred to as Germany's Michael Moore but his analysis is much more factual.
