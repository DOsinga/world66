All aboard for theatre, music, cinema and a pub. Five minutes walk from Potsdam Stadt S-Bahn station. An der Alten Fahrt, near Lange Brücke S Potsdam Stadt We-Su from 6pm 

**Filmmuseum Potsdam im Marstall**

The beautiful Marstall was built in 1685 and is now a place of pilgrimage for film fans who flock to the exhibition on 100 years of cinema. You can relax after in the cafe. Marsall Tel. 0331 / 2718112 S Potsdam Stadt, Tu-Su 10am -6pm admission: 2-10 DM, red. 2 +1 DM

**Hans Otto Theatre**

Three stages for drama, musical theatre and theatre for children and young people. A wide variety of plays from classics to contemporary theatre, and productions by the theatre's own company. Am Alten Markt, Tel. 0331 / 9811123 tram 93 to Otto-Nagel-Str. admission: 30 DM, red 18 DM
