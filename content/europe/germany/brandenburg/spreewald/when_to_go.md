---
title: "When to Go"
type: section
---

Late spring and early autumn are the best times to visit. May and June bring long days, warm temperatures, and the forest in full leaf without the peak-season crush. September is lovely — the crowds thin out, the light turns golden, and the meadows are often still dry enough for easy cycling.

July and August are busy, particularly around Lübbenau, which fills with day-trippers from Berlin on weekends. The boat tours still run well, but book ahead.

Winter is quiet and can be atmospheric — snow in the forest, frost on the canals, almost no one around — but many boat operators close from November to March.
