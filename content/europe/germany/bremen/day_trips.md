When visiting Bremen, take advantage of the nearby attractions. Whether you're a single backpacker, hiking through Europe, or with your family, these daytrips from Bremen will make your visit complete.

**German Maritime
Museum**

In Bremerhaven, learn about the history of the region at the
German Maritime Museum where you can see a wartime u-boat. There is also a Marine Zoo in town where you
can examine aquatic life in their natural habitat.

**Theme Parks**

Theme parks abound in this area. Heide Park Adventure World and Resort has an
exciting wooden roller coaster and many other exhilarating rides. Nearby in Bispingen, is the Snow Dome where
you can participate in winter sports all year round. Enjoy skiing, snowboarding or take a toboggan
ride. Walsrode boasts the world’s
largest bird park. Serengeti Park is in Hodenhagen and
you will ride a tour bus through exotic animal habitats. The Sea Life Aquarium in Hannover is a must
sea for those that love sharks, dolphins and tropical fish. Also in Hannover is one of the finest zoos in
Europe called the Adventure Zoo.

**Woflsburg**

Wolfsburg is the home of a technological center called
Phaeno. Go in the futuristic building to
interactive stations and labs where visitors can take part in scientific
experiments.

**Hamburg**

While in Bremen you must take a short trip to Hamburg,
Germany. There are many things to do
there including visiting museums, cruises, and other activities. Museum fur Volkerkunde highlights growth and
development of cultures from around the world.
It is open every day but Monday from 10am to 6pm and Thursdays 10am to
9pm. Children are to 18 years old are free and adults are €7.

**Haegensbeck’s
Tierpark**

Haegensbeck’s Tierpark is also in Hamburg and built in
1848. Wild animals move freely about in
outdoor corrals. The park hosts Jungle
Nights in June, Dances in July, and Classical Music in August. They also offer a tropical aquarium with over
13,000 fish and other animals in three levels.

**Rathaus**

If you like history, visit Rathaus with 647 rooms and an
ornate clock tower. It is in public
square and many cultural and civic events are scheduled. There is also a 35 minute tour.
