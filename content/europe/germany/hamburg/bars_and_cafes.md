---
title: "Bars and Cafes"
type: section
---

Hamburg drinks well. The Schanzenviertel around the **Schulterblatt** is the densest cluster of bars and small cafes, with a strong Portuguese and southern-European presence — coffee, Sagres beer, and grilled fish. **St. Pauli** and the side streets off the Reeperbahn are the live-music heartland, where the Beatles cut their teeth and where the city still goes to drink late. The **Lange Reihe** in St. Georg is the centre of Hamburg's gay scene and a relaxed all-comers strip of cafes. The **Portuguese Quarter** between the Michel and the harbour is the place for an early-evening glass of vinho verde.
