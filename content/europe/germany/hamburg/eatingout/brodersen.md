Rothenbaumchaussee 46, Telefon 040 / 45 81 19

www.restaurant-brodersen.de

A wonderful local restaurant, with deep traditions in the Hamburg style. They have friendly service, and intimate atmosphere. Local specialties are seasonal, and include Spargel, Mai-Schollen, Neue Matjes, Pfifferlinge, Grunkohl, Gansebrust.

Labskaus is a speciality and absolutely delicious. Don't miss out on the Bratkartoffeln, German Fried Potatoes, or the pfifferlinge chanterelle mushrooms if they are in season.

The Matjes herrings are heavenly!
