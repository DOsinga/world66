The leafy, well-off northern district, popular with media types and lawyers. The **Eppendorfer Landstrasse** and **Eppendorfer Baum** are the main shopping streets, lined with delicatessens, cafes and small designer shops. It's the place to come for a long Saturday brunch or to see how prosperous Hamburg lives. The U-Bahn line U3 runs through.
