---
title: "Getting Around"
type: section
---

Public transport systems in Hambur work very efficiently and reliably. We recommend buying the Hamburg Card rather then normal tickets or day passes. With its help you can travel for free and it also gives free or reduced admission to most of the city’s museums. This costs about 7 Euro a day and can be used from 6pm the day before. Or buy the three-day-card for 15 Euro or a group card for five people (13 Euro a day / 22 Euro three days).
