Europe's largest inner-city development, built since 2000 on 157 hectares of former dockland just south of the old town. The Speicherstadt warehouse district forms its northern edge; new residential and office towers, the Elbphilharmonie, the International Maritime Museum and the Überseequartier shopping district fill the rest. Architecturally a mixed bag, but worth walking through to see how a working port has been turned into a new neighbourhood. The U4 metro line runs out to the eastern end.
