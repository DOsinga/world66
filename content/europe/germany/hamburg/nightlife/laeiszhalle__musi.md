The Laeiszhalle (former Musikhalle Hamburg, English: Music hall Hamburg) is a concert hall in Hamburg, Germany and home to the Hamburger Symphoniker. Also concerts of popular music were held in this music venue, like the concert of the band Bob Marley & The Wailers during the Rastaman Vibration Tour in 1976.

The hall is named after the German shipowning company F. Laeisz, founder of the concert venue.

Rare, Intimate Concerts with Pianist Yul Anderson at The LAEISZHALLE - Musikhalle Hamburg
****

Hamburg will have an opportunity top experience the artist who is featured in the main soundtrack along with Nina Simone in Jonh Malkovich's Debut Directorial Film, ”The Dancer Upstairs” (See http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=oYLPiloWPSc).
This was the first soundtrack ever using a live recording. It is also the first performance of Bob Dylan's ”All along The Watchtower” on piano (See http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=2NzuPYl_5_0). It has been noted by critics as the best version of this song ever performed.

Yul Anderson, the Elusive Multi talented Artist; Pianist, Guitarist and Vocalist, is not content with existing musical categories. Anderson’s music is a musical universe; a mixture of 18th and 19th Century Classical Music rooted in American Black Music creating a Flowing and Rhythmic Solo Piano Improvisation.

Anderson is furthermore inspired by improvisers like Jimi Hendrix and Keith Jarrett, the impressionism of Debussy, Ravel and Bach.
John Malkovich enjoyed the music so much that he made a personal call to Yul Anderson and asked permission to use his live solo piano version of "All Along The Watchtower" as the main soundtrack in his debut directorial film "The Dancer Upstairs", starring Oscar Winner Javier Bardem.

"My meaning is to perform the ultimate piano experience expressed through sounds that carry the listener on a musical journey to a place where they have never been before", Anderson says as he ponders his musical vision.

Anderson will perform his newest improvisations including his much celebrated solo piano live performance of “All Along The Watchtower”. ”The Dancer Upstairs” movie trailer:
