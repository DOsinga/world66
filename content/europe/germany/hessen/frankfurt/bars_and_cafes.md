title: "Bars and Cafes in Frankfurt"
type: section
---

Frankfurt is a transportation and financial hub of Europe so there are plenty of **Frankfurt bars and cafes** found in all areas of the city. If you are looking for ambiance or just want to try some different cuisines you can find it all in Frankfurt.

**Have a Cocktail and Meet New People**

If you want to meet people and have some lively conversation while enjoying a great mixed drink, then stop in at Café bar on Schweizer Strasse 14. If you are not interested in interacting, then grab a seat and partake in some people watching.

If you love the movie Casablanca then stop by Casablanca bar. Inside there are Moroccan lamps, leather chairs and movie memorabilia. This is a groovy and comfortable spot for a drink. It is worth it to try one of the bartender’s specialties. As you sip your cocktail enjoy the sound of the piano and completely relax after a busy day.

**Historic Tavern**

Dauth Schnieder is a historic tavern that has been open for 150 years. You can enjoy a glass of the traditional and famous apple wine alongside a plate of fried potatoes, sausages and sauerkraut. Besides wine they also offer a great selection of Germany beers. Meet new people when sitting at the long tables or in the summer enjoy your drinks in the outdoor garden.

**Great Breakfast Cafes**

If you are interested in a good café for breakfast, then try Café Karin am Salzhausstrasse. This café is an institution in the city and is one of the best breakfasts. It can get very crowded for lunch so unless you like to wait try somewhere else. During the warmer months you can enjoy eating outside. Another great breakfast stop is Alte Café Schneider on Kaiserstrasse, where you can get excellent cake and coffee.

**Enjoy a Quiet Meal**

Cafe Bar Marigold on Zeil 1 has a great ambiance and you can easily lose track of the time while you are enjoying a drink and a meal. It is cozy and quiet and a popular spot for those looking for a refuge from the louder spots. A great new cafe option is Kaffeehaus near Berger Strasse. You can get breakfast all day long as well as soups, salads and desserts. You can relax and take your time dining.

**Coffee, Tea and More**

A café that specializes in coffee and tea from around the world is Kante. They also offer some light food choices, but it is the drinks that bring in the customers. Heidinger IV is a café and bakery found in the European Central Bank building. The sweets are gorgeous and delicious and they also offer sandwiches and wraps.
