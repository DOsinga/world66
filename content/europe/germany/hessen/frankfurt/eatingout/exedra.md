**Neo-Greco**

Situated across the way from Frankfurt's courthouse, Exedra boasts some of the best Greek cuisine in the city.

In
cool and spacious surroundings, trimmed with ceramic vases and changing
exhibitions of paintings, the food is presented in a friendly family
atmosphere.

Well worth trying are the courgettes with feta sauce as a starter or
the "tzatziki”. Fresh Mediterranean fish featuring dorade and branzino
grilled with herbs and served with fresh market vegetables are a must
as a main course.

To round up the "last judgement", try the as always excellent Greek yoghurt with nuts and honey.
