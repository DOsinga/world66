Situated across from Frankfurt's courthouse, Exedra boasts some of the best Greek cuisine in the city. In cool and spacious surroundings trimmed with ceramic vases and changing art exhibitions, the food is presented in a friendly family atmosphere. Try the courgettes with feta sauce or the tzatziki to start, then fresh Mediterranean fish grilled with herbs as a main. The Greek yoghurt with nuts and honey is an excellent finish.
