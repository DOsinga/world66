This popular place has been crowded every night since it opened some thirty-five years ago. Leftist students (or those who once were), artists and intellectuals come from far afield. Reservation is a must. If you are alone or with one other person, you never get a table of your own -- but table companions are almost always very nice and interesting. The food and wine are delicious. Sometimes the service is a bit slow. But did you really want to leave already?
