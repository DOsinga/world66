This popular place has been crowded every night since it opened some thirty five years ago. Leftist students, or those who once were it, artists and intellectuals who come from far, but also breast-feeding mothers from the neighbourhood can be found here. Reservation is a must. If you are alone or with one other person, you never get a table of your own. But what is so really miraculous in this place: your table-companions are almost always very nice and interesting! The food and the wine are delicious. Sometimes the service is a bit slow. But did you really want to leave already?
