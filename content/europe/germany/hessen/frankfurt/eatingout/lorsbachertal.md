The most popular and traditional restaurant in the Sachsenhausen quarter. Open Tue-Sat till midnight, Sun till 11pm, kitchen closes at 10:30pm. Main dishes like Rumpsteak or Schnitzel are reasonably priced, and the various soups are worth trying. Home-made Ebbelwoi (apple wine).
