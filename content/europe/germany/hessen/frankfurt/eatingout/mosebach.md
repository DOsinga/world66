For many guests the Mosebach is like their own living room -- a friendly, communicative atmosphere where every newcomer is almost immediately integrated into the family. In summer the wonderful garden under an old chestnut tree is the next best alternative to a trip to the countryside.

People come from all corners of town for the good and reasonably priced typical Frankfurt and regional cuisine. Thomas Mosebach personally controls quality of ingredients. An excellent place to eat well, try some fantastic German wines (and some pretty rare schnaps), experience a bit of Frankfurt life and make new friends.
