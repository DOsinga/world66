For many a guest the Mosebach is like their own living room - a real
friendly and communicative atmosphere where every newcomer is almost
immediately integrated into the family.

In summer the wonderful garden offers, under an old chestnut tree, the
next best alternative to a trip to the countryside. On such days it can
get really full and pretty lively. The crowd is mixed, young and old,
locals and guests in the city and always a lot of fun.

Alone the atmosphere and the apple wine would more than justify a
visit. But people come from all corners of town also for the good and
reasonably priced down-to-earth typical Frankfurt and regional cuisine.
Thomas Mosebach personally controls quality of ingredients and guests
can be assured to be served only the best of what the market offers.
The same attention to quality is applied to the, exclusively of German
origin, wines offered and Thomas regularly visits winegrowers to
guarantee this.

An excellent place to eat well, try some fantastic German wines (and
some pretty rare schnaps!), experience a bit of Frankfurt life and make
new friends.
