title: 'Shopping'

---

Frankfurt has a lot to offer shoppers, from designer boutiques to large shopping centres, with handicrafts, jewelry, perfume and much more.

**Goethestraße**

This district is considered as a shopper’s paradise. Goethestraße is
the most lavish street in Frankfurt where you can find exclusive stores,
high-end shops and boutiques that offers top designer products.
Goethestraße or Goethe Street is located just a few meters away from
Fressgass. If you are looking for haute couture from Ferragamo, Gucci,
Versace, Jill Sander, Channel, Prada or Hermes, this is the place to do
some shopping. You can also pick up some high class jewelry from Tiffany
and Cartier.

**Hallhuber**

Hallhuber is a fashionable clothing store in Frankfurt. Here you can
find trendy fashion pieces dresses, tunics, sports wear, casual wear,
office wear, knits, trousers and shirts. This clothing store has a
sleek, stylish feel with a slight industrial touch. Hallhuber is located
along the Goetheplatz right in the center of the city.

**Hessen Center**

This is a 3-floor shopping mall situated in the northeastern part of
Frankfurt and is one of the oldest shopping destinations within the
city. Hessen Center may be huge but it is the smallest among the 4
major shopping centers in Frankfurt. Here you will find more than 115
specialty boutiques and stores, as well as restaurants and cafés. You
can find a wide variety of jewelry, accessories, ready-made clothing,
shoes and perfumes at this place.

**Marc O'Polo**

Another popular shopping hot spot situated in the center of
Frankfurt is the Marc O’Polo Store. This store has a contemporary appeal
and offers great casual wear for both men and women. Here you can find
extensive lines of trousers, shoes, skirts, shirts and blouses all
made with natural materials like wool, cotton, silk and linen. Marc
O’Polo also offers more than 120 top of the line brands like Bali,
Birkenstock, Aigner, Versace, Timberland and more.

**Werth and Werth**

Werth and Werth is located right in the heart of Frankfurt and
offers a great selection of comfortable clothing and well known labels.
Here you can find high quality products from Just Cavalli, Jean Paul
Gautlier, Dolce and Gabbana, JOOP, Armani, Versace, Guess, Kenso and a
lot more. You can also buy fashionable jewelry, eyewear, underwear,
watches, accessories, men’s clothing, bags and shoes all under 1 roof
here in Werth and Werth.
