title: 'Things to Do'
type: section
---

Frankfurt's cultural scene punches well above its weight. The Museumsufer along the south bank of the Main is one of the best museum strips in Europe, and the city spends more per capita on the arts than any other in Germany. Beyond the museums, the reconstructed Römerberg, the Main Tower observation deck and Goethe's birthplace are the main draws.
