### [Naturschutzgebiet Kühkopf-Knoblochsaue](/europe/germany/hessen/riedstadt/sights/khkopfknoblochs)

*Nature Refuge*

Largest Nature Refuge in Hessen. Hiking/biking trails, playground
& pony wagon rides available. Two beer gardens (*Biergarten*) within its boundaries.

Accessible from Erfelden (pedestrian bridge), from Stockstadt (vehicle* bridge), or from Guntersblum by ferry.

* - Authorized vehicles ONLY!
