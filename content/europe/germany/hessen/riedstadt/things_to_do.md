title: 'Things to Do'
type: section
---

The main attraction near Riedstadt is the **Naturschutzgebiet Kühkopf-Knoblochsaue**, the largest nature refuge in Hessen. Hiking and biking trails, a playground and pony wagon rides are available, and there are two beer gardens within its boundaries. It is accessible from Erfelden by pedestrian bridge, from Stockstadt by vehicle bridge (authorised vehicles only), or from Guntersblum by ferry.
