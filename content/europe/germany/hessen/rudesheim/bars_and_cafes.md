---
title: 'Bars and Cafes'
type: section
---

Rudesheim has a good number of bars and cafes, many centred around the famous Drosselgasse, offering wine, music and entertainment.

**Breuer’s Rudesheimer Schloss **

This is a popular hot spot for locals and tourists alike. Breuer’s Rudesheimer Schloss is an old traditional wine tavern that serves lunch, snacks, afternoon coffee and dinner. Here you can find a great selection of local and regional beers and specialty wines. Live bands play every weekend and on special occasions like Christmas, Easter and New Year. You might want to step in to this place and enjoy yourself.

**Cafe Cream**

Cafe Cream is located on Geisenheimer Street in Rudesheim. This café serve fine cuisine with international flair and influence. Café Cream serves early-morning coffee, breakfast, lunch, snacks, afternoon coffee, dinner, tapas and after-work drinks as well as dessert wines. You can enjoy the night out with your friends in Café cream and sip your ice cold beer or coffee.

**Dudelsack Fun Rock Café**

This is a 14-year old pub/bar situated right in the center of the Rudesheim. Dudelsack Fun Rock Café is always packed with locals and tourists who want to have great time. You can also play billiards, darts and tabletop soccer or watch your favorite sport game in the widescreen LCD TV. Dudelsack Fun Rock Café also hosts especial events and parties like Oktoberfest, Halloween, Christmas and New Year’s Eve. Live bands perform in Dudelsack Fun Rock Café once every week.

**Adler Café **

Adler Café is located at 14 Rhein Strasse, Rudesheim. The café has a neat roof garden where you can sit back and relax over a cup of coffee. This café serves sumptuous food, great coffee, snacks and other alcoholic beverages. There is a band that plays at the café every Friday evening. You can stop at the cafe for a quick snack and coffee, or you can enjoy the evening with a couple of friends here.

**Wirtshaus Hannelore**

Wirtshaus Hannelore is a well-known place for local and tourists alike. It is a traditional inn started in 1954 in the equally famous Drosselgasse. This café serves traditional German dishes, local beers, Rudesheim wines and coffee. Live bands play here every day until the wee hours of the evening. You can either enjoy the entertainment inside or dine out in the private garden terrace and conservatory. Wirtshaus Hannelore also hosts parties and especial occasions like wedding, birthdays and anniversaries.
