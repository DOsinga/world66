**Budget Tips to Help Enjoy Your Vacation in Rudesheim **

It is easy to enjoy this romantic Rhine river town with a well-planned **Rudesheim budget travel**. Once you are in Germany visiting this lovely village, you will get to enjoy magnificent views of vineyards and a fresh, invigorating experience of countryside living. Find out how you can visit and enjoy all the attractions in this beautiful town with less cost.
**
Budget Airlines and Hotels
**

Plan according to your budget and stick to it. Prepare a budget for airfares, accommodation, and land transportation. Lufthansa/Austrian Airlines offers a fall travel sale for travels in Europe. Offer starts at $526+ round trip. Other Airlines like Airtrans and American Airlines also offer discounted airfares for a limited time. For comfortable but cheap accommodations, you should stay at the Altdeutshe Weistub, which is $80 per night, or at the Rudesheimer Hof for $75 per night.

**Travel Light **

Pack only the things that you need, avoid too heavy and too many luggage. What you will be saving in airfares and accommodations might be eaten up by baggage fees. Do not bring heavy sports equipments, it will be best to rent what you need in your destination. While packing, consider the length of your stay and the activities you want to do.

**Walk Instead of Riding**

Rudesheim is a small beautiful town which you can explore on foot. You can visit the nearby vineyards or hike to the Niederwald and enjoy the panoramic scenery of this wine country. You can also take leisurely walks by the Rhine River. By walking and hiking, you do not only save money, but can also get good exercise out of it too.

**Have a Picnic **

Instead of eating out at restaurants, bring a picnic basket with you on your tours to the Niederwalddenkmal memorial monument, Boosenburg and Worderburg castles. Another ideal place to explore where you can picnic is the Rhein Taunus, a park. Bring your picnic basket and enjoy your breakfast and lunch here under the trees and in open air.

**Rent a Bike Instead of a Car**

A bike is a cheaper means of transportation which you can use to go around town or to visit the various museums, castles and other attractions around Rudesheim, Bike rentals are available in most hotels, but if your hotel does not have bikes for rent, they will surely help you find a rental.
