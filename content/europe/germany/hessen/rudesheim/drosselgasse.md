title: Drosselgasse
type: poi

This narrow alley, just 144 metres long, offers non-stop live music, dancing and entertainment from late morning until well after midnight. You will meet people from all continents, enjoying wine and music in the wine bars and gardens of one of Germany's most famous streets.
