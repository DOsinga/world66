---
title: 'Getting Around'
type: section
---

Rudesheim is a small town easily explored on foot. It is close to Assmannshausen -- you can travel between the two by train or walk along the Rhine (6 to 7 km). The cable car takes you up to the Niederwald, while a chair lift brings you back down to Assmannshausen, with views of the vineyards along the way.

**Bicycle**

Several shops rent bicycles, and the area has trails suitable for both leisurely sightseeing and cross-country mountain biking. The national highways from Rudesheim to Bingen are closed to cars on the last Sunday of June but open to cyclists and in-line skaters.

**Train or Bus**

The rail station is on the Wiesbaden-Koblenz line, well connected to regional towns and main cities. Buses are operated by Omnibusverkehr Rhein-Nahe GmbH and offer a reasonable way to travel within the area.
