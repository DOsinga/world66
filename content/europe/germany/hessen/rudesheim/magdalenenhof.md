A family-run wine estate on the edge of Rüdesheim, set among the vineyards that climb the slopes above the Rhine. They produce their own Riesling and Spätburgunder and offer tastings year-round for groups of up to 65 people. The terrace looks out over the vines toward the river — a good spot to try a few glasses without the crowds of the Drosselgasse.
