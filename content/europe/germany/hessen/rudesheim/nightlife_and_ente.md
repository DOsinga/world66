---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment in Rudesheim"
type: section
---

In this romantic, little town located on the banks of the Rhine,
there is a plethora of **Rudesheim Nightlife **and entertainment
options to keep you busy. From the lively, music-filled streets of
Drosselgasse to the town’s nightclubs and taverns, visitors looking to
have a good night out will be spoiled for choice in Rudesheim. Here’s a
list of things to do on a night out in the town of Rudesheim.

**Drosselgasse**

Drosselgasse is also known as the heart of the nightlife scene in
Rudesheim. This narrow street is lined with bars and cafes and is packed
with tourists looking to have a good time. In fact, this street is
always lively from the day time onward through the late hours of the
night. Music, including live music, is always playing in all of the bars
and cafes. So even if you’ve just come for a walk on the Drosselgasse,
you have already joined the party!

**Brueer’s Rudesheimer Schloss**

Here, you can expect to have a good night out complete with good
food, well-made drinks, and a good selection of beer and wine. Live
bands play excellent music on the weekend and on special occasions. It
is for this reason that a good number of tourists visit this place.

**Dudelsack Fun Rock Café**

Situated in the middle of the Rudesheim area, Dudelsack Fun Rock Café
is one of those places that is always packed to the hilt with locals
and tourists alike. It is interesting to note that this pub has existed
for 14 long years! On a single day of the week, you can come here to
enjoy live music plated by bands. Other activities that you can engage
yourself in include fuse ball, snooker and darts. This pub also gets
very crowded and is filled with heady cheer during sports games which
are aired live on Dudelsack’s wide screen TV.

**Wirtshaus Hannelore**

This particular café-cum-bar is located on the bustling street of
Drosselgasse. Here, you will be able to enjoy traditional German
specialties along with a good selection of German beer. The place also
serves locally made wine. Every night, live entertainment can be found
here in the form of bands that don’t stop the music until the late hours
of the night.

**Adler Café**

Situated at 14 Rhein Strasse, Adler Café is a roof garden which
allows visitors to enjoy a relaxing night out. Adler Café serves up
excellent starters, coffee, beer, wine and other alcoholic drinks. And,
if you happen to be in the area on a Friday night, give Adler Café a
visit and listen to its live music played by a good band.
