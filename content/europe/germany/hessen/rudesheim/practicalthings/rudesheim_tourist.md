They really help in a friendly way with all touristic affairs in Ruedesheim and surroundings.

The **Rudesheim Tourist Office** offers the following services:

◆ All accommodation and services in and around Rudesheim and Assmannshausen (incl. boat tours, cable car, museum visits)

◆ Hotel reservations for individuals and groups (see internet link further below)

◆ Incentive trips

◆ Guided city tours and excursions

◆ Wine- and sparkling wine tasting, wine-seminars, guided walk through the vineyards

◆ Complete travel packages, also prepared for your individual wishes

◆ For business-travellers: seminars and conferences, where others enjoy their vacations

◆ New cultural programs involving the “UNESCO World Heritage” theme for individuals or groups
