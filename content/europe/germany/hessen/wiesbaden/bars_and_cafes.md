---
title: "Bars and Cafes"
type: section
---

Wiesbaden sits at the edge of the **Rheingau**, one of Germany's most famous Riesling regions, and wine bars here are serious about it — ask for a Grosses Gewächs from a local vineyard and you will rarely be disappointed. The cafes along Wilhelmstraße are the city's traditional parade ground; the Kurhaus bar adds a note of formal elegance to an evening.
