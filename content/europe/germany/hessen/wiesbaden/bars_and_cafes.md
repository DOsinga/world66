---
title: 'Bars and Cafes'
type: section
---

Wiesbaden is in Germany's wine country, and its bars and cafes reflect that heritage. Beyond German fare, you will find Greek, Italian and Thai options among others.

**Greco**

The Greco is said to serve the best lamb outside of Athens. All food is cooked in Greek style with traditional seasoning. The restaurant has an intimate feel to it.

**Vapiano**

An Italian restaurant modelled after Rome establishments. All pizza dough and pasta are made on the premises. The restaurant is inexpensive and the food high quality. In addition to Italian wine, the Vapiano showcases wine from the local vineyards.

**Casino Bar**

The bar at the Wiesbaden casino is more formal but very enjoyable. For a fancy night out, the casino bar adds a touch of elegance to an evening, with a wide selection of premium wine and beer and classic decor in high-gloss mahogany.
