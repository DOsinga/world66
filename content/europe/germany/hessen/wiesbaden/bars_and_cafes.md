**Greco**

The Greco is said to serve the best lamb outside of Athens. All food in the Greco is cooked in Greek style with the popular seasoning. The restaurant has an intimate feel to it.

There is also a bar for patrons to enjoy. In addition to Italian wine, the Vapiano showcases wine from the locate vineyards.
