**Wiesbaden Day Trips** includes trip to the best tourist attractions from Wiesbaden, Germany to its adjacent areas. Never miss the opportunity to visit these great places to make your trip complete unforgettable.

**Visit the Fifth Largest City in Germany**

Frankfurt am Main, also known as Frankfurt, is the fifth biggest city in Germany. It takes you about 35 to 45 minutes to reach Frankfurt by train. One single ticket for this is 6.60 Euros. From Wiesbaden, you can have your day trip to this largest city in the state of Hesse. Another pride of the people in Frankfurt is the St. Paul&rsquo;s Church. It is a national important monument in Germany because the first democratically chosen Parliament settled here in the year 1848. Likewise, you can find the famous Alte Oper old here in Frankfurt. It is an opera house that was constructed in the year 1880 by the architect Richard Lucae.

**Mayence Magnificence **

Mainz, called as Mayence in the English term up to the 21st century, is the capital of the German federal state of Rhineland-Palatinate, Germany. It is 14.48 kilometers away from Wiesbaden. You can reach Mainz by train. There are many places that can be visited in this area such as museum, cathedral, tower, palace, building, church, and many more sites. You should not miss to visit the Roman-Germanic Central Museum. This is the museum where you can the Medieval artifacts. One more site that you should see is the Mainz Cathedral of St. Martin. It is a very old cathedral thriving for over 1,000 years now. Furthermore, Marktbrunnen is a must-see because it is one of the biggest fountains in Germany during the Renaissance.
