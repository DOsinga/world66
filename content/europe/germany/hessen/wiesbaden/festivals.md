---
title: "Festivals in Wiesbaden"
type: section
---

**Wiesbaden Festivals** show the richness of their culture and tradition in several of their festivals. Many visitors come to Wiesbaden, Germany to witness their wonderful celebration.

**Theatre Festival**

Theatre Festival is being celebrated in the whole month of May. This festival is purely offered to the theatre in Wiesbaden. Among the planned activities that highlight the events are theatrical plays, dances and all other kinds of performing arts.

**Jazz Festival**

Jazz Festival is one of the music festivals in Wiesbaden that happens in the month of July. Nearly a hundred of jazz band performers all over the world flock together in Wiesbaden to perform uniquely all the types of jazz you want to hear.

**Rheingau Wine Festival**

Rheingau Wine Festival is a ten day Wiesbaden celebration in the month of August. There is an exhibition of local wines and sparkling wine in the surrounding area of the Wiesbaden palace square. There are booths around the area of the palace square and the vintners from Rheingau and Wiesbaden offer their wine and sparkling wine to the visitors. These guests are then invited to taste the promotion of the vintners&rsquo; new vintages. This festival was initiated 30 years ago by the Rheingau vintners and since then it has been a tradition to hold this event. To entertain the guests who take part in this festival, several musical artists are invited to perform in this celebration.

**Rheingau Musik Festival**

Rheingau Musik Festival is a festival in Wiesbaden that has started in the year 1988. This is the biggest musical festival in Germany that the focal point is on the classical music.
