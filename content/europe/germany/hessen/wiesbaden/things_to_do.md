---
title: "Things to Do in Wiesbaden"
type: section
---

Your **Wiesbaden Things to Do** list will consist of a whole range of activities. This southwestern city in Germany attracts tourists due to its rich cultural heritage. Read on to find out the main attractions in this city.
**
Park of Warmer Damm**

A visit to this park is a must. Warmer Damm is a park located right behind the Hessian Theater. The park covers four and one-half hectares of land. The main attraction is located right at the center of the park. There is a pond that covers two blocks of area and has a jet fountain. You will be able to view aquatic birds at this park all year round. The park has tall trees and lush green surroundings. Throughout the park you will find sculpture and other pieces of art.

**Kunsthaus**

Art has no barriers and the Kunsthaus is a prime example of the same. This German art gallery attracts tourists from around the world, to take in the view of this Mecca for art. The building itself goes back to 1863. Today, it gives the budding artists a platform to showcase their talent. You can glance through the various unique and usual exhibits at this gallery. You can also take art classes here.

**Palace Square**

Step out from your hotel and go on foot to the Palace Square. Not only is this a great walking tour but you will also be able to soak in the culture and the life of the locals in Wiesbaden. This is at the heart of Wiesbaden and represents the city life in the region. Filled with restaurants and theaters it makes for a great night out. If you set out in the morning you can scan though the various building and admire the architecture and designs.

**Market Church**

This church is a symbol of both an architectural as well as a social change that took place in the city of Wiesbaden. It stands tall, at 100 meters, and is right opposite to the City Palace. The construction of the church was started in 1852 and reflects various neo-Gothic styles. This is the tallest building in Wiesbaden.

**Market**

Visit the market located adjacent to the city hall and explore the local shopping scene. This market is open on Wednesdays and Saturdays and makes for a great shopping experience. Reach the market early to avoid the rush-hour crowd. The working hours of the market are between 7:00 a.m. to 2:00 p.m. Buy the most fresh and delicious fruits like apricots and berries at this market. There are several local food stalls that are a major crowd pleaser.
