The Wiesbaden **Travel Guide** gives a small idea of the great history of this little unassuming city which entered recorded history nearly 2000 years ago when Romans first built a military settlement here, about that time the mineral springs were discovered here. Since then the city has had a tumultuous history, either being made a capital or being destroyed completely.
