**The Best Time to Visit ****Wiesbaden****
**

If you are planning a family vacation to **Wiesbaden, when to go** is what you need to decide. There are a few things you need to know regarding the local weather and the various events held in Wiesbaden throughout the year. The city is ideal for visiting both in the summer and in the winter. Here are some details:

**Summer**

The summer is maybe the best time of the year to visit Wiesbaden, not only because the weather is great, but also because there are a lot of interesting events going on such as the European Festival for Contemporary Drama, the Wiesbaden Folklore Festival, the Rheingau Wine Festival, and many more. The temperatures are around 30 to 35 degrees Celsius, and pleasant summer rain drops every couple of days. The hottest months are July and August. However, high temperatures and humidity during these months may cause you a moderate discomfort.

**Winter**

The winter is a much more boring season, with the only major event being the Wiesbaden Christmas Market. The winter itself is not too cold, and has moderate amounts of snowfall, which makes it suitable for visiting the city. Snow usually doesn&rsquo;t exceed 4cm per month, and average temperatures rarely drop below -2 degrees Celsius, even in the coldest month of January. However, if you feel cold, you can always use the city&rsquo;s famous thermal springs and warm yourself up. And if you&rsquo;ve had any hidden hopes for practicing winter sports you will be disappointed, as the closest suitable place is located 100km away from Wiesbaden.
