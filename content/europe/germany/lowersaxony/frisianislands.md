---
title: "Frisian islands"
type: location
---

The Frisian islands lying like a pearl cord in front of the Frisian north sea coast attract every year thousands of visitors and overnight guests. They consist of seven islands named: Borkum, Juist, Norderney, Baltrum, Langeoog, Spiekeroog and Wangerooge.

  Just as their sister islands in the Netherlands (the west frisian islands) most of the east frisian islands are famous for their therapeutic bathes and health resorts. Some of the islands are completely car-free, such as Juist, Baltrum or Spiekeroog.
