Because this city is considered a "student-town", there are several
bars and nightclubs that draw young crowds. There are several free
magazines like "Pony", "Diggla" or "Trends & Fun" that you can find
in bars and clubs throughout the city and that give you information
about what's going on in the city that month.

### [Rodeo Bar](/europe/germany/lowersaxony/goettingen/nightlife_and_ente/rodeo_bar)

The Rodeo Bar is a very nice and stylish bar/club in the center of
Goettingen that aims at a crowd older than 21 (no "old" crowd though,
either). They are open almost every day a week. Check out the
Karaoke-Party on every last Thu in the month.

*Best cocktails in town*
