Hannover offers a variety of restaurants, bistros and pubs where you certainly find the menu of your choice. From “currywurst”, a favorite of the German chancellor Schroeder and former prime minister of Niedersachsen, to “foie gras” you will find everything. Try the various beer gardens and locations decorated in a rustic style and enjoy a cool beer!
