16 years ago the Waterloo beergarden opened, today it is a summer institution. They offer several types of beer from the tab (Pils, Alt, Weissbier) and the popular "Altbierbowle" (refreshing but it takes some getting used to). Stop your hunger with pizza, Bavarian snacks, Greek barbecue ir icecream. To know more see www.waterloo-biergarten.de
