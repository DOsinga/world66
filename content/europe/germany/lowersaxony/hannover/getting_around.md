**Suburban Railway: **

Hannover, like all mayor German cities, has extensive public transport system with trams busses and metro lines. They make it very easy to reach most destinations within the city and it’s surroundings without the trouble of having to find and pay for parking places The suburban railway system was finished just before the 2000 World expo so it now is one of the most modern in Europe.
