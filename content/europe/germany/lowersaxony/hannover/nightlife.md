type: section
---

Hannover has a rich and diverse nightlife scene. A large number of bars, ranging from the traditional style pubs to the most trendy of places, many disco's where you can move to the latest grooves and a good number of "biergartens": places where you can have a beer in a pleasant garden-like setting.
