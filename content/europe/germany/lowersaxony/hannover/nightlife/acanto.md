Even competitors must admit - the "Acanto" is the nicest bar in town. This noble bar is situated in the vaults of a former riding-stable. The interior is in Spanish style: lavish curtains, mirrors, chandeliers, fruit-decorations, flowers and candles everywhere. Everything matches the style, even the rest room. And the guests match the noble atmosphere, too. Elegant outfit is compulsory, nobody would wear trainers! Malicious people mumble that guests are bound to pay for the lavish decorations by the list of beverages. But the atmosphere is well worth it. 

Specials: weekend disco from 23h, Monday Cocktail Tasting from19h Beer: Herrenhäuser, Löwenbräu
