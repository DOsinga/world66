It's a different world! The times when this place was a cinema are past and gone. From the very first minute this location was accepted by Hannover's dancing crowd. And since then the party goes on every weekend. The gogo dancers really do their best - as well as the crowd. The "Altro Mondo" has several levels, thus one can wander through the "different world" and watch things going on from different levels. The interiour is also different: The main colours are blue and gold. To mention the music: the organizers believe in superb live-gigs. And the record collection includes House, Blackbeat and Soul.
