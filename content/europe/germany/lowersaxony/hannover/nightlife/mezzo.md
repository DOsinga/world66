This is the place where people meet after their shopping trip or for a chat with friends. Or they spend the whole day sitting by the large windows. The atmosphere is straightforward and for those who sleep long into the morning there is breakfast until 3 o'clock in the afternoon. The Mezzo has become a very special place. During the day you can meet students reading and learning but at night it's very different. You find the Mezzo in the middle of Hannover's "Bermuda triangle" - it's the perfect starting and meeting point for trips to the shops in the nearby shopping centre of the city. During the summer month the outside area of the mezzo is very popular and people can sit here well after midnight.
