A must for all whisky-lovers! Oscar's selection of 600 different whiskies from all over the world is the largest. Certainly, there are really special ones among them. The real connaisseur likes them straight. But also cocktail lovers and others will like it at Oscar's. Well-trained bar keepers will mix any drink foryou as extraordinary as can be. Those who want more than peanuts with the drinks can take one of the delicious snacks offered. Or smoke a cigar for a change? Oscar's offers a noticable selection. Oscar's Art Nouveau atmophere is sophisticated but not posh -it's time for individuality. Playboy magazine declared "Oscar's" the second best "classical" bar in Germany. Weren't they right?!
