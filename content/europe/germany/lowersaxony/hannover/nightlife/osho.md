Nothing much is heard from the Bhagwan fans nowadays, but everybody knows the "Baggy". The large glass rotunda behind the central station still belongs to Germany's Bhagwan discos and has become the meeting point for teenagers in Hannover. But of course there is still the Wednesday. That's when the ex-guest come along to celebrate the "Forever-Young-Party" . They dance to the hits of the old days from the Grease-classics to synthie-pop of the eighties. Nobody will bother about wrinkels in the faces in the bright disco lights. The division of the interior is cleverly made. From the dancing area in the middle you go down a few steps to the full-circled bar and there you can watch what's happening. If you find it too noisy in the daving area, you can go to the first floor for a chat in the Cafe Bar. And after the last song of the night, let's meet at the big cocktail bar for a last drink...
