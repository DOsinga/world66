The Palo has long since achieved a position in Hannover's club scene. There is a wide variety of music styles which delight a large number of fans. During the weekends there is mainly black music - funky and soul. Even famous stars like Mousse T. can by spied at the record players in Palo Palo now and then. So it won't be surprising that the place is somtimes crammed with people and you will get very close to your partner while dancing whether you like it or not. Anyway: the Palo is on top of the charts for Hannover's common night owls!
