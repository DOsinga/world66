Even copying a style can be an art - no other bar owner in Hannover has managed so far to copy the atmophere of Pepe's Mexican Bar. Small, but beautiful - in an ordinary residential building in the Friesenstraße you find a highlight of Hannover's bar scene. The owner used to work as an interior designer - you got it? He really had his fling here. With a lot of care for details and decoration - it's just unbeatable. In this Mexican atmosphere you can sit, listen to folklore and sip one of the seventy cocktails you can find in the list of beverages. And if further wishes concerning the cocktails can be fulfilled the barkeeper will see to that. The menu is typical Mexican. If you don't try the tapas it's your own fault!
