The city of Hannover is literally packed with sights. Ranging from the Old and the New Town Hall to the amazing number of Castles and Churches on such a small surface, not to forget about the largest city forest of Europe. Even if you only have come to the city for a fair or an exposition, take your time to explore the city and its sights.
