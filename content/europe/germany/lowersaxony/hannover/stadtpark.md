A formal park behind the Hannover Congress Centrum, laid out in 1951 for the first Bundesgartenschau (Federal Garden Show) and still carrying the legacy: rose gardens, sculptures, lawns, a Japanese tea house and a pavilion. A footbridge connects directly into the Eilenriede, so you can walk from manicured fountains into deep city forest in a few minutes.
