---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Hannover is greener and more walkable than its trade-fair reputation suggests. The compact reconstructed Old Town clusters around the Marktkirche and the Altes Rathaus, and from there it is a short stroll to the Maschsee lake or into the vast Eilenriede city forest. Add the baroque Herrenhausen Gardens, the Sprengel Museum's collection of modern art and the zoo's themed habitats, and you have enough to fill a weekend without ever leaving the inner ring.
