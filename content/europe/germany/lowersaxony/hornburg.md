Hornburg is an old city, first mentioned in 994. It can be easy reached by car, when you travel from [Braunschweig](/europe/germany/lowersaxony/braunschweig) to [Bad Ischl](/europe/austria/badischl) on the Autobahn 395. You can buy a really old house in this city.
