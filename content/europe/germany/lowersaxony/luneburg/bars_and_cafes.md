---
title: "Bars and Cafes"
type: section
---

The bar scene in Lüneburg is concentrated in two areas: the Stintmarkt waterfront for outdoor drinking in summer, and the streets around Am Sande and Große Bäckerstraße for year-round pubs and cafes. The city has a substantial student population — Leuphana University brings around 9,000 students — which keeps prices reasonable and the atmosphere lively on weekend nights.
