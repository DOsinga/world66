You can get around Salzgitter very comfortably by bus.
As the city consists of separate villages, sometimes a far cry off from one another, it might take you some time to get from one quarter to the other. Depending on your destination you might have to allow for an half-hour or 45 min ride. At night you have to take a taxi. There are no nightbusses.

### [Salzgitter By Bus (KVG)](/europe/germany/lowersaxony/salzgitter/getting_around/salzgitter_by_bus)

The main part of the busses in Salzgitter are run by the regional bus company KVG ("*Kraftverkehrsgesellschaft mbH Braunschweig*").
A KVG branch office is located at Salzgitter-Lebenstedt.
