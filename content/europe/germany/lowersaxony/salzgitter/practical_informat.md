### [Pharmacies' Emergency Service](/europe/germany/lowersaxony/salzgitter/practical_informat/pharmacies_emerge)

In Salzgitter there are pharmacies, that offer emercency service ("***Apothekennotdienst***") at night and on holidays. According to a plan for emergency service they take turns every night. There is a plan each for the northern and the southern Salzgitter area.

Usually it's not the pharmacy store that opens as a whole, but the pharmacist will come to the door upon call. You have to ring a bell at the pharmacy's main entrance. Then the pharmacist will open a small window in or next to the door and ask you for your wishes.
