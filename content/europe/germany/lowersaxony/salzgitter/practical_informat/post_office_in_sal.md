In Salzgitter there is at least one post office in each quarter.
In *Salzgitter-Lebenstedt* for example, you will find the main post office
diagonally opposite the city hall.

**Deutsche Post**;
Joachim-Campe-Str. 1 - 3;
38226 Salzgitter

**Opening hours**:
Monday to Friday: 8.30 a.m. to 6 p.m.
Saturday: 8.30 a.m. to 1 p.m.

To find more **branch offices** you can look at the following site (in German):
www.deutschepost.de/dpag?skin=hi &check=yes&lang=de_DE&xmlFile=304 53
(Click "Filial-Suche" and follow the instructions.)
