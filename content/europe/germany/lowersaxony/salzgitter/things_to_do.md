---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Salzgitter has no single tourist centre — its attractions are scattered across the 31 villages that make up the municipality. The medieval hilltop ruins of Burg Lichtenberg, the Renaissance Schloss Salder (now a local history museum), and an industrial tour of the working steelworks at Salzgitter-Watenstedt are the standouts.
