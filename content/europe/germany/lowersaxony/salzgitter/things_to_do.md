### [Salzgitter Ice Rink - "Eissporthalle am Salzgittersee"](/europe/germany/lowersaxony/salzgitter/things_to_do/salzgitter_ice_rin)

**"Eissporthalle am Salzgittersee":**
If you happen to visit Salzgitter in winter-time, try out the ice rink at the Salzgitter-Lake.
It's roofed, so you can skate even when it is raining.
Every second week, there is an "ice-disco", mostly frequented by teens.

The ice rink is open from October till Easter.
You can look up the current operating hours at:
www.salzgitter.de/frames/sp_auto _6311.html

Equipment: You can rent skates at the ice rink for about 2 Euro an hour. Of course it is cheaper to bring your own, but do you happen [more..](/europe/germany/lowersaxony/salzgitter/things_to_do/salzgitter_ice_rin)
