One of the original bars in the Jungbusch neighbourhood's transformation from working port area to nightlife district. A courtyard bar with outdoor seating that becomes a gathering point in warm weather. The crowd is mixed in the good sense — students, artists, and local workers share the same tables. Serves good regional beers on tap.
