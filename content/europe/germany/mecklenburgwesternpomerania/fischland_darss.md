type: location
---

Wonderful peninsula in the north of Germany made up of the islands of Fischland, Darss und Zingst. 



Great beachesn and good opportunities for hikes. 



Towns include: Dierhagen, Wustrow on Fischland, Ahrenshoop on Darsch and Prerow on Zingst.
