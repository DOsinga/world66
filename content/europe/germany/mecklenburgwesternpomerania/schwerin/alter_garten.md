The Old Garden is Schwerin's grandest square, a formal ensemble of classicist buildings arranged around an open space facing Lake Schwerin. On one side stands the Mecklenburgisches Staatstheater, on another the Staatliches Museum. The square connects directly to the castle bridge, making it the natural starting point for exploring the city centre.
