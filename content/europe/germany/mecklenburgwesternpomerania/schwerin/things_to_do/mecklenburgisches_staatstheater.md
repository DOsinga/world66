This grand neo-Renaissance theatre on the Alter Garten is widely regarded as one of Germany's finest. The programme covers opera, concerts, ballet, and drama throughout the season. In summer, the Schloss Theatre Festival stages open-air performances in the castle gardens, which is a memorable way to spend an evening.
