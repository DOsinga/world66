The State Museum houses one of the largest and finest collections of seventeenth and eighteenth-century Dutch and Flemish masters in northern Germany. The building itself is a handsome classicist structure on the Alter Garten square. Beyond the old masters, the collection includes medieval art, nineteenth-century German painting, and contemporary works. A surprisingly rich museum for a city this size.
