Travel Guide

The seaside resort "Warnemünde" is known to be [Rostock](/europe/germany/mecklenburgwesternpomerania/rostock)'s leisure paradise. In fact it has been part of the city since 1323. Today Warnemundeis one of the most popular seaside resorts on the Baltic Sea.
