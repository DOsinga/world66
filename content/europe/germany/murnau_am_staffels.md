Travel Guide

Choosen for several celebrities, among them, Gabrielle Münter and Wassily Kandinsky, who lived there for many years, Murnau stays on the way from Munich to Garmisch-Partenkirchen and Oberammergau region.

Situated only 45 min by train south Munich and just a a few kilometers from others well known touristic spots from Bavaria, Murnau with around 12.000 inhabitants, is situated at the foothills of Bavarian Alps with its romantic lakes and breathtaking landscapes.

Many pituresque small villages around and great nature, Murnau is place also of many cultural events year around.

Due to its central location, Murnau can easily be the place for those who wish to explore this part of Bavaria that includes Garmisch-Partnkirchen and the Zugspitz (the tallest mountain in Germany), Oberammergau, Several monateries and the castles of Ludwig II.
