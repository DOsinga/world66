---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment in North Rhin"
type: section
---

Even though the atmosphere is very businesslike during the day, **Bonn Nightlife** is a whole new story. Considered as the 19th largest city in Germany, Bonn is a great place to let your hair down and enjoy the lively nightlife. Read on to find out the top pubs and clubs in Bonn.

**Heimatlos**

Heimatlos is located in the Rheingasee area in Germany. Heimatlos is a lounge bar that is open on Thursdays, Fridays and Saturdays. This place is buzzing with youngsters wanting to have a good time. Beer and good music make for an enjoyable experience. This is one of the most high-profile lounge bars in all of Bonn. You can enjoy the music, which is not too overbearing, and have a decent conversation with your friends. The food served here is delightful, with international dishes like kebabs, mashed potatoes and lamb chops.

**Braugaus Machold**

Get a feel of an authentic German pub at Braugaus Machold. This pub was an exclusive brewery, and now has accommodated this spacious pub in its premise as well. The crowd here is more relaxed. Located in Heerstrabe, it is a favorite local hangout in Bonn. The bar stirs up some great cocktails. The beer is served chilled and at the right temperature. The pub opens at 7:00 p.m. and goes on way past midnight.

**Havana**

Havana is a popular nightclub in Bonn, and a famous spot on the Clemens Street. This club is considered to be one of the more upscale clubs in Bonn. The crowd here comprises of the cr&egrave;me de la cr&egrave;me of society. Havana has a great in-house DJ that plays both German as well as other popular western music. Hip Hop, rock, and other genres find their places on the playlist. Havana is a Cuban-style club that serves great drinks and stays open for late nights.
**
Carpe Nocteum**

If you are looking for an affordable night out, then head to Carpe Nocteum. This is a discotheque located close to the main station. Carpe Nocteum is famous amongst the college crowd, as the rates are not very high. The music played by the DJ is entertaining and maintains a highly energetic atmospheric in the clubs. The drinks served here are not very expensive. If you wish to party and dance till the break of dawn, this is the place to be in Bonn.

**Jazzgalarie**

The Jazzgalarie is the place to go to if you want to spend a romantic night out with your loved one. This is more like a dance club, but plays a lot of romantic numbers. Jazz music is most preferred by the patrons at this club. The club offers beer, wine and champagne, and has the best food menu in town.
