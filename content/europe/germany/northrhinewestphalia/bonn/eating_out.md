title: "Eating Out in Bonn"
type: section
---

**Bonn restaurants** offer the discerning holiday maker a diverse choice of cuisine to suit any occasion. The high standards of food and customer service make Bonn restaurants some of the top in Europe and with many offering great views over the Rhine River, you will find that you have ideal, relaxed dining options when you choose to stay in this city.

#### Italian Bonn Restaurants

One of the top Italian restaurants, recommended by visitors, is the Ristorante-Pizzeria La Vita. This is an inexpensive option and perfect for family meals. You will be served fresh fish, handmade pasta, and original homemade pizzas. There is a warm atmosphere and friendly staff.

Another great Italian option, although more expensive, is Il Gambero Rosso, which is family-run and friendly. The food is classic Italian style with a flair for seafood dishes.

#### Trendy Bonn Restaurants

The Rohm&uuml;hle is a perfect choice located right on the Rhine River in an old converted warehouse. It is a sophisticated, family-orientated restaurant, also offering a bar and caf&eacute;. There is a buffet selection for brunch and a great choice of menu items, which many people enjoy whilst on vacation. They are famed for their Sunday brunch and you can choose to sit in the large dining room or outside on the balcony.

There are over 300 000 students in Bonn which also contributes to the large number of trendy eateries and cafes in the city. These are also largely pubs and bars that offer food and you can find most of these in the Poppelsdorf area.

#### Gourmet Bonn Restaurants

Bonn is well-known for its gourmet cuisine and for having the largest concentration of gourmet restaurants in the country. The Bad Godesberg area provides the largest number of foreign cuisine restaurants in Bonn, including Greek, Italian, and Spanish among others. In the town square or Altstad area you will find many gourmet German-style restaurants like the Restaurant Strandhaus. The various hotels in Bonn also offer up market restaurants like the Hotel Gunnewig Residence, Rheinhotel Dressen, Restaurant Godesburg, and Em Hottche, to name a few.

With the variety of cuisine options including traditional German, to other Western foods, and more exotic options, you will easily find something to suit you and your family when you visit Bonn.
