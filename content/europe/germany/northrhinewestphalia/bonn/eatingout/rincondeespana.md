Very cosy and rather small Spanish tapas restaurant, lots of people, in summertime you can outside. Open every day besides monday till 1.00am. Expect to wait some time for a table - drink a "Kölsch" at the bar and wait, till the waiters show you your table, they have a fabulous overview above their customers, they are fast, friendly and a good reason to come back again.
