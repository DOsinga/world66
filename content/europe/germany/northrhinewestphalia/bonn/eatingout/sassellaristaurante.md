Excellent and expensive! This restaurant (in Kessenich) is one of the few with national reputation in Bonn. The kitchen is Italian of course, on highest level. Very good choice of wines (I like the Sfursat Valtellina, about 80,- DM/bottle - very rich and heavy).


 You can go there by car or tram (61/62 leave at stop "Pützstrasse"); 10 min from city center.
