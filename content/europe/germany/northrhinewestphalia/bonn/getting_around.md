There are different ways that you can get around in Bonn Germany, and some of the **Bonn transportation** that you can choose includes trains, busses, taxis and rental cars. You can choose the best way to travel around the city when you are there.

#### Train

The Bonn railway station has many features that you can find, including a service center that you can use before you head out for your journeys. If you want to find a more scenic route along the area, you can take the train along the Rhine and enjoy the scenery in the area while you travel. There are trains that go to most of the locations around Bonn, so it is an easy way to travel. You can find the train services to be affordable.

#### Bus Service

You can find that one of the easiest ways to get around in Bonn is the bus service. There are buses that go from the airport to hotels and many other parts of the city, which makes it easy to find one that is going in the direction you need. There are many stops, so if you want to stop to eat or shop, you can find another bus to catch to take you to the hotel. It is also affordable and one of the Bonn transportation ways that a lot of people choose when they are visiting the city.

#### Bicycling

If you want to explore the city of Bonn up close, you can choose to rent a bicycle. There are many people who bike everywhere around the city, and the streets are narrower than in some areas; biking is one of the best ways to get around and be able to stop whenever you want. You can also enjoy the many places to see wherever you are biking.

#### Taxis

You can find a taxi service in Bonn to take you where you need to go if you do not want to drive or take the train. It is more expensive than some of the other transportation options, but you can get the taxi service to pick you up at your hotel and take you to where you are going.

#### Rental Cars

You can also find rental cars that you can use when you are staying in the city. You may have to pay more if you travel a lot of miles, so if you are planning on traveling around the area, you may want to consider taking the train. But, if you are only going to be driving in the city, you may find it to be one of the best Bonn transportation ways to get around.
