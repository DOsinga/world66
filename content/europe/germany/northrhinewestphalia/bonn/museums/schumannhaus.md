Former lunatic asylum in Endenich where Robert
 Schumann spent the last years of his life (1854-1856).
 It houses the municipal music library and a memorial
 to the composer.


 Sebastianstraße 182.


 Opening hours: Mondays and Fridays 10-12, 4-7;
 Wednesdays and Thursdays 10-12, 3-7.
