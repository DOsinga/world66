---
title: "Alter Zoll"
type: poi
category: Sight
latitude: 50.7330
longitude: 7.1050
---

A remnant of the old city fortifications turned into a terrace with one of the finest Rhine views in western Germany. On a clear day you can see the Siebengebirge hills to the south. Locals bring beer and blankets on summer evenings -- it feels more like a park than a monument.
