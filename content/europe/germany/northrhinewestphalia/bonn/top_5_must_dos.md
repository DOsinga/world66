---
title: "Top 5 Must Do's in Bonn"
type: section
---

The **Bonn Must Do's** list is vast. There are many things to do
like visiting the botanical gardens to visiting Beethoven House.
**Poppelsdorfer Schloss Botanical Gardens**

The Poppeldorfes Schloss is a palace that was built for the Bishop of
Bonn. The palace became part of the University of Bonn and now it
houses the scientific achievements of the university. The botanical
gardens have 8,000 species of plants and a greenhouse is currently being
built.

Poppelsdorfer is a common destination for people who love classical
music. The performances take place every night of the summer. The
Poppelsdorfer Schloss and the botanical gardens are located on
Mekenheimer Allee 171, 53115 Bonn.
**Beethoven Haus
**

This house is where the musical genius was born. Many of his personal
possessions are on display. His last piano and his musical instruments
are still found in the house. The house has a small garden.

The Beethoven House is located on Bonngasse 20. Tourists can reach
Beethoven Haus by tram number 62 or 66.

**The Beethoven Festival**

The Beethoven Festival has a special place on Bonn must-do list. This
festival is held during the months of August and October. There are
many international orchestras, soloists and music composers who pay
tribute to Beethoven's music. One of the most renowned directors is
Gustavo Dudamel, a director for the Los Angeles Philharmonic.

The Beethoven Festival takes place at several locations in Bonn. Some
performances are given at the Pantheon, Beethoven Hall and the Post
Tower Lounge.

**Rhein in Flammen**

The Rhein in Flammen is a fireworks display that usually takes place
on the beginning of the month of May. Street vendors sell food and there
is live music being played. People can also enjoy the fireworks while
boating on the river. The boats need to be booked in advance. It is
possible to have dinner on a boat during the fireworks show.
**The Hardtbergbad
**

The Hardtbergbad should be included in any Bonn must-do activities.
There are many swimming pools in the Hardtbergbad. Kids can enjoy the
pool that has a 30-feet-high water slide. There is also a water-jet area
where children can splash each other.

The view from this location is breathtaking since the Hartbergbad is
located on top of a mountain. The Cologne Cathedral can be seen from
here.

The Hartbergbad is located in Dehlen, Bonn D-53125.
**Reinisches Landesmuseum
**

This museum has many different archaeological displays. One of the
most rare is the skull of a Neanderthal Man.

The Museum is located at Colmastrabe 14-16, 53115 in Bonn Germany.
