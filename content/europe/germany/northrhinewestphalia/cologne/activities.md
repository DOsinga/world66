**Activitys**

**Cycling along the Rhine**

Possessing an extensive network of bicycle lanes extending right into the city centre, Cologne can easily be explored on two wheels. What about taking a cycling tour along the Rhine? Bicycles can be hired at the Rheinpegel (water-depth gauge) in the Old City.  

**Swimming to Music**

Anyone attending one of the local swimming baths should be prepared for some unusual "side-shows" taking place at the edge of the pool; for example, you may find yourself celebrating an "Italian Week" under the motto "Una festa in piscina - aqua e musica". **Jogging in a team**

All the paths and rambler's trails are also suitable for joggers. Particularly popular are the routes along the river. Many of Cologne's most picturesque areas have been given over to clubs to stage weekly jogging meetings (free of charge). **Inline-Skating Around the Local Lakes**

To address the growing number of locals resident s and visitors (of all ages) are taking up inline-skating as a leisure pursuit, many organised sports clubs have begun by offering skating tours - for example, around the Fühlinger Lake.
