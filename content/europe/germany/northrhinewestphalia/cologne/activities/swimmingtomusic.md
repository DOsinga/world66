Anyone attending one of the local swimming baths should be prepared for some unusual "side-shows" taking place at the edge of the pool; for example, you may find yourself celebrating an "Italian Week" under the motto "Una festa in piscina - aqua e musica". By staging a series of such events and concerts, the municipal pools authority, KölnBäder GmbH, is hoping entice more landlubbers into the water at any one of their 11 indoor pools, three so-called combi-pools, an open-air pool and four natural pools. In addition there are other amenities including the adventure pool Aqualand and the Claudius-Therme thermal baths in the Rheinpark. Due to its treacherous currents, we advise against swimming in the Rhine.
