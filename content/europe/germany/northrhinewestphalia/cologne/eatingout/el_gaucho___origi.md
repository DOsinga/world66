Argentinian angus steaks, prepared on a central, open charcoal fire.
Watch and enjoy the taste of these Argentinian specialties - just like
gauchos in the pampas.
For some there is the best steaks of the world. El Gaucho not only stands for quality dining, but is also a part of authentic Argentinian culture in Germany and Köln (Cologne / Koeln).
