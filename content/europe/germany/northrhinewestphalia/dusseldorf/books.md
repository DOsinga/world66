These are some great books to help you in your travels in and around Dusseldorf.

### [Dusseldorf (CitySpots)](/europe/germany/northrhinewestphalia/dusseldorf/books/dusseldorf_citysp)

Dusseldorf,
released by the Thomas Cook Publishing Company, is a travel guide in
the CitySpots series. The book is 144 pages long, and measures 6.2 x
4.2 x 0.5 inches. It will easily slip into a back pocket or a travel
bag, such as a backpack or attaché.

The City of Dusseldorf itself is
one of Germany’s two economic centers, and is renowned for its many
fashion and trade fairs. It's one of the major fashion centers of
Europe. The CitySpots guide will provide you with ample details on
fashion event dates and locations, along with detailed maps of the City
and [more..](/europe/germany/northrhinewestphalia/dusseldorf/books/dusseldorf_citysp)
