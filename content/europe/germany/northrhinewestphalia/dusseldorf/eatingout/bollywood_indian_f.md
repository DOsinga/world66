BOLLYWOOD has established first of its planned 200 fast casual restaurants in Königsallee, Düsseldorf. The concept works exactly as it would in Mc Donalds but with a difference. You get served piping hot food at unbeatable prices. Currys, Kababs, Tikkas, Vegetable dishes, Rice and things just keep coming. Since its opening in November 2006, it is just running full all the time. It has become a favourite eating place for Stock brokers, Bankers & Lawyers from Düsseldorf. Youngsters and old keep on thronging in to this place. Ideal destination for travellers to have high class food at the budget prices.
