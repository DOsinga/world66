Chanakya is a chicly decorated Indian restaurant, with a glass fronted kitchen where you can see all the food preparation and Tandoori cooking taking place. The regional lean to the cuisine is North-Western Punjabi, but they change the menus with the seasons. The restaurant is roomy, so is good for business and popular with the celebrity set. Catering for private parties of up to 150 guests is available upon request. It is undoubtedly the best Indian restaurant in the mainland Europe .
