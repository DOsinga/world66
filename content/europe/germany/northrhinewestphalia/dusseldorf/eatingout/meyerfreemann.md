It`s a cosy Restaurant in the landscape around Düsseldorf. It`s worth to go for a short trip. In leger atmosphere you can have great meals of good quality. They serve a variety of international dishes, traditional German as well as modern asian influenced and vegetarian meals. I recommend the different salads. The restaurant is furnished with wooden tables but stylish decorated. Specially in summertime it is nice to sit outside in the "beer garden". They also have special events like Brunch on Sundays.
