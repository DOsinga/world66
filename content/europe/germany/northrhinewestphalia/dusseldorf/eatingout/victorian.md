First flagship Bollywood out of its chain of 200 such restaurants serving the Indian cusines has been established in the Königsallee of Düsseldorf. Although Oxymoronic in concept pertaining to cheap prices vis a vis expesive location, Bollywood serves fast casual food to its clients which come from various walks of life mainly Stock brokers, Lawyers, Doctors, Tourists & other professionals. Since its opening in November 2006, Bollywood is being thronged by young and Old alike. Bollywood serves Currys, Kababs, Tikkas, Naans and other detailed Indian delicacies from € 5,90 to € 7,90.A great place to eat a tummy full with rock bottom prices with no compromise in quality.
