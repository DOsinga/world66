We eat here on our last night staying at Hotel Renaissance Marriott Dusseldorf, which is 3 minutes walk away. What a pity we would have come here several times. Real Italian food cooked by a team of genuine Italian chefs! You know the food is going to be good when you see members of the Italian community & their children present!

Don't be put off by the simple decor of the restaurant. Look to the trendy bar and the open kitchen where you can see your dinner being prepared!

We both had the same meal : Spagetti scoglia-which was spaghetti with a variety of shellfish. A very generous portion with lots of seafood (10 Euro) followed by Steak-pan-fried with mushrooms, potatoes & fresh veg. The waiter said the steak was 200g- but in fact mine was about 300g & my husband's about 275g. ( 15 Euro)We only ate the steak & mushrooms as we had eaten all the pasta it was so good!! Even though the steak melted in the mouth we were unable to finish.

Unable to eat any of the gorgeous desserts on offer as we had already had a lot to eat we asked for 2 Grappa to finish. A lovely golden colored Grappa was produced, not the usual clear one. We agreed the best Grappa we have ever had. With the meal we shared a bottle of red wine from Sicily.

This restaurant is fantastic value for money- if you are a real fan of Italian food you will be very pleased. Next time we will definitely be back here!
