---
title: "When to Go in Dusseldorf"
type: section
---

Located along the River Rhine, Dusseldorf is a
glamorous city, and one of the most well-known towns in Germany, due to its fashion and
trade fairs, and high quality of life. With mild temperatures year-round, any
time of year is a good time to visit Dusseldorf.

However, you might want to plan your trip during one of Dusseldorf’s world-famous festivals in July
or November.

**Dusseldorf****’s Biggest Funfair on the Rhine**

Every July, Dusseldorf hosts what is known as “The
Biggest Funfair on the Rhine,” between July 18
and 26. This fun carnival, which started in 1316, takes place at
Rheinwiesen, and attracts more than 4 million people annually. The
family-friendly event features thrilling roller coasters and rides, and more than
300 booths, attractions and beer tents. The celebration lights up the sky with fireworks on July 24. With temperatures averaging in the
mid-70s during July, this is a fun time to visit Dusseldorf.

**Carnival and Street Parade in November**

If you are up for traveling in the winter, November is another good time to
visit Dusseldorf.
With temperatures averaging in the mid-40s during November, weather is mild
enough for traveling and touring Dusseldorf’s
many museums, art galleries, concert halls, shops, brew pubs and more. Dusseldorf hosts a
fantastic carnival with a street parade in November, which leads up to the
Christmas markets.

**Christmas Markets in December**

Tens of thousands of visitors shop for hand-made goodies, snacks, and gifts at Dusseldorf’s Christmas
markets. The Christmas markets, which are open every year between late November
and late December, can be found throughout Dusseldorf, with most of them centralized
downtown. You’ll find all kinds of Christmas and Hanukkah celebrations
throughout Dusseldorf
in November and December—including mulled red wine, roasted chestnuts and
festival holiday lights.

**Year-Round Sight-seeing**

Dusseldorf is a
lively city with several major attractions to visit any time of year. There is
unlimited entertainment, sight-seeing, shopping and dining to do in Dusseldorf whenever you
go. From old town to castles, museum, concerts, art galleries, parks and more,
Dusseldorf is a
fascinating vacation destination for people of all ages from all over the world.
Only one thing is guaranteed: the longer you can stay, the more fun you will
have in Dusseldorf.
