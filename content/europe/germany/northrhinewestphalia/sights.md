North Rhine-Westphalia (german: Nordrheinwestfahlen short:NRW) is one of germanys most beautiful landscapes. Here you get everything you need to life, lets start with the food: german food is quite deliciouser than most of the people think, the >>restaurante food<< (roasted potatoes with ham and eggs, or Pigshaxe (Schweinshaxe) with Sauerkraut) just as the cheaper food like frikadellen or... the legendary currywurst. If all these food don´t hit your taste you can also go to one of the thousands delidiouse Dönershops or Pizzerias.

But food isn´t the only beautiful thing at NRW, the landscape is beutiful, even the industrieal area in the ruhrgebiet has his one charme, people who like nature more than sights can see the awesome rhine landscape or take a spades-nod at a cow feast or one of the many little floats that flow through NRW.

The people in Germany are very friendly, if you have a question, don´t be shy at asking someone. Nobody will kill you.

People who are interested in history should come to north Rhine-Westphalia too, here are many museums of the first and second world war, german Artists like Goethe Haydn and Co. You can see in north rhine-westphalia also many fantastig buildings, it is worthwhile itself if you visite this buildings, almost in everyone you can arrange a guide.

The Traffic in NRW is easy but genius, the highwaynetwork is very big so you can get fast to almost every place in NRW.

If you want to see the highest highlight of NRW, just visite Cologne, Wesel or Xanten. Here is everything perfect, you can get very good food and beer, can see many sights because of the romans visite in Cologne and the landscape is beautiful because of the rhine, the Aue and the whole city of colongne.

But you shouold decide on your own, just visite it, you won´t be disappointed
