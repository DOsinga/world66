---
title: "Eating Out"
type: section
---

Koblenz cooking is hearty Rhineland fare — saumagen (stuffed pig's stomach), sauerbraten, and potato dishes that fortify you for a day on the river. The wine culture is serious, and most good restaurants carry extensive lists from the nearby Moselle and Rhine regions. The Altstadt has a cluster of traditional Weinstuben and restaurants around Am Plan and the Jesuitenplatz.
