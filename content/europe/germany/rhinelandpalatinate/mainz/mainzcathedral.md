The Mainzer Dom is a massive Romanesque cathedral begun in 975 by Archbishop Willigis. It has been rebuilt and expanded many times over the millennium since, blending Romanesque, Gothic, and Baroque elements. The interior houses significant medieval tombs and reliefs. Along with the cathedrals of Speyer and Worms, it is one of the great Imperial Cathedrals of the Rhine.
