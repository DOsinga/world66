---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Mainz rewards walking. The cathedral, the old town, the Rhine promenade, and the Gutenberg Museum are all within easy reach of each other. The churches are the standout sights — from the massive Romanesque cathedral to St. Stephan's Church with its luminous blue stained-glass windows by Marc Chagall, installed in the 1970s and 1980s as a gesture of Jewish-German reconciliation.
