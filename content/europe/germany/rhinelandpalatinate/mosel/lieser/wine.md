Lieser has a number of vineyards of national and international reputation. Here are a few that can be visited, including wine tastings.

**Weingut Sybille Kuntz**

Sybille Kuntz and Markus Kuntz-Riedlin run this winery to a high international standard, producing great, typical Mosel Riesling from Lieserer Niederberg-Helden, Lieserer Rosenlay, Bernkastel-Kueser Weisenstein and others.

Moselstr. 25, 54470 Lieser. Tel: +49-6531-91000.
