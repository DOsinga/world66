Nurburgring nightlife is mainly made up of watching rabbits and squirrels eating. However, there are some tastier options...

The fabulous *Shagkurve* is a mecca for the area's dogging community after 10pm. Herr and Frau Schmidt are renowned for their tantric sessions in a 2CV, which on occasions have lasted anything up to 9 hours, and attract a huge crowd.

At the other end of the chicane, Ben Doon and Phil McCavity are best avoided.
