---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment in Trier"
type: section
---

For young couples and couples without kids the **Trier Nightlife** is something that you need to take part in. Trier nightlife is not just focused on clubs and bars; you also have the option of going to the theater or even the movies.

**Exhaus**

If you are looking for the party spot, Exhaus is the place to be, despite the fact that it is not a club. The Exhaus is booked year round for a variety of concerts and other entertainment gigs. With the year-round booking and the wide range of events that are booked here, you are sure to find something that you will enjoy.

**FORUM**

If it is a dance club that you are looking for FORUM is the place to go. This dance club features international DJs, which means you will be in for a wide variety of music to dance to. If theme parties are to your liking, you can check into the Forum’s schedule of events to see what theme parties are coming up. Not only does Forum offer international DJs it also offers live music on different nights of the week.

**Theater Trier**

If you want to see an opera or even a ballet, this is the place to go. The Theater Trier offers you the chance to see things that you wouldn’t normally see at home. The nighttime shows consist of operas, ballets, theater and on occasion concerts. The lineup is constantly changing, so be sure to check out the schedule ahead of time to see what is playing when you come into town.

**TUFA-Tuchfabrik**

The TUFA, which is also called the Arts Center, is a great place to go if you are looking for a little bit quieter nightlife. TUFA is not a nightclub nor is it a theater, but it does offer you a variety of events. You can go to TUFA and catch live concerts, cultural events and even some theater.

**Walderdorff’s**

If you are looking for a nice place to grab a quick drink or even a few drinks, Walderdorff’s is the place to go. This pub is located in the Old Town of Trier and has plenty to offer its customers. Not only will you find plenty of drink choices, but you can also be treated to live bands or DJ nights. If you come by in the earlier part of the evening you can sometimes catch an after-work party going on.
