Worms offers something for every taste and every purse. Do you like good and heartwarming German food (with a home-brewed beer, perhaps)? Or are you inclined towards more international tastes like Thai-Food or Italian cooking? In any case, you will not be disappointed. Make sure to pay a visit to one of our ice cream parlours, famous throughout the region.
