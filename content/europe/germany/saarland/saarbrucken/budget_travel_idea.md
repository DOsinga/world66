**
Travel Budget**

When going to Germany by plane, search for airlines offering budget prices. There are several travel packages offered by various airlines for trips to Saarbrucken Airport. Your country of origin will determine what airlines to look for. You can travel cheaply by train or buses. Their transport system is efficient and disciplined, so you cannot lose money by missed train rides.
**
Food and Lodging**

To ensure that you get the regular rates from hotels, book earlier. On the spot reservations are generally more expensive. There are hotels, like Hotel Am Bahnhof, which have rates as low as $29 per night. This hotel is even close to the train station. The completely renovated Hotel Bruchwiese has comfortable beds for only $34. Luxurious hotels cost around $80 and above, per night. You can take advantage of budget meals from the following inexpensive restaurants: Alex Brasserie, the Asian Restaurant and Va Piano, where meals can cost somewhere between $15 to $24. Remember to try their succulent Schwenker, which is the steak specialty of the place.
