**Saarbrucken Restaurants** are varied, and they range from being completely casual to providing more elegant delicacies. No matter what tourists might like, Saarbrucken has the perfect place to dine on a variety of cuisines.

#### Burgerhof

Burgerhof is a casual burger and bistro restaurant located at Geisberg 2, Saarbrucken, 66132, Germany. Burgerhof is famous for their delicious sausage burger. Dining at the Bugerhof will cost around $20.00 USD per person.

#### Pizzeria Soma

Located at Dudweiler str 74, Saarbrucken, 66111, Germany, Pizzeria Soma is a sports-bar-style pizza parlor with music and games. Whether tourist want to play pool or darts with their friends, or just relax while drinking a beer and watching a sports game on the television, Pizzeria Soma is a deliciously fun shop in Saarbrucken, and will only cost around $30.00 USD per meal per person. This is one of the most up-and-coming of Saarbrucken restaurants.

#### Kimdo Sushi Bar

The Kimdo Sushi Bar is a very up close and personal experience, with authentic professionals trained in the art of sushi making. The Kimdo Sushi Bar only accommodates seating for 14 customers at a time, so it is easy for anyone to look behind the sneeze guard and see how the delicious and beautiful sushi rolls are made.

Kimdo Sushi Bar serves all kinds of sushi, sashimi, sake, and fine wines. Located at Mainzer Str. 61, 66121 Saarbrucken, Saarland, Germany, Kimdo Sushi Bar costs around $90.00 USD per person, but such high-quality sushi is quite hard to find outside Berlin. Kimdo Sushi bar adds an exotic taste to Saarbrucken restaurants.
