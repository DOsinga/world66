---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment in Saarbrucken"
type: section
---

The **Saarbrucken Nightlife **scene is one of the most varied in
Europe. There are many nightclubs that can attest to this. These
nightclubs have go-go dancers, cheap drinks and different themes.

**Kufa House**

This night club is located on Dudeweiler Road, Number 7. It is
recommended that tourists use a taxi cab from the hotel. This club plays
different types of music each night--Hip-Hop, house music and Rock.
Patrons must pay three euros as a cover fee and consume at least two
euros-worth of food or drink.

The Kufa House sells different types of beer and vodka.
**Cannossa Sports Bar**

People can reach this bar by taking the A1 motorway from the north.
This sports bar is located near Saarland University. Patrons can watch
live sports and eat salad and spaghetti. There is an "all you can eat"
spaghetti special for only five euros. The bar has different music shows
and cover price varies each night. This bar makes the Saarbrucken
nightlife events very exciting.

There are different events that are held at Cannossa Sports Bar.
People can dance salsa one night and on other nights they can listen to a
live rock band. The Sports Bar has a disc jockey and a live band. The
bar can be rented to celebrate birthdays and any special occasion.
**N-8 Night Club**

This club adds variety to the Saarbrucken nightlife scene. There are
different events that are held at this club. One night the band plays
house music and on others, they play all the club hits from the 1990s.
Some of the time they have topless waitresses and go-go dancers. The
club also has Havana club nights were they serve mojitos for two and
four euros. They play Reggae and Latin-style music.

The club is located on Saint Johanner Street and Trierer Street.

**Blau Night Club**

This is one one of the most amazing clubs that make up the
Saarbrucken nightlife variety. The Blau Night Club can be reached taking
the commuter train, the S-Bahn, line 1. Patrons should get off the
train when reaching Kaiserstrasse.

This nightclub has two main dance floors--the Flamingo Lounge and the
Theater. The Flamingo Lounge is pink and it's decorated with pink
flamingos. The lounge has couches for resting and smoking. The Theater
has LCD lights and the whole room catches a blue color when the lights
go on.
