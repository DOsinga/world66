By simply reading the Museum's title, one may think this is an
exhibition devoted to brushing your teeth and staying away from fats
and sugars. Really, the Museum is a facinating place devoted to
the study of Human Anatomy and life science in general. One can
explore the innerworkings of the human body and modern medicine.
