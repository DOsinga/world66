Pirna is a picturesque small town, about 15 km away from the city of [Dresden](/europe/germany/saxony/dresden) and the ist is best starting point for exploring the "Sächsische Schweiz" national nark. Translated into English the national park would be called "saxon switzerland". This name was carried out by two swiss artists who stayed in Pirna region in the 18th century. Having had a look to the beautiful landscape the had been reminded to their home region.

In Pirna you can stroll around through small and narrow streets in the old centre of the town and have a drink in one of the numerous bars. It is a beautiful town with all the things you need to spend a relaxing holiday.
