**general**

**Gaststätte "Zum Ritter"**

Historical castle tavern right in the city centre. 

address: Sternstr.

tel: (03 45) 2 94 30 27

zipcode: 06108 Halle (Saale)

**"Gasthof zum Moor"**

Opposite to the famous castle "Giebichenstein", very cosy atmosphere, two lounge rooms with 80 seats each, nice beergarden with 200 seats. 

address: Burgstr. 72

tel: (03 45) 5 20 00 33

zipcode: 06114 Halle (Saale)

**Restaurant "Ratsherrenklause"**

Right in the middle of the city centre this restaurant in an old vaulted cellar offers plain German dishes and traditional atmosphere. 

address: Rathausstr. 14

tel: (03 45) 2 08 27 58

zipcode: 06108 Halle (Saale)

**Ristorante italiano Rialto**

Italian specialties and a nice view on the five towers of Halle 

address: Marktplatz 24

tel: (03 45) 2 02 41 91

zipcode: 06108 Halle (Saale)

**Restaurant "Baba Jaga"**

Russian and Ukrainian specialties. 

address: Moritzzwinger 11

tel: (03 45) 2 90 66 72

zipcode: 06108 Halle (Saale)

**Gasthof "Pulvermanns Grab"**

Furnished in an old fashioned way this restaurant offers plain German food like ages ago. 

address: Howorkastr. 20

tel: (03 45) 5 32 22 88

zipcode: 06118 Halle (Saale)

**Localität "Spätschicht"**

Strange but exciting mixture of tavern, smithy and metalworking shop, styled in a design of the 1920s, in summertime there is a nice beer garden with 60 seats.  

address: Torstr. 20

tel: (03 45) 2 90 38 88

zipcode: 06110 Halle (Saale)
