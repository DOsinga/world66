Magdeburg's major forms of public transportation are trams, busses and S-bahn (or fast trains). The tram network is extensive. Tickets must be purchased in advance at a "Kiosk" or automated machines which can be found at most station stops.

  Students get a semester ticket at registration and can use any local public transport for the whole semester, free of charge as often as they wish.
