The **Schleswig-Holstein must do&rsquo;s** is a simple guide to the activities you can engage in while visiting this magnificent vacation destination. Schleswig- Holstein, Germany is a &ldquo;Children friendly&rdquo; region ideal for family or group vacations. The region offers various water and land-based activities for children, events for extreme sports fans, as well as health conscious people and amateur sports participants. With its romantic courtyards, historic manors, clean beaches, lush countryside and quiet fishing ports, guests can relax and enjoy a quiet time away from the din of city life.

**Take Part in Kiel&rsquo;s Biggest Yachting Festival**

This capital city of Schleswig-Holstein celebrates traditional festivals, water sports, and music, which tourists can attend. Kiel Week is one of Europe&rsquo;s best and biggest yachting festival. During the last week of June Kiel City becomes the Mecca of regatta sailors all over the world. You can either join in the sports or just be an enthusiastic spectator.

**Schleswig-Holstein Music Festival**

Experience excellent music played in extraordinary surroundings during musical and variety shows and watch for the best musicians who play in this world-renowned music festival. Your tour is not complete without listening to the awesome collections of music.

**Tour Historical Places and Buildings**

Schleswig-Holstein is host to numerous historical buildings and places. Visit Kiel, the capital state, and the navy monument. The Schilksee district where the Glucksburg Rosarium is located, and the Schleswig Palace, Haithabu Viking Museum, and the Reinsburg Canal should also be visited. The stronghold of the Wagrians in Oldenburg and the Pion castle are great sightseeing destinations, too. Lubek became famous as a UNESCO Heritage Site in 1967.

**Visit Waden National Park**

This park deserves a slot in your itinerary. Explore the Outdoor Adventure at Waden National Park. Ride a canoe and tour the long beaches, coasts, and lake shores of this region. Surrounded by the verdant forest and the lakes in this region, you can do nature-tripping, hiking, camping, or picnicking, and you can also visit the enchanting extensive lowland plains by motorbike or bicycle, or if you are athletic and adventurous, you can tour the countryside by walking. This national park holds hundreds of bird species; it has the densest bird population in Europe.

**Schleswig-Holstein Theme Parks**

There are many leisure and theme parks for children and parents as well as for grandparents, Schleswig-Holstein even boasts of having the steepest wooden roller coaster in the world. People of all ages can enjoy other types of theme park activities. There are extreme adventures for thrill seekers who enjoy having their hearts in their throats with amazing experiences of a lifetime.
