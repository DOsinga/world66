**Galerie Werkstatt Caroline Rügge **

The gallery inside a beautiful Old Town building presents artistic jewellery, containers and objects. You can purchase Karin Bablok pottery. Fascinating necklaces are available. The works of various guest artists are also very tempting - they complete the changing exhibitions here. 

address: Hüxstraße 63

openings: 11am-6pm Mon-Fri; 11am-2pm Sat and by appointment

**Galerie Koch-Westenhoff **

The gallery mainly presents 20th century works of art. You will find paintings and sculptures of various styles here. If you are looking for a particular piece, the staff will assist you in tracing and purchasing the desired object.

tel.: +49 451 72808

address: Hüxstraße 29

openings: 10am-6pm Tue-Fri; 10am-2pm Sat
