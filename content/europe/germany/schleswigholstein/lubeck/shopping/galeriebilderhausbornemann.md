The Bilderhaus Bornemann is located within historic walls and offers nice old objects like pieces of furniture and small antiques, as well as recent works of art. A permanent exhibition presents international artists and their works, representing different styles. Among the artists shown here are Rainer Liebhold and Anna Barry of Lübeck with some of their works, Marianna Fedorova from Munich with her gouaches, Irina Kolesnikova from Moscow with woven pictures and Ellen Shire from New York with her acrylic and wax crayon pictures.
