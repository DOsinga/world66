Visitors will find beautiful oil- paintings in various sizes at the tiny "Mystik" gallery. The pictures will inspire the observer to dream and send him or her on a mystic journey. But other objects are also to be found here in addition to those fantastic works. Apart from Celtic jewellery there are also magical and artistic amulets from various cultural circles. But if you haven´t got enough room at home or lack the money to purchase these works of art, you can also get mystic illustrations and pictures in the shape of postcards and little posters here.
