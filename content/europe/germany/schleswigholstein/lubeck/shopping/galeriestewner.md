This gallery is located inside an Old Town building which has been put under a preservation order, and it has beautiful objects in perfect shapes on display - modern skilled crafts like pottery, glass objects, jewellery, tapestry and curtains, but also sculptures. Paintings and other works of art are presented in changing exhibitions. Old Tibetian glass pearls with silver and Karneol details have been arranged into beautiful mystic necklaces which you can purchase.The pleasant atmosphere of the showrooms makes you feel comfortable, and you will get some good advice here.
