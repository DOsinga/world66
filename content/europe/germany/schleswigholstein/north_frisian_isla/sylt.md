Travel Guide

Beautiful Frisean island at the German North Coast with amazing beaches, impressive landscape of dunes and perfect summer weather. Sylt is also called the dearest island of Germany.

What the Hamptons are to New York in the summer that is Sylt to Berlin, Hamburg, Munich and any other town which boasts a high society.
