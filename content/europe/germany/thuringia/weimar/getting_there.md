**by train:** 

Weimar can be reached via direct routes from [Dresden](/europe/germany/saxony/dresden), [Berlin](/europe/germany/berlin), [Leipzig](/europe/germany/saxony/leipzig) and [Erfurt](/europe/germany/thuringia/erfurt), and from the west from [Frankfurt](/europe/germany/hesse/frankfurt) or [Kassel](/europe/germany/hesse/kassel). After the new section from [Nuremberg](/europe/germany/bavaria/nuremberg) is completed, there will also be a train route through Erfurt from the south. 

**by car:** 

Weimar lies along the E-W route of the three-lane highway E 40/A4. From the north take E 49/E, 51/A 9 ( [Leipzig](/europe/germany/saxony/leipzig)) to the Hermsdorfer interchange and then E 40/A4, - from the east ( [Dresden](/europe/germany/saxony/dresden)), just take E 40/A4. 

Finishing the approved Weimar bypass is a top priority of the federal government's highway planning. This will directly connect the industrial park area on the north side of Weimar to the Autobahn.
