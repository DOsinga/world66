Schiller moved to Weimar in 1799, drawn partly by Goethe's presence, and lived in this yellow townhouse on the Schillerstrasse until his death in 1805. The house is smaller and less grand than Goethe's, which suits Schiller's more straitened circumstances. His workroom, where he wrote *William Tell* and the *Ode to Joy* (later set by Beethoven), is preserved as he left it. Worth seeing alongside the Goethe house to understand the friendship between the two men.
