---
title: "Low Cost Connections"
type: poi
---

Making low-cost connections can often work out significantly cheaper and in many cases is the only way of getting between European cities unless you live close to a major low-cost airport. Some search engines can help you to find the cheapest connection between your departure and the destination.

Almost all low-cost connections go without any problems. However, you should leave enough time between them, at least two hours, because you will need to check your luggage in for each segment of the journey. You may also check the possibilities to insure against missed connections.
