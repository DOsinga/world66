title: "Taxistop / Eurostop"
type: poi
url: "www.taxistop.be"
---

A system that brings hitch-hikers and drivers in contact via internet. The hitch-hiker pays 3 euro per 100 km. Drivers and hitchers can put desired rides online, the site does the matching.
Especially useful for travel to and from Belgium.
