You will visit the **Rock's tunnels**, **Punta Europa**, **San Miguel caves **and **monkey reserve**.

**Gibraltar** is still of enormous strategic importance lying on a narrow neck which separates **Europe** from **Africa** and provides the only link between the Atlantic Ocean and the Mediterranean Sea.
