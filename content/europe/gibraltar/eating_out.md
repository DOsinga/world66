**Gibraltar**** restaurants** exhibit a variety of flavors and tastes. It is a multi-cultural country, so its restaurants cater to all the ethnic and international cuisines. There are several fast food chains in Gibraltar like Pizza Hut, McDonalds and others. However, if you want a fine dining experience, Gibraltar has a lot of options for you.

**Waterfront Restaurant**

Waterfront Restaurant is located in Ragged Staff Wharf, Queensway Quay, Gibraltar. It is open daily from 9am and beyond. This restaurant has been in the food industry business for more than 14 years. This restaurant offers local and international cuisines and serves enticing desserts that will keep you coming back. The restaurant personnel will welcome you with extraordinary manners and outstanding service. Not only that, the Waterfront Restaurant is located in a marina area that gives you a good ambiance as you savor the taste of your food. After your meal, you can go to a bar area at the terrace where you can have an informal drink. From the terrace, you can enjoy the scenic view of the marina and the sunset behind the mountains.

**House of Sacarello**

House of Sacarello is an eating place in Gibraltar located in Irish Town. It is a bistro-style restaurant that offers vegetarian selections, coffee, fresh soups, sandwiches, scones, salads and more delicious dishes. Many people come to visit this restaurant because of the great taste of their coffee and tea. In addition, House of Sacarello also serves excellent food at a good price.
