When planning a vacation to the tiny coastal British colony of Gibraltar, you should include places on our list of **Gibraltar Things to do.** Known to the world as the last British colony in Europe, Gibraltar is situated on the southern part of the Iberian Peninsula.

**The Rock of Gibraltar**

One of the first things you want to do while in Gibraltar is to visit the Rock of Gibraltar. The rock sits atop and overlooks the blue waves of the warm strait that cuts Gibraltar from the African continent. It offers a wonderful view for leisure walks and hiking. Barbary Apes or Macaques also thrive in the area. It also has been a delightful tourist attraction as some are fed by vacationers themselves.

**Gibraltar Museum**

Take a trip to the Gibraltar Museum. The museum is located at the heart of Gibraltar town on Bomb House Lane. The Gibraltar museum was founded in the 1930s and keeps a significant number of historical and cultural items. Model cannons used during the battle called the Great Siege of 1779 to 1783 are on display. Turkish baths also add to the delight of tourists. The baths were recently been discovered and are considered to be in such perfect preservation they may be the best in all of Europe.
