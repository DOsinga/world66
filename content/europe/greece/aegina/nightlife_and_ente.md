---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment"
type: section
---

Night life in Aegina gets particularly lively during week-ends,
when the Athenians travel to the island. Atmospheric bars, places
proposing live Greek music, pubs and clubs can be found in the capital
or in the touristy and busy coastal resorts where the hotels are also
located.
