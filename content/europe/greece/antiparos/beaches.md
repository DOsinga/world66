---
title: "Beaches"
type: section
---

Antiparos has a handful of quiet, tree-lined beaches with clear water and very few crowds. Most are on the southern and western coasts and can be reached by moped or on foot.

**Psaralyki** is the most accessible — two adjacent beaches close to town with shallow water, pedal boat rental and space for volleyball. The northern stretch is popular with families; the southern end is clothing-optional.

**Soros**, on the south-west coast, has crystalline water and two simple tavernas. It is laid-back and sparsely visited.

**Glifa** sits in a rural setting with views across to Paros. Southern trees provide shade in the afternoons. Ideal for families wanting solitude.

**Panagia** is the most peaceful — it empties out in the strong afternoon sun, leaving you more or less alone with the big shade trees and the water.
