Mimi`s Restaurant is famous for its Barbeque Pork, Lamb, Chicken and French Fries with grated cheese on top of them. It is in the suburb of HALANDRI. You ask anyone there and they will tell you where it is. It stays open until midnight. The best time to go is from 8:30pm-9:30pm.
