**Athens Festival**

This festival, running from the 12th of June until the 5th of October, takes place in various theatres scattered throughout town. **Wine Festival**

If you are in town between mid-July and September, take a bus or taxi to the suburb of Dafni for the annual wine Festival. It is a jovial afffair where wines can be tasted free of charge.
