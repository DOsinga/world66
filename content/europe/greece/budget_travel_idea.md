Venture
out on a** Greece Budget Travel **adventure by following the
pointers below. Greece is a beautiful holiday destination, filled
with history and culture.
**
****Getting
There**

If
you are traveling from the United Kingdom, Ryan Air and EasyJet are
the 2 carriers that provide great deals. Tourists from the United
States of America will be able to find good deals online. Make sure
you book in advance to avail the discount fares. The most economical
way to travel from Europe is to take a train to Italy and proceed to
Greece on a ferry.**
****
****Stay**

Normally,
Greece is considered to be a rich man's paradise. The hotels scream
luxury and are expensive. But there are a few hotels that will be
perfect for your Greece budget travel plans. The Attalos is a budget
class hotel in Athens and will cost you around 30 to 35 euros This
hotel offers great value for money. The hotel is well-maintained with
modern and comfortable rooms. Attached bath, free Internet access and
a fantastic view are some of the offerings of this hotel.

**Eating
Out
**
Greece
is a food lover's paradise. It has restaurants that cater to every
budget. From the swanky, upmarket restaurants to the street food,
this place has it all. Paradosiako is the epitome of good food at
affordable prices. Run by a husband and wife duo, this place is
famous for its seafood. Highly sought after by the locals as well as
tourists, this place offers fresh and perfectly cooked seafood in
true Greek style.

**Getting
Around**

You
can rent a car to get around Greece, but an economical mode would be
to take the public transport. The whole of Greece is well connected
by roads, rail and waterways. The buses will take you to all the
famous attractions in Greece. They are well-maintained and air
conditioned. The train will take you to places like Tripolis and
Kalamata and are a highly inexpensive mode of transport. The
public transport system will be a blessing to your Greece budget travel plans.

**Sightseeing**

Hit
the Cyclades, which is a cluster of islands in the Aegean Sea. This group of
islands is filled with Greek mythology and history. The best way to
access these islands is by taking a ferry ride. Get the tickets for
the deck category because this is the cheapest and easiest way to get to
the islands. If you wish to travel in the cabin class or take the
high speed jets, then it would be advisable to book in advance as you
will get cheaper rates with advance bookings.
