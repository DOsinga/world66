A small village on the west coast, tucked into a narrow isthmus between the sea and a wooded promontory topped by a Venetian castle. The castle is free to walk up to and the views across the bay are worth the climb. The village below has a handful of tavernas and colourful fishing boats pulled up on the shore. Quieter and less visited than Fiskardo.
