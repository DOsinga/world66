title: "Melissani Cave"
type: poi

latitude: 38.2543

---

An underground lake inside a collapsed cave near Sami, where the ceiling caved in millennia ago leaving a roofless chamber open to the sky. Boats row visitors across the turquoise water; in the middle of the day sunlight hits the lake at an angle that turns it an electric blue. The cave was used as a sanctuary of the god Pan in antiquity — ancient votives were found here in the 1950s.
