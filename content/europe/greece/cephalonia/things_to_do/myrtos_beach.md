---
title: "Myrtos Beach"
type: poi
category: Sight
latitude: 38.3588
longitude: 20.5283
---

One of the most photographed beaches in Greece — a long crescent of white pebbles and turquoise water framed by steep limestone cliffs. The best view is from the road above on the way down, where you can pull over and take it all in before descending. The beach itself has no shade and the pebbles are large, but the water is exceptional. Gets busy in July and August; arrive early or come in the shoulder season.
