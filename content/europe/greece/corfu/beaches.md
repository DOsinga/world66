---

Most of the islands of [Greece](/europe/greece) are to the South and Southeast of the
mainland, but [Corfu](/europe/greece/corfu) is located in the west, in the Ionian Sea. The
Ionian Sea is a beautiful body of water located between Greece and
southern Italy and is just as blue as the Mediterranean Sea that it is connected
to. The island is actually off of the coast of Albania, but Greek
ferries travel there daily and the history of Corfu ties in strongly to
the history of Greece. The warmest months are between June and
September, making it the perfect time to visit Corfu's popular
beaches.

**Ipsos Beach**

If you'd like to visit a beach where shops are within
walking distance, visit Ipsos Beach in the village of Ipsos. A road
runs along the entire length of the beach (which is quite long and
relatively narrow) and tourist-type shops, cafes, bars and small
markets line the area. The beach is pebbly, which means the ground is
cooler (you will be thankful for this after visiting some other
beaches) and chairs are set up all around the beach.

**Liapades**

Liapades is
a stunning beach on the north part of the island. This is the perfect
spot for those that don't care for the excitement brought on by large
groups of tourists. This is a great beach to relax and take in the
scenery in a peaceful manner. Chances are some of the only people you
will see on this beach are snorkelers which is a popular activity for
Liapades. This beach is close to the town of Corfu. Locals refer to
this area as "The Ropa Valley".

**Messonghi**

Messonghi beach in the town
with the same title is a beautiful resort beach. The town started as a
small village with mostly fishermen, but now tourism has found it's way
into Messonghi. The great thing about Corfu, though, is that tourism
may be popular on the island, but it is not a highly industrialized
area and most of the beaches are extremely clean and safe. Messonghi
beach has everything a lounger or a sportsman would want. If you'd like
to get your heart racing, rent one of their motor boats or jet skis. If
you'd like to just relax, take a dip in the sea and have a good read in
your sun-chair.

**Acharavi**

For families, try Acharavi beach. Equipped with sun-chairs and numerous activities, people of all ages can enjoy a day
here. Most everything you would need is located within walking
distance, including banks, hospitals, and grocery stores.
