---
title: "Day Trips"
type: section
---

While in Corfu, you have an opportunity to take some interesting
excursions to other islands in the area. Some of the most popular day
trip destinations are the island of Paxos and a trip to nearby Albania.

**Albania**

Excursions to Albania take you to the archaeological site of
Butrint. Take a ferry to Sarande which leaves daily at Corfu from 10 AM
and returns at 4:30 PM in the summer and 2PM in the winter. It takes
over an hour to get to Sarande. You will then travel the rest of the 12 miles
by car over some pretty rough road. Butrint was an important city
during the Roman Empire and was resurrected after the Dark Ages as an
Ottoman port. You will see a Greco-Roman theater that is amazingly well
preserved, yet it floods often making it look like a lake. A 6th
century baptistry is nearby and has a floor with depictions of
peacocks, trailing vines, grapes, urns, and other geometrical patterns
done in mosaic. This was the private baths of someone rather rich in
the town.

**Paxos**

Several excursion companies in Corfu organize day trips to the
island of Paxos, but you can get there yourself if you wish. Simply hop
on a seaplane and it only takes 15 minutes as opposed to an hour by
hydrofoil. Olive oil from Paxos is used in many
different products from shampoo to hand lotion. You can purchase these
products on the island. This is a tourist island and the people and
vendors there are very friendly and helpful. The beaches are beautiful
and quiet and most are rocky, but the water is so
clear that you can't help but jump in and swim. You can charter
a boat for about $40 to $60 for a trip around the island. You will get views of the western caves and cliffs and some
excursions actually go inside some of the caves so you can see the rock
formations.

In Corfu you can find many different excursion companies that take
you to Albania and Paxos and other small islands in the area as well as
to the mainland of Greece. The cost varies but this is a good way in
which you can see all there is to see in and around Corfu.
