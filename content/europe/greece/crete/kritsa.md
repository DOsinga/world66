The best resource for sights, hotels, restaurants, bars, what to do and see

Kritsa is a very picturesque little town at some 12 kilometres SW from
Agios Nikolaos. Build amphiteatrically on a large jutting promontory,
you will notice this splendid white location from afar.

The town is considered one of the most important centres of Cretan folk and weaving art.
