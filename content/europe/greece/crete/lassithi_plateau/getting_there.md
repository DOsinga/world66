**By bus

**Cheep and confortable KTEL busses to the Lassithi plateau run from [Heraklion](/europe/greece/crete/iraklion) bus station A once
a day.
.

**By car**

From [Heraklion](/europe/greece/crete/iraklion) ->E, most direct road.

Take the main coastal road -> E direction Agios Nikolaos. Just
before Hersonnissos, you'll find a crossroad leading to Lassithi plateau and the Dikti Mountains.
It's a bit of a steep climb to the Kera pass, from where you have a magnificent view over Lassithi plateau.

From Agios Nikolaos ->W, most direct road

Go W direction [Heraklion](/europe/greece/crete/iraklion), after some 10 kms head for Neapolis and
follow direction Tzermiado-Lassithi plateau. The road is it is asphalt
paved but rather narrow and winding.

From [Heraklion](/europe/greece/crete/iraklion) (->E) or Agios Nikolaos (->W), via Malia

Malia is about half way on the main road from Heraklion to Agios Nikolaos. Follow route signs Tzermiado-Lassithi plateau.

From [Heraklion](/europe/greece/crete/iraklion) ->S via Kastelli

Kastelli is on a less frequented paved secondary and alternative road from Heraklion to Tzermiado.

By following this, your trip will take more time but you will pass
through the villages of [Knossos](/europe/greece/crete/knossos), Peza, Aghies Parakies, Kastelli,
Potamies and finally get to Tzermiado.
