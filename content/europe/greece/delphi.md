title: "Delphi"
type: location
---

Delphi is primarily famous as a shrine to Apollo and for its Oracle. A priestess of Apollo, called the Pthia, would sit in a grotto and under the influence of foul smelling, intoxicating gasses she would come up with predictions of ones future - this is the site where Oedipus was told his terrible fate and Socrates that there was no man wiser than he! 

The pythia is gone of course, but the grotto remains and a lot of other Greek sights. There are temples, there is a running track (the Pythian games of Delhi were almost as important as the Olympics) and a lot of columns.
