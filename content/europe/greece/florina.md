Florina is located in the North-west corner of Greece, close to the border with {Macedonia}. The area is very beautiful, with rugged mountain scenery, great lakes (Mikri and Meghali Prespa - small and Big Prespa). In Mikri Prespa you will encounter a little-known small isle, Aghios Achileios, with a gorgeous view of the region.
