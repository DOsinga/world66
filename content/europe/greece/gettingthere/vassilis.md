The main entrance to Greece is Athens featuring its new, modern and
highly efficient airport, called Eleftherios Venizelos. Olympic
airways will take you to El. Venizelos from all major airports in
Europe. For information on flights outside Europe, please contact
your country's air carrier. Also, during peak holiday season, many
charter flights can be found directly to your holiday destination (
there is an airport on nearly every major island) or connect flights
around Greece.
