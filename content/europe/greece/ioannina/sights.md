**Lake Pamvotis and the Island**

The Lake Pamvotis is a trully ornament for Ioannina city. Its natural beauty makes difficult the comparison with other sights of the city.

In the middle of the Lake, there is an island that you should definitely visit! You can walk in the traditional streets, eat fishes and traditional dishes of the city, visit the great and historical museum of Ali Passas and a number of monasteries situated in the island.

**The Castle**

The Castle of Ioannina is one of the oldest castles in Greece. The old city of Ioannina is situated inside the Castle, with traditional houses, museums, mosques etc. Many historical elements still leave their presence, a piece of experience that is worth visiting!

**Cave of Perama**

The Cave of Perama was created a thousand years ago, but was discovered in 1940 by accident. The Cave is full of stalactites and stalagmites giving an unusual atmosphere in the place. Do not miss it!

**Pavlos Vrellis Museum**

It is one of the best museums in the whole world! The museum of wax effigies describes the history of Epirus and Greece. Impressive creations that you have to visit with no doubt!

**Other Museums**

If you love history and culture, then you should definitely visit the museums of the city that are remarkable sources of knowledge about many periods of the greek history, but also works of old and modern greek artists.
