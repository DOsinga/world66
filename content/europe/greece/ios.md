Mountainous Ios, with its green sea and beautiful beaches, is popular with a young crowd. Milopotamos is the place most people head for and offers sand, sun and quite a nightlife, if not on the beach than in one of the bars and discos. The island's main town is white Chora where one can spot the ruins of an old Apollo temple and the church of Panaghia Gremiotissa. You can either take the bus or hire a motorbike in Yialos (main port) and explore Plakato, where you can visit the tomb of the poet Homer (that is, it is claimed to be his tomb.)
