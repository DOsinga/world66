---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

Ios is connected to the rest of the Cyclades by ferry and high-speed hydrofoil from the port of Yialos.

**Ferries** run daily to [Piraeus](/europe/greece/piraeus) (7 hours), [Paros](/europe/greece/paros), [Naxos](/europe/greece/naxos), [Santorini](/europe/greece/santorini), and [Mykonos](/europe/greece/mykonos). Less frequent services connect to Sikinos, Folegandros, Anafi, Syros, Amorgos, [Milos](/europe/greece/milos), and [Crete](/europe/greece/crete).

**Hydrofoils and catamarans** offer faster connections to Santorini (30 minutes), Naxos (50 minutes), Paros, Mykonos, and other Cycladic islands.
