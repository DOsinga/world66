---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment"
type: section
---

Ios is as famous for its nightlife as Mykonos or Paros.
Especially in July and August, the island is full of people who have
come there to party and have fun all night long in the many clubs and
bars, and to leave the excitement in the extreme sports proposed in the
beach resort of Mylopotas.
