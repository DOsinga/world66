**The Gold Club: The Best Place to Enjoy a Sizzling Night in Katerini, Greece**

The **Katerini bars and cafes** provide abundant entertainment for nocturnal tourists. Katerini is a town in the Central Macedonia in Greece and has a breathtaking view of Mt. Olympus as well as old Greek architecture found all over the area. While Katerini is famous for its scenic sites by day, you can still enjoy Katerini at night by visiting the many bars and cafes that dot the center of the town. The best place to spend your night in Katerini is the Gold Club, where the best happening in town occurs.

#### **The Gold Club**

The Gold Club bar is located at Katerinis-Paralias Street near the old church. A lot of young locals and tourists frequent this place due to its fine interiors and exquisite ambiance. It is open from 10 p.m. until 1 a.m., and this is the best place to mingle with savvy locals and tourists who want safe and good fun in Katerini. However, drinks in this bar are expensive and can range between $20 and $30.

#### **Activities **

There are a lot of things to do in the Gold Club. Aside from drinking local and tasting international beverages, you can enjoy live music entertainment played mostly by local bands in Katerini. The club also has a big dance floor where you can simply dance your night away. If you get tired, you can sit and relax on the many stuffed chairs in the bar area, where you can sip your drink while engaging in conversation with your friends.
