type: section

**Katerini restaurants** are ready to serve you the most delectable dishes in town. In Katerini, you will never get hungry.

#### **Giouras Taverna
**

Giouras Taverna offers a different menu each day of the week. The restaurant personnel are welcoming and friendly. They are always willing to attend to every customer&rsquo;s need. The atmosphere is nice. You have the option to stay outside for a candlelit dinner or just stay inside the restaurant. Giouras offers large portions at lower prices. They prepare Greek dishes like Papoutsaki, Greek salad, tzaziki and scumbie. You must try their Papoutsaki, an eggplant stuffed with meat and cheese. They also serve seafood such as sea bass, fried calamari and grilled octopus. After your meal, a fruit dessert sweetened with traditional sweets is served. You can also try the traditional drink, Ouzo. In 2010, the menu ranges from $11 to $20 USD.

#### **Ice Factory
**

For a light meal, try Ice Factory for ice cream, cakes, sandwiches and other sweet food. You can purchase any of these at a very low price. These foods are perfect for light meals or for a snack. It is quite hard to communicate in English with some of their staff. However, you can still purchase the food you want by pointing to it. You must try their croissant filled with cream and fresh strawberries. You should also taste their ice cream, which is offered in a variety of flavors.
