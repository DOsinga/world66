Considering your **Katerini when to go** options, you should know that different seasons are suitable for different leisure activities in Katerini. As a tourist destination, Katerini is popular both with its beaches and with its sightseeing possibilities. The city experiences typical Mediterranean climate with hot summers and mild winters. Here is a detailed overview of the two different seasons, showing what leisure activities they are suitable for.

**Summer**

The summer is the best season to visit Katerini, if you want to take advantage of its excellent beaches. There are 3 beaches located about 5 to 6km away from Katerini. These are Korinos, Katerinoskala and Paralia. The air temperatures are excellent for having a nice and sunny day on the beach, with average levels of 25 degrees Celsius (77 degrees Fahrenheit). At the same time, the skies are clear and no clouds can hinder you from sunbathing on the shores of the Aegean.

**Winter**

The winter is a better season to see the nearby sights around Katerini, as it doesn&rsquo;t feel very comfortable to travel by road during the hot summer days. These sights are the ancient town of Dion and the Venetian Castle of Platamon. The winters are mild, but rainy, so all you need to do is to get familiar to the weather forecast and ensure that there won&rsquo;t be strong rains on your way to both sites. Otherwise, the temperatures are excellent for longer day trips, as their average values are about 7 degrees Celsius (45 degrees Fahrenheit).
