---
title: "When to Go"
type: section
---

High season is **June to early September**, when the beaches are at their warmest and the mountain hiking trails are all open — July and August bring Greek summer holidays and the coast gets busy. For climbing Mount Olympus, aim for late June through early October, when the upper refuges are open. Spring (April–May) is beautiful for the lower slopes and wildflowers. In winter the high peaks are capped in snow and only experienced mountaineers should attempt the summit.
