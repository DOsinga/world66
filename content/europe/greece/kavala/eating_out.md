Kavala, a picturesque prefecture which also serves as the main sea port to eastern Macedonia, is situated in the Bay of Kavala, and below are some of the **Kavala restaurants**. Famous for its varied ancient heritage, Kavala is very famous amongst tourists for its beaches, famous roman ruins, medieval castles and other attractions. Given the number of tourists who visit Kavala, there are several restaurants available here offering different cuisines. **
**

**Agora**

Situated in the Chrysoupoli municipality in the prefecture of Kavala, Agora is a small and beautiful restaurant offering its guests an exquisite selection of Greek wines along with cured meats and delicious cheese plates. If you dine here on Wednesdays, you can enjoy the live bands playing Greek music. At Agora, you can also enjoy some of their local specialty dishes. Visit the restaurant with your family or enjoy a special evening with a loved one; Agora has perfect settings for any type of occasions.

**Zafiris**

Situated in the Kavala municipality, the Zafiris restaurant is a perfect place to visit if you want a taste of local Greek cuisines and Greek wines. Zafiris also offers you a wonderful view from its restaurant. Enjoy their local dishes and salads prepared with Greek feta cheese. This is the best place to be is you want to try out some local delicacies in the Kavala municipality.

**Krampousa**

Krampousa is another famous Greek restaurant situated in the Thasos municipality of Kavala. Krampousa offers its guests a wide variety of cuisines to choose from. Famous for its local cuisines and Greek wines, this is a perfect place to be in Thasos if you want to enjoy authentic Greek cuisine. Try out their Greek Coffee, which is another specialty at Krampousa.
