title: "Getting Around in Kavala"
type: section
---

**Kavala transportation** will ensure that you have a wonderful and safe travel in Kavala, the second largest city in the northern Greece. Kavala City offers you the best transportation as you have fun touring around and enjoying this attractive city.

**Taxi**

Taxis in Kavala take you to your destination at a cheap fare. You should be aware that there are two rates of taxi meters in Kavala. The first rate applies from 5am till midnight. The other rate doubles the normal rate from midnight to 5am. In the city, taxi fare fraud is not prevalent. Yet, it still happens sometimes, so make sure that the rate given to you is correct. If you feel you have been charged higher than what it should be, you must ask for a receipt because they are obligated to give one. After that, take the plate number of the car. You need to report the driver to the tourist police.

**Car**

There are rental cars offered in Kavala. As you stepped out of the airport in Kavala, there will already a group of rental cars that are waiting for you. These rental cars have a range of fares that matches your budget. You must be very good and patient in scouting for the car that has the cheapest fare to bring you to your desired destination.

**Ferry Boat**

Ferry Boat is the means of transportation that is used to travel from Kavala to other island, such as Keramoti, Thassos, and Primus. There are also hydrofoils available to make your travel short.
