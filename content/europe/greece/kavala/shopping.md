---
title: "Shopping in Kavala"
type: section
---

The seaport city of Kavala is located in northern Greece. **Kavala Shopping** can range from open air markets to regular malls. Kavala is one of the most beautiful cities in Greece, and shoppers will find unique souvenirs to bring home.

**Agiou Nikolaou Market Area**

The Agiou Nikolaou Market is the oldest market area in Kavala. The market is located next to the Acropolis. Currently the market is undergoing a renovation. A number of trendy shops and restaurants have relocated to this area as the rents are cheaper and the location is better. Being near the Acropolis has opened up a number of opportunities for both businesses and shoppers. The shops sell souvenirs that emphasize the Acropolis and the history of Greece. Visitors can start their day by having a coffee in one the cafes, take in a little history and then tour the shops to see what treasures can be found.

**Kavala Muncipal Shopping Center**

The Kavala Muncipal Shopping Center is the largest shopping district in the city. This district contains a number of stores which sell everything from food to clothes. A shopper looking for wine to bring home will have a wonderful selection here. People on limited budgets can use the food shops to supplement their stay. There is a wide selection of jewelry and leather goods for sale. There are a number of shopkeepers who also sell souvenirs for people to take home. A savvy shopper might find an ancient Greek inspired ceramic to bring home or a tapestry to adorn a living room. Visitors can end their day with a stop at any of the open-aired restaurants or have a dance in a club once their shopping has ended.

**Panagia District**

The Panagia District is famous for two things--tobacco and marble. There are number of tobacco shops in this area that sell tobacco for pipes and cigars. For cigar aficionados the stores in this area can expand on their collections and knowledge. Some stores offer cigar-making courses for anyone interested in the art. White marble is the other export that makes Kavala famous. Known for having the whitest marble around this district is important to both leisure and business travelers. A savvy shopper can arrange for items to be shipped home.
