The city's main archaeological museum holds finds from the wider Kavala region spanning prehistory to the Byzantine period, with the strongest material from the ancient cities of Neapolis (the Greek precursor to Kavala), Philippi, and Amphipolis. Sculpture, ceramics, jewellery, and coins are well displayed. Closed Mondays.
