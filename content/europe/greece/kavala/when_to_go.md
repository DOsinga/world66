**Sun and Fun in the Greek Land of Kavala**

Settle the **Kavala when to go** question in your mind by reading this quick guide. At Kavala you will be able to witness the old and the new. You will find modern buildings and shopping squares on the western part of the city and the traditional houses and gardens on the eastern part. This town in Greece has the most beautiful view of the harbor and has several old castles and fishing areas.

**Tourist Season**

The months of July and August witness a major inflow of tourists. The weather is perfect for a holiday and both locals as well as foreigners come to Greece to take advantage of the sunny weather and beach related activities. It is important to make all hotel reservations in advance as you will not find on-the-spot accommodation during this period. The streets are crowded and restaurants are bustling with activities. This is the time when young people travel to Kavala and the nightlife is the most colorful.

**Beat the Crowd**

If you wish to avoid the crowd and are looking for a more relaxed vacation then May to June or September to August would be the apt time to visit Kavala. The weather is conducive and the beaches and historical attractions in Kavala can be enjoyed to the fullest. The nightlife picks up pace during the weekends. The prices of all things starting from hotels and restaurant to sightseeing packages are economical during this time. November to March is not a great time to go to Kavala as winter sets in with frequent showers.
