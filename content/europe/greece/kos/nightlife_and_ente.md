Being the second most popular and touristy island of the Dodecanese
after Rhodes, Kos has an intense and various nightlife. Most of the
lively bars and nightclubs of Kos are concentrated in the capital, Kos
Town and in the touristy resorts of the island. Bars are open until
very late in the night and clubs are partying until dawn.
