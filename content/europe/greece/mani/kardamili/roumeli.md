The companion volume to *Mani*, covering northern Greece — Epirus, Macedonia, the monasteries of Meteora. Together the two books are Leigh Fermor's love letter to a Greece that has since changed beyond recognition. If you've finished *Mani* and want more of the same voice and the same deep attention to landscape and history, this is the natural next step.
