---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

Meteora is most easily reached via the town of Kalambaka. Trains run from Athens (about 4.5 hours) and Thessaloniki (about 3 hours) to Kalambaka station, which sits right below the rocks. Buses from Trikala (30 minutes away) run several times daily. By car from Athens, it is about 350 km via the E92 motorway — roughly four hours. From Kalambaka, local buses run up to the monasteries, though service is limited and a car or taxi gives much more flexibility.
