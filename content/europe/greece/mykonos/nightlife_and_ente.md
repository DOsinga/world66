In the summertime Mykonos has for sure the most vibrant nightlife in
the whole Greece and probably, together with Ibiza and Ayia Napa, in
the whole Mediterranean.

Usually Mykonos nights start at 6-7 p.m. with a few parites on the
beach and last until 7-10 a.m., when the best party animals on the
island are still dancing on the various dancefloors. The clubs/discos
listed below will be categorized as:

- Pre-dinner (crowded between 6 p.m. and 10 p.m.)

- Post-dinner (crowded between 1 a.m. and 4 p.m. circa)

- Discoteques (crowded between 3 a.m. and 7-10 a.m.)

You'll find several PRs both on the beaches and in Mykonos Town that will advise you on
the best parties that are held every day, so just listen to them and
take your decision.

### Skandinavian Bar-Disco

Is the best post-dinner place, located in the centre of Mykonos Town. The Skandinavian is composed of 3 parts: 2 bars on the ground floor (free entrance) and one  dance floor at the 1st floor (entrance fee from 4 euro, depends on what you drink). Usually it gets crowded at 12-1 a.m.
