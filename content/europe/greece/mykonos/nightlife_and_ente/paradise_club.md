Paradise Club is a discoteque (open from 00 a.m. to 6 a.m.). The No1. Club in Mykonos with an amazing line up!

Entrance fee: Free in the afternoon, 15-20 euro at night.

In Summer 2006 played Erick Morillo, Junior Sanchez, George Morell, Todd Terry, Kenny Dope Gonzales, Matt Myer, Sander Kleinenberg, Spiller, Francesco Farfa, CJ Macintosh, Steve Edwards, Barbara Tucker, Chris Montana and many other Deejays.
