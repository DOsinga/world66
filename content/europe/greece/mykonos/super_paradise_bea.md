About 9 km southeast of Mykonos Town, Super Paradise is the island's most free-spirited beach — nudism is common, particularly at the far left end, and the crowd skews young and uninhibited. The water is a clear deep blue, the setting backed by dry hills.

Two beach clubs share the bay: Jackie O' and Super Paradise Beach Club. Both run from mid-morning into the evening with DJs, cocktails, and dancing that can get surprisingly serious by mid-afternoon. Sun beds and umbrellas are available through the clubs, though the space between them is technically free.

Getting here without a car requires either the caïque boat that runs from Platis Gialos (the most enjoyable option), or a taxi. The road down from the main island road is steep and winding. In high season, arrive before noon if you want a good spot.
