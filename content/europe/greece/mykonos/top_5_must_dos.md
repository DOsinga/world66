---
title: "Top 5 Must Do's in Mykonos"
type: section
---

Known for its white sandy beaches, stunning mountains and green valleys, Mykonos is a lively tourist destination for cosmopolitan travelers, clubbers and upscale gay tourists. The popularity of this Greek island has increased greatly in recent years, pushing the price up considerably. However, even with added tourism the island has enforced strict regulations to keep its pristine beaches and attractions beautiful. Here are the top 5 things to do when visiting Mykonos:

**Little Venice**
This idyllic area features buildings constructed directly on the sea’s edge with many of the balconies hanging right over the water. Twisting whitewashed streets are filled with shops selling clothes, gifts, linens and art. This area possesses the quintessential Greek island atmosphere and is a great place to wander through and stop for a drink, coffee or a light meal overlooking the sea.

**Paradise Beach**
This beach was not named for nothing – its white sand is some of the most beautiful on the island. Generally quite lively, you can walk right into a party here in the middle of the day. And if there isn’t one going on, step into the Paradise Beach Club at night to have a great time. Bars serve drinks right on the beach here and provide lounge chairs with umbrellas and there is music pumping all day long.

**Water Sports**
The island also offers the opportunity to participate in a variety of water sports, including water skiing, jet skiing, banana boating, tubing, canoeing, paragliding, windsurfing and kitesurfing. Whatever your fancy, there is a water sport here to suit it. Check with your hotel or resort to see what is offered in your vicinity and get planning for an action packed day of sporting.
**
Scuba Diving**
One of the most well known places in the Aegean Sea for scuba diving, Mykonos is the home to several well established dive centers and schools. The clear, warm waters around the island have numerous reefs and rocks to explore that are home to a variety of marine life.

**Sailing**
Take a relaxing cruise around the island on a rented skippered yacht to really explore the beauty of blue seas surrounding Mykonos. Travel to the other Cyclades Islands and get a real feel for the area on a relaxing afternoon.

This party island can be as serene and peaceful as it can be wild and action packed with long nights of partying. Enjoy some of the pleasures of the island during the day and unwind at night with any of the great clubs on the island.
