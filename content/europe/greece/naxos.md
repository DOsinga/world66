title: "Naxos"
type: location
---

Naxos is a truly unique Greek Island. It is one of the only Cyclades islands whos predominant industry is not tourism. You experience true Greek culture and amazing food-at amazing prices-everywhere you turn. The island is covered with ruins, history and secluded beaches. I enthusiastically recommend it. It can be reached by a ferry from [Athens](/europe/greece/athens).
