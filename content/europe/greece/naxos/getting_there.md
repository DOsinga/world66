---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

Naxos has a small airport with flights from Athens (about 35 minutes). In summer, several flights run daily, though they fill up fast — book early if you want to fly.

Most visitors arrive by ferry from Piraeus, the main port of [Athens](/europe/greece/athens). The trip takes around five hours on a conventional ferry or three and a half on a high-speed catamaran. Blue Star Ferries and SeaJets both operate the route. There are also frequent connections from [Paros](/europe/greece/paros) (just 30 minutes away), [Santorini](/europe/greece/santorini), Ios, and other Cycladic islands. In high season, boats run multiple times daily in all directions.
