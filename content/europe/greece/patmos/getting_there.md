---
title: "Getting There in Patmos"
type: section
---

Patmos is on the main ferry route from Pireus to Rhodes, most days more than one ferry operates during the summer. The ferry takes about 9 hours, but because they're the big ferries, they're less vulnerable to cancellation due to poor weather.

A Hydrofoil service also operates between Kos and Samos, serving Patmos on the way.

There is no airport on Patmos.
