Those who know about it, have loved it since 1985 when it first opened.
Others have recently discovered it, as it is not widely advertised.
However, the Ski Resort at Pertouli is one of the most easily
accessible ski resort of the country, just 45’ minutes away from the
town of [Trikala](/europe/greece/trikala), halfway from Elati to Pertouli.
It has
tracks for beginners and experienced skiers, two chalets, one at the
bottom and the other one on the top and two lifts, all of which make it
attractive with or without snow around.
Being close
to [Trikala](/europe/greece/trikala) and nearby Pertouli and Elati, it offers a great number of
advantages. Its infrastructure makes it ideal both for the young and
the old. The adults will enjoy the easy access and the passable route
as well as the tracks and the chalet, while the young ones will have
numerous opportunities to play and have fun. And all these, in the
midst of the charming landscape of the Pertouli grasslands.
