Pertouli is a small mountain village in the Pindus range of central Greece, about 45 minutes from [Trikala](/europe/greece/trikala). It sits on high grasslands surrounded by fir forest, at around 1,200 metres altitude, and is one of the more accessible ski areas in the country.

The ski centre has been operating since 1985 and has runs for beginners and experienced skiers, two chalets, and two lifts. It is small and uncrowded — a good option for a weekend trip from Thessaloniki or Trikala rather than a destination in its own right. In summer the grasslands are popular for hiking and horse riding, and the surrounding forest is home to deer and wild boar.

The nearby villages of Elati and Neraidochori have more accommodation options and make a good base for exploring the area.
