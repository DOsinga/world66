The **Pilion Railway** is a historic railway in the Pilion (Pelion) peninsula, Central Greece. Its construction was completed in 1904. 

For many decades it linked the village of **Milies** in the heart of the Pilion peninsula to main port city of **Volos**. Nowadays it is still exploited as a museum railway from Ano Lehonia on the coast to Milies in the mountains.
