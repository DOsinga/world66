Piraeus is a part of ancient Greece, Athens. Greek
cuisine is filled with simple, delicious tasting food that is well liked
by people all over the world. American cities are filled with Greek
restaurants and almost everyone has had a little taste of Mediterranean
flavors. Piraeus is a port city so the most popular dish in the area
are fish and seafood dishes. There are many restaurants but even more
common are pizzerias, taverns, and snack bars. One thing to remember
when you're looking for traditional souvlaki food is that Greeks eat
dinner pretty late at night so restaurants are more crowded around 8pm
to 10pm.

**Seafood Restaurants**

Since Piraeus is right on the sea this is a great place to get some
of the freshest seafood around. A lot of restaurants are right on the
seafront and get their fresh fish caught daily. Some places integrate
the fresh fish dishes into an extensive menu while others specialize in
seafood cuisine. These are a few recommended places to get the best
seafood in the area. Jimmy and the Fish is a great seafood restaurant
in a relaxed atmosphere. Seating is right on the dock overlooking the
waters or indoors. Prices here can be pretty expensive so if you're not
looking for a fine dining like experience you might try somewhere else.
Margaro is another seafood restaurant. Their specialty is the shrimp
and koutsomoura fish though their menu changes often. Prices here range
from $25 to $100 depending on the season. Other good options are Ammos
and Dourambeis.

**Souvlaki and Mediterranean Restaurants**

Souvlatzidika are places you can get souvlaki food. There are a lot
of these here since you are in Greece. One place to try is Vosporos
which does a lot of grilled and souvlaki food. The restaurant overlooks
a small bay and is a great place for a romantic night out. Prices range
from around $28 to $35. Hilies Kai Mia Geuseis is another place for
good Greek food. This is more of take out place with some chairs and
tables for eating in. It's pretty cheap food but good food with prices
averaging about $10.

**Taverns**

Taverns are a big thing in Piraeus and it's the best places to get
cheaper regional food. Iasonos 42 is a family owned tavern with a lot
of good regional food. It has a fun friendly atmosphere and it's a hot
spot for most locals. Other Taverns in Piraeus are the Kitchen Bar and
Cost costa.
