---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

The prefecture's highlights are natural rather than urban. Lake Kerkini, a Ramsar-protected wetland, is Greece's best birdwatching destination. The forest of Lailia offers hiking and, in winter, skiing. The ravine of Aggitis has striking rock formations and rare flora. In Serres itself, the acropolis and Byzantine churches reward a half-day visit.
