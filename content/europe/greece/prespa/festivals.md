“Panigiri”
The 'Panigiri' is a traditional open-air festival that usually takes place in the main square of a village in order to celebrate the name day of the patron saint of the village. It involves a live band playing the traditional music of the region, dancing, and food.

Prespia Festival
**Location: In front of the Basilica of **Agios Achillios, on the island of Agios Achillios
**Date: Last weekend in August**
**Description: The Prespia Festival, one of the most important cultural events in the Balkans, is held every year. Events take place on the island of Agios Achillios in ****the Small**** Prespa lake as well as various villages in the Florina prefecture. It attracts a great number of visitors from Greece and many of the neighbouring countries.**
** **
Festival of Beans
**Location: Between the two lakes, at Koula**
**Date: During August**
**Description: It is a good opportunity to taste the famous Prespa beans and enjoy traditional dances performed by children and adults.******

Balkan Sculpture
**The 'Symposium for Painters and Sculptors' takes place in the last week of June and the first week of July in Agios Achillios. Artists from various Balkan countries create artwork inspired by their impressions of the Prespa region. An open-air exhibition can be seen all year long on the island of Agios Achillios.**
