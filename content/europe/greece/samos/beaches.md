The beaches of Samos are one of the main cause of the high its
high popularity. There are numerous and various, proposing an
atmosphere and a landscape for all tastes. Indeed, Samos has many
touristy and popular beaches proposing sun beds and umbrellas rentals
as well as the possibility to enjoy various kinds of water sports. Many
hotels and accommodation are located there. But the island also
proposes numerous secluded coves and peaceful beaches with crystalline
waters.
