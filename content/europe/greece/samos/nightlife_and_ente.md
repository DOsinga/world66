---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment"
type: section
---

As the most popular destination of the North-Eastern Aegean Greece
Islands, Samos offers an intense and varied nightlife. The island has
plenty of restaurants, taverns with live, traditional music, cafes,
atmospheric bars and night clubs playing all kinds of music. The
capital, Karlovassi, Kokkari and Pythagorio are the most developed in
terms of nightlife.
