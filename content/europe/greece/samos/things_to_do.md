### [Aegeanscuba](/europe/greece/samos/things_to_do/aegeanscuba)

Samos Diving . Go (left click) !!! In Aegeanscuba Dive Center there aren't only 2 single words in a search machine. For us the Samos diving , is a way of life, is a way to teach how to dive , is a way to dive with our buddies. **YOU**. The dive sites of the area offer almost virgin diving with sponge wall coverings, many varieties of Nudibranch, Moray Eels, Sea Stars, Octopus, Lobsters and much more in the clearest waters you can imagine. What is more, you are unlikely to encounter another group of divers underwater.
