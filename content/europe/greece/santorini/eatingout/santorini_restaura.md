New Greek cuisine with focus on organic, farm-direct produce and seafood from the Mediterranean. Overlooking the volcanic Caldera of Oia Santorini.
Since its establishment in 1987, in the village of Oia (Ia) Santorini, Papagalos Restaurant has defined contemporary Greek cuisine. Voted as one of the best of Santorini Restaurants and being amongst the top 50 Greek restaurants, Papagalos Restaurant will fulfil your expectations of a perfect gastronomic experience.
With a focus on the freshest offerings of each season, Papagalos' Restaurant chefs allow the ingredients of the region to speak for themselves.
Papagalos' Restaurant cuisine continues to evolve. Each dish reveals its flavour, colour, and vitality and draws the diner into the enchantment of today's Papagalos Restaurant. The best restaurant to dine in Santorini since 1987.
For more information and reservation visit www.papagalosrestaurant.com
