---
title: "Top 5 Must Do's"
type: section
---

When visiting Santorini, the gorgeous island in the southern Aegean Sea, you would be hard pressed to not take in some of the incredible natural marvels. A modern island with a hot nightlife, Santorini is a paradise escape that features history, culture and modern day glamor all in one. Here are the 5 must dos when visiting Santorini, Greece:

**1: Cliff Walk from Oia to Fira**
See some of the most spectacular views in the city on the cliff top path from Oia to Fira. Join the path from the cobbled entry behind the Perivolas market and follow the path. Walking the entire trip can take about 5 hours, or you can catch the bus back to Oia from Imerovigli or take it all the way to Fira. Wear good walking shoes, bring water and sun protection, and be prepared for a stunning view all the way.

**2: Tour the Beaches**
Rent a car, hire a guide and hit the road for a fantastic tour of some incredible beaches. Check out Perissa, Red Beach, Kamari and Monolithos Beaches for a true taste of the diverse beaches here. Explore the black sand and red sanded beaches, the crystal clear waters and the well organized bars, cafes and taverns along the way.
**
3: Santorini Volcano**
There are many tours that take you to the volcano via boat and give you a tour of the volcano, hot springs and surrounding area. These tours generally last an afternoon and include swimming in the hot springs and hiking up the volcano area. Many tours also have beer and other beverages available for purchase and make the whole experience seem like a party boat. Most tours end with sunset viewing over the sea, a stunning sight that should not be missed while in Santorini.

**4: Ancient Thera**
The 11th century Dorian settlement features remains from Byzantine, Hellenistic and Roman periods. Reachable in a hike from Kamari or by car (through a very steep and narrow path), this is by far one highlights of the island. Wear a good pair of shoes and be prepared for some great views of the Aegean as you climb.

**5: Visit a Winery**
With several wineries on the island, you have your pick – Santo Winery, Antoniou Winery and Boutari Winery are three great choices to explore. Take a tour of the winery and enjoy a glass of wine and some great views to really finish out a beautiful trip to Santorini.
