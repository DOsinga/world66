---
title: "Eating Out in Stoupa"
type: section
---

Your **Stoupa Eating Out** experience will lead you on an authentic culinary adventure. This is because Stoupa is a small village that does not have luxurious five-star restaurants. Only about a dozen restaurants exist with the hotels. These modest restaurants, however, offer tasty, exotic Greek food that you cannot forget. Businesses are gradually growing because more tourists are now discovering the existence of this small but interesting vacation spot. Two great restaurants to try here are:

**Pefko Restaurant**

Located at the beachfront, this restaurant provides customers a refreshing view of the seaside. It not only offers a picture-perfect view, but also serves the widest variety of dishes in Stoupa. Some of these dishes include octopus with bacon, veal stew, stifado, meatballs with mint sauce, and mussel saganaki. All dishes are cooked uniquely, the Pefko way. If you want to explore Greek cuisine, then this is your restaurant. Try their Stoupa chicken and know why customers call it the chef&rsquo;s "secret recipe."

**Platia Taverna**

Platia Taverna serves Greek food like Ouzo with mezes and tsatsiki salads. The restaurant also serves fresh fruits and veggies. The home-like atmosphere will make you feel comfortable and at ease. After a hearty meal, you can savor the steaming coffee and delicious dessert. You can request a special preparation for dishes you want. You can order savory salads with ingredients straight from the garden. They also serve wine to diners who want to discover the taste of Greek wine. You will have a taste of all of these dishes at affordable prices.
