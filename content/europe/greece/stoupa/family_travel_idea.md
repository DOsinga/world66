If you are planning to go on **Stoupa family travel**, then you need to plan activities that you can do in Stoupa that will surely make you and your kids happy. Stoupa is a village located in the southern Peloponnese region in Greece. The town is famous for its beaches and old small houses where your family can enjoy visiting. Here are some of the activities that you can include in your family travel plan to Stoupa.

**The Maniots Houses**

The town of Stoupa has an area called Mani, where there are a lot of feudal houses and towers that stand on the area. These houses and towers are renovated, thus it is perfectly safe to go in and out from one structure to the other. You can take your whole family to this place to learn about the brief history of the feudal era of Stoupa, while your kids can have fun on the tower.

**The Beach**

The beach is an ideal kid magnet, thus if you are in Stoupa, you should visit the beach resorts there. One such beach that you can go to is the Kalogria Beach. This beach is blessed with calm and turquoise waters as well as great snorkeling sites. Your children will surely love to play in the water or drain their excess energy by playing Frisbee as you sit on the sun-kissed sand to enjoy the warmth of the Mediterranean sun.

**The Railway Park**

You need to take your entire family to the Kalamata Railway Park so that they will be able to see interesting old train engines decorated with flowers and vines. The railway park has an extensive display of disused engines and carriages. Your children will really love to run around the park to see old engines up close while you can spend your time in the park relaxing in the well-shaded cafes found inside the park.

**Agios Nikolaos**

The Agios Nikolaos is a small working fishing village in Stoupa and your children will surely enjoy an afternoon of fishing by casting fishing lines on to the calm waters off the coast of the village. Although there are no more big fishes to catch, your children will still have fun reeling in their small catch. This is a perfect way to bond with your family in Stoupa.
