---
title: "Getting Around"
type: section
---

Stoupa itself is tiny and walkable end-to-end in 15 minutes. The three main beaches are connected by footpaths and the coastal road. A car is essential for visiting anything beyond the village — the bus service along the coast (toward Kalamata in the north and Areopoli in the south) runs once or twice a day and is not reliable for day tripping. Taxis can be hired in the village for one-way trips. Car and scooter hire is available locally in season.
