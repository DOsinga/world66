**Stoupa Getting There Information You Can Use**

Some handy information about **Stoupa Getting There** options has been listed below:

**Stoupa Getting There by Air**

Stoupa is located in Peloponnese. Stoupa doesn&rsquo;t have an international airport of its own. Hence, all the international passengers arrive at neighboring cities like Athens or Kalamata. Stoupa is located about 45 km from the southern border of Kalamata and about 180 km from the capital city of Athens. However, direct flights to Kalamata may not available from all countries. In this case, reaching Athens is the only option.

Many charter flights arrive at Kalamata during the holiday season. There is only one international airport in Kalamata—the Kalamata International Airport (Araxos Airport). In Kalamata, visitors can easily find connecting cabs, rentals and minibuses to Stoupa. The other option is to take a flight to Athens and then reach Kalamata via a cab or a bus. It is recommended not to take a direct cab to Stoupa from Athens since the fares can be very steep. The public buses plying near the Athens International Airport present the most affordable way to reach Kalamata. Some chartered minibuses also function between Kalamata and Stoupa. For a big family or a group of travelers, the minibuses can be booked in advance for very competitive rates.

**Reaching Stoupa via Ferry-Road Route**

Stoupa is accessible via ferry from some of the neighboring, Italian cities with which it maintains relaxed, international borders. This list of cities includes:

- Ancona
- Brindisi
- Triest
- Bari
- Venice

The connecting waterways from these cities lead to Patras—a small town that acts as the connecting point to Stoupa. The ferries are small but offer a convenient ride. In Patras, visitors can find plenty of national and international car-rental providers. Stoupa is located about 250 km from Patras.

**Tips for Visitors Using Car Rentals for Reaching Stoupa **

Whether the car-rental is taken from Patras, Athens or Kalamata, there are some basic guidelines for international visitors. Firstly, having a valid, driving license is mandatory. People coming from the EU nations can furnish their national licenses whereas tourists from other countries need to provide an International Driving Permit. The minimum age requirement for seeking a car rental is 21 years but in Kalamata, some companies require the driver to be at least 25-years old. Visitors need to be careful about the paved roads, particularly those lining the coastal shores, since they are often devoid of barriers, road-signs and are reported to present navigational issues for first-time drivers.
