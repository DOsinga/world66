The 'A-Z Guide to Thassos' is a comprehensive, honest and unbiased book,
which covers all aspects of the island, its history and the many attractions
to be found both on Thassos and the neighbouring mainland.
Tony Oswin the author of the ‘A-Z Guide to Thassos’, is an
ex-pat from the UK, now living on the island. He realised the need for
such a book on his earlier visits to the Greek islands, during which he
always found it difficult, costly and time consuming acquiring information
locally. He has therefore meticulously covered all the subjects of interest
to the holidaymaker and general traveller, to help make their stay more
enjoyable and problem free.
