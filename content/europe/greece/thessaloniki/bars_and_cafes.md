---
title: "Bars and Cafes"
type: section
---

Thessaloniki takes its café-sitting more seriously than almost any other city in Europe. Students nurse a single freddo espresso for three hours along the seafront and nobody minds; in summer the tables spill out across half the pavement. Nightlife is concentrated in **Ladadika** behind the port and the lanes off Valaoritou for bars and clubs, and up in **Ano Poli** for quieter rooftop drinks with a view of the bay.
