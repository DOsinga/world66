A small student haunt that has been open more than thirty years, tucked down a side street opposite the White Tower — the Heineken sign outside is bigger than the bar's name, so look carefully. Low ceilings, a few steps up from the street, a wide range of draft beer, and a friendly crowd. Doors open at 9.30pm and stay open until everyone leaves.
