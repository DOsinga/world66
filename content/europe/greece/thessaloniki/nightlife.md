---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment"
type: section
---

Bars and Clubs tend to be concentrated in the narrow streets behind the quayside boulevard Nikis. There are as well many cafés up by the castle that keep going until late into the night. The cities main music venue is the multidisciplinary complex *Milos*, out in an old warehouse at Andreou Yioryiou 56 (Tel. 031 – 525 968 infoline). There you will find several tavernas, cafés and bars as well.
