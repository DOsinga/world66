Lotos Bar first opened its door 20yrs ago and has been a main haunt for students and the rest of us from that day forth..

Opposite the White Tower and down a small side street, it can be hard
to find, it`s Heineken sign (outside) being bigger than the bar`s name..

Looks just like a little house, steps up into the bar, low ceilings, bathrooms upstairs.

wide range of draft beer and friendly customers, who could ask for more...

Doors open at 9;30pm, they are not open during the day, as they close so late into the morning...
