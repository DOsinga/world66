This is the best museum of its kind in Greece, completely restored during the last years, with well-written commentaries accompanying displays on housing, costumes, everyday work and crafts. There is a sharp, highly un-folkloric emphasis on context: on the role of womwn in society, the clash between tradition and progress and the cycle of agricultural and religious festivals.
