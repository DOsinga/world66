---
title: "Festivals"
type: section
---

**St. George Day** is celebrated on a different date each year. Small festivals and feasts are held in small churches all around the island. 

**St. Irene Day** is celebrated on the 5th of May each year. A festival with traditional food and dancing is held at the church that is located in the Eristos valley near Megalo Horio. 

**St. Panteleimon Day** is celebrated on the 27th of July each year. It takes place at the Monastery of St. Panteleimon. This is the biggest festival with traditional food, local music and dancing begins on July 26 and lasts two days. St. Panteleimon is the Patron Saint of Tilos and this is by far the biggest and most celebrated festival for all Tilians. 

**The Dance of the Grail** is celebrated on the 28th of July every year in the Plaza of Archangel Michael in Megalo Horio. 

**Virgin Mary Nine Days** is celebrated on the 23rd of August each year with festivals at the churches of Livadia and Saint Antonios. 

**Holly Belt Day** is celebrated on the 31st of August each year at the church of Mikro Horio. The festival that is held in this abandoned village is an opportunity for the sons and daughters of Mikro Horio to return and celebrate life in the birthplace of their ancestors. 

**St. John Day** is celebrated on the 2nd of September every year with a festival that is held at a small church near Livadia. 

**Christmas, New Year’s Day, Epiphany, Easter and Virgin Mary’s Day** (on the 15th of August every year) are celebrated with communal feasts, traditional island music and dancing.
