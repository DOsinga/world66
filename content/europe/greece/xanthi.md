---
title: "Xanthi"
type: location
---

Xanthi is located in the north of Greece, some 220 km west of {Thessaloniki}. It is close to the border with {Bulgaria}.

The city was founded in the 11th century and has an oriental feel to it which may be due to the fact that the town has a significant muslim population. A stroll trhough the old town is the best way to spend an afternoon in Xanthi.
