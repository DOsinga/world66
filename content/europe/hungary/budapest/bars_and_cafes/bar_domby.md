Bar Domby is a cosmopolitan cocktail bar, located in the center of Budapest, near Metro station "Deak Ter". Open every day from 6 PM to 3 AM. The drinks are strong and the cocktails are professionally created by friendly barkeepers. I would recommend for travelers and specially if you want to meet local girls/guys, as this bar was packed with them every night. Show up in time to get in.
