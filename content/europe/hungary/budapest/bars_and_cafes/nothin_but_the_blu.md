Budapest's only authentic English pub has just opened. The Queen Vic is a cool new spot to meet friends and watch live Sky Sports everyday. They do live music 4 times a week, and also have the best CD library in town. The menu is "eclectic pub grub" with everything from Thai Spring Rolls to Mexican food. Very relaxed place!! These guys used to run the old Nothing But The Blues pub, and the great feeling is there in the new place as well.
