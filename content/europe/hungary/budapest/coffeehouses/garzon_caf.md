Two days ago I've arrived to Budapest and passed by some opening
ceremony of a Coffee House on the Wesselényi street (I think 24). The
interiour design is marvellous and service was very domesticated. Today
I've visited the place again and there were much less people and the
atmosphere was still the same: some calm music, good coffee and some
olives on the table. HIGHLY RECOMENDED!
