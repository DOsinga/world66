Esztergom is the spiritual capital of Hungary, where King Stephen was crowned as the first Christian king in 1000 AD. The town itself is modest, but the enormous basilica perched on a hill above the Danube makes the trip worthwhile. It is the largest church in Hungary, and the views from outside the cathedral stretch across the river into Slovakia. About 50 kilometers from Budapest, reachable by bus or train in around ninety minutes.
