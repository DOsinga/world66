A UNESCO World Heritage village about 100 kilometers northeast of Budapest, Holloko is a beautifully preserved example of traditional Hungarian rural life. The 13th-century castle overlooks whitewashed houses with wooden balconies, and the village still celebrates folk customs, especially during the Easter Festival when locals dress in traditional embroidered costumes. A longer day trip, but worth it if you want to see something of Hungary beyond Budapest.
