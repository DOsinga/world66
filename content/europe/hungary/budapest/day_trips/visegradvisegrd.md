The town of Visegrad is small and quiet, but the medieval ruins above it are impressive. A hilltop citadel offers sweeping views over one of the most beautiful bends in the Danube, and below it lie the partly reconstructed Royal Palace, Water Bastion, and Solomon Tower. Combine it with Esztergom or Szentendre for a full day along the Danube Bend.
