Budapest's most celebrated restaurant, operating since 1894 in a grand turn-of-the-century setting beside City Park. The menu is refined Hungarian cuisine, and the prices are accordingly high, but the quality and the setting justify it. The Sunday brunch is a Budapest institution. Reservations are essential.
