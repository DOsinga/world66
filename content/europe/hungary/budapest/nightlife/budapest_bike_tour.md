If you would like a peek into the Budapest-specific nightlife, take a
bike tour that whisks you away to old buildings with large scale yards
- turned into bars with a very interesting mixed crowd and good
underground/popular DJs. You will experience something only very few
can. This is not really on their website; it's only done upon request.
