---
title: 'Shopping'
type: section
---

Budapest offers good shopping for visitors looking beyond the usual souvenirs. The Great Market Hall at Fovam ter is the best place for paprika, salami, Tokaji wine, and embroidered textiles. Vaci utca is the main pedestrian shopping street but tends toward tourist prices. For something more interesting, explore the independent boutiques and vintage shops in the Jewish Quarter around Kazinczy utca, where you will find Hungarian design, second-hand clothing, and local crafts.
