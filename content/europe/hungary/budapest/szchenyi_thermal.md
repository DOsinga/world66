Szechenyi is the largest thermal bath complex in Europe, and its bright yellow neo-Baroque buildings in City Park make it one of Budapest's most recognizable landmarks. The three vast outdoor pools are the main draw, especially in winter when steam rises off the water and locals play chess on floating boards. Inside there are fifteen more pools, saunas, and steam rooms. All pools are mixed. Buy tickets online to skip the queue.
