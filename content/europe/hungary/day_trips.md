Vác

Once when you are at Hungary at Budapest you should take a trip to Vác. It's just 40 km North from Budapest and you can spend a pieceful day there. This town is 1000 years old. It was established in 1004 AD. It has a baroque squares and streets. The DAnube, which is the biggest river in Europe, turns near this nice town.

For further informatin go to www.vac.hu.
