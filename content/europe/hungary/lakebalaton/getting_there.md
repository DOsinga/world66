---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

Budapest is the main gateway. From Keleti or Déli station, fast trains (*gyors*) reach Balatonfüred and Siófok in around one and a half to two hours; the full journey to Keszthely at the western end takes about three hours. The south shore is better connected by train than the north shore, where services can be infrequent beyond Balatonfüred. By car, take the M7 motorway from Budapest — the south shore towns are well signed and easy to reach.

From Vienna, the drive takes about two and a half hours via the M1 and M7. Direct trains from Vienna Hauptbahnhof connect to Siófok and Keszthely via Budapest, though changing trains at Keleti or Déli is usually required.
