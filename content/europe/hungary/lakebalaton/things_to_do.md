---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

The lake itself is the main attraction — swimming, sailing, windsurfing, and kayaking fill the summer months. Beyond the water, the north shore offers wine tastings in hilltop villages, hiking in the Bakony hills, and one of Hungary's most photographed peninsulas. The Tihany Peninsula and the Festetics Palace in Keszthely are the region's two unmissable sights.
