### [Miskoltapolca](/europe/hungary/miskolc/family_travel_idea/miskoltapolca)

It's a very good place to relax, explore the near mountin, Bükk, or just have a nice time in the CaveBath. The Cave Bath has also indoor and outdoor pools. It has family pocket prices (e.g.: 2 adults and 2 children or 2 adults with 1 child). In the neighbourhood you can also find a bob filed which can be fun for the whole family.
