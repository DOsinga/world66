title: "Rab Ráby"
type: poi
---

Decked out like a homestead in the Hungarian countryside, the Rab Ráby is cluttered with bells, clocks, musical instruments and farm tools. Sit on wooden benches around the candlelit tables and soak in the atmosphere. The traditional Hungarian fare is reasonably priced, and excellent. The waiters - unlike at many other places! - have some spring in their step. Open 12:00 - 22:00. (690 - 890 Ft). 

address: Kucsera Ferenc u. 1
