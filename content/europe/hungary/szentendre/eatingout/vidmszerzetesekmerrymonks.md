You'll know the place when you see a statue of a friar hoisting a plate of fish. The menu here appears in more languages than Berlitz, and the riot of foreign banknotes displayed inside bears witness to how many foreigners have visited the Merry Monks over the years. The speciality, Monk's Pleasure (920 Ft), combines meat, potatoes, veggies and cheese, all cooked up in an earthen pot. Open 12:00 - 22:00. Closed Mon. (880 - 1880 Ft)
