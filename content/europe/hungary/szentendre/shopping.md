---
title: "Shopping"
type: section
---

Szentendre is well supplied with souvenir shops and art galleries selling Hungarian crafts, ceramics, embroidery, and folk art. Belle Epoque (near Fő tér), a family-run folk art shop in a national monument building from 1860, is one of the better options for authentic Hungarian artisan work. The shop claims that every item is handmade from natural materials using traditional techniques.
