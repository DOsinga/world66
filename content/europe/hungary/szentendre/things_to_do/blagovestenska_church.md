An 18th-century Serbian Orthodox church on the main square, with a striking Baroque and Rococo facade. The interior contains remarkable icons; a taped choir sings during visiting hours. The church is at the physical and historical centre of Szentendre's Serbian heritage. Open Tuesday to Sunday, 10am–5pm; admission charged.
