### [Paterhaz](/europe/hungary/szentes/eatingout/paterhaz)

Paterhaz is on the main street in Szentes. It offers a comfortable restaurant with excellent Hungarian cuisine in very pleasant surroundings. It is also a pension or panzio which contains a number of well-appointed rooms. The prices are very reasonable and the service very good.
