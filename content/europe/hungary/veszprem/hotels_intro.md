title: "Hotels Intro in Veszprem"
type: location
---

**Veszprem Hotels** are numerous in the oldest town in Hungary, located near Lake Balaton, which is a tourist area.

**Ramada Hotel and Resort**

This hotel is located near the Lake Balaton. This hotel offers its guest a scenic view of the pristine lake. The hotel has modern interiors and is particularly famous for its big glass walls to give the guests a maximum view of the lake. This hotel is also located in a big compound where people can play golf and other outdoor activities. Standard room rates of this hotel start at $150.

**Betekints Hotel**

Betekints Hotel is located in the heart of the city where most of the landmarks are located. The hotel offers the standard amenities of a three-star hotel such as clean air conditioned rooms, cable TV, hot and cold shower and a direct phone line. The hotel also offers ironing and laundry services as well as baby-sitting services. You might want to check out the on-site restaurant that serves the best Hungarian dishes in all of Veszprem. The standard hotel rooms are cheap and only cost $100.

**Anna Grand Hotel**

This quaint hotel offers rich interiors that depict old European designs. The hotel offers clean rooms with cheap rates. In fact, room rates in this hotel start from $100 to $200 and are inclusive of complimentary toiletries, free breakfast, air-conditioned quarters, cable TV and a direct phone line.

**Hotel Gizella**

This hotel is located in the heart of the city and is in close proximity to the many landmarks of the city. This four-star hotel boasts of its friendly staffs that are very courteous and efficient. The rooms at Hotel Gizella are also very spacious and are furnished with great interiors. Rooms at this hotel costs $90.

**Alba Villa Apartmanhotel**

For tourists who want to stay longer in Veszprem, then staying in the Alba Villa Apartmanhotel is the best option. It has large and clean apartments perfect for vacationing families. The staffs speak impeccable English and is helpful. The hotel also offers family-friendly facilities like a barbecue area, playground, indoor pool and a gym. Room rates are $150.
