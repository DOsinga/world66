yes stumbled into a indian(restaurant) eatery called indian mango on frakkastig 12 downtownjust off main st.laugavegur, it was early monday evening still it was quiet packed, the menu was nothing we've ever seen before in an indian restaurant ,6 duck courses,a few on pork and beef as well,vegetarian options as well, we decided to go for the icelandic scallop which was perched on spud bharta with tamarind chutny,and more, verry artistic as well, plate was designer luxury style.yummy scallops! my mate ordered the duck and i went for the icelandic guillemot it was done seasoned lightly and panfried,the best guillemot i ate on my entire trip to iceland, the duck breast was done goan style(xacuti) with grated coconut, came as spicy, tasty as i rember it to be at the taj hotel in goa, the food had a great look as one would find in the finest eaterys in the west. i heard latter that the chef has a 4 seasons background.quite comfortable seating, nice and pleasant feel,hospitable staff.Great cuisine and atmosphere.The food was not as expensive at some of the other recomended restaurants weve eaten at. For icelandic cuisine i quiet liked the 3 french men.
