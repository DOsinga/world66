Iceland now has its own low cost airline: Icelandexpress which flies
from Keflavik International in Iceland to Copenhagen, London and
Frankfurt-Hahn.

The low cost flight search engine dohop is very useful for finding
connecting flights with low cost airlines from many more places in
Europe.
