Icelandair is the official airline of Iceland. There are flights from major European cities and New York. Icelandair maintains regular scheduled flights to Iceland from the following cities: ALLYEAR: London, Glasgow, Copenhagen, Oslo, Paris, Stockholm, Amsterdam, Frankfurt, Halifax, Boston, New York, Baltimore and Minneapolis. SUMMERTIME ONLY: Zurich, Barcelona, Milan, Hamburg and Madrid. Iceland Express is a low-budget alternative to Icelandair. See By Air
