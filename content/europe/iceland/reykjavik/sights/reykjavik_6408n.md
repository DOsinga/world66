REYKJAVIK 64°08N 21°54W is the first in a series of travel books by paris photographer Ami Sioux.

She is currently shooting PARIS 48°50N 2°23E, which is set to be released Spring 2009.

The
idea for this book series was born when she was living in East Berlin
from 2001-2003 after a few people had handed her a hand-drawn map to
their home rather than given her just a street address because a lot of
people lived in unnumbered buildings on unnamed streets.

The
idea of the book in each city is to ask 50 people who live there to
hand-draw a map to a place of their choice. The importance
is that they make their map to a location that's "important to them
personally" or to "their personal viewpoint of their city".

She then use the hand-drawn maps herself to navigate to each location and take a photograph.

This
series shows the side of a city that's about the city that's seen by
people who live and work in the city every day. Its not a guide, but
rather a unique way of discovering different parts of the city.
