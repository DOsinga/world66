title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

For a small capital, Reykjavik packs in a surprising amount: a dramatic basalt-columned church, one of Europe's most striking concert halls, a thoughtful national museum, and a walkable old centre studded with sculpture and lakeside cafes. Most of the highlights sit within a thirty-minute walk of each other, and the surrounding streets are as much of the experience as the sights themselves.
