---
title: "Selfoss"
type: location
---

The biggest town in South Iceland, about 4.900 inhabitants. Situated at the Ölfusá river.
About 60 km away from [Reykjavik](/europe/iceland/reykjavik), between Hveragerdi and Hella.

Selfoss is also the name of a waterfall in the north of Iceland.
