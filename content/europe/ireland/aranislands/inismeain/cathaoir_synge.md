title: Cathaoir Synge (Synge's Chair)
type: poi

A stone seat on the southern cliffs where the playwright J.M. Synge spent his summers writing, looking out across the sound to Inis Mór and the open Atlantic. The views are superb — on rough days the big seas rolling in from the west crash against the cliffs below. It is an easy walk from the main road and one of the most atmospheric spots on the island.
