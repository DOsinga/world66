The island's Catholic church contains beautiful stained glass windows made by the Harry Clarke Studios, among the finest ecclesiastical glass artists Ireland has produced. The altar was built by the father of Patrick Pearse, who read the declaration of independence in 1916. The church has a warm wooden interior and sits on the main road in the centre of the island.
