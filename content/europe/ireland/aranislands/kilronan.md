Kilronan (Cill Rónáin) is the main village on Inis Mór, the largest of the [Aran Islands](/europe/ireland/aranislands). The ferry from Rossaveal or Doolin drops you at the pier here, and this is where you will find the island's few pubs, shops, and B&Bs.

The village itself is small — a handful of buildings around the harbour — but it is the starting point for exploring the island. Dún Aonghasa, the spectacular Bronze Age cliff fort on the western edge of Inis Mór, is about 8 km away by road. Most visitors rent a bike in Kilronan and ride out along the stone-walled lanes, stopping to look at the limestone karst landscape and the tiny fields that have been worked for centuries.
