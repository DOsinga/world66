Situated in the Left Bank area of Athlone approx. 3 minutes walk from Athlone Castle.   Open Monday to Saturday with serving times 10.30 a.m. to 6.00 p.m.. Dinner served from 6.00 p.m. onwards Thursday, Friday, Saturday - closed on Sundays and Bank Holidays. Menu ranges include fresh fish, steaks, Thai dishes and Ostrich. Groups up to 12 are catered for and there is a Courtyard Garden.
