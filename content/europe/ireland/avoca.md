Avoca Vale is one of the most beautiful valleys in Wicklow. The scenic village of Avoca is situated on the banks of the Avoca River. The village today is alive with tourists. The old courthouse has been refurbished by the Development Association and now houses an Information technology centre, library and tourist centre.
