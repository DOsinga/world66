Safe, sandy beach with lifeguard cover during summer months, just two minutes' walk from Balbriggan town centre and its DART station. Coastal paths run along the headland with broad views across the Irish Sea. A large colony of seals has made its home along the shoreline at Hampton Cove and can regularly be spotted around the harbour — a genuine highlight for kids and adults alike.
