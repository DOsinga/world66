Carlow is an insignifigant county in Ireland. In fact, some say that the only saving grace about Carlow is that it has 3 letters in common with Cork. It is quiet small, and its population is mainly comprised of sheep. Carlow people (esp girls) are generally incomprehensible to people outside of the region. for example, the english word "what" is often pronounced as "whaaaaaaaaaaaaaaaaaaa" in Carlow. Carlow people are also renowned for having weak stomachs.
