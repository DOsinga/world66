title: "Glenheights"
type: location
---

Located between Ballyvolane and Dublin Hill, the Heights is the last stop on the number 12 bus, if you can make it past the otherside of the glen aka.[The Glen](/europe/ireland/cork/theglen) , then you shall be able to visit one of the more highly charged areas of the City. The residents have a glowing charm about with electrifying personalities. This is largely due to the big fat pylons that are situated right through the area.
