Situated within the traditionally working class area of Guarranabraher (locally just called "Grawn"). It's visible from the opposing hills of the Glen. Bear in mind that this "palace" was in use during the 30s by the Catholic hierarchy, when the surrounding areas were nothing more than squalid slums and desparately poor. A testiment to inequality and religious power gone mad.
