The best aspect of this basic little place is that it's located on a hill, so you're always walking down. I mean come on people, it's so much easier. Of course this only applies for one direction, if you go the other way then you're going up hill, which, lets face it really sucks. I mean it's tiring, boring, hard on the knees- actually this place is not suitable for old folks or kids,....... actually we'd prefer if everyone would stay away, we've been having too many "up" hill accidents lately. We have lately hired a team of scientists to correct this problem though, so when our town, no wait thats not sounding right, when our area is designed to be completely down hill we would love for you to visit.
