---
title: "Getting Around in Mayfield"
type: section
---

The 'famous' number eight bus departs from several locations around
Mayfield and leaves every 20 minutes for the city centre. Don't
be surprised though to get three in a row, then none for an hour
afterwards. The trip to the centre will pass through several
narrow streets on the way down through the Northside, stopping in
Dillon's Cross, St Lukes, and finally, the town.

### [Taxi](/europe/ireland/cork/mayfield/getting_around/taxi)

A taxi to Mayfield from the town centre should take about 10-15 minutes outside of rush hour, and will cost from €7-€10.
