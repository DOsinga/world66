Saturdays: Midnight – 2.30am

The
Funkiest night in Cork happens every Saturday at
the Everyman Palace . Get blown away with our
explosive mix of games and music!

In
our main bar DJ Papa Cass and DJ Rabbit play all the hits from the 60s, 70s,
80s, 90s and modern day, including Soul, Disco and good old Rock and Roll!
Hearing is believing!

If that doesn’t take your fancy, then check out
our games area. We have Playstation, Connect 4, Electro Buzz, Draughts and
Twister. Fun for all you kidults!

Or if
you just want to relax with that someone special then our ‘chill out zone’ is
the place for you. Sip the night away on one of our beautiful cocktails or have
a glass of champagne in our intimate candle lit surroundings!

Whatever
you want from a night out we have it, Saturday night with a twist!
