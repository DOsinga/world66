Located just two minutes from Patrick's St.,  Rearden's is one of a new breed of Irish pub - "the superpub". It's cavernous interior and late licence ensure it's popularity with the young partygoer. Definitely not the place to go for a quiet contemplative pint!  The attached nightclub, "Havana Brown's" is also extremely popular and ensures that when the pub does finally close, you can dance the rest of the night away.  Rearden's also serves hot food throughout the day
