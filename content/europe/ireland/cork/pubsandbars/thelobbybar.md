The Lobby, just across from Cork's magnificent City Hall, deserves a medal for it's contribution to the music life of the city.
 
 Ever since Pat Conway took over this pub, more than 10 years ago, he has created a mecca for aspiring and established musicians alike in a city which, ironically, while full of talent, suffered from a dearth of places for those same people to showcase their skills.
 
 Always willing to give talent a chance; the tiny upstairs venue played host to the likes of Sinead Lohan, long before she achieved international success.
 
 A combination of paid gigs upstairs and free sessions in the main bar ensures there's always something to suit every musical taste.
 
 Oh! And the pint isn't bad either!
