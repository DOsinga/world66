Southern suburb of Cork.

Not to be confused with Glasheen. Stolen car burn-outs are the prime attraction of this area. Wander in astonishment at the nouveau riche 60s council estates as they try to replicate Milton Keynes in Cork city, although on a much much smaller scale. So small, that it didnt quite work out - hence the stolen cars...

Not as hard as Ballyphehane , which is the suburb directly to the north. Now that is really hardcore sham territory, which is best avoided by new visitors to Cork.
