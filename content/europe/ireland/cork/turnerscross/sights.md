Church of Christ The King (1931) Turners Cross is home to one of Irelands most beautiful Art Deco buildings, the Roman Catholic Church of Christ the King, designed by Chicago architect Barry Byrne.

It was the first church in the UK & Ireland to be constructed from re-enforced concrete and remains one of the biggest suspended-ceiling churches in Europe. The church is open daily to the puclic.

See http://www.turnerscross.com for more details
