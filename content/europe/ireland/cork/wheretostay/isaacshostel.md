title: "Isaac's Hostel"
type: location
---

A large hostel, (and IHH member) just 5 minutes walk from the rail and bus station, Isaac's central location makes it a useful base to explore Cork.   A range of large, single-sex dorms are available, as are some private rooms.  Isaac's own restaurant's; "Isaac's" and "Greene's" are good choices if you feel like treating yourself.

address: Mac Curtain St.

tel: +353 (0)21 4508388
