Youghal is a lovely town situated 30 miles east of cork. It is know by all (actually by no one) as little Monaco. Miles and miles of sandy beaches with top less women tanning their body all year round. Great place to be in he winter if you run out of bottle openers. Home to one of Irelands largest and greatest theme parks, Perks. A rival to disneyland, alton towers and the vic, it is headed up by its star, Perkie Bear.
