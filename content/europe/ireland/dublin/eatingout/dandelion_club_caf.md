The Dandelion is situated at St Stephens Green at the top of Graften Street, approx 10mins from Temple Bar and opposite the tram station. Fantastic location, lovely atmosphere and very friendly staff. The menu is not expensive and they do an Early Bird menu (as do some other establishments) where you can get a starter, main course, dessert and tea/coffee for 19 euros if you go in before 7pm.
