The Powerscourt Town Centre offers a variety of food types in a old mansion that was rebuilt. It has a number of restaurants. Tables are scattered around the first and second floor of the Centre and aren't reserved for any particular restaurant.
An Italian restaurant, "La Corte"is very good and offers authentic Italian dishes.

The Centre is close to Grafton Street and has two entrances. One is on Claredon Street and the other on South William Street
