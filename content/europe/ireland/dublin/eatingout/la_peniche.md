La Peniche is Dublin's only floating restaurant. It is totally unique on board the passenger ship MV RIASC on the Grand Canal.

Our mission is to provide a relaxed, high quality, and charming dining experience for the pleasure of Dubliners and visitors alike, in the fashion of Restaurant River Boats in European cities such as [Paris](/europe/france/paris) and [Amsterdam](/europe/netherlands/amsterdam).

The menu is Authentic French / Italian Bistro cuisine, with simple but hearty, reasonably-priced dishes using as many organic products and ingredients as possible.
