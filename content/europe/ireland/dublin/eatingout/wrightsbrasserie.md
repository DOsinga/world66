European restaurant specialising in fish. Black Sole, Monkfish, Fresh Dublin Bay Prawns, Scallops, Wild Salmon. Full restaurant as well as a wine bar with separate bar menu.
Excellent warm atmosphere with live pianist or jazz on Friday Nights.

**Wright's closed as a restaurant August2006.It is now an up market designer boutique.**
