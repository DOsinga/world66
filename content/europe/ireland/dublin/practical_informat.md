### Dublin Airport

Dublin Airport is about 12km north from the city center. A ride to the
city center in a taxicab will cost you about €20.00. The bus service
will cost you €1.70, unless you use the special airport shuttle, which
is slightly more expensive (€6.00) but saves you a quarter of an hour
on a trip of about 45 minutes.
