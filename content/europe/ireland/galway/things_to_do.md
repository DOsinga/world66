### [Bone Carving Studio and Gallery](/europe/ireland/galway/things_to_do/bone_carving_studi)

The Bone Carving Studio and Gallery is a craft workshop located in Galway city centre, just a minute's walk from Eyre Square. Visitors can tour the workshop, watch the bone carvings being made and see the carvings for sale there. The Bone Carving Studio also offers the only bone carving classes in Ireland, where visitors can design and make their own bone carving in just one day.
