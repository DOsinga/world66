**St. Canice's Cathedral**

Very nice 13th century cathedral, many carvings in black Kilkenny limestone. You can climb the tower.

**The Castle**

This is the big attraction. An imposing structure standing high above the river. Founded in the 12-th century, it was restored in the 19-th. Within the castle is the Butler Gallery. 

Tours take place every 20 mins during July and August and take approx 45-50 Mins.
