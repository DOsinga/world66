title: "Killarney"
type: location
---

Killarney is one of the best places in Ireland - and in the whole world for that matter- to explore the great outdoors. Hiking biking and what have you; it's all here and it is o so pretty.

 The town itself is not very beautiful though, but it has a long tradition of helping travelers on their way to some of the most beautiful spots. There are many hotels, travelers hang-outs and bars, so you are sure to enjoy yourself before you set out to the lakes around town or to the Ring of Kerry.
