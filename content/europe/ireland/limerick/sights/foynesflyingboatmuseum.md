This museum recalls the heyday of Foynes as the terminus for transatlantic flights. It commemorates the flying boats, the famous people who travelled on them between Europe and the US, the people in Foynes who provided the operational support, and of course, the original recipe for the now world-famous pick-me-up Irish coffee.The Foynes museum recalls this era with a comprehensive range of exhibits and graphic illustrations.
