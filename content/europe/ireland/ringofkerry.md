The **Ring of Kerry** is a peninsula at the western coast of Ireland that’s very popular as a hiking and cycling destination. Though the landscape with its hills and rough coastline is very beautiful, it’s popularity may scare off the visitors that seek solitude. For this solitary break, one should venture towards the **Dingle Penninsula.** The locals are quite incomprehensible most of the time due to the mixture of Gaelic and English language in some of these areas-known as **An Gaeltacht**, though are extremely friendly. Definetly the most scenic of areas throughout the whole of Ireland!
