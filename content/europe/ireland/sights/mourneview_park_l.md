Mourneview Park is the home ground of Glenavon Football Club in Lurgan, County Armagh. The stadium holds around 5,000 and has been the club's base since the early twentieth century. Glenavon compete in the NIFL Premiership, Northern Ireland's top division, and matchdays draw a loyal local crowd. The ground sits in a residential area of Lurgan, a town on the southern shore of Lough Neagh, and is easy to reach from Belfast or Dublin by road.
