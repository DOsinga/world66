South County Dublin, probably one of Europes most plesant commuter belts. The area of SCD; Dun Laoghaire, Killiney, Dalkey boasts proximmity to the city centre while being on the coast and a stones throw from the heartland of Irelands' Garden - Co Wicklow. The region is full of both natural and social amenities. You can get a 10spot over in the brack and take it to DunDreary for an afternoons viewing in the cinema. The hill of Killiney / Dalkey is champion and arguebly offers the best seats on all of the east cost for catching sunset.
