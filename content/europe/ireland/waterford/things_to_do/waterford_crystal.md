The Waterford Crystal visitor centre allows you to watch master craftspeople blow, shape, and cut crystal using centuries-old techniques. The factory tours run on weekdays and take you through the entire process from molten glass to finished product. There is a large shop with a wide range of crystal pieces on sale.
