The Abruzzo National Park, consisting of 50,000 hectares tel.plus 100,000 hectares of external protected area), is situated at the heart of the central Apennines, spanning Abruzzo, Lazio and Molise, and equidistant from the Adriatic and the Tyrennian seas. 


 The Park covers three Provinces tel.Aquila, Frosinone and Isernia), and twenty-two communes, and includes five distinct geographical areas: Alto Sangro, Marsica Fucenese and the Peligna valley in Abruzzo; the Comino valley in Lazio; the Mainarde in Molise.
