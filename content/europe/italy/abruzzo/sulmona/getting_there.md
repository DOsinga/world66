---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

Sulmona has a direct rail link with Rome via the regional line through Avezzano — the journey takes around two and a half hours. A secondary line runs south through the mountains to Castel di Sangro, and another connects via Terni in Umbria to [Naples](/europe/italy/campania/naples).

By car, Sulmona is reached from Rome via the A25 motorway (about 150km, under two hours). From the Adriatic coast, the SS17 runs inland from Pescara through the Popoli gorge.
