Ceningan Island Bali Indonesia.

Visit this magical Island and feel like your in Bali 30 years ago.

Stay at the Ceningan Cove Resort villa's at a very cheap price of $30usd a night.

Hire a motorbike and ride around the Island, surf the secret point and take the bridge over to Nusa Lemogan.

A must see in Bali.
