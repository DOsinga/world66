Lombardy's beautiful lakeside city of Lago Maggiore is a famous destination for travelers of all types, and the **Lago Maggiore bars and cafes** in the area only help to draw in more and more travelers to the location. Read on for a brief overview of some of the best cafes and bars in the region.

**Ristorante Locanda Calianna**

This famous restaurant has a cafe feel to it, and you would certainly be welcome to sit inside for an hour or more and drink a coffee or a glass of wine. As is customary with Italian restaurants in this area, the wait staff will not rush you out and will allow you to sit and enjoy your drink or small bite to eat. Ristorante Locanda Calianna features an excellent atmosphere and friendly service.

**La Gabella**

This comfortable bar and restaurant is a popular destination for locals in the Lago Maggiore area, and it's a wonderful place to enjoy a drink and to learn a bit about the culture of Lombardy and Italy in general. Suitable for travelers of all ages.

**Acquadulza Bar Wine Butega**

Acquadulza is the best "bar" in the Lago Maggiore region, though it has a feel that is closer to what many westerners would call a "cafe" instead. The atmosphere is generally relaxed, though the bar stays open late and carries a full range of different drinks.

Speak with a representative at your local place of accommodation for more advice about how to select the best bars and cafes to patronize during your visit to Lombardy.
