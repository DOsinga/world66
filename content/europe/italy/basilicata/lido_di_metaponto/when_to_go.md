---
title: "When to Go"
type: section
---

July and August are peak season — the beach is at its liveliest and the water is warm, but accommodation fills up quickly. June and September are quieter and often just as pleasant, with comfortable temperatures and fewer crowds. The surrounding region has a strong festival calendar: the Feast of the Madonna della Bruna in Matera on 2 July is one of southern Italy's most spectacular religious celebrations, just 40 km inland. Locally, the Feast of San Leone is marked in August.
