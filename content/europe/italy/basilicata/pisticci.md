---
title: "Pisticci"
type: location
---

Set in a region that is very rough and is great for hiking, Pisticci is
a village with a long history. The town boosted during the Norman
period. In 1688 the area surrounding the castle
was destroyed by a landslide which caused more than 400 deaths - many
inhabitants built small houses near the ravine - they can still be
seen. Nearby in San Basilio, you find the "Masseria Fortificata"
a Roman
castle built by Roger I and transformed into an abbey by Basilian monks
in 1133.
