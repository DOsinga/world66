A collection of [funny and touching stories about life in modern Rome](/europe/italy/books). Many of the stories are about ex-pat women, but unlike much of this kind of literature, author Megan Williams does not romanticise Italy. **Saving Rome** is a roller-coaster of a book about life in Rome and a must-read for anyone wanting to know what it's really like living in the city.
