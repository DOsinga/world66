title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Gioia Tauro is a practical base for exploring the southern Tyrrhenian coast. The Cittadella, the oldest quarter, has baroque buildings and the church of Sant'Antonio. In contrada Petra you can see ruins of a Roman necropolis and villa.

The Monday morning street market on Viale Don Sturzo runs from 8 AM to 1 PM and is a good place to find local produce, clothes, and household goods. Along the lungomare, the century-old Gaslini citrus plant makes an atmospheric backdrop for evening walks, especially in summer when free concerts are held there.

From here, day trips to [Tropea](/europe/italy/calabria/tropea), [Scilla](/europe/italy/calabria/scilla_1), and the ceramics workshops of Seminara are all straightforward.
