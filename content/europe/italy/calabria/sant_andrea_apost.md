A small town in Catanzaro province on the Ionian Sea coast. Sant'Andrea sits on a hillside above a long stretch of sandy beach, with views across to the Sila mountains inland. Like many towns on the Ionian side of Calabria, it is quiet and largely untouched by tourism.
