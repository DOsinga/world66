**by bus:**

 Buses stop in Piazza Flavio Gioia, head away from the water and pass through the white, arched portal or the street to the right of Piazza del Duomo. The bus terminal at the waterfront (P. Gioia) offers regularly bus connections to [Sorrento](/europe/italy/campania/sorrento), [Positano](/europe/italy/campania/positano), Salerno, Ravello and other destinations along the coast.
