**Tourist office:** Corso delle Repubbliche Marinare 19/21. Tel: +39 089 871107.

**Post office:** Corso delle Repubbliche Marinare 29 (next to tourist office). Open Mon–Fri 8:15am–6:30pm, Sat till noon.

**Emergencies:** 112. Police: Via Casamare 19, tel: +39 089 871022.

Amalfi has limited parking and the streets are narrow. In summer, the town gets very crowded — arrive by ferry from Salerno or Positano rather than driving. SITA buses connect Amalfi to Sorrento, Positano, Ravello, and Salerno from the bus terminal at Piazza Flavio Gioia. Ferries also run between Amalfi and Naples, Capri, and Positano.
