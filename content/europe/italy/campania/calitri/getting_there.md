---
title: "Getting to Calitri"
type: section
---

Calitri is in the Irpinia hills, about 80km from Naples and not served by rail. The easiest approach is by car.

**From Naples:** Take the A16 motorway east towards Canosa di Puglia. Exit at Lacedonia, then follow signs to Bisaccia and Calitri. About 1 hour 20 minutes from Naples.

**From Rome:** A3 south from Rome, then A30 to A16 east. Exit Lacedonia. Allow 3.5 to 4 hours.

There is no practical public transport from Naples to Calitri.
