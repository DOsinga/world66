Capri is an island off the coast from [Naples](/europe/italy/campania/naples). It is a popular place for daytrips. Villages on the island include Marina Grande - the place where you arrive by boat, and Anacapri, up in the mountains, and Marina Piccola whichg has a smaller port.

Other sights include the Blue Grotto, the Natural Arch and the Faraglioni.
