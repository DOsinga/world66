title: "Day Trips"
type: section
---

Planning a** capri day trip **from Capri doesn’t have to be a
headache. There are so many fun and interesting things to do around
Capri and they are relatively easy to get to. With the right planning,
your day away from home can feel like a week-long vacation.
Naples

Naples, Italy is a great place to spend a day and is so close to
Capri that you only need to take a quick ferry ride and begin
exploring. Take a ferry or hydrofoil ride from Marina Grande in Capri
to Molo Beverello in Naples. From there, the city is yours. Use the
public transportation system to get around at an affordable rate.
Although this may tack on extra time to your trip, it is a great way to
relax and take in the surroundings while you get from place to place. You can take a private tour of the Amalfi Coast, visiting some of the
most beautiful villages in the southern area of Italy: Sorrento,
Positano, Amalfi and Ravello. The tour takes about 8 hours to
complete, but is fairly affordable at $65.
Pompeii

Accessible by the same ferry or hydrofoil as Naples, Pompeii, Italy
is a fascinating city with an interesting history. If you are
interested in a guided tour but would like to spend time on other
things as well, try touring the city of Pompeii. These tours are a
little more expensive, at $85, but only last about 3 hours. On your
tour, you will get to explore the remains of the lost city. In 79
A.D., it was destroyed by a volcano, which desecrated everything in its
path and covered the city (and its 20,000 or so inhabitants) with
volcanic ash and pumice.
Ischia

Ischia is commonly regarded as the most beautiful island in the bay
of Naples. Transportation to the island is limited as there is no
airport. From Naples, however, you can take a ferry across the bay and
spend a few hours in the local shops, restaurants, or even go scuba
diving. Once there, you can find taxis to travel across the island, as
it is the largest in the bay. You can even take a mini-bus tour of the
island, giving you an over-view of all it has to offer. The thermal
spas of the island are the most popular attraction for visitors and are
known to help suppress specific ailments to the body. You can spend an
entire day at a thermal spa for complete relaxation and a mini-vacation.
