---
title: "Eating Out in Capri"
type: section
---

Capri's cuisine draws on the Mediterranean and the sea: seafood pasta, rabbit, fresh vegetables, stuffed courgettes and aubergines. Local ingredients include excellent anchovies, capers, small lobsters, cuttlefish, and the island's famously aromatic lemons. Eating near the piazzetta is always more expensive — walk ten minutes away from the tourist centre and prices drop considerably.

**Lido del Faro** (Anacapri, seaside promenade). Specialises in risotto and the local "totani e patate" (squid with potatoes). Tel: +39 081 837 1798.

**Faraglioni** (Via Camerelle 75, Capri). In the village centre, with local specialities including gnocchetti ai frutti di mare (gnocchi with seafood) and seasonal pasta dishes. Tel: +39 081 837 0320.

**Il Solitario** (Via Timpone 1, Anacapri). A pleasant restaurant with a garden, good for pasta and pizza. Closed Mondays. Tel: +39 081 837 1988.
