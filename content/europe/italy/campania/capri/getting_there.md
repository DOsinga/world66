---
title: "Getting to Capri"
type: section
---

Capri is island — you must arrive by sea. Ferries and hydrofoils run from both Naples and Sorrento.

**From Naples (Molo Beverello):** Ferry takes about 80 minutes (around €12); hydrofoil about 40 minutes (around €20). More frequent departures than from Sorrento.

**From Sorrento:** Ferry takes about 40 minutes; hydrofoil about 20 minutes. Sorrento is also a convenient base for exploring the whole coast.

You arrive at Marina Grande, the main port. From there, funiculars and buses run up to Capri town; a separate road leads to Anacapri. A small charge applies per piece of luggage on ferries.

Private water taxis (Taxi del Mare, Motoscafisti di Capri) can pick up and drop off anywhere around the island on request — expensive but useful for larger groups or remote beaches.
