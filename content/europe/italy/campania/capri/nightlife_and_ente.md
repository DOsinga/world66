---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment"
type: section
---

Capri is a slow stunner that doesn’t believe in a boisterous display
of its nightlife gems. Don’t be deceived by the islands serene daytime
persona, because come sunset, the place becomes a playground for the
region’s party pack. The **Capri Nightlife** Guide offers visitors information of the several options that glossily create the nocturnal silhouette of this Italian hotspot.

**Orientation**

Capri is undoubtedly one of the most high-profile tourist hotspots
that has witnessed a bevy of Hollywood celebrities. The paparazzi capture these forbidden details in several night spots of the island
and helped shape its nocturnal identity. Capri features a host of bars,
night clubs, pubs, cafés and live music often continuing till the wee hours of morning.
These aren’t really opulent haunts that believe in unnecessary
extravagance but provide abundant intimate spaces for hushed
conversations and enjoyable drinking sessions with pals.

**Where to go**

For wines that boast of a rich pedigree and refined flavors and have
been handpicked from all over the world, head to The Lanterna Verde- a
bar playing live music and stocking the most extensive collection of
pure wines and spirits in the entire island. For a more heritage
looking night club-head to Taverna O Guaraccino that was an ancient
press before being converted to a full-fledged nightclub. This night
hotspot offers lip-smacking starters and main course and plays the most
awesome local guitar music to keep its patrons enthralled. A heady
combination of sinful concoctions and haunting music. The Anema e Core
is an unassuming and modest little jewel that hides its ravishing
appeal under a humble garb. This is one of the most frequented
nightspots of Capri and is worth discovering for its Latin American
dance music and Neapolitan rhythms. Go people spotting at Bar Tiberio
which gives a great view of the Piazzette square. This one’s a classic
for its strategic location and array of fine wines. The Pulalli Wine
Bar located on the Capri square, adjacent to the famous clock tower has
a comfortable open air seating with delicious appetizers and
full-bodied traditional Neapolitan grub along with an assortment of
solid Italian wines.

Capri is not exactly a hardcore wild and reckless nightlife
destination but the night entertainment here is more pronounced by
informal drinking sessions where you let down your hair and catch up
with friends over a few drinks.
