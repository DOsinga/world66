Caserta is 27 km from Naples (about 40 minutes by train from Napoli Centrale). Province code: CE. Phone area code: 0823.

**Tourist office:** Piazza Dante 35. Tel: +39 0823 321137.

The Reggia di Caserta (Royal Palace) is the main attraction. It is a short walk from the train station. Open daily except Tuesdays; entry approximately €16. The park extends for 3km behind the palace; an electric vehicle can take you to the upper cascade if walking the full length is impractical.
