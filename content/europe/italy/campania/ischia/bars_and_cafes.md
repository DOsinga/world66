**Visit the Excellent Bar Calise**

Walking near the many **Ischia bars and cafes**, you may want to stop by at the Bar Calise. It is located on Antonio Sogliuzzo Street, close to the Ischia harbor. Bar Calise is one of the most renowned bars in town, with traditions dating from 1925 when the bar first opened (it has been at the current location since the 1960s). It is an excellent place to have a drink or two before going to a nightclub or to rest after a wild night.

#### **The Atmosphere**

As you enter the bar, you will discover a stylish modern interior. Relax and sit down in a comfortable chair near one of the lovely little wooden tables. You will be soon welcomed by an elegantly-dressed waiter, who will take your order immediately. While waiting, you will enjoy the beautiful wooden decorations and the impressive bar section. The interior is additionally complemented by many green plants, bright white lights and traditional Neapolitan music. If the weather is good enough, you can also take advantage of the bar&rsquo;s lovely tropical garden.

#### **The Menu**

Bar Calise offers an extensive menu of drinks and cocktails, but its main specialty are the desserts. The bar offers over 50 different types of cakes, as well as a wide selection of biscuits, chocolates, pastries, jellies, sweets and ice cream. All of them are made by highly qualified confectioners. You should try the strawberry cake and the fresh fruit cheesecake.
