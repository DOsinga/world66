---
title: "Bars and Cafes in Ischia"
type: section
---

Ischia's bar scene is relaxed — the island runs at spa-town pace even in high summer. The standout is **Bar Calise** (Via Antonio Sogliuzzo 69, near Ischia harbour), open since 1925. It's known as much for its pastries and ice cream as for its drinks — over 50 types of cake and an excellent selection of Neapolitan sweets. The tropical garden terrace is pleasant on warm evenings.

For a livelier evening, the harbour area of Ischia Porto has several bars with outdoor seating that stay open late. Forio has a cluster of bars near the Piazza Municipio.
