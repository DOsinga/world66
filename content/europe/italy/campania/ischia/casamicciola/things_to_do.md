### [Parco Thermale Castiglione](/europe/italy/campania/ischia/casamicciola/things_to_do/parco_thermale_cas)

In a beautiful green forest ten different pools, according to temperature and their curing abilities, are found in terraces that descend all the way to the sea. After leaving the entrance way ticket office below the free parking lot, take the funicular down or walk down the steps and leave your belongings in the check-in wardrobe and changing room.
