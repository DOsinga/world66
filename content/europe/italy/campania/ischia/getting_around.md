**Ischia transportation** has never been better, as this little volcanic island continues to rely on tourism as its main source of income. Tourists, especially those coming from Europe and Asia, simply can&rsquo;t resist the natural appeal of this beauty. Ischia is popular around the globe for its hot springs and volcanic mud, which many believe to have healing effects.

If is it your first time to visit Ischia, there&rsquo;s no reason to get scared. It&rsquo;s so easy to see places around the island, especially with the help of dependable Ischia transportation. You can feast your eyes on the rich natural wonders of the island, and enjoy your vacation by hopping from one island to another. Here are some of the modes of transport in Ischia:

**Buses**

Buses are mostly used by locals and tourists who want to explore the island without spending too much on transportation. In fact, there are tourists buses which provide a tour around the place, complete with a certified guide.

**Taxis**

Just like in most tourist destinations, taxis are easy to find, especially in the port and popular tourist spots. You can always take one if you prefer privacy. These taxis are similar to your regular cars, but there are some known as micro taxis which are open cars with awnings.

**Boats**

If you love to explore the nearby islands, tourist boats are docked at the port. You can hire one or go with other tourists to avoid paying too much for fuel and rent. Boats can also give you a tour of the whole island.
