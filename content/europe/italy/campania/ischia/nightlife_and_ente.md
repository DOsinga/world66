---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment"
type: section
---

Ischia's evenings are quieter than the mainland but livelier than their reputation suggests. The harbour area of Ischia Porto has bars and clubs that stay open late in summer. Most nightlife is relaxed rather than intense — outdoor bars, live music, gelato until midnight.

**O'Spasso** (Piazza degli Eroi, Ischia Porto) is one of the island's liveliest venues in summer, with cabaret, live music, and a full bar. Summer only.

**Bar Calise** (Via Antonio Sogliuzzo 69, near the harbour) is the go-to spot for dessert and drinks — the outdoor tropical garden terrace is a pleasant place to sit after dinner.

**La Mortella Gardens** (Forio) hosts concerts in its Greek amphitheatre through the season (April–November, weekends). Jazz, classical, and recital programmes. Tickets around €15 for adults.
