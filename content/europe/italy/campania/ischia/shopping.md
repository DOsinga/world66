title: "Shopping in Ischia"
type: section
---

**Ischia Shopping** is luxury at its best. You might want to
explore and check out the various shopping outlets spread across Ischia.
Read on to find out about the top stores that should not be missed on
your Ischia Shopping spree.

**Bottiglieri **

At the center of the city stands the prestigious Bottiglieri store.
This is a jewelry store beyond compare. It showcases the Italian passion
for jewelry. The designs are unique and precious stone adorn the
jewelry here. The store has a competent staff that will help you pick
the right designs. Several brands like Zoccai, Eberhard, and Comets are
featured in this store. Glance though the extensive collection of rings,
pendants and bracelets at this high profile store in Ischia.

**Scaglione**

Trust the Italians to get men’s fashion right. Scaglione is a
clothing store located on Via Alfredo and specializes in men’s apparel.
The store has a range that spans from casual to formal wear. Though
Scaglione carries clothing for men, the store is also well-known for a
women’s line of clothing. The Italian eye for detail is highlighted in
the intricacy of the design and in the cuts. It is a famous and most
preferred store by visitors and locals alike.

**Maja Curiosity**

Located in Piazzetta, Maja Curiosity is the place to go to if you
wish to take home souvenirs and gifts. This place has a wide selection
of items that are great value for the money. This is a highly
prestigious gift store in Ischia and features classic pieces of art. The
stone sculptures and the various ship models, make for great buys. You
can also purchase several vintage items from this store. You can also
buy body and bath products like minerals and lotions at this outlet. The
clocks and watches are designed to reflect the traditional style.
**
Maja
Gioielli**

Step into this traditional store in Ischia and explore the world of
corals and delicate jewelry. Each piece of jewelry is unique because it
is designed and made to fit individual tastes. The designs have a
Mediterranean influence and the corals are well-shaped. The store is
warm and inviting and the store has a marine-life theme.

**Salvatore
Shop**

For those who wish to get hold of something familiar in this foreign
land, the Salvatore Shop will provide for their needs. This is the sole
authorized Levis dealers in Ischia. The store stocks Levis products from
jeans and tees to shoes and sportswear. The store also carries other
brands like Harley and Into. Try on the latest fad at this store and
enjoy the shopping experience.
