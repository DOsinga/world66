A three-day guide by Italy specialists that structures the trip by neighborhood and builds in a Pompeii day trip on Day 2. Particularly strong on cultural context — it explains why each place matters (the Bourbon history, the Greek foundations, why da Michele only has two pizzas) rather than just listing names. Best for first-time visitors to the south of Italy who want depth alongside good food.
