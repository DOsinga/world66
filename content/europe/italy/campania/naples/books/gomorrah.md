Saviano's 2006 investigation into the Camorra — Naples's organised crime system — reads with the urgency of a thriller but is rigorously reported. He documents the construction rackets, the toxic waste dumping, the fashion industry supply chains, and the extraordinary brutality of the clans. Saviano has lived under police protection since publication. The book explains why parts of the Naples hinterland look the way they do.
