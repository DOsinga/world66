A spacious bar set along the Corso Vittorio Emanuele, one of Naples' grand thoroughfares that runs along the hillside above the city centre. The main room opens onto several smaller sitting rooms, making it a decent spot to settle in with a coffee or an evening aperitivo away from the chaos of the Quartieri Spagnoli below.
