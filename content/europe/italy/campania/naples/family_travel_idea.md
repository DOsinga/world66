---
title: "Family Travel Ideas in Naples"
type: section
---

Your **Naples family travel** should include activities that your entire family will enjoy, thus it is very important to plan the activities that you will be doing there. Naples is an Italian city which is known for its rich history, culture, art, architecture and food. For this reason, a lot of tourists frequent the city for sightseeing tours and cultural immersions. Thus, if you are one of the tourists who want to take your family to Naples, then here are some of the activities that you can do to make your stay in this beautiful city worthwhile.

**Piazza Matteotti**

During your family travel to Naples, it is important that you immerse your children to the fine culture of the city, thus the best place for you to begin your sightseeing tour is to go to the Piazza Matteotti, which is a castle that is listed as a World Heritage Site. This plaza has a museum, garden and walkways that will surely leave you and your kids marveling about how grand life used to be inside the castle.

**The City Acquario**

To leave your children amused, you can take them to the Acquario, which is just the city&rsquo;s aquarium. The aquarium has big tanks where you can see fishes that you will mostly find in the Mediterranean Sea. There is also a big glass walkway that allows you to see the aquarium&rsquo;s entire collection of fishes everywhere you look. Your kids will surely enjoy guessing the names of the fishes that they will see in the aquarium.

**Edenlandia Theme Park**

Release the young child inside of you and go to the Edenlandia Theme Park, which has a host of exciting rides such as roller coasters and others. The park also has a 3D Cinema if you want to watch movies together with your children.

**The Citta della Scienza**

For an educative excursion for the entire family, you should head off to the Citta della Scienza which is the science center and planetarium of Naples. You can do hands-on scientific experiments that you and your children can participate in.

**Vesuvio National Parks**

There are activities that your children can do in the Vesuvio National Parks aside from hiking and trekking. Your entire family can take on the sensory trail which is organized by the park rangers. This activity allows your entire family to observe the sights, sounds and scents of the local plant life.
