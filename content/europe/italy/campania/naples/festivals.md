---
title: "Festivals in Naples"
type: section
---

Tourists flock to the vibrant city of Naples to enjoy the vibrant **Naples** **festivals **that are celebrated throughout the year. These grand events highlight artistic treasures of the place. Arts and crafts lovers from far-off places come to watch these celebrations. Naples is known for its rich artistic treasures, and this is revealed only by visiting the city during festivals. During the festivals, the whole atmosphere of the city is color-splashed with films, music and artistic talents.
**

Art Festivals in Naples
**

Naples Art Association conducts various national and local festivals year-round in Naples. The national festivals include the Naples National Art Festival, the Naples Renaissance Fall Art Festival, the Downtown Naples Festival of Arts, and the Mercato Fine Arts Festival. Both tourists and local people of Naples flock to the venue to enjoy and support these festivals. Art and craft lovers take part in annual art festivals and indigenous festivals. Tourists visiting the city in the winters can witness the Naples International festival. This festival is dedicated to noteworthy films and documentaries. The Naples International Film Festival is held in November.
**

Blood Miracle Festival of San Gennaro
**

In the month of September, a religious festival called Blood Miracle Festival of San Gennaro is celebrated. Processions, rituals and carnivals are considered integral parts of this festival.

**New Year Celebration**

New Year is a one of the most colorful and festive events in Naples. Tourists can witness the fabulous fireworks display during the New Year&rsquo;s Eve celebrations. This is a time when several visitors from across the world gather here to bring in the New Year.
