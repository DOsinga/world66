There are many forms of **Naples Transportation** in this Italian city of Campania. Tourists flock to Naples to enjoy its rich history, art, architecture, food, and music. Thus, one is able to find a good amount of hotels here. Plus, there are various options for transport within Naples. It should be noted that traffic in Naples is very bad. Listed below are the ways one can get around in the city of Naples.

**From the Airport**

There are many options to reach the city of Naples from the airport. The best option would have to be the shuttle bus that leaves every ten minutes from the Naples International Airport. In fact, it is always on time, and will ensure no time is lost when getting into the city of Naples. However, if you opt for taxi, you can be sure of shelling out a large amount of money on the fare. Adding to this is the terrible traffic on the way to the main city of Naples. If you do take a taxi, make sure you have asked for a pre-paid taxi from the airport authorities.

**Transport within Naples**

When moving around in Naples, it is best advised to make use of public transportation. The most widely used is the Azienda Napolitana Mobilita bus, which runs throughout the city. A second choice of transportation within the city of Naples is the Funicular tram. The traffic is incredibly hectic, so it is best to avoid driving around in a car or in a taxi, which can get a bit expensive.
