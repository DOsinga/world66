---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment"
type: section
---

Naples' nightlife is concentrated in a few neighbourhoods. The Chiaia district has the most polished bars and clubs; the area around Piazza Bellini in the historic centre is livelier and more student-friendly. Discotheques tend to cluster along the seacoast drives heading out of the city.

The **Teatro di San Carlo** is one of the oldest and most celebrated opera houses in the world. Founded in 1737, it predates La Scala in Milan. The season runs from January to June and October to December. Check the schedule before you arrive if opera matters to you.
