Founded in 1737 and predating La Scala, Teatro di San Carlo is one of the oldest opera houses in the world. Six levels of gilded boxes surround a stage that opens into the gardens of the Palazzo Reale. Check the website for current schedules and ticket prices; popular performances sell out.
