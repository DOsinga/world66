Piazza Bellini is the drinking heart of the historic centre. Tables spill out from the surrounding bars and the atmosphere, especially in summer, is lively without being rowdy. The crowd skews young and local. In the Chiaia area, the streets around San Pasquale are lined with small wine bars open most evenings.
