Just outside of [Naples](/europe/italy/campania/naples) lies Pompeii, a city frozen in time when Mt Vesuvius erupted in 79AD covering the town and its inhabitants in lava. Today it is a bit of a tourist trap on the outside, but worth venturing in to see the ruins.
