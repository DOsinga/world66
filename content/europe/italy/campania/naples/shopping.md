Shopping in the city is actually pretty high on the list of entertaining Naples activities. Several major shopping streets surround Piazza dei Martiri -- a major Naples attraction. Via dei Millie, Via Filangieri, and Via Chiala are some of the most upscale shopping streets in the city. Via dei Millie, Via Filangieri, and Via Chiala streets feature luxury perfume shops and boutiques as well as everything from Prada, Gucci, and Versace to Cartier, Louis Vuitton, Damiani, and more. Head to bustling Via Chiaia and Via Toledo streets for clothes that are a bit more budget-friendly as well as a spattering of cute cafés and tasty little food shops. Continue reading through the listings to find out about some of the best places to shop in Naples.

### [Marinella](/europe/italy/campania/naples/shopping/marinella)

Marinella is Naples' most famous shops for several reasons: superb service, some of the highest quality merchandise in the world, and the coveted handmade ties, which average around $140 a pop. Even ex-president Bill Clinton made a special trip to Marinella just to buy a tie. Other high-profile clients (of course "high-profile" is an understatement) include Prince Charles, King Juan Carlos of Spain, and Italian industrial magnate Gianni Agnelli. Marinella opens at 6:30 a.m. as a courtesy to its special customers seeking that one-of-a-kind tie before [more..](/europe/italy/campania/naples/shopping/marinella)

### [Forcella Market](/europe/italy/campania/naples/shopping/forcella_market)

Forcella Market is an open-air market that has been described as "an incredible place where you can find anything, legal or not." Just a few things bargain hunters can expect to find here include: leather, perfume, accessories, alcohol, and locally made knick-knacks of all kinds. Forcella Market is located between piazza Garibaldi and corso Umberto and it's open from 9:00 a.m. to noon.

type: Markets



address: Between piazza Garibaldi and corso Umberto

### [Poggioreale Market](/europe/italy/campania/naples/shopping/poggioreale_market)

If you're looking for quality shoes of all kinds (and mens clothes), at amazingly low prices, head to the Poggioreale Jail. No, the shoes are not sold at the jail -- Poggioreale Market is roughly 0.62 miles past the jail at the crossing between Poggioreale and via Marino di Caramanico. The packed Poggioreale market is open Friday-Monday from 6:00 a.m.-1:00 p.m.

type: Markets



address: via Marino di Caramanico

### [La Feltrinelli](/europe/italy/campania/naples/shopping/la_feltrinelli)

Located at piazza dei Martini, La Feltrinelli has three floors of books and CDs to explore. The venue houses thousands of titles covering everything from Italian and foreign literature to philosophy, guidebooks, science, tourism, and more. Hundreds of CDs are available representing all genres of music from Iron Maiden to Keith Jarrett. Shoppers can enjoy discounts of up to 50% here and you will also find a ticket agency onsite if you're interested in catching a show later in the evening.
