Marinella is Naples' most famous shops for several reasons: superb service, some of the highest quality merchandise in the world, and the coveted handmade ties, which average around $140 a pop. Even ex-president Bill Clinton made a special trip to Marinella just to buy a tie. Other high-profile clients (of course "high-profile" is an understatement) include Prince Charles, King Juan Carlos of Spain, and Italian industrial magnate Gianni Agnelli. Marinella opens at 6:30 a.m. as a courtesy to its special customers seeking that one-of-a-kind tie before heading to work. Marinella doesn't advertise -- the store was made famous by word of mouth and of course, magnificent ties and timeless shirts and sweaters.
