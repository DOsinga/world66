Travel Guide

A national park in the south of region Campania. Ancient cradle of civilization, home of Parmenide's Eleatic School
which gave an extraordinary impetus to the development of Western
philosophical thought. Theater of myths lost in the mists of time,
where Ulysses encountered the Sirens and Palinuro was shipwrecked.
