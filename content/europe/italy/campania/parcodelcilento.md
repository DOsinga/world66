The National Park of Cilento and Vallo di Diano is the second italian Park, what surface is concerned. However,it is a park of fairly new institution. The landscape and vegetation of the park are very interesting.  You will find a lot of charming and impressive caves and bays. The zone is full of prehistoric "ruins" and it is known that it was already inhabited in the Paleolitic era (500.000 b.C.).

 Not only nature lovers will love this Park. Infact in the neighborhood there is plenty of huge discos for the lovers of the "night life". Shuttle buses organized from the discos themselves will bring you right on the dancing floor, from practically every of the many camping and villages in the Park. This has, of course,  some drawback, sometimes this buses are not really on time and you may have to wait 1 hour for them.  But, of course, you'll have still all the night in front of you...
