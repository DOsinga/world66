---
title: "Villa of Mysteries"
type: poi
---

The Villa is located on a slope, and is a fine example of a suburban antique villa. It is a small maze of living rooms, servant quarters, kitchens and gardens. The main attraction of the villa is the amazing fresco of a dionysian ritual. This is probably the most famous fresco of antiquity, both because of the subject and the sheer size of it. In true size you can see an entire initiation ritual of the dionysian mysteries. It was painted around 70-60 BC.
