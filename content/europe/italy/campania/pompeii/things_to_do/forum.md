title: "The Forum"
type: poi

---

The civic and commercial heart of ancient Pompeii, surrounded by the remains of temples, market halls, and the basilica (law courts). The Temple of Jupiter dominates the north end with Vesuvius framed directly behind it. Plaster casts of eruption victims are displayed in a small storage building on the western side — among the most affecting things you will see at the site. The forum is usually the first major area you reach from the Porta Marina entrance.
