---
title: "Villa of Mysteries"
type: poi
category: Sight
latitude: 40.7555
longitude: 14.4770
---

Located outside the main city walls, about 400 metres from the Porta Ercolano entrance. This large suburban villa contains what is arguably the finest fresco cycle of antiquity: a continuous painting covering three walls of a single room, depicting a Dionysian initiation ritual. The figures are life-size, the colours still vivid after nearly two thousand years. The rest of the villa shows the layout of a working agricultural estate, with a wine press and servants' quarters.
