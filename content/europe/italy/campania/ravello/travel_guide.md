This **Ravello Travel Guide** will help you venture out into this luxurious tourist destination in Italy. This town was developed for the sole purpose of tourism, and is nestled in the hills above Amalfi. Needless to say, the views from this destination will enchant your senses. Read on to make the most of your trip to Ravello.

**Traveling to Ravello**

You need to fly in to the Naples Airport in Italy. This is the international airport and several airlines like Air France, Air Italy, Austrian Airlines, British Airways, Easy Jet, Lufthansa, Wizz Air and the likes fly to this destination. From the airport, you can rent a car and drive to Ravello. Ravello is located at a distance of 65 km from the Naples International Airport. You can also take the bus from the airport to the Naples railway station. The train will take you to Salerno. From Salerno, you can travel by bus to Amalfi, and then from there, you can proceed to Ravello by road.

**Where to Stay**

Being a tourist destination, Ravello has a wide range of hotels and resorts to choose from. From the extra luxurious villas to the budget class hotel, this place has it all. For a mid level, yet classy place to stay, check in to the Hotel Caruso. It is located overlooking the coast of Amalfi. This hotel used to be a castle during the 11th century.

**Romantic Attractions**

Ravello has a picturesque surrounding. This tourist destination has several sights and sounds to keep the visitors entertained. The most interesting aspect about Ravello is its two famous gardens. The tropical garden and the breathtaking view of the coast, sets a great backdrop for honeymooners. Also the Cathedral and the marble museum is a must see

**Local Transport**

You can explore Ravello by the local taxi service. If you wish to visit the attraction close to Ravello you can hop on to the SITA. This is the local bus service and the most preferred mode of transport in this part of Italy. Cars are not allowed to enter the center of Ravello town and the parking charges are really expensive in the other areas.

**Place to Eat**

Ravello has several small sized cafes and restaurants. These dining areas are known to serve the most amazing local cuisine. Pasta features as the central dish in all the restaurants and seafood is the star attraction. Hotel Parsifal has earned the reputation of serving home cooked food with the true Italian warmth. The tortillas stuffed with grilled meat are the most ordered dish here.
