---
title: "Eating Out in Sorrento"
type: section
---

Sorrento's food is solid southern Italian — fresh pasta, seafood, good pizza, and the local lemons used in everything from sauces to desserts. The limoncello made here is the real thing; buy a bottle from a deli rather than a souvenir shop. The restaurants immediately around the Piazza Tasso are generally tourist-oriented and overpriced; walk a few streets away for better value.

**Ristorante Sant'Antonino** (Via Santa Maria delle Grazie 6, tel: +39 081 877 1200). Fresh local dishes including excellent pizza. Outdoor terrace. One of the more reliable options in the centre.
