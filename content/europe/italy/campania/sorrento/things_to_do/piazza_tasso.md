title: "Piazza Tasso"
type: poi
category: Sight
latitude: 40.6261
longitude: 14.3764
---

The main square of Sorrento, named after the 16th-century poet Torquato Tasso who was born here. Busy with traffic and cafes, it is more functional than beautiful, but it is the hub of the town and a good starting point for walks into the old streets to the west.
