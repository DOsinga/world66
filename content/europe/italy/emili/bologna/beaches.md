type: section

Bologna is the capital city of the Emilia Romagna province Italy. While there are small **Bologna Beaches **by the rivers that surround the city, the Adriatic beaches are a short distance to the east coast line.

**Sassi Neri Beach**

The Sassi Neri Beach is a reef beach off the coast of the Adriatic, close to Bologna. When driving up to the coast line this beach is impossible to miss. The first thing people noticed when arriving at the beach are the black cobble stones. The beach has been rated as a decent surfing beach and surfers come from all over the world to try the waves. There are a number of cliffs surrounding the beach and provide excellent trails for hikers to explore. One thing to keep in mind is that this beach is for more mature travelers. The majority of beach goers at Sassi Neri are nudists and it may not be the best the place for children to go.

**Fossacesia Marina**

The Fossacesia Marina is known for its excellent surfing conditions. This beach is easily accessible by public transportation and is within walking distance to the nearby town of Pescara. The Fossacesia Marina is one of the more popular beaches in the area. There is an old church by the shore for beach goers that are history buffs. The white sands of this beach make it inviting for anyone, regardless if whether they like to surf or swim. There are a number of nature trails nearby for anyone interested in hiking. A family friendly beach, there is something for everyone.

**Sottomarina******

The Sottomarina beach runs among a narrow stretch of the Adriatic Sea with a lagoon. There a number of private bathing houses located here. This stretch is quite developed with a number of cafes located on a broad walk so people do not have to go far if they need something. There are number of activities on this beach. People swim, surf or sunbathe. It is recommended that people try and walk along the shore to get a better feel for the coastline. Sottomarina is also a popular fishing destination. There are a number of fishermen that take out visitors so they can deep sea fish in the Adriatic. The Sotomarina is a family friendly beach for everyone.
