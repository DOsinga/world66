title: "Festivals in Bologna"
type: section
---

There are many **Bologna festivals** throughout the year, and most follow the religious calendar. Not only do people celebrate the religious holidays, but there are several festivals put on by various companies, theatre and music groups, and the city itself.

**Madonna di San Luca**

Easter is the most important religious festival of the year. The celebration focuses around the Madonna di San Luca. She is famous because after her image was brought to the city, the heavy rains that had plagued the city suddenly stopped in 1433.
