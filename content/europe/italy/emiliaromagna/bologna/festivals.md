---
title: 'Festivals'
type: section
---

Bologna has many festivals throughout the year, most following the religious calendar. The city also hosts a lively programme of music, theatre, and cultural events.

**Madonna di San Luca.** Easter is the most important religious festival of the year. The celebration focuses on the Madonna di San Luca, whose image is said to have stopped the heavy rains plaguing the city in 1433. Each year during Easter, her image is transported in procession to the Basilica di San Petronio.

**Cinema Ritrovato.** In late June and July, restored silent and sound films are screened outdoors in Piazza Maggiore -- a magical way to spend a summer evening.

**Mercatini di Santa Lucia.** One of Italy's oldest Christmas markets, held in the streets around the basilica in December. A Bologna tradition not to be missed.
