title: 'Il Cantinone'
type: poi
address: 'Via del Pratello 56A'
latitude: 44.495439
longitude: 11.331764
---

A great place to come after dinner with a group of friends to talk and listen to some occasional music. If you want a suggestion for an after-dinner drink, ask for a glass of *fragolino*, a sweet wine made from the fragolino grape. Since its alcohol content is too low to be legally sold as wine, fragolino is hard to find.
