**Il Cantinone**, Via del Pratello 56A, A great place to
come after dinner with a group of friends to talk and to listen
to some occasional music. If you want a suggestion for an
after-dinner drink, ask for a glass of *fragolino*,
which is a sweet wine that is made of the sweet fragolino
grape (since its alcohol content is too low, it can not be
sold as wine and so fragolino wine is hard to find!).
