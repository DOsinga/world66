Collection of paintings coming from neighbouring convents and monasteries. Find your way from medieval times (with the Giotto alterpiece) through the Renaissance to the Baroque period (don't miss out on the Carracci collection, the famous Bolognese masters). The Pinacoterca was founded at the beginning of the 19th century.

Due to problems with the staff half of the museum is open in the morning and the other half in the afternoon (summer 2005).
