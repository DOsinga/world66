Small and tucked away in a side street off Via Zamboni, with entrance under the Portico Bentivoglio next to the San Giacomo Church. Very nice, with great frescoes by major local painters from the fifteenth century. You are allowed to take pictures if you don't use your flash. Entrance is free.
