Built in small bay, Cattolica is favoured by a mild climate perfect for a relaxing holiday.


 
 The acient origin of its port is testified by the paleontological site on the bottom of the sea.


 
 Fishing is still one of the most important activities here. In summer, as part of a village folk festival with music and fireworks, tradition is renewed with Fish Rustida (grilled fish) prepared by the fishermen and served to visitors.


 
 The Deep-sea-fishing Championship and the Windsuff-day are sports events which indicate the vitality of this resort that has made the sea the main attraction.


 
 The Festiual of Flowers turns the town into a colourful garden to announce the official opening of the Tourist Season.


 
 Other events follow and the season is concluded with the Mystfest, the International Festival of Mystery and Thrillers.
