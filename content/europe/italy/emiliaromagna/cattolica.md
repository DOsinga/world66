Built in a small bay, Cattolica is favoured by a mild climate perfect for a relaxing holiday. The ancient origin of its port is testified by a paleontological site on the sea floor.

Fishing is still one of the most important activities here. In summer, as part of a village folk festival with music and fireworks, tradition is renewed with **Fish Rustida** -- grilled fish prepared by the fishermen and served to visitors. The Deep-sea-fishing Championship and the Windsurf Day are sports events that show the vitality of this resort.

The **Festival of Flowers** turns the town into a colourful garden to announce the official opening of the tourist season. Other events follow, and the season concludes with the **Mystfest**, the International Festival of Mystery and Thrillers.
