Comacchio is like a small [Venice](/europe/italy/veneto/venice), just 30 km north of [Ravenna](/europe/italy/emiliaromagna/ravenna). The town is located in the Valli, a series of lakes and marshes that make for wonderful walks and bicycle rides.

There are a few canals, an old bell tower, some stylish bridges, and a nice cathedral. The town is famous for its eels, prepared in every possible way and celebrated at an annual festival in autumn.

Just 5 km from Comacchio you find the Lidi di Comacchio (Lido di Pomposa, Lido degli Scacchi, Lido Estense), a series of seaside resorts with good beaches, hotels, and some nightlife, most of it geared towards families.
