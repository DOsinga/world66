**[Este Castle](/europe/italy/emiliaromagna/ferrara/sights/estecastle).** Built in 1385 by Bartolino da Novara, the castle is the most prominent sight in Ferrara. It is joined to the Palazzo Ducale by a covered and elevated passageway. From the sixteenth century onwards it became the court residence, acquiring its present unmistakable appearance. Largo Castello 1, open 9:30-17:30, closed Mondays.

**Palazzo Municipale.** The building of the Town Hall started in 1243. This was the residence of the Este family until the sixteenth century, when the court moved to the castle. Piazzetta Municipale.

**Palazzo Schifanoia.** Built outside the ancient walls by order of Alberto V d'Este in 1385 and later extended, above all during the era of Borso. The most famous room is the "Sala dei Mesi" (Room of the Months), frescoed by fifteenth-century Ferrarese painters. Via Scandiana 23.
