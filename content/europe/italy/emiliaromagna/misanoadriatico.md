This resort has three kilometres of beach marking the border between the regions of Romagna and Marche.

It is a good place for an active holiday: modern sports facilities, the **Misano World Circuit** at Santa Monica for important national and international motorcycle events, and the charming harbour at Portoverde, equipped to take over a thousand pleasure boats.
