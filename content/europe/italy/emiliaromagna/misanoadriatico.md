Misano Adriatico sits at the southern edge of the Romagna coast, where the Adriatic Riviera gives way to Marche. The three kilometres of sandy beach here are quieter than [Rimini](/europe/italy/emiliaromagna/rimini) to the north or [Cattolica](/europe/italy/emiliaromagna/cattolica) to the south, making it a reasonable base if you want coast without the crowds.

The town has two distinct draws. The **Misano World Circuit** at Santa Monica hosts major national and international motorcycle racing events, including MotoGP rounds — if you are a motorsport fan, this alone may be reason enough to visit. The circuit also offers track days for cars and motorcycles when racing is not scheduled.

The **Porto Verde** marina on the southern edge of town is the other anchor. It accommodates over a thousand pleasure boats and has a pleasant harbour-front strip of bars and restaurants. It is a good place to spend an evening regardless of whether you arrived by sea.
