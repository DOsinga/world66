---
title: Getting There
type: section
---

The nearest airport is **Rimini Federico Fellini International Airport** (RMI), about 18 km north, with seasonal connections to several European cities. Bologna airport (BLQ) is about 120 km away and has more year-round international routes.

By train, the closest station is **Cattolica–Misano–Gabicce**, served by regional trains on the Bologna–Ancona Adriatic coast line. From Rimini it is about 20 minutes; from Bologna, just over an hour. The station is around 3 km from the town centre, reachable by bus or taxi.

By road, the A14 Autostrada Adriatica runs just inland — exit at Cattolica and it is a short drive north to Misano. The SS16 coastal road also connects the resort strip running from Rimini down through Riccione, Cattolica, and Misano without using the motorway.
