The Museo Enzo Ferrari occupies the house where Enzo Ferrari was born in 1898, extended with a spectacular aluminium-clad pavilion designed by Jan Kaplický. The collection covers Ferrari's life and the history of the company from its origins as a racing team. This is not a general car museum — it's specifically about the man and the marque, and works whether or not you're a motorsport fan. The Galleria Ferrari in nearby Maranello (15km south) has a larger collection of racing and road cars.
