Pomposa is a beautiful old abbey in the Po Delta, about 50 km north of [Ravenna](/europe/italy/emiliaromagna/ravenna). The Benedictine abbey dates from the sixth century and was an important centre of learning in the Middle Ages -- it was here that Guido d'Arezzo developed the modern system of musical notation. The tall Romanesque bell tower is visible for miles across the flat marshlands.
