Nestled in the foothills of the Apennines and bisected by the Reno River, Porretta Terme is a bit off the beaten path, west of the main highway that links [Florence](/europe/italy/tuscany/florence) and [Bologna](/europe/italy/emiliaromagna/bologna). If you have time and want a reasonably priced spa experience, this is the place. Accessible by car or train, the town has a long history as a thermal destination and hosts the **Porretta Soul Festival**, one of Europe's most important soul and R&B music events, every July.
