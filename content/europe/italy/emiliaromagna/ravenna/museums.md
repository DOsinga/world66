### National Museum

Housed in the cloisters of the ex-Benedictine Monastery, in the monumental complex of San Vitale. The museum includes important collections of minor arts: funeral steles and epigraphs, archaeological fragments, a collection of textiles (from Coptic to Renaissance), ivories, icons, coins and medals, and a fascinating collection of ancient weapons. It also houses the fourteenth-century cycle of frescoes of Santa Chiara, which originally decorated the church of the Poor Clare Sisters. Floor mosaics from an archaeological complex in Via D'Azeglio, discovered in 1993, are also on display.
