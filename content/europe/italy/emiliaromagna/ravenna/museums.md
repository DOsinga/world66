### [National Museum](/europe/italy/emiliaromagna/ravenna/museums/national_museum)

Housed in the cloisters of the ex-Benedictine Monastery, in the
monumental complex of San Vitale, the National Museum includes
important collections of minor arts among which some funeral steles and
epigraphs, various archaeological fragments, a collection of textiles
(from Coptic to Renaissance), an important collection of ivories, a
rich collection of icons, a coins and medals section and a fascinating
collection of ancient weapons. It also houses the 14th century cycle of
frescoes of Santa Chiara which decorated the church of the Poor Clare
Sisters in Ravenna, [more..](/europe/italy/emiliaromagna/ravenna/museums/national_museum)
