---
title: "Bars and Cafes in Rimini"
type: section
---

Rimini is often referred to with terms like “the capital of Italian fun lovers” and the “Las Vegas of Europe.” This throbbing nightlife destination of the Mediterranean has a line-up of some of the finest bars and cafes in the region that serve party enthusiasts from all over the world. The **Rimini bars and cafes** guide lists some of the most popular hangout places in this buzzing party town.

**Bar Caffe Cavour**

Bar Caffe Cavour, located at Piazza Cavour Camillo 12, is one of the busiest bars around the chic Piazza Cavour entertainment district. A perfect place for people watching, the bar exudes a charming old world Italian elegance. Visitors can party with a tall glass of refreshing Prosecco or enjoy the scrumptious and fresh Italian delicacies.

**American Green Bar**

Located in Riccione, one of Rimini’s hippest districts, the American Green Bar is an easy medley of the cool American with the stylish Italian. The place has a lovely open terrace seating and a well-stocked bar that serves classic cocktails and an assortment of beers. The elegant atmosphere is further accentuated by the presence of a DJ who churns out some smooth numbers to entertain guests. American Green Bar is slightly on the expensive side and needs a booking during the peak tourist season, but all that’s worth it when you experience the energetic pulse of this trendy Rimini hotspot. The bar is open daily from 10 p.m. to 3 a.m.

**Bar La Loggia**

Bar La Loggia at 19 Piazza Maggiore is a glamorous venue that boasts of a fine collection of wines, liqueurs and other spirits. The bar is attractively located inside a medieval castle atop a hill in the center of Mondaino Square. Apart from being ultra-trendy, Bar La Loggia is cozy, comfortable and classy, and sports a unique antique decor that is hard to miss. The tresette bar and briscola area resemble a country-house and there are several pavement tables arranged for patrons who seek a more outdoorsy drinking and dining experience.

**Cantina Aula Magna**

This is an informal yet stylish cafe located at Piazzetta Agabiti 4 in the university area of Rimini. Cantina Aula Magna offers its patrons a wide assortment of snacks from delicious and fresh hand-made pizzas to sandwiches and other light appetizers. Visitors can also feast on some signature Italian desserts and a range of cappuccinos.

**Bio’s Cafe**

This one’s for the health-conscious lot. The cafe uses only pure organic products in all its items, which include a wide array of ice-creams and light snacks. A special menu is available for takeaways. Bio’s Cafe is located in the busy Via Bramante neighborhood.

Rimini basks in the distinction of being 1 of the trendiest spots in Italy and features several chic bars and cafes to live up to its party-destination status.
