---
title: "Budget Travel Ideas in Rimini"
type: section
---

One of the best ways to go on an affordable vacation, even if it is overseas, is to set a reasonable budget for your vacation. The key to setting a budget for your vacation is to gather **Rimini budget travel** ideas before you leave. This will let you know how much things cost, so you can save up the appropriate amount of money. When setting a budget, the best Rimini budget travel ideas are going to be the cheapest ones, and free is even better.**
**

**Budget Airlines
**

The first thing that comes to mind when you think of traveling is the cost of airfares. There are several low cost airlines that operate in and out of Rimini, with Air Berlin, TUIfly and Ryanair being some of the most popular ones. You can get a good discount on the price of tickets. Check with the airline office for discounts beforehand.**
******

**Beaches
**

During the summer months, especially August, life in Rimini is pretty much lived on the beaches. If you want to take advantage of the beautiful beaches, you don't have to pay to do so. There are plenty of free beaches you can visit along the Rimini Shoreline. What they have to offer is going to be limited, so come prepared.**
******

**Budget Accommodation**

There are several budget accommodations available in Rimini. You can choose from guesthouses, low budget hotels and backpackers hostels. Most of these places offer decent accommodation at a fraction of the price you will pay in bigger hotels. Several places provide online booking, but you can also walk into the place and check for available accommodation.**
******

**Transport**

One of the best ways to get around Rimini is by taking the public transport. You can either take the train or the bus, which are much cheaper than taking a taxi. You can purchase a train ticket a day or two in advance to avoid the rush, especially if you are traveling during the holiday season. You can also enjoy the city on a bicycle, which is a great way to take in the beauty of the place at your own pace.**
******

**Shopping**

Rimini offers its visitors some excellent budget shopping. Take a walk down the shopping areas and local markets and you will find several places where you can get good bargains. Several fairs are held during the year in Rimini, such as the Bird Fair in September, and the Bechi Fair and the San Michele of Arcangelo Festival in November. These are times when you can get good bargains on several artistic handcrafted products from local workshops.
