Authentic Italian osteria serving traditional homemade pastas with fresh ingredients cooked to order. Try the fantastic pasta e fagioli (creamy pasta and beans), tagliatelle ai porcini, or the excellent stuffed pastas: tortelloni made of chickpeas in rosemary sauce, ravioli with artichokes topped with fossa cheese. Fossa cheese literally means "cheese of the pit" -- it has an intense, somewhat piquant flavour that makes it excellent grated over pasta.

Main courses include baccala (salt cod), oven-baked rabbit with wild fennel, cured ham and rosemary ("in porchetta"), all served with huge portions of oven-roasted potatoes and stewed baby zucchini. The specials are usually worth a look, featuring whatever is in season.

Crisp local wine, friendly service, enjoyable atmosphere. Booking is advised.
