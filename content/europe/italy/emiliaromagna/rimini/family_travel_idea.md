---
title: "Family Travel Ideas in Rimini"
type: section
---

Planning a **Rimini Family Travel** vacation trip from a variety of other interesting and fun cities will make this trip the best you’ve ever had. This old-time Italian city is proud of its entertaining attractions which has drawn many people from all over the world. No less than sixteen ancient structures are here for you to view plus several theme parks and beaches await you. So whether you are fond of the outdoors or are more inclined to stay in a more relaxed environment, there is a lot here your family can enjoy.

**Aquafan**

The Aquafan is open from June to September which is considered the best time of the year to visit Rimini. If your family likes water parks this place is just what you need to participate in. There isn’t anything like it in all of Europe. All of the water rides are always being updated so you can have as much enjoyment as you are able to take. In addition to the water pools and slides the park boasts of several special attractions like the Baby Lagoon, white water rapids rides and the Elephant pool. Your family will have a time of their lives here.

**Miniature Italian Theme Park
**
Miniature is one of the 16 theme parks that are within the immediate area outside of Rimini. Just a quick ride out of town can take you to this well known park that is a delight for kids. All kinds of rides are available for you and your family to shout and laugh about. Stalls are throughout the park with all kinds of Italian style tricky games. So here at Miniature your family gets some education of the Italian culture in addition to have a great time.

**Beaches and Water Sports**

The beaches here in Rimini have the reputation of being the most friendly and fun-filled in all of Italy. The waterfront has shop after shop of items to buy or rent for every kind of water sport conceivable. There is no end to the pleasant time your family will have whether you are out for a tan or splashing around in the crystal clear waters.

**Children’s Market**

In the very center of Rimini is a marketplace where people gather to participate in all types of special arrangements. Concerts, marriages and fashion shows are staged here. But most important to your family is that a commerce market is here for kids. During the summer twice a week kids set up their stalls to sell their wares. It is a safe and fun place for your kids to engage in haggling with the local kids for merchandise while having a lot of laughs pretending to be buying like adults.
