---
title: 'Getting There'
type: section
---

**By train.** The train station is in the centre of Rimini, on Piazzale Cesare Battisti, ten minutes' walk from the seafront and the old town. [Ravenna](/europe/italy/emiliaromagna/ravenna), [Pesaro](/europe/italy/marche/pesaro), [Ancona](/europe/italy/marche/ancona), [Ferrara](/europe/italy/emiliaromagna/ferrara), [Padua](/europe/italy/veneto/padua), and [Bologna](/europe/italy/emiliaromagna/bologna) are all within an hour by train.

**By car.** It is easy to enter Rimini by car. Most hotels offer parking. Parking near the seaside is expensive but possible.

**By air.** The airport in nearby Miramare, Aeroporto Internazionale Federico Fellini, connects Rimini with European capitals and major Italian cities. In summer, air traffic increases with charter flights.
