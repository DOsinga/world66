---
title: 'Shopping'
type: section
---

**Corso d'Augusto.** One of Rimini's main streets, at the crossroad of Decumano and Cardo Massimi near the city centre. Several shops and boutiques selling branded clothing, pottery, and crafts. Gucci, Prada, Armani, and Dolce & Gabbana all have a presence here.

**Marina Centro.** A seaside shopping area along the seafront from Torre Pedrera to Miramare. Mostly beach-oriented stores, but you can also find traditional craft shops with good deals on souvenirs.

**Piazza Malatesta and Piazza Cavour.** The largest open-air street market in Emilia-Romagna takes place in Piazza Malatesta every Wednesday and in Piazza Cavour every Friday. Antiques, handcrafted items, clothing, and jewellery. A few antique shops and stalls are open almost every day.

**Le Befane Shopping Mall.** A modern shopping mall with 130 local and international shops, cafes, restaurants, and a multiplex cinema. Easy access from the railway station and buses.

**Viale Ceccarini.** A popular shopping street for the latest fashion trends and designer merchandise -- bags, shoes, belts, and clothing.
