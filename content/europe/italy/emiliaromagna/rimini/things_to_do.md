---
title: "Things to do in Rimini"
type: section
---

**Beaches**

These beaches are Blue Flag beaches as they are among the best quality beaches in the world. The beaches are clean and offer the perfect place to soak up the sun. The beach is 15 kilometers long. Swimming in the water is pleasant and offers a refreshing break from sun bathing. Other things to do on the beaches include a plenitude of water sports such as canoeing and pedal boats. Conditions at the beaches are suitable for surfing. Just off the beaches you can enjoy a delicious meal at a restaurant or an ice cold drink at one of the bars. The Adriatic Sea is also a good place to go snorkeling and there are a variety of fish to be seen. Boating and fishing are options for activities.

**Golfing**

For visitors who enjoy golfing there are golfing facilities in Rimini. There is a 18-hole golf course at Rimini golf club. This course is a championship course.

**Horse Back Riding**

Rimini has amazing scenery and horse riding is a good way to get around and explore Rimini's natural beauty.

**Nightlife**

Rimini has many nightime entertainment options. There are restaurants that offer fine dining and specialize in seafood dishes. Bars are a good place to start the night with a refreshing cocktail. The bars near the beach offer beautiful views and are the perfect spot for a romantic drink. Nightclubs thrive in Rimini and they offer a place to dance until the sun comes up.

**Dolphinarium**

There is a dolphinarium in Rimini which is an enjoyable activity for children as well as adults. This is a good way to spend the day if you have a family.
**
Amusement Park**

Italia in Miniture is an amusement park located in Rimini. This activity is suitable for children. The admission ticket provides visitors with unlimited access to all the rides in the park. One of the attractions is a giant map of Italy with minature representations of all the noteworthy monuments in Italy. There are also scenes depicting everyday life in italy. Visitors can walk around the map to get a good view of all the different areas. There are picnic areas to rest and enjoy the shade. There is a waterpark with rides. Smaller play areas are available for younger children. Food is available at kiosks and there are washrooms.

**Cruises**

Cruises are available off the coast of Rimini. You can choose between long and short cruises with meals and drinks.
