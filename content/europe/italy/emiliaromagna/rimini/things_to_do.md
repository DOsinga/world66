---
title: 'Things to Do'
type: section
---

**Beaches.** Rimini's 15-kilometre Blue Flag beach is clean and offers excellent conditions for swimming, sunbathing, and water sports including canoeing, pedal boats, surfing, and snorkelling. Restaurants and bars line the shore.

**Golfing.** The Rimini Golf Club has an 18-hole championship course.

**Horse riding.** A good way to explore Rimini's scenic countryside and the hills behind the coast.

**Nightlife.** Restaurants specialising in seafood, bars with beach views, and nightclubs that run until sunrise. Rimini's reputation as Italy's party capital is well earned.

**Dolphinarium.** An enjoyable activity for families, located in town.

**Italia in Miniatura.** An amusement park with a giant map of Italy featuring miniature representations of the country's most notable monuments, plus a waterpark, play areas for smaller children, and picnic spots.

**Cruises.** Available off the coast, with both long and short options including meals and drinks.
