---
title: "Six local beauties host ‘Walking Festival’ in Tuscany"
type: poi
---

From April 3 to October 25, hikers and nature lovers won’t want to miss an exciting trip to one of the six natural parks situated on the Tuscany coast or the chance to scout out the islands within the Tuscan Archipelago. From April 3 to June 28 and October 2-25, 2009, this lovely area will become the protagonist of a special event called the ‘Tuscan Coast & Islands - Walking Festival’. Six nature parks will be hosting hundreds of events, spotlighting routes that boast evocative landscape and many itinerary featuring archaeological treasures and stunning natural beauty.
The Tuscan Archipelago National Park, for example, is the largest marine park in the Mediterranean. From April 3 to May 10, tourists will be able to chose between more than one hundred trips being offered throughout its seven islands. On April 4, visitors to the inter-provincial Montioni Park can immerse themselves in 7,000 hectares of woods and valleys that stretch along the river and Cornia Pecora. Environmental excursion guides will be on hand to accompany participants along nature-paths where they can discover interesting historical sites. From May 2-31 the Regional Park of Migliarino, San Rossore, Massacciuccoli will also be hosting fascinating excursions. From May 10 to June 28, in the Regional Park of the Apuan Alps, you can take a peek at the area’s stunning marble caves. From October 2-25, visitors to the Regional Park of the Maremma can take shuttles from various locations and enjoy the Mediterranean landscape with its towering cliffs and sandy beaches.
