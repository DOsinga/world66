A small fishing village 10 km south of Trieste, just inside the Slovenian border, with a compact medieval centre built around a pretty harbour. It feels entirely unlike the grand Habsburg city nearby — smaller, warmer, more Venetian in character, with a 15th-century Duomo and a web of narrow streets behind the waterfront.

Reachable by the Delfino Verde ferry from the Trieste waterfront (check current timetables). Come for lunch or a late afternoon wander and dinner at one of the harbour-side restaurants. A good choice if you want a half-day excursion without the ticket price or crowds of Miramare.
