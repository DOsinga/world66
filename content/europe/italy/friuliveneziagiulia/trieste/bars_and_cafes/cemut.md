A small, unpretentious wine bar in the old city that embodies the Triestine *osmiza* tradition — local wine, good cicchetti, and no fuss. The name is Friulian dialect for "let's drink". Popular with locals after work and a good option if you want to drink well without paying cocktail-bar prices.

Go early evening; it fills quickly and there's no reservation system. The house Malvasia and Terrano (the local red, made from the same grape grown on the Carso plateau) are the things to order.
