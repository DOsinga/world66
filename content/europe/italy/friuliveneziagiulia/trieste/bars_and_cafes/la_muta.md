A relaxed wine bar in the Borgo Teresiano neighbourhood with a carefully chosen list of Friulian and Slovenian natural wines. The atmosphere is low-key and local — mismatched furniture, good lighting, no background music — and the staff know their producers well.

Good for an evening drink before or after dinner; the selection of small plates (cheese, charcuterie, bruschette) is solid enough to make a light meal of it if needed.
