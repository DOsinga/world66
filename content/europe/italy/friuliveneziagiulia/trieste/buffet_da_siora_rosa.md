A reliable alternative to Buffet da Pepi in the same Triestine *buffet* tradition — boiled meats, local wine, and the unpretentious atmosphere that defines this distinctly Central European style of eating. Slightly less famous than Pepi, which means fewer tourists and often shorter waits at lunchtime.
