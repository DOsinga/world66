A tightly structured hour-by-hour day by Nancy H. — starting at Piazza Unità for morning coffee, moving through the Roman Theatre and San Giusto hill, lunch at a traditional trattoria, an afternoon at Miramare Castle, sunset on the Molo Audace pier, and dinner at a seafood restaurant on the waterfront. Budget roughly €60–100 per person.
