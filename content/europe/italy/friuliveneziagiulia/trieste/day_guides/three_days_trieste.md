A three-day itinerary by Kat Smith (an expat in Italy) covering aperitivo bars in the Cavana neighbourhood, a self-guided walking tour of the historic centre, the hilltop castle, a seasonal osmiza dinner on the Carso, and a coastal ferry day to Barcola, Miramare, and the fishing village of Muggia.
