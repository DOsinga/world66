A long-standing osteria in the old city, popular with office workers at lunch and locals in the evening. The menu is traditional Triestine with the Austro-Hungarian undertones you'd expect: *goulash*, *jota*, *musetto* (cotechino-style pork sausage with polenta), and a reliable daily fish special. Draught wine served by the carafe.

Straightforward, unpretentious, and good value. The lunchtime set menu (soup, main, side, and a glass of wine) is one of the best deals in the centre.
