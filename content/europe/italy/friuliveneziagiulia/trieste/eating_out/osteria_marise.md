A neighbourhood osteria near the Canal Grande run by a family that has been cooking Triestine food in the same spot for decades. The menu is short and changes daily: expect *boreto alla Graesana* (vinegary fish stew), bigoli in salsa, grilled cuttlefish, and seasonal desserts. No concessions to tourism.

Inexpensive by city-centre standards. Arrive at 12:00 or 19:00 if you want a table without booking; it fills fast and the kitchen closes sharply when the food runs out.
