A small trattoria named after the ink-black cuttlefish dish that appears on almost every Triestine menu. The kitchen focuses on Adriatic seafood with minimal elaboration: *seppie al nero* (cuttlefish in its own ink, served with white polenta), grilled scampi, baccalà. The room is plain and the service is quick and direct — this is a lunch place, not a dinner event.

One of the better-value seafood options in the city centre. Book a day ahead for lunch at the weekend.
