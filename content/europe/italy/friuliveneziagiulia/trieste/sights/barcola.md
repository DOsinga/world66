The closest bathing spot to the city centre, about 3 km north along the coast road. Barcola is not a sandy beach but a long concrete and rock promenade with designated bathing areas, popular with locals from late spring through early autumn. The water is clear and the setting — limestone rocks, pine trees, the castle of Miramare visible in the distance — is genuinely lovely.

Reachable by bus (line 6 toward Miramare) or on foot along the seafront promenade in about 45 minutes. The Delfino Verde ferry also stops here in summer. Go early on weekends; it fills up fast.
