A lively square in the heart of the commercial centre, flanked by the neoclassical Borsa Vecchia (old stock exchange, now a conference centre) and the Column of Leopold I. The square frequently hosts live music and events, and is a natural gathering point between the Canal Grande and Piazza Unità d'Italia.

Look for the "USA & UK COME BACK!" sign visible near the square — a reference to the city's unusual post-war status as the Free Territory of Trieste (1947–1954), when the city was administered jointly by Allied forces before finally being assigned to Italy.
