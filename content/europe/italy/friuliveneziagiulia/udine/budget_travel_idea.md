---
title: "Budget Travel Ideas in Udine"
type: section
---

Vacationing in Udine can be a dream come true, with majestic views of the Italian countryside all around. Udine is open to travelers of all budget sizes, not just the rich and famous. Budget travel will not leave the visitor feeling cheated out of a magnificent holiday, but will leave a little extra change in your pocket to enjoy other aspects of the area.**
**

**Accommodation**

Fortunately for the frugal traveler, Udine makes several low cost, budget hotels and hostels available. Although some may offer only the basics, the traveler can rely on safety, cleanliness and a quiet place to rest their head. The Hotel Principe is an excellent example of how far one's money can go in Udine. Located near the center of town on the Vialle Europa, this hotel offers spacious, clean rooms with most modern conveniences. Singles, Doubles and Triples are on offer, making this hotel the perfect getaway for families. They have a multilingual staff that are very friendly and accommodating. Rooms start as low as 20 Euros per night, but room rates do vary highly according to season.**
**

**Food**

Eating fine Italian food does not have to cost a fortune and Udine provides many examples to support this fact. The Campana d'Oro restaurant is an excellent place to indulge in some traditional Italian dishes without blowing your whole travel budget. Located near Central Udine on the Piazza Maggio, the Campana D' Oro promises to leave you full. Portion sizes are enormous, with dishes ranging between 11 to 20 Euros. For quick snacks, coffee and sandwiches, the Caffe Bistros can't be beat. Located on the Piazza Matteotti, this cafe serves as a focal point for students, travelers and locals. Sandwiches and pastries are available for 2 to 3 euros each. It's easy to get caught up in the atmosphere and spend the day sipping coffee and watching the world go by.**
**

**Entertainment**

For those wanting to enjoy Udine on a budget, there are several low cost and even free activities to enjoy. Those that are fans of history may want to visit some of the local points of interest such as the Chiesa di San Fransisco, both a historical and religious site. The historic village of Gemona is a peaceful place to spend an afternoon. Udine also operates a movie theater near the town center, featuring films in Italian and English. Most of these activities are free or require a nominal cost.
