Civita Castellana is an artistic town which lies in the heart of the ancient Etruscan territory, not far from [Rome](/europe/italy/lazio/rome) and that has got an ancient tradition for ceramic arts. The archaeological sites of [Tarquinia}, [Cerveteri](/europe/italy/lazio/cerveteri) and [Ostia Antica](/europe/italy/lazio/ostiaantica), as well as [Viterbo](/europe/italy/lazio/viterbo), the medieval Papal seat, and the great Romanic cathedrals of Tuscania are easlily reachable from here.
