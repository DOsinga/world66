From Lake Bracciano there are plenty of opportunities for daytrip to discover beautiful places off the beaten path, with www.pontuali.com/marco

Set out on a half dayl day tour to explore and discover the Etruscan world. Pick up from your hotel, apartment or cruise ship and, through a panoramic drive, visit the vast Necropolises of Cerveteri, a masterpiece of creative genius that reveals aspects of life, death, and religious beliefs of the ancient Etruscans, and declared by UNESCO a World Heritage Site
Explore a real city with streets and tumulus tombs arranged like houses with entrance corridors , rooms with frescoes like the Tomb of Rilievi decorated with plaster reliefs of mythological figures.
Stop for a glass of wine and enjoy discovering the silent and beautiful Village and Castle of Santa Severa which occupies the area of the ancient site of Pyrgi, the Etruscan harbour town. In the centuries the first establishment was modified until it assumed the actual aspect that represents a real mediaeval village with its castle on the water-front.
On the way back to the ship struck by the landscape: large vineyards, tufa hills, Mediterranean trees and the Thyrrenian Sea. more details at www.pontuali.com/marco
