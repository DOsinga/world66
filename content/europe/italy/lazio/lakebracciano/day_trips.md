title: "Day Trips"
type: section
---

The area around Lake Bracciano is rich in Etruscan history. The Banditaccia necropolis at Cerveteri, a UNESCO World Heritage Site about 30 kilometres southwest, is one of the most impressive Etruscan sites in Italy — a vast city of the dead with streets of tumulus tombs, some decorated with plaster reliefs and frescoes. On the coast nearby, the medieval village and castle of Santa Severa sit on the site of ancient Pyrgi, the Etruscan harbour town.
