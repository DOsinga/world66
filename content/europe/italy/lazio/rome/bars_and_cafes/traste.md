A cocktail bar and tea room tucked into Trastevere. The mojitos and margaritas are well made, and there is a selection of over 30 teas ranging from standard green to specialty Lapsang and Oolong. The atmosphere is relaxed with chill-out and reggae music, making it a good spot to unwind after a day of sightseeing.
