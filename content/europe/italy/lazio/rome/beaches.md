---
title: 'Beaches'
type: section
---

Rome is not a beach city, but the coast is only 30 minutes away. Ostia is the closest and most accessible beach via the Roma-Lido train, though it gets packed on summer weekends. For cleaner water and a quieter experience, head south to Anzio or Nettuno, both reachable by train in about an hour. North of the city, Ladispoli offers dark volcanic sand and a more relaxed atmosphere.
