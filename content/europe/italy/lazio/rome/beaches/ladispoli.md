Quiet seaside town 45 minutes from central Rome by regional train from Termini (direct, frequent service). The dark volcanic sand beach is less packed than Ostia, with a 5–10 minute walk from Ladispoli station. Private beach clubs charge around €15–20 for a lounger and parasol; a free public stretch is also available. Good seafood restaurants in the town.
