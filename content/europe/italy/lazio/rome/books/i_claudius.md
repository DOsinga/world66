The Roman emperor Claudius narrates the story of his family — Augustus, Livia, Caligula — from the inside. Graves turned Suetonius and Tacitus into one of the most readable historical novels ever written. Walking around the Forum and the Palatine Hill after reading this is a different experience entirely.
