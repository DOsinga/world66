Moravia's 1947 novel follows Adriana, a working-class Roman woman navigating life as a model and prostitute in the years around the war. Grounded in the popular neighbourhoods — Prati, Nomentano — and sharply observed about class, body, and survival in a city that hasn't changed as much as it thinks it has.
