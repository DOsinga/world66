The symbol of Rome and its most visited monument. This amphitheatre was built shortly after 70 AD and could seat 50,000 spectators for gladiator combats, animal hunts, and even staged naval battles. The four-storey structure was used as a quarry through the Middle Ages until Pope Benedict XIV declared it a sacred site in the 18th century. Buy a combined ticket that covers the Colosseum, Roman Forum, and Palatine Hill -- and purchase it online or at the Palatine entrance to skip the enormous queue at the Colosseum itself. The holes visible throughout the structure mark where bronze fittings were once removed and melted down for weapons.
