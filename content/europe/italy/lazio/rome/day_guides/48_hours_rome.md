A food-first, experience-driven two-day guide that builds the itinerary around genuinely good coffee and eating rather than ticking boxes. Covers the Colosseum, Vatican, and Trastevere while naming specific cafes and restaurants for each meal. Best for first-timers who want to do the main sights and come home having eaten well.
