Eater's essential map of Rome restaurants, compiled and updated by contributor Katie Parla. One of the few major food publications that consistently keeps its Rome coverage current rather than recycling the same 1990s list. The 38 entries cover the spectrum from old-school neighbourhood trattorias to the newer wave of regional Italian cooking (Le Marche, Emilia-Romagna, Calabria) that has quietly made Rome more interesting over the last decade. Good for discovering that some of Rome's best pasta is actually not Roman at all.
