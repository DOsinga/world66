A focused guide to eating well in Rome without spending much — from the best pizza al taglio counters to market sandwiches and cheap-but-serious trattorias. Parla is direct about what's actually worth the money and what isn't, which matters in a city full of tourist-trap mediocrity. Covers bakeries, street food, market stalls, and a handful of sit-down spots where you can eat two proper courses for under €20. Essential for anyone who wants to eat like a Roman rather than like a tourist.
