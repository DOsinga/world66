A beautiful hill town at 390 meters above sea level, roughly 20 km from Rome. Frascati is famous for its white wine and its 16th-century villas, particularly Villa Aldobrandini with its gardens, grottoes, and fountains, and Villa Torlonia with its Theater of Fountains. The surrounding vineyards produce some of the best wine served in Rome's restaurants. Take a Cotral bus from the Anagnina stop of Metro Line A.
