The ancient port city of Rome, with remarkably well-preserved ruins that rival Pompeii but draw far fewer visitors. Wander through ancient streets, apartment blocks, taverns, and a beautifully intact theater. The nearby beach at Lido di Ostia is the closest to Rome -- packed on weekends but convenient via the Roma-Lido train from Piramide station (Metro Line B). The harbor town also has good seafood restaurants and a lively nightlife scene in summer.
