Traditional Roman trattoria in Trastevere, between Piazza Santa Maria in Trastevere and Piazza Sant'Egidio. Good food at moderate prices. In summer, tables outside put you in the middle of the neighbourhood's evening atmosphere. Closed Tuesdays.
