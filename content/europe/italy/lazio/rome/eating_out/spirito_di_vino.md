A family-run restaurant in Trastevere — husband and wife team, with their son as sommelier. The cooking is consistently excellent and the wine list is well chosen. Tables are few, the space is intimate, and reservations are essential. A little tucked away on a quiet street off Via Trastevere; worth the effort.
