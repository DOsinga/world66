title: "Ambasciata d'Abruzzo"

address: "Via Pietro Tacchini (Peter Turkey street!) in the Parioli district"

Excellent food. Appetizers (basically all (more than) you can eat.) Dessert, wine and liquores included in the fixed price meal that'll add 5 pounds. Too much to eat. Good quality. Specialties, wines, liqueurs, from the Abruzzi region. Fun. Rustic.
