This is one of the first 'restaurants' I tested in Rome (it is very well located, near Piazza Navona), and really fell in love with. To be honest, it's all but charming: that is the simplest typical 'trattoria' where you can go with a lot of friends and have a delicious and thin pizza with almost whatever you like on (just try the one with courgettes flowers!). Prices are really low, and the atmosphere is genuine and perfect if you feel like discovering the typical roman mood :)

Baffetto (little moustache, in Italian) is also the name of the owner, who is no longer that young but keeps on working 'till late at night. On the walls you'll see dozens of pictures of Italian VIP (actors and so on) who are used to coming to baffetto's.

Closed on Mondays.
