---
title: "Ristorante Abruzzi"
type: poi
address: "Via Vaccaro, 1 (at end of Piazza Santi Apostoli)."
---

A best buy. Located about mid-way between the Trevi Fountain and Trajan's Column. Small restaurant. Run by same family for all of the 35 years we've been going there. Quite inexpensive. Cannelloni Abruzzi the specialty, but everything well prepared. (Get there early for the specialty; they run out early!)
