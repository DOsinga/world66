Located in one of Rome's hottest nightlife districts -- Testaccio, San Remo Pizzeria is the best place in Rome for pizza. Patrons can choose from an assortment of toppings to pile onto a big thin-crust pie. San Remo is extremely popular, so get here early or you may have to wait in a long line -- especially if you want a seat outside. Hours: 6:30 p.m.-1:00 a.m.
