The 2008 Rome Chamber Music Festival
June 17–18–19 and June 24–25–26
Please
join us to help celebrate our fifth anniversary in the magnificent
Gonfalone Oratory. The program will feature the internationally
acclaimed clarinetist David Krakauer and his celebrated ensemble
Klezmer Madness!, selections from Vivaldi, Schubert, Händel and Schumann.
You will also hear students from Mercer’s McDuffie Center for Strings
and the Fiesole Conservatory perform Mendelssohn’s joyous octet. By
registering in advance, you will become a Friend of the Festival, which
entitles you to reserved seating, special rates at nearby hotels, and
other entertainment and transportation discounts.
The
2008 Rome Chamber Music Festival coincides with the 500th anniversary
of the Via Giulia and the 100th anniversary of the Santa Cecilia
Academy.
