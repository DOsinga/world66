The mother church of the Jesuit order, built between 1568 and 1575, and the model for Jesuit churches around the world. The nave frescoes by Baciccia depict the triumph of the Name of Christ with masterful foreshortening. Vignola designed the church; Giacomo della Porta completed the facade and dome.
