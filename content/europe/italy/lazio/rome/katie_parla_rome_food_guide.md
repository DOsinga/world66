The most authoritative and regularly updated guide to eating in Rome, by an American food journalist who has lived in the city for over twenty years and written several books on Roman cuisine. Organised by category — traditional trattorias, pizza (Roman and Neapolitan styles), Jewish Roman classics, street food, wine bars, coffee — with specific, opinionated picks and an honest word on what to skip. Updated quarterly. Best used alongside a map app: the recommendations span the whole city and reward going off the tourist trail.
