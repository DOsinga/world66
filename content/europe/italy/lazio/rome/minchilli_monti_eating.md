A room-by-room eating guide to Monti — Rome's bohemian village tucked between the Colosseum and Termini — by the food writer who helped put the neighbourhood on the map. Minchilli covers the local pizzeria where she sends her family, the wine bar with good outdoor tables, the tiny trattoria she considers one of her favourites in all of Rome, and the café that makes the prettiest cappuccino in the area. Monti has gentrified since this was written but the core recommendations have held up. A good base if you're staying in this part of the city.
