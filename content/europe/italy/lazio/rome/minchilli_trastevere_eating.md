A neighbourhood food walk through Trastevere by the Rome-based food writer who has spent decades mapping the city's eating culture. Minchilli's guides are street-level and personal — she picks the one trattoria she actually goes to, the bakery she trusts for pizza by the slice, the craft beer bar she brings friends to. This Trastevere edition covers lunch and dinner spots, a cheese shop that feels like stepping back forty years, and the gelaterie worth seeking out past the tourist strip. Pair with her Monti guide for a full day across two of Rome's most walkable neighbourhoods.
