A grand neoclassical piazza at the northern gate of Rome's historic center. The ancient Flaminian obelisk stands at its center, flanked by twin churches. The piazza has served as an open-air theater, stadium, and gathering place over the centuries. The Church of Santa Maria del Popolo on the north side contains masterworks by Caravaggio and is free to enter. The Pincio terrace on the east side offers one of the best sunset viewpoints in Rome.
