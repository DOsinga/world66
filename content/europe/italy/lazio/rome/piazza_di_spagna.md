Probably the most famous square in Rome, home to the Spanish Steps where Romans and tourists alike gather to see and be seen. At the bottom of the stairs sits the graceful Barcaccia fountain, and at the top the church of Trinita dei Monti offers fine views over the city. The surrounding streets are Rome's premier shopping district.
