Built on the foundations of Domitian's stadium, this elongated piazza retains the shape of the ancient racetrack. The centerpiece is Bernini's spectacular Fountain of the Four Rivers. In December a Christmas market fills the square. At the north end you can see the remains of the stadium foundations below street level. The church of Sant'Agnese in Agone on the west side is a fine example of baroque architecture. Stop at Tre Scalini for their legendary tartufo gelato.
