Dominated by the enormous Vittoriano monument -- the gleaming white marble tribute to King Victor Emanuele II that Romans derisively call the Wedding Cake or the Typewriter. A glass elevator takes you to the top for sweeping views over the Colosseum and the Forum. Inside you will find the Tomb of the Unknown Soldier and the Italian Reunification Museum. On the right side of the square, note the balcony of Palazzo Venezia from which Mussolini once delivered his speeches.
