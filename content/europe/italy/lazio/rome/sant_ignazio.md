The residents near this church refused to allow a large dome that would block their sunlight, so Andrea Pozzo painted a stunning trompe l'oeil fake dome on the ceiling instead. Stand on the golden disc in the nave floor and the illusion is completely convincing. Step away and the distortion becomes hilariously obvious. A clever and delightful piece of baroque stagecraft, near the Pantheon.
