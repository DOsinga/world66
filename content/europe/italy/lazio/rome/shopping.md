---
title: 'Shopping'
type: section
---

Rome's shopping scene ranges from luxury fashion houses to open-air flea markets. Via Condotti is the high-end designer strip, while Via del Corso caters to younger budgets. Via Frattina and Via Cola di Rienzo offer a good mix of quality and value. Most shops open around 10am, close for a long lunch, and reopen from 3:30 to 7:30pm. On Sundays, the Porta Portese flea market in Trastevere is the place to hunt for bargains and curiosities.
