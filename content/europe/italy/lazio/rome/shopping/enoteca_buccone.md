Historic wine shop in business since the 1800s, housed in the former coach house of a noble townhouse near Piazza del Popolo. Excellent selection of Italian wines including some rare vintages. Also sells cheese, charcuterie, olive oil, and spirits. A small restaurant at the back serves light lunches and dinners. Will ship wines internationally.
