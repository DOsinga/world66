The largest branch of Italy's upscale department store chain, located at Piazza Colonna. Trendy fashion for men, women, and children alongside cosmetics, perfumes, and household goods. Prices are more reasonable than the surrounding boutiques, and the rooftop terrace cafe offers views over the city.
