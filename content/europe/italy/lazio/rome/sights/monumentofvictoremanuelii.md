Walking for ten minutes from the Square where Constantine´s arch sits, we arrive to the Monumment of Victor Emanuel II.

 It is towering up under the Capitol, right in the heart of Rome where it’s impressing with its imposing dimension. In the middle of this monument there is rising up the altar of the Fatherland. Under the Statue of the Roma there is since 1921 the "Grave of the unknown soldier" or like we say in Italy "Il milite ignoto", which is supposed to remind us of the soldiers who died in the World War I.
