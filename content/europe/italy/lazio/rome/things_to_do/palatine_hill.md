---

According to legend, Romulus founded Rome on this hill on 21 April, 753 BC. Archaeological evidence confirms settlement from the early first millennium BC, including remains of the oldest city walls. Augustus built his palace here, and successive emperors followed, creating the imperial complex whose ruins now cover the hill. The ticket office here is the best place to buy the combined Colosseum/Forum/Palatine pass, as the queue is much shorter.
