First built around 442 AD, this church houses two treasures: the chains that held St. Peter during his imprisonment, displayed in a shrine before the altar, and Michelangelo's powerful statue of Moses, to the right of the altar. The church is slightly tucked away but close to the Colosseum and well worth the short detour.
