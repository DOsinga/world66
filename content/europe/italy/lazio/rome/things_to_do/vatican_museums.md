A treasure house that could take days to explore properly. The collection includes an extraordinary wing of classical sculpture featuring the Laocoon group and the Torso Belvedere, a pinacoteca with paintings from the 13th to the 19th century, and the crown jewels: Michelangelo's Sistine Chapel ceiling and Raphael's Stanze. Book tickets online well in advance to avoid the famously long queues. Allow at least half a day, and ideally a full one.
