The gelateria on Piazza Navona, renowned for its tartufo — a dense chocolate truffle ice cream, best ordered con panna (with whipped cream). Expensive for the location, but the tartufo is genuinely worth it. Order at the bar rather than sitting down to avoid the full tourist surcharge.
