---
title: 'When to Go'
type: section
---

Spring (April-May) and autumn (September-October) offer the best combination of pleasant weather and manageable crowds, though prices peak around Easter. Summer brings punishing heat above 30C but thinner tourist numbers and lower hotel rates -- plus outdoor concerts and cinema under the stars. Winter is mild by northern European standards, with occasional rain and temperatures between 3 and 14C, but the city is quieter and hotel prices drop significantly outside the Christmas period.
