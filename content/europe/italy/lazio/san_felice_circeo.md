Travel Guide

A truely magical and unspoiled area of beaches and natural beauty. This rock in the shape of a witches profile (giving it its name) stands alone on the Pontinian Plain, Also famous as the landing spot of Ulysses in the Iliad, this area is seeped in history, beauty and culture.

The Monte Circeo is a protected area of natural parkland.
