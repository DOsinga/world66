Hotel Villa Sciarra   Via di Montecompatri,30/D   Monte Porzio Catone, 40   Italy 

**Property type:** Hotel 

22 rooms. 2 floors. Built in 1960. Renovated in 2003. 

**Location:** Monte Porzio Catone is in the south of Rome, close to Frascati and in the heart of Castelli Romani Regional Park

**The hotel and restaurant Villa Sciarra **is located on a hill close to Monte Porzio Catone village. The garden is a green terrace overlooking the wonderful panorama of Rome and it is surrounded by a chesnut wood. The hotel is an ideal place to stay for people who love relax and tranquillity and is particularly suitable for families with children. Its 22 rooms, restyled in 2003, are all equipped with private bathroom, telephone and color tv. During the day a tv room, a bar service and a restaurant, with a menu based on typical local dishes and Castelli Romani wines, will make more pleasent your stay. Villa Sciarra is close to the Autostrada del Sole (5 minutes), to the main ring of Rome called Grande Raccordo Anulare (10 minutes), Frascati Railway Station (5 minutes), Underground Terminal Anagnina of Line “A” (15 minutes), Ciampino Airport (20 minutes) and Fiumicino Airport (40 minutes). In addition a shuttle service is available upon request. Internal parking is available for free. A meeting room is available for small congresses upon request.Major credit cards are welcome.

By car: if you come from the city centre take Via Tuscolana to Frascati (approx. 30 minutes), then go to Monte Porzio Catone (5 minutes). Reccomended alternative route: take the GRA ring (Grande Raccordo Anulare), then turn right at the exit Autostrada del Sole, direction Napoli, enter the Autostrada and turn right at the first exit Monte Porzio Catone. Now follow the signs to the Hotel.(15 minutes from GRA). How to reach Monte Porzio Catone from Rome airports From Fiumicino airport: By car: take the motorway Fiumicino – Roma, then turn right at the exit GRA (Grande Raccordo Anulare) toward Ciampino Airport, then turn right at the Autostrada del Sole direction Napoli, enter the Autostrada and turn right at the first exit Monte Porzio Catone.
