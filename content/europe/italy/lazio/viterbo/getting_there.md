---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

Viterbo is best reached by bus, because the local trains are slow. From Rome take subway line A to the Lepanto stop and transfer to the "Acotral" bus to Viterbo, which run 5 times per day. Daily buses also run to Orvieto and Civitavecchia. Arriving in Viterbo walk right along the city wall to the first gate "Porta Fiorentina" and descent along Via Matteotti.
