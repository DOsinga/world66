Alassio has the Buddello a little street of shops, the street goes behind the beach fromt set behind the hotels over looking the sea!

Theres a variety of shops mainly clothing but also kids toys, souveneirs and food snack stores! not forgetting the pizza and ice cream shops!

The shops open in the morning, untill lunch time when they close so the italians can have their much loved pasta! then they re-open at 4 untill late at night!
