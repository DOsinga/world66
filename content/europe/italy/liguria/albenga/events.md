---
title: "Events"
type: section
---

Festivals and major annual events are held throughout the year. A car rally called "Monti Savonesi" takes place in May. A Fiat 500 meeting in June draws enthusiasts from across Europe. In September a fair celebrates the local Pigato wine. A piano music festival in December brings musicians from across Italy. Throughout summer, open-air concerts run alongside local religious festivals and sagras celebrating the agricultural products of the plain.
