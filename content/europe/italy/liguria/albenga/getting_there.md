---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

Albenga is on the main Genoa–Ventimiglia railway line, with frequent trains from Genoa (about 1 hour), Turin, and Milan. The motorway exit (Albenga) on the A10 autostrada brings drivers into town within minutes. Riviera bus services connect the town to nearby coastal resorts. The nearest airports are Genoa Cristoforo Colombo (about 90 km east) and Nice Côte d'Azur (about 100 km west).
