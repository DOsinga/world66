### [Luni - Roman Ruins](/europe/italy/liguria/bocca_di_magra/day_trips/luni__roman_ruins)

During the time of the Romans, the city of Luni was a port because the sea once arrived further inland. At Luni, in the municipality of Ortonovo, there are archeological ruins, a museum and an amphitheater which was all covered in white marble. Hannibal marched his army through here around 200 BC. The Vikings mistook it for Rome.
