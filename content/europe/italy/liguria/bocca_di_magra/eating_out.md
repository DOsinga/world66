### [Capannina Ciccio](/europe/italy/liguria/bocca_di_magra/eating_out/capannina_ciccio)

Beautiful family run restaurant, right on the river in the picturesque village of Bocca di Magra. Great seafood, pasta and desserts. A must visit.

### [Ristorante La Ferrara](/europe/italy/liguria/bocca_di_magra/eating_out/ristorante_la_ferr)

The restaurant serves local fish dishes, and mushrooms and game when in season. A warm rustic environment with a terrace looking at the Apuan Alps and a private parking lot. Wine and olive oil from the Hills of Luni and the Cinque Terre are served.

You can taste their sliced meats and cheeses for €10. Close Monday except in August. All credit cards accepted.

### [Le Crepes di Marino Leonardo](/europe/italy/liguria/bocca_di_magra/eating_out/le_crepes_di_marin)

This snack bar is located in Via Fabbricotti in the park and opposite the departure dock for the boats to the beach of Punta Corvo or the boats that go to the other side of the river.

They serve all kinds of crepes, sandwiches, hamburgers, hot dogs and deliciously filled Italian tortillas. Open from 11am to 2 in the morning in summer. Open from 14:00 to 1 in the morning in the winter.

type: French
