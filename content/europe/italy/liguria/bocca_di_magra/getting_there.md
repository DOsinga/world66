---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

The nearest international airports are **Pisa** (about 75 km, an hour by car) and **Genoa** (around 110 km). By train, the station at **Sarzana**, on the main Genoa–Pisa line, is 10 minutes away by taxi or bus; a regional bus connects Sarzana with Ameglia and Bocca di Magra. By car, take the A12 motorway (Genoa–Livorno) and exit at Sarzana. In summer, small ferries from La Spezia and the Cinque Terre also call at the dock by the park.
