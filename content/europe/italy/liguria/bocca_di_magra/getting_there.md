---
title: "Getting There in Bocca di Magra"
type: section
---

There is a well-equipped airport for tourists at Luni (Ortonovo) otherwise there are international airports at Genoa and Pisa. The train stops at Sarzana and the autostrada exit for Bocca di Magra is Sarzana.

There are also many ferryboat services from many different ports that go to ports near Bocca di Magra. If you are coming by your own boat there is a well-equipped port with all services in Bocca di Magra.
