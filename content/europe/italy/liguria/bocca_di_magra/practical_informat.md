There is a port at Bocca di Magra and a shipyard. There are real estate agents, accommodation, restaurants, beaches, shops and a Roman ruin.

The Yache Club provides docking with water and electricity, accomodation, refreshments, technical assistance with rubber boat and Jet Ski rentals.

### [Ar-Nav Moorings and Shipyard](/europe/italy/liguria/bocca_di_magra/practical_informat/arnav_moorings_an)

Ar-Nav supplies all kinds of services to sail boats and motor boats. They will help you maneuver inside to the dock and manage and surveil your boat. There are all dock services like electricity, water, TV hook-up, a restaurant, laundry service (for your clothes and they'll wash your boat too!)
