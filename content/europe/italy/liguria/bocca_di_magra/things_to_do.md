---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Most visitors come to Bocca di Magra to use the boats — to the Cinque Terre, Portovenere, Punta Corvo, Palmaria, or simply along the coast — and to walk in the **Montemarcello-Magra Natural Park**, which covers the wooded headland on the opposite side of the river. A network of easy trails leads up through pine and holm-oak to the hill village of Montemarcello, with views over the gulf and the Apuan Alps. In the village itself, the **Monastero Santa Croce**, on top of the hill, has a pretty chapel and one of the finest views in the gulf. On the water, there are dive schools, small-boat hire, and the usual summer line-up of kayaks, paddle-boards and sailing lessons.
