From Bocca di Magra you can take ferryboat rides to Punta Corvo (The Black Bird's Point) if you can't climb down the steep 700 meters to the otherwise inaccessable black beach, or go for easy or difficult walks in the National Park of Monte Marcello. Just take in the sun or visit Roman ruins at Luni or Ameglia.

### [Monte Marcello National Park](/europe/italy/liguria/bocca_di_magra/things_to_do/monte_marcello_nat)

You can view this beautiful Maritime National Park by car, bicycle or on foot taking the easy paths or the more difficult paths according to your physical ability. Visit the town on the top of the hill for a voyage back into time.

type: Hiking



url: [www.parks.it](http://www.parks.it/parco.montemarcello.magra/Eiti.html)

### [Punta Corvo Beach](/europe/italy/liguria/bocca_di_magra/things_to_do/punta_corvo_beach)

The ferry boat can be boarded at Fiumaretto boarding dock in Bocca di Magra and it takes fifteen beautiful minutes to reach the black beach at Punta Corvo (The Black Bird's Point). Then all passengers are disembarked by way of a ladder that discends from the front of the boat to the beach. If you have an inflated boat or canoe - just throw it into the water toward the beach and then go down the ladder with your towel and water bottle to the beach to recuperate it. Wear rubber sandals. There is a lifeguard there to assist you while at the beach and while getting on and off the [more..](/europe/italy/liguria/bocca_di_magra/things_to_do/punta_corvo_beach)

type: Going to the Beach



url: www.comune.ameglia.sp.it

### [Ferryboat Rides](/europe/italy/liguria/bocca_di_magra/things_to_do/ferryboat_rides)

The 5 Terre - Gulf of the Poets - Maritime Tourist Service has fabulous trips to the 5 Terre along the coast, Portofino, Genoa, and Tuscan Islands like Capraia or the Island of Elba. To see Embarcadero departure ports check out the Italian web site page "LUOGHI DI IMBARCO".

tel: 0187 73 29 87

url: [www.navigazionegolfodeipoeti.it](http://www.navigazionegolfodeipoeti.it/)

address: Via Don Minzoni 13, 19121 La Spezia

email: navinfo@navigazionegolfodeipoeti.it

### [Navigation](/europe/italy/liguria/bocca_di_magra/things_to_do/navigation)

Navigazione Marittima has its Embarcadero departure dock at the public park by Vittoriani Street. Besides the excursions to the PUNTA CORVO beach every half hour from 9 to 19:00, it makes trips to the Cinque Terre for organized groups. In July and August it makes night time excursions to Porto Venere every Saturday at 21.30 and returns AT 23:30. There is a big parking lot 50 meters away from the Embarcadero.

type: Sailing



tel: 338 8269879

url: www.navigazionefocemagra.it

address: Via Fabbricotti 14, 19038 Bocca di Magra

email: info@navigazionefocemagra.it

### [Osso di Seppia Diving Center](/europe/italy/liguria/bocca_di_magra/things_to_do/osso_di_seppia_div)

Discover wrecked ships under the water, swim through underwater gardens, see the coral and starfish and all the fish and enjoy the world of water. Explore grottos. Buy from their shop or take lessons.
