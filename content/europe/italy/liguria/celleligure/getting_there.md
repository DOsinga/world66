---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

**By train:** Celle Ligure sits on the Genova–Ventimiglia line. The nearest station is Albisola, a couple of kilometres east; some services stop at Celle Ligure itself. Regular trains run from Genoa (about 30 minutes) and Savona (10 minutes).

**By car:** Take the A10 motorway (Genova–Ventimiglia) and exit at Celle Ligure. The Via Aurelia (SS1) runs through the town along the coast.

**By air:** The nearest airport is Genoa Cristoforo Colombo (GOA), about 35km east. From Genoa airport, take a taxi or shuttle to Genoa Brignole or Principe station and catch a Ventimiglia-bound train.
