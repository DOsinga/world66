Ceriale is now quite famous not all around Liguria but also around Italy and Europe as one of the biggest swimming resort: "Le Caravelle" which is a real paradise for all lovers of aquafun, sea-waves swimming pool and many other attractions. During summertime many events are held here to give to all the people the best amusements.
