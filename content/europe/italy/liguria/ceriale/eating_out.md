title: "Eating Out"
type: section
---

Ceriale has some good restaurants and pizza's all'around where you can eat well and in many cases not expensive.

 It is quite difficult to mention some of them so - speaking in many cases for direct experience - and just to talk about pizza you have to go to the old "Amsterdam" just near the seafront.  Fish and pizza are very good and not so expensive at "Franco"'s between [Albenga](/europe/italy/liguria/albenga) and Ceriale main road.

 If you do not mind the money and you want make yourself a present go to "Il Faro", just outside Ceriale. It is a bit expensive but it is worth. To conclude with a large choice of man-made ice creams every "gelateria" is pretty good but the "La Dolce Arte", on the sea-front walk is a must.  In the evening it is possible to hear good music in the "Jazz Pub" but in summertime many occasions of open air events are held here with all the bars open till late time to serve you.

Enrico Pelos, Ceriale editor, Liguria (general) editor, Osinga.com, Genoa, Italy.
