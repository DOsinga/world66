title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Corniglia is more about the experience of the place than individual attractions. The Church of Saint Peter (San Pietro), built in 1334 in the Genoese Gothic style, is the architectural highlight — its rose window is particularly fine. Beyond that, the pleasures are the panoramic terraces, the lanes threading through the old village, and the vineyard paths that connect the town to its neighbours.
