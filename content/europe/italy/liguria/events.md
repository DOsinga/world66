Festivals and major annual events of many types are held all around Liguria.


 The most famous is the Italian National Song Contest. It is held in [Sanremo](/europe/italy/liguria/italianriviera/sanremo) generally in Febrary, and it famous worldwide with the presence of very important Italian and foreigners singers and actors. This year is the 50th edition. A very important boat show (it is said to be the most important in the world) is held periodically in October in [Genoa](/europe/italy/liguria/genoa). Theatre major events are held in [Genoa](/europe/italy/liguria/genoa) all the year round and in many Italian Riviera towns and villages during summertime.


 Two important gastronomy festivals are held at [Lavagna](/europe/italy/liguria/italianriviera/tigullio/lavagna) with "The torta dei Fieschi", a colossal cake, with ancient costumes parade and at [Camogli](/europe/italy/liguria/italianriviera/paradisegulf/camogli) with "The sagra del pesce" Fried fish made with a giant specially made frying pan and where all persons present are offered a taste.


 One of the major events of the year 2000 is "El siglo de los Genovesis" (The Century of the Genoises). A stunning exibition of paintings and precious objects held at "Palazzo Ducale" (here in the picture) of [Genoa](/europe/italy/liguria/genoa). It is all about the century when Genoa was powerful along the known seas and around Europe and is due to last until May.


 One great boat exibition will involve [Genoa](/europe/italy/liguria/genoa) and many coastal villages for the 2000 Big Boat Ship Race. Every 4 years the most important appointment for all flower lovers is in [Genoa](/europe/italy/liguria/genoa) for the "Euroflora" Exibition.


 Detailed indications about major local events are given in the single respective town sections.
