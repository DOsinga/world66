---
title: "Eating Out"
type: section
---

Finale Ligure shares Liguria's famously light, almost vegetarian cuisine, built on olive oil, fresh herbs, and simple ingredients. Pesto is the star, but look out for trofie (hand-rolled pasta twists), pansoti stuffed with wild herbs, and focaccia di Recco — a thin, crispy bread filled with melting stracchino cheese. The coast means excellent blue fish: sardines, anchovies, and sea bass, alongside salt cod and stockfish. Rabbit, wild boar, and snails appear on menus too, often with pine nuts, walnuts, and mushrooms.
