**Get Ready to Experience Gastronomic Nirvana**

Your tastes buds will experience nirvana the Gram Gotto, a gem among** Genoa restaurants**. All activities in Genoa revolves around food, making it a food lover&rsquo;s paradise. Not only is Genoa an important sea port, but also one of the largest cities in Italy. It holds the credit of being home to Christopher Columbus, and the homes here used to be inhabited by the rich and the famous. Read on to find out about the food culture and a restaurant that will keep you coming back for more.
**
Gran Gotto**

Trust this restaurant to be featured on each and every Genoa food guide that you read. Gran Gotta is the oldest and the most popular dining place in Genoa. It is considered to be the pride of the city and is located close to the Brignole station. Another landmark close to Gran Gotta is the Fiera del Mare. The interiors are done up with class and an Italian charm.

**Food**

A vacation is incomplete without sampling the local flavors. Everything at Gran Gotta is about the food. Step in and get treated to an unforgettable Italian feast. Olive oil is the basis of all dishes here, and the pesto sauce is perfectly flavored with pine nuts and cheese. The focacce and the torte salate are pies made out of veggies and different kinds of cheese. The pansotti is a form of ravioli stuffed with meat and served with a unique walnut sauce. Seafood is what makes this restaurant stand out. The cappon margo is an elaborate main course made with fish seasoned with herbs and sauces, and is a local specialty.
