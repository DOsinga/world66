A step up from Trattoria da Maria in decor — slightly less rustic — but the same commitment to traditional Genoese cooking at honest prices. The pansotti al sugo di noci is excellent. Closed Sunday and Monday; open for lunch noon–2pm and dinner 7:30–10pm.
