---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

Genoa's Cristoforo Colombo airport handles flights from major European cities; it's 6km from the centre and connected by bus and taxi. By train, Genoa is well linked to Milan (1.5 hours), Turin (1.5 hours), La Spezia (1 hour), and Pisa. The A10 and A7 motorways feed in from the French Riviera and Milan respectively. Ferries run to Sardinia, Sicily, Tunisia, and Spain from the Porto di Genova.
