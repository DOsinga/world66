A Genoese institution in the eastern quarters. The Cuban-Italian ownership shows in the exceptional rum selection and the hand-rolled cigars behind the bar. Arrive for aperitivo and stay for cocktails — the retro wooden interior is genuinely warm, and it draws a loyal local crowd rather than a tourist one.
