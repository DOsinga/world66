The insect collections are particularly remarkable and they include hymenoptera, coleoptera, hemiptera, lepidoptera, orthoptera, odonata. Among the other invertebrates, there are remarkable collections of molluscs, arachnids and sponges. Among the vertebrates there many bird specimens (birds of paradise, parrots, kingfishers, cassowaries). Among the mammals: marsupials, bats, exotic rodents and African antelopes. The reptilian collections consist of 7,000 specimens belonging to 1,400 diffferent species; the fish collection consists of 2,000 species.
