The red palace facing Palazzo Bianco holds an equally impressive collection: Veronese, Guercino, Dürer, and some exceptional portraits by Van Dyck of the Brignole-Sale family who originally owned the building. The 17th-century frescoed ceilings alone are worth the visit. Open Tuesday through Saturday.
