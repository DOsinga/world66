---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

**By train**: Arenzano has its own station on the Genova-Ventimiglia line, making it an easy day trip from Genoa.

**By car**: Take the A10 motorway (Genova-Ventimiglia) and exit at Arenzano, or follow the old coastal road SS1 (Via Aurelia).

**By air**: The nearest airport is Genoa Cristoforo Colombo (GOA), about 6 km west of central Genoa and roughly 20 minutes from Arenzano.
