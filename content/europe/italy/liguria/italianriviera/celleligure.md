Celle Ligure is a small seaside town on the Italian Riviera, tucked between Cape Celle and Cape Olmo. The bay has a modest but sandy beach that draws summer visitors, while the steep hillsides behind are thick with umbrella pines. It is quieter and less touristy than neighbouring Savona or Varazze — a good base for a beach holiday with easy access to the rest of the Ligurian coast by train.
