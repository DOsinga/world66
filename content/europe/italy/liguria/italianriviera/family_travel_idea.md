An Italian Experience the Family Will Enjoy

The Italian Riviera, otherwise known as Liguria, possesses an amazing variety of beaches and resort towns, so here are a few **Italian Riviera family travel ideas**.
The Riviera of the Setting Sun is a must-visit location which runs
north from Genoa to the French Border. Most of the towns here are
connected via a widespread rail network which makes the towns easily
accessible for short day trips and sightseeing.
The Italian Riviera Family Travel Accommodations

There are many hotels to choose from in the Italian Riviera. The
Hotel Villa Steno offers you amazing views of the region, great service,
a vibrant atmosphere, and it is a great experience either for the lone
traveler, young couple or the entire family.

If a hotel is not where you want to spend your nights, then try out
one of the 414 bed-and-breakfast resorts. The La Torretta is one of the
top-rated bed and breakfasts in the region. Located in Manarola, it
provides you with excellent service and a quality breakfast. Another
B&B that is sure to make your holiday nights comfortable and
enjoyable is the Aria di Mare. With spacious and airy rooms, this
accommodation offers spectacular views from the top of Manarola.
Italian Riviera Family Travel and Entertainment

With so much on offer in the region, the one thing that will be
difficult is finding time to do everything. Angelo’s Boat Tour is a must
for any family. With immaculate water for snorkeling and diving, you
are sure to enjoy a day in the fresh air.

For those adventurous families, there is the Trail 2, Riomaggiore, an
excellent hike during the off-season which boasts amazing views of the
region. The Isola Palmaria Nature Reserve is the perfect place for the
family to relax and enjoy the local wildlife that surrounds the Riviera.
Located on an island just off the region’s shore, this reserve offers
nature lovers a perfect vacation.
