After Sori and Recco appears the sea dazzled climb to Ruta even through the primary importance is still due to nature, to the greenery of the plants, the colours of the flower which never will give way to the harsh grey of the cliffs. But not always has rock a heart of stone; there already, in the vicinity of Mulinetti, it spreads in the sun freshened by the waves of the sea. An invitation not to be declined in the many fine days of Paradise Gulf; first a nice bathe and then the dozy rlaxation of a nap in the sun.
