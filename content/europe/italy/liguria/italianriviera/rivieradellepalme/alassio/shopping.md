---
title: Shopping
type: section
---

The main shopping artery is Il Budello, a narrow pedestrian lane that runs behind the beachfront hotels parallel to the promenade. It is lined with boutiques, souvenir shops, food stores, and ice cream parlours that stay open late into summer evenings. Most shops follow Italian hours — open in the morning, closed for a long lunch, then reopening from mid-afternoon until about 10pm.
