title: "Rialto"
type: location
latitude: 44.225818

---

An agricultural center inland of Finale that
 produces chiefly oil and wine, it is a fairly
 scattered settlement. Rialto used to belong to
 the marquisate of the Finalese ruled by the Del
 Carretto. Under Spanish rule from 1598, it was
 acquired by the republic of Genoa in 1713. The
 parish church of San Pietro preserves traces of
 its original Romanesque structure and houses a
 late Gothic statue. A museum of rural culture
 has now been set up in it.
