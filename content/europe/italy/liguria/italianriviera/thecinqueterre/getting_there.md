title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

**By train (recommended)**: "Ferrovie dello Stato"/ Genova-Livorno railway (Cinque Terre stations).

 **By car**: "A1 2" motorway (Genova-Livorno): exit Sestri Levante, exit Levanto; S. S. 1 (Aurelia)  

 **Airport**: *Genoa (GOA) *(Cristoforo Colombo Sestri) (Web site: http://www.airport.genova.it) 6km (4 miles) west of the city (travel time – 20 minutes) has duty-free facilities. Buses are available to the city.

 Genoa-Cinque Terre 70-90Km
