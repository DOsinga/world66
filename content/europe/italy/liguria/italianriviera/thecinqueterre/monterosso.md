---
title: "Monterosso"
type: location
---

This is an ancient sea village. It is situated on the eastern side of the Ligurian Riviera, between the tip of the Punta Mesco and the island of Tinetto. It is the best equiped village for tourists in the whole of the "Cinque Terre". The hotels offer warm and friendly hospitality and the restaurants pro- vide dishes, typical of the ligurian coast. Also the famous wine of the "Cinque Terre" praise of wich was sung by Petrarca, is also available.
