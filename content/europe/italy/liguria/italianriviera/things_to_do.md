---
title: "Things to do in Italian Riviera"
type: section
---

In Italy’s haven for tourists, visitors will be able to find a good
number of **Italian Riviera Things to Do**. From its beaches to
hiking in the lush outdoors, to eating good food to fishing, the Italian
Riviera is a perfect location to spend a good number of days during
your vacation. Here is a list of things to do when visiting the Italian
Riviera.

**Get Adventurous**

The Italian Riviera offers many outdoor activities to its visitors.
You can enjoy adrenaline-pumped sports like rafting, canoeing and rock
climbing. Or, you can pack a day’s worth of food and water, and head out
into the great outdoors for a good trek. Other outdoor activities
include playing golf at one of the many golf clubs in the area, horse
riding and paragliding.

**Go Shopping**

The Italian Riviera houses many kinds of stores that will cater to
the needs of all. Plus, you should not miss out on purchasing famous
locally made products such as olive oil, olives, cheese, wines and
truffles. The town has many outdoor markets, all of which sell local
produce.

**Visit Architectural Delights**

When visiting the Italian Riviera, you should definitely not miss the
area’s architectural marvels. Visit the Cattedrale di San Lorenzo
Campanile, a cathedral known for its ornate interiors, and Palazzo
Ducale, a building that has existed since the Renaissance and is now
converted into an art museum. Other places of interest include the
Church of San Gregorio and the Basilica of Santa Maria di Castello.

**Sample Italian Fare**

Get a taste of the Italian way of life and sample the specialties of
the area. From seafood like mussels, tuna, lobster and crab cooked in
the local manner, to Buridda, a dish made from eel cooked with mushrooms
and wild herbs, food from the Italian Riviera will have your taste buds
tingling for more. Another must-try when in the area includes the
Ligurian pasta (trofie).

**Take a Boat Tour**

Go on a boat tour with the help of Agnelo’s Boat Tour Company, which
will show you the pristine blue waters and wonderful landscapes around
as it provides you with some good company from the owner of the company.
You will get to dive into the water if you like and the tour also
includes a lunch stop over.
