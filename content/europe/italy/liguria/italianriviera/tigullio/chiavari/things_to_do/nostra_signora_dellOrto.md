title: "Nostra Signora dell'Orto"
type: poi
category: Sight
latitude: 44.3160
longitude: 9.3222
---

Built between 1613 and 1633, this church stands in its own square and is preceded by a classicized pronaos — a colonnaded porch that gives it the air of a small temple. One of the most architecturally distinctive religious buildings in Chiavari.
