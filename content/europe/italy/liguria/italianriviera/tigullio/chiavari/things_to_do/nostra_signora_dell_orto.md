title: Nostra Signora dell'Orto
type: poi

Built between 1613 and 1633 in the square of the same name, this church is preceded by a classicised pronaos — a columned porch more typical of a Greek temple than a Ligurian church. The interior contains one of the principal Marian shrines of the region.
