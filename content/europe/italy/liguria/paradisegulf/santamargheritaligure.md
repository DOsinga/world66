The best resource for sights, hotels, restaurants, bars, what to do and see

Santa Margherita Ligure lies close to secluded villages and beautiful beaches but away from prowling tourist throngs. Blessed with the character and affordability of a small town, it has the accessibility of a large one, with enough "pensioni" that travelers can drop their backpacks here and spend some days axploring the more exclusive nooks.
