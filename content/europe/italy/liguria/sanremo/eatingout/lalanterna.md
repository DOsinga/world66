La Lanterna is a popular restaurant with locals, and has been serving the people of San Remo since 1917. The menu is excellent and includes delights such as fish soup (brodetto di pesce con crostini), Ligurian fish fry, scaloppine in Marsala sauce and sea bass and red snapper, baked with artichokes, olives, and white-wine sauce.
