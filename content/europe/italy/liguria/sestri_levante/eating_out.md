Sestri Levante's restaurants reflect the Ligurian coast's strengths: fresh fish, good olive oil, and local wines.

**Cantina del Polpo** — On Piazza Cavour in the old town, opened 1989. Specialises in octopus, squid, and cuttlefish with local wines. No credit cards. Reserve for dinner. The seasonal menu changes throughout the year.
