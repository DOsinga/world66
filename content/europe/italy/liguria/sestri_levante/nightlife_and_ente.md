---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment in Sestri Levante"
type: section
---

The **Sestri Levante nightlife** mainly consists of relaxing and enjoying the night at one of the restaurants or bars in the area. Sestri Levante is a picturesque town that is surrounded by mountains and seas. This old village is located on the border of the Gulf of Tigullio and has become one of the best undiscovered tourist destinations of Italy. It lies along the Mediterranean Sea, and it is set on a promontory.

#### Local Dining

There are several restaurants in Sestri Levante that serves great local cuisine and most of the restaurants take advantage of the beautiful scenery of the city. Polpo Mario is a favorite restaurant among the locals. This restaurant serves great Italian cuisine, as well as Italian wines. Polpo Mario has an outdoor seating area, which is a good place to watch the hustle and bustle of the city. They also have a wide selection of seafood that the city is known for.

Another popular restaurant is the Fiammenghilla Fieschi, which is situated in a 17th century villa. The villa is located near Sestri Levante that specializes in pizzas. The Fiammenghilla Fieschi has over 90 different types of pizzas to choose from. However, they also serve seafood dishes for those wanting to a little bit more than pizzas.

Il Polpino is another must-try for visitors of Sestri Levante. This local bar and restaurant features a wide selection of cocktails and aperitifs. Try one of their cocktails together with some of their antipasto.

#### Bars and Clubs

For travelers wanting a little bit of fun and entertainment, there are also several bars and clubs in Sestri Levante that will suit their needs. VT-bar is considered the best bar of the city.

#### Discotheques

Travelers looking for a night of dancing can choose from quite a few discoteca or discotheque in the area. The Circolo &ldquo;Arci Virgola&rdquo; is a popular place in the city. Mep Club is another local discotheque that features two main areas. One area is commonly used as a dance floor, while the other room is a lounge type area. The club is open every day featuring different types of music from live entertainment by a DJ or Latin music.

#### Theater

One can also catch a play or two at some of the theaters or cinemas in Sestri Levante. A popular theater of the city is the Cinema Teatro Ariston.

#### Festivals at Night

One of the best times to visit Sestri Levante is during one of their festivals. Festival Estivo Di Musica Da Camera is a great festival to experience. During this time, classical concerts are being hosts by different locations in the city from piazzas to churches. Concerti D&rsquo;Autunno or Autumn Concert is another musical festival in Sistre Levante that is held every week for the month of November and December.
