The **Sestri Levante Things to Do **list will set a calm and relaxed tone to your vacation. Sestri Levante is a seaside getaway in Liguria, Italy. This beach side village is a famous tourist destination and is known for its views and historical monuments. Read on to find out about the things to do in Sestri Levante.

**Visit the Churches**

This picturesque village in Italy is well known for its churches. The region comprises of more than 10 churches, each with an unique architecture. The Church of St. Sabina, St. Stefano, St. Maria are few of the famous churches in this region. But the church of St. Nicolo, is of utmost importance to the locals and is a must see sight in Sestri Levante. The church was built in the year 1151 and has been subjected to a series of renovation projects. The church boasts a Roman Gothic architecture and is a marvel to look at. It is located close the castles near the gulf.

**Marconi Towers**

Hike to the tallest point in Sestri Levante. This tower was initially used by a scientist named Guglielmo Maconi, during the 1930's. He used the tower in his transmission experiments. Today, it is one of the main attractions in Sestri Levante. The tower offers a breathtaking view of the village and the surrounding areas. The panoramic view extends to the Alps on a clear day. This tower can be seen from any point in Sestri Levante.

**Silent Bay**

The Silent Bay is a highly romantic and relaxing sea side in Sestri Levante. This is a great way to enjoy the natural beauty of this region. The water is crystal clear, which makes it the perfect setting for a swim. The houses in pastel shade that surround this area add a very classic touch to the landscape. During the day it is calm, and after sundown, the place transforms into a party paradise.

**Gualino Castle**

The Gualino Castle is the most beautiful structure in Sestri Levante. It was built in the 12th century. This castles later protected the masses from pirates. People in the village took refuge in this castle. Follow the seaside promenade to find this castle. The Gualino castle sits on a hill top, close to the sea side. It is considered to be the crown of the hill, and the walking trail to the castle adds a lot of excitement to the trip.

**Promenade**

The promenade and the surrounding attractions are what attracts the tourists to Sestri Levante. Enjoy the views of the beautiful Gulf of Rapallo at this promenade. The atmosphere is extremely romantic, with a several boats docked at the harbor. The view during sunset is worth a million bucks. This is a great way to enjoy the fresh air and the outdoors in Sestri Levante.
