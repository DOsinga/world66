---
title: "Eating Out"
type: section
---

Ligurian cooking is light, fragrant, and almost vegetarian, built around olive oil, fresh herbs, and vegetables. Pesto is the star, but look for **trofie al pesto**, **pansoti** (pasta filled with wild greens and ricotta), and **focaccia di Recco** stuffed with soft stracchino cheese. Fish means sardines, anchovies, and sea bass rather than shellfish. The classic **cappon magro** layers fish and vegetables into an elaborate cold salad that is worth seeking out.
