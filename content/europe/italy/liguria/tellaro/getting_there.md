You can get to Tellaro by bus, car or boat. There is a parking lot before you get into the town itself because there is no room for cars in this town. The town is rather made of of meter-wide passageways for pedestrians only ( and a cat or two!)

### [Bus](/europe/italy/liguria/tellaro/getting_there/bus)

Take the ATC bus from Lerici to Tellaro. Buses leave from Lerici (Comune) City Hall from 6:20 to 19:50. Or from Lerici, Piazza Garibaldi, from 6:21 to 19:50.

The bus leaves Tellaro for Lerici starting at 6:35 and the last one is at 20:05.
