Tellaro is quite enjoyable to explore because of its unusual structure with small passages between the houses, passages carved into the rocks above the sea, and swimming areas carved from the rocks or the boat launch.

The church seems to be a true monument to God in thanks for the sea, the sky and the land.

### [Slides of Tellaro](/europe/italy/liguria/tellaro/sights/slides_of_tellaro)

Wind your way through the main square of Tellaro (pedestrians only) and
then set off for the narrow pathways among the houses and along the
seaside.
