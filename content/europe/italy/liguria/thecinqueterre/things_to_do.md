Be sure to take the ferryboat rides which give you a great seaside view of all the five towns and you can hop on and off. If you like walking, there are the trails that line the seafront and go up and down the cliffs and some young people even jump off the rocks into the sea.

### Ferry Boat

Don't miss out on the many opportunities for a boat ride or organize a group excursion. You can even hop on and off and visit the towns of port.
