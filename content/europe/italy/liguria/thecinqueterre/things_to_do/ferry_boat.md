Don't miss out on the many opportunities for a boat ride or organize a group excursion. You can even hop on and off and visit the towns of port on the route connecting Monterosso, Vernazza, Manarola and Riomaggiore with five daily runs. This way you can see these towns without hiking or using the train or the roads. Don't miss the last boat!

Get a recent timetable and map to see the other tours. For example: Moneglia, Deiva, Bonassola or Levanto to the 5 Terre-Portovenere-Portofino. Or Marina di Pisa/Viareggio/Forte dei Marmi/Marina di Carrara /Marina di Massa to Portovenere-5 Terre-Portofino-Isola di Capraia or the Island of Elba. Plus many more.

The above tour of Massa di Carrara to Portovenere is exceptional because you get to see the coast from Bocca di Magra along to the bay before Lerici which is an incredible sight that you can't really see very well from the road. Then you can stop off at Portovenere (wear your swimsuit under your clothes because you can take a dip in the beautiful bay hidden behind the fort on the point where a lot of young people go) and have a walk around town. Then the boat circles around the facing islands of Palmaria, Tino and Tinetto and you can see some oyster cultivations in the sea.
