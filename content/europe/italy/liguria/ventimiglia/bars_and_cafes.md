**Enjoy an Evening at the Social Caf&eacute;**

If you&rsquo;re looking for somewhere special which stands out from the other **Ventimiglia bars and cafes**, you should consider visiting the Social Caf&eacute; located on Via Martiri. Not only is it a great place to have a drink, but it is also a place where you can hear some excellent live music.

#### **The Interior **

Social Caf&eacute; has a simple yet pleasant interior. The walls are painted orange, while the floor is covered with wood laminate. This excellent combination of colors creates a feeling of coziness and comfort. In contrast, the tables and chairs are made of dark brown wood and silver metal. They fit the whole design scheme of the caf&eacute;. All of the walls are decorated with various paintings and old musical instruments, which complement the atmosphere. Various live events are held here. Although the performances are primarily by jazz duos and trios, this does not exclude bands performing from time to time as well. Outdoor seating is available when the weather is sunny.

#### **The Menu **

Social Caf&eacute; has a simple but appealing menu. You can order a wide variety of drinks or try espresso. If you look around, you will probably notice most of the customers drinking beer. You can also order some fast food like a delicious hamburger with fries, a hot dog or a plate full of little sandwiches. This caf&eacute; is definitely worth trying when visiting Ventimiglia.
