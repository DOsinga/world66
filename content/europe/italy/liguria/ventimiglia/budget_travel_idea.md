---
title: "Budget Travel Ideas in Ventimiglia"
type: section
---

This small town is all about
great bargains and more bargains; that is why you won’t run out of great **Ventimiglia budget travel** ideas. You
don’t need to spend hundreds of dollars to see the architectural and historical
wonders of this town. Check out these ideas for cheapest and greatest
Ventimiglia exploration ever.

**Transport**

There are two railways
connecting Ventimiglia to other cities. If you have your own transportation you can take the Via Aurelia provincial
road to reach Ventimiglia. Once you are in Ventimiglia the cheapest and the
best way to go around is by riding a motorcycle. You can either rent a
motorcycle for a day or if you are brave enough you can hitch a ride with the
Italian motorcyclist going around town.

**Exploring
Around**

You do not need to spend
money in order to enjoy Ventimiglia. The town is packed with beautiful natural
views and architectural treasures. One of the most popular tourist destinations
in this town is the Giardini Botanici Hanbury (Hanbury
Botanical Gardens). This is the biggest botanical garden in Italy and the
finest in Europe. Another place you can
visit that won’t cost you a fortune is the Balzi Rossi caves that house
forensic evidence from the Quaternary period. Ventimiglia is virtually filled
with places to visit where you will not have to spend too much money.

**Great
Deals and Bargains**

If you want to score great
deals and bargains around town; make sure to do it on Friday. It is because
Friday is the day of the biggest street market in Ventimiglia. Hundred of tents
and stalls are built along the waterfront of the town and different
merchandises were showcased. You can find bags, shoes, clothes, foods,
jewelries and more at low prices. You can also try your skills at bargaining;
you are sure to get some good stuff. There are also a number of wine stores and
cigar stores that sell their products 15 to 20 percent less than the price in the US.

**Cheap
Accommodations**

Being a tourist destination,
there are several small hotels on the beachfront that offer good accommodations
at affordable rates. Ventimiglia is a place that has accommodations for every
traveler’s budget.

**Food**

If you want to save some
money, eat at one of the several restaurants and eateries on the road. The food
is good and the price is very affordable.
