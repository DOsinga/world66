**Ventimiglia: the Perfect Summer Destination
**

These **Ventimiglia practical information** tips will help you kick start your vacation plans. Ventimiglia is a small town located in the north of Italy. The River Roia runs right through the town, virtually dividing it into two. Being a town that is located on the coast, Ventimiglia is a popular tourist destination. Several tourists from all over France and Italy come to Ventimiglia to enjoy the summer. Here are a few tips that might help you plan that perfect vacation in Ventimiglia.

**Passport and Visa**

Italy is one of the countries under the Schengen Agreement. If you are from any of the countries that are part of this agreement, you do not need to have a visa to travel to Ventimiglia. However, you will need to produce a valid photo identity to prove which country you are from. If you are a citizen of the United States, you will need to have a valid passport, but you will not need to have a visa to visit Ventimiglia. You will be able to get a 90-days visa on arrival in Italy.

**Currency**

The Euro (&euro;) is the currency in use in Ventimiglia. If you are from the US or the UK, you will probably need to change your local currency into Euros before coming to Ventimiglia. You can also change your currency into Euros at any of the banks or money changers in Ventimiglia. ATMs are also available, and you can use credit cards for payments.

**Transportation**

Ventimiglia is a small town, and hence, there are not many travel options available. However, there are 2 railway stations in Ventimiglia. There are trains that go to France and to Cuneo.

**Climate**

Ventimiglia has a nice continental type of climate. Winter can be cold, with temperatures dropping down to -2 degrees Celsius. Summer is the best time to visit Ventimiglia, as the temperature is around 22 degrees Celsius to 24 degrees Celsius. During the month of August, the temperatures can rise up to 38 or even 42 degrees Celsius.

**Other Information**

Ventimiglia is located in the region of Liguria in the Imperia Province. The town has a total area of 54.1 sq km and has an elevation of 30 feet. Ventimiglia has a population of about 26,000. Ventimiglia falls in the CET (UTC+1) time zone. During summer, you will need to add 1 hour to your time, as DST is observed in Ventimiglia.
