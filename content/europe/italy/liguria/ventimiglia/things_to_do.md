---
title: "Things to do in Ventimiglia"
type: section
---

Tucked away in the northern corner of Italy is the small beautiful
town called **Ventimiglia. Things to do**
here range from shopping and boating to sightseeing. The Town is a popular tourist
destination for many, especially during the summer months. Here are a few
things to help you make the most of your visit to Ventimiglia.

**Churches**

One of the most popular things to do in any Italian town is
visiting the churches. There are a few beautiful churches in Ventimiglia and you will probably need half a day to visit and enjoy the beauty of these
churches. The Cattedrale dell’Assunta di Ventimiglia, Oratorio dei Neri and the
San Michele Arcangelo are 3 of the most popular churches here.

**Explore the Inlets**

Ventimiglia is located on the Gulf of Genoa and there is a
small harbor at the River Roya. You can come here and take a boat trip around
the different inlets. You will be able to enjoy the natural beauty of the place
in a special way. The boat ride will take you to some extraordinary places that
will leave you spellbound by their sheer beauty. This will probably take some
time, so plan on spending a whole day exploring the inlets.

**San Remo**

Located about 20 km from Ventimiglia, San Remo is
popular for its flowers. The place is a small fishing village that has been
transformed into a beautiful lively town. This is the best place to try some Pasta
al Pesto; you will never taste it better anywhere else in Italy. Though the
village is about 20 km away, it is worth the drive. Remember to take
your digital camera along for some amazing pictures.

**Market**

The Ventimiglia market is one of the main points in town and
is also the largest market in the area as well as a popular spot for tourists. You
will be able to bargain at most stalls in the market. The market includes
several clothing shops where you can get Polo and Lacoste shirts and t-shirts
for a fraction of what you will pay elsewhere in Italy. Another popular part of
the market is the leather bag section.

**Cheese Shopping**

You probably would never have thought of cheese shopping but
Ventimiglia is a great place to shop for cheese. The Friday market will give
you a chance to buy cheese at very cheap prices compared to what you will pay
back home. Pick up some of your favorite cheese to take with you you on a picnic.
