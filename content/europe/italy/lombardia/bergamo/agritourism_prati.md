An ecotourism farm that raises brown alpine cows and makes formagelle and taleggio cheese and butter. There are cattle, pigs and courtyard animals. All of these products can be bought or used in the restaurant. A small mountain hut has been reactivated at Parini Fields at 850 m. altitude and reacheable only by a foot trail (a 30 minute's walk) along an antique trail through the woods.

There are rooms with 20 beds and room outside for 5 tents in the summer season. Full board is €40. From Bergamo go north to Valle Brembana and pass through Vill d'Almè until you get to the small town of Sedrina. Turn left for Clèr and take the trail up 300 Meters higher for about 30 minutes. From here you can enjoy a unique panorama of the Brembana Valley and the Bergamo plains.

In July and August they have a festival of the mountains and a festival of traditional music from Bergamo.
