---
title: "Bars and Cafes"
type: section
---

Via Gombito, the main lane through Città Alta, is lined with bars that do double duty as patisseries, aperitivo spots, and evening hangouts. The custom here is the same as everywhere in Italy: stand at the bar for a quick espresso, or take a table outside and stay as long as you like. In the lower city, the area around Il Sentierone fills up at aperitivo hour with office workers and students.
