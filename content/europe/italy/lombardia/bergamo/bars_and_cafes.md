If Italians aren't spending their time eating out in a wonderful Italian restaurant with all the good food to choose from then they may be spending their time socializing in a bar mid-morning over cappucino and a sweet roll, before lunch with an aperitif and appetizers, in the afternoon for a great ice cream or before dinner for that great glass of wine or an aperitif or after dinner for a digestive drink to end the day in style with friends.

Therefore the concept of a bar in Italy includes a happy feeling that can involve the whole family with gorgeous views of gardens, valleys and the lower town; or it can be a pub atmosphere for mainly young adults who need to sit down, relax and talk or listen to music, all different kinds of music depending on the style of the bar or the event of that night.

### [Del Tasso](/europe/italy/lombardia/bergamo/bars_and_cafes/del_tasso)

Caffe' del Tasso is a historical place once frequented by artists and learned men located in Piazza Vecchia in CittÃ  Alta and it has elegant indoor and outdoor seating. They serve ice cream, tea, drinks, a little food and appetizers. It is the custom to linger at your table, take your time and absorb the gorgeous sites of Piazza Vecchia with its lights, people, monuments, water fountain and life. If you are lucky there may be an event or a puppet show but just the Piazza is a show in itself.



tel: 035 237 966

address: 3 Piazza Vecchia

### [La Tana](/europe/italy/lombardia/bergamo/bars_and_cafes/la_tana)

This trattoria and wine bar is located in a stone construction from 1400, one of the 100 towers in the upper town, with ceiling beams and the front door once was the entrance for horses and carriages. Hear the fire crackling in the fireplace and see the old well at the entrance while you taste wine, try cheeses and salamie or grilled meat.

address: VIA SAN LORENZO 25 24129 BERGAMO Alta

### [Pasticceria Cavour](/europe/italy/lombardia/bergamo/bars_and_cafes/pasticceria_cavour)

In the heart of the upper town, just two steps away from Piazza Vecchia, a famous statesman, Camillo Benso "the Count of Cavour", from Torino, founded this cafe in 1850. In this elegant Cafe you can choose from chocolates, bon bons, small pastries, candies, fruit chews, all kinds of croissants, krapfen and the Sacher cake while enjoying the Belle-epoche style with frescoes, mirrors, small tables and couches. It is renowned by the region.



accessibility: On foot - center of Città Alta

tel: +39.035.243418

address: VIA GOMBITO 7 24129 BERGAMO Alta
