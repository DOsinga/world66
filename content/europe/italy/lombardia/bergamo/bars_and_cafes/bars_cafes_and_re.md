There are even famous bars in Città Alta and many of the bars are also restaurants when it's mealtime. The most famous bar is Pasticceria Cavour in Via Gombito - the main road that goes down the center of Città Alta. In Pasticceria Cavour you can try the rich assortment of pastries and cakes of all kinds or on Sunday try the brunch and the buffet of appetizers to fill you up. http://bergamoalta/bar.it
