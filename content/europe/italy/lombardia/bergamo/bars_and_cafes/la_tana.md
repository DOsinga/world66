This trattoria and wine bar is located in a stone construction from 1400, one of the 100 towers in the upper town, with ceiling beams and the front door once was the entrance for horses and carriages. Hear the fire crackling in the fireplace and see the old well at the entrance while you taste wine, try cheeses and salamie or grilled meat.
