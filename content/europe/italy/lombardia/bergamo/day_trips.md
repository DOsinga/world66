---
title: "Day Trips"
type: section
---

The valleys north of Bergamo — Brembana and Seriana — are well worth a half or full day. San Pellegrino Terme (40 minutes by bus up the Brembana Valley) is a faded Belle Époque spa town with a casino and a river beach. Lake Iseo is an hour away by car and far less crowded than Como or Garda. Milan is 50 minutes by train if you need a city fix.
