San Pellegrino Terme, the home of the world famous mineral water, is in Brembana Valley. It is alongside the Brembana River and has a Casinò, bars, cafes, restaurants, tennis courts, swimming pools, mini golf, hotels, festivals and street markets.

Walk along the Brembo River or have a cappucino and socialize while watching the people walk by.

Or go to one of the lakes for a boat trip, a show or a concert.

### Brembana Valley

Brembana Valley is north of Bergamo and good for both winter and summer travel activities. Stay for a day, a week, a month or forever!
