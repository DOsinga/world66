Bergamo is well served in all respects by buses and taxis. And there are two funiculars. One goes from the end of bus 1 up to the Upper Town. Another one goes from the Upper Town up to San Vigilio. The view from both of these funiculars and the bars at the top are magnificent.

### Bus from Bergamo Centre to Malpensa Airport

There is an online ticket purchase as well as purchase directly from the bus company for the bus from Bergamo City Centre to Malpensa Airport and/or back. The bus station is in the same square as the Bergamo train station. The bus leaves and arrives at Platform 12 in the Bergamo Bus Sation (Stazione Autolinee).

It's offered by Zani Viaggi.
