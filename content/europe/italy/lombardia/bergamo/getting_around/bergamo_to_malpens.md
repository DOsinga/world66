There is an online ticket purchase as well as purchase directly from the bus company for the bus from Bergamo City Centre or Bergamo Airport to Malpensa Airport and/or back. The bus station is in the same square as the Bergamo train station. The bus leaves and arrives at Platform 12 or 9 (check the latest main schedule) in the Bergamo Bus Sation (Stazione Autolinee).

It's offered by Zani Viaggi.
