From 6 December 2008 to 6 January 2009 take the City Bus for €1.50, or the Province bus for €2.50 for the whole ATB network. Take the Free Shopping Bus in Piazzale Goisis or Celadina from 6 to 24 December on Saturdays and holidays and 12-22-23-24 December from 9:30 to 20:00 every 15 minutes. Or take the free "Gulliberg" tourist train between 6 and 24 December around the Center, and San Leonardo Borgo every Saturday from 14:30 and 19:30 and on Sundays and holidays along the shopping street of Citta Alta.

Tickets on sale at ATB Point in Porta Nuova 16, the Bus Station Ticket office in Piazzale Marconi or at authorized Newspaper stands or Tabbacchi.
