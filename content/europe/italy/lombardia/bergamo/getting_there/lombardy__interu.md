The S.A.B. bus company connects many locations outside the town of Bergamo and goes as far as the seaside and the mountains and valleys. The S.A.B. Station is beside the train station: as you leave the train station it's to the right. Their internet site includes links to inter-regional and international bus transportation and all kinds of tourist information and live web cams.****
