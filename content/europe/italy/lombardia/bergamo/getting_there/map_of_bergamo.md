There is an autostrada that links Bergamo to other cities. There are also state roads that go to the mountains, lakes or the surrounding country. You can go by bicycle, motorcycle, car or caravan.

Michelin has a very good map. Better yet ***BERGAMO TOUR.IT*** has City and Province maps (right hand center side of internet site) that can be printed out and best of all it has a virtual tour area both for the city and the province (upper right hand corner of the internet site).
