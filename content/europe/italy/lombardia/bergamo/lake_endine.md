A small, quiet lake in the Val Cavallina, about 40 minutes by bus from Bergamo. Lake Endine gets a fraction of the visitors that Como or Garda attract, which is exactly its appeal. The mountains reflect cleanly in the water, and the surrounding villages feel genuinely unhurried.

In summer, the lake is good for sailing, windsurfing, and canoeing. In winter it sometimes freezes over completely, drawing ice fishermen and a few brave skaters. The town of Monasterolo del Castello, on the eastern shore, makes the best base -- it has a small castle and a handful of places to eat.
