The Museum is in the seat of the Istituto Musicale (Palazzo della Misericordia) and has autographs, belongings and books of the great musician from Bergamo. Open Tuesday through Saturday from 9:30 to 12 and 14:00 to 17:00. Sunday 10 to 12 and 14 to 16.
Closed Mondays, holidays and August 15. Free entrance. During the Donizetti Festival visits are only by reservation.

Besides the museum of Gaetano Donizetti see also the famous artist's house, a characteistic example of a home from the beginning of the 1800s. Free entrance. Saturday and Sunday from 11 to 18:30. If you want to visit on other days you have to make reservations at least one week in advance at this number 035 399432.
