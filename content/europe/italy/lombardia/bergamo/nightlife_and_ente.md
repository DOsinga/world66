Concerts, Opera, and Theater are just some of the cultural entertainment. And depending on the date, you may run into culture in the streets in the form of puppet shows, Carnivale parades, processions, dancing, music and performances.

Sometimes there are so many events going on in this city of art that it becomes difficult to find a hotel. To find out what's going on when you are visiting, the public tourist information office can help you find the events you prefer most and then get you your hotel even if it means getting a place just slightly out of town (insist with the tourist information assistant to get you a hotel just out of town and directions how to get there), which means more or less 15 minutes away, and it's worth it. Especially since there are buses connecting most all locations now with the new city bus services.

At night it's fun to just stroll up and down the main street either in Città Alta or in the Città Bassa. There are lots of Cafe Bars in Via Gombito in Città Alta and all around.

For dancing and discoteques you may go out of town or, best of all, to the lakes or even Milan. Be sure to check out the festivals.
