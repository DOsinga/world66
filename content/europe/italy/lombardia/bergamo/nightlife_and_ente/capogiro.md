This is the closest discoteque to Bergamo - which means 15 minutes by ATB bus number 9A from Porta Nuova toward Marigolda but get off at Curno. Otherwise it's about 25 minutes from the Bergamo train station on foot. Sometimes there are free passes and discounts to get into discoteques or have a drink.
