City bus map and schedules (linee e orari) may be found at the internet address below. Bus 1C is the Airport bus (aeroporto). A tourist ticket good for three days is €5.00 and it includes the funiculars - one up to Citta Alta and another one even further up to San Vigilio and may be purchased outside the arrivals area of the airport to the left at "Zani".

Also a ticket good for one day (to be validated one time in the machine on the bus on the day of use) is €2.50. Otherwise one city bus ticket is €1 if you stay in the center zone of town (on the map it is zone 00.)

Bus tickets are also for sale in the train station to the right before you exit out the glass doors. On this internet site are listed more addresses for purchase of ATB bus tickets. You have to buy the ticket before getting on the bus.
