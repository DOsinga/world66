Thinking about buying and living abroad? More and more people are doing it - it's like reverse immigration. Those who have it all - except peace of mind - are bailing out to foreign countries just to have a better life with less hassle. But at the same time there is that airport connection to get you back to family and friends when needed or desired. For those of you who are really serious just remember that many people have done this before you.
