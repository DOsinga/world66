There are many tourist agencies in Bergamo to help you with any information you may need to make your stay complete. A guided tour? Skiing in the mountains? A special event?

There is a tourist information office on the main street in the lower town in Via Vittorio Emanuele 20 and there is one off the Old Square in the upper town in Vicolo Aquila Nera, 2.

In the Airport: Tourist office for Bergamo Tourism is the agency for tourists coming to Bergamo and the surrounding area. The office in the airport is open 7 days a week from 8.00 to 23.00. It offers free tourist information and hotel booking services and also distributes promotional material of Bergamo Province.
