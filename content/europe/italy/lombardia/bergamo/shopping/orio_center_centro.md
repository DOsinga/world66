The biggest shopping center in Italy, Orio Center Shopping Mall is right across the autostrada from the Bergamo - Orio al Serio Airport. It has 200 shops, restaurants, bars, a supermarket, a nursery where children can play, cash dispensers, an underground parking lot, restrooms and a fantastic view of Citta Alta and the Orio al Serio Airport from the upstairs cafe and restaurants. Take a bus there or follow the directions to the airport and turn off the motorway after the airport turnoff. Follow the street sign with the shopping cart on it - it's across the autostrada from the airport. Open from 9 to 22:00 and on Saturdays from 8:30 to 21:00. Closed Sundays except on special occasions.
