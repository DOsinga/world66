Prenditempo is a tour operator which proposes bike excursions in Bergamo area.
Medieval
cities and boroughs, lakes, rivers and beautiful mountains are the setting of bike adventures.

Excursions
are sized on different type of rider and any level of fitness: both
challenging riders and families can find their propers solution.

Prenditempo offers language guided excursions starting from Bergamo city and ring routes
starting from different places in the province.
