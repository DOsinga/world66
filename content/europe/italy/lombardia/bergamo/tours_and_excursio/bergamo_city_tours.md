Bergamo is a city full of history, art and traditions. Discover it walking along the narrow streets of Città Alta, the historical city center, or following the "Sentierone", the shopping boulevard in the modern town. And don't forget to spend at least a day in the bergamasco valleys among beautiful mountains, hills covered in vineyards and quaint lakes.

We offer individual or groups guided tours, tailor-made excursions to the lakes, daily guided visit to Milan and wine safari®, very special itineraries dedicated to wines, local products and fine food.
