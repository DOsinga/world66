The “Gruppo Guide Città di Bergamo”, founded in 1988, consists of 40 professionally qualified and certificated tourist guides who speak Italian, English, German, Spanish, Dutch; French. All the qualified tourist guides have got a certificate issued by the Lombardy Region “Regione Lombardia” and the Province of Bergamo to perform such an activity (in compliance with the Regional Law n. 65/1986).
Our expert guides are available for art exhibition and cultural events too and to meet your particular requests in order to assure you an unforgettable visit to Bergamo and its province.
