For nature one can visit parks, walking paths, waterfalls, mines, and meadows. For food and drink one can tour the local wine, cheese and oil producers, their land and products. For culture one can visit the Museums of Bergamo or the Province also by night, or go to theaters, or see examples of Romanic architecture. Visit the sancturaries of: Bergamo, the plains, the Iseo Lake or the Valleys of Imagna and Seriana.

See castles, theme parks: Acqua Center Park, Fantasyworld, Gardaland, Archeopark, Parco della Preistoria, Villaggio Africano, Caneva Sport Water Park, MiniItalia, spas, go on sport itineraries by biking, walking or horse back riding, cruising, or tour the best shopping places.
