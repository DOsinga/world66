The Lake of Lecco and Como is an upside down Y-shaped lake with two names depending on which leg you are looking at. It has ferryboats that can take you and your bike or car to Varenna, Bellagio and as far as Como. You can enjoy boating, camping, swimming, get married there, stay in a hotel or just relax.

Oftentimes there are concerts, art shows, fireworks and more depending on the day you are there.
