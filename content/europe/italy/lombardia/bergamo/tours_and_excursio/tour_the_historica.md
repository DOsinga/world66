Tour one or all five DIMORE STORICHE - the historical palaces of the oldest and richest families of Bergamo who were once the centers for cultural and traditional encounters and who still live in these homes.

The palaces are open for tours from April 23 to June 4 every Sunday from 10:00 to 13:00 and 14:00 to 19:00. Tickets are sold inside the entrance of each Palace and group tours can be reserved from Monday through Saturday. Tickets are sold for visiting just one palace, three palaces or all five palaces. Reduced tickets for ages 6 to 14.

Casa Palma Camozzi Vertova - Via San Lorenzo 21, Bergamo

Palazzo Agliardi - Via Pignolo 86, Bergamo

Palazzo Moroni - Via di Porta Dipinta 12, Bergamo

Palazzo Terzi - Piazza Terzi 2, Bergamo

Villa Grismondi Finardi -
