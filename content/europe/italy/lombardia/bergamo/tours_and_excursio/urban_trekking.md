Urban
trekking in Bergamo is one of the best ways to know and discover the essence of the city.
The
Upper Town of Bergamo is a unique place for urban trekking. The mix
of nature and culture that you will discover step by step, walking in
the hills of Bergamo, offers unforgettable emotions.
Prenditempo
excursions take trekkers all along cobbled streets, jagged paths,
terraces cultivated for centuries, beautiful gardens that adorn the
magnificent villas and ancient rural districts still perfectly
preserved.Prenditempo Guides accompany tourists along hidden paths, away
from the beaten tracks and bustle, for a real alternative experience of Bergamo.
