A guided walking tour of Bergamo's Città Alta, the medieval hilltop town ringed by 16th-century Venetian walls. The tour runs on Sundays in English, departing at 15:00 from Piazza Mercato delle Scarpe at the top of the funicular.

The route winds through the old town's narrow streets, taking in the Piazza Vecchia, the Basilica di Santa Maria Maggiore, the Colleoni Chapel, and the views from the Venetian walls. Allow about two hours. Book through Cooraltur or just show up at the meeting point.
