---
title: "Day Trips"
type: section
---

Brescia is a natural base for the **southern shore of Lake Garda** — the castle town of Sirmione is 40 minutes away by car or bus — and for the quieter **Lake Iseo** to the north. The **Franciacorta** wine region between them makes Italy's best sparkling wines and is an easy half-day drive of cellars and tastings. For skiing in winter, the slopes of Val Camonica are under two hours north.
