---
title: "Festivals in Brescia"
type: section
---

If you are planning a vacation to Brescia and want to find the best time to see the city, you may want to plan your trip when there are** Brescia festivals** taking place. Any of the festivals make a great time to see the city and learn more about the culture.

#### Mille Miglia

The Mille Miglia festival is held in April and May. It is a recreational event full of automobile races. The routes begin in Viale Venezia in Brescia and travel through the streets of Italy. This is a competition that brings in many different kinds of antique cars and is a showcase of some of the best cars on display. The races are fun to watch all along the routes. Choose a good spot to relax and enjoy the racing and fun.

#### Centomiglia

The Centomiglia festival is held in September. The festival is a regatta held on Lake Garda. There are European sailing competitions that are held, and hundreds of teams participate. The city celebrates the festival with many activities and events you can enjoy while watching the yachts.

#### The Festival of Franciacorta

The Festival of Franciacorta is held in September. It has many activities and foods to try and also guided tours of the wine cellars. You can experience some of the most beautiful parts of the city and enjoy the festival activities.
