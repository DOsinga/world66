---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

Brescia sits on the main Milan–Venice railway and the A4 autostrada, with frequent fast trains from [Milan](/europe/italy/lombardia/milan) (40 minutes), [Verona](/europe/italy/veneto/verona) (30 minutes) and Venice (1 hour 45 minutes). **Brescia-Montichiari (Gabriele d'Annunzio) Airport**, 18 km south of the centre, handles a limited number of low-cost flights; most visitors arrive via Milan Bergamo or Verona airports instead.
