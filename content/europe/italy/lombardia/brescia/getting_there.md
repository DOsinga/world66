Brescia is easy to reach both by road and by train, since it is located next to the [Milan](/europe/italy/lombardia/milan)-[Venice](/europe/italy/veneto/venice) highway and railway. The new Gabriele d'Annunzio ariport offers also some air connections with destinations in [Italy](/europe/italy) and in [Europe](/europe).

**all**

**By plane**

Brescia Montichiari Airport is just 18 km or approx. 20 minutes drive from the city centre. Verona and Milan are situated 50km and 120km respectively from the airport. Ryanair connects twice a day Brescia and London Stansted.

**By Train**

Brescia is located on the [Milan](/europe/italy/lombardia/milan) - [Venice](/europe/italy/veneto/venice) railway. Almost all the trains in servcice on this railway stop in Brescia. For a full train timetable, please, chec [more..](/europe/italy/lombardia/brescia/gettingthere/bytrain)

**By road**

If you come from east you can reach Brescia through the A4 highway, direction [Milan](/europe/italy/lombardia/milan) . From west you'd take the A4 highway, direction [Venice](/europe/italy/veneto/venice) .
