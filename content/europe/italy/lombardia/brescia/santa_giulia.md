An 8th-century Benedictine nunnery transformed into the city museum: eleven thousand objects spanning Roman Brescia, Lombard gold jewellery, medieval frescoes, and the spectacularly painted 16th-century **Coro delle Monache** (Nuns' Choir). The star piece is the **Cross of Desiderius**, a 9th-century processional cross studded with gems and reused Roman cameos. One of the best museums in northern Italy and part of Brescia's UNESCO listing.
