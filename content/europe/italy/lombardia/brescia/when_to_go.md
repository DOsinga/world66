**Climate in Brescia**

The climate in Brescia is continental; thus, it does not experience any storms or strong winds. The temperature in Brescia ranges from -1 to 10 degrees Celsius (30.2 degrees F to 50 degrees F) during winter, while during the summer, the temperature can range from 18 to 29 degrees Celsius (64.4 degrees F to 84 degrees F). Summer months in Brescia is from March to August, while the winter is from November to February. During summer, the city receives a volume of 250 to 300 mm of rain which makes the city streets wet but not flooded.

**Brescia Summer and Winter Activities**

The best time to go to Brescia is during the summer months but there are also winter activities in the Alps that you can enjoy during winter. In fact, Brescia can be visited all year round. During summer, Brescia has a lot of festivities such as the International Piano Festival and the Mille Miglia during April to June, while during winter, Brescia holds the Fair of San Faustino and Giovita in the month of February.
