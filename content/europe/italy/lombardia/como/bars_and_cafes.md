---
title: "Bars and Cafes"
type: section
---

Como is a small, unhurried city and its bar scene matches: aperitivo hour on
the lakefront terraces, espresso in the marble-clad caffès of the old town,
and wine bars tucked into medieval side streets. If you want late-night clubs
and dancing, Milan is 45 minutes by train — but for a relaxed evening drink
with a lake view, Como is hard to beat.
