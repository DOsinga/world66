---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

Getting to Como is not a big problem. It is located about 45 km north of Milan and can be easilly reached both by private or public transport.

 **By Air**

 You can reach Como from Lugano, Milan, Lucarno and Cameri. This takes between 25 and 45 minutes.

  **By car**

 From Switzerland or Milan, take motorway A9, exit at Como Monte Olimpino or Como Nord.

  **By Train**

 From Milan: trains leave from the central station to Como S.G. station every hour in the morning and afternoon.

 From Zürich: trains leave from central station to Como S.G> station about every two hours.
