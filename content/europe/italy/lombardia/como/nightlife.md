---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment"
type: section
---

Como is a rather small, quiet place and it does not have a very exiting nightlife. If you really want to make a night of it, you will probably be better of in Clubs & Dancing, at some 45 km south of Como. This major city will surely keep you awake all night. 

If you just want to have a good time, you might want to try finding a camping disco. The crowd will be very young, the music probably very tacky, but hey, you're on holiday!
