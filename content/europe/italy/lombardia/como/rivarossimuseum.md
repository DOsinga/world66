Rivarossi was one of the great names in model railways, founded in Como in
1946 and celebrated for its exquisitely detailed HO-scale locomotives. This
small museum holds every model the company produced, alongside pieces from
over a hundred other manufacturers worldwide. Admission is by appointment —
a curiosity for serious collectors, and an unexpectedly charming one.
