Como rewards visitors with an unusually rich mix of sacred architecture, Enlightenment science, and lakeside grandeur. The compact historic centre is walkable in an afternoon, but each corner turns up something worth lingering over — a Romanesque apse, a neoclassical colonnade, a silk workshop still humming behind an arched doorway.
