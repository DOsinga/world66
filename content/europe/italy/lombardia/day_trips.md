---
title: "Day Trips in Lombardia"
type: section
---

A variety of** Lombardia day trips **are available for tourists visiting the region. With its rich cultural past, blended with a contemporary lifestyle, Lombardia has a lot to offer to the tourists visiting Italy.

**A Day Trip to Pavia**

Pavia is an excellent destination for a day trip. Some of the noteworthy places in Pavia are the large fortified Castello Visconteo, the Broletto, the Duomo di Pavia, and the covered bridge of the Ticino River. Pavia is located on the Trenitalia train line, and with an efficient train service, it takes about half an hour reach there.

**Isole Borromee Day Trip**

This is one of the most beautiful corners along the Italian Lakes and is particularly worth visiting in spring or autumn. To get to Isole Borromee on the Lake Maggiore, tourists can take a train and spend a day out in the midst of natural beauty.

**The Chapels of Lombardy**

A good way to combine recreation with cultural discovery is to hike to some of the Alpine pilgrimage chapels for a day. They are accessible by car, but the real pleasure lies in going on foot through the fields, past the waterfalls, up the steep mountains just as pilgrims have done for centuries.

**Mantova Day Trips**

The splendid Mantova is situated in the midst of dreary marshlands. Tourists can take a day trip and see Andrea Mantegna's masterpiece, the frescoed walls of the Camera degli Sposi in the Palazzo Ducale.

**Day Trip to Milan**

Visitors can take a day trip to the wonderful city of Milan. Milan is Lombardy's capital and a prominent commercial center of Italy. There are several beautiful churches, museums, and castles to explore in Milan.
