---
title: "Salo"
type: location
---

For about two years Salo was the capital of Fascist Italy. In 1943 Italy surrendered to Allied Forces. Mussolini set up a fascist republic in Salo.

Salo is located on a charming gulf. The earthquake of 1901 destroyed most of the old buildings. Still, in the centre there are some historic buildings of interest as well as a few monuments.

The hilly hinterland is great for hiking. Salo is part of the High Brescia Garda Park, which extends up to Limone, comprising an area of 38,000 hectares.
