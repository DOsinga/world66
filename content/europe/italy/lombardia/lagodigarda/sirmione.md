---
title: "Sirmione"
type: location
---

On the south shore of the Lago di Garda you find the peninsula of Sirmione. The peninsula is famous for its beautiful countryside and the Caves of Catullo, an archaeological site of a former Roman villa situated on the tip of the peninsula. 

The Sirmione Spa, the largest privately owned thermal treatment centre in Italy, whose sulphurous waters originate from the depths of Lake Garda, has long been one of Sirmione's main attractions. There is plenty of accommodation available as well as frequent boat services to other lakeside towns and villages.
