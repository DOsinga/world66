The thermal complex draws on sulphurous springs that rise from the lake bed — the same waters the Romans used. The Aquaria wellness centre is open to day visitors and has indoor and outdoor thermal pools, including one that sits right on the edge of the lake. It's not cheap, but an afternoon here — soaking in warm water with the lake spread out in front of you — is one of the better ways to spend time in Sirmione. Book ahead in summer.
