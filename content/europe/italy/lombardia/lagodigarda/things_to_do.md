---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Lake Garda rewards exploration by boat as much as by road. The lakeside villages each have their own character: Sirmione at the southern tip is the most visited, with its medieval castle and Roman ruins; Gardone on the western shore has the extraordinary Vittoriale degli Italiani; Lazise and Peschiera are quieter and more authentically Italian. The Monte Baldo ridge above Malcesine offers hiking and mountain biking with sweeping views over the lake.
