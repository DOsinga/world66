---
title: "Top 5 Must Do's in Lago Maggiore"
type: section
---

**Lago Maggiore must do's** include parks, churches and museums. The lake
is surrounded by mountains and hills providing the perfect natural
setting for a relaxing holiday. The weather is mild and therefore Lake
Maggiore is visited by tourists throughout the year. The local area has
all the necessary facilities to provide tourists with a top class
holiday.

**Isola dei Pescatori**

This small island is located in Lake Maggiore and it is frequented by
tourists. It is only 350 meters long and 100 meters wide with a few
inhabitants. The island has a central street with smaller streets
running off this one providing access to the houses on the island. A
popular activity is to visit Isola dei Pescatori island to enjoy a
delicious meal at one of the unique restaurants. This quaint island has breath taking scenery and views over
the lake and is a must do when visiting Lago Maggiore.
Another attraction on the island is the church of San Vittore and ruins
of a chapel.

**Villa Pallavicino Park**

This large park is located near Stresa. It features botanical gardens
and a zoo. It is possible to view a variety of animal and plant species
while enjoying the fresh air. Birds living in the area include peacocks
and flamingos. There are paths through the botanical garden where you
can enjoy a
relaxing stroll. The park is an ideal
family activity as it is suitable for children of all ages and it is
possible to use a pushchair along the paths. There are refreshments on
sale when you need to take a
break and replenish your energy. The park has breath taking views over
Lago Maggiore. To enter the park, visitors need to pay a small entrance
fee.

**

Church of Santa Canterina del Sasso**

This attraction is a religious site that is rich in history and
legends. It is said that a merchant prayed to God to save his life
during a storm and when he survived he took refuge in the area that is now the church. The
church was constructed in the cliffs in the 12th century and the current
building is formed from joining chapels. A feature of this church is
beautiful paintings. The site has become a pilgrimage site receiving
many religious visitors annually. The church can be reached by boat or
car. It is necessary to climb steps to reach the church.

**Mottarone Mountain**

Mottarone Mountain can be reached by cable car or by
road. The mountain offers breath taking views of the surrounding areas
and it is a good place for photographers and artists. Hiking and
skiing are other suggestions for activities.

**Umbrella Museum**

This unique museum offers a different experience as you can learn about
the history of umbrellas. It is here that you can see umbrellas through
the ages from parasols to different types of umbrellas that are
currently being used. This museum offers an interesting and educational outing for children. There is a nominal entrance fee.
