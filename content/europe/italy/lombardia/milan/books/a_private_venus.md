The first of Scerbanenco's Duca Lamberti crime novels, set in 1960s Milan — the economic miracle city of factories, suburbs, and social dislocation. The detective is a disgraced doctor, the Milan he moves through is unglamorous and specific. Scerbanenco invented the modern Italian crime novel here, and the city he describes — industrial, restless, indifferent — is still recognisable beneath the fashion gloss.
