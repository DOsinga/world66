Eco's second novel is set partly in Milan — its publishing world, its occult bookshops, the Castello Sforzesco — and spirals into a vast conspiracy thriller about secret societies and the Templars. The Milan sections feel specific and local; the Bompiani publishing house (where Eco himself worked) is thinly veiled. A book about the city's intellectual culture as much as anything else.
