title: "The Betrothed (I Promessi Sposi)"
type: poi
author: "Alessandro Manzoni"
isbn: "978-0140444186"
---

The foundational Italian novel, set in 17th-century Lombardy under Spanish rule. Two peasants try to marry against the obstruction of a local bully, the church, and a plague. Manzoni wrote it in Milan and used the Lombard landscape and dialect as the backbone of a unified Italian literary language. Essential context for understanding this region's self-image.
