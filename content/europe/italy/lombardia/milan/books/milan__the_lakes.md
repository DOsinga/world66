Milan &
the Lakes is another good book that has rolled out from DK Publishing
house. The book offers an illuminating insight into the culture,
tourist spots and other places of historical and cultural importance of
the Italian nation.

The book describes about each and every place worth
watching in Milan, and does not leave any space, nook or corner
untouched that might be of some visitor interest. The book is published
in paperback and contains 248 pages, which are illustrative and contain
the colorful descriptions of the major places in Milan. The cover of
the book is very colorful, and by looking at the cover itself one can
imagine about the loads of information that it contains.

The Italian
art galleries, gardens, restaurants, hotels, museums and villas are
covered in the book and every minute detail that might interest you is
discussed. The book divides Italy into 6 major parts and
then these 6 parts are described in detail as the pages are turned.

The other two major sub-sections of the book are Traveler’s Needs and
Survival Guide, making it very simple and easy even for a first time
visitor to enjoy his stay in Milan. The book was
last revised in the year 2007, and although it has covered every
important historical change up to that point, some facts might
not be up to date. That’s the only drawback of the book.

The
book contains colorful maps, detailed analysis of major places of
interest and the compact size (8.6 x 5 x 0.7 inches) of the book makes
it very simple and easy to carry while traveling.
