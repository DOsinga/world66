A polished, editorial-style guide written by a Milan-based author. Prioritises design, food culture, and neighbourhoods over tourist checklist items — strong on aperitivo bars, the Navigli canal district, and the Fondazione Prada. Best for first-time visitors who want to eat and drink like a local rather than tick boxes.
