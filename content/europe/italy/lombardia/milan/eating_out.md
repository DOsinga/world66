---
title: "Eating Out"
type: section
---

For lunchtime it seems, that the Milan locals do not need much to be lucky, the city bursts of "paninotecas" and fast-food outlets, where they eat a quick and mostly cold lunch. But as in every Italian city dinner is the time for large meals and going out with friends. In Milan you will find a large selection of international restaurants as well as lots of marvellous "trattorie" or "ristoranti" specialized on local dishes. However, check out one of the Italian spots, where you will always find a delicious homemade meal for a fair price. Enjoy the Italian way of life (and eating), don't hesitate to ask about food, ingredients and day specials and don't forget to end you dinner with a desert and a caffé.
