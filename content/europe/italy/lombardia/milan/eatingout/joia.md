This haute-cuisine restaurant near Piazza della Repubblica offers delicious vegetarian dishes, that are artistically prepared by chef Pietro Leemann, who creates a new menu every three months. Dishes might include ravioli with basil, potatoes, pine nuts, and crisp green beans, or purple taglioni with spring vegetables. A fish menu is also available, and there is a no-smoking dining room.
