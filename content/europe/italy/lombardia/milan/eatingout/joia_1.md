**Joia is a restaurant to experience as a vegetarian.** For starters the menu is complex with an array of names like 'egg apparent'. And yes its truly a good night out to put your money aside for.

It's probably the only Michelin starred (1 star) veg only restaurant in Europe. The menu is complex and priced accordingly ... the basic 5 dish set menu / degustation is 60 euro without wine rising to 100 euro for the best meal you might ever have. Wine is extra, 3 selected top quality glasses will set you back another 30 euro

The atmosphere is plain ... more Swedish interior design ... thats ok ... you're going for the food. The dining areas (there are 3) are all small so its quiet and cozy ... maybe only 4 tables in each area.

If you want to try some Joia charm but not to the same price, look up the Joia Leggero at Corso Porta Ticinese 106
