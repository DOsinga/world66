La Topaia is situated near Porta Genova on the green line metro.
It is a traditional Italain restaraunt with live music and the entertainment that goes on into the early hours.
When you book a table it is for the whole evening.
The local clients often bring guitars and all join in the singing and playing.
The menu caters for meat eaters and vegitarians alike.
This is a very welcoming place speaking, Italian, Croasian, French, German and English.
