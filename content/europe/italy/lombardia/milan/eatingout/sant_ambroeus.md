The most beautiful *pasticceria* of the city. Sant Ambroeus is in the heart of the fashion district, near Via Montenapoleone and offers hand made candies, which can be either packed (even for travelling!) or eated inside in the internal tea room and outside.

At lunch a mediterranean menu is offered, and all day long dishes are ready to be eaten. A vaste offer of cakes, most of them so beautiful to be considered art pieces, can satisfy every client.

Ask for Miss Enrica to have everything wrapped in a unique manner, but always with the legendary silk blue ribbon.
