There is a third Airport which is considered a Milan Airport. It has three different names (or even more combinations of names): Milan Airport - Orio al Serio, Bergamo Airport, Orio al Serio Airport and so on. In any case its code is BGY just like the airport code of Malpensa Airport is MXP or Los Angeles International Airport is LAX.

The Orio al Serio Airport is located in the town of Orio al Serio which is right next to the town of Bergamo. It's very small so there is just one terminal. It's around 50 kilometres from Milan so it takes around 50 minutes to get to Milan by car if the traffic conditions are good.

There are various combinations of buses, trains, and so on. See [Bergamo](/europe/italy/lombardia/bergamo/getting_there/bergamo_airport_o).
