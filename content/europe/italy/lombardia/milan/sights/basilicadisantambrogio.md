Towards Piazza Sant' Ambrogio, one of the most fascinating places of
the whole city. Here you can admire the Basilica di Sant'Ambrogio in
Romanesque style, dedicated to the Saint patron of Milan. In the nearby
there is the Catholic University, former monastery of the Abbey.
