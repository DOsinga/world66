A must see is the Castello Sforzesco whose central tower (Torre del Filarete) dominates one of the most common and beautiful landscapes in Milan. The castle is situated in the Parco Sempione, opposite to the Arco della Pace dedicated to Napoleon. The construction of this castle was decided under the rule of the Visconti family, but it is thanks to the Sforza family if the castle reached the grandiosity you can still admire today.address: Via Pontaccio
