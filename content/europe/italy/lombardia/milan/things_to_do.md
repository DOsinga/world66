Milan rewards exploration across several different registers — Gothic and Renaissance churches, world-class art collections, 19th-century architecture, and contemporary design. The historic centre around the Duomo is the obvious starting point, but the Brera neighbourhood, the Navigli canal district, and the newer Porta Nuova area each have their own character.
