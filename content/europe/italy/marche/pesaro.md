Hometown of Italian composer Rossini, Pesaro is located on the Adriatic Coast. Situated on the Adriatic Sea, Pesaro's cityscape has a medieval center, and becomes increasingly modern the closer you get to the beach. Pesaro is home to around 95,000 people.
