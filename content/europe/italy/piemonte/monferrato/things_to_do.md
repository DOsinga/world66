### [On the steps of "Bianchetto"](/europe/italy/piemonte/monferrato/things_to_do/on_the_steps_of_b)

“Bianchetto” is a typical truffle and the tour will let you discover the beauties of Monferrato. Monferrato's primarily hilly terrain is known throughout the world for its many and wonderful wines. Spanning across a vast area of the province of Turin on the southeast and Cuneo (Langhe) on the northeast. The rich land, products, agreeable climate and invidious position have historically spurred feuds and the development of "signorie" since the middle ages.
