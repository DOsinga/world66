**Golf des iles borromees**

18-hole course located between Stresa and Lesa, in the heart of the Lake Maggiore region - 45 minutes from Milan, 6 km from Stresa, and less than 2 km from the (A26) Highway exit of Carpugnino, immerse in the beautiful nature around it. The Golf course is situated 500 metres above the sea level, with a southerly exposure, fresh during the Summer and sunny in Winter, is open all year execpt January. Between June and September included it is open seven day a week, during the other months it is closed on Mondays. The club house is cosy and comfortable, with a restaurant, driving range, putting green, 30 electric golf carts, golf sets and trolleys to hire complete the Club services.
