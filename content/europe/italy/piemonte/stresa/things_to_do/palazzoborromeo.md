The grand Baroque palace on Isola Bella is the centerpiece of the Borromean Islands. Built in the 17th century for the Borromeo family, the palace features lavishly decorated rooms, a shell-encrusted grotto, and spectacular terraced Italian gardens that cascade down to the lake. Reached by regular ferry from Stresa.
