One hundred and twenty of Europe's leading art galleries, museums and foundations, specialist publishers, shows and conferences with major representatives from the worlds of Art, Culture and Communications. This is the Artissima, the event dedicated to art, that has more than a purely cultural significance. Artissima is both an exhibition and a market and is a recently created, but already highly popular event, draws gallery owners, artists and critics to the city from all over Italy and from abroad. In particular, it serves to highlight the significant number of famous names and refined collectors who are based in Turin.
