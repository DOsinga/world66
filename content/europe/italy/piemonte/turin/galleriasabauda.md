The picture gallery of the Royal House of Savoy is one of Italy's largest art collections. It spans Piedmontese and Italian masters like Fra Angelico, Mantegna, and Veronese, alongside the country's finest collection of Flemish and Dutch paintings, including works by Van Eyck, Memling, Rembrandt, and Van Dyck.
