---
title: 'Getting Around'
type: section
---

Turin is compact and flat, so walking is the best way to explore the centre. The city also has an efficient public transport network of buses, trams, and a single metro line. An all-day GTT ticket costs a few euros and covers everything. Porta Nuova and Porta Susa are the two main train stations for connections within the city and beyond. The centre is well suited to cycling, and bike-sharing stations are scattered throughout.
