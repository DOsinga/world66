---
title: 'Things to Do'
type: section
---

Turin rewards the curious visitor with an extraordinary density of museums, palaces, and public spaces packed into its elegant grid of arcaded streets. The city's Savoy royal heritage means world-class collections around every corner, from the Egyptian Museum to the sprawling Venaria Reale palace complex. Beyond the museums, Turin is a city of sports fanatics, riverside parks, and hilltop panoramas that make it far more than just an indoor destination.
