Mungivacca is a residential district on the eastern outskirts of Bari, away from the historic centre and the tourist circuit. The area is mainly a mix of apartment buildings, supermarkets, and office blocks — it holds the headquarters of the Treasury and the regional offices of the Culture and Environment ministries, as well as the State Archive of Bari.

It is not a destination in its own right, but travellers staying in this part of the city will find it quiet and functional. The city centre and the old town (Bari Vecchia) are easily reached by bus or a short drive west.
