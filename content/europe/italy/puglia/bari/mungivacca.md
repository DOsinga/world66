The newest zone, the district of Mungivacca is found on the outskirts of the city. This is a mainly residential area, sprinkled with supermarkets and villas, as well as large glass palazzi. Here you will also find the archive of "Stato di Bari", as well as the HQ of the Treasury and the Ministeries of Culture and Enviroment.
