---

There are international flights to [Bari](/europe/italy/puglia/bari) and/or [Brinidisi](/europe/italy/puglia/brindisi) from several countries in Europe ( Albania , Austria , Belgium , France , Germany , Monaco , Monte-Negro , Rumania , Russia , Switzerland , UK ) and domestic flights from several cities, particularly Milan and Rome .
Trains from Rome to Bari take about five hours.
