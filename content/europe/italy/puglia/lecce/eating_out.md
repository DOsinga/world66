---
title: "Eating Out"
type: section
---

Lecce has a good local restaurant scene, with trattorias that feed a working-class and university-town clientele rather than tourists. Prices are reasonable. The local pasticciotto — a small oval pastry filled with custard cream — is the essential breakfast food; eat one at a bar before you do anything else.

**Tana dei Lupi**

Good value trattoria with solid local cooking.

address: 15, via della Saponea

**La Capannina**

Close to the railway station, with outdoor seating on a neo-classical piazza. Good antipasti and reliable Italian standards.

address: 13, Via Cairoli
