### [Santa Croce](/europe/italy/puglia/lecce/sights/santacroce)

The most exuberant example of Leccese baroque. Fantastic monsters amd caryatids adorn the facade. Inside the altar by Zimbalo sr. is wonderful.

### [Duomo](/europe/italy/puglia/lecce/sights/duomo)

The cathedral was completed in 1670 and was designed by Giuseppe Zimbalo one of the most famous architects of the Lecce style. The interiro mostly dates from the 18-th century.
