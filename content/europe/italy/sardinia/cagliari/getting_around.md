title: "Getting Around"
type: section
---

The old city centre is small enough to cover on foot. The main areas — Marina, Castello, Stampace, Villanova — are all within walking distance of each other, though Castello involves a climb.

**Bus**

CTM operates the city bus network. Line PF and Line M run to Poetto Beach. Line 1 and 5 cover the main city routes. Tickets can be bought at tabaccherie and validated on board. Day passes are available.

**Taxi**

Taxis are available at the airport, at the port, and at ranks throughout the centre. They can be hailed on the street but it's easier to call or use the RadioTaxi app.

**Car**

A car is not needed to explore central Cagliari and is a liability for parking. If you plan day trips to Tharros, the Costa Verde, or [Su Nuraxi](/europe/italy/sardinia/sunuraxi), rent one for those specific days.
