---
title: "Getting Around in Cagliari"
type: section
---

If you want to find the best **Cagliari transportation**, you can try a few different options. Some of the most popular ways to travel around the area include ferries, taxis and buses.

#### Ferries

Ferries are one of the ways you can get around from one side of the city to another or to take to another part of the island. You can board the ferries at the Ancient port and choose the route you want. The ferries are affordable, and they are able to seat a lot of people. You can find out from the port when the ferries depart and which one you should board.

#### Taxis

You can use the taxi services available in the city to go around to some of the historical sites or to the center of the town. The taxi services are affordable, and it is one of the methods of Cagliari transportation. Take a taxi from your hotel or the airport. There are usually taxis available along the streets by the main restaurants and hotels.

#### Buses

The bus service in the city is affordable and makes many stops along the main streets. It can be full, so you want to arrive at the bus station or any of the stops early to make sure that you are going to catch it. The routes are clearly marked, so once you know which route you need to take, it is easy to find and use to travel.
