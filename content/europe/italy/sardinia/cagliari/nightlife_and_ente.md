---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment in Cagliari"
type: section
---

To get the best out of your vacation here, you must engage in the **Cagliari nightlife**; it is the best on the island. You will find that the majority of the bars and clubs are located in the older districts, such as the Marina, Stampace and Castello Districts.

**Fiera Campinaria
**
This large staging area is the home to large rock concerts. These concerts happen throughout all the year and draw some big name international bands. There are also big name Italian bands that are filling the stage the rest of the time. This stage is the most widely used for live rock concerts in the area.
**
Exma
**
A huge complex just located outside of the city is where you will find a year round schedule of big name rock bands playing here. When the complex is not being used by rock groups, the place serves as an open air arena for jazz and other types of musical concerts and festivals.

**Castello District

**This district of Cagliari has a number of popular bars and clubs that have live music. A large a mount of the clubs here flow around the Piazza Yenne. The clubs here, such as Nostrum Libarium and the Bastion San Remy, are open year round but they attract more folks during the winter months. In the winter, the bars like De Candia can get so crowded it is hard to determine who are natives and who are visitors.

**Stampace District

**Going west of the Piazza del Carmine, you can find a number of attractive places to hang out at during the nighttime hours. Weekends here tend to get quite rowdy due to the fact that many clubs and bars sport live rock and disco music. Every so often you can even come across a 'rave' among the younger crowds. One of the more familiar bars is the Donegal; they provide live music, disco and karaoke. Hancock is a more rustic place that has rock and jazz concerts. Movida is also a highly frequented disco club.

**Marina District
**
Of all the districts, the Marina tends to be the most wild. During the weekends, it has a let it all hang out, partying mentality. Discos and rock clubs pepper all along the water front. Many music bars are situated along the famous Via Roma. It is here that you will find the disco club named Go Fish, which is the only bar that is proclaimed openly to be 'gay' in the whole city.
