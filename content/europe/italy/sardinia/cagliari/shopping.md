---
title: "Shopping in Cagliari"
type: section
---

Like most large Italian cities, there is a great wealth of shops in Cagliari, and indulging in some **Cagliari shopping** will be a satisfying experience. This city is situated on the coast of Sardinia, which allows it to be a perfect port to receive all kinds of imports. The same type of set up, like small shops and boutiques, is found in Cagliari, as it is in other Italian cities. There are several sections of the city that are more suited to specific types of merchandise being sold. No matter what amount of money you have to spend here, there is always a bargain at hand, plus a great time waiting for you.

#### Shopping on the Via Roma

Shopping opportunities are mostly in the Old City District of the city. The Via Roma is the best known portion of the district to go shopping. All along the venue, there are boutiques and shaded arcades that will offer a pleasant diversity of merchandise for you to buy. You can find ceramic pottery, hand carved items, and the much sought after gold filigree here. Shops stock many of the finest labels in fine clothing, shoes and accessories. Also, the La Rimascente, which is the main department store, will be found here at the very beginning of the street.

**La Rimascente Shooping Mall**

La Rimascente is the first major store you encounter if you are coming off a cruise line. It is the beginning of the Via Roma. The two story department store has generic and specialty stores where you can basically buy anything you need. The stores sell books, clothing, electronics, kid's merchandise and traveling items. It's specialty is women's clothing, jewelry, lingerie and accessories stores. Isola is a wonderful store to check out if you like Sarinian crafts; there is a wide range of products there that will bring a smile to even the most sceptical of visitors. If you are interested in the best crafted jewlery on sale, go and check out the Atillo Della Maria, which is the oldest jewelry store in the city.

#### Via Garibaldi and Via Alghero

A little farther away from the center of the historic district, you will come across Via Garibaldi. This street for shopping is nothing short of outstanding. On both sides of the street, you can find all types of specialty boutiques and shops. The merchandise ranges from books, footwear, souvenirs, clothing , sunglasses and all other trendy styles of products. Newstands and cafes are sprinkled among the shops, which are nice for a rest period. One of the main streets turning off of Via Garibaldi is the Via Alghero. Via Alghero is the place to go for buying the most expensive and newest suits and evening wear. This venue is without a doubt devoted to high-end shopping.
