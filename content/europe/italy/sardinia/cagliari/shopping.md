---
title: "Shopping"
type: section
---

Cagliari's main shopping street is Via Roma, which runs along the port under wide arcades. Here you'll find a mix of clothing boutiques, souvenir shops, and the La Rinascente department store at the beginning of the street — a good place to find Sardinian food products (bottarga, saffron, local wines) in a well-presented format. The Isola shop within La Rinascente carries a curated selection of Sardinian crafts, including ceramics, textiles, and cork items.

For more local shopping, Via Garibaldi (running inland from Via Roma) has a range of boutiques on both sides. Via Alghero, branching off it, tends toward higher-end clothing and jewellery.

Sardinian jewellery, particularly gold filigree work in traditional designs, is a worthwhile buy. The oldest jewellery shop in the city is the Atilio Della Maria on Via Roma. Coral jewellery from Alghero is also widely sold, though quality varies.

The Mercato di San Benedetto on Via Cocco Ortu (a short walk from Via Roma) is the main food market — one of the largest covered markets in Italy. The fish hall on the lower floor is particularly impressive in the morning.
