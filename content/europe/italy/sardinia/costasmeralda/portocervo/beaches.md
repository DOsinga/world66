---
title: "Beaches in Porto Cervo"
type: section
---

**Porto Cervo beaches** provide tourists a chance to have one of
the best seaside holidays possible. Porto Cervo is a seaside resort that
oozes luxury and entertainment. Here are a few recommendations for you.

**Colonna Beach Hotel and Resort**

The Colonna Beach Hotel and Resort is definitely a place full of
extravagance and luxury. This magnificent five-star beach resort is
situated along the Cala Granu Peninsula. It is surrounded by beautiful
gardens, man-made waterfalls and Mediterranean-style architecture. You
can enjoy Europe’s largest open seawater pool and Colonna’s first-class
facilities and amenities at this beach resort. This marvelous white sand
beach is a perfect place to enjoy a picnic.

**L’ea Bianca Luxury Resort**

This is a five-star luxury resort situated along the coast of Cala
dei Ginepri Bay. The magnificent beach at L’ea Bianca Luxury Resort is
one of the jewels here in Porto Cervo. The best part is the beautiful
white sand beach and crystal clear water make for a perfect day at the
beach.

**Nikki Beach**

If you want to mingle with the world’s jetsetters while soaking up
under the sun, then Nikki Beach is the place to go. This is one of
Sardinia’s best nightspots and perhaps one of the best in the world.
Nikki Beach is where most of the rich and famous stay and party every
night. From here you can enjoy the crystal clear waters of Cala Granu
Beach, as well as the sumptuous food offerings of Nikki Beach's
restaurant and sushi bar.

**Phi Beach**

This is one of the newest beach-bar destinations in Porto Cervo and
is fast rising to prominence. This place serves as a private beach in
the morning and transforms into the hottest beach club in the area as
soon as the sun sets. If you are looking for non-stop entertainment, Phi
Beach is the place to be.

**Marina di Porto Cervo **

Marina dei Porto Cervo is one of the most beautiful sheltered harbors
around Porto Cervo. It is situated within the Marina Village and has
700 berths that have electricity and water supply. If you own a boat you
might want to berth it here and enjoy the view and the entertainment
that takes place here. Even if you do not own a boat, you can still come
here and have a good time.
