---
title: "Beaches"
type: section
---

The beaches around Porto Cervo are among the most beautiful on the island. The water is a clear, deep turquoise and the sand is white and fine. The downside is that in July and August most beaches are extremely busy.

**Spiaggia del Pevero**

About 3 km south of Porto Cervo, a long sweep of white sand on a sheltered bay. Backed by pine trees. Has sun-lounger hire and a beach bar in season. Gets crowded.

**Cala Granu**

A smaller, intimate cove just south of the marina. The water is shallow and very clear, good for swimming. Easier to reach on foot from the marina.

**Liscia Ruja**

6 km from Porto Cervo, one of the longest beaches in the Costa Smeralda area. Several beach clubs operate here in summer. Worth the short drive.
