The city of Porto Cervo is home to many of the fun food and wine festivals that you can find when you are looking for **Porto Cervo festivals**. This city is host to some of the best festivals for showcasing wines and various types of cuisine that you can enjoy if you are visiting the city during the time the festivals are held.

#### The Porto Cervo Wine Festival

The Porto Cervo wine festival is held in May and offers everyone who participates some of the best wine in the world. Wine makers from all over Italy come to the city to showcase their specialty wines, and you can find a number of new and different blends that you can sample. If you are a fan of wine, this is one of the best times to travel to the city.

#### The Porto Cervo Food Festival

The Porto Cervo food festival is held in September and is one of the most varied festivals that offers a variety of cuisine from all over. You can sample traditional Italian dishes from some of the most well known chefs in the region and also be able to try a number of dishes. People come to enjoy the tastes and fun activities that are held during this festival, and if you enjoy trying new cuisine, this is one of the Porto Cervo festivals that you may want to explore.

#### Yacht Race in Porto Cervo

If you are a fan of yachts, you should check out the yacht racing festival that is held in mid-May in Porto Cervo. This is one of the most exclusive sailing events that is held in the country, and you can find a number of great spots to observe the yachts as they set sail to see who is the fastest. It is a fun event that you can enjoy outside, and there are also many other events that take place around the yacht festival. You can find plenty of activities to participate in if you choose this as one of the Porto Cervo festivals to attend.

#### Sardinia Cup Festival

This festival is held in September and is the last major sailing competition of the season. You can enjoy the different kinds of boats and the competition as many people come to the area to celebrate. There are plenty of events and festivities going on in Porto Cervo during this festival, and many places to see and enjoy while you are staying in the city.
