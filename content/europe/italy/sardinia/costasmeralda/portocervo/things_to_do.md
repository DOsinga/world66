---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Porto Cervo's main activity is the sea and the marina. Walking through the Piazzetta and along the old port is the classic introduction to the town. The Church of Stella Maris above the harbour is worth a visit for the El Greco attribution. Beyond the town, the beaches of the Costa Smeralda — Liscia Ruja, Capriccioli, Cala Granu — are the primary draw, with boat hire and diving widely available.
