Porto Rotondo is a resort village on the Costa Smeralda, a few kilometres south of Porto Cervo and developed around the same time in the 1960s by the Donà dalle Rose brothers. It centres on a small harbour and a piazza lined with shops and restaurants, and has a quieter, slightly more low-key feel than its more famous neighbour. The surrounding beaches, particularly Ira and Porto Rotondo beach, are clean and less crowded than those at Porto Cervo.
