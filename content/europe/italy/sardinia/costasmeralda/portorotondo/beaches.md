**Marinella Beach**

The largest beach near Porto Rotondo, 4 km north. Fine white sand stretching over 1 km, with water sports hire (windsurfing, sailing, rubber dinghies), a diving centre, restaurant, and bar. Good for active beach-goers; relatively quiet compared to the Costa Smeralda's most famous beaches.

**Spiaggia Ira (Punta Nuraghe)**

About 1 km from the centre, on the point that separates the Porto Rotondo Gulf from the Gulf of Cugnana. Fine white sand with some rocky outcrops, calm clear water, good for families. Popular with a well-heeled crowd. Has a beach bar and restaurant.

**Spiaggia Punta Volpe**

A quieter beach just outside town, with white sand between Mediterranean scrub and a view over the islands of Soffi and Mortorio.
