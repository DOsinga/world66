**Porto Rotondo Budget Travel Options**

**Porto Rotondo budget travel** options include walking around the village to local attractions and lodging in low-budget hotels to cut travel costs. Porto Rotondo in Esmeralda is a favorite vacation spot for engaging in waterfront activities like swimming, sailing waterskiing and scuba diving.

#### **Budget Hotel**

Li Tauli Hotel on the Emerald Coast is the closest hotel to Porto Rotondo and one of the least expensive. It costs only about $44 per night, and the hotel offers comfortable rooms and a great scenic view of the Gulf of Cugnana. It provides a free parking space for guests and is near the popular Rena Bianca Beach. Other nearby hotels cost from $62 to $150 per night, which makes Li Tauli a real budget deal. If you want a more spacious place, the hotel also provides villas or suites for those who want to splurge for their vacation. You can choose budget meals or expensive meals offered to hotel guests.

#### **Cheap Transportation**

The cheapest transportation is to use the buses. Taxicabs and chauffeured cars are expensive. One good option is to rent a bike to tour the surrounding area. You should be an excellent biker, though, to avoid accidents along the way. Biking through the village of Porto Rotondo would also allow you to enjoy the pristine mountainside and the refreshing sea breeze. Rena Bianca is a wonderful beach and is worth a visit. You can tour with a group to cut costs and earn discounts for group purchases. If you are with a group, hiring a car with sufficient seating capacity may prove cheaper.
