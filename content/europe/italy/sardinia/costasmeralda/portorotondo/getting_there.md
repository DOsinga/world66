---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

Porto Rotondo is 30 km from [Olbia](/europe/italy/sardinia/olbia) airport. A hire car is the practical option — the drive takes about 30 minutes on the SS125. Taxis from Olbia airport are available but expensive.

There is no bus service directly to Porto Rotondo; the nearest bus stop on the main Olbia–Arzachena route is a few kilometres from the village.

By sea, the marina accepts visiting yachts and there are some summer ferry connections from Olbia.
