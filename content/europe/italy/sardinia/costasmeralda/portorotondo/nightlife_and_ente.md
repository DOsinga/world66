---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment in Porto Rotondo"
type: section
---

After a tiring day, the best way to unwind in Porto Rotondo is to
enjoy its nightlife. This **Porto Rotondo nightlife** guide provides
tourists with information on some of the best places to visit for
entertaining and fun-filled evenings in the village.

**MCLUB

**Also
known as Mantra, MCLUB is one of the most popular nightclubs in Porto
Rotondo. Visitors can easily reach this club on foot from the marina or
city center. The club offers different kinds of entertainment to
visitors. There is a disco room for those who want to shake a leg. This
section has a bar where visitors can choose from a range of alcohols.
MCLUB also has a piano bar for those who are looking for a quieter place
to spend the evening. Visitors looking for absolutely no noise, but
just some good drinks and food, can relax at the furnished area in the
outdoor garden.

**Bar Darsena

**Tourists who aren’t
looking to spend too much money on a night out in Porto Rotondo should
visit this place. Bar Darsena is more like a café, but it is open all
day and serves several cocktails and alcoholic drinks. The bar is
located in a square that provides a view of the harbor. Although it
doesn’t offer a stunning view of the sea, it is relatively quiet and
ideal for those who want to stay away from crowds. The bar is mostly
open from mid May to September, making it a popular hangout for budget
conscious tourists.

**Country Club

**Located around 8
km from the center of Porto Rotondo is Country Club, another great place
to spend the evening. The location of this nightclub is picturesque as
overlooks the hills of Porto Rotondo. Whether you are looking to dance
away the night or simply want to sit back and enjoy music and a few
drinks, the Country Club is a good choice. The club also offers a range
of traditional dishes and gets full in the summer season when tourists
visit the village.

**Heaven Disco Club

**Another great
place to spend the night dancing is Heaven Disco Club, which is located
at Piazza Gabriele Sandri. This is one of the most popular places to be,
especially from May to September, when tourists flock to the village.
Heaven Disco Club is usually filled with those who want to dance till
the wee hours, but it also offers a wonderful place to sit down, have a
few drinks and enjoy different genres of music. The club changes its
offerings from time to time to keep up with the partying needs of
tourists. This makes it one of the best places to visit in Porto
Rotondo.
