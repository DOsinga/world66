---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment"
type: section
---

Porto Rotondo's nightlife is seasonal (May–September) and aimed at the wealthy Italian summer crowd. Things are busiest in July and August.

**MCLUB (Mantra)**

The main club, within walking distance of the marina. Has a disco room, a piano bar, and an outdoor garden area. One of the more accessible options on the Costa Smeralda circuit.

**Bar Darsena**

A café-bar on a square overlooking the harbour. Open all day; serves cocktails and light food. A lower-key alternative to the clubs, open roughly May to September.

**Heaven Disco Club**

On Piazza Gabriele Sandri. Dance-focused, popular from May to September. Changes its programme regularly.
