---
title: "Shopping"
type: section
---

Porto Rotondo's shopping is concentrated around its three piazzas. It leans toward high-end Italian fashion — Versace, Prada, Miu Miu, and similar labels have outlets here.

**Piazza Quadra**

The main piazza at the heart of the village. Designer boutiques alongside smaller shops.

**Piazza Kasbah**

Reached through narrow lanes from the harbour. Has both boutiques and smaller independent shops.

**Piazza San Marco**

Right on the harbour, good for browsing between boat-watching.

Outside the high season (July–August), most shops close. The off-season is peaceful but there isn't much commercial life.
