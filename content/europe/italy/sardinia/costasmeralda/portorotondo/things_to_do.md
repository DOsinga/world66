---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Ira beach is the main town beach — its calm, clear water and fine sand make it typical of the Costa Smeralda at its best. Rena Bianca, a few kilometres away, is larger with windsurfing and diving facilities on site. Boat trips from the harbour to the La Maddalena archipelago and Tavolara Island (a limestone outcrop popular for scuba diving) are easily arranged. The Yacht Club Porto Rotondo hosts sailing events through the summer, including the Big Game deep-sea fishing competition.
