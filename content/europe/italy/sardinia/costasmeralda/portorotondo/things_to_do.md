---

Porto Rotondo is primarily a beach and sailing destination. The piazza and marina are the social hub; the beaches are the main attraction.

**Beaches**

Ira Beach (Punta Nuraghe, 1 km from the centre) is the most popular — fine white sand, clear water, good for families and snorkelling. Marinella Beach (4 km north) is larger and better for water sports including windsurfing, sailing, and diving. Punta Volpe, just outside town, is quieter and has views over the islands of Soffi and Mortorio.

**Yacht Club Porto Rotondo**

Founded in 1985, the yacht club hosts several events through the summer including the Big Game deep-sea fishing competition in August. Even without a boat, the marina is worth a walk in the evening.

**Boat trips**

Several operators offer day trips by boat from the marina to the [La Maddalena Archipelago](/europe/italy/sardinia/lamaddelena) and Tavolara Island. Half-day snorkelling trips to nearby coves are also available.
