title: "Things to Do in Porto Rotondo"
type: section
---

There are a number of interesting **Porto Rotondo things to do** for tourists who are holidaying in this spectacular beach destination. Listed below are some of the popular tourist activities.

******Ira Beach

**Porto Rotondo is known for its pristine beaches. Ira beach is popular among celebrities, so if you&rsquo;re looking to catch a glimpse of a celebrity, this is where you should go. But Ira beach offers more than just celebrities. It is known for its beautiful blue waters and fine white sand. If you&rsquo;re the adventurous kind, you can even hire jet skis, pedal boats and canoes, or go snorkeling. If you&rsquo;re not, then sunbathe on one of the loungers placed on the beach. There are bars and restaurants along the beach that serve refreshing drinks and good food.

******Tavolara Island

**Tourists looking for something more exciting can opt for a boat trip to Tavolara Island, which is located northeast of the coast of Sardinia. The island is 5 km long, and is made up of limestone. It is a popular haunt for scuba diving. Here divers can view an impressive range of flora and fauna, including bottlenose dolphins, coral, sea anemones, and sponges. On lucky days, divers may also be able to see some specimens of the rare giant clam called Pinna nobilis.

******Rena Bianca

**Rena Bianca is located just a few miles from Porto Rotondo. It is one of the most unique beaches in the stretch of all of them because it consists of four different small beaches, creeks and ponds. It has a low seabed and is ideal for tourists who want to go windsurfing, snorkeling and diving. Visitors can also find a diving center, windsurfing and sailing schools, and rubber dingy and sailboat hire on the beach.

******Boat Excursions

**Porto Rotondo charters a number of boats and yachts. You can hire one of these and take a boat trip to some of the surrounding areas like Puerto Cannigione. From here, you can visit the archipelago of the La Maddalena Islands. With a chartered boat or yacht, you can also visit other exclusive resorts near Porto Rotondo, like Baia Sardinia and Porto Cervo.

******Yacht Club Porto Rotondo

**The Yacht Club Porto Rotondo is one of the most important structures in this seaside town. Founded in 1985, this club hosts many events in Porto Rotondo. Most of these events take place during the summer season. One of the most prestigious events it holds is the Big Game. This is a sporting event that includes deep sea fishing and is famous in the Mediterranean. This and other events have made the Yacht Club Porto Rotondo one of the most important tourist attractions in Italy.
