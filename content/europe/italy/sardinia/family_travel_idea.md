The Italian island of Sardinia is the perfect vacation spot for people of all ages. Visitors both young and old flock to this island paradise for sun, sandy beaches and ocean breezes. Sardinia is also a great spot for parents and children with **Sardinia Family** **Travel** some of the best in Western Europe.

**Family Accommodation**

Sardinia has plenty of child-friendly and family-oriented accommodations available. Independent companies such as Families Sardinia work to locate the perfect place to stay suited to your wants and needs. They also go the extra mile by providing activities for children so the parents can have some much needed down-time. For an additional fee, qualified babysitters and childminders can be hired so parents can have a night out or day away on their own.

**Take a Trip on the Andrea Jensen**

Children and adults alike will adore this trip. The Andrea Jensen is a trip that will take you around the coast and marine reserve. The boat anchors, allowing visitors time to swim in the crystal clear waters and snorkel if they desire. A lunch is provided as well as ample space for sun bathing. The Andrea Jensen tour is perfect for families due to the fact that it offers something that appeals to all age groups. Dolphins quite often make an appearance but obviously this cannot be guaranteed.

**Learn to Dive at the Nautilus Diving Center**

Learn to dive and take full advantage of the beaches and coves of Sardinia. The Nautilus diving center is one of the best training facilities in Europe. They offer high quality diving instruction to people of all ages. Those with dive experience or just beginning will find they have lots to learn from the instructors at Nautilus. The Nautilus diving center offers courses of varying length including PADI certification.
