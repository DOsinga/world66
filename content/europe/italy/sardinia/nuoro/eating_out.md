---
title: "Eating Out"
type: section
---

Nuoro sits in the Barbagia, and the food here is inland Sardinian — roast meats, porceddu (suckling pig slow-roasted over myrtle wood), handmade pasta such as malloreddus and culurgiones, and local cheeses like pecorino sardo. Fish barely features. Look for restaurants advertising cucina barbaricina, the traditional mountain cuisine of the interior.
