---
title: "Bars and Cafes"
type: section
---

Olbia's bar scene centres on Corso Umberto and the surrounding streets. Italian-style cafes serving espresso, cornetti, and aperitivo are the norm. In the evening, bars along the main corso fill up with a more local than tourist crowd, which makes the atmosphere enjoyable. The scene gets livelier in summer when visitors to the Costa Smeralda use Olbia as a base.
