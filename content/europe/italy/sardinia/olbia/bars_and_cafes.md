---
title: "Bars and Cafes"
type: section
---

Olbia has a decent bar scene centred on the Corso Umberto and the side streets around it. The evening aperitivo culture is alive here — from around 7pm, bars put out snacks and the outdoor tables fill up.

**Wine Bar Dioniso**

Via Aldo Moro, Olbia. A good spot for wine by the glass with a focus on Sardinian labels, plus light snacks. Not the most visible place but worth finding.

**Glitter Café**

Via Piro C., in the historic centre. A polished bar open from breakfast through to late night (3am at weekends). Good for breakfast, aperitivo, or an after-dinner drink.

**Bar Muzzetto di Demontis**

Corso Umberto. A traditional bar on the main street, good for coffee and pastries in the morning. Convenient for an early start before a ferry.
