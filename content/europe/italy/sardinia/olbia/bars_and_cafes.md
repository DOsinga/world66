---
title: "Bars and Cafes in Olbia"
type: section
---

Located in the northeast of Sardinia, Italy, Olbia is a
popular tourist destination. The beautiful sandy beaches and friendly people
have made it one of the most popular places to visit. This is the main link
between Sardinia and the Italian peninsula. Olbia has an exciting array of bars
and clubs spread all over the town, offering its visitors a chance to experience
its unique nightlife.

**Wine Bar Dioniso **
Aldo Moro Road, Olbia

Wine Bar Dioniso is one of Olbia’s cafés located in the
center of town. Step in here to enjoy some great coffee or outstanding
wine. The café is a good place to hang out and enjoy the evening listening to good music over a drink. The café also has tasty food available. Although the café is not a very popular place, the drinks and food is
outstanding.

**Glitter Café **
Piro C. Road, Olbia

Glitter Café is one of Olbia’s new generation cafés, located
in the historical center of Olbia. This is an elegant styled café, offering a sophisticated
image to its clients. The café is open every day from 7:30 a.m. to 12 midnight
and during the weekends up to 3:00 a.m. You can enjoy a great breakfast, brunch,
lunch, aperitif, or dinner at this fine café. The café features various activities
in the evening during the weekend.

**Bar Trattoria
Magna' E Bi' **
Copenaghen Road, Olbia

Located in the heart of Olbia and a few minutes from the
beautiful coast “Smeralda”, this bar is a popular place for many visiting
Olbia. You can enjoy an evening over a few
drinks from a wide selection of the best wine from Italy and around the world.
This is a great place for an evening out with your family or friends.

**Bar Muzzetto Di
Demontis **
Corso Umberto, Olbia

Bar Muzzetto is located near the waterfront and is a great
place to enjoy hot coffee and mouthwatering pastries. The café provides
its clients a comfortable ambiance with excellent service. The bar is not very
popular, but is one of those places where you can relax and enjoy the evening.

**Bar L'Angolo Di
Marras Giovanni **
D'Annunzio Gabriele Road, Olbia

Nestled in the center of Olbia, this coffee bar is a good
place to stop at while touring the town. The bar offers some good food, including
hot and cold beverages. Bar L’Angolo De Marras Giovanni is one of the authentic
Olbia bars in town.
