The coast around Olbia has several good beaches within easy reach of town.

**Pittulongu**

The closest beach to Olbia, 6 km north on the SP82, reachable by city bus. A curved bay of fine white sand with shallow, calm water — good for families. Has beach bars, sun-lounger hire, and water sports including windsurfing and canoeing.

**Porto Istana**

About 12 km southeast of town. Actually four small beaches separated by low rocky outcrops, facing the protected waters of the Tavolara marine zone. Shallow and good for swimming; popular with snorkellers.

**Bados**

Along the road toward Golfo Aranci, with several beachfront restaurants and hotels. Calm, shallow water, good for families.

**Le Saline**

A wider, more exposed beach with stronger currents and a steeper drop. Popular with windsurfers and kite-surfers for exactly those reasons. Not suitable for young children.
