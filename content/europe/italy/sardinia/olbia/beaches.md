---
title: "Beaches"
type: section
---

The closest beach to town is **Pittulongu**, 5km north on SP82, a crescent of fine white sand with sun beds, water sports rental, and easy bus connections. **Porto Istana**, 15km north, is a string of four beaches separated by rocky outcrops opposite the Tavolara marine zone — excellent for snorkelling and diving. For something wilder, **Le Saline** has strong currents and attracts windsurfers and kite-surfers. The beach at **Bados**, just north of Golfo Aranci, is shallow and family-friendly.

For the best beaches in the area, the Costa Smeralda strip north of town is unmatched but more accessible with a car.
