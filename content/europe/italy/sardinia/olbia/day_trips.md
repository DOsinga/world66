---
title: "Day Trips"
type: section
---

The **Maddalena Archipelago**, accessible by ferry from Palau (40km north), is one of the most beautiful stretches of coast in the Mediterranean: a national park of small granite islands with clear turquoise water. La Maddalena and Caprera are the main islands.

**Arzachena**, 25km north, is the base for prehistoric Nuragic monuments: the Giants' Tombs of Li Loghi and Coddu Vecchiu, and the circular necropolis of Li Muri, are all within a short drive. **Tavolara Island**, 10km offshore from Olbia, is a massive limestone rock with excellent snorkelling and a tiny restaurant; it's reachable by summer ferry from Porto San Paolo.
