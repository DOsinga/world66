---
title: "Getting Around"
type: section
---

Central Olbia is small and walkable. From the ferry port or train station, the old town and Corso Umberto are within ten minutes on foot.

City buses (lines 2 and 10) serve the airport and the nearby beaches at Pittulongu and Porto Istana. Car rental desks are at both the airport and the port — useful if you're heading out to the [Costa Smeralda](/europe/italy/sardinia/costasmeralda) or [La Maddalena](/europe/italy/sardinia/lamaddelena). Taxis are available at the airport, port, and main piazzas.
