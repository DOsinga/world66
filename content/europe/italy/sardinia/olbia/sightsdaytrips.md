The area north of Olbia has excellent day-trip options, ranging from prehistoric archaeology to the yacht-club glamour of the Costa Smeralda.

**Nearby beaches**

Pittulongu, 6 km from town, is the closest decent beach — a half-moon of white sand with calm water and good facilities. Porto Istana (12 km) is a more sheltered option, popular with families. Both are reachable by city bus.

**Tavolara**

A massive 566-metre limestone table rising from the sea 15 km east of Olbia, accessible by boat from Porto San Paolo. Boat trips run in summer; snorkelling and diving in the marine reserve below the rock are excellent.
