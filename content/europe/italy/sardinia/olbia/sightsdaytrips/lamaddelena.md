Only km. 3 by sea from [Palau](/australiaandpacific/palau). In the islands of this archipelago, which are considered a national park since 1996, we can find enchanting beaches, with a landscape of extraordinary suggestiness, that are unforgettable because of their beauty and variety. The most famous are that of La Maddalena, the biggest one; S. Maria; Spargi; Budelli with its pink beach and the already mentioned Caprera.
