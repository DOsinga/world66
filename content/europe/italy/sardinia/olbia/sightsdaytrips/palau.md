title: "{Palau}"
type: location
---

About 40 km from Olbia, Palau is well-known because of its patronized harbour which is connected with the Maddalena Archipelago. This small town is chatacterized by its beautiful beaches and the holiday places of its surroundings (Porto Raphael). It is really renown the peculiar Rock of the Bear, which has the shape of a plantigrade.
