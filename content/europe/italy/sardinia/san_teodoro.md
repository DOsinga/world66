title: "San Teodoro Travel Guide"
type: location

With 36 Kilometers coast, San Teodoro is one of the most famous places of Sardinia most of all for its wonderful beaches, in no way inferior to the most celebrated beach

In summer (and particularly in August) beaches are crowded but in spite of everything, they don't lose any charm thanks to the clearness of the sea, the rose-coloured rocks and the special white color of sand.
