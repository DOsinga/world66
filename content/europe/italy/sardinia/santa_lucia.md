The tiny picturesque fishing village of Santa Lucia is in a stunning
location in the gulf of Orosei. The village is surrounded by thick pine
woods, a long broad, white beach and the azure blue sea. Great bay for
an active DIY sailing holiday: Camping Selema lies directly on
this sandy beach only 300 meters from the village centre. The yachts,
catamarans, surfboards, sports and games material are all on the beach
ready for use. Enthusiastic instructors give daily sailing instruction
in yachts or catamarans to beginners and experienced sailors.
