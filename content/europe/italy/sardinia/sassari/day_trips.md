**Santa Trinita di Saccargia**

A pisan cathedral some 15 km on the road to [Olbia](/europe/italy/sardinia/olbia). Built in 1116 on the spot where the wife of a local judge was visited by the virgin who told her she was pregnant (the judges wife, not the virgin). The church is in an excellent shape and is well worth the detour.  [more..](/europe/italy/sardinia/sassari/daytrips/santatrinitadisaccargia)
