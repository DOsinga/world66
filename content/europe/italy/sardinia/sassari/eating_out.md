---
title: "Eating Out"
type: section
---

Sassari has a good selection of restaurants without the tourist premium of coastal resorts. The old town and the streets around Piazza d'Italia are the main areas to look.

**Da Gesuino**

17, Via Torres. A reliable choice for Sardinian cooking — the sort of place the locals go, with simple, honest food and reasonable prices. Good for a hearty lunch.
