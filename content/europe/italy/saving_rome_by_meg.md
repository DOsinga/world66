A collection of funny and touching stories about life in modern Rome. Many focus on expat women, but unlike much of this genre, Megan Williams doesn't romanticise Italy. *Saving Rome* is an honest, sometimes chaotic portrait of the city — the bureaucracy, the noise, the beauty, the frustration — and a good read for anyone wanting to know what living there is actually like rather than what the guidebooks say.
