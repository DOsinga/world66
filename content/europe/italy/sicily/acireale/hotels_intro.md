As a visitor to this destination, you will want to stay at one of the several **Acireale Hotels **that** **offers outstanding accommodations. You can find deluxe hotels as well as cheap budget hotels to enjoy your holiday breaks. All the hotels located at Acireale are known for homey services. Online booking of hotels in Acireale is an excellent way to save time and money. Various luxury villas are turned into hotels in Acireale to provide comfort to traveler. They offer world-class accommodation facilities to meet their needs. Tourists can book hotel rooms depending upon the budget. This coastal town has plenty of hotels and nice restaurants. Some hotels in Acireale are Dimsiway Hotel, Quality Hotel Excelsior Terme, B & B Casale Mediterraneo, Casamicciola and Hotel Santa Tecla Palace.

**Five-star Hotels**

Grande Albergo Maugeri, Hotel Aloha D&rsquo;Oro, Park Hotel Capomulini, Marina Palace Hotel and Congress Hall are some of the most prestigious hotels in Acireale. The rooms of these hotels are spacious and classy. You can visit different tourist places while staying there. Hotel employees are very friendly and are always ready to assist you. Ample car parking space is available here. All the rooms are equipped with Internet and Wi-Fi. These hotels also have breakfast bars.

**Three-star Hotels**

Three-star hotels are slightly less expensive than the five-star accommodations. They are also equipped with necessary amenities for a moderately luxurious stay. These hotels offer facilities like 24-hour room service, meeting rooms, bars, dining rooms, fitness centers, Internet access within the room, and cable. For additional amusement tourists can find sun loungers, bars and several entertainment facilities at the three-star hotels like Hotel Delle Terme, Antica Conceria, Palazzo Giovanni and Quality Hotel Excel Sior Terme.

**Villas **

Beautiful villas overlooking Mt. Etna make great staying places for tourists in Acireale. Single and double rooms are available which you can pick according to your requirements. Separate villas are also available for six or eight people. Villa Le Agavi and Acireale Vacation Villa are two of them.

**Resorts**

Lovely resorts nestled on the cliffs or near Mt Etna are preferred by tourists who are deeply in love with nature. Panorama Village and Camping and Camping La Timpa are popular resorts.

**Bed and Breakfasts**

For travelers looking for cheap and quality accommodations, the bed and breakfast inns make a great option. Bed and Breakfast Acireale Mare and Savoia Residence are two such accommodations.
