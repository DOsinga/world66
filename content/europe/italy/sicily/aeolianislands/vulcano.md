---
title: "Vulcano"
type: location
---

Vulcano is famous for its hot springs. They are located within easy walking distance from the harbour. If you have your swimming trukns with you you can just dip in immediately. The water is nice and warm, and it's free to go in.

You can also walk up to the crater where you will see many places where smoke comes out of the ground. Smell of sulphur is all around you. 

You can visit Vulcano as a daytrip from [Lipari](/europe/italy/sicily/aeolianislands/lipari) - there is no real infrastructure for staying on the island.
