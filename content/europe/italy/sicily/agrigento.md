Agrigento is a small town on the south coast of Sicily. The beach is good and the small streets of the old town are charming, but what brings you here are the Greek temples just outside of town.

The Valley of the Temples is one of the most extraordinary archaeological sites in the Mediterranean. The temples date from the 5th century BC, when Akragas was one of the wealthiest cities in the Greek world. The Temple of Concordia, glowing golden in the late afternoon light, is among the best-preserved Greek temples anywhere.
