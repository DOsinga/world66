The city of Cefalu that lies on the northern coast of Sicily in Italy has a plethora of **Cefalu Hotels**. Millions of tourists visit annually to enjoy this city's art, architecture, lively nightlife, restaurants and shopping. Thus, you will be able to find all types of accommodation in Cefalu. Here is a list of different kinds of accommodation options available in the city of Cefalu.

**Luxury Accommodation**

Cefalu has many 5-star accommodations on offer. However, be prepared to find this luxury in restored mansions and cottages, rather than skyscrapers. The Relais Santa Anastasia hotel is set in a restored Abbey and thus is charming. A room here would cost anything between $250 and $400, which includes every possible luxurious facility and amenity.

**Boutique Hotels**

Baddeck houses a good number of boutique hotels, all of which have been decorated in a very unique and tasteful way. The Art Hotel Atelier sul Mare is the most famous of the lot. The name itself suggests that it is very close to the sea. The most noteworthy feature of this hotel is the presence of contemporary artworks by renowned artists. The art rooms cost a bit more at $290. Further, this boutique hotel offers classes for pottery and courses to learn Italian and Italian food and wine. All in all, one can be sure of enjoying a well-rounded stay at one of Cefalu's many boutique hotels.

**4-star Hotels**

Baddeck also has many 4-star accommodations and not all of them are business hotels. Alberi del Paradiso is a good choice if you don't mind shelling out $100 for a room. This hotel in particular has retained the old-world Sicilian charm, complete with typical Sicilian ceramics. The hotel has a great restaurant set in ambient tents. Rooms are equipped with modern amenities and other facilities include use of jacuzzi, swimming pool, solarium, fitness centre and tennis court.

**Vacation Rentals**

If you are looking to enjoy Cefalu on a more personal level and maybe if you are traveling with the family or a large group of people, then we suggest you opt for a rental accommodation. The 4-storied Casa Duomo lies at the very center of Cefalu and has a well-equipped kitchen, living room, 2 bedrooms and 2 bathrooms. There are many more cute villas that can be rented for a good amount of time in Cefalu. Usually, such a rented accommodation would cost you around $300 a week, which is very worth the price.
