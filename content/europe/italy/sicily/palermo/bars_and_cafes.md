---
title: 'Bars and Cafes'
type: section
---

Palermo's cafe culture runs deep. Whether you want a quick espresso standing at the bar, a leisurely granita con brioche for breakfast, or a late-night aperitivo, there is no shortage of options. Via Principe di Belmonte is the main pedestrian strip for evening drinks, and in summer the nightlife gravitates to Mondello beach.

For something different, try Kursaal Kalhesa, a bar-restaurant hidden behind a book lounge, or Caffe d'Oriente for Tunisian-Italian fusion in an Arabic-themed setting. The gelato at Al Gelato 2 near the stadium is some of the best in the city.
