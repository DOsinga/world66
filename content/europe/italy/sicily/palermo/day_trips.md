---
title: 'Day Trips'
type: section
---

[Monreale](/europe/italy/sicily/monreale) is the essential day trip, just 8km from Palermo by city bus. The Norman cathedral there has some of the most spectacular Byzantine mosaics in the world. [Cefalu](/europe/italy/sicily/cefalu) on the north coast is an easy train ride away and makes for a lovely day by the sea. For something more adventurous, [Erice](/europe/italy/sicily/erice) is a medieval hilltop village above Trapani with sweeping coastal views.
