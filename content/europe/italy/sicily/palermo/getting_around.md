---
title: 'Getting Around'
type: section
---

Walking is the best way to see central Palermo, though the distances between sights can add up. City buses cover every corner and stretch out to [Monreale](/europe/italy/sicily/monreale) and Mondello. Buy tickets from tabacchi shops or the booths outside Stazione Centrale. A single ticket is valid for 90 minutes, and day passes are available.
