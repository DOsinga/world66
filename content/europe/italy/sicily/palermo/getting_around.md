---
title: "Getting Around"
type: section
---

Getting around on foot in Palermo you will soon find impractical and exhausting. City buses are easy to use, covering each corner of Palermo and streching out to [Monreale](/europe/italy/sicily/monreale) and Mondello. There is a flat far of 0,77 Euro (valid for 60 minutes) or 2,58 Euro (valid a whole day). You can buy tickets from the glass booths outside Stazione Centrale, at the southern end of Viale delle Liberta, or from every tabacchi shop.
