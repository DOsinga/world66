Private chapel of Roger II, great mosaics all over the place. **A must see**.

Entrance is on the second story of [Palazzo dei Normanni](/europe/italy/sicily/palermo/sights/palazzodeinormanni). Capella Palatina is monument from Norman times, founded by Ruggero II in 1130. Basilican in form, it features a nave and two aisles. The floor is mosaic; the walls have marble facing. The nave has a magnificent wooden caisson ceiling (c.1143) and "muqarnas" painted with Kufic figures and inscriptions; the small side aisles have pitch roofs with painted beams.
