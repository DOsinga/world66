---
title: 'Getting There'
type: section
---

Lampedusa has a small airport with daily flights from [Palermo](/europe/italy/sicily/palermo), taking about 35 minutes, with connections to other Italian cities. By sea, a SIREMAR ferry runs from Porto Empedocle near [Agrigento](/europe/italy/sicily/agrigento), taking about 8 hours with a stop at Linosa. Book ahead if you want to take a car or get a cabin.
