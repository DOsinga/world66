---
title: 'Getting There'
type: section
---

Daily flights connect Lampedusa with [Palermo](/europe/italy/sicily/palermo) in about 35 minutes, with onward connections to other Italian cities. The SIREMAR ferry from Porto Empedocle near [Agrigento](/europe/italy/sicily/agrigento) takes about 8 hours and stops at Linosa along the way. Booking in advance is advisable, especially in summer, if you want a cabin or to bring a car.
