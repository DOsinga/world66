Taormina is so beautifully located that it would not need great sights to attract visitors. The fact that it does have them, makes it an absolute must.


 Perched on a rocky promonitory at the foot of the Mount Etna and looking out over the azure blue mediterranean the town has attracted visitors over the centuries. Main attraction are the remains of th greek city that was located at about the same spot as the present day town.
