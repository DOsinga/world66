---
title: 'Things to Do'
type: section
---

Sicily rewards the active traveller. Hike the summit craters of [Mount Etna](/europe/italy/sicily/mount_etna_park), Europe's tallest active volcano, or trek to the top of [Stromboli](/europe/italy/sicily/aeolianislands/stromboli) at night to watch red-hot lava light up the darkness. The Alcantara Gorges offer a dramatic canyon walk through basalt formations.

Beyond the volcanoes, Sicily is packed with ancient sites. The Valley of the Temples in [Agrigento](/europe/italy/sicily/agrigento) and the Roman mosaics at [Piazza Armerina](/europe/italy/sicily/piazza_armerina) are world-class. Diving and snorkelling are excellent around the Aeolian and Pelagian islands, and the wine regions around Etna and Marsala are increasingly popular for tastings and tours.
