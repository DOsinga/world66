The largest and most important museum in the Trentino region, housed in a medieval castle complex expanded during the Renaissance. The collection covers medieval arts, graphics, archaeology, wooden sculptures, and numismatics. The castle was the seat of the prince-bishops of Trento for over 400 years. Open Tuesday–Sunday, 10:00–18:00.
