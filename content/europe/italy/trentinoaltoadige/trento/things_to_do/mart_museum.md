Technically in nearby Rovereto (25 minutes from Trento by train), MART is one of Italy's finest modern art museums, with over 12,000 works including major holdings of Italian Futurism and 20th-century European painting. The building by architect Mario Botta, centred on a glass dome, is itself worth seeing. Open Tuesday–Sunday.
