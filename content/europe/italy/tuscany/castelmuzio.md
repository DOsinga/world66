Castelmuzio is a tiny medieval hamlet in the Crete Senesi, perched on a hilltop between [Montepulciano](/europe/italy/tuscany/montepulciano) and [Pienza](/europe/italy/tuscany/pienza). Fewer than a hundred people live inside its tight ring of stone houses, and the whole place can be walked end to end in five minutes. What it offers is the view — wheat fields, cypresses, and the soft clay hills rolling away in every direction — and the silence. It makes a good base for exploring the surrounding Val d'Orcia countryside by car.
