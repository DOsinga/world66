**By car**:

 the port of Piombino can be reached in a few minutes after leaving the Livorno-Grosseto highway at the SVincenzo or Venturina exits

**By train**:

 connection for the port of Piombino at Campiglia Marittima station on the Genoa-Rome line.

You can see in the map principal connection **by bus**, **by sea** and **by air**.
