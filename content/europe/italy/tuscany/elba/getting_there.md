---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

Elba is reached by ferry from **Piombino** on the Tuscan coast — around an hour's crossing to Portoferraio. Three companies (Toremar, Moby, Blu Navy) run frequent sailings year-round, with departures every 30 to 60 minutes in summer. Piombino is easily reached from the Livorno–Grosseto (SS1) highway (exits S. Vincenzo or Venturina), and by train via Campiglia Marittima on the main Genoa–Rome line, with a short branch line running down to the port. A small airport at **Marina di Campo** has seasonal flights from Pisa, Milan and a few German cities. In July and August book ferry tickets well ahead if you are bringing a car.
