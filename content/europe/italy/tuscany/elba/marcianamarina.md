Marciana Marina is a small beach resort that has grown around an old fishing village on the North side of Elba at the foot of the Capanne mountains.


 It has a small harbour that is used by fisherboats as well as tourist yachts. It is a good base for going to the small island of Capraia (22 miles) or the coastal region of Capo Corso (30 miles).
