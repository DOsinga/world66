Per's Bike Rides is a new company in Florence and offers tourists a chance to get out of the city's hustle and bustle. Their two main tours are** 1) The Grand Tuscan Villa and Wine tour that run Monday-Saturday, and 2) The Lazy Sunday Downhill tour that run on Sundays.** They also give 3-day tours around Tuscany and private tours that are tailored for the customer. All tours include bikes, guide, visit to wineries and meals. Call or e-mail to book.

The pick up point is at** 9.30 am at the south end of the Ponte alle Grazie bridge** in the center of Florence. People are shuttled in a van for 10 minutes to get out of the city to their starting point in the country side. Their single day tours return here around 3-4 pm.

The price for single day tours is 8**0 euro** and include everything. For pricing of 3-day tours and private tours, please visit the website.

They can also help with booking of hotel rooms.
