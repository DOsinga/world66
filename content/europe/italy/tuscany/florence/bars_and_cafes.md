**Laze Around in One of Florence's Wine Cafes **

**Florence bars and cafes** are the main attractions of this beautiful tourist destination in Tuscany, Italy. The locals give a lot of importance to their free time, and you will find people sipping on wine in the street side cafes all day long. This is a great place to relax, unwind and get away from the busy city life. Take a breather and settle into one of the bars or cafes that Florence has to offer. Read on to find about a cafe that will leave you spellbound and completely rejuvenated.
**
Le Volpi El'uva**
This cafe has been a part of Florence for the past 25 years. It is located in the center of Florence close to Ponte Vecchio. The working hours are from 11:00 a.m. to 9:00 p.m. The cafe is open Monday to Saturday but is closed on Sundays.

**Wines**
This cafe is dedicated to wines. It has a collection of several Italian as well as French wines. The wines are sourced from boutique wineries in Italy. Wine is served by the bottle or by the glass. You can enjoy your wines without having to worry about the prices. You will also be presented with a cheese platter, and the waiting staff will recommend the perfect match. Most of the time the wine is paired with cheese and finger food that best compliments the wine. Red, white, and dessert wines are all featured on the wine list here.
