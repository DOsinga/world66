title: "A Room with a View"
type: poi
author: "E.M. Forster"
isbn: "978-0141441825"
---

A young Englishwoman arrives in Florence and finds her carefully ordered life upended by the city and by a man who refuses to observe the rules. Forster wrote it after his own Italian travels in 1901–02 and the Florence sections — Santa Croce, the Piazza della Signoria, the Fiesole hills — are observed precisely. The novel is also a sharp comedy about English repression meeting Italian directness.
