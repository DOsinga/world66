A biographical novel about Michelangelo, from his apprenticeship under Ghirlandaio in Florence through the Sistine Chapel. Stone researched it for years and the Florence sections — the Medici court, the workshops, the political turbulence under Savonarola — are dense and convincing. Reading it before visiting the Bargello, the Accademia, or the Duomo makes those spaces come alive.
