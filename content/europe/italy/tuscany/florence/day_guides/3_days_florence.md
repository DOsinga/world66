A comprehensive 72-hour guide with a logical day-by-day structure designed for first-time visitors. Balances the unmissable (Accademia, Uffizi, Duomo) with neighbourhood exploration in Oltrarno and San Lorenzo, includes specific dining stops for every meal, and adds a scenic afternoon to San Miniato al Monte and Piazzale Michelangelo. Best for a full weekend with no prior Florence experience.
