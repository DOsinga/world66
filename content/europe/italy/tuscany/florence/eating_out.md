---
title: "Eating Out in Florence"
type: section
---

Florence, Italy is a city that is known as a tourist destination. There is plenty of romance in the air around this city so let&rsquo;s not forget the romantic foods to try. Below are some excellent restaurants that will fuel the romantic appetite as well as the food appetite.

**Il Latini**

In the center of the table at this restaurant you will find a nice big bottle of wine waiting for you to be enjoyed. There are also hundred of hams hanging over head. It is a spectacular feast never to be forgotten.

**Badiani**

This restaurant is famous for its Buonntalenti. Supposedly, the original recipe turned up with some old manuscripts that the owner of Badiani found. This tasty gelato recipe has never been successfully copied elsewhere.

**Cammillo Trattoria**

This is an Italian seafood restaurant that serves Tuscan home-style meals that are moderately priced. It is an excellent location for large parties such as for a wedding.

**Cibreo Teatro del Sale**

This is one of the cheapest destinations to go for great food due the one time club membership fees that are paid upon entrance. However, after a person gets through the door, everything inside, including food in wine, is served as an all you can eat buffet style meal for one flat fee. Near the end of the evening, about 9:30, the buffet dies down to a lovely evening performance on stage.

**Dolci & Dolcezze**

This lovely little bakery is found with just a short walk outside the tourist district. It has beautiful displays full of chocolates, tarts, and countless other pastries. Yummy cappuccino drinks are also served. It is an absolute dream for those with a sweet tooth!
