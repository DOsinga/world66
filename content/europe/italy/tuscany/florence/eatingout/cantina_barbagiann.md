The Restaurant **Cantina Barbagianni** is one of the most characteristic places in Florence, well known for its creative cuisine but also for its traditional Tuscan dishes. You can enjoy a delicious meal in the beautiful entrance room and the two medieval basements that offer an unforgettable atmosphere of Florence of the past.

The rich menu includes starters, first courses, main courses and desserts prepared with high quality ingredients. Here you can taste for example starters of Tuscan salami and crostini (toasted croutons with chicken-liver sauce), wild boar carpaccio with aromatic cheese mousse, Polenta mille-feuille with vegetables and fossa cheese, Litte fish balls on aubergines cream and vegetables julienne.

Cantina Barbagianni follows this up with Tagliatelle with white rabbit sauce perfumed with thyme, Tagliolini with brocoli pesto sauce and Colonnata lard, Risotto with goat's cheese, chive and mushrooms mousse, Saffron and leeks spaghetti with sea bass fillet. The traditional Tuscan cuisine is represented by pici (short thick spaghetti) proposed with little Chianti meat balls or pasta in venison sauce. Pasta is all fresh and home made.

The second courses are mainly meat based and include Chicken brest Florentine style (stuffed with ricotta cheese and spinach), Wild boar Maremma style, Ossobuco (braised veal shank slice) with green pepper and spring onions, duck salad with baked pears accompanied with orange and balsamic vinegar sauce and some classicals like Rossini fillets of steak (with foie gras and truffle) or the famous "Bistecca alla Fiorentina" (Florentine grilled sirloin steak).

We recommed the delicious home made chocolate tile cake with yoghurt for dessert but also an original Standing milk with blueberry sauce or a delicate Apples and walnuts little cake with rosemary cream.

Accompany your meal with one of the many good wines on the excellent wine listand round it off with a liqueur of good grappa.
