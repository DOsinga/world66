Florence is the capital of its historical center has an impressive number of architectural and artistic beauties many of which can be observed along the streets and squares of the city, for a visitor who does not know Florence is easy to get lost in the midst of so many masterpieces , it is useful to have a route to follow to optimize the time and not get lost among the many streets and historic squares. In this article we propose a logical route with a starting point and an end point, in which are included the most important places to visit in Florence:

1) Basilica of San Lorenzo, the Medici Chapels
and Palazzo Medici. From here we reach the religious centre of Florence or the Basilica of Santa Maria Novella with its monumental square
2) Cathedral of Florence and its dome, great example of the
architecture of the Renaissance, Giotto's Bell Tower, the Baptistry and the Gates of Paradise.
3) The Medieval area, that tells us the story of the Guelphs and Ghibellines, of Dante and Beatrice and of the tower houses. Orsanmichele Church, built for the Florentines by their corporations, the church was once also their barn.
4) Republic Square, the focal point of Florentia, the Roman city
5) New Market and Statue of Porcellino, little pig, a symbol of Florence in the world
6) Piazza della Signoria, the political center of the city, with its Palazzo della Signoria, the Loggia dei Lanzi and statues symbol of political power of the Medici: Perseus by Benvenuto Cellini, Giambologna's Rape of the Sabines, the Fountain of Neptune more commonly known as the “Big White” and the copy of the statue of the statue: Michelangelo's David, symbol of the rationality of the Florentine Republic.
7) Ponte Veccio or Old Bridge
8) Palazzo Pitti, following the path of the Vasari Corridor, the airway built by Grand Duke Cosimo I to move from the Palazzo della Signoria to the Palazzo Pitti without going out in the streets
9) Piazza and Church Santo Spirito with its essential Brunelleschi style
10) Small tower San Niccolò – along the River Arno
11) Monumental Bridge Santa Trinita
12) Piazza Santa Trinita, Via Tornabuoni, the exclusive shopping street of Florence
13) Palazzo Strozzi and Piazza Strozzi
