---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment in Florence"
type: section
---

**Florence Nightlife** should not be missed at any cost. This capital city of Italy is a great place to let your hair down and enjoy a wild night out. Read on to find out about the top party places in Florence.

**Space Electronic**

Space Electronic is one of the most posh and happening clubs in Florence. It is located in Via Palazuolo, and is open from 7:00 p.m. until way past midnight. This club is spread over two floors, and is the quintessential nightlife entertainment. The crowd at the club is primarily young people around 30 years of age. The second floor is dedicated to a karaoke bar and an American styled bar. The dance floor is on the first floor.

**Yab**

Yab has been a part of the Florence nightlife for a long time now. It is located at the center of the city and is easily accessible. This is not the most chic club in town, but the locals prefer it, as it is inexpensive and plays great music. The beer at this club is served chilled with complimentary accompaniments. This club is mostly visited by youngsters in their 20's, and is an all-time favorite among the locals. This dance club is the place to go to if you like to shake a leg to 80's music.
**
Full Up**

For a more relaxed and orderly partying option, step into Full Up. It is located in the city center, close to Santa Croce. This was initially a piano bar and today is a popular dance club. This club organizes several themed nights. The samba nights are a major hit with the locals at Full Up. The music varies between punk and revival. This club is apt for the post-college crowd.

**Universale**

For those of you who are under 22, Universale is the place to be in Florence. You can party the true Italian way at this highly sought-after nightclub located in Via Pisana. This building was originally a theater, and its opulence remains today. You can start off your evening at Universale by dining at the restaurant in the balcony. By 11:00 p.m. the live bands start performing and charge up the ambiance. The club stays open till 3:00 a.m.

**Sweet Sugar**

This nightclub is apt for all age groups. Located close to Via Pandolfini, Sweet Sugar is a great place to party in Florence. The large bar is a great place to lounge around and stir up interesting conversations. The dance floor is always busy, as the DJ plays popular American songs as well as Italian favorites. The nightclub also has a section dedicated to live performances.
