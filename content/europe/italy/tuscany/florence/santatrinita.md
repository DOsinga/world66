A Gothic church built in the early 14th century with a façade by Buontalenti (late 16th century). The highlight is the Sassetti Chapel, with Domenico Ghirlandaio's fresco cycle depicting "Scenes from the Life of St. Francis of Assisi" (1483-86), which includes portraits of Lorenzo de' Medici and other Florentine notables.
