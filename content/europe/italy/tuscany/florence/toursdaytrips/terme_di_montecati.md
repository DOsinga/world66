One of Italy's most celebrated thermal spa complexes, located about 50 km northwest of Florence in the Valdinievole valley. Florentines have been coming here to detox and relax since the 18th century, when Pietro Leopoldo of Habsburg Lorraine developed the springs into a proper resort.

The standout building is the Tettuccio Terme — a spectacular neoclassical pavilion of colonnades, mosaics, and frescoes, built in the 1920s and still one of the grandest spa interiors in Europe. The Excelsior and La Salute establishments are more modern. Trains run from Florence Santa Maria Novella roughly every hour; the journey takes about 50 minutes.
