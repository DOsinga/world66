The Villa Demidoff park is at Pratolino, about 12 km north of Florence on the Via Bolognese. Little remains of the splendid villa that Buontalenti built for Francesco I de’ Medici in 1575, but the park has been beautifully restored and is open from April to October. Giambologna’s monumental statue of the Appennino (1579–89), a giant figure emerging from the rock above a pool, is an outstanding example of Florentine Mannerism and worth the trip alone.
