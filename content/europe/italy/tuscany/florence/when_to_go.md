This **Florence when to go **guide tells you the right time to visit this Tuscan city. Florence attracts many people from all over the globe because it is richly steeped in history, offering tourists an excellent insight into the past. Not only this, Florence also has many gardens, museums, art galleries and places to eat. The best time to visit Florence is during the months of September through December, when the weather is cooler and the tourists have disappeared.

**Summer Months**

Florence&rsquo;s peak season runs from February through October for a good nine months in a year. So, you can visit Florence anytime during this period. It can get a bit hot in Florence during the months of July and August. Further, it is also crowded with tourists during this time. So, you may want to think twice before holidaying in Florence during these months. Also, a lot of cafes and stores are closed during the month of August, since most of the locals in Florence are on holiday elsewhere.

**Cooler Climate**

The weather is much cooler from September onwards. In December, the weather is simply perfect and you won&rsquo;t encounter hordes of tourists at this time either. You can comfortably walk around Florence taking in the town&rsquo;s sights and sounds. The weather is still a bit sunny, but the cool air certainly brings relief to shoppers and explorers. In November, a good number of festivals take place in Florence, so it&rsquo;s a good time to be traveling to this Tuscan city.
