### [Museum of the Mountain Folk](/europe/italy/tuscany/florenceprovince/altomugelloarea/palazzuolo_sul_sen/museums/museum_of_the_moun)

Palazzo dei Capitani – Palazzuolo sul Senio

About 800 documents and objects, witnesses of the uses and customs of the past, are collected in the halls of Palazzo dei Capitani. There are agricultural machines and tools, objects of common use, furniture and other things that had to do with the daily life of the farmers and craftsmen in Palazzuolo.
There is also a collection of popular handmade objects of art: holy pictures, devotional objects among which a wooden mutilated crucifix

### [Archeological Museum of Alto-Mugello](/europe/italy/tuscany/florenceprovince/altomugelloarea/palazzuolo_sul_sen/museums/archeological_muse)

Palazzo dei Capitani - Palazzuolo Sul Senio

The exhibition is about the oldest and less known past of the territory of the Lamone, Senio and Santerno Valleys, supplying the instruments for an easy reading of the contents.
Since prehistoric archaeology covers a very vast span of time and presents itself complicated for the same human evolution, both from the somatic and cultural point of view, a big space has been attributed to the illustrations of the most important stages of this evolution.
