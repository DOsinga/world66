This commune is in the heart of the Chianti Classico wine producing area, 30km from Florence and 35km from Siena


 


 Fairs and Markets for typical produce of the area:


 **Weekly Markets:**


 
 Greve in Chianti - Saturday morning


 
 Panzano in Chianti - Sunday morning


 
 Strada in Chianti - Tuesday morning


 
 **Festivals and annual celebrations:**


 
 "Market of objects from the past" – EASTER MONDAY


 
 "Market of objects from the past" – Autumn edition- 2nd Sunday in the month of October


 
 "Show/market of plants and flowers" – 1st Sunday in the month of May


 
 "Chianti Classico Festival" – second week in the month of September


 
 Panzano in Chianti : "Celebration of the Good Season" ("Festa della Stagion Bona") 25 April


 
 San Polo in Chianti: "Iris Festival" – 2nd week in the month of May


 
 Strada in Chianti: "Festival of Peasant Civilisation "- 4th week in the month of April


 
 Chiocchio - "Boar Festival" – 4th week in the month of April


 
 Lucolena: "Chestnut Festival" – last Sunday in the month of October


 
 Montefioralle: "Fritters Feast " – 2nd or 3rd Sunday in the month of March
