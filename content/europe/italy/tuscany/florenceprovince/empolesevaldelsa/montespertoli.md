Montespertoli is a quiet Tuscan hill town about 30 kilometres southwest of Florence, sitting in the heart of the Chianti wine country. It is not a tourist destination in the usual sense — there are no blockbuster sights — but it is a fine base for exploring the rolling vineyard landscape between Florence and the Val d'Elsa.

The town itself is pleasant and unhurried, with a central piazza, a handful of restaurants serving honest Tuscan food, and views over the surrounding hills. The annual wine festival in June draws visitors from across the region. If you are looking for rural Tuscany without the crowds of Greve or San Gimignano, this is a good bet.
