Approach roads and recommended tourist trails:

  A1motorway: Firenze Certosa exit at a distance of 23 km. – Firenze-Signa exit at 20 km.

  A11motorway: Altopascio exit at 20 km

  FI-PI-LI Dual carriageway: Ginestra Fiorentina exit at 10 km.

  FI-SI Dual carriageway: S. Casciano exit at 18 km – Bargino exit at 15 km.
