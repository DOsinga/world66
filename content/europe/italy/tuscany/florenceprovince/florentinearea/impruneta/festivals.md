---
title: "Festivals in Impruneta"
type: section
---

Spring in the Square (Primavera in Piazza), last ten days of May. Held in Piazza Buondelmonti and the Basilica cloisters. The square is transformed into a beautiful garden with shows of plants, paving, terracotta objects and local craftsmanship, as well as an antiques exhibition.

Festa dell’Uva (Grape Festival), last Sunday in September. Since 1926 the four districts which make up the town have vied with each other, showing allegorical floats built by inhabitants of the districts using, amongst other things, huge quantities of grapes. The floats are accompanied by short shows, again organised by people of the districts. It is important to remember that the Festa dell’Uva started a year before the Chianti Wine Consortium was established.

San Luca Festival, held throughout the week of San Luca (18 October). This festival goes back more than a thousand years and is connected to the seasonal moving of livestock, when cattle change hands. Not only this but there are market stalls, meals, horse races and local produce on sale.

Impruneta Natale: Show-market of extra virgin olive oil (December)
