---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

Impruneta is around 13 km from the centre of Florence. For those with private transport, the area is easy to reach using the A1 motorway, leaving at the Firenze-Certosa exit. If coming from Siena, take the Siena-Florence dual carriageway to the end. From Florence, go through Galluzzo or the Piazzale Michelangelo and the Poggio Imperiale, crossing the area of Pozzolatico and Mezzomonte. 

  If travelling by train, alight from the Florence-S.Maria Novella station or the Florence Campo di Marte station.

   If travelling by plane, the nearest airport is the Florence-Peretola "A. Vespucci".
