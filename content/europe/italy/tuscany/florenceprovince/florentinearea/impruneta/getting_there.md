---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

Impruneta is around 13 km south of central Florence. By car, take the A1 motorway and exit at Firenze-Certosa; from Siena, follow the Siena–Florence dual carriageway to its end. From Florence you can also come through Galluzzo or via Piazzale Michelangelo and Poggio Imperiale, passing through Pozzolatico and Mezzomonte.

By public transport, take a bus from Florence. The nearest train stations are Florence Santa Maria Novella and Florence Campo di Marte, from which local buses run to Impruneta. The nearest airport is Florence-Peretola (Amerigo Vespucci).
