---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

Scandicci can be reached by car from the di Firenze Signa exit of the A1 motorway or from the Scandicci exit of the Florence - Pisa - Livorno dual carriageway.  

  The main points of entry into the town from Florence are from viale Nenni and via di Scandicci.

   If arriving by plane in Florence or Pisa, the reference point is the central Florence railway station Santa  Maria Novella.

   From the central Florence railway station take ATAF buses n. 16, 26 or 27 to Scandicci. 

 

 You can arrive in Scandicci by car either by leaving motorway A1 at Firenze Signa, or by leaving freeway Firenze - Pisa - Livorno at Scandicci. People coming from Florence can follow viale Nenni or via di Scandicci. Car traffic in Florence is subjected to a number of limits.
