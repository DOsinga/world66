- Footpath around the fortifications of the 16th century Fortezza di San Martino, built under orders from Cosimo de’ Medici, 1st Grand-duke of the Territory of the Mugello valley  - Visit to the Trebbio Castle, commissioned by Cosimo de’ Medici and built by Michelozzo Michelozzi on the ruins of a feudal tower,. Guided visits for groups on Tuesdays, Wednesdays, Thursdays and Fridays. For bookings call 0558458793.

 - Visit to the Convento di Bosco ai Frati, which, along with the church, was rebuilt according to a design by Michelozzo for Cosimo de’ Medici. Inside there is a small museum which houses the precious wooden crucifix attributed to Donatello. Open every day; for information telephone 055848111.

 - Mountain walking along the ridges of the Florentine Appenines.
