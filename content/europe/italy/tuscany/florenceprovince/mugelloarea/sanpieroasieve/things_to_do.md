---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

San Piero a Sieve and its surroundings are dense with Medici-era architecture. The fortress above the town, the castle at Trebbio, and the convent at Bosco ai Frati are all within a few kilometres of each other and can be visited in a half-day. The Apennine ridges above the valley are open for walking.
