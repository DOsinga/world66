title: "Incisa in val d'Arno"
type: location
---

Since Incisa is so easy to reach, it is the ideal starting point for visiting the main artistic cities in Tuscany: Florence,  Siena, Arezzo. It is also possible to start from Incisa and follow alternative itineraries, including the nearby areas of Chianti, the Vallombrosa Forest and the hills which act as watersheds between the Arno Valley and the Ema Valley.

  To Arezzo:

  Take the Autosole in a South direction, or continue along the SS 69 in a South direction.

  To Siena:

  Take the Autosole in a North direction as far as the Certosa exit, then take the dual carriageway to Siena.

  To the Chianti area:

  Travel towards Figline Valdarno on the SS 69, then take the provincial Chianti road towards Ponte agli Stolli and finally Greve in Chianti.

  To Vallombrosa:

  Take the provincial road to Reggello and then Vallombrosa.
