---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

Incisa is close to the "Incisa" exit on the Autosole motorway, on the Slow Railway Line from Florence to Arezzo, on the State Road SS 69 and on the San Donato Provincial Road.

  From Florence:

  Take the State Road SS 67 towards Pontassieve and then the SS 69 as far as Incisa in Val d’Arno.

  Take the S. Donato Provincial Road towards Bagno a Ripoli, continue along the S. Donato to Incisa in Val d’Arno.
