San Alessandro: Ex-oratory (built in 1591)of a company where, on 26 August 1786, the Bishop of Fiesole Ranieri Mancini ordered the parish of S. Alessandro all'Incisa to be founded, there being no church available at the time. The interior houses a board triptych painting by Andrea di Giusto. Tel. 0330/253420 (Don Luca).

  SS. Cosma and Damiano al Vivaio: a Baroque style church, built in 1514 and consecrated on 28 June 1538.

  It was restored several times in the following centuries. Inside the church there are several works of art including a Madonna with Child attributed to Donatello, some terracotta works by Della Robbia, 14 Stations of the Cross from the 17th century Florentine school and an 18th century organ. Tel. 055/8335022 (Don Leone - Don Mario).
