**Enjoy a Cup of Coffee or a Fine Drink at one of the Trendiest Cafes in Forte Dei Marmi**

Most **Forte Dei Marmi bars and cafes** are located near the waterfront Viale della Republica. This includes one of the trendiest cafes around, Caf&eacute; Morin. It is located on Caio Duilio Street, a crossing of Viale della Repubblica. The caf&eacute; is situated right next to the President Hotel and offers an excellent atmosphere and an extensive selection of drinks. Here are some more details on the place.

**The Atmosphere**

Entering Caf&eacute; Morin, you will see a large, stylishly-decorated room with classy modern interior. The whole atmosphere is enhanced by the sounds of excellent new jazz, house and lounge music. The tables are surrounded by lounges and comfortable chairs, with many plants placed all over the caf&eacute;. The walls are decorated with interesting pictures. The outdoor part of the Caf&eacute; creates a real tropical beach feeling, while the covered bar has a more "clubby" atmosphere. You should really dress to impress when visiting this place, if you want to blend up with the rest of its clients.

**The Menu**

Caf&eacute; Morin serves some excellent coffee, a wide selection of alcoholic drinks and some of the best tropical cocktails that you have ever tasted. The menu contains various snacks you can order to complement your drinks, like cups filled with nuts or plates full of little sandwiches. For a complete chill-out experience, you order a classic Arabian nargile.
