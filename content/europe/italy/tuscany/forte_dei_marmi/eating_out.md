---
title: "Eating Out in Forte dei Marmi"
type: section
---

The many **Forte Dei Marmi restaurants** give visitors a chance to enjoy some delicious meals while here on vacation. There are many good restaurants near the seafront of Forte Dei Marmi. Most restaurants are open night and day. The spectacular view comes from the welcoming azure sea, with the sparkling waves lapping the seashore, and the majestic Apuan Alps on the opposite side of the picture perfect scene. You do not want to miss this wonderful experience.

**Marechiaro Restaurant**

Marechiaro, like most restaurants, offer eating out in the open air and enjoying excellent seafood cuisine while admiring the marvelous view of the surroundings. You could enjoy your meal under a gazebo while breathing in the invigorating sea air. The menu consists of exotic dishes like raw seafood, shellfish and fish. It serves also zuppa di frutti di mare con pomodoro e basilica, a seafood salad with pine nuts and pesto sauce. Main courses include crostacel al vapore con aragosta e verdure in pinzimonio or steamed lobster and crayfish with raw vegetables. Dessert includes cheesecakes, yogurts and fresh fruits like pineapple. The fruits depend on what is in season for that particular month.

**Prices of food**

The usual price of food ranges from $37 to $50 per person excluding drinks. The delicious and mouth-watering dishes are well worth your money. You can also select whether to eat in the open, near the sea dock, at the gazebo or indoors. You can choose from several great options with whatever suits your mood. The restaurant also accommodates business luncheons, families and groups.
