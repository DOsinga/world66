**Forte Dei Marmi Festivals** showcase the resort town's richness in arts and culture. Forte dei Marmi is a well-liked beach resort in Tuscany, Italy. During the 16th century, it served as a port for exporting Italian marble. It is now surrounded with beautiful gardens and private villas. Forte Dei Marmi has been the main tourist destination for adventurous tourists who like water sports. Foreigners do not only visit the place because of the wonderful beach and water adventures but also because of its magnificent festival celebrations which truly captivate visitors.

#### **Beat the Summer Heat**

La Versiliana Festival is a festival that is always included in the annual events of Forte Dei Marmi. This festival is done every summer. The festival is held in a park of the same name. La Versiliana Park has a beautiful villa inside. The La Versiliana Festival has a line up of events including music, dance, poetry and cinema where one can truly enjoy summer to the fullest.

#### **Festival of with Potential**

The Festival of Satire and Caricature exposes the talents of its local drawing artists to the international arena. During this festival, all the cartoon artists from all around the world come to join in the different events. These cartoon and drawing artists share their knowledge and skills with one another to bring out more the best in them.
