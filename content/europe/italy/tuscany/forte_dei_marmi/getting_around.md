**Walk, Bike or Scoot in Forte Dei Marmi**

Walking is by no doubt the best decision for **Forte Dei Marmi transportation**. However, there are alternatives possible like renting a bicycle or a scooter. These are the most common ways of transportation not only for Forte Dei Marmi, but for the entire Tuscany region.

**Bicycles**

Bicycles are one of the most decent ways to get around Forte Dei Marmi next to walking by foot. The main reason for this is that most of the streets around the waterfront are either pedestrian or too overcrowded to move with a car. Bicycles can be rented at many various kiosks and at most hotels. They cost around $3 per hour. A wide selection of bicycles is available, including bicycles with baby seats attached. You will see by yourself that this is a very reliable form of transport within the town limits.

**Scooters**

Scooters are the motorized alternative of bicycles and are a preferred form of transport for people who want to travel faster around the city. Just be careful when driving on the non-pedestrian streets, since Italians aren&rsquo;t known as the best car drivers. Also, when renting a scooter, insist that they give you a helmet, since this is an important safety measure. Just like bicycles, scooters can be rented from various rental shops and hotels. They are more expensive than bicycles, but have the ability to carry up to 2 people, which is a definite plus.
