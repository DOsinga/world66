**The Various Seasons of Forte Dei Marmi **

If you want to enjoy your season in the sun, here is the **Forte Dei Marmi when to go** guide. The Forte Dei Marmi or the Fort of Marbles is mainly a bathing resort area. Its long, white beaches cater to tourists as well as the rich and the famous. The peak tourist time is during the summer season, when families from nearby countries visit. The Political Satire Festival in Forte Dei Marmi is one of the favorite events participated by 200 cartoonist from around the world. You can also shop in the weekly Wednesday market for designer clothes offered at low prices.

**Summer in Forte Dei Marmi **

Summer normally starts in June and ends in September with a high temperature of 28 degrees Celsius or 82.4 degrees Fahrenheit. Vacationers come in groups or whole families in this peak season. The parents bring their kids to enjoy the many kiddies&rsquo; attraction at the center of town. At Versiliana, a cultural center, the young enjoy various activities like sculpting, painting and drawing. The best time to visit this paradise is during summer, spring and autumn. During these seasons, the landscape is just spectacular.
**
Spring Season **

The spring season in Forte Dei Marmi begins in March to May. In the winter season and in late fall most hotels are closed. If you go to Forte Dei Marmi in early spring you can avoid the crowd. The temperature is tolerable and you will be able to enjoy sightseeing and exploring without the crowd milling around you.
