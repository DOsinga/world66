---
title: "Getting Around"
type: section
---

Public transport in Tuscany is reasonably good - trains are connecting [Pisa](/europe/italy/tuscany/pisa), [Lucca](/europe/italy/tuscany/lucca), Firenze, Prato, Pistoia, [Siena](/europe/italy/tuscany/siena) and [Arezzo](/europe/italy/tuscany/arezzo). Regular buses serve the cities off the train track such as Volterra, San Gimignano, Montalcino, San Galgano. As long as you travel between these towns, public transport isn't a problem at all. To go off the beaten tracks, it is still best to have your own means of transport.
