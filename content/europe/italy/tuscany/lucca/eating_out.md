Eating out is not a problem at all in lovely Lucca, where an abundance of "trattorie" serve good Tuscan food and numerous "pizzerie" that fill you stomach with delicious pizze. The central market occupies the large building at the west side of Piazza del Carmine, open Mon - Sat 7am - 1pm & 4 - 7.30 pm. There is an open air market every wednesday and saturday morning at Piazza Anfiteatro.

**general**

**Trattoria all' Astice**

Cosy and quiet restaurant located in the "centro storico" of Lucca, specialized in fish and seafood dishes, but also the meat and local dishes are worth a try. closed on monday (and month of may).

**Antica Locanda dell'Angelo **

Not far from Piazza San Michele, over the centuries, the "Locanda dell'Angelo" has always drawn a great number of Italians and foreign merchants who would come to Lucca to buy its renowned silk fabrics. Delicious homemade specialties & andelegant and quiet ourdoor dining experience.
