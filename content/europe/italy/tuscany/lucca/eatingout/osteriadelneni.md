The Osteria del Neni is located in one of the corners of antique Lucca, in the old narrow street of Via Pescheria, just near S.Michele square. Having a modern organization, the actual owner are coming back to taste and atmosphere of the old days. Having complete menues, Osteria del Neni will serve also snacks such as cheese, ham, salami, sweets all accompanied with a good glass of vine. Furthermore Osteria del Neni, and its winery, supply a selection of the best Chianti wines and Lucca hill wines.
