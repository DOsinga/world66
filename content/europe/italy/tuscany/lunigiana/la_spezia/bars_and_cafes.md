title: "Bars and Cafes in La Spezia"
type: section
---

**La Spezia Bars and Cafes** are spread across the city. La Spezia is a major commercial hub in Italy. This city is famous for its rich cultural and historic background. La Spezia is putting in all the efforts to transform into a fully-fledged tourist destination. Read on to find out about the bars and cafes in this city.

**Bars**

La Spezia is a great place to party. If you wish to get a glimpse into how the locals enjoy a party, then step into the Map Bar. This bar is located on the Sapri Avenue and is a favorite local bar. The flashing lights at the facade of the night club will lure you in. You can have a great evening with your group of friends at this club. Equipped with flat screen televisions and a bar that is fully stocked with local as well as international brands of alcohol, this is where the young hang out. If you need more options to choose from then take the 20-minute cab drive to the most-happening spot in the whole of La Spezia, the Marina.

**Cafe**

La Spezia has a quaint Italian charm about it. The streets are lined with cafes that have outdoor seating areas. You can retire into one of the cafes and watch the activities and the life on the La Spezia streets. The weather adds a romantic touch to your outdoor cafe experience. Lounge around in the All Inferno Cafe located in the Via Costa area. The cafe specializes in mouthwatering seafood preparations and has a reputation of serving the most amazing coffee in town. It also has a small bar, which caters to the guests looking for alcoholic drinks.
