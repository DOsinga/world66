---
title: "Eating Out in La Spezia"
type: section
---

La Spezia in Liguria is a great travel destination in North Western Italy and the **La Spezia Restaurants **serve the most lip-smacking delicacies. The city lies between Genoa and Pisa and has significant commercial importance as a port. If you are a food enthusiast, La Spezia will never let you down. In fact, you can try the tastiest cuisines here at the posh restaurants. Starting from cafes and pizzerias, La Spezia has deli, restaurants and fast food joints that serve local Italian cuisines, food with homely taste and pizzas with mouth-watering toppings. If you are looking to treat yourself with some light dishes like sandwiches or Paninis, hop the roadside cafes featuring great ambiance.

**Ristorante- Pizzeria Fratelli D&rsquo; Angelo**

This is an ideal food joint for those who love sea foods. Apart from the various delicacies prepared from sea weeds and fish, you can check out their delicious "garlic pizza bread." Do not forget to taste the wide range of luscious wines that are priced affordably.

**La Lucciola**

This is one of the best sea front restaurants overlooking the panoramic views of Porto Venere. Apart from the delicacies like "spaghetti alle vongole," fried fish and pizza you will like the atmosphere of the restaurant.

**All&rsquo;Inferno**

All&rsquo;Inferno is one of the good dining spots that you might try out at La Spezia. It serves quality seafood and Italian cuisines. You can have brunch or breakfast here and the meal costs around $21 or $28. The atmosphere is friendly and posh here.
