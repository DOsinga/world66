**La Spezia Family Travel Ideas for Italy Travelers**

Family vacationers searching for **La Spezia Family Travel **ideas should consider planning a trip to Cinque Terre.

**Reaching Cinque Terre **

Cinque Terre is well-connected through a series of trains including reputed rail services like Eurostar and Intercity trains. There are budgeted trains referred to as the Regional service that regularly run between Cinque Terre and La Spezia. The travel time is about 45 minutes and the journey is through scenic countryside locations. Most of the trains starting from La Spezia arrive at Levanto in Cinque Terre. From Levanto, cabs to nearby travel destinations are easily available.

**What to Expect in Cinque Terre**

Cinque Terre is a popular travel destination, located in the Italian Riviera. It is composed of five famous villages that exude the ancient, Roman-era aura. In Cinque Terre, a family can schedule a visit to these small villages and the nearby hillsides. The villages provide an insight into Roman history. The path is undemanding with paved roads. Another popular site is the Cinque Terre National Park that has been awarded the status of a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Family visitors can book discounted packages that combine small hikes, bicycling along the foothills and short boat trips.

**Popular Cinque Terre Family Expeditions **

Food and wine are the two most popular offerings of Cinque Terre, and the entire family can spend hours tasting the various regional delicacies. Cinque is regarded as the most authentic source of Ligurian food that is amazingly cheap at the local cafes. Visitors should try the pesto sauce that has a typical Italian flavor emanating from freshly-crushed basil and olive oil. Bakeries are more popular among children for Focaccia, a baked Italian snack, and Farinata, a crunchy sumptuous pancake. Adults in the family can taste the locally-procured wines prepared from grapes.
