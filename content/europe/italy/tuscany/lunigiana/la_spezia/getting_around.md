---
title: "Getting Around"
type: section
---

La Spezia is compact enough to explore on foot. For trips further afield, the train is the best option -- especially for the Cinque Terre, where the five villages are each a few minutes apart by rail.

**Train**

The regional train between La Spezia and Levanto stops at all five Cinque Terre villages, with departures roughly every hour. Trains also connect to other towns in the province. The service is reliable and well-maintained.

**Bus and taxi**

Local buses cover the city and surrounding areas. Taxis are easy to find on the street or by phone, with fixed rates. Car rental is available but largely unnecessary within the city.

**Boat**

The Consorzio Marittimo Turistico 5 Terre operates ferries through the Gulf of Poets and along the Cinque Terre coast. A round trip costs 23 to 25 euros. The boats are slower than the train but the views are far better -- weather permitting.
