If you are visiting **La Spezia, transoirtatuin** should not be a problem as it is a highly developed and the city is tourist-friendly. This Italian city offers various fast and economical travel options to the visitors. The Marina allows visitors to arrive by their own yachts or by cruise ships. Once in La Spezia you can make use of the train, taxi or the bus service. Read on to find out more.

**Train**

The train is a major means of transport in La Spezia. It connects to all major destinations in Italy. Many tourists consider La Spezia a base to travel to the famous Cinque Terre. Take the train that runs between Le Spezia and the city of Levanto, as it connects the five popular villages in Cinque Terre. The train is always on time and maintained well. With its reliable and friendly service this is the most preferred means on transport in La Spezia. The trains to Cinque Terre depart every hour. The train also connects various other towns in La Spezia.

**Road**

If you wish to explore the attraction in La Spezia then there are various options available. The taxis here are just a phone call away. Most of them are lined up on the streets so you will not find it difficult to get a cab. The rates a fixed so you do not have to worry about being overcharged. You can also rent your own car here. The local bus is also another option for getting around La Spezia. It is also the cheapest option available.
