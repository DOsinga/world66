---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

La Spezia is well connected by rail and road, and can also be reached by ferry from along the Ligurian coast.

**By train**

This is the easiest way to arrive. Trains run from Rome approximately every ninety minutes, from Genoa every half hour, and frequent regional services connect to Pisa and Florence. The station is central.

**By air**

The nearest airport is Galileo Galilei in Pisa, about 66 kilometres away. A rail connection from the airport to Pisa Centrale, then a change to the La Spezia line, takes one to two hours depending on connections.

**By car**

La Spezia lies on the A15, which connects to the A12 motorway between Genoa and Viareggio. It is roughly 45 minutes southeast of Genoa and 20 minutes from Viareggio.

**By boat**

Ferries run to Portovenere and the Cinque Terre from La Spezia's harbour. Note that ferry service can be suspended in poor weather, so the train is more reliable year-round.

**By bus**

The 11P bus connects La Spezia to Portovenere, departing every half hour.
