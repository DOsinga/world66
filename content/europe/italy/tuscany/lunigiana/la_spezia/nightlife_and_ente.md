---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment in La Spezia"
type: section
---

There are a number of places that you can visit to enjoy **La Spezia
nightlife.** This guide lists some of the best places tourists can
visit in the town to spend exciting or quiet evenings.

**Skaletta
Rock Club

**Located on Via Crispi in La Spezia, this is a
popular hangout for many locals and tourists. This club is perfect for
those who want to have a few drinks and engage in a little sporting
activity. Beer is the preferred drink here, but there are other options
visitors can choose from as well. Skaletta Rock Club is perfect for rock
music lovers, as this is the usual genre played here. The club also has
mini-football and pool tables to entertain sporting fans. Skaletta Rock
Club is a little out of the way from the city center, but is ideal for
those who prefer to stay away from the partying crowds.

**Map
Bar

**For something closer to the city center, try the Map Bar.
The place is usually full with La Spezia’s younger crowd. Located on
Sapri Ave, this is a lively and fun place to hang out till late night.
The bar has a few flat screen TVs that play Italian and English music
videos. Map Bar has a décor that reflects youthful exuberance, making it
appear more like an American club. Apart from the different drinks,
this bar also serves basic snacks like pizzas and sandwiches. Visitors
who become fans of this club can also buy their T-shirts as souvenirs.

**Enoteca
Eliseo

**Tourists who are looking for a quiet place to sit
back, sip some pleasant wine, and enjoy the local cuisine must visit
Enoteca Eliseo. Situated at Piazza Matteotti, visitors can enjoy a
wonderful view of the center. This wonderful wine bar plays classical
music and is decorated with candle lit tables. The place also serves
cocktails to cater to those who don’t prefer wine. Visitors should try
the olive snacks and capers here, as they are a tasteful example of
traditional Italian dishes. Enoteca Eliseo is especially perfect for
couples who are looking for a quiet, romantic evening.

**La
Cantina del Pescatore

**Situated in the historic center of
Monterosso al Mare, this is an ideal place for tourists who want to have
a quiet dinner with good drinks and great food. Visitors to La Cantina
del Pescatore are advised to try the wine here, as it comes from the
heritage site of Cinque Terre. Even the food here reflects the flavors
and tastes of that region.

**Bar Nuvo

**Another good
bar to visit in La Spezia is Bar Nuvo, which is located on Via
Monteverdi. The place serves a range of dishes and alcohols including
wines, beers and cocktails. It is ideal for those who want to enjoy an
evening of drinks, food and chatter. Bar Nuvo is also open in the
mornings and serves European and English breakfasts to tourists who want
a change from Italian breakfasts.
