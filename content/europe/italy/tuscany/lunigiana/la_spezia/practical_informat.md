This **La Spezia practical information** guide will help you better understand this beautiful tourist destination. It is located in the Lunigiana region in Italy. There are several interesting tourist attractions in and around La Spezia. Along with tourism, it is a major commercial and a naval hub in Italy. La Spezia proves to be a good base to venture out to other cities and to town. Read on to find out more.

**Climate**

La Spezia in Italy is blessed with a Mediterranean climate. The seasons here change from summer, winter, autumn and spring. During winter, the temperature can drop to 2 degrees, with the maximum being 10 degrees. Summer has a maximum temperature of 28 degrees and a minimum of 19 degrees. The summer season is at its peak in the month of July, whereas the winter is at its peak in January. La Spezia does not witness heavy snowfall. The humidity levels in summer and winter make the weather a tad bit uncomfortable. Autumn also shares space with rains.

**Accommodation**

The accommodations that La Spezia offers will suit travelers with any kind of budget. La Spezia has a long list of hotels to choose from. From budget class hotels to 5 stars, this place has it all. There are also luxurious villas that can be rented on a per night basis. There are several hostels, as well as home stay options in La Spezia. Hotel Nella located in Via Genova is a great mid level hotel in La Spezia. The hotel provides all amenities to the guests and charges around $100 per night.
