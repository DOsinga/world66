A casual, family-run restaurant in La Spezia popular with locals for its generous portions of seafood and pizza. The garlic pizza bread is a favourite starter, and the seafood pasta dishes use whatever came in fresh that morning. The wine list leans local and stays affordable. Service is relaxed and friendly, and you can eat well here without spending much.
