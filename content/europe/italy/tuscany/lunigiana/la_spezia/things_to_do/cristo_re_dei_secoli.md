La Spezia's cathedral, also known as Christ the King of Centuries, was designed by Adelberto Libera and completed in the 1970s. The bold modernist design stands in striking contrast to the city's nineteenth-century architecture, with a distinctive circular plan and clean concrete lines. Worth a look even if you don't normally seek out modern churches.
