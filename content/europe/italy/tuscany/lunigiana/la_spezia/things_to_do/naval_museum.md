Adjacent to the naval arsenal, the Museo Tecnico Navale houses one of Italy's most important maritime collections. Models, figureheads, weapons, and navigational instruments chart the history of the Italian navy and La Spezia's role as a naval headquarters since the 1850s. The arsenal itself, built in the 1860s and rebuilt after wartime bombing, is an impressive piece of military engineering visible from the waterfront.
