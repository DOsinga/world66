title: "When to Go in La Spezia"
type: section
---

Tuscany is a beautiful country and is beautiful year round.** La Spezia When to Go** is key in making your holiday everything you want and more. Be sure to book your holiday in advance to get the best deals.

**Fall**

September and October are the best times to travel to La Spezia. During the day you will find the temperatures warm but pleasant. In the evenings it does get a little chilly but warming up by an open fire is a perfect way to spend an evening in La Spezia. September and October are also grape harvesting time which make wine tasting a top activity. You will find that travel discounts are easy to come by during this time.

**Spring**

All the flowers are in full bloom during the spring months. While you will probably get some rain it is still a beautiful time to visit. Travel discounts are usually available during this time as well. May and June are the best months to visit if you want to see all the flowers in bloom.

**Summer**

The summer months are very hot and dry. You will also find that during mid-June to late August is the tourist season which makes coming by travel discounts difficult.

**Winter**

Winter is very cold and very wet. Winter is not the ideal time to travel to La Spezia. You will, however, find some of the best discounts during this season since it is off-season. If you decide to travel during the winter make sure you bring warm clothing.
