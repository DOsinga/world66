There are many **Monsummano Terme Hotels **to choose from if you are going to this small community in Italy. Monsummano Terme is popular among tourists all over the world since it has a lot of well-known spa resorts located in the area. Here are some hotels where you can stay in while you are enjoying the scenery or just enjoy a spa treatment.

**Relais Villa San Bastiano&lrm;**

This hotel is family-owned. The hosts as well as the staffs are very friendly and can cater to your needs 24/7. The rooms of the hotel are equipped with free Internet access, cable TV, hot and cold showers as well as a secure phone line. The hotel also has its own restaurant that serves delicious home-cooked Italian dishes. Standard double rooms cost $275.

**Hotel Residenza del Granduca**

Located in the center of the town, this hotel is a choice among many travelers due to its cheap rates. Standard room rates for single occupancy are $70. The rooms are very spacious and have windows that offer a great view of the entire town. The hotel is also equipped with modern facilities like a Wi-Fi access, fax and photocopying machines and other technologies that connect you to the outside world.

**Agriturismo Il Bottaccino&lrm;**

This is a bed and breakfast hotel which gives the guests an experience of living in a farmhouse. The hotel is located on the outskirts of the town and is situated in a big compound. The compound comes complete with great gardens where guests can stroll around to enjoy their day. Standard room rates of the hotel are $100 to $120.

**B&B La Casa di zia Lina&lrm;**

The hotel is located in the center of the town which is perfect for tourists who want to get a good glimpse of what Monsummano has to offer. The hotel is decorated with rich Italian fabrics and furniture. Its rooms come complete with big balconies to offer guests a great view of the town. The hotel also provides free Wi-Fi access. A double room can cost as much as $75.

**Grand Hotel Tettuccio**

A four-star hotel in Monsummano, Grand Hotel Tettuccio is a modern hotel that comes complete with facilities that will make the stay of the guests comfortable. It has modern interiors for its rooms, a hairdryer, telephone and even minibar. Room rates start from $100 to $150.
