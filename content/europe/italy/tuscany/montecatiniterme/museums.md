**Montecatini Terme Museums**

The biggest and quite possibly most frequented of the famous Tuscany
spa-set is Montecatini Terme in Italy. The healing waters of this
beautiful little hamlet have been the source and the major attraction
for countless numbers to come and take a dip in the healing and
energizing waters. The town also is known for its wonderful array of
museums that have been around since the 17th century, and have been the
home to the world's most treasured artifacts from time to time. One
such priceless and highly important piece of world art is the sculpture
of David by Michelangelo. The Academia Museum is home to this
impressive work of art, and can be seen daily.

**Museums of Montecatini Terme**

Besides one of the world’s most impressive collections of art, there
are a few other great museums to see and experience while in the
friendly confines of Montecatini Terme. Storico Giardino Garzoni Museum
and Art Studio is perched upon a hillside and looks as if it
could be positioned in the Roman Empire. It is easy to see how the
beauty and architectural Roman style has endured all these
centuries with barely a change. That's the feeling
one leaves the town of Montecatini Terme with, timeless beauty.
