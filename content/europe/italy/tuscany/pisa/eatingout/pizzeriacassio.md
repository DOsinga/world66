Only a few steps away from Piazza dei Miracoli (down Via Santa Maria and the left), this Pizzeria isn't one of the touristy traps. Very limited tables and opening hours (closed on weekends) underline the fact that most guests are locals having their lunch break here.  

address: Piazza Cavallotti 14

tel: +39 (0)50 - 55 34 69
