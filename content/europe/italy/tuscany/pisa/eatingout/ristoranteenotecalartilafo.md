L'Artilafo restaurant, a family run business, serves very creative Tuscan food with a weekly variable menu of sea-food, meat dishes, vegetarian dishes. The restaurant offers the possibility to choose among over 350 different Italian wines.
 The Restaurant opens only for dinner at 8pm. The L'Artilafo is situated in the historical centre of Pisa, close to the Arno River, only 1 km far away from the Piazza dei Miracoli and the Leaning Tower.
