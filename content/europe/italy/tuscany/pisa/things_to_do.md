Pisa offers a lot of things to do other than visiting** Leaning Tower** and **Piazza dei Miracoli**.

As the fulcrum of an area astride the provinces of Lucca and Livorno, its territory particularly suitable for farm-holidays, trekking, cycling, horse-riding, bird watching, golf, open-air and water sports, that can be practised on a fantastic coast with sand-beaches, perfectly equipped with bathing establishments, hotels and restaurants.

Learn Italian Language

The Istituto Linguistico Mediterraneo, founded in 1981, was one of the first **schools of Italian in Italy and in Tuscany**.

Italian language courses for foreigners are offered for semester or summer study abroad programs. Intensive courses and individual tutoring are also offered as well as cooking and art classes. Classes are held in Pisa and Viareggio.
