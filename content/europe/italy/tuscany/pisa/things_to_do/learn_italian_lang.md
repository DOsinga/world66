Centro giacomo Puccini, founded in 1988, was one of the first **schools of Italian in Italy and in Tuscany**.

Italian language courses for foreigners are offered for semester or summer study abroad programs. Intensive courses and individual tutoring are also offered as well as cooking and art classes. Classes are held in Viareggio.
