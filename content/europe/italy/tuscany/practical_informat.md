One of the most attractive zones in the province of Tuscany is the wine region of Chianti.

And one of the best ways to enjoy Chianti, or indeed all of Tuscany, is in type of accommodation called *agriturismo*,
which is a working farm that offers reasonably priced accommodation,
and often, though not always, meals made from the produce from the
farms.

Rooms or apartments in farmhouses or converted
haybarns is mostly self catering and ideal for
friends or family travelling together. Staying at an agriturismo is a
great way to get a relaxing insight into rural life in Tuscany
especially at wine harvest time in autumn.

A word of warning though, sometimes the agriturismo's can be quite
difficult to get to if you are travelling by public transport, so check
train and bus links before booking.
