Hotels, golf, polo games, regattas, beach-going, port facilities, water activities of all kinds and night-life make this somewhat high-class sea-side resort a rather exclusive environment for the aristocratic upper-class Italians.

But this doesn't mean that other people can't enjoy staying here as well because there are provisions for everyone including no-fee public beaches in between the private beaches, camping grounds nearby and supermarkets.

### [Punta Ala Practical Information](/europe/italy/tuscany/punta_ala/practical_informat/punta_ala_practica)

Punta Ala has all kinds of services, history, lodgings, hotels, restaurants, bars, boat dockings, supermarkets, shops, beaches, bike, walking and horse-back riding trails as well as many interesting day trips to Etruscan ruins, thermal water pools and gorgeous bays and gulfs.

See the weather live on the web cam, consult the maps and find out about the local events such as polo games, the America's Cup, bicycling races or concert shows of music.
