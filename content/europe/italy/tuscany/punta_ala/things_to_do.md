### [Golf Club Punta Ala](/europe/italy/tuscany/punta_ala/things_to_do/golf_club_punta_al)

Gold Club Punta Ala is located in a beautiful green Tuscan environment with Marritime Pine trees and a magnificent Club House. There are 18 holes, 6,168 metres and PAR 72. There is a bar, restaurant, a driving range, Golf Cars, and a Pro Shop. Due to the mild climate because of its special location it is open year round.
