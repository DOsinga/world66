One of the great Gothic cathedrals of Italy, distinguished by its striped black and white marble exterior. The interior is extraordinary: the inlaid marble floor (the most elaborate in existence, depicting scenes from the Old Testament) and the Libreria Piccolomini with its vivid Renaissance frescoes are both unmissable. The Museo dell'Opera del Duomo next door houses original sculptures by Duccio and Donatello.
