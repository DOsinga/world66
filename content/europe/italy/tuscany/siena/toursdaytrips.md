---
title: "Day Trips"
type: section
---

The Sienese countryside is among the finest in Italy. The abandoned Cistercian abbey of San Galgano (with its famous roofless nave) is 35 km southwest and takes half a day. Montalcino, home to Brunello di Montalcino wine, is 42 km south. The intact medieval walls of Monteriggioni are just 14 km north. The hill towns of Pienza, Montepulciano, and San Gimignano are all within an hour's drive.
