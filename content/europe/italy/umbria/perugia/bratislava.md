A small, welcoming bar on Via Fiorenzuola, a few minutes' walk from Perugia's centre. Bratislava draws a local crowd with its regular live music nights and relaxed atmosphere. It's the kind of place where the bartender remembers your order — a good antidote to the more polished spots around Corso Vannucci.
