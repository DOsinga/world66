Though renowned for chocolate – “Perugini” with its famous “baci” chocolate is the most famous in whole italy – Perugia also whips up a variety of delectable breads and pastries: be sure to taste the “mele al cartoccio” (apple pie) or the “torta di formaggio” (cheese bread). For fresh fruit and vegetables go to he open-air market in Piazza Europa (tue & sat mornings) or to the covered market in Piazza Matteotti (daily). For going out check some of our below mentioned favorites:
