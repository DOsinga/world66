**by train:**

 A few trains run from [Florence](/europe/italy/tuscany/florence) and [Rome](/europe/italy/lazio/rome) to Perugia, but change at Foligno on the Rome - [Ancona](/europe/italy/marche/ancona) line, or catch one of the frequent trains from Terontola on the Florence - Rome route for more service. Arriving in Perugia, you will find yourself well awat from the centre of Perugia on Piazza V. Veneto. But don't worry, buses run regularly (no. 6, 7, 11) to Piazza Italia in the centre (15 min journey).

  **by car:**

 Perugia is easy to reach by car. coming from the highway Firenze - Roma take the Valdichiana exit (from north) and the Orte exit (from south). Follow then the main street E 45.
