The American Restaurant & Music Bar Crazy Bull Café lies in Perugia in the Prepo area.  It may be easily reached from the Perugia-Bettolle motorway link, exit Prepo (approx. 200 m) and from Piazzale Europa through Via dei Filosofi, Via della Pallotta and finally Via Palermo, 21 / B.

 If you are real lovers of U.S. Food-Beverage & Music you have found your corner of paradise: dishes prepared in the respect of tradition with healthy ingredients and original dressings according to the old recipes of Texan and Mexican cuisine. The place is a most frequented meeting point for young people, both Italian and Foreigners, in the city of Perugia.
