A small, laid-back bar tucked into the streets near the Pozzo Etrusco (Etruscan Well) in Perugia's historic centre. Zooropa draws a mix of university students and locals with its affordable drinks and relaxed atmosphere. The bar hosts occasional live music nights, making it a good spot to wind down after a day exploring the hilltop city. Nothing fancy, but it has genuine character — the kind of place where you end up staying longer than planned.
