The old town of Spoleto is full of Roman remains. The amphitheatre near Piazza della Libertà, the Roman theatre (now used for festival performances), and a well-preserved 1st-century house on Via di Visiale all survive. Some of the steep, twisted streets with buildings forming arched bridges across them are worth exploring for their own sake — the layers of Roman, medieval, and later construction blend together in ways you rarely see so clearly.
