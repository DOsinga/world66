The Skyway is Courmayeur's signature cable car, rebuilt in 2015 with rotating gondolas that give passengers a full 360-degree view as they rise from Courmayeur (1,224 m) to Punta Helbronner on the Italian-French border at 3,466 m. The journey takes about twenty minutes and arrives at a glass-and-steel viewing platform perched on the ridge of the Mont Blanc massif. On a clear day the view takes in Mont Blanc itself, the Matterhorn, Monte Rosa, and the Grandes Jorasses — essentially the entire arc of the western Alps. In summer, from Punta Helbronner you can continue by cable car across the Vallée Blanche glacier to the Aiguille du Midi above [Chamonix](/europe/france/alpes/chamonix).
