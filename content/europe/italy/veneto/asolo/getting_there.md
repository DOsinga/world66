By Bus: 

Bus stop "Asolo - Ca' Vescovo" - minibus service to the town center Regular cus services to Bassano del Grappa, [Padua](/europe/italy/veneto/padua), via Castelfranco and [Treviso](/europe/italy/veneto/treviso), via Montebelluna.

  By Car:

 From the east: Highway exit Treviso Nord, via Montebelluna From the south: Highway exit Padova Est, via Castelfranco From the west: Highway exit Vicenza Est, via Cittadella - Castelfranco Highway exit Dueville, via Bassano 

 By train:

 The closest railway stations are:

 [Castelfranco Veneto](/europe/italy/veneto/castelfrancoveneto), km. 16 Bassano del Grappa, km. 16 Montebelluna, km. 15 From [Milan](/europe/italy/lombardia/milan) you can take the direct line "Freccia delle Dolomiti" to [Castelfranco Veneto](/europe/italy/veneto/castelfrancoveneto).
