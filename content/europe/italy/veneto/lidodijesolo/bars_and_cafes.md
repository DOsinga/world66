---
title: "Bars and Cafes in Lido di Jesolo"
type: section
---

A holiday in Lido di Jesolo, Italy can lead to a relaxing, calm feeling while you bask in the sun of the Italian riviera. The area provides plenty of sites, activities and places to eat. The Lido di Jesolo bars and cafes serve the needs of everyone, from those that want to experience the typical Italian sea side cuisine to those who want a taste of home.

**The Black Cat Pub**

For those visitors that desire a taste of something more familiar, The Black Cat Pub could be just what you need. A traditional Irish style pub to its very core, the customers rate everything high, including the atmosphere, food, value and hospitality. Although Irish by style, The Black Cat draws crowds from around the world, including a few locals. Sky television is available for those sports fans that must keep tabs on their favorite teams. It is located on Piazza Manncini in the center of Lido di Jesolo. They can be contacted by phone at 0421 372014.

**Hotel Cavaleri Palace**

For a taste of local cuisine hand picked by the Hotel chef, the restaurant at Hotel Cavaleri Palace rates incredibly high. Although inside one of Lido di Jesolo's finest hotels, the restaurant is open for the public to enjoy. Saturday brings a breakfast buffet fit for a king, featuring both Mediterranean and international dishes. A poolside snack bar available at midday gives both guests of the hotel and members of the public a chance to experience local cuisine. The menu is varied with light, healthy snacks to full course Italian lunches available only to guests. The Hotel Cavalieri Palace is located on Via Mascagni 1 in Lido di Jesolo. Reservation are recommended for those not staying in the hotel. They can be made by calling 0421 971 969.

**Marechiaro Cafe**

For a good time either outdoors or in, try the Marechiaro Cafe. A unique blend of English and Italian styles, guests flock to this beach side cafe for great drinks, snacks and ocean breezes. Classic style snacks, both Venetian and Anglo-American, are featured on their menu. Enjoy a beautiful ocean view by sitting out on their terrace, or stay inside and mingle with locals or other holiday makers. Guests give the food the highest marks but claim the drinks can be slightly pricey, costing between 8 to 10 euros. The Marechiaro Cafe is located Via Bafile 360 in Lido di Jesolo. They can be reached by phone at 0421 971836.
