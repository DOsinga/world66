---
title: "Beaches"
type: section
---

The beach at Lido di Jesolo runs for 15km along the Adriatic shore. It is well-maintained and organised: most of the beach is divided into private sections (stabilimenti), each run by a concessionaire who rents out umbrellas and sun loungers. The water is shallow and calm — good for families. There is also a free public beach (spiaggia libera), though it is smaller and without facilities. The sand is fine and golden; the summer sea temperature typically reaches 25–26°C.
