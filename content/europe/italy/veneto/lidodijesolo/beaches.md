---
title: "Beaches"
type: section
---

Lido di Jesolo is the ideal place for you to visit if you are a
beach lover. The whole area, close to the Adriatic Sea, is geared to the beach and tourists.

**Neat, Clean and Well-Organized**

The beach is kept very clean, neat and well organized. Each strop
is rented out to private promoters who make money by renting out beach
lounges and umbrellas. So you need not take anything except your
beachwear, a beach towel and some sunscreen. Everything else is
available on the beach. There are open showers at regular intervals and
public toilets, too.

**Eateries**

There are small eateries located at convenient distances from all
the small strips. So you need not step out of the beach even to eat.
Just swim and sunbathe and eat all day long. The beach-side shacks
serve everything from local delicacies to gourmet Mediterranean cuisine
and even cheap meat rolls that are quite filling.

This is an ideal destination if you want to relax by the beach. As
everything is well laid out and organized, you have to do nothing but
relax and relax again. The best way to de-stress after a long year of
work is to visit this beach and relax.

**Life Guards**

There are life guards and first aid points on the beach to serve all the tourists who flock to this great holiday destination.

This focus on helping tourists is great, but also lends an unreal
air to the whole scene. The town is unlike other Italian towns. The
rest of the town is all geared toward servicing the beach. There are
not many cultural places to visit.

**Free Beach**

There is a free beach, also. However, this is not kept clean and there are no life guards.

You will love a visit to Lido if you love beaches. There is not much else that you can do at Lido.

From here you can make a day trip to Venice to see the sights. That is the only cultural attraction close by.

Near the beach are aquariums and theme parks. There is also a boat
ride. These attractions are great for children and families with young
children. There is also a golf course for those with an inclination for
this game.
