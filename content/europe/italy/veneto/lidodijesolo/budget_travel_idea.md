---
title: "Budget Travel Ideas in Lido di Jesolo"
type: section
---

When it
comes to seeking adventures abroad on a budget, Lido di Jesolo, Italy does no wrong. Unlike
Monaco, Paris, New York or Nice, Lido di Jesolo can be visited
on a minute budget. Here are several ideas on how tourists and
visitors can save heaps of money on their travels to Lido di Jesolo,
Italy.

**What to Do**

Lido di Jesolo hosts many beautiful sights, scenic
attractions and fun hangouts. The most popular attraction is the
wonderful beaches. The best part about the
beach is that it's free. Tourists and locals enjoy the clean,
beautiful and warm Mediterranean waters. At the right time, tourists
can partake in one of the many swimming competitions or volleyball
tournaments.

The nightlife scene is incredible, featuring jazz clubs
such as Bacaro Jazz, which has an excellent tapas menu and world class
entertainment for cheap every night of the week. Many legends such as
Frankie “Stone Cold” Johnson, Toots Belmondo, and Frederico Avalone
have been featured performers. The streets of Lido di Jesolo, Italy are
lined with Irish pubs. The Guinness never runs out at the Fiddler’s Elbow. They have a menu which features inexpensive
pub favorites such as colcannon, blood sausage, and fish and chips.

**Where to Eat **

Lido de Jesolo features cheap eats to no end. Masses of
food can be had for pennies throughout the city. Along with Gelato
stands, many food carts line the streets offering Italian favorites,
barbecued meats, Italian sandwiches and Carpaccio – a local favorite.
Lining the beach are hordes of snack carts selling fresh fruit, cold
drinks, more gelato and pickled pig feet. For a huge meal, Jimmy
Laconte’s is a wonderful all-you-can eat buffet selling many local
favorites.

**Where to Stay **

Lido de Jesolo hosts a variety of hotels,
apartments, villa's, vacation houses, bungalows and camping villages.
For the backpacker on a budget, an insider secret is the Brachiole
de Jesolo hostel where during the right season, a visitor can stay for
$8 a night.

**Transportation **

Unlike the Canals of Venice, the public
transportation of Lido de Jesolo is very inexpensive. The local government
subsidizes the Taxi companies to promote tourism. The metro service is
the least expensive in Italy. Rental cars can be had for the tourists
desiring more independence.

Lido de Jesolo
offers a cornucopia of inexpensive delights for the traveler on a
budget. It has cheap eats, entertainment, hotel stays and
transportation.
