Lido di Jesolo Italy is a town with a resort feel. It comprises 15 km of golden sand beaches, but still caters to a variety of ages and tastes. Although Lido di Jesolo has a vibrant nightlife, there are still several travel options for Lido di Jesolo family travel.

**Accomodation**

Being a resort town, Lido di Jesolo offers the expected types of accommodation. High rise hotels decorate the shoreline for those wishing to relax in the sand and sea by day and take in the night life after the sun goes down. What surprises some travelers is how families can also enjoy the beauty of Lido di Jesolo, but still maintain a relaxing atmosphere to their holiday. Families that desire a quiet place to stay with more privacy will be pleased with the selection of apartments, apart-hotels and family cottages that are available for hire in Lido di Jesolo. The price range averages approximately 40 Euros per night, with most within walking distance from the sea. The Lido di Jesolo tourist information board is an excellent source for families looking for private and quiet accommodation. They can be visited on line at www.tourismojesoloeraclea.it.

**Activities**

One of the most obvious attractions that families can take part in at Lido di Jesolo is the beautiful beach. Families staying at one of the beach front hotels will enjoy some degree of privacy, as each hotel has its own designated section. The beach services are free to guests of the hotel. Another great activity for parents and children is the Aqualandia water park. Set in a Caribbean style theme, endless water activities and games are on offer, as well as amateur golf. The most current information on Aqualandia prices and services is available via their website at www.aqaulandia.it.

**Eating Out**

Lido di Jesolo provides several options for family dining. If your family's palate isn't tuned to the delectable array of sea food choices in the town then fear not. The Bounty Pub Pizzeria located at Via Andrea Bafile 30 offers a tasty alternative. Set in a traditional pub/bar atmosphere, pub style food and Italian-style pizzas will certainly please the flock. Several snack bars and ice cream parlors also line the beaches and main streets of Lido di Jesolo for those looking for a quick snack.
