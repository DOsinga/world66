---
title: "Getting Around in Lido di Jesolo"
type: section
---

The beach town of Lido di Jesolo is a very short drive away from the bigger town of Jesolo. Lido di Jesolo
attracts thousands of both domestic and international visitors each
year because of the town's beautiful 15 kilometer extension of beaches.**

Driving around the Gulf of Venezia**

Lido di Jesolo is located right at the Gulf of Venezia, in between the towns of Cavalino-Treporti on the west and Caorle on the east. Getting to Jesolo is quite easy through the A4 heading North and taking the Noventa di Piave exit. The distance between Cavalino-Treporti and Lido di Jesolo is about 20 kilometers or a 30 minute drive. The distance between Caorle and Lido di Jesolo and Caorle is about 30 kilometers and is about a 45 minute drive. The main highways that lead into Lido di Jesolo are the Via Caposile (SP43, which turns into Via Adriatico) and the Via Trestina Bassa (SP47). Once in Lido di Jesolo, the main avenue is the Via Danimarca. Common landmarks used by locals are the Park Hotel Brasilia, the Katia Hotel Lido di Jesolo, the Presidio Ospedaliero Jesolo and the Fondazione Marzotto.**

Hotel Shuttles**

Renting a car in Lido di Jesolo is the best way to get around the Gulf of Venezia,
however most hotels do offer shuttle services between partner hotels
and the main tourist attractions. When making your hotel reservation,
make sure to ask about the complimentary shuttle services, particularly
the routes and frequency.**

Taxi Services**

Taxi services are available around Lido di Jesolo but you need to make a reservation at least 30 minutes in advance. Also, taxi services are not available after midnight.
