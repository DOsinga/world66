---
title: "Getting Around"
type: section
---

Lido di Jesolo is a long, thin resort town — most of the action is along the beachfront or on the parallel Viale Bafile. Cycling is an excellent way to get around; bicycles can be rented from numerous outlets along the boulevard. The ATVO bus connects Jesolo town and the various beach areas. Taxis are available but expensive by Italian standards.
