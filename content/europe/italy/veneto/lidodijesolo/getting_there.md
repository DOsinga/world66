---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

The nearest airports are [Venice](/europe/italy/veneto/venice) Marco Polo (30km) and Treviso. From Marco Polo, ATVO buses run directly to Lido di Jesolo in about 40 minutes. From [Venice](/europe/italy/veneto/venice) Piazzale Roma, ATVO buses run hourly; from the Alilaguna dock at Punta Sabbioni (reachable by water bus from Venice) it is a short bus ride into town.
