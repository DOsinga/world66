---
title: "Shopping"
type: section
---

Shopping in Lido di Jesolo is resort-focused: beachwear, sunglasses, and souvenirs dominate the boutiques along Viale Bafile. For Venetian glassware and crafts, the shops in [Venice](/europe/italy/veneto/venice) and on Murano island are far superior. A weekly market runs during summer with clothing and local produce.
