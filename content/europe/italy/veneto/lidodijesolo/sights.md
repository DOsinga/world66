---
title: "Sights in Lido di Jesolo"
type: section
---

Lido di Jesolo, located in north-east Italy offers miles and miles of sandy beaches, a very happening nightlife and attracts thousands of tourists every year. The town tries to make the tourist’s experience as special as possible with the activities, facilities and atmosphere designed to suit the need of the modern traveler.

**HIT THE BEACH
**

The beach is the perfect destination for fun and frolic for the whole family. The Shimmering beaches, the golden sand and the crystal clear water make it perfect for swimming, sun bathing or just relaxing.
Italians are said to be sports enthusiasts, and that is exactly what you get here, adventure sports, water sports and many more, for the whole family.
There are abundant hotels, shops and pub/karaoke bars making it the ideal holiday and party destination.

**WILD WATER
**

Lido di Jesolo’s very own water-theme park, Aqualandia is one place that the children would look forward to. A visitor can choose from water rides, live shows and even dance classes. It is open daily from 10 am to 6 pm.The older members can opt for the mini golf course that is found just outside the park. Later you can hop on the "Jolly Rodger", a boat which takes the tourists on a pirate theme ride.

**
THE LITORALE DI CAVALLINO
**

It is a long stretch of reclaimed agricultural land that has been recently developed with modern settlements. It is a popular destination for wine tasters, sun lovers and camp goers.

**OLD JESOLO**

If ancient artifacts are what you fancy, then Old Jesolo would be a great location. There are ruins of an ancient church that take you back to an era forgotten.

**PLAY VILLAGE**

The Play Village, started around 10 years back is a renowned centre for sports and fitness enthusiasts. Equipped with a skate park, tennis court, baby corner, bar snack restaurant, this recreational centre mainly aims at ensuring everyone has a good time.

**
PISTA AZZURRA GO-KART CIRCUIT
**

The 50 year old Pista Azzurra Go-Kart Race Track is a globally recognized track. Many national and international go kart competitions have taken place here and the popularity is constantly increasing. When a race isn’t happening, the cars are available on rent by anyone who wishes to see how good he is on the go-kart track.

**
NATURAL HISTORY TOWN MUSEUM
**

This museum has 15,000 specimens of local flora, fauna on offer. It also has some additional marvelous items on display that have geological and archaeological importance.
