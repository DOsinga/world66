---
title: "Bars and Cafes"
type: section
---

Mestre has a genuine local bar scene that functions entirely independently of the tourist trade across the bridge. Prices are lower than Venice and the clientele is mostly Italian. The area around Piazza Ferretto is the best place to start.

**Al Vapor**

Live music from 9:30pm most nights. Call 041 930 796 to reserve a table (Tue–Sat, 6–7pm). The venue also shows work by local artists.

**Little Bear**

An Australian-style pub popular with locals. Good range of draught and bottled beers including Guinness and Belgian options. Food runs to sandwiches, bruschette, and Italian-style snacks. Closed Mondays.

**Macao Bar**

A compact lounge bar, good for a drink before heading out or winding down after. Open from 8am to 2am. Warm interior, not trying to be anything it isn't.

**Vittoria Nike Bar**

Open from 7am — serves breakfast, lunch, and evening cocktails. DJ nights on Fridays and Saturdays. A neighbourhood bar that covers all bases.
