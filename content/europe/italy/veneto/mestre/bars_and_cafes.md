**Enjoy Your Favorite Cuisine in Mestre **

Mestre is one of the scenic destinations in Venice that also stands out for its gastronomical attractions, with a number of bars, cafes, and restaurants, so let us have a look at some of the most popular **Mestre bars and cafes. **

**Al Vapor **

This is not only a great place to savor your favorite delicacies but also a great choice for those looking to have a vibrant nightlife in Mestre. Live music shows are conducted here from 09:30 PM, which usually lasts until midnight. Since live shows attract a large number of people everyday, you can reserve your table in advance by giving them a call at 041 930 796 anytime between 18:00 and 19:00 hrs from Tuesday through Saturday. This venue also exhibits artwork by local artists.

**Little Bear **

An Australian pub, this dining option in Mestre is especially noted for its interesting as well as unique ambiance. It enables you to sip your favorite beer, let it be Guinness, SLALOM strong lager, or Belgian beer, while having some fun and entertainment with your friends. Apart from these, it is also a great place to relish a wide range of other specialties, such as rolls, Club Sandwich, toasts, bruschette, Nachos with hot sauce, sandwich with chicken sauce, grilled vegetables with toasted cheese, and Italian specialties like piadine. The pub is open every day except for Monday.

In addition, Mestre is known for its patisseries.
