**Awesome Biking Tour from Mestre to Venice**

**Mestre Day** **Trips** can have eventful exploration into the neighboring towns or villages. Mestre, in Veneto, Italy is a romantic place of beauty and calmness. If you want to absorb the Italian colorful culture, then opt for a biking tour. There are 6 phases of the biking tour.

**Biking Tour**

The Biking Tour will start from Veneto all the way to Venice. This is a self-cycling tour, which can bring you all the way to Treviso, Venice. For a day&rsquo;s trip, you can start from Veneto and stop at Treviso, which is about 17 miles from Mestre. There, you can pass by the Burcis cemetery, which lies sunken in the river. These sunken barges became home to different bird species and fowls. At Treviso, you can explore the gentle rolling terrain. You can also visit the archaic but superb structure of Villa Palladio, and the wine producers of the village.

**Price of Trip**

The price is individualized based on what you want to spend on food and beverages. You may own the bike, or have rented it somewhere, so this is your major expenditure.
