---
title: "Things to do in Mestre"
type: section
---

Mestre is home to many histories and rich cultures. Though it is not the most popular city in Europe that people visit, it probably offers a lot more than most tourists imagine. Getting to Mestre can be done by either bus or plane, and getting around can be fun by riding a bicycle or walking among the thick crowd of pedestrians. Below is a list of some great **Mestre things to do **once you're there.

**Mestre Sights
**

There are a lot of sights in Mestre you can visit to relive its fascinating history. The city is home to a beautiful and majestic watchtower, and Mestre also has several theaters where you can watch a variety of shows. This city also has a piazza which is filled with many restaurants and eateries, where you can eat outside to enjoy the warmth of the sun while you eat. Also, located at the heart of all this food, is a contemporary water display which is really a sight to behold.

**Parks in Mestre**

Mestre has two major parks where people can go take pictures and eat together. You can also use these parks to get some exercise while traveling. Both these parks are equipped with amenities like basketball courts, skating rinks, tracks for runners, and swimming pools. For the children--and children at heart--Mestre also has a lot of children's parks, as well as a mini train to ride.

**Shopping in Mestre**

Another great way to get some light exercise is to check out the shopping Mestre has to offer. During weekdays, feast your eyes with its many stores where you can buy designer clothes, fresh vegetables and fruits.

**Enjoy Mestre’s Great Accommodations**

Mestre is home to a lot of hotels with courteous staffs. Many of them will work for your budget. They have four economy hotels which offer 5-star accommodation for people with smaller budgets. For people who can spend a little more, Mestre has more than 20 hotels with middle-class amenities and services. All of these establishments are very close to the sights in Mestre, as well as the city's exquisite bars and restaurants. These hotels have the most modern features, and almost always offer the signature breakfast in bed. If you are feeling a little lucky, some of these hotels also have casinos where you can play all your favorite card games.
