---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Mestre isn't a rival to Venice — it's a mainland town that works well as a base and has a few genuine attractions of its own. The main square, Piazza Ferretto, is pleasant for an evening stroll, ringed by bars and restaurants with a working watchtower from the 12th century overhead. The Candiani Cultural Center runs a year-round programme of music, theatre, and film that's more interesting than anything you'd find at a tourist-aimed Venice venue.
