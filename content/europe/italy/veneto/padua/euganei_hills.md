The
Euganean Hills are an area rich in character, from hill to
hill, along the Wine Route, the landscape is a succession of perfect
geometry of vineyards, villages and medieval castles, Palladian villas
and monasteries, all surrounded by lush vineyards and oak and chusnuts woods.

The '[Parco Regionale dei Colli Eugane](/europe/italy/veneto/padua)i' created to protect this zone of volcanic hills formed 35 million years ago, has a special guide of over 200 paths and routes for exploring the zone. Most are signposted
and are perfect for excursions on foot or by bike.
