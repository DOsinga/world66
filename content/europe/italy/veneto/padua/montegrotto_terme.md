The best resource for sights, hotels, restaurants, bars, what to do and see

**Montegrotto Terme **and** **Abano Terme are the ancient home of
Aponus, god of thermal waters and curative virtues, honored thousands
of years ago by the Venetians and Romans, who left a precious heritage
of wisdom and the tradition of the pleasures of thermal cures and
treatment. Today this tradition has been taken up by the ** wellness centers **of** Abano **and** Montegrotto Terme** which have made wellness their mission.
