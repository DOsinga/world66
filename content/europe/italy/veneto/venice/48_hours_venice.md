A tightly written 48-hour schedule curated by local Time Out editors, structured as a precise morning-afternoon-evening itinerary across two days. Leans toward art-and-culture travelers who also want to eat and drink well. Particularly strong at pairing each neighborhood (Dorsoduro, San Marco, Cannaregio) with nearby eating and drinking recommendations. Best for visitors who want both the Frari and the right bacaro to follow it with.
