Glamorous hotel bar with the kind of decor you'd expect to find in Venice -- think chandeliers, plush seating, and moody lighting. The cocktail list leans classic but is well executed, and the bartenders know their way around a Negroni. Beer starts around 4-5 euros; cocktails run higher. A good spot for a late-night drink away from the tourist crowds in San Marco.
