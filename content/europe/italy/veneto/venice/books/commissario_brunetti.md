The first of Leon's long-running crime series set in Venice, in which the cultured and morally serious Commissario Brunetti investigates a conductor's murder at La Fenice opera house. Leon has lived in Venice for decades and the novels are exceptional on the city's daily life — the bureaucracy, the flooding, the relationship between Venetians and tourists. Any volume works as an entry point.
