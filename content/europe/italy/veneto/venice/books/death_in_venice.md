author: "Thomas Mann"

A German writer on holiday in Venice becomes obsessed with a beautiful Polish boy and stays as cholera spreads through the city. Mann wrote it in 1912 and it captures something essential about Venice — the way the city's beauty feels inseparable from its decay and claustrophobia. Short enough to read in an afternoon; it will stay with you for the entire trip.
