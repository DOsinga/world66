Make sure to be on time in this charming trattoria, because people might kill for a seat and meal after 8PM. Closed on Mondays.

in July 2007 we tried the Al Cugnai on Piscina Forner near the Academia, family run and headed by the apparently famous crones, one of whom seemed to take a shine to us. Unpretentious but good food.
