This is probably the most renowned coffee shop in the world. Set under the arcades of the Procuratie Nuove in St. Mark’s Square, Venice, the Caffè Florian may rightfully claim to be one of the city’s symbols. Opened on 29 December 1720 by Floriano Francesconi under the name Venezia Trionfante, or Triumphant Venice, it soon became known by its patrons as "Florian’s".
