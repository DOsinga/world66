La Rivista is a very new place where to have dinner in Venice, a
little out from the standard touristic areas, but yet not far. Next to
the accademia bridge and art Gallery. Creative italian kitchen with
venetian inspiration, nice wine list at a reasonable price in summer is
also possible to dine outside. Closed on Monday.
