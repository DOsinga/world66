Trattoria Da Fiore (since 1984) has made just about every top ten list around. It's upscale, expensive, and it's one of the most popular restaurant in the region. The menu features innovative seafood dishes such as canapes with whipped stockfish, sardines "in soar," and grilled cuttlefish with fried vegetables from artichokes to squash blossoms. The interior offers two dining rooms with around eleven tables. And finally, the wine list is extensive, so be prepared to make some tough decisions.
