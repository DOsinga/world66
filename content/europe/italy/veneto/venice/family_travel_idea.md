---
title: "Family Travel Ideas"
type: section
---

Venice is a nice
place to take slightly older children and introduce them to the iconic
art, culture and beauty of the classic European destination. Here are
some family travel ideas to make the most of your vacation to Venice.

**Take Boat Rides**

Take enchanting boat rides through the beautiful canals and alleys of the city. These romantic rides let visitors view snapshots of
Venice’s cultural magnificence and are the most entertaining,
and convenient modes of discovering the pristine charm
of the bewitching islands.

**The Glass Island**

This is a sure-shot winner with the kids. The island of Murano is home to the world famous art of Murano glass. There
are artisans working on exquisite art pieces molded with fine glass.
Children enjoy the factory tour and buying glass beads, fish bowls,
Christmas decorations and glass miniature candies from souvenir
shops.

**Burano and Torcello**

The twin islands are located at about an hour from the heart of
Venice and are a great family retreat from the touristy bustle of
Venice. The destinations feature bright hand-painted homes and lush
greenery along with relics of ancient art and architecture.

**Pigeon-Feed at Piazza San Marco**

Young kids will love feeding huge flocks of pigeons at the main
Piazza San Marco center. Buy a bag of grains and let the children pose
for pictures with the affable pigeons perched on their head. There are
several street-side cafes and vendors selling corn along with a few
ice-cream parlors.

**Eating Out **

Most restaurants offer dishes that can be customized
and personalized according to children's preferences in most restaurants and the
pickier kids can even eat at pizzerias sprinkled throughout Venice. For
children yearning for comfort fast food, Mc Donald’s and all major fast
food chains have their outlet in the city.

Venice is a great place to acquaint your children with the snapshots
of a classic, fine and timeless European culture and makes for a
fun-filled, family holiday destination.
