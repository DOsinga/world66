The Regatta (naval review) Vogalonga is held, yearly, in Venice in mid
May. The regatta which started in 1975 preserved the tradition from the
middle ages where the Italian republics sailed in the Mediterranean Sea
in Amalfi and Genua, on the Arno River in Pisa and in the Venice's
lagoon. More than 400 boats in various kinds and sizes from 50 Venice's
clubs and private boats from all over the world participate in this
wonderful event.
