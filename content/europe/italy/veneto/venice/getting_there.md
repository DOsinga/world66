---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

Flights to Venice arrive at the Marco Polo Airport on the eastern edge of the lagoon. From there, you take an ACTV bus nr. 5 to the city centre, which leaves every 30 minutes. Other, more expensive, options are the ATVO buses or waterbuses. 



When you enter by train, you will arrive at the Santa Lucia train station. 

If you are arriving by car, which is not advisable, you can park either at the Piazzale Roma car park or on Tronchetto (an artificial island). Be prepared to queue for a long time and a steep price.
