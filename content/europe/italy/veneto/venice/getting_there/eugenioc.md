From Marco Polo Airport, the Alilaguna water-bus costs around €13 for a 75-minute boat trip to San Marco via Murano, Lido, and Arsenale. Water taxis are faster (30 minutes) but cost around €100. Tickets can be bought in advance at venicelink.com.
