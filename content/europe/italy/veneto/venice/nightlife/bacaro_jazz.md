You can call this Venice restaurant a small-plate restaurant, a tapas restaurant, a *Cichetti* restaurant or one of Venice's best bars. Whatever you call it, one thing is clear -- Bacaro is one of the top spots in Venice for tasty fare and listening to Jazz. Bacaro has an excellent selection of Jazz music from Miles Davis and John Coltrane to Billy Holiday and Ella Fitzgerald. The bar has one of the largest selection of cocktails in Venice as well. The kitchen is open from 1:00 p.m.-2:00 a.m. (possibly the latest open kitchen in the city) and the bar has it's famous happy hour between 5:00 p.m.-7:30 p.m. Bacaro Jazz is located in the center of Venice in front of the Central post office of the Rialto Bridge. It is a 10-minute walk from the train station and from San Marco square.
