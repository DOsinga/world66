Welcome to **Raging Pub**! Fired up and raging everyday until 2 AM.

**Raging Pub** is a trendy and upbeat pub-style bar conviniently found in the very heart of Venice's nightlife
scene - Campo Santa Margherita. Embelished in sports and drinking
apareal, we provide a laid back yet lively atmosphere where anyone can
kick back and have fun. With only the best in beer and spirits, whether
you are looking to a good spot to relax and meet people from all over
the world, have a good meal or have an unforgettable visit in Venice, a
good time is always guaranteed.

Our menu is extensive, with all the best in Italian eatery, served daily through dinner. With
multiple televisions, every seat is front row for the biggest sporting
events in the world - from soccer to boxing, from rugby to golf, we are
fans of every sport. Continue the fun with our signature cocktails or
our top-notch beer on draft served by our friendly bartenders. Please
come join us for lunch, dinner or a night on the town!
