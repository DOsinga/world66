Written by a solo travel blogger who has visited Venice multiple times, this is an opinionated rather than encyclopedic guide — it prioritizes wandering and getting lost over cramming in every museum. Combines a few essential sights (Doge's Palace, Rialto Bridge) with a strong emphasis on the evening aperitivo scene in Cannaregio. Best for first-timers who want a local flavor rather than a checklist.
