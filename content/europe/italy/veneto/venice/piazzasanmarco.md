title: Piazza San Marco
type: poi

This spacious square remains impressive to every visitor. Whether it is packed with tourists or nearly deserted in the early morning hours, it's one of the sights you'll remember longest. The piazza houses the Basilica di San Marco, the Campanile (99 metres high, with excellent views), the Torre dell'Orologio, the Palazzo Ducale, and the Procuratie. Arrive early to avoid the worst of the crowds.
