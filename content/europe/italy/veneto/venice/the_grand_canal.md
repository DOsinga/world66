title: The Grand Canal
type: poi

Grand views, galleries, museums, and beautiful Venetian Gothic palaces line the Grand Canal. The best way to see it is on the No. 1 vaporetto, which stops at every landing stage along the 3.8km route. The canal begins at Piazzetta San Marco and ends at the railway station.
