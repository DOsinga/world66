Peggy Guggenheim's personal collection of 20th-century art, housed in her former home on the Grand Canal in Dorsoduro. Strong on Cubism, Abstract Expressionism, and Surrealism — Picasso, Kandinsky, Duchamp, Pollock, Dalí. One of the most visited museums in Venice. Guggenheim herself is buried in the garden alongside her dogs.
