Some of the most interesting items are on display at Museo Storico Navale such as a lavish gondola that belonged to Peggy Guggenheim and several sheds where several historic barges are displayed. The Museum has three floors. The first contains a collection of Second World War torpedoes and artillery pieces as well as a 16th century frieze by Fernando Bertelli. The second floor contains a collection of model ships and it is also dedicated to naval hero Admiral Morosini. The third floor houses several decorative 17th and 18th century gondola prows as well as large collection of traditional fishing gear.
