H-play is an Italian company which uses game as a medium for experience, discovering and socialisation. We're working at "The Ruyi Venice Act" a project for launching a new touristic itinerary through the city of Venice: it's something like a treasure hunt , so to speak. Every participant will be provided by a special coded guide-book; by leaving one's own cell-phone number each tourist will receive every cue for the game, step by step. Technology in the service of cultural tourism and fun, this is what the whole thing is about. In this time w're working to the beta period.
You'll find more information at http://www.theruyi.com/.
