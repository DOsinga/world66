**By car, bus/coach: ** Verona is easily reached by taking the A4 Motorway SERENISSIMA (Milan/Venice, exit for the city centre: Verona Sud) or by taking the A22 Motorway Brennero-Modena (exit: Verona Nord).

  **By train: ** The main railway station is VERONA PORTA NUOVA, which is at the crossroads of both the Milan-Venice line and the Brennero-Rome line. There are direct trains and InterCity trains from all the main railwy stations in the North of Italy throughout the day (for train information: tel. 147-888088 from 7.00 am to 9.00 pm).

  **By plane: ** Verona's international airport CATULLO in Villafranca (phone 0458095666, from 6:00 am to 12:00 pm) is situated approximately 10 Km S-W of the city centre. There is a shuttle bus service to and from the airport every 20 minutes approx. from 6.10 am to 11.30 pm (for information tel. 0458004129; ticket L. 7.000). The airport bus terminal is outside Porta Nuova Railway Station. (For flight information: tel. 8095666 from 6.00 am to 11.30 pm).
