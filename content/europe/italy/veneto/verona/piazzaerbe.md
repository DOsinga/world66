Verona's oldest market square, built on the site of the Roman forum. Surrounded by medieval and Renaissance buildings — Palazzo Maffei, the Lamberti Tower, Casa Mazzanti with its 16th-century frescoes. Market stalls under large white umbrellas sell fruit, clothing, and souvenirs. One of the most picturesque squares in northern Italy.
