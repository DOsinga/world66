---
title: "Shopping"
type: section
---

Via Mazzini is Verona's main shopping street, connecting Piazza Bra to Piazza Erbe: pedestrianised, lined with fashion chains and independent boutiques, and busy in the evenings. Corso Porta Borsari, running west from Piazza Erbe, has a mix of antiques and wine shops. The daily market on Piazza Erbe sells fruit, vegetables, and souvenirs; the Saturday market spreads further and includes clothing and household goods. For wine, Bottega dei Vini (Vicolo Scudo di Francia 3) has an excellent cellar for both drinking and buying to take home.
