Verona's cathedral, consecrated in 1187, is a superb composite of Romanesque and Gothic forms. The double porch at the entrance, with columns on stylised lions, is the work of Nicolò. Inside, the main artistic highlight is Titian's Assumption above the altar in the Nichesola Chapel — his only work in Verona.
