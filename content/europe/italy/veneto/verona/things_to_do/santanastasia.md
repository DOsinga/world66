Verona's largest Gothic church, completed in the 15th century but started in 1290. The interior is impressive — frescoes including a famous Pisanello fresco depicting St. George, and works from pupils of Giotto. Easy to miss despite its size, hidden in the streets behind Piazza Erbe.
