title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Vicenza is essential for anyone interested in architecture. Andrea Palladio (1508–1580) moved here as a young man and used the city as his laboratory — his work here influenced everything from English country houses to the US Capitol. The main showcase is the Palazzo della Ragione (the Basilica Palladiana), but the Teatro Olimpico — the oldest surviving indoor theatre in the world, still with its original stage set — is equally extraordinary. Walking the city's streets is itself the experience; Palladian buildings appear around every corner.
