Travel Guide

Daugavpils based on the southeast of Latvia, near to borders of Lithunia, Belarus and Russia. It is the second biggest city of Latvia Republic. You can visit wild nature,jungles,a big fortress and many historical buildings...

In that city, mostly Russian minority lives; latvian,polish and other nationalities as well.
