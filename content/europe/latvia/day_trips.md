There are an endless array of day trips out of Riga. You can help organise these through www.out-there.eu

### [Daytrips out of Riga](/europe/latvia/day_trips/daytrips_out_of_ri)

Out-There.eu can organise a daytrip out of the Capital to some of Latvia's premier tourist destinations. Why not take the time to read about the daytrips we have on offer and escape from the hustle and bustle of Riga to some of the quieter and more tranquil must see's in Latvia's beautiful countryside and idyllic coast line.
