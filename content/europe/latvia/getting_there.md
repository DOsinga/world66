Getting to and from Latvia

### [Budget airlines which fly to Latvia

Use the European Low Cost Airline Guide search engine ( http://www.low-cost-airline.info](/europe/latvia/getting_there/budget_airlines_wh)) to find out which budget airlines such as Easyjet and Ryanair who fly into Latvia's main airport, Riga.

Often you can save many $/£/whatever by using these airlines instead of the main carriers.
