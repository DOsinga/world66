Latvia is located on the Baltic Sea, between [Lithuania](/europe/lithuania) and [Estonia](/europe/estonia). The country itself may be less famous than its capital [Riga](/europe/latvia/riga), one of the most beautiful cities of the Baltic region. About a third of the population of Latvia lives in the capital and for all purposes it is the best base for exploring the country. Since Latvia is relatively small, most of the other sights can be seen as daytrips from the capital.

[Jurmala](/europe/latvia/jurmala) is the best beach resort to go to. It has been popular with city dwellers from Latvia for over 150 years and the city houses some great Jugendstil architecture. Apart from the beaches there are also forests here where you can go hiking.
