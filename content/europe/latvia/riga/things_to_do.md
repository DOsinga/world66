title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Riga's chief draw is its **Art Nouveau** architecture — the city has the largest concentration of Jugendstil buildings of any in the world, mostly built in a frenzy between 1900 and 1914, with the densest cluster on Alberta iela and Elizabetes iela. The **Old Town** (Vecrīga) is compact, UNESCO-listed, and walkable in an afternoon; the Dome Cathedral, St Peter's, and the reconstructed House of the Blackheads are the set pieces. The **Central Market** in five huge repurposed Zeppelin hangars is one of the better markets in northern Europe.
