Liechtenstein's oldest inn, dating back over 600 years, sits on Herrengasse a short walk from the main pedestrian street. The restaurant serves a mix of international and regional dishes — expect well-made schnitzel, game in season, and an extensive wine list weighted towards local bottles. The atmosphere is formal without being stiff. Herrengasse 35.
