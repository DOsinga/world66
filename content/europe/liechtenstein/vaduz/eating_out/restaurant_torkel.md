Torkel sits in the middle of the Herawingert vineyard, inside a historic wine-pressing building belonging to the Prince of Liechtenstein. The chef specialises in freshwater fish and local produce, and the kitchen holds a Michelin star. Tables on the vine-shaded terrace are the best seats in Vaduz in summer. Book well in advance. Hintergass 9.
