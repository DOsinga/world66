Spread across a historic building and several branches, the National Museum covers the history, archaeology, and culture of Liechtenstein from prehistoric times to the present. The main building on Städtle houses permanent displays on the country's geology, natural history, and political development. The associated Postmuseum (Städtle 37) tells the surprisingly fascinating story of how Liechtenstein turned postage stamps into a significant source of national income — the collection of rare issues is one of the finest in Europe.
