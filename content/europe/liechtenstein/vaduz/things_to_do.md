---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Vaduz punches well above its size when it comes to museums. The Kunstmuseum, the National Museum, and the Postmuseum are all within a few minutes' walk of each other along Städtle, and together they make for a satisfying half-day. Above it all, the castle looms on its cliff — impossible to enter, but well worth the uphill walk for the views.
