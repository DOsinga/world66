---
title: "Shopping"
type: section
---

Druskininkai has the basics covered. Several supermarkets line Čiurlionio gatvė, including Maxima and IKI. For something more interesting, the open-air market nearby sells local produce, honey, and smoked meats. A second market — sometimes called the "Belarusian market" because of cross-border traders — is one bus stop further and is busiest on Fridays and Saturdays.
