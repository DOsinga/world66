**Supermarkets**

There are several self-service shops in Druskininkai. Two large and 1 small are in Ciurlionio street (Iki, Mini Maxima and Maxima)

**Marketplaces**

There are 2 marketplaces - one next to Iki and Maxima in Ciurlionio st., second ("belorussian" - open from 8 am, the biggest is on Friday and Saturday) one bus stop further.
