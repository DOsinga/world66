---
title: "Beaches"
type: section
---

Kaunas is landlocked but has several river and lake beaches that come alive in summer. The best is the **Kaunas Reservoir Beach** in Pazaislis Park, next to the monastery — clean water, good facilities, and a children's swimming area. Closer to the centre, **Panemunes Park Beach** sits on a loop of the Nemunas and is easy to reach on foot. **Lampedziai Lake Beach** in the west of the city is a smaller, quieter option surrounded by parkland.
