---
title: "Day Trips"
type: section
---

The **Hill of Crosses** near Siauliai is the most popular day trip from Kaunas — a pilgrimage site where thousands of crosses have been planted on a small hill since the 19th century. It is about two hours north by car or bus. Closer to hand, the town of **Trakai** with its island castle is roughly 90 minutes east via Vilnius. [Vilnius](/europe/lithuania/vilnius) itself is an easy day trip at just 100 km.
