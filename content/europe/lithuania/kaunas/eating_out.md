Kaunas has quite a nice selection of places where you can get something to eat. Here are some (don't skip the local beer!).

### [Bernelių Užeiga (Bajorkiemis)](/europe/lithuania/kaunas/eating_out/berneli_ueiga)

The "Bajorkiemis" (- manor of lords) is the biggest restaurant of the "Berneliu Uzeiga" brand. It is located on a fabulous landscape near the Kaunas sea (near the Kaunas - Vilnius highway). All the atmosphere is old-days-scented and many antiques decorate its yard, park and interior. The restaurant has excellent traditional Lithuanian dishes served by the staff wearing traditional attire.

Among the many entertainment this place offers you will find an antique shop, a barge-bar, a recreation zone for children, a sculpture park, a tiny petting zoo, an [more..](/europe/lithuania/kaunas/eating_out/berneli_ueiga)
