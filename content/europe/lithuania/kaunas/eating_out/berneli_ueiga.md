The "Bajorkiemis" (Manor of Lords) is the largest branch of the Berneliu Uzeiga restaurant chain, set in a sprawling complex near the Kaunas Reservoir off the Kaunas-Vilnius highway. The atmosphere leans hard into old Lithuania — antiques everywhere, staff in traditional dress, and a menu of hearty Lithuanian dishes. Beyond the restaurant there is a sculpture park, a petting zoo, a barge-bar on the water, and even a working forge. It is touristy but fun, and the food is genuinely good.
