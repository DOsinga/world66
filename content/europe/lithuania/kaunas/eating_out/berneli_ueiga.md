The "Bajorkiemis" (- manor of lords) is the biggest restaurant of the "Berneliu Uzeiga" brand. It is located on a fabulous landscape near the Kaunas sea (near the Kaunas - Vilnius highway). All the atmosphere is old-days-scented and many antiques decorate its yard, park and interior. The restaurant has excellent traditional Lithuanian dishes served by the staff wearing traditional attire.

Among the many entertainment this place offers you will find an antique shop, a barge-bar, a recreation zone for children, a sculpture park, a tiny petting zoo, an alehouse and a forge.

Opening hours: Mon 10:00-22:00, Tue-Thu 10:00-24:00.
