---
title: "Festivals in Kaunas"
type: section
---

**Kaunas festivals** are full of classical
and jazz music. Tourists will enjoy the city's many festivals, because
they are like big street parties, full of entertainment and dancing.

**Pazaislis Music Festival**

The biggest musical event in Lithuania is the Pazaislis music
festival. Visitors can enjoy the festivities for 3 months: June, July
and August. The number of performances at the Pazaislis Festival grows
constantly. Some of the artists are figures like Jacques Loussier, Andre
Arpino, Benoit Dunoyer de Segonzac and Aleksander Paley, among others.
The most outstanding Lithuanian musical styles are also featured at the
festival. There are also drama performances carried out at the Kaunas
castle, located near the convergence of Kauna's two rivers Nemunas and
Neris. There are many Kauna's festivals, however, Pazaislis has the best
jazz music.

**City's day in Kaunas
**

This is one of the most famous cultural events in Kaunas. It's
carried out on the 20th of May every year because in 1463 King
Kazimieras Jogailaitis renovated and extended the urban rights. The main
events are done on the streets and the old quarters plazas on May 18th,
19th and 20th. The city livens up, and the citizens gather to look at
the street fairs and shops, with the noise of local music. There are
costume parades, theatre processions, fairs and street stalls. The
celebration's main objective is to improve Kauna and Lithuania's image
in the world.

**Modern Dance International Festival**

The festival of the "Aura" dance is the most unique modern dance
theatre in Kaunas. This is the oldest and most important theatre in
Kaunas. The Aura spectacles have been presented and have won in various
festivals abroad during 1988 until 2007. People are delighed by the many
musical perfomances. Tourists can catch a glipse of Kaunas music and
history, by attending Kaunas Festivals and looking at the dances.
