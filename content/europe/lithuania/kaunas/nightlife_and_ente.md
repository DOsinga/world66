As the second largest city in Lithuania, **Kaunas nightlife** is filled with bars and nightclubs. Although the choices for entertainment is limited compared to some of the major cosmopolitan cities in other countries, you can rest assure that you do not have to stay inside your hotel room during night-time.

#### Bars and Nightclubs

With a large population of students, one can find several bars in Kaunas, most of which cater to the students in the area. The local bars are the most famous places to hang out both by the locals and tourists. Beer is the famous choice of drink since it is relatively inexpensive. There are several bars, however, that do offer wines aside from beer and cocktails. Belmontas and Brodveijus are considered as the best bars in the city.

With many new nightclubs opening up in the city, clubbing has become one of the popular night-time activities in Kaunas. There are several night spots in the city that cater to different crowds. Almost all of the clubs are situated in the city center so you can just walk around the area and take your pick. Most of the clubs are open until 4 a.m.. Heliosclub and Balti Drambliai are some of the best nightclubs in the area.

#### Musical and Drama Theaters

For avid theater goers, Kaunas is a good place to discover the Lithuanian performing arts since there are several theaters scattered around the city that presents many plays and shows. However, there are essentially three types of theaters in the city of Kaunas – musical, puppet and drama theater.

The Kaunas State Drama Theater is the biggest theater company in the city. It is also one of the oldest theater buildings that is still functioning in Kaunas, thus this theater is a historical site as well.

If you are more into musical plays then visit the Kaunas State Musical Theater which used to be the seat of modern drama, ballet, and opera theaters of Lithuania. This musical theater is a two-story building with a 500 square meter hall.

The Kaunas State Puppet Theater, on the other hand, is a theater devoted solely for puppet shows. The theater shows 20 puppet performances during theatrical season, five of which are often new plays.
