---
title: "Shopping in Kaunas"
type: section
---

The **Kaunas Shopping** experience is all about leisure and fun. There are several street-side stores as well as malls in this Lithuanian city of Kaunas. Read the guide below and explore the shopping options in this tourist destination.

**Laisves Aleja**

Laisves Aleja translates into The Freedom Avenue. This area is considered a mecca for shopping in the whole of Kaunas. The street is a 1.5-km long stretch and houses the best shops in town. The street provides a great atmosphere for shopping. Fountains, trees, shaded areas, benches all make for a great shopping experience. This stretch is mainly famous for clothes and all items related to fashion. The stores here open at 10:00 a.m. and close at 7:00 p.m. This is a famous street, hence finding it will not be a problem at all.

**Mega**

Enjoy your shopping spree in the relaxed and comfortable environment of this mall. Mega is one of the popular shopping malls in Kaunas and was opened in the year 2006. This fairly new mall has several stores selling clothes; make up, body and bath items and home d&eacute;cor. The mall also has a huge aquarium filled with various kinds of fish. The mall has a bowling alley and a food court for entertainment and snacking.

**Egles Galerija**

If you are an art lover then Kaunas has a lot in store for you. There are several art galleries in Kaunas but Egles Galerija stands out from the crowd. This art gallery is a great place to buy unique creations in the whole of Kaunas. It is located in Raguvos Gatve close to the Town hall. The paintings exhibited in this gallery are not very expensive and come in various sizes. You will also find paintings on a postcard here.

**Souvenir**

Having visited Kaunas it would be wise to take back some souvenirs and local memorabilia. The right place to buy these items of interest is the street side, located in the old town of Kaunas. You will find spectacular amber jewelry at this street. The prices are fixed, so you need not worry about being taken for a ride. Do not miss the chance to buy items made from the original linen fabric from this street. You can also find several local craft made out of clay at this souvenir street.
**
Knygynas Humanitas**

If you wish to buy books of any sort then the Knygynas Humanitas is the right store to go to. This bookstore is located next to the War Museum in the University basement. You will find books from all genres at this store. From fiction, self help, philosophy to local literature, this store stocks them all.
