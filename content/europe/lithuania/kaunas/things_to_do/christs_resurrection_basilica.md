This massive church on the hill above the New Town took over 70 years to complete — started when Lithuania gained independence in 1918, it was finished only in 2005. Reach it via the Green Hill Funicular for a memorable approach. The real reason to visit is the rooftop terrace, which offers panoramic views across Kaunas and the river valleys.
