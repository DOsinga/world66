The largest art museum in Lithuania, devoted to the work of M. K. Ciurlionis — a painter and composer who fused visual art with music in a style that anticipated abstract art by years. Beyond the Ciurlionis collection, the museum holds Lithuanian art from the 16th to 20th centuries and a fine collection of folk sculpture and painting.
