There are many things that should be known about **Kaunas: When To Go** is one of them. Tourists can visit during the spring time when the weather is relatively fresh and people can enjoy nature.

**Spring**

Spring is one of the more pleasant seasons to visit Kaunas. When to
go should be determined by the weather. Average temperatures are around
40 to 50 degrees F., with a slight wind chill.

It is recommended for tourists to dress warmly, and possibly bring or
buy a windbreaker jacket. Locals like to partake in vigorous indoor
activities, such as dancing, to keep them warm. The International Jazz
Festival begins in this season, so it is a great season to visit, and
join in on some warm activities.

**S****ummer**

Summer in Kaunas, like most other destinations, is the warmest season
of the year. Kaunas' average summer temperatures are anywhere between
55 degrees F., and 75 degrees F.

Summer is the most popular season of the year to visit Kaunas because
it is the least chilling season of the year, and it is possible for
tourists to wear their regular apparel, rather than piling jacket after
jacket on themselves.

Even though summer is the warmest season of the year, going to local
cafes for a hot drink at night is still a very popular thing to do among
people within the city of Kaunas. when to go is not that hard to
determine since summer seems to be an inviting season.

**Autumn**

Autumn is a very rainy season in Kaunas, and with an average of 50 to
60 degrees F. Traveling with a raincoat, long boots, and umbrella is
reccommended because with the volume of the rain, streets may become
flooded due to weak sewer systems. Local bars and restaurants close
early during this season because the winds come very strong and cold,
which in Kaunas, is a sign of snow arriving.

**Winter**

In winter seasons, the climate becomes hard to deal with, reaching
below-zero temperatures. Tourists are recommended to bring thick and
warm clothing to avoid intolerable weather situations.

Winter is not an ideal season to visit Kaunas, but if it is necessary
for tourists to travel to Kaunas during winter seasons, it is highly
recommended that they bring or buy very warm clothes such as waterproof
snow boots, thick socks, gloves, warm caps, scarves, and even ski masks
would be efficient. Preferred activities of this season consist of going
to theatef performances, or relaxing in a heated pub or cafe with a
warm beverage.

One of the many ideas for Kaunas when-to-go should be that travelers should stay away from Kaunas in the winter.
