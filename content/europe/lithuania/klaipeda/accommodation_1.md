Luxurious "Stora Antis" apartments located on the first floor (no lift) of one of the most famous houses of Klaipeda in the very center of the Old Town, on the bank of Dane River, close to Birzos Bridge (see interactive map in “Environment” section).
The building was constructed in 1915, wall of the buildings places a monument – money pot. Previously the building located Bank - Sparkasse .
It is very convenient to observe all the city’s celebrations, artists’ and craftsmen markets and listen to bell music concerts of Klaipeda Carillon from the tower located close to the Central Post Office from the balcony of the apartment. Concerts begin midday sharp every Sunday.
Windows of the apartments look towards the banking of the river, “Meridianas” sailing-ship, square in front of the sailing-ship and the Old Town.
The area of the apartments is 46 , 56 ,40 sq.m.
