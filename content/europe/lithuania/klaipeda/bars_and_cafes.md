---
title: "Bars and Cafes in Klaipeda"
type: section
---

Knowing about the different **Klaipeda Bars and Cafes** before your vacation can be really helpful when planning your vacation. The main reason that knowing about the best bars and cafes in town will help you decide where to go, but they can also help you know which ones to avoid.

**Blue Angels

**This bar is one of the most popular bars any night of the week. If you are looking for a great bar that is gay and lesbian friendly this is the place to go. Whether you are looking to meet somebody to spend a nice romantic evening with or just a smiling face you are most likely going to find that here. Not only do you have access to a full bar and dancing
but there is also live entertainment.

**Naktine Ledi
**
If you are looking for a great place to go on Friday or Saturday nights you should check out check out the Naktine Ledi. If the loud music inside gets to be a bit much you can always step out onto the patio for some fresh air. Mainly gays and lesbians frequent this bar, so you might want to avoid it if that's not your scene.

**Black Cat
**
If you enjoy looking out on the river at night this is the perfect bar for you because of its river-front location. Not only can you find something nice and cold to drink inside the bar, but they also have a great food selection, including American dishes. One of the most interesting things to note about the bar is that Adolph Hitler ate at the bar in 1939.

**Kurpiai
**
If you happen to enjoy jazz you will want to stop in at Kurpiai because it is known as the best jazz club in town, many even say in the country. Aside from having the best jazz music around you can also find some nice people to talk too. The bar is located in the middle of the Old Town so it is not hard to find.

**Navalis Caf&eacute;
**
If you are not interested in drinking or dancing Navalis Caf&eacute; offers you a great chance for people-watching. Not only can you watch people walking by on the street, but you can also get some of the best coffee and snacks in town.
