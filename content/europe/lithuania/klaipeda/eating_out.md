There are several **Klaipeda Restaurants** to cater to the needs of tourists from all over the world. Klaipeda, a Lithuania city situated at the mouth of Curonian Lagoon, is Lithuania&rsquo;s only sea port. Klaipeda is a picturesque city still bearing some of the old half timbered constructions, which adds an extra charm and elegance to the city. Klaipeda is a windy city, with several notable historic building and beach resorts. It is also easily accessible by all means of transport, allowing tourists to visit this place easily.

**Anikes Teatras**

The Anikes Teatras is situated in the heart of Klaipeda downtown near the Anika square. This restaurant is called the German restaurant by Klaipedains; the reason being that the owner is German, and it also happens to be a favorite hang out for Germans in the city. This is the perfect place to go if you are not in a hurry and want to enjoy your meal. Get a table either inside the restaurant or on the terrace from where you can enjoy the view of the square. The interiors of this restaurant are well done with fine textiles from France. This is the perfect place to relax and unwind after a long day, with great European food and beer.

**Friedricho Pasazas**

Friedricho Pasazas, which means Friedrich Passageway in English, is an exclusive restaurant in Klaipeda. It is also known as Friedrich Town. Friedrich town finds mention in the historical documents of Klaipeda. History has it that during the era of Friedrich III, this place was given permission by the citizens to be called Friedrich Town in the honor of Friedrich III. The place is something out of your imagination. Friedricho Pasazas gives you the feeling of walking on a small street in a Western European city. They serve great sea food and the best of wines. You can either be seated outside the restaurant or inside. The staff is friendly and eager to help.

**Stora Antis**

Stora Antis was an underground storage area in the past. Now, it is a beautiful underground restaurant serving some of the best Slavic cuisines like Russian dishes, Moldovan dishes and Ukrainian dishes. They also offer some exotic Lithuanian food. On your visit here, do try out the turkey dish with berry stuffing or sauce and the potato pancake dish. Stora Antis is frequented by Russian celebrities on their visit to Klaipeda. This is a great place to unwind and relax.
