---
title: "Getting Around"
type: section
---

The old town of Klaipeda is small enough to walk around. The ferry to the Curonian Spit runs from the terminal at the end of Žvejų Street and takes 5 minutes. City buses cover the wider city. Taxis are metered and inexpensive by Western European standards. A car is useful for exploring the coast south of Klaipeda and the Nemunas Delta.
