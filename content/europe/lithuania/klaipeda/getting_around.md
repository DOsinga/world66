There are many different ways that you can get around in Klaipeda, and you can find **Klaipeda transportation** that meets your needs on your trip. #### Ferries in the City

If you like to travel by ferry, you can find that there are great services available for ferries in the city. Most of the ferries leave on Sundays to go to the beaches. You can use the ferries to travel to the various beach locations around the city and find them to be an easy way to get around in Klaipeda. They are also fun to travel on, and you can enjoy the views from the ferries while you are traveling.

#### Exploring Klaipeda on Foot

If you like to spend more time stopping at the various shops and restaurants in the city, walking may be the best option for you. There are plenty of areas in the city where you can walk easily and this can also be a great way to catch a ferry without having to worry about leaving your car or bike on the other site. A lot of people choose walking as Klaipeda transportation when they are staying here. There are a lot of paved areas that make for excellent walking, and you can find that walking also allows you to take in more of what is going on in the city. Make sure that you have proper foot wear and clothing especially if you are going on longer walks through the city or in the other areas.

#### Traveling on the Train

The train service is Klaipeda is also a good choice because it allows you to see more of the country and see some of the great views. You can find that stations are easy to find and that you can catch one of the trains that goes around the area and to some of the other cities near Klaipeda.

#### Traveling on the Bus

Taking the bus around the city can be a very affordable option. You can find a number of bus stops around Klaipeda and find it easy to get to where you are going. A lot of people use the bus as Klaipeda transportation, and you can also use the buses to get to some of the beaches around the city.
