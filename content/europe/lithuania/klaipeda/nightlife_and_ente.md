---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment in Klaipeda"
type: section
---

**Klaipeda nightlife** is one that will not disappoint tourists, as there are a number of bars and clubs to choose from in the city. Listed below are some of the most popular places tourists must visit while holidaying in Klaipeda.

**Memelis

**This is probably the most popular hangout in Klaipeda because it offers visitors just about everything they want for a fun night out. Located along the Dane River on Zveja gatve 4, Memelis is a large red brick building which consists of a pub, restaurant, disco and brewery. The first floor is for visitors who want to sit back, sip some home made beer and feast on traditional cuisine. If you want to shake a leg, head to the second floor which is a disco. The third floor is used for celebrations and private parties. There isn&rsquo;t any entrance fee, and the place is open till about 3 a.m. Those who want to stay away from the crowd inside can opt to sit outside and enjoy a view of the river while they drink and eat.

**El Calor

**If you&rsquo;re looking for a place that offers more entertainment, visit El Calor, which is in the Old Town. This is one of the most popular nightclubs in the area. The place is an excellent mix of a cabaret, nightclub and cocktail bar. It has a strong Latin theme, and is open from Wednesdays to Sundays. Bands usually play here on Fridays, but a DJ takes over on the rest of the days. El Calor can let a bit crowded and hot, especially on weekends. Drink prices are also slightly higher on these days, and there is an entrance charge on weekends.

**Miesto Smukle

**Tourists looking for something less Lithuanian and something more continental must visit Miesto Smukle in Tiltu. Also known as the City Pub, this place is more like a British pub which serves excellent steak. Miesto Smukle also has a sophisticated wine menu and a wonderful range of beers. This place has two floors and a few soccer tables. There is also a terrace where visitors can enjoy drinking on warm summer evenings. The affordable menu and pleasant ambience make it an ideal place to while away evenings in Klaipeda.

**Muitine

**While most of the bars and clubs are aimed at attracting tourists, this little bar is meant for locals. It is the perfect place if you&rsquo;re looking to mingle with the people from Klaipeda. Muitine serves a good variety of alcohol and reasonably priced food. Located in Tilzes, this bar is part of a pleasant courtyard that offers a view of a quiet street.

Besides these, there are a number of other bars, clubs and even casinos that are open till well into the night, offering visitors excellent ways to spend their nights in this city.
