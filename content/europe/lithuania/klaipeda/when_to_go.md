---

If you are going to **Klaipeda When to Go** is something that you want to consider. Most people enjoy knowing when the best time to go to Klaipeda is because it allows them to plan a vacation that they are going to truly enjoy. On the other hand many people also love knowing when they should avoid Klaipeda because you can avoid certain things, such as the bad weather.

**September**

This is one of the best months to visit Klaipeda because of how nice the weather is. The temperatures during the month of September are not going to be too hot nor will they be too cold. The only problem with visiting during the month of September is how many other tourists will be visiting during the same time, which makes it hard to see everything you want to see.

**May
**
May is another great month to visit Klaipeda, but you will need to be prepared for a large number of tourists. The weather during the month of May is still relatively mild, but you will want to be prepared ahead of time for rainfall. One of the best things about visiting in May is that it is the beginning of the tourist season so not as many tourists will be visiting, so it won't be as crowded as it gets later on during the year.

**June
**
The month of June is going to be far busier than the month of May in terms of he number of tourists because of how many families travel during the month of June due to kids getting released from school. While the weather is mild during the beginning of June you want to be prepared for thunderstorms towards the end of the month.

**Summer
**
The other summer months are a great time to visit Klaipeda because of the mild weather. The mild weather allows you to participate in a variety of outdoor activities without having to worry about too much heat. For example, during the month of July the hottest it usually gets is 71 degrees F, while June and August stay around the 70s.

**Winter
**
Any of the winter months, but especially February, you want to avoid going to Klaipeda. The main reason is because of how cold it gets during the winter months. During these cold months there is not much to do because everything is shut down and the lakes and rivers are frozen over.
