Vilnius has a bus and trolleybus system covering most of the city. The Old Town is compact and best explored on foot.

### Taxis

There are several reputable taxi companies charging strictly metered fares. At the airport, Martonas yellow cabs queue outside — a trip to the city centre should cost no more than 20 Litas during the day and 30 Litas at night. It is cheaper to call a cab: Mersera (+370 52 788 888) and Martonas (+370 52 440 044) are both reliable, speak reasonable English, and have clean cars.
