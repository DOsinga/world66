---
title: Getting There
type: section
---

Vilnius International Airport (VNO) is about 7 km south of the city centre. Buses and taxis connect the airport to town; a taxi should cost no more than 20–30 Litas. Several European airlines operate direct flights from major cities.

By train, Vilnius is connected to other Baltic capitals and to Warsaw. The bus station next to the railway station has frequent services to Kaunas, Klaipėda, Riga, and Tallinn.
