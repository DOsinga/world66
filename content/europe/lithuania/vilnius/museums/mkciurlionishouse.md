Ciurlionis House in Vilnius was opened on September 24, 1995, commemorating the 120th anniversary of M.K.Ciurlionis. There in 1907 - 1908 lived and created the great Lithuanian artist and composer. Apart from the memorial room, there are also cozy premises for the concerts of chamber music, for the hearing of the recordings and the expositions of pictures. The aim of Ciurlionis House is to compile, save and spread the information about the life and creative work of M.K.Ciurlionis, to collect exhibits, arrange exhibitions, organize different events, thematic evenings, scientific conferences.
