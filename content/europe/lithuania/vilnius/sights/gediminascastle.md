The Gediminas castle is in the heart of the city. It is one of the first Gothic structures in Vilnius. The history of the castle is closely related to the formation of the city. The exposition in the castle museum reveals the eventful history of Lithuania. On the roof of the tower one may observe a breathtaking view of the Old Town.
