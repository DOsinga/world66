There are several reputable taxi companies in Vilnius, all charging strictly metered fares. At the airport you will see a queue of Martonas yellow cabs — the cost of a trip to the city centre should not run more than 20 Litas during the day and 30 Litas at night.

It is much cheaper to call a cab. Two good companies: Mersera (+370 52 788 888) and Martonas (+370 52 440 044). Both speak reasonable English, cars are clean, and service is reliable.
