title: "Top 5 Must Do's in Vilnius"
type: section
---

The Lithuanian Capital is a slow charmer that will tactfully engulf
you with its dark historical events and rich cultural legacy. This Vilnius Must Do's** **guide
offers visitors information on the most popular attractions to visit
and activities to undertake in this drearily attractive city.

**1. Vilnius Cathedral**

The splendiferous Vilnius Cathedral is a landmark edifice that
stands loftily in the Old City and has an explicit historical backdrop
that left it scarred during the events of the Soviet era. The spirited
monument later regained its glory and is now an impeccable illustration
of art, history and religion. The 13 th monument houses
several frescos and paintings and has a striking Greek temple like
appearance. Vilnius Cathedral was erected on an old pagan temple site
and has been restored innumerable times due to war and natural
calamities.

**2. Gediminas Castle**

Gediminas Castle in a classic and iconic 13 th century
monument that has dexterously served as a quarter for war prisoners
and a defense bastion along with donning the garb of a modern day
tourist attraction. The once completely devastated monument has been
meticulously restored and boasts of a well-stocked museum that exhibits
an array of memorabilia that shaped the ancient identity of Lithuania.
Climb the soaring octagonal tower for a stunning view of the old town
region of Vilnius

**3. ****European Park**

Head to the European Park, located to the north of old town center,
for the most exquisite pieces of sculpture. The sprawling 55-hectare
park attracts hordes of art connoisseurs to view its recherché works of
art. There is an all year round outdoor exhibition of several original
and finely sculptured creations that are displayed amidst lush woods
and natural springs. The park houses a restaurant serving decent fare
and guided tours can also be organized by booking in advance.

**4. Jewish Memorials**

This dismaying yet striking destination serves as a stark reminder
of the horror of the Jewish genocide by the Nazis during World War II
and was established in memory of the tragic event. The Jewish
Memorials also house a synagogue and a center depicting the pre-war
era Jewish lifestyle.

**5 Hot Air Balloon Sightseeing**

Go sightseeing on a romantic hot air balloon that will pick you up
from your desired location and give you a fantastic aerial view of
Vilnius. The duration is approximately an hour and charges can start
from about €250 per ride. A single balloon can accommodate up to four
people.
