Vartai is a contemporary art gallery tucked into the streets of Vilnius Old Town. The small space hosts rotating exhibitions of Lithuanian and international artists, with a focus on painting, sculpture, and mixed media. It is one of several private galleries that have helped shape the city's growing reputation as a hub for contemporary art in the Baltics. Worth a quick look if you are wandering the old town streets.
