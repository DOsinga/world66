Echternach was badly damaged during the Second World War. Reconstruction has been quite succesful and the smaal city has regained some of its former glory. The narrow streets , the medieval ramparts and old patrician houses look great in thier new shape and form. Highlights include the Gorge du Loup, the Museum of Prehistory and the 7th-century basilica and Benedictine abbey. Echtenach is about 30 km northeast of 
 Luxembourg city.
