---
title: "Bars and Cafes"
type: section
---

The Grund neighbourhood, in the valley below the old town, is the main draw for evening drinking — a cluster of bars and restaurants along the Alzette river with outdoor seating in summer. The Place d'Armes terraces are more touristy but hard to resist on a warm evening. Luxembourg has decent nightlife for a city its size, from long-running clubs to relaxed wine bars.
