One of Luxembourg City's most enduring nightlife institutions, going strong since 1984. Melusina opens as a restaurant on Friday and Saturday lunchtimes, then shifts into full club mode in the evening with guest DJs and a crowd that spans students, expats, and regulars who've been coming for decades.
