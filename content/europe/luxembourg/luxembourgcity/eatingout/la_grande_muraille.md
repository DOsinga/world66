A chinese restaurant near to Luxembourg city. You can take bus number 9 or 16 from the city center and it is just the bus station after the national airport. The bus gets there every 10-15min. You can also go by car and they have a free outdoor parking. The waiters/waitresses speak French, English, Mandarin and Cantonese. The restaurant is situated next to the hotel Campanile.

Some details about their services:

- Buffet from Monday to Friday (lunchtime) for 10eur/pers

- Softdrinks 1.80eur, coffee 1.80eur, beer 2.00eur, 1 glass of wine 2.00eur

- Chinese Fondue available

- free outdoor parking

- dogs allowed

- smoking not allowed

- 1km to the national airport

- 1min to the closest hotel (Campanile)

- 2min to the next bus station (bus numbers 9 and 16 every 10-15min)
