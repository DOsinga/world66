Above all children and youngsters scour the National Museum of Natural History with a sense of adventure, in a universe of fauna and flora. 
 Modern art is the mainstay of the “Casino Luxembourg”, the former citizen’s casino which was transformed into a meeting-place for contemporary art. The Art Gallery “Am Tunnel” for its part exhibits modern paintings and photos.
