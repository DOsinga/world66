Located in the Moselle valley on the German border, Wasserbillig is a transit town that many Germans visit for cheaper fuel. The town itself is unremarkable, but the valley around it is lovely, with vineyards lining the riverside producing crisp white wines. It's a convenient stop on the Moselle cycling route between Luxembourg City and Trier.
