Rather than ending the civil strife, NATO's intervention in Kosovo set in motion a series of events which would have violent repercussions throughout the Balkans over the next two years. This book, consisting primarily of Scott Taylor's first-hand observations and interviews with the people and players of the 2001 Macedonia military conflict, is a very personal account of war and its aftermath in Serbia and Macedonia.
