### [TavernaToscana](/europe/macedonia/skopje/eating_out/tavernatoscana)

Finally a real Italian Restaurant also in Skopje! La Taverna Toscana is a little restaurant in Lola Ribar street near Vero supermarket. Little and familiar restaurant. We found Vlatko who worked for years in Tuscany restaurants, learning the real secrets of italian good food. Try the pasta and tagliata, but on each season the menu changes (according to seasonal products). Top quality, means top ingredients and accuracy in preparations, so Taverna Toscana cost a little more than other italian or local touristic restaurants, but it worth completely this experience. ### [restaurant](/europe/macedonia/skopje/eating_out/restaurant__oreov)

“Oreov Lad’ is a national restaurant and its specialities are old macedonian dishes, cooked in the traditional way. The restaurant offer international cuisine and King Marko cuisine.

There are 180 seats inside the restaurant and 250 seats outside in the magnificent garden; two parking places for about fifty cars: children playground, live music on the weekend during the winter and every day in summer and two dancing podiums (indoor and outdoor).
