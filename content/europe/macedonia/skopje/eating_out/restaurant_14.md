Restaurant 14 is a few minutes drive away from the center of the city.

It delights with a tranquil and leisure atmosphere associated with tradition
and family gathering.

The restaurant' menu includes dishes from traditional Macedonian cuisine
prepared by highly experienced and qualified chefs. Beside the delicious
traditional food, macrobiotic and vegetarian dishes are available for the
guests.

With its 220 seats is an ideal spot for celebrations such as special
anniversaries, birthdays etc. Restaurant 14 prides itself on its consistently
high level of customer service and the restaurant team is trained to make you
feel at home satisfying each of your requirements.

**The restaurant 14** is a part of the catering services of the Skopski pazar AD company. . The company also owns the restaurant Aleksandrija in Kavadarci.
