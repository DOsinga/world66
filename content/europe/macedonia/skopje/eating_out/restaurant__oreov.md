“Oreov Lad’ is a national restaurant and its specialities are old macedonian dishes, cooked in the traditional way. The restaurant offer international cuisine and King Marko cuisine.

There are 180 seats inside the restaurant and 250 seats outside in the magnificent garden; two parking places for about fifty cars: children playground, live music on the weekend during the winter and every day in summer and two dancing podiums (indoor and outdoor).
