Skopje airport (SKP) is located about 15 kilometres from the city centre. Although it is a small airport, there are direct flights from [Milan](/europe/italy/lombardia/milan) (Alitalia), [Belgrade](/europe/serbia/belgrade) (JAT), [Vienna](/europe/austria/vienna) (Austrian), [Ljubljana](/europe/slovenia/ljubljana) (Adria), [Istanbul](/asia/turkey/istanbul) (Turkish), [Zurich](/europe/switzerland/zurich) (Macedonian), [Budapest](/europe/hungary/budapest) (Malev), and [Prague](/europe/czechrepublic/prague) (Czech). A taxi from the airport to the city centre costs around 20 Euro.
