Anyone who ever visited Malta knows that the white taxis you pick up on the streets are expensive and dangerous. However the country has a few reputable taxi firms who offer airport transfers and inland taxi transfers at reasonable rates. We recommend SwanSea Chauffeur Drive www.swanseacarhire.com and Active cars www.active-car-hire.com

Car rental in Malta is definetely the best way to get around as it allows you to explore hidden roads and off the track villages, churches and many treasures these islands have to offer. Car hire in Malta is also very cheap with cars starting from around €20 / day including tax and insurance. We recommend booking through www.maltarentacar.com or www.malta-car-hire.com

The above companies also offer private sightseeing tours and excursions for those who prefer not to dirve and require the service of a licensed guide or experience ddriver. Private tours do not come cheap but do offer a comprehensive way to find out more about Malta.
