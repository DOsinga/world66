---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Gozo rewards slow exploration. The Ggantija Temples near Xaghra are among the oldest freestanding structures in the world, predating the Egyptian pyramids. The walled citadel above Victoria offers panoramic views across the island. Ta' Pinu Basilica is a major pilgrimage site with an intimate atmosphere, and Calypso's Cave above Ramla Bay connects the island to Homeric legend.
