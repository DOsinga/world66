Internet was introduced on a commercial scale in Malta in late 1995. The number of Internet service providers, as well as subscribers has increased dramatically from the early days. Previously Internet was being used primarily by the University of Malta and the Maltese Government on a restricted basis.

**Wireless Internet**

Wireless Internet (WiFi, 802.11b) is available in such places as Pjazza Regina Valletta, the University of Malta, and various hotels. Top-up cards can be purchased at Go Mobile outlets.
