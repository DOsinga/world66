The Grand Masters' Palace on Republic Street was the seat of power for the Knights of St John and later the British Governor. Today it houses the Maltese Parliament. The State Rooms are open to visitors and contain a series of fresco cycles depicting the history of the Knights and the Great Siege of 1565. The Armoury, in a separate wing, displays one of Europe's finest collections of medieval armour — over 5,000 pieces, including the personal armour of several Grand Masters.
