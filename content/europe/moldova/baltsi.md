Baltsi (Bălți) is Moldova's second city, with a population of around 100,000 in the northern part of the country, roughly 120 km from Chisinau. It is primarily an industrial and commercial centre rather than a tourist destination, but it serves as a useful base for exploring northern Moldova — including the monasteries of Saharna and Curchi, and the fortress town of [Soroca](/europe/moldova/soroca).

The city has a market, a small historic centre, and basic services. There is regular bus and marshrutka service to Chisinau.
