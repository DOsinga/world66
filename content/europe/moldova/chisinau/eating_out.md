---
title: "Eating Out"
type: section
---

Moldovan cuisine blends Romanian and Eastern European influences — mamaliga (polenta), stuffed cabbage rolls (sarmale), ciorba soups, grilled meats, and a wide variety of pickled vegetables. The central market is the best place for fresh produce and local food. Restaurants in the centre range from traditional Moldovan to Russian to international. Prices are significantly lower than Western Europe.
