title: "Family Travel Ideas in Chisinau"
type: section
---

There are a lot of **Chisinau family travel **ideas that you can do with your entire family to enjoy your stay in the capital city of Moldova. Chisinau is the center of industry and culture in Moldova, which is why there are a lot of activities that you can enjoy as a family in this city. Here are some interesting activities that your family can engage in if you travel in Chisinau, which are perfect for both adults and children.

**The National History Museum**

While in Chisinau, your entire family should learn about the history of the city, thus the best place to learn the rich history of Chisinau is by visiting The National History Museum. Although the museum shows a lot of relics of the Ottoman and Soviet rule, which can interest a lot of adults, children can also look at the diorama that are showcased inside the museum to learn about the city&rsquo;s past in a fun and interactive way.

**Lake Valea Morilor**

While in Chisinau, your family should visit the Lake Valea Morilor which is found on the outskirts of Chisinau. The lake offers a lot of paddle boats and canoes for rent where you and your children can enjoy paddling and splashing water under the heat of the afternoon sun. The lake also offers other family-friendly activities like hiking tours and camping grounds. You and your children will definitely have fun while doing outdoor activities in Lake Valea Morilor.

**Parcul Dendrariu**

A large park located in Buiucani, this park is a great place to play sports and enjoy the sights and sounds of nature. It has a wide area which includes nice gardens, a lake, forest and a big sports field. You can spend your afternoon here looking at nature or you can do better by playing sports in the big sports field located at the center of the park. However, entrance to this park costs $0.65.

**The Curtea Vinatorolui**

To make dining less boring for your children, the best place where you can dine in is at The Curtea Vinatorolui. This restaurant serves contemporary dishes such as pizza and burger which children will surely enjoy, while adults can order wine and local Moldovian dishes. Moreover, the restaurant also has a small zoo to provide distraction to children who easily get bored after meals.
