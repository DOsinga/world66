---
title: "Getting Around"
type: section
---

Trolleybuses, buses, and marshrutki (shared minibuses) cover the city cheaply. Taxis are inexpensive by European standards; negotiate the fare before getting in, or use a ride-hailing app. The city centre is walkable. For day trips to Orheiul Vechi and the wineries, marshrutki or taxis are the standard option.
