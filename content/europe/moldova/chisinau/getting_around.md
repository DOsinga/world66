---
title: "Getting Around in Chisinau"
type: section
---

Getting around the capital of Moldova is not very difficult with the various **Chisinau transportation** options in the city. If you are planning a visit here, you might want to get familiar with the various options available. You can make use of the public transport system; the bus, the train and the minibus are some of the easiest ways of getting around.

**By Bus and Minibus**

You can ride the bus or the traditional trolley cars to take you to the many places in the city and what is great about riding these transportation systems is that they are cheap and reliable. The fare from bus or taxi usually costs between $5 and $10. Traveling within Chisinau and its outskirts, it is recommended you take the marshrutkas, which are privately operated minibuses. These minibuses are frequently seen in major bus routes so it is really accessible to a commuter. The cost of a ride on a marshrutkas within the city is only $0.30.

**By Car Rental**

While traveling in Chisinau, you can also opt to rent a car. These rent-a-car companies can cater to your specific needs. The rental car companies carry a wide variety of car brands and models at very competitive rates. However, in order to rent a car, you must be able to meet their requirements such as: at least 23-years old and owner of a driver's license for three years. You must also be forewarned that the road conditions of Chisinau are bad, as the roads are riddled with many potholes.
