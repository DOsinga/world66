---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment in Chisinau"
type: section
---

Not everybody is interested in what the Chisinau Nightlife has to offer during his or her vacation. However, these people have no idea what they are missing. The best part about the nightlife is that it is not just about bars and dance clubs.

Eminescu Music and Drama Theater

If you are interested in any kind of drama you will want to be sure to see at least one production that the Eminescu has to offer locals and tourists alike. Before reserving your tickets it is helpful to know that the theater specializes in Romanian productions, but does offer other types of productions on occasion. Your best bet is to call the theater ahead of time to see what is currently playing.

Philharmonia Concert Hall

If you are more interested in orchestras you will want to make a visit to the Philharmonia Concert Hall. This concert hall is home to Moldova’s Symphony Orchestra, but there is more to the concert hall than just the orchestra. You can also find the Doina Choir performing in the concert hall, which is some of the best folklore music around. You can also find performances by the Zhok National Dance Ensemble, if you are more into dancing than music.

Baltica

If you are looking for a nice place to relax and enjoy a drink or two this is one of the best places in town to go. Baltica is open Tuesday through Sunday from 8 p.m. to 4 a.m., so no matter what time you are looking to sit back and relax chances are Baltica is going to be open. Not only can you enjoy your evening with a drink or two you can also take part in the karaoke that is offered or have some sea snacks to help with those midnight munchies.

Déjà vu

If you are looking for a large choice in cocktails Déjà vu is the place to go because they have one of the largest selection of cocktails available, plus they are all pleasing to look at. Not only can you get almost any cocktail that you can think of the kitchen also prepares some of the best dishes in town. At Déjà Vu you will be treated to a large selection of European dishes, but be warned the chef does not share his recipes!

Nostalgie

If you are looking for a bar that you would find in the United States, then look no further. The bar is open during the day and night, which allows people to come in to quietly enjoy their drink or come in for some excitement later on in the evening. No matter what time you come in you will find great service, plus a large selection of drinks. During the nighttime hours you can come in and listen to some music, including one of the best DJs in the city.
