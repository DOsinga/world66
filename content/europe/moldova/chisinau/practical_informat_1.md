Hi,
Here in Chisinau, capital of Moldova, is renting an apartment for some days / weeks very popular. Much better value than in hotels.
Good examples of such a 1 bedroom apartment is Cel Mare 64-15 or for a 2 bedroom apartment Puskin 30-17. Put those adresses in "google" and you will see all information about such a possibilities. In average you will pay 3 till 6 times more for a quality hotel.
Have a nice day.
