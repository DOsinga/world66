**Downtown Chisinau: **The downtown or the center of the city is one of the most beautiful parts of the city because of its little old neighborhood with the buildings from 19th century and then the soviet architecture "not as nice but still pretty good for soviet era architecture" and now the new modern city witch is starting to appear with glass buildings and other kind of modern architecture. Then there are two very beautiful parks Which are the **Cathedral Park** and **Stefan Cel Mare Park** they are pretty good looking. Then are the Embassy's of different countries from around the world with a nice old architectural touch to them. Then there is the shopping which is all over Chisinau especially one of the best in the center. Then what kind of European city without its museums with there thousands of archaeological artifacts. and at the end there are the Moldovan parliament and the presidency. And not to forget the Romanian favorite name for there main streets which of course is the **Stefan cel Mare** boulevard and to show that Moldova is A Romanian country there is **Stefan cel Mare** statue in **Stefan Cel Mare Park** which was put there in 1930s when Moldova was part of Romania
