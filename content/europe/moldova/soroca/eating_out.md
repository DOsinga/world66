In Soroca there are lots of places to eat out those may be restaurants cafes or pizzeria.The best places are in the old town of Soroca they got some very good pizza. And the prices are very reasonable. Plus if you don't want to go out and eat they have a nice open market which you could by food for cheap money and safe healthy food.
