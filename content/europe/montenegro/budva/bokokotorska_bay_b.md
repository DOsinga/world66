A full-day boat excursion through the Bay of Kotor, and one of the best day trips from Budva. The tour typically starts with a bus ride to Tivat, then continues by boat to [Herceg Novi](/europe/montenegro/hercegnovi). The views along the bay are spectacular throughout.

You get about an hour in Herceg Novi — not nearly enough for a proper visit, but enough to get a feel for the town's steep streets and fortifications. After that the boat continues to a beach stop where you can swim or have lunch at a waterfront restaurant. Order your food first and swim while it's being prepared.

The next stop is the island of Gospa od Skrpjela (Our Lady of the Rocks), near Perast, with its small church and museum (1 euro entry). The final stop is [Kotor](/europe/montenegro/kotor), where you have an hour to explore the old town before returning to Budva by bus.
