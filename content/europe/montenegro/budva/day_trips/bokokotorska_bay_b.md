This trip is wonderful! **Dont't miss it.**
Don't forget your camera and your sun-block.

You'll go from Budva to Tivat by bus. Then you'll go by boat to Herceg Novi. All the way you'll be admiring beautiful views.

In [Herceg Novi](/europe/montenegro/hercegnovi) you'll spend about an hour trying to catch with your guide. Herceg Novi is very nice and quite a big place so one hour is not enough time to see it properly. Be prepared to go up and down a lot.

After Herceg Novi you'll continue by boat to some beach for an hour. There you may sweem ore have your lunch in a restaurant. You also can take small local boat to a very picturesque grotto.
If you wish to sweem and have lunch it's better to go to the restaurant firatly and then to go swimming while your lunch is prepared.

After this beach you'll continue to Perast, but you'll not visit Perast itself. You'll stay for an hour on small island Gospa od Skrpjela with very interesting church and small museum. If you wish to visit museum you have to pay 1 Euro (June, 2006).

The last stop of this trip is [Kotor](/europe/montenegro/kotor). You'll have one hour to look around and then you'll return to Budva by bus.
