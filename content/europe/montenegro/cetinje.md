The best resource for sights, hotels, restaurants, bars, what to do and see

For many years Cetinje was capital of Montenegro (see section [History](/europe/montenegro/cetinje/history)). Now Cetinje is small sleeping (somebody could say "dead") town. Many buildings looks like nobody lives there. It is still considered "the second capital" of Montenegro and the President of new state made his residence here.
