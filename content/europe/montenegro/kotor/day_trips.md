The Bay of Kotor and the surrounding area offer some of the best day trips in Montenegro. [Perast](/europe/montenegro/perast) is 12km along the bay road — a baroque town with two picturesque islands just offshore. [Herceg Novi](/europe/montenegro/hercegnovi) is at the mouth of the bay, about 40km, with its own layered old town and a calmer atmosphere than Kotor in peak season.

[Budva](/europe/montenegro/budva) is 30km south: more beach-oriented, busier in summer, but the walled old town on its peninsula is worth seeing. [Cetinje](/europe/montenegro/cetinje), the former royal capital, is 35km inland over the Lovćen pass — a short but spectacular drive.
