---
title: "Eating Out"
type: section
---

Kotor's best eating is seafood and Montenegrin grill. The old town has plenty of restaurants, but the tourist traffic means quality varies sharply — the places visible from the main squares are often not the best ones. Walk a block or two further in, or ask around. The market outside the Sea Gate is worth visiting for dried figs, prosciutto, local cheeses, and olive oil.
