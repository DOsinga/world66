Galion sits just outside the old town walls on the waterfront, with a terrace that looks back at the walls and the mountains. The setting is the main draw, but the food holds up: fresh fish, grilled or baked, with good local wine from the Plantaže winery. Slightly more expensive than the old town options, but the view justifies the extra euros at lunch or sunset.
