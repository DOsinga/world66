type: section
---

Kotor punches well above its size. The old town is compact enough to cover in a morning, but the walls, the fortress, the bay, and the surrounding villages keep you busy for days. The Cathedral of Saint Tryphon anchors the historic centre; the climb to the fortress above is the single best thing you can do here.
