title: "In Den Grooten Slock"
type: poi
address: "Zevenhuizen 1A"
latitude: 52.1564
longitude: 5.3906
---

The oldest café in Amersfoort, dating back to 1522. Beautiful facade and windows, and in summer you can sit out front and watch the square. Together with Happerij & Tapperij and In den Kleinen Hap just across the street, it forms a perfect trio for an evening in the city centre.
