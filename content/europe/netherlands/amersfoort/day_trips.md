Amersfoort's central position in the Netherlands makes it a good base for day trips. [Utrecht](/europe/netherlands/utrecht) is just ten minutes away by train and deserves a full day. The Veluwe — the largest forested area in the Netherlands — starts just east of the city and is excellent for cycling and walking. Kamp Amersfoort, the former concentration camp near Leusden on the southern edge of the city, is a sobering but important visit for understanding the Dutch experience of World War II.
