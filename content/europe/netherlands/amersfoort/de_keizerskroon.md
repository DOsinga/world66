A classic Dutch eetcafé on the Hof — the kind of place that serves stamppot in winter and grilled fish in summer, with portions large enough to satisfy a cyclist. The terrace on the square is one of the better places to watch Amersfoort life go by. Reasonable prices and an unpretentious atmosphere.
