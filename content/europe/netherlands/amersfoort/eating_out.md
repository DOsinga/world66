---
title: "Eating Out"
type: section
---

Amersfoort's dining scene is unpretentious and Dutch at heart — expect good portions, honest cooking, and a lot of gezelligheid. The city centre has a solid range from traditional Dutch pancake houses to Mediterranean-influenced bistros. Langestraat and the streets around the Hof are the main eating strips. For the quintessential local experience, try poffertjes (tiny fluffy pancakes with butter and sugar) or rookworst (smoked sausage) at one of the traditional eethuizen.
