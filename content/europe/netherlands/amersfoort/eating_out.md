**In Den Kleinen Hap—The Best Place to Enjoy Local Dishes in Amersfoort, Netherlands**

For a gastronomic adventure in the central Netherlands, it is important that you visit several **Amersfoort Restaurants** that dot the area. Amersfoort is a city that is characterized for having quaint and scenic architecture that is reminiscent of the medieval times. Although this city is famous for its tourist attractions, it also houses the famous In Den Kleinen Hap Restaurant, which serves the best local dishes in all of Amersfoort.

**In Den Kleinen Hap Restaurant**

Located in Langerstraat, this particular restaurant serves the best local cuisines in Amersfoort. The restaurant looks like an ordinary diner and has a small space with comfy interiors. It is a favorite breakfast place of both tourists and locals alike.

**Menu**

This particular restaurant is famous for its traditional fluffy pancakes called poffertjes, which is made with yeast. It is batter fried and sprinkled with honey and sugar. You can have a plate containing three to five poffertjes for only $5 to $10. This is also the place where you can enjoy the best coffee in the city. The restaurant usually serves poffertjes during breakfast, and most locals frequent this restaurant for this treat. However, the restaurant is also open for lunch and dinner, and they also serve the best rookworst*** ***(smoked sausages), appletaart (apple tart) and Hutspot (vegetable side dish). The restaurant also serves local beverages like lager and gin. Dining in this restaurant gives you the best gastronomic experience in all of Amersfoort. Food costs between $10 to $30.
