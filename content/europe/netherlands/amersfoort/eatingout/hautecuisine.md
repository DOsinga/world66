**De Monnick**

This is the best looking restaurant of the Netherlands! Located in an old Watergate (a city gate that is built over a canal) there are only 5 tables here. In summer there is a small terrace. There is no menu, so just let the cook surprise you. The combinations are unusal but they always taste ver [more..](/europe/netherlands/amersfoort/eatingout/hautecuisine/demonnick)
