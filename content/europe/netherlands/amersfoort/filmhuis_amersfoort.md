A grand café-style bar just behind the Joriskerk on the Groenmarkt. Live music on the square on Sundays. A good spot for a drink before or after a film.

Filmhuis Amersfoort is the city's art-house cinema, screening independent and foreign-language films year-round. The bar is open to non-cinema-goers too and gets busy on Sunday afternoons when live music plays on the square outside.
