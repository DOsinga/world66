---
title: "Getting Around"
type: section
---

The old centre is compact and best explored on foot — you can walk from one end to the other in fifteen minutes. For getting to the zoo or the outskirts, local buses run from Amersfoort Centraal. Canal boat tours are a pleasant way to see the city from the water, with guides explaining the history of the buildings and gates you pass. Cycling is, as everywhere in the Netherlands, the default mode of transport for locals.
