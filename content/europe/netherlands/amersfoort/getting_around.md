title: "Getting Around in Amersfoort"
type: section
---

**Amersfoort**** transportation** services offer you a comfortable trip around the city and its vicinity. Amersfoort is a fast growing and the second largest city of Utrecht, Netherlands. Within Amersfoort, getting from place to place is not difficult.

**Train**

Amersfoort is located at the center of the Netherlands. This is a strategic location because you can easily go in any direction by train when boarding at Amersfoort central station. Amersfoort has a lot of direct train routes, such as Amsterdam, Utrecht, Schiphol (Amsterdam Airport), The Hague, Rotterdam, and Hilversum to the west. And to the east, the stations are Zwolle, Leeuwarden, Assen, Apeldoorn, Groningen, Deventer, and Enschede. Aside from these major stations, Amersfoort central stations also have connections to smaller stations in nearby municipalities and communities. In addition, Amersfoort has two other train stations, namely Amersfoort Schothorst and Amersfoort Vathorst. ****

**Bus**

Amersfoort central station is the bus center of the city. There are two kinds of bus services in the city. These are the intra-city bus and the inter-city bus. The intra-city bus travels towards every place in the city, while the inter-city bus connects to the areas in the nearby provinces.

**Taxi **

Taxi is also available in the city of Amersfoort. There are standard rates for taxis. The place where you go doesn&rsquo;t matter.

**Boat**

Boats can be used to get from place to place while you sight see the beauty of Amersfoort. These small boats cruise around the canals of Amersfoort. There is a guide who tells you all the history behind the monuments that you pass by.
