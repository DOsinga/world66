---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

Amersfoort sits at the centre of the Dutch rail network, which makes it easy to reach from almost anywhere in the country. Direct trains run from [Amsterdam](/europe/netherlands/amsterdam) (about 35 minutes), [Utrecht](/europe/netherlands/utrecht) (10 minutes), and Schiphol Airport (45 minutes). From the east, there are direct connections to Zwolle, Apeldoorn, Deventer, and Enschede. Amersfoort Centraal is the main station; the city also has stations at Schothorst and Vathorst.

By car, Amersfoort is at the junction of the A1 (Amsterdam–Apeldoorn) and A28 (Utrecht–Zwolle), about 50 km east of Amsterdam.
