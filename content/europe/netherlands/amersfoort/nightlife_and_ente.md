---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment in Amersfoort"
type: section
---

When you get tired from walking around and looking at the amazing sights that Amersfoort has to offer, take a breather and explore the nightlife of the city. **Amersfoort Nightlife** is nothing less than what you'd find in Amsterdam or Utrecht. The city is packed with students, and there is always something going on. All you need to do is to go out and see what you find.

**In Den Grooten Slock**

The oldest caf&eacute; is definitely In den Grooten Slock Grand Caf&eacute;, which has a special kind of ambience that brings customers back time and time again. This place has beautiful fa&ccedil;ade and amazing windows. In the summer, you can sit in front and enjoy the sun. Just across the street, you'll find Happerij & Tapperij and In den "Kleinen" Hap, which, with In den Grooten Slock, make a perfect trio in the city center for the night out.

**Happerij & Tapperij pub**

This pub is the perhaps the most fun place a tourist can see in the city of Amersfoort. It is completely colorful, and you will enjoy the amazing food they serve there. There is everything from Mediterranean cuisine to the local snacks. Tourists often come for a meal and stay for an evening drink or two.

**3 Ringen Brewery**

This place is unique in the city since it is a small brewery and a pub in one. You can taste local beer there, which is some of the finest around. This place serves great food, and it is not expensive at all. Tourists can find locals in this place and mingle with them. The relaxed atmosphere in this place will just take you in, and soon you will find yourself chatting with the people around you.

**Lazy Louis Jazz Caf&eacute;**

This is one of the rare jazz caf&eacute;s in Amersfoort, and tourists will be amazed how good music can be in here. The summer garden is really nice, and usually crowded on a hot summer night. Live bands are playing here more often than anywhere else in the city, so getting a table can sometimes be a bit tricky.

**La Viasco**

This is a gay bar, but everyone is welcome, regardless of sexual orientation. This is the place where the night starts as just drinking beer and having a chat and ends up dancing and having fun all night long. The DJ that works here on weekends is excellent, and the atmosphere and crowd are always a blast. This place is mid-range as far as the prices go, but people often end up getting some drinks for free.
