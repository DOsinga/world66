**Demographics
**

Amersfoort is the 2nd largest city in the Utrecht province, with a population of almost 150,000 people. Population density is high with over 2200 residents per square km. Nearly all of the people belong to the Dutch ethnic group, and as such speak the Dutch language. The Dutch language is a West Germanic Language and has similarities to German, English and even French. From a grammatical point of view, Dutch is closest to German. It has almost the same word order, verb morphology and syntax. However, don&rsquo;t worry about language barriers as the people here are highly-educated, with over 90% speaking English and 70% speaking German as a secondary language.

**Currency
**

Like in every well-developed Western European Country, the official currency in the Netherlands is the Euro. You can buy a Euro for about $1.20. ATMs are widely available in Amersfoort, and you can easily use your credit or debit card to exchange money. You can also use your cards for doing direct payments at hotels and restaurants. However, if you still need money in cash, go to any of the money changers and change your local currency into Euros. This will work better than changing money at the hotel itself.
