A small modern kitchen on the Langestraat serving European small plates — think burrata, roasted vegetables, good charcuterie, and occasional Dutch touches like smoked eel. The wine list skews natural and the room is lively rather than hushed. Good for a relaxed evening with a few shared dishes; book ahead as it fills quickly on weekends.
