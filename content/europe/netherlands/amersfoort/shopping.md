---
title: "Shopping in Amersfoort"
type: section
---

Amersfoort, a cozy city retaining the old world charm, is situated in
the middle of Netherlands. It holds ample choices for some great **Amersfoort
shopping**. A short sojourn to Amersfoort can be very refreshing. It
might not be a shopper's heaven, but the small shops hold ample variety
to spice up your shopping spree.

**Shopping Streets of Amersfoort**

Street shopping can be great source of pure fun when you explore the
alleys of Amersfoort. Shops are set up in a long line from Riddergang to
Kamp. Langestraat, Varkensmarkt and Utrechtsestraat are areas included
in this chain. All shops are managed by the locals and sell different
unique items that you can purchase as souvenirs. The small side streets
in the Old Town segment of Amersfoort bustle with enthusiastic local and
foreign shoppers. There are plenty of bars, theaters, restaurants and
shops in these alleys. Countless shoppers hang out at these streets to
experience the true spirit of Amersfoort and enjoy a gala shopping
fling.

**Street Market **

The Street Market of Amersfoort is hosted at Hof Square on Friday and
Saturday mornings. At the stalls you can find clothes, fruits and
vegetables, fresh garden-picked flowers and the lip-smacking local
cuisine, Stroopwafels. A fish market is also held weekly on Fridays at
GroenMarket. Lieve Vrouwekerkhof hosts a flower market on every Friday
morning.** **

**Iittala Store**

Iittala is a one-stop shopping destination for an all-inclusive
littala collection. The store covers total area of 100 square meters.
Since its inauguration in 2004, it has become one of the most recognized
landmarks of Amersfoort. The store is open from 1:00 p.m. to 5:30 p.m.
on Mondays, 10:00 a.m. to 5:30 p.m. on Tuesdays, Wednesdays and Fridays
and Saturdays. On Thursdays it is open till 9:00 p.m.

**The Sessions**

The Sessions is a tiny shop that specializes in all sorts of music
and dance related goods. Trance, Techno, Drum and Bass, Lounge,
Chill-out, Deephouse, Progressive and many more styles are available at
their store; it's good enough to impress any music lover.

**Nootzaak**

If you are looking to purchase some good music items at a reasonably
low cost, Nootzaak secondhand music shop can cater to your needs. You
will find DVDs, CDs and LPs there. The shop is open 5 days a week and
closed on Sundays and Mondays.
