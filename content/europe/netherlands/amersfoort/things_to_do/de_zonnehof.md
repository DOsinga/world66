An exhibition space designed by Gerrit Rietveld, the architect behind the famous Rietveld Schröder House in Utrecht. The building itself is as much the attraction as whatever show happens to be on — clean lines, open spaces, and that unmistakable De Stijl sensibility. Worth a visit for architecture fans even if the current exhibition doesn't grab you.
