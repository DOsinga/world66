There are several locations in and around Amsterdam where you can play golf, a sport that becomes more and more popular in the Netherlands.


 Those who never tried it, those who thought it is too expensive or boring or not "sportive" enough, should give it a chance.


 Some locations, like Amstelborgh near the Ajax stadium (Arena), can be reached by public transport and are really cheap!
 

**Recommendations**

**Amstelborgh Golf- en Conference Center B.V. **

The golf course has several advantages that might be disadvantages for others: Amstelborgh is very easy to reach, by car 10-15 min. from the city center into direction Utrecht. Or by public transport to the stadium (Arena).


 Prices are modest, the driving range is free(!) and balls are cheap. [more..](/europe/netherlands/amsterdam/activities/golf/amstelborghgolfenconferencecenterbv)

**Openbare Golfbaan Spaarnwoude **

The golfcourse Spaarnwoude, 25 min by car from city center, is a public course, also open for non-members and partly open even for absolute beginners.


 Even though the course can be overcrowded on weekend, the atmosphere is more relaxed than on many other courses.

**Golf Course "De Hoge Dijk" **

"De Hoge Dijk" is an 18-hole competition course (PAR 71, 5755 meters), designed by the renowned golf course architect Joan Dudok van Heel. The first nine holes were inaugurated in 1990; the second nine holes have been ready for play since 1995.
