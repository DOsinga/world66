Finding scenic places to jog or power walk in Amsterdam is easy as the city has more than 30 parks and four beaches to choose from. Vondelpark – Amsterdam’s largest and busiest, is a popular place for jogging as well as Sarphatipark, which is smaller and more peaceful than Vondelpark. Sarphatipark is also a popular place for dog-walkers and grabbing a bite to eat at a snack bar or café. Other popular Amsterdam parks include: Amsterdamse Bos, Frankendael, Beatrixpark, and Amstelpark. Amsterdam’s four beaches also offer opportunities for jogging and walking as well as relaxing and sunning. Amsterdam beaches include: Blijburg, Citybeach "Het stenen hoofd" (Silodam), Nemo Beach (Oosterdok 2), and Noord.
