Amsterdam's largest and busiest street market, running daily (except Sunday) along Albert Cuypstraat through the heart of De Pijp. Herring, stroopwafels, cheap clothing, cheese, flowers, and household goods fill over 300 stalls for nearly a kilometre. Busy at all hours but best mid-morning on a weekday. The surrounding streets have some of the best independent food shops in the city.
