Burger Bar is newly opened in Amsterdam Centrum with a new concept of serving healthier fast food such as Hamburgers. Normally, people tend to confuse fast food with non-healthy food. Burger Bar Amsterdam challenges this confusion and is dedicated to serving tasty and healthy hamburgers.
**Our mission**
Influences by the fast pace of today’s life, our mission is to make and distribute healthy and tasty fast food. While the big fast food firms are in the business of mass selling of average hamburgers, we are in the business of making excellent hamburgers. The difference can only be tasted.

http://www.burger-bar.nl

What do we do?

Burger Bar hamburgers are made with 100% pure premium and fresh meat accompanied by fresh ingredients spiced with tasty sauces. Burger bar’s renowned Belgian French fries are freshly cut every day.
**Our menu includes: **

Hamburgers in three sizes: 125, 185 and 250 grams
Type of hamburgers: meat, chicken burger, lamb burger, entrecote burger fish filet burger and vegetarian burger
Choice of breads: White sesam, Brown sesam and Italian bread
Choice of toppings: cedar cheese, bacon, fried egg, mushrooms, pineapple, feta cheese, eggplant, guacamole and blue cheese
Our Belgian fries are sold in three sizes and served with your choice of sauce
What you should do?

You should pass by ant try us out. Writing on hamburger’s quality and taste is nice but the real test is trying it. so you are invited. We are located in downtown Amsterdam between the Damrak and NZ.Voorburgwal.
