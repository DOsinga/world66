Travel Tips When You’re With Your Pet!

Pets have held a special place in the hearts of many families all
around the globe.Different breeds of cats and dogs hold the highest
spots on the pet list.So when you go on a vacation it would be natural
for most to bring their pets along.Whether it be a short vacation, a
long vacation, one that would take a short drive, or one that would take
an airplane ride, pets will be coming along.So if you’re a pet lover
who would want to bring your cute pooch along on your next vacation,
here are some of the most important things you need to know.

**Practice**

It is important for you to practice your pet.Teach your pet how it is
to ride a car first before bringing him to a plane.Make sure your pet
is accustomed to the crate where he will be kept, make sure he is
accustomed to possible sounds he might be hearing on the trip, and teach
him calmness when he’s alone and possibly in the dark.Bring your pet to
the store with you and place him in his crate.Short trips will then
move on to longer ones.Accustom him to peeing in his crate if he needs
to.Pets get car sick too and it take practice to get used to traveling
for them as well.

**Papers**

Makes sure you have necessary requirements for your pets especially
when you are going out of the country.Contact your local government
agency and ask about requirements needed when bringing pets along with
you.There you would also be able to check requirements of the country
you are going to.It is important to have complete and updated papers for
your pet.

**Bookings**

Check with airlines regarding their policies on pets.Some allow pets
in the cabin while others keep pets at the luggage area.Ask for prices
for both and for requirements.Airlines that allow dogs on the cabin have
weight and size specifications.Ask about that and how big of a crate
you can bring.The crate usually should be able to fit under your
chair.You may also ask about fees and check in procedure as you might
have to do other inspections for your pet.

**Hotels**

Make sure you look for pet friendly hotels.It’s inappropriate to
bring in pets at hotels that do not allow pets.No matter how small your
chihua hua is, you will get caught for bringing in your pet.Better be
safe and book at pet friendly establishments.There you and your pet will
be able to relax and have fun.

**Food, Drinks, and Medications**

Bring with you necessary food, drinks, and medications for your pet.
There is a chance that the hotel you’ll stay in won’t have the brand of
food your pet needs.So be prepared and bring some along.

Taking your pet with you on your holiday vacation is an awesome thing to
do.So long as you prepare well and you are able to complete necessary
documentations then you’re good to go.Enjoy visiting pet friendly
countries like the Netherlands with your pet!
