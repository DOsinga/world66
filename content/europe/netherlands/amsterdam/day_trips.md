---
title: "Day Trips"
type: section
---

Amsterdam is an excellent base for the wider Netherlands. Haarlem is 20 minutes by train and has a beautiful old centre with less than a tenth of Amsterdam's tourist traffic. The Zaanse Schans open-air museum with its working windmills is half an hour north by train. Leiden and Delft are easy south-bound trips for Golden Age history and architecture. In spring, the Keukenhof tulip gardens near Lisse (late March to mid-May) are worth the trip.
