**Delft** is a great one-day escape not only because it is the charming hometown of the painter Vermeer and home to the Royal Dutch Delftware Manufactory, but you can also combine it with a trip to the nearby city of The Hague. In Delft, you can visit a number of all day art markets and flea markets, people-watch, trek across canal bridges, and enjoy some of the best syrup waffles the Netherlands has to offer. A quick trip to **The Hague** (the Netherlands capital) will take you to a number of amazing museums such as Mauritshuis (Vermeer and Rembrandt), amusement parks such as Madurodam, palaces such as the Peace Palace, and more.
