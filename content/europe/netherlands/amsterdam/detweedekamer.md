One of Amsterdam's better-known coffeeshops, tucked into a narrow alley just off the Spui. De Tweede Kamer — "the second chamber," which also happens to be the name of the Dutch parliament — predates the more famous De Dampkring that the same owners later opened nearby. It's smaller and more intimate: more like a cosy alternative grand café than a dark smoke den. In good weather there are benches outside on the Spui.
