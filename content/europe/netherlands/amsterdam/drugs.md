---
title: "Coffee Shops"
type: section
---

Cannabis is not technically legal in Amsterdam but tolerated under a policy of gedoogbeleid — official non-enforcement. Coffee shops are licensed to sell small amounts (max 5g) to adults over 18 for personal use. You cannot smoke in regular bars or public spaces. The quality and variety at the better shops is extraordinary; the staff are usually happy to explain what they have. If you eat space cake, start with less than you think you need and give it an hour before deciding it hasn't worked.
