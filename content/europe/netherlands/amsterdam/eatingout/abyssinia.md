Make sure you make a reservation when you want to enjoy one of the Ethiopian or Eritrean dishes in this charming little restaurant. Great staff, good prices, don't forget to wash your hands and do take an African beer (served in a wooden bowl with a slice of lemon) instead of your usual Heineken. Only problem is how to eat the pieces of oranges that come with the bill without scaring away your table company.** :** That's really cool stuff!
