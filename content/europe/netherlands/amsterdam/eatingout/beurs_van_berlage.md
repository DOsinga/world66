The former Exchange of Amsterdam, the monumental "Beurs van Berlage", nowadays is a prestigous center for culture, conferences and events.

It also houses a very pretty Café, in the former Main entrance, at the southside of the building. The Café offers good lunches, pastry and italian branded coffies and cappuccino. Three impressive mosaics by Art Nouveau artist Jan Toorop dominate the room.

March-October the Café is also running a beautiful terrace on the atmospheric "Beursplein" (Exchange-square)
