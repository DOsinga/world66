Buffet is a charming cafe and restaurant situated in the heart of the stylish Nine Streets (De Negen Straatjes) District of Amsterdam, perfectly positioned overlooking the Herengracht - one of Amsterdam's most beautiful canals, here you can sit and watch the world go by. It 's a wonderful spot to make an appointment or to read the newspapers and in fine weather you can sit outside. During the day, they serve a range of home made quiches, soups, Italian pasta home made cakes and fruit tarts and the 'speciality' of the house are their renowned truffle cheese omelettes. There's a wide and delicious selection of vegetarian options. Group bookings welcome. Everything is homemade using their own recipes with the finest ingredients and with the highest attention to detail.
