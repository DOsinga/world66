If you count the number of Indian restaurants you will not be surprised that some people call Amsterdam "Bombay on the Amstel". Nearly in every neighbourhood you can find Indian restaurants. Try the ones in the Lange Leidsedwarsstaat or around the Albert Cuyp market. 

**Restaurants**

**Bojo**

**Anthon :** Als je beestjes in je sla niet erg vindt is dit een prima plekje.

address: Lange Leidse Dwarsstraat 49 & 51

tel: 020 6227434/6268990

pc: 1017 NG

**Purna**

A nice small Indian restaurant near the Jordan. Friendly staff. The curries are very nice, although the rice portions could be larger. On the other hand the prices are rather modest in comparison to other Indian restaurants in Amsterdam. 

address: Harten Straat 29, 1016 CA Amsterdam

tel: +31-(0)20-6236772
