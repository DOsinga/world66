Indonesian food has in the range of Dutch restaurants a special position. Some typical Indonesian dishes (esp. "kip saté", which is three to five pieces of chicken grilled on a wooden spit, always served with peanut sauce), are considered Dutch heritage meanwhile. For Dutch children they are like burgers for Americans.
