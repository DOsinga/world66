**Restaurants**

**Trez**

Good intermational cuisine with a focus on mediterranean food. If the weather is good you can sit outside on one of the most trendy spots of the Pijp quarter. It is a bit on the expensive side though, with prices of 20 Euro for a main dish. 

address: Saenredamstraat 39 hs

pc: 1072 CC

tel: 020-6762495

**Febo**

The Febo is the Dutch answer to MacDonalds. Everything is deep fried in oil, most of it can be purchased 'from the wall'. Little windows seperate you from your food. Insert a few guilders and the window opens and the goodies are yours.
