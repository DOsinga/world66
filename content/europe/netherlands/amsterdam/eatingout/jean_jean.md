This is the one for culinairy enthousiasts. Chef Mattijs Koornneef
performs magic in this small restaurant in the heart of the Jordan
quarter. They have a small a la carte selection and a daily 3-course
meal with seasonal ingredients. The a la carte menu is good but if you
want the real feast, let Mattijs decide what he'll cook for you.

Just so you have an idea:

* Amuse: cucumbergranito with a soja jelly with Oysterfoam
* Crispy baked coquille st Jaques
* Lamb three ways

Usually it's wise to make reservations in advance. Closed on Mondays.
