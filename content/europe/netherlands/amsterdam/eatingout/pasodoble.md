---
title: "Paso Doble"
type: poi
---

Very busy tapas restaurant where you might want to book a table in advance or just squeeze in with the people at the big wooden table in the back and make friends for the evening. Great staff, quick service, sizzling pots and pans and excellent tapas that will finally make that girl keep her mouth shut, if only for a few seconds.... Be aware that you'll always stay longer than you intended and that jugs of Sangria do contain alcohol. 

address: Westerstraat 86

tel: 020 421 2670
