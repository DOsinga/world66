de Wijnbar is at the moment the most trendy place to go when you don't really want to eat or lounge, but just want to enjoy good wine and taste some goodies. It might be considered a tapas bar, but not strictly Spanish. It's cozy, well looked after and not too expensive. It's not cheap either, but if you check before you order and if you don't order extraordinary things you'll be all right even on a smaller budget.
