Toscanini is Amsterdam's most popular Italian restaurant, so if you're planning a visit, book well in advance. Open since 1985, Toscanini serves up fresh home-made pastas and Italian bread from the restaurants own bakery. Patrons can select from a fixed menu and a menu of the day with its specialties. The wine list consists of authentic Italian wines from regions located north to south of Italy. Toscanini is a non-smoking restaurant.
