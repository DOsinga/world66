The southern boundary of De Pijp — a wide boulevard cutting across the neighbourhood from west to east. More residential than Ferdinand Bolstraat, with a good run of neighbourhood cafes, a couple of bookshops, and the slower pace of a street where people actually live rather than visit. Useful for orienting yourself in the southern half of the neighbourhood.
