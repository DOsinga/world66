The eastern edge of De Pijp — a quieter street running parallel to Ferdinand Bolstraat with a string of neighbourhood restaurants and small shops. Less well-known than the market streets, which means lower prices and fewer tourists. The stretch around Gerard Doustraat junction is particularly good for eating.
