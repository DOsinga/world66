The daily-life shopping street of Oud-Zuid — independent food shops, a good cheese merchant, a flower market, and several cafes used by the neighbourhood rather than visitors. A few blocks from the PC Hooftstraat luxury strip but an entirely different register. Good for provisions before a Vondelpark afternoon.
