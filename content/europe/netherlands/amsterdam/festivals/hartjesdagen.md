Much smaller than the Gay Pride is the " Hartjesdagen" festival right
in the Nieuwmarkt area on the Zeedijk. The third weekend in August men
turn up as women, and women as men. And they have been doing so for
centuries! Some historians believe this fun packed romantic festival
dates back to the Middle ages. Its small scale is a definit plus. More
information can be found on the website: www.hartjesdagen.nl
