Frans Otten Stadion is Amsterdam's main ice rink, tucked behind the Olympic Stadium in the Stadionbuurt neighbourhood. The rink hosts figure skating, speed skating, and ice hockey, and is open to the public for recreational sessions year-round.

It draws a mix of serious skaters training for competition and families out for an afternoon. Skate hire is available at the rink. The setting is no-frills — this is a working sports facility, not a tourist attraction — but if you want to skate in Amsterdam, this is where to go.
