There are numerous places to hire bikes from. Some recommended include:


 **Take a bike**, stationsplein 12, Tel. 6248391, near Central Station. F9.50 per day, or F36 for a week.


 **Bike City**, Bloemgracht 70, Tel. 6263721, in the Jordaan, from F12.50 per day or F50 for a week.


 **MacBike**, Marnixstraat 220, Tel. 020-6266964, not far from Leidseplein, F12.50 for the first day, going down to F5 after a week or so.
