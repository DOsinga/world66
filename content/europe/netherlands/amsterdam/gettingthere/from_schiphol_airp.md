title: "From Schiphol Airport"
type: poi
type: "By Rail"
---

Getting to and from the airport is easy. Trains run from Central
Station every 15 minutes and there are also trains from other stations,
like Zuid. The train trip from Central Station to the airport will take
you about 20 minutes. A taxi will set you back about 30 Euros, the
train more like 4 Euros.
