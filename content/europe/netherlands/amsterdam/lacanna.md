La Canna is a smartshop at Nieuwendijk 123, right on Amsterdam's main pedestrian shopping street near Centraal Station. Smartshops in the Netherlands sell legal psychoactive products — think herbal supplements, truffles (psilocybin-containing magic truffles, legal here), kratom, and various botanical preparations. Staff can advise on what's what. It's a straightforward, no-frills shop in a convenient central location.
