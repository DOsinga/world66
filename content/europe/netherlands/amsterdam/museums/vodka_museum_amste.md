Experience a spectacular adventure in history: see, hear and experience the glamorous World of Vodka. Visit the first and only Museum of Vodka in Europe: “VodkaMuseum Amsterdam”! It is an adventure that takes you back in time from the origins of Vodka to the world of cocktails and bartending today. In the oldest building of Damrak (central tourist street of Amsterdam), you can receive a short guided tour through the 3-story museum! Here you will enjoy a collection of up to 500 various Vodkas and watch a VodkaMovie in the relaxing VodkaMuseum Lounge! At the VodkaMuseum Bar you can make a movie about your experience at the Museum and send it to your friends! But that’s not all! At the end of your tour you will receive a delicious VodkaMuseum surprise! After the tour you are welcome to stop by the VodkaShop where we offer you an amazing selection of Vodkas!
