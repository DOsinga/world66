On the last Sunday the best Latin party takes place on two floors at the Club Arena. The so-called Salsa Lounge is a must for every Salsa and Samba friend. It starts at 6 pm. but becomes crowded at 8 pm. There is a small Salsa work shop and a Samba during the early hours. Especially the Samba work-shop is great fun. In the Samba hall a DJ plays Latin Hip Hop and more while the Salsa crowd goes crazy on the first floor in the exotic environment of a former monastery.
