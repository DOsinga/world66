**Recommendations**

**Carels Eetcafe**

Next to Carel's Cafe is Carel's eetcafe, where many students from the Pijp come to eat. It's not really cheap but the atmosphere is great. Opposite is Carel's nacht cafe which stays open until 3 AM. It only gets crowded after midnight.

address: Frans Halsstraat 78

tel: 020 662 24 90
