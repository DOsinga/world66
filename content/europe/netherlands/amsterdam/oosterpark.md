The heart of the Oost neighbourhood — a Victorian park with a large pond, mature trees, and a distinctly local atmosphere. The park is surrounded by late-19th-century housing and used daily by the diverse community that gives Oost its character. The National Slavery Monument stands near the entrance; a quiet acknowledgment of a difficult history.
