The main canal of the old city — a wide, straight waterway running from Centraal Station toward the university. The canal has two faces: the southern stretch is lined with window prostitution and sex shops; the northern end, near the Oude Kerk and the university buildings, is quieter and architecturally exceptional. Worth walking the full length to see both.
