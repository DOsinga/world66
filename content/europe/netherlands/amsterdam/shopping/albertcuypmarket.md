The Albert Cuypmarket has more then 400 stalls selling local and exotic foodstuffs, spices and teas, clothing new and second hand and thousands of different odds and ends. You can wander around, sampling the offerings, or look at the public: On this market you notice how international Amsterdam is. People from half of all countries shop and sell here. 

The market runs daily between van Woustraat and de Ferdinand Bol (not on sunday). Get there with tram 16,24,25 or 4 from Central Station. The market is within walking distance from Leidseplein.
