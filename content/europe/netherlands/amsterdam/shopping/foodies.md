In these shops you will find local and imported specialties, funny food gifts and tasty souvenirs. 

**Shops**

**Jolanda en Fred de Leeuw**

Butchershop De Leeuw (Utrechtsestraat 92) presents a wide variety of red and white meats, delicacies, truffles, pâtés, mushrooms. Top quality, ditto prices.

**Supermarkets**

None of the cheaper Supermarkets have dependances in the center of Amsterdam, but you could do worse then to visit Albert Heijn on the Nieuwezijdsvoorburgwal opposite Damsquare.
