The best food-shopping street in Amsterdam — a long run of independent food merchants, wine shops, cheese dealers, Japanese kitchen suppliers, artisan bakeries, and delicatessens stretching from Westerpark toward Central Station. The range shifts between the two names (Haarlemmerstraat east of Haarlemmerplein, Haarlemmerdijk west of it) but the quality holds throughout. Come hungry.
