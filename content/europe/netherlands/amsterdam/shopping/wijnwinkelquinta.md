The Wijnwinkel Quinta (wine shop) is of course not a reason to visit
Amsterdam, but once you are already here and you like for example
portwine, well, then you should not miss this little shop. Vintage and
Tawnies back till the 40s - a great choice!

The house brand is Quinta do Castelhino, The 10 year aged tawny is really remarkable.
