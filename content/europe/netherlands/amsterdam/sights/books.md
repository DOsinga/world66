Amsterdam has many good bookshops. The biggest one is Scheltema which is located at the Koningsplein. In the Kalverstraat you can find English books at WH Smith and The American Bookstore.

The selection of books presented here is a personal choice. If you're thinking of experimenting with marihuana or hash, I would recommend of picking up the book 'hashish'. It contains alot of information and is very well written.
