**Sights**

**Waag**

Today the Waag (Weigh House) is a restaurant cum Cybercafe. The Weigh House at the Nieuwmarkt is the oldest secular public building in Amsterdam. It was designed as a city gate (Sint Antoniespoort, 1488) and as such it formed part of the medieval defences.  

address: Nieuwmarkt

**Royal Palace**

The Royal Palace (1648-1665) was designed as the most prestigious town hall of its time. It is justly famous for being a large and important cultural and historic building of 17th century Amsterdam.

**Beurs van Berlage**

Designed by Berlage, the most famous modern architect of the Netherlands, his Beurs was the starting point of Dutch Modern architecture. 

address: Beursplein 5
