On top of Post CS Building, next to central station where the Stedelijk
Museum has been temporarily transfered, you can discover quite a
fantastic view of Amsterdam !

At the 11th floor by the lift, you get access to the trendy
bar-restaurant-disco 11 where youngsters from the city go for a drink
or a night.

Next to the entry of the bar you can go upstairs to an outside bar,
accessible during summer and leading to a roof terrace on which you can
look around at the whole city with an original view on Nemo.
