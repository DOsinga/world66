The Van Gogh Museum is home to the largest collection of paintings by Vincent Van Gogh. Of Van Gogh’s 900 paintings and 1,200 drawings, the Van Gogh Museum has 200 paintings and 500 drawings. The museum also has 850 letters and works by Gauguin, Monet, Bernard, and Pissaro. The Van Gogh Museum also houses a study area and library, a restaurant and museum shop, and it also offers a general tour, a multimedia tour, and a special audio tour just for kids.
