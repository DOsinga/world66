Zeeburg is a new quarter that is being built north east of Amsterdam on a series of artificial island on the river IJ.

Zeeburg is basically a residential area famous for its modern
architecture buildings and for its nice restaurant. Anyway don't expect
anything monumental such as [La Defense](/europe/france/paris/sights/ladefense) in Paris or Canary Warf in London: you'll find just nice blocks of houses.
