The busiest square in Amsterdam, ringed with bars, restaurants and the old Stadsschouwburg theatre. In winter there is an outdoor ice rink. In summer every table spills onto the terrace and street performers work the crowds. It is the hub of Amsterdam's nightlife and the place to start or end an evening.
