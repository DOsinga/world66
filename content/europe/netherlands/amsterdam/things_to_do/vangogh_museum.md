The world's largest collection of Van Gogh's work — 200 paintings and 500 drawings — arranged to tell the story of his life from the dark Dutch peasant studies to the blazing colour of his Provence years. The neighbouring work by Gauguin, Toulouse-Lautrec and Millet puts him in context. Buy tickets in advance; the queue without them is brutal.
