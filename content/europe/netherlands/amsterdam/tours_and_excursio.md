---
title: "Tours and Excursions"
type: section
---

Canal boat tours are the most popular way to see Amsterdam from a different angle; there are hop-on-hop-off boats and smaller open launches that get further into the narrower canals. Walking tours of the Jordaan, the Jewish quarter and the Red Light District are offered by several operators, including some run by local residents. Rent a bike and pedal your own route along the canals for a less structured alternative.
