### Cocktail Workshop

A nice excursion for all group purphoses in Amsterdam is the Cocktail
Workshop. Booked by the tour organisation, the workshop is held in a
very hip bar near the Jordaan part of the city. Is available for groups
from 8 upto 20 persons, please make a reservation at least 2 weeks
before the event date.
