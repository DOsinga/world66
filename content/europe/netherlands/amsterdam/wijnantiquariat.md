Tucked behind a bookshop near the Rijksmuseum, this tiny shop buys and sells old wines and port, with bottles going back as far as 1895. The selection is curated and genuinely interesting — not just old but drinkable, and the owner knows the history of each bottle. There are usually a few good bottles open for tasting. Open Saturday noon–5pm and weekdays by appointment.
