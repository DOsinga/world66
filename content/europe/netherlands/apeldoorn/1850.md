A grand-café on Apeldoorn's main shopping street, housed in a building dating from — as the name suggests — the mid-nineteenth century. The interior mixes exposed brick and high ceilings with modern furnishings. Good for coffee during the day or a beer in the evening after browsing the shops along the Hoofdstraat.
