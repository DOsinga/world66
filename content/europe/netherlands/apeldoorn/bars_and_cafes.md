If you&rsquo;re looking for some trendy **Apeldoorn**** bars and cafes**, you should definitely visit Stadscaf&eacute; Van Kinsbergen. It is undoubtedly one of the trendiest cafes in Apeldoorn, located right in the heart of the city, on Nieuwstraat 295. The place opens up at 11 a.m. and frequently stays open until midnight, so it is suitable both for having an afternoon coffee or a drink in the evening.

**The Atmosphere at Stadscaf&eacute; Van Kinsbergen**

Stadscaf&eacute; Van Kinsbergen is situated inside a lovely historical building. A classy, modern interior reveals itself to you as you enter the caf&eacute;. You can choose to either sit at one of the many bar stools arranged around the bar, or at one of the stylish wooden tables. The furniture, walls and lanterns hanging from the ceiling are all colored in red and create a truly unique atmosphere. On certain days, free live music events are held for the guests of the caf&eacute;. There is also excellent outdoor seating available in the summer.

**The Menu at Stadscaf&eacute; Van Kinsbergen**

Stadscaf&eacute; Van Kinsbergen has an extensive menu, which includes not only drinks, but also various lunch and dinner options. The lunch menu consists mainly of snacks like sandwiches, omelets and salads, while the dinner menu is more like a restaurant menu, with various starters, main dishes and desserts. However, Stadscaf&eacute; Van Kinsbergen is most popular with its excellent cocktails. You can choose from over 50 different types and every Friday all cocktails are the same price ($5.50 per drink).
