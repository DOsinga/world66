**Some Recommendations for Planning Apeldoorn Day Trips**

Most **Apeldoorn day trips** include visiting the nearby towns which are famous for their scenic beauty. Apeldoorn is located in the Gelderland province of the Netherlands. Located just about 60 miles south of the capital city of Amsterdam, Apeldoorn is regarded as an important commercial and historic city. People planning Apeldoorn day trips should seriously consider visiting the village of Loenen.

#### **Reaching Loenen**

Located along the southeastern outskirts of the city, Loenen is considered the typical Dutch hamlet. It is set among lavish green gardens and historical monuments, and Loenen serves as the ideal day trip location. It is easily accessible, just 10 kilometers from Apeldoorn. Visitors can either rent a cab or take a bus to reach this village. Another option is to take a trip aboard the old steam train which runs a daily service from the Apeldoorn railway station. Loenen is among the many stops the train makes during its 22 kilometer route

#### **What to Do in Loenen**

On the way to Loenen, people can visit a local cemetery which is home to more than 2,500 graves of the World War II victims. Among the many gardens which adorn Loenen, the most beautiful gardens are the Huis Horst Gardens. They are themed around a Renaissance-era mansion that was built during the 16th century. Some of the garden facades were renovated during the 18th century. Among historic monuments in Loenen, the Loenersloot Castle is the most popular. It is located along the banks of Angstel River.
