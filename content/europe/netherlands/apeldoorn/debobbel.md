A traditional brown cafe on Apeldoorn's Hoofdstraat, De Bobbel is a good spot to settle in after an afternoon at Het Loo. The interior has the warm, lived-in feel typical of Dutch bruine kroegen, with dark wood paneling and a solid beer selection on tap. Unpretentious and reliably lively on weekend evenings.
