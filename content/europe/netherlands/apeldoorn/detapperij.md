De Tapperij is a classic brown cafe on Apeldoorn's main shopping street. The interior leans into the traditional Dutch gezelligheid — dark wood paneling, low lighting, and a long bar lined with tap handles. The beer selection rotates frequently and skews toward Dutch and Belgian craft brews.

It's a good spot for an afternoon drink after walking the Hoofdstraat, or for settling in on a rainy evening. The terrace out front catches sun when there is any.
