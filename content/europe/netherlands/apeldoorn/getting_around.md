**Different Means of Transportation in Apeldoorn**

When it comes to **Apeldoorn**** transportation**, visitors will encounter an excellent transport infrastructure. The transportation allows you to reach literally any point within the city.

#### **Buses**

The bus transport in Apeldoorn is handled by the major international transport company, Veolia Transport. There are over 15 bus lines serving the city and a complex transport network. The buses are well-maintained and have a special electronic ticketing system, which allows you to easily pay for your trip using a chip-card or a cell phone. A ride on the bus may cost as little as $0.60 if you use the 45-rides chip-card and at most $1 if you&rsquo;re using the 2-rides chip-card. There are also chip-cards for 3, 8 and 15 rides. Chip-cards can easily be bought from various kiosks around the bus stops and numerous local universal stores.

#### **Bicycles**

Riding a bicycle is by no doubt the most popular form of transportation in the Netherlands. In some of the smaller towns, the bicycles are the only transport available. However, considering the size of Apeldoorn, this method of transportation may not be the most convenient when it comes to traveling quickly. If you have enough time at your disposal, though, riding a bicycle is a good option. Apeldoorn has an excellent bicycle network, and bicycles are widely available for rent. Just keep in mind to lock your bicycle when you stop and park it somewhere.
