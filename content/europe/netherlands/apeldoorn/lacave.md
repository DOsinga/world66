A cozy wine bar on Apeldoorn's main shopping street, the Hoofdstraat. La Cave offers a solid selection of wines by the glass alongside simple snacks. The interior has a warm, vaulted feel that suits the name, and when the weather cooperates there are a few tables outside for watching the street life pass by.
