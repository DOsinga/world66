An **Apeldoorn nightlife** is filled with German beer and artistic shows. This small city is located in the province of Gelderland in Netherlands. There are many wonderful attractions in the area, however, after dark there are several ways for tourists to entertain themselves.

#### Dining Out

Dining out in Apeldoorn is an experience in itself. There is a wide selection of local restaurants in the area that serve local dishes. Don Quichotte Eten and Drinken is a small restaurant that has a cozy ambiance. It offers a wide selection of food items on the menu. For travelers wanting a casual yet affordable meal, this restaurant is recommended.

There are also restaurants that serve international cuisine, like Asia, which features a Vietnamese restaurant. Asia restaurants have both a la carte and buffet offerings that feature some of the well known Vietnamese foods. The restaurant is a non-smoking one, but outside seating is available depending on the weather condition.

For meat lovers, the Farmer&rsquo;s Steakhouse is a good option located in the center of Apeldoorn. The Farmer&rsquo;s has a country house ambiance, where diners can take a peek at the hustle and bustle of the main streets in Apeldoorn (since most of the tables are situated near the windows).

#### Caterplein Apeldoorn

For those looking for a real night out in Apeldoorn, they should check out the Caterplein Square, where one can find several pubs and bars. Aside from the pubs one can find in this city square, there are also restaurants and cafes that line up the square. There are also several art cafes in the area. Some of the popular bars in Caterplein are Caf&eacute; de graaf van Vlaanderen and Rockcaf&eacute; Ozzy. The Caterplein Apeldoorn is located at the tip of Hoofstraat.

#### Theaters

Alperdoon is also famed for its art theaters. There are several notable theaters in the area including the Orpheus Theatre Apeldoorn. It is considered to be one of the biggest theaters in Apeldoorn and one of the most modern in the Netherlands. This theater performs a wide selection of performing arts shows including ballet, dance music, dramatic art and cabaret. Aside from the main theater hall, this theater also features a restaurant and caf&eacute;.

The Podium Gigant Apeldoorn is another favorite location of locals. This theater features two small cinemas. One of the cinemas is the main theater, while the other room is used for concerts. There is also a caf&eacute; in the theater. Aside from these two theaters, there are also over 20 different theaters in Apeldoorn including Bioscoop Tivoli, Huis Voor Schoone Kunsten and Americahal.
