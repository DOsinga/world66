**Facts about Apeldoorn**

Knowledge of **Apeldoorn practical information** will help you make the most out of your vacation. Apeldoorn is located to the southeast of Amsterdam in the central region of Netherlands.

#### **Currency**

The local currency of Apeldoorn is the Euro (&euro;). Credit cards are widely accepted. As a precaution, it would be better to carry traveler's checks either in Euros, U.S. Dollars or Pounds Sterling. Traveler's checks can be cashed at numerous outlets in the airport as well as in major areas in Apeldoorn. All banks in Apeldoorn are closed on weekends, but the exchange bureaus are open every day. ATMs are present all over the city, and you will find a few which operate round the clock.

#### **Visa**

Tourists coming from countries like the U.S., New Zealand, Australia, Israel, Canada and Singapore can enter the Netherlands without a visa. These are among approximately 60 countries that only require a valid passport for entry. Travelers can stay for up to 3 months. European Union nationals can enter with their national ID card. Nationals from all other countries will have to secure a Schengen visa. This type of visa is issued by the Dutch Embassy. The validity of the visa can be from 2 days to 2 months. This visa will permit you to enter the Netherlands and visit Apeldoorn and all other cities. While applying for the visa, you should have a valid passport and documents that prove you have enough funds. If the need arises you can get the tourist visa extended for 3 months.
