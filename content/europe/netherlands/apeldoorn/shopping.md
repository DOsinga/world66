---
title: "Shopping"
type: section
---

Shopping in Apeldoorn is concentrated along the Hoofdstraat, the long main street running through the centre. The **Oranjerie** mall, about halfway down, has around 60 shops covering clothes, electronics, toys and accessories. It observes a "koopzondag" -- open the first Sunday of each month, typically 11:00 to 18:00.

Beyond the mall, the Hoofdstraat itself is lined with the usual Dutch high-street chains plus some independent shops. The **Paleis Het Loo museum shop** is worth a stop for china, art prints and postcards -- some items are reproductions from the palace collection. Open Tuesday through Sunday, 13:00 to 17:00.
