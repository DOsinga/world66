Hunting for bargains and ideal gift items in Apeldoorn, Netherlands, is fun and convenient, as long as you know how to get around the great **Apeldoorn shopping** centers. The municipality of Apeldoorn in the province of Gelderland, Netherlands, is a regional center with around 136,000 residents. Here, you can find a lot of shopping establishments that offer goods from high end products to casual souvenirs. Check out these places and enjoy a great shopping experience.

#### Oranjerie Mall

This shopping mall houses around 60 shops that sell a variety of merchandise. You will find a lot of clothing boutiques that cater to people of all ages, men and women alike. Kids will also have a great time since there&rsquo;s an abundance of toy and gaming shops. Find the time to explore the small booths that offer affordable accessories. The Oranjerie is also perfect for the techies. There are a lot of shops here selling reasonably priced gadgets and electronics. Women may also look for casual and luxurious jewelry and great cosmetic products. If you are planning to visit other tourist spots, consult with one of the many travel agencies inside the mall. Great furniture is also available and so are shops for those who have hobbies, like photography or sports.

Most malls in the Netherlands observe Sundays as the Sabbath day, and therefore refuse to open their doors. However, the Oranjerie Mall has a so-called &ldquo;koopzondag&rdquo; which is a provision that allows people to shop on the first Sunday of each month. Business hours vary from day to day, but this mall is usually open between 11:00 am and 6:00 pm.

#### Hoofdstraat Shops

You might have a hard time looking for Apeldoorn shopping centers outside the street of Hoofdstraat. Unlike most cities, Apeldoorn shops are heavily concentrated along one street, which happens to be Hoofdstraat. Having said that, once you&rsquo;re there, you will have a lot of shopping options to choose from. Shop around for affordable souvenirs and specialty craft stores.

#### Paleis Het Loo Museum Shop

This unique museum shop offers sophisticated pieces of china, artwork, souvenirs and postcards. The fact that the public museum shop is an extension of the royal palace originally built for King William III explains the pristine products that it has to offer. Some of the goods sold here are actually pieces from the palace itself. This is a great place to shop for gift items and decorative artwork and vases. It is open from 1:00 pm to 5:00 pm every day, except Mondays and declared holidays.

All in all, if you are in search of great items, unique works of art and other collectibles, these Apeldoorn shopping centers will never disappoint.
