title: "Things to Do in Apeldoorn"
type: section
---

While you are visiting here you will quickly see that there are a number of **Apeldoorn Things to Do **which will provide you with a satisfying vacation trip. Even though Apeldoorn is a reasonably small city, averaging at about 130,000 people it has become a favorite vacationing spot for many folks. You have several types of activities or events to go to like the Veluwe or a local museum that has all kinds of historical information that will astound you. Plus you have the opportunity to enjoy the great landscapes within and outside of the city. No matter what your tastes, there are plenty of things for you to do.

#### Cultuue Onder Dak Apeldoorn

A great opportunity to have a pleasant time while learning a lot about Apeldoorn and the surrounding area, this huge emporium has several ways to inform the locals as well as visitors about the Dutch culture. They have a huge library and modern museum area. You can see that they are trying to reach a broad public group and they explain the history of the area by using many topics. Literature, music and films are shown but they also have exhibits about the people who have made history. The emporium uses both conventional and interactive media means to share information.

#### The Veluwe

In and around Apeldoorn is an amazing system of bike-riding. trails The Veluwe park section especially has a network of bike tracks. You can devise your own bike tour by looking at a bike map and locating path intersections to see where you want to go and how far. This is a fantastic way to see the countryside of Apeldoorn by walking or bicycling.

#### Apenheul Primate Park

This park is a unique zoo which holds a distinct type of animal--monkeys. You will not have seen so many monkeys in all of your life. There isn't a place where so many different species of monkeys are living together. You can go through themed areas to see monkeys and other animals from a habitat like the Madagascar area or the Amazon area. If you like you can also take a trip along the tree tops to see what monkeys live up there.

#### Hotel et le Cafe De Paris

This restaurant has to be the most popular here in Apeldoorn. It certainly has the elegance of Paris about it. It is a lovely restaurant with that certain kind of atmosphere that lets you know you have walked into a special place. The food is great and the staff is exceptionally professional. The food is considered the best in the city by the locals. And, the drinks are reasonably priced also.

#### Nature Walk

During your time here you must take some time to indulge in the beautiful foliage that the city maintains in it's parks. The Oranjepark is the oldest, having been built in 1876. Other well-known parks are Verzetstrijderspark and Wilhelminapark which is named after a former queen. There a total of five parks in all and they are there for your enjoyment.
