---
title: "Bars and Cafes"
type: section
---

Arnhem's nightlife centres on the Korenmarkt and the streets around it. The small square packs in more places than you would expect.

**Cafe de Beugel** is a proper Dutch *bruin cafe* that serves its beer exclusively in old-fashioned swing-top bottles (*beugels*). No pints here -- just bottled beer and brown-cafe atmosphere.

**Bloopers** on the corner of Korenmarkt and Pauwstraat is a popular party cafe with dance and swing music.

For clubbing, the **Manhattan** and **Opera** are both on or near the Korenmarkt. Manhattan leans toward dance music; Opera ranges from Top 40 to house depending on the night.

**Brazzon** (Nieuwstraat 66) is a gay cafe with a friendly atmosphere and good music.
