---
title: "Shopping"
type: section
---

Arnhem's main shopping streets are the **Ketelstraat** and its continuation the **Vijzelstraat**, which together form the commercial spine of the centre. You will find the usual Dutch chains alongside more interesting independent shops.

For something different, head to the **Weverstraat** (off the Jansstraat). Three shops next to each other cater to different tastes: Deal for second-hand clothing, Britain for hip-hop and streetwear, and Soul for curated vintage from the 1970s.

The **Bijenkorf** department store is the biggest in town -- expensive, but the basement "Chill Out" zone has good gifts and trendy clothing. **Vroom & Dreesmann** on the Velperplein is the more everyday alternative.

On Saturdays, the **market on the Marktplein** is worth visiting. Walk down the Koningsstraat to reach it, and stop at **Dille & Kamille** in the same street for kitchen and garden supplies.

If you are hungry while shopping, **Tsjakka** in the Roggestraat does cheap and cheerful Dutch snacks -- try a *kroket*.
