---
title: "Eating Out"
type: section
---

Bergen has a surprisingly diverse dining scene for a small village, ranging from the standard Dutch-Chinese-Indonesian restaurant to Greek, Japanese, Italian, and French options. The standout is **De Kleine Javaan** (The Little Javanese) at Loudelsweg 49 — a tiny restaurant with just six tables serving a single item: the Javanese rice table. If you love Indonesian food, do not miss it. In summer it is wise to book ahead (phone: 072 5818835).
