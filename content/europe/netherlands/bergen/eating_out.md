---
title: "Eating Out"
type: section
---

Bergen punches well above its size for food. The main square and streets nearby hold a good mix — Dutch-Indonesian restaurants (a national staple), Greek, Japanese, Italian, and French options — so you're unlikely to be stuck. The standout for lovers of Indonesian food is De Kleine Javaan (The Little Javanese) on Loudelsweg 49, a ten-minute walk from the centre. It seats just six tables and serves one thing: the Javanese rice table. Reserve ahead in summer (+31 72 581 8835).
