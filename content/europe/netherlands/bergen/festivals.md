Every august: The International Holland Music Sessions, three weeks of classical music

First weekend of september: Jazz and Sail, three days of jazz music in Bergen and on the beach at Bergen aan Zee.

Every october: Kunsttiendaagse, ten days in which local artists open their doors for artlovers and explain how they work.

### [Jazz & Sail Bergen 2011](/europe/netherlands/bergen/festivals/jazz__sail_bergen)

First weekend of september: **Jazz & Sail Bergen**, three days of **jazz music** in Bergen and on the beach at Bergen aan Zee. On Saturday and Sunday there are also **catamaran races** organised.
