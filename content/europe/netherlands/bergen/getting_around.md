---
title: "Getting Around"
type: section
---

Bergen is a small village and easy to explore on foot. The beach at Bergen aan Zee is about 5 km west and reachable by bus or bicycle. Regular buses connect Bergen to [Alkmaar](/europe/netherlands/alkmaar), from where trains run to [Amsterdam](/europe/netherlands/amsterdam) in about 30 minutes. Cycling is the most pleasant way to explore the surrounding dunes and polders.
