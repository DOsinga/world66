---
title: "When to Go"
type: section
---

Bergen is pleasant year-round, but summer brings the fullest calendar of events. In August the International Holland Music Sessions draws top young classical musicians for three weeks of concerts — including performances in the Ruïnekerk. The first weekend of September brings Jazz & Sail, with jazz music in the village and on the beach at Bergen aan Zee. In October, the Kunsttiendaagse opens local artists' studios to visitors for ten days. Winters are quiet and mild by Dutch standards.
