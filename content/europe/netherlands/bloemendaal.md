Bloemendaal is a popular seaside resort about 30 km from [Amsterdam](/europe/netherlands/amsterdam). The beach is pretty much the same as in [Zandvoort](/europe/netherlands/zandvoort) but there are more popular beach clubs in Bloemendaal. The disadvantage of Bloemendaal is that you can't get there by direct train from Amsterdam, so you have to take the bus.
