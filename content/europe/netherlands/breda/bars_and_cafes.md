title: "Bars and Cafes in Breda"
type: section

The** Breda bars and cafes** are the perfect way to wrap up your day in Breda. Located in the southern side of Netherlands, Breda is packed with bars and other places that contribute to the city's thriving nightlife. Read on to find out about the best bars in town.

**Brooklyn**

Brooklyn is a famous bar in Breda. This bar is most visited by women. The reason behind this is the cocktails that are served here by the bartenders. Brooklyn is considered to be the most stylish bar located in the Haven market. This place has a unique feel to it. The ambiance is set by romantic lighting and the music at the bar is not overbearing. The cocktail list includes the cosmo, screwdriver, madras, apple martini and the likes. The food menu here boasts of several tempting and mouthwatering dishes. You cannot go wrong with the large plate of nachos in cheese and a drizzle of guacamole. The bar also has a variety of beer that is served at the right temperature.

**Dante**

For a cozy night-out, step in to Dante. Dante is cafe cum bar located in the market area in Grote. This bar is a hit amongst the young crowd, as unlike other bars in Breda, this one stays open way past 2:00 a.m. You are sure to order one drink after the other at this bar, as it has a great collection of alcohol. You can find everything from wines, champagnes, beer, whiskey, rum vodka and the likes here. The cafe styled menu has several finger foods to choose from. Chicken on sticks is the most ordered dish here.
