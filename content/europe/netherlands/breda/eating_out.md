title: "Eating Out in Breda"
type: section
---

**Breda restaurants** are a good reason for tourists to visit this historical city of Holland. There are a lot of restaurants in Breda, and they all serve quality and tasty food items. The food is cheap, as well, and the service is full of courtesy.

#### Tapas Bar Plan B

This restaurant is situated at Kasteelplein 2 Breda, and it offers a very relaxing and welcoming environment to tourists. Tapas Bar Plan B provides tasty and good quality food in a friendly and pleasant atmosphere. The main items are Spanish food and tapas. This restaurant also holds events and celebrations for the fun and joy of family and friends. Food prices depend on the dishes ordered, ranging from &euro;20 to &euro;30.

#### Restaurant Al Dente

This restaurant is situated at Nieuwe Ginnekenstraat 20, Breda and offers Italian dishes. This restaurant serves dinner and lunch most hours of the day and night, seven days a week. The price of food items varies from &euro;20 to &euro;40 Euros, depending upon the dish selection. The restaurant also provides a smoking zone, and there are various shopping centers around it.

#### Flinstering

Flinstering is situated at Grote Market 23, Breda. The dishes are mainly Mediterranean and are made with the freshest ingredients. The best vegetables of the season are used to cook some of the best dishes in Breda. This restaurant provides a relaxing, luxurious and soothing environment.

This is one of the Breda Restaurants that can be used to host wedding ceremonies; the prices of food range from &euro;35 to &euro;45, depending upon the selection of items.
