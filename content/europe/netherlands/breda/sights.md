### [Spanjaardsgat (Spaniards hole)](/europe/netherlands/breda/sights/spanjaardsgat_spa)

The famous Spanjaardsgat a waterport is named after the Spaniards occupation. The gate is famous because of the list with the peat boat (trojan horse like) which led to the siege of Breda. The Spanjaardsgat is part of Breda's castle, which nowadays houses the Royal Military Academy.

type: Hotspots



openingHours: Only during open castle day.
