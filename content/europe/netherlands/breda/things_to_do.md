### [Canoe and step trip](/europe/netherlands/breda/things_to_do/canoe_and_step_tri)

Discover the old city center by step and take a canoe trip through the newly build (old) harbour in the city center and around the singels (canals) that surround the city.

Available from March 1st and November 30th.
