The Beer Advertising Museum holds the largest collection of beer advertisements in the Netherlands -- hundreds of enamel signs, posters, and neon lights spanning 1900 to 1960, drawn from beer-producing countries across Europe. After browsing the collection, settle in at the museum's own pub, which serves 35 different beers. Open Sundays only, admission free. In summer there is an outdoor beer garden.
