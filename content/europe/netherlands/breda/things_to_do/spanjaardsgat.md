title: "Spanjaardsgat"
type: poi
category: Sight
latitude: 51.5900
longitude: 4.7730
---

This watergate at the rear of Breda Castle is where the famous peat barge slipped through in 1590 to recapture the city from the Spanish. The twin towers framing the gate make for one of Breda's most photographed spots, especially at night when they are lit up. Freely accessible at any time.
