An interesting day tour that takes you to the picturesque windmill town of [de Zaanse Schans](/europe/netherlands/dezaanseschans) which has a huge amount of traditional windmills and green painted houses and a stop to see the clog maker. On the way to the fishing villages of [Voledam](/europe/netherlands/volendam) and [Marken](/europe/netherlands/marken), a visit to a farmhouse where the cheese is made in the traditional way. An opportunity to buy some of the tasty cheese is available. Voledam is a beautiful fishing village where the main street is full of cafe's and restaurants. It is from here the ferry takes you to Markem a serene fishing village with typical wooden houses. The people sometimes dress in their colourful costumes but it is not guaranteed you will see this. The tickets include the boatride from Volendam to Marken and can be obtained at Dam Square.
