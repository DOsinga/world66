---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

If you do not have a car, the best way to get to The Hoge Veluwe is taking a train to station Ede Wageningen or Arnhem and rent a bike at the station. The bikes are not very expensive and buses are not very frequent and do not always drop you of exactly where you want. Within the park there are free bikes, you can use for the period of your visit.
