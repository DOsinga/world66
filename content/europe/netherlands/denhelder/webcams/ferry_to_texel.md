This cam is located on the roof of the Lands End Hotel in Den Helder on the mainland, showing actual weather conditions for the ferry crossing to Texel island.
Lands End hotel is situated less than 20 metres from the Texel Ferry terminal. *Guests have free access to internet.*
The streaming cam is online from 6 am to 9 pm. **
**+/- every XX:17 hrs. a ferry from Texel comes in.
+/- every XX:33 hrs. a ferry to Texel leaves the harbour.
By clicking the url link below the cam will be started from the Hotel page *(don't pay to much attention to the outmoded page design... the cam is ok!)*
*Internet Explorer ONLY !*
