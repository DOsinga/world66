An old village on the edge of the Drents-FrieseWoud It is situated
in Drenthe, a district in the north of the Netherlands. In the
village itself you'll find old farms and an old church. Just outside
the village is a "hunebed" a sort of dolmen.

In the woods nearby you
can bike or hike for hours.
