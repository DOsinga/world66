Diever doesn't really have nightlife, but every summer they perform
Shakespeare plays in the opne air theater in the woods (in Dutch).

### [Open air Shakespeare theater](/europe/netherlands/diever/nightlife_and_ente/open_air_shakespea)

On summerday evenings there are Shakespeare performances in the
open air since
1946. The actors are inhabitants of Diever.
