Edam is a cute little town juts north of Amsterdam. It has some very nice canals, and it is a nice place to rent a boat and go boating on the IJsselmeer.

Edam gave its name to the Edam cheese, the small ropund one with the red outside, but in town there is not much to remind you of this fact.


 Edam is a nice daytrip from Amsterdam, and can be combined with either [Volendam](/europe/netherlands/volendam), [Alkmaar](/europe/netherlands/alkmaar) or Hoorn.
