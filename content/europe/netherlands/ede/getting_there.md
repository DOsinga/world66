---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

Ede sits on the main Utrecht–Arnhem railway line, roughly halfway between the two cities. **Ede-Wageningen** is the larger station and the one you want for most intercity connections: frequent fast trains run from Utrecht (20 minutes), Arnhem (10 minutes), and — with one change — Amsterdam, Rotterdam and Schiphol Airport (around an hour). A smaller station, **Ede-Centrum**, is closer to the historic core. From Ede-Wageningen, bus 108 links the city with the village of Otterlo, where bus 106 continues into the Hoge Veluwe National Park to the Kröller-Müller Museum. By car, Ede is on the A12 motorway and is about 75 km from Amsterdam or 90 km from Rotterdam.
