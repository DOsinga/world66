Ede is located in the middle part of Holland between Utrecht and Arnhem. When traveling by plane you will probably arrive at Amsterdam Schiphol Airport. From here it doesn't take long to reach Ede (1 hr).

From Utrecht the train to Ede (destination Arnhem or Nijmegen) leaves every 15 minutes and will reach Ede-Wageningen Station in about 20 minutes.
From Arnhem the train to Ede leaves every 10 minutes and will reach Ede-Wageningen Station in about 10 minutes.
From the cities Amsterdam, Rotterdam, The Hague and Nijmegen the train to Ede leaves every 30 minutes and will reach Ede-Wageningen Station in about 30 minutes till 1 hour.

When you are travelling by bus or by train to Ede you are likely to arrive at Ede-Wageningen Station and from here on the city-centre is a 15 minutes walk; you could take a bus to the city centre (bus #3, #4, #5, #107 and #108S) or you could take the train (destination Amersfoort, the centre of Ede is the first stop).

The last option is that you come by car and the distances within Holland are never that long (a maximum of 3,5 hrs).
