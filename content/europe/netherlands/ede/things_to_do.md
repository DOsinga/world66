---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Almost everything worth seeing in Ede is actually in or around the **Hoge Veluwe National Park** on the east side of town — the landscape, the Kröller-Müller Museum, and the scattering of smaller villages, castles and museums that fringe the park. The town of Ede itself offers only a couple of modest sights: the old church in the centre and a small local history museum in the former Ede-Centrum railway station. Plan a full day for the park and the museum, and a half-day for anything else.
