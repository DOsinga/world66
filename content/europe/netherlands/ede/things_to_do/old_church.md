title: "Oude Kerk"
type: poi
category: "Sight"
address: "Grotestraat 58, 6711 AN Ede"
latitude: 52.046065
longitude: 5.671338
---

Ede's oldest surviving building — a modest late-Gothic parish church, the nave of which dates from around the 12th century and the squat tower from about 200 years later. It sits on a small square at the heart of what remains of the old village centre, now largely surrounded by 20th-century shopfronts.
