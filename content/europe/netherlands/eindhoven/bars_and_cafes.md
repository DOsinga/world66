---
title: "Bars and Cafes"
type: section
---

Eindhoven's nightlife is concentrated in two areas. Stratumseind is the longest pub street in the Netherlands, packed with bars and clubs on both sides. Stationsplein, near the central station, is more polished, with grand cafes and cocktail bars. In between, Wilhelminaplein and the Kleine Berg offer a mellower, more local scene with terrace cafes and the occasional live act.
