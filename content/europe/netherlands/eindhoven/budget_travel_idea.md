The city of Eindhoven, situated in the southern part of the Netherlands, has become one of the major tourist attractions among international travelers. Eindhoven’s rich nightlife, innumerable shopping centers, heritage monuments and year-round entertainment facilities can be enjoyed even without a lavish budget. The **Eindhoven budget travel** guide lists a few informative ideas to enjoy a fun-filled vacation in the city without burning a hole in your pocket.

**Budget Sightseeing and Activities**

Almost all the tourist attractions in Eindhoven can be done on a budget. The prime places of interest in the city of Eindhoven are the museums, parks and historical buildings. Van Abbemuseum is one of the most popular museums in the city. It boasts of various modern art exhibitions all year and houses several regional rare and cult artistic masterpieces. There is also a working mill named De Collse Water Mill that is situated on the outskirts of the city and perfectly reflects the modern persona of Eindhoven. There is the DAF museum which is of special interest to automobile lovers. It houses some of the oldest historical vehicles which beautifully represent the story behind the evolution and development of the automobile.

**Budget Shopping, Eating and Entertainment**

Shopping in the city of Eindhoven can be a pleasurable activity. Almost all the things that tourists wish to purchase are available at affordable prices here. Hauvel Galerieis is one such destination where a series of retail shops huddle together with their diverse wares. De Markt features several street-side shops and open-air markets, where tourists can pick up traditional items for good bargain prices.

The De Markt neighborhood also has many quaint cafes, bars and restaurants, where a person can enjoy a good meal on a budget. Visitors can also find several inexpensive cafes and restaurants on the street opposite Stationsplein, the central station. The Wilhelminaplein region also features some good mid-range eateries that serve appetizing local fare.

**Accommodations**

Eindhoven has a good offering of hotels. A few popular ones are the Holiday Inn Eindhoven, Park Plaza Mandarin Eindhoven and the Sofitel Cocagne Eindhoven, which are all excellent 4-star properties located in prime areas. During weekends they all offer rooms for as little as € 79,00. For budget accommodations, have a look at Smits Budget Hotel, located in the heart of Wilhermenialaan, also a popular place that offers clean rooms with a good view and a delicious breakfast. In addition to these, there are several economical lodges and hostels in an around the old town of Eindhoven.

Visitors don’t have to shell a large amount to get to know the historical city of Eindhoven. Its artistic legacy and heritage monuments along with charming street-side markets can be discovered and enjoyed without exorbitant costs.
