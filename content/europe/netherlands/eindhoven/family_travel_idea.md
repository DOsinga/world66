title: "Family Travel Ideas in Eindhoven"
type: section
---

Families spending their vacations in Eindhoven, Netherlands have plenty of destinations to choose from. From sports complexes to cultural centers, the city is abundant with attractions that lure parents and their kids to spend quality time together. The popular spots listed in the **Eindhoven family travel** guide are all worth a visit by families who are looking for great recreational options in the city.

**Genneper Park**

Lush greenery and several indoor and outdoor aquatic facilities make the Genneper Park an ideal place for family leisure activities. Located near the heart of the city, the park has a network of cycle trails and pathways where you can roam leisurely while enjoying nature's beauty.

The De Tongelreep Swimming Paradise lies at the heart of the Genneper Park. Considered one of the largest of its kind in Holland, the swimming complex is comprised of whirlpools, slides and steam and herbal baths, making it an ideal recreational site for kids seeking adventure as well as adults looking for some pampering and relaxation. Locals who treat their kids during the summer vacation find the De Tongelreep Swimming Paradise to be too good to be missed.

**Ice Sports Centre**

The skating rinks at the Ice Sports Centre are frequented by children, experienced skaters and professional athletes. One of the rinks is used to host tournaments, while the larger one is mostly reserved for experienced skaters. There is also a rink reserved especially for newbies or untrained skaters. Adult skaters usually flock the site every Friday night when there is a disco and some live music being played, while children frequent the place every Sunday morning along with their parents.

**Philips Stadium**

This majestic football stadium hosts the tournaments of the renowned Philips Sport Vereniging club. The 100-year-old sport facility can accommodate 35,000 spectators. The stadium is one of the premier tourist destinations in Eindhoven, that's why sports fans rush to buy tickets to secure coveted seats during major tournaments. Tourists can also avail of guided tours throughout the stadium, but this service needs to be booked ahead of their visit.

**Frits Philips Concert Hall (Muziekcentrum Frits Philips)**

This entertainment center regularly stages all kinds of concerts. The stadium consists of two halls: the Eindhoven concert hall, which has a capacity of 1,250 heads, and the Rabobank Hall, which can accommodate around 400 visitors. The music center, which is ranked among the best in Europe due to its superb acoustics, is not only popular among local music lovers but among the international audience as well. The concert hall has something to offer to almost all age groups, so parents and young ones will surely enjoy a performance. With its diverse musical offerings ranging from classical and orchestral music to rock and pop, Frits Philps attracts more than 150,000 visitors every year.

With its world-class entertainment centers and exciting recreational facilities, Eindhoven can guarantee you and your loved ones a memorable family vacation.
