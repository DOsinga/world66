A large green area on the southern edge of the centre with cycling and walking trails, a working watermill (De Collse Watermolen, made famous by Van Gogh), and the PreHistorisch Dorp open-air museum. The De Tongelreep swimming complex sits at the park's edge, with slides, wave pools, and outdoor sunbathing areas open in summer.
