---
title: "Shopping"
type: section
---

The main shopping streets radiate from the centre: Demer, Rechtestraat, Hermanus Boexstraat, and Vrijstraat. Two covered malls, the Heuvel Galerie and Piazza Centre, sit near the Markt. For something more interesting than chain stores, head to De Bergen, a neighbourhood of prewar houses converted into antique shops, galleries, and independent boutiques.
