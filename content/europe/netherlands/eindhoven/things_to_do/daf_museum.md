A shrine to the Dutch truck and car maker, with over 120 vehicles on display from the company's century-long history. Even if you are not a car enthusiast, the story of DAF's Variomatic transmission and its unlikely foray into passenger cars is genuinely interesting. Tongelresestraat 27.
