A cluster of WWII memorials in the city centre. The Bombardment Bas Relief remembers the 220 civilians killed in the German bombing of 19 September 1944. Nearby, the Liberation Monument honours the soldier, the resistance worker, and the civilian. The Liberation Bas Relief marks the meeting of American and British troops in Eindhoven. 18 Septemberplein.
