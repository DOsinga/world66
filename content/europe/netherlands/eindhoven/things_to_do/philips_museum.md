Housed in the original 1891 Philips light bulb factory, this museum traces the company's journey from incandescent lamps to electronics giant. Interactive exhibits cover everything from early radio technology to medical imaging. A must for anyone curious about how one company shaped an entire city. Emmasingel 31.
