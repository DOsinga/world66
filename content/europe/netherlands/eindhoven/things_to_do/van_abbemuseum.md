One of Europe's leading museums for modern and contemporary art, opened in 1936. The collection of over 2,700 works includes pieces by Picasso, Chagall, Kandinsky, El Lissitzky, Mondrian, and Appel. A striking new wing by Abel Cahen was added in 2003. Open Tuesday to Sunday, 11:00 to 17:00 (Thursday until 21:00). Bilderdijklaan 10.
