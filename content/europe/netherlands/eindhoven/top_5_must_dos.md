Eindhoven is the fifth largest city in the Netherlands. It is has a
bustling business center and an abundance of activities to enjoy.
Visitors to the city can explore the streets by bicycle. The bicycle
lanes make this method of transport convenient. There is a variety of
shopping and dining options ensuring that there is something for
everyone. Eindhoven is influential in the world of design and has
remarkable architectural creations. The city is rich with history and
culture as well as providing modern entertainment.
