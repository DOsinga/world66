Bolsward is one of the eleven cities of [Friesland](/europe/netherlands/friesland), a historic market town not far from the Afsluitdijk, the great dyke connecting Friesland with North Holland. It is one of the stops on the Elfstedentocht ice-skating route when the canals freeze hard enough.

The compact inner city has over 80 listed monuments. The ornate Renaissance city hall is the standout, but the Martinikerk (with a fine organ), the ruined Broerekerk, and the Oudheidskamer (a small antiquities museum) are all worth a look. Bolsward is a quiet town, easily visited as a side trip from [Leeuwarden](/europe/netherlands/friesland/leeuwarden) or Sneek.
