Heerenveen is the [Mecca](/asia/middleeast/saudiarabia/mecca) of speed skating. Although it is one of the bigger towns in Friesland, it is not a city, where [Sloten](/europe/netherlands/friesland/sloten) with only 500 inhabitants is.
