---
title: "Hindeloopen"
type: location
---

In steady decline since the 17-th century, Hindeloopen (Hylpen in Frisian) is a treaser trove on the IJsselmeer. Small cobble stone streets, old houses and the waterfront. One of the major sights is the Hidde Nijland museum housed in the fromer town hall, with a survey of 18 and 19-th century Hindeloopen culture.

Hindeloopen is also a good place to go for watersports on the IJsselmeer
