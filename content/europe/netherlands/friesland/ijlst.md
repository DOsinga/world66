Although IJlst is almost part of [Sneek](/europe/netherlands/friesland/sneek) it is a different city with it's onw city rights. The old center is cute, there is a wood sawing mill. IJlst was a center of the skating producing industry, but now it is mostly a touristic center for watersports.
