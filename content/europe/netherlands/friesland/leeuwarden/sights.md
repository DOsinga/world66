### [The Cambuurstadium](/europe/netherlands/friesland/leeuwarden/sights/the_cambuurstadium)

The 10.000-seater home ground of the footballclub SC Cambuur Leeuwarden. It's a typical English-looking stadium: four stands and in the middle of a neighbourhood.

SC Cambuur was formed on June 19 1964 and its colors are yellow and blue, the colors of the city Leeuwarden. The club comes out in the *Eerste Divisie*, the second division of The Netherlands. Even though Cambuur is mostly in the lowest places of the division, the club is one of the clubs that have the most supporters.

### [Oldehove](/europe/netherlands/friesland/leeuwarden/sights/oldehove)

The Oldehove is the old tower which stands crooked like the tower of Pisa. It's the second most leaning tower of the world.

It can be viewed inside and you can climb the stairs all the way to the top.
