Traveling to Giethoorn can be cost - effective by being monetarily savvy with a few **Giethoorn Budget Travel** ideas. A fulfilling vacation here can be inexpensive with a little preparation. Over the years Giethoorn has attracted a large numbers of tourists to the point it has been nicknamed "The Venice of the Netherlands." Transportation to the village can be inexpensive and staying there can leave some more cash for you to spend on having fun.

**Getting to Giethoorn**

There are very few options you have for arriving at this peculiar village. Many airlines offer off season discounts and this would be the best time to arrive here. Flying into Amsterdam will put you around two hours away from Giethoorn. The least expensive way to continue is to take a train from Amsterdam to Steenwik, which is a mere 15 minutes away. In Steenwik it would be best to take the bus. Taxis are available but there are very costly. Also, you could fly into several cities just a short distance away from Giethoorn like Ijumuiden or Rotterdam. From these cities you can rent a car at a reasonable price and you can explore the countryside on your two hour trip to Giethoorn.
**

Spending Cash in Giethoorn**

Accommodations in Giethoorn are cozy without burning up your budget. The bed and breakfasts here are the best way to go; they offer a warm environment and are somewhat cheaper than the hotels that are in Steenwik. Transportation here is very basic, in the village proper it is by canal boat. The only other way of getting around is a single bike path. Whether you travel by a ferry canal boat or a private punting craft, the cost is really cheap. Most of the attractions are going to be centered on cultural buildings, theme parks or just the special uniqueness of the village.
**

Outside of the Village**

For just a few Euros you can go to Meppel, a village that has a few reasonably priced night clubs and a market place where you can purchase items not available in Giethoorn. Steenwik is the largest city in proximity to Giethoorn and here you can find movie theaters and other more modern places which won’t break your bank either, allowing you to have the time of your life here in Giethoorn.
