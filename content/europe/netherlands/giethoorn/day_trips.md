type: section
---

**Giethoorn day trips** offer you the ideal way to explore
Netherlands and the village of Giethoorn in general as it is mostly
characterized by boat rides and cycling. This is due to the fact there
are no roads as there are rivers and lakes located everywhere.

Located deep in the heart of Netherlands is the village commonly
known as Giethoorn, located in the province of Overijssel, in an area
called Waterreijk. With an estimated population of about 2620 locals
residing in this peaceful place, it is the best getaway destination
for you and your family this holiday. Giethoorn is a unique place for
sightseeing with it being famous for its wooden bridge and marvelous
waterways.

**Family Farm**

Start off the Giethoorn day trip by visiting the family farm
histomobil which is located within Giethoorn. Enjoy a delicious
breakfast before beginning the day, with activities such as cycling,
boat trips, golf and canoeing all at your disposal.

Afterward, you can rent a boat and navigate your way through the
rivers as you get to see the numerous farm houses, which date back as
far as the eighteenth century.

**Bike Ride**

Apart from the boat tour, you and your family can spend quality time
and at the same time have fun with the cycle tour through village. There
are well mapped out paths that can be followed which are very safe for
any cyclist, young or old. There are several shops that you can rent
bikes from, at a small fee of course. You and your loved ones get to
enjoy yourself and at the same time it’s a nice way to exercise.

**Smits Paviljoen**

After the boat road, visit the Smits Paviljoen restaurant situated
on the outskirts of Giethoorn. The restaurant offers a wide range of
delicacies that make up the menu. With a seating capacity of about 300,
it’s ideal for hosting large groups of people.

**Restaurant’t Vonder **

Enjoy music while coming to terms with the beautiful scenery that is
offered by the water that is surrounding the restaurant. For newly
weds, it’s the ideal place to take that lunch or dinner as it not only
offers good food but also romance.

**Places to visit**

Get first hand information on the history of Giethoorn by visiting
the old earth museum. Usually it’s opened from 10am till 6pm, from
March to October.

Giethoorn day trips is a must for anyone looking forward to exploring this part of Netherlands.
