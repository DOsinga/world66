type: section
---

**Giethoorn restaurants**
offer visitors a chance to experience its unique cuisine. Being a popular
tourist destination in the Netherlands, this is one place where you can not
only enjoy the beauty of the place, but also the cuisine. Here are a few places
where you can relax and enjoy a delicious meal with your family while
holidaying in Giethoorn.

**Smit’s Pavilion**

This fine restaurant is located between the
Weerribben-Wieden National Park and Giethoorn. This is also one of the oldest
dining spots in town. If you are looking for a place to enjoy some fine
cuisine, this should be your first stop. You might want to try the traditional
buffet, because the spread is absolutely stunning. The restaurant serves breakfast,
lunch and dinner. The barbeque is something you must try. Enjoy the taste of
fresh meat, marinated with fresh herbs and cooked to perfection.

**Grand Café Fanfare **

Your visit to Giethoorn will be incomplete without visiting
this amazing restaurant. The restaurant is a great place to sit back and enjoy
the hospitality of Giethoorn and some fine cooking. There is a wide range from
which you can choose. Start you meal with a bowl of homemade fresh Creamy Mustard
Soup and move on to some Baked Duck or some Winterkost (stew). The restaurant
also has a menu just for kids.

**The Tulip Restaurant**

If you have never been to a farmhouse before, you will
surely enjoy your time at the Tulip Restaurant. This is an old farmhouse that
has been converted into a restaurant and offers its guests a breathtaking view
of the countryside. The atmosphere of the restaurant is perfect for a romantic
lunch. The menu includes some of the best dishes available in this side of the
country. Try the roasted lamb with mustard while you are at the restaurant.

**Café – Restaurant T’Vonder**

The T’Vonder Café – Restaurant is a nice place to enjoy a
quiet lunch while strolling in Giethoorn. If you have never tried apple pancake
and bacon, you can try that here at T’Vonder. The restaurant also has various
other dishes available. You can ask the chef for the special menu of day.

**Restaurant de Punter**

Punter’s is the place to sit back and enjoy a sumptuous dinner
in one of the most beautiful parts of Giethoorn. The cuisine here is highly
refined and every dish is cooked to perfection. You will be completely
satisfied dining at this fine restaurant. This is a family friendly restaurant
and is a great place to spend the evening with your family.
