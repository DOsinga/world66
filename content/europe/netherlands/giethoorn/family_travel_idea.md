If you want a unique vacation visiting the 'Venice of the North,'
check out the Netherlands' well kept secret of **Geithoorn. Family
travel **here is especially popular with many great accommodations and
activities for the entire family that will make you want extend your
trip to this picturesque location.

**Geithoorn Family Activities**

One of the top things to do in the city is explore the canals located
throughout the area. The easiest way to do this, as well as get
acquainted with the area, is to take a ride on a punter, the name for
the local boat that is similar to a gondola in Venice. This is a nice,
relaxing adventure that in most cases will provide you and your family
with some history and lore of the area via your knowledgeable guide.
Once you are escorted on a boat ride and get your bearings, try renting a
boat and navigating yourself.

Another great way to explore the city is on bike. With rentals available
in a few different locations, renting bikes for the whole family here
is easy. Pack a lunch (there is 1 small supermarket in town) and ride
out, following a map of the area, to the country side to see the
exquisite beauty of the surrounding untouched land. Stop in a quiet area
for a rest and lunch and enjoying the scenery of domestic animals
roaming free.

There are a couple of museums in the city that are not to be missed. The
Histo-Mobil offers a look at automobiles throughout time, as well as
the different ways that goods were transported into the city throughout
its history. The Museum 't Olde Maat Uus offers a look at living and
working in the city over the last 100 years. This volunteer-run farm is a
great look into the lives of ordinary citizens and the labor and issues
they dealt with and a nice adventure for young children.

**
Geithoorn Family Accommodations
**

Hotel De Jonge, Hotel de Harmonie and Hotel Prinsenije are all nice
hotels that work well for family travel. However, there are also many
great homes to rent if you will be staying in Geithoorn for a bit
longer or want a different experience than staying at a hotel. With numerous selections starting anywhere from $150 to $200 a night,
there are many options for larger families, including accommodations
sleeping up to 22 guests. By preparing a few meals at your rental, you and your family can
save big on food costs throughout your trip.

Geithoorn is a nice low key vacation getaway. If you and your family
like the relaxation and seeing the countryside, this is a perfect place
to enjoy several great days.
