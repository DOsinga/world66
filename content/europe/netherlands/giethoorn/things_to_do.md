---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Giethoorn's main activity is simply being here — drifting along the canals by boat, cycling through the peatlands, or wandering the old village on foot. Hire a whisper boat from one of the rental companies near the village entrance for a couple of hours on the water. The Museum 't Olde Maat Uus gives an honest picture of traditional life in the village, including a working farmstead run by volunteers (open April–October). Museum de Oude Aarde has fossils and gemstones for children and the geologically curious. Histo-Mobil is a small museum of vintage cars and motorcycles.
