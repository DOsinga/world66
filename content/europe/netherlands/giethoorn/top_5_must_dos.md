---
title: "Top 5 Must Do's in Giethoorn"
type: section
---

When you visit Netherlands, there will definitely be many **Giethoorn must do’s** on your list. But most of the time, because we do not have the luxury to take all the time we need in the world to get everything done in a couple of days stay, it is recommended that we only do the things that are worth doing in this beautiful place. Below are five of the most important and unique things that you can do in Giethorn, which would definitely make your stay worth every penny.

**Renting a House and a Bike to Explore the Whole of Giethoorn**

This is the first and most important thing you need to do when taking a vacation in Giethoorn – renting a house and a mode of transportation. And from there, you can play the rest of your day. By way of bicycle, you can stroll though the whole place and pass by little villages like the ones you see in the movies. You can find many signs and advertisements on the road which could guide you where to rent and how much the houses cost and also boat fares.

**Row the Boat**

Boats which are offered for rent in Giethoorn can be used for a couple of hours and for a maximum of one day. Go rowing and explore the river to see all the villages via water. You will also love passing under the beautiful canals in this place which are named “Little Venice." A word of advice: during weekends and holidays, renting a boat can be really hard. So, it is recommended that you book a boat ride as early as possible to avoid the long waiting.

**Get Some Boat Tour **

Most of the time, all you want in a vacation is to enjoy rather than get tired. This is also the reason why people want to commute via the public transport than to bring their own vehicles when it comes to vacations. If you have the same feeling over rowing the boat on your own and just want to enjoy the sights as a passenger, you can take the boat tours offered in Giethoorn. These boats will also take you to places that are very wonderful and new.

**Get Water Sport Fun in Geithoorn**

If you are a person who loves competition and you have that spirit of a sportsman wherever you go, you will love Geithoorn. They offer canoe renting, which will also take you to places and beautiful sights around the country.

**Visit the Venice of the North**

If you are feeling all romantic and want to relive Venice, you can do so here in Giethoorn. The lakes and rives that you pass by on boat have high rise bridges where you can pass underneath just like the the ones in the country of love.
