Gouda is 25 kilomter northeast of Rotterdam. The town is mostly famous because it has given its name to the best known kind of chees from the Netherlands, but the town itself is definately worrth a visit. It has a lot of charm and a lot of old buildings.


 The best places to visit are without a doubt the Markt (cheese market on thursdays, which you can miss) with right on the middle the Gothic city hall, of which you can find a copy in Japan in Holland village. Also on the markt is the Waag. The St. Janskerk is the big church and famous for its galss stained windows.
