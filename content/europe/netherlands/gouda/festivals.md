**Festivals**

**Gouda Pottery Festival**

A very nice event, the festival is the occasion for ceramic makers from all over Europe and further to meet and compete in a good ambiance. If the weather is good, it is certainly a must see, as the market place is covered with stands full of colorfull ceramic creations of all sizes and styles.
