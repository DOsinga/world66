---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

Gouda is well connected by rail. Direct trains run from [Rotterdam](/europe/netherlands/rotterdam) (25 minutes), Utrecht (30 minutes), and The Hague (30 minutes). From Amsterdam, take a train to Utrecht and change, or take a direct Intercity to Gouda (about 50 minutes). The station is a five-minute walk from the Markt.

By car, Gouda sits on the A12 (The Hague–Utrecht) and the N207. Parking is available on the outskirts of the centre; follow signs for the P+R facilities.
