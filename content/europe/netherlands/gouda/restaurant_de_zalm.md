A traditional Dutch eetcafé on the Markt with a straightforward menu — Dutch staples like stamppot, soups, and fried fish alongside a good selection of Dutch beers. The terrace overlooks the cheese market action on Thursday mornings. Prices are fair and the atmosphere is relaxed. No surprises, but exactly what you want after a morning in the open air.
