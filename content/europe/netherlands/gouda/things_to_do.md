---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Gouda's old centre is compact and walkable. The Markt is the starting point — the Gothic town hall, the Waag, and the Sint Janskerk are all within a few minutes of each other. Allow half a day to see the highlights; a full day if you want to explore the Gouda Museum and the quieter streets near the Hollandsche IJssel.
