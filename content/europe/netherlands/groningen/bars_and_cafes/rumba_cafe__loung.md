Latin vibes here! In my opinion the coolest place in Groningen. The place has two stories and has just been expanded. Downstairs you'll find the dancefloor and huge cocktail bar, upstairs there's the lounge & V.I.P. room, champagne, fine cocktails & cigar room. In the summer they have a super terrace at the back with lounge chairs & hammocks! Great interior & furniture. Great ambiance, music and cool people. Tight security, dress well to enter in weekends. Must visit !!
