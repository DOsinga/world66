This review on **Groningen restaurants** gives the best tips on Groningen cuisine. Groningen, the capital of Groningen province, has many beautiful tourist spots and is considered one of the most attractive destinations in the Netherlands. Along with the picturesque landscape, tourists are attracted by the local delicacies of Groningen, and its restaurants play the perfect host to satisfy the culinary buds of its tourists.

Some popular restaurants in Groningen are described as follow.

**Muller**

Undoubtedly one of the finest restaurants in Groningen, Muller offers an exquisite dining experience amid classic French surroundings. From pumpkin soup to cr&egrave;me sauce, there are many cuisine options available here. Don&rsquo;t miss out on the filet of lamb en croute, a specialty of Muller's.

**SchimmelpennickHuys Hotel**

This hotel-cum-restaurant is located in the heart of the city. This elegant and charming Hotel has three popular restaurants, namely Parelvisser, a fish restaurant, Femme Grecque, a Greek restaurant, and the Classique restaurant. The room tariff might be a bit higher than average, but the nice ambiance and hospitality of the staff is unparalleled. Also, the food quality is very good.

**Thai Jasmine**

If you love Thai cuisine, then this restaurant is a must when you visit Groningen. The food here is awesome and tastes like real Thai. Try out the red curry and hot salads.

**De Apedans restaurant **

The striking decorations and centuries-old architectural design of this restaurant grab the attention of every first-time visitors. Reasonable prices, food quality, along with the hospitality of the staff are all aspects that score positive points for this restaurant.
