' De Eerste Kamer' is a great place to eat if you don't have that much money, but also if you don't feel like choosing between 40 different meals from a menu.
At 'De Eerste Kamer' you only have 10 choices. 8 of them are diffrent kinds of meat, 1 is a soup and the last one is a vegetarien one. All of the above come with fright potato's and a rich salade.
Besides that the food is easy and cheap, its really good and the restaurant's mood is great. So if you're gonna eat there, you better be on time, because every night its packed. But it's worth it!
