Hotel Herberg Het Witte Paard is situated in Oostwold, a 10 minute drive southwest of the city of Groningen. Het Witte Paard offers comfortable and spacious hotelrooms, a hotelbar and lounge and an excellent restaurant. In summer one can dine outside with a beautiful view over the meadows with only a couple of church towers on the horizon.
