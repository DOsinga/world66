**Netherland Travel Planning: Groningen Family Travel Ideas **

Insightful **Groningen Family Travel** ideas can help travelers to plan a family-oriented vacation in the Netherlands. Groningen is among the most progressive urban centers in Netherlands. It is the state capital of the Groningen province. Some of the most recommended Groningen family travel ideas include:

**Groningen Canal Tours: Affordable, Enjoyable Family Tours**

Family travelers should try to plan their vacation in Groningen between June and September. This is the best season to embark upon extensive canal tours of the city and the entire province of Groningen. Other seasons can be quite cold and the canals are usually frozen. Rondvaartboten, or tour boats, are easily available at discounted family package prices. A family can rent a tour boat on an hourly basis or for half a day. These small boat rides take passengers across the extensive canal system of Groningen. This is the quickest yet the most comprehensive and discounted way of exploring the scenic countryside of Netherlands. Most of the canal tours begin from the main dock of Groningen, located close to the main railway station, near the Groninger Museum.

**Exploring Groningen Lakes: Entertaining Family Activities **

Another recommended family getaway is visiting Paterswoldsemeer, a vast lake situated on the outskirts of southern border of Groningen. This is a popular spot for watersports like sailing. There are small camping sites along the shore that are perfect for small families.

Another lake recommended for family visits is the Zuidlaardermeer, located about 8 km from the southeast border of Groningen. This lake has turned into a popular watersports destination and canoeing is the local favorite. Near Zuidlaardermeer Lake is the village of Zuidlaren. Visitors can easily reach this village afoot to explore the rural side of Dutch living and explore regional activities like seasonal horse fairs.
