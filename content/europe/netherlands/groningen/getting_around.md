type: section
---

Here are a few **Groningen transportation **tips you can use while visiting this place. One of the things you need not worry about while visiting Groningen is local transportation. The city has a well organized public transport system and also has adequate connections to other parts of the country. Some of the means of transportation you can use while in Groningen are the trains, buses, cars and even a bicycle.

**Train Services**

The city has two major train stations, the Northern Station and the Central Station. Most of the trains leave from the Central Station; you can also take a train to the Schiphol Airport from the Groningen train stations. If you want to travel to other parts of the country, you can take a train that passes through one of these two main stations. Take a train, and you will be able to reach Utrecht in less than two hours.

**Bus Services**

The bus service is quite good and frequent in Groningen. You can take a bus to reach center of the city from almost any area. The cost of the bus ticket is very reasonable, and this is one of the most preferred ways of getting around in Groningen.

**Cars and Bikes
**

You can also rent a car to be able to freely move around the place. If you like to explore the place on your own, a car would probably be best for you. Another great way to get around Groningen is to rent a bicycle and get around. This will also give you a chance to explore the place on your own pace.
