There is some **Groningen practical information** that you need to know in order to survive this little university city in Netherlands. This city was founded in A.D. 1100 and is famous for its universities that have medieval architecture. Here is some of the practical information that you need to know about Groningen to make your stay worthwhile.

**University City **

It is interesting to note that Groningen is a small city in area with a very small population consisting of about 185,000 inhabitants, it has about 50,000 students that are now residing in the area. These students come from all over the world that is why when you are touring the city, you will notice that many people around you are students taking a tour of the city.
