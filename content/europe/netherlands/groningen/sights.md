These are the main sights 

**Sights**

** Martini Toren**

The main sight is without a doubt the Martini Toren and the Church that belongs to it. They date back to the 11-th century, although the present construction is mostly form the 16-th. Open from noon-5pm from Tuesday to Saturday. The Martini Toren is located on the Grote Markt. 

** Grote Markt**

The old town square is not as pretty as it used to be. The reconstruction after WW II was not a big success.
