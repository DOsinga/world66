---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Groningen is a compact walk. The **Grote Markt** at the heart of town anchors the centre, with the 97-metre **Martini Tower** rising over it — climb it on a clear day. The **Groninger Museum** on its own little island opposite the central train station is the one essential visit: a wild deconstructivist building by Alessandro Mendini and Coop Himmelb(l)au, housing a strong collection that runs from 17th-century Dutch Masters to contemporary art. For anyone with an interest in maritime history, the **Noordelijk Scheepvaartmuseum** is worth an hour.
