---
title: "Beaches in Haarlem"
type: section
---

**Haarlem beaches** are frequented by tourists every year. Haarlem is situated 20 km from Amsterdam and the city is easy to reach by train, road or air. Haarlem is quieter
than Amsterdam and there are many tourist attractions to explore. The
area has a rich history and vibrant culture. Historical attractions can
be found around the city and there is also a colorful market that is
worth a visit. To take a break from sightseeing the nearby beaches are a good place to spend the day. Beaches offer fun for the whole family.

**Zandvoort Beach**

Zandvoort Beach is situated to the west of Haarlem. The sand stretches for 9 km. Zandvoort
is a popular family beach and there are many resorts in the area. The
beach is easy to access and can be reached by train, car or bus. There
are parking facilities but the beach is often crowded and it may be
difficult to find parking during peak times. An alternative is to ride
your bicycle to the beach. This will save you the hassle of finding
parking and it is also a good way to enjoy the surrounding scenery. The
ocean is part of the North Sea and it can be cold for swimming; however, the
water warms up slightly during summer months. Located along the beach
are pavilions as well as an abundance of tourist facilities such as restaurants and sunbeds. The restaurants
sell delicious local cuisine. There are many bars to enjoy a refreshing
drink after a day in the sun. Vendors sell ice cream in an assortment
of flavors. Nearby attractions include a casino. A promenade provides
a lovely area to go for a stroll and explore your surroundings.
Beautiful sunsets can be enjoyed from this beach.

**Bloemendaal Beach**

Bloemendaal Beach can be reached by bus or car. It is located next to Zandvoort Beach and it is approximately 10 minutes from Haarlem.
The sandy beach stretches for miles. It offers a respite from the
hustle and bustle of city life.This beach is popular among locals and
tourists. The beach is suitable for activities such as kite surfing. Bloemendaal
is well known for its parties and vibrant atmosphere. There are many
beach parties organized in the area, drawing crowds of party lovers.
Beach clubs organize activities and sports. At the end of the beach is
a national park with beautiful scenery along the sand dunes. There are
hiking and cycling paths through the park and tours are offered. The
entrance is 15 minutes by foot from the beach. Other sites in the
vicinity of the beach include gardens, golf courses and statues.
