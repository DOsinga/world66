---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

Getting to Haarlem from [Amsterdam](/europe/netherlands/amsterdam) Centraal takes about 20 minutes by train.
From Schiphol it is more convenient to go by bus. Line no 300, a red bus called Zuidtangent, runs frequently between Amsterdam and Haarlem and passes Schiphol Plaza just outside the arrivals hall. The travel takes about half an hour and ends at Haarlem railway station.
