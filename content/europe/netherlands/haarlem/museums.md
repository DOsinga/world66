Haarlem does maybe not have as many important museums as [Amsterdam](/europe/netherlands/amsterdam), but it does have a fair share.

**Museums**

**Teyler Museum**

The Teyler museum is one of the oldest museums in the Netherlands and started out as collection of Pieter Teyler a rich trader in Silk. He was very much interested in Art and Sciences and collected to give average people a chance to marvel at the miracles of modern science. **Frans Hals Museum**

Frans Hals museum has a very good collection of paintings from the 17-th century. Haarlem born painter Frans Hals has a prominent place (and deserves it).
