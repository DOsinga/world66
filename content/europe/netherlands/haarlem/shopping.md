---
title: "Shopping in Haarlem"
type: section
---

One of the most interesting things to do in **Haarlem **is
**shopping**. There are several places where you can get some good
bargains. Be it brand name items or locally produced items, you will be
able to find all that you need here in Haarlem.

**Open Market**

The weekend open market in Haarlem houses the finest and greatest
products in Netherlands. The market is a great place to bargain for
things like clothes, fashion accessories and many other things. The
market also has several fruits and vegetable vendors; you might want to
stock up on some fruit while you tour Haarlem. This weekend open market
takes place along the street of the City Hall’s front.

**Grote Houtstraa**

Grote Houtstraa is one of the main shopping areas in Haarlem. It
covers the heart of the city through the mains square of Houtplien. If
you are looking to pick up some brand item clothes, this the right place to
shop. There are also individual clothing, jewelry, sport stores,
restaurants and much more in this area. Grote Houtstraa operates
everyday from Monday to Saturday 9:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m.

**Botermarket**

Botermarket is situated in the small square in downtown Haarlem.
There is a fountain right in the middle of Botermarket that serves as
its landmark. This market is open from Monday to Friday from 9:00 a.m.
to 4:00 p.m. Botermarket is a great place to buy some souvenirs to take
home with you. The market is also a place where you can buy different
dry foodstuffs. There are also some international chain stores in
Botermarket like Ben and Jerry’s, H&M and C&A.

**Shoppingmall Schalkwijk**

Schalkwijk is another one of the popular shopping destinations in
Haarlem. Schalkwijk is an indoor shopping mall located in the southeast part
of the town. Schalkwijk houses lots of local and international shops
that offer different merchandise like ready-made clothing, foods, shoes,
books and more. Just like any other store, Schalkwijk shopping mall is
open from 9:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m.

**Vroom & Dreesman (V&D) Department Store**

This is a popular Dutch chain department store that has 62 branches
all over Netherlands. Vroom & Dreesman (V&D) Department Store
offers everything from books, jewelry, clothes, CDs, shoes and electronic
gadgets to home furnishings. If you are looking for something
in particular, chances are you will find it at Vroom & Dreesman’s.
The department store also houses the popular “La Place” multilevel food
court, so you can enjoy some Dutch delicacies while shopping.
