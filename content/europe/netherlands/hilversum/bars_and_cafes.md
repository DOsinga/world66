**Relax at a Cafe in Hilversum**

**Hilversum Bars and Cafes **are the best way to experience the Dutch hospitality. Known as the media city of Netherlands, Hilversum is located 30 km from Amsterdam. Shopping centers, sporting houses and monuments take center stage at this tourist destination. Read on to find out about a cafe that has won a lot of hearts in Hilversum.

**The Guardian**

This is a cafe-cum-bar that should not be missed. It is located in the shopping district of Groest in Hilversum. This cafe offers guests an opportunity to take a break from the shopping spree and enjoy a relaxing meal. The indoor seating is perfect and the comfortable atmosphere helps relieve stress. The cafe also has a street-side seating, which provides a nice backdrop as you enjoy your cup of coffee or sip on your glass of wine. The heaters in the patio make it possible to enjoy the outdoor seating even during winters. People from all age groups visit this cafe.
**
Food and Entertainment**

This cafe serves savories like fish and chips, chocolate muffins, burgers and sandwiches. The wine list boasts of fine wines from around the world. You can also enjoy the evening breeze with a cup of hot chocolate. The dessert here is to die for. Apple pie, chocolate mousse, cheese cake and ice creams grace the menu here. The cafe opens at 12:00 p.m. and stays open till way past midnight. After sundown the DJ plays some great music.
