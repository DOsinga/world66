---
title: "Bars and Cafes"
type: section
---

The Groest is the main strip for cafes and bars, with outdoor seating in good weather. The Guardian cafe-bar on the Groest has a relaxed atmosphere and stays open late with DJ sets in the evenings. Felix II is quieter, with rock and blues music. Le Journal and Musk cater to a younger crowd in the evenings. Most bars close around midnight on weekdays; later on weekends.
