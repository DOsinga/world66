**Café Brasserie De Jonge Haan**
A typical brown café with nice bar and excellent restaurant with non-smoking section. Nice art in the main restarant. Great atmosphere. Delicious food and wines. Good value for money. Free parking and only two steps away from Hilversum city centre.
Open: workdays from 9.00 to 24.00 and Saturday and Sunday from 12.00 to 24.00.

Address: 's Gravelandseweg 62,
1217 ET Hilversum ,
tel. +31 35 6245314
