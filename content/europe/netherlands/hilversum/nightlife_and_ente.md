---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment in Hilversum"
type: section
---

**Hilversum Nightlife** consists of many different activities. Like many other European cities, bars and clubs play a big role in the nightlife scene. According to the laws you can legally enter a bar at the age of 16, but that doesn&rsquo;t mean all bars allow people that young to enter a bar. Many of the bars in town do not let people under the age of 23 enter a bar, let alone people who are 21 or 18.

**Musk**

If you want to go to a bar that caters to Dutch celebrities, Musk is the place to be if you are 21 or older. However, it is not always easy to get into Musk so you want to be prepared ahead of time. To help improve your chances of entering the bar you will want to bring along some girls, and dress as neatly as possible. The best night to go is Thursdays because no entry fee is charged and it is by far easier to get in.

**Le Journal**

If you are between the ages of 18 and 25, Le Journal is the place to be after 10:30 p.m. The bar has two different floors that are used each night. The ground floor is a great place to sit back and enjoy a cheap beer during the week, but on the weekends the ground floor is just as loud as the first floor. Thursday and Fridays are two of the most popular nights to visit due to the "10 beers for 10 euros" deal.

**Felix II**

If you are interested in a bar that provides you with a quieter atmosphere but one that is still entertaining, stop in at Felix II. The main choice in music at the bar is rock and blues, so it generally attracts an older crowd. Occasionally you can even find live rock or blues bands playing.

**Cartouche**

If you are looking for a quieter place that serves dinner in addition to drinks, Cartouche is the place to go. The atmosphere is very relaxed; you will even find music quietly playing in the background. However, the music is not what you would expect to find inside a bar, it is more along the lines of folk music.

**G-Spot**

If dancing the night away is more to your liking, you will want to stop on by the G-Spot, which is open every night at 12:00 a.m. This is one of the most popular clubs in town because there is no entry fee, and its location makes it hard to miss. Inside you will find a variety of music styles, but the biggest focus is on pop and dance music.
