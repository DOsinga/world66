**Hilversum Sights** are not something which you will easily forget. Most of these sights are visible as you travel through the city. Sometimes it looks as if you are actually in an open gallery. Throughout Hilversum, there are truly amazing architectural landscapes and sculptures.

#### Architecture

W.M. Dudok, in essence, is responsible for creating modern-day Hilversum, designing around 75 buildings in Hilversum. There is a very unique style to Dudok&rsquo;s architecture. For those more knowledgeable of his architectural style, it was influenced by what is called &ldquo;Nieuwe Bouwen,&rdquo; meaning New Building, in English. This was a radical style from the first half of the 20th century in this part of the Europe. The Town Hall is Dudek&rsquo;s masterpiece and is built entirely of brick, set in a garden as if to announce the nature of Hilversum as a garden city. The harmonious architecture of this building has been commented on by many architects and must be seen to be appreciated.

#### Churches

- **Church of our Sweet Lady: **Some architectural marvels are also found in the churches of the city. The Church of our Sweet Lady is one such church. It was designed by Walter Ter Riele and was completed in 1910. It has a distinctive neo-Gothic style with stained glass windows that are absolutely breathtaking. The layout is also quite extraordinary.
