---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Beeld en Geluid (Museum for Sound and Vision) on the Mediapark is the main cultural attraction — an archive and museum of Dutch broadcasting history with an extraordinary coloured-glass facade. Dudok's Town Hall on Dudokpark is an architectural landmark worth seeing from the outside. The Goois Natuurreservaat offers walking and cycling on heathland and through forest; the southern reserve has Bronze Age burial mounds at Bussumerheide. Anna's Hoeve nature area has lakes and hills created in the 1930s as a public works project. The town centre shopping streets, especially the Groest, are good for independent shops and have outdoor cafe seating in good weather.
