title: "Travel Guide in Hilversum"
type: section
---

Using this **Hilversum Travel Guide** will allow you to plan every aspect of your vacation so that you don&rsquo;t miss anything. Hilversum is located between Amsterdam and Utrecht and is a great place to go when you want to get away from what the bigger cities have to offer. Hilversum is a great place for people who love exploring the outdoors because of the forest and lakes that surround the city.

**Getting Around**

Once you have reached the town the best way to get around town is on foot or on bike. Walking to where you need to go is easy because everything is located in the center of the city. Bikes can be used anywhere in the city and in the surrounding forests. There are three places in town where you can rent a bike for around $3.00 a day.

**Sights**

If you are interested in looking at the architecture of various old buildings Hilversum has a lot of different things to offer. One place that is something that shouldn&rsquo;t be missed is the Town Hall Hilversum, which was built in 1928. Town Hall Hilversum is known for its unique shape, but also its yellow bricks that were specially designed for the building. A guided tour is given every Sunday at 2 p.m. for about $9.00. Other sights around town include Gooiland, Kiosk, and the former AVRO Studios.

**Recreation**

Hiking and biking are the most popular recreational activities the town has to offer. Many people have a hard time seeing where the city ends and the forests begin because of how everything flows into each other. Anna&rsquo;s Hoeve is a great place to go to enjoy a day of hiking and bike riding, but be prepared for the hilly areas. However, if you want to stick closer to town you can go on over to Corversbos.

**Eating Out**

There are plenty of restaurants available to choose from in Hilversum. If you are looking for a cheap place to eat after the bar closes you should check out Apolonia, which on Fridays and Saturdays stays open until at least 3 a.m. The shawarma with pita bread and the doner kebabs are highly recommended. Da Familia or Lust are two restaurants well known for their international cuisine.

**Nightlife**

On Fridays and Saturdays most bars and clubs in the city of Hilversum are open until 3 a.m. During the week the same bars are open until 1 a.m. with a few exceptions. De Baron is a great bar to check out if you enjoy playing pool and darts while enjoying a beer. Club Gooiland is only open on Friday and Saturday nights and is a great place to go.
