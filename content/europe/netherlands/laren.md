Laren is a small, pretty town about halfway between [Amersfoort](/europe/netherlands/amersfoort) and [Amsterdam](/europe/netherlands/amsterdam). The main attraction to come here is the excellent Singer museum which focuses on sculpture. In the recent past they have done very good expositions on Camille Claudel and Auguste Rodin. The garden of the museum is a haven of piece and is a great place to have a tea.


 Accross the street from the museum there are a few pretty good restaurants.
