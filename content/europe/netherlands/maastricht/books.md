These are some books that might be able to aid you if you are traveling to Maastricht.

### [The Netherlands](/europe/netherlands/maastricht/books/the_netherlands)

The
Netherlands Eyewitness Travel Guide is the best companion for a
person planning to visit the Dutch country. The popular tourist
locations are well covered in the book and the high quality
photographic images enable the user to get an idea about the locations
to visit.

The book contains 480 pages and the latest edition is in
paperback, published by DK Publishing. The dimensions of book
are compact enough and this travel guide can come along with you while
moving around in the country.

The book contains detailed descriptions of
the museums and famous wind mills [more..](/europe/netherlands/maastricht/books/the_netherlands)
