title: "Eating Out"
type: section
---

Maastricht has long been considered one of the best cities in the Netherlands for food, with a restaurant culture closer to neighbouring Belgium and France than to Amsterdam. The Vrijthof square and the streets around it are full of options, from casual brasseries to serious French-influenced kitchens.

**Le Bon Vivant** — Capucijnenstraat 91. A neighbourhood favourite for French-influenced Dutch cooking. Tel: 043-321 0816.

**Restaurant Intermezzo** — Tongersestraat 5. A reliable choice in the heart of the old city. Tel: 043 325 78 66.
