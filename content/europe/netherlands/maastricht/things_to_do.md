title: "Things to do in Maastricht"
type: section
---

Maastricht is a city that is situated on both sides of the Meuse
River in the southern part of the Dutch province of Limburg. Maastricht
is popular especially for the shopping malls and recreational
centers; it is also known as the center of tradition, culture and
history. With its rich culture and history there are several
interesting things that you can do in Maastricht--such as visiting the
museum and taking a boat ride or an adventure trip. Here are some tips
on what you can do in Maastricht.

**St. Pietersberg Caves**

St. Pietersberg Caves are located at St. Pieter’s Fort in Maastricht and are easily
accessible. A short boat ride to the Fort you will drop you off at the
town, from which the caves are a short walk away. The boat ride costs
only about $10 and includes the $4 fee for entering the caves. There
is a guide who will be present to guide you around.

**Bonnefanten Museum**

The Bonnefanten Museum is an art
museum located at Avenue Ceramique, Maastricht. The museum houses
sculptures from the Maasland dating back from A.D. 1300 to A.D. 1550,
including several paintings from South Holland dating back from 1500 to
1750. The museum is a great place to visit.
