---
title: "Things to do in Maastricht"
type: section
---

Maastricht is a city that is situated on both sides of the Meuse
River in the southern part of the Dutch province of Limburg. Maastricht
is popular especially for the shopping malls and recreational
centers; it is also known as the center of tradition, culture and
history. With its rich culture and history there are several
interesting things that you can do in Maastricht--such as visiting the
museum and taking a boat ride or an adventure trip. Here are some tips
on what you can do in Maastricht.

**St. Pietersberg Caves**

St. Pietersberg Caves are located at St. Pieter’s Fort in Maastricht and are easily
accessible. A short boat ride to the Fort you will drop you off at the
town, from which the caves are a short walk away. The boat ride costs
only about $10 and includes the $4 fee for entering the caves. There
is a guide who will be present to guide you around.

**Bonnefanten Museum**

The Bonnefanten Museum is an art
museum located at Avenue Ceramique, Maastricht. The museum houses
sculptures from the Maasland dating back from A.D. 1300 to A.D. 1550,
including several paintings from South Holland dating back from 1500 to
1750. The museum is a great place to visit.

**Maastricht Caves**

The Maastricht Caves are a subterranean network of tunnels and
caves, and the sculptures at the caves are some of the oldest in this area
and date back to ancient Roman times. You can visit these extraordinary
caves on 1 of the tours available. There is a guide to take you
through and give you a brief history of the caves. Remember to take a flashlight and camera along with you.

**D’Artagnan Adventure Trip**

The D'Artagnan Adventure Trip is an adventure site in the
underground fortifications of Maastricht, commonly known as the
Casemates. The object of the trip is to find your way through the
fortifications of the 16th century. Only adults over the age
of 20 are permitted to take part in this adventure trip. If you decide to take the trip, remember to
wear comfortable clothing and to carry an electric flashlight. The adventure
trip is a great way to enjoy your day at the underground fortification.
You can get more information from the Maastricht Tourist Office.

**Boat Trips on the Maas**
A perfect way to enjoy the afternoon is to take a relaxing boat trip
on the Maas. The boat trips are available during the summer months,
from Easter to the middle of September. While on a boat trip you can
also visit the Zonneberg grottoes during the 3-hour trip. You can also take a trip to Liège (Belgium), a trip around
the city of Maastricht or enjoy a romantic night cruise at on a boat serving a candlelight buffet.
