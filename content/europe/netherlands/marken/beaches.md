#### The Largest Amount of Fun at the Largest Lake

The largest lake in the Netherlands is 1,100 square kilometers and completely surrounds the little island of Marken, offering plenty of great **Marken beaches**. The lake, known as the IJsselmeer, is popular for water sports and other activities in the still waters. If you want to go to one of the many beaches along the island coastline, you will also find plenty to do to keep the family occupied.

#### What to Look Forward to at the Marken Beaches

There are many different ways one can explore the island beaches. Whether you travel by car, boat, bicycle, or even take a nice long walk, an outing either along or on the border lakes of the island is an absolute must. You can relax on the island&rsquo;s beaches for the day, or you can spend the night on the inhabited island. If you travel there by boat, you can moor for the night and enjoy the natural, untouched surroundings while you rest peacefully. The beaches along the island are ideal for walking, relaxing and tanning.

#### Watersports and Other Activities at the Marken Beaches

The beaches around Marken offer visitors a variety of activities to keep themselves busy if tanning is not enough. You could go surfing, diving, sailing, water-skiing, fishing or swimming. If sailing is your thing, hire a fully furnished and equipped yacht to take a tranquil cruise around the shore lines and anchor for the night in the calm, rippling waters of the lake.

You can also enjoy some beach games or sports on the beaches like football, cricket or volleyball. One of the most popular games played on the Marken beaches is Picigin. Other than water polo, this game is practiced and played by the locals during the finer summer days. The object of the game is to keep the ball from falling to the ground, which can prove to be much harder than one would think, as the players try to challenge each other.

The options for fun are endless when visiting Marken Island, as you and your entire family will always have something to do on the lovely Marken beaches.
