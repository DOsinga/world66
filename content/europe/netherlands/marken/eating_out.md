**Marken Restaurants** have a very European flavor to them. Tourists can enjoy different cuisines such as French and Italian.

**De Taanderji**

This place is well known for its Dutch and French cuisine and is also popular for the Belgium food. The working hours of the restaurant differs based on the different months.

During the April to September, the restaurant is open from 11a.m. to 10p.m. where during October to March they close as early as 7p.m. Mondays are holidays. The cost of the main course ranges between $24 and $30, whereas for snacks it can cost up to $6.40 to $21.

The address of this place is Havenbuurt 1, Marken 1156 AL, The Netherlands.

**Oud Marken**

This is one of the Marken restaurants that is famous for the exquisite Dutch cuisine and is called with several names. Lunch Room Oud Marken, Old Marche Caf&eacute; Restaurant and Old Marken are few other ways of identifying this restaurant.

The prices range from $10.00 to $30.00 and most of the travelers visit this place for the Dutch delicacies. The restaurant is a part of water/land community which means that both the sea as well as land foods are served here.

The address of this place is Buurterstraat 19, Marken 1156 AP, The Netherlands.

**Aan de Haven**

Specializing in barbecued cuisine and grilled meat and also French cuisine, this restaurant is located on the water front. With the amazing view of the sea, the customers enjoy the delicacies by listening to smooth music. Dutch and Belgium cuisine can also be found here and are most preferred. This is one of the best Marken restaurants.

The address of this place is Kets 18, Marken 1156 AT, The Netherlands.
