---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment in Marken"
type: section
---

On the tiny island of Marken in the Netherlands, one can find a decent **Marken Nightlife** scenario. Marken is mainly a simple fishing village, but today, the island earns most of its income via tourism. You will find a good number of very pricey tourist shops as well as a few places of interest, mostly in hotels to visit at night. Here are the top places to visit if you wish to enjoy a night out in Marken.

**The HEM Hotel 't Tolhuus**

The HEM Hotel 't Tolhus in Marken is located in a very beautiful area. Besides offering a great place to stay, this hotel also houses a very nice bar. Seated in its cozy bar, you can enjoy well made drinks along with delicious small dishes.

**De Taanderij**

De Taanderij is a little restaurant and bar that is located at Havenbuurt in Marken. This is primarily a restaurant that is popular with the locals as well as the town's visiting tourists. However, many people come here in the evening to enjoy the warm and cozy ambience of the restaurant's bar. Must-tries at the restaurant are the cocktails and seafood starters. One can also enjoy drinks on the restaurant's terrace whenever the weather is pleasant.

**De Waag**

De Waag is a little French cafe that lies on Rode Steen in Marken in the beautiful Weigh House. Visitors should not miss the cafe's amazing starters along with its well-made drinks. In the night, you can be sure of enjoying a good amount of drinks in the company of new friends, expats and locals, all of whom will ensure you have a great evening. The bill for two drinks along with a starter sums up to $25.

**Bitterzoet**

Bitter zoet is a bar and pub that is always packed, mostly with tourists. Here, you can sip on your drink while you enjoy the music played by live bands or a DJ at times. Music played here ranges from jazz to world music.

**Hof Van Marken**

The hotel of Hof Van Marken lies on the border of IJsselmeer. This hotel houses a great restaurant that serves good lunch as well as dinner. But, most people come here to enjoy the cozy atmosphere that this restaurant exudes. Sip on well mixed drinks as you enjoy the light jazz music played in the background.
