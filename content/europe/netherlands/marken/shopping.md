title: "Shopping in Marken"
type: section
---

If you are keen to choose a lovely holiday spot this season that offers ample scopes for fun and activity, **Marken shopping** and sightseeing facilities can make it an ideal place for you to visit. Marken is a small coastal township in the Netherlands that features panoramic views of beaches and long-stretches sea waters. You can indulge in beach sports and interesting activities while staying at Marken. The lighthouse, church and Marker Museum are few of the places you must visit during your vacation in Marken.

**Different Shopping Experiences**

If you are a shopping freak, you can surely expect an interesting shopping spree at Marken which is far different from the regular ones. There are no big arcades or malls here, but the old world charm of the market area and the simple items available at the local market will easily satiate the shopaholic in you.

**Marken Shopping**

The island of Marken is not really popular as a shopping destination but it is well-known for the wood workshops that specialize in making wooden shoes. There are plenty of casual shops where you can get unique wood-crafted items that make great showpieces. The harbor area features souvenir shops and coffee junctions where you can hang out for hours.
**
Volendam Shopping**

Volendam is a small fishing village located very close to Marken. This village has often attracted the tourists for its interesting fishing boats. If you plan to take home some souvenirs, Volendam is the ideal place to shop for them. Here you will find a number of shops lining the streets and they sell interesting wooden-crafted stuffs. Anything that is a specialty of Marken is available here. Apart from that, you can find some gift shops selling flowers, books and other gift items.

**Edam Shopping**

Another nearby place that is ideal for shopping is Edam. Often people visit this area for the breathtakingly beautiful landscapes and the cheese factories. The small squares and little streets of the township retain the old world charm that any foreigner will enjoy. You can further check out the small shops at the streets which also sell beautiful hand crafted items ideal as souvenirs and gifts. Bookshops, groceries and stationery shops are also there. The best thing is to stop by a cheese factory of this "Cheese Village" and get some freshly prepared cheese. These cheese farms here excel in making quality cheese that is several times tastier that what you get in the market.
