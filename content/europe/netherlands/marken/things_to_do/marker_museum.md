Six former fishermen's cottages converted into a local history museum, one of which is preserved as a complete fisherman's household from the early 1930s with original furnishings and fittings. The remaining cottages hold rotating exhibits on Marken's history, fishing culture, traditional costume, and the long struggle with water. Open daily April to October; a small entrance fee applies. The address is Kerkbuurt 44–47.
