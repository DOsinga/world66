**Summer: The Best Time to Visit Marken
**

If you are considering your **Marken when to go** options, be informed that the weather is good enough for staying at the city both in the summer and in the winter. However, it&rsquo;s better to visit this small picturesque town in the summer, since its main attraction are the people dressed in traditional costumes. Visitors are more likely to see them on the streets during the sunny summer days than in the winter, when most of them stay in their homes.

#### **Summer
**

The best summer months are undoubtedly May and June, since the weather is pleasantly warm and not too hot. The average temperatures are about 20 degrees Celsius, and rainfalls are about 7 to 8 centimeters per month. The flowers are in their bloom and add color to the surroundings. In July and August, it gets hotter and the rainfall increases, with average temperatures reaching 23 degrees Celsius.

#### **Winter
**

When visiting Marken in the winter, take a note of the weather forecast. Winters are relatively mild, and it usually doesn&rsquo;t snow. Temperatures remain above 0 degrees Celsius, but sometimes winter storms start raging. The average precipitation is 12 to 13 centimeters. Floods are possible which can complicate your trip. Fortunately, the floods are usually not too serious, and you can take shelter at one of the local houses.
