Naarden is a walled city just south of Amsterdam. It is a bit posh, but very nice. There are many expensive shops with a focus on fashion and design. 
 


 Naarden is less than half an hour away from Amsterdam by train.
 


 With Easter there is always a special performance of the Mattheus Passion In Naarden which is worth to go to. It is sold out in advance on most occasions though.
