### [Restaurant Promers](/europe/netherlands/naarden/eating_out/restaurant_promers)

This is a very nice restaurant with excellent food in a beautiful atmosphere. It's located within the old city-walls of Naarden-Vesting. The interior is "light-chill-loungy".

I can strongly recommend this place after we had our wedding dinner there last year!
