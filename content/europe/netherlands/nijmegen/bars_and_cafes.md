---
title: "Bars and Cafes in Nijmegen"
type: section
---

While vacationing in this town in the Netherlands, you will find yourself surrounded by many different **Nijmegen Bars and Cafes** that you can visit, whether you're looking for a quick bite to eat or a relaxing atmosphere to sit and catch up with an old friend.

**Grand Café Riva**

The Grand Café Rivera is a great place to grab a quick bite to eat if you're looking for plenty of food for a reasonable amount. Most of the clientele that frequent the Grand Café Riva are students, so younger adults will find it more enjoyable than older couples will. Here you will be served your food and drink in a timely manner, but there will be no pressure to get up and leave.

**Café Samson**

One of the best things about Café Samson is that you will find a diverse crowd in attendance. Who is present will vary depending upon the time of the day that you choose to visit. When visiting Café Samson you will be seated and served by a waiter, which lends to the cozy atmosphere of this Nijmegen cafe. This is the perfect place to go and grab a quick drink after a day of window-shopping; each month they have a wine of the month. As far as food goes, your best choice is the weekly special, which is a sandwich, but the type of sandwich changes each week.

**Blonde Pater**

This is a nice place to grab lunch or just a quick cup of coffee. Most everybody will be comfortable visiting this busy and delightful café. If you come around noon you can expect a nice wait. Their sandwiches and salads are different from other cafes in town, also highly recommended is the mocha chino.

**Café in de Blaauwe Hand**

If you visit this café you simply must try a liqueur with whipped cream on top, but the fresh chocolate is also highly desirable. This little spot is a perfect fit for anybody, families included; while it mostly focuses on drinks, they do have limited selection of food. You can order the dish of the day or create your own dish using a card that allows you pick as you see fit.

**Café Jos Meesterschenkerij**

This café is more for adults who are looking for a nice place to have a few drinks. Café Jos has 16 different kinds of beers to choose from, in addition to their selection of wine, cognac and whiskey. If you are looking for a quick snack to have with your drink you can try the combination platter, which has a few different meats and cheeses, or you can have a plate of croquettes.
