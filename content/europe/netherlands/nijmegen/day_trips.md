title: "Day Trips in Nijmegen"
type: section
---

When you are looking for a destination to start out from when you travel the Netherlands consider **Nijmegen. Day** **trips** from this city can make your vacation very enjoyable. You will see that Nijmegen is a relatively restful city which makes for a good base to explore from. The train system here has 4 major terminals that will take you to where you desire to go. Also many tourists decide to travel by car, and although he city is a little crazy, once you get out of it is smooth riding.

**Short Trips
**
If you would like to start off on a short excursion first a good choice would be the Museumpark Orientialis. It is located in Helig Landstiching. The Museum gives you an in-depth understanding of how Judaism, Christianity and Islam have played an important part of shaping the character of present day Europe.

Another idea you could look into is a bus ride to Kleve, Germany. This is the perfect adventure day trip for you if you are interested about another country in addition to the Netherlands. The town is picturesque and old fashion so intend on having a relaxing time in Kleve. And to make things real easy for you there is a bus that leaves Nijmegen every hour up until evening on the weekdays.

**Long Trips

**Visiting the Metropolitan city of Eindhoven is a great way to spend your day. It is real easy to get there. Take a train to Hetrogenbush Station then transfer to Eindhoven. The time to get there is 90 minutes and the cost is about €15. Eindhoven sponsors a lot of festivals like Carnival in February, Hilaria Fun Fair during August which is great for children. Also the TROMP international music and festival is celebrated in November.
If you are really interested in making your time in Nijmegen enjoyable, a trip to Amsterdam is a must. It is real easy to get there. The train ride is about 1 hour and 45 minutes with the cost being reasonable at € 17 one-way. The city is filled with all kinds of attractions that you and your friends or family will enjoy. Have a look at the Van Gogh Museum or stroll along the walkways of the Artis Zoo. Take advantage of an unforgettable opportunity to travel the city by canal boat.
