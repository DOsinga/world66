---
title: "Sights"
type: section
---

Nijmegen contains a university, which lies in the south-east part
of the city. The Erasmus building, containing the Literacy,
Communications and Thelological faculty, is the highest building in the
city. Just take a bus there (or get there by bike), and take the elevator to the 20th floor. There you'll have a beautiful sight over the city.
