---
title: "Bars and Cafes"
type: section
---

There is exactly one cafe in Noordpolderzijl, and it is also the only house. **'t Zielhoes** (Sluice House) is open most days year-round and has become well-known throughout the region — a proper old-fashioned, living-room-style Dutch cafe where folk, country, Celtic, and Groninger music groups play. Worth a visit in its own right.
