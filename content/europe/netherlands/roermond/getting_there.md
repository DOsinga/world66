---
title: "Getting There in Roermond"
type: section
---

Roermond can be reached in 3 ways:

**by car**:

- take the A73 highway to Roermond and from there, follow the signs.
- take the A2 towards Maastricht (from Amsterdam, Utrecht, Den Bosch and Eindhoven), from there continue on the N280 to Roermond. From Maastricht and Sittard, take the A2 towards Utrecht and Amsterdam, and continue on the A280 (follow the signs).

There are lots of parking opportunities in the center of Roermond. Please keep in mind that it is not cheap (1,3 euro per hour or part of an hour!). You can reach the parking lots by following the ringway that runs around the city center, and from there follow the signs.

**by train**:

Roermond has a train station with trains that leave directly towards [Amsterdam](/europe/netherlands/amsterdam), [Utrecht](/europe/netherlands/utrecht), 's Hertogenbosch (also know as [Den Bosch](/europe/netherlands/denbosch)), [Eindhoven](/europe/netherlands/eindhoven), [Maastricht](/europe/netherlands/maastricht), Venlo and [Nijmegen](/europe/netherlands/nijmegen). Most trains leave every 30 minutes, starting at about 6:00 AM and ending at about 23:00.

**by bus**: **

**there are several busses going towards and leaving from Roermond. Most of them go to the surrounding villages and cities (some of those are definitely worth a visit as well). Almost all busses do stop at the bus station, which is right next to the train station. You can find the bus time tables there as well.
