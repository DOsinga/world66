---
title: "Bars and Cafes"
type: section
---

Rotterdam's nightlife centres on the Witte de Withstraat, where bars, galleries, and restaurants blur into each other. The Oude Haven waterfront has more polished terrace bars, while Nieuwe Binnenweg is the strip for brown cafes and late-night spots. For clubbing, the scene shifts south of the river around Maashaven.
