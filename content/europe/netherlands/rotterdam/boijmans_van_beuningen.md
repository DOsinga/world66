Rotterdam's finest art museum holds a collection that spans from Hieronymus Bosch and Bruegel through Rembrandt and Van Gogh to Dalí and modern Italian design. The applied arts wing -- furniture, glasswork, and ceramics -- is often overlooked but excellent. The mirrored Depot Boijmans Van Beuningen next door, opened in 2021, is the world's first publicly accessible art storage facility.
