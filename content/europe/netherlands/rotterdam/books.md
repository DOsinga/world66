**Rotterdam: City of the Future** by Tracy Metz and Maartje van den Heuvel — A richly illustrated account of how Rotterdam rebuilt itself after 1945 and became the most architecturally adventurous city in the Netherlands. Essential reading for understanding why the city looks the way it does.

**The Discovery of Heaven** by Harry Mulisch — His most ambitious novel, set partly in Rotterdam and Amsterdam, following two friends across the sweep of 20th-century European history. Long and rewarding.
