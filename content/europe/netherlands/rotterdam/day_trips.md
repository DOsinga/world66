### [Kinderdijk

Kinderdijk is a region 20km south west of Rotterdam which is famous for
its high concentration of windmills and for being a UNESCO heritage
site. Please, check the dedicated article](/europe/netherlands/rotterdam/day_trips/kinderdijk) for more information.
