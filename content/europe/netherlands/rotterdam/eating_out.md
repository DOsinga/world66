---
title: "Eating Out"
type: section
---

Rotterdam's port-city diversity shows up on the table. The West-Kruiskade is the street for cheap, authentic Indonesian, Surinamese, and Turkish food. The Witte de Withstraat has more upscale options. The Markthal -- a horseshoe-shaped apartment building arching over an indoor food market -- is both a food destination and an architectural landmark.
