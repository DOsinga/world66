A food market and artisan collective in a converted warehouse on the Katendrecht peninsula, on the south bank of the Maas. Local producers sell cheese, cured meat, fresh bread, craft beer (from the Kaapse Brouwers brewery on site), and other foods on Saturdays. The location — an old warehouse on the waterfront, with views back across the river to the skyline — is as good as the food. A Rotterdam favourite and a good reason to cross the Erasmus Bridge and explore the south bank.
