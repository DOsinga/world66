---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

Rotterdam Centraal station has high-speed Thalys connections to Paris, Brussels, and Antwerp, plus frequent Intercity trains to Amsterdam (40 minutes), The Hague (25 minutes), and Utrecht. Rotterdam The Hague Airport handles budget carriers, but most international flights arrive at Schiphol, connected by direct train in about 50 minutes. By car, the A13 and A20 motorways connect to the rest of the Randstad.
