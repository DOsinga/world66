Watertaxi Rotterdam provides watertaxiservices, ferryservices, roundtrips and contracttransport in the Rotterdam region. With it's stunningly fast Maastaxis in the whole area and with the classic Watertaxis HNY between Hotel New York and the Leuvehaven and the Veerhaven. This means for the Maastaxis you can call us at +3110-4030303 and point out from which landing stage you would like to embark and to which place you want to be taken. This is possible for one person, but for groups up to 12 persons per watertaxi as well. We will then tell you within what amount of time we can take you. If you so wish we can give you an estimation of the costs. The fares are calculated with a taximeter.
