title: "By Road"
type: poi
---

It is not very easy to get to Rotterdam by car, because of the many traffic jams. But when you finally arrived in this city you won't have any problems to find parking space.

Try parking garage 'Oude Haven' at Burgemeester van Walsumweg 728 in the centre, Parkeergarage Kiphof Kipstraat 177 or Parkeergarage Bijenkorf Aert van Nesstraat also in the centre of Rotterdam. If you find no place here, there are still several other options left.
