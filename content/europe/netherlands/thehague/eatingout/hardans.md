Located in an old wine cellar this is a very nice place to go to, right in the center. Park your car in the "Torengarage" just a 100 mtrs away.

A beautifull romantic restaurant with good quality diner for a friendly price, professional service and a lot of nice wines by the glass (ask for the illegall winebook). I had a splendid night and my friends as well. I will come back.
