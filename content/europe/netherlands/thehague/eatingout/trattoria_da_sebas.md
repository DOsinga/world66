Lovely Italian restaurant on the beautiful Javastraat, close to the city center.

"**Trattoria Da Sebastiano**" is a family-owned restaurant and has been offering genuine Italian cuisine to their guests for over 8 years.

The atmosphere is relaxed and informal and the prices are affordable, but the owners do not make any compromise on the quality of the ingredients used and food served.

Google around to get an idea of what our customers say about us... Then please visit us and see for yourself. **Arrivederci!**
