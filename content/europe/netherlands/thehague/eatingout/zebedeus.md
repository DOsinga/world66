Restaurant Zebedeüs is situated on a marvellous spot in a corner of the St. Jacob's or 'Great' Church, right in the centre of The Hague ('Den Haag'). In summer you can enjoy the terrace under the trees. Open seven days a week from 11am till 21.30 pm, Zebedeüs serves great coffee, rolls and lunch during the day. In the evening diner is served, with quality food and a no-nonsense atmosphere.
