**Museums**

**Museon**

Museon is a museum of science and a nice place to go on a rainy afternoon.  

address: Stadhouderslaan 41, Den Haag.

**Gemeentemuseum**

The Gemeentemuseum has a very good collection of (Dutch) modern art (Mondriaan, Escher e.g.). The building designed by Dutch architect BErlage is also impressive.
