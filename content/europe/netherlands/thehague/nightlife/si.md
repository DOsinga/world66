There are a lot of coffee shops in the Hague, they're just a bit more tucked away than in the 'Dam, the Hague being a more restrained, civilised kind of a place. Please forgive lack of names, I wasn't paying enough attention: you'll know them when you see them. Here's a few I saw/entered:

One on Hooikade, The Crow I think, just off Dr Kuyperstraat, very friendly, nice people. Soft drinks (juices etc), beer I think, but most importantly very nice smoke available: limited range but quality throughout. Beware of the V8...

One on Elandstraat, didnt go in, looked pretty commercialised but very plush.

One on Trompstraat, the Dizzy Duck. Lovely smoke, reasonable range, no menu. Clientele were not the greatest though, staff a little unfriendly but that may have just been me wandering around being a crap english tourist.

Any more I find I'll add. Enjoy your stay in the Hague, most civilised, friendly town I've been to.

There are two main coffee shops that i usually see, one is very close to central station called Sky High, its just off the street from central, right on the main street practically, seems very nice inside
