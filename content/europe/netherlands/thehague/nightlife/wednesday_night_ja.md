Every Wednesday night the *crème de la crème* of the Dutch Jazz scene play at the trendy lounge club, *Cocoon,* in The Hague, hosted by acoustic bass player, René van Beeck.

The jazz evening starts with a performance by well-known Dutch jazz musicians in a variety of formations. It’s followed by an organised jam session, flavoured with surprising grooves. Afterwards the blue notes of a jazzy club DJ take you into the night.

Entrance is free.

Situated in the middle of The Hague centre, *Cocoon *represents the unique combination of ‘Drink’, ‘Dine’ en ‘Dance’. Delicious cocktails, tasty food and quality wines are accompanied by accessible club music in a greatly comfortable and luxurious setting.

Cocoon is open on Tuesday till Saturday from 17:00 to 05:00.

On Sunday Cocoon opens at 16:00.

Cocoon is closed on Monday.
