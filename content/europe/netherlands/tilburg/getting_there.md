---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

Tilburg is well connected by rail. Direct trains run from Amsterdam Centraal (about 75 minutes), Utrecht (45 minutes), and Eindhoven (25 minutes). Intercity trains also link Tilburg to Breda (15 minutes) and Den Bosch (20 minutes). The station is a short walk from the city centre.

By car, Tilburg sits between the A58 (Breda–Eindhoven) and the A65, with easy access from the A2 corridor. Parking in the centre can be tight on market days; the Piushaven area has a large car park a short walk from the Heuvel.
