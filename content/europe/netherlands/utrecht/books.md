**De Ontdekking van de Hemel (The Discovery of Heaven)** by Harry Mulisch — Mulisch's most ambitious novel, partly set in Utrecht and Amsterdam, tracing the lives of two friends against the backdrop of 20th-century European history. The Utrecht scenes feel rooted in the city's university world. Available in English translation.

**The Evenings** by Gerard Reve — A week in the life of a young man in post-war Amsterdam, suffocating in family routine and Dutch provinciality. Reve grew up in Utrecht and the flat melancholy of Dutch winter life is perfectly rendered.
