title: "Day Trips in Utrecht"
type: section
---

Utrecht offers a beautiful landscape that is best explored via **Utrecht day trips**. Utrecht is the fourth largest city in Netherlands and one of its primary, commercial centers. People planning Utrecht day trips can consider the following options:

**Boating Trips **

Utrecht is connected by a large network of waterways. Thus, boating is the recommended form of exploring Utrecht&rsquo;s popular locations. In terms of choosing a ride, the traditional, sailor vessels are the most popular option among foreign tourists. Other options include flatboats, smacks and clippers. Many of these carriers are used by the locals for their fishing expeditions. Hence, a boat-ride provides every chance of discovering the native, fishing practices of Netherlands. Visitors can customize their boating tour in terms of the river they decide to pursue. Common options include, Hollandse IJsel, River Vecht and the Kromme Rijn.

**Canal Cruises **

Along with the boating trips, the canal cruises are fast emerging as the most favored option among visitors. Utrecht has an expansive canal network that helps to access the lesser-known locales. The most recommended canal tour includes stopping at the Stadkasteel Oudaen Brewery. Visitors can hope to get a free taste of the local brew here or buy their own supply at very cheap prices.

**Dom Tower **

Visitors should visit the Dom Tower—a local landmark of sorts. This is among the oldest of church towers in Netherlands. People can actually climb along its ascent and get a great view of the entire city. The locals believe that on clear days, even Amsterdam can be seen from this spot. There is a token entry-fee to view Utrecht&rsquo;s landscape from the Dom Tower.
