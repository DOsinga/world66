At 112 metres, the Dom Tower is the tallest church tower in the Netherlands — and the view from the top is the best in the country. A storm in 1674 tore the nave of the adjoining cathedral apart, and the two have been separated ever since. The original outline of the church is marked in the paving of the square. Climbing the 465 steps is how you earn the panorama.
