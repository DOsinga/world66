---
title: "Eating Out"
type: section
---

Utrecht has an eating scene that punches above its size, fuelled by 35,000 students and a growing foodie culture. The Oudegracht wharves are the prime terrace territory in summer; for serious eating, the streets around the Museumkwartier and the Twijnstraat are worth exploring. Dutch comfort food, Indonesian rijsttafel, and a cluster of good vegetarian kitchens all have a foothold here.
