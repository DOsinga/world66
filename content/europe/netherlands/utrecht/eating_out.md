---
title: "Eating Out"
type: section
---

Of course, you can grab a burger, go for a falafel or eat shawarma. However, Utrecht offers many options for eating out. There is always an ‘eetcafe’ somewhere along the way where you can have a tasty meal for a good price. Besides these eetcafes, there are plenty of restaurants that will suit your taste, ranging from Dutch pancakes to Greek souflakis and from Chinese Foe Yong Hai to Surinamese rotis.
