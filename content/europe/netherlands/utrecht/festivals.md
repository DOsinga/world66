---
title: "Festivals in Utrecht"
type: section
---

Utrecht is one of the most beautiful and most festive places in the world, and there are many **Utrecht festivals** for you to enjoy when you plan to visit the place.

**Animation Film Festival**

The Animation Film Festival is one of the few places, besides the San Diego animation festival and comic-con, where you can find some of the best and brightest new animated movies from all around the globe. This places allows amateur animators and writers to debut their new material to all sorts of different audiences depending on the day.

**Film Festival**

This film festival is where new up and coming directors, writers and even actors are able to make their debut. This festival allows amateurs to enter their own films to a rather diverse audience from day to day. This is a good way for new talented writers and directors to get discovered by industry professionals. The basic goal of this is to encourage a cooperation between the Netherlands and various other countries in order to create new and entertaining films. This festival is ran by the Netherlands Production Platform, also known as NPP. There are a few rules that you need to remember if you&rsquo;re going to be submitting your film to the festival.
