Finding a destination for your evening, and probably also for the rest of the night once you’ve got the hang of it, shouldn’t be brain-racking problem in Utrecht. Maybe only in the sense that you have to choose between going to one of the many cozy and brown cafes, watching a play, getting things started at the dance-floor of a good old goodies’ disco or techno club or seeing it all happen in front of your eyes at one of the stages with live music. Be inventive!
