When you meet up with Utrecht for the first time in your life- and all the following times, you have to admit that the city has a certain charm. Although you would expect Utrecht to have a mind-boggling complex for being the little sister of Amsterdam, most residents of Utrecht are convinced that Utrecht has its own atmosphere and character. Well, you can’t put them in the wrong when you’ve laid your eyes on the city. Near the ‘Grote Markt’, you will find narrow streets that are packed with brown cafes as brown, nostalgic and nice (or as waggish) as you will find them in Amsterdam. ‘Eetcafes’ won’t be a problem to find either. During summer, a lot of cafes move to their own terraces that overwhelm the desirable spots next to the canals. Every piece of solid ground is used and you can look up at the pedestrians or spot boats on eyelevel in the summer sun while enjoying a cold beer...
