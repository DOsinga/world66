A modern Dutch restaurant occupying one of the Oudegracht's lower-wharf spaces — the kind of setting that makes you understand why Utrecht's canal system is different from Amsterdam's. The kitchen focuses on seasonal produce from Dutch farms and fishermen, with changing menus and a reasonable lunch option. The terrace, sitting right at canal level, is one of the best tables in the city on a warm evening.
