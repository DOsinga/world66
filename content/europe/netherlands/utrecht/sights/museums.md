**Sights**

**Van Speelklok tot Pierement Museum**

The National Museum from Musical Clock to Street Organ. See and hear a large collection of musical instruments which work automatically.

**University Museum**

The permanent exhibition shows the world of science, from the past until the present. You have access to the Oude Hortus (historical garden) from the museum.

address: Lange Nieuwstraat 106

tel: 030-2538008

pc: 3512 PN

**Stichting Volkensterrenwacht Sonnenborgh**

The oldest observatory in the Netherlands.

address: Zonneburg 2

tel: 030-2311360

pc: 3512 NL

**Museum Catharijneconvent**

The museum shows the history of Christianity in the Netherlands.

address: Lange Nieuwstraat 38

tel: 030-2313835

pc: 3512 PH

**Aboriginal Art Museum **

Excellent collection of contemporary Aboriginal art.

address: Oudegracht 176

tel: 030-2380100

pc: 3511 NP

**Rietveld-Schröderhuis**

One of the best examples of the ‘Style’ architecture and designed by the famous Dutch architect Rietveld. Make appointments
 

address: Prins Hendriklaan 50 A

tel: 030-2362310

pc: 3583 EP

**Nederlands Spoorwegmuseum**

The Dutch Railway Museum that illustrates both the past and present of the Dutch Railways.

address: Maliebaanstation 1

tel: 030-2306206

pc: 3581 XW
