A Utrecht institution for Indonesian food, with the full rijsttafel experience — a parade of small dishes, spiced and slow-cooked, spread across the table until there is no room left. Nothing fancy about the room; everything is in the food. Utrecht's proximity to Amsterdam means there is healthy competition among Indonesian restaurants, and Warung Mini holds its own. Book ahead at weekends.
