If you are going to be planning a trip to Valkenburg, there are a lot of **Valkenburg bars and cafes** you can choose to stop and relax at while you are in the city.

#### Hotel Restaurant La Casa

This caf&eacute; and bar has a great terrace area and plenty of great choices. You can enjoy the casual meals which are served and the terrace area to enjoy a beer or cocktail. If you are looking for some Mexican cuisine while relaxing in the bar, you can find a number of traditional dishes.

#### Grendelpoort

The Grendelpoort is a historic pub which offers some of the best beers you will find. They also have light meals to enjoy with your beer, and you can try any of the beers they serve on tap. Sit outside or inside. The traditional-style pub has a lot of fun features, making it one of the Vulkenburg bars and cafes you may want to try.

#### Aan de Linde

The Aan de Linde is a great place to stop and enjoy an iced tea. You can also enjoy the hot beverages available, including hot chocolate and coffee. There are a variety of light meals to try, including oysters and mussels. If you like to stop and relax for a refreshing tea or a warm cup of coffee, this is a great place to see.
