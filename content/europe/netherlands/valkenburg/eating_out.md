If you are looking for **Valkenburg restaurants**, you will be able to find a number of places where you can enjoy the traditional cuisine of the area and also try some of the different local dishes available.

#### La Fleur

The La Fleur restaurant is a great place to enjoy different dishes made from flowers or from extracts. It is a small restaurant, and you will be able to get a full description of your meal so you know what it was made from and what flowers were used to create the flavors and colors in the food.

#### El Castillo

The El Castillo restaurant serves a blend of Mexican cuisine and many other flavors. You can enjoy the fresh vegetables, fruits and rice. You can also try any of the Mexican dishes served in a variety of levels of spice. If you like Mexican cuisine and a lot of variety, this is one of the Vulkenburg restaurants you should try to enjoy a nice meal.

#### Stads Restaurant

The Stads restaurant is a fun place to enjoy items from the grill. You can choose from a variety of grilled dishes or try one of the fish dishes. The portions are generous, and the tastes are memorable. You can try some of the different dishes and enjoy the cuisine of the area.
