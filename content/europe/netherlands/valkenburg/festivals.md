---
title: "Festivals in Valkenburg"
type: section
---

If you are planning a trip to Valkenburg and want to go when there is a lot of celebration and fun, choose to see the city when they are having one of the **Valkenburg festivals**.

#### Colours of Valkenburg

This is a street painting festival held in August. Many people come to the city to enjoy the street painting and the variety of entertainment. There are activities to enjoy and great performers to watch while you are enjoying the street painting.

#### Le Quinze Aout Festival

This festival is a celebration of folk art and music. If you enjoy any kind of folk music and like to see works created by artists from all over, you will enjoy this festival. There is plenty of entertainment and food to enjoy. This festival is held in August.

#### Christmas Market Festival

The Christmas market festival is held in November and December and has a number of traditional displays of Christmas items from all over the world. People can come and enjoy looking at the displays, trying the food and enjoying the music and atmosphere. There are a lot of activities held during the festival, and if you are looking for a great time to go to the area, this is one of the Valkenburg festivals you may want to see.
