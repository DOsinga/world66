---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment in Valkenburg"
type: section
---

When visiting Valkenburg, Netherlands, there are numerous activities available for you to get involved in, but once the sun sets many visitors want to experience the **Valkenburg nightlife**. Entertainment in Valkenburg is very similar to what any other city may offer, with a slew of bars and dance halls scattered across the city, but Valkenburg's infamous Holland Casino Valkenburg sets their nightlife apart from most other cities in Europe. With so many options, it may seem nerve racking trying to make a decision without first knowing what is available, what is highly acclaimed by local patrons or visitors, or even what is nearby your hotel. Here we will discuss a few of the options that you will have available during your visit to Valkenburg, Netherlands.

#### Nurgus

Nurgus is a popular bar/restaurant in Valkenburg, Netherlands that serves the entertainment needs of many Valkenburg citizens and tourists alike. During the night, the inside area of the pub converts into a bar and dance floor combination, the latter of which often caters to younger crowds during the weekends. Nurgus provides a terrific and lively atmosphere for the public to enjoy with friendly company as their customers dance their night away to great music.

#### Grendelpoort

If you would like to enjoy a somewhat calmer night, the oldest pub in the city, Grendelpoort, should be your first stop. Founded in 1912 and named after the city gate which it neighbors, Grendelpoort is very much a historical part of Valkenburg. Grendelpoort offers an eclectic blend of various draft beers with an excellent beer tap line up and also smartly provides an extensive snack menu to help their customers soak up some of what they drank at the bar.

#### Holland Casino Valkenburg

Undoubtedly and by far the most popular night time attraction in the city of Valkenburg would have to be the Holland Casino Valkenburg. Located at the top of the Cauberg and overlooking much of the city, the famous casino doubles as a landmark that can be recognized from afar from almost any street. Inside the casino's immaculate glass doors are over 300 slot and game machines and playing tables that service thousands monthly. The nightlife atmosphere and hospitable services from its employees allows guests to gamble late into the night as they enjoy the city.
