In **Valkenburg**,** things to do **range from immaculate museum exhibits to thrilling amusement parks. You will find that there are tons of attractions that are guaranteed to keep your attention occupied during your stay. There are opportunities for more fun around every corner in Valkenburg, making it quite easy to lose yourself in a good time. No matter your taste in music, food, or entertainment, you will be pleasantly surprised by the sheer number of activities there are available for you to do in this city. Here we will discuss a number of Valkenburg activities that will keep you out of your hotel room and out on the town while enjoying the best of what the city has to offer.

#### Area Historical Museum Valkenburg

The Area Historical Museum Valkenburg is a great first stop that will reveal many of the city's secrets and most significant historical happenings to visitors. Also available in this quaint and impressive little museum are beautiful modern arts exhibits from around the world that showcase the work of some of the most talented artists. The one hour tour is definitely worth your while, and you are bound to enjoy the artistic flair that Valkenburg has to offer.

#### Dino Monstercave

An amusement park that features life size statues of prehistoric dinosaurs, this thrill filled destination is an excellent choice for everyone, and not just the kids. Go see Monstercave where you can find the park's many dedications to these ancient giants, and learn more about what your favorite dinosaur was like in actuality. You can also enjoy many of the brand new rides now available throughout the park.

#### Fletcher Hotel Restaurant Valkenburg

The Fletcher Hotel Restaurant Valkenburg isn't just a luxury hotel with a classy restaurant attached. The amenities and services offered make this stop a must-do that could only be better if you were staying here. Once you leave your room to share a few drinks at the bar and lounge with friends, you can then make your way over to the hotel casino to try out your luck. The next day, you could enjoy a vigorous workout in the specialized hotel sports facility, which interestingly enough includes water sports. Later, rent a hotel bike and head off to view the city, which is easily accessible due to the hotel's close proximity to the heart of Valkenburg. There will be no shortage of things to do while enjoying your delightful stay in this exciting vacation spot.
