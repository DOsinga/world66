---
title: "Bars and Cafes in Volendam"
type: section
---

If you think this town in the Netherlands is just all about boat-riding and rich clothing history, then you might want to hear about the best** Volendam Bars and Cafes** available and think it over. So, plan your visit to this part of North Holland and don't miss including nightlife on your list. Here are some suggestions of party and chill places to go to.

**Bar de Molen**

Enjoy your drink with a cool crisp view of the port. Bar de Molen serves its customer beers and coffee, along with the wonderful sea ambiance that is just breathtaking. A view of the boats that Volendam’s most famous for adds to its place-to-visit points.

**Passage Bar**

Most commonly known for the wide range of drinks and mixes it has available to serve, Passage Bar is also renowned for its warm design and its lively customers. The place not only inspires people to chill but also to dance to good music and have fun all night long. This bar is also open during the day.

**Kakatoe Bar**

Chill your vacation away at the Kakatoe Bar as you sit and relax to the cool music or as you are socializing with the locals. This bar offers some of the best menus for drinks and is also considered by the Volendam residents as one of the best places to go to in town. So, dress to impress and get ready to expand your social network as you drink the night away.

**Roma Snackbar**

Are you in for some good snacks while roaming around Volendam? Well, if you think you have roamed the town enough for the day and are in need of a cold refreshing escape, be sure to spend your chill time at the Roma Snackbar. As its name implies, this bar offers a lot of yummy options when it comes to snacks, with a special hint of the local cuisine. Drink some beer and relax as you take in a few good comfort foods.

**Eetcafe Bar Max**

Whether the sun's high up or night’s crawling in the skies, you can go and spend some quality time with your family or with your friends at the popular Eetcafe Bar Max. True enough to its title, this bar not only offers you good food and drinks but also offers entertainment. So, whether you are stopping by for some cool drinks or coffee, you are always welcome at the Eetcafe Bar Max.
