#### On the Water&rsquo;s Edge in Volendam

**
Volendam beaches** are not this picturesque Dutch town&rsquo;s main attraction. There is a small patch of sandy beach on the water-side of the dike that protects the town; other than that, the best bet for waterside attractions include Volendam&rsquo;s famous harbor and a water park in the town itself. This is not to say that Volendam beaches are not worth a visit, but rather that they should not be the reason for you to visit the town which is better-known for its residents who dress in traditional Dutch gear, for the picture postcard loveliness of the town, and for its harbor side.

**
Volendam Beaches and Beaches Nearby**

There is a small beach. A walk along the town&rsquo;s dike is recommended, and coming across this cozy little beach makes for a pleasant stopping point. If you&rsquo;re looking to do more than just sink your toes into the sand, Marken, an &ldquo;island&rdquo; just off Volendam has a longer strip of beach leading up to its l

ighthouse. If you&rsquo;d rather have a full day on the beach, there are numerous spots south of Volendam and on the other side of Amsterdam, where places such as Bergen aan Zee and Cadzand, or even Blijburg offer more typical beach possibilities. These are also stunning towns to visit and all are easily reached from Volendam. There are also a number of &ldquo;beach tours&rdquo; that operate in Holland for the less certain traveler. Amsterdam City Tours Group is the standard option in this regard.

**
Volendam Beaches and Other Waterside Attractions**

A walk along the dike at Volendam will give you great views of the Ijsselmeer (Ij&rsquo;s lake, a man-made lake that was once the South Sea) and of the numerous boats that traverse it. Volendam&rsquo;s harbor itself is worth a visit, with historical attractions, fishmongers selling fish fresh off the boats, as well as a number of restaurants and cafes to enjoy with fellow-travelers from around the world. There is also a water park – Marina Park – with an outside pool and play area for youngsters.

All in all, Volendam is worth a visit, but if it is Volendam beaches you are after, your day would be better spent travelling from this lovely town to others nearby. Alternatively, Volendam offers other waterside attractions to enjoy that make up for the absence of a bona fide tourist beach.
