---
title: "Eating Out in Volendam"
type: section
---

If you want to experience the sumptuous Volendam cuisine, here are few **Volendam Restaurants** you can go to. Volendam is a small town in the Netherlands and it is known for its rustic fishing boats and traditional clothing styles. Volendam is a popular tourist destination, and the majestic natural scenery of the town can be found printed on several postcards. The town is also known for its local cuisine.

**Bistro D’ouwe Helling**

This is one of the popular restaurants that offers Dutch and Belgian cuisine. This restaurant is a great place to enjoy a meal with your family. The pea soup (Erwtensoep) and croquettes are must-tries at the restaurant. If you want to enjoy the mouthwatering dishes of this restaurant, it is recommended that you reserve a table.

**Dolce Vita**

The Dolce Vita is an Italian restaurant that serves some great dishes including pasta and pizza. This restaurant is situated right in the center of the town and offers a nice view of the harbor. Though the food is good, the service is somewhat slow. You can stop by this restaurant if you want some Italian food.

**Restaurant le Pompadour**

This restaurant is located near the main road of the city and along the beachfront. Restaurant le Pompadour serves Dutch cuisine and other seasonal dishes. The must-try at this restaurant is the fresh seafood dishes. The restaurant can accommodate up to 120 people at once, making it an ideal place for weddings and parties.

**Grand Café Restaurant De Dijk **

The Grand Café Restaurant De Dijk offers a wide selection of international dishes; however, the Dutch and Belgian dishes are the restaurant’s best offerings. This luxurious restaurant is ideally located in the heart of the town and right next to a lake. The restaurant interior is very classical and comfortable. The restaurant can accommodate about 80 people, making it a perfect venue for small gatherings and parties.

**Restaurant Van Den Hogen**

Restaurant Van Den Hogen is a huge restaurant right in the center of Volendam. It can accommodate a maximum of 140 people and has its own parking facilities. The restaurant is situated near the fish market and offers a great view of the harbor. You can watch the fishing boats come and go while enjoying a meal at the restaurant. One of the highlights of the restaurant is that it has been in business for over 75 years. It is famous for its amazing fish dishes.
