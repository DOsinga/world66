title: "Nightlife and Entertainment in Volendam"
type: section
---

There is a variety of **Volendam nightlife** to enjoy. You can enjoy an evening having a drink at a bar, go dancing, listen to a concert or attend a movie or performance. Volendam is not very large as most tourists will visit for a day to see the history of the region and the traditional costumes worn in the areas history. The nightlife is quite important as the sun sets late in the summer and most people like to sit out on the terraces and chat with friends or visit a local pub or lounge and talk over the day’s events.

**Enjoying a Drink at the Local Bar or Lounge**

A popular local bar enjoyed by locals and visitors is Sjaakie’s Café Bar. It is located on Meerzijde 29 and you can enjoy an evening rubbing elbows with the locals and relaxing in a comfortable atmosphere. Another bar that specializes in pool is found on Harlingenlaan and is called Eusie’s Pool Bar. Another good bar choice is the Eetcafe Bar Max.

**Concerts and the Music Scene**

You can find many large concerts in Volendam as many will come to support the local football club. You can see local Dutch concerts as well as international acts. There are several benefit concerts and more. While there are not many venues in the town you can find a wide variety of concerts throughout the year.

The city itself has produced many musicians as several players from the Amsterdam symphony are from the city and there are many rock bands and alternative musicians that have started here. Many of these bands started performing in the city and you can see some emerging artists perform before they hit it "big time."

If you are anywhere within the country for "parkpop" then you should attend as this is the largest music festival in the country. More than 350,000 people attend. Expect a few headliners and then the rest are local performers. There are 3 stages that have performances. This is the highlight in August.

**Have Fun at the Casino**

Nearby,you can enjoy some gambling at one of the many Holland casinos. Inside you can take your chance at the table games or slot machines. This can be a fun evening though the casino is not located in the city but is easily within traveling distance. This is the only casino company in the country per the licenses offered from the gaming commission.
