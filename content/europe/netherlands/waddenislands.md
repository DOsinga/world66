The Wadden Islands are north of [Friesland](/europe/netherlands/friesland) and [Alkmaar](/europe/netherlands/alkmaar). Texel, the biggest island is best reached from [Amsterdam](/europe/netherlands/amsterdam) or [Alkmaar](/europe/netherlands/alkmaar) via Den Helder (first train, then boat). Vlieland and Ameland are best reached from Harlingen in [Friesland](/europe/netherlands/friesland). The islands are a very nice place to spend a few days, enjoy the nature, walk along the shore. They are not as posh a destination as the German Wadden Islands are.
