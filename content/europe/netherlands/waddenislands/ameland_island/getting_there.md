---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

The ferry to Ameland departs from Holwerd on the Frisian mainland and takes about 45 minutes, arriving at Nes harbour. Wagenborg Ferries operates the service several times daily. Car reservations are recommended in summer — book via the Wagenborg website or by phone.

By public transport, take the train to Leeuwarden, then the bus to Holwerd ferry terminal. Direct connections are available from Amsterdam, Rotterdam, Utrecht, and Groningen to Leeuwarden. By car, follow the signs from Leeuwarden to Holwerd; parking at the port is available.

An alternative for the adventurous is to sail to Ameland on a traditional flat-bottomed boat, combining the crossing with a Wadden Sea experience.
