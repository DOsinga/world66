**By traditional sailing ship**

There are several travel agencies that offer organized sailing/cycling trips to two or more Wadden islands by authentic sailing ships, e.g. Wadfietsen.nl and Fiets-fun.nl.

### [Ferry Holwerd Ameland island](/europe/netherlands/waddenislands/ameland_island/getting_there/ferry_holwerd_am)

Departure/timetable and tariff information for the **ferry** Holwerd harbour-Ameland island.

Wagenborg Ferries offers a ferry service to Ameland island (Nes
harbour). The crossing takes approximately 45 minutes.

For information
about the actual timetables, call the Wagenborg information line or
check the website (see facts sheet below, ***click ****[English flag]***).

Tickets are purchased at the
ticket office of the Wagenborg terminal in Holwerd.

For the actual
rates, at *www.wpd.nl* below, click [rates [more..](/europe/netherlands/waddenislands/ameland_island/getting_there/ferry_holwerd_am)

### [Drive yourself](/europe/netherlands/waddenislands/ameland_island/getting_there/drive_yourself)

Click the link to access the ferry company's detailed **routeplanner**. Two different itineraries are available:

The **Polders** route. Give *muiden* in the [City] box.

The **the IJsselmeer Dam** /Closing-dike/Afsluitdijk route. Give *amsterdam* in the [City] box.

If you are not taking your car to Ameland, you can park it in the
parking area close to the port. Rates for this area: € 3,80 per day
(2005). The parking area is open daily from 30 minutes prior to the
arrival of the first boat until 15 minutes [more..](/europe/netherlands/waddenislands/ameland_island/getting_there/drive_yourself)
