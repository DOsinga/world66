Departure/timetable and tariff information for the **ferry** Holwerd harbour-Ameland island.

Wagenborg Ferries offers a ferry service to Ameland island (Nes
harbour). The crossing takes approximately 45 minutes.

For information
about the actual timetables, call the Wagenborg information line or
check the website (see facts sheet below, ***click ****[English flag]***).

Tickets are purchased at the
ticket office of the Wagenborg terminal in Holwerd.

For the actual
rates, at *www.wpd.nl* below, click [rates 200*].

To make a **car booking** to Ameland, you must either contact the
reservation department of the Nes/Ameland office at +31 (0)519 546111 or complete
an e-mail form available from the website's upper menu bar [Car reservation] to indicate your requirements.

A slide show of the crossing is also available at the website *(start it by clicking [Diashow overtocht] in the Ameland sub-menu).*
