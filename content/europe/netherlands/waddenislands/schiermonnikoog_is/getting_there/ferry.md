Departure/timetable and tariff information for the **ferry** Lauwersoog harbour-Schiermonnikoog harbour.

Wagenborg Ferries offers a ferry service to Schiermonnikoog harbour. The crossing takes approximately 45 minutes.

For information about the actual timetables, call the Wagenborg information line or check the website (see facts sheet below).

Tickets are purchased at the ticket office of the Wagenborg terminal in Lauwersoog.

For the actual rates, at *www.wpd.nl* below, click [English flag] > [rates 200*].

Schiermonnikoog ferry terminal is at 3 km from the village. Buses or
taxis can bring you there, or you can rent a bicycle directly at the
terminal area (which is a good idea, but this service is only available
in high season).

Please make a note that you’re not allowed to bring any motor
vehicle to Schiermonnikoog island without permission of the
municipality.
