Wagenborg Ferries runs the only scheduled service from Lauwersoog harbour on the Frisian mainland to Schiermonnikoog. The crossing takes about 45 minutes and runs several times a day year-round, with extra sailings in summer. Buy tickets at the Wagenborg terminal in Lauwersoog or online via wpd.nl.

The ferry terminal on Schiermonnikoog is about 3 km from the village. A connecting bus meets most arrivals, and taxis are available. In high season you can rent a bicycle right at the terminal — a good plan, since cycling is the main way to get around the island.

No private motor vehicles are allowed on Schiermonnikoog without a municipal permit, so leave the car at the Lauwersoog car park.
