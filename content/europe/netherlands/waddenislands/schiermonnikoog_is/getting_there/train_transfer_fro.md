To reach Schiermonnikoog, you first need to get to Lauwersoog, the ferry departure point. The nearest major train station is Leeuwarden, the capital of Friesland.

From Amsterdam Centraal, direct intercity trains to Leeuwarden run roughly every half hour and take about two hours and fifteen minutes. Rotterdam, Utrecht, and Groningen also have direct connections. Check [NS](https://www.ns.nl) for current schedules and fares.

From Leeuwarden station, take bus 50 (Arriva) to Lauwersoog harbour — the ride takes about 50 minutes. The bus schedule is timed to connect with ferry departures, but check in advance, especially outside summer. From Lauwersoog, the Wagenborg ferry crosses to Schiermonnikoog in about 45 minutes.
