Oosterend is a small village on the eastern end of Terschelling, quieter and more remote than [West-Terschelling](/europe/netherlands/waddenislands/terschelling/westterschelling). The village has a handful of cafes and is a good base for cycling to the wilder eastern beaches and the mudflats that stretch toward [Ameland](/europe/netherlands/waddenislands/ameland_island).
