Travel Guide

Located about halfway between [West-Terschelling](/europe/netherlands/waddenislands/terschelling_islan/westterschelling) and Oosteinde,
Formerum is a small village dominated by a wind-mill, where you can
drink coffee. The beach is 2 km away, going through a very nice forest
and then some pretty dunes.

On the way you pass a place where children can ride ponies for a few euros.

The town also has a supermarket and three or four bike rental shops.
