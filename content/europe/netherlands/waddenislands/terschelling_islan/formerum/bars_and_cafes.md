### [De Koffiemolen](/europe/netherlands/waddenislands/terschelling_islan/formerum/bars_and_cafes/de_koffiemolen)

The Koffiemolen is a working wind mill where you can have coffee and
cranberry pie. Inside there are over 50 old traditional coffee
mills and many other old tools.
