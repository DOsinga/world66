**By Train + Ferry **(polders route)**
**
Train (any station) to Harlingen harbour > Ferry to Terschelling.

**By Bus + Ferry **(Ijsselmeer Dam/closing dike/afsluitdijk route)**

**Train (any station) to Alkmaar > Bus to Harlingen harbour > Ferry to Terschelling.

**By Car + Ferry **(polders or Ijsselmeer Dam route)**
**You can take your car to the island or leave it at Harlingen harbour and rent a bicycle after the crossing.**
