**By (bus +) bike + on foot**, *14 kms, 20 minutes cycling and 5 hours of walking time.

Check map in url below.

*
At the ferry terminal 't Horntje, check when your ferry sails again in the afternoon or evening
Rent
a bike at the terminal (high season only): Fietsverhuur Veerhaven
Texel, tel. 0222-319588. Go to direction Den Hoorn (10 minutes).

In low season take a bus to Den Hoorn and rent a bike there: Fa. L. Bruining, Kerkstraat 2, 1797 AD Den Hoorn tel. 0222-319380.

If you arrived by bus, check when it leaves Den Hoorn again for the ferry in the afternoon or evening.

Then from Den Hoorn go W to De Grote Vlak (10 minutes).

Park your bike there and continue on foot direction W until you reach
the shore. Control your walking time and keep oriented to be able to
find your bicycle again!!! When arriving at the shore, go left to
follow the tide line. Size and
shape of the current beach plain vary under the influence of the sea...
*Safety Warning: Should there be any warning signs on your path, for your own safery go inland again! **Be aware that tide can rise very fast, especially here on the North Sea side of the island!*

You'll
see that you'll be rounding the southern tip of the island in about an
hour of walking... just
continue your shore walking for another 15 minutes, then head right N,
leaving the eastern part of beach plain on your right hand. Continue
walking N on the beach on the "land" side this time.
