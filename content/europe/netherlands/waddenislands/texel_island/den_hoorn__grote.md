title: Den Hoorn to De Hors Coastal Walk

This 14-kilometre loop combines cycling and walking to explore the wild southern tip of Texel. Start in Den Hoorn and cycle west to De Grote Vlak (about 10 minutes). Lock your bike and continue on foot toward the shore.

When you reach the beach, turn left and follow the tide line south. You will round the island's southern tip in roughly an hour, passing through De Hors -- a vast, shifting sand plain shaped by wind and sea. Continue north along the eastern beach for another 15 minutes before heading inland to find your bike.

Allow about five hours for the walking portion. Check ferry and bus times before you set out, especially in low season. In high season you can rent a bike at the ferry terminal (Fietsverhuur Veerhaven Texel, tel. 0222-319588); in low season, take the bus to Den Hoorn and rent from Fa. L. Bruining on Kerkstraat 2.

Safety note: tides on the North Sea side can rise fast. Watch for warning signs and do not get caught on the sand flats at high tide.
