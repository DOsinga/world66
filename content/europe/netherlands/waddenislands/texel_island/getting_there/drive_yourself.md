Route

From Amsterdam, take the A8/A9 direction Alkmaar > N9 direction Den Helder. In Den Helder, ferry pictograms indicate the route to the drive-in ticket windows at the ferry terminal.

Parking

It's your choice to bring your car to Texel or to leave it in Den Helder. There is a free of
charge terminal car park. To reach this car park, follow the ferry pictogram route "*Texel - Afhalen/Wegbrengen*" (arrivals/departures). The car park is not supervised.
