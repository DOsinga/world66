Departure/timetable and tariff information for the ferry [Den Helder](/europe/netherlands/denhelder) harbour-Texel island.

Teso ferry company offers a crossing service to Texel island ([Den Burg](/europe/netherlands/waddenislands/texel_island/den_burg)
harbour). Your trip takes about 20 minutes; bookings not necessary.

Global overview of departures and rates Den Helder - Texel island.
On
weekdays and under normal weather conditions, a ferry leaves Den Helder
harbour every hour from 6:30 am to 9:30 pm and a ferry departs from
Texel every hour from 6:00 am to 9:00 pm.

On Sundays and bank holidays, the ferry service begins one or two hours
later.

Return tickets:

Persons, bicycles and mopeds: € 3,00

Motorcycles,
including rider + passenger: € 8,10 - 10,60 (depending on the season)

Vehicles
up to 250 or 550 cm in length including passengers: € 12,20 or 24,50 - 17,50 or 35,00 ( depending on the season).

Check for more details and most recent rates: http://www.teso.nl/en_index3.htm
