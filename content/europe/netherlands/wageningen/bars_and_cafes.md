**Experience a Night Out in Onder de Linden Caf&eacute; in ****Wageningen, Netherlands**

The best way to spend your evening in Wageningen is to visit the one of the **Wageningen bars and cafes **that dot this central municipality in Netherlands. The reason why there are a lot of bars and caf&eacute;s in Wageningen is that the municipality houses the Wageningen University where local and international students study. There are a lot of bars in Wageningen but if you want to experience how locals spend their evenings here, then you ought to head off to the Onder de Linden Caf&eacute;.

**The Onder de Linden Caf&eacute;**

Located in Haagsteeg in Wageningen, the Onder de Linden Caf&eacute; is a favorite place of students and locals alike. The caf&eacute; has a small setting. It comes complete with a bar and a bartender, who serves local and imported beverages. Drinks in this caf&eacute; cost around $10 to $15. The bar is filled every night with students who just want to have a relaxed evening talking with friends and acquaintances.

**Other Attractions At Onder de Linden Caf&eacute;**

Aside from the lively atmosphere of the caf&eacute;, this place is also famous for its nightly live band. It does not have its own in-house band but the owners of the caf&eacute; invite famous as well as budding jazz and rock musicians to play at the center of their moderately-sized stage. This caf&eacute; does not have a big dance floor where you can dance your night away. However, the lively music offered by the live entertainers are enough to keep you entertained all night long.
