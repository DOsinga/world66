---
title: "Beaches in Wageningen"
type: section
---

**Wageningen beaches**, located on the Lower Rhine, are all
beautiful, and they're also quite famous because of the science and
research conducted by the university. Below is some useful information
about the beaches in this part of the world, should you decide to visit.
**Why Beaches are Important

**

The beaches create important corridors for animals and plants to move
from the dunes to the sea. Natural beaches are needed to preserve the
essential dune system off the coast. Because of the importance of
natural beaches, there are specific areas set aside for recreation and
others that are part of nature reserves.
**Going Au Natural

**

Most of the beaches in the Netherlands are clothing optional. You do
not have to be a member, so person can go to the beach and not have to
wear a bathing suit. Sometimes the beaches will be segregated into
separate areas, as some beach goers are not comfortable with those not
wearing clothing, and vice versa.
**Beach Activities

**

There are many water sports and activities to be found at the beach.
You can enjoy the sun and sand and relax. You might spend your hours
reading or doing some “people watching.” However, you can also go for
dune rides, sailing, water skiing, surfing, boating, jet skiing and
more. Many of the beaches also have bicycling and walking paths as well
as some great fishing spots.
**Where are the Beaches

**

You can find beaches in the Netherlands at Ameland, Breskens, Den
Helder, Egmond aan Zee, Groote Keeten, Wassenaar, Zeeland, and many more
places. The closest beaches to Wageningen are those near Amsterdam.

The majority of the beaches are on the west side of the Netherlands
and, because of its interior location, the city of Wageningen does not
have any of its own beaches. However, you can easily use the city as a
base to explore the rest of the country and visit its many excellent
beaches.
**Great Nearby Beaches at Zeeland

**

Some of the cleanest beaches in the world are found in Zeeland. These
beaches get the most sunlight of any beach, and there are more than 650
kilometers of coastline to explore. There are many beaches, but each
has its own personality and uniqueness, so you will soon find your
favorite. There are natural beaches for those that enjoy the quiet, and
active water sport beaches for those looking for a more exciting
pastime.
