**Kasteel Doorswerth: The Best Place to Spend Your Wageningen Day Trips **

If you are planning to embark on any **Wageningen day trips** while you are staying in this small historical town in central Netherlands, it is important to consider the place that you are going to visit in Wageningen. If you want to escape the real world, then the best place for you to spend your day trip in Wageningen is in Kasteel Doorwerth.

**Kasteel Doorwerth**

Situated in Doorwerth which is located in the outskirts of Wageningen, this is a splendid castle that has been around since time immemorial. The castle is open to the public and is considered to be one of the most famous landmarks of Doorwerth. Today, the castle is no longer inhabited and has been turned into a public attraction. The castle was built during 1260 and has been used as a home of royalties of the Netherlands. The place can be reached by riding a bus or driving a rented car to Doorwerth for two hours.

**Activities to do In ****Kasteel Doorwerth**

There are a lot of things to do in this scenic Medieval castle. The castle houses the Museum for Nature and Wildlife Preservation which is called Museum voor Natuur- en Wildbeheer* *located on the South Wing of the palace. The castle also has a big lawn which faces the river Rhine where tourists are allowed to have picnics during the afternoon. Sightseeing around the castle and its grounds is also allowed and are usually headed by tour guides.
