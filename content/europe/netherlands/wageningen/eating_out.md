---

There are a good number of **Wageningen Restaurants** to choose from when visiting this Danish city. An excellent option is the Restaurant &lsquo;t gesprek, which offers an inventive culinary experience, perfect for those who wish to sample true Danish food.

**Good Food**

You can be sure of enjoying a satisfying meal here at Restauarant t&rsquo; gesprek. You'll be happy to know that all of the restaurant&rsquo;s ingredients are locally sourced from in and around the area. The Holland Shrimp and red beet salad is a must to start off your meal here. Next, the potato soup with smoked eel and chive cream shouldn&rsquo;t be skipped. For the main course, the crispy fried veal is a great choice. Complete your meal with one of the deserts, such as macaroons with caramel syrup. A four-course meal such as this costs around $40.

**Relaxed Atmosphere**

Sitting in the Restaurant t&rsquo;gesprek, you feel like you may be seated in a friendly neighbor&rsquo;s house rather than a diner! This is because the entire restaurant is housed in a spacious cottage. The main restaurant, which can accommodate around 50 people, has a bright, airy and homely feel to it. The wooden cane furniture and potted plants add a wonderful touch. If you happen to be dining as a large group of people, you can opt for the "Living Room" section of Restaurant t&rsquo;gesprek, which can accommodate 14 people. After your meal, you can enjoy the beauty of the restaurant&rsquo;s garden by taking a walk in the midst of nature outside.
