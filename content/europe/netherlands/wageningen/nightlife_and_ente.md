**Wageningen Nightlife** is typically geared towards students at the local University. Wageningen, a small city in the Netherlands, is home to over 36,000 people, most of whom are University students. This article will discuss the different places visitors can go to enjoy nighttime activities in Wageningen.

#### Bars & Caf&eacute;s

Visitors can travel to the Market in Wageningen, where there&rsquo;s an abundance of bars, caf&eacute;s and restaurants. Here you can relax on the terrace, have drinks, and chat with others. It&rsquo;s one of the best places to go in the city if you&rsquo;re looking for nighttime activity.

Caf&eacute; De Tijd&lrm; is a trendy coffee house in Wageningen. Other Caf&eacute;s in the area include eetcafe De Kater, Caf&eacute; Penny Lane, Caf&eacute; de Korenbeurs, and Caf&eacute; Het Gat van Wageningen. Located 30 minutes away in AL Dreumel is a karaoke bar called Karaokeverhuur De Kleijn. It&rsquo;s a great place to relax with friends and enjoy the ambience of this modernized karaoke bar.

For dance clubs, it&rsquo;s best to head to a nearby city such as Amsterdam. Wageningen nightlife satisfies most, but it&rsquo;s limited to a few bars and caf&eacute;s. Wageningen still remains an ideal location because it&rsquo;s a peaceful, busy little city, yet it is close to neighboring cities in case visitors want a more diverse range of nightlife options.

#### Theater & Opera

One thing there's no shortage of in Wageningen are the theaters and Opera houses. The Molenstraattheater is a local theater that visitors can visit day or night. Located at Vijf Meiplein is a movie theater where patrons can go to relax and watch a film. Other nearby theaters and include the Junushoff Theater, De Wilde Wereld, and Theater 't Hemeltje.

#### Dining

A night on the town isn&rsquo;t complete without a nice meal to go with it. Sa Lolla is an authentic Italian restaurant that has been praised for its delicious food and reasonable pricing. Many locals will tell you that it is the best Italian eatery in the area.

Vrijheid is a local restaurant that serves Mexican cuisine. This restaurant has received great reviews on their food and customer service. Another local restaurant worth checking out is a Greek eatery called Macedonie. If Greek food isn&rsquo;t your style, check out Chinese Muur for great Chinese cuisine. Broodje Bram is a Dutch restaurant, enjoyed by both locals and tourists alike. Visit 't Steakhouse if you&rsquo;re in the mood for a Porter House.

Although Wageningen may not be a party scene, the laid-back nighttime activities offered are sometimes just what the doctor ordered. Still close enough to travel to a party town, it offers a peaceful and beautiful setting for a quiet night with old friends or a night filled with making new ones.
