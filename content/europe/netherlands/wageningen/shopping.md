---
title: "Shopping in Wageningen"
type: section
---

There are a few **Wageningen shopping** spots in this tranquil
little town situated in the province of Gelderland, Netherlands. Most of
the shops are open only for a limited number of hours each day. You
will enjoy picking up some great buys in Wageningen.

**Hans Textile**

Hans Textile is a small shop situated along the main shopping street
known as Hoogstraat. Here you will be able to pick up some trendy
ready-made clothing, sportswear and casual dresses. Hans also offers
household articles, shoes, children's toys and accessories. This is one
of the most frequented shops in this town because of its reasonable
prices and fashionable offerings. Hans is also known for trading high
quality and first grade fabrics and garments.

**'t Zonnetje**

't Zonnetje is a specialty store located in Wageningen. This store
offers a variety of health foods, fresh and dry herbs, herbal teas and
coffees from all over the world, as well as organic produce. 't Zonnetje
also sells handmade and scented candles, locally made sweets, giftware
and handicrafts like tea pots, stainless steel espresso pots, coffee
cups and mokjes. You can find different brands of espresso machines in
't Zonnetje. If you're looking to buy healthy foods, this is the right
place to do so.

**Kringloop**

Kringloop is a store that sells secondhand goods. Though you might
not be interested in buying secondhand items, you might want to give the
place a shot. This store has a variety of clothes, furniture, household
articles, shoes, bags and appliances. You can browse around in the shop
and who knows, you may see something that interests you. Kringloop is
located in Vijzelstraat, which is near the market square and the church.

**Wageningen Wet Market **

The Wageningen Wet Market is situated in Hoogstraat, Wageningen. Here
you can buy fresh local produce like fruits, vegetables, poultry, meats
and seafood. The wet market has a dry goods section where you can find
locally made products like casual clothing, sandals, accessories and
some handicrafts. The Wageningen Wet Market is open only during Saturday
and Wednesday, from 8:00 a.m. to 8:00 p.m.

**Wibra**

Wibra is another humble store that offers affordable yet trendy
items. This store is situated just a short distance from the university
in Wageningen. Most of the store's customers are students. The store has
a wide selection of fashionable clothes, shoes, bags, accessories, baby
clothes and toys.
