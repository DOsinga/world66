**The Perfect Place to Smoke Shisha in Zandvoort**

Zandvoort is a popular Dutch resort with nice beaches and **Zandvoort Bars and Cafes. **Nightlife in this popular Netherlands village is lively, and you can spend the day frolicking on the beach or shopping with your family. Grab a bite of authentic Dutch goodies in the cafes, or relax in one of the famous Zandvoort bars.

Rotana Bar is a favorite bar among shoppers and beach lovers. This bar is located conveniently in a small alley, just walking distance from the beach and at the center of Zandvoort. This is a great place to unwind after a tiring day of shopping. The bar has a nice interior with simple yet chic decorations. Great selections of Latin, Spanish and Arabic songs give the place a special and relaxing atmosphere. You will love the belly dancing and live music during weekends with live performances of belly dancers. The bar offers great tasting tapas and mezze, made fresh and perfect which goes well with your favorite cocktails.

If you are a coffee lover, Rotana bar serves the best tasting coffee or try the famous Moroccan tea with freshly picked mint leaves. You can relax and chill in Rotana&rsquo;s lounge and enjoy smoking shisha, which is tobacco-free and taste like herbs. You can enjoy the reasonably priced food and drinks all days of the week. You can rent the place or full lounge for private parties and birthdays. Email them at rotana@live.nl or call 06-26 347 357 for pricing and party reservations.
