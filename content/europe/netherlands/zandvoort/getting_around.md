---
title: "Getting Around in Zandvoort"
type: section
---

If you are heading to Zandvoort to enjoy a nice vacation at the beach, this **Zandvoort transportation** guide will give you an idea how you can get around. Being a small town with a total area of less than 18 sq miles, you do not have to worry about getting around. There are a few transportation options available in Zandvoort, and here are the options.

**Train**

The train station in Zandvoort is located very close to the beach, which makes it easy to get to if you are heading for the beach. There are trains that go to Amsterdam and Haarlem every 30 minutes.

**Car Rental**

You can also rent a car while you are here in Zandvoort. Being a small town, there are not many car rental agencies – just one.

**Tuk-Tuks**

This is definitely a must-try while in Zandvoort. This is a 3-wheeled vehicle that can get you to almost any place in Zandvoort. The Tuk-Tuks are similar to the Autos in India – just a bit different. You can stop one of these vehicles on the road and take a ride all over town and to the beach if you like. You can also book one of these to get you around any time you want.

**Bicycle**

If you into eco-friendly transport, nothing can be better than the bicycle. You can rent a bicycle at many places in Zandvoort and explore the town on your own. This is not only an eco-friendly mode of transport, but it will also keep you fit.
