Zandvoort, a major resort location, is well known for **Zandvoort Nightlife** and busy streets. Both locals and tourists intermingle as they enjoy the hot spots in Zandvoort.

#### Bars and Caf&eacute;s

**Rotana Shisha Bar:** Rotana Shisha Bar is well-known to the locals as being a great place to enjoy a cocktail or to smoke a Shisha pipe. This bar is conveniently located near shopping centers and the beach.

**Williams Pub:** Williams Pub is a great place to grab some late night grub, or grab an ice cold beer. Play a game of pool before your meal arrives or browse the local shops when you&rsquo;ve finished.

**La Paz:** La Paz is a coffee shop/lounge area that patrons can enjoy when they need a place to wind down. Although mellow, it can be a great place to have a good time with friends.

**Club Escape**: If you get the chance to travel to nearby Amsterdam, check out Club Escape. It&rsquo;s a modernized club with a distinct edge as one of the best clubs in the area. Club Escape offers a lounge, caf&eacute;, bar and more.

**Yanks:** Yanks is a coffee shop that is often frequented by tourists. The prices are reasonable and the location is convenient to the beach. The d&eacute;cor is unique, making this coffee shop stand out among others.

**Caf&eacute; Neuf:** Caf&eacute; Neuf is another local caf&eacute; known for its ambience and history. It&rsquo;s a great place to go with friends to sit and relax for the evening.

#### Other Nighttime Attractions

**Holland Casino Zandvoort:** Holland Casino Zandvoort is a great place to go when you need some nighttime excitement. Here you can gamble, drink, dine, shop and so much more.

**Beach Club Tien:** Beach Club Tien is an open, airy restaurant that offers great food and drinks. Take in the spectacular view of the ocean as you dine.

**Zandvoort Beach:** Zandvoort Beach is a great place to relax during the evening. Part of the beach is dedicated as a nudist beach. The beach has 10,000 visitors daily. This is a must-see when traveling to the area. There are often nighttime activities held here and it&rsquo;s easy to pass away the hours when enjoying the beautiful view of the ocean under the moonlight.

The smell of the ocean, the sights of the town and beautiful sandy beach, all make Zandvoort a wonderful vacation destination. Although it&rsquo;s a busy town, it still retains a laid back style and feel. The upbeat nightlife makes Zandvoort a real hot spot.
