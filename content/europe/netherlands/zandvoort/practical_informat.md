This **Zandvoort practical information** guide will give you some valuable information about this beach resort in the Netherlands. Zandvoort is one of the popular beach resorts because of its sandy beach and coastal dunes. If you are planning a vacation here, you will find the information in this guide helpful.

**Passport and Visa Requirements**

If you are from the US, you do not need to apply for a visa to enter the Netherlands and travel to Zandvoort. However, you will need to have a valid passport with at least 6 months validity. If you are from any of the Schengen countries, you do not need to apply for a visa to travel to Zandvoort.

**Circuit Park**

One of the reasons why people visit Zandvoort is because of the Zandvoort Circuit Park. This is the most important racing circuit in the country. The racing circuit is located near the coast line of the North Sea.

**Transportation**

There is a railway line that runs through Zandvoort. There are train services every 30 minutes to Amsterdam and Haarlem. There are also special train services from Haarlem to Zandvoort during the summer. You can also get around the place by car.

**Currency**

The Euro (&euro;) is legal tender in Zandvoort, so if you are from the US, UK or any other country, you will need to change your money to Euros. You can change your money at the banks or at the money changers. You can also use the ATM machines to withdraw cash while you are here in Zandvoort.
