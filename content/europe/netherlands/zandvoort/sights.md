YANKS coffeeshop is the place to go. Located near the Zandvoort Casino and movie theater, Yanks comprises of three floors of coffeshop and bar with pool tables, tv's with movie requests, and actual Indian teepees which one can sit under and enjoy the fascinating American Indian interior design. A must for any Zandvoort visitor. Reno
