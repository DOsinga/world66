---
title: "Things to do in Zandvoort"
type: section
---

While visiting **Zandvoort**,** things to do** will include
things that you can do in most every town in the Netherlands, but will
also include some things that can only be done in Zandvoort. Knowing
what you can do in the town of Zandvoort can help you plan out your
vacation.

**Car Races**

If you love watching NASCAR back home, you must make at least one
trip out to the car races in Zandvoort. The racetrack can be found on
Burgemeester van Alphenstraat in the roundabout on the left, but if you
cannot find it, any local will be able to point you in the proper
direction. Watching the races is a unique experience that you can fully
appreciate while the sounds of the engines are rumbling deep into your
ears.

**Zandvoorts Museum**

If you are interested in history, the museum should be one of the
first stops on your list. Inside the museum, you will be able to
discover the history behind the village of Zandvoort, which was once
known as a fisherman’s town. You can see firsthand what the houses
looked like, and you can also see the original fishing equipment that
they used. The museum is open Wednesday through Sunday from 1 pm to 5
pm.

**Yanks Indian Club**

If you are looking for a great place to grab a drink or relax while
smoking, Yanks is the place to go. One of the best things about Yanks is
that even if you don’t drink or smoke, you can still come in for a
visit and enjoy yourself. The club also features a video corner where
you can watch a movie while visiting with friends. The movies are
subject to change, so you can call ahead or look at their website to see
what is currently playing.

**Beach**

Swimming at the beach is the most popular recreational activity in
town, but you want to be careful where you go swimming. There are miles
of beaches located along the coast, but between poles 68 to 71 on the
South end of the coast, there is a designated nude beach. Aside from
swimming, you can also take part in kite surfing or other water
activities. One thing that you have to do is have a bite of fresh fish
that is served off the trucks that cruise the beach.

**Kerkstreet**

This main street (and side streets) provide the best shopping around
town. To get here, you can either drive into town or you can walk in
from the beach. If walking up the beach, you will want to look for the
casino and towerflats before turning into town. The streets in this area
are filled with shops and restaurants.
