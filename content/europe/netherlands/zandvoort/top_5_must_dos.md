---
title: "Top 5 Must Do's in Zandvoort"
type: section
---

Zandvoort is a lovely resort town in the Netherlands, and there are
several **Zandvoort must do’s **that should not be missed. The town
has several beautiful beaches, a racing circuit, and quite an extensive
history. You can quite easily visit Zandvoort from Amsterdam or Haarlem.
**1. Relax on the Beach

**

The beach is the main attraction in Zandvoort. It has beautiful white
sand and is perfect for swimming, sailing, surfing or just relaxing.
The beach stretches for seven kilometers and contains a nature reserve
and a naturalist beach. Along the beach are about thirty-eight
pavilions, which provide showers, food and activities for those that
have come to the beach. At the very south end of the beach is a
three-kilometer stretch that is strictly a nudist beach.
**2. Holland Casino

**

The Zandvoort Casino is right along the beach. This Holland casino is
right on the beach in a futuristic, glass-looking building. You can
find a lot of different games to play, and there are options for those
looking to spend big or those that just have a few Euros to play with.
You can find slot machines, American Roulette, Blackjack, Caribbean
Stud, and Punto Banco, to name a few.
**3. Formula 1 Racing

**

The Formula 1 Racing Circuit is very famous throughout Europe. The
very first race occurred at this circuit in 1939. Eventually, more than
112 hectares of dunes were converted into a racing track that runs four
kilometers in length. The circuit is still an exciting place, as there
are many races and events that go on throughout the year. Many people
make a weekend trip to Zandvoort just for the racing.
**4. Play Games at the Circus

**

At the center of town is the Zandvoort Circus. This is a huge
amusement hall built in 1986 that contains all different sorts of
electronic games. The circus contains slot machines, a cinema, arcades,
and more. There is something for everyone. This is the perfect spot for
when the rain drives everyone indoors.
**5. The Beauty of the Dunes

**

The Zuid-Kennemerland Nature Park is not to be missed, as it
encompasses the dune area along the coast of the Netherlands. There are
many activities within the par; you can find paths for cycling, hiking,
horse riding and even swimming. There are many animals and plants to
find, as many animals make their homes in the dunes, peat polders and
dune forests. This spot was once wet dune slacks, but much of the ground
water was drained away. The country is now trying to re-establish the
same groundwater levels.
