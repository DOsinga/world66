**Waterside market**

The rain can sometimes be so bad that little is to be found here but on anything like a reasonable day this is a great place to be. It is sometimes thought of as a fish market but there are also stalls with knitted goods, flowers, fruit, vegetables and other things.

However if you are mainly there to look, it is hte fish stalls which will probably take your attention: vacuum packed salmon to take home and jars of smaller delicacies, all sorts for self-catering and a fine selection of stuff for you to put in sandwiches - or ready-made sandwiches if you prefer. Unless you are wildly keen to buy them ready made, you should also look in the adjacent baker's shop!
