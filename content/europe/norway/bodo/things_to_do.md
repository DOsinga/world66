Bodø's surroundings are exceptional for outdoor activities. The coastline and offshore archipelago are ideal for sea kayaking, and the mountain terrain behind the city suits hiking at all levels — there are routes suitable for families as well as more serious ridge walks. Nordland Tursselskap organises guided trips and glacier hikes to Svartisen. The Norwegian Mountain Touring Association (DNT) and its local affiliate BOT have marked an extensive network of trails, all identified by the red "T" waymarks.

The Norwegian Mountain Touring Association (DNT) and the Bodø Regional Hiking Association (BOT) maintain marked trails throughout Nordland, all signed with a red "T".
