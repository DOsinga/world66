The area around Bodø has a wide range of walking possibilities, both along the coast through the offshore archipelago and in the mountain terrain behind the city. Routes suit all abilities, including families with young children.

For guided trips or glacier hikes at Svartisen, contact Nordland Tursselskap (tel: +47 90 63 60 86, www.nordlandturselskap.no). For self-guided routes, DNT (the Norwegian Mountain Touring Association) and its local affiliate BOT maintain an extensive network of marked trails across the region. Main routes are marked with a red "T".
