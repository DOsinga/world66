Norway green
tours – budget travel in Norway

Norway is famous for its beautiful fjord landscape, but is generally regarded
as an expensive destination. Norway Green Tours gives you advice on budget
travel in Norway that is environmentally friendly. You get a tour suggestion
with information on destinations, transport, activities and hostel
accommodation. Norway Green tours, is an initiative of the Norwegian branch of
Hostelling International. Visit Norway Green Tours on http://www.norwaygreentours.com
