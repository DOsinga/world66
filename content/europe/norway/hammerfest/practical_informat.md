---
title: "Practical Information in Hammerfest"
type: section
---

If you go there in the winter, make sure you take plenty clothes and don't forget a hat and gloves. When I was there it was 17th January and there was very strong winds, I had my bare hands exposed for about 30 seconds to take some photos and my fingers were stinging like mad! (Stan Bruce 17th January 2006)
