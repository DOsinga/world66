### [Anna Rogde schonner sailing!](/europe/norway/harstad/tours_and_excursio/anna_rogde_schonne)

Anna Rodge is the Worlds oldest "still sailing" schooner.

The ship is
situated in the town of Harstad. You can rent it and take it out to
sail. (The ship is about 92 feet).

If you want to see north Norway in the best possible way, you should check out the link below. Includes e-booking form.
