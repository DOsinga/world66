title: "Getting Around in Kirkenes"
type: section
---

Even though Kirkenes is a small town with only a few thousand inhabitants, there is still a lot to see. Various **Kirkenes transportation** methods are available for visitors. Each of these ways of transportation allows you to effectively get to where you need to go and experience all the area has to offer.

#### Taxi

Finding a taxi in Kirkenes may not be very easy, but if you&rsquo;re able to get one, it would get you from one place to the next in pretty good time, considering the town is small. You can find a taxi when you are in the town center, and you can also call the taxi service to schedule a pick-up time. However, if you are coming to town from the airport, taking a taxi to town would be a good idea for the 13 kilometer ride.

#### Rental Car

Having your own rental car is another great form of Kirkenes transportation. You do not need a special license to rent a car in the city. If you are not familiar with kilometers, you may want to first learn the differences in distances to understand the signage. With a rental car, you also have the freedom of choosing the routes you want to take, since you may wish to take the more scenic routes which take longer. You can find various car rental services at the airport or at one of the hotels in the area. Your fees will depend on how long you wish to have the car.
