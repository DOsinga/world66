---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment in Kirkenes"
type: section
---

**Kirkenes nightlife **will provide you with experiences that you will never forget.

**Sollia Gjestegard**

If you are interested in enjoying just dinner or even a party, this place has two restaurants. You can choose from the main menu available which includes: king crab, lamb, whale steak, reindeer and seafood. There are also various wines and drinks to choose from. They also cater for vegetarians but you have to book in advance. The resort also has a spa where you can relax before having your dinner.

**The Snow Hotel**

Kirkenes nightlife should include visiting this hotel built entirely of ice. You may visit the heated Lavvo restaurant located within the hotel where you can enjoy your dinner. For an evening drink, there is the Snow Bar where you can relax and enjoy your favorite cocktail. Make sure you carry warm clothing (thermal) as the temperatures are below zero. This hotel also provides overnight accommodations which are in a setting that you will not get anywhere else in the world.

**Ofelas Pub & Disco**

It is a very famous jazz bar with a dance floor. This is the place to spend your Friday or Saturday night. The crowd found here is mostly the older generation. The disco plays most of the favorite oldies and special requests. You will also enjoy your favorite cocktail or beer at the bar.

**Havna Pub**

This pub faces the harbor and is a place where you can really have a good time. Most of the sea crews like to spend their evenings here playing various games while enjoying their drinks. It is located on Johan Knudtzens Gate. The place is open from Wednesday to Sunday from 3 p.m. to 1 p.m.

**Ritz **

For the young at heart, pay a visit to this exclusive club and disco. Every Saturday and Sunday the resident DJ will keep you on your feet all night long. The ambiance is superb and the lighting and sound system are very modern. You will find this club on Dr Wessels Gate 17.

**Arctic Restaurant and Bar**

Kirkenes nightlife is not complete without your paying a visit to this fine establishment located inside the Rica Arctic Hotel. The place is very spacious and you will enjoy all kinds of international and local cuisines. This is the perfect place to have dinner with your loved one or friends. After your meal you can enjoy a drink at the bar while listening to your favorite cool music.
