title: "Shopping in Kirkenes"
type: section
---

This guide to **Kirkenes shopping** will provide you with detailed information on the best places to shop in the city, as well as useful tips. Kirkenes is a municipality in Finnmark County. There are various shops in Kirkenes that you can go to, most of which are located in the city center.

#### Kirkenes Shopping Center (Kirkenes Senter)

One of the main shopping malls in the city is the Kirkenes Senter. This shopping mall has over 20 different shops that offer a wide variety of products from clothes, sport equipment, shoes, pharmacy, perfume shops and salons. The Kirkenes Shopping Center is located in the town center close to some of the restaurants in the city.

#### Fretex

Take time to explore one of the second hand shops in Kirkenes called Fretex. This second-hand store offers a wide selection of products from shoes, bags and clothes. Most of the items are affordable. However, it is advisable to check each item first before you purchase them.

#### Sparkjop

Another shopping center in Kirkenes is Sparkjop, which features a wide selection of shops and boutiques. The shops in Sparkjop are divided into several categories including children&rsquo;s wear, women and men, house and home, as well as entertainment.

#### High 96

Situated near Kirkenes is High 96. This souvenir shop offers a large collection of special souvenirs including Russian souvenirs. This shop is located near the Russian border and is a must-see for all visitors of Kirkenes.

#### Grensekiosken at Storskog

Another notable souvenir shop is Grensekiosken at Storskog. This souvenir shop is home to a large selection of both Norwegian and Russian souvenirs. This souvenir shop is located near the border of Storskog, a few kilometers away from Kirkenes center.

#### AMFI Shopping Mall

AMFI Shopping Mall is another major shopping destination in Kirkenes. It is also a major tourist attraction in the city. Visitors can also find several international and local shops in this mall. The post office, pharmacy and liquor store is also found in AMFI Shopping Mall. The mall is situated in the center of the town.

#### Port shops

To experience the true Kirkenes shopping experience, visitors should visit one of the shops located along the port area. Visitors can find a variety of products being sold in these shops. Aside from souvenir shops, one can also find establishments that sell skiing accessories, clothes and other prime shopping items like perfume and accessories. There are also several local craft stores along the port area.
