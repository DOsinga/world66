You can expect to have a great time exploring several **Kirkenes sights** and all of the tourist attractions in the district. Kirkenes, in Finmark County, Norway, is a destination with a rich history, unique sights and great amenities fit for every vacationer.

#### The Midnight Sun

If you decide to visit Kirkenes between May 17 and July 21 of any year, you will be able to experience a unique phenomenon called &ldquo;The Midnight Sun&rdquo; (also called, &ldquo;Polar Day&rdquo;). This is when the sun is visible for a continuous 24 hours--even until midnight--any day between the said range of time. This occurrence is explained by the fact that Kirkenes is situated far north of the Arctic Circle and that the Earth&rsquo;s axis around the sun is slightly tilted. Setting aside scientific explanations, the Midnight Sun is surely an experience that you don&rsquo;t want to miss.

#### The Polar Nights

Opposite the Midnight Sun, is another unusual phenomenon that you can experience in Kirkenes. The Polar Nights is the term used to describe a day when the sun is nearly always below horizon. You can observe the Polar Nights from late November to late January. In this case, it&rsquo;s like having nights that go on for 24 hours.

#### Grenselandsmuseet

Gresnselandmuseet, or the Border Country Museum, is one of the Kirkenes sights that you should visit, especially if you have a great appreciation of the arts, culture and history. This museum features exhibits about the history of wars that affected Kirkenes over the years. You can also see representations of the district&rsquo;s mining industry. Works of the famous Sami artist, John Savio, are also showcased in the Border Country Museum. You will enjoy the relaxing ambiance of the place while feeding your abstract mind with great works of art.

#### Gabba Reindeer Safari Park

For the hiking enthusiasts, the Gabba Reindeer Safari Park is the spot to visit. You can get in touch with nature while observing wildlife and basking in the vast green landscape. Animal and nature lovers will have a great time exploring the area while being soothed by the cool and gentle winds.

#### The Aurora Borealis

This astronomical occurrence never fails to awe tourists. Believed to be an omen of bad luck during the early times, the Aurora Borealis or the Northern Lights is not your ordinary light show. Be amazed by the vivid greens, blues and reds that seem to coordinate with each other for a serene display. Don&rsquo;t forget to take some clear pictures if ever you have the chance to see these Northern Lights.

Getting the chance to see these Kirkenes sights and destinations will provide you with a great learning and vacation opportunity. Check out these attractions with your family or friends and get ready for an unusual, yet appealingly remarkable, travel experience.
