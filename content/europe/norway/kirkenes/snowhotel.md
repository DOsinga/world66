Rebuilt every December from ice blocks cut from the nearby lake, with carved ice sculptures in every room and an ice bar that serves cocktails in glasses made of — yes — ice. You can stay the night in a reindeer sleeping bag on a bed of ice, or just drop in for a tour. Open roughly December to April, before it melts.
