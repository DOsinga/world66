The Border Country Museum tells the story of Sør-Varanger: the iron mines that built Kirkenes, the brutal Second World War (the town was flattened by Luftwaffe bombings and Red Army liberation), and the Sami culture of the surrounding tundra. Includes a permanent exhibition of paintings by the Sami artist John Savio.
