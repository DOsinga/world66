Kristiansand has an international airport (Kjevik airport) which is located 16km from Kristiansand. Kristiansand (as well as the surrounding towns) can be reached by buses from the Airport.

For international travellers, most relevant are scheduled flights by KLM from Amsterdam and by SAS from Copenhagen and Oslo.
