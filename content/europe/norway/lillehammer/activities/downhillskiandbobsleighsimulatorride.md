Experience downhill skiing and bobsledding the way the athletes did during the 1994 Winter Olympics. Location: at the bottom of the hill of the Lysegårdsbakkene Ski Jumping Arena, close to the Håkon Hall  Getting there: About a 20 minutes walk from downtown Lillehammer. By bus: bus 005 to Ekromskogen stops by the Håkon Hall at the Stampesletta bus stop or bus 011 to the Mesna Vdg. skole bus stop
