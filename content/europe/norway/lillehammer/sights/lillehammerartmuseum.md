 The Lillehammer Art Museum foundation was established April, 1994. The foundation is a further development of the institution's Lillehammer City Collection of Paintings from 1927. The museum's permanent collection comprises Norwegian visual art from the 1800s up until today. It is considered one of the country's most important art museums with particularly important and representative works from the years 1820 - 1930.
