Welcome to the Lofoten Islands. Our webcam shows the wonderful
view outside our door! Here you can see the site of the mediaeval town
of Vågar and the mountain Vågakallen 942 m above sea level. As
you can see from our collection of stills , the daylight conditions
here are famtastic, so we recommend you to check back and visit our
webcam as often as possible, all year round.
