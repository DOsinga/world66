Stamsund is a fishing village on the island of Vestvågøy in [Lofoten](/europe/norway/lofotenislands), with about 1,400 inhabitants. It remains an active fishing port — the harbour, the drying racks, and the rorbuer (fishermen's cabins) along the waterfront give the place its character.

The Hurtigruten coastal express makes a regular stop at the Stamsund dock, both southbound from Svolvær and northbound from Bodø, making it one of the most scenic arrivals in Norway. Stamsund also has a surprisingly rich cultural life for its size, with two theatre groups and the Nordland Puppet Theatre.
