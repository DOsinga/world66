title: Øyfjellet Via Ferrata and Zipline
type: poi

The mountain Øyfjellet rises just across the bridge from the town centre, and on its flanks runs what is claimed to be the world's northernmost via ferrata. The iron-rung route climbs 400 metres of cliff face to the summit at 660 metres, with dramatic views over the fjord and town below. From the top, a 700-metre zipline brings you back down, landing in the gardens of Fru Haugans Hotel in the centre of Mosjøen — a genuinely memorable arrival. The activity is run by Hi North (hinorth.no) and requires booking in advance.
