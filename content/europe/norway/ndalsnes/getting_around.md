---
title: "Getting Around"
type: section
---

Åndalsnes itself is small enough to walk, but you will need wheels to reach the main attractions. Local buses run to Molde and Ålesund, and in summer the **Golden Route** bus to Geiranger operates — one of Norway's great scenic journeys, crossing the Trollstigen Pass and descending the Eagles' Highway to the fjord. The route only runs for a few months in summer, and even in June there can be snow at the top. A car gives the most flexibility for exploring the valley and mountain roads at your own pace.
