**A Plethora of Nature- Adventure in Honningsvag **

**North Cape Day Trips** are wonderful adventures into the vast expanse of the natural environment surrounding North Cape. You can engage in various day trips depending on your personal preferences. For nature lovers a trip to Honningsvag can serve as an extreme, memorable adventure.

**Honningsvag Home Port**

This home port is next to North Cape and is worth a day&rsquo;s trip. You can go by boat or by land rover to Gjesvaertappan, a small island of Honningsvag. On this island, you can watch thousands of sea birds and creatures in their natural habitat. This six-hour tour is worth $100.

**Knivskjelodden, Honningsvag**

You can also visit Knivskjelodden by car. This is the genuine northernmost point of the continent. From this point, you can hike and explore the surrounding beautiful terrain and basked in Mother Nature&rsquo;s pristine beauty and wonder. Its astounding flora and fauna can provide you an unforgettable experience. If you love the whiff of fresh air and the thrill of nature and its wild elements, then this trip is for you. Most tourists go on their own so the expenses will depend upon what they want to do. Price ranges from $87 to $203.

**North Cape Trek to Honningsvag**

During this the North Cape March Festival, the North Cape Tourist Office offers a tour from North Cape to Honningsvag covering back and forth, a 43-mile span of unadulterated exploration and sightseeing. You can personalize your trip to include activities like hiking, deep-sea fishing, sightseeing, and boat trips. The cost of the tour varies from the personal options you chose. You can prepare to spend from around $100 to $250 for this unique nature-tripping activity.
