A cluster of small islands off the western side of Magerøya whose cliffs are the summer home of enormous colonies of Atlantic puffins, razorbills, gannets, kittiwakes and white-tailed eagles. Boat safaris from the village of Gjesvær circle the islands without landing (the colonies are strictly protected). Two hours on the water with hundreds of thousands of seabirds overhead — a genuine highlight of the region.
