The actual cape: a 307-metre cliff dropping straight into the Arctic Ocean, with a steel globe monument on the edge and Nordkapphallen — a visitor centre built into the rock with a film theatre, cafe, and panoramic window onto the sea. Entry is 300+ NOK, famously expensive, but the view is genuinely worth the trip. Come just before midnight in midsummer to watch the midnight sun skim the horizon.
