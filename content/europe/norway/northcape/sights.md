You can count on a great travel experience when you explore several breath-taking **North Cape sights** and other tourist attractions in the area. North Cape, sometimes referred to as Nordkapp, is a headland in the Mager&oslash;ya island of Northern Norway. Learn about these vacation sights that you shouldn&rsquo;t miss.

#### Kirkeporten Cliffs

You have seen them in movies and on the cover of travel books. The Kirkeporten Cliffs will give you a feeling of luxury while giving you the chance to get in touch with nature. The unique rock formations that seem to be a gateway to paradise will be the perfect site for a great hike. Many establishments offer services and equipment that will let you camp in the area. Take the time to enjoy the cool breeze and the smell of the ocean while you bask in the natural beauty of the cliffs. Also, this place is famous for its amazing sunset view.

#### Nordkapphallen

This is one of the most visited tourist centers in North Cape. For just 215 NOK (Norwegian currency, USD exchange rates vary), you will get to see the artistic representation of the cape&rsquo;s landscapes, culture and history. This is best suited for those who appreciate artwork and cultural exhibits.

#### The Midnight Sun

Also known as the Polar Day, the Midnight Sun is the natural phenomenon wherein the sun is visible all throughout the day, even at midnight. You can experience this occurrence from mid-May to late July. Setting aside scientific explanations, the Midnight Sun is one of the North Cape sights that you should visit since there are only a handful of spots in the world where the Midnight Sun can be observed.

#### Children of the World Monument

Though the relevance of the location is debatable, the Children of the World Monument is a unique sight to see. It depicts a woman and her child looking at seven disks, each with a drawing made by a child from a different country. This is a great photo opportunity. Parking at this spot costs around NOK 195.

#### Nordkapp Museum

This museum, situated near the harbor, houses exhibits that can tell you a lot about the way of life on the island. This establishment caters to the enrichment of one&rsquo;s spatial skills. Exhibits are arranged in a thematic and chronologic order that helps tourists work out the message it wants to convey.

After visiting these North Cape sights and attractions, engage in several fun activities like diving, snorkeling and fishing. With these things in mind, having a complete and fulfilling travel experience will not be a problem at all.
