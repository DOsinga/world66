title: "Things to do in North Cape"
type: section
---

The **North Cape things to do** list has an element of mystery and
magic in it. Read on to find out more about this magical land in
Norway.

**Midnight Sun**

People from around the
globe travel to North Cape to witness this natural phenomenon. Filled
with mystery to the common man, the Midnight Sun is a fascinating
attraction in North Cape. Head to North Cape between May 14th and July
30th and witness the appearance of the sun, when the clock strikes 12 at
midnight. There is a valid and simple scientific explanation behind
this, but the locals regard this as a gesture from the Lords, as a
miracle from heaven. Come and experience this fascinating view.

**Northern Lights
**
The Northern Lights is another magical
exhibit by nature in North Cape. The Northern Lights are formed by the
interaction of electrons and the magnetic fields. The result is a spell
binding florescent green light spread across the sky. This generally
occurs during the winter nights. The best time to view this phenomenon
would be from September to April. In North Cape, there are areas that
are dedicated to view these lights.

**Bird Safari
**

Set out on a boat ride from Gjesvaerstappan Reserve to explore the 2
hour long bird safari. This journey will open a whole new world of
exotic birds to you. You will be taken to the island, where you will be
able to spot birds you have never seen before. Visitors are not allowed
on the island, but you get a great view of the puffins and the
kittiwakes from the boat. The breeding season is a great time go on this
safari and observe the breeding habits of the Razorbills, Arctic Skuas
and the likes.
**
Rafting****
**
This experience will
bring out the wild side in you. You will set out on an adventurous deep
sea rafting ride. This ride will take you to North Cape while stopping
at Kjelvik, the lighthouse and Hornvika. Not only will this give you the
biggest high of your life, but the rafting experience will also narrate
several local stories. It is a great way to have fun and soak in the
local culture.

**Explore**

You can set out on your own and explore various sights in North Cape.
The most famous tourist activity is to head to the northern most tip of
Europe, which is in North Cape. The Northernmost point is a plateau
with the view of the Arctic Ocean. After exploring the Northernmost
point, get your pictures taken at The Globe and head to the Children of
the Earth, which is a monument that was put in place to promote harmony
and peace in the world.
