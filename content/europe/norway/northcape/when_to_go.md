type: section
---

This **North Cape when to go **guide can give you vital information about the best months to visit this incredible place of wonder and amazement. If you want to witness the famous &ldquo;Midnight Sun Phenomenon,&rdquo; then visit North Cape in Norway. The sun never sets in this northernmost part of Europe during Midnight Sun. It is one of the most visited tourist spots because of its excellent panoramic views and natural beauty.

**When to Go**

The best months to visit North Cape for the Midnight Sun is in the summer from May 14 to July 30. During these months, you can have a full view of the sun for an unbelievable 24 hours. Imagine the variety of exciting activities you can do within this period. You can join a hiking group to the Glittertind, the most challenging hike in Norway, or experience the distinctive wild life at the very famous Jotunheimen National Park. These are some of the outdoor activities you can do during summer months. Only your lack of creativity will limit your adventure.

**Other Activities**

During winter, the Polar Nights is another phenomenon you can visit. This is the time when the sun never shines and the night never ends in certain polar circles. Another winter phenomenon is the Northern Lights or Aurora Borealis, when the sky lights up with a kaleidoscope of colors due to the interaction of the sun&rsquo;s rays with the earth&lsquo;s atmosphere. You can witness this natural phenomenon during any month, but it is more vivid during winter from November 21 to January 21.
