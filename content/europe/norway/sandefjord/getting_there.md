### [Getting there by air](/europe/norway/sandefjord/getting_there/getting_there_by_a)

By air: Ryanair flies from London Stansted to Sandefjord (or their term Oslo-Torp). Ryanair doesn't actually fly to Oslo, they fly to Sandefjord airport Torp. Ryanair also fly to Glasgow (Prestwick), Stockholm and Frankfurt (Hahn). KLM flies from here to Amsterdam. Wideroe fly to Copenhagen, Stockholm, Stavanger, Bergen and Trondheim. Coast Air fly to Haugesund. Wizzair flies from Gdansk, Katowice, Warsaw (Poland).
