---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

Sorlandet sits on the main E18 highway from Oslo, making it a straightforward drive south. The nearest airport with budget connections is Torp Sandefjord, just over two hours away, served by Ryanair from London Stansted and by KLM with connections to its wider network. Car hire is available at the airport from several companies.

From Oslo, the drive to Kristiansand takes around four hours. Norway's roads are well maintained and uncongested, and the route south passes through pleasant scenery. Tourist roads in the region have been selected for their views — mountain passes, outlook points, and coastal stretches that make the drive part of the trip.
