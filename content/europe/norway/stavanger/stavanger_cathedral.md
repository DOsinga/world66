title: Stavanger Cathedral
type: poi

Norway's oldest cathedral, with construction beginning in 1125 — making it the country's best-preserved medieval church. The building blends Romanesque and Gothic styles, with the western end showing the original Norman character and the choir reflecting later rebuilding. The interior is calm and handsome, with carved pew ends and a notable Baroque pulpit. Free to enter, and usually open during the day.
