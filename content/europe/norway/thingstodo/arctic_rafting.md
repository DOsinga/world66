Arctic Rafting is a adventure company in North of Norway close by the city of Mo i Rana.

Activities offered are: White Water Rafting, Kayaking, Guided Hiking Trips.

There are some information about other activities in the area like Horseback Riding and Sailing.

At the rafting base there is basic accommodation for backpacking people.
