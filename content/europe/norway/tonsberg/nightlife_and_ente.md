---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment in Tonsberg"
type: section
---

Tønsberg is the capital of nightlife and entertainment offers in the summertime. Go see one of the many standup shows or concerts, or visit the popular bars or restaurants by night. Enjoy a beer while watching the many luxurious boats that come to the town every summer to party. The town is known throughout the country for its good nightlife and atmosphere.
