type: section

Tromso is the seventh largest city in Norway, within the Arctic Circle. Tromso is
home to the Northernmost University in the world, and being a college
town, it has an abundance of nightlife and entertainment options.
Some refer to Tromso as "The Paris of the North."

**Storgata**

This is a thriving entertainment area within Tromso, and home to
many pubs, bars, restaurants and cafes. Many locals flock to Storgata
during lunchtime. Weekend nights get into
full swing with live entertainment, and even on Sundays you will find a
wind down kind of atmosphere due to all the weekend activities. If you
are looking for a variety of choices within close proximity, then head
to Storgata.

**Bla Rock**

Bla Rock** **in Strandgata is referred to as a Rock n Roll bar.
Bla Rock has different bars inside located on many levels, the beer
selection is decent, and they have occasional concerts and a resident DJ on
the weekends, as well as tons of rock and roll artifacts scattered
throughout the bar. Bla Rock also has a bar menu with mostly
sandwiches, wraps and salads.**
