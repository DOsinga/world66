---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

The Arctic Cathedral (Ishavskatedralen) across the Tromsø Bridge is the city's most photographed building — its triangular aluminum facade and enormous stained-glass window are unlike anything else in Norway. The Fjellheisen cable car ascends 420 metres to Storsteinen for panoramic views of the fjords and islands; in summer the top station stays busy past midnight with Midnight Sun watchers. The Tromsø Museum covers Sami culture and northern Norwegian history, the Polar Museum documents Arctic hunting and exploration, and Polaria has a walkthrough seal aquarium and a panoramic Arctic cinema. The Arctic-Alpine Botanical Garden — the world's northernmost — is free and best in July when the alpine plants are in flower.
