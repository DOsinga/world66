This museum documents the wool industry that made Bielsko-Biała prosperous. Four rooms trace the production process — from raw material preparation through weaving to finishing — with original machines, equipment, and documents. A separate section covers hat-making machinery, and there are side exhibitions on the city's fire brigade and water supply system. A small printing office and a "museum granary" full of old household equipment, radios, and typewriters round out the collection. If you have any interest in industrial history, this is a worthwhile visit.
