---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Bielsko-Biała's attractions cluster around its textile and artistic heritage. The castle museum is the obvious starting point, but the city's real character emerges in its smaller museums — a reconstructed weaver's house, a collection of industrial machinery, and an artist's villa in a nearby village. The old town is pleasant for a wander, and the hills are never far away.
