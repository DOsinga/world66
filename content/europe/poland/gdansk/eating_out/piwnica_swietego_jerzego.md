---
title: "Piwnica Swietego Jerzego"
type: poi
address: "Targ Weglowy 27, Gdansk"
latitude: 54.3497
longitude: 18.6481
---

A cellar restaurant just outside the Golden Gate, serving straightforward Polish food — pork cutlets, bigos, pierogi — at prices well below those inside the old town walls. Good value for a filling lunch before a day's sightseeing. Go through the Golden Gate heading west, turn right immediately, and head down the stairs.
