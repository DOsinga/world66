---
title: Getting Around
type: section
---

Gniezno is a compact city and the main sights are all walkable from the town centre. The cathedral district on Lech Hill is about ten minutes on foot from the market square. For getting to and from other cities, trains and buses are reliable and cheap.

**Railway station**

ul. Dworcowa 13 — regular trains to Poznań (around 40 minutes), with connections onwards to Warsaw and other major cities.

**Bus station**

ul. Dworcowa 15 — regional buses to surrounding towns and villages.

**Taxis**

Ranks at ul. Dworcowa, ul. Mieszka I, ul. Mickiewicza, ul. Staszica, and ul. Winiary. Radio taxis available on 919 or 426 41 61.
