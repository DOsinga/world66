---
title: "Bars and Cafes"
type: section
---

Katowice has a lively café and bar scene concentrated around ul. Mariacka — a pedestrian street east of the Rynek that comes alive in the evenings. The city has benefited from an influx of young people drawn by its creative scene and affordable rents, and the bars reflect that: independent, unpretentious, and generally good value by Polish standards.
