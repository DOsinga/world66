Housed in a historic townhouse on ul. Armii Krajowej, this museum covers both the military history of the fortress city and a broader survey of Polish weaponry through the centuries. It also hosts the permanent exhibition "The History of the Town," which traces Kołobrzeg from its early medieval origins as a salt-trading port through to the devastation and rebuilding of the 20th century.
