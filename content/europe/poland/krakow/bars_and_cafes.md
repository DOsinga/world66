This **Krakow Bars and Cafes** list will provide you with helpful information about the must-see and must-experience cafes and bars in Krakow. Known for its vodkas and quality beers, Krakow can be found in the Malopolskie Province, situated on the banks of the Wisla River in southern Poland. As you visit the city's streets, you come across many places that have bars in the basement and other clubs upstairs.

**Nic Nowego**

Nic Nowego, or "Nothing New" in English, is a modern Irish pub located in Old Town. It is a comfortable place with a friendly atmosphere. You can enjoy a wide selection of drinks and a couple of Irish ales here. The Irish barman provides great mixtures of drinks that could suit your taste buds. Aside from their drinks, you can also appreciate their art and photo gallery as you dig in to your Irish breakfast or your fresh seafood meal. In addition, Wi-Fi access was recently added to suit the preferences of both the younger crowd and professionals alike. This pub is open as early as 7 a.m., and stays open until the last guest leaves.

**Boogie Ulica Szpitalna**

This exciting sensational caf&eacute; is known for its vegetarian treats, such as smoothies and salads, and yummy snacks. You can also try a variety of alcoholic drinks including cocktails, beers and wines. You can enjoy the ambience as you watch great flicks on their screen. Boogie Ulica Szpitalna is open from 10 a.m. to 1 a.m. on weekdays, and 10am until the last guest leaves on weekends.
