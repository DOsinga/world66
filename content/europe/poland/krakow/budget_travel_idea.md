---

If you are going to travel to Krakow on a very tight budget, then it is important that you have a** Krakow budget travel **plan. This will help you enjoy the sights and sounds in Krakow without overspending. Krakow is a very beautiful city in Poland which attracts a lot of tourists every year. If you are one of the people who wants to visit Krakow but are running low in cash, then these budget travel ideas can help you have fun in Krakow.

**Budget Transportation**

The public transportation in Krakow consists of buses, trains and trams, and their ticket prices range from $0.7 to $7 depending on the distance of the destination you are going to. Thus, traveling around the city is a bit expensive using these public transportation options.

**Monuments in Krakow**

The Best thing about Krakow is that its monumental sites are located near one another, so it is really easy for you to get to your destination. You can walk the entire Royal Way from St. Florian&rsquo;s Gate and traverse it to get to the Wawel Castle. Once you are there, you can walk towards Plantry to visit the Old Town of Krakow. The best thing about these sites is that admission is totally free, which presents another advantage for a budget traveler like you. However, you need to arm yourself with a map and a reliable pair of sneakers and start walking to your destination.

**Budget Shopping**

If you want to bring mementos to your friends back home, then you should head off to the Old Town District for some shopping. The great thing about the market in the Old Town is that you will be able to haggle with the shop owners.

**Cheap Food**

To save money on your food expenses, then you should dine in the Bar Mleczny which is a kind of cafeteria which is prevalent during the communist rule. The food is delicious and reasonably priced too.

**Budget Accommodations**

Accommodations in Krakow can be very expensive, but if you sleep in a hostel, then you can cut back the cost of your accommodation into half. There are a lot of hostels that are located all over the city. Most hostels come with a comfortable bed as well as a restaurant. Accommodations in a hostel range from $20 to $30 per day.
