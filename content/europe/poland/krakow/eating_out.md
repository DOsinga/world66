There are many good **Krakow restaurants** that offer different types of food and varying prices. Krakow is famous because of its lavish and tasty dishes and restaurants.

#### Wierzynek Restaurant

There are many restaurants that offer Polish delicious food items in Krakow at various prices. One of the restaurants is Wierzynek restaurant situated at Rynek Glowny, 15 Market Square Krakow Poland. This restaurant has a good reputation with respect to great Polish food and offers a traditional menu. It offers deal meals for two, with wine at 40 Euro.

#### Krzyzykiem Restaurant

This restaurant is situated near Wierzynek restaurant at Rynek Glowny 39/40 Market Square Krakow. It also offers a variety of Polish dishes at very reasonable prices. Tourists love to visit this restaurant, and because of this, Krzyzykiem is a tasty addition to Krakow restaurants.

#### Trzy Papryczki

Trzy Papryczki is situated at Poselska 17 Krakow, which is a street in old town. It offers the best variety of pizzas, and its interior environment and the garden inside makes the lunch and dinner very enjoyable. The overall service is very good and prices are very reasonable.

#### Fabryka

Fabryka is situated at Jozefa street. This is another place to get good pizza and other varieties of Italian dishes. The internal environment is very pleasant and attractive, and provides an enjoyable dinner, breakfast or lunch.

#### Szara

This restaurant is situated at Rynek Glowny 6 Market Square Krakow, and it is considered one of the best restaurants in this city. It has beautiful construction and a friendly environment. Its specialties are Raraka, Caviar and Bouillabaisse soup. This is one of the most luxurious Krakow Restaurants.
