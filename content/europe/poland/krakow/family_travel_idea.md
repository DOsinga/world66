There are a lot of **Krakow family travel** ideas that you can include if you are traveling to Krakow with your entire family. The city of Krakow is located in the southern portion of Poland and is famous for its scenic sites like the Wawel Castle and Kazimierz. A family vacation in Krakow is very interesting because there are a lot of attractions that can be enjoyed by adults and children. So, here are some of the travel ideas that your family can do in Krakow.

**The Fire-Breathing Dragon**

Wawel Castle is not only a historic site that adults like to visit, but there are also a lot of fun things that are in store for the young travelers inside the castle. Before you get a yawn from your children, telling you that visiting the state rooms inside the Wawel Castle is boring, you can go to the foot of the castle by the Wisla which is also called the Dragon&rsquo;s Lair to see a fire-breathing dragon. Only the dragon&rsquo;s head is mobile thanks to robotics; it will surely excite your kids and probably you as well.

**Groteska Theater**

Children love magic and this is the reason why you need to take them to the Groteska Theater to see special magic shows. However, the shows only take place on Sundays, but it is still a great way to end your children&rsquo;s weekend.

**The Fantasy Park**

If you want to have a little family bonding time with your kids, then the best way to do it is to play sports in Fantasy Park. Fantasy Park houses a bowling alley, a pool, air hockey tables and video arcades. You can leave your kids playing in the arcade while you also go to the bar inside the park to get some real adult beverages like beer and wine.

**The Market Square**

The Market Square is the perfect place in Krakow for both kids and adults to enjoy. While adults can buy and haggle with unique souvenir items that they can take back home, children can enjoy chasing and feeding the pigeons. Moreover, your entire family can enjoy watching street performers that are numerous in the square while enjoying some cotton candies which can be bought in the many market stalls that surround the market square.
