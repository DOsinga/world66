---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment in Krakow"
type: section
---

If you think this city cannot offer more than its rich culture and
artistic attributes, you might want to think it over as the **Krakow
Nightlife** is one that any tourist shouldn’t dare to overlook. More
than the historical sites most visitors go to, Krakow also boasts of its
extremely alive entertainment industry at night. Here are some
suggestions of spots you might want to check out.

**Buddha
Drink and Garden**

As its name implies, this spot is the best
place to be if you want to just chill out and relax your night away.
Listen to jazz music as you savor in the oriental and cozy ambiance in
the Buddha Drink and Garden. Order a drink with your friends or with
your partner and enjoy the rest of your day relaxed.

**Fusion**

However, if you are in for some more lively music such as hip hop,
then you might want to include Fusion in your list. This club is most
popular among people who love to party and dance all night long. Song
remixes are also done every Fridays, by UK and Jamaican DJs. So, if
dancing is your type of relaxation, visit Fusion and enjoy meeting new
acquaintances.

**Midgard**

Unlike some clubs in
Krakow which only let the rich in, Midgrad is considered as one of the
best clubs that’s ever opened in the city for it can generate money
without imposing boundaries on clients. If you want to chill, dance or
listen to good techno music, you may go at Midgard and have fun the
rest of the night. Go with your friends and enjoy partying to the
fullest.

**Rdza**

If you are looking for some music
variations, you can go to Rdza and watch good performances from famous
artists all over the globe. From bass to drum acts, this spots offers
an alternative to those tourists who want to some serious hardcore
music for their trip. Try going to this club and make friends with
locals or with fellow tourist. Drinks are offered at reasonable prices
and by a surprisingly friendly staff.

**Goraczka**

Who could have never heard of the week long party club in Krakow? Well,
Goraczka is just a complete representation of the city as it doesn’t
sleep for the whole week. From Mondays to Sundays, you are sure to find a
lively crowd in this club. So, dress up in your best party outfit and
get ready to dance to good party music and learn to make new friends.
