---
title: "Shopping"
type: section
---

The Cloth Hall (Sukiennice) in the middle of the Market Square has been a trading floor since the Middle Ages and still houses stalls selling amber jewelry, folk crafts, and souvenirs. For more genuine craft shopping, explore the streets of Kazimierz, which has a growing number of design and vintage shops. The Sunday Stary Kleparz market, a 10-minute walk north of the Old Town, is a real local institution for fresh produce, flowers, and occasional antiques.
