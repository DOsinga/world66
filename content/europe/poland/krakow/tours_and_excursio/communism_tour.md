Once in a while, there are amazing tours that are so off the beaten
path, and offer such a strange mix of culture and history, that are so
bad, they're good. This is where Krakow Communism Tours comes into play.

The
idea behind the tour is to give the tourists a authentic communist
experience in Krakow; so they have found several old communist era cars
that they load you into, and give you a behind the scenes experience.
Check out their most amazing tour, Communism Tour and Disco:

"We
start in the afternoon with our standard Communism Tour, upgraded with
a visit to our authentic Communist apartment. After the tour the party
starts! Enjoy a VIP reservation at a Nowa Huta restaurant, unchanged
since the 1970s, and tuck in to a huge Polish dinner –during which you
can sample Polski beers and five different shots of vodka! Next, in the
same venue, it’s time for a very retro boogie at a real 1980s-style
Commie disco. A truly unique experience. Don’t worry about overdoing it
– our man, Mr. Vieslav (this time correctly attired) will be on hand to
drive you home."
Fantastic!!

The Commie Tour and Disco Includes: Standard Communism Tour in a Trabant.
First-hand details of what it was like living under Soviet occupation. Lunch in an authentic Commie milk bar.
Trabant driving lesson.
Visit to our authentic Communist apartment.
Communist worker Mr.Vieslav.
Delicious 2 course polish dinner.
Booze (2 beers and 5 vodkas on us!).
Disco entrance fee.
Mr.Vieslav's Commie disco moves.
