---
title: "When to Go"
type: section
---

May through September is the sweet spot: warm weather, long days, and a packed calendar of outdoor events and concerts. July and August bring the biggest crowds — the Market Square can feel overwhelmed, and accommodation books up fast. Late spring (May-June) and early autumn (September-October) offer good weather with fewer tourists and better prices. Winter is cold and sometimes snowy but has its own appeal: fewer visitors, lower prices, and a Christmas market on the square in December.
