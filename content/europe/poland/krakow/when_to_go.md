---
title: "When to Go in Krakow"
type: section
---

**Krakow when to go** is very important if you want to make the best out of your holiday. Depending on what type of activities you are looking to enjoy will determine what season you want to travel in.

**Summer**

The summer months in Krakow are May through October. During these months you will find that the weather is warm but very pleasant. July and August are the peak tourist time in Poland so keep that in mind when booking your trip. If you are looking for travel discounts traveling in May, June, September or October will give you the best discounts. You will find that during the months of July and August major tourist attractions are very crowded. Traveling before or after the peak season will allow you to have more privacy at some of the top sights.

**Autumn**

Autumn overlaps with the summer months in Krakow. September through November are considered Autumn months. The weather starts off very warm and as it gets closer to November it starts to cool down. Autumn is not a busy time in Poland which makes it a great time to travel.

**Winter**

The winters in Poland run from December to March with January and February being the coldest months. Some years Poland receives significant snow fall and other years there is hardly any snow at all. Winters tend to be very cold and very wet. You will find that you can get some fantastic travel packages during the winter months.
