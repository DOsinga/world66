Mragowo is a small town in Poland's Masurian Lake District, wedged between lakes Juno and Czos. It makes a good base for exploring the surrounding countryside of forests and waterways.

The town centre has a daily market where you can stock up on local Polish products, and a handful of restaurants with outdoor terraces in summer. Every August, Mragowo hosts the Piknik Country festival, one of Europe's largest country and western music events — an unlikely but genuinely fun draw in the middle of the Polish lake country.
