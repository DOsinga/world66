### [Poland Just Now recommends:](/europe/poland/top_5_must_dos/poland_just_now_re)

1 Visit towns like Warszawa, Krakow, Wroclaw, Poznan and Gdansk.
2 Go by car across Mazovia, Pomerania, Pomerelia, Silesia and Lesser Poland regions.
3 Hike in Carpathian Mountains, ride a bike along the Baltic Sea, take a yacht in one of Lake Districts, watch European bisons and ski in Sudetes.
4 Check diversity of Łowicz, Kurpie, Podhale, Kashubia and Upper Silesia cultural events.
5 Discover famous places like Malbork castle, Torun Old City, Wieliczka Salt Mine, Auschwitz Nazi Concentration Camp and Jasna Gora Monastery.
