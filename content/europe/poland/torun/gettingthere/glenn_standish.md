The closest airport to Torun is the international airport in Bydgoszcz (about 50kms west of the city). There are direct flights from London Stansted, Liverpool, Dublin, Birmingham and Dusseldorf with Ryanair. There are also flights to Berlin with Jet Air.

For more info on the Ryanair flights: www.ryanair.com
