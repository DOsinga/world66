Warsaw is well connected to several European cities. Some connections are:

[Berlin](/europe/germany/berlin) ([Germany](/europe/germany)): day trains "Berlin Warszawa Express" (3 times a day) from Berlin Hbf and night trains Berlin Lichtenberg -Moscow/Saratov/Petersburg.

[Frankfurt](/europe/germany/hessen/frankfurt) ([Germany](/europe/germany)) (via Cologne, Hannover, Berlin Ostbf.): night train EN Jan Kiepura

[Prague](/europe/czechrepublic/prague) ([Czech Republic](/europe/czechrepublic)): 2 night trains from Praha hl. n., 2 day trains (EuroCity "Praha" ) from Praha hl.n.

[Budapest](/europe/hungary/budapest) ([Hungary](/europe/hungary)): 1 day train ("EC Jozef Bem") , 1 night train ("Chopin/Galileo Galilei"), both from Budapest Keleti

[Vienna](/europe/austria/vienna) ([Austria](/europe/austria)): day trains EC "Sobieski" and "Polonia" and night train "Chopin" from Vienna Sudbahnhof

former Soviet Union states: various night trains from Moscow Belorusskaya, Petersburg, Kyjiv, Minsk, Chisinau, Odessa, Novosibirsk, Astana, etc.
