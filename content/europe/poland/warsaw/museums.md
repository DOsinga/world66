### Center of Contemporary Art (CSW)

Center of Contemporary Art is the place where you can see modern art from Poland and the rest of the world. It's situated near Łazienki Królewskie Park, on the crossroads of Trakt Królewski (the Royal Road) and Trasa Łazienkowska.

Admission free on Thursday!
