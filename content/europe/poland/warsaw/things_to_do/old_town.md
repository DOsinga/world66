The historic heart of Warsaw, painstakingly rebuilt after being completely destroyed by the Germans in 1944. The colourful Renaissance and Baroque townhouses, the market square, the Royal Castle, and the Cathedral of St John are all reconstructions completed between the 1950s and 1980s. The result is a remarkably faithful recreation that functions as both living city and memorial.
