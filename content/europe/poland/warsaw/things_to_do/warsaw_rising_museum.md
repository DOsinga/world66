The most important museum in Warsaw, covering the 63-day Warsaw Uprising of 1944 when the Polish Home Army rose against the German occupation. The collection is vast and the presentation is very good — genuinely moving rather than merely instructional. Plan at least two hours; three is better. Essential for anyone wanting to understand modern Polish history and identity.
