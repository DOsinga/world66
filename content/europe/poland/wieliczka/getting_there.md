---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

Wieliczka is easy to reach from [Kraków](/europe/poland/krakow), just 14 kilometres away, and most visitors arrive as a day trip.

**By train:** Direct trains run from Kraków Główny (main station) to Wieliczka Rynek-Kopalnia, the stop closest to the mine. The journey takes about 25 minutes and costs around 2.50 zł with a standard zone ticket (*bilet strefowy*), valid for two hours. Trains run frequently throughout the day. See [PKP Intercity](http://www.pr.pkp.pl) for timetables.

**By minibus:** Minibuses (busy) run regularly from the stop near Kraków's main train station and drop you directly at the mine entrance — often faster and more convenient than the train.

**By organised tour:** Many tour operators in Kraków offer half-day guided tours to the mine, which include transport and a guide inside.
