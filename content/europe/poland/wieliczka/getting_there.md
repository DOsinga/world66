How to get to Wieliczka?

### [Local Trains](/europe/poland/wieliczka/getting_there/local_trains)

There are direct trains from Cracow. The trip lasts 25 minutes and a normal ticket costs 2,50 zł ("bilet strefowy", valid 2 hours)
