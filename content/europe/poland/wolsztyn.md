Wolsztyn is a city in Wielkopolskie region, situated between Poznan and Zielona Gora. It is famous for the steam locomotive depot which is working all year round. It's the only place in Poland where you can go by a steam train, paying a normal ticket! (routes: Poznań-Wolsztyn, Wolsztyn-Leszno) Another attraction is the open-air museum of old country housing. Wolsztyn is famous also for being a birthplace of the famous scientist Robert Koch.
