We are three tourist guides, living in Gdańsk - North Poland.



We have licenses to guide around Gdansk, Sopot, Gdynia as well as the Malbork Castle. 



We are, so far, among the only six guides in Poland, who are licensed to guide both around the Tri-Cities Area AND around the Teutonic castle of Malbork (which, by the way, is the biggest gothic brick castle.). 



I am sure, I do not have to present Pomerania Region to you, as now it is quite famous abroad. Poland, so far, was Europe's best kept secret. So it is time to reveal the beauty of my Region.



Gdansk and Gdynia were always present in the world’s history – as here the II World War stared, here are one of the better prospering seaports in Europe; Shipyards, being the Vth in the world’s register of shipbuilding, and – here the Solidarity movement started, which had changed the political history of Europe. 



The Teutonic Castle in Malbork – enlisted in the UNESCO list of the World Heritage. The biggest brick gothic castle in the worldIt is an absolutely magic place – you have to see it.



We gladly show all that magic and beauty, as besides being guides, we are enchanted with the places we live in. 



We operate in English and German. Polish is our native language.



We have experience with various tourist groups such as government guests, cruise ships groups, coach groups, retired and children, Polish and international. 



We help in organising the stays and show the wonders of our region - to the individuals, as well as to the organized groups of tourists.
