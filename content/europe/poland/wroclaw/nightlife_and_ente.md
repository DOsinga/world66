Nightlife in wrocław starts just after 7pm, on weekends it may be hard to get a free table after 8pm in most popular pubs.
once u get to markret square (RYNEK) u will see many pubs&restaurants screaming for you.
***PUBS***
wrocławs** speciality** is **beer** often served with raspberry syroup
the **oldest local bee**r is brewed in SPIŻ pub right in the middle of RYNEK
also go to PIWNICA ŚWIDNICKA, the oldest pub in poland
ask for **WŁODKOWICA street **where many mysterious pubs are hidden : MLECZARNIA, PUB STARE MIASTO ...

nearby the ARKADY shopping mall u may find a railway, under which there are plenty **tiny pubs** with great amosphere and wide variety of drinks and beers. nowhere else u can find a place like this !
