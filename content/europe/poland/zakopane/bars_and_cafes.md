type: section
---

Tourists can find many **Zakopane bars and cafes** in and around the town center. The variety of choices ensures that every tourist can find the perfect place to spend their evenings in this beautiful Polish city.

**U- Boot

**Located in the town&rsquo;s center on Droga do Bialego 3 is one of the liveliest bars in Zakopane. It serves all kinds of alcohols and cocktails and good food too. Visitors can also enjoy bowling or playing pool here while sipping on their drinks. Some parts of the place resemble a hi-tech submarine. If you&rsquo;re looking for something more entertaining, visit this bar on the weekends, when it turns into a disco.

**Paparazzi

**This is one of the most popular bars in Zakopane and is also situated in the town center on Generala Galicy 8. The bar has a casual atmosphere, good d&eacute;cor, laid back music and great classic and exotic cocktails. Paparazzi Bar is the perfect place to spend the evening with good company and drinks. This bar also has sister bars in Warsaw and Cracow.

**Caf&eacute; Wiedenska

**If you are looking for a place to enjoy cakes, coffees and light snacks, Caf&eacute; Wiedenska is the perfect place to visit. Situated in the town center, this caf&eacute; boasts of elegant d&eacute;cor and wonderful food, which you can enjoy for hours. Try their hot chocolate after spending some time in the cold.

**Caf&eacute; Sabala

**The Poles are known to have a sweet tooth, and if you want to sample their favorite delicacies, make a trip to Caf&eacute; Sabala. Located on Krupowki 11 in the town center, this caf&eacute; is situated on a terrace. The caf&eacute; offers a range of dishes and coffees and also has a glass roof, which is perfect for snowy winter evenings. Caf&eacute; Sabala also serves alcoholic beverages, and this makes it a perfect spot at any time of the day.
