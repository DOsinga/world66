---
title: "Getting There in Zakopane"
type: section
---

The cheapiest way to get to Zakopane from the Krakow Balice airport is to take a fast
train from the airport (you can walk to the tiny trainstation about 800m passing
the bus stop on the way or take a shutle which stops just in front of
the international arrivals section) to the Krakow main railwaystation and then
a public bus from the bus station which is located next to the railway
station. There are the buses nearly every 20 minutes from 7am till
10pm. All together will cost about 6 EURO and takes about 3-4 hours.
The easiest way is to order a private transfer in our office (Prices:
1-3 persons 270PLN; 4-7 prs - 320 PLN; 8-14 prs - 370 PLN and 15-18
persons - 450 PLN). To get more information visit our website www.discoverZakopane.com or just send an e-mail: altius@discoverzakopane.com
