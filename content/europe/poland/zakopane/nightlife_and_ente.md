---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment in Zakopane"
type: section
---

**Zakopane nightlife** contains such a wide spectrum of activities
that encompassing it into one single stereotype is impossible. It is
not just that the options are so varied, it is also that there are a
quite a few things to be aware of. But for the savvy nightlifer,
Zakopane offers a lot of fun.
**Where to Start

**

There seems to be general consensus among the Zakopane nightlife
“experts” group that Krupowki Street is the place to start. Here there
are numerous bars, restaurants and shops. It is much the center of the
town. The problems, though, can be numerous. There are some very good
bars, but some are downright bad. It might seem strange that there is
such a difference in quality among these bars, but there is a reason for
this. Zakopane is two towns. One is the mountain tourist destination,
but the other is a small, provincial rural town. But if you stick to
known names with only a little deviation, you should be all right.
**Where to Go

**

You cannot go wrong with Café Piano. It has had many good reviews
from tourists. Nietota also is a well known name in the beer drinking
circles. Genaralla Callicy and Papparazi are also very decent places to
possibly go to. If you are into clubbing, Armstrong is a good place to
go. It has a buzzing atmosphere, which has made it a well known name not
just in Zakopane, but also in rest of Poland. There are actually back
rooms in this club, which seem to have come directly from the Arabian
Nights.
**Other Options

**

If you are slightly adventurous, you can try Antidotum. The place is
in the basement, but has an interesting atmosphere. It is very dark,
but does have a couple of comfortable places to sit. So, if you are that
kind of person, this may catch your fancy. Café Antrakt is a place
where poets like to hang out occasionally. Again, this is a specialty
kind of a place, and not everybody’s cup of tea or, in this case, mug of
beer. If you are in town on business or have children with you, Lobby
maybe the place for you. It has a nice relaxed atmosphere, and even has a
pool with turtles in it for the children to play with while the adults
enjoy a couple of drinks.
