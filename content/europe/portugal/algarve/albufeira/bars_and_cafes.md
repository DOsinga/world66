---
title: 'Bars and Cafes in Albufeira'
type: section
---

Albufeira has bars and cafes for every mood, from English pubs to all-night dance clubs. The strip is where the action concentrates, but the old town has quieter spots with better prices.

**BJ's** -- On the strip. Popular with young English and Irish crowds. Well-priced drinks, eclectic music, friendly staff.

**The Lord Nelson Bar and Pub** -- Owned by a local couple but keeps a proper English pub feel. Good food served at any hour, and there is a children's play area so parents can relax.

**Erin's Isle Irish** -- The Irish expat favourite. Beers around 1.50 euros, traditional Irish food including bacon and cabbage. Hospitable staff, welcoming to families.

**La Bamba** -- On the strip. Open during the day but the party does not really start until late evening. Dancing until dawn.

**Charlie's Bar** -- Quiet meals and excellent breakfasts during the day, lively entertainment at night until 3 a.m. Inexpensive food and drinks.

**The Cavern** -- In the old town, a bit hidden. English-owned, proper drinks, 60s and 70s music, and all the sport on TV. Cross the old town square past the Bank bar, take the left fork, walk two minutes, and turn left at the corner.
