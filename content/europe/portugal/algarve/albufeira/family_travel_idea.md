---
title: "Family Travel Ideas in Albufeira"
type: section
---

Albufeira is a destination for all ages to enjoy. There are bars and exciting nightlife for the younger generations to enjoy as well as the warm weather and walks for the older generations. Those traveling with children are also not left out as Albufeira family travel is some of the best in Europe.

**Zoo Marine Algarve**

The Zoo Marine Algarve is a great way for parents and children to spend the day. There is a large assortment of activities including dolphin and sea lion shows, demonstrations with birds of prey and several tropical bird exhibitions. Guests may also wish to visit the rescue center where employees work tirelessly to rescue and rehabilitate sick and injured ocean life. The center is open all year, making it a prefect place for the off-season or when the weather isn't cooperating.

**Slide and Splash Water Park**

When the beaches are too crowded the Slide and Splash Water Park makes the perfect family getaway. Escape the summer heat of southern Portugal by taking a trip down one of the many water slides, pools and tube rides. The Slide and Splash Water Park ranks as one of the favorite spots in the Algarve for both children and adults. It is open from 10 a.m. to 6 p.m. from April to October.

**Algarve Jeep and Zebra Safaris**

Have an African adventure in the heart of southern Portugal. Visitors have their choice of a full day jeep or combi-safari that includes an aqua show or a boat ride along the coast. Both children and adults will be amazed at the animals they will encounter on their journey.

**CrazyWorld**

Crazy World is a theme park packed full of things for parents and children to enjoy. Crazy World contains a small zoo, an out of the ordinary golf course, petting zoo and pony rides for young children. Taking swimsuits is recommended because there is a large swimming pool to enjoy. Crazy World also has plenty of bars and restaurants for adults to enjoy while their children enjoy the day.

**Swimming Pools**

Albufeira is complete with its own swimming pools that are open to the public. There is 1 pool at Rua Jose Carlos Ary Dos Santos and another at Quinta da Palmeira. Guests are required to bring towels, swimsuit, flip flops or other foot wear and a swimming cap. Changing rooms are available to all guests. Included on the site are a training pool, fun pool for children and a 25-meter pool for those who want to exercise.
