**Albufeira Transportation Options You Can Use**

**Albufeira Transportation**
within the town comes in local buses, car rentals, taxis, trains, and
boat cruises. Albufeira in Algarve Portugal is a town booming with
tourism and businesses. The different transportation provides
inexpensive local rides, while enjoying the impressive surrounding
environment.
**
Taxis Fixed Rate**

Taxis do not have
a metered payment. There are set prices for specific destinations. You
will have to haggle with the taxi driver first before agreeing to the
bargain. The standard rate is $3 for the first three miles and 95 cents
for the succeeding miles, but this can change depending on your
agreement. You might want to ascertain the price before hiring the taxi.

**Local Buses**

There
are several local buses, which are color coded and set for an assigned
destination. Trains are the same as buses. However, they travel to
destinations that are more distant. The bus rates are from $3. Trains
are more expensive, with prices from $7 to $8.
**
Car Rentals**

Choose
a company that can provide you less expensive prices. The cash out may
be more expensive than a bus and train, but in the long run, you can
save a lot for the car rental option because with a car you can go
anywhere in Albufeira, Algarve. It is very convenient, comfortable and
a reasonable price for tourists.

**Boat Cruises**

These
are explorations into the waterfront means of transportation in
Algarve. They even provide delicious meals on board. These trips can
cost from $3 to $50, depending on the destination of the cruise.
