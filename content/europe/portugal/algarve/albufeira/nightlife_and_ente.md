---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment in Albufeira"
type: section
---

As a resort town, Albufera's nightlife is constantly buzzing. Visitors will have no problem finding a large selection of pubs, clubs and discos in which to enjoy the evening hours.

**Matt's Bar**

Matt's Bar is conveniently located on the strip. It is one of the many places that attracts both the young crowds and those encroaching upon their 30s. Matt's **B**ar offers a range of live entertainment with one of the best acts being a local classic rock band. Guests are welcome to try the mechanical bull or simply have a laugh by watching other tourists being thrown every which way after they have had their try.

**Open Discotheque**

The Open Discotheque can get quite loud and crowded but it is a wonderful place to dance, mingle and meet people. The Open Discotheque is a favorite among tourists and locals so it's possible to meet someone from just about anywhere. The disco opens in the early evening but things don't really start getting going until after 10. It is open late, sometimes until the sun comes up--so pace yourself.

**Zansi Bar**

The Zansi Bar is located near the Albufeira Jardim hotel. It is a great spot to visit in the evenings and is known for great food and well-priced drinks. Karaoke nights are among some of the best times to visit as locals and tourists try their luck as the club's entertainment. The Zansi Bar is a great place to go for those who like to relax, eat, drink and have a laugh.

**Praca de Touros de Albufeira**

Vegetarians and animal rights' enthusiasts might wish to give this event a wide berth. The Praca de Touros is where the local bullfights are held. During the summer months the fights occur every Saturday evening with an occasional special event on Thursdays. Although bullfighting has become quite controversial throughout Spain and Portugal, it is still considered an art by many and provides reasonably priced entertainment.

**Cinemas**

For a relaxing night out that doesn't involve drinking or dancing until dawn, try the movies. Albufeira has 2 main movie theaters. The Estudio Alfa is near the town center and the Guia is located in the Albufeira shopping center. Both show films in English and Portuguese. If you're traveling with children or decide not to spend an evening on the strip, then a night at the movies can be a pleasant alternative.
