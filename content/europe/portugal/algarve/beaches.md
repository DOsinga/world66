In the Southwest corner of the Algarve, you will find the beautifull village of Sagres, totaly inserted in the Natural Park of Sudoeste Alentejano e Costa Vicentina.

Excelent surf and amazing deserted beaches awayt you here.

Excelent for families.

In Sagres you will find Memmo Baleeira Hotel Sagres, a recently rebuild Hotel, just above the pitoresc harbour of Baleeira and overlooking the south coast of the Algarve and the beautifull Martinhal islands
