JAILHOUSE is the only place in Carvoeiro that has live music every night. Resident musicains and guest players welcome. Beer garden , as well as roof terrace with pool tables. Fun atmosphere with everyone welcome.

HEMMINGWAYS offers a fantastic welcome, situated on the out road of the town. Serving snacks at lunchtime, or just a coffee or something stronger and while away the day watching the world go by amiably.

### [Rascals](/europe/portugal/algarve/carvoeiro/bars_and_cafes/rascals)

Heading up the steep hill to Monte Branco, this beautifully decorated bar with its own terrace facing out to sea, is an ideal resting stop. Serving anything from a beer to champagne (at reasonable prices) along with scrumptious sandwhiches and snacks. Recently refurbished it will soon become the place to be seen at in Carvoeiro.

### [JAILHOUSE](/europe/portugal/algarve/carvoeiro/bars_and_cafes/jailhouse)

This is the only live music venue in carvoeiro that has live music every night.

It is a Carvoeiro institution and has been here since the early 60's with lots of very famous acts and musicians visiting over the years. There is still a lively clientelle and there is dancing till the early hours either to the live music or disco dvd's most nights.

It is a beautiful old plaster and woodwork building and feels like a proper pub, not just a converted shop unit or cafe bar. Live music doesn't start till about 10pm so go for diner first then here afterwards for a [more..](/europe/portugal/algarve/carvoeiro/bars_and_cafes/jailhouse)



accessibility: close to carvoeiro post office

tel: 00351 282356235

address: rua do Escondidinho no 9

### [JAILHOUSE Carvoeiro](/europe/portugal/algarve/carvoeiro/bars_and_cafes/jailhouse_carvoeir)

The only place in Carvoeiro that has live music every night. Guest musicians are welcome and generally a fun atmosphere for locals and tourists alike.

Beer garden and terrace with pool tables also a good family attraction



accessibility: next to the post office
