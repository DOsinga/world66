---
title: 'Bars and Cafes in Carvoeiro'
type: section
---

**Jailhouse** -- A Carvoeiro institution since the early 1960s. The only place in town with live music every night. A beautiful old plaster and woodwork building that feels like a proper pub, not a converted shop unit. Guest musicians are welcome, and there is dancing until the early hours. Beer garden and roof terrace with pool tables. Live music starts around 10 p.m. Rua do Escondidinho 9, next to the post office. Tel: 282 356235.

**Hemingways** -- On the road out of town. Good for a coffee or something stronger at lunchtime, with snacks and a pleasant view.

**Rascals** -- Up the steep hill to Monte Branco. A beautifully decorated bar with a terrace facing out to sea. Serves everything from beer to champagne at reasonable prices, along with sandwiches and snacks.
