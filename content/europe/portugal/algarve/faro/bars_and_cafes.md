---
title: "Bars and Cafes"
type: section
---

Faro's café culture is unhurried and Portuguese — strong espresso, pastries, and no pressure to move along. The area around **Jardim Manuel Bívar** (the harbourside garden) has several pavement cafés good for watching life pass. In the evenings, the streets around **Rua Conselheiro Bívar** and the student areas near the university have a livelier bar scene. **Taberna da Sé** is a reliable spot for a drink near the old town.
