---
title: "Bars and Cafes"
type: section
---

Faro's cafe culture is unhurried and Portuguese — meaning strong espresso, pastries, and no pressure to move along. The area around Jardim Manuel Bivar (the harbourside garden) has several pavement cafes good for watching life pass. In the evenings, the streets around Rua Conselheiro Bivar and the student areas near the university have a livelier bar scene. Taberna da Sé is a reliable spot for a drink near the old town.
