---
title: "Beaches"
type: section
---

Faro's beaches are reached by short ferry from the pier near Jardim Manuel Bívar. **Ilha de Faro** (Praia de Faro) is the closest — a long barrier island with calm water on the lagoon side and Atlantic waves on the ocean side, and the only Faro beach reachable without a boat (via a small bridge from the mainland). **Ilha da Culatra** and **Ilha da Barreta** (also known as *Ilha Deserta*) are quieter and less developed, popular for birdwatching in the Ria Formosa Natural Park — flamingoes, sandwich terns and spoonbills. **Ilha de Armona**, further east near Olhão, has one of the best beaches in the region. For families with young children, **Burgau Beach** west toward Lagos is a safe horseshoe cove with calm water and tidal pools.
