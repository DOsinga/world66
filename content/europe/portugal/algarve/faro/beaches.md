---
title: "Beaches"
type: section
---

Faro's beaches are reached by short ferry from the pier near Jardim Manuel Bivar. Ilha de Faro (Praia de Faro) is the closest — a long barrier island with calm water on the lagoon side and Atlantic waves on the ocean side. Ilha da Culatra and Ilha da Barreta are quieter and less developed, popular for birdwatching in the Ria Formosa Natural Park. Ilha de Armona, further east near Olhão, has one of the best beaches in the region. Burgau Beach, west toward Lagos, is better for families with young children.
