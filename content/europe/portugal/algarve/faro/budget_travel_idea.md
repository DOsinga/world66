---
title: "Budget Travel Ideas in Faro"
type: section
---

Faro is located in southern Portugal and is a popular
tourist destination. **Faro budget travel** allows travelers from all over the
world to come and experience the beauty of Faro without having to pay too much
for their stay. The Subtropical - Mediterranean climate of Faro makes it
warm to hot during the daytime with temperatures ranging between 27 degrees
to 29 degrees
C. Here are some budget travel ideas if you are planning a visit to Faro.**

Low Cost Airlines**

There are several low cost airlines that operate regular
flights to Faro from several European countries. Airlines such as Ryanair, Aer
Lingus, Air Berlin, Monarch Airlines, Arkefly, Brussels Airlines and many more
offer regular flights to and from Faro every day. Check with the airline office
and compare rates before purchasing a ticket.**

Accommodations**

Accommodations are never a problem in Faro, you can choose
from a variety of accommodations according to your budget. Faro has several
budget hotels in the main commercial area, including some beautiful small
country hotels. Stay at one of the small country hotels just 15 minutes away
from the airport. These hotels offer clean accommodation and have good
restaurants where you can pick up something to eat.**

Transportation
**
Make use of the public transport system in Faro if you like
to save on transportation costs. The train and bus will get you to most places
and are not too expensive. You can also get a bicycle and cycle around the city;
this in fact is a great way to explore the city on your own pace.

**Shopping**

There are several shopping malls in Faro, most being in the Faro
and Algarve region. If you are looking for some good bargains, take a trip down
to the “Forum Algarve,”a large shopping mall situated close to the
airport. The Forum Algarve is an open-air mall where you can pick up a variety
of items; however, food and drinks are the main focus of the mall. You can pick
up some clothes here at very good prices.

**Albufuria**

Located about 30 minutes from the center of Faro, this is
where you can really enjoy budget nightlife. The place is filled with bars, cafés
and discos. You will be able to pick up any kind of food you want to eat here,
and the price is not expensive. So take a trip down to Albufuria and you will
spend less than half of what you would spend at other nightlife venues in the
city.
