---
title: "Eating Out"
type: section
---

Faro eats better than its transit-hub reputation suggests. Grilled fish is the thing to order — fresh from the Atlantic and the lagoon. Fim de Mundo on Rua Vasco de Gama is a family restaurant known for pork ribs and grilled chicken at honest prices (around €25 for two with wine). Costa Algarvia does the full Portuguese spread — chorizo, cured ham, fresh bread, grilled meats — in a multigenerational family atmosphere. For a genuine local experience, ask around for whichever restaurant the fishing families favour that season.
