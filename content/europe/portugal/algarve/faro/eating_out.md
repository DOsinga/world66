---
title: "Eating Out"
type: section
---

Faro eats better than its transit-hub reputation suggests. **Grilled fish** is the thing to order — fresh from the Atlantic and the lagoon. **Fim do Mundo** on Rua Vasco de Gama is a family restaurant known for pork ribs and grilled chicken at honest prices (around €25 for two with wine). **Costa Algarvia** does the full Portuguese spread — chorizo, cured ham, fresh bread, grilled meats — in a multigenerational family atmosphere. For something quieter, the small **Flor da Ameixa** near Estoi is a classic local *tasca* with the catch of the day cooked on an indoor grill by the owner. Prices in Faro are noticeably cheaper than in the resort towns further west along the coast.
