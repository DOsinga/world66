---
title: "Getting Around"
type: section
---

The old town is walkable. For the rest of Faro, local buses cover most areas and the train station connects to other Algarve towns along the coast. Most visitors hire a car at the airport — it's the practical choice for exploring the Algarve region. Taxis are readily available; Uber also operates here. For the lagoon islands and beaches, ferries run from the pier near Jardim Manuel Bívar throughout the day in season.
