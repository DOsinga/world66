---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

**Faro International Airport (FAO)** is a major hub for budget airlines — Ryanair, easyJet, and TAP all serve it heavily from UK and European cities. The airport is 3 km from the city centre; a taxi takes about 10 minutes. There are also bus and train connections from **Lisbon** (around 3.5 hours by train). If you're coming from Spain, the train from Seville via Huelva works, though it takes most of the day.
