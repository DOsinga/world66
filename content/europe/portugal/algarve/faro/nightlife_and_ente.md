title: "Nightlife and Entertainment in Faro"
type: section
---

Katherine Green

As a college town, **Faro nightlife** is active and exciting. While
much of the serious dancing happens at the large clubs just outside of
the city, there are many bars and clubs in the more centralized Rua do
Prior that are great for starting your night off. The city is well-known
throughout the region for being a serious party spot and if nightlife
is what you came for, Faro will surely deliver.

**Nightlife in Faro**

Start your night in any of the great bars situated on the main nightlife
strip of Rua do Prior. This student-driven nightlife area starts early
and stays open late, with a considerable pick up in business and
partying as the weekend nears. During Spring Break this area is also
significantly livelier with many bars and clubs filled to capacity.

Millennium III is a large club with a warehouse style feel to it that
features live DJs and the occasional live performance. Upa Upa and
Columbus are both also located in the same area and both have a nice
environment to relax in. Upa Upa opens rather early and stay open late,
with good music played all night and a nice open air cafe to lounge in.
Columbus is a bar with tables overlooking the park and harbor, and
makes a great place to lounge on a clear night.

T-clube and Kadoc are two of the biggest clubs in the area. T-clube is a
favorite among tourists and locals alike and is geared towards the
trendy, rich and beautiful. On weekends the club is packed and there is
normally a line to get on the dance floor. Kadoc can hold up to 8000
people and is the site of all really big live shows in the area, as well
as a very lively nightclub.

**Entertainment in Faro**

Teatro Lethes is a great small theater perfect for checking out a drama,
dance or music performance. Now owned by the Portuguese Red Cross, the
theater was built in 1874, is located in the center of town on Rua
Lethes and stages many shows throughout the year.

Step out of the ordinary and into one of the many exciting and lively
bars or other entertainment venues in Faro. With many options to choose
from you are sure to find a unique night spot that suits your tastes
when visiting Faro.
