---
title: "When to Go"
type: section
---

The Algarve has more annual sunshine than anywhere else in Europe, and Faro benefits accordingly. **June through September** is high season — warm enough for beach days, but crowded and expensive. **Late spring (April–May)** and **early autumn (September–October)** offer warm weather, manageable crowds, and better prices; these are arguably the best months. **Winter** is mild by northern European standards and the city is quieter and cheaper, though the ocean is too cold for swimming between late November and early March.
