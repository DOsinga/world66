**Restaurante Porches Velho** -- Housed in a 200-year-old wine cellar in one of the most traditional villages in the Algarve. Serves excellent Portuguese cuisine with some of the best dishes from the region. Main courses from around 13 euros. Rua da Praca 8400, Porches. Tel: 282 381 692.
