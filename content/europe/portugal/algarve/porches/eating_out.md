Coming from Portimão follow the N 125 into the direction Faro, approx 4kms after Lagoa and the International School of Algarve and the Porches Pottery please turn right off N 125 to Porches. After 15ms turn left into the village.

Coming from Faro follow the motorway A22 (Via do Infante) to the direction Portimão. At exit Alcantarilha leave A22 and follow N125 in direction Lagoa-Portimão. After approx. 3 kms leave N125 left into the village of Porches.

### [Restaurante Porches Velho](/europe/portugal/algarve/porches/eating_out/restaurante_porche)

Making a good use of a bicentary (205 years old) wine-cellar, placed in one of the most beautiful and traditional places of Algarve, Porches-Lagoa, appears this cosy restaurant with the best Portuguese food, with some of the best courses from Algarve.
