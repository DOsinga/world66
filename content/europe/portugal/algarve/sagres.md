Sagers is where once navigators thought that land ended and the ocean dropped off into nothingness, the end of the known world. Here was also Prince Henry the Navigator looked out over the sea and wondered if there was a route to the Indies. 
 


 
 The town is clean and has lovely white washed houses with the red tile roofs and a lack of tourist. The beaches here can be a bit windy.
