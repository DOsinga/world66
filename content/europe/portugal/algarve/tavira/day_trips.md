---
title: 'Day Trips'
type: section
---

**Ria Formosa Boat Tours** -- Boat trips through the Ria Formosa Natural Park, a lagoon system and migratory bird corridor that attracts around 30,000 birds annually. Several operators run tours from the Tavira waterfront.

**Horse-Drawn Carriage Tours** -- A leisurely way to see Tavira's historic centre, passing through its narrow streets and past its churches and traditional architecture.

**Cacela Velha** -- A tiny clifftop village about 12 km east of Tavira with a medieval church, a small fort, and stunning views over the Ria Formosa lagoon to the barrier islands beyond.
