---
title: ‘Things to do in Tavira’
type: section
---

**Explore the Churches** -- Tavira has an unusual concentration of churches for a small town. Santa Maria do Castelo, Misericordia, and Sao Jose are the highlights, spanning Gothic, Renaissance, and Manueline styles.

**Ria Formosa Natural Park** -- The park headquarters are in nearby Olhao, which offers a 3 km walking trail past a tide mill, traditional fishing boats, and free-roaming birdlife. Olhao itself is worth visiting for its ‘cubist’ fishermen’s quarter and neo-Arab style markets.

**Ilha de Tavira** -- Take the ferry to Tavira’s barrier island for wide, clean beaches with fewer crowds than the western Algarve.

**Castro Marim** -- The medieval castle overlooks the Guadiana estuary and the Spanish border, with sweeping views from its ramparts.
