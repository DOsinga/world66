---
title: 'Things to do in Vila Nova de Cacela'
type: section
---

Vila Nova de Cacela is a small town just off the N125, well worth a detour. The indoor fish market is a treat if you arrive early. The Red Cafe does good frango (BBQ chicken) with chips and salad, and Chicken George's on the crossroads in the centre is a more traditional option.

There are plenty of small bars in the centre area. The town has a monthly market on a Sunday -- try the frango sold with piri piri. A campsite is available for those who want to rough it, and the town sits on the main railway line to [Faro](/europe/portugal/algarve/faro).
