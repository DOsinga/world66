Arcos de Valdevez is the terminus of two separate bus routes from [Ponte de Lima](/europe/portugal/ponte_de_lima), both of which offer outstanding river scenery with mountains beyond. One route goes through the splendid little town of Ponte da Barca and the hamlet of Bravães with its idyllic Romanesque church.



If it’s not too hot, it should be easy enough to walk to Ponte da barca and if it is, the immediate riverside walks are exceedingly good.
