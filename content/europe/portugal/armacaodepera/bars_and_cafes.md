---
title: "Bars and Cafes in Armacao de Pera"
type: section
---

The **Armacao de Pera
bars and cafés** offer visitors a chance to explore the real essence of this
Portuguese fishing village and resort. Armacao de Pera is a small cosmopolitan
town coupled with overflowing wonders of nature. Therefore you won’t run out of something to do in one of
Europe’s beautiful tourist destinations. Exploring the bars and cafes around
Armacao will definitely take a great amount of your time; so you better start early
and spend time in savoring Armacao’s delicacies and popular
drinks.

**Capelinha Bar**

This is one of the most popular bars in Armacao de Pera. This
is a great place for a night out with friends and also a hot spot for the
weekend. The bar has great food, fabulous cocktails and includes Karaoke and a live
band. If you are looking for a place to enjoy the ball game over some good
drinks, Capelinha bar is the place to be.

**Water Dog Pub**

Water Dog Pub offers an authentic Armacao pub experience.
The pub serves authentic Armacao dishes and homemade steaks. The bar at the pub includes a wide selection
of draught beers and other drinks. The pub is open every day and is a great
place to relax and unwind.

**Sports Bar Algale**

If you are looking for homemade, reasonably price Portuguese
dishes and wide array of local beers, then Sports Bar Algale is the perfect
place for you. The bar is a hot spot for the younger crowd especially during
the weekends. There is a wide screen Plasma TV at the pub so you can watch your
favorite sports and all the Premier League games.

**Salgados Mar
Restaurante and Bar**

Salgados Mar Restaurante and bar is a place perfect for the
whole family. The bar offers fresh seafood, meat kebabs and homemade wine. The
bar also includes a kids’ menu and playground for your little ones. This is a HACCP
certified bar and is one of the most popular hangouts in town.

**Pasadena's Cafe Bar**

Pasadena’s Café Bar is a popular bar in town. This is one of
the cleanest bars around. If you are looking for a place to relax and enjoy
some fine drinks along with some mouthwatering dishes, this is the place to be.
The place has a well-stocked bar including some of the best wines around. The
place is not expensive, so you don’t have to worry about being fleeced.
