---
title: "Family Travel Ideas in Armacao de Pera"
type: section
---

If you're planning a trip to Portugal, you should consider visiting **Armacao
de Pera. Family travel** here is exceptional and there are many
activities that will keep your family entertained and engaged throughout
your visit to the city.

**Family Travel Sights**
One great
place to visit with family is the Zoomarine theme park. Here visitors
can see live shows featuring seals, sea lions, dolphins, tropical birds
and birds of prey. It is a highly entertaining adventure that is sure to
delight both the youngest and oldest members of your family.

Beach
lovers will enjoy the wonderful beaches of Armacao de Pera. Said to
have one of the nicest beaches in the area, the city is nicely appointed
with many dive shops and other outfitters offering a wide variety of
water sports. While older children may want to try scuba diving, the
younger set will certainly love playing on the great beaches and
swimming in the warm water of the city.

Armacao de Pera also
makes a great starting point for many other excursions in the area.
Staying in the smaller town can give you a relaxing feel and a great
opportunity to visit some larger cities without getting caught up in the
traffic and city life during your time there. Visit Seville, Gibralter,
Lisbon, Portimao, Lagos or Albugeira on one of the many group day trips
originating in the city to get a diverse look at the area surrounding Armacao de Pera.

**When to Visit**
Armacao de Pera is a
resort town and can be quite busy during peak months in the summer. When
traveling with small children, it is recommended to visit in the off
season to get the most value for your trip and avoid crowds that can be a
bit overwhelming at times in this small town.
**
Family
Accommodations**
In addition to many hotels in Armacao de Pera,
there are also several serviced apartments and other vacation rentals in
the city that make traveling with your family easy. Staying in an
apartment offers you the ability to do some light cooking and save money
over staying in a hotel. In many cases as well, apartments can hold
several guests, allowing everyone in your family to require just one
rental without incurring additional fees like at a hotel.

If you
are looking for a unique family adventure, Armacao de Pera is a great
place to check out.
