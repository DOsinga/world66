To enjoy the sights and sounds of Armaco de Pera, taking part in one of the **Armaco de Pera festivals** will surely give you the excitement that you need while staying in this beautiful town in Portugal. This town is popular for its beaches that stretch for several miles to Praia dos Pescadores. One of the festivals that you need to join is the Caldeirada Festival.

**Caldeirada Festival**

Armaco de Pera is blessed with the abundance of sea produce, so the best festival in this town is the Caldeirada Festival, which is a food festival held from June 24 to 27 each year. This particular festival is still new and has been celebrated by the town for only 12 years. Nevertheless, a lot of foodies go to Armaco de Pera to take part in this festival.

**Features of This Festival**

This festival features several fish dishes that are famous in the region. In fact, the term Caldeirada is acquired from a regional dish of fish stew and potatoes. Many local restaurants take part in this festival and also offer free samples of their own version of the local dish. This festival also features light merry-making after eating the sumptuous fish dishes.

However, if you are not too fond of fish, you will still be able to enjoy the festive atmosphere. The live music played during the festival will keep you entertained.
