**Armacao de Pera museums** may be few and far between, but if you
need a break from the sun and beautiful beaches, there are a couple of
notable stops that you can make to learn about the culture, art and
history of the region.

**Museums in Armacao de Pera**

Though more of a
theme park than museum, Zoomarine is a great educational stop on your
vacation to the Algarve and worth mentioning here. Enclosed in 17 acres,
this ocean theme park has recreated over 20 aquatic ecosystems and
features exhibits and shows with such animals as dolphins, birds of
prey, seals, sea lions and tropical birds. There is much to see and do
here, including watching a 4D movie, checking out the high dive show,
visiting the animal rehabilitation center, exploring the plant awareness
center and swimming with dolphins. This is a great stop for the whole
family and close to town.

Located just under 20 miles from Armacao de Pera in Lagos is the
Municipal Museum, or Museu Municipal Dr Jose Formosinho. This museum
contains many relics of the past centuries, including 16th century
vestments, a gallery of paintings, 17th century embroidery and cork
carvings. There are numerous antiquities here discovered through
excavations in the area and it is a great museum with diverse exhibits
suitable for people with varying interests.

After a couple days of
beach activities, break up your vacation to Armacao de Pera with a trip
to one of these fascinating area museums and you will be glad you did.
