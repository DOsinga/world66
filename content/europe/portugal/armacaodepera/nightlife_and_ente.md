---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment in Armacao de Pera"
type: section
---

There are few resort places around the world that will give you such an incredible chance to party like the **Armacao de Pera nightlife** during tourist season. Armacao de Pera is just one of many towns along the Algarve shoreline that is considered the best area to hang out. Venture all along the waterfront and you will have so many choices available where you and your friends will find entertainment, it will be hard to choose what club to stop at. When nighttime comes the village starts hopping, bars and nightclubs are filled with people while the restaurants remain open until early morning hours. Maybe you prefer a more quiet time for your nights, then it would be best to visit during the off season.
**

Top Forty, Rock and Roll and Rap Music**

Walk out onto the waterfront then step right into the wild partying. All along the venues places like **Capelinha Bar** and are filled with party-goers. Grab a beer at the **Water Dog** and find out why it is famous for showcasing the local talent. Most clubs you will find offer live bands playing top forty hits and rock and roll and a few places have rap music. Some clubs and bars offer a special attraction. These clubs have rooms which let you choose from musical styles like reggae, rock and roll, country or rhythm and blues. Many of the larger restaurants boast live music during the nighttime hours.
**

Disco, Easy Rock other Entertainment

**As you walk farther away from the seafront the intensity of the wild life lessens some. The hard core music clubs are less frequent while easy rock and disco clubs become more prominent. If this atmosphere is more to your liking, visit the **Guitar Bar** where native Portuguese music, called Fado, is played. A trip to Armaco de Pera would not be complete without experiencing this unique style of ethnic music. Check out a disco club if that style of music is joyful to you. You will be amazed at the array of colorful displays of lights on the buildings. In the old town, where most of the restaurants are, easy rock and jazz are on the menu for entertainment. **Hotel Garbe** happens to be one of the few hotels that have live stage shows or other types of excitement for you.
