title: "Shopping in Armacao de Pera"
type: section
---

**Armacao de Pera shopping** is top notch, offering a wide selection of up-to-date fashions, as well as an array of handmade gifts and souvenirs. Visitors to this amazing and beautiful destination will enjoy the great shopping throughout the region and the great prices that accompany it. Take a break from the beach and spend and afternoon or two shopping the wonderful assortment of stores here in Armacao de Pera.

**What to Buy in Armacao de Pera**

Along with designer fashions, there are many great handicrafts available for purchase in Armacao de Pera. Look for locally made jewelry, pottery and leather goods for some really fine crafts made by local artisans. The embroidered items here are exquisite, and savvy shoppers will find many nicely appointed linens to add to their collection.

**Where to Shop in Armacao de Pera**

There are two large shopping centers in the city - the Algarve Shopping Center and the Modelo Shopping Center. Both are centrally located in Armacao de Pera. There are also numerous other shops woven in with the bars and restaurants that line the entire commercial district to make shopping while out and about easy and convenient.

Just 18 kilometers to the east of Armacao de Pera is the city of Albufeira where there is also a wide range of shopping and a perfect spot for a day trip for shopping and other sightseeing. Rent a car or take the bus to this neighboring city for a great afternoon exploring both the sights and the unique shopping with a slightly wider selection of goods than in Aramacao de Pera.

One the first Monday of every month there is also a market in nearby Portimao that is definitely worth checking out if you are in the area. Here there are many handmade goods and it is a great place for people watching as well. While the shopping centers all have fixed prices for their goods, markets allow you to bargain with the vendors and in many cases, get a better deal on the items you purchase.

Armacao de Pera has many great products that are produced in the area that make great gifts or souvenirs. Look around throughout your stay and compare prices and quality.
