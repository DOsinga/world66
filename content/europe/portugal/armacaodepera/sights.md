---
title: "Sights in Armacao de Pera"
type: section
---

Armacao de Pera is a Portuguese parish town that is located in the municipality of Silves. Today, it is among one of the most popular tourist centers in Portugal due to its beautiful and fine beaches. The town is also famous for its seafood restaurants that take pride in their fresh fish. Armacao de Pera became well known for the numerous swanky hotels, resorts and grand villas that have been built in the hills, overlooking the bay and spread out near the coast. If you are looking for some fun under the sun or looking for a glamorous yet peaceful getaway, Armacao de Pera is the place for you.

**Algarve Beach**

Armacao de Pera is known for having the longest sandy beaches on the Algarve. The coast boasts of white sandy areas and the beauty of having a backdrop of ancient rock cliffs in the area. The water is very clear and the soft sand is ideal for children who are fond of making sand castles. The fine sandy beaches have attracted a lot of tourists, especially in summer. Off season, the beaches are able to provide a relaxing and quiet getaway.

**Armacao Fort**

This is one of the most popular tourist spots in the area which was originally a fishing harbor. Visitors can find ruins of a fortress that was built in the 18th century and was used to protect Armacao de Pera from the Barbary Pirates. This site is a blend of the past and the present in the area with its modern promenade and old cobbled streets. Exploring the remains of the fort, the chapel and the walls from the past is definitely a worthwhile experience.

**Monchique**

For an inspiring retreat in the rural area you can go to Monchique which is considered to be the home to several natural spas. Visitors can also find a lot of beautiful woodland trails in this area. This is a definite must see for nature lovers.

**Senhora da Rocha**

Senhora da Rocha is the area where development has reached its peak. You will find a lot of hotels, beach villas and holiday apartments here. The beaches are breathtaking. If you are fond of seafood, you can find a lot of good restaurants lining the beach where you can enjoy a feast, while having a great view of the sea.

**Aqualand Water Park**

For family vacations, this could be an interesting spot for you and your kids. You will be able to find numerous theme pools and exciting water slides to make the swimming experience fun.

Armacao de Pera has several natural and historical attractions along with luxury resorts and amusement parks that are worth exploring.
