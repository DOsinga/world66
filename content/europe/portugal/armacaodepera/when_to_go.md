---
title: "When to Go in Armacao de Pera"
type: section
---

If you are planning a trip to **Armacao de Pera, When to Go** is the most important piece of information that you will need to plan the perfect vacation. Knowing when to go to Armacao de Pera allows you to visit when you will be able to take advantage of everything that they have to offer.

**Climate**

During the months of July to September, you are going to be faced with an average temperature of 77 degrees F, which is perfect for most people, not too hot and not too cold. The reason that the temperatures are so mild in the summer months is due to the breeze that comes off the coast; even a slight ocean breeze can make things more bearable.

**July**

If you elect to visit the area in July, the end of July is best. During the last weekend of July you can experience the “Arraial de Petisco,” which is held at the sports ground. At the festival, you will be able to enjoy live music each night and a different main dish is featured each night, which allows you to try different foods. You can also take part in sampling the many Petiscos that are being offered throughout the festival.

**August**

During the beginning of August when you visit Armacao de Pera you can take part in the “Sons do Atlantico” Festival. This festival features live music each night and lasts for about 10 days. In addition to the festival, you can also attend the “Arraial de Petisco” during the first weekend of August, in case you missed it in July.

**September**

September doesn’t have any special festivals that you can attend but it is still a great time to go because the summer days are 12 hours long, which can all be spent outside on the beach or playing golf. Armacao de Pera offers plenty of outdoor activities that will keep you entertained during the long summer days.

**Months to Avoid**

One of the best things about Armacao de Pera is that the weather is mostly pleasant throughout the year, making it an easy vacation spot for many families. Although the weather is usually pleasant, the months of December and January offer the coldest temperatures of the year, seeing an average temperatures of 60 degrees F. You will also notice plenty of rain during the month of December because it is normal to see 12 to 14 days of rainfall during the month.
