title: "Boca do Inferno"
type: poi
category: Sight
latitude: 38.6956

---

The "Mouth of Hell" is a sea cave carved into the cliff face about 2 kilometres west of the town centre. When the Atlantic swell is up, waves drive into the cave with a deep boom and spray shoots upward through a hole in the roof. On calmer days it is less dramatic but still a striking piece of coastline. The walk along the clifftop from town is pleasant in its own right. There is a small market of craft stalls near the viewing area.
