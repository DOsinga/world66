---

The old fortress at the harbour mouth dates to the sixteenth century, built to guard the mouth of the Tagus against pirates and rival navies. The walls and bastions are largely intact, and today the interior houses a hotel, galleries, and artists' studios that are open to the public. It is possible to walk the outer ramparts for views over the harbour and the coast toward Estoril.
