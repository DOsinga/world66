title: "Sights in Castelo Branco"
type: section
---

Some of the places to visit are the Garden of the Episcopal
Paço (1725), the Park of the City, Cathedral Sé, the Castle of the
Templários, S. Gens Miradouro, The Museum Francisco Tavares Proença
Junior, located in the old Episcopal Paço, rich in arqueologic parts,
tapestries, numismatics and Portuguese quinhentist painting.

Other places to visit outside of the city are the Bridges of the
River Ocreza, Alpedrinha, Fundão, Monsanto, Old Village of Rodão and
Tower of the Boyfriends
