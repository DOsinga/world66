Travel Guide

The main visitor appeal of Condeixa, reached by regular buses from Coimbra, lies in its proximity to the Roman Archaeological site of Conimbriga.

This is extensive, mostly at foundation level, with some fascinating mosaics but at present (2008) thre are no information signs to tell you wha you are seeing.
