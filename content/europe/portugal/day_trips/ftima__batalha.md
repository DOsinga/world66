In this Sightseeing Tour we will get to know a little better an
important part of the Western Region of Portugal, where besides a visit
to the **Sanctuary of Our Lady of Fatima** , you will know unique monuments of the gothic and medieval architecture, as it is the respective cases of **Batalha Monastery** and the biggest church of Portugal, the **Monastery of St. Mary of Alcobaça** . And still have time to visit **Sítio of Nazaré** , on top of a cliff with a superb view over the fishing city of Nazaré.
