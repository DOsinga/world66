Through a landscape speckled with granite houses with wooden or wrought-iron balconies, you enter the Port Wine region form the city of [Oporto](/europe/portugal/oporto)- the Douro Valley - by way of Amarante and Vila Real. In the mountainous hinterland lies. Viseu - a city of warriors and painters.
