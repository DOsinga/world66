---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment in Funchal"
type: section
---

The one thing that you need to know is that the **Funchal Nightlife** is fairly quiet. There are plenty of places that you can go at night to enjoy a drink or an evening snack, but the late night entertainment is not a big thing during the week. Friday and Saturday nights provide the city of Funchal with the most activities, and whether you are looking for a show or dancing you can often find something open until the wee hours of the morning.

**Formula 1**

This dance club is one of the few that are open during the week until the early hours of the morning; it is one of the exceptions to the general rule. Inside Formula 1 you will find numerous dance floors, but there are also other activities for guests to enjoy. On the top floor you can find various gambling machines, and you can also find table football and a snooker.

**Fugitivo**

This is one of the most popular nightclubs in town and is located near the Casino Park Hotel. This nightclub is actually something that everybody needs to try because of the outstanding Samba shows. The Samba shows at Fugitivo are very colorful and are put on by Brazilian dancers.

**Casino**

If you're interested in trying your luck, why not stop in at the Casino? It's open daily, but the hours vary. On Fridays and Saturdays, the slot machines are open from 4:00 p.m. to 4:00 a.m., while Sunday through Thursday the hours are 3:00 p.m. to 3:00 a.m. The Game Room, which includes four blackjack tables and seven French roulette tables, is open from 8:00 p.m. to 3:00 a.m. Sunday through Thursday and on Fridays and Saturdays the Game Room is open from 8:00 p.m. to 4:00 a.m. Make sure you follow the dress code or you will not be admitted; stay out of sports clothes and you'll be fine.

**O Molhe**

Located on top of an old fort, O Molhe is one of the more popular discos in town. One of the unique features it provides is being enclosed in glass so that you can enjoy the views the town has to offer while you're dancing the night away. If you're not interested in dancing you can sit at the bar where you can drink and listen to music under the night sky. This disco is open from 12:00 a.m. to 6:00 a.m. on Fridays and Saturdays.

**Vespas**

This is the biggest disco in the town of Funchal; it is also the most popular among the locals. The disco is also popular among people who don't want to go home just yet, because of how late it's open. The disco is open Wednesday through Sunday from 12:00 a.m. to 6:00 a.m., but it is also open the night before any public holiday.
