---
title: "Eating Out in Lagos"
type: section
---

Eating in Lagos is a wonderful experience. Much
like Mediterranean, Portuguese food is very rich and filling using lots
of seafood and simple spices where available. Lagos rests on the shoreline so seafood here is plentiful
and fresh. Restaurants in the area serve mostly Portuguese food though
some menus may serve fusion or other dishes.

**Fine Dining**

Fine dining in the area can be very expensive though some places are
more moderately priced. Price however is usually thought
of as a reflection of the food; the higher the price, the better the
food. One of the most expensive restaurants in Lagos is Restaurante dos
Artistas. This is a European restaurant that serves a combination of
cuisines including local food. You may end up spending $200 for a dinner for
two but for most, this is a price they are willing to spend for such
amazing food. The restaurant serves seafood regularly along with lamb,
beef and poultry dishes. They also have an extensive fine wine menu to
pair with your meal.

**Moderately Priced**

You don’t have to spend a lot of money to get a great meal in Lagos.
Most restaurants in the area have fairly priced menus so that you can
get a little taste of Portugal without completely ruining your travel
funds. Most of these restaurants will serve you dinner at a price
around $20 a plate. One restaurant is Do Cais Bistro which has a
delicious menu that easily pleases most pallets. In the day, Do Cais
and their sister restaurant Linda’s serves sandwiches and salads at a
more than fair price. At night Do Cais has a different atmosphere and
their menu changes to a more profound pallet, serving steaks and
delicious seafood dishes.

**Budget Dining**

If even the moderately priced restaurants are too rich for your
blood, don’t threat. There are plenty of places that serve cheap food.
Usually the beach bars serve cheap but delicious food. Linda’s (Do
Cais’ sister restaurant) is there along with Bahia and Sao Roque. Peri
Peri Take away is in nearby Luz and has great Portugal food for less
than $10.You could also always opt for a complete liquid diet by
visiting all the great bars in the area!
