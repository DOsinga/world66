Restaurant Nilo is a beautiful portuguese restaurant in Lisbon. Situated in downtown ("baixa de Lisboa") they cook portuguese food at ancient way. If you want to taste the ancient portuguese cuisine from all regions of portugal from Minho up to Algarve try this: Algarve`s style pork meay with shrimps, fried veal fillets with Oporto wine sauce, the famous portuguese codfish or shellfish rice ... all that irrigate with the best portuguese wine...
