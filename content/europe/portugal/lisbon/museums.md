**Lisbon Museums **cover a variety of topics. Visitors of any
background will find a museum of interest.

**Aquarium and Science Center**

Lisbon's aquarium, or Oceanario, displays hundreds of local and
global species and is the largest aquarium in Europe. The aquarium
opened in 1998 and is located on the riverfront in the Parque das
Nacoes. The two-kilometer park consists of restaurants and amazing views
of the Vasco da Gama bridge.

Also located in the park is the Pavilhao do Conhecimento, or Lisbon's
science and technology museum. There are plenty of hands-on activities
for children of all ages.
**Archaeology Museum**
