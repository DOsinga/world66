In this circuit we will know the heritage of Lisbon/Sintra with the tasting Porto wines and main portuguese wines. The first tasting will be in Lisbon with the Porto Wine Tasting, it will be in a calm and comfortable atmosphere where you can proves the different types and marks of port. The second tasting will be in Sintra, in a select atmosphere where will be able to prove the best Portuguese wines. The gastronomy was also thought about the tourist that really wants to know our traditional food in a refined atmosphere. In this tour we also had the concern of the visit of two main monuments of Lisbon and Sintra.
