---
title: "Sintra - A day trip for a breath of fresh air"
type: location
---

[Sintra](/europe/portugal/sintra), about 30 km west of Lisbon, is a refreshing place to go for a day trip, especially to relax and renew after the demand’s of a big city. It’s a place to visit for its mountain-top views toward the coast, the Parque Natural de Sintra-Cascais, the funky palace, tranquil convents, and lush gardens.
