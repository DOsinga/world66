title: "South of the river"
type: location
---

This could possibly be done in a shorter time with planning - but it is best not rushing and some of the transport links may involve delay.

First take the longest ferry route across the river to Montijo. The trip is good but there's not a lot to see here so get a bus as soon as possible to Setubal. From here you can get a bus for a very pleasant ride to Sesimbra. You may well want to spend a bit of time here and it is good to take a local bus out to the fishing port and back. [If you are British, you may well wonder at the apparent prosperity of the industry here.]

Then take yet another bus to Cacilhas and return to the north bank by the short river crossing.

[If you only have time for one day out from Lisbon, you should go to Sintra but this is good if you have another day.]
