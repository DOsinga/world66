Ephemeral Museum - As I was looking for some new experiences and cool things to do/seen in Portugal, I found this one that I think it's really a nice and different idea of what I have seen till now. I believe this is a project developed by an advertising agency from Portugal for the Pampero Rum.

The name of the Museum is Ephemeral Museum and it's a selection of the best street art done in one of the most traditional neighbourhood of Lisbon - Bairro Alto. Go to the blog (www.museuefemero.blogspot.com) and download the map with the location of the works. You can also download the poadcasts, to hear on the iPod and then just take a tour in this open air museum. The English version is available. It's a new way of seeing street art and it seems it's going to have more works soon!

Enjoy!
