A neighbourhood cafe on Campo 24 de Agosto, a square a short walk from the city centre. The main draw is the francesinha — Porto's famous layered meat sandwich drowned in a spiced beer-and-tomato sauce — which here is done well and without tourist-trap pricing. Also serves coffee and milkshakes. A good spot to eat like a local rather than a visitor.
