Bugo Art Burgers is located at Miguel Bombarda Street, the Art Galleries’ Street of Oporto. Its main goal is to challenge the predefined concept of a hamburger place.
Having the Burger as its main theme, the menu was inspired by international gastronomy combined with the essence of Portuguese cuisine. Textures, flavors and sensations make BUGO a restaurant of experiences.

BUGO offers a different and varied menu that surprises. Where else could you taste a hamburger with chopsticks?
