Located on the Rua Senhora da Luz, in the heart of the seaside Foz district, is one of
Oporto’s** **most well-known café-confeitarias.

One cannot speak
of Tavi without mentioning traditional sweets, home-made ice creams,
chocolates, croissants, cakes and gourmet specialities.

Since its
remodeling in 2006, it has also operated as a full restaurant, meaning that at
any time of the day you can choose any one of the many gourmet dishes on offer
à la carte or opt for a light meal such as a quiche, crepe or salad.

In the summer, Tavi is open until midnight. From the
outdoor patio, you can enjoy the enthralling sunset while floating on a sea of
taste sensations. During the winter, the outdoor
patio is covered and heated, with throw-blankets available should you decide to
wrap yourself up and settle in for a cozy stay…

**Example menu:**

- *Octopuss Carpaccio with finely chopped peppers, olive
oil and balsamic dressing.*

*- Duck magret with raspberry chutney, garnished with
Portuguese style potatoes.*

*- 5 peppers steak, grilled lean-meat steak with 5
peppers sauce, garnished with potatoes salt-fried in olive oil and parsley*

*- Saffron tagliatelle with prawns and fresh, green
asparagus, sprinkled with coriander*
