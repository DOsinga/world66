The FC Porto Museum by BMG welcomes its visitors with the unique view of the Dragon Valkyrie, work of art of Joana Vasconcelos.

It offers a narrative of 120 years of history, spread through seven thousand square meters that hosts 27 thematic areas, while exhibiting 200 trophies and 280 videos, 80 per cent of which in an interactive environment.
