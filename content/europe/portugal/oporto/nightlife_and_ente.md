---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment"
type: section
---

Oporto is famous for it very much indoors but excited nightlife. You may start by going to samo glamorous bars at the Ribeira or in the old-center of the city. Then, go for one of the truly good discos like *Indústria*, *Chic* or *Estado Novo*.

If you're into the gay scene, notice first that Oporto is considerable more homofobic than [Lisbon](/europe/portugal/lisbon), for exemple. Must start your nightrip with *Lusitano*, a quite gay-friendly fashionable bar, and then go to Boys'r'us, a nice small disco, or *Pride*, a little bad-frequented but funny. To finish the night, *Moinho* is the most appropriate, problably the better gay disco in the city.
