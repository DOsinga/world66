title: Vila Nova de Gaia Port Cellars
type: poi

Technically in a different city across the river, the cellars where port wine is aged and bottled are the reason everyone comes to Porto. Taylor's, Graham's, and Sandeman each offer tours with tastings; the smaller independents like Ramos Pinto and Kopke give a less polished but more interesting version. Taylor's sits highest on the hill and has the best terrace view back over Porto.
