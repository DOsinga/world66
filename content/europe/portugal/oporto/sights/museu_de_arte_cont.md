The Museu de Arte Contemporânea de Serralves is part of a world
network (wich includes Tate Modern and other major
museums) and has featured major exhibitions such as
Andy Warhol, Francis Bacon, Nan Goldin and many other important
artits. The building's distinctive lines and its unique
location are well worth a trip. Be sure not to miss the
gardens and the Casa de Serralves.
