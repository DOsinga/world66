title: "Cathedral Sé"
type: poi
category: "Sight"
latitude: 41.1432
longitude: -8.6112
---

The fortress-like cathedral at the top of the old town, half Romanesque and half Gothic with a Baroque silver altar added later. The cloister, lined with blue and white azulejos depicting the life of the Virgin, is more rewarding than the interior. Great views west over the river from the terrace out front.
