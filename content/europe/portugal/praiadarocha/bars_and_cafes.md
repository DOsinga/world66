---
title: "Bars and Cafes"
type: section
---

The clifftop promenade (Avenida Tomás Cabreira) is lined with bars and restaurants. The resort has a lively nightlife in summer — bars stay open late and there is live music and clubs. Cheers Bar is a long-established option on the strip. Prices are reasonable by Western European standards; local beers and Algarve wine are good value.
