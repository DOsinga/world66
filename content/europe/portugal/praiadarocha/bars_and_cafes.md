**Enjoy the Night with Praia da Rocha Bars and Cafes**

**Praia da Rocha Bars and Cafes** are exciting places to be in this Portuguese town. The commercial, busy area of Praia teems with bars and cafes of different style and drinks.

**Cheers Bars**

It is one of the most popular among the Praia da Rocha Bars. It offers drinks and different types of beverages from international to local drinks. You can enjoy great dancing also, and lots of mingling with the locals and other tourists.

It is also a single ladies&rsquo; bar where you may meet your dream man. The nightlife extends until the wee hours of dawn in this vibrant Portuguese Town, where wine tastes like an elixir of joy, and an ambrosia that can wipe away all your traveling fatigue.

They have also a "videoke" where customers can sing along and listen to their voices. You can test your mettle in singing if you so desire without anyone minding. You can go alone or stay with a group. Being with a group is ideal because you would enjoy more of the wining, the dining and the singing. The bar offers some dishes that can go with the beverages. There is a wide variety of them, for those with culinary expertise.
**
Prices**

The price of drinks is reasonable for the relaxation and entertainment it offers guests. The food is also within an acceptable price range. If you buy in bulk, however, the prices will be even more affordable.
