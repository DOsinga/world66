---
title: "Beaches in Praia da Rocha"
type: section
---

Portugal is famous for its **Praia da Rocha Beaches**. It is a rocky beach that stretches on the coastline of Portimao, Algarve and the south of Portugal. The beaches in Praia da Rocha make up a wide, white sandy coastline that overlooks the North Atlantic Ocean. Here are some of the things that you need to know about the beaches in Praia da Rocha.

**Praia da Rocha **

Praia da Rocha is a wide beach that faces the North Atlantic Ocean. The beach is a favorite destination of beach combers, tourists and locals alike. Moreover, it has a large parking area and is very close to most hotels in Algarve. It also has bars, restaurants and toilets for added convenience. However, Praia da Rocha is jam-packed with tourists during weekends, so you might encounter problems with finding a vacant spot where you can lay down your beach blanket. This is a public beach, so you do not need to pay for anything.

**Praia do Alem&atilde;o**

If you want to escape the hustle and bustle of other beaches in Algarve, you can walk the vast expanse of the beach and go to Praia do Alem&atilde;o, located west from Praia da Rocha, for a more calming environment. This beach bordering Praia da Rocha also comes complete with restaurants, toilets and foot walks leading to the sandy bars, caves and lagoons on this public beach.

**Praia do Vau**

Praia do Vau is situated near Praia do Alem&atilde;o. This beach is surrounded by cheap holiday apartments, which usually cost $20 to $80 per night. The beach comes complete with a spacious parking area, sun beds and beach bars. You may want to enjoy a day with your family at this beach.

**Praia de Alvor **

This beach has a historic fishing village nearby. The beach in Praia de Alvor is very serene, which is perfect for people who want to escape the noise of the city. This beach offers a lot of water activities like windsurfing, waterskiing and sailing. The beach is also close to many decent bars and cafes. The beach is also situated in close proximity to a salt marsh, perfect for bird watching enthusiasts.

**Meia Praia**

The sea is very calm at Meia Praia; a lot of people love to go here because it is a perfect place for children to swim. The beach does not run out of good restaurants, cafes and snack bars, and windsurfing and paragliding are popular water sport in this area.
