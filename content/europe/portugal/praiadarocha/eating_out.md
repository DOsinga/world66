Unlike what you may think, **Praia da Rocha restaurants **don&rsquo;t specialize in traditional Portuguese cuisine; instead, they try to satisfy the demands of each tourist by offering a wide variety of international dishes. The restaurant life is focused on Tomas Cabreira Avenue – the main street of Praia da Rocha. In one of the many restaurants there, called the Big Red&rsquo;s Rib Shack, you'll have the chance to try the tastiest grilled ribs in Praia da Rocha.**
**

**Big Red's Rib Shack
**

Entering this small and cheerful place, you will be immediately welcomed by a friendly waiter. He will take your order really fast, and you won&rsquo;t have to wait for too long until your meal is ready. Big Red&rsquo;s Rib Shack opens up at 6 p.m. and works until late at night. Keep in mind that the Big Red&rsquo;s Rib Shack is closed every Monday.

The restaurant prepares undoubtedly the best grilled ribs in Praia da Rocha. Not only are they extremely tasty, but the portions are huge and the prices are unbelievably low. For example, at a price of $14, you can order a big rack of ribs with chips and salad. In addition, you can choose from 8 different sauces that come along with the ribs. And if you&rsquo;re not interested in the ribs, you can always order something else from the extensive menu of well-prepared food, like the delicious deep fried brie for example.
