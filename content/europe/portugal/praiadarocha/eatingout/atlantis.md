This restaurant is excellent value and very up market. Waiter service excellent. Went with daughter and husband meal cost around £40 for two courses and bottle of wine and two soft drinks. Had Tagliatele with Prawns (excellent) a big portion though. Husband had portugese steak (steak cooked in a mushroomy sauce with slices potato around the bowl all cooked in the oven). I would recommend this.
