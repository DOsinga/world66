**Enjoy the Sights of the Fiesa Festival in** **Praia da Rocha, Portugal**

If you want to enjoy your stay in Praia da Rocha, then the best time to go is during the **Praia da Rocha Festivals** held in the area. Praia da Rocha is a great beach town filled with resorts and located off the coast of Algarve, Portugal. This town is popular for its scenic beaches, so one of the best festivals to witness in Praia da Rocha is the Fiesa Festival.

#### **The Fiesa Festival**

This Fiesa Festival is a shortened term for the Fiesa International Sand Sculpture Festival. The festival is held during the summer months. The dates of the 2010 Fiesa Festival are May 22 to September 21, from 10:00 a.m. until midnight each day. The festival has been held annually since 2003 and is attended by people who like to build sand sculptures and those who want to take pictures of the sand sculptures.

**Attractions during the Fiesa Festival**

The beach is generally packed with beach combers and sand sculptors. You can see traditional sand castles and Hollywood-inspired sand sculptures. The festival is open to the public, but you have to pay for tickets to enter the festival. Children younger than five are admitted for free when accompanied by an adult. The beach is filled with a lot of tourists during the Fiesa Festival, and getting around can be quite difficult.
