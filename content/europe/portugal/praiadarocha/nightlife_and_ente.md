---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment in Praia da Rocha"
type: section
---

The **Praia da Rocha nightlife** is varied, as this is a very
popular resort. Most people come for the beach, but there is still
plenty to do once the sun goes down. This is a great place to visit at
any time of the year, as the summers are hot and dry and the winters are
quiet, warm and pleasant. Happily, you can go to the beach any time of
the year in Praia da Rocha.
**Nightlife at the Resort

**

Along the esplanade, you can find some of the top bars and
nightclubs. The Avenida Tomas Cabreira runs along the esplanade and
contains many glitzy bars. This was one of the first resorts in the
Algarve region. The Phoenix is the place to visit if you like to dance.
It stays open until 6 a.m. The Red Eye is a great bar where you can get
mixed drinks and play some pool or darts. This is a great spot to talk
with the locals.
**Great Dance Clubs

**

A fantastic disco and party bar is On The Rocks. As the name states,
this bar is perched right on the cliff side. On The Rocks offers great
music and lots of space on the dance floor. You can get up and dance or
simply opt to “people watch.” There is also a cliff side esplanade. They
show all the major sporting events on the large screen TV, and offer
your favorite drinks. If you are a fan of jazz and soul music, make sure
to stop into Bar Amuras. The music is live every Friday, and it all has
a Latin twist.

Katedral is another late night dance venue that is very popular among
business professionals and younger crowds. The music selection is
current, and there are three bars that encircle the central dance floor.
There is a terrace bar that spreads along the back, and you can take a
break from dancing and enjoy a great view of the beach.
**A Local Favorite

**

Farmer’s Pub is another local favorite, and it is one of the oldest
bars in the town. This has been a central spot for nightlife since 1980,
and pretty much everyone has stopped in for a beer here at some point
in time. You can expect to hear live music during the summer, and they
also show all of the main sporting events. This is the perfect spot to
start or end your evening.
**The Casino

**

You can also spend your evening in the massive casino at Praia da
Rocha. During the afternoon and early hours of the evening, you can play
the slot machines or table games. In the evening, the casino offers
live music and international shows. The casino has the main stage or
theater in town.
