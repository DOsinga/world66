Póvoa de Varzim offers several miles of golden sand beaches. Póvoa is known for more than a century as a beach therapy area.

### [Salgueira Beach](/europe/portugal/pvoa_de_varzim/beaches/salgueira_beach)

Salgueira Beach is *Passeio Alegre*'s north end beach and
it is a regional classic surfing beach, near the beach there's several
swimming pool complexes, restaurants, bullring, etc. In the beach you
can find beach bars and Buddha nightclub.



[[rate it]](/europe/portugal/pvoa_de_varzim/beaches/salgueira_beach)

### [Aguçadoura Beach](/europe/portugal/pvoa_de_varzim/beaches/aguadoura_beach)

located in the city's rural outskirts, Aguçadoura is the best surfing beach of Póvoa de Varzim, with a large dune area,
making it the longest beach in Póvoa. Every year in early August
there's the *Aguçadoura Beach Party*.

young straight couples. For flirting the best beaches are the quiet
rocky beaches north of the city, namely **Quião Beach ***(Praia do Quião)*. ### [Nudist beach](/europe/portugal/pvoa_de_varzim/beaches/rio_alto_beach)

Rio Alto Beach, also known as Estela Beach, is one of the few and the most popular naturist beach in Northern Portugal. it is located near the Estela Golf Course, just north of the Aguçadoura Surfing Beach.



[[rate it]](/europe/portugal/pvoa_de_varzim/beaches/rio_alto_beach)

### [Redonda Beach](/europe/portugal/pvoa_de_varzim/beaches/redonda_beach)

Redonda Beach is the south end beach of *Passeio Alegre* near the casino, the seaport and the *Passeio Alegre* square. In the beach there's the city beach library "Diana Bar", NOX nightclub and the historic café known as *Guardasol* the best place where you can taste the original "Francesinha Poveira", the city's very own fastfood.



### [Lagoa Beach](/europe/portugal/pvoa_de_varzim/beaches/lagoa_beach)

Lagoa Beach is located in a natural cove near highrises, it has one of the most famous beach bars of the city "*Bar da Praia*" literally "Beach Bar"
