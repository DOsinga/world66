Museums in Póvoa de Varzim

### [Museu de Etnografia e História](/europe/portugal/pvoa_de_varzim/museums/museu_de_etnografi)

Póvoa de Varzim ethnographic museum over the rich and unique fishing history and culture of Póvoa de Varzim in a historic noblesmen's home.

Museum is closed from September 2006 and will reopen on June 28th 2008 for expansion and reorganisation.



### [Archeology Museum of Cividade de Terroso](/europe/portugal/pvoa_de_varzim/museums/archeology_museum)

A small museum in the entrance of Cividade de Terroso, an hillfort Celtic city dating to 900 B.C., that contains some archaeological findings.



### [Museu da Igreja Românica de Rates](/europe/portugal/pvoa_de_varzim/museums/igreja_romnica_m)

Religious Museum in the historic rural town of São Pedro de Rates over the local ancient Romanesque Church.
