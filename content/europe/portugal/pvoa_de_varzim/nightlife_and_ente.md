Póvoa de Varzim entertainment district "Passeio Alegre" (in English "Happy Pavement") offers some of the trendiest nightclubs in Northern Portugal.

The
best time to go out is on Fridays-and Saturday-nights. Nighlife here starts ticking from 1 A.M. onwards and can last even after the sunrise. After leaving the nighclubs many head to bakeries to have breakfast.

Before 1 A.M. people often have a drink in cafés, bars and esplanades, gamble or see a cabaret in the casino, before heading to the beach, were the nighclubs are located.

### [Berber](/europe/portugal/pvoa_de_varzim/nightlife_and_ente/berber)

Decorated in Arabic style, Berber Tea Room of Póvoa de Varzim is located near Carvalhido esplanade area, in the middle of Avenida dos Banhos ( Passeio Alegre area).

Berber has pillow area, lowered tables in Arabic style, Arabic music and belly dance performers.
