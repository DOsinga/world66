Decorated in Arabic style, Berber Tea Room of Póvoa de Varzim is located near Carvalhido esplanade area, in the middle of Avenida dos Banhos ( Passeio Alegre area).

Berber has pillow area, lowered tables in Arabic style, Arabic music and belly dance performers.
