small shops at which to buy a variety of items: Port Wine, Traditional Handicrafts, Portuguese Music (Fado, Folk, Contemporary,

Adufes), Antiques, Books, Pottery, Embroideries, and Sintra's Cakes

### [Loja do Arco](/europe/portugal/sintra/shopping/loja_do_arco)

Loja do Arco, in the heart of the historic center of Sintra, has
dedicated itself since its founding in 1998 to disseminating Portuguese
culture in the areas of literature, music, and traditional crafts.

Loja
do Arco’s particular distinction is its collection of the **music** of
Portugal. With about 2000 available titles, it offers fado, Portuguese
folk, ethnic, classical, Renaissance, and contemporary music.
