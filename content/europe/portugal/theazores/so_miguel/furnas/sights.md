### [Terra Nostra Garden](/europe/portugal/theazores/so_miguel/furnas/sights/terra_nostra_garde)

One of the islands most beautiful gardens with endemic plants, ponds, streams and a basin with hot yellowish water which is supposed to make you young.Fine

**Entry: **€4 for adults and €2 for children (aged 2-10)
