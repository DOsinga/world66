### [Colégio 27](/europe/portugal/theazores/so_miguel/ponta_delgada/eating_out/colgio_27)

*International cooking, live music and jazz lounge club*

Trendy place in the middle of town nice place for a drink and chat.

Dinner menus on blackboards, 5* international cusine with above average prices.

Friendly staff that speaks swedish, english and portuguese.
