The main shopping mall, in the northern part of the city centre. Walk up from Praça Vasco da Gama or take the green minibus (Course B). Anchored by a Modelo hypermarket (Mon–Fri 9:00–23:00, Sat from 8:30, Sun 8:30–13:00), with the usual high-street chains — Zara, Mango, Massimo Dutti, The Body Shop — plus a cinema. Mall shops open daily 10:00–23:00.
