Shopping is not the main reason that one visits the Azores, but it can keep you busy for a day or so.

The stores in the centre are generally open 09:00-12:30 and 14:00-18:30 on weekdays, 09-13 on saturdays and closed on sundays.

### [Parque Atlântico](/europe/portugal/theazores/so_miguel/ponta_delgada/shopping/parque_atlntico)

Located at the nothern part of the city centra. If you walk up from Praça Vasco da Gama you eventually reach it. You can also take the green minibus, Course B.

Here you find Modelo, Massimo Dutti, Mango, Zara, The Body Shop, Burger King, Pizza Hut,

**Stores open:**

Monday - Sunday 10h-23h

**Hypermarket open:**

Monday - Friday 9h-23h

Saturday 08:30h-23h
