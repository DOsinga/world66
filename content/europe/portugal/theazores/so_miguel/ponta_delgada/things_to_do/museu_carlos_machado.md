The main museum of Ponta Delgada, split across two buildings in the old town. The natural history section covers the geology and wildlife of the Azores — the volcanic origins of the islands, endemic bird species, and deep-sea specimens dredged from the surrounding ocean. The ethnographic collection next door focuses on traditional Azorean crafts, religious art, and everyday objects from the 18th and 19th centuries. Worth an hour or two.
