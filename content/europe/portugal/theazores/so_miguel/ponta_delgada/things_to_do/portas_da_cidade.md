Three elegant Baroque archways that served as the ceremonial entrance to the old city. Built in the 18th century from local basalt and limestone, they stand in the main square facing the harbour and are the defining symbol of Ponta Delgada. The central arch is the tallest; all three are elaborately carved. Best viewed at night when they are lit up.
