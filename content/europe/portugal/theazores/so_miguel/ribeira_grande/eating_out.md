### [Ala Bote](/europe/portugal/theazores/so_miguel/ribeira_grande/eating_out/ala_bote)

On the shores of the Atlantic you can see the sun go down while enjoying among the best food served on São Miguel.

The staff speaks little english, but menues are available in several languages. See the chart of fished on the wall near the bar if you aren't familiar with the fishes in the menu.
