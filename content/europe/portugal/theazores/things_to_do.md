---
title: "Things to do in The Azores"
type: section
---

The Azores is a stunning cache of beautiful lagoons, pristine
islands, heritage sites and stimulating adventure sports that
mesmerizes visitors with its sheer dexterity**. The Azores Itinerary** offers visitors a well-planned itinerary for 7 days in this picturesque locale.

**Day 1 Lagoa adas Sete Cidades**

Visit Lagoa adas Sete Cidades, a gorgeous lagoon located in the
northwestern region of Ponta Delgada which was once a thriving
swimming and fishing retreat. It is well past its heydays now, but
still boasts of a few remains of an erstwhile rich ecosystem.

**Day 2 Take A day tour of Furnas**

Take A day tour of Furnas on a cruise ship that departs from the
south coast and passes through the serene Lagoa Village and Caloura
point. Visitors can stop at Vila Franca for a historical sightseeing
visit and refreshments break. The next halt is the valley of Furnas and
its postcard perfect lake encircled by hot springs. Mouth watering
meals are cooked in the boiling ground of the destination. Post Lunch
the tour arrives at the Furnas Village for a visit to the botanical
gardens and the natural mineral water springs. Visit the north coast
tea plantation and Santa Iria viewpoint. The tour culminates with a
return to the cruise ship or hotel. The tour costs € 156 per car for up
to four people.

**Day 3 ** **Sete Cidades**

Embark from your cruise ship or hotel through the rocky mountain
path to the enchanting twin lakes of Sete Cidades. The tour stops at
the aptly named Vista do Rei, which offers a brilliant view of the
region. Visit the Capelas pineapple plantation and return along the
same route. Tour costs € 81 per car upto 4 people.

**Day 4 ** **Whale Watching at Pico Island**

Fly to Faial from Ponta Delgada and take a boat to Pico Island for
watching a spectacular entourage of whales. Adventure buffs can also
climb the Pico Mountain.

**Day 5 Terceira Island**

Take a boat to S. Jorge and fly to the captivating Terceira Island.
Rent a car to explore the hidden beauty of all the surrounding islands.

**Day 6 Angra**

While on your way back from Terceira, Spend a day at Angra, an
ancient and rich historical city that has been incorporated as a UNESCO
heritage patrimony city.

**Day 7 Velvet Adventure Sailing**

The tour organizer offers boat trips between the islands surrounding
the Azores and moors on isolated marinas and peaceful, sparsely
populated bays.

The Azores is a super island retreat that awards visitors a true
serene castaway feeling in the backdrop of a rich marine ecology and
picturesque surrounding areas.
