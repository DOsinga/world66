Placed on the right banks of Tagus river lies the Portuguese Capital – **Lisbon**.
City that stretches between its hills and valleys, a city filled with
monuments some of which dating from the Discoveries. But not only
monuments make history and a whole lot more can be seen in this
beautiful metropolis.
