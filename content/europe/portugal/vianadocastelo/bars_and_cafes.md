---
title: "Bars and Cafes"
type: section
---

Viana do Castelo's nightlife is modest but friendly, concentrated in the old centre. Most bars are small, casual places where the crowd skews young — the city has a polytechnic that keeps things lively during term time.

**Bar Glamour** — A friendly spot popular with people in their twenties. Thursdays are the best night. Rua da Bandeira, 177.

**Bar Jardim** — A more refined option at the Melo Alvim inn, where you can drink to the sound of a piano. Av. Conde da Carreira, 28.

**Bar Galeria Nazoni** — A small, pleasant bar near the centre that draws a younger crowd. Rua do Espírito Santo, 10.
