**Restaurants**

**Conde do Camarido**

At the Restaurant Conde do Camarido, enjoy the tradition of our region cooking, as well as international specialities. 

address: Estalagem Melo Alvim, Av. Conde da Carreira, 28

tel: 258 810 8200

openings: Lunch, from 12,30 AM to 03,00 PM, Dinner, from 07,30 PM to 11,00 PM

**Os Três Potes**

A typical restaurant of the province of minho, installed in an old public oven. There you can see folk dance and music every saturday dinner (and fridays on high season). Nice and warm. Try "gambas grelhadas" (grilled schrimps), "papas sarrabulho" ( a typical pork dish), grilled fishes, octopus, [more..](/europe/portugal/vianadocastelo/eatingout/ostrspotes)

address: Beco Fornos, 9

tel: 258 82 99 28

**Taberna do Valentim**

Best tavern in town, closed on sundays. Try "caldeirada" (assorted fish) and "natas do céu" (heaven cream) at this typical tavern speciallized in fresh fish (it's so speciallized in fresh fish that it is closed in days of storm, when fish boats cannot go to sea). 

address: Rua Monsenhor Manuel Machado, 180 Bairro da Ribeira

**Casa d'Armas**

This restaurant is in an old medieval house, also one of the best restaurants in town, and for some people, the best. Worth reading the menu. 

address: Largo 5 de Outubro, 30
