---
title: "Day Trips"
type: section
---

Snagov Lake, about 40 kilometres north, has a monastery on an island that is the supposed burial place of Vlad Țepeș — the historical Dracula. The lake is pleasant for a half-day escape from the city. Mogoșoaia Palace, closer in to the northeast, is an elegant 18th-century manor in the Brâncovenesc style, set beside a lake and surrounded by parks. For a longer day trip, the Prahova Valley towns of Sinaia and Brașov are reachable by train in two to three hours and offer mountain scenery, medieval streets, and Peleș Castle.
