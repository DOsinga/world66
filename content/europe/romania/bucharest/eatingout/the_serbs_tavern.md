"The Serbian's Den", or in Romanian known as "Taverna Sarbului", is the perfect place to have a generous meal in a rustic environment. The Serbian Den's includes a chain of 4 restaurants across Romania located in: Bucharest, [Sinaia](/europe/romania/sinaia), [Brasov](/europe/romania/brasov) and [Timisoara](/europe/romania/timisoara)

In Bucharest, The Serbian's Den (Taverna Sarbului) is located close to Herastrau Park and offers a great variety of Eastern European food which includes meat based meals but also savoury local varieties of food which you will not find anywhere else.

The wines are also something that you shouldn’t miss. The house wine along with other couple of dozen famous wines around the world will make good company to a balanced flavoured course.
