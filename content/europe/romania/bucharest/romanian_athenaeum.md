title: Romanian Athenaeum
type: poi

A neoclassical concert hall on Calea Victoriei, built in 1888 and home to the George Enescu Philharmonic. The domed interior is decorated with a 70-metre fresco depicting scenes from Romanian history. Even if you do not attend a concert, the building is worth seeing from outside — it is the symbol of Bucharest's cultural aspirations.
