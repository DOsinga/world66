---
title: "Shopping"
type: section
---

The Old Centre around Lipscani street is the most interesting area for shopping — antique dealers, bookshops, and small Romanian designers line the cobbled streets. For everyday needs, Bucharest has large malls including AFI Cotroceni and Băneasa Shopping City. The open-air markets are worth exploring: Obor Market is a vast, chaotic affair selling everything from fresh produce to second-hand clothes.
