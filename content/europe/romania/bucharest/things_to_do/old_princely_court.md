The ruins of a palace built in the 1400s and expanded by Vlad Țepeș (the historical Dracula) in the 1500s. Set in the heart of the Old Centre, the excavated foundations and a small museum give a sense of medieval Bucharest. Nearby stands the Curtea Veche church, the oldest in the city, dating to 1559.
