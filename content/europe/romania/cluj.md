When you go through the outskirts of Cluj you prepare for the worst. Scores of ugly factories line the streets. This first impression is misleading however. The centre retains some of its glory form the days when it was a fine Hungarian town in Transsylvania. The town has quite a few nice sights, as well as a good theatre and some museums.
