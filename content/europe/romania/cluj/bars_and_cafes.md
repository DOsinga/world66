### [Gothic Cafe "Umbra de Noapte"](/europe/romania/cluj/barsandcafes/gothic_cafe_umbra)

**Cafe/Bar/Absintherie - the only full-time goth location in Romania** The whole Cafe Umbra crew speaks Romanian and English, partly
also Hungarian, French, Japaneze, Spanish and German.

"Umbra de Noapte" (Nachtschatten / Night Shade) is a place where you
can meet with friends or relax and read dark literature or
international magazines (from Germany, the Netherlands, France,
Finland, Italy, Australia and the UK) from our own library.
Enjoy varieties of coffee and tea specialties, softdrinks, beer, wine,
spirits; Romanias largest variety of Absinth; [more..](/europe/romania/cluj/barsandcafes/gothic_cafe_umbra)
