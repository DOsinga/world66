---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Cluj-Napoca's museums and historical sites cluster around the old center, most within walking distance of Piața Unirii. The Transylvanian History Museum is the standout, tracing the region's story from the Paleolithic through the Roman and medieval periods. The Art Museum, housed in the Bánffy Palace right on the main square, has a strong collection of Transylvanian painting and a striking baroque altar.
