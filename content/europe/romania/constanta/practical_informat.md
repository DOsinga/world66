Constanta Residence Apartments is situated in Constanta, just 1
mile from the Black Sea Coast at the gateway to the Mamaia Resort.

All year round, it offers comfortable 3-star accommodation in
spacious studios and 2-room apartments, equipped with modern furniture
and all necessary amenities.

The apartments offer individuality and independence and are a perfect
base either for leisure or business trips. There is no catering
service, but you can prepare your own food in the well-equipped kitchen.

For a stay of over 8 nights free cleaning services are offered.
