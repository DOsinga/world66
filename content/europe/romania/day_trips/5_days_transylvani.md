**Day
1 – BUCHAREST**. Arrival
Bucharest , at the Henri Coanda International Airport. Meeting your
guide and transfer to your hotel in Bucharest, in a 4**** Hotel.
Based on your flight time, short Bucharest city tour . Overnight in
Bucharest
**Day
2 - BUCHAREST- COZIA – SIBIU(270 km)**

After
breakfast we are leaving Bucharest, heading to Sibiu. On the way to
Sibiu, stop and visit the Cozia Monastery. Arrival in Sibiu . Sibiu
City Tour including The Large Square, The Small Square, Liars Bridge,
The Evangelical Church, Huet Square. Overnight and accommodation in a
**** Hotel in Sibiu.**
****Day
3 - SIBIU - BIERTAN – SIGHISOARA(110 km)**

After
breakfast visit The Brukenthal Museum and Astra Museum. We leave
Sibiu an heading to Sighisoara. Stop and visit Biertan fortified
Church, attested since 1283, now part of UNESCO World Heritage .
Arrival in the beautiful Sighisoara Citadel, (UNESCO World Heritage)
. Overnight and accommodation in a *** Hotel in Sighisoara .**
****Day
4 - SIGHISOARA - BRASOV(120 km)**

After
breakfast, a walking tour in the Medieval Citadel in medieval
clothes(if you wish) – it will take about 2 and a half hours. Try
the local Plum Brandy and wine. Traditional
Lunch in Dracula's House. Visit
the History Museum. The Museum consists of the clock tower, torture
chamber and the medieval arms exhibition. Arriving in Brasov.
Overnight and accommodation in a *** Hotel in Brasov

**Day
5 - BRASOV – SINAIA - BUCHAREST AIRPORT(180
km)**

After
breakfast, Brasov city Tour including The Black Church – here you
find the biggest medieval carpets exposition from the Eastern Europe.
Leaving Brasov and arrival in Sinaia. Visit the famous Peles Castle,
the last residence of the Romanian Kings. Transfer to Bucharest
International Airport, end of tour.
