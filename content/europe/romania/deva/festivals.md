---
title: "Festivals"
type: section
---

Every second week in January each year, DEVA is the centre of a very colourful festival 'Calusarul Transilvan', which translates very loosely as the Transylvanian Morris Dancers Festival.

DEVA is a good hub to reach traditional crafts and folk art festivals such as 'Târgu de Fete' on Gaina Mountain (Girls Fair), end of July each year, then 'The Kiss Fair' spring festival in Halmagiu, also in the Western Transylvanian Alps (Apuseni) in March, the Nedeia festival in Avram Iancu, 2nd Sunday in June, and many others. Eastern Orthodox Easter and Christmas periods each year are very popular with folk art and traditional dances and carols.
