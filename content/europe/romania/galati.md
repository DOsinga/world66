Located in the eastern part of [Romania](/europe/romania), the city is based on the siderurgic industry.

The SIDEX factory is employing 10% of the working population of the city.

With more then 300.000 persons, the Galati city is the 4th as population in Romania.

Dunarea De Jos University of Galati is an important univercity center.
