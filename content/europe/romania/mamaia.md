Mamaia is Romania's premier Black Sea resort, a narrow strip of land running 7 km between a magnificent sandy beach and Lake Siutghiol, just north of [Constanta](/europe/romania/constanta). The sand is fine and the beach is wide — one of the best on the Romanian coast.

The resort was purpose-built and caters especially well to families. From May to October you can try snorkelling, scuba diving, and paragliding, and the evenings bring a lively scene of restaurants, bars, and nightclubs. Mamaia also makes a good base for day trips to the ruins of the ancient Greek fortress at Histria, traditional villages in the Dobrogea region, and the [Danube Delta](/europe/romania/danubedelta) further north.
