The 6th century BC fortified town of Callatis has become today's balneary spa of Mangalia, with a special cure hotel. Here, as at Eforie Nord, Eforie Sud and the spa at Neptun, a wide variety of therapeuric treatments are available, including mineral-rich mud baths, thalassotherapy and the world famous Romanian Gerovital cure. Medical staff are highly quialified and clinics and consulting rooms remain open year-round. So you can combine professional treatment with all the pleasures of a seaside sojourn.
