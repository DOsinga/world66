---
title: "Beaches"
type: section
---

Pitesti is situated near the Arges river. There is a beach over there that also has 2 swiming pools (one for children and one for adults). There are also another 3 swiming pools not very far away from Arges river, but those are not related to it. There is also an enclosed olympic swimming pool; within the building, there are fitness rooms, saunas, and other rooms designated to recreational activities.
