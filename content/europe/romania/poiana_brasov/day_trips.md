a break from the city and head to the ski slopes of Poiana Brasov.

a perfect location to take to the hills. This independent tour
package includes accommodation in the modern 4 star Hotel Alpin,
airport transfers from Bucharest, 5 days worth of ski passes and if
needed a refresher ski lesson.
