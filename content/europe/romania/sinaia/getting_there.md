---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

Sinaia is about two hours north of [Bucharest](/europe/romania/bucharest) by road or rail, in the Prahova Valley. The small train station — a former stop on the Orient Express — connects Sinaia to Bucharest and the rest of Romania's rail network. Buses and minibuses also run from Bucharest. By air, fly into Bucharest's Henri Coandă International Airport and continue by train, bus or taxi.
