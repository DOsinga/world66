Sinaia, located two hours from the Capital, Bucharest, is accessible by rail, car, bus, or air.

### [Sinaia by Air](/europe/romania/sinaia/getting_there/sinaia)

Bucharest's aiport, Henri Coandă International connects Sinaia, [Bucharest](/europe/romania/bucharest) and the rest of [Romania](/europe/romania) to the world, with daily flights to all major points in Europe. Sinaia can be reached from Bucharest by taxi, bus or rail.

type: By Air



### [Sinaia by Rail](/europe/romania/sinaia/getting_there/sinaia_by_rail)

Sinaia's small trian station links the alpine village to the rest of Europe. The station, one of the nicest in [Romania](/europe/romania), was formally a stop of the famed Orient Express.

type: By Rail
