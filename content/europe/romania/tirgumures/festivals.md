**Tirgu Mures Festivals **are among the reasons that entice you to visit Tirgu Mures, a city found in Romania. Their festivals are related to their culture and way of life.

**Beer Festival**

The Beer Festival is an open air festival in Tirgu Mures. There are booths on the field that serve different kinds of beer. The visitors enjoy hanging-out with their friends. As they sip the beer from the glass, music is played to entertain them.

**Alter-Native International Short Film Festival**

The Alter-Native International Short Film Festival is an international film festival that started in year 1993. It aims to help and promote the art made by the fresh regional film makers and film artists. Cinematographers and other film fans from more than 18 countries come together to show their expertise and knowledge in the film making field. Alter-Native International Short Film Festival is a substitute of the worldwide television and movie that promotes the culture of a certain country. Through Alter-Native International Short Film Festival, one is not only able to have the chance to interact with the other artists, but there is also the chance to have a deeper understanding of the other country&rsquo;s tradition and culture.

**Peninsula Festival**

The Peninsula Festival is a festival that happens in the month of August. This festival is among the biggest festivals in Tirgu Mures that features international artists who possesses love for the music. The Peninsula festival is a four-day festival of fun. The activities included in this celebration are sports, music and culture.

**Wine Making Festival**

Wine Making Festival is among the festivals in Tirgu Mures that is celebrated every October. This celebration symbolizes the start of the grape harvest.
