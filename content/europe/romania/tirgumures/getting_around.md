---
title: "Getting Around in Tirgu mures"
type: section
---

**Tirgu Mures Transportation** provides comfortable travel within the city or from the city to its neighboring places. Tirgu Mures is a small city in Romania that can be toured by foot. However, if you find this uncomfortable, there are several mode of transportation that can transfer you from place to place within the city or outside of Tirgu Mures.

**Airplane**

Airplane is among the transportation methods that you can use to reach the city of Tirgu Mures. The airport in Tirgu Mures is found in Vidrasau, which is about 9 miles southwest of the heart of the city. From the airport, you can reach the city proper of Tirgu Mures by riding a shuttle bus or taxi.

**Bus**

Bus is among the means of transportation in Tirgu Mures that is used for domestic transport. It connects the city to the other places in Romania. The bus has a daily schedule from Tirgu Mures to the other part of Romania.

**Car**

Car can be used to travel around the city of Tirgu Mures. You can bring your own car or you can rent a car in the city. Be sure to fix the fare ahead before going for a ride. Do some bargaining if you think that you are being overcharged.

**Train**

Train is also available in the city of Tirgu Mures. The main train station is in Targu Mures, and the railway station is situated near the Citadel. There is a daily schedule of trains from or to Budapest. The travel lasts for 9 hours. The trip to the other parts of Western Europe is via Budapest.
