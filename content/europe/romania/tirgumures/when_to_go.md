---
title: "When to Go in Tirgu mures"
type: section
---

If you're planning a trip to **Tirgu Mures, when to go **will certainly be a major consideration. It's only natural to consider the time of year, but you'll likely also want to plan around major events in the area. The Peninsula Festival is Tirgu Mures' famous music festival, and is a great reason to visit this beautiful city.

**Seasons**

- Winter - Bundle up warm, as it can get extremely chilly in the mountains, but those brave enough to face the cold (it can drop to below freezing on occasion) will appreciate the snow-covered charm of the town and mountains.
- Spring - March through May sees the flowers popping into bloom and the days warming up to where you can enjoy outdoor activities during the day and only require an extra layer for those chilly evenings and early mornings.
- Summer - Once June hits, the chill of spring is just a distant memory. The days are warm, but thanks to the altitude never become exceedingly hot. And, as an added bonus, the Peninsula Festival (see below) comes to town!
- Autumn - Summer fades in autumn come September, and the days are often dry and cool. As in the spring, you'll need a layer or two to keep you comfortable when the sun isn't high in the sky, but all in all, it's a pleasant time of year to visit Tirgu Mures.

**The Peninsula Festival**

Tirgu Mures' main event comes around in July or August of every year: The Peninsula Festival. This rock, pop and electro music extravaganza is an excellent reason to visit the Romanian city. Bands from all over the world come to play the event, in recent years even including such notables as Korn, Nine Inch Nails, The Prodigy and Chumbawamba. The festival includes more than music, though, with bungee jumping, comedy routines and more rounding out the days. The Peninsula Festival is the perfect time to visit Tirgu Mures!
