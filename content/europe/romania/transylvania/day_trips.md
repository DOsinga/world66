Most
of the Transylvanian cities
are very old settlements.
Some of them we inherited
from the Romans. Each of
them has a captivating story
to tell to the interested
traveler. Medieval towns
and villages dot the
countryside of Transylvania.
This region of the world has
seen many turbulent times,
however the people of
Transylvania proudly and
openly welcome you to share
their history, and culture.
Many

Dracula fans
descend on Transylvania in
search of the famous
Prince/vampire; Vlad Tepes.
Considered a hero to some,
and a villain to others,
Vlad Tepes was the Prince of
Walachia, and a defiant
defender of Christian
beliefs against the Turks.
The history and story of
Vlad Tepes inspired Bram
Stoker to base his novel;
Dracula, on this Prince.
Legends and rumors vary from
region to region on the
subject of Vlad and Dracula;
however the legend lives on;
come see for yourself, and
let your imagination run
wild!
