Buddy Bear Hostel Moscow.

Buddy Bear Hostel Moscow is a comfortable, modern hostel in the heart
of Moscow. Located on the famous Garden Ring, our hostel is neighbors
with the gorgeous Russian Ministry of Foreign Affairs and Old Arbat
Street, making it no longer than a 10 minute walk to Red Square and
Kremlin. Being located very close to Smolenskaya metro station, Bear
Hostel is amazingly convenient for those going out to explore the best
that Russian capital has to offer.

Not only having a great location alone makes our budget hotel so
popular, but a fact that “Buddy Bear Hostel Moscow” is a part of the
independent hostel chain “Bear Hostels” and is probably the best
alternative to expensive Moscow hotels. For little money, you have a
chance to enjoy a warm, friendly atmosphere, cleanliness and special
room design.
