---
title: "Eating Out"
type: section
---

Irkutsk has a good range of restaurants for a Siberian city. Traditional Russian fare — pelmeny dumplings, borshch, and hearty meat stews — features prominently, but the city also has restaurants serving Buryat and Mongolian dishes, notably *buuzy* (steamed meat dumplings), which are ubiquitous and delicious. The 130th Quarter district is the most pleasant area to browse for a meal.
