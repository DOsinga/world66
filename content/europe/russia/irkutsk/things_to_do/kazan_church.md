title: "Church of Our Lady of Kazan"
type: poi
category: Sight
latitude: 52.2795

---

This ornate late-baroque church, built between 1760 and 1798, is one of the finest surviving examples of 18th-century religious architecture in Siberia. Its white and gold facade rises dramatically from a quiet residential neighbourhood, and the interior retains much of its original iconostasis. The church is active; services are held regularly.
