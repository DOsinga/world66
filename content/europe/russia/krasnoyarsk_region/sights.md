### [Plateau Putorana](/europe/russia/krasnoyarsk_region/sights/plateau_putorana)

This picturesque and mysterious mountainous land is located in the northern part of Krasnoyarsk region, to the east from city Norilsk. Plateau Putorana can be called the land of the falls, rivers and lakes. There is a great number of them here. One of the highest falls in Russia is located at a height of 108 meters on the river Kanda. In summertime Plateau Putorana is the best place for rafting, hunting and fishing.
