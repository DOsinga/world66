**Nooning Bar&Restaurant** is a Moscow embodiment of New York and London artistic loft style place.Eclectic minimalism is the main theme: from menu to design. It's for people who knows what quality is and can appreciate it.
Unique atmosphere and design of Nooning Bar&Restaurant was made by talanted architect **Maria Zhukova**, who is the ideologist of such well-known and popular Moscow places as Jelmosino, Friends and Clouds. Here you can find two zones. The lower one is for banquet rooms with karaoke, the second - restaurant, bar, Vip lounge and balcony with tables for guests. The unique thing is that both floors are connected with suspended music stage and cinema screen. Nooning Bar&Restaurant menu was elaborated by one of the most bright and outstanding moscow chief - **Said Fadley**, whose work causes invariable intrest of best restaurant critics. Among his merrits - 4 stars and 'The best Russian Restaurant in USA" award in New York restaurant Rasputin. **Alexey Molchanov** is responsible for bar, he is one of the leading Russian bartenders, Elizabeth Taylor and Mickey Rourke personal mixologist.

Nooning project was made by london team with unique experience and distinguished portfolio: London clubs as Movida, The Mayfair Club, Tantra, Maddox, Fifty St James, Opium, Hedges&Butler, The Cuckoo Club and joint projects with moscow teams and clubs as Goro Project (Dyagilev), Zepellin, First and etc. On Côte d'Azur the team was working with VIP Room, Nikki Beach, Jimmy'z and Baoli.

No limits in music politics, only best acid jazz, soul, blues, disco

Live music every day, DJs (Stas Modnik and Nina Kravitz), VJ, summer menu from Said Fadley
