This Georgian resturant offers a vast repast of traditional meats. Start with some smoked Georgian cheese. It's shaped like shoestrings and is quite elastic, but has an intense smokey flavor that provokes an apetite of Georgian proportions. Meat is the specialty. If you don't eat it, go elsewhere. Charming Georgian singers may be heard in the downstairs portion of the resturant on most evenings. Somewhat expensive if you take your time and order proper courses. If only I could tell you how to get there...locate it if you can. Worth the commitment.
