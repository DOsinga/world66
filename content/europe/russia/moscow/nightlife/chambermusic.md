---
title: "Chamber Music"
type: poi
---

Moscow's Chamber Musical Theatre headed by Boris Pokrovsky (Leningradsky prospekt, 71) is especially popular with the theatre-goers. The unusual is the repertoire of this theatre, it doesn't include classic performances, instead, it presents the unknown or little-known works of famous authors ("Nose" by Shostakovitch, Salieri's "Word After Music").
