type: poi

Many new, innovative and modern experimental theatres have appeared during the passed few years in Moscow. No doubt, the leading theatres are: Municipal Theatre "New Opera" directed by Evgeny Kolobov, the star of the world value, and "Helikon-Opera" directed by, Dmitry Bert, man. The young artists of these theatres do not relay on theatrical conservatism and old traditions. Thus, "Helikon-Opera" amazed the audience by the unusual interpretation of Verdi's "Aida". This performance has immediately become a real hit.
