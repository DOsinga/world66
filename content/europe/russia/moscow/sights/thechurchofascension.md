The Church of Ascension at Kolomenskoye located in the national park "[Kolomenskoye](/europe/russia/moscow/museums/kolomenskoyemuseumpreserve)" at the high bank of Moskva-River is the best representative of an old architectural style. The Church was built in 1532 in the honour of new-born Crown Prince.
