Stanislavsky occupies a grand space on Tverskaya Ulitsa, Moscow's main boulevard. By day it runs as a restaurant and bar; by night it shifts into club mode with a dance floor and DJ sets running until the small hours on weekends.

The crowd is a mix of office workers early in the evening and a younger nightlife crowd after midnight. The location near the Pushkinskaya/Tverskaya metro stops makes it easy to find and easy to leave. Kitchen open late; the food is better than you'd expect from a venue this size.
