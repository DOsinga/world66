The impressive walls of the Novgorod Kremlin (Detinets) date from the 15th century. Inside, St Sophia's Cathedral (1045-1050) is one of the oldest churches in Russia, with a beautiful golden dome and a well-preserved iconostasis. The complex once held 18 churches and 150 houses within its walls.
