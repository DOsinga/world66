**Public Holidays in 2000/2001**

 Celebration of the Public holidays in Russia is announced by the Russian Federation authorities during the week prior to the holiday.

 January 1st - ” **New Years Day**

 January 7th - ” **Orthodox Christmas**

 Febuary 23 - ” **Defenders of the Motherland Day**

 March 8 - ” **International Women's Day**

 May 1 - ” **International Labour Day**

 May 2 - ” **Spring Day**

 May 9 - ” **Victory Day**

 June 12 - ” **Independence Day**

 November 7 - ” **Revolution Day**

 December 12 - ” **Constitution Day**
