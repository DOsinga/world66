---
title: "Eating Out"
type: section
---

Eating out in St Petersburg ranges from quick blini kiosks to elaborate Russian fine dining. The city has a strong tradition of hearty soups, dumplings (pelmeni), and smoked fish. Georgian restaurants are everywhere and consistently good — look for khachapuri and khinkali. For a quick, cheap meal, the stolovaya (Soviet-style canteen) remains a local institution.
