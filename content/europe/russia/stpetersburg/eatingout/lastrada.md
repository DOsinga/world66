**LA STRADA** is a restaurant with a unique interior under a glass dome designed to represent an Italian village. There is a Florentine balcony on the second floor.

 For those who really love pizzas in their varieties, this is one of the best places to have a great treat. At **LA STRADA**, Pizzas are prepared in full view of the restaurant and are made strictly to order. Why don't you try the famous Tiramisu dessert, the Italian delicacies with baked potatoes, cakes and pies. An intimate banquet hall is available on request.

 To get to the restaurant, use the subway (metro). Get off at Nevsky Prospekt station and take the exit to Griboedova Canal. Walk down Nevsky Prospect. Bolshaya Konyushennaya is the third street to the right of Nevsky Prospekt. You can't miss the restaurant if you get this far.
