The most serious jazz venue in St Petersburg — a small club with a capacity of around 100 that regularly stages the city's finest jazz, blues, and acoustic acts. Nightly concerts start at 20:00 and end before 23:00 due to its location in a residential courtyard. Tickets are cheap (around $5-10) but reservations are strongly recommended, since even midweek nights fill up. Call after 15:00 to book.
