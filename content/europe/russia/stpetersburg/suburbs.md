Most of St. Petersburg's suburbs are very beautiful. They date back to ancient times of Imperialism. The most popular of the sururbs are Peterhof, Pavlovsk, Pushkin, Oranienbaum (Lomonosov), and Gatchina.

**Peterhof**

**Peterhof** is the kingdom of fountains and undoubtably the most beautiful of St. Petersburg's suburbs. It is located on the southern shore of the Finnish gulf 29 kilometers away from St. Petersburg. The palace and park ensembles are historical monuments of the 18th-19th centuries. **Pushkin/Tsarskoye Selo**

**Tsarskoye Selo (Pushkin)** is located about 25 kilometers south of St. Petersburg. Main features of Pushkin are the Palace and park complex.
