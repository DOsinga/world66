---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

St Petersburg is packed with world-class museums, imperial palaces, and monuments at every turn. The Hermitage dominates, but don't overlook the smaller museums and the city's remarkable street-level sights — the Bronze Horseman on Senate Square, the Aurora cruiser on the Neva, and Nevsky Prospekt itself, which reads like an open-air museum of Russian architecture.
