---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Ekaterinburg's attractions range from sombre historical sites to world-class mineral collections. The Cathedral on the Blood dominates any visit, but the city rewards those who dig deeper -- the Rastorguev-Kharitonov estate, the constructivist architecture of the 1920s and 1930s, and the excellent Geological Museum are all worth your time. The Europe-Asia border monument, about 40 km west of the city, is the classic photo stop.
