Travel Guide

South Ural (Yuzhny Ural) is... well, it is south part of Ural. Ural Region of Russia is traditionally divided into three parts: North, Center and South.

In South Ural you'll find such big industrial cities as [Chelyabinsk](/europe/russia/ural/south_ural/chelyabinsk) and Magnitogorsk, smaller towns as well as beautiful forests, lakes and rivers.
