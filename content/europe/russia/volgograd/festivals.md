---
title: "Festivals"
type: section
---

Maslenitsa (Pancake Week) in late February or early March marks the end of winter with street processions, pancake eating, and costume celebrations. Victory Day on 9 May is the most significant annual event in Volgograd — the city's role in the Battle of Stalingrad makes it one of the most important places in Russia for the commemoration, with ceremonies at Mamayev Kurgan drawing large crowds. City Day (usually September) celebrates the city's founding.
