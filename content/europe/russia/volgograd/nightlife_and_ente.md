---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment in Volgograd"
type: section
---

**Volgograd nightlife** can be filled with loud music and flashing lights or as simple as a moonlight stroll. You can learn a lot from the nightlife here in this grand city. It is only limited by your imagination.

#### Time to Eat

The real nightlife doesn't start until later in the evening, maybe around 10 p.m. or so. You can get a great meal at many of the hotel restaurants, each specializing in different culinary delights. You have your choice of Italian, European, typical Russian fare, to the more exotic found in other eating establishments found in the city. Small cafes offer sandwiches and cheese served with coffee. Enjoy a light meal or a formal dinner before hitting the hopping discos and pubs.

#### It's all in the Performance

There are live performances by way of music concerts, ballets, and other great genres; these can be seen at the Experimental Theater, located close to the Volga Embankment. You can enjoy a ballet and lose yourself in the magic of the fairy tales as the dancers twirl and fly through the air. You can also get tickets for the hottest rock concert or a play.

#### After the Show

Hit the discos and dance the night away. Nightclubs offer great DJs and live music for a thumping good time as you party with your friends and make new ones. The pubs offer a more intimate setting, where you can toast one another and get involved in great conversation. Enjoy a sporting event on the television or just an evening with the locals.

#### Drinking Lessons-Vodka 101

Chances are if you are going to party in Volgograd, you will be consuming an astonishing amount of vodka. You should really learn how to drink vodka with a local. It is a great way to strike up a conversation and make a new friend at the same time. It is a bonding experience. It is recommended that you do not chase vodka with beer. You will be regretting that decision in the morning if not before. Drink it in shots, not tankards and chase with juice or water and eat between shots. It is the sharing of the cheeses and breads that allow for a break in the drinking and great conversation.

#### Moonlight Stroll

If you have done all the drinking you want to do and you really don't want to stay in, take a moonlight stroll. It is especially nice at Maymav Hill. After the crowds have dispersed to other areas of the city and the guard has made their last march of the day, it is a perfect time to sit and see views of the city from the top of the hill. Enjoy the quiet and the feel of the night air as you look across Russia's second largest city and marvel at the tenacity of a nation that just would not give up.
