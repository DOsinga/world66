---
title: "Shopping in Volgograd"
type: section
---

**Volgograd Shopping** is unique in the sense that you can pretty much find whatever it is you desire in shopping centers that have combined true Russian culture and the more Western culture of the more modern nations. It is an adventure through small worlds of shops dealing in antiques to large scale kitchen appliances; from flashing beauty festivals to sobering museums and shopping centers, local foods and crafts, as well as international wares.

#### Festival

Festivals and events are a great time to go shopping in Volgograd. The most up-to-date beauty products and talents are displayed at the Festival Beauty. You can shop for the latest makeup fashions, hair fashions, and much more. Plus you get to view shows with models painted from head to toe and it is a glitzy time to be had by all. The Video Festival is also a great place to buy new videos and paraphernalia. It is the event of the season.

#### Museum Shops

Museum shops have books, replicas of exhibits, and ball caps. The Tolerance Museum in the Jewish quarter of Volgograd is a great place to shop for information on the Holocaust. You can also find unique Menorahs, stuffed animals, and tote bags. There are gifts for that perfect someone for Mother's day, Father's day, and birthdays. This is the perfect little shop for quiet moments and the perfect gift.

#### Local Markets

Markets are varied and unique; you can find more western style markets but the smaller Russian ones are by far less expensive and more pleasing to venture through. It will give you a taste of the real Volgograd and not one with a western face on it. Shopping centers in Volgograd provide shops for electronics, clothing, entertainment, and food. You can find kitchenware, telephones, car electronics, health and beauty, and toys. Stroll through one of the shopping centers and see what you can find to take home as a memento of your Volgograd holiday.

#### Malls

There are a few malls in Volgograd. One is the Evropa City Mall, where you will find the last trendy fashions, entertainment, music, movies, and interesting people. The Piraminda Shopping Center has everything you could possibly want with even a little nighttime entertainment to boot. You can shop til you drop or your wallet runs dry and you will still be able to find little treasures to take home and put on display. Buy a cd of traditional Russian music at one of the music stores or a pair of jeans at one of the clothing stores.
