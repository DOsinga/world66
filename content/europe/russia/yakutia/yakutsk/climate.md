---
title: "Climate"
type: location
---

Climate in Sakha Republic can be compared to a roller coaster ride with the temperature ranging from minus 60 C (approximately, -76 F) in winter, to plus 20 C (+68 F) in summer. It gives a temperature range of more than 80 degrees Celsius and more than 140 Fahrenheit! However, Yakutsk inhabitants promise that even at minus 60 C you can count on the warmest welcome here.
