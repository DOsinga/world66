Knez Mihailova Street of Belgrade with a row of mansions, nice examples of 19th century
architecture. This was the first street in Belgrade to be regulated and
officially named. Nowadays, the street is blocked off to all vehicular
traffic and reserved for the pedestrians only. It is the main shopping
mall and promenade connecting Republic Square with Kalemegdan Park and
the Fortress area (an archaeological and historical zone of the city).
