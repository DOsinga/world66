title: "The Bridges of Belgrade"
type: poi
type: "Hotspots"
---

Two parts of the modern city situated by the confluence of two rivers
are connected with 5 bridges on the Sava and 1 bridge on the Danube. On
the river's right bank is the historical nucleus of Belgrade and on
the left bank is New Belgrade, a modern residential area built after
WWII.
