If you visit Gjakova, one of the best places to go is Hard Rock Caffe - Gjakova, cheap prises and good music. Also well-known local band called "NA" plays live every Wednesday night. So don't miss it !!!

### [Flamur](/europe/serbia/kosovoprovince/gjakova/bars_and_cafes/flamur)

Another great meeting place in Gjakova is Pastry Cafe "Royal" Great location in the heart of Gjakova and a very friendly service. This establishment was destroyed by the serbian forces during the war in 1999 but owner Përparim Meka rebuilt it...twice. Once to restart his business and after a few yeas he tore the buliding down to give it a new look as per urban planning of downtown Gjakova.

### [Valon](/europe/serbia/kosovoprovince/gjakova/bars_and_cafes/valon)

In Valon is a very nice bar called Babylon. It is stationed in a place called 'Qarshi e Vogel'. You can have a very good time with nice music and drinks. They have the finest coffee in Gjakova town.
