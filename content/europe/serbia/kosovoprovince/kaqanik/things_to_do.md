---
title: "Things to do in Kaqanik"
type: section
---

It is important that you have a **Kaqanik things to do** list when
you stop by this small municipality in Kosovo. This municipality is
quite small with only 43,000 inhabitants, perfect for tourists who want
to escape to a place where there is peace and solitude, unlike the
hustle and bustle of big cities. Although small, there are quite a
number of things that you can do in Kaqanik.

**Visit the Old Turkish Fort**

The old Turkish fort is located in the heart of Kaqanik and overlooks
the entire center of the municipality. The Old Turkish fort is a relic
of the Ottoman rule in Kosovo which started during the early 1800s and
ended in the beginning of 1900s. It reflects the rich influence of
Ottomans in the region. The old fort is a great place to pick up and
savor the history of Kaqanik.

**Visiting the Lepenac River and Kaqanik Gorge**

The river of Lepenac is a majestic river that runs along the
municipality of Kaqanik. The most scenic part of the Lepenac River can
be seen at Kaqanik Gorge. Kaqanik Gorge is also a historic site, as
fierce battles were held here. It was a battle between the locals who
fought against the Ottoman rule during the 18th century. Aside from
taking pictures of Kaqanik Gorge, scenic boat tours on the river can
also be arranged through local guides.

**Hiking at the Sar Mountains**

Kaqanik lies near the foot of the Sar Mountains. A local tour on the
foot of the mountains is usually arranged during the summer months and
while you are hiking, and you will get to see some local wildlife such
as deer, wild boar and even bears roaming near the trail.

**Take a Bath the Turkish Way**

If you want to freshen up after hiking on the foot of the Sar
Mountains or boat riding in the Lepenac River in the Kaqanik Gorge, make
sure that you try the Turkish bath which is located in the center of
Kaqanik. The Turkish bath is a type of steam bath which is similar to
sauna. Male and female bathers have different bathing areas. The bathers
stay inside a hot room while they wash themselves and get a massage
before they go to the adjacent room to cool down. This is a perfect way
to end your day in Kaqanik.
