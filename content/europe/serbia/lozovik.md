Lozovik is a small town of about 6,000 people on the left bank of the Velika Morava river in central Serbia. The surrounding countryside is agricultural and peaceful, and the river offers fishing and walking along its banks. The local cuisine is typical of central Serbia — hearty grilled meats, fresh produce, and home-made rakija. It is an unassuming, quiet place best experienced as a stop on a journey through the Morava valley.
