Travel Guide

Sokobanja is situated in basin between the mountains Rtanj(1,560m) and
Ozren(1,117m), 400m above the sea level. Sokobanja is a famous spa and
tourist place in Serbia for its moderate continental climate and
immense surfaces of woods, fresh air and a lot of thermo-mineral
sources.
Sokobanja is famous spa in eastern Serbia.
