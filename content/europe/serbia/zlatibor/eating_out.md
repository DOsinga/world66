---
title: "Eating Out"
type: section
---

Zlatibor's food is straightforward mountain cooking: grilled meats, roasted lamb and pork, fresh kajmak, and the usual Serbian staples done well. The town has plenty of restaurants, but some of the best eating is in the villages nearby. Look out for locally made preserved meats, kajmak, and forest fruits at the Green Market — good provisions if you are self-catering.
