---

Located in the south on the Adriatic the Bay of Kotor is one of the nicest areas to visit in Montenegro. For centuries this bay was the boundary between Christianity and Islam. 





Today the bay is lined with numerous beaches, several tourist resorts and a few historical sites. The most important attractions in the area are  [Kotor](/europe/serbiaandmontenegro/kotor),  Risan with its fortress, the Island of Flowers near  Tivat and the monasteries of  Petrovac.
