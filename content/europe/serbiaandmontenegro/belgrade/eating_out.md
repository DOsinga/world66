---

Strangely enough some of the old restaurants are your best bet when eating out in Belgrade. They are cheap, plentyful and have basically what you need. Moving further upmarket you find the cafes around the National Gallery, which are fancy and cater to Western taste. Try the cakes and the fruit salads. If you are more into western food type you will feel comfortable at resturants such as McDonald's and

Pizza Hut

. There are many cafe districts and the most famous one is Strahinjica Bana street.
