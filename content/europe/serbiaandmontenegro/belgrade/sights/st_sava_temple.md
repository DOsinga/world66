Although still under construction, this monumental temple is the third
largest Orthodox church in Europe and with its huge cupola (4.000 tons
in weight) represents one of the most outstanding landmarks of
Belgrade. It is dedicated to St. Sava (Rastko Nemanjic), who founded
Serbian Orthodox Church and was the first archbishop.
