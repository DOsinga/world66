Mr. Branko Rabotic runs his own 'Belgrade Sightseeing' program (recommended by the brand-new **Bradt Travel Guide to Serbia**), a specialized and personalized Web-based reservation service for tour guide services in Belgrade & Serbia. It aims to help all visitors to Belgrade, but especially individual travelers by providing them with various travel services on the spot. Branko Rabotic is officially-licensed and has an extensive background as a travel agent. He offers a wide range of private city tours by car, minivan or just walking.

Mr. Rabotic is member of the Tour Guides Association of Serbia, as well as of Worlwide Guides.
