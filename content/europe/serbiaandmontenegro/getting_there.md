Air: 

The national airline is* JAT Airways (JU)*. There are daily flights to major European cities, as well as the Middle East and Northern Africa. Foreign carriers which have connections to Belgrade include *British Airways, Lufthansa, **Swiss,* *Austrian Airlines, Alitalia, Aeroflot, Uzbekistan Airlines,* and *Olympic Airlines*.

International airports: 

*Belgrade *(Surcin) is 20km (12 miles) west of the city. Smaller airports exist elsewhere such as *Podgorica* *(TGD)* (formerly Titograd) in Montenegro. 

Sea 

The principal passenger ports are Bar and Kotor.* BI *serves Kotor. Ferries link the Yugoslav Adriatic coast with Italian and Greek ports. The principal ferry routes are: Bari–Bar–Bari (ferry* Sveti Stefan *of the *Prekooceanska Plovidba Company *from Bar); and Rijeka–Rab–Zadar–Split–Hvar– Korcula–Dubrovnik– Bar–Corfu–Igoumenitsa (*Jadrolinija* ferry). 

Rail 

Connections and through coaches were once available from principal East and West European cities. It is still possible to travel to Belgrade via the Hungarian border crossing. For up-to-date information contact *Rail Europe* (tel: (0990) 848 848). International trains have couchette coaches as well as bar and dining cars. On some lines transport for cars is provided. 

Road 

The following frontier posts are open for road traffic: 

From 

Croatia 

Batrovci–Bajakovo. 

From 

Hungary 

Hercegszanto–Backi Breg (Bezdan); Tompa–Kelebija; Szeged Roszke–Horgos; Bacsalmas–Bajmok and Tiszasziget–Djala (both crossings for nationals of Yugoslavia and Hungary only). 

From 

Romania 

Jimbolia–Srpska Crnja; Stamora Moravita–Vatin; Naidas–Kaludaerova (Bela Crkva); and Portile de Fier–(Turnu Severin)–Daerdap (Kladovo). 

From 

Bulgaria 

Bregovo–Mokranje (Negotin); Kula–Vrska Cuka (Zajecar); Kalotina–Gradina; Otomanci–Ribarci; Kjustendil–Deve Bair (Kriva Palanka); Blagoevgrad–Delcevo; and Petric–Novo Selo. 

From 

Albania 

Podgradec–Cafa San (Struga); Kukes–Vrbnica; and Han i Hotit–Bozaj. 

From 

Former yugoslav republic of macedonia 

: Presevo–Tabanovce; Djeneral–Jankovic.
