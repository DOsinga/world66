The city cinema is a medieval building on the main square. It offers a
great selection of domestic and imported movies at reasonable prices,
but the best thing about visiting this place is seing the interior of
it. This is socialist esthetics at it's best (not ment to be
sarcastic). It has a huge panoramic screen and the walls are decorated
with tasteful, colorful and very artistic mosaic.
