**Privat Zdenka **is a guesthosue situated on the outskirts of the village Demanova. The village is located in the Low Tatra Mountains, just about 10 km from the biggest ski centre in Slovakia - Ski Centre Jasna. We offer acccommodation in an apartment which is situated on a separate ground floor of a family house. It has a separate entry. Guests have two double rooms at their disposal. Moreover, there is a fully-fitted kitchen, a bathroom with WC, TV with SAT, free WiFi connection and central heating. We are open all year round. Parking is free of charge - in the yard, just behind the windows.

In summer, you can have a grill party or an open fire near the river Demanovka. Children will certainly welcome the playground with sandpit and seesaw. Tourists come here particularly because of hiking tours, cycling tours, water sports and culture (open-ait museums of architecture). In winter you can go skiing to Jasna and throughout the year you have an opportunity to relax in one of the thermal parks - Besenova or Tatralandia.

We wish you a nice stay.
