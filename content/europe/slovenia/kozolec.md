Kozolec is one of most distinctive features of Slovenia, but unfortenatly one of lesser known.


 
 **What is kozolec?**


 The kozolec (hayrack) is a free standing, permanent, mainly wooden, vertical, open but roofed device for drying and storing the goods: hay and grain. it exists exclusively in ethnical territory of Slovene nation. (B. Juvanec)
