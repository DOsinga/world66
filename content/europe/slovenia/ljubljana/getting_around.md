---
title: "Getting Around"
type: section
---

**walking** 

Ljubljana is small city and the best way to get around is on foot. From the railway and bus stations to center is a short ten minute walk. Once you are on Presernov trg (square) or in front of City Hall, all of the major sights are within a ten minute walk. 

**bus** 

This is the preferred method of public transportation. The bus network is extensive and served at frequent intervals. You can pay the bus driver when you get on, but you MUST have the change ready. It's much better to buy bus tokens at any newsstand or post office. Bus tokens cost 100 SIT (0,5 EUR) if you pay at the bus it's around 30% more and you must have exact change! 

Night services are very reduced or there aren't any at all. 

**taxi** 

Lots of cabs can be found in the city center, especially at night after most buses stop running. They are quite expensive. 

**car** 

Parking is a major problem in Ljubljana. I suggest you park your car in a garage and continue on foot. Garages are generally well marked. The closest to the center is in Trg Republike (across from the Parliament building).
