Ljubljana's City Hall on Mestni trg, erected in 1484 and rebuilt in Baroque style in 1718. The arcade courtyard inside has the Narcissus Fountain by Francesco Robba. In front stands the Robba Fountain of the Three Carniolan Rivers (1751), a Ljubljana landmark.
