title: "Ljubljana Castle"
type: poi

latitude: 46.0488

---

The hilltop castle above the old town is the city's most prominent landmark. A funicular from the old town takes you up in two minutes. The views over the city and out to the Julian Alps on clear days are excellent. Inside there's a 3D film on Ljubljana's history and a watchtower worth climbing.
