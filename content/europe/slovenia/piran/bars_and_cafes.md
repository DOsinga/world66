---
title: "Bars and Cafes"
type: section
---

Piran is not a nightlife town, but it has a pleasant bar scene that runs late into summer evenings. The liveliest stretch is along the seafront promenade and around Tartini Square, where outdoor tables stay busy well after dark. Local Istrian wines — particularly Malvasia and Refosco from nearby vineyards — are worth seeking out over the international options.
