---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

Postojna is well connected by both bus and train from [Ljubljana](/europe/slovenia/ljubljana). Most visitors prefer the bus — services run frequently and drop you closer to the caves. The journey takes about an hour. By train, the station is a couple of kilometres from the cave entrance, so you will need a short taxi ride or a walk.

By car, Postojna is on the A1 motorway between Ljubljana and [Koper](/europe/slovenia/koper), about 50 kilometres southwest of the capital. Parking is available at the cave complex.
