### [Park Hotel Ptuj](/europe/slovenia/ptuj/practical_informat/park_hotel_ptuj)

Park Hotel Ptuj is a lovely hotel / museum located
in a town bourgeois house with a 5-century-long history. The newly restored house houses a coffeeshop with a good selection of coffees, beers and wines, 15 individually furnished 4-star rooms, an event centre and numerous antiques and paintings by Slovene and European masters. A lovely place to spend the night or at least enjoy a cup of coffee.
