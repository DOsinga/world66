The best resource for sights, hotels, restaurants, bars, what to do and see

Great mountains of Savinja Alp are perfect spot for hiking and mountaineering. Logar Valley Landscape Park is said to be one of the most beautiful alpine valleys in Europe. In 2009 Solčavsko won European Destination of Excellence Award for tourism in protected areas.

MUST SEE AND DO:

- Scenic Solčava Panoramic Road - perfect vistas and local food tasting;
- Natural parks (Robanov kot, Matkov kot, Golte, Logar Valley);
- Natural wonders (Snežna jama Cave, Rinka Waterfall);
- Cultural wonders (Coal Mining Museum in Velenje, the world's biggest Tito statue);
- Events (Flosarski bal wood rafting event, Pippi Festival in Velenje);
- Rafting and other whitewater activities on Savinja River (Savinja rules);
- Fishing on Savinja River;
- Sailing or Zorbing on Velenje Lakes;
- Savinjski želodec dry meet tasting;
- Skiing on Golte;
- Mozirski gaj flower park visiting
- Enjoying Topolšica Spa health and wellness programmes.
