KLUŽE FORT (Bovec* area*)

This exceptional fort, named Kluže, has guarded the passage between the sea and the Alps since ancient times. This historic site has been marked by the Venetians, the Habsburgs, Austrian and French soldiers, Napoleon, the Soča Front, and World War I.
