THE BOKA WATERFALL ( *[Bovec](/europe/slovenia/bovec) area* )
The waters of this mighty waterfall, which are accumulated in the Kanin Range and interwoven with karstic caves and chasms, fall freely during the first 106 metres, and then drop another 30 metres. Boka is the most powerful Slovenian and one of the most magnificent European waterfalls.
