---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

Algeciras is on the main rail and bus routes along the southern Spanish coast. Trains run to Ronda, Granada, and Madrid. Buses connect to [Málaga](/europe/spain/andalucia/malaga), [Cádiz](/europe/spain/andalucia/cadiz), [Seville](/europe/spain/andalucia/seville), and [Tarifa](/europe/spain/andalucia/tarifa).

The port has frequent ferry services to Ceuta (about an hour) and Tangier Med in Morocco (about two hours). Several companies operate — you can buy tickets at the port or online. Fast catamarans are quicker but cost more. Nearby [Tarifa](/europe/spain/andalucia/tarifa) also has a fast ferry to Tangier city centre, which is the more convenient crossing if you want to explore Tangier itself.
