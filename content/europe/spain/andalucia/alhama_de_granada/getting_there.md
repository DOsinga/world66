---
title: "Getting There in Alhama de Granada"
type: section
---

There is no problem with a car about plotting an excellent route between [Granada](/europe/spain/andalucia/granada) and Málaga that will pass through some of the lovely Axarchia region and Alhama de Granada. All you need is a map.

Many believe there is a problem with public transport; not so. There are numerous buses from Málaga to Velez-Málaga and T. Alsina Graells run a service each day from there to [Granada](/europe/spain/andalucia/granada) via Alhama de Granada.
