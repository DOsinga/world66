Sierra de Aracena - A Walk! Guidebook is published by Discovery Walking Guides Ltd. in 2005 (ISBN 1-899554-96-3). In addition to detailed walks and map sections, there's plenty of background information.

They also publish the most up to date and detailed map of the area, Sierra de Aracena Tour & Trail Map, ideal for walkers and cyclists (ISBN 1-899554-97-1)
