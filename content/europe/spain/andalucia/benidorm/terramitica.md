Benidorm is now the home of the recently finished Spanish version of Disney World. There you can experience a journey through five of the most ancient and legendary civilizations in Mediterranean Sea: Egypt, Greece, Rome, Iberia and The Islands. In TERRA MITICA you will be able to fight side by side with Ulysses, escaping from Cyclop, sail through the Nile river, face up The Minotaur, and see a pirate vessel sinking. It is well worth a visit if you have kids. If you need more information you can visit the unofficial site of TERRA MITICA
