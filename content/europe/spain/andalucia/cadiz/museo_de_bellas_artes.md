The city museum at Plaza de Mina contains one of the most important collections of Zurbarán paintings in the world — twenty canvases of saints and monks painted for a Carthusian monastery. The archaeological section covers Cadiz's Phoenician, Carthaginian, and Roman periods. Closed Mondays.
