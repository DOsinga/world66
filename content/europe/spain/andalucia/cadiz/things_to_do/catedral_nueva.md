type: poi

latitude: 36.5295

---

The great domed cathedral that dominates the old city, begun in 1722 and not completed until 1838. The interior is large and ornate, and you can descend to the crypt where the composer Manuel de Falla is buried. Climb the Torre de Poniente (west tower) for excellent views over the city and out to sea.
