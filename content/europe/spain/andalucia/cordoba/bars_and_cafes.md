---
title: "Bars and Cafes"
type: section
---

Córdoba's nightlife starts late and runs very late — pubs are supposed to close at 3am but in practice the door is locked and the party continues until dawn. The area around the Mezquita and the Judería has tourist-oriented bars; for a more local scene, head slightly further afield.

**Bodega Campos** (Calle Lineros 32) is Córdoba's most celebrated old bodega, dating from 1908 — a rambling warren of barrel-lined rooms where wine has soaked into the walls. Go for the atmosphere and the local wine.

**Bar El Juramento** is a classic tapas bar in the old quarter, good for a quick stop between sights.

**Plaza de la Corredera**, a handsome 17th-century square with a complete arcade on all four sides, has numerous café terraces and is a pleasant place for an evening drink.
