A century-old bodega east of the Mezquita, set in a series of vine-shaded patios and rooms hung with autographed Montilla casks (Tony Blair and Antonio Banderas have both signed barrels here). Famous above all for its own-label Montilla-Moriles fino — order it cold with a plate of jamón ibérico and you have one of the best simple pleasures in the city.
