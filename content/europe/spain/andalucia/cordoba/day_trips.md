---
title: "Day Trips"
type: section
---

**[Seville](/europe/spain/andalucia/seville)** (142 km) — about 1 hour 45 minutes by car, or around 45 minutes by high-speed AVE train. The full city visit deserves more than a day, but a focused day trip taking in the Alcázar, Cathedral, and Giralda tower is very worthwhile.

**[Granada](/europe/spain/andalucia/granada)** (210 km) — around 2 hours 20 minutes by car. The Alhambra alone justifies the trip; book tickets well in advance.

**[Jaén](/europe/spain/andalucia/jaen)** (119 km) — 1 hour 40 minutes. A less-visited city with its own impressive cathedral and the castle of Santa Catalina above the town.

**Medina Azahara** (8 km west) — the ruined palatial city of the Umayyad caliphate, visited on a half-day from Córdoba. Take the Consorcio shuttle bus from the city centre, or a taxi.
