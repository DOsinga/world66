In the foothills of the sierra of Córdoba, overlooking
the city, stands this unique restaurant, an ideal setting for both large-scale
and intimate celebrations.

The elegant restoration work has combined a profound respect for
the building’s history with modern elements of comfort and quality.

The restaurant has exclusive private rooms, large and exquisitely
decorated halls with spectacular views of Córdoba, a general purpose room, over
a thousand parking spaces and beautiful gardens, making an ideal setting for
all kinds of business or social events, weddings or family get-togethers.

The history and gastronomic excellence of Córdoba have been beautifully
combined in the incomparable Restaurante Castillo de La Albaida.
