---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment"
type: section
---

Nightlife in Córdoba (as in almost all Spain) is endless. Pubs are suposed to close at 3:00 am, but actually they don't! After thar hour, the bartender close the Pub's door and, to get in, you've got to knock the door. They open it and... surprise: the party has just started!
