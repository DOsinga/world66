One of the best provincial archaeology museums in Spain, occupying a Renaissance palace. The standout feature is the Roman patio discovered during construction — now incorporated into the museum itself. Excellent prehistoric sculptures of bulls and lions, and impressive Moorish-era pieces from Medina Azahara including a famous carved musk deer. Free for EU citizens; €1.50 for others.
