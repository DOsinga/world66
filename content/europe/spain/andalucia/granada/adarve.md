A relaxed bar on Calle Melchor Almagro, just below the Albaicín and within walking distance of the Alhambra. Adarve draws a mix of locals and visitors with its easy-going atmosphere and outdoor terrace. Good for a beer and tapas after a day of sightseeing in Granada's old quarters.
