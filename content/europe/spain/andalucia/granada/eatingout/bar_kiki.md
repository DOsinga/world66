Bar Kiki is a local favorite and often very crowded. This is a perfect spot for lunch and a chilled bottle of white wine during summer weekends. The food is very good and authentic, with several Mozarabic specialties. It is located next to Iglesia San Nicolas, which is next to the famous Mirador San Nicolas in the Albayzin.
