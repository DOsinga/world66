This restaurant next to the cathedral specializes in the traditional food of Granada and Andalusia.
 It was the favorite of the famous Spanish poet Federico García Lorca.


 The specials include fish and seafood dishes but also their famous Sacromonte omelette with brains and sweetmeat.
