A relaxed bar on a narrow street just off Plaza Nueva in Granada's old centre. Papalagi serves cocktails, teas, and light bites in a cozy, eclectically decorated space. The atmosphere leans bohemian -- mismatched furniture, warm lighting, and an unhurried vibe that fits the Albaicín side of town. A good spot to wind down after an evening walk through the old quarter.
