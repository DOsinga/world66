Right in the middle of the commercial town center, you can see what’s probably the most important instance of Spanish history. On a high pedestal, with allusive historic inscriptions Cristopher Columbus presents his project to Queen Isabel. The 19th century statue and the relieves on the pedestal were designed by Mariano Benlliure
