Absolutely worth a visit, the Museo ¨Casa de los Tiros¨ is an intriguing little museum, in which each room has a completely different atmosphere. The museum gives a fantastic general introduction to Granada itself, and although the building itself is quite historic, recent renovations have only improved it. There is no charge for residents of the European Union, and the museum is only 1 euro, for everyone else! For any international students studying in Granada, the museum is right next to the Centro de Lenguas Modernas at the University of Granada.
