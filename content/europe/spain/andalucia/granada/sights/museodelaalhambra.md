After the Castilian conquest, The Alhambra was modified and between 1526 and 1550 Pedro Machuca built the “Carlos V” Palace. This Palace is one of the most beautiful Renaissance Buildings in Spain. It now houses the Museo de la Alhambra. The museum has an excellent exhibition about muslim culture and the history of the Alhambra.
