The Salto de Cabrero is a rocky ridge above Grazalema, reached by crossing the fence left of the road above the village just after the fork where the road to Zahara diverges from the road to Benamahoma. Two ridges of rock outcrop make for a spectacular viewpoint, especially in late afternoon light. The walk out is lovely but the return route is limited, so allow time for retracing your steps.
