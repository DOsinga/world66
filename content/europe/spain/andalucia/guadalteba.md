Guadalteba is an area of outstanding natural beauty tucked into the hills of Málaga province, where a chain of reservoirs lies surrounded by mountains and neat rows of olive trees. The lakes make for superb picnic spots, and several restaurants on the hilltops offer views that stretch across the whole comarca.

The area has built a reputation as one of the best paragliding destinations in Andalucía. Beyond that, there is no shortage of things to do: climbing, horseriding, canoeing, kayaking, fishing, hiking, and cycling all have established circuits here.

Guadalteba sits at the heart of Andalucía, making it an ideal base for day trips. [Ronda](/europe/spain/andalucia/ronda) is just 20 minutes away and [Antequera](/europe/spain/andalucia/antequera) half an hour. [Sevilla](/europe/spain/andalucia/sevilla), [Granada](/europe/spain/andalucia/granada), [Córdoba](/europe/spain/andalucia/cordoba), Jerez, and [Málaga](/europe/spain/andalucia/malaga) are all within easy reach by car or train.
