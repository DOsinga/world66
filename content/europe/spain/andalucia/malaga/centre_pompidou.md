A satellite of the Paris museum, housed in the rainbow-glass cube on Muelle Uno at the port. Opened in 2015 as the first Pompidou outpost outside France, it shows a rotating selection of 20th- and 21st-century works from the parent collection — Picasso, Bacon, Magritte, Frida Kahlo, plus video and installation pieces.
