Málaga is the obvious base for exploring western Andalusia. The high-speed AVE train reaches **Córdoba** in under an hour and **Sevilla** in under two and a half. **Granada** and the Alhambra are an hour and a half by car or bus. Closer in, **Ronda** with its dramatic gorge and the white town of **Antequera** with its prehistoric dolmens are both easy half-day or full-day trips. For beach time without leaving the province, the resort strip of the Costa del Sol — **Marbella**, Torremolinos, Fuengirola — runs west along the coast.
