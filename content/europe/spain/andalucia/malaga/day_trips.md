During your trip to Spain, you may want to spend some time in The Costa Del Sol. Veteran travelers agree that the city of Malaga (the capital of The Costa Del Sol) is an excellent starting point for a good number of fantastic day trips throughout the scenic Costa Del Sol and beyond. Nearby towns (all less than 100 miles away and some are even under 30 miles away) include Almeria, Ronda, Antequera, Granada, and Adra.

Malaga is also located in close proximity to and a number of spectacular beach resorts. Marbella, Torremolinos, and Fuengirola are the considered the most popular beach resorts in the area. Visitors to Malaga can even take a day trip to Cordoba (109 miles away) or Madrid during their Spain vacation thanks to the AVE -- Europe's high-speed train ( www.raileurope.com).
