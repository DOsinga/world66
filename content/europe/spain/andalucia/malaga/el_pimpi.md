The most famous bar in the city, a sprawling rabbit-warren of rooms and patios near the Picasso Museum, with painted barrels signed by the great and the good of Spain (Antonio Banderas, who is part-owner, has his name on one). Touristy but fairly priced and consistently good — the patatas bravas, the *jamón ibérico*, and the local sweet Moscatel are all worth ordering.
