**Malaga Festivals** reflect the cheerful and friendly attitude of the people. The people of Malaga give their best as they celebrate the various festivals they have. Malaga&rsquo;s festivals are a blend of music, color, and many entertaining performances. Malaga is a city in Andalusia, Spain.****

**Semana Santa**

Semana Santa** **or Holy Week is a solemn occasion where the people of Malaga pray to their God and contemplate about the mistakes and wrongs things that they have done. Though this is a solemn celebration, there are still a full color and music scene. During Semana Santa, the people sing a lot of praise songs. Semana Santa is celebrated either in March or April. It has no fixed date.

**Feria de Malaga**

Feria de Malaga is the biggest celebration in Malaga that is held in the month of August. It is a week-long event of fun and excitement. The city celebrates this with several activities and lively presentations. A lot of visitors come to Malaga to witness this event.

**Arts Festival**

Malaga is the birthplace of one of the most well-known art figures in the world, Pablo Picasso. And so, many art and art festivals happen in this place. Malaga holds regular art exhibitions and festivals. This place provides a variety of art choices. In addition, if you have the passion in arts, you must visit Malaga in the month of October. October is known as the Picassian October. The whole month is offered to remember the artistic and genius work of Pablo Picasso.
