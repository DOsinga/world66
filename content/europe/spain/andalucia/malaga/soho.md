Málaga's self-declared arts district, a small grid of streets between the Alameda and the port. Once shabby, it has been transformed by a series of large-scale street murals — many by international names like D*Face, Obey, and Boa Mistura — commissioned as part of the city's MAUS urban-art project. Independent galleries, design shops, and cocktail bars now fill the ground floors.
