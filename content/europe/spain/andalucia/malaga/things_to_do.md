---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Málaga's headline sights cluster on a single hill above the old town: the Moorish **Alcazaba**, the higher **Gibralfaro Castle**, and the Roman theatre at their feet. Add the **Cathedral**, the **Picasso Museum**, and a wander through the old centre and Soho district and you have a strong two- or three-day city visit. Beyond the museums, the city's pleasure is in the small things — climbing for the view, pausing at a tapas bar, drifting between plazas in the late-afternoon light.
