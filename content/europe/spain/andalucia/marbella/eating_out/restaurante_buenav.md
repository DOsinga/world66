The Buenaventura Restaurant is located on a privileged site, in a relaxed, pedestrian area of the Ancient Quarter, one of the most beautiful and well preserved quarters on the Costa del Sol. Right in the heart of the historical and commercial centre of Marbella, in a beautiful plaza where the 18th Century Iglesia de la Encarnación,is to be found. The church, which lends its name to the plaza, quite possibly the most important building in Marbella. 

Seasonal market cooking, creative, refined and delicious, based on Traditional Andalusian Cuisine, with a personal touch and beautifully presented, in order to be at the height of the gastronomic demands of Marbella and its guests.

Our daily offer, apart from the Seasonal Menu, is made up by the Recommendations of the Day and a Tasting Menu with diverse samples of our cuisine.

The Dessert Menu deserves special mention: all desserts are elaborated in the Restaurant, right when they are requested. The Sweet Tooth's Delight is recommended as it is truly a temptation for all senses.
