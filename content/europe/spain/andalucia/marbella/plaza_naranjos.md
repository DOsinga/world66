title: Plaza de los Naranjos
type: poi
---

The heart of the old town, a small square shaded by orange trees and ringed by whitewashed buildings. A 15th-century Renaissance fountain sits in the middle; the 16th-century town hall and the 17th-century Casa del Corregidor line the edges. Tables spill out from cafés and restaurants — touristy, but the setting is genuine.
