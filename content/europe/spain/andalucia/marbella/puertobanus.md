type: poi

Marbella's marina, seven kilometres west of the old town. Whitewashed buildings, rows of super-yachts, designer boutiques and a slow parade of expensive cars along the quayside. Loud, showy and often ridiculous, but worth an evening stroll for the people-watching and a drink on a terrace.
