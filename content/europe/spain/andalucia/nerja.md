Nerja is located at the foot of the Sierra Tejera on soaring cliffs high above the sea some 50 kms to the east of
[Malaga](/europe/spain/andalucia/malaga). It is a very pretty, and still relatively undeveloped corner of real Spain that’s quite unlike the high rise resorts elsewhere on the Costa del Sol -- although there is a great deal of building work currently taking place. Here you will still find typical white-washed Andaluz houses adorned with geraniums, cobbled streets in the old town. At the same time there are some good beaches not too far below. Nerja is also a lively town with many nice restaurants, tapas bars and a good nightlife scene.
