Tutti Frutti Plaza.

Tutti Frutti plaza is a new bar based on the main Tutti Frutti square in Nerja. A wonderful place to have a quiet relaxing cocktail whilst still being surrounded by the hustle and bustle of the busy square.

Come and meet Anna and Miguel. Most friendly and accomodating of people. With a fantastic gay friendly atmosphere and fantastic prices on all drinks...possibly the lowest in town.

The bar is set back from the others and can be spotted with the Pink sign on the corner.
