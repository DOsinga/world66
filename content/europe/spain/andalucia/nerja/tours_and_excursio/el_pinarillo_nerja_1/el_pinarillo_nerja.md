El Pinarillo Nerja,
Welcome to the gateway to the sierras - El Pinarillo.
El Pinarillo is a picnic and barbeque area set in the heart of Nerja's National Park. There are purpose built barbeque areas, plenty of parking and play areas for the kids.
In early summer you can expect to find the air thick with butterflies. Lizards and snakes will be sunning themselves in the early morning sun and you may be lucky enough to see an Ibex the Andalucian mountain goat.
Many walkers and ramblers start their day off here being an ideal base to park up and use the facilities. The area is open from mid October through to early June. It is closed to vehicles in the summer months due to the potential fire risks and fires/barbeques are strictly not allowed.
El Pinarillo provides a gateway in to the National Park as there are many beautiful walks that can be taken from the area. We can provide guided walks that will take you into the mountains and valleys beyond exploring natural springs, cascading waterfalls and breath taking views. Walks can range from a few hours to all day depending on your ability.
The entrance to the National Park and El Pinarillo can be found on the left hand side just before the entrance to the Nerja caves which are well signposted. The road is unmade but passable for most vehicles and the drive takes typically 15mins. El Pinarillo is 5km inland and depending on your ability can take between 1 and 2 hours to walk. Remember though the best walks all start from El Pinarillo.
