Life Adventure Combi Jeep Tour, This is definitely one of our most popular jeep tours because of its great variety.

The day starts with a Tour in the National Park, The Sierra Almijara. You will have an excellent opportunity to see the wildlife of this unspoiled area. Our local guide Michael will take you on an easy stroll that goes on a broad path, while explaining about the history, flora and fauna that is typical for this part of Andalucia.

The walk is no longer that 2½ Km, but takes about 1½ hrs because of all the stops made to explain about the views, wildlife and abundance of herbs and flowers.

Before the safari tour continues by jeep, the guests are served refreshments at the mountain recreation area El pInarillo where the walk started.
