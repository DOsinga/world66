Life Adventure Jeep Tour full day,
Our Nerja Jeep Tour is recommended for those who want an insight to the beautiful Andalucian mountains, their flora and fauna, wildlife and spectacular views of unspoiled coastline.

After a Jeep Safari Tour of Nerja , many guests have commented that they have seen a traditional rural side of Spain, passing farmers and goat herders going about their old fashioned way of living.

The Jeep Tour starts in the National Park behind the famous caves of Nerja in El Pinarrillo , where guests will experience the wildlife in this beautiful unspoiled area. The tour continues up to a vantage point with spectacular views where you will be served refreshments. Throughout the journey you will be fed interesting facts and stories about the surrounding area by our highly entertaining guide Michael.

After further advancing into the Sierra of Almijara and Tejeda we stop at a typical Spanish restaurant where the guests enjoy lunch.

After lunch we cross the mountains again, stopping at some fantastic vantage spots before we bring you back to your Nerja accommodation . The tour takes between 7 & 8 hours.
Life Adventure Jeep Tour half day,
This is a shortened version of the above and lasts approx 3½ hours. Refreshments are included in the Nerja Jeep Safari tour .
