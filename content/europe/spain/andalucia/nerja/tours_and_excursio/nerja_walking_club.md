Nerja walking Club

Welcome to the walking club of Nerja. The Club is sponsored and run by Michael and Donna Tweehuijsen of Life Adventure S.L.

Our walking programme starts in April until the end of May. We then take a summer break as due to health and safety we do not walk in the high temperatures.

The programme restarts at the end of October and finishes mid December.

Our walks are normally on Sundays and include the following routes:

El Pinarillo, Fuente del Esparto
Umbria del Lobo
Frigiliana,cuesta de Jimenez, El Pinarillo
El Pinarillo, Rio Chillar
Rio Verde (Granada)
El Cielo
El Tajo del Almendron
Navachica
Sierra Nevada (Granada)
Cortijo del Iman
Frigiliana, Ruta del Limo, El Pinarillo

We have "Open Days" where non members are welcome to see if they would like to join the club.

Our programme of walks will be posted on the internet and available for download later this year.

The Club intends to do some nice walks in the Alpujaras and Sierra Nevada area. When transport is required the cost is split between the participants and is separate to the Club contribution. Initially the Club is sponsored by Life Adventure and the aim is to buy radio communication, GPS and mountain first aid kits with the Club contributions.

How to become a member of the Club

The annual cost for membership is 45 Euros and there is an initial one time payment of 15 Euros for administration costs.

Simply download and complete the enrolment form and send it to the address on the form. Membership payment can be made on your initial walk or if you wish to pay in advance please contact us to arrange this.

Please do not hesitate to contact us if you have any queries.

We look forward to welcoming you to our Club.
Michael and Donna
