Casa Carmelo in Santa Cruz District, Seville. This restaurant not only gives you a true Sevillian and Andalusian dining experience, but it also gives you a magnificent view of a typical sevillian house in Barrio Santa Cruz. Specialties include Andalusian Gazpacho, Paella, Sevillian Fried Fish, ... and homemade desserts. Tables on the outside terrace, dining al fresco. 

The gourmet palate will not be dispappointed here! Reservation recommended.
