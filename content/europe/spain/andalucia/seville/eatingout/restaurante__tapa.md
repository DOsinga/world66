Restaurant & Tapas Doña Elvira is located in the popular Plaza de Doña Elvira, a place emblematic of Barrio Santa Cruz, occupying a unique building that is probably the most photographed house in the neighborhood. 



Restaurante Doña Elvira has two comfortable dining rooms, though most customers prefer to eat outside (weather permitting, which is most of the year) on the open-air terrace set in the unique and oft-praised Plaza de Doña Elvira, under its flowering orange trees, where you have the opportunity to enjoy our Sevillian cuisine and savor, at the same time, the typical feel of Barrio Santa Cruz.
