**Restaurant **SANTA CRUZ. In Santa Cruz District, Sevilla. 

Resembling a picture postcard, this section of Seville gleams with weathered white stone houses and bursts of colorful window boxes. Santa Cruz serves sausages, cured hams, tapas starters and a variety of soups and seafood. Try the homemade pastries for dessert. Dinner is complemented by a wonderful wine list. Reservations recommended.
