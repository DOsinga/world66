During your Seville vacation, you will definitely want to pick up some unique items to take back home to either remind you of your trip or to give to family and friends. Well, you're in luck! Most of Seville's shopping venues sell mostly souvenirs as well as antiques, books, Flamenco wear, ceramics, porcelain, and crafts. Continue reading through the listings to find out about some of the great places to find unique gifts, souvenirs, and more.

### [Plaza del Duque](/europe/spain/andalucia/seville/shopping/plaza_del_duque)

Head to Plaza del Duque on Friday or Saturday to purchase unique crafts by local artists.

type: Markets



address: Plaza del Duque

### [Libreria Vertice](/europe/spain/andalucia/seville/shopping/libreria_vertice)

You won't find a wider selection of books in just about every language there is, at this American-owned bookstore. Located in the Santa Cruz area of Seville, Libreria Vertice sells hundreds of new and rare books in Spanish, Italian, English, and French.

type: Book shops



tel: +34 95 421 1654

url: [www.libreriavertice.com](http://www.libreriavertice.com)

address: C/San Fernando, 33-35 Sevilla

email: info@libreriavertice.com

### [Mercadillo de la Alameda](/europe/spain/andalucia/seville/shopping/mercadillo_de_la_a)

The Sunday morning flea market in the Macarena neighborhood of the city is one of the most popular flea markets in Seville. In addition to antiques, this Sunday morning flea market also sells clothes, music, crafts, and some of the strangest items you've ever seen. The flea market lasts from 7:00 a.m.-2:00 p.m. Plaza Alameda de Hercules.

type: Antiques



address: Plaza Alameda de Hercules

### [Sierpes and Tetuan](/europe/spain/andalucia/seville/shopping/sierpes_and_tetuan)

The main shopping district of Sierpes and Tetuan, particularly on Calle Tetuan, you will find a good number of boutiques and name brand stores such as Zara. Stroll over to El Corte Inglés and you will find everything from Levi's and Polo to Tommy Hilfiger and more.

type: Shopping centers and malls



address: Sierpes and Tetuan

### [El Jueves](/europe/spain/andalucia/seville/shopping/el_jueves)

Head over to the oldest market in Seville on Thursday mornings to find unique crafts, artwork, furnishings, gifts, and more. Located on Calle Feria, vendors at El Jueves typically set up shop starting at around 6:00 a.m. The market closes at 2:00 p.m.

type: Markets



tel: +34 954 383 532

address: C/Feria 59, Seville
