Visit** Cordoba **from Sevilla which is one of the three great Moorish cities of Andalucia where the Moorish atmosphere blends with the best produced by its Christian liberators.... 

Capital of Moorish Spanish, with an impressive 8th century Mosque, now a Cathedral. Enjoy a guided visit of the Mosque & Jewish quarter, see the Statue of Maimonides and walk the Calle de los Judios (Street of the Jews) to the ancient synagogue and also the Alcazar.
