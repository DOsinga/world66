On this private tour you will enjoy a panoramic tour of Sevilla's Plaza España and adjoining Maria Luisa Park... 

The park is one of the biggest in the city and is considered one of Europe's most beautiful. Palm trees, flower beds and pavilions are dotted through out. The Plaza de España is situated in the park and has become an important symbol of Sevilla, it was built for the Expo of 1929 where it was the centerpiece. A semi-circular canal spreads the front of the building. Fountains, monumental staircases and a mass of ceramic tile work of the benches surrounding make this quite a site to see.
