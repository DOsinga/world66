**Drink Beer and Watch Your Favorite Sports at Torre del Mar**

**Torre del Mar bars and cafes** are one of the exciting ways to spend your vacation in this southern coastal district of Spain. Torre del mar is actually a part of the municipality of Velez-Malaga, Axarquia. The coastal town is found on a sedimentary plain with scenic routes of Sun and Avocado. When in this place, you must go to El Copo, a seafront stretch filled with discotheques and bars side by side.

**Howards Bar**

Howards Bar is found in Torre del Mar, right in front of Las Yucas Caf&eacute; and Hotel. The place is just 20 meters away from the district's tram service. Howards Bar is a popular sports bar in town. Inside the bar, there are four 110-inch screen plasma TVs that show all Sky Sports. Howards Bar also has a sundeck for some al fresco dining and drinking. On the left side of the bar, there is a public area for kids to play around as their parents eat, watch or nurse a drink. The bar is open every day of the week from 12:00 noon until the wee hours of the morning.

**Food and Beverage**

The bar offers a wide range of the finest draught beers and international or local bottled beers. Two of the usual buys are Bulmers and Smirnoff Ice. A freshly brewed cup of coffee can also be had from the bar if you are driving. As for the food, a yummy selection of pies, sausage rolls, pastries, lasagna and shepherd's pie are the most requested ones.
