---
title: "Eating Out in Torre del Mar"
type: section
---

Although most **Torre ****del**** Mar restaurants** are located on the waterfront strip referred to as El Copo, you will find a great pizzeria on Andalucia Avenue 63, which is a definite must-visit. It is called Pizzeria La Mamma. Pizzeria La Mamma was opened back in the 1990s, and the locals say it is the best pizzeria not only in Torre del Mar, but also in the whole Andalucia region.

**Pizzeria La Mamma: The Atmosphere**

Pizzeria La Mamma has a really pleasant interior. The walls are covered with decorative stones and several interesting lampshades hang from the ceiling. Many lovely little tables are stylishly arranged inside the dining room. All of them are decked with sparklingly white tablecloths. The tables can accommodate up to 4 people, but if you&rsquo;re in a bigger group of friends, the staff will immediately connect two or more tables to ensure your comfort. In general, the staff is really friendly and helpful, which complements the excellent service.

**Pizzeria La Mamma: ****The Menu**

Pizzeria La Mamma offers an extensive menu of over 30 pizzas. All of them are baked in a wooden oven, which gives them a truly remarkable taste. The pizzas are prepared following only classic Italian recipes. Except the pizzas you can order some excellent Italian pasta, as well as various other European and traditional Spanish meals, both main dishes and starters. Prices are very affordable and even the most expensive pizzas cost no more than $10.
