---
title: "Festivals"
type: section
---

The Virgen del Carmen procession on 16 July is the town's most important festival — the statue of the patron of the sea is carried in a decorated boat along the coast in a fluvial procession, accompanied by fireworks and crowds on the beach. The Noche de San Juan on 23–24 June is celebrated across the Costa del Sol with bonfires on the beach at midnight, the traditional jumping of flames, and people wading into the sea.
