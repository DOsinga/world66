---
title: "Festivals in Torre del Mar"
type: section
---

**Torre del Mar festivals**** **are mainly composed of festivals celebrating the country's faith and religion. It is no doubt that they have such festivals because this city is part of a Catholic country. Torre del Mar has been among the main destinations for tourists in Costa del Sol during summer.

**Patron of the Sea**

La Virgen del Carmen is the patron of the sea. Annually, the people of Torre del Mar and its neighboring places come together to celebrate this every 16th of July. There is a procession and candle ceremony during this festival. Also, there is a fluvial procession, or water parade. The statue of the La Virgen del Mar is placed in a well-decorated boat as it sails in the water. As the water parade is occurring, the people cheer and make noises accompanied by bursts of fireworks.

**Bathing Time**

The feast of San Juan is a festival in Torre del Mar in which they believe that bathing at this period heals any skin problems. This festival is held every 24th day of June. San Juan Day is held on the beach, with people enjoying campouts with big bonfires and the sweet taste of barbeques. At midnight, the people go out to the sea to swim or to paddle. This is believed to bring good luck. Another activity that they do is the washing of the face and the feet three times because by doing this, their three wishes will be granted.
