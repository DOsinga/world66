****It is not a bad decision to read some **Torre ****del**** Mar practical information** prior to arriving at this lovely sea town. It will surely make you feel more confident when walking around the city and help you communicate with the locals. Here are some details on the town demographics and some currency information.

**Demographics**

Torre del Mar is located within the Axarquia area, right on the coast of the Mediterranean, which makes it an attractive holiday destination. The resident population consists of about 20 000 people, but it grows with the number of tourists added. Most of the local people are Spanish, but there are small US and European communities, since many people choose Torre del Mar as a place to retire. That&rsquo;s why except for Spanish, you will surely be able to communicate in English and you may hear some German as well.

**Currency**

The currency used here, as in the rest of Spain, is the Euro. It is a currency used in most European countries, which is a good thing, as you can easily buy it from literally every European country. Exchanging money is possible at the local hotels, although the rates aren&rsquo;t as good as the rates given in the banks of the bigger cities. However, you can use your credit cards instead, since they are widely accepted in Torre del Mar. Only smaller restaurants may require from you to pay in cash. When making payments, it is good to know that 1 Euro is equal to $1.20.
