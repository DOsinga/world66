---
title: "Things to Do in Torre del Mar"
type: section
---

In case you are wondering what to do in Torre del Mar, you might want to add these suggestions to your **Torre del Mar Things to do** list. Located between Malaga and the city of Nerja, Torre del Mar is a land in Spain, blessed with a beautiful coastline.

**Aqua Park**

Enter the aquatic world in Aquavelis, which is a major tourist attraction and entertainment center in Torre del Mar. This aqua park is one of a kind in this region and offers numerous rides and fun activities to visitors. It is well maintained with high levels of hygiene. The park offers various rides like water roller coaster, splash, water slides and the likes. The natural waves are another attraction here. It is located very close to the city center and the working hours are from 11:00 a.m. to 6:00 p.m. and you can receive a 40 percent discount at noon.
**
Camping **

The fabulous camping facility in Torre del Mar will be a perfect spot to camp with the whole family. Take in the beauty of the natural and picturesque surroundings of Torre del Mar at this campsite. You can set up your tents and enjoy the nature here. It is located close to the sea hence offers a great view. The campsite also has a swimming pool with water slides. There are restaurants and stores available for visitors.

**Go Karting**

Go karting is taken very seriously in Torre del Mar. It is a fun sport for the entire family to enjoy. Head to the Karting de Sol and feel your adrenalin pumping. It is located in on the N-340 road that goes to Malaga. It is open on all days of the week. The facility provides 2 circuits each for kids and adults. You can also ride motorbikes here. There is a mini golf area as well. If you wish to relax and enjoy a meal, you might want to check out the bar and restaurant.
**
Beach**

Take in the sun and the sand at the beach in Torre del Mar. The beach is a favorite hangout for the locals as well as the tourists. The water is crystal clear and the waves are not very rough. You can get a good tan at this beach as it has a fairly Mediterranean climate. You can go for a swim or simply take a walk on the golden gray sand. The beach also offers the visitors a great chance to set out on a cycling tour.

**Promenade
**

The Promenade that is recently developed houses the famous lighthouse and offers a majestic view of the city. You can view the mountains and the hills in this Mediterranean region. You will see numerous white houses lined up adjoining the sea. A walk on the promenade will take you to the bandstand area where you will witness various live performances.
