---
title: "La Caleta de Vélez"
type: poi
category: "Sight"
latitude: 36.7469
longitude: -4.0694
---

The fishing harbour and marina just east of Torre del Mar proper, still working and worth walking around early morning to watch the catch unloaded. The port is surrounded by seafood restaurants that source directly from the boats; grilled fish and boquerones (anchovies) are the local speciality. La Caleta gives Torre del Mar a more authentic note than the beach strip alone.
