If you're planning a trip to Spain, or if you're looking for an ideal beach location for your next vacation, look to this **Torre del Mar travel guide** for more information. Torre del Mar, or "Towers of the Sea" in Spanish, is one of the most popular coastal resort areas among European tourists. Many Spanish citizens travel to this southern town to get a taste of the beach. Read on for a brief overview of this unique and charming tourist city.

**Marvelous Beaches**

Torre del Mar is located in the Malaga region of Spain along the southern coast. The town itself has about 20,000 permanent residents, but this population balloons in the summer as the weather becomes nice and the beaches open. The beaches along the coast of the Mediterranean are Torre del Mar's biggest attraction. In fact, the two-kilometer (1.5 mile) beach front area is the longest continuous stretch of pristine beach in the Mediterranean.

**Delicious Seafood**

Another of the main draws to Torre del Mar is the food. Due to its ideal condition and location along the beach and the Mediterranean, Torre del Mar is home to some of the best seafood and the richest array of restaurants in Spain. Prices tend to be reflective of a standard tourist location, but they're not outrageous, particularly if you're traveling from another part of Europe. Due to the fluctuation between the United States dollar and the euro, however, American travelers may find prices to be high, on the whole.

**Nightlife**

El Copo is the famous nightlife area in Torre del Mar, and the clubs, bars and discos on this stretch of beachfront property rage all throughout the night during the summer months. Although the town does have a young and vibrant atmosphere, it's also enjoyable for families and older travelers who may be looking for a more relaxing trip as well.

Torre del Mar sports some of the finest hotels in Spain, so travelers who wish to stay in luxurious suites will find their fair share in this area. There are also more economical options for those on a budget, as well, making this travel option an appropriate one for all types of different adventurers. Consult with the Spanish Tourism Board for additional information and for recommendations of accommodations, activities and more within the Andalucia region and Torre del Mar.
