Considering your **Torre ****del**** Mar when to go** options, be sure that there is no better season for visiting the town than in the summer. Torre del Mar has a Mediterranean Subtropical climate, which results in hot summer and mild winter. And although the summer is the best season for taking advantage of the town beaches, tourists keep arriving in the winter as well, since Torre del Mar is an excellent place to escape from the everyday stress of the big city. Here are details on the weather in the summer and in the winter. Hopefully, they will help you in deciding when to go to Torre del Mar.

**Summer**

Normal day temperatures in the summer are about 30 degrees Celsius (86 degrees Fahrenheit). The nights are warm as well, with temperatures at night falling with no more than 8 degrees Celsius (15 degrees Fahrenheit). This makes it perfect for having a night out with friends after a long sunny day on the beach. The sea waters are perfect for swimming as their temperatures range from 20 to 22 degrees Celsius (68 to 72 degrees Fahrenheit). August can be a bit unpleasant, as humidity rises significantly.

**Winter**

Winter months hold steady temperatures of 15 to 17 degrees Celsius (59 to 63 degrees Fahrenheit).
