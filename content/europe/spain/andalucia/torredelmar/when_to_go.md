---
title: "When to Go"
type: section
---

July and August are peak season — hot (regularly above 30°C), busy, and expensive. June and September are better: warm enough for beach days, the sea has heated up by September, and the crowds thin noticeably. May and October are good for walking and sightseeing inland without the summer heat. Winters are mild (15–17°C) and sunny by northern European standards, and the town is almost entirely quiet; many beach restaurants close from November to March.
