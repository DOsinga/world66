title: "Bars and Cafes in Torremolinos"
type: section
---

Torremolinos, on the Costa Del Sol, plays host to all types of bars and eateries geared towards the package holiday maker and also caters to the most seasoned travelers.

**Tapas**

Tapas are snack-sized dishes that are served in virtually every bar and cafe in this region to keep hunger pangs at bay. Popular tapas include Tortilla Espanola - Spanish omelet, Patatas Bravas - potatoes in a spicy sauce and Calamares Fritos - fried squid. The list is endless and it is often a good idea to have a look at what dishes are popular with the locals in the cafe and then order some of those. Tapas are certainly a culinary experience which is not to be missed!

Calle Quintado Rosado in the Old Town plays host to several good quality tapas bars including Casa Pacos which sells a varied selection of these little dishes starting from 1 euro in price.

Bar la Bodega, Calle San Miguel 40, is very popular with locals and tourists alike and serves seafood at good prices. It is open Mon. to Sat. from 12:30 p.m. to 11:30 p.m. and Sun. from 12:30 p.m. to 7 p.m. Calle San Miguel is the place to find many little traditional tavernas for that typical and traditional Spanish feel.

**Bars**

There is certainly no shortage of bars in the Torremolinos area which is famed for its good nightlife--from music, karaoke and show bars to the quieter little tavernas dotted around its back streets.

In the center of town there are many touristy Flamenco bars such as the Taberna Pepe Lopez where for approximately 23 euro a person can watch the show from 10 p.m. A drink is included in the price.

**La Carihuela**

La Carihuela, the old town in the west of Torremolinos, is well worth a visit. This area is renowned for its traditional Andalucian cuisine and plays host to many charming cafes and bars in which seafood is a specialty although other dishes are also served. Also, there are some good salsa bars in this area, including the Atrevete which is home to 2 dance floors and welcomes both beginners and also the seasoned dancer.

**Nogalera**

This is a very gay friendly area in the center of the town and as such plays host to many bars and cafes that welcome people from every walk of life. The Kiss Music Bar is to be found here, and plays music from various decades and stays open until 4 a.m. The Rock Bar whose name is self explanatory is also situated in Nogalera.
