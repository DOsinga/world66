---
title: "Bars and Cafes"
type: section
---

La Carihuela has the best tapas scene — the streets off Calle Quintado Rosado have several good local bars where tapas start around €1. Bar la Bodega on Calle San Miguel is consistently popular with locals and tourists for its seafood. The Nogalera area in the town centre is Torremolinos's gay-friendly zone, with bars that stay open late. For a flamenco show with dinner, the Taberna Pepe Lopez (around €23 per person including a drink) is a reliable tourist option.
