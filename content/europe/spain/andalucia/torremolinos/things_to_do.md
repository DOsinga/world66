---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

The most satisfying thing to do in Torremolinos is simply walk La Carihuela early in the morning before the tourists emerge — the old fishermen's quarter has a different rhythm to the main resort. The 14th-century Torre de Pimentel, from which the town takes its name, is climbable for free and sits at twelve meters high. Parque La Batería, on a hill to the west, is a pleasant park with fountains and a lake built on a former military lookout. Cortijo del Tajo, on the outskirts, has Roman and pre-Roman archaeological remains worth a stop for the interested.
