Cantavieja gives good views from its hilltop position into the mountains of the Maestrazgo area. However there are not a lot of specific sights in the village itself, other than the church shown on the main Cantavieja page and the arcaded area to its right that includes the ayuntamiento [town hall] and the TIC.
