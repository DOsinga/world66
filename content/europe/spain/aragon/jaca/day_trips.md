title: "Day Trips"
type: section
---

The obvious day trip for those using public transport is across the French border and out to Oleron Ste Marie and back. This is easily achieved by first getting a train or bus to Canfranc, near the border. From outside the station SNCF [French Railways] runs a bus to Oleron Ste Marie through fine wooded mountain slopes and valleys.

NB There are also local buses from Canfranc to Jaca which can be caught at the stop in view a little ABOVE the station
