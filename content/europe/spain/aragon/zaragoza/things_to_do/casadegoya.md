Francisco de Goya was born in this modest stone house in the village of Fuendetodos, 44km south of Zaragoza, in 1746. The house has been restored to how it would have looked in his childhood and now functions as a small museum. The adjacent Museo del Grabado holds a good collection of Goya's prints. Worth combining with a visit to the nearby medieval village of Belchite for a full day out.
