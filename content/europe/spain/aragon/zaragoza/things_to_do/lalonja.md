The old stock exchange, a restrained Renaissance building completed in 1551, sits just off the Plaza del Pilar. The single-nave interior — a flat-beamed hall with slender Ionic columns — is used today for temporary exhibitions and is usually free to enter. Often overlooked in favour of the basilica next door, but worth a quick visit for the architecture alone.
