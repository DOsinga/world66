On the south of the island you’ll find Playa Des Cavallet. Don’t worry to much about what to wear here, it’s the island's official nudist beach. With its array of dunes and the long white strip of sand it’s one of the most beautiful beaches on the island. As might be suspected it’s also a hotspot for gay people, so be open minded or stay away if you’re easily offended.[back to Beaches](..)
