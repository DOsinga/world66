Ibiza is Europe's undisputed clubbing capital. The island's nightlife runs on a schedule of its own — doors open around midnight and parties run until noon the next day. The clubs are enormous, the sound systems world-class, and the DJ line-ups draw the biggest names in dance music every summer season.
