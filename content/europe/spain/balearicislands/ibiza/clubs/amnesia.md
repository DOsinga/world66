One of Ibiza's legendary clubs with a strong international feel. The best nights are organised by renowned clubs such as Cream in cooperation with Bugged Out, Delicatessen and Godskitchen. In former days this was an open-air disco, but new legislation forced it to get a roof. It is a beautiful venue — though it lacks the exclusivity of Pacha or El Divino. Disco-buses run from both Ibiza Town and San Antonio.
