Space is located in the centre of Playa d'en Bossa. Famous for its unique sun terrace and feared for its crazy all-dayers, it is the place to be for party animals who don't want to wait for the night. The weekly zenith is a 22-hour party that starts at eight on Sunday morning and runs until Monday.
