---

Your arrival by plane will be at Aeroport d'Eivissa (+34/971809000). A bus from the airport to the city centre of Ibiza is about one Euro. A taxi costs about 11 Euro. Other useful telephone numbers might be Iberia (International) :+34/901333222, Iberia (National):+34/901333111 and Air Europe:+34/971760612.
