Ibiza has not only a large collection of clubs. The same goes for eating out. Restaurants that can compete with the clubs when it comes down to atmosphere, tiny nostalgic eating places, ordinary places for a quick bite... whatever you're looking for, Ibiza probably has it. 

**general**

**El Faro**

In the beautifully layed-out garden with palm-trees, located at the end of the harbour, you can enjoy El Faro's Fish specialties. El Faro is especially known for its shell-fish. The Cocotreat is home-made Paella (with fish). The service is friendly and the prices are reasonable. 

address: Plaz Sa Riba 3, 07800 Baleares

tel: (+32) 971 31 75 78

**La Brasa**

The romantic garden enriched with flowers and candlelight give this restaurant an intimate atmosphere. Carmen, the owner of the place, has a collection of specialities on the menu, such as salads, salmon with lobster sauce, blueberry cheesecake and cactus fruit pudding. The kitchen is the experimental place par excellence. Prices are not very cheap, but the food is definitely worth it.  [more..](/europe/spain/balearicislands/ibiza/ibizatown/eatingout/labrasa)

address: Carrer Pere Sala 2, 07800 Baleares

tel: +34) 971 30 12 02

**Alfredo's**

This is the oldest restaurant of Ibiza! The restaurant, which was opened in 1934, has never changed its successful formula. You can have traditional Mediterranean food in this nostalgic place where far-off days are revived. They already have over ten sorts of Paella. The Cocotreat is Alfredo's famous black rice. It might not look that great, but it tastes delicious!   [more..](/europe/spain/balearicislands/ibiza/ibizatown/eatingout/alfredos)

address: Pg. Var de Rey 16, 07800 Baleares

tel: (+34) 971 31 12 74

**Meson De Paco**

Feel the authentic Spanish atmosphere of this restaurant. According to chef Paco, the secret of his well-known dishes lies in the fact that he uses wood instead of gas for his cooking. A lot of people entitle Meson De Paco as the best restaurant on Ibiza. Don't forget to order a bottle of wine, because the wine-list is compiled of absolute fine wines.  [more..](/europe/spain/balearicislands/ibiza/ibizatown/eatingout/mesondepaco)

address: Avenida Bartomeu de Rossello, 1507800 Baleares, Ibiza Town

tel: (+34) 971 31 42 24
