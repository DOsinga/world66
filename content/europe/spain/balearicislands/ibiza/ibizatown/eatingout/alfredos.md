This is the oldest restaurant of Ibiza! The restaurant, which was opened in 1934, has never changed its
 successful formula. You can have traditional Mediterranean food in this nostalgic place where far-off days are revived. They already have over ten sorts of Paella. The Cocotreat is Alfredo's famous black rice. It might not look that great, but it tastes delicious!
