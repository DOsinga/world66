The romantic garden enriched with flowers and candlelight give this restaurant an intimate atmosphere. Carmen, the owner of the place, has a collection of specialities on the menu, such as salads, salmon with
 lobster sauce, blueberry cheesecake and cactus fruit pudding. The kitchen is the experimental place par excellence. Prices are not very cheap, but the food is
 definitely worth it.
