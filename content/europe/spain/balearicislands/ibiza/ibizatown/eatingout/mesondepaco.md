Feel the authentic Spanish atmosphere of this restaurant. According to chef Paco, the secret of his well-known dishes lies in the fact that he uses wood instead of gas for his cooking. A lot of people entitle Meson De Paco as the best restaurant on Ibiza. Don't forget to order a bottle of wine, because the wine-list is compiled of absolute fine wines.
