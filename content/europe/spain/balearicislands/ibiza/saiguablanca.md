A splendid beach, not in the least because there is not much building upon the sand.
 Occasionally, the party crowd comes over to this spot to enjoy the total freedom that rules the beach. Although it is not officially recognized as a nudist beach, feel free to peel off all clothes for a good swim or for an even bronze.
