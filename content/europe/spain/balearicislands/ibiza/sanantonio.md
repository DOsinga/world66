The main beach at San Antonio de Portmany sits just steps from the town centre, which makes it both the most convenient and the most crowded stretch of sand on the island. It is a wide arc of fine sand with calm, shallow water - good for swimming and for recovering from the night before.

The beach fills up fast in high season, especially by mid-morning. If you want space, head to nearby Cala Gració or Cala Salada instead. But if you are happy with the buzz of sun loungers, beach bars, and a steady stream of people-watching, San Antonio delivers. Sunset views from the Café del Mar end of the bay are the main event.
