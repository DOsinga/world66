---
title: "Top 5 Must Do's in Ibiza"
type: section
---

Known primarily as a club paradise, **Ibiza **has many sides beyond its larger than life party atmosphere. Its stunning scenery, great hotels, authentic restaurants and secluded beaches make it a paradise in more ways than one. Here are the top 5 things to do on your trip to Ibiza:
**
Charter a Boat**
There are many private boat charters on the island that offer relaxing boat trips to enjoy the beautiful scenery. Choose between an afternoon cruise, a sunset viewing, or a romantic dinner with your special someone. Also many of the boats can be tailored around your requirements if you want to do any watersports or other specific activities.

**Explore the Beaches**
Talamanca Beach, Salinas Beach, Cala Gracio, Cala des Moro and Benirras Beach are among some of the top beaches. Rent a car and explore several of the beaches and their different attributes. Some are developed for tourism, while others are remote and virgin. Pack a lunch and spend a day checking out some of the diverse beaches the island has to offer.
**
Faro de Botafoc**
This lighthouse is right off the beach as you head to Talamanca. Enjoy some great panoramic views from here where it is particularly impressive at night viewing Ibiza town from afar. This is a great scenic spot to take pictures and enjoy the great vantage point for a relaxing afternoon or evening.
**
Museo de Arte Contemporaneo de Ibiza**
The island’s contemporary art museum organizes several exhibitions a year, consisting of both local contemporary artists and international acclaimed ones. The museum hosts a biennial international art exhibition that has been taking place since 1963. Situated in the old town, this museum is a great cultural break from the sun and hot weather.
**
Casca Antiguo de Dalt Vila**
This UNESCO World Heritage Site displays Phoenician, Roman, Moorish and medieval remains throughout its narrow and winding streets. A walled town teeming with historical buildings, this is the site of the Ibiza Cathedral, the first parish of Santa Maria started in the 1200s. There is an Archaeological Museum several churches and a castle to explore, making for a great day trip of adventure.

There is plenty of nightlife on the island to explore after dark on Ibiza. Check out some of these ideas during the day and add some spice to your night by exploring some of the many hot spots on the island for a relaxing and enjoyable vacation.
