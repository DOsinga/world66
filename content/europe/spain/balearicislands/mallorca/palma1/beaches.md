Palma is quintessentially Spanish in its breathtaking natural beauty and exotic cultural landscape. ** **This guide offers visitors helpful insights on the beaches that line-up to form a stunning coastal crown of the region.

**Orientation**

Palma’s beaches are an integral part of its well-defined and gallant
identity. It was through them that the town earned a cult status of a
maritime bigwig. The maritime exchange centers and sea tribunals
contributed to the grand historical sea-side legacy of the town. Most beaches in the region are nestled in the
town center and a few are even scattered around the semi-urban belts of
Palma. At first
the beaches of Palma may seem extremely hard to reach and it is a
fact that a majority of them are not easily accessible. However this
acts as a sheer inducer for visitors looking to challenge their own
exploratory skills.

**Playa Arenal
**

Playa Arenal is one of the most popular tourist beaches in Palma and
is located close to Palma de Mallorca. Playa de Palma is another
frequented spot that is relatively secluded and offers great
sunbathing opportunities. The shimmery sands of the beach are flanked
by glistening waters that appear strikingly beautiful and the
destination looks resplendent during twilight when the fiery sun melts
in the arms of the horizon. A simply stunning vista.

**Badia de Palma**

The clique of
beaches located around Badia de Palma are an excellent option for a
reclusive holiday. There are few beaches in this cluster that include
intimate beach-side restaurants, open air bars and a plethora of water
sports. The more inaccessible ones are housed within the coves of
Mallorca and they are worth every bit of the effort that takes to reach
them. Most beaches station lifeguards in summer and have strictly
demarcated swimming territory as the water here is notoriously
dangerous at times. A lot of beaches also permit nudism however it is
important to check with the authorities before you decide to throw
caution to the winds and a lot of other things along with it.

Palma is a delightful beach haunt that gives you the liberty to
discover it at your pace and plays the role of a throbbing tourist
resort and a tranquil getaway with equal aplomb.
