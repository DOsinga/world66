---
title: "Eating Out in Palma"
type: section
---

Palma de Mallorca is part of the Baleric islands in Spain. Cuisines
here range from all kinds of different flavors. There are influences
from many other nationalities and cultures including French, English,
Arab, Greek, Phoenician and Roman. In this area,
soups with noodles, meat or fish and vegetables are popular. Here are some of the best options for eating out in Mallorca.

**Expensive Restaurants**

There are a few restaurants in the area that specialize in fine
dining. The prices here are rather more expensive than average, but
are usually well worth the price. One such place is called **Forn de
Sant Joan**. This restaurant is in an old bakery and has been remodeled
into a fantastic restaurant. The atmosphere is elegant and modern with
a bar and several dining areas. There are four floors to meet your
seating needs and the menu is lengthy and diverse. They serve mostly
local cuisine but there are a few interesting alternatives. There is an
extensive tapas selection along with main dishes that include things
like foie gras and grilled prawns. Main dishes are mostly pastas and
fish or meat dishes. Prices here are pretty pricey but reviews on this
place all say if you’re going to spend a lot of money on a meal this is
the place to do it.

Other expensive restaurants in the area are Bar.co, Abaco and Purobeach Palma.

**Moderately Priced Restaurants**

The average priced restaurant around here offers main dishes at
around $20 to $30. Most of these serve local foods and your best bet is
to find a good Tapas place and order to share. One tapas restaurant is
Taberna de la Boveda which prices are around $20 depending on what you
get.

Other places to try are Mel d’abella and S’Espanja.

**Budget Restaurants**

Eating on a budget is not an impossible task here. There are
restaurants in the area that offer good cheap eats. You might be able
to find some street food or chains that can afford to have lower
prices. One place is Cellar Sa Premsa which served dishes for around
$10 a person. They have great service and good local food.

Other good places for budget dining are + NTLR and Piccola Italia.
