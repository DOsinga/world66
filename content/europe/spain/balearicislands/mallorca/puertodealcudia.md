---
title: "Puerto de Alcudia"
type: location
---

This bustling fishing port lies at the end of a 5-mile stretch of soft, white sandy beach, which shelves gently into the sea and is perfect for swimming or watersports. Venture inland and discover the small town of Alcudia itself, which goes back to the Middle Ages. Sunday and Tuesday are market days, when this quaint town comes to life and you can wander around the colourful stalls, searching for souvenirs.
