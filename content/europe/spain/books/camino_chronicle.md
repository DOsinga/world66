***Camino Chronicle: Walking to Santiago*** weaves together the background information – the rich history and the cultural setting – of Spain and the shock of 9/11 with the modern experience of hiking the Camino. In September 2001, the author and her husband set out to walk across Northern Spain on an ancient trail called the Camino de Santiago.
