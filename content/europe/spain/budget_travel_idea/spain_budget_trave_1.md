From its extensive beaches to ancient cities, **Spain** offers attractions and sights for **budget travel ideas**. The diverse cultures make for a fabulous vacation that will not break your wallet, or fail to satisfy.
Budget Transportation

Iberia, Spain’s national air carrier offers flights in and out
of Spain at a range of prices. The more budget friendly companies
include Click Air, Blue Air, and easyJet. Transportation by train is
another low-cost way for travelers coming from other European
destinations to enter Spain. Spain’s railroad systems are of the newest
in Europe, and come in handy when traveling between major cities,
however they stop little in small rural towns. The best way to travel
to and from Spain is through date flexibility. Travel rates in and out
of the country are at their lowest between October and March. While on
your holiday the most budget friendly form of transportation is by
train from city to city, or by bus and taxi within cities.
Accommodations

Well know amongst top European tourist destinations, Spain maintains
the reputation for its high standards in hotel upkeep. Many of Spain’s
tourist attracting areas are home to fine inns and establishments. To
break best deal on flight rates, it is usually best to book your flight
and accommodation package through a travel agency that has negotiated
discount rates with top of the line hotels. Specifically, Madrid offers
hotel rates as low as 16 euros a night for three start accommodations
when booked through an agency in advanced.
Guilt Free Activities

A trip to a beautiful Spanish beach, apart from the small parking
fees, is a cost-free way to spend your holiday. Along with soaking up
the sun and making memories for free, generally the eateries along the
beaches include casual budget friendly restaurants and cafes. The two
top tourist destinations in Spain, Madrid and Barcelona both offer a
variety of attractions that include budget friendly activities. There
is no need to miss out on seeing 2 of Spain’s most noted cities in
order to maintain a budget. Some of these budget friendly activities
include tours of museums, and the historical downtowns.

Regardless of your budget needs, Spain will not fail to please you
while on your holiday. With the right plan, and packaged deals Spain is
a great destination for your vacation while trying to not break the
bank account.
