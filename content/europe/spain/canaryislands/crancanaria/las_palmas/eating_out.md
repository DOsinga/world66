**Restaurante Casa Carmelo**

Popular steakhouse at Las Canteras. Located near the big hotels, it attract many tourists. 

Tends to be a bit noisy.

zipcode: 35005

address: Paseo de Las Canteras, 2

url: [www.laspalmasgccb.com](http://www.laspalmasgccb.com/es/servicios/eventos/casacarmelo.html)

tel: 928 46 90 56

**Casa Montesdeoca**

Classic spanish food in the old part of Las Palmas called Vegueta. 

The building was raised in the 16th century, but was renovated recently to get the original look. Dinner is served in the yard. 

*Open*: 13:30-16:00 & 20:00-24:00

email: casamontesdeoca@telefonica.net

PriceOfMenu: ¬30

address: C/. Montesdeoca, 10

tel: 928 333 466

**Casa Italia**

Very good Italian resaurant/pizzeria with food at reasonable prices. Excellent choice for shoppers at Avda Mesa y López. It is located just a few meters from the shopping street, on the Pasaje Finlandia.

Closed one day per week.

zipcode: 35007

PriceOfMenu: ¬8

address: Avda Mesa y López, 44

tel: 928 22 41 99
