El Labrador

Located a few kilometers (10 mins in a taxi) from Playa del Ingles, not far from Palmitos Park.

Good local food is served in the garden under the Papaya and Lemon trees.

*Open Hours*: 13:00-16:00 and 18:30-24:00

address: Montaña La Data, 36

tel: 928 14 12 88
