Finding good restaurants where nobody but tourists eat is not easy, but there are a few places that can be recommended.

Ristorante Rimini

This Italian restaurant has two locations in Playa del Inglés. One at Avda. de Gran Canaria, 28 (928 764 187), and the other in the same building as the hotel Corona Blanca on Avda. de Tenerife, 11.

The Tiramisù is served in generous portions!
