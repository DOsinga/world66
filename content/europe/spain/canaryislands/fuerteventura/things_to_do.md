Water Sports are one of the main attractions on Fuerteventura with the windsurf and kitesurf world cup being held each July/August.

The south has lots of wind mainly offshore and is only really good for windsurfing.

The North (Corralejo & El Cotillo) on the other hand, are good all-round locations for many different sports such as Surfing, Windsurfing & Kitesurfing. The main water sports centre is Flag Beach l( www.flagbeach.com) located on the outskirts of town, although there are other companies offering these sports located in and around Corralejo and El Cotillo.

You can also hire quads, take a trip to either lanzarote or the closer island of Lobos.
