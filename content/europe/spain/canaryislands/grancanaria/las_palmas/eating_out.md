Try Canarian cuisine in the capital of Gran Canaria.

### [Restaurante Casa Carmelo](/europe/spain/canaryislands/grancanaria/las_palmas/eating_out/restaurante_casa_c)

Popular steakhouse on Las Canteras beach, close to the big hotels. Tends to be lively and noisy. International menu. Address: Paseo de Las Canteras 2. Tel: 928 46 90 56.

### [Casa Italia](/europe/spain/canaryislands/grancanaria/las_palmas/eating_out/casa_italia)

Good Italian restaurant and pizzeria with reasonable prices, a short walk from the Mesa y López shopping street. Address: Pasaje Finlandia, Avda Mesa y López 44. Tel: 928 22 41 99. Closed one day per week.

### [Casa Montesdeoca](/europe/spain/canaryislands/grancanaria/las_palmas/eating_out/casa_montesdeoca)

Classic Spanish food in a 16th-century building in Vegueta, the old quarter. Dinner is served in the courtyard. Address: C/ Montesdeoca 10. Tel: 928 333 466. Open 13:30–16:00 and 20:00–24:00.
