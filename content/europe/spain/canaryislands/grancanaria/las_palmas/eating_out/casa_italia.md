Dependable Italian restaurant and pizzeria near the Mesa y López shopping street, with reasonable prices and a convivial atmosphere. A good option if you want a break from Canarian food. Address: Pasaje Finlandia, Avenida Mesa y López 44. Tel: 928 22 41 99.
