Classic Spanish and Canarian food served in the courtyard of a 16th-century mansion in Vegueta, a hundred metres from the cathedral. Dinner in the candlelit patio on a warm evening is about as good as Las Palmas dining gets. Prices are higher than the tapas bars nearby but the setting justifies it. Address: Calle Montesdeoca 10. Tel: 828 91 73 94. Open 13:30–16:00 and 20:00–24:00.
