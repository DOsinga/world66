Popular grill restaurant right on the Las Canteras promenade, specialising in Canarian-style grilled meats. Tends to be lively and a little noisy, which adds to the atmosphere. The location makes it an easy choice before or after a walk along the beach. Address: Paseo de Las Canteras 2. Tel: 928 46 90 56.
