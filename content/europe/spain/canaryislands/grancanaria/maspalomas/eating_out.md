The resort strip is packed with restaurants, but finding places that locals actually use takes a little effort.

### [El Labrador](/europe/spain/canaryislands/grancanaria/maspalomas/eating_out/el_labrador)

A few kilometres from Playa del Inglés, not far from Palmitos Park (about 10 minutes by taxi). Good local food served in a garden under papaya and lemon trees. Address: Montaña La Data 36. Tel: 928 14 12 88. Open 13:00–16:00 and 18:30–24:00.
