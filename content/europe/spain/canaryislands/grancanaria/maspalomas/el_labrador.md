A few kilometres from Playa del Inglés, not far from Palmitos Park (about 10 minutes by taxi). Good local food served in a garden under papaya and lemon trees. The Canarian specialities are the strongest part of the menu — try the papas arrugadas with mojo or the grilled goat cheese. Open 13:00-16:00 and 18:30-24:00.
