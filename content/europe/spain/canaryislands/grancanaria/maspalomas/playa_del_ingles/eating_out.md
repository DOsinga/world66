---
title: "Eating Out"
type: section
---

Finding good restaurants in a resort town where almost everyone is a tourist is not easy, but there are a few places worth seeking out.

**Ristorante Rimini** is a reliable Italian restaurant with two locations in Playa del Inglés — one at Avda. de Gran Canaria 28 (928 764 187) and another in the Hotel Corona Blanca building on Avda. de Tenerife 11 (928 764 364). The tiramisù comes in generous portions.
