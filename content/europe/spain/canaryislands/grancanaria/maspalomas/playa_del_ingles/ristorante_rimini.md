This Italian restaurant has two locations in Playa del Inglés. One at Avda. de Gran Canaria, 28 (928 764 187), and the other in the same building as the Hotel Corona Blanca on Avda. de Tenerife, 11. The pasta is freshly made and the pizza comes out of a proper wood-fired oven. The tiramisu is served in generous portions and worth saving room for.
