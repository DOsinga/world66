The neighboring island of [La Gomera](/world/europe/spain/canaryislands/lagomera) is only a 40-minute ferry ride from Los Cristianos in the south. Its ancient laurel forests, deep ravines, and the famous Silbo Gomero whistled language make it one of the most rewarding day trips from Tenerife.
