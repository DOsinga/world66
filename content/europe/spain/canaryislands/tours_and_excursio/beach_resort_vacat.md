On the exciting Playa del Inglés, only a few steps away from an endless sandy beach, you will find a spacious and family-friendly hotel resort with an atmosphere in which you will be able to relax and enjoy your holiday completely.

Amidst sub-tropical vegetation you will find the huge heated fresh-water pool with waterfall and surrounded by well-tended lawns. The Moorish style of the architecture makes the hotel a special experience by night when the impressive illumination turns it into a fairy-tale village from “Thousand-and-One Nights”.

One of the special features of our house is the Child’s Paradise where your little ones are taken care of by a trained professional staff. In addition to our animation programmes especially tailored for children (only during the summer holiday season), there is also a children’s playground as well as a children and baby pool.
