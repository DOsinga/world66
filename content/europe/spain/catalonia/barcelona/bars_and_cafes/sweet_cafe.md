Recently opened, Sweet Café is the newest venue in the Gaixample, the Gay neighbourhood in Barcelona. Wide, cosy and comfortable, is just the ideal place to have a good coffee or a drink, after work or before nightclubbing (open at 8 pm). The music is carefully selected by SouvenirDJ, and spreads from charming downtempo to electropop.

Tuesdays, Wednesdays, Thursdays and Sundays: 8:00pm-2:30pm
Fridays and Saturdays: 8:00pm-3:00am
(Closed on Mondays)
