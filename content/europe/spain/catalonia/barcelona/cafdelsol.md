A comfortable neighbourhood bar on the Plaça del Sol, which is the informal living room of the Gràcia district. Tables spill onto the square, there's a good selection of beers and wines, and the crowd is local rather than touristic. Good for an afternoon drink or a late-evening beer when the square fills up with people.
