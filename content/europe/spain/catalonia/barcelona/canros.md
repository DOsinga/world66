A Barceloneta institution since 1911, Can Ros serves straightforward Catalan seafood in a dining room that has barely changed in decades. The fideuà (a noodle paella) and the grilled fish of the day are the things to order. Portions are generous, prices are fair for the neighbourhood, and the waiters have been doing this long enough that they do not need to write your order down. Book ahead on weekends — the locals have not forgotten about this place even as trendier spots have opened nearby.
