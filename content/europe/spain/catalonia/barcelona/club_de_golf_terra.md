Club de Golf Terramar is an 18-hole course in Sitges, about 35 km southwest of Barcelona along the coast. The course runs close to the beach, and several holes have sea views -- it is one of the more scenic courses in Catalonia.

The layout is not especially long but demands accuracy, with narrow fairways lined by pine and palm trees. Green fees run around 75-90 EUR depending on the day. Booking a tee time in advance is recommended, especially on weekends.

Sitges itself is worth combining with a round -- the town has good restaurants, beaches, and a lively atmosphere that makes for a pleasant day trip from Barcelona.
