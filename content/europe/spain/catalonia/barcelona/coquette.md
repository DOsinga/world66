One of the better boutiques in the upmarket Turó Park neighbourhood, Coquette stocks a well-chosen edit of European designers — Isabel Marant, Chloé, Vanessa Bruno, and others that fit the wealthy residential character of this part of the city. The space is roomy and calm compared to the boutiques in the Gothic Quarter, and the staff actually know their stock. If you are shopping for occasion wear or serious everyday pieces rather than tourist trinkets, this is the right neighbourhood to browse.
