If you are an amusement park fan stop by Port Aventura a mega theme park at [Salou](/europe/spain/catalonia/salou) (60 mi/100 km south of Barcelona). Owned in part by the Anheuser-Busch Co. the park is designed with Catalonian Chinese Mexican Polynesian and Wild West motifs (the Catalans love amusement parks). 315 mi/500 km northeast of Madrid.
