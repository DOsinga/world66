The Salvador Dali Museum can be found in the town of [Figueres](/europe/spain/catalonia/figueres) about 75 mi/125 km north of Barcelona. The museum is a must-see because Dali designed the museum himself: It echoes his bizarre personality—look for the photo of the artist going to a bullfight with a loaf of bread for a hat.
