title: "Les Quinze Nits"
type: poi
address: "Place Reial"
---


Excellent value restaurant, right in the heart of Barri Gotic, in Placa Reial (nearest Metro: Liceu). The restaurant opens it's doors for dinner at 8.30pm. They don't take bookings, so the queue forms from about 8pm - join it to find out why, you won't have to queue for long and you won't be disappointed. The menu is varied, with a good selection, especially appetizers and starters, that isn't to say there isn't a variety of mains either (inc. Pasta, Meat and seafood dishes). 3 course (inc coffee and wine) for 3 people came to less than €60. The atmosphere is pleasant and the staff are attentive, making for very efficient service.
