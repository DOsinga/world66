A very nice* taperia* right in the Passeig de Gracia, the street of the famous buildings (Casa Batllò and the "Pedrera") created by Gaudi.

People can seat down and choose among more than 50 kinds of fresh,
colourful and tasty tapas (or "pinchos"). Excellent prices. Smoking
area.

Take away available.
