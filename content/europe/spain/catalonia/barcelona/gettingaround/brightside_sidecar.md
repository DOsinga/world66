Discover Barcelona in a unique way, cruising on a classic Sidecar motorcycle – a wonderful journey into the authentic sounds, scents and beats of this unforgettable city.

**BrightSide**'s expert private guides will tailor a tour for each traveler, providing a fine balance between deciphering the great attractions (such as La Sagrada Familia ), understanding the course of history and exploring the most authentic side of the city, beyond the conventional touristic trails.

Using high-tech intercommunication devices, the private guide and the passengers cruise the city debating the history, the architecture, the symbols and the lifestyle of Barcelona and its peoples. **BrightSide Barcelona** offers private guided tours on sidecar motorcycles that may last from 1.5 to 8 hours, day and night.

These vintage styled sidecars are the perfect time travelling machines for such journey across history. The tours follow a readymade route but are always tailored to each traveler's specific expectations.

Free hotel pick-up and fluent English speaking guides available.
