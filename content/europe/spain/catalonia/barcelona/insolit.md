A bar with a deliberately futuristic aesthetic, located inside the Maremagnum shopping and entertainment complex on a pier in the old harbour. Maremagnum is touristy by nature, but Insolit carved out its own identity with unusual decor. The harbour setting means the complex stays open later than most of Barcelona's shops.
