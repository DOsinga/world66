In the 1960s, a group of young artists in Barcelona used their work to challenge the establishment and push for social change. The movement became known as La Gauche Divine, and its spirit inspired this concept store in the Gothic Quarter.

The multi-functional space combines fashion, music, art and design. Find a piece of clothing from a local designer, or sit in El Petit Café surrounded by art displays and contemporary music. The Vintage Lounge stocks outlandish pieces from the Sixties and Seventies. Occasional Summer Catwalk events merge fashion shows with cocktails, performance art and DJ sets.
