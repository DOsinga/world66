A bar near Diagonal where drink prices rise and fall in real time based on demand, displayed on screens like a stock exchange. Order a popular drink and the price climbs; wait for a "crash" and everything gets cheap at once. The gimmick keeps things lively, especially on busy nights. The interior leans dark and clubby, with a young crowd. Good for a fun night out rather than a quiet drink.
