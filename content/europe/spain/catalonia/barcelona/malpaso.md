A small, dimly lit bar tucked into the narrow streets of the Barri Gotic, Malpaso draws a loyal crowd with its eclectic mix of live rock, indie, and alternative music. The space is tight — you'll be elbow to elbow on busy nights — but that's part of the charm. Drinks are cheap by Barcelona standards and the staff keep the energy high. It's the kind of place you stumble into at midnight and don't leave until closing. Look for the unassuming entrance on Carrer de Rauric, just off the Ramblas.
