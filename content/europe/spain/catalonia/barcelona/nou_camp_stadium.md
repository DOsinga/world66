Home of FC Barcelona and one of the largest football stadiums in Europe, with a capacity of nearly 100,000. Even if you're not a football fan, the sheer scale of the place is impressive. The stadium experience tour takes you through the players' tunnel, the press room, and the pitch-side benches, finishing at the club museum, which traces Barça's history from its founding in 1899 through the Cruyff and Messi eras. Match tickets are harder to come by — book well in advance for league games and expect to pay a premium for El Clásico.
