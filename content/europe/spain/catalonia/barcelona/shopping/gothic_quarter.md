Head to the Gothic Quarter for the best Antique shopping Barcelona has to offer. Here you will find unique little shops filled with everything from books and paintings to prints and furniture. Visit on Thursday for a massive antique market held in front of the cathedral from 10:00 a.m.-8:00 p.m.
