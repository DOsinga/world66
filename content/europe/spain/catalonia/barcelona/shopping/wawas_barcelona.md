A line of chic, modern, and colorful Barcelona souvenirs created by a team of local photographers, Wawas Barcelona offers postcards, Xocoa chocolate bars, and more for the saavy traveler. Wawas puts forth a carefully selected bank of images of Barcelona that refocus the city´s architectural tradition and discovers new points of view.
Barcelona is modernism, but also the tiled roofs of the Ciutat Vella, the Museo de Arte Contemporáneo, graffiti, urban furniture, palm trees, kilometers of beach against the sea, among many other attractions of the city.

The perfect memory of a stay in Barcelona, Postales Wawas are a collection of 54 images of the city that complement the traditional views of Barcelona with touch of humor and elegance that combine modernity and tradition. A lush collection of photos done by the hands of two Barcelonians who share with you they city in which they have lived their entire lives. These are the ideal postcards for those visiting Barcelona for the first time, those who have adopted it as a second city, and for regular visitors looking to take with them smaller details of Barcelona life.

A combination of innovation and sweetness has invaded Barcelona. Barcelona Bombon is a line of chocolate bars that seduces addicts of high-quality chocolate and gourmets of the souvenir world at the same time. Images of the city surround 85 grams of chocolate in seven different models that represent both the tradition and savoir faire of Xocoa and the distinctive view of Barcelona from Postales Wawas.

The products of Wawas are distributed in cultural centers such as Laie-La Perdera, Caixa Forum, CCCB, Fundació Antoni Tàpies, Macba, Fundació Joan Miró, Museu Tèxtil, gift shops and other sites emblematic of Barcelona, with great commercial success that represents a clear alternative and complement to the other products of the market.

Wawas also plans to open their first independent shop by the end of 2006.
