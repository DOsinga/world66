Rising above it all, you can have spectacular views over the city from this 18th century castle that was built on top of ruins dating from the 17th century.
 ** D. Ralphs:** A fine place to visit with military exhibits. Wonderful views of Barca and the port. A cablecar takes you there past a small fairground and the whole visit is not expensive.
