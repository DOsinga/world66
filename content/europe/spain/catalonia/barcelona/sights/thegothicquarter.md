This quarter is located in the Old Town and shows some fine pieces of architectural art from over several centuries. At the Plaza de La Seu, you can visit the very impressive Cathedral that dates from the 14th century and the Santa Maria del Mar. Besides that, it’s a fascinating area to just walk around, breathe in the atmosphere and relax at one of the many little cafes in one of the many winding streets. In one of the former palaces, dating from the 15th century, you can see the early works of Picasso. -- End of The Gothic Quarter Section

**La Seu Cathedral**

Built in the 14th century, this Gothic Cathedral is definitely one of the highlights of the Gothic Quarter. There are also three other palaces in the Cathedral compex: Cases dels Casonges, Casa del Dega and Case de L’Ardiaca.
