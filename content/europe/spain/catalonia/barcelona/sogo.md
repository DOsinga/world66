The Barcelona branch of the Japanese department store chain Sogo opened in 1999 on Avenida Diagonal, occupying a large building that had previously been an El Corte Inglés. It was the chain's only Spanish location, offering Japanese-style retail across multiple floors — fashion, homewares, food hall. It closed in 2003 after the parent company's financial difficulties, and the building has since been taken over by other retailers.
