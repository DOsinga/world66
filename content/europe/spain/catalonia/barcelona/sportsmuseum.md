The Museu de l'Esport Doctor Melcior Colet covers the history of sport in Catalonia, from the 1992 Olympic Games back to traditional Catalan activities like castells (human towers) and correfocs. The collection includes memorabilia, photographs, and equipment spanning more than a century. There is also a study centre attached for researchers. It is a niche museum, but worth a visit if you are interested in how sport and Catalan identity intertwine.
