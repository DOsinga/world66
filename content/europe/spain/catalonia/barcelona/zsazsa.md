An elegant cocktail bar on Carrer del Rosselló in the Eixample, Zsa-Zsa draws a well-dressed crowd with professionally crafted drinks and a refined atmosphere. The interior is sleek without being stuffy, making it a good choice when you want something more considered than the usual Barcelona bar scene. Cocktails are well-made and priced accordingly.
