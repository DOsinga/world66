Besalú is almost entirely mediaeval, its prime sight being the fortified bridge. It is reached, if you are on public transport, by bus from Gerona via [Banyoles](/europe/spain/catalonia/girona/banyoles) There are various tourist trips possible [a bit expensively] by mini-train but it may well be sufficient just to explore the buildings and alleys of the town.
