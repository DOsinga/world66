The Summer Festival of Vilanova gets off to a stylish start with a sunny morning procession of fantastical Catalan Giants, culminating in a colourful show of twirling dancers. Following this event, the atmosphere becomes electric as everyone looks skyward and cheers on the Human Castle team as the formations grow higher and higher. The Human Tower tradition is unique to Catalonia and is based upon a 17th century Valencian dance in which a small number of people balanced on each other’s shoulders. It has since evolved into the massively celebrated Catalan tradition that it is today. Human Castle days are rarely held in Barcelona centre, and the events round Catalonia are not hugely publicised.
