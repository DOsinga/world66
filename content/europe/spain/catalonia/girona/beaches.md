---

Along the province of Girona there is the famous COSTA BRAVA: beautiful beaches and cove-bays, still many deserted, especially off the season. Cadaques, Calella de Palafrugell, Palamos, **[Sant Feliu de Guíxols](/europe/spain/catalonia/girona/accommodation)** and Tossa de Mar are some of the notorious sea-villages worh visiting.
