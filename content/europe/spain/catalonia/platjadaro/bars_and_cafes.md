---
title: "Bars and Cafes in Platja d'Aro"
type: section
---

If eating in a fancy restaurant is not for you, another option that you have is eating at any of **Platja d’Aro Bars and Cafes**. The bars and cafes located around town can provide you with some of the best local food that you have ever eaten, but they can also provide you with a relaxing atmosphere, which can make the meal even more enjoyable.

**Café del Mar**

If you are looking for some of the best seafood around Café del Mar is the best place to go. Here you can get seafood until the late hours of the night. In addition to having, some of the best seafood around Café del Mar is also a relaxing place to enjoy a few drinks, especially after a nice big meal.

**Bar Skull**

If you are looking for more of a bar than a café, you should stop by and visit Bar Skull. Here you will find live entertainment during certain nights of the week, but you can always find a table or a seat inside the bar to grab a drink or 2. If you are looking for a great place that the locals go to have fun the Bar Skull is definitely a great place to go. In terms of food, Bar Skull doesn’t have much to offer, but they do offer light snacks.

**Can Basso**

If you are looking for a café that caters to the local clientele instead of the tourists, Can Basso is one of the best places to go. Here you can grab a quick drink during the afternoon, which can be very refreshing after a day of shopping or sightseeing. If you are more in the mood for some fine local cuisine, Can Basso can help you. In terms of local cuisine, the seafood is some of the best around because it is fresh.

**Hotels Planamar S.L.**

If you are looking for a place close to the beach to grab a cup of coffee or even a quick lunch or dinner this is the place for you, makes it even better if you are staying in the hotel. The food in the cafes located inside the hotel and the main restaurant of the hotel offer a range of choices, including vegetarian, but you can also find many international dishes to enjoy.

**Hotel Monterrey**

This hotel is also located along the beach, which makes it a great place to stop in for lunch or dinner. The restaurant offers many different choices of food, including a buffet service. All are welcome to stop in the restaurant; it is not only reserved for guests.
