**Platja d'Aro** **Beaches**
**
Platja Gran **

The largest beach is called Platja Gran
(Main Beach) and is closest to the center of town. It offers more
than 2 km of golden sand surrounded by tourist amenities and
services. The Red Cross provides life guard services for this beach.
Showers are available on the beach, and public bathrooms are located at
the south end of the beach, but every shop and restaurant along the
boardwalk offer public bathrooms as well. Beach volleyball, tennis,
water volleyball, beach rugby and football are available in
the “Beach Fun” area. In the middle of the beach, you can rent
pedal boats and there are 8 boardwalks around the beach.
**

Cala Rovira **

Cala Rovira, to the north of town, is a
busier beach but offers few amenities, such as a diving platform,
showers and a public bathroom that is located near the beach. There are no parking facilities, so drivers should park their cars in town. The Red Cross provides first aid and lifeguard services. There
is only one boardwalk that is located close to the Red Cross station.

**Cala sa Cova **

Cala sa Cova is at the northern end of
town and is only accessible by car via the road from Calonge. There are no amenities on this beach, but it is a perfect choice for those
looking for a relaxing time by the water.
**

Cala dels Canyers **

Cala dels Canyers is also at the north
end of the town and is a very small beach where trees literally touch
the water, leaving a very narrow strip of sand and stones. There are
no amenities, no parking is available and it is mostly frequented by
locals and preferred by people looking for peace and quiet and
contact with less developed surroundings.

Cala del Pi is a small cove offering no
amenities or parking, but the beach is pristine and quiet, since it
is mostly local swimmers.
**

Sa Conca **

Sa Conca is a pristine beach often used
in advertisements and in movies. It is not as urbanized as some of
the other beaches and offers minimal amenities, such as a diving
platform, public bathrooms behind the beach and 3 showers on the
beach. The Red Cross provides first aid and lifeguard services. There
is only one boardwalk that is located close to the Red Cross station.
**

Cala Pedrosa **

Cala Pedrosa is located at the southern
end of town within the s'Agaro housing estate and is usually very
quiet. There are no amenities available.
