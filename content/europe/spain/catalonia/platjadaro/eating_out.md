There are so many **Platja d’Aro Restaurants** to choose from that sometimes making a decision on where to go is hard. The best way to make a decision about where to go is to have some idea of what kind of food you like, but even then, that might not be enough. Sometimes knowing what other people have thought about a restaurant can help you make a faster decision.

**New Amsterdam**

If you are looking for a quiet, but kid friendly restaurant New Amsterdam is one of the best places to go. At this restaurant, you will find good food for a great price, but if you are looking for awesome service, you will want to go before 9 p.m. If you like to eat a salad with your meal you are given a free salad with any course that you order, so this can help you to save on your total bill.

**Aradi**

If possible, you will want to take a table out on the terrace, because there is nothing like eating a good meal in the fresh air, especially during the summer months. The restaurant is kid friendly, best dish possible for kids is the spaghetti Bolognese. As for adults, fried fish are always a good choice, but the special seafood entrée is highly recommended.

**Llevant**

This is a rather large restaurant, but they also have plenty of waiters to ensure that you are properly served. While the restaurant is always very crowded, your wait time will not be very long because of how efficiently the restaurant is managed. One of the best things about the restaurant is how many different options you have to choose from in terms of food selection, but no matter what you choose, you will not be disappointed.

**Pizzeria San Luis**

The Pizzeria San Luis is one of the best international restaurants in the area. Their pizza and pasta are great dishes to try, but they also serve some great local food. While you are in Platja d’Aro, you will want to try at least 1 or 2 traditional Catalan dishes, which this place can offer. At the end of each meal, you will be given a free “chupito” of liquor.
