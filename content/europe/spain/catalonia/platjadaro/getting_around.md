---
title: "Getting Around in Platja d'Aro"
type: section
---

**Traveling by Bus**

Traveling by bus is the most efficient way to travel. The cost is about 10
Euros for the whole trip. It does take some time though because you have to go
to 3 different stops. Those stops include Barcelona, Estacion du Nord and
then finally Playa de Aro. There is the aerobus option but it costs 16 Euros plus
2 Euros per piece of luggage.

**Traveling by Train**

There are trains that go to Barcelona then to Estacio de Sants and then to
Girona where you get the bus to Playa de Aro. Even though it seems safer, the bus
system does seem faster and cheaper.

**Traveling by Air**

There is an airline that will travel from most major airlines to Playa de
Aro. It is more expensive but a direct flight is much faster then other
methods. The prices, of course, change according to the time of the year but they
run around $150 to $200.

**Walking
**

Taking a flight will give you more time to see the wonders of the area. You
can try walking to many of them. The beaches with the relaxing sunsets are warm
during the day. You might even discover new and unmanned beaches just by walking
along the "Paseig Maritim".

The Benedormiens Castle is another exciting place to
visit. The history of the castle is fascinating and dates from the time of knights and ladys.
Don’t worry about the weather changing because you can still get along to the many
festivals and events that happen all year round.
