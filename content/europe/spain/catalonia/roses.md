Travel Guide

Roses on the Costa Brava. Is the biggest resort on the northern stretch of the Costa Brava, it is just 30 kilometres south of the French border. Roses has some of the best beaches and is a good base from which to explore the rugged coast of Cabo de Creus, Cadaques and Figueres.
Also ideally located to offer holiday makers an extraordinary range of activities, national parks, water sports, diving and many nature walks through untouched natural grounds.
