**Salou Bars and Cafes **are known to be some of the liveliest on the Spanish coast. The city is filled with bars and cafes offering live entertainment that will make your stay here a lot of fun including comedians, quiz nights, bingo, karaoke and impersonators. The resort is popular with Spanish, Welsh, English, Scottish, Irish, French, Belgian, Dutch, German, Austrian, and Russian tourists.
**Bars in Salou

The Rose and Crown**

The most friendly Bar in Spain inviting tourists and locals for great tasting and refreshing drinks in a home from home setting. Guests are able to enjoy their chosen drink while relaxing and soaking up the great atmosphere. Bingo and Quizzes start from the beginning of May and go on throughout the summer. Great Music is played all night and are regularly open late. Opening times are 12pm - 2am.

**Gabbers**

This is the oldest pub in the city of Salou, and best of all it is a family friendly pub. If you have children with you on vacation, stop in here for a drink during the early evening, this way your children can be entertained with the live DJ. The DJ entertains the crowd until late in the evening; once he is done, the disco begins. This is also the place to go in the morning if you are looking for British sausages instead of Spanish ones.

**Murphy’s**

This is your traditional Irish bar. If you are in the mood to watch sports then come sit down and grab a beer inside of Murphy’s. Live sports are always on display thanks to the satellite dish that they have installed. While watching the latest sporting event on the television, you can grab a Guinness, which is hard to find anywhere else.

**Habaneres**

If bars and discos are not to your liking, perhaps you would like to take a moonlight stroll along the port. If so, while walking along the port, be sure to stop and listen to the fishermen’s folk songs that are being played. If you take a moment to stop and listen, you will be treated to a ron cremate during intermission.  

**Knabbel and Babblel**

One of the best things about this bar is its name, because it makes you want to go and see what it is all about. As long as you visit the bar during high season, you will be treated to great music every night. Something else besides the name that stands out with this bar is there one of a kind drinking card, which is a system that allows you to purchase a 20 token drinking card, which you can use to get discounts on each drink that you order.
**Cafes in Salou
**

There are numerous cafes and open air bars throughout the city that cater to people wanting to enjoy a drink in the beautiful weather but don't want to be in the typical bar atmosphere. Check out Cafe Claq,
Crema, Kalea and El Pou for nice cafes perfect for a beautiful sunny
day.

Salou is a great place to relax and enjoy some great nightlife. Whether you are with your family or with friends, you can enjoy the many great nightspots available here and soak in some true Spanish culture. Explore the different bars, restaurants and cafes in the area to find your
favorite.
