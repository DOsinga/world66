Salou is a beautiful seaside resort in Catalonia Spain. It is located just 10 kilometers away from Tarragona and about 92 kilometers from Barcelona. Since it is a popular tourist destination, **Salou restaurants** are second to none and eating out in Salou is an experience by itself. There are several fine restaurants in Salou and if you are visiting the area you must stop by these fine restaurants to enjoy the fine cuisine here. Here are a few places where you will really enjoy the food.

**Arena Restaurant**

After being in the business for 7 years, Arena Restaurant has stood out to be one of the best restaurants in the region. The restaurant serves fine Mediterranean food. Maribel Guardiola, the chef, and his team, take care to provide clients with dishes rich in flavor and freshness. The restaurant also includes a special children’s meal. While you are at the restaurant try the Gilthead bream oven baked with anchovies, olives and tomatoes.

**Can Bas**

Can Bas is a small family owned restaurant in Salou. If you are looking for a nice homely place to enjoy a meal with your family, Can Bas is a good place to stop by. You must try the veal and the baked potatoes, you will not be sorry. The veal is cooked just right and is amazingly delicious. The restaurant is located close to the main street that leads to the beach.

**Lasal Restaurant**
Lasal Restaurant is located in the area of ​​the cliffs of Salou (Tarragona), with beautiful sea views where you can choose from a wide variety of dishes, tapas, sandwiches, pizzas, burgers, fish and rice at affordable prices.

**Villa Magda**

Villa Magda is another family owned restaurant that serves great food. If you like to enjoy your meal in a nice comfortable atmosphere away from crowds and noise, Villa Magda is the right choice. The restaurant has received several awards and is one of the most highly spoken of places in Salou. Try the seafood at this restaurant. A good idea would be the salmon fillet with pesto, tomato salsa, and olive oil.

**Hola Ola**

If you like Spanish food, Hola Ola is the place to stop by in Salou. The restaurant serves typical Spanish food and has a very relaxed atmosphere. Try the pork and chips, or the Cuban rice, both are excellent and will leave you wanting more. The restaurant allows smoking, so if you are not a smoker you might want to skip going here.

[**Texas Embassy**](/europe/spain/catalonia/salou/eating_out)

Texas Embassy is a place where you can enjoy a great plate of ribs or sirloin steak. The price at this restaurant is very reasonable and the people are friendly too. If you are looking for a place to have a meal with your family, Texas Embassy is a place worth considering. Look out for Billy the friendly guy at the entrance; he is the guy to talk to if you need any help.

**The Guinness Tavern**

The Guinness Tavern is slightly off the beaten track and so enjoys a peaceful garden terrace where you can enjoy good quality food and drink. A place to relax and enjoy your evening meal on the pine tree shaded garden. The menu is varied including best fillet steaks, steak and Guinness pie, chicken curry, homemade fish cakes and vegetarian dishes also many meals are available as half portions for children. Friendly welcome and great service.
