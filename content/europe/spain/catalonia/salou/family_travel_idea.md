This **Salou Family Travel** guide will give you some ideas to make family travel easier when visiting Salou.

In 2003, Salou has received the"Family Holiday Resort" designation from the Catalan Government.

**Accomodation**

Salou offers several hotels and resorts to suit all budgets. Room prices start around 60 € for double occupancy and most hotels will offer discounts for children. The hotels located close to Port Aventura are especially suited for families. They offer larger rooms to accommodate more people, and hotels such as the Hotel Gold River, offers 3 to 4 people family rooms for 170 € or a 5 to 6 people family room for 190 € per night, and these hotels offer unlimited access to the Port Aventura park.

**Beaches**

Salou offers several beaches along the coast. Ponent Beach is perfect for children with its gentle slope and calm water. There are a lot of amenities on site and there are several sand and water sports available.

Llevant Beach is the largest beach in Salou and offers a fully landscaped esplanade where you can relax on the grass or share a family picnic. For a fee, you can learn to windsurf, rent a seadoo, a canoe or pedal boat. The golden sand gently slopes into the water.

Capellans Beach is also perfect with families. The golden sand gently slopes into clear, shallow water and there are several amenities available.

**Port Aventura Park and Aquatic Park**

Port Aventura offers many rides and adventures that are perfect for kids and kids at heart. The park features roller coasters, rides and games with thematic areas. They also present shows, some geared toward children, and several shops and restaurants. The aquatic park features pools, wave pools, a lazy river, a white water rafting experience and some areas specifically designed for children. There is also a beach club offering a beautiful pool with a shallow area and some amenities to be used on the private beach area as well as different restaurants.
