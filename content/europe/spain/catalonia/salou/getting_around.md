---
title: "Getting Around"
type: section
---

Salou is compact enough to walk around, and local buses connect the resort to nearby towns and the airport. For trips to Barcelona or Tarragona, trains and buses run frequently.

**Train Travel**

RENFE trains run between Salou and Barcelona daily every 25 minutes to
1.5 hours. The cost is normally between 6 to 7 Euros for regular trains, but
intercity trains are usually pricier. Intercity trains may offer transportation
when you need it, but with planning to use the regular train is a better bargain.

**Bus Travel**

Salou is serviced by the bus company “Plana.” Their busses also connect Barcelona to
Salou, and run from 8:15 a. m. to 8:15 p.m. every 1 to 2 hours each day. The bus line that
connects travelers from Reus airport to Salou is the Costa Daurada line. It also
stops in La Pineda and Faro. A typical ride from Reus airport to Salou will cost
about 6 Euros and takes approximately 45 minutes. This bus line departs hourly
from 5:11 a.m. to 12:11 a.m. each day.

**Taxi Travel**

Salou offers many affordable and comfortable options for visiting travelers. However, Taxis are very expensive if travelling to Barcelona.
