---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

Salou is easy to reach from across Europe thanks to nearby Reus Airport and good rail connections from Barcelona.

**By Plane**

Reus Airport (REU) is about 15 minutes from downtown Salou. Several budget airlines, including Ryanair, fly here from cities across Europe, Ireland, and the UK, though schedules are seasonal. Shuttle buses run from the airport to Salou.

**By Bus or Train**

RENFE trains run between Barcelona and Salou every 30 to 90 minutes, taking roughly two and a half hours. The bus company Plana also offers direct service between Barcelona and Salou. From Reus Airport, the Costa Daurada bus line connects to Salou in about 45 minutes.
