Salou has the usual resort shopping -- beachwear boutiques, souvenir shops, and a weekly market. For self-caterers, supermarkets stock both local and imported goods.

**The British Supermarket**

Located in Calle Josep Carner, this shop has a wide range of English products, biscuits, chocolates, cooking sauces, drinks, beers, spirits, books, greeting cards and much more. Also supplies seasonal products like easter eggs and christmas puddings. It's open 7 days a week from 9am till late, all year round.
