---
title: "Sights in Salou"
type: section
---

**Salou sites** range from natural attractions such as beaches to
theme parks. The range of activities means that this is a good family
holiday
destination and there is something to keep all age groups occupied.The
town has become a seaside resort which attracts international tourists.
Salou has a port which has played an important part in the history of
the area. Salou's popularity among tourists means that it has well-developed tourist facilities and attractions.

**Beaches**

Salou has a warm Mediterranean climate which makes it the ideal place to
go on a beach holiday. The beaches are clean and many of the beaches
have been awarded blue flag status. Beaches include Platja de
Ponent, Platja dels Capellans, Platja Llarga and Cala Crancs. The white
sand stretches for miles and along the beach are hidden coves to
explore.

**Port Aventura**

This is a well know theme park that was formerly know as the Universal
Studios Theme Park. There is a variety of rides at this theme park
including the Furius Baco which is Europe's fastest roller coaster.
Other rides include The Hurricane and Dradon Khan. In the evening there
are breathtaking performances with fireworks, parades and floats.

**Salou Promenade**

Salou Promenade stretches alongside Llevant beach. It is a good place
to enjoy an evening stroll and get some fresh air while taking in the
scenery. Walking along the promenade you will pass some interesting
monuments and landmarks. Gardens and parks add to the beauty of the area.

**Sports**

There is a variety of water sports available. Golf enthusiasts can
choose between the 18-hole and 9-hole courses in the surrounding area.
There are also facilities for tennis, squash, para sailing and hiking.

**Aquopolis Water Park**

The Aquoplis Waterpark is 9 km from Salou and it is a well visited attraction perfect for a family outing.

**Salvador Dali Museum**

Salvador Dali Museum is a must see day trip from Salou. It contains some of Salvador Dali's most important paintings.

**House of Illusion**

House of Illusion provides a good nighttime outing for the whole family.
The magicians do tricks at people's tables and then there is a warm-up
act before the final spectacular magic show. There is a bar with wine
and beer at this establishment and a friendly staff.
