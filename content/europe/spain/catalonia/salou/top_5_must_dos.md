---
title: "Top 5 Must Do's in Salou"
type: section
---

Salou is located along the seaside in Catalonia, Spain. It is a popular
tourist destination for families and young travelers.** Salou's top 5 must do's**
include theme parks, relaxing on the beach and enjoying the vibrant
nightlife. With the range of activities offered, there is something for everyone at this holiday destination.

**PortAventura**

PortAventura is a theme park located 1 mile from Salou. It is rated
Europe's best theme park. You will need to spend a few days at this
theme park to take advantage of all that it has to offer. It has many
rides that will get your adrenaline pumping such as Dragon Khan which
is a roller coaster ride. There are also rides suitable for young
children and play areas. Universal Studios characters walk around the
theme park to entertain children.

**Caribe Aquatic Park**

The water park offers respite from the heat. Don't forget to bring your
costume so that you can go swimming. The water park is located at the
theme park but it has a separate admission fee.

**Nightlife**

Salou is well known for its vibrant nightlife and a night out is a must
do when visiting Salou. For families there are elegant restaurants
which offer delicious Spanish cuisine. Fish is one of the specialties.
International cuisine is also available. For visitors looking for a
party, there is an abundance of bars and pubs. The bars play both Spanish music and international music. Visitors can enjoy nightclubs
where they can dance the night away including the famous Pacha. July
and August are the months when the nightlife is at its busiest.

**Beach**

Playa Levante is the main beach and along this beach stretches a
promenade. The golden sandy beach is 1.2 km long. Playa Leyante is
frequented by tourists and therefore the facilities at this beach have
been well developed. There is a good atmosphere at this beach as all
the tourists gather to enjoy their day in the sun. Water sports are available at Playa Levante. Playa de Capellans is another beautiful beach
that is lined with cliffs and trees. Playa Poniente has less visitors
and is ideal for those looking to relax and escape the crowds.

**Illuminosa Fountain**

This majestic fountain is situated at the end of the seaside promenade.
At night the fountain is illuminated by a light show and music fills
the air. It is the perfect end to a evenings walk.
