---
title: "When to Go in Salou"
type: section
---

When planning a trip to **Salou, when to go **should be determined
by the climate and what your plans are when you get there. This
beautiful destination on the east coast of Spain is beautiful all year
long, however, during certain seasons the weather shines in Salou,
making it a fantastic destination for your next vacation.

**Weather in Salou******

The entire summer is prime time for tourism in Salou. From May to
October the daily temperature in Salou doesn't fall below 68 degrees and
from July to August it reaches upwards of 86 degrees. The most
comfortable weather and the best time to visit is May through June, when
large tourist groups have yet to arrive and temperatures are
comfortable, ranging from 70 degrees to 80 degrees.

July through August can be nice, though quite hot. This is also a very
busy time so it is important to note that if you will be traveling
during this time, be sure to book accommodations well in advance, as
both national and international travelers flood into the city throughout
the summer.

The fall months of September and October are also quite pleasant though
there is considerable rainfall at this time. This wetter period is
characterized by short, heavy downpours. The rest of the year, Salou is
dry and winters are mild. If you do not mind the cooler weather and
occasional rainfall, winter or fall is a great time to visit to avoid
the massive summer crowds.

**Salou Events
******

Salou, like many Spanish cities, is well known for its love of
festivals. With a calendar full of annual festivals, visitors will revel
at the immense celebration a Spanish fiesta brings. Most of the largest
festivals are in the summer from June through August when weather is at
its finest. During these citywide festivals, hotels and accommodations
can become extremely crowded. If you plan on traveling during the busy
season, it is recommended to begin planning your trip early to ensure
you can get the accommodations you prefer.

Though the weather is always pretty nice by most standards, Salou has
fluctuations in tourism throughout the year based on it. Select a time
that works best for you to travel to this beautiful resort town to enjoy
its festive atmosphere.
