---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment in Sitges"
type: section
---

If you love to party till you drop, then you might be interested in checking out the **Sitges Nightlife** scene. Located 35 km from Barcelona on the Mediterranean coast, Sitges is a paradise for nightlife and entertainment. The town has a range of clubs, pubs, restaurants, and bars to select from depending upon your budget, taste, and stamina! Here are a few recommendations for you.

**Caf&eacute; Mont Roig**

Cafe Mont Roig is one of the highly packed cafes in Sitges, despite its exorbitant prices. The reason for its soaring popularity is the vast open indoor and outdoor seating arrangement. This amalgamation of old and new style makes Cafe Mont Roig a great vantage point. It is a situated at Marquis de Mont Roig, 11-13, Sitges. However, you need to be aware that the service is pretty slow here at the caf&eacute;.

**L&rsquo;Atlantida**

L&rsquo;Atlantida is an open beach Spanish discotheque. It has rocking live music and fabulous DJ&rsquo;s from all over the world spinning awesome beats for dancing. Located at Platja de les Coves, Sitges, the discotheque is well liked by the young, cool crowd.

**Ma Maison**

Ma Maison is a large French restaurant with a beautiful ambiance and a comfy outdoor seating arrangement. It is open from 8 p.m. to midnight every day. Culinary delights range from Italian Bruschetta, Goats Cheese and Serrano Ham to Taglitalles with Shrimp and Vegetables. The &euro;20 meal is great value for money.

**Pacha**

Pacha is located on the outskirts of Sitges, right in the centre of the marina. It is a perfect hotspot for both gay and straight party lovers. This cool night club also has an exclusive &ldquo;gay&rdquo; night every Thursday.

**Parrots Club**

Parrots Club is famous for its beautiful view, amazing service, and, of course, great cocktails. It is popular with all age groups. This exclusive gay club is located in the centre of Plaza Industria, close to all the other gay bars and clubs in the port town of Sitges.

**El Piano**

The El Piano bar has a passionate atmosphere that will surely take your breath away! The highlight of the bar is the performance by the cabaret singers. The bar is popular with both locals and tourists. It is situated at Sant Bonavenutura 37.
