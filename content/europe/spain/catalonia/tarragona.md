Tarragona 'the Balcony of Spain' is, due to the Romans, the most elegant city of the Iberian peninsula. Just like in Barcelona Tarragona's main promenades are called 'Ramblas', where you can see the beautiful modernista buildings. Tarragona also has a beautiful beach which you can admire from the Balco del Mediterrani. This balcony divides the city into an old and new part. Furthermore there are interesting museums and the remains of Tarragona's Jewish quarter is also very worth seeing.
