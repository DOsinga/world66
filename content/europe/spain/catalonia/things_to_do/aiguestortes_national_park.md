The only national park in Catalonia, Aigüestortes covers high Pyrenean terrain of glacial lakes, twisted streams ("aigüestortes" means winding waters), and granite peaks above 3,000 metres. The eastern entrance near Espot and the western one near Boí are both popular starting points. Trails range from gentle lakeside walks to multi-day crossings of the park. Private cars are banned inside — jeep taxis from both villages keep it accessible without the crowds you'd get with road access.
