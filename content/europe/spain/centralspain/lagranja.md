La Granja
 
 
 
 **

La Granja
 **


 
 The beautiful Palace La Granja de San Ildefonso, which
 is situated at about 15 km from Segovia, was
 built in 1720 by Felipe V, following the classical
 French model of Versailles. The most exciting are
 the beautiful gardens and fontains, and the surprising
 collection of carpets in the interior.
