Travel Guide

The Costa Brava is a coastal area with 256 km of nice villages, beaches surrounded by Mediterranean vegetation, and little coves tucked away among green pine trees … just waiting for you to sit there and let your thoughts drift out over the sea.

The Costa Brava is also the best destination for scuba diver, specially the small archipelago of the Medes Islands, a protected area, just at one mile from de harbour of l'Estartit.

The proximity to the coast and the mouth of the River Ter provide these islands with a great quantity of organic material, which leads to the variety of flora and fauna that is typical of the Medes Islands.
