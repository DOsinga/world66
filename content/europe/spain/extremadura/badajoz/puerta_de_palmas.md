title: Puerta de Palmas
type: poi
---

A monumental gateway flanked by two round crenellated towers, built in the early 16th century. It faces the Puente de Palmas, a 15th-century bridge spanning the Guadiana, and served as the main entrance to the city for travellers arriving from Portugal. The gateway has been used variously as a triumphal arch and a prison over the centuries.
