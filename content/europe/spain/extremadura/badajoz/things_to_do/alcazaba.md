title: "Alcazaba of Badajoz"
type: poi
category: "Sight"
latitude: 38.8818

---

One of the largest Moorish fortresses in Spain, built in the 9th century by Ibn Marwan when he founded the city. The walls enclose a hilltop area of about eight hectares, with gardens, the Palace of the Dukes of La Roca, and views over the Guadiana valley. The Torre de Espantaperros, an octagonal watchtower, is the fortress's most distinctive feature.
