A 16th-century palace inside the Alcazaba, now home to the Provincial Archaeological Museum. The collection spans prehistory to the Moorish period, with over 15,000 items from sites across the province. The Megalithic section is particularly strong, and the Roman mosaics and Moorish ceramics are worth a look. The setting inside the fortress adds to the atmosphere.
