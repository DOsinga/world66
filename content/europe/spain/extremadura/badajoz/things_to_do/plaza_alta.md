The old main square of Badajoz, tucked below the Alcazaba walls. Restored with its distinctive red and white Mudéjar-style arches, it was the commercial and social heart of the city for centuries. Today it is quieter than it once was, but the architecture and the views up to the fortress make it worth the climb.
