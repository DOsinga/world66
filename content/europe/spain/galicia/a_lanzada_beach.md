---
title: "A Lanzada beach"
type: location
---

A Lanzada beach, Galicia, with the remains of a tower & a small
hermitage which was erected in mid-14th century. However, some of its
elements are clearly Romanesque: it only has one nave, the semicircular
apse, the rosette-shaped window and trusses with animal figures. Once
inside, the archs have an ogival influence. On the last Sunday in
August, a festival is celebrated round the temple, ending up with the
tradition of the "Bath of the nine waves".
