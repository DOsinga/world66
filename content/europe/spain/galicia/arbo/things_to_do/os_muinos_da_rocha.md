---
title: "Os Muiños da Rocha"
type: poi
category: "Sight"
latitude: 42.1477
longitude: -8.3189
---

A series of old stone flour mills along the Deva river, recently restored as an ethnographic park. The mills were used by local families to grind corn for centuries. Demonstrations show how grain was ground using the traditional water-powered millstones.
