title: Dunes of Corrubedo Natural Park
type: poi

The largest mobile dune on the Iberian Peninsula — a 1km-long, 13m-high ridge of shifting sand in the middle of a wetland nature park. The park around it protects coastal lagoons, marshes, and forest, and is home to over 200 plant species and dozens of water bird species. Boardwalks allow you to walk around the dune without damaging it. The nearby Corrubedo lighthouse marks the southern tip of the Barbanza coast.
