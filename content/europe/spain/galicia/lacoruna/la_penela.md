A well-regarded restaurant in the old town specialising in Galician fish and shellfish. The *mariscos* — fresh shellfish served simply on ice — are the centrepiece, and the fish of the day is almost always worth ordering. Prices are higher than the surrounding tapas bars but not unreasonable given the quality.

Good Albariño list. Booking recommended for dinner in summer.
