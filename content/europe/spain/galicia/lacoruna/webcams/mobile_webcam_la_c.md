Mobile webcam located in one of the highest buildings in the city.
The image updates every two seconds and allows for 360º panoramas. You can enjoy the beautiful view of the fishing port, the yachting port, the promenade, the Riazor and Orzán beaches, the Obelisk Millennium, the Hercule's Tower, the Méndez Núñez gardens and some streets in the old city.

Click the url below, then select *Province of A Coruña > A Coruña (in motion)*
