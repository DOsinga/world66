Built by the Romans in the 3rd
century, Lugo is dominated by its city walls. These run for a distance
of over two kilometres, with a walltop sentry path from which splendid
views can be had of the city, the Los Ancares scenery, the hills of the
county of Guntín and the Miño river valley. Dating from these times too
are the thermal springs at the Balneario de Lugo (Lugo spa) on the
banks of the Miño.
