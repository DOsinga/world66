Churches and Convents, close to the provincial museum,
we have the parish church of San Pedro. It was the temple of the old
convent of San Francisco and it is noticeable its gothic apse and its
front. The convent of Santo Domingo was founded in the 13th century and
in the 17th it suffered important changes. Today the church is left,
the parish of Santiago, the new one. The church of San Froilán, the
church of the Carmen and the chapel of San Roque are from the baroque
era. Outside the city we have the Romanesque churches of Meilán,
Bacerín and Mazo; and the baroque church of the Burgo.
