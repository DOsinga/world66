**Celtic Hill-Fort of Santa Tecla**, is one of the castros of latest construction which has a larger surface. Even though it seems to have an uneven disposition, its constructions were perfectly distributed and small streets and squares were detected. It was one of the first discoveries in Galicia (1913). Next to the road leading up to the mountain, some of its constructions, restored in modern times, can be contemplated.
