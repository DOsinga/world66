Noia's covered fish market on the waterfront is worth visiting in the morning when the catch comes in. Turbot, spider crab, percebes (barnacles), and various species of clam are the local specialities. Even if you are not buying, watching the auction — fast, loud, conducted in Galician — is a genuine slice of the town's working life. The market is most active on weekday mornings.
