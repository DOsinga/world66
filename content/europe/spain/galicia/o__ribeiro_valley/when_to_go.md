---
title: "When to Go"
type: section
---

The valley is pleasant from late spring through early autumn. Summers are warm but not as hot as inland Spain, and the surrounding hills keep things green.

Two festivals in Ribadavia are worth timing a visit around. The **Festa do Ribeiro** (late April to early May) celebrates the local wines with tastings, music, and food in the old town. The **Festa da Istoria** on 1 September is more unusual — townspeople dress in medieval costume and parade through the streets re-enacting scenes from Ribadavia's past. The tradition dates back centuries, lapsed in 1868, and was revived in recent decades. It's now one of the most distinctive cultural events in the province of Ourense.
