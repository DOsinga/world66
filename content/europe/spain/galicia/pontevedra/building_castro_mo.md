A small 18th-century baroque palace in the old town, now part of the Pontevedra Museum complex. The building dates to 1760 and was converted into museum space by Castelao and Sánchez Cantón. A balcony walkway connects it to the neighboring Fernández López building, and a stairway leads through to the García Flórez palace -- together the three buildings form an interconnected museum quarter worth wandering through.
