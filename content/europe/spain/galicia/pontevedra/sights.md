Pontevedra has its own Ria and therefore good sights abound. However one stands out, the old village of Combarro, right by the Ria on the way to O Grove. If you go by bus, you will need to ask where to get off as there is no sign of the beauty of the village nearby. There are roads turning into tracks which actually end in the Ria and numerous horreos, stone structures resembling coffins which are actually for storing grain, on legs to keep out rats and with crosses on top to keep out evil spirits. On postcards sold there the English translation is 'lurkng places.'
