Building Castro Monteagudo, first site for the Museum. Small baroque palace, with pleasant dimensions and ornament, whose construction was promoted in 1760 by Castro Monteagudo. Castelao and Sánchez Cantón contributed to its adaptation as a Museum and Argenti and Fernández Cochón conducted the enlargements.
