---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Pontevedra's old town is compact enough to cover on foot in a day, and the sights are woven into the fabric of the streets rather than clustered in a museum quarter. Beyond the city, the village of Combarro on the Ría de Pontevedra is one of the most atmospheric spots in Galicia — a cluster of hórreos and stone houses right at the water's edge, easily reached by bus.
