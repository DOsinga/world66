Leña Square, its setting builds up one of the most characteristic images of the town. "Leña" (firewood) used to be sold at this square to light up the old stoves. The three buildings of the Provincial Museum -Fernández López, Castro Monteagudo and García Flórez's Pazo- are situated on the square's right side.
