---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Pontevedra's old town is small enough to walk in an afternoon, but dense enough to reward a longer stay. The car-free centre — which the city council has maintained since the 1990s — makes it genuinely pleasant to wander. The main sights cluster within a few blocks.

The **Museum of Pontevedra** is spread across four historic buildings on Leña Square and is one of the better regional museums in Galicia. The **Santa María Basilica** and its Plateresque façade is the architectural highlight. The **Ruins of Santo Domingo** convent, maintained by the museum, contain the finest Gothic remains in the province.
