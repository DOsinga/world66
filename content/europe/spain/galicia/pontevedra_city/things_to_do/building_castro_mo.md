Building Castro Monteagudo is the original home of the Pontevedra Museum. A small baroque palace promoted in 1760, it was adapted as a museum through work by Castelao and Sánchez Cantón. A balconade connects it to the Fernández López building, with a stairway leading through to the García Flórez palace.
