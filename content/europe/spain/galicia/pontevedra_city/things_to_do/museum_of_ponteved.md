The Pontevedra Museum is spread across four historic buildings around Leña Square. The collection covers Galician art and archaeology from Roman times to the 20th century — prehistoric goldwork, medieval sculpture, Baroque religious art, and an important holdings of Castelao's drawings. The Spanish Painting Gallery traces work from Goya through the Romantics, Sorolla, the Basque painters, and the avant-gardes.

Open Tuesday–Saturday 10:00–13:30 and 16:30–20:00 (winter); 10:00–14:15 and 17:00–20:45 (summer). Sundays and holidays 11:00–13:00. Closed Monday.
