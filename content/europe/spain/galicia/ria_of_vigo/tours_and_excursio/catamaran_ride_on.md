A mix of sea and land, we start the day off by crossing over the bay of Vigo by catamaran ferry to the quaint village of Cangas. This village is on the Peninsula of O Morrazo, which we will dedicated the day to exploring.

At the very tip of the Peninsula we will walk around the Cabo Home (Man's Cape) and visit golden beach and 3 different lighthouses offering views of the area from different angles. One of the Lighthouse even dates back to the before the 9th Century used by the Celtic tribes of the region.

In the close by village of Donon we will enjoy lunch in a local restaurant offering excellent traditional cuisine with specialties of the area and excellent wines.

To capture the beauty of the bay of Vigo we will cross over the Rande Bridge back to Vigo where from you can enjoy views of Vigo, Cangas and the bay.
