---
title: "Bars and Cafes"
type: section
---

Santiago de Compostela is a university city as well as a pilgrimage destination, which means its nightlife is more active than you might expect. The Rúa do Franco and the streets around the old quarter are the main bar zone. Expect live galician bagpipe music at some bars, especially around festival time. The action generally starts late — bars fill up after 10pm, and clubs after midnight.
