A fixture of Santiago's nightlife, tucked into a stone building on Praza de Feixóo in the old town. During the week it functions as a relaxed bar with good music; Thursday nights turn into proper club nights. Sundays bring a popular chill-out session from 6pm to midnight -- a fine way to wind down after a weekend of exploring the city. The crowd skews local, which is refreshing in a town that can feel overrun with pilgrims.
