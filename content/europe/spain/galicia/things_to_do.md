---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Galicia's best sights are spread across a wild landscape — prehistoric monuments, Celtic hill-forts, Roman walls, and natural parks along a spectacular Atlantic coast. Most require a car to reach, but each one is worth the effort. The Corrubedo dunes, the Barbanza coast, and the Castro de Baroña peninsula are among the most visually striking spots in northwestern Spain.
