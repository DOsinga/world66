A well-preserved megalithic burial chamber in the Barbanza peninsula, dating back around 6,000 years. The capstone rests cleanly on its two upright slabs, making it one of the most photogenic dolmens in Galicia. It stands in open countryside near Ribeira, easy to reach and freely accessible. A short walk from the car park through low scrubland.
