A multi-day food and wine tour based in the Rías Baixas, the Albariño heartland. Starting in Santiago de Compostela, the route follows the Atlantic coast through small fishing villages and into the O Salnés valley, visiting wineries inside traditional Galician pazos (manor houses). Meals range from Michelin-starred restaurants in Santiago to dockside taverns serving just-caught spider crab in wicker baskets. Run by EuroAdventures, based in Vigo.
