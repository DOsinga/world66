A private chauffeur-guided day tour from Santiago de Compostela into the Rías Baixas, combining a stop at the Castro de Baroña Celtic hill-fort with a gourmet seafood lunch overlooking the sea and a winery visit in the O Salnés valley. One of the cleaner ways to combine Galicia's two main draws — its prehistoric sites and its wine — in a single day.
