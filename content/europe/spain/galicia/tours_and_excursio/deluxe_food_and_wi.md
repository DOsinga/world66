A multi-day tour covering all four of Galicia's wine regions -- Rias Baixas (Albarino), Ribeiro, Ribeira Sacra (Mencia), and Valdeorras (Godello) -- plus a detour into El Bierzo. The itinerary mixes winery visits with cultural stops: Santiago de Compostela's old town, a catamaran trip through the Sil canyon, and the Roman gold mines at Las Medulas. Accommodation is in paradors and rural pazos throughout, so this is not a budget option, but the access to small producers and private tastings makes it worthwhile if you're serious about Galician wine.
