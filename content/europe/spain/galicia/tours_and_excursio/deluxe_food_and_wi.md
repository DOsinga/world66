A multi-day tour covering all four of Galicia's wine regions — Rías Baixas (Albariño), Ribeiro, Ribeira Sacra (Mencía), and Valdeorras (Godello) — plus a detour into El Bierzo. The itinerary mixes winery visits with cultural stops: Santiago de Compostela's old town, a catamaran trip through the Sil canyon, and the Roman gold mines at Las Médulas. Accommodation in paradors and rural pazos throughout.
