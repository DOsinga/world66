Galicia
is often considered the fogotten region of Spain, but steeped in history
with
dramatic countryside, historic towns and villages, and some of the best
food
and wine to be had this is a region of Spain that needs to be explored.
Not
being a traditional tourist destination many of the best points of
intrest,
and experiences are not well advertised with many located of the beaten
track.

If you are looking to experience
true Galicia then Experience
Galicia will help you go
deep into this region of Spain
that the Spanish call "Green Spain". Having been living and farming
in the local area for generations our family have come to know the best
places
to eat, to visit, and to relax, predominately where the Spanish like to
frequent with many of our activities far from the traditional tourist
routes.
All we ask is you bring a desire for adventure and are happy to immerse
yourself in the wonderful Galician culture.

A family business we are small enough max group[ size 4 to offer unique
tours
that will ensure our guests get to see, taste and feel what Galicia has
to
offer with the emphasis on getting amongst the experiences rather than
watching
them from afar. Our tours are more active than traditional tours note
these can
be tailored to your ability, which ensures you see and experience more.
Accommodation
is in a traditional Casa Rural with swimming pool located in the heart
of the
Riberia Sacra.

To get a real taste of Galicia
sometimes you also have to be spontaneous and at Experience Galicia
we have
taken advantage of our small size and our ability to be flexible to
offer
trips to local one off events. So as well as the set itinerary for each
tour you
will also be invited to local fiestas and special events which may be on
during your stay. These range from town and village fiestas, usually in
the evening after supper, carnivals, and local farmer markets where you
can lunch with the locals.

Each tour is designed to offer a slightly different experience, however
if you
wish for us to create a tailor made tour or adjust any of our tours to
suite
your needs (i.e duration / intensity level of treks, number of days etc)
please
do not hesitate in contacting us.
