A family-run tour operation based in the Ribeira Sacra, offering small-group tours (max four people) that go well off the tourist trail. Activities include hiking, local fiestas, village markets, and farm visits. The owners have lived in the area for years and know where locals actually eat and gather. Accommodation is in a rural casa with a pool in the Ribeira Sacra valley. Good option for an active, immersive week in Galicia.
