A cooking-focused tour based in Aldán, a small fishing village in the Rías Baixas near Cangas. Guests cook traditional Galician coastal dishes alongside a local woman who runs the rural manor house where the tour is based. Between sessions there are boat trips to the Cíes or Ons islands, winery visits, and meals at restaurants from creative seafood kitchens to no-frills dockside taverns. Ends with a day in Santiago de Compostela.
