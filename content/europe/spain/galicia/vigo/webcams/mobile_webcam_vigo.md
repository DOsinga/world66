Webcam with 180º movement sited on the flat roof of the Hotel Bahía de Vigo.
It provides images of the ria, the maritime station, the cruiser's dock, the yachting port, the Mount A Guía, the avenue Montero Ríos and, in the background, the town of Moaña.
The image updates every two seconds.
