---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Vigo rewards exploration. The old Berbés fishing quarter, the hilltop Castro fortress with its sweeping views over the ría, and the fine-arts collection at the Quiñones de León Museum give the city genuine depth. The Atlantic light and the constant presence of the sea — ferries, fishing boats, the distant Cíes Islands — tie it all together.
