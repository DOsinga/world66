[Aranjuez](/europe/spain/centralspain/aranjuez), this majestic village and Royal Site in the south of Madrid has one of the most beautiful palaces of Spain: *El Palacio de Aranjuez*, sorrounded by its numerous gardens like *El Jardin del Principe* and *El Jardin del Palterre*. It's a must!
