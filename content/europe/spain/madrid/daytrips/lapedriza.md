---
title: "La Pedriza"
type: poi
---

This is a wonderful spot where you can enjoy original grantie sculptures. The way to reach this part of the mountain is by renting a car of taking the bus No 724 (every 30 min.) from the bus station in Plaza de Castilla. If you take the bus, you must get out in Manzanares el Real, a beautiful village with a famous castle, and then you must follow up the Manzanares river for about 3-4 km. You can also have a lunch and sleep in Giner de los Rios or in the nice and small village of Manzanares el Real.
