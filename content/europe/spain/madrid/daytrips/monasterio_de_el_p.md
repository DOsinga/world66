At roughly 62 miles north of Madrid, Monasterio de El Paular & Lozoya Valley makes a perfect day or overnight trip if you’re interested in spending time in a serene atmosphere and visiting unique sights. Most visitors recommend driving here, as the road trip is as scenic as the destination. The drive includes a climb towards a 6,100-foot mountain pass, views of snowcapped mountains, churches, monasteries, pine trees, brooks, and more. Lozoya Valley features lush landscapes, campgrounds, walking paths, and romantic picnic areas. If you prefer not to camp, a great hotel to rest for the night is the mountain hotel, Hotel Santa Maria de El Paular (www.hotelsantamariapaular.com), which features cozy rooms and several dining options. For additional information about this Madrid day trip, contact the Centro de Educacion Ambiental Puente del Perdo between the hours of 10 a.m.-6:00 p.m., 91-869-1757.
