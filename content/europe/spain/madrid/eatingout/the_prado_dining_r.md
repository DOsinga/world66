Classified as French, Asian, and Exotic Spanish, El Cenador del Prado attracts a unique crowd of foodies in search of innovative cuisine. One of the most popular dishes offered at this elaborate dining venue, complete with a plant-filled conservatory, is sliced potatoes and clams covered in a garlic and parsley sauce. The desserts are worthy of a taste as well.
