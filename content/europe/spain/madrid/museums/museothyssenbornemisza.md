Don't think that the [Prado](/europe/spain/madrid/museums/thepradomuseum) is the only art museum in Madrid. The
Thyssen is just as great, I think. They have a really great collection
of paintings from the Middle Ages.

The Museum houses the largest and best collection of pictures collected
by Baron Hans Thyssen-Bornemisza from 13th century to the present days.
Open from Tuesday to Sunday:10-19h. Mondays closed.
http://www.museothyssen.org/indexi.htm
