---
title: "The Corridor Houses"
type: poi
---

These houses are the most characteristic examples of accommodation of Madrilenian worlkers in the 18th century. Meson de Paredes 79 and La Corrala at 12 Tribulete Str. are the most famous and the latter has been declared a National Monument. The buildings are set around a central patio, each containing one toilet per floor, one kitchen and two bedrooms.
