A small, specialist museum housed in the Grassy building on Gran Vía, displaying a private collection of antique clocks and watches spanning several centuries. The collection includes ornate pocket watches, early wristwatches, and decorative table clocks. A curiosity rather than a must-see, but worth a quick look if you are passing.
