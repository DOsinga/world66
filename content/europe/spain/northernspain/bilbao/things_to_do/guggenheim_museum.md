Frank Gehry's titanium-clad masterpiece on the Nervión waterfront is one of the most celebrated buildings of the twentieth century. The swirling forms clad in thousands of precisely cut titanium panels house a world-class collection of modern and contemporary art, including works by Kandinsky, Picasso, Koons, and Pollock. Jeff Koons' giant floral sculpture *Puppy* guards the entrance. Allow two to three hours minimum. Open Tuesday to Sunday, 10am–8pm.
