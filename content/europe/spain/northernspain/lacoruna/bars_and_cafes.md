---
title: "Bars and Cafes"
type: section
---

The bar scene in La Coruña is concentrated in two areas: the old town, particularly around the Calle Franja and its side streets, where dozens of tapas bars pour Galician wine and serve pintxos at standing height; and the Ensanche, where the larger cafes and late-night bars cluster. Most places serve food well into the evening — the Spanish rhythm of eating late applies here too.
