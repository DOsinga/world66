---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

La Coruña's sights are spread across a compact peninsula, making them easy to cover on foot. The Roman lighthouse, the old town churches, and the seafront promenade are the core, but it is worth wandering the *galerías*-lined streets of the Ensanche too — the city's 19th-century expansion has an architectural coherence that rewards slow exploration.
