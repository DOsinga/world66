---
title: "Muros"
type: location
---

Muros is situated on a particularly unspoilt ria named after it and [Noia](/europe/spain/northernspain/noia). The bus ride from Santiago de Compostela is delightful, first through fine galician countryside and then, from Noia, along the coast of the Ria de Noia y Muros.

The town itself has some fine building but it is doubtful whether you will want more than a day trip.
