On this half day privately guided tour visit Oviedo Cathedral and Historic Quarter. The former Ovetum has been closely linked to the Asturian monarchy since its foundation (8th century), and even became the capital of the Kingdom. This fact has left an unquestionable medieval flavour in the historic quarter, which in modern times has been given a carefully-considered urban layout which is easy to move around. An exceptional starting point to approach examples of pre-Romanesque art with World Heritage status. Oviedo Cathedral, although Gothic in origin, also offers the visitor a unique opportunity to view an array of both Baroque and renaissance art.
