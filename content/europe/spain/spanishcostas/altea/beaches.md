---
title: "Beaches"
type: section
---

Altea's main beach is pebble rather than sand, which keeps the crowds thinner than the sandy resort beaches nearby. It runs in a long arc around the bay below the old town, backed by a seafood restaurant strip and a promenade.

For sand, head north to the beaches around [Calpe](/europe/spain/spanishcostas/calpe) or south toward [Benidorm](/europe/spain/spanishcostas/benidorm).
