Eating out in Benalmadena is always good. With many nice restaurants such as 'Pachinos' in the marina to the 'Rose and crown' for english food and Kareoke. Whatever it is you want you will find it in Benalmadena!

### [Tex Mex](/europe/spain/spanishcostas/benalmadena/eating_out/tex_mex)

A very nice restaurant near the marina. does lovely food but you will have to get there early or book (im not sure if you can book or not) because it is very popular and gets filled up quickly being such a small restaurant

type: Mexican

### [Pachinos](/europe/spain/spanishcostas/benalmadena/eating_out/pachinos)

This is a lovely restaurant in the heart of the Puerto Marina in Benalmadena, it does good italian food and lovely - cook-your-own-beef-on-a-cooking-brick.

type: Italian
