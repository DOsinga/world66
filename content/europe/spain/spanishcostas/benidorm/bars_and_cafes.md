---
title: "Bars and Cafes"
type: section
---

Benidorm's nightlife is famously relentless. The English-speaking strip along Calle Gerona and the surrounding streets runs well past midnight most nights in summer, with bars, pubs, and clubs packed shoulder to shoulder. It is not subtle, but it is lively. For something calmer, the old town has a handful of tapas bars and wine spots that attract a more local crowd.
