**Museo de la Huerta.**

A museum of anthropology based on the people of the Huerta. It can be found on the turn off to Alcantarilla on the Carretera de Andalucía. Phone. 968.80.03.40. 

tel: 968.80.03.40

**Museo de Ramón Gaya.**

This Museum is located in the Casa Pelarea in the Plaza de Santa Catalina. Phone. 968.22.10.99. 

address: Plaza de Santa Catalina

tel: 968.22.10.99

**Museo Salzillo**

The name of Francisco Salzillo is indisputably linked to Murcia. Although his father Nicolás was originally from Naples Franciso was born in the region in 1707 and died in 1738. **Museo Arqueológico.**

This museum houses prehistoric objects, Spanish-Arab ceramics from the 12th to the 14th century and Spanish ceramics from the 17th and 18th century.  

address: Alfonso X el Sabio, 7. Casa de la Cultura

tel: 968.23.46.02

**Museo de Bellas Artes**

Among the artists represented in this Museum of Fine Arts are: Ribera, Ribalta, Bassano, Esquivel, Madrazo, Sorolla, Romero de Torres and Picasso. It is located at Obispo Frutos, 12. Phone. 968.23.93.46. 

address: Obispo Frutos, 12

tel: 968.23.93.46.

**Museo de la Catedral.**

Among the many objects on display are chalices, monstrances and crowns of the Virgin de la Fuensanta. It is located at Calle Salzillo, 2. Phone. 968.21.63.44. 

address: Calle Salzillo, 2

tel: 968.21.63.44.
