Valencia's central market is a fine modernist building. In April 2005 it was undergoing restoration and, although it was open for business, it wasn't possible to admire its supposedly brilliant ceiling and dome.

It's one of the largest market buildings in Europe.

The so-called Plaza Redonda [Round Square! It's round OK but nothing square about it] has numerous habberdashery stalls.
