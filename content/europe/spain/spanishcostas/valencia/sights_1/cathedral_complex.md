The Cathedral complex comprises the Cathedral itself, its museum and the Miguelete bell-tower. The Cathedral has in a side-chapel what it claims to be the Holy Grail, the cup used at the Last Supper.

Apparently the claim satisfies the Vatican but one presumes Dan Brown might be less enthusiastic!
