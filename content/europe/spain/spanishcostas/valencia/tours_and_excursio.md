---
title: "Tours and Excursions"
type: section
---

There's a tourist bus trip around Valencia if you're desperately
pressed for time. Otherwise many sights are within walking distance of
each other and walking in Valencia is a delight. For places a bit
farther out the ordinary service buses are excellent with a flat rate
fare of €1.

However the Tourist Bus to the Albufera at €12, including a boat
trip on the lagoon a few miles south of the city centre, is probably
worth the money as the local buses are not helpfully timed if you are
on a short break.

Or you bike there. You can rent decent quality bikes for 12
euros, and it's a really good day trip - a couple of hours from the
centre each way, and the route is straightforward.

Numerous exotic birds are said to breed here but at least you will see **masses **of herons and egrets.
