Galicia, Northwestern Spain has always been known by National Spanish travelers as a destination to eat and drink an abundance of fresh Seafood and Fine Wines. Well, now Galicia's fame is becoming internationally known, thanks to high wine ratings in popular wine magazines like Wine Spectator and many others.

Albariño white wines of the Denomination of Origin Rias Baixas have been making the most impact internationally, but as well lies hidden regions in this land like the D.O. Ribeira Sacra on the banks of the River Sil and Miño with spectacular young red wines made of the Mencia grape variety.

Let us show you around these and other picturesque wine regions of Galicia, Northwestern Spain. Either traveling on your own or as a group, we can arrange an ideal food and wine experience just for you! If you would like to take a grand tour of the most important wine regions then try our private group tour... The Fine Wines, Food and Celtic Culture of Galicia . If you are able to get a group of 10 people or more together we will offer you a discounted rate in addition to a FREE SEAT on the tour! If you are traveling in a smaller party of 2 to 6 people this tour can also be done as a Chauffeur Driven or Self-Drive Tour .

We also have a very special small group tour planned for this July during the Festival of St. James that will be personally led by Sean O'Rourke, founder of EuroAdventures. This tour is a mix of walking and wine touring, starting off with walking some parts of the Camino de Santiago trail and then following down the Rias Baixas coast, the land of Albariño wines where you will visit wineries, fishing villages, Celtic Hill-forts, etc...

Just interested in visiting one region in depth? Then our... Savoring Albariño wines and Seafood on the Celtic Coast of Spain would be perfect for you! This tour as well can be done as a Chauffeur Driven or Self-Drive Tour .

For those of you who are just passing though and only have a day or two then get a taste of what Galicia has to offer by trying our... Albariño Wines and Celtic Sites in the Salnes Valley, Rias Baixas or O Rosal Valley a land of Albariño wines alongside the River Miño or Ribeira Sacra river valley and Ribeiro wine country and Soutomaior wine country, it's Castle and Live Celtic Music/Dinner in a Tavern .

Apart from all the suggested tours above we would be glad and customize any type of tour for you.
