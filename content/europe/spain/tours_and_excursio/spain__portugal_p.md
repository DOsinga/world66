While at shore in Spain and Portugal why not travel in style at your own pace with a private VIP guide. For small groups or couples it is a perfect option to explore various important port cities like... Barcelona, Lisbon, Vigo, Malaga, Cadiz, Porto, etc... Even venture off from the port city and visit surrounding areas.

For example from the port of Barcelona or Cadiz if you are wine lovers try an excursion to savor the Cava Wines of Catalonia or the Sherry of Jerez de la Frontera, even the Port wine of Porto and to top it off from Vigo you could try touring the undiscovered Albariño wine country, the list goes on.... Art lovers could try in Barcelona visiting the Picasso and Miró Museums with an expert art guide, not forgetting as well to mention the famous Guggenhiem Museum. Stroll through historic quarters and observe the architecture of monumental cities like Lisbon, Sevilla, Granada or Santiago de Compostela.

Before our after your cruise you could spend some time ashore visiting more in-depth certain areas, for example from the Port of Lisbon you could take a private driver/guided tour to visit The Douro Valley and Porto, as well in Barcelona your could try a 3 day cooking class to learn the secrets of Catalan cuisine. These are only a couple suggestions of many specialty tour itineraries we could offer you.

We have a wide selection of private shore excursions and extended specialty tours available from every port, if you can't find one that fits into your schedule or preferences let us know and we would be glad to quote for a customized tour just for you!
