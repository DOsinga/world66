Bjärka-Säby is a major manor south of Linköping in the province Östergötland. It was founded during the 14th century and is owned by the family Ekman since late 19th century. The castle from 1632 is now owned by the Pentacoste church. A new manor house was erected in the 1790s in classicistic style.
Bjärka-Säby is famous for its beautiful cultural landscape with wide, open grazing lands with scattered oaks and other broad-leaved trees. There are various arrangements for tourists.
