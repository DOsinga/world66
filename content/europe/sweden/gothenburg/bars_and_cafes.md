---
title: "Bars and Cafes"
type: section
---

Gothenburg claims more clubs per square metre than any other Swedish city, and the Kungsportsavenyn -- locals call it "the Avenyn" -- is where most of the action concentrates. Bars, pubs, and nightclubs line the avenue from the old town to Gotaplatsen, and you can go bar-hopping for hours without repeating yourself.

**Brasserie Ferdinand**

One of the most popular bars among locals, in a basement on Drottninggatan with soft lighting and an atmosphere unlike anywhere else in the city. More than 200 brands of beer on offer, plus a menu of burgers, sandwiches, and meatballs. Live music on weekends.

**7:ans**

An old bar that has been on Kungstorget 7 for over a hundred years. Atmospheric and unpretentious -- a great place for a quiet beer or glass of wine.

**Nefertiti**

One of Gothenburg's trendiest clubs, in a cool basement space at Hvitfeldtsplatsen 6. Primarily a jazz club, but also plays salsa, blues, hip hop, and soul.

**Heaven 23**

On the 23rd floor of the Hotel Gothia Towers, this bar and restaurant has sweeping views over the city. Come for a cocktail even if you skip the food.

**The Excet**

One of the largest clubs in the city, at Vasagatan 52 just off the Avenyn. Three floors, two dance floors, and a roof terrace.
