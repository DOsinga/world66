**Gothenburg, the Perfect Place to go Bar Hopping**

The **Gothenburg Bars and Cafes** keep the doors open all day long. This Swedish city is the second largest in the country, and while it has a calm and relaxed atmosphere during the day, it turns into a party hub at night. You can take a break from your sightseeing and retire into the warm bars or sit on the street side patio and enjoy some beer. Gothenburg is the place to be if you wish to have an exciting night with friends. Read on to find out about one bar that should not be missed at any cost.

**Brasserie Ferdinand**

The Brasserie Ferdinand is one of the most popular bars among the locals. It is located at the center of Gothenburg, making it accessible and easy to spot. The street where this bar is located is called Drottninggatan. Get ready for a unique experience, as the interior is unlike what you will find in the rest of the city. The bar is run in a basement and has soft lighting.
**
The Beer**

Trust the folks at Brasserie Ferdinand to treat you with a mind-boggling number of beers. The bar has more than 200 brands of beer stocked up. The tap beer choices will give you a natural high. Also there are several local, American and Continental dishes on the menu. You can order for hamburgers, sandwiches, meatballs, and fries. During the night, the bar is the most romantic place in the whole of Gothenburg, with live musical performances during weekends.
