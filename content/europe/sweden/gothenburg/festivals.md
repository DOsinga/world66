---
title: "Festivals in Gothenburg"
type: section
---

**Gothenburg festivals** are among the reasons for you to visit Gothenburg, Sweden. The city has fun and exciting festivals that will truly make your stay a memorable one. The largest and the most well-known festivals in Gothenburg happen during summer. The passion of the Gothenburg people in arts, like music and dance, can be reflected through their different festivals.

**Hammarkullekarnevalen**

Hammarkullekarnevalen is a well-known Swedish carnival in Gothenburg. This celebration lasts for three days and is held in the late days of May or early days of June. During this festival, a colorful procession takes place. There are also activities like salsa dancing and Caribbean-theme music that will bring you fun and excitement.

**Gothenburg Music Festival **

Gothenburg Music Festival happens at the end of June. In celebrating this event, there are bands and orchestras that march along Avenyn and Kungsportsplatsen.

**Gothenburg Dance and Theatre Festival**

Gothenburg Dance and Theatre Festival is one of the many festivals in Gothenburg that showcases the theatrical talents of their people. It happens over the whole month of August, where the different theatrical companies come to the venue and perform on stages all around Gothenburg. They deliver excellent performances to attract the large number of spectators.

**Lucia Festival**

Lucia Festival, popularly known as Luciadagen in Gothenburg, is among the unique festivals that they celebrate. Lucia festival is held annually every 13th day of December. In this event, there is a choir parade that takes place, and a young girl (Lucia) who is wearing a crown of candles leads the parade.
