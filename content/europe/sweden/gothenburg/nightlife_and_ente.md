---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment in Gothenburg"
type: section
---

**Gothenburg nightlife** is one of the best in Scandinavia. It is said that there are more clubs in Gothenburg per a square meter of the city than in any other city in Sweden. Many of the clubs are located on Gothenburg&rsquo;s main shopping street, the Kungsportsavenyn, or the &ldquo;Avenyn&rdquo;.

&ldquo;The Avenue&rdquo; is where locals and tourists gather in the evening to have a drink in the bars and pubs or go partying in the nightclubs. You can also start the evening with a meal in one of the restaurants, or sit in a trendy caf&eacute; and watch the world go by while planning your evening out.

**Bars and pubs**

There are dozens of bars on and around the Avenyn for having a relaxed drink after a day out shopping, or for getting ready for a night out partying. One of the most unique is the 7:ans, an old bar that has been in its spot on Kungstorget 7 for over a hundred years. This atmospheric pub is a great place to enjoy a beer or a glass of wine.

Some other good bars in the neighborhood are Bishop&rsquo;s Arms on Avenyn 36 and Lounge G&ouml;teborg at Avenyn 5, but there are so many drinking places here to choose from that you can go bar-hopping for the whole night and never get bored.

**Nightclubs in Gothenburg**

The Excet nightclub is one of the largest in the city. With its three different floors, two dance floors and its roof terrace, this club in Vasagatan 52 (just off Kungsportsavenyn) is one of the best in Gothenburg and always popular with locals and visitors.

If you like jazz, you couldn&rsquo;t do better than visit the Nefertiti. This jazz club is one of Gothenburg&rsquo;s trendiest clubs. As well as jazz the club plays salsa, blues, hip hop, soul and other music, and it can be found in a cool basement space at Hvitfeldtsplatsen 6.

The Deep Nattklubb at Kungsportsavenyen 15 and the Glow Night Club at Kungsportsavenyn 8, or the ParkLane at Kungsportsavenyn 38, are some other good places to dance the night away.

For a quieter night out, Heaven 23 is literally located on the 23rd floor of one of Gothenburg&rsquo;s tallest buildings, the Hotel Gothia Towers. This is a bar and a restaurant with amazing views over the city. You can have a great dinner in the restaurant or just come here for a cocktail in the stylish bar while enjoying the views.
