---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

Summertime there is a boat leaving Nynäshamn south of Stockhom in the morning, arriving at Gotska Sandön after 3 hours. The boat then continues to Fårösund on Gotland and sails back towards Gotska Sandön in the afternoon. After 2 hours it arrives at the island and then continues to Nynäshamn in the evening. There is no harbour on Gotska Sandön. The boat runs straight into the sandy shore, something that is not possible during heavy winds. It is therefore not uncommon that visitors on the island have to wait an extra day before they can return home; thus spare food should be brought. It should also be noted that a ship constructed for such conditions will not be very stable in rough sea; most people become seasick! It is also not possible to predict on which side of the island the boat will appear. Under certain weather conditions the passengers will have to walk up to 9 km to or from the boat.
