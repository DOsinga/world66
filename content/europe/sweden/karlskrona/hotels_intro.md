Named among UNESCO World Heritage Site, Karlskrona, Sweden is the perfect destination for all nature lovers, and you can take in the amazing scenery as you stay in one of the many **Karlskrona Hotels**. Come visit many of the islands of this port town and bask in the sun on any of its pristine beaches. Take tours of the many museums found here or simply amble along its picturesque streets. Many excellent hotels and resorts here promise a memorable vacation, one that you will never forget.

**Clarion Collection Hotel Carlscrona**

Situated right off the Baltic Sea and right in the heart of Karlskrona, this magnificent hotel offers 80 well furnished and uniquely decorated rooms. The world famous Blekinge Archipelago Harbor is right next to this hotel. Your stay at this hotel is all inclusive of the breakfast buffet and even dinner. The rooms on the ground floor come with a common patio, where you can relax during the day and soak in the smell of the ocean as you enjoy the scenery. The train station, center of the city and the Marine Museum are at walking distance from the hotel. With rooms starting from just over $100, it is considered by visitors as one of the best karlskrona Hotels to stay in.

**First Hotel Ja**

A bit on the expensive side, at $150 for a standard room, this hotel however provides some of the best service and amenities in Karlskrona. Situated inside a shopping complex, this four star** **hotel boasts 80 rooms, each with Wi-Fi Internet service and Laundry service 24/7. The physically impaired and working women can avail rooms specially designed for them. Guests can relax in their cozy rooms or go shopping at the mall. The hotel even has its own restaurant, bar and sauna. The reception can include you and your family in many of the activities and tours that it arranges on a daily basis, such as a tour of the archipelago, golf circuit, etc.

**Hotel Conrad**

Placed in a quite part of town close to the center of Kalskrona, this great family hotel is perfect for all those who just want to spend some quality time alone with their families. You can take a stroll down the beautiful serene streets or take a boat out to any of the secluded islands of the archipelago. The hotel includes free continental breakfast for its guests and free midday snacks and tea as well. It provides 58 rooms spread out over three buildings, each well furnished and decorated in a homely atmosphere. All the rooms have 24-hour Internet service and great room service too. With rooms starting at $114, this well-priced and well-located Karlskrona hotel is just right for letting go of your worries and anxieties.
