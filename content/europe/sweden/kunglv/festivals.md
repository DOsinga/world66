### [Kongahälla Medeltidsdagar](/europe/sweden/kunglv/festivals/kongahlla_medelt)

Bring your family to this very special occasion, preferably dressed in medievil clothing. However you are just as welcome "as you are" just watching the spectacular atmosphere. On Friday there is a real wedding, won by ordinary cityzens, in public. Look into to the different stalls of professional craftsmans, no crap is allowed here. Taste food as it was cocked long time ago but with todays excellent raw material. Watch the skillful knights in the tournament or just pop in for a beer. No credit cards are accepted. I can garantee that you will have fun and find something that [more..](/europe/sweden/kunglv/festivals/kongahlla_medelt)
