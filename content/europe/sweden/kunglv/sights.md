### [Classic cars meeting](/europe/sweden/kunglv/sights/gamla_torget)

Every Thursday from middle of May to early September there is a free event of watching classic cars, 30 years and older, at Gamla Torget. Everyone that has a classic car is welcome to park and let people admire it between 17.00 and 21.00. When the weather is nice it's usually crowded and when it's raining you might end up lonley, as an expectator with no cars to watch. If that happens the restaurant Gamla Torget 4 is recommendable. Hopefully you will enjoy lots of extraordinay cars in excellent shape on Thursday evenings. Enjoy and even bring your own [more..](/europe/sweden/kunglv/sights/gamla_torget)

### [Nytorget](/europe/sweden/kunglv/sights/nytorget)

There are a couple of squares in Kungälv. Gamla Torget=Old Square and Nytorget=New Sguare. At Nytorget you will find the staty of the three Kings meeting. Those were the former Kings of Sweden, Norway and Danmark. See the History section.
