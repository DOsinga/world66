Travel Guide

Linköping is an old town in south central Sweden, situated in a rich agriculture area in the province Östergötland. It is a diocesan capital and seat of the county administration and it is Sweden’s 7th largest city. It has one of the bigger universities in the country. The aeroplane construction of the SAAB group (SAAB Aerostructures and SAAB Aerosystems) is based in Linköping.
The cathedral is an impressive building and there is also a medieval castle. Otherwise, most of the older buildings have been moved and re-erected as a museum town. Among the museums the Air Force museum is also worth mentioning.
