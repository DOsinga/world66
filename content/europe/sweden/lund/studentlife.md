title: "Student Life"
type: location
---

Both student nations and student unions pride themselves on a varied and affordable choice of activities — concerts, dances, dinners, etc.  The city of Lund (and the municipality) also has a great deal to offer outside the sphere of students. There are about 400 organizations of varying kinds, and the sights of Lund are well worth a visit, even if you are not a tourist. Lund offers a great selection of pubs, restaurants, cafés, theatres and concerts. There is no limit to the possibilities — do try out the entertainments outside the world of the students!  Whether you are interested in sports, theatre, dance, the nature or the environment or something completely different, there is probably an organization or association for you to find in Lund.
