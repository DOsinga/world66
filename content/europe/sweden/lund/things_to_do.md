title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Lund punches well above its size when it comes to culture and history. The city's 1,000-year-old cathedral anchors a walkable medieval core, while Lund University has seeded the city with museums covering everything from ancient antiquities to medieval archaeology. The Botanical Gardens offer a green retreat, and the open-air Kulturen museum brings Scanian folk history vividly to life.
