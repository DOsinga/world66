An unusual and rewarding museum at Finngatan 2 devoted to preparatory sketches, maquettes, and models for public art and monuments across Scandinavia and beyond. The collection offers an intimate look at the creative process behind public sculpture — showing rough drawings and scale models alongside the finished commissions they gave rise to.
