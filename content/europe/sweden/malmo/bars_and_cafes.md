---
title: "Bars and Cafes"
type: section
---

Malmo has a lively bar and cafe scene that punches above its weight. Lilla Torg is the obvious starting point, its cobbled square ringed with outdoor terraces that fill up the moment the sun appears. Beyond the square you will find craft beer bars, cosy wine spots, and a handful of late-night clubs, with the proximity to Copenhagen keeping the music and cocktail culture sharp.
