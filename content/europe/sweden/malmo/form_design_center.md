Housed in a cluster of restored 16th-century grain warehouses on Lilla Torg, the Form/Design Center hosts rotating exhibitions on Scandinavian architecture, industrial design, and crafts. Admission is free and the exhibitions change regularly, making it worth a stop even on a short visit. The courtyard cafe is a pleasant spot in summer.
