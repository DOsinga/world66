It is easy to find ones favourite spot with the right kind of music, and Malmö also has the advantage of being close to Copenhagen and the continent with its influenses in music and art. Everything from nightclubs as the legendary Slagthuset, Kulturbolaget or Star bar, to special jazz- and musiclubs and the Konserthuset with Malmö Symphony Orchestra, the choice is your´s!
