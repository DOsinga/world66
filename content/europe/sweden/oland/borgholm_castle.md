The largest castle ruin in Scandinavia, Borgholm sits on a ridge above the town of Borgholm with views across the strait to the mainland. The original fortress dates to the 13th century; the current shell — roofless, blackened, vast — was burned in 1806. Walking through the gutted interiors and climbing the towers takes an hour or two. Music festivals are held in the courtyard in summer.
