title: Solliden Palace
type: poi

The Swedish royal family's summer home, a few kilometres south of Borgholm, is not open to the public but the surrounding park and gardens are. The grounds are beautiful — formal flowerbeds, woodland paths, a rose garden — and the building itself is visible from the garden walks. Worth combining with a visit to Borgholm Castle just up the road.
