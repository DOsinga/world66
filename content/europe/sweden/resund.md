The Öresund Region is comprised of the land surrounding the strait of Öresund, including the city of [Copenhagen](/europe/denmark/copenhagen) and the town of Helsingor on the Danish side and the towns of Malmö and Helsingborg, on the Swedish side. The two sides are linked by a bridge, Öresundsbron. 

Public transport is excellent in the area and there is lots to see and do. Trains run every half an hour and circle the whole region in both directions.
