Sarek National Park is loacted in Laponia / Lappland, in the north of Sweden. It is a high alpine park with arctic characteristics. Large areas are devoid of vegetation, consisting of boulders, snowfields and glaciers. No other Swedish area has so many majestic massifs and deep eroded valleys, so many peaked mountains and large glaciers
