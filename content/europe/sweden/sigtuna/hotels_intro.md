Given below are the details of some of the **Sigtuna hotels**. Sigtuna is a city situated in Stockholm County in Sweden. Sigtuna takes its name from an ancient royal estate. The city of Sigtuna holds a very vital position in Sweden&rsquo;s history. The city is just 10 km from the Arlanda airport. Established over 1,000 years ago, the city was one of the most significant cities of Sweden. Sigtuna was where Sweden&rsquo;s first coins were minted. Sigtuna&rsquo;s heritage has been hugely influenced by the Dominican Order. The monasteries and churches built by them still remain. With major waterways converging at Lake M&auml;laren, Sigtuna is easily accessible. To accommodate the tourist&rsquo;s inflow into the city, there are several hotels available in and around the city.

** Kristina Konferens & Hotell**

Hotel Kristina located in the heart of Sigtuna, is a carbon-free hotel and is just 15 minutes' drive from Arlanda Airport. The hotel offers the view of Lake M&auml;laren. You can choose from 64 rooms which are beautifully decorated with the finest fabrics from Dubai. You can book a room at Hotel Kristina starting from EUR 125 upwards. The rooms are well-equipped with modern amenities to make your stay there comfortable and a memorable one. You can enjoy your day at Sigtuna by exploring the city, its art galleries, museums, various wooden houses or walking on the lovely trails along the shores of Lake M&auml;laren.

**1909 Sigtuna Stads Hotell **

A unique small hotel situated in the quaint city of Sigtuna, 1909 Sigtuna Stads Hotell is gracefully reminiscent of a bygone era. This hotel has been able to preserve its olden-era charm, while being equipped with modern amenities for a modern day traveler. Offering you a choice of 26 rooms and access to bar, restaurant and dining area, this hotel is the only five-star hotel in the city of Sigtuna. This is the perfect place for you to relax and rejuvenate and forget the hustle and bustle of the city. If you enjoy fine dining, then do visit the restaurant and savor delicacies. To enjoy your holiday in the lap of luxury be prepared to spend, as rooms at Stads Hotell do not come cheap. You can book your rooms starting from EUR 208 upwards.

**Stora Branno**

Stora Branno, situated in Sigtuna, is best suited for you if you are a budget traveler. You can choose from a total of 101 rooms which are Nordic inspired and decently furnished. They are also equipped with most modern facilities. The in-house restaurant serves good food at reasonable prices. Guests can also use the spa and fitness centers available in the hotel. The hotel also offers you plenty of activities for your amusement and delight, like chocolate tasting, wine tasting, guided tours etc. Rooms can be obtained for prices starting from EUR 57 upwards.
