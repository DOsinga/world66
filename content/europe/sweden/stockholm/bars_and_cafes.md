**Café Ersta**

This café on Södermalm has reasonable prices and perhaps the best view in Stockholm from the outdoor tables.

It is also a restaurant with *lunch of the day* and a big sallad buffé.

**Getting there:** Bus to Ersta Sjukhus.

**Open:** Monday - Sunday 1 [more..](/europe/sweden/stockholm/bars_and_cafes/caf_ersta)

PC: http://www.ersta.se/ersta_for_alla/1_ersta_cafe-restaurang.htm

tel: 08-714 66 34

address: Erstagatan 1

**Café Lyran**

Built in 1867 as a summerhouse, this yellow wooden house is now a café. It is possible to sit inside, in the garden, or on the veranda facing Mälaren.

**Open** all year. mon: closed, tues-fri: 12-21, sat-sun: 12-18.

**Getting there:** T: *Bredäng*, and [more..](/europe/sweden/stockholm/bars_and_cafes/caf_lyran)

tel: 08-646 80 50

address: Ålgrytevägen 90
