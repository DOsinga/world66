A well-established vegetarian restaurant tucked into Gamla Stan on Stora Nygatan. The lunch buffet is the main draw -- hearty, affordable, and varied enough to keep regulars coming back. Dinner is served too, and the atmosphere is relaxed and unpretentious. A reliable choice if you want a meat-free meal in the old town.
