Family museum based on stories of Children's writer Astrid Lindgren (most famous for Pippi Longstockings). The museum features a ride through Astrid Lindgren's books as well as several interactive exhibits. The museum also has a nice cafeteria with a great view as well as one of the best stocked children's book stores in Sweden (including a large number in english and other languages).
