---
title: "Day Trips in Stora sjofallet national park"
type: section
---

Stora sjofallet is one of the absolute last wilderness areas in Europe. As wild as Alasaka ever will be, but not as remote as Siberia.

The main activity in the park is hiking and fishing. There are no snowmobiles permitted in the park (exept for samish peopel whom take care of their reeindeers).

The locals in Gallivare, the nearest town 200 km north est of the area use it for iceclimbing and, telemark, AT, randone or backcountryskiing. There are runs that could be done all year round. Wery steep sections is best skied in May, Jun or even July/Aug. That would be due to low avelanchedanger, nice an smooth snowpacks and still 500-1000 falling meters to go in midnightsun! Very nice.
