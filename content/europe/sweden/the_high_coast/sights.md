The High Coast bridge

The big river Ångermanälven has its outlet in the area. A huge and very beautiful bridge was built across it some years ago. This bridge can be said to be the gateway from the south to the High Coast area.

### Skuleskogen Nat. Park

Skuleskogen National Park
Skuleskogen National Park is part of the High Coast. It can be penetrated mainly by foot. It consists of a very rugged terrain with forest, naked rocks and exposed sea shores. Not far from here is the very steep mountain Skuleberget where mountaineering can be practiced in summer and skiing in winter.
