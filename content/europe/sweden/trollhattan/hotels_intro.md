Tourists would be delighted to find excellent and great value **Trollhattan hotels **just within the city center. Trollhattan is a young city blessed with natural beauty, most notably the river gorge that is said to be the loveliest site in Sweden. The town is sometimes called &ldquo;Trollywood&rdquo; as it is often used as production site for feature films.

**First Hotel Kung Oscar**

Business travelers will appreciate this 4-star hotel located close to the town center. Its 79 rooms are always clean and have a modern, elegant touch. Amenities include a sauna, TV lounge, sports bar, and lobby bar. There&rsquo;s a good variety of food at the evening buffet–which is free from Monday to Thursday. With efficient and friendly staff, this hotel is worth its price, starting at $110.

**Hotel Bele**

Hotel Bele provides perhaps the best value for your money. It is less than 1 km away from Trollh&auml;ttan Central Station and is walking distance from shopping, restaurants and nightclubs. The buffet breakfast is free and offers a delightful choice of breads, meats, cereals, cheeses, fruits, vegetables and juices. It has 31 rooms–costing between $70 and $130 per night–that are always kept flawlessly clean. Wi-Fi access and parking are free.

**Hotel Trollhattan**

For budget travelers, the hotel offers great value with its free buffet breakfast, convenient location in the heart of town, sauna and solarium facilities, and free internet. All 54 rooms are well kept and comfortable and the staffs are warm and friendly. The hotel does not have parking facilities, although public parking is near. Prices start at $80.

**Scandic Swania Hotel**

The Scandic Swania is ideally situated by the canal waterfront, so request for rooms with waterfront view. It boasts of ceiling paintings circa early 1900s. This 4-star hotel has an indoor pool, a workout room, a sauna and restaurant and bar / lounge. Business travelers will appreciate the conference and small meeting rooms and business services. The hotel does not have parking, although there is paid parking nearby. The staffs are multilingual and friendly. And unlike other hotels in the city, its front desk operates 24 hours. Prices start at $100.

**Bohusg&aring;rden Hotel & Konferens**

It could be pricey with rooms costing from $120 to $190, but the stunning waterfront and harbor views in almost all twin rooms makes up for it. The hotel also has the most complete facilities: indoor swimming pool with terrace, bar, Jacuzzi room that is shared with other guests, solarium and herbal baths. The pool terrace provides waterfront view and is a perfect place for a barbeque. Wireless Internet and parking are free.
