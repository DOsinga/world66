### [Slusscafeet](/europe/sweden/trollhttan/bars_and_cafes/slusscafeet)

Run by a local sports club *Skoftebyns IF*. Situated in the historical lock area with a good view over the surroundings.
Pleas note: The cafe is only open during summertime.
