### [Railway](/europe/sweden/trollhttan/getting_there/railway)

About 10 Trains a day go to/from Trollhättan.
There are direct connections to:
Oslo
Karlstad
Göteborg
Vänersborg
And cities in between

A double railway is under construction meaning an increased number of commuter trains to Göteborg within a few years.
