There are several museums in Trollhättan.
The [Canal museum](/europe/sweden/trollhttan/museums/the_canal_museum) tells the history of the canals and locks in the city. Innovatum is a museum for young and old with exhibits from Trollhättans industrial past. The people interested in the car brand Saab has an opportunity to visit the [Saab museum](/europe/sweden/trollhttan/museums/the_saab_museum).

### [Innovatum, kunskapens hus](/europe/sweden/trollhttan/museums/innovatum_kunskap)

Innovatum kunskapens hus (house of knowledge) is located in the old workshops of NOHAB. A section is devoted to Trollhättans facinating industrial history. The children can experience a number of fascinating physical and electrical experiments.

### [The Canal museum](/europe/sweden/trollhttan/museums/the_canal_museum)

This is a museum that describes the struggle to build the locks and canal. Some interesting equipment and ship models are shown. The opening hours depends on the season. In the summer the museum is open all days 10-19 hours. In the winter it is open Saturday and Sunday 12-17 hours.



tel: +46 520 47 22 07

openingHours: depends on season

url: [www2.visittrollhattan.se](http://www2.visittrollhattan.se/english/canallocks_eng.htm)

address: At the Upper Locks
