Both for the the party animal and the sports interested it is quite well provided in Trollhättan, considering that the city is not so big.
The list below is not complete, there are a lot more places to visit.
Most of the homepages below are in Swedish only. Due to Swedish regulations the latest closing date for the clubs and bars is 2 AM.

With about 50 pizzerias and kebabs + a number of other restaurants with pub facilities only a small selection of locations is listed.
