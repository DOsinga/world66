Nynäshamn is a little city located 6 miles south of Stockholm. It lives about 11.000 persons in the town. From Nynäshamn you can find the ferries to G´dansk and Visby.

It is a nice town with a small city center containing some cafes and shoping stores. Nynäshamn is mostly known as a harbour, actully "hamn" means harbour in swedish. The big thing to do in Nynäshamn is visiting the nature, we have a 7 km long walk called the ring road were you experience the typical swedish Archipelago. You can find a number of places to go for a swim. We have one big beach called "Nicksta" were you find a diving tower and mini golf, also a big camping area for tents and trailers. Around Nynäshamn you can find beutilful walking trails into the forest and diffrent natural resarvats. You can also find a lot of Viking history in and around the town. Their is a number of run rocks within a few miles of the city.
