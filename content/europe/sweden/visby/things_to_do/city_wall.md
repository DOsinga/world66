The 3.5-kilometre ring wall encircling Visby's old town is one of the best-preserved medieval fortifications in northern Europe. Forty-four of its original towers survive, and you can walk virtually the entire circuit along the outside. The north stretch offers the most dramatic views — the towers loom over terraced gardens and limestone cliffs dropping toward the sea. Enter through the main Österport (East Gate) or Norderport (North Gate) for the full theatrical effect.
