Travel Guide

**Vårgårda** is a very small town about 70kms from the city of Gothenburg.

The town is quite the most boring place on earth. The inhabitants are racist, narrow minded and seem to think that Vågårda is heaven on earth. Please dont go there! We do, then you'll realise how nice some places are; Bagdad, Calcutta, etc
