Getting to Västerås is easy if you are coming from the south. The connections to the north are not equally good.

**all**

[SJ

Intercity trains Stockholm](/europe/sweden/vsters/getting_there/vsters_central) - [Gothenburg](/europe/sweden/gothenburg) pass through Västerås every other hour. Trains to and from Stockholm runs one or two times an hour on week days. Regional trains takes 53 minutes, the intercity takes 59 minutes. One X2000 per day passes in each direction on the Stockholm - Gothenburg line.

url: [www.sj.se](http://www.sj.se)

tel: 0771-75 75 75

[Stockholm-Västerås Airport

Ryanair takes you to and from London](/europe/sweden/vsters/getting_there/stockholmvster) Stansted in just a little over two hours. Traffic will be diverted to London [Luton](/europe/unitedkingdom/england/eastern_england/luton) from january 19 2005. 

Direktflyg has traffic on the Västerås- Malmö route with their 19 pax aeroplanes.

url: [www.vasterasflygplats.se](http://www.vasterasflygplats.se/)

tel: 021-805610

zipcode: 721 31

[Swebus Express

Buses to/from Stockholm](/europe/sweden/vsters/getting_there/buses), Örebro, Karlstad, [Oslo](/europe/norway/oslo) and [Uppsala](/europe/sweden/uppsala).

url: [www.swebusexpress.se](http://www.swebusexpress.se)

tel: 0200-218 218

[Svenska Buss

Buses to/from Uppsala](/europe/sweden/vsters/getting_there/svenska_buss), Örebro, Karlstad and [Stockholm](/europe/sweden/stockholm).

url: [www.svenskabuss.se](http://www.svenskabuss.se)

tel: 0771-67 67 67

[Tåg i Bergslagen

Regional trains connects Västerås with northwest of Västmanland and southern Dalarna.

Tickets are bought at the station, on the train or at www.sj.se .

url: www.tagibergslagen.se](/europe/sweden/vsters/getting_there/tg_i_bergslagen)
