Intercity trains [Stockholm](/europe/sweden/stockholm) - [Gothenburg](/europe/sweden/gothenburg) pass through Västerås every other hour. Trains to and from Stockholm runs one or two times an hour on week days. Regional trains takes 53 minutes, the intercity takes 59 minutes. One X2000 per day passes in each direction on the Stockholm - Gothenburg line.
