Arbon is a small town on the southern shore of Lake Constance in the canton of Thurgau. Its old town sits on a promontory jutting into the lake, with the medieval Schloss Arbon and a cluster of half-timbered houses around it. The lakeside promenade is one of the prettiest on the Swiss side of the Bodensee.

The town has a long industrial history — the Saurer truck and textile machinery factory was based here for over a century, and the old factory buildings are gradually being converted into cultural spaces. The lakefront park, the views across to the German shore, and the relaxed pace make Arbon a pleasant stop between St. Gallen and Konstanz.
