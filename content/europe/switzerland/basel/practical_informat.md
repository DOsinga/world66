**Phone calls**

The international country code for Switzerland is +41 , the national city code for Basel is 061 , if you call from abroad dial +41 61

**Tourist offices**

There is a tourist office at the SBB train station (open every day) and a larger one in the centre at Schiffslände 5 (closed on Sundays)
