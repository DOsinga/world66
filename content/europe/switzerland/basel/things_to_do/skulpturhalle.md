An offshoot of the Antikenmuseum displaying plaster casts of the most important works of ancient Greek and Roman sculpture, including a reconstruction of the pediments of the Parthenon. Odd-sounding but surprisingly compelling — you can see pieces together here that in the original are scattered across museums worldwide.
