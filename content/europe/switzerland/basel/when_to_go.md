---
title: "When to Go"
type: section
---

Basel is pleasant year-round, but the standout event is the **Basel Carnival** (Basler Fasnacht), which starts at 4 am on the Monday after Ash Wednesday — precisely when the rest of Europe stops celebrating. Lanterns, fife-and-drum bands, and masked processions fill the old town for three days. It is one of the biggest carnivals in Switzerland and a genuinely memorable experience.

Summer brings the **Art Basel** fair in June, one of the world's most important contemporary art events. The Rhine is swimmable in summer and locals float downstream in waterproof bags — a uniquely Basel tradition.
