---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

**By train and bus** is the standard approach from Geneva or Zurich. Take the train to Sierre (on the main Lausanne–Brig line), then the SMC funicular railway from Sierre up to Montana — the ride takes about 12 minutes and runs frequently. From Montana, a free ski bus connects the resort. Total journey from Geneva is around 2.5 hours; from Zurich closer to 3 hours.

**By car**, Crans-Montana is about 2.5 hours from Geneva via the A9 motorway to Sierre, then a winding road up the mountain. Parking in the resort can be expensive and limited in peak season. If you are skiing, most accommodation provides parking or is near a lift.

**The nearest airports** are Geneva (GVA), about 2.5 hours away, and Sion (SIO), a small regional airport just 30 kilometres down the valley in the Rhône Valley. Sion has limited seasonal connections but is extremely convenient if you can find a flight. Zürich (ZRH) is roughly 3 hours by car or train.
