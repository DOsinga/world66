One of several small lakes scattered across the Crans-Montana plateau, Lac Grenon sits just above the town and is the most accessible. In summer it is popular for a swim or a picnic on the grassy banks. The reflection of the Valais peaks on a calm morning is one of the better free views in the resort. A short walking path circles the lake.
