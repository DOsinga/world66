Am Stram Gram is Geneva's theatre for young audiences, staging a full season of productions aimed at children and teenagers. The programming mixes classic fairy tales and new commissions, often with strong visual and physical theatre elements that work even if your French is limited. The intimate 200-seat hall on Route de Frontenex keeps sightlines close, so younger children stay engaged.

Worth checking the programme if you're travelling with kids — performances typically run Wednesday afternoons and weekends during the school year.
