Le Trois-Huit is a dance club on the Route de Thonon, about 15 minutes east of central Geneva along the lake. It draws a mixed crowd with DJ nights spanning house, techno, and Latin music. The terrace is a draw in summer. Dress code is relaxed by Geneva standards.
