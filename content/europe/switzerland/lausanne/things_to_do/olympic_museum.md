The world's largest archive of the Olympic Games, set in a modern building surrounded by sculpture on the lakefront at Ouchy. The permanent collection traces the history of the Games through objects, film, and interactive exhibits. It is well done and genuinely interesting even if you are not a sports fan. The terrace cafe overlooking the lake is a pleasant spot for lunch.
