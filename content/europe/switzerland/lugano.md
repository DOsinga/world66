Lugano is a very beautiful city in the south of [Switzerland](/europe/switzerland), practically on the border with [Italy](/europe/italy). The people speak Italian, dress Italian and eat Italian, so in many ways it feels like you are already in Italy.

The town is located on the borders of Lake Lugano and you have very beautiful views over the lake.

Lugano has many festivals for film and music and it's a very lively city.
