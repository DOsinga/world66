Montreux does have one interesting museums, the museum that shows the history of Montreux. The old Gothic church, dating from the 12th century, is worth a visit as well. Besides that, the surroundings lend themselves perfectly for a walk along the lake or gazing at the Alpine range in the summer sun. A must is a visit to the Château de Chillon, a historic masterpiece.

** Château de Chillon**

Nearby Montreux, on the shores of Lake Geneva, the impressive Château de Chillon is a must-see. The castle, that was built in the 13th century, was a residence of the Counts of Savoy.
