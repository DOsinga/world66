---
title: "Le Sentier des Toblerones"
type: poi
---

For those who enjoy walking Le Sentier des Toblerones is a nice walk. You can start from Nyon and make your way up to Bassins or you can do it the opposite way. the walk is divided into smaller sections to be visited. The full walk is seventeen kilometers.

The defensive toblerone were placed here after the first World War to avoid invasion. In particular the mentality is that if an army attempts to invade Switzerland the best policy would be to retreat into the mountains where defence is easier.

What remains is a line of concrete blocks, pill boxes and mined bridges which are all designed to block tanks.

Along this walk you will find paths through the trees along rivers, fields and valleys. You will see that some houses have the toblerones in their gardens and in other places you will be able to enter a pillbox to see the world from there

It's a moderate walk, estimated to take about four hours although fast walkers may do it in around 3hrs.

If you do the full walk then take some food with you as may find your appetite building once you are further from restaurants.
