Billed as the first music concert of the summer for those living in the region Le Caribana is set in a nice location. At the port the Crans with two stages with a variety of artists. Cheb Khaled and other artists have been in the past.

Located around half an hour's walk from Nyon but with a shuttle service from Nyon and the parkings for the festival access is easy.

When you come you will find a variety of food and drink, usually with a sports bar, ethnic food and more. Children are taken into consideration as well.
