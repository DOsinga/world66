**Schaffhausen** is a city in north-eastern Switzerland on the river
Rhine, next to the German border. It's an old city that become a member
of the Swiss Confederation in 1501. In 1944 Schaffhausen was
accidentally bombed by United States airforce that mistook neutral
Swiss town for [Germany](/europe/germany).
Current population of Schaffhausen is about 33700.
