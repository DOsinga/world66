Schiers is a small municipality in the Prättigau valley in the Swiss canton of Graubünden. The valley is tucked between the Rätikon and Silvretta mountain ranges and is a base for hiking and skiing. The village is on the road to Klosters and Davos, but quieter and considerably cheaper.
