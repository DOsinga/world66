Welcome to the walhallah of skiing. St Moritz attracts visitors from all over the world who want to beat the snow by (cross-country) skiing, bobsledding or trekking. No matter whether this is your first time on skis or whether you were born with them, the area has
 slopes for both the beginner and the expert. There are good accommodation facilities and it won’t be difficult to find a place where you can enjoy the après-ski. St Moritz is also known for its mineral springs.
