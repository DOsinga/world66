A small regional museum documenting the distinctive culture of the Engadin valley. The collection covers traditional furniture, domestic life, and the Romansh-speaking heritage of the region. The building itself is a fine example of an Engadin house, with thick walls and sgraffito decoration. Worth an hour if you want to understand the valley beyond the ski lifts.
