Nice, small bar with lots of tradition. One of the traditions is that
if you can climb over a wooden bar in the ceiling and drink a glass of
wine hanging upside down, without spilling the wine you can carve your
name in the walls or ceiling. Pretty hard, but the wine is free if you
succeed!
