---
title: "Bars and Cafes"
type: section
---

Dnipro has a lively café culture concentrated along Karl Marx Avenue (Prospekt Karla Marksa) and the Dnieper embankment. Ukrainian café chains, independent coffee shops, and bar-restaurants crowd the central strip. Varenyky (dumplings), borscht, and Ukrainian horilka (vodka) are the staples alongside European café fare. The city's student population keeps prices reasonable and hours late.
