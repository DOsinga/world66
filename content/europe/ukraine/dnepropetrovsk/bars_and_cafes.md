---
title: "Bars and Cafes in Dnepropetrovsk"
type: section
---

**Dneopropetrovsk bars and cafes** are inviting, and they always offer a good place to drink coffee, tea or a good beer. There are places which are full of luxury and there are places where people can relax and have a good time.

**Galinka Bar**

Located at 2b Donestk Motorway, there are 3 big halls: one for drinking with a bar and a discoteque, where Okun (seafood) is served and beer is $20.00 GRN; the second with pool tables; and the third one for private chatting and VIP use. The VIP area features a private room with a sauna, jacuzzi and private bar with high class wines. This bar is frequented by tourists and by upscale locals. The prices are a bit high, but the money spent in this place is good spent money. This is one of the most luxurious of Dnepropetrovsk bars and cafes.

**Toko Toko**

Toko Toko is a very simple and economic cafe shop located at 67D Karla Marksa Avenue. It serves coffee, pizza and beer for $15.00 GRN. The decor has a simple style with sofas like those of a house hall to sit down and sip the coffee. Wall-mounted TVs allow the visitors to watch TV while they enjoy a good drink.

**Puzata Xata Cafe**

Located at #5 Petrivskogo Square at the railway station, this coffee shop is bigger than most coffee shops. It's homely decor and colors go well with it's amazing outside facade. The cafe serves dishes like Chicken Kyiv and Green Borsch for $38.00 and $32.00 GRN ($4.00 USD). This is one of the many Dnepropetrovsk bars and cafes that comes with birthday services for the little ones, and a kids room destined for this. Also, this cafe is open on Sundays.
