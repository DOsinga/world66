---
title: "Festivals"
type: section
---

The Free Dance International Festival of Contemporary Dance and Performance, held annually in April and May, is the largest of its kind in Ukraine and brings international contemporary dance companies to the city's theatres. Dnipro has a historically significant Jewish community — one of the largest in Ukraine — and Jewish cultural events are held periodically. City Day (usually May) marks the founding of the city with concerts and celebrations along the embankment.
