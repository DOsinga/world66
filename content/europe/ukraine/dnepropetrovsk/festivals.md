**Dnepropetrovsk Festivals **shows the richness and diversity in culture that they have. Their festivals are known for being dazzling and vibrant.

**Jewish Festival**

The Jewish festival in Dnepropetrovsk is a lively and brilliant celebration. This is composed of a day or a series of days' observance to commemorate the important event in the history of the Jewish people.

**Free Dance International Festival of Contemporary Dance and Performance **

Free Dance International Festival of Contemporary Dance and Performance happens annually in the months of April and May. This festival is the only celebration in Ukraine that gathers together all the professional theaters of contemporary conceptual dance from many countries worldwide at its festival venues and theatrical stages. Free Dance International Festival of Contemporary Dance and Performance started in the year 2001 and it continues up to the present. Among the events during this festival are educational projects (workshops and creative laboratories), entertaining performances, and exhibitions and discussions. The aim of Free Dance Festival is to have a forum of various viewpoints and endless questions. Free Dance International Festival is exclusively a non-profit experimental project in the field of contemporary art. This festival has been successful because of the support coming from the international institutions and other donations from the volunteers.

**Bawdy Festival**

Bawdy festival is a unique festival in Dnepropetrovsk that happens every October.

**Jazz Festival**

Jazz Festival in Dnepropetrovsk is celebrated annually every October.

**Fireworks Festival**

Fireworks festival is the grandiose show of magnificent fireworks. The Sheriff World of Fireworks, a leading fireworks company in Ukraine, leads this astounding festival.
