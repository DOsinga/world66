**Enjoy Traveling in Dnepropetrovsk**

**Dnepropetrovsk transportation** is one of the most extensive in Ukraine. This is because the city is the center of industrial establishments in the area. Here are some options you might want to consider using while you are in Dnepropetrovsk.

**Travel within the City**

Within the city, you can travel using the trolley bus, the bus, and the city tram. These are cheap means of transportation, costing only from ten to fifty cents. They run from 6 a.m. to 1 a.m. The buses can take you to the magnificent Dnepropetrovsk city churches. You can also tour the neighboring suburbs of Igren and Pridneprovsk

An alternative transportation is the marshrutka, a type of taxi van that goes from one point of the city to another. Traffic is sometimes heavy for the marshrutka, but well skilled drivers can easily transport you from one point to another within the city. The marshrutka costs from $1 to $10. There are also taxis with which you can negotiate a prearranged price agreement. If you do not have trouble reading maps, then opting for a car rental may benefit you.

**Travel by Train**

The metro train is the cheapest mode of transportation, which costs only ten cents. It is also the fastest means of transportation within the city. You can also choose the train when you are going out of Dnepropetrovsk to the different places in Ukraine, like Poltava and Odessa. You can travel to the Carpathian Mountains by train and enjoy the picture-perfect views.
