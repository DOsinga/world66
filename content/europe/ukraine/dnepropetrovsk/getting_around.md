---
title: "Getting Around"
type: section
---

Dnipro has a metro (three stations on a single line through the centre), an extensive bus and trolleybus network, and marshrutkas (shared minibus taxis) running fixed routes across the city. Public transport is cheap. Taxis and ride-hailing apps operate city-wide. The city is spread out; the metro is useful for the main central corridor but most visitors use minibuses or taxis for wider exploration.
