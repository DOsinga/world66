Along the [Dnieper River](/europe/ukraine/dnepropetrovsk), tangled between old-world charm and new-world industry, is [Dnepropetrovsk](/europe/ukraine/dnepropetrovsk) and **Dnepropetrovsk Museums **ensure that the city's importance is not forgotten. Learn and enjoy.

**Dnepropetrovsk Museum of Art**

Founded in 1914, this complex has created a good reputation throughout the [world](/europe). With a collection of paintings, sculptures, religious icons and more, it has become the grand center of art within Dnepropetrovsk. Since its creation it has sought only the most impressive items for its exhibits. And it has succeeded: over 7000 artifacts can now be seen. And they are each worth the hassles in reaching this museum. It is one of the city’s most popular offerings and crowds will be heavy. Scheduling a tour is recommended. Purchase tickets early to spare yourself the complications.

**Dnepropetrovsk Aerospace Museum**

Of all Dnepropetrovsk museums, this is perhaps the most unique. Chronicling the city’s rise as a Soviet power, it offers guests the chance to see the explorations of air. Exhibitions include genuine crafts, the history of space travel, engine components and more. Wander the corridors and be amazed with all you find. Reveling in Dnepropetrovsk’s ingenuity, this is often (affectionately) named the Rocket Museum . It will thrill. No reservations are needed. Tickets can be purchased at the door and with no difficulty. Groups are welcomed.

**Dnepropetrovsk Museum of History **

No journey to the [Ukraine](/europe/ukraine) would be a true one without a visit here. The Dnepropetrovsk Museum of History is a massive tribute to tradition. Carefully preserving the culture of the city (and all that surrounds it), it has secured over 200,000 items. From dinosaur bones to the personal correspondence of[Grigory Potyomkin](/europe/ukraine/people), it houses unexpected treasures. Guests will be taken through 10 specific halls, each with their own eras to support. It’s not to be missed. Travelers, remember: this is an extremely popular destination. Plan well.

Dnepropetrovsk museums seek only one thing--to celebrate the past of a vital city. And all who visit will agree that they do.
