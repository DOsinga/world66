title: "Nightlife and Entertainment in Dnepropetrovsk"
type: section
---

There are many fun **Dnepropetrovsk Nightlife** locations available and there is range of activities to choose from. The nightclubs are quite legendary in the city. The vast majority of the nightlife and entertainment is found around the river.

**Bars, Nightclubs and Discos**

If you are looking for a shot of vodka, a drink and some casual food, then relax in the Shamrock Irish Pub. This is a much quieter bar and despite the name it does not feel like an Irish pub. If you enjoy beer, then you should try a beer-like drink named Kvass. Besides beer, the vodka is very inexpensive and you can find great choices at any bar or pub.

A great disco found in the very center of the city is Labirinth. This disco has 3 different rooms, 1 for techno, another for pop/rock and a third for dance music. This is one of the largest nightclubs in the city. Some other popular clubs include Pilot, Berloin Club, M3, Reporter and L’Orangerie. This is the city for nightclubs and the range of different music is extensive as you can find electronic, modern jazz, techno, rock, pop and more.

**Taking Part in the Movie**

If you are up for a movie, enjoy some of the most modern technology as the MOST-kino not only can show 3D movies but it can also show movies in 4D. The 4D room has effects such as flashes of light, rain, vibration, wind and fog. You will feel like you are really in the movie. The Pravda Cinema has deep turndown chairs and uses modern technology. At the cinema you can also find a video store and pizzeria.

**A Night at the Theater**

You can enjoy an evening at the theater or opera. The opera was where the rich and famous used to gather and many famous international stars have performed on this stage. Some other venues include the ballet theater and Ukrainian Drama Theater. You can even enjoy the circus at night which is quite spectacular when all lit up.

If you are by the opera house, then you should take in the fountain at night as it is all lit up. Another great spot for a concert is the Potina which is an open-air stage found in the Globa Park.

**Gambling at the Casino**

You can also enjoy your evening at the casino playing the slots or other table games. The Grand Hotel contains the casino. This casino is located downtown and it is a very popular spot as it is frequently busy and can get very noisy.
