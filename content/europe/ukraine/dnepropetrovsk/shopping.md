---
title: "Shopping in Dnepropetrovsk"
type: section
---

Dnepropetrovsk is the main industrial and trade center for the Ukraine so there is plenty of **Dnepropetrovsk shopping** to choose from. Besides the many industries the city has several institutes, academies and universities. It also has one of the best public transportation systems found.

**A Contemporary Wonder**

The main shopping center is the Europe shopping center which is worth a trip just to see the cool architecture. This shopping mall is very contemporary and is found in the historical center of the town. All of the stores are arranged around the central space which has a vertical atrium. This building is a great example of modern classicism though there are two modernist designs in the building, the zigzag of the escalators and an open corner glazed elevator.

**Finding Fashion in Dnepropetrovsk**

For fashion you can stop at TSUM which is located at Lenin Place. If you want mainly Western or European brands and stores then stop by the Grand Plaza as you can find Swarovski, Espirit and Kenzo. If you need an elaborate evening gown or something out of a fairytale then stop by Natalia Musachenko. This store also has bridal dresses. Another fun fashion studio to visit is the Giaterie.

Many people also enjoy the fashion available at the Karl Marx Parkway also known as Prospect Karl Marksa. On this avenue you can find luxury boutiques, department stores, galleries and more. This shopping area actually extends to several neighboring streets and you can find a square of stores dedicated to shopping.

**Traditional Products and Crafts**

If you just want to wander and browse than visit the Ozerka which is a medieval market. Here you can find inexpensive items, goods from local craftsman, clothes, food and drinks. If you want a traditional souvenir such as embroideries, wooden objects and paintings than you should go to the center of the city and see what is being offered in Handicraft’s Walk and the Arts Salon.

If you are specifically looking for souvenirs then check out the Exhibition which is across the street from, TSUM. Another good place to find traditional jewelery and paintings is Vernisazh. In particular there are gold amulets called Oberegi that are popular and were considered to be very valuable to the owner.

This city is also the place for Rushnyk. This is a type of embroidered cloth that is famous in the Ukraine. The most popular form is towels but you can find any type of embroidered cloth. Another traditional present are the many wooden statues that will suite any occasion.
