---
title: "Bars and Cafes"
type: section
---

Kyiv has a dense and varied nightlife concentrated around the Khreshchatyk and Podil neighbourhoods. The club scene is large and international, with venues covering electronic music, jazz, and live acts. Podil has a concentration of bars and cafes in a more relaxed setting. Andriyivskyy Descent has a number of small cafes and wine bars. The city's cafe culture is strong throughout the day, with espresso bars and patisseries on most central streets.
