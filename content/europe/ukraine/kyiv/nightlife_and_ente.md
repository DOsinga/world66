---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment"
type: section
---

Nightlife in the Ukraine is exciting and diverse. Kyiv is sometimes called a club city but there is much more to it than that. Kyiv is often teaming with foreign tourists that come there for diversity and for the fun they can have while they are there. On weekends and holidays you may be hard pressed to find tables and traffic can quickly become jammed with crowds.

Casinos used to fill the streets of Kyiv, but on July 1, 2009 the Russian government outlawed casinos and they had to cease operation. That didn’t put a halt to all the interesting things you can find in the city or stop the multitudes of clubs that crowd the streets in Kyiv. Clubs in Kyiv generally are expensive but if this is the type of fun you want, you will get all you need here. Hookahs, flavored tobacco water pipes, are prevalent as are professional dance and model shows. Don’t be surprised to see men dancing with each other as it is common and acceptable and some clubs pay pretty women to lure men in and spend money in their establishments. There are many strip clubs on the streets of Kyiv as well, so be sure the club you want to visit is what you think it is before you go in.

**Clubs**

Clubs have special nights for different types of music. Tuesday may be jazz night and Disco might be the favorite another night of the week. You may hear Salsa coming from one club and Heavy Metal from another. In fact, the Caribbean Club teaches salsa for a price so you can dance along.

**Restaurants**

Restaurants are everywhere in Kyiv and serve just about any dish including favorite dishes of the leaders of the Soviet Party. Most restaurants have stages and private VIP rooms. You may find a bar on one street that has German Beer and down the road there will be another pub called O’Brien’s that serves Guinness and English foods.

**Entertainment**

You can participate in many different activities in the establishments of Kyiv. If you like Karaoke, it abounds and often patrons can perform with professional musicians. Sorry Babushka is one of the most famous Karaoke venues in town. The Pioneer Ice Club comes complete with a skating rink and cafeteria. The River Palace floats and has a café, restaurant and dance floor. At the Trocadero you can experience one act plays or dance numbers.

You can see the diversity of the city of Kyiv. It doesn’t matter what kind of fun you want to have, you can find there.
