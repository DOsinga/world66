### [Communications

The international country code for Ukraine is +038

The city area code for Kyiv is 44

The public phone system in Ukraine is free of charge - local calls at
phone booths are free. However, the existing phone system is at times
unreliable and some public phone booths are inoperative.

Visitors may purchase prepaid phone cards for their cellular telephone at good rates from the many vendors around the city.
