If you are going to be in the Ukraine and want to stay in Lugansk, you can find many **Lugansk bars and cafes** that are fun to visit. Here are a few of the places that you may want to visit while you are staying in the city.

#### Aquarius

This restaurant and bar offers many options to enjoy including a winter garden, summer grounds, and European, Ukrainian, and Caucasian dishes. You can enjoy the live music that is played in the evenings and the cocktails that are available in the bar. You can relax with a cocktail near the summer or winter gardens, or experience a lively night of music in the bar.

#### Victoria Caf&eacute;

The Victoria Caf&eacute; offers a European menu and many drinks available including vodka, cognac, tequila, martinis, wines and cocktails. You can relax in the caf&eacute; and enjoy the delicious menu items while sampling some of the different cocktails available. There are a wide variety of drinks that are served and many of the items on the menu can be paired with a number of imported wines that are available.

#### Nikko Caf&eacute;

This is a Japanese themed caf&eacute; that offers many divine French imported wines that go nicely with the many Asian inspired menu options that are available. You can try a variety of different items and sample the cocktails that are served. From traditional to modern, the caf&eacute; offers something for everyone to try and is one of the Lugansk bars and cafes that you may want to check out.

#### Dionis

This bar and caf&eacute; offers many appetizers and starters including some of the local favorites like squid and the European options. You can enjoy the wines and cocktails that are served, and you may want to stop here for one of your Lugansk bars and cafes while you are in the city. If you are looking to try something new and want to experience the different tastes, try out Dionis.

#### Karertniy Dvor

If you are looking for quiet and relaxing music that is played by one of the local musicians, while sipping on a cocktail, the soft lighting and relaxing restaurant and bar area will surely please. There are many recipes available to offer you a different kind of meal to enjoy, and many cocktails and wines are available as well.
