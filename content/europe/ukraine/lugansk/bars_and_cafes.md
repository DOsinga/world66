---
title: "Bars and Cafes"
type: section
---

Lugansk's bar and cafe scene reflects its status as a young, working city — there are cocktail bars, European-style cafes, and a smattering of themed establishments. Aquarius on one of the central streets offers European, Ukrainian, and Caucasian dishes alongside a winter garden and live music. Victoria Café serves a European menu with a broad selection of wines and spirits, while Nikko is a Japanese-themed spot pairing Asian food with French wines. Dionis is a reliable local bar with appetisers and wines, and Karertniy Dvor offers a quieter atmosphere with soft music and cocktails.
