---
title: "Eating Out"
type: section
---

Lugansk's restaurant scene mixes local Ukrainian cooking with international themes. Texas Saloon brings a steakhouse atmosphere to the city centre with large portions of grilled meat. Irish Café Bar near the central market has two halls and outdoor summer seating. Pinta Beer Restaurant pairs its craft beers with hearty menu items, and Jungles Downtown offers a lavishly decorated setting with local and international cuisine.
