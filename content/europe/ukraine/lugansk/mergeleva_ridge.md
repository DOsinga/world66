title: Mergeleva Ridge

About 30 kilometres from Lugansk, this archaeological site contains Bronze Age burial mounds and the ruins of ancient structures dating to around 4000 BC. Discovered by students on an archaeological expedition, the ridge offers a glimpse into the prehistoric cultures of the Donets region. Best reached by car.
