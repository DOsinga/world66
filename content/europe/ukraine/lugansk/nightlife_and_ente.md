---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment in Lugansk"
type: section
---

**Lugansk Nightlife** will win you over. Lugansk is a city filled with young people and the city comes alive at night. This is a highly sought after party zone in Ukraine. Read on to find out more.

**Iceberg**

If you are looking at dazzling with the starsa then Iceberg is the perfect place to chill in Lugansk. This club is visited by the cr&egrave;me de la cr&egrave;me in Lugansk. It is located on Budyonny Street. This night club is a fairly new entry in the Lugansk's night scene. It has a separate VIP zone and an exclusive strip bar. The dance floor is the biggest in town and the music played here is a good mix of both Russian and English.

**Apelsin**

Located on Magnitogorskaya Street, Apelsin is a mix between a pub and a lounge bar. This night spot features regular live entertainment in the form of music and an orchestra. You can go to Apelsin, if you are looking at sitting down and sipping on your drink. The interiors are done to resemble a car workshop with cars being transformed into tables and various parts of the car being used as wall hangings.

**Pharaoh**

Pharaoh is another up market night club in Lugansk. This place is known for its live performances like modern ballet and the likes. During performance nights, the club has a standard cover charge. The club opens at 8:00 p.m. and goes on till 2:00 a.m. The club is comprised of three levels and the interiors are done up well. This club is on the more expensive side. It is located in the west end of the district, locally known as Artyom. Selena supermarket is a famous landmark next to this club.

**Gagarin**

This nightclub is something out of the ordinary. The management has found a winning recipe by combining a night club and a bowling alley. It is located on the Druzbha block behind the market on the eastern side. The DJ plays some amazing music from rock, hip hop to R&B. From Latino nights to disco time, this place has it all. DJ Arbooz is known to pump up an erotic and fun energy on the dance floor with his music.

**Good Zone**

If you are not in the mood to dance then venture in to the Good Zone. This night club hosts the best dance performances in town. The nightclub has exotic dancers who put up a great show every evening. The booze is priced right and you can spend the night dancing with beautiful men and women. It is located close to the Ukrainian theater on the Cheliuskintsev Street. The club opens around 7:00 p.m. and goes on till 1:00 a.m.
