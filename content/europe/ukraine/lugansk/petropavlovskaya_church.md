One of the few pre-revolutionary sacred buildings still standing in Lugansk, this 19th-century church in the Stone Ford district survived the Soviet demolitions (though heavily damaged) and has since been restored. Its architecture is a rare example of imperial-era ecclesiastical building in the region.
