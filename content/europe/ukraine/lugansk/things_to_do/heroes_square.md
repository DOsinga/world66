Located on Sovetskaya Street, Heroes Square commemorates soldiers who fell in the Second World War. A row of life-sized monuments depicts wartime scenes, with names of fallen soldiers inscribed on the main memorial and an obelisk at the centre. A quiet, reflective place in the heart of the city.
