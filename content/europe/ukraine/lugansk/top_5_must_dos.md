When you visit the culture rich city of Lugansk, Ukraine, consider these top 5 **Lugansk must do&rsquo;s** that will surely make your vacation packed full of fun.

#### 1. Catch a Gala at the Russian Drama Center

Nestled along Kotsyubinsky Street is a comfortable hub for those who are interested in the performing arts. The Russian Drama Center, an 800-seat theatre hall, features various drama performances and musical concerts. Usually, they employ different themes for such events. Also, there is always a fireworks display to watch out for at the end of the gala performances. Take note that the Russian Drama Center is closed during Mondays.

#### 2. Get Some Ukrainian Vodka

Vodka enthusiasts shouldn&rsquo;t skip visiting Luga-Nova. This beverage shop prides itself for having a wide selection of wines, Ukrainian beers and specialty vodkas. They have over 20 different kinds of vodka produced only from natural ingredients. It is also an ideal place for getting gifts for your fellow vodka fans. They sell VIP selections and unique bottles that will surely impress and flatter your friends.

#### 3. Get in Touch with Nature at May Day Park

This is an ideal activity for family vacationers. The kids will love the Ferris Wheel inside the park that overlooks the greenery, the grassy landscape and some animals that can be found in the park. You can also go for a hike to enjoy the calming ambiance and the gentle breeze that you won&rsquo;t experience in the busier part of the city. As an additional attraction, there&rsquo;s an in-house zoo that houses dozens of species of animals, and they also offer animal shows that will amuse the whole family.

#### 4. Hunt for Arts and Crafts at the Artist&rsquo;s Gallery

Along St. Etienne Street, there&rsquo;s the Artist&rsquo;s Gallery that will please tourists who are art inclined. It often features exhibits of different art genres. Also, it houses a shop for local handmade crafts like woodcarvings, dolls, ceramic products and even paintings. Most of these art pieces showcase Lugansk&rsquo;s culture and landscapes.

#### 5. Rev It Up in VIP Night Parties

People who are into night events will not be disappointed due to the abundance of bars and clubs scattered in the east and west ends of the district. Dance the night away at Iceberg, a premier high-end club that has a spacious VIP hall, a banquet area and a bar that serves refreshing cocktails at reasonable prices. Enjoy the hip music that this club has to offer.

All of these activities for people with varying interests are foolproof ways of spending a fun time with your family and friends. Check out these Lugansk must-do&rsquo;s for a remarkable vacation experience.
