Your **Lugansk when to go **plan should be properly premeditated so that you will enjoy your travel to this city without the fear of rain spoiling your sightseeing. Lugansk is a city that is located in Ukraine. This city is a favorite tourist destination due to its rolling hills and the scenic view of the Carpathian Mountains. However, before you pack up and get excited about your travel to Lugansk, here is some relevant information that you need to know when making your plans.

**Summer in Lugansk**

The climate in Lugansk is semi-arid, thus it only gets a minimum rainfall of 350mm during the summer. However, during the wet season, the city gets about 1500mm rain per month, thus if you want to enjoy your trip to Lugansk, then you need to make sure that you visit during summer time. Summer in Lugansk occurs during the months of April to August. The scenery around Lugansk comes alive as the flowers are in bloom and the Carpathian Mountains are no longer barren. Moreover, summer in this city is also blessed with an average sunlight of 1600 and 2400 hours per year, thereby giving you enough time to stroll around the city. Temperature during summer ranges from 18 and 24 degrees Celsius (64.4 and 75.2 degrees Fahrenheit), which makes the city relatively cool and perfect for sightseeing tourists.

**Avoid Lugansk during Wet Season**

If you do not like to travel during the wet season, then avoid going to Lugansk during the months of October to November. As mentioned earlier, the rainfall of Lugansk during wet season is 1500mm, which means that it rains all the time in the city. Although the wet season in Lugansk is not accompanied by strong winds, you still won&rsquo;t be able to plan your sightseeing trip around the city and the Carpathian Mountains.

**Mistrial Festival**

The best time to visit Lugansk is during their Mistrial Festival, which happens during summer. During this festival, you can see a lot of dancing on the city&rsquo;s center and locals wear their colorful national clothes. Moreover, you can also buy a lot of authentic Ukrainian trinkets that you can take back home to your loved ones. And, since this festival is held during summer, you can be assured that rain will not spoil all the dancing and merry-making that happens during this festival.
