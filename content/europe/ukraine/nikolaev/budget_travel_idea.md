---
title: "Budget Travel Ideas in Nikolaev"
type: section
---

**Nikolaev budget travel **allows you to take in some of the best
the city has to offer without breaking the bank. Activities for the
traveling family, senior travelers and the solo traveler on a budget
include:

**Nikolaev Children's Town Fairy Tale**

In the middle of Nikolaev is a children's delight--that's completely free to visit. (Some games may cost a little to play.) The popular park in the heart of Nikolaev
is Children's Town Fairy Tale, where kids can explore and play a
miniature amusement park made to look like something out of a European
fairy tale. There's a miniature castle, pirate ship and a false train
tp play in. Games and activities include a house of mirrors, swings,
trampolines, children's cars and a merry-go-round. After the kids have
had their fun, take the whole family to the nearby Fairy Tale Cafe,
where you can feed the entire family on a budget with basic casual
dining options.

**Visit the Nikolaev Zoo**

While families may be the most likely to enjoy the most popular zoo
in Ukraine, even adults and seniors delight in seeing the over 2,500
animals (350 different species) at the Nikolaev Zoo. Best of all,
admission is the equivalent of about US$4 for adults and US$2 for
children, making a day at the zoo a must-see part of Nikolaev budget travel. You can tour the Zoo Museum, have lunch at a cafe and enjoy the sights and sounds of the wild.

Tip: Try to visit the zoo during the spring or summer. (The summer
will be more crowded, though.) During the late fall and winter, the
animals are kept inside to protect them from the harsh climate. While
you can still tour the indoor facilities, it's more exciting to see the animals out in a natural environment than an indoor, artificial one. The indoor tour has a more unpleasant smell as well.

**Walk Along Sovetskya Street**

Spend a day just seeing what the city of Nikolaev has to offer its residents. Sovetskya Street, the main street of the city, is attractively framed by rows of trees on both sides. You'll find a plethora of stores and dining establishments to entertain you.

**Go for a Walk in the Park**

You have nineteen city parks from which to choose in Nikolaev. You
don't get activities much cheaper than strolling along a park avenue,
taking in the flora and sightseeing. Popular parks include Victory Park
and Petrovsky Park. You can see the coast of Black Sea from some of the city parks.
