---
title: "Eating Out in Nikolaev"
type: section
---

Traveling to the city of Nikolaev provides anyone with a lot of culture and history to enjoy, along with some of the best Nikolaev restaurants in the area. Anyone who likes to try new and exotic types of food will surely enjoy the variety of restaurants and foods available in Nikolaev, Ukraine. Here are some of the top rated restaurants to discover on a vacation to the city.

**Pharaon **

This restaurant offers many unique features to its guests. It is a Japanese style restaurant with a wide variety of Japanese cuisine. Aside from the restaurant there is also a dance floor and DJ. Guests can enjoy the flavors of the meals and relax in a dim lit restaurant then stay to dance on the hip new dance floor and music.

**Dixie Barbeque **

This restaurant is American inspired and offers a cozy atmosphere for anyone to enjoy. There is live music for listening pleasure and it is located near the center of the city. The menu is full of American favorites based on the barbeque style of cooking. It is located near many of the tourist stops which makes it a great place to stop and eat when traveling through the city,

**Hortitsa Pizza **

This restaurant has a variety of items that include the national dishes of the Ukraine, fritters, pizza, potato stews, coffee and teas, chicken and many other unique menu items with a variety of tasty treats. This unique pizzeria style restaurant has many different items to suit anyone who enjoys trying some of the food that is famous in the Ukraine as well as tasty desserts.

**Beloye Solntse Pustyni **

The name of this restaurant, Beloye Solntse Pustyni, translates to mean the white sun of the desert and was named after a famous film from the Soviet. It has an oriental kitchen and full menu of great authentic tastes from the Orient. After dinner guests can enjoy the dance party that takes place with some of the great music performed by local artists from the community.

**Kaktus **

This restaurant is found in a cinema that allows guests to sip on coffee or tea while enjoying a film, and then move on to dinner courses from a Ukrainian style menu. It is a great place to enjoy a movie or theatre performance and dinner.
