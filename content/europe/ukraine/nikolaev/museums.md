type: section
---

The Ukranian city of Nikolaev, also known as Mykolayiv in Ukranian,
has several museums of interest to travelers. The museums highlight the
city's historical and cultural significance.
**Museum of Shipbuilding and Fleet**

Established
in the late 1970's, the museum showcases the history of Nikolaev and
its importance to the shipbuilding industry and as a naval base. In
addition to its citizens building ships, Nikolaev also served as the
headquarters of the Russian Black Sea Naval Fleet. The museum displays
several replica ships. The city remains critical to the shipbuilding
industry.
