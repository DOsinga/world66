---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment in Nikolaev"
type: section
---

Nikolaev nightlife is very hectic and bustling where you can visit the various nightclubs, bars and restaurants. The majority of the nightclubs and bars have been established since Ukraine declared its independence from Russia and started enjoying economic growth early in the 1990’s. In today’s Ukraine you will find a bustling democracy where the people want to become a part of the European Community (EU).

**Residents: **Nikolaev is also known as Mykolaiv and is located in southern Ukraine. Odessa and Kiev are its next-door neighbors. The young people there are enjoying their freedom and express it by enjoying the night life. It has a population of approximately 500,000 people. Shipbuilding is a major industry. Nikolaev also has a very famous zoo.

**Nightclubs: **There are various nightclubs to enjoy. One of the most popular is Illusion located on Sadovaya Street. Other popular night clubs in central Nikolaev are Storm, Delerium and Monarch.

Some nightclubs have a more genteel appearance with sofas and armchairs available for people to relax while most have a dance floor for visitors to enjoy. Some of the clubs are centrally air-conditioned. Plasma TVs can be watched by those who are more interested in a virtual world than the frenzy next door.

One of the most popular clubs, Storm night-club, has a casino and slot-machines where people can gamble to their heart’s content. It also has a bowling alley where customers can participate in bowling tournaments. Frequent visitors range in age from 16 to 45.

**Restaurants:** Many of these nightclubs have restaurants. Continental food with European influence is quite popular with some restaurants also offering Japanese and Arabic food. Hookah smoking is available at a price.

**Parties and Music: **Dance parties are held with very loud music played by disc jockeys. Some disc jockeys are as popular as pop stars. Music can range from Karaoke, Rhythm and Blues (R&B), Metal, Rap to Electro music.

Strip tease dances are also a common occurrence. There are barman shows where the barman plays with flares and fire while mixing cocktails. They have powerful laser lighting to light up the dance floors in the night clubs. Some also host regular performances by European stars. During special occasions, you may see big stars also performing here.
