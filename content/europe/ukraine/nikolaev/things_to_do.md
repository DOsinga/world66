---
title: "Things to do in Nikolaev"
type: section
---

Nikolaev is an industrial city that is well know for ship building. In **Nikolaev, things to do** include museums, zoos, yachting, parks and swimming. The city is located between two rivers called the South Bug and the Ingul.

**Nikolaev Zoo**

A visit to the zoo is a fun outing for the whole family. It also offers an educational experience for children. The zoo in Nikolaev is the largest zoo in Ukraine. It was founded in 1901 by Leontovich and it one of the oldest zoos in Ukraine. The zoo boasts 350 different species of animals and it is home to approximately 2500 animals.

**Theater of Ukranian Drama and Musical Comedy**

The theater was founded in 1959. The theater reflects Nikolaev's reputation of being Ukraine's city of culture and education. Visit the theater to be entertained by riveting performances.

**Astronomical Observatory**

Take a trip to the astronomical observatory to be dazzled by views of the night sky.

**Nightlife**

Nikolaev has many nighttime entertainment options. You can enjoy a delicious meal at one of the many restaurants, or a relaxing drink at a bar. For those looking for a vibrant party, nightclubs in the area offer an evening full of dancing and fun. Bowling clubs and casinos are other sources of entertainment. Sovietskaya street is the main street and is a good place to head for a variety of entertainment options. In this street you can find restaurants which offer cuisine from around the world.

**Swimming**

There are two swimming pools in the city called "Zarya" and "Vodoley". Enjoy a refreshing swim at the pools or do some laps to get a good work out. The pools are equipped with modern conveniences and offer extra activities.

**Children's Town Fairy Tale

**This children's playground is located in the center of Nikolaev. The playground has a good atmosphere, and is a place for children to have fun and make new friends. Attractions include a locomotive and a ship. There is also a magical castle where children can play. The laughter room is equipped with false mirrors to keep your children entertained. Other activities are swings, merry go rounds and trampolines. For refreshments, visit Fairy tale cafe which is close to the playground.

**Parks**

The well kept parks offer a place to go for a walk or have a picnic. Among the many parks are Victory Park, Shevchenko park and Petrovsky Park. At the parks you can see trees, flowers and monuments.
