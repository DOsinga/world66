---
title: "Eating Out"
type: section
---

Birmingham is one of the best cities to eat in outside London. The city's diversity drives its food scene -- you can eat superb Kashmiri, Cantonese, Vietnamese, and Caribbean food here without trying hard. The Balti Triangle along Ladypool Road is the essential Birmingham food experience: over forty restaurants serving freshly prepared baltis at reasonable prices. The Chinatown district around Hurst Street has solid Chinese and Southeast Asian options. For something more upscale, the city centre has a growing number of ambitious restaurants.
