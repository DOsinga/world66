**Explore the Various Birmingham Caf&eacute;s**

You should stop by at one of these **Birmingham bars and caf&eacute;s** to enjoy a great evening. Birmingham is a city in West Midlands County, England. It is the most popular destination apart from London. Here are a few suggestions to help you enjoy your stay in Birmingham.

#### **Victoria Square Caf&eacute; **

Victoria Square Caf&eacute; is a place where you can enjoy a great evening of food and drinks and make some friends at the same time. Try the tomato and bacon sandwich while sipping on your favorite cup of coffee. If you want to try something different, go for the "Gluvine." This is a mug of warm German spiced wine. The caf&eacute; is a great place to enjoy an evening with your friends.

#### **Apres Bar**

Apres is a bar/grill/club that oozes class and style. If you are looking for a place to celebrate, network or just have a good time, Apres is the best place for you. The bar is open from 9 a.m. to 9 p.m. and has a great variety of delicious bar food. The legendary chili chicken wings are a must-try at this bar. The bar also has a wide selection of wine, beer and cocktails. If beer is what gets you going, you can try the continental style 4-pint pitchers together with your friends. There are also several exotic cocktails available. Just remember their &ldquo;happy hour&rdquo; is from 3 p.m. to 8 p.m., and this is when you can make the best of all the drinks.
