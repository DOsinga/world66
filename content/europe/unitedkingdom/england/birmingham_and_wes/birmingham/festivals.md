**Enjoy a Great Vacation in Birmingham**

You might want to take note of these **Birmingham festivals** if you are planning a trip to this amazing city in England. This is one of the most popular destinations in the United Kingdom. The city has 2 interesting music festivals that take place right through the year.

#### **Moseley Folk Festival**

The Moseley Folk Festival takes place in the month of September each year. This festival has been an exciting and popular festival for the last five years. The festival centers on music with traditional, contemporary, acoustic and experimental styles being played. The festival includes artists such as Donovan, Ukulele Orchestra of Great Britain, Spider John Koerner and many others. If you are in Birmingham during the month of September, this is one festival you do not want to miss.

#### **Birmingham International Jazz Festival**

The Birmingham International Jazz Festival is another popular event. If you love jazz, you will truly enjoy this festival. The jazz festival takes place across 70 venues in and around the city and across the region. If you like to see some great performances by world-class jazz artists, this is the festival to attend. The festival also includes some blues and swing music, which gives the festival a slight twist and makes it more appealing to music lovers. If you want to give your vacation a party atmosphere, visit Birmingham during the month of July.
