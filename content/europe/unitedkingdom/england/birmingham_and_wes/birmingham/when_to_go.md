**When to Visit ****Birmingham******

If you are planning a visit to **Birmingham**, **when to go** is probably what you are considering. Birmingham is suitable for a visit throughout the year, as it offers many indoor activities that don&rsquo;t depend on the weather. If you want to make the most of all the Birmingham outdoor attractions, you&rsquo;ll have to visit the city in the summer. The weather is warmer, and there are a lot of events.

#### **Birmingham**** throughout the Year**

Throughout the year, Birmingham impresses with its numerous art galleries and museums. Whenever it is raining, you can just go inside one of them and enjoy some marvelous artwork, ranging from modern to antique. Admission to a number of these places is either free or at a low price. The city attracts tourists with its shopping opportunities, and all the shops are open throughout the year.

#### **Birmingham**** in the Summer**

There are several things that make a difference in Birmingham during the summer. Families can have a long day of family fun taking one of the guided sightseeing walks and bus tours, which are only possible due to the good summer weather. The city&rsquo;s open spaces turn into festival arenas, hosting some interesting events like the Mostly Jazz Festival in July, the Moseley Folk Festival in August, the Six Summer Saturdays and the Heritage Open Day in early September.
