---
title: "Eating Out"
type: section
---

Coventry's eating scene is much improved and reflects the city's diverse population. The Foleshill Road corridor is the place to go for South Asian food — it's one of the better strips of Pakistani and Indian restaurants outside Birmingham, and prices are low. The city centre has the usual high-street chains but also a growing number of independent places.

For pub food, the city has some decent medieval pubs in the older parts of the centre. The Old Windmill on Spon Street is a genuine 15th-century timber-framed building serving straightforward food and real ales.
