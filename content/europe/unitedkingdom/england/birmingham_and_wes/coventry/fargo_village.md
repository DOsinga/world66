Coventry's most interesting eating and drinking destination, occupying a stretch of Far Gosford Street about a fifteen-minute walk east of the centre. The Village occupies former railway arches and a cluster of repurposed warehouses and containers, with a mix of independent shops, studios, and food traders. The Factory indoor food market has five permanent traders alongside an outdoor terrace, offering a range of cuisines — burgers, pizza, Asian noodles, and more — plus Twisted Barrel Ale, a craft brewery that operates on site and serves its own beers. Busiest at weekends; worth the walk.
