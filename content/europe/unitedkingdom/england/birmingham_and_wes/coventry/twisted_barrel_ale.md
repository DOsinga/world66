Coventry's best-known craft brewery, operating out of FarGo Village in the former railway arches on Far Gosford Street. They brew their own beers on site and serve them in the taproom, alongside guest ales and a range of bar snacks. The atmosphere is relaxed and unpretentious — it tends to attract a mixed crowd of students, locals, and visitors who've wandered over from the city centre. A good place to spend an afternoon, especially when the outdoor terrace is open.
