Travel Guide

Walsall is a town suburb of the Birmingham area. Full of high street shops, a market and various museums. Hold on to your wallet because otherwise it probably will not be there when you intend on leaving. If that has not attracted you to our town then the various unemployed people walking round the town with nothing to do but cause trouble for the people will drive you even further away. This town needs sorting. And quick.
