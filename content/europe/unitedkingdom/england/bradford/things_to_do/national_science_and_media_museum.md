One of the best free museums in the north of England. The collection covers the history of photography, film, television, and the internet, with hands-on exhibits that work for adults as well as children. The IMAX cinema is one of the largest screens in the country. It's located in a prominent 1980s building in the city centre, right on Bradford's pedestrianised core.
