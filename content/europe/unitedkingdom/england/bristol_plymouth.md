England's south-west is where the country gets wild. The coastline dominates, from the golden beaches of [Bournemouth](/europe/unitedkingdom/england/bristol_plymouth/bournemouth) and [Cornwall](/europe/unitedkingdom/england/bristol_plymouth/cornwall) to the dramatic cliffs of [Exmoor](/europe/unitedkingdom/england/bristol_plymouth/exmoor) and north [Devon](/europe/unitedkingdom/england/bristol_plymouth/devon). But there is far more than coast here.

[Bristol](/europe/unitedkingdom/england/bristol_plymouth/bristol) is the region's largest city, a creative, lively port with a long maritime history. [Bath](/europe/unitedkingdom/england/bristol_plymouth/bath) offers some of England's finest Georgian architecture and Roman remains. The cathedrals at [Exeter](/europe/unitedkingdom/england/bristol_plymouth/devon/exeter), [Wells](/europe/unitedkingdom/england/bristol_plymouth/wells), [Salisbury](/europe/unitedkingdom/england/bristol_plymouth/salisbury) and [Gloucester](/europe/unitedkingdom/england/bristol_plymouth/gloucestershire/gloucester) are among the best in the country.

Two national parks cover the moors: [Dartmoor](/europe/unitedkingdom/england/bristol_plymouth/devon/dartmoor) with its granite tors and Bronze Age remains, and [Exmoor](/europe/unitedkingdom/england/bristol_plymouth/exmoor) with its red deer and wooded valleys. The New Forest, straddling the border with Hampshire, became a national park in 2005. There are prehistoric sites at [Stonehenge](/europe/unitedkingdom/england/bristol_plymouth/stonehenge) and [Avebury](/europe/unitedkingdom/england/bristol_plymouth/avebury), splendid gardens open to the public, and the Scilly Isles off the tip of [Cornwall](/europe/unitedkingdom/england/bristol_plymouth/cornwall).

The [Cotswolds](/europe/unitedkingdom/england/bristol_plymouth/gloucestershire/cotswolds) in [Gloucestershire](/europe/unitedkingdom/england/bristol_plymouth/gloucestershire) offer honey-stone villages and gentle rolling hills. [Glastonbury](/europe/unitedkingdom/england/bristol_plymouth/glastonbury) has its abbey ruins and mystical associations. And [Swindon](/europe/unitedkingdom/england/bristol_plymouth/swindon), while not the region's beauty spot, has a notable railway museum and serves as a gateway to the Wiltshire countryside.
