**Cafe Retro** -- 18 York St. A popular, relaxed cafe in the centre of town.

**The Bridge Coffee Shop** -- 17 Pulteney Bridge. Coffee with a view over the weir.

**Bath Bun Tea Room** -- 3 Lilliput Court, North Parade Passage. Traditional tea room.

**Green Cat Cafe** -- 18 Northumberland Place.

**Riverside Juice Bar** -- 18 The Podium, Northgate St. Tel: 01225 444457.

**Metropolitan Cafe** -- 15 New Bond St. Tel: 01225 482680.

**Scoffs Wholefood Bakery & Cafe** -- 19-20 Kingsmead Square. Tel: 01225 462483.

**Kingwell Cafe** -- Timsbury, a few miles outside Bath. Tel: 01761 470417.
