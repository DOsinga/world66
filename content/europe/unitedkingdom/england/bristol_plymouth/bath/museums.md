---
title: 'Museums'
type: section
---

**The Jane Austen Centre** -- 40 Gay St. Tel: 01225 443000. A celebration of Bath's most famous literary resident, exploring how the city influenced her life and work.

**Holburne Museum of Art** -- Great Pulteney St. Tel: 01225 466669. Fine and decorative arts in a beautiful setting at the end of Great Pulteney Street.

**No. 1 Royal Crescent** -- 1 Royal Crescent. Tel: 01225 428126. A beautifully restored Georgian townhouse showing how the wealthy lived in 18th-century Bath.

**The American Museum in Britain** -- Claverton Manor. Tel: 01225 460503. American decorative arts from the 17th to 19th centuries, set in a manor house outside the city.

**Bath Postal Museum** -- 8 Broad St. The story of the postal service, housed where the first letter with a Penny Black stamp was posted in 1840.

**William Herschel Museum** -- 19 New King St. Tel: 01225 311342. The house where the astronomer discovered Uranus in 1781.

**The Museum of East Asian Art** -- 12 Bennett St. Tel: 01225 464640.

**Museum of Costume** -- Bennett St. Tel: 01225 477789. Now known as the Fashion Museum, housed in the Assembly Rooms.

**Building of Bath Museum** -- Countess of Huntingdon Chapel, The Vineyards.

**Bath Industrial Heritage Centre** -- Camden Works, Julian Rd. Tel: 01225 318348.

**Beckford's Tower & Museum** -- Lansdown Rd. Tel: 01225 422212.
