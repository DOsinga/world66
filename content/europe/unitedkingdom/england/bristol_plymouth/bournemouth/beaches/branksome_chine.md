Branksome beach is about a half hour walk along the promenade from Bournemouth pier. The beach offers a top class restaurant overlooking the sea, toilets, parking, lifeguard and a roped off area in the summer for safe bathing. A popular spot for windsurfers and paddleboarders. The Bournemouth weather is very mild for most of the year, but when the cold North wind blows Branksome is a particularly good place to go as you are well sheltered by the high cliffs.
