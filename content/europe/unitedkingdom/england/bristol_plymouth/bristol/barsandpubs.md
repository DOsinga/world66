**The Lion, Cliftonwood** -- Church Lane, Cliftonwood. A friendly local pub with good pub food, tucked away among the brightly coloured houses of Cliftonwood.

**Hope and Anchor** -- 38 Jacobs Wells Rd. Tel: 0117 929 2987. A good pub selling local beer from Bath Ales and decent food.

**Finnegans Wake** -- 31-35 Cotham Hill, Cotham. Tel: 0117 973 3793.

**Rose of Denmark** -- 6 Dowry Place, Hotwells. Tel: 0117 940 5866.

**Cambridge Arms** -- Coldharbour Rd, Redland. Tel: 0117 973 5754.

**Rat & Parrot** -- 71-73 Whiteladies Rd. Tel: 0117 973 0534.

**Post Office Tavern** -- 17 Westbury Hill, Westbury-on-Trym. Tel: 0117 940 1233.
