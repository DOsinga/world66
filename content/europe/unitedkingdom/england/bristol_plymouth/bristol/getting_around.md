---
title: 'Getting Around'
type: section
---

Central Bristol is walkable — the harbourside, old city, and Clifton are connected by a compact grid, though Clifton sits on a steep hill. Buses run by First West of England cover the rest of the city and the suburbs. The MetroBus rapid routes link the centre to Cribbs Causeway and south Bristol. Ferries on the floating harbour are scenic rather than fast. Cycling is well catered for with the harbourside path and the Bristol and Bath Railway Path.
