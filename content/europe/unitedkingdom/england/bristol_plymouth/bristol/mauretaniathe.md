A Bristol institution on Park Street, named after the Cunard ocean liner. The Mauretania has been a popular drinking spot for decades, drawing a mix of university students and locals to its ornate, slightly faded interior. The building itself is worth a look, with decorative tiling and period details. Park Street's steep slope means you'll have earned your pint by the time you walk up from the city centre.
