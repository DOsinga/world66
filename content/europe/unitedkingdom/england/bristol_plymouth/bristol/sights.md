**Cabot Tower** -- Brandon Hill. A Bristol landmark visible from many parts of the city. At night its beacon flashes a message in Morse code spelling B-R-I-S-T-O-L. The 100-foot tower commemorates John Cabot's voyage to America in 1497, when he discovered Newfoundland.

**Clifton Suspension Bridge** -- Brunel's iconic bridge spanning the Avon Gorge. One of the most photographed sights in the south-west.
