---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Bristol's highlights cluster around the harbour and the gorge. Brunel's Clifton Suspension Bridge and the SS Great Britain anchor the engineering heritage, while Cabot Tower on Brandon Hill gives the best view over the city. Bristol Museum & Art Gallery is free and covers everything from Egyptian mummies to Banksy; M Shed on the quayside tells the story of the city itself. Allow a morning for the harbourside and an afternoon for Clifton village.
