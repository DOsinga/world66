This claims to be England's highest town. It was once connected to Yelverton by a branch line of the Great Western Railway's line from
[Plymouth](/europe/unitedkingdom/england/bristol_plymouth/devon/plymouth) to
[Tavistock](/europe/unitedkingdom/england/bristol_plymouth/devon/tavistock), which wound remorselessly round some lesser tors. Ingra Tor halt must have been one of the bleakest in England.

The town still has the high security Dartmoor Prison which was built by French and American prisoners of war.

Every road around here is a scenic delight.
