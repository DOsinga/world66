Above all, Dartmoor is a place for walking. It is not everywhere that you can look out to so many scenic hill tops [tors] and then devise a walk to take in all of them.

If you want more guidance, there is an outstanding website: http://www.plymouthramblers.org.uk/dartmor2.html
