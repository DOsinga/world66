Newton Abbot currently has one local museum although plans and been around for sometime to convert disused engine sheds near the station into a railway museum.

### [Newton Abbot Town & G.W.R Museum](/europe/unitedkingdom/england/bristol_plymouth/devon/newtonabbot/museums/newton_abbot_town)

The towns only museum shows the changing life of Newtonians through the centuries. Admission is free and exhibits include Aller Vale pottery and many Great Western artefacts.
