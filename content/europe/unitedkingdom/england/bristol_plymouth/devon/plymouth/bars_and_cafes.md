Plymouth's pub scene is rooted in its naval history — Union Street was once the city's notorious sailor strip. The Barbican has the most atmospheric options: old pubs in Tudor buildings around the harbour. Keyham and Stonehouse have working-local pubs with less tourist traffic.

### [Avondale Arms](/europe/unitedkingdom/england/bristol_plymouth/devon/plymouth/bars_and_cafes/avondalearms)

address: Keyham Rd 206

tel: 01752 561188

### [Jesters](/europe/unitedkingdom/england/bristol_plymouth/devon/plymouth/bars_and_cafes/jesters)

A notorious Union Street club that caters to sailors, soldiers, and late-night regulars. Not for the faint-hearted, but a genuine piece of Plymouth nightlife.
