---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Plymouth's sights cluster around two areas: the Barbican, where the Elizabethan waterfront has survived intact, and Plymouth Hoe, the grassy headland above it. The rebuilt city centre has less to offer, but Drake Circus holds the city museum. Allow a full day to cover the waterfront properly.

The City Museum and Art Gallery on Drake Circus covers Plymouth's history from its Tudor heyday through the Blitz and beyond. The Park Pharmacy Trust at Thorn Park preserves a rare intact Victorian pharmacy interior.
