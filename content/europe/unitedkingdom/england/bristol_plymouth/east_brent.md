East Brent is a small village in the Axbridge area of Somerset, about seven miles south-east of Weston-super-Mare. The village church of St Mary is notable for its medieval tower, which bears carved figures said to represent King Ina of the West Saxons and his queen. The manor was originally given by Ina to Glastonbury Abbey and held by the monks until the dissolution of the monasteries.
