This is where you will find Glastonbury Abbey, where it is said King Arthur was once buried, as well as the Glastonbury Tor -- the tower on the hill that was supposedly known as Avalon. And the Holy Grail was said to have been here too. Drink from the Chalice Well spring and feel rejuvenated. Glastonbury should not be missed on your visit to England.
