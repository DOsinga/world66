---
title: "Llanthony Secunda Priory"
type: poi
category: Sight
latitude: 51.8548
longitude: -2.2574
---

The ruined outer court of an Augustinian priory founded in 1136, established by canons fleeing Welsh raiders from the original Llanthony Abbey in the Brecon Beacons. What survives includes a fifteenth-century tithe barn and some late medieval storage buildings, set in grounds that now serve as a nature reserve.

It sits tucked away near the docks, easily missed, but worth the short detour if you're in the area.
