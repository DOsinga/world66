---
title: 'Sights in Weymouth'
type: section
---

**Chesil Beach** -- A remarkable 18-mile shingle bank stretching from [Portland](/europe/unitedkingdom/england/bristol_plymouth) to West Bay. The pebbles are naturally graded by size, getting larger towards Portland. It is part of the Jurassic Coast, a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

**Weymouth Harbour** -- A lively working harbour with fishing boats, cafes and restaurants along the quayside.
