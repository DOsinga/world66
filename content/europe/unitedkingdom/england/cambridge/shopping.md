**Shops**

**All Saints Garden Craft Fair - Saturdays**

Local artists and craftspeople who make what they sell.Entrance is free, and many of the craftspeople can be seen in action. Held at All Saints Garden, opposite Trinity College, every Saturday throughout the year from 10am to 5pm.

  Regulars include photographers, painters, potters, ceramic a [more..](/europe/unitedkingdom/england/cambridge/shopping/allsaintsgardencraftfairsaturdays)

**Cambridge Farmers' Market - Sundays**

Held in Market Square each Sunday throughout the year, from 10:30am.

**Cambridge Art, Craft and Antique Market - Sundays**

Held every Sunday throughout the year from 10:20am. Busiest times are two to three months before Christmas - there may be disappointingly few stalls the rest of the year. A great shame considering the extremely high quality of the goods and variety of crafts available.

**Fisher Hall Craft Market - Saturdays**

Held every Saturday throughout the year in Fisher Hall near Market Square. A very good turnout each week of local craftspeople, ranging from metal puzzles to etched and painted silk velvet goods. Pottery, woodwork, gems, watercolours, photographs, gems and jewellery and a whole lot more.
