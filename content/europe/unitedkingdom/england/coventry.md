---
title: "Coventry"
type: location
---

Defines the phrase 'concrete jungle'. Bleak and soulless. What the German bombers failed to destroy in WWII the city planners have managed to destroy in the interim. Voted as the dirtiest city in Britain, Coventry people aren't unfriendly, they're downright hostile. After dark the city centre is deserted apart from drunks, druggies and lunatics. The pubs are violent, good restauraunts are non-existent. Avoid at all cost.
