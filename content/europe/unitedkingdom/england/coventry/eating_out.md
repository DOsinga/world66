---
title: "Eating Out"
type: section
---

Coventry's food scene has improved markedly since the City of Culture year and continues to evolve. FarGo Village on Far Gosford Street is the liveliest spot for eating, with street food stalls and independent traders operating from converted railway arches and a purpose-built indoor market called The Factory. The city centre has the usual chain restaurants around Broadgate, but the independent options are worth seeking out.
