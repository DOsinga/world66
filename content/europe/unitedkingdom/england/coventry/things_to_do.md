---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Coventry's main sights cluster in a small area around the two cathedrals — you can cover the essential ground in a half-day on foot. The Transport Museum, a ten-minute walk west, deserves a separate block of time. Beyond the obvious, St Mary's Guildhall is one of the most underrated medieval buildings in England, and the Priory ruins just off Priory Row add another layer to the city's long history.
