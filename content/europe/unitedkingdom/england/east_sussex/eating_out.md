The Hungry Monk

An old house in East Sussex, England has been converted into the Hungry Monk, a restaurant that offers a cozy atmosphere and high quality, creative dishes. Before your meal, relax in the sitting room with a drink by the stone fireplace and notice the low ceilings. Move on to the intimate main dining room or your own private dining room for dinner, where you can select an appetizer, main course, and desert from their fixed-price seasonal menu. Be sure to try their famous Banoffi Pie and take home a cookbook to try some recipes out for yourself!
