Eastern England stretches from just outside London to the North Sea and includes historic towns such as [Cambridge](/europe/unitedkingdom/england/eastern_england/cambridge), [Luton](/europe/unitedkingdom/england/eastern_england/luton) and [Ipswich](/europe/unitedkingdom/england/eastern_england/ipswich). There are fine cathedrals at [Ely](/europe/unitedkingdom/england/eastern_england/ely), [Peterborough](/europe/unitedkingdom/england/eastern_england/peterborough) and [Norwich](/europe/unitedkingdom/england/eastern_england/norwich). 

The beautiful East Anglian countryside is another reason to come, although further east in the area the flat scenery is not too exciting.
