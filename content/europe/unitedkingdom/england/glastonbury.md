This is where you'll find the Glastonbury Abbey where it is said King Arthur was once buried, as well as the location of the Glastonbury Tor; the Tower on the thill that was supposedly known as Avalon. And let's not forget that the Holy Grail was here. Drink from the spring and feel reguvenated. Glastonbury should not be missed on your visit to England.
