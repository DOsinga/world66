A free local history museum in Ravensknowle Park, covering Huddersfield's history from prehistoric times through its textile industry heyday. The collections include archaeology, natural history, and an extensive display on the wool and weaving trades that made the town prosperous. Compact and unpretentious, it's the kind of local museum that tells you more about a place than a thousand tourist brochures.
