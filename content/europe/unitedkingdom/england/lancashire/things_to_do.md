### [The Blackpool Tower Dungeon](/europe/unitedkingdom/england/lancashire/things_to_do/the_blackpool_towe)

The Blackpool Tower Dungeon is one of the must-see signature attractions of the Blackpool resort. With a full cast of brilliant actors, 10 shows, rich sets, special effects and an exciting drop ride, it's a 60 minute journey through 1000 years of Lancashire’s murky past. You and your companions walk through the Dungeon, moving from show to show, guided by our professional, theatrical actors.
