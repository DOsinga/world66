The historic city of
[Ripon](/europe/unitedkingdom/england/leeds_sheffield_a/ripon) and
[York](/europe/unitedkingdom/england/leeds_sheffield_a/york), the
[Yorkshire Dales](/europe/unitedkingdom/england/leeds_sheffield_a/yorkshire_dales), the coast and the North York Moors are waiting for you in the North of England. 

[Leeds](/europe/unitedkingdom/england/leeds_sheffield_a/leeds) and
[The Shoals - Florence, Tuscumbia, Sheffield and Muscle Shoals](/northamerica/unitedstates/alabama/the_shoals__flore) are the biggest cities in the region. Of the others
[Bradford](/europe/unitedkingdom/england/leeds_sheffield_a/bradford) is particularly worth a visit. They are big towns with many things to do, places to eat and nightlife opportunities.
