On the road to Queensbury is the Mumtaz Restaurant, not just another one for food of the sub continent but one of the very best, more expensive than the average for Bradford's prolific Asian restaurants but not expensive for the quality of its food. It has a separate takeaway section.

The Mumtaz has become a major supplier of English shops including ones as far apart as harrods in London and the Hebden Bridge Co-op in Calderdale
