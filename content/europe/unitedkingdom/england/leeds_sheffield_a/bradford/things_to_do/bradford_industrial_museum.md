Housed in a former spinning mill in the Eccleshill district, the Bradford Industrial Museum centres on working wool-textile machinery — spinning, weaving, and combing equipment that still runs on demonstration days. The mill manager's house and back-to-back workers' cottages have been preserved alongside, giving a complete picture of the industrial-era social hierarchy. One of England's first industrial museums, it shows its age in places but remains a genuine and informative collection.
