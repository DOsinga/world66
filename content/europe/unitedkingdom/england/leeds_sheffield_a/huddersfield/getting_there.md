---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

Huddersfield has a direct rail connection to Leeds (20 minutes) and Manchester Piccadilly (about 40 minutes), making it an easy day trip from either city. The station itself — a classical portico designed by James Pigott Pritchett in 1850 — is worth a moment of attention when you arrive. National Express coaches also serve the town from London and other major cities.
