title: Kirklees Light Railway
type: poi
---

A narrow-gauge steam railway running through the South Pennine countryside between Clayton West and Shelley. The 15-inch gauge locomotives are small but the scenery is genuine — rolling hills, old stone walls, and the quiet of the valley. A good half-day out, especially for families, and a tangible reminder of the industrial heritage that once stitched this landscape together.
