Skipton, just outside the [Yorkshire Dales](/europe/unitedkingdom/england/leeds_sheffield_a/yorkshire_dales) to the south, prides itself on being 'the Gateway to the Dales.' It is an attractive town with an ancient castle and, on market days, market stalls on both sides of the very wide main street. 

The town lies on the Leeds-Liverpool Canal and good boat trips are available.
