**Youth Hostels**

Youth Hostels in the South Pennines are located at Earby, Haworth and Mankinholes [Todmorden.]

Visit http://www.yha.org.uk/Stay_at_YHA/Regional/The_Yorkshire_Dales_and_South_Pennines.html then click on the hostel you want.
