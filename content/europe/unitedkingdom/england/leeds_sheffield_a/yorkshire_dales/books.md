### [Walking the Dales](/europe/unitedkingdom/england/leeds_sheffield_a/yorkshire_dales/books/walking_the_dales)

This is a book by Mike Harding, an entertainer known as 'The Rochdale Cowboy', who has been seen a lot on TV. It isn't a conventional guidebook but you can get any number of excellent ideas about things to see and do in the Dales.

[](http://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/tg/detail/-/0-7181-2701-3/ref=ase_osingacom?v=glance&s=books)

### [A Dales Album](/europe/unitedkingdom/england/leeds_sheffield_a/yorkshire_dales/books/a_dales_album)

The authors have written numerous books on the Dales and any of them are worth reading [and probably possessing if you are a local]. This one is a pictorial album of life in the Dales.

Chapter headings relate to things as diverse as industry, religion and transport.

[](http://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/tg/detail/-/0-460-86023-2/ref=ase_osingacom?v=glance&s=books)



author: Marie Hartley and Joan Ingilby

isbn: 0-460-86023-2
