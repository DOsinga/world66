**Youth Hostels**

Youth Hostels in the Yorkshire Dales are located at Bishopdale Valley, Dentdale, , Grinton Lodge, Hawes, Ingleton, Keld, Kettlewell, , Malham and. Stainforth.

Visit http://www.yha.org.uk/Stay_at_YHA/Regional/The_Yorkshire_Dales_and_South_Pennines.html then click on the hostel you want.
