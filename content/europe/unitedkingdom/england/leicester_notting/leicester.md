At first sight one of those industrial cities again, seriously damaged during the war and a everything but booming industrial life. However, the city has some interesting museums, quite a nice nightlife scene and a colourful Asian area. The city was founded in Roman times and some of the Roman ruins can be visited. If you've had it,
[York](/europe/unitedkingdom/england/leeds_sheffield_a/york) and
[Birmingham](/europe/unitedkingdom/england/birmingham_and_wes/birmingham) can be easily reached by either bus or train.
