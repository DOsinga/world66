---
title: "Bars and Cafes"
type: section
---

Nottingham has a deserved reputation for its pub and bar scene — the city centre packs in an extraordinary number of drinking establishments for its size, ranging from Victorian real-ale pubs to the student-oriented bars clustered around the universities. The Lace Market and Hockley neighbourhoods are the best areas to explore in the evening, with a mix of independent bars and live music venues sitting alongside the older boozers.
