London's oldest food market — there has been a market here since the twelfth century — and still one of its best. Come on Thursday, Friday, or Saturday morning for artisan bread, aged cheese, charcuterie, fresh pasta, rare-breed meat, and an overwhelming amount of street food. It's busy and a bit cramped, but genuinely excellent. The surrounding streets of Bermondsey and Southwark are worth wandering afterwards.

Borough High Street, Southwark. Thursday–Saturday.
