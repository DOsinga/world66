Fujiyama is a Japanese noodle bar, the food isn't vastly different from somewhere like Wagamama's but here it's much smaller and friendlier. It's very good value for money, for about £6 you get a plate of noodles that will fill up any big appetite.

They do ramen, bento boxes, don buri, miso soups, pan fried noodles and various curry and rice dishes. They also do some freshly squeezed juices along with the usual beers, wines and sake.

From Brixton station walk down Atlantic Road, and take a right after the Dog Star.
