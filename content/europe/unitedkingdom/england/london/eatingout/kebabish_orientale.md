The classic kebab shop is done one better by the servic of Mr. Akhtar and friends at this Willesden Green establishment. Regular customers can expect to establish a friendly rapport with these warm and welcoming people. Their Ckicken donar is excellent, and the nihiri with its lovely brown sauce and tender lamb meat chicken tikka is not to be missed. If you want a kebab shop, of which there are literally tens of thousands in London, be sure to seek this one out. 32 High Road in Willesden Green on the Jubilee line.
