Takes place in the city of London (the old bit) near the Bank tube stop, and outside of the Lord Mayor's house. Based on an old tradition of welcoming the Lord Mayor into the city and his new home. Many different floats and performers, and large crowds. In 2006 national acrobats from hong kong performed.

In 2008 the Lord Mayor's Show is on Saturday 8 November.
