An oral history of the city at the turn of the twenty-first century, assembled from interviews with more than two hundred Londoners: tube drivers, club promoters, immigrant workers, night-shift nurses, market traders, hedge fund managers. More than any single novel, this book gives you the texture of the city as actually lived. Read it on the Tube.
