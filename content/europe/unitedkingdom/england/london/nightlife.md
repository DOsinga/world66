Traditional old London taverns can be found allover the city, the seventeenth-century George Inn at Borough High Street or The Crown at New Oxford Street are among the most noticeable, but in the bar section there are a lot of alternatives. London’s nightlife is concentrated in West End, especially around Soho, which is still the main hangout for the hip and famous. It’s also the center of the gay scene.
