Very big clug, cheap drinks, different nights available, sat Trance, Tues alternitive ect. Good atmosphere, some live bands on Tuesdays.** Jay:** Camden Palace, or Peach as its known for Friday nights, is one the freekiest places Ive been 2! It used to be an opera theatre, and can get 1,500 clubbers inside. The dance floor is a good size and has a complex maze of corridors taking you around the club.
 From the outside you expect the inside to be like the London Dungeons, well.. hmm. It is not as clean as other clubs, and drugs are a major problem here, though authorities choose to ignore it.
 Friday nights are the best (Peach) with Djs such as Graham Gold, Ferry Corston, Brandon Block playing regulary. Music is progressive trance, as well as a small garage room, which is about the size of a small bar.
 There is only one dance floor, but there are many levels that offer a great view of the talent grooving below.
 The area is generally safe, but avoid the back streets and estates and you should get home alive.
 Drinks aren infact not cheap, do cost a bit when you find your wallet empty after a while.
 The club goes on till 6.30 am, and does tend to hurt the ears.
