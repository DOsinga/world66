Gatwick Airport (IATA: LGW, ICAO: EGKK) is London's second airport and
the second busiest airport in the UK after Heathrow in terms of
passengers per year. It is located between Horley in Surrey and Crawley
in West Sussex, approximately 40 km (25 miles) south of London, and an
equal distance north of Brighton.
