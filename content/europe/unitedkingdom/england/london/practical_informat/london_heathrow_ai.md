London Heathrow Airport (IATA: LHR, ICAO: EGLL), often referred to as
Heathrow, is the United Kingdom's busiest and best-connected airport.
It is the busiest airport in Europe and by international passenger
traffic is the busiest international airport in the world. By total
passenger traffic it is the world's third-busiest airport. Heathrow is
located in the London Borough of Hillingdon, 15 miles (24 km) west of
Charing Cross in Central London.
