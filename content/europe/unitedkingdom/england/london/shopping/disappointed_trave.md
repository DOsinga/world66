The British Boot Company in Camden Town claims that they are the original shop and the only shop selling real Dr. Martens boots. The price they charge is much higher than the other shoes shops, the onlineshop directed by the official Dr. Martens website, as well as the REAL Dr. Martens shop. They tend to provide false information. For example, they would tell a customer the pair of boots are the new release model of the year but in fact, they are the old model. Also, the price given from one staff can be different from another staff, and also different (higher) than the listed price on their own website. They don't let tourists to claim VAT if you buy boots with small size as they said the the government sees small size shoes as children shoes for school and so would not allow to claim VAT. Their dishonest and overcharge behaviour should be seriously be looked at because they ruins the reputation of London as well as one of the most famous brands, Dr. Martens.
