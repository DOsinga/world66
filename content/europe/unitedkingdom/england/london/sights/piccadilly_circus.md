Piccadilly Circus is a famous traffic intersection and public space of London's West End in the City of Westminster.

People watching at Piccadilly Circus is one of the top free things to do in London; local superstition says, you will see someone you know within 15 minutes of hanging on the south side of the Circus near the famous Eros statue.
