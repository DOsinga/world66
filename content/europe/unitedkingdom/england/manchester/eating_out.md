type: section
---

Quite frankly speaking, Manchester comes close to being the Walhallah of food. No matter what sort of cuisine you're looking for, you'll probably find it here. Indian restaurants are concentrated along the " Curry Mile",Wilmslow Road, Rusholme. Excellent Chinese food can be eaten in one of the many restaurants in Chinatown, the area behind Piccadilly Gardens. Pubs offer good daily menus that you can enjoy with a pint of lager. If you happen to be in a hurry, and you don't want to spend your time on eating out, there are plenty of opportunities to grab a kebab, burger or eat a large portion of fish and chips. Many of them stay open quite late, so if you happen to be hungry after you're experienced the exciting nightlife of Manchester, there's a fair chance that you won't have to search that long to find a place where you can appease your hunger.
