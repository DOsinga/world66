title: 'Things to Do'
type: section
---

**Carlisle Castle** has stood guard over the city since the 11th century. The keep, built by Henry II, contains a fascinating exhibition on the castle's military history, including its role in the border wars between England and Scotland. The castle is managed by English Heritage.

**Carlisle Cathedral**, founded in 1122, is one of England's smallest cathedrals but has a beautifully painted ceiling in the choir and fine medieval stained glass in the east window.
