---
title: "Sights in Clitheroe"
type: section
---

One sight is the view out from Clitheroe towards Pendle Hill but pride of place in the town must go to Clitheroe castle, one of the oldest in Lancashire [12th century] and the sole remaining one in the county to have had a royalist garrison during the civil war.
