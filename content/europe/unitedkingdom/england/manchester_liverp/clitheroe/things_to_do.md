The prime sight in Clitheroe is the view out towards Pendle Hill, but pride of place goes to **Clitheroe Castle**, one of the oldest in Lancashire. Dating from the 12th century, it is the only castle in the county to have held a Royalist garrison during the Civil War. The keep, though small, sits on a limestone crag with commanding views over the Ribble Valley.
