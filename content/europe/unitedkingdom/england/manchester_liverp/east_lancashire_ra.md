This preserved steam railway runs from Heywood in Greater Manchester through [Bury](/europe/unitedkingdom/england/manchester_liverp) and Ramsbottom to Rawtenstall. It is one of the finest heritage railways in the north of England, with regular steam and diesel services, themed events throughout the year and a chance to experience the golden age of rail travel.
