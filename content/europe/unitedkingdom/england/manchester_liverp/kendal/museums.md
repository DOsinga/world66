The **Museum of Lakeland Life**, an award winner, is located in a fine Georgian building, **Abbot’s Hall**, which also houses a prestigious art gallery. 

www.lakelandmuseum.org.uk. 

http://www.abbothall.org.uk/thegallery.shtml 

Only a short distance away along the river is the **Kendal Museum** with fine collections on natural history and archaeology. One gallery here is devoted to Alfred Wainwright. 

www.kendalmuseum.org.uk.
