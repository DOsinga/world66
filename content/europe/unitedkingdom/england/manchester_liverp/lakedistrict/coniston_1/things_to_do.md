It seems odd that in a district with so many natural lakes people should flock to see an artificial tarn, but **Tarn Hows**, owned by the National Trust, is one of Lakeland's most popular views. The roads are so narrow that they have become one-way, and there is an easy walk around the tarn, surrounded by peaks.

### Brantwood

John Ruskin lived all his later life at Brantwood. He possibly thought he was paying too much for a rather dilapidated property but reckoned it to be the best-situated house in the whole country for its views.

His tastes in refurbishing and furnishing the house reflected his closeness to the Arts and Crafts Movement. The house is still full of his original furniture, feeling much as though it were still his home. It sits on the eastern side of Coniston Water. Coniston, Cumbria. Tel: 015394 41396.
