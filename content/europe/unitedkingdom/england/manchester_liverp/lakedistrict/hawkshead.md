Hawkshead is one of the prettiest villages in the Lake District, well known for its associations with the poet William Wordsworth and for the Beatrix Potter Gallery.

The village has picturesque houses and narrow lanes and is as good a base as anywhere for a first visit to the district, as long as you have a car. Public transport is very limited.
