title: 'Kirk Fell'
type: poi
category: 'hiking'
latitude: 54.483323
longitude: -3.244074
---

Kirk Fell suffers from its situation between Great Gable and Pillar, which are both higher and finer fells. It is unlikely to be ascended on its own but can be conveniently combined with Great Gable, the traverse from one to the other via Beck Head incorporating some steep ground and easy scrambling.
