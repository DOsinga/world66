Pike o' Blisco is often incorporated into a long and rewarding circuit around the head of Langdale taking in Crinkle Crags, Bowfell and perhaps Rossett Pike and the Langdale Pikes as well. It is equally enjoyable as a short walk on its own. The path above Brown Gill gives superb views of the Langdale Pikes on the approach, and the summit looks across to Bowfell, one of Lakeland's finest mountains.
