A branch of the Tate housed in a converted Albert Dock warehouse, home to the National Collection of Modern Art outside London. The gallery hosts a rotating programme of exhibitions alongside its permanent collection, and the setting -- red brick, cast iron columns, views over the dock basin -- is as striking as the art inside.
