The only museum in the world completely dedicated to the Fab Four, established in 1990 at the Albert Dock.

The story is told in 18 separate features including the streets of Hamburg, a full-size replica of the Cavern Club, Abbey Road studios, a walk-through Yellow Submarine and the White Room dedicated to John Lennon. Acquisitions include George Harrison's first guitar and the evocative "Lenses of Lennon" display where you can peer through his iconic round spectacles.

Visitors can take an audio tour narrated by John Lennon's sister Julia, with contributions from Paul McCartney, George Martin and Cynthia Lennon, available in several languages.
