---
title: "Bars and Cafes"
type: section
---

Manchester's drinking scene runs from cosy pubs to cocktail bars and sweaty nightclubs, and it rarely slows down before the small hours. The Northern Quarter is the centre of gravity for independent bars with good music and a relaxed dress code. Deansgate Locks draws a flashier crowd, while Canal Street anchors one of the liveliest gay scenes in the country. Live music happens somewhere every night of the week.
