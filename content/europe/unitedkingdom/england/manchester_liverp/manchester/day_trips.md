**Knowsley Safari Park** is about 40 kilometres west of Manchester on the road to [Liverpool](/europe/unitedkingdom/england/manchester_liverp/liverpool), near the town of Prescot. The park offers a drive-through safari experience where the animals roam free while visitors travel in their cars.

The park features an 8-kilometre route where you can see lions, tigers, monkeys, elephants, zebras, antelope and giraffe. The lions and tigers are now in enclosures and the elephants have a paddock, but there is an alternative route for those who want to avoid close encounters with the monkeys. The park is open throughout the year from 10 a.m. to 4 p.m., weather permitting. You will need your own car.
