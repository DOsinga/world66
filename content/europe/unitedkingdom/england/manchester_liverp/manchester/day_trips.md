**Day Trips**

**Walking in the Peak District**

East of Manchester lies the Peak District, the hilly spine of England. From Manchester there are several possibilities to visit it. It takes an hour on the train from Manchester Piccadilly Station to Buxton. Get out at any little station on the way and within twenty minutes you are on the mo [more..](/europe/unitedkingdom/england/manchester/daytrips/walkinginthepeakdistrict)

**Manchester Airport Aviation Viewing Park **

See planes taking off and landing from near the end of the runway. Shop with aviation merchandise.  Location: About one mile from the main terminals, off the A538, near M56 motorway junction. 

address: Manchester Airport, Manchester, M90 1QX
