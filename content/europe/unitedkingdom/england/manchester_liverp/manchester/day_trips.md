**Have an Authentic Safari Experience right near Manchester**

You might want to check this guide for some of the available **Manchester**** day trips**. One of the places you can visit is the Knowsley Safari Park located about 40 kilometers west of town on the road to Liverpool, near the town of Prescot. The park offers a one-of-a-kind safari experience, copying the East African "Safari Park" concept, where the visitors are the ones placed in cars while the animals roam free. The Knowsley Safari Park is one of the most popular attractions in the region and is a must-visit for all guests of Manchester.

#### **Overview**

The Knowsley Safari Park features an 8-kilometer route for cars, where visitors can see a wide variety of exotic animals, including lions, tigers, monkeys, elephants, zebras, antelope and giraffe. When the park opened in 1971, all the animals roamed free. Today, certain safety measures are taken. The lions and tigers are now displayed in enclosures, and the elephants have a paddock. There is also an alternative route, which allows visitors to avoid close encounters with the monkeys. Generally, the place is secure, and you will have a truly memorable experience.

#### **Schedule and Prices**

The park is open throughout the year from 10 a.m. to 4 p.m., as long as the weather is not snowy. Keep in mind you will need to provide your own car to travel around the park. The admission fees are $22 for adults and $15 for children under 15 and seniors over 60.
