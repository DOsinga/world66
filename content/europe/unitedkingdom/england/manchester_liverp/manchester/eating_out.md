**Eat at the Best Restaurant in Manchester**

When talking about **Manchester**** restaurants**, you may be wondering about the best restaurant in the city. It is, undoubtedly, the Michael Caines restaurant, located at the Abode Hotel on 107 Piccadilly Square. It received the prestigious Pride of Manchester award for best restaurant several times. Although located inside a hotel, the restaurant is open to all visitors. Just remember to make reservations prior to visiting, and you will enjoy a wonderful fine dining experience.

#### **The Atmosphere**

Boasting a professional, friendly and efficient service, Michael Caines restaurant offers a classy atmosphere where you will feel absolutely comfortable. Both the cocktail bar and the dining room are dimly lit, and it creates a feeling of relaxation and joy. The lighting complements the impressive d&eacute;cor of the beautiful dark wood. The chefs are highly qualified, and the dishes are presented in a unique way. You will remember your meal at the restaurant.

#### **The Menu**

The restaurant menu offers an exciting range of dishes, focused on the best aspects of European cuisine. To complement them, you can choose from a carefully selected list of refined wines. The ingredients used for the meals are mostly fresh local produce, and you will surely notice the unique flavor they give to the food. You have to try the pan-fried scallops, the breast of wood pigeon or the rose veal fillet. The cheese menu is also great. Finish your meal with a cup of wonderful coffee and delicious petit fours.
