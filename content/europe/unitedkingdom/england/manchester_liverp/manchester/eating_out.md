---
title: "Eating Out"
type: section
---

Manchester's food scene reflects its diverse population. Chinatown, near Piccadilly Gardens, has some of the best Chinese cooking outside London. The Curry Mile in Rusholme is a strip of Indian and Pakistani restaurants that competes fiercely on price and quality. In the Northern Quarter, independent cafe-bars serve inventive, inexpensive food alongside imported beers and good coffee.
