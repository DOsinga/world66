---
title: 'Eating Out'
type: section
---

The **Michael Caines restaurant**, at the Abode Hotel on 107 Piccadilly, has received the Pride of Manchester award for best restaurant several times. Although located inside a hotel, the restaurant is open to all visitors -- just remember to make reservations.

The cocktail bar and dining room are dimly lit with impressive dark wood decor. The menu offers an exciting range of European dishes using mostly fresh local produce. Try the pan-fried scallops, the breast of wood pigeon or the rose veal fillet. The cheese selection is also excellent.
