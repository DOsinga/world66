title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Manchester packs an impressive range of museums, galleries, and historic buildings into a walkable city centre. The Museum of Science and Industry traces the city's industrial past, the Lowry showcases one of Britain's most distinctive painters, and the Imperial War Museum North is Daniel Libeskind's striking waterfront landmark. Between visits, the Victorian architecture above the shopfronts rewards anyone who looks up.
