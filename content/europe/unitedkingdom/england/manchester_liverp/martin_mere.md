Martin Mere is a wetland reserve owned by the Wildfowl and Wetlands Trust near Burscough in West Lancashire. Its permanent stock of captive foreign birds can be seen at any time of year, but the reserve is most exciting in winter when hundreds of migrating Whooper and Bewick's swans arrive from Siberia, along with numerous types of migrating ducks and geese.
