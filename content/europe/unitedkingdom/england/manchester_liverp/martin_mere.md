Martin Mere is a wetland reserve owned by the Wildfowl and Wetlands Trust, set in the flat Lancashire plain near Burscough. Its permanent collection of captive wildfowl from around the world can be seen at any time of year, but the reserve is most spectacular in winter, when hundreds of migrating Whooper and Bewick's swans arrive from Siberia alongside flocks of ducks and geese. The sight of several hundred wild swans on the mere at dusk is genuinely memorable.
