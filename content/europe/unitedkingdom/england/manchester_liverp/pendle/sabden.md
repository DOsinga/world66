**Sabden** is a quiet village tucked into the southern slopes of Pendle Hill in Lancashire. It is a good starting point for walking up Pendle Hill from the south — follow the road towards Clitheroe and pick up one of the well-marked paths.

The village itself is small but pleasant, with a couple of pubs and a community feel. The surrounding countryside offers excellent walking through rolling farmland and open moorland, with wide views across the Ribble Valley.
