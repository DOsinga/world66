title: 'Eating Out'
type: section
---

Preston is slowly but surely establishing itself as a player in the region's dining scene. There are formal restaurants, laid-back cafe bars and coffee shops, pubs, and traditional restaurants serving hearty breakfasts or fish and chips. With an increasing number of international restaurants, there is an opportunity to sample food from around the globe.

**Firm Favourites**

One of the best-loved places to eat is the **Bello Italian Restaurant**, which has been charming diners for over 30 years. Located in the Friargate area, it uses locally grown ingredients to create simple but delicious dishes, with good vegetarian options. **Farington Lodge**, a hotel in a Grade II listed building, has a Garden Restaurant offering contemporary dishes -- a nice spot for a special occasion.

**Something Different**

**Bistro Live** at 7 Avenham Street offers cabaret entertainment alongside food and drink. A young crowd heads here for dishes such as chicken breast stuffed with pancetta and asparagus, plus a good wine list and cocktail menu.

**International Dining**

Preston's multi-ethnic mix is reflected in scores of Indian, Chinese and Italian restaurants alongside Mexican, Spanish, Middle Eastern and Southeast Asian choices. For late-night snacks, there are plenty of places to pick up a kebab.
