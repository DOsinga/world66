Warrington is a large town in Cheshire, standing on the banks of the River Mersey roughly midway between [Liverpool](/europe/unitedkingdom/england/manchester_liverp/liverpool) and [Manchester](/europe/unitedkingdom/england/manchester_liverp/manchester). Its strategic position at a crossing point of the Mersey has made it important since Roman times.
