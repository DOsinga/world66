---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

Getting to Durham by train is easy enough - the North East mainline
from London to Edinburgh passes through Durham and many trains stop
here. The station itself is located right in the city centre.

By road, Durham is just off the A1 - simply turn off at the A690.
