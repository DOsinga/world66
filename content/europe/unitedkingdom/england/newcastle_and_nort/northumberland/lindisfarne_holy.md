title: "Lindisfarne [Holy Island}"
type: location
---

This is a lovely place in its own right and an exceptionally important site for England's religious history, Christianity having emerged in a pagan area in the 7th century.

The 'island' is semi-tidal and accessible by car on a land causeway except at high tide. Tide times and all types of information about the history and buildings can be found on www.**lindisfarne**.org.**uk**/
