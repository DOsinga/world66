Newcastle Upon Tyne is located in the North East of England, its
inhabitants are affectionately known as Geordies. The city has a number
of good shopping centres in the Town Centre as well as the Metro Centre
at Gateshead, which is not accesible by the cities Metro. The nightlife
in the town is particularly frenzied with numerous clubs.

Newcastle was an important ship building centre but now
concentrates mainly on work for the offshore oil industry. The port is
still quite busy and services ferries to Scandanavia and the
Netherlands. The coal industry has also departed the area.

The Tyne with its landmark bridges is a popular attraction for visitors.
