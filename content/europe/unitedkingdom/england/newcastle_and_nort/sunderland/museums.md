### [Sunderland museum and winter gardens

The Sunderlaland museum and winter gardens is located in the city
centre, it incudes a museum with a divers collection, a glasshouse and
a park

The collection covers the following topics:

mining

ship building

ceramics and glass

local history

All collections have roots in the local history, there are a lot of interactive displays

The glasshouse is complemented with a compact natural history section.

The museum has lots of hands-on displays

It can be recommended for family visits. The collections and the way
they are presented should appeal to more..](/europe/unitedkingdom/england/newcastle_and_nort/sunderland/museums/sunderland_museum)
