---
title: "Bars and Cafes"
type: section
---

Nottingham has one of the best pub scenes in England, with a mix of historic inns, craft beer bars, and student-friendly venues. Ye Olde Trip to Jerusalem, built into the rock beneath the castle, claims to be the oldest inn in England (dating to 1189, though that is contested). The Lace Market and Hockley neighbourhoods are the best areas for an evening out, with a concentration of independent bars and cafes.
