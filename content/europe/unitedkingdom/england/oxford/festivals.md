May 1st is Mayday. Pubs are open all day and there is singing from the top of Magdalen College Tower as 5am in the morning by the choir of young boys. They sing "madrigals" whatever they are. Quite nice. Bit surreal after a night of drinking too.

Students in dinner jackets and ball gowns then tend to throw themselves into the river off Magdalen Bridge. Also quite surreal.
