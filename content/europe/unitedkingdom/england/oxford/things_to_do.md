---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Most sights cluster around the Radcliffe Camera in the university quarter. Christ Church, Magdalen, and Merton colleges are among the most rewarding to visit; the Bodleian Library offers guided tours of its historic reading rooms. The Ashmolean Museum on Beaumont Street, the Pitt Rivers ethnographic collection, and the Natural History Museum are all free and excellent. For a slower afternoon, hire a punt on the Cherwell from Magdalen Bridge or Cherwell Boathouse.
