South of [London](/europe/unitedkingdom/england/london) you find a region that is steeped in history, but whose towns and cities buzz with life.

Where beautiful coastline and breathtaking landscapes form the backdrop to peaceful villages and magnificent stately homes and castles.

Highlights include [Oxford](/europe/unitedkingdom/england/south_east/oxford), [Dover](/europe/unitedkingdom/england/south_east/dover), [Portsmouth](/europe/unitedkingdom/england/south_east/portsmouth), [Southampton](/europe/unitedkingdom/england/south_east/southampton) and [Brighton](/europe/unitedkingdom/england/south_east/brighton), one of the most posh seaside resorts of the United Kingdom.
