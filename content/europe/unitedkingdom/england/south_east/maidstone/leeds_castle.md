One of the most photographed castles in England, Leeds Castle sits on two small islands in the middle of a lake five miles south-east of Maidstone. It looks almost too good to be true — and it is genuinely beautiful, with good gardens and grounds to explore. The interior has been heavily restored and is perhaps less interesting than the exterior, but the setting alone makes the trip worthwhile. Accessible by bus from Maidstone town centre.
