title: Maidstone Museum and Bentlif Art Gallery
type: poi
---

One of the best county museums in south-east England, housed in a sixteenth-century Elizabethan manor house in the town centre. The collection ranges from archaeology and natural history to decorative arts and fine art, and the Egyptian gallery — with a genuine mummy — is a particular draw. Admission is free.
