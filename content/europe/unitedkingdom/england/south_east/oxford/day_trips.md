Although Oxford is considered a day trip in itself, there are many fun side trips from this historic college town as well. During your Oxford trip, you will discover places like Great Milton, Woodstock, Blenheim Palace, Aylesbury, and Dorchester-on-Thames, within a few miles, where you will find elaborate gardens, grand castles, historic homes, magnificent cathedrals, unique museums, and more. Continue reading to learn about some of Oxford's best day trips.

### [Bicester](/europe/unitedkingdom/england/south_east/oxford/day_trips/bicester)

Located a short 20 minute drive (15 miles) from Oxford is the city of Bicester and home of Rousham Park House and Garden. Visitors can explore the pristine paths, immaculate meadows, and gorgeous groves, and gardens of this 18th-century English landscape park. The mansion was built in 1635, www.rousham.org, +44 0 1869 347110. Another top Bicester attraction is the Bicester Outlet Village Shopping where you can pick up high-end designer brand names for much, MUCH less than the retail price.
