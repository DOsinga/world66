Located a short 20 minute drive (15 miles) from Oxford is the city of Bicester and home of Rousham Park House and Garden. Visitors can explore the pristine paths, immaculate meadows, and gorgeous groves, and gardens of this 18th-century English landscape park. The mansion was built in 1635, www.rousham.org, +44 0 1869 347110. Another top Bicester attraction is the Bicester Outlet Village Shopping where you can pick up high-end designer brand names for much, MUCH less than the retail price. Just a few of the designer brands include: Versace, Burberry, Jaeger, Hugo Boss, Aquascutum, Molton Brown, and more. 50 Pingle Drive, Bicester UK, +44 1869 323200, www.bicestervillage.com. The Bicester Visitor Center is located onsite at Bicester Outlet Village Shopping, www.visit-northoxfordshire.co.uk, +44 0 1869 323200.
