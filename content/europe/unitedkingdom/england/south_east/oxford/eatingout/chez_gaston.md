Chez Gaston is located on North Parade Street just a short walk from the city center in Oxford. I have been a devoted fan since this restaurant opened in the middle 1990s. In the beginning, only French fare was available on the menu. By 2002, it was necessary for the chef to bow to the average Brit's tastes by including obligatory menu items such as curried chicken sandwiches, in order to survive.
