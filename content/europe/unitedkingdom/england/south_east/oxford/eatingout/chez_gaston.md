Chez Gaston is located on North Parade Street just a short walk from the city center in Oxford. I have been a devoted fan since this restaurant opened in the middle 1990s. In the beginning, only French fare was available on the menu. By 2002, it was necessary for the chef to bow to the average Brit's tastes by including obligatory menu items such as curried chicken sandwiches, in order to survive. The variety of crepes was scaled back to about half a dozen savoury and half a dozen sweet. This is a pity, as newer patrons will never have the chance to sample all of the delicious crepes. However, those crepes that are still available are scrumptious. I have never tasted a better meal. I have taken many friends and co-workers there to dine, and each and every one of them has agreed that Chez Gaston is a wonderful place. If I could, I would fly to England simply to have dinner at Chez Gaston once again. Do not pass up an opportunity to dine at this wonderful restaurant.
