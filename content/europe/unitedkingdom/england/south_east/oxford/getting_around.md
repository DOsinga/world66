Many of Oxford's major attractions are located in close proximity to one another, so the best way to get around is on foot. If you prefer to get around by bus or taxi, Oxford has several taxi companies and an excellent bus service. Taxi companies include:

**ABC Taxis: **+44 01865 770077/775577

**001 Taxis: **+44 0 1865 240000

**Radio Taxis: **+44 0 1865 242424/249743

**Royal Cars: **+44 0 1865 777333

**The Oxford Bus Company** (www.oxfordbus.co.uk) operates throughout the city with a main bus stop at Magdalen Street. The main bus is the #2, which travels down Banbury Road from the city centre to Summertown. Fares and schedules are available online and on buses.

The City of Oxford Motor Services Ltd. operates The Oxford Bus Company. Address: 3rd floor, 41-51 Grey Street Newcastle-Upon-Tyne NE1 6EE. Phone: +44 0 1865 785400, E-mail: info@oxfordbus.co.uk, Website: www.oxfordbus.co.uk.
