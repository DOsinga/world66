Most sights are in some way connected to the various colleges. The Christ Church Cathedral, for example, is part of the Christ Church College comple and Hertford college is the place to head for when you want to see the famous Bridge of Sighs. The impressive Radcliffe Camera, used as a reading room these days,can be seen as the centre point from which one can easily explore the colleges and museums, all concentrated in a not so vast area.
