---
title: "When to Go"
type: section
---

Oxford is busy year-round, but summer (July–August) brings the most tourists while the students are away on vacation. The colleges are most accessible during the summer term breaks. May Week in late June — despite the confusing name — is graduation season and the city fills with formal dress and celebrations. May Day itself, on 1 May, is the city's most distinctive event: the choir of Magdalen College sings madrigals from the top of the college tower at 6am, and students in black tie and ball gowns throw themselves into the river from Magdalen Bridge afterwards. It is exactly as strange as it sounds.
