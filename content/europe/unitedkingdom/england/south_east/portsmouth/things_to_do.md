---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Portsmouth's sights are largely maritime and military — the Historic Dockyard alone can absorb a full day with its three historic warships and museums, and Southsea's seafront adds the castle and the D-Day Story. Add the Spinnaker Tower for the view and Portchester Castle for the layered Roman-and-medieval history and you have two very full days.
