The centrepiece of any visit to Portsmouth and one of the best maritime attractions in the country. A single ticket covers **HMS Victory**, Nelson's flagship at Trafalgar in 1805 — you can stand on the spot where he fell in the cockpit; the extraordinary **Mary Rose Museum**, built around the recovered timbers of Henry VIII's Tudor warship, sunk in 1545 and raised from the Solent mud in 1982; **HMS Warrior** (1860), the world's first iron-hulled warship; and the Royal Naval Museum. Allow a full day.
