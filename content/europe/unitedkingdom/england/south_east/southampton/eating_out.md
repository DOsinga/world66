---
title: "Eating Out"
type: section
---

Southampton has a solid range of places to eat, with a particular strength in South Asian cuisine — a legacy of the city's long connection to the subcontinent through its port. The streets around Bedford Place and Oxford Street have the highest concentration of independent restaurants. The waterfront at Ocean Village has more options but at higher prices and lower quality; the Old Town pubs around Bugle Street and French Street are a better bet for a straightforward meal.
