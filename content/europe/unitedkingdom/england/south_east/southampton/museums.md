### [Jack Wilson](/europe/unitedkingdom/england/south_east/southampton/museums/jack_wilson)

This sounds a bit odd - but the old town, south of the Bargate, and the walls are an open air museum. Pick up one of the self-guided tours from the Tourist office and walk the walls from the Bargate, round to the West walls and along the remains of the South walls. Along the way you can take your pick from medieval walls, old churches, museums,Tudor buildings and historic pubs. Ther'es a lot more here than you might think, including connections to WW2 and the Invasion of France, and the Titanic.
