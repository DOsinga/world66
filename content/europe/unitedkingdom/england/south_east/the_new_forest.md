At the time of writing, November 2004, the New Forest in Hampshire is not yet a national park but it has been announced by the Government that it will be one. The 'New' bit could be misleading because it was New royal forest in the time of William the Conqueror and his son, william Rufus was killed there in what was officially designated a 'hunting accident'.
