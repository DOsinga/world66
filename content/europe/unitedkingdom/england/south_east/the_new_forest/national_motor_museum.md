The motor museum at Beaulieu holds around 250 vehicles, from early motorcars to land speed record holders including Donald Campbell's Bluebird and Thrust2. The site also includes the ruins of Beaulieu Abbey, Palace House (the home of Lord Montagu, who founded the collection), and grounds along the Beaulieu River. It is a genuine full-day destination rather than a quick stop. Admission is not cheap, but the combined ticket covers all attractions on the estate.
