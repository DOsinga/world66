---
title: "Woldingham"
type: location
---

Woldingham is a nice quiet village in Surrey, 20 miles from London, which is supposed to be the home of some of the richest people in England (based on net value per head!). Notwithstanding that, it remains quiet and unspoilt with no pub (an English village with no pub?) and some very nice walks. 30 minutes on the train from London, so if you are staying in London and want to escape London for the day, you'll have a good time. Remember to bring the picnic lunch!
