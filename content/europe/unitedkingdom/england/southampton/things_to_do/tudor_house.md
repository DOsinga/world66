A handsome timber-framed merchant's house from around 1495, set on a quiet lane in the heart of the Old Town. The rooms inside follow the story of Southampton from the medieval period to the twentieth century, with a particular focus on the people who actually lived here. The walled garden is a careful recreation of a Tudor garden and worth seeing in its own right. Free to enter.
