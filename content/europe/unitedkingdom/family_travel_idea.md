**United Kingdom Family Travel
**
Vacationing in the United Kingdom can be a fun trip for
all. There are many **Family Travel Ideas** to choose from and most are always child friendly. Here are just a few you may wish to consider
when visiting the United Kingdom:

**Stonehenge**

One of the largest and oldest tourist attractions in the world,
Stonehenge is a fascinating place to visit by both young and old. Wander around the carefully arranged giant stones and learn about the history of this archaeological site.**

Edinburgh Castle**

Said to be built around the Bronze Age era, Edinburgh Castle
is one of the most beautiful locations in all of the United Kingdom. It’s built well above sea level and perched high
on a part of an extinct volcano. Stroll through the castle and ruins. You’ll
find much to do and see.**

Museums**

There are several museums of interest to visit in the United
Kingdom including:

- The Natural History Museum which contains the
most abundant collection of complete dinosaur skeletons.- Madame Tussard’s Wax Museum where you can see
the largest collection of famed wax personalities from all over the world.- The British Museum which houses an extensive
collection of artifacts that date back to the beginning of the human race and
are from various historical parts of our planet.**Take a Ride**

There are a couple of wonderful theme parks to visit and
ride rides. One being Alton Towers with
a variety of both outdoor and indoor rides including a huge water park and hot
spring; Legoland with approximately 150 rides, waterslides, shows, boat trips
and so much more; or take a ride on the towering London Eye, one of the largest
observation wheels in the world. It
takes visitors up a staggering 135 meters above the city of London. **

Longleat**

Visit Longleat and get a little family fun for everyone. It’s
been voted UK Family Attraction of the Year several times and is said to never
disappoint. Here you will find a safari park, boat rides on lakes full of seals, a huge maze to run around in and much more. **

The Deep**

In this amazing aquarium, the deepest in Europe, you will
find many strange and wonderful creatures to gaze at, from jellyfish, manta
rays, sharks, saw fish plus many other species of fish. Here you will have many activities and things
to keep your entire family busy for hours.
