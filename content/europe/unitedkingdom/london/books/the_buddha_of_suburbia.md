---
title: "The Buddha of Suburbia"
type: poi
author: "Hanif Kureishi"
isbn: "978-0143115748"
---

A coming-of-age novel set in south London's suburbs in the 1970s, following a mixed-race teenager moving between the dull commuter belt and the louche world of arts London. Funny, sharp, and still fresh — it captures the tensions between suburb and city, tradition and reinvention, that define London's outer rings as much today as then.
