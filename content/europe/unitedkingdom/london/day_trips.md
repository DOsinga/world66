London's position in southern England puts a remarkable number of places within a day's reach by train. Bath is 1h25 from Paddington and can absorb a full day easily. Oxford and Cambridge are under an hour from Paddington and King's Cross respectively, and are best visited midweek when the students are around and the tourist coaches aren't overwhelming. Brighton is 50 minutes from Victoria and makes an excellent half-day or full-day trip; the beach and the Lanes are the draws. Windsor, 35 minutes from Paddington, is smaller but the castle is extraordinary and the walk along the Thames is lovely.
