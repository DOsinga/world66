The permanent collection of international modern and contemporary art occupies a converted Bankside power station, and the building itself is half the attraction. The Turbine Hall is one of the best free spaces in London — rotating large-scale commissions fill it throughout the year. The permanent collection is strong on Rothko, Picasso, and the major movements of the twentieth century.

Bankside, South Bank. Open daily. Free for the permanent collection.
