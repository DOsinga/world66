Nearly a thousand years old and still guarded by Yeoman Warders in Tudor dress, the Tower is a genuine relic of medieval power rather than a theme park recreation. The Crown Jewels are here — genuinely dazzling and stranger than you expect — and the exhibition on the history of the tower as a royal palace, armoury, and prison is among the best in the city. The views from the battlements over Tower Bridge are excellent.

Not cheap, but worth the ticket. Book online to skip the queue.

Tower Hill, City of London. Open Tuesday–Sunday (closed Monday in winter).
