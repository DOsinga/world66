**Briston **is the 2nd largest village in Norfolk. It has 1 church that does not have a spire. There is a primary school, and a post office, a sports and social centre and a housing estate called Lowmax and a garage called Eeks. There is a co-op and a mace food store, a local butchers and a bakery.** Briston **is a neighbouring village to Melton Constable. ***[Norwich](/europe/unitedkingdom/england/eastern_england/norwich) **is the nearest city. Holt is the nearest town.*
