**Democratic Enviromental Society of Senya** is a micronation located in [Lenwade (Great Witchingham)](/europe/unitedkingdom/norfolk/lenwade_great_wit). The Micronation is sometimes shortened to DES Senya. The President of Senya is President Barnaby Hands. Senya is having a general election sometime during mid-2011. Senya was founded on 27 March 2010. It was suppost to be called Backofmigaden but the name changed in mid-March before the country was founded. Senya recognizes New Russia & New Pambia.
