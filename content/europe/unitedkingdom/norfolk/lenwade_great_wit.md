Lenwade is a hamlet in central Norfolk, around 12 miles north-west of Norwich on the road to Fakenham. It lies close to Great Witchingham, a larger village that is the centre of the civil parish; the two are often confused.

The main draw for visitors is the Dinosaur Adventure Park (formerly Dinosaur Park), a family attraction set in woodland just outside the village, well signposted from the main road.

Bus services run through Lenwade on the Norwich to Fakenham and Wells-next-the-Sea route. There is no railway station.
