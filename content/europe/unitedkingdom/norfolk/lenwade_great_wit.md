Travel Guide

**LENWADE **is a large hamlet in Norfolk, about 12 miles from [Norwich](/europe/unitedkingdom/england/eastern_england/norwich). Lenwade is sometimes mistook for **GREAT WITCHINGHAM**, a village about 2 miles away which is headquaters for the parish. Lenwade itself has a church, but this has been dissused scince 2006.

Lenwade is best known for the **DINOSAUR PARK** which is just off the main village at the first cross-roads coming from Norwich.

Lenwade does not have an airport or a train station, but it does have 3 bus stations that do X29 services between [Norwich](/europe/unitedkingdom/england/eastern_england/norwich) and Fakenham, and futher down to Wells.

Services include a butchers, a bakery, a fish & chip shop, a newsagents, a post office and a bordered up church. There is also 2 hotels. There is also the only Zoological Garden in Norfolk.

**SPORTS**

There is plenty of places to row and there is also a cricket pitch, which is home of **WITCHINGHAM C.C.. **There is also a recreation ground.

**OTHER FACILITIES**

There is a little primary school called Great Witchingham and there is also a bottle bank. There is a massive wooded area that is great for BMX'ing and Mountain Bike and Walking. Also there are two areas where people can fish.
