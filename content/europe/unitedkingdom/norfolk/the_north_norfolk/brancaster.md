**Brancaster** is a little village located between Titchwell and Brancaster Staithe. From just outside you can see the RSPB site at Titchwell and the many islands off the coast of Brancaster Staithe. Brancaster contains lots of shops and a church in addition to a golf course. There is a little Primary school called Brancaster CofE VA Primary School that provides education to around 65 4-11 year old students. The school is on a little lane that futher up is a farm. This road often floods and spills water through Brancaster down to the golf corse and then on to the beach. The Beach is home to lots of seals but they are difficult to see and can be easily mistaken for rocks. The Road runs from King's Lynn to Great Yarmouth and eventually to [Lowestoft](/europe/unitedkingdom/suffolk/lowestoft)
