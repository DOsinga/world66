A very eclectic restaurant, Bourbon serves a good mix of food, from pizza, pasta and burgers to asian rice and noodle dishes, all with a modern twist. The surroundings are extravagant and neo-classical, with greek pillars and bronze statues, while the quiet modern music adds a relaxing ambience to the restaurant.
