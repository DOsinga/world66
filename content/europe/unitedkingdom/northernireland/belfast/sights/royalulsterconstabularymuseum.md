This museum includes displays of uniforms and equipment, photographs and memorabilia relating to the Irish Constabulary since its formation in 1822. The 'Royal' prefix was added in 1867 and in 1922 the Royal Ulster Constabulary was created.
 
 Open 10am - 12.30pm and 2pm - 4.30pm, Monday to Friday.
