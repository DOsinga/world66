Warrenpoint is a beautiful seaside town located in County Down, with the many festivals happening during the warmer months and fantastic night life, it's a must for all to visit! Warrenpoint is located just 6 miles from the city of Newry, to get to Warrenpoint just follow the A2 from Newry.
