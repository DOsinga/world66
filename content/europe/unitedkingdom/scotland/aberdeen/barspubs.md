---
title: "Bars and Cafes"
type: section
---

Aberdeen's cafe scene clusters in two areas: the city centre around Union Street and Belmont Street, and the seafront along the Esplanade at Aberdeen Beach. The Belmont Street area has the more interesting independent places — cocktail bars, craft beer pubs, and coffee shops in granite buildings. The beach esplanade is more casual, with traditional cafes serving fry-ups and ice cream to walkers.
