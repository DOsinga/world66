164 King Street, Aberdeen. Tel: 01224 646323. Open all year Mon-Fri 1000-1600, Tues & Fri also 1900-2200 and Sat 1000-1300. Closed local and public holidays (contact shop for details). Wide range of reference books, microfiche, microfilm for genealogical research. Basic advice given free. Research guides, maps and local history material for sale.
