Polmuir Road, Aberdeen. Tel: 01224 585310. Open all year including Bank Holidays, Christmas etc. Dec-Mar 0930-1600, Apr 0930-1700, May-Sept 0930-2100, Oct 0930-2030, Nov 0930-1700. Europe's largest indoor garden collection. Flora and fauna from around the world, fish and birds, "talking" cactus during summer. Licensed restaurant. Artists in summer. Disabled toilets. Admission free.
