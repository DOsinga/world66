---
title: "Day Trips in Banff"
type: section
---

Day trips are best if you go by car, south to [Aberdeen](/europe/unitedkingdom/scotland/aberdeen), east along the coast to [Fraserburgh](/europe/unitedkingdom/scotland/fraserburgh) or [Peterhead](/europe/unitedkingdom/scotland/peterhead), west along the coast to Buckie or [Elgin](/europe/unitedkingdom/scotland/elgin), or south west to the many whisky distilleries.
