Banff Museum on the High Street Banff is open during the summer months and is free entry, housed in the same building as the library. For bird watchers there is a large collection of stuffed birds.

### [Banffshire Maritime and Heritage Museum](/europe/unitedkingdom/scotland/banff/museums/banffshire_maritim)

A new museum in temporary premises in [Macduff](/europe/unitedkingdom/scotland/macduff) will be open 14th July 2006. Temporary exhibitions have been held in 2003 in Banff and 2004 in [Macduff](/europe/unitedkingdom/scotland/macduff). The museum will celebrate mainly the fishing heritage, but also has a large oil-rig model and a bit on ship-building.

### [Duff House Gallery](/europe/unitedkingdom/scotland/banff/museums/duff_house_gallery)

Banff's most known landmark, run by Historic Scotland and the National Galleries. Hindreds of paintings wonderfully shown in this fine mansion house, also has a cafe and a nice shop. If you fancy a Scottish wedding it can be arranged here.
