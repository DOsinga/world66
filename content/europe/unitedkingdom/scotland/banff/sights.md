### [Boyndlie Visitors Centre](/europe/unitedkingdom/scotland/banff/sights/boyndlie_visitors)

Relatively new centre which usually has an art or craft exhibition, nice gardens with pond, fountain and information board regarding the 'Banff Strike Wing' which flew from Boyndlie Aerodrome during world war two. It also has it's own cafe.
