---
title: "Day Trips"
type: section
---

Scotland is compact enough that some of its most dramatic landscapes are within a few hours of the cities. From Edinburgh or Glasgow you can reach Loch Ness and the Highland glens in a day, and the Isle of Skye makes for a rewarding overnight or long-day trip. The Borders, Argyll, and Perthshire are all within striking distance for those who want to escape the city without a full journey north.
