If you happen to be in Edinburgh mid August, you probably won't have a problem how to spend your evenings. Edinburgh hosts the international festival for several weeks and every day there is an abundance of cultural and artistic performances. However, tickets are a must and usually booked in advance, so what to do next when you are the only one without a ticket? Edinburgh has plenty of pubs, clubs and theatres that will keep you busy for the evening - if not for the whole night. Check out 'The List' for the latest news, hotspots and gigs.
