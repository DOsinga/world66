Savour the delights of the **Scottish Highlands** with your **tour guide**;
see rivers, lochs, and mountains, and experience plenty of rich Highland history
- with an overnight stay in the **Highland capital of Inverness**.

Enjoy a flexible itinerary touring with your guide - you may choose to visit
the fascinating **Culloden Battlefield**, where the **
Jacobites** and **Bonnie Prince Charlie** were defeated by
the **Duke of Cumberland**, and glimpse the ancient stones of the
**Neolithic Clava Clairns**, before the opportunity for a visit and
whisky tasting at **Tomatin Distillery**.
