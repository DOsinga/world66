Places in Fyvie to eat out are the 'Sheiling Tor' which has a fish and chip shop take-away, a cafe and a restauarant or the Vale Hotel. So even a small village can cater for the needs of all.

### [Vale Hotel](/europe/unitedkingdom/scotland/fyvie/eating_out/vale_hotel)

Vale Hotel good food at a reasonable price, you can't miss the hotel it's on the right as you enter the village.
