title: "Museums"
type: section
---

There are no museums in Fyvie, except for Fyvie Castle itself which to my mind is greater then any museum. Nearest museums are the Auld Post office Museum [Turriff](/europe/unitedkingdom/scotland/turriff), the Session Cottage [Turriff](/europe/unitedkingdom/scotland/turriff) about 5 miles north. [Banff](/northamerica/canada/alberta/banff) museum about 20 miles north. Inverurie museum about 10 miles south west. [Fraserburgh](/europe/unitedkingdom/scotland/fraserburgh) Heritage centre and the Scottish Lighthouse Museum [Fraserburgh](/europe/unitedkingdom/scotland/fraserburgh) about 25 miles north east. Arbuthnott museum [Peterhead](/europe/unitedkingdom/scotland/peterhead) about 20 miles east. Or the many museums in [Aberdeen](/europe/unitedkingdom/scotland/aberdeen) about 20 miles south.
