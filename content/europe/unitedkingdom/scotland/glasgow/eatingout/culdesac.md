Downstairs from the Attic, and upstairs from its similarly named restaurant, this a cool bar both in its Ashton Lane location and its clientele. This being another pub popular with the student crowd, the place is usually very busy, with loud upbeat music in the background, and a slightly larger capacity than its partner bar above it. Still definitely worth a stop.
