**general**

**Archaos**

Famous club and venue.  

**Candy Bar**

A very distinctive bar in Hope Street, with a very distinctive looking site. Named "one of the best places to be seen in Glasgow" by the Glasgow Food Guide.  

**The Inn on the Green**

A restaurant and hotel on the edge of Glasgow Green. On the site, you can find a menu and upcoming events for the restaurant, and room rates and pictures of the accommodation.  

**Leonardo and Company Restaurants**

At 77 Bothwell Street. Pizza, pasta and bistro style food with own brand beer in a relaxed atmosphere.  

**McMonagles Restaurant**

Dine aboard the good ship Debra Rose on the Forth and Clyde Canal.

**Nairns**

Information on celebrity chef Nick Nairn's award winning restaurant and hotel.  

**Nice'n'sleazy**

An interactive site from a favourite bar and venue for musicians and music lovers in Glasgow. Site includes animations, games, jukebox and jobs.  

**78 St Vincent**

Located on the City Centre's St Vincent Street. Open for dinner and lunch - read the menu online. 

**The Truffle Club **

Tableside dancing club. 

**The Tunnel **

Popular club that regularly features guest DJ's from all overthe world. Site requires flash plugin.  

**Babaza**

Bar and club in Royal Exchange Square  

**Cafe JJ**

Located in Dumbarton Road. Site features menu online. Discount vouchers are available.
