**Collins Gallery**

at the University of Strathclyde 

**Glasgow Art Gallery & Museum**

Natural history exhibits. Home to works by Botticelli, Rembrant, Millet, Monet, Van Gogh, Derain and Picasso.  

**The Hunterian Museum and Art Gallery**

Exhibitions include Romans in Scotland during the first century AD, with emphasis on the Antonine Wall frontier.  

**Museum of Transport**

Features reproduction of a 1930s Glasgow street scene, complete with cinema and subway station.  

**People's Palace**

Contemporary displays capture the spirit of Glaswegian working class in industrial times.  

**Glasgow School of Arts**

For those of you interested in design and architecture, this is well worth seing. Glasgow School of Arts is the masterpiece of **Charles Rennie Mackintosh**, one of the pioneers of the Modern Movement. There are other C.R. Mackintosh sights in Glasgow, such as the Willow Tea Rooms.
