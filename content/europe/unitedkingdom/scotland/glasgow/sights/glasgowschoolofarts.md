For those of you interested in design and architecture, this is well worth seing. Glasgow School of Arts is the masterpiece of **Charles Rennie Mackintosh**, one of the pioneers of the Modern Movement. There are other C.R. Mackintosh sights in Glasgow, such as the Willow Tea Rooms.
