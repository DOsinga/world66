A volunteer-run museum at the bottom of Duff Street, just south of the harbour lighthouse. Packed with models of fishing boats, oil rigs and supply vessels, alongside artefacts from the area's fishing and shipbuilding history. Open in the summer season; entry is free, donations welcome.
