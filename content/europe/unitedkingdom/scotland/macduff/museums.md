### [Banffshire Maritime Heritage Association](/europe/unitedkingdom/scotland/macduff/museums/banffshire_maritim)

At the bottom of Duff St near the harbour, due south of the lighthouse there is a new museum which opened on the 14th July 2006, it will be open for the summer season until the end of October 2007. The museum is packed with interesting artefacts all about fishing and shipbuilding in the area, they also have models of a large oil-rig and a supply doat, several fishing boat models and many other items of maritime heritage.
