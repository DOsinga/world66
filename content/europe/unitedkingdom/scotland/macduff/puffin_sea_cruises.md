Two- to three-hour trips leaving Macduff harbour, usually at 10am and 2pm in summer. Head east to the gannet colony at Troup Head and the film-set village of Pennan, or west past Findlater Castle and Sandend beach. Dolphins and minke whales turn up regularly; puffins and guillemots are near-guaranteed.
