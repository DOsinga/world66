### [Temple of Venus](/europe/unitedkingdom/scotland/macduff/sights/temple_of_venus)

Sitting high on Doune hill is the Temple of Venus, from the temple great views of neighbouring Banff and the Moray Firth can be had.

### [Tarlair](/europe/unitedkingdom/scotland/macduff/sights/tarlair)

Tarlair was once a busy open air swimming pool. It is know only used by model boat owners. The e'e o' the needle rock formation near the pool is a notable landmark.
