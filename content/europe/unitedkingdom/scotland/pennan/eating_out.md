---
title: "Eating Out"
type: section
---

The Pennan Inn is your one and only choice in the village, and it does the job well — a tasty menu and a solid selection of beers and wines. For more variety, head east to [New Aberdour](/europe/unitedkingdom/scotland/new_aberdour) or west to [Gardenstown](/europe/unitedkingdom/scotland/gardenstown), both of which have hotels with restaurants.
