### [Peterhead Golf Club](/europe/unitedkingdom/scotland/peterhead/things_to_do/peterhead_golf_clu)

The club says "You can play 18 holes on Scotland's 18th oldest course". Peterhead golf club was established in 1841. Today the club has a 9 hole course and an 18 hole course both set amoungst the dunes on the links at the northern end of the town.
