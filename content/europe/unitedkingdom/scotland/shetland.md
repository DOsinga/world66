Shetland islands is a group of over one hundred islands north of
[Scotland](/europe/unitedkingdom/scotland). Soothing sand beaches, mesmerizing sheer cliffs, enthralling wildlife, astounding archaeology and a compelling culture is what you can expect to find on the islands.
