Travel Guide

The northern most island of the Shetland Islands is great for bird- and seal watching. If you want to be somewhere remote, Hermaness,
on the nothern tip of the isle is the place to be. There are huge
cliffs were birds nest and you can see them feed on fish of the
coast. There are a few small comunities on the island and a tiny
airfield wich has flights to Tingwal (Lerwick).
