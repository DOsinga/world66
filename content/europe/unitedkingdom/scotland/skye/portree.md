Portree is the capital and main town of Skye, on the northeastern coast of the island. The harbour, framed by brightly painted houses and a backdrop of green hills, is the most photographed view on the island. The town has a supermarket, banks, restaurants, and accommodation of most types — it is the logical base for exploring northern Skye, the Trotternish peninsula, and Dunvegan.
