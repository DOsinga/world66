---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

Buses run to Portree from Inverness (around 3.5 hours), Fort William, and Glasgow. By car, take the A87 across the Skye Bridge through Broadford — the bridge has been toll-free since 2004. The town itself is small enough to walk around. Car and bicycle hire are available, but in summer book car hire well ahead as demand outstrips supply on the island.
