Sligachan is a small hamlet at the northern end of Glen Sligachan, at the junction of the main Skye roads. It is the classic starting point for walks into the Black Cuillin. The Sligachan Hotel, with its large outdoor beer garden, has been welcoming climbers and walkers since the Victorian era. An old humpback bridge over the Sligachan River is a well-known photographic subject, framed by the Cuillin peaks.
