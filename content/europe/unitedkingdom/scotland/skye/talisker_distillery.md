The only distillery on Skye, at Carbost on the south shore of Loch Harport, beneath the Cuillin hills. Talisker single malt is known for its peaty, maritime character — intense, smoky, and long-finishing. The distillery runs guided tours that cover the full production process; the tour ends with a tasting. The distillery shop sells a range of expressions and whisky-related products. One of the best distillery experiences in Scotland.
