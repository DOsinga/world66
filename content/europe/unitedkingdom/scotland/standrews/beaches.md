title: "Beaches in St Andrews"
type: section
---

St-Andrews has 3 main beaches, West Sands, Castle Sands and East Sands.
being very flat these beaches are subject to very big changes in
surface wether it is high or low tide (up to 300yards for the west
sands) sometimes they are pratically non existant and sometimes there
is enough space to build a full scale 18 hole golf course on it (with
space to spare).

West sand is the longest of the the three with approximately 1.5 miles,
it is a very nice beach to go for a walk, at the end of it there is
very often people playing with kites, kitsurfing, kiteboarding and
kitebuggying are of the most popular activities. People also come to
play some beach rugby/football.

Castle sand is the smallest with only about 200yards wide, it is just
below the castle. The 1st of may this is the location of the famous May
Dip where hundreds of students go for a dip in the north sea. Here the
water is quite a bit warmer than on the other beaches (expecially when
it is sunny).

East sands is a beach that is quite popular for a little walk especially with children because it is so close to town.
