St Andrews packs several good restaurants and pubs into a relatively small area. The nightlife might not be lively by big-city standards, but there are many places a traveller can rest with a glass of whisky or beer.

### [Byre Theatre](/europe/unitedkingdom/scotland/standrews/nightlifeandentertainment/byre_theatre)

The Byre Theatre offers a wide range of performances and events, an
exhibition space, conference facilities, two bars and a quality
café/restaurant. The shows are done by professionals and locals
(including student productions).

It has a very friendly and informal environment.
