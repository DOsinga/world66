As you go from Torridon [Fasaig] village towards Kinlochewe by car, postbus or foot, you have the bulk of the Liathach t your left. It will be clear when you leave it for the start of the Beinn Eighe range and you will find a carpark just above this and the start of a track between the two mountains.Follow it and, just after the col between the mountain, take the right fork to the corrie of Coire Mhic Fhearchair which is the only potential challenger to Coire Toll an Lochan on An Teallach to the title of Mainland Best. **It is worth walking here even if the further ascent is beyond you. **In this case consider going back and following the other part of the path right round Liathach until you reach the Alligin car park. 

If you are going on, it will be a matter of how far. Public transport is mightily limited so you may want to descend not too far up Glen Torridon but, if you have two cars, a much longer walk along the top is possible.
