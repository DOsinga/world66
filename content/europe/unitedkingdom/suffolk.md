Travel Guide

Suffolk is the one of the counties that make up East Anglia. Suffolk contains no cities. However there are many towns, including:

Ipswich, Lowestoft, Bury St. Edmunds and Woodbridge.

Suffolk contains the most easternly point in the UK, which is marked by a wind turbine. It is a very dry county.
