**Lowestoft** is a town in Suffolk and is the most easternly point of the [United Kingdom](/europe/unitedkingdom). Lowestoft has three train stations. There main destinations reachable by train are [Norwich](/europe/unitedkingdom/england/eastern_england/norwich), Ipswich & [London](/europe/unitedkingdom/england/london). Lowestoft is made up of several village that include Carton Colville, Kirkley & Pakefield. There are two football teams, they are **Lowestoft Town FC **& **Kirkley & Pakefield FC**. Lowestoft contains the theme park Pleasurewood Hills and a couple of zoos. Lowestoft has very good network of roads, and is easily excessable from [London](/europe/unitedkingdom/england/london) by the A12.
