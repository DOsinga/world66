United Kingdom with its rich heritage and unique culture attracts millions of tourists every year. **United Kingdom Must Do’s** are as featured below:

**1. The London Eye**

Visiting the London Eye is one of the must do’s when you are on a trip to the United Kingdom. The London Eye was constructed as a part of the millennium celebration but within a short while it drew admiration from both Londoners and tourists. In the last ten years, about 30 million people have visited this marvel. This 135 meter tall structure provides an amazing 360 degree view of the sprawling London city. If you plan to board the London Eye, make sure to get your ticket in advance as the queue is long enough.

**2. Thermae Bath Spa, Bath**

Thermae Bath Spa is another must do in the [United Kingdom](/europe/unitedkingdom/beaches). The natural hot water of the springs in Bath has long been popular among tourists as well as local inhabitants. In fact, the Bath springs are the only source of natural hot water in the UK. Opened in 2006 in the beautiful city called Bath, Thermae Bath Spa is a modern spa center with facilities including a naturally heated rooftop pool and an indoor pool, a large steam room and body treatment. The naturally heated water of the Bath springs is used here to refresh your body and mind. Undoubtedly, this spa will act like a tonic to pump fatigue out of your body.

**3. Thames River Cruise**

The Thames river cruise offers a beautiful view of [London](/europe/unitedkingdom/northernireland). A journey along the most famous river in the world will give you wonderful scenic coverage of almost all the wonderful sights of London. On one hand, the very look of classical structures of Westminster, Leicester square, the Strand, Covent Garden, Trafalgar Square from the waterfront will just take your breath away; on the other hand, the modern marvels like The O2, London Eye and The Thames Flood Barrier will make you spellbound.
**
4. The Falkirk Wheel, Scotland**

UK is not only about castles and churches but about modern engineering marvels like The London Eye and The Falkirk Wheel. The Falkirk Wheel may give you an impression of the 25th century Sci-Fi structure or that of a Spacecraft. The passage from North Sea to Atlantic was closed way back in 1933 but only after the construction of The Falkirk Wheel the navigation restarted in 2002 through central Scotland. It is the world’s one and only rotating boat lift.

**5. Shopping at Westfield, London**

If you want to splurge on shopping in [UK](/europe/unitedkingdom), Westfield is the place for you. A host of famous designer boutiques are scattered all over Westfield like Twenty8Twelve, Choice, Tiffany & C0. and Versace. All big international brands mostly make their UK debut at Westfield. Apart from plush boutiques or high street fashion, Westfield is also famed for jewelry shopping.
