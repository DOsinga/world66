The best resource for sights, hotels, restaurants, bars, what to do and see

Beaumaris is reached by going east from the bridges. it is a picturesque town whose crowning glory is one of the most scenic of the castles built by Edward I of England to contain Wales after his victories in battle late in the 13th century CE. The castle has a splendid mat and drawbridge.
