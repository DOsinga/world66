Travel Guide

There are no modern buildings at Din Lligwy. There are three different ages of ancient monuments.

The most recent is the chapel of ease that used to serve a medieval village, of hwich no real trace exists.

The next is a roman era settlement consisting of a walled enclosure with rectangular and round houses.

The oldest is a neolithic burial chamber with a huge capstone.
