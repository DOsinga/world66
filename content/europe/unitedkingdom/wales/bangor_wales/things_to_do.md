---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Bangor itself is compact, but the immediate surroundings offer plenty to keep you busy. The National Trust's Penrhyn Castle is the headline attraction just east of the city, while birdwatchers will find the Spinnies reserve rewarding. The Menai Strait shoreline makes for pleasant walks, and the mountains of Snowdonia are less than half an hour's drive south.
