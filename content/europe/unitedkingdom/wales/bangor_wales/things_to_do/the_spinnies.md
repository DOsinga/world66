title: "The Spinnies"
type: poi
category: "Sight"
latitude: 53.2310
longitude: -4.0790
---

Two bird hides maintained by the North Wales Wildlife Trust, near the shore just east of the Penrhyn Castle estate. Numerous little egrets can usually be seen from the hides, and with patience you may spot a kingfisher. A quiet, easy-to-reach spot for birdwatchers — no long walks required.
