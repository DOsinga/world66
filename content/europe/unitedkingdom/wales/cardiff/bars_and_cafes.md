---
title: "Bars and Cafes"
type: section
---

Cardiff has a lively pub and bar scene, particularly along St. Mary Street and in the arcades of the city centre. On rugby match days the city becomes one enormous pub.

**Owain Glyndŵr** is a standout — a pub with a genuine Welsh atmosphere, good beer and food, that feels like a country pub despite being in the centre of the capital.
