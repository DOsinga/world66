title: "Gower"
type: location
---

Although it is much smaller Gower rivals the more visited Pembrokeshire Coast National Park and the Lleyn Peninsula in the beauty of its coastal scenery, It sticks out to the west from near Swansea, from where you will have to get a bus unless you have your own transport. 

The scenery is amazingly varied for the size of the area. Perhaps Rhosili with the semi-island of Worms Head [tide tables available at NT hut at the cliff top] is the mst striking of all.
