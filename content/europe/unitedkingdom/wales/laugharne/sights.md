**Dylan Thomas Boathouse**

Here is where Dylan Thomas lived from 1949-1953. A very pretty, but small museum. 

**Laugharen Castel**

Built in the 12-th century, but completely restyled in Tudor style by John Perrot. It's a very stylish castle and well worth a visit. 

**Town Hall**

Accross the street from the entrance of the castle. The Italian style campanile was used as a prison cell in the old days.
