Machynlleth [pronounced roughly like Macuncleth] is a town passed heading south from Dolgellau. It makes a good cheap rail trip in an evening from Pwllheli in the [Lleyn Peninsula](/europe/unitedkingdom/wales/lleyn_peninsula)but is perhaps best known for the Centre for Alternative Technology a short distance away on the road north.
