Swansea's revived docklands, rebuilt around a 600-berth marina. The quarter has the National Waterfront Museum, the Dylan Thomas Theatre, a string of waterside restaurants and pubs, and a pleasant promenade out along the breakwater. The most attractive part of modern Swansea.
