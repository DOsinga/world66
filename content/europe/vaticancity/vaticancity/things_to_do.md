Almost everything worth seeing in Vatican City is concentrated in two areas: the basilica complex and the Vatican Museums. Between them, they can easily fill a full day. Book museum tickets in advance — the queue for walk-in entry can run two hours or more, while online booking (€20 plus a €5 booking fee) lets you walk straight in.
