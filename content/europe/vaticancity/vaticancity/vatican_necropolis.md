An ancient cemetery lies beneath St. Peter's Basilica, preserved since the 4th century when Constantine ordered the original basilica built directly over it. A guided excavation tour (90 minutes, ages 15 and up, maximum 12 per group) takes you underground to see the mausoleums, frescoes, and what is believed to be the tomb of St. Peter, identified by archaeologists in excavations beginning in 1939. Book well in advance — these tours sell out weeks ahead and cannot be booked on arrival.
