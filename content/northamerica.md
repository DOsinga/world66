North America consists of three countries, the [United States](/northamerica/unitedstates) , [Canada](/northamerica/canada) and [Greenland](/northamerica/greenland) . 



Of these countries, the U.S. receives by far the largest amount of travellers. [New York](/northamerica/unitedstates/newyorkstate/newyork) , [San Francisco](/northamerica/unitedstates/california/sanfranciscobayarea/sanfrancisco) , [Chicago](/northamerica/unitedstates/illinois/chicago) , [Boston](/northamerica/unitedstates/massachusetts/boston) , [Los Angeles](/northamerica/unitedstates/california/losangeles) , [Seattle](/northamerica/unitedstates/washington/seattle) are only some of the well known and exciting cities. These places may not have as many old monuments as you usually find in Europe, but sheer natural beauty makes up for the lack of history. The [Everglades](/northamerica/unitedstates/florida/everglades) , [Yellowstone](/northamerica/unitedstates/montana/yellowstone) and [Yosemite](/northamerica/unitedstates/california/highsierra/yosemite) are among the best National parks in the world. 

[Canada](/northamerica/canada) has great cities like [Toronto](/northamerica/canada/ontario/toronto) ,and [Vancouver](/northamerica/canada/britishcolumbia/vancouver) , where the view around Vancouver bay is stunning. Together with the two cities in the French speaking part, [Montreal](/northamerica/canada/quebec/montreal) and [Quebec City](/northamerica/canada/quebec/quebeccity) , these places with many museums and lots of sights are well worth a visit.In between there is lots of nature, woods, rivers, lakes, mountains so the richness of the countryside combined on a large area makes Canada the second biggest country in the world. 

[Greenland](/northamerica/greenland) is great for adventurous traveling if you are not afraid to catch a cold. Large parts of Greenland are covered with snow all year round. 

For info on [Mexico](/centralamericathecaribbean/mexico) , Central America and the Caribbean please have a look at the Central America & the Caribbean section.
