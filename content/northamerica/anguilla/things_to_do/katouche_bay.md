title: "Katouche Bay"
type: poi
category: "Sight"
latitude: 18.2130
longitude: -63.1100
---

One of the few places on Anguilla where you can feel like you have made some effort to earn your beach. A hiking trail leads through dry scrub forest past a cave before descending to a secluded cove. The walk is short but pleasant, and the swim at the end is the reward.
