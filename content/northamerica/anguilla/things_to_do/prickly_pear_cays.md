title: "Prickly Pear Cays"
type: poi
category: "Sight"
latitude: 18.2750
longitude: -63.1833
---

A short boat ride from Anguilla's north coast, Prickly Pear offers good reefs for snorkelling and diving alongside an uncrowded beach. Scrub Island to the east has more dramatic underwater scenery. Several operators run day trips from the main island — ask at your accommodation or at the Island Harbour fishing village.
