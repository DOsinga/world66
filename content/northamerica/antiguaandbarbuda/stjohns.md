Restored colonial buildings line the waterfront of [Antigua](/northamerica/antiguaandbarbuda)'s capital, which sits on the northwest coast of the island. Stroll down the old streets, visit the National Museum, and admire the twin-towered cathedral of St John the Divine. Redcliffe Quay, a restored set of old sugar warehouses, is a popular spot for shopping and restaurants.
