Reading about a destination before or during a trip adds texture to what you see. A few titles worth looking for on Aruba:

**Adventure Guide to Aruba, Bonaire & Curacao** by Lynne M. Sullivan — a practical guide to the three ABC islands with good coverage of diving, hiking, sailing and outdoor activities. Detailed and written for travelers who want to get off the resort strip.
