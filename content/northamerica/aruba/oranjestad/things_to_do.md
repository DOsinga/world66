---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Oranjestad is compact enough to explore on foot in a couple of hours. The Dutch-colonial centre, Fort Zoutman, and the harbour market are all within easy walking distance. The Archaeological Museum behind the San Francisco Church has pre-Columbian artefacts, and the small Numismatic Museum near the fort displays coins from over 350 countries — admission is free.
