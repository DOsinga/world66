An extremely in-depth guide to one of the most unique islands of the Bahamas. This glorified little sandbar is only 50 miles from Florida, but worlds apart from any place on earth. The Bimini Cruising Guide has everything you want to know (and some things you dind't know you wanted to know) about Bimini, and gives you a taste of what this little island has to offer.
