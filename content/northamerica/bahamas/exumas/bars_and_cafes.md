---
title: "Bars and Cafes"
type: section
---

Nightlife in the Exumas is low-key and centred on beach bars, hotel lounges, and the Fish Fry strip north of George Town. The signature drink is the Goombay Smash — rum, coconut, pineapple — and hunting for the best version on the island is a legitimate pastime. Things pick up on Friday and Sunday nights at the Fish Fry, and Wednesday nights at the Exuma Yacht Club upstairs bar.
