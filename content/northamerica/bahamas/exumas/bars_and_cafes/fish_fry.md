Not a single venue but a strip of shacks and bars along Queen's Highway just north of George Town. Friday and Sunday nights are the big ones — locals and visitors mingle around picnic tables while stalls serve fried fish, cracked conch, and conch salad, and music thumps from multiple directions. Shirley's, Aces, Big 12 Sports Bar, Charlie's, and The Out Deck all have their regulars. The atmosphere is casual and fun, and it is the closest the Exumas get to a proper night out.
