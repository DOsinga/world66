---
title: "Three Sisters Beach"
type: poi
latitude: 23.4900
longitude: -75.8500
---

Named for three distinctive rock formations just offshore, this is a long stretch of Atlantic-facing coast between Rokers Point and Moss Town. The water is rougher here than on the sheltered western beaches, and the reef creates good snorkelling. The elevated ridge behind the beach gives wide views along the coast. Sandals Emerald Bay resort sits at the southern end, but the beach itself is public and largely empty.
