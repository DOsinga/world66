A roadside shack on the beach in Steventon, north of George Town. Big D makes conch salad to order — chopped fresh conch with mango, hot peppers, onion, and citrus — and it may be the best on the island. Watching the preparation is half the experience. Closed Mondays. Simple, cheap, and exactly what the Exumas are about.
