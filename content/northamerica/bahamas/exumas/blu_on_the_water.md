The closest thing the Exumas have to fine dining. Chef Ty brings South Florida technique to island ingredients, with dishes that blend Caribbean, Asian, and contemporary styles. The conch burger and Pollo Exuma are favourites. Perched on a hill about ten minutes southeast of George Town, the view across the water is gorgeous, especially at sunset. Reservations recommended for dinner.
