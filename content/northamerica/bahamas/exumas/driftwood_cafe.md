type: poi

A small cafe in central George Town, good for breakfast sandwiches, pizzas, and a cold drink on the covered patio. The staff are friendly, the portions are solid, and it is one of the few places in town where you can get a reliable morning meal. Nothing fancy, but that is not what you are here for.
