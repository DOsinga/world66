A George Town institution on the waterfront. Monday nights are Rake 'n' Scrape nights — live Bahamian folk music with goatskin drums, saws, and accordions starting around 8pm. The grouper fingers are solid and the atmosphere is the most authentically Bahamian you will find in town. It is the kind of place where locals and visitors actually mix.
