---
title: "Forbes Hill Beach"
type: poi
latitude: 23.4500
longitude: -75.8650
---

At the entrance to Little Exuma, Forbes Hill has calm, crystal-clear water and interesting rock formations along the shore. There are scattered trees for shade and the beach is well-maintained. The water is shallow and the surf is low, making it a relaxed alternative to the more popular Tropic of Cancer Beach further south.
