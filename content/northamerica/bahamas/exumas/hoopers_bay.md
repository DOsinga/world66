---
title: "Hoopers Bay"
type: poi
latitude: 23.5300
longitude: -75.7800
---

A quiet bay about five minutes north of George Town, known for the sea turtles that swim near the piers and approach people in the water. The beach is small and the setting is peaceful — a good spot for a morning swim and some unexpected wildlife. Park off Queen's Highway and follow a short path to the shore.
