A long, calm beach on the western side of Great Exuma, between George Town and Hoopers Bay. The water is shallow and sheltered, making it good for families and weak swimmers. Tropical trees along the shore provide natural shade — a rarity on Exuma beaches. Splash Bar and Grill sits just behind the beach at Palm Bay, so you can combine a beach day with cold drinks and conch fritters without moving far.

There is a small parking area off Queen's Highway. The beach is rarely busy even in high season.
