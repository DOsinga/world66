A beachfront seafood grill in Williams Town on Little Exuma, about 25 minutes south of George Town. Grilled lobster tail with fried onions and rice and beans is the thing to order. The shaded patio overlooks the water, and sharks and stingrays sometimes cruise the shallows in front of you while you eat. Mom's Bakery next door sells bread and pastries. Call ahead — they close when the catch runs out.
