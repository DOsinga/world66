At the Fish Fry strip north of George Town on Queen's Highway, Shirley's is a local favourite for deep-fried grouper, crab cakes, and coconut-garlic grouper wraps. The portions are large and the prices are fair by Exuma standards. There is Wi-Fi, which makes it popular with the cruising sailor crowd. The Fish Fry area comes alive on Friday and Sunday evenings with multiple stalls, music, and a festive atmosphere.
