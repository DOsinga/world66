At Palm Bay Beach Club, about five minutes from George Town, Splash has a waterfront patio with seven bar swings instead of stools — exactly as fun as it sounds. Conch fritters are good, the Goombay Smash is strong, and Jolly Hall Beach is right there when you need to cool off. Open throughout the day.
