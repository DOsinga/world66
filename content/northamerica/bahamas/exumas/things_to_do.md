type: section
---

The Exumas are about water. Snorkelling, diving, boating, and beach-hopping fill most days here, and the clarity of the sea makes even a lazy swim feel like an event. The cays north of Great Exuma hold the headline attractions — swimming pigs, underwater grottoes, protected reefs — but there is plenty on the main islands too, from kayaking through mangrove creeks to exploring quiet settlements where not much has changed in decades.
