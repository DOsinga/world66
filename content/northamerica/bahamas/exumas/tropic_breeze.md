A chef-run beach grill in Williams Town, Little Exuma — about 20 minutes south of George Town. The seafood platter, lobster panini, and lobster tacos are all good. The covered patio faces the water and the quality is a cut above most beach shacks. Call ahead to confirm they are open and what is on; it is a small operation and runs on whatever is fresh.
