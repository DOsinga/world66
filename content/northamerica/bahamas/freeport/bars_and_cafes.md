---
title: "Bars and Cafes"
type: section
---

Port Lucaya Marketplace is the centre of Freeport's nightlife — a waterfront complex of bars, restaurants, and live music venues that stays active into the early hours. The atmosphere is lively but relaxed. Bahamas Mama Bar is a small, locally popular spot open until 4am, playing rock and reggae and serving the house Bahamas Mama cocktail (rum, coconut juice, pineapple juice, and grenadine). Count Basie Square in Port Lucaya often has live Bahamian music.
