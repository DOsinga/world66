---
title: "Getting Around"
type: section
---

Renting a car is the most practical way to explore Grand Bahama Island — public transport is limited and the main sights (Lucayan National Park, the beaches) are spread out. Remember to drive on the left. Taxis are available at the airport and from hotels. Brad's Car Rental at Freeport International Airport is a local option; international chains also have counters. Within Port Lucaya and the nearby beach areas, walking is fine. Freeport International Airport receives direct flights from several Florida cities and from Nassau.
