---
title: "When to Go"
type: section
---

Freeport has a tropical climate and is pleasant year-round, but the best time to visit is mid-December through April — the dry season with warm but not oppressive temperatures (averaging 24°C). This is also peak season with higher prices. Summer (June–August) is hot and humid but still good for beach and water activities. Hurricane season runs June to November, with the peak risk in August and September; this is when prices are lowest and the island is quietest.
