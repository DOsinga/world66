Located right on the harbor between the Straw Market and the Hilton Hotel, this Mexican Restaurant has a great view of everything that goes by in the harbor. Senor Frog's is really HOPPING! The environment is very party-like with lots of friendly people and Karaoke singing. If you don't smile while you're there, they might hold you down and tickle you. There is also a bar and a shop (Shop open 9 to 11pm). Restaurant hours: 10am-1am.
