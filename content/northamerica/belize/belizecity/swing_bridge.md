The Swing Bridge over Haulover Creek is the symbolic centre of Belize City — the only manually operated swing bridge still in daily use in the Americas. It is swung open twice a day (at 5:30 am and 5:30 pm) to allow boat traffic through. Standing on the bridge gives a good view of the city's two halves and the waterfront life below.
