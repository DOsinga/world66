Housed in a beautifully restored Victorian colonial jail on Gabourel Lane, the Museum of Belize covers the country's natural history, archaeology, and colonial past. The Maya jade collection and the exhibits on Belize's reef system are highlights. One of the better museums in Central America for its size.
