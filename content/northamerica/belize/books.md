**Travel guides**

The Moon Belize guide (various editions) is the most thorough printed guide to the country and is updated regularly. Lonely Planet covered Belize as part of its Guatemala, Belize & Yucatán guide for many years.

**Culture and history**

*Belize: A Concise History* by Brian Dyde gives a solid overview of the country's colonial past and road to independence.

*Sastun: My Apprenticeship with a Maya Healer* by Rosita Arvigo (ISBN 006250259X) is a memorable account of learning traditional Maya medicine from a healer in the Cayo district. Arvigo went on to found the Ix Chel Tropical Research Foundation and helped preserve vast tracts of jungle.

*The Mopan: Culture and Ethnicity in a Changing Belizean Community* by James R. Gregory (ISBN 0913134740) is an academic study of the Mopan Maya, useful background if you're spending time in the west.

**Natural history**

*A Field Guide to the Birds of Mexico and Northern Central America* by Steve Howell and Sophie Webb is the best birding reference for Belize. The country has over 600 recorded species.

*Reef Fish Identification: Tropical Pacific* — if you're diving the reef, a waterproof identification guide is worth packing.
