**Belize City**** Nightlife Destinations**

Although most travelers aren&rsquo;t aware of the lively **Belize City**** nightlife** scene, the city has something to offer to visitors who want to have some late-night fun.

#### **Princess Casino**

The Princess Casino is located inside the Princess Hotel, right on the waterfront of Belize City, and is the largest casino in Central America. Access to it is public, so even people who aren&rsquo;t guests of the hotel can enter. However, you should carry your ID card or passport; otherwise, the guards won&rsquo;t let you inside. The casino offers the classic blackjack, Caribbean poker and American roulette and is equipped with more than 400 different gaming machines. It is open every day from 12 a.m. to 4 a.m. and works 365 days a year.

#### **Club Calypso**

Club Calypso is located right next to the Princess Hotel. It is a nice bar set on a pier over the water, offering a pleasant atmosphere for going out at night. The fun is definitely guaranteed in here, with every Thursday being Karaoke night and local bands performing every weekend, when the place gets literally packed. The music performed is usually punta or reggae. Except for the wonderful atmosphere, the club offers an excellent selection of drinks and some delicious appetizers including seafood, chicken dishes and hamburgers.

#### **Radisson Poolside Bar**

This classy bar is located inside the Radisson hotel in Belize and is popular both among the city elite and travelers from all over the world. The bar impresses with a wide selection of exotic cocktails and a menu of delicious appetizers. The entertainment is guaranteed, with relaxing lounge music being performed live every day. The open-air part of the bar offers some spectacular views to the wonderful Radisson garden and to the Caribbean.

#### **Caesar&rsquo;s Palace**

The Caesar&rsquo;s Palace is a popular small bar situated in the northern part of Belize City. It is open only during the weekends and usually gets overcrowded. You will surely have a great time, especially if you like the loud companies and Latin music.

#### **Copa Cabana Latin Club**

An excellent dance club located very close to the Belize City harbor, the Copa Cabana Latin club is a great place to try out your moves under the sounds of merengue and salsa music. Have a couple of drinks, make some new friends and enjoy some wonderful moments.
