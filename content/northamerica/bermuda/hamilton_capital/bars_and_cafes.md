---
title: "Bars and Cafes"
type: section
---

The Pickled Onion on Front Street is the liveliest bar in Hamilton — waterfront tables, live music seven nights a week in season, and a crowd of locals and visitors. North Rock Brewing Company is Bermuda's only brewpub, with fresh craft beers and a good pub-food menu. Opus Bar and Lounge (Reid Street) is quieter and smarter, good for wine and espresso. Robin Hood Pub on Richmond Road is a popular sports bar. The Swizzle Inn, just outside the city, claims to have invented the Dark 'n' Stormy rum cocktail (or at least the Rum Swizzle) and is a Bermudian institution.
