title: "Day Trips in Hamilton (Capital City)"
type: section
---

Since there are many options for things to see and do in Bermuda, you can plan a variety of **Hamilton Day Trips** while vacationing there. Here are some highly recommended day trip ideas that will accommodate all types of travelers.

**Crystal and Fantasy Caves**

Visit the Crystal Caves consisting of beautiful crystal stalactites and stalagmites which surround a deep clear lake. Or visit Crystal's sister cave, the Fantasy Cave which is illuminated by calcite mineral formations that look like frozen waterfalls. These are definitely two of Bermuda's must see natural wonders.

**Fort Hamilton**

This 19th century Victorian Fortress has lavish gardens surrounding the main fort, plenty of tunnels to explore and former prisons to view. The reason the fort was initially built was to protect the United Kingdom's Royal Naval Dockyard from attack by the United States during the Civil War.

**Bermuda Beaches **

Beaches along the Bermuda coastline are possibly some of the most beautiful in the world. With their pink sands, supreme surf and warm sun, it's hard to pick just one. A few tourist favorites are Horseshoe Bay Beach, Astwood Cove Beach and Elbow Beach. You will find so much to do at each including kayaking, swimming, snorkeling, windsurfing, surfing and more.
**
Go Golfing**

There are several golf courses in Bermuda and one course in particular is even claimed to be one of the best public golf courses in the world. That would be The Port Royal Golf Course which is a beautiful course with spectacular ocean views from every green or hole.

**Scuba Diving and Snorkeling **

Find many qualified dive shops and diving charters available to go scuba diving or snorkeling. There are several places to dive and explore including shipwrecks and reefs. You can either book a charter and go on a dive with professionals, or if you're experienced, there are places you can rent equipment and enjoy diving or snorkeling on your own. One such location is Tobacco Bay Beach which rents all necessary equipment for very affordable prices. No matter how you go about it, Hamilton Bermuda waters are definitely ideal to experience it all.

**Bermuda Spas **

Feel like a day of pampering, relaxation and just getting away from everything and everyone? Treat yourself to a spa day at one of the excellent spa locations in Hamilton Bermuda. Two of the best spas in the area belong to Cambridge Beach Resort and The Fairmont Southampton. Spend the day here and leave feeling rejuvenated and like a whole new you.
