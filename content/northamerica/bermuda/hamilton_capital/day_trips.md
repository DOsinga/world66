---
title: "Day Trips"
type: section
---

**Royal Naval Dockyard** in Sandys Parish (accessible by fast ferry from Hamilton in 20 minutes) is Bermuda's biggest half-day excursion — the National Museum of Bermuda occupies the old commissioner's house and ramparts, with extensive maritime and military collections, and Dolphin Quest is located in the Keep Pond.

**Crystal Caves** in Hamilton Parish (bus accessible) are a genuine natural wonder: limestone caverns with pontoon bridges over deep clear cave lakes, formed over millions of years. The adjacent Fantasy Cave is equally impressive.

**St George's** in the east is Bermuda's first capital (1612) and a UNESCO World Heritage Site: narrow lanes, 17th-century buildings, and the Unfinished Church. Accessible by bus in 40 minutes.

**Scuba diving and snorkelling**: Bermuda has some of the Atlantic's best wreck diving, with over 300 wrecks in the surrounding waters. Dive operators in Hamilton and at Dockyard run daily trips.
