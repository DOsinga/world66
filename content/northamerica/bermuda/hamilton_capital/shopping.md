---
title: "Shopping in Hamilton (Capital City)"
type: section
---

The **Hamilton shopping**
experience will leave you satisfied and will also allow you to pick up some
great stuff at very reasonable prices. There are several places where you can “shop
till you drop.” Here are some suggestions.

**The 27th
Century Boutique**

This is one of the popular places to shop for clothes in
Hamilton. The 27th Century Boutique houses exclusive clothing lines
from Jack F, Orly, Joseph Ribkoff and other manufacturers from Costa Rica,
Holland, Paris, Jamaica, London and Belgium. You can find things for children
and adults in this fine store.

**The Bermuda Triangle**

Named after the mysterious Caribbean’s “Bermuda’s Triangle,”
this shop is packed with the most fascinating clothing designs and jewelries. It also offers a large selection of arts and crafts, signature Bermudian cottage
designs, ornaments and gifts. You can pick up some fine hand-crafted items at
this place, including a host of other things.

**Brown and Company**

Situated in the middle of the Hamilton, Brown and Company is one
of the largest shopping venues in the city. It has a complete line of must-haves
from clothes, jewelry and books to fragrances, home décor and more. You can also
eat out with your friends and choose from gourmet coffee, desserts, pastries
and a lot more from the Birdcage Cafes. If you are looking for greeting cards,
books and gift ideas you can find them at Hallmark bookstore inside Brown and
Company.

**The Clock Tower Mall**

It is the most stunning shopping venue in the city of
Hamilton. It has a 100-foot tower and houses Bermuda’s major department stores
and boutiques. The most popular section in the Clock Tower Mall is the Dockyard
Section which features product line from A.S. Cooper, glassworks, arts and
crafts. The Clock Tower Mall is open 7 days a week from 9:00 a.m. to 6:00 p.m.

**A.S. Cooper and Sons
LTD**

Established during 1897, A.S. Cooper and Sons LTD. is
located on Front Street, Hamilton. It houses fine crystals and high quality
dinnerware and other gifts ideas and figurines. Its cosmetic and fragrance
department carries popular brands international and local brands of perfume and
make-up. A.S Cooper carries fine
jewelries from local and international jewelers. It also features commodities
from the A.S Cooper family like A.S. Copper Express that offers a huge selection
of clothing and accessories. A.S. Cooper Homes offers home décor, candles and
gift and A.S Cooper Harbourside, on the other hand, offers fragrances, sportswear,
custom jewelry and accessories.
