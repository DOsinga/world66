Classic burgers, smoke house chicken and ribs, really big sandwiches, thick shakes and lots of great American food draw the crowds to this high energy "temple of rock 'n roll". Memorabilia includes famous gold and platinum records, clothing worn by the stars, and retired instruments from legendary musicians. The restaurant attracts all types and ages of people. Open from lunch till late.
