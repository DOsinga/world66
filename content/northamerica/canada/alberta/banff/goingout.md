---
title: "Bars and Nightlife"
type: section
---

Banff punches well above its weight for nightlife, given its size. The main strip along Banff Avenue and the side streets off it concentrate a good range of bars, pubs, and clubs catering to the mix of locals, ski bums, and international visitors. Expect lively evenings after the slopes close, with live music most weekends and a friendly après-ski atmosphere year-round.
