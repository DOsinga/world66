A western cantina at 203 Caribou Street with a split personality: downstairs is a casual restaurant serving Tex-Mex staples like nachos, burritos, and quesadillas, while upstairs the rooftop patio is one of Banff's best spots for a drink on a warm evening. The crowd is relaxed and unpretentious — ski bums in winter, hikers in summer. Margaritas are the move here. It fills up fast on weekends, so arrive early if you want a spot on the roof.
