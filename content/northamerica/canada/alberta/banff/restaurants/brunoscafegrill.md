Named after local photographer and mountain guide Bruno Engler, this eclectic and fun bistro is a cozy, casual spot. Breakfast 'til 5 pm. Burgers, pizza, fresh squeezed juices, vegetarian dishes, and locally brewed beer and roasted coffee keep the locals coming. Lunch $6-$9; entrées $9-$12. Daily 7 am-11 pm ('til 2 am during summer).  Tel: 1 403 762-8115  Address: 304 Caribou St, Banff Alberta T0L 0C0
