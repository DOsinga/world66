Constructed of hand-hewn timber, this restaurant prepares innovative Rocky Mountain cuisine, from local meats, herbs and berries. Breakfast is Canadian favourites and dishes like smoked duck Benedict, and zucchini and carrot French toast. Homemade soups, unique salads, sandwiches, and mountain tea for two comprise lunch. Dinner includes game platter, roasted salmon, Alberta beef tenderloin, caribou, buffalo rib-eye steak and rack of venison. Fresh breads and desserts.
