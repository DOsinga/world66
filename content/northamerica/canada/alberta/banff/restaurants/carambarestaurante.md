New to Banff, Caramba (Spanish for "WOW") has been wowing travellers in Whistler for some time. Fun, casual atmosphere, and open kitchen with forno pizza oven, rotisserie and flaming grill. Many dishes such as calamari, penne Putanesca and Melanzana pizza are Mediterranean-inspired, but BC seafood, Alberta beef and Asian specialties share the menu. Food is flavourful and generous portions invite sharing (entrees $10-$15). Innovative breakfasts ($6-$10) including buffet.
