Located in the heart of downtown Banff, Fuze Finer Dining is one of the Town's newest dining additions.

With a globally inspired menu, diners will be able to find flavors and tastes from around the world. East indian, French, Thai, Canadian are just some of the influences found on Fuze's award winning menu.

A wine selection that focuses on smaller production, harder-to-find wines adds to the uniqueness of this fine dining establishment.

When we say fine dining, don't think starch white table cloths and stuffy service. Fuze is relaxing and very modern in its appearance. While not something you would expect in a mountain town, Fuze brings a sense of sophistication and hipness to the local dining scene.
