Creative Canadian cuisine and select Canadian wines and beers at this elegant new restaurant operated by the well-regarded Ticino group. Continental breakfast buffet ($6), full buffet ($9) or order a la carte. Daily 7-10:30 am and 5:30-10:30 pm.
