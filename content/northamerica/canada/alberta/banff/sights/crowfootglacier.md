title: "Crowfoot Glacier ."
type: poi

---

One of over a hundred glaciers that can be seen along the Icefields Parkway. Years ago, this glacier resembled a crows foot, with three large toe-like extensions. The lower "toe" of this foot has receded so much that only two toes remain. 
 Address: 197 km (123 mi) S of Jasper/33 km (20.6 mi) N of Lake Louise.
