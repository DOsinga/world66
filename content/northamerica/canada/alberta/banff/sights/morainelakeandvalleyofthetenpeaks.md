title: "Moraine Lake and Valley of the Ten Peaks ."
type: poi
---

Formerly pictured on the back of the Canadian $20 bill, this emerald green lake is set before a backdrop of sharp peaks. To the N is Mount Temple, the highest mountain in the Bow Range and third highest in Banff National Park, A good area for hiking. Visit the Parks Canada Information Bureau in the town of Banff or the Visitor Centre in Lake Louise for information on the trails.  Address: 12 km (7.5 mi) E from Lake Louise access road -
