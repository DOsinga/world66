No other guide covers the Canadian Rockies as thoroughly as this insightful and up-to-date Handbook. Andrew Hempstead combines practical travel tips with a keen insider's perspective to capture the drama of this awe-inspiring region of North America. Extensive restaurant and lodging options, detailed trail descriptions, suggestions for maximizing your visits to the five national parks, and easy-to-use maps make this the perfect book for anyone exploring the area. If you would like to see the author's personal website, please visit Andrew Hempstead.
