Calgary is serviced by two major highways; the Trans-Canada (1) running East/West, and the Queen Elizabeth II Highway running North/South. The Trans-Canada stretches across [Canada](/northamerica/canada), and connects Calgary to nearly every major city in the country, including [Vancouver](/northamerica/canada/britishcolumbia/vancouver), [Toronto](/northamerica/canada/ontario/toronto), and [Montreal](/northamerica/canada/quebec/montreal). The QEII meanwhile, links Calgary to the provincial capital, [Edmonton](/northamerica/canada/alberta/edmonton), as well as the [United States](/northamerica/unitedstates), where it connects with I-15 at the border.
