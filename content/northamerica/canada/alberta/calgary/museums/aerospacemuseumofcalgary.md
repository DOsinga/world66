Aircraft, aeronautical engines and artifacts trace the history of the aviation industry from one of Calgary’s first airplanes, the “West Wind” in 1914, to today’s jets. The exhibit History of Flight explores the human quest for flight. On-going restoration work is on view in the hangar. Daily 10 am - 5 pm. $6 adults; $3.50 stu/ sen; $2 child, family $15. Bus #57 from the Whitehorn C-Train stop.   Tel: 1 403 250-3752  Address: 4629 McCall Way NE
