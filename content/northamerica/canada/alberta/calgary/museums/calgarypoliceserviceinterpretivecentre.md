type: poi

This educational centre puts crime under the microscope and is designed to “tell it like it is” about crime. Junior detectives can match wits with interactive computer/video displays, sit at the wheel of a computer-equipped police car and try on police uniforms. Call for hours. $2 adults, free sen/youth. At the City Hall C-Train stop.   Tel: 1 403 268-4566  Address: 2nd level, 316 - 7 Ave SE Calgary
