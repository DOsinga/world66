The Alberta Wheat Pool’s museum features a model train display depicting the movement of grain from the prairies through the Rockies to Vancouver, a working model grain elevator, films and other artifacts describing the history of grains up to the present. Open year round Mon to Fri 10 am - 4 pm. Free. Parking on Stampede Grounds $4.50. Anderson C-Train to Victoria Park/Stampede stop. 
 
 Tel: 1 403 263-4594 
 Address: Plus 15 Level, Roundup Centre, Stampede Park
