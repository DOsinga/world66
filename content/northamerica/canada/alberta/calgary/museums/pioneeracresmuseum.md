The museum has one of the largest exhibits of antique farm machinery used by the pioneers of western Canada. Also a 1914 home, a blacksmith shop and the Women's World building featuring artifacts from the early part of the 20th century. Visit the gift shop. May 29 Model and Toy Show. Aug 6, 7 and 8 Annual Show Includes an antique tractor pull; admission $7(trailor parking). July 24 Horse Driving Competition. Admission is $5 for adults; children 12 & under free.
