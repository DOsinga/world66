THE CAT HOUSE INC. is definitely one of the most unique stores in Calgary - a store devoted entirely to cats and cat people. It is both a gift store for cat lovers, with thousands of gift items with a cat motif for people (such as jewellery, stationery, clothing, home decor, etc) as well as a pet store exclusively for cats, carrying anything you need to care for or pamper a cat (from food and litter right up to large climbing/scratching furniture for the cats). THE CAT HOUSE the largest selection of cat-related merchandise in all of Canada. If you're travelling and missing your own cats you can even visit with the two resident shop cats: Lesley Anne and Buddy Guy. Located in The Stadium Shopping Centre on the TransCanada Highway at 29th Street NW, THE CAT HOUSE INC is easy to find, with lots of free parking.
