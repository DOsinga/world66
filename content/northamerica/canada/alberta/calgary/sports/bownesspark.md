title: "Bowness Park ."
type: location
---

This northwest park occupies 30 hectares in the river valley. Take a brisk winter walk or do some cross-country skiing on the walking trails that circle the park. Check out the Stoney Trail supension bridge and majestic firs at the west end of the park. The lagoon becomes a skating rink in the winter months. Warm-up at the Lagoon Cafe with a cappuccino, open daily 10 am - 6 pm. Tel: 1 403 221-3955 Address: 8900 - 48 Ave NW
