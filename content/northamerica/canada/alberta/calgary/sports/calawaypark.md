Western Canada’s largest outdoor family amusement park is in its 18th year of providing fun and entertainment. A total of 27 rides. There are daily live musical shows; ball crawl; 20 eateries; shopping; western-themed mini-golf. Calaway’s RV park and campground is footsteps away. Sept 5 to Oct 12 '98 wknds 11 am - 6 pm. Complete park admission: adults $18.50, child $12.50. $10 over 50. 240-3824 for events. Located 10 km (6 miles) west on Trans-Canada Hwy. 240-3822. on grounds; call about rides. Fax: 1 403 242-3885
