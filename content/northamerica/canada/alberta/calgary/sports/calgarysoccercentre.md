The first purpose-built indoor soccer facility in North America, the centre has two 63-foot clearspan roofs covering seven indoor soccer fields, three with flooring to allow in-line hockey and ball hockey, spectator seating and support facilities. A restaurant and pub are also on-site.   Tel: 1 403 279-8453  Address: 7000 - 48 St SE, travel east on Glenmore Tr to 52 St, north on 52 St a few blocks  ** Sarah Berens:** This is the best place ever to be. I know as a 13-year-old that I could hang out here all weekend. During the winter you can see a wide range of soccer abilities ranging from the youngest kids in the Under 5 catagory to the 50+ age groups as well. There is a balance between the Girls and Boys, as soccer has become one of the most popular sports amoungst kids these days. During the Spring, Summer and Fall months you can see some soccerm fencing tournaments, lacrosse, field hockey, and a variety of groups that use this wonderful facility to center around. I highly recomend the Calgary Soccer Center, as I have been in there many times in my life for 3 or 4 different sports. I hope that when you take the time to go and see this wonderful facility, that you will enjoy it as much as I have.
