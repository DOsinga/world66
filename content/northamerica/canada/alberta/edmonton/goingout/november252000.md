Global Visions Festival .  Much more than just a film festival the Global Visions Festival is a cultural, educational, and inspirational event. Set in the Edmonton Arts district (the Citadel Theatre and Stanly A. Milner Library) Global Visions offers a program of documentary films that expose the realities of our world. In addition to local and international films, visit the Global Marketplace, art exhibit, cultural performances, workshops and meet filmmakers.
