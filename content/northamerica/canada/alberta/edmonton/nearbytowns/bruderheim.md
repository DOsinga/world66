Bruderheim means “home of the brethren” and was named by German
Moravian settlers in 1894. The Moravian Church built in 1895,
commemorates the first Protestant congregation in Western Canada and is
still used., 796-3775. Skaro Shrine, 22 km(13.75 mi) northeast of town
on Hwy 45, is made of local fieldstone and is the object of an annual
pilgrimage in mid-Aug by descendants of Ukrainian pioneers.
