This town, south of Edmonton on Hwy. 2, and 5 minutes from the
International Airport has two aquatic centres and a world class curling
facility, extensive parks and natural areas, Telford Lake, Leduc Woods
House Museum and an active arts and cultural community, as well as the
Leduc No. 1 Oilwell, celebrating its 50th anniversary this summer. For
more information on the town or to purchase a Leduc No. 1 Commemorative
Coin call 986-5454. Directions: South of Edmonton on Hwy. 2, and 5
