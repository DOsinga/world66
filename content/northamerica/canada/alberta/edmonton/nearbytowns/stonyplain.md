Stony Plain offers a community alive with arts and culture, the home of
15 life size murals painted on buildings by Albertan artists as well as
the Oppertshauser Art Gallery and Parkland Potters' Guild. Miles of
pathways through natural forests offer a haven for running and cycling
enthusiasts while a championship 18-hole golf course, tennis courts,
baseball diamonds and outdoor swimming pools provide others with plenty
to do. While in town visit the Multicultural Centre, the Country Lace
Cottage Tea House and Gift Shop, Andrew Wolf Winery as well as many
unique shops and galleries. Call for more information. Tel: 1 780
963-2151 Address: Located west of the city on hwy. 16 or 16X
