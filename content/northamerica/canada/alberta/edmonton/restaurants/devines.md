This renovated house on a hilltop overlooking the river valley is a cozy place for innovative hors d'oeuvre and intimate lunches and dinners. Cuisine is a blend of Canadian and French. Offerings from the dinner menu include steamed Atlantic mussels, rack of lamb and a variety of home made pâtés. Patios off the lounge and main dining area offer a stunning view. Sun to Thu 11 am - 11 pm; Fri & Sat 11 am - midnight. (AE, DC, MC,V)  Tel: 1 780 482-6402  Address: 9712 - 111 Street Edmonton Alberta
