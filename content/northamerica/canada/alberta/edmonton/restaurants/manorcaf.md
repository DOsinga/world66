Situated in a historic home circa 1929, this handsome restaurant serves up a fusion of international flavors with offerings such as Asian prawn salad, Indonesian spring rolls and beef and chicken Thai sticks. Main course selections include smoked salmon pasta, schnitzel and jambalaya. Mon to Sat 11 am - 11 pm; Sun 5 pm - 10 pm.
