---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Edmonton's main attractions are the **North Saskatchewan River Valley** (North America's largest urban parkland, with a ribbon of trails through the middle of the city), the **Royal Alberta Museum**, the **Muttart Conservatory** (four pyramid-shaped greenhouses of biome plants), and — for better or worse — the **West Edmonton Mall**, for decades the largest shopping mall in the world and still one of Edmonton's best-known export images.
