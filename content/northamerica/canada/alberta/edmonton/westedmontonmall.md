This exceptional shopping destination boasts a full-sized skating rink, a huge indoor waterpark, a submarine lagoon and the largest indoor amusement park in the world. Of course, there is always the shopping, too, with almost 1,000 retail outlets. There is also a very classy hotel (Fantasyland), so you never need to go outside while visiting.
