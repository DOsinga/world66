Located 10 minutes from the town of Jasper, the Tramway is Canada's longest aerial tram operation. The upper terminal at 2,500 metres (8,200 ft) offers spectacular view of the surrounding area. A one-way trip in the 30 passenger cabin takes 7 minutes. Cafeteria, dining room, lounge and gift shops are offered.
