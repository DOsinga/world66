Maligne Lake offers one of the largest attractions in Jasper National Park. Visitors can choose from scenic cruises to world-famous Spirit Island, white water rafting, horsebackriding, guided trout fishing, hiking, canoe renting and shopping. In winter guided hiking tours of the frozen Maligne Canyon and X-country skiing are very popular.
