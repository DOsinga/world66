title: "Miette Hot Springs ."
type: location
type: "general"
---

- Run by the Canadian Parks Service, the complex consists of two outdoor bathing pools - one deep and one shallow - fed by hot sulphur springwater chlorinated and cooled to approximately 40 degrees Celsius (104 degrees F). An interpretative display explains the local geology, and hiking trails and a picnic area are available.
Tel: 1 780 866-3939
Address: Jasper National Park, Jasper, Alberta.
