Mount Edith Cavell, 3363 m above sea level, was named after a British nurse who was executed by the Germans for helping allied troops escape. Angel Glacier lies in a saddle area on the NE slope.  Address: 29 km (11.8) S of Jasper on Hwy. 93, then Hwy. 93A
