From here you can view the Sunwapta River, which is known as a braided river. The bottom of the valley consists of sand and gravel, which spreads the river across the valley floor in interlacing channels. The Stutfield Glacier holds a stunning pair of ice falls which flow down Mt. Stutfield.

Location: 95 km (59.3 mi) south of Jasper l35 km (84.3 mi) north of Lake Louise.
