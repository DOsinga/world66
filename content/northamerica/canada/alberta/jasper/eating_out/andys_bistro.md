An intimate candlelit bistro where owner Andy Allenbach runs the kitchen himself. The menu shifts with the seasons — expect game, AAA beef, lamb, pasta, and fish, all presented with care. Good Canadian wine list and an interesting selection of port and scotch. Reservations recommended. 606 Patricia Street.
