After exploring the outdoors, come inside, relax, and have some fun.

**Place to go outs**

**Astoria Hotel . **

De'd Dog Bar & Grill has big-screen TV, video lottery, pool table and darts. Ales and single malt scotch. Off-sales.  Tel: 1 (403) 852-3351  Address: 404 Connaught Dr,  

**Athabasca Hotel . **

Night Club hosts new music bands and dancing; pub and games room adjacent. O'Shea's Lounge has video lottery and big screen TV. 11 am-2 am.  Tel: 1 (403) 852-3386  Address: 510 Patricia St,  

**Chateau Jasper . **

Intimate, elegant Bonhomme Lounge has a harpist most nights.  Tel: 1 (403) 852-5644  Address: 96 Geikie St,  

**Lobstick Lodge . **

Skyline Lounge offers a mountain view, fieldstone fireplace, and big pillowed couches. Happy hr 5-8 pm. Patio with BBQ menu (summer).  Tel: 1 (403) 852-4431  Address: 3rd flr, Lobstick Lodge, Geikie and Juniper St,  

**Nick's Bar . **

Glass atrium with sports and ski movies on big-screen TV. Occasional pianist.  Tel: 1 (403) 852-4966  Address: Juniper St between Connaught Dr and Geikie St,  

**Villa Caruso . **

Comfortable lounge TV and snacks. Outdoor decks with mountain views.  Toll Free: 1 (403) 852-3920  Address: Corner, Connaught Dr and Hazel Ave,  

**Whistlers Inn . **

Neighbourhood pub--fireplace, 10 ales on tap, darts, pool table, big-screen sports TV and occasional entertainment at Whistle Stop Pub.  Tel: 1 (403) 852-3361  Address: 105 Miette Ave,  

**Buckles Saloon . **

Western food and decor. Maligne Lodge, W end Connaught Dr, 852-7074.  

**Earl's . **

Bar and outdoor patio with view. Margarita Mon; Hawaii 5-0 Tues; martini Thurs specials.  Tel: 1 780 852-2393  Address: 2nd fl, 600 Patricia St, Jasper AB T0E 1E0  

**Jasper Inn . **

Dining lounge overlooks the pool. Sports TV. Snacks, including fondue.  Tel: 1 (403) 852-3232  Address: Geikie and Bonhomme Sts,  

**Jasper Park Lodge . **

Emerald Lounge offers BBQs on the patio (summer) and sandwiches and casseroles by the fireplace. Palisade Lounge is an elegant setting for light fare and cocktails. Both have spectacular views of Lac Beauvert and patio decks. Tent City Sports Lounge offers pub fare, billiards and darts.  Tel: 1 [more..](/northamerica/canada/alberta/jasper/goingout/jasperparklodge)

**Jasper Pizza Place . **

Bar and restaurant with beers on tap, pool tables, and wood-burning pizza oven. Roof top patio.  Tel: 1 (403) 852-3225  Address: 402 Connaught Dr,  

**Marmot Lodge . **

Cozy English lodge atmosphere at Fireside Lounge. Board games. Fri jazz piano, Sat local talent, Sun jam night. 5 pm-mid (happy hr 5-8 pm). BBQ patio for groups (summer). 86 Connaught Dr, 852-4471.  

**Pete's on Patricia . **

High-energy dancing. DJ and occasional live music (quieter during the day). Mountain biking, snowboarding, music videos and sports on big screen TV.  Tel: 1 780 852-6262  Address: 2nd fl, 614 Patricia St, Jasper AB T0E 1E0  

**Chaba Theatre . **

First-run movies are screened evenings in two comfortable theatres. $6.75 adult, $5.75 youth, $4.75 senior, $3.50 child.  Tel: 1 780 852-4749  Address: 604 Connaught Dr. Jasper AB
