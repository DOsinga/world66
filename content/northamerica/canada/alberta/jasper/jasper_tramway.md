Canada's longest aerial tramway carries you 2,500 metres up Whistlers Mountain in a seven-minute ride. The upper station has a cafeteria and gift shop, and a boardwalk trail leads to the true summit with views stretching across six mountain ranges. Located 10 minutes south of town.
