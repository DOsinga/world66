title: Medicine Lake
type: poi

A lake that disappears. An underground drainage system causes the water level to rise in summer and drain almost completely by autumn, which baffled early visitors and gave the lake its name. Interpretive signs along the road explain the geology. Located 29 km east of Jasper along Highway 16.
