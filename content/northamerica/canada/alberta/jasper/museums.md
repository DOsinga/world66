**Museums**

**Den Wildlife Museum . **

130 life-sized wildlife specimens mounted in panoramic cases that resemble natural settings. Birds and animals, both large and small, from Western Canada and the Northern Territories have been assembled over 45 years by Canada's best taxidermists. $3, families $6.  Tel: 1 780 852-3361  Address: [more..](/northamerica/canada/alberta/jasper/museums/denwildlifemuseum)
