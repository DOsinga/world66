Fine Indian and Inuit art, including soapstone, whalebone and jade sculptures by master carvers that depict the unique culture of Canada's Native peoples. Original paintings and limited edition prints by Norval Morrisseau and others, as well as exquisite jewellery, many pieces set with ammolite, a colourful gemstone native to Alberta. Souvenir shirts, moccasins, guide books, books of photography and mementos. Closed Jan and Feb.  Tel: 1 780 852-3583  Address: Maligne Rd, 11 km (7 mi) SE of Jasper townsite, Jasper Alberta
