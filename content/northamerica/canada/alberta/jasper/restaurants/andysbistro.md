Accomplished restauranteur Andy Allenbach supervises the kitchen and ensures guests feel welcome at his intimate candlelit bistro. The menu changes to reflect local produce seasons and available ingredients, but expect game, pasta, AAA beef, fish, lamb, veal and vegetarian selections elegantly presented with fresh vegetables, innovative sides and interesting sauces ($16-$28). Inspired appetizers and desserts. 35 wines (many from Canada), 30 beers, and exotic port and scotch.
