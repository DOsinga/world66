In a country setting with views of Mt Kerkeslin and the Athabasca River, Becker's offers fine gourmet dining. Sample brie, with Westphalia ham and spinach in puff pastry, asparagus pesto phyllo, lake trout, rack of lamb Provincale, AAA beef tenderloin with goat cheese crust and peppercorn melot sauce, Malaysian chicken, and turtle pie. The morning buffet ($8 and $10) features egg dishes, breakfast meats, home baking and fresh fruit. Jasper residents often choose Becker's for a special night out. éesEntr $12-$25. Children's menu.
