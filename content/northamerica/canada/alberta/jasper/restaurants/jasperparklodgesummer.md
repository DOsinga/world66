The Grand Canadian Lodge Experience features both family and fine dining: Meadows Cafe: Breakfast from the buffet or á la carte. Family and alpine Italian cuisine for lunch and dinner plus nightly Italian buffet. Moose's Nook Canadiana Grill Room: Warm surroundings and regional Canadian specialties. Sunday Brunch at the Lodge: Fabulous array of family favourites and specialties. The Palisade Lounge: Intimate, elegant setting for cocktails and light meals. Lakeview patio. Emerald Lounge: Casual fare with fireside seating. Lunch BBQ on the lakeview terrace. Edith Cavell Dining Room: Exquisite gourmet cuisine and impeccable service. 4-Diamond Award winner. Lake and mountain view. Reservations required. Jackets required for gents. The Beauvert Dining Room: Continental table d'hote dinner, breakfast buffet 6:30 am. View of mountains and lake. Spike Lounge & Deli: Golf Clubhouse serves lunch, light fare and cocktails. Clubhouse deck. Tent City Sports Lounge: Pub food and family atmosphere with sports TV, darts, pool table and shuffleboard. Dining rooms offer live entertainment nightly.  Tel: 1 780 852-6052  Address: Information and reservations, Jasper Alberta
