The "Grand Canadian Lodge Experience" includes both family and fine dining. Meadows Cafe offers a buffet and a la carte breakfast, and family favourites at dinner. Moose's Nook is open weekends and holidays featuring, ethnic dinner buffets. Gourmet cuisine and fine service is assured at the Four Diamond Award winning Edith Cavell Dining Room. A spectacular Sun brunch is served in the Beauvert Dining Room. Emerald Fireside Lounge and Palisade Lounge offer stunning mountain views, intimate spots and light meals. Pub fare is served at Tent City Sports Lounge.  Tel: 1 780 852-6052  Address: Reservations and information, Jasper AB
