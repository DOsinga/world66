Owners Yanni and Lessia welcome guests to their comfortable restaurant. Greek and Ukrainian dishes are specialties--perogies, holobtsy (stuffed cabbage rolls), moussaka, souvlaki and paithakia (lamb chops). Other tastes are satisfied with eight pasta varieties, pizza, seafood, charbroiled Alberta steaks, jambalaya and stir fries, and BBQ chicken and ribs. Lunch specials ($5) include 8 inch pizza, (2 toppings), beef burger with fries, or Caesar salad. Entrées $9-$20.
