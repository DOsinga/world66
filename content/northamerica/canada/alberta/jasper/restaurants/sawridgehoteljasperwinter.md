Located in an atrium of greenery, award-winng Walter's Dining Room serves fresh and affordable fare during the ski season. Panini sandwiches (club, roasted vegetable and deli meat), pasta (shrimp, Italian sausage, primavera), burgers, traditional sandwiches (roast turkey, Montreal smoked beef) and West Coast wraps are mostly $6-$10. The nightly table d'hote menu often features AAA Alberta steaks, BC salmon, lamb and wild game (venison, caribou). Their Sat night carved baron of beef skiers buffet ($XX, 6-12 $XX, -6 free) is a Jasper tradition. A la carte breakfast, plus buffet weekends and hol ($11, 6-12 $6, -6 free).
