Alpine Village Cabin Resort

Enjoy Cottage Life in Jasper National Park. Located 2 km south of Downtown Jasper in the heart of Jasper National Park is the quiet and scenic Alpine Village. Log Cabins and Mountain Chalets are spread over a well maintained and forested property, accross form the Athabasca River. Most cabins feature wood-burning fireplaces, kitchens and private patios. Large out-door hot tub, play ground and quiet sitting areas. Repeat winner of the Alberta Housekeeping award and a Jasper's only 3 diamond AAA rated cabin resort. Great off season rates.
