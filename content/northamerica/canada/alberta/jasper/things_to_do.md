---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Jasper National Park is the largest park in the Canadian Rockies, and most of what you will want to see and do is out in the wilderness rather than in town. Waterfalls, lakes, canyons, and mountain viewpoints are all within easy driving distance. The Jasper Tramway lifts you above the treeline for panoramic views, while boat cruises on Maligne Lake take you to the iconic Spirit Island.
