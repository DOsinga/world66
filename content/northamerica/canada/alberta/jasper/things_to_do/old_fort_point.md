---
title: "Old Fort Point"
type: poi
category: "Sight"
latitude: 52.8691
longitude: -118.0555
---

A rocky knoll near the site of an early fur trade post, just 1.6 km south of town. The loop trail to the top gives excellent views over the Athabasca River valley and the town of Jasper. From here you can also set out on the 17 km trail to the Valley of the Five Lakes.
