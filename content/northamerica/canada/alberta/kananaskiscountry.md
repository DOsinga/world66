Located one hour west of [Calgary](/northamerica/canada/alberta/calgary), where the foothills meet the Rockies, Kananaskis Country is one of Alberta's best kept secrets. This 2000 km2 area boasts some of the most beautiful mountain scenery in the world.

Kananaskis is an outdoor playground, with facilities for camping, hiking, skiing, mountain biking, climbing, horseback riding and off road activities. If you prefer a more civilized experience, there are a number of four star hotels and a world class golf course located in the heart of the Kananaskis Valley.

Kananaskis Country is located just south of the Trans Canada highway, 60 miles west of Calgary. The roads into the region are paved and well travelled. Don't miss a visit to this exciting area!
