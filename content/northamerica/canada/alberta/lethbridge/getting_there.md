---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

Lethbridge is at the crossroads of Highways 3, 4, 5 and 25. It is an hour north of the US border by travelling on the I-90 through Montana. It is two hours south of Calgary if you travel on Highways 2 and 3. 

The Lethbridge County airport is serviced by Air Canada, Regional One and Integra Air. These airlines provide daily service to Calgary, Edmonton, Kelowna and Vancouver.
