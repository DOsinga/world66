**Alberta Birds of Prey Center**

A working conservation centre featuring the hawks, falcons, eagles and owls of Alberta. Animal rehabilitation and education on predatory birds is highly featured. More information can be obtained by visiting Hawks and Owls The center is located 10 minutes east of Lethbridge on Highway #3.
