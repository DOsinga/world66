A compact field guide covering the bird species found across Alberta, Saskatchewan, and Manitoba. Chris Fisher provides species accounts with colour illustrations, habitat notes, and range maps, making it a practical companion for birding trips through the prairies. Particularly useful for identifying grassland specialists like burrowing owls, sharp-tailed grouse, and Sprague's pipit that you won't find in more general North American guides.
