Two hours east of Calgary, the badlands of Dinosaur Provincial Park contain one of the world's richest dinosaur fossil beds — 35 species unearthed and counting. The UNESCO World Heritage landscape itself is striking: eroded hoodoos, coulees and canyon walls carved from ancient riverbeds. Guided bus tours run into restricted areas where you can see bones still emerging from the rock.
