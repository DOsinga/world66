Near Drumheller in the heart of Alberta's badlands, the Royal Tyrrell Museum holds one of the world's most impressive collections of dinosaur remains — over 40 mounted skeletons on display. It's not just for kids; the paleontology here is genuinely world-class, and the surrounding badlands landscape is worth the drive out on its own. Plan at least half a day.
