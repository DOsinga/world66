title: Beachway Park
type: poi

A narrow strip of sandy beach at the eastern end of Lake Ontario, tucked between Burlington's downtown and the Burlington Bay Canal. The beach is free to access with parking, washrooms, and change rooms on site. A paved multi-use trail runs the length of the park and connects across the canal to Hamilton's waterfront trail network, making it a useful link for cyclists heading between the two cities as well as a pleasant spot for a summer afternoon.
