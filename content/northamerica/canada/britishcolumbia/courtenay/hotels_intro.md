The city of Courtenay is the only city in the Comox Valley region in British Columbia, Canada.The city takes its name from the Courtenay River that flows through the city. Courtney is one of Canada&rsquo;s rapidly developing cities. The city has a lot to offer to its tourists. Given below are details on some of the **Courtenay Hotels**.

**Old House Village Hotel And Spa**

The Old House Village Hotel And Spa is a premium boutique hotel in the heart of Comox Valley. For your stay here, you can choose from the 79 rooms which consist of deluxe studios, one- and two-bedroom suites and the penthouse suite. Located centrally, this hotel is just minutes away from the city's attractions and from the Comox airport. You can secure your room at the Old House Village Hotel and Spa for prices starting from $129 CAD. The rooms are elegantly furnished and offer you the beautiful view of Courtenay River or the Beaufont Mountain Range. The in-house restaurant is the best place to be after your day outdoors. You can enjoy their intimate and relaxing atmosphere and the great food. As a guest you also have access to their world class spa and jacuzzi.

**Holiday Inn Express Hotel And Suites**

Situated just 4.57 miles from the Comox airport is the Holiday Inn Express Hotel. This hotel is an ideal location if you want to be close to the airport without having to stay too far away from the city. The hotel, overlooking the Washington Mountain offers a great view to all its guests from all the rooms. You can choose from the guest rooms, executive rooms and the suites and the room charges starts from $125 CAD. The rooms are neat and clean and well-done with a modern touch to them. When staying here you can have access to the hotel's fine spa and fitness center, indoor swimming pool, hot tub and the in-house water slide. Your tiny tots can play in the children&rsquo;s activity area within the hotel. Holiday Inn Express offers its guests complimentary breakfasts.

**Travelodge Courtenay**

If you are a budget traveler, then Travelodge is the best place for you to obtain accommodations. Located in the heart of the city, it is close to the city&rsquo;s attractions, shopping centers and restaurants. Rooms are neat and clean and well-equipped with all modern amenities, starting from $88 CAD. You can choose from the kitchenette rooms, deluxe rooms, superior rooms and standard rooms. You can also relax by the outdoor heated pool. Travelodge offers its guests a complimentary continental breakfast.
