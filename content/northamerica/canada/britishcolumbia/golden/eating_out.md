---
title: "Eating Out"
type: section
---

Kicking Horse Mountain Resort brought a surprisingly good dining scene to this small mountain town. **Eagle's Eye**, at 2,350 metres, is Canada's highest full-service restaurant — the views are spectacular and the dinner menu is genuinely excellent. **Cedar House Café** sits on a hillside south of town overlooking the Columbia River wetlands and is worth the short drive. In town, **The Kicking Horse Grill** and **The Turning Point** are local favourites.
