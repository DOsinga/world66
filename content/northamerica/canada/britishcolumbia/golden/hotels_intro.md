Golden is a small town in the heart of the untouched wilderness located in the south eastern part of British Columbia, Canada, and there are a lot of **Golden hotels** to choose from. The city is huddled in the Rocky Mountain channel and built at the meeting point of the Kicking Horse and Columbia Rivers. Golden is three hours from the nearest major airport in Calgary, Canada. Golden is very popular for its extreme adventure options and draws tourists from all around the world. Surrounded by the Yoho National Park, Glacier National Park, Banff National Park and Kootenay National Park, Golden is indeed a perfect getaway spot from the hustle and bustle of the modern world. Golden offers you a wide variety of accommodations ranging from hostels and camps to cabin, motels, hotels and resorts.

**Copper Horse Lodge**

Situated around 14 kilometers from the heart of Golden, British Columbia, the Copper Horse Lodge is a petite boutique mountain lodge. Nested between the Canadian mountains, the options of outdoor activities are aplenty. If you want to enjoy contemporary and elegant accommodations in a mountain setting, this is the perfect place to be. You can choose from their 10 rooms or suits and also avail access to the world class spa facilities within the lodge. The rooms are tastefully decorated and are equipped with modern amenities. Rooms are available here starting form $170 per night. The in-house restaurant and bar, Corks, is the perfect place to unwind after your day outdoors. You can also avail their transportation service to the national parks nearby.

**Best Western Mountain View Inn**

The Best Western is situated in the center of Canadian Rockies, three hours travel from Calgary International Airport and just few kilometers from downtown and several other recreational spots. All the 72 rooms here are equipped with lavish fixtures and modern amenities. You can spend your day at the heated indoor pool with your friends and family. Guests who like to stay connected can avail the Internet access provided at the inn. The rooms are available at the Best Western Mountain View Inn starting from US$130 per night.

**Kicking Horse River Lodge**

Situated within walking distance of downtown area and the out door activities, this lodge is apt if you do not have your own vehicle. Rooms are available here starting from US$150 per night. You can experience the amazing view of the Kicking Horse River and the Purcell Mountains and Kicking Horse Resort from this lodge. The building has been decorated with log and timber furniture, adding a rustic charm to the place. Despite the rustic charm, this place is well equipped with all the modern amenities. You can choose from 10 loft rooms or 5 main floor rooms with expansive window views. Two Dorms are also available if you are traveling in a group. Enjoy the some delicious home made dishes at the caf&eacute; in the lodge.
