Beautiful plants and flowers can be seen along paths at the Peace Garden (2320 78th Avenue) Open from 8am-8pm.

 The Jerseyland Organic Dairy can be found at 2690 Almond Gardens Road East. They have taste tours from 11am-5pm.

 The Boundary Museum holds volumes of history and is open from June - September 9am-4pm at 7370 5th Street.

 The Grand Forks Art Gallery and Gift Shop contains work by local, national and international artists. Find it near the Museum at 7340 5th Street.
