There are a large variety of **Nanaimo hotels** to choose from which will suit a variety of budgets. Nanaimo is located on the east coast of beautiful Vancouver Island in British Columbia and is the 2nd largest city on the island, next to the capital city of Victoria. It is a quick ferry ride to or from Vancouver.

**Grand Hotel**

The Grand Hotel is close to the ferry docks and the Nanaimo airport. It features 72 guest rooms and the Cornerstone Restaurant and Lounge; the restaurant also offers a Sunday brunch. Three days a week the hotel offers an English afternoon tea. The hotel also offers a wedding planner for those who would like to get married on the grounds. The Grand Hotel has a pool, an exercise room and underground parking. It also offers dry cleaning services, meeting rooms and broadband Internet. Room rates range from $149-$259 a night.

**Inn on Long Lake**

The Inn on Long Lake is on the banks of Long Lake. It offers 62 smoking and non-smoking rooms; each room has a fridge, a coffee maker with free coffee and tea, and a microwave. Every room in the hotel overlooks Long Lake. The hotel accepts pets and offers high-speed Internet. It also features a whirlpool, an exercise room and free parking. The Inn does not feature a restaurant, but there are many in the general area. Room rates range from $139-$226 a night.

**Coast Bastion Inn**

The Coast Bastion Inn, like most hotels in Nanaimo, is located downtown near the ferry terminal. It features the Minnoz Restaurant and Lounge, which specializes in local fresh seafood. It is a pet friendly hotel with 24-hour room service. Most rooms have ocean views and provide free tea and coffee, as well as free high-speed wireless Internet access. The hotel also has a spa and oxygen bar. The Inn also provides meeting rooms for business and weddings. Room rates range from $143-$375 a night.

**Buccaneer Inn**

The Buccaneer Inn is a family owned hotel close to the ferry docks which sits just across the road from the Newcastle Channel. It features kitchen suites and studio rooms. The kitchens suites have separate bedrooms from the main sitting room and kitchen. Unlike many hotels in Nanaimo, the Inn does not accept pets and smoking is banned completely. Each room offers free high-speed wireless Internet. There is also the possibility of renting out rooms for an extended stay. Room rates range from $60-$149 a night.

In addition to the above named hotels, Nanaimo has many of the chain hotels. These include Howard Johnson's, Best Western, Travelodge, Ramada Inn and Days Inn.
