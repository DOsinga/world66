**Osoyoos Hotels** continue to grow in number as the developing community of Osoyoos in British, Columbia becomes more progressive. About two miles from the U.S. border and 248.5 miles from Vancouver, Osoyoos is the center of the Desert Wine Country. Its semi-arid climate makes Osoyoos&rsquo; summers dry and hot and its winters mild.

**Best Western Sunrise Inn**

Centrally situated in Osoyoos, the hotel offers excellent dining options and a free continental breakfast. The rooms are comfortable and equipped with modern conveniences, namely air-conditioning, kitchen utensils, and phones. The hotel also has an indoor swimming pool, and a modern fitness facility. Its major advantage is its proximity to local attractions like the tranquil Osoyoos Lake, the NKMip Desert, the Heritage Center that showcases the history of the Okanagan Nation, and Rattlesnake Canyon Amusement Park. The room rate is $92 per night.

**Watermark Beach Resort**

Situated near scenic Osoyoos Lake, this hotel features a marina, an outdoor pool with a water slide, spa centers, and a fully-equipped fitness center. The hotel also has a steam room and a wine bar. Suites provide full kitchens or kitchenettes and have private balconies with excellent views of the lake and the surrounding countryside. It is near the Osoyoos Museum and Mount Baldy Ski Area. The hotel offers wine tasting tours and sports activities. The price is $91to $98 per night.

**Walnut Beach Resort **

Walnut Beach Resort is next to Osoyoos Lake. The hotel features kitchens, flat screen TV, Wi-Fi, and MP3 docking stations in all its hotel suites. The hotel has laundry facilities, steam rooms, fitness centers, an on-site convenience store and business center. If you love the outdoors, you can go skiing, kayaking, horseback riding, and hiking. For those who want some privacy, the hotel has a private beach where you can relax and have some quiet time. Room rate is $221 to $309 per night.

**Holiday Inn Hotel and Osoyoos Suites**

This Inn offers one of the best business facilities in the area. The hotel can accommodate meetings, conferences in its spacious patios, lounges, boardrooms, and restaurant. The Holiday Inn is an appropriate venue for business opportunities because it stands between the junction of two highways, highway no. 3 and 97. The hotel has its own private beach and marina and offers rentals for ski boats, canoes, kayaks, moorage, and fuel access. Room rates are $118 per night.

**Coast Beach Hotel Osoyoos **

Coast Beach Hotel is also within the vicinity of Lake Osoyoos. Rooms have TV, high speed Internet, in-room coffee and tea service. It also provides wheelchair accessible rooms and a private beach. The Osoyoos Museum and the Haynes Point Provincial Park are within the area. Room rates are $80 per night.
