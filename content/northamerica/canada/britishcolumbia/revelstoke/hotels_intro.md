Revelstoke, located west of Banff National Park in British Columbia, has a number of **Revelstoke hotels **to choose from. The town has been an important railroad town for over 100 years. Travelers concerned about being near the railroad should ask the hotel staff or check the location carefully prior to booking a hotel room. It is less known to tourists, but is popular among skiers and those seeking a more authentic town setting.

In addition to hotel options, there are also plenty of bed and breakfasts.

#### **Revelstoke Mountain Resort**

The most noticeable provider of lodgings in Revelstoke is the RMR. With multiple restaurants and amenities, the Revelstoke Mountain Resort offers two hotels: the Nelson Lodge and the Sandman Inn. The Nelson Lodge is located at the base of the mountain and is just steps from the lift. Located in town, the Sandman Inn Revelstoke is the largest hotel in the area, with 173 rooms. It has two indoor pools and free high-speed Internet access. Some of the rooms have kitchenettes. There is a town shuttle which stops at several downtown locations, including the Nelson Lodge and Sandman Inn.

#### **Town of Revelstoke**

There are hotels of various sizes and price ranges located in town. At the higher end of the price range is the Regent Inn, which also boasts one of the area's fine dining restaurants. The Alpine Inn and Suites has several two-bedroom suites, some of which have kitchenettes. The Swiss Chalet Motel provides 22 modest accommodations, free breakfast, and free Internet service. The Days Inn & Suites is the newest hotel in town. For those looking for smaller establishments, the Inn on the River has two accommodations overlooking the river, and the Courthouse Inn offers ten units.

#### **Outside of Town**

Hotels outside of town include the Hillcrest Hotel and the Three Valley Lake Chateau, which is bordered by the Monashee Mountains. The Glacier House Hotel and Resort offers plenty of activities and amenities for the active traveler. The Griffin Lake Mountain Lodge is a smaller facility which provides free canoe rentals, breakfast and Internet. The Mulvehill Creek Wilderness Inn offers eight rooms in a scenic, adult setting which is also a wedding chapel. Those looking for camping or more rustic experiences might be interested in the Lamplighter Campground, Canyon Hot Springs Resort or the Noah's Ark Resort, known as an "RV adventure park."

**Hostel**

Samesun operates the hostel in Revelstoke. The hostel is located near the intersection of Boyle and Second Streets downtown. There are modern accommodations and cooking facilities in the hostel.
