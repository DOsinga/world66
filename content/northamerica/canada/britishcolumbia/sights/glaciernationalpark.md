Glacier National Park helps preserve the interior rainforest and features a set of spectacular glaciers and icefields. The park encloses 1349 square kilometers of the Selkirk and Purcell mountain ranges. The park also protects the ranges of endangered species like the grizzly bear and mountain caribou. For additional information, including a virtual tour of the park, visit Glacier National Park
