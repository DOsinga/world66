title: "Gwaii Haanas"
type: poi
---

The Gwaii Haanas National Marine Conservation Area Reserve is a coastal sanctuary of 138 islands which help preserve unique island flora and fauna and Haida cultural artifacts. More than a million seabirds have been counted in the area during nesting season. Note that access to the park is only by aircraft and boat. For more information on the park, visit Gwaii Haanas
