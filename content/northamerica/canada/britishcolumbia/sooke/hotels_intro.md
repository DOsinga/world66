Sooke, British Columbia, is a small town west of the provincial capital of Victoria. It is a waterfront town, and many of the **Sooke hotels **offer great views.

As a smaller town, hotel options are more limited, so travelers interested in visiting Sooke should plan well in advance. The privately owned and operated lodgings may require non-refundable deposits or longer cancellation windows. Staying closer to Victoria, only 20 miles away, may be an option if Sooke lodgings are unavailable.

#### **Sooke Harbour House**

Sooke Harbour House has been accommodating guests since 1929. The inn offers 28 rooms which have all been recently renovated. Views include the rocky Whiffen Spit Park, the harbor, the Olympic Mountains and the Strait. There is also a spa and a restaurant with a menu which changes daily. Many of the vegetables used in the unique dishes are grown on-site at the Sooke Harbour Farm.

#### **Bed and Breakfasts**

This small, scenic town is the perfect location for a number of bed and breakfasts. Vacation home rentals are also available.

- **Arbutus Cove Guest House and Cottages **offers three private rooms and two guest cottages. It is located on private waterfront property. Fishing and trail hiking are among the nearby activities.
- **Swan Haven Cottages **has four, two-bedroom cottages on the banks of the Sooke River. Each cottage has a private hot tub and a barbecue.
- **The Lodge at Weir's Beach **offers organic breakfasts and yoga classes for those staying in its six guest rooms or suites. The lodge has Pacific beach access
- **Blue Sky B&B **has two modern suites with ocean views. Breakfast is served each day in the room or on the private deck. There is also a common lounge for guests.

#### **Something Different**

Those travelers looking for unique accommodations have several alternative options.

- **Dreamings, The Elements Bed and Breakfast** offers two rooms which focus on Canadian artists. Only one room offers an ensuite private bathroom, but both rooms have a private deck. Dreamings is bordered by East Sooke Regional Park.
- **Gordons Beach Farm Stay Bed and Breakfast **has one cabin and three bed and breakfast rooms in its hobby farm. Tour the property, observe the farm animals, marvel at the 180-degree ocean views or see some of Sooke's other attractions.
- **Malahat Farm at Muir Creek **offers a three-bedroom cottage as well as rooms in the main farmhouse. Visit the garden or the resident farm animals.
