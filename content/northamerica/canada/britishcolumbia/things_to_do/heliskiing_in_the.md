Canadian Mountain Holidays offers amazing tours of some of the world's
most exclusive and spectacular wilderness skiing opportunities. They
have assembled an international team of experts and guides that will
ensure you get the most out of your heli-ski vacation package.

Canadian
Mountain Holidays operates 12 distinct ski area in the remote mountains
of British Columbia and have a total ski area about a third the size of
Switzerland. Their 3 - 7 day packages include everything you need to
have a good time, guided heli-skiing or snowboarding, lodge
accomodation, all meals, ski usage, and transportation from the airport.
