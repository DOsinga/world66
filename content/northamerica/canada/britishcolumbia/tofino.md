Tofino is located on the secluded West Coast of Vancouver Island in the province of British Columbia, Canada. Initially a resource-based town, Tofino has become a very popular tourist destination for those seeking a quiet vacation away from the city. The popular West Coast Trail starts in Tofino. As well, Clayoquot Sound is right on Tofino's doorstep. Pacific Rim National Park is one of Canada's most beautiful parks and is also in close proximity to Tofino. Attractions and activities include, but are not limited to, whale watching, fishing, beachcombing, surfing and hiking.
