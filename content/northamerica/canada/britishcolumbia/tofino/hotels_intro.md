Tofino, British Columbia, is a small but popular destination. Given its small size, there is a large number of **Tofino hotels**. Given the area's popularity, the rates for most of the hotels are relatively high during the peak summer season.

Traffic in Tofino can be challenging during peak times, so travelers may wish to look at the proximity of lodging to town and learn about any shuttles the hotels may run.

#### **Premium Hotels**

The following hotels capitalize on their locations and reputations. The rates are among the highest in the area.**
**

- The **Wickaninnish Inn **is considered one of the most** **romantic hotels in the area. The Inn is also known for its world-class restaurant. There is direct access to Chesterman Beach, and all rooms boast ocean views. Open since 1955, the Wickaninnish is one of the older lodging facilities in Tofino.**
**
- Pacific Sands Beach Resort boasts 1,000 feet of beach-front lodging, some of which have fully-equipped kitchens. The resort offers DVDs, Wi-Fi, bike rentals and anything else guests might want.
- **The Long Beach Lodge Resort **opened in 2002 and has 20 cottages and 45 private lodge rooms. The resort has amazing ocean views which can be seen from many of the accommodations. Breakfast is served daily in the Great Room. Surfing equipment can be rented on-site, and lessons are provided.

#### **Second Tier Hotels
**

Although these hotels are beautiful and comfortable, the rates are slightly lower than the Wickaninnish, Pacific Sands and Long Beach Lodge.

- **Middle Beach Lodge **offers private beach access and secluded lodging near the heart of Tofino. There are two separate lodges and sets of cabins which share a dining room.
- **The Best Western Tin Wis Resort Hotel **has beachfront rooms, a fitness club and spa. It is located on Clayoquot Sound.
- **Cox Bay Beach Resort **offers 40 suites, a spa and community fire pit. There is direct access to Cox Bay Beach.

#### **More Budget-Friendly**

- Smaller accommodations are available, such as
- **Duffin Cove Resort**, which offers 13 rooms, many with ocean views.
- **The Inn at Tough City **has eight modestly-appointed rooms and a dining room, all with views of Clayoquot Sound. There are also several bed and breakfasts in Tofino.
- **MacKenzie Beach Resort** has one-, two- and three-bedroom cabins, one- and two-room suites, camping and RV sites. The camping and RV sites may not be reserved in advance. There is an indoor heated pool and a shared barbecue area. It is located on beautiful MacKenzie Beach.
- **Crystal Cove Beach Resort **offers cabins and RV sites and sits at the edge of MacKenzie Beach.
