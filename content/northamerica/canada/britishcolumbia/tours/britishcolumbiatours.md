British Columbia Tours offers a variety of customized tours of the province. Travel from the urban centre of Vancouver into BC's interior on the Mountain Ranching and Okanagan Wine Tour; get a feel for the west on the Wilderness Discovery Tour; hit the slopes on the British Columbia Ski Tour; and test the waters with the Coastal Fishing and Mountain Tour. Small group sizes allow for flexibility. Tour fees include accommodation, transportation, meals and specified activities.
