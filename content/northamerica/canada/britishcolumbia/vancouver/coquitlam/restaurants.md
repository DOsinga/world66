A number of fine restaurants can be found in the communities surrounding Vancouver. Below are listed some of the Coquitlam restaurants we've received reviews on.

### [Milestone's Palomino Grill](/northamerica/canada/britishcolumbia/vancouver/coquitlam/restaurants/milestonespalominogrill)

This new restaurant has been very busy since opening and is a great place to go for lunch or dinner. They have a variety of great items on the menu including several pasta dishes, fish entrees and burgers. A vegetarian could find many tempting platters to choose from here. The food is deliciously prepared and well presented. The staff are friendly and helpful and the service quick. The atmosphere is a bit on the noisy side but not enough to pass up this eatery. Located at 2745 Barnet Highway in Coquitlam.
