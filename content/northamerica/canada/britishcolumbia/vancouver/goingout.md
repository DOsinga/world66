Vancouver's nightlife is concentrated along Granville Street downtown, where dance clubs, live music venues, and cocktail bars line up side by side. The scene ranges from polished lounges in Yaletown to dive bars in Gastown to the craft brewery taprooms that have taken over several industrial blocks on the east side. Richards Street has several well-known clubs, and the Davie Street strip in the West End has a lively bar scene.
