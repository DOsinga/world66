Painfully ‘hip’ bar/lounge inside the much-hyped Opus Hotel in
Yaletown. Beyond the fashionable clientele and contemporary décor
there’s tapas, cocktails and an extensive list of imported and domestic
wines and spirits. Signature small plates include 'O' Dim Sum, merguez
corn dog and shish kebob, and Yuzu-spiced Garlic Wings.
