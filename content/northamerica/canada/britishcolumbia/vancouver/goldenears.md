title: "golden ears"
type: location
---

Just a short skip out of town this provincial park offers some of the finest vistas this side of the divide. complete w/ everthing from legendary lost gold strike to mountaineering and swimming/boating this provincial park is well worth the 60 min. drive east of vancouver. 

p.s. the alouette lake is actually feels warmer than the ambient air temp. and is quite the refreshing dip on a chilly late summer day.
