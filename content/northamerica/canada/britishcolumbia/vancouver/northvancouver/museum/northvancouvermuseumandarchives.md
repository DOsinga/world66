This community museum houses exhibits celebrating the social, industrial and cultural development of North Vancouver. Logging, shipbuilding, ferry and streetcar systems, early schools and hospitals are popular topics. To May 14 Nootka: A Return to a Forgotten History Archival materials documenting the meeting of the First Peoples with the British and Spanish. Open Wed.-Sun. from 12-5 p.m. The archives are open Wed.-Fri. 9.30 a.m.-4.30 p.m. Admission free. Tel: 987-5618 Address: 209 W. 4th St., North Vancouver.
