(West coast style) In Burnaby, Hastings and Willingdon area - just a few stores from Anton's. Jennifer and Eric had two of their last three anniversary dinners at this restaurant - one of Eric's all time favourites. This restaurant is run by a husband and wife team (he is the chef, she handles the rest). Small, but good wine list. Small restaurant, tastefully decorated with gold wall in a pear theme, and great food. Lots of game, venison and rabbit. You can get away with $100 for two people if you order wine by the glass. Has a chef tasting menu as well as a la carte. The menu reflects the seasons.
