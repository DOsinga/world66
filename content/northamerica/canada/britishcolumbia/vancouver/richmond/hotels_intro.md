Richmond, British Columbia, Canada is called the "Island City" as it's situated on the Strait of Georgia, a large channel of water between Vancouver Island and the Pacific Coast. Richmond is considered to be an early European settlement and was first named and founded in 1862. If you're considering visiting this area, there are numerous **Richmond Hotels** available to choose from for your vacation accommodations. The area of Richmond is close to downtown Vancouver, British Columbia, and Vancouver International Airport as well. Richmond is well known for being a beautiful area, taking pride in its overall natural environment and for making their city one that is welcoming to both current residents and visitors.

**Airport Hotels**

- The Fairmont Vancouver Airport rates begin at around $230 nightly for this fpir-star hotel. It's a soundproof luxury accommodation with a wonderful spa and it's located within the airport compound.
- Sheraton Vancouver Airport Hotel offers guests the best of everything with a luxury experience and close to the Vancouver Airport; all for $230 per night.
- The Holiday Inn Express Vancouver-Airport is a three-star accommodation beginning at approximately $120 nightly and conveniently located minutes from the International Airport.
- Comfort Inn Airport accommodations are around $100 per night for this three-star hotel which is completely non-smoking throughout. You'll receive free Wi-Fi service and free local calls. There is an on-site liquor store, outdoor heated pool, and nearby recommended restaurant called The Atrium Restaurant and Lounge.
- Recently opened in 2010, The Westin Wall Centre Airport hotel has a starting rate of $125 nightly.

**Resorts and Inns **

- The Quality Hotel at Richmond is a two-star hotel ranging in price from $140 to $150 per night, and is located in the heart of Richmond city, and 10 minutes away from the Vancouver International Airport.
- Executive Inn Express at Richmond has a starting rate of $70 nightly, is a three-star accommodation, and considers itself to be a full facility hotel that provides you with the location you require and the comfort you deserve, all at an affordable price.
- The Best Western Abercorn Inn is a two-star hotel that is 100 percent non-smoking throughout. It boasts elegant accommodations and amenities, and is only three miles from the airport.
- The River Rock Casino Resort's rates begin at $165 per night for this three-star resort accommodation. They offer style and luxury for business travelers or families looking to relax. Enjoy the comfort of the hotel and the exciting possibilities of the on-site casino.
- The Holiday Inn Express Hotel & Suites is rated at one-star, costs between $120 and $170 nightly, and states they're close to local attractions, businesses and the airport.
