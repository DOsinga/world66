The Vancouver Five in One Smartvisit Card provides entry to five of the city's leading attractions for one price. Cardholders can enjoy quick and cashless entry to Capilano Suspension Bridge, Vancouver Aquarium, Vancouver Lookout, IMAX Theatre at Canada Place and Dr. Sun Yat-Sen Classical Chinese Garden. With the Vancouver Five in One, guest save over 20% off regular retail admission. The card is valid for five consecutive days.

Price (including applicable taxes)

Adult $70

Child $47

Consider combining the card with a Big Bus tour where you can hop-on and hop-off at your leisure at 22 stops around the city. Enjoy colourful commentary about the city in these vintage double decker and open-top buses. Big Bus tour tickets are valid for two days.

The Vancouver Five in One Card is available for purchase at the Big Bus Welcome Centre (Gastown) at 317 Water Street.
