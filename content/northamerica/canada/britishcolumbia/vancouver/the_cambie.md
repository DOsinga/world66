The Cambie is one of Vancouver's oldest pubs, in the heart of Historic Gastown. It draws an eclectic crowd of locals and backpackers -- the attached hostel ensures a steady stream of travellers -- and is about as unpretentious as a bar can get. The beer is cheap (pitchers are among the most affordable in the city), and you can drink in the main room, the beer garden, or what used to be the smoking room. Expect to show ID regardless of your age, and arrive before 9pm on weekends if you want to avoid a queue.
