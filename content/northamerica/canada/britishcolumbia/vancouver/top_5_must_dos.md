---
title: "Top 5 Must Do's in Vancouver"
type: section
---

Vancouver is a seamless amalgamation of rugged wilderness and
polished civilization, so be sure to try out these top 5 Vancouver Must Do's. Its breath-taking natural beauty and bountiful
wildlife make Vancouver a destination that succeeds in wooing you without
trying too hard. Few places have the potential to match the heady
medley of Vancouver’s attractions and activities.

**Explore Grouse Mountain**

It's practically a sin to visit Vancouver without making a trip to
the Grouse Mountain. There are countless activities you can indulge
in from catching a glimpse of the seductive valley from the most
strategic vantage point peak to taking a mountain zipline adventure or witnessing
a thrilling contest between competing lumberjacks.

**Visit Stanley Park**

Stanley Park is undoubtedly Vancouver’s most popular attraction. It
lures visitors with its old-fashioned influence and country-side
appeal. Take a charming horse-drawn carriage tour, where an expert
guide will take you through attractions like Deadman’s Island, the
Vancouver Harbor, Rose Garden and the coastal Cedar Forest and spill
the beans on Vancouver’s long-veiled secrets.Other attractions worth
seeing are the Minter Gardens, a gorgeous floral drapery of vibrant
colors and delicate scents. There are 11 themed gardens amidst lush
watefalls and restaurants serving delectable cuisine.

**Go On a Cruise**

Go on an unforgettable cruise along the tranquil waters of Vancouver
with Harbour Cruises and Events, a famous cruise company in the city.
Cruises run four times a day with choices ranging from Sunset Dinner
Cruises to specialty luncheons and even theme cruises. Watch the beauty
of the azure waters come alive from your paddleboat and absorb the
information disseminated via live commentary on the wonders of
Vancouver.

**Visit the Capilano Suspension Bridge**

Make sure you visit the Capilano Suspension Bridge, Vancouver’s most
emblematic tourist attraction. The excitement never ceases here with
action packed activites like nature trails, crossing over to the
Treetops Adventure and climbing bridges to explore places of unknown
wilderness.

**Explore the Hell's Gate Airtram**

Tourists with an affinity for the occult and after life should check
out the Hell’s Gate Airtram with a drive through the Fraser Canyon on
the ‘Gold Rush Trail’. It is a place mythically touted to be the
playing field of the dead and is situated beyond the narrow path of the
Fraser River. Hell’s Gate Airtram houses a few observatory decks, gift
shops and a quaint albeit lip-smacking fare serving café.

If you're planning a visit to Vancouver, make sure you have these 5 great activities on your to-do list.
