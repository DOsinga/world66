Canada's largest aquarium, in the centre of Stanley Park. Strong on Pacific Northwest marine life — giant Pacific octopus, sea otters, harbour seals — plus an Amazonian gallery, a tropical reef tank and a Treasures of the BC Coast exhibit. The aquarium ended cetacean displays in 2017 and now focuses on rescue, rehabilitation and conservation work.
