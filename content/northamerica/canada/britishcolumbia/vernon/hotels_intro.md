**Enjoy your Stay in Vernon with the Best Hotels**

**Vernon hotels** in Vernon, British Columbia are a mixture of luxurious and budget hotels that cater to all types of travelers. Whether you are on a tight budget or on a luxury trip, you can select a hotel that will meet your needs.

**Prestige Hotel and Resort**

Centrally located near business centers and local tourist attractions, this hotel provides luxury rooms with amenities that include one room lodgings and spacious designer suites. Hotel services include hot tubs, indoor pools, laundry, fitness center and a business center. Room amenities include plasma TV, refrigerator, duvets, high-speed Internet access, hair dryer, coffeemaker and iron. The room cost per night during slower seasons ranges from $119.95 to $329.95. During peak seasons, the price ranges from $159.95 to $399.95.

**Super 8 Hotel**

This hotel provides a jacuzzi and an indoor pool. Rooms offer high speed wireless Internet, a TV, and a refrigerator. You can take advantage of the SuperStart breakfast offered to hotel guests. Its proximity to local areas of interest makes it a good place for guests who want to walk to destinations. The room rates range from $115 to $170 per night, excluding local taxes and additional services like safe warranties. Local taxes range from $17.25 to $25.50. The safe warranty charges will depend on the length and the type of additional service you want.

**Travel Lodge Vernon**

This hotel offers smoking and non-smoking rooms for travelers. Air-conditioned rooms provide amenities like refrigerators, TVs, local phones, and coffeemakers. Hotel services include free newspapers, a bar, a business center, a restaurant, and a shopping center. A seasonal heated pool is available too. Room rates range from $87 to $123 per night.

**The Village Green Hotel**

For health-conscious tourists, this hotel offers a hundred percent smoke free environment. Its proximity to local attractions, golf courses, and mountain resorts makes it an ideal place to stay while in Vernon. Rooms are temperature controlled and feature private balconies, which provide a fascinating view of the surrounding areas. Room amenities include TVs, wireless Internet, coffeemakers, hairdryers and ice vending machines. Cost per room ranges from $87 to $177 per night.

**Holiday Inn Express**

This hotel caters to visitors who travel for business. The hotel provides a business center where you can email, fax and conduct business. It also has a health and fitness center, a spa, and an entertainment and recreation room. Each room provides high-speed Internet access, free local calls, and cable TV with a pay per view feature. Room rates range from $159 to $179 per night.
