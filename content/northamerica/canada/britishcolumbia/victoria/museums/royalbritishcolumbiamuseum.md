The Royal British Columbia Museum is a place of discovery. Through three unique galleries, the Museum showcases the human and natural history of British Columbia, and features periodic exhibitions of international renown. Highly realistic and inviting displays, such as the Ice Age and Coastal Forest dioramas, provide visitors with a sense of having truly experienced the authentic settings of many exhibits.  Tel: 1 250 387-3701 Fax: 1 250 356-8197 Toll Free: 1-800-661-5411  Address: 675 Belleville St Victoria BC V8W 9W2

The Museum is located on the beautiful Inner Harbour waterfront. For information on current exhibits, and to see a live webcam view of the Inner Harbour, visit the Royal BC Museum. The image of the Inner Harbour will update every 30 seconds.
