type: poi
---

The "Artifacts Society" as we are known by locally, was formed in 1968 by a group of people with similar interests who used to demonstrate their antique agricultural machinery at the annual North & South Saanich Agricultural Society's Fall Fair. This group, ably led by Willard Michell (Dec.) founded the society then started to put together a collection of artifacts in accordance with their goal stated on the home page, always striving to have a representative piece from all facets of pioneer life in our neighbourhood. This collection has now grown to thousands of artifacts! Willard was host to this collection, housing it in an unused barn on his farm.
