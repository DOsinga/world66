The province's flagship museum, sitting on the Inner Harbour just behind the Parliament Buildings. Its strength is the First Peoples gallery, with one of the best collections of Northwest Coast carving and regalia anywhere, and the Old Town exhibit, a full-scale recreation of a turn-of-the-century BC street complete with cobbles, gaslight and a working cinema. The natural history floor walks you from coastal rainforest to ocean shoreline. Allow at least half a day.

Address: 675 Belleville Street.
