The oldest public art gallery in British Columbia, housed partly in a Victorian mansion in the Rockland neighbourhood near Craigdarroch Castle. The collection leans toward Asian art — including a Shinto shrine in the garden, the only one of its kind in North America — alongside Emily Carr canvases and rotating shows of contemporary Canadian work.

Address: 1040 Moss Street.
