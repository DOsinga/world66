**The Edgewater**
"We loved out time at The Edgewater" said Gourmet Magazine.
The Edgewater is a wonderful low key affair in the most stunning location imaginable. The dining room is sided with glass on 3 sides and located at the end of a peninsula jutting out into Green Lake with views to everywhere.
For 11 years, Chef Thomas Piekarski has been creating exquisite west coast cuisine using seafood and wild game.
"Arguably the best in Whistler", said Bob Howells in Avenues Magazine/Outside Magazine.
