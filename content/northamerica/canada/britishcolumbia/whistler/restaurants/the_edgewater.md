Set at the end of a narrow peninsula jutting into Green Lake, the Edgewater dining room is enclosed in glass on three sides — the lake and mountains fill the view from nearly every seat. The kitchen focuses on west coast ingredients: seafood, wild game, and seasonal produce. It's a proper sit-down meal rather than a quick bite, and the setting makes it worth the drive out from the village.
