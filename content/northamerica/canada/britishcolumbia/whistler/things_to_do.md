Whistler Attractions & Holiday info: What to see and do in Whistler, Canada

From crisp mountain air to crystal clear lakes and lush emerald forests, Whistler is a natural year-round playground. Blending harmoniously with the environment, Whistler serves up an attractive mix of cosmopolitan amenities and pristine beauty.

Explore, experience, relax. Temperatures rise to an average of 27C or 80F in July and August and the Village is buzzing with daily street entertainment. Enjoy famous restaurants, engaging nightlife and a variety of shops. Whistler also offers a multitude of summer activities. The 3 designer golf courses in the Whistler Valley, and four more spectacular courses in the Sea to Sky Corridor, have made Whistler a world-class golf destination!
