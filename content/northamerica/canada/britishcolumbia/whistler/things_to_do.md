---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Whistler is a year-round outdoor playground. In winter, Whistler and Blackcomb mountains offer some of the best skiing in North America. In summer the mountains stay busy with mountain biking, hiking, and lift-served sightseeing. The valley below has world-class golf courses, kayaking on the lakes, zip-lining, and guided fishing trips for rainbow trout and salmon on the local rivers.
