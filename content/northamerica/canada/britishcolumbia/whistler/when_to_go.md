---
title: "When to Go"
type: section
---

Whistler is in British Columbia, Canada, which is near
the Sea to Sky Highway. This is a famous ski destination which makes
the winter the best time to go.

**Ski Season: November – April **

Whistler has two large mountains, Whistler Mountain and Blackcomb
Mountain. Ski season is long here; from the end of November all the
way to April and these mountains have an average of 30 feet of snow per
year. It is perfect because the temperatures don’t get below zero and
stay around 32 degrees F most of the time. Spring is not a good time to
go because of unpredictable weather and in May snow melts.

**December - March**

If you are coming to Whistler to ski the best time to go is December
through March. In December the mountains don’t have a lot of traffic
and there are deals on rooms and meals at restaurants. There is a
chance there won’t be snow but that doesn’t happen too often. The
Whistler Film Festival takes place in the first few days of December.
Enjoy four days of films, workshops, seminars, special events and great
parties. Around Christmas and New Years the rates for accommodations
rise sharply and restaurants are full, but the nightlife is great and
there are parades and fireworks in the village. First Night Whistler
falls on New Years Eve and goes into New Years. This is a family event
with no alcohol allowed and features live bands, craft workshops,
entertainment on the street and a bunch of fun.

**January**

January has the best snowfalls of the year and the crowds are
minimal. It does get very cold during January, but you can curl up in
front of the fireplace at the resort with many discounted deals.

**February - March **

February and March are great times to go to Whistler with the
exception of spring break--stay away then. Accumulations of snow have
built up and skiing is great during this time. Watch out in 2010 as
some of the games of the Winter Olympics will be played there and you
will not be able to get rooms or get on the slopes at all. At the end
of February beginning of March is the Paralympic Winter Games. In the
first week of March Whistler hosts WinterPride one of the biggest gay
ski weeks with many activities all over town.

Whistler can provide you with a great time all year round but come during December to March in order to get the best out of it.
