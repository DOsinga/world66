---
title: "Wildlife"
type: section
---

British Columbia is one of the richest wildlife provinces in Canada. The temperate rainforests, mountain ranges, river systems, and Pacific coastline support an astonishing range of species. You can realistically see orcas, grizzly bears, humpback whales, bald eagles, and mountain goats all in a single trip if you plan well.

Do not feed wildlife anywhere in the province — in city parks or in the countryside. Fed animals lose their fear of humans, start approaching people, complaints follow, and the animal is usually destroyed. Keep them wild.
