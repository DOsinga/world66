---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment in Hamilton"
type: section
---

If you’re looking to have a great time while visiting Hamilton,
Ontario, Canada, you should definitely explore the nightlife and check
out the best entertainment venues.

**The Sands Bar**

The Sands Bar, self proclaimed “Hamilton’s hottest outdoor patio
bar”, has weekend beach parties every weekend. The outdoor décor
includes neon lighted palm trees and tiki torches to set the mood for a
relaxing beach ambiance. Outdoor tables create a romantic dining
experience with the a bar that offers v.i.p bottle service. The dj
plays the latest music until closing time.

**Dirty Dog Saloon**

Or, if you’re into line dancing, two stepping and trying your hand
at riding a mechanical bull, put on your cowboy hat and visit the Dirty
Dog Saloon. Self proclaimed “the hottest pub in Steeltown,” it’s a
complete contrast from the Sands Bar. Most of us hav e watched people
ride mechanical bulls on television and it looked like lots of fun, so
after the experience at the saloon, you’ll know why it looked so fun on
television. It’ll be fun to have contests with friends to see who can
ride the bull the longest. Some even make it more interesting by making
wagers. In addition, at the saloon, you’ll get a blast from the past
and dance to music from the 80’s, and 90’s, and country with a little
rock music. The saloon offers an affordable $5 cover charge and drink
specials on Thursday, Friday and Saturday nights.

**Tailgate Charlie’s All-Star Café **

After a great time dancing and partying at the Sands Bar and the
Dirty Dog Saloon, grab some appetizing food at Tailgate Charlie’s
All-Star Café, self proclaimed “the undisputed king of sports.” If you
love Mediterranean classic dishes, pastas, or “all American burgers and
fries (to name few) - you’re certain to find something delicious on the
menu. Desserts include deep fried ice cream, colossal cheese cake, and
deep dish apple pie. Tailgate Charlie’s has the convenience of two
venues in one as the lower level houses the Liquid Kitty Lounge, self
proclaimed “an innovation in live music.” Go and have a great meal
while enjoying the excitement of your favorite sports teams, then relax
or dance of those calories on the lower level.

**Live Entertainment Venues**

The heart of Hamilton has the city’s largest live entertainment and
convention facilities. The Great Hall at Hamilton Place, Copps
Coliseum, and Hamilton Convention Centre are the city's best spots for
live entertainment, sports, trade shows, conventions, filming and
concerts. Upon visiting, check the event schedule at all three
locations for fun entertainment you won’t want to miss.
