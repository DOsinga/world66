The city of Kingston was the first capitol of Canada, before
confederation and as a result of the rich history behind the foundation
of Kingston, the city has wonderful architecture and a strong tourism
economy.

Attractions include Fort Henry, The Thousand Islands, and Queen's University.

Read more about Kingston here: http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kingston%2C_Ontario
