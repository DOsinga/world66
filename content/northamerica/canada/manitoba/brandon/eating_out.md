title: "Eating Out"
type: section
---

There are some good restaurants but not much for ethnic fare. Suggestions:

**Gulliver's** (for anything & anyone, anytime), Olivier's (for a quiet date), **Remington's **(upscale steak & seafood), **The Keg** (hip steakhouse), **Calhoon's** (grill with frequent live music), **Rhapsody Bar & Grill** (Carribean influence), **Allessio's** (pasta plus -- monster portions), **Albert's** (small & humble but great prime rib), **Astoria** (pizza & pasta), **Joey's Only** (casual fish & chicken), and **Double Happiness **(Chinese -- humble setting but good buffet), **Pizza Express** (hole-in-the-wall pizza+ joint, also deliver).

Restaurants in the **Royal Oak Inn**, **Victoria Inn** and **Canad Inn** are very good for anything and offer some great buffets.

Best coffee & ice/cream dessert spots: **Scarlatti's** and **Forbidden Flavors**.
