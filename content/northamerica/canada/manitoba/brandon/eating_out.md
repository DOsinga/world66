---
title: "Eating Out"
type: section
---

Brandon has a solid if not extensive restaurant scene. Remington's is the local upscale option for steak and seafood. The Keg does reliable steakhouse fare. For something more casual, Calhoon's is a grill with frequent live music, and Allessio's serves generous portions of pasta. The hotel restaurants at the Royal Oak Inn, Victoria Inn, and Canad Inn are dependable choices with good buffets. For coffee and dessert, Scarlatti's is a local favourite.
