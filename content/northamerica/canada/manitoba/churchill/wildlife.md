Artic wildlife is spectacular and well worth any efforts taken to carry yourself into the north country. Do not irritate the wildlife or treat them as pets. No point getting eaten on your holiday travels. 

The best time to visit the polar bears is mid- to late-October, before Hudson Bay freezes over.

### [Tundra Buggy Tours Ltd.](/northamerica/canada/manitoba/churchill/wildlife/tundrabuggytoursltd)

This company runs tours to see Arctic wildlife - everything from polar bears to whales. For more information and some spectacular wildlife photographs, visit http://www.tundrabuggytours.com.
