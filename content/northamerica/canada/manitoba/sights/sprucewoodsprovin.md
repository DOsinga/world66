Sprucewoods is about a hour and a half from Winnipeg. It is a beautiful camp ground. There you can take up hiking, swimming, canoe and mini golf. There is a lot more but there is some much to write. While there you can visit spirit sand.... manitoba's only desert. The park also features many hiking paths that take you along the assiboine river.
