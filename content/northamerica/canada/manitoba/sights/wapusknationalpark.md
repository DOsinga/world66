Wapusk National Park encloses one of the largest polar bear maternity denning areas in the world and preserves a portion of the Hudson-James lowland. Access to this park is difficult, so please check with park authorities on access options before heading towards this park. For more information, plus a virtual tour, visit Wapusk National Park
