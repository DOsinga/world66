### [The Manitoba Museum](/northamerica/canada/manitoba/winnipeg/museums/manitobamuseumofmanandnature)

One of the finest interpretive museums in Canada, the collection reflects the heritage of Manitoba. The Urban Gallery recreates turn-of-the-century Winnipeg and houses the spectacular Nonsuch replica , a 17th Century Sailing ship whose 1668 voyage led to the founding of the Hudson Bay Company. The Science Gallery features hands-on science exhibits, and there is also a Planetarium.
