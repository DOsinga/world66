An outdoor air park and indoor museum. The largest outdoor park of its
kind in Canada features 13 aircraft tracing the history of military
flight in Canada, ranging from a World War II bomber to a CF 5 fighter.
The Air Command Museum is open Mon-Fri 8 am-4 pm, weekends by
appointment. Free admission. Tel: 1 204 833-2500 ext 5993 Address: Air
Force Way, north of Sharp Blvd Winnipeg Manitoba
