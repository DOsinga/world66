title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Winnipeg's headline sight is the **Canadian Museum for Human Rights** — a Daniel Libeskind building wrapped in stone and glass, opened in 2014, and the first national museum built outside Ottawa. Around it, **The Forks** is a waterfront public space with a market hall, an old railway bridge, and skating trails in winter. The **Manitoba Museum** a few blocks north has the full-size replica of the Nonsuch, the 1660s ship that launched the Hudson's Bay Company. The **Exchange District**, a National Historic Site of brick warehouses, has been the city's film double for 1930s New York a hundred times over.
