Andrew Hempstead's guide covers Alberta, British Columbia, the Northwest Territories, and the Yukon. It is particularly strong on the less-visited corners of western Canada, though it also provides solid coverage of [Vancouver](/northamerica/canada/britishcolumbia/vancouver), Victoria, the Canadian Rockies, and [Calgary](/northamerica/canada/alberta/calgary). A good companion for anyone heading beyond the usual stops.
