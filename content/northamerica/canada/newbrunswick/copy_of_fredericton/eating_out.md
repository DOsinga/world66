### [Oscars Bar & Bistro](/northamerica/canada/newbrunswick/copy_of_fredericton/eating_out/oscars_bar__bistr)

Funky artistic decor. Lots of water features including a bubble wall, aquarium and waterfall. Cozy and romantic. Service is good. Food is fantastic and reasonably priced. Offers lots of specials like 1/2 price apps on Tuesday. Live Jazz on Sat.

Check out their website: www.oscarsbarandbistro.ca
