St John's can be cold and very wet and you might feel like having a nice dinner in one of the numerous restaurants of the city. Most of them offer fish, mainly cod, and a cosy atmosphere.

Enjoy your meal in one of the most famous places such as the Westminster or Pasta Plus for example and forget about the bad weather. People gather to have fun and you'll certainly love spending a night with "real" Canadians.

Two Indian restaurants, many Italian ones, and a buffet in many places at lunch time for you to eat "as much as you can".

Discover one of the many culinary treasures of St John's thanks to the list below: 

**all**

**Cellar Restaurant (The)**

Located in the heart of downtown St. John's, this restaurant is well known and a good choice for visitors. Among the favourite dishes you'll find fresh and smoked fish chowder, warm spinach salad, scallops, lobster platter, blackened salmon, steaks and lamb rack and you'll certainly enjoy deserts such as a bakeapple cheesecake, a cranberry-apple crisp, a creme caramel or a tiramisu.
