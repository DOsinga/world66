Fort Smith sits at the entrance to Wood Buffalo National Park and offers excellent access to wilderness experiences. The Slave River rapids — among the most dramatic whitewater in northern Canada — are right in town, and the Northern Life Museum provides good context on the region's history and indigenous cultures.
