The rapids of the Slave River at Fort Smith — including the Mountain Portage Rapids and the Rapids of the Drowned — are among the most impressive whitewater in Canada and are considered by kayakers to be some of the finest rapids on the continent. The riverbanks around the rapids are also good habitat for white pelicans.
