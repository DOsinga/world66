**Quttinirpaaq National Park**

Quttinirpaaq National Park (formerly Ellesmere Island National Park) is located at the north end of the northermost island in the Canadian Arctic archipelago. This park has glaciers, ice caps, beautiful mountains, polar desert, artic tundra and even a fertile oasis. Trips here usually entail camping and extensive hiking.  [more..](/northamerica/canada/nunavut/iqaluit/sights/quttinirpaaqnationalpark)
