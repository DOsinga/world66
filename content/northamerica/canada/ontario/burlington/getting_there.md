---
title: "Getting There in Burlington"
type: section
---

By AIR - The closest airports that would serve Burlington are the John Munro International Airport (Hamilton-YHM) and Lester B Pearson International Airport (Toronto-YYZ).

By AUTOMOBILE - From Niagara area or the USA - on the Queen Elizabeth Way (QEW).

From Toronto area - on the QEW, Hwy 407 (toll) or Dundas St. (formerly Hwy #5)

From Detroit or London areas - by Hwy 401 then Hwy 403

By BUS (INTERCITY) -

By BUS (LOCAL TRANSIT) - **Oakville Transit** operates mid-days on Oakville Transit Route 14 to the Burlington Bus Terminal via Lakeshore Road from Oakville. The **Hamilton Street Railway** operates Route 11-Parkdale service from Hamilton, also to the Bus Terminal.

By RAIL - **VIA Rail Canada** has the Aldershot Rail Station located in the eastern portion of Burlington on Waterdown Road. Passengers should refer to CORRIDOR schedules on VIA Rail Canada's site for service. In addition, **GO Transit** offers rush hour service from Aldershot to Toronto, hourl service weekdays from Burlington Station and Appleby Station to Toronto. Weekends will require transfers from buses to trains in Oakville.
