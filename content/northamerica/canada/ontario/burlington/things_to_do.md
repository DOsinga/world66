---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Burlington's main visitor draws are along or near the waterfront. Spencer Smith Park and the adjacent Beachway Park offer swimming and lakeside walking. The Royal Botanical Gardens is Canada's largest botanical garden complex and is worth at least half a day. The Joseph Brant Museum and Ireland House Museum provide a window into the area's 19th-century history, and the Burlington Art Centre shows Canadian contemporary ceramics and craft.
