**Happy Jacks** is likely the first restaurant you'll hear of when asking for a restaurant in Fort Erie.

Specializing in Cantonese/Chinese cuisine, the restaurant is a far distant relative of the typical take-away that you come across in many other cities in Canada and the United States.

The restaurant offers a relaxing, comfortable atmosphere with splashes of red and tans throughout the restaurant. The wait staff is helpful and courteous.
