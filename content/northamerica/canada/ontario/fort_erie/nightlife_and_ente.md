---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment in Fort Erie"
type: section
---

A hidden gem in Fort Erie is GARRISON LITTLE THEATRE which has been voted Best Comedy Dinner Theatre in Niagara Region for many years. This community group of fun-loving volunteers produces three shows per season, 6 performances each. Audiences are in for a treat: $35.00 CD for a full course meal and show (evenings) and just $20.00 for a matinee with lunch. Media and audiences are raving about the high standards of meals and acting.
Garrison Little Theatre will be celebrating Quarter Century next season. You really owe it to yourself to check this out! Especially if you live in Buffalo - it is just a hop across the Peace Bridge. www.garrisonlittletheatre.com
