---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Fort Erie's main outdoor draw is Crystal Beach, which retains a good sandy shore on Lake Erie even though the famous amusement park that once operated here is long gone. The Mather Arch near the Peace Bridge is the site of the annual Friendship Festival. The Niagara Parkway, following the river northward, makes an excellent leisure drive toward Chippawa and Niagara Falls. For indoor entertainment, Garrison Little Theatre — a community dinner theatre that has been producing shows for over 25 years — is well regarded in the region.
