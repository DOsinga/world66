title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

Located on the far west end of the Greater Toronto Area (aka GTA), Hamilton can be reached from a variety of transportation modes.

DRIVING: Located very close to the QEW (Queen Elizabeth Way).

BUS: Trentway Wagar offers line run service from the Greater Niagara Region, including Niagara Falls, St. Catharines, Grimsby, Beamsville, and Vineland. GO Transit offers QEW Express service frequently throughout the day. In addition to bus service, there is three route trips weekdays using train service into the Hunter Street rail station in Hamilton to Toronto.

RAILWAY: VIA Rail's Aldershot Rail Station is located in Burlington, the next city to the east of Hamilton. Check VIA Rail's website for information on it's "Corridor" service between Windsor and Ottawa...

AIR: Hamilton's Airport is rather small in size, but has regularly scheduled service on Air Canada and WestJet.
