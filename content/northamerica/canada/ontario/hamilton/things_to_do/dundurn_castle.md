A National Historic Site and the grandest early Victorian mansion in Ontario, completed in 1835 for Sir Allan Napier MacNab, future Premier of the United Canadas. Over 40 rooms have been restored to 1855, showing both the upstairs life of the MacNab family and the downstairs life of their servants. Costumed guides lead tours.
