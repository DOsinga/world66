---
title: "When to Go"
type: section
---

Summer (June to August) is the peak season, when Fort Henry and the waterfront attractions are fully operating and the Thousand Islands boat tours run on full schedules. Winters are cold and snowy — Kingston sits in a snow belt, and temperatures regularly drop well below freezing from December through February. Spring and fall are pleasant for walking the downtown, and the fall colours in October can be striking. The university calendar shapes the city's rhythm: things are livelier from September through April when students are in town.
