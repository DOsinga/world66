**Mississauga Hotels** are unique in luxury and comfort. These count with all the options and refinements one would expect from five-star hotels..

**Hilton Toronto Airport Hotel & Suites 4 stars**

The closest hotel outside of Toronto International Airport is on West Mississauga. This gigantic hotel's rates begin at $180.00 USD for the Single Select room and go up to $250 with two king-size beds equipped with a Whirlpool washing machine and landscaping sight of North or South Tower outside.

The modern and air-conditioned ambiance and excellent food are there to satisfy the demanding palate. The hotel also counts with lounges where the guests can watch the latest news or sport events. The people that want a fun experience can submerge on the pool, enter to the two available saunas or visit the spa. The restaurant includes every type of international and local dish and counts with it's own experienced chef.

**Conference Centre Hotel **

The hotel's address is 6750 Mississauga Rd avenue and it is the scenario of the most important business events in Mississauga. Rates begin at $198 for a single room with large marital bed and go up to $230 for the Deluxe room with free Internet access. Each room counts with a terrace.

The good news for couples is that this and other Mississauga Hotels offer wedding services with catering service included. There is also a babysitting facility and the women in charge are very kind to the children.

The business facilities, a great 50.000 square feet complex of which 30.000 are unobstructed conference rooms, allow an easy coordination for events. Includes exhibit halls and banquet facilities. Most of the important places are situated on just one floor, making it ideal to carry out the most important corporate events with practicality. The hotel is special for this type of events.

**Quality Inn & Suites Airport Central Hotels**

The Quality Inn Central Hotels location is 5599 Ambler Drive isn't costly like the previous two. A single with one king-size bed is at $90.00 USD, while the king bed and double pull-out couch one is at $109.00 USD. All rooms are equipped with television, voice mail, and air-conditioning. Advantages offered include pool, Internet access, business center, hydromassage, and gym.

This is one of the Mississauga hotels that does not charge extra for children accompanying the guests. The hotel is located on a usually crowded place so many bus transport routes pass right outside the main entrance and finding a taxi outside is no problem.

Outside of the hotel, two Ontario marinas, two golf courses and the lakefront with boat service next to the hotel adorn the landscape sight.
