---
title: "7 Day Itinerary in Niagara falls"
type: section
---

One of the most popular attractions for thousands of
tourists to the United States and Canada is the Niagara Falls. There are
several places to visit and things to do in Niagara Falls. If you are planning
a visit to the spectacular falls, here is an itinerary you can use.

**Day 1 – Goat Island**

Take a trip to Goat Island, which is by far one of the best
places to view the falls. You can get to Goat Island by walking across the
bridge or by car. Goat Island also has the 3 Sisters Island from where you can
view the statue “Nikola Tesla”.

**Day 2 - Maid of the
Mist**

If you want to feel the power of the Falls, this is a great place to do so.
The Maid of the Mist is the oldest attraction in Niagara Falls. Take the boat tour and
you will be able to go down the river closer to the falls. You can take the
boat from either side, the United States or Canada.

**Day 3 – The Skylon
Tower**

With its height of 236 meters, the Skylon Tower offers
visitors an unsurpassed view of the Niagara Falls. The Skylon Tower also has a
revolving restaurant where you can enjoy a romantic meal with amazing views.

**Day 4 - Journey behind the Falls**

The journey behind the falls takes you down below where you
can see the awesome sight of the water crashing down. You can park your car at
the parking lot and take this awesome journey that will leave a lasting
impression in your memory.

**Day 5 - Whirlpool
Aero Car**

The Aero car has been in service since 1916 and is very
popular with tourists. You will have an amazing view of the falls from the car,watching the water suddenly change direction as it creates one of the most breathtaking
sights – the Niagara Whirlpool.

**Day 6 - White Water
Walk**

The White Water Walk is one of the most popular attractions
in Niagara Falls. The walk will give you an experience to remember all your life as you walk on the edge of the wildest whitewater in the world.

**Day 7 – Helicopter Ride**

Take the helicopter ride over the Niagara Falls to give you a breathtaking view of the great falls from the air. Flying over the
falls in the helicopter is one of the things not to be missed while visiting
Niagara Falls.
