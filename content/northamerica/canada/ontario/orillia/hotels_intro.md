Orillia, famously known as the Sunshine City, is located in Simcoe County in Ontario, Canada, and you can use this **Orillia hotels** guide to help plan your vacation to Orillia. Orillia is flanked by Lake Simcoe and Lake Couchiching, and this huge waterfront and its culturally rich heritage makes it one of the most sought after tourist spots in Ontario, Canada. The specialty of the lakes is that you can travel to the waterways of the world from there as they are interconnected. Several tourists visit Orillia for its various attractions like boating, fishing, exploring the city, etc. Whatever your reason may be, there are accommodations available in Orillia to suit every traveler. Given below are some of the Orillia Hotels.

**Fern Resort**

Fern Resort, situated around 9 kilometers from the main city, is the best place if you want to get away from the city and stay close to the waterfront. The Resort is about 110 years old and is tucked away on a point at Lake Couchiching. You can choose from 105 spacious and attractively decorated rooms and suites. There is a room or suite to suit every traveler's mood here. The in house dining area is a warm place with attentive service, providing exquisite breakfast, luncheon and dinner options. Your children can enjoy their special buffet too. On your stay here, you can keep yourself occupied with some outdoor adventure activities, or with their variety of sports options. You can enjoy your day at the waterfront or get pampered in their fitness and recreation centers. Rooms begin from US$150 a night.

**Comfort Inn**

Situated in the heart of the city and with easy access to Casino Rama and several local attractions in Orillia, Canada, the Comfort Inn is the first choice for several tourists. You can choose from 80 rooms in the Inn which are clean and neat. If you are an outdoor enthusiast, the surrounding areas offer several outdoor adventure options. On your stay here, you can enjoy their free breakfast, Internet connection, local calls and 24 hour tea and coffee service. The staff treats you with utmost warmth and hospitality. There are several restaurants available nearby which offer you a wide variety of menus. Prices start from about US$131 a night.

**Casino Rama**

If you enjoy the rush of gambling, trying your luck at the slot machines, gaming tables and other entertainment in a casino, then stay at Casino Rama. Conveniently located in an accessible location by all modes of transport, Casino Rama is the only Casino in Ontario. You can choose form 300 suites available in the luxury hotel. The suites are comfortable with a fire place, television and other modern amenities to make you feel at home. You can spend your day in the Casino or pamper yourself at their spa, where you can avail complementary massages. Choose from the ten restaurants available in Casino Rama, each one offering you delicacies to tickle your taste buds. Prices start from about US$170 a night.
