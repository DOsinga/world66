---
title: "Bars and Cafes"
type: section
---

Ottawa's drinking life is concentrated in two places: the **ByWard Market** for late nights and rowdier crowds, and **Elgin Street** for a slightly more relaxed mix of pubs, cocktail bars and craft beer rooms. **Wellington West**, **Hintonburg** and **Westboro** are where the better cocktail bars and third-wave coffee shops have opened in the last decade. The city has a small but serious craft beer scene — keep an eye out for local breweries like Beyond the Pale, Dominion City and Beau's.
