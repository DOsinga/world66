---
title: "Day Trips"
type: section
---

Ottawa is a good base for getting out into the Canadian Shield and the Ottawa River valley. **Gatineau Park**, only fifteen minutes across the river in Quebec, has hiking, swimming and (in autumn) some of the best leaf colour in eastern Canada. The **Ottawa River** west of the city offers some of the best whitewater rafting in North America — operators run day trips from spring through autumn out of the Foresters Falls area, about 90 minutes upriver. Further afield, **Montreal** and **Kingston** are both reachable in two hours by car or train for a long day out.
