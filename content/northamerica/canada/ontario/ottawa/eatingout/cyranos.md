Cyrano's offers a delightful blend of Turkish and Italian dishes. They have the best Caesar salad in Canada, and the charming decor makes the restaurant a wonderful place for a date. The menu varies from traditional Turkish cuisine to homemade pizzas. I found the restaurant to be very accomodating of the special requests made by our group, and would highly recommend the restaurant to anyone. It is quite popular, so I would also suggest calling to make a reservation if you are looking to have dinner there between 7pm and 9pm.
