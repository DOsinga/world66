The Lumière Festival is a well planned and safe lantern festival, based on similar festivals in Victoria and Vancouver and designed so that the community can be creative and come together for a night of calm and enjoyment. Whether you are on your own or with your family, a teen or a senior, this Festival will AMAZE you, mostly because of the imaginative lanterns you, your friends and neighbours have created from some tissue paper and bamboo. Children are delighted to be part of the magic, wings bumping behind them as they proudly carry their very own jam jar lantern. The Lumière Festival is a chance to witness the beauty of light!

Imagine walking through a park on a warm summer night… you follow a melodic band along the path at the water’s edge, your friends and neighbours are part of this procession each carrying their own paper lantern that they have created at a Lumière lantern workshop… live performers add to the magical atmosphere… in the water you spot a giant paper lantern mermaid and canoes float by carrying their own brightly lit lanterns - they tantalize with their intoxicating blend of shadow, light and colour … you hear a choir and are unable to locate the performers until you are directly under the tree in which they are sitting so you stop to listen and help your child tie a wish on the wishing tree … many children and adults are dressed in midsummer eve costumes - fairies, pixies, elves - magic wands, wings, colourful cloaks, and elf ears are everywhere … in the field you walk the lantern labyrinth made entirely from small lanterns …. And finally as the light begins to dim you begin to experience the magic of seeing thousands of colourful handmade lanterns all walking the same route creating one flickering line of magical light.
