---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment in Ottawa"
type: section
---

While you are here in the city, you just have to check out the **Ottawa Nightlife**. When the sun goes down, the city erupts with many activities to do. No matter if you are here on a budget or here to splurge, there are places where you can find fun and laughter. Whether you enjoy dancing the night away or if you are interested in a more relaxing atmosphere, you can find what you are looking for. Ottawa is packed with nightclubs and other interesting venues of easing entertainment.

#### Mercury Lounge

Mercury Lounge is a dynamic music club that blends several styles of music to enhance the output of the blues. You can dance to electronic, house , soul, jazz and funk. The drinks are reasonably priced. Tickets usually cost about ten dollars. The club is real easy to find since it is located right in the west side of the downtown area, or in the Bywater club square as it is commonly known.

#### Babylon Nightclub

This wild and wacky nightclub always has a spirited atmosphere. Every night is either led by a live band or a pumping DJ. Wednesday is their special night, offering cheap beverages from 10pm through 2am. The cover charge is normally three dollars, unless there is a live band, then tickets always cost ten dollars. This is a great place to take a group of friends to have a fun time.

#### The Collection Bar & Club 56

Enjoy this upper class martini lounge by relaxing in the establishment's unique atmosphere. The place revolves around the outstanding nightly DJ's. You have the opportunity to have a drink and soak up the sounds of jazz, funk, soul, hip-hop and rock. They are open seven days a week from 8pm until 2am. This is a perfect place to take a date for a night of romance.

#### Rainbow Bistro

If you happen to be looking for a club to relax and soak up some blues, then the 'Bow is just the destination for you. This two story building has been furnished to reflect a comfortable setting. One great aspect of the 'Bow is that they have live performances every night of the week. The entertainment ranges from internationally known bands to local up and coming talent.

#### Avant-Garde Bar

Avant-Garde Bar is a Russian owned bar and coffee house. Part of the fun of coming here is checking out all of the Russian decor. The bar is designed in bold colors, curved mirrors and Russian propaganda posters. They serve alcoholic beverages, coffee, sandwiches and pastries. The entertainment varies on any night from live bands to poetry readings.
