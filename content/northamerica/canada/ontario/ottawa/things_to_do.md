Ottawa's sights cluster in three groups. The political core sits on Parliament Hill above the Ottawa River, with the Supreme Court and Library of Parliament a short walk away. The national museums are spread across the city — most are walkable from downtown but a couple (Aviation, Science and Tech) sit further out and are worth the trip.
