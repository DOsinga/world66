The southernmost tip of mainland Canada, jutting into Lake Erie at roughly the same latitude as northern California. Birders make pilgrimages here every spring and fall — over 370 species have been recorded during migration. In autumn, clouds of Monarch butterflies stop here on their way to Mexico.
