---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

Prince Edward County has no train or bus service worth relying on — a car is essentially essential. From Toronto, take Highway 401 east to Trenton or Belleville (roughly two hours), then follow the county roads south across the bridge. From Kingston, it's about forty minutes west on the 401 then south.

Via Rail stops at Belleville, which is the closest rail station to the county. From there you'll need a car — taxis are available but expensive, and rental options in Belleville are limited. A handful of shuttle services operate during peak season connecting Belleville to Picton; check locally for current operators.
