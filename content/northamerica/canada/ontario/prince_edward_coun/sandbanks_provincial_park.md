The centrepiece of any visit to the county. Sandbanks contains the world's largest freshwater sand dune system — some dunes rise ten metres — and three distinct beach areas: Sandbanks Beach, Outlet Beach (a long spit between the lake and West Lake), and Dunes Beach. Outlet Beach is the most striking, with warm, shallow water on the lagoon side ideal for families. Book your campsite months in advance for summer weekends; the park fills completely. Day-use passes are sold online.
