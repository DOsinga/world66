---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

The county's main draw is Sandbanks Provincial Park, but there's more than enough to fill a long weekend beyond it. Cycling the back roads between villages is a pleasure — the terrain is flat, traffic is light, and there's usually a winery or farmgate stand within reach. The arts scene punches above its weight for a rural county: galleries, studios, and the Regent Theatre in Picton run year-round.
