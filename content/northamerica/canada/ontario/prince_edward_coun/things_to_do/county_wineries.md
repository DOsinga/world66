Prince Edward County has over forty wineries, most clustered along the wine route between Wellington, Hillier, and Waupoos. The county's cool climate and limestone-heavy soil suit Pinot Noir and Chardonnay particularly well. Norman Hardie Winery and Huff Estates are the best-known stops; Trail Estate and Closson Chase are smaller and worth seeking out. Most tasting rooms are open May through October, with reduced hours in the shoulder seasons. Cycling between wineries is entirely feasible on the gentler stretches of the route.
