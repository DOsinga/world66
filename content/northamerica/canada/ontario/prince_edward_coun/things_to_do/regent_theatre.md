Picton's beautifully restored 1920s cinema is the cultural anchor of the county. It screens arthouse and independent films, hosts live music, and runs the Picton Cinematheque series. It's one of those rare small-town venues that consistently punches above its weight — worth checking the programme before you visit to plan around a show.
