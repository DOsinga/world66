---
title: "When to Go"
type: section
---

The sweet spot is late May through June or September through early October. Summer (July–August) is peak season: Sandbanks is packed, accommodation prices spike, and restaurant reservations become necessary weeks out. The weather is warm and reliable, so the crowds are forgivable, but mid-week visits make a real difference.

September is the best month. Harvest season brings fruit stands, winery events, and the Taste! food festival. The beaches clear out after Labour Day but the water is still warm. Foliage starts turning by mid-October and the county quiets to near-silence. Winter is very slow — many restaurants and shops close entirely from November to April, so check ahead before visiting off-season.
