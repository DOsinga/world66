title: Pukaskwa National Park
type: poi

A true wilderness park on the northern shore of Lake Superior, preserving a chunk of the ancient Canadian Shield that feels genuinely remote. Wolves, moose, woodland caribou, and lynx roam here. Access is limited to boat or float plane for most of the park — plan ahead and come prepared.
