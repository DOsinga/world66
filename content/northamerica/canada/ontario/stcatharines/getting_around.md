The St. Catharines Transit Commission operates weekday and Saturday service in the City of St Catharines and modified service on less routes on Sundays. Visit www.yourbus.com for more information.

St. Catharines Transit also links up with Niagara Transit and Welland Transit services at the Brock University, in the south section of the city.
