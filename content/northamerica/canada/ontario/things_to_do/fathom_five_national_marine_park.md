title: "Fathom Five National Marine Park"
type: poi
category: "Sight"
latitude: 45.2833
longitude: -81.6667
---

Canada's first national marine park, protecting 20 islands at the mouth of Georgian Bay. Twenty-two shipwrecks lie within park boundaries, making it a favourite for divers. The underwater visibility here is exceptional, and glass-bottom boat tours let non-divers peek at the wrecks below.
