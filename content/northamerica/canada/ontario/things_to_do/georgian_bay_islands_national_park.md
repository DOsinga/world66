title: Georgian Bay Islands National Park
type: poi

A collection of islands in Lake Huron known for an unusual mix of granite outcrops, hardwood forests, and an impressive variety of reptiles and amphibians — more species than almost anywhere else in Canada. Access is by boat only, which keeps the crowds manageable. Beausoleil Island is the main hub.
