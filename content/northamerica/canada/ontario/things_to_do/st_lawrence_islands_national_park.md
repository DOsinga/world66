Canada's smallest national park, scattered across a string of islands in the Thousand Islands stretch of the St. Lawrence River in eastern Ontario. The paddling and sea kayaking here are superb, and the water is unusually warm by Canadian standards. Rent a boat in Gananoque to explore at your own pace.
