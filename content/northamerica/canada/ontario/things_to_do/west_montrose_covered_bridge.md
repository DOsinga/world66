title: "West Montrose Covered Bridge"
type: poi
category: "Sight"
latitude: 43.585613
longitude: -80.481482
---

The last remaining covered bridge in Ontario, built in 1881 over the Grand River between the Guelph area and St. Jacobs/Elmira. It's 198 feet long and still open to vehicular traffic — locals call it the Kissing Bridge. Watch for Mennonite horse-and-buggy crossings.
