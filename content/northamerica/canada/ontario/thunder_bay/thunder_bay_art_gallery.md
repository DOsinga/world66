type: poi

One of the most significant collections of contemporary Indigenous art in Canada. The gallery focuses on work by First Nations artists from Northwestern Ontario and the surrounding region, and the quality and range of the collection is well above what you might expect from a city this size. There are also touring exhibitions and programming that connects the art to living Anishinaabe culture. Located on the Confederation College campus.
