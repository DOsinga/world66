Each day, thousands of visitors arriving at Toronto’s Lester B. Pearson International Airport take advantage of Pacific Western's Airport Express to downtown Toronto.

Convenient and economical, the fully accessible Ground Transportation
service operates every 20 minutes during peak periods and every 30
minutes during off-peak periods. This service can accommodate all
schedules and offers travelers a number of major downtown hotels as a
destination choice.

**Fares**

One Way: $18.50 (valid for 30 days from date of purchase)
Return: $29.95 (valid for one year)
10 Trip Book: 134.95 (valid for one year)

Student and Seniors receive a 10% discount for one way fares only
Children 11 and under traveling with an adult, travel free

GST is included in the fares

**Service and Routes

**
Airport Express operate 365 days a year, from 4 am to 1 am. Below is a
list of pick up and drop off locations that are serviced:

* Metropolitan & U of T Residence
* Toronto Coach Terminal
* Delta Chelsea Inn
* Bond Place
* Sheraton Centre
* Holiday Inn on King
* Intercontinental Hotel
* Fairmont Royal York Hotel
* Westin Harbour Castle

**Where To Find Them

**
Customer Service Representatives are positioned at both International
Terminals, and have the following locations within each Terminal:

Terminal 1 - Arrivals Level (Outside Domestic Baggage Claim Area), bus departs from Posts B and E.
Terminal 3 - Located Curbside at Area # 25

These
locations are strategically placed for ease of passenger travel. Each
Customer Service Representative has the knowledge of Toronto and the
ability to assist travelers with information and advice on how to get
to their final destinations.

**How To Purchase Tickets

**
Tickets could be purchased at the above locations from the Customer
Service Representatives, and also from the drivers or selected hotels
located on the route. However, the quickest and cheapest way is online.
