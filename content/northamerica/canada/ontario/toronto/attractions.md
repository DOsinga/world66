**CN Tower . **

Located in the heart of Downtown Toronto, the world's tallest building (553 metres) stands between the SkyDome and the Metro Toronto Convention Centre on Front Street, just north of Bremner Blvd. Near the top, a 24 sq. metre glass floor allows visitors to watch the streets under their feet. **Harbourside Playhouse . **

Harbourside Playhouse is a year round, professional theatre company offering patrons quality entertainment at affordable prices. Located in Port Credit, Mississauga's Village on the Lake, Harbourside Playhouse has quickly become one of Ontario's fastest growing theatres.
