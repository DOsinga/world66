No trip to Toronto would be complete without a trip to the fabled St. Lawrence Market at the corner of Front Street and Jarvis Street. Built in 1850 the Market has been serving up Toronto's best selection of fresh meat and produce as well as an amazing selection of cheap lunch and dinner options. The place bustles with hungry shoppers Tuesday to Saturday, with Friday lunches an especially busy time. Check out Mustachio's in the basement and their humungous veal and eggplant sandwich ($6) - though come early and be prepared to join the queue. Open Tuesday to Saturday.
