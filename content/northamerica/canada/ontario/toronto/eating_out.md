Toronto is the largest city in Canada situated in the province of Ontario, and there are many** Toronto restaurants** to choose from when dining out in this city. Toronto is situated on the northwest shore of Lake Ontario and is a prime city in Canada. From aboriginal dwellers to the cosmopolitan population that Toronto boasts of today, the city has a long and interesting history and culture. Being a vibrant city, Toronto has a lot to offer to its visitors, from places to dine to entertainment activities to historical monuments. Given below are some of the Toronto Restaurants.

**Canoe Restaurant and Bar**

Situated on the Wellington Street in the TD Bank Tower on the 54th Floor, this is one of the best restaurants on Toronto. Choose from their continental menu and some local Canadian dishes on your visit here. You should try out the roast hind of Yukon caribou with zucchini, which is one of their famous Canadian dishes. Since it is located in the financial district of Toronto, you will get to see several business men in the restaurant. The ambiance is great, and the friendly staff go out of their way to make you feel comfortable and at home. The bar serves some of the best drinks in Toronto. The overall experience in the Canoe is amazing, thus making it one of the must-visit restaurants.

**Auberge Du Pommier**

Situated on Yonge Street, Toronto, Canada, Auberge Du Pommier is one of the best French restaurants in Toronto. The restaurant is housed in a 19th century building and is one of the best known names in fine French dining. If you are on a romantic holiday or on your honeymoon, then this is the place for you. This posh restaurant offers you some of the best modern French cuisine and a choice from 500 labeled wine selections. Seasonal menus accompanied by cozy fireplaces in winter and floral terraces in summer steal the show at Auberge Du Pommier. There cannot be a better place for your romantic dinner.

**Little India**

Situated on Queen Street in the heart of Toronto, this is the best place to visit if you want to try out some authentic Indian cuisine. Indian hospitality to the hilt is what you can expect here. The staff is friendly and eager to help. On your visit here, do not miss out the Butter and Tandoori Chicken cooked with authentic Indian spices. This is a nice place to unwind and relax.
