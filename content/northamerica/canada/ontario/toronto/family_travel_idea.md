---
title: "Family Travel Ideas in Toronto"
type: section
---

If you are planning a Canada fishing trip but aren't sure
where to go, I'd highly suggest planning a trip to Ontario. Ontario fishing can
be extremely rewarding, particularly if you are an angler (or a fisherman who
enjoys catching pike fish), because Ontario has plenty of walleye fishing and
muskie fishing opportunities. Even if you aren't an angler but are planning on
going fishing in Ontario, you should try to squeeze in some walleye and musky
fishing, as this type of fishing is really what a Canada fishing trip is all
about.
