First or the hundreth time in Toronto? This place will always make you
happy! It's all you need to look for in fun and classy evening
entertainment in the busiest city in Canada. Start out with late
afternoon cocktail on the patio and fabulous dinner with Persian
twist from renown chefs that have proven their skills at the Gypsy-Coop
oldschool modern eatery and spread out their wings to feed you at the
corner of entertainment district with the feel of exotic lands and
flavors. Chill on leather sofa with a drink surrounded by colorful
carpets and groovy tunes by local and visiting DJ's. Watch the place
get packed as the night steams up and brings some beautiful people with
trendy focus and in a need for a place to be seen. You can make it
whathever you might want it to be. An after movie drink lounge(one of
the biggest movie theaters in the city is just across the street), or
make it your dinner venue before hard partying in a hundred clubs next
door,shop till you drop on Queen street west and stop over for a tasty
grub or make it your final destination for a chilled sunday afternoon
begining with brunch and ending with the doorguy leading you out after
closing time.
