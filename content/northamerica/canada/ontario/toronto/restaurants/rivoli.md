This is a popular with locals and has been for many years. There is
only perks to this place, a fantastic patio on the busy, popular and
funky Queen Street West strip, great restaurant with walls lined with
regularly changing and interesting art (in a good way), side bar with
great service, fantastic international food to please all tastebuds,
back room with nightly bands (some really cool ones) and best of all an
upstairs, full floor poolhall, that has somewhere around 15 tables,with
a bar that also serves food.

The menu will give you difficulties, it's fairly large and contains
some fantastic dishes from different corners of the world, that you
will be very tempted to sample. It's fine dining at it's most casual
for reasonable prices.The main courses are anywhere $10-$20 for a plate
and a complete dinner with a glass of wine or a beer and a tip will
cost you about $50/person.
