A hidden treasure of amazingingly good and affordable food the St. Lawrence market offers up everything you can imagine to do it yourself or be treated like a king. Check out Mustachio's in the basement and their amazing veal and eggplant sandwich ($6). The main level hosts several sandwich joints with meat being the primary ingredient - though you won't have to look far for vegetarian options. The basement offers up Chinese, Japanese, Ukrainian, Polish, Greek, Vegan and a plethera of additional choices for any indecisive guest.
