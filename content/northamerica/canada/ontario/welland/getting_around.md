---
title: "Getting Around in Welland"
type: section
---

Welland Transit runs city bus service on weekdays and Saturdays; there is no Sunday service. Routes cover the main residential areas, shopping plazas, Niagara College, and a Brock University shuttle. Buses run roughly every 30 minutes. For current routes and schedules, check the [City of Welland transit page](http://www.city.welland.on.ca).
