Welland Transit is the public transportation provider for the City of Thorold.

City buses operate every day except Sunday & Holidays, and run approximately every 30 minutes.

### [Welland Transit Commission](/northamerica/canada/ontario/welland/getting_around/welland_transit_co)

Welland Transit operates ten city routes and the Brock University shuttle during weekdays and saturdays.

Route 1 - Broadway via Main...serves primarily residential area in southwest portion of Welland, along Windsor, Riverside, Broadway and King.

Route 2 - Rice Road...serves Welland Plaza and Northtown Plaza along Prince Charles Drive, Thorold Road, Clare, Rice, Wilson and Prince Charles Drive.

Route 3 - First Avenue...serves Niagara College and the Y.M.C.A. along Summit, Centennial, Woodlawn and First Av.
