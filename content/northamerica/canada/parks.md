Canada features a tremendous assortment of national parks featuring wildlife, natural features, historical and heritage sites. Some parks are in such remote locations there is a reasonable chance you would not meet other visitors as you explored such parks. Others are located along major highway systems.

**Points of Interest**

**Parks Canada**

Parks Canada is the federal government agency responsible for managing Canada's national park system. This agency has prepared a site containing comprehensive information on Canada's national park system, heritage and historical sites, and related maps and literature. A fascinating feature of this site is the provision of on-line narrated video tours of selected parks throughout Canada. To learn more about Canada's national park system, and to obtain information on specific national parks, visit Parks Canada [more..](/northamerica/canada/parks/parkscanada)
