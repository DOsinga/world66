**Charlottetown hotels** come in all shapes and sizes, catering to a range of tastes and all pocket sizes. The city&rsquo;s central location draws families, tourists and businesspeople alike. As a result, Charlottetown hotels look to accommodate the very different requirements of these visitors to the city. For the visitor, there are picturesque guesthouses, lodges and hotels to choose from, with factors such as rates and location to consider.

**
Charlottetown Hotels for Families**

Places like Shaw&rsquo;s Hotels and Cottages offer no-frills options for a family getaway. Shaw&rsquo;s outdoors setting, with its trail to the beach and the water, is ideal for families and couples looking for a peaceful, out-the-way place to stay. Walking the trail, kayaking and cycling are all options here. The accommodation itself is quite sparse, though spacious, but the food, service and setting make its average price of $75 a night quite competitive.

**
Charlottetown Hotels and Guesthouses**

There are a number of fine guesthouses in Charlottetown which cater to those tourists looking to experience the city itself, its historic downtown district or one of its many festivals. The Heritage Harbour House Inn is one of these. Ideally located on Grafton Street, near the harbor front and downtown, the Inn is extremely popular with its guests. Well known for its outstanding hospitality, outstanding meals and professional yet friendly service, the Three-Star rated HHH has an average rate of $99 per night.

**
Top Star Charlottetown Hotels **

The Delta Prince Edward on Queen Street is a Four-Star hotel in the heart of Charlottetown. Its efficiency of service, high-speed wireless Internet connection and central location are ideal for business people visiting the city (although these features suit every guest). It offers all the amenities one would expect from a highly-rated hotel: indoor swimming pools, spa and fitness center; golf simulator, babysitting and laundry services, valet parking, and there are two on-site restaurants to choose from when dining in. Naturally, this also means that the average rate per night is pricy: $199; but then you get what you pay for.

Charlottetown hotels offer a wide range of options to visitors. Rates vary according to the nature of service you may need. For no-frills fun, you can expect to pay far less than if you require speedy service or easy access to the heart of the city. You will however find the famed Canadian friendliness throughout.
