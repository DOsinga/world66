Souris is a small, friendly town at the eastern tip of Prince Edward Island, about 60 km from Charlottetown. The town has clean sandy beaches along the Northumberland Strait and the nearby Basin Head Provincial Park — known for its "singing sands" — is a popular summer destination. Souris is also the departure point for the ferry to the Magdalen Islands (Îles-de-la-Madeleine) in Quebec. The local fishing economy is reflected in a good selection of seafood restaurants in town.
