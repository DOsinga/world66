**Mount-Laurier town**
Benedictine Abbey, City centre, Cultural centre, Exposition hall, Alix House, Belvedere, lineair Parc.

**Des Ruisseaux **region
For fishing, several well-stocked lakes.

**Saint-Aimé-du-Lac-des-Iles **region
Barrage of the Vieux Moulin (Old Mill), nature trail round Gaucher Lake.
