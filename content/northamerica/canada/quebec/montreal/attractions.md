Montréal is one of those amazing cities where a person can discover something new each and every day.

**Points of Interest**

**La Ronde**

Montreal's largest amusement park is La Ronde - situated in the parc des Iles. To see a live view of the park, and to obtain information on opening hours, visit La Ronde.

**Old Port of Montréal **

For live views of the Port d'Escale, the Artist's Promenade and the Interactive Science Center, visit the Old Port of Montréal . This image updates every 20 seconds.

**Montréal Biodome**

The Montréal Biodome features four striking natural environments of North and South America: the Tropical Forest, the Laurentian Forest, the St. Lawrence Marine ecosystem, and the Arctic and Antarctic Polar Worlds. For a live view of the subantarctic zone, visit the Biodome. This image updates every 20 seconds.

**Montréal Olympic Stadium . **

The Montréal Olympic Stadium was built for the 1976 Summer Olympic Games in Montréal. After the olympics the stadium was used for the purpose of providing a facility to young athletes. It is now the home of the Montréal Expos and also serves as a venue for major events such as concerts and competitions. 
 Tel: 514 790-1245 
 Address: 4549 av. Pierre de Coubertin Montréal QC H1V 3N7 - (View on Map)
