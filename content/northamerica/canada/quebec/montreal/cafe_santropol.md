A Montréal institution in the Plateau since 1976, Santropol serves creative sandwiches on thick bread stacked with unusual combinations — the Minted Ham (ham, mint, apples) is a favourite, as is the Killer Tomato for vegetarians. The teas are their own blends and worth trying. The old house with a garden terrace is pleasant in summer. Doubles as a social enterprise; profits support community food programs.
