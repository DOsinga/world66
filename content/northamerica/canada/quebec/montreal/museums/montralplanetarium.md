For over 30 years the Montréal Planetarium has introduced people to the wonders of the stars and space. This season's schedule includes seven multimedia productions: Asteroids, Lost in Space, L'Univers du Petit Prince (French only), Tomorrow, the Moon, A Night of Magic, The End of the World, and The Star of the Magi.
