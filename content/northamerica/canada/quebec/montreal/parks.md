**Parc des Îles**

Montréal's largest park, Parc de Îles is easily reached by metro and a popular venue for outdoor lovers and athletes. Nautical equipment rental available. 15 km trail for biking and inline skating, rentals available. 3km trail for hiking, activity fee required.
