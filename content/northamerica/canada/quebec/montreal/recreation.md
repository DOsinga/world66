**Montréal Expos . **

Major League Baseball's Montréal Expos are a fixture is Canadian sports. Formed in 1969, the Expos have enjoyed tremendous success as fanchise as well as a tourist destination. The Big O, the attractive stadium built for the 1976 Olympic Games and now the Expos home turf, is located on Sherbrooke Street at Olympic Park. General Admission $7. 
 Tel: 8Go-Expo
 Toll Free: 1-800-GO-EXPOS

**Montréal Canadiens . **

One of the National Hockey League's original teams, the Montréal Canadians have won an incredible 24 Stanley Cups over the years. Former greats include Maurice the "Rocket" Richard, Guy Lafleur and Ken Dryden.
