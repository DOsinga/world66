Profile

The largest indoor amusement center in Quebec !
Ever since it first opened in 1969, the Recreatheque has been providing children and adults something to smile about. Featuring a wide range of activities for the entire family, Recreatheque, with its 225 000 square feet of FunFou is THE place to enjoy a wonderful time!

Canada ’s biggest rollerblading rink!
The Roulatheque is still one of the most popular activities within the Recreatheque. With its 18 000 square feet rink, multi-coloured lights and swinging rythms, the Roulatheque brings rollerblading to a whole new level!

The outstanding recreational mega-complex!
Kids will love the Bananazoo, an indoor playground including a gigantic maze, an electric train, face painting, animation and much more! The Himalaya Jet thrills amusement-ride fans while the arcades feature the best electronic games on the market. Tennis, racquetball, ping-pong and a gym challenges fitness and hand-eye coordination. Minigolf, billiard and the new bowling section with famous music keeps family and friends in a perfect mood.

Delta Laser tag game uses strategy and team spirit to capture the enemy’s home base.
An extraordinary experience is awaiting you at the Detla Laser tag, where special effects and smoke shows put you in a terrific mood. This interactive game of precision and offensive challenges the most audacious players.

Recreatheque can be rented out for birthday parties, school field trips and corporate events. Food is available on site. Activities schedules and prices vary; consult the www.recreatheque.com for more information.
