**Cafe Santropol . **

Santropol is a one-of-kind Montréal experience. The restaurant serves a variety of interesting sandwiches (try the Minted Ham with ham, mint and apples, vegetarians try the Killer Tomato ), desserts, their own special teas, and organic coffees. Tel: 514 842-3110 Fax: 514 284-4256 Address: 3 [more..](/northamerica/canada/quebec/montreal/restaurants/cafesantropol)

**Whisky Café . **

The Whisky Café serves a large selection of fine whiskies, ports, grappas, cognacs and cigars, with a limited selection of food as accompaniment. There is a ventilated cigar room next to the main seating area.  Tel: 514 278 2646  Address: 5800 Boul. St. Laruent Montréal QC H2T 1T3  

**Ferreira Cafe Trattoria . **

Cafe Ferriera serves modern European fare along with an impressive wine list which includes a large selection of ports. Tel: 514 848-0988 Fax: 514 848-9375 Address: 1446, rue Peel Montréal Québec 

**Le Festin du Gouverneur . **
