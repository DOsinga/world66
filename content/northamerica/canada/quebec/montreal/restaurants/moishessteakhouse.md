Worst service ever. 

My first and last time. 

Reservations were for 18:45. Had to stand with about 50 other people until a table was ready for us. We were seated at 19:45. Order was taken at 20:00, and food was brought out at 20:45. 

We spilled a glass of wine which spilled on the table cloth, and into the piclkle plate. They never changed the pickles, nor asked if we wanted them changed. BRB

We had to actually ask from fresh pepper. You would think at such an establishement, they would bring it without being asked. Once brought, it was put by the waiter on one partie's steak. The other three were never asked if they wanted fresh pepper. The pepper was just put on the table, and the waiter went away. 

We were never even asked if we wanted another bottle of wine. 

Although the food was OK, I will never ever go there again.
