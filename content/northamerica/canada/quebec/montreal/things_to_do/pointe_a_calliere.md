The best museum in Montréal for understanding the city's history. Built directly above the archaeological remains of the city's birthplace, the museum takes you underground to walk among the actual foundations and artefacts of 17th-century Montréal. The multimedia presentations are unusually well-produced. Temporary exhibitions tend to be ambitious and draw international loans. Located in Old Montréal on Place Royale, one of the oldest public squares in North America. Allow two to three hours. Closed Mondays outside summer.
