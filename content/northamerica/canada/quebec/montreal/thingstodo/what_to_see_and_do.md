Montreal Attractions & Holiday info

Montreal, the island city, possesses a truly romantic and elegant atmosphere if somewhat chaotic. Claiming status as the second largest French-speaking city in the world, it is linked to its European roots by language and by architecture, reminding the visitor of other, older cities - worn but magnificent. It is a city in which style appears to count above all else, celebrating the religious roots of the city by the building of literally dozens of beautiful churches, and hosting a ragbag of festival events each spring and autumn. Montreal's reputation as a great place for a night - or a day - out is justly earned.
