Montreal is well worth exploring. Some of the agencies below can assist you as you discover Montreal.

**Points of Interest**

**Montreal AML Cruises**

Largest range of trips on the Saint Lawrence River. Cruise, dinner-cruise, boats and bilingual interpretation. Tel: 1 514 842-3871 Toll Free: 1-800-667-3131 Address: Old Port/de l'Horloge Pier Montréal QC 
 
 [more..](/northamerica/canada/quebec/montreal/tours/montrealamlcruises)

**Delco Aviation Ltée **

Wide range of aerial excursions over the city of Montreal and other tourist areas. Seaplanes available during summer and Hydraskis in winter. Tel: 1 450 663-4311 

**Bateau-Mouche au Vieux-Port de Montréal**

An exclusive tour aboard a glass roofed boat. 
 Tel: 1 514 849-9952 Fax: 1 514 849-9851
 Toll Free: 1 800 361-9952 
 Address: Old Port/Jacques-Cartier Pier Montréal QC 
 

**Saute-Moutons Jet-Boating & Lachine Rapids Tours **

Jet boat rides on the Lachine Rapids, rafting, and speed boating. Lunch and dinner packages are available for groups. 
 Tel: 514 284-9607 Fax: 514 287-9401 
 Address: Old Port / the Clock Tower Pier Montréal QC H2Y 2C6 
 
 [more..](/northamerica/canada/quebec/montreal/tours/sautemoutonsjetboatinglachinerapidstours)

**Vélo-Tour Montréal**

Bicycle tours in English, French and Spanish. Bicycle rentals available. 
 Tel: 1 514 236-8356 
 Address: 99 de la Commune Street West Montréal QC 
 

**Autocar Connaisseur - Gray Line**

A wide range of guided sightseeing tours. Enjoy a tour of Montréal on board a street car replicated from the old days. 
 Tel: 1 514 934-1222 
 Address: 1001 du Square-Dorchester Street Montréal QC 
 [more..](/northamerica/canada/quebec/montreal/tours/autocarconnaisseurgrayline)

**Descentes sur le Saint-Laurent **

Rafting adventures and Hydro-Jet. Free shuttle service from the Infotouriste Centre. 
 Tel: 1 514 767-2230 
 Address: 1001 du Square-Dorchester Street Montréal QC 
 

**Les Services de Calèches et Traîneaux Lucky Luc **

A unique experience as you tour Montréal in the comfort of a carriage, sleigh, or long coach. 
 Tel: 1 514 934-6105 

**Autocar Royal**

City tours offered in English and French. Daily departures. The Carrousel Tour offers ten stop-off points during the summer. 
 Tel: 1 514 871-4733 
 Address: Infotouriste Centre, 1001 du Square-Dorchester St. Montréal QC

**Guido Nincheri, Stained Glass Alchemist**

Guided motor/walking tours, discovering Montréal's churches' stained glass by Guido Nincheri, the great glass painting master. 
 Tel: 1 514 256-4636 
 Address: Leaving from the Masionneuve Market Place Montréal QC

 Tel: 1 514 496-PORT 
 Address: Old Port of Montréal, Montréal QC
