---
title: "L'Ardoise"
type: poi
address: "71 rue Saint-Paul, Québec City, QC G1K 3V8"
---

A reliable bistro in the Lower Town, known above all for its mussels. The all-you-can-eat moules formula is excellent value, with around a dozen different sauce preparations to choose from — including some creative Québécois variations. Good quality ingredients and a relaxed atmosphere. Popular and often busy; worth booking ahead.
