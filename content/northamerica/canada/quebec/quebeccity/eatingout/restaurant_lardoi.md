One word sums it up: Excellent!

There is a menu here, but the specialty is mussels. My significant other and I visited in April 2006 and both ordered the all-you-can-eat mussels. Mussels are a favorite of mine and I have had them prepared many different ways. These were safely the most delicious I have had. There are about a dozen different sauces to choose from. The four we tried were all fantastic, due in part to the chef's creativity and care and also the superb quality of the starting ingredients. Friendly service in a nice bistro atmosphere.
