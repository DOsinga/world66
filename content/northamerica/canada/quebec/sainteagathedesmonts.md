Ste. Agathe-des-Monts, a picturesque corner of the world. Rich in culture and heritage, yours to discover and rediscover.

Our town is a unique destination with a multitude of possibilities. It is 20 minutes from Saint-Sauveur and Mont Tremblant. The magnificent Lac des Sables located in the heart of the town is always appealing to visitors. As well, you can enjoy a variety of outdoor activities all year round.

In Ste. Agathe-des-Monts, you will find a variety of lodging facilities as well as restaurants which will fancy any taste. Shops and boutiques are open to make your stay more enjoyable. 

It is the destination by choice.
