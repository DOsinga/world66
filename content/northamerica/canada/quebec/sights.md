**Mingan Archipelago National Park**

Mingan Archipelago National Park consists of a string of islands located along the north shore of the Gulf of St. Lawrence. Approximately 40 islands and 2000 islets and reefs are within park boundaries. **Forillon National Park**

Forillon National Park was created in 1970 and helps preserve a portion of the Gaspe Peninsula. The park encloses 244 square kilometers and is well known for containing large colonies of sea birds. For more information, and a virtual tour of park features, visit Forillon National Park. 

**La Mauricie National Park**

La Mauricie National Park preserves a portion of the southwestern Canadian Shield. The park was created in 1970 and encloses 536 square kilometers. For more information, plus a virtual tour of park features, visit La Mauricie National Park. 

**Saguenay - St. Lawrence Marine Park**

Saguenay - St. Lawrence Marine Park helps protect a rich diversity of marine life including whales and seals. For more information on park features, visit Saguenay - St. Lawrence Marine Park.
