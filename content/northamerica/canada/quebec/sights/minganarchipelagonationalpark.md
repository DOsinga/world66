Mingan Archipelago National Park consists of a string of islands located along the north shore of the Gulf of St. Lawrence. Approximately 40 islands and 2000 islets and reefs are within park boundaries. The park was created in 1984 to help protect large seabird colonies, seals, dolphins and whales. For more information, plus a virtual tour of park features, visit Mingan Archipelago National Park.
