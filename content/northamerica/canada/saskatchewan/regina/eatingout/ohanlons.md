This place has to be the best Irish pub in Canada. The decor, music and staff make it as authentic as possible. The food is out of this world and cheap like borsht.

It's nice to sit on the deck and have a pint with friends. The beer is maybe a bit more expensive than you would want it to be, but that could be said for almost any beer that you drink now-a-days.
