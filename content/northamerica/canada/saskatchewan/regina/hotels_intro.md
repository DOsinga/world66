Regina, Saskatchewan, Canada, also called the &ldquo;Queen City&rdquo; is host to many **Regina hotels**. Named after Queen Victoria, Regina is the capital of Saskatchewan. It is the second largest city in the province and it was formerly the headquarters for the Northwest territories and is 9.4 miles from the U.S. border.

**Delta Hotel Regina **

Ensconced at the center of Regina, Delta hotel offers visitors an access to its sports facilities and business travelers an on-site business facility in which they can transact their activities. Guests can visit the Casino Regina by a skyway. This hotel has all the modern facilities expected of a four-star hotel. Delta hotel offers disabled access and child and baby-sitting services. Guests can also bring their pets along with them to this hotel. Prices range from $100 to $110.

**Holiday Inn Express Hotels and Suites Regina**

The Holiday Inn Regina is one of the best hotels in the area. Situated in the middle of downtown Regina it offers both business and leisure travelers access to the interest points in the Regina area. It offers all the usual facilities and it is near the Saskatchewan Science Center, Casino Regina, the Wascana Center, and University of Regina. Room rates are $100 per night.

**Quality Hotel Regina**

Another quality hotel in downtown Regina, This is the center of action-business and leisure. The air-conditioned rooms provide the usual amenities like TVs, hair dryers, phones and an Internet connection. The hotel has also on-site restaurants, fitness facilities, spas, and bars. The balance of the rich culture of Regina and the emerging technology will make a visitor&rsquo;s stay an unforgettable experience. Rooms cost from $100 to $110 a night.

**Regina Inn Hotel**

This newly-renovated hotel offers a wonderful view of downtown Regina and Wascana Lake. Painted dark brown, beige, and mocha with German chocolate accents, the refurbished rooms have 100 percent pure cotton bed sheets and subdued lighting, if needed. The hotel provides guests complimentary use of parking space, fitness and business facilities. The renovated hotel has spacious rooms available to guests and business class travelers. Room rates at this hotel are $110 a night.
**
Sandman Hotel and Spa Regina**

This hotel is ideal for rest and relaxation as well as for business opportunities. The rooms include air-conditioning, high speed Internet, TV, and dry cleaning. If you want to pamper yourself, you can spend time in the hotel&rsquo;s state-of-the art spa, massage and beauty center. The management is also planning to put up a Moxie classic grill, indoor pool and a whirlpool. Rooms cost $110 per night.
