A small, unpretentious diner close to Wascana Centre in the south end of downtown. The menu leans toward soups, sandwiches, and daily specials — straightforward cooking at reasonable prices. Popular with the weekday office crowd, so go before the lunch rush or risk a wait. The kitchen wraps up service by mid-afternoon, so it's strictly a breakfast and lunch stop.
