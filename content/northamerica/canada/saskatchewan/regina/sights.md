Regina has too many sights to mention here. We are proud of the sights, hidden and not, that can be found by people not familiar with the city. If you keep and eye or two open you may just find something you had been looking for all your life.

The lake is nice in the summer.

If you sit infront of Roca Jacks during the summer there isn't really much more you need to see than people walking around and enjoying the day.
