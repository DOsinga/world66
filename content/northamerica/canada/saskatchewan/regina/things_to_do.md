Some of its main attractions are:

**Wascana Park** - a man-made, 2,300-acre park in the middle of the city. It is the third largest urban park in Canada and one of the largest in North America.

**RCMP Depot** - This is where all RCMP cadets go for their initial law enforcement training. The Depot includes a museum and a heritage centre. Every Tuesday between July 1st and August 12, visitors can watch the Sunset-Retreat Ceremony.

**Buffalo Days and Agribition** - A summer fair that includes a midway of dozens of rides, displays and events.

**Regina Downtown** - 700 retail stores, theatres (movie and live theatre) and restaurants among parks and office towers.

**Maxwell's Water Park** - Waterslide theme park with eight slides, river ride, flash flood and gator walk. Also has a picnic area, volleyball courts and baseball diamonds.

**Royal Saskatchewan Museum** - Showcases of geological, ecological and First Nations history.

**Regina Floral Conservatory** - Tropical collection of plants and flowers.

**Casino Regina** - 600 slot machines, 35 game tables, Canada's finest poker room, dining, gift shop and show lounge.

Visit http://www.tourismregina.com/ for more attractions.
