While you are looking for a place to stay while visiting, the types of **Saskatoon Hotels **available to you will are sure to make you comfortable during your visit here. The "City of Bridges" has seen an increase in tourism over the last few decades, and with it has come an increase in places to rest while you are there.

#### High-end Places

#### Hilton Garden Inn Saskatoon

This is located right in the heart of the downtown area across the street from the Midtown Plaza Shopping Plaza and the Saskatoon Convention Centre. The four-star hotel is nice for any type of visit, but is especially great for business trips. The guestrooms are equipped with all the amenities that make it comfortable, including one king or two queen beds, cable TV with pay-per-view movies, and a refrigerator. High-speed internet access is also available, and the hotel is equipped with a first-class business center.

#### Country Inn and Suites by Carlson Saskatoon

This country-style inn has all the services and facilities you could want. There are regular guestrooms or suites available, a nice fitness center, and an on-site restaurant. The rooms are large and airy, with high-speed internet access and cable TV provided. The staff is professional and will make you feel right at home.

#### Mid-Range Accommodations

#### Delta Bessborough Hotel

This attractive hotel is situated on the banks of the South Saskatchewan River. The hotel has a large fitness center as well as a sauna and a massage treatment room. You have the choice of experiencing their fine evening cuisine in the on-site dinning room. It is a pleasant place for a romantic get-away or a family vacation.

#### Travelodge Hotel

This comfortable hotel is located near the airport and is in close proximity to the downtown area. All your needs are met with their many amenities such as free wireless internet, refrigerators, and microwaves, and they also welcome pets for a small fee. Their specialty is that they have 11 new themed rooms like "outer space" or "under the sea," to spark your imagination.

#### **Budget Hotels**

#### Senator Hotel

This hotel is located in the heart of the downtown area, just a few blocks west of the Saskatchewan River. This reasonably priced hotel has a nice European boutique style to it. Fine dining and great shopping are just around the corner. This is just the place to stay if you are on a budget.

#### Westgate Inn Motel

On the western skirts of downtown is where you will find this motel. This motel is just for you if you are looking to get more for your dollar. It is clean and the staff are professional who will make you feel comfortable. They have just a few amenities, but enough to take care of basic needs.
