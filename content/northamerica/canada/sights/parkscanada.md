Parks Canada is the federal government agency responsible for managing Canada's national park system. This agency has prepared a site containing comprehensive information on Canada's national park system, heritage and historical sites, and related maps and literature. A fascinating feature of this site is the provision of on-line narrated video tours of selected parks throughout Canada. To learn more about Canada's national park system, and to obtain information on specific national parks, visit Parks Canada 

If you have a story or information about any of Canada's parks, please let the world know about it - send us the information.
