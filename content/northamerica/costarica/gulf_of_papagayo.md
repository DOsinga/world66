title: "Gulf of Papagayo"
type: location
---

The **Golfo de Papagayo** is on the northern Pacific coast in Guanacaste province, near Liberia. The area has developed rapidly into a major resort zone, with several large all-inclusive hotels on private beaches. The dry season here is long (November through May) and the water is calm. It's a good base for boat trips, sport fishing, and diving excursions to the Bat Islands. The nearby town of [Liberia](/northamerica/costarica/liberia) has the closest international airport (LIR).
