Opposite the Hotel El Establo, Cafe Flor de Vida is one of the best options for vegetarians in the Monteverde area. The menu includes vegan dishes too — smoothie bowls, fresh salads, and grain-based plates alongside Costa Rican staples. A relaxed spot for a healthy lunch between forest walks.
