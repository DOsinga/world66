---
title: "Eating Out"
type: section
---

Puerto Viejo's food scene punches above its weight. Caribbean flavours dominate — rice and beans cooked in coconut milk, fresh fish, patacones, and jerk chicken — but you will also find Italian, Israeli, and fusion restaurants along the main road. The town's laid-back vibe extends to dining: many places are open-air, service is unhurried, and the best seats are the ones closest to the beach.
