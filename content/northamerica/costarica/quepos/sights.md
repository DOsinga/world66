Great little fishing town next to manuel antonio park. Downtown has some funky bars. El Banco and Gran Escape. Lots of local restaurants and bars downtown. Up the hill to Manuel antonio are the nicer hotels and restaurants. Mono-azul and Costa verde are 2 good places to stay. All the way at the end is the beach. Beautiful white sand beach with good swimming. The park entrance is to the left. Nude/gay beach is up to your right over some rocks .looks like you can't go any farther. A graet place that is b eing found by the mainstream tourists
