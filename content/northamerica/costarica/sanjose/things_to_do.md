---
title: Things to Do
type: section
---

San José rewards those who look beyond the chaos. The National Theatre is the city's architectural crown jewel — an extravagant 19th-century opera house. The Gold Museum beneath the Plaza de la Cultura houses the finest pre-Columbian gold collection in Central America. The National Museum, set in a former fortress, traces Costa Rican history from pre-Columbian times to the present. Wander the central markets for a street-level view of daily life.
