The Jade Museum holds the largest collection of American jade in the world. Beyond jade, the museum displays pre-Columbian ceramics, stone carvings, and gold miniatures. The collection spans cultures from across Costa Rica and neighbouring regions, offering context on the diverse peoples who inhabited Central America before European contact.
