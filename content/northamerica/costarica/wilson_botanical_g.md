---
title: "Wilson Botanical Gardens"
type: location
---

Founded in 1963 by Robert and Catherine Wilson, the Wilson Botanical Gardens is now part of the Las Cruces Biological Station, operated by the Organization for Tropical Studies (OTS). The gardens hold over 1,000 plant species, including one of the world's largest palm collections, and the surrounding forest is excellent for birding — over 300 species recorded. Well-maintained trails make it easy to explore. The gardens are 6 km south of [San Vito](/northamerica/costarica/san_vito).
