title: "Festivals in Sosua"
type: section
---

The main annual **Sosua festival** draws music lovers from all around to enjoy world-class jazz music every fall. While Sosua itself is a quiet fishing village only developed into a more tourist-drawing tropical resort town in the 1980s, it is also home to one of the most famous jazz festivals in the world.

#### Dominican Republic Jazz Festival

Every November, a variety of local and international musicians are invited to share three days of music, rhythm and fun with the locals and tourists residing on the tropical northern shores of the Dominican Republic. In past years, the festivities have passed between the three neighboring cities of Puerto Plata, Sosua and Cabarete, but more recently have been consolidated to the beaches of Cabarete, 38 km and 15km east of Puerto Plata and Sosua, respectively.

Kicking off with an evening show on Friday night, this world-renowned extravaganza is more than just a music festival, but also hosts workshops, master classes and a youth concert series to help pass the love of music to the younger generation. Saturday's festivities begin at 9am and continue well into the evening, while Sunday typically allows for rest and recovery during the day and finishes the festival off with an evening finale. Locals, families and tourists alike enjoy the sounds of the relaxing music and delight in the delicious Dominican cuisine during this fun-filled, admission-free festival.
