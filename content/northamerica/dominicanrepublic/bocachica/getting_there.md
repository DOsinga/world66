---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

Boca Chica is about 20 minutes by taxi from **Las Américas International Airport (SDQ)** and 30–40 minutes from central Santo Domingo on the Autovía del Este. Cheap *guagua* (shared minibus) services run frequently along the highway from Santo Domingo's Parque Enriquillo, and taxis are plentiful. Plenty of day-trippers come out for the beach and head back to the capital in the evening.
