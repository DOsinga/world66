A friendly, centrally located spot that works as a sandwich bar by day and a pub by night, open from 10:30am until late, seven days a week. The "famous" Caesar cocktail is a hit with Canadian visitors, but the crowd is international — Canadians, Americans, British, and Europeans all mix here. Happy hour runs 5-8pm daily, and the bar stocks premium liquors that are hard to find elsewhere in Sosua.
