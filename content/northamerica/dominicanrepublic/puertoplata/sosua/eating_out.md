---
title: "Eating Out"
type: section
---

Sosua has a surprising range of restaurants for a small beach town. The main strip of Pedro Clisante in El Batey has most of the options. Morua Mai, Gaudi, and Waterfront are the best for fine dining. For casual meals, Britannia, Tipico Alberto, PJ, and La Roca are reliable. Oh La Vache does good French-style pizza. Jolly Roger on Pedro Clisante serves Canadian-style pub food at $3-9 per dish. For Dominican food, try La Finca or Sosua Mofongo. Several restaurants on the waterfront at Piergiorgio's and the Sosua Bay Resort offer sea views with dinner.
