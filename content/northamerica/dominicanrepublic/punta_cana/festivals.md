There are a few **Punta Cana Festivals** to keep in mind when traveling to this area of the Dominican Republic. Each has its own different theme and most are held during the summer months when the majority of tourists are vacationing.

**Caribbean Festival** (Daily from April to December)

The most exciting festival in Punta Cana to take part in is the Caribbean Festival. More than just a mere festival, it's more like a daylong boat excursion and festival in one. Board the festival's catamaran and cruise through crystal blue waters where guests are allowed to swim with sharks and stingrays, plus do a bit of snorkeling. Then interact with a stingray under the boat crew's supervision, by feeding and holding them. You'll then board the boat once again to cruise along the Bavaro coastline to another location to snorkel some more and view many different types of beautiful tropical fish.
