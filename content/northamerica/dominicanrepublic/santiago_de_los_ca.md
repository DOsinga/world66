Travel Guide

Santiago de los Caballeros is the second major city of the Dominican Republic. Located in the north region of the island, it holds the largest Tobacco Plantations in the country and the most important agro-industrial companies.

Santo is a vibrant city full of entertainment, local attractions, golf courses and beaches nearby as well as great shopping malls and artistic presentations.
