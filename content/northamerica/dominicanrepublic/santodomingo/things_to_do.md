---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Beyond the colonial history, Santo Domingo has plenty to keep you busy. The **National Botanical Gardens** — over 200 acres of flowers, palms, and 300 types of orchids — are considered among the best in the Caribbean. The Japanese Garden inside is particularly popular. Open daily 9am to 6pm.

Scuba diving at **La Caleta** offers calm waters, coral reefs, tropical fish, and two shipwrecks to explore. Several dive centres offer PADI certification. Between December and April, the **Silver Bank Sanctuary** near Puerto Plata is where humpback whales come to breed — half-day and full-day boat tours are available.

Baseball is the national passion. Santo Domingo has training grounds that feed players into the US Major Leagues, and catching a game in season is a great way to spend an evening.
