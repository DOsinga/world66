Both the USA and Canada have comprehensive airline systems to not only cities and major towns but also to local and municipal Airports. Major USA airlines include United, American, Delta, NWA and Continental. Other airlines including Southwest and Jet Blue offers value for money flights. In Canada the main airlines are Air Canada and Westjet. Airfares vary a great deal, it is a good idea to compare airfares from different airlines to find the best airfare deal.
