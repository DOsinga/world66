---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment"
type: location
---

There are no cinemas in Greenland, but films are occasionally shown at the community center or library. All showings are generally advertised in the local paper of the relevant town and posted on the town notice boards. In most major towns, there are restaurants and "pubs". The price level of an average 2 to 3-course menu at a restaurant starts from approx. US $25.

 The cost of alcoholic beverages in restaurants and pubs is higher. E.g., a beer costs approx. US $6 and a bottle of wine upwards of about US $40. Prices include tips.  The pubs are open to people over 18 years of age only. In some places there is a discotheque, while in others there are live bands.
