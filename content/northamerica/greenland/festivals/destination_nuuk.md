In the beginning of may, there is going to be an Blues and Jazz Festival in the capital of Greenland, Nuuk. The festival is to be started the 4th of may until the 8th of may. The arrangement will be helt in the culture house, and maybe other places in town, and there will be musicians from Skandinavia and Greenland.
