---
title: "Getting There in Narsarsuaq"
type: section
---

Air Greenland flies once a week (twice in summer) from Copenhagen. Air Iceland (NOT Icelandair) flies frequently in summer from Reykjavik (NOT Keflavik). There are domestic flights four days a week from Nuuk. Boat transfers from Qaqortoq, Narsaq and other places in the area can be arranged locally.
