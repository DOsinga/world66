---
title: "Getting There in Nuuk"
type: section
---

Nuuk airport is too small for intercontinental flights, but there are flights a few times a week by Air Greenland from Keflavik and by Air Iceland (NOT Icelandair) from Reykjavik (NOT Keflavik). From Europe Nuuk is reached through the international airports at Kangerlussuaq and [Narsarsuaq](/northamerica/greenland/narsarsuaq) from where Air Greenland has domestic flights in connection with its flights from Copenhagen. There are no regular flights to Greenland from USA or Canada.
