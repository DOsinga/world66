title: Electricity
type: poi
---

Greenland runs on 220-volt power, the same standard as mainland Europe. Two-pin European plugs are standard. In smaller villages and at sheep-farming stations, however, you may encounter places with no electricity at all, or with power available only during certain hours of the day or night — bring a headlamp and don't rely on being able to charge devices.
