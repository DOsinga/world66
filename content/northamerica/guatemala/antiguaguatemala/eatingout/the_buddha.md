The Buddha in San Pedro la Laguna,
Guatemala is a fabulous all in one backpacker's oasis. Great Asian
food!! Cool, sleek and stylish bar with pool table, dart board &
lots of crazy fun drinks. The rooftop terrace has one of the finest
views I've ever seen... the bar sits upon a lake shore within a
volcanic caldera amidst several other volcanos!! The second floor
lounge is plush and comfortable and you can smoke fruity tobaccos out of
their Egyptian Sheesha/Nargila/Hookah water pipes while watching movies in Dolby surround sound on the big screen.
Awesome place!!
