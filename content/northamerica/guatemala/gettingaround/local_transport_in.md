Traveling around Guatemala by bus in these modern times is completely different from the chicken bus days that we have all heard about or experienced in the past. Today the buses are modern, with comfortable seats, and they run on schedule. Just about any point in the country can be reached the same day in reasonable comfort and at a reasonable price. Connections are easily made and most of the staff that run the buses are courteous to the traveller. Also, crossing the borders to neighbouring countries has been made easier and much more convenient.

There are several bus lines that you can find just by using your search engine. Full schedules and prices are given. These days the dollar is worth about 7.50 quetzales, which is the national currency as well as the national bird. Just a short time at the computer will allow you to plan a trip around this beautiful country.

Of course, tour companies in the main tourist centers offer shuttle services to and from the major tourist attractions in well-maintained, relatively new buses and mini-vans. Tour companies can plan your entire trip for you of course. Once again the internet will give you the information that you need.

Don't be afraid to use the local transport here in Guatemala. Sometimes it is the most memorable and enjoyable part of your vacation.
