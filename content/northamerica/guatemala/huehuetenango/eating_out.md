---
title: "Eating Out"
type: section
---

Huehue is a working Guatemalan town and its restaurants are straightforward — grilled meats, *comida típica*, *pepián* stews and *tamales* — rather than aimed at tourists. A handful of restaurants near the central Parque Central serve decent set lunches for a few dollars. For something more substantial, **Restaurante Las Brasas** and **Restaurante Le Kaf** are long-running local favourites. The central market, south of the plaza, is the best place for cheap breakfasts, fruit, and *antojitos* prepared on the spot.
