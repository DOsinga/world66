A non-profit weaving cooperative and museum in Santiago Atitlan, run by and for Tz'utujil Maya women who weave using traditional backstrap looms. The small museum explains the history and techniques of the textile tradition, and the shop sells high-quality woven pieces at fair prices that go directly to the weavers. One of the most worthwhile purchases you can make at the lake.
