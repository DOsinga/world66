Maximon is a syncretic Mayan-Catholic folk deity venerated in Santiago Atitlan. His image — a dressed figure of a man wearing stacked hats and holding a cigar — is housed in a different local's home each year and receives offerings of rum, cigarettes, and money. Finding him requires asking in the town (children will offer to lead you for a small tip). The experience is genuine and unlike anything else in the highlands; visitors are welcome but should behave respectfully.
