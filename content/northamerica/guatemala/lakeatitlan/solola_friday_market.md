The town of Solola, above Panajachel on the crater rim, holds a large Friday market that draws vendors from across the highlands. It is significantly more local than Chichicastenango and less visited by tourists. The sight of men in the distinctive Solola traje — striped shirts and cowboy-style aprons — makes it one of the most photographically striking markets in Guatemala. Minibuses run from Panajachel frequently.
