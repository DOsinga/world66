---
title: "When to Go"
type: section
---

The dry season from November to April is the best time to visit. Skies are reliably clear in the mornings, the lake is calm, and hiking conditions on the volcanoes are good. The rainy season from May to October brings heavy afternoon downpours that can make lanchas inadvisable and trails slippery. Mornings are usually clear even during the rainy season, and the vegetation is at its most intensely green. The lake is busiest around Christmas and Easter, when Guatemalan visitors arrive in numbers.
