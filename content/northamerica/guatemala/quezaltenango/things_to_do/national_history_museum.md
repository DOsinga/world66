Located in the Casa de Cultura de Occidente on the south side of the main park, this museum displays pottery from the Pacific lowland area, Pre-Columbian ceramics and jade, traditional indigenous costumes, and taxidermy of local fauna. Open Tuesday–Saturday, 9:00–12:00 and 14:00–17:00.
