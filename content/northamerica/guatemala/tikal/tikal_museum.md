A small museum near the park entrance housing artefacts excavated from the site, including pottery, jade ornaments, bone carvings, and shell work. The star exhibit is a reconstruction of the jade-laden burial of Jasaw Chan K'awiil I, found inside Temple I. Worth a visit after you have explored the ruins to put what you have seen in context. Separate admission fee required.
