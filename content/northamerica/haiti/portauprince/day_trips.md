**Petionville**

An excursion should be made into the hills behind Port-au-Prince to the suburb of Petionville (though it is also a good choice for a place to stay). High above the city it has some of Haiti’s loveliest hotels as well as a casino a 9-hole golf course and tennis courts. **Fort St Jacques**

Ft. Jacques Artisanat is a forge where handmade furniture and decorative objects are sold.  

**Kenscoff**

The Tuesday market in nearby Kenscoff is also worth visiting. From here you get great views of the harbor and city.
