Roatan has become a major tourist destination, therefore getting to the Island is very easy. From The United States, Taca International airlines flies non-stop from Houston on Saturdays and from Miami on Sundays. Continental Flies non-stop twice on saturday from Houston and there is also a flight on sunday. there will be another flight on wednesday starting june. Delta Air Lines flies non-stop from Atlanta on saturdays. There is talkings about American Airlines flying non-stop to the Island from Ft. Lauradale and Dallas later this year or early in 2007. There is two charters from Toronto Canada on Skyservice and Westjet and one from Milan, Italy on Blue Panorama.
