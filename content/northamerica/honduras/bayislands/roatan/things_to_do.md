---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Diving and snorkeling dominate the agenda on Roatan — the reef is right there, sometimes just a short swim from shore. But there is more to the island than the underwater world. You can visit the Garifuna community at Punta Gorda, hike through the jungle interior, zip-line above the canopy at Gumbalimba Park, or simply find a quiet stretch of beach and settle in. The Roatan Marine Park organizes reef cleanups and educational dives if you want to give something back.
