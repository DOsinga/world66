**Cathedral of Comayagua **

One of the most impressive in Central America. Its clock has been continuously ticking since the 1500s. 

**Archaeological Museum of Comayagua**

Documentation on excavations particularly on the El Cajon Archaeological Project 

**Museum of Colonial Religious Art **

Has good religious artifacts said to be more than 3 000 years old as well as gold and silver items from the 1500s-1700s
