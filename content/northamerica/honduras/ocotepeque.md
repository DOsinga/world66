Nueva Ocotepeque is a small town in the far west of Honduras, near the point where Honduras, Guatemala, and El Salvador meet. It is a border town and transit point rather than a destination in itself, but travelers crossing between the three countries often pass through here. The El Poy crossing into El Salvador is a few kilometres south; the Agua Caliente crossing into Guatemala is nearby.

The surrounding highlands are attractive and undervisited, with cooler temperatures than the lowlands and some good hiking terrain.
