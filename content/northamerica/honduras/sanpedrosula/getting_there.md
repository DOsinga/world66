---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

Ramon Villeda Morales International Airport (SAP) is the main gateway to northern Honduras. Several airlines serve the airport with connections through the US and Central America.

Overland, there are daily bus services from Guatemala City (Hedman Alas), San Salvador (King Quality), and Managua via San Jose (Ticabus). Domestic buses connect San Pedro Sula to Tegucigalpa, La Ceiba, and the north coast towns.
