Travel Guide

Lost in the magnificent Blue Mountains of Jamaica, Rafjam bed and
breakfast is a group of houses and apartments. Between two rivers, the
apartments are in the middle of nature. Protected from the breeze by the
hills and warmed up by the sun, the cove is a little paradise. Away
from the noise and the heat of Kingston, you will find quietness and
freedom here – at one of the few Blue Mountain resorts in Jamaica.
