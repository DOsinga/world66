Five-pointed Spanish star fort on a hill above the harbour — for two centuries the defender of the Manila Galleons against Dutch and English pirates, and now the **Museo Histórico de Acapulco**. Fifteen galleries cover the Nahua founding of the town, the trade with Manila, piracy, and Mexican independence. The rooftop gives a good view across the bay.
