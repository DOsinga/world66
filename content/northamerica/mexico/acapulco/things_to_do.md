---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Acapulco is a beach town, and the beach is why you come. Beyond the sand, the most iconic sight is **La Quebrada** — the 35-metre cliff off which local divers plunge five times a day into a narrow inlet, timing their jumps to the waves. The **Fort of San Diego**, a five-pointed Spanish star fort above the harbour, houses the city's history museum and is worth an hour. The **Zócalo** is the social heart of the old town, particularly in the evenings.
