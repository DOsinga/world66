title: "Family Travel Ideas in Akumal"
type: section
---

Families visiting the Akumal area have many accommodation choices. They range from all inclusive hotels to private villas and condominiums. Deciding on what it is you like to do and what your budget is will help you determine what type of lodging will be best suited for your vacation. If getting out on day trips and exploring the area is something that appeals to you, then go the route of a condominium or villa. This will enable you to make in house meals and pack a cooler with snacks and beverages for the day. If lounging on the beach, reading, taking it easy and having multiple food and beverage options with minimal effort involved is your style, then by all means, look at staying in an all inclusive hotel.

### [Condominium Rentals](/northamerica/mexico/akumal/family_travel_idea/condominium_rental)

For beachfront private condominiums and villas, visit Sol Maya Rentals. They have a large selection of vacation rentals and excellent customer service.
