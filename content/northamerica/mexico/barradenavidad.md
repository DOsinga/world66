Barra de Navidad is a small welcoming town about 4 hours north of Puerto Vallarta. It is a perfect vacation for travellers looking for a small town where you can enjoy the beach fishing fine drinks and good company with the locals. Piper Lovers blues bar is a great place to spend an evening there are several hotels that are reasonably priced. Spend a week there or only a few days its a stop on the west coast you wont want to miss.
