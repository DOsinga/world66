Los Arcos is the most recognizable landmark in Cabo San Lucas. It marks the southernmost tip of the Baja Peninsula as it fades away into the beautiful Sea of Cortez.

Take a boat out from the harbor in order to get a close up view of Los Arcos. There is a hidden beach here, too, called Lovers' Beach, because it is only accessible from the water.

Whales abound in this area, and when the season is right, you'll see many of them all around your vessel. This is a popular spot for sportfishing, too.
