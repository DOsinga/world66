Cabo San Lucas is a beach resort in Los Cabos in Mexico and is famous for its **Cabo San Lucas day trips**. Cabo San Lucas is also famous for its beach resorts and white sandy beaches, with the best scuba diving locations in the world and the distinctive sea arch. Given below are some Cabo San Lucas day trips.

**Todos Santos**

One of the most popular excursions is the day trip from Cabo San Lucas to Todos Santos. Situated just an hour away from Cabo San Lucas, this small Mexican town is a perfect place to relax and rejuvenate. The drive to Todos Santos takes you through the beautiful pacific coast and the rugged mountains. The tranquil town has some great shopping options and amazing restaurants. The very famous Hotel California is one of the hottest tourist attractions in Todos Santos. You can also enjoy surfing in the beaches of Todos Santos. Ensure that you plan your trip well and avoid driving at night, as it is not advisable to drive around after dark in Mexico.

**La Paz**

Situated on the Sea of Cortez, La Paz is at a distance of close to 100 miles from Cabo San Lucas. On your trip to La Paz, you can visit some ancient heritage sites, churches and buildings. Apart from sightseeing, you can also enjoy your day just strolling down the roads of La Paz and sampling the local delicacies. There are some great places to shop too at La Paz.
