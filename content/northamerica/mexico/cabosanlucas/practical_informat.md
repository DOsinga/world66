Located at the far south end of sunny Baja Mexico, **Cabo San Lucas Practical Information** makes your trip to this tourist hot spot easier to navigate and enjoy.

The city is broken down into three distinct districts. They include:

- San Jose Del Cabo- This is the old part of town located nearest to the airport. You'll find historical gems and shiny new resorts here.
- Cabo San Lucas- This is the new part of town and is the tourist hot spot you're likely looking for here.
- The Corridor- This is the 20 mile stretch that connects the two parts of town. You'll find beautiful golf courses and resorts here.

**Currency & Language**

English is widely spoken as is Spanish. The US Dollar is widely accepted as well.**
**

**Getting Here**

All major airlines have service to this party town. Flying is the preferred way to make it into Cabo San Lucas. Once in, you'll find a parade of taxis ready to take you anywhere you want to go. **
**

**See and Do **

The beaches and nightlife are the main draw here. Aside from the traditional draws of sand, surf and bars at night, you can also find many things to do involving nature and local landmarks such as the Land's End area. Many rock formations are seen here presenting breathtaking photo opportunities. Come during low tide and you can walk along Lover's Beach to Poseidon's Arch, a formation carved out of rock by wind and sea. You can also see two rock islands where sea lions like to sun themselves. The city also has a beautiful botanical garden at the Cultural Center boasting sweeping views of all of Cabo.
