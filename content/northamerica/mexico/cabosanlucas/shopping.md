title: "Shopping in Cabo san lucas"
type: section
---

**Cabo San Lucas Shopping** features a wide variety of stores including malls, market places, boutiques, galleries, and souvenir and gift shops. Cabo San Lucas is best known for its beautiful beaches and pampering resorts, but it also offers some spectacular shopping opportunities too. You will find many areas that sell handcrafted goods, but there are also stores that offer imported and well known American goods for tourists who'd rather stick with what they know best. Listed are some of the more popular places to shop while vacationing in Cabo San Lucas.

**Costa Real Cabo Resort Shopping Mall **

The Costa Real Cabo Resort has a superb shopping mall of 140 stores and shops located within the resort itself. And thankfully, you don't have to be a paying guest at the resort to visit the shopping mall located on the resort's waterfront marina. Roam from shop to shop, or sit right by the water and watch as other tourists, fishermen and yachts come and go.

**Sol Dorado**

The Sol Dorado gallery carries a varied assortment of fine arts, crafts, blown glass, sculptures, handicrafts, souvenirs, woodworking, paintings and so many other authentic items. It is one of the best places to find the unique pieces created by Mexico's best artists.

**Cabo San Lucas Official Store**

The Official Cabo San Lucas Store can be found in Plaza del Sol and is a fun boutique featuring the latest in beachwear and summer wear items. The store is considered quite trendy and will draw the attention of more young adults and teens. Then again, if you're young at heart and like the trends, this store could be for you too.

**Puerto Paraiso Entertainment Plaza**

The Puerto Paraiso Entertainment Plaza is an exquisite mall that caters to the more upscale shopper with a sophisticated style and taste. It is the finest shopping mall ever built in all of the southern Baja California area complete with indoor waterfalls, finer restaurants and nicer apparel and gift items. It&rsquo;s a larger mall with a good deal of open space to walk through and browse each of the lovely shops and vendors.

**Plaza del Mar**

The Plaza Del Mar is located in the heart of Cabo San Lucas next to El Squid Roe, a lively night club and restaurant. Plaza del Mar features a fascinating variety of stores and shops that sell everything from surf gear, summer wear, bags, hats, sandals, sunglasses, all the necessary beach accessories, plus some nice little bars and restaurants to grab a meal or a couple of drinks.
