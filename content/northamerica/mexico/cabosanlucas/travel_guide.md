Cabo San Lucas, Mexico, better known as "Cabo" to its regular visitors, is a world renowned vacation destination for people of all ages, as it has sunshine year-round, and it offers some of the nicest beaches in all of Mexico. Use this **Cabo San Lucas travel guide** and plan the perfect getaway for your next vacation.

**Where to Stay**

The best new hotel in Cabo San Lucas is the Welk Resort's Sirena del Mar. This gem sits high on a rocky bluff looking down on the beautiful Sea of Cortez. What makes this new accommodation so attractive are the low costs, clean, elegant rooms and villas (approximately 40 in all), and no matter what part of the resort you stay in, you have incredible views. You can also opt for higher end luxury condos or villas. If you're planning to bring a large party to Cabo for vacation, or perhaps for a wedding, this would be your best type of accommodation. Last, but certainly not least, is the Rancho Pescadero Hotel located about an hour's drive north of Cabo San Lucas. It's definitely a more secluded accommodation for those truly looking to get away from it all.

**Things to Do**

Cabo is not only a great place to get away from it all at the beach, it also offers an abundance of activities and things you can do. If you love the water, go hang at the beach and bask in the sun, or learn to surf or scuba dive. There are instructional areas at most beaches for both activities. Or if you're looking to go on an adventure in Cabo, visit Green Zebra Adventures and go 4x4ing Mexican style over rocks, steep trails and unruly off-road terrains. You can also go on dolphin encounters tours, fly through the air via secure zip lines and much more.

**Dining
**
For the best food, prices and the real-deal in a Cabo experience, dine at the Cabo Cantina for their tasty Mexican dishes and seafood. They also offer a sports bar equipped with big-screen Plasma TV's and live bands. Down by the marina, you'll find a variety of restaurants to choose from ranging from casual dining to gourmet cuisine. Some of the well known dining establishments are the Tiki Bar, Baja Cantina and Alexander's. Downtown you'll also find the Brazil Steakhouse, Mi Casa, Salvatore's and Gardenia's.
