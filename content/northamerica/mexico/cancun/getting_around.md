---

Cancun is one of the most important and beautiful travel spots in Mexico. It
is visited by millions of tourist each year and most importantly, during summer
months.

**Getting There**

Getting to Cancun is pretty easy as most major airlines flight directly into
Cancun’s International Airport. Some of the airlines traveling to
Cancun are United Airlines, Iberia, Mexicana, Air
Canada, American Airlines, Air France and US Airways. Cancun’s International
Airport handles the second largest volume of passengers in Mexico, just after
Mexico City’s International Airport. It is a pretty modern and safe airport.

** Getting Around**

After you check-in in your hotel, it will be time to go out and start
touring the city. Using the bus system in Cancun is the cheapest and best way to
get around and you can practically reach every beach or sight from the Hotel
Zone to Cancun’s You can go downtown, Puerto Juarez or to the famous Plaza Las Americas for
just about $7 pesos. You can also travel by bus to other cities within Mexico
such as Tulum or Méridia. If you want to spend a day or 2 in the
nearby Isla Mujeres, you can take a ferry for around $4.00 USD and the travel
time varies from 15 to 30 minutes. Ferries offer services everyday from 6:00 a.m.
to 8:00 p.m. and leave from Puerto Juarez.
