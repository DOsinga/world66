---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment"
type: section
---

Cancun is famous for its nightclubs. It is possible to find large or small places with different kinds of music. Mostly in the hotel zone, the nightclubs have the service of open bar arround 45 USD. Nightclubs in Cancun have shows, dj´s, bikini contest, wet t shirt contest and foam and water party.
