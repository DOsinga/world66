---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Water sports dominate: snorkeling, diving, parasailing, and deep-sea fishing are all easily arranged from the beach. The Archaeological Museum near the Convention Center has over 800 Mayan artifacts from Tulum, El Rey, and Cobá — worth an hour before or after a day trip. The underwater sculpture park MUSA (Museo Subacuático de Arte), accessible by glass-bottom boat or diving, is genuinely unusual. For cultural context, the Museo de Arte Popular Mexicano in the Hotel Zone covers Mexican folk art and is free.
