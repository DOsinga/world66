Located near the Convention Center in the Hotel Zone, this museum houses over 800 artifacts from Mayan sites including Tulum, El Rey, and Cobá. The collection includes ceramics, sculptures, and burial objects from the pre-Columbian era. A good place to get context before heading out to the ruins. Open daily 9am–8pm.
