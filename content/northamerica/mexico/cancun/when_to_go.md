---
title: "When to Go"
type: section
---

December through April is the dry season and the most popular time to visit — cooler temperatures, little rain, and calm seas good for diving. Christmas and Easter weeks are peak season; book well in advance and expect high prices. May through November is wetter and hotter, with the best beach weather in July and August despite some afternoon showers. Hurricane season runs June through October, with September the most active month. Spring break (typically March) brings a younger crowd and significantly more noise.
