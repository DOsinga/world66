---
title: "Day Trips in Chichen Itza"
type: section
---

Chichen Itza is in the Yucatan peninsula of Mexico where there is so
much to see and do. While in the area it is a great idea to take a few
day trips outside the city to see more ancient ruins or enjoy the water
and sun.

**Balankanche**

Chichen Itza is the premier site for ancient ruins but there are
others outside of the city. Balankanche or the throne of the Jaguar
Priest is nearby. It was discovered in a limestone cave in 1959 and is
in a labyrinth of underground caverns and rivers. There is artificial
lighting so you can see the clay dishes, jars and other objects that
strew the floor just where they were left. It is thought that this is a
Toltec burial site. There is a chamber under the altar with a limestone
formation resembling the sacred ceiba tree. There is another alter
further in that is surrounded by water and dedicated to Tlaloc a Toltec
rain god. There is a fee to get in of 22.00 and it is open 9:00am to
7:00pm everyday.

**Rio Lagartos**

Rio Lagartos is about 32 miles away and is a small sea side town.
Nearby there is an estuary where pink and red flamingos go to breed and
there are other interesting birds as well.

**Puerto Morelos**

If you are an experienced diver or can snorkel take a 30 minute trip
to Puerto Morelos which is near the Palancar Reef. Wet Set Dive will
help you to organize a dive or snorkel trip to the reef. While you are
there make sure to go to the many restaurants and small shops in the
area. Puerto Morelos is a charming town with a lot to offer.

**Xcaret**

Xcaret is an ocean themed park that will give you and your family
fodder to talk about for years. Price to get in is $45 USD and it is
open from about 11:00 am to 7:30 pm every day. You have to be 9 years
old or older to participate. In the Sea Trek feature visitors don
helmets that allow them to dive in among the fish and other aquatic
life. There is also the opportunity to go on a submarine to see the sea
life. Not everything is underwater at Xcaret. There is a butterfly
pavilion where butterflies will fly all about. You can also participate
in a bat cave, watch marine turtles, and much more.
This should not be missed.

While you are in the area of Chichen Itza be sure to go to other sites around the area for a great time .
