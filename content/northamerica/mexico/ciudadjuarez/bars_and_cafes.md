**Ciudad Juarez bars and cafes** rank among the most fun and lively in all of Mexico. This brief guide gives some general information on a pick of the bunch.

**Eat, Drink and be Merry at Salon Mexico**

Located directly opposite the street from Rio Grande Mall, you'll find Salon Mexico on Aveniuda 16 de Septembre. The beers are cheap (around the $2 mark), and large groups of older clients tend to turn up on Sundays at 3 pm with lots of food to share. This will really give you a taste of the real Mexico.

**Dance the Night Away**

One of the best Ciudad Juarez bars and cafes, the Salon Mexico has a large dance floor for you to boogey on and first-rate entertainment at the weekends. A professional, standard 10-piece band plays a whole plethora of Mexican music, and dance lessons can be taken on Wednesday nights if you're not feeling too confident about your moves.

**Famous Aspects**

The Salon Mexico has several claims to fame, the first being that some extremely well-loved Spanish language movies were filmed on its premises. The 1989 painting that graces its foyer depicts the owner Senor Baca strolling across the street with Maria Bonita, owner of the renowned Maria Bonita restaurant. The street in the painting is directly in front of the Salon Mexico.

**Safe, Clean and Happy**

Compared to many Ciudad Juarez bars and cafes, the Salon Mexico prides itself in providing a safe, clean and comfortable atmosphere where drugs are not tolerated and troublemakers are not welcome.
