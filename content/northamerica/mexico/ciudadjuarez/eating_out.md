**Ciudad Juarez Restaurants** take charge of your sumptuous meals in Ciudad Juarez. These restaurants in the city are great for business deals, family gatherings, and other occasions because they serve the most authentic dish starting from the international cuisines down to the local ones that will satisfy you.

**A Perfect Ambience for a Perfect Food**

Shangrila is a world famous Cantonese restaurant in Ciudad Juarez. It provides a great food in a soothing ambience. When you get inside the restaurant, you will be astounded of its elegant style and decoration. It has been in the food industry business for over four decades providing outstanding services and first-class dishes. Among the food that it serves are the superb pork ribs, distinctive almond Duck, and delectable shrimp with lobster dressing. Shangrila is the most frequent visited restaurant in the city of Juarez. Everybody comes to savor the taste of their dishes and to experience the elegant atmosphere that they offer.

**Best Japanese Restaurant in Town**

Taika Sushi Bar is a Japanese restaurant located in Radisson Casa Grande hotel. If you want to have a taste of Japanese food, do not go any further than this restaurant. Taika Sushi Bar offers the most palatable Japanese food in Ciudad Juarez. You should not miss to try their Sashimi, Tamaki, and Tempura dishes. Aside from the great Japanese food that they offer, they also have excellent service that satisfies the costumer. Taika Sushi Bar has also a great atmosphere. The interior design of the restaurant becomes attractive because of its decorations such as attractive paper lamps and bamboo plants that are pleasing to the eyes.
