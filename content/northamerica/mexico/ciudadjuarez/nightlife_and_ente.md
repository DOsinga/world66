The **Ciudad Juarez nightlife** has been a popular part of North American culture, both in the United States and Mexico, since the strict prohibition laws of the 1920s. When alcohol was outlawed in the US during the 1920’s it still flowed passionately in the border town of Ciudad Juarez. This is how Ciudad Juarez, formerly known as Juarez, got its name for being one of the biggest party cities in Mexico. There is now a vibrant social scene in Ciudad Juarez, primarily stretching across a particular area of town known as Avenida Juarez. There plenty of clubs in beautiful Ciudad Juarez, but there are a few clubs in particular that are favorites among the Ciudad Juarez partiers.

**Don Quinton**

Don Quinton is the perfect introduction to Ciudad Juarez nightlife. Here you will find traditional Mexican as well as international cocktails. There is live music every night at Don Quinton, and if the mood strikes, it isn’t uncommon for partiers to join the band on stage. If you want a fun place to party, dance and possibly sing the night away, then Don Quinton is the club for you.

**La Sereta**

If you are into raves or electronic music, La Sereta can fit the bill. This popular night club features fantastic state-of-the-art light shows as well as a bevy of talented DJs. The club has a reputation for getting some of the most popular DJs in the world to spin among its flashing lights and wildly dancing guests. If trance, techno, and house are your scene, La Sereta is the place to be.

**Four Bar**

Providing all of the usual frills associated with a night out, including colorful lights, dancing and liquor, is just a part of what Four Bar does for its fun-loving guests. The bar offers a fantastic house band that plays requests all night long. The music tends to stay along the lines of popular and disco hits known throughout Mexico and the US.

**La Mulata**

La Mulata is a star in the Ciudad Juarez nightlife scene. This is one of the most laid-back bars in Ciudad Juarez, but also the most popular. The atmosphere is casual and relaxed, with a steady stream of casual soft lounge music to keep guests entertained. This is a trendy cocktail bar, the kind of place where people go to be seen.

Regardless of what type of music you like, or what your agenda for nightlife is all about, Ciudad Juarez has a nightclub to meet your needs. Next time you are in the Rio Grande area, visit Ciudad Juarez for a night you will never forget.
