---
title: "Sights"
type: section
---

The Museum of History in the former Customs House on Avenida 16 de Septiembre is free and explains the city's outsized role in the Mexican Revolution. The Mission de Guadalupe, next to the Cathedral on the Plaza Principal, has original paintings and altars that date to the early colonial period. The Plaza itself is an active public space with locals gathering throughout the day. The Mercado Tipico (city market) a few blocks up is an old two-storey building of handicrafts and art; several good restaurants with strolling musicians occupy the forecourt.
