title: "Things to do in Ciudad juarez"
type: section
---

There are many **Ciudad Juarez things to do** as the city is
thriving and is one of the fastest growing in Mexico. There is a
plethora of museums, historical buildings and more to see and explore.
If you are traveling through one of the four ports from the United
States to Mexico, then spend a day or two and see some of the main
attractions.
**Learn in Museums
**

A very interesting museum is the Museo Anahuacalli. This museum was
designed by Diego Rivera and is made of volcanic stone. It houses all of
his pre-Columbian art. You can also visit the museum focusing on his
wife, Frida Kahlo. The Frida Kahlo museum is dedicated to this famous
woman artist and contains much of her work. The Museo Dolores Olmedo
Patino contains 13 galleries that are home to many paintings done by
Mexican artists.

A museum that should not be missed is the National Museum of
Anthropology as this contains one of the most comprehensive collections
of natural history found in the world. The exhibits are interactive and
give a great picture of the history and culture of Mexico. You can
easily spend the entire day viewing the 13 exhibit halls.
**Visit the Cathedral
**

The Cathedral of the Virgin of Guadalupe is a beautiful building that
was originally constructed in 1945 but was ruined in a fire and rebuilt
in 1975. It is dedicated to the Virgin of Guadalupe and the interior
contains a beautiful stained glass window that depicts the Virgin and
what is known as the miracle of the roses. On a particularly hot day,
step into the Cathedral to cool down and enjoy some quiet meditation.
**Attend a Bullfight
**

You can see a real bullfight in the city near the Plaza del Toro or
the Plaza Monumental. This is quite exciting and not very expensive. It
is everything you have seen in the movies with the graceful and athletic
bullfighters and the large stampeding bull going for the red cap. It is
definitely a great way to spend the afternoon.
