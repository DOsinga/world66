The city of Ciudad Juarez is right on the Rio Grande River across
from El Paso, Texas and there are many **Ciudad Juarez Must Do's**
that you should not miss. There are several different ports of entry to
the United States and Mexico in the city and this means many people
travel to the city. It is considered a city of the future and you should
stop in on your travels and see some of the top attractions.
