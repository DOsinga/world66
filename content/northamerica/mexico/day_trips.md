**ISLA HOLBOX & WHALE SHARK WATCHING**

From Cancun/Riviera Maya to Isla Holbox it takes you only three hours. First stop Chiquila' from where you will take a boat and cross over to Holbox Island.

The life there seems to stop. Only wild beaches, birds, coconuts and street made of sand. Recently called "La Isla del Tiburon Ballena" (The Whale Shark Island) worldwide, it's the perfect spot for a two day trips. Following some additional information that will make your stay easier to organise:
