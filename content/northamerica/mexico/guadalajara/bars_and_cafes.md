---
title: "Bars and Cafes in Guadalajara"
type: section
---

Guadalajara is a fine place to visit and enjoying your
evenings or nights at some of the **Guadalajara
Bars and Cafes **is highly recommended. There are several popular bars and
cafes where you can relax and take in the evening over a few drinks and some
fantastic live music. Here are a few places that are worth stepping into.

**Wall Street**

Wall Street is located in the northwest of Guadalajara. This
is the financial center of Guadalajara and Wall Street lets that feel resonate
in its atmosphere. The bar is priced a bit high at $180 on average per person
but the service and entertainment is worth every dollar spent. If you like
Brazilian music, visit the bar during the week. The music will make you want to
dance. The bar is open Monday to Saturday 1:30 p.m. to 3:00 a.m., Sundays 6:00
p.m. to 3:00 a.m.

**Lobby Lounge**

Lobby Lounge is another one of the popular bars in
Guadalajara. Located inside the Clarum 101, Lobby Lounge is a comfortable and
ultra modern bar. If you are looking for a nice place to relax and read a book
over a drink, Lobby Lounge is just the place for you. This is a full range bar
that also serves coffee for those who want to just sit back and relax.

**Tamborazo de Don Juan**

Tamborazo de Don Juan is a popular hot spot in Guadalajara.
If you are looking for a fun filled place to spend the evening enjoying your
drink, this is the place to be. There are live bands that perform here on a
regular basis, so music is never a problem. The bar is open Tuesday to Sunday
9:00 p.m. to 3:00 a.m. Put on your dancing shoes and enjoy the night at this
great place.

**Anime Bar**

If you are looking for something out of the ordinary while
visiting Guadalajara, stop by at the Anime bar. The low lighting and soft music
make it a perfect spot for a romantic evening. The lit up bottles on the
shelves will give you a feeling of being in a special place. The bar features
several popular DJs and is known as one of the most popular spots in town.

**Bar Americas**

Bar Americas is another popular hangout in Guadalajara. The
bar is located in Plaza Americas and is the place where you can let yourself
move to beat of the music. The bar includes live music from local bands and
also DJ’s from other countries. Step into the bar and enjoy yourself.
