**Guadalajara Budget Travel** is possible for all visitors to
Mexico as long as they follow a couple of simple pieces of advice. Here
are some budget travel ideas to allow you to save money during your
visit to Guadalajara.

**Getting around Guadalajara**

Public
transportation in Guadalajara is quite inexpensive with bus fares
ranging between $1.50 to $2.00 per person. Getting around with the bus
in Guadalajara requires you to get acquainted with a couple of the city
landmarks because locals don't identify the buses by numbers but rather by the landmarks from where they depart or pass. It does take a
bit of getting used to this system but just ask the locals for help. Another option is to contact the kiosks from
the Secretary of Tourism of Mexico that are around the historic
district.

Taxis are another inexpensive option to get around
Guadalajara. Just be sure to follow a couple rules of thumb. First,
make sure that the taxi that you choose is equipped with a taxi meter. If there is no meter you will have to negotiate a rate with the driver. Second, make
sure that your driver resets the meter and starts it after you tell him
your destination. Third, be aware that some taxi drivers may not use a
taxi meter if you jump on a cab during the early hours of the morning
after a long night of partying. Be ready to negotiate a rate based on
some suggestions from locals.

**Saving Money with Hostels**

Having a Hostelling International membership comes really handy in Guadalajara because it will give you access to 3 hostels: the Hospedarte Hostel (Efraín González Luna 2075 in the Colonia Americana, reservations at +52-33-3615-4957), the Hostel de Maria (Nueva Galicia No. 924 in the Colonia Centro Histórico, reservations at +52-33-36-1462-30) and the Hostel Guadalajara (Calle Maestranza
No. 147, reservations at +52-333-5627520). These 3 hostels are withing
walking distance to each other, which is great if you're traveling to
Guadalajara with a large party and everybody cannot be accommodated in
the same hotel.

Prices range between $12 to $15 depending on the room type but the good news is that breakfast is included in all room types.

**Other Discounts with Hostelling International**If you're a member of the Hostelling
International network, then you will also receive discounts at several
bars, restaurants, museums, movie theaters and some retail shops around
Guadalajara. Make sure to ask at any of the 3 hostels for the
latest version of the Hostelling International booklet that lists all the places where your card will provide savings.
