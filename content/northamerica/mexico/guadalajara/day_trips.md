The countryside around Guadalajara offers some of Mexico's best day trips. The town of [Tequila](/northamerica/mexico/tequila) is about 90 minutes west, where you can tour distilleries and walk the agave fields. [Ajijic](/northamerica/mexico/ajijic) sits on the shore of Lake Chapala, Mexico's largest lake, and makes for a relaxing day of wandering its art galleries and lakeside restaurants.
