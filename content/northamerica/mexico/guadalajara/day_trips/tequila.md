The town of [Tequila](/northamerica/mexico/tequila) is about 90 minutes from Guadalajara, and is famed for the alcoholic beverage of the same name. You can take a second class bus to Tequila from Guadalajara's old bus station (near Parque Aqua Azul) for MXN50 one-way. Once there, walk to the main square, where you can decide on which of the many distilleries to visit. There is also a church on the square, and a museum to visit.
