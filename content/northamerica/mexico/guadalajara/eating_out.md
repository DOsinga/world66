---
title: "Eating Out in Guadalajara"
type: section
---

There are several **Guadalajara restaurants **where
you can enjoy some fine cuisine while visiting this diverse city. The
flavors of Guadalajara are distinct in this city proud of its cultural
heritage. There is a wide variety of Mexican and international
restaurants to choose from with something for all budgets. The following
are a few places to enjoy the first class cuisine of Guadalajara.

**Piaf**

Piaf is a fine restaurant that offers simple but delicious French
cuisine. The atmosphere is somewhat romantic with a live string quartet
in the evenings. Service is friendly and the food satisfies a need for
something different. If you are looking to enjoy a romantic dinner with
your partner, this restaurant will be perfect. Piaf is closed on Sunday.
Most dishes run from $16 to 30.

**Las Margaritas**

Las Margaritas is another popular restaurant that offers a wide range
of tasty vegetarian dishes. This restaurant is a great way for non-meat
eaters to enjoy local fare. Prices range from $6 to $10. The restaurant
is open from 8 a.m. until 9 p.m.

**Café Madoka**

The scene starts here early in the morning and it’s a great place to
come for the excellent food and the original atmosphere. Well known for
the men who spend hours playing dominoes, the Café Madoka is considered a
‘Guadalajaran Institute’ as it is one of the oldest in the city, a
place where journalists used to gather--and it is still considered a cross-road
for meetings of the minds. If you are looking for a place where you can
interact with the locals, Café Madoka is the right place.

**La Cathedral del Antojito**

Located in an impressive Colonial-style house above a bridal shop, this eclectic establishment serves tacos and tortillas. If you are
looking for a place to sit back and enjoy some good food, La Cathedral
is the place. If you are on a tight budget, this restaurant is an
excellent place to get a good meal for under $2.

**La Playita**

La Playita is a popular eat-out for both tourists and locals alike.
La Playita serves up very good food at very reasonable prices. Famous for tortas de lomo doble carne con aguacate (the pork and avocado
special), this is one place not to be missed on the culinary tour! This
is one restaurant you would not want to miss while holidaying in
Guadalajara. All dishes are under $7.
