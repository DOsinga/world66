Here is a brief **Guadalajara family travel** guide that highlights some popular places which you can visit with your family.

**Guadalajara**** Zoo**

This is one of the best destinations in the city for the entire family. Situated on the slope of Huentitan, the expansive Guadalajara Zoo is home to about 360 species of animals. Though there are buses that go to the zoo, it is best to take a cab if you want to avoid confusion. The zoo also has a remarkable reptile section, an area to observe nocturnal species, a petting zoo, 2 aviaries, a roofed auditorium that can seat 3,500 people and an interactive museum. Visitors can also find soda fountains, cafés, souvenir shops and stroller rentals here.

**Catedral Metropolitana

**Catedral Metropolitana is another very popular tourist attraction. Although construction of this beautiful building started in 1561, it was only consecrated in 1616. It has undergone many changes since then. Today, it reflects a Moorish, baroque Gothic neo-classical architecture. There are several altars and each has a story behind it. Around the place there are many galleries, exhibits, stores and restaurants that tourists can walk besides.

**Museo de Cera

**The Museo de Cera, or Wax Museum, as it is also known is another ideal place for the entire family to visit. The museum is located in the Plaza de la Liberación and was opened in 1994. Today it houses more than 100 wax models of different well known personalities. These include famous Mexican figures, artists, politicians and Hollywood celebrities. Tickets are reasonably priced and the wax models are quite realistic.

**Tobolandia

**Families looking for something more fun and exciting to do should visit the Tobolandia Water Park. It is located near one of the country’s most important and biggest lakes, Lago de Chapala. This bathing resort has a nice artificial river, big slides and heated pools that will keep children entertained all day. It also has areas to sunbathe for people who just want to relax and take advantage of the sunny weather. There are eateries where people can replenish and refresh themselves.
