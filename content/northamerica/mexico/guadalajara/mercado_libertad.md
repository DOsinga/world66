title: Mercado Libertad (San Juan de Dios)
type: poi

The largest traditional indoor market in Latin America, spread over three floors. The ground level is food -- stalls serving birria, pozole, and fresh juices. Upper levels have leather goods, huaraches, pottery, and souvenirs at prices well below what you will pay in tourist shops. It is loud, crowded, and essential. Go hungry.
