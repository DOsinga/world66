An interactive children's museum with hands-on science and technology exhibits spread across several themed areas. Good for kids roughly 6 to 14, though younger children will find things to do. Open Tuesday to Friday 9:00--18:00, weekends 11:00--19:00. Adults 40 pesos, children 25 pesos, with group discounts available.
