An interactive children's museum in Zapopan, northwest of the city centre, with hands-on science and technology exhibits spread across several themed areas. Good for kids roughly 6 to 14, though younger children will find things to do. Open Tuesday to Friday 9:00--18:00, weekends 11:00--19:00.
