**Social Scene: Bars and Cafes**

**Guaymas bars and cafes **are a great way for visitors to connect with the local social scene. The coastal city of Guayamas is located in the southwest part of Mexico.

**Seafood Cafes**

One of the most wonderful things of Guayamas is its proximity to the ocean. As the cruise industry is an important part of the city&rsquo;s economy, many business owners have set up locations on the marina. Seafood is a daily part of the menu at local eateries. For instance, Club Nautico is new caf&eacute; that opened in the marina of the city and is getting praise for the quality of the seafood. There is live music in the caf&eacute;, making it an attractive place for visitors to come and relax.

**Beach Bars**

Guayamas has a number of bars in the area too, along the coast line that most of the tourists prefer. For example the La Cobacha, a sports bar along the marina, comes highly recommended for its lively atmosphere. Patrons to the bar can enjoy the local soccer game at the bar. The food is highly rated. Dishes include grilled vegetables, and the cooks make use of the proximity to the ocean, as shrimp is a big feature on the menu. The hotels are also a great place for people to find bars or cafes. Guaymas has some first rate hotels that incorporate some elegant bars. For people looking for an even evening with a touch of elegance, consider one of the resorts.
