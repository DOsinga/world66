---
title: "Bars and Cafes"
type: section
---

Guaymas has a modest bar scene centred on the marina and waterfront. Club Nautico at the marina is a well-regarded café with live music and fresh seafood. La Cobacha, a sports bar along the marina strip, is popular with locals for its relaxed atmosphere and solid food — shrimp features prominently. The resort hotels in San Carlos offer more polished bar options for those who want something quieter.
