---
title: "Beaches in Guaymas"
type: section
---

**Guaymas Beaches** are both very beautiful and also very
budget-friendly. This is a combination which is not often found. Guaymas
is not an incredibly popular tourist destination, although it has a lot
of visitors from the U.S due to its proximity. There is an element of
adventure that makes the trip to the beaches of Guaymas that much more
fun.
**Miramar

**

Miramar is one of the most beautiful beaches to walk on. It is
located at Bahai de Bacochibampo. If you come in the first few months of
the year, maybe you can time it with the Carnival. You could also see
the whales coming to mate in the Sea of Cortez. If you are a
photographer and/or a whale lover, you are going to really enjoy this.
Also, Guaymas is the place of the only pearl farm in both North and
South America. This is located in Bacochibampo Bay. Thus, a walk on a
beautiful beach also has some great opportunities to go with it.
**Natural Harbor of Guaymas

**

Guaymas has a natural harbor about which a lot has been written. The
combination of the mountains surrounding the bay and the glass clear
waters of the Sea of Cortez give an exhilarating feeling. This is a
great place to boat or go diving. In fact, many regulars just park their
boats here and come for their beach vacation. The opportunity of taking
a photograph at sunset in the harbor from Malcone should not be missed.
**Sea of Cortez

**

The Sea of Cortez has a very diverse aquatic life and is known as the
aquarium of the world. An opportunity to really see a diverse amount of
species awaits you here. The Sea of Cortez is known as the Gulf of
California in the U.S. It is also known by the name of Vermillion Sea.
If you are a scuba diver, this place will make you feel like a kid in a
candy store. Of course, you absolutely have to have an underwater
camera. You will see an incredible variety of fish, from hammerhead
sharks to sea lions and mantas. The temperatures are also perfect,
ranging between 60 degrees in the winter and 80 degrees in the summer.
August is known to be a very good time to scuba dive and vacation here.
