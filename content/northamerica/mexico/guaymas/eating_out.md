**Los Barcos - The Best Seafood Restaurant in Guaymas
**

While vacationing in the Mexican city of Guaymas you'll find that the majority of **Guaymas restaurants** offer seafood. This is due to the city's proximity to the Sea of Cortez. Many locals make their living as fishermen, so the town has some of the best seafood around. Los Barcos, a restaurant in downtown Guaymas, offers some of the best seafood around, at prices that tourist will appreciate.

**Best Dishes**

All of the restaurant's seafood dishes are fresh and come highly recommended. If you enjoy shrimp, you can opt for a shrimp taco or even a shrimp saut&eacute;, but if you don&rsquo;t want to be that daring you can always stick with the seafood standard, breaded shrimp. Even though Los Barcos has some of the best seafood in town they also serve other types of food, such as chicken tacos.

**Insider Information**

If you do opt to come into Los Barcos, you will need to make sure that you have cash on hand, either US dollars or Mexican pesos. Most restaurants in Guaymas do not accept credit cards and Los Barcos is no exception. Something else to keep in mind when paying a visit to Los Barcos is that they usually close the kitchen early, around 6:30 p.m.
