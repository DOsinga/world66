**Tips for Having a Car in Guaymas
**

Given the considerable influx of local and foreign tourists, **Guaymas transportation** is well-developed. Here are some tips on how to get around Guaymas.

#### **Renting a Car**

****

****

****

If you're planning to rent a car in Guaymas or any other city in Mexico, it is best to apply for an international driver's license before arriving. Within North America, you can apply for an international driver's license at a local AAA office. The international driver's license is the most common form of proving your driving ability. If renting a car is not an option, you can always use the local bus system, which charges 5 Mexican pesos for every trip.

#### **Bringing your Own Car from the United States**

If you're bringing your own car from the United States, make sure to purchase the necessary insurance. Your auto insurance in the U.S. is not valid in Mexico as valid proof of insurance. Also, if you're bringing your vehicle from the U.S., make sure to register it at Mexican customs. This step is not included in the customs process, and you need to look for the appropriate customs office at your entrance point to Mexico.

Be sure to park only in the appropriate places. Instead of towing your car for being improperly parked, local authorities will take away your license plate. You must pay your fine at the local police station to receive your license plate. You cannot re-enter the United States without a license plate.
