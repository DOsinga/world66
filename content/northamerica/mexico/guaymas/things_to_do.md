---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Guaymas rewards visitors who go looking. The harbour and the Sea of Cortez are the main draws — diving, boating, and watching the sunset from the Malecón are all genuinely worthwhile. Serdan Street provides a concentrated dose of colonial and post-independence architecture, while the Carnival in February transforms the city for two weeks of music, floats, and fireworks.
