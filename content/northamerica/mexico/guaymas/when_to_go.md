Tourists need to know different things about **Guaymas. When to go** is one of them because Guaymas is a port and is often warm and dry.

#### Spring

The spring goes through March April and May in Mexico. This season along with summer is pretty hot in Guaymas so the preferred activities are fishing navigating on kayak and snorkeling at the Pedro Nolasco, an island near the shore of the main port in Guaymas.

For those looking for adventure Villa Ecoturistica on the Las Barajitas Canyon 20 km north of San Carlos, is a zone of 1000 ha. of desert and two km of pristine shores where three different ecosystems converge.

#### Summer

With the temperatures reaching 40 degrees, the tourists can visit resorts and pools like the Balneario San Carlos at the north or simply head to the shores near the port of Guaymas to enjoy a day inside the cold water that will refresh the most insider fibers of the body. Guaymas when-to-go guides should take into consideration that summers are hot so tourists should remain hydrated.

#### Autumn

During the September, October and November months the rainfall increases, being this in form of drizzles and soft rains, Tourists should attend an event like the Festival of the Calaca on November 1, but try to bring an umbrella.

This is a good time for tourists to visit one of the many restaurants in Guaymas. When to go should be determined by the weather.

**Winter**

Winter begins in December and ends in February. This season is not very cold, and the minimum temperature reaches 18 degrees C. The Guaymas Carnival opens February 11. The indoors activities like going to watch a movie at the Cinema Guaymas located at 20th Street South #69 at the intersection of Malecon Malpica corner are preferred.
