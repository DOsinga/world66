The twin resorts of Ixtapa and Zihuatanejo are located on the Pacific Coast of Mexico 144 miles northwest of [Acapulco](/northamerica/mexico/acapulco). Ixtapa is the government-planned resort of high-rise hotels and nightclubs. Zihuatanejo is a fishing village with a more authentic Mexican flavor. The two towns, just four miles apart, make the perfect combination.
