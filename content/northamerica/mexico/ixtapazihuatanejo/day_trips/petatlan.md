A small town about an hour southeast of Zihuatanejo by bus, Petatlan is worth a half-day trip for its handsome central church and the surrounding streets lined with gold and silver jewelry stalls. The artisan trade here is long-established and prices are good compared to resort-town shops. Grab lunch at one of the small cafes around the plaza before catching a bus back. Buses run frequently from the central station in downtown Zihua.
