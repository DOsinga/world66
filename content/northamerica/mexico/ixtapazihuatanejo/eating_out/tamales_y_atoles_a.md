The family owned and operated Tamales Y Atoles Any's has served
traditional and authentic Mexican food for almost 20 years. Located in
El Centro, the bright yellow building with the tall, thatched roof is
easy to spot.

Any's is opened year-round 6 days a week, closing on Sundays.

Tamales are the house specialty, with a selection of more than a dozen
varieties. At 12 pesos, these tamales are so big they will satisfy all
but the biggest apetite.

In addition to tamales the menu includes tostadas, tacos, enchiladas, soups, and much more.

Pozole, a regional Thursday tradition, is available every day at
Tamales Y Atoles Any's. A hearty hominy stew with a choice of pork or
chicken, pozole is served with a variety of condiments including onion,
radishes, jalepenos, oregano, avacado, and chili powder. After pozole,
enjoy a complimentary jarrito of the house mezcal.

From November through March a weekly cultural dinner show highlights
traditional dance and music from the various regions of Mexico.
