### [Sandra Gomez](/northamerica/mexico/juchipila/things_to_do/sandra_gomez)

*Hotel Paraiso Caxcan* located about 1/2 north on Hwy 54 offers a great getaway for you and your family. This hotel was built over thermal springs with a great view of the canyon. The swimming pool water is warm/hot and many people come here as well due to the healing powers the waters are said to have. The town is called San Miguel.
