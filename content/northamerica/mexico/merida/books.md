About books and reading in and about the Yucatan

### [Merida English Library](/northamerica/mexico/merida/books/merida_english_lib)

This isn't a book...its a lot of books.

In Merida, you won't find many English books for sale. Even the residents have a hard time with this. Thank Goddess for the Merida English Library, a seemingly small but rather vast treasure trove of English language books. Anyone is welcome. Come browse the stacks, meet some of the locals, and read in the garden in back.

For more info check out their website

http://www.meridaenglishlibrary.com

### [Merida Bookstores](/northamerica/mexico/merida/books/merida_bookstores)

For books in English, visit **Amate Books** on the corner of Calle 60 and 49 - the big yellow colonial buidling. Kai, the manager, has a wide selection of books on archeology, the Mayans, art, architecture, cooking, photography, etc. etc. etc. The list is long!!

**Dante** is a chain of bookstores, with locations all over the city and in the malls. They carry a little of everything, even some English books occasionally. They have books for kids, books on Yucatecan history and best sellers in Spanish.They also have a line of tourism books in [more..](/northamerica/mexico/merida/books/merida_bookstores)
