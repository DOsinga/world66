Kukis By Maru, located in the Gran Plaza mall in the northern part of the city where the Prolongacion del Paseo de Montejo becomes the highway to Progreso, is the place to go when you need your fix of a homemade bran muffin, some freshly baked chocolate chip cookies or a slab of made-from-scratch carrot cake. Along with the usual assortment of coffees, Kukis By Maru also has a unique, crunchy biscotti, with the most almonds per bite of any biscotti I have ever tasted. There are several other baked items to choose from, always fresh, always homemade.
