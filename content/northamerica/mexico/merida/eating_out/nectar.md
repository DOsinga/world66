International seems a little staid a classification for the adventurous menu items the chefs put together at Nectar. Having just completed a working vacation at the Fat Duck in London (voted the best restaurant in the world) chef Solis is back creating spectacular new combinations that are a delight to look at and sublime on the palate. How about an example? Well there is the idea of combining *chicharron* (fried pork rind) with shrimp... breading the latter with the former and making it absolutely exquisite. The wine list is the largest in town, and they have their own sommelier-type individual who will recommend the perfect complement to your menu selection.

Oh and they are closed on Tuesdays.
