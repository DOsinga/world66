The food at Villa Maria is
good, solid European food. What they call Continental cuisine. There is
a lovely bar and they fix a good martini. The ambiance is
lovely...quiet, mood lit hacienda-style eating right in the middle of
the city. There is nothing else like it in downtown Merida. If you are
looking for a romantic or just quiet dinner, try Villa Maria. The
prices...did I mention the prices are VERY reasonable...10 to 12 bucks
for an entree. The Pear Dali dessert, by the way, is to die for. You
can walk to Villa Maria from downtown...don't let the high class
appearance put you off. Its not as cheap as some places in the Yucatan,
but a lot cheaper than places in Playa or Cancun, and a lot more
peaceful! One last thing I should mention...you will not find a place
in Mexico, in my opinion, with better service. The waiters are very
well trained, the cook usually comes out to say hello to the diners.
Its a very civilized place.
