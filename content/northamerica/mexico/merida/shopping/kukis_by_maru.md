If you have tried Mexican bakeries and find yourself just craving a
REAL chocolate chip cookie, you must go to Kukis by Maru in the Gran
Plaza Mall. You'll probably want to go to the mall at some point,
anyway...maybe to see a movie or something. Kuki's is on the second
floor and is open every day of the week. They serve fresh-baked
chocolate chip, oatmeal, peanut butter and other cookies, carrot cake,
brownies and great muffins. They also have coffee and capuccino. And
the cookies are GREAT!
