One thing is your regular tshirts and another is taking a piece of Yucatan with you in the form of a ... tshirt!

Yucatecans are famous throughout Mexico for their peculiar way of speaking, which freely combines modern Spanish with older Spanish words now in disuse elsewhere, and Mayan terms inherited from the Mayan culture that flourished here.

Mayan Xic offers a variety of tshirts featuring extremely local expressions - nothing rude, mind you - that are appreciated by Spanish speakers all over the world. Granted, this is not your typical souvenir shirt and will probably not strike a chord with anyone who doesn't speak at least a little Spanish, but will appeal to someone whose interest runs a little off the beaten 'tourist' track.
