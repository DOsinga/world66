This is a lovely nursery about 40 minutes outside of Merida that specializes in tropical and exotic plants. You can go there, spend the day and find out about how to care for your tropical plants, and then buy a rhizome that will be packed up for your trip home. The nursery is just outside of Muna on the way to Uxmal. There is also one casita there that Lucy, the owner, will rent out. Its a lovely place with a large bathroom, a kitchen, a front and back porch...and its located right in the garden.
