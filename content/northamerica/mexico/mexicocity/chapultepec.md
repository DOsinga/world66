type: poi

The vast green lung of central Mexico City, twice the size of Central Park. The park holds Chapultepec Castle on its hill (the only royal castle in the Americas, briefly home to the Habsburg Emperor Maximilian), the Anthropology Museum, the Modern Art Museum, the Tamayo, the zoo, and a lake with rowing boats. The first section is busiest at weekends, when families pour in for the day.
