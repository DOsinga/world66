---
title: "Tula"
type: poi
---

The capital of the Toltec is situated outside the town of Tula Allende
in Hidalgo. To get there, you can take a 90 minute bus ride from
Autobuses del Norte, costing MXN 52. After arriving at Tula, you can
hail a cab to the ruins for about MXN 25.
