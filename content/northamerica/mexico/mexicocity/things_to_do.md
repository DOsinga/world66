---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

The historic centre, the Aztec ruins, the Diego Rivera murals, and the world-class Anthropology Museum will keep most travellers busy for several days. Beyond the obvious, the great pleasure of Mexico City is wandering its neighbourhoods — colonial Centro Histórico, leafy Roma and Condesa, village-like Coyoacán and San Ángel — each with its own pace and personality.
