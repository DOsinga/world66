Thw whole town of Puerto Morelos is on a fantastic white sand beach that stretches for blocks and blocks. It is very wide and there are restaurants and lodgings that are right on the beach.

### [Fisherman's Landing](/northamerica/mexico/morelos/beaches/fishermans_landin)

Watch the fishermen pull in the day's catch by the pier and mingle with them.
