type: section

There are only a few **Nueva Rosita bars and cafes** where you can dine and catch up with your friends. If you happen to visit Nueva Rosita, then the best place to kill your time is to spend it at the GlobalNet, which is an Internet caf&eacute; and coffee bar. This is a popular spot for both tourists and locals alike.

**The GlobalNet Caf&eacute;**

This caf&eacute; also serves as an Internet caf&eacute; where you can check the latest news of the outside world. Located on Queretaro Street in Zaragosa, the GlobalNet Caf&eacute; has a quaint setting. It has few coffee tables at one side and about 8 computer units scattered throughout the place. The caf&eacute; also has scanners, printers and other latest networking tools which can help a busy tourist who wants nothing more than to check emails but, at the same time, enjoy coffee and donuts as well. Food costs between $10 and $20 which is paid separately from the $15 per hour usage of the Internet.

**Activities
**

Aside from using the Internet, this caf&eacute; also offers centralized soft music that either has Western or Mexican origin. Since there are some tables in the vicinity, you can order coffee and donuts while you talk with your friends, or just kill time before going to the next engagement.
