title: "Festivals in Nueva Rosita"
type: section
---

**Nueva Rosita festivals **are year around and express the local culture of the area. The city of Nueva Rosita is located in the northeast part of Mexico and was originally founded when companies established the first coal mines.

**Independence Day Festival
**

Travelers to Nueva Rosita will find all sorts of festivals through out the year. Nueva Rosita is a patriotic part of Mexico and the residents have a festival to celebrate Mexican Independence Day. Residents often link Independence Day to their desire to remain independent of the United States. Festivals often highlight the struggles that workers had with management during the 19th century in an effort to remain independent from northern companies. The town of Rosita is smaller then more popular cities, like Mexico City or Puerta Vallarta, but the smaller size ensures more intimate festivities.

**Music Festivals**

During the year, there are several Latin music festivals, like the Latin Energy Festival, for singers in the industry. A number of groups, like Yahari, have been discovered this way. The festivals offer a chance for struggling musicians to get noticed in the entrainment industry. Organizers of the festivals offer workshops to the participants so they can fine tune their skills. As the Latin American population has grown in the United States, executives have come to these festivals to find groups that appeal to the Hispanic population.

Nueva Rosita is city with a lot of heart and tradition. Enjoying the festivals is a great way for visitors to experience the true character of the city.
