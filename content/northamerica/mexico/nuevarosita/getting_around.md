**Various Transportation Options in Nueva Rosita**

There are various **Nueva Rosita transportation** options available in town. Instead of using buses and taxis, you should consider walking since the city isn&rsquo;t that big, and the streets are safe enough. Nevertheless, motorized transport is a good option, but the only problem is that you&rsquo;ll have to be able to speak Spanish, as most bus and taxi drivers aren&rsquo;t that good in English. Here are some details on the local transport.**
**

**Buses**

The public transport in Nueva Rosita consists of buses. Tickets are very cheap, but make sure you are carrying small change to buy the tickets. You will have to buy a ticket from the driver, and the drivers usually require that you give them exact change. Riding on the bus can be an unpleasant experience during peak hours since there are no passenger restrictions, and buses get overcrowded, with people even hanging from their doors sometimes. Make sure to blend with the crowd, as if you get recognized as a foreigner, you may attract the attention of pick-pockets.

**Taxis**

Taxis are another form of transport in Nueva Rosita. Most of them have no meters, and you&rsquo;ll have to negotiate the price with the driver (in Spanish). Nevertheless, taxi is very affordable and much cheaper than taxis in Europe or the States. Don&rsquo;t be surprised to see that all the taxis are painted in different colors and that all the vehicles are different models, as there are no taxi standards in Mexico.
