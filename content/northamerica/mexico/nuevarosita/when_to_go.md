---
title: "When to Go in Nueva Rosita"
type: section
---

When planning your next trip to **Nueva Rosita**, **when to go** should be the first question that you have to figure out. Depending on the time of the year, you will have different activities available to you. Here are some things to consider.

**Summer
**

A lot of the most popular activities include visits to the Los Sabinitos, the Los Gritos and the San Antonio parks. During the summer months, the weather can get really hot, and it might be very uncomfortable for people with breathing complications and asthma. If you have one of these conditions, it is a good idea to avoid the summer months to avoid any medical complications. If you are up to the challenge, you will be happy to hear that a lot of the guided tours end at lakes and rivers, where snorkeling and swimming are often enjoyed.

**Non-Summer Months**

Non-summer months are great to visit the Area de Preteccion de Flora y Fauna Cuatrocienegas because this beautiful nature reserve is located in the middle of the dessert. During the non-summer months, the days are not as hot as during the summer. However, if you're planning to camp, you will need to bring warm clothes as it gets really breezy and temperatures drop considerably.

**Throughout the Year**

No matter what time of the year you decide to visit Nueva Rosita, you will be happy to hear that there will be several cultural activities available for you such as visits to the Panteon Municipal and the San Juan Church. Other year-round activities include visits to the local markets.
