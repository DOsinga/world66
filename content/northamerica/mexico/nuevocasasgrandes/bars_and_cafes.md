title: "Bars and Cafes in Nuevo Casas Grandes"
type: section
---

Nuevo Casas Grandes is a sleepy city situated on the San Miguel River in the state of Chihuahua and is famous for its **Nuevo Casas Grandes bars and caf&eacute;s**. Other that its bars and cafes, Nuevo Casas Grandes also is famous for the varied historical and archaeological attractions present in and around the city. The place is also famous for its proximity to the potter village and the mysterious ancient ruins of Paquime. The city is surrounded by several orchards, adding to its charm. Given its proximity to several famous tourist attractions, Nuevo Casa Grandes has several tourists visiting all around the year. Given below are some of the Nuevo Casas Grandes Bars and Caf&eacute;s.

**Las Guacamayas B&B**

The Las Guacamayas B&B, apart from providing great accommodations to the tourists traveling to Nuevo Casa Grandes, is a great cafe that is nestled on the edge of the small Casas Grandes village. Situated just few minutes away from Nuevo Casa Grandes, this place offers its guests everything that they need, from accommodations in their beautiful and charming 11 rooms to a lovely garden where guests can relax with their coffee. There is also a spacious kitchen where the breakfast is served. The Las Guacamayas B&B has been built mimicking the techniques used by the inhabitants of the old town on Paquime. Aside from enjoying your cup of coffee, you can also shop at the beautiful gallery that sells some of the best handicrafts in that area.

**Dino&rsquo;s Pizza**

Dino&rsquo;s Pizza is another famous pizza joint and a caf&eacute; in Nuevo Casa Grande, offering some of the best pizzas in the city. The place is neat and clean and the staff is friendly. Dino&rsquo;s Pizza is famous for its schezuan style dishes and the lovely fresh strong coffee that they serve. On your visit here, you can see men in cowboy hats always lingering here for the string fresh coffee.

**Hotel Pinon **

Hotel Pinon is another option available for tourists visiting Nuevo Casas Grandes. The hotel, apart from its restaurant, also has a bar which has a really cozy and warm ambiance. This is the right place to be after a long day out exploring the neighboring areas around the city. The bar offers some of the best authentic Mexican drinks that you can enjoy. If you are visiting this place on the weekends, then you can enjoy some great Mexican food with some wonderful and melodious Mexican music. There is a live band that performs in the hotel premises during the weekends.
