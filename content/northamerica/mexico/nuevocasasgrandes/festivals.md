---
title: "Festivals in Nuevo Casas Grandes"
type: section
---

People planning a vacation in Mexico are advised to participate in **Nuevo Casas Grandes Festivals**. Nuevo Grandes is located along the foothills of the Mesa del Norte Mountains. Nuevo Casas Grandes is popular for its historical and archaeological sites. However, this north Mexican city should also be visited for its cultural heritage that is best explained symbolized by its festivals. Some Nuevo Casas Grandes Festivals have been discussed below:

**1. Cultural Festival—International Puppet Festival**

This is among Mexico&rsquo;s most prominent international festivals. It is held in July, in the Monterrey town of Nuevo Casas. This festival is also acknowledged for putting Nuevo Leon on the world map. This event witnesses participation of native puppeteers and puppet-makers and encourages international participation from South American and Caribbean nations, too. Lately, this festival has also become a breeding ground for short-film producers and screenwriters from Mexico to explain their ideas via puppet shows. The Puppet Festival is perfect for family travelers looking for wholesome entertainment options for the entire family. There are minimal entry-charges and on some days, the entry is free.

**2. Religious Festival—Festival of Posadas**

This festival is celebrated for nearly, nine days. The celebrations begin on December 16 and the final procession is hosted on December 24. The Posadas is among the most revered of traditional celebrations in Mexico. Nuevo Grandes is counted among the prominent places where Posadas is still celebrated with enthusiasm. This festival celebrates the journey taken upon by Joseph and Mary on their way to Bethlehem and commemorates the hardships they endured in doing so. Visitors keen to understand Mexican traditions should definitely attend the processions. The festivities include recitation of hymns. Every house is decorated with a definite theme which is sometimes determined by the local, governing councils.
