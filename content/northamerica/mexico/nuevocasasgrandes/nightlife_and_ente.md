---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment in Nuevo Casas Grandes"
type: section
---

The **Nuevo Casas Grandes Nightlife** is practically nonexistent. Most people think that all Mexican cities have some kind of happening nightlife because of how much the cities rely on tourism. However, this is not always the case, especially when you go further inland. The happening nightlife that many vacationers have come to expect from Mexican cities can be found on the coastal cities, which see the most tourism.

**Panaderia La Guadalupana**

If you are looking for pan dulces you will want to come in here for a visit. Not only can you get the best pan dulces in town you can also see how they are made. Even though a bakery can use different methods to make pan dulces this bakery still follows the traditional method, which is a treat to see. To make the most of your visit select a pan dulce to take back to your hotel to enjoy. Pan dulces go great with a cup of hot coffee or even hot chocolate.

**Friends**

This is one of the best places in town to be, especially if you are a part of the younger crowd. At Friends you will be able to enjoy a fun-filled evening of pizza and hamburgers, while sitting back and hanging out with friends. If you enjoy playing billiards be sure to play at least one game before you go. If you are looking for an easy way to cool off on a hot night you need to grab a cup of gelato ice cream; there are over 20 flavors to choose from and all are made on-site.

**La Reina de Michoacan**

If you don&rsquo;t want to hang out at the local hot spot, but are still interested in grabbing a cup of ice cream this is the best place to go. There are several of these stores located throughout the town, but the most popular one is located in the main plaza. If you are not in the mood for ice cream be sure to check out the fruit drinks or fruit-ice-on-a-stick that is offered.

**Tacos**

Visiting any city in Mexico is not complete unless you get some really good tacos. To get the best tacos in town you will want to go for the al aire fresco tacos, which also include shrimp tacos. In Nuevo Casas Grandes you can find these tacos at the Madero intersection right across the street from the Pemex station.

**El Rincon Oriental**

If you are looking for some sushi in Mexico, El Rincon Oriental is definitely a place to try, and it is open until 10 p.m. every night except Mondays. You can find this restaurant across the street from the Motel Casas Grandes. You can also visit other restaurants in town during the evening, but not all of them will be open late into the evening.
