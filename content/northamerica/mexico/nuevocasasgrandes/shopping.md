---
title: "Shopping in Nuevo Casas Grandes"
type: section
---

The state of Chihuahua in Mexico is in the northern and central-eastern portion of the country, and people oftentimes travel to the upper reaches of this state to visit the historic town of **Nuevo Casas Grandes**; **shopping**, sightseeing and much more await at this scenic destination. The population of Nuevo Casas Grandes is under 100,000, although this does not typically include the Mennonite and Mormon communities that live outside of the city borders. Read on for a brief overview of the best shopping experiences to find in the Mexican city of Nuevo Casas Grandes.

**Casas Grandes**

A few miles southwest of the city proper is a great historical and archaeological reservoir of sites that has been collectively termed Casas Grandes. This area has provided many clues into the nature of ancient societies that lived in the area. A visit to Casas Grandes will reward the traveler with both a historical perspective and shopping opportunities. There are street vendors and others selling arts, crafts and small souvenirs around the site of Casas Grandes. Be sure to barter in order to get the best prices on all goods in this area.

**Mennonite Community**

The Mennonite community outside of the border of Nuevo Casas Grandes thrives by selling fruits and vegetables. The orchards and farms that they keep are among the finest in this part of Mexico, and many travelers enjoy purchasing fresh foods from the Mennonites. This is both an opportunity to support a unique culture and society as well as a chance to learn about the history of the region around Nuevos Casas Grandes too.

**Downtown**

While the central area in Nuevo Casas Grandes is not large, it does provide you with ample opportunities to purchase a variety of goods. These include general goods and products that you may need for your stay or for your travels, but you can also find unique souvenirs, many of which are hand crafted and unique. Nuevo Casas Grandes does not have any major shopping centers or luxury stores, so it's best to focus your shopping efforts on locally produced art pieces and other wares of this type.

Nuevo Casas Grandes is a central Mexican city with plenty of opportunities for cultural study and exploration, including a few very good and inexpensive shopping opportunities as well. Ask the Mexican tourism board for additional assistance.
