title: "When to Go in Nuevo Casas Grandes"
type: section
---

When visiting **Nuevo Casas Grandes, when to go** is an important choice to consider. Founded in the 1870s, Nuevo Casas Grandes is located in the northwestern part of the Mexican state of Chiuaua along the Casas Grandes River. It borrowed its name from another town called Casas Grandes, about a mile away. It is an area that is known for its archeological and historical history. Below are descriptions of ideal times to visit this historic community.

**Travel Warnings**

The recent attacks on US citizens have prompted the US Embassy in Mexico to issue a warning to Americans while traveling in certain parts of Mexico, including the state of Chiuaua. Use extreme caution and delay any unnecessary travel in some parts of Durango, Coahuila, and Chiuaua.

**Weather**

Nuevo Casas Grandes has a dessert-like climate. The hottest months are June and July, while the coolest is December. Even at its coolest, it is comfortable in the 50s. Nights, however, can get a little the cold side, depending on the time of year.

Hurricane season lasts from June to November. Although Nuevo Casas Grandes is inland, it is a good idea to be aware of where any potential hurricane threats might be located, because violent storms can often occur inland as a result of hurricanes.

**Festivals**

July is an excellent time to visit if you want to attend the international mountain biking festival (festival internacional de ciclismo de monta&ntilde;a) that takes place in breathtaking Copper Canyon on July 9th and 10th. It is the best time of year for adventure seekers and athletes to attend. Activities include road races, down hill races, guided rides, and some kids activities.

Also taking place in the Casas Grande region during the month of July is the Festival Nueva Paquime, which takes place July 18th through the 26th. It is one of the most prestigious archeological sites in North Mexico. The festival is about celebrating the Paquime culture. Many cultural events will take place, including handmade crafts, poetry readings, and concerts.

**High Season vs Low Season**

During summer months and certain holidays, Mexicans like to travel. Due their traveling habits, you may find that hotels are crowded during these times. It might be a good idea to avoid visiting during these times, or to book your room in advance. Being a Catholic area, Nuevo Casas Grandes's most popular holidays are Christmas and Easter.
