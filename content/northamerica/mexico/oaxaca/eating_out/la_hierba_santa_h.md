November 2008

I just visited Oaxaca City and went to a new amazing restaurant in the
historic center of Oaxaca. I ate there several times and it is called
Restaurante La Hierba Santa at Calle Matamoros 101, just near by Santo
domingo church. The food is international with a

delicate Mexican twist
and the chef Miguel Jimenez is the former head chef at Los Danzantes! He
and a friend now decided to start this great restaurant. While

being
there they showed me a book that Miguel appears in which illustrates
the best chefs of the states of Mexico, called Viva La Revolucion New Food
from

Mexico. This is a must try restaurant. The food is excellent and the prices are very reasonable for the quality food.
