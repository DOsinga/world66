---
title: "Shopping"
type: section
---

Oaxaca is the best place in Mexico to buy traditional crafts, and most of what you find is made in the Zapotec villages around the city: **wool rugs** from Teotitlán del Valle (ask for pieces dyed with natural cochineal), **black pottery** from San Bartolo Coyotepec, **green-glazed pottery** from Atzompa, and the famously colourful carved wooden **alebrijes** from Arrazola and San Martín Tilcajete. In the city, the stores along Macedonio Alcalá, García Vigil and 5 de Mayo carry high-end examples; for better prices and the thrill of bargaining, head to the **Benito Juárez market** or the huge **Abastos market** west of the centre (best on Saturdays). You can also drive the villages themselves and buy direct from the craftspeople.
