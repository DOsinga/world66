Fight poverty in Oaxaca, see microfinance and development first-hand, and get a perspective on the city you won't get anywhere else. Fundación En Vía** **is a non-profit organization that is working to combine the power of microfinance with the power of tourism to fight poverty around Oaxaca.

We run day-long tours to pueblos nearby where participants get to meet women living in the town who are applying for our loans. Participants learn about the town, and the women's lives and businesses. At the end of the tour, 100% of the tour fee goes as an interest-free microloan to the women we visited.

Find out more at www.envia.org.
