title: "Templo de Santo Domingo"
type: poi
category: "Sight"
address: "Calle Macedonio Alcalá, Centro"
latitude: 17.0679
longitude: -96.7234
---

The most spectacular baroque church in Mexico. The Dominicans built it between 1570 and 1608, and the interior is almost entirely covered in gilded plaster — a dense ceiling of genealogical trees, biblical scenes and angels that culminates in the extraordinary domed ceiling of the Rosary Chapel. The adjacent former monastery now houses the **Museo de las Culturas de Oaxaca**, whose centrepiece is the treasure of gold, jade and pearl grave goods recovered from Tomb 7 at Monte Albán.
