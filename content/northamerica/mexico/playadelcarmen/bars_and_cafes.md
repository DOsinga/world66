---
title: "Bars and Cafes in Playa del Carmen"
type: section
---

Although Playa del Carmen is primarily visited for its pristine Caribbean waters and its soft white sands, it is also popular for **Playa del Carmen bars and cafes**. The place is also famous for its caves and cenotes and its one-of-a-kind Mayan ruins which are all over the place. During the day, tourists relax on the beach, or walk along the main street Quinto Avenida, or the 5th Avenue, which is the city’s shopping highlight. But a vacation will not be the same without an exploration of the city’s vibrant night life. Playa del Carmen bars and cafes should definitely be part of your itinerary. After all, Mexico is known for its authentic Cuban cigars and the pleasurably intoxicating spirits – cerveza, rum and Tequila.

**Fusion and Calle 6**

Tourists love visiting beach bars such as Fusion and Calle 6. There is live music to make your bar experience come alive. Coming to bars by the beach during the summer can be a challenge, as these as usually packed, but off season, they’re great.

**Dirty Martini Lounge**

Vacationers love the Dirty Martini Lounge because the party ambiance is cool and not cramped like other bars. It’s perfect for people looking for a nightcap or a place to unwind to enjoy a typical Playa evening. There is a hotel on-site and they serve food, such as pizza, if you are in the mood for a midnight snack.

**El Bistro en la Quinta and El Bistro at La Tortuga**

El Bistro en la Quinta and El Bistro at La Tortuga are two cafes that offer an relaxing ambiance and ultimately good food. They are open for breakfast, lunch and dinner and are popular for their friendly and professional services. These two bistros are best recommended for those who like perfect dining experiences.

**Playa del Carmen**

Something new? Try a nightlife tour in Playa del Carmen. Located on 4th Avenue, Playacrawl is a bar / club crawl service. What it does is gather tourists interested in trying out the city’s party scene and night life and tour them to at least 5 different bars in one night. The bars have diverse music genres to enjoy as well as different crowds to mingle with. If your idea of fun is bar hopping, then Playacrawl should be at the top of your list.

**5th Avenue**

If you have been shopping all day and would just like dinner and a few drinks before heading to your hotel, don’t leave 5th Avenue. It boasts of restaurants and bars with diverse gastronomic offerings – take your pick. One downside is that most of these bars and dining spots close before midnight.
