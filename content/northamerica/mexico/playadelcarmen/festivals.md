---
title: "Festivals"
type: section
---

The Riviera Maya Jazz Festival in November is the most distinctive annual event — free outdoor concerts on the beach over several evenings, drawing international jazz musicians. Día de los Muertos (1–2 November) is celebrated with altars, parades, and the characteristic sugar skull imagery throughout the town. Carnival in February or March brings street parties and processions. The town's beach clubs host regular themed nights and events year-round.
