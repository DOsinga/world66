---
title: "Getting There in Playa del Carmen"
type: section
---

**Airport and Buses**

If you're planning a trip to **Playa del Carmen, getting there** is pretty straightforward. All major airlines fly into Cancun International Airport, which is 45 minutes away from Playa del Carmen. From Cancun, you can take a direct bus to Playa del Carmen. The bus line is called the Riviera. You can catch it outside the airport terminal, and cost of a ticket is about $7 USD.

**Colectivo**

For about $16 dollars, you can take a “colectivo.” It's a van that fits 10 people and makes stops at major hotels. Tickets can be purchased at kiosks outside the airport. The downside to this plan is the wait: you have to allow passengers to gather before leaving, and the drop-offs can take a while. Considering that the ride itself is 45 minutes long, this may not be your top choice.

**Taxis**

If you prefer to take a taxi, be ready to shell out a bundle of cash; the fare is approximately $65 dollars. To avoid heat and lines, you may want to make a reservation with a car service through companies like Resorthoppa. Expect a similar rate.

**Cozumel Ferry**

Flying to the island of Cozumel is also an option. If you opt to go this route, you will have to take a ferry to Playa del Carmen. The ferries depart from the main pier every hour or so during the morning.

**Tip for Drivers**

Highway 307 goes through Playa del Carmen. No matter what city you are coming from, this will be the last leg of your trip.
