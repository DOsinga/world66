A European-style restaurant and bakery near the Plazuela de los Sapos antique market in the city centre. The menu changes with the seasons, leaning Mediterranean, and the sourdough bread and pastries are baked in-house. The owners speak English and German. A good spot for a leisurely breakfast or a lunch with wine.
