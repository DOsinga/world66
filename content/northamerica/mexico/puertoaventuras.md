Puerto Aventuras is a gated resort community on Mexico's Riviera Maya, about 20 km south of Playa del Carmen. Built around a sheltered marina, it is quieter and more manicured than the towns on either side — more condo resort than Mexican beach town.

The marina is the hub, with restaurants, shops, and a small dolphin encounter facility. The beach inside the resort is calm and well-maintained, and the offshore reef provides decent snorkelling. Deep-sea fishing charters run out of the marina, and the area is a convenient base for visiting the ruins at [Tulum](/northamerica/mexico/tulum) and the cenotes scattered through the surrounding jungle.
