Sol Maya Vacation Rentals is located in Puerto Aventuras, Mexico. They offer a broad selection of beach front condominiums and private villas for family vacation rentals.

Sol Maya Vacation Rentals is owned and operated by Andrew and Lisa Leigh. Concierge services include ground transportation, pre-arrival grocery stock, chef services, babysitting services, massages, tee times, fishing charters, scuba diving, restaurant reservations, tours and excursions.
