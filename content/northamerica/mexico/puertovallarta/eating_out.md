---
title: "Eating Out"
type: section
---

Puerto Vallarta's food scene ranges from beachfront ceviche stands to serious fine dining. The annual gourmet festival draws top chefs from across Mexico. Seafood is the obvious strength — fresh snapper, shrimp, and lobster are on every menu — but you will also find excellent French, Italian, and fusion cooking, especially around the Zona Romántica and along the coast toward Bucerías.
