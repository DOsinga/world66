A zip-line adventure park near Boca de Tomatlán, about 30 minutes south of town. Sixteen lines span over 3 kilometres of jungle canopy, including runs called Black Diamond and The Big Enchilada. The park also has an animal sanctuary with monkeys, iguanas, and macaws, plus kayaking, swimming, and hiking trails. Getting there is part of the fun — a motorboat across the bay, then a bus ride up into the hills. Children must be at least five years old and 40 inches tall; weight limit 130 kilograms.
