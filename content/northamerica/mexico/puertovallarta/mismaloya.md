A small fishing village 15 minutes south of Puerto Vallarta by car, tucked into a cove on Highway 200. The beach faces the granite arches of Los Arcos National Marine Park, making it a natural base for snorkelling and diving trips. The ruins of the *Night of the Iguana* film set are still visible on the hillside above the south end of the beach, along with the shell of John Huston's old café. There is also a sea turtle sanctuary nearby. Boats and tours can be arranged from the beach.
