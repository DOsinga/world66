---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment"
type: section
---

Puerto Vallarta will entice you with its vivacious spirit and
gregarious attitude and much as you can love or hate the town’s
nightlife, there’s no way to ignore it. The Puerto Vallarta nightlife
offers visitors of the nocturnal character the
best places to visit to get a glimpse of the town’s famous nightlife.

**Orientation**

The destination has a robust and unrestrained nightlife that doesn’t
believe in playing it down. Puerto Vallarta is a haven for party
animals and night revelers. There is a superb line-up of dancing clubs,
open bars, pubs, cafes, beach clubs and live music destinations that
have attainted nothing short of a cult status in the region and has
catapulted it to the big league of tourist gems in Mexico. The locals
are extremely warm and hospitable and there are ample opportunities to
strike interesting conversations with them. Each club here hosts
special night entertainment events and the spirits never stop soaring.
Most clubs in the town are open late into the night and play brilliant
live music to accentuate the local charm. Music genres range from
seductive jazz and salsa to classic reggae and pulsating rock. Sip a
margarita while catching a spectacular view of a tangerine sky at
sunset from a charming terrace bar or go cruise along one of the
twilight boats with delicious cocktails served on board. Alternatively
have a romantic meal in an intimate setting in one of the several cozy
bars sprinkled around town.

**Where to go **

Party on a cool catamaran for an unforgettable party experience
under Puerto Vallarta’s night sky. The Vallarta Adventure cruise
travels to the Las Caletas which incidentally is the former abode of
John Huston. Guests are entertained with some super native dance
performances and a sumptuous buffet. The cruise embarks at 6 p.m. daily
and lasts for four hours. For a popular local dance club, head to J
& B Salsa Club that plays pure and inviting live Latin music and
compels visitors to shake a leg to its salsa and samba renditions. For
gay and lesbian revelry, the best option is Disco Club Paco Paco. They
have a transvestite performance on weekends and feature a great open
air rooftop bar. Find the hip and happening of Puerto Vallarta at Nikki
Beach, a hotspot heavily inspired by the night haunts at Florida and
the French Riviera. It has an expansive outdoor beach-side lounge and
an intimate indoor dining space that serves delectable signature
dishes. Go there for their celebrated beach brunches on Sundays. The
oddly titled No Name Bar & Grill has an extensive collection of
spirits and serves lip-smacking barbecued ribs. Hilo is a high
spirited club and music destination for teeny boppers and the early
twenties brigade. They play good house and rock. Other popular club
and music destinations include Mariachi Loco, Route 66 and Zoo.

Puerto Vallarta is a dazzler, day or night not withstanding and parties with unapologetic gusto.
