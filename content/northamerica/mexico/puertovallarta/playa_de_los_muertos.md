Puerto Vallarta's most popular beach, stretching south from the Zona Romántica. Busy, colourful, and unapologetically social — beach vendors, restaurants spilling onto the sand, jet ski rentals, and a steady parade of people. The southern end around Blue Chairs is the centre of the city's LGBT beach scene. Water taxis to Yelapa and Las Ánimas depart from Los Muertos Pier at the northern end.
