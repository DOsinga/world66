---
title: "Shopping"
type: section
---

The best shopping in Puerto Vallarta is scattered between the Centro and the Zona Romántica. The [Mercado Municipal Río Cuale](/northamerica/mexico/puertovallarta/mercado_rio_cuale) is the place for Mexican crafts, silver, and souvenirs. Along the Malecón and on Basilio Badillo in the Zona Romántica you will find art galleries, boutiques, and consignment shops. The weekly art gallery crawl (October through April) is worth timing a visit around.
