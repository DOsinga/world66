---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Puerto Vallarta is made for doing things outside. **The Malecón** — a 1.6-kilometre seafront promenade lined with sculptures and musicians — is the first stop, best at sunset. From there, wander south over the Río Cuale into the **Zona Romántica**, the old-town grid of cobblestones, whitewashed houses, and the pink-steepled Church of Guadalupe. Beyond the town: boat trips to **Las Ánimas** and **Yelapa**, snorkelling at **Los Arcos National Marine Park**, whale watching between December and March, and day tours up into the Sierra Madre to the old silver village of **San Sebastián del Oeste**.
