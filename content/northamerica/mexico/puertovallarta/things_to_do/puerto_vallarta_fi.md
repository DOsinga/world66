Puerto Vallarta Fishing offers deep sea fishing expeditions to the major sportfishing sites of the Marietas Islands, El Morro, Corbetena, and The Banks (*El Banco*). Also offered is shared fishing, where you buy a space on a fishing trip, sharing the boat with other people for a lower cost.

World-class sportfishing trips fish for 1,000 lb. Marlin (Black and Blue), 800 lb. Yellowfin Tuna, and great prizes like Sailfish, Wahoo, and Mahi Mahi (*Dorado*) depart every day from Los Peines Marina in Puerto Vallarta. Specialty trips available for species like Roosterfish as well.

Boats available range from top-tier luxury yachts to economy models designed to get you on the water for as little money as possible. You get to choose what type of boat fits your budget and needs. Toll-Free, english-speaking customer service is available 7 days a week, and secure online or telephone booking using a credit card offers peace of mind.
