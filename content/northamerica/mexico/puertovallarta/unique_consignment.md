An antiques and consignment shop in the Zona Romántica, open since 2003. The stock is mostly Mexican-made — chandeliers, bronze and wood sculptures, Talavera pottery, sarapes, masks, oil paintings, iron candelabros, and colonial-style rustic furniture. Good for one-of-a-kind souvenirs or larger pieces if you are furnishing a place. Open Monday to Friday, 10 a.m. to 5 p.m.
