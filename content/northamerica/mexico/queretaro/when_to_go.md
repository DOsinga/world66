There are many **Queretaro when to go** tips people should know. One important factor is the different climate depending on the region. The south is more cool and humid, with frequent rain or hail during the summer. The temperature is more ideal during the summer.

**Spring**

Queretaro is also known as the City of Eternal Spring because the climate is constantly in a state of spring-like weather. The temperature stays at about 64 degrees Fahrenheit or 18 degrees Celsius year-round.

During the spring, the temperatures range from 54 degrees to 85 degrees Fahrenheit or 12 degrees to 29 degrees Celsius. The spring season in Queretaro is during the months of March, April, May.

**Summer**

The summer temperatures range from 57 degrees to 81 degrees Fahrenheit or 14 to 27 degrees Celsius. The summer season in Queretaro is during the months of June, July and August.

Due to the cooler temperatures, it would be optimal to visit Queretaro during this time. The weather is not too hot or too cold.

**Autumn**

The autumn temperatures in Queretaro range from 52 degrees to 77 degrees Fahrenheit or 11 degrees to 26 degrees Celsius. The autumn season is during the months of September, October and November.

**Winter**

One factor tourists need to take into consideration is that the winter temperatures in Queretaro range from 45 degrees to 73 degrees Fahrenheit or 7 to 23 degrees Celsius. The winter is during the months of December, January and February.
