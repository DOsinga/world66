---
title: Getting Around
type: section
---

Within the resort, most things are within easy walking distance — the marina, the dive shops, the restaurants, and the beach are all clustered together. **Golf carts** are available to rent at the entrance and are a popular way to move between the residential areas and the marina. To explore the wider Riviera Maya — the cenotes, Tulum, Akumal, and Playa del Carmen — you will want a rental car. Cars can be arranged through agencies in Playa del Carmen, or booked in advance through the usual international companies at Cancún airport.
