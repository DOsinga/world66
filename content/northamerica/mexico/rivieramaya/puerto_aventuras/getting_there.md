---
title: Getting There
type: section
---

Puerto Aventuras is on Highway 307 (the Cancún–Tulum coastal road), about 20 km south of Playa del Carmen and 100 km south of Cancún International Airport — roughly an hour's drive from the airport. By **bus**, take any colectivo or ADO coach from Playa del Carmen toward Tulum and ask to be dropped at the Puerto Aventuras entrance; the journey takes around 25 minutes. From **Cancún airport**, ADO coaches run direct to Playa del Carmen from the terminal; from there, change to a southbound colectivo. If you are arriving with luggage, a private transfer or rental car from the airport is more practical.
