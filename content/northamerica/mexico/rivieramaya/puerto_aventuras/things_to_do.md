---
title: Things to Do
type: section
---

Puerto Aventuras is primarily a diving destination. The marina has two well-equipped dive shops — **Dive Aventuras** and **Scubamex** — offering reef dives, cenote dives, and PADI courses. The Caribbean reef just offshore is healthy, and the cenotes scattered through the jungle inland are genuinely spectacular: clear, cold, cathedral-like spaces that you can snorkel or dive through. The resort also has a **9-hole golf course** set around the marina, and the marina itself has decent sport fishing for sailfish and mahi-mahi from November to April.

The **Dolphin Discovery** facility in the marina is the most controversial feature of Puerto Aventuras. It is popular with package tourists but draws criticism from marine conservationists; if you have concerns about captive cetaceans, skip it and spend the money on a cenote dive instead.
