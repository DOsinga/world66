type: section

Located on the Yucatan Peninsula of Mexico and just south of Cancun,
you will find The Riviera Maya, also known as The Mayan Riviera. The region has seen a growth spurt over the
past 10 years while becoming a top travel destination. You will find
amazing hotels, fabulous restaurants, and long stretching white sand
beaches with crystal aquamarine water. The Riviera Maya has a little
something to offer everyone. Be prepared to travel for attractions
though, as the Riviera is vast and spread out.

**Playa del Carmen**

Playa del Carmen is the main stop on the Riviera Maya and offers
amazing beaches, a very busy beach town. Quinta Avenida (5th Avenue)
is the main strip for shopping, restaurants, hotels, and resorts. If
you are looking for it, it can be found on Quinta Avenida. From Playa
del Carmen you can hop on the ferry to Cozumel where you will find
amazing shopping for local crafts, and the diving and snorkeling rival
some of the best in the world. Many tourists prefer staying in Playa
del Carmen as opposed to Cancun, it is quieter, and not as busy, yet
still provides all the entertainment you need.

**Mayan Ruins**

Tulum, and Chichen Itza, are the famous Mayan ruin sites located on
the Yucatan peninsula and all are located within a short distance from
Playa del Carmen. There are many tours to the ruins run by local tour
offices, you can also rent a car, take a bus, or take a coletivo. If
you are interested in taking a tour it is advisable to book a
reservation through your hotel or tour office in advance.

**Xcaret**

Xcaret, also known as the Mayan Eco Disney World, is a fun filled
family park. It is easy to spend a whole day here. Don't miss the
butterfly pavillion, and the tradition Mexican dance and music. Also,
bring your swim gear cause the swimming here is excellente!

**Xel-Ha **

Xel-Ha is for the diving and snorkeling enthusiast. Xel-Ha is a
fresh water bay where many sea creatures set up home. The not so
advanced snorkeler will feel safe here as there are lifeguards on duty.

**Playcar Golf Course**

Playacar is the spot for the Golfer in your group. The excellent 18
hole golf course next to the Playacar Palace Resort is excellent. The
Xaman-Ha Aviary is also located in Playacar and is home to many exotic
birds.

**Underwater Caves**

Hohoch Jungle Crossing is not to be missed! Venture deep into the
tropical jungle and snorkel through one of the largest underwater cave
systems in the world.
