There are a great many things to see and do in the countryside surrounding San Cristobal that can be easily reached in a day trip, including the beautiful Lagos de Montebello, the stunning Sumidero Cañon and the fascinating indigenous Maya villages of San Juan Chamula and Zinacantan. San Cristobal is filled with tour operators that will be happy to book a tour for you.

### [Cielo y Tierra Tours](/northamerica/mexico/sancristobaldelascasas/day_trips/cielo_y_tierra_tou)

If you're looking for a unique and memorable experience, Cielo y Tierra Tours specializes in cultural day trips in the beautiful Maya highlands of Chiapas.

This small, friendly tour company offers half-day trips to the indigenous Maya villages of San Juan Chamula and Zinacantan, as well as some of the more remote villages, well-known for their colorful markets and festivals and magnificent handwoven textiles.
