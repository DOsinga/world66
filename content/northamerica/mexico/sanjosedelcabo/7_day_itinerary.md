---
title: "7 Day Itinerary in San Jose del Cabo"
type: section
---

Originally discovered and founded by the Jesuit Missionaries in 1730, San Jose del Cabo has today soared to the league of top holiday destinations around the world due to its rich marine ecology, beautiful beaches and abundant natural beauty. Here is a well-planned seven day itinerary to help tourists explore the diverse beauty of this chic beach resort.

**Day 1- Chichein Lzai**

For a first-hand account of ancient Mexican civilization and culture, visit Chichein Lzai-a place full of old churches and ancient palaces. Visitors can enjoy a full-bodied Mexican meal at the destination in the backdrop of traditional mariachi music. In the evening catch up on a dance rendition at the Cultural Center to wind up the day-long culture fest.

**Day 2- Baja**

Take an exciting and adventurous Baja ride to enjoy the experience of an exhilarating desert drive. Visitors can take off for the three hour long whale watching water tour that concludes at the Baja Peninsula. There are also a few other expeditions that tourists can try, including kayaking, snorkeling and speed boat riding.

**Day 3- Spa Magic**

Take off for the famous Pueblo Bonito Sunset Beach Resort Spa in the backdrop of the tranquil Pacific Ocean. The expert massuer's offer several oil treatments and rejuvination packages along with a special therapy 'Shiroabhyanga' massage, a mixture of several ayurvedic techniques. After a morning at the spa, head to the southern tip of Baja for the famous rock cluster formations and the eroded 'Arch of Poseidon' passageway. Several sea lions can be observed near the 'Los Frailes' rock islets.

**Day 4- Bird Sanctuary**

Visit the San Jose Estuary and Bird Sanctuary and enjoy the sights of a large variety of indigenous and migratory aerials gracing the park. There are over 250 species of birds, including the exotic and rare egrets, brown pelicans and the pink-beaked herons. In the evening, visitors can head for a beautiful sunset cruise aboard a luxurious party catamaran.

**Day 5- Horse Ride through Baja**

Ride a horse to explore the wild, desert beauty of the Arroyo San Carlos region and the Venado Blanco belt that is interspersed with splendid rock formations and humongous canyons. There are lush hills, forests and beaches on the fascinating trail.

**Day 6- Beach Fun**

Spend a day at the famed beaches of San Jose. Start with the white-sanded and strangely christened Divorce Beach to relax and sunbathe on the clear coastal bed. Move to the eastern belt of the sprawling harbor for the popular Cabo San Lucas Beach. Visitors can go swimming, skiing, boating, kayaking and parasailing through the beach that stretches right up to the Villa del Palmer. Enjoy a session of beach volleyball or laze around in the warm glow of the Mexican sun.

**Day 7-Shopping!**

Take the day off from sightseeing to pick up unique wooden artifacts and pewter objects that are some of the famous souvenirs available in the region along with some fine pieces of ethnic jewelry, vanilla produce and several designer apparels. Several exclusive labels are avialable at duty free prices. Visitors can also enjoy the fresh pastries and ice-creams sold at local sidewalk cafes and parlors.

San Jose beckons visitors with its clear, sandy beaches; water sports activities, natural parks and a rich marine heritage.
