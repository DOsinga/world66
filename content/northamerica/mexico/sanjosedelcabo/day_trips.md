---
title: "Day Trips in San Jose del Cabo"
type: section
---

San Jose del Cabo is a charming and laidback little town situated in Baja California Sur, Mexico, separated from mainland Mexico by the Sea of Cortez. This provincial paradise is within close proximity to the Los Cabos airport and is a choice destination for those who want to unwind and witness the beauty of Mexico. Listed below are fabulous day trips that can be made from San Jose del Cabo.

**Miraflores**

Miraflores is a picturesque village known for excellent leather products and furniture handicrafts, located about 25 miles north of San Jose del Cabo. Tourists with half a day to spare may want to take in some of the local history that Miraflores has to offer, purchase a belt, wallet or saddle and then head off to Santiago for lunch.

**Santiago**

Farther down the highway from Miraflores is Santiago, which is a small town full of lush orchards and colorful farms. This area is also home to the only zoo that can be found in the southern Baja region. If their schedule permits, tourists may want to go on a day hike into the mountains and take in the spectacular view.

**El Chorro Hot Spring**

Located at the base of the Agua Caliente Canyon, El Chorro Hot Spring is the perfect place to go to for a soothing dip in natural, soft waters. Visitors may also opt to go on a relaxing hike up to the canyon, where they will stumble upon a number of tiny waterfalls and isolated pools.

**Buena Vista – Los Barriles**

Around 45 miles north of San Jose del Cabo are Buena Vista and Los Barriles, the twin coastal towns that make up the East Cape. This area is an extremely popular spot for windsurfing, surfing, and sports fishing but visitors must remember to bring their own gear. Mile after mile of serene beaches are what draw locals and tourists alike to this part of the East Cape. With the help of a local guide, tourists can also discover nearby cave paintings and hike up the mountains of Sierra de La Laguna.

**San Bartolo
**

San Bartolo, approximately 10 miles north of Buena Vista – Los Barriles, is a lovely tropical village. It is a great place for tourists to buy fresh fruits such as avocados and mangoes, local candies and other local delicacies such as empanadas.

Tourists looking for a vacation to remember should definitely consider taking day trips from San Jose del Cabo. Vacationers can bask under gorgeous weather, bathe in beautiful blue and green waters, and experience the charm of traditional Mexico.
