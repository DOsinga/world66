title: "Family Travel Ideas in San Jose del Cabo"
type: section
---

Many visitors refer to both Cabo San Lucas and San Jose del Cabo as
“Cabo,” but the 2 could be no more different; when looking for a
destination for your next vacation, consider the many child-oriented **San
Jose del Cabo family travel **options. While the southern Cabo San
Lucas is teeming with rich nightlife and raucous entertainment, the
northern San Jose del Cabo is a perfect destination for a relaxing and
memorable family vacation. Packed with cliffs and enclaves along the
scenic coastline of the Pacific Ocean, there are many activities and
sights to keep you and your family entertained throughout your stay.

**Family Outdoor Activities**

San Jose del Cabo is home to a complete range of outdoor activities
that will intrigue just about anyone’s tastes. From water sports to land
sports to even extreme sports, there are countless ways to spend your
vacation outdoors with your family.

Whale watching and snorkeling are great activities that can be
enjoyed by children as young as 4. The Dolphin Center is also a great
kid-pleaser where both kids and adults can join in a 1-hour swim with the
dolphins. Older children may be interested in scuba diving or
parasailing during their stay, both very safe and suitable for children
over 12.

Fishing is also a great family activity in San Jose del Cabo.
Depending on your family’s interest and budget, there are numerous
rental options here that can have you out sailing at dawn to catch some
amazing fish.

There are many hikes to picturesque waterfalls and other natural
sites that are suitable for young children through to adults. Ask at
your hotel or tour operator to arrange a trip suitable to the ages of
your children.

**Family Accommodations**

An all-inclusive resort is a great option for families wishing to
really sit back and relax during their vacation. These resorts are
plentiful here and include meals, drinks and even activities bundled
into 1 package. Two favorites for family vacationers are the Crown
Plaza Resort and the Melia Cabo Real.

The Crown Plaza Resort offers many daytime activities for children as
well as an evening club for teenagers. At the Melia Cabo Real, guests
are allowed access to a beautiful and safe beach and there are
activities for children throughout the day for toddlers to 12-year-olds.

Even the hotels that are not all inclusive have a range of activities
available for children. The Westin Resort and Spa is one of the most
notable options of this variety in San Jose del Cabo, offering free
activities for kids ages 4 to 12.

San Jose del Cabo is a great family destination that can provide a
relaxing and fun-filled time for you and your family.
