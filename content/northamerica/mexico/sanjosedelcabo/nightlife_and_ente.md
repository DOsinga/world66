---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment in San Jose del Cabo"
type: section
---

****

San Jose del Cabo is a beautiful tourist destination in Mexico. The
city is located in Baja California Sur Mexico. When the sun sets on
this beautiful place, San Jose del Cabo nightlife starts getting heated
up. There are several places where you can enjoy margaritas, tequila,
dancing, partying and much more. Being popular with tourist the
entertainment and partying goes on right through the night and into the
early hours of the morning. The following are a few places where you
can feel the rhythm San Jose del Cabo’s nightlife.

**Los Amigos Smoke Shop & Cigar Bar**

San Jose del Cabo is a quiet place compared to its noisy neighbor
Cabo San Lucas. Nevertheless, there are a few places like Los Amigos
Smoke Shop around. This is one of the classy places where you can
comfortably enjoy a cigar with a full glass of Tequila, scotch or any
other drink. You can also enjoy some custom made coffee at Los Amigos.
The special roasted coffee available here is one of the best in San
Jose del Cabo. This bar is also popular with those in their 40s and
upward.

**Iguana Bar & Grill**

Iguana Bar and Grill is another fine nightlife spot in San Jose del
Cabo. The place is a popular hangout for those who like to enjoy their
grill over some Tequila and good Mexican music. The bar is ideally
located in Mijares Blvd., and is a good place to spend the evening. It
is a hot spot for the young and trendy.

**La Santa Wine Bar**

Sit back and enjoy some fine wine at this wine bar located in
downtown San Jose del Cabo. The bar is a classy cavern with a wine list
of almost all the best wines. The bar also serves some light food and
desserts. Electronic music is the popular style of music played at the
bar.

**Red**

Red is located right is the middle of the hotel zone in San Jose del
Cabo. This is a classic martini bar that offers its visitors a chance
to enjoy their martini together with some special wood-fired pizzas.
The bar also features DJ music and is a nice hangout for those looking
for a “not too wild” place.

**Tropicana**

Tropicana is open from Tuesday to Sunday 7 a.m. to 2 a.m. If you are
looking for a place to enjoy a few drinks to some live Mariachi music,
Tropicana is the place to be. The bar also features Cuban and Mexican
music on weekends from 9:30 p.m. to 1 a.m. The bar is a popular spot
for those in their 30s.
