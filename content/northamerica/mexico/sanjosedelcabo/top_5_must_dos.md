---
title: "Top 5 Must Do's in San Jose del Cabo"
type: section
---

If you are looking for quiet old world charm coupled with all modern day comforts, San Jose Del Cabo is the place to head for. Several pristine beaches invite those in a mood to relax while loads of exciting water sports await the more adventurously inclined and what’s more, a unique fishing experience awaits for those who may be pros as well as for the lay ‘fisherman’. If you play golf, this is the right destination. Then there’s a paradise waiting for the bird- watchers. And for shoppers--rest assured you will not go back empty handed. These are the five must do’s when you visit San Jose del Cabo :

**1.Beaches, Beaches and Beaches**

Costa Azul, the shimmering sands which stretch as far as the eye can see, is the most popular beach. On its west side you will find San Jose’s surfing breaks, another favorite destination. To its east is La Playita, famed for its panga boats which can be used for sightseeing as well as fishing. However, East Cape is the ultimate beach destination for those seeking miles and miles of privacy and a tryst with unspoiled seashores.
And while on the seashore, what is better than hauling a marine catch? Bottomfish, dorado, marlin, roosterfish, sailfish, surf fish, tuna, and wahoo--the list is endless. The variety of fish is mind boggling and will tempt even the most reluctant tourist to have a go at fishing--and tourists sure won’t be disappointed. That’s the least to be said about sport fishing in San Jose.

**2.Golf, here I come!
**

San Jose is famed for its gold courses which measure up to the best in the world. And the climate is golf-friendly all through the year.

**3.Water sports – what a sport!**

Glass bottom boat cruises, parasailing, scuba diving, sea kayaking, snorkeling, surfing, swimming, wave runners, wind surfing and whale watching let you have your fill and get the adrenalin rushing through your veins.

**4.Bird Watchers’ Paradise**

Be ready to let your eyes pop out at the sight of an incredible number of birds, and to compete with the aerials for your attention are the marines--fish, turtle and mammals of the sea. Be sure to find both types in abundance in a protected estuary south east of the city.

**
5.Shopping Spree, Duty Free!
**

What to shop for when you are looking to take back mementos? Designer sportswear, hand painted clothes, jewelry, pewter objects, vanilla, wood carvings--all at duty free prices!

Want more? How about horseback riding? That’s one more tourist attraction. All in all, San Jose is the dream destination for you to take the family on your next holiday, for it has almost everything for nearly everyone!
