**Discover the Best Transportation in San Luis Potosi**

Once you arrive in **San Luis Potosi, transportation** is something that needs to be figured out. In San Luis Potosi there are a variety of different ways that you can get around town. The methods that you use to get around town are going to include buses, taxis, and trolleys or walking. How you opt to get around town is going to depend on how much money you are willing to spend, plus where you plan on going.

**Public Transportation**

Taking any type of public transportation is going to cost money, but luckily, nothing is very expensive. The type of public transportation you opt to take is going to vary based on where you plan on going. For example, if you plan to visit Mexico City you will want to take a bus to reach your destination. If you are simply trying to get around town you can take one of the cities trolleys for fairly cheap. Taxis are also another great method for traveling around San Luis Potosi, but will often cost more because you have to pay the fare and tip the driver.

**Walking**

Walking is actually the most convenient way to travel around San Luis Potosi because of how easy it is to reach most attractions by foot. However, just keep in mind that you will not be able to walk all over town, so you are going to have to rely on public transportation for the further distances.
