---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment in San Luis Potosi"
type: section
---

**San Luis Potosi nightlife **has various bars, clubs and restaurants where you can enjoy your night out.

**Avenida Venustiano Carranza**

This trendy bar found on Venustiano Carranza Avenue has a very spacious lounge with the right lighting and furnishing. Here you can enjoy your drink while listening to music by the resident disc jockey. Here you will find all kinds of beers, shots, cocktails, wines and house specials. You can also get something to eat from the kitchen.

**El Mexico de Frida**

San Luis Potosi nightlife should include sampling Mexican food. Visit this elegant restaurant where you will enjoy freshly cooked dishes. Try out the famous tortillas and some spicy side dishes. You will also get a chance to meet many people as this is a popular eating place during the night. It is located on Valentin Gama Street.

**Cafe del Teatro**

For music and food, make a point of visiting this cafe. You will get the chance to listen to all kinds of live music from 9 a.m. during the weekends after having taken your meal. Let the night fade away as you have your drink to the sound of your favorite tunes. The cafe charges an entrance fee of 60 pesos.

**Staff**

Dancing is part of San Luis Potosi nightlife, so make a point of heading to this club located on Carranza Street. You will find all the latest Latina music here. The crowd is very exciting and the DJ usually keeps everyone on his or her feet. The bar area is spacious so if you feel like just watching and having your drink, this is just the place to do that.

**La Cantina**

For the true feeling of what kind of entertainment goes on in a Mexican cantina, visit this place. There is a band that performs all kinds of music from modern to classics. Enjoy your tequila shots, beer or cocktails at the bar while listening to Latin tunes. The staff makes you feel as if you are going back in time with their traditional costumes and sombreros. They also provide local delicacies on their menu.

**Sutton Club
**

San Luis Potosi nightlife is not complete without your going to this state of the art discotheque. Located in the Holiday Inn, this club has all those special effects and sound equipment found in world class establishments. You will really enjoy yourself here. The dance floor is spacious and the bar area very cozy. This is the perfect place to spend your Saturday night from 10 p.m. until very late in the night.
