**How to Manage in San Luis Potosi**

While you are visiting **San Luis Potosi practical information** can be useful because it can help you with planning your trip. Practical information can also help prepare you for handling any emergencies that might come up, but the information can also help keep you safe in a country you are not familiar with.

**Staying Safe**

One of the biggest concerns that people have when it comes to visiting a foreign country is whether it is safe to go out at night or not. Unlike some of its neighboring cities San Luis Potosi is considered a safe city. However, like many other cities around the world it is not recommended for women to go into the seedy areas of town alone, especially at night. If you are aware of your surroundings and stay away from places that look dangerous you will be perfectly safe traveling the city at night.

**Currency**

Since San Luis Potosi is one of the larger cities in Mexico, they accept more than just pesos in terms of currency. While pesos are the main currency used throughout Mexico the larger cities also accept American money, as well as most major credit cards. San Luis Potosi has ATMs located throughout the city that tourists can access, but you must have a valid ATM or credit card. If you need to exchange money for the correct currency you can do it at any bank located in the city.
