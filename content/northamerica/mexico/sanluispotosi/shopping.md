**San Luis Potosi shopping** will give you a chance to take home some Mexican gifts or souvenirs.

**Fonart**

For an all-purpose San Luis Potosi shopping, this is the store to go to. It is a store that has all that you need to purchase. It is located at Morelos street. Operating time is 9 a.m. until 2 p.m. and 4 p.m. up to 7 p.m. during weekdays. Saturdays, the place is open from 10 a.m. until 5 p.m.

**La Casa del Artesano**

You will find this store along Jardin Colon Street. It is owned by the government and sells all kinds of crafts that are made in Mexico. It is the perfect place to get your family and friends some presents and souvenirs for yourself. The place opens at 10 a.m. and closes at 8 p.m. Mondays to Fridays. Saturdays it is open until 5 p.m.

**Mercado Hidalgo**

San Luis Potosi shopping should include going to this place where you will buy all kinds of Mexican foods. You will get fresh fruits, pastries and the local cuisine. Other items for sale include: handmade containers, clothing and furnishings made from straw. You will find all kinds of things like shoes, handicrafts, provisions and hardware in this building. Nearby are various inns where you can enjoy a drink.

**Mercado Republica**

Also a huge market, you will get all kinds of Mexican memorabilia here. Buy a sombrero hat, silver accessories, blankets, handicrafts, pareos, vanilla, ties and dyed t-shirts and tequila. You** **can also have a local meal or snack from the various stalls.

**Constanzo Chocolates
**

Another favorite San Luis Potosi shopping pastime is getting the local candy made by this well-known factory. You will find their shops all over the city and they are open six days a week until late at night. You can also purchase some to take home to your friends.

**Plaza Tangamanga**

This is a mall found in the heart of the city center where you can get most of the designer clothes and accessories. Many items can be found here. If you need to get a meal, there are various restaurants offering a variety on foods. Children can enjoy themselves in the game mall. If you need to watch a movie, there is also a cinema hall available.

**Downtown Stores**

This is the ultimate San Luis Potosi shopping experience. The city is surrounded by mineral rich areas, so in this area you will find all kinds of precious jewelery on sale. You will also find carvings, hand crafts, paintings, dolls, metallic gifts, purses and wallets. For book lovers, you will find exceptional literature and old books on sale.
