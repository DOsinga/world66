---
title: "Top 5 Must Do's in San Luis Potosi"
type: section
---

**San Luis Potosi must do's** are quite many and you will love visiting this Mexican city.

**Downtown**

San Luis Potosi must-do's should start with going round the city to see the various sites. Start with going to the Plaza de Armas which was constructed during the colonial times. Other buildings of interest include the cathedral and Gorbierno Palace. Head to the Temple of Carmen which is a very important site. There is also a small church (Camarin de la Virgen) which is painted golden. If you are lucky to be here during the festivities in December, you may get a chance to witness a ceremony where birds of all kinds are released by the locals. A stroll along the streets will also allow you buy souvenirs to take back home.

**Tangamanga Park**

A visit to this aquatic park will allow you to have a wonderful time. There is an observation post where you can view the surrounding natural beauty. Within the park is a waterfall that has a pool where you can enjoy a swim during a hot day. This park also offers facilities like hiking, nature walks and sports areas where everyone can enjoy themselves. An art museum and theaters are also located within the park.

**National Museum of the Mask**

Another San Luis Potosi must-do is going to this museum to see displays of masks that are famous worldwide. Created and hand painted by artists during the olden days, you will be amazed at how creative these people were. The collected works are among the largest in the whole world.

**Holiday Inn**

This hotel has a lot of exciting things to offer during your visit to this city. It has a gardens where you can stroll and take memorable pictures. Children are also provided with a fully-equipped playground. Have a relaxing swim in the heated pool or have a massage in the spa. For some lunch, the Maravedi cafeteria will cater to your every need with meals from a world-class chef. Enjoy delicious grilled meats and salads at the Escudeiro restaurant in the evening. For your night entertainment, the Centro Nocturno El Malandante center has some variety shows. If you love dancing, then the Sutton Club is the place to be at night.

**Gogorron**

Your final San Luis Potosi must-do is visiting the area's world famous spa. Here you will find thermal water that is said to have healing qualities. There is also a holiday camp where you can enjoy your meals, take a swim or just relax in Roman baths.
