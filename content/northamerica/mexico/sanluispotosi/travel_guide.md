This **San Luis Potosi Travel Guide **shows why this is one of the affluent cities situated in Mexico. This is a primordial city renowned due to its mines of gold, silver and other metals. The modern city, however, enjoys all the luxuries, methods of transportation, plazas, huge buildings and over one million residents. This San Luis Potosi Travel Guide is to bestow an idea about the modern city, its important places, important hotels and restaurants that are required to be known by travelers.

#### The Westin Hotel

The Westin in San Luis Potosi is the spectacular hotel which acquires three stars in ranking. It is situated at Avenida Real De Lomas #1000. An easy way to reach to this hotel is via shuttle system which carries the travelers to the hotel from the airport.

This hotel is furnished with broad band Internet connection or Wi-Fi convenient for businessmen and travelers.

#### La Parroquia Restaurant

This restaurant is situated at Av Carranza 303 Centro, San Luis Potos&iacute;. It is a very famous restaurant situated at Fundadores Square in the center of the city. This is an old traditional restaurant which provides marvelous food. A special dish of this restaurant is Enchiladas Potosinas which is very famous among the locals.

#### Plaza de Armas

This plaza is situated at Centro Historico San Luis Potos&iacute; and is bounded by pedestrian streets only. The main pedestrian streets are Hidalgo and Zaragoza, which extends to Jardin Colon. This plaza hosts city&rsquo;s cathedral,l Cathedral Potosina.

#### Palacio de Gobiemo

The street address of this plaza is Jard&iacute;n Hidalgo, #11, San Luis Potos&iacute;. It is situated near to Plaza de Armas on the street of Zaragoza. The Palacio de Gobierno was built in between the 17th and 18th centuries and possesses memorable items inside such as historical objects and statues.

#### Casa de la Cultura

The street address of this place is Av. V. Carranza 1815, San Luis Potosi, Mexico. It was developed in early 20th century and it is the center of cultural events like painting, folk and archaeological objects. It has a new classical building which has gardens, and tracks for walking. There have been displays of art, music concerts, sports and films on cinema inside it.
