**Pick a Date and Travel to San Luis Potosi**

When planning your itinerary for a trip to **San Luis Potosi**,** when to go** is an aspect you'll have to consider. Located at the northern part of Mexico City, San Luis Potosi is a getaway within Mexico. The city is rich in architecture and is a major tourist destination adorned with beautifully landscaped gardens. The sights here are a treat to the eyes. The Cathedrals, beaches and museum add to the city's charm.

**Year Round Good Weather
**
San Luis Potosi can be visited any time of the year. The weather in this part of Mexico is inviting all year round. Unlike other parts of Mexico, the summer months between May to September are not faced with extreme heat and humidity. As the city lies elevated at 6,200 feet, even the summers are temperate relative to the rest of Mexico.

**Tourist Season**

Even though it is a year round holiday destination, it has its peak tourist seasons. The months between July and August is when San Luis Potosi witnesses an influx of tourists. Even the locals flock to this destination during these months. The city is buzzing with activity and the atmosphere is charged up. If you wish to avoid the crowd that comes with the tourist season, you can visit San Luis Potosi during October to May. The city goes into a sweet slumber, but you can enjoy the attractions minus the hustle and bustle. The only disadvantage of traveling during this period is that you might miss out on the various street performances and other activities that are held during the tourist season.
