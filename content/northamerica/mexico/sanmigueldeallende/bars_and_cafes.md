type: section
---

If you are going to be traveling to Mexico and want to enjoy some of the best dining in the area, you can enjoy the **San Miguel de Allende bars and cafes**. There are many different kinds of cuisines and dishes that are available, and you can enjoy a different type of food and restaurant each time that you go out.

#### Mexican Cafes

If you are looking to sample some of the best in Mexican cuisine, there are a few cafes and restaurants that you need to try when you are enjoying the San Miguel de Allende bars and cafes. The El Pegaso is an eclectic mix of Mexican and Asian dishes and provides a fun atmosphere. The Ole Ole offers traditional fajitas that are sure to offer the traditional tastes of Mexico.

#### French and European Cuisine

If you want to enjoy some of the other types of cuisine found in the city, you can enjoy the La Buena Vide which is a caf&eacute; and bakery. You can enjoy a number of French pastries and gourmet breads. You can also go to the La Europa to enjoy the wines, Italian cheeses and imported goods from Europe.

#### Bars with Live Music

If you enjoy a night out and want to have great drinks and listen to the sounds of local musicians, there are a number of clubs and bars that you can enjoy in the Jardin Square including the Mama Mia and Agave Azul. You can also enjoy the live bands and musicians that play at Bellas Artes and go to the Bella Italia for house bands or the El Viejo Topo for many acts including jazz music.

#### Mexican Bakeries and Coffee

If you like to have a sweet treat when you are enjoying the San Miguel de Allende Bars and Cafes, you can find the Blue Door Bakery and Cafe that features traditional Mexican pastries that you can select and take with you. You can also enjoy a rich blend of coffee and enjoy the different roasts that are available to go with the pastries.

#### Themed Bars

You can find a number of restaurants and bars that specialize in themes to create a unique blend of culture and cuisine, including the La Capilla which is found alongside Parroquia and offers rooftop views, Mexican specialties and strolling musicians. You can also enjoy the Ole Ole bar for its unique bullfighting d&eacute;cor and menu items.
