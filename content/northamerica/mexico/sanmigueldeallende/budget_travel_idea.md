**Enjoying San Miguel de Allende on a Budget**

**San Miguel de Allende budget travel** is possible, but you will
need to do your research. Here are 2 things you should know when
planning a trip to San Miguel de Allende on a low budget.
**Getting There on a Budget**

Even though it is possible to drive to San Miguel de Allende, it is
not a good idea. Not only does the city have poor road infrastructure
for modern cars, but parking in the city is very expensive. It is a
better idea to fly to Leon (IATA airport code: BJX) or Mexico City (IATA
airport code: MEX) and then take a bus or shuttle straight to San
Miguel de Allende. Do not travel by taxi from the airport, because it
will be much more expensive. If you're flying from within Mexico, start
looking for airfares on smaller airlines such as Interjet and Viva
Aerobus, because they provide deeper discounts to compete with the more
established airlines like Mexicana and Aeromexico. Most Mexican discount
airlines offer email alert services for low fares and promotions.
**Save Money with Hostelling International**

Accommodation in San Miguel de Allende is limited, and it is best to
secure it before you arrive to the city. Make sure to secure your
accommodations with the lowest possible price by using your Hostelling
International membership card. Application to this network within Mexico
is limited to locals and current exchange students, so it is best to
apply for your membership card at the local office of Hostelling
International in your country of origin.
