There are plenty of **San Miguel de Allende family travel **options
for family vacationers headed towards Mexico. San Miguel is counted
among the most welcoming and culturally-progressive cities of Mexico. It
is safe for family travelers.

One of the best, family-travel options in San Miguel de Allende is to
undertake a walking tour of the city and its surrounding locales.
However, the walking tour option explained below is vastly different
from the usual, commercially-packaged tours listed in tourist guides.

**Patronato Pro Ninos Walking Tour**

A non-profit organization, Patronato Pro Ninos conducts the most
comprehensive walking tours of San Miguel de Allende. Unlike the
packaged tours, the itinerary is relaxed and elaborative. It takes
visitors along all the historic parts of San Miguel, including the
churches like Parroquia. The tour covers the entire, ten-block spread of
the town. This includes visit to:

**Jardin**

Also called the central square or the town square, it is the hub of
all activities in San Miguel. Visitors can listen to street music or sit
under the shade of trees and converse with the town’s residents.

**Tuesday Market**

This locale is usually missing from privately-operated tours. This flea market is typically Mexican and worth visiting.

**La Gruta**

Another feature limited to walking tours organized by Patronato Pro
Ninos. Visitors get to spend a few hours in a private park located along
the road leading to Dolores Hidalgo. The park has warm-water spring
pools and a canal.

**Bellas Artes**

This cultural spot regularly organizes concerts and plays. Occasionally, shows by art galleries are also organized.

**Walking Tour Schedule**

Tours are regularly scheduled on Mondays, Wednesdays and Fridays.
