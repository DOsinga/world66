---
title: "Festivals in San Miguel de Allende"
type: section
---

There are many ways to enjoy your travels to Mexico, and if you visit during the **San Miguel de Allende festivals**, you can enjoy the culture, music and entertainment of the city. There are many fun festivals held each year and if you want to visit the area, you may want to go when one of the festivals is taking place.

**Carnival Day**

The carnival day festival starts on March 5th in the city. This is a 5-day celebration where you will see dancing, floats, music and food. There are many fun activities to enjoy during the festival and a great way to enjoy the culture, dancing and music of San Miguel de Allende.

**International Music Festival**

This fun festival is held on June 21st and showcases music and performers from all over. You'll see many of the local artists and different types of music. There are also bands, dances and plenty of celebrations in the city at the festival.

**Pamplonada Festival**

This annual event involves the traditional running of the bulls festival and is held the third Saturday in September. You will get to see a wild display of people in the streets running from the bulls that are released. It is exciting to watch, and if you want to get close to the action you can get a seat on one of the nearby streets to watch the fun.

**International Jazz Festival**

The International Jazz Festival is held in November. It features jazz musicians and bands from all over. Many of the local performers participate in the event, and if you are a fan of jazz, you should check it out and enjoy some of the best music around. There are also many festive displays around the city.

**Annual Chamber Music Festival**

This music festival is held in August and it's truly a showcase of the different musicians and bands from all over. You can catch many different types of performances and entertainment at this festival. The streets are full of musicians and sounds of different beats and songs being played all across the town to enjoy.
