---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment in San Miguel de Allende"
type: section
---

The** San Miguel de Allende Nightlife** is full of flair. There are many different bars that have international themes, including Irish pubs.

**Limerick Pub**

One example of the varied nightlife of San Miguel is Limerick Pub. The address for this pub is Umaran Number 24. This pub shows live sporting events on their televisions accompanied with a variety of drinks, from martinis to Guinness beers. The atmosphere in this pub is fun because people can shoot darts and play pool. The bar opens the whole week. The schedule for the bar is as follows: Monday to Thursday 7 am to 1:30 am, Friday to Thursday 7 pm to 3:00 and Sunday 7pm to 1:30 am.

Most people like the music when they visit this pub. They play songs from the band U2 and The Cranberries. The decorations make it look like an authentic pub; there's even an Irish phone booth at the entrance of the restaurant.

#### Harry's New Orleans Cafe

This cafe is one of a kind. People can get the feeling that they are really on the French Quarters of New Orleans instead of being in San Miguel de Allende. Jazz music fills the air of this New Orleans style cafe. The food is Cajun-Creole style and is enjoyed by most tourists who visit San Miguel de Allende. This is a good place to find exquisite dishes made out of oysters, shrimp cocktails and seafood. The bar is well stocked, where people can find imported and Mexican alcoholic beverages. Tourists will love the New Orleans style atmosphere and the live music. Harry's New Orleans Cafe address is Hidalgo Number 12.

#### Restaurante Bar El Campanario

This bar is located inside an ancient mansion. The structure is more than 400 years old and it is one of the best examples of Colonial Style architecture. Restaurante Bar El Campanario offers great flavor and music to the everyday San Miguel de Allende Nightlife. This restaurant has a wide variety of pastas, soups, meats and drinks that are served with the most strict of hygiene standards. One of the best dishes of the hotel is the Sopa Azteca. The address for this restaurant is Canal Numero 34.
