---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

From Puerto Vallarta international airport, drive or bus north on Highway MEX 200 toward Compostela and Tepic. Sayulita is about 35 km (45 minutes) north of the airport. After passing through Bucerias and the turnoff to Punta Mita (bear left), the highway narrows to two lanes and winds over a small mountain with sharp curves — watch for livestock and slow vehicles. After passing through the village of San Ignacio, a small sign marks the turnoff to Sayulita. Follow the road three kilometres into town.

Buses from Puerto Vallarta's bus station run to Sayulita regularly throughout the day.
