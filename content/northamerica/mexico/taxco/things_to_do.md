Hello, I'm a US citizen and US Army veteran living in Taxco, Mexico. I fell in love with Taxco after I took my vacation here a couple years ago. There is so much to do and see! The shopping is great and the food is wonderful. the are many nice hotels including a 5 star resort with a golf course and many historical sites. The town is famous for it's silver. Shopping for silver one will be very delighted at the beutiful selection at unbelievable low prices. The Gruta (caves) of Cacauahmilpa a just outside Taxco and they are some of the best is North America. Truly a site not to be missed!! In adition to the caves there are many hiking trails around the city to water falls and beutiful natural springs. Shopping for indigeonous crafts, masks and baskets is also popular.
