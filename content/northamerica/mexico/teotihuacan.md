Travel Guide

The Teotihuacán ruins are located about 50km northeast of [Mexico City](/northamerica/mexico/mexicocity),
it takes about an hour to get from the capital to the archeological
site.

Ancient Teotihuacán was built between the first and the seventh century after Christ and was the biggest city of the continent.

La Pyrámide del Sol (Pyramid of the sun) is one of the largest pyramids
of the world, its length is about 220m and its height about 70m.
