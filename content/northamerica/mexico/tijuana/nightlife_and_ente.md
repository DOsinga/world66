"I stopped going to TJ because of what I consider higher prices than the value returned. The businesses are making money but only the owners are getting the payoffs. If it was not for the 18 year old drinking age, the place will be empty. Couiela after a few visits is expensive for what you get. You can't get a Yellow Page phone book. It is not worth the effort. Gasoline costs too much for a trip to TJ. TJ is not an adventure, it's a major undertaking."
