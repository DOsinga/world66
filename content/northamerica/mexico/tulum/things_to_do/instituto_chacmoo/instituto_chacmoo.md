Now you can study Spanish while enjoying all the good weather and activities that the Tulum area offers. **Instituto Chac-Mool Spanish School Tulum** is a sister school to the very popular Cuernavaca Instituto Chac-Mool. Private and small peer group classes are available for adults plus there are Spanish language programs for kids and teens. Available year round, you can study at the school for as many weeks as you like. And the school will arrange for you to stay with a local Mexican host family, if you like. Weekends offer guided excursions and during the week special activities like Mexican cooking classes, yoga and Latin dance lessons are available.
