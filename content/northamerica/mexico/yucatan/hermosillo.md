Hermosillo is a bit off the tourist track in Mexico but it is a nice town to visit. Great desert landscapes, good beaches neraby and a peaceful country atmosphere and some of the finest mexican food around.

One hour away by road you get to outstanding beaches. You can go to [Bahia De Kino](/northamerica/mexico/bahiadekino) for a relaxing day or two on the beach.
