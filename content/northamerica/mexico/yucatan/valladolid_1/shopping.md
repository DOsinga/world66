---
title: "Shopping in Valladolid"
type: section
---

Valladolid has one of the best Mexican Folk Art stores in Yucatan : * Yalat, *located at the northeast corner of the Main Square . This lovely boutique specializes in Mexican Masters and offers a selective collection of traditional arts and crafts. Here you will find exquisite Mayan weaves, designer jewelry, ancient Maya replicas of the highest quality. No other city in the region has anything as selective and of such taste as this small boutique shop. They accept major credit cards and do international deliveries.
