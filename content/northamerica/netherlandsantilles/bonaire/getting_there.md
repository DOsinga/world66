title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

**By air**

Flamingo Airport (BON) is 4km south of Kralendijk. There are direct flights from Amsterdam with KLM (seasonal), and connections through Curaçao, Aruba, and Miami on various carriers. Most North American visitors connect through Miami or Curaçao.

**By sea**

There are no regular passenger ferry services to Bonaire. The island is a stop on some Caribbean cruise itineraries.
