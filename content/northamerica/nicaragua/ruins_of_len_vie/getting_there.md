---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

León Viejo is about 35 km east of [León](/northamerica/nicaragua/len), near the village of Puerto Momotombo on the shore of Lake Managua. The easiest approach is by car or taxi from León; the drive takes around 45 minutes on paved road. There is no direct public bus to the ruins — from León you can take a bus toward La Paz Centro and arrange onward transport, but a hired vehicle is far more practical for a day trip.
