San Juan del Sur now has an e-magazine;[sjdsnow](/northamerica/nicaragua/sanjuandelsur/practical_informat) with Old Time Radio shows(OTR) free on the internet. The locals and new comers enjoy listening to the OTR shows to help remember the "golden days". The shows are changed regularly by a local web master.
