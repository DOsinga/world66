---
title: "Darien gap"
type: location
---

The Darien Gap is the bit of Transamerican that is missing. No road, just jungle and that's the fun of it. By canoe and by foot you can make it all the way to Colombia, from where it is possible to continue by car or public transport. 

When you want to go to Colombia and do not want to cross throught the thick of the jungle, the only alternative is going to [Colon](/northamerica/panama/colon)Colon and trying to get a boat to Porto Belo near the Colombian border from there.
