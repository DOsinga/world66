---
title: "When to Go"
type: section
---

The dry season from January to April is the best time to visit. Trails are passable, rivers are lower, and the risk of being stranded by floods or washed-out tracks is much reduced. From May to November the rains are heavy and relentless — rivers rise fast, trails become impassable, and boat travel on the rivers is more difficult. The rainy season is not impossible to navigate, but it significantly limits where you can go. December can go either way.
