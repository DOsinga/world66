---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment in Panama City"
type: section
---

These **Panama City nightlife** venues are sure to give you an
exciting holiday here in Panama City. These popular nightspots offer
great food, good drinks and good music.

**Club La Vela**

Club La Vela is the largest, trendiest and most respected nightclub
here in Panama City. The club is a great place for parties and events, with special theme rooms that offer an extraordinary atmosphere as well as great
music and light shows. The latest addition in its growing theme rooms is
the Thunderdome. The Rock Arena is one place in the club that serves as
concert venue for popular bands. The Underground and Night Gallery are
the rooms where you can dance to the beat of famous DJs. Club La Vela is
located on the white sand beaches of Panama City, and has been the
official venue for MTV’s Spring Break since 1996.

**Harpoon Harry's**

Harpoon Harry's is situated on the beach front and offers a unique
experience. Many visitors are captured by Harpoon Harry's great food and its wide
selection of alcoholic drinks. If you are looking for a great place to
unwind and enjoy the night, Harpoon Harry’s is the right place to be. Here you can dance all night and have a good time.

**Boatyard Club**

This is a wonderful hot spot for locals and tourists alike. Boatyard
Club is located in Panama City’s Grand Lagoon, in North Lagoon Drive.
Boatyard has a laid back atmosphere with bright-colored and stylish
furniture. If you are looking for a place where you can enjoy a good
night out, Boatyard is the perfect place for you. The club serves
Caribbean dishes such as seasoned Mahi Mahi and shrimp. But the real buzz is
about the fresh oysters and sushi. The club is open till midnight on weeknights and
until 2 a.m. during the weekends.

**Schooner's**

Schooner’s is a one-stop nightspot that hosts special events and
great beach parties. Schooner's is one of the best places to enjoy the
night out in Panama City. If you are longing for a night filled with
great music, dancing and excellent food, step into Schooner's and join the party. Schooners is known as the “last local beach club” in
Panama City, and it offers a great view of the sunset as well as sumptuous
lunch and dinner.

**Spinnaker Beach Club**

This is another not-to-miss top beach club in Panama City. Spinnaker
is situated in Thomas Drive. The
club has live bands that performance in the open-air deck. If you want
to dance the night away, this is one of the places you can do that
and more. With great food, excellent music and a great atmosphere, Spinnaker
Beach Club has it all.
