---
title: "Beaches"
type: section
---

Ponce is not primarily a beach town, but La Guancha on the southern waterfront is worth a visit. The boardwalk has outdoor cafes and the public beach is good for an easy swim. For serious beach time, the offshore nature reserve island of Caja de Muertos — about 8 miles out — has clear water and good snorkelling over coral reefs, with nurse sharks and eagle rays common sights. Snorkelling excursions depart from the waterfront.
