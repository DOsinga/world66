The Spanish Revival mansion built in the 1930s by the Serrallés family, founders of the Don Q rum distillery. Set on El Vigia hill with panoramic views over Ponce. Tours of the mansion convey life in a wealthy colonial household; the grounds include a Japanese garden and a small Butterfly Garden.
