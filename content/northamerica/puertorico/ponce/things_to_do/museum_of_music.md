Traces the rich musical history of the island through memorabilia of famous musicians and displays of indigenous Taíno, Spanish, and African musical instruments. Housed in a restored colonial building near the plaza, it is a quick but worthwhile visit for anyone interested in how Puerto Rico's musical traditions developed.
