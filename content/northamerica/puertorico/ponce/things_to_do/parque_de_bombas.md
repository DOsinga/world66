A red-and-black striped firehouse built in 1883 that has become one of Puerto Rico's most photographed buildings. Originally built for the 1882 Exhibition Fair, it served as the city's main fire station for over a century. Now a small museum, it sits right on Plaza las Delicias and is free to enter.
