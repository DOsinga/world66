The main square of Ponce is one of the loveliest in Puerto Rico, lined with trees and fountains. The illuminated Fountain of the Lions and the distinctive red-and-black Parque de Bombas firehouse (1883) define its character. Our Lady of Guadalupe Cathedral stands on the northern edge.
