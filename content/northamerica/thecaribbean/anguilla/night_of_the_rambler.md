A novel about the 1967 revolution — the rebellion in which Anguillans expelled their Kittitian administrators and demanded the right to remain a British territory. Kobbe writes with the sprawling energy of the Latin American tradition about an event that deserves to be better known. An excellent book to read on the ferry from St. Martin.
