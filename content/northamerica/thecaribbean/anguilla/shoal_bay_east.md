The beach most visitors come for. A long crescent of powdery white sand backed by sea grape trees, with clear, shallow water ranging from pale turquoise to deep blue. You can rent chairs and umbrellas, snorkel just offshore, and eat at a handful of casual beach bars. It is the island's most famous beach for good reason.
