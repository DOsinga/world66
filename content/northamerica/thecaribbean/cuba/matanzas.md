Matanzas is the capital of Matanzas province, about 90 kilometers east of Havana and 30 minutes from [Varadero](/northamerica/thecaribbean/cuba/varadero). The city sits on a deep bay where three rivers meet and was once a prosperous sugar port; its 19th-century wealth is still visible in the Teatro Sauto, one of the finest small opera houses in the Americas.

Matanzas is also the cradle of several Afro-Cuban musical forms — danzón, rumba, and the conga de comparsa all have strong roots here. The city has an active cultural life and is worth a stop between Havana and Varadero.
