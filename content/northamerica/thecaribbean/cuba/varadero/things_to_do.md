---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Varadero's beach — 20 km of white sand with calm Caribbean water — is the reason most people come and the place where most time is spent. Beyond the beach, water activities include scuba diving, dolphin encounters, glass-bottom boat trips, and fishing. Parque Josone, a shaded park in the centre of town, has gardens, a small lake, restaurants, and food stands — a good break from the beach heat. The Parque Ecológico Varahicacos at the eastern end of the peninsula has caves with indigenous pictographs and walking trails through scrubby coastal forest. For the genuinely adventurous, the Centro Internacional de Deportes Aéreos offers parachute jumps and ultralight flights over the beach.
