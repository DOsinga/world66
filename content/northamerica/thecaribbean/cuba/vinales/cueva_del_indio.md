title: Cueva del Indio

A cave 5.5 km north of the village, through which a small underground river flows. Visitors walk through the illuminated cavern and then board a motorboat for a 400-metre ride along the river before emerging back into daylight. It is unabashedly touristy — souvenir sellers await at the exit — but the cave itself is genuinely impressive, with good stalactite formations and the novelty of a subterranean boat ride.
