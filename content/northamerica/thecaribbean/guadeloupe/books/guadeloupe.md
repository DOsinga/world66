A French-language travel guide to Guadeloupe from Ulysses Travel Guides. Covers the main islands with practical information and color photography. Useful primarily for francophone travelers; English-language guidebook coverage of Guadeloupe is limited, so this remains a solid option for readers comfortable in French.
