type: poi

One of the finest art collections in the Caribbean, housed in a striking modernist building designed by Edward Durell Stone. The permanent collection includes works by Velázquez, Rubens, and Rodin, alongside an extensive collection of Puerto Rican art. Well worth a couple of hours.
