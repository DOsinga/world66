A Spanish Revival mansion built in the 1930s by the Serrallés family, founders of Don Q rum. You can tour the mansion and its grounds, which give a vivid impression of how a wealthy colonial family lived. The hilltop setting offers panoramic views over Ponce and the Caribbean coast. Nearby, the Vigia Cross is a concrete structure commemorating an old Spanish watchtower — worth the short walk for the view.
