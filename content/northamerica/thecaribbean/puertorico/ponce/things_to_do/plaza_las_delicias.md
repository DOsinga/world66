title: "Plaza las Delicias"
type: poi

latitude: 18.0123

---

The main square of Ponce and one of the loveliest in Puerto Rico, with its lighted Fountain of the Lions at the centre. The plaza is flanked by the Cathedral of Our Lady of Guadalupe and the iconic Parque de Bombas firehouse. It is the natural starting point for exploring the old city.
