There is a regular car and passenger ferry service linking [Port of Spain](/northamerica/trinidadandtobago/port_of_spain) in Trinidad with Scarborough in Tobago. The crossing takes roughly two and a half hours and can be rough due to strong currents between the islands. Check the Trinidad and Tobago ferry authority website for current schedules and fares.
