title: Flying Between Trinidad and Tobago
type: poi

Caribbean Airlines operates several daily flights between Trinidad's Piarco International Airport near Port of Spain and Tobago's A.N.R. Robinson International Airport at Crown Point. The flight takes about 20 minutes. Fares vary but expect to pay around 300-500 TTD one way. Book ahead during peak season (Carnival and school holidays) as flights fill up fast. The airport at Crown Point is small and straightforward, with taxis readily available outside.
