Pine Cay is a small private island between Providenciales and North Caicos, accessible only to guests of the Meridian Club, a low-key resort that has been operating here since the 1970s. There are no day-trippers, no day visitors, and no public ferry.

The island is covered in casuarina pines and ringed with an almost entirely deserted beach. The surrounding waters are part of a nature reserve, and the snorkelling and diving are excellent. Flamingos visit regularly from North Caicos.

If you are not staying at the Meridian Club, Pine Cay is essentially off limits.
