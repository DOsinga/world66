Birmingham's identity is shaped by two forces: the civil rights movement that changed America, and the iron and steel industry that built the city. The Civil Rights District downtown -- the institute, the church, Kelly Ingram Park -- is the essential stop. Beyond that, Vulcan Park offers the best views, and Railroad Park is a good place to take a break downtown.
