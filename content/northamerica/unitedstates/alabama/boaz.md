Boaz is a small town in northern Alabama near Lake Guntersville and Guntersville State Park. It calls itself a shopper's paradise, and with good reason -- hundreds of factory outlet stores draw bargain hunters from across the region. If you are in the area for the lake or the park, the outlet shopping is a convenient bonus.
