Travel Guide

Dauphin Island is a beautiful barrier island in the Gulf of Mexico,
just off the coast. Dauphin Island is home to the Civil War Era
Fort Gaines which is open to the public, and the Estuarium, an aquarium
facility open to the public featuring sea life from the area.
