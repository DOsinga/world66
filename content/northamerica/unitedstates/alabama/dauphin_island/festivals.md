The Dauphin Island Marina is a full service marina located minutes from the Gulf of Mexico at the mouth of Mobile Bay. Set inside a sheltered bay, the Marina is safe and secure for traveling yachtsmen and fishermen, and the perfect spot to launch for day trips in and around the Gulf.
For more information, please contact the Dauphin Island Marina at 251-861-8707 or visit www.dauphinislandmarina.com. Dauphin Island is just 33 miles south of Mobile, Alabama at the end of the Dauphin Island Parkway. With its miles of white sand beach, shaded hiking and biking trails, fishing, island shopping and dining, and accommodations for any budget, Dauphin Island is Alabama’s Family Beach ®.
