---
title: 'Festivals'
type: section
---

The **Alabama Deep Sea Fishing Rodeo**, held in July at the Dauphin Island Marina, is one of the largest fishing tournaments on the Gulf Coast. Run by the Mobile Jaycees, it draws anglers from across the country.

The marina hosts over two dozen fishing tournaments between May and September, including the **World Championship Red Snapper Tournament** running through the summer months, kids' fishing events, and various specialty rodeos.

The **spring bird migration** from April through May is not technically a festival, but it functions as one. Birders from across the country descend on the island to witness the trans-Gulf migration spectacle, and the community embraces the annual influx with the same enthusiasm as any organized event.
