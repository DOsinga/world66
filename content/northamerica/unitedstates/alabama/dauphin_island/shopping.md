---
title: 'Shopping'
type: section
---

Dauphin Island shopping is concentrated along Bienville Boulevard on the west end. **The Ancient Mariner** is the classic beachside souvenir shop -- shells, flip-flops, swim suits, Guy Harvey fishing shirts, and Dauphin Island caps with designs found only here. They also rent bikes, which is a pleasant way to explore the island.

**The Sand Box** is a nautically themed gift shop full of beach decor and a unique line of nautical Christmas ornaments. **Marti's of Dauphin Island** carries clothing and accessories curated by a former New York model -- higher-end pieces you will not find elsewhere in southern Alabama.

**1st Run Video** carries used books for beach reading, handcrafted driftwood writing instruments made from surf-smoothed materials, and Sand Island Lighthouse merchandise with proceeds supporting the lighthouse restoration.
