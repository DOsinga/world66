---
title: 'Nightlife and Entertainment'
type: section
---

The Shoals area nightlife reflects the region's deep musical roots. Concerts and clubs feature a mix of country, Southern rock, Celtic, jazz, blues, and classical music. Several local restaurants and art galleries host live entertainment regularly.

**The Zodiac Play House** (416 N Court Street) is home to the Zodiac Players, a community theatre company staging around five productions per season -- musicals, comedy, and drama. The University of North Alabama's strong theatre department provides a steady stream of talent. Notable former members include actor George "Goober" Lindsey and Alabama Senator Howell Heflin.

The Shoals area has three active theatre groups, and the university and local organizations bring in touring performers throughout the year.
