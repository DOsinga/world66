The Zodiac Players are a community theatre company staging around five productions per season, including musicals, comedy, and drama. The University of North Alabama's strong theatre department ensures a steady pool of talent, and many students and graduates perform here. Notable former members include actor George "Goober" Lindsey and Alabama Senator Howell Heflin.
