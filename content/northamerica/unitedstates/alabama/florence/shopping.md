---
title: 'Shopping'
type: section
---

Florence offers several distinct shopping areas. The downtown arts district is full of boutiques and art galleries. The English Village Shopping Center at the corner of Darby and Florence Boulevard has upscale shops. Cox Creek Parkway has the Regency Square Mall and major retailers.

The Shoals area is good for antique hunting. The Shoals Antique Dealers Association covers shops across Florence, Sheffield, [Tuscumbia](/northamerica/unitedstates/alabama/tuscumbia), and Muscle Shoals.
