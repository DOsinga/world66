Florence offers a number of different shopping districts. Visitors will find everything from upscale one of a kind gift shops to large chain stores. Serious shoppers will want to explore the downtown arts district full of boutiques and art galleries, the posh English Village Shopping Center at the corner of Darby and Florence Blvd, and Cox Creek Parkway with the Regency Square Mall and other major retailers.

### Shoals Antique Dealers Association

The Shoals Antique Dealers Association offers a web site with information on all the local antiques dealers in the Shoals area. The site provides addresses, photographs, contact information and details about what each shops sells. It's a good reference for anyone tracking down antiques in Florence, Sheffield, Tuscumbia, or Muscle Shoals Alabama.
