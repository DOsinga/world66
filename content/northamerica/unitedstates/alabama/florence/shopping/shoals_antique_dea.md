The Shoals Antique Dealers Association covers all the local antiques dealers in the Shoals area. Their website provides addresses, photographs, contact information, and details about what each shop sells. A useful starting point for anyone hunting antiques in Florence, Sheffield, Tuscumbia, or Muscle Shoals.
