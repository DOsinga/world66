---

The Shoals area around Florence is rich in history and culture, with enough attractions to fill several days.

**Rosenbaum House** (601 Riverview Drive) -- The only Frank Lloyd Wright-designed building in Alabama, this Usonian-style home was built in 1939-40 and completely restored in 2001. Tours by appointment only.

**Belle Mont Mansion** (off US 43, south of [Tuscumbia](/northamerica/unitedstates/alabama/tuscumbia)) -- An excellent example of Jeffersonian-style architecture from the early 1800s, known for its high-quality brickwork and contrasting wood trim. Open for tours on Sundays or by appointment.

**Dismals Canyon** (Phil Campbell) -- A National Natural Landmark featuring a canyon, nature and mountain bike trails, canoeing, camping, swimming, and lodges. A unique geological site in the region.

**Natchez Trace Parkway** -- This scenic highway follows the historic route from Natchez, Mississippi to Nashville, Tennessee, passing through western Colbert County. The parkway offers historical sites and outdoor activities along the way.

**Lions Club Antiques Show and Sale** -- The Florence Lions Club hosts an annual three-day antiques show in late October or early November, featuring dealers from around the country selling fine antiques, jewelry, glassware, furniture, and collectibles. Proceeds benefit local charities.
