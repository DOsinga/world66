Fort Morgan is on the opposite side of Mobile Bay from Fort Gaines. Located about 20 miles west of Gulf Shores, this fort helped defend the area during the War of 1812 and the Civil War. The Army closed the fort in 1946 after World War II. A museum is nearby. A ferry provides a link to Fort Gaines.
