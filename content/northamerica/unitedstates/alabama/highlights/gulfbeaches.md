The beaches of Alabama's Gulf Coast are second to none. Unlike the Pacific beaches these are sugar-white; unlike Florida, these are clean and uncrowded. Gulf Shores and Orange Beach are the two main resort towns. The beach is a great relaxing family vacation destination!
