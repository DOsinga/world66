---
title: "International Motorsports Hall of Fame & the Talladega Superspeedway"
type: location
---

The Talladega Superspeedway is not only home to some pretty important NASCAR races, it is also the home of the International Motorsports Hall of Fame, which has over 150 cars and counting! The Texaco Walk of Fame is located nearby in the town of Talladega, and is a growing tribute to the influential members of stock car racing.
