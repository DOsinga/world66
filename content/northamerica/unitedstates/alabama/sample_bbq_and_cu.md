This year head South to Alabama to sample some award winning BBQ, and culinary delights as the state celebrates The Year Of Alabama Food. From Seafood Festivals to Brews and Barbecue created by award winning brewers and chefs from across the state; there will be plenty of exceptional food to enjoy. It's hard to pick one event, so we've selected five festive food events to join across the state.
