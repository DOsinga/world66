Northwest Alabama Children's Advocacy Center http://nwacac.org/ The Northwest Alabama Children's Advocacy Center was formed as part of a network of advocacy centers across the United States. Although this network is national in scope, we take special pride in the fact that it was begun by our own congressman, U.S. Representative Bud Cramer. Our purpose is to assist and offer supportive counseling to children, adolescents and their families who have sustained trauma resulting from abuse, violence, or neglect. We offer supervised visitation as directed by the court system, and we work with children who are caught up in complicated or abusive divorces. * The center is an advocate for all children.
