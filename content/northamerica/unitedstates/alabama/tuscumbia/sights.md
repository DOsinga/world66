### [Ivy Green](/northamerica/unitedstates/alabama/tuscumbia/sights/ivy_green)

This is the birthplace of Helen Keller. An excellent time to visit Ivy
Green is the last week of June during the annual Helen Keller Festival.

Helen Adams Keller was born a healthy
child on June 27, 1880, to Captain Arthur H. and Kate Adams Keller of
Tuscumbia. At the tender age of 19 months, she was stricken with a
severe illness which left her blind and deaf. At
the age of six, the half-wild, deaf
and blind girl was taken by her parents to see Dr. Alexander Graham
Bell. Because of her visit, Helen was united with her teacher Anne
Mansfield Sullivan on March 3, 1887. [more..](/northamerica/unitedstates/alabama/tuscumbia/sights/ivy_green)
