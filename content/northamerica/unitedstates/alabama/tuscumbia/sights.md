**Ivy Green** -- The birthplace of Helen Keller, built in 1820. The simple white clapboard home is open for tours year-round, and the annual Helen Keller Festival in late June features performances of "The Miracle Worker" on the grounds.

**Alabama Music Hall of Fame** (US Highway 72 West) -- Dedicated to over 500 musical achievers from Alabama across rock, rhythm and blues, gospel, country, opera, and contemporary music. The Hall of Fame is a fitting tribute for the Shoals area, once known as the "Hit Recording Capital of the World."
