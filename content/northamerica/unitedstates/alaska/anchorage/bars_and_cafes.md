---
title: "Bars and Cafes"
type: section
---

Anchorage has a lively bar scene concentrated along Spenard Road and in the downtown core. Chilkoot Charlie's on Spenard Road has been the city's most famous venue for decades — sprawling, loud, and distinctly Alaskan. Humpy's on 6th Avenue draws a college crowd with a huge craft beer selection and live acoustic music. Bear's Tooth Theater Brewpub on 27th is a good option for microbrews and wood-fired pizza alongside a second-run film.
