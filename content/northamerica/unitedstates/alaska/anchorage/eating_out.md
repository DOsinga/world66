---
title: "Eating Out"
type: section
---

Anchorage has over 300 restaurants and the seafood is the main reason to eat well here. Salmon, halibut, king crab, and scallops appear on menus at every level. Glacier Brewhouse on 5th Avenue combines good Alaskan seafood with a wood-fired grill and house microbrews. Simon and Seafort's on L Street is where locals go for a proper dinner with views over Cook Inlet.
