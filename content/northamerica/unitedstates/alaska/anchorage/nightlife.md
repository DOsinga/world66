**Chilkoot Charlie’s**

2435 Spenard Road

907-272-1010

Description:

Chilkoot Charlie's has been Alaska's most famous nightspot for decades. After an evening here, you'll understand why. Though it's expanded and become a bit mainstream, the rustic (dingy?) bare wood, keg bar-stools, and sawdust maintain its Alaskan flavor.

**Mr Whitekey’s Fly By Night Club - Cabaret**

3300 Spenard Road

907-279-7726

Description:

Hosts The Whale Fat Follies, a cabaret with a satirical, bawdy slant on Alaska and its people. Show $12–17.

**Hot Rods**

4848 Old Seward Highway

907 562 5701

Other than 'Koot's, this is the bar and dance club for those in their 20s and 30s. Tons of theme nights, pool, and good rowdy fun. A cornerstone of the Anchorage single scene.

**Humpy's Great Alaskan Alehouse: Restaurant-bar**

610 W 6th Ave

907-276-2337

Description:

This is a popular college style bar with more than thirty microbrews, English and Belgian bottled beers, thirty single malts and acoustic music.

**Glacier Brewhouse: Restaurant-bar**

737 W 5th St

907-274-2739

Decription:

Restaurant and microbrewery, plus a full range of wines by the glass, including dessert wines.

**Mad Myrna's**

530 E. 5th Ave

907-276-9762

http://www.alaska.net/~madmyrna/

Mad Myrna's is *the* gay nightclub in Anchorage, but straight folks are warmly welcome. This is a fun club with the expected '70s and '80s pop music. Many nights are "themed" and they often have special events. Unfortunately, Alaska is generally a homophobic place but this a fun safe haven.

**Bear's Tooth Theater Brewpub**

1230 W. 27th Ave.

907-276-4200

This is a great place for a night on the town with folks of all ages. This theater serves fantastic microbrews and woodfired pizzas at your seat amongst cozy Alaskan decor. Admission is $2 for second-run/classic movies, and sometimes there are local music acts.

**Blues Central**

825 W. Northern Lights Blvd.

907/272-1341

For a casual night with great local bands, head on over.

**A-K Korral Saloon**

2421 E. Tudor Rd.

They've got both kinds of music: country *and* western!
