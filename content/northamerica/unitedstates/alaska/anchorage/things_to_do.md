---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Anchorage is most valuable as a base for exploring the surrounding wilderness rather than as a destination in itself. That said, the Tony Knowles Coastal Trail along the inlet is excellent for cycling or walking. The Anchorage Museum is the best introduction to Alaska's history and culture. Most visitors use the city to prepare for day trips to Denali, Kenai Fjords, and Portage Glacier.
