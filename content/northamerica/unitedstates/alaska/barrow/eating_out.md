Barrow has a few surprisingly great restaurants. The prices are all over $10+ per plate. Barrow is a damp town - no restaurants sell alcohol.

### [Northern Lights Restaurant](/northamerica/unitedstates/alaska/barrow/eating_out/northern_lights_re)

Specializing in Korean food, one of the fanciest restaurants in Barrow.

type: International



[[rate it]](/northamerica/unitedstates/alaska/barrow/eating_out/northern_lights_re)

tel: (907) 852-3300

address: 5122 Herman St

### [Pepe's North of the Border](/northamerica/unitedstates/alaska/barrow/eating_out/pepes)

This is the place you'll find the tourists. The food is bland, but the atmosphere more than makes up for it.

type: Mexican



[[rate it]](/northamerica/unitedstates/alaska/barrow/eating_out/pepes)

tel: (907) 852-8200

address: 1204 Agvik St

PriceOfMenu: $15+

### [Arctic Pizza](/northamerica/unitedstates/alaska/barrow/eating_out/arctic_pizza)

Great pizza and Italian fare with a view of the Arctic Ocean. The service is slow.

type: Italian



### [Osaka Restaurant](/northamerica/unitedstates/alaska/barrow/eating_out/osaka_restaurant)

A simple restaurant with good udon, sushi, and tempura. American-style breakfasts are also nice.

type: Japanese



tel: (907) 852-4100

address: 980 Stevenson St

PriceOfMenu: $12+
