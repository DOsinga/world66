---
title: "Eating Out"
type: section
---

Juneau has a solid restaurant scene for a city its size, with fresh seafood the natural focus. The waterfront area around Marine Way has the highest concentration of restaurants. Hangar on the Wharf at 2 Marine Way is a reliable choice for seafood in a lively setting — the coconut prawns and king crab cakes are popular starters, and the menu covers everything from jambalaya to stuffed tortellini. Doc Water's Pub, also at #2 Marine Way, is a more casual option with an adventurous menu that includes oysters, halibut cerviche, and Hawaiian-inflected specials; they also serve breakfast. For pizza and sandwiches, Bullwinkle's on 318 Willoughby Avenue is a local favourite with a long menu of specialty pies and hearty sandwiches.
