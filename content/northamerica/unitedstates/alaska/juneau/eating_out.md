type: section
---

Juneau restaurants are a plenty. But if you are hungry for great seafood and want to dine on the waterfront, check out Hangar on the Wharf located at 2 Marine Way. The seafood is always fresh and the atmosphere is fun. Start your dinner with appetizers like coconut prawns, tempura crabtails, spinach artichoke dip, king crab cakes, or calamari. Salads come in choices of blackened salmon, blackened grilled chicken, poached pear & gorgonzola, and charred ahi spinach. Entrees include jambalaya, chicken fried steak & gravy, fettuccine, stuffed tortellini, and jumbo cheese ravioli. For hours and directions, visit their website at hangar.hangaronthewharf.com.

A good choice in Juneau restaurants for seafood is Doc Water's Pub at #2 Marine Way. Customers can dine on specials like coconut calamari, salmon or halibut burgers, or saffron breakfast prawns. Regular menu items include oysters, halibut cerviche, wasabi blasters, beer-battered mushrooms, gorgonzola steak salad, pico green chicken salad, avacado bacon burger, paella, grilled haibut in adobo, sansho ahi, and Hawaiian red salt crusted flank steak. Doc's serves breakfast as well. Enjoy in the morning a breakfast burrito, breakfast burger, or steak & eggs. For directions and just to see a cool website, go to docwaterspub.com.

A Juneau restaurant for great pizza is Bullwinkle's on 318 Willoughby Avenue. Customers can enjoy buffalo wings, shrimp salad, vegetarian salad, chef salad, and green salad. Pizza comes in with such toppings as mesquite chicken, salami, bacon, white mushroom, and sausage. Specialty pizzas are garlic chicken, beef/chicken taco, garlic veggie supreme, vegetarian, barbecue chicken, and all meat combo. They offer sandwiches here as well. Popular choices are prime rib & cheddar, yukon jack, rueben, old Juneau, smokey jack, turkey treat, and french dip. For directions and hours, check out their website at bullwinklespizza.com
