---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

Making Juneau travel a reality is easier than you think. Several major carriers provide service in and out of Juneau International Airport. They are Alaska, American, Delta, Northwest, and United. There are no trains or buses traveling to Juneau. However, another alternative is to go by cruise ship. There are cruises that depart from California, Oregon, or Washington State. Another way to travel to Juneau is by car. You can drive to Juneau by going through the AICan. If you decide to drive, bring your passport, license, registration, insurance, and cash as there is an entrance fee to Canada.
