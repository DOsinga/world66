---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Outdoor and scenic activities are the main reasons for visiting Petersburg. You are surrounded by the Tongass National Forest — you could simply take a hike up a mountain. Drive or hitch out the road to visit the Crystal Lake Salmon Hatchery, go steelhead fishing, or in winter watch Trumpeter Swans on the river flats.

Charter boat captains can take you out to see humpback whales in Frederick Sound, to LeConte Glacier with its calving icebergs, to a river to watch black bears fishing for salmon, or to a quiet spot where you can catch your own halibut or king salmon.

The town has a small museum with native artefacts and local history — modest but worth a look if the weather drives you indoors.
