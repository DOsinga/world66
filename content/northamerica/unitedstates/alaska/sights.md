 **Places to Go:**

 **Alaska Experience Center** (I truely recommend for everyone to visit here. It is great.)

 705 West 6th Avenue

 Anchorage, Alaska 99501

 (907) 276-3730

 Description:

 **An Omnivision Motion Picture Experience**

 180 degree wraparound screen unfolds the majesty of Alaska's wildlife, scenery, and people before your eyes

 **A Seismic Sensation**

 Experience what the "Great Alaska Earthquake of 1964" might have been similar to.

 Open Daily: Summer Hours 9am to 9pm / Winter Hours 12 am to 6 pm

 The Alaska Experience Theatre...... Adults $6.99 Children $3.99

 The Alaska Earthquake Exhibit...... Adults $4.99 Children $3.99

 Combined Discount...... Adults $9.99 Children $6.99

 Also has a great gift shop featuring Alaska's finest gifts.

 **Imaginarium**

 The Imaginarium, Alaska’s only hands-on Science Discovery Center... (This place is great. My children and I loved going there.)

 The Imaginarium is open every day except New Year's Day, Easter Sunday, July 4th, Thanksgiving Day, and Christmas Day. 

 Child (2-12yrs)... $4

 Adult (13-64yrs)... $5

 Senior (65 and above)... $4
