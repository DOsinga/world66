Here are some of the facts about Alaska:

 Tony Knowles is the current Governor of Alaska.

 Size: 591,004 square miles. (approximately 1/5th the size of the total area of the United States) Population: 614,010

 Statehood: January 3, 1959

 State Motto: “North to the Future”

 State tree: Sitka Spruce

 State fish: Salmon

 State Fossil: Wooly Mammoth

 State Marine Animal: Bowhead Whale

 Stae mineral: Gold

 State gem: Jade

 State Sport: Dog Mushing

 State flower: Forget-me-not

 State Bird: Willow Ptarmigan 

 Longest River: Yukon River (1,400 of the total 1,770 miles of the river are in Alaska). The Yukon is also the 5th longest river in North America.

 Largest Lake: Lake Iliamna, with 1,000 square miles.

 Highest peak:  Mt. McKinley 20,320 feet (This is also the highest peak in all of North America)
