The Historical Society Museum is housed in a trapper cabin originally built in 1916 and details the history of Talkeetna including early mining efforts, development of the Alaskan Railroad, and mountain avation. On display is a 12-foot by 12-foot scale model of Denali with photographs, and mountain climbing displays.
