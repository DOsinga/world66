Travel Guide

One of the biggest cities on the Navajo Nation, Chinle is in northwest Arizona, near the New Mexico border. It's the gateway to Canyon de Chelly National Monument, a canyon system full of ancient pueblo ruins. There are 3 motels in town, along with a nice campground (no hookups) at the national monument.

Chinle has other services as well: several restaurants, trading posts, and a Basha's Dine Market supermarket.
