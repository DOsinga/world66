title: "Eating Out in Arizona"
type: section
---

**Grand Canyon restaurants** are available in and outside of the Grand Canyon Park and village. These restaurants provide a combination of American, Latin and Mexican cuisines. After a tiresome day of touring around the Grand Canyon, you better reward yourself with the tastiest food served in these restaurants.

#### **Best Mexican Food**

Pancho McGillicuddy's is a restaurant serving American, Latin, Mexican and Southwestern cuisines on William Railroad Avenue. Its main specialty is Mexican food like burritos, fajitas and enchiladas. They also offer tasty American burgers. This restaurant is open daily from 11 a.m. until 10 p.m. Pancho McGillicuddy&rsquo;s also has a bar which is open until 2 a.m. The restaurant is good for family and group gatherings. A smoking area is provided outside the restaurant. You can also dine outside if you want great views. There is live entertainment at times which adds to the atmosphere.

#### **Quick Dining**

Maswik Cafeteria is an eating place in the Grand Canyon National Park that provides simple American, Latin and Mexican food. This cafeteria is open everyday from 6 a.m. to 10 p.m. They also have a lounge that is open from 5 p.m. to 11 p.m. on weekdays and 3 p.m. to 11 p.m. on weekends. A variety of food is served. If you are a light eater, you can order salads or sandwiches. Try their Mexican entrees and pastas for more filling meals. There is also a sports bar in the area where you can go for another form of relaxation.
