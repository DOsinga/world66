---
title: "Eating Out"
type: section
---

Arizona's food scene reflects its geography and history — Mexican and Sonoran food is everywhere, particularly in Tucson, which has a strong claim to being one of the best cities in the US for regional Mexican cuisine. Phoenix has grown into a serious dining city with strong farm-to-table and Southwestern fare. Outside the cities, towns near the Grand Canyon and along Route 66 keep things simple and filling.
