The Arboretum, located just outside of Flagstaff, is a botanical garden, nature center, and scientific research station. It is home to one of the nation's largest collections of high country wildflowers, with over 2,500 species of plants in the collection. On Fridays through Mondays, at noon and 2:00 p.m., visitors can also watch a Wild Birds of Prey program. Tours of the 200-acre facility take place each day at 11:00 a.m. and 1:00 p.m. For more information, visit www.thearb.org.
