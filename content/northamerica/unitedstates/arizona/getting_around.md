title: "Getting Around in Arizona"
type: section
---

There are various methods of **Grand Canyon transportation** you can use to reach the famous Grand Canyon in Arizona. You can also use these methods as you tour around the Grand Canyon Village.

#### **Travel By Car**

There are many entrances to the Grand Canyon. The North Rim entrance is harder to reach. The nicer entrance is the South entrance, and there are several ways to get there. Having a car would make your travel more flexible. Depending upon your route to the area, there are many beautiful sights where you can stop for a while to explore.

#### **Be Shuttled by Bus**

There are several private tour companies which can take you to the Grand Canyon for varying prices. Tour buses would also facilitate your stay and sight-seeing once you arrive. Once in the Grand Canyon Village, there is a free shuttle bus. You can travel anywhere you want to go along the rim that is 50 miles long. There are three shuttle buses that will take you to three different places. If you see a blue shuttle bus, it will take you around the park. If your destination is the South Kaibab Trail, you can take the green shuttle bus. The red shuttle takes you to Hermit&rsquo;s rest and other sites.
