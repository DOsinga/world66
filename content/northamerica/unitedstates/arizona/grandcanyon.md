---
title: "Grand Canyon"
type: location
---

Located entirely in northern Arizona, the park encompasses 277
miles of the Colorado River and adjacent uplands. One of the most
spectacular examples of erosion anywhere in the world, Grand Canyon is
unmatched in the incomparable vistas it offers to visitors on the rim.
Grand Canyon National Park is a World Heritage Site.

Expect crowds during the spring, summer, and fall months. During
these months reservations for camping and lodging are essential. If
crowds are not for you, check out the private tours and adventure
packages that are available from the good folks at DiscoveryTreks.com.

When visiting the South Rim, be sure to go to Canyon View
Information Plaza, there you will find information to help plan your
Grand Canyon visit.
