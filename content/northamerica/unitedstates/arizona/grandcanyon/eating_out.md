---
title: "Eating Out"
type: section
---

If you got stuck at the ditch of the Canyon by Colorado River, there is a Phantom Ranch along Kaibab Trail. For snack, they have basic food, such as, apple, bagel with cheese ($2.50), raisin, chocolate bars, and drinks. Cold beer and wine are offered, too. Dinner ($22/person) can be reserved months in advance. Breakfast and lunch is walk-in.
