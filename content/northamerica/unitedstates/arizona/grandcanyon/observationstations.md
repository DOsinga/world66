### YAVAPAI OBSERVATION STATION, SOUTH RIM

Location - Located 5 miles north of the park's south entrance. (South Rim)  Exhibits - Contains temporary exhibits about the fossil record at Grand Canyon. A panorama of the canyon is visible through the building's large windows.  Available Facilities - Educational materials about the park and region are sold in the Grand Canyon Association bookstore. Public restrooms located next to the parking lot.
