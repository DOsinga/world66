Mesa's location in the East Valley makes it a natural launching point for day trips across central Arizona. [Phoenix](/northamerica/unitedstates/arizona/phoenix) and [Tempe](/northamerica/unitedstates/arizona/tempe) are both within a short drive, and the Superstition Mountains and Apache Trail offer some of the most dramatic desert scenery in the state.
