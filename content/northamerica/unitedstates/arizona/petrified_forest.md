Ancient logs turned to multicolored crystal over 225 million years — the Petrified Forest is stranger and more beautiful in person than any photo suggests. The park also contains sections of the Painted Desert, with badlands striped in lavender, red, and ochre. Drive the 28-mile park road or get out and hike the backcountry for real solitude.
