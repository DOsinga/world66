---
title: 'Bars and Cafes'
type: section
---

Phoenix's bar scene ranges from dive bars and sports grills to craft cocktail lounges and live music venues. The Roosevelt Row arts district downtown has the most concentrated nightlife, but good spots are scattered across the valley. Tempe's Mill Avenue caters to the college crowd, while Old Town Scottsdale leans upscale. Most places stay open late and keep prices reasonable.
