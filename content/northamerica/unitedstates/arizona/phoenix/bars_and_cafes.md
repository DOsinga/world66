### [The Rhythm Room](/northamerica/unitedstates/arizona/phoenix/bars_and_cafes/the_rhythm_room)

In addition to The Rhythm Room being one of Phoenix’s premiere venues, it also offers a large bar with a wide variety of drinks and amenities. Ranging from your classic PBR to first rate whisky, The Rhythm Room has all the alcohol you need to get any party started. In addition, you can enjoy some of the fine music that passes through the Rhythm Room’s doors. It is well known that Charles Barkeley often plays guitar at the venue as well as many other famous Arizona natives. ### [16th Street Sports Bar](/northamerica/unitedstates/arizona/phoenix/bars_and_cafes/16th_street_sports)

The 16th Street Sports Bar is a premiere place for drinking and eating in Phoenix. In terms of Phoenix nightlife, you cant get any closer to the action and intimacy of small town living. When it comes to Phoenix, too many of the bars are elaborate and fancy, the 16th Street Sports bar and grill is simple and classic. Offering excellent drinks and tasty microbrews, the 16th street sports bar has anything and everything a sports nut could want. Located on 16th street, the sports bar offers a unique array of beers on tap as well as a select beer of the month.



tel: 602-285-5555

address: 6522 North 16th Street
