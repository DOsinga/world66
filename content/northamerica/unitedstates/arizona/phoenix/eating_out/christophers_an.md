Christopher’s Crush is an amazing place to go when looking for the winning combination of late night dining and drinks. What is interesting to note about this lounge is how the bar and restaurant are well integrated while still preserving an amazing and intimate feel to it. There is a unique chef’s table in the restaurant which seats 10 guests and gives guests a chance to look directly into the amazingly crafted open air kitchen. In addition to their regular menu, there are tasting menu’s that are simply to die for. From pizza to burgers, Christopher’s Crush has all you need for an excellent night out. It is definitely at the top of the list for Phoenix restaurants.
