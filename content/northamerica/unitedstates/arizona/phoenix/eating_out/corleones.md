In Phoenix, there are many restaurants, bar and grills, malls and fine dining locations for you to choose from. One that is a no- brainer for fast lunch- time service is Corleone’s. Corleone’s offers several Phoenix locations and is one of the best Cheesesteaks in the city. Started by a bunch of Philly natives, Corleone’s makes a cheesteak that is authentic, from the chopped onions down to the delicious cheese whiz spread. The prices are a little high but it is worth it for these tasty cheese steaks. In addition to the traditional Cheese steak, Corleone’s offers several unique spins on the Philly favorite.
