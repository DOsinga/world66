District combines distinctive flavors and styles to your tradition Phoenix restaurant. Offering a lovely wine bar along with an amazing kitchen, District serves breakfast, lunch and dinner and turns into a lounge at select hours of the day. The menu boasts everything from salads, sandwiches, short ribs, chicken pot pie, to cedar roasted pacific salmon. The menu is a bit pricy but all in all this place is a great value for the amazing quality of food you are getting. Some events District hosts include live music and wine events. All of them are often fun and include special discounts and delicious small plate dishes.
