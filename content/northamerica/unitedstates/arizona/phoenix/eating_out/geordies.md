Geordie’s restaurant is one of the best places to go when looking for delicious Phoenix restaurants. Located in the historic and fascinating Wrigley Mansion, the restaurant serves dinner on Friday and Saturdays from 5 to 9pm. In addition, the menu served at Georgie’s is a combination of American cuisine and other world fare. Geordie’s also offers lunch on Tuesday through Saturdays. The food served at lunch is delightful and comes with a unique menu concept. The idea is that customers pay what they think is fair for an entrée, no set prices! Get down to the Wrigley Mansion as soon as you can!
