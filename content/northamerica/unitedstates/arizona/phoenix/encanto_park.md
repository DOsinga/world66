Encanto Park is a sprawling 200-acre park full of picnic tables and trails. It stays open until midnight, making late-night visits possible. The park includes Enchanted Island, an amusement park with rides for young children, plus a playground and swimming pool. Basketball courts, racquetball courts, and a softball field round out the offerings.
