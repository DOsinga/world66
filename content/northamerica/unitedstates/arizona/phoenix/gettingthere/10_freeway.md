One of the easiest and most direct ways to get to phoenix from the west coast is on the 10 freeway. The 10 Freeway will take you directly to Phoenix by car or bus in approximately six hours. The drive itself is not particularly scenic, but offers several key stops which will make the drive bearable and perhaps even enjoyable. One key stop off of the 10 is Blithe. Offering a variety of food stops as well as gas, Blithe marks the perfect halfway point for any traveler. In addition to Blythe, there are several other small cities on the way that might be worth stopping at. The best thing about the 10, as pertaining to Phoenix travel, is that it offers a direct and reliable freeway that is rarely closed down and flooded with construction.
