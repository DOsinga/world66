Historic Heritage Square is the original center of Phoenix, now part of the Heritage and Science Park. Worth seeing are the Rosson House, Salt River Project's Silva House, Stevens House, and five more historic homes. A weekly farmers market draws locals and visitors alike, and the Doll and Toy Museum adds a quirky touch. The early-1900s architecture alone makes it worth a stroll.
