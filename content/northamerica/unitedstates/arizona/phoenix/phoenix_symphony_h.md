The Phoenix Symphony is the only professional orchestra in Arizona, maintaining 75 members and performing in the Phoenix Symphony Hall from September to May each year. Beyond the orchestra, the hall hosts a variety of community events and educational programs. The repertoire ranges from orchestral classics to pop favorites.
