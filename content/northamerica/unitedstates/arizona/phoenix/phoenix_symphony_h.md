Home to the Phoenix Symphony, Arizona's only full-time professional orchestra, with a 75-member ensemble performing from September through May. The 2,300-seat hall in downtown Phoenix also hosts touring Broadway shows, ballet, and community events throughout the year.

The repertoire mixes classical standards with pops concerts and film-score nights, making it accessible even if you're not a regular concertgoer. The hall sits in the civic complex near the convention centre, with plenty of restaurants within walking distance for a pre-show dinner.
