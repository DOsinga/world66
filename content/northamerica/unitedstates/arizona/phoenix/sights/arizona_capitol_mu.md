The Arizona Capitol Museum is classically designed and was built in 1900. The building has 15 tons of copper on the outside of it. In the museum, there are a wide variety of exhibits and installations, including a large tribute to the USS Arizona that was sunk at Pearl Harbor. There is a piece of the ship at the museum, as well as artifacts from the people who survived the attack. Group tours of the exhibits are entirely possible and should be made in advance. There is a nice gift shop with local items for sale. There are also some paintings by the great artist Lon Megargee. Phoenix attractions, such as this, make Phoenix a great place to visit.
