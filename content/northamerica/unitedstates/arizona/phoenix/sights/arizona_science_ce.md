The Arizona Science Center is the perfect place to go if you or your children are really into learning about science and the universe. The museum building itself is a unique architectural wonder. With fun and interactive exhibits, the Arizona Sicence Center’s goal is to amuse and educate its guests. Some exhibits include: All about Me and Fab Lab. Other attractions in the Center include: laser light shows, night sky dome, Dorrance Planetarium, and the Irene P. Flinn Theater. What is so unique about the theater, is that it contains a massive five story high screen. Phoenix attractions have never been more amusing.
