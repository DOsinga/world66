The Chinese Cultural Center offers unique Chinese architecture and design to the masses. With a wide variety of traditional Chinese shops and gardens, people can purchase everything from fresh fish to timeless Chinese artifacts. The grounds are beautifully decorated with gardens, ponds, and statues of historic Chinese figures. Some excellent restaurants in the area include the Sampan Seafood Restaurant, who offers up fresh Asian fare. In addition to the wonderful Asian supermarket, visitors will have no trouble locating the Chinese Cultural Center, in that it is right off of interstate 1-. Phoenix attractions, such as this, should not go unnoticed.
