Focusing entirely on Desert plants, Phoenix’s very own Desert Botanical Gardens houses a large array of plants, including endangered species. The Gardens have been expanded in the past two years to allow room for the Harrington Cactus and Succulent Galleries. In addition to the specific plants, the Desert Botanical Gardens offer 5 different trails, each themed to highlight unique aspects of the Sonoran desert. The Gardens are open for a long time each day and provide terrain that suits people who are looking for a more casual stroll then say an intense mountain hike. The Desert Botanical Gardens also have a wide variety of birds that will entertain and delight any bird lover.
