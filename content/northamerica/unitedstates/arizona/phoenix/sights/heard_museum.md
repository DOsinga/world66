The Heard Museum is an excellent Museum full of Native American art and historic artifacts. In addition, the museum explains the different cultures of Native American tribes in the southwest. With live and interactive performances as well as detailed displays in the museum, this is the perfect place to get a better idea of how the original Phoenix residents lived. In addition to art being on display, artists come to the museum and work while you watch, giving you a better understanding of how this traditional work is crafted. Musicians also perform, making Tucson attractions such as this, something worth seeing.
