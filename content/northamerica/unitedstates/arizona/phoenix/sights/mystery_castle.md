Mystery Castle is sure to be the only Castle you have ever seen made from granite, recycled bottles and bricks. Constructed in 1930, tours of the Castle are given throughout the year. It’s not free (approximately 5 dollars) but it is worth it to see this one of a kind piece of Phoenix architecture. Furnished with traditional southwest furniture, the grounds boast 18 rooms, 13 fireplaces and so much more. The grounds are surrounded by cacti and trees, while stone steps lead you to the castle door. Phoenix attractions, such as this, are one of a kind and simply cannot be seen anywhere else in the world.
