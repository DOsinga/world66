The Phoenix Art Museum offers permanent as well as rotating attractions throughout the year. Some of the more permanent installations include miniature interiors, Asia, Spanish, western and a wide variety of 20th century art. If you have children, there are a variety of hands on activities for kids to enjoy and interact with. The gallery for children is called the Artworks Gallery. Because there are a variety of rotating exhibits, your best bet is to check their website for updates on new installations and traveling exhibits. Phoenix attractions such as this, highlight the culture that pervades Phoenix and its inhabitants.
