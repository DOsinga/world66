What is so unique about the Pioneer Arizona Living History Musuem, is that it is so much more than a museum. It is actually an entire town, with a jail, sheriff’s office, dress shop and schoolhouse. All of them are displayed as they once were in the turn of the century. There is also a unique and distinctive chapel that Victorian style weddings take place in. For kids, there is an area called the Critter Corral that has a variety of small animals that the kids can interact with. Lunch and dinner are served in this small town and you should always call ahead for a list of new events and activities taking place at this, one of the best Phoenix attractions.
