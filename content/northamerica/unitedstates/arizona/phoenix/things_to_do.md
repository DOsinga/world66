---
title: 'Things to Do'
type: section
---

Phoenix offers an impressive range of activities for a desert city. Hike Camelback Mountain or South Mountain Park for sweeping valley views, explore world-class museums like the Heard Museum and the Phoenix Art Museum, or catch a Diamondbacks game at Chase Field. The city's quirky side shows up in places like Mystery Castle and the Pioneer Arizona Living History Museum. Whatever your pace, there is plenty here to fill your days.
