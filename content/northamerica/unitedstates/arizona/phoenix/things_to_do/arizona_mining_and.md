If you have any interest in rocks and gems, the Arizona Mining and Mineral Museum is the place. Exhibits include fossils, mineral samples, turquoise ore, meteorites, and moon rocks. The gift shop sells minerals and rocks, and Ross Mofford's personal collection of Arizona memorabilia is a highlight.
