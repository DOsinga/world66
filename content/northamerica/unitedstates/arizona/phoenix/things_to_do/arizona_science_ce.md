The Arizona Science Center is packed with fun, interactive exhibits aimed at both kids and adults. Highlights include laser light shows, a planetarium, and the Irene P. Flinn Theater with its massive five-story screen. The museum building itself is an architectural standout. A great rainy-day option, though rain is admittedly rare in Phoenix.
