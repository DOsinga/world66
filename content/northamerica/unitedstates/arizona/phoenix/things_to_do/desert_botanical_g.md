Focusing entirely on desert plants, the Desert Botanical Gardens houses a large array of species, including endangered ones. The gardens have been expanded to allow room for the Harrington Cactus and Succulent Galleries. Five different trails are each themed to highlight unique aspects of the Sonoran Desert. The terrain suits people looking for a casual stroll rather than an intense mountain hike, and a wide variety of birds will delight any birder.
