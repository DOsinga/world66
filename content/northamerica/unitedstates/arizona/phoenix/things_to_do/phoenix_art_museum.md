The Phoenix Art Museum offers permanent and rotating exhibitions throughout the year. Permanent collections include miniature interiors, Asian, Spanish, Western, and a wide variety of 20th-century art. The Artworks Gallery provides hands-on activities for children. Check the website for updates on new installations and traveling exhibits.
