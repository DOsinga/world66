---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment"
type: section
---

Scottsdale is host to some of the hottest nightclubs around, from techno to hiphop and rock to jazz there is something for everyone. The party goes on seven days a week and there is never a shortage of people to party with.

This makes for long lines that can sometimes seem like they are never going to end. You could make friends with the doorman if you are going to be in town long enough or you could just remember to bring your Scottsdale VIP Passport.

A bar hoppers dream come true.
