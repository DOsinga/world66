One of the most striking canyon landscapes in the Southwest, Canyon de Chelly has been home to Navajo families for generations. White House Ruin, a cliff dwelling tucked into the canyon wall, is the one trail you can hike without a guide — everything else requires a Navajo guide, which is worth arranging.
