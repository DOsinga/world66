The only place in the US where organ pipe cactus grows wild, this monument on the Mexican border protects a stretch of Sonoran Desert that feels genuinely remote. The Ajo Mountain Drive is a 21-mile scenic loop that passes through some of the richest desert scenery in the state. Spring wildflowers can be spectacular after a wet winter.
