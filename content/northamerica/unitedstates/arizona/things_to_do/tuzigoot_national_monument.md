A hilltop Sinagua pueblo overlooking the Verde Valley, Tuzigoot was occupied between roughly 1125 and 1425 CE and at its peak housed around 225 people. The site is compact and easy to explore, and the views across the valley toward the red rocks of Sedona are excellent. Combine with nearby Montezuma Castle for a full day of Verde Valley archaeology.
