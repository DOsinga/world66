---
title: "Bars and Cafes"
type: section
---

Tombstone's saloons are half the reason to visit. Most line Allen Street and lean hard into the Wild West theme — swinging doors, old wood bars, country and western music. Big Nose Kate's Saloon is the most famous, housed in a building that dates to 1881 and named after Doc Holliday's girlfriend. The Crystal Palace, across the street, was one of the original grand saloons and still pours drinks. For something quieter, Vogan's Alley Bar is a local favorite tucked just off the main drag.
