---
title: "Eating Out"
type: section
---

Tombstone is a small town, so do not expect a wide culinary scene. Most restaurants cluster along Allen Street and cater to the tourist crowd with burgers, steaks, and Tex-Mex. Nellie Cashman's, named after a real-life Tombstone figure who ran a boarding house in the 1880s, serves solid home-style American food. For Mexican, Don Teodoro's is a reliable choice. The Longhorn Restaurant occupies a historic building and does decent steaks.
