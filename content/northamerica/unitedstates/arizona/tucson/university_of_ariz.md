Tucson attractions are often linked to the large state school that inhabits the region. The University of Arizona Museum of Art offers a wide array of original works as well as a series of rotating exhibits and exhibitions. One major collection at the gallery is that of Samuel J. Kress. With over 60 European paintings, decorative objects and sculptures, the collection is vital to the museum itself. Many of the works date between the 14th and 19th centuries. With a massive 26 panel retablo painted by Ciudad Rodrigo, this collection is both unique and impressive. Located extremely close to the campus, the University of Arizona Museum of Art is the perfect place to find some culture on your vacation to the desert.
