Robson's Mining World
Open October 1 to May 1
PO Box 3465
Wickenburg, Arizona 85358
Tel: 928.685.2609
Fax: 928.685.4164
www.robsonsminingworld.com
The Worlds largest collection of antique mining equipment,
a first class Restaurant, a General Store with Antique shopping
and Ice Cream Parlor. Museums of Grocery, Barber & Assay Office,
Newspaper and Post Office, plus over 30 buildings full of ancient
equipment of all kinds.
