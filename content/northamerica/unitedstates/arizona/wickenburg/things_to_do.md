---
title: "Things to do in Wickenburg"
type: section
---

Wickenburg's main draw is its mining heritage and Old West character. The town has a couple of worthwhile museums and the surrounding desert is good for hiking and horseback riding.

**Desert Caballeros Western Museum** — Billed as Arizona's most western museum, this is a solid collection of Western art and regional history at 21 North Frontier Street. Open Monday to Saturday 10:00 am to 5:00 pm and Sunday noon to 4:00 pm (closed Mondays in July and August). Admission is around $6.

**Robson's Mining World** — Open October through May, this sprawling outdoor museum claims the world's largest collection of antique mining equipment. Beyond the equipment, you will find over 30 buildings full of historical displays including a recreated general store, assay office, newspaper office, and post office. There is also a restaurant and an ice cream parlor on site.
