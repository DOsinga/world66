Bentonville, Arkansas is best known as the world headquarters of Wal-Mart Stores, the largest retailer in the world. Of course Wal-Mart is the major employer in the Bentonville area. The city square is home to Sam Walton´s Five and Dime, the store that started the Wal-Mart empire. The original Wal-Mart headquarters also has a museum there.
