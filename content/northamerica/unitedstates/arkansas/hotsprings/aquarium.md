title: Arkansas Alligator Farm & Petting Zoo
type: poi

One of Hot Springs' most enduring tourist attractions — over 300 alligators from hatchlings to ten-footers, plus a petting zoo with goats, llamas, and deer. Unashamedly old-school and kitschy, which is part of the charm.
