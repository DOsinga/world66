Springdale is the fourth-largest city in Arkansas, located in the Ozark Mountains in the northwest corner of the state. It is the headquarters of Tyson Foods and often called the chicken capital of the world — the poultry industry dominates the local economy. The surrounding Ozarks countryside offers good hiking and mountain biking.
