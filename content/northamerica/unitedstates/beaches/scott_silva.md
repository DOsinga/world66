Black's Beach in La Jolla is perhaps California's best beach break wave. It carries size, shape and speed like few others. When it's head-high or higher, be careful unless you are an experienced surfer. Depending on the conditions and swell direction, Black's produces plenty of rights, lefts and A-frame sets.

The beach itself lies at the bottom of a privately owned bluff in an upscale residential area. Access to the beach is had--by those without keys to the gate--by parking on La Jolla Farms Rd. and walking down the half-mile long road that winds down the ravine. You can get to La Jolla Farms Rd. by taking Torrey Pines Rd. that runs between UC San Diego and the ocean.

Black's was previously notorious as a nude beach, but authorities began to discourage this several years ago. It is a nice, seclude sandy beach that extends North all the way to Leucadia (about a mile or more) and runs, incidentally, below Torrey Pines Golf Course where Tiger Woods recently won the US Open with a fractured leg. The surf break itself begins straight out from the small parking lot at the bottom of the access road and extends north a couple hundred yards.
