Alameda, California:
**Alameda Island was originally part of a Spanish land grant that ranged
from San Leandro to Berkeley, but the area officially became the city
of Alameda in 1884. Today, this bustling island city is an important
shipping port that is also known for its stunning Victorian homes,
tree-lined streets, beautiful parks, historical monuments, and a
variety of fun attractions. Located just four miles from downtown
Oakland and 15 minutes away from San Francisco, Alameda is a popular
destination for visitors and business travelers alike.
