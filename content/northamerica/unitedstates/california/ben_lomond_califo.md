In the San Lorenzo Valley, nestled in the redwoods, is Ben Lomond — named by an early Scottish immigrant who missed his homeland. Highway 9, the lifeblood of the valley, connects the three valley towns of [Felton](/northamerica/unitedstates/california/centralcoast/felton), Ben Lomond, and Boulder Creek with the outside world.

Ben Lomond is home to the Park Hall, where the Mountain Community Theater puts on plays several times a year, and Highlands Park, which hosts the annual Highland Games Festival. Local colour is on display at establishments like Henflings, Ciao Bella, and The Tyrolean Inn with their annual Oktoberfest. All this in a scenic valley with a river running through it and gorgeous redwood trees all around.
