---
title: "Ben Lomond, California"
type: location
---

In the San Lorenzo Valley, nestled in the redwoods is Ben Lomond.
Named by an early Scottish immigrant who missed his homeland, Ben
Lomond is a warm and inviting community with a highway running through
it. Highway 9 is the lifeblood of the San Lorenzo Valley
connecting all three Valley towns ([Felton](/northamerica/unitedstates/california/centralcoast/felton), [Ben Lomond, California](/northamerica/unitedstates/california/ben_lomond_califo), and Boulder
Creek) with the outside world. Ben Lomond is home to the Park
Hall where the Mountain Community Theater puts on plays several times a
year. It is home to Highlands Park which hosts the annual
Highland Games Festival. Local color is on display at
establishments like Henflings, Ciao Bella, and The Tyrolean Inn with
their annual Oktoberfest. All this in a scenic valley with a
river running through it and gorgeous Redwood trees all around.
Well worth a visit.
