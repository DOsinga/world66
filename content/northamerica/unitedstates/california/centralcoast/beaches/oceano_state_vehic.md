One of the few places in California where you can drive directly onto the beach. The Oceano Dunes stretch for several miles along the coast south of Pismo Beach, with rolling sand dunes reaching up to 30 metres high.

ATVs, 4x4s, motorcycles, and regular cars share the sand, and on holiday weekends the place gets busy enough that State Parks limits vehicle entry. Camping right on the beach is possible and popular -- reserve well ahead for summer. The dunes themselves are worth exploring on foot too, especially at sunset when the light catches the ridgelines.
