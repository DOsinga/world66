Miller moved to Big Sur in 1944 and lived there for nearly twenty years. This book — part memoir, part rhapsody — describes the life he built on Partington Ridge: the landscape, the neighbors, the peculiar paradise of living far from everything. It is rambling and sometimes maddening, and it captures the place better than anything else written about it.
