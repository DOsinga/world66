Central California Coast Interactive CD-ROM

This one-of-a-kind interactive digital tour guide highlighting the Central Coast, was designed for use by both visitors to and residents of the area, the CD-ROM creates a unique multimedia experience, and can be used as a travel guide, for educational purposes, or for pure entertainment. With a few clicks of a mouse, it is possible to find accommodations, plan interesting activities (whale watching, hiking, bicycling, kayaking, etc), and learn about the history of region.

The Central California Coast Interactive CD-ROM contains a photo library with more than 500 high-quality photos and captions from Monterey County, Hearst Castle, and Cambria, a 10 minute full-screen slide show with music, over 100 pages of printable information guides and maps, website links, contact information and addresses, computer desktop wallpaper photos, and many more features. Guides for each community include a general description of the area as well as history, basic travel information such as lodging, dining, and transportation, things to do and see, and full color printable maps. For the more adventurous, there are activity guides on kayaking, whale watching, bicycling, hiking and walking, diving, golf, and camping, as well as guides for many of the area’s outstanding beaches. There is also a collection of wildlife and nature guides with detailed information on whales and dolphins, sea otters, seals and sea lions, birds, the Monterey Bay National Marine Sanctuary, and a primer on environmental conservation and stewardship. Each of these guides can be printed and the CD makes it easy to create a customized travel guide that can be carried along on adventures.
