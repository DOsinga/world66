### [The Bike Path](/northamerica/unitedstates/california/centralcoast/monterey/thingstodo/the_bike_path)

Surrounding much of the Monterey Bay, from Pacific Grove Lovers Point to Seaside, is the scenic Bike Path. This two lane bike path with ample room for casual strolling on foot as well is one of the best activities for either the one-day tourist or the twenty year resident. The views along this scenic path (which was built when an early 20th century railway line was torn out) are world-class.

### [Point Lobos State Reserve](/northamerica/unitedstates/california/centralcoast/monterey/thingstodo/point_lobos_state)

Point Lobos State Reserve, located just three miles south of Carmel along Pacific Coast Highway 1, is well worth a visit if you love wildlife and the outdoors. The roar of the waves and central coast's changing climate from fog, to bright sunshine, to rain shower lend this reserve its particular charm in any season of the year.

Near the main parking lot, take a short walk along a winding trail to see the crowd of sea lions on their own rocky island.
