Oxnard and Ventura are shoreline communities with the Coast Range in their backyards and the Pacific Ocean at their doorsteps. Be sure to tour the new 31-mile Scenic Coastal Drive that weaves through the coastal cities of Port Hueneme, Oxnard and Ventura.
