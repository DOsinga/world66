One of the finest maritime art museums on the West Coast, housed at Channel Islands Harbor. The permanent collection spans 350 years of maritime history with ship models, paintings, and navigational instruments. Rotating exhibitions showcase contemporary maritime artists. Free docent-guided tours are available; call ahead for group visits.
