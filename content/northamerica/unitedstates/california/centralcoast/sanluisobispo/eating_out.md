---
title: "Eating Out in San Luis Obispo"
type: section
---

San Luis Obispo features a number of great restaurants of all variety, from Barbeque to Bistros, Seafood to Saloons. MenuClub, a local business located just outside downtown, has an up-to-date restaurant guide for all the restaurants in the area. They also take orders for pickup or delivery for some of the restaurants. Go to slo.menuclub.com for a complete guide for the dining in San Luis Obispo.
