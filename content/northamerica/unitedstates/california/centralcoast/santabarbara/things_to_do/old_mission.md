Founded in 1786 and still an active parish, Santa Barbara's mission is one of the most beautiful of the California chain. The twin-towered facade and rose garden are the iconic image of the city. A small museum inside covers the mission's history. The cemetery contains the graves of thousands of Chumash people who worked and lived here.
