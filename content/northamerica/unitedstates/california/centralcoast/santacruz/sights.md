### [West Cliff Drive](/northamerica/unitedstates/california/centralcoast/santacruz/sights/west_cliff_drive)

Best place to take a stroll on the Pacific Coast. The views from here are of the Santa Cruz Wharf and the Boardwalk. You can even see two light houses. The end of West Cliff Drive takes you to Natural Bridges State Park, where in the winter you can see the monarch butterflies.
