---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Santa Maria's main draws are culinary and viticultural. The Foxen Canyon Wine Trail offers tastings at dozens of wineries in a laid-back rural setting, while the town itself is the place to try authentic Santa Maria-style barbecue. The Santa Maria Museum of Flight, on Airpark Drive near the airport, is a small but enthusiastic collection for aviation buffs.
