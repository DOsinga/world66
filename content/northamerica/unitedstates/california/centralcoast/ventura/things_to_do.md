---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Ventura's main attractions are the Channel Islands, the beach, and the historic downtown.

**Channel Islands National Park** — Boat trips depart from Ventura Harbor to Santa Cruz Island (the most popular), Anacapa, and the outer islands. Hiking, sea kayaking through caves, snorkelling, and wildlife watching. Book in advance in summer.

**Mission San Buenaventura** — The last of the 21 California missions founded by the Franciscans. Small museum and a peaceful garden.

**Ventura County Museum of History and Art** — 100 E. Main St. Regional history from Chumash times through the oil era. Good rotating exhibitions.
