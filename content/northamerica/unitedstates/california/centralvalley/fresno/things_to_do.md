title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Fresno's attractions lean toward nature and family outings, with a few quirky surprises mixed in.

**Forestiere Underground Gardens** — Sicilian immigrant Baldassare Forestiere spent 40 years hand-digging this network of underground rooms, passageways, and gardens as an escape from the valley heat. There are fruit trees growing underground. One of the most unusual things you'll see anywhere in California. 5021 W. Shaw Ave.

**Chaffee Zoological Gardens** — A solid regional zoo in Roeding Park, with African adventure and stingray exhibits. Good for families. 894 W. Belmont Ave.

**Rotary Storyland and Playland** — A children's theme park next to the zoo in Roeding Park, with storybook-themed play areas and rides. 890 W. Belmont Ave.

**Legion of Valor Museum** — A small military museum dedicated to recipients of the Medal of Honor and other valor awards. 2425 Fresno St.

**River Park** — An open-air shopping and dining center anchored by a large movie theater. One of the more pleasant spots in Fresno for an evening out. 7750 N. Blackstone Ave.
