Housed in Hanford's original 1905 Carnegie Library building, this local history museum covers Kings County from the days of the Yokuts people through the ranching era and the Chinese railroad communities. Small but well-curated, with rotating exhibits alongside the permanent collection. The building itself — a classic Carnegie design — is worth a look.

**Address:** 109 E. 8th St., Hanford 93230. **Phone:** 559-584-1367.
