A 1929 movie palace that has been beautifully restored and still operates as a live performance venue. The ornate interior — with its original plasterwork and atmospheric lighting — is the real draw; catching a show here is a treat. Check the schedule online before you visit as programming varies.

**Address:** 326 N. Irwin St., Hanford 93230. **Phone:** 559-584-7423.
