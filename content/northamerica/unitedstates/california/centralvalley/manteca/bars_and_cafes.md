### [Delicato Vineyards](/northamerica/unitedstates/california/centralvalley/manteca/bars_and_cafes/delicato_vineyards)

The fourth largest family run winery in the world, is just one of the many wineries that do the Manteca area. Started in 1924, the third generation of the Indelicato family is now producing wine and have been named US Wine producer of the Year for an unprecedented three times at the International Wine & Spirits Competition. The Manteca wine tasting room and gift shop is open daily (Except Christmas) from 9 a.m. to 5:30 p.m.



tel: 209-824-3600

address: 12001 S Highway 99

### [Gnekow Family Winery](/northamerica/unitedstates/california/centralvalley/manteca/bars_and_cafes/gnekow_family_wine)

Wine tasting room is open daily from 11 a.m. to 5 p.m. 10 miles northeast of Manteca. Go online to www.gnekowwinery.com



tel: 1-800-446-3569

address: 17347 East Gawne Road, Collegeville

### [Kelley Brothers Brewing Co.](/northamerica/unitedstates/california/centralvalley/manteca/bars_and_cafes/kelley_brothers_br)

Offers a unique line of its own spirits as well as those from the grape. Accommodates 450 people in the remodeled shell of the former El Rey Theatre that burned following the showing of "The Towering Inferno" on August 9, 1975. For more information go online to www.kelleybrewing.com



tel: 209-825-1727

address: 112 E Yosemite Ave

### [Chocolate](/northamerica/unitedstates/california/centralvalley/manteca/bars_and_cafes/chocolate)

You have three places you can get chocolate. There is a Ghiradelli factory outlet store on Roth Road fronting Interstate 5 near the Roth Road exit in Lathrop. It also features its famous ice cream fountain. For more information go to www.ghiradelli.com. There is a Rocky Mountain Chocolate Factory at 303 East Yosemite Avenue. Call 209-823-6500 for more information. There is also a fudge shop inside of Bass Pro Shops' Union Road and Highway 120 store.



### [Almonds](/northamerica/unitedstates/california/centralvalley/manteca/bars_and_cafes/almonds)

The Blue Diamond Growers Store is 10 miles south of Manteca off Highway 99 in Modesto. Call 209-545-3222 for more information. California SnackCo, 309 Cherry Lane, has specialty nuts and chocolates. Information can be accessed at www.californiasnackco.com or call 209-603-7961



### [California Fresh Produce](/northamerica/unitedstates/california/centralvalley/manteca/bars_and_cafes/california_fresh_p)

Generations of Bay Area travelers heading to and from Yosemite Valley as well as the Sierra and the Gold Country have picked up California fresh produce raning from local almonds and watermelons to local corn, apples, tomatoes and more at half dozen fruist stands that can be found along East Highway 120 between Highway 99 in Manteca and Escalon



### [Cheese](/northamerica/unitedstates/california/centralvalley/manteca/bars_and_cafes/cheese)

If you want some unique cheese, it's an 18-mile drive from Manteca past almond orachards, grape vineyards and dairies to the Oakdale Cheese facotry on Highway 120. Tours can be arranged by calling in advance. For more information go online to www.oakdalecheese.com



tel: 209-848-3139

address: 10040 State Hwy. 120
