---
title: "Bars and Cafes"
type: section
---

The Manteca area is almond and wine country — several wineries within a short drive offer tasting rooms. The Delicato Vineyards tasting room on Highway 99 is among the largest, while smaller family operations like Gnekow Family Winery are worth the short drive into the country. Kelley Brothers Brewing Co. in downtown Manteca, in a converted theatre building, handles the beer side of things.
