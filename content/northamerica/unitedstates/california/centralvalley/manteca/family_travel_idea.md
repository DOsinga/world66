### [Big League Dreams](/northamerica/unitedstates/california/centralvalley/manteca/family_travel_idea/big_league_dreams)

Ever dream of playing on Wrigley Field? Do you daydream about what it would be like to hit one over the infamous Green Monster left field fence at Fenway Park? You don't have to travel to Chicago or Boston, just to Manteca where you'll find the big League Dreams sports complex and indoor soccer arena. Six Major League Baseball fields have been replicated in proportionate size for softball play right down to details that a die-hard baseball buff would appreciate. The year-round facility has artificial turf plus two restaurants where fans can catch the action at [more..](/northamerica/unitedstates/california/centralvalley/manteca/family_travel_idea/big_league_dreams)



tel: 209-824-2400

### [Soccer](/northamerica/unitedstates/california/centralvalley/manteca/family_travel_idea/soccer)

Not only does Manteca have the premier indoor soccer arena for recreation play in the Northern San Joaquin Valley but it has 10 highly regarded soccer fields including eight at Woodward Park-with four lighted fields. The soil conditions means play can go on in Manteca even during the rainy season when those in the Bay Area often can't even practice due to poor drainage. By having eieght soccer fields in one place, Manteca hosts a variety of various skilled recreation soccer tournaments. An 11th field is now being added as well.



tel: 209-823-8470

### [BMX Park](/northamerica/unitedstates/california/centralvalley/manteca/family_travel_idea/bmx_park)

A state-of-the-art BMX Park is expected to open in mid-2009 at Spreckels Recreation Park. Unlike other BMX parks located on the soutskirts of towns, this is in the heart of the community near shopping and services. Manteca also offers 8 miles of bike paths, a skate park, an interactive water play feature as well as 52 parks for recreational use.



tel: 209-823-8470
