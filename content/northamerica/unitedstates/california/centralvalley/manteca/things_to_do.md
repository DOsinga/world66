### [Dell'Osso Farms Corn Maze](/northamerica/unitedstates/california/centralvalley/manteca/things_to_do/dellosso_farms_co)

More than 125,000 people visit the Dell'Osso Corn Maze each year next door to Manteca in Lathrop off Interstate 5. It features a 40 acre corn maze seen either by the light of day or by the glimmer of stars and flashlights in the evening. There is also a haunted house, games, go-carts, pumpkins sales, hayrides and more. but the star attraction is the pumpkin blasters. Farmer Ron Dell'Osso has designed and had siz "pumpkin blasters" welded from steel. For $5, you get a bucket of ammo--12 mini-pumpkins to be exact--that you can fire away at targets that [more..](/northamerica/unitedstates/california/centralvalley/manteca/things_to_do/dellosso_farms_co)

### [The California Delta](/northamerica/unitedstates/california/centralvalley/manteca/things_to_do/the_california_del)

Unparalleled for water activites with 1,000 miles of meandering waterways. This is where over 70 percent of California's water flows either to San Francisco Bay or south into the California Aqueduct. manteca is near the Mossdale Boat Launch where Interstate 5 crosses the San Joaquin river and a host of smaller launch sites. ### [Stanislaus River Parks](/northamerica/unitedstates/california/centralvalley/manteca/things_to_do/stanislaus_river_p)

Stretch for some 20 miles from historic Kinights Ferry with the longest covered wooden bridge to Manteca's backyard. There is hiking, picnicking, camping, fishing, rafting, and more along the lazy river with its canopy of shade blocking out the hustle and bustle of every day life.

For information on the park system: www.spk.usace.army.mil/organizations/cespk-co/lakes/stanislaus.html or call 209-881-3517

For information on rafting: Sunshine Rafting Adventure-www.rafadventure.com or call 1-800-829-7238, River [more..](/northamerica/unitedstates/california/centralvalley/manteca/things_to_do/stanislaus_river_p)

type: Rafting



### [Caswell Memorial State Park](/northamerica/unitedstates/california/centralvalley/manteca/things_to_do/caswell_memorial_s)

Offering one of the bigest remaining riparian woodlands that once dominated the Central Valley landscape. The park is siz miles south of Manteca via Austin Road off Highway 99 interchange. There is swimming, hiking, trails and camping.

### [Manteca Golf Course](/northamerica/unitedstates/california/centralvalley/manteca/things_to_do/manteca_golf_cours)

Manteca boasts a challenging 18-hole golf course that has the most play in the area due to design, pricing, and drainage. There are also two 9-hole golf courses within 6 miles of Manteca including Jack Tone golf Course in Ripon--go online at www.jacktonegolf.net or call 209-599-2973--and the French Camp RV Park and Golf Course in French Camp--go online at www.frenchcamp.com or call 209-234-3030

type: Golf



tel: 209-825-2500

url: [www.mantecagolf.org](http://www.mantecagolf.org)

address: 305 North Union Road
