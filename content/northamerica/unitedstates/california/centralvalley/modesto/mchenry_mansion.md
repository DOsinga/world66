A beautifully preserved Victorian house museum built in 1883 for Modesto's first mayor, Robert McHenry. The mansion was later associated with the Gallo wine family, who helped fund its restoration. Furnished with period antiques, it offers a vivid window into the wealth and taste of California's Central Valley in the Gilded Age. Free tours run Thursday through Sunday.
