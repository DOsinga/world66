### [Kings River Arts Council](/northamerica/unitedstates/california/centralvalley/reedley/thingstodo/kings_river_arts_c)

The Kings River Arts Council does their best to provide many art shows and exhibits year round.

Third Thursday's of every month is ArtHop on Reedley's G Street - ArtStop. Local businesses display original artwork in their location, open their doors from 5:00 to 8:00 pm to host the artist reception and public attendees to meet the artist and view the wonderful artwork. All of the ArtStop locations are within walking distance of one another. Parking is easy and free, and their are several restuarants, cafes, and a pub on G Street.
