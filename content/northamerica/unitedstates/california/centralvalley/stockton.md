Sample the asparagus in Stockton. Then, cool down at the Oakwood Lake Waterpark in Manteca.
 The surrounding Delta, formed by the Sacramento, San Joaquin, and Mokelumne rivers, provides superb boating and fishing. Angle for striped bass and, in the fall, salmon and steelhead. Rent a houseboat to explore the waterways and waterfront towns of Brannan Island, Locke, Isleton, Brentwood, and Walnut Grove. Near Suisun City, the Grizzly Island Wildlife Area is a prime spot for hiking, bicycling, and taking wildlife photographs.
