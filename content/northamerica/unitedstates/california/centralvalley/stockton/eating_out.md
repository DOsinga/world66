---
title: "Eating Out"
type: section
---

For a large city, Stockton is not so well represented in the dining category. There are several good Mexican restaurants, such as Il Grullenese on Charter Way (watch out, this is an "exciting" area), or El Fogon on Thornton Avenue. There is excellent Chinese food, and the French place, Le Bistro, on Benjamin Holt, is good but overpriced for the area. Italian food is simply not good. The Greek place, Papagollos (I think) at the end of Pacific Ave is great. 

There is a section of town starting at Pacific Ave and Harding st called "the Miracle Mile". Here are several good restaurants and a few decent pubs.
