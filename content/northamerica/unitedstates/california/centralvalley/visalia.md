Picturesque Visalia, one of the oldest cities in the valley, is a gateway to the Sequoia and Kings Canyon national parks. Visit the Central California Chinese Cultural Center to see artifacts and tapestries. Savor nature in the nearby Kaweah Oaks Preserve.
