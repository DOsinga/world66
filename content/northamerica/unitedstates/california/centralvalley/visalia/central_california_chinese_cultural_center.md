A small but thoughtful museum documenting the history of Chinese immigrants in the Central Valley. The collection includes historic tapestries, ceramics, and cultural artifacts, along with exhibits on the Chinese laborers who built the railroads and worked the valley's farms. It's a piece of local history that doesn't get much mainstream attention, which makes a visit feel particularly worthwhile.
