Located inside Mooney Grove Park, this county museum covers the full sweep of Tulare County history — Native American basketry, Gold Rush artifacts, Dust Bowl photographs, and exhibits on the waves of immigrants who shaped the valley. The Native American basket collection is particularly impressive. The park itself, with its ancient oaks and picnic areas, is worth a visit in its own right.
