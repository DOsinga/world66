Another haven for rest and relaxation is Palm Springs.
 
 Visit the Palm Springs Desert Museum and you'll find Western art and Native American artifacts. Get a splendid view of the valley on the Palm Springs Aerial Tramway, a 14-minute ride up 10,800-foot Mt. San Jacinto to the pines and cool mountain air.
