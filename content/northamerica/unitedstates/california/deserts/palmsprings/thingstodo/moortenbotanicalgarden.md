The Moorten "Desertland" Botanical Garden is a compact showcase of
desert trees and cacti. Four-acre Moorten Botanical Garden nurtures
more
than 3,000 plant varieties in settings that simulate their original
environments. Native American artifacts, rock, and crystal are
exhibited. Every square inch is packed with desert specimens. It is
truly a desert gem, well worth a visit. The map you receive upon
entering indicates the different regions where the plants are native.

Monday through Saturday from
9:00 to 4:30 (except closed on Wednesdays) and on Sunday from 10:00 to 4:00
