The Public Domain is located just west of the town of Mojave, CA. It is comprised of a 2.5 acre parcel of land, on the side of a windswept hill that is surrounded on three sides by a privately-owned wind farm. This piece of land will hopefully someday become a permanant public commons, available for anyone in the world to use and share in perpetuity - and protected from eminent domain. For more information please visit www.thisisthepublicdomain.org
