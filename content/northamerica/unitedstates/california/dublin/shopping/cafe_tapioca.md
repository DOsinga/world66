A family-owned bubble tea spot on Regional Street that has built a loyal following in the Tri-Valley. The menu runs deep — dozens of pearl tea flavors including some unusual options like avocado green tea smoothie alongside the standard fruit blends. You can get any drink with or without tapioca pearls, and they also serve light food including decent chicken dishes. Nothing fancy, just a friendly neighborhood shop that gets the basics right.
