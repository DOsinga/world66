Housed in the old Wells Fargo building at 823 Sutter Street — itself a historical artefact, with its original vault still intact — the Folsom History Museum covers the town's Gold Rush origins, its role as a Pony Express stop, and the story of the first long-distance electrical transmission in the United States, which ran from Folsom to Sacramento in 1895. Small but well-curated. Phone: 916/985-2707.
