A small zoo in Folsom City Park that specialises in animals that cannot be returned to the wild — rescued or injured wildlife rather than animals bred in captivity. It is modest in size but genuine in purpose, and makes for a relaxed hour, particularly with children. Located at the corner of Natoma and Stafford Streets in the city park. Phone: 916/351-3527.
