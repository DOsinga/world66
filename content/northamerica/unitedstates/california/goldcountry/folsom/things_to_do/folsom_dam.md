Folsom Dam spans the American River about five kilometres north of the city centre and holds back Folsom Lake, a 115-square-kilometre reservoir that serves as the main water supply for the Sacramento region. The dam itself is a large concrete gravity structure completed in 1955. Tours of the interior — through the tunnels and inspection galleries — run on selected days and are worth booking ahead. Even without a tour, the view from the road across the spillway is impressive. Address: 7794 Folsom Dam Road. Phone: 916/989-7275.
