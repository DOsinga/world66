In Murphys, join Ulysses S. Grant, Mark Twain, and Black Bart in the guest log of the Murphys Hotel. Wineries, shops, and bed-and-breakfasts add to the town's charms. Head east toward Arnold to see the giant sequoias in Calaveras Big Trees State Park.
