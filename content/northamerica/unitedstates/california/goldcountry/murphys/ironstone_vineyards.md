Part winery, part Gold Rush museum, part spectacle. Ironstone offers complimentary tours of its underground wine caves and a Heritage Museum that includes one of the largest crystalline gold specimens ever found. The tasting room, gourmet deli, and event calendar make it easy to spend a half-day here.
