title: "Mercer Caverns"
type: poi

---

Discovered in 1885 by prospector Walter Mercer, these limestone caverns are a genuine surprise — a series of chambers decorated with aragonite crystals, stalactites, and cave formations that took millions of years to grow. Guided tours run regularly and last about an hour. The caverns stay a cool 55°F year-round, so bring a layer.
