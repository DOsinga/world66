Nevada City once was a seasonal getaway for the wealthy. Attractions include the Nevada Theatre (the state's oldest continuously operating theater), Miners Foundry, and the Firehouse Museum, as well as Malakoff Diggins State Historic Park, site of the largest hydraulic gold mine. Today, the area features flourishing wineries.
