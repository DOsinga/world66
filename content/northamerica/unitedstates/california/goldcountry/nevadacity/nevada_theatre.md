California's oldest continuously operating theater building, open since 1865. The rustic Victorian building on Broad Street seats 240 on the main floor plus balcony, and runs a mix of live performances, films, and community events year-round. Even if you don't catch a show, the exterior is worth a look — it's one of the anchor buildings of the historic downtown.
