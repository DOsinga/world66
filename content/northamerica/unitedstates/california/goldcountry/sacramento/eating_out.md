### [Fox and Goose Public House](/northamerica/unitedstates/california/goldcountry/sacramento/eating_out/fox_and_goose_publ)

Tasty English food. Also, many selections for vegetarians. Just may be the best breakfast in Sacramento. Live Music Monday-Saturday nights including "open mic" every Monday, one of Sacramento's greatest traditions since the early 70's. A place to enjoy Sacramento's great talented musicians while enjoying a drink or a bite to eat.

type: International



[[rate it]](/northamerica/unitedstates/california/goldcountry/sacramento/eating_out/fox_and_goose_publ)

address: 1001 R Street
