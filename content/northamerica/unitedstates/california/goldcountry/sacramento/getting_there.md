---
title: "Getting There in Sacramento"
type: section
---

Sacramento travel is a breeze with Southwest Airlines providing service to and from Sacramento International Airport. Southwest flies between over 45 cities nationwide. To find more information, such as fares and scheduling, visit their website at southwest.com. Other airlines carriers servicing Sacramento are United, American, US Airways, Frontier, Delta, Northwest, Alaska, and Continental. Amtrak provides service as well and has a train station in the city at 401 I Street. For directions, go to amtrak.com If you want to travel by bus, Greyhound (greyhound.com) is your best option. The bus depot is at 715 L Street.
