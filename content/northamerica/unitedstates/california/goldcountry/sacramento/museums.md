### [Towe Museum](/northamerica/unitedstates/california/goldcountry/sacramento/museums/towemuseum)

Classic cars of all shapes and sizes. The Towe Museum has everything from some of the first "horseless carriages" to newer classics. Inexpensive entrance fee goes toward the continuation of the museum. Exhibits change regularly so there is always something new when you visit!

type: general



tel: 916/442-6802
