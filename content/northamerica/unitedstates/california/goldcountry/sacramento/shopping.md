---
title: "Shopping in Sacramento"
type: section
---

To enjoy Sacramento shopping, take a trip to Arden Fair Shopping Center on Arden Way. Visitors can find the latest fashion at great prices in stores such as Aeropostale, Aldo, Armani Exchange, Banana Republic, Cache, Chico's, Coach, J. Crew, Kate Spade, Nordstrom, and Juicy Couture. When you get hungry, enjoy great tasting food at Johnny Rockets or Panda Express. For desert, indulge yourself with some homemade, hand-mixed ice cream from Cold Stone Creamery. There is also an indoor carousel for children to ride. Afterwards, take them to the Build-A-Bear Workshop store. For directions, check out their website at ardenfair.com.
