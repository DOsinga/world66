---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment in South Lake Tahoe"
type: section
---

Twenty-four hour gaming is not the only thing South Lake Tahoe severs
up for nightlife. The South Lake Tahoe nightlife is vibrant and
exciting with a eclectic group of bars, dance clubs, and pubs that make
after dark as fun as the day time romps in the mountains and lake.
Enjoy a cocktail at Nightclubs like Vex, Blu, and Opal Ultra lounge,
where the fun and dancing go all night long. South Lake Tahoe Nightlife
is a great way to wind down after playing in South Lake Tahoe’s
beautiful outdoor playground. South Lake Tahoe Nightlife has something
for everyone. More nightlife information at www.southlaketahoe.com/nightlife
