**Lake Elsinore, California is an gem in a beautiful setting. Accessible enough to be an easy daytrip from any Southern California location, the Southland's only *natural* lake, Lake Elsinore features jet-sking and motor-boating or fishing. The many entertainments of Lake Elsinore are *handgliding from elevations along the famous Ortega Highway* *leading into town*, p****arachuting from the local airport by the lake, m*otorcycle rentals and* *dirt track by the lake*, wine and hot air balloon tours in nearby Temecula, casino gambling and great shows at Penchanga Casino in nearby Temecula. Lake Elsinore is a great bargain now for a vacation home.**

**Lori Heston, Lake Elsinore**
