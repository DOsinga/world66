Scandia Amusement Park is a leader in safe, clean family fun. Some 25 rides and attractions include go-karts, bumper boats, two miniature golf courses, batting cages, and a newly refurbished arcade. The “Scandia Screamer” is the Inland Empire ’s tallest roller coater. Scandia also provides excellent banquet and picnic facilities. Located on the I15 between I10 and Route 60. Open 7 days a week at 10 a.m. Call for seasonal closing hours, group events and catering details. 1155 S. Wanamaker Avenue, Ontario , CA 91761 -- (909) 390-3091

Dave & Buster’s -- It’s always a good time at Dave & Buster’s! The fabulous menu offers a variety of gourmet salads, steaks, ribs & seafood and is the perfect environment to enjoy happy hour or just some time with the family! Dave & Buster’s specializes in business meetings, murder mystery dinner theaters and private events for groups of 10 - 3,000 in a private or semi private environment. 60,000 sq. ft. of event space including 16 pocket billiard tables, 4 shuffleboard tables, golf simulators, two full service bars, audio visual accessibility and 250 video simulators and arcade games. 4821 Mills Circle, Ontario, CA 91764 909-987-1557

GameWorks at the Ontario Millsserves as the ultimate playground for the young and young at heart. An unmatched entertainment zone that invites guests to eat, drink, party, play, or simply unwind. GameWorks strives to meet a variety of moods and needs by offering finger foods at the snack bar, scrumptious meals at the sizzling grill, drinks with flair at the full-service bar, and a massive, high-intensity gaming floor categorized into separate zones, including “The Loading Dock” and “The Arena,” which allows guests to zoom right to their favorite games and attractions. Ask about group events, team building and catering services. 4541 Mills Circle, Ontario, CA 91764 (909) 987-4263
