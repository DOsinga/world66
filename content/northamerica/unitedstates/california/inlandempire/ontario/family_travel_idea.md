---
title: 'Family Travel Ideas'
type: section
---

**Scandia Amusement Park** -- A good option for family fun with go-karts, bumper boats, two miniature golf courses, batting cages, and an arcade. Located on I-15 between I-10 and Route 60. Open daily from 10 AM. 1155 S. Wanamaker Avenue. Tel: 909-390-3091.

**Cucamonga-Guasti Regional Park** -- The water slides and pedal boat rentals keep kids happy, and there is plenty of space for picnics and running around.

**Planes of Fame Air Museum** -- Kids who like planes will love seeing real World War II fighters up close. The first-Saturday-of-the-month flying demonstrations are a highlight.
