**Ontario Museum of History and Art** -- A small but worthwhile museum highlighting the city's heritage, with exhibits on Route 66, Euclid Avenue, and the citrus industry. Their storytellers program, artist workshops, and musical events add life to the permanent collections. Free admission. Open Wednesday through Sunday, noon to 4:00 PM. 225 S. Euclid Avenue. Tel: 909-983-3198.
