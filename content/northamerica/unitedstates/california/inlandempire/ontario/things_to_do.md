---
title: 'Things to Do'
type: section
---

**Cucamonga-Guasti Regional Park** -- A 150-acre park with two lakes for fishing and swimming, pedal boat rentals, water slides, and plenty of picnic space. A good family day out.

**Graber Olive House** -- Tour this working olive cannery dating from 1894. The small museum explains the olive-curing process, and you can sample and buy olives in the gift shop. The shaded picnic area under old olive trees is a pleasant spot.

**Planes of Fame Air Museum** -- Located at the nearby Chino Airport, this museum has an impressive collection of flyable World War II aircraft. On the first Saturday of each month, one of the planes takes to the air for a demonstration flight.
