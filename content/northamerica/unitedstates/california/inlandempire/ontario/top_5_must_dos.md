---
title: "Top 5 Must Do's"
type: section
---

**1. Shop Ontario Mills** -- This 1.7-million-square-foot outlet center has more than 200 stores, from Polo Ralph Lauren to a LEGO outlet. Most area hotels offer free shuttle service. Located at the intersection of I-10 and I-15.

**2. Tour Graber Olive House** -- One of the oldest working olive canneries in California, dating from 1894. Walk through the aging cellars, taste the olives, and buy a jar to take home.

**3. Catch a show at Citizens Business Bank Arena** -- This 10,000-seat venue hosts concerts, minor-league hockey, NBA Development League basketball, and family shows year-round.

**4. Visit Halgren's Candies** -- A local institution for handmade gourmet candy: brandied cherry cordials, caramel-dipped chocolate-covered apricots, and 12-inch chocolate-covered strawberries. 1206 North Grove Avenue. Tel: 909-986-4836.

**5. Explore Logan's Candies** -- Handmade candies since 1933, with more than 200 varieties including hand-dipped chocolates, truffles, and fudge. The holiday candy-making demonstrations are a local tradition. 125 West B Street. Tel: 909-984-5410.
