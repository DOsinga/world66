### [Annual Redlands Bicycle Classic](/northamerica/unitedstates/california/inlandempire/redlands/festivals/annual_redlands_bi)

Since 1985, not too long after the Summer Olympics, the city of Redlands decided to gather a plan and start a professional bike
race. The races did not attract many professionals when they were first
initiated, since many bicyclists were around the world competing. The
first professionals to race in the Redlands Bicycle Classic, included
several members of the U.S. team and they were the highlight of the
events, taking home all 4 awards. An event that was started in 1985
with a budget of about $14,000 is now an event that has a $55,000
combined budget for both men and women.
