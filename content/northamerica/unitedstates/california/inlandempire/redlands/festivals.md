type: section

**Redlands Festival** -- Every spring, Redlands heats up with more than 30 events celebrating citrus heritage and culture: dinners and tours, concerts, a flower show and garden tour, Art in the Park, a Cinco de Mayo celebration, and a Salute to Redlands Citrus at Kimberly Crest House and Gardens.

**Redlands Bicycle Classic** -- A professional cycling race held annually since 1985, shortly after the Los Angeles Summer Olympics inspired the event. The multi-day race now attracts top national and international riders and draws spectators along the tree-lined streets. [redlandsclassic.com](http://www.redlandsclassic.com)

**Inland Empire Jazz & Blues Festival** -- A weekend of live jazz and blues that rotates venues in the wider Inland Empire area. Check local listings for dates and locations.
