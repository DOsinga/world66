Kimberly Crest
is modeled after a French chateau and has been lovingly restored. In
fact, it has been lived in by only three families since being built in
1897 by Mrs. Cornelia Hill. It was bought by Mrs. Helen Kimberly, and
then Mrs. Elizabeth Shirk. For people familiar with Hollywood, the
Magic Castle in Hollywood is an exact mirror image of Kimberly Crest.
The Magic Castle was remodeled as a private nightclub for magic shows and dining. Admission donation is $7 for adults, $6 for students and seniors, and $3 for children 6-12; children 5 and under will be free. Special Tours will be $6 per person.

Groups larger than 10 and school groups may schedule tours on other days. For information about group tours please contact KSA directly.
