**Redlands Bowl** -- The Redlands Bowl Summer Music Festival is the highlight of entertainment in Redlands. Running since 1924, this free outdoor concert series offers symphonies, ballet, opera, jazz, and culturally diverse music and dance. Performances are Tuesday and Friday evenings from late June through August at 8:15 PM. The amphitheater seats about 4,000 on benches with room for 2,000 more on the surrounding grass -- first come, first served. Parking is available along residential streets and in a public lot at the corner of Citrus Avenue and Eureka Street. Tel: 909-793-7316. [redlandsbowl.org](http://www.redlandsbowl.org/)
