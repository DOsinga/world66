Tri City Shopping Center in Redlands hosts more than 60 shops, restaurants, and service businesses. The center also hosts a popular series of seasonal community events: the Thanksgiving Snowtacular with imported snow and visits with Santa, the Springtacular family festival, and the Back-to-School Showtacular.
