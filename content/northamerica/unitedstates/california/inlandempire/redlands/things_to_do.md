**Edwards Mansion** -- This magnificent 14-room Victorian was built on Cajon Street in 1890 by pioneer citrus grower James S. Edwards. In 1973 it was cut in two and moved five miles through Redlands to its current site on the old Barton Ranch. Adjacent to the mansion is the Orange Tree Wedding Chapel, incorporating stained glass windows and pews from a 1904 Lutheran church. The mansion and its gardens are now an event venue. 2064 Orange Tree Lane. Tel: 909-793-2031. [edwardsmansion.com](http://www.edwardsmansion.com)
