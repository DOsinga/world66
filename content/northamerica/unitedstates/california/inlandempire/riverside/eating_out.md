**The Old Spaghetti Factory** -- A Riverside institution on Mission Inn Avenue with authentic vintage decor and pleasant staff. The menu ranges from spaghetti with marinara to chicken parmigiana, with a full bar and a good wine list. Portions are generous and prices are reasonable at $8-12 per person. 3191 Mission Inn Avenue. Tel: 951-784-4417. [osf.com](http://www.osf.com)
