### [The Old Spaghetti Factory](/northamerica/unitedstates/california/inlandempire/riverside/eating_out/the_old_spaghetti)

When entering The Old Spaghetti Factory you will no doubt notice it's authentic, charming decor and pleasant staff. OSF serves a variety of Italian dishes ranging anywhere from spaghetti with marinara to chicken parmagiana. The restaurant has a full bar including a good sized wine listing. Tasty cuisine matched with great prices equals a delightful dining experience.
