### [Chinese Memorial Pavilion](/northamerica/unitedstates/california/inlandempire/riverside/sights/chinesememorialpavilion)

The Chinese Memorial Pavilion is dedicated to the Chinese pioneers who traveled out west helping to populate the Riverside area. It is also meant to symbolize the enduring friendship between the people of the United States and China.
