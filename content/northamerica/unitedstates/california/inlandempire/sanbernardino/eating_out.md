**Le Rendez-Vous** -- A hidden jewel in a residential area of north San Bernardino, at the foot of the mountains. This quaint French restaurant has long been a favorite for travelers on the way home from [Big Bear Lake](/northamerica/unitedstates/california/inlandempire/bigbearlake) or Lake Arrowhead. Large wood beams against white ceilings add a rustic touch. The menu changes regularly and includes classics like escargots bourguignon, coq au vin, roast duckling a l'orange, and rabbit. Crepes Suzette and cherries jubilee appear frequently for dessert. About $50 per person. 4775 Sierra Way. Tel: 909-883-1231.

**Delhi Palace** -- East Indian cuisine at surprisingly low prices. The daily all-you-can-eat buffet for under $8 includes naan, tandoori chicken, lentils, and spinach. The menu is extensive, the decor is nice, and the chai is excellent. 2001 Diners Court. Tel: 909-884-9966.

**Tokyo Tokyo** -- The first surprise is the 4,000-gallon underground koi pond with a thick glass covering you can walk on. This full-menu Japanese restaurant features an exceptional sushi bar with generous portions and seasonal specialties -- in season you can get hairy crab. There are tatami rooms, a large sushi bar, and a covered patio. $25-50 per person. 990 Ontario Mills Drive, [Ontario](/northamerica/unitedstates/california/inlandempire/ontario). Tel: 888-81-TOKYO.
