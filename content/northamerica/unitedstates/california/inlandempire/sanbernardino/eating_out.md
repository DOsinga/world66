### [Delhi Palace](/northamerica/unitedstates/california/inlandempire/sanbernardino/eating_out/delhi_palace)

A real surprise! East Indian quisine at drive-thru prices. Daily
all-you-can-eat buffet for under $8 includes nan, tandoori chicken,
lentils, spinach. The menu is rather extensive. The decor is very nice.
The staff is very courteous. The chai is better than the best you have
found elsewhere (St*rb*cks).
