A hidden jewel in a residential area of north San Bernardino, set at the foot of the mountains. Le Rendez-Vous has long been a local favorite and a popular dining choice for travelers on the way home from Big Bear or Lake Arrowhead. Large wood beams against white ceilings add a rustic touch.

The menu changes regularly and features classic French dishes: escargots bourguignon, coq au vin, roast duckling a l'orange, and rabbit. Crepes Suzette and cherries jubilee appear frequently on the dessert menu alongside fresh pastries. The owner and head chef, Jean-Pierre, regularly offers specials not on the menu.

The wine list is fairly priced and well above average, with some nice half-bottles. The mandatory French onion soup gratinee is matched in taste but not exceeded anywhere, and the vichyssoise is much the same. The house salad dressing is unrivaled. About $50 per person.
