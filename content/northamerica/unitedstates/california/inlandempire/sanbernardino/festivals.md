### [Route 66 Rendezvous Festival](/northamerica/unitedstates/california/inlandempire/sanbernardino/festivals/route_66_festival)

The award-winning Stater Bros. Route 66 Rendezvous ® is celebrating its 18 th year as California ’s largest cruisin’ classic car show! Scheduled for September 13-16, 2007, the 4-day, 3-night car show is the largest Route 66 automotive celebrations on the road. Over 500,000 spectators and car enthusiasts celebrate the Mother Road as over 2,000 vehicles park and cruise 35 blocks of downtown San Bernardino , Calif.
