**Route 66 Rendezvous Festival** -- California's largest classic car show, held each September in downtown San Bernardino. Over 2,000 vehicles park and cruise 35 blocks of the city, drawing more than 500,000 spectators over four days. Activities include neon light contests, burnouts, open header runs, live entertainment, and celebrity appearances. The event celebrates the Mother Road and has been running since 1990. [route-66.org](http://www.route-66.org)
