### [San Bernardino County Museum](/northamerica/unitedstates/california/inlandempire/sanbernardino/museums/san_bernardino_cou)

The
**San Bernardino County Museum**, in Redlands, California,
is a regional museum with exhibits and collections in cultural
and natural history. Special exhibits, the Exploration Station
live animal discovery center, extensive research collections,
and public programs for adults, families, students, and children
are all part of the museum experience.

### [San Bernardino Route 66 Museum](/northamerica/unitedstates/california/inlandempire/sanbernardino/museums/san_bernardino_rou)

Located on the site of the original McDonald's brothers hamburger
restaurant. Long denied by Ray Kroc after he convinced the brothers to
franchise the restaurant. At the time they had 4 restaurant locations.
The museum is the project of Albert Okura, the entrepreneur behind
the growing Juan Pollo restaurant chain primarily found
in the Inland Empire. Admission is free.
