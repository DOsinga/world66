**San Bernardino County Museum** -- A regional museum in nearby [Redlands](/northamerica/unitedstates/california/inlandempire/redlands) with exhibits in cultural and natural history, an Exploration Station live-animal discovery center, and extensive research collections. 2024 Orange Tree Lane, Redlands. Open Tuesday through Sunday, 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM. Tel: 909-307-2669.

**A Special Place Children's Museum** -- An interactive, hands-on museum with a toddler area, face painting, and educational activities. Open Tuesday through Friday 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM, Saturday 11:00 AM to 4:00 PM. 1003 East Highland Avenue. Tel: 909-881-6514.

**San Bernardino Route 66 Museum** -- Located on the site of the original McDonald's brothers hamburger restaurant, long before Ray Kroc built the franchise. The museum is the project of Albert Okura, the entrepreneur behind the Juan Pollo restaurant chain. Free admission. Open daily 10:00 AM to 5:00 PM. 1398 North E Street. Tel: 909-885-6324.
