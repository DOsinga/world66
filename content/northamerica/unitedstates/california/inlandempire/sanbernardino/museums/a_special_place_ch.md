An interactive, hands-on museum aimed at young children. The toddler area has plenty of toys, educational videos, and a face-painting station. There is a large selection of dress-up clothing for boys and girls -- captain caps, helmets, cowboy hats, princess outfits, and plenty of accessories. A karaoke machine lets kids sing along and watch themselves on video.

Computers with educational games, an Xbox station, a child-size pool table, arts and crafts supplies, and large easels with watercolors round out the offerings. The gift shop has inexpensive trinkets, and the museum takes reservations for private birthday parties where your group gets the place to yourselves for three hours.

Open Tuesday through Friday, 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM. Saturday 11:00 AM to 4:00 PM. Closed Sundays and Mondays. General admission $5, children under 3 free, seniors $3.75.
