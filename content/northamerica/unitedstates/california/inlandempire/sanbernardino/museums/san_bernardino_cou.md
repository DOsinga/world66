A regional museum in [Redlands](/northamerica/unitedstates/california/inlandempire/redlands) with exhibits and collections in cultural and natural history. Special exhibits, the Exploration Station live-animal discovery center, extensive research collections, and public programs for adults, families, students, and children are all part of the experience.

Open Tuesday through Sunday and holiday Mondays, 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM. Closed most Mondays, Thanksgiving, December 25, and January 1. Exploration Station hours: Tuesday through Friday 10:00 AM to 1:00 PM, Saturday and Sunday 1:00 to 4:00 PM. Museum Store open Tuesday through Saturday 10:00 AM to 5:00 PM, Sunday noon to 5:00 PM.
