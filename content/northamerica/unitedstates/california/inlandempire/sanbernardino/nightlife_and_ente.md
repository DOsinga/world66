### [California Theater of Performing Arts](/northamerica/unitedstates/california/inlandempire/sanbernardino/nightlife_and_ente/california_theater)

The beautiful and historic California Theatre of the Performing Arts was built in 1928 in opulent Spanish/Moorish style and is beautifully preserved both inside and out. The theater serves as home to many San Bernardino organizations including the San Bernardino Symphony Orchestra, under the direction of Maestro Carlo Ponti Jr, the Inland Dance Theater, San Bernardino Valley Concert Association, Sinfonia Mexicana and Theater Arts International.
