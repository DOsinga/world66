**San Bernardino Symphony Orchestra** -- Founded in 1929, the oldest and most prestigious orchestra in the Inland Empire. Performs at the California Theatre of the Performing Arts. Tel: 909-381-5388. [sanbernardinosymphony.org](http://www.sanbernardinosymphony.org/)

**California Theatre of the Performing Arts** -- Built in 1928 in opulent Spanish-Moorish style, this beautifully preserved theater is home to the Symphony, the Inland Dance Theater, and touring stage productions. 562 West 4th Street. Tel: 909-885-5152.

**Morongo Casino Resort & Spa** -- A Native American casino in Cabazon with 2,000 slots, 100 table games, ten restaurants, and a 310-room hotel. Open 24 hours. 49500 Seminole Drive, Cabazon. Tel: 800-252-4499.

**San Manuel Indian Bingo and Casino** -- One of the original Indian bingo locations, now grown into a full casino with slots, poker rooms, bingo, and free lounge entertainment. They provide shuttle transportation from surrounding areas. 777 San Manuel Avenue, Highland. Tel: 800-331-9536. [sanmanuel.com](http://www.sanmanuel.com)
