Founded in 1929 by Maestro James K. Guthrie, the San Bernardino Symphony is the oldest and most prestigious orchestra in the Inland Empire and the region's first fully professional ensemble. Performances are held at the California Theatre in downtown San Bernardino, whose unique design highlights the deep, rich sound of the orchestra.

The season ranges from spectacular movie music and Stevie Wonder tributes to spellbinding performances of classical masterpieces. Office hours: Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM to 4:00 PM.
