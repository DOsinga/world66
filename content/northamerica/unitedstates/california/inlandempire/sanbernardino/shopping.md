**Ontario Mills Mall** -- The largest one-story mall west of the Mississippi, with shopping, dining, and movie screens. Located in nearby [Ontario](/northamerica/unitedstates/california/inlandempire/ontario). One Mills Circle. Tel: 909-484-8301. [ontariomills.com](http://www.ontariomills.com)

**Galleano Winery** -- A family winery in Mira Loma's Wineville area, operating since the Galleano family purchased the land in 1927. The winery sits at the southern fringe of the Cucamonga Valley, once famous for wine-growing. 4231 Wineville Road, Mira Loma. Tel: 951-685-5376. [galleanowinery.com](http://www.galleanowinery.com)

**Filippi Winery** -- One of the last two functioning wineries in the Cucamonga Valley, housed in the Thomas Brothers Winery in [Rancho Cucamonga](/northamerica/unitedstates/california/inlandempire/rancho_cucamonga), "California's Oldest Winery." The Filippi family has grown premium wine grapes in the Cucamonga area since 1922 and has earned over 200 awards. Open Monday through Saturday, 10:00 AM to 6:00 PM, Sunday noon to 5:00 PM. 12467 Base Line Road, Rancho Cucamonga. Tel: 909-899-5755.

**Hadley Fruit Orchards** -- Founded in 1931 in Banning, Hadley's sells natural fruit, butters, jams, cereals, candies, bakery goods, vitamins, and their own organic wines. Tel: 800-854-5655. [hadleyfruitorchards.com](http://www.hadleyfruitorchards.com)
