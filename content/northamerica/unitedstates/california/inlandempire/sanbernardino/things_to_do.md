### [San Gorgonio Wilderness](/northamerica/unitedstates/california/inlandempire/sanbernardino/thingstodo/san_gorgonio_wilde)

The San Gorgonio Wilderness is the climax region of the San Bernardino Mountains in Southern California. Located on the San Bernardino National Forest approximately 25 miles east of San Bernardino, the Wilderness receives approximately 200,000 visitors per year. Its 58,969 acres harbor two small lakes, meadows, streams, 100 miles of trail, densely forested northern slopes, and rugged terrain. Elevations range from 4,400 feet to 11,499 atop namesake Mt. San Gorgonio. ### [Silverwood Lake](/northamerica/unitedstates/california/inlandempire/sanbernardino/thingstodo/silverwood_lake)

Lake Silverwood is located just 30 miles from the city of San Bernadino. This beautiful 2400 acre recreation area is at an altitude of 3380 feet. The surface area of the lake is 980 acres with 13 miles of shoreline.

Trout are routinely stocked by the California Department of Fish and Game . The lake also has a significant stripped bass population along with catfish, large mouth bass and crappie. Striped Bass have been caught weighing over 50 pounds.
