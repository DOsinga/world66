Hyundai Pavilion at Glen Helen is the nation's largest amphitheater,
with the capacity to accommodate over 65,000 people. Located where the
15 and 215 freeways meet in Devore, the Pavilion hosts some of Southern
California's biggest concert events. Situated within the Glen Helen
Regional Park, the venues beautiful natural setting readily lends
itself to concerts and festivals. The concert season runs from April
through October and has featured such artists as Brooks and Dunn, Sammy
Hagar, Tom Petty, CHER, Eminem, Aerosmith, ZZTop, KISS as well as
special events like the World's famous Ozzfest and the KROQ Inland
Invasion. The state of the art sound system and venue lay-out lead to
the ideal concert experience. We welcome you to enjoy a season of
world-class music at the Hyundai Pavilion at Glen Helen.
