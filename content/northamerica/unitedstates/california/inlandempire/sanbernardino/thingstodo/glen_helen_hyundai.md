The nation's largest amphitheater, with capacity for over 65,000 people. Located where the 15 and 215 freeways meet in Devore, within Glen Helen Regional Park, the venue's natural setting is ideal for concerts and festivals. The concert season runs from April through October and has featured artists from Tom Petty and Aerosmith to Eminem and KISS, along with events like Ozzfest.
