The first Rendezvous took place in 1990 at Glen Helen Regional Park in Devore with 300 vehicles and about 4,000 spectators. A cruise down E Street and a concert were highlights of that first event. The event moved to the National Orange Show grounds in 1991, then to the heart of downtown San Bernardino in 1992, celebrating the 66th anniversary of Route 66.

The turning point came in 1997 when Stater Bros. Market, a locally owned supermarket chain, came on board as title sponsor. Attendance has grown each year to an estimated 500,000 at recent events, making it California's largest classic car celebration.
