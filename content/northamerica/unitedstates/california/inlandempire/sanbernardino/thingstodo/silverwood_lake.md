A 2,400-acre recreation area at 3,380 feet elevation, just 30 miles from San Bernardino. The lake covers 980 acres with 13 miles of shoreline. Trout are routinely stocked by the California Department of Fish and Game. The lake also has a significant striped bass population along with catfish, largemouth bass, and crappie -- striped bass over 50 pounds have been caught here.

Entry fee $8 per vehicle, boat launch $8. Bring correct cash or a check for the self-pay gate.
