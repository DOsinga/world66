Home of the Miracle Mile ski trail. In 1950, sportsman Tommi Tyndall recognized skiing as a growing sport in Southern California and persuaded friends, relatives, and ski enthusiasts to invest in Snow Summit, Inc. After two years of work with the Forest Service, construction began in 1952 on an access road, a mile-long chairlift, the Log Chute ski run, and base area slopes.

In 2002, Snow Summit purchased Bear Mountain, and today Big Bear Mountain Resorts combines both to form Southern California's number-one ski destination.
