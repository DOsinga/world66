Snow Valley is one of the original Southern California Mountain
Resorts. Revel in its long history providing recreation to millions of
outdoor enthusiasts with over nearly 70 years of excellence in winter
recreation.

Originally named "Fish Camp" for the pond located nearby (located at
the top of Chair 5); the hill quickly became a favorite among the few
locals who knew how to ski.

In 1938, Arrowhead Springs Hotel took over the site with the intention
of offering their guests skiing at the "Fish Camp" in the winter, as
well as swimming and tennis in the summer. In 1941, "Fish Camp" was
renamed "Snow Valley Ski Resort".
