One of the original Southern California mountain resorts, with nearly 70 years of winter recreation. Originally named "Fish Camp" for the pond at the top of Chair 5, the hill became a favorite among locals who knew how to ski. In 1938, Arrowhead Springs Hotel took over the site to offer guests skiing in winter and swimming and tennis in summer. In 1941, Fish Camp was renamed Snow Valley Ski Resort.

Located five miles east of downtown Running Springs.
