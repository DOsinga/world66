Founded on the Butterfield Stage Route, Temecula is the gateway to California's youngest wine country. The Temecula Valley is home to more than 40 wineries, and you can see them from a hot-air balloon, a hang glider, or simply by car. Most wineries offer tastings and many have restaurants attached.

Old Town Temecula, centered on Front Street, is a walkable stretch of antique shops, restaurants, and western-style storefronts. The annual Temecula Valley Balloon and Wine Festival in June draws crowds from across Southern California.
