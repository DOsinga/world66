title: "7 Day Itinerary in Los Angeles"
type: section
---

Historic buildings, a buzzing downtown and Hollywood, all in one city, Los Angeles. This city of Angels, is where the stars come down and make you feel like one too.
Day 1: Hello Hollywood.

Hollywood, the home of the stars, a dream for movie buffs and visited by millions every year.
Bring to life, the greatest moments in cinema by visiting the Universal Studios. Thrilling rides, daring stunts and live shows, are the attractions at the world’s biggest movie studio. Move on to the Chinese theatre and the legendary Hollywood walk of fame and end the trip with a visit your favorite celebrity homes.
Day 2 : Wax Celebrities at Hollywood Museum
It’s a tribute to celebrity figures and a celebrated Hollywood attraction. Located at the Hollywood boulevard, it has displayed its marvelous art works, which are almost as good as the originals. It homes Hollywood Biggies, Television stars and many more eminent faces .
Day 3 A date with Disney
If a magical family day is on your mind, then the Disneyland Resort is the place to be. You can visit the Disneyland park, to meet Mickey and his extended family. Followed by Downtown Disney which is a shopping, entertainment and restaurant hub.
Day 4 Tour L.A.
If you’d like to see a bird’s eye view of this gorgeous city, you can opt for a private helicopter tour. Another option would be a cruise; you can take your pick from a brunch cruise, a romantic gondola trip or a dinner cruise. They offer you a perfect blend of gourmet cuisine, sightseeing and entertainment.
Day 5 By the beach

For those who want a bright sunny day, a gorgeous tan and a lively seashore, there is Santa Monica. It offers an assortment of activities, from swimming and surfing to volleyball and biking. The vibrant community, convenient location and plentiful hotels make it a very enjoyable destination.

Day 6 Griffith Park
This park is perfect for a day in the open, whether you want to picnic or go hiking.
The Griffith Observatory is a haven for astronomy lovers and is California’s most admired attractions.

Day 7 The real L.A.
L.A. is a globally recognized fashion center. Exclusive boutiques like Beverly Boulevard offer you a cool and modern shopping area whereas the Robertson Boulevard is a sophisticated and calm version. For luxury shopping, Rodeo drive has plentiful designer stores like Chanel and Tiffany’s. Downtown L.A’s famous fashion district is a bargainer’s paradise where you get everything from fabric to shoes at very low prices.

L.A. is many cities in one and one that radiates classic glamour, timeless beauty and breathtaking scenic beauty.
