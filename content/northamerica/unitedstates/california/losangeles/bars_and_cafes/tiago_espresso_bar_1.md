Tiago Espresso Bar + Kitchen is a third wave coffee house that was established in 2009 in Hollywood California. At Tiago a Direct-Trade model is used and we are ecco friendly. Intelligentsia and Barefoot coffee roasters are the main coffee's used. Execution is rooted in the Italian tradition, in which coffee is embraced as a culinary art.

The Tiago food menu has a nice and easy diverse selection of Argentine and French inspired sandwiches, soups, salads and wraps and holds a nice balance for vegetarians and non-vegetarians alike. Orders can be placed by phone to take-away/pick-up and dining in house is also available. The menu is downloable through the website.

Everything at Tiago is made to order, paramount is freshness and manner of preparation.
