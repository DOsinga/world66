title: "Playa del Rey"
type: poi
---

Quietly tucked along the Los Angeles coastline halfway between
Manhattan and Venice Beach, Playa del Rey is most likely one of the
lesser-known beaches in the area. With its close-knit community and
lack of parking meters, Playa del Rey looks like a vacation but feels
just like home.
This sandy spot typically accommodates a smaller, more
relaxing crowd, as it sets just off Culver Boulevard in a town full of
pet friendly people and a strip full of great hamburger bars all within
walking distance. Playa del Rey is a top location for surfing, volleyball and catching some quality snapshots. Take the bike path down to the beach to Manhattan or cruise up to Venice through the Marina and parks.
For a nice lunch on a sunny afternoon, visit the Shack or stop into Outlaws for a home cooked meal while you watch the sun go down from their spacious deck. For convenience and parking with ease, Playa del Rey is the beach.
