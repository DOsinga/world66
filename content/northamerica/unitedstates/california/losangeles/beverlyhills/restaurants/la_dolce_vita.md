This famed, some would say legendary, Italian eatery
in Beverly Hills is a throwback to the good, old days in Los Angeles when folks
actually dressed up for a night on the town. The place was a hotbed for the
who’s who. The décor remains dark and sophisticated; one might close
their eyes and feel the presence of Frank Sinatra and Dean Martin holding court
in one of the plush booths. And apparently, as research points out, every
president since Kennedy has enjoyed a meal at La Dolce Vita. Nostalgia and
presidential history aside, the food remains delightful; lasagna, veal, steak, capellini,
risotto, linguini and so on – it’s all here and it’s quite superb.
