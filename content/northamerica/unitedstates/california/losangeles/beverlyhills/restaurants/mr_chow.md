A Beverly Hills institution since 1974, Mr. Chow is a celebrity haunt serving Beijing-style Chinese cuisine in a sleek black-and-white dining room. The menu is deliberately limited; the Drunken Fish and hand-pulled noodles are the dishes to order. As much about the scene as the food.
