Featured on television shows like
Good Morning America and the Oprah Winfrey Show, Sprinkles is the place
in Los Angeles to get gourmet cupcakes. This increasingly popular
dessert takes one of America's favorite baked treats to a whole new
level, offering 10 varieties a day, 5 of which are always available and
the remaining 5 of which vary from day to day. Flavors range from the
basic (milk chocolate or strawberry) to the exotic (ginger lemon or
chai latte). The shop is hugely popular, but also a bit pricey (at
$3.25 a pop), so if indulgent desserts are your thing, be sure to make
a stop here.
