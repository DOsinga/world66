A three-block street lined with flagship stores of the world's major luxury brands — Gucci, Prada, Chanel, Hermès, Louis Vuitton, Cartier — running south from Santa Monica Boulevard to Wilshire. The street is more of a spectacle than a shopping destination for most visitors; the architectural extravagance of the stores is part of the show. Adjacent Two Rodeo (a pedestrianised cobblestone street modelled on a European shopping street) adds to the effect. Rodeo Drive became globally synonymous with extreme wealth largely through its role in the 1990 film Pretty Woman.
