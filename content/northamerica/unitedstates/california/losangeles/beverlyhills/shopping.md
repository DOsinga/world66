---
title: "Shopping in Beverly Hills"
type: section
---

Beverly Hills, located in the heart of Los Angeles, has often been referred to as the "shopping mecca" of the United States. Beverly Hills
is most known for Rodeo Drive, a street blanketed with high fashion
shops and boutiques. While there is much to be said about Rodeo Drive,
Beverly Hills also offers shopping to buyers that wish to spend less
than every penny they have! Check out these places to shop in Beverly
Hills:

**Rodeo Drive**

Despite the main shopping
stretch only being three blocks long, Rodeo Drive
houses fashions right off the runway. While shopping on Rodeo Drive,
keep your eyes out for stars and celebrities. The shops that line Rodeo
Drive are mostly ones for the rich and famous. These shops include
Dolce and Gabbanna, Gianni Versace, Chanel, Tiffany's, Louis Vuitton
and many others.

Even if you don't have loads of money to drop
in these shops, Rodeo Drive is still a great place to walk around, star
watch and grab a bite to eat.

**The Grove**

The Grove is one of the hottest places to hang out, celebrity watch,
shop and dine. It is often referred to as the epicenter of
entertainment. At the grove you can walk through the park-like grounds,
watch children ride on the miniature train, shop at the finest of shops
and have the option to eat food from almost every culture. The shopping
can't be beat at the Grove, with stores like Nordstrom's, American
Girl, Nike and many more.

**The Beverly Center**
Being
one of the largest malls in the Los Angeles area, there is no doubt
that The Beverly Center has everything one could desire. The mall has
high end stores as well as major department stores like Macy's and
Sears. There are restaurants throughout the mall and even a food court
on the third floor. The mall prides itself on its diverse collection of
stores, ranging from Foot Locker to La Perla lingerie.

The mall
is open seven days a week and hours vary depending on the season. There
is a parking garage located at the corner of La Cieniga Ave. and
Wilshire Blvd.

**The Westside Pavilion**

The Westside
Pavilion is another mall in Beverly Hills that has everything from A to
Z. They have stores with designer fashions and they also have vendor
kiosks that sell homemade wares. This mall is unique because it is an
outdoor mall. Seeing that Beverly Hills is known for its beautiful
weather, it is always a pleasure to shop and dine in the great
outdoors!
