---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Beverly Hills' main activity is window-shopping on Rodeo Drive and people-watching on the surrounding streets. Barneys New York faces Rodeo Drive on Wilshire. North of Santa Monica Boulevard the residential streets climb into the hills, where movie star homes have been a tourist attraction since the 1920s — star maps are sold at newsagents throughout the city. The Los Angeles County Museum of Art (LACMA) and the La Brea Tar Pits are nearby on Wilshire, technically in the Mid-Wilshire neighbourhood rather than Beverly Hills proper.
