The circular art deco Casino Building is Catalina's landmark, rising from the point at the north end of Avalon Bay. Built in 1929 by William Wrigley Jr., it never actually housed a casino -- the name comes from the Italian word for gathering place. The ground floor holds a classic movie theatre with a full pipe organ, while the upper level is a grand ballroom with a 180-foot circular dance floor. Guided tours run daily and are worth the time for the murals alone.
