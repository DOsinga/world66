Coral Tree Express

Located in the Westfield Century City outdoor shopping
center, this smaller version of the popular Brentwood restaurant location
(Coral Tree Café) is upstairs in the comfy and contemporary food court. Great
for breakfast, lunch or dinner, the menu features organic egg scrambles, salads
and sandwiches, paninis, soups and a wide assortment of coffees/teas, blended
drinks, smoothies and deserts.

10250 Santa Monica Blvd Los Angeles, CA 90067 (310) 553-8733
