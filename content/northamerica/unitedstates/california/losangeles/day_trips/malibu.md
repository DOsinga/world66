Just north of Santa Monica rests the luxurious beach town called Malibu. This community is often known for its celebrity population and exclusive shops and restaurants. One of the more well- known Los Angeles attractions, Malibu offers beautiful beaches and a feeling of serenity that is often hard to grasp in Los Angeles. Because Malibu is so close to the heart of Los Angeles, this potential day trip will take no time at all and leave you refreshed and revived. Choose from many restaurants and boutiques offering fine jewelry and rare antiques. Surfing and hiking are some of the many activities that can be done in Malibu.
