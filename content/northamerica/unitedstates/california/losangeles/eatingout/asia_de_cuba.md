With its innovative fusion menu, hot Latin beat and renowned Philippe Starck design, Asia de Cuba at Mondrian is one of Los Angeles' hottest restaurants. International restaurateur Jeffrey Chodorow brings his critically acclaimed nouveau Asian-Latino cuisine to the West Coast. Diners may choose to be served either indoors with its chic white-on-white interior, or on the sensuous patio which includes a stunning trellis area comprised of enormously overscaled terra cotta pots planted with ficus trees and flowering bougainvillea vines.
