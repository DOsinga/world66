Located in Marina Del Rey, C&O's Trattoria provides casual outdoor Italian dining at reasonable prices. Famous for its never-ending supply of garlic bread balls, C&O's is a perfect spot for a meal with friends, birthday party, or even a date. Along with a good selection of Italian dishes, C&O's has a reasonably priced house wine (red) and friendly service as well. Street parking is limited, but nearby parking at the Marina is available for $3, a much better deal compared to the $6 valet outside the restaurant. For parties of 6 or more, make reservations in advance, but the wait is otherwise manageable for smaller parties.
