Crustacean Beverly Hills has been serving up delicious Asian cuisine since 1997. The entrance features a "Walk on Water" design complete with a serpentine aquarium that winds through the restaurants cocktail lounge, leading to the French Colonial dining room. Crustacean was considered the nations best new restaurant by Esquire magazine when it first opened in 1997 and it has since been profiled in *The Wall Street Journal*, *Bon Appetit*, and *Food and Wine Magazine*, to name a few. The menu features delicate Asian Tapas, soups, salads and sashimi, large plates, lite vegetarian dishes, and a dishes from the "AN's Secret Kitchen." An excellent example is the Colossal Royal Tiger Prawns, charbroiled with AN’s Famous Garlic Noodles paired with either Bergstrom "Cumberland Reserve" or Pinot Noir, Oregon.
