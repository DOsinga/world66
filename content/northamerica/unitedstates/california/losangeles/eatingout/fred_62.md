This 24-Hour Retro Diner gem is hidden away in Silverlake, a couple blocks north of Sunset on Vermont. Stylized ambience and service accompanied by a soundtrack that seamlessly ties all the charm and 'cool' together. A local favorite that just about defines Diner chic. Food is simple but extremely satisfying with great twists on old classics.
