Johnnie’s Coffee is the best kept breakfast and lunch spot in the Marina. Enjoy breakfast and lunch all day on the lovely patio along with music of your choice. Or pick up to go. Johnnie’s specializes in home made breakfast and lunch served all day along with THE best organic coffee drinks including: iced mochas, green tea, lattes, cappuccino, protein shakes, iced tea, soda and more. We offer soy drinks and sell ionized alakaline water by the glass too. The famous breakfast burrito is a top seller made with baby roasted potatoes, excellent breakfast frittatas, the famous McJohnnie’s Eggmuffin, delicious made-to-order lo-carb wraps, and sandwiches on French Baguette or 7-grain bread – with your choice of toppings and a FREE home made side dish- choose from crunchy coleslaw, bean salad or green salad with corn chips. NO pressed meat –real chiciken breast, turkey breast, albacore tuna, hormone-free cheeses used-- top quality ingredients. Made to order salads include: Caesar, Cobb, Veggie, Feta/Cranberry/Walnut salad, Tuna on salad, Chicken Breast salad and Greek Salad. Open 7-5 Mon. – Sat. & 8-5 Sun. We also offer pick up - any size order for singles, groups, offices and business breakfasts and lunches. Just Call 310-301-9489 to order. Check out the full menu and coffee drinks at our web site .
