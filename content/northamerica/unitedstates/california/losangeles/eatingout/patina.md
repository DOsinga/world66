This luxury Los Angeles restaurant is located in Walt Disney Concert Hall -- a downtown L.A. landmark. Patrons can select from seasonal tasting menus, the daily chef's menu, and a variety of cheese and caviar carts. Patina also features an elaborate wine list and decadent desserts, such as the Classic French Toast, Poached Apricot Cardamom Crumble with Ginger Ice Cream, served up with lunch or dinner. Signature cocktails are popular here as well as the sleek patio perfect for al fresco dining on a summer day.
