A Los Angeles staple for over 100 years, Philippe’s specializes in French dip sandwiches. The sandwiches are affordable and offer up five different types of meat including: Pork, Beef, Ham, Lamb and Turkey. As far as Los Angeles restaurants go, Philippe’s gives you a quality product for a price you can afford. In addition, its convenient downtown location allows for tourists as well as commuters to drop in for a cup of coffee or piece of apple pie covered with American cheese. Other deserts include tapioca pudding, rice pudding, banana cream and chocolate pie. When ordering, lines form in front of the counter and can stretch to the back of the restaurant. Get there early!
