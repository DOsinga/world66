With locations in South Beach and New York, Table 8 serves up an interesting mix of authentic California cuisine with a New York vibe that's reflected in the decor and in the food. Chef Govind's menu features everything from gourmet grilled cheese sandwiches with pulled short rib to olive poached sea trout. The menu also features a list of excellent desserts (try the chocolate semifreddo cake) and an impressive wine list. Guests can dine in the main dining room or in the lounge, which features a short menu ihighlighting several Table 8 favorites.
