Located in Los Angeles' hip Silver Lake area, Tantra takes Indian cuisine to a new level. Considered "modern Indian Cuisine," the Tantra menu features dishes such as Tandoori Monkfish, Black Lentils, South Indian Vegetable Stir-Fry, and Roasted Eggplant, to name a few. In addition to an exotic dining room with semi-private silk-canopied areas for guests, Tantra also features a lounge area which offers a tasty selection of foods on a special bar menu as well as signature cocktails and a DJ spinning eclectic tunes.
