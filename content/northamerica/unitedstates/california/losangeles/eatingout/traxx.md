Traxx is located in the Historic Union Station, on the eastern edge of downtown Los Angeles. The restaurant has an excellent lunch and dinner menu as well as a lively bar serving up spectacular Traxx Martini's. The dinner menu features small plates and large plates filled with fusion fare and the lunch menu features small plates, full plates, soups, salads, and sandwiches.
