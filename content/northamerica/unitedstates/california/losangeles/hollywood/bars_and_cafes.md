type: section

**Hollywood bars and cafes** are some of the best in the country. Some of these bars and cafes are frequented by movie directors, writers, producers and artists.

#### Caf&eacute; Muse

Located in the Theatre Row of Hollywood is Caf&eacute; Muse. This caf&eacute; and coffee shop features a rotating art gallery and live entertainment. It serves breakfast, lunch and dinner. Caf&eacute; Muse serves organic coffee, espresso and a wide variety of tea. It is known for vegan and vegetarian dishes, including make-your-own salad which is a favorite among the locals. If you are craving something different, try the vegan tortillas, sweet potato hash browns or the organic tofu and eggs.

#### Ivan Kane&rsquo;s Caf&eacute; Was

Caf&eacute; Was is the brainchild of trendsetter Ivan Kane&rsquo;s. Caf&eacute; Was is a bistro and piano lounge offering fine dining in a bistro-style setting. It is open every day from 6 p.m. until 2 a.m. They open for lunch from Monday to Friday starting at 11 a.m. until 3 p.m. and brunch on weekends at 11 a.m. on Saturday and 10 a.m. every Sunday. It also features live entertainment every night. Caf&eacute; Was also has a wide selection of cocktails from the classic to the custom-made drinks. You can also check out their extensive champagne and wine menu if you are feeling a little extravagant. They have an absinthe Mezzanine bar where you can just hang out and relax. The design of the caf&eacute; in itself is a feast for the eyes with its grand staircase and a balcony with a magnificent candle display.

#### Good Luck Bar

Good Luck Bar is a funky bar in the heart of Hollywood. It is located on the corner of Hillhurst Avenue and Sunset. This bar has a Chinese-style atmosphere and is decorated almost entirely in red. Good Luck Bar has a wide selection of cocktails and drinks so you can order any drink under the sun. This bar is one of the favorite hang-outs among the trendy, younger crowd, so it is advisable to get a reservation. It opens during the week at 7 p.m. and at 8 p.m. on weekends. The bar generally closes at 2 a.m.
