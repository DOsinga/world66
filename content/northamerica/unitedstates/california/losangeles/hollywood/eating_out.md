title: "Eating Out in Hollywood"
type: section
---

If you are going to see the glamor and stars in Hollywood, you can find that there are a lot of **Hollywood restaurants** that you can stop and enjoy. There is a great variety of choices, including:

#### Ca Del Sole

The Ca Del Sole restaurant offers Italian cuisine and has the look of a Venetian country inn. You can enjoy some of the signature dishes that they serve including the lobster, crab cakes and more. The authentic taste of the dishes will make you feel like you are in Italy. There is also an outdoor patio to dine on and relax.

#### Caf&eacute; des Artistes

The Caf&eacute; des Artistes restaurant serves French cuisine and is open late for those who enjoy a later dinner meal. The casual French cuisine offers many different tastes to enjoy and there are soothing walls and art in the restaurant. There is also an outdoor patio area to enjoy drinks and the views of the city. The restaurant offers the feel of a French restaurant, but is casual to relax at and enjoy the fine foods.

#### Dakota

The Dakota restaurant is a three meal restaurant that is one of the hottest places to eat in Hollywood. The executive chef prepares a number of meals to try and the classic historic hotel gives the restaurant a rich feel that you are sure to like when you are dining. The Dakota is well known for its cuisine and makes it one of the Hollywood restaurants that you may want to try out.

#### The Tower Bar at the Sunset Tower Hotel

The Tower bar and restaurant is a trendy lounge and restaurant that has a menu that features Italian cuisine and a California bistro. There are views of the skyline to enjoy while dining and relaxing with your favorite drinks. The bar has a long list of drinks to try and features a lot of trendy new foods and dishes to try out.

#### The Spanish Kitchen

The Spanish kitchen restaurant is a modern hacienda that is big with a glamorous crowd. It has low lighting, chandeliers, and the staff is adorned in traditional attire. The restaurant serves Mexican cuisine that makes it one of the best to try when you are looking for Hollywood restaurants. If you enjoy Mexican cuisine and the feel of a fun restaurant, you can find a number of great items to choose from on the menu that are popular choices.
