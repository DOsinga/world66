title: "Festivals in Hollywood"
type: section
---

Choosing the best time to travel to Hollywood could be when one of the famous **Hollywood festivals** is going on. From culture to food and everything that you can see at a festival, there are many things to enjoy.

#### Mango Festival

This summer festival is held July 9th-11th and has a lot of great activities and tasty foods for everyone to enjoy. You can taste some of the best foods and drinks that are made with mangoes. The festival is to celebrate the mangoes of India. There are games for the children to enjoy, and you can enjoy learning more about the mangoes and have your chance to buy a mango tree.

#### Hollywood Book Festival

The Hollywood book festival is held in July and is a great event for anyone who enjoys books, collects books, sells books or writes books. There are many different kinds of books that are presented at the festival, and you can enjoy seeing some of the newest releases that you can buy during the festival. This is one of the Hollywood festivals that will offer you the chance to see the authors, check out the latest in new books, and learn more about the process of publishing and selling books.

#### Cannes Film Festival

This film festival is one of the popular events that you can attend to see some of the stars from Hollywood, and also see some of the newer films that are being submitted for the contest. Film makers from all over come to the festival to try and get their short film to win the awards. If you enjoy movies and watching a variety of different kinds of movies, the Cannes Film Festival is one of the Hollywood festivals that you can check out. Many aspiring film makers come out to enter the short films that they have worked hard to create with the chance to win the best new independent film during the festival.

#### Cinecon Classic Film Festival

This festival is held on Labor Day in Hollywood. Anyone who enjoys watching classic movies, and wants to see and learn more about how they are made, and get to watch some of the new versions of the classics, can enjoy this festival. There are a lot of great activities that you can be a part of during the festival and see some of the best movies that have been made, created and produced in Hollywood that everyone enjoys watching.
