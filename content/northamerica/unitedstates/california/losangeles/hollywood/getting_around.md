---
title: "Getting Around in Hollywood"
type: section
---

Being in Hollywood can give you plenty of things to see and many different **Hollywood transportation** methods to choose from. There are a lot of ways to enjoy all that the city has to offer and to see the stars.

#### City Bus

There are a lot of bus routes that you can take around Hollywood. You can find tour lines and buses, and take a tour of the hills, including some of the mansions of the stars. If you are looking to get around in the city, you can find that the bus lines are easy to use and you can get a day pass for $3 that will give you access to any of the routes that you want to take. There is also the DASH bus service that goes between the major tourist spots that you can take for under a dollar per ride.

#### Taxi

Finding a taxi in Hollywood is not difficult, but it can take awhile to get to some of the tourist places that you want to see. If you are going to see Universal Studios or some of the other landmarks, you may want to consider taking a bus or a tour instead. The taxi is one of the more costly ways to travel the city.

#### Rental Cars

Getting a rental car in Hollywood can be one of the best ways to get around. If you are going to be seeing a lot of different places like some of the convention centers, malls, studios and other tourist attractions, taking a car can be a good option. You can find a car rental service at the airports and also in some of the tourist areas. Your rates depend on how long you need to use the car. Keep in mind that the traffic in the Los Angeles area is often bad, and it could take you longer than expected to get from place to place.

#### Walking

Getting around by foot can be a good way to explore more of the city and see everything, but some of the places like the famous studios and recording areas are further apart. If you are going to be walking, you may want to plan your Hollywood transportation around the location.

#### Rail

The Hollywood rail system is a popular choice for tourists because it will take you quickly to any of the studios, celebrity tours, and other spots that you want to see. Taking the rail is easy, and you can find the right one to get to almost anywhere that you want.
