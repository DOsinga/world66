---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment in Hollywood"
type: section
---

**Hollywood nightlife** is by far some of the best in all of California. Hollywood offers nightlife and entertainment for everyone. Just remember to dress to impress, because the nightlife here often includes celebrity sightings.

**The Roxy**

The Roxy is a one of the top nightclubs in Hollywood. The Roxy focuses on live music from national big names to local up-and-coming artists. You can also find DJ's spinning some of the hottest music tracks. Most nights, you have to be twenty-one or older to get in, but occasionally the Roxy will offer "eighteen and up" nights.

**Avalon Hollywood**

Avalon Hollywood used to be Hollywood Palace, before a few young entrepreneurs gave the nightclub a much needed face lift and turned it into one of the hottest nightclubs in all of Hollywood. You can often find big-name headliners performing at Avalon, and there are top DJs playing house and techno all night long.

**The Hollywood Improv**

The Hollywood Improv features different comedians on a weekly basis. The best way to know who is performing is to call ahead and get the schedule. The Hollywood Improv is a great place to kick back and get some good laughs in. Monday is the only day that the Improv requires guests to be over twenty-one. The other six days of the week, all ages are welcome. Just keep in mind that the show might not always be appropriate for all ages.

**Amagi**

Amagi is a Japanese restaurant during the day and happening karaoke bar in during the evening. The drinks are good and inexpensive, and almost everybody at Amagi gets up on stage to give karaoke a try, regardless of singing ability. If karaoke isn't your thing, just grab a seat by the bar and enjoy watching the action.
