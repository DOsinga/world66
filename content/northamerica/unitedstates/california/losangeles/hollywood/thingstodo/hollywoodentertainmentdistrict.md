The Hollywood Entertainment District (Hollywood Blvd. from La Brea
to Gower) includes Walk of Fame, Mann's Chinese Theatre, and
world-famous intersection of Hollywood & Vine.

Don't got there alone at night! It's nice for tourists in the
daytime, what with all the tacky gift shops and other tourists. Also, a
great place for future screenwriters to get ideas for characters. All
you have to do is just sit in a fast-food place (so that people from
all walks of life come in-mine happened to be the Jack in the Box) and
just listen to their conversations. It's like a cross between an E
Hollywood True Story (washed-up actors), wannabe actors and the whole
movie crowd, bums, and fellow tourists. Prime spot for people-watching!
But it certainly shows the seedy side of LA.
