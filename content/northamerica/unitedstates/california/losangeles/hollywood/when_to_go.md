**Visiting Hollywood at the Best Time of Year**

When planning your trip to **Hollywood, when to go **is one of the first decisions you'll make. While there are advantages to visiting the "Diamond of the Gold Coast" throughout the year, you may find one time of year is more ideal for you than the others.

#### **Spring**

The average temperatures in Hollywood in the spring months are somewhere around 60 to 66 degrees Fahrenheit, making the spring months a beautifully mild time to visit the area. Don't be surprised if the temperature dips down to about 50 degrees or reaches above 75 degrees. It's best to pack clothes in layers.

Rainfall is at one of its highest levels in March but is actually fairly minimal in April and May. It wouldn't hurt to pack a poncho, but you may not need it in the later months. Spring is the second most popular time to visit the area, so expect a few crowds.

#### **Summer**

The most popular time for tourists to visit Hollywood is the summer, when temperatures are at their hottest and the beaches are the most crowded. If you enjoy hot weather, this may be the ideal time for you. Average temperatures are in the mid-70s, although you may find temperatures ranging from the mid-60s to the high 80s. Rainfall is incredibly low, so chances are you won't need to pack your rain gear. However, be sure to pack plenty of sunscreen.

Using the beach and touring the popular spots may be difficult during the summer, since you'll have to battle the locals as well as other tourists for room.

#### **Autumn**

Autumn is a pleasant time to visit Hollywood, especially since crowds are dying down somewhat and temperatures are still warm. Expect temperatures in the mid-70s in early autumn to the lower-60s in late autumn, although don't be surprised to find temperatures as high as the lower 80s or as low as the lower 50s. Rainfall is low to moderate and picks up later in the season. Bringing along layers of clothes is a good idea.

#### **Winter**

There's never an "off season" when it comes to touring Hollywood, as the draw of the area and the year-round pleasant weather make the location an ideal place to visit any time of year. However, if you're hoping to deal with the fewest amount of crowds, winter is the time of year to go. Temperatures reach their coldest at an average of the upper 50s. Highs may be in the upper 60s and lows may reach the upper 40s. Rainfall is at its greatest, although you won't have to worry about snow or ice storms. Pack rain gear and clothes to keep you warm, but pack a few T-shirts as well.
