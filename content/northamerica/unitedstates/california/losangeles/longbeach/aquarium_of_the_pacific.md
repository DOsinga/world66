title: Aquarium of the Pacific
type: poi

One of the largest aquariums in the US, focused on the habitats of the Pacific Ocean. Over 12,000 animals representing 500-plus species, from playful sea otters to mesmerising jellyfish. The outdoor shark lagoon and the lorikeet aviary are highlights. Allow at least half a day.

100 Aquarium Way, Long Beach, CA 90802.
