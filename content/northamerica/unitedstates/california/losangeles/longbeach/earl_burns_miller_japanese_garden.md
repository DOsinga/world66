A tranquil Japanese garden tucked away on the Cal State Long Beach campus. Koi ponds, stone bridges, and carefully tended plantings make it a peaceful escape from the city. Free admission, though hours are limited — check before you go.

Earl Warren Drive, Cal State University Long Beach, CA 90840.
