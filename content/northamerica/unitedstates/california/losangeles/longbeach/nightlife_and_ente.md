type: section
---

There is plenty of **Long Beach**** nightlife** to keep anyone busy. In fact, it seems that you can&rsquo;t go a block without running into a night club, sports bar or late night restaurant. The great part about Long Beach is that there are so many different walks of life that live in the area that the nightlife tends to go along with the pattern. This means plenty of opportunity for anyone to find just what they are looking for. When visiting the Long Beach area, you won&rsquo;t want to pass up an opportunity to check out these hot spots:

**The Basement Lounge**

**Address**: 149 Linden Ave. Long Beach, CA 90802 [www.basementloungelb.com](http://www.basementloungelb.com/)

This night club features an eclectic mixture of music, art and the area&rsquo;s culture. Every night between seven and nine thirty, you can take part in their special social hour which includes drink specials and the opportunity to get to know people who live in the area. Awesome local bands play here on a regular basis and it is a great place to have a night cap or to work off dinner with some dancing.

**Cohiba Nightclub and Lounge**

**Address**: 110 E. Broadway Long Beach, CA 90802 [www.cohibalongbeach.com](http://www.cohibalongbeach.com/)

You will find a nice relaxing environment here early in the evening, with live Jazz music most nights, and an amazing dinner menu. If you stay late enough, you can get your boogie on with live DJs that spin hip hop music until the wee hours of the morning, making Long Beach nightlife extremely flexible.

**The Laughing Factory**

**Address**: 151 S. Pine Ave. Long Beach, CA 90802 [www.laughfactory.com](http://www.laughfactory.com/)

If you are in the mood for laughs, this is the best place to go in Long Beach. The weekends always offer something special and all start comedians are featured throughout the week. Shows tend to start anywhere from eight to ten in the evening, so you can easily schedule dinner and dancing around the night&rsquo;s laugh track.

**The Pike at Rainbow Harbor**

**Address**: 95 S. Pine St. Long Beach, CA 90802 [www.thepikeatlongbeach.com](http://www.thepikeatlongbeach.com/)

The Pike is the perfect place for families that are looking for Long Beach nightlife. People of all ages come to the Pike to eat, explore, shop and even swim. It is a great place for adults to get a drink while the kids have fun exploring the little shops and entertainment venues throughout the area. Pizza restaurants, ice cream stores and even an arcade help round out this venue.
