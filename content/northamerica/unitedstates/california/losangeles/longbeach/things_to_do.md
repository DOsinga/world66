**Recommendations**

**Aquarium of the Pacific**

The Aquarium of the Pacific is a celebration of the world's largest and most diverse body of water. Through dozens of breathtaking exhibits, you'll meet more than 10,000 of this ocean's inhabitants, including some 550 different species. 100 Aquarium Way 90802 562/590-3100 Fax: 562/590-3109 www.aq [more..](/northamerica/unitedstates/california/losangeles/longbeach/thingstodo/aquariumofthepacific)
