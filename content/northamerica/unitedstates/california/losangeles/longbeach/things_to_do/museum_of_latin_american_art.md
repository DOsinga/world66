The only museum in the western United States dedicated exclusively to contemporary Latin American and Latino art. The permanent collection and rotating exhibitions are consistently strong, and the sculpture garden is a pleasant spot to linger. Small enough to see in an hour or two.

628 Alamitos Avenue, Long Beach, CA 90802.
