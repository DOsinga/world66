Manhattan Beach is one of the South Bay beach towns south of LAX, sandwiched between [Hermosa Beach](/northamerica/unitedstates/california/losangeles/hermosabeach) and El Segundo. It is quieter and more upscale than its neighbours, with a pleasant downtown strip along Manhattan Beach Boulevard leading down to the pier.

The Manhattan Beach Pier juts out into the ocean at the west end of Manhattan Beach Boulevard, with a small aquarium at its tip. The beach itself is wide and well-maintained, popular with volleyball players and surfers. The Strand, a paved bike path running along the coast, connects the South Bay beach towns and is good for a long walk or cycle.
