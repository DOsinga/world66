---
title: "Bars and Cafes"
type: section
---

Marina del Rey's bar scene is largely based in its restaurants — most of the waterfront places turn into lively evening spots. Jer-Ne at the Ritz-Carlton plays lounge and house music Thursday and Friday nights. Casa Escobar has dancing nightly (live band Wednesdays, Flamenco Thursdays, DJ on weekends). Tony P's Dockside Grill is the place for sports on a wall of screens. Hornblower Cruises from Fisherman's Village offers seasonal Sunset Cocktail Cruises (spring and summer, around $30) and Friday night blues dinner cruises.
