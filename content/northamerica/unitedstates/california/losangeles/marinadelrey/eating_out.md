---
title: "Eating Out"
type: section
---

Marina del Rey specialises in waterfront dining — most of the better restaurants have harbour views or heated outdoor terraces overlooking the marina. Seafood dominates the menus. Cafe del Rey on Admiralty Way is the pick of the bunch, receiving fresh fish daily from named boats; the weekly changing "day boat" specials are excellent. Shanghai Red's and Tony P's Dockside Grill in Fisherman's Village are more casual, good-value options with proper harbour views. Chart House is a reliable seafood chain. For something different, Casa Escobar serves Mexican food with live music and dancing most evenings.
