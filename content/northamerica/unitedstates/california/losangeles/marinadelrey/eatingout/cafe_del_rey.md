Eclectic World; open daily for lunch and dinner; Sunday a la carte brunch.

With its spectacular marina view, Cafe del Rey has rooms for private events and a garden patio atmosphere enhanced with the warmth of a fireplace and floor to ceiling windows. Fresh fish is shipped to Cafe del Rey every day, landing on your plate within 24 hours of coming out of the water. The "day boat del rey" specials change frequently and are listed on the menu along with the name of the boat, the captain and the geographic area of the catch.
