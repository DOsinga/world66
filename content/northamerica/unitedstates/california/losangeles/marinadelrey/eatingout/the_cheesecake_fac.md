Located next to Mother's Beach (Marina Beach), this chain favorite has something for everyone. The signature desserts are reason alone to dine here. Enjoy your meal at one of the many tables by the large windows overlooking the marina scene or outdoors on one of the heated patios. Plus, they offer rooms for private parties, large and small.
