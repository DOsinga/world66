---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment"
type: section
---

Marina del Rey's most popular restaurants become the city's epicenter for nightlife. Live music, dancing and lively bars spring to life at night. And of course, since we are at the marina, many choices will include dance-dinner cruises.

Hear Satellite Radio DJs play lounge and house tunes Thursdays and Fridays at *Jer-Ne *in The Ritz-Carlton (Admiralty Way).

Enter the chic club-like outdoor lounge at *Glow* in the Marina del Rey Marriott (Admiralty Way).

Visit *Casa Escobar* for dancing every night (except Monday): live band Wednesday nights; Flamenco Thursdays; DJ Fridays and Saturdays; Salsa lessons Sundays (Admiralty Way).

Unwind with piano music nightly in the fireside lounge at *Cafe Del Rey *(Admiralty Way).

Live music Friday and Saturday evenings at *The Warehouse Restaurant* (Admiralty Way).

For sporting events claim a spot in front of over 20 big screen at T*ony P's Dockside Grill & Tavern (*Admiralty Way)*.*

Hornblower Cruises & Events (Fisherman's Village) offers a fabulous Wednesday Sunset Cocktail Cruise during the spring and summer months. Board at 6:00pm and cruise around the marina until 8:30pm. The price of about $30 includes complimenary appetizers and the two-hour cruise. Cash bar for Beverages.

If your looking for a dinner dance cruise, Hornblower also offers a Friday Night Blues Dinner Cruise with live music and a dinner buffet for about $70.00. And they also offer a Saturday Dinner Cruise for a bit more. Sunday morning they have a Champagne Brunch Cruise for about $50. Board at 11:30 and then cruise until 2:00pm.
