THE MARINA'S NEW CABARET - JAZZ FOR THIEVES
Every once in a while, something magical and captivating comes along that is actually worth it's salt. Jennifer's Cabaret presents Jazz For Thieves is one such weekly French-inspired cabaret show, located in the heart of Marina Del Rey in the Napa Room of The Organic Panificio at 4211 Admiralty Way, live Fridays from 8 'til late.

Every Friday night, host Jennifer Jones welcomes residents, tourists and visitors to her intimate dimly-lit cabaret room. With nearly a dozen crescent-shaped private booths, candles and recessed lighting, relaxing in style has never been easier. The restaurant and cafe offer organic cusine, wine and spirits, as well as Italian and seafood fare to satisfy your need for comfort after a long week.

Jennifer is an elegant, fun and gracious host, remeniscent of days past. She personally greeting every patron, ensuring they are well taken care of, entertained and ready to enjoy an escape to another world. Jazz For Thieves encompasses an aray of titillating entertainers, including the soulful Jazz For Thieves band, zany MC, Sandy Seacrest, dressed in a martini glass dress complete with olive, and weekly crooner Gregg Marx, Emmy-Winner famous for his daytime drama characters David Banning on Days of our Lives and Tom Hughes on As the World Turns.

Jennifer's Cabaret provides an elegant magnet for LA's top cabaret talent, such as David Lucky, cute and clever musical comedian and Comedy Central writer; award-winning comedian Tommy Savitt; Magic Castle magician Zach Waldman; and internationally-known burlesque dancer, the beautiful and sassy Stephanie Clarice Paris, fresh and inspired from her latest European tour.

Jennifer credits the success of Jazz For Thieves to a combination of good luck and continued gratitude for the performers and fans who return week after week to perform, support the show and get their sexy escape on Friday evenings to start their weekend right. She suggest arriving early for dinner and hanging out to enjoy the most magical Friday night around, right here in our very own neighborhood. There is a dingy dock for ambitious boaters to motor right up to the patio door for added fun and magical adventure.

The venue is all-ages, as the edgy show begins after 9:30 PM. Tickets can be purchased in advance for $15 on the website at www.JazzForThieves.com, or at the door for $20 and are for the entire evening of entertainment. Kids are $5 and students with ID are free when space allows. For information, call 805-708-1550.
