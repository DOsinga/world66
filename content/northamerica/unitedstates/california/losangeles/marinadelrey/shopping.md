---
title: "Shopping"
type: section
---

Marina del Rey has neighbourhood shopping rather than destination retail. The Waterside Marina del Rey Shopping Center on Admiralty Way has boutiques, gift shops, and a 24-hour Ralph's Market. Villa Marina Marketplace (Mindanao and Glencoe) is the largest mall in the area, with 85 shops, restaurants, and a 12-screen cinema. West Marine (two locations) covers all boating supplies and nautical gear.
