Marina del Rey offers neighborhood shopping within walking distance from local hotels.

*Waterside Marina del Rey Shopping Center* Local boutiques and one -of-a-kind treasures; Bank of America (ATM); Ralphs Market (open 24 hours, with deli); men, women and children's clothing; gift shops; restaurants, cafes and ice cream; boating supplies; post office and UPS shipping; open daily. Admiralty Way between Mindanao and Fiji Way.

*Marina Beach Shopping Center* Shop for essentials and local services here. Wells Fargo Bank (ATM), bagels, pet store, haircuts, quick service cafes and the favorite of locals, Joni's Coffee Roaster & Juice Joint. Open daily. Washington Blvd.

*Villa Marina Marketplace * 85 shops, restaurants, quick service cafes, and movie theaters (12 screens); 24-hour market; drug store; self-service laundry; women's, men's, children's clothing and shoes; sports equipment; books, gifts, jewelry; services including haircuts, nail salon and tanning salon; CitiBank (ATM). Open daily. Mindanao & Glencoe Ave.

*Marina Square* Restaurants, quick service cafes, coffee bar and ice cream; nail salon and tanning salon. Open daily. Lincoln Blvd. and Mindanao.
