Nautical store has ocean maps, boating nessecities and accessories, clothing and shoes for the boater, fishing license and gear. If you own a boat, this Marina del Rey shop can help you out. Two locations -- one in the Waterside Shopping Center and one on Fiji near Fisherman's Village. If you can't find what you're looking for at one locations, chances are very good that they'll have it at the other.
