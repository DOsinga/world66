The Norton Simon Museum is a well-known and prestigious museum, located in Pasadena. Housing paintings and sculptures from a wide variety of periods and movements, the Norton Simon is an excellent place to go for a day trip to relax and take in the masterworks of the last two centuries. Located on the grounds of the museum is a beautiful garden, filled with rare plants and unique sculptures. As far as Los Angeles activities go, The Norton Simon Museum is sure to both entertain and stimulate anyone who walks through its doors. In addition, admission is free for all students.
