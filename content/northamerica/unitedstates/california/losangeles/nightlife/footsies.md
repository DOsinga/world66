Los Angeles nightlife is often based on the glitz and glamour of Hollywood. Expensive bars with table service are usually the standard. If you are looking to get away from all of the chaos and noise of the city, there are some smaller bars in Los Angeles that provide their customers with amazing service and delicious drinks. Located in Highland Park, Footsies offers a quiet and intimate environment with an excellent jukebox. In addition, Footsies offers up a pool table to keep you entertained while you are having fun with your friends. The bar itself is uniquely designed with subtle themes and a long bar located on the right side of the bar upon entering.
