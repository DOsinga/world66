### [California Institute of Technology](/northamerica/unitedstates/california/losangeles/pasadena/sights/californiainstituteoftechnology)

Really nice university campus! No wonder that almost every weekend
people hold their wedding recptions, or get their wedding pics taken
there (sometimes they do both). Impressive architecture and generally a
calm, relaxing atmosphere (unless you happen to be a student!)

### [The Gamble House](/northamerica/unitedstates/california/losangeles/pasadena/sights/thegamblehouse)

Kind of boring, but architecture students would probably like it.
There are 3 USC architecture students living there at any given time
(or at least that's what the tour guide said-and this was a few years
ago).
