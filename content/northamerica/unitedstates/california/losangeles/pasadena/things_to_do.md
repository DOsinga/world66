---
title: "Things to do in Pasadena"
type: section
---

There are
plenty of activities to do and sights to see in Pasadena. This historical city is full of
shops, restaurants and museums. The city of Pasadena has a unique scene combining both
the elements of city life and that old-town feel. It showcases its own culture
and charm with its historic homes and buildings, some of which have been
revamped and opened to the public.

**Norton Simon Museum**

This art
museum contains a variety of art such as Asian and European art. It also has an
outdoor sculpture garden which makes for gorgeous photographs. Admission is
free to all visitors on the first Friday of the month from 6 p.m. to 9 p.m.
Otherwise, regular admission is $8 for adults, $4 for seniors, free for
students with valid ID and all individuals under 18 years of age. The museum is
closed on Tuesdays.**

Paseo Colorado**

Located
right on Colorado Blvd.
is Paseo Colorado.
In addition to the luxury apartments housed in this area, there are plentiful
shops and restaurants that can be visited for that spur-of-the-moment shopping
spree. Most of them are chain stores including a Macy’s department store. So if
you’re looking for something familiar, the shops at Paseo Colorado are a good place to go. **

Old Town Pasadena**

This area
has some of Pasadena’s
oldest buildings. Most of them have been converted to retail space but they are
still a great sight to see as most of its original facades have been treated
for preservation. Many local restaurants and boutiques can be found here as
well as some recognizable chain stores and eateries. Make sure to walk through
the alleys as there are hidden gems in them such as coffee shops and dessert
boutiques.

**Jet Propulsion Laboratory**

This lab is
a NASA operated lab that is responsible for monitoring all unmanned satellites
and research contraptions that get sent into space. Tours of the lab are
available to the public several times a month, usually during Monday and
Wednesday at 1 p.m. For more information on public tours, you may call (818)
354-9314.

**Walking Tours**

Whether you’re by yourself or would like to join a group, there are a number of
walking tours available in Pasadena.
One of the more notable ones is the Pasadena Public Art Walking Tour sponsored
by the City of Pasadena.
This tour takes you through various pieces of art located throughout the city. Maps
can be downloaded at the City of Pasadena’s
website. This best part about this activity is that it’s free.
