Now part of USC, this small museum is housed in a striking Chinese Imperial Palace-style building, one of only a handful of such structures in the United States. The permanent collection covers art and artifacts from across Asia and the Pacific Islands. The interior courtyard garden is a quiet retreat from Colorado Boulevard. A manageable size — you can see everything in an hour or so.
