North of Hollywood in the San Fernando Valley, visit Universal Studios [Hollywood](/northamerica/unitedstates/california/losangeles/hollywood), the famous movie theme park, and check out Jurassic Park or go Back to the Future. Shop and dine at Universal's Citywalk, and feast your eyes with a visit to the Museum of Neon Art. If television is your passion, tour the sets and see the shows at NBC Studios in Burbank. 

Griffith Park, 4,000 acres of fun in the foothills of the Santa Monica Mountains, is home to the impressive Autry Museum of Western Heritage, the stunning Los Angeles Zoo, riding and hiking trails, a great train collection, an observatory, a golf course, a 1926 merry-go-round, and much more.
