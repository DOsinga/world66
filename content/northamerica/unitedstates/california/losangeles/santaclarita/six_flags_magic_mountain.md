One of the most intense theme parks in the world, Six Flags Magic Mountain holds the record for the most roller coasters of any park on earth. Rides like Twisted Colossus, Full Throttle, and Tatsu push passengers through inversions, launches, and flying positions that leave your stomach somewhere back at the entrance. Adjacent Hurricane Harbor water park is open May through September and requires separate admission — worth combining for a full day out.

26101 Magic Mountain Parkway, Valencia, CA 91355.
