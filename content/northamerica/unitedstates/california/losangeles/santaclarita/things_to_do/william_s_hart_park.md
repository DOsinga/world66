The former ranch and mansion of silent film star William S. Hart, donated to the county upon his death in 1946. The Spanish Colonial Revival mansion sits on a hill above the grounds and is filled with Hart's personal collection of Western art and Native American artifacts — he was meticulous about authenticity in his cowboy films and it shows. The grounds are home to a small herd of American bison. Free admission to the park; small fee for mansion tours.

24151 San Fernando Road, Newhall, CA 91321.
