---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Santa Monica packs a lot into a compact beach city. The pier is the obvious anchor, but the real pleasure is in wandering — along the cliffs at Palisades Park, through the galleries at Bergamot Station, or down the Third Street Promenade as the evening crowd rolls in. Muscle Beach, the open-air gym on the sand, is worth a detour for the spectacle alone.
