A former trolley station converted into a campus of art galleries and a cultural center. The galleries show everything from photography to contemporary painting, most open Tuesday to Saturday 10am–6pm. Admission to the galleries is free. The Santa Monica Museum of Art (now the Institute of Contemporary Art, Los Angeles) started here.
