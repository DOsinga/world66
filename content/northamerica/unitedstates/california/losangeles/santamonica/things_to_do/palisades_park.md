title: "Palisades Park"
type: poi

latitude: 34.0142

---

A long, narrow strip of park running along the clifftop above the Pacific Coast Highway, with sweeping views from Palos Verdes to Malibu. Popular with joggers and morning walkers. The benches along the bluff are a good place to watch the sun go down over the water.
