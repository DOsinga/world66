A pedestrianized stretch of shops, restaurants, and street performers — the social heart of Santa Monica. Busiest in the evening when beach-goers arrive for dinner and a film. Three movie theatres within a short walk. The performers can be genuinely good; the shopping is mainstream but convenient.
