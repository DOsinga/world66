The Beverly Hills flagship of John Varvatos occupies a stretch of Wilshire Boulevard near Rodeo Drive. The brand is known for menswear that blends rock-and-roll sensibility with Italian tailoring -- leather jackets, slim-cut suits, and boots that look like they belong backstage. Prices are high-end but the quality is there. Worth a stop if you're browsing the luxury shops along this strip.
