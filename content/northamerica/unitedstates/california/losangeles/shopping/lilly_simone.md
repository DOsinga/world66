Los Angeles shopping can often be an overwhelming and daunting task. If you are looking for a place with excellent women’s clothing but without the hassle of long lines and loud crowds, then Lilly Simone Boutique is the place for you. Located in the quiet neighborhood of Eagle Rock, Lilly Simone offers premiere designers from across the country. Many of the brands and companies are of the most current fashions and often LA based. In addition to clothing, Lilly Simone offers gift items, housewares and antiques to all of its customers. Because it is located in Eagle Rock, it may be a bit of a drive for someone staying on the Westside of LA, but it is worth it.
