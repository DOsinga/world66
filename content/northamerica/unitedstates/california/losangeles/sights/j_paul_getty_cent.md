The J. Paul Getty Center (or just “Getty Center”) features spectacular grounds, unique gardens, and excellent views of the city. The Getty Center houses an outstanding collection of European paintings, drawings, sculptures, and illuminated manuscripts, as well as decorative arts, and European and American photographs. On January 28, 2006, the Getty Center unveiled the J. Paul Getty Museum at the Getty Villa in Malibu. The Malibu center location houses 44,000 works of art from the Museums collection of Etruscan, Greek, and Roman antiquities. 1,200 works are currently on display.
