---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Los Angeles rewards those willing to look beyond the obvious. The city's sheer size means its best experiences are scattered across dozens of distinct neighborhoods — from the asphalt-bubbling weirdness of the La Brea Tar Pits on Wilshire to the sublime hilltop galleries of the Getty Center overlooking the Pacific. A car helps, but not every neighborhood requires one. Give yourself more days than you think you need.
