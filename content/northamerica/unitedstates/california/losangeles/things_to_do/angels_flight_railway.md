The world's shortest railway — a funicular that climbs 298 feet up Bunker Hill — has been a Downtown fixture since 1901, though it has had a rocky history of closures and accidents. Today it runs again, offering a brief but charming ride between Hill Street and California Plaza. The two original orange-and-black cable cars, Olivet and Sinai, are as photogenic as ever.
