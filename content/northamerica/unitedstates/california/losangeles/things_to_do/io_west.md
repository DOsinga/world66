The LA outpost of Chicago's legendary improv institution, iO West runs nightly shows featuring both improv and sketch comedy. It's a training ground as well as a performance space — many of its alumni have ended up on Saturday Night Live and beyond. Shows are cheap, the crowds are enthusiastic, and it's a reliable way to have a genuinely funny evening in Hollywood.
