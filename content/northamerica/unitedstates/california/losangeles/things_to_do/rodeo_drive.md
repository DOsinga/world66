Three blocks of Beverly Hills that have come to represent a certain idea of Los Angeles excess. The shops are serious designer — Chanel, Gucci, Harry Winston — and the cars parked outside tend to be similarly aspirational. Most people just walk, gawk, and leave without spending anything, which is entirely fine. The brick-paved Via Rodeo cul-de-sac at the south end is the most photogenic stretch.
