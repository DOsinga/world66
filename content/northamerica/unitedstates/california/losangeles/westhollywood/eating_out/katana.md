Designer Dodd Mitchell’s sleek, Japanese-style bar/restaurant on the
Sunset Strip mixes Hollywood glitz and inventive cuisine. The sushi bar
is crowded. The robata bar is crowded. Women are scantily clad. Men
ogle. The outside patio is crowded and the scene is distinctive
Hollywood hip. No longer one of the ‘buzz-worthy’ destinations on the
restaurant scene, Katana remains a solid choice for appetizing Japanese
fare with an equally stylish clientele and interior design
