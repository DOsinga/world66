A culinary fixture on the Sunset
Strip since 1971 Mirabelle remains a popular fine dining bistro,
courtesy of the Germanides Family. Understated in décor (modern interiors
with a supper club vibe) the cooking is the real star here. Signature
dishes include classic crab bisque, Chilean Seabass, Filet Mignon and
chocolate soufflé. Besides the dining room and patio there is also an
oft-visited bar/lounge.
