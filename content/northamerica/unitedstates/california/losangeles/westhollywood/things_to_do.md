title: "Things to do in West Hollywood"
type: section
---

Santa Monica
Boulevard and the legendary Sunset Strip, are in West Hollywood California. This community provides many things to do
from shopping to spas. If you're looking for entertainment, you’ll find it all here.

With a walk down Sunset Strip you can see all the shops,
nightclubs, comedy clubs and other attractions. Sunset Strip became the
heart of social activity in the 1930’s, becoming infamous with its
glitzy night clubs and star activity. You can see just about every
architectural style
there are from Art Deco to Googie.

When one thinks of West Hollywood shopping automatically comes to mind.
Here you can find some of the most interesting shopping experiences of
your life. There are malls, boutiques,
high end shops, and you can find just about anything here. You can shop, or at least window shop in the
high class shops on The Avenues of Art & Design that include Robertson and
Beverly Blvds and Melrose Avenue . You can see Armani Casa, and Williams Sonoma
and the Museum of
Modern Art also resides
here.

If you are an art lover you will love West
Hollywood with its unique galleries. The Pacific Design
Center is the Mecca for interior design
and contains galleries galore as well as other shops for design. There is 750,000 square feet
of interior design delights waiting for those who love to decorate.

Several Celebrities have called West Hollywood their home including Madonna,
Liberace and Johnny Depp. Many
celebrities go to West Hollywood to enjoy
shopping opportunities and to frequent the bars and night clubs. You just might see one on the street so keep
your eyes open.

There are scores of spas in West
Hollywood that will relax and sooth your body and mind. You can enjoy facials, massages, and other
beauty regimens that will leave you wanting much more.

Entertainment venues are plentiful in West Hollywood and you can
find just about anything to suit your
fancy. The Roxy Theater has been around
since the 70’s and provides a great dance floor and marvelous music. It
is a party in and of itself. Whiskey A-Go-Go makes its home in West
Hollywood and still holds the title of best hard
rock club in the area. The phrase Go Go
Girls was born here. You can enjoy
comics at the Comedy Store and see some great adlib that will have you
laughing
your socks off.

Just take a walk up Sunset Strip and see what kind of things
you can find. It be will like nothing
anywhere else on earth.
