Manhattan Beach is one of the South Bay beach towns south of LAX, sandwiched between Hermosa Beach and El Segundo along the [California](/northamerica/unitedstates/california) coast. It has a quieter, more affluent feel than nearby Venice or Santa Monica — the Strand, a paved path running along the beach, is the social centre, busy with joggers, cyclists, and people walking dogs.

The pier stretches out over good surf breaks, and the Roundhouse Marine Studies Lab at its end is a free aquarium. Downtown Manhattan Beach, a few blocks inland, has restaurants and shops along Manhattan Beach Boulevard. It is a pleasant, low-key beach town — not a destination in itself, but a good place to spend a day if you are in the LA area and want sand without the crowds.
