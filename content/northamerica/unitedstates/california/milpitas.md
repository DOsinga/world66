The world-famous Silicon Valley in beautiful Northern California! This
extraordinary place was once known as the “Valley of the Heart’s
Delight” - for many reasons, not the least of which is its natural
beauty. In addition to all the great attractions in the area-like Great
America and the Winchester Mystery House-the views from every direction
of the Valley are beautiful.
