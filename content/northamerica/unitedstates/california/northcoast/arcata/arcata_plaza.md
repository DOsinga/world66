The Plaza is the heart of Arcata — a grassy square lined with independent bookshops, cafes, co-ops, and the handsome 1857 Humboldt County Courthouse. On summer evenings it fills with students, locals, and the occasional traveling musician. The farmers market runs here on Saturdays from June through October. It's walkable from anywhere in the city center and a natural starting point for exploring.
