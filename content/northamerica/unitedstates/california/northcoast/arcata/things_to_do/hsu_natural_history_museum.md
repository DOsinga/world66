The Natural History Museum at Cal Poly Humboldt (formerly Humboldt State University) is a small but rewarding collection focused on the ecology and geology of Northern California. Exhibits cover local wildlife, tide pool ecosystems, and regional fossils. It's free to enter and worth a stop if you're already walking the campus. The adjacent Redwood Park trailhead is just a few minutes away.

Address: 1315 G St, Arcata, CA 95521. Phone: 707-826-4479.
