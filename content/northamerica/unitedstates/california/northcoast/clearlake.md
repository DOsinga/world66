---
title: "Clear Lake"
type: location
---

Lake County is a family resort community. People come here to enjoy the water sports on Clear Lake, California's largest natural lake. Another popular pastime is sampling the wines produced by the county's six wineries. One once was owned by 19th-century British actress Lillie Langtry, a friend of Oscar Wilde. While in Lake County, be sure to sample a Bartlett pear, produced by one of the many area orchards.
