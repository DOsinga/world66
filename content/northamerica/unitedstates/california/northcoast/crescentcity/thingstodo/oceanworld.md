Ocean World is an aquarium with a fully guided tour to the wonders under the sea that is great for the whole family. You will start your journey at the touch and feel tide pool. Then wander under the waters to see a half-million gallon aquarium full of sharks, wolf eels and local rock fish. You will have a chance to pet a shark, if you dare. The tour ends with an amazing sea lion performance. The one of a kind gift shop has something for everyone in the family. With great clothing styles, toys and gifts Ocean World is ranked as one of the best gift shops on the northcoast.
