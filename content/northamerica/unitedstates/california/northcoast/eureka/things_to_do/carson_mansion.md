The Carson Mansion at 143 M Street is the most extravagant Victorian house in Eureka and one of the most photographed in the country. Built in 1886 for lumber baron William Carson, it piles on every decorative device available to the era: turrets, widow's walk, wraparound porch, carved woodwork in 18 different varieties of wood. It is privately owned by a men's club and not open for interior tours, but the exterior alone makes the walk worthwhile.
