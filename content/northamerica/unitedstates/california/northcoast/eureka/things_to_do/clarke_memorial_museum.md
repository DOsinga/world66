The Clarke at 240 E Street holds one of the better collections of Northwest California Native American basketry and regalia in the region, along with exhibits on the Victorian-era logging and fishing industries that built the city. The building itself is a 1912 neoclassical bank. Free admission; closed Mondays. Phone: 707/443-1947.
