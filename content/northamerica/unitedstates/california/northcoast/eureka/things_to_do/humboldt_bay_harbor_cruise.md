The MV Madaket, a small passenger vessel that has been running harbor tours since 1910, departs from the foot of C Street for 75-minute cruises of Humboldt Bay. It's a good way to get a feel for the scale of the bay — the second-largest coastal bay in California — and to see the waterfront from the water. Cruises run daily in summer; check seasonal schedules at 707/445-1910.
