Old Town runs along the waterfront between roughly C and M Streets, centered on 2nd and F Streets. It's the historic commercial district from the lumber-boom era, with a concentration of Victorian-era brick and wood-frame buildings that have been repurposed as galleries, restaurants, and shops. The district is walkable and compact. The boardwalk along the waterfront gives good views of the bay and the fishing fleet.
