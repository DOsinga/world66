California's oldest zoo, open since 1907, sits inside Sequoia Park at 3414 W Street — a city park anchored by a small stand of old-growth coastal redwoods. The zoo is small and free, with a focus on native California species alongside the usual mix. The redwood grove alone is worth the visit; it's one of the few places in Eureka where the trees that made this town are still standing.
