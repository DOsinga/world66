Garberville is a small town in Humboldt County on the southern edge of the Redwood region, about 220 miles north of San Francisco on US-101. It serves as a gateway to the Avenue of the Giants, the 31-mile scenic road through some of the finest old-growth coastal redwood forest in the world.

The town has a handful of motels, diners, and gas stations that cater to road trippers heading north into Humboldt County or turning off toward the Lost Coast. It is also the main services stop for visitors to Richardson Grove State Park, a few miles to the south, which has camping and hiking among mature redwoods.
