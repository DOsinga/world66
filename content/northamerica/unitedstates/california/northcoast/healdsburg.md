Healdsburg is a quaint town of about 11,000 people. Known for their tasty wines, Healdsburg is only a stone's throw from San Francisco. Fine eating establishments and cozy coffee shops speckle the downtown plaza area where friendly people greet you at every corner. The perfect place for wine tasting, the Alexander and Dry Creek Valleys are beatiful to look at as well with the abundance of breathtaking views.
