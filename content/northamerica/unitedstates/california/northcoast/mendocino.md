Mendocino, an historic Cape Cod-style enclave, doubles as the New England town of "Cabot Cove" in the television show "Murder, She Wrote". The entire village of Mendocino, established by New England whalers, is on the National Register of Historic Places. Fine shops, galleries, and snug inns abound in this renowned artists' colony.
