Petaluma's downtown is one of the best-preserved Victorian commercial districts in California, with block after block of ornate 1880s and 1890s brick buildings along Petaluma Boulevard and Kentucky Street. The area has been used as a film location so often — most famously for American Graffiti — that you may feel a vague sense of recognition. It's a genuine working downtown with independent shops, good restaurants, and a farmers market on Saturday mornings.
