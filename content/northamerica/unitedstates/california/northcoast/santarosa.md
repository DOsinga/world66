In Santa Rosa, tour the Luther Burbank Home and Gardens and the Church Built from One Tree, now a museum devoted to Robert L. Ripley of Believe It or Not fame. 

 North of Santa Rosa, you can canoe in the Russian River past lovely vineyards. More than 50 wineries are clustered along the river's banks in the Russian River Valley. Hike the nature trail in Armstrong Redwoods State Reserve near Guerneville, which is home to many of the Russian River area's resorts and restaurants.
