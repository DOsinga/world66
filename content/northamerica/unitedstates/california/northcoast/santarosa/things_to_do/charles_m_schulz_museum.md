The reason most people detour into Santa Rosa. Schulz drew Peanuts from his studio here for nearly thirty years, and this museum does the strip justice — original artwork, a recreation of his workspace, and rotating exhibitions that go deeper than nostalgia. The outdoor garden has a Snoopy labyrinth made from 5,000 ceramic tiles that kids love. Right next door is Snoopy's Home Ice, the ice arena Schulz built in 1969 and where he played hockey into his seventies.
