A small chapel built entirely from a single redwood tree in 1875 — a quirky bit of Americana that Robert Ripley featured in Believe It or Not. The building now houses a museum about Ripley, who grew up in Santa Rosa. Worth a quick stop if you're in the area, though the museum itself is modest.
