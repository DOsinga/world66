The oldest commercial district in Santa Rosa, built from local stone in the early 1900s around the Northwestern Pacific Railroad depot. The restored buildings now house antique shops, restaurants, and a few tasting rooms. It's the most atmospheric corner of town and a good starting point for exploring downtown on foot.
