### [College Cove](/northamerica/unitedstates/california/northcoast/trinidad/thingstodo/college_cove)

College Cove is an amazing beach as you can tell in the pictures. You
can walk along the beach and see little waterfalls and watch the surf
roll in. This beach is nice because there is shade to protect you from
the sun and logs to sit on. College Cove is really protected from the
wind and big surf. Many local surfers go here when the swell is really
big to surf some mellow sets. I felt like I was in Hawaii when I
sunbathed here and listened to birds singing. There is a bit of a hike
