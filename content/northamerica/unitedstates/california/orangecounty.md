Quintessential California, Orange County is the state's favorite family destination, due to its many theme parks, amusements, historic and cultural facilities, mild climate, and great beaches.

The county is anchored by Anaheim and Buena Park, Southern California's theme- park heaven. And, Orange County's splendid, 42-mile, picture-postcard-perfect coastline is recognized around the world.
