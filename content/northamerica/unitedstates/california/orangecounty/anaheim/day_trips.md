title: "Day Trips in Anaheim"
type: section
---

The Anaheim and Orange County area of California is always
bustling with things to do. Head to a beach, a theme park or even travel south
to San Diego which is only a couple of hours away. If you will be traveling
there, here’s a variety of **Anaheim day trip ideas** you could consider.
**
Theme Park Day Trips**

There are several theme parks to visit such as:-

Knott’s Berry
Farm, a theme park and water park combined- Disneyland, world
famous for Pirates of the Caribbean, It’s a Small World and many other
Disney themed rides- Six Flags Magic
Mountain where you’ll find over 260 acres of rides, attractions and shows- Universal
Studios where you can go behind the scenes of famous movies and television
shows- Wild Rivers
Water Park, containing over 40 water rides and activities- Raging Waters
Water Park which is the largest water park in California and has over 50
acres of attractions, activities and rides

**California’s Beaches Day Trips**The western border of California is lined with nothing
but beaches, and some of the closest locations to include on a day trip:

-

Huntington Beach
Pier which has a large number of special events and family fun happening
daily- Catalina Island which
is a beautiful location and a short boat ride 26 miles out to sea- The gorgeous
city of Long Beach where you could climb aboard the Queen Mary, wander
around the ship, or visit the city’s world class aquarium

**Animal Parks Day Trips**Just south of Anaheim and Orange County is the fun city
of San Diego which offers:-

The San Diego
Zoo housing over 3000 animals and plants
- The San Diego
Wild Animal Park where you’re able to drive around on 2,000 acres with
wild animals roaming free, much like they would in their native lands- The Sea World
Adventure Park containing over 150 acres of animal attractions and shows,
plus petting pools, shark exhibits and aquariums.

**Hollywood Day Trips**Last but not least, stop in Hollywood and visit the
following: -

The breathtaking
Mann’s Chinese Theater and stroll along the Hollywood Walk of Fame finding
celebrity stars and actual hand or footprints in the cement slabs- Stop off on
Sunset Strip where the rich and famous shop- Tour Beverly
Hills and view some of the more famous homes of various celebrities.

You won’t regret taking any one of these day trip
excursions.
