Downtown Disney, located adjacent to the Disneyland Resort, is a shopping and entertainment district. Stretching over a mile long, the strip offers a variety of shops, restaurants, bars and attractions. It is an enjoyable spot to spend an evening, or to relax after a day at Disneyland or California Adventure. Shops in Downtown Disney include Sephora, World of Disney and the LEGO Imagination Center. Restaurants and bars include ESPN Zone, Rainforest Cafe, and the House of Blues.

The Downtown Disney district has ample parking, and for those visiting Disneyland Resort or California Adventure, is easily within walking distance of the main entrance plaza. In addition, the monorail from Tomorrowland in Disneyland, stops in Downtown Disney.

Downtown Disney is free to visit, and is open late. The Disneyland Hotel, the Grand Californian Hotel and the Paradise Pier Hotel are all adjacent.
