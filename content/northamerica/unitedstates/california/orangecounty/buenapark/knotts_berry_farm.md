The nation's oldest themed amusement park, Knott's Berry Farm has grown from a roadside berry stand and chicken dinner restaurant into a full destination resort. Highlights include Perilous Plunge, one of the world's tallest water rides, and Ghost Town, a recreated 1880s Gold Rush town that anchors the park's California heritage. The adjacent Soak City waterpark is open in summer.

Wheelchair accessible.
