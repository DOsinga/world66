---
title: "Shopping in Costa Mesa"
type: section
---

Costa Mesa offers great shopping opportunities. There is more to shopping than just South Coast Plaza which is home to many famous brands of fashion and high end boutique stores. One of the best shopping districts is on East 17th Street at the end of 55 Freeway. East 17th Street offers a great wine store called Hi Times, an "earthy" grocery store called Mothers and neat shopping center called 17th Street Promende which features Mi Casa Mexican Restaurant, Where's the Party, and My First Page.

Directly across the street from Mother's Market is Hemphill's Rugs & Carpets. This floor covering store has been featured in "Shopping Secrets of Southern California" and KCAL TV's Mendelson's Best Buys. The store features over 70 name brands of carpet, hardwood floor and area rugs. Visitors frequently shop and have their purchases shipped home. The high end local clientele have rugs and carpet shipped to their vacation homes in Hawaii, Idaho and Montana. Be sure to see the selection of hand crafted Tibetan rugs, Southwest designs from Romania and custom hand tufted rugs from Hong Kong. Major name brands include Ralph Lauren, Masland, Fabrica, Tommy Bahama, Moda, Couristan and Schumacher.

Hemphill's Rugs & Carpets has the most complete selection of natural fibers anywhere. Sisal, cork, seagrass, abacca, raffia and wool. They specialize in natural sisal area rugs from Ralph Lauren, Merida Meridia, Fibreworks, Design Materials, Unique Carpets. The selection of bindings such as linen, leather, cotton, microfiber, fabric, and chenille is unmatched.

Another great bargain is the Swap Meet at the Orange County Fairgrounds on Saturdays and Sundays.
