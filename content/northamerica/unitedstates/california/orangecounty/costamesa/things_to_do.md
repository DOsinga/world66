---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Costa Mesa's main draws are its performing arts venues. The Segerstrom Center for the Arts on Town Center Drive hosts touring Broadway shows, opera, and ballet. Next door, the South Coast Repertory Theatre has been producing new American plays since 1964 and has won a Tony Award. For something more offbeat, The LAB on Bristol Street is a converted warehouse complex with independent shops, galleries, and cafes.
