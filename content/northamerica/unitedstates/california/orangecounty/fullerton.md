---
title: "Fullerton"
type: location
---

Located 22 miles southeast of metropolitan Los Angeles, in the center of North Orange County, Fullerton houses a mix of residential, commercial and industrial environments. The Fullerton Transportation Center allows easy transportation via bus, Amtrak or Metrolink. There is a variety of restaurants within walking distance downtown. Also, of interest is the Fullerton Arboretum, the Fullerton Musuem, Plummer Auditorium and the Muckenthaler.
