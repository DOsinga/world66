Here is a gallery where you can go back in time to the start of color printing with the invent of color lithography. You can view one of the largest collections of original vintage posters on the West Coast of the U.S.A. in this gallery. You have the feeling that you are visiting Paris with the collection of advertising posters or in a museum viewing the World War One and Two originals available for purchase.

Located across the street from the Surf and Sand Hotel; next to the French 75 Bisto at 1476 S. Coast Highway; Laguna Beach, CA 92651. Tel: 949-376-7422. Closed Mondays.
