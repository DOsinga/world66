---
title: "Eating Out"
type: section
---

Laguna Niguel's dining options are spread across its commercial corridors rather than concentrated in a walkable centre. **Saltwater Grill** on the corner of La Paz and Aliso Creek serves Mexican dishes with a California twist — the calamari tacos and chile rellenos are local favorites, and there is outdoor seating and a full bar. Across the street, **Bella Italian Kitchen** is a small, casual spot known for its chicken pesto rigatoni.
