### [Brian](/northamerica/unitedstates/california/orangecounty/lagunaniguel/eatingout/brian)

Laguna Niguel is a small community by California standards and there are numerous restaurants within its boundaries.

Saltwater Grill (located on the corner of La Paz and Aliso Creek) offers numerous Mexican dishes with their own twist. I enjoy their calamari tacos and the chile rellenos are top shelf. Saltwater also offers outdoor seating, live music and a full bar. It's definitely a local favorite.

Right across the street from Saltwater Grill is Bella Italian Kitchen. This little hole in the wall makes some of the best chicken pesto rigatoni I've ever [more..](/northamerica/unitedstates/california/orangecounty/lagunaniguel/eatingout/brian)
