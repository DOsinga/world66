---
title: "Shopping in Newport Beach"
type: section
---

Just down the street from the intersection of Irvine Avenue and Westcliff Drive in Newport Beach is the shopping district of East 17th Street. Stores along this shopping meca include Mother's Market, Hi Times Wine, Ruby's Diner, Staples, Hemphill's Rugs & Carpets, Where's the Party, Mi Casa Restaurant, Beach Pit BBQ, First Page, French's Bakery and more.

It seems that all of Orange County refers to Hemphill's Rugs & Carpets as their top choice of all floorcovering stores in the area. Hemphill's features 70 name brands of hardwood floor, wool and Stainmaster carpet and area rugs of all types. They have the major brands such as Karastan, Ralph Lauren, Richard Marshall Olde Boards, Nourison, Stanton and more. Their selection of natural fiber rugs such as seagrass, sisal and abaca is unmatched.

Where's the Party is a complete party planning store with all of your party needs. Their selection of seasonal gifts and accessories is well presented and always welcomes in the new season.

Hi Times is the wine store in all of Orange County. They have terrific monthly buys and offer a newsletter that is informative. Their staff is always willing to suggest a terrific bottle for personal consumption or as a gift.
