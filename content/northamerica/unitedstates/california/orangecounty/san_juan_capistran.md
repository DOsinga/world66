Travel Guide

The Mission in San Juan Capistrano is Orange County's only mission and one of it's leading attractions. There are several activities for kids including an audio tour for kids in both English and Spanish. And there is something for the whole family with our lush gardens, koi ponds, historic adobe buildings and museum rooms with displays that help history come alive for our visitors. Please see our website for specials for Orange County residents, and other discounts and coupons.
