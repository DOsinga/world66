Oxnard is the largest city in Ventura County in terms of population. It is located at the western edge of the Oxnard Plain, and is one of the world's most important agricultural centers, with its distinction as the strawberry and lima bean capital. Founded in 1903, it is home to nearly 200,000 citizens.
