The best resource for sights, hotels, restaurants, bars, what to do and see

Monterey Bay is about 90 miles south of Palo Alto and well worth the
trip. It all starts with a drive along Highway 1 that features stunning
views of the California coastline. Once you reach the Monterey
Peninsula, you’ll have plenty of things to do. Historic Cannery Row is
home to quaint shops, unique antique stores, and the marvelous Monterey
Bay Aquarium.
