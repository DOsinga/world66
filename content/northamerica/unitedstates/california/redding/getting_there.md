---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

Redding is on Interstate 5, about 160 miles north of Sacramento and 240 miles south of the Oregon border. By car from San Francisco, allow about 3.5 hours; from Portland, about 4.5 hours. Amtrak's Coast Starlight train stops at Redding on its Los Angeles–Seattle route, though service is infrequent and often runs late. Redding Municipal Airport (RDD) has daily flights to San Francisco and Los Angeles via United and American, though fares are typically high for the short distance — most people drive or connect through Sacramento.
