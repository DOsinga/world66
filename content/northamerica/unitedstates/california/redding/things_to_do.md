---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

The Sundial Bridge is the obvious starting point — walk it, then pick up the Sacramento River Trail heading upstream through cottonwood and oak. For bigger adventures, the drive up to Shasta Dam takes about 20 minutes from downtown and rewards you with one of the largest concrete dams in the United States and views across the reservoir. Whiskeytown Lake, to the west on Highway 299, has good swimming and kayaking with far fewer crowds than Shasta.
