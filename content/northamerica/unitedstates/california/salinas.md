Travel Guide

At Salinas, CA, you’ll find yourself right in the midst of the inviting California coast, and just a stone’s throw from the exciting Bay Area, you’ll find yourself within driving distance to a plethora of long, spacious beaches, the John Steinbeck House, Bay area attractions, city tours, and plenty of activities to keep you entertained throughout your visit.
