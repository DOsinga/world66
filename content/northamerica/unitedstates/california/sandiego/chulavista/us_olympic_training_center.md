One of only three year-round Olympic training centers in the United States, this facility on the plateau above Otay Lakes is where elite athletes come to prepare for international competition. Free daily tours take you through the velodrome, aquatics center, and fields used by track and field, soccer, and archery teams. The Spirit Store sells official US Olympic merchandise. Tours run daily; check the current schedule at the visitor center on arrival. 2800 Olympic Pkwy, Chula Vista.
