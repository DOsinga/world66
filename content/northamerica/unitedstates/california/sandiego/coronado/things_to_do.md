## Vietnam Unit Memorial Monument

The Vietnam Unit Memorial Monument is located on the Naval Amphibious Base, Coronado. Three small boats from the "Brown Water Navy" era provide the background for the Memorial Wall — a PBR (Patrol Boat River), a PCF (Patrol Craft Fast), and a CCB (Command Communications Boat). These boats point towards a curved concrete wall sixty feet long and eight feet high, displaying the names of all Navy and Coast Guard personnel who died supporting naval operations in Vietnam during 1960-1975. The names are grouped by the unit to which each person was attached at the time of their death.

The memorial was dedicated on 21 May 2005. Procurement and restoration of the boats were carried out by veterans from the Swift Boat Sailors Association, the Gamewardens of Vietnam, and the Mobile Riverine Force Association, using only private funds and donated services.
