Del Mar is a small, affluent beach community on the coast north of [San Diego](/northamerica/unitedstates/california/sandiego). The wide sandy beach draws sun-worshippers year-round, and in winter migrating California grey whales can be spotted from the bluffs.

Del Mar Plaza, perched above the ocean, has an eclectic collection of boutiques and restaurants with sea views. The Del Mar Thoroughbred Club hosts summer horse racing — "where the turf meets the surf" — and the annual Del Mar Fair (now the San Diego County Fair) is a major local event in June and July.
