Sun- and sea-worshippers congregate in coastal [La Jolla](/northamerica/unitedstates/california/sandiego/lajolla), Del Mar, and [Solana Beach](/northamerica/unitedstates/california/sandiego/solanabeach), where migrating California gray whales can be seen in winter. In Del Mar, cruise Del Mar Plaza, an eclectic collection of boutiques and restaurants, and visit the Del Mar Thoroughbred Club for horse racing.
