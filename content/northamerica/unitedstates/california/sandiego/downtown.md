Downtown San Diego is the city's urban core, stretching from the waterfront north to Balboa Park. The [Gaslamp Quarter](/northamerica/unitedstates/california/sandiego/gaslampquarter) anchors the nightlife, with dozens of bars and restaurants packed into its Victorian-era blocks. Weekends draw big crowds, so expect lines at the popular spots.
