In addition to having boutique-style beers, it has fantastic food in that typically American bigger-is-better style. The Texas Fire Chilli Steak has to be eaten to be believed. Just don't drink the wine if you're from Europe or Australia! Be sure to sign up for the 'Mug Club' card for discounted (big) beers and drinking incentives on your way to the golden keg!
