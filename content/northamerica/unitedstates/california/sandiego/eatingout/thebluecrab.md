If you are in the mood for an affordable, yet elegant evening of seafood dining with a waterfront theme then The Blue Crab is the place for you. Conveniently located on beautiful San Diego Bay near Shelter Island, The Blue Crab offers tempting lunches and dinners. Fresh fish, lobster, swordfish, fish and chips, shrimp, scallops and even steak and chicken can be found on the varied menu. But the specialty of the house are the incredible Maryland Blue Crab Cakes. These are light and flavorful and made fresh daily. A romantic harborside view and great food make the Blue Crab a winner
