Everyone always wants something good and tasty to start their day with. VG's (which stands for Very Good) is not a restaurant but their baked good and donuts are the absolute finest in quality and flavor. If you are in a hurry and just want a cup of coffee and the best donut you will ever eat then be sure to stop by VG's which is located not far from the beach in Cardiff-By-The-Sea (about 30 miles north of downtown San Diego).
