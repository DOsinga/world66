North and inland from San Diego is Escondido, home to the exotic African and Asian animals of the 2,200-acre San Diego Wild Animal Park, dedicated to the preservation of endangered species. Escondido's Heritage Row presents a century of history. Check out memorabilia of famed orchestra leader Lawrence Welk at the resort bearing his name. Golf on courses in Santee, Fallbrook, Rancho Bernardo, or Rancho Santa Fe.
