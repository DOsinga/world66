Although known primarily as a veritable playground for sun-loving visitors in search of watery fun, Oceanside also has a thriving nightlife located mostly in the downtown area. With 7 bars in a 3 block radius Oceanside offers a wide variety of entertainment, from the laid back Haunted Head Saloon to the clubby Margarita Rocks, from karaoke night at McCabes to the local's hangout at OMTI. More information on Oceanside nightlife can be found online at http://www.osidealive.com.

For a little more variety, visitors (and locals) can head a few minutes south to [Carlsbad](/northamerica/unitedstates/california/sandiego/carlsbad), [Del Mar](/northamerica/unitedstates/california/sandiego/delmar), or even [San Diego](/northamerica/unitedstates/california/sandiego) for a slightly more upscale ambiance.

### [Haunted Head Saloon

The Haunted Head Saloon provides one of the more mellow bar-going experiences in Oceanside. A relatively small venue, the saloon offers a single pool table, several puzzle bar-games, an ever-busy jukebox, and a strangely compelling pirate theme. More information on the Haunted Head Saloon can be found at http://www.osidealive.com/entertainment/item/776/](/northamerica/unitedstates/california/sandiego/oceanside/nightlife_and_ente/haunted_head_saloo)

Mostly, however, the Haunted Head Saloon is a place where Oceanside residents and locally stationed military mingle in a very relaxed environment.
