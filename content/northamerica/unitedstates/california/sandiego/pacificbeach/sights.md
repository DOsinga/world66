---
title: "Sights in Pacific Beach"
type: section
---

Pacific Beach and Mission Beach are in the same vicinity. The greatest tourist attraction is the nice beaches, which become so packed during the summer it is hard to find room. The scenery hands down is why people come, though the bars and resturants are world class, in fact World Famous is an excellent resturant on the property of the Surfer Hotel on the beach. Pacific beach is sandwiched between Mt. Soledad to the north which offers breathtaking views, and the bay to the south. Many people come here and never leave.
