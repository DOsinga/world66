San Diego has some of the best beaches in California.  Coronado beach - located behind the Del Coronado Hotel on Coronado Island, one of my favorites (fewer people);   Mission Beach - great beach town with several miles of sandy beaches and hundreds of thousands of tourists during the summer, teens and families love this town located between San Diego and La Jolla near Sea World;   La Jolla beaches - there are several along the coastline of this very upscale community, some with sand, others just rocks, a few with seals and sea lions located just down the hill from the towne centre, Prospect Avenue.   Torrey Pines State Park - moving up the coast line and located between La Jolla and Del Mar is Torrey Pines State Park home to the most beautiful and romatic beaches, as well as San Diego's only nude beach - Black's Beach.   Del Mar beaches - finally, last but not least, are the great beaches in Del Mar just minutes from downtown San Diego via I-5.   Water temperatures during the summer range in the 62-68F range (16-20C, i think that's right!)And, best of all, the sun shines 350 days a year.
