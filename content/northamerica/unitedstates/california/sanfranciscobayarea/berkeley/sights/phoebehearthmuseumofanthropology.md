Founded in 1901, the Hearst Museum houses the oldest and largest anthropological collection in the West. The Museum preserves and interprets a global record of material culture through its more than 3.8 million objects. It also promotes the history and diversity of human cultures through research, exhibitions, and programs. The museum is located in Berkeley California, on the University campus at the corner of Bancroft and College Avenue. Regular hours are Wednesday through Saturday, 10:00 am to 4:30 pm, and Sunday from 12:00 noon to 4:00 pm. For a list of current exhibitions and upcoming special events, please visit: http://hearstmuseum.berkeley.edu/
