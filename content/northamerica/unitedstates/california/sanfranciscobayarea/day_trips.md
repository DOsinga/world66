Just had a terriffic day in Sausalito. Took the ferryboat over - a world class ride! Hooked up with the ***[Sausalito Wooden Boat Tour](/northamerica/unitedstates/california/sanfranciscobayarea) -*** What a trip! The guide is a local artist who wrote a book and has amazing tales to tell about the waterfront community. Got to go aboard a few boats, met the locals, learn a ton of stuff about wooden boats and she served us snacks at her waterside studio. One of a kind .
