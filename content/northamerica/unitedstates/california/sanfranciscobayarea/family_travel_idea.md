title: "Family Travel Ideas in San Francisco Bay Area"
type: section
---

Pier 39 is a popular San Francisco Bay Area entertainment complex, located two blocks east of Fisherman&rsquo;s Wharf, that is well suited for **San Francisco family travel**. Here are some activities to add to your itinerary for a fun family filled day at Pier 39:

**Carousel**

The kids will love a ride on the uniquely handcrafted Italian carousel located on the first level at the Bay end of the complex. Don&rsquo;t forget to take a happy snap or two of the children with famous landmarks depicted on the carousel, such as the Golden Gate Bridge, Chinatown and Alcatraz.

**Sea Lions**

The sometimes boisterous and barking sea lions are the real celebrities on the West Marina side of Pier 39 or the K-Dock. Informative talks are provided by friendly volunteer guides from the Marine Mammal Center kiosk, located next to the sea lions.

**Aquarium of the Bay**

Get up close and personal with the sharks, rays, octupuses at this underwater attraction. The kids will love the Giant Pacific Octopus observation bubble, the animal feedings and behind the scenes tours. The Aquarium of the Bay is open daily from 9am to 8pm.

**Street Performers**

Whilst visiting Pier 39, the family will enjoy the antics and tricks of performing magicians, jugglers and mime artists. Make your way to the Crystal Geyser Spring Water Center Stage for regular street performer shows throughout the day.

**Dining and Shopping**

Amongst the flurry of a fun filled day, make sure that you take the family to have a meal at one of the many exciting restaurants located at Pier 39 or to do some last minute souvenir shopping.
