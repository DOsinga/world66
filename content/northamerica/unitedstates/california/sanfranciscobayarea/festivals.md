---
title: "Festivals in San Francisco Bay Area"
type: section
---

San Francisco is a city that is full of culture, entertainment and excitement. There are several **San Francisco festivals** going on year round. If you are looking for something to do in San Francisco be sure to check out the festivals.

**San Francisco Waterfront Festival**

The San Francisco Waterfront Festival is held every year on the 4th of July. The festival is held along the pier and starts at noon. You will find arts and crafts, entertainment, street vendors and plenty of food. The night comes to an end with a amazing fireworks display that usually starts at about 9pm.

**North Beach Jazz Festival**

The North Beach Jazz Festival offers some of the hottest musicians from all over the country. The North Beach Jazz Festival is a 5-day event that provides excellent entertainment and some of the best nightlife. The July festival always starts on a Wednesday and ends on a Sunday but the dates vary from year to year.

**Ghirardelli Square Chocalate Festival**

If you are a chocolate lover, this is one of the best festivals in all of San Francisco. Ghiradelli is known for having some of the best chocolate around. The festival happens during the month of September and admission is free. Keep in mind that you will need to buy a ticket in order to taste all the delicious chocolates.

**Oktoberfest By the Bay**

Oktoberfest By the Bay is the perfect place to spend time with friends and family. You can also taste excellent beers from all over the world. The festival happens every October and usually runs for 3 days. You can count on Oktoberfest for plenty of beer, food and live entertainment.

**Macy's Union Square Tree Lighting Ceremony**

Every year Macy's in Union Square puts on a fabulous tree lighting ceremony. The ceremony takes place the day after Thanksgiving and lasts throughout the day. Street vendors and local artists sell handcrafted items and there is plenty of live entertainment going on. Spending the day after Thanksgiving at Macy's Union Square is a great way to start the holiday season.

**Chinese New Year Parade**

The Chinese New Year Parade is a major festival in San Francisco. People from all over come to enjoy this entertaining event. The festival happens every year based on when Chinese New Years falls. You will find tasty Chinese food, entertainment, fireworks, a parade and much more!
