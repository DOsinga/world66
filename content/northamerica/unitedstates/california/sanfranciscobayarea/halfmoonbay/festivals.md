**Great Festivals in Half Moon Bay
**

Depending on what time of the year it is, you might want to stop and look around one of the **Half Moon Bay Festivals**. All year long on the third Saturday of every month, with the exception of October, Half Moon Bay has a Coastal Flower Market. During May to September, the market is held outdoors, but during the cold winter months the market is moved indoors. Not only do you have this event going on, but also during other months there are unique festivals for that people come from miles around to enjoy.

**Half Moon Bay Art & Pumpkin Festival**

This festival is kind of a continuation the World Championship Pumpkin Weigh-off that takes place on Columbus Day. The festival is held the weekend following Columbus Day each year and runs along Main Street from 10:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. This festival invites you to try out some great food, but you can also take part in some great arts and crafts. The pumpkin that won the weigh-off will be on display for the festival, but there will also be pumpkin carving contests as well as costume contests.

**Half Moon Bay Chamarita Festival**

If you are visiting Half Moon Bay during the month of June, you will need to look into this great Portuguese festival, but keep in mind that the dates do change depending on the year. The festival features some great marching bands, as well as some amazing equestrian groups. While watching the parade you will also see queens in beautiful ball gowns walking down Main Street.
