**Half Moon Bay transportation **can be anything from cars to busing to old fashioned walking. The beach at Half Moon Bay is located in California just south of San Francisco.

#### **Getting Around in Half Moon Bay**

In California, car is king, and getting around is easy if the visitor can drive. There are number of rental agencies in the area, including Enterprise Rent-A-Car. Half Moon Bay is located by Highway 92 which intersects with the 101 and 280 freeway systems. These 2 freeways connect Half Moon Bay with the rest of California. Greyhound buses also serve the Half Moon Bay area. For people looking for a day trip to the bay, this might be the best alternative. As most visitors come from San Francisco, a bus might be more practical. San Francisco has an excellent public transportation system, so having a rental car within the city is unnecessary, especially with the lack of parking.

While in Half Moon Bay, people can get around quite well by foot. In the main tourist section of the town, there are number of foot paths for people to cut through the town. However, cars are the most convenient way to get around the metro area. Anyone planning to leave the bay after a stay in Half Moon Bay may also wish to rent a car.
