**Coming to Half Moon Bay – Make Sure you Pack the Right Clothes**

If you are going to be visiting **Half Moon Bay, practical information** is going to be very important, especially if you have never been there before. Knowing what you can expect while visiting Half Moon Bay will allow you to plan your vacation perfectly because you will know what to pack based on the type of weather you will be experiencing. However, you will also know what to do to help stay safe while visiting this beautiful coastal city.

**Hazards**

When visiting Half Moon Bay, the main thing that you need to be on the lookout for is the fog. The fog is a daily part of life in Half Moon Bay because of how close the city is to the coast. The worst part about the fog is trying to drive in it; if you are not experienced in driving in the fog, you need to prepare yourself ahead of time. If the fog is bad you will want to avoid driving altogether if you can. The best part about the fog is that it will soon wear off as the day finally starts to warm up.

**Beaches**

There are numerous state beaches located along the coast and the best time to go visit the beach is during June. During June, you can spend hours on the beach, well after 8:00 p.m., as long as you are dressed appropriately. No matter what time of year you visit the beaches in Half Moon Bay, always bring a sweater because it cools off drastically at night.
