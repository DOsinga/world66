A Lebanese restaurant on Lighthouse Avenue run by one owner-cook, which tells you everything you need to know: the food is made to order and the service is unhurried. The menu covers both meat and vegetarian dishes and prices are reasonable by Monterey standards. Good for a relaxed lunch, but not if you are in a hurry — the kitchen moves at its own pace.

309 Lighthouse Ave, Monterey, CA.
