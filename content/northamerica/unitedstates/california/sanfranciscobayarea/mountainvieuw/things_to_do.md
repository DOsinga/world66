---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Mountain View's main cultural attraction is the **Computer History Museum** at 1401 N. Shoreline Blvd — an extensive collection covering the full arc of computing history, from early mechanical calculators to modern processors. The exhibits are well-designed and engrossing even for non-specialists.

**NASA Ames Research Center** at Moffett Field is an active government facility and not freely accessible, but the visitor centre is open on weekdays and the historic Hangar One — one of the largest wooden structures in the world — is visible from the road. The adjacent **Shoreline at Mountain View** park offers bay-trail cycling, kayaking, and birdwatching in the tidal marshes.
