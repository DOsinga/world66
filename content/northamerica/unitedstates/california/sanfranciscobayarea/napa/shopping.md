### [Frank Family Winery](/northamerica/unitedstates/california/sanfranciscobayarea/napa/shopping/frank_family_winer)

Take
a trip back in time at the Frank Family Vineyards. First constructed as
the Larkmead Winery in 1884, the building was refinished with native
sandstone from the nearby hills in 1906 and still stands tall today.
The massive stone edifice is considered an archetype of California’s
wine country; it appears on the National Register of Historical Places
and is listed as a Point of Historical Interest in the state of
California.

Housed in the historic Larkmead winery in Napa Valley, Frank Family
