Saint Helena is the heart of the Wine Country, just a half-hours drive from
[Napa](/northamerica/unitedstates/california/sanfranciscobayarea/napa). Saint Helena offers a large variety of wineries and restaurants including Tra Vigne, Pizzeria Tra Vigne, Market, Taylor Refresher, and more. Each shop in Saint Helena is unique, as no chain businesses are allowed, with the exception of KFC, A&W, and Safeway, which were in Saint Helena long before that law was established. Saint Helena also has a four-school district, consisting of a primary school, elementary school, middle school, and high school. Saint Helena also offers an assortment of recreational activities, with many parks and bike trails to choose from, including Saint Helena's very own Mount Saint Helena located just North of Main Street.
