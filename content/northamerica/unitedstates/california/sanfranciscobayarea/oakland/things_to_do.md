---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Oakland packs a surprising range into a compact city. The Oakland Museum of California is the standout, combining art, history, and ecology under one roof, but the real character comes from exploring the neighborhoods — the waterfront bustle of Jack London Square, the Victorian calm of Preservation Park, the trails and telescopes up in the hills. Lake Merritt anchors the center of town with a walking loop, boat rentals, and a bird sanctuary that has been operating since 1870.
