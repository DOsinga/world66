A reliable Thai spot on University Avenue serving solid versions of the classics — pad thai, curries, larb — at prices that feel reasonable by Palo Alto standards. It has been a neighborhood staple for many years, which in this part of the Bay Area counts for something. Good for a quick lunch before heading back to campus.
