When you visit Stanford University, be sure to take a tour of Memorial Church, the Cantor Center for the Visual Arts, and the Hoover Institution and Observation Tower (great views of the SF Bay). It's also great to stroll around the campus between the Oval and the Student Union (Main Quad). More information can be found at **http://www.stanford.edu/dept/visitorinfo/**

There are free tours starting on the steps of Memorial Auditorium pretty much every day at 11:00am and 3:15pm. Be sure to check **http://events.stanford.edu/** to see if anything of interest is happening on the day of your visit (always lots of stuff going on).
