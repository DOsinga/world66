---
title: 'Bars and Cafes'
type: section
---

San Francisco's bar scene is as diverse as its neighborhoods. The Castro remains the heart of the city's legendary LGBTQ nightlife, with bars ranging from raucous dance clubs to laid-back neighborhood pubs. Polk Street, the Mission, and SoMa each have their own distinct scenes. The city is also a serious craft cocktail town, and the coffee culture rivals Seattle's.

Whatever you are in the mood for -- a dive bar with a pool table, a rooftop with Bay views, or a historic saloon with swinging doors -- you will find it here.
