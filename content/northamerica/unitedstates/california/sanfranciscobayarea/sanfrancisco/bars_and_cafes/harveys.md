Harvey's is a welcoming gay and lesbian bar and restaurant on Castro Street, perfect for meeting friends over a burger. Named after Harvey Milk, the pioneering civil rights leader who was assassinated in 1978, it hosts drag shows on the second and fourth Saturdays and occasional comedy and jazz on weeknights.
