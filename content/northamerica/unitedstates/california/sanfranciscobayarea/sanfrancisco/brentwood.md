An hour's drive east of San Francisco, Brentwood sits on the eastern side of Mt. Diablo in the Central Valley. Known nationally for its sweet corn, the area is also popular for its Harvest Time farm trail, with over 30 farms and ranches offering U-Pick experiences for stone fruits, cherries, and other seasonal produce. The annual Cornfest each July draws visitors from across the state.
