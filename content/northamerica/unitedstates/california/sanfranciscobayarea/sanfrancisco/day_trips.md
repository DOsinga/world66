---
title: 'Day Trips'
type: section
---

San Francisco's location makes it an excellent base for day trips. Wine country in Napa and Sonoma is less than an hour north. The redwood forests of Muir Woods are just across the Golden Gate Bridge. Berkeley, with its university campus and excellent restaurant scene, is a quick BART ride away. And the dramatic Mendocino Coast, while a longer drive, rewards the effort with some of the most beautiful coastline in California.

You will not want to leave the city most days, but when you do, the options are outstanding.
