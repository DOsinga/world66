While San Francisco is not a very “leave-able” city, you may decide to take a relaxing day trip during your wonderful San Francisco vacation anyway. What’s great about San Francisco’s location is that here are so many excellent places to explore just a few miles or less than an hour drive away, that you just might decide to stay for a night or two. One of San Francisco’s most excellent day trips is Napa. Located a mere 46 miles from San Francisco, this gorgeous city of just over 120,000 lucky residents sits at the center of one of the richest wine regions on the planet. Napa is the largest city in Napa Valley and it is also the oldest city in the valley.

Besides a location that offers easy access to dozens of wineries such as the Hess Collection Winery and Vineyards ( *www.hesscollection.com*), Napa also features a wonderful assortment of inns and hotels such as the tranquil Napa River Inn (* www.napariverinn.com*) and the calming Carneros Inn (* www.thecarnerosinn.com*), and excellent restaurants – try Bistro Don Giovanni (* www.bistrodongiovanni.com*). The area even features several beautiful 9-hole golf courses belonging to the Chardonnay Golf Club, spread out over 150-acres that must be seen to be believed. The Chardonnay Golf Club -- 27 holes of championship golf -- is located at 2555 Jameson Canyon Road, 707-257-1900, *www.chardonnaygolfclub.com*.

### [Mendocino Coast](/northamerica/unitedstates/california/sanfranciscobayarea/sanfrancisco/day_trips/mendocino_coast)

The Mendocino Coast, roughly 3 hours north of San Francisco (and well worth the scenic drive), is an idyllic hideaway surrounded by coastal mountains with beaches, camping, and the famous Skunk Train ( www.skunktrain.com). This happens to be one of the most scenic train rides in the world. Other area attractions include: The Ford House Museum and Visitor Center, Mendocino Coast Botanical Gardens, Jughandle Creek Farm & Nature Center, Mendocino Art Center, Opera Fresca, the 1909 Point Cabrillo Light Station & Preserve, and the Triangle Tattoo Museum -- to name a [more..](/northamerica/unitedstates/california/sanfranciscobayarea/sanfrancisco/day_trips/mendocino_coast)

### [Brentwood](/northamerica/unitedstates/california/sanfranciscobayarea/sanfrancisco/day_trips/brentwood)

Brentwood, an hour’s drive East of San Francisco, is one of several towns located on the morning side of Mt. Diablo just below scenic rolling hills in the Central Valley . This area is a viable agricultural area, due to its unique micro climate, amidst new affordable homes and major shopping areas. Known nationally for its Brentwood Sweet Corn, the area is also well-known for its Harvest Time farm trail with over 30 farms and ranches offering the visitor a unique U-Pick experience of apricots, peaches, pears, nectarines, cherries and other equally delicious and fresh [more..](/northamerica/unitedstates/california/sanfranciscobayarea/sanfrancisco/day_trips/brentwood)



### [Berkeley](/northamerica/unitedstates/california/sanfranciscobayarea/sanfrancisco/day_trips/berkeley)

For history's sake -- you absolutely must visit Berkeley during your San Francisco vacation. Not only is Berkeley the birthplace of Liberalism, it is home to the prestigious University of California, Berkeley -- the flagship of California's public university system. Just 13 miles from San Francisco, Berkeley is easily reached by San Francisco Bay Area Rapid Transit (BART) - www.bart.gov.
