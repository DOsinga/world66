The Mendocino Coast, roughly 3 hours north of San Francisco (and well worth the scenic drive), is an idyllic hideaway surrounded by coastal mountains with beaches, camping, and the famous Skunk Train ( www.skunktrain.com). This happens to be one of the most scenic train rides in the world. Other area attractions include: The Ford House Museum and Visitor Center, Mendocino Coast Botanical Gardens, Jughandle Creek Farm & Nature Center, Mendocino Art Center, Opera Fresca, the 1909 Point Cabrillo Light Station & Preserve, and the Triangle Tattoo Museum -- to name a few. For information about these and many other Mendocino attractions, visit the Mendocino Coast Chamber of Commerce website at www.mendocinocoast.com.
