Updated Italian, known for its pizzas, A16 uses the best of local fresh ingredients to create a wonderful dining experience. Reservations recommended.

A good location in the Marina neighborhood of SF, combined with excellent food, makes A16 always crowded on the weekends. Parking may be tough also - use the vallet around the corner near Lombard Street.
