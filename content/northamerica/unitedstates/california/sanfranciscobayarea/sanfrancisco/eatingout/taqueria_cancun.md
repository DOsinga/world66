There are two Cancun locations - one in the Mission near 20th and
Mission Street and the other at 6th and Market downtown. Both
serve the same fare but the Market street location is dirtier, smaller
and subject to crazier street folk.

Some prefer the Market street location for its authentic San Francisco
vibe while others will prefer the more spacious Mission street
venue. Both places serve the same fantastic Mexican food at rock
bottom prices. For less than $5 you can wrap your hands around a
super burrito with your choice of meat and have a basket of chips and
homemade salsas. People across the United States routinely cite
San Francisco's Mission district as having the best Mexican food and
Cancun is an establishment helping the city maintain its reputation.

The pollo asado (grilled chicken) is highly recommended in taco, burrito or quesadilla form.
