**PIER 39's Sea Lions Arrival, Thursday January 19th, 2006 -- 11:00 a.m. - 5:00 p.m.**

PIER 39 commemorates the sixteenth anniversary of the sea lions’ arrival. Visitors will see, hear and enjoy hundreds of sea lions in close proximity. Docents from The Marine Mammal Center will be educating the public at K-Dock, and by the Whale Bus exhibit in the West Park.
