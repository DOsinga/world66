**Sunday, November 30th, 2005 - PIER 39’s Holiday Harmony - ****noon to 5:30 p.m. **

PIER 39’s **Holiday Harmony**, Sunday November 20th, 2005 -- noon
to 5:30 p.m. -A multi-cultural celebration, PIER 39’s Holiday Harmony
will feature Bay Area groups performing seasonal favorites from around
the world on Sunday, November 20, 2005. PIER 39’s majestic tree,
adorned with 400 glistening ornaments, 500 multi-colored bows and 2,000
twinkling lights will be lit at 5:30 p.m. in the Entrance Plaza.
Enchanting entertainment includes a: Chanukah Celebration, Kwanzaa
Celebration and Christmas Celebration.
