**"Preschool Teachers Make a Difference" Awards Presented by
Habitot Children’s Museum**
Habitot Children's Museum presents its first annual "Preschool Teachers Make a Difference" awards program. The awards will be given to eleven outstanding preschool educators in the nine Bay Area counties and will be accepted through May 29. For an application, visit www.habitot.org.

**
4/18: Habitot Children’s Museum Celebrates 11th Anniversary**

(Berkeley, CA) April 18, 2009- Join us for Habitot’s 11th Birthday Bash, a FREE Admission Day sponsored by Life is Good Foundation with a jazz performance by Guy Gash and his Sharp Five Jazz Band from 2:00-4:00pm. Lovee the Clown will also help celebrate with bubbles, puppet shows and songs from 11:00-12:30pm. In the Art Studio, children and families will help decorate Habitot’s giant birthday cake with icings and sprinkles. All visitors will enjoy birthday stories in the Multicultural Reading Tent at 10:00am-1:00pm. For more information please visit http://www.habitot.org.

Habitot Children's Museum
The East Bay's Discovery Museum for young
children. Our hands-on exhibits, drop-in art studio and events are
perfectly tailored for infants, toddlers, preschoolers and families.
Habitot offers classes for children, parents and educators, plus group
visits, birthday parties, workshops and family resources.
