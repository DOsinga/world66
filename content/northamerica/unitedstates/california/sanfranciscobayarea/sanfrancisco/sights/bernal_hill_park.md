Just south of the Mission is Bernal Heights -- and one of the best parts of Bernal Heights is its park. A hill juts high into the air and is closed off to traffic for hiking, walking dogs, and enjoying the draw-dropping 360-degree view of San Francisco and its surroundings. It's easy to drive up Folsom St. from Army/Caesar Chavez, just a few blocks, and you'll see cars parked on the side of the road. Get out and walk around and be sure to bring your camera for the very best views in the city! (And it's not known or over-run by tourists, the way Twin Peaks is.)
