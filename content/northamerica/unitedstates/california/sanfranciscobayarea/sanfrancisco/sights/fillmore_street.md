The Fillmore neighborhood of San Francisco is not an "official"
neighborhood, but the term is used by many locals to
describe the area. Fillmore Street stretches from the
Marina Green at the tip of the peninsula to Duboce Street just above
Market. However, most people use the term the Fillmore to refer to the
area along Fillmore between Jackson Street in Pacific Heights and
Golden Gate in the Western Addition. The Fillmore encompasses both one
of the most affluent neighborhoods in San Francisco as well as one of
the least affluent. Fillmore enjoys some of San Francisco's really
great neighborhood shops and restaurants.
