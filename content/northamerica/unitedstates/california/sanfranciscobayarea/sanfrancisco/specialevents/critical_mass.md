Every last Friday of a month, a large group of bicyclists gathers at
5:30 pm at Justin Herman Plaze which is at the bottom of Market St.
(aka Embarcadero Plaza). They then set of on a secret route through the
city to manifest the importance of non-carbound traffic in a modern
city.
