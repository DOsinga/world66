PIER 39 presents its popular Tulipmania showcasing 39,000 tulips throughout both levels of PIER 39. This year’s display will feature a variety of whimsical topiaries interspersed among the flower beds. PIER 39’s Tulipmania has become a favorite of many because of the unique tulip color combinations, and variety of tulips that are in clear view for the public to enjoy. For several days, visitors absorb the visual splendor and gardening tips offered during the “tulip tours” headed by PIER 39’s landscape team.
