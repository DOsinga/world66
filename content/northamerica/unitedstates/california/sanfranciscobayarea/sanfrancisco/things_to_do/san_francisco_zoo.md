The San Francisco Zoo sits on the western edge of the city, bordered by the Pacific Ocean. It is home to over 250 species, including grizzly bears, penguins, and a popular lemur forest. The zoo is a good option for families, especially on the warmer days that occasionally bless this foggy part of town.
