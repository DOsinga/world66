Every Cinco de Mayo weekend there's a parade in downtown San Jose in honor of
the Mexican-American culture. The festivities that go along with the
parade bring musicians, dancers, food vendors and activists to
Guadalupe River Park. Around 300,000 people attend San Jose's Cinco de
Mayo Parade & Festival to learn about and celebrate Mexican
heritage. Free admission.
