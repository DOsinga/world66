type: section
---

Do you know the way to San Jose? You should, since there are many options in making San Jose travel possible. If you decide to fly, one great option is to go with Southwest Airlines. There are presently over 45 cities they provide service to. To see if you are able to fly from your city to San Jose, go to their website, southwest.com. There are other airline carriers that fly to and from San Jose as well, such as American, Northwest, Continental, Delta, US Airways, Frontier, and Alaska. Amtrak (rail) and Greyhound (bus) are other options as well.
