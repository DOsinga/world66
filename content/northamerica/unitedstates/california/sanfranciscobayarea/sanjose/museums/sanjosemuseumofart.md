**What is the San Jose Museum of Art?**

SJMA is the leading institution dedicated to visual culture in
Silicon Valley. It is a community anchor, ensuring artistic excellence
and access for an extraordinarily diverse populace. It is a
contemporary art center whose acclaimed exhibitions have ranged across
modern masterworks to the newest frontiers of art. It is a cultural
crossroads, more than doubling its attendance since instituting a free
admission policy. It is the largest visual arts education provider in
Santa Clara County. It is a source of inspiration, contemplation, and
delight for a fast-moving community. It is a Museum of the future.Admission to the San Jose Museum of Art is free to everyone, every day!
