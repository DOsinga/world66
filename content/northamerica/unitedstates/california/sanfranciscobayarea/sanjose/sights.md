One San Jose attraction is The Winchester Mystery House. Designed on tablecloths and paper by Sarah Winchester, this home took 38 years to complete. This house has 40 staircases, 52 skylights, 47 fireplaces, 19 chimneys, 5 or 6 kitchens, 2 basements, 10,000 window panes, and gas lights that operate with a push of a button. The Winchester Mystery House hosts Flashlight Tours every Friday the 13th and around Halloween. Guests tour this creepy house with only flashlights and moonlight. These tours sell out quickly, so advance ordering is recommended. For information, directions and rates, visit their website at winchestermysteryhouse.com.
