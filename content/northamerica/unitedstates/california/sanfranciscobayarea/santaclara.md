Be a kid at Paramount's Great America theme park in Santa Clara. Garlic lovers must stop in nearby Gilroy, the World's Garlic Capital. Also near, at Big Basin Redwoods State Park, see 330-foot-tall redwoods and ride through them on a steam train at Roaring Camp in Felton.
