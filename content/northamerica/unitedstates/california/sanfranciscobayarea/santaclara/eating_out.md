### [University Chicken](/northamerica/unitedstates/california/sanfranciscobayarea/santaclara/eating_out/university_chicken)

If you enjoy spicy chicken, this is your Graceland! The atmosphere is campy. It is located just across the street from the University of Santa Clara. The menu allows for you to select the degree of spicy...from mild to atomic to global thermo-nuclear! For the very bravest of souls, there is 911. 911 is not on the regular menu and should not be attempted by the *normal*. I live 200 miles away, but everytime I visit family a trip to University Chicken is a must.
