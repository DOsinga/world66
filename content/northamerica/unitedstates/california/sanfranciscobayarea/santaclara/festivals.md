### [Gilroy Garlic Festival](/northamerica/unitedstates/california/sanfranciscobayarea/santaclara/festivals/gilroy_garlic_fest)

Since 1979 the **Gilroy Garlic Festival** has been the place to try
and enjoy all things garlic. Entertainment includes live music
and a garlic cook-off. Be sure not to miss the garlic fries and
garlic ice cream!

The festival is usually held near the end of July.

http://www.gilroygarlicfestival.com/

### [Mushroom Mardi Gras](/northamerica/unitedstates/california/sanfranciscobayarea/santaclara/festivals/mushroom_mardi_gra)

Have a great time while enjoying arts and crafts, live music, and mushroom themed food at the **Morgan Hill Mushroom Mardi Gras**.

Usually held near the end of May in downtown Morgan Hill.
