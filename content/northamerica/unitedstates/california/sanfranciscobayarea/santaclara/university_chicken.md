A no-frills chicken spot right across El Camino Real from Santa Clara University, known for its heat scale that runs from mild all the way up to the off-menu "911" level. The vibe is casual and a little campy, the portions are generous, and the gallon pitchers of beer help take the edge off if you overestimate your spice tolerance. A local favorite that has been feeding hungry students and families for years.
