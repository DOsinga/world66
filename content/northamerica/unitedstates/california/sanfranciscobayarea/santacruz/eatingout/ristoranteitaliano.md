This restaurant is the best italian in Santa Cruz. I've lived in Santa Cruz for most of my 30 years. This delicious restaurant is always our choice for visiting friends, family get-togethers, or even a romantic dinner. On summer fog-less nights you can sit outside on the heated patio. The prices are affordable (entrees 8-16$) and there is something to please everyone.
