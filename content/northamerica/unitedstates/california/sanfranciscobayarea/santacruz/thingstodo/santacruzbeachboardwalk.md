California's only beachside amusement park features 30 rides, Neptune's Kingdom adventure entertainment center. Open daily Memorial Day to Labor Day. Weekends, holidays rest of year. 

The whole thing looks a bit on the tacky side and the house of horrors is unimpressive. The one slow ride going over it all is worth the money just for getting a view of it all from above. The water ride is also ok. 

The high light of the board walk must be the roller coaster, called Giant Dipper, which is made from wood and 80 years old.
