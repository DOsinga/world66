Caught between Campbell and Cupertino, the Westgate region is probably best known for quiet neighborhoods, shopping and the occasional resident seeing what he can edit on a travel site. 

Hidden in this bustling crossroads are many unique restaurants catering to tastes from Pizza to Persian with a wide variety of other tastes in between. A thriving commercial center boasts a wide range of shops and truly embodies the communities motto - *Westgate - Gateway to whatever happens to be west of here!*
