**The Best Seafood Bar-Cafe in Santa Barbara
**

One of the best **Santa Barbara Bars and Cafes** is The Endless Summer bar-caf&eacute;. In fact, The Endless Summer bar-caf&eacute; has been specifically mentioned as one of the best places to go for seafood in *Sunset Magazine*. The Endless Summer bar-caf&eacute; is located right along the waterfront, so while enjoying a tasty meal, you can enjoy the view of the harbor.

**D&eacute;cor**

Inside The Endless Summer bar-caf&eacute; you are going to find a very different kind of d&eacute;cor, especially for a famous restaurant in Santa Barbara. Inside the restaurant you will find surfboards hanging everywhere, but you can also watch endless surf videos while you wait for your meal. The reason that The Endless Summer bar-caf&eacute; is decorated with surfboards and surf videos is because it is named after the surfing movie &ldquo;The Endless Summer&rdquo; that was made in the 1960s. In fact while enjoying your meal, you might even get to meet the filmmaker of the movie, Bruce Brown.

**Dishes**

The Endless Summer bar-caf&eacute; only uses the freshest seafood. In fact, their seafood comes from the fisherman&rsquo;s market that is located right around the corner each day. One of the most popular local dishes is the Hope Ranch oysters, but not everybody is a fan of oysters. Something else that comes highly recommended is the popcorn shrimp and the fish tacos. If you are in the mood for a burger you should try the Rincon burger.
