---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment in Santa Barbara"
type: section
---

**Santa Barbara nightlife** is an experience that every visitor should enjoy while touring this Californian city.

**Blush Restaurant and Lounge**

This restaurant provides you with the ideal atmosphere to enjoy your evening meal and drink. You will find the menu extensive and the food fresh and tasty. They also serve a range of wines to suit your taste buds. Specials include: ladies nights on Tuesdays and half price on all bottles of wine on Mondays. Happy hour is everyday between 4 p.m. and 7 p.m. It is located on State Street.

**Coast Restaurant and Bar**

Santa Barbara nightlife should include visiting this bar & restaurant found within The Canary Hotel on Carrillo Street. Enjoy freshly-cooked seafood and other local dishes prepared by experienced kitchen staff. Try out the house specials available which will leave you yearning for more. The dining area is quite elegant and spacious. After a delicious meal, have a drink at the bar while enjoying the cool soothing music available.

**The Marquee**

Located on 1212 State Street, this grand establishment is a must visit. Located inside the Granada Theater, you will find the finest setting aimed at bringing back theatrical memories. Here you will enjoy household cocktails, beers, champagne, martinis and wines. The menu is very extensive with all kinds of dishes. Try out the house special oysters, shrimp, crab legs, caviar and lobster. The deserts are very tasty so make a point of having some.

**EOS Lounge**

For exciting Santa Barbara nightlife, go to this nightclub located on Anacapa Street. Here you will experience a mix of Californian and other cultures. For music and dancing lovers, the illuminated dance floor and nice music at the lounge will keep you on your feet. They also have happy hour every night and sometimes entertain the patrons with fashion shows and live bands. You will meet new friends in this nightclub.

**Rocks**

If you want to enjoy a wild night out in this city, make a point of going to this joint located on State Street. The place is very spacious with 3 bars. You can dine upstairs where there is ample seating space. There is also a terrace where you can enjoy your drink. The dance floor downstairs is the place to dance the night away. The music is good and the crowd very friendly. You will enjoy your night here.

**Dargan's Irish Pub**

For the true Irish hospitality and entertainment, visit this pub located at Ortega Street. You will be able to sample your favorite pint of Guinness, Irish whiskey and all kinds of special Irish dishes. You can also play a game of pool or darts. A local Irish band plays live music on Saturdays.

Santa Barbara nightlife is a truly worthwhile experience.
