title: "Sights in Santa Barbara"
type: section
---

**Santa Barbara sights** are for any visitor looking forward to enjoying beautiful surroundings during your vacation.

**Old Mission Santa Barbara**

It is among the oldest of all Santa Barbara sights. This mission dating back to the 1700s is the place to start your tour. You will be able to go on guided or self-guided tours around the mission museum. Tickets range from $5 to $1 depending on one's age. There is also a special tour (art & architecture) which you can book in advance. You will also be able to see the well-kept gardens, cemetery and vistas. There is also a very beautiful ancient church.

**Santa Barbara Zoo**

This 30-acre zoo is a very unique sight surrounded by ocean and mountains. You will find botanical gardens where over 500 species of animals exotic plants are conserved. It is open from 10 a.m. to 5 p.m. daily. Here you will find African wildlife (lions, cranes, tortoise, and giraffes), very rare Amur leopards, reptiles, apes and all kinds of insects. Educational zoo camps and sleepovers are also offered. They also have a restaurant where you can relax after the long tour. It is located on 500 Ninos Drive.

**Stearns Wharf**

It is among one of the most interesting Santa Barbara sights worth visiting. This ancient wharf is the place to watch local fishermen at work. Early in the morning you will see all kinds of fish that have been brought in. Take a walk while enjoying the breathtaking views of the Pacific Ocean and shoreline. Here you will also find the Ty Warner Sea Center where you can be in close contact with various kinds of sea creatures. You may be able to touch a shark. They also have education tours for all ages.

**Santa Barbara Museum of Art**

Found on State Street, this museum has a lot of collections on exhibit. Here you will discover antiques, paintings, photos and artwork from all over the world. The museum provides tours daily from 12 noon. During summer you can enjoy the various events like performances and exhibits done during the night. You will be able to meet new friends while enjoying yourself.

**Santa Barbara Botanic Garden**

One of the most beautiful Santa Barbara sights is this garden located at Mission Canyon Road. This historic landmark will allow you to take a nature walk while enjoying flora and fauna that is hard to find in one place. There is 40 acres with nearly six miles of path for you to explore. The garden library also has thousands of books and collections for you to peruse. There is also a garden shop where you can buy those memorable gifts to take home.
