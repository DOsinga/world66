---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Santa Barbara has a well-developed outdoor offer. The Santa Barbara Botanic Garden covers 65 acres of natural land with 5.5 miles of walking trails through native California flora — a genuinely beautiful place, not a manicured showpiece. The Santa Ynez Mountains directly behind the city have more than a dozen hiking trails; the Tunnel Trail is the most demanding, climbing to La Cumbre Peak at nearly 4,000 feet. Paragliding is well established here, with the mountains providing excellent conditions. Stearns Wharf extends into the harbour and has fishing, whale watching, and the Ty Warner Sea Center for marine encounters. The Santa Barbara Wine Country, with dozens of tasting rooms accessible by a marked wine trail, starts just north of the city.
