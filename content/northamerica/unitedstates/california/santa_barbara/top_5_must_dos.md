title: "Top 5 Must Do's in Santa Barbara"
type: section
---

There are very many **Santa Barbara must do's** to choose from.

**Santa Barbara Mission**

This is one of Santa Barbara must-do's. Located on Laguna Street, this ancient mission was built in the 1700s offers guided and self-guided tours for individuals and groups. The tour will take you through many rooms displaying ancient items and history. You will be able to see artwork and basketry by the Chumash Indians. Antiques and artifacts made during the colonial period can also be found here. A 200-year-old church is also found here. Make a point of visiting the garden and cemetery.

**Stern's Wharf**

Another Santa Barbara must-do is visiting this wharf on Cabrillo Boulevard. This wharf which is more than 130 years old and was entirely built with hardwood. Take a leisurely walk along the pier as you may see dolphins and whales, or do some fishing. There are plenty of restaurants and eateries available where you can get seafood and all kinds of food and drinks.

**Ty Warner Sea Center**

This sea center is open daily from 10 a.m. to 5 p.m. Here you will learn all about marine and life at sea. You will get an opportunity to come into close contact and touch some of the animals. There are many exhibits on display that you can photograph. There is a theater where you will see how beautiful the Santa Barbara Channel is. Walk through a tunneled aquarium to watch all kinds of sea creatures. You can also experience life as an oceanographer while here.

**Foxen Canyon Wine Trail**

Santa Barbara must-do's should include going on a California wine-tasting expedition. You can start with Andrew Murray which is a tasting room. Along the way you will find vineyards and wineries that have picnic spots and offer wine tasting. Firestone Vineyard and Curtis Winery are some of them. Rancho Sisquoc has a beautiful picnic ground. Most of these vineyards open at 10 a.m. You will really enjoy going on this tour.

**East Beach**

Santa Barbara must-do's is not complete without enjoying a day at the beach. There's lots of fun for all on this beach. Play a game of beach volleyball, ride a bike or roller blade. You can also decide to just lie down and get a suntan. During the weekend, there are afternoon football games and shows. If you feel hungry, there are restaurants and world-class hotels along the beach. There are also plenty of play areas for kids. Parking is not a problem.
