This is a **Santa Barbara travel guide** that will show you what to expect when visiting this California coastal city.

**Facts about Santa Barbara**

Know as the American Riviera, this city is located in the state of California between Los Angeles and San Francisco. This paradise is world renown for its resorts and hotels. Its coastline is one of the longest in the United States. Its beaches are known to be among the most beautiful. The mountains surrounding it are also magnificent. Getting to this city from anywhere in the U.S. or the world is easy as there is an international airport nearby. It is also part of the California wine country. Here many famous people own holiday homes and beach properties.

**What to Do**

A Santa Barbara travel guide will give you an insight of what lies in store when you get there. The city organizes special events all year like the Big Dogs Festival and a beach professional volleyball tournament. Every weekend is unique. The city parks are unique and very beautiful where you can do many activities like having picnics, jogging, strolling, riding bikes and rollerblading.

On Sundays, you can enjoy an art show done by the Santa Barbara's artists at Cabrillo Boulevard. For those who are diving enthusiasts there are many diving schools and clubs where you can hire a boat and equipment or simply take some lessons. Stearns Wharf is the place to go for some fishing at the end of the pier or get a boat and fish at sea. You may also get a chance to watch whales or dolphins swimming near the wharf. There is also the Ty Warner Sea Center where you can have the chance to experience close encounters with all kinds of marine life.

**Beaches**

Surfing is one of the most popular past times at the beach. Head to Leadbetter Beach which has nice waves for beginners. Most of the beaches have surfboards for rent. Beach volleyball is another sport that is very popular among the locals and tourists alike. There are many beaches to choose from depending on whether you want to have fun, get a suntan, have a picnic or take swim.

**Wine Country**

A Santa Barbara travel guide must include information about going on a wine-tasting adventure in the many wineries and vineyards available. Take a tour through the wine trails and have a chance of sampling all kinds of wines. You can also pack a picnic as most of the vineyards provides spaces.

For a complete Santa Barbara travel guide, visit the local tourist center to enjoy beautiful tourist destination.
