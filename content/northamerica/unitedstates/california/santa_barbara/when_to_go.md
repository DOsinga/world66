Santa Barbara has a mild Mediterranean climate year-round, with an average temperature of around 18°C (64°F). Spring (April to June) and autumn (September to November) are the most pleasant times to visit. Summer is warmer and draws larger crowds; marine fog is common in early summer mornings. Winter is mild but can bring occasional rain and rougher coastal conditions.

#### **Santa Barbara Travel Seasons**

The two preferred travel seasons are April to June and September to November. During April, May and the early part of June, the weather is pleasant with bright sun during the day. The period from September to November has a similar pattern with a pleasant wind.

The summer season is preferred by many to explore the city and indulge in daytime excursions. This is also the time when beachgoers from neighboring cities come to Santa Barbara. Making early bookings for summer time Santa Barbara trips is recommended.

**Santa Barbara Climate**

Santa Barbara has a moderate, coastal climate for most of the year. The annual, temperature averages around 64 degrees Fahrenheit. Summers can be slightly humid. Due to a substantial coastline, the city is vulnerable to having fog. Rains are seldom a problem and are usually restricted to brief showers. The best part about the climate is a soothing, ocean breeze that is prevalent for most part of the year. During the winter months of December, January and February, the coastal waters can be rough with harder rains to spoil plans.
