Santa Cruz has numerous drinking establishments located along Pacific Avenue.

### [The Bistro Bar](/northamerica/unitedstates/california/santa_cruz/bars_and_cafes/the_bistro_bar)

The Bistro Bar of The Casablanca Inn & Bistro offers an intimate setting for lunch and a view of the bay with an entertaining foreground of "people, flying pelicans, the occasional train, beach volley ball games, woodies, etc."
