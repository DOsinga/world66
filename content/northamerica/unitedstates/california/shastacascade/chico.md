---
title: "Chico"
type: location
---

In Chico, see gracious living preserved at gold-miner-and-gentleman-farmer John Bidwell's mansion and grounds, in Butte County's Bidwell Mansion State Historic Park. The lovely Italianate mansion-testament to Bidwell's vast wealth-is open for guided tours. Microbrewery fans will enjoy tours of the Sierra Nevada Brewing Company, also in Chico. Beyond Lake Oroville, view Feather Falls, the sixth-largest waterfall in the United States.
