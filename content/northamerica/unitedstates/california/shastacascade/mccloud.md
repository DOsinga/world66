Rustic McCloud, a historic lumbermill town, has quaint bed and breakfast inns and boutiques.  Majestic Mt. Shasta provides great downhill and cross-country skiing in winter and hiking in summer. At its base lies the quaint city of [Mount Shasta](/northamerica/unitedstates/california/shastacascade/mountshasta), with lodging and dining. Lake Siskiyou offers camping, fishing, boating, and windsurfing.
