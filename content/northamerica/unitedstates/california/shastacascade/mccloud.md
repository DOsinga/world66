McCloud is a small historic lumber mill town at the base of Mount Shasta in northern California. It has reinvented itself as a quiet getaway with bed-and-breakfast inns and a handful of shops, set against the spectacular backdrop of the 4,300-metre volcano.

The McCloud River, running through forested canyon land south of town, has three waterfalls accessible from a single trail — Lower, Middle, and Upper McCloud Falls. The hike is one of the best short walks in the Shasta region. In winter, the nearby Mount Shasta Ski Park offers downhill and cross-country skiing.
