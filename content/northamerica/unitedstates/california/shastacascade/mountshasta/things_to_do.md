---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Outdoor activity is the main draw here. The mountain itself is the centrepiece — hiking trails fan out across the lower slopes in summer, and the Mount Shasta Ski Park offers downhill and cross-country skiing in winter. Lake Siskiyou, just west of town, is popular for swimming, fishing, boating, and windsurfing when the weather cooperates.
