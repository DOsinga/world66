In Red Bluff, visit the William B. Ide Adobe State Historic Park-named after the first and only president of the California Republic-where you can see an 1846 pioneer adobe. View historical photographs and Native American artifacts at the Kelly-Griggs House Museum, a restored Victorian mansion. A cluster of antiques shops attract collectors of all kinds.
