Relax in Redding's Caldwell City Park, or tour the Redding Museum of Art and History, with its Wintu Indian artifacts. Also visit the Carter House Natural Science Museum. Shop for yesterday's treasures at the antiques shops in Cottonwood. Or, for modern-day goods, stop at the Shasta Factory Outlets mall in Anderson.
