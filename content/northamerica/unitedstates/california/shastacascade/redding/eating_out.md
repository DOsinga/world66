---
title: "Eating Out in Redding"
type: section
---

If you go to Redding, keep in mind that Redding's best secret is its restaurants. Look around - there are tons of them! If you want to try something in that area, try Holiday Market's custom sandwiches (you just check off what you want and give it to the server; they will make it for you.) Most importantly, it is very good. If you go to Back Bear Diner or Gironda's, be prepared for huge portions and great food, except if you go to Black Bear Diner and order spaghetti ***with sauce***, because they put way too much oregano in the sauce. Otherwise, eat whatever you want, because it's all good.
