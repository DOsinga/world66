A cluster of historic buildings assembled in a city park, anchored by the Murphy Avenue Victorian farmhouse that dates to 1895. The Sunnyvale Historical Museum occupies the old Carnegie library building nearby and covers the orchard era, the military years at Moffett, and the early semiconductor industry. Free entry; open Tuesday–Sunday. Guided tours available on weekend afternoons.
