**Watsonville, California Hotel - Comfort Inn Watsonville**

The Comfort Inn Watsonville is a moderately priced hotel conveniently
located in Watsonville, California in the heart of the Central Coast
just a few miles away from Monterey and Santa Cruz and fun area
attractions. Offering spacious and modern accommodations, family
friendly rooms and suites and discount vacation packages, the Comfort
Inn is the convenient hotel choice for your next family vacation,
romantic weekend getaway or business trip to Watsonville and the
Monterey Bay area.
