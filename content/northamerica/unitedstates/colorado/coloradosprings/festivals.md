**Memorial Weekend in May brings the arrival of Territory Days

**Travel to the south side of Colorado Springs and enter Old Colorado City. From here, you'll be transported back in time to when Colorado City was a bustling town in the large territory of Colorado.

These three days to play mark the beginning of summer in Colorado Springs. There are a multitude of street vendors - arts & crafts and plenty of food. Local bands play on the streets between 23rd and 27th streets on Colorado Avenue. The music typically starts at 10 o'clock and lasts until early evening.

There is no entrance fee and a free shuttle service allows for easy parking in the Coronado High School's parking lot (1590 W. Fillmore St.).
