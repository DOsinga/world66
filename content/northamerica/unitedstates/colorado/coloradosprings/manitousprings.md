Just outside
[Colorado Springs](/northamerica/unitedstates/colorado/coloradosprings), Manitou is made up of cliffside dwellings and one main street with touristy shops and an artsy flair. Manitou used to be famous for it's spring water. It has quite a few great restaurants, including the Briarhurst Manor. There's a salt water taffy store and a penny arcade. Old hotels in this area are being restored.
