The Rocky Mountain Dinosaur Resource Center (RMDRC) is a new museum in Woodland Park, Colorado, (just 20 minutes from ColoradoSprings) featuring fossils from North America’s Cretaceous Period including marine reptiles, pterosaurs, dinosaurs, a working fossil lab viewable by visitors, children's area, small theatre, hands-on educational activities, special events, and a great gift shop. Please visit our web site at www.rmdrc.com.
