Denver is home to several professional sports teams, including the Broncos, Rockies, Avalanche, and Nuggets. It is easy and fun to catch a game any time of the year.

 For all you football fans, don't miss a home game of the Denver Broncos. The Broncos are two time world champions and will be playing in famous "Mile High" stadium for only one more seasons. The fans are some of the best in the NFL and you will quickly be adopted into the "Orange Crush."

 Denver's baseball team is the Colorado Rockies. The Rockies play in Coors Field, which should be a stop on any tourist's list. The stadium is designed in the classic ballpark style and offers incredible views of the Rocky Mountians. Enjoy a relaxing afternoon in the outfield and then join the huge crowds of fans at the several sports bars lining Blake Street. For those die-hard baseball fans, tours of the stadium are available and the Rockies even have their own microbrewery on the site!

 The Colorado Avalanche is the newest edition to Denver's sports team. In their debut season here in Denver, the Avs won the Stanley Cup and captured the hearts of fans all over Colorado. The Avs are starting this season in the brand new Pepsi Center, which is worth seeing in itself. 

 Denver's basketball team is the Nuggets. Although the team has had a rough past couple of seasons, it is still worth catching a game if you have the time. Because of the Nuggets lack of popularity recently, there are great deals on tickets. This is by far the cheapest way to explore the inside of the new Pepsi Center.
