For upscale Denver shopping, take a trip to Cherry Creek Shopping Center on 1st Avenue. The latest styles in fashion and accessories can be found at such stores as Allen Edmonds, bebe, BOSS Hugo Boss, Burberry, Cole Haan, Crabtree & Evelyn, Juicy Couture, LaCoste, Michael Kors, Movado, Polo Ralph Lauren, Saks Fifth Avenue, Tiffany & Co., and Tommy Bahama. You can also catch the latest movie at Cherry Creek Stadium 8. If you get hungry, dine at California Pizza Kitchen, Panda Express, Johnny Rockets. Afterwards, indulge your sweet tooth at Haagen-Dazs. For driving directions, visit their website at shopcherrycreek.com.
