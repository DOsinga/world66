Keystone is a ski resort town in Summit County, Colorado, about 75 miles west of Denver along Interstate 70. The resort spreads across three mountains and is known for its long season, night skiing, and family-friendly runs, making it a popular choice for Denver residents on weekend trips.

In summer the resort converts to mountain biking, golf, and hiking, with a lift-served bike park and trails through the surrounding White River National Forest. The village at the base area has the usual resort mix of restaurants and shops, and the nearby towns of Dillon and Silverthorne provide more affordable dining and lodging. [Breckenridge](/northamerica/unitedstates/colorado/breckenridge) is just over the ridge, about fifteen minutes by car.
