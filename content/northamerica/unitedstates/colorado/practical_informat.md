Colorado Springs is about 70 miles south of Denver Colorado, in the United States. It is in the mountians, and great for people who love adventure. A famous landmark is Pikes Peak. Visitors to Colorado Springs can spend time hiking and mountian biking and enjoying the terrain of the mountians. There is also a fine arts center, and a museum of mining and industry. There are Broadmoor, and Embassy Suites for mid range hotels stays. There are also lots of different kinds of resturants to enjoy once you work up an appetite from hiking in the mountians.
