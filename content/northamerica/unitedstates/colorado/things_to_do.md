Outdoor activities abound in the Rocky Mountain state. With over 300 days of sunshine a year, Colorado offers a great environment to get out and play. One of the most popular past times is whitewater rafting. This is a sport that almost anyone can participate in under the direction of a qualified guide. Whether a beginner or a seasoned whitewater veteran, Colorado's rivers are diverse enough to offer something for everyone. The most popular river for whitewater rafting in Colorado is the Arkansas River. One of the states longest rivers, it travels over 110 miles through canyons and gorges before finally reaching the eastern plains of Colorado to continue its journey for an additional 1300 miles to the east.
