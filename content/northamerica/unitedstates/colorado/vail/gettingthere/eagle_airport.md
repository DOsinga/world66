The eagle airport is located approx. 35 minutes east of Vail. It is served during the ski season by *American Airlines*, *US Air*, *Continintal*, *Northwest*, *Delta*, and *United*.

Ground transportation from the **Eagle County Airport** to the city of **Vail** is found in the from of ***taxis***, ***rental cars***, ***shared shuttle service***, and ***public bus***.

The cost of a **taxi** is around **$150**, and they can hold up to 4 passengers. Look for a counter inside the terminal (on the west end).

All major rental car companys can be found at the Airport. The
rental cars can be rented at the ticket counter side of the airport
(east end).

Shared shuttle service can be found in the from of a Colorado Mountain
Express van. They have a counter located next to the taxi counter
at the west end of the terminal. Cost for door-to-door shuttle is
$**45**.

The Eagle County bus stop is located outside of the terminal at the
rental car return. There is a heated bus stop to wait at.
Cost of the bus **$3**.
