Travel Guide

Enfield is a town located in Hartford County, Connecticut,
United States. The population was 45,212 at the 2000 census. It sits on the
border with Longmeadow, Massachusetts and East Longmeadow, Massachusetts to the
north, Somers to the east, East Windsor and Ellington to the south, and the
Connecticut River (towns of Suffield and Windsor Locks) to the west.
