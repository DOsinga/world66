You might want to consider using these **New Haven day trip** ideas while visiting New Haven. Maybe you want to escape the university feel of New Haven, or perhaps you want to tour around Connecticut with friends. Either way, there are plenty of places to go and enjoy a nice day trip.

#### **Mystic, Connecticut**

Mystic is famous for being a water town. Most of their tourism is centered around their port, the Mystic Aquarium and sailing tours. One of the most famous sailing tours, Argia Mystic Cruises, offers tours to see the Mystic River, Harbor, the different lighthouses and peppering islands. There are plenty of galleries and homes of historic sea captains to visit here. While in town, drop by the famous seafood restaurants such as Abbott&rsquo;s Lobster in the Rough and S&P Oyster Co. for some of the freshest catches of the day.

#### **New York City**

New York City is just about an hour and a half away drive from New Haven. Whichever borough you want to explore for the day, whether Manhattan, Brooklyn or Queens, make sure to get around on foot to fully appreciate the street sights and sounds. New York City is lined with skyscrapers, chic art galleries, large stores, beautiful museums and bountiful restaurants. New York is also famous for their Broadway shows and performances by the New York City Ballet or the Metropolitan Opera. Don&rsquo;t forget to visit some of the famous architectural landmarks such as the Guggenheim, the Museum of Modern Art and the Empire State Building.
