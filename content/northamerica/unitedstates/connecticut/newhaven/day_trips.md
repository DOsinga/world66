New Haven sits on the Connecticut coast roughly halfway between New York City and Boston, making it a good base for day trips in either direction.

Mystic, about an hour east along the coast, is a classic New England maritime town. The Mystic Seaport Museum recreates a 19th-century village around a working shipyard, and the Mystic Aquarium is one of the best in the region. While there, eat at Abbott's Lobster in the Rough for lobster rolls by the water.
