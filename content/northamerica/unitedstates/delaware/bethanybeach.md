Bethany Beach is a small, family-oriented beach town on the Delaware coast, often called one of the "Quiet Resorts" along with Fenwick Island and South Bethany. It lacks the boardwalk amusements and nightlife of Rehoboth Beach a few miles north, and that is exactly the point — people come here for the beach itself and not much else.

The beach is wide and clean, with a short boardwalk and a bandstand that hosts free concerts in summer. The town centre has a handful of restaurants and ice cream shops along Garfield Parkway. Delaware Seashore State Park is just north of town, with additional beach access and good surf fishing. Bethany is a popular weekend and summer rental destination for families from the Washington, D.C. and Baltimore area.
