Known worldwide as a first-class resort town, Delray Beach is located
in southern Palm Beach County, directly on the Atlantic Ocean. Delray
Beach offers the visitor a full range of amenities and, of course, the
incomparable beauty of nearly two miles of well-groomed, easily
accessible beach.
