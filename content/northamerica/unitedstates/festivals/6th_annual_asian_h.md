If you're lucky enough to be in San Francisco in May, don't miss the largest gathering of Asian and Pacific Americans in the country at the 6h Annual Asian Heritage Street Celebrtaion (www.asianfairsf.com) on Saturday, May 15, 2010, 11 - 6 p.m., outside the Asian Art Museum leading up to Little Saigon close to City Hall. Two stages for non-stop live entertainment, an Anime area, mah jong court, children's rides and activities, wine tasting, Thai kick-boxing, a dance competition and much more are planned. Visit the diverse countries of Asia through different cuisines. For unique gifts, shop the Asian-themed arts and crafts. Free and open to the public.

Festival proceeds benefit local and national non-profit organizations.
