Asian Week Foundation presents its **6th Annual Asian Heritage Street Celebration** (www.asianfairsf.com) on Saturday, May 15, 2010, 11 a.m. - 6 p.m., outside the Asian Art Museum leading up to Little Saigon in San Francisco's City Hall area. Two stages for non-stop live performances, an Anime area, mah jong court, children's rides and activities, Thai kick-boxing, dance competition, wine tasting and much more are planned. Visit the countries of Asia through the diverse cuisines of each. Find unique shopping in Asian-themed arts and crafts. **Free and open to the public**.

Festival proceeds benefit local and national non-profit organizations.
