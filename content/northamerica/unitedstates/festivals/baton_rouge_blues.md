The 2015 Baton Rouge Blues Festival will be held on April 11 in downtown Baton Rouge. Originating in 1981, the Baton Rouge Blues Festival is one of the oldest free blues festivals in America. The festival’s mission is to encourage promotion, preservation and advancement of the "Swamp Blues" music native to Baton Rouge, Louisiana.EVERYBODY - young people and not-so-young people - are welcome to join freely the event !
