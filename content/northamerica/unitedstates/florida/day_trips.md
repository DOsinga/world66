Looking to get away for the day in Florida? Then try one of these **Florida Day Trips** and it could be your perfect therapy for fun and relaxation. Choose from going to the beach, visiting a museum, an aquarium, zoo or theme park; or simply hopping in the car and taking a relaxing drive to a beautiful destination.

**Day Trips in Central Florida**
- Fantasy of Flight aviation museum with one of a kind experiences of adventure biplanes and balloon flights.- Lakeridge Winery and Vineyards, wine testing at its best and the largest in all of Florida. Silver Springs, Florida's very first nature theme park and water playland.- Wet'n Wild Orlando, an invigorating water park, housing the very best water rides Florida has to offer. There are even cool rides for toddlers and kids of all ages including a huge Surf and Lagoon wave pool.
**Day Trips in South Florida**
- Flamingo Gardens is a favorite wildlife habitat and botanical garden in South Florida.- Lion Country Safari is a wildlife park where you can drive yourself around in your own vehicle and have a real safari type experience with all sorts of animals like elephants, giraffe, lions and more.**Day Trips on the East Coast of Florida**
- Fort Clinch State Park allows you to go back in time to approximately the 1800s when touring this original fort.- Kennedy Space Center offers a space adventure like no other. Experience the uniqueness of the center and learn all about missions from actual astronauts.- St. Augustine Florida offers a historical museum, ghost hunting, exceptional shopping and beaches. There are many attractions to choose from here including the famous lighthouse available for tour and exploration.- World Golf Hall of Fame caters to golf enthusiasts and allows you to interact in various exhibition areas. Be entertained and educated on everything to do with one of the favorite games of the world.**Day Trips on the West Coast of Florida **
- The Florida Aquarium where you can view and interact with all kinds of sea life.- Honeymoon Island State Park, a romantic beach destination that gives a feeling of seclusion but still easily accessible.- Tarpon Springs named the "Sponge Capital of the World," where you can go deep sea fishing, shop or take a cruise to the Gulf of Mexico.
**Day Trips to North Florida and the Panhandle**
- Amelia Island Museum of History offers over 4,000 years of Florida history and historic walking tours.
