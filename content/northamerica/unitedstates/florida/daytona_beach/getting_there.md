---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

The local airport is Daytona Beach International Airport (DAB), which is serviced by Continental and Delta Airlines. There are also a number of private charter services which operate out of the Daytona airport, such as Vintage Props and Jets. During busy times, it is difficult to book a direct flight arriving at DBIA.

The next nearest airport is Orlando-Sanford Airport (SFB), about 35 miles away, which is serviced by PanAm Airlines. Furthur away are Orlando International (MCO)and Jacksonville International Airports (JAX), both about seventy miles away from Daytona Beach. Orlando and Jacksonville offer service by most of the major airlines. 

Train service to Daytona Beach is offered by AMTRAK, with a stop in the nearby town of Deland. Greyhound Bus Lines has a station in Daytona Beach.

If you are driving, Interstate 95 skirts just west of Daytona Beach, with Exit 261 dropping you off on International Speedway Boulevard (US-92).
