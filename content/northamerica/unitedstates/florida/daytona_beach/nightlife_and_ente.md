---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment in Daytona Beach"
type: section
---

There are very few places in Florida that can compare to the **Daytona Beach Nightlife **arena for having a night of fun. If you like an easy-going atmosphere like a jazz bar or if you prefer to dance the night away there are plenty of places to visit. A good deal of the bars are either located along the beachfront or on Main Street. Most places cater to people who are here on special events like the Daytona 500 fans or spring vacation college students. But no matter there, all tourists are welcome anytime.

#### Bank and Blues Club

This highly-acclaimed watering hole is located on Main Street in what used to be an old bank. This is considered a more upscale place to hangout at than most of the other bars located around it. They have live jazz and blues music in the evenings. You will find the service here is top-notch and the drinks are reasonably priced.

#### Razzle

This famous discotheque has been around for a long time. The interesting disco special effects like strobe lights and a fascinating laser show keep the folks coming back. The music that is played here every night by a live DJ focuses mostly on the modern dance hits although you can hear a few oldies here and there. The DJ's have also been known to take quite a few requests. It is easy to find since it is located on Main Street with a lot of the other bars and clubs in the city.

#### The Aquarium Restaurant

By being located right on the water front you experience the feel of a laid back and soothing atmosphere. This restaurant and bar is the perfect establishment to go to if you are looking for a less wild but pleasant night. The place is trendy and usually attracts a more mature crowd. On Friday nights there is live folk music from 5 p.m. to 9 p.m. and then at 10 p.m. the live music changes to soft rock.

#### Dirty Harry's

It is impossible to miss this establishment since it is situated right in the center of the entertainment street. Like the name suggests this bar on Main Street has an interesting reputation. For a large part of the year the bar caters to a rougher style of crowd, but the bar has the capacity to draw all types of tourists. It is a very well-known place to party during biker conventions as well as spring breaks.

#### Giggle's Comedy Club

If you are on a low budget and are looking for a special night out then Giggle's is the place for you. On Wednesday night starting at 8 p.m. you can see nationally-acclaimed comedians perform. This is a highly festive place where you can have a fun time at little expense. The drinks they sell during the shows are really reasonable.
