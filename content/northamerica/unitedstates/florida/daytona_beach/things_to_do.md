---
title: Things to Do
type: section
---

Daytona Beach's highlights stretch along the coast and the edge of Ponce Inlet. The beach itself is the centrepiece — wide, flat, and open to cars at a modest fee — while the lighthouse at Ponce Inlet is the area's most rewarding attraction. The Daytona International Speedway dominates the western approach to the city and offers tours even outside race season.
