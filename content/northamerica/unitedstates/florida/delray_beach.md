Delray Beach is a small city on the Atlantic coast of Palm Beach County, between Boca Raton and Boynton Beach. It has a pleasant downtown centred on Atlantic Avenue, a wide pedestrian-friendly street lined with restaurants, galleries, and shops that runs straight down to the beach.

The beach itself is broad and well-maintained, one of the better stretches of coast in the Palm Beach area. The city has a more relaxed, walkable character than many Florida beach towns, and the arts scene is genuinely active — galleries are numerous and the Morikami Museum and Japanese Gardens, a short drive inland, is one of the most distinctive cultural institutions in South Florida.
