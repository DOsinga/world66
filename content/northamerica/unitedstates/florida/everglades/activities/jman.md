With an experienced guide in a small group you will travel by canoe or kayak through the watery wilderness of Everglades National Park. Depending on the length of your trip you may see the remnants of ancient Calusa Indian civilizations hidden among the mangroves or paddle next to playful dolphins. Perhaps you may even see an osprey dive into Chokoloskee Bay and pluck out a fish! If you are lucky you may spot the endangered West Indian Manatee!
