Florida is one of the best places in the world for an entertaining family vacation, so **Florida family travel **is
all about picking the very best locales for entertaining the kids,
parents and grandparents alike--and saving the rest for another trip.
Make sure you include stops to these places on your next family
vacation to Florida:

**Walt Disney World Resort
**

The family vacation destination that started it all - Orlando's Walt
Disney World Resort is the largest and most visited collection of theme
parks and attractions in the world. Children love the rides, water
parks and being immersed in the world of famous Disney characters. Adults can enjoy romantic
getaways, sporting excursions, art, cooking and other recreational
classes, as well as late-night musical performances and much more. Family
members of all ages can spend weeks at Walt Disney World Resort and
never get bored.

**Wannado City**

This indoor role-playing park in Ft. Lauderdale's Sawgrass Mills Mall is the first of its kind in the nation. Children get to experience miniature "grown-up" lives inside Wannado
City, where they'll be able to choose exciting professions, such as
chef, fire fighter, police officer, doctor, nurse, banker, circus
performer, theater actor, pilot, television reporter, dancer, lawyer, fashion designer, radio DJ and much more. They earn Wannado City "money" to spend around the miniature city after learning about financial responsibility as they open up a bank account at the Wannado City bank.

Wannado City is for children ages 2 to 14, but older siblings,
parents and grandparents can participate by becoming background players
in their world. You can also just relax at the lounge where Wi-Fi and refreshments are available and let the kids run wild.
