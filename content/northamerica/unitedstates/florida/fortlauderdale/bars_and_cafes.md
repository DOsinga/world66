---
title: "Bars and Cafes"
type: section
---

The nightlife is concentrated on three strips. **Las Olas Boulevard** has sidewalk cafés, wine bars and well-dressed cocktail places — **O'Hara's Jazz Café**, a fixture on Las Olas for decades, still has live jazz seven nights a week. **Himmarshee Street** in downtown Fort Lauderdale is rowdier, with nightclubs, sports bars and a lively after-work crowd. And along the beachfront, **A1A** is lined with open-air bars where the music goes late and the dress code is effectively swimwear. For something quieter, **The Poor House** on SW 3rd Avenue is a long-running dive bar with local blues and jazz.
