La Bonne Crêpe, located conveniently along Olas Blvd., offers authentic French cuisine featuring crepes with superb cheese and fresh seafood, as well as meats, eggs and vegetables. The Parisian-style restaurant pleases with its sidewalk allure and intimate setting indoors. An enormous assortment of crepes, both for main courses and for desserts, amazes the visitor who is able to watch the chefs cook their breakfast, lunch or dinner in the open kitchen. Specialties include Crêpe Nova Superbe with smoked salmon, asparagus, capers and béchamel. La Bonne Crepe is one of the most delectable of St. Lauderdale restaurants.
