Visiting the George and Dragon is an imaginary trip to the British Isles with over 1,000 pieces of memorabilia and photos, from the turn of the eighteenth century to modern times including sports, music and theater history.

The Fine Dining Restaurant has traditional British and International cuisine with wines to complement this gourmet fare. As one customer recently commented: "The George and Dragon have the best food I've ever experienced".

There are two separate bars - The Abbey Road Bar replicates a traditional British pub featuring late night music, dancing and live entertainment. And don't forget to check out our 'pub grub' menu and our award-winning fish and chips!

The Penny Lane Bar has an upscale traditional British pub atmosphere. Penny Lane is a non-smoking piano bar.
