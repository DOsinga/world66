---
title: "Museums in Fort Lauderdale"
type: section
---

The Museum of Science and Discovery, right in Fort Lauderdale, offers over 200 fascinating interactive exhibits, including the largest living Atlantic coral reef in captivity, sharks and a 12-foot snake. Pet alligators are ready to charm viewers and turtles and iguanas will excite the visitor. At the Museum of Science and Discovery visitors can take a simulated trip to the Moon or Mars at Runways to Rockets and then watch an IMAX film adventure on the biggest screen in Florida. It will take an entire day to absorb all there is to see at this museum.
