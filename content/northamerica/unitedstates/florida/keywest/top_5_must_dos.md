---
title: "Top 5 Must Do's in Key West"
type: section
---

Key West is manna from heaven for water sports buffs and seaside fun junkies, so you won't want to miss these top 5 Key West must do's.
This is a treasure trove of attractions that range from the simple yet
scintillating beach sunset to the exotic deep water adventures and high
action boat races. The brilliant conservatories, museums, natural parks
and ancient ruins of historically significant forts are the icing on
the cake.

**1. Visit the Nature Conservatory**

The Key West Butterfly and Nature Conservatory is a must visit for
its enchanting ambiance, which is the habitat of thousands of exotic
species of butterflies and multihued birds from all over the globe. Live your fairy tale fantasy at this almost surreal location that is
decorated with lush greenery and rippling waterfalls to complete the
magical look. There is an in-house learning center that traces and
imparts knowledge on the evolution of the fascinating butterfly. Make
sure to pick up a souvenir from the gift shop.

**2. Adventure on the Seaside**

Get seduced by the adventurous zeal of the seaside town and embark
on a snorkeling or scuba diving session at the famed crystal azure
waters of Key West. There are other pulsating activities like
parasailing, kayaking, jet skiing and many more that are earnestly
committed to offering tourists the thrill and excitement of a lifetime.

**3. Go on a Cruise**

The faint-hearted can try a more unruffled approach of discovering
the town with sunset sails and day cruises overlooking the coastal
delights of Key West along with its pristine water. There are also
regular marine excursion tours conducted by several groups in Key West.
Watch out for the exciting Dolphin Safari Charter where you get up,
close and person with these gracious and affable mammals.

**4. Check Out Ripley's Believe It or Not**

Get set to view some of the most astonishing aspects of nature’s
persona at Ripley’s Believe It or Not, a sprawling 12,000 square feet
attraction housing hundreds of quirky and bizarre exhibits. There are
thirteen oddly themed galleries that offer rare sightings such as the
one in a ten million white buffalo ‘Miracle’. This one’s a fun place
for an outing with the kids.
