Aventura Mall is one of Miami's largest mall featuring more than 250 upscale shops and a massive 24-screen theater with stadium seating. The mall has everything from Abercrombie and Fitch to Zara and AG to Urban Outfitters. Bloomingdale's, Macy's and Sears can also be found here as well as a salon and day spa. Mall Hours: Monday-Saturday 10:00 a.m.-9:30 p.m. and Sunday 12:00 p.m.-8:00 p.m.
