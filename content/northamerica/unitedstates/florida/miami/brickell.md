Miami's financial district south of the river has transformed into the city's densest residential neighbourhood — a forest of glass condo towers with a Brickell City Centre mega-complex at its heart. The area is walkable by Miami standards, with restaurants, bars, and shops clustered along Brickell Avenue and in the City Centre. The free Metromover loops through Brickell and connects to downtown, making it a convenient base. The dining scene skews upscale Latin American and Asian, with rooftop bars offering skyline views. Brickell is at its liveliest on weekday evenings when the after-work crowd fills the terraces.
