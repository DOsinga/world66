"Caribbean fusion" is the name of the game at Ortanique on the Mile and one visit to this restaurant is never enough for locals and tourists alike. Located in beautiful Coral Gables, Ortanique on the Mile serves up main courses such as Pan Sautéed Bahamian Black Grouper with Ortanique Orange Liquer Sauce, Jerk Double Pork Chop with Rum Guava and Caribbean Bouillabaisse, to name a few. Ortanique on the Mile is open for lunch Monday through Friday, and dinner daily. The restaurant features live Jazz at night. Reservations highly recommended.
