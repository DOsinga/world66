Things to Know about transportation from Port of Miami.

Port of Miami is considered the Cruise City of the World, if you are coming from outside of Florida there are two majors airports to choose; Miami International Airport and Fort Lauderdale-Hollywood Airport.

.Miami International Airport is 15 minutes away by driving.
.Fort Lauderdale Airport is 45 minutes away by driving.

There are 3 main different transportation to choose from/to Port of Miami.
1- Taxis, cost around $25 to Miami Airport and $75 to Fort Lauderdale Airport.
2- Buses from cruise lines, cost around $20 per person to Miami Airport and $30 per person to Fort Lauderdale Airport.
3- Shuttle companies, cost around $10 per person to Miami Airport and $20 per person to Ft Lauderdale Airport.

One of the shuttle company is Express Shuttle: http://www.ExpressShuttleMiami.com/miamicruises.html
