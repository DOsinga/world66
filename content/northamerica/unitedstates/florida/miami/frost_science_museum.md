Adjacent to PAMM in Museum Park, the Frost Museum combines a planetarium, a science centre, and a three-storey aquarium in one striking bayfront complex designed by Grimshaw Architects. The aquarium is the highlight — a 500,000-gallon Gulf Stream tank you view first from above, looking down into open water, and then from below through an acrylic lens as sharks and rays glide overhead. The rooftop terrace offers panoramic views of Biscayne Bay and the Miami skyline.
