A no-frills counter-service spot that has been frying some of the best seafood in Miami since the 1960s. The fish sandwich — a whole fried yellowtail snapper on Cuban bread — is legendary. Croquetas, fried shrimp, and conch fritters round out the menu. There is barely any seating, the line moves slowly, and nothing costs much. Cash only.
