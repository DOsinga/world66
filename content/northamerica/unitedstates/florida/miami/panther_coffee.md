The original Wynwood location of Miami's homegrown specialty coffee roaster, Panther is the neighbourhood's unofficial living room. Beans are roasted in small batches on-site, and the espresso and cold brew are excellent. The large open-air space, with a mix of indoor and outdoor seating, is a natural base for exploring Wynwood's galleries and murals.
