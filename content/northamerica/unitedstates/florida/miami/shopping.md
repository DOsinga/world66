Miami shopping ranges from open-air luxury malls to neighbourhood markets. The [Miami Design District](/northamerica/unitedstates/florida/miami/shopping/miami_design_distr) has become a destination in its own right, with flagship stores from most major fashion houses alongside galleries and restaurants. For more mainstream shopping, [Aventura Mall](/northamerica/unitedstates/florida/miami/shopping/aventura_mall) to the north and [Village of Merrick Park](/northamerica/unitedstates/florida/miami/shopping/village_of_merrick) in Coral Gables are the big draws. The [Coconut Grove Farmers Market](/northamerica/unitedstates/florida/miami/shopping/coconut_grove_farm) is worth a Saturday morning visit for local produce and prepared food.
