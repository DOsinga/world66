Looking for a deal? How about a steal? Then head over to Loehmann's Fashion Island for deep discounts on designer fashions for men and women. This open-air mall is also home to Barnes & Noble bookstore as well as a number of excellent restaurants including Presso Restaurant and Martini Bar, Fuji Hana, and Fish 54.
