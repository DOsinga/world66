South Beach is Miami’s hottest spot for nightlife, dining, shopping, and recreation. A major South Beach attraction is the Art Deco Historic District. The buildings in the district were constructed in the early 1900s and “they have influenced” buildings around the world. Art Deco District Tours are available every Wednesday-Sunday. Local architects and historians conduct the tours.
