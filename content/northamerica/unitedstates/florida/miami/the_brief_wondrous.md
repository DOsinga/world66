Not set in Miami but essential reading for understanding the Dominican diaspora that is part of the city's fabric. Díaz's Pulitzer Prize-winning novel tracks three generations of a Dominican family cursed by a dictator's ghost, blending street slang, footnoted history, and science fiction references into something entirely its own.
