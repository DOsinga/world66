The Village of Merrick Park is located in upscale Coral Gables Florida, so it should come as no surprise that visitors to VMP will find everything from Ann Taylor and Betsey Johnson to Gucci, Jimmy Choo, and Tiffany & Co. The Village of Merrick Park is also popular for its numerous international restaurants such as Cafe Ibiza and OLA Steak & Tapas.
