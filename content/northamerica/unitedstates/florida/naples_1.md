Travel Guide

Naples travel has never been easier. There are several airline carriers that provide service to and from Southwest Florida International Airport in Fort Myers which is 35 miles away from Naples. They are Air Canada, Air Tran, American, Cape Air, Delta, JetBlue, Midwest, Northwest, Southwest, Spirit, Sun Country, Sunwing, United, US Airways, USA 3000, and WestJet. If traveling by train, Amtrak has a drop off stop in Fort Myers. Greyhound bus travels to Naples. Naples has an airport, Naples Municipal Airport. However, only two charter airlines provide service to it. They are Yellow Air Taxi and Gulf Coast Airways.
