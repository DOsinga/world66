**Astounding Sights in St. Augustine**

**St. Augustine sights** are worth a visit because of their historical value. Most of these sights give you rich information of the vibrant past of the city of St. Augustine, Florida.

**Cathedral Basilica of St. Augustine**

Based on church records, this is the oldest cathedral in St Augustine. It is also the premier Catholic Basilica established in the United States. The intricate combination of designs display the history that the cathedral went through. The merging of Spanish, English and European influences are reflected in the modifications and renovations done after the war. You can admire the astounding architecture from outside or inside the church. Even from a distance, with its towering bell tower, it is one of the city&rsquo;s oldest but finest sights.

**St. Augustine Lighthouse and Museum**

This astounding museum has the goal to provide educational and informational data to people about the explorations of the sea and the preservation of these historical findings. It is in partnership with the Smithsonian, which is a well-known organization responsible in maritime exploration, environmental studies, and preservation.

**Castillo de San Marcos National Monument**

This is a part of St. Augustine&rsquo;s colorful history. It served as a fort to protect people from British raids and as a garrison and prison during the revolutionary war. Aside from the ancient cannons, its turrets and moats provide an interesting part of the tour. You can tour this monument and take superb pictures of the cannons and turrets. You can also take pictures of the powerful cannons as they fire them.

**Colonial Spanish Quarter Museum**

The Spanish influence in this Museum Village is predominant. As you tour the village, you can observe the old colonial Spaniard way of life. If you want your family to learn about culture and people, a tour of this village museum will be very educational. Your children will have a unique experience interacting with people in eighteenth-century costumes. Even their behavior and manner of speaking depicts the ancient times.

**Fountain of Youth National Archeological Site**

This is the pride of North America for its archaic displays of antiques and archeological findings. The Fountain of Youth believed to maintain youthfulness provides an interesting site for tourists. You can also visit the world renowned Indian Burial Grounds, the amazing Discovery Globe, the Shipwreck remains and the superb Navigator&rsquo;s Planetarium. You can bring your lunch with your and picnic with your friends while feeding the peacocks in the area.
