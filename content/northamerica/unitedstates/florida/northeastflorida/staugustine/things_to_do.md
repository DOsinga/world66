---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

St. Augustine's attractions are concentrated in and around the old city, making it easy to cover the highlights on foot. The Castillo de San Marcos and the Colonial Spanish Quarter are the best historical experiences. The Lightner Museum and Flagler College are worth seeing for their extraordinary Gilded Age architecture. Across the Bridge of Lions, the lighthouse and the Alligator Farm provide a more casual afternoon. The San Sebastian Winery, near the Florida East Coast Railroad headquarters, offers free tours and tastings of local muscadine wines.
