A living history museum in the heart of the old city, recreating life in 18th-century Spanish colonial St. Augustine. Re-enactors in period costume demonstrate blacksmithing, weaving, cooking, and other daily activities of the 1740s in a series of restored structures. Good for families and for getting a genuine feel for the colonial era.
