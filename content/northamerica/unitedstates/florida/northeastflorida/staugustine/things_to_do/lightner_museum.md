Housed in Henry Flagler's former Alcazar Hotel (1888), the Lightner Museum has an eclectic collection of Victorian art nouveau, mechanical musical instruments, cut glass, and curiosities. The cavernous indoor swimming pool of the old hotel has been converted into a café. One of the more unusual museums in Florida.
