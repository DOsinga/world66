Get ready to eat great seafood ; tap your toes to live bluegrass, folk and country music, and then stroll through more then 100 arts and crafts exhibitors. These are just a few of the highlights of the 30th Annual Lions Seafood Festival to be held in St. Augustine on March 11-13th, 2011. Tons of delicious seafood, outstanding music and affordable arts & crafts. Fun for all ages, so bring the kids. Contests and prizes too .Come to Francis Field at 29 W. Castillo Drive in St. Augustine, next to the Visitors Center. Festival hours are 3-9 p.m. on Friday, March 11th, 10 a.m. to 9 p.m. on Saturday, and 10 a.m. to 5 p.m. on Sunday. $2 for adults, kids 12 and under are free! Proceeds benefit Lions Club charities. Visit lionsfestival.com . Presented by Winn Dixie
