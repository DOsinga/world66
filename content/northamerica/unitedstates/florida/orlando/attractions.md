Orlando is one of the world's top theme park destinations. Walt Disney World is the centrepiece — a vast complex with four major parks (Magic Kingdom, EPCOT, Hollywood Studios, and Animal Kingdom) plus water parks and shopping districts. Universal Studios and Universal's Islands of Adventure are the main competitor, with several Harry Potter-themed areas. Beyond the major parks, International Drive has a string of smaller attractions and shows.

Theme park tickets are expensive and the parks are large; plan your days carefully to avoid spending most of your time in queues.
