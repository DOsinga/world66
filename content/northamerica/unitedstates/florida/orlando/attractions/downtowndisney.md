Walt Disney World Resort Lake Buena Vista, FL 32830 Phone: 407/824-4321 Fax: 407/390-5033 Website: http://www.disneyworld.com. Find multiple shops, restaurants, and nightclubs to occupy your time. Dowtown Disney Marketplace features shops and restaurants such as The Rainforest Cafe and the newest restaurant the Earl of Sandwich. Pleasure Island is the spot for adults who are interested in nightlife. There are about eight clubs that can be accessed with one admission. Downtown Disney Westside has Wolfgang Puck's cafe, Cirque du Soleil, and DisneyQuest, a five-story interactive play area with multiple attractions for all ages. Hours vary according to season.
