---
title: "Bars and Cafes"
type: section
---

Orlando's nightlife stretches well beyond the theme parks. Downtown's bars and live music venues along Orange Avenue and Wall Street Plaza draw a local crowd, while the International Drive corridor and Disney Springs cater to visitors looking for a polished night out. CityWalk at Universal is the most tourist-heavy option, a loop of themed bars and clubs. Thornton Park and the Mills 50 district are quieter and more interesting for a proper drink.
