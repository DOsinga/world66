---
title: "Shopping"
type: section
---

Orlando's outlets — Orlando International Premium Outlets and Orlando Vineland Premium Outlets, both on the south end of International Drive — are a major draw for bargain hunters, with name-brand stores discounted year-round. For a more traditional mall, the Mall at Millenia gathers the luxury brands (Louis Vuitton, Tiffany, Cartier) alongside the usual big-box anchors. The Florida Mall is larger and more mainstream, good for a broad range without the premium price tag. Disney Springs doubles as a shopping district and entertainment complex.
