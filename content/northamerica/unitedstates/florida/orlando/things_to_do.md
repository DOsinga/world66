title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Orlando is one of the world's top theme park destinations. Walt Disney World is the centrepiece — a small city of parks including Magic Kingdom, Epcot, Hollywood Studios, and Animal Kingdom, plus two water parks and a shopping district. Universal Orlando, a short drive away, runs Islands of Adventure and Universal Studios, both heavy on blockbuster IP and home to the Wizarding World of Harry Potter.

Beyond the big names, SeaWorld, Gatorland, and a string of smaller attractions on International Drive round out the roster. For something a bit different, the Kennedy Space Center is an hour east on the coast. Park tickets are expensive and the parks are vast — pick your days carefully to avoid spending most of your time in queues.
