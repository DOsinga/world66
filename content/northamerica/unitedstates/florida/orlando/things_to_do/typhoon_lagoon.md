One of Walt Disney World's two water parks, built around a shipwreck theme — a shrimp boat perched on a volcano after a fictional typhoon. The centrepiece is one of the largest wave pools in North America, flanked by water slides, a lazy river, and a snorkelling reef stocked with tropical fish and small sharks. Good for families with a range of ages; the smaller kids' area is well-designed.
