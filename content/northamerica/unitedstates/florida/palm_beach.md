The town of Palm Beach is located within the county of Palm Beach.

The town of Palm Beach is very small and is the home to countless millionaires, a few billionaires and wealth to the absolute extreme. There are more Rolls Royces here per head of population than anywhere else in the world (except Hong Kong).
