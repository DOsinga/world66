type: section

Schooners on Panama City Beach says it's the last local beach club. I don't think anything on Panama City Beach is local any more. Over the last 5 years they have made some drastic changes to the beach. In my opinion, the beach's Mayor and city councilmen have opened the flood gates and allowed anyone one with money to come in and destroy the beach with condos and more condos and high rise hotels. Panama City beach is no longer the sleepy little family tourist area. Change has not only brought destruction to Panama Citu beach but higher prices and increased crime rate. Rarely a week goes by that a murder hasn't been committed. Read the local news paper, " The News Herald". You'll read about increase gang activity and police killing.

Schooners used to be my favorite beach club. Harder to find now. If you can find it, it is stuck in between an ugly parking garage... yes a multi-level, ugly parking garage right on the beach!

You'll pay high prices for poor food at Schooners. Here is an example
1 hamburger with fries,
1 chicken sandwich with fries
2 kids meals
1 lg diet coke
1 beer
Total $54.00.
Hamburger and chicken sandwich was good, fries were plan old crinkle cut frozen fries and the kids' meals were pitiful. Schooners is no longer the old Schooners... I believe the last local beach club has left the beach.

Glory
