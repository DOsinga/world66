---
title: "Things to do in Pensacola"
type: section
---

If you are visiting Pensacola Florida, and have the beach, which is absolutely beautiful as your only destination, set aside some time to visit historic downtown in the city, guided tours are available of historic district. Strolling through the city is safe and charming. If you are traveling with bicycles even better, since the southern side of city is bordered by Pensacola bay and biking is fun along the bayfront.

Other nearby attractions are Pensacola Naval Air Station and IMAX Theater, And soon to open Maritime Park, And the zoo (always fun) in Gulf Breeze.

If shopping is your bag, their are many upscale antique shops, and malls, specialty shops of all sorts, and unique cafe's. Also the brand names are plenty.

Check who is in town, many big names perform at the civic center on a regular basis.

The art festival is super in the first week of November, and the fireworks are great over the bay in July, and downtown is always decorated for Christmas

Then when you go back to the beach, visit Fort Pickens state park, the Fort is amazing and free guided tours are available, plus you can wander around all you want.

There is so much more, just wanted to put some highlights out there to make the most of your stay.
