---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Beyond the beach, Pensacola has more to offer than you might expect. The historic downtown district is worth a guided tour or a self-guided stroll — the architecture spans several centuries and the streets are safe and walkable. Cycling along the bayfront on the southern edge of the city is a pleasant way to spend an afternoon.

The Pensacola Naval Air Station houses the National Naval Aviation Museum and an IMAX theatre. The Zoo Northwest Florida in nearby Gulf Breeze is a good family option.
