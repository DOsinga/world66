### [Monks Steamer Bar](/northamerica/unitedstates/florida/sarasota/bars_and_cafes/monks_steamer_bar)

Monks Steamer bar is a true raw bar. With a menu of oysters, (try them "Monkefella") clams, mussels, and shrimp combined with a decent draft beer selection and an always-friendly clientele, Monks is a pretty chill, dive-ish bar. They're reasonably priced for Sarasota, too.

If you like games, they've got a couple of pool tables and a dartboard. Plenty of seating, either in booths or around the bar.
