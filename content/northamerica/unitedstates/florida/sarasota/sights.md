There are numerous sights and things to do in Sarasota such as the beaches, Selby Botanical Gardens, historic downtown, Asolo Repertory Theatre and much more.

### [Selby Botanical Gardens](/northamerica/unitedstates/florida/sarasota/sights/selby_botanical_ga)

The Selby Botanical Gardens are located along Sarasota's waterfront and highlight Florida's tropical climate and plants. The historic Selby House is located on the grounds and open to the public with your admission to the Gardens.

### [Asolo Repertory Theatre](/northamerica/unitedstates/florida/sarasota/sights/asolo_repertory_th)

The Asolo Repertory Theatre is located on the University grounds. It has professional live theater in it and the plays and entertainment are very good.
