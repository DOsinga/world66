Just 35 miles east of [Orlando](/northamerica/unitedstates/florida/orlando), Florida's Pace Coast offers 72 miles of pristine Atlantic beaches. Along this impressive stretch of coastline, unspoiled nature preserves such as [Canaveral National Seashore](/northamerica/unitedstates/florida/spacecoastarea/canaveralnationalseashore) exist in perfect harmony with the space-age attractions of Kennedy Space Center Visitor Complex, Port Canaveral and the U.S. Astronaut Hall of Fame. Throw in the Cocoa Beach Pier and one-of-a-kind Ron Jon Surf Shop, and you've got something for everyone.
