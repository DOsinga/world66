---
title: "Festivals in St. Augustine"
type: section
---

There are a number of **St. Augustine Festivals** all year around. The festivals bring St. Augustine to life with several activities. St. Augustine is a coastal town in northeast Florida and the oldest existing city in the United States.

**Night Lights Festival**

The Night Lights Festival happens in St. Augustine during late November to the end of December. Storekeepers at the bay front decorate their stores in celebration of the holiday season. The festival can be traced back to it&rsquo;s Spanish heritage. During colonial times it was tradition to light a white candle in the window of a school front. During the festival there are a number of events to celebrate the holiday. There is a Christmas Regatta where the boats in the harbor hold a parade, every boat from yachts to schooners is represented. They decorate their boats to reflect the holiday season. On Saturdays there are antique fairs for shoppers to enjoy and at night there are carols as people walk around the lighted shops.

**Annual Fall Art and Craft Festival**

The Annual Fall Art and Craft Festival happens during Thanksgiving weekend on Francis Field and is a great place to bring holiday gifts. The fair brings in presenters from all over the country. Everything from pottery to paintings are represented. For tourists looking for a touch of culture, this festival is for them. The organizers of the festival sponsor workshops for everyone, no matter their age. This is a great destination for families and business travelers alike. Parents can explore the stands while their children can take art lessons.

**Rhythm and Ribs Festival**

People coming to this festival remember the food and crowds. There are a number of events during the festival, including live concerts. There are music workshops for children to enjoy as their parents tour the festival. This festival attracts all the best barbeque grillers from the southeast. Each year there are approximately 20,000 people who come to the Rhythm and Ribs Festival to enjoy music and ribs and to donate to charity. The festival gets awards each year.
