---
title: "Shopping"
type: section
---

Tallahassee is home to two major malls - the Tallahassee Mall and Governor's Square Mall. The latter is a more traditional mall, while the former is home to more-typical stand-alone stores. Governor's Square, located along Apalachee Pkwy., includes Sears, Burdines, J.C. Penney's and more and is where most of Tallahassee's residents shop. Tallahassee Mall includes a Dillard's, Barnes & Noble, Oshman's and a 21-seat AMC theatre and is used more for social outings.

Tallahassee is also home to three Wal-Marts, 11 Publix grocery stores and numerous other chain-like stores and small businesses. No major specialty retailers call Tallahassee home.
