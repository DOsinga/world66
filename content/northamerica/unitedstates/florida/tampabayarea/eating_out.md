---
title: "Eating Out"
type: section
---

Traveling is hungry work! And if you are vegetarian, things can get tricky. Luckily, Tampa boasts a huge assortment of choices when it come time to eat. For vegetarians, Trang Viet Cuisine on Fowler Avenue in Tampa is an awesome spot to enjoy a wide array of Asian style foods without meat. Some favorites are Vegan Curry Stew, Vegetarian Summer rolls, and Imitation Chicken Curry. Jasmine Thai on North Dale Mabry also hosts a large array of Tofu and vegetable dishes, and is quite excellent tasting to boot. For eating in a hurry, Evos (South Howard, Westshore Mall, St. Pete)serves traditional fast food fare like burgers and fries, but without meat. If you are in the mood for some spicy Mexican, give Taqueria a try. They are on South Howard in the Soho area.
