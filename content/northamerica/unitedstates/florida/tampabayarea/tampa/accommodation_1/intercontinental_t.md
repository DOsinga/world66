After a $20 million renovation, the [InterContinental Tampa](/northamerica/unitedstates/florida/tampabayarea/tampa) is the newest luxury hotel in the bustling, upscale Westshore business & shopping district. Centrally located in the heart of Tampa, the hotel is 3 miles from Tampa Airport and 5 miles from downtown and the Convention Center. The InterContinental Tampa Hotel is conveniently located to Tampa area attractions including Raymond James Stadium, Legends Field, The St Petersburg Times Forum and the beautiful Gulf Coast Beaches. World class shopping and dining are all within walking distance of the hotel.

The InterContinental Tampa features 323 elegantly appointed guestrooms and 21,000 sf of newly renovated meeting space including two spectacular garden atriums; making the hotel the perfect location for meetings, events and social gatherings. Among the many amenities our guests enjoy are; wireless Internet access, flat screen televisions, fully stocked refreshment bars, twice daily housekeeping service, airline kiosks and concierge services. Relax with friends in Shula's Steak House or No Name Lounge where you will enjoy exceptional service in this world class restaurant created by legendary Miami Dolphins coach Don Shula.
