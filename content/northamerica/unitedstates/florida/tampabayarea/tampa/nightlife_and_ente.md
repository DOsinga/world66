---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment in Tampa"
type: section
---

**Best for Casual Travelers**

The Rack is a great place to go to relax after a long day of exploring Tampa. It's a lounge with billiards, liquor bar, and some of the best sushi in Tampa. Dress casually and enjoy a nice night of playing pool and taking shots, both in the game and at the bar. Travelers can even catch a sports game on one of the thirteen plasma televisions in the bar. The Rack is open every night until 3:00 am and is nicely located in Hyde Park. The Rack is a great place for any traveler who wants to have a fun evening without adhering to posh club standards.

MacDinton's Irish Pub & Restaurant is a place made for the term "dinner and drinks". The food here is traditional Irish pub food and is delicious. There is an outdoor patio to enjoy an evening under the stars. This area is the place where you can just enjoy the atmosphere with a Guinness in your hand. The decor keeps to the the theme of the bar and helps transform your night into something a little more than the average bar experience. There are different drink specials every night and you must be at least 21 years old to enter. The staff at MacDinton's is fast and friendly. If you need a ride home MacDinton's will pick up the bill with the company Zingo's. They will even take your car home as well.

**Upscale Fun**

The Blue Martini is an upscale jazz lounge perfect for the traveler ready to get dressed-up and have fun. The atmosphere is elegant and unique. The lounge offers many signature martini's and other specialty drinks. There's live entertainment on most nights. It's a great place to meet new people or mingle with some you already know. The Blue Martini is open every night until 3:00 am.

Temple is another upscale club in Tampa. It's complete with valet parking and several VIP sections. Personal bottle service will make your night feel extra special and with the choice of four bars, you will have no problem finding a fabulous drink. Thursday nights are Latin night. This is a time when the club really comes alive. Temple is the perfect place for a couple to have a romantic and exciting evening.

**Perfect for Everyone**

GameWorks is a place made for adults who are children at heart. It is a restaurant, bar, and arcade all rolled into one amazing place for all ages to enjoy. The arcade games are not included in the price of your meal and will be an additional cost to consider. There are also areas to play pool and darts. After the hour of 10:00 pm no one under the age of 18 years old is allowed to enter.
