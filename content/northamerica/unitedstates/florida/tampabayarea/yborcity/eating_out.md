### [The Columbia](/northamerica/unitedstates/florida/tampabayarea/yborcity/eatingout/the_columbia)

This restaurant is the ultimate in Ybor City for an experience of the 'old time' Latin charm of the area. They serve a more traditionally Spanish menu than most of the more Cuban restaurants in the area. The menu is heavy on seafood and red meat, with enormous portions. They have good sangria, mixed right at the table. It is probably also worth the extra $6 to sit in the room with the dancers, so that you can get a flamenco show while you eat your dinner. They also have an adjoining museum that chronicles the 100 year history of the restaurant and [more..](/northamerica/unitedstates/florida/tampabayarea/yborcity/eatingout/the_columbia)
