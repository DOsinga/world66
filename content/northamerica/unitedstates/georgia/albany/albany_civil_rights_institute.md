title: Albany Civil Rights Institute
type: poi

Documents the Albany Movement of the early 1960s, one of the most significant early campaigns of the Civil Rights era. The exhibits trace how local activists, supported by Martin Luther King Jr., challenged segregation in Albany — with mixed results but important lessons for the broader movement.
