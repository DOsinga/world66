**The Albany Ballet Theater**

The Albany Ballet promotes the art of dance in the community through performances of classical ballet, modern ballet and jazz. The Ballet presents several performances a year at the Albany Municipal Auditorium.  

**The Albany Symphony Orchestra**

The Albany Symphony Orchestra is Southwest Georgia's only professional orchestra and is under the direction of a full-time resident conductor. The orchestra performs a 5 concert series on Saturday evenings at the Albany Municipal Auditorium and 3 matinee concerts on Sunday at Darton College.   [more..](/northamerica/unitedstates/georgia/albany/nightlife/thealbanysymphonyorchestra)

**The Albany Municipal Auditorium**

Albany's magnificent Historic Auditorium is fast gaining the reputation as a Center for Performing Arts, in the heart of Albany and Southwest Georgia.  

**Theatre Albany**

Theatre Albany offers five major productions during the regular season in its 314 seat main stage theatre. In addition, productions are offered in the intimate Studio theatre, as well as children's theatre, reader's theatre, summer youth workshops, and adult acting workshops  

**The Albany Chorale**

The Albany chorale is a long standing choral group presenting three concerts a year of classical, jazz, madrigal and American folk music. A small Chamber Ensemble group is chosen by auditions and is available for special community events. In addition, the Chorale performs with the Albany Symphony orchestra during the Christmas season and at other special musical presentations.   [more..](/northamerica/unitedstates/georgia/albany/nightlife/thealbanychorale)

**The Ritz Cultural Center**

The Ritz Cultural Center offers youth programs providing opportunities for involvement and participation in the arts. Youths may work individually or in a group, exploring their imagination and creativity through storytelling, photography, painting, dance, poetry, puppetry, drama, sculpture and crafts.   [more..](/northamerica/unitedstates/georgia/albany/nightlife/theritzculturalcenter)
