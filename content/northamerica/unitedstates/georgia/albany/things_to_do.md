---
title: "Things to Do in Albany"
type: section
---

Albany's main cultural attractions are clustered near downtown. The Albany Civil Rights Institute documents the Albany Movement of the early 1960s, when the city became a focal point of the Civil Rights campaign. The Albany Museum of Art holds a strong permanent collection including 19th and 20th century American and European works and one of the largest collections of traditional African art in the Southeast. The Flint RiverQuarium on the riverfront is a freshwater aquarium with a focus on Georgia's river ecosystems.

The Albany Ballet Theater and Albany Symphony Orchestra provide regular performances at the Municipal Auditorium, which has a fine beaux-arts interior worth seeing.
