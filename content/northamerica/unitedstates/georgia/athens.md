Athens is a university town about 65 miles northeast of Atlanta, home to the University of Georgia and a music scene that produced R.E.M. and the B-52s in the late 1970s and '80s. It's an appealing place to spend a day or two — good food, live music venues, and a walkable downtown centered on Broad Street.

The University of Georgia campus is worth wandering, particularly the older buildings around North Campus. The State Botanical Garden on campus is free and surprisingly extensive. Downtown Athens concentrates most of the restaurants, bars, and live music clubs; it's lively on weekends during the academic year.
