---
title: "Bars and Cafes in Atlanta"
type: section
---

If a trip to nothern Georgia is in your future, there are several Hiawassee Georgia Bars and Cafes that come highly recommended.

**The Yacht Club Boat House and Tiki Bar
**Located at Lake Chatuge Marina, this restaurant and bar offers great American cuisine: hamburgers, sandwiches, wings, wraps and curly fries. The Yacht Club and Tiki Bar is a popular local hangout and the atmosphere is light and casual. The Tiki Bar is right on the lake and during the summer months presents live music. The Yacht Club Boat House is open 7 days a week from 11:00 am until 10:00 pm, except on Friday and Saturday when it stays open until midnight.

**Southside Bar and Grill**
Southside Bar and Grill is a favorite among locals and visitors alike. With its very friendly ambience and awesome food (the specialty is Wings and Ribs), this is the place to unwind at the end of the day. Prices are reasonable. The most expensive item on the dinner menu is the 9 oz Tilapia Dinner for $11.95. For your drinking pleasure, you have a choice of beer, wine, and cocktails, including frozen drinks and Schnapps. Southside hosts a variety of entertainment including karaoke, darts, pinball and arcade games and a pool table.

**Nacoochee Grill**
A smoke free restaurant located near the Habersham Winery, the Nacoochee Grill serves Habersham wines. Opened for lunch and dinner, prices are moderate. While reservations are not required, they are highly recommended especially in the summer when tourism is high. Promoting casual fine dining, specialties of the house are seafood, steaks and local favorites.

**The Troll Tavern**
Located in Helen Georgia, the Troll Tavern offers a variety of dishes, including ethnic Mexican, poultry, seafood and pasta. A full service bar makes available a wide selection of beer and specialty drinks. Whether you are seated indoors during the winter months or outdoors on the river side during summer, the atmosphere is casual and fun.

**Mermaids and the Moosehead Lounge**
Both of these are located in the Ridges Resort and Club at Lake Chatuge. In a setting where the decor matches the name, The Moosehead Lounge is open for cocktails and snacks 7 nights a week and offers a cozy lodge where family and friends can mingle over drinks. If your plan is dancing, then Mermaids is the place to go. Mermaids hosts a full service bar, current top 40 music and plenty of space to dance. There are varying schedules for the seasons, but generally Mermaids is open every Friday and Saturday from 9:00 pm to 2:00 am.
