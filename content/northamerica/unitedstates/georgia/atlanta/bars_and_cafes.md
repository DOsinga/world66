---
title: "Bars and Cafes in Atlanta"
type: section
---

Atlanta's bar scene is concentrated in a handful of neighborhoods. Buckhead has the densest cluster of nightlife, leaning toward upscale bars and clubs. Little Five Points and East Atlanta Village are grittier, with dive bars, live music venues, and a younger crowd. Virginia-Highland is a good middle ground — walkable, neighborhood-scale, and a mix of sports bars, wine bars, and restaurants with late hours.

The Beltline corridor, particularly around Krog Street Market and Ponce City Market, has become a social hub with outdoor seating, food halls, and bars within easy walking or biking distance.

Craft beer has a strong presence — Monday Night Brewery, Sweetwater, and Wild Heaven are among the local breweries worth visiting. Most have taprooms open to the public.
