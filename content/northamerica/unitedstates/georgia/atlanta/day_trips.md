---
title: "Day Trips"
type: section
---

Stone Mountain makes the easiest day trip from Atlanta — about 16 miles east on US-78. White Water park (Six Flags White Water) is 20 minutes northwest on I-75, worth considering if you're travelling with children in summer. Chattanooga, Tennessee is 90 minutes north on I-75 and has the Tennessee Aquarium and several Civil War sites.
