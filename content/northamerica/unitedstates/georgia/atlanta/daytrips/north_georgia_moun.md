---
title: "North Georgia Mountains"
type: poi
---

Atlanta is nestled in the base of the beautiful Blue Ridge Mountains,
and with just a short drive into the country (less than an hour north),
you can experience all the splender of mountain life and still have
time to make it back for the Atlanta nightlife.

The mountains are packed full of fun things to do: hiking, camping,
swimming, tubing, rafting, biking, horseback riding and fishing.
You can even hike part of the Appalachian Trail: Springer Mountain at
Amicalola Falls is the Southern Terminus of the AT.

Any of the locals can tell you their favority mountain destination, but
some of the highlights are Dahlonega, the site of the first American
gold rush; Helen, a touristy and fun alpine-look-alike town; and Athens,
artsy home to University of Georgia.
