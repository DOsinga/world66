The Georgia Dome is home to the Atlanta Falcons football team. The Georgia Dome is located in downtown Atlanta, near many of the city's other attractions including the Philips Arena, [CNN Center](/northamerica/unitedstates/georgia/atlanta/cnn_center), and Centennial Olympic Park.
