The World of Coca-Cola celebrated its Grand Opening in its new location on May 24, 2007. With 60,000 square feet for you to explore, the World of Coca-Cola features more than 1,200 artifacts from around the world. Activities include:

*4-D Theater (3D movie with moving seats).

*Fully functioning bottling line that produces commemorative 8-ounce bottles of Coca-Cola.

*Over 60 different products to sample.
