Atlanta's major art museum occupies a striking Richard Meier-designed building in Midtown, expanded by Renzo Piano in 2005. The permanent collection spans American and European art from the 19th century onward, with strong photography and decorative arts holdings. Major touring exhibitions make regular stops here. Thursday evenings are half-price after 4pm.
