Zoo Atlanta is home to one of the largest collections of giant pandas in the United States — the main draw for most visitors. The zoo sits in Grant Park, a historic neighborhood just south of downtown. The African savanna section is well done, with gorillas, orangutans, and African elephants in naturalistic habitats.
