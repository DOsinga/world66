Travel Guide

Augusta is home to the Masters Golf Tournament held every Spring. The public is allowed on the grounds of the Augusta National Golf Course only during the week of the Masters. No access is permitted for the remaining 51 weeks. The entrance is gated off and security clearance is required for the gates to open. The best a tourist can do is either drive or walk by the golf course.

Not much appears to be going on in town. The tourist information office is worth visiting as it has a nice gift shop. The best thing in town is the lovely walkway alongside the river behind the main street.
