Cartersville is a small city about 45 miles northwest of Atlanta in Bartow County. It's a convenient stop between Atlanta and Chattanooga on I-75. The main draw for visitors is Etowah Indian Mounds, one of the best-preserved pre-Columbian Native American sites in the eastern United States — large platform mounds dating from 1000–1550 AD, with a museum on site. The Booth Western Art Museum downtown has an impressive collection of Western American painting and sculpture.
