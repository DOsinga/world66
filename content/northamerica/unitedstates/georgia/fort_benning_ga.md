Fort Benning (now Fort Moore) is a large US Army installation southwest of Columbus, Georgia. It's one of the largest military bases in the world and home to the National Infantry Museum and Soldier Center, which is open to the public and is a significant attraction in its own right — covering the history of the US infantry from the Revolution to the present day. Entry is free.
