Fort Oglethorpe is a small city in northwest Georgia near the Tennessee border, best known as the gateway to Chickamauga and Chattanooga National Military Park — the largest Civil War battlefield park in the United States. The park preserves the site of the 1863 Battle of Chickamauga, one of the bloodiest engagements of the war, with well-maintained earthworks, monuments, and a visitor center. The battlefield spans both Georgia and Tennessee and can take a full day to explore properly by car or bicycle.
