Travel Guide

Two hours north of Atlanta, Enota is situated between Helen, Hiawassee and Blairsville. Enota is surrounded by towering Georgia mountains and 750,000 acres of the Appalachian Mountain Forest. Enota Mountain Retreat is on 60 acres and blessed with five pristine streams and four waterfalls to enrich both the body and the soul. Rated as one of the best 100 retreat facilities nationwide, Enota can host large and small groups with a choice of lodging, a full compliment of meals, and meeting spaces.
Enota is based on shared ecological and social values and Rudolph Steiners biodynamic agricultural principals. Working with the principle of not taking more from the Earth than one gives back, Enota has the potential to be sustainable indefinitely, cooperating and living in co-creation with nature.
