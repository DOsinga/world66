---
title: "Eating Out in Hiawassee"
type: section
---

Dining in Hiawassee is more varied than you'd expect for such a small mountain town. Downtown has a handful of local restaurants and cafes, and the Ridges Resort on Lake Chatuge has several dining options with lake views.

**Michaelee's Chocolate Caffe** on Main Street is deceptively fancy for its name — a proper sit-down restaurant serving salads, paninis, pasta, seafood, and more. Good for a casual lunch or a quieter dinner. Reservations recommended at weekends.

**The Ridges Resort: The Oaks** offers the best views in town alongside Lake Chatuge. A mix of steaks, seafood, lamb, and local produce. Dinner reservations recommended Tuesday–Saturday; Sunday brunch buffet 11am–2:30pm.

**Mamasu's Brick Oven Pizza**, near the Georgia Mountain Fairgrounds, is the casual option — wood-fired pizza, pasta, and beer. Open daily.
