---
title: "Shopping in Hiawassee GA"
type: section
---

**Hiawassee shopping **offers travelers the talent of local
artisans and the spirit of a mountain town in this small, southern
city. Lovers of the great outdoors and people who appreciate the value of
hand-crafted small-town arts and crafts alike will revel in the
shopping that Hiawassee has to offer.

**ArtWorks Artisan Center
**

While traveling through Hiawassee, you may not have time to take an art class, but you can shop for premier, hand-crafted works of art made by the local citizens. Offerings at ArtWorks
Artisan Center include paintings, jewelry, furniture, pottery,
textiles, woodcarving, baskets, gourds, photography ceramics and other
hand-crafted works of art, ranging in style from picturesque to quaint. There's art for every price range and taste. When you shop at the ArtWorks Artisan Center, you'll be supporting the talented locals of this small town.

**Bearwassee Cabin Furniture and Decor**

Once you've been to Hiawassee, you'll want to take home a little of that rustic, mountain talent. What better way to capture the mountain
experience than decorating your home with hand-crafted cabin furniture?
With offerings such as chairs, benches, dining room tables, bedroom
suites, end tables, mantles and picture frames, there's a bit of cabin
flavor for every budget and for every traveler, who may not have the
space on the plane, in the car or back home to bring back the whole
cabin. Travelers can stop by to shop for pre-made furniture and decor or they can spend an afternoon watching the artisan
behind it all. "DJ" (David Jones), crafts custom-made furniture for visitors
right in his backyard. Making an appointment beforehand might be the
best option if you want to have custom furniture made.

**Body Sense**

Ladies--and gentlemen not afraid of pampering themselves--will want to make a stop at Body Sense, the premier natural day spa and
boutique in the town. Not only does the store offer massages and body
treatments, travelers can shop for natural, organic body lotions,
soaps, facial care and hair care products. There's also a selection of
aromatherapy candles , jewelry, women's clothing and music available.
Spa services start at $35 for 30 minutes. Buy any basic spa service and
add a massage, saving $5. Or, buy spa treatments in bulk for your entire
stay and save as much as $15.

**Struts Shoe Store**

Offering more than just shoes,
Struts Shoe Store is a great place to stop for handbags, clothes,
jewelry, leggings, belts, ties, backpacks, hats and scarves. The shoes
range from casual to dress, from camping to clogs and from boots to high
heels. Ask about special orders for special-width feet.
