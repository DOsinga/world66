Depending on what you plan on doing in
Georgia will determine your trip to **Hiawassee GA and when to go**.
Offering many different activities from outdoor sporting to sightseeing
adventures, the climate in Georgia can be mild at times, as well as
overwhelmingly hot at others. Planning your trip based on your desired
activities while there is your best bet to ensure that you have a great
time visiting Hiawassee.

**Best Time to Visit Hiawassee**

**Summer**

Many travelers to
Hiawassee schedule their trips during the highest tourist traffic in the
summer. At this time of year, the weather in Hiawassee is warm and with
the kids out of school, it makes for a convenient time to travel.
However, during the summer months the entire state is quite crowded and
if you want to avoid some of the tourist rush, this may not be the time
for you to visit. Additionally, at this time of year, gas prices rise
for summer travel, so anyone planning on visiting Hiawassee by car will
certainly want to take that into consideration.

**Spring**

Behind summer,
spring is the next most popular time to visit Hiawassee. The rains from
the mild rainy season end after April and spring flowers begin to appear
throughout the city. Visiting attractions at this time will be great,
as children are still in school, so there will be little hassle with
crowds. Temperatures during this time are cooler and less humid, making
the entire trip more pleasant.

**Summer Camping**

If you plan on camping in
Hiawassee, you will need to book your trip from June to September.
Outside of these months, many campgrounds close. Be sure to plan
appropriately and book your campground many months in advance, as the
campgrounds throughout the state are primarily only open during the high
season, making them fill up rather fast for prime weekends.

There
is really no bad time to visit Hiawassee, as the mild winters and short
rainy season make it a really great year round destination. Spring and
fall are generally thought of as the best time to visit because of the
stunning colors they bring with them. Depending on what your plan is
when you arrive in Hiawassee, you can plan a trip accordingly around
what activities you like to do to have the most enjoyable trip possible.
