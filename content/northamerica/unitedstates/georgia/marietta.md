Marietta is the seat of Cobb County, about 20 miles northwest of Atlanta. A well-preserved historic downtown square with antique shops, restaurants, and a small local theater makes it a pleasant half-day excursion from the city. The Marietta Museum of History and the Confederate Cemetery are on or near the square.

Kennesaw Mountain National Battlefield Park, just north of Marietta, preserves earthworks and artillery positions from the 1864 Atlanta Campaign. The park has excellent hiking trails with sweeping views of the Atlanta skyline.
