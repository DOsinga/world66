Norcross is a small city in Gwinnett County, about 20 miles northeast of downtown Atlanta. Its historic downtown is compact and walkable with restaurants, a community theatre, and some preserved Victorian-era buildings. The area is part of metro Atlanta's diverse suburban belt and has a large immigrant community with many international restaurants nearby.
