Savannah is very beautiful city in the Southeast of Georgia. History is still very much alive in its streets and stately squares. 

But Savannah is also a very lively place. Savannah's historic waterfront is lined with more than 100 unique shops and galleries, fabulous restaurants, seductive nightspots, and elegant inns and hotels. It's a great place to go at night. 

Savannah is an excellent starting point for day trips. Close to the city are a few good beaches for example in Hilton Head or [Beaufort](/europe/luxembourg/beaufort). For hsitory buffs, [Fort Pulaski](/northamerica/unitedstates/georgia/fortpulaski) is an great historic fort. Further down the coasts are more forts, e.g. on [St. Simons Island](/northamerica/unitedstates/georgia/stsimonsisland).
