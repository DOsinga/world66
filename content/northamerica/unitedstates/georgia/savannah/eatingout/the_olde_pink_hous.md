**The Olde Pink House Restaurant.**Built in 1771, this restaurant was originally a private home and has
served at one time as a bank, a tearoom, and as headquarters for one of
Sherman's generals. Today, this bright pink building houses the Pink
House Restaurant, serving traditional Low Country cuisine, including
sautéed shrimp, grits, fresh local blue crab, collard greens, fried
oysters, yams, and other traditional southern favorites in a formal
setting. To get more information, visit http://www.plantersinnsavannah.com or
Call 800-554-1187
