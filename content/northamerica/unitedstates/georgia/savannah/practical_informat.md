title: "Practical Information in Savannah"
type: section
---

Luxurious and beautiful, the Westin Savannah Harbor Golf Resort and Spa will make you feel like a new person. Southern heritage shows through at the Hilton Savannah Desoto. These hotels are near St. John's Cathedral. One of the best values in Savannah is at America's Best Value Inn. Stay close to the airport at Savannah's Day's Inn Airport Entrance. Both of these hotels are near the airport.
