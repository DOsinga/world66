### [High Musuem](/northamerica/unitedstates/georgia/sights/high_musuem)

The High Museum is located on Peachtree Street in Atlanta's midtown area. It is part of the Woodruff Center campus. The original Richard Meier building (now referred to as the Stent Family wing) is complimented by the recent Renzo Piano additions that face Lombardy Way and 16th Streets. It is the major art museum in Atlanta.
