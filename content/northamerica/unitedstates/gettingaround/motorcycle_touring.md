title: "Motorcycle Touring"

In addition to getting around by automobile, those who ride can travel by ***motorcycle***. Depending on your destination and distance to it, you might favor riding the interstate highways, or perhaps take a more scenic byway that still gets you there quickly. Check out the [Mother Road](/northamerica/unitedstates/gettingaround/route_66__the_m) on your bike.
