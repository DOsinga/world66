Almost every city in the United States has a commercial airport with in a hour and a half drive of it. Many large cities will have two, three and sometimes even five commercial airports. Most major European and Asian Carriers fly to the United States . Most South American and Caribbean Carriers fly to the United States also. Some African Carriers like Egypt Air and South African Airways fly to the US too.
From Europe : It is very easy to arrive to the United States from Western Europe as many airports have non stop flights from Western European Countries with either domestic or International Carriers. London-USA flights are the most popular.

Some cities that have non-stop service to Europe are: New York, Los Angeles, Miami, Washington DC, Newark, Houston, Dallas, San Francisco, Boston, Detroit, Minneapolis, Seattle, Phoenix, Orlando, Tampa, Ft. Myers, San Juan, Chicago, Atlanta, Philadelphia, Charlotte, Raleigh.

From Asia : From Asia it’s your gateway in the United States would most likely be: Los Angeles , San Francisco or New York .

From South America : From South America your most likely transit stop is almost a given will be Miami . As Miami is the Gateway to the Americas . Other airports which have non-stop service to South America : Los Angles, New York , Atlanta , Dallas .

From Africa : You can either connect in South America or Europe . But if flying some of the few non-stops flights or direct flight to the United States from Africa your flight will land in Atlanta or New York .

From Central America or Caribbean: From Central America or the Caribbean your most likely transfer point in the United States will be Miami. Also from the Caribbean San Juan since American Airlines has a hub there. Other airports which can be transfer points: Ft. Lauderdale , New York , Houston , Dallas , Atlanta , and Philadelphia .

From Australia New Zealand or the Pacific: It is almost guaranteed that your connecting point will be either Los Angeles or San Francisco .

From the Middles East: New York, Newark, Chicago, Atlanta and Miami have non-stop service to the Middle East.

From Canada or Mexico : From Canada it is very easy to get a non-stop flight to your destination with a Canadian or American Carrier. From Mexico Aero México and Mexicana both fly to the United States . Also American Airlines and Continental Airlines have a large presence in Mexico . Most likely transfer points would be: Houston, Dallas, or Miami .
