Honolulu is the capital of Hawaii, located on the island of [Oahu](/northamerica/unitedstates/hawaii/oahu). For most visitors it will be the first place they arrive and the source of their first impressions of the islands.

The places you will want to see include Chinatown, Waikiki Beach, and the Manoa Valley.
