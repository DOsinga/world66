Honolulu is the capital of Hawaii. It is located on the island of Oahu. For most visitors of Hawaii it will be the first place they go to and it will give them their first impressions of the islands.


 The places you will surely want to go to when you are here include Chinatown, Waikiki Beach and the Manao Valley.
