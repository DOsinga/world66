Waikiki beach starts 4 kilometer from Honolulu city. It is one of the finest stretches of beach in the world. World class hotels line the shore and there is always something going on.

**Beachs**

**Waimea Bay**

Located on the North Shore of Oahu, you can reach here by car in 45 minutes from Honolulu. Many locals favour this beach as the surf is high and you can watch and observe the best who dare brave the high waves of the Pacific which crash on this carved inlet.   Beach weddings at Waimea Bay are popular too! Along the way, stop by "Jameson's By The Sea" for some sauteed oysters - the best! [more..](/northamerica/unitedstates/hawaii/honolulu/beaches/waimeabay)
