It is possible to get to Hawaii in three ways:

1. Book a flight yourself -

Usually the cheapest way to go, there are many airlines that fly to Hawaii. United usually has a few good deals for off-season flights, and they are usually on time. Another good airline, if you are coming off the west coast is Aloha. Aloha is mainly an inter-island carrier but they have recently started flying out of a few west coast airports to locations in Hawaii. The flights are generally cheaper but don't expect the same food as a larger carrier. 

2. Find a package deal (like Suntrips or Plesant Hawaiian)

Package deals may look cheap, but that is only because they are cheap. Expect terminal delays and bad rental cars. 

3. Go through a travel agent

Some specialize in trips to Hawaii, many visit annually. Though a little more expensive than booking a trip yourself, this is one of the better ways to go if you are a first-timer
