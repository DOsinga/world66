---
title: "Beaches"
type: section
---

Kauai is the oldest of the Hawaiian islands and an interesting result of which is it has the most sand beaches of all of the islands. They range from perfect surf brakes to hidden coves where you can find all of the privacy you could ever want.

Snorkeling is a favorite past time and Kauai beaches offers some of the best opportunities in Hawaii. Make sure to visit Tunnels beach on the north shore to see the turtles swimming through the reef.
