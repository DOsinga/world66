Land in Kauai is very fertile and it is
home to guava, coffee, sugar cane and pineapple. Therefore, it is no
secret that the local cuisine in Hawaii is rich in fresh produce and
amazing dining experiences. Here are some recommendations for your
culinary experience during your stay in Kauai.**

The Beach House**

This award winning restaurant is on everyone's
must do list for Kauai. Chef Todd Barrett's expertise in preparing a
wide array of delicious selections specializing in fresh local Hawaiian
fish makes The Beach House a highlight of locals and visitors alike.
The Beach House is located at 5022 Lawai Road in Koloa and reservations are welcomed at (808) 742-1424.
**

Bar Acuda**

Jim Moffat's Bar Acuda emphasizes local fish and organic produce above all. Chef Moffat's travels throughout Southern Europe left an indelible imprint on his tasty tapas and appetizers menu. Try the bacalao (Portuguese salt cod) served with potatoes, garlic and cream. The grilled fig crostata topped with Meyer lemon ice cream is a sweet end to any meal guaranteed! Bar Acuda is located at 5-5161 Kuhio Highway in Hanalei and reservations are welcomed at (808) 826-7081.
**

Gaylord's at Kilohana**

This is a local favorite for the tastiest Sunday brunch in Kauai. Gaylord's at Kilohana
serves lunch and dinner daily and its menu includes Hawaiian and
international dishes including its own version for venison, lamb,
seafood, pastas and desserts. This restaurant makes an extra effort to
grow its own spices and herbs in itsKilohana gardens, which add a unique taste to its dishes. Gaylord's at Kilohana is located at 3-2087 Kaumualii Highway in Lihue and reservations are welcomed at (808) 245-9593.**

Roy's Restaurant**

Your
visit to Hawaii will not be complete without a visit to Roy's
Restaurant and Kauai is the perfect place to enjoy Roy's award winning
cuisine. The Roy's brand is well known because there are over 36 Roy’s locations around the world, 27 in the continental United States, 7 in Hawaii, 1 in Japan and 1 in Guam. Executive Chef Jason Verdun makes sure that every plate is guaranteed to please his guests. You can make your reservations at (808) 886-4321 and you can find Roy's Restaurant at 250 Waikoloa Beach Drive in Waikoloa.
