The fifth largest and least developed of the Hawaiian Islands, Molokai is only 20 minutes by air from [Maui](/northamerica/unitedstates/hawaii/maui) and [Oahu](/northamerica/unitedstates/hawaii/oahu).

Molokai's population, numbering fewer than 7,000, includes the highest percentage of people of native Hawaiian ancestry of any of the islands. Because of their friendliness to visitors, Molokai is known as "the Friendly Isle."
