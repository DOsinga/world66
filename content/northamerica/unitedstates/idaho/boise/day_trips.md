title: "Day Trips in Boise"
type: section
---

For those of us who love plants and gardening, one of your Boise activities must be a trip to Idaho Botanical Gardens. This non-profit organization provides an educational foundation for those who love or are interested in plants and horticulture. Visitors can see an outdoor classroom, alpine rock garden, the Lewis and Clark Plant Garden, a herb garden, and an iris collection. If traveling with your kids, they are going to love the Children's Garden. They can help tend to the planted vegetables and can visit the butterfly garden. For hours, directions, and admission information, visit their website at idahobotanicalgarden.org.
