Tetonia is a tiny farming community at the northern end of Teton Valley, Idaho. It has little in the way of tourist infrastructure but the views of the Teton Range from here are excellent — on a clear day the jagged peaks dominate the entire eastern horizon. Nearby [Victor](/northamerica/unitedstates/idaho/teton_valley/victor) and Driggs offer more amenities for visitors to the valley.
