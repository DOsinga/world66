Travel Guide

Home of the National Old Time Fiddlers Contest. This contest is over 50 years old now. It is held the 3rd full week of June every year. Fiddlers from all over the US travel here and compete to see who is the best. Carnival, food booths, crafts, activities for all ages, classic small town parade are just some of the activities.

Located at the confluence of the Weiser River and the Snake River on the Oregon border, this small town still hangs on to its historical past as it looks to the future.
