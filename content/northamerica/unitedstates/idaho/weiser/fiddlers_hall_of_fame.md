A small but genuine museum dedicated to the history of old-time fiddling and the National Old Time Fiddlers' Contest. Displays include memorabilia, instruments, and photographs chronicling more than five decades of the contest. It's the right size for what it is — you can cover it in an hour — and the staff are usually enthusiastic talkers if you want to go deeper. Located in the Courthouse Square area in downtown Weiser.
