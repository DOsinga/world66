This is the state of the Illini Indians who have long since gone. First the French to Illinois and established a fort near to where a river flowed into a big Lake. The big lake is Lake Michigan, the river the Chicago River and the place current downtown [Chicago](/northamerica/unitedstates/illinois/chicago). The whole state is known to the outside world really only for this City. Although Illinois has a wonderful outdoors in the South of the state near the capital, Springfield, most visitors will probably stick around Chicago. Whatever you decide to do you will enjoy yourself, but expect to make a lot of miles in a rented car if you want to leave the city.
