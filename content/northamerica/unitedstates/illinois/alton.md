Travel Guide

Each year restaurant's cook up amazing recipe's to enjoy, and you can sample many of them during Alton’s Restaurant Week that starts in Mid January. During Restaurant Week, many of Alton’s locally-owned restaurants come together to offer the best menu items at a great price. This is the ideal way to try a new restaurant because there are no passes to buy, coupons to carry or cards to punch. Just pull up a chair during Alton’s Restaurant Week for a tasty lunch or dinner meal offered at participating restaurants.

Getting There:Learn more about restaurant's participating in Alton’s Restaurant Week by visiting www.altonrestaurantweek.com.
