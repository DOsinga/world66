A small city on the Mississippi River bluffs north of St. Louis. Alton is known for its antique shops, its connection to the Lincoln-Douglas debates, and a lively restaurant scene. The annual Restaurant Week in mid-January is a good excuse to sample locally-owned restaurants at friendly prices.
