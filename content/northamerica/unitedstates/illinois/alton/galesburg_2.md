<p><strong>Join The Galesburg Historical Society Chocolate Festival</strong></p>
<br>
<p>In Galesburg, Illinois you can indulge your sweet tooth in an endless buffet
of tortes, cakes, pies, cookies, candies, beverages and more at the Galesburg
Historical Society Chocolate Festival in Mid February. This annual festival
features both homemade and commercially prepared chocolate treats on the third
floor of the Galesburg Antiques Mall. After getting your chocolate fix, explore
the mall to discover an eclectic collection of antiques and rare furniture
items.</p><br>
