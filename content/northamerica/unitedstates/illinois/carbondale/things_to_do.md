type: section
---

If you are interested in the outdoors, Southern Illinois is home to the
Shawnee National Forest. This 280,000 sq. acre forest is home to all
kinds of outdoor activites. To assist you in getting the most of your
vacation try hiring a guide. **Shawnee Adventure Guides** offers guided
rock climbing, rappelling, kayaking, canoeing, backpacking, hiking and
more. They are located in Makanda, IL but guide all over Southern
Illinois. Look at their website for trips and locations;
www.ShawneeAdventrueGuides.com
