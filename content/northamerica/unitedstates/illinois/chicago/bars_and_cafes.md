---
title: 'Bars and Cafes'
type: section
---

Chicago has a thriving live music and bar scene spread across its neighborhoods. The city's blues and jazz clubs are legendary, and the indie rock venues in Wicker Park and Lakeview have launched countless bands. For a quieter evening, the craft beer scene is strong -- Chicago is home to several excellent breweries -- and the cafe culture in neighborhoods like Andersonville and Logan Square is well worth exploring.
