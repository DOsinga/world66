Naperville has the best of both worlds. It's big enough to still have a city feel (population 140,000, the 4th largest city in Illinois), but far enough away from the city to have a more relaxed feel. Located only 30 miles from Chicago, Naperville is easily reached by Metra Train (www.metrarail.com) or by car. Major points of interest include: Downtown Naperville and the Riverwalk, Centennial Beach, Moser Millennium Carillon and Moser Tower, and the 13-acre outdoor living history Museum "Naper Settlement" (www.napersettlement.org). The DuPage Children's Museum and (www.dupagechildrensmuseum.org) Naperville Trolley Tours are excellent attractions for kids!
