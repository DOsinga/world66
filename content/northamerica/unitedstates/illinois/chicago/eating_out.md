---
title: 'Eating Out'
type: section
---

Chicago takes its food seriously, and the range runs from Michelin-starred restaurants to some of the best street food in America. The city's signature dishes -- deep-dish pizza, Italian beef sandwiches, Chicago-style hot dogs -- are worth seeking out at the places that do them right. Beyond the classics, neighborhoods like Pilsen, Devon Avenue, and Chinatown offer outstanding ethnic food at honest prices.
