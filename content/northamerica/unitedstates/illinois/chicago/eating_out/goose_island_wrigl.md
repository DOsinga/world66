Chicago's homegrown Goose Island brewery runs this Wrigleyville pub, a natural stop before or after a Cubs game. Over 50 beers on the menu, solid bar food, and the $1 pub chips are a must. The crowd is a mix of baseball fans and neighborhood regulars.
