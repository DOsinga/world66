A legendary late-night hot dog stand in Lincoln Park where the staff is famous for their sharp tongues and sassy attitudes. The Chicago-style dogs and cheese fries are good, but the real draw is the atmosphere -- especially after midnight on weekends. Not for the easily offended, but an authentic Chicago experience.
