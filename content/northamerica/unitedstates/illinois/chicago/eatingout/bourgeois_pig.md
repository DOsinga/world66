This cafe might as well be straight out of Paris; there's even a model Eiffel Tower outside the window. Tucked away on the outskirts of the DePaul University campus, the Pig swelters with intellectualism. Stacks of books, jars of loose tea, and vintage trinkets line shelves on the walls while muffins, cookies, ice cream, bagels, and other baked goods are piled up around the counter. The cafe offers a variety of soups and salads, but the best items are the sandwiches. You can build your own sandwich or chose a premade option named after books, like Hamlet or the Egg and I. Eat your goodies on the first floor at wood tables listening to retro rock music or upstairs listening to classical music. Outdoor seating options include the ground and upper floors.
