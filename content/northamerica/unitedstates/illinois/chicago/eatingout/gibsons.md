Premire Chicago Steak house. Huge protions and waiters with great experience. 
 Meat is brought to the table for viewing before ordering and the side dishes are enough for 
 2 or 3 people to share. The waiters will never recommend a well done steak.
 Everything is big here, including the bill, expect to pay $40 to $50 a person with salads and sides
 Drinks will make it a little more expensive. A true
 Chicago experience.
