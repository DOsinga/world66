Goose Island Beer Company is a microbrewery in Chicago that serves excellent beer brewed in Chicago and a variety of classic bar foods. The Wrigleyville location attracts pub goers and baseball fans alike and is a good place to go before or after the game. Try one of over 50 beers and the $1 pub chips.
