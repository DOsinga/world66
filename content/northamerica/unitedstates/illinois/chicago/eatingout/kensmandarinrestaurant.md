Ken's is a great restaurant tucked into the Lincoln Village Mall and it is quite easy to miss it. While not fancy inside their food is some of the best in the far north side of Chicago. Particularly recommended are there Mandarin Stir Fried Noodles, Kung Pao Chicken and Cashew Shrimp. Appetizers are good standard fare. Excellent neighborhood restaurant. Much higher quality than the standard greasy swill you often find in most northside Chinese food restaurants.
