Samah is both a middle eastern restaurant and hookah lounge. Sit in a private booth seperated by curtains on sofas or pillows on the floor around a low table. Hookahs are $15 with 19 tobacco flavors to chose from. Belly dancers will entertain you on Thursday and Friday nights, and the hummus and falafel are to die for. Be sure to make a reservation in advance.
