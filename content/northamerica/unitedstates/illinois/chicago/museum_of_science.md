A massive hands-on science museum in Hyde Park with exhibits that genuinely engage both children and adults. The highlights include a walkthrough German U-505 submarine, a recreated coal mine, and an Omnimax theater. The building itself -- originally built for the 1893 World's Columbian Exposition -- is worth seeing. Plan for at least half a day.
