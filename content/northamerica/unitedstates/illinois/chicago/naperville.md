Only 30 miles west of Chicago and easily reached by Metra train, Naperville has a charming downtown centered on the Riverwalk along the DuPage River. The 13-acre Naper Settlement is a living history museum that brings the 19th century to life. Centennial Beach, a former quarry turned swimming hole, is a local favorite in summer. With a population of around 150,000, it has enough restaurants, shops, and green space to fill a pleasant day away from the city.
