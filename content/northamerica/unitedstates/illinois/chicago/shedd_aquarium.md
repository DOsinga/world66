The world's largest indoor aquarium features ecosystems from around the globe, including a Pacific Northwest coast, a Caribbean reef, and an Amazon rainforest exhibit. The Wild Reef shark habitat and the Oceanarium with its dolphins and beluga whales are the highlights. Part of the Museum Campus, so it combines well with the Field Museum and Adler Planetarium in a single day.
