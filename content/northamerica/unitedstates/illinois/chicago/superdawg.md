A Chicago institution since 1948, this drive-in hot dog stand on the far northwest side is worth the trip for the experience alone. Look for the two giant hot dog figures on the roof -- one winking at the other. The Superdawg itself comes in a box with pickled green tomato and crinkle-cut fries. Pure, unpretentious Chicago.
