Winter, summer, spring or fall, there is always something fun and exciting to do in Chicago. This means, no matter when you decide to take your Chicago trip, you will always find something to suit your fancy. During the winter months, Millennium Park is like a winter wonderland with ice skaters enjoying the 16,000-square-foot ice- skating rink or tourists checking out the artwork and architecture by famous artists, designers, and landscapers such as Kathryn Gustafson, Frank Gehry, Juame Plensa, and Anish Kapoor. During the warmer months, biking along the Lake Shore Drive bike path is popular or sunbathing at North Avenue Beach. Continue reading to find out more about things to do in Chicago -- for all seasons!

### [North Avenue Beach](/northamerica/unitedstates/illinois/chicago/things_to_do/north_avenue_beach)

North Avenue Beach is the most popular beach in Chicago. During the summer months, the beach hosts championship volleyball games and other events, it has an outdoor workout area, rollerblade rink, a popular bar & grill called "Castaways," and it is located along one of the most famous bike paths in the city -- the Lake Shore Drive bike path.

type: Going to the Beach



tel: 773-327-2706

address: 1600 N. Lake Shore Drive

### [Navy Pier](/northamerica/unitedstates/illinois/chicago/things_to_do/navy_pier)

Navy Pier is the place where locals and people from the surrounding areas and beyond come to play. In fact, the number of visitors to Navy Pier is estimated at somewhere around 8 million. In addition to dozens of opportunities to take a cruise around Lake Michigan, a speed boat ride, or a number of architectural cruises, the Pier has a massive Ferris wheel, a game center, and a food court, several bar & grill restaurants, seafood restaurants, souvenir shops, and more. Navy Pier is located at 600 East Grand Avenue and is easily accessed by bus, taxi, subway, and a free [more..](/northamerica/unitedstates/illinois/chicago/things_to_do/navy_pier)

type: Sailing



tel: 312-595-Pier; 800-595-Pier

url: [www.navypier.com](http://www.navypier.com)

address: 600 East Grand Avenue

email: navypierinfo@mpea.com

### [Shedd Aquarium](/northamerica/unitedstates/illinois/chicago/things_to_do/shedd_aquarium)

Not only has John G. Shedd aquarium been a leader in the zoo and aquarium profession for more than 75 years, the building is a national historic landmark. The aquarium features several massive exhibits including: Wild Reef -- Sharks at Shedd, Oceanarium, Waters of the World, Caribbean Reef, and Amazon Rising. Shedd has regularly scheduled special exhibits.

type: Other



tel: 312-939-2438

url: [www.sheddaquarium.org](http://www.sheddaquarium.org)

address: 1200 S. Lake Shore Drive

### [Bike Chicago](/northamerica/unitedstates/illinois/chicago/things_to_do/bike_chicago)

There are a number of bike paths located throughout the streets of Chicago, but the most popular (and most scenic) bike path in the city is the path that runs along Lake Shore Drive. With the drive to the west of you and the lake Michigan to the east, this ride is certainly a feast for the eyes. Bike Chicago has the largest bike rental fleet in the city. In addition to bike rentals, Bike Chicago offers guided bicycle tours of the city (neighborhoods, sculptures, the lakefront, and more), segway tours, and group outings. Bike Chicago has several locations: Riverwalk at [more..](/northamerica/unitedstates/illinois/chicago/things_to_do/bike_chicago)

type: Cycling



tel: 312-595-9600

url: [www.bikechicago.com](http://www.bikechicago.com)

address: 600 E. Grand Avenue, Navy Pier

email: info@bikechicago.com

### [Sydney R. Marovitz Golf Course](/northamerica/unitedstates/illinois/chicago/things_to_do/sydney_r_marovitz)

If you want to take some time out to play golf in the city, look no further than the Sydney R. Marovitz Golf Course. Part of the Chicago Park District, this lakefront golf course is a 3,240 yard, par 36 course. The first hole runs parallel to Lake Michigan. Onsite is a snack bar and Clock Tower restaurant. Just a short walk away, you will also find several tennis courts, a bird sanctuary, Chicago's famous totem pole statue, and the north end of the Lake Shore Drive bike path.

type: Golf



tel: 312-742-7930

address: 3600 N. Recreation Drive

### [The Field Museum](/northamerica/unitedstates/illinois/chicago/things_to_do/the_field_museum)

One of the top natural history museums in the world, the Field Museum is home to more than 20 million specimens and a whopping 25,000-volume natural history library. Permanent exhibits offer a trip back in time to Ancient Egypt and temporary exhibits have included famous traveling exhibits such as the Auschwitz Album and King Tut, to name a few.

type: Other



tel: 312-665-7683

url: [www.fieldmuseum.org](http://www.fieldmuseum.org)

address: 1400 S. Lake Shore Drive

### [Chicago River Canoe & Kayak](/northamerica/unitedstates/illinois/chicago/things_to_do/chicago_river_cano)

Yes! You *can go* kayaking right in the heart of the city on a private trip or with a group. Chicago River Canoe & Kayak offers a number of kayaking events such as cardio kayak, trips to the areas rivers, moonlight dinner paddles, and more. Visit the website for details or call directly to speak with a guide.

url: [www.chicagoriverpaddle.com](http://www.chicagoriverpaddle.com)

address: 3400 N. Rockwell
