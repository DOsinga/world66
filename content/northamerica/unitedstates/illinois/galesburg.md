A small college town in western Illinois, home to Knox College and the birthplace of Carl Sandburg. The Galesburg Historical Society Chocolate Festival in mid-February is a local highlight, with an all-you-can-eat buffet of tortes, cakes, pies, and candies on the third floor of the Galesburg Antiques Mall. The mall itself is worth a visit for antiques and vintage furniture.
