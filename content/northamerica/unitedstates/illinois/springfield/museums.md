The Abraham Lincoln Presidential Library and Museum
is a 200,000

square foot complex situated in downtown Springfield, Illinois, and
is complemented by a meticulously restored Union Station Visitors
Center (completion scheduled 2006) and an adjacent parking facility
(completion scheduled May 2005).
