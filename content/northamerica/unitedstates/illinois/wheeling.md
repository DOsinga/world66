A northwest suburb of Chicago known for "Restaurant Row" along Milwaukee Avenue, where a concentration of high-quality restaurants -- many of them Asian and South Asian -- draws diners from across the region. Located about 30 minutes from downtown Chicago, Wheeling is worth a detour if you are after a good meal outside the city.
