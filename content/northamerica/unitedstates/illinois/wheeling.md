Travel Guide

Wheeling is one of Chicagoland's up-and-coming cities. Located about
25-30 minutes Northwest of Chicago, Wheeling is known for it's vast
array of high quality restaurants. "Restaurant Row" is the nickname for
the area along Milwaukee Avenue occupied by these fine eating
establishments. The heart of Wheeling is at the intersection of
Milwaukee and Dundee, where two beautiful fountains were erected in
recent years marking Wheeling's prosperous emergence.
