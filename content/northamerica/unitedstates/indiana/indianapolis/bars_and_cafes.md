---
title: "Bars and Cafes"
type: section
---

Indianapolis has a solid bar scene, particularly downtown. The oldest bar in the city, the Slippery Noodle Inn at 372 South Meridian Street, doubles as a live blues venue — check out the bullet holes in the walls from its rougher days. Nicky Blaine's Cocktail Lounge at 20 North Meridian Street is a quieter option with good martinis and a cigar-friendly atmosphere.
