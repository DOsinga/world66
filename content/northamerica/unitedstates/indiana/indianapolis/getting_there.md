---
title: "Getting There in Indianapolis"
type: section
---

Making Indianapolis travel a reality is easier. There are several airline carriers that are providing service in and out of Indianapolis International Airport. Travelers have a choice of choosing from American, Air Canada, Delta, Midwest, US Airways, Continental, Frontier, Northwest, and United. Two low budget fare airlines, Southwest and Air Tran, provide service to and from Indianapolis as well. Amtrak provides rail service and has a train station at 350 South Illinois Street. For directions and hours, visit their website at amtrak.com. For those who wish to travel to Indianapolis by bus, Greyhound or Megabus are your best options.
