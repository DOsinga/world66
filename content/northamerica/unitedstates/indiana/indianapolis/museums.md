---
title: "Museums in Indianapolis"
type: section
---

If you are traveling with your children, make sure one of your Indianapolis activities is a trip to The Children's Museum of Indianapolis. Your kids will get a kick out of seeing and participating in all the exhibits, events, and activities. There is Playscape where grownups and children under five can play together. Lily Theater offers a daily live show. Story Avenue lets children learn about history of storytelling. Make sure you take the little ones on a carousel ride in the Carousel Wishes and Dreams. For more information, including pricing and directions, visit their website at childrensmuseum.org.
