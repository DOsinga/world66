Rensselaer is a small city of around 4,000 people just off I-65 amid the plains of northern Indiana. **Amtrak** still makes stops in Rensselaer, though only two daily; in fact, the town's name is taken from a man named Rensselaer who was involved with the railroad when it was coming in. The buns for the **White Castle** hamburger chain are baked in Rensselaer, which helps keep its economy stable.

Though small, Rensselaer has a small private Catholic college, **St. Joseph's College of Indiana.** It also has a decent local chain **Arni's** pizza parlor, Chinese restaurant, and a seasonal ice cream parlor called the **Busy Bee**.
