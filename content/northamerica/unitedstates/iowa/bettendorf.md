Bettendorf is one of the four cities that make up the Quad Cities metropolitan area, straddling the Mississippi River on the Iowa–Illinois border. It is the quieter, more residential half of the Iowa side — neighbouring [Davenport](/northamerica/unitedstates/iowa/davenport) is the commercial centre — but has its own appeal for visitors passing through.

The main draw is the riverfront. The Isle Casino Hotel has a prominent position along the Mississippi, and the surrounding area offers a pleasant bike trail that follows the river. The Family Museum is a surprisingly good children's attraction. For anything more substantial — dining, nightlife, cultural events — Davenport and the Illinois cities of Moline and Rock Island are a short drive away.
