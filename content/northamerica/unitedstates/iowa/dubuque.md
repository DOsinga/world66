This evolving city along the Mississippi River offers beautiful scenery as well as cultual activity. River front offers new river museums, casinos,hotels and more. Enjoy boating, fishing, water skiing and snow skiing. Tour the cities historical district. Indoor water slide. Live theatre.Three colleges. Malls, churches and movie theatres. Beautiful countryside and river view parks.Make sure to take a road trip north on the Great River Road. An unforgetable visit and a wonderful place to live. Friendly faces everywhere.
