Home to a disproportionate number of attractions, including castles, the Moorehead House, a palatial skating rink and a half-scale replica of the H.M.S. *Bounty*, Ida Grove occupies a unique spot on the Iowa landscape. Worth a side trip if you're in the neighborhood.
