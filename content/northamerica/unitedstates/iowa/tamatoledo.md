Travel Guide

Tama-Toldeo is a small city with a big community. It is home to the
South Tama Trojans. Tama-Toledo holds the annual Proclammation Day
Powwow in July. Also, during the month of May, the annual Fireman
Carnival is held for four days. It may be a small town but it isn't as
bad as most, AYE!
