McPherson is a small town in central Kansas, set in the rolling wheat and prairie country along I-135. It is a quiet place most of the year, but comes alive on the fourth weekend of September for the McPherson Scottish Festival & Highland Games — one of the larger Celtic festivals in the Great Plains, featuring caber toss, Highland dance, bagpipe competitions, and a gathering of clan societies at Lakeside Park.
