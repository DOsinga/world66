Oxford is a small town located in Sumner County in south-central Kansas. Check out the Lions Club watermelon feed the first week of September each year. And Christmas in the country is a popular December event. Be careful when driving through Oxford, though. It is known as a bit of a speed trap if you are not watching your velocity. The biggest news in town lately is the addition of a Casey's General Store -- a modern convenience store with a pizza kitchen.
