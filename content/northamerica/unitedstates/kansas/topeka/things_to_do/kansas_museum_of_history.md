The state history museum covers Kansas from its Indigenous peoples through the frontier period, the Civil War, and into the twentieth century. The collection includes a full-size Santa Fe Trail wagon, a Cheyenne tipi, and exhibits on the Dust Bowl. Solid rather than spectacular, but good context for understanding the state if you're traveling more broadly through Kansas.
