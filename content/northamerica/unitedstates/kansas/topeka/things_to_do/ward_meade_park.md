type: poi
category: Sight
latitude: 39.0731

A five-acre Victorian complex in north Topeka with a restored mansion, pioneer cabin, one-room schoolhouse, and train depot, surrounded by botanical gardens. The mansion offers period-costumed tours and Victorian-style lunches. It's a pleasant afternoon stop, particularly in good weather when the gardens are in bloom.
