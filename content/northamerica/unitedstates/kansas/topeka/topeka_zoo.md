title: Topeka Zoo and Conservation Center
type: poi

A well-regarded city zoo known for its Gorilla Encounter — an enclosure where the glass wall inverts the usual arrangement, putting visitors inside while the gorillas roam an open outdoor habitat. The tropical rain forest building is also worth a look. Not a destination zoo on its own, but genuinely good for an hour or two, especially with children.
