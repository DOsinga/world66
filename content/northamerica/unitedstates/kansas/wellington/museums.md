### [The Chisolm Trail Museum](/northamerica/unitedstates/kansas/wellington/museums/the_chisolm_trail)

The Chisolm Trail Museum in Wellington is just across from the Sumner County Courthouse downtown in the old three story Hatcher Hospital. The Museum was located there in 1965 a few years after the hospital closed. Anyone who likes old stuff will think this place is great -- there are three floors with 40 rooms of artifacts from the past depicting life around Wellington, just 7 miles off the original Chisolm Trail where all the cattle drives used to come through.
