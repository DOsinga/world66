If you are a baseball fan, then one of your Louisville activities must be a trip to the Louisville Slugger Museum. This place gives an extensive history on the game of baseball and shows how the Louisville Slugger bat has remained a part of it all. You can see bats used by Babe Ruth and Joe DiMaggio. Be sure to visit the gift shop. There, you can buy a personalized Louisville Slugger bat with your name and/or an event. A popular item is the "Going to Bat Against Breast Cancer" pink bat. For more information, visit their website at sluggermuseum.org.
