---

If you plan on doing some shopping while in Louisville, be sure to check out the Jefferson Mall. They have all of the stores that you would want to shop at. Enjoy bargains at Old Navy, Bath & Body Works, Lids, Waldenbooks, Things Remembered, American Eagle Outfitters, Men's Warehouse, Charlotte Russe, Victoria's Secret, Macy's, and Kay Jewelers. When you get hungry, grab a bite at Sbarro, Chick-fil-A, Great Steak & Potato, Sarku Japan, and Max Orient. Be sure to check out their website, shopjefferson-mall.com before traveling to Louisville. They include great saving coupons that can be used at selective stores.
