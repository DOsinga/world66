Be sure to visit the magnificent
**
Louisiana State Capitol Building**

This National Historic Landmark is a 34-story, 450-foot limestone Marvel. It is a stunning example of Art Deco, and because it is built where Art Deco never really flourished, the style becomes more visible and vibrant. The Building features an observation deck overlooking Baton Rouge. The
hall inside contains larger than life statues of some of Louisiana's
governors. Definetily worht a look!
