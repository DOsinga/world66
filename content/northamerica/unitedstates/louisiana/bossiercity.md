Bossier City sits across the Red River from [Shreveport](/northamerica/unitedstates/louisiana/shreveport), and the two cities function as a single metropolitan area. Bossier's main draw is its cluster of riverboat casinos along the Red River — this is where most of the area's gaming action is concentrated.

The Boardwalk, a shopping and entertainment district along the river, gives the waterfront some life. Barksdale Air Force Base, home to the 2nd Bomb Wing, is a major presence in the city. Beyond the casinos and the base, Bossier City is a practical stop for anyone passing through on Interstate 20 between Dallas and Jackson.
