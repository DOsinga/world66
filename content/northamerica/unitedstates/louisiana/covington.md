Travel Guide

The city of Covington is the parish seat of St. Tammany Parish, in the
US state of Louisiana. It is located at a fork of the Bogue Falaya and
the Tchefuncte River. As of the 2000 census, the city had a total
population of 8,483.

Earliest known settlement by Europeans was in 1800 by Jacques Dreux. In
1813 John Wharton Collins established a town here with the name of
Wharton.In the late 20th century many people who worked in New Orleans started
living in Covington, commuting across Lake Pontchartrain on the Lake
Pontchartrain Causeway. Population continues to grow, bringing
Covington ever more into the Greater New Orleans Metropolitan area. In
2003, Covington elected its first female and first Republican, Candace
Watkins, as Mayor.
