The home of Delta Airlines and the worlds first Coca-Cola Bottling Plant, Monroe, Louisiana is rich in history.

A perfect travel destination for any outdoorsman featuring Watersports on the Ouachita River and on Bayou Desiard, hunting, fishing and more.

Places to See:

Food - Johnny's Pizza (A Louisiana Tradition), The Warehouse (Dining on the Ouachita River), Cormier's Cajun (Cajun Food served in a converted Gas Station.. Doesn't get more authentic than that)

El Song Gardens

Beidenharn Bible Museum

Antique Alley (West Monroe, LA)
