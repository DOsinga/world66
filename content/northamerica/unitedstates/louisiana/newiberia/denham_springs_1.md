Denham Springs is the fastest growing and largest city in Livingston Parish. They have one of the best public school systems in the state of Louisiana. The new Bass Pro Shops has brought with it an economic shot in the arm for the area. A new Sam's is in the building process plus other economic development is on the horizon. There is talk of a new Wal-Mart going in north of Denham Springs. It won't be long before Denham Springs expands its city limits both north and south. Keep an eye on this city because it is going places!
