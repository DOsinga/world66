**New Orleans nightlife** is for anyone wishing to enjoy themselves in every way you can imagine.

**Molly's**

This bar found on Toulouse Street has all kinds of day specials available. Here you will enjoy your drink while listening to the jukebox which has a variety of music to suit everyone's taste. The dress code is usually casual and the place is open Monday to Thursday 2 p.m. to 6 a.m. and Friday to Sunday at 12 p.m. to 6 a.m. Enjoy a pint of Guinness at a very special price on Thursdays (Sin Night). The bar staff is very friendly and the atmosphere exciting.

**Yo Mama's**

New Orleans nightlife should include going to this favorite local joint. You will find it on Saint Peter street near Pat O'Brien's. Food and drinks are available with a wide menu to choose from. Other amenities include: satellite sports TV, video poker and your favorite music. They also specialize in famous Mexican tequilas. As the night wears on, feel free to order the specialties from the kitchen which doesn't close till dawn. During weekends, they have all kinds of tasty seafood on the menu. This place also has an upstairs pub where you can enjoy a private moment with your friends. There is also a dance floor for anyone wishing to dance to the in-house DJ's music or special requests.

**Blue Nile Music Club**

If you enjoy live music, then this is the place to visit. Many famous bands play jazz and blues in this club which has two floors. A variety of drinks and cocktails are also available. There is also music being played by the resident DJ for you to dance to. You will find the club on Frenchmen Street along Elysian Fields Avenue.

**Cafe Lafitte in Exile**

New Orleans nightlife for all kinds of travelers should include visiting this unique bar. It is located on Bourbon Street and open from 1 p.m. to 5 a.m. on Mondays and Tuesdays and 24 hours the rest of the days. This place has an upstairs (with balcony) and downstairs pub. They both have music and video for your entertainment. Upstairs, you can enjoy a game of pool. Everyday they have happy hour when drinks are at low prices. They also have karaoke nights, disco night and special request nights.

**Coyote Ugly Club**

New Orleans nightlife is not complete without going to this saloon. Here the lady bartenders will entertain you with dances, and fire tricks. The atmosphere in this place is just like being in a cabaret. All kinds of beers, shots and cocktails are available from the bar. They also have happy hour every weekday.

New Orleans nightlife will leave you yearning for more of this bustling city.
