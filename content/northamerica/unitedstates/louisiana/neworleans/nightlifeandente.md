Pat O'Brien's is a first stop for the traditional New Orleans drink - the hurricane. The Main Bar is a great place with a long wooden bar an "old bar" feel. Hurricanes are a strong drink so if you are interested in something lighter try the Rainstorm which is made with Malibu rum.

The Old Opera House

The Old Opera House has a three for one happy hour during summer months. Bands playing cajun or country music can typically be found playing here.
