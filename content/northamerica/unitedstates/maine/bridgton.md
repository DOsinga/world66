Travel Guide

Discover the magic of the Greater Bridgton Lakes Region located in
the heart of western Maine’s lakes and mountains. Situated between
Portland, Freeport and North Conway, the Greater Bridgton Lakes Region
is a four-season family-friendly vacation destination with something
for everyone. Enjoy water sports in the spring and summer, snow and ice
sports in the winter and beautiful foliage in the fall. In any season
the Greater Bridgton Lakes Region area offers you the facilities to
enjoy a myriad of recreational opportunities. If
it's a relaxing retreat you're looking for, you've come to the right
place. The air is clean, the water pure, the woods inspiring, the
sights breathtaking and the night sky bright with millions of stars.
Our beautiful region is less than an hour’s drive from Portland,
Freeport and North Conway, NH.
