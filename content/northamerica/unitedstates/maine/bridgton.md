Bridgton sits in the heart of western Maine's lakes and mountains region, roughly equidistant from Portland, Freeport, and North Conway, New Hampshire. It is a four-season town -- water sports in summer, foliage drives in fall, skiing and snowmobiling in winter. The surrounding lakes and clean mountain air have made it a family vacation destination for generations.
