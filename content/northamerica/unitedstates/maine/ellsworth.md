Ellsworth is the commercial hub of Hancock County and the last town with major services before [Bar Harbor](/northamerica/unitedstates/maine/bar_harbor) and [Acadia National Park](/northamerica/unitedstates/maine/acadia). Most visitors pass through on their way to the coast, but the town has a pleasant downtown along the Union River.
