Fryeburg is a small town on the Maine-New Hampshire border, surrounded by the western Maine foothills. The Saco River draws canoeists and kayakers in summer, and the area has an extensive snowmobile trail system in winter. The town's biggest draw is the Fryeburg Fair, the largest agricultural fair in New England, held every October.
