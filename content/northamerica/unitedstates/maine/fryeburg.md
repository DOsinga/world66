Fryeburg Is a small town on the Border of Maine and New Hampshire. Fry eburg hosts a variety of activities such as canoeing the saco river, snowmobiling the vast trail system, a variety of small campgrounds, hiking in the western Maine foothills ande a bevy of other activities too numerous to mention. One of the towns biggest attractions is the Fryeburg Fair. this is the largest agricultural fair in New England.
