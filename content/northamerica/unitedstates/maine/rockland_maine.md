---
title: "Rockland Maine"
type: location
---

A unique town on the coast of Maine, featuring a beautiful lighthouse, windjammers, wonderful shops, restaurants and all the lobster you can eat. Billed as the lobster capital of the world and home to many outstanding festivals. During the summer and Fall, there are festivals every weekend. The downtown area, offers a quaint main street full of many unique stores and restaurants. While there, be sure to visit the Famous Saltwater Trading Company, the lighthouse museum and the Breakwater Lighthouse.
