---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment in Baltimore"
type: section
---

Baltimore is filled with places to dance all night. Several bars and clubs are packed every night with locals and
tourists alike. There are always drink specials advertised and the city is great for bar hopping. It's a good idea to look
up the different drink specials and events in bars and clubs all in a specific area of the city so you can better attack your nightlife adventures.

Most of the clubs seem to be for
the 20s to 30s age range and are very casual. They rarely have a
dress code and cover charges are usually low or only for special
events. There are different themed clubs or clubs that have theme nights,
but all of the clubs are known to have live entertainment and great drinks!

**Club Orpheus**

Club Orpheus is located in downtown Baltimore, near Little Italy. The
atmosphere is a little dark, with mostly industrial music. Many goth and punk people frequent it but it has a full
bar and great light and sound system. The club has a $5 admission price and remains open until 2 a.m.

**Sonar**

Sonar is a club located in a large warehouse and usually acts as the late
night party place for the younger crowd. There is usually a cover
charge but the amount depends on the events of the evening. Occasional big name
bands play at Sonar, but for the most part local talent and DJs
dominate the dance floors. Sonar has fairly inexpensive drinks and pool
tables.

**The Party Block **

The Party Block is a block where 3 clubs are located.
There's Paddock Nite Club, Big Kahuna Beach Club and Rush, and all of them
offer different kinds of music for different tastes ranging from hip-hop and live bands to techno. There
is a cover charge for those who arrive after 10 p.m. but the single charge is for all 3 clubs. Drinks cost approximatley $5 each and the clubs all stay open until 2 a.m.

**Bars**

There are several bars in the city. Some offer events like Karaoke
or dance night but for the most part the one thing they all have in
common is that they serve really great drinks. One great bar is the Ellicott Mills
Brewing Company that has an extensive menu of microbrews. Any adult who enjoys beer would like to get a drink or 2 at this
place. Throughout the city there are many other bars for all ages and interests.
