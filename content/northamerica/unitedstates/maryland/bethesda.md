Travel Guide

Bethesda is the place to go if you are visiting Washington D.C. Less
than 20 min. if you are driving and about 45 min. from the Bethesda
Metro to Metro Center. There is a Marriot by the Metro and that is a
good place to stay. Bethesda is usually less than an hour from:
Gathursburg, Dulles Airport, Regan Airport, BWI Airport, Union Station,
Baltimore, Rockville, Annapolis, Glen Echo Park (A kids favorite), and
many other places. Also, Bethesda has more rusturaunts per capita than
any oher US city.
