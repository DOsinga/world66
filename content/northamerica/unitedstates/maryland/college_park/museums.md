---
title: "Museums in College Park"
type: section
---

College Park is home to the **College Park Aviation Museum** (*http://www.pgparks.com/places/historic/cpam/*).
This museum is
located on the grounds of the world's oldest continuously operating
airport. Admission is $4 for adults, $3 for seniors, $3 per person for
groups of 10 or more, and $2 for children and students (children under
2 are free).
The College Park Aviation Museum is open daily from 10am to 5pm (except
major holidays) for self-guided tours.
