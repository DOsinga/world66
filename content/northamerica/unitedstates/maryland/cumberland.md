Cumberland is in western Maryland, on the North Branch of the Potomac River and just across from [West Virginia](/northamerica/unitedstates/westvirginia). It began as a railroad and canal port and has always had an industrial base. There are many great examples of beautiful architecture in Cumberland, many churches, and a wide variety of outdoor activities are within arm's reach. Interstate 68 passes right through Cumberland.
