Founded in 1626, the
City of Beverly is one of the oldest communities in the state.
Residents describe their city as the birthplace of the United States Navy, noting that the first ship commissioned by the Navy first sailed from Beverly Harbor.

Located in northeastern Massachusetts, Beverly is bordered by Danvers
on the west, Wenham on the north and east, Wenham and Manchester on the
east, and Beverly Harbor and the Danvers River on the south. Beverly is
7 miles north of Lynn; 18 miles north of Boston; 88 miles south of
Portland, Maine; and 238 miles from New York City.
