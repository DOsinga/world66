---
title: 'Bars and Cafes'
type: section
---

Boston has a strong bar and cafe culture, from historic Irish pubs to trendy cocktail lounges. The neighborhoods each have their own character: Beacon Hill is refined, the South End is stylish, and the area around TD Garden gets rowdy on game nights.

For live music, check the schedules at venues like the Paradise Rock Club on Commonwealth Avenue. For a quieter evening, the cafes in the South End and Back Bay are reliable choices.
