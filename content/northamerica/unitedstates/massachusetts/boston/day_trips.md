Boston makes an excellent base for exploring New England. [Cape Cod](/northamerica/unitedstates/massachusetts/capecod), [Martha's Vineyard](/northamerica/unitedstates/massachusetts/marthasvineyard), and [Nantucket](/northamerica/unitedstates/massachusetts/nantucket) are all reachable by ferry or car. Inland, the historic towns of [Concord](/northamerica/unitedstates/massachusetts/concord), [Lexington](/northamerica/unitedstates/massachusetts/lexington) and [Plymouth](/northamerica/unitedstates/massachusetts/plymouth) offer a window into the country's founding. Old Sturbridge Village, one of the largest living-history museums in the United States, is about an hour west.
