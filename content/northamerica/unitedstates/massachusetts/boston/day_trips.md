title: 'Day Trips'
type: section
---

Boston makes an excellent base for exploring New England. [Martha's Vineyard](/northamerica/unitedstates/massachusetts/marthasvineyard), [Nantucket](/northamerica/unitedstates/massachusetts/nantucket), [Cape Cod](/northamerica/unitedstates/massachusetts/capecod), and the Boston Harbor Islands are all reachable by ferry. Inland, historic towns like [Concord](/northamerica/unitedstates/massachusetts/concord) and [Sturbridge](/northamerica/unitedstates/massachusetts/sturbridge) offer a window into early American life.

Even the smallest surrounding towns hold some of the state's biggest attractions. Old Sturbridge Village alone draws a million visitors a year, and the Blue Hills Trail offers 125 miles of hiking just 10 miles south of the city.
