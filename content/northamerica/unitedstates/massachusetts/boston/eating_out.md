Did you know that Boston is called the 'Bean Town'? The city is famous for its baked beans. However, this does not mean that it is the most important dish. Boston offers all sorts of restaurants, ranging from tasty take-away places to restaurants near the harbour with everlasting dinner-parties and exclusive fishdishes. Don't forget to taste the seafood, as Massachusetts boasts to be the second­ largest in the fishing business after Alaska. Difficult to find a menu that lacks fish-dishes.
