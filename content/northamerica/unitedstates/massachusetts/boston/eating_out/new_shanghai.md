An authentic Shanghai restaurant in Chinatown with an enormous selection of entrees, cold dishes, appetizers, and desserts. The highly skilled Shanghai chefs carry on the tradition of earlier establishments in this location. The restaurant seats more than 120 guests and has a banquet room for private gatherings. A must for anyone who wants to try real Shanghainese cuisine without flying to China.
