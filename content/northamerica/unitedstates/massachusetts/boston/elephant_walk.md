If you want to try something different, head to Elephant Walk. This Cambodian restaurant has been featured on the Boston 100 list of Boston's Finest Dining establishments. Elephant Walk is popular for fusing the very best elements of French and Cambodian cuisine, producing a unique blend of flavors that cannot be found anywhere else in the area. Additional locations in Cambridge and Waltham.
