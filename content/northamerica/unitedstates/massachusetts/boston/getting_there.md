---
title: 'Getting There'
type: section
---

Most visitors arrive via Logan International Airport, which sits just across the harbor from downtown and is well connected by the T subway system. Amtrak serves Boston at four stations, with high-speed Acela service running the Northeast Corridor from Washington DC. By car, the Massachusetts Turnpike (I-90) is the main route from the west, while I-95 connects north and south along the coast.
