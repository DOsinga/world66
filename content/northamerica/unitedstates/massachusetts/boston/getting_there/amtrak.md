Amtrak serves four locations in the immediate Boston area: North Station, South Station, Back Bay, and Route 128. South Station hosts the high-speed Acela trains running the Northeast Corridor between Boston and Washington DC.

Trains to New Hampshire and Maine (the Downeaster) operate out of North Station only. There is no direct transit connection between North and South stations, so transferring between them is on you.
