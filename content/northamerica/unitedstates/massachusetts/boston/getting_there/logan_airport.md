The Boston metro area is served by Logan International Airport (BOS), with every major US carrier and many international airlines including British Airways, Lufthansa, Aer Lingus, and Icelandair. Shuttle flights to New York, Philadelphia, and Washington DC run daily.

If your destination is western Massachusetts, Connecticut, or Rhode Island, also consider flights into Providence (PVD) or Hartford (BDL). For Vermont, New Hampshire, or Maine, Manchester NH (MHT) or Portland ME (PWM) will save you a long drive out of Boston.
