A French-inspired cafe on Tremont Street in the South End, the kind of place where you can linger over a bowl of café au lait and a galette buckwheat crepe without feeling rushed. The menu runs to sweet and savory crepes, simple salads, and sandwiches. The room is small and the vibe is genuinely Parisian — bistro chairs, a relaxed pace, and no pretension. A good breakfast or lunch stop when exploring the South End's gallery-lined streets.
