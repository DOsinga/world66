It may be a chain, but it is a staple Boston establishment. Legal has several locations throughout the city, including Park Plaza, the Prudential Center, and even Logan Airport. The atmosphere is good, the food is superb, and most importantly the seafood is always fresh. The shellfish is reliably great -- stick to what they do best and skip the steak.
