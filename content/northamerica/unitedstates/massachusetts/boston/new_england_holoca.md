The New England Holocaust Memorial stands in Carmen Park on Congress Street near Faneuil Hall, along the Freedom Trail. Six luminous glass towers rise 54 feet, each etched with numbers representing the six million Jews killed in the Holocaust. It is a powerful, sobering stop. Walking distance from Haymarket (Green and Orange lines), Government Center (Blue and Green), or State Street (Blue and Orange).
