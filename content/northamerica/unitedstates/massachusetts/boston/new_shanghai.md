A long-running Shanghainese restaurant in Boston's Chinatown with an enormous menu of cold dishes, soup dumplings, braised pork belly, and other regional specialities. The kitchen carries on the tradition of earlier establishments at this address. Seating for over 120, plus a banquet room for private gatherings. Worth seeking out if you want real Shanghai flavours without the typical Cantonese-American fare that dominates the neighbourhood.
