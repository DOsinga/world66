From textiles to mobile art installations, there are a variety of museums in Lowell covering a range of topics.

### [American Textile History Museum](/northamerica/unitedstates/massachusetts/lowell/museums/american_textile_h)

"The American Textile History Museum tells America’s story through the art, history, and science of our textiles." (from the ATHM website)

The American Textile History Museum originally opened in 1960, under the name "The Merrimack Valley Textile Museum". Since 1997, it has been located in the historic Kitson Manufacturing Building on Dutton St, steps from the Lowell National Historical Park.

Permanent exhibits include "Textiles In America", a showcase in the history of the textile industry in America through the last 250 years; and The [more..](/northamerica/unitedstates/massachusetts/lowell/museums/american_textile_h)
