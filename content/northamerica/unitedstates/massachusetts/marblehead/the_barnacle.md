A Marblehead institution on Front Street, right on the waterfront. The Barnacle is known for its fish and chips -- battered, fried, and served in generous portions. The menu is straightforward seafood, the atmosphere is casual, and the harbor views from the outdoor seating are hard to beat. Expect a line in summer.
