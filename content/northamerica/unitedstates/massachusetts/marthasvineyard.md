Martha's Vineyard is a small island just off of [Cape Cod](/northamerica/unitedstates/massachusetts/capecod) and next to [Nantucket](/northamerica/unitedstates/massachusetts/nantucket). It is a popular summer vacation spot as well as home to a fair number of year-round residents.
