A popular restaurant located a few minutes walk from downtown, with spectacular view of the Edgartown Lighthouse.

Varied menu with many fish options, a popular brunch on sundays and the
option to take your cocktails on the veranda before or after the meal.

Reservations are recommended during the summer season and during holidays.
