The nightlife in Edgartown is quiet, even during the peak season. But there are a couple of nice bars spread around the town.

**Contributors**
November 01, 2005

### [David Ryan's](/northamerica/unitedstates/massachusetts/marthasvineyard/edgartown/nightlife_and_ente/david_ryans)

Popular hangout year around. Beers for about $5. Very crowded during the summer season.

### [The Wharf](/northamerica/unitedstates/massachusetts/marthasvineyard/edgartown/nightlife_and_ente/the_wharf)

Large pub and restaurant. Kareoke on Sundays.

Attracts a younger crowd than David Ryan's and can be very noisy during the summer.

type: Pubs



ClosingTime: 1

address: Lower Mainstreet

### [The Hideaway](/northamerica/unitedstates/massachusetts/marthasvineyard/edgartown/nightlife_and_ente/the_hideaway)

A stylish bar which is a bit tricky to find. Located within the
Shiretown Inn property it is usually very quiet. A good selection of
drinks and friendly bartenders.

type: Wine bars



tel: 508 627 1387

address: 44 North Water Street
