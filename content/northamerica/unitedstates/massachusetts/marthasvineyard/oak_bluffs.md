The largest of the six towns of [Martha's Vineyard](/northamerica/unitedstates/massachusetts/marthasvineyard), Oak Bluffs is a popular tourist destination as well as a historic religious campground. The colorful gingerbread cottages of the Martha's Vineyard Camp Meeting Association are one of the most photographed sights on the island.
