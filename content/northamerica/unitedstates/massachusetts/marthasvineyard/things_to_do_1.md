title: "Things to do in Martha's Vineyard"
type: location
---

On your way to South Beach, stop in at the Katama General Store. This quaint, little sundry has everything from gourmet goodies to beach accessories to all your essentials. They also have fabulous take-out food and boxed lunches...particularly the lobster rolls! 170 Katama Road, Edgartown 508-627-5071
