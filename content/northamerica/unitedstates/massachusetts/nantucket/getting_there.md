---
title: "Getting There in Nantucket"
type: section
---

Located 30 miles off the shore of mainland Massachusetts, there are only two ways to get to Nantucket. You can either get there by ferry, or by small plane. Many people prefer to come to the island by ferry, as it is always calming and enjoyable. As you enter the Nantucket harbor you pass by a number of picturesque sights. One of the first things you see is the Brandt Point Lighthouse welcoming you to Nantucket. As you look around you also see exclusive beach side properties, beautiful sailboats, and enormous yachts. To arrive on island by plane is also an experience you will never forget. As you make your way over the Atlantic Ocean, you get a bird’s eye view of the island. It is a chance for you to see the unique shape that is Nantucket. Once you have pulled in and docked the ferry or touched ground in a plane, you step onto the island and there is this feeling of serenity and excitement that surrounds and embodies you. You take a deep breath and sigh because you’re in Nantucket. All your worries and troubles are left on the boat behind you.
