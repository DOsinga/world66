---
title: "Shopping in Nantucket"
type: section
---

Downtown Nantucket is the center of island activity and has been since the 1700’s. Downtown offers a variety of casual and fine dining experiences. For breakfast, try the French toast at Arno’s, the crab cakes for lunch at Even Keel and dinner at the White Elephant. You can never go wrong at any of the restaurants on Nantucket. For shopping downtown is full of one of a kind stores. Old boathouses have been turned into little shops, boutiques and galleries. All the stores and restaurants on the island are all original to Nantucket and are owned by locals. There is always something happening downtown. On the weekends, Main Street is full of old-fashioned trucks, some filled with fresh picked vegetables from Bartlett Farm and some filled with wildflowers.
