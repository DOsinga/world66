---
title: 'Things to do'
type: section
---

Nantucket is a small island but there is plenty to fill your days. The Whaling Museum downtown tells the story of the island's seafaring past. Beaches ring the island -- some wild and windswept, others calm and sheltered. Biking is one of the best ways to explore, with flat, well-maintained paths connecting the main villages.

Keep an eye out for the man in the top hat and cape downtown -- that is William Alexander, the guide of the Nantucket Haunted Hike, a fun walking tour through the island's most supposedly haunted buildings.
