When downtown you will always see a man wearing a top hat and cape. Don’t be alarmed! This is the William Alexander, a Nantucket native and the guide of “The Nantucket Haunted Hike.” While on the tour, you are shown around to a number of haunted homes, inns and restaurants and informed of their frightening stories. It’s a fun way to learn about a little bit about the history of Nantucket. When asked about the ghost tour the Mangano family replied, "The best haunted tour on Nantucket Island. Great Nantucket history combined with Will's theatrics is very captivating...Don't miss it when you are visiting Nantucket...WE LOVED IT (The Nantucket Haunted Hike)!"
