Located in Newton Upper Falls, Bob's Sub Stop is a favorite of Newton locals who have visited the local sandwich shop since they were in middle school. Serving up delicious hot and cold subs for breakfast, lunch, and very early dinners, with fresh vegetables and perfect portions, Bob's is a Newton favorite for a reason. The best sub at Bob's is undoubtedly its Chicken Finger Sub, which is a dream-like marriage between warm breaded chicken and delicious sandwich toppings. Perhaps the best part about Bob's is its friendly service. Its owners have been part of the Newton community for years and are more than happy to accomodate middle and high schoolers who hang out and munch on breakfast sandwiches while waiting for the bus to take them to nearly Brown Middle School, Oak Hill Middle School, or Newton South High School. More of a pick up place than a sit down, its delicious subs, generous portions of french fries, and friendly service make Bob's a Newton landmark.
