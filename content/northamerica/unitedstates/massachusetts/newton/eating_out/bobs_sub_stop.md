A Newton landmark in Upper Falls. Bob's serves hot and cold subs for breakfast, lunch, and early dinners with fresh vegetables and perfect portions. The Chicken Finger Sub is the standout. More of a pick-up place than a sit-down, but the friendly service and generous portions of fries make it worth the trip.
