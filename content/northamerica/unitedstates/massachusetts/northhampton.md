**Northampton** is a lively college town in the Pioneer Valley of western Massachusetts, home to Smith College and a thriving arts and dining scene. Main Street is packed with independent bookshops, cafes, galleries, and restaurants — it is one of the most walkable downtowns in New England.

The town has a long progressive tradition and a large LGBTQ community. Colonial-era buildings survive along South Street, Elm Street, and Bridge Street, giving the centre an attractive, historic feel. The Connecticut River and surrounding hills offer good cycling and hiking, and [Amherst](/northamerica/unitedstates/massachusetts/amherst), with UMass and Amherst College, is just 15 minutes east.
