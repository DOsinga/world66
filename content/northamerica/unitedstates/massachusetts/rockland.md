The Rockland area is full of forests, wildlife, lakes, ponds,
beaches and parks for fishing, swimming, hiking, hunting, and boating.
Visitors can enjoy several public and private golf courses and
indoor facilities for tennis, racquetball, bowling, skating, and
exercising. At local municipal parks, you’ll enjoy swimming,
hiking, hunting, boating, and other sports. The Rockland area is
also home to an abundance of lakes and ponds with great fishing
spot. Soak up the sun and surf at beautiful Nantasket Beach, located
juts 8 miles from the Best Western – Rockland.

Rockland and surrounding areas offers an array of cultural and
recreational activities. Visit a historical museum, catch a Boston
Red Sox baseball game, or shop at one of the region's centers.
The community-based symphony, the Children's Museum in Easton,
and the Fuller Art Museum provide a variety of cultural activities
for people of all ages.
