**Seekonk, MA Hotel Area Destinations:**

Travel 60 miles east of our Seekonk, MA hotel and you’ll come upon a veritable paradise known as Cape Cod. It has scenic beauty, and the activities are endless. From hiking and biking to shopping and dining to golfing and fishing, Cape Cod is an ideal day-trip destination.

Take a step back in time when you visit Old Sturbridge Village. Situated 60 miles west of the Ramada Providence Conference Center, the Northeast’s largest outdoor history museum mirrors a 1830s New England town. Explore more than 40 original buildings, including a district school, bank, sawmill, and blacksmith shop.

Located 40 miles from our Seekonk, MA hotel is Plimoth Plantation in
Plymouth, MA. Experience what life was like during the 17th century. A
fascinating living museum, Plimoth Plantation boasts costumed
interpreters and exhibits such as a crafts center, a myriad of
educational sites, and a barn with rare breed animals.

Newport, Rhode Island, which is 36 miles from Seekonk, MA, is a haven
for great beaches. Easton's Beach, also known as First Beach, features
great views of the Cliff Walk, a 3.5 mile National Recreation Trail,
while Second Beach is a very popular with surfers.
