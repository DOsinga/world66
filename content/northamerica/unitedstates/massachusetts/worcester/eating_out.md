### [Cafe Dulce](/northamerica/unitedstates/massachusetts/worcester/eating_out/cafe_dulce)

Serves only coffee and expresso specialty drinks and desserts; a great place to stop after dinner for dessert and coffee. I highly recommend the carrot pineapple cake and the hot chocolate with peppermint schnapps!
