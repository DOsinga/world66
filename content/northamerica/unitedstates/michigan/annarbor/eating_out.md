Where to start? Ann Arbor is the home of dozens of fabulous restaurants. Try Wasabi for sushi, Grazie for Italian, Cafe Zola for some of the best meals you've ever had. Don't miss the Fleetwood Diner for burgers and omelets. Conor O'Neill's does some delightful appetizers to go with their imported draft beers.

**Espresso Royale Caffe, Main St**

Downtown cafe with free wifi. Good sized cavernous space, but no windows, so best for when you need to get things done.

address: Main St

**Sweetwaters Cafe, William St**

Downtown cafe with lots of light and windows. Great for people watching. No wifi. Recommended: ginger-lemon tea, hot.
