---
title: "Eating Out"
type: section
---

Ann Arbor punches well above its size when it comes to dining. The university community supports a wide range of cuisines, and the city has a genuine restaurant culture rather than just the usual chain-heavy college-town sprawl. You will find good sushi, Thai, Ethiopian, and Italian alongside local diners that have been feeding students and professors for decades. Main Street and the streets around it are the main dining corridors downtown.
