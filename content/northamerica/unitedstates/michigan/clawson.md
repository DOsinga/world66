Travel Guide

[**Clawson** is a city located in](/northamerica/unitedstates/michigan/addition) Oakland County, Michigan. As of the 2000 census, the city had a total population of 12,732..

Geography
According to the United States Census Bureau, the city has a total area of 5.7 km² (2.2 mi²). 5.7 km² (2.2 mi²) of it is land and none of it is covered by water.

Demographics

As of the census 2 of 2000, there are 12,732 people, 5,572 households, and 3,259 families residing in the city. The population density
is 2,234.5/km² (5,787.0/mi²). There are 5,676 housing units at an
average density of 996.1/km² (2,579.9/mi²). The racial makeup of the
city is 96.10% White, 0.80% African American, 0.34% Native American, 1.32% Asian, 0.00% Pacific Islander, 0.25% from other races, and 1.19% from two or more races. 1.14% of the population are Hispanic or Latino of any race.

There are 5,572 households out of which 25.1% have children under the age of 18 living with them, 46.8% are married couples
living together, 8.9% have a female householder with no husband
present, and 41.5% are non-families. 35.2% of all households are made
up of individuals and 12.5% have someone living alone who is 65 years
of age or older. The average household size is 2.26 and the average
family size is 2.98.

In the city the population is spread out with 20.6% under the age of
18, 7.5% from 18 to 24, 34.8% from 25 to 44, 21.9% from 45 to 64, and
15.2% who are 65 years of age or older. The median age is 38 years. For
every 100 females there are 94.0 males. For every 100 females age 18
and over, there are 89.5 males.

The median income for a household in the city is $50,929, and the
median income for a family is $64,684. Males have a median income of
$45,242 versus $30,679 for females. The per capita income for the city is $25,676. 3.5% of the population and 1.4% of families are below the poverty line.
Out of the total population, 1.6% of those under the age of 18 and 4.8%
of those 65 and older are living below the poverty line.

In the 1940's and 1950's, Clawson was a stronghold for white racist groups such as the Ku Klux Klan. Today, Clawson remains one of the least racially-integrated cities in Oakland County.
