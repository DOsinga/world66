### [Bishop International Airport](/northamerica/unitedstates/michigan/flint/gettingthere/bishop_internation)

Bishop is a small, yet fastly growing, airport just to the west of
Flint. Most flights that start here tend to link up with larger
airports, such as Detroit Metro or Northern Kentucky/Cincinatti.
Generally the price to fly out of flight is $20-$30 more than out of
Detroit, but that's a small price for not having to deal with that
hassle.

Major carriers are Northwest, Delta, Continental, AirTran, and other smaller carriers. Limited direct flights.
