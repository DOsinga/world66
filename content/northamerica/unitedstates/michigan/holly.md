Holly is a small Michigan town that feels like it never left the early 1900s. The downtown is packed with antique shops and local artists, and everybody seems to know everybody. You will find it right off I-75 at exit 102 -- head west into town, or east to Mt. Holly for skiing in winter.

The Holly Hotel on historic Battle Alley is the main draw: comedy club in the basement, elegant dining upstairs, and a reputation for being haunted. At Christmas the town puts on a festival with a tree lighting ceremony that suits the old-time architecture perfectly.

Late summer brings the Carry Nation Festival -- craft show, carnival for kids, and a beer tent for the grown-ups.
