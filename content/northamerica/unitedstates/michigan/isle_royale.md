**Isle Royale National Park** is a National Park comprised of Isle Royale and the surrounding waters and small islands,
a wilderness preserve in northwest Lake Superior. It's easily
identified on maps of the Great Lakes: Lake Superior resembles the
profile of a snarling wolf; Isle Royale is the eye. Although it's
closer to Ontario or even Minnesota, it's part of the state of [Michigan](/northamerica/unitedstates/michigan). Despite the French spelling, the name of the island is pronounced like the English *royal*: "ROY-al". For complete information see http://wikitravel.org/en/Isle_Royale_National_Park
