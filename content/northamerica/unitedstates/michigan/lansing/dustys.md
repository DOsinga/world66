Dusty's is a long-standing East Lansing institution on Grand River Avenue — part wine shop, part bistro, part neighborhood gathering spot. The wine list is award-winning and seriously deep, with a strong selection by the glass. Food is contemporary American, executed well. The 40-seat Bordeaux Room can be reserved for private dining. This is the kind of place that regulars feel genuinely lucky to have.
