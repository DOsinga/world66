The area’s ethnic restaurants showcase the diverse flavor of our
residents and offer dining options to satisfy any craving. Affordable
options mean dining out in Greater Lansing won’t break your bank.

Begin your culinary tour of mid-Michigan at Clara’s Lansing
Station. The restaurant is housed in a refurbished railroad station
that gives every guest a memorable meal in a one-of-a-kind atmosphere.
From fine dining to quaint cafes, Greater Lansing offers it all!
