---
title: 'Eating Out'
type: section
---

Greater Lansing's diverse population keeps the restaurant scene interesting. Clara's, housed in the restored 1903 Michigan Central Railroad Station, is a local institution. DeLuca's on West Willow Street serves award-winning pizza in a family atmosphere. For Middle Eastern food, try Sultans Express downtown. The Eastwood Towne Center and Old Town both have solid clusters of restaurants worth exploring.

Michigan State University brings international flavors too, with good Chinese, Mexican, and Indian options scattered around East Lansing and along the main corridors.
