Emil's has been feeding Lansing since 1921, making it the oldest restaurant in the city. The menu is built around homemade Italian staples — lasagna, spaghetti, veal parmesan — done without fuss. The dining room has a comfortable old-school feel, and portions are generous. A solid pick if you want something familiar and well-executed on East Michigan Avenue.
