---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

The Capital Region International Airport offers domestic flights and is about ten minutes from downtown. Lansing sits at the intersection of I-96 and I-69, making it an easy drive from Detroit (about 90 minutes), Grand Rapids (about an hour), or Chicago (about four hours). The Capital Area Transportation Authority (CATA) runs local bus service, and Greyhound connects Lansing to other Michigan cities.
