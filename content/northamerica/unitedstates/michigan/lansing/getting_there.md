---
title: 'Getting There'
type: section
---

Lansing is served by Capital Region International Airport with domestic connections. The Capital Area Transportation Authority (CATA) runs bus service throughout the greater Lansing area and the Michigan State University campus. Lansing sits at the intersection of I-69 and I-96, making it easily accessible by car from Detroit (about 90 minutes east) and Grand Rapids (about 70 minutes west).
