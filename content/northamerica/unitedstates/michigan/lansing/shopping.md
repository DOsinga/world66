Greater Lansing offers endless places to address those last minute
wardrobe needs or to spend a leisurely day window-shopping. The
Eastwood Towne Center is a modern outdoor shopping plaza complete with
stores to cater to every ones’ needs, a modern movie theatre, and
restaurants featuring diverse cuisine.

Two other enclosed malls on the
west and east sides of the region surround the area in a shopper’s
paradise.

If you are searching for the perfect antiques to take home as a
souvenir, then look no further than Williamston and Old Town. Both
communities offer a unique array of priceless treasures.

The Lansing
City Market is the perfect place to enjoy downtown Lansing in the
company of fresh flowers and friendly faces. Mingle with the locals in
search of farm fresh produce then relax with lunch at any of the fine
restaurants lining Washington Square.

### [Old Town Lansing](/northamerica/unitedstates/michigan/lansing/shopping/old_town_lansing)

Old Town encompasses Lansing's original settlement and is connected to
downtown Lansing via the River Trail along the Grand River. Old Town's
exciting business mix includes ice cream, art galleries, restaurants,
antiques and specialty stores. Burchard and Turner Park are situated in
the heart of the district and serve as the site of lively festivals and
events throughout the year. Grand River Ave. and Turner St., one mile
north of downtown Lansing.

type: Shopping centers and malls



tel: (517) 485-4283

url: [www.oldtownmainstreet.org](http://www.oldtownmainstreet.org)

address: 1232 Turner St.

### [Downtown Lansing](/northamerica/unitedstates/michigan/lansing/shopping/downtown_lansing)

Join the regulars in the heart of downtown Lansing. The diverse
restaurants, delis and nightspots define the area as much more than a
shopping district. It's a place to meet friends and colleagues while
enjoying fresh air, visiting unique shops, havinga bite to eat and
checking out new lofts and galleries. It's all within walking distance
of the State Capitol Building, three higher education facilities,
Oldsmobile Park and the River Trail. Individual store hours vary.
