---
title: 'Shopping'
type: section
---

Greater Lansing offers shopping from modern outdoor plazas to antique-filled districts. Eastwood Towne Center is the main upscale shopping destination with stores like Pottery Barn and J. Crew alongside restaurants. The Lansing Mall on West Saginaw Highway covers the mainstream retail bases.

For something with more character, Old Town on Grand River Avenue has art galleries, specialty shops, and antique stores in a walkable historic setting. The Lansing City Market, established in 1909, operates downtown with farm-fresh produce and local vendors. Williamston, east of Lansing, is another good antique-hunting ground.
