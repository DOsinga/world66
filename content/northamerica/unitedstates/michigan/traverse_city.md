Cherry capital of Michigan and possibly the world! Every July there is the cherry festival that brings out the artists and the food. TC sports a zoo on the water, a light house on the penisula and plenty of shopping downtown. A great weekend get away! 

Don't miss the wine vineyards on the penisula for wine tasting and adventure seeking. **Bring your camera**!
