Faribault (pronounced /ˈfɛərboʊ/ FAIR-boh)
is a city in Rice County, Minnesota,
United States. The population was 20,818 at the 2000 census,
and currently has a population of 22,009. It is the county
seat of Rice County. Faribault is approximately 50 miles
(80 km) south of Minneapolis and St. Paul.
