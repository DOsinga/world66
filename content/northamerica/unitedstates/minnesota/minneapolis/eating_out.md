---
title: "Eating Out"
type: section
---

One wouldn't think that good, down home, country barbeque could be found in the Twin Cities. But a Minneapolis-St. Paul restaurant that serves this is Famous Dave's. Feast on such entrees as baby back ribs, rib tips, beef brisket, Memphis-style ribs, hot links, and pulled pork. Order some cole slaw, fries, potato salad, mac and cheese, and baked beans to go along with it. Vegetarians can enjoy Famous Dave's too with such delectables as grilled salmon, catfish, or one of their huge salads. Be sure to leave room for desert--Famous Dave's makes homemade bread pudding. Check out their website at famousdaves.com
