Everyone is familiar with the Mall of America. But Minneapolis-St. Paul shopping can be done at other area malls as well. At the Rosedale Mall in Roseville, MN, you can enjoy sales and bargains at such stores as Apple, Bebe, J. Crew, Nine West, Ann Taylor, Abercrombie & Fitch, Cache, and DSW Shoes. Since Roseville is a suburb of St. Paul, getting there is very easy. Just drive east straight down Snelling Avenue and you will run right into Rosedale Mall. For more drive and directory information, check out their website at myrosedale.com.
