The word "elegance" comes to mind when you look at Rochester. Rochester has a history as a haven for the arts. Internationally recognized Eastman School of Music has made its home in Rochester since 1921. With the Rochester Philharmonic Orchestra, GeVa Theatre, Strong Museum, Eastman Theatre, Strasenburgh Planetarium, Memorial Art Gallery and the International Museum of Photography at George Eastman House, Rochester's vivid images will captivate your senses. 

Downtown Rochester offers all facilities any traveler could want. For fast foods & boutiques or fine dining & department stores, you'll find it all on or near Main St., from the Cultural District to the Four Corners area. For recreation head for Manhattan Square Park (free concerts, ice skating, playground) or one of the riverside parks.
