---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

Jackson travel has never been easier thanks to the many airline carriers offering service to and from Jackson-Evers International Airport. Airlines flying to Jackson include American, Continental, Delta, Northwest, Southwest, and US Airways. Several airlines also offer non-stop flights to selected cities. If you decide to travel by train, Amtrak has service to Jackson. The train station is located at the Station Building at 300 West Capitol Street. For hours and directions, go to their website at amtrak.com. Greyhound provides bus service to Jackson from a many cities nationwide. For schedule and fares, go to greyhound.com.
