title: "Shopping"
type: section
---

If you want to do some Jackson shopping, check out MetroCenter Mall at 3645 Highway 80 West in Jackson. Being that it is the largest shopping mall in Mississippi, you can definitely find some bargains and good sales here. Enjoy shopping at stores like Bath & Body Works, Catherine’s, Burlington Coat Factory, Enzo, Ashley Stewart, The Finish Line, Zales Jewelers, Victoria’s Secret, Champs, FootAction USA, GNC, Radio Shack, Sears, and The Sunglass Shop. If you get hungry, there is a food court with fast food places like Mandarin Cafe, American Deli, Steak Escape, and The Ice Cream Shoppe.
