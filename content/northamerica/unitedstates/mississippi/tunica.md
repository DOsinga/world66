Tunica, Mississippi sits about 30 miles south of [Memphis](/northamerica/unitedstates/tennessee/memphis), [Tennessee](/northamerica/unitedstates/tennessee). It is a large gambling center, but before 1992, when the gaming industry arrived, this was the poorest county in the United States.

Today nine large casinos are sprinkled throughout the cotton fields in Tunica, Mississippi.
