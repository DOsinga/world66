title: "Things to do"
type: section
---

The lights of Tunica put the spark in Mississippi. The 9 casino resorts the community is home to draw big name entertainment from all over the nation. Regardless which day of the week it is, there is always some must-see entertainment resounding through any one of the casinos.

Big lights and nightlife aren't the only draws of Tunica. The area is rich in history and boasts an amazing river-front park offering chartered cruises on the Mighty Mississippi, as well as an overpowering sense of the past.

Tunica's great location just 20 minutes south of Memphis, Tennessee creates the opportunity for a sight seeing day-trip into Memphis. And if you're a University of Memphis Tigers fan Tunica is the perfect place to stay since it's close enough for a pleasant drive into Memphis, but gets you out of the downtown crowds of Memphis.

No matter what you like to do, Tunica has something for everyone.
