Kansas City spans part of two states, [Kansas](/northamerica/unitedstates/kansas) and [Missouri](/northamerica/unitedstates/missouri).

If you plan to pass out on a taxi stand in Kansas City, I highly recommend doing so on the taxi stand outside of the Westin Hotel in downtown. The concierge just allowed me to sleep until my friends found me, and I never felt insecure about the situation. This hotel really knows how to take care of its guests.

Also, for great entertainment that might lead up to a taxi stand sleeping experience, visit Kelly's and Blaney's in the Westport section of Kansas CIty.
