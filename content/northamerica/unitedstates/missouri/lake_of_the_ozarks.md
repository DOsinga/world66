The Lake of the Ozarks is located in the center of the state, 45 miles
south of the capital city, Jefferson City. It is a 70 mile long
manmade lake. It is a popular power boating lake. There are
restaurants and nigtht clubs on the water. They can be accessed
by both land and sea.

It is a growing retirement community because of the mild winters and because of the water.
