### [Metrolink](/northamerica/unitedstates/missouri/stlouis/getting_around/metrolink)

[St. Louis](/northamerica/unitedstates/missouri/stlouis) has a light rail system that runs from Lambert
International Airport through the northern portion of the county then
into downtown. It then continues across the Mississippi River into
Illinois where it makes a few stops. A recently-opened branch line also runs from Forest Park (home to the St. Louis Zoo, Science Center, golf courses, History Museum, Art Museum, and other attractions) past Washington University, through upscale Clayton, past the major shopping destination of St. Louis Galleria and continues [more..](/northamerica/unitedstates/missouri/stlouis/getting_around/metrolink)
