In Montana we like steak so here are some of the best steak houses in town. Some others as well.

**all**

**Jakes Premium Steak House**

Probably the best place for steak in Billings. Reservations accepted. Simple dinners (including sandwiches and burgers) run under $10, full dinners are $15-25.

hours: M-F 11:30am-2am, Sat 4pm-2am, closed on sundays

address: 2701 first ave. No.

tel: 406-259-9375

**Cactus Creek Steak Outfitters**

A good variety of meals to please just about anyone. A casino and bar as well. A huge outdoor deck makes dining there in the summertime a real treat. The prices are very reasonable.

address: 2928 King Av. West

tel: 406-656-9090

**The Rex**

The Rex is an excellent place to eat, with a variety of aged steaks, fresh seafood, game and pastas. 

 Call for reservations, dining room opens at 5pm daily 

address: 2401 Montana Ave.

tel: 406-245-7477
