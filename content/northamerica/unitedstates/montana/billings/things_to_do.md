---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Billings punches above its weight for a mid-sized western city. The Rimrocks offer easy hikes with panoramic views, while the museums downtown cover everything from contemporary art to the history of the Yellowstone Valley. Some of the most rewarding sights are just outside the city -- prehistoric cave paintings, a zoo focused on northern-latitude wildlife, and the preserved home of a Crow chief.
