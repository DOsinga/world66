The preserved log home and trading post of Plenty Coups, the last principal chief of the Crow Nation. The small museum offers interpretive displays on Crow history and Plenty Coups' role as a diplomat and leader during a period of enormous change. Picnic sites on the grounds. Located 35 miles south of Billings and one mile west of Pryor, within the Crow Reservation. Open May through September.
