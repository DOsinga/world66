A 70-acre zoo and botanical garden focused on animals from temperate and northern latitudes. Residents include Siberian tigers, North American river otters, red pandas, sika deer, and black-footed ferrets. There is a sensory garden, a homestead area, and a children's zoo. A good stop if you are traveling with kids. Located at 2100 Shiloh Road on the west side of Billings. Open year-round.
