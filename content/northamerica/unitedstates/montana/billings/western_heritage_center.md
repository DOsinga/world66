A regional museum housed in the historic Parmly Billings Memorial Library building on Montana Avenue. Rotating exhibits cover the cultural history of the Yellowstone Valley -- ranching, railroads, Native American life, and the everyday objects of the northern plains. Exhibits change every six weeks. Open year-round.
