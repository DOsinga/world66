Bozeman is the fastest-growing city in Montana, home to Montana State University and a popular base for visiting Yellowstone National Park, about two hours' drive to the south. The town sits in the Gallatin Valley, ringed by mountain ranges, and the outdoor recreation is exceptional.

The **Museum of the Rockies** is the main cultural attraction, with an outstanding collection of dinosaur fossils. **Bridger Bowl** and nearby ski areas offer some of the best skiing in the Northern Rockies. In summer, the **Gallatin Canyon** and its river draw hikers, fly fishers, and rafters. **Main Street** downtown has a lively stretch of restaurants, breweries, and independent shops.

Bozeman is the first major city on I-90 west of Livingston and has a busy regional airport with direct flights from many US cities.
