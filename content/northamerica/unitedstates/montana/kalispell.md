**Kalispell** is a city in and the county seat of Flathead
County, Montana, United States. The population was 14,223 at the 2000 census.
Kalispell is the largest city and commercial center of Northwest Montana. The
name Kalispell is a Salish word meaning "flat land above the lake."
Kalispell is the nearest population center to Glacier National Park.
