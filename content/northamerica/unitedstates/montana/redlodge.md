Red Lodge is a beautiful small town at the base of the Beartooth Mountains. you must go through Red Lodge on your way to the northeast entrance to Yellowstone Park, but on the way you will go over Beartooth Pass which is probably the most scenic drive in the lower 48 states. 


 Red Lodge also has a great ski area and many other outdoor activities.
