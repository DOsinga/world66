title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

Red Lodge is located just 43 miles off Intersate 90, just south of Laurel.Hwy 212. You can also take the exit off the intersate at Columbus Hwy78, this will take you through some nice small towns and is very scenic. 

 You can also get to Red Lodge from the east going through Belfry MT and over Bearcreek Pass.
