---
title: "Virginia City"
type: location
---

Virginia City, Montana is frozen in time. It is a remarkably well preserved old west Victorian mining town. When the gold ran out, there was still enough left so that homes and businesses were occupied, but there was not enough wealth to remodel. So it froze, and now represents the whole Victorian era. It is a true and original Old West. It is a gem, held within an incredibly rich area of natural beauty, recreation and history.
