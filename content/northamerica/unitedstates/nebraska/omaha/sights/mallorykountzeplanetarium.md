The Mallory Kountze Planetarium is located in the Durham Science Center on the University of Nebraska at Omaha campus. It offers various planetarium shows and when weather permits, a rooftop observatory. Schedule of public shows and a listing of offered show titles is available on the website: ** www.physics.unomaha.edu/planet**
