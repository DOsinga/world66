One of the best zoos in the country. The Desert Dome is the world's largest geodesic dome and houses a full arid environment; below it sits Kingdoms of the Night, a walk-through nocturnal exhibit. The Lied Jungle is a multi-storey indoor rainforest, and the Scott Aquarium has a walk-through acrylic tunnel. Budget at least half a day.
