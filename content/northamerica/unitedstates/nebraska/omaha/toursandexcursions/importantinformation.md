Let the gentle current and soft breezes lull you into a separate time and place, as you cruise the Missouri River in the newly renovated River City Star. The Star is fully-equiped and staffed for fun, a cruise on the Star makes every gathering a truly unique and memorable event.

The River City Star is berthed at Miller's Landing, less than once mile north of downtown Omaha on Abbott Drive.
