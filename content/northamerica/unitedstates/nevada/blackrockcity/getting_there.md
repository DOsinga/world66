---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

The nearest major airport is **Reno-Tahoe International** (RNO), about three hours' drive southwest. From Reno, take I-80 east about 50 km to exit 43 (Wadsworth/Pyramid Lake) and follow Nevada Highway 447 north — past Pyramid Lake, Nixon, Empire and finally **Gerlach** — to the Burning Man gate off Highway 34. Expect heavy traffic and long queues at the entrance on opening day, and similarly brutal queues out on the Sunday and Monday after the burn ("Exodus"). Arrive with a full tank of gas, plenty of water, and patience.
