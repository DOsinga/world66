---
title: "Black Rock"
type: poi
---

Get set for beauty beyond your wildest expectations. The hills are alive with layers of color that change with the time of day. The untouched beauty of this desert landscape will stay with you long after you leave. the Black Rock Desert first became known for its location on the Lassen-Applegate Trail, one of the main wagon routes used by settlers heading West. The desert continues to be the site of history-making feats. In fact, in 1983, Richard Noble se the world land speed record of more than 633 miles per hour in his rocket-like car.
