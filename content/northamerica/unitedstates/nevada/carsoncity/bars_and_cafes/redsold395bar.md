Eclectic decor that is a must-see when visiting Carson City. The menu is interesting and varied — the Rocky Mountain Oysters are a local dare for first-timers. Over 50 beers on tap and as many again in bottles. Karaoke nights, shuffleboard, and patio parties in the summer.
