Red's Old 395 is a Carson City institution with wall-to-wall eclectic decor that takes a few visits to fully absorb. The menu goes well beyond bar food -- the Rocky Mountain Oysters are a local dare for first-timers, and the burgers are solid.

Over 50 beers on tap and as many again in bottles make this the best beer selection in town. Karaoke nights, shuffleboard, and patio parties in the summer keep it lively. It is the kind of place where regulars know the bartender and visitors feel welcome anyway.
