title: "Carson Valley (Minden/Gardnerville/Genoa)"
type: location
---

Minden and Gardnerville are just south of [Carson City](/northamerica/unitedstates/nevada/carsoncity), before you cross back into California. Just to the east are the towering peaks of the Sierra Nevada, but these towns lie in a fertile valley. The best thing about the region is that it offers both entertainment & history without the crowds of Vegas or Reno.

A focal point for the area is the Carson Valley Inn. This is a large casino hotel with some very upscale restaurants. Be sure to eat at JT's in Gardnerville, a traditional Basque restaurant where everything is served family style...and very tasty! Also be sure to have a beer at the Genoa Tavern, the oldest continually operated drinking establishment in Nevada.
