Incline Village harbors a variety of eating establishments that range from the casual to the elegant. Don't look for the typical chain fast food joints here. If fine dining is your desire, choices abound.

### [T's Rotisserie](/northamerica/unitedstates/nevada/incline_village/eating_out/ts_rotisserie)

T's barbecues tri-tip and chicken daily. Their burritos are incredible. I used to work nearby and made T's a lunchtime stop two or three times a week. What it lacks in ambiance, it make up in flavor. The locals definitely know about this place!
