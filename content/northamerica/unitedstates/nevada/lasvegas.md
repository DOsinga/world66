Viva Las Vegas! In the middle of the desert Las Vegas is like a mirage, like a fata morgana. The colours of the casinos' and their neon lights, contrast sharply with the dry and barren land around. 



What brings you to Vegas is clear: it's all about having fun, more precisely having fun and gambling. 

And that is precisely what Vegas offers: mindboggling out of this world, oversized Casino's in every shape and size: Eiffel towers, Roman palaces, Egyptian Pyramids. You don't need to go to Europe or Africa to see this. Vegas offers all of that and more.
