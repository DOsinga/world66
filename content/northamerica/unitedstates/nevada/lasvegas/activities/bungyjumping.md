**Points of Interest**

**A J Hackett-Bungy**

Jump from a platform 171 feet high (17 stories) over water. Must be at least 13 years old and weigh at least 90 pounds. Those under 18 must be accompanied by a parent or legal guardian. 

address: 810 Circus Circus Drive
