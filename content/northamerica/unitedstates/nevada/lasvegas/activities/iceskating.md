**Activitys**

**The Santa Fe Ice Arena **

Stadium-size arena for public skating; private and group lessons; and hockey games and practice. Senior skating noon-2 p.m. Wednesdays. 

address: the Santa Fe, 4949 N. Rancho Drive

**Sahara Ice Palace**

39,000-square-foot arena offers public skate sessions, classes, clinics and skating programs,  

address: 800 Karen Ave.

**Las Vegas Ice Gardens**

Membership skating daily, plus lessons for figure skating and hockey, and youth and adult hockey leagues. 

address: 3896 Swenson St.
