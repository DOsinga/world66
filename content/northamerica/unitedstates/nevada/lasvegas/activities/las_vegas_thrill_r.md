If you have the stomach for a ride that will shoot you 160 feet straight up into the air in 2.5 seconds (at 45 mph), then the Big Shot ($13) ride at the Stratosphere is the thrill ride for you. At 1,081 feet above the Strip, you just won’t get a better view than this if you can bear to look down. Other Stratosphere thrill rides include: X-Scream ($12) and Insanity ($12). The Stratosphere also has the tallest observation tower in the United States – it reaches 1,149 feet into the Las Vegas skyline.
