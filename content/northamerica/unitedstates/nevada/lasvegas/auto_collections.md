The Auto Collections is one of the largest classic car showrooms in the world, housed inside The LINQ Hotel (formerly the Imperial Palace). The collection rotates but typically features around 250 vehicles spanning a century of automotive history — everything from pre-war Duesenbergs to muscle cars and celebrity-owned rides.

Many of the cars are for sale, which gives the place a slightly different feel from a traditional museum. Prices range from attainable to seven figures. Highlights have included vehicles owned by Johnny Cash, Elvis, and various U.S. presidents. The fifth-floor location in a parking structure is easy to miss, but the collection rewards a visit for car enthusiasts.
