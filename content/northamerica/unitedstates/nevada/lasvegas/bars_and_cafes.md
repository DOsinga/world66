---
title: 'Bars and Cafes'
type: section
---

Las Vegas nightlife ranges from rowdy party bars to sophisticated cocktail lounges. Coyote Ugly at New York-New York lives up to the movie hype -- shots, beer, and dancing on the bar. For something quieter, the Promenade Cafe at the Rampart Casino in the Marriott Summerlin offers elegant dining at reasonable prices, well away from the Strip chaos. Most casino hotels have their own bars and lounges worth exploring, from the sleek Tryst at the Wynn to the hipster-friendly Double Down Saloon east of the Strip.
