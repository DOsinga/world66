The Roman-themed mega-resort has been a Strip landmark since 1966, and it still impresses. The Forum Shops atrium has a painted sky that shifts from dawn to dusk, the Garden of the Gods pool complex is absurdly lavish, and the casino floor sprawls on and on past marble columns and toga-clad statues. The Colosseum concert hall draws top-tier residencies. Even if you don't gamble, walk through for the sheer spectacle of the place -- it's peak Vegas.
