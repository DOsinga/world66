There are so many Casino's in Vegas that it is not worthwhile to try to mention all of them here. Here is just a short list of some more interesting ones. But the best is to drive up and down the Strip and look for yourself. You will not believe your eyes! It's [Paris](/europe/france/paris), [New York](/northamerica/unitedstates/newyorkstate/newyork) ancient [Rome](/europe/italy/lazio/rome) and [Egypt](/africa/egypt) at the same time.
 

**Points of Interest**

**Bally's**

Offres a touch of class and goes address: 3799 Las Vegas Blvd

**New York-New York**

As good as going to New York!
 

address: 3790 Las Vegas Blvd

**Paris Las Vegas**

Why fly to Europe. The Eiffel tower is right here.
 

address: 3655 Las Vegas Boulevard

**Bourbon Street**

Louisiana style Casino. Great Cajun food!
 

address: 120 East Flamingo Road

**Caesars Palace**

Big Casino in Roman style.
 

**Flamingo**

Flamingo, built by Bugsy, was the start of it all. 
 

address: 3555 Las Vegas Blvd. S.

**Golden Nugget**

One of the nicest of Casino's
 

address: 129 Fremont St.

**Hard Rock Hotel**

With the big guitar on the outside, you can't miss it. Has many memorabilia of Rock stars
 

address: 4455 Paradise Rd

**Imperial Palace**

Oriental style casino. Great food as well.
 

address: 3535 Las Vegas

**Treasure Island **

Carribean style Casino with Long John Silver as the surprise guest.
 

address: 3300 Las Vegas Blvd
