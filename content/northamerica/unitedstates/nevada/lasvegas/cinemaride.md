A 3D motion simulator ride located on the Las Vegas Strip near the Forum Shops at Caesars Palace. These simulator attractions were once a staple of Vegas entertainment, offering short, jolting rides synced to on-screen footage. Note that this attraction may no longer be operating — Las Vegas turns over its entertainment offerings frequently. Check before making a special trip.
