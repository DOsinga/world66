More than 10 million visitors travel to Hoover Dam each year to explore one of the world's greatest engineering and architectural marvels. And the great thing about this day trip is even if you only have three days to spend in Las Vegas, Hoover Dam is so close (30 miles southeast from Las Vegas), that you can visit anytime of the day. You can rent a car and go it alone to Hoover Dam or you can opt for a Deluxe Grayline Tour (www.grayline.com). Deluxe Grayline Tours are roughly 7-1/2 hours long and they include buffet lunch and a side trip to Ethel M Chocolate Factory. Admission to Hoover Dam's Discovery Tour is $11. Grayline Tours depart daily at 7:30 a.m. Cost: $49.

Also located in the Lake Mead region: Clark County Museum, Death Valley, and Floyd Lamb State Park.
