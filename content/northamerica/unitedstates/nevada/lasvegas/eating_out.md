---
title: 'Eating Out'
type: section
---

Las Vegas has evolved from cheap buffets to a genuine culinary destination. Celebrity chefs line the Strip -- you will find outposts from Wolfgang Puck, Emeril Lagasse, and many others. For something different, head off-Strip: Soul 2 Soul on North Rainbow serves excellent Southern-Cajun-Caribbean fusion, and Mr. J's Fish Market near Rainbow and I-95 specializes in Louisiana fried fish. The Wildflower Cafe at Meadows Mall is a full-service restaurant offering breakfast, lunch, and dinner in a calmer setting.
