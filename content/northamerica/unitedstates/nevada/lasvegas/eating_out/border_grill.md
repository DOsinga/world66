Located in Mandalay Resort & Casino, Border Grill is considered the best Mexican food in Las Vegas. Run by Mary Sue Milliken and Susan Feniger of the Food Network's "Two Hot Tamales," Border Grill serves up authentic Mexican cuisine such as the carne asada quesadillas, plantain empanadas, and hearty beef tacos in a festive environment. The cuisine here is expensive, but it's well worth it!
