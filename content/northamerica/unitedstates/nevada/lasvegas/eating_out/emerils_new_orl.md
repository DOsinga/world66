The 225-seat Emeril’s New Orleans Fish House needs no introduction. The menu features Emeril Lagasse’s signature New Orleans flavor in dishes such as Emeril’s New Orleans barbeque shrimp with petite rosemary biscuit and the fish house seafood gumbo. Try the apple cobbler for dessert. Emeril’s New Orleans Fish House is located in the MGM Grand Hotel at 3799 Las Vegas Boulevard South. Lunch hours are Monday-Sunday, 11:30 a.m.-2:30 p.m.; dinner hours are Monday-Sunday, 5:30 p.m.-10:30 p.m.
