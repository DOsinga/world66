Located in the Bellagio Hotel, Sensi serves up eclectic fare including Italian, Asian-inspired, American, and seafood dishes. The environment is rather relaxed with a dining room that features an open kitchen in the center of the room, waterfalls, carved stone, and mirrored chrome. Sensi also features an impressive wine list with selections from New Zealand, Australia, and other wine regions of the world. Sensi is located within the Spa Tower past Essentials. Hours of Operation: Daily Lunch: 11:00 a.m. - 2:30 p.m. Dinner: 5:00 p.m. - 10:30 p.m.
