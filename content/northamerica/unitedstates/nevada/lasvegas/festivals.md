**Popular Las Vegas Festivals**

**Las Vegas Festivals** embody the vibrancy and spirited nature which have given Las Vegas the reputation of being one of the most happening cities across the world. Las Vegas is famous for a vast range of year-around events.

#### **Las Vegas Jazz Festival**

Also called the City of Lights Festival, this is an adored music event. The main emphasis is on jazz. Jazz musicians from across the U.S. converge upon Las Vegas. This festival is usually held during the last week of April and is typically held in Hills Park in Summerlin. The festival has been a popular musical event for nearly two decades, and the number of participants has been soaring with every passing season. There is usually another similarly-themed festival, such as the R&B Festival, held as a part of the Jazz Festival.

#### **World Archery Festival**

This is among the few international-level sports festivals hosted in Las Vegas. Competitions held as a part of the festival begin in Las Vegas and end in Atlantic City. Apart from the international teams participating here, the main attraction is the large pavilions. These are essentially trade-shows wherein all kinds of sports equipment, including clothing and accessories, are displayed. Most of the shopping pavilions are arranged around the entrance of the Riviera Hotel and Casino. This festival is held in early February.

#### **Chinese New Year Festival**

This version of the New Year has become an annual event in Las Vegas. The celebrations begin during the first week of February. Most of the festivities, including the firework shows, are organized by the Las Vegas Cultural Center. Events include a fortune teller, dance performances and food.
