---
title: 'Festivals'
type: section
---

Las Vegas hosts festivals year-round. The Las Vegas Jazz Festival (also called the City of Lights Festival) draws jazz musicians from across the country to Hills Park in Summerlin during the last week of April. The World Archery Festival in early February is an international-level competition with large trade-show pavilions around the Riviera Hotel. Chinese New Year celebrations kick off in the first week of February with fireworks, dance performances, and food organized by the Las Vegas Cultural Center.
