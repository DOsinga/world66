Freemont street is where las vegas and all its glory originated from.It lies about a mile away from the strip and a safer option for visiting it would be to take a cab there.There is a magnificent lit up ceiling that puts on a laser show every hour,all in different themes,such as Elvis,the sixties,boogie music etc

Alternatively,if you wish to stay on freemont street, your best bet would be to stay in the **Golden Nugget** hotel,this is the only hotel on freemont street that has pool amenities and not only that but it opens up right onto freemont street itself.
