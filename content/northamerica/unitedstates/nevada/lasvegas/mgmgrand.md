The world's largest hotel with 5,005 rooms, 14 restaurants, an extensive pool complex and a lion enclosure. The outdoor pool complex is one of the best in Vegas -- hard to believe you are on one of the busiest streets in the world when you are lounging there. The movie-themed casino is less flashy than some Strip neighbors, but offers reasonably priced rooms and a central location.
