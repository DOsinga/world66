One of the largest hotels in the world, with around 5,000 rooms, 14 restaurants, and an outdoor pool complex that genuinely feels removed from the Strip — a rare thing on Las Vegas Boulevard. The movie-themed casino is less flashy than some neighbors but the rooms are reasonably priced and the central location is hard to beat. The lion habitat that once occupied the property closed in 2012.
