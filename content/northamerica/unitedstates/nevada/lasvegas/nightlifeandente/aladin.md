One of the newer casino hotels on the strip, the Alladin is located across the street from Paris. The decor is, as the name implies, themed in a Knights of Arabia or Disney's Alladin vein (sans Robin Williams). The gambling here is some of the best around. Reasonably priced table games, the nicest dealers you'll meet, and quick (free) beverage service. They even have single deck blackjack tables with $10 (and sometimes as low as $5) minimums. (Single deck blackjack offers the best odds for the players; more decks = bigger house advantage)
