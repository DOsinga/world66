Extremely elegant Casino and Hotel in European style. Shopping is taken
to new heights in Las Vegas with shops such as Tiffany's and Gucci
enticing passerby's. Keep your eyes open as this tends to be where the
rich and famous enjoy visiting!

The hotel fountains span more than 1,000 feet and soar up to 240 feet in the
air. The shows are choreographed to music ranging from Luciano
Pavarotti to Frank Sinatra.
