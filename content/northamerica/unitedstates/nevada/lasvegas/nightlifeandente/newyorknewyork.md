As good as going to New York! With the added realism of a group of terrible unfriendly and pretentious staff.

The Nine Fine Irishmen "genuine Irish pub" was so stuffy my travelmate was 'shusssshed' by the bartender for having the audacity to laugh out loud!

People, this is Las Vegas, at night... in a bar.... need I say more?

Save your money and stay at the MGM Grand where people actually look like they are having a good time.
