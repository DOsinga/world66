Studio 54 is the quintissential Las Vegas nightclub. Podium dancers, circus performers and strippers all create an electic atmosphere of glamour and debauchary, condusive with its home in the ciry of sin. Located in MGM Grand, the largest hotel in the world, Studio 54 attracts visitors from all along the strip to experience its state of the art lights and video.
