The V Theater, located in the Miracle Mile Shops at the Planet Hollywood Resort and Casino is currently home to 8 Las Vegas shows.
**
V the Ultimate Variety Show** has something for everyone. Comedy, magic, aerial acrobatics, dancing
and some of the wildest modern-day Vaudeville acts you will ever see. V
truly is the Ultimate Variety Show. It is a high energy show perfect for all ages. Shows nightly, 7:00PM & 8:30PM.

**Fab Four Live** is a Beatles tribute show featuring Paul, John, George and Ringo look-alikes performing the Beatles' greatest hits such as "I Want to Hold
Your Hand", "Hard Days Night", "Twist and Shout," "Sergeant Peppers" and "Hey
Jude." Fab Four Live recreates the music and excitement of a real
Beatles concert as these talented musicians perform many of their
greatest hits *live* on stage. Shows 5:30PM Friday – Wednesday, dark Thursday.

**Mesmerized** is an energetic mix of comedy, hypnosis and chaos. You can
see the show or BE the show! Come to watch hilarious audience
volunteers live onstage as Marc
Savard whips the crowd into a frenzy. Mesmerized is a wild and funny hypnosis show that gives you a chance to be a part of the show. Shows 10:00PM Saturday-Thursday, dark Friday. Ages 18+ only.

**Gregory Popovich's Comedy Pet Theater** is a blend of unique comedy,
world-championship juggling, and the extraordinary talents of
performing pets. As seen on the Tonight Show, Letterman, and America’s Got Talent, his amazing cast of dogs and cats have been rescued
from animal shelters and transformed into Vegas stars! Comedy Pet
Theater is a
perfect family show and lots of fun for people who love animals. Shows 4:00PM Friday – Tuesday, 5:30PM Thursday, dark Wednesday.

**American Storm** is an all male revue, with many of
the cast members being winners of VH1 TV’s Strip Search reality show. Men of the show have been featured on the cover of Playgirl and Muscle & Fitness
magazines. The men dance in themed performances and even let the ladies in on a little bit of the action. Shows are 10:00 PM Fridays and 11:30 PM Saturdays. Ages 18+ only.

**Stripper 101** is an interactive class is designed to help women of all ages, shapes
and sizes feel great about themselves by releasing their sensual side.
Students are encouraged to lose their inhibitions while having fun
learning sexy strip club moves with boas, chairs and of course, poles!
(No nudity.) Graduates earn an official souvenir Las Vegas Stripper
License. Multiple classes daily. Ladies 18+ only. Great fun for Bachelorette parties.

**The Mentalist** features Gerry McCambridge, star of television’s hit show The Mentalist, and reality show
Phenomenon. Gerry performs comedy, magic and mindreading
live on stage for astounded audiences and he thrills with incredible
mentalism skills. His ability to get inside the minds of others has
garnered worldwide attention. You won’t believe it until you see him
LIVE. Shows nightly except Wednesday, 7:30PM. Ages 13 and up.

**S****in City Comedy Show** features beautiful girls and famous comedians taking the stage in this hilarious
show that combines sexy burlesque with world-class stand up comedy. The stunning ladies of the Sin City
Dollz perform seductive dance numbers and two headliner comics keep
audiences rolling in the aisles with laughter. Shows nightly 9:00 PM. Ages 18+. Upcoming acts include:

May 18-24 - Greg Vaccarello with Gary Caouette

May 25-31 - Bruce Mickelson with Rob Sherwood
