The Planetarium at the College of Southern Nevada's Cheyenne campus runs public shows covering astronomy and the night sky. It's an understated counterpoint to the Strip — small, affordable, and genuinely educational. Shows run on weekends; check the schedule before going as programming changes. The Mojave Desert location means the real reward is stepping outside afterward: dark skies are within easy driving distance of Las Vegas, and the planetarium's shows give useful context for what you'll see.
