If you’re hungry for some simple but hearty American fare for only a few bucks, check out Rainbow Bar & Grill. The menu features burgers, pizza, salads, subs, fries, fish and chips, pasta, steak, and more. Rainbow Bar & Grill is open all night and they serve hearty breakfasts all day -- try the fried chicken and waffles. Rainbow Bar & Grill, in all its rock and roll themed, glory will be moving to a new location in Las Vegas. The Grand Opening is scheduled for Fall 2008.
