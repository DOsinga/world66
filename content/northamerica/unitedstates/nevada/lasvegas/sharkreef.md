The main tank holds more than 1 million gallons of water and features a 20-foot-long acrylic tunnel for surround viewing. This predator-focused attraction features 2,000 animals from 100 different species, including 13 shark species, plus jellyfish, manta rays, sea turtles, and crocodiles. There is also a touch tank for children.
