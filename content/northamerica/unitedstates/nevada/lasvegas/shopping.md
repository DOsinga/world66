---
title: 'Shopping'
type: section
---

Shopping in Las Vegas runs from high-end designer boutiques to vintage treasure hunts. The Fashion Show mall on the Strip has 250+ stores including Nordstrom, Neiman Marcus, and Saks Fifth Avenue. For outlet bargains, the Las Vegas Premium Outlets offers Coach, Dolce & Gabbana, and Ralph Lauren at serious discounts -- worth braving the heat. Town Square Las Vegas is a sprawling open-air center south of the Strip with an eclectic mix of retailers. For something unique, The Attic on South Main Street is one of the largest vintage clothing stores in the country.
