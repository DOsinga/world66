Las Vegas has a good number of antique stores to choose from, each with its own identity and treasures. One such antique venue is Antiques at the Market -- a collection of little antique stalls under one roof. One of Las Vegas' newer antique venues, Antiques at the Market is an excellent place to pick up unique gifts to take back home to family and friends. Open 10:00 a.m.-6:00 p.m. Monday-Saturday and Sunday from 12:00 p.m.-5:00 p.m.
