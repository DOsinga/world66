The Cloud Nine Balloon is the World’s Largest Land Tethered Helium Balloon ride. Its custom-built circular gondola holds 25-30 passengers allowing guests to safely soar 500 feet above the City of Las Vegas. The balloon is environmentally friendly and virtually silent. Day and night flights are available. Cloud Nine is open from 10 am - 10pm Sunday through Thursday. 10 am - Midnight on Friday and Saturday.
