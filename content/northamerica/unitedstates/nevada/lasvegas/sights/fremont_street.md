Fremont Street is where Las Vegas and all its glory originated from.It
lies about a mile away from the strip and a safer option for visiting
it would be to take a cab there.There is the Fremont Street Experience,
a magnificent lit up ceiling that puts on a laser show every hour, all
in different themes, such as Elvis, the sixties, boogie music etc.

The Experience was recently modernized, so the shows are now
higher resolution, and television shows can be displayed on
the ceiling. Alternatively,if
you wish to stay on Fremont Street, your best bet would be to stay in
the Golden Nugget hotel or in the Plaza hotel, the only hotel on
Fremont Street that has pool amenities.
