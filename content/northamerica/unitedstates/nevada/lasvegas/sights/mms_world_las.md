This is an excellent attraction for people of all ages (note the late night hours) -- but it is definitely a favorite for kids. The 28,000 square-foot, four-story retail-entertainment venue features M&M dispensers that can only be found in Las Vegas, a variety of M&M colors you have never seen before, and a free 3-D movie. Other features include novelties, one-of-a-kind M&M’s World gifts and souvenirs, creative displays such as colorful cars, and giant M&M's and of course, you can purchase some M&M's for the road. M&M World is located in the Showcase Mall at 3785 Las Vegas Blvd South. Cost: Free!
