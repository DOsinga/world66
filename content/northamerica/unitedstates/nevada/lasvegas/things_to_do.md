---
title: 'Things to Do'
type: section
---

Las Vegas is far more than slot machines and poker tables. The Strip itself is a spectacle -- a mile-long parade of themed mega-resorts, free fountain shows, erupting volcanoes, and neon that never sleeps. Beyond the casinos, you will find world-class shows, thrill rides atop the Stratosphere, and quirky attractions like M&M's World. Head off-Strip for desert hikes, helicopter tours of the Grand Canyon, and hot air balloon rides at sunrise.
