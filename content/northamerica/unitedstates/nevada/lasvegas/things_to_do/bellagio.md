Extremely elegant casino and hotel in European style, with shops such as Tiffany's and Gucci. The hotel fountains span more than 1,000 feet and soar up to 240 feet in the air, choreographed to music ranging from Luciano Pavarotti to Frank Sinatra. The fountain show alone is worth the visit.
