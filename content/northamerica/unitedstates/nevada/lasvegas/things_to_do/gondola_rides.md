A romantic ride down the Grand Canal past cafes, under bridges and balconies, complete with a singing gondolier. Choose between indoor or outdoor rides. Indoor rides cost $15 per person (gondola seats 4) and $60 for a private two-passenger gondola. Hours are Sunday-Thursday 10 a.m.-11 p.m. and Friday-Saturday 10 a.m.-midnight.
