A 28,000 square-foot, four-story retail-entertainment venue in the Showcase Mall. Features M&M dispensers you can only find in Las Vegas, a huge variety of colors, a free 3-D movie, creative displays with colorful cars and giant M&M statues, and of course plenty of chocolate to purchase. A hit with kids and adults alike. Free admission.
