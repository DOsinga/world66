### [Holi Festival Reno 2006

Area students and community will jointly celebrate the lively Holi festival at University of Nevada-Reno (UNR) campus on Saturday, April one.

Jointly organized by India Association of Northern Nevada (IANN) and International Club of UNR, it will be held at Manzanita Bowl on the Campus. In case of rain/snow, it will be moved to Student Services Building of UNR, according to Navgeet Zed, President of International Club. It will include spraying colors, games, East Indian music and East Indian snacks, and will be held from 11 am to one pm.
