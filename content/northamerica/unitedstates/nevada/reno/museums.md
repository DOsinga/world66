---
title: 'Museums'
type: section
---

The National Automobile Museum in downtown Reno has the largest collection of antique automobiles in the world. Featured exhibits include the New York to Paris Auto Race of 1907 and a Celebrity Autos section -- do not leave without seeing the Cadillac once owned by Elvis Presley. Audio tours are available with paid admission. Visit automuseum.org for hours and rates.
