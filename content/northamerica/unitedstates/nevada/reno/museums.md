---
title: "Museums"
type: section
---

There are more Reno activities than just gambling. If you like classic cars, then be sure to check out the National Automobile Museum in downtown Reno. This museum has the largest collection of antique automobiles in the world. There are featured exhibits like the New York to Paris Auto Race of 1907. Another popular exhibit is Celebrity Autos. There is an arts & collectibles collection as well. Don't leave without taking a look at the Cadillac that was once owned by Elvis Presley. Audio tours are available with paid admission. For admission rates and hours, visit their website at automuseum.org.
