Travel Guide

Manchester is New Hampshire's largest city. It is nicknamed by locals
"ManchVegas." Home of the school that Adam Sandler attended, Central
High School is fantastic!

There are also a collection of malls, called the Malls of New Hampshire in the Southern part of the city.
