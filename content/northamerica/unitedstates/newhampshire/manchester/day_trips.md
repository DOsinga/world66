**Relieve the Everyday Stress at ****Bear**** ****Brook**** ****State Park****
**

A **Manchester, New Hampshire Day Trip** to the Bear Brook State
Park gives you an excellent opportunity to relieve yourself from the
accumulated everyday stress. This 40-square-kilometers preserve is
located near Allenstown, about 20 km north of Manchester. You have to
exit the city through Hooksett Road and then turn right on Pinewood
Road, just before entering Suncook. Admission fees for the park are $4
for adults and $2 for children. Children below five years and seniors
over 65 years are admitted free.

**Camping
**

The Bear Brook State Park is a popular camping destination. Its
campground has 96 campsites and is located right on the shore of Beaver
Pond. Campsites cost $23 nightly. Beaver Pond offers numerous water
activities, among which are fishing, swimming, canoeing and sunbathing.
The campground has its dedicated laundry and bathroom facilities. In
addition, there is also a camping store, where you can get any needed
supplies. There is a picnic area nearby as well.

**Other Activities**

In addition to camping, Bear Brook State Park offers many other
leisure activities. Numerous hiking trails are available and you can
always visit one of the museums located within the park. Those are the
Camping Museum, the Snowmobile Museum and the Civilian Conservation
Corps Museum. You can also take an archery course at the park’s archery
range and if you want to learn more about the surrounding nature, you
can visit the Nature Center.
