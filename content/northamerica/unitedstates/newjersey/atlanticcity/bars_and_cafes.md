---
title: "Bars and Cafes"
type: section
---

Most of Atlantic City's nightlife is inside the casinos, which between them have dozens of bars, lounges, clubs and showrooms. **Borgata**, at the marina end of town, has the best concentration of upmarket bars and the **Music Box** showroom; **Hard Rock** has the HRAC Live arena for concerts; **Caesars** and **Harrah's** run clubs along the boardwalk strip. Outside the casinos, the historic **Irish Pub** on St James Place has been pouring pints of Guinness to locals since the 1890s and is the best place in the city for a quiet drink in an unreconstructed setting. **Tun Tavern**, near the Convention Center, is a microbrewery named for the 1685 Philadelphia tavern where the US Marine Corps was founded; the beer is decent and the atmosphere low-key.
