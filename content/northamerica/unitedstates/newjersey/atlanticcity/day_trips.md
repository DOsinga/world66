Atlantic City is well placed for day trips up and down the mid-Atlantic coast. **[Philadelphia](/northamerica/unitedstates/pennsylvania/philadelphia)**, an hour's drive west, has Independence Hall, the Liberty Bell, the Barnes Foundation and one of the best art museums in the country. **[Cape May](/northamerica/unitedstates/newjersey/cape_may)**, 45 minutes south, is a famously well-preserved Victorian seaside town and a world-class bird-watching destination during the autumn migration. **Ocean City**, halfway between them, is the family-friendly alcohol-free alternative. **New York City** is doable as a long day out — around 2.5 hours by car, or 2 hours 15 on the NJ Transit Atlantic City Express — and **Washington, D.C.** is about 3.5 hours south by road.
