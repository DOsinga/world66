title: "Getting Around in Atlantic City"
type: section
---

**TAXICABS**

Atlantic City has an ample amount of taxicabs. The rate for in-town transportation is quite reasonable, between $6-$11. When traveling out of town, the rates rise dramatically, reserving a car or sedan service would be a lower cost option.

**JITNEYS**

Jitneys are Atlantic City's answer to mass transit around town. They are light blue colored mini buses that travel clockwise & counterclockwise around the city. There are stops all over the city, in front of all the casinos and every few blocks in between. Jitneys run very frequently, and it is rare to wait more than ten minutes for one. The rate is $2 per trip.

**ROLLING CHAIRS**

An Atlantic City novelty. Sit back and relax on a two seat (padded bench) mobile chair, while being pushed up and down Atlantic City's historic boardwalk. Rates vary from $10 to $30.
