Travel Guide

Cape May is a quiet city on the southern tip of New Jersey. It is home to a lovely sandy beach, many upscale bed and breakfast inns and lovely Victorian type buildings. The easiest way to reach it from the south is taking the ferry across Delaware Bay to Cape May. A stroll through the city streets looking at the well-kept buildings reminds you of a by-gone era.

If the prices at the B&B's are too high in the summer tourist season, try visiting Cape May in the off-season when prices are lower.
