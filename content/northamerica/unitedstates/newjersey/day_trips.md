**Hanover Marriott**
http://www.marriott.com/hotels/travel/ewrho-hanover-marriott/

Located just 20 minutes from Newark Liberty International Airport, the Hanover Marriott is a beautiful hotel in Hanover, NJ. With a location convenient to many Fortune 500 companies and just 40 minutes from New York City, this Whippany hotel is perfect for business meetings or weekend getaways in NJ. The Hanover Marriott features 353 newly renovated rooms, an elegant and comfortable lobby, state-of-the-art fitness facilities and functional meeting rooms. Additionally, the hotel is home to the Auld Shebeen Irish Pub, an authentic Irish pub built in Ireland from native materials and reassembled at the Hanover Marriott.

The unique setting of the hotel, minutes away from bustling cities yet surrounded by beautiful and serene countryside, make the Hanover Marriott a perfect northern New Jersey wedding location. The 10,750 square foot Grand Ballroom, seasoned event management team and award winning chef make sure that any wedding reception at this Morris County hotel is a memorable one.
