**Manhattan**

A day-trip to Manhattan is quite the adventure. It is over an hour away with traffic averaged into the time. You can travel into the city by car, but the traffic can get very heavy and aggravating. Another means of transportation into Manhattan is via train. This is the best of the two options because you will not have to find a parking spot for the car or worry about navigating through the busy New York streets. If you have not yet been to Manhattan, then I suggest just exploring the city once you arrive. Grab a map at the train station so you don't get too lost. There are so many unique places to discover that you'll probably miss your train
