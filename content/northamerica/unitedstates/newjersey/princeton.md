title: "Princeton"
type: location
---

Princeton is one of the oldest settlements in the nation, dating back to the 1600s.

Princeton is home to the prestigious Princeton University as well as to Princeton Theological Seminary, a world renound (Presbyterian) Seminary.

Driving through Princeton, one sees many wonderful shops, old churches (such as the historic Trinity Episcopal Church), and beautiful mansions dating back over 200 years.

Princeton Cemetery is home to at least one president as well as many other famous founders of America.
