Sunspot National Solar Observatory

Check out another of Alamogordo attractions that requires a good half- to full day to fully experience. The Sunspot National Solar Observatory is located on Sacramento Peak in Sunspot, New Mexico. The site is about 32 miles up the New Mexico scenic byway of 6563. The observatory is situated in the Lincoln National Forest in the Sacramento Mountains. Free, self-guided tours are available, with maps from the Visitor Center. Regular guided tours are offered in the summer months of June, July and August, and a nominal fee is charged to recoup expenses. Plan your visit ahead of time by taking a virtual tour on the Sunspot website.

575-434-7190
Open 10:00 a.m. to 6:00 p.m. April through December
