The Sacramento Mountains behind Alamogordo offer some excellent day trips. The Sunspot National Solar Observatory, about 32 miles up scenic byway NM-6563 on Sacramento Peak, has free self-guided tours and guided tours in summer. The drive through Lincoln National Forest is half the fun. [White Sands](/northamerica/unitedstates/newmexico/whitesands) is the obvious day trip at just 15 miles, and [Cloudcroft](/northamerica/unitedstates/newmexico/cloudcroft) in the mountains above makes a cool escape on a hot day.
