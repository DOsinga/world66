A quirky museum in a restored 1898 Southern Pacific depot building, stuffed with model trains and miniature railroads spread across five cars. If you have any nostalgia for model railways, this place will get you. Even if you don't, the building itself is worth a look, and the outdoor narrow-gauge train rides on weekends are genuinely fun.
