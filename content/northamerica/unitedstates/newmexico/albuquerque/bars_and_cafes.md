---
title: 'Bars and Cafes'
type: section
---

Albuquerque has a lively bar scene spread across several neighborhoods. The Nob Hill area along Central Avenue draws a younger crowd to its eclectic mix of pubs and lounges, while downtown and Old Town offer more laid-back spots for a drink after a day of sightseeing. You will also find comedy clubs and live music venues scattered around the city.
