Laffs has been the comedy anchor in Albuquerque for years, hosting touring stand-ups alongside regular open mic nights that draw the city's aspiring comedians. The room is intimate enough that there's no bad seat, and the shows lean toward mainstream comedy rather than anything experimental. Shows run Thursday through Sunday; weekend headliners are typically mid-tier national acts. Tickets are reasonably priced and the bar keeps things moving.
