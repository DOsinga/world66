---
title: 'Things to Do'
type: section
---

Albuquerque has more going on than most visitors expect. Ride the Sandia Peak Tramway for staggering views, explore the petroglyphs at the national monument on the west side, or wander the galleries and museums around Old Town. The International Balloon Fiesta in October is the marquee event, but hot air balloon rides are available year-round. The Bosque Trail along the Rio Grande is perfect for a morning walk or bike ride through the cottonwoods.
