Angel Fire is a small ski resort town in the Moreno Valley, close to [Taos](/northamerica/unitedstates/newmexico/taos) Ski Valley and Red River. The resort has the only half-pipe in New Mexico and is a more relaxed, family-friendly alternative to Taos. In summer, the area offers hiking, mountain biking, and fishing in the surrounding Carson National Forest.
