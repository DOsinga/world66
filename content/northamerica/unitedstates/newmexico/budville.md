Travel Guide

Budville is a semi-ghost town along Route 66 in Cibola County. It's main claim to fame was the trading post operated by Bud Rice, a native of nearby Cubero. Bud ran a towing service and garage, and was also a judge in the area. He was killed in a crime that remains unsolved to this day. The trading post still stands, but is not open for business.
