Clovis is a town of about 32,000 near the Texas border, surrounded by cattle ranches and open plains. It is not a prime tourist destination, but it works as a stopover between New Mexico's northern mountains and its southern deserts. Clovis is also home to Cannon Air Force Base, and the town that gave the Clovis archaeological culture its name -- some of the earliest evidence of human habitation in North America was found here.
