Gallup is the Indian arts and crafts hub of the Southwest, with over 70 retail and wholesale outlets dealing in jewelry, baskets, pottery, rugs, and paintings from the region's tribal nations. It can be overwhelming. If you want genuine reservation-style red moccasins with white soles, or a hand-woven Navajo rug, this is where you come.
