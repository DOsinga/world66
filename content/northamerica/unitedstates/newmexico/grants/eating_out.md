Typical of a small town, choices here are a bit limited, but the chow is good. New Mexican dominates, and it's easy to find southwest favorites like Indian Tacos, sopaipillas, carne adovada, and other treats. Look for local Indians selling the famed oven bread by the side of the road.

### [El Cafecito](/northamerica/unitedstates/newmexico/grants/eating_out/el_cafecito)

A small family run joint on Santa Fe avenue, El Cafecito has the basic New Mexican dishes covered. Sopaipillas, Burros, Navajo Tacos, etc. can be had here.

### [WOW Diner](/northamerica/unitedstates/newmexico/grants/eating_out/wow_diner)

Newly opened, this glittering stainless steel diner features an extensive and overwhelming menu. The dining area is small, but cozy. Good food!

### [La Ventana Steak House](/northamerica/unitedstates/newmexico/grants/eating_out/la_ventana_steak_h)

La Ventana is perhaps the nicest restaurant in Grants. Steaks, seafood, and New Mexican are available, along with a full bar. It can get very busy, so get a reservation.

### [El Ranchero](/northamerica/unitedstates/newmexico/grants/eating_out/el_ranchero)

A small family run restaurant in Milan, this is the place for killer hot red chile. Classic menu of New Mexican favorites, along with Navajo (Indian) Tacos.

type: Mexican



tel: 505-876-1032

address: 705 Hwy 66

PriceOfMenu: inexpensive

### [Canton Cafe](/northamerica/unitedstates/newmexico/grants/eating_out/canton_cafe)

A wonderful Route 66 classic, this little place features a decent buffet (try the shrimp with green beans, or the tasty egg rolls), but also an extensive menu. The dated decor inside is vintage 66.

type: Chinese



tel: 505-287-8314

address: 1212 E. Santa Fe Ave.

PriceOfMenu: inexpensive

### [La Hacienda](/northamerica/unitedstates/newmexico/grants/eating_out/la_hacienda)

Not your typical Mexican restaurant, this place serves food that's a cut above. Try the fabulous tortilla soup, with avocado, chicken, broccoli, and cauliflower in a savory caldo. Or how about Camarones al Mojo, garlic shrimp with mushrooms. The building is an eye catching red and yellow structure.

type: Mexican



tel: 505-287-5561

address: 907 N 1st Street

PriceOfMenu: moderate
