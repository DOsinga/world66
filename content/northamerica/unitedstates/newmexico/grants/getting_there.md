---
title: "Getting There in Grants"
type: section
---

The easiest way is via driving. Grants is situated on I-40, between Albuquerque and Gallup. It's about an hour from either town. There is a municipal airport for the airplane owners, so it's possible to fly in as well, and a couple of local businesses rent cars. Bus service is provided by Greyhound. There is currently no rail service, even though the Amtrak Southwest Chief passes through town. And of course, you can hike or cycle into town; the Continental Divide National Scenic Trail goes from Canada to Mexico and passes through town, along with the similar Great Divide Mountain Bike Route.
