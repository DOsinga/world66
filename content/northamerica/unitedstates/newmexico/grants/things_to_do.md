title: 'Things to Do'
type: section
---

Grants punches above its weight for outdoor activities. Hike sacred Mount Taylor, explore lava tube caves at [El Malpais](/northamerica/unitedstates/newmexico/elmapais), mountain bike through ponderosa and aspen in the Zuni Mountains, or cruise a long intact section of Route 66 from Mesita to the Continental Divide. The Grants Mining Museum downtown tells the story of the uranium boom that transformed the town in the 1950s.
