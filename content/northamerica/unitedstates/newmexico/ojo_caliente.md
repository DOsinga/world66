Travel Guide

Glorious Ojo Caliente. A Spanish name meaning "Hot Springs", this tiny little hamlet boasts one of the grooviest hot springs resorts anywhere. It's a small, intimate place, with no big industrial development, and a decidedly off-the-beaten-track feel.
