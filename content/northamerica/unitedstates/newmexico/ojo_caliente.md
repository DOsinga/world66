The name means "hot springs" in Spanish, and that is exactly what brings people to this tiny hamlet north of [Espanola](/northamerica/unitedstates/newmexico/espanola). The Ojo Caliente Mineral Springs resort is one of the grooviest hot springs spots anywhere -- intimate, unhurried, and thoroughly off the beaten track. Multiple geothermal pools with different mineral compositions emerge from the hillside, and the surrounding high desert landscape adds to the sense of being somewhere timeless.
