Roswell is most famous for the 1947 incident when a U.F.O. supposedly crashed nearby. Roswell now hosts the International U.F.O. Museum & Reasearch Center, and holds the U.F.O. Festival every 4th of July weekend. A quirky town where the lampposts resemble alien heads, and the local stores have alien murals painted on their windows, Roswell is an interesting place to visit.
