Upon entering the UFO Museum, you must explore all the information the Museum has on the 1947 Roswell Incident. Or you might chat with a resident volunteer about alien abductions. Whatever your fancy, the Museum is full of information in the history, science, and research about UFO and alien-related events worldwide.
