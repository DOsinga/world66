---
title: 'Getting There'
type: section
---

Ruidoso has no commercial air service. The nearest airports are in [Alamogordo](/northamerica/unitedstates/newmexico/alamogordo) (Alamogordo-White Sands Regional, tiny and limited), El Paso (about 120 miles south, with full connections), and [Albuquerque](/northamerica/unitedstates/newmexico/albuquerque) (about 200 miles north). Most visitors drive.

By road, the standard approaches are:
- **From the west:** NM-70 through the Tularosa Valley from [Alamogordo](/northamerica/unitedstates/newmexico/alamogordo) — about an hour, with the descent from the mountains offering dramatic views of White Sands below.
- **From the east:** US-380 or NM-70 from [Roswell](/northamerica/unitedstates/newmexico/roswell) — about 75 miles.
- **From the north:** US-54 south to Carrizozo, then US-380 east and NM-37 south through the Capitan Mountains.

There is no reliable bus service to Ruidoso.
