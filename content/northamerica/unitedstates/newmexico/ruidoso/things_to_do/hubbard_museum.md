Located in Ruidoso Downs just east of town, the Hubbard Museum holds an impressive collection focused on the American West — horses above all, but also Native American artifacts, cowboy gear, stagecoaches, and frontier-era art. The horse collection is genuinely world-class, fitting given the town's identity around the racetrack. Plan a couple of hours. 26301 US Highway 70, Ruidoso Downs.
