  The All American Quarter Horse Futurity, run here each Labor Day, carries a purse that has reached over $3 million — making it the richest quarter-horse race in the world. The race covers 440 yards and is over in about 21 seconds.
---

Ruidoso Downs hosts quarter-horse racing from May through Labor Day, with the All American Futurity on Labor Day weekend drawing the biggest crowds of the year. Even if you have no particular interest in horse racing, an afternoon at the track — with a cold beer, a small wager, and the Sacramento Mountains as a backdrop — is a fine way to spend time. The casino on the grounds operates year-round. 1461 US Highway 70, Ruidoso Downs.
