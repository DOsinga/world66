The best resource for sights, hotels, restaurants, bars, what to do and see

On old Route 66 in Cibola County, San Fidel is a tiny little hamlet that has a couple of things worth note. For Route 66 roadies, there's the ruins of the Whiting Brothers Chief Rancho motel to the west of town, and the White Arrow Garage in town. For art lovers, there's a hip little gallery to the east of the mission church. Finally, for wine lovers, there's a small winery to the north of town that brews a kicking Riesling.
