---
title: 'Day Trips'
type: section
---

Northern New Mexico offers some of the best day trips in the state. Visit the nearby pueblos and, if you are lucky, catch a feast day procession or traditional dance. The hot springs at [Ojo Caliente](/northamerica/unitedstates/newmexico/ojo_caliente) are about an hour north. In winter, Ski Santa Fe is just 16 miles up the mountain. The High Road to [Taos](/northamerica/unitedstates/newmexico/taos) through Chimayo, Truchas, and the mountain villages is one of the most scenic drives in the Southwest.
