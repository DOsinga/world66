### [El Santuario de Chimayo](/northamerica/unitedstates/newmexico/santafe/day_trips/el_santuario_de_ch)

A place filled with Spiritual feelings and miracles. The tale has it that in 1810, while performing penances Don Bernardo Abeyta saw a light in a hill near the Santa Cruz River and so he dug in the ground to find a crucifix, perhaps originally from Guatemala. El Santuario de Chimayo houses this crucifix and also holy dirt from this holy ground which lifts sadness and cures ailments.

Take SR 76 east from Espanola to Chimayo and turn right on Juan Medina Road. Summer visiting hours are from 9 to 5.

### [Ojo Caliente Mineral Springs and Spa](/northamerica/unitedstates/newmexico/santafe/day_trips/ojo_caliente_miner)

Lodging, spa from the time of the Indians (and the only place in the world with four different geothermal mineral waters coming from the hill!), restaurant or picnic areas, hiking, activities and a store that sells their own spa products make this worth a day's visit.

$16 gets you in the mud! Besides the mud bath, there are many pools with tranquility that verges to the point of being sacred and everyone is even whispering. Massages, private baths and locker rooms available.
