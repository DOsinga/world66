---
title: 'Things to Do'
type: section
---

Santa Fe has more museums per capita than almost any American city, and they are all worth visiting. The Museum of International Folk Art and the Georgia O'Keeffe Museum are standouts. The Santa Fe Opera, set in an open-air theater in the hills north of town, runs in summer and is world-class. Beyond the cultural offerings, the Santa Fe National Forest provides hiking and mountain biking in summer and skiing in winter. Check out Cowgirls in the Guadalupe district for live music and a good time.
