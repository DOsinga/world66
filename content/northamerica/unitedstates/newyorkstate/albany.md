As you travel north you will reach the capital of New York around exits 23 and 24. To take a look at the New York capital, state buildings, state university, and state museam you just need to get off of exit 23. This picture is just one of the many common areas where you can enjoy your day at the University at Albany. Albany is not only the capital of the state, but a wealthy reasource of fun activities for you to enjoy while you are in New York.
