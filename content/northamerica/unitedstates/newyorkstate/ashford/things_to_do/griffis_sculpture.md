Nestled in the northern Allegheny foothills, the Griffis Sculpture Park was the vision of Buffalo sculptor Larry Griffis, Jr. The park encompasses 425 acres and includes more than 250 works displayed in a variety of natural settings. These sculptures are intended to be interactive and many can be climbed in or on by visitors. The park also hosts a number of educational workshops and cultural events.

Admission is $5.00 for adults, $3.00 for seniors and students. Children under 12 are free.
