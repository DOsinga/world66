Auburn is home to Centro of Cayuga, an operating subsidiary and part of the regional Central New York Regional Transporation Authority (CNYRTA).

The Centro of Cayuga system radiates from Downtown Auburn on five routes during the daylight hours.

In addition, the parent Centro system (Centro of Onondaga-Syracuse) operates a commuter route to Syracuse and the Syracuse Regional Transportation Center on four different routes (38, 138, 236, 238).

At this time, Centro of Onondaga is the only intercity service, and operates into Syracuse, where it connects with Greyhound and Trailways at the Regional Transportation Center.

### [Centro of Cayuga](/northamerica/unitedstates/newyorkstate/auburn/getting_around/centro_of_cayuga)

1 West Genesee (to Finger Lakes Mall)
2 Franklin (to Grant Avenue Plaza - also service Cayuga Comm. College)
3 Grant (to Grant Avenue Plaza)
4 State (to Finger Lakes Mall)
