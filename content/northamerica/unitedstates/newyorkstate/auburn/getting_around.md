---
title: "Getting Around in Auburn"
type: section
---

Auburn is served by Centro of Cayuga, part of the Central New York Regional Transportation Authority (CNYRTA). The bus system radiates from downtown Auburn on five routes during daylight hours.

For connections beyond Auburn, Centro of Onondaga operates commuter routes to Syracuse and the Syracuse Regional Transportation Center, where you can connect with Greyhound and Trailways for longer-distance travel.

**Centro of Cayuga routes:**

- 1 West Genesee (to Finger Lakes Mall)
- 2 Franklin (to Grant Avenue Plaza, also serves Cayuga Community College)
- 3 Grant (to Grant Avenue Plaza)
- 4 State (to Finger Lakes Mall)

Phone: 315-685-7075. Website: [centro.org](http://www.centro.org)
