---
title: "Practical Information in Batavia"
type: section
---

If driving, Batavia is rather easy to get to using the NEW YORK STATE THRUWAY. Exit at Exit 48 BATAVIA. The city center is just south of the exit.

If needing to fly, the closest two commercial airports are ROCHESTER (ROC) and BUFFALO (BUF). Most major airlines service these airports. When arriving at the airports, you can get to Downtown Buffalo or Downtown Rochester...connecting at their respective bus terminals to either Greyhound or New York Trailways buses.
