type: section

One thing Buffalo is famous for is its nightlife.

The Chippawa District, the busiest bar strip hosts a number of bars, restaurants and dance floors for nearly anyone.

In the County of Erie, most bars, taverns and nightclubs have a liquor license, allowing them to serve alcohol until 4:00am! Most surrounding counties may have limits on their serving hours, varying from as early as 12:00 midnight, up to 2:00am.

In addition, no sales of alcohol may be permitted before 8:00am weekdays and 12:00 noon on Sundays. This is a STATE LAW, and is heavily enforced.

When entering a bar, you may be required to provide legitamate proof of your age, as well as identification on WHO you are. Many busy nightclubs may refuse entry to those without valid IDs. A drivers license and passport are two failsafe proofs of identifications, or DMV issued non-drivers ID.
