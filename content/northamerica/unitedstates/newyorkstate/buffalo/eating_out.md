type: section

Of all cities in the Northeast United States, Buffalo stands out in reference to it's food.

The City of Buffalo is home to the Buffalo Style Chicken Wing, and Roast Beef on Kimmelweck (or Weck for short) to name a couple.

In addition, the region loves the variety of ethnic cuisines, and has a restaurant SOMEWHERE in the city that offers that type of cuisine.

The Chicken Wing reigns supreme, and if you are interested in eating them (if you haven't already), most restuarants carry it on their menus.
