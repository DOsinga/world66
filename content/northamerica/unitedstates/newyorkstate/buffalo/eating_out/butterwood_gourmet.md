Butterwood Gourmet Desserts is located in the charming village of Williamsville, out Main Street from the City of Buffalo.

Butterwood Gourmet Desserts is well known nationally for it's excellent quality desserts.

Prices can be a little on the expensive side, but most would agree that the cost is worth it.
