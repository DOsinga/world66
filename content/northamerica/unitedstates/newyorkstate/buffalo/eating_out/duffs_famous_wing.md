One of the most renowned chicken wing restaraunts, it rivals the Anchor Bar for the best wings in Buffalo. This restaraunt gives you a sense of what Western New Yorkers are all about. It isn't a fancy place to eat, but the service is good and the prices are cheap. A great place to spend an evening of dining.
