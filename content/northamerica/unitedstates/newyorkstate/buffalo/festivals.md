Buffalo is well known for the popularity of a number of its festivals, primarily held during the summer months each year.

### [Taste of Buffalo](/northamerica/unitedstates/newyorkstate/buffalo/festivals/taste_of_buffalo)

Held the weekend immediately following the July 4th weekend, the [Taste of Buffalo](/northamerica/unitedstates/newyorkstate/buffalo/festivals) is one of Buffalo's largest street festivals.

Once held on Delaware Avenue, moved then to Main Street and then returning to Delaware Avenue, the Taste of Buffalo hosts over 140 restaurants that offer samples of some of their most popular dishes (not including the Buffalo Chicken Wing!).

### [Allentown Art Festival](/northamerica/unitedstates/newyorkstate/buffalo/festivals/allentown_art_fest)

Held June 10-11, 2006, the Allentown Art Festival is one of the largest arts festivals in the eastern portion of the United States.
