Buffalo benefits from a comprehensive public transportation system that reaches virtually most communities in Erie and Niagara Counties. The Niagara Frontier Transportation Authority's Metro Bus and Rail system operates a number of hubs in which passengers can easily transfer between bus and rail routes.

Service operates DAILY in the City of Buffalo, with less frequent or no service to some outer areas on weekends and certain holidays. The fare varies from $1.50 - 2.25 depending on the distance travelled (zone crossing).

NFTA Metro information can be reached three ways:

716-855-7211 (voice)
info@nfta.com (e-mail)
www.nfta.com/metro (website)
