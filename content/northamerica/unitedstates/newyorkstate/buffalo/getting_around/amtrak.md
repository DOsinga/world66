The Niagara Frontier Transportation Authority operates a multi-modal transit system, using buses, light rail, and shuttle vans to service the counties of Erie and Niagara.

The City of Buffalo offers the best service hours, with many operating daily from early morning until late night.

The City of Niagara Falls and suburban service may operate less frequently, and may force passengers to walk a little further to their destinations.

The Buffalo Transportation Center located at North Division and Ellicott Streets has an information booth manned by extremely knowlegable staff, and will be able to issue virtually any schedule or a system map, if needed.

The information booth also began offering day passes, weekend passes and monthly or 30 day passes from the "Big Blue" machines on either side of the kiosk. These are available 24 hours-a-day, and accept currency, coins, and credit/debit cards.

To speak to a person, the kiosk is open Monday through Friday ONLY between 8:30am and 4:30pm.
