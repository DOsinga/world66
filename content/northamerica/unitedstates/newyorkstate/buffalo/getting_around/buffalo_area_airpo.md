The Buffalo Niagara region has two airports:

BUF (the Greater Buffalo Niagara International Airport) is located due east of the City of Buffalo, and is approximately a 40 minute bus ride to the city center (downtown). This airport currently handles ALL commercial air traffic.

IAG (Niagara Falls International Airport) is located north of Buffalo, in the town of Niagara. A couple charter airlines have recently shown interest in returning commercial and charter air service, but nothing has resulted from the talks.
