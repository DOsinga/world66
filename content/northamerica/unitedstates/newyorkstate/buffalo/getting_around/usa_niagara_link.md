The** NIAGARA FRONTIER TRANSPORTATION AUTHORITY** offers two routes in Niagara Falls that utilize trolley buses to transport visitors to sights of interest.

ROUTE 55T - Pine Avenue

*Operates hourly seven-days-a-week between Downtown Niagara Falls and Summit Park Mall serving Pine Avenue. This special bus departs both terminals ON the hour, from 9:00am with the last bus leaving at 12:00 midnight.*

ROUTE 205 - USA Niagara Link

*Operates every 15-35 minutes, looping throughout Niagara Falls.*

Those staying at participating hotels experience the pleasure of riding these buses FREE of CHARGE using a special pass, obtainable at PARTICIPATING hotels front service desk.
