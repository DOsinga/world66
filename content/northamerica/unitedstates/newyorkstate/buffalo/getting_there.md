---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

Getting to Buffalo is an easy task from a number of major airport hubs.

The major airport hubs serviced include:

JFK - New York (Kennedy)
---Jet Blue

LGA - New York (LaGuardia)
---US Airways

ORD - Chicago (O'Hare)
---United Airlines and American Eagle (American Airlines)

EWR - Newark (Liberty)
--- US Airways

IAD - Washington (Dulles)
--- Independence Air

DCA - Washington (National)
--- US Airways

PHX - Phoenix (Sky Harbor International)
--- Southwest

BWI - Baltimore (Baltimore Washington International)
--- US Airways, Southwest Airlines

MDW Chicago (Midway)
--- Southwest Airlines

LAS Las Vegas (McCarran)
--- Southwest Airlines

In addition, Amtrak offers a few trains per day from New York Penn Station and a once-a-day trip from Chicago. Also there is service, once daily from Toronto.
