The Texas Hot style hot dog is another regional delicacy, teaming a Sahlens or Wardynski hot dog with mustard, slivered white onion and Texas Style meat sauce on a steamed hot dog roll.

The Best Texas Red Hot around is Nestor's Texas Red Hots at 102 Webster st. in North Tonawanda Ny. right down the street form the Riviera Theatre. They make their sauce from a secret family recipe and they make it right at the store. You can buy their sauce and its frezeable too. you have got to try them. They only use Sahlens hot dogs.

The most recognizable name is Seneca Texas Hots, located at the Seneca Street City Line for years. Other places exist, hence the (insert name here) reference at the beginning.

Another Texas Style dish popping up is the "*Buffalo Plate*", fashioned after the Nick Tahou's in Rochester NY's "*Garbage Plate*". Another one to chalk up to local cuisine find the customer with french fries or hash brown potatos, macaroni salad, two hotdogs (chopped up), slivered onions, mustard and texas sauce poured over the top.
