The internationally recognized **Broadway Market** is located in the old Polish Neighborhood of the east side of Buffalo.

Inside are many food retailers, including fresh european style bakers, butchers and grocers.

A stand-alone ***Save-A-Lot*** food store is also on the property.

Market hours can vary, and stores (except ***Save-A-Lot***) are **CLOSED **on Sundays.
