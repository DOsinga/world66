---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

The city's great strength is its architecture. Walk Elmwood Avenue and Delaware Avenue for the turn-of-the-century mansions, tour **Frank Lloyd Wright's Darwin Martin House** complex on Jewett Parkway, and look up at Louis Sullivan's **Guaranty Building** downtown — one of the earliest steel-frame skyscrapers. The **Albright-Knox / Buffalo AKG** on Elmwood is the one essential museum. **Canalside** on the waterfront has been rebuilt into the city's best public space. And **Niagara Falls** is half an hour away.
