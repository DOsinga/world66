---
title: "Getting Around"
type: section
---

Planes, trains, buses and automobiles are the way to get to and from Cheektowaga!

The Greater Buffalo Niagara International Airport is located on Genesee Street between Cayuga and Transit Roads. You can arrive into Cheektowaga using most major air carriers, with Southwest and Jet Blue being the primary operators into the airport.

Train-wise, the town also houses the BUFFALO-DEPEW station on Dick Road between Broadway and Walden Avenue. ALL Amtrak trains serving the area will serve this station, including the only stop in Buffalo area for the "Lake Shore Limited" line.

Though there is no inter-city agency serving Cheektowaga, the NFTA offers low-cost service to the City Center of Buffalo, where you can connect to intercity carriers at that transportation center.
