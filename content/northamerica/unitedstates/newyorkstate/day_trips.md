---
title: "Day Trips in New York State"
type: section
---

Taking** New York state day trips **can make your trip to the
state of New York even more enriching because it will give you the
opportunity to visit other states or even another country. Depending on
where your trip to the state of New York takes you, one of these
locations is bound to provide an entertaining day trip destination:

**Spend a Day in New Jersey**

You can get to Fort Lee, New Jersey, from southern New York in just
a few minutes by crossing the George Washington Bridge. Bus and trains
(via the Port Authority of New York and New Jersey, or PATH) also run
between New York City and New Jersey many times per day, making it
possible to get to destinations as far out as Newark in only 22 minutes
without doing any driving.

In Newark, you can make a stop at popular tourist destinations such
as the Newark Museum, which showcases American art and a world-renowned
collection of Tibetan artifacts. The museum even includes a zoo and a
preserved Victorian-era house, making it possible to spend the entire
day just at that location.

**Visit New England**

Depending on your destination in New York state, driving over the
border into Vermont, Massachusetts or New Hampshire is an easy way to
visit a piece of New England for a day. You can also take a train into
major cities within these states from New York City if you're staying
at the southern part of the state. Visiting cities in New England often
allows you to tour locations of historical importance to the founding
of the nation.

**Head to Canada**

If you're on the northwest end of the state, a day trip to Canada
can make a fun excursion and, depending on your starting point, you can
get there in mere minutes. An excellent Canadian destination for your
day trip from the Buffalo area is the Canadian side of Niagara Falls,
which anyone who has seen the falls from both sides can tell you is the
more spectacular view. There's also plenty to do in the Niagara Falls
area, including arcade games, carnival rides, dining, theater and
visiting museums and an aquarium.

Toronto, just an hour and a half from the Buffalo area, makes
another great day trip destination. Explore the museums, parks and
attractions such as the Toronto Zoo in Canada's largest city. Climb the
Canadian National Tower, the world's tallest free-standing tower, for a
great view of the city, particularly after the sun has set.

Just remember that every family member age 16 or older will need a
passport to get over the border--even just for a day trip. If you don't
have passports for children ages 15 and younger, you must provide their
birth certificates.
