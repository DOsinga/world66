While there are not many exotic cuisines located in Elmira, there are a
number of extremely good takes on American favorites worth checking out.

**Contributors**
October 20, 2005

### [m & m redhots](/northamerica/unitedstates/newyorkstate/elmira/eating_out/m__m_hots)

A longtime Elmira institution made famous by their impressive meat
sauce for their hot dogs.  Many patrons come in buying the hot
dogs, but also purchasing a pint of the sauce to take home. 
Impressive cheese dogs because they warm the cheese on the grill before
adding it to the dog.  Not a large menu, but warm atmosphere and
definitely an easy place to hang out and hear the local chatter.

Watch the hours.  m & m hots is not open during the weekends
anymore, so you have to catch them between Monday through Friday.

### [Pudgie's Pizza](/northamerica/unitedstates/newyorkstate/elmira/eating_out/pudgies_pizza)

A longtime Elmira favorite. Pudgies has several locations around Elmira and offer nice Italian subs, thick crusted pizza, and much more. The pizza sports a unique taste due to a blend of spices found in the sauce. Founded by a local Elmiran, the crowd spans from young through adult.

### [Southside Sub Shop](/northamerica/unitedstates/newyorkstate/elmira/eating_out/southside_sub_shop)

Definitely an Elmira favorite! Their jumbo italian subs come in fresh baked bread packed full of meat with a nice sprinkling of oils and parmesean cheese. Although this place is generally staffed by high schoolers, the sub making craft has extended throughout the years and high quality has been maintained. Anyone in Elmira will tell you this is the best place to get subs!
