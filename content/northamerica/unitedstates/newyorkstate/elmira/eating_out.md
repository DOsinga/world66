---
title: "Eating Out"
type: section
---

Elmira is not a food destination, but it has a handful of long-standing local favourites worth knowing about. The cuisine is straightforward American comfort food.

**M & M Red Hots** on Baldwin Street is an Elmira institution, famous for their hot dogs with a rich house-made meat sauce. Regulars buy pints of the sauce to take home. The cheese dogs are particularly good -- they warm the cheese on the grill before adding it to the dog. Note that they are only open Monday through Friday.

**Pudgie's Pizza** has several locations around town and is a local chain founded in Elmira. The pizza has a distinctive thick crust and a well-spiced sauce. They also do decent Italian subs.
