### [Mark Twain's Study](/northamerica/unitedstates/newyorkstate/elmira/sights/mark_twains_study)

Mark Twain's famous octagonal shaped study that was originally located on Quarry Farm in Elmira, was placed at Elmira College many years ago. The study can be easily accessed from the public entrance on College Ave.

### [Mark Twain's Grave](/northamerica/unitedstates/newyorkstate/elmira/sights/mark_twains_grave)

As mentioned in the description of the city, Mark Twain holds his final resting place here. Small site located near the roadway in a large cemetary, there is very little fanfare and simply a bunch of headstones. The grave can be found in Woodlawn Cemetary located in West Elmira.
