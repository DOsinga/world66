Take a tour, group, bus, private tour of Elmira's National Historic District. Known as the "Near Westside Neighborhood". Designated nationaly as having the largest concentration of Victorian home architecture. Go inside many restored Victorian homes in the historic district. Re-live Victorian history in these beutiful mansions.
