The college population keeps Ithaca's bar scene active year-round. Most bars are concentrated around the Commons and Collegetown. Busy nights on weekends can mean short queues at the door.

### [Common Ground](/northamerica/unitedstates/newyorkstate/ithaca_tompkins_c/bars_and_cafes/common_ground)

The only LGBT bar in the area, Common Ground is about 3 miles south of downtown on Danby Road. It draws a mixed crowd and hosts regular events — check their website for what's on.
