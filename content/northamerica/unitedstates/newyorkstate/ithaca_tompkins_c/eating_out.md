Like many other cities of the world, Ithaca is home to a diverse collection of restaurants sure to please nearly every palate.

The Downtown Ithaca Commons is bustling with a selection of restaurants and taverns.

Most city and rural buses terminate near the commons. Check with bus schedules to confirm last trip times so you are not stuck there!

In addition, Collegetown (Cornell Campus) has the second largest selection of restaurants in a common area.

### [Purity Ice Cream](/northamerica/unitedstates/newyorkstate/ithaca_tompkins_c/eating_out/purity_ice_cream)

Purity Ice Cream, originally opened here in Ithaca, is over 70 years old and serves an enormous variety of ice cream flavors, all made from local, high quality ingredients. Although Purity does feature many flavors that other national brands will offer, you will also find some flavors that will only exist at Purity. Some of these flavors include Finger Lakes Tourist, which has white chocolate chunks and hazelnut pieces in chocolate ice cream, and Sleepers Awake!, coffee ice cream with fudge swirls and chocolate chunks.

### [Viva Taqueria & Cantina](/northamerica/unitedstates/newyorkstate/ithaca_tompkins_c/eating_out/viva_taqueria__ca)

This lively Mexican restaurant is centrally located downtown directly off of the commons. The food is made fresh everyday. Prices are inexpensive despite the quality and portion size. The margaritas are one of the main features. The tequila bar is stocked with every type of 100% blue agave tequila available and they only use fresh squeezed lime juice (no pre-made sour mix here). You will not be disappointed in this gem that some locals eat at on a daily basis.
