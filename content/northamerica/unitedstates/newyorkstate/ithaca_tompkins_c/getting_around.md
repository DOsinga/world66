TCAT (Tompkins Consolidated Area Transit) covers most of the city and runs frequent service between the Commons and Cornell campus. Regional connections run to Elmira, Corland, and Binghamton. Most routes converge near the Downtown Commons — check schedules if you're out late, as service thins in the evenings.

### [TCAT (Tompkins Consolidated Area Transit)](/northamerica/unitedstates/newyorkstate/ithaca_tompkins_c/getting_around/tcat_tompkins_con)

The Tompkins Consolidated Area Transit system is the primary carrier for Tompkins County, NY. Using the name TCAT Bus, the service covers almost every corner of the City of Ithaca NY, rural service to a number of communities such as Trumansburg and Dryden, and a campus-wide shuttle service for the Cornell University Campus.

With the existance of the Cornell Campus, there is frequent DAILY service between the Commons (Downtown Ithaca) and the Cornell campus.
