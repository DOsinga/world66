As mentioned, Ithaca is known for a number of colleges and universities, which make up a substantial portion of the city population.

Most nightclubs may require a short wait at entrances on weekends, and visitors are urged to dress appropriately from the elements.

### [Common Ground](/northamerica/unitedstates/newyorkstate/ithaca_tompkins_c/nightlife_and_ente/common_ground)

The Common Ground is not located in the Commons (Downtown Ithaca), but 3.3 miles (according to Google) south of the city.

Being the only LGBT bar in the area, the bar caters to everyone.

Their website will list upcoming events.
