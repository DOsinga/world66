---
title: "Practical Information in Ithaca (Tompkins County)"
type: section
---

With the size of Ithaca as a smaller city and as a college town, it is wisely recommended to place and CONFIRM reservations at any of the hotels in the Ithaca area.

Ithaca has a number of hotels with national brand names and most reservations can easily be made online.

In addition, public transportation is limited on routes not serving shopping areas and the Cornell Campus. It is recommended to check schedules while planning outings so that you will not be forced to board a cab
