The Ithaca Commons downtown has independent boutiques and local shops. Collegetown near Cornell adds more options, mostly student-oriented. Pyramid Mall on North Triphammer Road is the main suburban mall, with Sears, Bon Ton, and Target as anchors.

### [Pyramid Mall](/northamerica/unitedstates/newyorkstate/ithaca_tompkins_c/shopping/pyramid_mall)

Mall hours: Monday through Saturday 10am to 9pm, Sunday 11am to 5pm.
Please check with your favorite stores and the cinemas for extended hours.
Management Office Hours: Monday through Friday 8am - 5pm.

The Pyramid Mall is located north of Ithaca on North Triphammer Road, near NYS 13.

Anchor stores include Sears, Bon Ton and Target.

[TCAT](/northamerica/unitedstates/newyorkstate/ithaca_tompkins_c/getting_around/tcat_tompkins_con)Routes 13, 30, 32, 37, 44, 45 and 52 service the mall area.
