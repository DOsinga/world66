### [Adirondack Winery & Tasting Room](/northamerica/unitedstates/newyorkstate/lakegeorge/lakegeorgevillag/things_to_do/adirondack_winery)

Adirondack Winery & Tasting Room is the region's premier Micro-Winery, Located in the heart of Lake George Village.

The winery offers an impressive selection of wines that are all hand-crafted on premises by their own wine-maker.

When visiting the winery, you must participate in a wine tasting session, which includes a souvenir glass to take home with you. Awesome cheddar cheese plates are available to go along with your tasting.

The winery sells wine-related accessories and gifts, as well as gourmet foods, cheeses, chocolates, and more.
