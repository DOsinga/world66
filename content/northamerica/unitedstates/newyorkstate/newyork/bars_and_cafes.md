**Stardust Diner**

As fifties videos play on TVs, you can enjoy harty american breakfasts! Some good dishes are: Blue Suede burger ($10.50), meatloaf ($11.95), Gina Lola Brigetta lasagna ($11.25), and reuben ($10.50).

**Alices Tea Cup**

Alice's Tea Cup is a tea room. It's nice and cozy. There is a lot of Alice's Wonderland pictures and merchandise.

address: Off of 73rd on Columbus Ave. (102 Columbus Ave.)
