---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment"
type: section
---

Brooklyn is the largest borough in New York City. Being the second
most densely populated area in the world means that there are a lot of
people; a lot of people means a lot of stuff to do. Daytime activities
can range from museums to visiting Coney Island. You may be more
interested in a sports venue or just sightseeing Brooklyn’s unique
architectural history. This all may leave you wondering: what is there
to do at night? If nocturnal fun is what you seek, Brooklyn has your
answer.

**Bars and Pubs**

If you’re looking for a place to drink, Brooklyn has a wide variety
of bars and pubs. Most bars offer some kind of musical entertainment in
the forms of live music or DJ. You can find a price range that fits
your budget and a menu that will fit your preferred drinks. Try 4th Avenue Pub for a fine selection of different beers and microbrews. For an odd mix, go to South 4th
Bar and Café. There you can take your pick of hard liquors and beer or
a great selection of coffees and teas. The Jakewalk is also a good
place for fondue and drinks. Sunny’s Bar has a lot of great reviews.
With live music played here frequently and a friendly bar staff, people
really enjoy this spot. It’s right on the edge of the water and very
difficult to access but once you get there you’ll know it was worth it.

**Dancing**

Dance clubs are plentiful in Brooklyn. There is a good mix of DJs
and live bands that play at different dance clubs all the time. If
you’re really interested in what’s being preformed that night try and
check the venue website or look through a local newspaper. One of the
best dance clubs in Brooklyn is the Sputnik. Upstairs is a yummy
restaurant with really good burgers and downstairs is the club with a
full bar and stage for performers. Every couple of days a new DJ or
band is playing so you should be able to catch a show pretty easily.
Other good clubs to try are Studio B, Hugs and Adam’s Lounge.

Brooklyn has a lot of other interesting late night activities. You
might want to check out a comedy club like Comic Strip Live, or you
could try out a Karaoke bar like 100 Fun. Whatever you enjoy doing when
the sun goes down you’ll be able to find it in Brooklyn.
