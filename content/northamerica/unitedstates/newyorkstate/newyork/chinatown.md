type: poi

The largest Chinatown in the United States, packed into a few dense blocks of Canal, Mott, and Bayard Streets. Come for the food: dim sum parlors, roast duck shops, hand-pulled noodle joints, and bubble tea stands line every street. It bleeds into Little Italy and the Lower East Side, making the whole area rewarding to simply wander.
