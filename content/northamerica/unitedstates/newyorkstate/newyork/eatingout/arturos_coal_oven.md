Considered a hidden gem, Arturo's Coal Oven Pizza serves some of the best pizza in NYC. In addition to smoky pizzas, Arturo's serves up seafood, chicken dishes, veal, and pasta dishes. Arturo's is located in New York's famous Greenwich Village.
