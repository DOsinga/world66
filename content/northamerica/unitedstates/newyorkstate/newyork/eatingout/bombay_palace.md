They do set menus and also a set buffet. This is an upmarket emporium as you might guess from the location - prices reflect this fact. For a meal that you would pay $30 elsewhere, you pay $45+ 

That said, the food is consistent and I have revisited many times

The Bhel Puri as a starter is stunning (a vegetarian dish consisting of rice and spice!)
