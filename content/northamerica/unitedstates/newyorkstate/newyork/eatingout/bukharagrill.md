Bukhara Grill is offering New York a new dining experience...flavors of the long-forgotten Northwest frontiers of India.

The restaurant offers a very spacious, rustic, yet highly sophisticated interior with an old-fashioned stonewall finish. Hand-woven Persian carpets are elegantly draped from the ceilings, and the atmosphere as a whole does indeed satisfy the yearnings of a mind bent toward whimsical creativity.

A passionate team of servers and chefs, who believe in absolute perfection, help to enhance your dining experience to a higher degree of satisfaction, a true feast for all five senses.

Become enchanted by the cascading waterfall, or walk up to the terrace, where you may indulge your eyes with a breath-taking view. Hand pick the succulent kababs, drenched in flavorful Indian spices. A rarified cuisine, coupled with a complementing wine list, is what Bukhara Grill offers.
