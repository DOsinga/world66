This little noodle bar serves up Japanese-inspired cuisine, and attracts hoards of people around lunch time. No restaurant-style seating, just sitting by the the counter. Food is good though, and prices are reasonable.

Open: Sun-Thu 12 pm to 11 pm
Fri & Sat 12 pm to 12 am
