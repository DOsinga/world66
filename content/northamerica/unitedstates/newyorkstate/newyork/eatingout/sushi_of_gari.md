You'll have to get in line to eat at this small Upper East Side restaurant and pay a pretty penny to boot. This is a place where the attention is placed directly upon the food and how good it's supposed to taste. This is a very small restaurant and I would not recommend it for large groups. Be sure to call ahead and get a reservation.
