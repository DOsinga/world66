We found this great place after seeing it on the Food Network. When you
walk in it looks like you are going back in time to an old trailer
park. Lots of Elvis stuff and even a trailer. Good food that was served
up fast with sweet potato fries. I myself am not a fan of sweet
potatoes, but these were really good. Beer from a can or a few on tap.
Typical American foods like burgers, dogs, grilled cheese. Inexpensive
fun place to eat. You'll probably want to bring your camera.
