New York , NY , 10471 Overlooking the Hudson River and housed in the Hebrew Home for the Aged, the Judaica Museum recounts 150 years of Jewish history and includes some 800 artifacts that represent the traditional Jewish ceremonies held in synagogues and homes. The museum offers a variety of cultural and educational programs including films, a library, lectures and courses.
