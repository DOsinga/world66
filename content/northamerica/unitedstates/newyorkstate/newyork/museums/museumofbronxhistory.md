New York , NY , 10467 The Museum of Bronx History is located in the Valentine-Varian House which was built in 1758 by a blacksmith. The fieldstone house is said to be the second oldest house in the borough. There are exhibits depicting life in 18th century Bronx as well as a display on how the area grew. Guided tours are available by the Historical Society.
