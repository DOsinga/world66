The Neue Galerie is a German and Austrian art museum focusing on fine and decorative arts from the early twentieth century. Featured here are works from Gustav Klimt, Egon Schiele and Otto Beckmann. It's located near the Met along Museum Mile in a beautiful mansion along Fifth Avenue. Located inside is the Cafe Sabarsky, which serves delicious Austrian cuisine. The coffee is arguably one of the best in New York, which goes along wonderfully with their apple strudel.
