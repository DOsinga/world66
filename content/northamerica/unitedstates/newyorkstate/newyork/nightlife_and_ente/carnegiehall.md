Musicians have been dreaming about Carnegie Hall since 1891, and now, during your visit to New York, you can find out why. Legendary performers such as Ella Fitzgerald, the Beatles, Frank Sinatra, Bob Dylan, and Duke Ellington have played here as well as many of the world's leading orchestras and solo performers. Carnegie Hall houses a 2,804-seat auditorium, the 599-seat Zankel Hall, and the Rose Museum.
