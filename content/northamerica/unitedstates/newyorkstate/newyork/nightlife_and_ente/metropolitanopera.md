Founded in 1883, The Metropolitan Opera recently celebrated its 125th Anniversary. Currently, the Metropolitan Opera has six new productions scheduled and 22 revivals. The Metropolitan Opera is located on the Upper West Side of Manhattan, between West 62nd and 65th Streets and Columbus and Amsterdam Avenues.
