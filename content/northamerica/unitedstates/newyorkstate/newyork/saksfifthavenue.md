The flagship Saks has occupied this spot across from Rockefeller Center since 1924. Ten floors of designer fashion, cosmetics, and accessories, anchored by an impressive shoe department on the eighth floor. The holiday window displays along Fifth Avenue draw enormous crowds each December. Even if you're not buying, the art deco building and the sheer scale of the place are worth a look.
