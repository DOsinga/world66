New York has probably the best shopping opportunities in the world. So if you are in the right mood for it, take your credit card and then shop until you drop! 

**Shops**

**Macy's Herald Square**

This turn of the century department store stands as icon for shoppers around the globe. The World's Largest Department Store! It takes up an entire city block and shopping can last for hours.

address: Broadway at 34th Street

tel: 212.695.4400

**SAKS FIFTH AVENUE **

Elegant shopping to the extreme. This department store offers a wonderful array of prestigious boutiques featuring top name designers. A definite experience for shoppers of all skill levels. 

address: Fifth Avenue at 49th Street

tel: 212.753.4000

**9th Street Shops and more**
