If you go East from Broadway you will head into New Yorks own China Town. Pseudo-pagodas crowning the roofsm bright banners lining the streets, and temple bells help create the aura of actually being right in China. The architectgure, people, and food help create a traditional Chinese atmosphere in midst of a busy city.
