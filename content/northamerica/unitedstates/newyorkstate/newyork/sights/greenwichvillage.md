Going north on Broadway you will pass by Greenwich Village, a collection of many small villages. Todays village residents are primarily business people who are drawn by the small town neighborness and closeness to the city. You'll see clusters of old homes which have been renovated. This part of the village is home to the cities Italian population. In order to successfully navigate you will need a detailed map. It will be comforting to find that the villagers are friendly and willing to help you.
