**Queensbury** is a town in the foothills of the Adirondacks in upstate New York, just north of Glens Falls and at the southern tip of Lake George. It is the gateway to the Adirondack Park and a common stopping point for travellers heading into the mountains.

The town is known for the Adirondack Balloon Festival each September, when dozens of hot air balloons launch from Floyd Bennett Memorial Airport. The Great Escape, a large amusement park, is a popular family destination. Lake George, a few minutes north, offers swimming, boating, and lakeside dining in summer.
