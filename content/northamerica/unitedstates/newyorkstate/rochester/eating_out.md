### [Nick Tahou's](/northamerica/unitedstates/newyorkstate/rochester/eating_out/nick_tahous)

Come across almost any native Rochesterian, and there may be a reference to Nick Tahou's, a local eatery that has an original concoction, referred to as the *Garbage Plate*.

A gastronomic feat for almost anyone, and not recommended for the peckish, the Garbage Plate features a heap of macaroni salad, home fries (potatos), and baked beans, topped off with two hamburgers or two hot dogs (chopped up) and smothered in texas style hot sauce.

### [California Rollin' Sushi Bar](/northamerica/unitedstates/newyorkstate/rochester/eating_out/california_rollin)

Sushi bar serving a large variety of sushi that will satisfy experienced sushi-eaters AND first-timers. One can find authentic raw fish dishes as well as cooked sushi dishes here. Innovative dessert sushi is a must-try.

Close to the Memorial Art Gallery; offers special deals on Sundays.

type: Japanese



accessibility: high

tel: 585-271-8990

address: 274 Goodman St, Rochester, NY 14620

PriceOfMenu: 5-15
