### [Regional Transit Service (part of the RGRTA)](/northamerica/unitedstates/newyorkstate/rochester/getting_around/regional_transit_s)

For those that don't travel with a rental car, the **Regional Transit Service** (RTS) may be right for you!

Service operates daily on a majority of the city routes, with appropriately adjusted weekend service. Weekend city service is about every 45 minutes, from earlier than 7:00am and runs into the late hours until after midnight.

Most routes operate from Downtown Rochester, on Main Street between Clinton and St. Paul streets. A number of other routes (park and ride/suburban) operate from the Midtown Plaza bus [more..](/northamerica/unitedstates/newyorkstate/rochester/getting_around/regional_transit_s)



tel: 585-288-1700

### [Greater Rochester International Airport](/northamerica/unitedstates/newyorkstate/rochester/getting_around/monroe_county_airp)

Formerly known as the Monroe County Airport, the Greater Rochester International Airport (GRIA) provides the utmost
in air travel services to accommodate your individual needs.

The GRIA features a modern two-story terminal, enclosed parking
garage, and two-level roadway with separate avenues for arrivals and
departures. It is a medium hub airport which handles 220 flights per
day to over 20 cities (22 airports), serves more than 2.5 million
passengers annually, and houses the operations of 16 air transportation
providers. The terminal features artworks and [more..](/northamerica/unitedstates/newyorkstate/rochester/getting_around/monroe_county_airp)



url: www.rocairport.com
