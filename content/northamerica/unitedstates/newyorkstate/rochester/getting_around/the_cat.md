Unfortunately, the new authorities in Rochester 's
mayoral offices do not have the foresight to see what the "Fast Ferry"
project would eventually mean to the city. Therefore, the open link between Rochester ,
NY and one of the world's greatest cities, Toronto ,
Ontario CA, has been severed. This is a sad day for the
city of Rochester .
