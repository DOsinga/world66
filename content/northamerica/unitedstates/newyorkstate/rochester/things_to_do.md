Rochester tour Company offers guided tours of Rochester.

Walking and motorized tours begin at 11 AM and last about three hours.

Start: One Bausch and Lomb Circle, North of Washington Square Park, next to GeVa Theater, Rochester, NY 14604.

Tour guide takes visitors to Susan B Anthony's, Frederick Douglass', George Eastman's and Maud (Humphrey) Bogart's houses. View Genesee River and Erie Canal, Winston Churchill's mother's site and more.
