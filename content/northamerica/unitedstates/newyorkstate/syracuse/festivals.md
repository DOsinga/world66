Syracuse hosts the New York State Fair amoung many other festivals throughout the year.

**all**

**Westcott Street Cultural Fair**

Westcott Street's Cultural Fair emphasises unity in this diverse community. Many people attend. There are crafts, games, and food. The fair itself, doesn't cost a thing. In 2004, the fair will be on September 19th. 

address: Westcott St.

date: 3rd Sunday in September

**New York State Fair**

New York State's Fair is located at the fairgrounds during the last few weeks of August.

address: New York State Fair Grounds
