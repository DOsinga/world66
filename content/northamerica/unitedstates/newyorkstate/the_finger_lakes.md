The best resource for sights, hotels, restaurants, bars, what to do and see

The Finger Lakes region of New York is truly a beautiful one. The
region is named for the eleven long, thin lakes that were carved by
glaciers long ago. It boasts a rich history involving Mark Twain, the
struggle for women's rights, aviation, and more. A number of the lakes
are also well known for growing grapes and wine making.

For more information check out http://www.fingerlakes.org or http://visitfingerlakes.com
