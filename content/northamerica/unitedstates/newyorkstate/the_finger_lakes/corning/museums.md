### [Rockwell Museum of Western Art](/northamerica/unitedstates/newyorkstate/the_finger_lakes/corning/museums/rockwell_museum_of)

Are you a Western history buff? You will find a large collection of original paintings and bronze sculptures dedicated to the Old West by renowned artist Frank Remington and Charles Russell. The museum also has a large collection of Native American art. There is a small giftshop located on the first floor, and the museum is generally not too busy or overcrowed.

### [Corning Museum of Glass](/northamerica/unitedstates/newyorkstate/the_finger_lakes/corning/museums/corning_museum_of)

The Corning Museum of Glass in Corning, NY, is part of the long-standing glass tradition of the region. The museum has the largest glass collection in the world (more than 45,000 pieces from 35 centuries), a Hot Glass Show where gaffers create glass objects in front of live audiences, a Walk-in Workshop where visitors can make their own glass creations, and the chance to see factory workers creating glass from a sand mixture, blowing it into shape, and then refining it on a spinning wheel.
