Boone is a popular destination in the North Carolina mountains, drawing visitors in winter for skiing, in summer for the pleasant temperatures, and in autumn for spectacular leaf color along the Blue Ridge Parkway. Home to Appalachian State University, the town has a youthful energy and more restaurants and shops than you would expect for its size.
