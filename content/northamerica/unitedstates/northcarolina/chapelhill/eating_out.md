---
title: 'Eating Out in Chapel Hill'
type: section
---

Chapel Hill has one of the highest numbers of restaurants per capita in North Carolina. The best places to eat are found along Franklin Street, the town's main drag. Many cater to the student population, but there are plenty of options for those willing to spend a bit more.

**Strong's Coffee** and the **Carolina Coffee Shop** are both worth a stop for a drink. **McAlister's** does good sandwiches for a quick lunch. For Middle Eastern and Greek food, both **Aladdin's** and the **Mediterranean Deli** are reliable choices.

Nearby Carrboro also has excellent restaurants and coffee shops -- it is a short walk from Franklin Street and worth exploring.
