---
title: 'Nightlife and Entertainment in Charlotte'
type: section
---

Charlotte's nightlife is concentrated in a few walkable areas. **Tryon Street** downtown has a mix of bars, restaurants, and live music venues. The **South End** district, connected by the LYNX light rail, is popular with young professionals and has a steady rotation of new restaurants and breweries opening alongside established favorites.

The **NoDa** (North Davidson) arts district is the more bohemian option, with dive bars, live music venues, and galleries that stay open late. If you are looking for something more polished, the **Epicentre** complex downtown has upscale bars and clubs.

Charlotte also has a strong live music scene. The **Blumenthal Performing Arts Center** hosts touring Broadway shows and concerts, while smaller venues in NoDa and Plaza Midwood book local and regional acts most nights of the week.
