Charlotte is the largest city in the state of North Carolina, making sure that there a plenty of things to do and see. You'll find a list of things to do below.

### [Sea Life Aquarium Charlotte- Concord](/northamerica/unitedstates/northcarolina/charlotte/things_to_do/sea_life_aquarium)

SEA LIFE Charlotte-Concord Aquarium will transport you into the amazing underwater world. Brush noses with sharks and prepare for astonishingly close views of everything from humble starfish and seahorses to graceful rays. To get any closer you’d have to get wet!
