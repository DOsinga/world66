---
title: 'Things to Do in Charlotte'
type: section
---

Charlotte is the largest city in North Carolina, and there is plenty to keep you busy.

The **US National Whitewater Center** on the Catawba River offers whitewater rafting, kayaking, rock climbing, zip lines, and mountain biking -- all within city limits. It is one of the most unusual urban outdoor facilities in the country and worth a visit even if you just want to watch from the restaurant patio.

**Freedom Park** is the city's most popular green space, with a lake, walking trails, and summer concerts. The nearby **Mint Museum Randolph** houses an impressive collection of American, European, and pre-Columbian art.

Charlotte is NASCAR country. Many racing teams are headquartered in and around the city, and the **Charlotte Motor Speedway** in nearby Concord hosts major races. Several race shops offer tours -- the **Hendrick Motorsports Museum** is free and open to the public on weekdays.

The **SEA LIFE Charlotte-Concord Aquarium** at Concord Mills is a good family outing, with sharks, rays, seahorses, and interactive touch pools. The adjacent shopping complex is one of the largest in the Southeast.
