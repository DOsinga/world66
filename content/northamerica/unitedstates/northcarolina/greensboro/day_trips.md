---
title: 'Day Trips from Greensboro'
type: section
---

**Hanging Rock State Park** -- An hour and a half north of Greensboro, Hanging Rock offers rocky mountain trails, boating, fishing, and swimming. Rowboats and canoes are available to rent (no private boats). The hiking is excellent but wear proper footwear -- the trails are rocky and uneven. The Exhibit Hall in the Visitors Complex covers the area's cultural and environmental history.

**Mount Airy** -- Just over an hour northwest, Mount Airy is the town where Andy Griffith grew up and the inspiration for Mayberry. Whether or not you are a fan of the show, the small-town charm is genuine. Main Street has quaint shops and cafes, and the mountain backdrop is beautiful. A good trip for anyone who wants a relaxed day in a classic Southern small town.

**Old Stone Winery** -- About an hour south in [Salisbury](/northamerica/unitedstates/northcarolina/salisbury), this working winery grows its own grapes and offers tastings and tours. Tastings include a souvenir glass. Open Tuesday through Saturday from 11 AM to 6 PM, Sunday from 1 PM to 5 PM, closed Mondays.
