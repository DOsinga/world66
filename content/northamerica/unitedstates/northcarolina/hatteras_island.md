Hatteras Island is the destination for vacationers in search of surfing, fishing, camping, birdwatching, beachcombing, or simply watching the sunset with family and friends. Part of the [Outer Banks](/northamerica/unitedstates/northcarolina/outerbanks), this long, narrow island is wilder and less developed than the northern beaches, with small villages separated by stretches of open dune and marsh.

The **Cape Hatteras Lighthouse**, at 198 feet, is the tallest brick lighthouse in the United States. You can climb to the top in season for views that stretch across the island from ocean to sound. The fishing -- both surf casting and offshore charter trips -- is some of the best on the East Coast, and the consistent wind and waves make Hatteras one of the premier kiteboarding and windsurfing spots in North America.
