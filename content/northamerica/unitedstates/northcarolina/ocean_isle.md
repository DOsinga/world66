Ocean Isle Beach is a quiet, unspoiled family beach on the southern North Carolina coast, just north of [Myrtle Beach](/northamerica/unitedstates/southcarolina/myrtlebeach). The island has managed to avoid the heavy commercial development of its neighbours, making it a good choice for families looking for a peaceful week by the water.

The beach itself is wide and uncrowded, with gentle surf suitable for younger swimmers. Most visitors rent a house for the week and settle into the rhythm of the island — mornings on the sand, afternoons fishing off the pier, evenings on the porch. There are a handful of restaurants and shops along the causeway, but this is not a place you come to for nightlife.
