Old Fort is a small mountain town in [McDowell County](/northamerica/unitedstates/northcarolina/mcdowell_county), sitting at the eastern gateway to the North Carolina mountains.

**Andrew's Geyser** is a few minutes from the center of town. The train station houses a small museum, and the **Mountain Gateway Museum** down the street offers a view of Appalachian history. **Talis Gallery** in the Appalachian Artisan Society Gallery sells eclectic art, beads, and photography. **Turtle Creek Pottery** is a short drive up Bat Cave Road.

Reconstruction of **Fort Davidson**, a pioneer-era fort, is underway and hosts an annual Civil War event in early summer. On Friday nights, **Old Fort Mountain Music** offers free country music -- you can jam, dance, or just listen.
