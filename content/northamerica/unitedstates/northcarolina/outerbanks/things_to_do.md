---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

The Outer Banks rewards those who want to be outside. The Wright Brothers National Memorial at Kill Devil Hills marks the site of the first powered flight in 1903. Cape Hatteras Lighthouse is the tallest brick lighthouse in America and one of the most recognisable on the Atlantic coast. The beaches themselves — wide, dune-backed, and relatively uncrowded — are the main draw, along with excellent surfing at Hatteras and sport fishing year-round.
