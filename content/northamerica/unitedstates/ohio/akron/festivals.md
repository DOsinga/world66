The National Hamburger Festival / June 21st and 22nd 2007

A bit of a promo piece for the upcoming festival but I wanted to concentrate on the first year, 2006. Many places lay claim to the hamburger, Wisconsin, Texas, Connecticut, Akron and Hamburg, NY from my stomping grounds. This is a cool festival that's going into it's second year. Hamburger joints from all over the country descend upon Akron, OH to show their wares and fill you up with MEAT. The entertainment last year was provided by your truly and my band Pirate Dreams (www.piratedreams.com). Why Akron, OH you may ask? Well, the story I personally subscribe to is that the hamburger was created many years ago by a stand at The Erie County Fair in Hamburg, NY by a vendor from Akron, OH after they ran out of sausage at their stand. They made a new sandwich out of ground beef and named the new concoction after the city they were in. This is the only story that accounts for the name.

Anyways, great festival. Take a trip out to Akron in June. It's defintiely worth it.

### [Lock 3 live](/northamerica/unitedstates/ohio/akron/festivals/lock_3_live)

Another fun festival is the lock 3 live series that happens all summer long. It's basically a string of great bands all summer long that play in a cozy little pavilion set up at one of the old canal locks. You can find some great live music and good local food on the warm summer nights here.
