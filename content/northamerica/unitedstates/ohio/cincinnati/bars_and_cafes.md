---
title: "Bars and Cafes"
type: section
---

Cincinnati's best nightlife is in Over the Rhine. Main Street in this neighbourhood has a dense run of bars, live music venues, and coffeehouses. The Cincinnati Symphony and Cincinnati Opera both perform at the ornate Music Hall at 1243 Elm Street — one of the finest concert halls in the country, worth attending regardless of what's on. The Main Street Entertainment District, centred on 1000 Main Street, has bars open until 2:30am.
