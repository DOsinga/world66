---
title: "Museums in Cincinnati"
type: section
---

If you are an art lover, one of your Cincinnati activities while on vacation should be a trip to the Cincinnati Art Museum at 953 Eden Park Drive. In addition to showing many traveling exhibits and collections yearly, the Cincinnati Art Museum is home to over 55,000 pieces of art. The art is group into categories such as Far Eastern Art, Art of Africa, and Photographs. An example of an exhibition subject is the Alfa Romeo car. The general admission is free and the museum is open six days a week. For directions and hours, visit their website at cincinnatiartmuseum.org.
