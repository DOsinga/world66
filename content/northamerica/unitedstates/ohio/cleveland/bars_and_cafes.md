---
title: "Bars and Cafes"
type: section
---

Cleveland's bar scene is spread across distinct neighborhoods, each with its own character. The Warehouse District on W.6th has upscale cocktail bars. Ohio City — centered on the West Side Market — is where Great Lakes Brewing anchors a more laid-back strip. Tremont has dive bars and art-crowd haunts. Coventry Village in Cleveland Heights draws the college crowd with coffee shops and the Grog Shop music venue. Little Italy is better for early evening than late night.
