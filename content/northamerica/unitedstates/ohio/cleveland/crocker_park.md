One of Cleveland's newest shopping centers, serving the affluent west side suburbs. An outdoor main-street center in Westlake off I-90 with retailers like H&M, Urban Outfitters, Coach, J. Crew, and Banana Republic, alongside boutiques unique to Cleveland. Restaurants and a movie theater round out the mix.
