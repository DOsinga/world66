title: 'Festivals'
type: section
---

Cleveland's rich history and diversity support many festivals throughout the year. There is a good chance something will be going on when you visit, and a festival of interest might help you decide which weekend to come.

### St. Patrick's Day Parade

One of the largest St. Patrick's Day parades in the country and one of the most authentic Irish American festivals. Cleveland has the Irish American population to support it, and the parade is a favorite annual event for all ages. Festivities start early downtown, and every bar and pub gets packed wall to wall.

### Tri-C Jazz Festival

A long-running and well-respected annual jazz festival featuring legends as well as younger musicians. Performances take place throughout the week at the end of April at various venues.
