Because Cleveland has a rich history and diversity, it is able to support many festivals throughout the year. Cleveland festivals draw large crowds from throughout the region and even from outside the region, but Cleveland festivals are not overly touristy. A good chance exists that some kind of festival will be going on when you are in town, or a festival of interest might help you decide what weekend to come to Cleveland for a visit.

### [St. Patrick's Day Parade](/northamerica/unitedstates/ohio/cleveland/festivals/st_patricks_day)

The Cleveland St. Patrick's Day Parade has to be one of the largest St. Patrick's Day parades in the country, and one of the most authentic Irish American festivals in the country. Cleveland certainly has the Irish American population to support such a great parade, and it is a favorite annual event for all ages and people from all over town. Festivities start very early downtown and all bars and pubs downtown get packed wall to wall.

### [Tri-C Jazz Festival](/northamerica/unitedstates/ohio/cleveland/festivals/tric_jazz_festiva)

This is a long running and well respected annual jazz festival that features jazz legends as well as younger jazz musicians. A variety of performances take place throughout the week at the end of April.
