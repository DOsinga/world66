In April 2006 Cleveland RTA created two complimentary circulator bus routes that act like and even resemble trolleys exclusive for downtown Cleveland. This is a great way to get around downtown from one sight to another and to explore a little bit of downtown worry-free. This is not a sight-seeing tour trolley bus. The drivers do not narrate their routes like sight-seeing tours, and these trolley bus lines do not go to other parts of the city where many worthy sights are to be found. The drivers however, are equipped with knowledge of downtown for visitors with practical inquiries. The buses run regularly and pick up at stops every ten minutes. The buses themselves are easily identifiable from other buses by having a more traditional look to them and painted forest green. The interiors are very pleasent and detailed with many upgrades unusual of other public buses. These trolley buses are fare-free for an indefinate time period.
