Cleveland Hopkins International Airport is on the West Side of
Cleveland, and is one of the oldest international airports in the
United States. It is a Continental Airlines hub, and also served
extensively by Southwest Airlines and others. The airport links
directly to Tower City downtown by train at the bargain price of $1.50.
