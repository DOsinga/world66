Starting in April 2006 the British company Megabus began providing low fare intercity express bus service in the Midwest. Chicago serves as the hub of this intercity bus service. The trip between Cleveland and Chicago takes aproximately six hours, and fares start at $1. The Megabus stop in Cleveland is adjacent to the Tower City complex in the center of Cleveland on Public Square. From the Megabus stop you can walk to many of Cleveland's fine restaurants, attractions and hotels, or hook up with the county-wide transit system's central hub in the lower levels of Tower City.
