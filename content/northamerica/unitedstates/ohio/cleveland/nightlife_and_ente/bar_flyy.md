This dance club hit Cleveland's Warehouse District in the summer of 2005, and quickly became a favorite. This multi-level club with a retro hipster atmosphere and thumping beats helps to slightly set Cleveland apart from other national nightlife centers. Bar Flyy is easily the one dance club to visit when in town, and enjoys a desireable location on W.6th in the Warehouse District.
