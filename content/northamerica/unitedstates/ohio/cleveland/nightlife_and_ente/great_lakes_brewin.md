Known for award-winning beer brewed on-site with strict adherence to high-quality organic ingredients. Food items are also grown on-site in their own greenhouse. The pub has an antique bar that Eliot Ness himself once frequented, a stone-wall cellar bar, a casual but classy restaurant, and a large brick patio on Market Avenue with views of the West Side Market and the gothic spires of St. Ignatius High School. Tour the brewery, visit the gift shop, and pick up a Cleveland souvenir. Great Lakes proves that America can make great beer.
