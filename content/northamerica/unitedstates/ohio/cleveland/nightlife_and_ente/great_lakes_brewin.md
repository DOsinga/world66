Great Lakes Brewing Company is known for its award winning brew and strict adherence to high quality organic ingredients. The beer is brewed on site and the indgredients of food items are also grown on site in their own greenhouse. This is a great place to go when in town for great beer, great food, and great atmosphere. No trip to Cleveland or Ohio City would be complete without a stop, and you might want to consider taking a tour of the brewery. There is a nice gift shop available here and the perfect place to pick up a Cleveland souvenir. Great Lakes Company proves to Belgians, Austrians and Germans that America can make great beer; only in Cleveland! Great lakes has an excellent pub section with an antique bar that Elliot Ness himself had gone to, a cool stone-wall cellar bar, a casual but classy restaurant section, and a large brick patio out front on Market Avenue with views of the West Side Market on Market Square and the gothic spires of St. Ignatius High School.
