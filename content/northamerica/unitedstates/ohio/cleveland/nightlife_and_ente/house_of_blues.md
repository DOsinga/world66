Cleveland is home to a House of Blues venue. House of Blues features up-and-coming artists as well as more established artists in the music scene. The variety of artists, intimate venue, bars, restaurant component, and unique quality of each indvidual venue is what House of Blues is known for. The Cleveland venue is the anchor of an emerging entertainment district downtown on E.4th and Euclid in a converted furniture store building. Since its openning in late 2004 it has quickly become a Cleveland hotspot, and was picked by basketball superstar Lebron James as the spot for his 21st birthday bash.
