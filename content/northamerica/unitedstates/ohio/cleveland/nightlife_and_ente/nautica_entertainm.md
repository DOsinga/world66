One of the largest entertainment complexes in the country. Major concerts, comedy, dancing, pool, restaurants, and trolley tours are packed into an old factory building nicknamed "the Powerhouse" on the west bank of the Flats. Excellent views of the Cuyahoga River and the Cleveland skyline.
