This performing arts center is a Cleveland institution. It features various forms of theater, ballet, opera, and occasionaly classic films in beautiful lavish theaters typical of the early twentieth century. Playhouse Square uses five theaters: the Palace, State, Ohio, Allen, and Hanna near eachother on Euclid Avenue around the intersection of E.14th. Playhouse Square is just a ten minute walk from Public Square. This is believed to be the second largest theater district in the country after Broadway in New York City, so if you enjoy theater you should seriously consider getting tickets for a show here.
