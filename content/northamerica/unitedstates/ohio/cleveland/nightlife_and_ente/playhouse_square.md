A Cleveland institution featuring theater, ballet, opera, and classic films in five lavish early twentieth-century theaters: the Palace, State, Ohio, Allen, and Hanna on Euclid Avenue around E. 14th. A ten-minute walk from Public Square and believed to be the second-largest theater district in the country after Broadway.
