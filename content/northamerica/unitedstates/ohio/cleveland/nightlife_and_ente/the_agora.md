The Agora is one of Cleveland's and the country's finest rock concert venues. It has been a Cleveland institution for over forty years, and hosted many rock legends. The Agora has received many awards and high rankings by national reviewers. It is one of the few venues that set the standard for rock venues across the globe. For rock and roll enthusiasts in town visiting the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame, this is probably your best choice for live rock music in the city given the historic nature of this venue and the prestige of its lineup. It is located between downtown and University Circle on Euclid Avenue. It is too far to walk from downtown, and you really should take a cab or the bus if you don't have a car.
