The predecessor to the modern suburban shopping center, completed in 1929 as the gateway to Shaker Heights. Beautiful Georgian architecture with the Shaker Rapid running through the center. The retail mix goes through cycles, but the historic quality always remains strong. Boutiques, restaurants, and a movie theater. Easily accessible by Rapid from Tower City.
