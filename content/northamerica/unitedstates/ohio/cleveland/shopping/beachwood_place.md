Beachwood Place has a very upscale niche and is located in Beachwood off I-271. Beachwood Place is considered the premier shopping center in Northeast Ohio, and serves Cleveland's extremely affluent east side suburbs. It carries notable retailers like Nordstroms, Saks Fifth Avenue, Guess?, Cache, BCBG, Pottery Barn, Arden B. and many of the same found at Crocker Park. There are a number of boutiques located in this shopping center like the very trendy Nabici Collection. Beachwood Place has been a mainstay for a long time, but continues to expand with new shops and restaurants every year.
