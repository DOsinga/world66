This relatively new shopping center in Northeast Ohio is often overlooked, but it is a teriffic combination of good quality shops and restaurants with an outdoor streetscape component as well as an indoor atrium component. It is located off I-271 in Woodmere and very near Beachwood Place and Legacy Village, and on the way to Chagrin Falls.
