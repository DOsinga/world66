This is an exclusive low-volume boutique in Cleveland's downtown Warehouse District carrying designer fashions. The apparel matches the cutting-edge high energy of Cleveland's Warehouse District. It is a good example and catalyst for Cleveland's revolution as a trend-setting city in the world of fashion. It features apparel of local designers as well as top international labels. Brigade has some exclusive rights on sales in Ohio on some of the labels they carry. This boutique is open until 8:00pm most days and is worth stopping in while frequenting the Warehouse District.
