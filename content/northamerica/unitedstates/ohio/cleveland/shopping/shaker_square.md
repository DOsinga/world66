Shaker Square is the predecessor to the modern day suburban shopping center. It was completed in 1929 and serves as the gateway to one of Cleveland's and the country's most distinguished and historic suburbs; Shaker Heights. Its architecture is beautiful Georgian, and the square's setting is very picturesque with the Shaker Rapid running through the center of the square. The Rapid stop at Shaker Square makes it easily accessible from Tower City for visitors. Shaker Square goes through cycles of a fine and poor retail mix, but the historic quality and beauty always remains strong. Boutiques, restaurants and a movie theater make it an enjoyable and unique shopping experience.
