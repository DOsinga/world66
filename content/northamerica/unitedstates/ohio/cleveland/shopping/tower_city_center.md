The Avenue at Tower City has a combination of major retailers, odd shops, and an excellent foodcourt. The Avenue is the retail component of the Tower City Center. Tower City is in the heart of downtown on Public Square and connected to the city's signature skyscraper Terminal Tower, central transit hub, hotels, movie theater, restaurants, and walkways to sport complexes. Parking in Tower City garages are very inexpensive with validation from customer service with the purchase of goods.
