---
title: 'Civic Center'
type: poi
---

A fine example of an American city civic center, with local, county, and federal government buildings. The mall at its center has large grassy areas, fountains, benches, and views of Lake Erie, surrounded by beautiful neoclassical civic buildings and the Convention Center. A nice spot for a picnic lunch or photos. Located between Lakeside and Rockwell Avenue along E. 6th St.
