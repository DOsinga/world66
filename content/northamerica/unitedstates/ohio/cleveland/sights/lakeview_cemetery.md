More famous people are buried here than anywhere else in Cleveland: industrialist John D. Rockefeller, President James A. Garfield, and law enforcement agent Eliot Ness, among many politicians, industrialists, and sports figures. A beautiful garden cemetery surrounded by University Circle, Little Italy, and Coventry Village.
