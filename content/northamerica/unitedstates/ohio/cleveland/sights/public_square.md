The heart of downtown Cleveland, a park intersected by Ontario and Superior Avenues. Fountains, benches, and trees make it popular with downtown workers at lunchtime and a go-to spot for photos. Anchored by the city's three tallest buildings: Terminal Tower, Key Tower, and the BP Building. The square is nearly as old as the city itself.
