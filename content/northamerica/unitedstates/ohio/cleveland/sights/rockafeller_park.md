Cleveland's central park, donated by John D. Rockefeller. It stretches between Lake Erie and University Circle along Martin Luther King Boulevard, winding under romantic stone bridges. The park contains trails, a lagoon, a greenhouse, and the Cultural Gardens, where every ethnic group in Cleveland's history has a dedicated garden.
