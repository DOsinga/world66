Cleveland's beautiful Gothic Catholic cathedral, a landmark on E. 9th Street in the financial district. The current structure was completed in the late 1940s with a warm sandstone facade and a rich interior of deep blue, gold, red, and dark wood. The stained glass windows and celestial scene on the ceiling are particularly beautiful. Peaceful chapels and the relics of the Roman Christian martyr St. Christine are on display. Open throughout the day.
