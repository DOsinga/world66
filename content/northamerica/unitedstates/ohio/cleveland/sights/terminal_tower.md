The city's signature skyscraper, completed in 1929 and once one of the tallest buildings in the world. Built in neoclassical style when most skyscrapers of the era were Art Deco, making it highly unique and identifiable. It anchors Public Square and the Tower City Center complex.

Tower City is a large indoor complex housing shops, office towers, the city's two most prestigious hotels, restaurants, a food court, indoor walkways to sports venues, a movie theater, and the city's transit hub, all connected by atriums, fountains, and marble. An excellent place to start your visit.
