The geographic and symbolic heart of downtown Cleveland. This park at the crossing of Ontario and Superior Avenues has fountains, benches, and trees, framed by Terminal Tower, Key Tower, and the BP Building. A popular lunch spot for office workers and a natural orientation point for visitors. Clevelanders have defended the square's existence through multiple redevelopment threats — it's a point of local pride.
