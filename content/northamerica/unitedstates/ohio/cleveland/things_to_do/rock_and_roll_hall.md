The number one tourist draw in Cleveland, and deservedly so. Since opening in 1995 in an I.M. Pei-designed building on the lakefront, it's housed the most extensive public collection of rock and roll memorabilia anywhere. The exhibits are well-curated, the cinema presentations pack a punch, and the gift shop stocks genuinely hard-to-find items. Even if you think you're not that interested, give it a few hours.
