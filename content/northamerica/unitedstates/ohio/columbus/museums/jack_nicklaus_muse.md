Opened in May 2002 and located on the campus of The Ohio State University, the museum exhibits memorabilia of Jack's successful golf career and highlights his contributions to the history of the game. Photos and memorabilia chronicle his life from growing up in Columbus, attending The Ohio State University, to his successful career on the PGA Tour and later the Senior PGA Tour. Included are trophies from Nicklaus' record 18 major championships.
