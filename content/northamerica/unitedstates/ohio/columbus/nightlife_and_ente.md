---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment in Columbus"
type: section
---

If you are looking for a great time, the best way is to seek out the places of the **Columbus Nightlife **scene. The premiere aspect of the city's nightlife is the diversity of activities and places to attend. Whether you enjoy a nice night out to the theatre or are looking for a wild dancing nightclub, Columbus has it all right here for you.

#### Cloak and Dagger Dinner Theatre

This is top-rated evening establishment where you can have an excellent meal while being entertained at the same time. The performances are changing all the time. The shows are divided among mysteries, comedies and tragedy. The meals served here are superb, and each course is given at just the right time during the show. The price for the dinner and show is a comfortable $40 dollars.

#### Wexner Center

The Wexner Center is the place to go for a state-of-the-art entertainment site here in Columbus. The contemporary arts you can experience include visual art displays by well known as well as up-and-coming artists. You have the opportunity to view films and videos by past and present filmmakers from around the world. One of the major fun-filled aspects of the center's choices of exhibitions is that they tend to be unusual and a bit eccentric.

#### The Treehouse

This club is the hot-spot for Columbus's nightlife live music scene. The bar was built a few decades ago around a live silver maple tree. In 1999, it opened as a premiere downtown music club. The club always plays host to popular national bands as well as new local bands. You never know who is going to be showing up to play. On Wednesday nights they have Open Mic Night, starting at 9:30 pm.

#### Sundance Saloon and Dancehall

Under the Sundance Saloon's roof you will find three vibrant nightclubs. First on the list is Columbus's largest country venue. You will find line dancing and two-stepping here all night long. A long-time special event is their mechanical bull riding, which is enormously fun to do. Next is the section called Crawdaddy's. Here you can dance to the best of the top 40's dance songs from the 80's until the present. Last is their Little Nashville Recording Studio, which is a bar that has karaoke on Friday and Saturday nights.

#### Char-Bar

This is a nice neighborhood bar that is great place to check out if you want a peaceful evening out on the city.The atmosphere tends to put patrons at ease, as the staff is professional and it has an open patio. The drink prices are very reasonable, and there is a wide variety to choose from on the menu.
