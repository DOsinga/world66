Columbus is not the most exciting city in the United States, but there are some interesting sights.

**all**

**Ohio Historical Society**

The Ohio historical society museum gives a fine overview of the history of the state. Exhibits include an overview of the flora and fauna of the state, a comprehensive look at the various Native American civilizations with diagrams of major mound builder centers and lots of artifacts, and an exhibit chronicling the history of the state from 1803 to the 1970s. Outside, there is a recreation of the central square of a typical Ohio village. The buildings have unfortunately been closed since 2002 due to budget cuts, but you can still walk around the outside. There are many interactive exhibits for children. [more..](/northamerica/unitedstates/ohio/columbus/sights/ohio_historical_so)

**Columbus Museum of Art**

Not the most impressive art museum in the world, there are still some paintings of interest. The old masters gallery contains a Ruebens, and the collection of impressionists and post impressionists gives a pretty comprehensive overview of those movements. Displays range from the Italian Renaissance to Modern Art. There is a nice children's wing. [more..](/northamerica/unitedstates/ohio/columbus/sights/columbus_museum_of)

**James Thurber House**

This is a partially restored house from the late 19th century that James Thurber lived in while attending OSU. A self guided tour includes anecdotes from Thurber's time living there. The house is on a cul-de-sac that features several restored late nineteenth century houses, though none of the others are open to the public. [more..](/northamerica/unitedstates/ohio/columbus/sights/james_thurber_hous)

**Ohio Statehouse**

This impressive example of Greek Revival Architecture was built between 1839 and 1861 and has an interesting drum-shaped dome. Visitors are free to walk through much of the building and are able to peak into both the house and senate chambers. There is a small museum in the basement that traces the history of the building. [more..](/northamerica/unitedstates/ohio/columbus/sights/ohio_statehouse)
