---

Cuyahoga Valley National Park, located between [Cleveland](/northamerica/unitedstates/ohio/cleveland) and [Akron](/northamerica/unitedstates/ohio/akron), features a wide variety of natural, cultural, and historical resources. Visitors enjoy picnicking, hiking, bike trails, bridle trails, winter sports, golf, fishing, and ranger-guided programs.
