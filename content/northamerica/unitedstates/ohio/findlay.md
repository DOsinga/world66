The best resource for sights, hotels, restaurants, bars, what to do and see

City of approximately 40,000 people located about 45 miles south of Toledo and 35 miles north of Lima on I-75. Thanks to an United States Congressional declaration, Findlay is known as “ Flag City , USA ”. Home to the University of Findlay as well as a base of operations for the Marathon Oil Corporation (was the headquarters until the early 1990’s). If you want to know more about the wonderful City of Findlay , look it up on Wikipedia!
