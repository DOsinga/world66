Newark is
located in Central Ohio , about 40 miles east of Columbus .
This proximity to the capital makes Newark
attractive to visitors and residents. People in the area have access to all the
comforts of the big city (concerts, restaurants, sports, shopping, etc.) while
escaping the trappings of the big city. The birthplace of acclaimed jazz
artist, Jon Hendricks, the father of vocalese, and the National
Heisey Glass
Museum ,
Newark offers a little something
for everyone. Newark is the largest city and the county seat for Licking County.
