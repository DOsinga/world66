Travel Guide

Founded
as a bustling railroad junction in 1838, the Village of Obetz
today combines historic charm with prosperous business development
to create a vibrant center of commerce and resident community
in the heart of Central Ohio. The Village is undergoing a
major transformation and rapidly becoming one of the region's
most desirable destinations for new residents and corporate
citizens alike.

In 2003,
the Village of Obetz celebrated the 75th anniversary of its
incorporation, with festivities that commemorated this special
event. As we celebrate the rich legacy of our past, we also
remain committed to moving the Village forward as a top business
location and an excellent place to call home.
