---
title: "Shopping"
type: section
---

Ohio's Amish Country is best known for its handcrafted furniture — solid wood pieces made by local craftsmen, widely sold through showrooms along US Route 62. Quilts are the other signature product: Amish women produce intricate geometric quilts that are sold throughout the region. Berlin and Sugarcreek have the highest concentration of shops; farm auctions across the county offer a more authentic buying experience.
