---
title: "Shopping in Ohio's Amish Country"
type: section
---

**Ohio's Amish Country Shopping** experience will satisfy the
shopaholic in you. You can get your hands on home made quilts, furniture
and several unique furniture. This is the second-most-popular touristic
destination in Ohio and is well known for its shopping scene. This
place offers a fun an exciting shopping extravaganza. Read on to find
out more.

**55 West**

Located on the West Jackson
Street in Amish Country, 55 West is a great place to shop for gifts and
memorabilia. The store is spread over two floors and offers visitors a
wide range of items to choose from. It is open from 10:00 a.m. to 8:00
p.m. You can buy antique pieces and works of art from this store.
Paintings, sculptures and clay figures are displayed in the store.
Handmade jewelry is a popular item that is sold at 55 West. You will
also find several home décor items like pillow covers, drapes and lamps.

**Country
Essentials**

Enter this store and enjoy the fresh fragrance
that engulfs you at the entrance. Country Essentials in a well known
store located on Jackson St. Amish Country. The store sells the best
beauty products in town. All products at this store are homemade.
Lotions, bath shower gels and soaps take up the shelf space at this
store. The products are in high demand and this store will add freshness
to your shopping spree in Amish Country. The best-selling products are
the Spa Experience soaps and lotions.

**Homespun Treasure**

If
you have home décor in mind, then look no further. Homespun Treasure is
an exclusive home décor store located one mile from Walnut Creek in
Amish Country. It offers a wide selection of rugs, table mats, lights,
drapes and more. The unique vintage collection is graceful and full of
charm. The store also features art work that would fit perfectly in your
homes. The scented candle section will take your breath away.

**Berlin
Creek – Gallery**

The Berlin Creek – Gallery is an art lover’s
paradise. This gallery features works of art from famous local painters
as well as those from around the country. The paintings reflect the
Amish country landscape. There are several types of paintings displayed
in this gallery. From landscapes to portraits and wildlife, this place
has a whole range of art. If you are lucky, you will get the opportunity
to talk to the artists themselves. You can buy the paintings and they
will be delivered to your hotel. The working hours are from 10:00 a.m.
to 5:00 p.m.
**
Antique Mall**

The huge antique mall is located right in the center of Amish
Country. It spreads over two floors and features beautiful antique
pieces. The designs on the antique furniture are wonderful and the
glassware will mesmerize you with its elegance. There are several tools,
coins and other collectibles that this store offers to its visitors.
