---
title: "Things to do in Ohio's Amish Country"
type: section
---

The** Ohio's Amish Country things to do** list will guarantee you
all-around entertainment. Central Ohio is home to this beautiful tourist
destination called Amish Country. Experience the Amish culture and take
in the natural beauty of this place. Amish country has a lot to offer
to the visitors in terms of attractions. Read on to find out more.
**
Heritage
Center**

Discover the history and the culture of Amish country
at this Heritage Center. It is located on County Road in Berlin, Ohio.
The Center boasts of a 265-feet-long mural which narrates the story of
Amish Country's past. It talks about the trade and the artistic talent
of the region and gives you a glimpse into the lifestyle of the natives
of Amish Country. The working hours are from 9:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. The
in-house book store has an extensive range of books about the Amish
region. You can also buy handicrafts made in the region at this shop.

**Wine
Cellar**

Walk into the world of wines at the Wine Cellar.
Located on the Der Marktplatz, this winery features several
award-winning wines. Tours are available from 10:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m.
You will be taken on a winery tour and shown the process of wine making.
At the end of the tour, sample the various award-winning wines. The
guide shows you the right way to taste the wine and gives you a quick
lesson on spotting good quality wines. If you wish to buy any of those,
the Wine Cellar has a wine shop.
**
Coblentz Chocolate Company**

Imagine
your favorite dream of walking into a chocolate factory coming true.
The Coblentz is a family-run factory that specializes in making fine
quality chocolates and is located in Walnut Creek in Amish Country. The
factory offers a tour of the facility. You can actually view the
chocolate-making process and take in the spellbinding aroma of the warm
chocolate. The factory has a chocolate and candy store for the visitors
where you can sample and buy the candy of your choice.
**
Black
Diamond Golfing**

Amish Country offers a spectacular golf
course to the golfing enthusiasts. The Black Diamond is a highly popular
18-hole golf course in Amish County. The landscaping at this golf
course will blow you away. Lush green surroundings adorned with
waterfalls, ponds and lakes make for a great game of golf. Refreshments
are served at the cottages on the waterfront. The gold course is also
open on Sundays.

**Biblewalk**

Witness the Bible coming
to life at this wax museum in Amish Country. The tour lasts for 60
minutes and takes you through the life of Christ and the Old Testament.
The museum has life-size wax models and makes for a true spiritual
journey. The museum has more than 300 figures and portrays over 70
scenes. The museum is open from Monday to Saturday and the working hours
are between 10:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m.
