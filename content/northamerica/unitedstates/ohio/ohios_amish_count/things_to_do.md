---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

The main activity is exploring the back roads by car or bicycle and experiencing the working Amish farming landscape. The Amish Heritage Center in Berlin has a large mural depicting the history of the region. Cheese houses and farm stands are spread throughout the countryside. The Coblentz Chocolate Company in Walnut Creek is a long-running family factory that makes fudge and chocolate and welcomes visitors.
