**Ohio’s Amish Country must do’s** features many offerings for those visiting this North American town in Ohio. Amish Country offers tourists tours of the working lifestyle ad customs of the Amish. Learning about the simple lifestyle the Amish lead and how they get by is entertaining when visiting this Ohio area on vacation.

Guided Tours

Start Ohio’s Amish Country must do’s with a guided tour of the hard working Amish community. View the beauty of the scenery while watching locals make arts and crafts. The simplicity of the Amish lifestyle shines through the value of the items they make and provide an escape from the everyday bustle of city life. Tours of the Amish Country features adventures that take in the farms, factories, and restaurants. Amish tours offers a little bit of Amish culture with the different tours that include visitors on Amish customs and industries. Holiday tours offered at Christmas offer plenty of activities for the entire family.

Amish Door Village

Eat in a casual environment at the Amish Door Village featuring breakfast, lunch, and dinner. Dinner theatre offers comedy, concerts, and recreations of popular characters in Amish culture and religion. The cuisine at the Amish Door Village includes real home cooking with roast beef slow-cooked, and roasted chicken. Enjoy ice cream during the summer months and shop at the Marketplace general store for treats to take home.

Broad Run Cheesehouse & Swiss Heritage Winery

Tour the working Swiss cheese factory and winery for one of Ohio’s Amish Country must do’s for entertainment. Watch the cheese making process from visiting the cows to making the cheese. Purchase samples for a taste of the exquisite cheese and wine at the wine counter. The gift shop provides the factory’s best recipes for purchase. Tours last about fifteen to twenty minutes while visiting the Broad Run.

Ashtabula County Covered Bridges

Ashtabula County is one of the most interesting of Ohio’s Amish Country must do’s with eighteen covered bridges, according to Ohio Amish Country Marketing. If you enjoy taking pictures of covered bridges and architecture, seek out the many bridges available in Ashtabula County. With the oldest bridge dating for any age while visiting Amish Country in Ohio. Lehman’s offers simple life appliances, hand tools, toys, and so much more. While shopping at Lehman’s, view the expansive displays with the history of the Amish lifestyle found in the items themselves.
