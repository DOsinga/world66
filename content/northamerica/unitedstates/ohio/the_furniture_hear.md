The Furniture Heartland is located in Amish Country of central Ohio. The region has earned this name due to its concentration of furniture related businesses. In fact, the Furniture Heartland has more solid wood furniture manufacturers and retailers than any other region. If you are looking to find just the right piece of solid wood or custom furniture...look no further than the Furniture Heartland.
