Of, as you all know, the entire world is talking about the newest resident to grace the Queen City - aka Cincinnati - **Ken Griffey, Jr.**. In easily the greatest thing to happen to the Reds since the Big Red Machine, the city once touted as troubled is now rejoicing as if it was the Second coming of Christ. Baseball is back, and in a big way! 

The defending American League Central Champs, the **Cleveland Indians**, open Spring Training with little pomp and circumstance, after losing long time Skipper, Mike Hargrove, to the nefarious Baltimore Orioles...it was time for Grover to grovel off, and now it's up to Charlie Emanuel to get the Tribe on track for continued dominance of the American League Central...now if only they could find a way to beat Pedro Martinez... 

**Old Mother Winter** has yet to release her grasp of the Buckeye state, dropping more snow and continuing to blow chilly temperatures around like Dorothy and Toto in the Wizard of Oz. Residents in the northeast corner should expect more snow for a few more weeks, thanks to the effects of lucious Lake Erie; while the Southern portion of the state can begin to break out the gardening tools...warmer temps are on the way.
