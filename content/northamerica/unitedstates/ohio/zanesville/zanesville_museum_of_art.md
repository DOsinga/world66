The Zanesville Museum of Art holds a collection stronger than you'd expect for a city this size, anchored by American art pottery — McCoy, Roseville, Weller, and other manufacturers that operated in this part of Ohio when the region's clay deposits made it the pottery capital of the country. The American painting collection spans the 19th and early 20th centuries. Admission is modest and the galleries are never crowded.
