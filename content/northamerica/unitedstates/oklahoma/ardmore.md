Ardmore, in south-central Oklahoma, sits about halfway between Oklahoma City and Dallas. It is a good base for exploring the Arbuckle Mountains, [Chickasaw National Recreation Area](/northamerica/unitedstates/oklahoma/chickasaw), Lake Murray State Park, and Lake Texoma State Park.

The city is one of several Oklahoma towns along the railroad between Dallas and Oklahoma City named after cities on the Pennsylvania line. It was founded in 1887.
