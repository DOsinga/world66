**Welcome to Route 66 Cottage in Bristow, OK!**

Upon arrival at Route 66 Cottage in Bristow, OK, you will see a small picket fence across the front lawn with "Burma Shave" style signs giving welcome. Then you will cruise along the painted sidewalk Route 66 Cottage Highway that leads up onto the porch and into the cottage and winds from stop to stop within. 

Your first stop on the Route 66 Cottage Highway will be the drive-in theater living area featuring vintage vehicle seating complete with drive-in speakers and your personal big screen for viewing movies. There will also be a Shoe Tree where you can add a paper "Shoe" with your comments. 

The next stop would be your motel room featuring a luxury queen size bed appointed with Route 66 memorabilia including a faux window and mural looking out on a scene featuring popular landmarks seen while cruising on Route 66. 

From there, you would travel the Route 66 Cottage Highway to the "Gas Station Restroom". The restroom key on a large wooden block labeled "Restroom" will be hanging just outside the door for you (but we won't really make it necessary to use it – it will be just for show – after all, when you gotta' go, you gotta' go). 

And the last stop, of course, would be your personal eating area in "Sondra's Diner", named after the owner's mother who dearly loved Route 66 in her lifetime. It will have faux neon lights surrounding the exterior and a corner booth for breakfast dining inside. Just through the back of Sondra's Diner will be a convenience kitchen with a microwave, coffee pot, and refrigerator and a laundry room. 

Bristow Route 66 Cottage will be inspected by and committed to the high standards set by the Oklahoma Bed and Breakfast Association and offer the finest in comfort, service and hospitality to our guests. 

Route 66 Cottage is scheduled to open in the spring of 2005. There will be a private pre-opening gala for the neighborhood with a Grand Opening for the public shortly thereafter. We will have a website up soon, **www.route66cottage.com**, so that you can track our progress. Meanwhile, keep track of us at {**http://route66cottage.visitbristowok.com**} 

**Remember: **

When you "Get Your Kicks on Route 66", we hope you'll "check in" to our Route 66 Cottage.
