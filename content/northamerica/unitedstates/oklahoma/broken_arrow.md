Broken Arrow is the largest suburb of [Tulsa](/northamerica/unitedstates/oklahoma/tulsa), sitting just southeast of the city in eastern Tulsa County. With a population pushing past 115,000, it has grown into a proper city in its own right — though most visitors will use it as a base for exploring the Tulsa area rather than as a destination on its own. The Rose District downtown has a handful of restaurants and shops worth a wander, and antique hunters will find a few good spots scattered along the main roads.
