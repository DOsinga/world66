### [Persimmon Hollow Antique Village](/northamerica/unitedstates/oklahoma/broken_arrow/shopping/persimmon_hollow_a)

Persimmon Hollow Antique Village is one of those places a traveler will remember long after they have visited there. About 45 antiques dealers can be found in numerous buildings on the campus of Persimmon Hollow. Shoppers will find everything from Fiesta Ware, to Cracker Jack prizes among the various collections available. There are dealers specializing in depression and elegant glassware, toys, advertising collectibles of all kinds, pottery, tools, and pop culture. It really is a cool place to see a lot of collectibles, and there is no admission fee.
