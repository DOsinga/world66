Located on Historic [Route 66](/northamerica/unitedstates/gettingaround/route_66__the_m) in Rogers County, Claremore is a city of nearly 16,000 people. It is best known as the home of the **Will Rogers Memorial**, a tribute to Oklahoma's beloved native son. The **J. M. Davis Gun Museum** and the annual **Chili and Bluegrass Festival** are also worth a stop.
