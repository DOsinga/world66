Located on Historic [Route 66](/northamerica/unitedstates/gettingaround/route_66__the_m) in Rogers County, Oklahoma, Claremore is a city of nearly 16,000 people. It is most noteably home of the Will Rogers memorial, a tribute to Oklahoma's native son. Also in Claremore is the J. M. Davis Gun Museum, and the annual Chili and Bluegrass Festival.
