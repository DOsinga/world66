Del City is a working-class suburb of about 22,000 people on the southeast side of [Oklahoma City](/northamerica/unitedstates/oklahoma/oklahomacity), bordered by Tinker Air Force Base to the east. The base, one of the largest Air Force installations in the country, has shaped the community since the 1940s, and many Del City residents have ties to the military.

The city is not a tourist destination, but it has a few points of interest for visitors in the Oklahoma City metro area. The community is home to a cluster of Vietnamese and Asian restaurants along SE 29th Street, reflecting the diverse population that settled near the base. Del City also sits close to the intersection of I-40 and I-35, making it a practical if unremarkable place to stop when driving through central Oklahoma.

For anything more substantial -- museums, nightlife, dining -- you are better off heading into downtown Oklahoma City, which is about 15 minutes west.
