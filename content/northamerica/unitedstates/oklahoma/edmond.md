Travel Guide

Located on Historic [Route 66](/northamerica/unitedstates/gettingaround/route_66__the_m) in Oklahoma County, Oklahoma, Edmond is a city of over 68,000 people. Olympic gymnast Shannon Miller (seven-time Olympic medalist, five-time individual world champion, the most decorated gymnast, male or female, in American history) is from Edmond.
