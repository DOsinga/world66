Located on Historic [Route 66](/northamerica/unitedstates/gettingaround/route_66__the_m) in Oklahoma County, Edmond is a city of over 68,000 people north of [Oklahoma City](/northamerica/unitedstates/oklahoma/oklahomacity). Olympic gymnast Shannon Miller -- seven-time Olympic medalist and the most decorated gymnast in American history -- is from Edmond.
