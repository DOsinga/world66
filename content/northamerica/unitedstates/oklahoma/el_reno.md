Located on Historic [Route 66](/northamerica/unitedstates/gettingaround/route_66__the_m) in Canadian County, El Reno is a city of over 16,000 people. The town is famous for its onion-fried burgers and hosts an annual Fried Onion Burger Day Festival each May.
