Located on Historic [Route 66](/northamerica/unitedstates/gettingaround/route_66__the_m) in Beckham County in western Oklahoma, Elk City is a city of over 10,000 people. The **National Route 66 Museum** complex here includes museums on the Old Town, Farm and Ranch, Blacksmith, and Transportation history of the region.
