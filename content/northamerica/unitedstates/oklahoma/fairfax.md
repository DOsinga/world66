Fairfax is a town of over 1,500 people in Osage County. Ballerina Maria Tallchief, one of the most famous Native American ballerinas, was from Fairfax. The town is part of the Osage Nation and gained renewed attention through David Grann's book *Killers of the Flower Moon*, which documents the Osage murders of the 1920s.
