### [Get Your Kicks on Historic Route 66](/northamerica/unitedstates/oklahoma/getting_around/get_your_kicks_on)

There are 48 Oklahoma cities and towns that sit alongside some portion of Route 66. The principle cities on the "Mother Road" from northeast to southwest are [Miami](/northamerica/unitedstates/oklahoma/miami), [Vinita](/northamerica/unitedstates/oklahoma/vinita), [Claremore](/northamerica/unitedstates/oklahoma/claremore), [Tulsa](/northamerica/unitedstates/oklahoma/tulsa), [Sapulpa](/northamerica/unitedstates/oklahoma/sapulpa), [Chandler](/northamerica/unitedstates/oklahoma/chandler), [more..](/northamerica/unitedstates/oklahoma/getting_around/get_your_kicks_on)
