Travel Guide

Located on the shores of the Grand Lake O'the Cherokees in Delaware County, Oklahoma, Grove is a town of over 5,500 people and is considered one of the top retirement areas in the nation. Grove is home to Har-Ber Village, one of the largest antiques museums in the United States put together by Jones Trucking Lines, Inc. founder Harvey and Bernice Jones. Other attractions in and near Grove include Bernice State Park, Cayuga Mission Church, Cherokee Grove Golf Course, the 1909 Corey Hotel, General Stan Watie Grave Site, Grand Grove Opry, Grand Lake Casino, Grand Trolley, Honey Creek State Park, Kountry Kousins Jamboree Theatre, Lendonwood Botanical Gardens, Micky Mantle Memorial Exhibit, Paddlewheel Riverboat Excursions, Patricia Island Golf Course, Satsuki Gardens, Starbird's National Rod and Custom Car Hall of Fame, and, of course, Grand Lake.
