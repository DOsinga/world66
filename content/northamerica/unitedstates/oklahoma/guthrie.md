Located in Logan County in central Oklahoma, Guthrie is a city of 10,000 people. It was originally designated as the state capital before it was relocated to [Oklahoma City](/northamerica/unitedstates/oklahoma/oklahomacity) in 1910. The town's Victorian-era downtown is one of the largest urban districts on the National Register of Historic Places.
