Located in Comanche County in southwestern Oklahoma, Lawton is a city of over 92,000 people -- the third largest in the state. It is the gateway to the Wichita Mountains Wildlife Refuge, home to Fort Sill (one of the Army's most important installations), and the **Museum of the Great Plains**.
