Located on Historic [Route 66](/northamerica/unitedstates/gettingaround/route_66__the_m) in Ottawa County in the far northeast corner of Oklahoma, Miami (pronounced "My-AM-uh" locally) is a city of nearly 14,000 people. It is one of the first Route 66 towns you hit when entering Oklahoma from the east.
