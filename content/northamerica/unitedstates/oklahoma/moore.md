Located in Cleveland County just south of [Oklahoma City](/northamerica/unitedstates/oklahoma/oklahomacity), Moore is a city of over 41,000 people. The city has been hit by several devastating tornadoes, including an EF5 in 2013, and is part of the area known as Tornado Alley.
