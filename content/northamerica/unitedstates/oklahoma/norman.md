Travel Guide

Located in Cleveland County, Oklahoma, Norman is a city of over 95,000 people. Norman is probably best known as the home of the University of Oklahoma. Actor James Garner and singer Vince Gill and engineer Karl Guthe Jansky (an important figure in the development of radio astronomy) were from Norman.
