Located in Cleveland County, Norman is a city of over 95,000 people and home to the **University of Oklahoma**. The university drives much of the city's culture and energy, especially on football Saturdays when the Sooners play. Actor James Garner, singer Vince Gill, and radio astronomy pioneer Karl Jansky were from Norman.
