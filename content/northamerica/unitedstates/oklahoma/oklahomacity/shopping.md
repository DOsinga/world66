---
title: 'Shopping'
type: section
---

From the antiques and collectibles shops up and down May Street to several flea markets, Oklahoma City is a surprisingly good place for treasure hunters.

The **Oklahoma City Antique District**, just north of the fairgrounds starting at 10th and May, runs several blocks up May Street with a dozen or more antique malls and shops.

**Old Paris Flea Market** at 1111 South Eastern is a classic Midwestern flea market with a mix of new merchandise, garage sale finds, and genuine antiques. Worth the trip for shoppers willing to dig.

**Mary's Ole Time Swap Meet** at 7925 NE 23rd (corner of 23rd and Midwest) draws collectors of every stripe on weekends. Mostly outdoors, so best in good weather. Open year-round, 6 a.m. to 7 p.m. Saturday and Sunday.

**Pack Rats** at 2712 NW 10th Street, just off May Avenue, is a pop-culture collector's paradise. Character collectibles, advertising memorabilia, and just about anything anyone has ever thought about collecting.
