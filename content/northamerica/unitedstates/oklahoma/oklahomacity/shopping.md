### [Old Paris Flea Market](/northamerica/unitedstates/oklahoma/oklahomacity/shopping/old_paris_flea_mar)

Old Paris Flea Market is one of those midwestern flea markets where one will find a mix of new merchandise, garage sale stuff, and great old junk. However, there are still some old time antiques and collectibles dealers there who will make the trip worthwhile for shoppers who are willing to be a little adventurous.

### [Mary´s Ole Time Swap Meet](/northamerica/unitedstates/oklahoma/oklahomacity/shopping/marys_ole_time_sw)

No matter what they collect, collectors of everything converge on Mary´s Ole Time Swap Meet early on weekends to find the bargains. This flea market is mostly outdoors, so it is great during good weather. Mary's is open year-round from 6 a.m. to 7 p.m. every Saturday and Sunday.

### [Oklahoma City Antique District](/northamerica/unitedstates/oklahoma/oklahomacity/shopping/oklahoma_city_anti)

There are numerous shops that have antiques and collectibles that can be found in the Antique District of Oklahma City. Located just north of the Oklahoma Fairgrounds, the Antique Dirstrict starts with several antique malls and shop at the Intersection of 10th and May and runs several blocks up May Street. ### [Pack Rats](/northamerica/unitedstates/oklahoma/oklahomacity/shopping/pack_rats)

Pack Rats located at 2712 N.W. 10th Street, just of May Street, has got to be one of the coolest places in the world. Visitors will find just about anything that anyone has ever thought about collecting in the line of popular culture. There are a lot of character collectibles and advertising collectibles there, as well as a little of every other type of collectibles one can imagine. For those who just like to look at neat stuff, Pack Rats is a great place to see it.

type: Antiques



tel: (495) 942-4744

address: 2712 N.W. 10th Street, just off May Avenue
