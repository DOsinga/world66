http://villageparkmall.com Village Park South is a local shopping mall located on North May Avenue, between Heffner and Britton in the heart of The Village in Oklahoma City. The mall has plenty of parking with easy access to all the stores. There are a number of shops including sporting goods, a classic resale shop, a home theater store selling high end equipment and the services to install and design your room, a mail room and tag agency, and a number of clothing stores.

Village Park South shares its parking lot with Village Park North. Stores in Village Park North include: Relax the Back a store selling products to eliminate back pain; Tech-Lock a lock smith; ChiroPlus Wellness Center; Express Travel Services; as well as a clothing store.

Across the street you will find another small strip mall with a wonderful Chinese Restaurant.
