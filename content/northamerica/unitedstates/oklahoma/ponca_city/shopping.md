### [Ponca Plaza Flea Market](/northamerica/unitedstates/oklahoma/ponca_city/shopping/ponca_plaza_flea_m)

The flea market at the Ponca Plaza shopping center has been held on Sundays from 7 a.m. to 1 p.m. for decades. It runs from early March to Early November at 14th and Hartford. It is a good mix of collectibles and antiques with new stuff as well as garage sale stuff.
