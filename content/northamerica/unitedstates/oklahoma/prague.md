A town of over 2,100 people in Lincoln County, Prague is the hometown of Jim Thorpe (1887-1953), one of the greatest athletes of the 20th century. Thorpe won gold medals in the pentathlon and decathlon at the 1912 Olympics and went on to play professional football and baseball.
