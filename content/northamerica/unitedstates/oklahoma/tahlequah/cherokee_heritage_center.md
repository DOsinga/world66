South of downtown, the Cherokee Heritage Center is the best place in the region to understand the history and culture of the Cherokee people. The complex includes a museum covering the Trail of Tears and Cherokee life before and after removal, a reconstructed 17th-century village, and an outdoor drama performed in summer. It is a serious and well-done institution.
