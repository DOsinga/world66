Lake Fort Gibson lies west of Tahlequah, formed by the impoundment of the Grand (Neosho) River. It is one of the largest lakes in Oklahoma and popular for fishing — bass, crappie, and catfish are the main targets — as well as camping and watersports. The surrounding area has several public access points and camping areas.
