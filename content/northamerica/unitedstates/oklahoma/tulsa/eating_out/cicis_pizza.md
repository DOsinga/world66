Cici's is a solid budget option -- an all-you-can-eat pizza buffet with soft drink for a few dollars. The location in Belle Aire Shopping Center just north of I-44 on Peoria is easy to find. They also serve pasta, salad, appetizers, and desserts. Multiple locations around Tulsa.
