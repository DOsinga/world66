There is no better place to get your curry on for an incredible eating experience than at India Palace. Guldeep and Manjit Singh are two brothers who came with their family recipes from India to bring the spices and atmosphere of their culture to Oklahoma.

The tandor oven plays a central role in the India Palace kitchen where they cook tandori chicken, kabobs, and pan (flat bread) in many different flavors. Also very popular are the currys and chicken briyani (chicken with rice and spices) is most excellent.

The prices for these dishes are quite reasonable, because of the expense of using the authentic spices for these foods. If you like it a little more spicy, or less spicy, then be sure and tell you waiter, because the cooks will make it to your preference. The service at India Palace is a great as the food. Fine dining in the evenings on Sunday through Thursday from 5 to 10 p.m., and Friday and Saturday from 5:30 to 10:30 p.m.

For those who don't know Indian cuisine, the lunch buffet is an excellent place to start. Many new customers find themselves going to India Palace for the first time at lunch with friends, and they get hooked on the place. $6.95 is an amazing price for such and excellent selection of Indian foods. The buffet is daily from 11:00 a.m. to 2:30 p.m.
