---
title: 'Shopping'
type: section
---

One of the best things to do in Tulsa is hit the flea markets. Bargain hunters and antiques collectors will find plenty to keep them busy.

The **Tulsa Flea Market** at Expo Square (4145 E. 21st Street) runs every Saturday from 8 a.m. to 5 p.m. (except during the State Fair in September-October). Hundreds of dealers bring antiques, collectibles, furniture, and glassware. Get there early for Amish bakery items before they sell out.

The **Great American Flea Market** at 9210-9244 E. Admiral Place is one of the largest indoor flea markets in the Midwest, with over 500 dealers in year-round air-conditioned comfort. Conveniently located south of I-244 near the Mingo traffic circle.

Beyond the flea markets, Tulsa has numerous antiques shops scattered around town and some excellent malls.
