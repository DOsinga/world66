With over 500 dealers, Great American Flea Market is one of the largest indoor flea markets in the midwest. It is conveniently located south of I-244 on Admiral Place near the Mingo traffic circle. There are also dealers in the parking lot, but the big draw is shopping in year-round air conditioned comfort inside.
