Tulsa Flea Market is held each Saturday 8 a.m.-5 p.m. in Exchange Buildings I and II at Expo Square. There is no flea market from the middle of September to the middle of October due to the Tulsa State Fair.

Tulsa Flea Market is without doubt one of the finest flea markets for antiques and collectibles anywhere. Hundreds of dealers bring in thier finest items to sell in the flea market on Saturdays. Everything from furniture to glassware can be found there. Those who get there early might be able to buy some Amish bakery items before they are all gone.
