---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Tulsa's oil-boom wealth built museums and an art-deco downtown that together make it one of the most surprising cities in the central United States. The **Philbrook Museum of Art**, set in a 1920s Italianate villa with formal gardens, is the standout; the **Gilcrease Museum** holds one of the country's largest collections of Western American art. Downtown has more art-deco buildings per block than almost anywhere else in the US. **Cherry Street** and the **Brady Arts District** are where the independent shops, bars, and galleries cluster.
