The Tulsa Flea Market is held every Saturday from 8 a.m. to 5 p.m. in Exchange Buildings I and II at Expo Square. There is no flea market from mid-September to mid-October due to the Tulsa State Fair.

This is one of the finest flea markets for antiques and collectibles anywhere. Hundreds of dealers bring their best items -- everything from furniture to glassware. Get there early for Amish bakery goods before they sell out.
