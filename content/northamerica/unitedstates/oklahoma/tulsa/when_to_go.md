---
title: "When to Go"
type: section
---

Spring and fall are the best seasons in Tulsa — summers are hot and humid, winters cold with occasional ice storms. April through early June and September through October bring the most comfortable weather and the fullest festival calendar.

**Mayfest** in May turns downtown into a week-long arts and music festival. **Juneteenth** celebrations honour African American heritage. **Tulsa Oktoberfest** at River West Festival Park (2100 S. Jackson) is among the best in the country — Bavarian bands, German food, and craft vendors every October.

The **Gateway International Balloon Festival** and **Jazz on Greenwood** are popular summer draws. The **Tulsa State Fair** fills Expo Square each autumn. **Christmas Lights in the Park** at the River Parks Pedestrian Bridge runs through December and into January.

The Tulsa Philharmonic Orchestra, Ballet, and Opera all perform through the autumn and winter at the Tulsa Performing Arts Center downtown.
