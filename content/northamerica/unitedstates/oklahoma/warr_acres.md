Warr Acres is a small city enclosed within the [Oklahoma City](/northamerica/unitedstates/oklahoma/oklahomacity) metropolitan area, sitting along [Route 66](/northamerica/unitedstates/gettingaround/route_66__the_m) in Oklahoma County. It is essentially a suburb, but one with its own identity — a residential community that incorporated in the 1940s to avoid annexation by Oklahoma City. For Route 66 travellers, it is a pass-through point on the historic highway's path through the metro area.
