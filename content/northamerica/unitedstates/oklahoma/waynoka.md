Located in Woods County in northwestern Oklahoma, Waynoka is a town of nearly 1,000 people. It is best known for the **Annual Rattlesnake Hunt and Carnival** and nearby **Little Sahara State Park**, an excellent place for four-wheeling and dune buggy riding on sand dunes.
