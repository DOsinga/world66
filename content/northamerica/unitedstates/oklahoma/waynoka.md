Travel Guide

Located in Woods County, Oklahoma, Waynoka is a town of nearly 1,000 people. Waynoka is most noted for its Annual Rattlesnake Hunt and Carnival, and the Little Sahara State Park, which is an excellent place to go fourwheeling or drive a dune buggy up the side of sand dunes.
