Travel Guide

Located in Latimer County, Oklahoma, Wilburton is a city of almost 3,000 people.

Wilburton, Oklahoma is the home of the Eastern Oklahoma State University Mountaineers. Nestled at the end of the San Bois mountain range, Wilburton's greastest claim to fame would have to be Robber's Cave State Park located approximately 5 miles north of Wilburton on highway 2. The park used to be used as a hideout by legendary old west outlaws Jesse James and Bell Star. Every Septemeber, Wilburton will host a festival/bike rally at Robber's Cave State park.
