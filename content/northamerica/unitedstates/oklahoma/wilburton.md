Located in Latimer County in southeastern Oklahoma, Wilburton is a city of almost 3,000 people nestled at the end of the San Bois mountain range. It is home to Eastern Oklahoma State College.

The main draw is **Robbers Cave State Park**, about 5 miles north on Highway 2. The park was reputedly used as a hideout by Old West outlaws Jesse James and Belle Starr. Every September, Wilburton hosts a festival and bike rally at the park.
