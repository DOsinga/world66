Located on Historic [Route 66](/northamerica/unitedstates/gettingaround/route_66__the_m) in Canadian County, Yukon is a city of about 21,000 people west of [Oklahoma City](/northamerica/unitedstates/oklahoma/oklahomacity). Country singer Garth Brooks grew up in Yukon.
