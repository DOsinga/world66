Situated at the union of the Willamette and Columbia rivers, Portland
is a vibrant city of unique charms - the stately elms in the South Park
Blocks, the tranquility of the Japanese Gardens, the view from Pittock
Mansion, miles of hiking trails in Forest Park, and the largest
classical Chinese garden and forested urban park in the country. Visit
Portland and experience why Money magazine proclaimed it the country’s
"Best Big City.”

The City of Roses offers intense natural beauty, a bustling
entertainment scene, and sumptuous dining -easily accessed via the
light-rail system. From Portland’s excellent art museums to the
colorful rose test gardens, Portland is an alluring mix of scenery and
greenery. Grab a bite and a microbrew from a myriad of restaurants and
brewpubs. Shop in The Pearl or Clackamas Town Center, browse original
art the Portland Saturday Market, or catch a live show in the arts
district.
