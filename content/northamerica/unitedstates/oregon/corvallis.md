Corvallis is the home of Oregon State University and the county seat of Benton County — a compact, walkable city of around 50,000 in the heart of the Willamette Valley. The university gives it an active café culture and a younger energy than most towns of its size in Oregon.

It makes an excellent base for a Willamette Valley trip: the Pacific coast is less than an hour west, the Cascade mountains are within reach to the east, and Portland is 90 minutes north. Wine country surrounds it — the Eola-Amity Hills and Chehalem Mountains appellations are both close by.
