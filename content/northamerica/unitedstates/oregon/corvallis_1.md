Travel Guide

Corvallis is the home of Oregon State University, the county seat of Benton County, and a wonderful small town of (about) 50,000 people.

Corvallis is a great "hub" for a Willamette Valley vacation, being within easy driving distance of the coast as well as the Cascade mountains.

There is much more that should go here. Volunteers welcome!
