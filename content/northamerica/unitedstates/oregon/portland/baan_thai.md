Thai restaurant near Portland State University on SW Broadway. The menu covers the full range of Thai standards done well, and the lunch specials are good value. Dishes are available from mild to very spicy — the staff will ask and they mean it when they say hot. A reliable neighborhood spot rather than a destination restaurant, but consistently good.
