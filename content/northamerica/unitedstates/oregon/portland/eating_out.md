---
title: "Eating Out"
type: section
---

Portland has a strong independent restaurant scene. The Pearl District and NW 23rd Avenue are the most concentrated dining areas. The Saturday Market under the Burnside Bridge runs year-round and is one of the best places to eat cheaply and well. Food carts (Portland's famous clusters of permanent carts) are found across the city and cover everything from Vietnamese to Ethiopian to wood-fired pizza. Andina in the Pearl District is well regarded for Peruvian food.
