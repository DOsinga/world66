Baan Thai, located across the street from Portland State University,
features a full range of excellently prepared Thai standards, as well
as their own specials, such as their unusually named Volcano Chicken.
Dishes are available from entirely mild to mouth-destroying spicy, with
a steep scale—waiters warn you if you order medium or stronger. Lunch
specials are a particularly good value.
