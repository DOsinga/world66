Sivilaya Thai Restaurant, with a large menu of typical Thai dishes,
features excellent food and a very warm welcome. The owner, José,
usually greets you as you walk in, always with a handshake and
sometimes with a pat on the back. An appetizer, Thai Ice Tea or Coffee
or hot tea, and a small dessert come on the house. Expect to pay $7-10
per person for reasonably large proportions, or up to $15 if you get
seafood or specials.
