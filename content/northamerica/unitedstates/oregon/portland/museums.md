### [Oregon Museum of Science and Industry (OMSI)

OMSI is great for kids. It has hundreds of hands on activities and you
can spend a full rainy day there and not get bored. But if all you want
to do is see an awesome movie check out the IMAX theater that gives you
a 360 view of space travel, scuba diving, race car driving, or an
Africa safari.

Museum admission: adults $8.50, seniors (63+) and youth (3-13) $6.50.
OMNIMAX theatre: separate admission charge, adults $8.50, seniors (63+)
and youth (3-13) $6.50.

Open Tu-Su 9:30AM - 5:30PM (after Labor Day to mid-June)
