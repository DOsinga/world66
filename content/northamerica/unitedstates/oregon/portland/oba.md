Oba! brings Brazilian and Latin American flavours to the Pearl District, with a menu that spans ceviche, plantain dishes, and grilled meats alongside solid cocktails — the caipirinhas and mojitos are reliable. The room is lively and the crowd tends toward the after-work crowd looking for both food and a drink. Good for groups.
