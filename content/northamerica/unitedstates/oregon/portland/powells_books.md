The largest independent bookstore in the United States, occupying an entire city block on West Burnside. Over a million new, used, and rare books arranged in colour-coded rooms across multiple floors. The free map at the entrance is genuinely useful. Open every day of the year; the rare book room has first editions going back centuries. A Portland institution.
