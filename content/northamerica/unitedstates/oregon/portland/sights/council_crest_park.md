Council Crest Park claims to be the highest point in all of Portland. Historically speaking, Council Crest Park is where Native Americans lived and discussed the future of their tribes. In addition to experiencing this unique part of Americas history, the Council Crest Park also offers excellent views of local mountains and the city of Portland. From this park you can see Mount St. Helens as well as Mount Rainier and Mount Hood. It is best to visit this park on a clear day so you can take full advantage of all of the amazing views the park has to offer. Portland attractions usually involve a trip to the park!
