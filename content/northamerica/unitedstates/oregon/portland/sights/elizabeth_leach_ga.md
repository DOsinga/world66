The Elizabeth Leach Gallery is a small art gallery in the Portland area that was developed in 1981. It is known as the second oldest gallery in the Portland area. This gives you an idea of how recent the development of an art scene in Portland has been. The Elizabeth Leach Gallery offers a variety of paintings, photos and sculptures from national and local artists. In addition, the gallery is well known for showcasing emerging local artists and talent. If you call or check their website, you can find all the info you need on the gallery and what new exhibits they have. Portland attractions have never been more artistic!
