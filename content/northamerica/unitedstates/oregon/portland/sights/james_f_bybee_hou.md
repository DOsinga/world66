Sauvie Island is a beautiful getaway for people looking for an island adventure in Portland. On this island is Howell Territorial Park. This area was designed for river trading around the turn of the century. The James F. Bybee House was built in 1858 and is available for tours and is an excellent place to observe. The house is so well preserved, that it is sure to help you understand how people lived in old time Portland. The house is mostly open on weekends so it is always best to call to make sure they are open. Portland attractions should always be this historic.
