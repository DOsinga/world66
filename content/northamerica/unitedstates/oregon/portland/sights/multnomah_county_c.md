The Multnomah County Central Library has recently been remodeled and is well-known as Oregon’s oldest library. The stairs in the library are made of marble and many of the new furnishings are a welcome surprise. The Library has now become more accessible for handicapped people. In addition to the simply massive book collection that is found within the Multnomah County Central Library, a large selection of computers can be found inside for your viewing pleasure. There is a children’s library within the library named after the great Beverly Cleary. The library is an excellent Portland attraction to stop at if you are looking for something fun and educational to do.
