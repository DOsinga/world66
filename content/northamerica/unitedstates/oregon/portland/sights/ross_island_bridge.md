Ross Island Bridge is another excellent bridge that crosses the mighty Willamette River. Although this is a pretty basic bridge, it has been in use since 1926 so you know that it is durable. In addition, because Portland is a relatively new city, having a bridge that was built all the way back in 1926 is a pretty big deal for the city of Portland. In addition, the building was designed by the great Gustav Linderthal. Even if you do not stop to observe this bridge in great detail, you will most likely past over it at least once during your exciting trip to Portland. Portland attractions should always be this convenient!
