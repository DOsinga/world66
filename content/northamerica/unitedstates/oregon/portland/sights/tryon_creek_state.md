Tryon Creek State park is extremely close to downtown Portland, and offers a fresh and refreshing day out for you and your family. The park is a massive 645 acres and is perfect for hiking and observing nature. The Tryon Creek is an excellent stream that boasts steelhead trout during certain months of the year. In addition, there are horse trails for all of you equestrian lovers as well as a paved trail for the more novice hiker. Next to Tyson Creek is the Glenn L. Jackson Shelter, which is the perfect place to stop and listen to the squirrels and blue jays.
