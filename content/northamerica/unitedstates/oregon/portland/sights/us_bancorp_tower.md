The U.S. Bancorp Tower is a fantastic piece of modern architecture. Located right in the center of Portland, the U.S. Bancorp Tower is visible from nearly every part of Portland. In addition, the building is comprised of 43 floors and it is known as Portland’s tallest high rise. The building houses a variety of companies and corporations, make it a central place for business. Despite this, it is still a great place to simply gaze at and observe its architectural wonders. There is really no need to go inside the U.S. Bancorp Tower in that it is a mostly generic interior. Portland attractions such as this show Portland’s diversity.
