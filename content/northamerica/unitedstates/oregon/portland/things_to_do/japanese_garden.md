Five-and-a-half acres of meticulously maintained Japanese garden in Washington Park, consistently rated one of the most authentic outside Japan. Five distinct garden styles — Tea Garden, Strolling Pond Garden, Natural Garden, Dry Landscape Garden, and Flat Garden — spread across a hillside above the city. Views of Mount Hood on clear days. Admission fee; shuttle bus from the Washington Park MAX station.
