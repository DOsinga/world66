[Portland](/northamerica/unitedstates/oregon/portland)offers sights, sounds, and a wonderful cityscape. Looking out over the water at night is a tremendous spectacle. If you're looking for anything you can find it here. http://oregon.areaguides.net/

Don't just stay for a night, plan a few days, as there are so many tours and half day trips available in the area, you'll need to stay a minimum of 3 days. Most airlines fly directly to Portland, but driving there will take you through the beautiful countryside of either [Washington](/northamerica/unitedstates/washington), [California](/northamerica/unitedstates/california), or Southwestern Idaho.
If you are looking for a high end hotel to enjoy on your next relaxing vacation, the Riverplace Hotel and the Residence Inn by Marriott are two hotels that offer great amenities and local attractions in the Portland area. The Riverplace hotel is located near the downtown river, close to the airport. It has the distinction of being the only hotel located on the Willamette River . The Residence Inn by Marriott is located close to the airport in the downtown center near attractions which include the famous Lloyd center. If you are looking for luxury but at a lower price, the Doubletree Hotel and the Country Inn and Suites are two hotels that are budget friendly and offer wonderful rooms with limited amenities. The Doubletree hotel is near the downtown area and is located near the airport. The Llyod Center Mall is located next door to the hotel. The CountyInn and Suites is located near the airport and is only five miles from shopping stores (Ikea) and the PDX.
