Although Salem is maybe not as exciting as [Portland](/northamerica/unitedstates/oregon/portland) there is plenty to do here. The city is set in a beautiful valley which is one of Oregon's best wine producing regions. Many of the wine 'chateaux' can be visited and the wines can be tasted & bought.
