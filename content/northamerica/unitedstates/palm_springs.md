The best resource for sights, hotels, restaurants, bars, what to do and see

Palm Springs is a desert oasis with a cosmopolitan twist. A visit to Palm Springs is an opportunity to dine in world class restaurants, lounge at legendary boutique hotels, and discover why mid-century modern enthusiasts gather here each year. For the outdoor adventurer, Palm Springs offers magnificent hiking, jeep tours, and the magnificent Palm Springs Aerial Tramway. Know for its near perfect weather year-round, in Palm Springs, you will enjoy over 360 days of sunshine a year.
