Benton is a small borough in Columbia County, but the real draw is nearby Ricketts Glen State Park, home to one of Pennsylvania's most spectacular hiking trails.

The Falls Trail is a 3-mile loop through old-growth forest that passes more than 20 named waterfalls, the tallest being 94-foot Ganoga Falls. The trail is steep and can be muddy and slippery, so wear proper hiking boots. Allow a full day if you want to stop and take in each waterfall properly. The park also offers camping near Lake Jean and swimming in summer. Ricketts Glen is off Route 487, about 30 miles north of Bloomsburg.
