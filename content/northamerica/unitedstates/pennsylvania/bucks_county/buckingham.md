Buckingham is the largest township in Bucks County, covering 33 square miles of rolling farmland east of Doylestown. Settled around 1700, it remains largely rural and residential, with stone farmhouses, horse properties, and country roads lined with old trees. It is a quiet base for exploring the surrounding Bucks County attractions — [New Hope](/northamerica/unitedstates/pennsylvania/newhope) and Peddlers Village are just minutes away.
