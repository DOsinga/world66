### [Baci Ristorante](/northamerica/unitedstates/pennsylvania/bucks_county/buckingham/eating_out/baci_ristorante)

We offer splendid Northen Italian cuisine in a charming 17th century stone and stucco building that is an ideal Bucks County country restaurant. Enjoy an intimate dining experience in our stone walled dining room with fireplace and ambiance to spare, or seasonal balcony dining.

Baci is conveniently situated in [Buckingham](/northamerica/unitedstates/pennsylvania/bucks_county/buckingham), just minutes from New Hope, Peddlers Village and [Doylestown](/northamerica/unitedstates/pennsylvania/bucks_county/doylestown).

### [Heart of Oak Pub](/northamerica/unitedstates/pennsylvania/bucks_county/buckingham/eating_out/heart_of_oak_pub)

The Heart of Oak Pub is very close to what you would find in England with a cheery intimate atmosphere and a pub menu with hearty British food, sandwiches and snacks. There are 14 imported beers on tap and entertainment Friday & Saturday evenings.

The Heart of Oak Pub is conveniently situated in [Buckingham](/northamerica/unitedstates/pennsylvania/bucks_county/buckingham), just minutes from [New Hope](/northamerica/unitedstates/pennsylvania/newhope), Peddlers Village and [more..](/northamerica/unitedstates/pennsylvania/bucks_county/buckingham/eating_out/heart_of_oak_pub)
