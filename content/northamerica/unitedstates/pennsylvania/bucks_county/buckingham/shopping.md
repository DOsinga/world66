### [None Such Farm Market](/northamerica/unitedstates/pennsylvania/bucks_county/buckingham/shopping/none_such_farm_mar)

None Such Farm Market is a family owned and operated produce market in [Buckingham, Pa](/northamerica/unitedstates/pennsylvania/bucks_county/buckingham). just across the street from None Such Farm on Route 263.

The farm market has everything you need for a great meal, including fresh produce, local and our own, a full service meat shop featuring our own farm raised angus beef, a deli with Boar’s head meats and cheese, dairy products, fresh breads, dry goods, flowers, bedding plants, and much more.
