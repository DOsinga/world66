Travel Guide

Lahaska is a small village in [Buckingham Township](/northamerica/unitedstates/pennsylvania/bucks_county/buckingham), [Bucks County](/northamerica/unitedstates/pennsylvania/bucks_county).

It was apparently once known as Hentown, named after a six acre piece of property that housed a chicken hatchery, a barn and a grouping of chicken coops.
