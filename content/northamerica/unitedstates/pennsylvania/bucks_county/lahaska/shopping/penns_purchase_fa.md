Located just 1 hour North of [Philadelphia](/northamerica/unitedstates/pennsylvania/philadelphia), **Penn's Purchase Factory Outlet Stores** offers over fifty of the best names in outlet shopping nestled in a charming country village setting.

We are conveniently located on Route 202, adjacent to Peddler's Village and within a five-mile radius to [New Hope](/northamerica/unitedstates/pennsylvania/newhope) and [Doylestown](/northamerica/unitedstates/pennsylvania/bucks_county/doylestown). Famous outlets include Osh Kosh B'Gosh, Bose Factory Store, G.H. Bass Outlet, Nine West, and many more as listed in the directory.
