Delaware County is located close to Philadelphia in Pennsylvania. Media is the capital of the county, which played an important rol in American Delaware County,history.


 Delaware County is the fourth most populous region in the state even though it has the third smallest land mass. Forty-nine distinctive municipalities are within its boundaries, each with its own respective government whether as a township, city or borough.
