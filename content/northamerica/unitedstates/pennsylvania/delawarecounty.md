Delaware County lies just southwest of [Philadelphia](/northamerica/unitedstates/pennsylvania/philadelphia) and is the fourth most populous county in the state despite having the third smallest land area. The county seat is Media, a walkable borough with a lively restaurant row along State Street.

Forty-nine municipalities make up the county, each with its own government. The area played a significant role in early American history -- William Penn first landed at Chester in 1682. Today, Delaware County is mostly suburban, with Swarthmore College and Villanova University among its well-known institutions.
