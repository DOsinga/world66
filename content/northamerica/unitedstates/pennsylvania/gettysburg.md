Just one of the historic cities in Pennsylvania, Gettysburg was the site of one of the most famous battles in Civil War history. Much of the town is filled with tourists, local folk in period dress, and students visiting the abundance of museums and shops. Popular destinations are the self-guided tour of the battlefields and the Wax Museum.

Gettysburg is also home to Gettysburg College, a small and rigorous institution of higher learning located a stone's throw from the battlefields.
