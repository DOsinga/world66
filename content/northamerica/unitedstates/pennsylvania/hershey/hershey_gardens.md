A 23-acre botanical garden that started as Milton Hershey's personal rose garden in 1937, now holding over 3,500 varieties of roses along with themed butterfly, Japanese, and children's gardens. The butterfly atrium runs from spring through fall and is the highlight for most visitors. Quieter and more genuinely pleasant than anything else in town.
