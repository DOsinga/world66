A full-scale amusement park with more than 70 rides, including a dozen roller coasters ranging from the classic wooden Comet to the inverted Steel Force. Water rides, a wave pool, and ZooAmerica — a North American wildlife park — are included with admission. It gets crowded on summer weekends; arrive early or buy tickets online in advance.
