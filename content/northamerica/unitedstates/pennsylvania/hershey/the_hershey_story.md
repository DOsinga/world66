A well-done museum covering Milton Hershey's life, his chocolate empire, and the model town he built for his workers. The chocolate lab lets you make and taste your own chocolate. More interesting than the Chocolate World ride if you want to understand what made Hershey historically significant — he built schools, homes, and a complete community, not just a factory.
