To get to Philadelphia from most points in the Northeast, the cheapest option will most likely be by bus. www.busjunction.com is a search engine for discount bus tickets to and from major cities in the northeast, including tickets from Megabus and Bolt Bus, the two most popular intercity bus lines. The least expensive option will be the so-called "chinatown" bus lines. These lowfare bus carriers pickup from Chinatown in Philly (121 N 11th Street) and go to New York, Washington DC and Baltimore. GotoBus.com sells tickets and has schedule information for most of the Chinatown bus companies that serve Philadelphia. But be forewarned, travelling on the Chinatown buses is not for the faint of heart.
Greyhound and Trailways have service to other cities within Pennsylvania and connect to points throughhout the US. The main bus terminal is located at 1001 FILBERT ST Philadelphia, PA
