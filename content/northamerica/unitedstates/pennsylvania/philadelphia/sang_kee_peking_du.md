A modest-looking establishment on the edge of Philadelphia's Chinatown, but one of the best Chinese restaurants in the city. The food is outstanding, service is fast, and it's quite cheap. The restaurant was renovated a few years ago, swapping its crowded formica-and-linoleum grittiness for something brighter and friendlier — fortunately, the food stayed the same.
