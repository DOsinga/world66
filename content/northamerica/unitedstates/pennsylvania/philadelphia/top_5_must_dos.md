### [North America / United States / Pennsylvania / Philadelphia - Top 5 Must Do's](/northamerica/unitedstates/pennsylvania/philadelphia/top_5_must_dos/north_america__un)

Philadelphia is a city rich with history and the birthplace of the Constitution. It is also famous for its restaurants and sports teams. While there is plenty to do in Philadelphia the following Must Do’s will give you the true local experience.

**Eat a Philly Cheesesteak**

All the hype is true; a Philly Cheesesteak is one of the most delicious sandwiches in the world. There are many places to get a cheesesteak in Philadelphia, my favorite is the “pizza steak wit wiz and fried onions” from Jim’s Steaks at 4th and South.
