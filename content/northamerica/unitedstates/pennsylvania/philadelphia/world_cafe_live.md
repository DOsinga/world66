Two performance spaces plus the studios of WXPN-FM, the public radio station whose playlist set the template for adult-alternative radio across the country. Downstairs Live seats 650 and hosts acoustic acts through world-beat to indie rock. Full kitchens on both floors; you can eat dinner and catch a show without moving.
