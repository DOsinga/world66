**Carnegie Museum of Natural History** is famous for its dinosaur fossil collection and shares a building with the Carnegie Museum of Art -- you can visit both on one ticket. Students should bring ID for discounted admission. Address: 4400 Forbes Ave. Tel: 412-622-3131. Website: [carnegiemuseums.org](http://www.carnegiemuseums.org/cmnh/).

**Andy Warhol Museum** is the largest museum in North America dedicated to a single artist. Warhol was born in Pittsburgh, and the museum houses an extensive collection of his art, films, and archives across seven floors. Closed Mondays. Address: 117 Sandusky Street. Hours: 10am-5pm.

**Phipps Conservatory and Botanical Gardens** combines horticulture, art, and seasonal flower shows in a stunning Victorian glasshouse in Schenley Park. Open Tuesday through Sunday, 9am to 5pm. Address: One Schenley Park (Frank Curto Drive). Tel: 412-622-6914.
