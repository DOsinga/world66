---
title: "Shopping in Pittsburgh"
type: section
---

Conveniently located only five minutes from Pittsburgh International Airport at 100 Robinson Centre Drive, if you are in the mood for some Pittsburgh shopping, be sure to go to The Mall at Robinson. Find stylish clothing, footwear, accessories, and beauty products at Macy's, American Eagle, Banana Republic, H&M, Charlotte Russe, Victoria's Secret, Ann Taylor Loft, BabyGap, Build-A-Bear Workshop, Yankee Candle, Dakota Watch Company, and IKEA. You can grab a bite at Max Orient or Chick-fil-A. Or, you can dine at the Olive Garden or Houlihan's. There is also free internet terminals as well. For directions, visit their website at shoprobinsonmall.com.
