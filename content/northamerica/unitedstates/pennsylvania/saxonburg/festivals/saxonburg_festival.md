Annual festival that generally occurs on the first or second Saturday of September. Lots of arts and crafts booths are present, some traditional fundraisers for Knoch clubs, others just area residents making knick-knacks to sell. Occasionally teachers work food booths here. There's a parade by the Knoch Marching Band, and the Saxonburg 5k in the morning.
