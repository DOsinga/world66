Travel Guide

A small city of roughly 4,000 people along I-65 between [Lafayette](/northamerica/unitedstates/indiana/lafayette), [Indiana](/northamerica/unitedstates/indiana) (one hour south), and [Chicago](/northamerica/unitedstates/illinois/chicago) (two to three hours, depending on road construction), Rensselaer is the seat of Jasper County. It has a pleasant **courthouse** and a small Catholic college, **St. Joseph's College.** There are some antique shops and a good restaurant/bar beside the police station across from the courthouse. About two blocks away is the **Busy Bee** ice cream stand. Open in warm weather, it serves great shakes, but beware: The sulphur content in the water often results in gas, unless you've spent about two days in the area, after which your body adjusts. Rensselaer can be reached by bus and train.
