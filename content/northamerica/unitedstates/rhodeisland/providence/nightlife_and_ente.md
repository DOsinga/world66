---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment in Providence"
type: section
---

There are very few places in Rhode Island that can compare to the festive **Providence Nightlife** scene for having a great time. No matter what kind of entertainment you are looking for Providence has it. There are a few theaters which host a variety of plays if that is what you enjoy. Or if you are looking to go out and dance there are plenty of clubs to choose from. Even fpr those who would like to just go out for a relaxing drink, those places are here in Providence, also. So no matter what style of nightlife suits you Providence nightlife is here to satisfy you.

#### Bovi's Tavern

If you are looking for a nice jazz bar then Bovi's is just the place for you. The atmosphere of this establishment just enhances the music that is played here every night. The building is just a bit small but very comfortable. Monday's are a special entertainment night because local musicians get together for rocking jam sessions. All other nights hosts various jazz groups.

#### Dave's and Buster's

This establishment is sure to draw out the kid in any adult. It is an arcade complex made for grown ups. It is a great place to hang out at and just have fun while drinking a beer. They have all the newest in interactive and simulation games. Several full bars are located strategically around the huge arcade so you will never be far away from a beer or cocktail.

#### Cable Car Cinema and Cafe

This is one of Providence's unique nightlife attractions. For over 25 years this cinema has entertained tourists and locals with its array of independent and foreign films. It also hosts festivals and special events. Their services include selling light meals like sandwiches, salads and soups. The admission prices are inexpensive; adults are charged $ 9.00, children and seniors get in for $ 7.00.

#### Tazza's

This establishment is for those who are interested in a serious night of dancing and hanging out. The crowd that gathers here is usually very congenial. A lot of tourists come here to get out at night and want to have fun. They have live music every night of the week. The prices for drinks are reasonable and the cover charge is about the average price going.

#### Blake's Tavern

Blake's Tavern is definitely a place to check out if you want a relaxing night of hanging out. The furnishings are quite simple, giving it a relaxed atmosphere and the service only enhances this. There are several areas to lounge in. like the comfortable library room. Or you can go toward the back to Room 66 where it allows you to have a peaceful time with your date. The tavern has several beers on tap and prices are always cheap.
