---
title: "Shopping in Providence"
type: section
---

If you are in the mood for some Providence shopping while on vacation, check out the mall at Providence Place. There are over 150 great stores and you are bound to find some deals and bargains on clothing in the latest fashion, health and beauty products, or anything else you may want. Enjoy shopping at such places as Aveda, bebe, The Body Shop, Borders, Crate & Barrel, J. Crew, J. Jill, Papyrus, Pottery Barn, Tiffany & Co, Williams-Sonoma, and Yankee Candle. If you feel like eating, dine at Uno Chicago Grill, Nordstrom Cafe, The Melting Pot, or The Cheesecake Factory.
