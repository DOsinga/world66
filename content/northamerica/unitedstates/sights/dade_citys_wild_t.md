Dade City's Wild THings is a 501(c)3 non profit zoo located in Historical Dade City, Florida. Stop at the downtown gift shop located on 37245 Meridian Ave, Dade City, 33525 featuring zoo animal themed home decor, jewery, tiffany lamps, photos, art work, stuffed animals, educational toys, games and gifts for all ages. All proceeds go to support the animals. From there you can jump on our authentic trolley to take a short ride to the zoo. Yo will be guided by an experienced and educated guide how will answer all your questions. Your tour will take a 2-3 hour walking tour through the botanical gardens to see a diverse collection of over 190 exotic animals. You will be amazed at how close you will be to stalking tigers, playing bears, lions roaring, monkeys playing, otters swimming, birds calling and so much more. ALl the animals will be with 3 feet and interactive with your guide. You will also have an opportunity at the end of every tour for animal interactions with our baby animals. It will be an experience you will never forget. call 352-567-WILD (9453) or www.dadecityswildthings.com Hours are Tues - Sat 9:00 - 5:00 Tours times 9:30 & 2:00 .
