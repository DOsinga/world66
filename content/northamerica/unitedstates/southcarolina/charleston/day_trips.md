---
title: "Day Trips"
type: section
---

The **Ashley River plantations** — Magnolia, Middleton Place, and the starkly beautiful Drayton Hall — are 30–40 minutes' drive northwest of the city and each offers a different perspective on Lowcountry history and the lives of the enslaved people who built it. The beach towns of **Sullivan's Island** and **Folly Beach** are half an hour east, with Atlantic surf and relaxed seafood shacks. Further afield, historic **Beaufort** and the **ACE Basin** wetlands make a long but rewarding day trip down the coast.
