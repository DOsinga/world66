Market Street Saloon

A night of drinks, music and dancing awaits the visitor to the Market Street Saloon. In fact when passing by, music will probably being pouring out into the streets and firing up the surrounding area. This is a bar where almost anything goes and where women are encouraged to jump on the bar and dance their hearts out. The bartenders also join into the fun with their boot stomping to the rock-n-roll, country, and hip hop music that prevails. Or they might be playing with their tequila filled squirt guns as they do individual dance solos.
