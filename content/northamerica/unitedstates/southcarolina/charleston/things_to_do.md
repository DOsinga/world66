Hunley Submarine

A visit to the recovered Hunley submarine is one of the most popular of Charleston activities. The Confederate submarine, manually powered by nine soldiers, managed to set off an explosive when it approach the Union ship Housatonic and sink it. Unfortunately, the Hunley also sank and settled on the ocean floor. This incident occurred in February of 1864 and it was not until August, 2000, that the sub was recovered. Now visitors can view this early submarine from the deck above her holding tank at the former Charleston Navy Yard where it is undergoing conservation.
