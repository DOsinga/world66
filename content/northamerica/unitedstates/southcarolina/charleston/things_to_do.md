---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Charleston is best seen on foot: the **historic district** south of Broad Street is small, flat, and packed with 18th-century houses, churches and the Battery waterfront. Allow one day for the peninsula itself and another for harbour trips out to Fort Sumter, the plantations along the Ashley River (Magnolia, Middleton, Drayton Hall), and the beaches on Sullivan's Island and Folly Beach.
