---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Columbia has a mix of Civil War history, museums, and easy access to nature.

**South Carolina State Museum** — 301 Gervais St. Four floors of state history, natural science, and art in a converted textile mill. Good for a couple of hours.

**Congaree National Park** — About 20 minutes southeast of the city. Walk the elevated boardwalk through old-growth floodplain forest. Free admission, no crowds.

**Palmetto Armory** — 1802 Lincoln St. A Civil War-era weapons factory that produced muskets, rifles, and pistols bearing the palmetto tree insignia for Confederate troops.
