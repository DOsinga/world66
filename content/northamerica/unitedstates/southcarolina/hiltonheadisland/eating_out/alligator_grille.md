A well-regarded restaurant in Hilton Head's Park Plaza area, Alligator Grille serves seafood, steaks, and dishes like roasted duck spring rolls, crab and brie wontons, and a double-cut pork chop with onion jam. The wine list leans toward interesting American and Spanish selections. Service is friendly, cocktails are well made, and the desserts are homemade.
