By Automobile: From north or south, take I-95 to US 278 (South Carolina Exit 8). Follow US 278 east for approximately 17 miles until you reach Hilton Head Island. Continue on US 278 (William Hilton Parkway) for 10 miles. (Note: On Hilton Head Island, the Cross Island Parkway offers an alternative to US 278, but requires a $1.00 toll.)
