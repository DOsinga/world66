The second largest barrier island on the East Coast and a premier
vacation destination, Hilton Head Island, South Carolina has
world-famous areas such as Harbour Town in Sea Pines, Van Der Meer
Tennis Center, and others. There are also not-so-famous areas under the
sunny blue skies and within walking distance of the splendid beaches
that fringe the Atlantic Ocean. Folly Field is one such hidden
treasure, with affordable homes and villas for rent. The architecture
of Folly Field homes is pleasantly eclectic, from modern mansions to
old-fashioned beach cottages. Their interior designs are comfortable
and cozy, many with six or more bedrooms to accommodate large parties
and extended families.

As seen elsewhere on Hilton Head
Island, well-tended shrubs, palmettos, oaks, pine trees, and varieties
of flowers surround the houses, villas, and hotels. The buildings
themselves, because of town laws and conservation ordinances, do not
clash with the natural beauty of the foliage around them. The white and
caramel-colored sandy beaches are wide and clean. At Folly Field, red
sunrises on the beach energize that morning jog, brisk walk or bike
ride beside the dunes.

Located on the southern end of Hilton
Head Island, Forest Beach is within walking, running, or bicycling
distance of everything exciting Hilton Head Island has to offer. Three
miles of beach run along its drive, great for summer fun. Gather a
group of friends and family for a game of volleyball on the beach, swim
in the ocean or pools, or "work" on a tan as your chore for the day.

After
a fun day on the beach, children will enjoy cooling off in the swimming
pool at your home or villa, or a round of miniature golf and other
games at nearby Adventure Cove. A short drive in either direction from
Folly Field leads to casual and fine-dining restaurants along William
Hilton Parkway, the town's main thoroughfare. There are also
days'-worth of shopping a mile or so away at Pineland Station, Shelter
Cove Mall and Shoppes on the Parkway. For the golfer, more than 19
world-class golf courses can be found throughout Hilton Head Island.

The
vacation homes, villas, and condos of Folly Field are designed with
you, the guest, in mind. They have all the amenities and luxuries you
enjoy, with comfort and coziness to relax you during your vacation.
With the Atlantic beach nearby or just outside your window, renting a
home in the Folly Field neighborhood will make your stay on Hilton Head
Island luxurious and fun for everyone.
