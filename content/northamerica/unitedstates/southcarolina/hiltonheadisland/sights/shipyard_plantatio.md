Shipyard Plantation covers over 800 acres of land on beautiful Hilton Head Island . With a full spectrum of amenities, Shipyard Plantation is known as being both a residential as well as a resort community. There are over 2,000 homes and villas, consisting of single family homes as well as condo residences and rental properties.

The Crowne Plaza Resort offers guests and residents access to fine dining, nightclubs, meeting rooms, shops, indoor and outdoor swimming pools, and an on-site health spa. Also located in Shipyard Plantation is the Van Der Meer Shipyard Racquet Club, one of Tennis Magazine's "5- Greatest US Tennis Resorts." This tennis resort offers 23 Championship courts nestled among the magnificent oaks and beautiful landscaped grounds. There are even covered tennis courts in the Shipyard Racquet Club. Special clinics and programs have been designed to provide for thorough, compressive learning.

Shipyard is also home to 27 holes of magnificent golf. The Shipyard Golf Club is a local favorite. With three incredible 9-hole resort golf courses, built within Hilton Head Island's beautiful natural setting of majestic Carolina pine trees, this club offers golfers the challenge and beauty of a world-class golf course.

Villas are the major rental properties in Shipyard Plantation although there are a few vacation homes. Adjacent to the Crown Plaza , Shipyard Plantation has a private beach club and reserved parking for the resort's guests. Bicycling is very popular in Shipyard, and there are more than twelve miles of bike paths. If you are interested in vacationing on Hilton Head Island and would like to stay in Shipyard Plantation, please look over our website and contact us with any questions that you may have.
