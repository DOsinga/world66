Fort Thompson sits along the banks of the Missouri River near the Big Bend Dam, in the middle of the Crow Creek Native American reservation of the Lakota tribe. It is one of the poorest counties in the nation, but what the area lacks in wealth it makes up for in stark beauty.

There is not much to do entertainment-wise beyond a casino, but the barren back roads off I-90 and the hills rising behind the blue Missouri River provide genuinely memorable scenery. If you get the chance, seek out the tribal elders — their stories of the land are worth the trip alone.
