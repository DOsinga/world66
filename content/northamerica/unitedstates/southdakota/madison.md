Madison is a small city of about 7,000 people in eastern South Dakota, roughly 40 miles northwest of [Sioux Falls](/northamerica/unitedstates/southdakota/siouxfalls). It sits on the shore of Lake Herman and is the home of Dakota State University, which gives it a bit more life than most towns this size on the prairie.

Lake Herman State Park, just west of town, is a pleasant spot for swimming, fishing, and camping in summer. The park includes a historic homestead cabin from 1871.
