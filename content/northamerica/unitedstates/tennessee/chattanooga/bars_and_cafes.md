---
title: "Bars and Cafes"
type: section
---

The North Shore and Southside neighborhoods have the densest concentration of bars and coffee shops. Frazier Avenue on the North Shore is a good place to start an evening, while the Southside around Main Street has a growing cluster of craft breweries and taprooms. The Bluff View Art District is the spot for a quieter coffee or dessert -- Rembrandt's, in a small stone building with a vine-covered terrace, has been a local favorite for years.
