---
title: "Festivals"
type: section
---

Chattanooga's annual Riverbend Festival is an event at which a number of well-known artists, such as Boyz II Men, Cowboy Mouth, Trace Atkins, Kid Rock, the Newsboys, and many local artists perform over a period of nine days in June. It is one of the biggest music events in the Southeast with six stages set up downtown along the banks of the river. There are fireworks and other acts that take place throughout the week as well with plenty of activities for children to enjoy. There is always an incredible turnout for the event of the summer and is highly recommended to all ages.
