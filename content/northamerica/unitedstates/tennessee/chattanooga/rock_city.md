A garden built among ancient rock formations on the brow of Lookout Mountain. The trail winds through narrow crevices, past massive boulders, and over a suspension bridge to Lover's Leap, where the famous "See Seven States" viewpoint looks out over a vast stretch of the Tennessee Valley. Part tourist attraction, part genuine geological wonder -- the rock formations are 200 million years old.
