One may have wondered exactly what the billboards at every mile marker coming into Chattanooga mean by "See Rock City." As one of Chattanooga's biggest tourist attractions, Rock City sits on the bluff of Lookout Mountain overlooking miles and miles of Tennessee. In one particular part of Rock City, one can see seven states at one time. The attraction was created by and is still owned and run by the Chapin family who actually lives in Rock City. Tourists follow the winding trails throughout the park to come across the bridge at Lover's Leap, Mother Goose Village, the Rainbow Room, and Fairyland Caverns, only a few of the adventures at Rock City. The location of the gardens and its creativity turn Rock City into an enchanting and fun family visit.
