Billed as the steepest passenger railway in the world, with a maximum grade of 72.7%. The cars climb from the St. Elmo neighborhood at the base of Lookout Mountain to the summit in about fifteen minutes. The upper station has an observation deck with panoramic views. A practical way to reach Point Park and the mountaintop attractions without driving, and a fun ride in its own right.
