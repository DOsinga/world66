A 145-foot underground waterfall inside Lookout Mountain, reached by elevator and a guided walk through a limestone cave. The falls are dramatically lit, and the cave passage itself is interesting -- stalactites, flowstone, and narrow corridors. It is a commercial attraction and can feel crowded, but the waterfall at the end is genuinely impressive.
