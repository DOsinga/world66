** Military Memorial Museum **
** We may be small but we have more military items then you can imagine, including Bazooka Rockets-Bayonets-Guns-Knifes-Scottish & German uniforms-Hula skirt made of parachute strings to name a few. Our items represent every branch of service and an area for each war and conflict,starting with the Civil War and ending with Iraq.. Artifacts from the P.O.W. camp are also on display.. **
** So drop in and take a free tour. **
