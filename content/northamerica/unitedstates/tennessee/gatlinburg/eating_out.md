---
title: "Eating Out"
type: section
---

Gatlinburg has more restaurants than you could sample in a month of vacations. Most cater to the tourist trade and are reliably mediocre, but there are genuine highlights if you know where to look — particularly the old-school Southern spots that have been here since before the tourism boom took hold. Mountain-style cooking, smoked meats, and Southern breakfast are where the town genuinely excels.
