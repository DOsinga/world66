The first federally licensed moonshine distillery in Tennessee, opened in 2010 after the county voted wet. The tasting room on the main strip offers free samples of their range — from straight white corn whiskey to fruit-flavored jars that go down dangerously easily. It's touristy but genuinely fun, and the moonshine is decent. The holler (their second, larger location just off the strip) has live music on weekends.
