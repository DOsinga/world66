title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Gatlinburg's main draw is proximity to the [Great Smoky Mountains](/northamerica/unitedstates/tennessee/greatsmokymountains) — the most visited national park in the country. Beyond the park, the town itself offers a string of commercial attractions that range from genuinely fun to gloriously tacky. The Ripley's franchise alone has six separate venues in town, which tells you something about the atmosphere.
