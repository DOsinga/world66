A chairlift that hauls you up above the rooftops to a ridge overlooking the town and the mountains beyond. The SkyBridge at the top is one of the longest pedestrian suspension bridges in North America — 680 feet across, with a glass-floored center section for those who want to test their nerves. The views of the Smokies are excellent. Best in the morning before the haze builds.
