West Knoxville is a shopping and dining paradise for both locals and visitors. Your main areas for dining are found along Kingston Pike (Hwy 11) and N. Peter's Rd/Parkside Dr. Along these roads, you can find just about any type and price range of food you could want. A few extra special places to check out include:

Puleo's Grille

A top rated restaurant featuring steaks, italian cuisine, and seafood, all freshly prepared and delightful to the tastebuds. You'll also find a fine selection of southern favorites, soups and salads, and desserts to ease your appetite.
