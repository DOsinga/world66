There are several locations in Knoxville which host festivals, exhibits, and fairs designed to give visitors and locals alike some interesting and entertaining things to do. World's Fair Park, Chilhowee Park, The Candy Factory, Knoxville Museum of Art, and the new Knoxville Convention Center generally feature something throughout the year.

Tennessee Valley Fair

The Tennessee Valley Fair is a large event held every fall featuring a midway full of rides and games, exhibits, a farmer's area with tractor pulls and shows, concerts, and tradition fair food and fun.
