---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment in Memphis"
type: section
---

**Memphis nightlife **is very exciting in this particular city of Tennessee. Memphis comes alive at nighttime thanks to the many places where folks can enjoy music, dancing and other forms of entertainment. Here are some of the best places where people can enjoy their night in Memphis.

**B.B. King Blues Club and Grill**

Located at the heart of Times Square, the B.B. King Blues Club and Grill offers nightly Jazz music. The club is very spacious and can allow 500 seated individuals in one event. The club also features jazz musicians from all over the world. If you love to spend your night listening to interesting music, then heading off to this club is the best thing that you can do.

**Club 152 **

Located on Beale Street, this particular club is the perfect place to view live bands. Club 152 is a three-story building which offers a lot of room to all its guests. It has a big dance floor so guests can enjoy a night of dancing to the tune of DJ mixes or live bands. This particular club is frequented by a lot of local yuppies and teens, as it offers upbeat and trendy music.

**The New Daisy Theatre**

Just near Club 152, this is a place to see live theater performances and musicals hosted weekly. It also offers live band performances from local and national bands. This particular establishment does not have any dance floor, but it's still good for both music and theater arts lovers.

**Plush Club**

If you are looking for a quiet place to go out and drink casually with your friends, then you can go to the Plush Club which is also located along Beale Street. The club offers live bands but no dance floor. It also offers a wide variety of cocktails so that you can truly enjoy chatting with your friends.

**Hollywood Disco**

This particular disco is located along Beale St. and boasts of a big dance floor and its own in-house DJ that mixes the music well. It is frequented by a lot of people due to its upbeat music and big dance area.
