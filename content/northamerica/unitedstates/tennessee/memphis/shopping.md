title: Shopping
type: section
---

For upscale Memphis shopping, check out the Laurelwood Shopping Center on Popular Avenue between Perkins Extended and Grove Park Rd. Men, women, and children can find beautiful, fashionable clothing, accessories, and personal items at such stores and boutiques as Talbots, Zoe, Bella Viaggia, Chico's, Cotton Tail, James Davis, J. Jill, Joseph, R. Forsythe's, R. Sole, and Sachi. Also, for redecorating your home, take a look at Crocker Antiques & Garden or the David Lusk Gallery. When you get hungry, grab a bite at Panera Bread, Bronte, or Rafferty's Restaurant & Bar. For store hours and directions, visit their website, laurelwoodmemphis.com.
