The small storefront studio on Union Avenue where Sam Phillips recorded Elvis Presley, Johnny Cash, Jerry Lee Lewis, and Roy Orbison in the 1950s. The original equipment is still in place and the studio still functions as a recording space. Tours run every hour and are led by guides who clearly love the material — the story of how Memphis produced rock and roll, country, and blues in one cramped room is told well here.
