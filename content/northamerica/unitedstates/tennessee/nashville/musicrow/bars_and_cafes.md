**Bobby&rsquo;s Idle Hour Tavern: ****Music**

One of the best things about this little bar is that there is no cover band playing, it is simply people who are inside the bar passing a guitar around while playing their own personal tunes. Not only can will you be treated to some original music, but you can also take part in some great sing-alongs.

**Bobby&rsquo;s Idle Hour Tavern: ****Atmosphere**

One of the things that makes Bobby&rsquo;s so unique is that it is what tourists would consider a dive, and nobody even argues that it is not. However, even though it is considered a dive, it is still one of the best places to go if you are looking for a cold beer on a hot day and want to avoid all of the more expensive touristy bars and cafes. Just make sure that you bring cash because plastic is not accepted.
