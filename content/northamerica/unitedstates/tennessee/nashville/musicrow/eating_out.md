---
title: "Eating Out in Music Row"
type: section
---

There are a lot of fun **Music Row restaurants** you can find when you are in Nashville. If you enjoy the southern cuisine and music, you can find a lot of great places to stop, enjoy a meal and relax for dinner.

#### Midtown Caf&eacute;

The Midtown Caf&eacute; offers a casual dining room and serves fresh seafood, prime meats, lobster and other dishes. You can enjoy the different cuisines available there and try any of the seafood dishes. The seafood is fresh and offers a taste of southern cooking. You can also try some of the beer and wine available on the menu.

#### Goten Japanese Restaurant

If you want to find a place to try some great Asian cuisine, the Goten Japanese restaurant is a good choice. You can choose from the many rice, vegetable and meat dishes in a traditional Japanese cooking style. If you like to try something different, this is one of the Music Row restaurants you might like.

#### Otter Chicken Tenders

The Otter Chicken Tenders restaurants is a local favorite which offers many different dishes made with chicken. There are buckets of chicken available and also a wide selection of beer on tap. You can enjoy the southern food served and the fun atmosphere of the bar area.
