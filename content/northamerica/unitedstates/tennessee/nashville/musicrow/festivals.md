**Music Row Festivals in Nashville
**

Most **Music Row Festivals** are themed on the musical heritage of Music Row, a small area located south of Nashville, Tennessee. Music Row is a significant country music destination. The commercial part of Music Row is populated with businesses related to the music industry. This includes music recording studios, offices of recording labels and music-licensing firms.

#### **Songwriters Festival**

The Songwriters Festival has traditionally been hosted across the major county music locales, and Music Row is among its most popular venues. This festival is usually held in the last week of May or early June. The emphasis is on live performances. Visitors can participate in conferences and networking which explore the evolution of country music. In the open house event, visitors are given the chance to perform on the stage. Although county music is the main theme, songwriters across all genres can be found here. People who reach the venue a few days in advance can register for a wide range of instructor-led classes. These workshops are popular among budding songwriters and musicians.

#### **National Folk Festival**

This festival moves around from city to city but will be on Music Row for the next several years. This festival can be understood as the oldest, multicultural festival that aims to unite people across different ethnic groups. It is self-proclaimed to be the longest running event of its kind in the U.S. The event includes numerous pavilions showcasing the best in regional food, storytelling formats and demonstrations.
