title: "Nightlife and Entertainment in Music Row"
type: section
---

**Music Row Nightlife** is made up of different bars and restaurants. Music Row is a hub for the Nashville music industry and it is located at the southwest of Nashville. Being a home to many musicians, this place is full of places that play enchanting music to amuse the audience.

#### Lime

This Latin spot, located at 1904 Broadway, is the best of its kind giving amazing ambiance to the audience and a great night life. People can find a wide array of cocktails and special features that keep them coming back to have fun in Lime. The popular attractions of this Music Row nightlife place are their Margarita del Diablo, cocktails, nachos and Red Sangria. Though the lowest price range between $16 and $25, it is most preferred bar and restaurant for the Latin cuisine in Nashville.

#### Broadway Brewhouse and Mojo Grill

Known for a wide range of brews, Broadway Brewhouse and Mojo Grill has a lot to offer. The new branch, at 8098 Highway 100 in Nashville, is equipped with a 60-feet-long bar and about 72 beer taps, making it a favorite place for Music Row nightlife. Other branches are located in the downtown at 317 Broadway, Broadway West at 7108 Charlotte Park and 1900 Broadway with the lowest price of $9.

#### Flying Saucer

Flying Saucer a popular bar in the old baggage area of the station and is housed down Broadway from Music Row. This bar gets frequent visitors as it is considered a beer barrel with over 200 types of beer. People always seem to order liquor but the best choice would be the beer.

Flying Saucer's address is 111 10th Avenue South, Nashville, TN.

#### Music Row Bar and Grill

Adding to the vibe of nightlife, Music Row Bar and Grill is located near the Vanderbilt University Library, making it handy for the staff and students. One of the features that make this section unique is that it allows the members to smoke. It is a great place for singles for the quality of crowd that gathers there. There are night shows for which the doors are generally opened by 8 p.m. and there is an age limit. Sometimes it is over 18 and other times it is over 21, depending on the show. Some of the popular shows are Jenifer Stroke&rsquo;s pop show, Team Illuminati&rsquo;s rock show, Kari Smith&rsquo;s country music and many more.

Music Row Bar and Grill's address is 416 21st Avenue South, Nashville, TN 37212
