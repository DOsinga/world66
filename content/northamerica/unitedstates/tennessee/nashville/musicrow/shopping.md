**Music Row Shopping** gives people an intricate look of the Nashville music industry. Music Row is situated southwest of downtown Nashville, Tennessee. The street address of Music Row is 16th/17th Ave. South - Division Street and Music Square around Demonbreun Street, Nashville, TN. Music Row is famous due to its musical shows, musical business and entertainment. A person can find music memorabilia and musical instruments for sale. Tourists will get a feel of the Country Music Capital of the World and can also enjoy shopping a variety of items including: clothes, food, and musical instruments.

#### Pangaea

Pangaea is a shopping mall adjacent to Music Row and its address is 1721 21st Ave S Nashville TN 37212. A lot of items are being sold here like women's clothing, home decoration items, gifts, household items, and music memorabilia. A buyer can find candles, perfumes, cards, jewelry and many other souvenirs.

#### William's Fine Violins

William's Fine Violins is situated at 1218 17th Avenue South Nashville, Tennessee 37212. This shop sells different types of instruments instruments like violins. People who are fond of music can also get lessons to learn how to play violin. Different types of musical instruments and accessories are also being sold in William's Fine Violins.

#### Edgehill Village

This fantastic shopping center is situated at Edgehill Avenue and Villa PI Nashville, TN 37246. There is a variety of boutiques which possess great dresses and designs. It is an upscale shopping mall and offers items according to the budgets of purchasers.

This shopping center has a bubble tea caf&eacute; called Fat Straw, an studio for art and music and a bakery that sells delicious desserts. A person may find versatile type of dresses and clothes from the boutiques, furniture and the items needed for a wedding except wedding dresses. This Village adds comfort and style to any Music Row shopping spree.

#### J & J's Market & Caf&eacute;

It is situated at 1912 Broadway, adjacent to Music Row. This is famous for its caf&eacute; and the book store that carry different national and international publications, magazines and newspapers. This shop also sells exotic and international candies, cigarettes and different beverages.
