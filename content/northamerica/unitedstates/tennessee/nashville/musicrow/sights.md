---
title: "Sights in Music Row"
type: section
---

Nashville, Tennessee is home to a huge number of different **Music Row sights**. Music Row is the heart of downtown Nashville, full of country music bars and museums. There are also a variety of other sights and important activities to do in this part of historic and beautiful Nashville city. Read on for a few of the must-see sights in this lovely city.

**Studio B**

Studio B is the RCA recording studio that sports a roster of recording artists several dozen long. Many of the most famous and important country music artists recorded here during the several decades of its history. The studio provides tours and information on some of the most famous artists to have recorded here, as well as recordings of those hits that came out of this important studio.

**Country Music Hall of Fame**

The Country Music Hall of Fame has been recently relocated a few blocks away from Music Row proper, but this special museum is still a must-see for any traveler in the city of Nashville. The Country Music Hall of Fame is the primary museum that honors country music artists from throughout the generations. See exhibits on some of your favorite and most famous country music artists that have a history in Nashville, leading up to present-day superstars.

**Grand Ole Opry**

The Grand Ole Opry is the most important and single most popular tourist attraction in Nashville. This is something you simply must do in the city, whether or not you're a fan of country music. The Grand Ole Opry has played host to virtually all of the major country music stars that have performed in this city over the years. There are still regular performances here as well as tours and other historic information.

**Opryland Hotel Gardens**

If you're interested in getting outside and enjoying the famous Nashville, Tennessee outdoors, the Opryland Hotel Gardens is a great place to do so. The Opryland Hotel is a beautiful and historic hotel situated on a large expanse of gardens. Whether you're interested in relaxing outside or going for a scenic walk, the gardens provide a great opportunity for adventurers and tourists of all ages.

Music Row in Nashville is full of wonderful sights to see, but no trip to Nashville would be complete without an exploration of the rest of the city as well. Be sure to check out the sites outside of Music Row to make your trip complete.**
