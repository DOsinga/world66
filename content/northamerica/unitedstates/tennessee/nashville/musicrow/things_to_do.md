---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Nashville, Tennessee is famous for the wide variety of activities on **Music Row**; **things to do** on this site typically focus around music. This part of Nashville, just southwest of downtown, is full of historic sites with significance to the country music industry. However, for the moor adventurous tourist to the Nashville area, there are also activities in this city with other draws as well. Read on for a few of the best outdoorsy activities in the Nashville, Tennesse area.

**Opryland Hotel Gardens**

Opryland Hotel is a historic landmark in the Nashville area and has played host to may famous musicians and other celebrities throughout the many years of its history. There are a variety of things to do on the grounds of this beautiful site. Visit the Opryland Hotel reception for details about the various activities that are available.

**Nashville Zoo**

The Zoo in Nashville is an award-winning and unique area dedicated to safely and comfortably housing hundreds of different animals. While the zoo itself is smaller than many others in major American cities, it provides an excellent outdoor adventure, particularly for those with families. Admission is very cheap, and there is plenty of shade for those who aren't interested in staying the sun for too long.

**Radnor Lake State Park**

The state park at Radnor Lake is a useful outdoor site for those who have had their fill of indoor sites in the Nashville area. This lovely man-made lake sports plenty of hiking trails and watersports activities. Big time adventure seekers may not find their thrills are satisfied here, but the place is a good excursion for those looking to get out of the city without traveling too far.

**Edwin and Percy Warner Parks**

Both of these parks, located just a few miles outside of Music Row, sport a variety of different outdoor activities, including hiking and more. This set of activities is appropriate for families and more adventurous younger tourists both.

**Belmont Mansion**

A historic mansion belonging to a wealthy Nashville family, this old mansion is a lovely site situated on a large and beautiful grounds. The grounds have several different hiking trails and other types of outdoor activities as well, located right within the heart of Nashville and close to Music Row.

Be sure to look at the many country music sites in Music Row and other parts of Nashville to make your vacation complete.
