The Bluebird Cafe is a small listening room in the Green Hills neighbourhood, about 6 miles south of downtown. Since 1982 it has been one of the most important venues in Nashville for songwriters performing their own material in an intimate setting — the audience is expected to stay quiet while musicians play. Many of country music's biggest names performed here early in their careers, and the venue was featured in the TV series Nashville. Capacity is under 200; advance reservations are strongly recommended.
