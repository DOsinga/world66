---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Nashville's headline attractions are centred on music history, and they are genuinely exceptional. The Ryman Auditorium and the Country Music Hall of Fame are not to be missed. Beyond the music, Centennial Park and its full-scale Parthenon replica make for an unexpected afternoon. The Bluebird Cafe requires planning ahead — shows sell out — but is worth the effort for anyone serious about the songwriting tradition that made this city famous.
