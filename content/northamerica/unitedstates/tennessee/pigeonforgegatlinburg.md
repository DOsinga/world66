---
title: "Pigeon Forge & Gatlinburg"
type: location
---

Pigeon Forge is a tourist-trap on the road from Knoxville to Gatlinburg. There are all kinds of activities, from Dollywood (Dolly Parton's amusement park) to Ripley's Believe It or Not.

Gatlinburg is also a tourist trap, but much less tacky. It is a small, scenic town right at the base of the Great Smoky Mountains and offers great outdoor activities in addition to candy shop(pe)s and souvenir stores.
