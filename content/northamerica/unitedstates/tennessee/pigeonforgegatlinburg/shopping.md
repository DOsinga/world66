---
title: "Shopping in Pigeon Forge & Gatlinburg"
type: section
---

**Pigeon Forge & Gatlinburg shopping **has plenty to offer
everyone from the extreme shopping enthusiast to the traveler looking to
bring home that one-of-a-kind souvenir. No matter your budget and what
you're looking for, you're sure to enjoy shopping at at least one of
these locations:

**Pigeon Forge Outlet Malls**

This is where the shopaholics head for incredible deals on hot name
brands, even if they're usually just a little out of season. Casual
shoppers, too, will find the outlet malls a nice place to relax and grab
a bit to eat. The mall has mostly clothes stores, such as Dress Barn,
Casual Corner, Hanes, Rue 21, Levi's and Maidenform. There are also
stores that sell books, jewelry, housewares, music and more. Don't
forget to stop by the Smoky Mountain Brewery outlet for more unique--and
delicious--souvenirs. Prices are affordable for the most part and much
cheaper than you'll find at a traditional mall. There are four main
outlet mall locations, so there's no need to shop at the same complex
twice!

**Three Bears General Store**

No one can visit the Smoky Mountains without making a stop at the
Three Bears General Store in Pigeon Forge. Smoky Mountain souvenirs,
from the cheesy to the beautiful, are sold in bulk here, as well as
fresh and homemade fudge, candy, ice cream, popcorn and deli food.
Arcade games can keep the kids entertained while you shop. You can also
have your picture taken in an "old-time" sepia or black and white
fashion. Perhaps most surprisingly, you and your children can view and
feed actual black bears in a large habitat outside the shop for only $3
admission per person. Talk about an experience you can only get in the
Smoky Mountains!

**All Sauced Up**

After having some Smoky Mountains cooking, you're sure to want to be
able to bring the experience back home, but you'll never be able to get
the sauce and spices just right. All Sauced Up in Gatlinburg has over
4000 different items, 1000 of which are just different varieties of hot
sauces! If hot sauces aren't your thing, there are jams and jellies,
peanut butters, salsas and salad dressings, too.

**Gatlinburg Mountain Mall**

If the traditional outdoor shopping mall experience is your thing,
Gatlinburg Mountain Mall offers that and more. The entire mall is given a
"mountain" theme, so that the architecture, ambiance and 30-foot tall
indoor water fountain reminds you of how close you are to the gorgeous
Smoky Mountains (and makes shopping so much more fun). Dozens of
merchants sell everything from clothes to jewelry to souvenirs exclusive
to the Smoky Mountains. Prices are generally moderate.
