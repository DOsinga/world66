The obvious day trip from Amarillo is Palo Duro Canyon, which lies just outside [Canyon](/northamerica/unitedstates/texas/amarillo/canyon), 25 miles to the southeast. The canyon is dramatic enough that it is genuinely surprising more people don't know about it — 800-foot walls of red, orange, and yellow rock carved by the Prairie Dog Town Fork of the Red River. Allow a full day if you want to hike.
