Arlington sits midway between [Dallas](/northamerica/unitedstates/texas/dallas) and [Fort Worth](/northamerica/unitedstates/texas/fort_worth) and is no small suburb at 350,000 residents. It is home to the Texas Rangers' baseball stadium, AT&T Stadium (where the Dallas Cowboys play), and Six Flags Over Texas.
