---
title: 'Getting Around'
type: section
---

Austin-Bergstrom International Airport is 6 miles southeast of the city center, with buses, taxis, shuttles, and car rentals available.

Capital Metro runs the city's public bus network with inexpensive neighborhood express and downtown routes. Parking downtown is difficult and enforcement is aggressive, so look for municipal garages or take a cab. Rideshare services are widely available.

Cycling is a viable option in many parts of the city, with bike rentals available in the city center and an expanding network of bike lanes.
