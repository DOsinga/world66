Not only is Austin a business and technology hub, the
city also thrives as a base of alternative culture in a traditionally
conservative state, bringing music, arts, and theater to its youthful
residents all year round. Austin’s music scene is the
heartbeat of the city and the official slogan of Austin as “**The
Live Music Capital of the World**” couldn’t be more appropriate.
An endless stream of musicians has come out of Austin and there is a
huge variety of live music events at every time of year.
