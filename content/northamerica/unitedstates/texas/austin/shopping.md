---
title: 'Shopping'
type: section
---

Austin's shopping leans quirky and independent. South Congress (SoCo) is the standout, with vintage shops, handmade goods, and the Armadillo Market for all things Texan. First Thursday each month brings live music and late-night shopping to the strip.

**2nd Street District**

A hip downtown area with designer boutiques and contemporary furniture shops alongside fine dining.

**Artist's Market**

Also known as the Renaissance Market on 23rd Street, this open-air market showcases Austin's independent craft makers and artisans daily. Paintings, textiles, and handmade curiosities, often with the artists at work during your visit.

**The Arboretum**

A small, park-like mall with about 40 stores, fountains, and a picnic area. Good for basics and a pleasant place to browse.
