Sightseeing spots in Austin, Texas

### [Congree Avenue Bridge Bats](/northamerica/unitedstates/texas/austin/sights/congree_avenue_bri)

The Congress Avenue
Bridge provides shelter for the largest
urban colony of Mexican free-tailed bats in the United
States which reside underneath the bridge. At dusk from March to October visitors can
watch up to 1.5 million bats make their nocturnal flight out from their home
under the bridge to feed on over 10,000 pounds of insects, bringing a whole new
show to the Austin nightlife scene. Tours of the city are available that end at
the Congress Avenue Bridge for the spectacular bat flight, with the best bat
watching opportunities on dry nights from July to September.
