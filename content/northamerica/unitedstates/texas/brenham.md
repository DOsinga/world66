Travel Guide

If you like ice cream and are in Texas, you most likely will indulge in Blue Bell, maybe even while visiting Brenham. Origination of Blue Bell with a population of 14,000+, Brenham offers small-town charm while slowly growing into a metropolis of Starbucksaholics. Besides Blue Bell ("the best ice cream in the country," by the way), Brenham's countryside offers beauty with green pastures in the summer and blue pastures in the spring (for all the bluebonnets, of course!). Like most small towns, "downtown" offers quaint antique shops, boutiques, restaurants, and the necessary court house in the middle of the three block area, which of course is decorated just as you would expect of a small town at Christmas. Located between Houston and Austin along Highway 290, Brenham will welcome you with delight.
