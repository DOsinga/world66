Brenham is the birthplace of Blue Bell ice cream, and that alone makes it worth a stop. Located between [Houston](/northamerica/unitedstates/texas/houston) and [Austin](/northamerica/unitedstates/texas/austin) along Highway 290, this small town of about 14,000 offers antique shops, boutiques, and restaurants around a charming courthouse square. The countryside is beautiful: green pastures in summer, carpets of bluebonnets in spring.
