One of the largest art museums in the US, with a collection spanning 5,000 years and strong holdings in American art, pre-Columbian works, and 20th-century painting and sculpture. The building itself -- designed by Edward Larrabee Barnes and completed in 1984 -- is worth a visit. Admission to the permanent collection is free.
