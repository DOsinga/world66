Dallas has a conventions, festivals, and events that are so large in number and variety that perhaps only LA or NYC provides more.

### NTPCUG (North Texas PC Users Group)

The NTPCUG hosts many events, including SIGs for XP, Word, Excel, Access, PDA's, e-Commerce, and more. Most events are on the third Saturday of each month; some are on the second Saturday.
