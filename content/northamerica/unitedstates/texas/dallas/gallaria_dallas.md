The Galleria Dallas is one of the city's landmark shopping destinations, anchored by a soaring glass atrium and a full-size ice skating rink at its center. Over 200 stores range from high-end department stores to specialty boutiques. The food options go well beyond typical mall fare, and the ice rink makes it a popular family outing even for people who aren't there to shop.
