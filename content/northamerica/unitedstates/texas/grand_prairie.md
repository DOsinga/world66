Travel Guide

**Grand Prairie** is a city in the Prairies and Lakes region of Texas.

Discover plenty of kid-friendly things to do along with an ample array of
tourist attractions in Grand Prairie, Texas and in the surrounding Dallas/Fort
Worth area. Experience fun in the sun at Lynn Creek Park, the shops and museums
of the Dallas West End Historic District and an outrageous water playground at
Splash Factory. Get more details about what neighborhood can offer you by
checking out the list below.

Travel 15 miles east of our Grand Prairie, Texas hotel and you’ll arrive at one
of the hippest and most popular destinations for tourists and locals alike. The
Dallas West End Historic District features exquisitely restored historical
buildings that house everything from charming retail shops and interesting
museums to exciting nightclubs and first-class restaurants.

Cedar Hill
State Park is only six miles from our Grand Prairie, Texas hotel but once you’re
there, you’ll feel like you’re a million miles away. This 1,800-acre nature
preserve is situated on the 7500-acre Joe Pool Reservoir. Enjoy more than 100
miles of shoreline plus a boatload of activities, such as mountain biking,
hiking, swimming, fishing and bird watching. Cedar Hill State Park is an outdoor
enthusiast’s dream

Fun amusement parks, scenic state parks and thoroughly entertaining thoroughbred
racing parks are within 10 miles of our Grand Prairie, Texas hotel.
