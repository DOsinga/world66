Houston's sprawl means every neighborhood has its own drinking personality. Montrose is bohemian and eclectic, Washington Avenue is young and rowdy, and the Heights offers craft cocktails in cozy settings.

**Spotlight Karaoke Club**

One of the biggest yet most intimate karaoke palaces you will find anywhere, with private rooms and a massive song menu. Owner Mr. Lee has built a following that crosses every demographic. Located on Westheimer Road and Fountainview, just outside Loop 610 about two miles west of the Galleria.
