Brazosport is the name for the region south of Houston in Brazoria County. It includes the cities of Clute, Lake Jackson, Angleton, Freeport, Brazoria, Surfside Beach, and Quintana Beach among others. The beaches here are quieter and less developed than Galveston, and Surfside offers some of the closest Gulf swimming to Houston.
