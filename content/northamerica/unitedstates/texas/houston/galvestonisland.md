A barrier island about an hour south of Houston, Galveston has beaches, a historic downtown strand district, and Moody Gardens with its aquarium pyramid, rainforest pyramid, and IMAX theaters. The Lone Star Flight Museum is next door. It makes an easy day trip from Houston or a weekend destination in its own right.
