### Spotlight Karaoke Club

One of the biggest, yet most intimate (in private rooms) karaoke
palace in the world (and I've been to them in the Far East). Mr. Lee's
(a native Houstonian of Chinese ancestry) Spotlight karaoke. Folks of
all nationalities (including a surprisingly large # of good ole boys
and georgeous women) test their pipes on singing a HUGE song menu.

Located on Westheimer Rd and Fountainview just outside of loop 610 about 2 miles west of the Galleria.
