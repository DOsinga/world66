---
title: "People"
type: section
---

You could never hope to find a friendlier people in such a large city. It is evident when you visit, and the norm if you live here. Call it Texas, Call it Houston, but the atmosphere is a bit different, and it's in quite a good way. This is a place where people will still wave to you when you wave to them or converse with you if you start talking to them.

The city is home to consulates and trade offices from 77 different nations.
