Texas Attractions:

There is no shortage of exciting things to see and do around Plano, Texas. Just minutes from NYLO Plano at Legacy hotel, visitors will find a host of attractions to suit any interest. Enjoy everything from shopping at Stonebriar Centre to live concert entertainment and professional sports games at American Airlines Center, to cultural attractions such as the Dallas Museum of Art and Angelika Film Center.
