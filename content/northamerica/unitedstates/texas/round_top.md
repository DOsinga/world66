Travel Guide

Round Top is a small burg of approximately 90 inhabitants, although on weekends it is a bit more crowded with day-trippers from Houston and Austin. It is well-known locally for its smattering of restaurants and cafes, its antique fair and the local german heritage. Especially nice is Henkel Square; a collection of restored period homes just off the main square which has its own extant district courthouse, a rarity in Texas, famous for its beautiful county courthouses. Also nice is the historic Winedale area nearby on the road to Belleville. Nearby towns are Fayetteville, Warrenton, Rutersville, Oldenburg, and La Grange, the county seat of Fayette County.
