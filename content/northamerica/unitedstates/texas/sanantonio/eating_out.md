### [La Palmita](/northamerica/unitedstates/texas/sanantonio/eating_out/la_plamita)

This is true example of a place where the locals dine.

La Palmita has been serving classic Mexican food to their friends and neigbors for over sixty years. It is still family owned and family run. Make sure you ask for Abe!

Besides pretty much anything on the menu, the Guadalajara plate is highly recommended. It's the ultimate dish! And if you're wallet is not that fat, any taco ordered is amazingly delicous and astonishingly affordable.

Note: La Palmita is closed on Sunday.

### [Paesano's Restaurant](/northamerica/unitedstates/texas/sanantonio/eating_out/paesanos_restaura)

You never know who will see at Paesano's from David Robinson to .... Me!

Although it's an Italian Restaurant the Filet Mignon is the best in town. As a Medium Well Steak eater, I have ALWAYS been able to cut my steak with a fork.

Other must try dishes:

Giant Calamari - Giant and Tender.. The way Calamari should be

Locals Secret:

Order the Special Salad. It's not on the menu but is always a crowd pleaser.
