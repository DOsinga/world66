---
title: 'Shopping'
type: section
---

San Antonio offers everything from River Walk boutiques to open-air flea markets. The city's shopping reflects its cultural mix, with Western curios alongside designer fashion.

**Rivercenter Mall**

Built to resemble a monolithic glass house right on the River Walk, this mall lets you watch boats glide by while you shop. Macy's anchors it, with familiar brands throughout.

**Mission Market**

The best-kept secret on San Antonio's south side. This open-air flea market sells handmade crafts, household items, and collectibles that change daily. Great for treasure hunting.

**Alamo Quarry Market**

A former factory complex repurposed into a shopping center, still marked by its original smokestacks. Mix of well-known retailers and specialty stores, plus restaurants and theaters.

**The Shops at La Cantera**

San Antonio's high-end option, an open-air mall with designer stores like Hugo Boss and Burberry alongside four department stores including Nordstrom and Neiman Marcus.

**Buckhorn Museum Curio Store**

Described as "the oddest store in the world," this is the place for tanned leather, handmade silver belt buckles, and anything that screams Texas or Wild West.
