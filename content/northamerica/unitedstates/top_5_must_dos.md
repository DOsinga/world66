The United States is home to an eclectic line-up of tourist
attractions and must do’s sure to amaze any traveler. Here are the top 5 must do’s
that will help visitors savor the spirited charm, relentless energy and
stimulating enthusiasm of the invincible North American nation.

**Fishing Trip in Bayou Country**

Take a fishing boat trip of the pristine Bayou Country region in
South-East Louisiana. The place is one of the most unique spots in the
country and is a huge mass of wetland in stark contrast to the general
topography of the region. Take a walk down the longest street on
earth-the Bayou Lafourche that networks several towns. The local
culture with its delectable and spicy cuisine, Cajun music and tap
dancing combined with fascinating boat tours and sprawling plantations
makes this one of the most distinctive and versatile attractions in the
nation.

**Climb the Mt.Katahdin**

Take an invigorating trek of the 5,267 feet Mt Katahdin, housed in
the Baxter State Park territory in the Northern region of Maine. The
best time to climb is between July to October when the climate is
pleasant and lush foliage begins to accumulate in the region. The
Baxter Park is an expansive reserve of fishing, country camping,
canoeing and trail walking through the panoramic forests. Combine
adventure and natural beauty and you have a holiday that can rarely go
wrong.

**Walk in the Giant Redwoods**

There are redwoods and there are the Giant Redwoods in California, a
lush ecological zone nestling some of the loftiest trees ever
chronicled. The magnificent green wonder growing along the Californian
coast tower the Big Sur landscape and carry on all the way to the South
of Oregon. The enormous Giant Sequoias found in more arid regions of
the country are worth witnessing along with a drive through the
Garberville countryside. For a scenic trek, go on trail snaking through
Sequoia Park and Redwood National Park.

**The Glamor Quotient**

You can’t travel to the United States without experiencing the
glitz, glamor and razzmatazz of its glossy entertainment
district-Hollywood. Located in the scenic northwestern region of Los
Angeles, California, the shimmering region is home to several plush
movie studios, historic theatres, swank neighborhoods, large production
houses and film processing centers. In short, this is the nucleus of
the American film industry. Watch premiers and concerts at prime
theatres and enjoy the nightlife and Hollywood Walk of Museum-a chic
entertainment museum that celebrates the nation’s passion for
performing arts.

**Walt Disney World**

Though clichéd you can’t not visit the largest and slickest theme
amusement park in the world. The fantasy destination is a permanent
fixture on the wish of almost all the children in the world with its
enchanting theme parks, animal shows, live events, thrilling rides,
luxury resorts, golf courses and avant-garde studios.

United States is a kaleidoscope of ravishing natural beauty, slick
technology, versatile topography and timeless sophistication that
present several opportunities for exploration in this eclectic nation.

About Santa Fe

Santa Fe, New Mexico is one of the most distinctive destinations in the U.S., known for its indigenous architecture, Native American and Hispanic heritage, vibrant arts community, and beautiful natural setting. Santa Fe was designated the United States’ first UNESCO Creative City, in Folk Art, Crafts, and Design, on July 6, 2005.
