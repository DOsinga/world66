The best resource for sights, hotels, restaurants, bars, what to do and see

Situated in the beautiful Ogden Valley, Huntsville is surrounded by natural beauty. Nearby are the Monte Cristo Mountains, loads of National Forest lands, and three ski resorts. Pineview Reservoir provides boating, fishing, and camping. The Trappist Monastery is a place for solitude and reflection, and has a wonderful gift shop. Finally, the Shooting Star Saloon is Utah's oldest saloon, and a fine place to get a bite to eat.
