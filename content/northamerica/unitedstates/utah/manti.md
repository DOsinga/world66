The best resource for sights, hotels, restaurants, bars, what to do and see

Founded in 1849 by Mormon Pioneers, Manti is the home of the
Manti temple. The city also hosts a number of annual events such as the Mormon
Miracle Pageant and the Sanpete County Fair. Manti boasts a rich history and
continues to grow every day.

Aside from the Miracle Pageant and the County Fair, Manti also hosts the
following events in 2009:

Easter Egg Hunt (Saturday, April 11 at the City Park)

Mormon Miracle Pageant (June 18-20 and 23-27)

Old Fashioned Fourth of July (Saturday, July 4)

Manti Mountain ATV Ride (August)

Christmas Light Parade (Friday, November 27)
