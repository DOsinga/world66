Located at Hill Air Force Base south of Ogden near Roy, this free museum has one of the best collections of American military aircraft in the country. Two huge hangars showcase everything from canvas-covered biplanes to the SR-71 Blackbird, the fastest airplane ever built. The collection of Cold War "century series" jets is particularly impressive, and a lineup of heavy aircraft includes a rare B-47 Stratojet and a B-52 Stratofortress.
