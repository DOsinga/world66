Once the crossroads of the west, this grand station still captures the glamour and excitement of rail travel. Inside, museum exhibits cover railroad and automotive history, and special events like model railroad shows often take place in the main lobby. Enormous locomotives from the glory days of the Union Pacific sit outside, and the historic 25th Street district is just across the street.
