type: section

Whether you live in the Park City area, or you are planning your
vacation to this beautiful mountain town, it's guaranteed that you will
be overwhelmed with the many dining options offered. With 300 miles of
mountain biking, the best snow on earth, and over 120 restaurants, Park
City has been described as “a paradise for outdoor enthusiasts who love
to eat”.

Finally,
there is now a totally comprehensive online restaurant guide that will
make your life easy and your stomach satisfied. SkiTownRestaurants.com offers
restaurant menus, dining coupons, restaurant pictures, and user
reviews. Plus, if you need any help with your dining decisions, use
the “Ask a Local” feature! They are incredibly quick at responding to
your questions and provide true insider information.

SkiTownRestaurants.com
is owned and operated by 2 Park City locals who have spent their lives
dedicated to the resort lifestyles, good food, and deep snow.
