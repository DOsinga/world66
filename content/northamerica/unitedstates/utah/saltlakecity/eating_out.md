**The Red Iguana**

Locally owned for many years, the Red Iguana prides itself on providiing authentic Mexican food at a great price. If you want good food and some money left in your pocket for a movie, this is the place the locals go.

address: 736 W. North Temple

tel: 801-322-1489

PriceOfMenu: $8 - $15 per entree

priceofmenu: $8 - $15 per entree

**Cafe Rio**

Cafe Rio is amazing. It is the hot spot you need to visit. Get the Pork Burrito Enchilada style. You won't be disappointed. It's got a sweet atmosphere. Check it out, but don't be surprised when you see a line out the door, especially on weekends.

**The Wasatch Broiler**

With a Mediterranean flair, the three locations each serve a distinct clientele for the Wasatch Broiler. Salads are large and fresh, just like the fish. The chicken and other meats are marinated for days in natural juices and spices. The lemon and paprika mixture provide an agreeable mixture to the palate. Each atmosphere is different, from semi formal to a quaint patio near a river. Save space for the desserts as they are wonderful.
