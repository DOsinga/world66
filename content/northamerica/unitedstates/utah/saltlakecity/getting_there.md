---
title: "Getting There in Salt Lake City"
type: section
---

Making Salt Lake City travel a reality has never been easier! Many airline carriers provide service to and from Salt Lake City International Airport. Southwest Airlines, known for its low budget fares, flies in and out of Salt Lake City. Other airline carriers are American, Continental, Delta, Frontier, jetBlue, United, and US Airways. Amtrak services Salt Lake City and has a train station at 340 South 600 West. For station hours, visit their website at amtrak.com. If you decide to travel by bus, Greyhound provides service between over 300 cities nationwide. To see scheduling, go to greyhound.com.
