Montpelier is the smallest state capital of the United States with a population of less than 10,000 people. The ton is located in a picturesque valley along the Winooski River in Central Vermont. The size and historic neighborhoods make it a wonderful city to visit. Montpelier was founded in the 1700s which has provided the city with a rich history and a large inventory of historic buildings. The city has a large number of buildings included on the national register of historic places, many of which can be seen along one of the historic walking tours arranged by the tourist office.


 
 Montpelier is also located in the heart of Vermont ski country and is a good place to start your skiing holiday in Vermont.
