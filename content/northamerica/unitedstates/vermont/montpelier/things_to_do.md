The Lazy Pear Gallery at 154 Main Street is a family friendly art
gallery that everyone can enjoy. Set in a beautifully renovated
Victorian mansion, the gallery exhibits works by Vermont artists with
fun, playful, and humorous styles. Highlights include the
community art board where visitors can express their creative
inspirations; the edible and inedible food art in the pastry case which
includes a daily viewing menu of light pastries; and the streamed in
jazz music that gives the gallery the feel of a cafe more than a
museum. The art work is of a very high quality but prices range
to fit all budgets, original works of art are available for as
little as $15. The gallery exhibits new works on a monthly basis as
well as a permant group of artists. More information and
direction can be viewed at www.lazypear.com.
