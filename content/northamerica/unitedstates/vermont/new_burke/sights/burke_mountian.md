Burke Mountain is a ski resort in Vermont's Northeast Kingdom with a 2,000-foot vertical drop and around 30 trails. It draws a loyal following for its uncrowded slopes, well-groomed cross-country trails, and lack of the commercial buzz you find at bigger Vermont resorts. In summer the auto toll road to the summit offers panoramic views across the Kingdom.
