Kingdom Trails is one of the premier mountain biking destinations in the eastern United States, spread across more than 100 miles of trails on the hills around East Burke. The network runs over private land -- farmers and landowners grant access in exchange for a modest day pass -- and covers everything from smooth cross-country singletrack to steep downhill runs. In winter the trails convert to cross-country skiing and snowshoeing. The nearby Burke Mountain ski area adds lift-served mountain biking in summer. The village of East Burke has grown around the trails, with bike shops, cafes, and lodges catering to riders.
