Westmore is a tiny town on Vermont's fourth largest lake. Containing the Lake Willoughby National Recreation Area, as well as several state parks it is a tourist and recreational mecca. In the heart of the Northeast Kingdom it is a favorite spot for vacationing Vermonters.
