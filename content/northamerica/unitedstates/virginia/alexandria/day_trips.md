---
title: "Day Trips in Alexandria"
type: section
---

Alexandria attractions include the scenic George Washington Mount Vernon Estate and Gardens, which is an easy day trip from Alexandria. Open every day of the year, it is a living history lesson and provides information into George Washington's life. Located about 15 miles down the Potomac River in northern Virginia, the estate includes the mansion building and several out buildings, including the slave quarters, the stables, a greenhouse and the kitchen as well as a working 18th century farm. It also provides beautiful views of the Potomac River, which winds its way past the estate to the Atlantic Ocean 300 miles away.
