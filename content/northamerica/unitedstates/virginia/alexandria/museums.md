---
title: "Museums in Alexandria"
type: section
---

A fascinating and educational experience can be found at the Alexandria Black History Museum, which is one of many of Alexandria activities. The history of the African-American in Virginia is fully explored, including the many contributions that African Americans have made both to Virginia and to the United States. Exhibits stretch from the late 1700s to today and focus on slavery, abolition, and the civil rights movement and other timely topics. The museum is made up of a reading room and library as well as a heritage park and is well worth a visit.
