---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment in Alexandria"
type: section
---

Alexandria nightlife includes one of the best options for theater and dining. The West End Dinner Theater combines talent and superb food to insure a perfect evening of dining and acting too. This family oriented and affordable choice makes sure to entertain, but before the show even starts, there’s a terrific dinner, including salad, main course, and of course dessert. Another dessert is available during the intermission at an extra charge for those with a big sweet tooth. Each show combines music and singing with great acting for a thrilling theatrical experience.
