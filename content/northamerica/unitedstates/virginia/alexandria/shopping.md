---
title: "Shopping in Alexandria"
type: section
---

Alexandria shopping would not be complete without a visit to Hysteria in trendy Old Town. A hip woman’s clothing boutique, it offers designer labels and high end styles for every occasion. Open on a daily basis, clients make appointments to shop and see their private collection. While there, customers are treated with cocktails and hors d’oeuvres. While unwinding, clients will check out clothes designed by the likes of Diane von Furstenberg, Autumn Cashmere, Trina Turk, Tory Burch and many others. And the townhouse shop is conveniently located in central Old Town close to restaurants and theaters.
