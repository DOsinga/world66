Friendship Firehouse MuseumOne of many Alexandria attractions is the Friendship Firehouse Museum. Established in 1774, it was the first volunteer fire company in the city. The current museum building was first built in 1855 and then remodeled in 1871. Visitors to the Friendship Firehouse Museum will see the historic building, which was so important to the community and which was renovated in 1992. The museum is filled with the charm of past, especially its Engine Room where hand-powered fire engines, leather water buckets, and parts of early rubber hose speak to the fire-fighting techniques of the past
