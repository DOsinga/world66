title: "Day Trips"
type: section
---

Arlington attractions are plentiful since it is a short distance from Washington DC. One such attraction is the Washington Monument. Named after the first president George Washington, it can be seen from up to thirty miles away. Admission is free, but everyone visiting will need to obtain a ticket. Tickets are given starting at 8:30 a.m. and are same day and timed. You can pick up to six tickets at a time and can get them for a specific time. The most convenient way to get to the Washington Monument is by public transportation. Check out their website npr.gov/wamo.
