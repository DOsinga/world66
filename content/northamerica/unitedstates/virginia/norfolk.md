Norfolk has a nice airport. There is a brand new mall downtown.

The [Hampton Roads Naval Museum](/northamerica/unitedstates/virginia/norfolk/hamptonroadsnava) is free, but some parts are not.

The [Chrysler Museum of Art](/northamerica/unitedstates/virginia/norfolk/chryslermuseumof) is pretty cool. It is not free, of course.
