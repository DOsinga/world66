From parks to bars to movies to shopping, Richmond is an exciting spot to be.

### [The Byrd Theater](/northamerica/unitedstates/virginia/richmond/nightlife_and_ente/the_byrd_theater)

As a historic movie theater, the Byrd features cheap tickets to second run films, relatively inexpensive snacks, slightly uncomfortable seats, and an experience that can't be missed! Saturday nights, you can hear a live organ before the show, and some weekend evenings also feature midnight screenings of classic movies. The location is ideal, in the middle of the restaurants, shopping, coffee shops, and bars of the Cary Street shopping district.
