---
title: "Shopping"
type: section
---

For character, head to **Carytown**, the mile-long strip of independent boutiques, bookshops, vintage stores and galleries that runs along West Cary Street between Thompson Street and the Boulevard — it is the most pleasant place to wander in the city. For larger stores, **Short Pump Town Center**, west of downtown, and **Stony Point Fashion Park**, to the south, are the main upscale outdoor malls, with Nordstrom, Crate & Barrel, Pottery Barn and the usual suspects. Richmond also has a solid used-bookshop scene — the family-run **Book Exchange** on Midlothian Turnpike has one of the largest selections in Virginia and a resident store cat.
