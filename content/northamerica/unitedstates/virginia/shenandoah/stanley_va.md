Travel Guide

***Farmhouse Restaurant*** is located in [**Stanley, VA**](/northamerica/unitedstates/virginia/shenandoah). About 20-30 minutes north of Shenandoah located near the base of the mountains. It's about 200 years old & has excellent food. Dinner is on expensive side, but quanity & quality make it worthwhile. It also has an inn for accomodations.
