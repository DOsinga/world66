The most important thing to do in any city is shopping, right? In [Chesapeake](/northamerica/unitedstates/virginia/chesapeake) there is an adorable gift shop where most of the items are handcrafted either by local artists or via the Fair Trade. Of course they have other things too, but the handcrafted things are the best! It's perfect for souvenir shopping. It's easy to find from Virginia Beach, Norfolk, or Portsmouth, so no matter where you are visiting, give it a go!

They are located at 1464 Mt Pleasant Rd #14, Chesapeak VA
