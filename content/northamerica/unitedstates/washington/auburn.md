This community of 43,000 is centrally located in the growing and thriving Green River Valley between Seattle and Tacoma. Auburn’s location provides access to a wide variety of outdoor activities such as golf, hiking, skiing, boating and water sports. Scenic Mount Rainier National Park is a 60-minute drive away, and Crystal Mountain Ski Resort and The Summit at Snoqualmie are less than one hour away. The Auburn Area is graced with 26 parks, 4 golf courses, 2 large swimming pools, and a system of trails for jogging, horseback riding, bicycling and roller blading. It is also home to SuperMall, Muckleshoot Casino, and Emerald Downs racetrack. You'll be glad you planned to stay a night or two when you see how much there is to do in Auburn. You'll find the best of both shoping worlds-the Northwest's Largest Outlet Mall and an historic downtown. For the serious bargain hunter, SuperMall offers over 130 stores featuring national name brand retailers like Old Navy, GAP Outlet, Banana Republic Factory Store, Kenneth Cole New York, Tommy Hilfiger company Store, Nordstron Rack, Liz Claiborne Outlet and many, many more, all under one roof! As a thank you for visiting Auburn, all out-of-town visitors will receive a free discount book loaded with over 100 coupons to help you save even more at the SuperMall. If you're looking for a hometown shopping experience, you'll want to visit historic downtown Auburn. You'll find old fashioned merchant hospitality, shops with crafts, antiques and specialty gifts, and unique family-owned restaurants, all in a charming main street setting.
