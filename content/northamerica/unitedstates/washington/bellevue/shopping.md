title: "Shopping in Bellevue"
type: section
---

Anchored by 125,000 square foot Neiman Marcus department store, The Shops at The Bravern offers a sophisticated collection of 40 fashion retailers, restaurants and lifestyle services, many of which debuted for the first time in the Pacific Northwest. The elegant, vibrant open-air village that is the setting for The Shops features two ceremonial arrival courts, valet parking and concierge services with subterranean parking for 3,100 shoppers, office tenants and residents. In addition to Neiman Marcus, the mix of merchants who make up the 305,000 square feet of retail includes Anthropologie, Arthur, Brooks Brothers Country Club, David Lawrence, DNA 2050, Eye Society, Hermès, Jimmy Choo, Louis Vuitton, Masins, Piazza Sempione, Salvatore Ferragamo, Silberman/Brown Stationers, Sur La Table, Tory Burch, Wolford, Artisanal Brasserie & Wine Bar, The Artisanal Table Pizzeria & Enoteca, John Howie Steak, Mariposa, Trophy Cupcakes, Wild Ginger, Bank of America, DavidBartonGym and Red Door Spa.

Shopping at The Bravern is designed as an experience to be savored, from the personal and gracious service to the visually lush landscapes and the beautifully appointed gathering areas, indoors and out.
