Travel Guide

Best Western Peppertree Inn at Omak is a two leaf rated "Eco-Friendly" hotel. All rooms are spacious with ***flat screen TV's***. Complimentary breakfast with fresh waffles, scrambled eggs, and biscuits and gravy. FREE Hi-Speed Wireless Internet & 24 hour pool/spa/gym.

Omak Washington is located 25 minutes from the Canadian border. Located conveniently off of Highway 97, the highway to the 2010 Vancouver Olympics.
