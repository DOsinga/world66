The best resource for sights, hotels, restaurants, bars, what to do and see

Issaquah is about 16 miles east of Seattle, right off Interstate 90. Nice area to take a break and stretch your legs. You'll find plenty of nice places to eat. From shopping to hiking, you'll find it in Issaquah

Must see sites are the Cougar Mountain Zoo and the Salmon Hatchery.
