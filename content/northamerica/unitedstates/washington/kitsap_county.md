Located on the Kitsap Peninsula, the major claims to fame in this quiet area are Naval Base Kitsap, divided into two bases, Bangor, the Trident Submarine Base in Silverdale, and Bremerton, home to Bremerton Naval Base and Puget Sound Naval Shipyard.
