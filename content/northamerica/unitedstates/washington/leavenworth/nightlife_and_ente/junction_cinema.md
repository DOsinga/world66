Come to the Junction Cinema while visiting Leavenworth to see great movies and enjoy real hospitality. While at the movies try our fresh popcorn with real melted butter and other great snacks. You'll be surprised by the quality of the theater and the hospitality of the staff. A pleasant reminder of movie theater service from years gone by. If you arrive early, enjoy a round of miniature golf, shoot a game of pool with your friends, or play a few games in the video arcade.
