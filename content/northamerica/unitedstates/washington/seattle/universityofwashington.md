Located in North Seattle, this campus rivals most in its architecture and landscape. Available to the public are two museums, a medicinal herb garden, botany greenhouse tours, the Washington Park Arboretum, Fish Hatchery Tours and a historic observatory. For more information about visiting the campus, go to the following link: http://www.washington.edu/home/about.html
