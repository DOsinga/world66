Often a hub for protests, Red Square draws an especially large crowd of
students during the lunch hour. Perched high atop Kane Hall- the cam
shows Suzzalo Library to the left of the image, and Gerberding Hall-
home of the president's office= to the right. On a sunny and clear day,
Mt. Rainier can be seen framed in the image of the carefully landscaped
area between both buildings known as Rainier Vista.
