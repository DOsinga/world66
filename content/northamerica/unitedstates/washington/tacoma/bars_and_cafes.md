If your looking for a good place to meet up with friends and watch the game, or your just looking to relax and play some pool, try Mickey O'Reilly's. This sports bar is great even if your not into Monday night football. It's got a nice bar and a relaxing atmosphere. Located at 8726 South Hosmer, it's just off of I-5 and a short distance from Ft. Lewis and McChord Airforce base.
