### [Whale Watching](/northamerica/unitedstates/washington/westport/things_to_do/whale_watching)

During their annual migration from Baja California to the Bearing Sea, Gray Whales enter Gray's Harbor for a rest. The Westport Charter fleet offers whale watching excursions for reasonable prices ($20-$30 per person).
