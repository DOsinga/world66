Mount Vernon, the home of George Washington, is an easy day trip from Washington, DC. Located 15 miles south of DC in northern Virginia, the estate includes the mansion building and several out buildings, including the slave quarters, the stables, a greenhouse and the kitchen as well as a working 18th century farm. Open every day of the year, it is a living history lesson and provides information into George Washington's life. It also provides beautiful views of the Potamac River, which winds its way past the estate to the Atlantic Ocean 300 miles away.
