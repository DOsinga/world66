Woo Lae Oak is Korean cuisine at it's best. The Bul Go Ki (Korean BBQ beef) is brought out raw and is cooked at the grill located in the table. The Chap Chae (stir fried noodles with beef and vegetables--enough for 3-4 people) is a perfect appetizer. There is always something for everyone, including a vegetarian menu. The green tea ice cream for dessert is smooth and delicious.

Always one of my favorites, I make an effort to go to Woo Lae Oak whenever I am in town.
