Washington, D.C. has a great subway system, called Metro (Washington Metropolitan Area Transit Authority). Five lines connect the Maryland and Virginia suburbs to the center of the city. The trains are clean, generally on-time, and very safe. They can also be very crowded with commuters during rush hours and with tourists on the weekends, especially if something big is going on downtown. Be prepared to stand during these times.
