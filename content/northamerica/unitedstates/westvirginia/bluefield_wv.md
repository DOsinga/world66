Bluefield West Virginia is on the border of WV and VA. There is a Bluefield Virginia on the VA side.

Bluefield WV is known as the "air-conditioned" city because it rarely gets very hot in it's mountain-cooled environment. If the temperature reaches 90 F or above, the town gives out free lemonade.
