The most appealing neighborhood in Charleston, the East End runs along the south bank of the Kanawha River from roughly Broad Street east to California Avenue. Victorian houses in various states of restoration line the side streets, and Virginia Street claims one of the longest continuous city blocks in the country. The riverside promenade makes for a good morning walk. Several B&Bs have opened here in recent years, making it the most pleasant place to stay in the city.
