A 9,300-acre state forest a few miles south of downtown, Kanawha State Forest offers a real escape from the city. Wooded ridgelines, Davis Creek, hiking and mountain biking trails, campsites, and a public swimming pool make it popular with locals year-round. Getting there from downtown: take the South Side Bridge, turn left on Loudon Heights Road, then right on Connell Road down into the Loudendale community — follow that road to the end.
