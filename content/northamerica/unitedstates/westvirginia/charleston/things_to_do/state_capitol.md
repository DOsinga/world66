Cass Gilbert's 1932 capitol is the most impressive building in the state — a 293-foot gold dome that outshines the national model it's modeled on. The grounds are free to wander and include the state history museum, several Civil War monuments, and the striking "Lincoln Walks at Midnight" bronze. The black squirrels that live on the grounds are a local curiosity.
