Nestled away in the rolling hills of North Central West Virginia along side the Monongahela, Tygart and West Fork Rivers, is the City of Fairmont. Fairmont is the county seat of Marion County and with a population of just over 20,000 it is the largest municipality in Marion County. The City was chartered on February 18, 1899 and celebrated its 100th anniversary throughout 1999.
