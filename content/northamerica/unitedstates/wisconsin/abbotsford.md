Travel Guide

**Abbotsford** is a city in Clark and Marathon Counties in the state of Wisconsin. The population was 1,956 at the 2000 census. Abbotsford is nicknamed
"Wisconsin's First City" due to its alphabetical place on a list of
Wisconsin cities.
