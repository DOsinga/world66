---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

The outdoors is the main draw around Black River Falls. Black River State Forest offers ATV and snowmobile trails in season, and Wazee Lake — one of the deepest inland lakes in Wisconsin at over 350 feet — is good for swimming and scuba diving. If you prefer something more manicured, Skyline Golf Course sits conveniently on the edge of town.
