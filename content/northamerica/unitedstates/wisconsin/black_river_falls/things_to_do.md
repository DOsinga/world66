### [Skyline](/northamerica/unitedstates/wisconsin/black_river_falls/things_to_do/skyline)

Skyline golf is located in Black River Falls, Wisconsin. This is conveniently located along I-94, ½ way between Minneapolis and Madison. We cater to groups ranging from 2 to over 200. We make it fun and easy for you to hold a group outing at Skyline.

Skyline Golf Course's beautiful and challenging 18 hole championship course is open to the public, with the clubhouse and pro shop open year round.
