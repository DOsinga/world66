title: "Getting Around in Eau Claire"
type: section
---

The Eau Claire area is undergoing a number of major construction projects in 2003-2007. These include Hwy 53 (Hastings Way), Hwy 12 (Clairmont Ave) and Hwy 93. Best to view the WI DOT Webpage ( http://www.dot.state.wi.us/projects/nw.htm) for the latest.
