### [Action City](/northamerica/unitedstates/wisconsin/eau_claire/things_to_do/action_city)

Action City is a 55,000 square foot family fun center in Eau Claire, Wisconsin. It's located along Interstate 94 on the city's south side. Action City offers more than 100 arcade games, indoor go karts, bumper cars, laser tag, batting cages, a climbing wall, mini bowling, mini golf, an outdoor slick track and great dining options. Monkey in the Middle Pizza is Action City's own namebrand, made to order pizza. You can also choose Erbert and Gerbert's subs, which is a franchise that began in Eau Claire many years ago. ### [Irvine Park](/northamerica/unitedstates/wisconsin/eau_claire/things_to_do/irvine_park)

In nearby Chippewa Falls. Visitors will find over 300 acres of natural and scenic beauty. This park offers a zoo, a scenic dam, a museum, a petting zoo, and historical displays. Picnic shelters and restrooms are available. Visitors will find Irvine Park by taking Bridge Street N. from Chippewa Falls to Highway 124 N. Turn left on Bridgewater Avenue and the park is located on the right.

### [Shopping at Oakwood Mall](/northamerica/unitedstates/wisconsin/eau_claire/things_to_do/chippewa_valley_ra_1)

Oakwood Mall, western Wisconsin's largest regional shopping center with over 125 stores and restaurants, serves a market area with a population of nearly one-half million people. The center features a new hospital-themed children's soft play area, 12-screen theater and a cafe court with 10 eateries. Recent new store additions include Hollister & Co., Yankee Candle, Cinnabon, Timberlodge Steakhouse and Younker's.
