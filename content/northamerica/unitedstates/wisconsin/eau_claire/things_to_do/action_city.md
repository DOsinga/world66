Action City is a 55,000 square foot family fun center in Eau Claire, Wisconsin. It's located along Interstate 94 on the city's south side. Action City offers more than 100 arcade games, indoor go karts, bumper cars, laser tag, batting cages, a climbing wall, mini bowling, mini golf, an outdoor slick track and great dining options. Monkey in the Middle Pizza is Action City's own namebrand, made to order pizza. You can also choose Erbert and Gerbert's subs, which is a franchise that began in Eau Claire many years ago. The Fast Lane offers nachos, hotdogs, slushes, steak and pulled pork sandwiches, and lot of other "grab 'n go" foods. Action City prides itself on good, clean, family fun and proves it by offering a smoke-free, drug-free, non alcoholic environment. Voted "Best Form of Entertainment" in 2006 by local newspaper readers, it's a great place to bring your friends and family for a day outing or an entire weekend of fun. There are also plans underway to building an adjacent hotel and waterpark, which should be open in the spring of 2008. Check out Action City's website at www.actioncityfun.com or give the business a call at (715) 838-9663.
