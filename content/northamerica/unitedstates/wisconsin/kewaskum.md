The best resource for sights, hotels, restaurants, bars, what to do and see

Called "The Gateway To The Kettle Moraine", the small rural Village of Kewaskum sits just south of a State Forest that is preserving the topographic geography formed during the last Ice Age. Hunt, fish, swim, or hike in one of Wisconsin's most picturesque yet accessable areas. Visit http://www.kewaskum.org/
