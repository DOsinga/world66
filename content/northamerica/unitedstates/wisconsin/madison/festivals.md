** Art Fair Off the Square - July 9 and 10 in 2005. **

Located in downtown Madison along Martin Luther King Junior Boulevard and on the Monona Terrace Convention Centre Esplanade, the Art Fair Off the Square takes place July 9 and 10 in 2005. Hours of the fair are 9 a.m. to 6 p.m. on Saturday and 10 a.m. to 5 p.m. on Sunday. This is a showcase for about 140 Wisconsin exhibitors, and the two-day event includes live music, refreshments and children's entertainment. The show features ceramics, painting, drawings, sculpture, jewellery, engravings, serigraphs, paper-making, and more.

** Capital Jazz Fest **

Held at the Madison Marroitt West Hotel, four bands will play one set during each session. The 2005 fest runs from Friday 22nd April until Sunday 24th April.

**Corvette on the Isthmus**

An annual car show in Madison. More information available at http://www.corvettesontheisthmus.com/

**Hog Wild BBQ Competition**

Various BBQ competitions, fun and entertainment for all the family. A variety of Microbrews available for your tasting pleasure too!
