**Milwaukee Public Museum**

The Milwaukee Public Museum offers 150,000 square feet of exhibit space for exploration. The *Streets of Old Milwaukee* exhibit allows visitors to take a step back in time to the downtown streets of the late 19th century. The *Puelicher Butterfly Wing* contains free-flying butterflies from around the world, and visitors are able to stand amid the beautiful and amazing fancy fliers. Additionally, there is an IMAX theatre located on the premises. [more..](/northamerica/unitedstates/wisconsin/milwaukee/museums/milwaukee_public_m)
