---
title: "Shopping in Milwaukee"
type: section
---

If you are in the mood to do some Milwaukee shopping, check out The Shops of Grand Avenue in downtown Milwaukee. There isn't a lot of stores, but this two story shopping mall is something to see. You can find great bargains and sales at such stores The Children's Place, Famous Footwear, GameStop, Lane Bryant, Lids, Old Navy, Radio Shack, TJ Maxx, Trade Secret, and Scented Garden. When you are hungry, you can eat at Subway, Arby's, Cajun Grille, or Wild Flower Bakery. For a sit down meal, visit Applebee's. For more information, check out their website at grandavenueshop.com.
