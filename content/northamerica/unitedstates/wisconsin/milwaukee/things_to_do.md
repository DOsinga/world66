Milwaukee Brewers 

The city is home to the MLB team, The Milwaukee Brewers - playing at Miller Park. 

The team have a lovely brand new stadium that is certainly worth a visit for a tour, without even seeing a game. 

Tickets are never difficult to get hold of on gameday, so if there is a game on just turn up and watch. Its even fun in the bleechers.
