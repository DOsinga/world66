**St. Patrick's Day**

Each March Leprechauns rename the town to 'New Dublin' for a week of Irish festivities with Wisconsin's largest Saint Patrick's Day parade and an Irish Fest. Other activities include; Irish Caroling, Irish family entertainment, Finnegan’s Wake, an Irish Ceili, and corned beef and cabbage served in local restaurants. (Finnegan's Wake is a parody of an Irish funeral and the Ceili is an Irish dance social for family and friends). Visit www.newdublin.com
