---
title: "Getting There in Cheyenne"
type: section
---

Making Cheyenne travel a reality has never been easier. Several airline carriers provide service to and from Cheyenne Regional Airport. Airlines that fly to Cheyenne are American, United, Frontier, US Airways, and Great Lakes. If you decide to go by train, Amtrak does not have a train station in Cheyenne. Travelers would have to go to the nearest station which is in Fort Collins, Colorado, about 41 miles away. For directions, hours, and prices, visit their website at amtrak.com. Greyhound provides bus service to Cheyenne from numerous cities nationwide. Schedules and fares can be found at greyhound.com.
