---
title: "Museums in Cheyenne"
type: section
---

If you enjoy the rodeo, then one of your Cheyenne activities should be a visit to Cheyenne Frontier Days Old West Museum. There are several western art exhibitions, over 55,000 artifacts, and genuine clothing from the 1800s. Examples of what you will see here are a collection of over 100 horse-drawn carriages and wagons, western and folk art, and a Hall of Fame that celebrates those who have made a contribution to Cheyenne Frontier Days. The children will enjoy the kids' room where there are interactive learning displays and hands-on activities. Hours, directions, and prices can be found at oldmuseum.org.
