---
title: "Shopping in Cheyenne"
type: section
---

**Frontier Mall** (1400 Dell Range Boulevard) is the main shopping centre in Cheyenne. It has the standard national chains — American Eagle, Dillard's, Boot Barn, Bath & Body Works, Kay Jewelers — along with a food court. Standard suburban mall, but it covers the basics. Hours and directory at frontiermall.com.
