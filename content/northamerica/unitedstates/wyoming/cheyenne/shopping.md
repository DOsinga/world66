---
title: "Shopping in Cheyenne"
type: section
---

If you are in the mood for some Cheyenne shopping, check out the Frontier Mall at 1400 Dell Range Boulevard. With all the stores and shops there, visitors are sure to find many bargains and deals. Enjoy sales at such places as Bath & Body Works, GNC, American Eagle Outfitters, Christopher & Banks, Dillard’s, Boot Barn, Kay Jewelers, PacSun, Pet City, Sunglass Hut, Trade Secret, Gymboree, FYE, and Finish Line. If you get hungry, eat at Subway, Chick-fil-A, Chipotle, Sbarro, Taco John’s, Country Buffet, and Olive Garden. For directions and mall hours, go to their website at frontiermall.com.
