---
title: "Things to do in Cheyenne"
type: section
---

If you are into gardening and horticulture, one of your Cheyenne activities should be a visit to Cheyenne Botanic Garden. Visitors can see several hundred different kinds of plants and flowers in one of their many houses and gardens. For example, the Bedont Rose Garden houses many varieties of roses, along with a small water fountain and a teakwood bench swing. One cool thing about their webpage is there are gardening tips and recipes for such dishes as grilled potato salad, baked fruit curry, zucchini bacon cheese casserole, and sweet potato soufflé. For more information, visit their website at botanic.org.
