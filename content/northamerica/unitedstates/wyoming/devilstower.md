Devil's Tower is in northeastern Wyoming, near the South Dakota border. It is an impressive natural monument, the solid core of an extinct volcano. There are thousands of vertical cylinders of rock shooting up hundreds of feet from the prairie below. The park around the tower is great for hiking. Definately a must-see if you're in this part of the country.
