Travel Guide

Evanston, Wyoming, fresh mountain air invigorates your body's senses. Freedom allows you to kick up your heels. Fun keeps you smiling during three hundred days of sunshine annually.
A special place where you will experience a generous serving of Wyoming friendship, a firm handshake and a genuine "Howdy." With a friendly population of 11,507,
Evanston enjoys a unique quality of life. Nestled in the Bear River Valley, the city is framed to the south by the majestic High Uinta Mountains. The Bear River flows through the community, meandering past Bear River State Park where elk and bison roam, and a community parkway where deer and people play. Our enviable quality of life is cherished by those who visit and live here.
