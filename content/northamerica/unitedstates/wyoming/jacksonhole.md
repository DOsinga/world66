Jackson hole is the perfect destination for an active holiday. Close by there are national parks of Grand Teton and [Yellowstone](/northamerica/unitedstates/montana/yellowstone), three major skiing resorts and many other places that are perfect for outdoor activities such as fishing, golfing, hiking, horseback Riding, Kayak and Canoe trips or scenic floats. The area is also rich in wildlife and Jackson hole is the ideal place to organize a trip to view it.
