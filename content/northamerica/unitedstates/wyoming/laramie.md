Travel Guide

Home of the University of Wyoming, Laramie is about 1 hour away from the state capital Cheyenne. Very close to ski areas and beautiful landscapes, Laramie is a great place to visit. History is all around you with areas like the Wyoming Territorial Park which includes the original prison where Butch Cassidy once was held.
