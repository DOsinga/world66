title: "Practical Information in Yellowstone"
type: section
---

Summer: Park entrances open on different dates when snow crews are able
to clear the roads. Visit the following Web address to learn the
projected dates for this year.
(http://www.nps.gov/yell/planvisit/orientation/travel/roadopen.htm) The
season runs from mid-April to late-October. Once an entrance/road opens
it is open 24 hours. The only exceptions are caused by road
construction and weather-caused restrictions.

Winter: The season runs from mid-December to mid-March. The road
for the North Entrance at Gardiner, MT to the Northeast Entrance at
Cooke City, MT is open to wheeled-vehicle use year around. Only
over-snow vehicles are allowed on other park roads.
