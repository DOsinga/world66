Long Stays in Buenos Aires

Buenos Aires is visited by several students who stay in the city for at least 6 months. Also its a good destinations for those planing to do charity work for a couple of month and of course for people coming to do bussiness.
For a stay longer than a month, it is higly recommended to stay in a rented apartment rather than in a Hotel, Hostel or Residencia. In an apartment comfort and privacy is combined with a good price. For students there are rooms for rent available in some of the best areas of the city.
There are many offers in Buenos Aires made by several Rental Agencies. Depending on the type of accomodation the prices will be different.
Casa34 is an agency which offers a variety of apartments and student houses in the city, mainly in Palermo, Recoleta and the Center.
