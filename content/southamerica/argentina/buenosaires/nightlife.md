---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment"
type: section
---

The city is famed for its nightlife especially in the neighborhood of La Boca and San Telmo where tango bars are plentiful. In typical Latin fashion the dinner hour is late (about 11 pm). After dining many people retire to one of the outdoor cafes for coffee or people watching. Discos start even later—most open around 2 am.  

**all**

**Free performances @ San Telmo**

There are free tango performances on Sundays outside the San Telmo flea. 

**Colon Theater**

The place to see and be seen however is the exquisite Colon Theater the city’s opera house. Acoustically one of the world’s best it is lavishly decorated in gold and plush velvet (in the evening you’ll see equally stunning outfits on the patrons).
