The place to see and be seen however is the exquisite Colon Theater the city’s opera house. Acoustically one of the world’s best it is lavishly decorated in gold and plush velvet (in the evening you’ll see equally stunning outfits on the patrons). If you can’t catch a performance be sure to take one of the six daily tours.
