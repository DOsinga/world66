One of the bigger and more eclectic clubs in Buenos Aires. The main floor is a large dance space surrounded by bars on three sides and a stage on the fourth, painted in red with white furniture. Upstairs splits into three areas: a lounge overlooking the dance floor, a more intimate space, and a quirky mini-disco inside a transparent inverted cone that plays pop and Latin music while the main floor sticks to electronic, progressive and drum & bass.

The crowd is mixed — all ages, all backgrounds, plenty of tourists alongside locals. Open Thursday through Sunday. Friday nights often have "canilla libre" (all-you-can-drink) promotions.
