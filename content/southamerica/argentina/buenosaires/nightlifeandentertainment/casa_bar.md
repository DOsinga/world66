This is a new bar in the heart of Recoleta ( Santa Fe & Rodriguez Pena ) situated in a 3 story classic petite style house. It is an International themed bar with an Argentine flavor. Currently it opens at 7 PM for Happy Hour ( 7 - 12 - 2 x 1 beers, double shot drinks, free food ) and keeps going until 4 AM or later depending on the crowd. They offer a large selection of top import beers, Heineken on tap, and the best spirits shelf in the city.

Theme & live music nights, private party/event hosting, food in-house ( limited but very good ) and delivery brought in, excellent music format, televised international sports on 42 inch LCD flat screen TVs and a very attentive staff. They also serve Illy coffee and postre.

Soon they will be opening the 2nd and 3rd floors and operate a full service kitchen & coffee house. There is free WIFI and a smoking section available. Caters to the over 21 crowd and they are continually improving the bar day by day. Best bar ( and most stunning ) by far in Recoleta and the only one that operates more like a traditional bar you would find in Europe or North America. Portenos, Expats and tourists will all enjoy this place.
