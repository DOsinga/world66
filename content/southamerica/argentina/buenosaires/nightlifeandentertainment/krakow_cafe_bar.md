A top-notch bar for a relaxed night out in San Telmo, right off Defensa. The taps are memorable, especially the pilsner varieties from Gambrinus and Antares. The atmosphere is unpretentious and international, with pub food, a large selection of spirits, and a hip but relaxed crowd. Go early to grab a seat on one of the low-slung sofas.
