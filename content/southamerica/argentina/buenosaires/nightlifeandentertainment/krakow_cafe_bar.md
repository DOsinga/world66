Buenos
Aires Bar Krakow is a top notch bar for a chill night outing in San Telmo. It
is located on Venezuela
474 right of Defensa. The taps there are memorable, especially the pilsner
varieties from Gambrinus and Antares. The bar has very un-pretentious international
vibe thanks to interesting pub cuisine, enormous selection of hard liquors,
latest music hits and the hip and relaxed crowd. Go early to grab a seat in one
of low-slung sofas and enjoy a pub game or play Nintendo Wii. A must see when visiting Buenos Aires.
