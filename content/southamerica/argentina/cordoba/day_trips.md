---
title: "Day Trips"
type: section
---

The Sierras de Córdoba are the main reason to linger in the region. Villa Los Aromos, 70 km south of the city, is a pleasant riverside retreat with swimming in the Río Anisacate — be cautious after heavy rain when water levels rise quickly. Capilla del Monte, about 100 km north, is the base for climbing Cerro Uritorco and has a quirky reputation for UFO sightings. Alta Gracia, 36 km south, has Che Guevara's childhood home (now a museum) and a fine Jesuit estancia.

All these destinations can be reached by bus from the main terminal in the capital.
