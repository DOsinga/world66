---
title: "Day Trips"
type: section
---

The sierra villages around Córdoba city are the main draw, and most can be reached by bus from the central terminal. A few highlights for day trips:

**Villa Los Aromos**, about 70 km south, is a quiet holiday village popular with Argentinians in summer. The main attraction is the Río Anisacate — a beautiful spot for swimming, though beware of sudden rises in the river level after rainfall. Nearby villages like La Bolsa and Alta Gracia are worth combining into a loop. Buses run via the Sierras de Calamuchita line from the terminal.

**Capilla del Monte**, about 100 km north, sits at the foot of Cerro Uritorco, a mountain said to possess special powers. The climb to the summit is straightforward and rewards with sweeping views of the sierras. The town attracts a spiritual crowd — UFO sightings are part of the local lore. Buses run from the terminal via El Serra or La Calera.
