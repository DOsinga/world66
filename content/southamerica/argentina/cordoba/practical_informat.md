If you want to move around in the province of Cordoba you have a wide network of buslines available (all leaving from the main bus terminal in the capital). Another option is renting a car at the airport or in the centre of the capital. You have to realise yourself that driving a car is a risky adventure.

Another option which is taking taxis or the so called remises. For turists from Europe or the USA this is a cheap option to move around in the capital or the close surroundings. With a remis driver you can negotiate a fixed price for the trip.

More info about the capital and the province at: http://www.argentinaturistica.com/2coriresenia.htm
