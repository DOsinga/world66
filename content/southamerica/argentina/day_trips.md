### Day trip to Tigre

If you are in Buenos Aires and want to see a nice landscape take the train to Tigre, in the suburbs.

From Retiro Train Station you will have about an hour trip till Tigre
(trains finish there, you won't get lost). You can see the artisans
fair, buy fresh fruits, and take a boat trip along the islands of Delta.

One way ticket costs less than two Argentine Pesos
