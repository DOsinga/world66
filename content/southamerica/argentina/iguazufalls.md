title: "Iguazu falls"
type: location
---

Although very much commercialized and touristy, the Iguazu falls are still well worth the detour. They are located on the border between [Brazil](/southamerica/brazil) and Argentina, and can be reached from both sides as well as from [Paraguay](/southamerica/paraguay).

 Legend attributes their origin to the anger of a god who lost the girl he loved. When you look down the Gargantaga del Diablo (Devil's throat) you will believe that there is some truth in this story.
