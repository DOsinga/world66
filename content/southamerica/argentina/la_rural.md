Argentina's premier agricultural and equestrian show, held in Palermo, Buenos Aires in late July. La Rural (officially the Exposición Rural) brings together livestock, farming equipment, horse shows, and traditional gaucho demonstrations. It's been running since 1878 and offers a window into the country's deep agricultural identity, even in the middle of a major city.
