---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Mendoza packs more into its compact centre than its size might suggest, and the surrounding valleys and mountains extend the options considerably. The city's parks and plazas reward a slow wander, while the Ruinas de San Francisco and Museo Fundacional connect the city to its pre-earthquake past. A short drive from the centre, the wineries of Luján de Cuyo and Maipú offer a different kind of afternoon entirely.
