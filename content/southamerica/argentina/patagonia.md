Made famous by Bruce Chatwin, Patagonia covers the southern part of Argentinia up to the  Tierra del fuego . It is a land of rugged beauty and wildenress, great for hiking and other outdoor sports.  

 Patagonia does not have any big cities - the ski resort of  [Bariloche](/southamerica/argentina/bariloche) and oil boom town  Comodoro Rivadavia are probably the closest thing - but city life is not what you are here for. The main attractions are the Glacieres National Park near the border with Chile, the  Tierra del fuego ,  Valdes Peninsula , the  Ephemeral Lakes and the  Torres del Paine National Park.
