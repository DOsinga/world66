Made famous by Bruce Chatwin, Patagonia covers the southern part of Argentinia up to the 
 [Tierra del fuego](/southamerica/argentina/tierradelfuego)
 . It is a land of rugged beauty and wildenress, great for hiking and other outdoor sports. 
 


 Patagonia does not have any big cities - the ski resort of 
 [Bariloche](/southamerica/argentina/bariloche)
 and oil boom town 
 Comodoro Rivadavia
 are probably the closest thing - but city life is not what you are here for. The main attractions are the
 [Glacieres National Park](/southamerica/argentina/patagonia/glacieresnationalpark)
 near the border with Chile, the 
 [Tierra del fuego](/southamerica/argentina/tierradelfuego)
 , 
 [Valdes Peninsula](/southamerica/argentina/patagonia/valdespeninsula)
 , the 
 Ephemeral Lakes
 and the 
 [Torres del Paine](/southamerica/argentina/patagonia/torresdelpaine)
 National Park.
