title: "Ushuaia Travel Guide"
type: location
---

Located on the southern coast of Tierra del Fuego, on the Beagle
Channel, Ushuaia rightfully claims to be the city furthest south
in the world. It is located close to several National Parks and
there are also boat tours of the islands in the Channel.

More information on Ushuaia Travel at Wikitravel.org
