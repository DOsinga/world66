Salta is located in the Lerma valley in a mountainous region in the North-West of Argentina. The town has quite a few nice colonial mansions and churches and is a good base for exploring the region.


 Teh city centre is small enough to explore on foot. To Plaza 9 de Julio is the real centre of town, and here you find the Cathedral dating back to the 1850-ies and the Cabildo. East of the centre is the Cerro San Bernardo which can be reached by cable car (spanish:teleferico). From the Parque San Martin you get a great view over the city.
