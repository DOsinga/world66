A downtown pizzeria on Caseros that locals regularly name the best in Salta. The menu runs the full range of Argentine pizza styles, from thick fugazzeta to thin-crust classics. Portions are generous and it's a reliable choice for a sit-down meal in the centre. Not the cheapest option, but the quality justifies the price.
