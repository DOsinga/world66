The Tierra del Fuego is the big island on the southern tip of Argentina and Chile. It's a treat for adventurous travelers and offers great outdoor opportunities.

[Ushaia](/southamerica/argentina/tierradelfuego/ushaia), the southernmost city in the world, is the best place from where you can start exploring the island.

- [Ushaia](/southamerica/argentina/tierradelfuego/ushaia)
