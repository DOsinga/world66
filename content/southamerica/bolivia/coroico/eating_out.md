### [El Cafetal](/southamerica/bolivia/coroico/eating_out/el_cafetal)

This cozy open air restaurant offers delicious cuisine with tropical and French flavors. Specialties are curry, lama meat, trout, soufflés, crepes and tasty veggie food .

Open every day for lunch and dinner, ac cept on Tuesdays .

type: French



[[rate it]](/southamerica/bolivia/coroico/eating_out/el_cafetal)

### [Villa Bonita](/southamerica/bolivia/coroico/eating_out/villa_bonita)

Enjoy homemade ice cream in Villa Bonita’s lovely peaceful garden with nice Yungas views. If you’re still hungry after dessert, try their vegetarian specialties from Italy , Switzerland and India.
Closed on Monday and Tuesday. Open from noon to sunset.

type: International



[[rate it]](/southamerica/bolivia/coroico/eating_out/villa_bonita)

### [Bamboos](/southamerica/bolivia/coroico/eating_out/bamboos)

This restaurant cum bar offers quality Mexican food (burritos, tacos and the like). Enjoy your excellent cocktail at half price between 6 and 7 PM.

Open: every day from 11 am to 11 pm

type: Mexican

### [La Casa](/southamerica/bolivia/coroico/eating_out/la_casa)

Here you can enjoy delicious meat, chicken and cheese fondue. Start dieting tomorrow and have chocolate fondue for desert.

Open: every night form 6 pm.

### [Back-Stube](/southamerica/bolivia/coroico/eating_out/backstube)

This German pastry has a beautiful flowery terrace with a panoramic view where you can enjoy homemade cakes, pastries and delicious breakfasts. It’s a restaurant too, with German specialties.
