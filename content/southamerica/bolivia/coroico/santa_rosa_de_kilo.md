title: Santa Rosa de Kilo Kilo
type: poi

At the end of the Suapi road, an hour further from Coroico, this remote village sees perhaps two groups of tourists a year. The ride there is spectacular as the forest changes with altitude, and just before the village you pass the odd Kilo Kilo Alto hacienda house.

From the village a two-hour walk (one way) leads to pre-Columbian rock drawings. The trail passes through beautiful forest full of birds and butterflies, with giant palms at the lower elevations. The drawings are beside the road just before a small bridge over a mountain stream. The last stretch runs along a ridge between two valleys. The rivers are strong, but confident swimmers can take a dip.
