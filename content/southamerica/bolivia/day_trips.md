**Bolivia Day Trips **make your vacation an unforgettable one. Have a fun vacation in Bolivia by visiting its tourist attractions for a day. The attractions immerse visitors in culture, history and lifestyle.**
**

#### **La Paz**

La Paz is the destination in Bolivia for most travelers who arrive by plane. It is a large city where one can enjoy shopping and dining in small caf&eacute;s and traditional restaurants. In addition to exploring the markets, you can also check out the city's unique architecture.**
**

#### **Sucre**

Visit Sucre to experience the local Bolivian culture. The small town is located near the sea and has magnificent views and a warm atmosphere. The influences of colonial Spain and the Roman Catholic church remain visible in Sucre.

#### **Potosi**

Potosi is over 4,200 meters above sea level, making it one of the higher cities in the world. It is among the wealthiest cities in Bolivia because it serves as the domicile of several silver mines. Enjoy a day in this city by having a trip to its mining areas. Travel through the caves and tunnels of the mines. The buildings in Potosi are also attractive because of its colonial architecture style.**
**

#### **Salt Desert of Uyuni**

The Salt Desert of Uyuni is a distinctive desert in Bolivia which is worth exploring. When you reach the Uyuni desert, you will see a large salt lake and a depot which manufactures salt bricks. The desert also has a salt-surrounded island and a hotel made from salt.
