### [La Paz Walking Tours](/southamerica/bolivia/lapaz/things_to_do/la_paz_walking_tou)

**La Paz Walking Tours**

First-of-its-kind, offers tours (English/Spanish) in downtown La Paz, Bolivia
guided by locals. Not only visits **all the main attractions **but also **hidden
cultural spots** that most tourist miss when in La Paz. Private tours are
available. Tours run Mon-Sat at 10 AM & 2:30 PM. 2 and a half-3 hours
long. Includes food tasting. Additionally, Pub Crawling tours are
available on week-ends.
