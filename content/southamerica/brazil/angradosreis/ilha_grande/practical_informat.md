The way to get there from Rio , is to catch a bus to 'Angra dos Reis' from the 'Costa Verde' stand in Rio ’s central bus station. The journey is about 1:30 -2 hours and once arrived at Angra, the marina is only 15 minute cab drive away. From the marina there are boats that go directly to Ilha Grande. The boat ride is another hour.

For accommodation, there are many little hostels everywhere and it wouldn't be hard to find a place. There is a small tourist office just on the left side of marina on the island which provides help for travellers with accommodation.Remember:

- There are no cash machines on the island

- There are no cars on the island, so travel light.

- Pack bug repellent.

- Try to arrive early on the island as all the boats leave for the beaches and diving before 11am .- Take food and drink with you if you go the beaches, as the island is largly untouched and which means no stores by the beautiful beaches.
