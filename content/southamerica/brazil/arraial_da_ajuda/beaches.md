There are an infinite number of deserted beaches along the coast of Arraial and surrounding villages.

Check the photo galley for some of them.

Most visited are: Mucugé, Parracho and Pitinga beaches.

More quiet: Araçaipe, dos Pescadores, Taípe, Rio da Barra
