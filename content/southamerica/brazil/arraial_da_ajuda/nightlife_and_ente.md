One of the high points in Arraial is the night life. Parties everywhere, beach parties, Trance parties, forro, popular shows, live bands, fancy bars, reggae parties, full moon parties ! You choose !

Carnival, New Year and Sao Joao (in June/july) attract lots of people that are coming here only for partying all the time.
