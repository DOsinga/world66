To get to Arraial, you can buy a bus ticket from any city in Brazil to Porto Seguro. At Porto Seguro bus station, take a bus to the ferry boat (10min) and after crossing the river (5min), take another bus (15min) to the center of Arraial.

Get off the bus at the main square and then you'll see the church. That´s the centre of the village. From there, you´ll see the sea from the top and at night, this is the best spot to see the full moon.

You can also fly into Porto Seguro and then get a taxi / bus to the ferry boat. There are regular flights from most capital cities in Brazil and also some charter flights from europe.
