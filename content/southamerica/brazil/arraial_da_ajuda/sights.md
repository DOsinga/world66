---
title: "Sights in Arraial da Ajuda"
type: section
---

There are many place in the surrounding for visitors to have a great time in Arraial. Lagoa Azul with its medicinal mud, the village of Trancoso, Praia do Espelho (one of the 10 most beautiful beaches in Brazil) and Caraiva, where there is no eletric light.

There is also the historical centre in Porto Seguro, with the first church built in Brazil. 7 Km from Porto Seguro, you can visit the Indian Reserva Jaqueira and spend the days with the Indians while they show you many things about their culture, cuisine, music, etc.
