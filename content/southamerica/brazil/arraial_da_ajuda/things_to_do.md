title: "Things to do in Arraial da Ajuda"
type: section
---

If you go to Arraial dont miss:

- Capoeira lessons with one of the most famous group in the world. Mestre Railson has his primer school here and it´s one of the best places to learn more about this Brazilian cultural martial art.

- Zouk lessons: mixture of lambada and salsa, it´s a very sensual partner dance which you will never want to stop dancing.

- Afro dance: worldly known teacher
