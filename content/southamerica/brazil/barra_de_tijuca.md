On the south side of [Rio de Janeiro](/southamerica/brazil/riodejaneiro), Barra da Tijuca has wide beaches, a quieter atmosphere, and modern shopping malls. It feels more like a Miami suburb than the rest of Rio — all wide avenues, gated condominiums, and beachfront high-rises. The beaches are long and less crowded than Copacabana or Ipanema, making it a good choice if you want space and surf without the tourist crowds.
