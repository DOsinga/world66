The Iguatemi Belém was inaugurated in 27 of October of 1993 and is
situated in the quarter Batista Campos, commercial area of the city of
Belém. It possesss in its mix 190 store, between Square of Feeding,
store of convenience, leisure, main griffes national of clothes,
services, decoration, eletroeletrônicos and also the presence of
store anchors as C&A, Lojas Americanas, Visão and Y. Yamada. The
parking, in 05 levels, disponibilized 1000 simultaneous vacant, had
access through 03 entrances and 03 exits vehicles. Situated in the Avenue Padre Eutíquio, one of the avenues of bigger circulation of
Belém and for the Avenue São Pedro where the accesses main of the
parking meet. The project allows, through its generous vain and free
right foot, that in any part of the building if has a total vision of
the store. The zenithal illumination throughout its structure makes
possible an excellent natural illumination. The comfort still is
assured by the spotless security, conditional air, bathroom family,
fraldário, cultural kiosks of services and events. The Iguatemi has
in its area of influence a population of 981.000 inhabitants being
that 70% of its inhabitants belong to the classrooms, B and C. Its
localization allows the facilitated access of automobiles where of any
point of the city shopping in 10 minutes can practically be reached
approximately, beyond the availability of collective transport of good
quality allowing that let us have an average flow of consumers in the
order of 1.350.000 people /mês.
