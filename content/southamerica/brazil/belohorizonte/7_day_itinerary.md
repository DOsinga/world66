Bel Horizonte, the capital of the Brazilian state Minas Gerais, makes for a great destination for any holiday. Here is a comprehensive 7-Day Itinerary to assist you through the must-sees the region has to offer.

**Day 1: Tour of the City**

A great way to start out your holiday in Belo Horizonte is with a guided tour of the city. Tour Guide Brazil offers day packages at a reasonable price. Getting to know your home for the next seven days is essential and a great informational experience.

**Day 2: Central Market Shopping Center**

The Central Market Shopping Center located downtown offers more than 300 shops, stands and merchants. You will find everything from locally grown produce to lavish antiques. The Central Market provides more than enough aisles of shops to keep you busy for hours.

**Day 3: Belo Horizonte Caves**

In the surrounding areas of Belo Horizonte are several cave attractions. A visit to the Maquine Cave is an extraordinary way to spend a day while in Belo Horizonte. It's one of the oldest caves in Brazil and has more than 500 passages. It's accessible to the public for informational viewing at no cost, or at a modest fee there are guided tours.

**Day 4: Belo Horizonte Football Match**

Belo Horizonte is home to Brazil's second largest soccer stadium, Mineiro Stadium. The stadium's home team is the famous Clube Atlético Mineiro that has been around since 1908. A visit to a traditional Brazilian soccer match will keep you at the edge of your seat with its excitement.

**Day 5: Belo Horizonte Zoo**

The Belo Horizonte Zoo is a must-see while in the area. Belo Horizonte Zoo features exotic animals native to Brazil and greater South America. Visitors will experience animal exhibits that feel like authentic South American rain forests.

**Day 6: Gold Digger's Towns**

Belo Horizonte is in the center of Brazil's 18th century mining region. Nearby towns Congonhas and Ouro Preto still maintain the traditional gold mining heritage of Brazil and make for a fabulous day trip. To get to Congonhas and Ouro Preto, public transportation is available at a small price.

**Day 7: Minas Gerais Beaches**

Minas Gerais is the perfect spot to relax in the sunshine. Here you will come to experience the famous Brazilian beach culture.

With the right itinerary, Belo Horizonte makes for an exceptional experience while vacationing in Brazilian paradise. Its range of attractions will meet your expectations and provide you with the experience of a lifetime.
