---
title: "Day Trips"
type: section
---

Belo Horizonte sits in the heart of Brazil's 18th-century mining region, surrounded by colonial towns and natural parks that make excellent day trips.

The gold-mining towns of [Ouro Preto](/southamerica/brazil/ouropreto) and Congonhas are the most popular excursions. Both preserve the baroque architecture and churches of the colonial era and are reachable by public bus. The Maquine Cave, one of Brazil's oldest, has over 500 passages and is open for self-guided or guided visits. Parque do Caraca to the east offers mountain hiking through Atlantic Forest at high elevation — bring a jacket, as it gets cold after sunset even in summer.
