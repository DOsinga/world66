title: "Family Travel Ideas in Belo horizonte"
type: section
---

Belo Horizonte, Brazil is an excellent location for a family holiday in South America. Travel for the family is perfect from April
to October, during the season of warm weather. The city has a wide
variety of attractions and things to do. Here is an
overview of things to know and enjoyable places to visit while
traveling with your family in Belo Horizonte.**
**

**Where to Stay**

The best area to stay in Belo Horizonte with your family is going to
be in the downtown area. The Normandy Hotel has many accommodations
desirable for family travel, in an ideal location downtown close to
shopping, restaurants and Municipal Park. The hotel's closeness to
Municipal Park gives your family a place to relax, stroll through
gardens, play on the playground and even canoe through the park's
ponds.

**Safety Precautions**

It's crucial to be aware of the Belo Horizonte's safety
precautions while traveling with your family. While on the streets, it's important to watch for motorcycles and private vehicles. In
Belo Horizonte, drivers sometimes run stop signs and breach the area's
traffic laws. To prepare your family for emergency circumstances, let them know that the local area emergency line is 190 for
police and 192 for medical emergencies.

**Family Entertainment**

Belo Horizonte offers many fun family attractions. The Belo
Horizonte Zoo is a fun place to take the family to see some of South
America's most exotic species. If your family loves sports, the Mineiro
Stadium is home to the famous Clube Atlético Mineiro soccer team. There
is nothing like an authentic Brazilian soccer match in one of the
largest stadiums in the world.

**Educational Attractions**

If looking for a learning filled vacation experience to broaden
your family's horizon, Belo Horizonte has many popular museums that
are guaranteed to satisfy. The Museum of Arts and Crafts is open daily until
7pm and has a low admission fee of only $4 a person. This is a great
place for your family to learn about the beautiful artwork, pottery
and fine items that have been hand crafted by the Brazilian people for
hundreds of years.

Your family can have a splendid time in Belo Horizonte with a
well planned trip of activities. The city has a lot to offer
to family travelers, with its attractions ranging from entertaining
sports events to historical museums that challenge the mind.
