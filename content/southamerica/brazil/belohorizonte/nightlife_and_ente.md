title: "Nightlife and Entertainment"
type: section

Belo Horizonte nightlife is comparable to Amsterdam and New York. Thousands of thematic bars, prive clubs, cybercafes, giant rock/techno outdoor concerts, carnival, and moore, much moore. The regional food and the "Cachaça", international drink from Minas Gerais are the most top rated of this wonderful city!
