title: "Practical Information in Belo horizonte"
type: section
---

Belo Horizonte is located in the southeast of Brazil and is the
capital of Minas Gerais. It's an amazing place
with several parks, including the beautiful "Parque das Mangabeiras,"
from where you can have a spectacular view of the city. Here is some
practical information that will help you make the most of your visit to
Belo Horizonte.

**Telephone**

While you are in Belo Horizonte you will surely use your telephone
and you need to be aware of the country and area code in case someone
from back home wants to phone you. The international code for Brazil is
55 and the area code for Belo Horizonte is 31, so if someone calls you,
they should dial 1-55-31 and then your phone number.

**Time Difference**

One of the most confusing things while traveling is the difference
in time. The time difference between Belo Horizonte and Eastern
Standard Time in the US is 3 hours. If the EST is 12 noon, the time in
Belo Horizonte will be 3 p.m. It's wise to adjust your sleeping time a
bit before traveling to Belo Horizonte, so that you can enjoy your
holiday.

**Safety**

As a tourist in Belo Horizonte you must be alert and watch out for
thieves just as in any other city. Try not to keep all your money in
one place in your hand bag or pouch. If you need to take a bus at the
bus station, watch out for petty thieves. Try not to walk alone in the
dark and in lonely places. An extra bit of care taken can make your
holiday a memorable experience.

**Medical Services**

Check with your health service provider for any health warnings
before leaving your country. It is best to keep yourself fit at all
times; report any feeling of illness to your doctor before leaving.
While in Belo Horizonte if you don't feel too well, contact the local
hospital for a check-up with the doctor.

**Weather**

It is best to check the local weather in Belo Horizonte before
planning a holiday. September is spring in Belo Horizonte, summer is in
December, autumn in March and winter in June. These seasons are
different if you are coming in from the United States or Europe, so it's best to be informed beforehand. Though there is a difference in
temperature during each season, it is commonly known that there are two
clearly defined seasons in Belo Horizonte. It is hot and humid from
October to March, and cold and dry from April to September. Temperatures range between 18 to 23 degrees Celsius.
