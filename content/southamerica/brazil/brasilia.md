Brasilia is the modern capital of Brazil. The city was built according to a design by the German architect Neumann.


 Brasilia is quite pretty but it lacks the charm of [Rio de Janeiro](/southamerica/brazil/riodejaneiro) or Sao Paolo. The expression administrative capital is true in many senses.
