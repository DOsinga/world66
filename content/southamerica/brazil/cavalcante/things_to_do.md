---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

The Chapada dos Veadeiros offers an extraordinary range of outdoor activities. Hiking and trekking through the cerrado to hidden waterfalls is the main draw — many trails lead to natural swimming pools at the base of cascades. Birdwatching is excellent in the diverse cerrado habitat. More adventurous options include rappelling down canyon walls, canyoning, mountain biking, and horseback riding through the savanna. Natural warm springs in the area are particularly enjoyable at night or during the cooler months.
