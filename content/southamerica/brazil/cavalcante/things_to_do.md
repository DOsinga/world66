---
title: "Things to do in Cavalcante"
type: section
---

When you are in Chapada dos Veadeiros, you can do a multitude of things - **birdwatching, hiking, swimming, trekking, relaxing, rappelling, cycling and mountain biking, horseback hiding and canyoning**. You can integrate these **Chapada dos veadeiros activities and tours**
with the long walks and exploration trips in the Cerrado. There is a
natural warm water that you should visit these during the monsoons or
at night.
