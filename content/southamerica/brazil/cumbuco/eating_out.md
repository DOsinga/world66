**0031 Restaurant in Cumbuco**

Situated in the centre of Cumbuco on the beach, this beautifull Dutc owned restaurant serves the world on your plate. From the Thai curry to the Mexican burrito and the Indonesian satay..

the staff is very helpfull and willing to explain you everything there is to know about the nice caipifrutas or the famous caiparina.

the restaurant is open from monday until saturday from 18.00 hours until 23.00 hours. In the high season (oktober until january) they serve a daily special for just 10 reais. Check it out!!
