Curitiba is located in the Parana State of Brazil and it is about 500km away from Sao Paulo. It is a well planned and modern city with a number of attractions. There is a railway line, first in Brazil going from Curitiba to Paranaqua thorugh the mountains. The journey itself is breath taking.
