---
title: "Family Travel Ideas in Brazil"
type: section
---

Just because Brazil has a renowned nightlife and is known as a great destination for couples does not mean it doesn’t have anything to offer family travelers. This diverse country is loaded with zoos, parks, museums and shows that will have your children enthralled. When you want a little break from the city life, take a trip to the countryside to enjoy camping or join an educational family-oriented tour of the rainforest.

In most locations there are family friendly hotels and resorts that can meet your family’s needs. Situated both in bustling areas as well as off-the-beaten path locales, Brazil’s tourism industry meets the needs of family travelers with many accommodations providing activity groups for older children and teens as well as babysitting services for those under 3 or 4 years old. Whether children are participating in supervised activities or by your side, Brazil has some great attractions for the younger set.

**Rio de Janeiro Family Travel**
This metro area has many opportunities for family adventure. Enjoy the city’s botanical gardens, get some amazing views from the Corcovado Mountain and explore Tijuca Forest, the largest urban rainforest in the world. The Barra de Tijuca is a nice beach for children and Ipanema Beach has an amazing sunset that is not to be missed. Kids can douse themselves in body paint at the Indian Museum and hourly toy rental at the Parque do Catete makes for a fun-filled afternoon. Leblon Beach has a free play area complete with toys for kids and the city’s zoo, Jardim Zoologico, is also a great choice for children. Wet and Wild Rio, the city’s waterpark, is also a fun place to cool off on a hot afternoon.

**Sao Paulo Family Travel**
Sao Paulo has a fascinating snake study center – Butanta - that is a great stop for any lovers of reptiles in your family. The Praia do Pulso beach is a nice kid-friendly choice here. There is a variety of sporting events, shows and parks to choose from throughout the city as well. Visit Siqueira Campos Park and enjoy a nice play area, complete with swings and slides for younger children.

**Amazon Family Travel **
There is no better way to get your child interested in ecology than to give them a firsthand look at the Amazon. Join one of many family-friendly tours and stay any of the wide variety of family lodging available at this amazing attraction.
