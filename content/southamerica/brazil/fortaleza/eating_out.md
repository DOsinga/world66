---
title: "Eating Out in Fortaleza"
type: section
---

Fortaleza is a city that has several great restaurants
including many street vendors. The range of food available is wide and the
variety is large given that Fortaleza is blessed with several tropical fruits.
Many of the restaurants specialize in Brazilian cuisine which is popular among
the many tourists that visit the city. Some of the most popular dishes are the seafood
dishes, prepared in a unique method.

**Alimenta Bistro**

Avenida Dom Luís, 1112, Aldeota.

Phone 3267.5885

The Alimenta Bistro is one of the most popular restaurants
in Fortaleza. The restaurant offers an excellent variety of dishes prepared
with utmost care and detail. The restaurant was elected as the best restaurant
two years in a row and the chef Fernando Barroso was chosen as the chef of the
year in 2005. The restaurant is open Tuesday to Friday 6 p.m. to midnight and
Saturday 11 a.m. to 3 p.m. and 6 p.m. to midnight.

**Coco Babmu**

Canuto de Aguiar Street, 1317

Phone: (55 85) 242 7557

The Coco Bambu is one of the restaurants with a difference.
The restaurant has tables and benches made of logs, the candles placed on the tables
make it really stand out as a special place. The Coco Bambu restaurant serves Japanese
food and is one of the best in the area.

**Delicias do Mar**

Varjota near Aldeota

The Delicias do Mar is by far one of the best seafood
restaurants in Fortaleza. The restaurant was voted as the best seafood
restaurant by the Veja Magazine. The restaurant serves great seafood dishes;
however, the service is not up to the mark. On the overall the restaurant is a
great place to dine.

**Pulcinella**

Rua Osvaldo Cruz, 640, Aldeota, 3261-3411

(And) Avenida Senador Virgílio Távora, 600, 3244-0133.

The Pulcinella restaurant is the best Italian restaurant in
Fortaleza, the restaurant has two outlets and both are popular. The restaurant
serves excellent Italian food; one of its specialties is the Pasta with
Seafood. The restaurant is open Monday to Thursday Noon to 3 p.m. and 6 p.m. to
1 a.m.

**Colher de Pau**

Rua Frederico Borges, 206, Varjota, 3267-3773

Colher de Pau (Wooden Spoon) is a restaurant that is popular
with many, the food served is excellent. The dishes include galinha caipira,
carne de sol, buchada, etc. These dishes are very popular and some require
prior ordering. The restaurant is open
Sunday to Thursday 11 a.m. to midnight, Friday to Saturday 11 a.m. to 2 a.m.
The Colher de Pau can seat 380 guests.
