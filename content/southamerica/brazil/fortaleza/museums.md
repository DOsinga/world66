---
title: "Museums in Fortaleza"
type: section
---

The bountiful beaches, warm clear waters and searing heat can make it tempting to spend every daylight hour on the beach, but Fortaleza also boasts some interesting museums and cultural centers that are well worth a visit should the weather take a turn for the worse. With its many colonial buildings, many parts of Fortaleza seem like open-air museums in their own right, and the area's rich history makes visiting the city's museums well worthwhile even if it isn't a rainy day.
