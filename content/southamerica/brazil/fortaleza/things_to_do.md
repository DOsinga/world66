---
title: "Things to do in Fortaleza"
type: section
---

Located in northeast of Brazil, Fortaleza is the capital of
Ceara. Fortaleza has a population of over 2.5 million; it is the 5th
largest city in Brazil and is a popular tourist destination. Fortaleza is a
destination that is filled with nightclubs, restaurants and bars which are open
every day of the week. Being a popular destination for tourists there are many
things to do in Fortaleza.

**The Beach Park**

The Beach Park is located near the seashore in Fortaleza;
this is a complex that includes a Hotel, Museum, Aqua Park and a Souvenir
store. The park is a great place to spend the day or the weekend, with its
waterfalls, waterslides and its artificial river, it is a popular spot among
tourists.

**Fortaleza City Tour**

Take a tour of the city and discover its amazing history.
The guided tour of the city takes about 2 hours; you will visit the historical
buildings including the President Castelo Branco Mausoleum, the Metropolitan
Cathedral and much more. This is a great way to explore the city and get to
know its history and culture. The hotels normally have exact details of the
tour and its schedule departure.

**Canoa Quebrada**

Canoa Quebrada is located about 95 miles from the city and
is a great place for a one day adventure. This is one of the most popular spots
in Fortaleza. Try the special seafood dishes that are available here and do not
miss the buggy ride through the sand dunes.

**The Cumbuco Beach**

The Cumbico Beach is a beautiful white sand beach situated
in a secluded spot with an extraordinary view. There are several bars and
restaurants at Cumbuco Beach where you can enjoy some exquisite cuisine. A
refreshing swim in the Parnamirim is recommended.

**Lagoinha Beach**

Take a 65 mile drive from the center of the city to the
Lagoinha Beach. This is a beautiful fantasy beach filled with tropical
vegetation. There is a small Lagoon that is deep which is great for swimming.
The lush coconut trees offer a great place to relax. You can take a buggy or
ride a horse and explore the beauty of the sand dunes on the beach. A trip on a
‘jangada’ (sailboat) over the calm waters is a great way to make the most of
your day at this beautiful beach. There are local restaurants where you can enjoy
excellent fresh seafood dishes before ending your day and heading back to Fortaleza.
