Iguacu Falls are located on the Argentinian side and Brazilian. The river is the border for the two countries. The falls should be over 2700 metres in width. The amount of single falls changes cause of the rain period brings a lot more water. That's much of the information that I can remember from my trip there. Altough I didn't see any animals. Only tucans and butterflies. All the pussy cats were in the woods and didn't want to disturb them :) The park itself is beautiful and these falls are amazing!
