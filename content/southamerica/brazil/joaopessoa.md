Joao Pessoa is a big busy place in the east of Brazil, just north of [Olinda](/southamerica/brazil/olinda) and [Recife](/southamerica/brazil/recife). The main reason people come here is for the beaches, but before you head for the prayas you should at least check out the Igresia Sao Francisco one of the nicest churches of the country.


 The Lagoa is a nice place to relax a bit, it's a big park. Buses leave from here to Tambau one of the main beaches just east of town with an exciting nightlife scene. Praia Cabo Branco, Praia da Penha and Ponte de Seixas (easternmost point of South America) are more quiet and located to the south.
