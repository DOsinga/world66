---
title: "Budget Travel Ideas in Manaus"
type: section
---

Choosing** Manaus** as a vacation destination offers you the opportunity to get some beneficial options on **budget travel ideas**. Manaus is a very active vacation spot year round but the months from September through November seem to be the busiest. So you may want to see what the non-seasonal time prices are. A lot of budget specials from transportation companies are always showing up. So what we have done is given you a few ideas of what to expect when comparing prices for the best rates you can get.
**

Long Flights**

Budget flights need to be booked at least 7 days before departure date. If you’re traveling from Europe to Manaus there is an airline company named **TAM** that may have the best rates for you. An example is a round trip ticket from Barcelona to Manaus is priced at $1,150 per person that includes fees and taxes. In comparison prices can range up to $1,600 per person. Out of the Netherlands the price is even less at $1004 per person but can rise up to $1,200. Flights have at least 1 lay over, so be aware of that. And the comfort may be less than a higher-placed flight.

**Short Flights **

Flights originating out of the United States or within Brazil are quite inexpensive. A round trip flight on Copa or TAM out of Miami, Florida begins at around $555, and may cost up to $700. Most airlines that travel within the country usually charges about the same rates, and a round trip from Rio de Janiero to Manaus costs $470 as a best deal price.

**Long Cruises

**To those who like long cruises Holland American Cruise Lines may be your best bet for a budget deal. There is a 23-day Amazon Explorer cruise that is a must for those who want to see the Brazilian coast and its inner regions. The price starts at $3,599 for an interior cabin, $ 4,199 for an ocean view cabin, $ 6,999 for a verandah and $9,699 for a suite.
**

Short Cruises **

If you are a traveler or native to Brazil the short cruises are very affordable and there are a lot of budget deals there for you. General Cruises have 6-day tours that start in Rio de Janiero and go to Manaus for the budget price of $2,400 per person. The line also offers a 4-day cruise for 1,499 per person. Both of these cruises are the cheapest you will find for their nice accommodations. Big Five Tours also have some inexpensive boat tours.
