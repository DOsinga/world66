---
title: "Museums in Manaus"
type: section
---

Manaus is well known for its outdoor activities and natural beauty. However, there are several very popular museums including, but certainly not limited to, the Amazonia Natural Science Museum, the Indian Museum, the Museum of Northern Man, and the Port Museum. Some have a small admission fee, but they are informative and interesting.

**The Amazonia Natural Science Museum**

This museum makes for an amazing rainy day activity. With embalmed animals, live animals, insects, and plants, it is easy to discover and learn about the local flora and fauna. Obviously this museum is mainly a hub for tourists, as the local already know about the local plant and wildlife. In many ways, it is like a small zoo. Children tend to love this place, so it is perfect to go as a family.

**The Museum of Northern Man**

If you are interested in the culture and customs of Northern Brazilians, this museum is excellent. It provides information about their way of life and has tons of artifacts on display such as cooking items, clothing, traditional costumes, and hunting tools.

**The Indian Museum**

Similar to the Museum of Northern Man, the Indian Museum has thousands of items collected from the indigenous cultures of the area. In addition to the collection itself, it also has a section of indigenous arts and crafts on sale.

**The Port Museum**

This museum delves into the history of Manaus to a time when the English created the entire port area of the city. In the Port Museum, you will find the maps, documents, instruments, and tools of the English during that time period.
