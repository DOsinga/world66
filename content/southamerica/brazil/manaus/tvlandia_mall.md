One of the larger shopping centres in Manaus, sitting on Avenida Djalma Batista — the city's main commercial artery. The mall has a decent selection of Brazilian chain stores, a food court, and a multi-screen cinema. Air conditioning alone makes it a welcome retreat from the Amazonian heat. Not a destination in itself, but useful if you need to pick up supplies, catch a movie, or just cool down between excursions.
