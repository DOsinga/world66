The Pestana Rio Atlantica is a great hotel. The Marina Palace Hotel is an immaculate place to stay. The Pestana Rio Atlantica and the Marina Palace Hotel are both fine examples of luxury Niteroi hotels. The Bestana Rio Atlantica is close to both Sugar Loaf and Botanical Gardens, which both offer a great time. The Marina Palace is within walking distance of Ipanema and Copacabana Beaches. If you're looking for a cheap place to spend the night, Hotel Paysandu and Aeroporto Othon both offer breakfast and internet access. Hotel Payasandu and Aeroporto Othon are both within a few kilometers of lakes, beaches and many quality restaurants.
