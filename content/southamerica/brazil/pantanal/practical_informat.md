The EXPLORE PANTANAL BRASIL TURISMO, is a ECOTURISM and ECO ADVENTURE TRAVEL AGENCY with 20 years experience. The owner Marcello Yndio from the Kadiweu tribe knows the Pantanal and the Serra Bodoquena with his flora & fauna, culture, history by his heart and shares his knowlegde with his guests. We offer multilingual receptive and tours for small and big groups (in English, Spanish, German , French, Japanese and a decent Hebrew). We are specialized in Eco- Adventure with a great knowlegde about the Pantanal, Ecological Turism – Expeditions – Pantanal Research - Birdwatching – and river crusing. We are operating PANTANAL, BONITO, BODOQUENA, IGUASSU FALLS and CURITIBA.
