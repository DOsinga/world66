---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

The Pantanal rewards patient, curious travellers. Wildlife watching is the main draw — caimans, capybaras, giant otters, tapirs, anteaters, and a staggering variety of birds including the jabiru stork, hyacinth macaw, and tuiuiú. Guided boat trips along the rivers, horse rides into the wetland, and night safaris (spotlighting caiman eyes in the dark) are all standard offerings from the eco-lodges and base towns.
