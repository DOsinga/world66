---
title: "Paranaguá"
type: location
---

For many people Paranguá is just a port city, the end of the line from
the bus and train from [Curitiba](/southamerica/brazil/curitiba). But it is a scenic, laid back place,
perfect for a daytrip.

Although it is a big port, large ships can't be seen from the beach in
the city center. But if you are lucky, you catch the fishermen bringing
in the catch of the day.
