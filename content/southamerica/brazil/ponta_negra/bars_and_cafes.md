### [The Hemingway](/southamerica/brazil/ponta_negra/bars_and_cafes/the_hemmingway)

Situated at the promenade in the same building as the Norweigian society and hotel. It's owned by a retiered Norwiegian citizen. It has most of the drinks, snacks and small meals that you can whish. Large screen telivision and comfortable furnitures. You will also find a lot of Norweigian papers here.
