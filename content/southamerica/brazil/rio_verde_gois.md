Rio Verde means green river. It is a booming town of
approximately 150,000 habitants. The economy in Rio Verde is fed
largely by the corn, soybean and milo producers. In the last 7
years there is a hog and chicken (Perdigão) outfit that has moved in
creating many jobs as well as a ketchup processing plant. There
is a Russian colony north of Rio Verde and an American colony northwest
of Rio Verde.
