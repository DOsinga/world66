---
title: '7-Day Itinerary'
type: section
---

**Day 1 -- Christ the Redeemer and Santa Teresa.** Start early with the cog train up to [Corcovado](/southamerica/brazil/riodejaneiro/things_to_do/corcovado) before the crowds arrive. After taking in the view, head to the bohemian hillside neighbourhood of Santa Teresa for lunch -- narrow cobblestone streets, colonial houses, artists' studios and excellent restaurants like [Sobrenatural](/southamerica/brazil/riodejaneiro/eatingout/sobrenatural). In the evening, walk down to Lapa for your first taste of Rio's nightlife.

**Day 2 -- Sugar Loaf and Urca.** Take the cable car up [Sugar Loaf Mountain](/southamerica/brazil/riodejaneiro/things_to_do/corcovado) in the afternoon for sunset views. Before or after, explore the quiet neighbourhood of [Urca](/southamerica/brazil/riodejaneiro/things_to_do/urca_and_praia_ver) and its small red-sand beach, Praia Vermelha, at the base of the mountain.

**Day 3 -- Beach day in Ipanema and Leblon.** Spend the day at [Ipanema](/southamerica/brazil/riodejaneiro/beaches/ipanema) and [Leblon](/southamerica/brazil/riodejaneiro/beaches/leblon). Rent a chair, try an acai bowl from a beach vendor, watch the surfers at Arpoador. In the evening, explore the restaurants and bars along Rua Dias Ferreira in Leblon.

**Day 4 -- Centro and the museums.** Head downtown to see Rio's colonial and imperial past. Visit the [Banco do Brasil Cultural Center](/southamerica/brazil/riodejaneiro/things_to_do/banco_do_brasil_cu), the Imperial Palace, and the ornate Real Gabinete Portugues de Leitura. Have lunch at one of the cheap and cheerful restaurants in the streets around Praca XV. In the afternoon, walk along the waterfront to the [Museum of Modern Art](/southamerica/brazil/riodejaneiro/things_to_do/modernartmuseum).

**Day 5 -- Tijuca Forest and Botanical Garden.** Hike in the [Tijuca Forest](/southamerica/brazil/riodejaneiro/things_to_do/floresta_da_tijuca) in the morning -- trails range from easy to challenging. Cool off afterward at the [Botanical Garden](/southamerica/brazil/riodejaneiro/things_to_do/botanicalgarden), a peaceful oasis of tropical plants. Stop at [Parque Lage](/southamerica/brazil/riodejaneiro/things_to_do/parque_lage) for coffee in its beautiful courtyard cafe.

**Day 6 -- West Zone beaches.** Head out to [Barra da Tijuca](/southamerica/brazil/riodejaneiro/beaches/barra_da_tijuca) for a long stretch of open beach, or continue further to [Prainha](/southamerica/brazil/riodejaneiro/beaches/prainha) for surfing in a wild setting. If the weather is bad, the malls in Barra offer a full day of shopping and cinema.

**Day 7 -- Niteroi or Copacabana.** Take the ferry across the bay to Niteroi for Oscar Niemeyer's Contemporary Art Museum and the dramatic ocean beaches on the far side. Or spend your last day on [Copacabana](/southamerica/brazil/riodejaneiro/beaches/copacabana), soaking up the atmosphere, eating acai on the beachfront and picking up last souvenirs at the [Feira Hippie](/southamerica/brazil/riodejaneiro/shopping/feirartei) if it is Sunday.
