title: 'Palaphita Kitch'
type: poi
address: 'Kiosk 20, Lagoa Rodrigo de Freitas'
latitude: -22.972
longitude: -43.212
---

One of the best kiosk bars on the Lagoa, with sofas facing the water and views of Corcovado. The decor uses sustainably sourced wood and the menu draws on Amazonian cuisine -- try the bruschetta de berinjela or the coalho cheese with dried tomatoes. Drinks include creative caipivodkas with tropical fruit. A beautiful spot for a sunset drink.
