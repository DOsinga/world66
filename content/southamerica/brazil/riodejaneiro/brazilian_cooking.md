Several operators in Rio offer hands-on cooking classes where you learn to prepare Brazilian staples like moqueca (fish stew), caipirinhas, cassava dishes and brigadeiros. Classes typically last a few hours, include ingredients and drinks, and are conducted in English. It is a fun way to bring a taste of Brazil home with you. Most are based in Copacabana or Ipanema and cater to small groups.
