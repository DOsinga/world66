Rio Boat Party is an afternoon cruise for young people that goes on a short 5-hour tour around Rio’s harbor bay. The cruise can take up to 80 people max. and stops every now and then so people can splash around and go for a refreshing swim.
My friends and I had a blast when we went on Rio’s Boat Party. We got soooo hammered (the caipirinhas were for free), the view was great and so were the girls. Too bad the cruise only happens twice a month!
