**AMIR RESTAURANT **
[http://www.amirrestaurante.com.br/scripts/index.asp](/southamerica/brazil/riodejaneiro/eatingout)

Amir is a very special place. As I got there, I felt myself in a "Arabian Nights" Scenario, but not in the cheesy way. Somehow they managed to create a very special ambiance. Now, lets go to what matters the most: the food. Everybody knows Arabic food, in each place of the globe you will find it, but is hard to find it so well done as in Amir. Winner for 5 times in a row the best Arabic Restaurant in Rio, Amir has the traditional dishes with an special touch. Besides all that, they also have a great belly dance show, every Thursday night. I left the place delighted in all senses.
