This is a nice place over at Ipanema, with a great view of the Arpoador, where they serve some of the most delicious Brazilian food I have tried to date. One of my favorites is the "pargo" fish, served with rice and potatoes. Because its cooked in a thick layer of salt, the fish becomes soft and tender. They have their other secret cooking techniques that add up to the flavor, making this restaurant a great addition to the top dining spots in Rio.
