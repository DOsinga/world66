Serving a wonderful bbq this restaurant has a perfect meat and excelent seafood. It's one of the best ones I've ever been. The price is not cheap, but the food is so good that is a real delight to eat there. From lobster, huge shrimps and caviar to the best argentinian beef you'll ever try, this place is worthy going. From Monday to Friday the prices are cheaper (around R$42,00) and on holidays, holiday eve's and Saturday/ Sunday a bit more expensive (around R$50,00). (On Sundays after 8 pm the prices are also R$ 42,00). The website of the restaurant gives an idea of the food they serve. There are 2 Barra Brasa's. One located in Barra and one in Leblon. I'd recommend you to walk in the Leblon beach and later on enjoy this marvellous food. You won't regret ! :)
