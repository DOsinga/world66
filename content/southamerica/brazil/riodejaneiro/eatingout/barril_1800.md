**Barril 1800 **is one of those great places you find out when you most need it and couldn't become more satisfied. The huge house, just across the street of Ipanema Beach, is used to become frequenters that leave the sand and go there for a nice meal. The place is also a temple of carioca Bohemia, and is always full every night of the week, probably because the great draft beer they offer. The local cuisine restaurant is tasty and in large amounts, enough to share among 2 or even 3 people. The prices are very nice and the service too. I enjoyed it very much !
