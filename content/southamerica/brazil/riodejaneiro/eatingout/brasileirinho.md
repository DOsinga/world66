Do you like Brazillian food? Have you even tried it? If you tried and did not like it, you have not tried the restaurant Brasileirinho in Ipanema. Located at Rua Jangadeiros at the popular General Osorio square, this place has very authentic Brazillian food and is not expensive. They serve the famous "feijoada" dish which is like a black bean soup with meat in it, very delicious. They also have other tastie options like ground beef with thin string pieces of potatoes and fried eggs with "farofa", which is made with manioc, a type of vegtable very common in Brazil. They also have some tastie drinks, like the "caipirinhas". Very good food, very good times.
