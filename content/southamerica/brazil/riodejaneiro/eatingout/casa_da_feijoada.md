After a breakfast composed
of açai and a grilled cheese sandwich, my friend and I weren't hungry until late
afternoon, when we made the wise decision to try the main local dish
(drumroll)... the Feijoada Brasileira! We went to this restaurant in the
Ipanema neighborhood aptly called **Casa da Feijoada** and by now, hungry as we
were, went straight for the main dish. These guys make a mean feijoada, tell
you what! We also sipped some caipirinhas, which we were told, go together like
peas and carrots when it comes to feijoada. A superb experience indeed!
