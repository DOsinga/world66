Few people around the world had the privilege to get to know the Portuguese food. Put that small country full of seas has so many delicious dishes I couldnt believe. Now you must be thinking, "*why is this guy talking about Portuguese food on the Rio de Janeiro's part of the website?" *The thing is that Brazil used to be a Portugal colony and the influences of the European country in the South America paradise remain till nowadays. I found out all that during my unforgettable days in Rio, and the stage of such a great experience was **CBF RESTAURANT. **Placed on a lovely area of Downtown Rio, the antique house, full of history serves aside from some Spanish dishes, like paella, the traditional Portuguese dishes such as cod fish with baked potatoes, olives and
shrimp, just fabulous. All for great prices and cozy ambiance! Deserve a try !
