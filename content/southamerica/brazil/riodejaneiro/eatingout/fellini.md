For those who lives or visits the Zona Sul of Rio, Fellini is the best option in for eating out. Varied options of gastronomy, good attendance and delicious desserts.You can choose to "help yourself" and pay by the pound or opt for the all-you-can-eat buffet. In either options you can treat yourself with seafood, filet mignon, japonese food and fresh green salads.
