Can you image that in Rio de Janeiro there's a place so unique that
can be called an Italian Bistro? Italian because the menu is all based on the
Pizza land gastronomy, but you will find only the modern and sophisticated
tendencies of this traditional cuisine. Bistro because the flair and coziness
of those places are preset everywhere. With 3 floors, the third a terrace right
under Cristo's Statue, the ambiance could not be nicer. For passioned lovers,
**Lulu** is a great choice. You can have a quiet place full of candle light to
inpire your romance. For group of friends, also a good idea, after all, who
doesnt enjoy a well prepared meal in a beautifully decorated environment?
