Via Sete has a great atmosphere! I was on a diet, while in Brazil, and there I could choose lots of light foods to eat. There they offer great plates made with salads and grilled meat. To drink, I ordered strawberry frozen, that was made with 100% of fruit.

**Visitor Testimonial:**

Being a veggie, I was gladly surprised that at Via Sete in Ipanema they have a veggie menu. I had a dish that’s tofu with cereals, vegetables and mushrooms and it was really good. Their salad mix is all made up of organic vegetables, too. Their juice menu is also worth mentioning, because there are many options and some really exotic fruits that at least here in France we’re not really familiar with (like acai). I recommend it!
