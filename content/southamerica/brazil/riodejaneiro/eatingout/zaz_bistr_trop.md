In Rio, there is plenty of diversity when it comes to eating out. The Ipanema neighbourhood (which is between Leblon and Copacabana) is no exception to this rule. One of the better places I found was the** Zaza Bistro**, at Joana Angelica street, which was near my hotel. Since I was eating at the hotel almost every day of my stay, I wanted a change of menu. I asked the concierge if he knew where I could get a good meal around that area, so he handed me this guide called Rio For Partiers which has a lot of useful information if you´re new to the city. I decided to go for the closest and it turned out it was magnificent in all its entirety! Great tasty, quite exotic and original food with tasty drinks, and quite cozy as much, so put this place down as one of your dining spots. Cheers!
