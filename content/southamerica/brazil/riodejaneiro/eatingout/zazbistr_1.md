**The Zazá Bistrô kitsch decoration. Walls in colors and famous phrases in everywhere, red roses in velvet.
A wonderful work of trendy Isabela Piereck. **

**The audacious combinations of flavors of Southeast Asian, North African spices and seasonings of Latin America with Brazilian ingredients are registered trademark of the bolivian chef, Checho Gonzales. ****From his kitchen comes the Sea-bess Fish with Banana Purée served with fresh hearts of palm, herb oil & small red peppers; the Sealed Tuna to the gravy of mango and ginger with a Sicillian lemon couscous, nuts and cilantro.****Irresistible, the Banana-carpaccio brulée comes warm
with cinnamon and tapioca ice cream.**

**This mix of Art trend, exotic cuisine and a lot of romantism becomes a very plesant ambient: The Zazá Bistrô!**
