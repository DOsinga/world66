Brazil is famous for
its original and unique food, there is no doubt about it. The thing is that
after spending a whole week staying a lot of heavy dishes, All I and my Boyfriend
wanted was a taste of international, contemporary food in a nice, cool and hype
place. The talented chef of **ZUKA**, was very smart by mixing Brazilian classics
to Thai satays and French nouveau ,with harmony and good taste. Not only the
food was delicious but also exquisite and beautifully put together. The prices
are sure not the cheapest in Rio , but I'm sure
it worthed every dime. The trendy but not
pretensious restaurant is a magical place to have an exotic and tasty
dinner. I got really surprised
by the ambience: cosy but modern, full of hip and intersting people. Definitly
a must try
