Buses are the least expensive mode of transport. They can be very convenient but confusing. Look for a listing of the major destinations on the front of the bus (though mostly they're only as specific as the neighborhoods the bus travels through). Certain higher-priced buses (again, the price is usually advertized on a sign on the front or side of the bus) are air-conditioned and very comfortable. Several of these run along the major beachfront roads in Ipanema and Copacabana. Buses can be dangerous at times (check out the film "Bus 174" for a spectacular example), and the drivers can be very aggressive -- so make sure you're holding onto something tight.
