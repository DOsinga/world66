The Metro is a very efficient way of going from one place to another. It is air conditioned and clean. There are two lines. It operates from 6 am to 11 pm on working days, but only from 2 pm - 8 pm on Saturdays. Closed on Holidays. There are different ticket, so go to a ticket counter and find out the one that suits you: single, multi or inegrated bus & metro tickets.
