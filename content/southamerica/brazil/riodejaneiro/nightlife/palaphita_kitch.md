The winner in the category best kiosk, the Palaphita Kitch has strengths as a decoration ecologically correct, which uses wood reforestation, the menu inspired by the cuisine of Amazonia, where the owner was born, and sofas in front of the amazing visual of " Lagoa Rodrigo de Freitas". For the house, The owner, Mario, brought unusual food, which comes from his experience as a member of "Casa dos Sabores" buffet.
Some delights such as the "bruschetta de berinjela" or a portion of four "antepastos" - pâté with chutney of açaí, the "coalho" cheese with dried tomatoes, sautéed with vegetables and jambu and "pirarucu" dry. To drink, caipvodca of tangerine with "mangarataia" (ginger), (national vodka) and (imported vodka).
