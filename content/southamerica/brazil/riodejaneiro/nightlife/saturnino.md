Saturnino is a bar in Jardim Botanico, filled with hip locals. Lots of variation on the caipirinha and drinks. On the top list you can find Mule, (tangerine, kiwi, passion fruit and
strawberries with vodka and energic), the Saturnino Fresh (vodka, tangerine,
lemon and mandarine) and Coconut vanilla (water and scrapings of the
coconut and vanilla Absolut).
Whatever the choice, will surely combine
with one of the sophisticated snacks: harumaki of brie with
apricot jam (portion), their shrimp croquette (portion) or
the mushroom-balls with buffalo mozzarella. Serve
Rotter craft beer.
