**Shenanigans** is an Irish pub found right in the middle of Zona Sul Rio. You can order drinks and attempt to sit or play pool (the pub is generally crowded) or make your way through the people to the small balcony and enjoy conversation, a beer, and a cigarette.

-------------------------------------

Best pub ever in Rio is called Shenanigans! It’s a real cozy place, with lots of good drinks and beers…and if you love billiards, that’s the place to go too! I had some pretty good steaks there too, the food is delicious! And the things I loved the most, I guess, was that I found many other “gringos” in there, so, I felt somewhat close to home! Great place!
