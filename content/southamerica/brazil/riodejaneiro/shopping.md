---
title: 'Shopping'
type: section
---

Shopping in Rio ranges from enormous air-conditioned malls to street markets and specialist shops. The **Feira Hippie** at Praca General Osorio in Ipanema runs every Sunday and is the best place to find handicrafts, art and souvenirs. [Pe de Boi](/southamerica/brazil/riodejaneiro/shopping/pe_de_boi) in Laranjeiras stocks high-quality Brazilian folk art and crafts. [Brasil & Cia](/southamerica/brazil/riodejaneiro/shopping/brasil__cia) in Ipanema specialises in Brazilian-made gifts, from hammocks to cachaça.

For music lovers, [Modern Sound](/southamerica/brazil/riodejaneiro/shopping/modern_sound) on Rua Barata Ribeiro in Copacabana is an institution -- a large music store with a cafe and regular live performances. For mall shopping, [BarraShopping](/southamerica/brazil/riodejaneiro/shopping/barra_shoppe) in Barra da Tijuca is one of the largest in South America, while [Shopping da Gavea](/southamerica/brazil/riodejaneiro/shopping/shopping_da_gavea) is smaller but more curated.
