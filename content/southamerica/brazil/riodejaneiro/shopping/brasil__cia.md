Brasil & Cia is a local craft /Brazilian
art store in the heart of Ipanema, very close to the beach, in Rio de Janeiro . Everybody knows how rich of
art and culture is Brazil ,
the weird thing is that is very difficult to find a decent store, with
no-cheesy stuff to buy for nice prices. Brazil & Cia products are selected
among the largest crafts and popular art centers in Brazil . They have Brazilian popular
art objects that reflect important aspects of the local culture. The products
are exquisite, alive. Makes you wanna buy it all, for your home and as
souvenirs for relatives and friends.
