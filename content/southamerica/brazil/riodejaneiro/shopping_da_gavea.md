A mid-sized, upscale shopping center in the residential Gávea neighborhood, near the Jardim Botânico and PUC university. The mall is quieter than the big complexes like BarraShopping, which makes it a more relaxed place to browse. The ground-floor food court has several good sit-down restaurants, and the Estação NET cinema upstairs shows a mix of mainstream and arthouse films — a favorite with the local university crowd. Shops lean toward Brazilian fashion and home goods rather than international chains.
