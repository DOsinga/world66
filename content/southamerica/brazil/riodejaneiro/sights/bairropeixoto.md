This landmark art-deco district right in the middle of Copacabana's delicious urban chaos is a haven of piece, preserving a small-town atmosphere. Buildings are 4-stories high or lower. There is a quiet square where the elderly play chess next to children in the playground. There is a youth hostel in the area, and Bairro Peixoto is also popular with international budget traveler. Walk a few blocks and you are on the main street or the beach.
