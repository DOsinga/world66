This is not Rio de Janeiro, but [Niteroi](/southamerica/brazil/niteroi), another city!

The Fort that was protecting the entrance to the Guanabara Bay which you can see best from top of Sugar Loaf is a must visit when staying in Rio de Janeiro. You can go there taking the Barcas - Rio/Niteroi ferry connection - at Praca 15 de Novembro at Centro. At the terminal in Niterói is leaving a tourist bus from the tourist authority of Niterói - Neltur. You will have a wonderful impression of the Sugar Loaf from the other side of the Bay and learn something about the history of this bullwark of protection.
See a virtual tour on The Rio de Janeiro Photo Guide to get convinced.
