The quiet neighbourhood of Urca sits at the base of Sugar Loaf Mountain, right on Guanabara Bay. Its calm, tree-lined streets feel like a small town rather than part of a megacity. Praia Vermelha (Red Beach), a small sheltered cove at the foot of the cable car station, has coarse reddish sand and gentle waves -- a peaceful alternative to the big Zona Sul beaches. The Pista Claudio Coutinho trail runs along the waterfront from here, offering easy walking with views of the bay.
