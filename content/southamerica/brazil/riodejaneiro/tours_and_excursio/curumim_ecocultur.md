Curumim is an eco and cultural tourism company based in Rio de Janeiro.

We offer unique, private tours which give the traveler the chance to meet Rio from a different perspective. We offer the regular sightseeing tours, during which we visit all the highlights (including Corcovado and Pão de Açucar) of the city, but we also offer tours during which we provide the traveler with the opportunity to get to know Rio´s nature, culture and inhabitants. Something out of the ordinary: more in the eco-cultural direction. Travelers who are interested in getting themselves more involved with our wonderful city and culture should definitely take a look at our website. Among the possibilities are bicycle tours, hiking tours, tours during which museums and galleries will be visited and last but not least a true Brazilian cooking experience!
All tours are private, flexible and can be easily adapted to personal wishes. Our guides speak many different languages and all tours include pick-up service from the hotel.
