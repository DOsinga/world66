It's not too difficult to find your way around Rio, the metro system is very good, although not particularly extensive, but the bus network will take you pretty much everywhere.

Buses and yellow taxis can be found at the airport, although taking the bus from the airport can be risky. Yellow taxis are generally quite good and cost effective for short journeys, just bare in mind that many taxi drivers drive like Ayreton Senna!

Other options are available in the form of private, unmarked taxi services such as Rio Airport Transfer. These companies are very good, although they do cost a little more than a yellow cab. The advantage with private companies is that they will generally meet you at the airport, their cars are air-conditioned, often with tinted windows, some will allow you to pay prior to arriving at the airport which means you don't have to visit a mony machine at the airport, and they tend to employ much calmer drivers. These companies can be found by searching google for "Rio Airport Transfers" or by going directly to www.rioairporttransfer.com
