### [International Convention of RPG](/southamerica/brazil/saopaulo/festivals/international_conv)

Every year, the "Devir Livraria" (Bookshop Devir) promotes the biggest RPG (Role Playing Game) event in the country, usually taking place in parks or big convention spaces. The event consists of stands of RPG editors (International and Brazilian), fan clubs and Comics. A huge space is reserved for playing table RPG, lkjlive action role playing (LARP) and card games. A lot of seminars about RPG, fantasy and science-fiction related themes are given. There are also lots of stands with drink and food.

Many times, international authors and known people from the Comics and RPG [more..](/southamerica/brazil/saopaulo/festivals/international_conv)

### [São Paulo Gay Parade](/southamerica/brazil/saopaulo/festivals/so_paulo_gay_par)

São Paulo hosts the largest Gay Parade in the world; the 2006 edition had more than 2,000,000 attendants.
Besides the Parade itself, there are several events regarding GLS rights.

In 2007, the Parade will be on June 10th.
The official website is:

### [F1 Grand Prix of Interlagos](/southamerica/brazil/saopaulo/festivals/f1_grand_prix_of_i)

Placed at the Interlagos Autodrome, where Ayrton Senna, Emerson
Fittipaldi and Rubens Barricello once trained, the Grand Prix is part
of the circuit of the FIA world championship.

### [Achiropita Festival (Festa da Achiropita)](/southamerica/brazil/saopaulo/festivals/festa_da_achiropit)

Traditional Itallian festival organized by the local church "Paroquia de Nossa Senhora da Achiropita". Admission is free. The festival is composed by stands selling Italian food, sweets, snacks and crafts along two streets in the Bixiga (a traditional Italian neighborhood) usually in August.



[[rate it]](/southamerica/brazil/saopaulo/festivals/festa_da_achiropit)

url: [www.achiropita.org.br](http://www.achiropita.org.br/)

address: Rua Treze de Maio, Rua Sao Vicente
