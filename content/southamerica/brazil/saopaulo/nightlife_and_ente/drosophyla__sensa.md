From the outside it looks like an old central São Paulo house... it has no name or signposting on the street.... when you get inside you are in world class bar, decorated in with Brazilian 'contemporary baroque style' and with a beautiful 'oriental garden'. Brazilian and international 'petiscos'... full meals... great cocktails and very cold beers... the public is quite varied but includes many video-makers, artists and journalists....
