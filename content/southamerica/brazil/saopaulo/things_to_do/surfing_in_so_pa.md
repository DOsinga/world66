**You can find **everything you want on this america's lead city of fashion, remember: you are in São Paulo, the city that never stops. 

Surfing is one of most popular activities among the youth because of easy access to the beaches. 

The best place to surf is the north coast, between Guarujá and Ubatuba, just beneath the Green Coast; you will find out why... 

Any kind of surf wear and supplies are available on most of the malls, for example on Paulista Shopping Center.
