---
title: "Top 5 Must Do's in Sao Paulo"
type: section
---

Sao Paulo is the largest city in Brazil and is also the 7th
largest metropolitan area in the world. The city is a popular tourist destination and
has several beautiful, renowned landmarks. A visit will be
incomplete without visiting and taking part in some of its amazing activities.
Here is a list of the top 5 must-do’s while visiting Sao Paolo.**
**

**1. Train Ride to the Countryside**

One of the things every traveler should do before leaving
San Paulo is take a train ride into the countryside. Escape from the rush of
the city traffic and enjoy a pleasant ride on the train.
The ride will take you to some of the beautiful urban towns where you can get
off and take a stroll down the roads and enjoy nature at its best. This is a
good way for you to relax and rejuvenate before leaving san Paulo.**
**

**2. St. Nicholas
Cathedral**

The St. Nicholas Russian Orthodox Cathedral at first sight may
look like a “Pagoda” to many people, but this is an ancient church in San Paulo.
Though the church is located in the center of San Paulo, it's surrounded by
beautiful low gardens and is a peaceful place. A visit to this Cathedral is a
must before leaving.**
**

**3. Japantown**

Unlike many other cities in the world, Brazil has its own
“Japantown” instead of a Chinatown. This is a unique experience for those
visiting Brazil, to find a Japantown in the center of San Paulo. The place is
a bit expensive but is worth the visit. You can experience the best of Japan
and Brazil in this great place. This is a must-do while visiting San Paulo.**
**

**4. Central Market**

Another must-do while visiting San Paulo is taking a trip to
the Central market. When traveling to any country, one of the best ways to
experience the best of its local culture and flavor is to take a trip to the
central market. A great place to enjoy local
Brazilian shopping at its best. You can walk around and
buy Brazilian products. Visiting the central market is an experience you will
enjoy.**
**

**5. Jaragua Peak**

Enjoy the beautiful view of San Paulo from this spot. The
Jaragua hills are a forest reserve that offers visitors a unique experience of
viewing the beauty of this city. Visiting the Jaragua hills is a must before
leaving San Paulo. The reserve is open daily from 7 a.m. to 6 p.m.
