Eliana R. S. Souza runs individualized tours for visitors to Sao Paolo. She speaks excellent English and is very knowledgeable about the city and its history. She can customize the tours to fit your interests. She charges about $70 for three hours, which includes the use of her car, which can take three adults. She will pick you up and drop you off. I had only a day in the city and found it very useful to go around with her. She can be reached at spintours@terra.com.br or at 55 11 9185-2623
