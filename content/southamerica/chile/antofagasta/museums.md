**Museo Historico Regional**

Inside the former Aduana (Customs house) you can find this museum. The museum displays interesting items and visual displays of the history of the region. The explanations are unfortunately only in Spanish and therefore the visitors' interest might be reduced if you do not speak the language.  [more..](/southamerica/chile/antofagasta/museums/museohistoricoregional)

openinghours: 10 am - 1 pm, 3:30 pm - 6:30 pm Tuesday and Saturday, Sunday from 11 am - 2 pm.
