---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

The main gateway is [Calama](/southamerica/chile/calama), which has an airport with regular flights from Santiago. From Calama it is about an hour by bus or shared transfer to [San Pedro de Atacama](/southamerica/chile/sanpedrodeatacama), the main base for visiting the desert. Alternatively, you can fly into [Antofagasta](/southamerica/chile/antofagasta), [Arica](/southamerica/chile/arica), or [Iquique](/southamerica/chile/iquique) and travel south overland.

The Atacama also sits at a crossroads for overland travellers heading between Chile, [Bolivia](/southamerica/bolivia), and [Peru](/southamerica/peru). The border crossing at Hito Cajón connects to Uyuni and the Bolivian salt flats — a popular route done as a multi-day jeep tour.
