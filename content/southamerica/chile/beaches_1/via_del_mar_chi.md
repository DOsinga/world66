Excellent mediterranean style city, 600.000 population, 1 hour from capital santiago, many beaches, best climate in Dec-Feb (24-30 °C), good nightlife, hotels from 5 to 1/2 stars. Chilean are great people, friendly. The country is safe, well developed and extremely diverse and beautiful ("Nature's Hideout), from deserts, mountain ranges, lakes, beaches, rain forests, etc. Excellent food.
