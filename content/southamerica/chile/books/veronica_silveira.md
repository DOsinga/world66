I recommend a book on Chile which has just been published by Palgrave-Macmillan. The title is 'A history of the British presence in Chile', and it covers a wide range of topics, from the time of the pirates, through the British presence in the War of Independence, the Battle of Coronel in the First World War, and the decline of British influence after 1939. The book is well researched and very readable, and is I believe the only book on this fascinating history that has ever been published.
