is an absolute must for anyone passing through Concepcion. The name means "thirty-something" and refers to the number choices of empanadas available at the restaurant. One empanada will do for a light dinner, and two if you are hungry, and at about $1.50 each, are a great deal. The restaurant also serves alcohol, with wine and Pisco Sours the favorites. This restaurant is appropriate as the destination for a low-key night out, or as the precurser to an all-night outing at one of the nearby night clubs.
