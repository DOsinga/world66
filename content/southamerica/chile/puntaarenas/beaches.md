---
title: "Beaches in Punta Arenas"
type: section
---

**Punta Arenas** **beaches** are the perfect place to spend the
day. Most of the beaches offer excellent swimming and a wide variety of
water activities. Keep in mind that most of these beaches offer a light
breeze, making the temperatures feel cooler than what they are. It is
very easy to get a sunburn so make sure to bring plenty of sunscreen and
water with you.

**Punta Arenas Beach**

Punta Arenas Beach is one long beach that stretches through a small
village. This beach is not usually crowded and you will not usually be
approached by beach vendors. The waves are normally very mild and the
water stays shallow, making this beach the ideal place to swim. Punta
Arenas beach is an excellent beach to bring small children. You will
find several restaurants along the beach and cabanas to rent. Punta
Arenas is the perfect beach if you are looking for some privacy.

**La Restinga Lagoon**

La Restinga Lagoon is a great place to spend the day with children.
La Restinga is in a national park and is considered a lagoon and a
beach. You do have to take a short boat ride to get here. The beach is a
very long sandbar and you find lot of restaurants, bathrooms and rental
companies. The waters are very shallow, making this and ideal place for
kids to swim. The water is normally very warm, the sand is soft and has
several seashells. The children will also enjoy looking for the small
crabs that like to hide on the rocks. La Restinga is also home to over a
hundred different bird species. Some of the birds located here can not
be found anywhere else in the world.

**Playa Caribe Beach**

Playa Caribe is one of the more popular beaches. You will normally
find a lot of action going on here. The waters here get deep fairly
quickly so use caution if you have young children with you. Older
children and adults will enjoy the moderate waves on this beach. You can
rent boats and jet skis at Playa Caribe making this the perfect beach
to enjoy water activities. There are several restaurants and bars
located along this beach. You will also find that Playa Caribe is
considered the party beach. People play their music loudly and even
dance right on the beach!
