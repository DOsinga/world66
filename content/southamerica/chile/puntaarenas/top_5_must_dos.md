---
title: "Top 5 Must Do's in Punta Arenas"
type: section
---

There are many **Punta Arenas Must Do's**. Some of the activities
range from visiting museums to sight seeing. There is a great number of
things to do in Punta Arenas.

**Visiting El Museo del Recuerdo**

This museum has a botanical garden, a shepherd's home and a shed. The
museum showcases one of the earliest homes in the region of Punta
Arena. People can walk around the botanical garden and look at the
display of agricultural equipment that has been used by the early
population of Punta Arenas.

Tourists who wish to visit this Museum can take a taxi from Zona
Franca to the museum. The admission is $2 USD for adults. Children enter
the museum without paying.

**Boating to Isla Magdalena Pinguinera**

This is an island that is two hours away from Punta Arenas by boat.
The island is a breeding ground and a natural habitat for the Magallanes
penguins. The penguins are not afraid of humans and do not run away
from humans at sight. Tourists should not wander around to avoid
upsetting the penguins or stepping on their eggs.

The tour of the island is one hour long. It is best to visit the
island during the months of October to February.

**Cerro Mirador Ski Center**

This ski resort is located inside the Magallanes National Reserve of
Punta Arenas. This recreational area is 2,000 feet above sea level and
people can see as far as Punta Arenas City and Tierra del Fuego. The
influx of tourists is at its highest during the months of June to
September.

Tourists can ski and jump from the slopes. There are also snowshoe
walking tours of the area. The ski resort offers transportation services
that vary in price according to the number of vehicles and people
visiting the slopes.
**Shopping at Zona Franca**

One of the activities on Punta Arena must-do list is visiting Zona
Franca. Tourists can find anything from household electronics to
imported designer brands. There are two floors that make up the store.
One floor is mainly for people who buy wholesale items.
**Parque Nacional Alberto De Agostini**

This park has many glaciers and is located to the southwest of Punta
Arenas. People can either reach the park by boat or by bus. This park
should not be missed.
