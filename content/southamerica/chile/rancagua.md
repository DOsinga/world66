City situated 85 kilometres to Santiago, was founded 1743 for Manso de Velasco, is next to Cachapoal River and has 250.000 inhabitants. 

Is know for the Rancagua Disaster in 1814 when Chile lost with the Realists Forces in the center city, is know too for to stay near to El Teniente Mine, the largest copper mine of the world, in when is Sewell, a beautiful old mine camp; and is famous for its agriculture activity.
