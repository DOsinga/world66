Santiago de Chile was founded in 1489. The location of the Chilean capital is quite spectacular: the snowcapped mountains of the Andes provide the backdrop for the city. 







Santiago is not a stylish city it is dreary and grey even in the depths of summer. Strangely it is very much the center of the world as far as Chileans are concerned. For nightlife, eating out or anything at all this is the place to be. The best place to experience these things is the La Pintana neighbourhood. 

For a good view of the city it is a good idea to take the funicular to Cerro San Cristobal. Santiago offers some great possibilities for daytrips as well. [Valparaiso](/southamerica/chile/valparaiso) is not far away, the mountains have great hiking opportunities, the ocean is nearby.
