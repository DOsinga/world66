Another option is to use long distance bus lines. If coming from
Buenos Aires, it is possible to use modern buses with "servicio cama"
(a comfortable reclining bed) or "semi-cama" (similar to air first
class) from the Retiro bus depot in Buenos Aires to Mendoza, a city
famous for its wines, next to the Andes mountains. For example ANDESMAR
leaves Buenos Aires in the evening every hour starting at 6:30 PM.
"Servicio cama" is about 115 Argentine pesos, about US$39.28 and
"servicio semi-cama" is AR$95.00, about US$32.45. The 6:30PM bus from
Retiro in Buenos Aires arrives in Mendoza next day at 8:24 AM.
Then from Mendoza, a similar bus from ANDESMAR, TUR-BUS or CATA, are
available for the Andes crossing to Santiago. Highly recommended to
appreciate the beauty of the Andes mountains and admire the magnificent
Anconcagua mountain, the tallest mountain in the world after the
Himalayas, about 6962 meters or 22,841 feet high. The trip for the
Andes crossing is about 5 hours and about US$20.00.
