One of the finest pre-Columbian art museums in South America, housed in the old Royal Customs House on Bandera 361. The collection covers ten thousand years of art and artefacts from Mesoamerica to Patagonia. The textile room and the Andean gold collection are particular highlights. Open Tuesday to Sunday.
