---
title: Beaches
type: section
---

Viña del Mar has a long stretch of Pacific beaches, though the Humboldt Current keeps the water cold year-round. **Playa de Viña** in front of the casino is the most central and busiest beach. **Playa Renaca** to the north is livelier and more popular with young crowds, with craft stalls and restaurants lining the road behind it. **Playa Cochoa**, sheltered by a small peninsula, is calmer and safer for swimming. **Playa Los Marineros** is good for sunbathing and fishing but has strong currents.
