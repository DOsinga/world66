The **Bici-Cafe, **in Bogota's historic center, offers bike tours and rentals, with bilingual guides and both beach cruisers and multi-speed bikes.

The Bici-Cafe is located in the center of the Historic Candelaria neighborhood, near many hotels and hostels. They have tours of the historic center, cultural and religious sites and the city parks, among others.

It also has the largest selection of used books in English, as well as some in French and German, in the neighborhood.

And it serves food including crepes, sandwiches and fruit juices.

Its website is: [www.bogotabiketours.com](/southamerica/colombia/bogota/activitiessports/sightseeing_tours)
