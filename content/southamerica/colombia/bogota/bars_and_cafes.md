---
title: "Bars and Cafes"
type: section
---

Bogota's nightlife scene splits between the upscale Zona Rosa around Carrera 11-13 and Calles 80-86, where you will find cocktail bars and clubs, and the grittier but more characterful spots in La Candelaria and Chapinero. Salsa bars are the real highlight -- even if you cannot dance, the energy is infectious. Take a taxi home at night, no matter where you are.
