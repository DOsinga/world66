For you meat eaters out there. This place serves the best meat in town (plus fish and chicken dishes for non red meat eaters)

Huge portions with lots of side dishes (included with the main dish)

Very nice amosphere (a nice colombian feel) and excellent service. The price is not cheap for local standards but still qute affordable in terms of dollars and definitively worth the $$.

they have two locations. one very close to 70th street and Carrera Septima (the addres must be somnething like Carrera 6 # 70-something)
