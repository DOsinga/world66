In my opinion, one of the best restaurants in the Zona G. Good selection and variety on the menu.

The presentation of the tomato soup was interesting. Made for a perfect starter on a cool Bogota evening whilst sitting at an outdoor table.

This is definitely one to go back to. You leave feeling full but not overly stuffed.
