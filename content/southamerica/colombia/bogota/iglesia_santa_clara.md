title: Iglesia Museo Santa Clara
type: poi

Now a museum, this 17th-century church has one of the most lavishly decorated interiors in Bogota. Every surface is covered with paintings, gilded altarpieces, and carved wood. The contrast between the plain exterior and the explosion of colour inside is striking.
