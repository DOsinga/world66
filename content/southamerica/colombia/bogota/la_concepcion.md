---
title: "Iglesia de la Concepcion"
type: poi
category: "Sight"
address: "Calle 10 No 9-50"
latitude: 4.5982
longitude: -74.0771
---

Home to Bogota's most impressive Mudejar vault -- a style imported from Spain that blends Islamic geometric patterns with Christian architecture. The church is often overlooked by visitors heading to the bigger sights nearby, which makes it a peaceful stop.
