type: poi

The world's largest collection of pre-Columbian gold, with over 30,000 pieces of gold work plus ceramics, textiles, and stone objects. The Gold Room on the top floor dims the lights and illuminates the centrepiece collection in stages -- it is genuinely breathtaking. Open Tuesday to Saturday 9am--6pm, Sunday 10am--4pm. Closed Mondays. Small admission fee.
