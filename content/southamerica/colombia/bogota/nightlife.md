title: "Nightlife and Entertainment"
type: section
---

There are many good bars and discos in Bogota. Most of them are located in the so-called Zona Rosa around Carerra 11/13, Calle 80/86. Other good places can be found at Carerra 5 with Calle 25. Although a rather safe area do not walk home, but take a taxi.
