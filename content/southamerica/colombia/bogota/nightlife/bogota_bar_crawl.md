So what's the idea..?!?
A
Bar Crawl (aka Pub Crawl or Bar Hopping) is a one night tour of several
bars and pubs generally located within walking distance of each other.
With the Bogota Bar Crawl you will experience 4 to 5 unique bars in one
night, never having to wait in line or pay cover, and receiving free
welcome shots and discounts at every stop!

You will get to
experience Bogota's crazy nightlife like never before, and our
experienced staff will show you the most popular and exciting spots in
the city EVERY saturday night!
