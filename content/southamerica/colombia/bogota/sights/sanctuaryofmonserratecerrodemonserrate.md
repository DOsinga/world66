Not a very old church but an important place of pilgrimage. The statue of the Senor Caido (Fallen Christ) is supposed to be responsible for a number of miracles. It is located outside of Bogota on the Monserrate mountain. Great views over the city! A cable car brings you on top for US$5 return trip. It is highly recommended to the trip on foot only with the crowds on Sunday, otherwise it is very likely to be robbed. **Justin :** This is the bets place to go if you want to have a good view over the city.
