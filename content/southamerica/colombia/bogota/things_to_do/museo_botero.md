Fernando Botero donated over 200 works to the Banco de la Republica -- his own voluptuous sculptures and paintings alongside pieces by Picasso, Monet, Renoir, and Dalí. The collection fills a beautifully restored colonial house in La Candelaria. Free admission. Open Monday to Saturday 9am--7pm, Sunday 10am--5pm. Closed Tuesdays.
