This is a place for middle and upper class Calenos and Calenas. They have live Spanish pop music from a very good house band. Call a day in advance to reserve a table for a group of friends. Drinks are bought by the bottle and the servers will bring buckets of ice, jugs of orange juice, etc. The place can get rather roudy with everybody singing along, and dancing on the seats or tables is common. This place is quite packed but a lot of fun with a group of people.
