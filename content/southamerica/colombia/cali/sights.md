Cali is not really known for its sights. But there are still a number of interesting spots to explore. 

**Sights**

**San Francisco Church**

The church and monastery of San Francisco has an impressive belltower. 

**Museo del Arte Moderno La Tertulia**

The museum is located a little bit out of the centre and has displays South American art. 

address: Avenida Colombia, 5-105 Oeste

openinghours: Tue-Sun, 9 am - 1 pm, 3 pm - 7 pm

**Church of San Antonio**

Nice views from the top.

address: Colina de San Antonio

**La Merced**

The oldest church of the city. It houses two museums: The museum of colonial art (Museo del Arte Colonial) and Archelogical museum (Museo Arqueologico) with precolumbian pottery. The museums are open from 8.30 - 12.30 and 13.30 - 17.30, Sunday closed.
