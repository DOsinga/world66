**Ideal Weather Conditions Year Round**
The geographical location of Cali blesses this city with a semi - tropical climate that varies between 14 to 35 degrees C throughout the year (57 to 97 F degrees). Therefore, any month of the year is a perfect time to visit Cali. The breezy afternoons of Cali are quite refreshing, especially on sunny days with
clear skies. When visiting Cali, it's important to remember that the
city is between 2,000 to 4,000 meters above sea level, so on your first
day of arrival to Cali, take some time to get used to the height and
avoid physical activities.**

The Rainy Seasons**

The 2 rainy seasons of Cali are between April and May, and October to
November. Try to avoid your visit during those
months, especially if you're planning to walk around the historic
downtown area or the zona rosa (the pink area, where most
modern restaurants, malls and bars are located). Rain is quite
unpredictable but you can be sure that if it starts to rain, it will do
so for a long time.

**La Feria de Cali**

Since 1957, every December 25th to 30th, the Cali Fair has taken place in Cali. This fair is also known as the Sugar Cane Fair or the Salsa Fair because you will see plenty of both.
Also available during the Cali Fair are bullfights, parades, rodeo
events, horseback riding events and live music concerts. If you're
planning to visit Cali during the December holidays, book your trip at
least 2 months in advance because hotel rooms go fast. Another
important event in Cali during December is the 10 kilometer race known
as the Carrera del Rio Cali.

**Important Colombian Holidays

**If you had to choose only 1 month to visit
Cali, then you should select June. June houses 3 long-weekends. The three holidays are Corpus
Christi Day, Sacred Heart Day, and Saints Paul and Peter's Day. There
will be a lot of religious themed parades around the city so
expect traffic too be hectic. The parades are colorful and are a great
opportunity to take some postcard quality photographs.
