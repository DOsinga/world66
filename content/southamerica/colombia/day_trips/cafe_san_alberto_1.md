OVERVIEW

If you are looking to find premium Colombian coffee experience then look no further. We know you will enjoy this comprehensive coffee tour and tasting experience at Cafe San Alberto in beautiful Buenavista (translated “good view”), Quindio Colombia. Tour the beautiful Hacienda San Alberto farm and learn all about the coffee process from start to finish. After your tour of the coffee nursery, coffee fields and processing plant, you will participate in a “Sensorial experience” to awaken your senses so that you may fully understand and enjoy the complex art of coffee tasting and quality selection.

In 1972, Gustavo Monroy Leyva bought the farm, Hacienda La Alsacia, renamed it Hacienda San Alberto, in honor of his son Gustavo Alberto, who died in a plane crash. In 1996 the owners began renovations with a new vision for environmental responsibility. In 2006 Hacienda San Alberto was awarded an Utz certification in honor of their environmentally and socially responsible practices. Hacienda San Alberto proudly employs single mothers from Buena Vista to complete the hand selection portion of the quintuple selection process, contributing to the stability of local economy.

The Terraza opened in 2009 to welcome locals and visitors alike to enjoy premium coffee and views of the breathtaking landscape of Buenavista as all as in-depth educational coffee tours and tasting experiences.

HIGHLIGHTS

Enjoy a comprehensive 2.5 to 3 hour tour of the farm and the entire coffee process with a knowledgable and friendly English-speaking guide. Savor delicious premium high mountain coffee which has undergone the rigorous quintuple selection process and learn all about what makes it so tasty. Participate in a comprehensive “sensory experience” and learn all about the art of coffee selection and coffee quality control.
