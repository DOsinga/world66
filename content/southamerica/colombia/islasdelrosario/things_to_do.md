---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

The main draw at Islas del Rosario is underwater. Snorkelling and scuba diving on the coral reefs are the primary activities, with visibility often exceeding 20 metres. Boat tours typically stop at several reef sites and include lunch on one of the islands. On the surface, the beaches on the smaller islands are quiet and beautiful — the kind that feel genuinely remote despite the short boat ride from Cartagena.
