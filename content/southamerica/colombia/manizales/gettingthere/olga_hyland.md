Manizales airport connects the city with many other major cities in Colombia, and the flight from Bogota is only 40 minutes long. The new Autopista del Cafe, connects the city with [Pereira](/southamerica/colombia/pereira) and [Armenia](/europe/armenia) to the south in a two hour journey. To the north you are only two and a half hours from Medellin.
