---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

The best things to do in Medellin are the ones that show you how much the city has changed. Ride the Metrocable over rooftops that were no-go zones thirty years ago. Walk Comuna 13's outdoor escalators with a local guide who remembers when they were built. Stand in Plaza Botero among sixty tons of bronze donated by a painter who grew up a few blocks away.
