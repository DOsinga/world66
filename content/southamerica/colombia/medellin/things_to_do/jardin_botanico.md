Fourteen hectares of garden in the heart of the city, with a striking wooden-latticed Orquideorama shading an open-air orchid collection. Free to enter, a good place to hide from the midday sun. The Parque Explora science museum and the University of Antioquia are next door, all reachable on foot from the Universidad metro stop.
