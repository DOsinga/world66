Palmira is a mid-sized city in the Valle del Cauca, just 25 km south of [Cali](/southamerica/colombia/cali) and easily reached by frequent buses. Known as the agricultural capital of Colombia, it sits in the heart of sugar cane country — the flat, fertile valley floor stretches out to the Andes on both sides.

The city is pleasant and walkable, with a lively central plaza and the Parque del Azúcar reflecting its sugar heritage. Most travellers pass through on the way to or from Cali, but the Botanical Garden of Cali (which is actually in Palmira) is worth a stop, with an excellent collection of tropical plants and orchids.
