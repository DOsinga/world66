The Parque Nacional de Los Nevados is home to the huge Volcano of Nevado Ruiz.


 To get to the park the best is to head for [Manizales](/southamerica/colombia/manizales) first and enter the park form there. A four-wheel-drive rental vehicle is a good idea for exploring the park as is a guide: Colombians are getting serious about protecting what is left of their environment and it is difficult to acquire camping permits from the park entrance station without hiring a local escort.

The park is unique in its kind. The high altitude volcano vegetation, the lakes and the active volcanoes are absoluteley worth visiting.
