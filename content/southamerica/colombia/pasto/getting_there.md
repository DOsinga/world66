There are daily flight to [Cali](/southamerica/colombia/cali) and [Bogota](/southamerica/colombia/bogota) from the airport, which is located some 30 km north of the city on the road to Cali.

 The bus terminal is just south of town. Nine hours to [Cali](/southamerica/colombia/cali), six to [Popayan](/southamerica/colombia/popayan). There are daily departures for [Bogota](/southamerica/colombia/bogota): a long trip of 22 hours.
