Pereira is the center of the traditional Coffee Grown Zone (Zona Cafetera), 1600 meters high and 26-31ºC. It's between Armenia and Manizales. You can enjoy many places near Pereira: Parque de los Nevados, Valle de Cocora, Laguna del Otun. You can arrive by car from Cali or Medellin (3 hours more or less) or by plane from Bogotá or medellín (45").
