---
title: "Bars and Cafes in Pereira"
type: section
---

Although it's not the largest city in the area, there are still many **Pereira Bars and Cafes** that you can visit. Where you will want to go while in town will depend on what you are in the mood for, because some places will be able to cater to your desires better than others. For example, if you are looking for something to eat you can stop in at Sayonara. For a beer, you're better off going to Bristol.

**Bristol**

If you are looking for a nice cozy place to have a beer, especially on a hot summer night, Bristol is the place to go. One of the best things about going to Bristol is the homemade beer that is available for patrons. You can also sit back and listen to music or chat it up with the locals.

**La Tienda de Olmedo
**

If you are talking to the locals and they are not sure about the bar you are referring to, you should ask them about La Tienda de Olmedo. If you are looking for a bar that offers you friendly service in a quaint atmosphere, this is the place to go. Inside the small bar you will find hundreds of records to choose from, but most of them are tangos with a few Spanish bands thrown in.

**Mama Flor**

If you are looking for a place to eat that specializes in local dishes, Mama Flor is a great place to try. Not only can you grab a bite to eat, but you can also sit out on the balcony and enjoy a cool drink under the night stars. You must try the Sopa de Guineo, which is a plantain that is served with rice, ground beef and avocado.

**Delight**

This restaurant opens during the lunch hour, but has some great dinner entrees to choose from. Upon walking into the caf&eacute; you will see all of their dishes on display. One thing that you need to watch out for is that the meals are very heavy, but they taste amazing.****

**Mango Biche**

If you are looking for a place that allows you to dance the night away while enjoying a few drinks, Mango Biche is the place for you. Mango Biche is in the La Popa area where most of the other dance clubs and bars are located, which makes it easy to get from one to the other as the night goes on.
