---
title: "Eating Out"
type: section
---

Pereira's food scene leans toward hearty Colombian staples: bandeja paisa, sancocho, and freshly roasted chicken. You will find most restaurants along Carrera 7 and Carrera 8 in the city center. For something more upscale, the area around Avenida Circunvalar has a cluster of international and grill restaurants.
