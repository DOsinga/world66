---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment in Pereira"
type: section
---

**Pereira**** nightlife** boasts of many bars and clubs in and around the city. This makes it easy for tourists to find the right places to spend their evenings in Pereira. Here are some of the well known bars and clubs tourists can visit:

**Tantra Bar**

Located at Prometheus, this is definitely one of the most popular bars in the city. The bar has a mix of Eastern and Asian d&eacute;cor and takes its inspiration from the best Buddha Bar. The place offers a wide selection of alcohol and local dishes, but it is the music that makes it a stand-out bar. The mixed harmonies of world and lounge music is very soothing. From Tuesday to Saturday, 6 p.m. onwards, Tantra Bar is a bar-restaurant. But on Fridays and Saturdays after 10 p.m. the place turns into a disco where visitors can dance the night away.

**Mango Biche**

This is one of the most popular clubs in Pereira, where locals and tourists dance through the night. Mango Biche is situated in the Menga area on the old Cali road. The club usually starts to get full at about 11 p.m., but the partying doesn&rsquo;t end till early morning. This is a perfect place for drinking and dancing. The club is especially popular for the music it plays. This includes a different kind of rumba, which is a delight for locals and tourists.

**Bristol Bar**

If dancing isn&rsquo;t your thing and you would rather spend your evening in a quiet way, visit the Bristol Bar. Located at Carrera 7, this bar is close to Parque de Bolivar. Bristol Bar is a great place to hangout if you are a beer drinker. This beer house is located in a colonial building, which gives the place an old casual feeling. The bar also serves other kinds of alcohol and a good range of dishes. The place is perfect for those who don&rsquo;t want to spend too much on their nights out in the city.

**El Rincon Clasico**

Tourists who are on a budget and are looking for a less expensive place to spend their evenings in Pereira should try El Rincon Clasico. This is a relatively small bar compared to the others that cater to tourists in the city. It is located at Carrera 2, near the city&rsquo;s downtown area. The bar has numerous vinyl records and most of the music played here is Spanish and tango. El Rincon Clasico is also called La Tienda de Olmedo and is an ideal place to visit if you are looking for a local experience.

Besides these popular ones, there are a number of other clubs and bars tourists can choose from. Most of these are located close to the city center.
