There are many good bars and discos in Pereira. Most of them are located in the so-called Pink Place (Zona Rosa) around Variante Turín - La Popa, more of 50 discotecs, pubs and nigth clubs. Other good places can be found at center of the city. Although a rather safe area do not walk home, but take a taxi.
