title: Olaya Herrera
type: poi

A hilltop park offering a full 360-degree panorama over Pereira and the surrounding coffee valleys. To the north, east, and south you see the city against the western slope of the Cordillera Central. To the west, the coffee plantations of La Virginia and Santa Rosa de Cabal stretch toward the horizon.
