type: poi

latitude: 4.8138

Near Matecana airport, this is the largest zoo in Colombia with around 800 animals across 150 species from the Americas, Africa, and Asia. It also includes a botanical garden with native and endemic flora and a small natural history museum. A good half-day outing, especially with children.
