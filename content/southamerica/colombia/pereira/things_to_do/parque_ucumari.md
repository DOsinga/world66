---
title: "Santuario de Fauna y Flora Otun Quimbaya"
type: poi
category: Sight
latitude: 4.7350
longitude: -75.5750
---

About 30 km southeast of the city, this 42-square-kilometer reserve protects Andean cloud forest within the agricultural zone. The nature trails wind through dense forest past waterfalls, and birders come for the more than 180 recorded species including several endemic hummingbirds.
