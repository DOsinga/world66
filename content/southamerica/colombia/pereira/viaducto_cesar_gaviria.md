title: Viaducto Cesar Gaviria Trujillo
type: poi
---

One of South America's longest cable-stayed bridges, completed in 1997 and named after Colombia's 40th president, who was born in Pereira. The bridge connects Pereira with neighboring Dosquebradas and is an impressive piece of engineering best appreciated from the valley below.
