title: Humilladero Bridge
type: poi
---

A handsome colonial-era brick bridge spanning the Rio Molino, built in the 18th century to connect the city centre to the eastern neighbourhoods. It is one of Popayan's most recognisable landmarks and a pleasant spot for a stroll.
