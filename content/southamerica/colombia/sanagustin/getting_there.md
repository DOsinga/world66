---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

About five buses go to [Popayan](/southamerica/colombia/popayan) and back each day. The road through Isnos is very spectacular but can be pretty rough on your stomach. The trip takes up to 8 hours.

 Coomotor has direct buses all the way to [Bogota](/southamerica/colombia/bogota), that take about 12 hours.

 If you want to continue to Terradientro you need to take the bus to La Plata and change there. The whole trip can take a good 8 hours. Accommodation is available in La Plata.
