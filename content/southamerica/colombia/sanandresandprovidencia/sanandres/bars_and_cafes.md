**A Night to Remember**

**San Andres bars and caf&eacute;s** contribute to the famous nightlife and party scenes in the island. San Andres is located at a distance of 720 km from the coast of Colombia. It is part of a chain of islands and is known for its crystal clear beaches. This island is also a famous honeymoon destination. San Andres has several bars, cafes and shacks which offer visitors plenty of entertainment.

#### **Wet**

Wet is one of the highly sought-after bars in San Andres. It is located in the upscale area in the Colombia Avenue. The indoor seating area is cozy, and the interiors are plush and classy. Leather cushions and low seating make it a great place to lounge. The outdoor area faces the beach and offers a breathtaking view during sunset. The bar is open from 11:00 a.m. to past midnight. The in-house DJ plays incredible music, and the dance floor gets crowded after 10:00 p.m.

The bar is right next to the dance floor, and the bar-side seating is highly entertaining. You can enjoy the action on the dance floor and get quick and easy access to your drinks. The bar stocks alcohol from around the world. There are more than 100 varieties of beer. The margaritas are a must try as are the mojitos and the island ice tea. The finger food compliments the drinks. You can also take a break from the music and crowds and walk to the beach from the bar.
