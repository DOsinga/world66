---
title: "Sights"
type: section
---

The biggest tourist attraction in Santa Rosa de Cabal is the hot springs, located 9 Km from the city. The resort infrastructure is not quite developed, but remains very popular and a very attractive place to visit. Perhaps the only inconvenience is the Hotel at the end of the road; prices were hefty despite the low-season and the accommodations are limited for couples and singles. This old style hotel is more appropriate for families or groups of five or more; the singles or couples are restricted to the old facilities, lacking individual bathrooms and other essential modern amenities. It's unfortunate that such a great location is not administered with a more pragmatic philosophy.
