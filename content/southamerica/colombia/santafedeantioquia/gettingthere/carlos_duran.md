From Medellin, take the metro towards Niquia and get off at the Estacion Caribe cross the pedestrian bridge to the (Terminal del Norte).
Go to the first floor and look for Transportes Gómez Hernández o Transportes Urabá (both are bus lines). Take the bus going to Santa Fe de Antioquia or towards Urabá,
The ticket is less than 8 USD.
Upon arrival to Santa Fe de Antioquia, you will be at the Terminal de transporte de Santa Fe de Antioquia.
