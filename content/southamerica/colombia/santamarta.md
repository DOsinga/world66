You shouldn’t miss Santa Marta. There are so many reasons to come here: to relax, because the area has great beaches, to visit the oldest town of Colombia (founded in 1525!) or to explore the ancient culture of the Tayrones, who lived in the area before the Spanish came. The Ciudad Perdida or lost city of the Tayrones can be visited in a 5 day trek through a national park. This will be an experience you will never forget. 



Santa Marta is also a very lively place with good nightlife. The clubs near the beach attract a colourful crowd.
