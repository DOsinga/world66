Located two kilometres offshore from El Rodadero beach, reached by motorboat. The aquarium has a variety of marine species and a dolphin show; the attached museum displays nautical equipment and Inca ceramics. The boat ride out is part of the fun. Open daily 9:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m.
