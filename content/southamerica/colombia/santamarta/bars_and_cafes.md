---
title: "Bars and Cafes"
type: section
---

Santa Marta's nightlife centres on Calles 17 and 18 between Carreras 2 and 4, where clubs and bars line both sides of the street. The scene leans local -- vallenato, champeta, and reggaeton dominate -- but you will find places playing rock and international music too. Things get going late, especially on weekends.
