**Rock n&rsquo; Roll Party at A Santa Marta Bar**

Spending a vivacious night in one of **Santa Marta bars and cafes** is the hippest thing to do in your vacation to this Colombian municipality. Santa Marta is also known as the Pearl of America, where tourism is one of the important economies aside from its maritime port. Santa Marta is more noted for its beaches with beautiful sunsets happening on the Caribbean Sea.

**Crab&rsquo;s Bar**

Crab&rsquo;s Bar is located at Calle del Pozo Calle, in the heart of the cultural and tourism district of Santa Maria. The bar has been operating since 1999 and widely popular as the center of rock, arts and blues. Crab&rsquo;s Bar is an iconic bar in Colombia for offering its customers, original entertainment with a local cultural flavor to it. Winning in the Bogota rock scene, further cemented Crab&rsquo;s Bar reputation as the hippest rock 'n roll bar in town. Crab&rsquo;s Bar is actually situated in a colonial house, preserving the original outside structure of the house and ostentatiously painted red to connote the color of a crab.

**Crab&rsquo;s Bar Entertainment**

Come to Crab&rsquo;s Bar for a dousing round of rock n&rsquo; roll music while drinking beer. Just a side note, the owner also VJ&rsquo;s videos of all great classic rock bands. The bar only serves local beer brands such as Club Colombia. You can also partake in a game of darts inside the bar. And, if you&rsquo;re not the shy type, get up on the stage and sing along to your favorite music.
