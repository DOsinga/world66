An institution in Santa Marta's historic centre, Crab's Bar has been the city's rock and blues hub since 1999. The bar occupies a colonial house painted bright red, and the owner VJs classic rock videos while the crowd drinks Club Colombia beer. There is a dart board and an open stage if you want to sing along. Not fancy, but full of character.
