---
title: "Beaches"
type: section
---

Santa Marta offers great snorkeling and diving but some may prefer El Rodadero a nearby resort area that also has a great beach and even an aquarium. Big-game fishing is excellent and at certain times of the year tarpon are plentiful in the Manzanares River. 







About two hours away is the town of Cienaga Grande (on the Magdalena River) where those who enjoy fishing will be in heaven.
