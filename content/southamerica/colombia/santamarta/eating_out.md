title: "Eating Out in Santa marta"
type: section
---

There are several **Santa Marta Restaurants** that offers visitors the chance to enjoy the unique cuisine of this city. You can also choose from a wide range of local and international cuisines like Asian, Continental, Mediterranean and Fusion. Santa Marta’s cuisine has been influenced by different nations, which is one of the many reasons to try out the local restaurants here in the city. If you want to taste the perfect mixture and fusion of Santa Marta’s cuisine, step into one these famous restaurants.

**Burukuka**

This restaurant offers a wonderful fusion of international and traditional cuisine. You can choose to eat outside the restaurant and enjoy a majestic view of the Caribbean Sea and surrounding mountain ranges. The gentle sea breeze will work up an appetite that will only enhance your meal here at Burukuka. The restaurant serves some mouthwatering dishes. Many people enjoy the sweet plantains with cream cheese topped with chiccarrone, and Memphis-style baby back ribs.

**Pizza Vomito**

The Pizza Vomito is another must-visit gastronomical hot spot, found in Rodadero, Santa Marta. This restaurant is one of the most popular fast food places in Santa Marta. Pizza Vomito serves traditional Columbian snacks such as hotdogs with cheese, Salchipapas, exotic pizzas, and soups. The restaurant is open every day from 9:00 a.m. to 8:00 p.m., and is a great place to stop by if you're looking for a quick bite.

**Donde Chucho**

If you love seafood, especially shrimp, you must step into Donde Chucho. This is one of the finest seafood restaurants in Santa Marta. This restaurant serves Columbian dishes as well as Old Italian delicacies. The menu includes pasta, antipasto, pizza, risotto, and a wide selection of seafood. Donde Chucho is located right in the center of Santa Marta, just two blocks from the city’s bay. This is a great place to enjoy a family lunch or dinner while vacationing in Santa Marta.

**Gran Manuel**

Gran Manuel is a very popular restaurant in Santa Marta. Situated on the beachfront in Santa Marta, Gran Manuel has a wide selection of seafood dishes, and serves the freshest seafood available. Gran Manuel is popular for its delicious yet reasonably priced dishes and its wide selection of fine wines. The restaurant is perfect if you want to enjoy seafood here in Santa Marta.

**Ben & Josep's**

Ben & Josep's is another popular fusion restaurant in Santa Marta. This restaurant serves local and international cuisine--with a twist. One of the most popular dishes here at the restaurant is the baby beef served with various sauces. The restaurant also has a wide selection of local and international wines.
