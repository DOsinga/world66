The hacienda where Simón Bolívar spent his final days in 1830, now a museum and memorial. The grounds include the original sugarcane estate, a botanical garden, and the Museo Bolivariano with art donated by the countries Bolívar helped liberate. A peaceful place with genuine historical weight. Open daily 9:30–16:30.
