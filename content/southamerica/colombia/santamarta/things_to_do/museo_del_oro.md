Housed in the Casa de la Aduana, a fine colonial mansion on the plaza, the Gold Museum displays Tayrona pottery, gold work, and an impressive scale model of Ciudad Perdida. A good introduction to the indigenous cultures of the Sierra Nevada before heading out to see the real thing. Open Monday to Friday, 8:00-11:45 a.m. and 2:00-4:45 p.m. Free admission.
