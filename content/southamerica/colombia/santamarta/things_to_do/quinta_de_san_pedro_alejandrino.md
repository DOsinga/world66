The hacienda where Simon Bolivar spent his final days in 1830, now a museum and memorial. The grounds include the original sugarcane estate, a botanical garden, and the Museo Bolivariano with art donated by the countries Bolivar helped liberate. A peaceful place with genuine historical weight. Open daily 9:30 a.m. to 4:30 p.m.
