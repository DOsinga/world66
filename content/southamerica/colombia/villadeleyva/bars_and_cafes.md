here are several **Villa**** de Leyva bars and cafés**
that offer delectable food, good coffee, great drinks and superb
entertainment in this beautiful colonial town.

**Pastelería Francesa**

Pastelería Francesa is located two blocks away from the town’s main
square. This is a small, old-style French bakery and café that serves
authentic French pastries, breads, coffee and hot chocolate. The menu
also includes tarts, baguettes, quiches, white and whole wheat bread,
cakes and other homemade treats. While at Pastelería Francesa some of
the treats you must try are the pain au chocolat, croissant de
almendras, feijoa tart, coffee and hot choco.

**El Cortijo **

El Cortijo is a famous hot spot among the locals of Villa de Leyva.
It is a simple restaurant-bar with a laid back and rustic atmosphere,
perfect for casual dining or chilling out with friends. El Cortijo is
famous for the sumptuous and mouth-watering grilled meats. This
bar/grill and restaurant also serves ice cold beers, wines and
cocktails. It features a live band that performs every night.

**Al Horno**

This is a vibrant little café which serves a great selection of local
and international cuisines. Al Horno is artistically decorated and has a
colorful vibe and bubbly atmosphere. The restaurant menu includes 12
different kinds of pizzas, pasta, sandwiches, burgers, crepes, desserts,
arepas, different coffee mixes and hot chocolate. Al Horno offers a
wide selection of good wines and if ever you fall for the artworks
hanging by the cafe's walls, you're welcome to buy it.

**Café Plaza**

Café Plaza is a nice and warm café situated in the southern part of
the Plaza Mayor. Here you can find delicious pastries and cakes, as well
as local delicacies, coffee, alcoholic beverages and unique warm fruit
drinks. The café includes wireless Internet connection and IDD-capable telephones
(International Direct Dial), so you can get
connected with the outside world while sipping on your favorite cup of
coffee.

**La Terraza **

Another popular hot spot in Villa de Leyva is the La Terraza Bar and
Restaurant. It is located in the northern part of Plaza Mayor and it
serves International/Western and local dishes. This restaurant serves
fresh seafood, pizzas, pastas and ceviches. La Terraza serves lunch but
the best time to eat here is at dusk, because you can see the sun setting in the south. During the evening, La Terraza is transformed into
a full bar and entertainment area.
