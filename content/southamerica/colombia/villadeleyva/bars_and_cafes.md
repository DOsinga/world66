---
title: "Bars and Cafes"
type: section
---

The Plaza Mayor and the streets around it are where Villa de Leyva's cafe life concentrates. Pastelería Francesa, two blocks from the square, serves proper French pastries and coffee in an old-style setting. Café Plaza, on the southern edge of the Plaza Mayor, has local pastries and wifi. La Terraza, on the northern side, is best at dusk when you can watch the sun go down while the kitchen produces ceviche and pasta; it converts to a full bar in the evening. El Cortijo is the locals' choice for grilled meats and cold beer, with live music nightly.
