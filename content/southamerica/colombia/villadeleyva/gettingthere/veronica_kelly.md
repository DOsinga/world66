Well it takes about 2 hours 30 minutes to get there, and you can
take a taxi, as the fares are quite cheap, or you can take a coach. The
best type of coach to take is called the 'Libertadores'. But this will
take you to a town called Tunja where you can take a smaller bus to get
to villa de leyva.

There are also two direct ¨Libertadores¨, not via Tunja, buses a day from Bogotá.
