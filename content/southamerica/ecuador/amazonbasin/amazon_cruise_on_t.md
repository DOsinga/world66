A riverboat cruise through Ecuador's Amazon basin aboard the Manatee Amazon Explorer. The vessel is purpose-built for jungle cruises with modern amenities, and the route winds through tributaries of the Napo River into primary rainforest. Expect guided jungle walks, canoe trips through flooded forest, visits to indigenous communities, and birdwatching from observation decks. Multi-day itineraries are standard; book through operators in Quito.
