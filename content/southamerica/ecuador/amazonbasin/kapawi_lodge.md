A locally run tour operation based in Coca (Puerto Francisco de Orellana), operating since 1971. The guides are native to the region with deep knowledge of the Amazon's geography, flora, and fauna. Tours include jungle walks, dugout canoe trips, and canopy tower visits along the Napo River and its tributaries. Particularly strong on birdwatching. A good choice for travellers who want an authentic, locally guided experience rather than a luxury lodge operation.
