Spread across an expansive stretch of fine gray sand, Atacames is a
beachside playground for Ecuadorians. Foreigners are more the exception
than the norm. Part hustler, part street-side vendor and welcoming
hotel owner, Atacames is a town with many faces, which seem to change
as the sun moves across the sky. To read more about Atacames visit
http://www.vivatravelguides.com/south-america/ecuador/the-northern-coast-and-lowlands/esmeraldas/atacames
