Inside the Basilica is an entertaining collection of murals which describe local miracles attributed to the Virgin of the Holy Waters, the town's spiritual protector. In October, when the town holds an annual celebration in her honor, the streets burst alive with music, dancing and, to the Virgin's chagrin, much drinking.
