title: "Basilica de la Virgen del Agua Santa"

latitude: -1.3965

---

Inside the basilica is an entertaining collection of murals describing local miracles attributed to the Virgen del Agua Santa (Virgin of the Holy Waters), the town's spiritual protector. In October, when the town holds an annual celebration in her honour, the streets come alive with music, dancing and — to the Virgin's chagrin — much drinking.
