Playas is a local beach that is less crowded then Salinas, Montanita or Esmeraldas. It is 25 miles from Guayaquil so it makes for a wonderful day trip. There are taxis and buses going there, or you can rent a car and take the North route out of town. Hotel rooms to put your things in right across from the beach for $20-$25 for the day. The local cevicherias are clean and tasty serving some of the best ceviches in the country (As the locals will tell you.) All of them are numbered so you can take your pick from the waiters and waitresses waving you in to eat at their establishment. The waves are just big enough to jump in unlike the more tranquile Salinas. This is beach is not appropriate for families with small children as depending on the time of year, the rip tide can grow exceptionally powerful. Use your discresion.
