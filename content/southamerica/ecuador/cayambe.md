Cayambe is a medium-sized Andean city, located at the foot of the
volcano of the same name. Most visitors to Ecuador will get to see it,
as it is squarely on the route between Quito and Otavalo, although
there is little reason for tourists to stop there. To read more about Cayambe, visit http://www.vivatravelguides.com/south-america/ecuador/andean-highlands/northern-andes/cayambe
