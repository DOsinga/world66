---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

Most visitors fly from Quito — the flight takes about 30 minutes. Airlines serving the route change frequently, so check current schedules. Flights are also available from Guayaquil and Cuenca, usually with a stop in Quito.

The bus from Quito to Coca takes around eight hours. The views climbing from the rainforest up into the cordillera are spectacular, but the journey is long and can be cold at night. Expect to pass through several military checkpoints along the way where passports will be checked.
