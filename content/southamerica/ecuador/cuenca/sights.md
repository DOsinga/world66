**Catedral de la Immaculada **

This cathedral was built in 1885 and was intended to be the largest in South America. However, a slight miscalculation meant that two main towers at the front of the building could not support the envisaged domes. **El Sagrario **

El Sagrario (or Old Cathedral), is located on Plaza Abdon Calderon and dates from 1557 when the present Cuenca was founded.
