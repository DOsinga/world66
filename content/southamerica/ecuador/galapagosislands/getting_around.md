EMETEBE operates an avioneta (10 passenger) service between the islands of San Cristobal, Santa Cruz and Isabela. Depending on demand, there are daily flights. You may also want to inquire about special offers which include overnight stays in hotels.

The main office of EMETEBE is located in Baquerizo Moreno, San Cristobal Island. Telephone No. (593) 05 520 036 (for international calls omit the 0 in front of the 5.)
