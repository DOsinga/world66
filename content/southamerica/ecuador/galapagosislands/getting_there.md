The airline TAME has two daily flights to the island of Baltra, from where a short bus ride, a brief boat ride and another almost one hour ride by bus take you to the village of Puerto Ayora on Santa Cruz island. This is the major tourist spot on the Galapagos Islands. If you haven't already booked a tour on the continent you can do it here at any of the many agencies, and maybe obtain a major discount. 

TAME offers two daily flights to Baltra, Galapagos. 

TAME also flies to the island of San Cristobal twice a week. Although the airport is a 5 minute bus ride from the village of Baquerizo Moreno, it has less traffic, because most of the cruise agencies are located on Santa Cruz island.
