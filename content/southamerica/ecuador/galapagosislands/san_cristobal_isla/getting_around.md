---
title: "Getting Around"
type: section
---

Puerto Baquerizo Moreno is small enough to walk. The main pier, Interpretation Center, waterfront plaza, and most restaurants and dive operators are all within 15 minutes on foot.

For sites outside town — the Galapaguera tortoise reserve, Puerto Chino beach, El Junco lagoon — you will need a taxi or a guided day tour. Taxis are cheap and plentiful; agree on a price before you get in. Rentable bicycles are available for the road to the highlands if you prefer to go independently, but the terrain is hilly.

Most visitor sites around the island (Kicker Rock, Punta Pitt, Isla Lobos) are only accessible by boat. Local tour operators on the waterfront run daily trips; prices are similar across operators and negotiating is accepted, especially for small groups.
