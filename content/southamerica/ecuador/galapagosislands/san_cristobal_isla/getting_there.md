---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

San Cristóbal's airport (SCY) receives daily flights from Quito and Guayaquil operated by LATAM and Avianca. Flight time from Guayaquil is around 1 hour 45 minutes; from Quito, about 2 hours 30 minutes with a stop. The airport is a 10-minute taxi ride from Puerto Baquerizo Moreno.

All visitors to the Galápagos must pay a National Park entrance fee on arrival ($200 for foreign adults as of 2024). You will also need to show proof of onward travel and complete a biosecurity inspection — all food and plant material may be confiscated. Flights into San Cristóbal are often cheaper than flights to Baltra (the main Santa Cruz gateway), making it an economical entry point.

Inter-island ferries connect Puerto Baquerizo Moreno with Puerto Ayora (Santa Cruz) and Puerto Villamil (Isabela). The crossing to Santa Cruz takes about 2.5 hours and can be rough; motion sickness tablets are worth having. Depart from the main pier in Puerto Baquerizo Moreno.
