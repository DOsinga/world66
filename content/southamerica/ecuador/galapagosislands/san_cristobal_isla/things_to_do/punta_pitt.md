Punta Pitt, at the northeastern tip of San Cristóbal, is the only place in the Galápagos where you can see all three species of booby — blue-footed, red-footed, and Nazca — nesting side by side. The red-footed boobies are particularly striking; their feet are exactly the colour you'd hope they'd be.

The site is reached by boat (around 2 hours from Puerto Baquerizo Moreno) and involves a wet landing followed by a moderately strenuous hike up a steep trail. The views from the top are excellent, and sea lions haul out on the beach at the base. Most operators combine Punta Pitt with a stop at Isla Lobos or Kicker Rock on the same day trip.
