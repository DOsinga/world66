**BALTRA - ****Size: **unknown / Max altitude: 15 m / Geography: Basaltic plateau, tectonic uplifting, and dry. 

**Fact(s): **Main airport (built during WWII by Americans). Mostly just visited for landing and beginning tour. 

**BARTOLOME - ****Size: **1.2 sq. kms**** / Max altitude: 114 m**** / Geography:** **Black volcanic sand, golden sand coves, tuff cones, spatter cones, scoria formations. Sights: Pinnacle Rock (70 m)**** 

**Fact(s):** **Landing: **Dry (hike) wet (beach)**** / *Flora***: **Salt bush, Morning glory, Prickly pear cactus, Red & White mangroves. *Fauna:* Galapagos Penguin, Great blue heron. *Sea life:* Spotted eagle rays, sea turtles (nesting Jan-March), sea lion colony, sharks. ***Activities:* **Short hike to** Pinnacle Rock** (70 m) with a view of 5 Islands, snorkelling
