An international restaurant on the main strip of Urdesa, one of Guayaquil's leafier and more walkable neighbourhoods. Blu serves a mix of Mediterranean and Latin American-influenced dishes in a contemporary setting. V.E. Estrada Avenue is lined with restaurants and bars, so if Blu doesn't suit your mood, you can easily wander the block and find something else.
