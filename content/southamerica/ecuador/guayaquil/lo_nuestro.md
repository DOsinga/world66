Lo Nuestro serves traditional Ecuadorian cuisine in the Urdesa neighbourhood, one of Guayaquil's most pleasant residential districts. The dining room is decorated in a nostalgic style with old photographs and antique furnishings that evoke early twentieth-century coastal Ecuador.

The menu covers classic coastal dishes — encebollado, ceviche, seco de chivo, and arroz con menestra — prepared with care and generous portions. It's a reliable choice for visitors wanting to try well-executed local cooking in comfortable surroundings. Popular for weekend lunches with families.
