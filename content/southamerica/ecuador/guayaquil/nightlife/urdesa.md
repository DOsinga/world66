The Urdesa sector, a high-middle class neighborhood where you can find restaurants, bars and discos of different prices and tastes, is a very recommended place for travellers to visit . The main strip here is Av. Victor Emilio Estrada, very crowded at all hours where fast food restaurants such as Burger King, Pizza Hut, Dominos are located, as well as other local restaurants such as La Tablita where you can have a nice piece of roast beef or a BBQ rib, potatos and a drink for $6 -$8, or Pollos Gus where you can get a 1/4 broasted chicken for $4, or Mariscos Azul where a nice cebiche (seafood cocktail) is found for a little more than $8 (of course fancier restaurants are also an option, serving dinner for $15 - $50 per person). The typical "carreta" (a small wagon) serves hamburgers, hot dogs or tacos for $2, among which is the vary famous carreta "El Capi", serving the same kind of but better quality food for an extra buck (they open until late at night, so after visiting one of the clubs in the sector you can go visit them). On this street there are also the bars El Manantial and Chapus, where you can have a beer for $2 or $3, while listening to tropical music. If you prefer dancing and/or clubbing, you can find in this same sector, at Av. Las Monjas, the Shopping Center "Aventura Plaza" (a shopping square with a big Iguana sculpture, offering free parking and security at no cost) which features sport and karaoke bars, small discos and a club (some good restaurants as well). The name of the club is Bloom and opens from 20:00 - 04:00 (Thursday to Saturday only). It is a mid-size club for 250 people aprox. with a dance floor, variety of music and drinks, very popular among people from 20 to 40 y.o. The entrance fee is $20 consumable but if you are an International traveller you can enter for free and pay as you drink showing your passport or International ID. The costs ranges from $3 to $5 a beer, and $6 to $10 a glass of rum, vodka, whisky or a cocktail, or a bottle starting from $75 . If you prefer reserving a table or need further information you can email bloom.barlounge@gmail.com - It's higly recommended at your departure of the place asking the host to get you a taxi and avoiding taking one at the street.
