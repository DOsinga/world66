---
title: "Eating Out"
type: section
---

Loja's dining scene is unpretentious and affordable. The central square has a tourist map marking several good options, and you will find simple restaurants serving Ecuadorian staples — rice, beans, grilled meat, and fresh fruit juices — throughout the centre. Vegetarian options are easier to find here than in many Ecuadorian cities, thanks to the university crowd.
