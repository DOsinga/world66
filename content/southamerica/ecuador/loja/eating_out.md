The tourist map you can get on the central square contains a few good places to eat. Besides that Cafe Restaurant Diego´s offers a good service.

### [Cafeteria Restaurant Diego´s](/southamerica/ecuador/loja/eating_out/cafeteria_restaura)

It´s a restaurant at the first floor of a colonial building. The kitchen is simple but tasty, with plenty of vegetarian alternatives. The main reason to visit is the tranquil and beautiful setting of the place, overlooking a garden. An excellent place for dinner or a late night beer. With a little restyling it could be a great place. Just a few *cuadra*´s from the central *parque*.

Fun detail: the bathroom takes it name litteraly: toilet, shower and bath.
