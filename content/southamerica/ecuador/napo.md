Travel Guide

The Napo Province hostssome breathtaking cloud forests, páramo
(grasslands), and one of Ecuador's most beautiful snow-capped
volcanoes, Antisana. It is also one of the easiest points to visit the
Amazon Basin from Quito.
To read more about Napo, visit http://www.vivatravelguides.com/south-america/ecuador/amazon-basin/napo
