Hello! I was in Ecuador at the begging of the year and am planning to go back there again in November. While I was in Ecuador, my base for all the trips was Quito, of course and while I was there, I stayed at this lovely small hotel called Cayman Hotel. I think this is the best choice for someone looking to know Quito and it´s people. It has a great location, but what I liked the most it was the family atmosphere that I got there. After a while everyone felt like home, I met other guests watching TV next to the fireplace in the common room. The staff and the owners helped a lot and all spoke very good English. The decoration was also nice, simple but with so much taste! I think that you should list this hotel, I had a great experience and probably other people would too.
