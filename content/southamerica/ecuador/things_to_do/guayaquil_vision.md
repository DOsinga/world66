This double decker bus takes you around the city of Guayaquil. Your tour guide explains the history of the buildings and how the city is changing now with the ongoing revitalization efforts. Bus picks you up around 7:00 at the Southern end of Malecon 2000 (The riverfront bordwalk and mall) and takes you around the Central and Northern parts of the city. Cost starts at $15.00 and is educational if you want to learn about the city. Tours offered at different lengths and destinations and are available in both Spanish and English.
