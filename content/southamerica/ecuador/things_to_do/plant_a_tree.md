A charity-run ecotourism project supporting reforestation and the local community offers basic farmstays in family homes in a poor agricultural area of great natural beauty. The farmstay price includes bed, breakfast & planting a tree.

Eco-tourists plant one tree for each day of their stay - how else could your visit leave an environmental benefit that could last for 30 or 40 years?

As well as tree-planting, you can also go trekking, bird-watching, rafting and horseriding.
