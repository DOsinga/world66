Mindo is a great place for watching hummingbirds as most of the hotels put out feeders for them. This makes it easier to photograph them. You can also see hummingbirds in most other parts of Ecuador, even in the middle of Quito.

One undeveloped area where you can see several types of hummingbird is San Luis de Pambil, in Bolivar Province in the warmer, lower part of the province. CRACYP, a local reforestation charity, has a photo gallery to give you some idea of some of the hummingbirds you might see in Ecuador.
