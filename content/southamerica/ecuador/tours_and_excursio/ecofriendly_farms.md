Bed & breakfast eco-tourism in family homes in the warm, sub-tropical part of Bolivar Province in the western foothills of the Andes. A charity-run project supporting reforestation and the local community in a poor but beautiful agricultural area. Farmstay price includes bed, breakfast & planting a tree. Adventure activities & guided tours – rafting, trekking, photography, horse-riding, bird-watching, chocolate-making, tree-planting, etc. Includes a useful ‘weather calendar’ for deciding when best to visit Ecuador. Fun for families, great for couples or groups, safe and welcoming for single travellers.

You can get there by bus from [Quito](/southamerica/ecuador/quito) (~6 hours) or [Guayaquil](/southamerica/ecuador/guayaquil) (~3-4 hours) but the most adventurous way to arrive is the 2-day downhill hike from Salinas de Guranda, also in Bolivar Province.
