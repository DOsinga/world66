**LOCATION ECUADOR SOUTH AMERICA COCA ORELLANA**
We are located in Ecuador South America east of the Andes in the town of Puerto Francisco de Orellana or Coca as it is more commonly called, in the province of Orellana only a short distance from the Peruvian border. Coca sits at the confluence of three major rivers: Payamino, Coca and the Napo, which eventually flow into the Amazon River . There are several different reserves and parks within a relatively short distance from Coca by boat that offer the professional, intermediate or beginner with great opportunities to observe a wide variety of species living in their natural habitat.
