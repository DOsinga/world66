---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

Saul is accessible only by light aircraft from Cayenne. Air Guyane operates scheduled flights several times a week; the flight takes about 45 minutes over unbroken rainforest. Book well in advance, especially during the dry season (July–November), as the small planes fill up quickly.

There is no road access to Saul. The airstrip is the only way in and out.
