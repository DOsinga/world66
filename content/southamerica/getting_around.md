There isn’t a border you can’t cross in South America. They are all open. Good long distant buses (with airco) take you from one side of the continent to the other. It only gets a bit more complicated when yopu want to visit the Guyanas from Brazil or the other way around. But in general South America offers you travel as it was meant to be. Getting to Central-America from South-America, that’s where it gets to be exciting
