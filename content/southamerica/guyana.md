Guyana, or British Guyana, was the first of the three Guyana's to be independent. Before may 1966 it was the only British colony on the South American mainland. 



Guyana borders on [Suriname](/southamerica/suriname) , [French Guiana](/southamerica/frenchguiana) and [Brazil](/southamerica/brazil) . 

[Georgetown](/southamerica/guyana/georgetown) the capital is the largest city. [New amsterdam](/southamerica/guyana/newamsterdam) is a an old Dutch town. The Dutch and the English had eternal wars in the Guyana's and the borders changed a lot. 

[Kaieteur falls](/southamerica/guyana/kaieteurfalls) in the south of the country is a spectacular attraction. The falls are 5 times higher than Niagara and located in the middle of an unspoilt rainforest. A hike in the tropical rainforest will be an unforgettable experience.
