---
title: "Getting Around"
type: section
---

Georgetown is walkable in the centre but sprawling enough that you will need transport for anything further out. The two main options are minibuses and taxis.

Minibuses run fixed routes around the city and are cheap — fares range from roughly 60 to 1,000 Guyana Dollars depending on distance. They are crowded and not recommended after dark, particularly for solo travellers.

Taxis are the better option for most visitors, and essential at night. Many are not metered, so agree on the fare before you get in. The ride from Cheddi Jagan International Airport to downtown Georgetown is fixed at around 4,000 Guyana Dollars. Your hotel can usually arrange a reliable driver. Using taxis recommended by your accommodation is the safest approach.
