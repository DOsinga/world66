---
title: "St. George's Cathedral"
type: poi
category: Sight
latitude: 6.8076
longitude: -58.1572
---

One of the largest wooden churches in the world and one of the tallest wooden buildings anywhere, this Anglican Gothic cathedral was completed in 1899 and rises 43 metres above North Road. The interior is cool and quiet even on the hottest days, with stained glass windows and a vaulted wooden ceiling. The structure is remarkable for its scale — it is hard to believe it is built entirely of native timber.
