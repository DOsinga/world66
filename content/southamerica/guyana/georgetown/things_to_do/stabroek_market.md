The cast-iron Victorian market hall at the foot of Robb Street is the beating heart of Georgetown. The clocktower is visible from blocks away and makes the best orientation point in the city. Inside, the market sells everything from fresh produce and spices to fabrics and electronics. It is always crowded, always loud, and always interesting. Keep an eye on your belongings.
