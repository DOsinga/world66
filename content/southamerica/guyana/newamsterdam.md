On the eastbank of the Berenice River, is the picturesque town of New Amsterdam. Lots of old colonial buildings dating back to the time the Dutch were still in charge here. New Amsterdam can be reached easily by minibus and ferry from [Georgetown](/northamerica/unitedstates/delaware/georgetown).
