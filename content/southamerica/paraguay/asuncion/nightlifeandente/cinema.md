There are several options for moviebuffs. There is a *cine* right on the Plaza de Armes, which offers films in a very peculiar atmosphere.

The huge shopping centers a few kilometers from the center (easily reached by city bus) all have their own cinema. The biggest is Shopping del Sol.

For more arty films, the Spanish consulate and the Alianza Francesa are a regular option. Most of the time, the people at the tourist office (Calle Palma) can help you find what´s on.
