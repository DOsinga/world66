---
title: "Eating Out"
type: section
---

Ciudad del Este's food reflects its border-town mix: Paraguayan staples like **sopa paraguaya** (a savoury cornbread), **chipa guasú** (corn and cheese pudding) and grilled **asado**, alongside Brazilian and Lebanese places serving the city's significant Arab community. Downtown restaurants are mostly functional rather than memorable; for something nicer, head to the hotel restaurants along the Super Carretera that link the city to Itaipú.
