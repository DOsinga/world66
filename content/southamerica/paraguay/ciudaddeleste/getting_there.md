---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

**Guaraní International Airport** (AGT) serves Ciudad del Este with domestic flights from Asunción and limited international connections, though most visitors arrive overland across the Friendship Bridge from **Foz do Iguaçu**, Brazil — a ten-minute walk or taxi ride that involves two border stamps if you are clearing both immigration posts. Long-distance buses run from Asunción (about five hours on the paved Ruta 2), and night buses continue on to Brazilian cities further east.
