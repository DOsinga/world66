---
title: "When to Go"
type: section
---

Encarnacion has a subtropical climate with hot, humid summers and mild winters. The best time to visit is during the carnival season in February, when the city comes alive with its famous Carnaval Encarnaceno. The summer months (December to March) are also good for sportfishing on the Parana River, though temperatures regularly exceed 35°C. Winter (June to August) is milder and pleasant for visiting the nearby Jesuit mission ruins.
