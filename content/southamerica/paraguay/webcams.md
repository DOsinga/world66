*** Sights in Paraguay***

****

There are many beautiful sights in Paraguay. Some sights are telling a story, like old historical buildings, and giant monuments. However, the sights that I find most amazing are the vibrant, lucious, and breath-taking waterfalls. The falls are relaxing and refreshing for the soul. You just feel like you could float on a cloud. The falls are the most beautiful and enjoying thing you could ever experience. Remember; Paraguay- The country with the Falls.
