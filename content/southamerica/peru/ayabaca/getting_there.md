---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

Most travelers will arrive from [Piura](/southamerica/peru/piura). There are direct busses from there to Ayabaca, taking at least six hours to travel about 200 kilometers. Las Vegas and Poderoso Cautivo run daily busses. A more comfortable and interesting way to travel is by car. First you can take a minibus to Sullana, a town in the Piura province (2 sol). In Sullana take a mototaxi to the terminal for Ayabaca (2 sol). Arrive in the early morning and you can get a car that drives up the mountain. It will be a white Toyota Corolla that won´t leave unless there are 5 passengers each paying 20 soles. If no passengers arrive, he should leave if he receives a total of 100 soles. This way you will be able to appreciate the ever-changing landscape more thoroughly.
