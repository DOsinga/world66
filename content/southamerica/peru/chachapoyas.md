Chachapoyas (2,335 meters above sea level) 

It is one of remote areas of Peru. It is a land of many and quite interesting archaeological sites and a beautiful landscapes that includes interesting flora and fauna. 

**The city of Chachapoyas was found by** Alonso De Alvarado on September 5, 1538, 

In the surroundings we can find different and amazing archaeological sites built by an ancient culture called The Chachapooyas. They were excellent warriors. The Inca conquered them after many battles. 

Without doubt one of the most important is the fortress of Kuelap located in a top of a mountain. It was discovered before Machu Picchu
