title: "Huaca Esmeralda"
type: poi
category: Sight
latitude: -8.1074
longitude: -79.0614
---

A Chimú ceremonial platform mound on the outskirts of Chan Chan, closer to Trujillo. The adobe walls still show traces of painted friezes — fish, birds, nets — though erosion has softened them considerably. Included in the Chan Chan entry ticket.
