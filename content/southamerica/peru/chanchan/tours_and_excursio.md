### [InkaNatura Travel](/southamerica/peru/chanchan/tours_and_excursio/inkanatura_travel)

Tour operator with offices in Chiclayo that offers high quality and personalized service in the North Coast (Chiclayo and Trujillo). It also operates programs and expeditions to Chachapoyas including visits to the amazing fortress of Kuelap. Chiclayo and Trujillo tours.

Offices in Lima, Cusco and Chiclayo. InkaNatura also manages rainforest lodges in Manu and Tambopata and it has just started to operate Inca trail to Machu Picchu programs from Cusco city.

We found them expensive & their guide did not explain much or any longer than the run of the mill operators.
