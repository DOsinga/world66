Chiclayo is an important town on the northern coast, founded in the 1560-ies by a Spanish priest. About 250.000. people live here and it is busy place and an economically important town.

 The reason to come to Chiclayo, is the Bruening Archaeological Museum with gold found in the neighbouring archeological finds. The museum is located in [Lambayeque](/southamerica/peru/lambayeque) which is just 11 km north of Chiclayo.
