In nearby [Lambayeque](/southamerica/peru/lambayeque), about 11 km north of Chiclayo. The museum houses an excellent collection of pre-Columbian gold, ceramics, and textiles from the Sipán tombs and the Moche, Lambayeque, and Chimú cultures. You can get there by minibus from Chiclayo.
