Pimentel has an attractive beach and enough bars and restaurants to make an afternoon of it. A slowly disintegrating wooden pier with an old train rail adds atmosphere, especially in the fog. Traditional cane fishing boats (*caballitos de totora*) line the beach.

Santa Rosa is close enough to walk to, but watch out for the sun.
