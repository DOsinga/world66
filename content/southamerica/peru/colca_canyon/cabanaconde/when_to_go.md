title: "When to Go in Cabanaconde"
type: section

There are ATMs in almost all the pelcas mentioned, check a guidebook for specifics. I carry USD to convert because like most (all?) poor countries, there are tons of moneychangers offering rates with tiny sub 1% spreads. Whether to carry cash or ATM cards is really a matter of preference and your comfort level carrying a lot of cash.
