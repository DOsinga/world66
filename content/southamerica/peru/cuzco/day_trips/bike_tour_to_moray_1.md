**Mixed Tour To Discover Agricultural Laboratory, Salt Mines And The Best Inca Archaeological Site Of South America 2 Days - 1 Night**

**http://www.peruadventurestours.com/en/cusco/maras_moray_machupicchu_bike_tour.html**

Moray, Maras And Machu Picchu bike tour are dedicated for all mountain biking lovers, this bike tour goes through agricultural laboratory, salt mines and the most famous Inca archaeological site in South America.
Bike ride on the sacred valley offer you to get know local Andean communities and learn their amazing life style. Mountain Biking is a relatively new sport in Peru, but new routes are rapidly being opened up, many of which offer excellent opportunities to ride.
This tour combine to visit the most famous fertile valley of as know sacred valley of the Inca, located between Cusco and Machu Picchu. This tour includes two days of amazing and adventurous mountain bike in Cusco región.
We invite you join us and get the best experience riding every single trucks or dirt roads in th Andean countryside.
This tour dont demand to be experienced biker, everyone can be join this tour, the most important is to like to explore and having more contact with nature.
Tour Itinerary
DAY 1: CUSCO - BIKE TOUR TO MORAY AND MARAS - AGUAS CALIENTES

At 7am we pick you up from the hotel and travel by own private vehicle to Chincheros, which takes 40 minutes. Here we begin our cross country biking tour on the nice Andean landscape. We visit Lake Huaypo, Maras-Moray, Salt mining and finally enjoy doing downhill biking to Urubamba in the Sacred Valley. Maras is an Inca settlement, and is a place named Salineras, are extensive terracing salt areas, controlled, and used since before Inca days. This trip is for beginners, and experience bikers, you can choose to ride in asphalt road, dirt road and single trucks. Will be a nice experience mixing the sport and knowledge of the Inca culture. After 5 hours biking 35km (or more) stopping at different view points. The lunch willbe in Ollantaytambo and then at 4pm we aboard the train to Aguas Calientes (2 hours of travel), welcoming reception and then transfer to the hotel in Aguas Calientes (L).
DAY 2: AGUAS CALIENTES - MACHU PICCHU - CUSCO

1st OPTION
At 4:30am breakfast and begin of 2 hours climb to Machu Picchu archaeological site (2500m).
2nd OPTION
At 6am breakfast and half hour later aboard the firts tourist bus to drop us to Machu Picchu main door, and to be the first visitors Inside Machu Picchu archaeological site you will have complete guided tour of this spectacular and mystical place. The guided tour include walk through the different section such as urban, agriculture, and the religions zone was used by the Inca Culture. According withthe time and your feeling you can coordinating with the tour guide climb to Huayna Picchu (Young Mountain) this climbing take around 1 hour to get the summit from where you can have the best view for photos of all Machu Picchu and of deep green canyon surrounding the sacred Inca City. At according time descend to Machu Picchu and then free time to explorer on your own. At mentioned time aboard a tourist bus to descend to Aguas Calientes town. Lunch will be in town and then we will aboard the backpacker train to Ollantaytambo (sacred valley) and from here we take a private van to drop us to the hote in Cusco (B - L).
