About 1 hours wlak from the city centre, this is the best Inca ruin in the immediate vicinity of Cuzco. Climb the steep street of Resbalosa and turn right at the top. Continue until you reach a hairpin bend in the road. This is where the old Inca road from Cuzco to Sacsa begins. Follow this road.
