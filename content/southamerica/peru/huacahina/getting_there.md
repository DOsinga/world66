---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

From [Ica](/southamerica/peru/ica) just take a taxi to Huacachina. It is only 4 kilometers away from the center of Ica. The price is 3-4 soles per ride.
If you don't know what place to stay in, the taxi drivers are usually willing to wait for you and take you to the next place. Although everything is within walking distance, it's nicer when somebody carries you and your bags.
