The buggie is a sort of vehicle designed to ride the sand dunes.
Riding these vehicles feels like a roller coaster. The guide rides around,
and then drops you at the top of the dunes.
There, you are supplied with
sand boards for skiing, or falling downhill. The buggies wait at the
bottom, ready to take you the next hilltop.

The tour takes about 2 hours and finishes around the sunset. It cost 40 soles at the time of the writing.
