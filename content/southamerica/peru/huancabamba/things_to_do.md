If you are in Huancabamba, ask around for which** lagunas** are easiest to
reach. If you´re interested in **shamanism**, you´ll have to get higher in
to the mountains, where the best ones live. Ask around in the city where to look. You can also combine the two and go to a laguna with a shaman, which can be quite a refreshing experience.
