Huanchaco's location on the northern coast makes it an ideal base for exploring the major archaeological and colonial sites of the region. Chan Chan, the largest pre-Columbian adobe city in the world, is only a short taxi or combi ride away. Trujillo, the regional capital, has a handsome colonial centre and is the starting point for visiting the Moche pyramids of Huaca del Sol and Huaca de la Luna.
