---
title: "Huanchaco Pier"
type: poi
category: Sight
latitude: -8.0831
longitude: -79.1236
---

The long wooden pier extends from the southern end of the beach into the Pacific. It is the natural gathering point at dusk, when the light falls across the water and the fishermen are pulling in for the day. Worth a short walk for the views back toward the church on the hill and the line of caballitos on the sand.
