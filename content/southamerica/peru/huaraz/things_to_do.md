---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Huaraz is all about the outdoors. The Cordillera Blanca and Cordillera Huayhuash offer world-class trekking, from the classic four-day Santa Cruz trek to serious mountaineering on peaks like Alpamayo and Huascaran. Rock climbing, ice climbing, and canyoning are all available through agencies on Avenida Luzuriaga, and you can rent quality gear in town if you did not bring your own.
