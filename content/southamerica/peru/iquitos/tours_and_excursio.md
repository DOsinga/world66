**Pacaya Samiria Amazon Lodge**

Pacaya Samiria Amazon Lodge offers guided excursions to the Pacaya-Samiria National Reservem which has a great and unique variety of flora and fauna, and is also known as the “Jungle of the Mirrors” due to the fact that the jungle and the sky are reflected so clearly in the dark waters of its rivers and lakes, that the visitors have the sensation of navigating throughout endless mirrors of water. There, visitors will also have the chance to observe exotic pink dolphin pf the Amazon at their natural habitat. Activities also include visits to the local communities, hikes in primary rainforest, observation of flora and fauna, birdwatching, canoe rides, fishing and many more.
