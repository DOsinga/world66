One of Lima's best steakhouses, and a Miraflores institution. The beef is exceptional — some argue it's Argentine-style, but the preparation is distinctly Peruvian. If you're not ravenous, the anticuchos (beef heart skewers) with papas a la huancaína on the side are an excellent choice. The menu del dia at lunch is good value, but the real reason to come is an evening meal with a proper cut. Expect to spend a bit more than average, but it earns it.
