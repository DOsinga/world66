---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

Juliaca has flights to [Lima](/southamerica/peru/lima) for USD59-89 and to Arequipa for USD49-69. If you fly directly, take it easy the first few days, as the height will slow you down. You might suffer symptoms of altitude sickness.

 There are busses to [Cuzco](/southamerica/peru/cuzco), 7 hours and 9 USD, Arequipa, USD 11, 11 hours and [Lima](/southamerica/peru/lima), up to 40 hours. You can get a direct bus to [La Paz](/southamerica/bolivia/lapaz), but you might consider breaking the trip at Cococabana or even take a tour that visits some of the islands along the way.

 There are also trains to [Cuzco](/southamerica/peru/cuzco), which are rather expensive, because of the special tourist prices and no longer faster. They are also infrequent, leaving only every other day or so.
