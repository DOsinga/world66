The best resource for sights, hotels, restaurants, bars, what to do and see

Moray is a town in Peru approx. 50 km North West of Cuzco that is famous for its Incan agricultural terraces. They are said to be used by Incas to study the effects of different climatic conditions on crops. Definitely worth a visit.
