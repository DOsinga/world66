---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

The Nazca Lines are the reason most travellers come here, and the only practical way to see them is from a small plane. Beyond the lines, the Cahuachi ceremonial site, the Antonini Museum, and the Maria Reiche Museum give context to the Nazca culture. The Pampas Galeras vicuña reserve, 90 km away in the Andes, is a worthwhile half-day trip.
