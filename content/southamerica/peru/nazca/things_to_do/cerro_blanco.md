title: "Cerro Blanco"
type: poi
category: "Sight"
latitude: -14.8517
longitude: -74.8945
---

A massive sand dune rising about 2,078 metres above sea level on the eastern edge of the Nazca plain — said to be one of the tallest sand dunes in the world. The ascent takes three to four hours through the dunes; the views across the Nazca Valley and towards the Andes are exceptional. Sandboarding is possible on the descent. The dune is about 9 km from Nazca town; guided tours are available from agencies in the centre.
