### [Jaén - San Ignacio - Jaén](/southamerica/peru/northern_pacific_t/jan/day_trips/jan__san_ignaci)

For those passing by Jaén on route to or from [Chachapoyas](/southamerica/peru/chachapoyas), it's feasible to visit [San ignacio](/centralamericathecaribbean/belize/sanignacio) in one day, than come back. If you’re on route to or from Vilcabamba, [Ecuador](/southamerica/ecuador), you’ll have to run less.

### [Buena Esperanza Beach](/southamerica/peru/northern_pacific_t/jan/day_trips/buena_esperanza_be)

More an afternoon trip than a day trip, you can go to a riverside beach pretty easily. Buena Esperanza at the Rio Huancabamba is a good example of that.
At the bus terminal on the main boulevard out of town (direction [Chiclayo](/southamerica/peru/chiclayo) – [Chachapoyas](/southamerica/peru/chachapoyas)), you can either take a colectivo car (2,5 soles) or a combi minibus (2 soles) to Buena Esperanza.

### [Jaén - Bagua Chica - Santa Rosa - Puentosillos - Jaén](/southamerica/peru/northern_pacific_t/jan/day_trips/jan__bagua_chic)

From Jaén you can visit these places in a day, if you rush it a bit.

In Jaén, take a minibus to Bagua Chica. Watch out, as Bagua Grande also exists. Price: 5 to 10 soles. The road is nice and in good condition, with a river crossing on a pretty little bridge.

Bagua Chica isn't exactly attractive. You might arrive in the Terminal Terrestre or busterminal. From there walk or take a mototaxi to the market area (0,5 to 1 soles), near the Plaza de Armas.
