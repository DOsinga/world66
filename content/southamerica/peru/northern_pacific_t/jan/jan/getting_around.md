---
title: "Getting Around"
type: section
---

If you mind walking - and the heat can be a great minder - you can take
a mototaxi at -any- time. In this small city there must be at least
2000 of them.

For most distances they should charge 1 sol, or 1.5 sol maximum. At
night prices are slightly higher. Most of the time it´s much easier to
get to a place if you just hop in and state your destination, than to
walk around and look for it yourself.
