---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

Most bus companies operate from Avenida Sanchez Cerro, blocks 11 and 12. It is about 14 hours to [Lima](/southamerica/peru/lima), 3 hours to [Chiclayo](/southamerica/peru/chiclayo), and 5 hours to Tumbes near the Ecuadorian border. Frequent buses run to Sullana (20 minutes), from where you can find onward transport to [Ecuador](/southamerica/ecuador).
