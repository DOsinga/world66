type: section
---

Most bus comapnies are on the Avenida Sanchez Cerro on block 11 and 12. It's 14 hours and 14 dollar to [Lima](/southamerica/peru/lima). 3 hours and 3 bucks to [Chiclayo](/southamerica/peru/chiclayo), 7 to [Trujillo](/centralamericathecaribbean/honduras/trujillo) (change bus in Chiclayo), 5 to Tumbes.

 There are frequent buses to Sullana just 20 minutes. From there you can find onward transport to the border with [Ecuador](/southamerica/ecuador).
