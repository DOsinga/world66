Pucallpa is a fast growing jungle town that can be reached by boat from [Iquitos](/southamerica/peru/iquitos). For travelers it is a good base to see some more jungle, to visit the National Park - actually more like a zoo, that offers good opportunities to see jungle animals, or to go to the Inidan village of Yarinacocha on lake Yarinocacho. The lake is very pretty and wort a visit.
