---
title: "Pinturas Rupestres Faical"
type: poi
category: "Sight"
latitude: -5.1050
longitude: -78.9100
---

Pre-Columbian rock art about 20 minutes by road from San Ignacio, on a scenic dirt road. The age of the paintings is estimated between 1,800 and 5,000 years. They have been bleaching since the forest around them was cleared about 25 years ago, and may not survive many more decades without protection. Entrance is 2 soles. Hire a car with driver from town for around 50 soles for the round trip.
