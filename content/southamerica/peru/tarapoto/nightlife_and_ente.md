### [Papillon](/southamerica/peru/tarapoto/nightlife_and_ente/papillon)

Papillon is probably the most known disco in the San Martin district, probably because of the band of the same name. The building is a large barn made of concrete and steel, but several levels of dancefloors and bars make it a popular place to dance, watch other people dance, and be watched dancing. Rows and rows of motorcycles in front give a good idea about the number of people inside the disco on any given night. It is probably best to take a mototaxi to go there, even for locals, because they don´t want to worry about getting their motorcycle stolen.
