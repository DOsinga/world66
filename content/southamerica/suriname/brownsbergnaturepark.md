Brownsberg is about 100km south of [Paramaribo](/southamerica/suriname/paramaribo) and is connected by road. You can reach it in as little as one and a half hours. 

The park comprises an area of montane tropical rainforest overlooking one of the world's largest reservoirs, the W J van Blommestein Meer. Guided tours are available and include a short walk on the Mazaroni plateau, which gives fine views of the reservoir to the east, and a longer hike which involves a steep descent into a canyon with small but attractive waterfalls. 

It is possible to book the whole trip in Paramaribo and do it in just one day.
