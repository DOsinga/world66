A long-running Paramaribo club with three dancefloors, located on the strip just across from the Torarica Hotel on the waterfront. The music runs from dancehall to hip-hop to local Surinamese sounds. Friday and Saturday are the main nights — don't arrive before 1am. Surinamese parties start very late and go until early morning.
