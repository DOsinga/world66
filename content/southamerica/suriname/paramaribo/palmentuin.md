The Palm Garden beside the Presidential Palace is the most pleasant green space in the city centre — a formal garden of royal palms with benches, shade, and a resident troop of capuchin monkeys that treat the treetops as their personal highway. It is the natural place to rest between sights in the historic district and, in the late afternoon, fills with office workers, school children, and vendors selling fresh coconut water.
