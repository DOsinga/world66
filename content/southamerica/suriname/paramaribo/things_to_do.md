---
title: "Things to Do"
type: section
---

Paramaribo's UNESCO-listed historic centre rewards slow exploration on foot. The main concentration of sights runs from the waterfront at Fort Zeelandia inland through Onafhankelijkheidsplein and along Keizerstraat, where some of the city's most remarkable religious buildings stand in close proximity. Allow at least half a day for the fort and old city, and save time for the Palmentuin — the most pleasant green space in the centre.
