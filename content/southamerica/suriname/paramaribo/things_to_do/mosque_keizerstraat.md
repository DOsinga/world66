The Ahmadiyya mosque on Keizerstraat is one of the largest mosques in the Caribbean and, with the Neveh Shalom Synagogue standing immediately next door, forms one of the most photographed religious streetscapes in South America. The building is a handsome structure and the congregation is welcoming to respectful visitors outside of prayer times.
