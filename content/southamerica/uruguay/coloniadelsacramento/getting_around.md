---
title: "Getting Around"
type: section
---

All the main attractions are in the old town region, so you can reach almost everything walking.

If you want to go to the Plaza de Toros, the cheapest way (beside a 1 hour walk) is to get a public bus. From the old town, the one that says "Parque San Rafael" will do, although most of the public buses go along the coast to the north (just ask the driver, they are usually polite and will be glad to help you).

A more expensive option is to rent a scooter, or a golf car. A bycicle is also a great option.
