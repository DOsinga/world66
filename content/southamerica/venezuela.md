Venezuela is located on the Carribean coast of South America. It borders on [Guyana](/southamerica/guyana) , [Brazil](/southamerica/brazil) , and [Colombia](/southamerica/colombia) .Venezuela has produced more winners of the Miss Universe per capita than any other country; the statistical proof that the country has many beautiful women. But of course the country has a lot more to offer. 



The capital city of [Caracas](/southamerica/venezuela/caracas) is a busy place and there are always things going on there. You shouldn't miss out on it. The beach resort of [Margarita island](/southamerica/venezuela/margaritaisland) is popular with people looking for a place to relax and get a tan. [Angel falls](/southamerica/venezuela/angelfalls) are one of the most spectacular natural wonders in the world. If you think Niagara is great you should really go here. 

The city of [Maracaibo](/southamerica/venezuela/maracaibo) , an oil boom town, in the west of the country has little to offer to the tourist.
