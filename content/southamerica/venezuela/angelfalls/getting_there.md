---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

There are three ways to see the falls: a flightseeing trip from [Caracas](/southamerica/venezuela/caracas), an overland trek that takes several days from [Canaima](/southamerica/venezuela/canaima) or an overnight canoe trip. Two types of flights take you by the falls: A one-day trip leaves either [Margarita island](/southamerica/venezuela/margaritaisland) or [Puerto la cruz](/southamerica/venezuela/puertolacruz) early in the morning makes passes over the falls lands for lunch then returns. The (preferred) two-day trip flies over the falls and along the Orinoco River then lands for the night at Canaima (25 mi/40 km northwest of Angel Falls) and returns to Caracas the following day.
