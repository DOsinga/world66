Being the first ever book to reveal the hidden strategy of the rich class to steal profits from the state-run oil company PDVSA the book gives an overview on the economic and political situation in Venezuela and analyses the past as well as very recent development. By this the reader will gain an in-depth knowledge on the most decisive issues and confrontations. The book includes an interview with President Hugo Chavez about all controversies in Venezuela's society and is recommended by the UK's Foreign & Commonwealth Office's Library.
