**Shops**

**Edificio la Francia **

Placed in the city centre, near to the Capitolio, the Plaza Bolivar, the Catedral the Calvario, it has nine floors of gold and jewelry shops and dealers. You can surely find any kind of jewerly you are looking for, all of it made with venezuelan gold, one of the world's finest.

**Centro SAMBIL**

This is the biggest Latin American Shopping Centre. There you'll find the greatest cafes, nice cinemas and an impressive Bowling!

**Centro SAN IGNACIO**

During the day, this is the most exclusice place to shop, where you'll find the most famous trends. During the night time there you'll find the best cafes and discos of all the city, you better visit it!
