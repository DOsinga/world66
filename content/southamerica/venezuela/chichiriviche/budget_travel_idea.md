Posada Aloe is a family run small hotel located inside the Morrocoy national park, Chichiriviche; Venezuela. One of the most important for the incredible variety of species, birds in particular.The main attractions are the small coral islands with sandy beaches and cristal clear waters. Just 2 blocks from the carribean sea, Posada Aloe has 6 nicely decorated rooms, swimming pool, restaurant. In Posada Aloe you can rent boats to the islands, rent a boat for the whole day, rent kayaks, book a trip to the desert of Coro, book a diving tour or just relax!
Why choose Posada Aloe? Because is clean, friendly, close to beaches and city center,has open spaces, garden and good food
What makes Posada Aloe your best option? The fact that is a family run posada; you won't get a service......you'll be part of the family.
How can I make a reservation? There are 2 ways: 1) Stating by mail your will to book a room and confirm with an other mail a week before the date of the booking and then paying at the check in. 2) money transfer on a european bank account.
