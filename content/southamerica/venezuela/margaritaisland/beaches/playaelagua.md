---
title: "Playa El Agua"
type: poi
latitude: 11.1482
longitude: -63.8662
---

The island's most famous beach and one of the longest in the Caribbean -- roughly four kilometres of white sand backed by coconut palms. The surf can be strong, which makes it fun but not ideal for small children. Beach restaurants and kiosks line the road behind the sand, and the scene gets lively on weekends and holidays.
