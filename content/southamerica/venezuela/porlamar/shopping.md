title: "Shopping in Porlamar"
type: section
---

Great **Porlamar shopping** centers await avid shoppers who are in search of the best bargains and products in Porlamar, Isla Margarita, Venezuela. Home to shops ranging from small street side booths to high end boutiques, Porlamar City is a great travel and shopping destination. Here, you will find shops for everything that you need.

#### Sambil Margarita

This is one of the premier shopping malls in Porlamar. Fashion boutiques for women and men alike are abundant. Though you may not be able to bid on your price since prices are almost always fixed here, prices remain competitive and huge sales are often available. The same goes for other products like food items, gadgets and electronics for the techies, furniture for your house, and toys for the kids. The place remains busy all throughout the day. This shopping experience is convenient because similar stores are grouped together. There are s lot of lounges, cafes and restaurants to choose from if you want to take a short break from shopping amid all the offers around.

**El Mercado de los Conejeros**

For those who prefer bargains in a more local and casual set-up, visit El Mercado de los Conejeros. Most of the booths and stalls here sell native products like bags, wallets and accessories. It is almost always packed with people. The shops usually open early, around 8:00 am, and close at around 2:00 pm. Prices of different goods are often negotiable.

#### Tierra de Arte

Tierra de Arte is a well-known Porlamar shopping destination that specializes in handmade crafts and souvenirs. Located in downtown Porlamar, this small shop is also famous for personalized items. You will find a wide selection of woven products like hammocks and bags. They also offer wood carvings like miniature wooden boats, pottery and traditional clothes. People who are fans of such products should visit Tierra de Arte for interesting finds.

#### Rattan

For your small everyday shopping needs, visit the Rattan Depot and Plaza. It follows a format similar to that of Target in the USA. If you need to buy food items and other knickknacks, particularly in bulk, then the Rattan Depot and Plaza is the best place for you. Prices are non-negotiable, but the products are generally less expensive compared to those of the other malls.

Aside from the resorts, entertainment hubs and great hotels, the ability to shop at these Porlamar shopping centers is a good reason to plan a vacation to Isla Margarita, Venezuela. You can enjoy the warm hospitality from the locals, the exciting products and the shopping experience that you are looking for.
