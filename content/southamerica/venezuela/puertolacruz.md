This is one of the biggest resorts of the whole country. Here you'll find everything: from luxury hotels, posh districts and enormous shopping centres to popular streets. Puerto la Cruz is undoubtely a very fun place! A walk throug the *Paseo Colón* in the sunshine is an unique experience, sitting in any restaurant and eat their famous *empanadas* will take you to glory. When getting around, you MUST go to Mochima, a National Park which has plenty of small islands with white-sandy beaches. You can go as well to Santa Fé, a little town up in the near mountain where you'll have superb views and the feeling of not being in the beach....
