Venezuela is located on the Caribbean coast of South America. It borders with Guyana , Brazil , and Colombia .Venezuela has produced more winners of the Miss Universe per capita than any other country; the statistical proof that the country has many beautiful women. But of course the country has a lot more to offer. The capital city of Caracas is a busy place and there are always things going on there. You shouldn't miss out on it. The beach resort of Margarita island is popular with people looking for a place to relax and get a tan. Angel falls are one of the most spectacular natural wonders in the world. If you think Niagara is great you should really go here. The city of Maracaibo , an oil boom town, in the west of the country has good things to offer to the tourist too. A 160km to the West of Caracas and situated in the center of Venezuela is Valencia is the second largest city and the country's fastest growing economy.
