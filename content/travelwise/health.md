[TravelWise](/world/travelwise) **>** [Health](/world/travelwise/health)

**World66** wants to provide you health-relevant information for any country you want to travel to. The main editor of our health pages is a travel-experienced physician, and we do accept only health care professionals as co-editors (as an open system we gladly accept any remarks in the "Notes" section, but the core of the health pages is exclusively looked after by our editors). Despite our efforts in keeping our health section up to date and to avoid any errors, we cannot guarantee for a 100% correctness and thus we can accept no responsibility for any consequences possibly resulting from inaccurate or misleading information.

The content here is not to replace consulting a real doctor, but it does give you an idea what might happen.
