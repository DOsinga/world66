[TravelWise](/world/travelwise) **>** [Money](/world/travelwise/money)

Take as much as you can of this handy commodity, is what they used to say in the old days. Well, that's no longer completely true. ATMs have spread around the world, credit cards are accepted almost everywhere, so there is more to the subject of money than just carrying cash.

American Express and other companies offer cards that act like credit or ATM cards but are issued specifically for your trip, like traveler's checques. These are safer than cash and can be replaced if stolen.
