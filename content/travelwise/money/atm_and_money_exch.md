I advise anyone travelling to countries where money can be drawn from ATM machines to use them as there are several advantages.

One of the main ones is the elimination of Traveller's Cheques and the need to carry cash from your home country. Also, most banks, worldwide, give very favorable exchange rates on credit and debit cards. These rates are almost always better than exchange houses.

Please check with your invidual banking establishments regarding "processing fees" added to you your account for withdrawals at banks in foriegn locations.

Bank of America (www.bankofamerica.com) has been quoted that "a $5.00 fee per occasion is assessed on accounts that draw currency from foreign located ATM, outside of their banking network".

The best recommendation is to check with your individual banking establishment before making your trip. Each banking establishment, obviously, has different rules and regulations due to variances in their governmental laws and policies.

Some banks also may lock your card from foreign transactions without prior notification. Most banks will be happy to addend your account with information so that if your card is flagged because of significant foreign activity, they can verify that it is in fact you making transactions on your card.
