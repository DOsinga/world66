Dixcove is a fishermans village in the south-west of Ghana. 

The beaches near Dixcove are perfect: white sand, great wheather, palm trees... The most beautyfull one is Busua beach. You'll get there with a taxi or a 5-10 min walk trough a shadefull bushroad. 

There is a beautiful Fort, the Medal Cross Fort, which overlooks the bay. Inheritance from the Dutch defence. It has a natural harbour and the port is a lively area. You can buy fresh sharkmeat, swordfish and lobsters for which the area is renowned.
