---
title: "Fakobly"
type: location
---

Fakobly is 9 km from [Tieni-Siabli](/africa/ivorycoast/tienisiabli) and some 25 from [Man](/africa/ivorycoast/man). It lies on the banks of a tributary of the Sassandra River. There are hippo's and crocodiles in the river and you can hire a priogue for a small tour on the water. The local Wobe girls also do an attracive kind of dance known as temate, but it is often hard to find out in advance when there will be a performance.
