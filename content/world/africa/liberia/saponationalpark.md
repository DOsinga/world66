Cover more than 250,000 acres over 4 counties(Grand Gedah, River Gee, Sinoe and Maryland county). Largest National Park in Liberia. Area has so many wild animal(Elephant, Piggmy Hipo, Leopard, Chimpanzee...), insect and wild flower(orchid). Part of this National park-never been survey/enter by Human before.

It is one of the very last virgin forest in West Africa.
