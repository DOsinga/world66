title: "Ghadhames"
type: location
---

Ghadhames is one of the nicest places to visit. The traditional desert architecture make it an interesting stop. The beauty of the nearby region make it an ideal trip form Triploi either just for getting some taste of the desert or on the way further south, to [Sebha](/africa/libya/sebha) and then on to [Niger](/africa/niger).

 This traditional oasis village was once the southernmost outpost of the Roman Empire. In some ways Ghadhames still marks the southern limit of civilization before the terrain is taken over by the sands of the Sahara. *230 mi/370 km southwest of Tripoli. *
