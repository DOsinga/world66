**National Festival of Popular Arts**

The Festival of Popular Arts takes place in June and lasts for two weeks. It mainly takes place amid the ruins of the El Badi Palace. **Tourist Fantasia**

A fantasia is a traditional Moroccan celebration with armed horsmen in full combat dress showing off their agility. Real fantasias are held when there is a Moulou or an occasion like the Fete du Throne. **Fete du Throne**

On the 3 of march the Fete du throne is celebrated. Often big Fantasias are held just outside of the city guides -better than the tourist ones- and bands perform on the Jma-l-Fna.
