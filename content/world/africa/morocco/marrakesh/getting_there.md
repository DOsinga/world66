---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

Marrakesh is the southern transport hub of Morocco. To the north trains are the best option. There are frequent and comfortable trains to [Rabat](/africa/morocco/rabat), [Casablanca](/africa/morocco/casablanca) and even all the way to [Tangier](/africa/morocco/tangier). 

To [Essaouira](/africa/morocco/essaouira) there are frequent buses. To Asni and [Ourika](/africa/morocco/ourika) your best option is s shared taxi (grand taxi). 

To the south the choice is between bus and grand taxi, the latter gives you a little more freedom of movement. To [Ouarzazate](/africa/morocco/ouarzazate) there's only one route you can take (Tizi-n-Tichka), but to [Agadir](/africa/morocco/agadir) and [Taroudant](/africa/morocco/taroudant) there are more passes across the atlas. I prefer the Tizi-n-test, but drivers may be unwilling to go this way.
