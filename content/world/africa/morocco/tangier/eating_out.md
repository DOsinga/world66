For eating out in Tangier you have three options. Cheap places in the Medina, or more upmarket places in the Ville Nouvelle or try the restaurants on the Seafront. When you eat out in Tangier you should at least try the fish dishes. The Swordfish Tajine is one of my personal favourites.
