---
title: "Getting Around"
type: section
---

The town of Tineghir is so small you won't need a mapp. getting aroudn in the oasis is also easy enough. There is a big road leading up to the Gorges via Taorirt, Ait Oujena and Ait Ouaghitine, but if you are walking you might as well try to find your way through the palmery. There is also a smalle road starting at Tidirini and going to Imarirene via Ihajamen and Ait Bujan, but this does not take you to the Gorge. Good walk though.

 You can also follow the Oued Tineghir south to Ait Aissa ou Brahim via Ait Mohammed and el-Herat.
