title: "Yankari Game Reserve"
type: location
---

Yankari National Park is a very beautiful place to visit. For me, it has been the highlight of my visits to West Africa.

The Wikki Warm Springs is one of nature's amazing secrets. It is unbelievable how crystal clear and warm the water is.

The wildlife in the park is interesting too. I have seen some of Africa's greatest beasts, elephants, lions, hippos, monkeys, antelopes, etc.

Its worth a visit if one ever decided to visit Nigeria.

watch out for Baboons though, they're after your pickincik baskets...
