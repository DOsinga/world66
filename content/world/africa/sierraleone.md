Sierra Leone is ravaged by internal wars. This diamond rich small country in West Africa has been on the news more often than most travelers like - and most of the news coverage was about hands cut off and refugees.


 
 Should the country get back to its feet than the best place to start exploring it is [Freetown](/africa/sierraleone/freetown) the capital. It is by far the city with the best infrastructure, there are some nice restaurants run by Lebanese immigrants. Good beaches are nearby.
