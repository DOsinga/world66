Air

The main airline running services to Togo is *Air Afrique (RK) *in which Togo is a shareholder. Togo has become an important transit point for air travel in Africa. There are frequent flights to major African destinations.

Approximate flight time

From Lom&eacute; to *London* is 7 hours.

International airport:

*Lom&eacute; (LFW) *is* *4km (2.5 miles) northeast of the city. Airport facilities include bar restaurant snack bar shops bank post office duty-free shop and car hire. Taxi and bus services are available to the city centre.

Sea

Ferries from Benin and Ghana call at Lom&eacute; and coastal ports. For details contact the port authorities.

Road

There are routes from Benin Ghana and Burkina Faso but conditions are unreliable.
