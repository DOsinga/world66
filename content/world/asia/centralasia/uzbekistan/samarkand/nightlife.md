**Oasis Beer Garden**

For a cheap, clean dinner and joint venture beer, go to the Oasis Beer Garden. One can sit on tree stump stools around tree stump tables while the uniformed, English speaking servants bring beers from the log cabin beer house. The grass is nice and a river winds through the park and adjacent to [more..](/asia/centralasia/uzbekistan/samarkand/nightlife/oasisbeergarden)

**Green Hall**

One of the latest places to open is the indoor/outdoor discoteque The Green Hall. It comes highly recommended. 

**Praga Beer Factory**

The Praga Beer Factory has a bar attached. One can walk through a human sized beer keg. Watch out for the waiters. Bills become inflated as the customers drink more and one has to pay for the complementory beer nuts.
