Bandar e Anzali is a beautiful piece of land, dream of my childhood and my retirement destination.

If you get a chance to visit Iran, spend a day or two to visit this beautiful city. I suggest you just walk in the city, pass the bridge that connects Anzali to Ghazian and just breathe. Although the Shah's palace is a nice place to visit, the nature is way much more enjoyable. Fishing in Mordab and swimming in Caspian sea is an absolute joy.
