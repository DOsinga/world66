In northwest Iran near the Turkish border lies Tabriz. Once the capital of Armenia it is now one of the biggest cities of Iran. The population of Tabriz consists for a large part of Azeri Turks. 

Sights include the the ruined 14th-century Ali Shah Mosque, the 13th-century Blue Mosque and the Friday Mosque. The Bazar is also worth a few hours of your time. 

Daytrips can be made to [Ardabil](/asia/middleeast/iran/ardabil) where you find the Shaikh Safi Tomb and to salt water Lake Urmia just west of Tabriz. [Candowan](/asia/middleeast/iran/candowan) and [El-Goli](/asia/middleeast/iran/elgoli) are also nice trips.
