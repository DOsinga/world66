Just west of [Jerash](/asia/middleeast/jordan/jerash) the castle of Ajlun is another highlight of northern Jordan. The Qalat er-Rabad, as the Ajlun Castle is locally knwon, was the base of the Arab forces of Saladin, when he defeated the Crusaders in the 12th century. The Ajlun mountains are famous for their lush vegetation and thick green forests and a good place for hikes.

Close by is [Anjara](/asia/middleeast/jordan/anjara) with an old church as the must see.
