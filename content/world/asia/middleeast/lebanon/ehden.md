Ehden is located on Mount Lebanon, close to the Kadisha Gorge. It is a good base for hiking in the area, whihc is one of the most beautiful in the whole of the Middle East. 

There are a few old houses and churches to see here too, but most people will come for the natural beuaty of the region.*
