The Palestinian Authority consists parts of both the [Gaza Strip](/asia/middleeast/palestinianauthority/gazastrip) and the West Bank. They can only be visited from [Israel](/asia/middleeast/israel) due to the continuing violence.

The West Bank, consisting of Judea and Sammaria, does have a lot to see and do, though. It is a must for anyone interested in Biblical history, and are willing to risk a stone upside the head. Be it the Tomb of the Patriarchs in the midst of [Hebron](/asia/middleeast/palestinianauthority/hebron), the many sites of [Bethlehem](/asia/middleeast/palestinianauthority/bethlehem), the site of the Tabernacle outside of Shiloh, or Joseph's Tomb in Shchem (aka [Nablus](/asia/middleeast/palestinianauthority/nablus)), this is where the Old Testament occured.
