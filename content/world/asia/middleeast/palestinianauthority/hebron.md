---
title: "Hebron"
type: location
---

Hebron is quite a nice city. Unfortunately it is also a place of religious tensions due to the fact that it is seen by both Moslems and Jews as a very holy place; the graves of Abraham, Isaac, and Jacob are to be found in the old center. 

If you visit Hebron the grave of Abraham is one of the places you should not miss. It has two entrances: one for the mosque part and one for the synagogue part. Both are heavily guarded. 

The souq (or market) is a very lively place and has some good bargains. On market days you are likely to see Palestinians in traditional garb, fastened with a dynamite belt.
