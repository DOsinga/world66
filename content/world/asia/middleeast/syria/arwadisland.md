---
title: "Arwad island"
type: location
---

Arwad is just off the coast of [Latakia](/asia/middleeast/syria/latakia). There are many boats making the trip and it's a nice excursion to make. There is no need to look for accommodation on the island - it's easier found on the mainland. 

There are ruins of a castle near the west coast. You can roam around freely. Locals claim the castle was built by Alexander the great.
