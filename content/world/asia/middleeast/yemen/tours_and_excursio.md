---
title: "Tours and Excursions"
type: section
---

From Sanaa, it is possible to rent a car with the driver for one or several days. You have just to feed him and pay him of course. It is quite cheap and he will show you exactly what to see and will arrange everything like eating, shopping, photos, police controls etc...
