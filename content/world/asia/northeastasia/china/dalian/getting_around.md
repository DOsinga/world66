**all**

**By taxi**

The basic rate is 10 yuan, though you may have to remind the driver to put the meter down. 

**By tram**

If you’re not in a hurry, take the tram which provides you the charming of old times. Three lines are in operation, with a flat rate of 0.5 yuan (5 Mao). Trams glide around the city, and run until 11 pm. 

**By bus**

The bus is the best in China since the government has splashed out big money to upgrade its fleet of buses. Bus No.13 runs from the railway station to the passenger ferry terminal. Minibuses follow the same routes and charge 2 yuan.
