**all**

**By air**

Having domestic and international air connections. There’re daily flights from Beijing (1 hour and 10 minutes), Shanghai (1 hour and 35 minutes), Guangzhou (2 hours and 55 minutes), Hong Kong (3 hours) to Dalian.

**By boat**

Regular boats from Tianjin, Shanghai, Qingdao, Yantai and Weihai can take you to Dalian. Tickets can be bought at the ferry terminal in the east of Dalian, not far from the Friendship Hotel.
