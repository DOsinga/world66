As the largest provincial region in China, Xijiang covers a great area of 1,600,000 sq kilometers, with 17.2 millions people living here. It is a home to many national minorities with Han Chinese moving relocated just recently. 

   Standing on the ancient Silk Road, Xinjiang is famous for being dominated by desert, mountains, stunning lakes, green grasslands, and its ethnic religion and colorful customs. Although remote and desolate, it is still a wonderful place retaining an air of mystery, energy and enthusiasm.

 To follow the whole Silk Road is a unique experience.  [Kashgar](/asia/northeastasia/china/xinjiangprovince/kashgar) close to the  [Khunjerab pass](/asia/northeastasia/china/xinjiangprovince/khunjerabpass) is one of the main tourist attractions of the West of China. The sunday market is a great event and should not be missed.  [Urumqi](/asia/northeastasia/china/xinjiangprovince/urumqi) and  [Turpan](/asia/northeastasia/china/xinjiangprovince/turpan) are other old cities on the Silk Road that are worth a visit.
