**{Dali}**

Dali is a perfect place to tune out for a while and forget about trains, planes, and bone-jarring buses. The area has become a Mecca for travelers, but it’s easy enough to escape the crowds on the narrow backstreets lined with old stone houses. The main habitants of the region are the Bai. 

**{Lijiang}**

Lijiang is a fascinating and beautifully preserved center of culture of Naxi nationality. **{Xishuang banna}**

Xishuang banna is one of the most fascinating areas in China, home to the Dai people as well as several other minorities. It's full of beautiful countryside, remarkable wildlife and interesting villages as well as the Menghai, Mengzhe and Menghan Buddhist temples.
