Bangladesh is a very densly populated country in South Asia. It shares a very lonb border with neighbour [India](/asia/southasia/india) and if it wasn't for a short border of 25 kilomters with [Myanmar](/asia/southeastasia/myanmar) India would be the only neighbour.

 Bangladesh was the Eastern part of [Pakistan](/asia/southasia/pakistan) after the split between India and Pakistan in 1948/9. The Bengals then had a second struggle for independance with the Paki's, but now they have their own country.

 [Dhaka](/asia/southasia/bangladesh/dhaka) is the capital city of Bangladesh. It is the largest city as well. It is the best starting point for travels in the country.

 In the south, near the Myanmar border, [Chittagong](/asia/southasia/bangladesh/chittagong) is the second city. It is a pretty place with tombs and mosques to visit.

 Cox Bazar has some good beaches nearby and is the ideal place to go when you want to relax a bit.
