title: "Paro"
type: location
---

Paro is the airport of Bhutan and your most probable point of entry. It's not a bad place to start: the valley is one of the most beautiful in Bhutan. 

Among the sights are the Ta Dzong temple the Rinchen Pung Dzong temple and the Dungtse Lhakhang temple. 

Paro market has an interesting market, held in the weekend, where you can see lcal produce being sold. **
