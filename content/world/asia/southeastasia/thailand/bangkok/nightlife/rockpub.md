This is a good place for fans of Heavy Metal music. This pub is located opposite of the Asia hotel on Phayathai Rd. in walking distance of Siam Square.** Reke:** Like most live music places in thailand the guitarists kick ass, and so they do at Rock Pub. This place, though they play farang music, is a place mainly for thais. You could ask for your favourite old metal stuff, but they wont play it unless you tell a thai to ask the band. Wish there were more metal bars in BKK.
