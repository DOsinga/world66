Khao Takiab Bay is just beyond the Khao Takiab Mountain and the Golden Buddha. Many hotels and resorts such as the Hyatt Regency, Kaban Tamor, S'Mor Spa and Supatra resorts are located close to Khao Takiab Mountain but just beyond is Khao Takiab Bay with the Bluewave Hotel, Takiab beach Hotel, Anantasilla beach Resort, Luna Beach Hutes, Ocean Breeze, the Boathouse and many other small and nice accommodations.

At Khao Takiab Bay proper there are palm tress with beach loungers and beach umbrellas, also watersports plus fishing trips and boat trips. Banana Boat and Jetski. Also there is Elephant Riding and Horse Riding near the beach. You can also go cycling south along the beach towards Suan Son beach further along the coast. The water at Khao Takiab Bay is very clear and only the gentlest of waves, the beach is also very clean. beachside cafes with friendly vendors who do not trouble you.
