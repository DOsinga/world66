Hua Hin offers many choices for eating out. Hua Hin is famous for Thai and Seafood but also has restaurants offering a range of cuisine from around the world. The Fishing Pier area of central Hua Hin offers many Thai and Seafood restaurants including Cha Lay, Ketsarin restaurant, Fisherman's Wharf and the Oyster Pot. Another area especially for real Thai seafood is Khao Takiab beach, just a short 10 minute ride from the centre of Hua Hin. Here you will find famous seafood restaurants such as La Mer and Rua Rimtarn Restaurant.

Takiab Terrace Restaurant

Modern and trendy restaurant at Khao Takiab Bay offering Thai, Seafood and International cuisine. Excellent presentation at very reasonable prices. Fusian Asian cuisine plus steaks and grills. 60's/70's music with friendly staff.
