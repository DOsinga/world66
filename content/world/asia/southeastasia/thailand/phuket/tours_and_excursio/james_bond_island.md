title: "James Bond Island"
type: poi
---

Near Phuket Island, in Pang Nga Bay, the island where "The Man With the Golden Gun" was filmed can be visited. This James Bond Film from 1972, starred Roger Moore. (Click on the subtitle for an image)

It is a whole day or half day excursion. You will also visit a large cave Temple with many fine Buddha Images, inhabited by macaques, and a Muslim village on a small island. If you take the whole day excursion, the trip will also include a canoe trip.
