Great Songkhla Lake

Thailand's largest lake. You can visit two wildfowl sanctuaries. The Khu Khut Waterfowl Park is located near the city of Songhkla. Take the highway No. 4107. There you can hire a boat. It is a great place to visit for bird-watchers. The other one is called Nok Nam Thale Noi and is located at the north-east of the lake (near Phattalung).** thomas t.k.m:** great thailand ;)
