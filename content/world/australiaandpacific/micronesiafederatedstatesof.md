Spread across 2,500 kilometers of the Western Central Pacific Ocean, just north of the equator, the four states of [Chuuk](/australiaandpacific/micronesiafederatedstatesof/chuuk), [Kosrae](/australiaandpacific/micronesiafederatedstatesof/kosrae), [Pohnpei](/australiaandpacific/micronesiafederatedstatesof/pohnpei) and [Yap](/australiaandpacific/micronesiafederatedstatesof/yap) and their 607 islands (only 65 are inhabited) form the Federated States of Micronesia (FSM).

 The FSM combine a great climate, paradiselike islands and a laid back atmosphere. On the islands you have the cahnce to see a traditional village dance, take a hike to a secluded lake or waterfall, or make a dive beneath the sea to a coral laden shipwreck.
