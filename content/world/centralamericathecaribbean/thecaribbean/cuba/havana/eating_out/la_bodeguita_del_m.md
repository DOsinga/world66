Havana's La Bodeguita del Medio has long been known as a wonderful gathering place for cocktails, cigars, and the local cuisine. 

It used to be the place where Hemmingway sat daily and sipped his mohito's. Now it's crammed with tourists wanting to taste the same mohito's. If you like paying trice the normal mohito price for seeing a couple of Hemmingway photo's, this is just the place for you.
