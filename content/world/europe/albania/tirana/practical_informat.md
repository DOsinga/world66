GENERAL DESCRIPTION. 

BECAUSE OF THE MEDITERRANEAN CLIMATE, THE COASTAL REGIONS OF ALBANIA EXPERIENCE MILD, WET WINTERS WITH DRY, WARM SUMMERS. 

MOUNTAIN AREAS DIFFER SINCE THEY ARE SUBJECTED TO A MORE CONTINENTAL CLIMATE, THEY EXPERIENCE HOT SUMMERS AND COLD WINTERS. PRECIPITATION IS QUITE HEAVY WITH 55IN/1,400MM PER YEAR. COAST: SUMMERS MAY BE QUITE HUMID BUT ARE RARELY TOO HOT, BECAUSE OF THE PLEASANT SEA BREEZE. WINTER CAN BE MILD BUT ALSO MAY BE COLD BECAUSE OF OCCASIONAL NORTHERN/EASTERN WINDS. MOUNTAIN AND INLAND AREAS EXPERIENCE MUCH THE SAME CONDITIONS AS THE COAST BUT WITH COOLER, LESS HUMID SUMMERS. 

CLOTHING : IN SUMMER LIGHTWEIGHT CLOTHING IS RECOMMENDED, FOR EXAMPLE COTTON.
