**Horse and carriage rides**

You can find these carriages everywhere in the town. You can start at Market square or the Begijnhof. Price: €27,50 for up to 5 people.

**Ramparts**

Take a walk along the ramparts which surround the city. A full circle of the city is about 9 KM.
