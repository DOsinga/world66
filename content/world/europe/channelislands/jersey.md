Jersey is the biggest of the Channel islands. It is actually more easily reached by boat from St. Malo in France than from the United Kingdom. It is beautiful Island with a relaxed and friendly lifestyle - where clear air, the clean sparkling sea, and the spectacular scenery set it apart from any other holiday destination.

 From the broad, sandy bays of the south to the rocky cliffs of the north, from the quiet country lanes to the bustling streets of the Island's capital St.Helier, you'll find plenty to entertain, amuse and utterly delight you. 

 It is packed with historic sites, fascinating museums and world-class attractions, although these can never eclipse Jersey's unique natural beauty. Whatever the month, whatever the weather, Jersey is a destination that everyone can enjoy.
