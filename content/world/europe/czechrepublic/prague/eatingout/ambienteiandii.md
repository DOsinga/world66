Open: Mon.- Fri. 11-24, Sat., Sun., 16-24. Both Ambiente restaurants positively ooze with atmosphere. Eccentric, warm colours and strange bits and pieces lying around. The above restaurant No. 1 in Prague 2 must have a specially designed extraction system from the kitchen in the basement that pumps excruciatingly pleasant smells into the street outside. The food is interesting, with ribs, pastas, grilled chicken salads and other enticing dishes. The hot chocolate fondue is also rather good. Prices are low for the quality which is reasonably consistent.
