International kitchen, open daily 12-16 and 18-23. The champagne Sunday brunch between 11.30 and 15.30 is the best value in town. For 1300 crowns (around 8 euros) you help yourself to a sumptious buffet of hot and cold dishes, everything from caviar to hand made chocolates while the waiters keep your champagne glass constantly topped up.
