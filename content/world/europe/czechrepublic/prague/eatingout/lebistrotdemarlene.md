Open: Mon.-Fri. 12.00 - 14.30, 19.30 - 22.15, Sat. 19.30 - 22.15. One of the best restaurants in Prague. Often described as country style cuisine, I have never had food like this in the country. The food is fresh and imaginative and the service is good. The atmosphere is pleasant even though it is possible that you will find yourself sitting on a kind of metal picnic chair. They probably won't take you without a reservation here, so don't forget. Reasonable prices too, at around 750 Kc per person. The lamb is usually out of this world and the starters all good, especially the mushroom flan.
