Open: Daily. 12.00 - 01.00

This is a very attractive restaurant that has three faces, French, Italian and Czech. The Czech part exists in a baronial hall type of setting with the foundations of Charles Bridge forming part of the structure. The food, if slightly pricey for such fare, is nicely presented and well cooked. If you want to sample Czech food at its best and in interesting surroundings, then this is the place for you. (AMEX, MC, V)
