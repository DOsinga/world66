Pälkäne is a municipality in [Tampere region (Pirkanmaa)](/europe/finland/tampere). It has abt.
4300 inhabitants. There are many big lakes in Pälkäne. The biggest ones
are Pälkänevesi and Mallasvesi. These lakes are connected by river **Kostianvirta**, which was formed in 1604. Kostianvirta is famous for a *Battle of Kostianvirta*
between the Russian and Swedish armies. The Swedish army was defeated,
and Finland was occupied by the Russian 8 years afterwards.
