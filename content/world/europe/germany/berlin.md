I was in Berlin this summer, and I have to say there is no city in Europe that parties harder than Berlin. The nightlife of most cities is tame (in some instances lame) in comparison to this city. There are lots of great clubs and dance parlors - ask around for the best places. 

The Berliners were friendly and extremely helpful. Most of the usual good places to go are in West Berlin, but I found the eastern part of the city more fascinating than the western part. Berlin has an amazing number of sights, though it lacks the charm of Paris as a city. The citizens of Berlin more than make up for this, though.
