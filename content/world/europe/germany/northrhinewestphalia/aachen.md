Aachen is located close to the border with [Netherlands](/europe/netherlands) and Belgim. It shares an airport with [Maastricht](/europe/netherlands/maastricht) and [Liege](/europe/belgium/liege). It's a small city which reached its peak during the 9-th centurty when Charlemagne made it his capital. 

Sights in town include the Aachener Dom, a cathedral built especially for Charlemagne.
