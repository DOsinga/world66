Bonn is a pretty university town in the West of Germany. It was the capital of West-Germany before the fall of the wall. 

Bonn actually consists of a number of villages along the Rhine and has quite a laid back village like athmosphere.

It has a few very interesting museums on Germany history and is a nice daytrip from [Cologne](/europe/germany/northrhinewestphalia/cologne).
