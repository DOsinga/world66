Trier's pedestrian (and shopping) zone, beginning at the Porta Nigra and extending far beyond Main Market, offers a wide range of department stores, speciality and souvenir shops. Coins, including Roman coins - many found in the Trier area - are available not only in antique shops but also at Sparkasse Trier, Simeonstrasse, and the Tourist Information. Other typical Trier gifts/souvenirs can be found in the Craftsman's Court (Kunsthandwerkerhof, Simeonstiftplatz 2, next to the Porta Nigra). 

For Trier is the shopping mall of a larger vicinity - est. about 300 000 people - it offers a quite good range of shops. For any reasons Trier has quite a lot of shoe shops in the center ... 

Most businesses in Trier are open: Monday - Friday from 9:30 a.m. - 6:30 p.m. (8:30 on Thursdays), Saturday 9:30 a.m. - 1:00/2:00 p.m.; on the first Saturday of the month hours are extended to 4:00 p.m. (April-September) or 6:00 p.m. (October-March).
