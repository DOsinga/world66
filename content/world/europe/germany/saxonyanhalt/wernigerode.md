---
title: "Wernigerode"
type: location
---

Wernigerode is a charming town in the Harz mountains. Wernigerode became a city in the 13-th century and it is from that period that the main attractions, the **City Castle** (Schloss) and the ** Rathaus**date.

In the old center you will find many more old houses that are worth checking out. The markt is a good place to start exploring the center. After walking around for some time, you should head for Cafe Wien, a typical German bakery, in a house that's over 400 years old.

 Wernigerode is an ideal base for exploring the Harz. People who like trains should take a ride on the metre gauge rail, it's a great way of seeing some of the Harz.
