---
title: "Getting Around"
type: section
---

There is hardly need for a getting around section for Europe. There are hardly any travel restrictions, all countries in Western Europe have a good infrastructure. Boats, fast trains and buses can take you anywhere. Hitchhiking is good, particulary so in Germany. In the East thing are a bit harder. In some former communist countries they would still like you to tell them were you go and what you do from day to day. It is in the Caucasus that things really get exciting and you have to keep an eye on political developments in order to know if the beaches of Baku and surroundings are safe.
