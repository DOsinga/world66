---
title: "Getting There"
type: section
---

To get to Ardee from Dublin you would take the N2 through Ashbourne, then through Slane, then through Collon and the next town you reach is Ardee. When you come into Ardee there is a Church on your left hand side after the church take a right hand turn and Sip & Surf is situated on the left hand side of that road (Williams Street).
