Sean na Mbads

Intimate rustic pub at the end of a winding country road, a mere 30 mins drive from the city centre. Marvellous pub food (with extensive menu) plus a restaurant with the most superb home made fare. Great pub and wonderfully reasonable grub. Say hello to Ben, the resident black labrador. Take a walk on the beach at Rocky Bay and have a hot toddy by the fire in winter or sit out in the delightful garden during those hazy days of summer....PERFECT
