Just short off the main shopping street in Taormina, this superb restaurant can be found. 

The wine list is generous. But as always, don't order wine which is only made from Sicilian grapes. Try to find someone that is blended with any of the french grapes. 

The restaurant has got its name from the dragon tree in the garden.
