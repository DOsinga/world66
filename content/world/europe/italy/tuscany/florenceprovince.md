  The province of Florence has one major attraction: Florence, Firenze in Italian, the city of Art. No one should miss it. But this region has many other treasures waiting for the visitor.

 We have divided the province in different smaller regions. Each of these is worth a visit. It is possible to visit the different regions form the capital of Florence, but many of the smaller towns have excellent accommodation, sometimes in the most beautiful of settings. You may need more than two weeks.
