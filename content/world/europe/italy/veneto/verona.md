Verona is a great city in northern Italy. It is close to [Milan](/europe/italy/lombardia/milan) and the Garda lake. [Venice](/europe/italy/veneto/venice) is also within easy reach.

When you enter the old city of Verona the roman theater is the first sight you pass. It is huge and in summer you can go and see Operas performed here. The whole old city is filled with beautiful Renaissance buildings. You can wander around for hours.

The house of Juliette is one of the major tourist spots, although it is very doubtful that this is where she lived. What is most interesting is the wall where lovers from all over the world have written their names.

From Verona you can make daytrips to the Garda Lake and even Venice is doable. **
