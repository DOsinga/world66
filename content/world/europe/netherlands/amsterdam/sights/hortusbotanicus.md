Great place to relax. You can have coffee and tea in the Orangerie. Entrance is free if you have a student card.

The Hortus serves good food too. If you are looking for a place to lie down on the grass you'd better visit the Wertheim park opposite the Hortus. For exotic plants the hortus is the right place indeed.
