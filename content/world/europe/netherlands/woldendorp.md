Woldendorp is a rural village in the north of the Netherlands, close to Delfzijl. The area has a quiet rural charm. Flat land with long canals. 

Main sight in town is the 13-th century church. The Termunterzijldiep offers good walks along the canal. From Woldendorp you can take beautiful bicycle routes such as the Dollardroute and the Oldambtroute. 

For accommodation you'll find in Woldendorp one Hotel and one Bed and Breakfast .

Woldendorp is close to the Waddenzee, where you can find numbers of waterbirds.
