The whole trip passes through splendid scenery, the amount visible depending much on the time of year. Even in good weather it is possible to select some days as having particularly outstanding scenery. These are selected below:-

[Day 9 - Island delight!

This day starts at
Harstad](/world/europe/norway/hurtigruten/sights/day_9__island_del) and ends st Stamsund in the
[Wadden islands](/europe/netherlands/waddenislands)after passing through major parts of both these islands and [first] the Vesterålen Islands. Everything is a thrill from the bird rocks to the narrow navigation channels to the bridges, to the small towns, to the fabulous though short Trollsfjord to the Lofotens.
