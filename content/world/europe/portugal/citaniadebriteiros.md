---
title: "Citania de briteiros"
type: location
---

This is a most difficult site to find but well worth the visit of you are interested in Iron Age/ Roman History. The site interpretation is almost non existant but a new visitor centre is under construction. The views from the top are spectacular/ It is alleged that there is a lot of information on the site at the museum in Guimares. Whilst I could find Guimares, the Museum was impossible.
