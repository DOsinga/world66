Actually we can't say that there is nothing to see in Turda. It is famous for its salt mine (Salina Turda), whose origins date back to the Roman times. It is a magnificent salt mine, which is worth visit it. It is true that it is not o well promoted (unfortunately Romanians still have to work on this chapter) but it is relly worth seeing it. Sometimes concerts are held insie the mine. The constant temperature is 10 ° C. www.iquebec.com/
