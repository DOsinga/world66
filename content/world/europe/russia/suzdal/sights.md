**Sights**

  **Suzdal** has a gerat number of ancient sites to visit and learn more about the develpment of the Russian History.

 There is a small settlement Kideksha not far from Suzdal. Once it had been the summer residence of Prince Yuri Dolgoruky, the founder of Moscow.

The Church of Boris and Gleb, the monument of Vladimir-Suzdal architecture is located in the settlement.
