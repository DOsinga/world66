**pizza places** 

- Pizzeria Foculus 

Gregorciceva 3, tel 215 643 

Open until midnight. 

*Classic pizzas, and a well-stocked salad bar. Good location, very near the National and University Library (NUK). Could be crowded.* 

- Pizzeria Cerin 

Trubarjeva 52, tel 320 990 

Open until 11pm. 

*Classic pizzas, very nice place, nice atmosphere. At the north end of the old town, on the banks of Ljubljanica River.* 

**fast food** 

*All major fast food establishments are located in the city center, near the main Post office on the corner of Copova and Slovenska street. It's a couple hundred meters from Presernov square and the Tripple bridge towards Tivoli park.*
