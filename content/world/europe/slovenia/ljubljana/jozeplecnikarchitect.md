Jože Plečnik (1872-1957) is the most important Slovene Architect. He worked in Vienna, Prague (reconstruction of Hradcani castle complex) and Ljubljana. In Ljubljana Plecnik can be seen everywhere, some of his works:

- Banks of Ljubljanica river

All bridges across Ljubljanica in city center:

- Karlovski most

- Čevljarski most (Cobbler's bridge)

- Tromostovje (Triple bridge)

- Zmajski most (Dragon bridge)

buildings

- National and University Library

- Križanke monastery

- Market place

and many others.

*When walking through Ljubljana you can recognize Plecnik's works by his style. All of above listed sights are in city center and even if you try you won't succeed missing any of them*
