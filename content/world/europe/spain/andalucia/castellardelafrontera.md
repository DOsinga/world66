---
title: "Castellar de la Frontera"
type: location
---

Castellar de la Frontera is the first white town and one that may warrant a brief look, if you have time. However, the village has a strange feel to it. The inhabitants of the village were moved to another village and many affluent foreigners moved into Castellar de la Frontera during a relocation scheme in 1971. Understandably, some suspicion remains.
