title: "Cueva de la Pileta"
type: poi
---

The prehistoric Cueva de la Pileta is on the route between
[Gaucin](/europe/spain/andalucia/gaucin) and
[Ronda](/europe/spain/andalucia/ronda). By train, it’s an hour’s walk from the Benaojan-Montejaque station. If you are in a car, take the road to Benaojan and follow the signs. There are remarkable pre-historic paintings of animals in a series of caverns, dating from around 25,000 BC. The walk takes an hour and is in Spanish. Visitors carry there own lanterns. It can be chilly, so bring a sweater!
