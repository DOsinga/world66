---
title: "Plaza de los Naranjos"
type: poi
---

It is a beautiful square in typical Andalusian style with some Moorish influences, and its name comes from the many orange-trees growing there. Have a look at its 15th century Renaissance fountain, the 16th century town-hall and the 17th century Casa del Corregidor. Starting from here you may have a walk through the romantic narrow streets in between white houses with balconies decorated with flowers. Below Avenida del Mar, you will find a beautiful promenade along the sea.
