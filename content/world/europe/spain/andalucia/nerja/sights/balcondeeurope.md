The Balcony of Europe is the major landmarks of Nerja – a superb, palm-lined promenade that runs along a towering cliff. Views from here are simply stunning.  ** BRUCE O'MARA:** My wife and I stayed there a few weeks ago and were very impressed. The prices were very attractive for a double room and the staff were very helpful, recommending tourist attractions such as the local caves and nearby smaller villages that we would find interesting. We tried our faulty Spanish, and were greeted in helpful English. It is particularly well situated with excellent and stunning views of the coast and short walking distance to little bars and cafes. Although easy to find and central this large 4 Star hotel is set back from the street on a square away from the noise.
