By road, 150 km from Lerida, 307 km from Barcelona and 322 km from Madrid.

 Aviaco flies from Madrid twice a day, Monday to Friday, and once on Saturdays and Sundays. There are also Aviaco flights from Barcelona and Jerez every day except Saturdays; from Paris on Tuesdays, Thursdays and Sundays; and from London on Mondays, Wednesdays and Fridays.
