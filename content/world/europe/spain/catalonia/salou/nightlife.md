---
title: "Nightlife and Entertainment"
type: section
---

The nightlife of Salou is justifiably famous and throughout the year the different bars, pubs, discos, etc. guarantee a good time for all. For those looking for a more relaxing way of spending the evening, the pavement terraces of Salou's cafes and ice cream parlours provide a tranquil alternative. These establishments can be found in every corner of the resort. The area around Murillo and Carles Buigas Streets is known as the Slammers, because of its multitude of lively music bars popular with the youngest visitors. But there are pubs and discotheques catering for all types of people and all tastes, giving everybody, whatever their age, the chance to spend some unforgettable nights in Salou.
