title: "Ciudad Rodrigo"
type: location
---

Ciudad Rodrigo is basically a frontier city although it is still some way to Portugal. A trip from Salamanca is a welcome change from the touristic frenzy which sometimes dominates Salamanca.

Just inside the city walls from the bus stop, the Cathedral has some fine sculpture around the bases of the columns in the cloisters. However it is above all a city for just strollling, enjoying the narrow streets and the fine buildings. You can enjoy trying to count the stone sculpted coats of arms which abound in the city. The Plaza Mayor, whereas it does not hold a candle to that of Salamanca in grandeur, is very pleasant for a person-watching break over a cup of coffee.
