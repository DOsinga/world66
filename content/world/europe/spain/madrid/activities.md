**Activitys**

**Ballooning**

If you want to see Madrid from a hot air balloon, you should just phone to Pasajeros del viento, tel. 91-815-07-13 for reservation. It costs 20000 Pts for adults and 10000 Pts for children. 

**Golf**

If you want to play golf, the addresses for contact are El Olivar de la Hinojosa, Campo de las Naciones, tel. 91-721-18-89 and Villamadrid, Moncloa 2, tel. 91-550-20-10. 

**Flamenco en Vivo**

Finally, I could recommend a spontaneous Flamenco en Vivo from Monday to Saturday from ten o’clock in the evening until the early morning.  

address: c/Caja Baja 34

tel: 91-365-5264.

**Perfect walking, tapas and wine Tour**

I recommend the Old Restaurants in Madrid Tour, fantastic!!! Look for them by searching on Google for "Old Madrid"
