**

Restaurants **

Madrid is also better known as the capital of restaurants. You’ll find them on every street corner, whether specialised in the typical Spanish cuisine or in one of the many others. Most restaurants are pleasant places with an excellent kitchen where you can already have a menu for about €8.- to €12.-. The most typical Spanish plates follow the Mediterranean diet, including seafood, olive and a lot of fruits and legumes.
