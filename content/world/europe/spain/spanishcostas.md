The Spanish mediterranean coast is blessed with great beaches & a good climate. 

Right from the Spanish border, the beaches and towns of Catalonia are perfect for holidays - see the [Catalonia](/europe/spain/catalonia) section for full details. In the center the province of Murcia has many great beaches as well: the famous city [Benidorm](/europe/spain/spanishcostas/benidorm) is mostly visited by elderly people; it's a perfect place to stay in wintertime. Also of great interest is the town [Elche](/europe/spain/spanishcostas/elche), 20 km away from [Alicante](/europe/spain/spanishcostas/alicante), the air gateway to the Costa Blanca. Last but not least the city of [Murcia](/europe/spain/spanishcostas/murcia) itself is worth a visit as well. 

If you go further south, xxploring the villages and the coastline between Algeciras and [Cadiz](/europe/spain/andalucia/cadiz) - the Costa de la Luz - is a fantastic journey through rolling hills, relaxed villages, and remote stretches of sand. The view across the straits is beautiful, even on a less than clear day, as Morocco’s small villages and mosques peak out from the hills across the strait. [Tarifa](/europe/spain/andalucia/tarifa) is famous for wind-surfing, [Conil](/europe/spain/andalucia/conil) has an enjoyable atmosphere with yellow sand stretching around a beautiful bay. [Vejer de la Frontera](/europe/spain/andalucia/vejerdelafrontera), is a classical looking Moorish town, where until ten years ago, the women wore long cloaks that veiled their faces, much like their Arab neighbors. Cadiz, one of the oldest settlements in Spain, is a relaxed port town with well-preserved architecture from the eighteenth century. Cadiz is the place to be for Semana Santa (Easter week), with fantastic religious processions and a carnavalesque atmosphere. [Puerto de Santa Maria](/europe/spain/andalucia/puertodesantamaria), Rota (a US base) and [Chipiona](/europe/spain/andalucia/chipiona) are resort towns with beautiful beaches and clear waters. Sanlúcar has a shell-encrusted river beach, which is usually deserted with very warm waters.
