Kastanjeg 9.  Tel: 046-14 44 44.  Open daily 1-6 p.m. by arrangement. Research library, stamp appraisals, etc.

Finng. 2.  Tel: 046-222 72 83.  Open Tue-Sat noon-4 p.m., Sun 1-5 p.m.  Wed during semester 6:30-8:30 p.m.  Sketches and models of public art in Scandinavia and other countries.

Kiliansg 10.  Tel: 046-14 01 05.  Open Fri 1-3 p.m. Group visits by arrangement.  Exhibit with local minerals from the entire world and  typical Scanian minerals.
