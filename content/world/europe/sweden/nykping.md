---
title: "Nyköping"
type: location
---

Nyköping is one of the oldest towns in Sweden. It was founded 1183 and was for some time in Swedish history the capitol for this nation. Today it is a good example of Swedish mid size city. About 35 000 people live in the city centre and 50 000 in the municipal. A walk through Nyköping is well spent time. It is a nice place with beautiful surroundings.
