It is the coast which comes to mind first when considering this area and probably deservedly so. However we should not forget the wonderful cathedrals at
[Exeter](/europe/unitedkingdom/england/bristol_plymouth/devon/exeter),
[Wells](/northamerica/unitedstates/maine/wells),
Plymouth and the South-West,
[Salisbury](/europe/unitedkingdom/england/bristol_plymouth/salisbury) and
[Gloucester](/europe/unitedkingdom/england/bristol_plymouth/gloucestershire/gloucester). 

There are two national parks in the area,
[Dartmoor](/europe/unitedkingdom/england/bristol_plymouth/devon/dartmoor) and Exmoor, and a new one, the New Forest, is about to be created. There are splendid gardens open to the public and prehistoric remains of Bronze [Dartmoor] and Iron [Dorset] Ages and there are the Scilly Isles.
