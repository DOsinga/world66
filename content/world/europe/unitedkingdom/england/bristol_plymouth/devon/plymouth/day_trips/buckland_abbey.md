This is equally easy to visit from
[Tavistock](/europe/unitedkingdom/england/bristol_plymouth/devon/tavistock). It is situated in a
[Dartmoor](/europe/unitedkingdom/england/bristol_plymouth/devon/dartmoor) setting near Yelverton. 

Built as a Cistercian monastery in the 13th century it ceased to be occupied by monks during the 16th century dissolution. It then became a house and at was owned at different times by Sir Richard Grenville and Sir Francis Drake. 

The building has seen many additions and changes since Tudor times and is now a museum.
