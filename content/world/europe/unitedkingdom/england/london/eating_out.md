England may not be famous for its food, but London sure does have a range of very good restaurants catering for every appetite.

 If you are looking for Middle Eastern or Indian food, one of the best area's is in Bayswater, just North of Kensington Garden. Soho is of course the best place for Chinese (or Thai). Italian restaurants can be found all over the place.
