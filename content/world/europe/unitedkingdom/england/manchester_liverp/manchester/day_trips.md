[Walking in the Peak District

East of Manchester lies the Peak District, the hilly spine of England. From Manchester there are several possibilities to visit it. It takes an hour on the train from Manchester Piccadilly Station to Buxton. Get out at any little station on the way and within twenty minutes you are on the moors or in the fields. For wild nature take the train to Edale( be prepared for mud) For more rural landscapes go to Buxton or Whaley Bridge. The view at Monsal Head (Great Longstone, near Tideswell) is fantastic on a sunny day. For something of both you could try Hebden Bridge (in West Yorkshire, not Peak District). A good map is useful to find the little footpaths that make England such a great country for walking.
