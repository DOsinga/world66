---
title: "Getting Around"
type: section
---

The centre of Manchester is walkable. If you like to go to places like the Withworth Gallery, just south of the centre, you can hop on the frequent buses any time of day.  Oxford road is the spine of the student life, and many bus companies fight for the students favour.

  For further out you should take the bus from Piccadilly Gardens, there is a travel centre there (office hours) that can help you. There is also a tramline going north-south from Bury to Altrincham. Also the train can be a option; there are many local trains that stop in suburban Manchester. Although, frankly, there is no reason to visit those. (I know, I lived it)
