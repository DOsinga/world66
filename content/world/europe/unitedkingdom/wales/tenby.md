Tenby is one of those old fashion beach resorts. There is a lot of
glitter and glamour here, but it is all done in a rather stylish way.
Although it is mostly popular with retired people, there are quite a
few activities for the young as well. In summer the town is really
crowded and it is wise to book ahead.

The city goes back to the 9-th century and because of the
strategic importance many castles where built in the area. Little
England beyond Wales, as the area south of the Landsker Line is known
has 16 big castles alone.

Tenby is an excellent base for doing some walks along the Pembrokeshire Coast Path.

Tenby is a great holiday place. But also a great place to live.
even though many teenager you ask would probably say it sucked!
but i wouldn't want to live any where else (and i doubt they would
either).

Tenby great because its got shops, bars, pub, cafes,
resturants, hotels, B&B's, park, a leisure centre, beaches(ITS
GOT FOUR!!) churches, castles and loads, loads more.

Tiny Tenby even has got a massive FOUR schools which includes
one of the best senior schools in the area ( although most of the
students would probably disagree). All in all i think Tenby is a great
place and keeps on changing and getting better, so i say get down here
and take a look for yourselves.
