50's style diner with home cooked meals "like Mom used to make." 45's play on the juke box. The soda jerk prepares old fashioned milk shakes, banana Kongs and floats. Meatloaf, roast turkey, roast beef, grilled salmon, chili and meal Caesar, plus homemade burgers and deep-dish apple pie. Hearty breakfasts from 8 am. Hot and cold sandwiches at lunch. Most meals $7-$12. Licensed. Tel: 1 403 762-5529 Address: 221 Banff Ave, Banff AB T0L 0C0
