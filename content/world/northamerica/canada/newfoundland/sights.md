Gros Morne National Park

Gros Morne National Park was declared a World Heritage Site in 1987. The park is home to 20 species of land mammals (including moose, black bear, fox, caribou and beaver), over 230 species of birds, 11 species of fish, 2 amphibian species, 417 species of bryophytes and 711 species of vascular plants. One study estimates that over 7700 moose live in the park. For more information on the park, including a picture tour of park features, visit Gros Morne Park National Park.
