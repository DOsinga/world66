Mississauga is Toronto's western neighbour and Canada's fastest growing city with a population in excess of 600,000. Headquarters to many Canadian businesses, including Microsoft Canada and GE Canada, Mississuaga's combination of central location and excellent transportation infrastructure serve as magnets to both industry and residents. Home of Pearson International Airport, Air Canada's primary hub, Mississauga is a prime port of entry for both tourists and business personnel coming to Canada. More information can be found at the City's website at http://www.mississauga.ca/portal/discover.
