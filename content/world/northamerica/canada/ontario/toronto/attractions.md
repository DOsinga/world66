Toronto possesses a diversity of interesting places to visit. 

Go downtown and experience the nightlife on a Friday night. There are many bars, lounges, nightclubs and cafes to cater to your needs. You can have a great time anywhere on Adelaide, or Richmond St. Dont forget to Speak your mind at SPEAKERS CORNER Much Music Building on Queen St & John St . Its a very diverse city to explore .There are ethnic areas such as 'Little Italy and Portugal, Not to mention the Greeks on Danforth Ave.

CN Tower .

Located in the heart of Downtown Toronto, the world's tallest building (553 metres) stands between the SkyDome and the Metro Toronto Convention Centre on Front Street, just north of Bremner Blvd. Near the top, a 24 sq. metre glass floor allows visitors to watch the streets under their feet.
