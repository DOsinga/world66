Alaska is very beautiful and has so many things to do and places to go. From the highest peaks of **Mt.McKinley** to the miles of coastline that seem to go on forever.

Everywhere you turn there are thickly wooded areas teaming with
wildlife of all types. Free, majestic eagles soar overhead and the
Aurora Borealis dance across the sky.

One of the most important events is the **Iditarod Dog Sled Race**, a race that runs from Anchorage to Nome every year.
