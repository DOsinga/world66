Sedona was voted "The Most Beautiful Place in America" by USA's
Weekend's Annual Travel Report. Sedona is situated in a unique
geological area, it is surrounded by red-rock monoliths, and is close
to Oak Creek Canyon.

Towering above Sedona is Mogollon Rim, which constitutes the
southwestern boundary of the vast Colorado Plateau, is home to the
largest contiguous stand of ponderosa pine in the world. The plateau
extends into parts of five other states.

Also close by is the Coconino National Forest, which encompasses seven wilderness areas.

Sedona combines luxurious resorts, bed & breakfasts, and
small shops. Impressive art galleries and fine restaurants are nestled
in an unusually rugged canyon surrounded by an expansive national
forest.
