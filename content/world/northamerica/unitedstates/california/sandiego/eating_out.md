In this section I will try to give you the traveler some ideas on interesting places to eat in the county. Most of these I consider to be reasonably priced and offer some really tasty food to tempt your taste buds. So come with me as we head to our table for some delectable delights, refreshing beverages, and good conversation.

[Fillippis Pizza Grotto

Located not far from downtown in the area known locally as Little Italy is an old yet very charming authentic Italian eatery. Fillippis is not an ordinary restaurant. It is located in an old storefront. And in fact as you go through the front door, you first encounter the quaint little Italian store filled with all the great smells and items you would actually find if you were in Italy! As you make your way through the store, you will finally reach the unpretentious restaurant with its red checkerboard tablecloths. The food is very good. Lasagna, veal, spaghetti and meatballs, submarine sandwiches, anitpasto salads, minestrone soup, and of course mouthwatering pizza! So if you are hungry for hearty italian dishes then Fillippis is for you! more..](/world/northamerica/unitedstates/california/sandiego/eatingout/fillippispizzagrotto)
