'Put your make up on & fix your hair up pretty, and meet me tonight in Atlantic City!' Bruce Springsteen knows - he was born and raised in New Jersey - that New Jersey is the best place to have fun East of
[Las Vegas](/northamerica/unitedstates/nevada/lasvegas). And since Vegas does not have the ccean so close by, you might as well head for Atlantic City straight away.

But there is of course more than just casinos - there is the Lighthouse at the entrance of town, there are excellent golf courses nearby and there is a lot of nightlife, bars, pubs, clubs, you name it.

For people with kids, Storybook land is a good option at only 25 km from the City. Rides, playground, picnic area and zoo can be found in this children's theme park. Kids can walk through 50 buildings and displays.
