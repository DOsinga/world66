New Jersey is just south of
[New York](/northamerica/unitedstates/newyorkstate/newyork), just 'cross the river to the Jersey side' as Jersey born Bruce Springsteen sings. The state has a lot of things to explore: A big part of the Apalachian trail, the
[Delaware Water Gap](/northamerica/unitedstates/newjersey/delawarewatergap), an exciting coast with
[Atlantic City](/northamerica/unitedstates/newjersey/atlanticcity) - the gambling capital of the East Coast - and
[Great Egg Harbor](/northamerica/unitedstates/newjersey/greateggharbor) as two very diverse highlights.

[Trenton](/northamerica/unitedstates/newjersey/trenton) the capital is located right in the centre of the state and is a good base for exploring the region.

In
[West Orange](/northamerica/unitedstates/newjersey/westorange) you can visit the old laboratory of Thomas Edison whose inventions changed the lives of millions of people all over the world.
