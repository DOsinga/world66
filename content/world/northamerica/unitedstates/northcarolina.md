North Carolina is a state full of wonderful things to visit. From the
[Smoky Mountains](/northamerica/unitedstates/tennessee/smokymountains) in the west of the state, to the beaches in near
[Cape Lookout](/northamerica/unitedstates/northcarolina/capelookout), Cape Hatteras or near
[Fort Raleigh](/northamerica/unitedstates/northcarolina/fortraleigh) there is a lot to discover. 



[Charlotte](/northamerica/unitedstates/northcarolina/charlotte) and
[Raleigh](/northamerica/unitedstates/northcarolina/raleigh) are the biggest cities in North Carolina. Both are rich in history and deserve a visit of at least of few days.
[Wilmington](/northamerica/unitedstates/delaware/wilmington) on the Cape Fear coast is an ideal base for a beach holiday as well as a treasure of 19-th century architecture. 



[Charlotte](/northamerica/unitedstates/northcarolina/charlotte) is the largest city in North Carolina.
