South Dakota has much more to offer than most people think. Most of the people who get to see some of what it has to offer are traveling through the state on Interstate 90. I have been through South Dakota many times. Headed west through S. Dakota is
[Sioux Falls](/northamerica/unitedstates/southdakota/siouxfalls) which is supposed to be one of the safest cities in the states. More westward in Chamberlain is a beautiful place also along the Missouri River, Nice rest area in this area to take a break and take a short hike to rest your legs. Southwest S. Dakota In The
[Black Hills](/northamerica/unitedstates/southdakota/blackhills), offers a lot for the tourist or the people and family's passing through the state.You may remember in history class the pictures of
[Mount Rushmore](/northamerica/unitedstates/southdakota/mountrushmore), but being there seeing it upclose is a whole different story.
