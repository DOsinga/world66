**Festivals**

**New Year's Celebration**

The Copacobana beach is also is home to Rio's famous New Year's celebration. It attracts over 2 million people from all over the world! Following a local tradition, most people dress in white for good luck. Offering a white flower to Yemanjá, the goddess of the seas, is also a part of the ritual [more..](/southamerica/brazil/riodejaneiro/festivals/newyearscelebration)

**Carnival**

Carnival in Rio is the only real Carnival. Everybody is out dancing in the streets. Participants plan (and save up for) their colorful exquisite costumes for a year (though some costumes seem to be little more than glitter and one or two sequins). The best costumes can be seen on Sunday and Monda [more..](/southamerica/brazil/riodejaneiro/festivals/carnival)
