title: "Quito"
type: location
---

Quito is one of the highest capitals in the world, at an elevation of more than 2,000 meter. Big mountains, including an active volcano loom over the city. 

 Quito is not the biggest city in Ecuador (that's [Guayaquil](/southamerica/ecuador/guayaquil)) but is the main transport hub, so you will spend some time here. It is a very relaxing place and the best place in South America for studying Spanish. 

 The Old town of Quito is really special and should not be missed. It is full with museums, churches and other sights. It is also an area full of life. Lots of small shops and eateries make it pleasant to stroll around.
