There are many tour operators and tour companies in South America offering all types of activities. From multisport to cultural expeditions. Many shoestring travellers prefer to do things on their own, which sometimes is the best way to go, but many times we missout on unique attractions that could have easily been pointed out by a local expert.

South America tours should be oriented to enhancing the experience of the traveller, hotel and cruise bookings are easier by the day by visiting the root suppliers website, but guided treks, mountain biking, family stays, jungle expeditions and other off the path tours are best done in company of local experts.

So if you want something more than a beach vacation or a city tour in South America, check out a tour and see if it can enhance your experience.
