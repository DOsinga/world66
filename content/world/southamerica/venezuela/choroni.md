---
title: "Choroni"
type: location
---

Placed in the Aragua State and very near from Caracas, this is one of the most popular places of the *caraqueños* to have fun and relax. To reach there you've got to travel through the National Park *Henry Pittier* which is considered one of the places with more bio-diversity in the world! Once you've land there, this oldy town will make you fall in love with it, because not only you'll find a great beach, but all the sorroundings. Near Choroni is Puerto Colombia, and in both towns you'll find enough places to stay. Be aware of going to the square during the night, you'll find the best drumb party you'll see ever!
