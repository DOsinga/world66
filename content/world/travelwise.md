[TravelWise](/travelwise)

Travelwise is World66's collection of travel tips organized in the categories below.
The approach we take is to have information on general travel related questions, like how to stay healthy on the road, how to get the best deals on flights, how to deal with common problems for travelers like con artists etc.

Travelwise is not a bulletin board where you can ask questions. Nor is it the place to look for information regarding specific travel destinations. For tips on a certain destination, look it up in the destination database.

The categories are fixed, i.e. if you need a new one, mail us, but you can add articles or edit existing ones, just like anything else on this site
