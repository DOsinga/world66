What it does

From World66 page to day-by-day itinerary in minutes.

Tab.bi lets you build a trip directly from World66 content. Browse a city page, pin destinations and POIs to a plan, arrange them by day — then share the whole thing with a passphrase instead of a login.

Plans can be collaborative. Give the passphrase to your travel companion and you're both editing the same itinerary in real time.

Tab.bi is built entirely on the World66 content graph — every stop in a plan traces back to a page in content/. Better content means better trip plans.

No account required
Plans are protected by a passphrase, not a login. Share the phrase with your travel companion — that's it. No email, no password reset, no privacy leak.
Powered by W66 content
Every stop is a World66 page — city guides, neighbourhoods, restaurants, museums. As the content improves, your plans get richer automatically.
Day-by-day structure
Organise stops into days with notes per stop. Reorder by drag-and-drop. Print or share a clean itinerary view.
Open source
The plans_app lives in the World66 repo. Fork it, extend it, run your own instance. The W66 content licence applies to all content used in plans.
