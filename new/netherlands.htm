the Netherlands: General

The Netherlands is a small country in the Northwest of Europe, between the North Sea, Germany and Belgium.

Its recorded history starts with the Roman invasion halfway the first century A.D. but it had its heydays in the 17th century when it disputed hegemony of the Seven Seas with the English and the Spanish empires. In that period New York, parts of Brazil, a series of forts along the African Coast and the Colonies in Indonesia formed part of the vast Dutch Empire. Read more...
