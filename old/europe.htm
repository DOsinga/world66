Europe: General

The old world is really quite old. Every other town in Europe is packed with monuments dating back several centuries. Little villages were once capital the capital of a kingdom. The countries are small, but most of them were once the center of an empire that embraced the whole world.

Traveling in Europe seems to be underestimated, especially by the younger generation and budget travelers. To say it clearly: Europe offers more than most other regions in the world. There is hardly anything that cannot be found in one of the megacities, like London, Paris, Rome or Berlin. Read more...
