# Bardo Museum

*Africa > Algeria > Algiers > Museums > Bardomuseum*

This impressive collection of indigenous artefacts from various regions of Algeria is housed in a beautiful Moorish style mansion. The museum features exhibits on prehistoric findings and ethnography as well as regional jewellery, costumes, leatherwork and weaponry.

 3 avenue Franklin Roosevelt, at the top of rue Didouche Mourad  Hours: 9am-12 noon & 2pm-5pm daily except Friday morning and Saturday
