# Sights

*Africa > Algeria > Laghouat > Sights*

Most interesting sites to visit in Laghouat are: -Zgag El Hajaj (Casba or old city) -Oued M'zi side walk - El Borj and its beautiful gardens - Ain Madhi (Main Tidjanies Capital) - Sidi Makhlouf with its prehistorical paintings - Kourdane and Aurelie Picard Museum. To enjoy all those sights you me request a native person assistance.
