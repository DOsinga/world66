# Getting Around in Luanda

*Africa > Angola > Luanda > Getting Around*

**Traveling by Airline**

If you need to travel within Angola there are many flights that travel all
over. These flights will take you to cities including places near Luanda. The
prices differ according to the place you long to travel to. The Angola National
Airlines will accommodate you as needed. Even though prices differ depending on
your starting point, the company has announced that there is a 70 percent drop in fares. This is a huge savings no matter where you begin your trip. The company
is also equipped to help those who speak English. By calling the London office
you will be taken care of properly.

**Traveling by Car**

Driving in Luanda is a good idea for those who want to see the country from
a ground view level but there are things to be careful of. The roads are not in
good condition and there are no basic rules for driving--so watch out for
accidents. It is also not recommended to drive after dark or outside the city
limits. Gas stations are abundant inside the city but outside you might develop
problems. Even though there are a few problems traveling by car is an easy way
to get around. Prices for cars differ, based on the company and the length of time you want to
use the car. Prices range from $60 to $210, and if you want a
driver that is an extra $30 to $45 charge.
