# Lubango

*Africa > Angola > Lubango*

Very nice city in the Huila province. It has a mountain range as backdrop and serves as a starting point to several daytrips, e.g. to Bimbe, Tundavala and Serra da Leba. The best road in Angola connects Lubango to [Namibe](/africa/angola/namibe). Other road connections to Huambo and [Benguela](/africa/angola/benguela) are dismal.
