# Beaches

*Africa > Beaches*

Many African countries have great beaches. Here are just a few suggestions.

[Kenya](/africa/kenya) has very good beaches, [Mombasa](/africa/kenya/thecoast/mombasa) is the big city from where you can explore them all.

[South Africa](/africa/southafrica) has beaches where you can sun and tan, while watching pinguins.

[Morocco](/africa/morocco) has nice Atlantic beaches where you can surf. [Essaouira](/africa/morocco/essaouira) is the place to head for.
