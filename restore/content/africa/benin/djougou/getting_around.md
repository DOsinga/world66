# Getting Around

*Africa > Benin > Djougou > Getting Around*

Djougou is small enough to get around on foot. However, when staying in a hotel just outside the city, most zemidjans will take you there for a small fee.

### [Zemidjan](/africa/benin/djougou/getting_around/moto)

As everywhere in Benin, a lot of motorbikes function as taxis. They are called zemi, which is short for zemidjan. In Djougou you can either stop them in the street, or go to a zemi stop, where a lot of them are together.
