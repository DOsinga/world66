# Eating Out in Douala

*Africa > Cameroon > Douala > Eating Out*

**Enjoy Excellent Food at the Broadway in ****Douala**

When looking for something unique that stands out from the rest **Douala**** restaurants**, you should visit the Broadway. This is a signature restaurant for Douala, and every traveler should visit it while staying at the city. A place suitable both for casual and elegant guests, the Broadway offers excellent food and atmosphere.

#### **The Atmosphere**

The Broadway is separated into 3 different parts. It has a dining room, a pool table room and a bar and lounge area with stage, where bands perform live for guests of the restaurant. The music you can hear varies from jazz and blues to traditional African and R&B. The lounge area is dimly lit, ensuring a great nightlife experience. The dining room is well-arranged and creates a feeling of comfort and coziness. The staff is friendly, and the service is fantastic. You will enjoy some great moments there.

#### **The Menu**

The restaurant offers an extensive menu focusing on European cuisine but impresses mostly with its delicious seafood. Some of the seafood delicacies you should try are the gambas &agrave; l&rsquo;espagnole, grilled prawns, and the different kinds of grilled fish. If you want to try something else, go for the veal stew, the grilled ribs or the beef Bourguignon. Don&rsquo;t forget to order a bottle of good wine to complement these dishes. The Broadways offers a nice selection of wines at reasonable prices. Finally, finish your meal with some fantastic crepes flambees or profiteroles.
