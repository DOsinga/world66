# Nightlife and Entertainment in Douala

*Africa > Cameroon > Douala > Nightlife And Ente*

If you want a good time in Africa, then **Douala nightlife** needs
to be experienced. This is a city that in many senses never sleeps and
you can party any night of the week. The local people are so warm and
welcoming that the atmosphere is always pleasant. In some places be wary
that you will have to adhere to certain dress codes, but for the most
part Douala nightlife is a relaxed affair.

**The Broadway**

The riotous atmosphere of this bar is provided by a truly eclectic
range of live bands that play almost every night ranging across the
jazz, blues, R and B and African genres. Thus this place could be called
a restaurant and a cabaret bar. The clientele is free to eat, drink,
dance and be merry and people are so friendly that you won't spend too
long on your own if you arrive on your own. This hip joint is located at
Rue Toyota, Bonapriso, Douala.

**The Senat**

With its astonishing range of single malt whiskey, you'd be forgiven
for thinking you'd found a little part of Scotland in the midst of
Africa. The live jazz played by the tight house band is a joy to listen
to and has the bonus of being led by a man who looks like a young Miles
Davis. You'll also find a truly superlative restaurant in The Senat
which serves up genuine local dishes that will make your mouth
water--definitely a highlight of Douala nightlife.

**The Club**

Generally more upscale than a lot of its competitors, The Club has
been renovated in recent times but has retained its chic charm. You'll
find cold "33" beer served all night and a selection of pool tables to
challenge your friends on. Live music sometimes happens at this venue.

**Mega Slots**

An exciting combination of nightspot and casino, Mega Slots boasts
two floors of gaming machines, bars and dance floors. Fun as this place
is, it is something of a meat market with aggressive females looking for
men. If you don't want any of this sort of action then you're best
advised to stay away from the disco area and just have a flutter on the
slot machines.

**La Cigale**

Proving that live music is really the lifeblood of Douala nightlife,
La Cigale presents a stunning array of singers, musicians and
performers. The advantage of La Cigale is that there aren't too many
girls who will want to chase you for money, allowing you to just relax
and enjoy the music.
