# Crossing the Sahara

*Africa > Chad > Crossingthesahara*

The good news is: it seems to be possible to cross the Sahara from Libya to Chad, at least it was in 1999. That's the most recent report we have recieved. The road goes first to [Sebha](/africa/libya/sebha) and then on to al Qatrun both in Libya, and the onto Chad.
