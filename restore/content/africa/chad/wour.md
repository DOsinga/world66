# Wour

*Africa > Chad > Wour*

They don't see a lot of foreign tourists in Wour. It is the border post on the route from Libya (al Qatrun) to the [Tibesti mountains](/africa/chad/tibestimountains). On of the most exciting, and least traveled, trans Saharan routes. Wour itself is not very exciting though. But here you can get some supplies. Be prepared to bribe here and there if your papers are not 100% in order (they never are).
