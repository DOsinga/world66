# Felucca Tour On The Nile

*Africa > Egypt > Aswan > Day Trips > Felucca Tour On Th*

Itinerary:-
Felucca tour on the Nile

Discover the marvelous nature on the River Nile. You will be picked up from your hotel by an air-conditioned modern vehicle to enjoy a Felucca tour on the Nile, start sailing by felucca for approx. 2 Hours. Enjoy the nice breeze of the Nile start your tour by visiting the Elephantine Island to see the temple of Khnum, the Island Museum and the Nilometer, and enjoy your lunch. You can also enjoy watching the landscape, Mixing of Colors between light blue for sky, yellow for desert, green for land and navy blue for the River Nile. then transfer back to your hotel in Aswan.

Price Includes:-
• Pick up service from your hotel in Aswan and return
• English Egyptologist guide
• Entrance fees to the mentioned historical places
• All transfers by a modern air-conditioned vehicle
• Felucca ride for about 02 hours
• A bottle of mineral water during your trip
• All service charges and taxes
