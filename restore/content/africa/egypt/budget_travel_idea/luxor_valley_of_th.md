Tour Itinerary:

**Day 1: Safag Port - Visit Karnak and Luxor Temples ( East Bank )**
You will be picked up from your Ship in **Safaga Port **by your tour guide, then you will drive to**Luxor **for three and a half hours safety driving with amazing view for the mountain and desert, arrive **Luxor** and start to visit the majestic**Karnak temple **was
built 1300 years ago it was the greatest example of worship in history
and was dedicated to the God Amon his wife Mut and their son Khonsu.
Then you will continue to visit **Luxor temple **it was built by Amenhotep III, it was connected to**Karnak temple **by
a large alley called a dromo which is lined by sphinxes. From there you
will transfer to your hotel in Luxor for overnight stay, with optional
tour to attend the **sound and light show **in**Karnak temple **at night.

**Day 2: Visit Valley of the Kings, Hatshepsut temple - Safag Port**
We will pick you up from your hotel in **Luxor** by you private Egyptologist English guide and private A/C van then you will be transferred to the west bank of The **Nile River **you will be visiting the two colossal statues of Amenhotep III (**Colossi of Memnon**), and then visit the **Valley of the Kings**,
where the magnificent tombs discovered, those tombs were carved deep
into the desert rock, richly decorated and filled with treasures for the
afterlife of the pharaohs, you will visit 3 tombs Continue the tour to
visit the **Hatshepsut temple** at El Deir El Bahary, this impressive temple dedicated to the **Queen Hatshepsut **the
only female pharaoh rises out of the desert plain in a series of
terraces and merges with the sheer limestone cliffs that surround it,
free time in Luxor, then return back to **Safaga port Red Sea** with direct transfer to your ship in **Safaga port, Red Sea.**
