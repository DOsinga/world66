# Egypt in Focus Program

*Africa > Egypt > Cairo > Activities > Egypt In Focus Pro*

Brief Itinerary:

• Day 01: Arrival Cairo.
• Day 02: Visit the Great Pyramids of Giza, Sphinx, Valley Temple of Chephren and Sakkara necropolis.
• Day 03: Domestic flight Cairo- Aswan , Visit the High Dam, Philae Temple & Nile Cruise embarkation.
• Day 04: Visit Kom Ombo & Edfu Temples.
• Day 05: Visit Valley of the Kings, Hatshepsut Temple, Colossi of Memnon & Karnak Temples.
• Day 06: Disembarkation, visit Dandra & Abydos Temple & overnight in Luxor.
• Day 07: Domestic flight to Cairo.
• Day 08: visit Egyptian Museum, Sladin Citadel, Alabaster Mosque & Kan El-Kalili
• Day 09: Free at Leisure & overnight.
• Day 10: Final Departure.
