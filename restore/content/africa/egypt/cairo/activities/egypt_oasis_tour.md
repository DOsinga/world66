# Egypt Oasis Tour

*Africa > Egypt > Cairo > Activities > Egypt Oasis Tour*

The 10 Days: Egypt Oasis Tour is a relaxing yet completely surreal adventure for anyone who enjoys culture, nature, and history.

**Itinerary: Egypt Oasis Tour, 10 Days**

**Day 1: Arrive in Egypt**
Our expert representative will meet you at the Cairo International Airport. You will then transfer to your hotel aboard a modern, air-conditioned vehicle for a bit of rest and to spend the night.

**Day 2: Giza Plateau, Egyptian Museum, Coptic & Islamic Cairo**
You will enjoy breakfast at your hotel in Cairo. After breakfast you will be met by your personal guide who will accompany you to the Giza Plateau to visit one of the seven wonders of the ancient world; the Great Pyramids of Cheops, Chefren and Mykerinus, the famous Sphinx, and the Valley Temple facing the Sphinx. Then transfer to explore the Egyptian Museum. Continue to Coptic Cairo to visit the Hanging Church and Ben Ezra Synagogue. End this amazing day of sightseeing combining fascinating history with shopping in the vibrant Khan El Khalili Bazaar, which dates back to 1382. Back to hotel. Overnight in Cairo.

**Day 3 & 4: Bahariya Oasis and White Desert Trekking**
Wake up in the early morning and drive by an private air-conditioned modern van to Bahariya Oasis. Take a 4x4 Jeep to the Agabat. Enjoy watching the beautiful powder sand in the desert. Start trekking from here to the White Desert (daily 35 km trekking). Camp in the desert for 2 nights.

**Day 5: Farafra & Dakhla**
After enjoying breakfast, you will enjoy a trip into the Farafra Oasis aboard a Jeep, where you will have lunch. You will finish the day within the Dakhla Oasis, in the Islamic village of El Qasr.

**Day 6: Kharga**
After Breakfast, enjoy the day from the morning until afternoon trekking to the camp site (about 20 km trekking) to watch the sunset. Overnight camping in the Kharga Desert.

**Day 7: Luxor**
On day 7 of this oasis tour Egypt you’ll break camp early and travel via air-conditioned vehicle to Luxor, with a stop at the Bagawat Christian Cemetery. On arrival in Luxor, you will check into your hotel for the night.

**Day 8: Luxor Sightseeing**
You will have breakfast in the hotel, and then visit the Valley of the Kings, as well as the Colossi of Memnon, the Karnak Temples and the Luxor Temple. You will board a sleeper train that evening, bound for Cairo. You’ll have dinner and sleep aboard the train.

　
**Day 9: Back to Cairo**
In Cairo you will be met by our representative in the Giza train station and escorted to your hotel to relax and spend the night in Cairo.

**Day 10: Final Departure**
After breakfast in the hotel, you will head to the Cairo International Airport for your departing flight.
