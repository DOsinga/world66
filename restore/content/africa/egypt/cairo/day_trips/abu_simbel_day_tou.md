# Abu Simbel day tour by coach from Aswan

*Africa > Egypt > Cairo > Day Trips > Abu Simbel Day Tou*

With
Youregypttours.com See the magnificent temples of Abu Simbel on a
minibus tour from Aswan. With a qualified Egyptologist as your guide,
you’ll enjoy a personalized visit to Abu Simbel. With its four
rock-hewn pharaohs, your guide will explain the history of the site,
including how the temples were discovered by accident in 1813. Due to
the large number of visitors to Abu Simbel, guides are not permitted
inside either the Great Temple of Ramses II or the smaller Temple of
Hathor.
