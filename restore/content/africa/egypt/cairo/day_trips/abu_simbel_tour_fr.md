**Tour Itinerary: **

Our **Abu Simbel tour **will
start with pick up service from your hotel in **Cairo**,
where our REP will meet you with aboard reflecting your name, then you will be
transferred by air-conditioned vehicle to **Cairo**
airport for flight to **Abu Simbel**. In **Abu Simbel** you will be accompanied by **private** Egyptologist guide to visit two temples. **Abu Simbel** temple was carved into a cliff on the
banks of Nile as a grand display of the power and territory of **Ramses II** the Great. It was dedicated to the gods
Amun, Ra-Horakhty, and Ptah, as well as to the deified **Ramses II **himself. It is generally considered the
grandest and most beautiful of the temples commissioned during the reign of **Ramses II**, and one of the most beautiful in Egypt.

The **temple** of Hathor and **Nefertari**, also known as the Small Temple, was
built about one hundred meters northeast of the **temple**
of **Ramses II** and was dedicated to
the goddess Hathor and Ramses II's chief consort, **Nefertari**. This was in fact the second time in **ancient** **Egyptian** history that a **temple** was dedicated to a queen.Then u will be
driven to **Abu Simbel** airport to fly to **Cairo**, with next transfer to your hotel.
