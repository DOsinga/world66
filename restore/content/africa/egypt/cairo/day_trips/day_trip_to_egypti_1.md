Itinerary:-
**Day trip to Egyptian Museum & Old Cairo**

Our representative will pick you up from your hotel in **Cairo or Giza** to enjoy
a **day trip to Egyptian museum & Old** **Cairo**, first visit the
**Egyptian antiquities Museum** commonly known as **the Egyptian Museum**
which located in the capital **city of Cairo**, where you can see the most extensive
collection of** ancient antiquities** , & the famous golden mask of
**Tutankhamen** among other amazing **pharaonic artifacts** , finally continue your
tour to visit** Old Cairo** where you will see **Mary Gerges church** , **Hanging**
** church** , **Ben Ezra Synagogue**, then back to your hotel in **Cairo or Giza**.
