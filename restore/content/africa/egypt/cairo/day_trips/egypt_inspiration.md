**Tour Itinerary

(Arrival Cairo)**
Day 1 : Upon arrival Cairo international airport you will be meeting by our representative who will hold a sign which include your name, & assist you in handling all your procedures through passport control , customs formalities & luggage area, then transfer you to your hotel via air conditioned modern vehicle & overnight in Cairo .

**(Pyramids – Egyptian Museum)**
Day 2 : After having your breakfast , enjoy a full day tour to visit the enigmatic Pyramids of Giza of King Cheops , Chefren , Mykernuis , Sphinx , & Valley temple which are the most famous monuments of ancient Egypt, you can feel the mysteries surrounding their symbolism , design & purpose have inspired passionate debate, it’s likely that many of these mysteries will never be solved, then proceed your fantastic tour to visit the Egyptian antiquities museum commonly known as the Egyptian Museum which located in the capital city of Cairo, where you can see the most extensive collection of ancient antiquities, then transfer to Giza train station for departure to Aswan by first class sleeping train & overnight (Dinner & Breakfast will be served on board)

**(Aswan – Felucca)**
Day 3: Arrival Aswan train station, meet & assist by our representative , transfer to your hotel for refreshment, have a full view of the city by enjoying A felucca ride on the River Nile around Elephantine island which is a part of Aswan was previously called the Yeb Island , the Yeb was one of the foremost commercial centers of Egypt ivory, gold, camels & caravans had made the island famous throughout Egypt, Yeb in Nubian means ELEPHANT & thus the Greeks began to call it as the Elephantine Island , then back to your hotel & overnight in Aswan .

**(High Dam – Unfinished Obelisk & Philae)**
Day 4: After having your breakfast, enjoy visiting the High Dam which is one of the world’s largest dams & its lake is the second in the world, spread to Sudan, Unfinished Obelisk which is constructed out of the syenite granite mined in Aswan & Philae temple which dedicated to be the goddess Isis, at evening transfer to Aswan train station for departure back to Cairo by first class sleeping train & overnight on board (Dinner & Breakfast will be served on board)

**(Arrival Giza – Optional Sound & Light Show)**
Day 5: Arrival Giza train station , meet & assist by our representative , transfer to your hotel for refreshment , free day in Cairo or an Optional visit to attend Sound & Light show at pyramids area, it’s an enchanting experience not to be missed , you may never know exactly how the magnificent pyramids were built ,the riddle of Sphinx may remain one of the world’s oldest mysteries, you are visiting the pyramids in the 21st century, thousands of years after they were built & they will continue to endure for generations, back to your hotel & overnight in Cairo .

**(Final departure)**
Day 6: After having your breakfast, transfer to Cairo international airport for final departure, assistance through formalities by our representative.
