# King tut family Adventure (10 days 9 nights)

*Africa > Egypt > Cairo > Family Travel Idea > King Tut Family Ad*

**Tour itinerary:**

**Day 1**: Cairo (Arrival)

Youregypttours.com
representative will meet & greet you at Cairo International
Airport, and handle your procedures through passport control, luggage
area, customs formalities. Then you will be transferred to your hotel
(with pool) by a private air-conditioned vehicle, Overnight in Cairo.

**Day 2**: Giza (Pyramids& sphinx)

After
breakfast in the hotel, Step back in time to the days of ancient Egypt
on a fantastic tour of the Great Pyramids of Giza and the great Sphinx,
then take a camel ride for the whole family, You will have free time to
enter one of the pyramids (additional cost), though your guide is not
permitted to enter with you, You will also have the chance to visit a
Papyrus Institute to see how the famous artwork is made and to teach
the kids how to write their names in hieroglyphics, (optional tour)
sound and light show of the pyramids, overnight in Cairo.

**Day 3**: Cairo (Egyptian museum, Coptic Cairo)

After
breakfast in the hotel (check out), then proceed to visit Cairo's
famous Egyptian Museum houses 165,000 Egyptian artifacts, but the
priceless treasures of King Tutankhamen are the highlights. His six
gilded coffins dominate the main hall, while his magnificent death mask
is the star attraction. Have free time to explore at your leisure.
Continuing to the ancient sites and culture of Coptic Christians, Visit
Abu Serga where the Holy family sorts refuge, and Ben Ezra, one of the
oldest synagogues in Cairo. Learn about Christian life in Egypt.
Transfer to Giza train station for departure to Aswan by Sleeper train,
overnight on train.

**Day 4**: Aswan (Philae Temple, Nubian village)

Our
representative will meet and greet you at Aswan railway station, then
transfer to your 5 star hotel (check in), then proceed to enjoy the
beauty of Philae Temple's island location, visit a local village and
school to teach the kids the Arabic alphabet & numbers, (if open)
and enjoy lunch at the home of an Egyptian family. And we will learn
about Nubian life in Egypt, overnight in Aswan.

**Day 5**: Aswan (Abu Simbel, felucca)

Early
morning to the impressive Temples of Ramses II at Abu Simbel (optional
tour), afternoon boarding at the felucca to sail to Kom Ombo, overnight
on board.

**Day 6**: Aswan (Kom Ombo, Edfu)

Sailing to Kom Ombo, then drive via Kom Ombo temple & Edfu temple to Luxor then check in your hotel, overnight in Luxor.

**Day 7**: Luxor (Valley of the Kings, Hatshepsut, Memnon)

Early
morning hot air balloon (optional tour), after that Enjoy an excursion
to the Valley of the Kings, the Temple of Hatshepsut, the powerful
ancient Egyptian queen, and the Colossi of Memnon. Back to hotel and
overnight in Luxor.

**Day 8**: Luxor (Karnak, Luxor)

After
breakfast, check out from your hotel. Tour the gigantic Great Temple of
Amen at Karnak, and the mighty Temple of Luxor, Optional trip to Sound
& Light Show at Karnak Temple, overnight in Luxor.

**Day 9**: Cairo (Islamic Cairo)

Early
morning check out from hotel, transfer to Luxor railway station for
departure to Cairo. Afternoon Look beyond the pyramids and pharaohs,
and discover the Citadel of Salah el Din where Mohamed Ali Alabaster
Mosque stays and the Mosque and Madrassa (school) of Sultan Hassan,
continuing to Khan el Khalili Bazaars Enjoy free time to stroll through
the maze of streets and try your luck bargaining with the shop owners
for a souvenir of your trip to Egypt. (Optional tour) Cairo nights;
Egyptian Cultural Dinner Nile cruise and Show, overnight in Cairo.

**Day 10**: Cairo (Flight home)

Breakfast at hotel and check out from hotel, then transfer to Cairo International Airport for your final departure.
