# Cairo Airport Shuttle

*Africa > Egypt > Cairo > Gettingaround > Cairo Airport Shut*

The Cairo Airport Shuttle comes highly recommended. New, comfortable, air-conditioned, seven-seater vans. Great group rates. Total for 7 people to downtown is 70Le (+tip). Please see the attached link.

Call +20 22 265 3937 to make a reservation and upon arrival go to the shuttle desk. You will be escorted to your van and driver.

For the return trip, the drivers are prompt - to the minute! - and will get you to the airport in record time, but make sure you're at the right terminal. For International flights to the US, be sure to confirm that you're at TERMINAL 2, vs. Departure Hall 2.

Overall, great value for money, with excellent hassle free comfort.
