# Day Tour to Pyramids & Egyptian Museum

*Africa > Egypt > Day Trips > Day Tour To Pyrami 1*

Pick up from your hotel in Cairo , enjoy an excursion to visit the Great Pyramids of Cheops, Chefren and Mykerinus & see the Sphinx , which was discovered in 1912 carved from a single piece of stone, it is a lion with a portrait of a Pharaoh's head, it is also believed by many to be a likeness of Pharaoh Kefren. and then to Valley Temple. After that pay a visit to the Egyptian Museum where you can see the collection of Tutankhamun among other splendid pharaonic artifacts. Back to your hotel in Cairo .
