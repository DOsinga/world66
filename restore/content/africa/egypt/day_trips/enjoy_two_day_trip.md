Tour Itinerary

Day 1: Hurghada - Cairo sightseeing - Luxor

Available Travel representative will pick you up from your hotel to Hurghada Airport, for for a trip to cairo from hurghada by short flight to Cairo, where you will start your trip in Cairo the capital of Egypt and biggest city in Africa with your certificated tour guide, first you will admire the River Nile which flows from south to north. Then you will visit the Great Giza Pyramids which is ONE of the Seven Wonders of the ancient world, the Great Pyramid of Cheops, middle pyramids of Chephren and the little one of Mykerinos and mysterious Sphinx; continue to visit the valley temple facing the great Sphinx. Lunch will be at a local restaurant to taste the Egyptian food, and then you will continue your tour to visit the Egyptian Museum. The present Museum building was constructed in 1900 in the neo-classical style by the French architect Marcel Dourgnon. The Museum exhibits collections that now exceed 120,000 objects ranging from the pre-historic era to the Greco-Roman period. Free time in Cairo or you will be accompanied to the old market in Cairo Khan El Khalili built in 1382 by the Emir Djaharks El Khalili. It is considered to be one of Cairo's most important shopping areas. After that you will transfer to Cairo Airport for your flight from cairo to Luxor with direct transfer to your hotel in Luxor. Overnight stay in Luxor.

Day 2: Luxor Sightseeing Tours - Hurghada

Available Travel representative will pick you up from your hotel in Luxor by air conditioned vehicle by your certificated tour guide to the west bank to visit the Valley of the kings, where you will enter three tombs, continue your way to visit the valley of the queens, and colossi of Memnoun. Then you will take a felucca ride in the Nile to cross from the west to the east bank. Lunch will be including. After that you will move to visit the Karnak temple, the largest place of worship ever built, the temple contain many temples inside. Free time in Luxor or you will have a shopping tour in the old market in Luxor. Then you will transfer to your hotel in Hurghada.
