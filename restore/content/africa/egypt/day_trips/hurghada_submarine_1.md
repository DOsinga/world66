# Hurghada Submarine Trip

*Africa > Egypt > Day Trips > Hurghada Submarine 1*

Jeep Safari Trip to the Desert in Hurghada

Tour Type:Jeep Tour
Tour Length:Half day
City Tour:Jeep Safari trip
Pick up Point:Your hotel in Hurghada
Drop off Point:Your hotel in Hurghada

Tour Itinerary : -

Albaraa Travel REP will pick you up from your hotel in Hurghada by 4x4 WD Toyota land cruiser. After that you will drive for 35 km in the Eastern desert (East of the river Nile) towards a Bedouin village, in a half day guided tour to know more about the Bedouin life (Houses, food, Education, religion, etc.) Followed by a camel ride . Then you will attend the amazing sunset view in the desert with barbeque dinner, mineral water and soft drinks. After dinner you will enjoy a folkloric bedouin show and songs, then you will drive back to your hotel in the way you will stop for watching stars at night continue driving back to your hotel in Hurghada.

Tour Includes : -

- Transfers from your hotel in Hurghada and return by jeep 4x4
- Panorama view of Hurghada
- Bedouin village tour
- English speaking Bedouin tour guide
- Camel ride in the desert
- Barbeque dinner with 1 mineral water and 1 soft drink
- In the evening attend the Bedouin show
- Romantic stop to see the stars away from the city lights
