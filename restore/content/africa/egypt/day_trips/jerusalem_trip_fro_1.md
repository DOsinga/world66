Tour Itinerary : -

Jerusalem tour and Dead sea
Join us in one of our Hurghada tours to Jerusalem city and Dead sea to have a Jerusalem tour and swim in the Dead sea as our Jerusalem tour will start by picking you up from your hotel in Hurghada by air-conditioned van to Hurghada airport for your short flight to Sharm E lsheikh, From Sharm El Sheikh airport you will be driven to Egyptian-Israel border in Taba to cross it. In the way to Jerusalem city you can have breakfast (breakfast box). Upon arrival to Jerusalem city you will be accompagnied by our guide to start your Jerusalem tour as you will visit the Mount of Olives for beautiful panorama of the Old City, Temple Mount gilded Dome of the Rock. Proceed your Jerusalem tour to visit in Old Town the Wailing Wall (wall of Al Borak), Via Dolorosa, Basilica of the Holy Grave. Continue your jerusalem tour to Mont Zion to visit The Cenacle, believed to be the site of Jesus' Last Supper, Tomb of King David, Church of the Holy Sepulchre. Our next step in Jerusalem tour will be to Bethlehem - where the Basilica of the Nativity stands - built over the grotto, regarded as the birthplace of Jesus in Jerusalem city. The church is one of the oldest Christian churches in jerusalem city, where masses are celebrated. Afterwards we will continue our Jerusalem tour to the Dead Sea for swimming. Then you will return back to Taba to cross border and driven to Sharm El Sheikh airport to fly back to Hurghada with followed transfer to your hotel in Hurghada.( end of Hurghada tours, Jerusalem tour)
