# Sharm Tours to Cairo By Flight

*Africa > Egypt > Day Trips > Sharm Tours To Cai*

A chance to visit the exquisite Giza Pyramids and the Sphinx the enormous statue with a head of a pharaoh and lion's body. Also see the Valley Temple of the Pyramid of king Chephren. Then we will move on to the Egyptian Museum which features artifacts from the Pharaonic period. The museum displays a rare collection of 5000 years of art which is considered the largest most precious collection of Egyptian art in the world. Over 250,000 genuine artifacts are presented, including an exhibit dedicated to Tutankhamen collection of treasure, gold and jewelry which was enclosed in his tomb for over 3,500 years before it was discovered in the 1920s when his tomb was excavated. You may have a Shopping at Khan El Khalili Bazaar, the most famous bazaar in the Middle east. Fly Back to Sharm.
