# Cross Egypt Challenge Rally

*Africa > Egypt > Festivals > Cross Egypt Challe*

Cross Egypt Challenge is a series of cross-country rallies
conducted throughout the most challenging roads and tracks in Egypt.
This breathtaking challenge is fascinating for spectators and riders
alike. Through combining the best of adventure travel and extreme
sports, Cross Egypt Challenge aims to promote tourism, and alternative
sports in Egypt.

Adventure-loving riders from the four corners of the globe came to Egypt to take part in Cross Egypt Challenge in a bid to promote tourism and
give the world a glimpse of Egyptian hospitality.
