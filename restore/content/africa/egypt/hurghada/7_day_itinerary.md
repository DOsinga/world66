# 7 Day Itinerary in Hurghada

*Africa > Egypt > Hurghada > 7 Day Itinerary*

Hurghada has more to it than the Red Sea. Of course people come here mainly
for diving, but much to their
delight, discover that there are several other fascinating aspects to Hurghada’s
personality. Here is a well-planned 7 day itinerary to help visitors enjoy the
charming, old-fashioned Egyptian city.

**Day 1 - Red Sea Diving**

Start your tour with diving and snorkeling at the Red Sea, an excellent
center for underwater sports and a rich marine life. Visitors can spot
impressive coral reefs and tons of tropical fish. Diving can be organized by
your hotel or by one of the several diving centers in the region. Other water
sports activities include windsurfing, parasailing and jet skiing. Take a glass
boat ride, where you can see magnificent corals from the glass bottom of the
boat.

**Day 2** **- Desert Safari**

Take a desert safari by jeep or on camel back organized by most hotels and
tour companies. The camel ride route takes visitors through the Biblical plains
to see ancient monuments and ruins. Tourists can also go quad-biking in the
Sahara desert and share tea and conversation with the local tribes.

**Day 3 - Giftun Islands**

Take a boat trip to Gifton Islands, a virtually uninhabited patch of
phenomenal natural beauty. Visit the Giftun Island National Park to enjoy the
stunning coral gardens and swim amidst rich coral reefs of the region. This is
also a great spot of sunbathing on the warm white sand.

**Day 4 - Shop**

Visitors can pick up lovely souvenirs from the Sekella Hight Street, the road
adjacent to the beach and downtown Hurghada. The city center has several bazaars
featuring items that can be purchased at throw-away prices. There are a few
reasonably-priced souvenir stores in New Hurghada and New Marina.

**Day 5 - Sharm-El-Shiekh**

Sharm-El-Shiekh is another super diving destination located a few kilometers
from Hurghada. It is also a nice historical town, where visitors can see lovely
old churches and other ancient monuments.

**Day 6 - El Dahar **

Take a walk through the El Dahar Street that gives you a real feel of an old
Egyptian neighborhood. There are several eateries, souvenir shops, street-side
markets and ancient homes. Head to the churches located in the center of the
town and finish off with a visit to the Chill Beach Bar, a popular hangout with
nice tropical huts and lazy hammocks.

**Day 7 - Red Sea Aquarium**

Visit the Red Sea Aquarium that houses a huge variety of marine life
inhabiting the region. The exhibits are well-organized and clearly labeled with
elaborate diagrams and textual information. The aquarium is open daily from 9 a.m.
to 10 p.m.

Hurghada is a haven for underwater adventure tourists with several diving and
snorkeling centers spread throughout the Red Sea. There are other interesting
islands, desert safaris and old town quarters that can be explored from or in
Hurghada.
