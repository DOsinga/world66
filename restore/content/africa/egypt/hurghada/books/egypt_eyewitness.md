# Egypt (Eyewitness Travel Guides)

*Africa > Egypt > Hurghada > Books > Egypt Eyewitness*

**Egypt (Eyewitness Travel Guides), DK Publishing, ISBN: 075662875X **is part of a very popular series of travel guides and gives a good basic overview of tourist highlights of the country.

**Picture Perfect**

As with the other books in the Eyewitness Travel Guide range, the Egypt guide is beautifully illustrated with many photographs.

**Maps**

Unfortunately although there are many maps in the guide there are no Hurghada specific ones.

**Hurghada Specific Information**

There are a few pages on Hurghada, touching upon its hotels, restaurants and local activities. However there is a little more information on the general geographical area and this is quite helpful.

**General Information**

There is an expansive information guide with regards to both practical and travel information for leisure travellers. I feel that the guide is not extensive enough for the seasoned traveller who likes to get off the beaten track but it is fine for someone on holiday who is looking to see the sights.

There is a section on basic Arabic phrases which are handy to know. However on balance I would like to reiterate that this is more of a general Egypt guide and that if you are looking for an in depth overview of Hurghada, perhaps contacting the local tourist office or undertaking an Internet search may be of more help in the long term.

All in all, this is a very attractively presented book with good general information, and very good Cairo specific information. It is not so great with regards to Hurghada however but I would recommend it for its good historical and cultural information on the country as a whole.
