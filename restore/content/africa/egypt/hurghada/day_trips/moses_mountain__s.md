# Moses Mountain & St. Catherine Monastery Excursions from Dahab

*Africa > Egypt > Hurghada > Day Trips > Moses Mountain  S*

Departure : Everyday Except Sundays & Fridays

Length : Approx 15 hours

Type : By A/C vehicle

Guide : English speaking guide

Itinerary:-
(Overnight trip to Moses Mountain & St. Catherine Monastery from Dahab)

Our representative will pick you up from your hotel in Dahab by a private air-conditioned modern vehicle at 00:00 p.m. Then drive to St.Catheine city. It takes 01 hour and half driving. Upon arrival, start climbing the Moses’ Mountain begins at 2:00 a.m and coming down at 7:00 a.m. Then proceed to visit St. Catherine Monastery which is considered one of the most famous monasteries in the world and it was dedicated to one of Alexander’s rulers, Saint Catherine who was tortured to death for the sake of her new faith. Then transfer to your hotel in Dahab.
