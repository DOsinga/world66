# Family Travel Ideas in Hurghada

*Africa > Egypt > Hurghada > Family Travel Idea*

Hurghada is a beautiful, family friendly coastal resort that is
popular all over the world for its deep sea diving and rich coral reef
collection. Here is a guide to enjoying an activity-packed, fun-filled
family vacation in the town of the Red Sea.

**Diving **

This is obviously a favorite family activity in Hurghada. There are
innumerable private beach resorts and islands on the Red Sea that have
their own diving centers with expert instructors and high-end diving
gear. The diving sessions are designed to accommodate all levels of
proficiency from beginners to expert divers. Some of the most popular
diving centers in town are the Orca Diving Center at Le Pacha Resort,
Aquanaut Blue Heaven Center, Jasmin Diving Center at the Aladdin Resort
, Prince Diving Center and Evolutions Dive Center. Other popular water
sports activities include snorkeling, para sailing, surfing and speed
boat riding. The centers follow special safety norms for children and
have separate sessions and special guided underwater diving tours for
them.

**Desert Safaris **

Adventure-holidays oriented families will enjoy a safari through the
deserts to discover ancient sites and heritage trade routes. The best
way to savor a authentic, nomadic desert experience is to take a camel
ride like the erstwhile Arab Merchants. The act brings alive the
mystical, fairy-tale persona of the Middle East. Camel rides pass
through old Biblical Plains and old monuments to help families
re-create images of faraway deserts in no man’s land. Families can also
go on quad biking trips through the desert.

**Giftun Islands**

Gittun Islands is a popular family attraction from Hurghada. The
stunning natural island can be reached by a boat trip in a matter of
minutes and features a gorgeous collection of corals reefs, tropical
fish and other examples of abundant underwater ecology. There is a
Giftun island National Park that houses a coral garden full of bright
corals in dazzling hues. The white, sandy beach is ideal for swimming
and sunbathing. Visitors can also take a walk through the coastal
stretch to spot the interesting oceanic life of the region or take a
glass boat ride to enjoy spotting coral reefs in emerald waters from
the glass bottom of the boat.

**Red Sea Aquarium **

The Red Sea Aquarium is a great place to take the children to
acquaint them with the copious and diverse marine life of Hurdhada.
There are barbs, sharks, stingrays, cattle –fish and whole lot of other
fascinating species in an absolutely ingeniously created natural
environment. Visitors will almost feel like they are walking underwater
amidst these creatures. There is an educational center in the premises
that has regular information sessions and talks about Hurghada’s marine
life. The creatures are all well-categorized with diagrams and
important information on their lineage, habits and quirks.

Hurghada is an exciting diving resort that has several enjoyable
activities for families including water sports, desert safaris, coral
gardens and marine museums.
