# Ministry of Sound

*Africa > Egypt > Hurghada > Nightlife And Ente > Ministry Of Sound*

[Hurghada](/africa/egypt/hurghada)'s **Ministry of Sound Beach Club** is the world's only beachside Ministry venue, a simply stunning 2500 capacity club, with a raised VIP area behind the DJ booth, a circular dance-floor and a boat shaped bar just before the beach.

Covered in palm trees glowing with lights, the soundsystem is undoubtedly one of the country's best.

With a groove-filled weekly schedule and regular guest DJ slots from international names such as Brandon Block, Ratpack, Marco V, Miss Kelly Marie, Judge Jules and more there's something for everyone with a love of house, techno and R&B/hip-hop.
