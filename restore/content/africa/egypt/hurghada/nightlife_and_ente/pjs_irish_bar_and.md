# PJ's Irish Bar and Restaurant

*Africa > Egypt > Hurghada > Nightlife And Ente > Pjs Irish Bar And*

PJ's Irish Bar and Restaurant is located in Sekalla, beside Roma hotel.

It is a family run Irish bar and offers a friendly location to relax during the day, fun nights including pub quiz's and karoke.

It has a large screen for showing all English Football and many other sporting activities.

In true Irish style the alcohol is cheap and the craic is great.

Join us for an excellent night out for all the family.
