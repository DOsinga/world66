# 1Day tour to Edfu & Kom Ombo from Luxor

*Africa > Egypt > Luxor > Day Trips > 1Day Tour To Edfu*

1Day tour to Edfu & Kom Ombo from Luxor $ 90

Go Egypt Travel representative will pick you up from your hotel by air-conditioned modern van to visit Edfu, as soon as you arrive Edfu you will enjoy a horse carriage going to Edfu temple, 123 km. north of Aswan on the West Bank of the Nile, which was dedicated to the worship of god Horus represented as a falcon, which consider one of the most beautiful Egyptian temples. Then you will be transferred to visit Kom Ombo temple, 45km. north of Aswan, built on a hillside overlooking the Nile, it was constructed in the Ptolemaic Roman era for the worship of god Haroeris and Sobek, the crocodile god.
