# Nightlife and Entertainment in Mansoura

*Africa > Egypt > Mansoura > Nightlife And Ente*

Mansoura is a conservative city where foreigners do not visit that often. For this reason there are not a lot of entertainment options offered, and alcohol is not offered in many places.

The 2 main hotels in the city, namely the Global Hotel and the Marshal Hotel, as well as a handful of restaurants will serve alcohol, but visitors should be careful about public drunkenness and wearing short mini-skirts out, as this is frowned upon. In fact women are expected to cover at least a third of their bodies at all times regardless of the humid climate.

There are some cafes, called Ahwa’s that you can visit but you should rather not use the public transportation as the streets are very crowded in Mansoura. This city is situated on the banks of the Nile River and this in itself provides great entertainment as you can relax and enjoy the views.

The Mansoura University provides students with entertainment on the three campuses including restaurants, the Olympic Village and an eating hall.
