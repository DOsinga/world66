# Farsha Cafe in Sharm El Sheikh: Magical place to visit

*Africa > Egypt > Sharmelsheikh > Bars And Cafes > Farsha Cafe In Sha*

Farsha Cafe is definitely one of the most beautiful chillout cafés in the world, located in Hadaba, at the slope of a cliff overlooking a lighthouse and with the most amazing views over the Red Sea and Ras Mohammad. As you descent the winding stairs down to the cafe you cannot help having your breath taken away by the beauty of the place and the sea below it with the boats, the restaurants around the cafe, the lighthouse, the beach. The Cafe is really a very romantic place, with lush oriental carpets, futons and cushions laid out within a movie like setting straight from some magical Arabian story. You will be surrounded by antique-like oriental furniture and decorations, candles everywhere, colourful paper lampions, wooden carved window frames and doors, placed randomly as if you could use them to escape reality. As you relax on the low seating enjoy the amazing herbal and aromatic teas (I recommend the cinnamon one) or indulge in something more substantial. No alcohol is ever going to be needed there as you will already feel dazed with the beauty of the place. A sensuous chill out music, with just the right volume, will sooth you and is just loud enough to block out the soft voices of tourists and locals alike who seem equally amazed at this place sipping their drinks and smoking shisha.

Perfect for: Proposal! Post-dinner chill out. Or just relaxed talks with friends and loved ones.

For more information about Farsha go to: http://farshacafe.com/ - they have some fabulous panoramic photos on the site so you can admire the beauty of the place.
