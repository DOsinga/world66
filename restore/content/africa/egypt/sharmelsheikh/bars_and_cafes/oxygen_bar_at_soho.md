# Oxygen Bar at Soho Square Sharm El Sheikh

*Africa > Egypt > Sharmelsheikh > Bars And Cafes > Oxygen Bar At Soho*

Sited on the ground floor below the Pangaea nightclub, this bar creates that exciting feeling of anticipation for a good night ahead. The "latest sounds" can be heard here, pitched at just the right level as to create a great setting that makes this trendy bar a regular hangout for many people.

With the added feature of an Oxygen counter, where you can refresh and revialise yourself with a dose of pure oxygen, to recover from a hard day on the beach or a late night clubbing the day before.
