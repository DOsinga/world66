Itinerary:-
(Cairo tours & excursions from Sharm by flight)

Our representative will pick you up from your hotel in Sharm & transfer you to Sharm airport for flight to Cairo, upon arrival Cairo airport, you will be met & assisted by our Cairo representative. After that our Egyptologist guide will accompany you to discover the enigmatic Pyramids of Giza of King Cheops , Chefren , Mykernuis , Sphinx , & Valley temple which are the most famous monuments of ancient Egypt, you can feel the mysteries surrounding their symbolism , design & purpose have inspired passionate debate, it’s likely that many of these mysteries will never be solved, then proceed your fantastic tour to visit the Egyptian antiquities museum commonly known as the Egyptian Museum which located in the capital city of Cairo, where you can see the most extensive collection of ancient antiquities , & the famous golden mask of Tutankhamun among other amazing pharaonic artifacts, then you will have a time for shopping at Khan El Khalili the most famous bazaar in the Middle east. In the evening you will be driven to Cairo Airport for flight back to Sharm.
