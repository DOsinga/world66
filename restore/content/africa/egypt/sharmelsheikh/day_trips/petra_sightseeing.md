# Petra Sightseeing tour from Sharm El Sheikh By plane

*Africa > Egypt > Sharmelsheikh > Day Trips > Petra Sightseeing*

Itinerary:-
**(Petra Tours from Sharm El Sheikh By Flight)**

Maestro Online Travel representative will pick you up from your hotel in **Sharm** at 4:30 am. by a modern air-conditioned vehicle to **Sharm **International Airport. Your Plane will take off at 6:00 am. and arriving at **Aqaba** Airport at 7:00 a.m. You will be transferred from **Aqaba** Airport to **Petra** by an air-conditioned vehicle accompanied by our representative to join your group (not private tour). Enjoy visiting **Nabataean City** tracing the **Nabataean** Arab tribes that settled there since the 6th century B.C. You will be dazzled by the temples and tombs that are carved into the rock. Visit the **Siq canyon**, the natural geological feature formed in the sandstone rocks, the dark and narrow entrance to the ancient city of **Petra**. Visit also **Al Khazina**, the building is carved out of sandstone and is a popular tourist attraction in **Petra**. After that you will be transferred to **Aqaba** Airport to return to your hotel in **Sharm** at 08:30 pm
