# Bars and Cafes in Accra

*Africa > Ghana > Accra > Bars And Cafes*

The Accra people pride themselves on their hospitality and cultural
uniqueness. This is easily carried over to their nightlife, in the Accra bars and cafés. Like all capital cities, Accra is the heart and soul of Ghana. It’s
the administrative, economic, cultural and educational capital of the
nation. Travel guides and news stories all talk about the
friendliness of the people here. It doesn’t take
long to strike up a conversation or make a friend in Accra.

Most of the trendy restaurants and bars are located in Osu, a suburb
of Accra, which is known for its nightlife. From live performances by
bands and troupes playing the traditional music, to the more modern hip
hop music, it's all there.

**Champs Sports Bar**

It’s the first theme bar in Accra and boasts of Mexican cuisine. On
Fridays and Saturdays, you can watch the big games live and also join
in the karaoke.

**Fusion**

One of Accra’s more up market restaurant bars, catering mainly to
the expatriate community. The best is Wednesdays if you’re into karaoke.

**Chester’s Place**

This is a popular pub with both tourists and locals. You can either
meet with the who’s who of Accra in person here, or enjoy the jam
sessions that go on till late.

**Bassline **

This jazz bar caters to the more mature and sophisticated. There's a full house on Fridays, with live jazz acts in session.

**7 Star **

This bar is open all week which includes a different theme each
night. Weekends pulsate with electrifying music for dancing and
partying into the early morning hours.

**Boomerang**

This bar packs in a good crowd on Friday and Saturday nights with a
good mix of Accra youth. It explodes with people on weekends and is a
very popular spot. This is one of the few bars in Accra that
encompasses both a bar scene and a café.

**Vibe Café**

This café is considered the finest radio station in Accra, Vibe FM,
which also hosts a café at its location on Farrar Avenue. The unique
thing about the café is that in the whole of the continent of Africa,
this is the only radio café. The delectable choices on the menu and the
breathtaking view of Accra from the café’s patio make it a favorite
with not just the locals but also the tourists.

Among the bars and cafés in this tropical locale, there’s something
for everyone. Tourists and locals alike love these top picks, and you
will too when traveling to this unique part of the world.
