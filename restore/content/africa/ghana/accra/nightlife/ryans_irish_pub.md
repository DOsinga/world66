# Ryan's Irish Pub & Restaurant

*Africa > Ghana > Accra > Nightlife > Ryans Irish Pub*

[Ryan's](/africa/ghana/accra/nightlife/ryans_irish_pub) is the first and only Irish Pub in West Africa. Situated in historic Osu, in the heart of the shopping and nightlife centre of Ghana's capital city.

Ryan's caters mainly to the expatriate and local business communities, but the clientele ranges from the very young to the not so young, right through all the age groups
