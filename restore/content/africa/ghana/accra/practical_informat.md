# Practical Information in Accra

*Africa > Ghana > Accra > Practical Informat*

Accra is the capital city of the African country Ghana. The Republic of Ghana
is a small country located in Western Africa, bordered by the Ivory Coast,
Burkina Faso and Togo, and it lies on the coast of the Gulf of Guinea. Accra is not
only the capital city, but also the most populous city in Ghana. Accra is one of
the safer cities in Africa and most visitors find that walking around Accra is, in
general, hassle free, but it is best to always be on the lookout for
pickpockets.

**Location - **Accra is located on the southern coast of Ghana in Western
Africa, on the Gulf of Guinea. Accra is a port city and
is the administrative, and economic hub of Ghana.

**Population - **With Accra being the capital city of Ghana, it is also
recognized as the most populous city--with approximately 1.96 million residents
according to GeoNames geographical study. Much like the United States, Ghana
conducts a countrywide census every 10 years, and in 2000 the count was
approximately 1.5 million residents. Accra, Ghana is growing at a steady pace
due to its tourist charm and warm, sunny climate.

**Language - **The official language of Ghana is English, due to its early
British colonial roots. There are also 9 other languages that the government
of Ghana has sponsored: Akan, Dagaare/Wale, Dangme, Dagbane, Ga, Ewe, Kasem, and
Nzema. The 2 dialects of Akan that are most popular are Twi and Fante and are the dialects are widely spoken in Accra, and they are the dialects that are becoming increasingly popular
with the locals.

**Time Zone - **The time zone for Accra is Standard Time Zone, Greenwich
Mean Time, or GMT (Identical to the United Kingdom). Ghana does not recognize
daylight saving time.

**Currency - **The Ghanaian Cedi is the unit of currency for the country and is referred to as the GHS. The Cedi is divided into 100 pesewas. Cedi
banknotes come in 1,5,10,20,and 50 Cedi form.

**Electricity - **240V/50Hz, 230 volts is the most popular type of
voltage. Three round pins arranged in a triangle, or two parallel flat pins with
ground pin (same as the United Kingdom) are the main types of outlet plugs. If
you are traveling with electronic devices that do not operate with 230 volts,
you will need a voltage converter (international plug adaptor).

**Telephone - ** The country code for Ghana is 233, and the international
city code for Accra is 21.

**Weather - **Ghana is a tropical country. Accra is located in the dry
zone and maintains an average temperature annually of 72 degrees F to
80 degrees F. The rainy season is from April to June, but the rains are
not typically continuous throughout the day.
