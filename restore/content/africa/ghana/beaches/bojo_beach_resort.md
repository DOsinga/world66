BOJO BEACH RESORT

Bojo Beach Resort is set up to promote hospitality and Tourism Activities as well as recreational facility. Bojo Beach Resort is located at Bortianor, on the Kokrobite Road, a suburb of Accra and is one of the most attractive and well kept beaches in Ghana. In order to go the the beach one has to take a short boat ride across the Densu river. The Beach is located at the delta of the Densu river and the Atlantic Ocean. It is fabulous and unique. It is nature's best kept secret in Ghana.
