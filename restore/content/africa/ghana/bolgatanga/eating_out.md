# Eating Out in Bolgatanga

*Africa > Ghana > Bolgatanga > Eating Out*

Hi Taste in the new Lorry Park has the best goat in the city! It is tender unlike most of the other places in the town, and the soups there are delicions. Try the fufu, too! A delightful, bustling place (where the service is not the best, but the food is worth the wait!)!

### [The Black Star](/africa/ghana/bolgatanga/eating_out/the_black_star)

The Black Star is one of the best places to eat in town (if you can find the chef). The relaxed atmosphere is almost as charming as the head chef, Clement. They have a wide variety of Ghanaian and Western dishes, and the chef has a special nack with garlic pasta!
