# Dixcove

*Africa > Ghana > Dixcove*

Dixcove is in the south-west of Ghana. The beaches at Dixcove are perfect: white sand, great wheather, palm trees.

 There is a beautiful Fort, the Medal Cross Fort, which overlooks the bay. It has a natural harbour and the port is a lively area. You can buy fresh lobesters, for which the area is renowned.
