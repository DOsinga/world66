# Kakum nature reserve

*Africa > Ghana > Kakumnaturereserve*

Kakum nature reserve lies in Central Region near Cape Coast the regions capital. You can get there by lorry or taxi.

In the park, there's a small museum about nature and forest reserve, and you can walk around in asmall part of what's left of a Rainforest in Ghana.

Either you walk on the ground or you take the canopy walk which'll take you above the smallest trees and in the highest part of the forest. Really, it's a must for everyone.
