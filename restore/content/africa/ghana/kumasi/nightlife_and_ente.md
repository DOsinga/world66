# Nightlife and Entertainment in Kumasi

*Africa > Ghana > Kumasi > Nightlife And Ente*

The city of Kumasi is full of nightclubs, movie centres, spots, etc. The streets of Bantama especially Bantmaa High Street is the one to talk of when one want to enjoy himself. it is said that the people of Bantama never sleep. Also the city has Kiravi Night Club, Fox Trap, Genesis Night Club, Cafe Masserati, Yegoala etc. Kiravi Night Club has won the number night club Ghana awards in Ghana for three consecutive years. Aside night Clubs, the City has about 23 FM stations, and 2 cable TV Stations - Fontomfrom and Crystal tv, Hotels, etc.
