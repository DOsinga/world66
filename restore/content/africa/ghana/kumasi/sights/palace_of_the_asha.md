# Palace of the Asantehene

*Africa > Ghana > Kumasi > Sights > Palace Of The Asha*

Visitng this palace with a guide you get an intresting view on the life inside the castle walls. Actually the place you're visiting is the old palace of the Ashantihene, but it is stuffed with history-related objects and paintings/pictures of the old ashanti kings.
