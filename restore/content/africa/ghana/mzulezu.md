# Mzulezu

*Africa > Ghana > Mzulezu*

A small village in the middle between Takoradi and the Côte d'Ivoir boarder, but really interesting to visit is Mzulezu. It's located quiet close to the coast, in the middle of a lake or swomp. The only way to get to the village is by canoe, and you best pay for a guide who will take you there.
