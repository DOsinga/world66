# Money

*Africa > Guineabissau > Practicalinformat > Money*

Currency : CFA : 655-665 CFA = 1 euro

Banks : No credit cards, no TC, and forget ATM's..Carry cash only preferably euros. The only Bank accepting to change currency is the BAO (located very close the Ta Mar, and the Pensao Central (a 30 seconds walk)). The BCEA in front of the Pensao Central only caters for locals, and the BIGB (international bank of Guinea Bissao) is closed. Facts dating feb 2004, subject to constant change.

Change : Found anywhere near the central market. Prefer to change in the "official" change shops. Transactions are discrete. "Change dealers" open from 8h to 10 pm monday to sunday, so you really won't have to worry. Going rate seems to be 655 CFA, but with big notes, rates should be higher.
