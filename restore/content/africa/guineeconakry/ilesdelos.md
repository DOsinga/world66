# Iles de los

*Africa > Guineeconakry > Ilesdelos*

The Iles de Los are a group of islands where people from [Conakry](/africa/guineeconakry/conakry) come to relax and have a good time. Most expats go to Ile de Roume which is quiet. Guineans go to the Ile de Kassa which is quite lively, especially in weekends. 



Boats from Conakry leave from the beach near the Novotel. You either can rent a boat to yourself or take one of the regular priogues that go to Soro village.
