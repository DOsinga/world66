# Labe

*Africa > Guineeconakry > Labe*

Labe is about 400 km north of [Conakry](/africa/guineeconakry/conakry) at the end of the tar road through the Fouta Djalon. It's the third town of the country and a major stopping point on the road to [Guinea-Bissau](/africa/guineabissau). Labe is not a very attractive town, but for the adventurous it is a good base for exploring the Fouta Djalon. 120 km north of Labe, Mount Loura is over 1500 meter high.
