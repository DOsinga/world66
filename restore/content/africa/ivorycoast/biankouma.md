# Biankouma

*Africa > Ivorycoast > Biankouma*

Some 40 km north of [Man](/africa/ivorycoast/man) on the road to Touba, Bainkouma is best visited on sundays, when you can see the acrobatic Goua dancers. The old town is quite attractive as well. Before entering you should ask the village chief, before taking a pciture you should make sure you are not photgraphing a fetish which is bad luck.

 There is a hotel here (Hotel du Mont Sangbe), but you may consider sleeping in [Gouessesso](/africa/ivorycoast/gouessesso).
