# Sights in Nyeri

*Africa > Kenya > Centralhighlands > Nyeri > Sights*

Here are a few hotspots.

### [Gakere Rd](/africa/kenya/centralhighlands/nyeri/sights/gakere_rd)

This is the main street in Nyeri town leading from the bus park to the middle of town, most visitors to Nyeri town coming by public transport (Matatu) will most likely find themseleves on this street first thing. Here you will find everything from restaurants, supermarkets, Taxi, bookshops to Cyber Cafe's.

### [Aberdare](/africa/kenya/centralhighlands/nyeri/sights/aberdare)

Most visitors to Kenya will most likely be out to watch wildlife. When in Nyeri Aberdare National park is a must, here are most likely to see Elephants, Gazelles, Leopards, Lions and many other species of wildlife.

There is also the infamous Treetops Hotel located inside the park

type: Parks, Gardens and Zoos
