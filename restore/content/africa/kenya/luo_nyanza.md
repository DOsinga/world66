# Luo Nyanza Travel Guide

*Africa > Kenya > Luo Nyanza*

The best resource for sights, hotels, restaurants, bars, what to do and see

Luo Nyanza is the region around the Kavirondo Gulf of Lake Victoria. It is the home of the Great Jaramogi, Oginga Odinga, Oder a Kango, and now Hon. Raila Odinga and Senior Barack Obama and Sarah Obama.
The Luos are the fishermen, the Nilotics and the Occupiers of the Provincila Capital of Nyanza - kisumu city.
