Club Rio- This Bar and grill is located right in the heart of Mombasa.has the feel of a sports bar with a good number of screens adorning the walls.Excellent food too-reasonably priced.Right in the heart of downtown Mombasa.

The place seems to be popular with bueracrats from the surrounding government district.A great surprise was the decent rooms they have,starting at $15 including continental breakfast.

The place was overflowing the saturday they had a big English premier league game.Major crowd on Friday and Saturday night too.Good place to chiil out.
