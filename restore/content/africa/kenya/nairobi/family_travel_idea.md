# Family Travel Ideas

*Africa > Kenya > Nairobi > Family Travel Idea*

Nairobi is a natural and fun-filled family holiday destination that
offers several opportunities for wildlife spotting and mystical
cultural discoveries. Here are a few ideas to make the most of
your family vacation in this wild Kenyan hotspot.

**Nairobi**** National Park**** **

Hire a jeep and go game viewing and mammal spotting at Nairobi’s
most illustrious wildlife park. Children will enjoy viewing some of
most magnificent species of the big cats along with the wild
buffaloes, rhinos, giraffes, zebras, warthogs and a host of other
animals and birds in their natural surroundings. Visitors can also hire
an experienced guide and take a nature walking trip at the Education
Center. The park houses an Animal Orphanage that tends to sick and
injured animals and nurses them back to health. March and April are the
best months to visit the park since the yearly migration period offers
an opportunity to view a greater variety of wildlife species.

**Giraffe**** Center**

The Giraffe Center is one of the most popular attractions for kids
traveling to Nairobi. The center houses the endangered Rothschild
Giraffes amidst a tranquil natural environment and visitors can hobnob
with the graceful creatures, while indulging in delightful ‘giraffe
feeding’ sessions. There is a self-guided nature trail for travelers
looking to explore the natural beauty of the region. Visitors can also
view several species of rare and indigenous birds in the lush greenery
of the park.

**Riuki Cultural Center **

This is a nice place to get acquainted the tribal culture and ethnic
profile of Kenya. The colorful Riuki Cultural Center hosts several
local dance presentations, theatrical performances and cultural
lectures to keep travelers enthralled. Visitors can even witness
customs relating to the civilization and daily lifestyle of the Kikuyu
Tribe and sample delicious local cuisine at the food center.

**National**** Museum of Kenya **

One of the most frequented family attractions in Nairobi is the
National Museum of Kenya, a storehouse of pre-historical wonders and
native artifacts. There is an art gallery showcasing the aesthetic
prowess of Nairobi along with a National Archives Center that houses a
huge collection of Kenyan snapshots and art.

**Snake**** Park**

View rare and fascinating varieties of reptiles including venomous
snakes and deadly native crocodiles at the Snake Park. The poisonous
and quirkily colored snakes are housed inside thin glass cages and
deeply dug pits. One of the most startling and interesting vistas is
the sight of the crocodile devouring its prey in a matter of seconds. This one’s an exciting place to experience the intriguing reptiles of
Kenya.

Nairobi is a striking montage of the African wildlife and has
several cultural attractions and family wildlife viewing activities.
