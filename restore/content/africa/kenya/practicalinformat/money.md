# Money

*Africa > Kenya > Practicalinformat > Money*

Currency in Kenya is the Shilling (KSh). Most of the prices in this section are listed in Kenya Shilling. The prices are based on prices in 1998. However, in 1999, the Shilling fell substantially. Therefore, for many hotels and restaurants, expect to pay 10% - 25% more than the prices I have quoted.  Currently, (December 1999), 70 Kenyan Shillings are equal to one U.S. Dollar.
