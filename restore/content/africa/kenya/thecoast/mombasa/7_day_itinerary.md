# 7 Day Itinerary in Mombasa

*Africa > Kenya > Thecoast > Mombasa > 7 Day Itinerary*

7 days is a perfect amount of time to explore Mombasa and its
environs. Check out this 7-day itinerary so that you can make the most
out of your adventure of a lifetime to Kenya.**

**

**Day 1: Arrive and Get Settled
**

Arrive via train or airplane, find your hotel and get settled in.
Get acquainted with the city and stroll through the Old Town to get a
feel for the city. Sign up for a charted dinner cruise in an Arab dhow,
where you can have some stunning views of the city and enjoy a world
class meal.

**Day 2: Local Culture**

Start your day off with a trip to Ft Jesus, the city’s largest
museum and attraction. Built in 1593 by the Portuguese, this storied
fort now has daily tours that offer a glimpse into prison cells and
torture rooms for slaves before they were traded. The adjoining museum
gives a nice overview of the fort’s role throughout the city’s history.
Enjoy lunch and head over to Old Town to walk through Mombasa’s varied
architecture, which tells the stories of its history with its strong
European, Arab and African influences. Hire a guide to really get the
most out of your experience.

**Day 3: Beach Day**

Hit one of the local beaches and enjoy a day of fun in the sun. Both
Bamburi Beach and Nyali Beach are within a short distance from Mombasa,
and are the best beaches in the area. Relax on the beach and soak up
some sun or spend your day enjoying some of the watersports available
such as snorkeling, scuba diving and water skiing. Eat near the beaches
at one of the many cafes and street stands for lunch, try one of the
resorts for dinner and be sure to stick around for sunset on the beach.

**Day 4:** **Safari Day Trip**

Use this day to visit a nearby safari and see close up the
breathtaking African wildlife. Located within easy driving distance for
a day trip, both the Shimba Hills National Reserve and the Tsavo East
and West National Park have large wildlife communities of elephants,
leopards, lions and giraffes. No trip to Kenya is complete without a
wildlife excursion and this should not be missed. Book a tour through
your hotel or local travel agency.

**Day 5: Culture and Beach**

Head north to Malinda today and combine a day of culture with the
beach. Check out the Gedi Ruins just south of Malinda, where you can
stop and see the ancient lost civilization’s ruins. Then head to the
Malinda beach to spend some time snorkeling on the beautiful reefs
here. Book a trip to the Malinda Marine Park to enjoy some really
amazing views of ocean life.

**Day 6: Shopping and Beach**

Spend some time at the shops along Moi Avenue and Nyerere Street
where you can find many traditional handicrafts like woven baskets,
carvings and clothing. Return to one of your favorite beaches in the
afternoon for a final swim, snorkel or just a relaxing afternoon.

**Day 7: Return Home**

Eat breakfast in Mombasa one last time and head to the airport or train station to head home.
