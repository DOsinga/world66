# Tembo Disco

*Africa > Kenya > Thecoast > Mombasa > Entertainment > Tembo Disco*

**Tembo Entertainment Plaza** is the largest Entertainment complex on Kenya's Coast, comprising of a **large Open Air Disco** (capacity ca. 3000 people), two **Barbeque Restaurants**, a **Billiard Lounge**, a **Beer Garden, **a **GOGO Cocktail Bar** and **Guest Rooms**. It's set up on different layers in an old quarry. The
Tembo is opened daily, the Beer Garden 24hrs around the clock and the Disco
from 9pm until 6am.
Access to Tembo is very convenient and easy since it's located direct on the Mombasa-Malindi Main Road.
A State of the Art music and light systems, Top DJ's and the enchanting tropical athmosphere make **Tembo the ideal place to dance the night away**.
Regular Cabaret Shows, Musical Concerts, Competitions, Theme Nights and other Entertainment spice up the night.
