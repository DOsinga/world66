# Burch's camp site

*Africa > Kenya > Theriftvalley > Naivasha > Eatingoutentertainment > Burchscampsite*

The Burch’s camp is situated at the shores of the Lake Naivasha , 100kms North West of Nairobi , and the cleanest fresh water lake in the Rift Valley. The area is famous for its bird life, beautiful sceneries including highlands and escarpments.
The Lake Naivasha derived its name from a Maasai word ‘Naiposha’ which means a depression with water.
The Burch’s camp is located about 8Kms from Naivasha town off Moi south drive. Better placed in terms of accessibility and closeness to the lake, lush green vegetation, well manicured grounds, swaying tree branches, bird singing and fresh air welcomes you to a home away from home.
