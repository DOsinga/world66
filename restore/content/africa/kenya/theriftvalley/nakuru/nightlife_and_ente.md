# Nightlife and Entertainment in Nakuru

*Africa > Kenya > Theriftvalley > Nakuru > Nightlife And Ente*

Nakuru is one of the most happening spots in the all of Kenya, and there are many wonderful places to visit when it comes to the **Nakuru nightlife. **There are all sorts of clubs and bars you can visit, including:

**Dimples Disco**

Dimples disco is a small club that&rsquo;s lively on the weekends. This club is located at the west end of Kenyatta Rd, near the roundabout. It is an upstairs building and leads directly in.

**Mahogany**

Directly across the hall from Dimples Disco is a bar called Mahogany. This place is perfect for listening to the latest tunes, shooting pool and eating nyama choma, more commonly known in the west as barbeque.

**Millennium **

One of the best places to get barbeque in the middle of a club is a place called Millennium. Millennium is a barbeque during the day and a dance floor at night. This dance floor is mostly out doors and has three separate bars and four different pool tables. This place seats about a hundred people comfortably, however, during the weekends, the place is usually packed with at least triple that number.

**Coco Savana Disco**

If you&rsquo;re interested in going to a more fancy up to do club, then the Coco Savana Disco is what you&rsquo;re looking for. This area is meant for the more well to do and wealthy of the young.

**Taidy&rsquo;s**

Taidy&rsquo;s is both a restaurant and a party joint. This place includes a night club, which has become increasingly popular with the youth. At night this place becomes a night club, a dance floor and a bar.

**Tickles**

Tickles is a simple bar located above the Piranha restaurant. This is a bar and a disco. Thanks to the fact that it&rsquo;s close to a restaurant, if you&rsquo;re hungry for some real food, you can just go right over there.

**Summit**

Summit is a new recently opened bar and night club that&rsquo;s become one of the more popular joints in the area. It gets natural aesthetic appeal from the fact that it&rsquo;s located directly adjacent to the Lake Nakuru national park. This place has garnered popularity from the fact that it&rsquo;s both aesthetically pleasing and affordable. Unfortunately, the sound system doesn&rsquo;t do much and the DJ doesn&rsquo;t have popular reviews, but on the other hand, the place is known for it&rsquo;s charm and the down to earth cooking that it offers it&rsquo;s patrons.
