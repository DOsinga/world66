# Getting There

*Africa > Lesotho > Getting There*

Air

Lesotho's national airline is *Lesotho Airways Corporation (QL)*. Lesotho Airways has daily flights to and from Johannesburg (flight time is approximately 1 hour 10 minutes) where connections to the rest of the world can be made.

Approximate flight time

From Maseru to *London* is 14 hours (including a stopover of 2 hours).

International airport:

*Maseru (MSU)* (Moshoeshoe I International) is 18km (11 miles) south of Maseru. Buses go to the city (travel time  30 minutes). Airport facilities include a bank and bureau de change (with limited opening hours on Tuesday and Friday) bar restaurant (open 0800-1630) flight information (0800-1630) left luggage facilities car rental and post office (open 0800-1630).

Departure tax

M20. Transit passengers and children under five years of age are exempt.

Road

There are three major road links to South Africa: at Maseru Bridge at Ficksburg Bridge and at Caledonsport. Other crossing points exist but the road surfaces are less good. All of these are open by 0800 but some close as early as 1600.

Rail

Lesotho is linked with the South African railway system by a short line (2.6km/1.6 miles) from Maseru to Marseilles on the Bloemfontein/Natal main line.
