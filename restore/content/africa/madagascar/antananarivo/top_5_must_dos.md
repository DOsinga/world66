# Top 5 Must Do's in Antananarivo

*Africa > Madagascar > Antananarivo > Top 5 Must Dos*

There some **Antananarivo must do's** when you reach this city
which is the capital of Madagascar. You are sure to enjoy your stay
here.

Among the many things to do here are sightseeing, enjoying wildlife
like birdwatching, and for the adventurous you can go hiking.

**Tsingy de Bemaraha Strict Nature Reserve**

A visit to this reserve is an Antananarivo must do. Take a tour to
one of the most unique places in all of Madagascar. This place full of
limestone eroded valleys and hills. The spectacular views are
breathtaking. You will also find jungles, lagoons, and marshes that are
untouched.

**Wildlife Tours**

As for wildlife, this place is full of all kinds of endangered
species. Here you will be able to see lemurs, chameleons and exotic birds
in places like the Special Reserve of Andasibe. You will also find rare
plants and wild flowers here. Close by is a village where you can
witness the culture of the local people.

**Royal Hill of Ambohimanga

**

Another Antananarivo must do's is visiting this hill. You will find
faithfuls from all over visiting the various holy places on the hill.
Religious rituals are also performed at various places here. The hill was
once a place where the nobles of Madagascar used to live. It also a
place full of history.

**Shopping**

There are various places to do your shopping in Antananarivo. Zoma, which is open daily, is the place for souvenirs. Another market is
Tana where you will get all kinds of curios. Independence Avenue
is the place to get things like local jewelry or house decorations like
wall hangings. You may also get all kinds of beautiful fabric to take
home. Also, visit Atelier Jacaranda where you will get all kinds of
batiks on sale.

**Entertainment**

The last Antananarivo must do before your trip ends is to enjoy
yourself. Centre Culturel Albert Camus has various shows, plays and
sometimes artists exhibit their paintings. Le Six is a famous joint where
you can while away the night. Here, you will get quality music (every
night there is a different culture night) and all kinds of drinks.
Another place to go dancing is Le Bus where various musicians perform on
Thursdays and during the other nights you will get different kinds of
music. You may also find them hosting a theme party so make sure you
visit this place.

These are the 5 Antananarivo must do's when you travel to this
wonderful Madagascan city.
