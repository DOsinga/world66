# Sights

*Africa > Mali > Djenne > Sights*

The main sight of town is without a doubt the mosque of Djenne, but do wander about town a bit to explore the other interesting things.

**Sights**

**Market**

Monday is market day in Djenne. People from the area around come to town to buy and sell goods. It is great to walk around, to look at the goods (the cloth, especially), and to haggle. People are friendly and easy going generally speaking. 

**Madrassa**

There is a big number of madrassa's or Coranic schools in Djenne. They are not always very easy to find but a local guide can show them to you and explain about the teaching methods that are still in use.
