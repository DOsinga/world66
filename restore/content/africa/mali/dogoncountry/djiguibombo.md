# Djiguibombo

*Africa > Mali > Dogoncountry > Djiguibombo*

Djiguibombo is easy to reach from [Bandiagara](/africa/mali/dogoncountry/bandiagara) and is an excellent place from which to explore Ende, Teli and Kani Kombole. All these Dogon villages have so called 'Case de Passage' where you can spend the night. Djiguibombo has one as well. To get to and from Bandiagara use a mobylette or walk down the escarpment.
