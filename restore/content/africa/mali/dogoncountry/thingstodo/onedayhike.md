# One day hike

*Africa > Mali > Dogoncountry > Thingstodo > Onedayhike*

For a one day hike, start in
[Sanga](/africa/mali/dogoncountry/sanga). There are different one day hikes starting and finishing there, called Petit Tour (7 km), Moyen Tour (10 km) and Grand Tour (15 km).

From
[Bankas](/africa/mali/dogoncountry/bankas) you can also make a one day trip to the escarpment, but it will be a full day, starting early and returning quite late.

From
[Bandiagara](/africa/mali/dogoncountry/bandiagara) it is not well feasible to do it as one day trip. Better take two days.
