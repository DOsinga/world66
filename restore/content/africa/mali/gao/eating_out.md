# Eating Out

*Africa > Mali > Gao > Eating Out*

The food in the market is pretty okay. Those with sensitive digestion may want to go easy on the meat. Try any of the hotels in the section Gao/accommodation or one of the following addresses for a complete meal.

**Restaurants**

**Le Camerouni**

This is a cheap place to get a meal. It's in the area around thePalais de Justice.

**Le Senegalais**

A somewhat more expensive place doing better food. In the area around the Palais the Justice.
