# Sights

*Africa > Mali > Mopti > Sights*

The main sight is the town isn't itself with its bad structures, small streets and laid back atmosphere. Here is a list things you will want to photograph while you wander around town. 

**Sights**

**The Mosque**

The Mosque in Mopti was built in 1935. It was modelled after the one in [Djenne](/africa/mali/djenne) . It is hard to be let in if you are not a muslim, but you can climb the roof of one of the houses nearby to let you have a sneak view of the mosque. FUCK OFF!!

**River trips**

A nice excursion can be a trip with a prirogue on the river. If you make a somewhat lonegr trip, you can visit nearby villages (Kaladoga e.g.). Go to the Bar Bozo and you will easily find a boat owner willing to take you. Dusk is a good time.

**Grand Marche**

The Grand Marche is in the Old Town, not too far from the Mosque. Thursday is market day. There is a lot of dried fish to be bought, as well as salt form the salt mines near [Timbuktu](/africa/mali/timbuktu).
