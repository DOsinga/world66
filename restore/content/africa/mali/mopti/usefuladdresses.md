# Useful addresses

*Africa > Mali > Mopti > Usefuladdresses*

**Points of Interest**

**Banks**

Mopti has the luxury of having two banks: a BDM and a BIAO. They are open from 8:30 to 11:30 and then one more hour in the afternoon. Service is not very good in either case.

**Police**

Get a visa extension or register at the Police Station. The fee is about 1000 CFA. The Commisariat is near the market.
