# Le Nil Bleu

*Africa > Morocco > Agadir > Eatingout > Le Nil Bleu*

A fantastic restaurant...one we spent most nights of our holiday at! Located on the beach front.

The staff are friendly and the food is delicious. I recommend the Tajine (local cuisine of meat and veg served in a clay pot!) You will really enjoy.
