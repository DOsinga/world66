# Shopping

*Africa > Morocco > Agadir > Shopping*

Apart from the big souk of Agadir, there is another but small shop called Medina Souvenirs, where you can find morocco handicraft, gifts and decorations (wood ornaments, jewelry, fossils, carpets, slippers etc )

Medina souvenirs is situated at the avenue of Massira near the main souk. Txis drivers know where.

In Medina Souvenirs, prices are put on all products. They also do the export of teh morocco handicraft and the argan oil (culinary and cosmetics)

For people who are interested in dealing with Medina Souvenirs as a local privider for handicraft, you can go to : http://www.medina-souvenirs.com
