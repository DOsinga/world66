**Oued ZAT (river)**

Ait Ourir is crossed by Oued Zat, Zat river. Nowadays, it's nearly empty from March to December but we still its last fury (1986/87) when the bridge (called "bridge of Zat") and half of "Coq Hardy", a lovely "Auberge" falled down the river.
