# Desert trip from Marrakech

*Africa > Morocco > Day Trips > Desert Trip From M*

Marrakech Desert Trips is a licensed and fully bonded travel company based in Marrrakech, Morocco. We are specialized in organizing hassle free desert trips from Marrakech to Morocco Sahara desert on a private or shared budget tours basis. Our tours vary from a 2 days desert trip from Marrakech to Zagora, a 3 days desert trip from Marrakech to Merzouga and a 4 days desert trip from Marrakech to both Zagora and Merzouga via the volcanic plateau of the Anti Atlas Mountains.
All our Morocco desert tours do include a camel trekking activity and an overnight sleep at our Morocco desert camps in Erg Chebbi, Erg Cehgaga, or Zagora sand dunes.
Please contact us for all your Morocco desert enquiries.
