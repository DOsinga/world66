# Getting There

*Africa > Morocco > Eljadida > Getting There*

El Jadida is 100 km south of Casablanca. It is reached most easily by bus. There is a railway station but trains to it are irregular and the station itself is far out of town. Also frequent buses going south to [Safi](/africa/morocco/safi), Oulidia and [Essaouira](/africa/morocco/essaouira). You can find shared taxi's to [Azemmour](/africa/morocco/azemmour).
