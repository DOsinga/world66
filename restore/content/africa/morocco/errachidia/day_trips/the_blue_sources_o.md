# The Blue Sources of Meski

*Africa > Morocco > Errachidia > Day Trips > The Blue Sources O*

The blue sources of Meski is a spring gushing fourth from the rock of a hillside and forms a huge blue water lake. It is located about 21 km from the city and is quite a beautiful sight to see surrounded by a beautiful palm plantation in the middle of the dessert. Its refreshing waters attract swimmers and the travellers who stop off during their jeep tours before venturing off into the dessert.
