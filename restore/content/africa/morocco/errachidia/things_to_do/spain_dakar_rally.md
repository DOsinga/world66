# Lisbon - Dakar Rally 2006

*Africa > Morocco > Errachidia > Things To Do > Spain Dakar Rally*

The Rally in 2006 took off from Lisbon, Portugal, and went through Errachidia. On Monday, 2 January the race went from Nador to Errachidia. Then On the next day, 3 January, the race went from Errachidia to Ouarzazate.
