# Trek in Toubkal

*Africa > Morocco > Imlil > Day Trips > Trek In Toubkal*

**Tizi n'Tamaterte (2279m of Altitude) Half or Full Day - **
**A gentel walk to the est of Dar Achain towards Tizi n’Tamaterte (2279m) will take you to see the most typical valley in the high atlas mountains, which Imnane valley, several berber villages and also a spectavulaire view of the high atlas mountains, lunch on the pass & a break before going back to the house. **

****

****

****

**Toubkal Ascent 2 days & 1 night - **
**After breakfast, walk along Ait Mizane valley, following the mule path & cross the Marabaout Sidi Chamharouch continue to Toubkal hut. Overnight stay. **
**The next day after an early breakfast, start Mt Toubkal ascent which takes approx 3h walking uphill, picnic lunch at the peak, enjoy the views and back down to the hut for lunch which takes & 2h walking downhill. On the afternoon continue to Imlil, night at Dar Achain.**
