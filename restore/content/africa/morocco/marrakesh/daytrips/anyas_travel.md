*Africa > Morocco > Marrakesh > Daytrips > Anyas Travel*

**Anyas Travel** is a full-service travel company located in Marrakech, Morocco, specializing in individual and group tours. Whether you have a destination in mind or you want to leave the planning to us, **we are here to show you the best of Morocco .**

Example:

Leave Marrakesh at 9 a.m. and travel through the Atlas Mountains, home to mainly Berber people. En route to the valley, we'll stop in a Berber village to have breakfast with a Berber family followed by a short camel ride. Upon arrival in Setti Fatma, we'll take a short hike up to the waterfalls and stop for a view of the Atlas Mountains. We'll stop for lunch along the way and arrive back in Marrakesh around 5:30 p.m.
