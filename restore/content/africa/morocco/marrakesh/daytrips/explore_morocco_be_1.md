# Explore Morocco beyond Marrakech

*Africa > Morocco > Marrakesh > Daytrips > Explore Morocco Be 1*

Taking a day trip out is perfect if you only have a short stay in Marrakech and want

to experience Morocco beyond the red city.

With the Atlas
Mountains, the Atlantic coast and some of Morocco’s most beautiful
sights only a short drive away the options are plenty and varied.

We
suggest you explore the coastal town of Essaouira, the beautiful Ouzoud
Waterfalls, the lush Ourika Valley or the dramatic and picturesque Ait
Ben Haddou.

If you have a few days to spare, we
suggest you go on a 2 or 3 day tour to explore the desert dunes and
spend a night in a desert camp under dozens of shooting stars.

For more details about day trips go to: **[http://berber-tours-morocco.com/day-trips/](/africa/morocco/marrakesh/daytrips)**
