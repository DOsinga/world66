**Merzouga New Year's Eve Party 2007-2008**

[HAVEN LA CHANCE DESERT HOTEL](/africa/morocco/merzouga/accommodation/haven_la_chance_de) PRESENTS

A NEW YEAR'S EVE PARTY UNDER THE STARS

The Black G'naoui tribe will dance and play their drums throughout the night- under the stars at Haven La Chance Hotel- Merzouga.

The highlight of the evening is the MECHUI lamb that has been been roasting slowly over a wooden fire for hours in a Berber adobe oven.

It is like a Disney World experience as you watch it brought to the
big tent while the G'naoui play on- under the cool of the night by a huge Bonfire.

Champagne is provided to bring in the New Year here in Merzouga , Morocco .

The magic of the desert is enhanced by sleeping in Khaimas- Nomad tents- along the Palm trees next to the hotel.

There are still a few rooms available in the hotel- with the toilets and showers
located down the hall.
