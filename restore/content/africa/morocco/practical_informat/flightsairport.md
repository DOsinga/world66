# Flights/Airport

*Africa > Morocco > Practical Informat > Flightsairport*

Flights: The most important piece of practical advice we can offer is to ensure that you get a seat near to the front of the aircraft and to get to passport control as quickly as possible. Queues here can take ages at times, and if you rush to get to passport control you can save an hour or more of queue time.
