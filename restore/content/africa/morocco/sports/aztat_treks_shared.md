# Aztat Treks shared Desert Tours

*Africa > Morocco > Sports > Aztat Treks Shared*

**Aztat Treks ( Part of Mountain Travel Morocco )**
**Mohamed Aztat**, an official mountain & desert tour guide Berber by origin and local to the Imlil region, previously worked as a muleteer, cook and tour guide for many British companies, speak excellent English, French, Arabic and Berber.
Mohamed has 13 years of experience working in the Atlas Mountains and is a graduate of the Mountain Guides School in Tabant ***(Centre CFAMM***), The Royal Moroccan Federation of Skiing and Mountaineering and is an accredited wild country assessor for expeditions in Morocco. Mohamed has been variously described as **"the best guide with whom I have walked"** ***by*** ***Conde Nas***t, **"One of the top guides in the Atlas Mountains"** ***by BBC Radio 2***, and **"one of the star guides of the Atlas"** ***by the Lonely Planet***. He truly loves his job and the region he grew up and lives in . Always proud to introduce his culture to visitors.

Mohamed offer shared desert tours at :
2 days/1night Zagora trip : 75€ per person
3 days/2nights erg chebbi & ait Benhaddou trip : 110€ per person
