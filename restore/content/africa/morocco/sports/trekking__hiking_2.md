# Trekking & Hiking in Morocco

*Africa > Morocco > Sports > Trekking  Hiking 2*

Discover untouched waterfalls and communities of real life. Experience mule trekking in the Atlas mountains, the most panoramic and breath taking views and scenery of North Africa. Guided by local official guides, staying in local residences, meet the Berber people and share the experience of a lifetime.
