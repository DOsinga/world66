# Painted Rocks

*Africa > Morocco > Tafraoute > Sights > Paintedrocks*

The mysterious Painted Rocks is a plain of boulders painted red, blue and black by a Belgian artist. The Rocks are in the valley and are easily accessible. Best time to visit is February, when the almond trees are blossoming.
