The National Association of Alternative Tourism
Situated in the North Moroccan city of Tetouan, the National Association of Alternative Tourism is an association that is specialised in leisure and sustainable development . Created in Octuber 2009, our mission is to promote the natural resources and culture of Morocco, supporting and contributing to Moroccan communities and residents by partnering with Moroccan authorities, tourist professionals, and local governments across the country. To support this mission, the National Association of Alternative Tourism serves individual travellers, travel agencies, organizations, sports and environmental clubs, as well as non-governmental organizations around the world- supporting alternative tourism. Our ludic activities are variegated. They include planning and hosting tailor-made leisure activities for both individuals and organizations
