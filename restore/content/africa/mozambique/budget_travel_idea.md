# Budget Travel Ideas in Mozambique

*Africa > Mozambique > Budget Travel Idea*

The **Mozambique Budget Travel**
planning is going to be a breeze. Mozambique is a famous tourist spot
and caters to travelers with any kind of budget. The tips below will
help you successfully complete your budget travel planning. Make the
most out of your Mozambique trip as it is a destination like no other.

**
** **Getting There**

Take
a flight into Maputo or Beira, both of which have international airports. Make
sure to book in advance to avail the discount fares that airlines
offer. LAM offers cheap flights into Maputo. Another option would be to
fly into South Africa and proceed to Mozambique by bus. The bus is an
economical mode of transport.

**Accommodations** **

**Accommodations can add up to a chunk of your travel expense. But Mozambique offers a
range of options for the budget conscious traveler. There are several
budget class hotels, hostels, guest houses as well as home stay facilities in
Mozambique. Barra Lake is one such lodging option. The rates are highly
economical. Located on the Inhambane
province, the hotel is equipped with all modern facilities. It is a
safe place for tourists. Similarly all provinces in Mozambique have
hotels that cater to tourists on a shoe string budget.

**Eating Out** **

**
Food
is the focal point of the culture in Mozambique. Treat yourself to the
famous Peri-Peri glazed prawns and chicken, sought after by the locals
and the tourists alike. Food here is inexpensive and is sure to please
the toughest of the food critics. The beach side in Mozambique is
famous for its barbecues. The seafood found here is fresh and always
cooked to perfection.

**Traveling Around** **

**
You
can travel in and around Mozambique by the public transport system. The
railways, put down during the colonial period, are a great way to access
various provinces in Mozambique. Not only does it provide easy access
but is also inexpensive. The local bus service also connects to major
attractions in Mozambique and will be easy on your wallet. Taxis are
available in Mozambique and are not very expensive but you
need to be an expert in the art of haggling. If the drivers realize that you
are a tourist they will try and hike up the fares.

**Sightseeing** **

**
If
you are a budget traveler the best way to explore the natural beauty
and other attractions in Mozambique will be to set out on a backpacking
tour. There are several tour operators that have economical backpacking
tours. You can also explore Mozambique on your own. The Benguerra
Islands offers great sights and scenes to the tourists. ** ** From bird watching to basking under the sun on the beach, this place has a lot to offer.
