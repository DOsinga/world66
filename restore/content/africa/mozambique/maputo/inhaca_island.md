# Inhaca Island Travel Guide

*Africa > Mozambique > Maputo > Inhaca Island*

The best resource for sights, hotels, restaurants, bars, what to do and see

Inhaca island is situated just outside Maputo. The island is a marine nature reserve, and is well known for its variety of birds. When staying on Inhaca, it is possible to take small boat trips to Portuguese Island, which has long, white beaches.
