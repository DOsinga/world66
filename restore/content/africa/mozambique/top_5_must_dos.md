# Top 5 Must Do's in Mozambique

*Africa > Mozambique > Top 5 Must Dos*

The top **Mozambique must do’s** in Southern Africa, are
experiencing the breathtaking country.

**Scuba Diving**

This is the best spot to go for scuba diving due to its unique
variety of marine life, crystal clear waters and untouched coral reefs
that offer magnificent sights that will take your breath away. A place
such as the Bazaruto archipelago is quite popular when it comes to
viewing coral reefs that have not been tampered with. For those who
have taken up scuba diving and snorkeling as a passionate hobby, a
place like Inhambane is a paradise for you as you get the opportunity to
view marine life such a the sting ray, sharks and the
elegant creature like the whale. For those who would like to witness a
spectacular event such as the migration of the humpback whales, the best
time to do so is during the months of June to September. Another place
that you can view whale sharks is the Inhaca Island and local boat
operators can offer services for transportation for a small fee.

**Fishing**

Another Mozambique must do, is challenging fishing that can only be
found in Mozambique, especially in the Guinjata Bay as well as the
Mozambique Channel where the waters are quite deep. A fish caught in
this region can weight up to 450 km, but
fishermen are encouraged to only catch the fish then release it back to the
water so as to preserve the fishes. Fishing is a fun sport and can be
done as a family activity, and fly-fishing is very fun for the kids.

**Turtles in Ponta Mamoli**

Just 25 km from the Kosi Bay, Ponta Mamoli gives
tourists the chance of a life time to see turtles and also how turtles
are nested. With species such as leatherback and loggerhead turtles
tourists get the chance to view how the eggs are laid but this only
happens between the month of November and March. The whole family can
view this spectacular phenomenon whereby the female turtles crawl along
the beaches during the night and dig holes where they lay the eggs then
cover them with sand; it's truly a mind blowing experience.

**Jardim Tunduru Botanical Gardens **

While exploring the beautiful streets of Maputo be sure to visit the
Jardim Tunduru Botanical Gardens. At the entrance of the garden guests
are greeted with a statue of the first president of the republic of
Mozambique, Samora Machel. Experience the sight offered by this garden
while having a picnic with a loved one or with the family. Located
within the garden are restaurants where you can enjoy refreshments and
also there are tennis courts where children can play.

**Bird Watching**

For those who love bird watching this is the place to visit as it has
over 600 bird species to be viewed. A place like the Bazaruto
archipelago is the best place to see hundreds of birds.These
are some of the Mozambique must do's.
