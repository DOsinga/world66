### [Namib Desert](/africa/namibia/sights/namib_desert)

Be sure to get a tour guide as this is one of the most dangerous deserts in the world. It gets extremely hot during the day and well below zero at night. The desert houses the biggest moving (and non-moving) sand dunes in the world, and most places are open to the public - for free :)

Always take some water with you, and keep water in your vehicle as cars can easily overheat.

Once again, make sure you tell someone where your going and get a tour guide if possible as many holidays have ended in tragedy when tourists get lost.
