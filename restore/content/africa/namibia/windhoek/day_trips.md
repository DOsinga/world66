# Day Trips in Windhoek

*Africa > Namibia > Windhoek > Day Trips*

Windhoek is an excellent base
to explore the surrounding mountains and diverse topography of Namibia
along with the country’s flourishing cultural and historical getaways.
Here are some day trip options to make the most of your stay in
Windhoek.

**Arnhem Caves**

The humongous and intimidating Kalahari Desert is one of the most
fantastic day trip destinations from Windhoek. Experience the parched
and rugged desert appeal of the region along with traces of a rich
historical legacy at the Arnhem caves. The magnificent caves whisper
startling accounts of Namibia’s tryst with a richly antediluvian
culture. There are several ancient snapshots and historical ruins that
create a silhouette of a culturally blessed region. Hire a four wheeler
for an action-packed drive through the unruly bends of Kalahari.

**Twyfelfontein**

For witnessing an assortment of visually stunning rock paintings and
vintage rock art that covers a whopping 2,500 different designs,
Twefelfontein a nice day trip option. Visitors can view prehistoric
engravings and 3,000 year old petroglyphs illustrating the game
animals of Namibia. There is also a distinctly created Visitor
Information Center that houses an exhibition section and a small
souvenir shop.

**Spitzkoppe**

Spitzkoppe is a spectacular island that boasts of dramatic granite
peaks, lush grass plains and one of the most celebrated mountains in
Namibia, the Groot Spitzkop. This region is a haven for hikers,
mountaineers and rock climbing purists, who swear by its exhilarating
terrains and untamed paths. The main Spitzkoppe Mountain is definitely
not for amateur climbers and requires the expertise of seasoned hikers.
Make sure you are well-equipped for this can be a dangerous hiking
trail even for proficient climbers. The huge granite rocks are
reminders of the intense volcanic activity witnessed by Spitzkoppe
millions of years ago. Watch the beautiful bleeding sunset of the
region before biding it adieu.

**Brandberg**

Brandberg scores for its aesthetic rock engravings and the famous
‘White Lady’ rock painting that has attained a cult status in the
region. The exquisite piece of artistic genius has stirred several
debates relating to its origin, meaning and significance. Visitors will
have to take a guided tour to the Tsisab Ravine to catch a view of the
celebrated painting that is circumscribed by engravings of animals and
other nature inspired symbols.
Windhoek is surrounded by striking mountain peaks and interesting
archaic caves that showcase the artistic legacy of Namibia and its rich
culture though fascinating nature induced rock engravings and paintings.
