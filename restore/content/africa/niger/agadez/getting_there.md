# Getting There

*Africa > Niger > Agadez > Getting There*

Most people who come from the South, take the Tahoua - Birnin Konni road, which is a lot better than the [Zinder](/africa/niger/zinder) road. There is still a good 100 km of road missing on the latter one. But if you have a 4WD, and like a bit of adventure, the Zinder one is a lot more fun. If you are going to Zinder it is still faster than the long detour via Tahoua.
