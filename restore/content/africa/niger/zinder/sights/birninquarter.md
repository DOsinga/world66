# Birnin Quarter

*Africa > Niger > Zinder > Sights > Birninquarter*

The Birnin quarter is southeast of the modern city. It has some of the finest examples of traditional Hausa architecture, some even better than you'll find in [Kano](/africa/nigeria/kano) (Nigeria). There is also an old French Foreign Legion fort, and there are some quiet and peaceful gardens you can visit.
