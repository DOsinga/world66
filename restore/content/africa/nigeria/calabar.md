# Calabar

*Africa > Nigeria > Calabar*

Calabar is the wettest city of Nigeria. Located in the far South East of the country it is an important stop over on your way to [Cameroon](/africa/cameroon).

 The town is small for Nigerian standards and pleasant enough. There is a good market, a nice museum and you can make a short excursion to Creek Town. The veiws over Calabar river are quite good as well.

 If you want to see Gorilla's, the [Cross River National Park](/africa/nigeria/crossrivernationalpark) supposedly houses a few. Take a bush taxi to Ikom and arrange the rest of the trip there.
