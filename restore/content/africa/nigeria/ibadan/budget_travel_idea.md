# Budget Travel Ideas in Ibadan

*Africa > Nigeria > Ibadan > Budget Travel Idea*

Use this **Ibadan budget travel** guide to cut costs while you are
traveling to this city in Nigeria. If you are on a budget, then
backpacking is a valid option. Traveling through buses from other cities
and traveling by danfos is the cheapest, costing only about $10 to $40.

**Other Travel Options**

Choosing budget hotels is
easy. You can obtain the information readily. One such hotel is the
Devine International Hotel, which provides comfortable rooms for only
$50 a night. You can have a picturesque view of your surroundings in
this inexpensive hotel. Regular rates of hotels range from $60 to $250
per night. To enjoy your visit and be able to interact with the locals,
you can visit the University of Ibadan, where you get to visit great
attractions coming from their biota. You can also interact with the
people and learn more about their remarkable culture and practices.

**Budget Restaurants**

If
you are not too finicky with food, then you can eat at local food
stores for only $10 to $20. You could also bring a prepared sandwich and
some drinks with you, if you opt to go backpacking. If you are more
adventurous, you can seek out hidden eateries, which are much cheaper,
because of their location. Know the basic language, however, and always
go in groups or with a trusted friend or tourist guide. Be careful
though not to cut on costs so much that it could danger your health and
safety. It pays to know when to save and when not to. This will depend
upon each particular situation that you are in.
