# Lagos

*Africa > Nigeria > Lagos*

Lagos is very spread out and it is hard to get a good grip on the the place. The city consists of Lagos island, Victoria islands and a lot of suburbs, which constitute complete cities of their own.

 Most visitors who come to Lagos are here for business, and touristic infrastructure is lacking a bit. The town does have an interesting museum filled with replica's of some of the best African Art you can find in Europe and the US, there are good beaches nearby, and in places like Ikeja or in Bar Beach there is always something going on.
