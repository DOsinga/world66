# Family Travel Ideas in Lagos

*Africa > Nigeria > Lagos > Family Travel Idea*

**Lagos family travel** has something for everyone to enjoy in the beautiful scenery of Lagos, Nigeria. Surrounding areas are a short drive and provide entertainment for kids and adults for a great family outing. Remember the Nigerian vacation by participating in activities together the whole family will love.

**Eko Tourist Beach Resort**

The Eko Tourist Beach Resort has many activities along the Akodo Beach for kids and adults. Even teenagers will like the atmosphere here and can participate in body boarding in the Atlantic. There is an amusement part at the resort where kids and teens can play games and ride the park rides. A children’s swimming pool is kept clean for kids to enjoy. Boat rides and many sporting activities are located at this beach for the entire family to have a fun time together.

**Kajuru Castle**

Although Kajuru was built in 1978, this amazing castle provides entertainment for the whole family. Staying here is a good idea if the family needs [accommodations](/africa/nigeria/lagos/accommodation). Visit the castle for a day but be sure to obtain permission from the manager first before going. Tour guides are available to take tourists through the castle. There is a beautiful swimming pool with water slides for kids and teens to enjoy. The castle is a wonder and provides spectacular views of the city of Kaduna.

**Silverbird Galleria**

If the family needs some Western traditions, visit the Silverbird Galleria for shopping and [entertainment](/africa/nigeria/lagos/things_to_do). The cinema has 6 screens and offers box office movies. Bowl with the family at the bowling alley at Silverbird Galleria. Stroll through the expansive mall to look at the [shops](/africa/nigeria/lagos/shopping)or buy items while there.

**Lekki Conservation Centre**

Spend an [entire day](/africa/nigeria/lagos/day_trips) exploring the ecosystems of the Lekki Conservation Centre. Kids of all ages will enjoy seeing the mona monkeys, crocodiles and several other species of animals. Walk along the trails and climb the tree platforms to see the views. The family can enjoy their time together while appreciating nature in this area of Nigeria.

**La Campagne Tropicana**

This [beach](/africa/nigeria/lagos/beaches)resort has many activities that are available for adults and children. Horse back riding is among the activities that the family can enjoy together. Test your skills at the obstacle course that is located on the resort. Break the record for best male or female performance and there is a cash prize that is awarded. Fish in the ocean or enjoy a canoe trip for a family experience of Nigeria sure to be remembered.
