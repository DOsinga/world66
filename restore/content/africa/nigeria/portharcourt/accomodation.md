**Blues Café**

A watering hole mainly for Sunday afternoons where expatriates are able to meet local ladies and listen to some well-known music. Show up at about 3 in the afternoon and stay 'til whenever. Also has a hairdresser specializing in Western style hair cuts. 

**Embassy Hotel**

Embassy Hotel is a fairly new and cosy hotel not far away from Port Harcourt Airport (about 20 km). It has 5 Executive Rooms and 9 Standard Rooms. The facilities include a roof top bar and a verande on the 1st floor and groundfloor where it is always nice and cool.
