Andoni Elephant Reserve
Located southeast of Port Harcourt on Andoni Island, a coastal barrier island that is nature best kept secret in the oil- rich Niger Delta, island measuring 124 km2 is only in the region that boost biodiversity holding a small group of subspecies of African Forest Elephants.
The reserve is also famous for miles of Atlantic beaches overlooking Gulf og Guinea.
