# Getting There

*Africa > Reunion > Getting There*

Air

The main airline to serve R&eacute;union is *Air France (AF).* Other airlines operating to R&eacute;union include *AOM Air Libert&eacute; *and *Corsair*. There are often discounts on flights during the periods of March to May and October to November.

Approximate flight time

From R&eacute;union to *London *is 14 hours 40 minutes (via Paris).

International airport:

*Saint-Denis de la R&eacute;union (RUN)*

(Roland-Garros) is 8km (4.9 miles) from the town (travel time  20 minutes). Airport facilities include banks/bureaux de change (0900-1630) cash machines bars a restaurant post office and car rental.

Sea

Both freight and passenger lines (a large number are French) put in at Pointe-des-Galets.
