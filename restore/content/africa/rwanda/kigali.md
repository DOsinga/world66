# Kigali

*Africa > Rwanda > Kigali*

Kigali is the capital of Rwanda and it's most important city. It's mainly an administrative centre and even before the civil war in Rwanda there wasn't really a lot to do and see.

It is a useful place to arrange stuff for a trip to the Virunga Volcanoes in the [Volcanoes national park](/africa/rwanda/volcanoesnationalpark), the [Akagera national park](/africa/rwanda/akageranationalpark) and the region around Lake Kivu, however. The ORTPN is the place to go to arrange such excursions. For most national parks it makes sense to use their services because tourist infrastructure is not always readily available.
