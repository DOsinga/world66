**Scam-watching**

Watch scammers sending their "business proposals" and receiving answers sent by scam-baiters. It's fun but be careful: Scammers are dangerous criminals.

address: any cybercafe

**Jogging**

In Dakar you will see quite some people jogging.The favourite route is along the Corniche West leading to the University. You can join of course.

**Tennis**

Most of the major hotels have tennis courts which non guests can pay to use. Otherwise try le Cercle de l'Union, a private club near the Oceanarium. They also have squash courts.

**Diving**

Senegal’s best scuba diving is in the water off either N’Gor Island or village. South of Dakar there are larger beaches at M’Bour and Saly. The Cap Vert area is graced with sand dunes baobab trees beautiful Atlantic views and a small beach with snorkeling nearby.
