# Diving

*Africa > Senegal > Dakar > Activities > Diving*

Senegal’s best scuba diving is in the water off either N’Gor Island or village. South of Dakar there are larger beaches at M’Bour and Saly. The Cap Vert area is graced with sand dunes baobab trees beautiful Atlantic views and a small beach with snorkeling nearby. Toubab Diallo is a very pleasant fishing village that lacks the crowds and hustle of Dakar Mbour and Saly. Public transportation to the village is available out of Diam Ndiado on the road between Dakar and Thies.
