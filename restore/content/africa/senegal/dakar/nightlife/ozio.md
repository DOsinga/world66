# Ozio

*Africa > Senegal > Dakar > Nightlife > Ozio*

I would like to recommend a new restaurant in Dakar. Ozio is the first in a kind in Senegal and on the west African Coast. The minimalistic architectural design made mostly of local materiel, the concept which consists of an international cuisine restaurant with a lounge and bar area where light house and ethnic music is contantly played makes it the happening place in Dakar since the February opening. The restaurant was also acclaimed in the french newspaper " Le Figaro" in one of the week-end special editions in March 2001. Located in the heart of Dakar, on Victor Hugo street, the restaurant has become the place to be seen in.  it is definetly a place to visit when in Dakar.  The address and phone number of the restaurant are:  31 rue Voctir Hugo - Dakar phone: 823 87 87** paul smith:** outstanding.
