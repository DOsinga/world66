# Accommodation

*Africa > Seychelles > Denisisland > Accommodation 1*

DENIS ISLAND, Unrivalled in Private Island Experience.

350 acres of untouched foliage, exotic flowers and idyllic white sandy
beaches with 'unrivalled' swimming year round are the exclusive domain
of this island's privileged guests.

Holiday makers enjoy a supreme sense of well being in their private beachside villa. far from the madding crowd.

Denis Island is renowned for it's culinary delights. The ingredients
used are gathered from the island's vegetable garden and the daily
catch of fresh fish. This Eco-Friendly Resort is self sufficient with
most of its basic needs.

Denis has the well known Seychelles Hotelier, Alain St.Ange, as its General Manager.
