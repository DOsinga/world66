# La Digue Island Resort

*Africa > Seychelles > Eatingout > Ladigueislandresort*

A two- or three-star hotel on the beach on La Digue (A fairly averge-looking place where the rack-rate is US$400 for half-board in a beach-front cabana!). We sweated in the shade by the modest pool, and ate a very modest lunch of two fruit juices, a pizza and some chicken wings, which set us back a more than modest US$40. *(Contributed by Howard Banwell)*
