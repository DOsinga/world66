# Getting There

*Africa > Seychelles > Getting There*

Air

The Seychelles' national airline is *Air Seychelles (HM).* Other airlines flying to the Seychelles include *British Airways Air France Kenya Airways* and* Aeroflot-Russian International Airlines.*

Approximate flight times

From Mah&eacute; to *London *is 10 hours (15 hours via Nairobi) and to *New York* is 24 hours (via London).

International airport

*Mah&eacute; Island (SEZ) *(Seychelles International) is 10km (6 miles) southeast from Victoria (travel time  20 minutes). Some coach services are provided by agents and taxis are available. Airport facilities include an outgoing duty-free shop banking and currency exchange facilities (0830-1230 Monday to Friday; 0830-1200 Saturday) car hire and restaurant/bar (0800 until the last flight).

Departure tax

US$40 or equivalent payable in hard currency. Children under 12 years of age are exempt. From 1st April 1999 visitors have the option of either paying the US$40 departure tax or purchasing a *Gold Card* costing US$100 also payable in hard currency which will be valid for life. The Gold Card will give visitors unlimited access to all the government-owned nature reserves and national parks and will include the departure tax. From 1st November 1999 the departure tax will be discontinued and the Gold Card will become compulsory for all visitors arriving in the Seychelles. It is valid for all return visits.

Sea

Cruise and cargo ships call at Mah&eacute; but there are no scheduled services.
