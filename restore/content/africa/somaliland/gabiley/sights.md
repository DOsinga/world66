# Sights in Gabiley

*Africa > Somaliland > Gabiley > Sights*

Gabiley (Capital of Gabiley district) is the heart of Somaliland agriculture and the most populated district as well. Gabiley is at 52 KM west of Hargeisa the capital. Climatically it is moderate through out the year as it is 1450 meters above sea level.

The agriculture is main resources in Gabiley region and there are new projects under construction now , university and vacational institutes.
