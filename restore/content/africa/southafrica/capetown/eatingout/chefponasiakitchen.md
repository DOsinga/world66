# Chef Pon's Asian Kitchen

*Africa > Southafrica > Capetown > Eatingout > Chefponasiakitchen*

Chef Pon's provides a wide selection of Asian dishes, ranging from Mongolian to Thai to Japanese, all clearly marked on the menu. It is a great place for relaxing and having a delicious Asian dish at a decent price. The smoking section has a good view of the non-stop activity in the kitchen and if they say the Jungle Curry is hot, it really is hot! (Which is a good thing of course)
