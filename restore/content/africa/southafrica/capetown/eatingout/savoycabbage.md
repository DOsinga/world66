# Savoy Cabbage

*Africa > Southafrica > Capetown > Eatingout > Savoycabbage*

For a more classy evening out, the Savoy Cabbage is a good choice. It has a very stylish interior combining an old building with modern fittings. The food is exellent, Continental / European, with a reasonable choice in vegetarian dishes. The menu changes daily and organic seasonal indredients are widely used. A very wide range of good wines by the glass and mainly from small boutique estates, so experiment.
