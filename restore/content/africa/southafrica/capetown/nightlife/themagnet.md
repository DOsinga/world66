# The Magnet

*Africa > Southafrica > Capetown > Nightlife > Themagnet*

An intellectual coffee drinkers paradise. Open all the time till very late. Caters for an upmarket, albeit groovy crowd. This place seems to feed off an atmosphere created by old skool, retro flicks. Always is full on the weekend, due to the Funktion just around the corner. Perfect for post-clubbing chill-out sessions.
