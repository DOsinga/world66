**Chapman's Peak drive**

For the most stunning coalstal views you have ever seen. This 10 kn. drive takes you along the sheer cliffs between colorful sedimentary rocks facing Chapman's bay. This coastal drive from Hout Bay to Noordhoek will take your breath away. 

**Cape Peninsula National Park**

The CPNP is a small but beautiful nature reserve right on the tip of the Cape Peninsula. This small area has one of the worlds highest densities in plant species. It is home to 2,285 plant species (by comparison, the British Isles have 1,492 species).
