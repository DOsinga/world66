# La Casa Nostra

*Africa > Southafrica > Durban > Eatingout > La Casa Nostra*

One of the best Italian trattoria's in the country. Locals try to keep it a secret! Checked tablecloths, cold beer, Food cooked according to family recipes, great service and personal attention from Alessandro, make for a great meal. the Pasta should be world famous. You can't book, so get there early. It's also just a slow waddle from the beach after lunch. They're closed Mondays.
