# Roma Revolving

*Africa > Southafrica > Durban > Eatingout > Roma Revolving*

One of the only revolving restaurants in the world, this restaurant serves delicious Italian food. They also offer some more unusual dishes such as crocodile steak!

They have one of those old fashioned sweet carts which is filled with divine desserts. By South African standards this restaurant is considered to be on the pricey side but I went out with 8 people, all drinking alcohol and paid around GBP£80 including the tip!!!

My family have been going to this restaurant for years - in fact the owner was my mum's first boyfriend!!
