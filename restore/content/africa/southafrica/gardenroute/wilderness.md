# Wilderness

*Africa > Southafrica > Gardenroute > Wilderness*

The Fairy Knowe Backpackers is one of the most charming and beautifully set stops along the Garden Route. From here, one can swim in the Indian Ocean, go horseback riding, canoing, hike to waterfalls, visit the Map of Africa or just relax and enjoy the diverse animal residents calling this their home. It is also a convenient stop, as the Choo-choo train will stop literally at the front yard upon request. Definitely a place worth remembering for this traveller.
