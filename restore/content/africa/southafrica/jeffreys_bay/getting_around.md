Study English as a foreign language

Blue Ocean Language School is an International English Language School situated in Jeffreys Bay, Eastern Cape, South Africa - the heart of SA's surfing mecca.

Jeffreys Bay is directly on the border between the Sunshine Coast and the Garden Route, and offers visitors easy access to numerous experiences and sightings, eg. viewing the Big 5, adrenalin sport venues, hiking trails, dolphin and whale viewing and world famous surfing - Supertubes is the annual host to the ASP Billabong Pro Series.

Teachers are all experienced and qualified, and the school is situated in Wavecrest, one of the safest areas in Jeffreys Bay. Within comfortable walking distance of pharmacies, doctors, dentists, restaurants, coffee shops, pubs, supermarkets, surf gear shops and beaches (Magnatubes is 150m away, and the world renowned Supertubes is a mere 900m away).

Blue Ocean Language School offers the following courses:

•General English
•General English Focus
•Business English
•Business English Focus
•Travel English
•Private English
•FCE & CAE Exam Preparation
•Business Specific Courses (eg. Aviation English, Legal English, Human Resources English, Marketing & Advertising English, Presentation English, Logistics English and Energy Industry English)
Free shuttle services to and from Port Elizabeth Airport and bus drop-off points, free wireless internet and free laundry service for students utilising the Student Residence.
