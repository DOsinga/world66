# South African

*Africa > Southafrica > Johannesburg > Eatingout > Southafrican*

For a real taste of South Africa you can dine at one of these restaurants.

**Restaurants**

**Delfini Restaurant **

Located in the Eastern Suburbs of Johannesburg, they serve African food.

address: Olympic United Centre, 42 Hunter Street, Yeoville

tel: 011-6483500

**Marcos**

Old Style / Traditional and South African food. 

address: Entrance 7, Nothgate shopping centre, Northumberland Road

tel: 011-7942936

**Ramkietjie **

They serve Seafood and South African food. Booking is essential. 

address: 35 Peter Road, Tres Jolie, Honeydew, Roodepoort

tel: 011-9581050

**Bushveld Pub and Diner **

Located in the Northern Suburbs of Johannesburg, they serve pub-fare and South African food. 

address: Shop 24, The Valley Centre, 396 Jan Smuts Avenue, Craighall Park

tel: 011-3260170
