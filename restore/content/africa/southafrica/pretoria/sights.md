**Kruger House**

A short walk from Church Square in Pretoria, the residence of President Paul Kruger has been turned into a museum and national monument. Kruger made his home in this unpretentious little house from 1883 until 1900, when he went into exile in Europe. He died in Switzerland in 1904. **The Voortrekker Monument**

The Voortrekker Monument is a massive granite monument designed by Gerhard Moerdyk and built as a national shrine to commemorate the Voortrekkers.
