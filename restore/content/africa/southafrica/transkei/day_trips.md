# Day Trips in Transkei

*Africa > Southafrica > Transkei > Day Trips*

**Exploring the Seams of Transkei or Wild Coast**

There are several Transkei day trips to enjoy while you are on vacation. Now known as the Wild Coast, Transkei lives up to its name with several opportunities for** **great excursions. There are many interesting sights and sounds to see in the wild and spontaneous side of South Africa.

#### **Morgan&rsquo;s Bay**

Technically, Morgan&rsquo;s Bay is still part of the Eastern Cape of the Wild Coast, although it is relatively far from the original Transkei group of villages and municipalities. Morgan&rsquo;s Bay is a small village which features the famous cliffs and a beach front that stretches a mile long. Morgan&rsquo;s Bay and its beach are a great way to spend some time with family and loved ones while camping in front of its waters and watching the sunrise, sunset and ships. If you&rsquo;re up for more activity, you can also go whale and dolphin watching, surfing and canoeing. For land activities, you can go rock climbing, trekking, horse riding or bird watching. Once a month, in keeping with the tradition of farmers going to this place for some vacation and to sell their products, Morgan&rsquo;s Bay holds a craft market.

#### **Cintsa**

Just 40 kilometers off of East London in the Eastern Cape of South Africa, Cintsa is an adventurer&rsquo;s destination with plenty of budget hotels, hostels and bed and breakfasts. You can also camp in its beaches. They also have a rock pool which is a bonus to its stretches of beach front. This is a small village, and it is easy to explore by walking.
