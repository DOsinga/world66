# Umzumbe Surf House Travel Guide

*Africa > Southafrica > Umzumbe Surf House*

Umzumbe Surf House is conveniently located steps away from Umzumbe beach and it's famous right hand point break. Only 90ks South of [Durban](/africa/southafrica/durban), the beach-front hostel guesthouse is a great place for surfers, backpackers, students and volunteers looking to beach-it on the East Coast of South Africa. There are daily surf lessons and surfboard rentals, guided surf trips, guided walks to the nearest waterfalls and many more activities to get involved in.

Umzumbe Surf House is owned and managed by a South African & American couple, who run Surf Tours along the entire eastern coastline of South Africa. Facilities at Umzumbe Surf House include Hammocks, Safe Car Parking, Internet Access, Laundry, Bed Linen Included, Towels, Security, Camping, a Safe, Guest Kitchen and a small Cafe Style Menu offering African grown coffee's, Smoothies and Tacos.
