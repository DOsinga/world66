# 7 Day Itinerary in Arusha

*Africa > Tanzania > Arusha > 7 Day Itinerary*

Northern Tanzania Flying Safari

Experience a Northern Tanzania Flying Safari, you will get to see the Tarangire National Park, the Serengeti National Park and the Ngorongoro Crater on this luxury lodge flying safari of Tanzania.
This Flying Lodge Safari starts and ends in Arusha, Tanzania.and includes Tarangire National Park, Serengeti National Park and a visit to the Ngorongoro Crater. You will experience the diverse cultural, wildlife and scenery of Northern Tanzania, while staying in luxury lodges.
Tanzania has one of the largest, wildest animal populations in the world. Wildebeest, monkey, antelope, lion, cheetah, crocodile, gazelle, and flamingo - you name them. Blessed with some of the world's largest game reserves, the country also has one of the highest concentrations of elephant and buffalo. A Wildlife Experience not to be missed!
Highlights: Good sightings of elephant and buffalo in Tarangire National Park, excellent game viewing all year round in the Serengeti National Park, magnificent views of Ngorongoro Crater
Type of Safari: Luxury Lodge Flying Safari
Plane used: Cessna or similar
Group Size: 2 to 6 clients
Accommodation: Luxury 4* Lodges
**Day 1-2: Lake Manyara National Park**
Upon your arrival at Kilimanjaro International Airport you will be met by a Jenman Safaris representative who will transfer you to Arusha Airport where you board your scheduled flight to Manyara Airstrip. Daily flights depart at 08:30 and 13:00. You will be met by a representative from Lake Manyara Tree Lodge for your game-drive transfer, 3 hours and 30 minutes, to the lodge. Spend two nights at Lake Manyara Tree Lodge. Accommodation is in a stilted treehouse nestled in boughs of mahogany trees. Your stay is on a full board basis including scheduled activities and park fees. Activities include game-drives in Lake Manyara National Park
**Day 3-5: Serengeti National Park**
After breakfast you will be transferred to the airstrip where you board your scheduled flight to Grumeti, Kleins or Serengeti Under Canvas Airstrip. From the airstrip a short game-drive transfer, will bring you to camp. Spend three nights at Klein’s Camp. Accommodation is in a thatched cottage. Your stay is on a full board basis including scheduled activities and concession fees.Activities include game-drives and nature walks on the Klein’s Camp private concession. The afternoon game-drives extend after sunset with the aid of a spotlight, returning to the camp after dark. Klein’s Camp community visits are available at an additional cost and may be booked directly at the lodge, subject to availability of vehicles.
**Day 6-7: Ngorongoro Crater Conservation Area**
Enjoy an early morning game-drive and breakfast before your transfer to the airstrip for your scheduled flight to Manyara Airstrip. You will be met by a representative from Ngorongoro Crater Lodge for your road transfer, 2 hours, to the lodge. Spend two nights at Ngorongoro Crater Lodge. Accommodation is in a sumptuous double suite. Each suite is tended by a butler. Your stay is on a full board basis including scheduled activities and park fees. Activities include one game-drive on the Crater floor. Additional activities may be booked directly at the lodge, subject to extra cost and availability of vehicles. Such activities include excursions to Olduvai Gorge and Shifting Sands, a visit to a Maasai village and walks at Empakai Crater. Massages are offered for those who need to relax after a day’s game-viewing.
**Day 8: Arusha**
After breakfast you will be transferred to Manyara Airstrip where you board your scheduled flight to Arusha Airport. On arrival at 12:45 you will be transferred to Kilimanjaro International Airport, where our servces end.
