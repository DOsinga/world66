# 7 Day Itinerary in Dar es salaam

*Africa > Tanzania > Daressalaam > 7 Day Itinerary*

Once the British left East Africa and returned many of the colonial
States back to self rule, a plethora of altruistic activities sprung
forth, and one such spectacular location is Dar es Saalam, Tanzania. This
West African nation has been a tourist paradise, and a
destination in which many travelers come back time and time again.

If
this is the first vacation to Dar es Saalam then it would be wise to
have an itinerary in place. One of the best ways to enjoy a trip through beautiful South
Africa is to hop aboard the Roves’ Rail. This train is one of the most
luxurious ones in the entire world. It departs from Cape Town and winds
its way through safari-type terrain, and will leave the traveler
breathless with its beauty. Guests that board this beautifully
restored train will feel like the great explorer Livingston did when he
first set eyes on the continent of Africa.

**The Roves’ Rail Itinerary**

Hopping aboard the rails is a wonderful way to see South Africa, and
to see it firsthand for oneself. As the train starts to roll out of
the Cape Town Station, the traveler starts to immediately see the real
Africa. There is a stop at the famous Kruger National Park, with an
overnight stay at the wonderful Victoria Falls Hotel, which is right
across from the lovely Victoria Falls. The view is simply
astonishing. Upon entering Dar es Saalam and the port of Tanzania, the
journey will end, but the memories will never cease. **

11:00 Cape Town Station

**Saturday**

13:00 Arrive Kimberly. Lunch at the Kimberly Club and a quick trip to The Big Hole and Diamond Museum.
17:20 Depart Kimberly with a quick dinner, before laying it down for the night on the train.

**Sunday**

11:30 Arrive in Pertora. Spend the day visiting the wonderful
Pertoran. Landmarks such as the Victoria Hotel. Return to train for
nighttime activities.
18:00 Depart Victoria.

**Monday**

11:00 Arrive at Hoedspruit for a day’s stay at the private game lodge. This
includes hunting, fishing and other sportsman activities.**
**

**Tuesday**

A visit to the Hoedspruit Wild Dog and Cheetah project, which will definitely have the interest and the attention of all.
18:00 Depart Hoedspruit.

**Wednesday**

01:00 Arrive in Cadoona and continue on throughout Mazinga. to the Port of Dar es Saalam, while eating breakfast on the train.

### [7 DAY, 6 NIGHT -Selous & Mikumi](/africa/tanzania/daressalaam/7_day_itinerary/7_day_6_night_se)

**Day 1;-** Depart Dar to drive to Selous, On arrival check in at **Rufiji River Camp** The tented camp is situated in the north-eastern part of the Selous Game Reserve on a high bank overlooking the mighty Rufiji River and the plain beyond. The camp accommodates guests in 20 comfortable tents. Each tent has twin beds with mosquito nets, en-suite bathroom and a veranda facing the river. The central restaurant provides excellent cuisine.
