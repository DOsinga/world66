# Getting There in Iringa

*Africa > Tanzania > Iringa > Getting There*

There are several daily buses from Dar es Salaam and daily connections from Mbeya, Kyela, Songea, [Njombe](/africa/tanzania/njombe) and Dodoma . There is also a direct bus from Moshi – Arusha, although run by a company with a very bad reputation. The road from Dar es Salaam and Morogoro is tarmac and of good quality, as is its continuation to Mbeya. For the Dodoma road only a fourwheeldrive can be recommended. Iringa airport is not served by any regular flights.
