# Museums in Mikumi national park

*Africa > Tanzania > Mikuminationalpark > Museums*

Where you stop to pay the fee to enter the park, there is also a little park museum. This museum has free entré. Here you can get an overwiew of the park, and see some of the animals you can expect to meet while you are on safari. Here are also some brochures and other souvenirs.
