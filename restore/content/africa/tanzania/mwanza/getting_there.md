# Getting There

*Africa > Tanzania > Mwanza > Getting There*

Mzanza is centrally located in the country. It is relatively close to the Serengeti which makes it a good starting place for safari's but maybe not as good as [Arusha](/africa/tanzania/arusha).

From Mzanza, direct flights go to [Bukoba](/africa/tanzania/bukoba), [Arusha](/africa/tanzania/arusha) and [Dar es salaam](/africa/tanzania/daressalaam) among others.
