# Getting Around in Zanzibar

*Africa > Tanzania > Zanzibar > Getting Around*

Salem Construction Ltd. is a Building & Civil Engineering Construction company that was registered in September 1978. Since then the company has undertaken several industrial, commercial, civil and residential contracts.
**Providing Environmentally Sound Post Frame Solutions…**

Superior quality isn’t our only goal…bringing you environmentally sound constructions is just as important to us. We take the time to choose the best materials and contractors for the job – and being “green” is part of our definition of best! We strive to minimize the environmental impact of all our buildings.

**Design:**
All material is ordered and manufactured to the exact sizes of the building blueprints and plans, allowing for very little excess waste. All excess packages, metal and wood are recycled at various facilities in our area.
