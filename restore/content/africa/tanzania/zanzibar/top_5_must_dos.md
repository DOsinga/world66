# Top 5 Must Do's in Zanzibar

*Africa > Tanzania > Zanzibar > Top 5 Must Dos*

Zanzibar is a panoramic and archetypically exotic African Island
that is situated off the East Coast of Tanzania. It has an attractive
and versatile mish-mash of things to do and places to see, making it
difficult to choose only 5 Zanzibar Must Do’s. From traces of a
rich historical legacy to the spice and slave
trade era to the charming old markets crammed with Omani Arab merchants
–it’s all here along with a myriad of other reasons for tourists to visit
this enchanting African getaway.

**Visit Pemba Island**

Go snorkeling, scuba diving or deep sea fishing at
Pemba Island. The island is strewn with flourishing coral gardens and a
stunningly beautiful oceanic backdrop with crystal clear waters and a
pristine coast. This popular destination is forever bustling with high
energy marine adventure activities. There are regular sessions on scuba
diving conducted by expert PADI divers.

**Visit Stone Town**

For a staple fare of African and Arabic grandiose, head to Stone
Town-an ancient quarter of the island that is declared a World Heritage
Site. There are several heritage attractions here including People’s
Palace- an enchanting destination for art, history and culture
aficionados. Watch the opulence of the Sultans come alive in the
memorabilia of this museum that houses royal displays and other
artifacts from the 19th century. Tourists can also visit the
Darajani market for fresh local produce and their weekly antique fair.
Other attractions worth visiting in the vicinity are Hamamni Persian
Baths and the Stone Town Ruins.

**Visit the Ngezi Forest Reserve**

For a potpourri of adventure and action packed activities look no
further than the Ngezi Forest Reserve situated at the Pemba Island.
This multi-functional destination is packed with things to do and makes
even the most decisive tourist experience ambivalence. This reserve is
home to several rare and exotic plant and wildlife species. Bird
watchers can have a field day with sightings of unusual aerials. Hikers
can take a walking trail from the entrance point and encircle the
entire canopy. You can just go to Ngezi with no agenda and soak in
nature’s glory amidst tranquil settings to soothe your nerves.

**Explore the Zanzibar Islands**

Take a stroll along the famed beaches of Zanzibar Islands and revel
in the cerulean blue waters to experience superb serenity and a stress
free sensation.

**Visit Mangapwani**

Watch the history of the slave tradition come alive at The Slave
Chambers and Coral Caves of Mangapwani, a place that houses several
secretive chambers used to hide slaves and transport them in large
numbers on merchant ships.

If you are visiting Zanzibar, make sure to have these 5 exciting, beautiful and cultural experiences on your to-do list.
