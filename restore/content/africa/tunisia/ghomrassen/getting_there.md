# Getting There

*Africa > Tunisia > Ghomrassen > Getting There*

There are buses from Ghomrassen to [Gabes](/africa/tunisia/gabes) that connect to the train to [Tunis](/africa/tunisia/tunis). The whole trip should take about 8 hours. Tickets about 20 dinar.

The same bus also stops in {Medenine} and Tataouin.
