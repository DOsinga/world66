**Gorilla Trekking in Uganda**

Gorilla trekking is an unforgettable and life-changing experience. It's a
unique adventure in the heart of Africa’s most spectacular rainforests.

A typical gorilla trek can take anything
from 15 minutes to 8 hours, depending on where the gorillas are
located. Gorilla trekking safaris trek deep into the rainforest in search of the
magnificent mountain gorillas.

It doesn't matter how short or long the trek is, because spending time in the
company of a gorilla family is something
extraordinary.

**Lisa Marsden**, owner of **XA Wildlife Safaris**,
comes accredited with a wealth of knowledge about the gorillas and
gorilla trekking in Uganda and Rwanda. Her renowned expertise is a
product of her accrued travel experience over the years in the Uganda
region. She enjoys the challenge of creating and customizing tours.
