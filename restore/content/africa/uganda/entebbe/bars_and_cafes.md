# Bars and Cafes in Entebbe

*Africa > Uganda > Entebbe > Bars And Cafes*

**Entebbe bars and cafes** are reputed by travelers to be among the best available in all Africa. Truly there is something for everyone whether you want to party hard all night or relax with friends.

**Red Rooster**

A cracking night out for all can be had in Red Rooster, Entebbe's sports-themed bar wherein four large flatscreens are available for those who wish to watch football, rugby and athletics. You can drink cheap, cold beers while you do this and the advantage is that, comparatively, Red Rooster is not quite as busy as some of its competitors. Outside the bar is a very pleasant and large garden area where you can kick back and relax with a beer just as long as you watch out you don't get sunburned.

**Club X5**

For those who want to pump up the night a little, Club X5 is one of those Entebbe bars and cafes that really offers classy entertainment. Saturday nights are truly a winner in this two-story mega club playing the coolest music from around the world. The ambiance is described as energizing and the crowd is effortlessly cool.

**Mirembe Bar**

For a more chilled out experience all round, drop into the Mirembe Bar where the staff are friendly and amiable and there is always soccer on the TV whatever the time of night or day. The beers are cheap here and many travelers use it as a starting point before heading up to one the bigger Entebber bars and cafes for a wild night out.
