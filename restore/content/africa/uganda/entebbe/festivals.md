# Festivals in Entebbe

*Africa > Uganda > Entebbe > Festivals*

Entebbe is a city in Uganda, also known as Kampala, and there are a number of **Entebbe festivals **that make the city desirable to visitors.

**Amakula Kampala International Film Festival**

The Amakula Kampala International Film Festival is relatively new, but has gathered a lot of attention in the film world. Founded in 2004, the Amakula Kampala festival lasts for a week and half. Traditionally, the festival has been held in May, but this year will be from October 29 to November 6th. This festival focuses on African cinema. The organizers show films from all over the world, but keep the focus on young African film makers. There are also workshops for young actors to learn techniques from the best film makers in Africa.

**Kampala International Jazz Festival**

The Kampala International Jazz Festival is held once year in Munyonyo Commonwealth Resort by Lake Victoria. The festival happens each during September or October. The best jazz musicians in Africa come to this festival to perform. For tourists who are music lovers, this festival is an excellent opportunity to catch a live concert near the serenity of Lake Victoria. The organizers plan both live concerts and workshops for aspiring musicians. The Kampala Jazz Festival is an up and coming event in the worldwide music scene.
