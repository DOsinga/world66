# When to Go in Entebbe

*Africa > Uganda > Entebbe > When To Go*

When planning a trip to **Entebbe, when to go** depends on many factors due to its many attractions. The weather can be an obstacle during certain times of the year. Entebbe is a popular tourist area, so the high and low seasons should be considered when planning a trip.

**Weather**

Based on weather, the best time to visit Entebbe is during the months of July, March or October. The weather during this time is favorable because it is neither too sunny nor too chilly. You will get to enjoy some sunshine during the day and moderately cool evenings. You will be able to access most of the tourist attractions due to the lack of rain which makes some of the roads impassable during other months of the year.

**High and Low Season**

During the Christmas and New Year holidays, most of the resorts and hotels are usually fully booked. If you need to travel during the festive season, you can make your reservations two months in advance to avoid the hike in prices. Most of the tourists from Europe and America flock here during December and January to escape the winter in their home countries.

The month of March and the period from May through September is usually considered the low tourist season, and accommodations are plenty. During the low season, most of the tour and travel operators have cheap packages which include flights, hotel booking and tours around the city.
