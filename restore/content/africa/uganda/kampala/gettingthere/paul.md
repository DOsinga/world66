# Taxis

*Africa > Uganda > Kampala > Gettingthere > Paul*

On landing at Entebe International Airport it is easier to use special hire taxi to Kampala. It costs around only Ug shs 40,000/=. It is also easy to hike a lift from people coming from the airport. This is only safe if the car has come to pick a passenger. Otherwise public transport is available at around Ug shs 2,000/= to Kampala. The journey normally takes 45 minutes.
