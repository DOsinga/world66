# Museums

*Africa > Zimbabwe > Harare > Museums*

**Museums**

**Queen Victoria**

The Queen Victoria Museum (near the Sheraton) requires an hour to see its natural history displays and another hour could be spent at the  

**National Gallery**

**Harare Gardens**
