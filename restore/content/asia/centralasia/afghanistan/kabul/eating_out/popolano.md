# Popo Lano

*Asia > Centralasia > Afghanistan > Kabul > Eating Out > Popolano*

They serve Italian food (pizza & pasta) as well as some Afghan dishes (which are much cheaper). They have vegetarian pizzas and one or two pasta dishes as well as a deceant salad bar.

Excellent pizza crust. Very crispy and flaky. Real jalapenos with a kick. This place doesn't skimp on the toppings and they deliver.
