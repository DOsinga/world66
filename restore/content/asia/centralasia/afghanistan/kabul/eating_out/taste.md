# Taste

*Asia > Centralasia > Afghanistan > Kabul > Eating Out > Taste*

Take the first left off Flower Street as you are coming from Chicken Street and the restaurant is in an alley off Flower Street.

The menu is a combination of Afghan, Indian, and continental cuisine, and food can be eaten in or carried out. Waiters are quite attentive as well; this place is entirely Afghan owned and operated.

The Hot and Sour soup and the Super Sonic burger are good. Good Cappuccino as well. It’s a very clean place and well run by Zadran and his friends.
