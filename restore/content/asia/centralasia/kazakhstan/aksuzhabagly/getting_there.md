# Getting There

*Asia > Centralasia > Kazakhstan > Aksuzhabagly > Getting There*

Trains regularly leave Almaty 2 station at 18:20 and arrive in Tulkabas station located 20mins from Zhabagly at 06:53 the next morning. Local taxis can be taken to the homestays or arranged in advanced through the local coordinator. Returning is equally as easy with the train leaving at 19:35 at night and arriving in Almaty 2 at 9:00 .
