# Family Travel Ideas in Almaty

*Asia > Centralasia > Kazakhstan > Almaty > Family Travel Idea*

Almaty is a prime cultural and economic hub of central Asia and is a
very popular tourist destination for its snow-capped mountains, winter
sports activities, magnificent architecture and dramatic canyons. Here
are some family holiday ideas to make the most of this former
Kazakhstan Capital.

**Chimbulak Ski Resort**

Enjoy a fun-filled family skiing outing at the Chimbulak Ski Resort,
which can be reached by taking a bus from Satpayev and Baitursynuly at
8 am daily. Thrilling skiing and snowboarding events are
conducted on the intimidating paths of the region in winters and
equipment is also available for hire. Chimbulak is an excellent base to
go hiking past a pretty dam and several thrilling glaciers to catch a
breath-taking vista of the city from the lofty peak. The hike can be
difficult but is immensely rewarding and adventurous. The resort is
open from November-April.

**Medeo Ice-skating Stadium**

Visitors can check out the Olympic sized Medeo Ice-skating Stadium
located just outside Almaty town. The winter tourist hotspot exudes a
fun, family ambiance and also houses a striking natural water swimming
pool at the foot of the ice-rink. The ice-cold water sometimes dips
below 15 degrees Celsius. An enjoyable retreat for bonding over
pleasurable ice-skating sessions.

**Lake-side fun**

The Big Almaty Lake is a stunning mountain lake located on the
outskirts of the city. The idyllic spot is circumscribed by imposing
peaks and majestic gorges and is at a level of 2510 above sea level.
The attractive lake fascinating changes its shade from emerald green to
turquoise depending on the weather and the season. This is a perfect
setting for an enjoyable outdoor family picnic.

**Cable Car Ride**

Take a cable car ride atop the Kotobe to catch awe-inspiring views
of the attractive mountains, glaciers, vineyards and canyons of Almaty.
The ride commences from Satpaeva and Dostyk and is priced at
approximately $5 per person for a one way trip. A pleasurable must-do
family activity to enjoy the striking beauty of the region.

**Tamgaly Petroglyphs**

Located at approximately two hours from the main Almaty town, the
Tamgaly Pertoglyphs is a lovely picnic spot. The drive to the
destination is one of the most beautiful in the region and packs a lot
of visual treats and heady natural attractions. Fill a large picnic
basket and enjoy a pleasant family outing.

Almaty features a nice assortment of family travel attractions and
activities that include ski resorts, mountain hiking trails, lake-side
picnic spots and cable car rides.
