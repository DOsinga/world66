# The Green Bazaar

*Asia > Centralasia > Kazakhstan > Almaty > Sights > The Green Bazaar*

The Central Market, otherwise known as the Green Bazaar, brings to life
the finest traditions of the Asian bazaar. This is not just a market,
but a meeting-place of various Asian cultures in miniature. You can
sample the produce grown throughout Central Asia. The fruit and
vegetables at the Green Bazaar are good value in summer.
