# Starskys Club

*Asia > Middleeast > Cyprus > Ayianapa > Nightlife And Ente > Starskys Club*

Don't be fooled by the name this club this is not a Starskys and Hutch themed club. Starskys is for the clubbers who like dance to commercially based music. Had a wicked time there, and the club is big, holding well over 1000. There is a big bar downstairs in the middle with lots of girls dancing on it, my mate was one of them!! Upstairs there are 2 more bars so you can stand upstairs and watch what is going on from there. They have a shop too, where we bought some hats and a tshirt.
