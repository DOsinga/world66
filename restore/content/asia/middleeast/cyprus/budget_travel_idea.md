# Budget Travel Ideas in Cyprus

*Asia > Middleeast > Cyprus > Budget Travel Idea*

You don’t have to spend hundreds of dollars to enjoy a holiday in
Cyprus; these **Cyprus Budget Travel** ideas will help you enjoy your
holiday without spending lots of money.

**Budget Transportation**

Cyprus is accessible by air and water. If you are planning to
travel by air, there are several low cost airlines that operate flights
to Cyprus. These are some of the airlines you might want to check out:
Easyjet, Jet2, Baboo, Blue Air, Brussels Airlines, Jetairfly, etc. You
can also travel to Cyprus by boat. There are ferries and mini cruises
that go to Cyprus from Germany and other places like Syria, Greek
Islands and Lebanon. If you have your own car or vehicle with you, you
can take the freighter cruises from Southampton, Portugal, Italy and
other European ports.

**Budget Accommodation**

There are lots of hotels in Cyprus, most of which are luxury and
5-star hotels. However, there are few that offer low-cost, cheap
accommodations. The Atlantica Golden Beach Hotel and the Hilltop Gardens
Hotel Apts, both which in Paphos, offer standard rooms at $30 / night.
You can also check The Ship Inn that offers rooms for $22 / night.

**Budget Dining**

The several roadside eat-outs in Cyprus are where you can get cheap food. These eat-outs offers delicious and affordable local
delicacies like: Halloumi, Taramosalata, Tahini and more. You can also
try these budget restaurants: Chloe’s Chinese Restaurant in Paphos,
Lisa’s Bistro in Limassol, Niazi’s Take Away Restaurant in Kyrenia, IL
Forno in Nicosia and many more.

**Low-Budget Attractions**

You can discover and explore the island of Cyprus without splurging
and over-spending. There are lots of places to see and visit in this
beautiful island in the Middle East. Some places you can visit are: the
Troodo’s Mountain, Cave Greko, the Akamas Peninsula, Mount Olympus,
Coral Bay, the Caledonian Falls and the Avakas George. If you want to
explore the historical places in Cyprus you can go to the Tomb of
Kings, Platres, Khirokitia, Hala Sultan Tekke, Limassol, Larnaca and many more places. The best part is that it does not cost you a fortune to visit these
places.

**Budget Shopping**

It’s always nice to take home some souvenirs and memoirs from your
trips and vacations. Although Cypriots are not price-conscious shoppers,
you can still find good deals and affordable merchandises around the
Island. Some of the places you might want to shop would be at the
bazaars and the street markets. Shops and stores within these places
offers a variety of products like crafts, gifts, clothes, textiles,
fashion accessories and much more. You can pick up items at 30% less
than the normal price at these places.
