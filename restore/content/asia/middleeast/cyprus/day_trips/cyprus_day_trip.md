# Cyprus Day Trip

*Asia > Middleeast > Cyprus > Day Trips > Cyprus Day Trip*

Cyprus Day Trips are indeed fascinating experiences whether you choose a Guided Tour or a Boat trip or a more adventurous Jeep Safari. Cyprus is a fragmented island nation in the Mediterranean, and has for long remained an exquisite holiday retreat and a dream destination for most Europeans. Tourists who are in need of something romantic, can opt for a night tour that would include live music and dancing as well as a traditional Cypriot meal.

Cyprus Guided Tour

You can either join a Guided Day Tour of Cyprus or undertake a self-guided Cyprus tour. There is a plethora of guided tour packages available in Cyprus to meet with individual convenience and taste.

Cyprus Day Guided Tours will take you though the Troodos Mountains that will cover visits to many Cypriot villages, Nicosia city, Laiki Yitonia, Limassol town, the foothills of Troodos and Lefkara villages, Paphos town, and all places of historic significance.

The Day Guided Cyprus tours can be booked with one of the many tour operators, either directly or through local travel agents. Cyprus boasts of a diverse history and replete with natural holiday spots, and museums. Any guided day tour will invariably cover all places a tourist should see.

Even if you are on a self-guided tour, moving around Cyprus is far from difficult.

There are reliable bus services and transport facilities in Cyprus. Surprisingly, car rental are quite economical and affordable. But you should be guarded when behind the wheels as the locals quite often disobey road signals.

Cyprus Boat Excursions

If you are keen to make your Day Cyprus Tour a memorable one, the obvious thing to opt for is a boat cruise. Contact some of the most reliable boat firms Cyprus and an ideal choice would be to set sail from Limassol Port westwards, cruise along the picturesque coast, view some of the most romantic and salubrious sandy beaches of Cyprus.

Experience the cool gentle sea breezes that waft across while you cruise the Limassol coastline. In most cases, the hospitable boat crew will provide you refreshments and even a sumptuous buffet. A boat trip in Cyprus will provide you opportunities to relax in comfort, indulge in octopus fishing, sunbathe in catamaran and enjoy the breathtakingly beautiful sceneries all the way.

Cyprus Jeep Safaris

Jeep safari Day Trip in Cyprus is an extraordinary experience – partcularly for those who are fond of adventure and risk-taking. You can travel with a 4-wheel drive Land Rover Defender or such similar vehicles and you will closely observe and explore the less accessible and hence the less visited hotspots of the island.

Troodos Jeep Safaris will take you through treacherous hills and green valleys and across Kouris, the biggest dam on the island. Opting for the Akamas Jeep safari, you can explore the strange land of Akamas. You can observe the Byzantine ruins that are enveloped by rare flowers, exotic birds and enchanting landscapes.
