# Eating Out

*Asia > Middleeast > Cyprus > Limassol > Eating Out*

Those who like Mediterranean food will like Limassol restaurants, there are lots of traditional meze restaurants in the towncentre and in the eastern quarters. Near the beach there are many places that serve you standard tourist greek meal as well as the continental european food. Some of these restaurants are not bad, but for a more typical meal, try out the following: 

**general**

**The Old Fox Restaurant**

An excellent restaurant to suit all tastes. English style breakfast and dinner to traditional Greek fare. The owners were really friendly and offered a wonderfulcozy warm, and friendly atmosphere. I highly recommend it. 

address: 12-13 Amathus Avenue

openinghours: All day

tel: (5)324596

**Ouzeri to Theseion**

Live music, really delicious and great meze dishes. The restaurant only opens about nine in the evening and starts filling at about ten. Anexartisias Street 130. Tel: (05)347744
