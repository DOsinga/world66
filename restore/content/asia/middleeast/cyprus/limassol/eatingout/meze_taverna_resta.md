# Meze Taverna Restaurant

*Asia > Middleeast > Cyprus > Limassol > Eatingout > Meze Taverna Resta*

Lovely family run restaurant located near the old port in Limassol. Owned by Diomides and Kicoulla Machlouzarides who have over 30 years experience in the business. They opened their 1st restaurant in St Albans, Herts, UK in 1975 and returned to Cyprus in 1991 bringing with them all the quality and home made recipies that gave them so much success. Well worth a Visit.
