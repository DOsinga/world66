# Berbers Museum

*Asia > Middleeast > Cyprus > Northerncyprus > Levkosa > Sights > Berbersmuseum*

The Berbers museum was the former family residence of Major Ilhan, a major in the Army Medical Corps, who was killed in 1963 by EOKA fighters. Here you will find plentiful evidence of the continous feud between Turkish and Greek Cypriots.
