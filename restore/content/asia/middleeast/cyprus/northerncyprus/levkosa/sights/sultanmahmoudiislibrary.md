# Sultan Mahmoud II's Library

*Asia > Middleeast > Cyprus > Northerncyprus > Levkosa > Sights > Sultanmahmoudiislibrary*

Built in 1829 by the Turk Governor Al Ruhi, the Sultans Library hosts thousands of volumes from the great Sultan's private library. On the window sill of the small reading room you will see the Sultan's crest and signature.
