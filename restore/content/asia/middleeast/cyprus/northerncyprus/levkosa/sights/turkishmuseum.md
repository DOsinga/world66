# Turkish Museum

*Asia > Middleeast > Cyprus > Northerncyprus > Levkosa > Sights > Turkishmuseum*

Called the "Mevlevi Tekke" this museum exhibits old handicraft works and impressions of former daily life in the nowadays separated city. It is located in an old 17th century building, a former Dervish sanctuary, near the Kyrenia Gate.
