# Family Travel Ideas in Paphos

*Asia > Middleeast > Cyprus > Paphos > Family Travel Idea*

Paphos is not a typical family holiday destination brimming
with conventional attractions and sightseeing options. However, this is
precisely what makes it a city shrouded in mystery and a fascinating
unconventional aura. Here are some ideas for discovering the unusual
charm of Paphos on a family vacation.

**George’s Ranch **

Get into the cowboy act at George’s Ranch at Agios Georgios Road and
enjoy the countryside beauty of the Sea Caves strip with a pleasant and
enjoyable horse riding journey that is complete with stylish riding
hats and a friendly guide who takes you through the mystery and history
of the region. The ride also includes transportation from a few holiday
villas and insurance. This is a super idea for families and large
groups to bond over delightful sightseeing sessions. For more details
call +357 99647790 or +357 26621064.

**Ah! Museums **

You can’t savor the essence of Cyprus’ history, culture and struggle
for independence without visiting one of the several museums that
foster the illustrious heritage profile of the region. The Paphos
Archeological Museum is a good place to get the children acquainted
with the historical legacy and archeological magnificence of the
Neolithic Age. The Byzantine Museum and Ethnographic Museum are other
nice visits for their religious artifacts and folk art memorabilia.

**Pafos**** Bird Park**

A beautiful natural getaway that is very popular with kids, Pafos
Bird Park located at St. George. Pegeia is a tranquil and relaxing
nature park featuring an array of colorful species of birds in the
backdrop of lush greenery and a striking landscape.

**Aphrodite**** Water Park**

The Aphrodite Water Park is the perfect retreat for a lazy summer
afternoon with kids in tow. There is a huge collection of thrilling
rides and exciting water pools for all ages including a kiddies pool
featuring a shipwreck, the Lazy River, a wave pool and even the largest
family rafting center in the continent.

**Scuba Diving **

This one’s for the slightly older kids. Go scuba diving along the
Akamas or Wreck of Zenobia with some of the most experienced diving
schools in the region, located near main harbor of Paphos. Diving
equipment is easily available on hire and diving sessions are designed
to accommodate all levels of proficiency. This is a nice family water adventure
session to discover the coastal beauty of Paphos.

Paphos still remains a relatively undiscovered family holiday
destination and offers umpteen choices for heritage and natural
sightseeing if visitors look beyond its unassuming façade.
