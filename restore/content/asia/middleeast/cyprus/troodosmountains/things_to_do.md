# Things to do in Troodos mountains

*Asia > Middleeast > Cyprus > Troodosmountains > Things To Do*

Complete your Mediterranean trip with these five** Troodos Mountains
things to do**. The Troodos Mountain Range forms the backbone of
Cyprus and stretches across most of the western side of the country.
There are countless things to do here, and we have compiled those that
will guarantee you a vacation you will always cherish.

**Church Visits**

At the height of the Byzantine Empire, nine churches were built in
the Troodos Mountains range. Today, all nine churches are included in
the official list of World Heritage sites. The following are the nine
renowned churches; Stavros tou Ayiasmati, Panayia tou Araka, Timiou
Stavrou at pelendri, Ayios Nikolaos tis Stegis, Panayia Pdithou,
Assinou, Ayios Ioannis Lampadistis, Panayia tou Moutoula and lastly,
Archangel Michael at Pedhoulas.

**Long Walks**

Troodos Mountains also offer a fantastic natural sight-seeing
surrounding. Walking holidays in the resort of Platres offer wonderful
leisure time, as the resort is placed at exactly 1200 meters above sea
level. Taking long walks is a highly suggested way to enjoy the natural
beauty of the shady pine forest cut by loose surface road tracks, which
are formerly used as mule trails and old road routes.

**Kykkos Monastery**

The world-famous Kykkos Monastery is perhaps the most lavish among
all the monasteries in the island nation of Cyprus, and was founded
sometime at the end of the 11th century. It is also called the Panagia,
as it is dedicated to the Blessed Virgin Mary. The monastery is known
for its museum, which keeps a collection of icons, intricate
woodcarvings, manuscripts, and Cypriot antiques that come from the
island’s earlier times. One significant icon is ascribed to Saint Luke
the Evangelist.

**Jeep Safaris**

Jumpstart your vacation and set the explorer spirit in you free with
an adventure into the heart of the Troodos Mountain Ranges. Jeep Safaris
in 4WD Land Rovers would take you into a trek unlike any other. See for
yourself how time seems to be locked up in some fo the villages, which
still hold ancient customs as they were years and years ago.

**Winter Skis**

The Troodos Mountain range does not only offer leisure walks during
summer, but it also transforms itself into a skiing hotspot for active
vacationers during winter. Mount Olympus has seven runs available during
and in between New Year to the end of March.
