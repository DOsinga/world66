### [Reza Nezami](/asia/middleeast/iran/kermanshah/sights/reza_nezami)

A special area at 20 miles from kermanshah to Tehran beside the highway.

That's one of the first places in Iran that man kind live there,from stone age till now.

There are some caves with human life witnesses,big Garden,an spring with cold snowy water, a lake, an ancient site that include several period of Iran history and glory.

Some say this place has metaphysical energy which help you to feel better in mind and body.
