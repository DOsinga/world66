# Bars and Cafes in Tehran

*Asia > Middleeast > Iran > Tehran > Bars And Cafes*

The **Tehran bars and cafes** you will find in Iran are full of culture and different kinds of drinks and food you can enjoy. The variety is vast, and you are sure to find a place you will like.

#### Chelo Kebab

The Chelo Kebab is a small caf&eacute; with a lot to offer. You can find a number of food items to order and take to your table to eat. The caf&eacute; is affordable, and if you like to try kabobs, there are many different meats you can order.

#### Iranian Traditional

This caf&eacute; is a fun place to stop and enjoy a meal or have a drink. There are a lot of people who gather at the bar to enjoy the music and lively entertainment. If you enjoy having a social event with others and enjoying great food and drinks, this caf&eacute; is one of the Tehran bars and cafes you should see.

#### Azari

The Azari is a traditional style caf&eacute; which offers a lot of cultural favorites. You can sit and relax while you listen to the live music being performed, and you can choose from the traditional cuisines that are served. If you want to try something different, there are many different kinds of meat dishes you may want to order. The Azari may be one of the stops you want to make on your vacation in the city.
