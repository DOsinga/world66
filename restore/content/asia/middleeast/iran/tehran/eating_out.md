# Eating Out in Tehran

*Asia > Middleeast > Iran > Tehran > Eating Out*

Finding a great place to eat in Tehran provides you with a lot of options in **Tehran restaurants**. You may want to try some of the traditional dishes that are served.

#### Alef Resto

The Alef Resto restaurant has a variety of choices. You can enjoy the Italian dishes or the American dishes that are served. There are also a lot of different dishes which are unique to the restaurant. There are different kinds of music played in the restaurant, and you can relax and enjoy the dishes or try or a local favorite.

#### Nasim Shomal

The Nasim Shomal restaurant is a family-style place to eat and has something for adults and children to enjoy. The prices are very affordable. If you want to find a place with a lot of good food and things for children to enjoy, this is one of the Tehran restaurants you should try when you are on vacation.

#### Ferdossi

This is a traditional-style restaurant with beautiful scenery, and it is a nice place to relax with a good meal. There is also a saloon area where you can sit and relax with your meal or drinks and enjoy the many traditional items available on the menu. The Ferdossi is one of the Tehran restaurants to enjoy, and it is also very affordable.
