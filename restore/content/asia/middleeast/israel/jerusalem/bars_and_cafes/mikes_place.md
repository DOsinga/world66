# Mike's Place

*Asia > Middleeast > Israel > Jerusalem > Bars And Cafes > Mikes Place*

A good place for nightly live music (including open mic nights) as well as some good drink specials during their happy hour. There is plenty of outdoor as well as indoor seating. In the Russian compound, amongst a bunch of other fun bars. A lot of foreigners (mostly American) and decent food.
