# Uncle Moustach's felafel

*Asia > Middleeast > Israel > Jerusalem > Eatingout > Uncle Moustachs F*

Uncle Moustaches, just inside Herod's gate in the Old City of Jerusalem is a cheap but wonderful place to get fleafel, chicken and rice soup and stories. Not a fancy place but goot eats at a good price. Ask to see the traveler's book if they still have it and read people's entries from all over the world. See: http://www.flickr.com/photos/dolgin/4619918904/

Enjoy!
