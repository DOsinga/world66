# Eating Out

*Asia > Middleeast > Israel > Telaviv > Eating Out*

[Tel Aviv](/asia/middleeast/israel/telaviv) has restaurants in almost all the styles in world. From typical Jewish and Middle Eastern food, to South American, European and Asian cousines.
Prices and setting vary.
The restaurants are scattered all over town. They are not all concentrated in one place.

### [Pronto](/asia/middleeast/israel/telaviv/eating_out/pronto)

Pronto is a classical upmarket Italian restaurant. It has superb food, served in classical style. Contrary to many other Italian restaurants, it is not Kosher, thus the dishes are not limited. Meat and dairy products are both served, sometimes together.
It is the only Italian restaurant that was officialy acknowledged by the Italian embassy.
A good choice fpr special occasions.

type: Italian
