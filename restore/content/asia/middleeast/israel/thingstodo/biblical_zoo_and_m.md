# Biblical Zoo and Miniature Israel

*Asia > Middleeast > Israel > Thingstodo > Biblical Zoo And M*

Anyone with time on their hands should visit the Biblical Zoo or the Miniature Israel. The zoo is accessible by train from anywhere in Israel. From Jerusalem, it's only one station away from the central train station. Miniature Israel is a really cool place to visit especially with children. It also gives people an idea of what there is to see in Israel
