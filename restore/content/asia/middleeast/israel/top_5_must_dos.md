# Top 5 Must Do's in Israel

*Asia > Middleeast > Israel > Top 5 Must Dos*

The Israel-Trail.

this is a hiking trail that takes you from the north point of the israeli border, close to the Hermon mountain, all the way to the south point of taba and eilat!

almost 1000 kilometers of great landscape, people, remote places and various sites- from the green of the north to the majestic negev desert.

amazing walking trail, well marked - a lifetime experience!!!!

for more details look for the israel trail in google.
