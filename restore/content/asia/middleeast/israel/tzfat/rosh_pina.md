# Rosh Pina Travel Guide

*Asia > Middleeast > Israel > Tzfat > Rosh Pina*

The best resource for sights, hotels, restaurants, bars, what to do and see

Rosh Pina is one of the original settlements in Israel. It straddles the Meron mountain range. The houses and shops are stunning with their cobblestone streets and outdoor cafes and restaurants. It's definitely worth a visit.
