# Budget Travel Ideas in Amman

*Asia > Middleeast > Jordan > Amman > Budget Travel Idea*

Jordan has always been famous for its ruined Roman cities, including
the castles of the Crusaders, numerous biblical sites and the infamous
desert citadels. The country’s capital city Amman is very culturally
diverse and is quickly becoming a metropolis filled with modern
amenities. Featuring varied backdrops ranging from the red desert sands
to the beautiful coral filled gulf, this area of the world has a lot to
offer the traveler. Amman makes a great starting point for one to
explore the country and has many opportunities for those on a budget.

**Things to do**

The most beautiful place advised to see in Amman is the sunset at
the viewpoint situated near Citadel where you can listen to the muezzin
call from the viewpoint and see the entire city in front of you. Other
notable attractions include the Roman Theatre, the Citadel and Darat al
Funun. With admission fees ranging from $1 to $3, these sites are
filled with historical and cultural significance, while perfect for the
budget traveler.

Shopping in Amman is a great way to spend a day with countless malls
and shopping areas that are suitable even for those on a budget. The
Mecca Mall, Plaza Mall, Abdoun Mall and City Mall are all good choices
to peruse. The downtown Amman area known as the Balad is also a nice
shopping area where most prices are negotiable.

**What to eat**

Amman offers to its visitors numerous different styles of
restaurants and cuisines with prices ranging from super cheap to
moderate. For budget travelers, Arabic food is going to be the least
expensive and most widely available. Shwarma is a local favorite in
Amman, while Jordan’s specialty mansaf should not be missed.

**What to buy**

Amman has numerous antique shops located all over the city that are
great to locate a timeless souvenir. Some other souvenirs specific to
the area could include handcrafted daggers, Bedouin-style clothing,
olive wood carvings and an antique brass tea or coffee pot.

**Where to stay**

Amman has a complete range of accommodation options for people. With
very basic one star accommodation to luxurious five star. Some of the
best budget hotels include Al-Marmin, Cliff Hostel and Amman Castle
Hotel. These accommodations offer great service at a cheap rate.

Plan on spending a couple days in this capital city before launching
off to the other sites of Jordan and experience the old and new
cultures of Amman melding to form a unique cosmopolitan city.
