Asia / Middle East / Jordan / Amman - Budget
Travel Ideas

Jordan, one of the Middle
Eastern countries has always been famous for its ruined roman cities; castles
of the crusaders, biblical sites and of course the desert citadels. Amman,
which is known to be Jordan’s capital city, is very culturally diverse and
modern in its own ways. This city is literally light years away from what was
called the Middle Eastern exoticism. This region of the country offers itself
the wildest adventures for all the tourists to experience; it also has to offer
the varied backdrops of nature ranging from the red desert sands to the
incredibly beautiful coral filled gulf.
Amman makes a great base for one to explore the country. It also holds a
few items that interest many travelers. The people are very passionate and they
welcome visitors with their arms wide open.

Things to do

The most beautiful
moment advised to see is the sunset at the viewpoint situated near Citadel.
When you listen to the muezzin call from the viewpoint, you will witness the
whole city in front of you. Shopping is what everyone loves to do and it is
highly advisable here. The Mecca mall may be your best location. The other huge
malls are Plaza Mall, Abdoun Mall and City Mall. Even if you are on a budget travel,
the shops and stores at these malls are cheap and affordable. The cosmopolitan
crowd visits many nightclubs and bars.
Another place that is a must visit is the Book@Cafe which has everything
from seesha, food, drinks and it is indeed one of the best places to chill out
at.

What to eat

Amman offers to its
visitor’s different styles of restaurants and cuisines. Prices offer from ultra
cheap to moderate depending on what one likes to eat. For those on a budget travel
Arabic food is the most affordable and can be found anywhere, shwarma being the
most favorite.

What to buy

Amman has numerous antique shops located all over the city. For
more adventurous shopping Balad is the best place, it gives a more primitive
feel and the shops are cheap and negotiable since prices are not always marked.
This is the best place for all the budget travelers. Some souvenirs could
include the crafted daggers, Bedouin clothing etc

Where to stay

Amman has a complete range of accommodation options for people.
With very basic one star accommodation to luxurious five star. Some of the
budget hotels are Al-Marmin, Cliff Hostel, Amman Castle hotel etc that offer
its best service at a cheap rate.
