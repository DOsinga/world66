# Sights

*Asia > Middleeast > Jordan > Amman > Sights*

Some of the sections in Amman have very steep hills and homes are built atop these. There are stairs leading to each level for shorter access roots. Be sure to wear a good pair of walking shoes as you explore the architecture of the city.

Although there are many extremes when it comes to homes in Amman. Everything from the very wealthy to the poorest of poor. All have their own unique beauty. The warmth of a Jordanian home is no less or greater because of the size or expense. You will always be treated with kindness and respect and courtesy and given the best of everything when you are a guest in a Jordanian home. Treating others with such care and consideration is the pride of Jordan.
