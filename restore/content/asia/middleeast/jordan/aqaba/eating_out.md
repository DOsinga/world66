# Eating Out in Aqaba

*Asia > Middleeast > Jordan > Aqaba > Eating Out*

Aqaba is the only seaport in Jordan. **Aqaba Restaurants** range from cafes to fancy restaurants. Being close to the sea, the restaurants are invested in seafood. This part of Jordan is at the corner of Israel and Egypt borders.

**Fast Food Arabic Style**

There a number of fast chains in Aqaba. They range from McDonalds to local Jordanian chains. Dishes include Alibaba&rsquo;s shrimp and pita bread on the beach or burgers from McDonald&rsquo;s. Fast food from the local establishments is some of the more interesting choices in the area. The stands offer great local food with low prices.

**Cafes**

Cafes are a big part of life in Jordanian. People come to the cafes to gossip and catch up on local events. In this seaside the majority of the caf&eacute; serve simple seafood dishes. Some are Internet caf&eacute;s for tourists who need to check email. The cafes offer good food at reasonable prices. Ali Baba is a famous caf&eacute; by the beach that sells kabobs and shrimp.

**Restaurants**

There are several excellent restaurants in Aqaba, especially in the hotels. The food is world class and highly sought after. Local chefs have integrated the favors of the Mediterranean into their food. As this city is near Israel and Egypt, their influences have been seen in the food. Egyptian barbeque is a popular format in Aqaba and is offered in nearly restaurant. Another point of consideration is the abundant sea life in the area. Seafood is a must-have as the cooks work it into the majority of dishes.
