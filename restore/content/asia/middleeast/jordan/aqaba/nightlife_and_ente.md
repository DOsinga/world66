# Nightlife and Entertainment in Aqaba

*Asia > Middleeast > Jordan > Aqaba > Nightlife And Ente*

**Aqaba Nightlife** is a combination of local hangouts and trendy 5-star lounges and bars. This is a seaport in Jordan that transforms into a partying haven after sundown. Read on to find out about the nightlife in Aqaba.

**Oasis Lounge**

To witness the upscale partying scene venture out to the Oasis Lounge. It is located at the lobby level of the Days Inn hotel on Saada Street. This place is well known among the elite and older age group, as the ambiance is more calm and relaxing. The lounge is a great place to stir up a conversation with friends. The music is perfect and creates a very romantic atmosphere. Alcoholic drinks like beer, wine, champagne and liquor are served at this lounge.

**Tumbelweed Bar**

This is a famous bar in the whole of Aqaba. It's located right at the center of the city and attracts the youth of the city. The bar has a spacious dance floor and the in-house DJ will make you groove to various international and local tracks. The beer mugs are extra large and the margaritas are out of this world. If you are looking to get a pulse of the way the youth parties, then the Tumbelweed is the place to be.

**Rovers**

Rovers is a popular pub among the locals in Aqaba. It overlooks the waterfront and is located in the Gateway Complex. The patio at Rovers makes for a great outdoor partying scene. The pub is designed to resemble an English bar. The bar has tap beer and the bartenders put on a great show. The food menu also boasts of several Western dishes like fish and chips, pastas and more.

**Friends
**
This is a causal club located in the Gateway Complex in Aqaba. The major landmark next to this pub is the McDonald's. The atmosphere is extremely relaxed and the dress code is casual. The DJ plays Western music from hip hop to rock and also country. This is a great place to drink, eat and party the night away. This club stays open till midnight.

**Kenzi Lounge**

Enjoy the nightlife in Aqaba with luxury and comfort to keep you company. The Kenzi Lounge is a high-end night spot. The Radisson Hotel hosts this exotic lounge. It is known for its elegance and class. The lounge has a cigar bar that features world-famous brands. The cognac and eats are something to look forward to. Enjoy the plush yet warm atmosphere of this lounge that stays open till 1:00 a.m.
