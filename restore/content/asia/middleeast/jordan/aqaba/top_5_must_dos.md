# Top 5 Must Do's in Aqaba

*Asia > Middleeast > Jordan > Aqaba > Top 5 Must Dos*

The list of **Aqaba must do&rsquo;s** can be long when you are going to be traveling to the area. There are some attractions that you should be sure to fit into your schedule and enjoy while you are on vacation.

#### 1. Scuba Diving in the Red Sea

The Red Sea offers crystal waters and plenty of underwater life to enjoy. You can find scuba gear to rent and take lessons if you need to learn more about scuba diving in Aqaba. You can enjoy the many dive spots that allow you to explore the underwater world and swim. You can also enjoy the coral reef that attracts many tourists from all over to enjoy some of the best scuba diving in the area.

#### 2. Jordon Artist Tours

You can experience the excitement and fun of the city and area on a seven-day cycling tour. You can explore the different artisan displays at each stop and stay that you make on the trip, and if you are a fan of cycling, this is one of the best ways to enjoy the area and you may want to add it to your list of Aqaba must do&rsquo;s.

#### 3. Ayla Ruins

If you want to learn more about the culture in Aqaba, you should visit the Ayla ruins. They were built around A.D. 650 and the first Islamic city that was built outside of the Arabian peninsula. The site is open anytime for you to check out the unique structures and learn more about the culture.

#### 4. Aqaba Archaeological Museum

The Aqaba Archaeological Museum has a collection of rare treasures from seventh to 12th centuries A.D. You can enjoy viewing the many items on display in the museum and enjoy the rich culture of the city when you are traveling. You should consider adding this spot to your Aqaba must do&rsquo;s if you like to learn more about the artifacts that are found and kept in the museum.

#### 5. Aqaba Bird Observatory

The Aqaba Bird Observatory is home to more than 350 species of birds. If you are a fan of birds, there are some of the most rare and beautiful birds that are in the observatory for you to enjoy. You can learn more about each of the species and see where they are found. The birds in the observatory are brought in from all over and enjoyed by many each year. Spring and autumn are the best times to see the birds when you are in the area.
