# Temple of Artemis

*Asia > Middleeast > Jordan > Jerash > Sights > Templeofartemis*

Artemis was the Goddess of chastity, protectress of nature and children.Often seen in the forests and lakes in the company of Nymphs. The temple was built on an area covering 34,000 m square, overlooking the city.

Dates back to the 2nd century Ad.
