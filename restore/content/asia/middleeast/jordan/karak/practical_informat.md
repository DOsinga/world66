# Practical Information in Karak

*Asia > Middleeast > Jordan > Karak > Practical Informat*

Audio guided tours are possible by renting the ***easyguide*** audio guide which comes with a map of the citadel and directions on how to go about the formidable castle in order to understand the layout and history of the place. The multi-lingual audio guide machines are available for rent at the ***easyguide*** booth out side the castle
