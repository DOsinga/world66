POSTAL SERVICES
Postal services are available at the post office & post boxes are
placed in all hotels except for the Jordan Hotel. Guests staying at Jordan Hotel
can post their letters at the adjacent Captain’s Hotel. Cost of stamp for a letter
or a postcard to Europe is about ( JD 0.500 ) available with VJV representative or at the
post office but not in hotels.
