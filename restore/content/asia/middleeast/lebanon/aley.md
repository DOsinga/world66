# aley

*Asia > Middleeast > Lebanon > Aley*

Aley is located in the mountains near Beirut, and features many nice traditional Lebanese castles and houses. It is also a popular area to hang out, with restaurants and such atop the mountains. It is a nice summer getaway because of the cool air.
