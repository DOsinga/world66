# Getting Around in Beirut

*Asia > Middleeast > Lebanon > Beirut > Getting Around*

If you are a newbie in Lebanon, you&rsquo;ll be able to get around safely with **Beirut transportation**. Known as the capital city of Lebanon, Beirut attracts tourists with its historic and beautiful landmarks and festivities. The city is very accessible via land, sea, and air.

Despite the devastating damage of war a few years ago, Beirut can still claim its title of Paris of the East. Thanks to its location on the Mediterranean Sea, it is a top summer getaway for Europeans and Arabs. It offers recreation for young and old alike. The well-organized and disciplined Beirut transportation makes everybody&rsquo;s stay in this city a memorable one. Here are the modes of transportation that can take you to Beirut and around the city.

**Buses**

Buses are numerous in Beirut. There are privately owned buses that can take you on a tour with other tourists at very affordable fares while there are also those owned by the government. You can take the bus for an adventure-filled day around the city and never worry about getting lost.

**Taxis**

If you prefer to explore the whole place all by yourself, your best option is to take the cab. There are many taxis around with very friendly drivers to take you wherever you want to go. Just have your map ready or ask the driver himself for directions.

**Ferries**

Ferries can also take you to Beirut since the city is a port of entry. You can take one from Cyprus and enjoy the view from the sea. You can always take the bus or hire a taxi to take you to your destination upon arrival.
