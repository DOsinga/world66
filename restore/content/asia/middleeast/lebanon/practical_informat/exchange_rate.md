# Exchange rate

*Asia > Middleeast > Lebanon > Practical Informat > Exchange Rate*

The Lebanese Lira is pegged to the US Dollar. The rate is about 1510 Lira to 1 USD. Most places accept US Dollars at an exchange rate of 1500 Lira to the Dollar.
