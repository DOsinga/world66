# Things to do

*Asia > Middleeast > Lebanon > Tyre > Things To Do*

Beside visiting the monuments, you can also enjoy the Sea Life Museum as well as enjoy scuba diving with the newly opened **Hiram Scuba Diving** club.

Facing the Mina, Hiram club is equiped with the finest scuba gear for divers to enjoy an unforgettable underwater adventure in the clear water of Tyre.

From "**The Crater**" (36 - 60 m) to the "**Fresh Water Fountains"** (35 - 50 m) to the various reefs (11 - 20 m) and monuments of the old city laying at few meters under the sea, diving Tyre will be the most fasinating experience ever.
