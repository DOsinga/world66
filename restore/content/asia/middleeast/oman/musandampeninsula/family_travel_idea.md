# Family Travel Ideas in Musandam peninsula

*Asia > Middleeast > Oman > Musandampeninsula > Family Travel Idea*

The Musandam Peninsula juts out into the Straits of Hormuz. It is a geographical enclave of Oman, separated from the main country by the UAE. It also has a very distinct landscape of jagged peaks and an interesting coastline. The fjords rival those of Norway in natural splendor and beauty.

**Best Ways to Go Sightseeing**

There is a highway that runs down the entire west coast of the peninsula. Unless you have very young children, a drive down the coast is recommended. From the highway one can see the various peaks as well as the beautiful coast. The sight is certainly worth the trip by road.

**Dhow Ride
**

Yet another mode of transport, perhaps the most popular is by the traditional dhows. These have been fitted out to take tourists around. You can hire one and take your family on a pleasant and exploratory trip down the many waterways. Unlike in the case of motorized transports which put off the dolphins, the dhows attract the dolphins that come in fairly close to them. Your family, including your children, will enjoy these sightings. Come prepared to finish off the trip with a campfire by a beautiful isolated beach. Again, this is something that the entire family will enjoy and take part in.

**Water Sports
**

As the Musandam Peninsula has to be visited for its natural beauty, there are no other entertaining sights to see. Taking a cruise down the waterways is the most relaxing thing that you can do. If you have teenagers with you, they can go snorkeling and diving with you. Take your own gear, because rentals can be quite high in this area.

**Fjords
**

This trip is certainly worthwhile for the whole family to experience nature at its best. There are few sights more majestic than the fjords of Musandam. The views are simply breathtaking.
The best way to reach Musandam Peninsula from Muscat is by ferry. It's said to be the world’s fastest and is certainly quicker than crossing borders as you would have to do if you traveled by road.

Make the most of your trip by taking a three-day cruise and seeing all that this treasure trove of natural beauty has to offer the nature lover. It is definitely worth taking the trouble to visit this place with your family. There is indeed something here for everyone.
