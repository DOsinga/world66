# Getting Around in Muscat

*Asia > Middleeast > Oman > Muscat > Getting Around*

**Various Muscat Transportation Methods**

Tips one needs to know about **Muscat transportation** in getting around the area are few. Muscat is the capital city of Oman and the largest city in the country. The city is located along the Gulf of Oman and is proximity to the Straits of Hormuz. So, what are the available modes of transportation in Muscat?

**Muscat Taxis **

Most tourists in Muscat prefer to ride the taxi because of its convenience and cheap cost. There are two taxi companies that are quite reliable and these are the Comfort Line and the Hello Taxis. Buses are also another transportation option in Muscat. The Oman National Transport bus line services Muscat and other regions in Oman. Buses are also convenient for tourists to ride because the bus lines offer excellent service and have routes within and beyond the city of Muscat. Riding a private car is also a good option to tour the city of Muscat. Muscat&rsquo;s roads are well-paved and well kept. Road signs are written in both Arabic and English which makes it easier for tourists to navigate the city of Muscat.

**Transportation Tips **

Taxi fares are not metered. The best way to pay for your taxi fare is to have an idea of the cost of the fare by asking one&rsquo;s hotel or host. If you ask the cab driver before getting in, this would show you are new to the place, so you will need to avoid this as much as possible.
