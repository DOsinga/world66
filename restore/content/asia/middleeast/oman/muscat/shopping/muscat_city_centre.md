# Muscat City Centre

*Asia > Middleeast > Oman > Muscat > Shopping > Muscat City Centre*

**Muscat City Centre
**Muscat City Centre is the largest mall in Oman, it is located 5 Km's away from the Muscat International Airport, it has all kinds of shops from clothing shops (Zara, Gap, Forever21, Babyshop, and many others), computer shops (E-Max, Nokia, Samsung and a few others), bookshops (Borders, all though not as big as the one in Dubai, but quite enough), Furniture shops are also available. It also has a big food court with games for kids and older ones. In the food court you can find: KFC, McDonalds, Hardees, Subway and many many other shops that are known world-wide.

**Carrefour
**The mall also has a big section of Carrefour which has all the household items at the lowest prices in the country.

**Parking Lots
**The mall's marking lot has just been expanded, and now it has 3 floors of parking lots which can hold up to 300,000 cars.**
