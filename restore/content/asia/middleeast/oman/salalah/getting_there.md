# Getting There in Salalah

*Asia > Middleeast > Oman > Salalah > Getting There*

Salalah is conveniently connected by direct flights to other cities in Oman,
the Middle East and India and has a well-established road network to link it
with other Omani destinations. Here is a handy guide to getting to Salalah
using various modes of transport.

**Air Travel**

Oman Air schedules 3 flights a day from Muscat to Salalah and the flight takes
approximately 90 minutes. There are convenient direct
flights between Dubai and Salalah scheduled twice a week on Sundays and
Tuesdays. There are also a few direct flights linking Salalah with India's cities of Kochi and Thiruvananthapuram.

**Road Transport**

A car trip from Muscat to Salalah takes around 12 to14 hours with several detours along the labyrinth roads of Oman. The exciting journey covers small
picturesque towns including Nizwa, Haima and Rusayl. There are also some rough
dunes, small hamlets and old fields to discover en route. Try to schedule the
drive at early evening or during the night since it can be extremely stressful to
travel through the scorching heat of the day.

There is a very efficient and well-networked daily bus service that connects
all cities of Oman with reasonable rates compared to the exorbitant private taxi
fares. The 12-hour Muscat-Salalah trip is priced at $ 20 and visitors can enjoy
pretty views of the Omani landscape along the way. The buses are relatively
safe and functionally comfortable.

Salalah has a well-connected transport system that links it to all towns in
Oman and several others in neighboring countries.
