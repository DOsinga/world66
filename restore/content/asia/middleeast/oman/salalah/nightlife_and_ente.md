**Salalah - Nightlife and Entertainment**

The nightlife scene in Salalah is somewhat restrained and insipid, given that
it’s still a deeply conventional Islamic city and a boisterous nightlife is not
exactly in tandem with local religious customs. However party enthusiasts need
not fret because there is a handful of night clubs, bars and pubs in 5-star hotels
that play haunting Arabic melodies and smooth R & B tunes. At Salalah, more
than the venues, the fun lies in interesting people-watching sessions. Here is a
guide to the nightlife of Salalah.

**Mayfair**

This smart-casual English-pub style venue at the Hilton Salalah Resort is a
great place to get together with friends over a mug of beer, while cheering for
your favorite team playing at a sporting event being telecast on a giant-sized
screen. The ambiance is classy and the pub is adorned with deep chocolate-brown furniture.
The place is informal and cozy without losing its classical appeal. Visitors can
feast on scrumptious burgers, huge club sandwiches and local delicacies like
meat kebabs and rolls. Head to Mayfair if you are looking for a noisy yet
intimate night out. The bar is closed from 3 p.m. to 6 p.m.

**Whispers Lounge**

This plush lounge, also located at the Hilton, is a very popular nightspot
with tourists and the local expat community. There are live band performances
and dance shows along with a line-up of other entertainment programs. The place
is also known to serve brilliant cocktails and an array of fine wines along with
some nice international starters. The lounge is open from daily from 9 p.m. to
3 a.m.

**Al Luban Night Club**

The Al Luban Night Club, housed at the lobby level of Crowne Plaza Resort, is
a restaurant cum night club that is frequented by tourists and well-heeled
locals. The elegant and expensive party destination serves gourmet cuisine and
features live performances by several local and international brands. The DJ
plays great tunes and there are some special weekend nights, when local singers
and dancers are invited to entertain the crowds. The glamorous party haunt is
one of the few spots in Salalah, where you can let your hair down and dance like
crazy.

**Al Khareef Pub**

Al Khareef is a richly decorated, sophisticated pub located in the Crowne
Plaza Resort and is a crowd puller for its lovely setting in the backdrop of a
stunning Arabian Sea view. This popular hangout features a quiet, casual and upscale ambiance without appearing ostentatious. The cocktails are some
of the best in town and the place is a great option for a romantic evening.

Salalah has a relatively restricted and staid nightlife, owing to the fact
that liquor laws are a little stringent and liquor permits are only given to
non-Islamic tourists and expats.
