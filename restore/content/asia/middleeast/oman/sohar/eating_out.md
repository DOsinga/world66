# Eating Out in Sohar

*Asia > Middleeast > Oman > Sohar > Eating Out*

Finding a place to eat in Sohar Oman can bring you to a number of **Sohar restaurants**. There is something for every taste in the city and you can find the best restaurants that have the cuisine, price, and location that you are looking for. There are restaurants that serve American cuisine, Indian cuisine, Lebanese cuisine, Thai cuisine, Chinese cuisine, and seafood dishes that offer everyone something new to try on the vacation.

#### Silk Route

The Silk Route restaurant in Sohar offers Chinese and Thai cuisine. The restaurant is located on the first floor of the Sohar Plaza. This is a more expensive restaurant, but is excellent if you are looking for some great menu items that offer many tastes of traditional Chinese and Thai cuisine. The restaurant also caters to those who are looking for the variety of Chinese and Thai cuisine that are difficult to find in most locations.

#### Al Sallan

The Al Sallan restaurant is located in the Al Wadi hotel is Sohar. It offers dining near the views of the pool terrace and has a variety of menu items. You can find Indian cuisine and also Asian cuisine among other local favorites on the menu. The views of the relaxing pool offer a nice place to enjoy your dinner and try something new on the menu from the Indian cuisine that is available to enjoy.

#### Sohar Beach Restaurant

The Sohar Beach restaurant is located in the Sohar Beach resort and offers views of the beachside to enjoy while you sample a variety of seafood dishes that are prepared in the restaurant. The restaurant is more expensive than some of the others, but you can enjoy the beach views and also the many seafood items that are available for cuisine and to try on your vacation.

#### Golden Goal

The Golden Goal restaurant offers a variety of different items on the menu and the main specialty of the restaurant is the Indian cuisine that is offered. The prices are affordable, and you can enjoy the different tastes of the Indian food that are traditional and also there are other menu items that you can enjoy.

#### Club Tropicana

Club Tropicana is located in the Al Buraimi Hotel and offers a variety of Indian and Lebanese dishes to choose from. The hotel offers affordable dining in the city and if you want to find Lebanese cuisine at a good price, you can find this restaurant to be one of the Sohar restaurants that you should try.
