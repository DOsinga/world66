# Shopping in Sohar

*Asia > Middleeast > Oman > Sohar > Shopping*

The** Sohar Shopping** experience will impress you with its glamor and high fashion. Sohar is one of the main cities in Oman and is a jewel in the Middle Eastern crown. Read on to find out about the shopping scene in Sohar.****

**Safeer Mall**

Experience the opulence involved in the shopping scene in Sohar by visiting the Safeer Mall. The architecture of this mall reflects a Middle Eastern style. The Mall features 76 outlets and 8 different food courts. The main attraction is the amusement park spread across 30,000 square feet. There are several designer brands available and you can buy things ranging from fashion accessories to home d&eacute;cor here. There are several supermarkets in this Mall. The Safeer Mall is located in the heart of Sohar city. The mall is open from 11:00 a.m. to 10:00 p.m.

**Al Fair**

Al Fair is a popular supermarket in Sohar. It is a major landmark in the city; hence you will not have a problem finding it. The supermarket is a great place to find international products. Al Fair has the reputation of catering to the international crowd and tourists in Sohar. The supermarket has various international products. From everyday items to spices and dressings, this place has it all.

**Lulu**

Lulu is one of the largest hypermarkets in Sohar. It is located very close to the Sallan beach in Sohar. This is an ultra-modern facility in Sohar that caters to each and every need of the customers. From the fresh food section to a food court, this place has it all. There is a separate playing area for the kids. You can buy various home appliance and d&eacute;cor items as well.

**Centerpoint**

Centerpoint is another mall that you will find in the city center in Sohar. This mall houses several brands that focus on fashion. Footwear, hats, perfumes and cosmetics, you name it and you will find it in this mall. Take a stroll through the food court and choose between the wide variety of international and local cuisines.

**Al Hujra**

To get a feel of the local culture and shopping habits, visit the street side market in Al Hujra. This market dwells among the mega malls and shopping centers in Sohar. This is a typical Middle Eastern market that is known for its traditional offerings. You can buy souvenirs here. There are several shops that sell hookahs, the traditional smoking pot. The chicken roll of shawarma sold on this street is a true treat.
