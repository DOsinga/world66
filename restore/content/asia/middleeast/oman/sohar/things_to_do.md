# Things to Do in Sohar

*Asia > Middleeast > Oman > Sohar > Things To Do*

In **Sohar, things to do **are plentiful. Sohar is located in northern Oman and is the home town of Sindbad the Sailor. Sohar is the largest city in the country of Oman and was at one point an important Islamic port in the region. This region is popular for tourists because it is home to clean beaches and other interesting archeological sights. To learn more about things to do in Sohar, read on.

**See Sohar Castle**

Sohar Castle is perhaps the most famous feature of the city and it was built between the 13th and 14th century. After Imam Nasser bin Murshid Al Ya'arubi conquered the Portuguese and expelled them from the castle, it was then used as an administration center for the rulers of the dynasty. This fascinating castle is home to a museum that visitors will want to explore while they tour the castle. The castle is also considered a historical monument of Sohar, and it's a good idea to visit it while you are in the region.

**Relax on the Beaches of Sohar**

Sohar is home to attractive, safe beaches where tourists can relax and unwind during their stay in the city. The beaches of Sohar are also known for their clean and refreshing waters, making them even more enjoyable.

**Visit the Parks**

Sohar has two major parks. Silver Jubilee Park which is located in Sallan, and Sohar Park which is in Al Humbar. Touring the parks is a great way to spend an afternoon in Sohar, especially if you are looking for a more low-key activity.

**Shop in Sohar**

Sohar has shopping centers that include the traditional Souk in the town center and the market located near the Sohar Castle in Al Hujra. There are also several new malls and markets in Sohar, including the Safeer Hypermarket, Al Fair, Lulu, and the Centerpoint. Shopping within this city can be a great way for travelers to tour the city and see the sights along the way.

**Ride Your Bike Around the City**

Hopping on a bike and riding around the city of Sohar can be a great way for you to experience the scenery and culture of the city and to see what goes on in the area. The weather can be hot and humid in the summer, but from September until April the weather is generally milder. Temperatures rarely drop enough to make outdoor activities impossible, so Sohar is an ideal location to be outdoors all year long.
