# When to Go in Sohar

*Asia > Middleeast > Oman > Sohar > When To Go*

If you are planning a visit to **Sohar, when to go **is one of the things you will need to decide. This small city is located outside the capital of Oman, and is a popular tourist destination. The city is near the beach, and sailing is a deep-rooted tradition of the people living in Sohar. This also contributes to the attractiveness of Sohar as a travel destination. Here is some of the information about the various times to go to Sohar.

**April to September - Sailing
**

The summer months in Sohar are usually from April to September. They are characterized by strong winds that can reach 6mph. The summer months are the best time to go sailing, which is what makes Sohar famous all over the world. Moreover, there are a lot of sailing activities and events that take place during these months. If you are planning a visit during the summer, you will be able to participate in these activities.

**May and June Sightseeing**

The best time to go sightseeing in Sohar is during the months from May to June, since the country gets an average of 11 hours of sunligh. This means there's plenty of time to stroll around and see all the sights in the city, such as the Sohar Castle.

**November to February**

If you tour the city during the months of November to February, then the average sunlight will just be 8 hours a day, which means that you will have less time to spend exploring the city. However, this limitation is compensated by the fact that Sohar&rsquo;s nightlife comes more alive during the winter months than any other season in the city.

**October - Sohar Music Festival **

There are dozens of things to do during the autumn months in Sohar. One of the activities that you should not miss out on is the Sohar International Jazz Music Festiva,l which takes place at the Crown Plaza Hotel every October.

**All Year Destination**

Sohar gets moderate yet windy weather all year round, and this predectable weather makes it easy to travel to Sohar during any time of year. Just make sure that you wear appropriate clothing like trousers and long sleeves so as not to invite any apprehensions from most traditional Omani in the street.
