# Shopping in Dammam

*Asia > Middleeast > Saudiarabia > Dammam > Shopping*

**Dammam shopping** provides some of the best shopping in all of Saudi Arabia. People in Saudi Arabia really love to shop and it shows as you walk through the malls. Since nightclubs and bars are banned in Saudi Arabia shopping is a major form of entertainment. You should note that women are not allowed to work in sales so you will only find men working in the shops. Most of the time the shops do not have dressing rooms. It is not recommended that women try on clothes in the back of the store.

**Al Bilad Mall**

The Al Bilad Mall is a great place to shop and do some sightseeing. You will often find women and children walking through the mall just enjoying the day. Al Bilad Mall offers all the shopping you could ask for. You can find everything from traditional Saudi items to Western items. There are also several music shops and video game stores.

**Tamimi Markets**

Tamimi Markets is similar to the grocery store Safeway. The Tamimi Market is a excellent place to pick up any food or personal hygiene items you might need. Keep in mind that the prices and items might be different than what your used to back at home.

**Obeikan Bookstore**

You can find all the reading materials you want at Obeikan Bookstore. Certain items that you find in the western culture are banned in Saudi bookstores. Even though you might not find some items you are familiar with it is still worth a visit to this bookstore. The Obeikan offers a wide selection of reading materials.

**The Baby Shop**

If you are in Saudi Arabia with a baby then this is a store you should take note of. You can find all the baby items you need at The Baby Shop. This store is a one stop shop for everything related to babies.

**Ladies Mall**

The Ladies Mall is a mall that only women are allowed in. As a woman you should be able to find personal hygine items, clothing and beautiful jewelry. There is a wide selection of shopping for the ladies at this mall.

**Al Shatea Mall**

Al Shatea Mall is a major shopping mall in the heart of Dammam. This mall offers women's clothing, men's clothing, children clothing, jewelry, electronics and home decor. There is also a food court in this mall that has some tasty fast food restaurants.
