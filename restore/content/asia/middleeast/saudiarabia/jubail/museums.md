# Museums in Jubail

*Asia > Middleeast > Saudiarabia > Jubail > Museums*

Jubail is a very efficient industrial city but unfortunately there is almost no tourist attraction that focuses on fun and entertainment. This also includes **museums** and galleries. Even though there is proof that human habitation here dates back 7000 years nothing has been kept for display of this heritage to the public. If you find yourself in Jubail and you want to do some research or are perhaps curious about the local history you have to travel a short way to other cities in hope of finding the information you require.

In the city of Damman, which is about 80 miles away, is the home of several museums you may find of interest. **The Damman National Museum** is your starting place. Located on the 4th floor of the main public library in the downtown district it is easy to find. Further south about 15 miles is the city of Al Khobar that is a treasure chest of places that have information and artifacts of Saudi Arabia. These places are a must to see for travelers.
