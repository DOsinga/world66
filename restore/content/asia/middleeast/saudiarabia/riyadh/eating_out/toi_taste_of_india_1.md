# TOI Taste of India Restaurant

*Asia > Middleeast > Saudiarabia > Riyadh > Eating Out > Toi Taste Of India 1*

TOI Restaurant provides you hygienic and delicious Indian (North and South) food at reasonable rates. The settings are nice and clean, dishes range from delicious biryanis and tandoors to south Indian rice specialities. I make a fortnightly trip to Batha just to stroll around where the life is in Riyadh and in TOI restaurant, discovered a place to satisfy my culinary cravings too. Found some items like parotta, tandoor chicken, beef curry, biryani, mutton masala, fish masala as the best in town.

TOI is located in the Batha main street, near the second pedestrian cross - just behind the Mist multi-shop.
