# When to Go in Damascus

*Asia > Middleeast > Syria > Damascus > When To Go*

The best time to travel is important to know when you are planning a trip and you want to know **Damascus when to go** options so that you can choose the best time of year to get the most enjoyment on your vacation. You can also choose the best time to go based on your interests in some of the famous festivals that the city hosts each year.

#### Travel in the Months of March to May

Choosing to travel in the months of March to May allows you to enjoy the sun and warm climate with minimal rain and wet weather. You can avoid the excessive heat that you would see if you traveled June through August. You can also avoid the cold and wet seasons that are in December to February.

#### Travel in the Autumn

If you want to enjoy Damascus, "when to go tips" suggest that you may want to visit in the autumn months to enjoy the warm weather without having to have the excessive heat, and you can also avoid the wet months in the winter that will allow you to enjoy the places that you are going to see and to catch some of the best weather conditions that are found in Syria.

#### See the Damascus Film Festival

The annual film festival is held in Damascus every November and has been drawing large numbers of travelers from all over to come and enjoy the movies that are based upon the Arab countries, Latin America and Asia. This event is a great way to experience the culture of Damascus and take in some of the films that you may normally not be able to find.

#### Enjoy and Celebrate the Baalbek Festival

This festival is held in July and August and it is a festival with performers in music and dance from all over. The festival attracts many visitors to see the best of the cultural performances every summer, and is a great time to see what the music and dance is about in Damascus.

#### See the Array of Beauty in the Teshreen Park Flower Show

Anyone who enjoys the beauty of exotic and rare flowers is sure to enjoy the flower show that is held each year in July and August in Damascus. Exhibitors from Europe and the Middle East bring some of the most rare and beautiful flowers to the show and this event attracts tourists from all over to see the best flowers in the area of Damascus.
