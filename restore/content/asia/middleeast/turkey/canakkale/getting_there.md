# Getting There

*Asia > Middleeast > Turkey > Canakkale > Getting There*

Several bus companies have buses passing through Çanakkale. Buses run almost every hour of the day from Istanbul, 300 kms to the North of Çanakkale, to Izmir, 350 kms to the South of Çanakkale. They stop in Çanakkale en-route as they board the ferry to cross the Dardanelles here.

 Cost from Istanbul to Çanakkale is approx USD$ 12.

 Hassle Free Travel Agency runs a service from Istanbul to Çanakkale and then onto Selçuk the following day. Click here for more information.

 Fez Travel have a hop-on hop-off bus service which also stops overnight in Çanakkale Click here for more information.
