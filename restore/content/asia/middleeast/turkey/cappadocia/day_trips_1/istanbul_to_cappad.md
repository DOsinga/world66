# Istanbul to Cappadocia 3 Days Tour / By Air

*Asia > Middleeast > Turkey > Cappadocia > Day Trips 1 > Istanbul To Cappad*

Goreme - Uchisar - Avanos - Kaymakli - Pasabag - Ortahisar. (2 Nights / 3 days)

Tour Itinerary
Day 01: Istanbul - Cappadocia
Transfer from your hotel to the airport to take an early flight from Istanbul to Kayseri. Upon your arrival you will meet with your guide and begin the tour. On this full day tour you will visit; Devrent Valley, Zelve Open-air Museum, Monk's valley (Pasabagi) where you will find the world known fairy chimneys. Have lunch in Avanos, famous for pottery makers and continue on to the Goreme Open-Air Museum where you will visit the numerous churches and monasteries. Depart for a panoramic view of Esentepe and then on to Uchisar, a natural rock castle. After visits, drive to your hotel in Urgup to make your check in and stay overnight in Capadoccia. (Included meals: Breakfast & Lunch)

Day 02: Highlights of Capadoccia
After breakfast at the hotel, depart for the second part of your visit to the Cappadocia Region. We will hike through the Rose Valley visiting churches along the way. Afterwards, visit the old Christian village of Cavusin. Stop for lunch in Pigeons Valley and then continue on to see the marvellous construction of the Kaymakli Underground City. You will also visit the Ortahisar Natural Rock Castle. After the tour drive back to your hotel and stay overnight. (Included meals: Breakfast & Lunch)

Day 03: Cappadocia to Istanbul
After breakfast at the hotel you will be transferred to the airport for your return flight to Istanbul. Arrive at Istanbul airport. End of our services.(Included meals: Breakfast & Lunch)
