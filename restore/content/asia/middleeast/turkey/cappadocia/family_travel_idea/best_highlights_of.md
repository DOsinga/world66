**Full Itinerary**

**DAY 1: Ephesus and The House of Virgin Mary Tour**
We will pick you up from your hotel early in the morning and transfer you to the airport or bus terminal according to your request. Flight from Istanbul to Izmir takes approximately one and a half hours and bus trip takes approximately ten hours. A guide will meet you and transfer you to your hotel in Kusadasi. You will be picked up again around 9.30am and joined to the full day Ephesus Tour after your check-in. We will visit to the Temple of Artemis, which once was one of the Seven Wonders of the ancient world and then the world famous ancient Greco- Roman City of Ephesus, the most well preserved example in the world. After visiting these impressive sites, you will have a break for lunch. After lunch we will visit The House of Virgin Mary, where it is believed she spent her last days. This is a holy place for both Christians and Muslims. Afterwards you will visit Isabey Mosque and Saint John Basilica. The Ephesus tour ends around 5.00pm and after the tour, you will be transferred to your hotel in Kusadasi. Overnight in Kusadasi.

**DAY 2: Pamukkale and Hierapolis Tour**
Again early in the morning we will pick you up from the hotel and go to the meeting point in Selcuk and then we'll begin the Pamukkale Tour after three hours bus trip from Selcuk. Upon arrival in Pamukkale, the group will have a short break for lunch and after lunch we will begin your guided tour of the Pamukkale and Hierapolis with a professional and licensed tour guide. In Pamukkale, we will see the Cotton Cliffs, one of the natural wonders of the world formed by deposits of calcareous salts. Also you will visit the ancient ruins of the City of Hierapolis with its basilica, theatre and Roman Baths. At the end of the guided tour, you will have some free time to explore the area on your own. As an option, you may choose to swim in the Pamukkale antique pools (at own expense) during this free time. The tour guide will inform you about the exact meeting point and time. At the end of the tour, you will be transferred to the bus station for your overnight bus to Cappadocia. The overnight bus departs from Pamukkale to Cappadocia at around 9.00pm. You will have free time at the bus terminal and if you want you can have your dinner or get relax until the bus departure time. Your overnight bus to Cappadocia takes approximately ten hours and it is fully equipped, air-conditioned and non-smoking local bus. You will have three breaks on the way to Cappadocia for your personal needs. You will meet another guide upon arrival in the Goreme bus terminal and be transferred to you hotel in Cappadocia.

**DAY 3: Northern Cappadocia Tour**
After check-in to the hotel can have your breakfast and be ready for meet up with your tour guide at around 9:30am. The tour will start with a visit to the Devrent Valley to see the unique fairy chimneys. Next you will visit the Pasabag Monk’s Valley to see the mushroom-shaped pinnacles. After this visit, you will drive to the city of Avanos. After lunch you will visit the beautiful Open Air Museum of Goreme where the remains of rock-cut churches and Christian settlements date back hundreds of years. Finally towards the end of the day tour before returning to your hotel, you will be able to visit the natural castles of Uchisar. At the end of the tour you will be dropped back to your hotel in the evening around 5.00pm. Overnight in Cappadocia.

**DAY 4: Southern Cappadocia Tour**
You can have your breakfast and be ready at around 9:30am. Your guide will pick you up from the hotel, give a short brief about the tour and begin your full day tour of South Cappadocia. The tour will begin with a visit to the Red Valley where you will be able to explore the famous rock-cut churches and hike approximately 5 km through the Gulludere (Rose Valley). Next you will visit Cavusin village which is famous for its houses and churches of the Christian Clergymen. We will take a break for lunch around noon. After lunch you will drive to the Pigeon Valley. In the afternoon you will visit the Kaymakli underground city where early Christians lived. Finally towards the end of the tour before returning, we will visit the natural castles at Ortahisar. At the end of the tour, you will be dropped to the Kayseri or Nevsehir airport or bus terminal for your late evening flight or bus trip to Istanbul. Upon your arrival in Istanbul you will be picked up and transferred to your hotel. End of Tour.
