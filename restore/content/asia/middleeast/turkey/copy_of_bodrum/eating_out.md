**Ibo**

Serves excellent Turkish food, especially meze and is popular with the locals too. Try its hunkar beygendi, a dish of tender lamb served with a puréed aubergine and cheese sauce. 

address: Restaurant Alley

**Golden Plate Restaurant**

For international dishes, try the Golden Plate Restaurant near Halikarnas, Good for veggies, too.
