# Getting Around in Fethiye

*Asia > Middleeast > Turkey > Fethiye > Getting Around*

**Traveling by Bus**

You can get to Fethiye by bus. There are many buses so usually a
reservation is not required. The buses are very grand buses but do not have
toilets. The fare range is from TL30 to TL45 so the price is very reasonable. There
are frequent stops so you can see many different interesting spots along the
way. Getting around Fethiye is easy. One popular option is the mini-bus. The
mini-bus is the easiest and most efficient way to travel. You can go to anywhere
in the city. You just need to make sure the bus driver is going to the place you
want to go to. With gas prices being around 3 TL, the mini-buses offer a cheap
price. Of course, the price changes depending on your destination.

**Traveling by Car**

Fethiye can be accessed from any major highway so traveling by car is a
breeze. The cars can be rented at the airport before you leave or in Fethiye
city itself. Your hotel will allow you to rent a car directly through them or
you can even you can go to their office in the downtown area. They will even
deliver the car to your address or your hotel. If you need a pickup for the
airport they will also offer this service. The cars, of course, change in price
according to style but the range is basically from 35 Euros to 90 Euros. The
time you will be traveling to Fethiye also makes the price higher or lower.
