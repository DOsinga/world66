# Gallipoli Tours

*Asia > Middleeast > Turkey > Gallipoli > Day Trips > Gallipoli Tours*

Gallipoli & Troy 2 days 1 Night
Day 1:
Early morning pick up from hotel and drive to Canakkale. We will visit WWI Battlefields of Gallipoli including Lone Pine and Chunuk Bair memorials, Anzac Cove, the Nek, Johnston's Jolly and the Kabatepe Gallipoli Museum. Stay the night at Canakkale.
Day 2:
DAfter breakfast drive to Ancient City Of Troy, visit Troy Ruins and The Trojan Horse, afternoon drive back Istanbul or Selcuk/Kusadasi.
