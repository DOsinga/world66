# By Sea

*Asia > Middleeast > Turkey > Gettingthere > By Sea*

**Passenger Ferries: **Apart from numerous cruises in
the Mediterranean, several foreign shipping companies have regular
services to the ports of Trabzon, Samsun, Istanbul, Dikili, Izmir,
Cesme, Kusadasi, Bodrum, Marmaris, Antalya, Alanya, Mersin and
Iskenderun.

**Car Ferries: **There are several car ferries for
tourists who wish to bring their cars while sightseeing: Connections
run from Venice, Ancona, Brindisi and Bari to Istanbul, Izmir, Cesme,
Kusadasi, Marmaris and Antalya.
