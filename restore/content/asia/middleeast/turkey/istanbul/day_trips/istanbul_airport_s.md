# Istanbul Airport Transfers

*Asia > Middleeast > Turkey > Istanbul > Day Trips > Istanbul Airport S*

**Istanbul Transfers Airport - Hotel - Pier**

Are you taking a cruise from Istanbul,Turkey? Maybe you''d prefer a private transfer from the airport or your hotel. For our clients who book any private tour, we are also happy to arrange private transfer from Istanbul''s airport into central Istanbul or the pier. The prices below are based on the total number of passengers (including children) and the maximum amount of bags (luggage). Small hand bags such as purses or backpacks which can be held during the transfer are not included in the maximum pieces of luggage. Included in the rate is an English speaking host to meet you inside the airport.

**Meet and Greet-** It is important to note that these rates include an English Speaking meet and greet service. This person will meet you inside the airport with a sign with your name on it and then walk you to your waiting vehicle. The service is offered because drivers cannot leave vehicles unattended to meet you.
