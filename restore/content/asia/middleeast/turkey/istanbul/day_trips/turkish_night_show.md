# Turkish Night Show

*Asia > Middleeast > Turkey > Istanbul > Day Trips > Turkish Night Show*

**Turkish night - Dinner folk and belly dance show**
( Traditional music - dance evening - Istanbul)
**Itinerary**
you will be picked up from your hotel and will transfer you to the showand at end of the show we will transfer you back to your hotel. Midnight is the time for ending.

* Live music

* Salads
* 9 different kind of mezees on the ordovr plate.
* 2 type of hot Appetizers
* 3 different kind, Optional main course ( beef, Chicken, Fish ) Vegetarian dishes are available
* Seasonal fruits
* First local drinks(vodka, gin, raki, beer, soft drinks )
