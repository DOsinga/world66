# Ciragan Palace

*Asia > Middleeast > Turkey > Istanbul > Eatingout > Ciraganpalace*

The Ciragan Palace is great. It is not only a former Sultan's palace it is also one of the best restaurants in Istanbul. They offer traditional Ottoman food that you hardly will find somewhere else like that. Historical receipts and surrounding: What do you want more!? Attention: it is really not one of the cheapest on that list!
