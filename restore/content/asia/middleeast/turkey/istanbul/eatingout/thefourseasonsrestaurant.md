# THE FOUR SEASONS RESTAURANT

*Asia > Middleeast > Turkey > Istanbul > Eatingout > Thefourseasonsrestaurant*

Beyoglu: There's a Turkish saying about the weather in Istanbul that goes something like 'four seasons in one day'. No matter what the weather is like you should treat yourself to a true culinary experience at the Four Seasons Restaurant one day soon! Located on Istiklal just a stone's throw away from the Tünel tram station, this prestigious restaurant has been serving some of the most delectable European and Turkish dishes this side of heaven to discerning locals, international diplomats, journalists and business people for almost 25 years. Rare is the restaurant that survives for this long, let alone with the founder and original management still sane!

  The Four Seasons Restaurant is like a precious gem - it keeps gaining in value as the years pass. Although such refined splendour might be a bit expensive for frequent romantic dinners for two, it is amazingly affordable if you try their fixed-menu lunch, available weekdays from noon to 3:00. After you've been seated in the impeccably designed dining room, have perused the extensive menu of truly continental fare and have opted for the three-course lunch special, you will pleasantly surprised by the quantity of choice they make available to you. For example, some recent appetisers have included Smoked Norwegian Salmon, Garlic Mushrooms, Breaded Calamari, and a wonderful Tomato and Herb Soup.

  The stylishly presented main courses are like works of art and include Mushroom and Spinach Crepes, Pesto Linguini, Köfte Bordelaise, and Lemon Chicken. The offerings change almost every day and you're sure to find something excitingly different to taste. As if you don't feel pampered enough by now, wait until you are presented with tableside coffee service! Everything is flawlessly served by the expert waiting staff and your eyes will roll with delight when they tempt you with selections from the dessert cart. The pleasant English owner and the cordial staff have painstakingly created a touch of Europe right here in Istanbul. After dining like royalty you will feel just like you've taken a short holiday to almost any cosmopolitan city you can name with world class restaurants you can't afford. Well now you can!
