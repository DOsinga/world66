# Sultan Ahmet Camii

*Asia > Middleeast > Turkey > Istanbul > Sights > Sultanahmetcamii*

The Sultan Ahmet Cami (or the Blue Mosques - a name that it does not deserves in my opinion) is impressive in first place because of its size and the elegant composed outside fronts and surrounding. It is from inside less impressive, some say it is a bit disappointing. Nevertheless it is a must and Sultan Ahmet is with its six minarets the biggest mosques of Istanbul. The complex was erected in only nine years under the reign of Sultan Ahmet I (finished in 1616). The name the Blue Mosques was given because of the 21.000 Iznik tiles inside the mosque.
