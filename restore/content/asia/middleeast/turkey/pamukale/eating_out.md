# Eating Out in Pamukale

*Asia > Middleeast > Turkey > Pamukale > Eating Out*

**Delicious Restaurant in the Center of Pamukale
**

When trying to decide which of all the **Pamukale Restaurants** to visit, consider dining at the KayaÅ Wine House on Attat&uuml;rk Street, right in the center of Pamukale. This is one delicious restaurant and an excellent spot for lunch or dinner. Prices are very affordable, with main dishes ranging from $4 to $10 and most expensive drinks no more than $14.

**The Atmosphere
**

The restaurant has a beautiful green garden with many tables situated right under leafy trees. The nice wooden fence is low enough to give you a pleasant view to the streets outside. In addition the fence is decorated with beautiful sculptures. Entering inside the restaurant you will see more tables and a nice little bar. All the walls and the floor are decorated with lovely oriental-themed carpets. There is an excellent Wi-Fi connection throughout the whole place and a huge TV screen that broadcasts the best sport events.**
**

**The Menu
**

KayaÅ Wine House offers an extensive menu of both traditional local and international cuisine. The menu is translated into English, so you can easily choose something from it and place your order. The Turkish meals are served on a special metal hotplate with a flame burning underneath. You might want to try the Mixed Grill – an excellent combination of meat, rice and vegetables. You should also taste one of the incredible cocktails and consider smoking some nargileh if you are a smoker. At KayaÅ you can drink some of the finest wines or order freezing-cold beer.
