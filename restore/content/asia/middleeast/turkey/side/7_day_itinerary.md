# 7 Day Itinerary in Side

*Asia > Middleeast > Turkey > Side > 7 Day Itinerary*

Having a **Side itinerary** will make your trip go great. Side is
a peninsula on the Mediterranean coast of Turkey, surrounded by
beautiful beaches and great views. From beach time fun in the sun to
beautiful walks through the heart of Side, your entire trip will
definitely be a memorable experience.

**Day 1: Arrive and Relax **

The most difficult part of any vacation is remembering to escape
from the real world in order to relax. On your first day in Side treat
yourself to a day of blissful laziness. Take a walk on the beach, read
a book in your hotel lobby, or even sit in your hotel room and watch a
movie.

**Day 2: Stroll through Side**

Enjoy an English Breakfast at the Aquarius Restaurant and then
stroll through Side to catch a glimpse of one of the many historic
ruins, like the waterside Temples of Athena and Apollo or the Roman
Amphitheater. Then, pop into a few of the various shops scattered
throughout the town. For lunch, pack a meal and head toward the
waterfalls on the Manavgat River. Have a nice dinner at your hotel.

**Day 3: Beach Fun**

Side is surrounded by beautiful beaches. If you are feeling
adventurous, take part in one of many different water activities,
including water skiing, sailing, or parasailing. For a more quiet day,
walk down the beach until you get away from the crowd, find a great
spot to sit, and enjoy the hot sun and coastal breeze.

**Day 4: Play Tourist
**

If you're getting tired of walking, you can catch a ride with a
street car, bus or taxi to the Side Museum. This museum was a former
Roman Bath which has now been transformed to display a collection of
archeological artifacts. For dinner, eat at Orfoz right at sunset; that
is when the view of the western beach is at its peak.

**Day 5: Day Trip
**

Take the bus to Antalya for an amazing glimpse of the the country's
hottest tourist destination. Visit one of the many museums, eat lunch
at any number of restaurants, and then have a cocktail while watching
people walk by.

**Day 6: Golf Your Stress Away**

You can't leave Side without experiencing its great golf course.
Wake-up early and stay on the greens until late. Then enjoy Turkish
cuisine at a small local restaurant.
**
Day 7: Pack, Relax and Depart**

Your trip has come to an end, but before you go make sure you have
someone take one last picture of you in this beautiful Turkish city.
