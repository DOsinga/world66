# Eating Out in Side

*Asia > Middleeast > Turkey > Side > Eating Out*

Side is an ancient Greek city in Turkey. It is located in the region
of Anatolia and the province of Antalya. **Side restaurants** provide
visitors with a first hand experience of its unique cuisine. It is also
known for its resorts and historical sites. If you are visiting Side,
stopping by at some of its fine restaurants to enjoy Turkish cuisine is
highly recommended.

**Aphrodite Restaurant**

Aphrodite Restaurant is one of the popular restaurants in the city.
The restaurant presents a wide variety of Turkish as well as
international cuisines. The restaurant is ideally located near the
harbor and presents a stunning view. Normally the restaurant is crowded,
so it would be advisable to check before going there for a meal. The
restaurant also provides free pick-up service from your hotel and is
reasonably priced.

**Sound Waves Restaurant **** **

Sound Waves Restaurant is another restaurant that is popular for
their fine cuisine and welcoming ambiance. With its vast menu and highly
professional service, this is a place you would like to enjoy a meal
with your family. While at the restaurant ask for the chef’s special.

**Gul Restaurant**

Gul is located in the old town of Side and is open during the summer
months. Though it is a bit difficult to spot, you can ask anyone in the
old town for directions and you can easily get to the restaurant. The
menu at this restaurant varies from day to day. The restaurant also
offers complimentary starters and tea or coffee at the end of your meal.
This restaurant is one not to be missed while visiting Side.

**Moonlight Restaurant**

Moonlight Restaurant is a seafood restaurant in Side; however, there
are also other non-seafood dishes that are available. If you are looking
for a place to enjoy a sumptuous dinner over some fine Turkish wine,
this is the place. The restaurant is located in the eastern side of the
city overlooking the seafront of Side. The restaurant is also a great
place to enjoy a romantic dinner with your loved one.

**Zumrut Restaurant**

Delicious food and great service has made this restaurant stand out
as one of the best restaurants in Side. Step into this fine restaurant
and be treated to a sumptuous meal, after the meal the restaurant will
serve you with complimentary long pita bread with garlic butter and
fresh food. The restaurant is located in the old harbor area of Side.
