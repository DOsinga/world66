# Getting Around in Side

*Asia > Middleeast > Turkey > Side > Getting Around*

**Traveling by Car**

Side, Turkey is a beautiful city where people go to see the wonders of
nature. With waterfalls in the mountain region as well as clubs and sea sports
along the ocean you will have a great time. You can hire a car while in Side to
get to your destination. A car can take you to the monuments that are lit up at
night. It will also allow you to get to the beach for the afternoon swim. Prices
for renting a car in Side, Turkey range from $152 to $219 for a 3-day
period depending on the type of car you are looking for. The cars range in size
from economy cars to compact cars. You can get them in manual or automatic
modes.

** Traveling by Bus or Walking**

Many places are easy to walk to from most hotels in Side, Turkey. If however
you need a different form of transportation be assured there is another option.
There are free buses that travel from most hotels to the local beaches. If you
want to see more of Side there is a bus that will also take you around the
country. It costs only 50P which is about $31. This bus will take you to other
cities as well. If you need to go even further there is a bus terminal that will
carry you to every part of Turkey. The price differs depending on where you want
to travel too. It is a dependable way to travel.
