# Nightlife and Entertainment in Side

*Asia > Middleeast > Turkey > Side > Nightlife And Ente*

This article covers** Side Nightlife and Entertainment** in an in-depth and thorough manner. The scene at Side couldn't be more exciting, chock full as it is with excellent bars and nightclubs whose variety is immense. After a long relaxing day on the beach or touring the historical monuments, you can unwind in real style in Side, whatever your preference or budget. Whatever kind of night out you want, Side can cater for it.

**Oxyd Disco is the Place to Party ** Arguably the most popular night spot in this increasingly popular resort, Oxyd Disco is jam-packed in high season with thousands of revelers living it large. There is no dress code, so you can relax, but it is advisable to look a little bit stylish. Guest DJs from Turkey and other countries regularly play banging sets of R&B and Turkish dance music here. With 2 floors and an open-air pool area, guests can wear swimming suits under their clubbing clothes if they fancy a dip during the music.

**Other Notable Clubs and Bars ** Korner Bar and Restaurant has great atmosphere, although it is a little more relaxed. The DJ there is renowned to have an astonishing knowledge of music. The Lighthouse is an open-air raver's favorite, is located in the old quarter of the town, and is open until the wee hours. The big hotels in Side such as Hotel Asteria and Sertkaya Hotel also feature great bars serving Chinese, Italian and Turkish food until very late.

**Sports Bars ** Both Turks and visitors often enjoy watching sport in bars in the evening. The best places to do that in Side are Bistro 2000 and the Golden Fizz, both of which show English Premier League and other games. Bistro 2000 also has the advantage of being a first-rate restaurant offering local and international cuisine, European-style breakfasts and great drinks promotions.

**Beach Nightlife** There are plenty of places to go in the evening that are situated on the golden-sand beaches of Side. Hukukcular serves cheap beer and excellent cheap food, including garlic-buttered fresh fish pita bread, a perfect snack for an evening of drinking. If you want a more chilled-out alternative you can go for a walk along the beach or even through some of Side's many historic ruins at night.
