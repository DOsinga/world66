# Bars and Cafes in Abu dhabi

*Asia > Middleeast > Unitedarabemirates > Abudhabi > Bars And Cafes*

The **Abu Dhabi Bars and Cafes** make the city a hot party spot. The bars, pubs and cafes are spread out at all key areas in the city. It is a great infusion of elements from around the world. Even if you are single or a woman traveler, there is nothing to worry as the bars and other locations are extremely tourist friendly and safe.

**
Tequilana Discotheque**

From Salsa to Karaoke, this discotheque located in the Hilton International hotel in Abu Dhabi, offers a treat to the party lovers. The DJ keeps the dance floor sizzling with the electrifying music. The theme is that of a cool shack on a beach, and the food served here will keep you wanting for more. It is a highly popular joint among locals and you find most of the high flyer's visiting this joint.

**
Zenith
**

The shining jewel in Sheraton Resorts crown, Zenith is a high intensity, high energy club. The Fusion night is set ablaze by the in house DJ's and offers unlimited drinks for the ladies. Zenith also offers free wine and champagne during its “Bubbly” nights. Dance, drink and party till you drop.

**
Le Gauloises

**
Exotic cocktails, scrumptious finger food, live music and entertainment, has made this the most sought after night club in Abu Dhabi. Located in Le Meridien, this elite club is highly classy and attracts the crème de la crème of the society. All items on the food menu are unique and the bar boasts of the most expensive drinks in the world.

**AllyPally Corner
**

Situated in the most accessible tourist sport on the Corniche, this is a true English style Pub. Enjoy the European menu with Jazz and Blues live music playing in the background. The ambiance is done up in a way to exude warmth, with dart board and a pool table.

**The Chequers
**

The best wine bar in the whole of Abu Dhabi, The Chequers offers the guests a unique experience. Right from its back and white décor to the impeccably laid out European menu, there is no reason to be disappointed here. Make sure you get in as soon as the door opens, as this place has a reputation of getting full in record time. The youth in Abu Dhabi swear by this bar.
