# Getting Around in Abu dhabi

*Asia > Middleeast > Unitedarabemirates > Abudhabi > Getting Around*

As one of the richest and most modern cities in the world, **Abu Dhabi transportation **is just as wonderful as its facilities and infrastructure. No matter where you want to go, you can find appropriate transportation that is reasonably priced, comfortable and easy to use, as well.

**Taxis **

The white and gold taxis are the primary means for local transport in this emirate, especially if you do not have your own car. Hiring a taxi can take you anywhere within the emirate or even from here to another emirate. You would also have to use another taxi for traveling back to Abu Dhabi. Taxis in this emirate charge fare according to the price that has been issued by the emirate. While this is great, most taxi drivers can’t speak English, so communication can be a problem at times.

**Bus
**
If you are looking for a cheaper means of transportation to get around this Abu Dhabi, you can try the buses. Most of the buses here are air conditioned and also have separate seating areas for men and women. Traveling around Abu Dhabi only takes a few dirhams, which is quite reasonable. You can also find public bus transportation services between Abu Dhabi and other emirates. These are frequent and cost between Dhs.15 to Dhs.30, depending on where you are traveling.

**Car
**
You can also find car hire services to get around Abu Dhabi. However, this can be expensive, particularly if you plan to do a lot of sightseeing. Car hire is the most popular means of transportation in this emirate as it is hot all throughout the year.
