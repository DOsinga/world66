# Beaches in Fujairah

*Asia > Middleeast > Unitedarabemirates > Fujairah > Beaches*

Fujairah is known throughout the United Arab Emirates as a prime location for a business trip or conference. Small and secluded, Fujairah isn't known as a relaxing and exciting vacation spot, but this town is nonetheless home to a variety of impressive things to do for everyone. Fjuairah beaches are some of the little known highlights of this city. The majority of these beaches are only accessible if you're a guest at one of Fujairah's many hotels and resorts, so keep that in mind when booking a place to stay. When packing for an afternoon out on the beach, remember to bring a high SPF sunscreen with you and no alcohol, as public drinking is illegal anywhere in the United Arab Emirates.

**Le Meridian Al Aqah Beach Resort

**The beach off the Le Meridian Al Aqah Beach Resort is not one you will want to pass on. Whether taking a dip in the clear, warm waters off or relaxing under a beach umbrella, the views from this beach are spectacular. Not only allowing guests to partake in a peaceful afternoon, the Le Meridian Al Aqah Beach Resort also offers a variety of activities such as scuba diving and fly fishing for an additional fee.

**Sandy Beach Resort

**With a clean beach directly on the Indian Ocean, the beach at the Sandy Beach Resort is a perfect location to spend a quiet afternoon sipping a cocktail while lounging in a beach chair. This beach has a perfect view of Snoopy Island which is located just off the coast line. The waters between the beach and Snoopy Island are rich with marine life making it a perfect location for snorkeling and diving. Home to the Sandy Beach Diving Center, this beach makes it easy to experience the underwater marvels the Indian Ocean has to offer.

**Royal Beach

**The Royal Beach Hotel and Resort offers a large beach that is open to the public. Offering over 240 meters of oceanfront beach, the Royal Beach also is home to a professional dive center, giving guests another chance to view the spectacular marine life and a chance to dive and view a number of shipwrecks. Dives are safe and supervised and offer an unique and exciting experience. If you're a guest at this resort, you will also be able to use one of the 2 spacious pools with amazing sea views.
