# Eating Out in Fujairah

*Asia > Middleeast > Unitedarabemirates > Fujairah > Eating Out*

Fujairah is just the place you want to travel to in order to partake in the best of Arabic and Indian cuisine. No matter where you go in the city while you are exploring all the interesting places you will find a place to eat. **Restaurants** range in size from the large dinning rooms in the fancy hotels to more quiet cafes and vendors amid business buildings. The night time souk market extravaganza has eateries located throughout the selling booths. If traditional food doesn’t please you that’s okay. Fujairah even has western fast food restaurants for you. One of the very special treats of eating at Fujairahian restaurants is that the service provided by the waiters is so professional. It makes the trip that more extraordinary to vacation here.

**City Restaurants

**Indian cuisines served at the restaurants are to die for. The most well known of these are; **The Saferon Restaurant, Delhi Darbar** **Restaurant **and the **Al Mashreq Restaurant**. If you are piqued into tasting Arabic food the **Gulf Kitchen** is going to be a delightful experience. Chinese food can be found but it is rare. So then you will want to go to the **Lulu Restaurant**, where they serve the best Chinese in Fujairah.

You may want to consider stopping at a small café while you are shopping just to become a little more familiar with the local culture's delicacies. The cooks in these small restaurants are able to prepare incredible dishes using the spices this country is so well recognized for.
**

Hotel Restaurants

**Along the beach front are some very elaborate hotels with several restaurants in each one of them. Here are just a few of them to give you an idea of what choices you have. The Hilton hotel has 2 restaurants that are highly regarded.** ****Neputunia** offers you dinners that are themed-based, focusing on Mediterranean dishes or some of the local cuisine. **The Sailors** **Restaurant** is located right at the beachfront just yards from the Indian Ocean. Its main attraction is the excellent way the chefs prepare local seafood dishes. The service there is top rated. At the Rotana Resort and Spa you can chose from many restaurants, each having its own style and atmosphere. A seaside view from a seat in the **Mozaiques Restaurant** gives you the opportunity to choose from its international menus. A special attraction here on Wednesday, Thursday and Friday nights are theme menus from various countries.
