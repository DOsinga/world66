# Hadramawt

*Asia > Middleeast > Yemen > Hadramawt*

Hadramawt is a mountainous region home to nomadic tribes. This vast and scenic area stretches 350 mi/565 km from the Arabian Sea to the Saudi border. The area may be far away and hard to get to, but it has some stunning scenery and one of the most fantastic sights in the world: [Shibam](/asia/middleeast/yemen/shibam)
