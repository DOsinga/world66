# Nightlife and Entertainment in Asia

*Asia > Nightlife And Ente*

The **Ipoh nightlife **is fast gaining popularity for its bars, clubs, restaurants and other activities. Back in the 1930's, it was a bustling place for its tin trade, but has turned as a quiet city in the midst of a valley as the tin mining has ceased. Ipoh is a favorite destination for many Malays and foreigners. Getting there by bus or by train to experience the many surprises of Ipoh is definitely a worthy trip.

**Collinas**

Collinas is a sports bar located in Ipoh. If you are looking for a place where you can chill out and enjoy yourself while watching a game, this is the place to be. The bar has a nice atmosphere and is a great place to make new friends. The bar serves a variety of food and drinks. You will surely enjoy yourself at this bar.

**Rum Jungle**

Rum Jungle is the newest happening place at Ipoh that comes alive at night. It is located at Sunway, just 15 minutes away from Ipoh Town. The place features live band performances and is a great place where you can enjoy the night. The place is usually packed with party goers and is three times bigger than the one in Kuala Lumpur. It has a creative design in that you will feel like partying in a jungle. However, you must be aware that this is an open air pub – a very large pub!

**Lan Kwai Fong Karaoke Fun Pub**

Spend time with your friends singing your hearts out at the Lan Kwai Fong Karaoke Fun Pub. It has the best karaoke audio, visual and lighting system. An added bonus at the pub is the games section, where you can play games like darts, ping-pong and billiards. It is located at the basement of Hotel Excelsior.

**Mike's Place**

This is a pub overlooking the lake and is a great place to enjoy the night partying. The pub serves food and drinks 5:00 p.m. to 1:00 a.m. It also has a live band performing – a band that will keep you on your feet with their wonderful music. If you are looking for a place to dance and party, head over to Mike&rsquo;s Place.

**Stairway Pub and Bistro**

This pub and bistro is one of the best places where you can enjoy a karaoke night. The pub has state of the art karaoke equipment and is popular among several locals in Ipoh. The pub also has a variety of food and drinks to choose from.
