# Dalian International Fashionable Dress Festival

*Asia > Northeastasia > China > Dalian > Festivals > Dalianinternationalfashionabledressfestival*

Started in 1989 and celebrate in September every year. The event includes dress, culture and tour. During the festival, more than ten thousands people of the world join this great event. It becomes one of the most important fashionable dress festivals in the world.
