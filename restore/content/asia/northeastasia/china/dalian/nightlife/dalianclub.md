# Club Spark

*Asia > Northeastasia > China > Dalian > Nightlife > Dalianclub*

The most popular hiphop club in dalian city. Clean atmosphere and very nice music. The djs play all kinds of hiphop, from old school to new school. They have 5 foreign djs. dj cowboy (philippines) dj yas (japan) dj ken (japan) dj oscar (spain) and dj joon (korea).. Club Spark is the perfect place to dance, drink and meet new friends.
