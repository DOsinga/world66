# Zhongshan Guangchang (Sun Yatsen Square)

*Asia > Northeastasia > China > Dalian > Sights > Zhongshanguangchangsunyatsensquare*

The center of entertainment and communication for Dalian's citizen, a panorama of grand buildings encircling a roundabout. In the middle of this circular plaza, thousands of couples practice western dance with music on every summer night. Others play badminton, or just sit and chat, while children play with dogs. And the pick of the city's classical architecture can be found here.
