# When to Go in Dalian

*Asia > Northeastasia > China > Dalian > When To Go*

Dalian, a popular Chinese tourist
destination, embodies the exotic oriental charm of the east along with the
cutting-edge technology and infrastructure of the west. This When To Go guide offers visitors
information on the best time to visit this East Asian hotspot and catch Dalian
at its best.**

Description**

Analyzing the best time to visit
Dalian can be a daunting task. The fascinating city is akin to one roaring
party that refuses die down in spirit
and is chock-a-block with activities and events throughout the year to keep its
revelers enthralled. Dalian has the good fortune of being located in the
northern hemisphere and is blessed with a pleasant weather that on the average
pegs at 10-5 Centigrade. Even in its peak winters, Dalian never sees a
temperature of less than -4 degree
Centigrade and is a delightful blob of sunshine for over 2,500 hours on an
average each year.**

Activities**

For swimming and thrilling water
sports that are quiet a crowd-puller in the region, the best time to visit is
in the winters (December-February).
Swimming buffs will have a whale of a time during the Winter Swimming Festival
that attracts hundreds of swimmers from all over the world who compete in one
of the most exhilarating environments for a highly revered title. Another
strategic time to visit Dalian is during the Lunar New Year or Chinese New Year
revelry. The streets of the city transform
into a dazzling mass of light, energy and vivacity and the celebration sees
some of the most opulent displays of fireworks and road-side carnivals.
Visitors can also gorge on authentic and tastefully whipped up oriental
delicacies during the new year celebrations. The entire town has a super
line-up of entertainment shows and festivities that go on till the wee hours of
morning. **

Fall to Winter**

Haute-Couture aficionados can plan
their trip in September when the Dalian International Fashion Festival is held.
The event sees the crème de la crème of the international fashion circuit grace
it with their most high-end and impressive collection.

Christmas is another great time
for the city to show its eclectic festival spirit. The town looks resplendent
in the glow of Christmas trees ornamenting it on every street. There are innumerable events and
parties that light up the festivity party night scene in Dalian.
