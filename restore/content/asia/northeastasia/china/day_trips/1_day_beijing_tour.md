# 1 Day Beijing tour to Simatai Great Wall & Jinshanling Great Wall Tour

*Asia > Northeastasia > China > Day Trips > 1 Day Beijing Tour*

1 At 6:00am pick you up from your hotel in downtown Beijing. **We offer simple box-breakfast: sandwich, water and some fruits.**

2
Then We will drive to Jinshanling Great Wall. It takes about 2 hour to
get a station. There you can have a short rest and buy some food like
biscuit, bread and so on ( you may feel hungry during hiking from
Jinshanling Great Wall to Simatai Great Wall which takes 4 hours). And
then go on driving to Jinshanling Great Wall (1.5 hour).

Jinshanling
Great Wall lies within Loaning county, Hebei province, 130 km from
Beijing. It used to be under the jurisdiction of Gubeilu, an important
doorway in the northeast defense line of the Ming Dynasty. It´s very
precipitous, so you´d better wear a pair of non-slip shoes.

To
the east of the Jinshanling Great Wall there lays the quiet and remote
Simatai Great Wall. Hiking from Jinshanling Great Wall for 2 hours, you
will get to Simatai Great Wall. The hike is about 12 KM and takes about
4-5 hours. Get down the hill at the foot of Simatai at about 3:30pm.
The ruinous state of this part of the Wall gives it an air of
authenticity that has earned it the reputation of being the most
beautiful section of the Great Wall. Unlike the sections to be seen at
Badaling Great Wall or Mutianyu Great Wall that have undergone
extensive restoration, the wall at Simatai Great Wall has received very
little attention. Here, the wall really looks as one would expect it to
look some 500 years after it was built during the Ming Dynasty. It will
take you 2 hours get down from Simatai Great Wall.

3 Have traditional buffet Chinese lunch at a local restaurant on the foot of Simatai Great Wall.

4 After lunch, transferred back to your downtown hotel in Beijing.
