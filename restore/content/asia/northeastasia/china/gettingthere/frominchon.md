# From Inchon

*Asia > Northeastasia > China > Gettingthere > Frominchon*

From [Inchon](/asia/northeastasia/southkorea/inchon) in South Korea there are passenger ships to Weihai and Tianjin. For Weihai they leave in the afternoon and arrive in the morning - twice a week. The service to Tianjin takes more than 24 hours and leaves every five days or so.
