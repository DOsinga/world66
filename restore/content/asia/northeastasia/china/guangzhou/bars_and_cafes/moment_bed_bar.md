# Moment Bed Bar

*Asia > Northeastasia > China > Guangzhou > Bars And Cafes > Moment Bed Bar*

This is a brand new high class bar just south of the river. Well designed and with a relaxing atmosphere, the 'bed bar' is quite comfortable if a little unusual. The bar also runs a full restaurant of western food, much of which is quite authentic in ingredients and taste. Nightly bands and frequent events make this a bar fast growing in popularity with the high-end.
