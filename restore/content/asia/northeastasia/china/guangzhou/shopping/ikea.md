# IKEA

*Asia > Northeastasia > China > Guangzhou > Shopping > Ikea*

For tourists, IKEA is more like an attraction than a shopping destination. Obviously you don't want to buy furnitures here to bring them home. But it may be fasinating to look at, comparing to your IKEA at home.

If you miss bad American food, you can get 6-yuan hot dog with drink; 1-yuan ice cream, and 8-yuan chicken salad.

If you crave Swedish snacks, grab some here for the plane.
