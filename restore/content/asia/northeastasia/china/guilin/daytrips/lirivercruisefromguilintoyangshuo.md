# Li River cruise from Guilin to Yangshuo

*Asia > Northeastasia > China > Guilin > Daytrips > Lirivercruisefromguilintoyangshuo*

Boats usually depart from Guilin docks on Binjiang Lu, though if the water level of the river is low, buses will take you 40 minutes downstream. The cruise lasts about four hours, with lunch served on board, and to get back to Guilin involves a two-hour bus ride. Prices start at around 500 yuan (including lunch and return bus).
