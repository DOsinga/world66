*Asia > Northeastasia > China > Guilin > Daytrips > Moonhill*

10km west of Yangshuo. A forty-five minute ascent on steps up this distinctively shaped hill is rewarded by fabulous views of surrounding peaks and paddy fields. (Entry fee: 3 yuan)
