# Fairy Singer San Jie Liu Show

*Asia > Northeastasia > China > Guilin > Sights > Fairy Singer San J*

Guilin is not only renowned for its beautiful landscapes; it also has rich ethnic cultures since 28 ethnic groups have inhabited in Guilin for long, such as Zhuang, Dong, Miao and Yao People. Pure ethnic flavor, unique dance and song culture of them have formed a colorful culture picture.

The folk song of Zhuang minority is especially popular and is reflected in the highly-acclaimed operatic performance : Liu San Jie. Originally based on famous film set in the mountains and rivers of Guilin Liu San Jie is the story of the three sisters, the main character of the movie, was played by Ms. Huang Wanqiu has gone on represent the artistic flair and talent of Guangxi and have subsequently become a household name.
