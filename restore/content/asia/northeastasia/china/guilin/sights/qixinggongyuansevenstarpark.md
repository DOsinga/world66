# Qixing Gongyuan (Seven Star Park)

*Asia > Northeastasia > China > Guilin > Sights > Qixinggongyuansevenstarpark*

Just east of central Guilin. The reason for coming to Guilin is to see the karsts, formed some 200 million years ago when sea submerged the entire region. Several hills within the boundaries of the city itself offer excellent views of the surrounding area. Entry fee: 35 yuan
