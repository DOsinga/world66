# The Big Banyan Tree in Yangshuo

*Asia > Northeastasia > China > Guilin > Sights > The Big Banyan Tre*

Located by the Jinbao River in the Moon Hill scenic area in Gaotian Township, Yangshuo County, the big banyan tree measures 17 metres high, 7.05 metres in diameter. Its round crown covers more than 100 square metres. With twisted roots and gnarled branches, the tree boasts a history of 1400 years. The surrounding countryside along the Jinbao River also offers a spectacular view. The Big Banyan had become famous after a scene of the movie Liu Sanjie was shot there. During the scene folk song singe] Liu Sanjie expresses affection to her lover by throwing an embroidered ball to him.
