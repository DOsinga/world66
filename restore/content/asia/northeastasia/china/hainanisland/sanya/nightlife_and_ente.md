# Nightlife and Entertainment in Sanya

*Asia > Northeastasia > China > Hainanisland > Sanya > Nightlife And Ente*

As with all seaside
holiday cities, **Sanya nightlife and entertainment** has some excellent
offerings. Due to its tropical climate and year round sunny weather, many
locals wait until the late evening before going out to party. Getting out at
night is therefore one of the best ways to meet the local people. Sanya has a
wealth of options for visitors in the evenings including theatre, relaxing cafes
and exclusive nightclubs. Here are a few of the highlights:

**Zuoan Coffee and
Bar**

Situated on the left
side of Sanya Bay near the port, Zuoan Coffee and Bar
is a cool and relaxing place to hang out at night. The relaxing atmosphere is
complimented by old French music and the bar features decorations depicting the
local Hainan culture, including handcrafted
ornaments and embroideries displayed on the walls. Zuoan offers a varied menu
that includes both local and international food. Pizza is the specialty, with 16 different
homemade varieties on offer. Zuoan also has a huge coffee menu and wine list.

**Sanya Universal
Theatre**

Being the only
theatre on the whole of Hainan Island, Sanya Universal Theatre is therefore
extremely popular and sometimes it may even be sold out, so you need to book
tickets beforehand in order to guarantee a good seat. The theatre performs many
local plays and dances, including the well known poetic song and dance show
Luhuitou and Dadase,
which has won many awards in China
and is acclaimed for its special effects.

**Times Coast
Street Bar**

The Times Coast
Street Bar is actually a collection of several bars, all integrated into one
building, a huge open square piazza. All the bars are decorated in tropical
beach style with bamboo tables and chairs, hammocks and big cushions for the
patrons to relax in. This area is popular with a young, local Chinese crowd and
foreigners alike. Some of the most popular bars here are the well known Allen
Club, Noah’s Ark Bar and the Bujianbusan Bar.

**Be There or Be
Square Bar**

Be There or Be
Square Bar is one of the most popular places in Sanya to go for live music. The
bar is located next to the river on Hexi road, which is very central and
therefore easy to find. Be There or Be Square actually features two bars. On
the ground floor, the ‘Showing Bar’ features live music from local bands that
usually perform covers of American rock bands. Underground, the ‘Slow Bar’ is
more of a chill out room. Much quieter than upstairs and with soft lighting,
this is a place where patrons can sit in comfort and chat, watch the football
or play some rounds of pool.

**Lidu Nightclub **

Lidu is probably
unique among clubs in Sanya in that it remains extremely popular year after
year due to its exclusive status. Located in the basement of the 5 star Sanya
Maintint Hotel, Lidu is a big draw for the many rich ‘new Chinese’ including
both locals and tourists from the mainland. Lido
is a very hip place with a young crowd and cool vibe. Lidu features some top
European and Chinese DJ’s playing trance, techno and hip hop and stays open
late.
