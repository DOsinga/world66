# Shopping in Sanya

*Asia > Northeastasia > China > Hainanisland > Sanya > Shopping*

There are many **Sanya Shopping** opportunities waiting for you in this attractive Chinese city. The unique goods that Sanya offers make it one of the most popular shopping destinations in the region. The emphasis lies particularly on these:

**Pearls**

Sanya is very popular with its seawater pearls. They are huge in size and very high in quality. Shops and merchants all around the city are selling a wide variety of jewels and decorations made out of pearls, but be very careful when buying from the merchants near the beach, since many of the articles they sell are fake. Instead, buy from trusted shops like GN Pearl or Heren Pearl, and don&rsquo;t forget to get some pearl powder--it is said that this is the secret lying behind the long-lasting youth and beauty of the Chinese women.

**Coral Souvenirs**

A lot of souvenirs and decorations made out of corals are sold around the city, too. They are really stunning, but do not be tempted to purchase them; because corals are endangered species, their sale is strictly prohibited by law. They won&rsquo;t even let you carry them on the plane home, so shopping for pearls is not a good idea. Furthermore, through buying corals, you contribute to the destruction of the local marine ecosystem.

**Crystal**** Wares**

Hainan Crystal is rated as the best in China, and is used a lot in jewelry and decoration. Most of it is produced in the nearby town of Yanglan. You&rsquo;ll notice that the crystal wares have different colors. The locals say that each color symbolizes a different value such as health, justice, fortune, or something else. Check out the shopping malls for some more interesting crystal wares. These include the Summer Mall, the Pearl Square, the Yifang Department Store, the Tianhong Shopping Center, and the Jiayuan Shopping Center.

**Exotic Fruits**

All kinds of fresh exotic fruits are being sold on the streets of Sanya. Have you ever heard of durian, carambola, wampee, or jack fruit? They are only a small part of the impressive diversity of fruits the Sanya Market could offer. For more, you may want to check the Wanghao, the Wanfulong or the Fuleduo supermarkets.

**Colorful, Traditional Clothes**

Finally, you may want to go and buy some of the colorful traditional clothes. They are very bright and decorated with different tropical motifs like palm trees, sunny beaches, and ocean waters. Some great places for buying clothes are the Hongqi Shopping Street and the Jiefang Road.
