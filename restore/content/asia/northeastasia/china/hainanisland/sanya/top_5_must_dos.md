# Top 5 Must Do's in Sanya

*Asia > Northeastasia > China > Hainanisland > Sanya > Top 5 Must Dos*

Here is a list of the **Sanya must do's** that you should do when you are visiting this quaint city in the Southern Hainan province of China. Considered as the little Hawaii in China, Sanya offers not only a breathtaking view of the sea, but also some great scenic views of the mountains. Here are the top 5 must do&rsquo;s in Sanya.

**1. Get a Scuba Diving License **

While in Sanya, the first thing that you should do is get a scuba diving license from the many diving centers in the area. One of the institutions that offer scuba diving licenses is the Hainan Diving Club, which has a lot of competent diving instructors that are PADI accredited to assist you while diving in the open waters. While getting your license, you can also get a glimpse of the breathtaking underwater view of Sanya.

**2. Take a Local Tour**

Sanya is blessed with a great deal of rice fields and farming villages. You might want to take a local tour of the rice fields and villages to get an idea of how locals live their daily lives. You can arrange with your travel agent for a tour of the Nanshan Cultural Tourist Area, where you can see the locals at their work. These tours also allow you to sleep in an authentic farmhouse and live with local farmers for one day.

**3. Hiking in the Forest**

The next thing that you should do is head off to the outskirts of the city and embark on a hiking trip in the many trails in the forests of Sanya. You will surely marvel at the exotic flora and fauna that you will see in the forest trails.

**4. Sunbathe in Baifu Bay**

Never leave Sanya without visiting the Baifu Bay. Baifu Bay is characterized with calm waters and tropical weather; thus, the place is perfect for sunbathers as well as swimmers. Sunbathers can also play beach volley or indulge in snorkeling or kayaking for the entire day.

**5. Barhopping **

Sanya is a progressive city, so it has a lot of entertainment establishments in the heart of the city itself. Barhopping completes the top five must do list in Sanya. The most popular joint in Sanya is the Night Cat, which offers eclectic music from local beats to international music. This is a perfect place to meet and mingle with the locals before you head off to your next destination.
