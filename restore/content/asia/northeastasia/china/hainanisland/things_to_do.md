# Things to do in Hainan island

*Asia > Northeastasia > China > Hainanisland > Things To Do*

There are many things to do on Hainan island, making it popular among
international tourists as well as Chinese tourists. The natural beauty
and beaches make the island a place worth visiting.

**Fishing**

The island offers good conditions for fishing and fishing is a
significant part of Hainan island's economy. Grouper, Spanish mackerel
and tuna are among the fish species found in abundance in the area.

**Cycling**

Enjoy the scenery of the island by going cycling. Tour companies are
available for organized trips. Families can enjoy the cycling tours.

**Red Forest Nature Reserve**

Visit Red Forest Nature Reserve to learn more about this evergreen
forest. When the tide is high the trees roots are submerged in water so
that it seems as though the trees are growing out of the sea. There is
an abundance of fish and shrimp.

**Dongjiao Coconut Plantation**

The plantation is located 20 minutes away from Qinglan Harbour and can
be reached by ferry. The plantation is host to different coconut
species and has more than half a million trees. Here you can learn more
about coconut trees and enjoy walking through the plantation. The trees
provide shade from the sun. Chalets and resorts offer accommodation.

**Hainan Tropical Wildlife Park and Botanical Garden**

This area is home to more than 4000 rare birds and has more than 200
species. There is also a variety of plants including rare species.
There is a tropical fruit forest.

**National Crocodile Lake Zoo**

This activity is great for kids as it is both educational and
interesting. It is located 20 kilometers from Haikou city. It stretches
over 133, 000 square meters. There are more than 10,000 crocodiles as
well as crocodile shows and elephant shows.

**Bird Watching**

Hainan forest is home to a variety of bird species, making it a bird watcher's haven.

**Golf**

Hainan has golf facilities and driving ranges. Golfers can enjoy a game
of golf while enjoying the magnificent scenery. Among the golf
courses are Haikou Meishi Golf Course and the International Golf Club.

**Tennis**

Many large hotels have tennis facilities and some even offer tennis lessons. The Sea Tennis Club is an option.

**Water Sports**

Scuba diving and snorkeling are among the water sports available.
Hainan island has diving in natural conditions. The water is clear and
warm all year round with temperatures between 19 C and 26 C. While
diving you can see the beautiful coral formations and a variety of fish
species. Swimming in the warm water is another enjoyable activity. Wind
surfing and jet skiing are other activities that visitors can take part in.
