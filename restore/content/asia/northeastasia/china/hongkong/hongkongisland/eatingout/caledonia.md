# Caledonia

*Asia > Northeastasia > China > Hongkong > Hongkongisland > Eatingout > Caledonia*

Hong Kong's only authentic Scottish restaurant and bar, Caledonia is home to Asia's largest selection of single malt whisky. From its amazing 'Wall of Whisky' in The Whisky Bar, through its Scottish and house ales in the relaxing Lounge Bar, to its fine a la carte seafood restaurant, The Argyll Room, Caledonia is a taste of traditional Scotland in the heart of the Central business district. Honouring the Scots who have contributed so much to Scotland, and who were behind many of the big name companies that have shaped the face of Hong Kong, Caledonia presents an interesting insight into the history of the territory.
