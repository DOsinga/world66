# Getting Around

*Asia > Northeastasia > China > Hongkong > Lantau > Getting Around*

The simplest way for a traveler to get around Lantau and see the sights is to take a guided tour. They are very informative and usually relatively inexpensive. The ferry service to
[Hong Kong Island](/asia/northeastasia/china/hongkong/hongkongisland) is very fast and is fairly regular.
