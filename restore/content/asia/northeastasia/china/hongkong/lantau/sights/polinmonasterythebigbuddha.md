# Po Lin Monastery & The Big Buddha

*Asia > Northeastasia > China > Hongkong > Lantau > Sights > Polinmonasterythebigbuddha*

One of Hong Kong's major Buddhist centres, Po Lin Monastery, is Lantau's most popular tourist attraction. Set amid spectacular mountain scenery, the monastery shares Ngong Ping Plateau with the famous Big Buddha, the world's largest, seated, outdoor, bronze Buddha statue. Visitors can walk up the 268 steps to visit the 26-metre-high, 202-tonne wonder. More ambitious hikers can climb nearby Lantau Peak.
