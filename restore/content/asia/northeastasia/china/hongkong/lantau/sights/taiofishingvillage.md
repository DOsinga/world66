# Tai O Fishing Village

*Asia > Northeastasia > China > Hongkong > Lantau > Sights > Taiofishingvillage*

One hour by bus from Mui Wo, brings you to the fishing village of Tai O. Many of the Tank who live here, have built their homes built on stilts above the tidal waters. Their enchanting world is an amateur photographer's delight.
