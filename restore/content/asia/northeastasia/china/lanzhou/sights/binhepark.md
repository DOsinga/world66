# Binhe Park

*Asia > Northeastasia > China > Lanzhou > Sights > Binhepark*

Lying in the north of Lanzhou, along the bank of the Yellow River, Binhe Park is an open park with flowers, grasses, and sculptures. Also it is known as the “Green Travel Corridor”. The group sculptures in the park, such as Green Hope, Mother of Yellow River, Camel Team on the Silk Road, Pilgrims to the East, and so on, reflects the long history of Lanzhou from different aspects.   Now the park has become a reset place for the Lanzhou people in the summer, where they can get together chatting, practicing martial arts, Taji, dancing and strolling.
