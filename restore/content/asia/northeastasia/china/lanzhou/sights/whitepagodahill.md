# White Pagoda Hill

*Asia > Northeastasia > China > Lanzhou > Sights > Whitepagodahill*

On the northern bank of the Yellow River near the Old Zhongshan Bridge, White Pagoda Hill is a pleasant and well-managed place decorated with pavilions, teahouses, plant nursery and small walkways. On the top of the mountain, there is a white tower, companying with a temple, which is why the White Pagoda Hill came into use. The White Pagoda Temple is now the Buddhist place. There are also a group of temple buildings, which are outstanding for their distinct design and unique layout.
