# Boa Mesa

*Asia > Northeastasia > China > Macau > Eatingout > Boa Mesa*

The best restaurant in Macau we were able to find was Boa Mesa on Travessa de S. Domingos, No 16a. If someone is looking for a european flair dinning do not hesitate and visit them. Main courses are around 70 to 90 HKD and they have really nice portugese red wines starting from 120 HKD. The owner is Portugese guy very friendly and helpfull, I could even check my air ticket on his computer. We just felt sorry that we stayed in Macau only for a day.
