# Lin Fung Miu Temple

*Asia > Northeastasia > China > Macau > Sights > Linfungmiutemple*

This ‘Lotus Temple’ is one of the finest and also one of the oldest in Macau. It was presumably built before the Portuguese arrived at the beginning of the 16th century.
