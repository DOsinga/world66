# Nanjing Lukou Airport (NKG)

*Asia > Northeastasia > China > Nanjing > Gettingthere > Nanjing Airport*

Nanjing is served by an International Airport, with flights to various points in Asia and within China. It is currently served by Cathay Pacific, China Southern, Asiana, Singapore Airlines and since 2008 also from Lufthansa with direct flights to Europe.
