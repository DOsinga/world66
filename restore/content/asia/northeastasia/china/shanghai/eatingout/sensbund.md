# Sens&Bund

*Asia > Northeastasia > China > Shanghai > Eatingout > Sensbund*

The Pourcel brothers, Jacques and Laurent have won much acclaim for
their restaurant Le Jardin Des Sens, for which they got their third
Michelin star in 1998. In 2004, they opened Sens&Bund at the Bund18
complex. Food and setting are exquisite. You'll be back for more!
