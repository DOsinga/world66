**Sights**

**Chengdu Cultural Park**

Chengdu Cultural Park is next door to the Green Goat Temple. Admission: 5 yuan.  

address: Yihuan Lu (Chengdu's ring road)

openinghours: 6 am - 8 pm, daily.

**Giant Panda Breeding Research Center**

Sichuan is home to the endangered giant panda, and for years scientists have been researching its habits in an attempt to breed more pandas for release into the wild. About a dozen pandas live here. **River Viewing Pavilion Park**

The pavilion is a four-story Qing wooden structure overlooking the Jinjiang River. The park is famous for its lush forests of bamboo, and boasts over 150 varieties of bamboo from China, Japan and Southeast Asia.
