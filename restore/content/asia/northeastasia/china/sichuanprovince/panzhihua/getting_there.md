# Getting There in Panzhihua

*Asia > Northeastasia > China > Sichuanprovince > Panzhihua > Getting There*

Train

When you want to arrive, you can choose train first. The Chengdu-Kunming railway offers 8 trains everyday form Beijing, Xi'an, Chengdu, Chongqing and Kunming.

Air

You can also get there by air. So far, there are 3 airlines includes Air China, Sichuan Airlines and China Eastern. They can take you from Chengdu, Chongqing and Kunming.
