# Lhasa

*Asia > Northeastasia > China > Tibet > Lhasa*

Lhasa is the capital of Tibet. Tibet is quite an exotic place to visit. You will feel like you somehow landed in an issue of National Geographic: colorful monks with prayer wheels, magnificient landscapes, great monasteries and palaces and funny smelling yaks.

Lhasa can be reached from [Chengdu](/asia/northeastasia/china/sichuanprovince/chengdu) by plane (two daily flights) and from [Beijing](/asia/northeastasia/china/beijing). From Golmud you can take a bus with a stop in Amdo. From [Nepal](/asia/southasia/nepal) you can fly or come by bus. You need to get the necessary permits form the Chinese embassy. For the brave: try to get here from [Kashgar](/asia/northeastasia/china/xinjiangprovince/kashgar).
