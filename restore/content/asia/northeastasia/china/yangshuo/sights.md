### [Yangshuo West Street](/asia/northeastasia/china/yangshuo/sights/yangshuo_west_stre)

The history of West Street in Yangshuo can be traced back to 590 AD (Sui Dynasty in China) and it is more than 1400 years old to this day. The street was 200 meters in length and less than 4 metes in width when the county was founded, and got its name because of its location at the west of the county seat.

Stretching for less than 1 km now and paved with flagstones, West Street is an amazing global pedestrian street with old traditional Chinese dwellings on both sides of it. ### [Cormorant Fishing Tours](/asia/northeastasia/china/yangshuo/sights/cormorant_fishing)

Many guesthouses and travel agents in Yangshuo can arrange tours where you can witness this amazing fishing technique that has been practiced for centuries in this region. Fishermen head out onto the river with their cormorants, birds which are renowned for their fishing ability. A tie is fastened around the base of the bird’s throat and they are then sent to catch fish which they can’t swallow – instead they cough them up for the fishermen. These days, the technique is mainly used only for the entertainment of tourists.
