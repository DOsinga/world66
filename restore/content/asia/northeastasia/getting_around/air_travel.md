# Air Travel

*Asia > Northeastasia > Getting Around > Air Travel*

This is by far, the easiest way to get into any of the countries in
North-East Asia. The main international airports for [Japan](/asia/northeastasia/japan) are:
[Tokyo](/asia/northeastasia/japan/tokyo)-Narita, [Osaka](/asia/northeastasia/japan/honshu/osaka)-Kansai, [Nagoya](/asia/northeastasia/japan/honshu/nagoya)-Centair and [Fukuoka](/asia/northeastasia/japan/kyushu/fukuoka). For
[South Korea](/asia/northeastasia/southkorea), it is Seoul-Incheon. In [China](/asia/northeastasia/china), the main international
airports are: [Beijing](/asia/northeastasia/china/beijing), [Shanghai](/asia/northeastasia/china/shanghai)-Pudong and [Guangzhou](/asia/northeastasia/china/guangzhou). [Hong Kong](/asia/northeastasia/china/hongkong)
is also an international air-hub.
