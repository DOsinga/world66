Iconic Guides are a series of in-depth and absorbing audio tours of the monuments of some of the world’s greatest ancient civilisations, including Japan, Egypt and Greece. The Japanese series contains a range of guides to some of the most popular temple sites in ancient Kyoto and Nara.

Written by specialist historians, each guide provides an unparalleled level of detailed information into the cultural, artistic and architectural heritage of these ancient destinations.

The stimulating and evocative commentaries are designed to enrich any visit to these spectacular sites. Each guide has been designed to give complete flexibility, enabling you to explore at your own pace and giving you information when you want it. They are certainly a cost-effective way of seeing the 'sights', without having to join a traditional tour party.

Easy to use, Iconic Guides come in MP3 format and can be played on any MP3-compatible device, such as Apple’s iPods and iPhones, as well as most mobile phones.
