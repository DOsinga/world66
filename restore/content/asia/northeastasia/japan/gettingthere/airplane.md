# Airplane

*Asia > Northeastasia > Japan > Gettingthere > Airplane*

Being an island country, Japan is usually entered via airplane. There
are three main international airports in Japan. NRT, New Tokyo
International airport, located in Chiba about one hour outside of
Tokyo, KIX Kansai International Airport located in Osaka Bay, and newly
opened NGO Nagoya International Airport in Ise Bay, South of Nagoya.
