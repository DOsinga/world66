# Amanohashidate Travel Guide

*Asia > Northeastasia > Japan > Honshu > Amanohashidate*

The best resource for sights, hotels, restaurants, bars, what to do and see

Amanohashidate (Bridge to Heaven) is one of the Nihon Sangei, or Three
Great Views of Japan. It is actually a long sand-spit, covered with
pine trees. There are chair-lifts at each end, to bring you to higher
elevation to see the sand-spit. You are supposed to observe it, by
bending over, and viewing it framed by your legs!
