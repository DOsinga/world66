# Sights

*Asia > Northeastasia > Japan > Honshu > Hiroshima > Sights*

Most people go to Hiroshima to visit the A-bomb Dome, and the various Peace Memorials and Museums.

Not far from Hiroshima, is the island of Miyajima, home of the
Itsukushima Shrine, whose red torii gate is one of the Nihon Sangei.
