# Sights

*Asia > Northeastasia > Japan > Honshu > Mito > Sights*

**Kairakuen park**

It takes 15 min by bus from Mito station north. This park is very big
in conjunction with Senba lake park. This park is counted as "Big
three" in Japan (the others Kenrokuen park in Kanazawa and Kourakuen in
Okayama).

There are a lot of plum trees and in March there are a lot of travelers
nationwide and some from foreign countries. The entrance fee is free
(except Koubuntei, former Tokugawa's house inside the park)
