**Art Museum**

Located in Shirakawa Park, this museum was designed by Kisho Kurokawa, one of Japan's leading architects. The museum's permanent collection of Japanese and Western art is displayed in rotation, as well as special exhibitions from galleries world-wide.    

address: 052-212-0001
