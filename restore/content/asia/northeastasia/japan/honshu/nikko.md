# Nikko

*Asia > Northeastasia > Japan > Honshu > Nikko*

Nikko is a popular daytrip from [Tokyo](/asia/northeastasia/japan/tokyo) and one of Japan's favourite tourist attractions. The shrines and the temples in the city, and above all the spledid Tosho-gu Shrine are among the best sighst in the country so you should include Nikko in your trip even if you have only one week.

 The main attraction is the Tosho-gu shrine. More Chinese than Japanese this building dates back to the 1650s, but was restored in 1818. Other attracions include the Futara-san-jija shrine, the Taiyuin-byo, basically a smaller version of the Tosho-gu, the Shin-Kyo bridge and the Rinno-ji Temple.

 A trip to Chuzenji-ko Lake is nice too. Almost an hour by bus along a winding road. Beautiful sceneray and a 100 meter high waterfall popular with Japanese suicidals are the main attraction.
