# Getting There

*Asia > Northeastasia > Japan > Honshu > Yokohama > Getting There*

The best way to reach Yokohama form
[Tokyo](/asia/northeastasia/japan/tokyo) is by train. The fast train takes only 30 minutes. You have the choice between the JR Keihin Tohoku line, the JR Yokosuka line, Tokyo Toyoko line and Keihin Kyuko line.
