# Sights

*Asia > Northeastasia > Japan > Ryukyuislands > Ishigakiisland > Sights*

There is a cave open late which you can walk through for a small fee. There are some interesting fossils in the cave, but the tacky rap music blasting in the second half was kind of disconcerting...

There are several lovely beaches available on the northern and western parts of the island, and charming small mountains in the central part. You can take a glass-bottom boat tour and see coral reefs and giant clams. If you're lucky you might see the adorable tiny blue crabs on the afternoon beach.

**all**

Shiraho Reef

This reef is found off of the southwest coast. It features one of the world's largest and finest examples of blue coral.

Mount Omoto

This is the highest point in the prefecture, at 526 meters.

Kabira Bay

This bay, known as one of the 100 most scenic places in Japan, is the first venue in the world where cultured black pearls were first produced (in 1963).
