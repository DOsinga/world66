# Festivals

*Asia > Northeastasia > Japan > Ryukyuislands > Okinawaisland > Festivals 1*

Hija River Koi-nobori Festival:

Participants hang approxiamtely 500 *koi-nobori* (carp-shaped streamers) of various sizes around the Hija River to pray for children's health. The festival takes place (in 2004) from April 25 to May 11, with a special family day on April 25th.
