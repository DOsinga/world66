# Shopping

*Asia > Northeastasia > Japan > Tokyo > Asakusa > Shopping*

Best shopping is in the Nakamise. Nakamise litterary means something like "shops inside". You find the Nakamise just past the Kaminarimon Gate. There are many stalls where you get something to eat like rice crackers or cakes and the area always looks like it is some sort of a festival.
