# Hama Rikyu garden

*Asia > Northeastasia > Japan > Tokyo > Roppongi > Sights > Hamarikyugarden*

Once the private duck hunting reserve of the Matsudaira (branch of the Tokugawa). The garden is on the water which gives it a really open feeling. From the pier in the garden you can take a boat to
[Asakusa](/asia/northeastasia/japan/tokyo/asakusa).
