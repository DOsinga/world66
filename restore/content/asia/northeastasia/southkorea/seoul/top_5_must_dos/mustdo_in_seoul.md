# Must-do in Seoul

*Asia > Northeastasia > Southkorea > Seoul > Top 5 Must Dos > Mustdo In Seoul*

1) Go shopping in Dongdaemun Shopping area at subway line 2 - Dongdaemun History and Cultural Park station. It is intereresting to go after 10pm on weekends and watch how the locals bargain with the shops.

2) Try singing in a KTV as the KTV is really affordable at about 15,000 - 20,000won per hour for 1 room.

3) Taking the Han River Cruise to see the beauty of Seoul in the evening.

4) Going to a sauna and try korean-style scrubbing method - watch out as the dead skin will be scrubbed off leaving you red as lobster

5) Watching a traditional dance and eating korean full-course meal at Korea House.
