### [Janbeers](/asia/northeastasia/southkorea/suwon/nightlifeandentertainment/janbeers)

This is a really laid-back bar with cheap beer, a pool table, darts and a jukebox!!! If you take a taxi just tell him "aju day sam gori" and he'll drop you off at the intersection. Janbeers is next to the Tous Le Jour, right in the corner.
