# Korean Archery

*Asia > Northeastasia > Southkorea > Suwon > Thingstodo > Korean Archery*

Opportunities are presented to experience the culture of old times by practicing traditional Korean archery wearing traditional costumes at the military exercise grounds of Hwaseong Fortress.

**Tourist Information

**- Dates of operation : March.20 ~November.30

- Location : Yeonmudae

- For tourists from home and abroad

(junior high(middle) school students and above)

- Reservations : Yeonmudae Tourist Information Offices
