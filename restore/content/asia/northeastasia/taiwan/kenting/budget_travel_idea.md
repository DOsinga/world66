# Budget Travel Ideas in Kenting

*Asia > Northeastasia > Taiwan > Kenting > Budget Travel Idea*

smithadevadas

As Taiwan's very own Hawaii, Kenting is a budget travelers dream destination. A picturesque locale on the southern part of Taiwan, it is known for its sun, sand and a resort like atmosphere. The beaches in Kenting are extremely beautiful. You can spend days together on the beach, indulging in various water activities. Explore the marine life in Houbihu and go scuba diving. The other must do activities are a visit to the Kenting National Park and ride on the famous Baisha Biker trail. Here are a few tips for the budget conscious traveler visiting Kenting.

**Discount Airlines **

Make sure to book your air tickets well in advance. This way you will be able to take advantage of the discount fairs that airlines come up with. Once in Taiwan, connecting buses and trains are a cost effective mode of reaching Kenting.

**Getting Around**

The best way to experience Kenting is on a motorbike. It is the most cost effective and time saving way of absorbing the natural beauty of Kenting. Renting and registering for a bike takes 10 minutes and you are provided with a detailed map of the city. Rental cars and other means of transport seem futile when motorbikes are available for rentals.

**Accommodation**

The most expensive aspect while going on a holiday is the accommodation, especially if it is a tourist destination. Kenting offeres a variety of options for the budget conscious traveler. The most cost effective method is to pick any of the home stay options in Kenting street; the most famous street in Kenting which provides home stay facilitates or cheap hotels. It is advisable to consider a weekday as you will get better rates. Don't forget to bargain.

**Shopping**

The Kenting street is the Mecca for the shopaholics. This night market is bustling with activities. Go with the 'shop till you drop' attitude and bag everything from fashionable clothes to accessories, souvenirs to other gift items. Shopping here will never cause a hole in your wallet.

**Food**

Kenting is a sea food lovers paradise. With the numerous street side stalls selling fresh sea food, you can completely eat to your hearts content. The other local delicacies are a must try and a real pleasure to your pallet. If you're not the experimental kind, then you have KFC and McDonalds as well. The local restaurants also have all you can eat buffets at economical rates.
