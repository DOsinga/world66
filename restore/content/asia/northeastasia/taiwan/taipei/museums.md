**Taipei Gallery **

Since its opening, the Taipei Gallery has exhibited both ancient Chinese arts and contemporary arts from Taiwan. Most exhibitions come from major museums and private collections in Taiwan. In addition to introducing Taiwan's modern art, the gallery is a venue for artists from around the world whose works relate to Chinese culture and society. Phone: (212) 373-1854, Admission: Free   [more..](/asia/northeastasia/taiwan/taipei/museums/taipeigallery)

**National Museum of History**

An interesting collection of artifacts and art are exhibited in the National Museum of History. The museum is located within the splendid Botanical Gardens of Taipei. Phone: +88-6-223610270 

address: 49 Nanhai Road

**National Palace Museum**

Some people rate it the best Chinese musuem in the world. It exhibits more than 700,000 items that illustrate the culture and tradition of China. It is the world’s largest collection and reflects 7,000 years of Chinese history. Exhibits display paintings, historical relics, calligraphy, books and documents, jades, woodcarvings, bronzes, and more.  [more..](/asia/northeastasia/taiwan/taipei/museums/nationalpalacemuseum)

address: 221 Chihshan Road

**Miniatures Museum of Taiwan **

It is the largest miniature museum in Asia and it is really entertaining. The exhibition is known for the great detail in hand-carved people, figures, and scenery.  Phone: +886-2-2515-0583 

address: Basement, 96 Chien Kuo North Road
