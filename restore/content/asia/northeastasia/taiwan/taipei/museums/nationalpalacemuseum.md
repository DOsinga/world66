# National Palace Museum

*Asia > Northeastasia > Taiwan > Taipei > Museums > Nationalpalacemuseum*

Ranked as one of the four best museums in the world, Taipei's National Palace Museum is home to the largest collection of Chinese art in the world. So vast is the collection that it takes 12 years to display everything (the displays are rotated regularly).

Photography is not allowed inside the Museum.

Getting there by public transportation: take the MRT (Danshui line), alight at Shilin Station, then take the route 30 red bus to the Museum.
