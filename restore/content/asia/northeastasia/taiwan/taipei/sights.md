# Sights

*Asia > Northeastasia > Taiwan > Taipei > Sights*

Taipei has long been underrated as a destination for tourists, as a week or two here will surely prove. The city houses quite a number of excellent museums, galleries, and historical sights that are well worth visiting. Admittedly, the first impression made on most people is a crowded and polluted urban jungle, but in time the many charms and paradoxes of this exciting place will show themselves.

**National Theatre**

The National Theatre is one of two buildings, which compose the National Chiang Kai-shek Cultural Center. Demonstrating the artistic concepts of classical Chinese architecture, The National Theatre is modeled on the beautiful palace Ta-ho Hall. The dazzling theatre offers celebrated opera and theatre performances year-round [more..](/asia/northeastasia/taiwan/taipei/sights/nationaltheatre)
