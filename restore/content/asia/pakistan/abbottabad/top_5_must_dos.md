# Top 5 Must Do's in Abbottabad

*Asia > Pakistan > Abbottabad > Top 5 Must Dos*

Located about 121 kilometers from Islamabad, Abbottabad is one of the most interesting places in Pakistan, and there are many **Abbottabad must do's**. Surrounded by magnificent mountain peaks and pines, this place is the gateway to some of the most interesting places in the country. To make the most out of your visit, here are 5 Abbottabad must do&rsquo;s.

#### 1. Check out the Old Buildings and the Lovely Gardens

Abbottabad is famous for its old buildings and beautiful gardens. When visiting this city, take the time to walk around and admire the old churches and government buildings. The Central Church and the Ilyasi Mosque are some of the must see structures in the area. The church has a uniquely British design, while the Mosque is built directly over a stream coming from the mountain.

#### 2. Play Golf

The golf courses around Abottabad are famous for their world class facilities. The Piffer&rsquo;s Golf Club owned and run by the Pakistan Army has a huge area and its lush green surroundings are very relaxing. If you want to play golf, this place is your best option. Don&rsquo;t worry if you do not have your golf clubs with you; you can always rent a set at the site.

#### 3. Watch Cultural Shows

If you want to get to know the culture of this place better, start by watching some cultural presentations at the Jalal Baba auditorium. They usually present musical programs and plays in the afternoon for visitors.

#### 4. Marvel at the Sunrise over Thandiani

When in Pakistan during the winter, you should brave the cold in the morning and take the time to watch the sunrise over Thandiani peak. The snow clad mountain of Thadiani with its small plateau offers a breathtaking view of the sunrise. Located about 2,700 meters above sea level, this place offers some of the most enchanting views in Pakistan. The Thandiani is one of the most popular summer retreats in this part of the world, so if you happen to be in Pakistan during this time of the year, you should explore this mountain. The road to Thandiani may not be the best in the world, but is lined with stately pine trees with occasional bursts of colorful flowers.

#### 5. Explore the Nathiagali

This hill resort located about 31 kilometers from Abbottabad is worth exploring any time of the year. To reach this place, hire a jeep from Abbottabad or if you are fit and you are looking for some adventure, rent a bike or simply walk to the resort. A word of caution though, you should not take the long trek all by yourself especially if you are not very familiar with this area.
