# When to Go in Bahawalpur

*Asia > Pakistan > Bahawalpur > When To Go*

**Bahawalpur, when to go** is useful information when planning your trip. Each season has its own climate and you should decide to go based on what activities you plan on enjoying. Bahawalpur is the 12th largest city in Pakistan. You will find Bahawalpur to be a modern city that is rich in history and culture.

**Summer**

The summer months in Bahawalpur are very hot and dry. Visiting during the summer months should be avoided if possible. Most travelers are not used to the extreme temperatures. If you do travel during the summer months you will want to wear plenty of sunscreen, stay hydrated and wear clothes that protect your skin from the sun. You should also limit outdoor activities during the summer months as it is very easy to become dehydrated and end up with heat exhaustion. Summer offers the best travel discounts because of the extreme temperatures. If you are willing to put up with the heat and spend most of your days indoors you will be rewarded with excellent discounts.

**Fall**

The fall months are normally hot but bearable. You should still use the same precautions in the fall months as you would in the summer. Outdoor activities are normally okay to do during the summer months as long as you plan accordingly with the daily temperatures. The temperatures do start to drop towards late September and early October.

**Winter**

The winter months are a popular time to visit Bahawalpur. The weather has cooled down and you can enjoy a lot more outdoor activities. There are times when it gets very cold so make sure to pack accordingly. While winter is a popular time to visit you can normally get travel discounts if you book your trip in advance.

**Spring**

Spring is the busiest time in Bahawalpur. You will find Bahawalpur to be crowded but still very enjoyable during the spring months. The weather is perfect for all types of activities. Spring is also the time when you will find several festivals going on. Planning in advance will help ensure you get the best price possible. You should also keep in mind that top tourist attractions will be busy so make sure to arrive early. February to April are the busiest times in Bahawalpur. If you travel during the month of May you will probably find that most of the crowds have died down.
