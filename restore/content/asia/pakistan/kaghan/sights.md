# Sights in Kaghan

*Asia > Pakistan > Kaghan > Sights*

While you are traveling through Pakistan, make sure you visit some of the **Kaghan Valley Sights**. Located in the Mansehra district in the Lesser Himalayas, this valley is one of the most beautiful areas to visit. This perfect place offers just the thing with its snow-topped mountains, lush valleys, waterfalls as well as streams and glaciers. Whether you enjoy hiking the highest peaks or rafting in the cool rivers, Kaghan Valley will not disappoint.

#### Sari and Paya

Traveling from Shogran, take the drive through the woodlands to the peak of Paya ridge. Once there, you will have the beautiful scene of Malika Parbat below you.

#### Babusar Pass

In mid-July, when the road becomes passable, traveling to Babusar Pass is essential. The panoramic views of snowy mountaintops and the extensive valleys below are absolutely breath-taking. On your way to Babusar Pass, take a stop at Lake Dudupat Sar to enjoy some fresh-water trout fishing or a picnic.

#### Saiful Miluk

If you are looking for a more relaxed family activity while visiting the Kaghan Valley, why not ride a pony around Lake Saiful Muluk? Also available are 2-3 day rides in the Upper Kaghan Valley. Some of the most thrilling journeys can be taken from Besal to Lake Dudupat or to the top of Babusar Pass. As such, Kaghan Valley itself has many lakes and springs surrounding the valley.

#### Malika Parbat and Musaka Mussalla

The Kaghan Valley offers amateur climbers to study the fundamental skills of mountain-climbing. Malika Parbat, Makra Top and Musaka Mussalla are the major peaks in the Kaghan Valley. Malika Parbat, although not as tall as the others, is the most challenging to climb. Spending an afternoon accomplishing a feat of climbing a mountain is one of the more exhilarating things to do in Kaghan Valley.

#### Garam Chashma

All around the Kaghan Valley are waterfalls that drop thousands of feet and one of them is Garam Chashma. This is a hot water spring that is famous among the tourists for its boiling spring water, containing Sulphur. It is said that the spring water cures many diseases like headaches, skin problem, rheumatism and gout.

#### Kafir Kalash Tribe

If you are in Kaghan Valley, then you must visit the Kafir Kalash Tribe. It is among the most famous tourist points due to its historic values. It is said that soldiers of Alexander of Macedonia settled at this place and discovered the nearby spring. Please note that tourists need to get special permits from the Deputy Commissioner in order to visit the place.
