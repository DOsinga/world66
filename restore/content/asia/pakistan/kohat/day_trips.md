# Day Trips in Kohat

*Asia > Pakistan > Kohat > Day Trips*

A good place to travel on a **Kohat day trip** is to the nearby tourist destination of Peshawar. Kohat is located in the Khyber Pakhtunkhwa Province in north-eastern Pakistan and is only about 64 km (40 miles) south of Peshawar, the province&rsquo;s capital.

#### Getting There

Travel to and from Peshawar can be done via the Indus Highway, or N55, utilizing the 1.8 km long Kohat Tunnel which opened to traffic in June 2003. Buses and cars alike can utilize this improved route and the trip between the two cities typically takes just over an hour. Always confirm current road conditions in the area before traveling.

#### Peshawar Sights and Attractions

Peshawar is one of the oldest cities in the world and has a rich history and vibrant culture. A large variety of goods is available to tourists in Peshawar such as traditional carpets, jewelry, pottery, clothing, and artwork which are sold at the many bazaars around the city. One of the most famous bazaars is the colorful and crowded Kissa Kahani Bazaar which was known historically as the &ldquo;street of storytellers&rdquo; and now sells items including bamboo, sweets, dried fruits, leather goods, and clothing.

As a popular tourist destination, Peshawar features many parks, museums, mosques, mausoleums, and monuments, in addition to an abundant shopping scene, a large variety of restaurants and cafes, and many hotels. One of the most famous attractions in the area is the Khyber Railway train safari: a three hour ride on a century-old train through the historic and beautiful Pakistani countryside to the nearby town of Landi Kotal.
