# Eating Out in Kohat

*Asia > Pakistan > Kohat > Eating Out*

Located in northern Pakistan near the Afghanistan border, **Kohat restaurants** feature a mixture of Pakistani and Afghanistani dishes. Even thought there a limited number of restaurants to choose from, Kohat is still a place where tourists can have great meal. Local delicatessens are worth trying, especially their sweets.

**Chappal Kebab**

This place is named after its famous beef kebab, one of the best in Pakistan. It takes the shape of the sole of a shoe. Nearby restaurant sell the same kebab but they are not as highly regarded as this one. More like a food stand than a full service restaurant, Chappal Kebab has excellent tea, but beware that there is no waiter. The few tables available in front are almost always occupied by locals.

**Tikka Tikka**

This restaurant serves amazingly good tikka and karai. Even though the restaurants in nearby Namak Mandi, serves the best food, this restaurant holds it own. Tikka is barbecued meat and karai is stew full of oil with chili and tomato. With very reasonable prices you can also order meat by kilogram and eat as much as you like.

**Bazzar**

Kohat Bazzar is not just a marketplace for shopping, you can find excellent food and drink stands as well. Try faluda, the sweet meal and you can always grab some special Pakistan bread with cheese and honey. Great grub for breakfast.
