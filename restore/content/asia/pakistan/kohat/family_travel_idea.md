# Family Travel Ideas in Kohat

*Asia > Pakistan > Kohat > Family Travel Idea*

**Go Nature Tripping with Your Family in Incredible Kohat**

**Kohat family travel** is an exciting adventure for the entire family. You will get to journey into Pakistan, where spicy food and natural wonders abound. Your family can fly to any of the international airports at Lahore, Peshawar or Karachi. A military airport is found at Kohat, but it does not cater to International flights.

**Places to Stay **

Hotel Nadria and Greenhill Hotel are found in Kohat, but it is not certain they accept foreign tourists. You can find the nearest international hotels in Peshawar. The Pearl Continental Hotel is a great place to stay, with its comfortable rooms and affordable prices. You can request a family room arrangements when you make your reservation. The hotel provides amenities for the whole family like fitness centers, restaurants, cafes, indoor and outdoor swimming pools and gift shops where your children can enjoy shopping for exotic mementos.

**Places to Visit **

Nature enthusiasts would enjoy the flora and fauna around Kohat because it is like no other. Your family can marvel at the different plant species. Happily gurgling streams and rivers will heighten your adventure. You can also visit Kohat Pass, which is a historic mountain pass to Peshawar. If your family prefers less physical exertion, then you can visit the incredible tomb of the saint, Haji Bahadur Ali Abdullah Shah. You can also shop in the local bazaar found in the area.
