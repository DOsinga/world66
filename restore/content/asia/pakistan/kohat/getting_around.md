# Getting Around in Kohat

*Asia > Pakistan > Kohat > Getting Around*

**Kohat Transportation **brings each visitor safely from one place to another. This transportation in Kohat is found in huge numbers in the town. Once you are in Kohat, you do not need to worry about going to your destination. They will be in charge of your secured transport around the town or from the town to the other adjacent places.

**Bus**

The bus is among the forms of transportation used in Kohat for long-distance travel. You need to ride a bus from Kohat to transfer to the other towns or vice versa. The bus fare depends on the distance you have traveled. Logically, the nearer the place you travel, the lesser is the fare. You need to ask for the schedule of the departure of the bus to ensure your travel schedule and plans are followed.

**Rickshaw**

Rickshaws are a well-known mode of transportation in Kohat because they are easy on the pocket. They are three-wheeler vehicles used to service the small town of Kohat. This three-wheeler rickshaw is better to manipulate than a bicycle and it uses less gasoline than a four-wheeler vehicle. There is no fixed rickshaw fare so you need to negotiate with the driver before you start the ride. It is recommended for you to ask the local residents how much fee it takes to your destination. In that case, you will not be overcharged by the driver. The rickshaw is commonly used by the people of Kohat as a mode of transporting their goods. Some of them also use this vehicle as a rest venue.
