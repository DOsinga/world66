# Things to Do in Kohat

*Asia > Pakistan > Kohat > Things To Do*

When visiting **Kohat**,** things to do **include visiting museums, shopping, and visiting old forts. Tourists will find Kohat to be a place of tranquility.

**Peshawar Museum**

The Peshawar Museum is an old British style building, consisting of an entire stone statue collection of Buddha's life. The museum also has the largest collection of Gandhara art in the world and other large priceless collections, including artifacts from Pakistan's Northern cultures, manuscripts, and inscriptions.

**Qissa Khawani Bazaar**

The Qissa Khawani Bazaar, also known as the "street of story tellers," is located in the center of the city and was once a crossroad to hundreds of professional story tellers.

Even though the story teller's numbers have drastically decreased, the streets of Kohat is still considered the melting pot, as the streets are still filled with a diverse variation of shoppers buying fruits, vegetables, clothing, jewelery, knick knacks, and more. Visiting this bazaar is one of the best things to do in Kohat because Qissa Khawani Bazaar is a must see destination for adventurous tourists.

**Fort Bala Hisar**

The headquarters of the frontier corpse, Fort Bala Hisar is one of the most historic places of Peshawar, but most tourists visit Fort Bala Hisar for its amazing view of the entire city.

Fort Bala Hisar is directly in the middle of Kohat, so anyone standing at the top of the 15 acre fort can see the entire city in motion, especially at night, when the city is lit up like the night sky. This is one of the most exciting things to do in Kohat.
