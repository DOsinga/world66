# Top 5 Must Do's in Kohat

*Asia > Pakistan > Kohat > Top 5 Must Dos*

Located in Pakistan, the town of Kohat has experienced some tough times with militants and politics in the recent years. However, with the situation easing up, the tourism trade is beginning to look up. So, here are the top 5 **Kohat Must Do&rsquo;s**.

**1. Experience the nature**

Home to some of the most diverse flora and fauna in the region, the town of Kohat and the area surrounding it is the best place for nature lovers. With the rains coming consistently throughout the year, the landscape of the place barely changes--and there are lush trees and shrubs year round, complemented by seasonal flowers. The edge of the Kohat district meets the banks of the Indus River, so for those who enjoy looking at something different in nature, a short drive out to the river banks can make for an enjoyable weekend afternoon.

**2. Walk along the Kohat Fort**

The historical Kohat Fort watches over the main parts of the city, where the old city used to stand. Much of the fort has corroded into walls, but it stills stands as a photographic destination for tourists. Around the Fort is the main city area of the town, so be sure to have a walk around as well. Be very careful, though. Since this is one of the busiest parts of the city, there has been a high concentration of militant movement and suicide attacks, so be very careful not to stay too long.

**3. See the Tanda Dam**

Kohat itself is found in a valley, and the dam overlooks the city, storing the water for the drier parts of the year. Apart from being a practical structure for the people, the dam is also home to a large majority of Kohat&rsquo;s wildlife. Jackal and wild foxes are common here, even though most of these cannot be spotted in the day. Instead, delight yourself in some bird watching, and see some of these adorable creatures land on the side of the dam walls for a drink and a rest.

**4. Stopover at Kohat Pass**

Another of the city&rsquo;s historical sites, the Kohat Pass was once a strategic military point during ancient wars for soldiers to stop over or to store ammunition and food. Now, it is a tourist destination, with shops selling some of the most interesting things any visitor to Kohat can find. The Pass is infamous for its black market weapon trade--anything from swords and knives to guns and automatics can be found here. But those looking to walk on the safe side of the law can always opt for the ornamental displays of letter openers carved with traditional designs and laid with semi-precious stones.

**5. Visit the Kohat Town**

Kohat would never be Kohat without its town center, and the perfect way to start and end a tour of the city would be to walk around the town area. With stalls and bazaars lining the streets and busy locals hurrying about their daily business, the town typifies the daily lives of the locals and gives Kohat its local flavor.
