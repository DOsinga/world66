# When to Go in Kohat

*Asia > Pakistan > Kohat > When To Go*

This **Kohat: When to Go** guide will provide vital information about this Pakistan city, which stands as the district&rsquo;s capital. Influenced by the British, there are still existing railways and forts that tourists find interesting. Kohat experiences extreme weather conditions with a temperature as low as -2 degrees C during the winter months of November to April. During the summer months of May to September, temperature can go as high as 47 degrees C. May to October are the rainy months when heavy monsoon rains fall almost daily; making it difficult to leave your hotel room.

**Winter—Best Destinations **

The best places to visit during November to April are the mountain slopes where you can go skiing, mountain climbing and rock climbing. You can go trekking in the slopes of Bangash and Miranzai. You can join tours, which can go, as far as the Karakoram Mountains. Be ready for very cold temperatures, though.
**
Summer—Best Destinations**

Because of extremely high temperatures during summer, you can visit Kohat, but you should prepare for extremely hot weather. The weather is cooler in the mountains, especially if some ice glaciers have not yet melted entirely. You could even join a group trek to the majestic Himalayas. The best months to visit are October and September, because the summer temperature is starting to get lower. October also is the month when rains start to dwindle. You can visit the sparkling streams and clear rivers in Jungle Khel and engage in fishing, boating, kayaking and white water rafting. Marvel at the flora and fauna found only in Pakistan.
