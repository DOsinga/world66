# Getting Around in Murree

*Asia > Pakistan > Murree > Getting Around*

**Murree transportation** is available in Murree to take you to your desired destination. Murree is among the largest resort towns in Galyat, Pakistan. It is a well-known summer resort in Islamabad. People huddle together in this place to enjoy the cold and relaxing atmosphere that the area offers.

**Car**

Cars are the mode of transportation that will take the visitor from Islamabad to Murree. If you do not have your own transportation, you can hire a car from the Faiz Abad Bus Stand in Rawalpindi. In order for you to save money, it is better if you can find a Murree driver that will take you to Murree. These Murree drivers charge less of a fare than the other drivers. The normal rental fee for a car is 700 Rs, but you can still lower it down by bidding with the Murree driver at around 300 to 350 Rs (going to the famous resort town). Moreover, if you are going to Nathiagali from Murree, it is preferred for you to take the recommended car of your hotel. This is helpful for you, since the driver can also serve as your guide.

**Bus**

Daewoo Coach is the bus that operates from Rawalpindi to Murree. The Daewoo Coach terminal is situated near the Rawalpindi Peshwar Road. The fare from Rawalpindi to Murree is around 100 Rs, and the travel takes about an hour and a half. There is also an available non-aircon bus, and its fare costs around 35 to 60 Rs.
