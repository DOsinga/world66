# Things to Do in Murree

*Asia > Pakistan > Murree > Things To Do*

This guide will discuss **Murree things to do**,** **as there are plenty of activities you can indulge in. Murree is a beautiful town located in Pakistan, built in 1853. Over the years, the population has significantly grown, as well as the number of tourists each year. It&rsquo;s considered a romantic spot for lovers to vacation, as well as families.

#### Shop at Mall Road

Mall Road is one of the most visited places in Murree. People go there to browse the markets, souvenir shops and restaurants. The road is closed off from traffic and is often more crowded during the evening.

#### Explore Kashmir Point

Kashmir point is loved and cherished by locals and tourists alike. Many people come and visit Kashmir point during the day, to experience everything the point has to offer. It&rsquo;s a great location to take pictures of the area, to hike or picnic.

#### Visit Cliffden Road Cemetery

The Cliffden Road Cemetery is significant because of its historical value and the fact that it is one of the oldest cemeteries in Murree. It is also the largest cemetery in the area. Some of the oldest gravestones date back to the 1860&rsquo;s.

#### Sightseeing at Pindi Point

At Pindi Point, you can take chairlifts high above the tree tops. Here, you can view the snowcapped mountains and wooded lands. Another attraction at Hindi Point is a tomb that is thought to belong to the Virgin Mary. Visitors who receive permission are allowed to view the tomb.

#### Listen to Music at Purple Haze

Although Murree does have its own brewery, the area itself is considered &ldquo;dry,&rdquo; which means they don&rsquo;t serve alcohol in the town. Most nightlife takes place in surround areas such as Karachi or Islamabad. Karachi is known as &ldquo;The city that never sleeps,&rdquo; so it&rsquo;s more fitting to tailor this nightlife section towards Karachi. Purple Haze is a club located in Karachi. Live bands entertain the crowd on weekends, as they are engulfed by the haze from their Shisha pipes. Top the night off by ordering from their decadent dessert menu.

The Base Rock Caf&eacute; is located in Karachi and is known as a dance club featuring as assortment of music styles. The Caf&eacute; holds themed nights in honor of famous musicians and bands and offers karaoke to its patrons.
