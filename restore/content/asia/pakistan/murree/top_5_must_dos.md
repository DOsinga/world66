# Top 5 Must Do's in Murree

*Asia > Pakistan > Murree > Top 5 Must Dos*

**Murree Must Do&rsquo;s** includes a variety of ideas. Murree is located on the border between Azad Kashmir and Punjab. Murree is known as a romantic getaway for travels all throughout the year.

#### 1. Visit Kashmir Point

Kashmir Point is a beautiful place to visit while visiting Murree. Here you can sightsee, hike, take pictures, go horseback riding, or sit on the terrace at the Point and enjoy your coffee. The view from Kashmir Point is breathtaking. See snowcapped mountains in the distance and feel time stand still as you gaze at the beautiful surroundings from Kashmir Point.

#### 2. Explore Pindi Point

Pindi Point is one of the best places to visit while traveling through Murree. Pindi Point has chairlifts that visitors can use to get a bird's eye view of the area and snowcapped peaks and valleys.

Also located at Pindi Point is a tomb believed to belong to the Virgin Mary. Although no one knows for sure, many locals will defend the idea of this being the final resting place of the mother of Jesus Christ.

#### 3. Enjoy Ayubia National Park

Travel to a nearby province and visit the Ayubia National Park. Home to 203 species of birds, 31 species of mammals and 104 species of plant life, Ayubia National Park is a must-see for any traveler.

A chairlift can be used by visitors to observe the beautiful forested hills. There are numerous hotels and restaurants located in the area. The park is a great place to hike and bike ride along trails.

#### 4. Shop at Mall Road

Mall Road is a must-see when traveling through Murree. Here you will find a huge market, selling an assortment of crafts, souvenirs and much more. Many restaurants are also located at Mall Road. The road is closed to traffic so that visitors can shop and browse the market place. It is usually best to visit Mall Road during the evening as it can become overcrowded during the day.

#### 5. Pray at Holy Trinity Church

Holy Trinity Church can be seen on Mall Road. The church was dedicated in 1857 and still remains functional in Murree. In colonial times, the church was the center of life in Murree. Located across from the church are the post office, general merchants and tailors.

The journey through Murree can be a beautiful one. There are so many sights and adventures that will captivate you on your travels. Murree is a beautiful place filled with rich history and culture. After you visit the area you may find yourself planning your next trip to Murree, Pakistan!
