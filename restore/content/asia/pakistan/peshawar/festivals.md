# Festivals in Peshawar

*Asia > Pakistan > Peshawar > Festivals*

There are many fun **Peshawar festivals** to enjoy while you in the area. If you are going to plan a trip to Pakistan, you may want to schedule your visit to coincide with one of the festivals and events.

#### Shab-e-Barat

The Shab-e-Barat festival is held in the city on the 14th day of Shaaban, which is in the 8th Islamic month. The festival is celebrated with prayer, fireworks and sweet dishes. It is a time for friends and families to celebrate.

#### Eid-ul-Fitr Festival

The Eid-ul-Fitr Festival is held in Peshawar at the end of fasting on the first of Shawwal, which is the 10th month of the Islamic calendar. There are special prayers after the sun rise, and many people bring sweet meals to exchange and enjoy with their friends and family.

#### Pakistan Day Festival

The Pakistan Day Festival is held to celebrate the day of the Pakistan resolution led by the Muslims from South Asia on March 23, 1940. There is a military parade located at Lahore, Peshawar and goes through the areas of Quetta and then Karachi. One of the main features of the festival is a grand parade held in Islamabad. If you enjoy parades and celebrations, you can enjoy this one of the many Peshawar festivals.

#### Horse and Cattle Show Festival

The Horse and Cattle Show Festival is held in Dera Ismail Khan near the city. There are a lot of activities and celebrations during the end of March through the first week of April. There are games, different kinds of folk dances, local music, cattle races to watch and many exhibits of local crafts available for you to see and enjoy while attending the festival. You can get a good look at what the local artists and musicians create.

#### Basant Festival

The Basant festival is called the festival of the kites and was started in India. It has been dubbed an Indian hobby, buy many of the cities now celebrate the festival by flying kites of all colors in the sky. The festival is held in the spring, and everyone in the city can go out and enjoy the kites flown throughout the city. This is one of the Peshawar festivals you may want to check out and enjoy for the many beautiful kites to see from any of the areas of the town.
