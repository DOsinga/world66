# Practical Information in Peshawar

*Asia > Pakistan > Peshawar > Practical Informat*

**Visit the Historic City of Peshawar**

This **Peshawar practical information** guide will give you valuable tips and help you plan your trip to this ancient city in Pakistan. Peshawar is located in the province of Khyber Pakhtunkhwa and also serves as the capital of this province. The city is a popular city and lies to the west of the Indus River.

#### **Safety**

If you are planning a trip, you will need to check with your travel agent and about any travel restrictions issued by your government. Peshawar has become unstable and volatile because of the fighting involving the Pakistani army and the Taliban. You will need to make sure there are no travel restrictions before you leave.

#### **Entry Requirements**

You will need to have a passport with at least 6 months of validity remaining before you apply for a visa to Pakistan. You will need to submit 2 passport photos along with the visa application form. You will also need to have a letter of invitation from your host in Pakistan, including a photocopy of the host's Pakistani passport, confirmation of accommodation, duration and purpose of visit. A single entry visa for up to 90 days will cost you around $120 USD for U.S. citizens only in the summer of 2010.

#### **Climate**

The summer in Peshawar can be hot and uncomfortable with temperatures reaching 40 degrees Celsius (104 degrees Fahrenheit). The temperatures during winter range from 4 degrees Celsius (30 degrees Fahrenheit) to 18 degrees Celsius (65 degrees Fahrenheit). Peshawar experiences rainfall both during summer and winter.

#### **Population**

Peshawar is a city with a population of over 2.5 million and has a growth rate higher than other cities in Pakistan. Its population is half urban and half rural.

#### **Airport**

Peshawar is served by the Peshawar International Airport. The airport is located about 25 minutes from the city center. Several airlines like PIA, Emirates, Etihad, Qatar Airlines and Kuwait Airlines operate regular flights in and out of Peshawar. The airport serves both international flights as well as domestic flights to other cities in Pakistan. If you are traveling from the United States or any European country, you will have to take a flight to Lahore, Karachi or Islamabad and then take a connecting flight to Peshawar.

#### **Currency**

The currency in Pakistan is the rupee. In summer 2010, the exchange rate was approximately 80 to 85 rupees to $1 USD. The currency has had a period of inflation over the last several years which has more recently stabilized.
