**Visit this Ancient City in Pakistan**

This **Rahim Yar Khan practical information** guide will give you some valuable insight into what you need to visit this city. Rahim Yar Khan is a city with a long history and is located in the south of Pakistan. If you are planning a visit to this city, you will have to get to any of the cities in Pakistan first and then proceed to Rahim Yar Khan.

**Visa and Passport**

You will need to have a valid passport to be able to get a visa to Pakistan and to proceed to Rahim Yar Khan. Your passport will need to have at least 6 months validity remaining. You will also need to provide 2 passport photographs along with your application for a visa. A single entry visa to Pakistan for 90 days will cost you $190.

**Currency**

The Pakistani Rupee is legal tender in Rahim Yar Khan; $1 is about PKR 86.59 (Pakistani Rupees). You will need to change your local currency at the money changers at the airport or at the banks. On your way out you will need to change all your Pakistani Rupees back into your local currency.

**Customs**

Just like in every country, carrying firearms, drugs or smuggling is a punishable offence in Pakistan.

**Culture**

Rahim Yar Khan is a Muslim city, so you will need to be aware of cultural and religious customs while visiting the city. If you are entering a mosque, it is best to ask advice on the customs followed before entering.
