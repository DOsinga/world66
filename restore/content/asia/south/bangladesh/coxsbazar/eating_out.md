# Eating Out in Cox's Bazar

*Asia > South > Bangladesh > Coxsbazar > Eating Out*

**Cox's Bazar Restaurants** offer a wide variety of seafood cuisine showcasing Bangladesh's local recipes, but there are also fast food and European dishes to taste. The western dishes are served mostly in major hotels. Prices are in Taka (tk) and American dollars. Follow these restaurant tips:

**Jhawban Restaurant & Poushee Restaurant**

Located on Hotel Sayeman Road at Mookthijoddha Sarani street south of Sea Beach Rd, these are two Cox's Bazar restaurants that are next to each other and that offer typical Bangladeshi food. The place's main dish is fried fish, one of the most delicious Cox's Bazar local cuisine recipes. It also serves chicken, smoked hilsa, chingri and prawn curry with rice. Both restaurants get full at lunch time. The prices go from $60.00 TK to $130.00 TK or $1.00 USD to $2.00 USD.

**Mermaid Eco Resort Restaurant**

The Mermaid Eco Resort is at the Sea Inn Beach and Hotel along Marine Drive. Adjacent to Inani Beach, people can enjoy some of the best wine in the country and they can also enjoy some of the best authentic Italian, Chinese, Continental as well as Bangladeshi cuisines. Many local bands play live music for the tourist entertainment. Prices go from $4.00 USD to $6.00 USD

**Seagull Hotel Restaurants**

The Seagull Hotel located at Cox's Bazar Hotel Zone (zone Shantinagar Dhaka) counts with it's complex of five restaurants along which offers Bangladeshi and international recipes. There is also a terrace to enjoy the ocean breeze and observe the landscaping vision of Bay of Bengal. Prices range from 300 to 1400 Tk or $4.00 USD to $20.00 USD. One of the best feautures that many Cox's Bazar restaurants have is their natural surroundings.
