# Bars and Cafes in Batu Pahat

*Asia > South > Malaysia > Batupahat > Bars And Cafes*

There are a lot of great **Batu Pahat bars and cafes** you can enjoy when you are in the city. There are many different kinds of cuisine served, and you can enjoy the local feel to the restaurants.

#### Kopitiam Ton Fang

The Kopitiam Ton Fang is a great place to stop for porridge and soy sauce. There are a lot of different dishes you can try and mixed platters of food which offer a variety of foods. Tofu, eggs and pork are some of the most common dishes you will find in the caf&eacute;, and it is a great place to try some of the local favorites.

#### Restoran Swee Bee Hainanese

The Restoran Swee Bee Hainanese caf&eacute; offers specialty dishes such as the Hainanese chicken rice. The chicken is made with bean sprouts, and there are also tofu dishes available. There are starters to enjoy and some of the local favorite drinks. This is among the best ways to enjoy the city and the different tastes that it offers.

#### Ah See Noodle House

The Ah See Noodle House is a caf&eacute; and coffeehouse that offers many different tastes to enjoy. You can stop in for a refreshing drink or cocktail or enjoy one of the many specialty coffee drinks which are prepared fresh and served to you. If you want to try some of the best local coffees and light foods, you can choose this as one of the Batu Pahat restaurants that you see in the city.
