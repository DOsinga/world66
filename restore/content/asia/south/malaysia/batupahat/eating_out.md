# Eating Out in Batu Pahat

*Asia > South > Malaysia > Batupahat > Eating Out*

**Batu Pahat Restaurants and Succulent Malaysian Dishes**

Malaysia is a food center in Southeast Asia as evidenced by **Batu Pahat restaurants** that serve delicious, exotic local dishes. You can choose from vegetarian restaurants, to Chinese eateries to authentic Malaysian cuisine. One popular restaurant in Batu Pahat is Restaurant Sy, which is located in the heart of Batu Pahat.

#### **Restaurant Sy**

This restaurant offers various dishes to ignite your sense of taste. They have vegetable and succulent seafood dishes that are difficult to ignore. Their specialty dish is &ldquo;Over Bridge Pork Ribs&rdquo; which is a savory and tasty meat dish. Their fried brinjal is tasty and different from other brinjal recipes. They also serve tofu with some veggies. Their soup is one of the best in the area with local condiments to lend the native flavor coming from the country. You can savor Malaysian dishes with a dash of Western cuisine in this restaurant. The ambiance of the place is cozy and clean. You can have peace and enjoy your meal without unnecessary interruptions.

#### **Prices**

The price of dishes range from $10 to $20 in summer 2010. The more expensive price, as compared to other restaurants, is justified by the fresh, mouth-watering Malaysian cuisine they offer. That is why even the locals patronize it well. They also have large servings two people can share which can cut costs for customers on a tight budget. If you want to eat at Restaurant Sy and do not have the budget, you can opt for vegetable dishes, which are cheaper but delicious as well.
