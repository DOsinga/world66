# Festivals in Batu Pahat

*Asia > South > Malaysia > Batupahat > Festivals*

**Exploring Malaysia's Harvest Festivals: Batu Pahat Festivals**

**Batu Pahat festivals **are among the most popular of regional festivals celebrated in Malaysia. Numerous ethnic groups and cultural influences prevail in Malaysia, giving the nation a calendar full of regional and cultural celebrations. However, due to the agricultural economy of the nation, harvest-themed festivals with a religious interpretation are among the most widely celebrated.

#### **Tadau Kaamatan
**

This festival is celebrated by the farming communities and the indigenous tribes of Malaysia. However, the festival is celebrated in different ways across the nation. In some parts, it is celebrated as a thanksgiving to the gods for their annual offering of food for the local people. The festivities include shows and exhibitions apart from cultural programs that provide a glimpse into the rural life and religious beliefs of Malaysia.

#### **Harum Manis**

Apart from the celebrations associated with Tadau Kaamatan, Batu Pahat celebrates its own regional version of the festival, called the Harum Manis Festival. It is usually celebrated in the second week of May. The festivities attract huge participation from the surrounding towns. The theme of the festival is aimed at celebrating the mango crop. This means tasting freshly picked mangoes at various counters set up along the streets. The main attraction of the festival is the Harum Manis mango which is only found in this part of Malaysia and is a national favorite. The main venue is the Bukit Harumanis Mango Field, located in Kuala Perlis, Batu Pahat.
