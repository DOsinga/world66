# Getting Around in Batu Pahat

*Asia > South > Malaysia > Batupahat > Getting Around*

**Local Transport in Batu Pahat**

Here are some **Batu Pahat transportation** options you can use while traveling to this destination. If you&rsquo;re looking for something quick and reliable, you will probably choose to ride on a taxi. There are also some trishaws, which offer an alternative especially suitable for adventurous travelers.

#### **Taxis**

Taxis are one of the most popular forms of transportation for guests of Batu Pahat. All taxis are equipped with meters, and the drivers are required by law to use them. However, many drivers aren&rsquo;t strict about this measure, so make sure the driver puts the meter on when you jump on a taxi. A taxi trip around town shouldn&rsquo;t cost more than $4 in summer 2010. Keep that in mind, because many of the drivers try to charge $5 per person when seeing foreigners. Tipping is optional, but most people do it.

#### **Trishaws**

Riding on a trishaw is an exciting experience when you&rsquo;re in Batu Pahat. In the past, this was one of the most decent transportation options for people who wanted to travel around town. However, there are only few trishaws left in the city. Most of them gather near the Courts store close to the bus station. The trishaw is a 3-wheeled vehicle similar to a bicycle. It is operated by a driver and capable of accommodating no more than 2 passengers. Riding on it is a fun and great way to discover the city sights from another point of view.
