# Things to Do in Batu Pahat

*Asia > South > Malaysia > Batupahat > Things To Do*

The** Batu Pahat things to do** list has an interesting mix of famous monuments and outdoor activities. This tourist destination is located in Malaysia and is a must visit. Read on to find out more.

**Mur Circle**

The Mur Circle is the most celebrated spot in the whole of Batu Pahat. A few years back, it was known for the gigantic tree that stood at the center of the road. Today, even though the tree has been trimmed, visitors are taken to this spot. According to the tour guides here, all visitors must commence their Batu Pahat exploration from this point. This is the heart of Batu Pahat, and you will find the area buzzing with activity. This place is the perfect venue in Batu Pahat to find a king sized breakfast.
**
The Well**

The Well in Batu Pahat is another famous attraction. If you ask the locals, they will tell you that the well was discovered by the legendary warrior Hang Tuah. It is said that the district derives its name from this well. This Well is located on the seaside in the area called Kampung Minyak. The origin of this well dates back to the 15th century.
**
The Night Market**

You are in Malaysia, hence you cannot get away from the night market craze. Batu Pahat has several night markets spread across the district. But, the most prominent amongst them is the night market adjacent to the Blue Mosque. Ask the locals to direct you towards this market. This is a well known spot in Batu Pahat. Here, you can buy all kinds of things like footwear, sarongs and beaded jewelry. You will find several souvenirs to take back home. The best part about this market is the food. The stalls here serve the best barbecue dishes. Shrimps, lamb on skewers chicken in peanut sauce are few of the offering here.

**Fishing**

Another famous activity that should not be missed on your trip to Batu Pahat is the Fishing Spot. This attraction is located at a distance of 8 kilometers from Batu Pahat and adds a fun quotient to your vacation. This fresh water fishing experience will make you feel like a professional, as this place promises a catch to every visitor. Rohu, Soon Hock and Catfish are some of the inhabitants here.

**The Mosque**

The Mosque, in the town area, is a popular attraction in Batu Phar. A visit to this mosque will reveal the beautiful architecture and design. This is a large mosque and the blue faced will catch you fancy. It is located on the Kulang Road at the junction. The local name for the mosque is Masjid Jamesk.
