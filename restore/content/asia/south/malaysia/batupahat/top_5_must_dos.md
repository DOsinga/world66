# Top 5 Must Do's in Batu Pahat

*Asia > South > Malaysia > Batupahat > Top 5 Must Dos*

The district of Batu Pahat in Johor, Malaysia is a big traveler&rsquo;s destination, offering great sights, good food, and several activities that are considered to be Batu **Pahat must do&rsquo;s**.

Learn about these 5 activities that you shouldn&rsquo;t skip when you visit Batu Pahat, Malaysia.

#### 1. Freshwater Fishing in Pusat Memancing

Pusat Memancing is a great place for fishing enthusiasts. Here, you will enjoy the clear waters of this fishing center while having fun catching a variety of fish. There is an abundance of big Patins, Rohus, Pacus, and Soon Hocks. They offer fishing gear at affordable rates, and the locals will be more than happy to give you great tips on how to get a good haul.

#### 2. Prawn Hunting in Udang Galah

Tourists who are not fond of casting fishing rods but want to enjoy the clear waters of Udang Galah will have a great experience catching prawns in their natural habitat. You can rent or buy everything that you need, from nets to bait from local establishments near the area. This activity is simple enough for kids who just want to get into the fresh waters of Udang Galah. Indeed, prawn fishing is gradually being established as a primary ecotourism business as well as one of the many Batu Pahat must-do&rsquo;s.

#### 3. Golfing in VIP Sports Clubs

If you are in to a high-end sports activity, there is no better way to spend a clear day than playing golf in a premier sports club like the Bukit Banang Golf and Country Club. This facility is equipped with a vast 18-hole golf course that can cater to the needs of experts and beginners alike. If you prefer a basic golfing experience, go for the first 9 holes called &ldquo;The Valley Nine.&rdquo; If you want a more challenging game, then you can play in the second set of holes called &ldquo;The Lake Nine.&rdquo; The club also offers shops selling sports equipment and amenities for tennis, volleyball, basketball, and swimming.

#### 4. Shopping and Eating at Pasar Malams

Pasar Malams are areas packed with food stalls and small craft shops scattered in Batu Pahat and other districts of Malaysia that are open to the public come night time. You can&rsquo;t say that you have been to Batu Pahat if you haven&rsquo;t experienced munching on a wide selection of street food and native Malay dishes.

#### 5. Nightclubbing at De Palais

Tourist who love to party will have a blast in this hip party hub. De Palais, located near Carrefour, is famous for its mash-ups of hip-hop and house music, which ignite the party mood of its patrons.

These 5 Batu Pahat must-do&rsquo;s are some of the promising activities that you can experience when you visit this tourist-friendly district in Johor, Malaysia.
