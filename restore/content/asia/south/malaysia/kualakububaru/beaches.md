# Beaches in Kuala Kubu Baru

*Asia > South > Malaysia > Kualakububaru > Beaches*

If your travels take you to Malaysia, then there are many enjoyable **Kuala Kubu Baru beaches** where you can enjoy soaking up sun and splashing in blue waters. Whether family fun is your destination, or you just want to privately lay about in the sun Kuala Kubu Baru has the beach for you.

**Berjaya Langkawi Beach**

Perched on this beach is a resort which gives it's guests the chance to enjoy a mystical and mythical experience that can only be found on the Berjaya Langkawai islands. This is the perfect place for those who would like to find a place to relax and enjoy perfect tranquility and the fantastic beauty of nature. The resort on the beach is located in the Burau Bay on the northern part of the island and is just a fifteen-minute drive from the airport. The decor is inspired by traditional Malaysian culture, and the 70 acres of sprawling beach is only made more beautiful by it's backdrop of a five million-year-old rainforest.

**Kuala Terengganu Beach**

This is one of the most famous beaches that Kuala Kubu Baru has to offer and is also known as Pantai Batu Buruk. There is also an annual beach festival held on its shores each year. It has breathtaking sparkling clear turquoise water surrounded by salt and peppered sands. The beach itself can be found just one km away from the main city and can even be reached by walking. The beach is lined with pine trees, and during windy days many people enjoy flying kites on the beach. It is especially beautiful during the night, and is a perfect place for couples to go on a romantic evening stroll across it's soft sands. Another attraction worth seeing is the Losong Museum, which is located just six km from the major city. Pulau Duyung, known as the Mermaid Island, is also the venue for the Moonson Cup. The Tengku Tengah Zahara Mosque, called the Floating Mosque by the locals, is another beautiful attraction. The Istana Maziah, which is a place built especially for the Terengganu's Sultan is another perfect place to see. There is also a small fishing village placed perfectly on the small peninsular near the Terengganu River.

**Teluk Chempedak Beach**

This beach is located only five short km from the town of Kuantan and is sprinkled with many high class hotels. Water sports are perfect for this beach such as sailing, skiing, sunbathing and sailing. This beach offers sparkling white sands and beautifully crystal clear blue waters. Just a short walk away is a path that leads through the Teluk Cempedak Forest Reserve straight through to the delightful Pelindung Beach. If your looking for more of a jungle trek there is also an alternative route that leads through to a mini-zoo.
