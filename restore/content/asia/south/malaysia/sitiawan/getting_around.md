# Getting Around in Sitiawan

*Asia > South > Malaysia > Sitiawan > Getting Around*

Among all of the things tourists have to take into consideration, **Sitiawan Getting around**, is of the most important aspects of a trip to plan out. Below are some of the ways visitors get from here to there in Sitiawan.

#### Star Shuttle Bus

The Star Shuttle Bus is popular among travelers in Sitiawan because it is cheaper than a taxi, the seating is roomy and comfortable, and it runs between Sitiawan, and Kuala Lumpur, a neighbor city full of tourist-favored attractions. The Star Shuttle bus is great for transporting tourists from one city to the next, but isn&rsquo;t logical for a night on the town. Star Shuttle will cost a traveler around MYR45, or 13USD, one way.

#### Rental Car

The more favored mode of transportation for tourists, and one of the best ways of getting around Sitiawan, is renting a car. This allows you the convenience of having a ride whenever you need one. AVIS, a rent-a-car company, is located at the airport, and will charge you an average of 156MYR, or $47 USD, per day.

#### Taxi

Taxis are also a popular mode of transportation when it comes to getting around Sitiawan because visitors can find them almost anywhere. They are very convenient, and can take tourists anywhere around the city. Taxi fares vary from taxi to taxi, but the average cost of a half-hour trip in Sitiawan will cost tourists around $50.00 MYR, or about $14 USD.
