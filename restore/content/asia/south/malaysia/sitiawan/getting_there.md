# Getting There in Sitiawan

*Asia > South > Malaysia > Sitiawan > Getting There*

For most visitors traveling to **Sitiawan**,** getting there **begins with a flight into Malaysia&rsquo;s International Kuala Lumpur Airport. You can fly into KL from most major major airports around the world, and from KL Airport to Sitiawan, getting there is an enjoyable travel experience. All the usual transportation options are available for visitors.

**From Singapore and Thailand**

From neighboring countries Singapore and Thailand, transportation can be done by road also. Many coach services from these countries will take you directly to the capital of the area, Ipoh, where you can then catch a taxi or bus to Sitiawan. Ipoh also has a small airport, but flight availability should be checked first with your tour operator, or at KL International Airport.

**Car Hire
**

Renting a car is still the favorite option for many tourists, as it presents many options. It is an easy way to plan your trip, get around in your own time, and it gives you the freedom to explore. Hire from international car hire companies at KL Airport, a hotel or from their city offices in your area, then relax and head to Sitiawan in your own time. Make sure your driving license meets with local regulations.

**Taxis
**

A very popular and inexpensive way to travel when heading for Sitiawan is by taxi. You will find taxis for hire everywhere, and they can be hired for the trip, by the day and for longer periods by arrangement. Fares vary from driver to driver, but negotiate your price based on your individual requirements. If you are traveling to Sitiawan from any distance, remember that the driver has to return to his pick up point, which could be many miles away.

**Bus**

Sit back and let someone else do the driving, while you bask in the freedom of enjoying some of the world&rsquo;s most tropical countryside. For example, the Star Shuttle Bus service can be booked in Kuala Lumpur and will take you very comfortably on to Sitiawan, and at an equally comfortable price.

The many travel options and the journey to Sitiawan is a dream for sightseeing enthusiasts, as you pass through jungles and rainforests, and drive past huge rice fields and rubber plantations. In Sitiawan, getting there is a journey of discovery you will remember forever, so always have your camera ready.
