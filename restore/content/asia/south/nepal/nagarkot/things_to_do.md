# Things to Do in Nagarkot

*Asia > South > Nepal > Nagarkot > Things To Do*

During your visit to **Nagarkot**,** things to do** are going to range from taking a walk to shopping and sightseeing. What you include on your list of things to do in Nagarkot is going to depend on what you enjoy doing while on vacation, but also on whom you are traveling with. For example, if you are traveling with young children you are not going to spend all night dancing in a club; instead, you'll be looking for family-friendly things to do during the day.

**Sunrise**

Watching the sunrise over the Himalayas is something that should not be missed. You're going to have to get up quite early, but you can see the sunrise over the Himalayas from just about anywhere in town that has a good view of the mountain range.

**Changu Narayan**

If you plan to do this you will want to set aside most of one day. To visit Changu Narayan you will need to walk through the Kathmandu valley to reach the Changu Narayan temple. The walk itself is more than eight miles long, so be prepared for a hike. The best part about the hike is the beautiful scenery and the villages that you get to explore. Once you get to the temple you can explore the medieval town that was built around it.

**Villages**

You can easily walk or ride a mountain bike to the nearest villages to enjoy a day of shopping or sightseeing. One village that you should visit is Sankhu, especially if you want to do some sightseeing. The Vajrayogini Temple, which was erected in honor of the goddess Vajrayogini, is located about one mile outside of the village.

**Nature Hikes**

One thing that you will notice about the village of Nagarkot is that it is very relaxed. There is no hectic schedule to follow and no busy streets. People who visit Nagarkot are there to relax and enjoy themselves. They often come for the beautiful views of the Himalayas that the hilltop village provides, but they also come to enjoy the lovely greenery that surrounds every part of the village. If you are looking for something to do in the village, you can go on a hike through the mountains or along the numerous trails that weave throughout the village.

**Meditation and Yoga**

In the village you can find yoga or meditation instructors or you can venture out on your own. There is no better place to practice meditation or yoga than Nagarkot, because of how quiet and peaceful the village is. Yoga and meditation are popular in this village because of how relaxed everyone is.
