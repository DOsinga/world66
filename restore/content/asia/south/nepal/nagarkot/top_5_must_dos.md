# Top 5 Must Do's in Nagarkot

*Asia > South > Nepal > Nagarkot > Top 5 Must Dos*

A list of **Nagarkot must do&rsquo;s** will help you while you are at this beautiful place, which lies 32km northeast of Kathmandu.

#### 1. Walk to Changu Narayan

This is Kathmandu Valley&rsquo;s most ancient temple. It is about 13km from Nagarkot. The fantastic scenery and villages here will leave you spell-bound, and the small medieval town built around the temple is also very beautiful to look at. Take a guided tour for directions and a brief lesson on the history of the temple. The usual charge is Rs.500. The Changu Narayan lies to the west of Nagarkot, past Bhaktapur towards Kathmandu.

#### 2. Trek to get a View of Nature

Nagarkot is clean, friendly, and beautiful. Those looking to hike can visit the neighboring villages by foot or bicycle. Wildlife enthusiasts love to engage in bird watching or look at the various plants and animals found in Nagarkot. The real beauty of Nagarkot lies in the fact that it has so many relaxation options available. You can do what you like at your own pace. Whether you are a nature lover or someone just looking to unwide, you have various options to keep you occupied.

#### 3. Lie in Bed and Watch the Sunrise

By starting your day early, you can get the best of what nature has to offer. You can either walk outside or lie in bed to watch the sunrise over the Himalayan Mountain. This is breathtaking and relaxing, and is one of the major attractions of Nagarkot, since it is the nearest point from the Kathmandu Valley.

#### 4. Play with the Village Children

You will see a lot of friendly faces while you are at the neighboring villages of Nagarkot. The children like to play, and this is exactly what you can do. You feel good after it. If you are lucky, they will even teach you a few of their folksongs as well!

#### 5. Hike to the Vajrayogini Temple in Sankhu

Also known as the Gum Baha, the temple is the shrine of the powerful Buddhist tantric, Vajrayogini. It is a Buddhist temple. It was constructed in the year 1665, and is located in the ancient city of Sankhu. There are a number of sculptures here, so take the help of a guide to understand the significance of each.

Keep the children occupied in the hotel or resort by carrying plenty of activities and games. You can also watch satellite TV that is available in most resorts. But the best thing to do in Nagarkot, truly, is to head out and simply wander around, soaking in the beauty that nature has to offer.
