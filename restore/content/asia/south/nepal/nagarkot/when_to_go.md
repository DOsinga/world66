# When to Go in Nagarkot

*Asia > South > Nepal > Nagarkot > When To Go*

When planning a trip to the Napalese city of Nagarkot, when to go is key to the success of your travels. Perfectly located for photographic opportunities of Mt. Everest and other breathtaking scenery. Nagarkot is 7200 feet above sea level which needs to be taken into consideration, especially if you plan to do any trekking. At that elevation, and at the elevations you may be ascending to in your hikes, weather cna be severe. Nagarkot is home to some of the best mini-trekking possibilities in the entire Nepal region, you'll want to schedule your trip to coincide with the best hiking conditions.

Be very cautious while scaling mountain peaks and trails. The most important part of the trip is that you return home safely.

**The Best Time to Travel**

The best time to travel to Nagarkot may be right after the monsoon season, because the surrounding area is covered in green fields and an abundance of activities are available. Monsoons season lasts from June through August, so try to book your trip in September and October and into winter. Besides climbing and hiking, you can also enjoy the countryside in all its splendor. In fact, if you travel to Nagarkot during the end of the monsoon season you will breathe the cleanest air you ever had the satisfaction of inhaling. When you finally arrive at Nagarkot, ensure you get up early and stay up late to catch the sublime sunrises and sunsets—you will not regret it

**When Not To Travel **

The beginning of the monsoon season is also a fine choice but it will not be as visually appealing as at the end of the monsoon season. You should avoid traveling to Nagarkot in the peak summer months at all costs because you are vulnerable to monsoons virtually every minute of the day and your views of the mountains will disappear. Mudslides are also very common and can cause road blockages, which can interrupt your Nagarkot experience.

For the best views of the Himalayan Peaks you will need to ensure you are on the hilltop any time between October and April. Nagarkot is home to the most stunningly beautiful peaks in the entire world—do not miss them by scheduling your mountain adventure at the wrong time.
