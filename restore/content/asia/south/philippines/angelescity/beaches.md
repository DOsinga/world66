# Beaches in Angeles City

*Asia > South > Philippines > Angelescity > Beaches*

There's been a real revolution in the accessibility of** Angeles City Beaches** since the construction of the Subic-Clark-Tarlac expressway road in 2008. Now a whole range of gorgeous beaches in the Subic Bay area are easily reachable from the city.

**White Rock Water Park**

This recently expanded 7 hectare park is situated in a beautiful little cove with its own very private beach. Aside from the obvious beach bum activities there are other recreational and spa things to do such as using the swimming pools. Perhaps the standout activity is the water park itself which will bring pleasure to the entire family.

**Moonbay Marina
**

Moonbay Marina is a reclaimed strip of beach that has been converted into a luxury hotel and entertainment area. This best of Angeles City beaches is not only good for a night out drinking and partying but excellent for watersports.

**Olongapo City**

The largest city in Zambales province, Olongapo City offers 2 first-class grey sand beaches. Although tourists will find that this city is filled with ample opportunities to engage in sexual activities, especially because the city is filled with plenty of prostitutes playing their trade, there are other activities on offer that won't offend any travelers, such as beach barbecues and darts bars. Barrio Barretto is the best beach in Olongapo and home to the best seafront hotels and resorts.

**Driftwood Beach**

Located along from Barrio Barretto, this is a quieter and calmer example of Angeles City beaches, appealing to local tourists from the big cities such as Manila as much as foreign travelers. It is conveniently located on the National Highway and even provides a small car park. If you are interested in staying for the longer term then there are a few cottages available for tent behind the sea wall.
