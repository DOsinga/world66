**Getting Around Binan Laguna**

Binan Laguna, often referred to as just Binan or City of Binan, is a large industrial zone in the Phillipines. **Binan Laguna transportation** has developed to meet the needs of not only the frequent local and foreign business travelers, but also the needs of locals. Here are some thing to consider when getting around Binan Laguna.

#### **Getting Around by Bus**

This is one of the most convenient and cost-efficient ways to get around the city because there are several types of buses, each one depending on your budget and need. There are small minivans which are more expensive than regular buses, but they are a faster way to move around downtown during rush hour. However, they can only be found along popular routes. The regular buses are good if you're traveling on a tight budget and you generally travel in non-rush hours. When traveling by bus, don't carry large sums of money and avoid wearing expensive jewelry, earrings or watches.

#### **Getting Around by Taxi**

Taxis are a comfortable way to get around Binan Laguna. If traveling alone or late at night, it is a good idea to take a taxi rather than a bus. After boarding a taxi, make sure your driver turns on the meter only after he knows where to take you. The usual flag down rate ranges from 30 to 40 Philippine Pesos. Also, it is a good idea to carry a mix of smaller bills because drivers often will claim not to have change in an effort to make some extra cash. Generally, it is better to provide landmarks and main avenues as directions rather than specific street addresses. Therefore, try to become familiar with major landmarks and main avenues through a city guide before your arrival to the Philippines.
