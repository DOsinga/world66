# Nightlife and Entertainment in Binan Laguna

*Asia > South > Philippines > Binanlaguna > Nightlife And Ente*

The **Binan Laguna nightlife** is not quite as active as you would expect from a city. Many of the bars and pubs are actually found outside of the city in nearby villages. However, each of the suburbs and villages offer a different style of nightlife, so there is something for everyone. This city is one of the newest in Laguna, and it is near Manila. There are several tourist attractions that draw many families and young people to the city.

#### Great Spots for a Drink

Nirwana Gardens is a very popular night spot, as it contains the Calypso Bar, La Luna, and The Pub. The Calypso Bar is a Caribbean themed bar. They play Calypso and Reggae music all night long. This bar is actually a floating bar, as it is built on stilts out on the sea. Go out for a drink and enjoy the cool ocean breezes. The Pub is located at the Nirwana resort and offers karaoke. La Luna has an open air disco because it is right on the beach. This is the spot for beach parties and dancing. There is a complimentary shuttle available from the resorts to Nirwana Gardens.

Padi&rsquo;s Point Restaurant and Bar is another popular bar. They offer many special events and competitions. You can earn points on the drinks you purchase, which can be used for raffles and other drawings.

#### Dance the Night Away

If you want to go out dancing, then the best nightclub on the islands is Silk. This is in the Binan Lagoon resort. It contains nightly entertainment, house dj's, a disco, lounge, club, and more. They have the latest urban entertainment, making this the party spot.

Besides participating in dancing, you can also view some excellent dancing shows and a mock war show that originates from this spot of the world called Maglalatick. Dancing is an important part of the Philippines history, and the folk dance performance should not be missed.

#### Some Family Fun

The best place for family nightlife is at the Club Med. They have many different shows such as trapeze, fashion, and dancing that everyone in the family can enjoy. They have a full team that is devoted to bringing new and diverse events to the club. Besides great entertainment, they also have several different dining options. Have a meal at the main restaurant or go out for some dining on the beach. Club Med also has a special kids club so the adults can enjoy themselves, and the children have activities tailored specifically or their tastes.
