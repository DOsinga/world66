# Practical Information in Binan Laguna

*Asia > South > Philippines > Binanlaguna > Practical Informat*

**What You Need to Know about Binan Laguna **

When traveling in Binan Laguna, praqctical informaiton will help you negotiate most situations. Binan Laguna is the third most populous city in the Laguna province of the Philippines, with a nearly 300,000 residents. The majority of them belong to the Tagalog ethnic group, but there are representatives of most minor ethnic groups as well. The languages you can use for communication are Tagalog and English. Keep in mind that the Philippine English has a good number of unique words and phrases that may be a bit challenging for visitors to understand.

**Money**

The local currency is called Philippine peso and for a dollar you can get about 46 to 47 Philippine pesos. In fact, the US dollar is the only reliable exchange currency here, so if that isn&rsquo;t your home currency, make sure to buy some dollars prior to arriving at Binan Laguna. You can change other currencies as well, but at very poor rates, causing you significant loses. Currency can be exchanged in banks, hotels, and exchange bureaus and in larger department stores. ATMs are also widely available.

**Visas and Custom Restrictions**

All travelers with a valid passport and a return ticket, or a ticket to another destination, can enter the country without a visa and stay up to 21 days. You are allowed to import no more than two cartons of cigarettes and 1 liter of alcohol. There is no limit on the foreign currency you bring, but you can&rsquo;t enter or leave the country with more than 5000 Philippine Pesos.
