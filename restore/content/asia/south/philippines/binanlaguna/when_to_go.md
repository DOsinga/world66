# When to Go in Binan Laguna

*Asia > South > Philippines > Binanlaguna > When To Go*

When traveling to **Binan Laguna**, **when to go** is a pretty easy decision to make. However, several factors may influence your decision.

**The Best Season to Visit Binan Laguna**

The dry season, from December to April, is usually the best time to visit the Philippines and Binan Laguna in particular. Typhoons, which are quite frequent in this country, do not strike during this time of year and the dry weather is perfect for sunbathing and sightseeing. Most festivals take place during the months of December and May. Therefore, if you are in the mood in fiestas, those may definitely be months to consider.

However, visitors should be aware that, during the months of April and May, temperatures are usually extremely high in Binan Laguna. Wearing sunscreen, as well as hats, and drinking lots of water is therefore highly recommended in order to avoid dehydration. In the Philippines, a fervent Christian country, major Christian holidays are celebrated by the entire population. Since workers usually get a few days off for Christmas and Easter, it is usually quite difficult to travel in the country during those periods.

**Advice for Cost-Conscious Travelers**

Obviously, visiting Binan Laguna off-season can be much cheaper than during the dry season. It is usual for hotels, for example, to offer a 50% discount on rates during the rainy season, from August to November. Flights also tend to be considerably cheaper during that period.

However, don't forget that typhoons are common in that area during the rainy season. In addition to the obvious safety issues, those violent storms may also force you to stay indoors for several days in a row, therefore seriously compromising your trip.
