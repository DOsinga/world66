# Eating Out in Boracay

*Asia > South > Philippines > Boracay > Eating Out*

**Boracay restaurants **offer a variety of sumptuous meals. Boracay Island in the Philippines is the top tourist destination in the country. Because of this, there are a number of restaurants that serve suitable cuisines to tourists from various cultures.
****

#### **Exclusively for Chicken Lovers**

Andok&rsquo;s Boracay Restaurant is a Filipino restaurant that offers grilled chicken for takeout orders. However, Andok&rsquo;s Boracay Restaurant has two dine-in restaurants in Boracay that caters the most delicious roasted and fried chicken meals to chicken-loving customers.

Since Andok&rsquo;s restaurant is a local eatery, it prepares Filipino food at a very affordable price. If you are curious about traditional Filipino food, then you must try this restaurant. You must experience their chicken and pork dishes such as roasted and fried chicken, litson kawali, crispy pata, sisig and many others. You can visit this restaurant any time of the day as it is open for 24 hours.

#### **A Variety of Cuisines**

Artista Boracay Restaurant is a popular restaurant that is situated on Diniwid Beach. They offer a variety of American, Italian, Swiss and Filipino cuisines. It has a nice ambiance. It offers a panoramic view where you can see a beautiful Boracay Sunset. The must-try food in this restaurant is their chicken cuisine, Artista&rsquo;s Chicken Cacciatore, which continues to be the favorite food of their regular customers. You can chill out in this wonderful restaurant and enjoy the best food they offer at a reasonable price.
