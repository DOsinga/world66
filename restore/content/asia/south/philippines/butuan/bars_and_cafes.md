# Bars and Cafes in Butuan

*Asia > South > Philippines > Butuan > Bars And Cafes*

Butuan has a very active night life, thus there are many great **Butuan bars and cafes**. Often times, there isn&rsquo;t much better to do at night, especially during the rainy season in the month of December. True Brew and Margie's offer comfortable atmospheres and fresh offerings.

#### **Try The True Brew**

Locals and travelers alike, claim you cannot be in Butuan without stopping by the favorite coffee shop, True Brew. It is so much of a favorite it has even been featured in many magazines. Located on JC Aquino Avenue, it opens around lunch time and remains open until about midnight. With its home-like ambiance, it is easy to settle in to enjoy a good meal or a good cup of coffee.

#### **Touch Base With Home At Margie&rsquo;s**

Located within walking distance of True Brew, Margie&rsquo;s Kitchen is the place to go after True Brew closes. However, you don&rsquo;t have to be a night owl to enjoy Margie&rsquo;s. The prices are specifically aimed to meet the demands of the middle to high income market. They supposedly have the best cakes in town to finish off a great meal. Be sure to try the famed Cookie Monster cake when you visit. Additional best-selling items include cappuccino brownies, pandan cake, blueberry cheesecake and chocolate mousse cake. The desserts are always fresh because they are freshly prepared all day long. Margie's Kitchen also offers Internet connections for those who need to connect with those outside of Butuan.
