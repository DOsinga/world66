# Getting Around in Butuan

*Asia > South > Philippines > Butuan > Getting Around*

Butuan City is part of Mindanao and it belongs specifically to Agusan del Norte Province. There is nothing to worry about the transportation in Butuan. **Butuan City Transportation** comes in variety of forms that can accommodate a large number of passengers.

**Minicab**

Minicabs travel along the major routes of the Butuan City. They are really small so a tall man would have a hard time of getting in and out. Minicab can accommodate a total of 15 passengers – three in front seats and six for each row of two at the back.

**Taxi **

In Butuan City, you cannot see an empty taxi passing by. This is because the taxis stand by and stay on call. It means that a passenger can only ride a taxi if they call or request for it. If you want to be picked in a certain place, you need to call the taxi operator for a request. There should be a pre-negotiated fare before the sending off.

**Motorcyle**

Motorcycles are the major form of transportation in Butuan City. This kind of transportation can load up to 10 to 12 passengers.

**Tricycle**

Tricycles are very common in the Philippines. However, the tricycle in Butuan City is a bit different from other tricycles. It can carry six passengers. The front of it resembles a cab. You can use this mode of transportation for special short trips on routes which is not serviced by the minicab and jeepney.

**Jeepney**

The jeepney is used for suburban travels. If you are going to transfer from one town to another town, you need to take a jeepney.
