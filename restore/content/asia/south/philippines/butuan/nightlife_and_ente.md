# Nightlife and Entertainment in Butuan

*Asia > South > Philippines > Butuan > Nightlife And Ente*

**Butuan Nightlife** offers to local and foreign tourists a night of dining, music, singing and dancing, or a quiet peaceful communion with nature in a secluded beachfront place. Located at the mouth of the Agusan River, and about 490 miles south of Manila, Butuan City is the commercial center of Agusan Valley. During the day, you can explore the rich culture of this small city and at night, enjoy a simple but memorable time.

**Caraga Square**

If you like open places with the night wind on your face as you dance and listen to music, then Caraga Square is for you. It is a club, conveniently situated at Butuan&rsquo;s City Center. It features all types of music from acoustic sounds, to pop, soul, rock and jazz. You can opt to sit and listen to the band or you can also dance the night away with other revelers.

**Blue Haven, Karaga KTV Bar**

Found at the ground floor of the popular Karaga Hotel, this "&ldquo;videoke&rdquo; bar serves fine wine and beer. The KTV bar provides an outlet for you to hone your singing skills. They have a wide array of music selections that you can choose from. You can also have a choice of coffee or beer in one corner and just listen to others display their thespian talents.

**Blue Lagoon Grill Bar**

One of the finest grill bars within the city, Blue Lagoon Grill Bar, serves wine and delicious cuisine to patrons. &ldquo;Inasal,&rdquo; the native chicken dish for which Butuan is known, is a treat. The combination of native and International dishes will make the experience an adventure for culinary enthusiasts. Savor their native-inspired juicy steaks and spare ribs.

**Tubay Coastline**

About 24 miles from Butuan city proper, this serene place will allow you to relax and unwind. You can de-stress and have a night swim in clear waters, have a romantic nighttime stroll along the seashore, or have a quiet, time savoring Mother Nature through the vast expanse of water, the starlit sky and the beauty of the flora and fauna around you.

**Gaisano Mall**

The mother of all malls in Butuan, this mall provides a venue for late-night shopping. You can buy almost all the things you need from this mall. It also houses an assortment of pizza joints, burger joints, and restaurants offering native and international dishes. You can have a late night supper after your shopping spree.
