# Bars and Cafes in Dagupan City

*Asia > South > Philippines > Dagupancity > Bars And Cafes*

The aptly-named Hall of Fame Bar has to be the very best of **Dagupan City bars and cafes**. This is no mean feat, given how happening this part of the world is and how much the locals like to go out, get merry, and enjoy themselves.

**The Best Seafood in Town**

Travelers and locals alike have testified that the Hall of Fame serves probably the best seafood dishes in all of Dagupan City, its crowning dish being the Bonuan Bangus (milkfish). Aside from this, they serve all the old Filipino favorites, from chicharon to lechon kawali and lechon manok.

**A Great Hangout**

More than other Dagupan city bars and cafes, this joint is really the place to meet people. Punters get together around the dart board and the live acoustic music, which features sets from great local bands such as Segunda Band, Yvan and the Beanstalks, and the reggae group Sayote Band. There are also regular beer-drinking contests with a wide range of prizes. As if that isn't enough, you can socialize virtually with the free Wifi connection.

**Where Is It?**

The Hall of Fame is so popular it even justifies running its own website where photos can be uploaded by customers, and a regular blog by Simon keeps everyone updated on how things at this top-rated example of Dagupan bars and cafes are going. Its address: is 2/F Orient Pacific Center, Perez Boulevard, on the corner of Rizal Street. You can also call (075) 523-3263.
