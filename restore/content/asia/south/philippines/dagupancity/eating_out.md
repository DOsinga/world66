# Eating Out in Dagupan City

*Asia > South > Philippines > Dagupancity > Eating Out*

**Dagupan City restaurants **provide you with decent meals at reasonable prices. In Dagupan City, you can find all the best seafood dishes including the local specialty, bangus.

#### **Bangus Specialty**

Silverio&rsquo;s Restaurant is a popular restaurant in Dagupan City because of its most delectable Bangus dishes. The restaurant serves local Filipino food. There is a wide array of food to choose from, but bangus is the specialty of this restaurant and also the most famous product in the city. Silverio's serves the most delicious bangus at a low price. At around 500 pesos, you are already full and satisfied.

#### **A Restaurant by the Bridge**

Dowell Bridge Restaurant is near the river and serves all kinds of seafoods like bangus and shrimp. Aside from the seafood, they also have pork, chicken and beef dishes. All these are cooked Filipino style. Dowell Bridge Restaurant serves excellent food, and they also have a nice ambiance. In the evenings, you will enjoy the most atmosphere because there are live singers and musicians. The music is soothing to the customers. This restaurant is also a good place for you to hang out with friends. You drink Filipino beer while you are chatting. Try the Kilawen, a dish made of fresh fish. With a great food and a perfect ambiance, the restaurant is reasonably priced. In 2010, the price of the meal is less than $10 USD.
