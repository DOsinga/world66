# When to Go in Dagupan City

*Asia > South > Philippines > Dagupancity > When To Go*

**Dagupan City: when to go** is the first question you should ask before you plan for a trip. Dagupan City is well-known all over the Philippines because of its Bangus Festival. Visitors should consider the average temperatures and amount of precipitation prior to planning a trip so you can visit this wonderful city at the best and most comfortable time.

#### **Chill on April**

April is the best time for the Filipinos and foreigners to plan their travel to Dagupan. This is a dry season in the Philippines, so you are not worried about getting wet as you travel around the city. It is also the time when the people of Dagupan celebrate the annual Bangus Festival. This festival is popular all throughout the Philippines. There are a lot of activities during the festival to make your visit a memorable one. Visitors from outside of the Philippines should keep in mind the temperatures in April are among the highest of the year.

#### **Remember December**

December is also a good time to visit Dagupan. This is the month when the people of Dagupan celebrate their city fiesta. The Dagupan City Fiesta happens yearly every 16 to 30 days of December to commemorate the greatness of their patron, St. John the Evangelist. December is one of the cooler and drier months in Dagupan, so the weather will be nice to see the city.
