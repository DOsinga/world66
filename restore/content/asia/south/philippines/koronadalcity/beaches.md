# Beaches in Koronadal City

*Asia > South > Philippines > Koronadalcity > Beaches*

If your summer vacation traveling takes you to Koronadal City, then there are plenty of **Koronadal City beaches **where you can spend time soaking up the sun. Whether you are traveling alone, with family or friends or on a business trip there is the perfect beach for you. Koronadal is popularly known to it's locals as Marbel and is the capital city of the South Cotabato province. Within this city you will find a huge variety of different cultures, influences, dialects and indigenous traditions that can only be found in Koronadal City.

**Mindanai Beach**

This beach edges crystal clear blue waters that sparkle like sapphires, and the sands that surround it are clean and a soft white that make this beach a beautiful place to spend some time in. Tall palm trees and quaint little grass huts decorate the shores of this wonderful summer vacation spot, giving this beach the perfect atmosphere for any occasion. For the complete travel package you can stay at one of the many beach resorts that speckle the surrounding area. The Sarangani Bay Beach Resort is a popular spot for tourists to spend their time, and offers a variety of rooms.

**Puerto Galera**

The name of this beach literally means "Port of Galleons" and it initially became popular among the seafaring travelers during the times when galleon trade was popular--sometime in the beginning of the 10th century. It came equipped with it's own natural harbor, which until very recently was dubbed the most beautiful and safest harbor in the world. This beach is a popular place to scuba dive because of its diverse quantities of soft and hard corals, as well as the multi-colored tropical fish that call the coral home. The beautiful sands of this beach are surrounded by jungle and hills, giving this beach the perfect look of seclusion for those who prefer to feel as though they are in less-populated areas.

**More Puerto Galera**

On this beach there is the Coco Beach Island Resort which is a wonderful place to spend your time. This specific resort offers many high quality amenities that will make your trip to Puerto Galera especially memorable and comfortable including diving lessons, a shopping arcade, Cable Jeepney rides, swimming pools, a full service spa and a class to learn how to make natural coco soap. If this is a trip your children are going to join you on, there are many things especially catered to keeping them occupied as well which include an entire children's playground.
