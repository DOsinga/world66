# Festivals in Koronadal City

*Asia > South > Philippines > Koronadalcity > Festivals*

**Koronadal**** City**** festivals **reveal the city&rsquo;s rich culture, tradition, and beliefs. Koronadal City is a place in Mindanao that takes pride in its culture by having a festival to introduce the city.

**Dance During the Hinugyaw Festival**

The Hinugyaw Festival is a one-week celebration in the first week of January. There are many activities planned to make the occasion meaningful and colorful. Some of the activities include Celebrity Night, Band Explosion, a food fest, Search for Mr. and Ms. Koronadal, Marbele&ntilde;o Mardi Gras. Hinugyaw Festival ends with a major event, which is the Hinugyaw sa Dalan. This Hinugyaw sa Dalan is a street dancing competition wherein there are many contenders from all over the city and its neighboring town who go dancing around the city and compete with each other.

**Patron Fiesta, a Time to Show Reverence**

The people of Koronadal City celebrate the day of their patron saint St. Anthony de Padua in the month of June. This fiesta is done to thank the patron saint for giving them a bountiful blessing throughout the year.

**The Impressive Celebration of Tinalak Festival**

Tinalak Festival is held annually in the month of July to commemorate the foundation anniversary of the city. T&rsquo;nalak is a significant cloth in Koronadal City that serves as their cultural representation.

**Charter Anniversary to Commemorate the City Day**

Charter Anniversary is a commemoration of Koronadal&rsquo;s city-hood in the month of October. The activities during this celebration include a night fest, food and beer festival, concerts, and mardi gras.

**Hinugyaw sa Pascua, a Reason to Enjoy your Christmas**

Hinugyaw sa Pasco happens in the month of December. It is a night by night merriment that includes caroling and dancing. There is also a Christmas Bazaar in the city where you can buy items at low prices.
