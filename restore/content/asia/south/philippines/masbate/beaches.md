# Beaches in Masbate

*Asia > South > Philippines > Masbate > Beaches*

Masbate is one of the most popular island provinces in the Philippines, and tourists can find a number of **Masbate**** beaches** in and around the area.

**Boracay**** White Beach

**Located about 190 km from Masbate, this is a white sand beach that lies on the western coast of Boracay Island. The beach stretches for 5 km and has turquoise blue waters, making it one of the most popular destinations in Philippines. Tourists can enjoy a number of water sports at Boracay White Beach or simply soak up some sun. The beach also has a beachfront path which separates it from the bars, restaurants, shops, and hotels that line it.

**Malapascua

**About 127 km from Masbate is the island of Malapascua, which is also known as Little Boracay. The beaches on this island are supposed to be some of the most beautiful in the world. Bounty beach is one of the beaches on Malapascua, and is perfect for those who prefer a peaceful and quiet place. Visitors to this beach will only find a few dive shops and beach bars around the area. This makes the beach ideal for couples who would like to enjoy long walks on a beautiful beach.

**Puraran**** Beach

**Tourists looking for a little more adventure and water action should take a trip to Puraran Beach, situated about 169 km from Masbate. This beach offers some of the best surf in the Philippines. The surf here is good all through the year, but it is considered best from August to October. Even if you aren&rsquo;t a surfer, you can still enjoy other water activities like snorkeling and swimming, thanks to the extensive coral reefs around the beach. The beach boasts clean white sand, which is perfect for relaxing and sunbathing.

**Bituon**** Beach

**This beach is relatively small, and is located towards the south of Mobo. It is actually a private beach, but worth a visit for tourists looking for a picturesque place. The beach has a 1 km long stretch of white sand and crystal clear waters. Also located along the beach is an abundant green beach land, which adds to its beauty. Visitors can enjoy walking along this beautiful stretch of beach or take a swim in the pristine waters.
