# Getting Around in Quezon Province

*Asia > South > Philippines > Quezonprovince > Getting Around*

**Quezon Province Transportation** takes you to your destination anywhere in the province. Once you are in this place, you need not to be apprehensive of transferring from one place to another.

Quezon Province belongs to the Calabarzon region in Luzon. It is named after the former president of the Philippines, President Manuel L. Quezon. Quezon Province is composed of 40 municipalities and one city which is Lucena City, its capital.

**Exclusively for big Group Travelers**

A bus is a good choice as a mode of transportation for long travels. Quezon Province has 40 municipalities. And so, if you are going to travel in far distances, a bus will be comfortable for you. Moreover, it can carry approximately 50 travelers or more. So if you are going to travel as a big group and you do not want to separate, this is the best transportation that will provide comfortable as you have your fun journey in the whole province.

**The Universal Transportation**

The jeepney is the universal transportation in the Philippines. In Quezon Province itself, you can ride in a jeepney if you are going to transfer to a nearby town or municipality. You can also hire this jeepney when you want to go sightseeing. You just need to contact the driver and agree with the payment and the time and the day as when you are going to start the tour.

**Suitable for Short Distance Travels**

A tricycle is the type of transportation that is also used within the place. You can hire this transportation but be reminded that this is only for short distance travels. Moreover, this transportation loads only a group of five travelers or less.
