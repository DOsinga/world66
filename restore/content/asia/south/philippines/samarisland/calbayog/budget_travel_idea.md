# Budget Travel Ideas in Calbayog

*Asia > South > Philippines > Samarisland > Calbayog > Budget Travel Idea*

**Calbayog budget travel **is very easy since there are a lot of accommodations, restaurants and transportation options in the city that are cheap. Calbayog is the first class city in Samar, Philippines, and it is frequented by a lot of backpackers due to its natural attractions. However, when traveling to Calbayog, you need not break your bank as you can use these travel ideas to still enjoy a trip to Calbayog, even if you are running on a very tight budget.

**Budget Hotels**

There are a lot of hotels found all over Calbayog, but if you are on a tight budget, you need to consider checking in a cheap accommodation. One such accommodation where you can stay is the Eduardo&rsquo;s Tourist Hotel. The hotel has modest rooms that are air conditioned. The accommodations in the hotel cost $10 per night. The hotel is simple and for a budget traveler, this hotel is the best one that you can come across in Calbayog City.

**Pedal-Driven Cars**

There are a lot of transportation options that can help you get around the city easily. However, if you want to save money, then forget hiring a taxi cab, which has a starting fare of $1 for the first 4 km. The best transportation option that you can take is to ride the pedal-driven cars, which are also called tricycles. This transportation option is very cheap and you need to pay only $0.2 for the fare. However, while riding the tricycle*,* you need to be aware of unscrupulous drivers who charge $1 per ride.

**Fast Food Restaurants**

Fast Food restaurants are cheaper eating places in Calbayog. The best fast food restaurant where you can eat in Calbayog is at Jollibee, which is the Filipino counterpart of McDonald&rsquo;s. A complete meal of burgers and fries will cost you less than $2. Aside from eating fast foods, you can also eat at makeshift restaurants called carinderia, which are seen everywhere in Calbayog City. However, you need to make sure that you have a strong stomach since most of these restaurants are located near the streets where dusts and virtually anything can get into the pre-cooked food being sold.

**Public Beaches**

There are a lot of public beaches in Calbayog, thus you can always spend your time frolicking under the sun or swimming in the clear waters of the beach without worrying about spending too much.
