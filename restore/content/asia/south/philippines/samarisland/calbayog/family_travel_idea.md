# Family Travel Ideas in Calbayog

*Asia > South > Philippines > Samarisland > Calbayog > Family Travel Idea*

If you are taking your entire family for **Calbayog family travel**, then you need to plan activities that your entire family will enjoy. Calbayog is a first class city located in the province of Samar, Philippines, and this city is famous for its rustic villages located in the outskirts of the city as well as its mile-long beaches. For your whole family to enjoy a trip to Calbayog, here are some of the activities that you need to include in your travel itinerary.

**The Beach**

Beaches in Calbayog appeal to any type of tourist. Thus, the best way to spend your trip in Calbayog is to visit the many beach resorts in the city. Such beaches that you can visit are the Malajog Beach and Bagacay Beach, which are located in close proximity to the city proper. The beaches are a favorite spot for locals and tourists who want to have a picnic under the sun. While you are basking under the sun, your children can enjoy the different water-related sports like snorkeling and beach volleyball.

**The Giant Mawacat Slide**

If you want your entire family to have a great adventure in Calbayog, then you should not miss out on trying the giant natural slide in Barangay Mawacat. This slide is a natural spoon-like rock formation, which is inclined by 45 degrees and is 50 meters long. The rock is smoothened out by flowing water. You can slide this 50-meter cistern and land on an inviting pool below.

**Binaliw Isle**

If you want to have a memorable vacation, then you ought to island hop from the mainland Calbayog to the Binaliw Isle. Your whole family will surely love looking at the diverse marine wildlife marooned at the shore during low tide such as star fishes and coral fishes.

**The Samar Archaeological Museum**

While touring Calbayog, it is important that your entire family also learn about the history of the city and the best place to do it is to visit the Samar Archaeological Museum and Research Center located in Christ the King College. This is the only museum in the city. and it displays old artifacts from old civilizations that flourished in Calbayog.

**Falls Hopping in Calbayog**

Calbayog has a lot of waterfalls, thus another great way to spend your family trip in this city is to visit the many waterfalls that lie in the area, such as the Pan-as Falls and Tabokno Falls.
