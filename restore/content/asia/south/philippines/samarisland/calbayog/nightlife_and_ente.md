# Nightlife and Entertainment in Calbayog

*Asia > South > Philippines > Samarisland > Calbayog > Nightlife And Ente*

**Calbayog nightlife** usually involves hanging out in simple bars and pubs while drinking local beer. Calbayog City is a first-class city located in the island of Samar, and the locals seem to enjoy the local nightlife and entertainment by visiting the many cheap pubs and bars that dot the city. Here are some of the clubs and bars where visitors can enjoy the nightlife in Calbayog.

**The Movie House**

As the name implies, this bar used to be a movie house once, but has been renovated to become a disco and a concert hall for local artists. The bar is very cheap, as the entrance and drinks only cost $5. The bar offers live and upbeat music performed by local and popular bands in the country.

**Cobra&rsquo;s Den**

It is located on the main highway of Calbayog City, so tourists cannot miss it. This bar boasts a friendly staff and crew that will cater to the needs of their customers. The bar also offers karaoke for customers who want to sing their hearts out. This is a perfect place to stop over for tourists traveling to the different parts of Samar. Food and drinks cost around $10.

**Kamalig Music Lounge**

This pub is located in Barangay Rawis and offers cozy ambiance and live music performed by local artists. Most visitors spend time in this bar not only for pica-pica (finger food) and drinks, but also to mingle with the locals. The bar has an average-sized dance floor where guests can dance their night away with the music performed by the local band. A night out with friends in this bar usually cost between $5 and $10, which is reasonably cheap.

**Estudio**

This bar only offers live acoustic music, pica-pica and drinks. Unfortunately, there is no dance floor in the bar, but this does not curtail the fun that tourists want to have while visiting the bar. The bar boasts of the many selections of pica-pica on the menu. Moreover, this place is perfect for those who want to spend their evening talking with friends and just relaxing.

**Manuwel&rsquo;s Music Lounge**

Located in Barangay Obrero, this bar offers music-all-night fun to all of its customers. The bar features live bands playing upbeat and international songs while visitors drink local beer and other cocktails, and you can even request to have your favorite songs played. A night out in this bar usually costs between $5 and $10.
