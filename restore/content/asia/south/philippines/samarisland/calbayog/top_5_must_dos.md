# Top 5 Must Do's in Calbayog

*Asia > South > Philippines > Samarisland > Calbayog > Top 5 Must Dos*

When touring through the Philippines on your next vacation stop off in Calbayog and enjoy these 5 **Calbayog Must Do&rsquo;s**. Calbayog is located on the western side of Samar Island and is the third largest city in the Philippines.

#### 1. Spend a Day at Malajog Beach

Malajog Beach is the most popular beach in Calbayog. The sand is grayish in color, the water as blue as the sky and the palm trees sway back and forth in the breeze. This is definitely a beautiful beach to spend the afternoon at.

#### 2.Visit the Waterfalls

Calbayog has many waterfalls, but Bangon-Bugtong Falls is the most impressive. As you follow up the stream, you will come to a large circular pond at the base of this waterfall. The pond is big enough to swim in and deep enough to dive into. Other waterfalls include Pan-As Falls, Ton-ok Falls, Tobokno Falls and Lologayan Falls. Each one of these is a beautiful natural wonder to be visited and appreciated.

#### 3. Go Caving

If you like caves, Calbayog offers an assortment of caves to explore. Danao cave, Guinogo-An cave, Bologno cave and Tinogo-An cave are the most popular caves to explore. Danao cave is made up of 7 interior structures. Various snakes and bats call this cave home, so if you are not uneasy when it comes to snakes and bats, this cave is an awesome place to visit.

#### 4. Slide Down the Mawacat Slide

The Mawacat Slide is a 50 meter long natural slide in Calbayog. Its moss-covered tube carries water from the top to the waiting pool below. Gliding down this natural water slide is extraordinary. The pool at the bottom is refreshingly cool and large enough to enjoy a quick swim before climbing to the top of the Mawacat Slide again.

#### 5. Take a Relaxing Soak in the Mapaso Hot Spring

The Mapaso Spring is literally a hot spring. Mapaso means &ldquo;hot&rdquo; and the temperatures of this spring reach to almost boiling. Some say that the crustaceans that live in this spring are pink because the heat of the water cooks them. The water soothes and relaxes away aches and pains, and some locals believe the water can remove many skin problems. There are picnic cottages around the pool so it is easy to spend a whole day here just relaxing.

If you are considering travelling through Calbayog on your next trip to the Philippines, stop and enjoy the local wonders of nature. With the caves, waterfalls and hot-spring, you may never want to leave.
