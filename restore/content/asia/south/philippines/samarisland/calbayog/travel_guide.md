# Travel Guide in Calbayog

*Asia > South > Philippines > Samarisland > Calbayog > Travel Guide*

Use this **Calbayog travel guide **to enjoy holidays here. Calbayog is located in the western region of Samar, and it's known for beautiful scenery, waterfalls, caves and lots of water activities.

**Accessibility **

You can reach Calbayog by taking the Asian Spirit flight from Manila. It flies three days a week. You can also take a bus from Catarman and Catbalogan, which offers daily service via Allen. Jeepneys can also be good option if you want to enjoy the scenic beauty. Jeepneys drop you at Capoocan terminal from where you can take a tricycle to reach the town.

**Accommodations**

Calbayog city offers a number of hotels and resorts for all types of budgets. You can choose to stay in Eduardo's Tourist Hotel, Central Inn and Resort, Bagacay Beach Resort or Sigma Lodge, to name just a few. Your vacation is best experienced by staying at one of the resorts on or near the beach. This will give you a chance to explore the beautiful beaches or even have a great family picnic on the beach.

**Popular Destinations**

Calbayog is known as the city of waterfalls. You can discover lots of beautiful scenery and caves in this untouched city. Visiting Guinogoan Cave, located in the fringes of Brgy, can be a real adventure for you. Other popular destinations include Malopalo Stone Marble Mountain, Mapaso Spring, Malajog Beach, Bugtong Falls and the Calbayog Cathedral.

**Nightlife and Entertainment**

Calbayog does not offer much in terms of nightlife and entertainment. However, you can find few places where you can enjoy the whole night. You can use tricycles, pedicabs and private vehicles to reach these places. You can go to the movie house and spend less than a dollar. The movie houses are open till late at night and organize live events and music. The people of Calbayog are music lovers. You can enjoy great music evenings with drinks at the Kamalig Music Lounge, Marleen's Music Lounge and Manuwel's Music Lounge.

**Dining
**

Make sure to taste the local Filipino food and other cuisines available at the restaurants and food stalls. You can enjoy a great meal at Bread Mix and Cakes, Rebelito's Refreshment, Bernardo's Refreshment, Mister Donut, Orange Brutus and Cantilena. One of the best things to do while on vacation is to enjoy the local cuisine. Every destination has something special, and Calbayog is no exception.
