# Festivals in San Pablo City

*Asia > South > Philippines > Sanpablocity > Festivals*

If you are traveling to the Philippines and want to see some great entertainment and culture, you can enjoy the **San Pablo City festivals**. The city is home to many unique and interesting festivals each year that draw in numbers of people from all over.

#### Lao New Year Water Festival

This festival is an annual event in San Pablo and takes place in May. The event is a great way to experience the culture of San Pablo and celebrate the Lao New Year. There is great food, attire, art, dance, and music that you can enjoy at the festival.

#### Coco Festival Celebration

The Coco Festival in San Pablo City brings in performers and entertainment from all over. Each year the festival is held in January and lasts for a week to allow everyone plenty of time to enjoy the food, fun and music that makes the festival bring in people and tourists to see the culture. Each year the festival celebrates the coconut which is one of the largest industries in the city and provides much of the income for the area. There is an array of festivities that are included in this festival and a great time to visit the city.

#### Mayflower Festival

The Mayflower Festival is held in May annual and is a traditional festival that celebrates Flores de Maria. Before the ordering of the flowers, there is a parade in the streets and plenty of costumes and celebrating as the parade goes through the streets of the town. The festival provides plenty of culture and tradition as the girls and ladies adorn themselves in beautiful gowns for the parade and enjoy the beauty of the flowers and decorations.

#### Festivals of Light

The Festivals of Light is a Christmas festival that is held annually in December. It is one of the great San Pablo City festivals to enjoy and many of the residents display colorful displays on their houses and there are many strings of lights that are found throughout the city for everyone to enjoy and in celebration of the holiday the city provides a city wide festival for all residents and those who are visiting in the city.

#### October Festival

The October Festival is also the market by the lake and is held in October annually in San Pablo city. The festival celebrates the commerce and trade for the city and there are many displays and exhibits to enjoy in the city and a great time to see the city.
