# Day Trips in Tacloban City

*Asia > South > Philippines > Taclobancity > Day Trips*

People planning a vacation in the Philippines should consider **Tacloban day trips**. Tacloban rose to significance because of its unique location. Tacloban lies along the most important international, trading route of the Philippines, along the southern border of Manila. This is why Tacloban was the first Philippines city to be classified as a &lsquo;Highly Urbanized City&rsquo;. People can consider the following when planning their Tacloban day trips:

**Marabut**

Marabut is a small island, located a short distance away from Tacloban. Here, visitors can spend time at the Marabut Marine Park and the neighboring beach. There are many islands in the vicinity. Tour operators offer short trips that are aimed at exploring these smaller, unknown islands. An essential part of this tour is visiting the towering, bizarre formations along some of the islands. Those who are a bit adventurous can individually explore the beach coves. At the beach, visitors can expect top-class instructors for teaching them the basics of aqua sports, like snorkeling and kayaking.

**Sohoton
**

Any Tacloban itinerary is incomplete without visiting the Sohoton Caves. This site has now been converted into a national park. Here, visitors can participate in guided tours through exotic and somewhat-daunting cave formations. These are massive, stalactite/limestone caves. For reaching the park, cheap boat-rides are available throughout the day from Tacloban. After visiting the caves, visitors can kayak their way to the nearby, Sohoton Natural Bridge. This site is historically significant, being home to relics that are many thousand-years old. Further, visitors can walk down to the Basey Church that was built during the colonial, Spanish in the 17th century.
