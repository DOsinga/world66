# Nightlife and Entertainment in Tacloban City

*Asia > South > Philippines > Taclobancity > Nightlife And Ente*

There are a lot of ways to enjoy **Tacloban City nightlife.** In fact, the best way to have fun at night in Tacloban City is by visiting the many entertainment areas in this capital city of Leyte, a small island in the Philippines. Here are some of the best bars and entertainment places in Tacloban City.

**Inzomnia**

This bar, located on Real Street, is jam packed during Friday and Saturday nights. This bar is frequented not only by tourists but also by the young and yuppie locals, and is a place where hip parties happen every night. Inzomnia has its own DJ, who plays only the hippest music. The bar serves food as well as drinks at reasonable prices, from $10 to $15.

**M/V Schooner Night Club**

This club is very interesting because it looks like a ship from the outside, which is how it got its name. It is located just a few meters away from Inzomnia. This club boasts attractive ladies-in-waiting, who usually serve the food and drinks. Alcoholic drinks at this club will cost you about $10.

**After 8 Music Lounge**

Located in Kalipayan Road near the center of the city, this bar only offers live music. The staff are cordial, and they serve with smiles. The bar is average in quailty, and has refurbished furniture, but although this is the case, it is still a great place to hang out for people who do not want to spend their evenings in bars that play loud music. The food and entertainment cost from $5 to $10 here.

**Tavern Videoke Bar**

For tourists who want to experience a unique night out in Tacloban City, the Tavern Bar is the place to be. It is located inside the Leyte Park Hotel, and offers customers a public singing booth where they can sing their hearts out. Tourists need not have a good singing voice to qualify for singing in front of the booth; all they need is the confidence to sing in public. The bar also has several pool tables for those who do not want to expose their singing to the public.

**Andreleys Restobar**

The bar is very spacious, and offers a wide dance floor that can accommodate 200 individuals. The bar also serves good food and great entertainment for a price of $10 to $15. The bar also has its own pool table, DJ and karaoke room.
