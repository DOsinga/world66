# When to Go in Tacloban City

*Asia > South > Philippines > Taclobancity > When To Go*

If you are going on a family vacation to **Tacloban city**,** when to go **is what you need to decide first. Tacloban is a highly urbanized city. The name of the city was taken from a bamboo device that fishermen used to catch crabs, shrimps of fish which were locally called taklob. Soon, these fishermen began to call the place Takloban, to denote the place where they left their taklob to catch seafood. Here are reasons why you should visit Tacloban in June.

**Climate and Weather**

Generally, the climate of the Philippines is just the wet and dry season. In Tacloban City, the wettest months are November and December; however, rainfall is fairly distributed all the year round. Along with the rain, the months of December and January are quite cool compared to the rest of the months. For the months of May to June, temperature can be as high as 31.45 degrees Celsius. The months of April and May experience the least amount of rainfall.

**Pintados-Kasadyaan Festival**

This festival is usually held on June 29th of each year. Thus, in order to experience this one of a kind festival, it is best to visit Tcloban city in the later part of June. This festivity has cultural and religious roots, because it is actually based on the tattooed and painted warriors of the past, pintados. The celebration is highlighted by ritual dances and parades that happen on the main streets of the city.
