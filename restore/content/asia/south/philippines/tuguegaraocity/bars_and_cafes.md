# Bars and Cafes in Tuguegarao City

*Asia > South > Philippines > Tuguegaraocity > Bars And Cafes*

The city of Tuguegarao is the capital of Cagayan, Philippines. **Tuguegarao City bars and cafes **have been revitalized since the 1990&rsquo;s and provide a vibrant social scene.

#### **Tuguegarao Social Scene**

Tuguegarao is a smaller city but large enough to enjoy an active social scene. The city was originally found by missionaries in 17th century, and it just celebrated its 400-year anniversary. The bars in the city offer great drinks and dancing for the patrons. Visitors will find a bar for every occasion. For the business traveler, there are a number of elegant bars in the hotels. For people looking for causal settings, they can hit any of the bars in the main drag and enjoy a beer. Tuguegarao also has a vibrant gay scene. There are several excellent gay bars for visitors to enjoy. No matter the venue, customers will enjoy the scene and get a chance to meet other travelers and locals. Cafes are another good way to meet people. For smokers, some of the cafes have indoor sitting areas for rainy weather. Another benefit of the cafes is Wi-Fi access. Many of the hotels still do not have Internet access, and the ones that do mainly have access in the lobby. The cafes offer Wi-Fi access for customers, which is especially helpful for business travelers.
