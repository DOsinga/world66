# Festivals in Tuguegarao City

*Asia > South > Philippines > Tuguegaraocity > Festivals*

**Tuguegarao city festivals **show a sense of pride and harmony. Their ultimate goal for celebrating these festivals is not only to showcase the tradition and culture of their people but to unite all the people of Tuguegarao regardless of their differences.

**Pay-vurulun**

The Pav-vurulun festival is an annual patronal fiesta in Tuguegarao City. This is a one-week celebration where each day is filled with meaningful activities. The people of Tuguegarao City do a lot of preparation for this festival. Some of the activities are beauty pageants, pancit-eating contests, street dancing and a battle of the bands. The street dancing and the battle of the bands are the main events. Before the street dancing contest, you may see the participants practicing for their dances. Some of them search the fields and forests of Tuguegarao City to get the materials they can use in making their costumes. The word &ldquo;Pav-vurulun&rdquo; means a sense of belonging, and so this festival truly brings people of Tuguegarao City close to one another.

**Maskota**

Maskota is a well-known wedding dance in Tuguegarao City. The term "Maskota" refers to the style of the skirt of a woman who performs the Maskota dance. As this is a wedding dance, the participants in this festival are married couples or partners in a wedding party. This is an amusing celebration that makes the audience giggle as they see the wedding partners dancing.
