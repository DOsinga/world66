# Things to Do in Tuguegarao City

*Asia > South > Philippines > Tuguegaraocity > Things To Do*

There are plenty of activities that you can include in your **Tuguegarao City things to do** list. Tuguegarao is a first-class city in the Philippines and is located in the northern part of the country. It is surrounded with a lot of geographical landmarks and the Sierra Madre mountain range is among them. For this reason, Tuguegarao is the best place to do nature trips. Here are some of the things that you must include in your to do list while in this city.

**Spelunking in Callao Caves**

The outskirts of the city is where you can see the 7-chambered Callao Caves. You can arrange at the local tourism board or at the hotel where you are staying for a spelunking tour in the cave for as low as $25. Usually, the tour guides will secure the harness, helmets and head lamp for you.

**Kayaking on the Pinacanauan River**

You will find the Pinacanauan River near the Callao Caves. After you had your fill of the scenic stalagmites and stalactites in Callao Caves, then you should head back to the Pinacanauan River for a kayaking adventure. There are a lot of kayaking stations located along the shores of the river where you can rent the equipment for as low as $20.

**Visit Religious Sites**

The city has a lot of churches as pilgrimage sites for you to visit and renew your faith. However, if you are not a Christian, you can still marvel at the interesting architecture of the churches in Tuguegarao. These churches include Saint Peter&rsquo;s Cathedral and Saint Paul&rsquo;s Cathedral. You can also climb the Iguig Calvary Hills to see larger-than-life statues of the Mount Calvary scenes as well as get a good panoramic view of the city.

**Ride the Calesa**

Although the city is already considered a first-class city in the Philippines, the calesa, a horse-drawn wagon, is still the major form of transportation. Experience riding this horse-drawn wagon as the driver maneuvers you to the modern business districts as well as the old streets of the city.

**Shop for Ibanag Delicacies**

The attractions in Tuguegarao are not the only things that they are famous for. In fact, the city is also popular for its Ibanag delicacies. Delicacies such as pastillas, longganisa, Carabao&rsquo;s milk and tablea, which is a local bitter chocolate, are very popular among locals and tourists alike.
