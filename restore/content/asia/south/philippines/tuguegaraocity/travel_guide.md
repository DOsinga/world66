# Travel Guide in Tuguegarao City

*Asia > South > Philippines > Tuguegaraocity > Travel Guide*

Having a **Tuguegarao City Travel Guide** will help you decide on where to stay and what to do while on vacation. Tuguegarao City is located in the Cagayan Valley, where it also happens to be the capital of the province Cagayan. Tuguegarao City is known for its outdoor activities more than it is known for its nightlife. Whether you are looking for a kayaking trip or a cave to explore you will be sure to find it in Tuguegarao City.

**Accommodations**

If you are looking for a great place to stay you will need to check out Hotel Roma. Many people enjoy staying here because it is the biggest hotel in Tuguegarao City, but it is also in a great location because it is close to many restaurants. The Ivory Hotel is another great place to stay if you are going to be in the northern part of town, Ivory Hotel rooms start around $13 a night for two people.

**Eating Out**

While in Tuguegarao City you are going to have to take the time to sample some of the local cuisine. One of the best ways to start off is sampling the various breads and pastries that places have to offer. One bakery to visit is the Crown Bakeshop, which is located right next to the Roma Hotel. When stopping in be sure to try the cheese rolls and chicken ala king roll. If you happen to stop in around lunch, the clubhouse sandwich is highly recommended. For dinner you can visit Kainang Pilipino Restaurant where the Crispy Pata is considered the house special.

**Things To Do**

If you are interested in outdoor activities you will need to go kayaking on the Pinacanauan River, but be prepared for at least an hour's ride down the river. Perhaps the most memorable thing to do in Tuguegarao City is spelunking, which the Callao Caves are among the most popular. If you do decide to go explore the caves you want to ensure you do not go after 4 p.m. during monsoon season because it gets dangerous really fast.

**Getting Around**

Getting to Tuguegarao City can be done by airplane or bus. If you are going to take the bus it is recommended that you use the Florida Bus Lines, but make sure it is a sleeper bus. This line is recommended because of the comfort of the seats as they recline all the way back. The drive into Tuguegarao City is long from any point of entry. Florida Bus Lines is a bit more expensive, but the trip in should only cost about $21. The best way to get anywhere in town is by Jeepney because they can travel anywhere and transport large loads.

**Shopping**

Being that Tuguegarao City is quickly becoming a tourist area more and more shopping opportunities are presenting themselves. If you do decide to walk around the town to see what all of the little shops have to offer, two things that you need to purchase are at least one pack of milk candy and some Longganisa, which is simply pork sausage.
