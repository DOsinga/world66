# Beaches in Pusan

*Asia > South > Pusan > Beaches*

A holiday in Pusan must be enjoyed by spending a couple of days at some of the beautiful **Pusan beaches**. Pusan has great weather all year long which makes it perfect for a day out at the beach.

**Gwangalli Beach **

This is one of the most popular beaches in Pusan, Korea. Gwangalli Beach is a 14-km-long white sand beach situated in Busan-si Suyeong-gu Gwangan 2-dong. The beach is under the local government&rsquo;s water cleaning program so you are assured that you will enjoy fine white sand and crystal clear water every time. You can rent a yacht and enjoy a day sailing with your family and friends or engage in water sport activities like jet skiing, scuba diving, snorkeling and windsurfing. This beach is a perfect spot if you want to enjoy a day of fun-filled activity.

**Songjeong Beach**

Songjeong Beach is another well-known beach in Pusan and it is located in Haeundae-gu, Songjeong-dong. This white sand beach is more than a km long and has shallow waters, making it a great family-friendly beach. You can explore the evergreen groves of Jukdo or go sailing with your family. Songiljeong Pavilion is a place where you can experience the greatest sunrise and moonrise views in all of Pusan. Songjeong Beach is also home to the annual Songjeong Beach Festival and the Harvest Full-Moon Seaweed Festival.

**Haeundae Beach**

This is no doubt the most famous beach in Pusan. Haeundae Beach is known for the "Hae Un Dae" stone carving made by Choi Chi-Won of the Silla Dynasty. This is a one-and-a-half km beach situated in Haeundae-gu U1-dong which features a beach library and cultural center. This beach serves as the venue for special cultural events and annual festivities. You can enjoy a picnic at this beach with your family.

**Songdo Beach**

If you want to enjoy a less crowded and more serene beach experience you can go to Songdo Beach. This beach is located in the southern part of Pusan. The beach is smaller compared to the other popular beaches like the Haeundae Beach but it offers the same quality white sand and pristine sea waters. This is where most of the locals in Pusan go to relax and enjoy a day out at the beach.

**Ilgwang Beach **

Ilgwang Beach, on the other hand, offers a more traditional atmosphere than the other beaches in Pusan. The sands here are darker and the water is a bit warmer. This beach is not so crowded and offers a comforting environment. Here you can enjoy water sports like snorkeling and diving.
