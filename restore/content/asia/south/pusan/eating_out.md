# Eating Out in Pusan

*Asia > South > Pusan > Eating Out*

Pusan, the Korean pronunciation of the place Busan in South Korea, is a beautiful place attracting tourists from all over the world, and given below are some of the **Pusan Restaurants**. Given the inflow of tourists, Pusan has several restaurants serving various cuisines.

**Seamen's Club**

The Seaman's club is a very famous restaurant in Pusan. Located near the port close to the Pusan station, this restaurant specializes in western cuisines, and thus is thronged by homesick travelers and sailors. Choose from the club sandwich, steak or other American cuisines for lunch. The food here is very affordable and delicious. The restaurant has a bar where you can relax with your drink after a hectic day in the city. If you are very particular about the ambiance of a restaurant, then this is not the place for you, as Seamen&rsquo;s club is a no frills restaurant.

**Amby's**

Amby's s another restaurant in Pusan that is a favorite hang out for expatriates, European tourists and seafarers. The multi lingual staff makes you feel at home at Amby's. You can choose from European cuisines or authentic Korean delicacies at Amby's. They have an excellent choice of drinks too. On your visit to Amby&rsquo;s, do try out the Spanish Brandy which is highly recommended. The rates are very reasonable, and they offer you great food and service.
