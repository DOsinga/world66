# Festivals in Pusan

*Asia > South > Pusan > Festivals*

**Pusan festivals** always deliver a world-class event to visitors. Pusan, a city in South Korea, has been hosting international events for years, and never failed to please.

**Pusan**** International Film Festival**

Pusan International Film Festival (PIFF) is the biggest film festival in Asia. It started in the year 1996. With the help of the local and international film experts and the enthusiasm of the audience, this festival has been a success. This is considered as the very first international film festival that has taken place in Korea. The aim of producing this film festival is to create a film that will reflect the Korean culture, and also, to introduce the Korean cinema in the film business internationally.

**Fireworks Festival**

Pusan Fireworks Festival is a world-class festival. Witnessing this occasion will for sure leave a long-lasting mark in your memory. This festival includes the showcasing of 85,000 fireworks and lasers with the integration of flamboyant music. In addition to the spectacular scene that you will enjoy, there are also amenities in the area that include a place to stay and a place to eat for the audience.

**Busan Biennale**

Busan Biennale is a complete art celebration that combines three different festivals such as Busan Sea Festival, Busan Youth Biennale, and Busan Outdoor Sculpture Symposium. This festival is a venue where the locals can interact with people from other countries and converse with each other.

**Jagalchi Festival**

Jagalchi Festival is the largest seafood festival in South Korea. It is held every October at the Jagalchi Market. Among the events during this festival are the reenactments of traditional fishing folk traditions, free tours around Taejongdae and Songdo Islands, and visit to the Jagalchi Fish Market.
