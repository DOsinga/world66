# Shopping in Pusan

*Asia > South > Pusan > Shopping*

You might want to make a shopping list as you head out to experience the **Pusan shopping **scene while vacationing in Pusan. Here are a few interesting places to check out.

**Pusan University**

This is the best place to find trendy and unique fashion pieces that come with affordable price tags. The Pusan University station is divided into two major sections. The first section is for the branded stores and branded discount stores. This section is situated in the front part of the university. You can also find Korean fashion shops that offer trendy fashion pieces at discounted prices. The second section is for mainstream fashion pieces and stores that caries their own designs and creations. Here you will surely find exceptional and one-of-a-kind fashion pieces. You are bound to splurge as you shop at this place, which is good as far as shopping goes.

**Gukje Market**

This is a colorful and vibrant street market situated in downtown Pusan. Gukje Market is a popular shopping destination for locals and tourist alike especially if you want to pick up some great items. There are hundreds of stores that line the Gukje Market which offer almost everything, from basic necessities to one-of a-kind pieces. You will be able to buy home appliances, gadgets, jewelry, clothes, shoes, bags, trinkets and locally made handicrafts. The market is open every day except on the first and third Sundays of the month.

**SeoMyon District**

SeoMyon is Pusan&rsquo;s major commercial district and is located right in the center of Pusan. Here you will find several stores that sell a variety of items. There are over 600 shops around the SeoMyon District which sell jewelry, gadgets, clothing, accessories, shoes, bags and other crafts. You will also be able to fine several local and international brands available at many of the shops. This district also features an underground arcade that is jam-packed with shoppers all year round. SeoMyon is easily accessible through the SeoMyon Subway Station.

**Paradise Duty Free**

Take your shopping experience a few notches up by shopping at the Paradise Duty Free Department Store, in Haeundau-gu. This is a four-storey mall which features dozens of stores, boutiques, food shops and entertainment centers. You can buy branded clothes, shoes, bags, jewelry, perfumes and tailored suits at this store.

**Lotte**

Lotte is an exclusive upscale store that sells branded clothing for both men and women. The store can be compared to Macy&rsquo;s in the U.S. You can also find Lotte all over South Korea. This store offers high quality formal suits, dresses, casual wear, office wear, shoes and bags, as well as intricate accessories.
