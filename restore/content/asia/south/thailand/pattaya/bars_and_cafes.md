# Bars and Cafes in Pattaya

*Asia > South > Thailand > Pattaya > Bars And Cafes*

Pattaya offers total entertainment for travelers who want to try the famous **Pattaya Bars and Cafes**. Spend a perfect beach holiday in Pattaya with its beautiful beaches or do some serious shopping here.

**Den Bar**

Den Bar, which is in Pattayaland Soi 1, offers beers 24 hours a day at reasonable prices. You will not get bored because the owner and staff will keep you entertained. They have a wide choice of local as well as imported beer and spirits. A large pool table awaits those who want to play against the bar staff. It is conveniently located near the beach and just minutes away from the main nightlife scene.

**Shamrock Bar and Restaurant**

The bar located, in Soi Payyaland Two, Pattaya Beach, is famous for its inexpensive but delicious meals. It always offer authentic Thai food with Thai Stew as an all-time favorite among loyal customers. Breakfasts come in three categories: light, regular and full English breakfasts. If you like eating breakfast around midnight, the owner will gladly give in to your request. The bar also serves cold beers and the bar is always open for a fun, relaxed night.

**Ace Caf&eacute;**

Ace Caf&eacute;, in Jomtien Beach, is a popular choice among tourists and expats for its English home-cooked meals served in big portions. Ace Cafe offers a good choice of English favorites, like the monster burgers, mashed potatoes, superb steaks and pork chops with the cafe's signature applesauce. If you crave for Thai food, the cafe serves it in huge portions, too. Though this is more of a restaurant than a bar, the cafe still serves icy-cold beers.
