# Getting Around in Pattaya

*Asia > South > Thailand > Pattaya > Getting Around*

Travelers who frequent Pattaya for a nice vacation would find **Pattaya Transportation** easily available. Getting around is not difficult and you can find a taxi, motorbike or take the bus anytime if you want to go to the beach or visit the popular bars and restaurants.

**Songthaew**

Songthaew or baht buses is a popular mode of transportation in Pattaya; it is a dark blue colored pick-up truck with metal bars at the sides and with comfortable benches at the back Fare within Pattaya is 5 baht, but tourists usually pay double or 10 baht. It follows a regular route, but you can pay extra if you want to charter a songthaew. The white-colored songthaew travels through the main Bangkok highways and airport and cost around 20 baht.

**Motorbike Taxi**

Motorbike taxis in Pattaya are easily identifiable with the drivers wearing colorful vests. If you need one, you can flag them down anywhere on the streets or you can check the street corners for some drivers playing checkers while waiting for customers. Fare is usually between 30 to 40 baht, cheaper than renting a songthaew. Locals usually use the motorbike taxis for short distances around town.

**Bus**

Bus services are available in Pattaya with trips as early as 6 a.m. daily. Fare is 30 Baht for one way. You can also buy a ticket worth 90 Baht good for one day. For frequent travelers that prefer to ride the bus, you can purchase tickets good for three days for only 180 Baht or a month&rsquo;s worth of travel for only 900 Baht.
