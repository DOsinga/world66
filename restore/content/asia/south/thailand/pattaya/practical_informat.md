# Practical Information in Pattaya

*Asia > South > Thailand > Pattaya > Practical Informat*

This **Pattaya Practical Information** guide will help you immensely in planning your holiday. Pattaya is located at a distance of 165 kilometers from Bangkok and is a major tourist destination. This fishing town has transformed into a full-blown commercial paradise. Read on to get more information on Pattaya.

**Climate**

The climate in Pattaya is similar to rest of Thailand. The annual monsoon season starts in the month of May and goes on till October. The rains are extremely heavy during these months. Hence the months between May to October witnesses a drop in the tourist activity. This is because the beach becomes less accessible and enjoyable and there are not many historical sites or monuments in Pattaya. December and January brings really good weather to Pattaya. The wind is cool and there will be no rain to spoil your vacation.

**Safety Precautions**

Pattaya is famous for its shopping scene. There are several items of interest that you can buy here. From electronics to fashion, this place is a shopper&rsquo;s paradise. But there are several complaints that have been lodged by tourists as they have been cheated during their shopping spree in Pattaya. So make sure you take necessary precautions while buying duty items like precious stones and jewelry. You might be handed an authenticity certificate but it might be a bogus one. If possible go shopping with a local. While partying the night away do not accept drinks from strangers as Pattaya has a notorious record of drugging and then mugging visitors.
