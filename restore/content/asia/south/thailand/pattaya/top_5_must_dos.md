# Top 5 Must Do's in Pattaya

*Asia > South > Thailand > Pattaya > Top 5 Must Dos*

Pattaya has many great sights that it boasts of and it is, indeed, a great place to indulge in different kinds of adventurous activities. **Pattaya Must Dos** are so many in number that picking a few is very difficult. Some of the best must-dos are featured below:

**Alcazar Show**

One of the best cabaret shows in the world, the Alcazar cabaret show performed by the Lady Boys has successfully been captivating people&rsquo;s minds for over three decades now. The show is performed by more than 400 artists. The show also tries to combine the dance styles from all over the world. From which ever part of the world you belong, you will definitely find a dance number being performed exactly in your style. The color and grandeur of the show will hypnotize you and take you to a different world all together.

**Gem Shopping**

If you are visiting Pattaya then visiting a gem factory is a must. The tour of a gem factory will start with a guided rail tour of the origin of different natural stones. It will be followed by the actual gem-cutting factory visit. Once you feel you know all about gems, you will be taken to a display of the best cut gems in the world. The gem factory showroom is the biggest in the world and has almost all types of precious stones beautifully cut and displayed. Once you are over with the gem section and you enter the gold section the beauty and the intricate design of the gold jewelries will blow your mind. The pearl section is also big and the factory has a huge collection of pearl necklaces.

**Coral Island Tour**

Koh Larn Coral Island, only a 20-minute boat ride from Pattaya Beach, is an excellent location for any type of traveler. You can spend your time swimming or boating or biking. If you are a little adventurous then you can go parasailing or for an undersea walk among the corals. If you do not want to get wet then you can see the coral and the marine life from the glass bottom boats. If you want to get up close to the marine life and the colorful coral then take a walk under the sea. The wonderful experience will remain with you all through your life.

**A Day in Elephant Village**

Elephant Village, on the Sukhumvit Road, is an excellent place to spend your day among the biggest mammals on land. You will be amazed by the forestry skills shown by the elephants. The demonstration of how to catch a wild elephant will take your breath away and still if you are not totally flabbergasted, the football skills of the elephants will certainly make you feel so. The elephant ride across the village will certainly end your day at the village on a high.

**Dinner at Pattaya Water Park's Revolving Restaurant**

Pattaya Water Park is nestled between Pattaya Beach and Jomtien Island. The restaurant is situated on a 600-feet revolving tower. From the top you can see both the beautiful coastline and the city. The restaurant serves both Thai and Continental cuisines. The taste of the food is excellent and the panoramic view of the nightlights of Pattaya from the restaurant is heavenly.
