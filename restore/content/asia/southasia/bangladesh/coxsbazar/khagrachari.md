Khagrachari is a district in Bangladesh. It is under Chittagong Division. Locally it is known as Khagrachari but origin name was Phalang Htaung who first has enhabited at this location and was established in 1860 by Remrochai Chowdhury. Khagrachari is the home twon of Mong Chief/Mong Raja.

Khagrachhari District with an area of 2789.60 sq km, is bounded by the Indian State of Tripura on the north, Rangamati district on the east, Chittagong district on the west.

It's most magnificent interesting places for tourist is natural hills, forest, waterfalls and aborigine and tribal culture, green tree, hilly road and diverce people also.
