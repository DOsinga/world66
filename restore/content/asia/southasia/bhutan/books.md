# Books

*Asia > Southasia > Bhutan > Books*

If you are really interested to learn more about Bhutan, and to know how it happened to be the way it is now, there are some interesting books that you can read.

 *Beyond the Sky and the Earth :A Journey into Bhutan * by Jamie Zeppa is a simple and interesting book by a Canadian Volunteer teacher who got married to a Bhutanese man and has lived in Bhutan for about 10 years. I found it quite interesting. Besides, there are many books on history for the more serious reader. For the environmentally minded, there are books on the unique plants and animals of Bhutan and its surrounding regions.
