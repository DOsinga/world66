# The Art Cafe

*Asia > Southasia > Bhutan > Thimphu > Eating Out > The Art Cafe*

The Art Cafe is probably the cutest and cleanest restaurant in Thimphu.
They serve mainly sandwiches and pastrys and have a small outside
courtyard. There are daily specials which sometime change and sometimes
do not. The chicken wraps are always on and a deal at less then a
dollar.
