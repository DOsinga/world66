# Beaches

*Asia > Southasia > India > Andamanandnicobarislands > Beaches*

Havelock Island is the best island for tourists in the Andaman Islands. Havelock is just 2 hours away from the capital island (where the airport is located) of Port Blair. It also has **beach no. 7**, which has been rated as the **best beach in Asia** by **Time Magazine** in November, 2004. The 2km white sand beach is very calm between October - April and excellent for swimming and there is even a cove which is good for snorkelling.

There is only one exclusive eco-resort behind this beach, Barefoot at Havelock, Jungle Resort. The company which runs this resort also runs a scuba diving center and flights from Bangkok to the Andaman Islands. You can get more info in their website http://www.barefootindia.com/ .
