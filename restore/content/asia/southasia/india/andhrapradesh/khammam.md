# Khammam

*Asia > Southasia > India > Andhrapradesh > Khammam*

Khammam is a town with many old buildings, some as old as 1000 years. The Qila (fort) is in the centre of the town. It was constructed during 10th Century and has been the center of administration up to 18th Century. There is one lake called ' Lakaram Cheruvu '.

There are old temples build during 16th century. The " Narasimha Swamy " temple on a small hillock and the " Gunti Malleswara Swamy " temple are two important places. lord " Anjaneya " is also a largely worshipped having 8 temples in the town.
