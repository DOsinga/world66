# Festivals in Khodad

*Asia > Southasia > India > Andhrapradesh > Khodad > Festivals*

-----< ***Fesitival: Batakamma (Bodemma)*** >----

This is the fesitival commonly celebrated by Indians as Nav Rathri or Gouri Puja. But, in the village of Khodad and several others districts in Telangana it is celebrated differently. They worship Goddess Gouri by decorating it with flowers, singing flok songs. Its mainly celebrated by girls and women.
