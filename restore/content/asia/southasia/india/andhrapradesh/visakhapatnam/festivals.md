### [Navy Day](/asia/southasia/india/andhrapradesh/visakhapatnam/festivals/navy_day)

Being the Head Querters of Eastern Navel command Visakhapatnam home to the many of the Navy's Offices, and etc.

Every Year As Part Of Annula Celebrations Indian Navy Celerates with
all its glory shown the people by displaying the Navy's Weapons, their
ships,Guns and the troops in White.Eastern Navel Command Displays all
its glory with the Parade and many entertainment events and entry to
some of its ships.

Navy Day as they call is one more day of charm to the city especially
to the beach road where thousends gather to view the display of navy
ships and more.

### [Lumbini Festival](/asia/southasia/india/andhrapradesh/visakhapatnam/festivals/lumbini_festival)

To celebrate the many Budhist sites in the region Governament conducts
lumbini Festival when all the sites are specially decorated and
specilist tours are conducted to the buddhist sites of the region.

### [ISKCON Utsav](/asia/southasia/india/andhrapradesh/visakhapatnam/festivals/iskcon__utsav)

Grand Cultural Fest called "ISKCON UTSAV" at Hare Krishna Land,Sagaranagar from Sept 30th to Oct 2nd 2006 during Dassera Holidays. to promote Indian culture and create awareness about the activities of ISKCON(International Society for Krishna Consciousness).

The stalls would display indigenous products, spiritual articles and also commercial items by the participating companies. The utsav will include 50 booth Bazzar displaying various products, food stall from all over India, merry-go-round-Giant wheel, children games etc. There would be Gita Parayanam and Bhajans on [more..](/asia/southasia/india/andhrapradesh/visakhapatnam/festivals/iskcon__utsav)
