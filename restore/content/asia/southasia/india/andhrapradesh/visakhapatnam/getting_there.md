### [Railway Network](/asia/southasia/india/andhrapradesh/visakhapatnam/getting_there/railway_network)

Visakhapatnam is well connected by Rail. Being one of the important station on Kolkata- Chennai line Viakhapatnam is atmost 2 trains away from any part of india.

You can have your tickets booked and check your schedule on the official web site of Indian railways.
