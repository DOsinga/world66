# Mandarmoni Sea Beach

*Asia > Southasia > India > Beaches > Mandarmoni Sea Bea*

Mandarmoni or Mandarmani, a quiet virgin beach in east midnapore. The second widest sea beach in the world. Thousands of small crabs crawl around the 13 km. long virgin beach making it look like a simmering field of the red flower Mandar. Years ago the resemblance ignited the imagination of the locals. They named it Mandarboni. In course of time it has become Mandarmoni.
Mandarmani boasts of a 13 km long motorable beach, probably the longest motorable beach road in India. On the beach you will undoubtedly get the chance to feel the divinity and tranquility.

Located East Midnapur near Digha. You can travel to Mandarmoni by bus or by car from Kolkata.
