# Calcutta

*Asia > Southasia > India > Calcutta*

Some people call it the city of joy. One of the busiest and most crowded and oldest of Indian cities, you wont believe unless you have seen Calcutta with your own eyes how heritage and modernization can all flourish at the same place. 

Caution: dont get lost in the unending streets of this city and think before you trust anyone ;) 

For more info see Calcutta. 

If you are planning a trip to Calcutta, you may ask for help from a team of students who maintain www.sdey.org by mailing them at sdey@india.com.
