# Day Trips in Silvassa

*Asia > Southasia > India > Dadraandnagarhaveli > Silvassa > Day Trips*

There are plenty of places that you can include in your **Silvassa Day Trips. **However,
traveling to the many towns and places to enjoy a day trip in Silvassa
can be tedious considering that these places are very far from one
another. So, it is important to only choose the best place to spend a
day trip if this is your first time in Silvassa. If you are visiting
this capital town of the Indian Territory of Dadra and Nagar Haveli,
then you ought to go to Daman Beach Town.

**Daman Beach Town**

You need to travel 28 kilometers from Silvassa to reach the Daman
Beach Town. Daman Beach Town is near, but since you will be traversing
the busy highways of Silvassa, it will take at the most 2 hours to get
to Daman. This town is famous for its scenic sun-kissed beaches that
are blessed with calm waters as well as warm sand. You can either ride
a taxi or a bus to get to this place but to reach your destination
faster, you can always rent a car in Silvassa.

**Attractions in Daman Beach Town**

Daman has a big water park where you can enjoy a lot of water sports
activities such as swimming, scuba diving and snorkeling in the warm
water of the Indian Ocean. Daman also has natural gardens where you can
spend your day lounging and enjoying the fresh air in the town. There
are many things that you can do in Daman that will leave you
preoccupied for the entire day.
