# Eating Out

*Asia > Southasia > India > Goa > Eating Out*

**general**

**Sheetal - Candolim**

Also near the HBR, Candolim beach is a speciality Indian restaurant. The waiters are all dressed smart in Indian outfits with cheap silver service. At the end of the meal they surprise the women with a rose and the men with the bill.  A definate must if you are in this area. Oh also the price is a cheap or expensive as any of the other tourist restaurants in the area.   [more..](/asia/southasia/india/goa/eatingout/sheetalcandolim)

**Sea Shell - Candolim**

Nr the Canara Bank, candolim  A choice of continental and Indian.  A restaurant with real good service, ask for willy the waiter and tell him that I sent you. He is always smiles and can help you with menu.  Priced a bit higher than the others this place is a favourate for the steaks and the cauliflower sauce.   [more..](/asia/southasia/india/goa/eatingout/seashellcandolim)

**Silver Sands Holiday Village - Candolim**

On the Candolim Beach near Holiday Beach Resort. This place serves a choice of continental, Goan, chinese and Indian. Go for the chinese! 

**Mico's**

A restaurant that is run by a goan lad (Mico)with a smile and his pretty english wife that knows quality and service from her days as an airhostess on BA. Try the stuffed Jacket Potatoes!

address: Nr, Canara Bank, Candolinm Beach Road

**Little Italy**

A veg Itilain restaurant that gets my vote for the quality, service, ambience and an all round thums up .. check it out - Sicilian Chef too!

address: Nr. Goan Heritage Hotel, Calangute
