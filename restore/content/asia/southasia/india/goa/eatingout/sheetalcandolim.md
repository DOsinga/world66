# Sheetal - Candolim

*Asia > Southasia > India > Goa > Eatingout > Sheetalcandolim*

Also near the HBR, Candolim beach is a speciality Indian restaurant. The waiters are all dressed smart in Indian outfits with cheap silver service. At the end of the meal they surprise the women with a rose and the men with the bill.  A definate must if you are in this area. Oh also the price is a cheap or expensive as any of the other tourist restaurants in the area.
