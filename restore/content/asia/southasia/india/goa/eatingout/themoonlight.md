# The Moonlight

*Asia > Southasia > India > Goa > Eatingout > Themoonlight*

Fabulous place to relax, santana and theresa will welcome you to their wonderful bar and restaurant with open arms. Vast selection of food and very well stocked bar. Marvellous ambience,relaxed and not overbearing and of course cheap! Just off the beach road in candolim, ask for the moonlight, you'll enjoy it.
