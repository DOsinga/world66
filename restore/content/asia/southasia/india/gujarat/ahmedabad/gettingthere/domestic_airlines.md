# Domestic Airlines

*Asia > Southasia > India > Gujarat > Ahmedabad > Gettingthere > Domestic Airlines*

There are plenty of options for cheap flights to and from
Ahmedabad. Here are just a few of them. To travel by Air, your best
choice is to check online schedules or all the airlines mentioned
below. Also try searching Ahmedabad Airport website and Indian Airlines
and Air India websites.

You can check for the Flight Availability from Ahmedabad to any
destination of your choice and plan the onward journey accordingly as
you need. Gujaratis and people with interest at heart of Gujarat may
also want to check out Gujarat Airways.

** Indian Airlines:** Many flights. Three to four a
day to Mumbai. Two to Delhi. Also provides connectivity to Jaipur,
Jodhpur, Indore, Bangalore, Hyderabad and Calcutta with easy flights.
Weight allowance 30 Kg. Flights are cheaper by almost 40% if booked
ahead of 20 days however those tickets cannot be changed or cancelled.

** Jet Airways: **Ask about discounts for people
under 30 (25%) & students (50%) Other discounts are also available
but free baggage is only 20 Kg unless directly connecting the same day
to or from an international flight.

** AirDeccan: **Dirt-cheap tickets even at the last minute.

**Spice Jet:** A little bit nicer then AirDeccan but not as cheap.

**Kingfisher Airlines:** The Indian version of Virgin.
** Gujarat Airways: ** Relatively new airline, not much known as yet.
