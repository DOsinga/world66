# International Airlines flying into and out of Ahmedabad

*Asia > Southasia > India > Gujarat > Ahmedabad > Gettingthere > International Air*

From Singapore: **Singapore Airlines**

From Kuala Lumpur: **Malaysian Airlines**

From London and Dubai direct, LA and New York via Mumbai: **Air India**

From Manchester: **SAS**

From Muscat, Kuwait, Sharjah and Abu Dhabi direct: **Indian Airlines**

Note 1: Pending applications are from **Thai Airways** for flights to/from Bangkok, from **ANA/JAL** for flights to Tokyo Narita or Osaka.

Note: 2 A number of tourist airlines pass via Ahmedabad with just
a refuelling stop because Ahmedabad is on the direct route, as a crow
flies, between Asia and the Middle East, Asia and Europe and
Australia/New Zealand and Europe. Major chartered tourist airlines that
stop for refuelling at Ahmedabad include Finn Air flights between
Helsinki and Pukhet, Helsinki and Langkawi and Helsinki and Bali.
