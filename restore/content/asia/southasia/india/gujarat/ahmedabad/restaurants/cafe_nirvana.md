# Cafe NIrvana

*Asia > Southasia > India > Gujarat > Ahmedabad > Restaurants > Cafe Nirvana*

**Nirvana Cafe** ,

For the first time in Ahmedabad, you have an eatery and art shop combined,
which has come up at a happening spot at Commerce 6 Roads, not far from C.G Road. Nirvana
in Buddhism means " State of bliss happiness n beyond ". The cafe
interior has a subtle Buddhist art backdrop. There is lounging space for
books and art lovers and in terms of food, the place offers main course lunch n dinner of a menu range which includes original Punjabi, ethnic Gujarati, Mexican n Italian fare from soups to desserts and since it is open all day there are snacks available any time - wraps, sandwiches, pizza, nachos with dips, French fries with dip, quesadilla n even Dosa n Utthappan, in addition to mocktails, coffees, teas n beverages - have your pick to suite your taste, mood n appetite!

Its just near C.G Road, Commerce 6 Raods , Swapnil 5 Building,
PH:- 40098158
