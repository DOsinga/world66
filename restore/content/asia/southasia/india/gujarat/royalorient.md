**ROYAL ORIENT**

 If you want to be treated like a Maharaja, if you want to feel the royal experience and the royal way of hospitality, then the Royal Orient is an absolute must for you.  **THE ROYAL ORIENT** Train is a joint venture of the Gujarat Tourism Corporation and the Indian Railways and will keep you on the rails with its programmed journey of seven days/nights. This train starts from Delhi passing through Chittaurgarh, Udaipur, Modhera, Patan, Ahmedabad, SasanGir, Ahmedpur-Mandvi, Diu and Palitana, the pink city of Jaipur and returning back to Delhi again. You will pass through several states, amongst them at least Gujarat and Rajasthan.

  You will get three days/nights in a train covering Modhera, Patan, Ahmedabad, the Gir Forest, Ahmedpur-Mandvi, Diu and Palitana in Gujarat State. The rates are fixed and include breakfast, lunch, dinner (you will, however, be charged an extra fee for alcoholic drinks). If you feel like it, you can opt for a break and leave the train at one of the stations and do some proper sightseeing. For those with less money to spend, you can also jump on board for only one or two days. Liquor is banned in the State of Gujarat and the Royal Orient is the only place where the foreign tourist can get their drinks without problems. In this train, you will be treated like a Maharaja and the perfect a combination of travelling by night and sightseeing by day, gives you good value for both your time an your money. The restaurant serves three kinds of food, i.e. Indian, Continental and Chinese cuisine.

  The Royal Orient has fourteen air-conditioned coupes/saloons with two dining cars, well equipped with TV and VCR. Each coupe is fitted with music channel speakers and you can listen to the music of your choice- your widh is their command. You can order snacks at any time of the day and they can be provided in your cabin as well. The bar, bearing the name "The Watering Hole", is fully stocked with the finest of Indian Malt Whiskies, wines, rum, Scotch and beer (again against an additional fee). The Royal Orient has both single as double sharings and is well equiped with hot and cold baths. Every coupe has to attendants that will do their utmost best to molly-coddle you.
