# Day Trips in baroda

*Asia > Southasia > India > Gujarat > Vadodara > Baroda > Day Trips*

S Cube Water Park, Opp. Ajwa Lake - Vrundavan Garden in Vadodara is the most beautiful water park of gujarat. It has 14 slides, wave pool, and Gujarat's only Boomrango slide. This water park is so colorful and clean that it is the best place to take pictures. It has multicuisine restaurant where chinese food is the best.

Ticket rates for S Cube Water park is Rs. 400 which includes Rs. 100 of food coupons. Combo ticket for Water Park and Amusement Park is Rs. 500.
