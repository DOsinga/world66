# Paprika Grill

*Asia > Southasia > India > Gujarat > Vadodara > Restaurants > Paprika Grill*

Paprika Grill offers one of the best dining experiences in Baroda. The food is incredible, the staff is courteous, the modern ambiance is nice, and the location is superb. I love their Sizzlers (served on a hot skillet), Pasta Dishes, Soups, and Mocktails. After having eaten at the other Sizzler place, and at the renowned Italian Pasta chain in Baroda, it was clear that Paprika Grill's food is simply the best. The pastas are "al dente" and the sizzlers are just the right temperature. My favorite dishes include Mexican Chili Soup, BBQ Fish, Lamb Kheema Sizzler with the Indian Sauce, Formaggio Rossi and Penne Arrabbiata pastas. And finish with their amazing Cold Coffee. Yummy!!
