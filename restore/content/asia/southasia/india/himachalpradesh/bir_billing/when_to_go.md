The best season for paragliding starts from first week of october and last upto november end. After that I starts to snow and snow last upto end of feburary. Spring season starts from first week of March and last upto mid May. Though after mid april, it is too bumpy for paragliding.

Also, if you are interested in lama dance and visiting monestries, october and march are the best. Their is teaching at Sherabling sometime between end of March and April.
