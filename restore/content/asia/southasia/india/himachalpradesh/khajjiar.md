# Khajjiar

*Asia > Southasia > India > Himachalpradesh > Khajjiar*

Located 27 Km from Delhousie, Khajjiar is also more popularly known as India’s Switzerland. Khajjiar is a saucer shaped small area. A dense forest of Devdar trees surrounds the town with a small lake in the centre. There is a temple dedicated to Khajjinag which was believed to be built in the 12th century. You can see some life-sized wooden images of five pandavas in the temple. If time allows you to, try to stay here a couple of days.
