# Manali

*Asia > Southasia > India > Himachalpradesh > Manali*

The main reason to go this far is to visit the 450 year old Dhoongri Temple dedicated to Goddess Hadimba. It has unique wood carvings. Other attractions include the Tibetan Monastery, the majestic Beas River and Jagatsukh, the ancient capital of Manali. At walking distance, you'll find the hot water springs of Vashist, the beautiful Brighu Lake and other equally spectacular sights.
