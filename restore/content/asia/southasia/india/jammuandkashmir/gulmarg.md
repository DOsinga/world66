# Gulmarg

*Asia > Southasia > India > Jammuandkashmir > Gulmarg*

Gulmarg is a beautiful valley best known for its winter sports. J&K
government has taken some interesting steps to make it world class
winter destination for tourists. One can travel on the Gondola
(cable car) to have a view of the valley and reach on the other side of
the mountain. There are horse rides to go round the place. Even when the other part of the country is dying of heat.
