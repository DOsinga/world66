# Day Trips in Srinagar

*Asia > Southasia > India > Jammuandkashmir > Srinagar > Day Trips*

For nature lovers **Srinagar Day Trips **can be a memorable
experience for a lifetime. Nestled in the lap of the Himalayas, Srinagar is
also known as “Heaven on Earth” for its awesome scenic beauty. There
are many fun filled activities and sightseeing places nearby that
everyone can enjoy.

**Day Trip to Gulmarg from Srinagar **

One of the most favored tourist destinations for people who love skiing and trekking
is Gulmarg. This place is well known for its mountain slopes and flower
valleys. Gulmarg is situated in Baramulla district at a distance of 55
km from Srinagar. There are many sightseeing places like the Alpather
Lake, Baba Reshi shrine, cable car to Kondoor. However, it is paradise
for those looking for adventure. There are many trekking trails ranging
from medium to hard to choose from. The enchanting beauty of the place
leaves every tourist spellbound.

**Day Trip to Dachigam National Park**

This is a perfect destination for animal lovers. Dachigam National
Park is situated at a distance of around 22 km from Srinagar and is
home to many rare species of Himalayan fauna and flora. Amid the
natural beauty of the wild you can also find exotic plants like wild
cherry, apricot and chestnut. It is also home to Kashmir Stag, hungul,
an endangered species of red deer in the world. Day trip to Dachigam
National Park is more of a thrilling experience with so much wildness
among the natural beauty.

**Day Trip to Charar-e-Sharif **

Charar-e-Sharif is the religious destination for both Hindus and
Muslims alike. Situated at a distance of around 40 km from Srinagar
this shrine is dedicated to Sufi Saint, Sheikh Noor-ud-Din Noorani, and
is around six centuries old. This is a perfect day trip for those who are religious. This place is well known for preaching communal
harmony. The shrine has been destructed many times with the recent
being in 1995 and reconstructed. However the faith of the people still
remains unmoved with lots of pilgrims making visits here daily.

**Day Trip to Yusmarg from Srinagar **

Yusmarg is situated at a distance of around 47 km from Srinagar and
is the perfect destination for trekkers. Situated in Badgam district of the
state Yusmarg, it is well known for the milky river, Doodh Ganga and its
picturesque locale. Other than trekking you can also enjoy a leisurely
stroll along the banks of the milky river. Hiking in the surrounding
hills is also very popular among the tourist. Nilnag Lake is another
attraction of the place.
