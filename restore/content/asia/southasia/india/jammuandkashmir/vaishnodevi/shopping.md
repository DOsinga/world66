# Shopping in Vaishnodevi

*Asia > Southasia > India > Jammuandkashmir > Vaishnodevi > Shopping*

One of the most sacred pilgrimage centers of the Hindus in India is the **Vaishodevi. Shopping**
here is like any other pilgrimage destination in the country, with lots
of souvenir shops set up by the shrine board. Other than the Katra main
town, the base camp also has more shopping options.

**Spiritual Items**

Vaishnodevi is visited by pilgrims throughout the year and almost
everyone wants to take home a souvenir of their journey. The
Matavishnodevi Shrine Board has set up many small souvenir shops
throughout the trek, starting from the Katra bus stand. These shops
mostly have spiritual items which are sold at a very nominal rates.
Aarti Audio Cassettes and CDs

Trekking up all the way to the holy shrine, one would always hear
these songs played on the loudspeakers. The souvenir shops always have a
good collection of the spiritual music of Vaishnodevi, which you can
buy for your home.
Laminated Photographs of Matavaishnodevi

Pilgrims love to buy these pictures, which are available in various
sizes and are affordable. They are usually placed in the place of
worship in their homes and offered prayers everyday.
Gold and Silver Coins

These coins carry an impression of Matavaishnodevi Pindis and are
available in the Prasad counters and Bhavan banks only. They are
designed by the shrine board and the rates vary as per the denomination
chosen. Devotees have the belief that keeping a gold or silver coin of
Matavaishnodevi in their safe boxes will increase their wealth.
Bangles

Those who love the tinkling sound of the glass bangles can best shop
for them here. They are available in various designs and colors, which
are very luring. Mix and match these items with the dress you wear.
Rates depend on the designs and quality of the bangles.
Shawls

Kashmiri handloom is very popular worldwide. They come in unique
designs and colors. Shawls of Kashmir have a wide range of options to
choose from based upon the quality and designs. You can shop for them
throughout Katra and Jammu.
Kashmiri Carpets

Kashmiri carpet are known world wide for their fine craftsmanship.
They are considered a collector’s possession and are one of the
expensive shopping items here. You can buy a wall hanging, floor carpet
or rug during your trip here.
