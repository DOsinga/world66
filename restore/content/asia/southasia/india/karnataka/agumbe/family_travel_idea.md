It was one
of those beautiful Saturdays of November Joy and me set off on an adventure to
Western Ghats just 45 Km from or home in Santhekatte Kallianpur Udupi.

The weather
was absolutely perfect and we decided to ride on TB the best mean of transport
for off road experiment winding through forests of Hebri the country roads path
ways and small narrow bridges that linked the banks of streams.

Kudlu falls
one of the wonderful spectacular cascading waterfall is enchanting it is just
one of the nearest water fall to in Udupi. To reach this serene wonderland we
get into a forest that is dense trekking is not so difficult as the cool shed
gives a shelter. During the adventure if you feel tired there is lots of place
to rest under huge trees. Fresh water is in abundant supply here. As everywhere
streams flow with music of nature. Birds everywhere flying or perching singing
make you gratify.

Do not forget
the Kudlu falls it’s near to you, visit once in your life time and more you
will crave once you go you will feel young! Because you belong to nature and
she is your mother nature.
