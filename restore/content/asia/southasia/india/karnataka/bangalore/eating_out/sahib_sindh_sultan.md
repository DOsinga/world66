# Sahib Sindh Sultan

*Asia > Southasia > India > Karnataka > Bangalore > Eating Out > Sahib Sindh Sultan*

Amazing north western frontier food with a unique ambience. This restaurant of BJN group surely stands out for its delicious starters. Conviniently located at the Forum Mall in Koramangla, this restaurant is usually brimming with customers. The plus point here is the service. The waiters and management surely make it worth the wait and visit. Be warned, getting reservation for weekends is neigh impossible. Bookings start from Tuesday onwards.
