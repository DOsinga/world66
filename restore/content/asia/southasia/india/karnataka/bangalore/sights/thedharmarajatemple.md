# The Dharmaraja Temple

*Asia > Southasia > India > Karnataka > Bangalore > Sights > Thedharmarajatemple*

This temple in nazarethpet is the starting point of the annual Karaga procession. The revered idols here are the Shri Dharmaraja Swamy, hero of the Mahabharata and Lord Krishna.
