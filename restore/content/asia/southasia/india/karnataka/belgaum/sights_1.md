### [tushar](/asia/southasia/india/karnataka/belgaum/sights_1/tushar)

Belgaum, mainly being a residential town, has not many places to chill around. But one can still manage to visit the fort and the Jain Basadi inside which has been renovated to some extent.

One can also pay a visit to the Belurgad fort which is at a distance of about 20 kms. from the main town. You can have a good trekking experience as this place is not fully connected by road. You will have to bear some trouble in reaching there as this place is in a mess.

Other than these, there are other places which are near the town but almost a 3 hour drive away. There's [more..](/asia/southasia/india/karnataka/belgaum/sights_1/tushar)
