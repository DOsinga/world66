# Kollam

*Asia > Southasia > India > Kerala > Kollam*

Located 70km north of [Thiruvananthapuram](/asia/southasia/india/kerala/thiruvananthapuram), Kollam is the cashew capital of India. It is also one of the oldest ports on the Malabar coast, and a perfect gateway to backwaters of Kerala. 

The eight hour boat trip between Kollam and Allappuzhais the longest and most enchanting experience on the backwaters of Krala.
