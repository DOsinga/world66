# Mundakayam

*Asia > Southasia > India > Kerala > Mundakayam*

Mundakayam is the gateway to the High Ranges of Kerala, and is in the Kottayam District,at the border of kottayam and idukki districts. It is from here that the highranges starts. It's one of the most developed towns in Kerala, with beautiful rubber plantations, and amazing greenery, which characterizes the God Own Country. Nearby locations, Panchalimedu, Kuttikkanam, Peermade, Thekkady etc.
