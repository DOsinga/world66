# Bharatha Puzha

*Asia > Southasia > India > Kerala > Ottapalam > Beaches > Indian River*

1 kms from Ottapalam town is the Bharathapuzha. Clean crisp
water and less polluted water. Not safe to move in that area after dark
becoz of anti-social elements. Comfortable for a lazy evening stroll.

At present the municipality authority is going to develop a large entertainment facility in this area.
