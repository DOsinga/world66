# Festivals

*Asia > Southasia > India > Kerala > Ottapalam > Festivals*

Ottapalam is the heart of Valluvanad culture. Its rich in festivals. There are hundreds of hindu temples situated in and arroud ottapalam. March to May end is the season for "Pooram" festivals celebrated in various temples.

Pooram festivals are colorful shows of Valluvanadan culture.
