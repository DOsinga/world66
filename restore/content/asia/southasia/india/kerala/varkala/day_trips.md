# Day Trips in Varkala

*Asia > Southasia > India > Kerala > Varkala > Day Trips*

**Verkala Day Trips **in** **India have different places for people to enjoy and have a good time.

**Periyar National Park and Tiger Reserve**

Periyar National Park and Tiger Reserve is one of the 27 tiger
reserves in India, and is only about an hour away from Verkala. Periyar
National Park and Tiger Reserve’s employees are made entirely up of
volunteer workers, so all admission prices are directly helping the
conservation of forests, and the endangered animals within the reserve.

Admission prices vary anywhere from $1,00RS, to $5.000RS (or $2.00
USD TO $107.00 USD) Admissions prices vary so much because there are so
many different attractions available at the Periyar National Park and
Tiger Reserve, such as river rafting, overnight camping, hiking,
walking, and sight seeing multiple habitats, such as the Tiger Reserve.

The National Park is a sanctuary for animals and tourists contribute
to their care every time they visit the park. This park is one of the
best Verkala day trips options.

**Mattancherry Palace Kochi**

Mattancherry Palace Kochi is a beautiful 16th century Dutch building
that is now a modern day Hindu temple. Located at Kochi, Kerala, India,
about an hour away from Verkala, Tourists come from around the world to
see the wondrous interior architecture, and priceless artifacts that
are on display.

Mattancherry Palace was the home of many former raja‘s, and has many
of the passed raja’s personal belongings, portraits, and exhibits on
display for the public to enjoy. Random Annual renovation is held at
the Mattancherry Palace, so it might be a good idea to contact the
Palace to make sure your time visiting isn’t in renovation season.

Verkala day trips visit beautiful monastaries and Mattancherry Palace is one of them.
