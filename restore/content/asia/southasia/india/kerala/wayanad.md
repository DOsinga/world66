# Wayanad Travel Guide

*Asia > Southasia > India > Kerala > Wayanad*

The best resource for sights, hotels, restaurants, bars, what to do and see

One of the fourteen districts in Kerala (India), Wayanad is situated
in an elevated picturesque mountainous plateau in Western Ghats.

There is a land not far from Calicut, the city of Zamorins, yet a
world apart from Kerala's agricultural and industrial epicentres. It is
a quiet place where scenic beauty, wild life and traditions matter,
simplicity is a virtue and beauty still blossoms from the mountainous
horizon and from the green glaze of alluring vegetation.
