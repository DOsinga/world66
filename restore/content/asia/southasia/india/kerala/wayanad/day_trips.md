# Day Trips

*Asia > Southasia > India > Kerala > Wayanad > Day Trips*

Wayand with its rolling hills and temperate climate offers some interesting day trip and trekking options. Most local hotes have a nice tourist map of the district, which display the different options which include trips to waterfalls, a river island and a hike up the tallest peak, Chembra, in the district.
