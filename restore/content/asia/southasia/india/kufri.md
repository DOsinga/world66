# Kufri Travel Guide

*Asia > Southasia > India > Kufri*

**Kufri**, small little town nearby Shimla. Can be reached by bus about 45 minutes from Shimla bus terminus.

The main attractions are Indira Gandhi Park [Rs5 per person] and Kufri Himalayan Zoo Park [Rs20]. Can be seen brown bear, samber to yak. Also local market with fresh veggies and fruits like *naam* [mango], red cherry and plum.
