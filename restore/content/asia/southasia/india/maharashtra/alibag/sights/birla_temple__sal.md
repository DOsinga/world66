# Birla Temple, Salav

*Asia > Southasia > India > Maharashtra > Alibag > Sights > Birla Temple  Sal*

Vikram Vinayak Mandir famously known as Birla Mandir is
approximatly 20 KMs from Alibag on the way to Janjira ( Near Vikram
Ispat). The temple is open from 6.00 a.m. to 11.00 a.m. and
4.30 p.m. to 9.00 p.m.
