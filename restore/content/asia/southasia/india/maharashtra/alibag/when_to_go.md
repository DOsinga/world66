# When to Go in Alibag

*Asia > Southasia > India > Maharashtra > Alibag > When To Go*

**Visit Alibag during the Tourist Season**

If you want to visit **Alibag, when to go** to the town** **is
an important decision that you must make. Alibag is located in the state
of Maharashtra, which is one of the most beautiful states in India. The
town’s main attraction are its fine beaches, with tourism being the
main source of income for the local residents. If you want to take the
most of your stay and have a pleasant experience, visit Alibag during
the tourist season.

**Tourist Season**

The tourist season lasts from September to April. Alibag experiences a
moderate climate, and although most of the tourist season takes place
in the winter, this is undoubtedly the best time for a beach holiday,
since temperatures reach levels of 34 degrees Celsius (93 degrees
Fahrenheit) and fall no lower than 12 degrees Celsius (54 degrees
Fahrenheit). At the same time, the weather is very dry, and you can
enjoy long days of sunbathing. The only month, when you can experience
some discomfort is October, as the land starts drying up from the summer
rains and humidity increases drastically, creating a feeling of
unpleasant heat.

**Rest of the Year**

Although it is possible to visit Alibag through the rest of the year,
it won’t likely be a pleasant experience. In May the town experiences
peak temperatures and unbearable heat. May is also a month of strong
thunderstorms. June, July and August are the months when the monsoon
season takes place. They are characterized by strong and massive rains.
