# Getting There in Ashta

*Asia > Southasia > India > Maharashtra > Ashta > Getting There*

Geographical location of Ashta makes it very convinient to get there. It is located on state highway and close to sangli and kolhapur. The nearest airport is kolhapur. You can take flights from Mumbai (its a weekly flight). You can take bus to Sangli or Miraj. Every bus that goes to Sangli via Ashta takes a stop at Ashta. If you want to travel by train , you will have to first get to sangli or Miraj and from there you can easily get bus trasport to Ashta.
