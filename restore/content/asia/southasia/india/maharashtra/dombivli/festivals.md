# Festivals

*Asia > Southasia > India > Maharashtra > Dombivli > Festivals*

Diwali

It is the one of them most happening festival which is celebrated with a great pomp in the city with flying crackers and lights all over.

Ganesh Chaturthi

It has gained lot of importance as many private mandals have come up who are alsways engaged in celebrating this festival with great joy.

Guddi Padwa

It has created a lot of awareness towards the Indian Culture among the youngsters of Dombivli,so it is being celebrated every year by lighting crackers and with Prabhat Pheri in Marathi.
