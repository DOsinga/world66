# Day Trips

*Asia > Southasia > India > Maharashtra > Jalgaon > Day Trips*

Jalgaon has major attraction Tourist Spots, Places of Religious Importance/ Interest which can be covered in 1 day.

1. Ajanta Caves Towards Aurangabad 55kms from Jalgaon
2. Avchit Hanuman Towards Rameshwaram 15kms from Jalgaon
3. Ellora Towards Aurangabad 150kms from Jalgaon
4. Omkareshwar Temple in Jalgaon City
5. Unapdev (Hot Water Spring) Towards Chopda 35kms from Jalgaon
6. Nathban, Undirkhede, Parola Taluka Towards Dhulia 50kms from Jalgaon
7. Rameshwaram (Ancient Mahadev temple at confluence of rivers Tapi, Girana and Anjani) Towards Chopda via Idgaon and Kanalda 36kms from Jalgaon
8. Mahrishi Kanvashram, Kanalda Far near to Jalgaon City 8kms from Jalgaon
9. Shirsoli Lake Towards Pachora 6kms from Jalgaon
10. Shree Manudevi Temple Adgaon Towards Chopda 36kms from Jalgaon
11. Shree Padmalaya (Ancient Lord Ganpati Temple) Towards Pachora 35kms from Jalgaon
12. Shree Changdev Temple (Located at Confluence of Tapi & Purna rivers) Towards Muktainagar 50kms from Jalgaon
13. Shree Sant Muktabai Temple, Kothali Muktai Nagar 46kms from Jalgaon
14. Patnadevi Temple Towards Chalisgaon 70kms from Jalgaon
15. Pal Forestry & Hill Station Raver Taluka 80kms from Jalgaon
16. Suki Dam Raver Taluka 90kms from Jalgaon
17. Langda Amba Near Raver Taluka 110kms from Jalgaon
18. Ganesha Temple, Tarsod far near from Jalgaon city approx. 6kms
19. Kundeshwar (Mini waterfall & Temple) far near to Nashirabad 18kms from Jalgaon
20. Gandhi Philosophical Temple Amalner 100kms approx. from Jalgaon
21. Ancient Hemadpanthi Temples Waghali, Taluka: Chalisgaon 70kms approx. from Jalgaon

Tour Packages Description:

1. Jalgaon to Ajanta-1 day Tour
(Tour begins from Jalgaon, travel to Fardapur T-Point, Fardapur to Ajanta by MTDC Bus, Ajanta Sight Seeing, Fardapur return, Fardapur to Jalgaon back)
Tour Expense : INR 1300 - 1500 (US $ 29 - 35) Approx

2. Jalgaon to Ajanta, Aurangabad drop-1 day Tour
Tour begins from Jalgaon city, travel to Fardapur T-Point, Fardapur to Ajanta by MTDC Bus, Ajanta Sight Seeing, Fardapur return, Fardapur to Aurangabad, Aurangabad Sight Seeing, Aurangabad drop)
Tour Expense : INR 1800-2000 (US $ 40-45) Approx

3. Jalgaon to Ajanta, Ellora, Aurangabad City, Jalgaon-2 days Tour
Day 1: Tour begins from Jalgaon city, travel to Fardapur T-Point, Fardapur to Ajanta by MTDC Bus, Ajanta Sight Seeing, Fardapur return, Fardapur to Aurangabad, Night Stay at Aurangabad
Day 2: Aurangabad to Ellora, Ellora Sight Seeing, Ellora to Aurangabad City, Aurangabad Sight Seeing, Aurangabad to Jalgaon back
Tour Expense : INR 3000-3400 (US $ 67-75) Approx

4. Jalgaon to Ajanta, Ellora, Aurangabad City, Aurangabad drop-2 days Tour
Day 1:Tour begins from Jalgaon city, travel to Fardapur T-Point, Fardapur to Ajanta by MTDC Bus, Ajanta Sight Seeing, Fardapur return, Fardapur to Aurangabad, Night Stay at Aurangabad
Day 2: Aurangabad to Ellora, Ellora Sight Seeing, Ellora to Aurangabad City, Aurangabad Sight Seeing, Aurangabad city drop
Tour Expense : INR 2500-3000 (US $ 60-70) Approx

5. Jalgaon to Shegaon, Anand Sagar, and Jalgaon-1 day Tour
Tour begins from Jalgaon, travel to Shegaon, Shegaon Darshan, Anand Sagar, return to Jalgaon)
Tour Expense : INR 1900-2300 (US $ 42-50) Approx

6. Jalgaon to Shirdi Sai Baba Temple, and Jalgaon-1 day Tour
Tour begins from Jalgaon, travel to Shirdi, Sai Baba Temple Darshan, return to Jalgaon)
Tour Expense : INR 2700-3600 (US $ 67-80) Approx
