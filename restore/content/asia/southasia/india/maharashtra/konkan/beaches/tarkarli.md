# Tarkarli

*Asia > Southasia > India > Maharashtra > Konkan > Beaches > Tarkarli*

Tarkarli situated 6 kms. south of Malvan has the long and narrow stretch of beach, with its pristine waters.

**Getting There:
**Tarkarli is located near city of Malvan ( 6
km from Malvan). Malvan is 546 kms. away from Mumbai
and 160 kms away from Kolhapur.

Nearest airport is Panjim ( Goa) and Kolhapur.

**By Road:
**From Kolhapur : GaganBawada - Vaibhavwadi - Kankawali - Malvan.
From Mumbai: Panvel - Pen - Ratnagiri - Kankawali - Malvan

**By Rail:
**Kudal on the Konkan Railway, then by bus or rickshaw**
**
