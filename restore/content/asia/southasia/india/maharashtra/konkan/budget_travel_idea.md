# Budget Travel Ideas in Konkan

*Asia > Southasia > India > Maharashtra > Konkan > Budget Travel Idea*

**Konkan Budget Travel** has lots of options to save money. You can enjoy your trip to
the best without worrying much about your pocket with just proper
planning, because the the region is still unexplored by tourist.

**Reaching Konkan **
**By Air
**
Ratnagiri Airport is the nearest airport and operates only domestic airline services from Mumbai and Pune.
Those planning a trip here on budget can easily save money by keeping a
good track of the offers that the airlines give time to time. Go Air
Airlines and Kingfisher Fly Airlines are some of the best domestic air
services for economic travel.

**
By Train
**
Train is the easiest and cheapest mode of transportation to reach Konkan. It is also the most comfortable mode of transport for a relaxed
tour of the region. The train services are available from all major
cities in the country. Konkan Kanya Express and Mandovi Express are some of the trains to reach here from Mumbai.
**

By Road
**
Konkan region is well connected with roads to all the major towns
and cities of the nearby states. Both government and private bus
services ply to this region. Budget travelers can opt for government
bus services as compared to other services as they are comparatively
cheaper. However, due to heavy traffic on the highways, it usually
takes more time to reach the destination, which makes the entire journey very
tiring.

**Accommodation**

Since the region is still developing as a major tourist destination,
there are several budget accommodations available. They offer all the
basic amenities needed for comfortable stay. However, it is advisable to
choose your accommodation nearby the main attractions of the area so
that you save money on commutation. Many locals also offer lodging
facility at nominal charges and are safe because the community is
very tourist friendly. However, it is advisable to make inquiries about
the facilities beforehand.

**Getting Around**
Konkan Railways connect major tourist attractions of the region and
is the best option for budget travelers. The State Transport Public Bus is
also another cheap and comfortable mode of transportation here. However,
they can be very crowded. The auto rickshaw is the easiest and most comfortable form of transportation. They are comparatively
cheaper than hiring private taxis. However, it is advisable to bargain
the rates before boarding.
