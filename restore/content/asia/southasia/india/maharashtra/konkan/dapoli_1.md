Welcome to our quaint village called Dapoli situated in Ratnagiri district of Maharashtra.
Dapoli is a part of the undiscovered jewel – Konkan. It is not only undiscovered but also untouched and unspoilt. And yes it’s in India and still has virgin beaches & biodiverse forests.
A preferred British encampment of yesteryears and birthplace of many a luminaries like Maharshi Karve, Sane Guruji Wrangler Paranjape and so on, Dapoli is a mini-hill station in the lush green Konkan belt.
Sequestered between Mumbai and Goa and flanked by the famous pristine beaches of Murud, Karde and Anjarle in its periphery, Dapoli is accessible and conveniently away from quite a few Urban settlements like Mumbai, Pune etc. for whom maddeningly crowded beaches and the saturated hill stations are the only natural quick fixes for an immediate break.
