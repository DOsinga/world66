# Tambaldeg Travel Guide

*Asia > Southasia > India > Maharashtra > Konkan > Tambaldeg*

The best resource for sights, hotels, restaurants, bars, what to do and see

Tambaldeg is one of small village of sindhudurga district (Taluka Deogad). This is famous for Beach. The Occupation of the people in this village is fishing. Tambaldeg is surrounded by white sand.

This village is located on Mumbai Goa highway and 500km from mumbai.

So many temples are also also famous like Shree Kunkeshwar Mandir at Kunkeshwar beach. Rameshwar Mandir Mithbav, Ramesh Mandir at Achara. Gajbadevi Mandir at Mithbao(Dagare wadi). Peoples going to native place in vacation were also visit to these temples.**
