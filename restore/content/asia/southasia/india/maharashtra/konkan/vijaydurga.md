IT ALSO HAS A MTDC LODGING FACILITY RIGHT NEXT TO THE FORT. TE ANCIENT "RAMESHWAR " TEMPLLE IS JUST A COUPLE OF KM FROM THE FORT. THE "WAGHOTAN" CREEK STARTS FROM HERE AND IS A GREAT PLACE TO WATCH DOLPHINS IN ACTION. THIS BELT IS ALSO FAMOUS FOR JUICY ALPHONSO MANGOES AND IS EXPORTED ACROSS THE WORLD.
