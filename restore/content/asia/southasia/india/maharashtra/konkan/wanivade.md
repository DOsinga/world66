# Wanivade Travel Guide

**WANIVADE** is one of the villages of Deogad Taluka of Sindhudurga district. This village is famous for Alphanso Mango. The occupation of the people in this village is farming and fishing. This village is located near Mond-Baparde.

**Pavnaidevi **temple is also famous in Deogad taluka. **Bhairaidevi** Mandir @ Mond. **Kavaladevi **Mandir at Tembavali. People going to native places in vacation also visit these temples.
