# Budget Travel Ideas in Lonavala

*Asia > Southasia > India > Maharashtra > Lonavala > Budget Travel Idea*

**Some Penny-Pinching Tips for Your Lonavala Trip**

Use these tips for **Lonavala budget travel**. This busy hill
station in Maharashta is an ideal day trip excursion from bigger cities
like Mumbai and is beautiful any time of the year but especially during
the monsoon season.** **The whole area is surrounded by natural
attractions like its valleys, greenery, gardens and waterfalls, but
seeing them does not have to be expensive.
**Budget Hotels**

Being a popular destination outside of bigger cities like Mumbai,
Lonavala does have its own share of five-star hotels. However, there is
no need to stay in an expensive hotel when you should be going outdoors.
There are several clean and affordable hotels and bed and breakfasts.
Try Hotel Star Regency, Hotel Surya Resort, Shiv Shanti Resort, Lions
Den, Kohinoor Exotica and Dreamland Hotel.
**Take the Bus**

A bus is the most convenient way to go to attractions like Rajmachi
Point and Bushi Dam, which are only 15 minutes away by bus. A taxi would
probably be the fastest and most convenient way to go, but it can be an
unnecessary expense at roughly $4 to $5 for a one-way trip. One tip
when traveling by bus is to be careful of your handbag or purse.
