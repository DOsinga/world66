# Getting There

*Asia > Southasia > India > Maharashtra > Mahabaleshwar > Getting There*

You cannot reach Mahabaleshwar by Bus or by Taxi. The Nearest Station is Poona. 

**By Road:** By Road it is connected with a Good Motorable Road and on the way all the facilities are there. You will find Dhabas, i.e. some sort of a small hotel like in open space, where you can wait for some time and relax, after a long drive. It takes atleast 10 Hours from Bombay. The two different Routes are available, which are from Bombay and another from Poona. While starting from Bombay, You have to take the route from Panvel and via Mahad, you will reach Mahabaleshwar. If you are starting from Poona, you can reach Mahabaleshwar via Via and takes 4 hours. 

Here I am giving the details about the Travel Operators and Booking Agents, so that you can book the tickets from them. Normally, these Travel Bookings can be done from three places, which are at Khetwadi, Crawford Market and one is at Dadar. The Normal Charges for Mahabaleshwar is From rs: 180 to Rs:280 as per the Low and High Seasonwise. High Season means, Holi, Dewali, Christmas, April End and May. At this time even the Hotels Charges are High. In the Offseason Hotels give discount as many as 30 to 40%.
