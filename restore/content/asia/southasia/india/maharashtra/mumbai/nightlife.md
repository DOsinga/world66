# Nightlife and Entertainment

*Asia > Southasia > India > Maharashtra > Mumbai > Nightlife*

Nightlife is sparkling in Mumbai and there are loads of discos, bars and cinemas. Although you might feel tired after a whole day of new impressions, you might regain some of your energy on the dance floor. Most discos ask an entrance fee and at some discos, they only allow in couples.
