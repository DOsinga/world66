# H20

*Asia > Southasia > India > Maharashtra > Mumbai > Shopping > H20*

H20 in Mumbai sells all designer ware like Hugo Boss, Pierre Cardin, Fcuk, Armani, Diesel, Polo Sport and every other big designer brand you can think off at a fraction of what it would cost you at any other place in the world. Since everything is made in India these guys get hold of their surplus stuff and sell it to you for prices so low youd want to kill yourself for ever shopping elsewhere. Authenticity is not an issue here as all their stuff is direct from these designer factories.
