# Table Land

*Asia > Southasia > India > Maharashtra > Panchgani > Sights > Tableland*

This is the piece of land on top of the hill and remarkably flat. This place is very attractive for film shooters and there is a fair chance that you'll bump into one of the celebrities of the Hindi cinema, such as Govinda and Karishma Kapoor. You can either walk or hire a horse (30Rs) for the trip that takes about 5-8 minutes. From the market, it will take you about 15 to 20 minutes to arrive at Table Land. The full round of the Table Land is about 3 to 3.5 kms. Look out for 'Den Dine Restaurant' which is in a small cave and decorated with old wooden chairs and dining tables made from tree-trunks. It has a very pleasant atmosphere and the small water fountain keeps you cool. Be aware that you have to pay for taking pictures (10Rs).
