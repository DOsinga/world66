# Shinde Chatri

*Asia > Southasia > India > Maharashtra > Pune > Sights > Shinde Chatri*

Amongst the architectural prides of Pune, the memorial to the great
warrior **Mahadji Shinde**, the commander-in-chief of the Maratha army,
between the years 1760 to 1780 under the mighty Peshwas. The temple
adorned with Rajasthani architecture is an imposing edifice situated at
Wanowrie.
