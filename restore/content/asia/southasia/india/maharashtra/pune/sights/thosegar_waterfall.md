# Thosegar Waterfall

*Asia > Southasia > India > Maharashtra > Pune > Sights > Thosegar Waterfall*

Monsoon season is always the attraction for the picnic lovers. And now
a days people are looking for different spots - especially in the rainy
season. Thoseghar is near from Satara and a clam and quite newly
developing picnic spot.

Thoseghar is very famous for it's high
Waterfalls .. These Waterfalls are very attractive and creates a loud
noise in the calm nature. These waterfalls are also accompanied by a
clean lake, dark woods and highly hilly region.

You can enjoy various small waterfalls here which are about 15-20 meters high and one of around 500 meters high.
Thoseghar
is really a wonderful place where you can enjoy the beauty of the
nature. The best season to visit Thoseghar and enjoy is between July to
November.
