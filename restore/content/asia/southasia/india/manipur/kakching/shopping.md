# Shopping

*Asia > Southasia > India > Manipur > Kakching > Shopping*

There are two major markets for shopping - Main Market and Super Market. All kinds of goods, ranging from vegetables to eletronics, are available at reasonable rate in these two markets. Kakching market is famous for garments imported from Myanmar. Two things you should not forget to buy are Soibum(bamboo shoot) and Chakkrup(A bamboo woven handicfart article for keeping cooked food covered). Some goods you can buy include electronic goods, garments, handloom and handicraft products, etc.
