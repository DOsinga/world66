# Shopping

*Asia > Southasia > India > Manipur > Kakching > Shopping 1*

There are two main markets for shopping Main Market and Super Market. All kinds of goods, vegetables to computer, are available at reasonable rate in these two market. Kakching market is famous for garments imported from Myanmar. Two things you will not forget to buy are Soibum(bamboo shades) and Chakkrup(A rice cover made by bamboo). Some goods you can buy includes electronic goods, garments, handloom and handicraf products, etc.
