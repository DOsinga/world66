# Tengnoupal

*Asia > Southasia > India > Manipur > Tengnoupal*

Tengnpupal is 70 km south of Imphal. It is located at the highest point of the Imphal-Burma road and offers some excellent views of the valley of Manipur. A little bit further down is Moreh, the border town of India on the Indo-Burma road. For accommodation, you can try the Forest Bungalow (for reservations, contact the principal chief conservator of the forest). Or the hotel Imphal (Rs 20, 40 or, for you can have a bed in a dormitory for 15 Rs).
