# Festivals in Berhampur

*Asia > Southasia > India > Orissa > Berhampur > Festivals*

one of the major festival in here is Takurani Yatra , as Berhampur is blessed by MAA BUDHI SANTANI Takurani goddess.pepole says that this yatra is celebrated as the goddess visiting to her parents house once in two years at Desbehara street . In this occasion people celebrate for 25 to 30days up to return of the goddess from her parent place to the main temple.
