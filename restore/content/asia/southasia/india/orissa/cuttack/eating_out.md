# Eating Out

*Asia > Southasia > India > Orissa > Cuttack > Eating Out*

Cuttack is very famous for its fast food items like *Dahi Bara*, *Aaloo Dum*, *Gupchup* etc. You can get these items in plenty from any of the street-side food centers and believe me, they are really tasty !! Among other places, the following is a list of them:   Dahi bara and aaloo dum in front of Barabati Stadium   Indian food in Akbari Restaurant.   Indian food in a place called Dwaraka (it is in the Brindaban Cinema complex).
