# By Bus

*Asia > Southasia > India > Orissa > Rourkela > Gettingthere > Roadways To And Fr*

Rourkela has one of the best city bus services in Orissa.Adding delight to commuters it has got auto rickshaw service that is on a shared basis which is even more comfortable and faster.The frequencies of buses are 5 mins as compared to 2 mins for autos.

Then there are bus service to each big and small places in Orissa.One can avail a luxury service at any point of time but the preffered ones are at night which is the best in all over Orissa.According to requirements one can choose from n number of buses plying to different places.Recently the State transport corporation started the Volvo bus service giving a tough challenge to the private services.One will not believe the amount of luxury are provided in buses made avail at nominal rates.So the next time if you want to go somewhere in Orissa from Rourkela do avail the bus service.I am sure you will love it.
