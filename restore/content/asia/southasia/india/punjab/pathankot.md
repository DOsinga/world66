# Pathankot

*Asia > Southasia > India > Punjab > Pathankot*

Pathankot is a travel hub in Northern India. It is connected by rail and road and situated in the picturesque foothills of [Kangra](/asia/southasia/india/himachalpradesh/kangra) and [Dalhousie](/asia/southasia/india/himachalpradesh/dalhousie) near river Chakki is an important link city for going to [Jammu and Kashmir](/asia/southasia/india/jammuandkashmir), Dalhousie, Chamba, Kangra and the deep Himalayas. It is surrounded by picturesque spots; Shahpur Kandi with a hanging rest house, Madhopur Headworks which date back to the Mughal era, Malikpur where India's foremost hydraulic research station is situated.
