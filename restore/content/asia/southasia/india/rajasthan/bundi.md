# Bundi Travel Guide

*Asia > Southasia > India > Rajasthan > Bundi*

The best resource for sights, hotels, restaurants, bars, what to do and see

approx 40 Km from [Kota](/asia/southasia/india/rajasthan/kota) lies Bundi, a hidden beauty yet untouched by tourists, one of very few destinations left in [Rajasthan](/asia/southasia/india/rajasthan). the main attraction here is the fortress standing on the hill in shape of star- Taragarh, overlooking the town. but interesting are its streets right at the bottom of the hill, where there are many havelis, converted now in hotels or shpos- selling handicrafts and artefacts. most one can do here is just relax for a while away from main tourist destinations of [Rajasthan](/asia/southasia/india/rajasthan) and enjoy evening walks and sunsets near the lake.
