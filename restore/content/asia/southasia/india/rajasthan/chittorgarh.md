# Chittorgarh

*Asia > Southasia > India > Rajasthan > Chittorgarh*

Chittorgarh has many sights: old city walls and ruined palaces.

Western railways connect Chittorgarh on the metre guage with [Ajmer](/asia/southasia/india/rajasthan/ajmer), [Delhi](/asia/southasia/india/delhi), [Jaipur](/asia/southasia/india/rajasthan/jaipur), Ratlam and [Udaipur](/asia/southasia/india/rajasthan/udaipur).
