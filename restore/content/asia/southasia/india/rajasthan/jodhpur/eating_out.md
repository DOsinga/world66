# Eating Out

*Asia > Southasia > India > Rajasthan > Jodhpur > Eating Out*

We recommend **Midtown Restaurant** which is right in front of Railway Station in a heritage hotel "**Shanti Bhawan".** Midtown is a world famous restaurant and is also very well recommended **by all the leading guide books. One can find the best** Rajasthani food here. Its **Rajasthani Pizza** is world famous. **Rajasthani Thali and Kabuli** are just awesome. And everything else is just excellent. **A Must for all Tourists to visit.** It also has one of the **largest Menu of India.**
