# The Theosophical Society

*Asia > Southasia > India > Tamilnadu > Chennai > Sights > Thetheosophicalsociety*

A sprawling area of beautiful trees and shrubs in the heart of the bustling
[Chennai](/asia/southasia/india/tamilnadu/chennai) city, Theosophical Society, in
Adyar, is a place one should not miss. Famous for its legendary Banyan tree (more than 5000 years old), underwhich JK (Jiddu Krishnamurthy) had given his world famous discourses.-Ram Shridhar
