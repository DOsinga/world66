# Practical Information in Kovilpatti

*Asia > Southasia > India > Tamilnadu > Kovilpatti > Practical Informat*

Very industrailized place. Rich in cultural heritage.

Railway Station: approx. 2 kmm from the centre of the town. Trains to Chennai(Capital of Tamilnadu state), Madurai and Trivandrum are available. The nearest airport among these is madurai, 95 kms from Kovilpatti.

Bus Station: The buses provide connectivity to almost all the locations in the state, often there will be broken journey. Direct buses to Chennai are available, mainly in the evenings.

Location: Located in the NH-7 (Varanasi to Kanyakumari), convenient access to all major towns and cities nearby
