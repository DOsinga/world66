# Beaches in Rameshwaram

*Asia > Southasia > India > Tamilnadu > Rameshwaram > Beaches*

The **Rameshwaram beaches** are pristine and ideal for those who
simply love to experience water sports and fun activities at the
beachside. The Rameshwaram beach is located in the Gulf of Mannar and
the airport at its close vicinity is in Madurai. According to the
legends the beach was named after the popular Ramanathaswamy Temple.
Apart from taking a stroll on the unspoiled beaches you can also
participate in activities like bird watching, exploring historical
shrines, and visiting the marine observatory.

**Rameshwaram Beach Location: **

The beach is situated about 40 km away from ‘Jaffna Peninsula’ of Sri
Lanka. The location of Rameshwaram beach is right at the top of Indian
peninsula, in the Gulf of Mannar. The beach is not just another
seaside location but a religiously significant place. There are plenty
of temples near the beach which attracts loads of pilgrims from all
over the globe.

**Reaching the Rameshwaram Beach**

The beach at Rameshwaram is quite well-connected to the major cities
which help tourists reach here without much hassle. The closest airport
is in Madurai. There are several flights to all metro cities from this
airport. It is also connected with the south western railways. Nearest
cities from where you can reach Rameshwaram include Thanjavur,
Chennai, Coimbatore and Trichy. Rameshwaram is also connected with
Mandapam via Indira Gandhi Bridge. Bus services are also available from
Pondicherry, Thanjavur, Trichy, Thiruvallur, Chennai and Kanyakumari.

**Nearby Tourist Attractions**

There are plenty of popular tourist places that are close to the
Rameshwaram beach and a must visit for tourists. Erwadi, Dhanushkodi,
Gandamadana Parvatham, Agnitheertham and Ramanathaswamy Temple are few
of the popular destinations. The Ramnathswamy Temple was built in 17th
century from which the beach derived its name. The magnificent
articulated columns, corridors and gopurams will leave the visitors
awestruck. Dhanushkodi is located at the farthest end of the Rameshwaram
Island. Kothandaramaswamy temple is close to the beach.

**Activities at Rameshwaram beach:**

The most exciting activity that will attract any tourist is watching
the underwater marine life. Across a vast area of the sea, oceanic
animals and weeds are preserved carefully which you can watch with a
professional guide. Marine creatures like star fishes, sponges, crabs
and turtles are present in this marine observatory. Pilgrims offer
prayers at the nearby temples.

**Time to visit**

The ideal season for visiting the Rameshwaram beach starts from the
month of January and lasts till March. The sea remains calm and serene
during this period. The shallow water gives you ample opportunity to
swim around enjoying the classic beauty of the ocean.
