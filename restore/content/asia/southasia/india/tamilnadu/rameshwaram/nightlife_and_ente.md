# Nightlife and Entertainment in Rameshwaram

*Asia > Southasia > India > Tamilnadu > Rameshwaram > Nightlife And Ente*

Rameswaram is a town in the Ramanathapuram
District of in the Indian state of Tamil Nadu. Rameshwaram, in
Tamilnadu, is a famous pilgrimage tourism destination in India.This
particular town is located on the Pamban island, which is only connected
to the main land by the Pamban Bridge. A lot of devotees visit this
holy island in Tamil Nadu, and aside from that, you will enjoy also the **Rameswaram Nightlife** with your friends.

**Bike and Barrel **

The Bike and Barrel is a popular British-themed disco that’s become a
favorite with the locals, the wealthy, the young, and the hot to trot.

**Distil**

Distil is a bar dedicated to energy. This place is perfect for high
energy and pre-dinner drinks. If you’re interested in the culture of the
youth, then this is the bar that you’re going to want to go to.

**Flame Le Club**

The interesting thing about this night club is that it’s lit entirely
by electric torch light. You can find this club at Le Royal Meridian
Hotel, GST Road, St. Thomas Mount.

**Fort St. George**

This place is a bar that that’s perfectly complimentary to the area's business class elite.

**Kalakshetra Foundation**

The Kalakshetra foundation is a dance hall and university founded by
the famous dancer Rukmini Devi. Most of the time this is more of a
university, but more often than not, you can drop in and watch the
dances.

**The Leather Bar**

This area is a trendy park and bar made entirely out of leather, including the floor.

**Mocha **

This place offers coffee and small dishes as well as hookahs. This
place caters mostly to the young and wealthy. The grounds of this area
include lotus ponds and a great deal of shade. This makes this
particular café the perfect place to get away from the dust and noise of
the rest of the area.

**Zara‘s**

Zara‘s is a bar that serves cocktails wines and Spanish snacks. This
place caters mostly to the wealthy and the elite. This is seen in the
fact that men are not allowed to wear sandals in the area.

**Pasha’s**

This is a fashionable lounge where DJ’s play music for the area out
into the hotel lobby. The party is from 8 p.m. to 12 p.m. and you need
to pay 1,000 rupees for entry.
