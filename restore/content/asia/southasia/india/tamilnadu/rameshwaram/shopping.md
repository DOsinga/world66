# Shopping in Rameshwaram

*Asia > Southasia > India > Tamilnadu > Rameshwaram > Shopping*

Rameshwaram is the land of temples and offers a lot to its visitors
including the exciting **Rameshwaram shopping **sprees. There are
beaches alongside the long-stretched blue ocean water that holds a wide
variety of marine treasures. You are free to explore the temples and
indulge in water sports for utmost entertainment. You can easily find a
number of hotels and guest houses in Rameshwaram which can accommodate
huge numbers of tourists even during the festivals. Taste countless
mouth watering delicacies at the popular restaurants and local dining
joints in Rameshwaram. There are plenty of temples and holy places that
attract countless pilgrims round the year. If you are a shopaholic,
Rameshwaram has a lot to offer in its galore.

**Shopping at the Temple Area**

Rameshwaram has numerous temples and each of these temples have a
shopping area around it. These shops sell exquisite items which include
wall-decorative items, statues and other delicate stuffs. There are
local people who excel in making these handcrafted items. You can buy
framed photographs of Gods and Goddesses at these nearby shops which
make great souvenirs for pilgrims and also common visitors.

**Silk Emporiums in Rameshwaram**

Rameshwaram is popularly known for the silk saris. Delicate silk is
the main product here which impresses women a lot. You can find a number
of shops selling silken dress materials and saris with great designs,
hand works and many more. This silk is a traditional product of Southern
India and is quite expensive. Many foreign visitors also pick these
gorgeous materials with fine textures for themselves and their close
ones.

**Khadi Items in Rameshwaram**

Rameshwaram shops also sell different dress materials and other
stuffs made from khadi. These are woven products made from cotton
threads. The texture of this fabric is thick and is ideal for winter
wear. You can buy saris, stoles, traditional ‘Punjabi’ made of khadi
fabric which is less expensive yet unique.

**Local Handicrafts**

The local handmade stuffs available in Rameshwaram are unique and
great to look at. Being a city by the sea shore, in Rameshwaram you can
purchase decorative pieces and ornaments made from seashells. Wall
hanging objects and curtains made from seashells are equally popular.

**Jewelry**

Jewelry made from beads is another highlighted shopping item in
Rameshwaram. You can also buy earrings and necklaces made of palm leaves
and coconut shells which are not only ornaments but also great work of
art.
