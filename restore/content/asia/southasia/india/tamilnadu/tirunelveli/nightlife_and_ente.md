# Nightlife and Entertainment in Tirunelveli

*Asia > Southasia > India > Tamilnadu > Tirunelveli > Nightlife And Ente*

**Tirunelveli nightlife** is more easygoing and probably you won’t
find a lot of pubs and clubs, but still you will find enough things to
entertain yourself. Hidden in the north of India, in the province of
Tamil Nadu , this city is famous for its food and the best sweets,
halva, in India. Don’t miss the chance to spend few nights here
exploring the nightlife and looking for entertainment.

**Arunagiri - Tirunelveli National Theatre**

This area is well-known for its folk dances which are part of its
cultural heritage. When girls are dancing, that is actually a story to
be told, and every dance is given as a worship of gods. Bharatanatyam is
the most popular dance. This is the oldest form of performing arts not
particularly connected to this area but the whole India is dancing this
dance. This is not just a simple dance but a conjunction of many
different art styles. The national theatre in the city of Tirunelveli is
performing traditional dances almost every night and tourists can
always find tickets in tourist information office at the theater.
Bommalattam is the famous puppet show and every village and city around
has its own shows and usually tourists can see these shows at festivals.
Sometimes they perform in theater.

**Bright Browsing Centre**

This place is located on the first floor of the Roche Complex and it
is a nice place to chat and grab some drinks with friends. The music is
not so loud and this place looks like a country club more than a bar but
it is still a great place to hang out in the evening. Don’t miss the
chance to try the food that is served here, and if you are not a fan of
hot and spicy just tell the person waiting on you.

**Annapoorna Hotel**

This hotel is located in the city center and has a really nice Indian
restaurant and a disco bar and probably tourists will have a feeling
going back in time dancing here. The music is local or the 1970s and
1980s, but still a charming place where you can easily spend the whole
night. It is probably one of the rare places in the city where you can
dance.

**Ganesh Theatre**
Bollywood movies are extremely popular around India and cinemas are
working around the clock so tourists can always grab a movie or two in
the evening. If the movie is new it is necessary to buy tickets a bit
earlier. Ganesh Theatre is nice and cozy and completely packed with
photos of famous Indian move stars.
