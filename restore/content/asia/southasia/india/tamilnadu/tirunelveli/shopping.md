# Shopping in Tirunelveli

*Asia > Southasia > India > Tamilnadu > Tirunelveli > Shopping*

There are a good deal of **Tirunelveli Shopping** hot spots.
Tourists who come to Tirunelveli not only enjoy the city's diverse art,
culture, food and history, but also indulge themselves in a good deal of
retail therapy. Tirunelveli is famous far and wide for its local
souvenirs, textiles, handicrafts, mats, pottery and jewelry. Here
are Tirunelveli's shopping hotspots and things you shouldn't leave
without buying from this coastal city.

**Souvenirs**

Local handicrafts made by artisans from Tirunelveli itself should not
be given a skip. If you like music, Meena Flutes on Dr. Ambedkar Nagar
road in the Pettai area of Tirunelveli should be your first priority.
This shop stocks locally made bamboo flutes, drum sticks and other local
instruments. Poombuhar Handicraft Emporium is best if you want to have a
general look at Tirunelveli's handicrafts to pick up the best of the
lot.

**Textiles**

Tirunelveli is known for its locally woven textiles which are
available in a wide range of fabrics and colors. Some shops even sell
saris, bed sheets and curtains made of this beautifully woven material.
Anand Textiles on West Car Street in Nellai Town is a good option. Anil
Textiles is another shop which sells a wide variety of materials, and is
located on 100 Feet Road.

**Shopping Centers**

If being a mall rat is how you want to do your shopping in
Tirunelveli, then head to one of the city's many shopping centers. All
of the city's shopping complexes house a great many shops that sell a
wide range of products. Curaisi Shopping Center in Palayamkottai and
Kavitha Shopping Center at Tirunelveli Junction are excellent
destinations.

**Ceramic Pottery**

If you happen to be on the lookout for one of Tirunelveli's famous
ceramic or terracotta pots and vases, then you won't have to look any
further. This is because the ceramic and terracotta industry is a
thriving one in Tirunelveli. Terracotta Industries, a shop at
Thenpothai, will be a good option.

**Jewelry**

Gold and silver jewelry as well as ornaments made out of shells, wood
and clay are very famous in Tirunelveli. Head to Amutha Jewelers, which
is situated on West Car Street in Tirunelveli. A V L Goldsmith Works is
a good choice if you want to purchase gold. It can be found at 102
Abirami Nagar in the city of Tirunelveli.
