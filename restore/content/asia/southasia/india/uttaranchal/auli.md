# Auli Travel Guide

*Asia > Southasia > India > Uttaranchal > Auli*

The best resource for sights, hotels, restaurants, bars, what to do and see

Auli features a ski resort run by the state-owned Garwahl Mandal Vikas Nigam, which has what is arguably some of the cheapest skiing in the world. It is connected by road to [Haridwar](/asia/southasia/india/uttaranchal/haridwar), a day's bus journey away.
