# People in pauri sumari village

*Asia > Southasia > India > Uttaranchal > Srinagar Pauri Ga > Pauri Sumari Villa > People*

This village is dominated by the 'Brahmins'. The 'Kala' cast in 'Brahmins' of Garhwal is known to originate from this village. This village is inhabited by other cast also, such as Bahuguna, Nautiyal, Sundriyal, Ghildiyal and Kagdiyal.

Every year, the villagers celebrate a famous festival locally called 'jaggi', which is a yagya. There are many temples in the village premises including Nagdev, Nagrasani, Kwilon Nagraja, and Indragaman.
