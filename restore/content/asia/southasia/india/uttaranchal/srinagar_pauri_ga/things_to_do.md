# Things to do in Srinagar (Pauri Garhwal)

*Asia > Southasia > India > Uttaranchal > Srinagar Pauri Ga > Things To Do*

This **Srinagar (Pauri Gahwal) Things to do** list will give you an
insight into the mythological history of India. There are several
temples with unique architecture in this town, located in the state of
Uttaranchal in India. Read on to find out about the must-see and must-do
activities in Srinagar.

**Sri Yantra**

Sri Yantra
is a highly-worshiped structure in Srinagar. It is located on the
outskirts of the town in the riverbed of the Alaknanda River. This holy
shrine dates back to the eighth century. Sri Yantra is considered to be
weapon used by a goddess to kill the demons. The town gets its name from
this auspicious weapon. During the eighth century human sacrifices were
made here as a sign of respect and devotion. But this practice was
banned by the efforts of the great guru, Shankaracharya.

**Temples**

Srinagar and the surrounding areas are brimming with temples that
showcase the Hindu faith. Every temple has a primary deity. The most
noteworthy among the temples in Srinagar is Keshorai Math. The
architecture of this temple will win you over. Huge stones were used to
erect this temple and the stones are carved with designs. It is located
close to the town's center.

**Kanvashram**

This
place is located close to Srinagar. This place forms the background for a
famous tale in the Indian mythology. The legend has it that, centuries
ago, a Swami by the name Vishwamitra used to meditate here. A heavenly
beauty named Menka used her beauty to break his meditation. A lot of
tourists and people from other parts on India visit this historical
location. It is also a great archaeological site. You need to travel 14
km from Kotdwar to reach Kanvashram.

**Archaeological Sites**

The archaeological site of Develgarh is a must-see location in
Srinagar. It is located at a distance of 19 km from the town
of Srinagar. You will still find archaeological teams in action at this
spot. Several stone temples and sculptures were unraveled here.
**
Shankar Math**

Shankar Math is a pious monastery in Srinagar. It is located just 3 km
from Srinagar. This sanctum was built by the great Shankaracharya.
People flock to this Math in the search for inner peace and happiness.
There are several yoga sessions conducted in the Math, and is a great
way to spend some time with your inner-self and understand the pursuit
of enlightenment that several followers wish for. You can also learn
about the work and the preachings of Adi Shankaracharya.
