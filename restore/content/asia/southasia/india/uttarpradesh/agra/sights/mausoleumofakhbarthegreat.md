# Mausoleum of Akhbar the Great

*Asia > Southasia > India > Uttarpradesh > Agra > Sights > Mausoleumofakhbarthegreat*

Not in Agra itself, but some ten kilometres away, this mausoleum offers a good example of the transitional phase in architectural styles. The mausoleum is constructed of both sandstone and marble.
