# Festivals

*Asia > Southasia > India > Uttarpradesh > Festivals*

Holi and Diwali are the main festivals. Holi is the festival of color when people throw color on each other. Diwali is the the festival of lights and firecrackers. Holi is celebrated in spring season at the onset of summer and when crops are harvested. Diwali is celebrated at the onset of winter in October or November.
