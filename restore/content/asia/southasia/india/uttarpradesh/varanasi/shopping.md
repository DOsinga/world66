# Shopping in Varanasi

*Asia > Southasia > India > Uttarpradesh > Varanasi > Shopping*

The holy city of Varanasi in Uttar Pradesh offers a good **Varanasi shopping**
experience. Varanasi is regarded as one of the holiest cities
especially for Hindus. People from all over come here to this city that
is situated on the banks of the River Ganga. One can do a good deal of
shopping for musical instruments, clothes, flower decorations and holy
items in the city of Varanasi. Here is a list of places to go shopping
when visiting the city of Varanasi, also known as Benaras.

**Ganga Silk
**

Varanasi is famous for its beautiful silk saris. A sari is an Indian
attire for women. Head to Ganga Silk in Varanasi if you want to shop
for saris. Here, you will find silk brocade material and different
types of saris in silk. You will not be able to haggle at this store,
as prices at Ganga Silk are fixed. If you don't know anything about
saris or what type of silk to purchase, the knowledgeable staff will
explain everything down to the last detail.

**Book Shops
**

If you love to read and are interested in purchasing books when in
Varanasi, then there are a good many options. Harmony Book store at
Assi Ghat in Varanasi is one option. Then there's Indica Books which is
also situated at Assi Ghat. Other notable boo shops include Pilgrims
Book House, which can be found the the Monkey Temple, and Modern Book
House, which is located in Nadesar.

**Pashmina Shawls**

Silk and pashmina shawls are also very famous in Varanasi. A shop
called Baba Black Sheep is a good choice if you want to buy shawls that
are priced reasonably. Tailang Silk Emporium is another great place to
buy such shawls.

**Miscellaneous Shopping**

If you want to purchase musical instruments, bangles, masks and
Buddhist statues, head to shopping zones like Gyan Vapi, Thateri Bazar,
Vishwanath Gali, Lahurabir or Godoulia. At these shopping zones, you
will find a good number of shops that sell anything and everything.

**Handmade Products**

Open Hand Shop is a good option if you are looking to buy natural,
hand-made products like recycled paper, organic fabrics and wooden
products. Bhadohi, which is a little town that is located some 40 km
from the city of Varanasi is famous for its carpets. These handmade
carpets sport Persian designs, floral finery and other designs. But, if
you don't want to get too far from Varanasi, then Thatheri Bazaar
houses many shops that sell handmade woven carpets.
