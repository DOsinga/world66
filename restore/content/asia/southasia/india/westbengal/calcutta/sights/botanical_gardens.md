# Botanical Gardens

*Asia > Southasia > India > Westbengal > Calcutta > Sights > Botanical Gardens*

The Botanical Gardens are the largest and the oldest in India. This
lovely picnic spot is located on the other side of the river Hooghly
and is famous for its 250 year old banyan tree whose circumferance is
982m. It can be reached either by a ferry across the Hooghly or by bus.
