# Rapidrunner Expeditions

*Asia > Southasia > Nepal > Family Travel Idea > Rapidrunner Expedi*

Rapidrunner Expeditions, based in Pokhara, offers world class rafting adventures with superb quality and safety. The Nepali/British husband and wife owners give a warm welcome to everyone and make sure that all their trips are special experiences. Trips vary from a half day to 12 days long, but if you have the time, try to spend at least one night at their remote river beach camps. Sitting next to a drift wood fire under a canopy of the stars - a truly magical experience! For Families they offer a fantastic 2 day trip on the Lower Seti River which is ideally combined with a visit to Chitwan National Park.
