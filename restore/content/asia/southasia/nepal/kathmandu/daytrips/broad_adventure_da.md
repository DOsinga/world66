# Broad Adventure Day trip

*Asia > Southasia > Nepal > Kathmandu > Daytrips > Broad Adventure Da*

A day trip is all about the excursion one can carry within a day’s time. It is a relativey short period, yet contains lots of excitement. A day trip within Nepal includes varieties of travel.Based in Kathmandu valley, visiting various monuments of religious and cultural importance within the valley and going for sightseeing in and around Kathmandu valley can give you a day full of excitements.Outside Kathmandu valley Pokhara, Chitwan and Lumbini are other quite remarkable places for a day’s trip. In pokhara, a full day is quite promising with the views of lakes and snow clad mountains. In chitwan, jungle safari and various floras and faunas welcome you. A day trip in Lumbini, the birth place of Gautam Buddha, is worthy of exploring beauties of Buddhism. The advantage of a day trip is that you can leave your hotel or home, have a day’s fun and return back to your previous place.
