# Mountain Climbing

*Asia > Southasia > Nepal > Mountainclimbing*

While mountain climbing can be wonderful in Nepal only experienced climbers should even consider it. Some 18 peaks don’t require a great deal of red tape to climb and an additional 69 peaks can be climbed but require a lot of planning—apply to the Nepal Ministry of Tourism at least six months in advance. The climbing season is different from the trekking season: April to early June is best for climbing and September-October is also possible. Trekking agencies in Nepal can usually assist climbers.
