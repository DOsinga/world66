# Sights

*Asia > Southasia > Nepal > Sights*

The capital of Nepal, Kathmandu is famous for medieval atmosphere and hundreds of masterpiece temples and monuments dating back to medieval to ancient times. Besides, Nepal is known among adventure lovers as a land for the high and beautiful Himalayan mountains. With Mt. Everest and 8 out of 10 highest mountains in its territory, Nepal is an ideal destination for trekking and climbing on a holiday. The classic Everest and Annapurna trails and more other hidden Alpine jewels make Nepal a trekker's paradise.
