# Langrial Travel Guide

*Asia > Southasia > Pakistan > Abbottabad > Langrial 1*

The best resource for sights, hotels, restaurants, bars, what to do and see

- **INTRODUCTION:** Langrial is a beautiful village and the 51 Union Council of
Abbottabad District. Langrial has the four weathers though out the year spring,
winter. Village Langrial is the central place of its other parts being a Union
Council.

- ** LOCATION:** Village
Langrial is situated at 33° 56' 0" North, 73° 7' 0" East. It is
situated 24 km in Southeast of the Great Silk Way Root and 32 km on the
Lora-Ghora Gali road. It is located with village Gandian in West, village Karhaki
in East, village Dubran in South and the village Dana-Tarehri in North.

- **SUBDIVISIONS:** The Union Council is subdivided into the following areas:
Basgora, Chotala, Dabran, Danna Nooral, Faqir Mohammad, Gali Batgran, Karkaki,
Langrial, Lassan and Manjia. Other villages are Kialapain and Basara.

- **RELIGION:** All the people of village & UC Langrial are Muslim.

- **CUSTOM:** In village Langrial the east custom especially Islamic east
custom is practiced.

- **LIFESTYLE:** The life style is simple and Islamic in village Langrial and
around. The main source of income is agro based but the people also have some
other jobs as well in private & public sectors. The literacy rate is now a
days around 45% and the youth is 90% literate. Both the men & women
contribute in the daily walk of life.

- **FACILITIES:** Although Langrial is
a rural and mountainous area but all the basic facilities are available in
village Langrial like Food, Pure water, Hospital, Roads, Schools, College, and
the means of communication.

- **SPORTS:** In sports the Cricket is the most famous and well known game
in village Langrial but some other games like football and kabadi are also
played in some places. Cricket series and the tournaments are played through
out the year at different grounds and places in Langrial.
