# Mirpur Hall Road Travel Guide

*Asia > Southasia > Pakistan > Azad Kashmir > Mirpur Hall Road*

The best resource for sights, hotels, restaurants, bars, what to do and see

Hall Road is the popular road running between check-post in Nathya and Mega Mart in chowk. Its really good to walk out on that stretch of road. You find yourself walking from the Nathya point where the area is illuminated at very basic levels. From a distance you see the chowk (round-about), all lit up with car show rooms and the Mega Mart. Its funny, a four mile road sort of boils down to a ten minute walk whilst chatting away with the local buds, deciding on whether to have a burgur or go to the mart to dine.

Sad thing is that if you're there (in mirpur) on a break and are enjoying your time, time really runs out and before you know it you're back at the airport heading away!!
