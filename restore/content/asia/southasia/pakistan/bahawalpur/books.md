# Books in Bahawalpur

*Asia > Southasia > Pakistan > Bahawalpur > Books*

Zahid Ali Khan got higher education in earth sciences but he worked as a political analyst, researcher,prose and poetry writer. His two books have been published so far. One is novel and second is poetic history of Bahawalpur. He always wanted to promote his local talent of different walks of life especially from the field of literature,education and politics. He wants to share his experience with his audience so he will use these social internet tools for his columns,comments,sketches and analysis on local,national and international current and other important issues. Bahawalpur is known as educational city since long when it was an indepent state. Last ruler of Bahawalpur state Sir Sadiq Muhammad Khan Abbasi was a great person.
