# Getting Around

*Asia > Southasia > Pakistan > Bahawalpur > Getting Around*

It was pre-planned picnic programe with friends to camp at river side, away from noisy life of city on weekend. So we planned to camp in area called Bait Bhutto, on the bank of river Chanab, almost 120 km away from my city, in south. We were 6 friends in Jeep, fully equipped with our own camps and other necessary items of cooking etc. We set up our camps, few friends made their arrangements for fishing in river...

It was wonderful sight when the sun was going down to give us magical night with crystal clear sky above and to wake people up in some other part of the world...

Friends were assigned jobs to do according to their potential and expertise... Since my expertise are considered to be best in photography and reading books, so I was assigned the same where as other were doing cooking, eracting tents, collecting wood to burn for fire etc...

The area is quite rich in agriculture as water for irrigation is available round the year, normally people there are prosperous and very hospitable. Many people settled there came to meet us with the desire to accept their kind offer of dinner at night or breakfast in the morning....

The most thrilling part such kind of picnic where I have to stay out for a night or day is to find a place to use as confined washroom, so for that I usually travel mile in a radius to b sure that the plcae is safe from path of passing by human beings, strolling animals etc............ therefore i prefer to finish all these natural jobs at night... that is also a good tip for all those who are not use to spend night out, away from luxuries of home....

I took so many pics, few were too good to represent the life style of people living there, their hardships, their customes etc.... I Shall be posting those pics as well here.... Thanks....

Jawad H. Sumra

Reaching at Picnic spot we went is quite eaasy....

If one has own conveince like jeep, then its very fast and comfertable mode of transport.

Otherwise there are multiple choice of public transport like Air Conditioned Buses, non A.C buses leaves after almost every 5 minutes from Bahwalpur to its South towards Rahim Yar Khan, Sadiq Abad and finaly to Karachi...

After reaching Tranda Muhammad Pannah, drop from ur Transport and take local mean of transport like Rickshaw and traveller has to hire motor bike to reach near river bank.
