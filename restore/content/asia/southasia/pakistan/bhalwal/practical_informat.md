**Manzoor Hayat colony **

** Manzoor Hayat colony is one of most important area of Bhalwal .It is named by the name of Manzoor hayat Noon, Who was the son of 7th Prime minister of Pakistan, Mr. Feroz Khan Noon.It has 18 streets and further divided into sub colonies and towns like Asmat colony,Ijaz colony,Hafiz town,Sakhi Sulman town etc.There are two Govt primary schools and many private schools.And the famous street is street no 12.**
