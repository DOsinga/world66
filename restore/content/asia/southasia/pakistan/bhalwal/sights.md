### [Ali Pur Noon](/asia/southasia/pakistan/bhalwal/sights/ali_pur_noon)

Ali Pur Noon is a historical village near Bhera in Sargodha District, Punjab , Pakistan . It was settled in the nineteenth century by the Noon family , one chief Malik Anwer Ali Noon son of Malik Sultan Ali Khan Noon of the village Sultan Pur Noon who moved here from Sardar Pur Noon along with their staff and people to cutivate the lands. Ali Pur Noon which is one of the twenty villages belonging to the Noon family was resided by the family chief. Ali Pur Noon is inhabited by over 24,000 people. ### [SULMAN PURA (ALI)](/asia/southasia/pakistan/bhalwal/sights/sulman_pura_ali)

SULMAN PURA are biggest urban town in Bhalwal.

It has 28 streets and a beautiful canal which is laid in the center of Bhalwal City. Sulman Pura is within distance to a general bus stand (2.2km), Railway station (2km), and a main Bazaar (1.3 Km).

Stolan Street is the 7th largest street in Sulman Pura and people call it the heart of the city.

### [Street # 07, Suleman pura Bhalwal](/asia/southasia/pakistan/bhalwal/sights/street__07_sulem)

Sulemanpura (Gubindpura) is an historical Muhallah along the canal in Bhalwal City Sargodha District, Punjab , Pakistan. It was settled in the nineteenth century by the Seikh Guru like Gubind Name and it is called Gubindpura. It has 28 streets and 5 beautiful Masjids, 1 Government Middle School with boys & girls and has many private schools. Sulemanpura has a population of 24,000. In the east, there is a beautiful canal and in the west, Mukhtar colony and in the North, Ashraf colony, and in the South, old Bhalwal Bypass.
