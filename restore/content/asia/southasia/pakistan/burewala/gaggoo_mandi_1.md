* **Gaggoo Mandi** * is a small town of tehsil Burewala district Vehari. There are various beliefs about naming * (GAGGOO) * this town. Widely believed is the one that the location was named after a **sub-tribe/kalan of Jutts that is * GHOG ***. **Mandi** the part of the name refers to a monthly animal market * (Mandi Moweshian) *. A famous and huge monthly gathering for hire / purchase of animals is regularly held near to this town at Chaka No 225/EB. People from all over Punjab and even of NWFP come to this site for this business concerns. Similarly the grain market of Gaggoo Mandi is also quite known in the region. People of the surrounding villages are mostly related with agriculture and live stock. There are good number of cotton innings factories in this town. The town is gradually developing and encompassing the neighboring villages.
