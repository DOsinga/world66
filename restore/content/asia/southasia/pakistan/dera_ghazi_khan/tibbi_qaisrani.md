Tibbi Qaisrani (Tibbi Kaura Khan Qaisrani) is well growing Union
Counsel of tehsil Taunsa. Population: 30,000 approximately There is
only one girls high school for this highly populated area, and a
secondary school for boys. This area is mostly agricultural and most of
the people belong to agriculture..
**This land produced many highly
dignified people. Sardar Kaura Khan Qaisrani was one of them. He was
the Hero of history. He faught against the English during Their reign.
In his honor this city was named as Tibbi Kaura Khan, later it was
changed to Tibbi Qaisrani. PTV (Pakistan Television) telecast a play
named "Kaura Khan" in a series of "Wafa Kay Paikar" to solute this
great Hero of history**
