# Top 5 Must Do's in Faisalabad

*Asia > Southasia > Pakistan > Faisalabad > Top 5 Must Dos*

When it comes to **Faisalabad must do's** the list is long and varied. Despite being a business city and not very old, the history and variety of activities for visitors of Faisalabad can’t be beat.

**1. Faisalabad Clock Tower**

The Faisalabad Clock Tower, or Ghenta Ghar as the locals call it, is one of Faisalabad's most popular attractions. This traditional open air market contains 8 bazaars that have a distinct pattern visible only from the air. The pattern that is visible is a Union Jack representative of the British Raj period during which time the Faisalabad Clock Tower was built.

**2. Shop at Rex City **

For a more modern shopping experience visit Rex City, Faisalabad's computer shopping mega center. This mall offers several discount computer, mobile and CD shops. All of your software and hardware needs can be met in this one-stop shop.

If you are looking for a more varied modern shopping experience visit Kohinoor One. This retail outlet has it all. There are restaurants, clothing shops and retail outlets of all kinds. The shopping in Faisalabad caters to a wide audience with something for everyone.

**3. Happy Land Water Park**

Happy Land Water Park is a complete entertainment center for family members of all ages. The park boasts the biggest waterslides in all of Pakistan. There are swings for all ages and a variety of other activities, making Happy Land the standard for international entertainment while in Faisalabad.

**4. Getwala Swimming Pool**

Getwala Swimming Pool is a local favorite. This huge pool attracts a big crowd, especially during the sweltering summer months. If you want to relax with a nice dip in one of Pakistan's largest pools, stop by Getwala with the family.

**5. Sinbad Amusement Park**

Sinbad Amusement Park is a more traditional park located in the heart of Faisalabad. This park has it all: flume rides, an arcade, as well as national and international cricket competitions.

Regardless of what you are looking for, Faisalabad should have something to suit your needs. This world renowned business city, the 2nd largest in Pakistan, offers around the clock entertainment and atmosphere for all ages.
