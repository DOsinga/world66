it is covered by Four Famous Mountain peaks ok Karokurm Regions. North is covered by 'Diran Peak', North East 'Papurash Peak', South East is covered by 'Dubani peak', and west is coverd by 'Rakapushi Peak'. Only enterance is from Bagrote river side(south).
