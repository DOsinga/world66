General InformationTOURIST ATTRACTIONS IN & AROUND HOTEL HUNZA EMBASSY

A tourist’s dream both for domestic and foreign. Karimabad (Baltit) was
capital of former Hunza state. It retains this status and is the heart
of Hunza. Baltit fort is the inheritor of many secrets of Ayesho Dynasty
and historical events. At the backside of Baltit fort a sequence of
peaks, showing their existence with their great heights Ulter I
& II (7350 m.) unclimbed until 1999. On the other side of Baltit is
Altit fort and the village which share and rival both the heritage and
history with Baltit and Baltit fort.
