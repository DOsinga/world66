# Olive Garden Restaurant

*Asia > Southasia > Pakistan > Islamabad > Eating Out > Olive Garden Resta*

**Olive Garden** is snuggly tucked away in the posh
locality of F-7/3 Islamabad. The intimate and gracious atmosphere of
the Olive Garden offers various delicacies to eat.
The aroma inside is terrific and it is
as clean and friendly as quick pizza places get. We focus on an
Italian menu with an emphasis on pizza.

**Atmosphere**: The place has a wood finished décor.

**More Reasons to Go**: The live music is
a great part of your dining experience at the restaurant. Friendly and
attentive waiters. Olive Garden is great for private parties.

**Eating & Drinking**: Open for lunch and dinner.

**Details to Note**: Delivery of the
complete menu is available. Catering for various events can be arranged
(call for details & quotes). Smoking is permitted in smoking areas.
Entrances and walkways are wheelchair friendly. Children are welcome.

**Ways to Pay**: Cash, MasterCard, Visa and American Express.
**Reservations are always welcome but not necessary except for larger parties or on weekends.**
