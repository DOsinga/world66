# Pakistan Museum of Natural History

*Asia > Southasia > Pakistan > Islamabad > Museums > Pakistanmuseumofnaturalhistory*

The Museum depicts the early human history, geology, and the wild life of Pakistan. The exhibits are of particular interest to students and children. It is open to public from 9:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m. except Friday. Entry is free.
