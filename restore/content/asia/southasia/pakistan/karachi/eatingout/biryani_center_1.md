# Biryani Center

*Asia > Southasia > Pakistan > Karachi > Eatingout > Biryani Center 1*

The name says it all. Biryani center is the place if one likes to eat biryani. Situated in the heart of karachi's up-n-coming food street in Husainabad. This restuarant provides good food at reasonable price. The restaurant is opens until the wee hours. Their salad and raita is also very good.*** ***
