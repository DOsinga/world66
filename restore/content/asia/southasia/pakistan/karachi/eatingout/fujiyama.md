# Fujiyama

*Asia > Southasia > Pakistan > Karachi > Eatingout > Fujiyama*

Authentic Japanese food while you enjoy panaromic views of the city from (I think) 15th or 16th floor of Avari Towers Hotel rooftop. My personal favourite restaurant in karachi for the past 12 years ! Great service, superb food, sophisticated ambience. Per person will cost about Rupees 800 (US$13).
