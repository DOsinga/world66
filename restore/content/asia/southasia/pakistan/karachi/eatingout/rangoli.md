# Rangoli

*Asia > Southasia > Pakistan > Karachi > Eatingout > Rangoli*

Rangoli,
an avalanche of colours, an oath to a culinary adventure, the
very symbol of a warm welcome and a revolution in the world of
delectable cuisines awaits you. Rangoli is located at Arena,
an impressive architectural structure housing a variety of
international standard games and attractions such as
ice-skating, miniature golf, rock climbing, paintball and
Brunswick bowling alleys.

At Rangoli, you'll step into a whirl of festive colours where
the aromas will beckon you towards a lavish, absolutely
mouth-watering buffet. This feast, which is indeed fit for a
king, plays host to more than 30 scrumptious dishes, 20 types
of fresh salads from around the world and 22 desserts to tide
over the sweet tooth Pakistanis are world renowned for. And
you need not worry about broken diets or expanding waist
lines, for our buffet holds sugar-free desserts so delicious
in taste that its unbelievable something so sinful is so low
in calories.

For those who enjoy classical and traditional tastes, we have
the all time favorite “Sajji” and “Ouzi”. These are
featured in the “Food Bazaar” which is a stones throw from
the buffet area. A magical place, which is an immaculate
miniature of the bazaars from the era of Shah Jehan and Mumtaz
Mehal.

Live
cooking is our special gift to culinary adventurists. We
strongly recommend the scrumptious “Prawn Tempura with Hot
and Sweet Sauce" and the succulent "Seekh
Kebab", that share the spotlight in the art of enticing
ones taste buds. Accompany them with a cold, sweet Lassi or
fresh seasonal juices as perfect compliments.

So every Arabian night after the sky becomes studded with
twinkling diamonds, we welcome you to get swept away into a
"RANGOLI" of unforgettable aesthetics that will
touch your very soul and make this colorful rendezvous a very
memorable experience.
