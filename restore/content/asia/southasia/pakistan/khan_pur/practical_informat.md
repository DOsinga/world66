**poets of khanpur**
Hafeez Shahid is a famous poet of khanpur.He wrote five books of poetry.
[1]Safer Roshni Ka
[2]Charagh e Harf
[3]Mahtab e Ghazal
[4]Ye Darya Par Kerna Hai
[5]Fasila Dermeyan Wohi Hai Abhi
He has also written lyrics for two punjabi films in 70s.He lives in Jinah town.His sixth book is soon coming by name "Sooraj badal raha hai".
By Yawer Azeem Yawer
