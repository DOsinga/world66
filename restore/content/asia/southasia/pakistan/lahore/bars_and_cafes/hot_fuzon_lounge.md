# Hot Fuzon Lounge

*Asia > Southasia > Pakistan > Lahore > Bars And Cafes > Hot Fuzon Lounge*

The Hot Fuzon Lounge on M. M. Alam Road in Gulberg, Lahore has become a
hot favorite of Lahoris in the last few months. The owners Maryam and
Sikander started with a location in Defense and then, a few months ago,
opened the M.M.Alam Road location, right below Cafe Zouk (another
popular hangout that has gracefully survived the onslaught of
competition for almost 15 years now).Hot Fuzon is a fully Wi-Fi

In
a setting that inspires long conversations and impromptu get togethers
with friends, Hot Fuzon initially offered a menu of interesting
cocktails and many variations of coffee, with sandwiches, burgers, and
umpteen varieties of dessert. Hot Fuzon launched a full menu with many
appetizers and entrees, to add to its earlier 'lounge' menu. This will
undoubtedly make Hot Fuzon's customers happier, because now Hot Fuzon
can be their one-stop shop for all they want to eat.
