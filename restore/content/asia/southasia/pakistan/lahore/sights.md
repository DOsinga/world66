# Sights

*Asia > Southasia > Pakistan > Lahore > Sights*

**general**

**Tomb of Emperor Jahangir **

With its beautiful surrounding gardens the tomb is a must see. Jahangir was one of the most important Moghul emperors.  

**Lahore Fort**

The Bara Dari Pavilion and the Shish Mahal (Palace of Mirrors) in the Lahore Fort are great sights. In the fort a sound and light show is held Monday Thursday and Saturday.  ** fariha:** lahore fort is the best building of lahore and i like it so much.

**Shalimar Garden**

Designed by the architect of the Taj Mahal 

**Bagh-e-Jinnah Park**

A lively city park with a grand library 

**Badshahi Mosque**

The second largest in the country after the King Faisal Mosque in Islamabad

**Lahore Museum **

16 galleries containing carpets Korans and 1 500 miniature paintings. 

**Kim’s gun (Zamzama) **

Kim's gun was made famous by Rudyard Kipling book. It is located in front of Punjab University.
