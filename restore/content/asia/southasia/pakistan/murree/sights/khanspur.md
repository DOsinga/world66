# Marian Tomb

*Asia > Southasia > Pakistan > Murree > Sights > Khanspur*

Many locals believe that Mary, mother of Jesus, is buried in Murree and
that the town is named after her (formerly Mari). Her supposed tomb sits
on a hill underneath a television tower. Locals can point you in the
right direction. You may need special permission to visit.
