# Sights

*Asia > Southasia > Pakistan > Nowshera > Sights*

Kana Khel [Bahadar Baba] a place worth visiting. Tourists from all the
country and especially from the province rush at the place on
occassions like eid and other religious festivals.The place has got a
unique history of its kind.One can enjoy and may have desires for the
future when once the place is visited.It is just at a distance of 20
minutes, the road is a bit hard but it doesn't stop people to spend
their holidays at the adventurous hill station.
