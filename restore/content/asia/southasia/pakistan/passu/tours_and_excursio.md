**ITINERARY – 22 days / 21 nights ** 



Day 01 Arrival Islamabad. 

Day 02 Islamabad / Peshawar (approx. 4-5 hrs driving). 

Day 03 Peshawar – Day excursion to Khyber Pass and Overnight Peshawar. 

Day 04 Peshawar / Bumburet Kalash Valley (approx. 7-8 hrs). 

Day 05 Bumburet (Free day to explore the Bumburet Village and experience the life style of Kalash). 

Day 06 Explore Rumbur valley and then Drive to Chitral. Overnight Chitral. (Approx. 4-5 hrs). 

Day 07 Drive to Shandur Pass (3734 m). Overnight camping. 

Day 08 Drive to Phandar, Ghizer Valley. (Approx. 4-5 hrs). Famous for it trout fishes, You can enjoy fishing in this beautiful valley. Overnight Phandar. 

Day 09 Drive to Gilgit (Approx. 4-5 hrs). Overnight Gilgit. 

Day 10 Drive to Karimabad. (Approx. 3 hrs). Overnight Karimabad. 

Day 11 Day hike to Duiker base camp, (approx. 3-4 hrs trekking) .Afternoon explore Karimabad Bazaar. Overnight Karimabad. 

Day 12 Drive from Karimabad / Khunjarab Pass (approx. 6-7 hrs). Overnight Passu. 

Day 13 Drive from Passu to Shimshal valley. (Approx.3 hrs).Recently opens for jeep. Overnight camping. 

Day 14 Shimshal / Passu. Overnight passu. 

Day 15 Passu / Gilgit. 

Day 16 Gilgit / Skardu (approx. 6 hrs). 

Day 17 Day excursion to Shiger Valley, and back to Skardu for overnight stay. 

Day 18 Skardu / Deosai (4-5 hrs). Overnight camping. 

Day 19 Drive to Rama, Astor valley. (Approx.5-6 hrs). Overnight camping. 

Day 20 Excursion to Rama Lake (3482 m). From here you can view Chongra Mountain, part of Naga Parbat. And then drive back to Chilas. Overnight chilas. 

Day 21 Drive to Islamabad. (approx. 11 hrs). Overnight Islamabad. 

Day 22 Fly back to home.
