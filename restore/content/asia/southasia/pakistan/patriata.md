# Patriata

*Asia > Southasia > Pakistan > Patriata*

Patriata is a hill resort some 25 km from [Murree](/asia/southasia/pakistan/murree). It has become quite popular over recnet years. There is a chairlift and cable car system, which takes visitors upto Patriata Ridge in two stages from Gulehra Gali. The first stage is by chair lift up to Patriata Bazaar. From here visitors transfer to cable car gondolas for the ride up to Patriata Ride. A restaurant has opene up on the top.
