# Getting There

*Asia > Southasia > Pakistan > Quetta > Getting There*

Quetta is connected to the rest of the country by road, rail and air. The R.C.D. Highway connects it to [Karachi](/asia/southasia/pakistan/karachi) and then on (via Koh-e-Taftan) to [Tehran](/asia/middleeast/iran/tehran), Iran, 1435 kms away. The road to Sibi connects it with Punjab and upper Sindh. The road via Loralai - Fort Munro -D.G. Khan and [Multan](/asia/southasia/pakistan/multan) is a short route for Punjab. The Chaman Road is a link between the county and the Afghan border. Quetta is linked by PIA with Karachi, Lahore and Islamabad by daily flights.
