# Cafe Lala G

*Asia > Southasia > Pakistan > Rahimyarkhan > Bars And Cafes > Muazzam*

Cafe Lala G is the most popular Restaurant in Rahim Yar Khan city. It is situated on the main shahi road near iqbal complex. This is a very good restaurant as well as a living motel. Cafe Lamis covers the Continental, BBQ & Chinese Food. A good place for the tourists and foreigners to stay and eat.The decor and ambience are second to none. the service is excellent and Lahorites will find it easily challenging the standards they are used to.

A must for all those who wish to dine in class!
