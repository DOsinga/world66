# Khair Garh Fort

*Asia > Southasia > Pakistan > Rahimyarkhan > Sights > Khair Garh Fort*

Remnants and ruins of this fort are located, about 40 kilometers south of Khanpur city, in the Cholistan area. In 1189 A.H. Haji Khan, son of Ikhtiar Khan built it and named it Khair Garh.
