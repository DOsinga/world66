# Museums in Sargodha

*Asia > Southasia > Pakistan > Sargodha > Museums*

Pakistan has some of the most famous museums in Asia. If you are traveling to the area be sure to see some of the history and culture that is presented in the Pakistan museums. Of all of the museums in Pakistan that are worth the time to visit, the National Museum of Pakistan is the one that everyone should see.

**History**

This museum was introduced in 1949 and features some of the great pieces of art and culture from ancient civilizations including records and manuscripts from ancient times. Anyone who enjoys learning about history will be sure to enjoy the items on display. Walking through the museum people will be able to travel through history from the early civilizations to more modern times. The giant map of the city details where and when the various buildings were constructed as well as other interesting facts about the country of Pakistan.

**Art**

Among the many displays in the museum, people can see the best of what every culture has contributed to the area. There are unique pieces of history kept in the museum and some of the art pieces are one of a kind. The art ranges from ancient to more modern and depicts each of the century’s customs and heritage through paintings, pottery and other art forms on display for everyone to enjoy. It is also a great way to learn more about the ancient cultures and see what some civilizations were able to create. Anyone who enjoys looking at art will find many great pieces to study at the National Museum of Pakistan.
