# Bhera

*Asia > Southasia > Pakistan > Sargodha > Sights > Bhera*

A city in the Sargodha District. It is home town of Sufi Saint Hazrat Mehar Ali Shah. Alexandar
the Great fought with Porus around this city. The bank of River Jehlum
is also near by and good picnic point.
