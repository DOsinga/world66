# Getting There in Sialkot

*Asia > Southasia > Pakistan > Sialkot > Getting There*

By plane

Sialkot International Airport has started functioning in 2007 and at present, flights are available from and to Karachi . PIA would start non-stop flight between Sialkot to Manchester and Dubai as well as Haj flights from Sialkot International Airport early in 2008. Emirates is also expected to start its flights early 2008 to Dubai . Airblue will operate domestically from Islamabad , Multan and Karachi early 2008.
By train

Sialkot used to be a junction in British time with trains leaving for Jammu and Gudaspur. Plans are under consideration to open the border for an international train between Sialkot and Jammu . Express trains to and from Lahore , Rawalpindi , Faisalabad , Multan , Narowal and Karachi are available daily. The Railway station is situated in the center of the city. Other suburb train stations are Ugoki and Sambrial.
By car

Sialkot is attached with National Highway (N-5) through Gujranwala and Wazirabad. A duel carriage way is under construction between Sialkot and Wazirabad. From Lahore it's about 2 hours' drive while from Islamabad it's about 4 hours. Sialkot-Lahore Motorway (M-9) is also under construction.
By bus

All the buses and coach stations are located on Jail Road . Daeewoo Bus service is available from Rawalpindi , Lahore , Gujranwala and Mulatn. By coach, Sialkot is about 2.5 hrs from Lahore and 4.5 hrs from Islamabad .
