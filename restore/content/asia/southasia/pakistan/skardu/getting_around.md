# Getting Around in Skardu

*Asia > Southasia > Pakistan > Skardu > Getting Around*

As **Skardu** is a well known mountaineering paradise on the earth, there are a number of possiblities for visitors and mountain lovers depending upon the passion, skills and physical conditions of the visitor.

There are many destinations accessible through a 4x4 jeep, horse riding and along the world's wonders in the remote valleys by trekking. **Deo Sai** plains and mountains, **Kachura** Lake, **Satpara** Lake, **Khaplu**, **Korpochu** Fort and many other places are easily accessible if u hire a public or private jeep from the Skardu bazar.

The remote valleys and the treks along these valleys are mostly for those who are fit and having enough passion and walking capabilities for long days and a number of days depending on the trek selection.

**Shigar** valley, most famous in the world, gateway for the world's highest peaks, largest galcier and world's most popular mountaineering trek **'Concordia - Baltoro'**. At the end of the trek, **K-2**, **Broad Peak**, **Gasherbrum** range (8000 meter peaks) and many over 7000 meters peaks throught the way dont let you think anything else.

**Hushe**, another valley of Baltistan, famous for its rough and though **Karakoram** adventures. **Masherbrum** peak at the end of the valley and many other possible treks around Hushe are the reason for attraction for many travllers of the world.

In addition there are so many options a mountain and adventure travller may count on as the unique experience and wonderful time spend in Karakorams.
