# Full Day Kandy Tour

*Asia > Southasia > Srilanka > Day Trips > Full Day Kandy Tou 1*

pick up at 8AM from your hotel or airport if you are transiting at airport and drive to to Kandy enroute we will stop at Pinnawala Elephant orphanage where you can see the elephants being raised with great care and affection. You can take pictures of elephants as they take bath at the nearby river.
Later we will continue to Kandy this royal city, sheltered by the hills and looped by the Mahaweli the longest river of Sri Lanka. We will take you straight to the temple of the tooth relic. Later you will visit the botanical gardens of Kandy and spice and fruits market.
Return at 4 - 5 PM
