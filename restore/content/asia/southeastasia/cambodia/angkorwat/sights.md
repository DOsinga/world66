# Sights

*Asia > Southeastasia > Cambodia > Angkorwat > Sights*

Angkor Wat is best visited in the afternoon when the sinking sun lights up its west-facing main gate. Many tourists also come just before dawn to see the first lights bounce off the magnificent temple when the sun peeps through the horizon. Those who have seen it in the moonlight said it’s a magical experience but tight security has since been imposed to prevent looting and tourists are not allowed to stay on after dark.
