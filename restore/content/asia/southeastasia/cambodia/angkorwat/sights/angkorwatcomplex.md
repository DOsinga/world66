# Angkor Wat Complex

*Asia > Southeastasia > Cambodia > Angkorwat > Sights > Angkorwatcomplex*

The architectural masterpiece is perfect in its composition, balance and proportions and ranks as one of the finest monuments in the world. Fortunately, it is also one of the best preserved. Buddhist priests occupied the temple after the Thais sacked the capital city of Angkor Thom in 1432 and helped keep it in its pristine condition over the centuries.
