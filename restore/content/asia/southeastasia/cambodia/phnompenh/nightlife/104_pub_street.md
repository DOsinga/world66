# 104 Pub Street

*Asia > Southeastasia > Cambodia > Phnompenh > Nightlife > 104 Pub Street*

Not just one bar but now seven and soon to be eight bars located in close proximity. On 104 street there is the aptly named OneZeroFour, Power Bar, Barazza, Zanzibar, Monsoon, Rose Bar, Pickled Parrot. Just around the corner is Cafe Fluer DÁsie and the Green Vespa. All very nice bars and a good alternative to 51st. OneZeroFour is a sister bar to the legendary DV8 bar on street 148, just around the corner from Jungle Bar.
