# DV8 Bar & Guesthouse

*Asia > Southeastasia > Cambodia > Phnompenh > Nightlife > Dv8*

A great way to start or finish your night in Phnom Penh. Ideal for the single man but also very friendly to everyone.

A relaxed bar with friendly and appreciative staff. DV8 offers some of
the best rock music, pool table, indoor and outdoor seating and
accommodation id you need it. A small bar but a fun bar, suited best
for single men or women looking for a new experience. The pool table
and balcony on the second floor has been the scene of many any
interesting night.
