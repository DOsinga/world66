# Foreign Correspondents Club

*Asia > Southeastasia > Cambodia > Phnompenh > Nightlife > Foreigncorrespondentsclub*

This place is always full of journalists, photographers and many expatriates who live and work in the city. The walls are covered with black and white, documentary style photographs. It's a little more expensive than other places but is definitely worth a visit. Music is The music is usually late 60's and early 70's rock. ** :** Very good place to have a drink a relax. Beautiful view over the Tonle Sap river. However, after 9 pm there is no more action there.
