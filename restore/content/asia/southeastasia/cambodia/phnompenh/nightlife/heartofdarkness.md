# Heart of Darkness

*Asia > Southeastasia > Cambodia > Phnompenh > Nightlife > Heartofdarkness*

Not closed. And not only expats. And defiantely a pickup bar. The heart is dead quiet until around 11pm, when it starts to get a little crazy - it's non-stop from there until the wee hours of the morning. It can get rough at times, but that's what happens when you pack a dance floor not much bigger than a couple of pool tables with drunken louts - expat and local, men and woman.
