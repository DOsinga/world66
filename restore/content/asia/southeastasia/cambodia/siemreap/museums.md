**Silk Factory**

Artisans D'Angkor runs a silk farm and manufacturing facility about 20 minutes outside of town.  A full tour and thorough explanation of work in English is given for free, with a tour guide for each visitor group, no matter how small.  The facility is nice to see.  You will need to hire a car [more..](/asia/southeastasia/cambodia/siemreap/museums/silkfactory)
