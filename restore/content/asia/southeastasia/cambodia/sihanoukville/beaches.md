# Beaches

*Asia > Southeastasia > Cambodia > Sihanoukville > Beaches*

**all**

**Ochheuteal Beach **

Ochheuteal Beach is becoming touristy. Many hotels can be found on the developed end along with some great seafood restaurants.  

**Sokha Beach **

This beach seems to be the most popular. The southern end which is a short walk is recommended to do some snorkeling.
