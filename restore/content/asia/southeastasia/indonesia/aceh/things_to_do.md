# Things to do

*Asia > Southeastasia > Indonesia > Aceh > Things To Do*

Fantastic diving on Pulau Weh (called "Sabang" by the locals, the name of the city on the island). If you're certified, expect to pay €25 for your first dive, €20 for every dive afterward.

Aceh is also great for hiking and mountain biking and bicycling. More information can be found on the Aceh Trek and MTB site and the Aceh Adventure Site (http://www.aceh-adventure.org). The website of the Aceh Bicycle community is another great resouce of information ].

Surfing is great at Lok'na beach where you can also rent boards and do surfing courses.
