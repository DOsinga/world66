# Nightlife and Entertainment

*Asia > Southeastasia > Indonesia > Bali > Denpasar > Nightlife*

When you want to experience the real nightlife on Indonesian ground, you’ll probably have to move on to Kuta. Denpasar has some nice bars but the real thing is happening in and around Kuta. If you still feel like dancing after a whole day of sun, sand and fresh air, the clubs and bars will be at your beck and call in Kuta.
