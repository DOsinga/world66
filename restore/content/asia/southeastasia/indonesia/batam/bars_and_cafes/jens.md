# Jens

*Asia > Southeastasia > Indonesia > Batam > Bars And Cafes > Jens*

There is an excellent new bar close to the ferry terminal at Harbour Bay (Batu Ampar). The name is Ar-bar. Friendly staff, plain and elegant design. The bar is in short walking disance from the ferry terminal. I spent my waiting time there last time I took the ferry to Singapore. The Ar-bar is still not known by all but certainly worth it!
