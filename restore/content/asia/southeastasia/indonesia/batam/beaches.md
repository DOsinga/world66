# Beaches in Batam

*Asia > Southeastasia > Indonesia > Batam > Beaches*

One of the popular tourist destinations in Southeast Asia is Batam
Island, Indonesia. **Batam Beaches** are the biggest draw on the island. The
beaches here offer everything one needs for a great vacation.

**Water Front City Beach Area **

If you are here to enjoy the water sports activities then Water Front
City Area of the island town is the perfect beach area for you.
Situated at a walking distance on the western side of the island, this
beach area offers excellent water sports facilities for all age groups.
You can enjoy paddle boating, para-sailing and banana boat rides among
other water activities here. The Water Front City Area also offers
a swimming facility for children. Here is the place where you can spend
hours with your family during your trip to Batam. There are several
hotels in the area so that you do not have to stay away from your place
of interest. The beach is a few-minutes walk from Holiday Inn Hotel
in Batam Island.

**Barelang Beach Area**

This beach area is preferred by people who love a tranquil atmosphere
away from the maddening crowd. It is perfect place to sit and enjoy the
waves dancing to the tunes of the breeze. Barelang Beaches are the
best places to view the ocean and the numerous islands. The beaches here
offer restaurants, changing rooms and toilets. Barelang Beach Area offers
an excellent atmosphere for relaxation with nature. It is a perfect place for
honeymoon couples or lovers. The area is situated in the south of the
Batam Island and you have to hire a taxi to reach it.

**Nongsa Beach Area **

Nongsa is a very famous tourist destination in Batam. It is situated
in the northern part of the island and close to Nongsapura Ferry
Terminal which is the main ferry center in Batam that connects to
Singapore. The area has several beaches and resorts which offer the
tourist swimming as well as water sports activities like para-sailing,
snorkeling in the reef area, boat rides. Turi Beach Resort and Batam
View Beach Resort are two renowned resorts along side the beaches
which offer excellent views of the ocean and island. You can enjoy
fishing trips in the Nongsa River or have a boat tour arranged by
these resorts.
