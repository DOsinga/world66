# Shopping in Batam

*Asia > Southeastasia > Indonesia > Batam > Shopping*

Batam is an island in Indonesia and is a popular tourist
destination. The island is also popular for its shopping areas since it is part
of the free trade zone. In 2006 a resolution was passed which made Batam part
of a Special Economic Zone together with Singapore and eliminated VAT and other
tariffs for goods shipped between Batam and Singapore. This has made Batam
become a shoppers’ paradise. There are several places where you can get great
discounts on a variety of products.

**
Center Point**

Jalan Imam Bonjoi

This one of the more upscale shopping centers in Batam and
it is popular with Singaporeans. The mall has several shops, restaurants,
supermarkets and much more. If you are looking to spend part of the morning shopping,
this is a good place to begin. Several big hotels have their own shuttle
services to pick up and drop their customers to this shopping mall.

**DC Mall**

This is one of the largest shopping malls in Batam. The mall
has several brand clothing stores like, Abercrombie & Fitch, Fila and
Esprit. You can get good clothes at bargain prices of $10 at the mall. The mall
has many shops unoccupied from the time the casinos in the mall were closed.

**Robinson &
Ramayana**

This is an authentic Indonesian shopping mall where you will
not find many foreigners. However, if you visit any country, taking a trip to
one of the local shopping malls is a good idea; you will be able to get some
amazing bargains. This shopping mall is the perfect place to pick up some
authentic Indonesian clothing and a host of other things. There are several
places where you can eat at in the mall, both Indonesian and western cuisine is
available. The mall is open every day from 9:30 a.m. to 10 p.m.

**Lucky Plaza**

Nagaya Town

This is a good place to pick up electronic items including
CDs and DVDs. There is virtually every electronic item on display at the shops
in the plaza, but remember to bargain to get a good price. The mall also shops
selling shoes, clothes and make-up for ladies.

**Panbil Mall**

At Panbil Mall you can get almost anything you need at very
reasonable prices. The mall is a popular stop for many working class people
staying nearby. The smaller shops in the mall offer good prices and allow
bargaining, but the bigger departmental stores do not allow bargaining. This is
a good place to pick up stuff at real Indonesian prices.
