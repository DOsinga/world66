# Batam Golf

*Asia > Southeastasia > Indonesia > Batam > Things To Do > Batam Golf*

Batam boasts several championship golf courses. Prices are are a fraction of what you would pay in nearby Singapore.

Those wishing to make a break of it and organise a Batam Golf break should check out coconut-golf who have accommodation 5 minutes away from the first tee at Indah Puri which is probably the best course on the island as far as degree of difficulty goes.
