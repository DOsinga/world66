# Rawa Pening Lake

*Asia > Southeastasia > Indonesia > Java > Ambarawa > Sights > Rawa Pening Lake*

Rawa Pening lake is a good object for those who like photography. Sunrise comes out from the other side of the lake and can be seen a simple daily activity from the locals. Like fishermen going out with their boats to the heart of the lake to catch the fish.

Believe it or not, the hills where visitors can see the sunrise named as *Bukit Cinta* [Love Hills].
