# Things to do in Borobudur

*Asia > Southeastasia > Indonesia > Java > Borobudur > Things To Do*

If you go to Borobudur, but you not riding elephant surrounding Borobudur temple.. mmphh.... that's you'rs loss...
Making travel through villages and having directly experience of Javanese's traditional life.
While traveling on the elephant you could enjoy the beauty of nature, such as the wide greeny farming area with Borobudur temple at the left-side background and Menoreh hill at the right-side.
During 2,5 hours on the elephant you will have an exciting and unforgetable experience.
