# Foodstalls

*Asia > Southeastasia > Indonesia > Java > Jakarta > Eatingout > Foodstalls1*

All around you can find local vendors selling food. When your stomach has finally accustomed to the local food it is a must to try the local sate kambing(goat satay; goat meat roasted on a coal fire), bakmi goreng (fried noodles), nasi goreng (fried rice) and rujak (Indonesian style fruit salad).

It was said the taste of the food sold by the street vendors are (sometimes) better than those sold at a 5-star hotel!
