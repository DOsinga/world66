# Hot Planet

*Asia > Southeastasia > Indonesia > Java > Jakarta > Eatingout > Hot Planet*

Open 24 hours and situated in Sarinah Building, within walking distance in jalan jaksa. Serving delicious meals from breakfast type of menu [available until 11 o'clock], lunch and dinner with variety of dessert.

Several fave choices in Hot Planet: NZ beefsteak, Laksa Singapore, Club Sandwiches, Chicken or Meat zizzling steak and ice cream rujak [Indonesian fruit salad served with cups of vanila ice cream].

Beside at Jalan Thamrin, chain of Hot Planet also available in Bintaro Plaza and Blok M Plaza.
