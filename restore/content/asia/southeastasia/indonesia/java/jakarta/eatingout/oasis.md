# Oasis

*Asia > Southeastasia > Indonesia > Java > Jakarta > Eatingout > Oasis*

One of the top end boutique restaurants in Jakarta. Already got several achievement from tourism board of Indonesia, also several from foreign countries.

The most valuable from this restaurant is, the place itself. It used to be an Indonesian famous painter Raden Saleh's house. So you can find several of his masterpieces here, plus several collection of wooden statues and kitcheneries. And made you surprise, since the famous rijstaffel served with old ceramic porcelain wares which are his private collection.

Rijstaffel is something "do not miss" thing from Oasis. But the receptionist will mentioned minimum package is for 3 pax. Since rijstaffel will be served by 12 maids who bring manykind of the delicacies, from steam rice, chicken in heavy coconut milk, meat in chili sauce, shrimp crackers, friend banana etc. They'll come to your table and you'll pick from the platters they're bring next to us. It's very enjoyable and while enjoy our meals, Batak's folk songs will performed live by mini group contents of 4 person wearing Ulos [traditional Batak's saroong].
