# Cazbar

*Asia > Southeastasia > Indonesia > Java > Jakarta > Nightlife > Cazbar*

The cazbar is a bussiness style cafe offering free Wi-Fi internet, daily imported newspapers, and is opened from 7am till 12pm. An extensive food menu offers full breakfasts and Asian & European food. The upstairs sports bar offers all popular sports with a focus on Saturday and Sunday night English Premier League action, rugby, cricket, formula 1 etc. Every Friday Blues Jam Session and every Saturday a live acoustic band Its location in Mega Kuningan, close to the Ritz Carlton, Marriott, Bellagio, Gran melia, is easily reachable from Sudirman & Kuningan.
