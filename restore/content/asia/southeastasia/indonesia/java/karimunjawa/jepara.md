# Jepara Travel Guide

*Asia > Southeastasia > Indonesia > Java > Karimunjawa > Jepara*

The best resource for sights, hotels, restaurants, bars, what to do and see

The entry point to Karimunjawa archipelago by ferry from Kartini harbour.

Jepara also known as Kota Ukir or Carving City. Many craftmen live here to make beautiful wooden carvings. From drawer, *gebyok* [carved doorway], manykind of furniture until statues.
