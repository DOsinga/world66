# Getting There

*Asia > Southeastasia > Indonesia > Lake Maninjau > Getting There*

From Tabing airport in Padang, get a metered taxi to direction Maninjau. Or, take a bus outside of the airport to direction Pariaman and Bukittinggi. Then change a bus to direction Lubuk Basung via Maninjau.
