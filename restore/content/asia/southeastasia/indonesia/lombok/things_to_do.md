# Things to do in Lombok

*Asia > Southeastasia > Indonesia > Lombok > Things To Do*

If you hit Lombok and you are surfer or wannabe surfer, don't miss the opportunity to ride some of the best waves.
No need to go to Desert point, you can have a pretty decent surf in Kuta or Senggigi.
