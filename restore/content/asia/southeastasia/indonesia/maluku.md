# Maluku

*Asia > Southeastasia > Indonesia > Maluku*

The islands of Maluku have great beaches, old forts and good diving. [Ambon](/asia/southeastasia/indonesia/maluku/ambon) is the main island, [Ternate](/asia/southeastasia/indonesia/maluku/ternate) has great volcanic scenery, the [Banda islands](/asia/southeastasia/indonesia/maluku/bandaislands) are true gems: old forts, the Gunung Api volcano and great beaches to sun, dive and tan.

## Destinations

- [Ambon](/asia/southeastasia/indonesia/maluku/ambon)
- [Banda islands](/asia/southeastasia/indonesia/maluku/bandaislands)
- [Ternate](/asia/southeastasia/indonesia/maluku/ternate)
