# Practical Information

*Asia > Southeastasia > Indonesia > Nias > Practical Informat*

Internet cafes. There are no internet cafes in the capital (March 2006) but UN or NGO offices on the main road may allow you to connect your laptop to their wireless system.

ATM. There are ATMs in Gunungsitoli, however, the maximum amount for each withdrawal is small. Three withdrawals a day are possible for up to about USD 320 or so.
