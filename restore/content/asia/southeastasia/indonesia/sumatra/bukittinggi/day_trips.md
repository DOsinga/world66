# Day Trips

*Asia > Southeastasia > Indonesia > Sumatra > Bukittinggi > Day Trips*

One nice day trip is to Lake Maninjau, which is a nice lake just about 2 - 3 hours by bus. Go to the bus terminal and take a local bus to lake maninjau. Costs about 6000 Rp (April 2004). You can also rent a motorbike there and drive around the lake...
