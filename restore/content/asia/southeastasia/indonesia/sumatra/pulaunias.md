# Pulau Nias

*Asia > Southeastasia > Indonesia > Sumatra > Pulaunias*

One of Indonesia's most exotic destinations, Nias Island combines a great surfing break, the most beautiful of beaches and a culture older than history.

 Nias has the size of [Bali](/asia/southeastasia/indonesia/bali) but outside of the months of June and July it is a quiet place and great to relax. The most important places on the island are Gunung Sitoli in the north, and Teluk Daram in the south. Teluk Daram is close to Lagundri which has the best beaches and surfing.
