# Lao

*Asia > Southeastasia > Laos > Luangprabang > Eatingout > Lao*

It is highly recommended to stroll around the city and check out places where the locals eat. Along the Mekong River you can find several small open-air restaurants with quite good Lao food.  

**Restaurants**

**Malee Lao Food**

A small eatery serves good Lao foon on Thanon Phu Wao. Try the Green Papaya Salad. 

address: Thanon Phu Wao

**Lao Food Vegetarian**

Small garden cafe that serves traditional Lao food. 

address: Near Villa Santi
