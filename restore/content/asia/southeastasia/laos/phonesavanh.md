# Phonesavanh

*Asia > Southeastasia > Laos > Phonesavanh*

One of the last mysteries of Asia, the **Plain of Jars**, lies near to **Phonesavanh**. Despite many theories and speculation by archaeologists, there is no accepted explanation for the countless 8 feet high earthernware jars lying scattered over the plateau.  Some of the jars were destroyed by the secret American bombing of [Laos](/asia/southeastasia/laos). A visit to area is enhanced by visits to the local ethnic minority villages of the Hmong.
