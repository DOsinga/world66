# Bor Pen Nyang Bar & Restaurant

*Asia > Southeastasia > Laos > Vientiane > Eatingout > Bor Pen Nyang Bar*

4th Floor Roof top Bar with amazing western speciality dishes (such as bangers & mash, chicken parmigianna etc) as well as having an extensive local dish selection. The best view of the mekong you will ever see. A real sight when there is a good sunset.

Also great music and lots of entertainment. The BPN also offers the finest whisky selection in asia and will stock just about any liquer you can think of (includ. absinthe which is on offer at $2 a shot every thursday... watch it).

Recently added this year, is the new secong floor music room which will feature a live band playing every night.
