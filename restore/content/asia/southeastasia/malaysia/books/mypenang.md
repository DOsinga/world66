# myPenang

*Asia > Southeastasia > Malaysia > Books > Mypenang*

A personal travel guide on Penang, Malaysia. An inside guide to Penang
written by a Malaysian author who wishes to share the many attractions
and treasures with you. This guide book is filled with interesting,
useful information and fully illustrated with colour photos and
maps. Get tips on how to get there, where to go and what to eat.
Enjoy what the Pearl of the Orient has to offer and savour the best of
Asia's street food.
