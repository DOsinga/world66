# Nightlife and Entertainment

*Asia > Southeastasia > Malaysia > Cameronhighlands > Nightlife*

There is not much entertainment at night, but you can practice your skills as a singer. Karaoke bars are very popular with the local population. Most places close at midnight. 

**Place to go outs**

**Pines Lounge**

A Karaoke bar located at Rosa Passadena in Brinchang. Phone: +60-5-4912288. 

**Infinite Gain Karaoke**

Infinite Gain Karaoke bar on Jalan Besar at Brinchang. Phone: +60-5-4911091. Open from 8pm - midnight.  

**Twilight Zone Disco**

Located at Strawberry Park Resort, Lot 195 & 196 in Tanah Rata. One of the few places for dancing. Phone: +60-5-4911166.
