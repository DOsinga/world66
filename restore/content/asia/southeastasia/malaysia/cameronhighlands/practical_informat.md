# Practical Information in Cameron highlands

*Asia > Southeastasia > Malaysia > Cameronhighlands > Practical Informat*

**Cameron Highland Practical Information** is an important tool for the one planning to make a trip to the beautiful city. The information includes the financial, demographic, climate, health and cultural points. These highlands are situated about 20 km to the east of Ipoh, on the east border between Perak and Pahang states. It is about 1500 meter above sea level.

**Population**

In the Cameron Highlands, the population noted as per the year 2007 is 34,800 with the density of population of 48.87 per square km. Total area of the city is 712 square km. Fifty-seven percent of the population is Malays, while the rest are Chinese, Indians and some other ethnic groups.

The literacy rate noted here is 88 percent. Internet facilities are readily available.

**Religion and Language**

Islam is the official religion of Cameron Highlands. Mainly the people residing here belong to the Muslim community. But the other religions too are practiced here. These include Christiaity, Buddhism, Hindu, Confucianism, Daoism, Sikhs and Animist.The official language of the Cameron Highlands is Bahasa Melayu (also known as Malay).

**Weather**

Cameron Highland is situated on the east coast of Peninsular Malaysia. It is basically in a tropical region. It enjoys a cool and pleasant atmosphere with temperatures not rising above 20 degree C. The temperature rarely falls below 10 degrees C. The rainy season here falls from September to December. It is rare to see a completely clear sky on the normal days of the year.

**Currency**

The currency of Cameron Highlands is ‘Ringgit’ RM. It is divided into 100 sen. You can find here the notes ranging from 1 RM to 100 RM. The coins are from 1 Sen to 50 Sen. You can find the image of ‘Tuanku Abdul Rahman.’ the first president of Cameron Highlands is pictured on all the notes of the Ringgit.

**Notes**

If you are planning a trip to the Cameron Highlands, you should have a tourist visa. For business purposes you should have the business visas. An international license for driving purpose is mandatory here. For consuming alcohol, you need to have the license which can be taken in licensed bar.
