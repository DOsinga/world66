# Geological Museum

*Asia > Southeastasia > Malaysia > Ipoh > Museums > Geologicalmuseum*

As you'd expect from a place rich as geologically interesting as Ipoh, there is the Geological Museum, established in 1957 and housing over 600 geological specimens of minerals, ores, precious stones and metals, fossils and apparently the world's best examples of cassiterite.
