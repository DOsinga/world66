# Shopping

*Asia > Southeastasia > Malaysia > Johorbahru > Shopping*

Johor Bahru is a shopper’s paradise. Situated in a place
which is a main transit point into and out of Singapore, Johor Bahru is an upscale
shopping city. There are various shopping malls located all around the city. The
shopping malls are popular with locals as well as with tourists and are
normally open till late in the night. Most of the shopping malls offer good
bargains and provide a good opportunity for some great shopping.

**City Square**

108 Jalan Wong Ah Fook

Johor Baharu 80000 Malaysia

+60 7 221 9989

http://www.city-square.com/

Located about 1 kilometer from the Johor Bahru Immigration
Complex, the City Square shopping center is a place that has something for
everyone. This is a good place to buy jewelry, clothing, electronics, fashion accessories,
toys and much more. The prices are reasonable but it is advisable to bargain to
get the best price. The shopping center is open Monday to Sunday 10 a.m. to 10
p.m.

**Holiday Plaza**

Jalan Dato Sulaiman

Johor Baharu 80250 Malaysia

+60 7 276 2620

The Holiday Plaza is situated adjacent to the Crowne Plaza
in Johor Bahru. There are about 450 shops in this complex and is an excellent
place to shop for clothing, electronics, fashion wear accessories, etc. Tip:
The higher the floor, the less expensive the goods will be. So take a look at
the shops in the higher floors. The shopping complex is open Monday to Sunday
10 a.m. to 10 p.m.

**ZON Shopping Complex**

88 Jalan Ibrahim Sultan

Johor Baharu 80300 Malaysia

+60 7 221 2888

http://www.zon.com.my/deptstore.html

Open from Monday to Sunday 10 a.m. to 10 p.m. this shopping
complex is a 5 story building filled with all kinds of imaginable things. With
a 250,000 square feet shopping area, this complex is one place that promises to
give you a real shopping experience.

**Plaza Pelangi**

2 Jalan Kuning

Johor Baharu 80400 Malaysia

+60 7 276 2216

Plaza Pelangi is probably the best shopping complex in Johor
Bahru. The shopping complex is home to a range of popular brand names such as
Sports Station, Royal Sporting House, Esprit, etc. There are various
restaurants in the shopping complex including a large amusement center on the 5th
floor. The shopping center is open Monday to Sunday 10 a.m. to 10 p.m.

**KOMTAR**

Jalan Wong Ah Fook

Johor Baharu 80000 Malaysia

+60 7 222 3590

The Komtar is one of Johor Bahru’s older shopping complexes.
Located in downtown Johor Bahru, the complex includes a branch of Parkson chain
stores. The complex is a popular place for shoppers with a low budget. The
complex is open Monday to Sunday 10:30 a.m. to 9 p.m.
