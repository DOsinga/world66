# Things to do

*Asia > Southeastasia > Malaysia > Johorbahru > Things To Do*

A high energy, multicultural city like Johor Bahru is
filled with virtually hundreds of things to do, from shopping, to eating out at
great restaurants, to visiting the different attractions in the city, Johor Bahru
is a traveler’s paradise. Situated in a part of the world that is visited by
tourists all through the year, Johor Bahru offers tourists a chance to
experience the beauty and vibrancy of the city – an experience that will leave
a lasting impression in your memory for many years to come.

**Sultan Abu Bakar Royal Museum**

Jalan Air Molek

Johor Baharu 80500 Malaysia

+60 7 223 0555

A visit to the Sultan Abu Bakar Royal Museum will bring you
face to face with the history and legacy of the Johor Sultanate. Several of the
items on display are from the 19th century; exhibits include the
first postal stamp issued in Johor, gold coins, antique furniture, Chinese
pottery and many other interesting objects. The museum is open Saturday to
Thursday 9 a.m. to 5 p.m.

**Johor Old Chinese Temple**

Jalan Trus

Johor Baharu 80000 Malaysia

+60 7 277 7858

Located right between skyscrapers, the Johor Old Chinese
Temple is one of the oldest buildings in Johor Bahru. The temple was
constructed towards the 19th century; it was renovated during 1995
and 1996. The temple is a holy place for the Chinese and is a popular tourist
spot.

**Endau-Rompin National Park**

JKR 475 Bukit Timbalan

Johor Baharu 80000 Malaysia

+60 7 223 7471

The beautiful Endau-Rompin National Park is a 488 square kilometers
rainforest. This is the oldest tropical in the whole world. The park features
various outdoor activities such as camping, trekking, fishing, fishing, etc. The
park is open Monday to Friday 9 a.m. to 5 p.m.

**Mount Ophir**

Between the border of Muar and Melaka

Johor Baharu Malaysia

+60 7 224 2000

Mount Ophir is a rocky mountain forest reserve. There are
clearly marked trails that lead to the summit which is 1276 meters high. There
is a resort that provides chalets which you can hire if you would like to spend
a few days in this beautiful mountain forest reserve. The Reserve is open and
accessible Monday to Sunday 24 hours.

**Istana Garden**

Istana Bukit Serene

Johor Baharu 80000 Malaysia

+60 7 223 4935

The Istana Garden is located within the grounds of the Grand
Palace. The garden is well maintained and offers excellent views that are
second to none. The garden is also a perfect place for a stroll in the
afternoon. The garden is open Monday to Sunday 7 a.m. to 7 p.m.
