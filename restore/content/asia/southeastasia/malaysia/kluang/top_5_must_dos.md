# Top 5 Must Do's in Kluang

*Asia > Southeastasia > Malaysia > Kluang > Top 5 Must Dos*

When you are going to be traveling to Kluang in Malaysia, there are
many local attractions to see and, if you are looking to take in
everything that the area has to offer, here are **Kluang must do’s**:
**1. Sports and Leisure**

There are many sport facilities and leisure activities to enjoy while
in Kluang. These include the Kluang Golf and Country Club, the Guthrie
Golf and Country Club, the Kompleks Sukan MPK, The Pamol Golf Club,
Rebel Futsal Indoor Soccer, Klung Recreation and Sport Center and the
Public Swimming Pool. These activities provide fun and entertainment
while you are on vacation.
**2. Shopping**

There are a lot of great shopping centers in Kluang that tourists can
stop and find everything that they are looking for. Some of the best
shopping centers to see include the Plaza BCB, Kluang Parade, The Store
Giant Supermarket and YC Superstore. The business area of Kluang hosts
many places to eat and to shop. The Kluang Mall is newly built and
houses many great shops to find everything that you want.
**3. Nature and Parks**

The Tropical Village is a theme park that many tourists stop at to
have fun and see many of the famous structures of the area. Other great
nature parks to enjoy in the area are the Gunung Lambak Recreational
forest, the Gunung Belumut Recreational Forest and the Tasik Mardi
Recreational forest.
**4. Food and Dining**

There are a lot of great places to stop for coffee and to eat in the
city including the Kluang Railway Station Coffee House, the Chinese
Traditional Local Famous Eatery, the Rong Seri Restaurant and the
Takohut. All of these places offer the best in traditional cuisine and a
taste of the area and culture for everyone to enjoy.
**5. Heritage and Landmarks**

There are many places to visit on vacation in Kluang, including the
Kluang Railway station, the Masjid Jamek Kluang, Little India, Old
building at Jalan Lambak and Old Charms. These famous attractions and
landmarks are full of history and heritage and make a great place to
stop and see while in the area. Tourists can learn more about the
history and culture of the people in the area and find a lot of great
places to see on vacation. The area is home to many famous attractions
and landmarks to enjoy that are all nearby the city of Kluang and easy
to see while visiting.
