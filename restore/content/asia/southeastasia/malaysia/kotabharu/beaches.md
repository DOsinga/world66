# Beaches in Kota bharu

*Asia > Southeastasia > Malaysia > Kotabharu > Beaches*

Despite the Islamic conservatism of the region that frowns upon
excessive exhibition of flesh, **Kotabharu beaches** are fun and
happening places, full of gorgeous powdery sand and clean sparkling
water. Local people have had to battle erosion of the beaches by strong
surf, with wave breakers constructed of boulders being erected along the
shoreline. Be aware that certain beaches have more or less disappeared
as a result of the erosion and anti-erosion strategies.

**The Beach of Passionate Love**

Perhaps the most famous of Kotabharu beaches, the Beach of Passionate
Love is full of food stalls serving delicious local seafood and the
cool breeze that is a great relief to tourists. The only problem here is
that swimming is potentially dangerous due to the strong currents. This
beach was originally named "PCP Beach" but later changed perhaps due to
the chemical connotations.

**Pantai Dasar Sabak**

The Japanese landed here in 1941 to mount their invasion of Malaya
(as it was then known) but thankfully, this beach has become a lot safer
since then. Nearly 13 km away from Khotabharu, Pantai Dasar Sabak also
refers to the a nearby quaint little fishing village where tourists can
observe traditional Malay life and buy great souvenirs from a whole
range of shops. Swimming is possible off this very best of Khotabharu
beaches in the cool blue sea, as are snorkeling, sailing, surfing,
boating and windsurfing. The more sedentary traveler can indulge in some
serious sunbathing instead. The surrounding landscape is truly
beautiful, consisting of verdant countryside and picturesque scenery
into which exciting excursions are possible. The dominant cuisine in
Pantai Dasar Sabak is excellent seafood and fish so these are
must-tries. Most tourists agree that this is the place to go for
beach-related fun.

**Perdana Beach Resort**

If you want a hotel and resort with its own scenic private beach then
this is the place for you. Replete with restaurants, tennis courts, a
swimming pool with bars within it and dozens of excellent rooms, Perdana
Beach Resort is an accommodation and beach-visiting in style. If you
get tired of one of the best Kotabharu beaches available, partake of one
of the excellent alternative activities available--lounging in the
garden, playing golf on the exclusive course, utilizing the gym/fitness
facilities, being pampered in the spa, playing squash, hanging out in
the jacuzzi, letting the kids play in the designated kids' room,
relaxing in the steam room and using one of the three swimming pools.
