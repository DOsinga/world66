# Pizzeria Traudi

*Asia > Southeastasia > Malaysia > Kotabharu > Eating Out > Pizzeria Traudi*

Swiss chef Urs Beutler is looking forward to welcoming you at Pizzeria Traudi! Choose from a variety of pizze from the woodfire oven, pasta or Western meat dishes, fresh fruit juices and organic teas.

Here's how to find us (it's a 10 minutes walk from center town):

From the bus station walk down to the junction with the Kamdar building on the right and CIMB Bank on the left. At that junction turn left into Jalan Kebun Sultan. Walk until the second traffic light - the junction with the two Hayaki Coffeeshops, one to the left and one to the right of the road. Turn right (between the two Hayaki) into Jalan Kebun Raja. This street ends in a T-junction with Jalan Dusun Raja - and that's where we are: Across the T-junction Jalan Kebun Raja into Jalan Dusun Raja.

*We are open Monday-Saturday, 6.30pm until midnight, and Wednesday-Friday also for lunch from 1pm-2.30pm. We serve pizza from the woodfire oven (evening only) starting at RM 25, spaghetti (from RM 18) and several meat dishes (from RM 32), all Western style. Our Set Lunch (changes daily) sells at RM 15, and our Lunch Special (sliced chicken in curry and cream sauce with rice, iced lemon tea) at RM 12 . We can't serve alcohol, but feel free to bring your own :)*
