# Things to do in Kota bharu

*Asia > Southeastasia > Malaysia > Kotabharu > Things To Do*

If you are visiting Kotabharu, some of the things you might want to
add to your list of **Kotabharu things to do **would be: visiting a
museum, exploring the town, shopping, enjoying the local delicacies or
even diving. Here are a few recommendations for you.

**Istana Jahar**

Every city has a culture of its own, just like the city Kotabharu. It
is often called “the Islamic City,” since it observes the Islamic
principles in every aspect of the native’s daily lives. You might want
to visit the Istana Jahar, which is also known as the Museum of
Royal Traditions. You will be able to see intricately carved wooden
panels all over the palace. This is a great place to take in the culture
of Kotabharu.

**Taste the Good Food**

As a traveler, the first thing that will surely enter your mind when
you visit a place is to taste the native delicacies. According to the
travelers that stayed in Kotabharu, the city’s great food always
encourages them to keep coming back. Ayam Percik is a must-try while
visiting Kota Bharu, this is a chicken dish that is marinated and
roasted over a charcoal flame. Once the chicken is cooked, a creamy
coconut sauce is poured over the chicken and it is served hot. Nasi
kerabu, Nasi berlauk and Nasi dagang are some of the popular local
dishes you would not want to miss.

**Shopping Galore**

Pasar Siti Khadijah (central market) is the most famous shopping
destination in the city. There is also a commercial building next to the
central market called the Kotabharu Trade Center (KBTC), one of the
largest malls in the city since its opening in 2009. Shopping for native
clothes, accessories and souvenirs is certainly worth your time and
budget.

**Fun in the Sun at the Beaches**

Kotabharu’s magnificent beaches include the famous "Beach of
Passionate Love." Although tough waves make swimming perilous, people
leisurely walk alongside the beach, taking pleasure in the cool zephyr
and enjoying drinks from various food stalls.

**Wreck Diving**

One of the things you might want to try in Kota Bharu is "wreck
diving." This is a new activity that has just recently become very
popular with tourists in Kota Bharu. However, there is only one dive
operator that offers this attraction. You will have the chance to dive
and see the wreck of the Japanese transport ship which sunk during the
Great Pacific War. You will be amazed to see the several different
species of marine life at the wreck. You can take a boat ride to the
wreck and enjoy the dive.
