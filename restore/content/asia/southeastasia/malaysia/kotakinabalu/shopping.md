# Shopping in Kota Kinabalu

*Asia > Southeastasia > Malaysia > Kotakinabalu > Shopping*

Kota Kinabalu is a shopper’s paradise and features several up market
stores selling designer merchandise alongside quaint street-side flea
markets offering local goods at phenomenal prices. The discounted rates
and bargain deals coupled with a vast array of choices makes Kota a
preferred shopping haunt. Here is a shopping guide to pick the best
stuff off the shelves of Kota Kainabalu’s stores and old bazaars.

**Kota Kinabalu Handicraft Market**

A great place nestled adjacent to the waterfront, The Kota Kinabalu
Handicraft Market offers an assortment of local handicrafts, pearls,
batik printed fabric, vibrant sarongs, floor mats and decorative
brooches along with Islamic scriptures, home décor items and colorful
crystals at rock-bottom prices. There are also some nice carved statues
and ratthan handicrafts. Don’t forget to haggle for favorable prices as
the merchants are extremely amiable and will offer tourists interesting
insights and information on the region. Most items are sourced from
neighboring Asian regions, while a few are imported from Austria.

**Sunday Flea Market**

The Sunday flea market held at the lower strip of Gaya Street in the
heart of Kota Kinabalu puts up an array of fresh local produce and
traditional merchandize for sale. There are home-grown vegetables and
fruits apart from hardware goods, local jewelry, traditional fabrics,
printed t-shirts and regional artifacts. The market is operational from
6am-1pm every Sunday.

**Shopping Malls**

Kota Kinabalu has several high-end shopping malls housing chic
designer stores that sell high-end items at fixed and discounted
prices. Center Point is one of the most famous malls in the city and
houses brands like Calvin Klein, U2 Dockers, Timberland, Guess and
British India. Other major malls are KK Plaza, Wawasan Plaza, Wisma
Merdeka and Karamunsing Complex. Wasawan, Likas Square and Centre Point
feature factory outlets of reputed brands and visitors can expect some
great bargains on hiking apparel and gear apart from low-rates on
normal clothing items. Karamunsing Complex is great for picking up
high-quality electronic gadgets at inexpensive rates. The malls offer
more attractive deals during the mega shopping carnival from June to
September. Most malls are operational from 10am to 10pm.

Kota Kinabalu is a shopping hotspot that is frequented by avid
shoppers for its quality merchandise at bargain deals and discounted
prices. The destination is as celebrated for its old-fashioned street
bazaars as its chic shopping malls housing designer brands.
