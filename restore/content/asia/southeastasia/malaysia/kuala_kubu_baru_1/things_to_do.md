# Things to do in Kuala Kubu Baru

*Asia > Southeastasia > Malaysia > Kuala Kubu Baru 1 > Things To Do*

Kuala Kubu Baru is a small town situated in the northern part of
Selangor, Malaysia. Even though this is just a little town, there are
lots of **Kuala Kubu Baru Things to Do** that you can enjoy.

**White Water Rafting**

One of Malaysia's most popular white water rafting adventures can be
found in Kuala Kubu Baru. This activity starts
with the safety briefing and instruction about basic paddling techniques.
The rafting trip will last for 3 hours and features nine different
rapids. Each group has 6 participants and everyone is provided with
a helmet and safety gear. This is one adventure you will remember for
a long time.

**Jungle Trekking**

Take a trek, climb hills and cross some rivers in Kuala Kubu Baru's
Genting Highlands. These adventures require physical and mental
strength. However, seeing Kuala Kubu Baru from 1,000 feet above sea level,
exploring the jungle and enjoying the waterfalls are all definitely
worth the trek. When you reach the Sungai Sendat waterfall, you can swim
and have a picnic with your friends or family.

**Ulu Kalong Hot Spring **

Ulu Kalong is a hidden small hot spring right in the center of a palm
oil plantation. It is a 20m upstream with 50cm high walls and 30cm deep
water. The spring is pretty hot but still tolerable; the temperature is
good to soak your feet or even your whole body. Just a few minutes of
soaking in the Ulu Kalong Hot Spring can definitely relax your nerves
and senses.

**Fraser's Hills**

Exploring the famous Fraser's Hills is another great adventure in
Kuala Kubu Baru. Fraser's Hills is a colonial hill station that offers
majestic views, a great collection of flora and fauna and great sport
activities. You will be captivated by the building itself and the old
clock tower, as well as the Nature's Educational Center and the nearby
waterfalls. You can also go hiking, play tennis, golf, bird watch and
shop in the souvenir shop. There is also a restaurant available so you
can sit back and have a good meal.

**Golf Course**

You can enjoy and play in two different golf
courses just minutes away from Fraser's Hills, the 18-hole KKB Golf
Course and the 9-hole Royal Fraser's Golf Club. Both golf courses offer
comprehensive facilities and world class amenities. You
can enjoy the picturesque view of Kuala Kubu Baru while playing golf.
