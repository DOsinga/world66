**QBA LATIN BAR & GRILL, A WINE & CIGAR BAR AT THE WESTIN KUALA LUMPUR COMBINES A LATIN GRILL WITH LIVE LATIN ENTERTAINMENT**

Qba Latin Bar & Grill has it all! From the magnificent interior to the live Latin band performances, steak restaurant with authentic Latin American cuisines, the biggest Cuban rum collection to the cigar selection – Qba lets you have a taste of what is a multi-level Latin experience.

Qba Latin Grill is essentially a steak restaurant with Latin American flavours. Its cuisine simply reflects the Latin Americas – an aged landscape of the sea and the rainforest that demands flavours that dazzle, bright ingredients and vivid seasoning. With this philosophy in mind, Qba Latin Grill cooks according to its landscape, creating vibrant flavours of lime, ginger, chilli, sweet coconut and bananas that grow in abundance in the region.
