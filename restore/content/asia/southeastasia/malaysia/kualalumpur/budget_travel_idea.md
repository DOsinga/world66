# Budget Travel Ideas in Kuala Lumpur

*Asia > Southeastasia > Malaysia > Kualalumpur > Budget Travel Idea*

If you are
looking for a place that combines history and culture with modern
standards then the place to go is Kuala Lumpur in Malaysia. This city
is vibrant, with entertainment at night to keep you moving, and many
museums and towers to view in the day time. It may not be as glamorous
as other cities but it does boast of being safe for travelers.

**What to Do **

Take a trip to the world’s largest twin towers. It will make your
head spin as you ride a fast elevator to the top. There are many
restaurants and shops inside, so you will want to make a day of it.
There are also concert halls and museums so you can get the feel of the
Islamic world. There is a theme park in Kuala Lumpur that will wet
your body as you travel down water slides and attempt a ride on the
river. The Wildlife Park and Extreme Park might be separate from the
water park, but they are also an adventure waiting for you.

**Where to Stay**

Sunway Lagoon Resort Hotel is an easy stop for those wanting to
visit the parks. The price to stay in the hotel includes tickets to the
water park, and you can purchase tickets to the Wildlife Park and
Extreme Park from the front desk at a discount. If you want an easy
access to the twin towers, then try the Mandarin Oriental Kuala Lumpur.
It is located next to the twin towers. The hotel attracts many visitors
and the staff works hard to make your stay a nice one with good food,
excellent laundry service and inexpensive rates.

**Where to Eat **

There are many restaurants that have food from all over the world. If
you want to have authentic food from India, try the Passage Thru’
India. It has a nice atmosphere as well as excellent food. It makes you
feel like you have stepped into a new place, with
inexpensive prices. If you are looking for Malaysian food, try the
Bansar Seafood Garden Restaurant. They have a large variety of seafood,
not only from Malaysia but many Asian countries. With its low prices it's the perfect stop for a budget traveler. If you need some western style food, try TGI
Friday’s. It will bring a little bit of taste from home. The Rama V
will take you into Thai. With excellent food and wonderful service you
will feel relaxed, and with the low prices, it's a nice break.

Kuala
Lumpur is an excellent experience for anyone who wants to try many
things at one time. With food from all over the world, it's your one
stop adventure.
