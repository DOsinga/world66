# Petronas Twin Towers

*Asia > Southeastasia > Malaysia > Kualalumpur > Sights > Petronastwintowers*

Petronas Tower is the 2nd tallest building in the world (After Teipei Tower has overtaken it). It is located in an area refered to as the Golden Triangle. Surrounded on all sides by shopping, commerce and entertainment outlets, you'll feel like you're in another country altogether. This area contains all the expensive hotels and restaurants. It is not the area if you want to save money.
