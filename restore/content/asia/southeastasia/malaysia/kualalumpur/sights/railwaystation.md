# Railway Station

*Asia > Southeastasia > Malaysia > Kualalumpur > Sights > Railwaystation*

Locate at Jalan Sultan Hishamuddin you will see the Railway Station. In itself it is a beautiful example of Moorish style architecture. It was built in 1910. The Station is open daily from 7:30 am - 10:30pm.
