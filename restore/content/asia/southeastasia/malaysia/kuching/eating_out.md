# Eating Out

*Asia > Southeastasia > Malaysia > Kuching > Eating Out*

There are wide variety of good places for eating out in Kuching.

### [Home Delicious](/asia/southeastasia/malaysia/kuching/eating_out/home_delicious)

Great place to savour Malaysian home cook food. Rice with a choice of different kinds of meat, vegetables, fish and soup. Great for dinner or lunch! Don't forget to try the minced pork and assam fish soup.

type: Chinese

### [Nam Hoi Restaurant](/asia/southeastasia/malaysia/kuching/eating_out/nam_hoi_restaurant)

Located at Kota Sentosa (7th Mile Bazaar), you can find all sort of mouth-watering local food such as kolo-mee, kueh-chap (the real deal!), laksa, and fast food with rice. You can also order dishes or arrange for buffet style catering for your functions.

### [Sharing Planet](/asia/southeastasia/malaysia/kuching/eating_out/sharing_planet__a)

Sharing Planet is an uniquely designed wooden built restaurant in Kuching, you can enjoy western food anywhere else, but dinning here will definitely be one of your unforgetable experience!

Cosy, quiet environment, and you can hear the natural symphony of insects choirs in the night time, yes, the choirs are the real insects singing!

This planet serves western food, pasta and a wide variety of drinks selection, located about 4KM away from downtown Kuching, getting a cab is the only way to it (usually cost RM15 one way), but be rest assured it is a place worth [more..](/asia/southeastasia/malaysia/kuching/eating_out/sharing_planet__a)
