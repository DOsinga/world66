# Bars and Cafes in Langkawi

*Asia > Southeastasia > Malaysia > Langkawi > Bars And Cafes*

Whether you like getting out into the rowdy groups or are more inclined to restful atmospheres **Langkawi Bars and Cafes** offer both opportunities. Since the natives really encourage interaction with them by tourists. A bar or café is just the place to get to know them. A night time drink with your family or friends could open up possibilities to get to see what the Malaysian people are really like.

**Marine Bar and Lounge
**
This hangout is a part of the Rebak Marina Resort** **that has a reputation as one of the top-notch cocktail bars anywhere on the island. Visitors tend to enjoy this facility mostly because the location is in the heart of a tropical scenic area. Plus the large choice of exotic cocktails adds to the austere aura of the place. For those who like a more calming place to have a drink the** **Marine Bar and Lounge is a paradise.

**Little Lyias Chillout Café**

On the Cenag beach is where you will find this comfortable Asian style building with patrons sitting on the porch or out on the beach sipping cocktails. Little Lyias has earned a well-known reputation for serving excellent cocktails. The café specializes in 13 types such as Wild Night Out, Planters Punch or Blue Hawaiian Lagoon. This cafe draws people who are more prone to seek friendships so this place is a chill-out area but a lot of people hangout here.
**

Urwald Café

**This café is set back away from the congested areas in Langkawi. The setting gives it a soothing feel. The Asian music is sure to set you at ease and the cuisine is excellent. The cafe's fine selection of tropical cocktails makes this café is unequaled on the island. It is a must to visit while you are here in Langkawi.
**

Aquarium Seafood Garden

**If you like live performances with your food and drink then this place will interest you. The restaurant has live performances of Thai music and dance every night. Seafood is a specialty here, but it is considered the best place to go if you happen to find wine or champagne especially appealing.
**

Sunba Retro Bar

**You will find a lot of trendy bars in Langkawi yet one stands out from all the others. The Sunba Retro Bar is a hopping place to go out and have a drink or 2. Retro means that music from the 60s to the 80s are played every night, sometimes by a live band and on other nights when a DJ is on stage. You will fit right in if you like dancing or playing pool. The building is attractive with an old-style Malaysian house. This is a great site for the younger generations of adults.
