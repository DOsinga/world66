# Junglewalla Tours

*Asia > Southeastasia > Malaysia > Langkawi > Day Trips > Malaysia Wildlife*

Junglewalla was
founded in 1994 by the region's premier naturalist to make the diversity of this
region available visitors.

In addition to tourism Wild Langkawi offers consultancy services to
government, NGO's and the tourism industry and are active in lobbying for
ecological causes. The company is active in research and clients may extend
their visits to participate in research projects.
