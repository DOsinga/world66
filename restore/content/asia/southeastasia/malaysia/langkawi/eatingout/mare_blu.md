# Mare Blu

*Asia > Southeastasia > Malaysia > Langkawi > Eatingout > Mare Blu*

'''Mare Blu''' at Perdana Quay in the newly openend Telaga Harbour Park (Pantai Kok) is the only authentic Italien restaurant on Langkawi island. Lorenzo, Partner & chef offers typical italien food, good wines and draft beer. The Mediteranian style surrounding is just beautiful and the tables are set up right on the waterfront. The Telaga Harbour Park is near the Hotels Mutiara Burau Bay, Bejaya, Sheraton Beach and Tanjung Sanctuary, Datai & Andaman.
