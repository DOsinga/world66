# The Light House Restaurant

*Asia > Southeastasia > Malaysia > Langkawi > Eatingout > The Light House Re*

This is a delightful restaurant with a magical setting on the beach in Pantai Cenang Pulau. The service was excellent and the food was beyond belief and the prices were amazingly cheap for the standard of restaurant.

The other excellent Cafe was the Red Tomato Garden Cafe, this was the only place that actually served pllenty of tomatoes in a salad. The presentation again was of international standard and the staff and owner were wonderful. The menu would be ideal for vegietarians.
