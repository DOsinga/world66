# Top 5 Must Do's in Langkawi

*Asia > Southeastasia > Malaysia > Langkawi > Top 5 Must Dos*

When vacationing in the tropical paradise of **Langkawi,
things to do **include exploring the culture and natural beauty of
the island. Covered with dense rainforests dating back millions of
years, the traditional lifestyle is alive on this lush island full of
caves, waterfalls and stunning views. Here are the top 5 things you
should be sure to explore when visiting Langkawi.

**Take an Island Tour**

This is the best way to get acquainted with the surroundings and see all
the island has to offer. Rent a car, get a map and head over to the
tourist information center in Kuah. Get some practical information and
stroll through the neighboring 20-hectare Lagenda Park. Head to Cenang
Beach and check out the Underwater World Aquarium, as well as the lovely
beach and environs that has become very popular with backpackers.

**Explore the Seven Wells Waterfall**

At Mat Cincang Mountain, there are 7 pools connected by waterfalls that
are spectacular to visit and swim in. Believed by area residents to be
the haunt of mountain fairies, the spot is great for swimming and easy
to reach. Drive to the steps at the foot of the mountain and climb up to
the lowest pool.

**Take a Boat Ride**

With a myriad of small islands surrounding Langkawi, there are a lot of
great places to explore. Take a 15-minute ride to Pregnant Maiden island
for a great hike through the rainforest and a trip into the Cave of the
Banshee. End the day with a swim in the beautiful freshwater lake
there.

**Enjoy Water Sports**

Whether you enjoy diving or snorkeling, Langkawi has something for
everyone. The Payar Island Marine Park is a great place for snorkelers,
as it has calm and shallow waters with lots of tropical reef fish, as
well as baby blacktip sharks. Diving is accesible year round here and
there are many dive operators on the island that can take you on a great
adventure diving.

**Tour Mangroves**

Near Tanjung Rhu Beach, visitors can go on a boat ride through the
mangroves that winds through several different mangrove swamps, a dense
forest and several limestone caves. Not to be missed on a trip to
Langkawi, the mangrove tour offers a unique look at the island and the
many contrasting features that make it up.

Enjoy your trip to this
beautiful island by stepping outside the box and trying something
new and exciting on your trip to Langkawi. You will be glad you did!
