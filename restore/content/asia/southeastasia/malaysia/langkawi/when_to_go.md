# When to Go in Langkawi

*Asia > Southeastasia > Malaysia > Langkawi > When To Go*

Langkawi in Taiwan is a jewel in South East Asia's crown. It has the credential of being the largest island amongst the cluster of islands. Langkawi is blessed with picture perfect landscapes and a mesmerizing marine backdrop. It is a culmination of lush green forests, sun kissed beaches, lagoons and waterfalls. Where else can you bask in the sun and get all the pampering and receive the warm hospitality that Langkawi is popular for? Get to know the culture and the history of this mystical island. Read on to find out the appropriate time to visit this beautiful and one of a kind tourist destination.

**When to go**

Before you swipe your card to make travel arrangements, spare some time in the planning process. Even though Langkawi is a paradise for those looking to have fun, there are certain elements that could dampen your visits. The weather plays a very crucial role. Also plan your trip based on the various activities that you wish to indulge in.

**Avoid the monsoon**

The infamous Langkawi monsoon can spoil the enchanting sights of Langkawi, seen from the Cable car. When it rains it pours in this island. In order to avoid this, it is advisable not to plan your trip between April and October.

**Right time to enjoy the water bodies**

The best time to visit Langkawi to experience the coral reefs and the marine life in all its glory is between April and June. The visibility factor plays a key role and during these months visibility is around 10 meters in depth. But during the right season the visibility can be as high as 25 meters. The water is normally 29 degrees Celsius.

**Summer time**

The best time to plan your beach resort vacation is from February to October. The weather during these months is welcoming to a beach lover and you can escape the rains as well. The temperature in this tropical island ranges from 23 degrees to 34 degrees Celsius.

**Season for Festivals**

The April, May and June months hosts a series of festivals in Langkawi. The most worthwhile of them are the Water Festival, the Sailing event, the wilderness challenge, and the International golf championship. Plan your tips to coincide with these festivals, as this is the best time to see Langkawi in all its splendor.
