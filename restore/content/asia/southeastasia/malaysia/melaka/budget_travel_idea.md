# Budget Travel Ideas in Melaka

*Asia > Southeastasia > Malaysia > Melaka > Budget Travel Idea*

If you are visiting Melaka, there are several **Melaka
budget travel** ideas that can help you make the most of your holiday.
There are several great places and things you can do in Melaka without
spending too much money. One of the biggest concerns for travelers to
any place is, how much is it going to cost? However, in Melaka you do
not have to worry about that. Here are a few tips on how you can save
some money while on holiday in Melaka.

**Budget Transportation**

To reach Melaka you can take a 4-hour ride on a bus or taxi
from Singapore; this costs from $12 to $20. If you want to travel by air, you can, but there is no direct
air services in Melaka. So you’ll have to fly to Singapore or Kuala
Lumpur and take a 2-hour drive to the City of Melaka. For traveling within Melaka, take the trishaw. You can also
take a boat ride that cruise along the Melaka River.

**Budget [Accommodation](/asia/southeastasia/malaysia/melaka/accommodation)**

There are several hotels that offer budget accommodations in Melaka.
If you want to stay in the center of Melaka, you might want to
consider staying in Hotel Melia Horizon. Room rates are about $24.
Melaka is a place where you will be able to find accommodations for any
kind of budget. Several hotels offer quality accommodation under $30 per
night.

**Affordable Shopping**

Jonker Walk is one of the most popular places to pick up things at
very cheap prices. Take a walk down the market and don’t forget to
bargain because you are bound to get some amazing prices. Some of the things
available at the market are: fresh and dried product like meats,
vegetables and fruits, electronic gadgets, clothes and trinkets.

**Affordable [Restaurants](/asia/southeastasia/malaysia/melaka/eating_out)**

Melaka is a place where you do not need to spend too much money on
food. Enjoy authentic Baba and Nyonya cuisine at the roadside
restaurants. You will be able to enjoy dishes like: satay, wan tan mee,
chap chye, popiah, mee siam and ice kakang. There are also several
restaurants that offer excellent cuisine at affordable rates.

**Budget Hot Spots**

There are lots of historical places in Melaka that you can explore
without spending a dime. You can visit the old-fashioned churches, the
Dutch Graveyard, historical ruins like A’Famosa and more. The museum is a
great place to visit. You can enjoy Melaka’s colorful history by
visiting the museums and cultural villages.
