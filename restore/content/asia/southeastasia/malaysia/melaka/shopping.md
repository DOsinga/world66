# Shopping in Melaka

*Asia > Southeastasia > Malaysia > Melaka > Shopping*

Melaka, or Malacca, is one of
Malaysia’s favored tourist destinations and the **Melaka shopping** experience will leave you feeling satisfied with
all that you have bought. A small historic port city situated in West Malaysia,
Melaka is considered as tourist habitat par excellence. You can shop around for
antiques, spices, fresh fruits and vegetables and more.

**Melaka
Old Chinatown**

Jonker Street is the perfect
place to buy antiques. This is an antique shopper’s haven; you will find
different antiques and artifacts dating back to 300 years. However the houses
on Jonker Street are mostly built during the 17th century, so simply
walking down the streets will give you a piece of history that you cannot buy
anywhere. There are several art galleries and souvenir shops along the Jonker
Street. So wander around and pick up a few antique items to take home with you.

**Melaka's
New Chinatown**

Melaka's New Chinatown is situated
along the eastern banks of the River Melaka. New Chinatown offers shoppers
a wide selection of shops. Madam King’s Department Store offers
ready-made clothes, jewelry, fabrics, herbs, plastic pots and flowers, sundries
and more. You can find several good bargains at these shops.

**Mahkota
Parade **

The Mahkota Parade shopping
complex is a family friendly, 4-story entertainment and shopping center. It
houses a number of shopping stores, food stalls, book shops, souvenir shops,
a bowling alley and a Cineplex. The Parkson Grand, on the other hand, is a major
department store that operates within the Mahkota Parade. You will be able to
shop for almost anything at the Mahkota Parade.

**Jalan
Hang Tuah**

Jalan Hang Tuah is the main
commercial and financial street of Melaka. Within this district you will find
different banks, hotels and shopping centers like Fajar Shopping Centre, Melaka
Center Market and Khelass Department Store. The most prominent shopping center
in this are is the Hang Tuah Mall. It was conceptualized to meet, if not
surpass, Kuala Lumpur’s Bintang Walk. The Hang Tuah Mall area houses different
shopping stores and hosts exciting activities for visitors. Enjoy free Wi-Fi internet
access at the Hang Tuah Mall.

**Bukit
Baru Area**

The Bukit Baru Area is also
known as New Hill. Bukit Baru Area is a suburban residential area where you
will find the Jusco Shopping Center and Kota Mas Shopping Center. Both these
shopping centers are popular shopping complexes and house many international
food franchises and upper class stores like A&W, The Store and Telekoms.
This is a great place to walk around and shop for things you need.
