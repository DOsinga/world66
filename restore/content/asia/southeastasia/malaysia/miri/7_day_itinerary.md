# 7 Day Itinerary in Miri

*Asia > Southeastasia > Malaysia > Miri > 7 Day Itinerary*

The entire **Miri 7 day itinerary** usually starts at the break of day, so wake up early enough. It's always good to familiarize
yourself with the town's history first.

**Day 1 – Canada Hill/Crocodile Farm**

Start your day by touring the city of Miri and a drive to Canada
Hill, which is an excellent lookout point over the whole city and its
coastline. Malaysia's first oil well, discovered in 1910 and now known
as The Grand old Lady, sits as a relic on Canada Hill. On Kuala Baram
road, visit Miri Crocodile Farm cum mini zoo and the Asean Bridge, which
links Miri and Brunei. On your way back to the city, stop at the
biggest Taoist Temple in South East Asia. Visit Miri City Fan
recreation park, as it is one of the most frequented.

**Day 2 – Miri Heritage Centre/Iban Longhouses **

A visit to the Miri Heritage Centre is a good way to start another
day. You can shop for handicrafts. Proceed to Tamu Muhibbah, a
local market selling jungle produce and products. In the afternoon pay
a visit to the Iban (famed headhunters of Borneo) longhouses known as
Gerempung Indu, where traditional ceremonies were held in the olden days.

**Day 3 – Niah National Park**

Leave early morning for a scenic drive to Niah National Park. You
must register and check in. Have some lunch, then cross the Niah River and
take one of the jungle trails available in the park. Spend a quiet
evening and dinner at your hotel.

**Day 4 – Gunung Mulu National Park**

A day trip to the Gunung Mulu National Park is a true
adventure. This park is famous for its caves and the expeditions. You
will get to see its limestone karst formations, enormous caves, rock
pinnacles, cliffs and gorges. The park is also home to an enormous
colony of wrinkle-lipped bats.

**Day 5 - Mulu Summit **

Mulu Park provides guided hikes up the mountain. Along the
route you will find forest huts at 3 camps that provide shelter,
cooking facilities (cooking utensils, stove, cutlery), toilets and
water (rain water).

**Day 6 – Mulu Summit **

After an early morning breakfast at 8:00 am, prepare for the trek
back to the park base camp to head back to Mulu for a pleasant and
relaxing evening.

**Day 7 – Taman Selera Beach**

Your last day can be well spent at Taman Selera, which is one of the
most visited beaches in Miri. A great place to go for a picnic, and a
lot of have fun.

The Miri 7 day itinerary will leave you wanting to come back for more breathtaking sights and scenery.
