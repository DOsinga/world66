# Getting There in Miri

*Asia > Southeastasia > Malaysia > Miri > Getting There*

The**
Miri Getting There **options are quite straight forward and simple
to choose from. Miri is considered to be Sarawak's second largest
city and is its northern gateway. It thus serves as a major transport
hub to other areas in Sarawak. It has a population of approximately
300,000 people. It is a great diving destination and is also well-known for its parks. While traveling to Miri make sure you have a
valid visa. The visa has to be stamped for a period of 6 months. You
will have to go through immigration at Sarawak, even if you had an immigration check
at Sabah or at Peninsular Malaysia.**
****
****By Air **

From
your port of destination check for flights into the Miri
International Airport, which is one of the largest airports in
Malaysia and has high passenger traffaic. The airport received
its international status in the year 2009. There are daily flights
from Johar Bahru, Labuan and Kula Lumpur to Miri.**
****
****Airport Transfers****

**
Once
at the Miri International airport, you can use taxi service to
get to your destination. Remember to buy the coupon for the
taxi at the airport. The taxi coupon ensures that you are not
cheated by local cab drivers, as you make the payment at the airport
to the official authorities. While making reservations at hotels in
Miri ask for airport pick ups. Generally hotels include airport pick
up and return as a complimentary service.
