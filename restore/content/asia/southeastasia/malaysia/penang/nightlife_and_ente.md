# Nightlife and Entertainment in Penang

*Asia > Southeastasia > Malaysia > Penang > Nightlife And Ente*

If you are visiting the “City of Life,” you would not want to miss
out on the** Penang Nightlife **scene. The various nightlife venues
here will surely jazz up your vacation. Here are a few recommendations
for you.

**Slippery Senorita, George Town**

Almost all of the bars and night clubs in George Town are situated on
the Upper Penang Road. The most popular night spot in this area is the
Slippery Senorita. This Latin dance club offers delectable food, a good
selection of wines and beers and great entertainment. The highlights of
this bar are the talented and highly skilled bartenders who flip
bottles, glasses and execute magic tricks using fruits, cards, napkins
and anything at hand. Live bands also perform every night at the
Slippery Senorita.

**75°C Restaurant & Bar, Gurney Drive**

75°C Restaurant & Bar is a tasteful and classy restaurant/bar
situated right along Gurney Drive. This tourist hot spot serves a wide
array of continental dishes like rib-eye steak and hamburger delight.
The bar also offers a great selection of beers and alcoholic beverages
that have interesting names like “Dawn of the Beauty” and “Michael
Jackson.”

**Juru Auto City, Seberang Penai**

The most popular spot here in Seberang Penai is the Juru Auto City,
which is located along the North-South Highway. This is a 1-stop
center that houses a number of restaurants, carnival and auto showrooms.
Juru Auto City is well-known for its popular food chain restaurants
like Subway, Tao Asian Cuisine, Manhattan Fish Market and Swensens. The
party never stops here; at sundown, Juru Auto City brightens up and makes
way for some non-stop carnivals.

**Sunset Bistro, Batu Feringghi**

Dine out with friends and family while enjoying a majestic beachfront
views in Sunset Bistro in Batu Feringghi. This tiny beach bar offers
Western and Chinese dishes. Enjoy the cool ocean breeze and relaxing
sound of the waves while drinking your ice cold beer. The Sunset Bistro
opens from 6:00 p.m. to 4:00 a.m.

**Batu Feringghi Night Market**

Night-out and shopping is a perfect combination of what the Batu
Feringghi Night Market is all about. This is a market that is open from
7:00 p.m. to the early hours of the morning. There are several
restaurants and pubs that are in the area. If you are looking for a
place where you can have a late night drink and walk around, this is the
place.
