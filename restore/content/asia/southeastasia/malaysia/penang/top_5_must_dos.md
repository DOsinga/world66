# Top 5 Must Do's in Penang

*Asia > Southeastasia > Malaysia > Penang > Top 5 Must Dos*

Because the city has lots of sights and attractions, getting the most exciting ideas for your list of **Penang Must Do's**
can be really hard. Penang is considered the "Pearl of the Orient," and has the mixed culture of the Eastern and Western regions.

** 1. Jungle Trekking & Camping **

If you want an exciting adventure while visiting Penang, the Teluk
Bahang's reserve forest can be the place for you. Its challenging
walkways make jungle trekking even more thrilling, and its pristine
beaches take away the exhausted spirits of trekkers. The site is
perfect for camping, as the sea breeze is just enough for you to get a
good sleep at night.

**2. Garden Strolling**

After visiting crowded tourist spots in the city, visit
the Penang Botanical Gardens to take a relaxing garden stroll. Family
gatherings are often held here. You can take pictures of different types of plants and trees
as you walk along the paths. These walkways lead to the activity
centers where you can get someexercise.

**3. Fish Viewing and Feeding**

Koi fish feeding is one of the major activities in the Penang
Aquarium that you should not miss. Unknown to many visitors, this
aquarium situated inside the Fisheries Research Institute (FRI) is a
haven for many aquatic animals that are native to the country. An
80,000-liter tank houses the large fishes so that visitors can view
them easily. The turtles can also be fed, provided that you follow the
strict rules of feeding given by the accommodating aquarium staff. You
can also visit the small koi pond where the bright-colored fishes can
be found.

**4. Trishaw Riding**

An odd yet fun way of exploring the beauty of Penang is by means of
riding the trishaw. Preferred by many visitors, a trishaw is a popular
native vehicle with three wheels and powered by peddling. It is a
healthy way of having a lovely city tour, as you get some exercise while
viewing the attractions in Penang. If you don't want to drive
a trishaw all by yourself, then a local rider can take you to a tour
while giving you information about each attraction. Hiring a
trishaw rider for a tour guide is good enough, since they are
well-acquainted with the places.

**5. Swimming at ****Batu Feringghi**

Take a dive and swim through the clear waters in the Batu Feringghi.
Besides swimming, the famous destination also has many restaurants that
serve foods from almost every part of the world.
