# Kalaw

*Asia > Southeastasia > Myanmar > Kalaw*

"If we could take Kalaw with us, we would!" the British are reported to have said when leaving Myanmar.  This old hill station on the rim of the Shan Plateau, in the Pineland, remains a favourite place for holidays. The main attractions of Kalaw are the town itself, with its mock-Tudor colonial bungalows, its ethnic mix of people, and its setting within trekking distance of many ethnic minority villages.
