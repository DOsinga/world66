# Money Matters

*Asia > Southeastasia > Myanmar > Moneymatters*

** Money matters**

 There are 3 currencies in Myanmar: KYAT is the national currency  FEC is the currency installed by the government to "help you to get rid of your US$". The locals can have them too. 1 FEC = 1 US$.  US$: Officially you're supposed to change all your US$ in FEC's first. However, the 300 FEC's you've gotten at the airport should suffice, and you can then change your US$ on the black market into Kyats. 

 If you change money at the bank, you will get 6 Kyats for 1 FEC, however at the black market you will get 350 Kyat for 1 FEC !!!  Use your 300 FEC's as much as possible for the official things like airtickets, long busrides, airporttax, hotels etc...   

 TC's are not generally accepted, unless you change them into FEC's.  It's almost impossible to get cash advances with your creditcard. You can pay airline tickets and expensive hotels with them. Note that Mastercard is no longer accepted since they've withdrawn from the country.  It's very easy to change money on the black market. Everyone will offer you to change. Best places are hotels, restaurants and shops.
