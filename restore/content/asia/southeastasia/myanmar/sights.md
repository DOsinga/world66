*Asia > Southeastasia > Myanmar > Sights*

ONE OF THE MAJOR ATTRACTIONS OF VISITING MYANMAR IS HAVING THE MULTIPLE FOOD CUISINES AVAILABLE IN MYANMAR. SOME OF THE VERY GOOD EATING OUT JOINTS RECOMMENDED ARE:

FOR MYANMAR CUISINE: PLEASE DO NOT FORGET TO VISIT THE HOLIDAY RESTAURANT ON PO SEIN LANE. IT IS A MULTI STORIED RESTAURANT AND SERVES EXCELLENT MYANMAR FOOD WITH THE SERVICE BEING JUST GOOD.

FOR THAI CUISINE: YOU CAN FIND A LOT OF THAI RESTAURANTS, BUT MY RECOMMENDATION IS PADOMAR OWNED BY THE VERY GENIAL U SUNNY. THE FOOD IS EXCELLENT, THE COCKTAILS VERY GOOD AND THE AMBIENCE JUST RIGH. WITH AN EXCELLENT SNOOKER TABLE AND AN OPEN LAWN, THIS IS JUST THE PLACE FOR YOU TO VISIT IN THE EVENING BESIDES THEY HAVE A COLLECTION OF WORLD MUSIC WHICH SETS IT APART FROM ANY PLACE IN MYANMAR.

FOR INDIAN CUISINE: MY RECOMENDATION AND THE BEST ONE AT THAT IS CC HUB. SITUATED IN PEARL CONDOMINIUM BASEMENT JUST AT THE CORNER OF SAYA SAN ROAD AND KABA AYE PAGAODA ROAD THEY SERVE EXQUISITE INDIAN CUISINE, TRY OUT THE TANDOORI CHICKEN, THE NAAN AND ALSO SOME OF THE SPECIAL INDIA CURRIES. THE ATMOSPHERE IS ALSO EXCELLENT THOUGH A BIT LOUD ON WEDNEDAYS AND SATURDAY EVENINGS WHICH ARE CELEBRATED AS WESTERN AND INDIAN NIGHTS RESPECTIVELY. THEY ALSO SERVE ITALIAN PIZZA'S AND SPAGETTIES. THE PIZZA'S ARE WITH EXOTIC TOPPINGS. WORTH A TRY ONCE AND YOU WILL VISIT AGAIN AND AGAIN FOR THE FOOD.
