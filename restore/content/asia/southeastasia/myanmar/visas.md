# Visas

*Asia > Southeastasia > Myanmar > Visas*

Visa fee: Subject to present visa fee at Myanmese embassy of your country. 

 Processing Time: Approximately 1 working day, excluding postal delivery time 

 Requirements: 

 1 - Two visa application forms

 2 - Three passport size photographs

 3 - Valid passport

 4 - Bank cheque for visa fee (cashiers cheque or similar)

 5 - Self-addressed, stamped envelope (registered mail)

 6 - Entry form

 Additional requirements for business visas: Confirmation letter from the employer or the Myanmar partner company showing reason and duration of stay 

 Confirmation of onward travel (photo-copy of flight ticket or similar) 

 Procedure:   The above documents should be sent to the embassy of Myanmar in your country (by registered mail). Once the visa is approved by the authorities, the passport is posted back to you in your self-addressed/stamped/registered envelope (the visa covers one full page in the passport).  Validity of Visa 

 The visa is valid for 28 days of travel, within three months from date of issue and is single entry only. 

 Compulsary Exchange   Upon arrival every foreigner travelling independently, (i.e. not in a tour group), must exchange US\$ 300 to FEC 300 (Foreign Exchange Certificates). These FECs are legal tender in Myanmar and have the same value of the US dollar. FECs cannot be changed back upon departure. This procedure to to ensure that tourists spend a minimum of $300 during their stay.
