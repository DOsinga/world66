# Nightlife and Entertainment in Port Moresby

*Asia > Southeastasia > Papuanewguinea > Portmoresby > Nightlife And Ente*

**Port Moresby nightlife** consists mainly of going to well-heeled clubs and bars-cum-restaurants as there are very few nightclubs here.

**The Returned Services club**

The Returned Services club (RSL) is just a few steps away from the Davara Hotel. It is situated on the beach side of the road. War veterans gather here and trade war time stories. Tourists are welcome and can join on a temporary basis for a nominal fee. As the day fades away and night falls, members start entering the club where they can watch TV, play video games and order food. They have a restaurant that provides outdoor and indoor seating area. The Coral Sea is behind the club building and you can enjoy a great view while eating, drinking and generally making merry!.

**The Royal Papua Yacht Club**

The Royal Papua Yacht Club is a club that provides both offshore and harbor yachting where members can also park their yachts. It holds yacht racing competitions at regular intervals. Members make full use of their bar and restaurant in the evenings when they can enjoy a quiet drink and good food. The most popular items on its menu are steaks, pasta and seafood. Its buffets are also quit popular. It is a place where business deals are struck over dinner. The phone number is (675)321-1700.

**Ela Beach**

Ela Beach is a superb place to enjoy Port Moresby nightlife. Strolling along the beach on a full moonlit night will give a heady feeling! There are lots of food stalls on the beach where you can enjoy delectable food.

**The Pondo Bar**

The Pondo Bar is a great place to start up a chat with the local people over drinks. There is a balcony where you can view the people roaming the streets. The bar is open all days of the week. On Thursdays only ladies are allowed in the bar.

**The Shady Rest Hotel Bar**

The Shady Rest Hotel Bar is also a popular bar of the region and is operational through the entire night. Here apart from drinking pleasure you will also get to enjoy live music by the local bands of the region.

**Lamana **

Lamana is a huge nightclub with an outdoor dance floor that has a Greek theme. It is a very trendy night club with three dancing floors and five bars. It is multi-storied and caters to all age groups. There are live in-house music bands that have extravagant lights as well as the best sound and visual projection system at their disposal. The latest international music is played. It also has a good security system in place and is packed with local people on the weekends. It is situated near the Holiday Inn Hotel in Hohole suburb.

**Chilli Club **

Chilli Club, also known as the British Club, offers drinks and dancing in the late evening. It also has pool tables where players enjoy their game.
