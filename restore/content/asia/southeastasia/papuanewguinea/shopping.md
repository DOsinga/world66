# Shopping

*Asia > Southeastasia > Papuanewguinea > Shopping*

You can bargain for a great range of handicrafts and other souvenirs in Papua New Guinea. Some of the must-buy souvenirs include basketwork from the islands, carvings from the north coast, tapa cloth from the northern province, woven articles from the highlands, and shells and pottery from the coast. These products are available in special shops, in most hotel foyers, and in street shops. The Boroko Shopping Centre in Port Moresby is the best place to bargain especially on Saturday mornings. Specially packaged Arabica coffee is also an ideal gift that you would like to carry back home.
