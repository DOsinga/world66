# San Antonio beach, n. samar

*Asia > Southeastasia > Philippines > Calbayog > Beaches > San Antonio Beach*

San Antonio island is an islet next to Capul and Biri Rock formations on the Northern Samar side. It is accessible by 20 minute motorized banca ride from San Isidro or Victoria. I'd suggest Victoria is closest, and from Calbayog airport to Victoria is an hour ride.
