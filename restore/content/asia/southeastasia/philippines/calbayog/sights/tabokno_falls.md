# Tabokno Falls

*Asia > Southeastasia > Philippines > Calbayog > Sights > Tabokno Falls*

Located a hour drive from Calbayog City and 40-minute walk from the villages.

A beautiful and scenic waterfall. This wonderful
creation of nature is a favorite picnic site of local residents and
their source of potable water.
