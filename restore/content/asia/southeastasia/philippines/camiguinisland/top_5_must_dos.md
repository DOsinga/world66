# Top 5 Must Do's in Camiguin island

*Asia > Southeastasia > Philippines > Camiguinisland > Top 5 Must Dos*

If you are going to be traveling to the Philippines and are looking
for a great place to relax and enjoy some of the fun, you should visit
Camiguin Island and find the **Camiguin Island must do’s**. The
beautiful area around Camiguin Island is home to many fun activities and
sights to enjoy on your vacation.
**1. Waterfalls**

If you enjoy being out in nature and want to see some of the most
beautiful waterfalls in the area, you can find many to see while in
Camiguin Island. There are the Katibawasan Falls and the Tuscan Falls.
They are beautiful to watch, and it's relaxing to enjoy the cascading
water and rock formations that provide beauty and serenity.
**2. Hot and Cold Springs**

The Tangub Hot Spring, Macao Cold Spring, Sto. Nino Cold Spring and
the Ardent Hot Spring are some of the best springs to enjoy while on
vacation. You can relax and enjoy the scenery and sounds of the soothing
springs. Each of the springs is located on the island and provides
facilities to use and enjoy while in the area. Tourists can walk, hike
and picnic in the locations and enjoy being out in nature.
**3. Historic Landmarks**

Tourists can enjoy the many unique landmarks that are found in
Camiguin Island like the Catarman Church Ruins, Cross Marker and the
Observatory Station. These landmarks are a great way to learn more about
the culture and people of the city, and to enjoy seeing some of the
oldest ruins and landmarks around.
**4. Cultures**

The people of Camiguin Island welcome you with a culture that is full
of color and excitement. You can enjoy learning more about the
settlers in the area and the many talents and arts of the people who are
now living on the island. You can enjoy the music, sounds and beauty of
the island and a warm and relaxing atmosphere from the people who live
in the area.
**5. Food and Drink**

There are many places to enjoy a great meal and enjoy the foods that
are grown on the island. The people of the island are friendly and warm,
and you will find a variety of fresh foods to enjoy at every stop along
the way. You can find hotels and resorts to stay that offer warm
greetings and great food. You can also learn more about the foods that
are locally grown by the people in the area.
