# When to Go in Camiguin island

*Asia > Southeastasia > Philippines > Camiguinisland > When To Go*

If you are planning a holiday in **Camiguin Island, when to go**
is probably what is on your mind. Camiguin is a wonderful island
paradise; however, if you want to enjoy the entire Island, it is best to
plan your holiday when there are exciting things happening on Camiguin
Island.

**Summer Season (March to May)**

Since most of Camiguin’s main attractions are connected to water and
the sea, the best and safest time to visit is during the summer months
or dry season. Summer is from the month of March to May. During these
months there are no typhoons or bad weather that can spoil your holiday.
This is the perfect time to enjoy the great beaches on Camiguin Island,
soak under the sun and get that perfect tan you always wanted.

**Panaad (Holy Week)**

If you want to relax, wind down and rejuvenate your life, visiting
Camiguin during Holy Week would be a great option. There are lots of
spiritual and religious activities that take place all over the island
during Lent. Locals call this time "Panaad." You might want to visit
Camiguin Island during this time and take part in some of the religious
activities.

**Town Fiesta (May)**

Filipinos love fiestas, and most of the fiestas in the Philippines are
celebrated during the month of May, and Camiguin is not an exemption.
During the whole month of May, Camiguin is filled with various
celebrations and parties. The most important day is May 15. You
will enjoy yourself to the maximum if you visit Camiguin Island during
this time. Fiestas are always all about fun, food and partying.

**San Juan Hibok-Hibokan (June)**

San Juan Hibok-Hibokan is an annual festival that takes place on
the June 24. The festival is a tribute to St. John the Baptist and
is usually celebrated on the beaches of Agohay and/or Cabua-an. During
the San Juan Hibok-Hibokan festival, locals and visitors compete in
different water sports like aqua palo sebo and boat racing. There are
also street processions and parades as well as the Miss Hibok-Hibokan
beauty pageant.

**Lansones Festival (October)**

Camiguin is well known not only for its beaches and vast vegetation
but also for its woodlands. One of the most popular produce in
Camiguin Island is the Lansones fruit. The Camiguin Island local
government has set apart the third week of October to celebrate the
harvest of the Lansones. This is a time of celebration you would not
want to miss while vacationing in Camiguin Island.
