# ILA PUTI

*Asia > Southeastasia > Philippines > Cebu > Cebucity > Eatingout > Ila Puti*

ILA PUTI restaurant serves freshly cooked dishes with asian & fusion flavors. Best-sellers would be Saigon Adobo, Penang Plato, Salpicado, Southside Ribs, Salted Egg Fried Rice, Pasta Indio, Stroganov and Pasta Puti among many.

ILA PUTI also served breakfast. A must try is the Silog Special. They also have bacon & eggs, and chorizo and eggs.

ILA PUTI also servers their signature 22oz blended drinks, from iced tea, green tea, pink lemonade, strawberry, calamansi and more!

Just started to serve sizzling burger steaks, quarter pounder burgers on whole wheat buns.
