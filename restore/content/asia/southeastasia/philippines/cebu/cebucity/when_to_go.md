# When to Go in Cebu City

*Asia > Southeastasia > Philippines > Cebu > Cebucity > When To Go*

**Cebu City-When to Go** It's easy to know when to go to Cebu City because Cebu City's seasonal weather is very predictable. Generally, the weather is Cebu City is acceptable all year round. However, while it is feasible to go to Cebu City at any time of the year, there are also some seasons that are a better time to go than others.

**December thru February: Cold Season**

Cebu City’s climate peaks during its cold season, when the weather is cool and dry. From December to February temperatures range from 18 degrees C (64.4 degrees F) to 26 degrees C (78.8 degrees C). This would be the perfect time to travel because while the weather is only mildly warm, there is less rainfall than any other time in the year. Temperatures only approach 18 degrees C at night, so you can expect the weather to stay above 22 degrees C (71.6 degrees C) for most of the day. January is also a great month to travel because Sinulog, an annual religious festival celebrating their conversion to Christianity, happens during this time.

**March and April: Hot, Hot, Hot!**

March and April are also decent months to travel, but be careful. These summer months can reach over 40 degrees C (104 degrees F) on some days, and Cebu City’s high average humidity (over 80 percent in some seasons) can make the heat feel even worse. April, however, is the driest month of the year; if you have a choice between traveling in March or April, choose the latter.

**June through November: Rain Season**

June through November (particularly September) is usually the worst time to travel. Although the average temperature remains between 26 degrees C and 28 degrees C, this period is also Cebu City’s wet season, when average rainfall in this period reaches around 1300 millimeters (51.18 inches). Rainfall is especially bad because some of the roads in Cebu City are underdeveloped and will most likely be closed off. Hotels and resorts may offer incredible deals to travel here during this time, but don’t be fooled; they only do this because they are aware of how unpleasant the weather can be. If at all possible, avoid visiting during this time.

**Overall Expectations**

Overall, you should feel confident that Cebu’s weather will be relatively stable for each season. Although Cebu experiences a heavy amount of rainfall each year, it also stays sunny and warm nearly year round. There will be occasions when average temperatures fluctuate from week to week, but usually you can expect the weather to remain consistent for your entire trip. Whatever time you plan to travel, make sure to have fun and bring lots of sunscreen!
