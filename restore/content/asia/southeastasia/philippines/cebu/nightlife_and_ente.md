# Nightlife and Entertainment in Cebu

*Asia > Southeastasia > Philippines > Cebu > Nightlife And Ente*

Cebu is one of the most urban cities in the [Philippines](/asia/southeastasia/philippines), and it is known for its vibrant nightlife. Whether you are looking for a place to party or you just want to relax and listen to music, you will find that there are a lot of options available in this great city. Here are some of the places that offer excellent night entertainment in [Cebu](/asia/southeastasia/philippines/cebu).

**Discos and Dance Clubs**

If you are one of those who love dancing and wild partying, there are a number of popular dance clubs and discos that you can visit. One of the best places for dancing and socializing in Cebu is the Sunflower Disco. This is the largest and most famous discotheque in the [city](/asia/southeastasia/philippines/cebu/cebucity), and it is the preferred night entertainment spot for trendy locals and tourists. There is a live band in this club that performs the latest dance music, and a dance competition will be held every Thursday. The Sunflower Disco is located close to Salinas Drive, where there are many other establishments that offer great night entertainment.

The Club Vudu is another well-known dance club in Cebu. This place is popular among people who are in their twenties, and it is also frequented by expats and tourists. It is smaller than most other dance clubs in the city, but it does get hopping on weekends. The club plays house music as well as R&B and hip hop. It is located in the Crossroads Mall. Other dance clubs that are recommended include the Bai Disco in the [Cebu Plaza Hotel](/asia/southeastasia/philippines/cebu/accommodation), Pump in Mango Square and Inner Circle in the Reclamation Area.

**Pubs and Bars**

Cebu also has numerous bars and pubs where you can have a few drinks with friends or watch the latest sports events on television. The Autoshop Bar and Restaurant is one of the hottest bars in Cebu, and it can get very crowded on weekends. If you are planning to [dine](/asia/southeastasia/philippines/cebu/eating_out) in this place, make sure you get there earlier. The partying usually starts around 10.00 pm, with wonderful hip hop, R&B and other kinds of music being played.

If you want to go to a sports bar, you can try the Badger Sports and Dining Pub. This pub has a big screen television that shows various sports programs, and it is a great place for socializing and networking. Other popular pubs and bars in Cebu include City Lights, Alibi, Bachelors Too, Bar Cane, Pub 24, Club Amigo, The Barn and St. Moritz Pub and Bar.
