# Ragtime Jazz Bar

*Asia > Southeastasia > Philippines > Dagupancity > Bars And Cafes > Ragtime Jazz Bar*

Ragtime, located at the corner of Perez, Herrero and MH del Pilar
streets, is a cozy bar that plays a lot of jazz and rnb music. Also
available is nightly live music featuring a variety of bands that play
everything from acoustic to 60s pop.
