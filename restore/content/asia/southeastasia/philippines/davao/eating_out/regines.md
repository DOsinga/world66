# Coco's

*Asia > Southeastasia > Philippines > Davao > Eating Out > Regines*

****An excellent restaurant mainly featuring seafood. Also with an excellent Roasted Pig.

Regine's located on F. Torres Street, Davao City is no longer. It now called Coco's Grill. The have a great menu from pizza to lobster. This is one of many favorite restaurants in Davao for the wife and I. Great food and service.
