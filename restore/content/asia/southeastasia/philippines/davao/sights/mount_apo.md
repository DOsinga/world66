# Mount Apo National Park

*Asia > Southeastasia > Philippines > Davao > Sights > Mount Apo*

At 2,954 meters, this is the country's tallest peak. Other
attractions nearby include Lake Venado, and several falls
and hot springs.

The Department of Tourism Regional Office organizes climbs to Mt Apo, which usually take place around March or April.
