# Chin Loong

*Asia > Southeastasia > Philippines > Dumaguetecity > Eating Out > Chin Loong*

Located along Dumaguete's famous Boulevard stretch. It serves mostly
Chinese food and some local delicacies like barbecued pork and chicken.
The main eating area is airconditioned and an al fresco corner is
available for smokers. Price is affordable, food is average in taste,
but the place is usually packed at night time.
