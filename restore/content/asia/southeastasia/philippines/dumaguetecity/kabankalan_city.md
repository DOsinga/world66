# Kabankalan City Travel Guide

the rising city of southern negros! welcome to Kabankalan! this is a first class city in the sugar bowl of the philippines.. a place where nature is preserved while getting an advancement in developing it structural environment for a more better city.. sooner the new domestic airport will be operating with great connections to most of the destinations in the archipelago. Kabankalan city is rising next to negros occidental's capital city which is Bacolod. Kabankalan could offer nature lovers the pleasure of caving, trekking, swimming under Mag-aso falls, mountain hiking going to balicaocao highland resort overviewing the beautiful scene of the city. Located 2 hours from bacolod and 4 hours from dumaguete. Lodging establishments of great service is always available.
