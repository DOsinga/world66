# Beaches in General Santos City

*Asia > Southeastasia > Philippines > Generalsantoscity > Beaches*

General Santos City is a beautiful coastal town where visitors can indulge their appetites for water sports and seafood. The seafood here is reputed to be one of the freshest in the world. The tuna alone is one of a kind. Also, there are many spots in the city from which you can enjoy water sports.

**
Sarangani Bay
**

This is the most common diving area. It is situated just outside the province, and is an easy drive away. The beach is clean and well-maintained. Most people will enjoy the view of the sea from this point.

**Tinoto Wall
**

This diving spot is great for the unusual rock formations at the deep end of the sea. It's considered a top rate place for deep sea diving. Otherwise, there is not much to see here.

**Rocky Beach
**

You can dive in shallow waters here and witness the underwater reefs. This place is best for those who prefer shallow water diving. The underwater reefs are a visual treat to behold.

**
Kamanga Reef
**

Again, this is a good place for shallow water diving and seeing the underwater reef. You will find some fantastic marine life here with the reefs.

**Veteran's Beach
**

This is a great place to enjoy the mild climate and the sun, sand and sea. The weather is especially good from November to March. You will be taken in by the beauty of this beach.

**London Beach Resort
**

This is a nice place to enjoy serene, peaceful relaxation. There is not much of a crowd here and you will have the place almost to yourself. An ideal spot if you want some precious `me’ time.

**
Rajah Beach Resort
**

The beach at this resort is simply out of this world and very clean. You can unwind here away from the crowd. You will love the easy charm of this place.

**Santos City Beach
**

This beach is lined with thatched roof huts. making for interesting rustic scenery. If you love a rustic setting rather than a posh touristy theme, head here for some rudimentary charm. The ambiance is perfect for a day out in the sun.

All the beaches mentioned above are great places to relax in and catch some time in the sun. Enjoy the sea, fresh sea-food and the beaches if you visit Gensan.
