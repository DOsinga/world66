# Have Sam Thai

*Asia > Southeastasia > Philippines > Generalsantoscity > Eating Out 1 > Have Sam Thai 1*

For an Authentic Thai experience head on to Have Sam Thai located at Tiongson St., Lagao General Santos City wherein popular dishes such as the Pad Thai, Beef Khao Pad, Namtok, Beef in Red Curry, Chicken Pandan and Money Bags are served at a very affordable price of 75php to 85php. The staff is friendly and the ambience is that of the side streets of Thailand.
