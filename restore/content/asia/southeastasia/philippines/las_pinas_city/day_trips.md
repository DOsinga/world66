# Day Trips in Las Pinas City

*Asia > Southeastasia > Philippines > Las Pinas City > Day Trips*

If you want to travel to Las Pinas City, part of the National Capital Region of Philippines, here is a list of popular destinations for **Las Pina City day trips**.

**Manila Bay

**This is one of the closest places from Las Pinas City. Manila Bay is considered to be one of the finest harbors in the world. Visitors to this beautiful place can enjoy a view of superb waters and volcanic peaks that are topped with tropical foliage. A few km into Manila Bay are the islands of Caballo and Corrigidor. If you leave early in the morning you should be able to make a quick trip to these islands. Besides these, there are many other sights to visit around the bay.

**Paranaque City
**
This is an ideal place to visit for a 1-day trip. Founded in 1572, Paranaque city is rich in history and culture. However, it has changed considerably over the years. Today it is part of the municipalities and cities that make up Metro Manila. Paranaque City offers a number of interesting sights like Manila Memorial Park, Jaka Plaza and Elrode Boxing Center. In addition to this, Paranaque City boasts of numerous shopping malls, making it the perfect destination for shoppers. It also has several restaurants and pubs that serve different South Asian cuisines.

**Muntinlupa City

**Tourists looking to take in a little history of Philippines should visit the neighboring city of Muntinlupa. The city is quite urbanized and is also as the Emerald City of Philippines. A must visit place in Muntinlupa City is the Jamboree Lake, a small natural lake that has existed for a very long time. It is ideal for nature lovers and those who want to get away from tropical heat. Besides this, the Japanese Garden Cemetery, Memorial Hill and Director’s Quarters are some interesting places to visit.

**San Pedro, Laguna
**
This is a small laid back town situated across Lake Atitlan. A new roadway that was introduced a few years ago has made it possible to get to San Pedro in Laguna very easily from Las Pinas City. This getaway is perfect for anyone with an adventurous spirit. San Pedro offers a number of hiking trails that can be visited with a guide. Tourists can spend time sitting by the lake and enjoying the scenic beauty of the variety of foliage. The town also has many restaurants that serve great food at very reasonable prices.
