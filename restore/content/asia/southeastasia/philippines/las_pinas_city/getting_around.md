# Getting Around in Las Pinas City

*Asia > Southeastasia > Philippines > Las Pinas City > Getting Around*

Las Piñas is roughly translated as "the pineapples" in
English. This place boasts as having the first organ made out of bamboo. The organ
of the main church was made out of bamboo due to the resistance of bamboo in
the weather in Las Piñas. There are many ways
of getting around in Las Piñas.

**Ferries**

The ferries provide an
amazing way to travel in the Philippines. These ferries help carry people from 1 island to another. If you do not seek to travel a long distance you can jump
aboard a banca boat. The ferries offer a way to mingle with the people of this
wonderful country. The ferries are practically free and because of
this, many people in the Philippines chose this as their means of transportation
when moving about.

**Buses**

The buses in the
Philippines do not have a central station. The main lines all have stations in some of
the most important cities of the country. You can try Aladdin Transit or Baliwag
Transit Incorporated. These buses can take you all over metro Manila. Some of
these buses can even take you straight to your ferry so you can continue your
trip. People should expect to pay around P$400-600 when boarding a bus inside
Las Piñas City.

**Taxis**

There are taxis in
almost every part of Las Piñas. There are
yellow taxis and white taxis. The white taxis tend to be cheaper than
the yellow taxis but both tend to be equally efficient. Sometimes
they can charge as much as US$40 per a single ride. Make sure that your driver
uses the meter for many times people get cheated by the driver.

**Jeepney**

The Jeepney is one of
the most ingenious and the least expensive means of transportation around Las Piñas. The Jeepney are small model jeeps made in Las Piñas and are the most common means of transportation
here. Of all the means of transportation the Jeepney is the least expensive.

**Private Vehicle**

If you really want
to avoid the hassle of driving and the delays that it takes to wait for transport,
you can use a private vehicle. There are many different car rental places in
Las Piñas. When you rent your own car you can
get around quickly. Of course you have to cover rental fees and provide your
own gasoline but this is a good alternative when you are getting around Las Piñas with the whole family.
