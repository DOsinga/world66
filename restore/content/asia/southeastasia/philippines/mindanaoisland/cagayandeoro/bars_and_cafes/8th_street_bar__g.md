# 8th Street Bar & Grill

*Asia > Southeastasia > Philippines > Mindanaoisland > Cagayandeoro > Bars And Cafes > 8Th Street Bar  G*

Located at 8th Street and Tomas Saco, Nazareth, 8th Street Bar & Grill is one of the latest addition to the growing list of nightlife destinations at Cagayan de Oro. Great food, superb entertainment and accessible location makes 8th Street Bar & Grill a must to visit when in Cagayan de Oro. Acoustic sessions every Wednesdays, Fridays and Saturdays from 9pm to 1am. Bar is open from Mondays to Sundays from 5pm to 1am. Also accepting special events e.g birthdays, parties, corporate events.
