# Nightlife and Entertainment in Cagayan de Oro

*Asia > Southeastasia > Philippines > Mindanaoisland > Cagayandeoro > Nightlife And Ente*

The City of Cagayan de Oro is located along the central
coast of Northern Mindanao. It's the center
for commerce and trade for Northern Mindanao, and it is classified as a first
class city. Cagayan de Oro is a place that's popular with tourists visiting
the Philippines. Any city which is a tourist destination is normally filled
with nightclubs and entertainment venues, and Cagayan de Oro is no exception.
There are several great places where you can enjoy your night in Cagayan de
Oro.**
**

**Kamogawa Japanese
Restaurant**

Kamogawa is a fine restaurant serving authentic Japanese food.
Located in the heart of Cagayan de Oro, the restaurant is popular with many
tourists. It provides three dining options: private dining rooms,
white-linen dining tables or at the sushi bar. The restaurant is clean and provides
excellent service.**
**

**Picasso Bar**

Picasso Bar is located on the premises of the Dynasty Court
Hotel. The bar provides live music every evening, and is popular with the young,
hip crowd. Located near the Saint Augustine Cathedral, it's a great
place to enjoy your night.**
**

**Spooks**

Spooks is one of the cleanest bars in Cagayan de Oro. The
bar provides excellent service to its patrons, and each customer is taken good
care of by efficient staff. The drinks at the bar are not overpriced and you
pay for what you order – nothing extra. Spooks is a great place to hang out and
enjoy a drink. One visit to this bar will have you coming back again and again.**
**

**Bigby’s Café**

If you are missing your American coffee and food, head over
to Bigby’s Café. The café serves a variety of dishes and is a comfortable place
to relax and enjoy your beer. Bigby’s is located on Rosario Crescent in the center
of Cagayan de Oro. The atmosphere is casual and you can easily
strike up a conversation with someone.**
**

**Vienna Kaffeehaus**

Vienna Kaffeehaus is one of the best places for European
cuisine. Located in downtown Cagayan de Oro, this café is very popular among
tourists. Vienna Kaffeehaus gives you to opportunity to enjoy several authentic
European dishes. The service offered here is one of the best. You must try the
coffee – it tastes so good. Vienna Kaffeehaus has outlets in several parts of
the Philippines.
