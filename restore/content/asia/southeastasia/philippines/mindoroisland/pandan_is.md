Pandan Grande Island ( Sablayan ). It is a 30-hectare islet surrounded by white sand beach and is also the site of a 20-room diver's lodge made of native cottages. This beautiful little coral islet is located within snorkeling distance from a submarine drop-off inhabited by schools of reef. more info visit www.pandan.com
