*Asia > Southeastasia > Philippines > Negrosisland > Bacolod > Beaches > Mishfish*

Punta Bulata Beach Resort in the south is one of the favored destinations white white sand beach and excellent accommodations.

Further down south you will find more resorts in Sipalay. Lots of tiny dive resorts here with their own charm. Easy Diving and Takatuka are some of the popular ones.

To the North is Jomabo Island, which is great for day-trippers.
