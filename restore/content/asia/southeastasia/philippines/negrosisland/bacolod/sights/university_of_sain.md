# University Of Saint La Salle

*Asia > Southeastasia > Philippines > Negrosisland > Bacolod > Sights > University Of Sain*

10 hectares of land was donated by Hon. Alfredo Montelibano to the Christian Brothers. The La Salle College was Founded by Bro. Dennis Ruland, FSC, Bro. Hugh Wester, FSC, and Bro. Felix Masson.
The University Grounds serves as host to 2005 SEA Games for Beach Volleyball and Boxing. The University serves as the catalyst of transfprmation of Bacolod City and also to the whole country, garnered as one of the top 20 Universities in the country and being outstanding in its fields of discipline covered under its curriculum.
