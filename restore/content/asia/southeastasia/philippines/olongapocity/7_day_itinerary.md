# 7 Day Itinerary in Olongapo City

*Asia > Southeastasia > Philippines > Olongapocity > 7 Day Itinerary*

There are lots of thing to do
and explore in Olongapo City; that is why it is recommended that you have your
own **Olongapo 7-day itinerary** so you can spend your time properly and you won’t get lost in the vast
must-see and must-do collection Olongapo City can offer you.

**Day
1 - Exploring Culture and History**

Spend a day enjoying the
history and culture of Olongapo City. Visit the Olongapo City Museum where you will
find a vast collection of paintings, dioramas and artifacts. You can
also visit the Aeta tribes and learn their tradition and lifestyle. Take the
whole day to explore and enjoy the unique culture of Olongapo City.

**Day
2 - Nature Tripping**

Explore the natural beauty
of Olongapo in a different way. One of the best ways to enjoy your holiday here
is to take a trip to the rich tropical area. Popular destinations in Olongapo that
involve nature are Zoobic Safari, Ocean Adventure, The Tree Top Adventure and
Mt Pinatubo Trail Trekking.

**Day
3 – Head for the Beach **

Besides the vast tropical
rain forest, Olongapo also has beautiful white sand beaches. You can visit any
of the popular beaches like Half Moon Beach, Dungaree Beach and Driftwood
Beach. If you enjoy water sports like surfing, skiing and diving these are the
must-visit resorts: Camayan Beach Resort, White Rock, Johan’s and Arizona
International. Enjoy a picnic at any of these beaches.

**Day
4 - Water Sports**

If you have visited all the
beautiful beaches and you are bored soaking under the sun try some water
sports. If you enjoy surfing, skiing and/or diving these are the must-visit
resorts: Camayan Beach Resort, White Rock, Johan’s and Arizona International.

**Day
5 – Local Cuisine**

Take a break from physical
exploration and take a walk in the center of town. Enjoy yourself with the local
and international cuisine from different Olongapo restaurants and eateries. One
of the things you need to do while on holiday is to enjoy local cuisine.

**Day
6 - Bar Hopping**

Your vacation is not
complete without having good time with your friends, great music and great
beer. Pump up your blood and adrenalin and go bar hopping. Popular destinations
are Gigolo Disco House, Pier One and Asian Reef. There are also open-air bars
along the shore and deep within the city.

**Day
7 - Shopping Galore**

Spend a day shopping before
leaving Olongapo City. You can buy some souvenirs and gifts for your family or
friends from any of the several shops in town. Popular shopping destinations in
Olongapo are Rocker Boutique, Olongapo Mall, Duty Free Mall and the downtown
wet market.
