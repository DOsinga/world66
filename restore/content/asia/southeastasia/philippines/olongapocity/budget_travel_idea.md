# Budget Travel Ideas in Olongapo City

*Asia > Southeastasia > Philippines > Olongapocity > Budget Travel Idea*

There are several **Olongapo City budget travel** ideas that can make your visit to Olongapo City a pleasurable one. Olongapo City is one of the popular tourist destinations in the Philippines but you don’t need to splurge if you plan to pay a visit. Here are a few tips you can use.

**Economical Transportation**

There are 3 ways you can get to Olongapo City; by air, water and/or land. Olongapo has a modern international airport--Subic Bay International Airport. There are several budget airlines that operate flights to Olongapo City. If you choose to travel by sea; you make take a ferry boat, ship ride or RORO boats that will lead you to the Subic Freeport. If you are not apprehensive by long land journey you may take domestic buses or taxis that are bound to Olongapo City.

**Low-priced Accommodations**

If you are looking for affordable yet cozy accommodation, you can try the Bayfront Hotel. They have clean, air-conditioned rooms with TV and refrigerator. The hotel also has a swimming pool and the hotel's staff is well trained and friendly. Another affordable hotel accommodation is the Zanzibar hotel that offers rooms on a twin sharing basis. Make use of the free Wi-Fi available in the hotel.

**Affordable but Sumptuous Cuisine**

Rice is an Asian food staple and one of the most popular rice-based Asian dishes is the congee. You can have a serving of congee at Arby’s for Php 20; this is great for breakfast or snack. You can find other sumptuous but affordable dishes around the city because small restaurants and eateries are plentiful on the streets of Olongapo. From here you can find local delicacies like Adobo, Menudo and Bulalo for only Php50.

**Where to Go**

You can also visit some historical spots in Olongapo City. Places like the Volunteer Park, Ulo Ni Apo Monument, Marikit Park and the World Peace Memorial Dove are great places to visit and do not cost you anything.

**Shopping Galore**

You can pick up some great stuff at bargain prices at these places. Royal Subic is the biggest department store in town and there you can find great bargains and surplus name-brand merchandise. Value City offers an extensive line of products; you will be able to get some unique items at this place. Island Souvenirs offers a wide array of gift ideas and souvenirs. Make sure you bargain at these places to get the best rates.
