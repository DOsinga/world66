# Things to do in San Pablo City

*Asia > Southeastasia > Philippines > Sanpablocity > Things To Do*

Known as the “city with seven lakes” there are several **San Pablo
City things to do**. Here are a few ideas you might want to consider
while on holiday in San Pablo City.

**Bamboo Rafting **

It’s nice to explore the pristine waters of San Pablo’s seven lakes,
and the best way to do it is by bamboo rafting. You can rent bamboo
rafts for only $5 per person and travel around the 48-hectares twin
lakes of Yamboo and Pandin. This tour will take you 10 to 30 minutes.
You might want to take your camera along to get some great photos.

**Biking around Sampaloc Lake**

Sampaloc Lake is the biggest among the seven lakes in San Pablo City.
The lake covers an area of 104 hectares and is 27 meters deep. You can
rent a bike and ride around the lake to take in the beautiful scenery.
If you are with a friend you might want to rent the bike with a sidecar.
This will cost you under $2 an hour. You will also be able to visit
several fish farms at the lake.

**Viaje Del Sol **

When you go to San Pablo you don’t want to miss the sumptuous
gastronomical offerings of this city that showcase the culture,
tradition and heritage of Lagunians. Viaje Del Sol literally means "the
way of the sun" and it is a famous culinary journey around the provinces
of Laguna, Batangas and Quezon. Through this tour you can discover
several hidden and off-the-beaten-paths within San Pablo City. Places
you will explore on the tour are the Herbana Farms, Sitio de Amor,
Kusina Salud, Casa San Pablo and more.

**Picnic at Bato Resort**

Bato Resort is located in Barangay San Cristobal, near the boundaries
of San Pablo and Dolores, Quezon. This rustic-looking resort features
ice cold running water from Mt. Banahaw. There are two man-made
waterfalls and large swimming pools for kids and adults. The lush
vegetation and clean and free-flowing river of Bato Resort makes it a
perfect place for family picnics and chilling out with friends.

**Bar Hopping**

After sunset you can still get out and party with friends and go bar
hopping within the city of San Pablo. There are several good places that
offer great entertainment, affordable food and ice cold beer. Places
you can go are La Pizzeria, Red Chili Resto Bar, Golden Star, Coco
Place, Q5 Corner Stage and ZYPP Zone.
