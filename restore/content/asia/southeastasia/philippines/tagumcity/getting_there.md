# Getting There

*Asia > Southeastasia > Philippines > Tagumcity > Getting There*

AIR. Tagum is accessible via Davao International Airport serviced by all major domestic airlines with direct flights from Manila and Cebu. About an hour travel from the Davao airport to the City of Tagum through an overland trasnport via aircon or non-aircon buses or taxis. LAND. Tagum's road network is the crossroad center of the northern part of Region XI, Southern Mindanao via Maharlika Highways. It is the access road from Davao City to the Provinces of Davao Oriental, Compostela Valley and portion of Bukidnon and Agusan del Sur road which connect the city to other major destination in the region and in the entire Mindanao even up to Pasay City, Metro Manila. SEA. The city can be reached via Port of Sasa, Davao City that serves and catered passenger lines plying the route that networks to Zamboanga City, Iloilo City, Cebu and Manila. From the port take an overland transport via aircon or non-aircon buses or taxis.
