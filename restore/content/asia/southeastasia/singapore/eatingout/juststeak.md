# Just Steak

*Asia > Southeastasia > Singapore > Eatingout > Juststeak*

Just as the name suggests, steak is the main attraction of this restaurant. For those meat lovers, the steaks here are cooked to perfection and at a reasonable price as well with light side dishes as well. For those who prefer less meat, the restaurant serves smaller portions as well. With an overall rating of eight out of ten, the dress code is smart casual here too.
