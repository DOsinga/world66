# Lawry's The Prime Rib

*Asia > Southeastasia > Singapore > Eatingout > Lawrystheprimerib*

For those of you who can't get enough of the best prime beef, then Lawry's is the place to go to quench your appetite. It is a little less expensive compared to the other restaurants with suculent beef and the neccessary side dishes to add flavour. The atmosphere in the restaurant compliments the decor.
