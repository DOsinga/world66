# Singapore Fashion Festival 2005

*Asia > Southeastasia > Singapore > Festivals > Singapore Fashion*

The festival was held from 23 Mar to 3 April at Orchard Road. It was one of the most glamourous events of the year. It featured the latest local and international designer fashions. Come and see for yourself at the virtual tour of SFF at www.singaporesights.com.
