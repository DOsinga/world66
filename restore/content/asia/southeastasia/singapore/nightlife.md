# Nightlife and Entertainment

*Asia > Southeastasia > Singapore > Nightlife*

If you are looking for entertainment, then Sungai Buloh is one of the places to go and have some fun before exploring the rest of the island. You will find many wild creature there. For those of you who want to try their luck at singing, this place has a fair few karoke lounges to keep you busy. By the way, it is estimated that Singapore hosts about 700 clubs, bars, pubs and discos. So share your thougts about them with your fellow travellers. 

For those animal lovers, try Night Safari. This is a night zoo where you can ride around on a tram and see nocturnal animals. Walking through this truly unique zoo is a must on any trip to Singapore. Geylang (Spore Red District) is one place where every cabby knows the way. Ask for it and you will be greatly awarded :)
