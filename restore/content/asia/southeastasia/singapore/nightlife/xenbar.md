# Xenbar

*Asia > Southeastasia > Singapore > Nightlife > Xenbar*

If you're wondering where in Sinagpore you can find all week Salsa,
this is The place. It serves $5 buffettes, dishes up Salsa lessons you
can take as a one time thing or over a month, and hosts two dance
floors that are absolutely condusive for Salsa dancing. They also play
Tango music some nights too, and host a live band occassionally.
