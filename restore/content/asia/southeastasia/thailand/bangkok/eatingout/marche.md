# Marche

*Asia > Southeastasia > Thailand > Bangkok > Eatingout > Marche*

This is actually a micro brewery and a huge bistro serving all kinds of food. Everthing is freshly prepared. You can choose from Indian, Italian, Japanese, Thai, Chinese, Indonesian, German, Swiss and other international cuisines. On some days there is even live music. Highly recommended.
