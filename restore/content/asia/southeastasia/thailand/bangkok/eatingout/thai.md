# Thai

*Asia > Southeastasia > Thailand > Bangkok > Eatingout > Thai*

**Restaurants**

**108**

It is a rather charming place and the food is fine and medium spicy. By the way, the running joke that all prices end in 8 Baht. 

address: Phra Athit Rd, Banglampoo

tel: +66-26291218

**Hemlock**

This is a small place located opposite of the River Express Boat near the Banglampoo/Khao San Road area. It is run by a group of ex-students from the close Thammasat University. Cheap and good. Open daily, from 3 pm - midnight. 

address: 56 Phra Athit Rd.

**Gallery Cafe **

Pretty good Thai food in a special environment - four floors of Asian and modern art. Tom Yam Kung soup has been recommended. Lunch and dinner. 

address: 1293-1295 Charoen Krung Rd.

tel: +66-22340053

**Baan Chiang**

Excellent Thai restaurant in an old Thai house with a cluttered feel. Credit cards accepted. Open daily 11 am - 2 pm and 5 pm - 10:30 pm. They serve also Northeastern food (Isaan food). 

address: 14 Soi Srivieng, Surasak Rd. (near Silom Rd.)

tel: +66-2-2367045

**Room with a View**

A great place in a Soi going off the busy Payathai Road. 5 minutes walk from Ari Skytrain station. Inexpensive, but nice athmosphere with a nice garden. 

address: 15 Pahonyothin 2, Pahanyothin Rd., Payathai

openinghours: Lunch and Dinner, daily

tel: +66-(0)2-2783964
