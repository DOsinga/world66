# The Barbican

*Asia > Southeastasia > Thailand > Bangkok > Nightlife > Thebarbican*

This rather british-style pub is good place to meet business expatriates. It serves good food and drinks and has a friendly staff. Unfortunately it is located in Thanon Thaniya, a side street of Silom Rd., which caters for Japanese sex tourists, but this should not keep visitors away if you like these kinds of pubs.
