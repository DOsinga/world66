**Crocodile Farm**

The Crocodile Farm is located at Samut Prakarn Rd. If you like to see crocodiles, this place has more than 30,000 of them. There are shows and lots of interesting exhibits. Daily 7 am-6 pm. Admission: 300 baht. Phone 387-1166. 

**Snake Farm**

The Snake Farm, near the city center, is great fun for kids and adults alike. Run by the Red Cross as a center for collecting venom (to make antitoxin), you can watch as handlers milk poison from cobras, black mambas and other fearsome snakes. Daily shows 10:30 am and 2 pm.
