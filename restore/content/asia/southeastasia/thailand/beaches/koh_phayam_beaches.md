# Koh Phayam Beaches

*Asia > Southeastasia > Thailand > Beaches > Koh Phayam Beaches*

Koh Phayam island - kangaroo shape small Thailand island, located in North Andaman sea, near Ranong, Thailand and Port Victoria - Burma.
Koh Phayam island have white sand beaches, live coral reefs and become new travel destination in Thailand recently. Small roads. only for motorcycle or bicycle, no main electricity , just several small resorts and bungalows accommodation.
Two main beaches - Ao Kao Kwai - Buffalo Bay and Ao Yai - Long beach.
Even in high season - November- April, beaches not crowed and can always find place without people at all.
