# Kohjum Lodge

*Asia > Southeastasia > Thailand > Beaches > Kohjumlodge*

Koh Jum Lodge is located on the unspoiled Koh Jum Island between
Krabi and Koh Lanta. Sixteen cosy cottages in traditional Andaman style
are set in a natural coconut plantation and tropical gardens.
