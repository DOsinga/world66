# Thailand beaches on the Sunrise Coast

*Asia > Southeastasia > Thailand > Beaches > Thailand Beaches O*

Beaches on the Sunrise Coast of Thailand south of bangkok include Cha Am, Hua Hin and Khao Takiab, Pranburi, Dolphin Bay Beach and Sam Roi Yot beaches. Beaches in Baan Krut, Bang Saphan, Koh Talu Island and the beaches north of Chumphon. Beaches vary from very busy in Hua Hin to very quiet beaches. There are towns and villages along the coast. The highway and train line runs south along the area near the coast.
