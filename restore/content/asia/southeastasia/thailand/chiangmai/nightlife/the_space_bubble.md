# The Space Bubble

*Asia > Southeastasia > Thailand > Chiangmai > Nightlife > The Space Bubble*

The Space Bubble is a well known night club in Chiang Mai. It is a hotel disco and part of the Pornping Tower Hotel.

Every night there are many people sweating on the loud trance/techno beats that the in-house dj's are spinning on their turntables. Not only many tourists, but also lots of locals visit the Space Bubble, which makes it a nice place to learn more about the thai people....Also the pritty loud music keeps most of the older male sextourists away, so if you're looking for a good party, to take your mind of all the temples, head over to the Space Bubble.

Or, as the friendly people from the Pornping Tower Hotel put it: 'enjoys yourself with the music from the diskette, and has fun with your own style of dancing' -brilliant-

If you're on a budget trip and don't have much money to spend, the Space Bubble might be a bit expensive: the entry fee is 100Bath and a bottle of beer too. Also the dj's repeat their sets a couple of nights, they don't deliver a new set every night

After the Bubble closes, it might be a good idea to continue your party at the always crowded Spicy bar, where they play more hiphop and popular music.

Have fun in Chiang Mai!
