# Buddhist Weddings or any Weddings

*Asia > Southeastasia > Thailand > Chiangmai > Things To Do > Buddhist Weddings*

One of the most unique and romantic things to do in Chiang Mai is to get married. Yes I said get married!

You can bring the love of your life to chiang Mai and Thai the Knot. It is truly a spiritual and beautiful option to the boringly ordinary beach or theme weddings that most international resorts now offer.

Your wedding can be a authentic Thai Buddhist wedding or a Christian ceremony preformed by an English speaking Christian Minister, in a tropical garden paradise, the choice is yours.

Thailand weddings (locally known as Canna cards) has been offering weddings to western couples for over 5 years, as well as Thai couples for over 10 years, they are really Chiang Mai's first wedding boutique, opened to serve Thai couples. They have grown and now offer this unique service on their own private rural estate. They arrange the legalities too, so it is legal back home in the States or where ever you come from as well.
