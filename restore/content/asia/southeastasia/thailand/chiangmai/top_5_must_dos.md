Chiang Mai Attractions

Home to more than 300 temples, boundless natural beauty and mushrooming wildlife, Chiang Mai is a wonderful place to visit. There are plenty of tourist attractions which make the city a favorite for both domestic and foreign tourists.
A city that has something special for each tourist, Chiang Mai will simply charm you over with its diversity. The tourist attractions in Chiang Mai will appeal to you, irrespective of your area of interest. If you are a nature lover, there are a number of wildlife reserves that showcase the myriad forms of flora and fauna thriving in the region.

If you are interested in adventure, Chiang Mai has offers a choice of a number of activities like trekking, bird watching, mountain biking, bamboo rafting and so on. If you have come here to seek relaxation, then the spa resorts will make your holiday extremely special and refreshing.

There are numerous museums and archeological sites that are popular Chiang Mai tourist attractions. They provide an insight into the history and culture of the city. The temples are true relics of people's beliefs and faith. Most of these temples are built in accordance with Buddhism.

Wat Chedi Luang is one of the most popular Chiang Mai Tourist attractions. This temple complex houses a large Lanna-style stupa that dates back to 1441. Wat Chiang Man has a stone slab inscription engraved in the year 1581. There are two images of Lord Buddha, enclosed in a glass cabinet.
Doi Sutherp is a spectacular mountain range located in tourist. The Chiang Dao Caves are well-known tourist attractions, especially for adventurers. A set of five caves consist of age-old images of Buddha and are perfect for trekking and exploration.

Bo Sang Umbrella Village is a handicraft center where you can shop for different kinds of umbrellas. Hand made by local artistes, these umbrellas come in a variety of shapes, sizes and colors.
