# Doi Mae Salong

*Asia > Southeastasia > Thailand > Chiangrai > Aroundchiangrai > Doimaesalong*

Doi Mae Salong is the mountain village close to the Burmese border, can be reached by car. A number of hilltribes villages can be seen on the way and there is a superb panoramic view from summit.
