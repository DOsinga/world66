# Festivals

*Asia > Southeastasia > Thailand > Hatyai > Festivals*

**all**

**Sat Duan Sip Festival**

The festival of the Tenth Lunar Month. During this festival the Buddhists offer food to the Buddhist monks to get merit. Usually the festival lasts of a fortnight.

**Chinese Lunar Festival**

The typical festival does not differ very much from others in the country. The Thai-Chinese make offerings in gratidude for past and future fortune. Lion and dragon dances can be witnessed on the street then.
