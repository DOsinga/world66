# Sat Duan Sip Festival

*Asia > Southeastasia > Thailand > Hatyai > Festivals > Satduansipfestival*

The festival of the Tenth Lunar Month. During this festival the Buddhists offer food to the Buddhist monks to get merit. Usually the festival lasts of a fortnight.
