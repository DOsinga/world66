# Khao Takiab Bay Beach Hua Hin

*Asia > Southeastasia > Thailand > Huahin > Beaches > Khao Takiab Bay*

Khao Takiab Bay just past the Golden Buddha and Khao Takiab Mountain. This is a wide and long stretch of beach with firm sand and clear clean water, waves are just ripples so swimming is fine. You can also go for Elephant riding and watersports, also boat trips to islands off the Hua Hin coast and also fishing boat trips to Koh Singto Island. Along this Hua Hin beach you will find beach loungers and beach chairs, umbrellas, beach massage, beach food and drinks, cycling around is popular and bicycles can be rented. Just take the green pick-up bus from Hua hin Market to Khao Takiab and then follow the road to the right near the many temples and you will be in Khao Takiab Bay area.
