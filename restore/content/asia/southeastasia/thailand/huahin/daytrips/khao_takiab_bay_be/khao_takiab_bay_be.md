# Khao Takiab Bay Beach

*Asia > Southeastasia > Thailand > Huahin > Daytrips > Khao Takiab Bay Be > Khao Takiab Bay Be*

Located just 5 km south of the town center Khao Takiab Bay beach is a popular and relaxing beach area where you can also enjoy Elephant Rides, Horse riding, Horse and carriage rides, boat trips and fishing boat trips, All terrain off-road riding,seafood and beach restaurants, banana boats and other activities, this is know as the fun beach area especially at weekends.
