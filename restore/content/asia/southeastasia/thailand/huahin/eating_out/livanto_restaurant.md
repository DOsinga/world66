LIVANTO THAI-EUROPEAN RESTAURANT

Serving Thai and European fine family dining. Imported steaks from Australia
and New Zealand.High quality food and beverages at reasonable
prices.

Livanto also offers a romantic Cocktail Lounge and Piano bar on
the second floor. An experienced bartender can mix your favourite cocktail. A
wide choice of international wines can be selected from our cellar.

Round
off your evening with an Illy coffee, Ronnefelt tea and Premium imported New
Zealand Ice cream.

Our restaurant has inside and outside seating. We are
also equipped for larger groups . Wedding parties and small business events.
Lunch meetings can be set in our second floor cocktail lounge transferring it to
a conference lunch or dinner venue. A WiFi system ensures contact to the world
wide web.

Livanto is conveniently located in the centre of Hua Hin within
walking distance of the beach and shopping
areas
