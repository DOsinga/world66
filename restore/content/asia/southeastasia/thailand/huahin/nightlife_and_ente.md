# Nightlife and Entertainment

*Asia > Southeastasia > Thailand > Huahin > Nightlife And Ente*

Hua Hin offers releaxing nightlife mainly concentrated around excellent restaurants, local sidewalk cafe bars and a large number of lively bars where the drinks are cheap and the mood releaxed and friendly. There are seveal disco pubs, at the Hilton Hotel and close by at Star Planet at the City Beach Hotel. For Thai classic dance the Sasi restaurant near the Hyatt Regency Hotel is a must.

### Hua Hin Downtown

The central area of Hua Hin offers a large range of friendly bars where you can enjoy cheap drinks, easy company and nice music. Most are located in the sois ( streets ) near Naresdamri Road in the center of Hua hin
