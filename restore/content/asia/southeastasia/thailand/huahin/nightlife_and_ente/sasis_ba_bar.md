# Sasi's Ba Bar

*Asia > Southeastasia > Thailand > Huahin > Nightlife And Ente > Sasis Ba Bar*

Sasi's Ba Bar is the Pub and Restaurant. It offers Dinner from 6.00 p.m. and Live Music until 1.00 a.m. daily. Soft Rock, Ballad, Easy Listening and Latin are favorite here. There is a full bar serving Beers, Cocktails, and Wines. There are a variety of foods to choose from the menu. The popular ones are Rib Eye Steak (NZ Beef) with Pepper Sauce, Grilled Sausages Combination, and Deep Fried Pig Leg as in Germany.
