# Practical Information

*Asia > Southeastasia > Thailand > Huahin > Practical Informat*

Hua Hin is 180 kms south of Bangkok on the Coast of the Gulf of Thailand. Hua Hin is considored Thailand's oldest beach resort. 

How to get to Hua Hin: Express buses operate every 40 minutes from Bangkok to Hua Hin. Travel time 3 hrs 20 mins. Taxis and Limousine services from Bangkok to Hua Hin travel time about 2 hrs 30 mins. Trains operate frequenty from bangkok to Hua hin, about 4 hrs 20 mins. 

Shopping: Hua Hin has day and night markets, small shops, arts and crafts shops, clothing shops, luxury goods. 

Hua Hin Hotels: Hua Hin Hotels range from budget hotels, mid range hotels, luxury hotels and resorts. Hotels in Hua Hin are in the town center or along the beaches. Hua Hin also has many luxury resorts many with Spas. There are also Guesthouses and Bed and Breakfast accommodations in Hua Hin. 

Climate and weather: Hua Hin is a year round destinations with no extremes of temperature, rainfall is very low, most of the year is sunny and dry, rainfall does occur between October and December but this is usually for a short time only. The climate is very mild with a cooling sea breeze.
