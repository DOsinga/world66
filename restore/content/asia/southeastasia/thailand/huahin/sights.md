**Sights**

**Khao Takiab, Khao Krailat**

These two hills are only 4 kilometers from Hua Hin township. A local bus service from Hua Hin to Khao Takiab is located on the sea shore It is convenient to get to the top of Khao Takiab where it is cool and shady and one can view local scenery. 

**Sea Pine Tree Garden**

The Sea Pine Garden is situated eight kilometers from Hua Hin. A left turn is made at Km. 240 for a distance a 500 meters. The original name of this garden was "Pradiphat Garden Convalescence and RelaxationThere Area" and is taken care of by Army Welfare Office. **Khao Tao **

This hill is located in the area of Amphoe Hua Hin Office. Between Km. 243-244 there is a one kilometer branch road on the left which leads to the spot. There are two beautiful beaches called Sai No Beach and Sai Yai Beach.    

**Panee Butterfly Garden**

The Panee Butterfly Garden, with a vast collection of butterflies against a natural backdrop of tranquil grounds, is the newest attraction in Hua Hin. For more information, tel/fax +66-32-512642. 

openinghours: 10 am - 6 pm, daily

**Night Market**

Hua Hin Night market opens around 4pm till midnight, great food, and lots of bargains. The market represents a genuine Thai style of life. You can find local food, fruits, drinks, souvenirs or even clothes. 

**Klai Kangwon Palace**

King Phra Plokklao built it in the year 1926. The palace is located 3 kilometers from Amphoe Hua Hin Office. A permit must be obtained from the Royal Household Office prior to a visit.
