# Khao Takiab, Khao Krailat

*Asia > Southeastasia > Thailand > Huahin > Sights > Khaotakiabkhaokrailat*

These two hills are only 4 kilometers from Hua Hin township. A local bus service from Hua Hin to Khao Takiab is located on the sea shore It is convenient to get to the top of Khao Takiab where it is cool and shady and one can view local scenery. Khao Tabiab Bay is thought by many to be the best beach in the area being very wide and long with shallow swimming and clear seas.
