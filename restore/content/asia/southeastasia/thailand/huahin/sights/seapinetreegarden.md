# Sea Pine Tree Garden

*Asia > Southeastasia > Thailand > Huahin > Sights > Seapinetreegarden*

The Sea Pine Garden is situated eight kilometers from Hua Hin. A left turn is made at Km. 240 for a distance a 500 meters. The original name of this garden was "Pradiphat Garden Convalescence and RelaxationThere Area" and is taken care of by Army Welfare Office. There are food shops and accommodation for the public.
