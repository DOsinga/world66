# Jomtien Beach

*Asia > Southeastasia > Thailand > Jomtien Beach*

Jomtien Beach is a popular beach location 10 km beyond Pattaya. Popular for vacationers and weekend visitors. Jomtien beach offers beach, watersports, shopping and restaurants. Local excursions include trips to Koh Larn island and other locations along the coast. Pattaya is 10 minutes away.
