# Full Moon Party

*Asia > Southeastasia > Thailand > Kohsamui > Activities > Edd*

Once a month and also half moon parties now on a near by Island constant boats on the night, one of the biggest beach parties in Thailand and by now quite legendary. But beware use only lisenced boats and also don't buy drugs at the party as many tourists have found out quite often undercover police.
