# Bluestars Kayaking & Snorkeling Day Trip

*Asia > Southeastasia > Thailand > Kohsamui > Day Trips > Bluestars Kayaking 1*

[**Bluestars Kayaking & Snorkeling**](/asia/southeastasia/thailand/kohsamui/day_trips)

**Blue Stars Kayaking tours** will take you from **Koh Samui** to Angthong Marine Park which became famous by the book and the movie **"THE BEACH"**. The **kayaking & snorkeling tour** begins with a pickup service from any Hotel on **Koh Samui**, between 07:15 – 07:30 in the morning.

As soon as our tour boat has arrived to **Angthong**, it's time for the action! Start **kayaking** and experience the beautiful landscape while kayaking along the southern cost of “Ko Mae Ko” island until you stop on a beach for your walk up for **sightseeing** to the "Green Lagoon" or “Emerald Lake”, a hidden saltwater lake inside of “Ko Mae Ko”.

After this first impression of kayaking and sightseeing it is time for a delicious lunch buffet. Selected delicious Thai dishes
will be available at the lunch buffet on our ****tour boat. While enjoying your lunch, the boat cruises
through the breathtaking scenery from “Ko Mae Ko” to the northern
part of **Angthong Marine Park** with “Ko Tai Plao” as your destination for the second **kayaking adventure** and the snorkeling experience.

To find out more about the kayaking and snorkeling day tour feel free to browse our website at: www.bluestars.info
