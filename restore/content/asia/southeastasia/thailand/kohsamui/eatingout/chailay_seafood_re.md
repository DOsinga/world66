# Chailay Seafood Restaurant & Beach Bar

*Asia > Southeastasia > Thailand > Kohsamui > Eatingout > Chailay Seafood Re*

***Authentic Thai food at authentic Thai prices served in a stunning
location.***

Located directly
on the famous Big Buddha Beach, on the tropical tourist
destination island of
Koh Samui, Chailay Seafood Restaurant & Bar enjoys stunning views over Big
Buddha Bay and the islands of Koh Som & Koh Phangan, as well as experiencing
the most glorious of sunsets. Get in early for a beachside seat and relax with a
cooling drink as the sun goes down!

Visitors and locals alike are
welcome to come and sample Thai &
European dishes in our relaxed
restaurant on the beach front.

Our Beach Bar is now open for those who
are not hungry but want to experience the views with a drink in hand, enjoy good
company and listen to a varied genre of music to suit the mood. Please feel free
to pop in to say hello and relax, especially if you are on your own.

Free Wi-Fi Internet access is available to all customers with our dedicated
2Mb connection so you can surf the net with your own laptop while sunning
yourself on the beach! We also have free pool, bar games, big screen TV (solely
for major sports events such as football, cricket, tennis) and friendly, fun
staff who will do their best to ensure you enjoy your time with us.

See our ** Live Music**
section for details of our regular Beach Bar party nights!!!
