# Tham Lot

*Asia > Southeastasia > Thailand > Maehongson > Excursions > Thamlot*

A large limestone cave with a wide stream running through it. It is located eight km north of [Soppong](/asia/southeastasia/thailand/maehongson/soppong). It is one of the longest caves in Southeast Asia. You can book a tour at Mae Hong Son at probably every tour operator.
