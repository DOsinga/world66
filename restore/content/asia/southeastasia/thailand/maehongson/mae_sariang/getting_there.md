# Getting There in Mae Sariang

*Asia > Southeastasia > Thailand > Maehongson > Mae Sariang > Getting There*

**From Chiang Mai:**

Public buses depart daily from Arcade bus station. Approx 4.5 hr journey time. Fan buses depart; 8.00, 13.30, 15.00, 20.00, 21.00. Air con buses depart; 11.00, 21.00

**From Mae Hong Son:**

Public buses depart daily from Mae Hong Son bus station. Approx 4 hours journey time. Fan buses depart; 06.00, 08.00, 12.30, 14.00, 20.00 Air con buses depart; 10.30, 21.00

**From Bangkok:**

Take the overnight bus to Mae Hong Son which stops off at Mae Sariang. Approx 12 hrs journey time. Air con (VIP) buses depart 15.00 and 18.00. Fan buses depart 20.45
