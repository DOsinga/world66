**Keangsopa Waterfalls**

The waterfalls are located 72 km out of town and are recommended as the best in the region. The falls signposted in English, are two km south of Hwy. 12 - just follow the English signs.  

**Tung Saleng Luang National Park**

Located 80 km west of Phitsanulok is a 1,200-square-km park covered by wooded mountains with a variety of flora and fauna. Accommodations in park bungalows can be booked in advance from the Forestry Department (tel. 02-579-0529) in Bangkok
