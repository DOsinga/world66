**Wat Mahathat**

The most important thing to see is the Jinaraj Buddha inside Wat Mahathat (Wat Yai). The wat was erected in 1482 by King Boromatrailokanat of Ayuthaya as the symbol of his domination over Sukhothai and Phitsanulok.
