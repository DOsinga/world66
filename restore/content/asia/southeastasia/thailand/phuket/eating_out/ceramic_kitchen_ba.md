# Ceramic Kitchen Bar and Restaurant

*Asia > Southeastasia > Thailand > Phuket > Eating Out > Ceramic Kitchen Ba*

Enjoy a unique dining experience in the cosy atmosphere of a clay house and ceramic art gallery, while sipping on your choice of a selection of wines from our international bar, which also offers an experience in local Thai rums. Savour our large variety of fusion cuisine, such as our tasty smoked duck and (ANOTHER DISH), which is also available for lunch. For those looking for a relaxing break from a busy day, afternoon coffee and cakes are also available in the courtyard. -Call 076 272 151-2 Open : 13:00 - 24:00 Western and Thai food

This place is apparently pretty new, but had a terrific range of dishes, both Thai and European, all at reasonable prices. Nice surroundings, a good singer and friendly staff. Great food - we tried the duck, roast pork and several other dishes, and all were the sort you would travel a long way to get again. Well worth the journey.
