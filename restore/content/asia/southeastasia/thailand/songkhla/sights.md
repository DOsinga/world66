**National Museum**

The National Museum is located between Rong Meung and Chana Roads. The building is of southern Sino-Portuguese architecture and is over 100 years old. The museum has an excellent collection of exhibits from all over Thailand. **Wat Matchimawat**

Wat Matchimawat is situated on Saiburi Rd. It is the largest temple in Songkhla and over 400 years old. Inside the bot are some fine frescoes depicting the history of Songkhla and Buddhist philosophy. **Wat Chai Mongkhon**

Wat Chai Mongkhon is located on the corner of Petch Mongkhon and Chai Mongkhon Roads. A pagoda enshrines a highly revered Buddha bone relic that was brought to the temple by a Buddhist monk called, Na Issaro. The monk visited Sri Lanka in 1892 and was presented the relic by a rich merchant.
