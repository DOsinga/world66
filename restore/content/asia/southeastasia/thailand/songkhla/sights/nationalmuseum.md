# National Museum

*Asia > Southeastasia > Thailand > Songkhla > Sights > Nationalmuseum*

The National Museum is located between Rong Meung and Chana Roads. The building is of southern Sino-Portuguese architecture and is over 100 years old. The museum has an excellent collection of exhibits from all over Thailand. Amongst the displays are many artefacts from the Srivajayan period, as well as prehistoric finds. 

address: Rongmuang Road

openinghours: Open from 9 am - 12 am and 1 pm - 4 pm. Closed on Mondays, Tuesdays and Public Holidays.
