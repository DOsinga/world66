Koh Chang, Thailand is so much more than just White Sand, Turquoise Sea and Unbroken Blue Sky. There are many fun and exciting things to do and see while you are visiting Koh Chang. While some activities you can plan on your own, others are more convenient and cost effective if you book one of the Organised Tours.

We have selected some of Koh Chang’s more popular Tours including Snorkeling and Diving, Elephant Trekking and Jungle Walks. Deep Sea Fishing, Squid Fishing, ATV Tours and even a tree top adventure park are all available to book online

Booking your Tour on i-sitekohchang.com is easy. Scroll through the Koh Chang Tour listings. Click on the Tour image to see the full details of each Tour available.

i-site Koh Chang also specialise in organizing Holiday Accommodation and Short Breaks to the Tropical Island of Koh Chang. We also offer Transfers to Koh Chang as well as Transport around Thailand and to some of the more popular destinations in Cambodia.
