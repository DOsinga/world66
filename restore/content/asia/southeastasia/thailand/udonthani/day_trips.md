**Ban Chiang**

Ban Chiang is a tiny village 50 kilometres east of Udon Thani and best visited as a day trip from Udon.  Ban Chiang is the site of a major archeological excavation and the home of the distinctive swirl-design pots which you see all around Udon Thani.
