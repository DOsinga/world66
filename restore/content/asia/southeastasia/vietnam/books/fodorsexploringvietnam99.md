# Fodor's Exploring Vietnam '99

*Asia > Southeastasia > Vietnam > Books > Fodorsexploringvietnam99*

The difficulty any guide publisher has with VietNam is keeping up with the pace of change - which is truly remarkable. Rated as a developing country, VietNam has the vigour and drive of a developed nation.

Whilst the contents of the Fodor's VietNam guide is good, it is dated and therefore its utility reduced.

Check the available publications out at your local library then buy the most suitable for your need.
