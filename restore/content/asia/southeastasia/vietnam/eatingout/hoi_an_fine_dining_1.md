# HOI AN FINE DINING RESTAURANT

*Asia > Southeastasia > Vietnam > Eatingout > Hoi An Fine Dining 1*

“ A royal cuisine house , a place to enjoy the imperial dishes “
Coming to HOI AN Restaurant , travelers are feeling to be lost in a quiet and ancient space.The images of the ancient town is shown from a nice wooden screen, sophisticatedly carved tables and chairs and even the silk lantern or the embroidered pictures on the wall.The high wooden ceilings with exquisite interior, bringing the rustic features to HOI AN restaurant.

Not only that, HOI AN Restaurant dishes have been appreciated highly that no place can be compared with by domestic and overseas dinners. In addition to the five star standard service, the flavor in each dish has created a good reputation to Hoi An Restaurant to make the tourists go from this surprise to the other surprise,due to create each dish is a passion that the cook would like to bring to the customers.

Some famous dishes from Hoi An Restaurant: Hoi An spring rolls, raw fish salad in Hoi An style, Cao Lau, shrimp and pork pancake, fired shrimp thin cakes,grilled chicken with pineapple leaf.... and the specific dishes from 3 regions of the country.
