HALONG 1 DAY

AM: 8.00. We pick up you at our office and depart for Halong.
Have a short break in Hai duong city ( 20 minutes ). Arrive in Halong around
11:30. Get on boat and have lunch on boat. After lunch, take the cruise to
visit Halong bay (4 hours). You have chance to take many nice photoes about the
Halong bay’s scenery. Visit Thien Cung and Dau Go caves – the most beautiful
cave in Halong bay with the individual shapes created by splendid stalactites
and stalagmites. Continue boat trip to enjoy swimming and sunbathing. Go cruise
back to Halong harbor. Return to Hanoi.
Arrive in Hanoi
around 7.00Pm. End of services.

Services include: Air conditional bus, english speaking tourguide ,
boat trip, lunch and visiting fees.

Exclude: Drink, Insurance and Personal Expenses
