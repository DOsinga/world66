*Asia > Southeastasia > Vietnam > Hanoi > Eating Out > Luna Dautunno Han*

Luna d’autunno Hanoi is an Italian restaurant that offers flavorful and genuine Italian food.

Established in 1999, by a group of Italians is now a part of a larger
group of Italian Restaurants all located in South-East Asia.

The restaurant is located in Dien Bien Phu Street; number 11B the
street that starting from the western part of the inner city reaches
the Ho Chi Minh Mausoleum.
