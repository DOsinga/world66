# Nightlife and Entertainment

*Asia > Southeastasia > Vietnam > Hanoi > Nightlife And Ente*

The Rose hotel is a charming family run 10 Rooms balcony establishment in the heart of HaNoi Oldquarter. The rooms come with all the amenities you would expect and some you wouldn't such as in room computer with internet access (ADSL) and balconies. They are tastefully furnished, full of light and fresh roses add warmth to the rooms. Local fruit, complimentary breakfast, and helpful service set this hotel apart from the rest. We also provide an alternative to the large tour companies and can arrange a variety of local experiences you won't forget.

A short 2 minute walk to the famous Hoan Kiem Lake takes you past many colorful silk shops and local arts and crafts. We are also located across the street from one of Hanoi's most famous Bun Cha restaurants. You won't be disappointed. Let our family be a part of your Hanoi experience.
