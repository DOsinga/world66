# My Son

*Asia > Southeastasia > Vietnam > Myson*

**My Son** is the former Cham (ethnic minority) capital. The town is found at the end of a bumpy road through tropical jungle. The beautifully carved stone sculptures and the ruins of old buildings reveal mysteries up to 1500 years old. My Son is considered to be Champa's counterpart to the grand cities of South-East Asia's other Indian-influenced civilisations: [Angkor wat](/asia/southeastasia/cambodia/angkorwat) in Cambodia, Pagan in Myanmar, Ayuthaya in Thailand and [Borobudur](/asia/southeastasia/indonesia/java/borobudur) on Java/Indonesia.
