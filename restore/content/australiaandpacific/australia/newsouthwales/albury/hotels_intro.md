**Albury hotels **offer comfort, quality and great value, and the accommodations are ideal for both leisure and business travelers. Here are some Albury Hotels.

**First Floor Luxury Service Apartments**

Providing no frills service for travelers who are looking to move around the city without the fancies of a hotel, the First Floor Luxury Service Apartments are the place to go. Good for those with families or couples looking for a home-like environment, the service apartments are a fully furnished place, generally with two bedrooms. For those visiting the city with a group of friends, the apartments may be a good option. The spacious living rooms and dining areas, and fully equipped kitchens, are great for throwing house parties or having gatherings. The apartments are conveniently located near the shops and supermarkets, as well as sightseeing spots. Apartment Rates start from AUD$120 to AUD$300 a night.

**The Hume Inn Motel **

Providing the best value-for-money deals for backpackers and interstate travelers, the Hume Inn Motel has been an establishment in Albury for some time. Tourists looking for something on a lower price scale can always depend on the Hume Inn. The Inn itself is family run, and is a 44 room little compound that provides a somewhat free and easy service for guests. Upon request, transportation from the airport can be arranged, as can be anything from 24 hour check in and extra bedding, to requests for air conditioned rooms and late night meals. A laundry room for guests can be found at the back of the motel, next to the outdoor swimming pool and BBQ pits. Average Rates at The Hume Inn Motel** ** cost AUD$80 a day.
