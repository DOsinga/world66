# Katoomba

*Australiaandpacific > Australia > Newsouthwales > Katoomba*

Katoomba is the small town next to the Blue Mountains, home of The Three Sisters and Echo Point. The town is a small, but very art deco style tourist town. Some stay for just a day, but you'll have plenty to do if you decide to stay longer.
