# Intercity train

*Australiaandpacific > Australia > Newsouthwales > Katoomba > Getting There > Intercity Train*

You can catch an intercity train from Sydney, which will take around 2 hours and will cost around 14 AUD. Check timetables in advance as they do not run as often as local trains.
