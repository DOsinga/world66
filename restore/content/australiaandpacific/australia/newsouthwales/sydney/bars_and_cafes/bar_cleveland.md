# Bar Cleveland

*Australiaandpacific > Australia > Newsouthwales > Sydney > Bars And Cafes > Bar Cleveland*

This heritage listed building dates back to 1915 when it was known as
"The Cleveland Inn" and a traditional 'locals' pub. Situated on the
corner of Bourke and busy Cleveland Street, on the fringe of Surry
Hills and Redfern, Bar Cleveland offers a unique casual atmosphere as
well as the Level 1 Cocktail Bar and Function Room .

Located close to the Entertainment Quarter, SCG and Aussie Stadium, Bar
Cleveland is the perfect meeting place before or after a game. Also,
with the Crown St Restaurants and Oxford St clubs nearby, why not kick
start your night here or have that late nighcap - whatever the occasion
we have something to suit your tastes.
