# Chinese Noodle Restaurant

*Australiaandpacific > Australia > Newsouthwales > Sydney > Eatingout > Chinese Noodle Res*

This is a tiny, cheap hand-pulled noodle place with fantastic food. It's been featured in Cheap Eats and the Good Food Guide a few times and is always busy. It's open every day from 10am - 11pm, I think! You sit at tiny tables and eat freshly cooked food in Northern Chinese (quite different to normal Chinese restaurant menus). The bill for two dishes is usually around $20 or less and depending on what you order, you may leave with a box of leftovers to eat later. Very yummt food, I recommend the vegetarian fried noodles and the eggplant, potato and capsicum stirfry (which has converted a number of non-eggplant eaters to eggplant-lovers!)
