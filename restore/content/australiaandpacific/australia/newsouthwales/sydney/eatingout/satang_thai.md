# Satang Thai

*Australiaandpacific > Australia > Newsouthwales > Sydney > Eatingout > Satang Thai*

A very small and cute place to eat out. It only has some 4 or 5 tables and many people take their food out with them. Very delicious thai food! Prices vary from 7 to 10 AUD.
