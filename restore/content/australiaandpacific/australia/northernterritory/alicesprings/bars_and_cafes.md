# Bars and Cafes in Alice Springs

*Australiaandpacific > Australia > Northernterritory > Alicesprings > Bars And Cafes*

Before going to Alice Springs it can be very helpful in finding out about the various **Alice Springs Bars and Cafes**. Knowing what the town has to offer in terms of bars and cafes can help you decide where to go when you want to grab a quick bite to eat or when you want a nice cold beer.

**Bojangles Saloon & Restaurant
**
If you are interested in trying out some of the local dishes, such as Camel Kangaroo or even Emu this is one of the best places to go. If you are not interested in a full meal with your drinks Bojanlges Saloon & Restaurant offers you a full menu of light snacks, such as nachos or sandwiches.

**Bar Doppio**

This caf&eacute; has been in town for over 13 years ad it is still serving some of the best food in town. Bar Doppio is open seven days a week for both breakfast and lunch, but are well known for the amazing coffee and teas that they serve. If coming in for a cup of coffee you will be able to select your preferred roast. They also serve muffins and biscuits, along with cakes that go great with a hot cup of coffee or tea.

**Balloons Bistro
**
This is not your run of the mill bar, despite the easy and casual manner of the place. The Balloons Bistro will provide you with some amazing service, all while enjoying the view of the pool and the gardens of the Crowne Plaza. The Balloons Bistro serves both a full American breakfast for the more picky eaters among the group, but you will also be offered the choice of a continental breakfast.

**Coolamon Caf&eacute;
**
This quaint little caf&eacute; is located within Desert Park and is open each day from 9 a.m. to 5 p.m. One of the best things about this caf&eacute; is that even though you are eating from a self-served buffet you are still getting that authentic caf&eacute; dining experience because of the quality and choices of foods.

**The Lane Caf&eacute; and Restaurant
**
If you are in the mood for a typical French Caf&eacute; this is the place to go. The Lane Caf&eacute; serves your typical French bistro food, but it is of the highest quality in all of Alice Springs. If you stop in for lunch be sure to grab a gourmet pizza that has been wood-fired, it is more than enough to share with your friends.
