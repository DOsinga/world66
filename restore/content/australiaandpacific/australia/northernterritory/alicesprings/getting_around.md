# Getting Around in Alice Springs

*Australiaandpacific > Australia > Northernterritory > Alicesprings > Getting Around*

There are a number of ways to travel in the area of Alice Springs, and you can find the **Alice Springs transportation** that is best for you. Some of the best ways to get around in the city include bus tours, trains, and rental cars.

#### Bus Tours

Bus tours are one of the best ways to get around in Alice Springs because you will be able to go to all of the best places to see and enjoy the tour without having to worry about navigating for yourself. There are different tours available, including full-day tours, sightseeing tours, safari tours, and city tours to some of the attractions in the area. You can find that they are affordable and easy to use.

#### Train

Taking the train is a fun way to experience Alice Springs. It is one of the Alice Springs transportation modes that you can take to see the sights in the city, and also get to tour around some of the outback and the country. You can find the train station in town and hop on board to go to another city near Alice Springs, or relax and enjoy the country to spot wildlife and the desert areas. There are many trains that depart the city on Fridays and Saturdays to choose from, and also on other days of the week if you are going to be taking a longer ride.

#### Rental Cars

Rental cars are great if you want to get out and explore the country on your own. You can find the rates to be affordable, but you may have to pay more for mileage if you travel longer distances. You can stay in town and see the activities and sights, or you can take your rental out to the outback to see some of the country and views. There are maps available to help you get around in the city and in the area when you are driving. Rental cars are one of the popular Alice Springs transportation methods that people choose if they want the freedom to see what they want on vacation.

#### Taxis

You can find taxis that can take you anywhere in the city that you want to see. They are available for round trips to go to your hotel. You can also take them one-way if you are going to be taking a train ride or going on a bus tour. You can find that the taxi fares are affordable and can be a good choice if you are not familiar with the area.
