# Hotels Intro in Mooloolaba

*Australiaandpacific > Australia > Queensland > Mooloolaba > Hotels Intro*

This list of **Mooloolaba Hotels** will supply you with a good idea of the hotels and accommodations in Mooloolaba, Sunshine Coast in Queensland. This beach-side town offers a wide selection of hotels, from affordable options to luxurious ones, which are all well-maintained and reasonably priced. The beach-front location of these accommodations make them an ideal place to enjoy Mooloolaba's temperate climate.

**Osprey Apartments**

One of the most popular accommodations in Queensland's Sunshine Coast is the Osprey Apartments. The casual, spacious apartments of Osprey allow you to catch fantastic views of the ocean and garden from their east- and west-facing balconies. For an average price of $141 per night, you can already choose which view you want to see as you wake up. In addition to this, you can also opt for the penthouse apartments that have a private access to the rooftop as well as the use of their outdoor furniture, hot tub and BBQ facilities.

**Peninsular Beachfront Resort**

You can experience Mooloolaba with one of the 65 modern spacious apartments and penthouses provided by the Peninsular Beachfront Resort. These fully self-contained bedrooms give you the opportunity to witness the beauty of the Mooloolaba Beach and Bay. Aside from their amazing ocean views, you can also enjoy a broadband connection right within your room. Most importantly, you can enjoy a swim on the famous Mooloolaba Beach, which is only 25 steps away from the resort. All of this for an average price of $346 per night.

**Mooloolaba Beach Backpackers**

The Mooloolaba Beach Backpackers is a modern hostel situated right in the heart of the Sunshine Coast cosmopolitan. If you are more of a budget traveler, you can choose to stay in a dormitory shared by four people or the more private double rooms. The affordable accommodation that the Mooloolaba Beach Backpackers offers aim to aid travelers to enjoy their stay in the Sunshine Coast without necessarily spending too much. The average price per night is $43, inclusive of an Internet connection and fully-equipped floors.

**Raffles Mooloolaba**

This four-and-a-half star holiday apartment is located at the heart of Mooloolaba. The location of Raffles Mooloolaba makes it accessible to the famous tourist spots in Sunshine Coast. You may find yourself only less than five minutes away from the Underwater World and other necessary establishments, such as restaurants, boutiques and coffee shops. Deluxe two-bedrooms cost $490 for two nights.

**Frangipani House**

If you want to have a more private time, away from all the busy tourist spots in Mooloolaba, then you can opt for this exotic country guest house. Located in the Mooloolah Valley, you can appreciate nature more with its well-maintained gardens. The guest house has various amenities such as pools, spas and even an outdoor wood-fired pizza oven. You can stay at the guest house for two nights for a minimum of $600.
