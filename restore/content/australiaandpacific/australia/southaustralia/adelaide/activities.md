# Activities

*Australiaandpacific > Australia > Southaustralia > Adelaide > Activities*

Plenty of things to do in and around Adelaide. If you have already seen the wine region and the wildlife parks, then it might be a nice idea to go to the Adelaide Aquatic Centre. Whatever sort of water or leisure activities you’re looking for, you can most definitely exercise them in the Aquatic Centre. Or when you feel like taking a swim in the sea, take the Glenelg tram to one of the nearby beaches.
