# Sagarmatha Nepali Restaurant

Sagarmatha Nepali Restaurant is the first authentic nepalese restaurnt
in Adelaide, Australia. It serves authentic nepalese cuisine as well as
some popular south asian regional dishes. It has won many awards since
its establishment in 2000. The purpose of this restaurant is to promote
Nepalese culture through authentic nepale cuisine and
other cultural activities.
