# Shopping in Adelaide

*Australiaandpacific > Australia > Southaustralia > Adelaide > Shopping*

**Adelaide Shopping** comes as a superb treat to visitors in the fifth largest city in South Australia, Adelaide. This is because there are several large shopping centers within the city, which offer everything a shopper needs: beauty and health shops, entertainment, special services, department stores, finance centers and food outlets. Here are a few recommendations for you, places where you can shop till you drop.

**Rundle Mall**

Named after John Rundle, a famous businessman, Rundle Mall is the premier shopping center in Adelaide. A one-stop-mall provides almost anything for an ardent shopper. Its more than 500 retail stores offer various shopping delights like opal gemstones, clothes, bags, accessories, books, antiques, wines, and even fresh fruits and flowers. Aside from the arcades, and department stores, there are also cafes and restaurants which serve both local and foreign dishes.

**Harbour Town**

This is a veritable haven for shoppers who want to purchase top quality brands directly and at lesser prices than their usual retail prices. The array of highly branded product outlets provides an affordable shopping experience for tourists and locals alike. Tourists can take advantage of the Tourism Club, which provides special discounts and offers, like complimentary refreshments, safe comfortable lounge area, and taxi and luggage services.

**Old Port Canal Shopping Center**

Found on Commercial Road, this shopping center provides various services namely, finance services like ATMs, banks, and foreign exchange centers. There are also food outlets, shoe repair shops, and dry cleaning outlets. You can opt to shop in entertainment and travel shops or buy gorgeous items from health and beauty shops.

**Westfield Tea Tree Plaza**

Housing about 240 shops selling women&rsquo;s, men&rsquo;s and children&rsquo;s apparel and paraphernalia, Westfield offers guests the comfort of shopping and touring the mall&rsquo;s various shops. Tourists have free, ample parking spaces, wheelchair and scooter services, and rooms for babies and mothers who want to clean-up. It is one of the best malls in Adelaide because it has something for everyone.

**West Lakes Mall**

Strategically located near the AAMI Football Stadium, this classy West Lakes Mall is home to the well-known international food court, the specialty shops, K-mart, and Woolworths products. The &ldquo;Shop til&rsquo; You Drop&ldquo; slogan applies to this huge shopping mall. Spend your day shopping and touring the grand mall with its interesting antique shops and exotic stores.
