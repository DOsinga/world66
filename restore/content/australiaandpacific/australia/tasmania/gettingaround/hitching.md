Unfortunately, I must urge you **NOT** to hitch rides in Tasmania. In recent years several tourists have disappeared along the East coast, and their cases have not been solved. While Tasmania is generally a safe destination, I post this warning to illustrate that you should generally never travel alone and not accept rides from strangers. In fact I would cast this warning to all of Australia. A particularly famous case in recent years have been the Berlangalo murders, in which a serial killer picked up hitch-hikers on seven separate occasions - couples as well as singles - and drove them to an isolated state forest to murder them.
