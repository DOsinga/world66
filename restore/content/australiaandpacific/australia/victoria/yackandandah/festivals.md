Spring Migration Festival Yackandandah is an annual event on the Yackandandah Calendar. This festival is Gay and Lesbian orientated but people from all walks of life are welcome to attend regardless of sexual orientation.. hey you don't need to be gay to have fun. So come along and join in the festivities. This year our third festival will be a bumper weekend of fabulous fun with a special cocktail party, meet and greet night with drag comedy and dancing. there's lots more to do. please visit our website. springmigration . com . au (this event is supported by Tourism Victoria, Outback Calling (an Australian Federal government initiative along with our many other sponsors and supporters) Thank you all and please come and join us this year. Andy Stevens & Gary Hayward (organisers)
