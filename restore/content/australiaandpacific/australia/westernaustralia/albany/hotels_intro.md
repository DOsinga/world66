# Hotels Intro in Albany

*Australiaandpacific > Australia > Westernaustralia > Albany > Hotels Intro*

**Albany hotels** offer comfortable rooms, equipped with the amenities and special services that make tourists&rsquo; stay comfortable and memorable. Albany is a perfect getaway for people who want to experience the beauty of nature and the outdoors. The hotels usually organize a variety of exciting adventures like whale watching, wine tours, wildflower tours, waterfront activities, and the exploration of historic places.

**The Rocks Albany**

Once home to Albany's state governors, this historic hotel offers luxurious and elegant accommodations to visitors. Most of the rooms have a panoramic view of the Princes Royal Harbor and the surrounding beautiful gardens. Sports buffs can play in the tennis court and music lovers can play the grand piano in the music room. Guests can spend quiet evenings at the hotel&rsquo;s gardens or the sunset room reading a book or relaxing. The romantic surroundings make it an ideal place for honeymooners. The room rates are from $355 to $419 per night.

**Park Avenue Holiday Units**

The hotel units are clean and comfortable. If you prefer simplicity and adequacy primarily, then this is a good place to stay. The hotel provides excellent kitchen and barbecue utensils which make the activity fun and enjoyable. It is a quiet and peaceful place to stay. The room rates during off season are from $100 to $160 and during peak seasons, $145 to $190 a night.

**Metro Inn Albany**

About six miles from Albany airport, the Inn is excellent for visitors who want a quick tour to the city&rsquo;s wildlife and marine life. It is ideal for all types of visitors because of the fully furnished rooms, the children&rsquo;s playground, an indoor swimming pool, and the promise of a wonderful outdoor adventure. An in-house a-la-carte restaurant, the La Fontana provides good food while a bar offers good wine to guests who want to enjoy the evening. The room rates range from $84 to $120 depending on the size.

**Albany Gardens Holiday Resort**

This hotel resort provides a wide choice between deluxe, chalets, and holiday units. All air-conditioned rooms are comfortable and fully furnished. Guests could cook barbecue in the hotel&rsquo;s spacious outdoor barbecue area or take advantage of their camp kitchen. Its proximity to the playground, the animal sanctuary, the beautiful garden, Whale World and various nature tripping activities makes it a good place for tourists who want to optimize their time within the city. The room rates are from $100 to $160 per night.

**Emu Point Motel**

For tourists who want the fresh, brackish, sea air on their faces, this beachfront motel is the answer. Situated within a stone&rsquo;s throw away from Emu Point Beach, Emu point provides comfortable and nicely furnished rooms. Each room has the following amenities: refrigerator, direct dial phone, radio, hair dryer and an Internet access. Room rates range from $66 to $83 a night.
