# Beaches

*Australiaandpacific > Australia > Westernaustralia > Beaches*

There are some excellent beaches to explore in WA. Probably the best-known beaches are located in the Margaret River area. This does not imply that one can surf on the river because Margaret River is located in the vicinity of Bunbury and has fine beaches for swimming and surfing. It isn’t without reason that some of the beaches have names such as ‘Suicides’, so be prepared. Rottnest Island, just off the coast of Fremantle also offers some excellent surfing possibilities.
