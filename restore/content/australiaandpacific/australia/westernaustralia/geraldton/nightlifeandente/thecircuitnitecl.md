# The Nitey

*Australiaandpacific > Australia > Westernaustralia > Geraldton > Nightlifeandente > Thecircuitnitecl*

The newly renamed Nitey is open from Thursday to Saturday and features three seperate areas including Pelsaerts Lounge, a sportsmans bar and the main nightclub/disco area.

For patrons comfort, the middle sports bar and Pelsaerts Lounge are now smoke free areas.

The Nitey offers a big dancefloor, , room to move around and a great party atmosphere.

Combining the great music, the relaxed atmposhere, Geraldton's finest up and coming DJ; DJ Yager, good-time atmosphere, you will never want to leave.

The Nitey is within walking distance from most of the pubs and bars in [Geraldton](/australiaandpacific/australia/westernaustralia/geraldton) and are they are open til late on most nights
