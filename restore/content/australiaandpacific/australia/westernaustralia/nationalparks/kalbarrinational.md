# Kalbarri National Park

*Australiaandpacific > Australia > Westernaustralia > Nationalparks > Kalbarrinational*

[Kalbarri](/australiaandpacific/australia/westernaustralia/kalbarri) National Park is situated 550 kilometres North of
[Perth](/australiaandpacific/australia/westernaustralia/perth) and boast a wide range of natural landscapes from rolling coastal and Mallee plains to deep river gorges and cliff faces, as well as sandy beaches and slow, winding rivers.
