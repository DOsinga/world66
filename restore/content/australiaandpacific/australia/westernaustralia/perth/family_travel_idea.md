# Family Travel Ideas

*Australiaandpacific > Australia > Westernaustralia > Perth > Family Travel Idea*

Perth has many things that can be done with the family in mind.
There are attractions that will appeal to both young and old in this
city.

**Kings Park**

Perth is the home of some wonderful parks. Kings Park has the
Lotterwest Family Area with playground equipment especially for kids
under six years old. For the parents there is a café.Older children will love Synergy Parkland with a water and dinosaurs theme.

**Beaches**

Perth has several family friendly beaches that make good family
outings during the warm months. Most have playgrounds and inexpensive
food. Hillary’s has a wonderful family beach where little kids can
swim. There is an area with trampolines and rope climbing for older
kids.

**The Perth Zoo**

The Perth Zoo is a perfect place to spend a day with the family.
There are over 1300 animals in the zoo. The zoo is open 9am to 5pm
every day and it is a no smoking zoo but does have 2 designated areas
where you can smoke. Adult prices are $20 and children 4 to 15 are
$10.

**The Aquarium of Western Australia**

The Aquarium of Western Australia is nearby. There is an underwater
tunnel at this Aquarium that will stun you and your kids with the
incredible beauty of the ocean. The Café serves great meals and snacks
throughout the day so there is no need to go anywhere else. It is open
from 10 am to 5pm and adult admission is $26.50 with children 4 to 15
$15. They offer a deal for a family of 2 adults and 2 children at $69.

**Perth Bell Tower**

The most notable site in Perth is the Perth Bell Tower, a tall glass
spire at the Swan River. It is especially beautiful at night because
the lighting changes colors. There is an observation deck which will
give you a magnificent view of the city. You can get up close to the
bells to see their immenseness and hear them ring.

**Scitech**

Scitech, the leading science center, is in Perth. Exhibitions are
live and the best one is the Horizon Planetarium where you can sit back
and watch the night sky even in the day time. It is open 9:30am to 4pm
and weekends 10am to 5pm.

**Hotel Novotel**

Where should you stay with a family? Hotel Novotel in Perth Langly
has a family deal that is great. They have family rooms, play areas
and menus for kids. Kids stay free and so is breakfast.

Take your family to Perth to participate in the many family activities that are available.
