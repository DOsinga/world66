# Club Bay View

*Australiaandpacific > Australia > Westernaustralia > Perth > Nightlife > Club Bay View*

Home to the 20-something university crowd on a Thursday, come here for some loud music and youngsters keen to have a good time but can't wait for the weekend. Does not get busy until around 11pm, so if you prefer an early start, make sure to loosen with a few drinks around the corner at the Claremont Hotel before strolling down to "Clubba".
