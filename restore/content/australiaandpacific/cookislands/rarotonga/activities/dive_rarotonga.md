**DIVE RAROTONGA 5*IDC**

**
**At **Dive Rarotonga** your safety is our priority. We dive the
beautiful reef surrounding Rarotonga 4 times a day, 7 days a week.All trips are
for small groups (max 6) and are guided as standard. We dive on all sides of
the island with sites ranging from easy going coral dives to more adventurous
caverns and drop offs for the experienced diver plus the southern passages. We offer friendly,
professional and fun diving environment! For safety and comfort our boats are
always manned with a rescue trained skipper who keeps a look out whilst you are
diving. We also carry DAN oxygen and first aid on board Dive Rarotonga is a
PADI 5 Star Instructor Development facility, so for those interested in
learning to dive or continuing their dive education, we offer PADI courses from
first bubbles through to PADI instructor. Why not complete your PADI instructor
course in Rarotonga’s clear, warm tropical
water? We also offer TDI courses and we are a BSAC resort centre. We have a
purpose built classroom, 3m deep training pool, analysed O2 clean air,
toilet/shower and locker facilities on site. Other services include: shop,
servicing, nitrox, camera hire, hydrostatic cylinder testing and hotel
pickup/drop off.
