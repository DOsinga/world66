### [Nad's Handicraft](/australiaandpacific/fiji/vitilevu/nadi/shopping/nads_handicraft)

**Nad's Handicraft**, located on the northern end of nadi town or the first on the right from the airport end, specializes in traditional fijian handicrafts, souveniers, clothing, fijian art gallery and fine jewellery. Stores located in *Nadi, Suva (MHCC), Crusoe's Retreat (Manfriday) *and* Capricorn International Hotel*. Nad's also has an exclusive fine jewellery store at the *Sofitel Fiji Resort (Denerau Island)* trading as **Inspired by nads**.
