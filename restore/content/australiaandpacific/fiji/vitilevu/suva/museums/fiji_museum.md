# Fiji Museum

*Australiaandpacific > Fiji > Vitilevu > Suva > Museums > Fiji Museum*

Fiji Museum is a 'must see' destination for all visitors to Suva. It
contains a wealth of information about the history and culture of Fiji.

The Museum is located in Thurston Gardens on Victoria Parade, a short walk from the centre of Suva.
