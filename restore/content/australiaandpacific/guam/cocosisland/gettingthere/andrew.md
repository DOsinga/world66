# National Jet Airlines

*Australiaandpacific > Guam > Cocosisland > Gettingthere > Andrew*

National Jet Airlines, affiliated with Qantas, provides flights to the
Cocos twice each week. It is an excellent destination, but a little
pricey. One-way tickets cost around $1100, while a return trip will
cost you about $1600. To find out more information about getting to the
Cocos, go to http://www.nationaljet.com.au/.
