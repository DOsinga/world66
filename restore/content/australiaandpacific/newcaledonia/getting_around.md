Air Cal&eacute;donie domestic services serve several towns on Grande Terre each of the Loyalty Islands &Icirc;le des Pins and Waala on &Icirc;les Belep. You can also charter private planes and helicopters. The road network on Grande Terre is extensive and all the main roads are sealed and nearly every major town or large village is connected by bus leaving from Noumea's bus station (*gare routi&egrave;re*). All car rental companies are based in Noumea but unfortunately their rates are high and none will rent to anyone under 21. Animals and pedestrians on secondary roads can make driving hazardous in some areas particularly at night.
