### [Marie Tjibaou Cultural centre](/australiaandpacific/newcaledonia/noumea/sights/tjibaou_cultural_c)

The Tjibaou Cultural centre is a great day out with artists, art, dancers, and musicians from all over the world. The centre also features The Kanak Path, which gives an insight into the myths and legends of the Kanak people.

The centre is situated on the Tina Peninsular, Noumea.

type: Public Buildings

World66 rating:

tel: 687 41 45 55

### [Parc Foresteir](/australiaandpacific/newcaledonia/noumea/sights/parc_foresteir)

Parc Foresteir is a bushland park that offers a leisurely walk with great views of the coast. The park feature endangered animals and offers an array of birdlife.

The park is located just 3 minutes from Noumea’s centre, and can be reached by a small train that leaves for the park four times a day.

type: Parks, Gardens and Zoos
