# Parc Foresteir

*Australiaandpacific > Newcaledonia > Noumea > Sights > Parc Foresteir*

Parc Foresteir is a bushland park that offers a leisurely walk with great views of the coast. The park feature endangered animals and offers an array of birdlife.

The park is located just 3 minutes from Noumea’s centre, and can be reached by a small train that leaves for the park four times a day.
