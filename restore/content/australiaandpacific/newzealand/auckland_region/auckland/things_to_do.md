# Things to do

*Australiaandpacific > Newzealand > Auckland Region > Auckland > Things To Do*

Coast to Coast Tours is an eco-tour of the West Auckland region. It is owned and operated by New Zealander's and they show and explain this area; (- it's where thier family has lived since 1922, and you also get the chance to visit their own sheep farm and have afternoon tea inside their historic Homestead!)

This is a one day tour, with transport, and lunch is included in the price, as well as a visit to Auckland's rainforest area (the Waitakere Rainges), craft-shopping, wine-tasting, honey-sampling, and seeing the gannet sea-bird colony at Muriwai Beach.

You'll even have the chance to buy and plant your own Carbon Credit Native tree to offset the carbon emissions made on your tour.
