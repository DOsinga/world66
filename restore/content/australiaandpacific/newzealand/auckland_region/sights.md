# Sights

*Australiaandpacific > Newzealand > Auckland Region > Sights*

New Zealand is famous for its outdoors. Around Auckland are 21 Regional Parks each features something special.

The **Waitakere Ranges** are one of them. The Ranges are about 30 minutes west of central Auckland. The Arataki
Visitor Centre on Scenic Drive in 9 km distance from Titirangi is the place to get
information about the park. Covered are popular surf and swimming beaches,
secluded beaches, walks, hikes and tramping trails and many highlights of outdoor
trips. Educational displays about the Ranges biodiversity as well as nature
trails in varying length are present. Stunning views and picnic facilities
round the introduction experience up.
