# Marlborough

*Australiaandpacific > Newzealand > Marlborough*

**Marlborough** is a major winegrowing region of
[New Zealand](/australiaandpacific/newzealand). Located in the northeast corner of the
[South Island](/australiaandpacific/newzealand/south_island), it is sheltered from the prevaling winds and has a warm dry climate.
