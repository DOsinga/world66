# Getting There

*Australiaandpacific > Newzealand > Marlborough > Milfordsound > Getting There*

From
[Dunedin](/australiaandpacific/newzealand/dunedin) take **State Highway 1** to *Gore* and turn west onto **State Highway 94** and travel to junction with **State Highway 6** near *Lumsden*. Time: Allow 4 hours, Distance: 181km 

From
[Queenstown](/australiaandpacific/newzealand/queenstown) or
[Invercargill](/australiaandpacific/newzealand/invercargill) take **State Highway 6** to junction with **State Highway 94** near *Lumsden.* Queenstown: Time: 2 hours, Distance: 101 km. Invercargill: Time: 90 min, Distance: 87 km. 

At *Lumsden* turn onto **State Highway 94** and travel **west** to *Te Anau*. Time: 1 hour to 90 minutes. Distance: 78 km 

Stopping for a*** full tank*** of petrol at *Te Anau* is recommended. 

Drive through *Te Anau* and follow the highway **north** through the *Homer Tunnel* and down into *Milford Sound*. Time: Allow 3 hours. Distance: 121 km 

Driving times assume travelling 80km/h on open stretches of road and include a few minutes for short stops.
