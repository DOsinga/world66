# Hawkes Bay

*Australiaandpacific > Newzealand > North Island > Hawkes Bay*

The **Hawkes Bay** lies on the east coast of the [North Island](/australiaandpacific/newzealand/north_island) of [New Zealand](/australiaandpacific/newzealand). It is supposedly named for the large bay it lies in, *Hawke Bay*, but an *s* was added. A colonial spelling mistake? 

Places in the Hawkes Bay include:

[Napier](/australiaandpacific/newzealand/napier)

[Wairoa District](/australiaandpacific/newzealand/wairoa_district)
