# Manawatu

*Australiaandpacific > Newzealand > North Island > Manawatu*

The **Manawatu** is named for its great river. Powerful enough to cut a gorge through the main mountain range that separtates the eastern and western coasts of the [North Island](/australiaandpacific/newzealand/north_island), the *Manawatu River* drains both sides of the mountain ranges. 

Places in the Manawatu include:

[Palmerston North](/australiaandpacific/newzealand/palmerstonnorth)- the largest city

[Levin](/australiaandpacific/newzealand/levin)
