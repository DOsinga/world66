# Northland

*Australiaandpacific > Newzealand > North Island > Northland*

**Northland** is the northern part of the [North Island](/australiaandpacific/newzealand/north_island), north of [Auckland](/australiaandpacific/newzealand/auckland). 

Places in Northland include:

[Whangarei](/australiaandpacific/newzealand/whangarei) - largest city in the region

[Mangawhai heads](/australiaandpacific/newzealand/mangawhaiheads) - a beach near Mangawhai - a tiny town in south Northland

[Kaitaia](/australiaandpacific/newzealand/kaitaia)- in the Far North

[Bay of islands](/australiaandpacific/newzealand/bayofislands) including [Kerikeri](/australiaandpacific/newzealand/kerikeri) and [Romantic Russell](/australiaandpacific/newzealand/romanticrussell)
