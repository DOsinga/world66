# Te Anau Travel Guide

*Australiaandpacific > Newzealand > Otago > Fiordland > Te Anau*

The best resource for sights, hotels, restaurants, bars, what to do and see

Te Anau is a small town on the east shore of Lake Te Anau. Its geographic location is important becasue it links Queenstown and Milford in the middle of the hiway, where it takes an equal 2 hours to both Milford and Queenstown.

It's also the departure point of the world famous Milford Track. Fill up ur petro if you are going to Milford Sound coz this is the most remote and primitive forest area in the world. Petro station only locates in Milford and Te Anau, not on the way between.
