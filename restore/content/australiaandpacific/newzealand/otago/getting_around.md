# Getting Around

*Australiaandpacific > Newzealand > Otago > Getting Around*

Like the rest of New Zeland you will find public transport options leave you wanting.

If you really want to see the best of the country, you are goinf to have to invest in your own transport.

This applies in Southland more than any other region in south island.

You can pick up campers in Christchurch.

New Zealand is the best motorcycling country in the world due to it's well maintained winding roads.
If you are a biker, or have a bike license, this is the number one option to see the country for a **lasting impression**.
