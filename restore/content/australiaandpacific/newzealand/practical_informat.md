# Practical Information

*Australiaandpacific > Newzealand > Practical Informat*

The **Emergency Telephone Number** to use in [New Zealand](/australiaandpacific/newzealand) is **111** .

Use this number to contact the **Police**, **Fire** or **Ambulance** services in case of an emergency.

However, other emergency telephone numbers such as 911, 999 and 112 may also work.
