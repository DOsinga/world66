# Sights

*Australiaandpacific > Newzealand > Sights*

New Zealand has some spectacular scenery.

[Milford sound](/australiaandpacific/newzealand/milfordsound) is, perhaps, the most spectacular. But there is also:

The rest of [Fiordland](/australiaandpacific/newzealand/south_island/southland/fiordland) which is a National Park, but there are others, including [Arthurs pass national park](/australiaandpacific/newzealand/arthurspassnationalpark) and [Egmont national park](/australiaandpacific/newzealand/egmontnationalpark).

The lakes and mountains around [Queenstown](/australiaandpacific/newzealand/queenstown) and [Wanaka](/australiaandpacific/newzealand/wanaka)

The [Franz Josef](/australiaandpacific/newzealand/franzjosef) and Fox Glaciers on the [West Coast](/australiaandpacific/newzealand/south_island/west_coast)

The geothermal wonderland in the [Bay of Plenty](/australiaandpacific/newzealand/north_island/bay_of_plenty) around [Rotorua](/australiaandpacific/newzealand/rotorua) and the [Taupo](/australiaandpacific/newzealand/taupo) Volcanic Zone.
