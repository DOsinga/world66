# Palau

*Australiaandpacific > Palau*

Palau is Micronesia's westernmost island chain and is located roughly speaking between [Indonesia](/asia/southeastasia/indonesia) and the [Philippines](/asia/southeastasia/philippines) . The islands offer great natural beauty, untouched wilderness, intact culture and remoteness; the ideal ingredients for a relaxing holiday. Besides the beaches, the relaxing lifestyle and the diving, the main natural attraction are the Rock islands . This collection of mushroom-shaped islets are largely uninhabited and are located in a large lagoon that harbors one of the world's greatest concentrations of corals, fish and other marine life. 

[Koror](/australiaandpacific/palau/koror) is one of the main resorts. It has white sand beaches, swimming pools and top-notch restaurants. On Palau's largest island of [Babeldoab](/australiaandpacific/palau/babeldoab) , remote waterfalls and hiking trails amid a lush jungle set the stage for nature lovers and adventure seekers. Here, along the edges of this dense wildlife, Palauan villages still survive.
