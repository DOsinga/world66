# British High Commissioner's Residence

*Australiaandpacific > Tonga > Tongatapu > Nukualofa > Sights > Britishhighcommissionersresidence*

Not far from the Royal Palace. See the cannons taken from the privateer Port au Prince captured 1806 at Ha'apai. The story of the capture and survival of a few of its English crew is told by William Mariner in the book "Tonga Islands."
