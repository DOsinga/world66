# Getting Around in Freeport

*Centralamericathecaribbean > Bahamas > Freeport > Getting Around*

**Rent a Car and Explore Freeport**

This **Freeport transportation** guide will help you plan and organize getting around in this island destination. This will help you also to save on cost and eliminate the hassles of transportation from your vacation. Freeport is a modern city and is the second most visited tourist destination in the Bahamas. One of the transportation options is to rent a car, which will make travel in Freeport a breeze.

#### **Car Rentals**

Car rentals should be the first in your list of transportation options in Bahamas because it is a free trade port. Goods sold here are tax-free. Imagine the loads of items you can buy during your trip. By renting a car, you will be able to carry all the things that you buy and not have to worry about lugging everything around in your hand. Further, you are free to stop and visit anywhere in Freeport with your rented car. Drivers must remember to drive on the left as British driving rules apply. Visitors may use their home drivers' licenses for up to three months. A stay longer than three months requires an international driver's license.

#### **Brad&rsquo;s Car Rental**

One of the famous rental cars in Freeport is Brad&rsquo;s Car Rental. The establishment is conveniently situated at the Freeport Bahamas International Airport. You can choose from the wide array of car models, vans or SUVs. The rates for car rentals start at $35 per day in 2010, but discounts may be available.
