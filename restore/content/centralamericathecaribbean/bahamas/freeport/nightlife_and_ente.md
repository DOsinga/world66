# Nightlife and Entertainment in Freeport

*Centralamericathecaribbean > Bahamas > Freeport > Nightlife And Ente*

The city of Freeport on the island of Grand Bahama has a great **Freeport Nightlife** and Entertainment scene. The island city is rich in history with regards to its discovery by Christopher Columbus. This explains the influx of tourists who come here to explore, to indulge in lazy beach activities, and to eat and make merry. Here is a list of fun things to do in Freeport when you plan to paint the town red.

**Goombaya Show**

Bahamian music and dance is not complete without the use of the Goombay drums. This intrinsic part of the Bahamian culture can be experienced at the Goombaya Show, which takes place every Tuesday and Saturday at the Country Club. Lively musicians and dancers in rainbow-hued costumes put on a vibrant show that displays the traditional music of the Bahamas. A ticket for the Goombaya Show will set you back $45.

**Casino**

Visit Freeport's only casino, located at the lush Our Lucaya Resort. The Isle of Capri Grand Casino is enormous in size and houses over 350 slot machines and 20 tables for games like Roulette, BlackJack and Poker. You can always head to the Club Cove lounge and bar for a drink or two, complete with good music and dinner shows.

**Nightclubs**

There are a couple of excellent nightclubs in Freeport in the Bahamas. Jokers Wild, which generally plays Bahamian music, is one not to be missed. Club Amnesia is perfect for those who want to dance the night away to lively beats. Club 2000 has a lit-up dance floor and plays a good choice of music.

**Count Basie Square**

If you happen to be on the lookout for excellent live music and entertainment, then head straight for Count Basie Square, a square that was named in memory of a renowned jazz performer. Even if you head to Count Basie Square in the daytime, you will always find it entertaining, with music coming from everywhere. Besides live music, one can also enjoy various types of dancing here, such as the limbo. If you require more information, a scheduled list of events can be obtained from the Port Lucaya Marina.

**Sunset Cruise**

Enjoy a cruise on Bahamian waters against a beautiful sunset. The Bahama Mama Sunset & Dinner Cruise offers free-flowing cocktails and starters. Also included in the price of this three-hour cruise is a live show, complete with music and dancing that can be enjoyed by the guests onboard. The journey in itself is very scenic and coupled with live entertainment, the Bahama Mama Sunset & Dinner Cruise is one night time activity that is not to be missed.
