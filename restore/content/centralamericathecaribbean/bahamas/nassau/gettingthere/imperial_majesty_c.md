# Imperial Majesty Cruise Line

*Centralamericathecaribbean > Bahamas > Nassau > Gettingthere > Imperial Majesty C*

From Port Everglades, Fort Lauderdale, Florida, set sail on the Regale Empress which is the Imperial Majesty's flagship and cruise to the Caribbean Island of Nassau, New Providence Island. Have fun on board in the pool, the fitness sauna, go to the disco or the shows and have great meals for breakfast, lunch and dinner! You will arrive at Prince George Wharf at the center of Nassau where taxis or horse drawn carriages take you to your spot. Bon Voyage!
