# Bars and Cafes in Hamilton (Capital City)

*Centralamericathecaribbean > Bermuda > Hamilton Capital > Bars And Cafes*

While visiting Hamilton it
is advisable to experience the unique **Hamilton
Bars and Cafés** scene. Influenced by different cultures and ideas, the bars
and cafes in Hamilton, Bermuda have different set ups and vibrant themes.
Whether it’s an informal “English Pub” or laid-back “sport bar” or casual
lounges, there are plenty of great places where you can enjoy yourself. Most
fine-dining restaurants are located near the highway but for you to find the
best bars and cafes you must go deeper within the city of Hamilton. Here are a
few popular spots.

**Pickled
Onion Restaurant and Bar**

Looking for a Caribbean
Island surprise? Then the Pickled Onion Restaurant and Bar is the perfect place
for you. It offers a perfect combination of great food, great music, great
waterfront view and laid-back island atmosphere. Plus the restaurant has one of the
highly rated chefs in Central America. Pickled Onion is one of the busiest
restaurants and bars in Bermuda, especially during weekends.

**Opus
Café and Lounge**

It is a perfect blend of
chic urban and modern lifestyle right in the heart of Hamilton, Bermuda. Opus
Bar and Lounge offer homemade pastries, tapas and salads, international teas,
European coffees and vast selection of wines and spirits. Opus is a comfy and
classy place where people can unwind and meet up with friends. Opus also offers Wi-Fi
connection so you can surf the net while sipping your hot espresso. This is a
great place to relax and spend the evening.

**Robin
Hood Pub and Restaurant**

Robin Hood Pub and
Restaurant is modeled after a rustic old English tavern. This is one of the
most popular sports bars around the city of Hamilton. You can play over 50
shooter’s games or watch your favorite sport while drinking your favorite beer or munching on burgers,
chips, pizza, curries and/or steak.

**North
Rock Brewing Company**

This is the only brewpub in
Bermuda that offers a distinctive combination of brewery and restaurant. North Rock Brewing Company offers the freshest
draught beer that has been brewed right in the heart of the bar. This pub is well-known to both locals and
tourists. This is a great place to enjoy some time with your friends while on
vacation.

**White
Horse Pub and Restaurant**

This is the most popular and
oldest pub in the city of Hamilton. White Horse Pub and Restaurant has live
band entertainment and souvenir shops. You can enjoy your great seafood fare,
prime meats and signature White Horse dishes with a spectacular view of the
majestic St. George Harbor.
