# Eating Out in Hamilton (Capital City)

*Centralamericathecaribbean > Bermuda > Hamilton Capital > Eating Out*

If you are on holiday in
Hamilton, you must stop by some of the fine **Hamilton restaurants**. Hamilton is one of the 9
parishes of Bermuda and serves as the center of commerce, entertainment and
government. Although Hamilton is still
considered as one of the smallest cities in the world with regards to its
population and land area; there is still wide array of must-see and must-visit
spots in this small heaven. There are at least 79 restaurants in Hamilton but
you might want to start your gastronomical journey with these hot spots:

**Fresco’s
Restaurant and Wine Bar**

Fresco’s Restaurant and Wine
Bar is a great place to enjoy some fine Caribbean seafood; not to mention
Fresco’s finest wine selection that compliments every meal. Fresco’s Restaurant
also offers non-seafood and a fusion-style menu. They also boast of outdoor
patio-dining, an intimate fine dining ambience, award-winning chefs and an upstairs
wine bar. Fresco’s is a great place to enjoy a meal with your family.

**Blu
Bar and Grill**

This is one of Bermuda’s premier restaurants. Blu Bar and
Grill offers superior cuts of meats and fish hot off the grill. It offers
strong American and southwestern cuisine with majestic views of Belmont Hills
and the turquoise sea. The restaurant also offers finest selection of local and
international wines. Blu Bar and Grill has special promotions like Bar and Sushi
Happy Hour every afternoon. With its
fine cuisine and excellent service, Blu Bar and Grill has made a name for itself
in Hamilton.

**Hog
Penny**

One of Hamilton’s oldest
establishments, Hog Penny offers great food and good time. The restaurant offers a wide
array of international and local cuisine. Plus a live band performs every night
for additional entertainment. So, if you are looking for a place to enjoy your
meal with some fantastic music, Hog Penny is the place to stop by.

**The
Lobster Pot**

This is one of the finest
and must-try Hamilton seafood restaurants around. The fish chowder and Surf
‘n’ Turf are popular to locals and tourists alike. But, of course, if you want
the freshest catch from the sea be ready to pay a hefty price.

**Island
Cuisine Restaurant**

This restaurant is a place
to experience the true-blue, traditional Bermudian cuisine. Island Cuisine
offers a huge selection of fairly-price home-style cooked dishes. The restaurant's traditional
Bermudian breakfast, seafood entrees like jumbo shrimp, fish and Wahoo, soups
like chowders, split-pea soups and Portuguese Kale and delicious desserts
like Bermudian-style bread pudding are very popular. Plus, the restaurant is family-friendly,
smoke-free and provides a fresh dining atmosphere.
