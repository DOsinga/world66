Corcovado Trek

Day 21 Puerto Jiménez – Los Patos Corcovado National Park

Basically, the first day you will start from Los Patos station to Sirena Biological Station, before los patos station you have to cross the river several times and you will have spectacular landscapes. After reaching the ranger station of Los Patos you will hike 20 km (12 mile) across the heart of Corcovado Park to Sirena Biological Station on the Pacific Coast, along the way you will see a lot wildlife, You will be starting the real adventure into Corcovado National Park, it has one of the highest levels of biodiversity in the world. The climate is very hot and humid which enables the trees to grow to heights of 70 meters. The park contains fourth of the tree species of Costa Rica. Corcovado protects over 400 species of birds, 116 species of amphibians and reptiles and 139 mammals. Corcovado is one of the only two places in the country where are living squirrel monkeys. When you are very lucky you can spot the harpy eagle, which’s in extinction. This day you will hike 8-9 hours with your guide. Accommodation: at Sirena Ranger Station. [Breakfast, box- Lunch and Dinner]

Day 22 Sirena Biological Station

Your base camp overlooks a spectacular, wild tropical beach surrounded by a vast rain forest wilderness. Habitat of jaguar, tapir, squirrel monkey, peccaries and a healthy population of scarlet macaws. Today you hike and explore the surrounding environment including a night walk with flashlights in search of nocturnal wildlife. Overnight: Sirena at Corcovado National Park (Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner)
