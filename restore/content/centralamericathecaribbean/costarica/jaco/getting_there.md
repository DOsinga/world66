# Getting There in Jaco

*Centralamericathecaribbean > Costarica > Jaco > Getting There*

Best way to get there from San jose airport is www.easyridecr.com they offer daily shuttle every hour from 6.00 am till 9.00 pm in modern vans, they cover Playa herradura and Playa hermosa, also Easy Ride returns to San jose airport every hour

**rates start from $35 one way per person**
