# Things to do

*Centralamericathecaribbean > Costarica > Pacuare Protected > Things To Do*

The **Pacuare Jungle Lodge** is located on the banks of the Pacuare River and offers guests an exciting white water rafting trip and overnight stay in one of Costa Rica's most beautiful rainforest environments.

The lodge has nine palm-thatched bungalows including a hilltop honeymoon suite that offers panoramic views of the river and surrounding jungle. High quality meals are included, and there are a number of optional tours available while at the lodge.
