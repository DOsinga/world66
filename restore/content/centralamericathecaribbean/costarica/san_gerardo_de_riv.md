# San Gerardo de Rivas

*Centralamericathecaribbean > Costarica > San Gerardo De Riv*

San Gerardo is located in the south. It is just a small town but it is the most convenient starting point for a visit to [Chirripo](/centralamericathecaribbean/costarica/chirripo) National Park, one of the highlights of the country, with its excellent hiking opportunities.
