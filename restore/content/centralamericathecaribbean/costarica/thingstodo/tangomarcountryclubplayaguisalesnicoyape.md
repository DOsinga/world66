# Tango Mar Country Club, Playa Guisales, Nicoya Pe

*Centralamericathecaribbean > Costarica > Thingstodo > Tangomarcountryclubplayaguisalesnicoyape*

While we usually avoid recommending nine hole golf courses, Tango Mar is one of the exceptions. The course is perfect for tuning up the most important and most neglected part of your game. Laid out over gently rolling grounds and across the cliffs above the ocean, the resort's neatly groomed 10 hole executive golf course gives you a "great day's play" in the tropical surroundings of palm trees and exotic birds.
