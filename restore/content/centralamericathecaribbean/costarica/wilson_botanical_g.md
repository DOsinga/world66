# Wilson Botanical Gardens Travel Guide

*Centralamericathecaribbean > Costarica > Wilson Botanical G*

The best resource for sights, hotels, restaurants, bars, what to do and see

Founded in 1963 by Robert and Catherine Wilson, the Wilson Botanical Gardens offers a great intro to the wealth of bird and plant life of Costa Rica. More than 1000 different plants, 300 kinds of bird and over 800 different butterflies. Best of all: the park is easy to visit with some easy tio do trails.

The gardens are just 6 km form [San Vito](/centralamericathecaribbean/costarica/san_vito).
