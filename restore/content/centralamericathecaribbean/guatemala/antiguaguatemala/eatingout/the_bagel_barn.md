# The Bagel Barn

*Centralamericathecaribbean > Guatemala > Antiguaguatemala > Eatingout > The Bagel Barn*

**Centrally located (10 meters off central park), travellers come here to get their fix of bagels, excellent coffee and wifi internet for those travelling with their own laptops. Bagel sandwiches include different breakfast and lunch selections using quality ingredients such as fresh mozzarella cheese, real cheddar, etc. Its a home away from home, a very cosy environment, movies showing in the afternoon and evenings. A great meeting place and starting point for your day in Antigua Guatemala. For those considering a change in lifestyle, the owners of The Bagel Barn are currently considering setting up franchises around Central America and Mexico. Contact them if interested.**
