# Getting There

*Centralamericathecaribbean > Guatemala > Getting There*

**all**

**By boat**

There are several international passenger services from North America the Far East and Europe to Santo Tomás de Castilla and Puerto Quetzal. Cargo services run to the Pacific ports of San José and Champerico. There are also seven direct lines linking Guatemala with the Far East.  

**By bus**

There are bus services from Mexico and El Salvador Nicaragua Costa Rica and Panama. The route south is liable to disruption due to the political situation in this part of Central America. **By train**

There is no rail service at present. The national railway company is currently being transferred to the private sector.  

**By air**

Guatemala's national airlines are Grupo Taca (TA) and Mayan World Airlines (EY). American Airlines operates daily flights to Guatemala via Miami or Dallas.
