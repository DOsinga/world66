# By bus

*Centralamericathecaribbean > Guatemala > Gettingthere > Bybus*

There are bus services from Mexico and El Salvador Nicaragua Costa Rica and Panama. The route south is liable to disruption due to the political situation in this part of Central America. Border crossings can be subject to considerable delays. The buses used by some companies are comfortable and air-conditioned but it is vital to book as far in advance as possible for every stage of the journey.
