# El Tapeo

*Centralamericathecaribbean > Guatemala > Guatemalacity > Eatingout > El Tapeo*

This is one of the least-known, best restaurants in Guatemala City in the Zona Viva. His tortilla espanola is one of the best in the city, and Luis will prepare a fine paella with advance notice.

A special treat are the papas de la casa and entrecote.
