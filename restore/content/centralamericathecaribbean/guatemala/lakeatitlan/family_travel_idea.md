# Family Travel Ideas in Lake Atitlan

*Centralamericathecaribbean > Guatemala > Lakeatitlan > Family Travel Idea*

One of the great advantage of Lake Atitlan is to offer a big selection of houses for rent for families. You can rent a house on the waterfront with Kayaks and enjoy the lake at his maximum. It is great for youngster to discover how locals are fishing from their wooden canoos meanwhile they swing by with their kayaks.. Or to dive along the crabs divers when they are snorkeling... You can also choose to rent a house near hiking areas like San Pedro, San Juan La Laguna or San Marcos La Laguna.
