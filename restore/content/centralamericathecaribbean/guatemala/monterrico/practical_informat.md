**Hotel "Café del Sol"**

A nice place to stay is the Hotel&Restaurant "Cafe del Sol" on the beach of Monterrico. Excellent food, nice and clean rooms, good tunes and close to the center, so you can walk to all the places of interest. The prices are moderate for what you get: from 20 to 40 US Dollars for a double room. You get swiss birchermuesli for breakfast, whole grain bread and delicous seafood. Check out the special offers on the website: www.cafe-del-sol.com There is quiete a range of activities: looking for turles laying there eggs, boat tours in the mangrove swamps in the early morning to enjoy the sunrise, horsebackriding on the beach, deepseafishing can be arranged as well as whale&dolfphin watching, besides just read and relax in hammocks, enjoy a cocktail at sunset and be amazed by the wild and powerful pacific.
