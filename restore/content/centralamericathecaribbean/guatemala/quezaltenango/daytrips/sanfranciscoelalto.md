# San Francisco El Alto

*Centralamericathecaribbean > Guatemala > Quezaltenango > Daytrips > Sanfranciscoelalto*

San Francisco El Alto is the most picturesque town in the region. From
the town's plaza one can see the volcanoes Santa Maria and Cerro
Quemado, as well as the peak of Zunil. The livestock market on Fridays
is one of the most spectacular in the country.
