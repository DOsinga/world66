# From Mexico

*Centralamericathecaribbean > Guatemala > Tikal > Gettingthere > Frommexico*

It is possible to cross directly from
Palenque in Mexico to Tikal. You have to go via either the Rio san Pedro or the Rio Usumacinta. It is also posible to take a bus from Chetumal on the Mexico/ Belize border. There is a direct bus at 6am and several other buses that make a stop in belize city.
