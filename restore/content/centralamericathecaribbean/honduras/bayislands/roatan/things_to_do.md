# Things to do

*Centralamericathecaribbean > Honduras > Bayislands > Roatan > Things To Do*

There are museums, submarine dives, scuba diving, snorkling, biking, sailing, motor cycles and motor scooters. You can fish, go trekking or just lay back and relax on the beach.

### [Coconut Tree Divers](/centralamericathecaribbean/honduras/bayislands/roatan/things_to_do/coconut_tree_diver)

The most popular reason to come to Roatan is to go scuba-diving. Coconut Tree Divers can take you to four different dive sites a day to choose from for a total of 36 pristine dive spots.
