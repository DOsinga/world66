### [Copantl](/centralamericathecaribbean/honduras/sanpedrosula/nightlifeandentertainment/copantl)

The Copantl hotel has a casino attached. The casino has traditional slot machine, and typical table games, blackjack, roullette, poker. The casino has to Hold em tables for private play in the back of the casino. It is normal though to have a crowd, as most people in honduras are not acustomed to seeing that kind of money played in a game.

type: Casinos

World66 rating:
