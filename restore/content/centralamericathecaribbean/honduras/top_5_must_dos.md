# Top 5 Must Do's in Honduras

*Centralamericathecaribbean > Honduras > Top 5 Must Dos*

**1) Cusuco National Park tours**
***Junglexpedition Tours*** number one trip. Choose from; Hiking, Mountain biking, Waterfall abseiling, Camping, Night tours, Bird Watching tours, and many more activities.

**2) Copan Arqueological Site**
The Mayan world of Copan's arqueological site is a trip you can miss at Honduras. There is also a range of other activities you can do here. Coffee tour, Horse back ridding, Canopy, Hot springs, Macaw bird watching.

**3) Miami garifuna village & Punta Sal National Park**
Punta Sal has one of the most exotic beaches on the north coast of Honduras. Leaving by boat from the garifuna village of "Miami" this beaches give you a perfect combination of an adventourous and relax day.

**4) Taulabe Caves & Pullanpanzak Waterfalls **
For a soft day one day tour this are your options. Walk through 700 meters of the Taulabe caves and watch all the diferent shapes of the stones that molded in thousands of years. Pullapanzak Waterfalls continue to amaze visitors for its breath takin views.

**5) Lake Yohoa boat tours**
Leaving from San Pedro Sula to lake Yohoa is just 1 hour and half. Yohoa Lake is the biggest lake of the country and has over 400 species of birds.
