# La Paz Centro Travel Guide

*Centralamericathecaribbean > Nicaragua > La Paz Centro*

La Paz Centro is Municipal of Leon. It is located in
a hot dry northwest part of Nicaragua. This central region is home to
approximitely 10,000 people. La Paz Centro has several small villages
called comarcas. La Paz Centro straddles the continental divide. It is also known for its pottery.
