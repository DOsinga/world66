# Top 5 Must Do's in Laguna de Apoyo

*Centralamericathecaribbean > Nicaragua > Laguna De Apoyo > Top 5 Must Dos*

One of the top things to do when visiting the entrancing lake, Laguna de Apoyo, is spend time at Estacion Biologica and Lake Apoyo Spanish School. This biological research station and school for Spanish language learning is run by the knowledgeable Dr. Jeffrey McCrary. The research on moths, butterflies, cichlids, birds and other animals is fascinating. Volunteers can help with conservation efforts by planting trees and participating in research studies. The teachers at the Spanish school are excellent. Experienced teachers such as Lorenzo Lopez make learning fun and easy. The setting is perfect for study. You will learn Spanish in the middle of lush surroundings, listening to howler monkeys in the day and frogs at night. The station is comfortable, and the food prepared by the cooks is organic and delicious!
