# Getting Around in San Juan del Sur

*Centralamericathecaribbean > Nicaragua > Sanjuandelsur > Getting Around*

When deciding among the several choices for **San Juan del Sur
Transportation**, it is important that you consider two factors: how
much time you have available in San Juan del Sur and what is your
budget.

**Lots of Time and Small Budget**

Under this scenario, the best option is to use the public buses, also
known as "chicken buses" because they can get very very crowded like
"chickens in a coop." Most buses in San Juan del Sur will resemble
school buses from North American cities. The main bus station is right
in front of the main market. Traveling by bus takes a long time but it
will allow you to save lots of money. It is not recommended to wear
flashy jewelry and watches, while riding the bus. Also avoid carrying
large bags or suitcases, and carrying large amounts of money.

**Short on Time and Medium Budget**

If you don't have that much time available on your visit to
Nicaragua, then it is a good idea to either use the express buses or
rent a car.

The express buses are a noticeable upgrade from the chicken buses
because they have air conditioning and individual seats. These buses
travel much faster than the regular buses because they make fewer stops.

Renting a car in San Juan del Sur is best done at the Managua
International Airport (IATA airport code: MGA), where you will be able
to find several local (Targa and Lugo) and international car rental
agencies (Alamo, Dollar and Hertz). It is a good idea to apply for an
international driver's license before arriving to Nicaragua because it
is the best proof of your driving ability around the country.
