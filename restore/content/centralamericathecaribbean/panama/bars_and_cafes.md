# Bars and Cafes in Panama

*Centralamericathecaribbean > Panama > Bars And Cafes*

**Panama City Bars and Cafes** are favored among visitors to the country. The nightlife in Panama City is full of energy and numerous establishments are available for enjoying a night on the town. Some areas such as the Business District and Casco Viejo offer a broad selection of venues for food and drink. From wine bars to tapas restaurants, the culture of the city comes to life on a nightly basis.

**Where to Go**

Many Panama City bars and cafes follow the structure of bars and clubs from other parts of the world. Steinbok, located in the Business District offers a variety of international beers and authentic German cuisine for patrons. The 11 year old restaurant also offers a selection of wines and traditional German decor.

**Where to Go for Music**

Located in Casco Viejo, Cafe Havana is an area favorite for enjoying Cuban cuisine and music. Panama City bars and cafes provide unique atmospheres for patrons and Cafe Havana is no different. Guests may also enjoy a selection of Cuban cigars while listening to Latin rhythms.

**Upscale Cafes**

Visitors to the country may also find Panama City bars and cafes along the nightlife strip known as Calle Uruguay. Calle Uruguay is filled with popular bars and restaurants such as the Loft and Green House, a popular lounge and cafe. Green House features an upscale dining atmosphere with stylish decor and electronic music. Guests who visit Green House enjoy a selection of fresh juices and an open bar. Greenhouse is a unique cafe experience for visitors and a very popular hub for entertainment in the city.
