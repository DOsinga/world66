# Bars and Cafes

*Centralamericathecaribbean > Panama > Bocasdeltoro > Bars And Cafes*

**When in Bocas del Toro you must stop by Toro Loco bar and restaurant located on Central Avenue, one street off the main street behind the park. It's an American-owned bar that offers free Wifi, darts, delicious bar food including burgers, quesadillas, chicken fingers and wings and chili all $6 and under. They also have five televisions around the bar that carry sports all day long! Free popcorn for everyone and happy hour is everyday from 3-6pm. A great place in town to have a cold beer and catch up with old friends or make new ones.**
