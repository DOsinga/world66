# Day Trips in Bocas del toro

*Centralamericathecaribbean > Panama > Bocasdeltoro > Day Trips*

**Bocas del Toro day trips** are a terrific way for you to
experience as much of this area of Panama on your vacation. Here are a
few suggestions for things to places to include in your day trip
excursion.
- Dolphins Bay is an area of Cristobal Island with very
large dolphin populations. Plan to make this a stop on a day trip.- Red Frog Beach with its white sandy beach and red poisonous dart
frogs is a great way to spend the day. Swim in the sea and play on the
beach, or spend the afternoon looking for frogs.- Cays Coral, also known as Crawl Key, offers some of the best snorkeling in the Caribbean, with its colorful coral reefs.- Punta Vieja, or Old Point as it is also known, lends its name to a
terrific place to spend the day relaxing and taking in some sun
time. Pack a lunch and bring a good book.- Bastimentos Marine National Park is a nature refuge rain forest for
many of the island's inhabitants, such as toucans, five varieties of
monkeys (including howler, white faced and tamarin) and sloths, just to
name a few. A great day trip will include a stop at Salt Creek Village,
where you will discover various species of fish and vegetation, as well as Red
Frog Beach.- An Isla Colon boat tour is a fantastic way for you to spend the day
in Bocas del Toro Archipelago. After sailing around the island, where
you could spot one of four different marine turtle species, you should
plan on stopping at Swans Cay, where you will see a collection of
tropical birds, such as red billed birds and brown boobies.- You could spend the day scuba diving and snorkeling in Bocas del
Toro. There are countless varieties of corals, fish, reefs,
shipwrecks, treasures, and caves.- The beach of Boca del Drago is a recommended place to spend the
afternoon. The warm turquoise water and fresh "fish of the day"
restaurant provide the perfect setting for a day at the beach.- Spend the day bike riding. The island is 20 miles long by 8 miles
wide. It has a dirt road that cuts across and is safe for mountain bike
riding. You can rent a bike in the town and ride for the day. Take
along a picnic lunch and make a whole day of it.

Most of these day trips cost about $25USD per person. Scuba diving
excursions cost slightly more, while snorkeling is about $12 per person
for the day.
