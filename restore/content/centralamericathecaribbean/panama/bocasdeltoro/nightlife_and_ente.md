# Nightlife and Entertainment in Bocas del toro

*Centralamericathecaribbean > Panama > Bocasdeltoro > Nightlife And Ente*

Acclaimed for its beaches, Bocas del toro also offers an impressive
selection of bars and clubs for visitors to enjoy. There is nothing
more relaxing than a long day in the sun and sand, followed by an
evening of excellent drinks and dancing.

**Barco Hundido**

Barco Hundido is perhaps the most visited nightlife spot in Bocas
Town. This bar is also known as the Shipwreck Bar, and it provides a unique
atmosphere. It's actually built atop a shipwreck with a dock
surrounding the premises. At night, lights turn on outside of the bar
and fish can be seen gliding through the water. Barco Hunido plays a
range of music - from salsa to rock - and is definitely known as a popular
spot for dancing. The dress attire is generally casual.

**Toro Loco**

For sports lovers, Toro Loco is a popular spot, located behind the
park in the middle of Bocas Town. Toro Loco serves wings and burgers
along with the option of watching 5 televisions broadcasting sports
games and events from around the world. Happy hour at Toro Loco starts
at 3 pm and lasts until 6 pm. Buena Vista is also a well known sports
bar-located on the water with satellite televisions and strong
margaritas.

**Other Hot Spots**

Various other hot spots include Mundo Taitu - a friendly bar with an
extended happy hour time. Drink specials run throughout the night at
Mundo Taitu, and are fairly priced. A mixed crowd can be found here and
bartenders are friendly. La Iguana is located in Bocas Town and is
known for its dynamic draw as a pub, bar and surf club. The beer is
cold and the environment is casual and island style.

Although Bahia Hotel is not necessarily known as a bar, a visit is
recommended in order to taste the genuine Italian pizza that is served
nightly. Bahia Hotel definitely takes the prize for the best pizza in
Bocas del Toro.

**Vaqeros Isla**

For travelers looking for an escape from the hustle and bustle of
Bocas Town, there is an option to hire a boat and take a short ride to
Vaqeros Isla. Upon arrival, visitors will be greeted with a warm
private ambiance and a small thatched roof bar proudly serving some of
the coldest beers in Panama. Enjoy a wide variety of strong mixed
drinks and arrange a time for your captain to return and drive back to
Bocas Town. There is no need to dress fancy for the boat ride.
Everything is casual at Vaqeros Isla.
