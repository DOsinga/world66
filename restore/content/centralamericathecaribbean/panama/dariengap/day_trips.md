# Day Trips in Darien gap

*Centralamericathecaribbean > Panama > Dariengap > Day Trips*

**When visiting Panama's Darien Gap**, **day trips** will help you explore the area in detail. The Darien Gap is a large and mostly inaccessible swamp and tropical forest area forms a natural border between Panama and Columbia. The wildlife here is abundant and scientists from all over the world flock to the area to study the habitat. Tourists who venture into the area can interact with native people in small remote villages. Visitors should expect a distinct lack of amenities and comforts. The famous Pan-American Highway connecting Alaska and Chile is disconnected in this area, a clear illustration of how remote this area really is.

**Boat Tour**

The Darien Gap is accessible only via boat, airplane and on foot. This is an UNESCO protected area so only small airplanes are allowed to fly in. Tourists can start their trip from La Palma, the regional capital where they will find no shortage of tour agencies and locals who have the experience to take you safely through the swamp and forests. The boat ride will take tourists to local villages. You'll want to make stops to walk around the forest and observe birds and wildlife. Macaws, harpy eagles and parrots are just few bird species that you can see in the forest. These tours last from 5 to 15 days. That way tourist can experience the true wilderness. But if you have a limited time, one-day tours are quite an adventure.

**Remote Village Tour**

Darien Gap is a home to Embera-Wounaan and Kuna Indians so tourists can get a canoe ride and easily get to their villages in a day. Be reminded that the guide in this area is a must. Interacting with locals is quite an experience. Their customs and traditions are interesting and probably never seen before in any other culture. The chance to try local dishes shouldn&rsquo;t be missed.

**Hike tour to Pirre Station**

This is a place where tourists can cool off in a waterfall and have a ride on a natural water slide after a day of hiking. The hike is usually muddy and going through tough tropical forest but it is worth every minute. The Pirre Cloud forest will give tourists complete new experience being in a realm of never lifting mist.
