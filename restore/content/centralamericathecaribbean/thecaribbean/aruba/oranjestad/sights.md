**Sights**

**Archaeological Museum**

The museum exhibits items of Aruba's prehistoric culture. It is located behind the San Francisco Church. Call 279-828-979 for more information. 

address: Irausquinplein 2a

openinghours: Mon-Fri, 8 am-noon and 1:30-4:30 pm

**Numismatic Museum**

It's a collection of a guy called Mario. It displays coins from more than 350 countries. The museum is located near Fort Zouman. Admission is free, but donations are welcome. 

address: Zuidstraat 7

openinghours: Mon-Fri, 7:30 am-4:30 pm
