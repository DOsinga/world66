# Beaches

*Centralamericathecaribbean > Thecaribbean > Cuba > Beaches*

Great beaches are everwhere around Cuba. We're talking about a Caribean island here!

Most beaches are great. White sands, palm trees, cyan color water, the works. In general the beaches in the north are a little bit better than those in the south.

If lying on the beach is the thing for you, go to Varadero. This is the big beach town. The Varadero penisula contains hundreds of beach resorts with great beaches and water sports.

Try Cayo Santa Maria for nude bathing.
