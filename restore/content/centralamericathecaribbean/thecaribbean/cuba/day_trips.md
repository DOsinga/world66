# Day Trips in Cuba

*Centralamericathecaribbean > Thecaribbean > Cuba > Day Trips*

Visitors have lots of options to consider when planning **Cuba Day Trips**. Cuba offers a vast range of activities in the form of eco-tours, beach trips and visiting historic places. Some of the most popular Cuba day trips feature Pinar del Rio - one of Cuba's most commercially-progressive provinces. Pinar del Rio offers some exciting day trip options like Vinales.

**Reaching Vinales - West Cuba Day Trips
**

Vinales is a small Cuban town located in the western part of Pinar del Rio province. Vinales is situated toward the western end of the Cuban island stretch. Vinales is largely regarded as an old-world colonial town with wooden houses, tiled pavements and many remnants dating back to the 18th century. Visitors can take a day trip to Vinales aboard any of the major public bus services. This town is well-connected to all the neighboring cities like Baracoa, Varadero, Havana, Santiago de Cuba and Cienfuegos. Choice of transportation also includes rental cars/bikes or local taxis.

**What to Expect in Vinales Day Trips**

**Vinales Valley**

The Vinales Valley has been turned into a nature reserve and features among UNESCO's list of World Heritage Sites. People can visit the popular Botanical Gardens. The flora and fauna here consists of many endemic plants like the Cayman Oak and regional varieties of palm trees. Visitors can walk the hilly parts along the outskirts of Vinales to observe popular birds like the Cuban trogon and the Cuban solitaire.

**Exploring Rural Cuba**

Vinales is recommended for discovering the traditional agricultural practices and Cuban music/crafts. The landscape is essentially rural and visitors can understand the native way of living. People can consider visiting the tobacco farming sites to share a cigar or coffee with the locals. Some tour operators offer Farmyard Packages wherein visitors get to spend about half a day in a typical Cuban village site.

**Visiting Caves**

Among popular cave sites, Cave Indio and Cave of Jose Miguel are popular. Visitors can also take guided tours into the cave complex of Santo Tomas Caves. A rather bizarre attraction here is a cave that is converted into a nightclub of sorts during the weekends!

**Hiking Trails**

Visitors can proceed along the hiking trails at the foothills of steep mountain slopes. Near the famous Dos Hermanas Valley, visitors can take pictures of the prehistoric murals. Bicycle and motorcycle rentals are easily available for a minimum deposit.

**Insight into Cuban History**

A must-visit in Vinales day trips is the El Palenque Cimarrones. This place was used as a hideout by runaway slaves. The residents have recreated the 19th-century setting that prevailed here, providing glimpses into the Slave Trade that deeply impacted the cultural heritage of Cuba.
