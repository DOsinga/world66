# Eating Out in Sosua

*Centralamericathecaribbean > Thecaribbean > Dominica > Puertoplata > Sosua > Eating Out*

If you want to find some of the best **Sosua restaurants** while you are traveling, there are a number of tropical spots to enjoy a great meal and have some of the local dishes.

#### Provocon

The Provocon restaurant offers a lot of different dishes including many specialties. There are traditional chicken dishes and also some of the local favorites. The prices are great. If you are looking to try some of the cultural cuisine, you can stop here and try different items prepared with traditional spices and flavors.

#### Captain Cook

The Captain Cook restaurant is a great place to stop if you enjoy fish and seafood. The restaurants serves many different fish and seafood dishes which are local favorites. They also have a variety of starters and drinks to go with any of the main dishes you order. If you want to find a great place for seafood, this is one of the Sosua restaurants worth trying.

#### Armando

The Armando restaurant is a small but elegant place to stop and enjoy the sea bass, wine, coffee and many other dishes available. You can choose from the different kinds of local cuisine to really experience the Island food. Relax in the dining area with your favorite drink or coffee to enjoy.
