# Getting Around in Sosua

*Centralamericathecaribbean > Thecaribbean > Dominica > Puertoplata > Sosua > Getting Around*

**Sosua transportation **facilities put forth an interesting mix of regular and some unique modes of commuting. Please note that visitors in Sosua don&rsquo;t need to worry about the range of transport options found in Sosua. This is a rather small town, and most parts are easily walkable. The most common and easily-located Sosua transportation facilities include:

**Taxis & Unofficial Pick-ups**

Sosua does have a small fleet of official tourist taxis, but these are expensive and seem to be used only by passengers arriving from the airport. The most common form of a "taxi-like" facility is the unofficial rides offered by the locals. Visitors don&rsquo;t need to bother about prices when getting aboard a pick-up like this. They can simply demand a ride on any of these makeshift taxis and hand-out whatever token amount of money they deem is the appropriate price! There are no minimum prices, and most of the drivers don&rsquo;t argue. The other, slightly adventurous, form of getting a ride in Sosua is the motoconcho—motorcycles used by locals to provide impromptu rides for a meager amount.

**Guaguas**

Using the Guagua is the cheapest and most undemanding way of getting around Sosua. These buses run multiple services to nearby towns like Cabarete, Puerto Plata and Rio San Juan, making it easier to undertake day trips. Guaguas are essentially small buses, a bit like large vans, that stop at the wave-of-a-hand and make plenty of unscheduled stops. While this may sound uncomfortable at first, it ensures that visitors can bank upon finding a ride in Sosua. It is advised to pack lightly when using this bus service since overcrowding seems to be the norm here.

**Car Rentals**

There are few car rentals in Sosua since the town isn't very big. Most of the rentals are found near the airport. Visitors shouldn&rsquo;t expect to find lots of choices among the cars offered. The deposit amount and daily rental rates are quite economical.
