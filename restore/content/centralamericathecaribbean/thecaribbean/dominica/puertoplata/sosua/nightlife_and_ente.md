# Nightlife and Entertainment in Sosua

*Centralamericathecaribbean > Thecaribbean > Dominica > Puertoplata > Sosua > Nightlife And Ente*

The tiny town of Sosua in the Dominican Republic has a great **Sosua Nightlife** scene happening. As it is, Sosua is known for its powdery white beaches and a wide variety of hotels and resorts. It's no wonder that this town is frequented by tourists throughout the year. Sosua has a good number of bars and night clubs. Here is a list of the top nightlife zones in Sosua.

**Jolly Roger Bar and Grill**

Jolly Roger Bar and Grill is one of the most popular bars in Sosua and is also packed to the core at any time of the year. The bar has a big TV for sports games too. A DJ usually plays excellent music or bands sometimes entertain the crowd. Jolly Roger offers a $50 deal from Monday through Saturday, which entitles customers unlimited drinks from 12:00 p.m. to 08:00 p.m. Tuesday afternoons see Bingo games, while Friday is set aside for a Dart Tournament. Karaoke is also available on any night. Jolly Roger Bar and Grill is situated in El Batey.

**El Flow**

El Flow is a fun bar and cafe that plays Salsa and Dominican music. Drinks here are priced very well and a bottle of the best beer will set you back of less than $1. If you love dances like Bachata and Merengue, then you will enjoy El Flow as people are always grooving to the tasteful music played here.

**Club 59**

Club 59 lies above the Merengue Bar. Usually, you will not have to pay any cover charge to enter, but when the club hosts special acts, then a cover charge of around $3. Music ranges from electronic music to Afro beats, and the dance floor is always crowded and lively. It must be noted that drinks can get a bit expensive here.

**Ruby's Lounge**

Ruby's Lounge lies at the corner of Calle Arzeno in the Sosua Bay area. This dance club-cum-lounge bar was previously known as Voodoo Lounge. It is a two-storied club and music ranges from jazz to Latin soul and Blues. The club sometimes has karaoke nights. But, come the weekends, it's the in-house DJ as well as special artists who liven up the night. If you arrive before 8:00 p.m., you can get a good discount on your alcoholic drinks.

**Club Latinos**

Behind the famous Life Bar in Sosua, lies the now popular Club Latinos. The dance floor is always pulsating with dancers who come here to groove with their partners to the excellent choice of Latino, Latin American and Salsa music. If you aren't much of a dancer, you can opt for a bar stool at the club's ultra-long bar. Club Latinos has been designed in keeping with the tropical mood, complete with beautiful paintings. The music is churned out live by a DJ.
