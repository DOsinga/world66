# When to Go in Sosua

*Centralamericathecaribbean > Thecaribbean > Dominica > Puertoplata > Sosua > When To Go*

If you are planning a vacation to the Caribbean city of **Sosua**,** when to go **isn't something you have to spend much of your time worrying about. This is because Sosua has a typically Caribbean weather with warm daytime temperatures throughout the year and is devoid of any climatic extremes.

**Sosua Weather
**

The climate in Sosua can be best described as tropical with bright sunshine and clear skies. The average temperature is around 30 degrees Celsius. Yes, the humidity is high, but not to the extent of making the outdoors uncomfortable. This makes Sosua a prefect destination for beach-goers. On an average, the city receives about eight hours of bright sunshine daily.

**Sosua Rainfall Pattern**

The short, wet season that Sosua witnesses lasts from October-to-January, but the rainfall is usually moderate. Sosua doesn&rsquo;t have disturbing, climatic features like hurricanes. During the wet months, the higher humidity may be an issue for some visitors. June-to-early August is the wettest period in Sosua, and the sporadic rains can hamper traveling/beaching activities.

**Best Time to Visit Sousa—the Sunshine Destination**

Most of the holidaymakers arriving in Sosua tend to prefer the December to January period. This is when the holiday seasons sets in across the world, and bringing in the New Year on the island paradise of Sosua seems to be a preferred option. However, it should be noted that the huge rush during this time does drive up the accommodation/rental prices. People not bothered by the higher prices should visit Sosua during the Christmas/New Year period when the island wears a distinctive, festive look. The budgeted traveler or those seeking a bit of tranquility should visit Sosua during February, late August or November. These months offer economical pricing and lower daytime temperatures and are free from the holiday rush.
