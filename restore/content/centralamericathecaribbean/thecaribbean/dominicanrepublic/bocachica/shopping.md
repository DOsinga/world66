# Shopping in Boca chica

*Centralamericathecaribbean > Thecaribbean > Dominicanrepublic > Bocachica > Shopping*

If you are holidaying in Boca Chica, it is worth checking out the few
**Boca Chica shopping** spots. You will be able to pick up a few
things in these places.

**Boca Chica Fashion Boutique**

The Boca Chica Fashion Boutique is a little fashion store situated in
Plaza Caracol and is easily accessible from the main highway. At this
boutique you will find a nice collection of men and women’s clothing
like denim pants, blouses, shirts, skirts and dresses, as well as shoes,
bags, hats and caps. If you buy several items from this shop you might
get a personal dinner invitation from the owner.

**Cupido Italia Moda**

This is another humble place to shop in Boca Chica. The Cupido Italia
Moda is a boutique and beauty salon rolled into one. Here you can pick
up some nice Italian fashion clothing and accessories. Some of the items
on sale here are colorful skirts, blouses, dresses, pants and shirts,
as well as bags, scarves and perfumes. Cupido Italia Moda also offers
beauty salon services such as hair cutting, styling, coloring, braiding
and hair lost treatment, manicure, pedicure and body massage.

**La Reina del Pelo**

La Reina del Pelo is another little boutique situated in the Distrito
National of Boca Chica just opposite the Isla Bonita restaurant. This
boutique is the place to pick up some trendy fashion wear like denim
pants, blouses, shirts, skirts and more. If you left your swimwear at
home, you can pick up some at this boutique. You might want to have a
look the bags, shoes, wigs, sandals, belts, and accessories all
available at discount prices.

**Punto Italia Gourmet**

You would not want to miss out shopping at Punto Italia Gourmet. This
shop is situated in downtown Boca Chica. Punto Italia Gourmet offers a
wide selection of Italian gourmet products such as salami, pasta,
cheeses, wines and more. You can buy some for your self or buy extra to
take home for your family and friends. If you have more time you can,
sit down and feast on these different Italian delicacies available at
the shop.

**Beachfront Shopping**

The beachfront is another nice place to shop. You can try the stalls
and shops along the beaches of Boca Chica. There are several shops and
stalls lining the 6 blocks of beachfront area parallel to the beach.
Here you can find several items like local crafts, artworks, cigars,
rums and more. You can also pick up some beach sandals, sunscreen
lotions and fashion jewelry here at the beachfront.
