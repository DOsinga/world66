# Things to do in Boca chica

*Centralamericathecaribbean > Thecaribbean > Dominicanrepublic > Bocachica > Things To Do*

With Boca Chica’s wonderful climate and location you won’t run out of
**Boca Chica things to do** while you are on holiday in this
beautiful destination. Here are a few recommendations.

**Deep Sea Fishing**

Boca Chica has a rich marine biodiversity and is a perfect place to
schedule fishing trips together with your family and friends. If you did
not bring any fishing gear with you, there are several shops where you
can rent these from here in Boca Chica. There are several deep sea
fishing areas just 10 to 30 miles offshore. From these spots you can
catch big wahoo, marlin, dorado, barracuda, mako and more. If you are
unfamiliar with the waters of Boca Chica, there are few groups who
arrange fishing trips such as Hispaniola and Bertram.

**Play Golf**

Although Boca Chica is just a humble place, the game of golf has
taken a firm hold here. You can go to the Guavaberry Golf and Country
Club, situated in the northern side of Boca Chica for a nice game of
golf. The golf club features well manicured and lush 18-hole golf
course, unique landscapes, wonderful rock formations, man-made
waterfalls and ponds. The golf club also houses a club house,
restaurant, outdoor pool and lodging villas.

**Water Sports**

If you love water sports, Boca Chica has lot of things in store of
you. You can go scuba diving, snorkeling, kayaking, jet skiing, wind
surfing and sailing. You can also enroll for a training session with the
Orca Divers Diving School for only $130. If you already know how to
dive the diving school has guided diving trips for only $60 per dive.
Popular diving sites that you can explore are the Coral Garden, Manta
Reef, Max Mountain, Catalina Island, Cueva Taina and the National
Submarine Park.

**Beach Hopping**

There are lots of beaches you can explore in this beautiful place.
The Boca Chica Beach is a one-mile property which has a semi-circle cove
and natural break wall. Other great beaches in Boca Chica are La
Matica, Los Pinos and Playa Andes.

**Casino Galore**

You might want to try your luck at one of Boca Chico’s casinos. Most
of Boca Chica’s casinos have a colorful Caribbean atmosphere. Everyone
is given a chance to become a VIP member in these casinos. Popular
casinos are the Occidental El Dorado Casino, the Renaissance Jaragua
Hotel and Casino and the Playa Chiquita Resort and Casino.
