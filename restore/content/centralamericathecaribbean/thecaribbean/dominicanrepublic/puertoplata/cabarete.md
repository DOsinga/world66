# Cabarete

*Centralamericathecaribbean > Thecaribbean > Dominicanrepublic > Puertoplata > Cabarete*

A few miles further to the east from Sosua, there is Cabarate. This place is good for surf and other watersports, it has some great beachbars (especially when you walk along the beach, away from the main street). And, only two miles away there is a magnificent piece of rainforest which you can visit.
