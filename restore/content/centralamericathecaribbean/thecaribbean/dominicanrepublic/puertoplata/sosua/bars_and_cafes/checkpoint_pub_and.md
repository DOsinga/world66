# Checkpoint Pub and Sandwich Bar

*Centralamericathecaribbean > Thecaribbean > Dominicanrepublic > Puertoplata > Sosua > Bars And Cafes > Checkpoint Pub And*

Friendly, centrally located Sandwich Bar (daytime) and Pub, open from 10:30AM- until late, 7 days a week. Located in Beachway/Bommarito Plaza right in town, here you can find and meet great people from all over the world.

Their "famous" Caesar is a favorite drink with Canadian customers, however here you will meet Canadians, Americans, British and Europeans that have one common interest - to meet new people and enjoy Sosua. Happy Hour from 5-8, daily! Premium liquors also available that are hard to find elsewhere in Sosua.
