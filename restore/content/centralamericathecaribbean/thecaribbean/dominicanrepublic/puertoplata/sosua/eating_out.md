# Eating Out in Sosua

*Centralamericathecaribbean > Thecaribbean > Dominicanrepublic > Puertoplata > Sosua > Eating Out*

There are many good restaurants in Sosua, offering many types of food. Places like **Morua Mai**, **Gaudí**, and Waterfront have the best fine foods, while in the other hand there are many casual food spots such as Britannia, **Tipico Alberto**, Baileys, Cubanos Café, PJ and La Roca. Other places worth mentioned are Oh La Vache ( French pizza joint ), jolly Roger ( Canadian ), La Finca ( Dominican ) and Sosua Mofongo ( Dominican, new ). Sosua has countless bars to enjoy drinks, some of them full of ladies who will always be on the look out for single, adventurous tourists.

The main strip in Sosua is Pedro Clisante, where most of the best places are located. The second main street is Alejo Martinez, and if you want something nice on the water try Piergiorgio`s and the restaurants ( 3 ) of the Sosua Bay Resort and Victorian House. The selection of food in the area varies with foreign and local dishes, such as seafood, pastas, fillets, Mexican, Chinese and even dishes from the far East in some places. Many resorts in Sosua offer their own fine food in the restaurants, though most tourists prefer to try outside restaurants for diversity and amenity. A different sort of travelers, villa rentals customers often request that the chef service be taken to them in private, so private chef services are also available for those who want to enjoy a higher level, with menus that include all the best foods (** Golden Treasures Sosua Villas** offer private chef service ) For those who also want to buy their own groceries, there are 4 supermarkets in Sosua, and a few liquor stores with very good prices.
