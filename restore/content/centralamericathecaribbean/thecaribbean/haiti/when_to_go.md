# When to Go in Haiti

*Centralamericathecaribbean > Thecaribbean > Haiti > When To Go*

The best time to go weather wise is November till March, when day temperatures are on average between 23 and 32 degrees celcius (70s and 80s Fahrenheit) and nights are between 15 and
27 celcius (60s-70s Fahrenheit). The rainy season is from May till July, but even when it rains, it usually lasts for only an hour or two in the evening. Hurricane season is July till October. The hill country is always about 10 degrees cooler.
