# Kingston

*Centralamericathecaribbean > Thecaribbean > Jamaica > Kingston*

Kingstons city life is among the best in the Caribbean. There are good restaurants, first class hotels and great shopping deals - try the Chelsea Galleries or the Sovereign Shopping Centre.

The city has a few excellent museums museums, such as the Devon House, the National Gallery or the Arawak museum. Bob Marley fans are in luck. Kingston has the one and only Bob Marley Museum.  In the harbour you can visit Fort Charels which has protected Jamaica against invaders since 1655.

 Castleton Botanical Gardens, Hope Botanical Gardens and the Caymannas Park are the lungs of the city. Castelton is a wonderland of flowers and shrubs, Caymanas has horse racing on Wednesday, Saturday and holidays.
