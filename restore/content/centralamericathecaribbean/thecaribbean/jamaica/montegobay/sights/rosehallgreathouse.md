# Rose Hall Great House

*Centralamericathecaribbean > Thecaribbean > Jamaica > Montegobay > Sights > Rosehallgreathouse*

Visit the legendary home of the infamous Annie Palmer. The "White Witch" of Jamaica. She is reported to have killed several unlucky husbands and brutalized her slaves until she herself was murdered. People swear they see her suffering ghost roaming the property. Beautifully restored and worth the visit.
