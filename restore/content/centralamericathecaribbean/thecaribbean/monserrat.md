# Monserrat

*Centralamericathecaribbean > Thecaribbean > Monserrat*

**A small volcanic island mainly inhabited by Irish settlers fleeing persecution, Monserrat is off the beaten Caribbean track. The main reason for this is that it's one of the few islands without good beaches. It does have beautiful hills, forests, rivers and waterfalls. It's a sort of tropical version of Ireland. Hiking, nature watching and climbing are favourite ways to spend the day on the island. **



**A big volcano eruption in 1998 has disrupted life on the island in a dramatic way. It might still take a while until everything is back to normal. **
