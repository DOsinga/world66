# Getting There

*Centralamericathecaribbean > Thecaribbean > Montserrat > Getting There*

Blackburne airport is covered by pyroclastic material from the volcano and impossible to use. However, from the 11th of July 2005 a new airport is operating at Gerald's. There are now several daily flights from Antigua and St. Maarten. NB The ferry service from Antigua has been discontinued.
