# Bequia

*Centralamericathecaribbean > Thecaribbean > Saintvincentandthegrenadines > Bequia*

Bequia is a tiny island measuring 7 square miles with a approximate population of 7000 people. It is part of the St.Vincent Grenadines and is surounded by the Caribbean Sea to the West and the Atlantic Ocean to the East. 

It is a quiet place, ideal for those needing a little peace and and tranquility.
