# Union Island

*Centralamericathecaribbean > Thecaribbean > Saintvincentandthegrenadines > Unionisland > Gettingthere > Union Island*

You need to choose a gateway into Union Island. San Juan, Puerto Rico is a hub for most flights from the USA and Canada. Carriers making connection with inter-island flights include Air Canada, Air Jamaica, American Airlines American Trans Air, BWIA, Continental, Delta Northwest, Tower Airlines, United and US Airways. Inter-island operators include LIAT, Caribbean Star and American Eagle.
