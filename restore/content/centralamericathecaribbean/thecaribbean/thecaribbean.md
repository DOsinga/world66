# The Caribbean

*Centralamericathecaribbean > Thecaribbean > Thecaribbean*

The Caribbean is named after the Carib Indians who inhabited most of the beautiful islands before Columbus' Santa Maria became the first cruise ship to enter the area. Many have followed and will continue to do so. 

The reason is obvious: this is a very beautiful area with white sandy beaches, cocotrees, great nighlife, happy and welcoming people. In the Caribbean you can find anything to make your holiday perfect. 

The bigger countries [Cuba](/centralamericathecaribbean/thecaribbean/cuba), [Haiti](/centralamericathecaribbean/thecaribbean/haiti), [Dominican Republic](/centralamericathecaribbean/thecaribbean/dominicanrepublic) and [Jamaica](/centralamericathecaribbean/thecaribbean/jamaica) are probably most interesting to visit from a cultural perspective. Unfortunately they are not the quiet paradises the others are.
