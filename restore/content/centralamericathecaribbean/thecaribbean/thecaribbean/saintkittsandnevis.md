With lush forests and a dormant volcano, St Kitts and it's sister island [Nevis](/centralamericathecaribbean/thecaribbean/saintkittsandnevis/nevis) are often called 'the secret [The Caribbean](/centralamericathecaribbean/thecaribbean).'   St Kitts was an British colony for hundreds of years, and the legacy lingers with afternoon tea and Cricket.
