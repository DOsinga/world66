# Castara Bay

*Centralamericathecaribbean > Thecaribbean > Trinidadandtobago > Tobago > Beaches > Castara Bay*

Castara Bay, on the Caribbean coast, is divided into two bays – *B**ig Bay*, just in front of Castara Village, and *Little Bay*, more secluded and great for snorkelling. Both are surrounded by lush vegetation.
