# When to Go in Boca Chica

*Centralamericathecaribbean > Theccribbean > Dominicanrepublic > Bocachica > When To Go*

This **Boca Chica when to go** guide tells interested visitors the best time to visit this town. Dominican Republic's Boca Chica is known as the Caribbean's best kept secret, because its sea houses the largest reef-protected bay known to man. People also love Boca Chica because of its laidback lifestyle, cheap shopping, good restaurants, pristine beaches and vibrant nightlife. Interestingly, Boca Chica has weather so good, that any time of the year is practically great to visit this sunny beach town.

**November to March**

This is Boca Chica's high season time. During these months the temperature is around 80 degrees Fahrenheit in the day, and usually drops by 10 degrees Fahrenheit at night. Obviously, at this time of the year, you will find a lot of tourists here. Thus, prices are automatically raised by guesthouses and hotels. If you want to experience weather which is not very warm, but on the cooler side, then visit Boca Chica during the months of February and March.

**April to October**

From April to October, Boca Chica experiences lesser crowds as this period falls outside the high season. Prices are lower, crowds are less and even the weather is not so sunny. In fact, during the months of September and October, Boca Chica receives heavy rain and sometimes even storms. This is also a good time to visit if you want to enjoy a good sense of privacy with the beach all to yourself. But, for this to happen, you must be lucky enough that it does not rain on that particular day!
