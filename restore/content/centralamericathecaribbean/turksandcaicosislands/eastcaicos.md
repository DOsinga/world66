# East caicos

*Centralamericathecaribbean > Turksandcaicosislands > Eastcaicos*

East caicos is an uninhabited island but is large in size (18 square miles). Swamps and mangroves inundate a majority of the island, you can find the highest point of the islands here. 

There are good beaches and it is fun to explore Jacksonville, a deserted city.
