Europe



The old world is really quite old. Every other town in Europe is packed with monuments dating back several centuries. Little villages were once capital the capital of a kingdom. The countries are small, but most of them were once the center of an empire that embraced the whole world.


Travel in Europe, especially in the rich North West, is not particularly rough or demanding, with the exception of the Balkan, Russia and the Caucasus. In Western-Europe things are well taken care of, it is easy to get from A to B, but all this comfort has a drawback: you pay for it. There are however ways of having a good time in Europe without spending too much. Take a train from Denmark to Switserland for less then 20 Euro with the whole family for example.
