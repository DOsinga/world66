# Rafting in Berat, Albania

*Europe > Albania > Day Trips > Rafting In Berat 2*

**One day rafting**

**Guide **

Travelers wishing to embark on the Osumi Canyon trip should arrive in the morning at Berat City. The drive to the Canyon is 60km and takes about an hour and a half. After arriving at the Canyon, the guides will assist you in preparation for the adventure. This trip will provide magnificent scenery as you float by the Canyon walls and will certainly make for a memorable experience. Eight waterfalls, a cathedral, the unspoiled nature, and the soothing sounds of running water combine to create a truly unique trip. A packaged lunch is prepared in advance and will be served under the blue waterfall. The river trip lasts about 1.5—5 hours, and depends largely on the water level.

Departure from the Canyons.

Itinerary permitting, some trip participants may opt to meet with a regatta team in Corovoda. The trip requires a minimum of 5 people, and can accommodate a maximum of 15 people.

**Two days rafting**

**Guide **

Day One

This two day itinerary allows for a night’s accommodations at the Castle Park Hotel and a more leisurely, direct departure from the hotel the following morning.

After arriving at the Hotel, take some time to relax and enjoy your historic, finely crafted quarters. You will be enchanted by the fresh scent of surrounding pines and the sound of songbirds. This tranquil environment is truly rejuvenating to the body, mind, and spirit. Enjoy a scrumptious traditional dinner prepared by our chef and served either indoors or out, depending on your preference. All your needs will be attended to by our courteous and professional staff.

Day Two

Awake refreshed and enjoy breakfast at the Hotel. Embark on the drive to the Canyon, which is 60km and takes about an hour and a half.

After arriving at the Canyon, the guides will assist you in preparation for the rafting adventure. This trip will provide magnificent scenery as you float by the Canyon walls and will certainly make for a memorable experience. Eight waterfalls, a cathedral, the unspoiled nature, and the soothing sounds of running water combine to create a truly unique trip. A packaged lunch is prepared in advance and will be served under the blue waterfall. The river trip lasts about 3—4 hours, and depends largely on the water level.

Departure from the Canyons.

Itinerary permitting, some trip participants may opt to meet with a regatta team in Corovoda. The trip requires a minimum of 5 people, and can accommodate a maximum of 15 people.
