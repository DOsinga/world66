# Budget Travel Ideas in Saranda

*Europe > Albania > Saranda > Budget Travel Idea*

If you are visiting **Saranda Budget Travel** ideas are something that you will want to look into. By budgeting yourself when you go on vacation you will spend less on vacation, but just because you spend less doesn’t mean that you will see less on your vacation. Many times people who follow various budget ideas find that they can see and do more on vacation because they have extra money.

**Budget Airlines**

Saving money on airfare is going to be one of the best things that you can do because your airfare is going to be the most expensive part of your trip. There are numerous things that you can do to save money on airfare, including booking your own flight. To find the lowest airfare you will want to search through the Internet, and you may even find some great last minute deals. One of the cheaper airlines that flies into Albania from various locations is AirOne, so be sure to check its price.

**Accommodations**
While staying in Saranda you will be limited to 2 choices, unless you elect to stay on the beach. Your first choice is staying in a hotel and your second choice is a hostel, but if you wish to stay on the beach, you can find a vacation rental for your stay. If you elect to stay in a hostel you will have plenty of choices, but one of the newest hostels is Backpackers SR or you can go with the Hairy Lemon, which was voted as the best hostel in 2008.

**Dining**
While in Albania one thing that you will notice is that the food is very inexpensive, which means you can eat out every night without having to worry about saving money. Perhaps the best part about eating out is that you can sample all of the interesting foods that Albania has to offer . While in Saranda, you will want to try Bourek, which is a fillo pie that is filled with meat and cheese or even spinach and cheese.

**Transportation**

To get to Saranda your best choice is to take the ferry; it is also one of the cheapest methods of travel to get to Saranda. You can also take the hydrofoil service, but it is quite a bit more expensive than the regular ferry. Buses and the mini bus are other cheap options for traveling to Saranda. Once inside Saranda you will want to take taxicabs, but do so in groups because you will be able to split the fare.

**Excursions**

The cheapest excursion for travelers in Saranda is the beach because it costs nothing. However, while on vacation you are not going to want to go strictly to the beach. Traveling to nearby Corfu for a day is another great excursion; while there make sure you go out on a half-day cruise or even make a stop at Sissi's Palace.
