# The Adriatic Coast

*Europe > Albania > Top 5 Must Dos > The Adriatic Coast*

One of the greatest assets of Albania is its Adriatic coastline, the water and beaches being very clean by Mediterranean norms.

The Adriatic is the coast with the large, white sand beaches and safe, shallow water for swimming. The beach season is open and you can swim in the sea from approx. the beginning of May to mid October. The Albanian Adriatic coast is being touted as the next Croatia among adventurous beach-seekers.

The main beach resort on the Adriatic Sea is: Mali i Robit
Mali i Robit is one of the most popular beaches in the country. It is located only 25 km west of the Tirana airport and 15 km south of Durrës. It features gorgeous white sand beaches, breathtaking sea views, and the shade of pine forests. The resort attracts a mixture of Albanian and international tourists and is growing in popularity every year.
