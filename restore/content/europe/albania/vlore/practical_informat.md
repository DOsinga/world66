**.* Will English get you out of trouble?***

**YES,** with anyone under 30. With older people try Italian and your chances of getting around will increase dramatically, well, virtually everyone in town speaks Italian anyway.

**. *Where to stay?***

**-**Anything along the coast would be a great idea. It should not take you long to scout around for a hotel/hostal. Most of them will have access to a semi private cove/beach and restaurant/bar and offer great hospitality.

. ***Can you pay in Euros?***

**YES,** and you will be very welcome. Although, if you need to stop by and grab a bite or drink you had better have some LEK change, which in most cases your hotel staff will be able to facilitate at a daily exchange rate.

. ***What is public transport like?***

A major NO-NO. But, there is a pretty convenient mini van service which you can stop on the road and which will drive you anywhere around the local coast.
