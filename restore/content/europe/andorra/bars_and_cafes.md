# Bars and Cafes in Andorra

*Europe > Andorra > Bars And Cafes*

Nestled in the Pyrenees, Andorra is not such a sleepy town as many assume, and it gives way to many **Andorra la Vella bars and cafes **for both tourists and locals to relax. There are pubs, bars and cafes for both the young and old to hang out and for those looking to spend a quiet time with friends.

#### **Ambit**

Ambit is one of the liveliest and most fashionable places. The two-story club is found in Erts, just 15 minutes away from the main town. The concept of the club is of a typical suburban house having a house party as seen in its modest exteriors. The club has a DJ to play live music, several lounging areas, smoking areas and pool tables. Ambit is a part of a string of clubs found in the same area.

#### **Buda Bar**

Another popular place is Buda Bar which is more of a lounge club which serves Andorran-Mediterranean food. This bar plays house and electro music with its in-house DJ Gustavo Sosa. Sosa is a well-known spinner in these parts, and it is to him the popularity of this bar is majorly credited. The top floor is bare for those who want to dance the night away. Buda Bar is one of the top resto-bars in the area but also one of the most expensive. When you&rsquo;re there, get ready to shell out as much as $30 for drinks.
