# Eating Out in Andorra

*Europe > Andorra > Eating Out*

**Visit Els dos Ca&ccedil;adors Restaurant in Andorra la Vella
**

There are several **Andorra**** la Vella restaurants** that are popular with both tourists and locals alike. One of the better ones is the Els dos Ca&ccedil;adors restaurant on Pla&ccedil;a Reb&eacute;s, right in the center of Andorra la Vella.

#### **Atmosphere**

The restaurant is situated inside a marvelous stone building and tries to imitate the look of a hunting lodge. In fact "Els dos Ca&ccedil;adors" means "The Two Hunters" in Catalan. As you enter the restaurant, you&rsquo;ll see a large bar surrounded by many bar stools and a dining room with a total of 15 tables. The atmosphere is warm, and the interior is lovely, although the stone walls make it a bit dark. The staff is helpful and friendly.

#### **Cuisine**

Els dos Ca&ccedil;adors is known for its incredible cuisine. The restaurant serves many Spanish and French dishes and also offers traditional Andorran cuisine. If you want to experience the latter, order Trinxat as a starter. It is a cake made of potatoes and cabbage. Escudella, stewed chicken, sausages and meatballs, is a good choice for a main dish. Try Coques for dessert, which are cakes made of wheat flour, sugar and eggs. You can complement the dishes with some excellent local wine or tasty Andorran beer.
