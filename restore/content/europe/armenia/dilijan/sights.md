***HAGHARTSIN restaurant-hotel-bar complex*** is
located in a wonderful place of Dilijan, among picturesque mountains
covered with colorful woods. There’s a large guarded car and bus
parking.
There
are 22 comfortable rooms in the hotel, with large double/twin beds,
satellite TV and luxury SPA/massage shower cabins. The restaurant part
of the complex consists of modern and stylish restaurant hall (160
seats), dining rooms (32 seats), a bar, open-air cafe(110 seats) and 9
cozy little houses(144 seats): 9 closed type and 4 open type, exposed
to fresh mountain air. Also there is a conference hall (60 seats) with
large plasma screen and satellite TV and a VIP-room for business
meetings (10 seats).

national cuisine.
