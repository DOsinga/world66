# Lori Marz Travel Guide

*Europe > Armenia > Lori Marz*

The best resource for sights, hotels, restaurants, bars, what to do and see

The Lori region of Armenia is in the very north, in the center of
Armenia's border with Georgia. The three four main geographic features
are the valley that Vanadzor - Armenia's third largest city lies in,
the Debed Rivier gorge, the valley that Stepanavan lies in, and the
mountains along the northern border, surrounding Mt. Lalvar.

Lori
is without a doubt one of the richest areas to visit in terms of
tourist attractions and natural splendor. The Debed River canyon is
full of things to see, and forms a beautiful backdrop for all the
sites. It's hard not to imagine it teeming with activity 1,000 years
ago, when most of the greatest monuments and monasteries were built.
