# Yeghegnadzor Travel Guide

*Europe > Armenia > Vayots Dzor Marz > Yeghegnadzor*

The best resource for sights, hotels, restaurants, bars, what to do and see

The regional capital, Yeghegnadzor is a nice small town in the heart of the region, just off the main highway leading south from Yerevan.

The town itself is not remarkable, but it can make a good base for exploring the region.
