# Cheers Pub Yerevan

*Europe > Armenia > Yerevan > Nightlifeandente > Cheers Pub Yerevan 1*

Cheers is the best hang-out for tourists, ex-pats, diasporan and local Armenians. It is open now in a new location on 3, Abovyan Street, AOKS Building.

The music varies from classic rock, reggae, funky and soul to ethnic Greek, Gypsy and Armenian. Drinks include a wide range of local and international beers and spirits. As for food, it's limited within sandwiches and fries.
