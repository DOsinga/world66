# Relax

*Europe > Armenia > Yerevan > Nightlifeandente > Relax*

Relax moved to its new home at the basement of the new build "Citadel Bussines Centre". It remains one of the most popular clubs.

It looks like a brand new western club with a huge bar and excelent sound, but it kept its style as the core of the night-life in Yerevan.

Very good resident and guest DJs, good food and you can really have a good party in there. Like all disco's in Yerevan, if you come in and it seems empty, there's probably a party going on somehere else (try Astral or Club One), but otherwise this is the place to be. starts at 22:00/23:00 and dies at around 3. Most people go home or to Club One. Visitors are the the new rich, Yuppies and expats.
