# Day Trips

*Europe > Austria > Day Trips*

For a more adventurous day, make a trip out to Tyrol, Austria to do some white water rafting with Feel Free Rafting. This amazing rafting company teaches you all the basics for a true rafting experience through the Alps of Austria. The guides are very professional and experienced. Whether you have a large group, or a family of four, this is a must-do trip!
