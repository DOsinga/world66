### Tokyo

Since 2003 Tokyo is the place to be in the innercity for lovers of
Japanese food. Sushi in a modern, but straightforward way. A 25 Meter
long Assembly-line
delivers Sushi to the guest in an "all you can eat" setting. Spanish,
Italia and Austrian wines are on the menu. The a la Carte courses are
worth a special mention as is the Bento-Box, a lunchyime favorite.

type: Japanese
