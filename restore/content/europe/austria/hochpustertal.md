# Hochpustertal

*Europe > Austria > Hochpustertal*

It is one of the most beautiful high valleys in East Tirol close to the
South Tirolean Dolomites. The resorts of Sillian, Heinfels, Strassen,
Abfaltersbach, Anras,Ausservillgraten, Kartitsch, Ober- and UnterTilliach are located at an elevation
of between 950 m and 1,400 m.

The region's holiday offer is as varied as its landscape. Tennis,
paragliding, mountain biking, cycling, swimming, cross-border hikes,
horse-drawn carriage rides, culture and tradition.

Choose from various accommodation types: hotels, farmhouses, inns, camp sites, etc.
