# Bars and Cafes in Schladming

*Europe > Austria > Schladming > Bars And Cafes*

Following their slogan &ldquo;Gem&uuml;tlichkeit&rdquo; which translates to the English word &ldquo;coziness,&rdquo; most **Schladming Bars and Cafes** offer just that for their customers. Even If Schladming is considered a mountain village, one does not have to worry about where to hang out and have a good meal. There are several options for every tourist and traveler.

#### **Caf&eacute;-Hotel Perner**

This small family-run caf&eacute; and hotel is nestled in the Rohrmoos Plateau. The outdoor caf&eacute; serves buffet breakfast for its guests and has a pleasant view of the surrounding trees and walking paths. In the winter, it is an ideal stop after a long day of skiing in the slops.

#### **Das Kleine Caf&eacute;**

For those addicted to football, visit Das Kleine Caf&eacute; where one can check on some football action on television and discuss or argue with other football fans. One can also dine al fresco or sip on that favorite latte macchiato, espresso or milch and do some people-watching on the quiet streets.

#### **Marias Mexican**

Like the name implies, Marias Mexican is a Mexican-inspired bar that plays live music almost all days of the week. It brings one of the largest crowds of tourists and locals alike. They serve Corona and Sol with plenty of light cocktails and something to munch on such as nachos and cheese, quesadillas and soft tacos for those who would rather eat than drink. They also serve wine.

#### **Siglu Bar**

There are plenty of things happening at Siglu all days of the week. On Mondays and Thursdays, one can look forward to live music from different bands. Tuesday includes runs of 80's and 90's music, apt for those who want to reminisce over Jagermeister. On Wednesdays, soon after midnight, there is a "Buy one, Take one" deal on drinks. Fridays and Saturdays are when things really kick off in the place. Fridays are Ladies Night, and Saturdays are ideal to hang out with friends because drinks are cheap. Don&rsquo;t forget Sunday, considered as their "Austropop 4Ever Day," which is an ideal time for tourists to come in droves and listen to Austria&rsquo;s pop music scene.

#### **X-Small Bar**

Simply put, the X-Small Bar is not a party bar. It is more of a hangout place where one can enjoy some of the best collection of Austrian wines to complement their equally tasty pizzas.
