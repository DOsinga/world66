# Shopping in Schladming

*Europe > Austria > Schladming > Shopping*

A big part of any vacation is going shopping and in **Schladming, Shopping** is something that cannot be missed. Shopping in Schladming is always interesting because of the numerous shops that are located throughout town.

**Blue Tomato**

If you are looking for any kind of ski or snowboarding equipment this is one of the best places to go. Not only do they carry some of the best equipment around, but they also sell a wide variety of clothing apparel. Not only can you find everything you need for skiing or snowboarding in the shop, but you can also find stuff for surfing. The store is located in the center of Schladming close to the Hauptplatz.

**Candlelight Shopping**

You can only experience this special shopping experience on Thursdays from December 18 to the end of the Christmas season. Candles light up the entire city center, which helps add to the relaxed atmosphere. All of the shops that participate in Candlelight shopping keep their doors open until 9 p.m. One unique feature is that rather than walking or taking a taxi from store to store you can enjoy a free horse drawn sleigh ride from storefront to storefront.

**Ski Lenz**

Being that Schladming is a major ski resort town it only makes sense that there are numerous ski shops located throughout town. Ski Lentz is one of the better ski shops in town because they can handle just about anything that comes their way and they are located directly on the slopes, so if something happens while you are skiing you can get it fixed right away. This ski shop also offers rental skis and boats that are in the best possible condition.

**LIBRO Handelsgesellschaft**

This bookstore is located in the Hauptplatz region of Schladming. If you are interested in finding books on local history or even new releases, this is the place to come. Not only do they sell books, but they also offer CDS and DVDs for you to purchase. This store offers a little bit of stuff for everybody in the group, so be sure to check it out.

**Klieber Josef**

If you are looking for a great place to buy shoes or a nice handbag this is the best place to go in Schladming. One of the best things about shopping in this store is how well the personnel are trained, but their selection is also outstanding. Not only do they have a huge selection of shoes and bags, they are all competitively priced. In terms of shoes you can find something for the whole family or something for any occasion.
