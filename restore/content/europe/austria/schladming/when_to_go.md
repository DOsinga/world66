# When to Go in Schladming

*Europe > Austria > Schladming > When To Go*

**Schladming Attracts Tourists throughout the Year**

Considering a visit to Schladming, your **Schladming when to go** decision must conform with a couple of things. First of all, you should inform yourself on the local climate and see if the weather will suit your requirements. Second, you should see what the different tourist seasons offer in terms of activities, events and sightseeing.

#### **Climate Overview**

Schladming is a mountain town, situated at an elevation of almost 800 meters, and it experiences an alpine climate. This climate is characterized by fresh summers and well-expressed winters. The summers are warm, but not too hot, and have long sunny days with up to 18 hours of daylight. Rains fall no more than 6 days per month. The winters always have negative temperatures, with the average values being around -5 degrees Celsius (23 degrees Fahrenheit). The precipitation at this time of the year is always in the form of snow.

#### **Winter Activities**

The winter season usually starts by the end of November and lasts until March. Thanks to the weather conditions, Schladming has turned into a major ski resort in the area. If you like winter sports, you should visit during this time of the year. Schladming offers some excellent ski runs, suitable both for beginners and advanced skiers. Those who have never experienced skiing could learn at one of the several ski schools in town. Ski jumping and ski trekking are also available.

#### **Summer Activities**

The summer season starts from mid-May and lasts to mid-September. In the summer, Schladming attracts visitors with its hiking opportunities and biking trails. The nice, cool weather makes for a pleasant experience. There are over 1000 kilometers of hiking and biking trails in the surrounding area, suitable both for professionals and beginners.

#### **Events**

Various events are held in Schladming throughout the year. Most notable are the Mountain Bike Championship in June, the World Ski Championship in February and the Retro Cars Parade in July.

#### **Sights**

Schladming also has some lovely sights. The best time to see them is from April to October, when there is no snow and you can have a clear view of them. The weather will be also good enough for you to walk on foot from sight to sight.
