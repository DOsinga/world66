# Musicfestival Steyr

*Europe > Austria > Steyr > Events > Musicfestivalsteyr*

The Musicfestival takes an important part of cultural life in Steyr. This festival differtitates from the various festivals in Upper Austria, because in Steyr young artist who are at the begtinning of their carrer, can show their skills to an interested audience. In the year 2000 the shocking muical "Rocky Horror Picture Show" was played in the Old Theatre.
