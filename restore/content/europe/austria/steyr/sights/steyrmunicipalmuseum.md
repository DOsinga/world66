# Steyr municipal museum

*Europe > Austria > Steyr > Sights > Steyrmunicipalmuseum*

The Innerberger Stadel, a two-gable storehouse lavishly decorated with sgraffitto and built between 1611 and 1613, is used as municipal museum today. The exhibitions mainly focus on iron processing. Scythe hammer, nail smithy, a collection of knives and of rifles produced by the Steyrer Industriellen Werndl remind of the former heyday of this town of iron. Ethnological collections, e.g. the "Lambergschen Krippenfiguren". Opening hours: April - October: Tue - Sun 10am - 3pm, November - March: Wed - sun 10am - 3pm, guided tours on advance booking.
