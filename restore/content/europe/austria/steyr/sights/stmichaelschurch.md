# St. Michael's Church

*Europe > Austria > Steyr > Sights > Stmichaelschurch*

This church is situated on the left bank of the river Steyr, opposite "Zwischenbrücken". Built as a Jesuit Church in 1635. To the left, in front of the church: former hospital church with former hospital (Bürgerspital). Endowed by Queen Elisabeth - early 14th century. In the near of christkindl-world.
