# The Four Bells

*Europe > Austria > Vienna > Nightlife > The Four Bells*

An Irish pub located in the 4th district near the ring and naschmarkt. They offer a wide range of beer food and musik. There is also a good quiz night on Mondays (should book in advance) and a karaoke night downstairs on Thursdays.

Its a great place to watch football (soccer), they televise games from all major European leagues and European competitions.
