# Steffl Department Store Vienna

*Europe > Austria > Vienna > Shopping > Steffl Department*

The Steffl Department Store Vienna is centrally located on Vienna's oldest shopping street Kärntner Straße in the 1st district, right around the corner from the famous St. Stephen's Cathedral.
The department store offers Vienna's greatest selection of international designer fashion brands. This includes women's fashion, men's fashion and children's fashion from designer like Armani, Alexander McQueen, Burberry, Hugo Boss, Tommy Hilfiger, Just Cavalli, Jimmy Choo, Valentino, Just Cavalli, Polo Ralph Lauren and many more.
On its 9 floors "Steffl" also offers a wide selection of designer bags, accessories, lingerie, make-up and a whole floor for men's and women' shoes.
The "Skybar" bar, cafe & restaurant on the top floor offers a great way to relax after an extensive shopping trip and boast an exceptional, amazing view, overlooking Vienna's city center.
