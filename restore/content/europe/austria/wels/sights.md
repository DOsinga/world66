**Minoritenkloster Wels (monastery)**

The Welser Polheimer congregation founded thie monastery in 1280, 20 years later they started building the church, the original choir preserved. This year the city opened an attached museum where exhibitions and cultural events are held. 

fax: +43 (0)7242-47904

email: office@tourism-wels.at

address: Minoritenplatz, A-4600 Wels

tel: +43 (0)7242-43495

**Lederer**

 Of the four medieval town gates only the Lederer tower preserved (the others were destroyed in the 19th century), no tours available.  

address: Stadtplatz 34, A-4600 Wels
