# Bars and Cafes in Zell am See

*Europe > Austria > Zellamsee > Bars And Cafes*

If you are on holiday in Zell am See, you must step into one of the
several **Zell am See Bars and Cafés **to experience what this
Austrian district has to offer its visitors.

**Latini Hotel-Bar Uno**

This is one of the most famous hot spots in Zell am See. The bar at
Latini Hotel has a comfortable and warm atmosphere where you can enjoy a
cold glass of beer, wine or cocktail. Drinks are available at
discounted prices everyday from 4 p.m. to 6 p.m. If you feel like showing
off your singing capabilities, you can do so during the karaoke nights.

**Villa Crazy Daisy Bar**

The on-site café at this restaurant is worth visiting. The bar at
Crazy Daisy is where you can chill out and party with your friends. The
bar has a wide selection of beers and wines. Every night the bar
provides live entertainment from local bands and DJs. If you don’t like
dancing, you can go to Crazy Daisy’s Beer Heaven where you can sit back
and enjoy a few ice cold beers.

**George's Bar**

George’s Bar is located within the premises of the St George Hotel,
located in Schiller Straße in Zell am See. The bar has a cozy laid back
atmosphere and is a perfect place where you can enjoy the afternoon
sipping your coffee or even a glass of wine or beer. All the wine comes
from the bar’s own walk-in wine cellar. If you are interested, you can
go on a mini-tour of the wine cellar and even taste a few wines while
you are there.

**Our’s Lounge Café, Bistro and Bar**

Our’s Lounge Café, Bistro and Bar is set up right in the center of
Zell am See. The café is a nice clean place with a comfortable
atmosphere. The café serves breakfast, lunch and dinners as well as
light snacks, cocktails, cognac, brandy, wines and coffee and tea.
You might want to try the fine cigars available at the café before
leaving.

**Eichenhof Restaurant and Terrassen Café**

This restaurant and café is located 10 minutes from the city center of
Zell am See. The Eichenhof Restaurant and Terrassen Café not only offer
the best Austrian and International cuisine but as well as the best
panoramic view of the Lake Zell. This is the best place to unwind and
enjoy Zell am See. Step into this fine café and enjoy a nice hot cup of
coffee after a tour of the town.
