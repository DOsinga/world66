# Family Travel Ideas in Zell am See

*Europe > Austria > Zellamsee > Family Travel Idea*

A great option for a family vacation experience is a trip to Zell am See. The majestic scenery is the backdrop for an opportunity to be physically invigorated, while learning a life-changing skill.

Zell am See is nestled in the central Alps and lies geographically
near the city of Salzburg and east of Kitzbuhel. Even more
dynamically, the majestic Dientener Berge mountain range lifts upwards and proportionately raises the inspiration level of those
who gaze upon this natural wonder.

**Skiing**

Zell am See presents an excellent opportunity to
indulge in a wonderful ski vacation. In fact, given the altitude and
the presence of glaciers, a skiing vacation can be experienced all year
round, with the exception of the peak summer months.

**Family Friendly**

What makes family travel in Zell am See so appealing
is that the focus of the area is on families as well. There are a number of ski slopes that are conducive to various
levels of skiing experience, ranging from beginner to expert. In
addition, there are many skiing schools that offer a quality experience
for individuals to learn how to ski or polish skills.

**Winter Wonderland**

Zell
am See is also noted for other wonderful winterland sports. Some include the age-old sport of
tobogganing, or sliding down the various tobogganing slopes with the use
of a sled. Nestled in between the peaks of this
mountainous area is Lake Thumersbach, which freezes over during the
winter months. This allows for an
individual to lace up a pair of ice skates and skate on the lake, while
enjoying the seduction and awesomeness of creation.

In addition, there are many other pleasures that the family
unit can enjoy while vacationing at Zell am See. These include sightseeing excursions, dining opportunities that
provide a quality taste of the local fare and shopping outings.
