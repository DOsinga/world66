# Books

*Europe > Belarus > Books*

There is not a great selection of books about Belarus, but we have tried to include a good variety. However, if you want to buy books once you get to Belarus, there are plenty. There are some good bookstores around Minsk, not to mention many stands where many different kinds of books are sold. 

 **Central Bookstore**

 Skoriny 19, tel. (+375-17) 227 49 18/226 10 52. Open 10:00 - 15:00, 16:00 - 19:00, Sat 10:00 - 15:00, 16:00 - 18:00. Closed Sun.  This bookstore has the largest selection of Russian and Belarusian books in Minsk. There are also a few souvenirs and even posters of the President.
