**Het Oerwoud**

"Het Oerwoud" meaning The Jungle is a easy to find bar in the center of the city near the river "Schelde". Like most bars it is open untill the early morning hours. Many locals use it as a place to meet and start a long night out. The kitchen is open all night and the home made tomate soup is [more..](/europe/belgium/antwerp/nightlife/cafes/hetoerwoud)

**Het Paeters Vaetje**

Small pub with little mezzanine floor. Like in every real Flemish pub you can get something surprisingly tasty to eat at any time. Furthermore, this cafe has over 15O kinds of beers!

address: Left from the cathedral tower

openinghours: every day till morning
