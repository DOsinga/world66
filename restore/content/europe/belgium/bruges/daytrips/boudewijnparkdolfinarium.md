# Boudewijnpark & Dolfinarium

*Europe > Belgium > Bruges > Daytrips > Boudewijnparkdolfinarium*

Boudewijnpark & Dolfinarium, A. Debaeckestraat 12, Sint-Michiels : attractive family theme park with lots of games for children, ice rink, giant wheel, horror-castle, rollerskate rink, midget golf, etc.. At the Dolfinarium (1.600 seats), playful dolphins and sea-lions offer a fascinating show. 

 The panoramic aquarama allows spectators to have a look under the water surface.. 

 Bus nr. 7 and/or 17 St.-Michiels (boarding point: Biekorf)). 

 By car : via Koning Albertlaan.
