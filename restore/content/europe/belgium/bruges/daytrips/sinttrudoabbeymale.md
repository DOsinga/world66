# Sint-Trudo Abbey Male

*Europe > Belgium > Bruges > Daytrips > Sinttrudoabbeymale*

Sint-Trudo Abbey Male : former castle of the Counts of Flanders, with a 14th-15th century keep. Restored and enlarged from 1952 to 1972. Today convent of the canonesses of the Holy Sepulchre. Near the Abbey is the former town hall of Male (the 'Hooghe Huus") and an ancient pillory ("pelderijn").
