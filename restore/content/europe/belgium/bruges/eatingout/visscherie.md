# Visscherie

*Europe > Belgium > Bruges > Eatingout > Visscherie*

At the nice and small square called Huidevettersplein there are many good restaurants including the Duc De Bourgogne (very classy, very expensive), Mossselkelder (if you like mussles), 't Huidevettershuis (nice) ane the Vischerie (fish) neither of them is cheap. Expect about €60,- for a menu.
