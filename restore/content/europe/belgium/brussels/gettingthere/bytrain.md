# By Train

*Europe > Belgium > Brussels > Gettingthere > Bytrain*

The train too is an easy means to travel to Flanders. Most international railway lines link Brussels and Antwerp to several other major European cities. The European high speed train project, which not only includes Thalys and Eurostar but also the Eurotunnel (or Chunnel as it is also called, linking the continent to the UK) aims to operate links between London, Paris, Lille, Brussels, Antwerp, Liège, Cologne and Düsseldorf. In view of its favourable geographic location, our country will be a most important hub for this European high speed train-network. The extension of the Thalys-network as far as Oostende, means cities like Brugge and Gent can now be reached in a mimimum of time and with optimum comfort.
