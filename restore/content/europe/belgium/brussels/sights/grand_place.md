# Grand Place

*Europe > Belgium > Brussels > Sights > Grand Place*

The most famous and finest square in the Lower Town area. After a
bombardment in 1695, the city hall was the only building that remained
standing. The guild houses have their own specific frontage but they
are all in complete harmony with each other.
