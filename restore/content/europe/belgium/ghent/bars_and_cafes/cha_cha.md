# Het Oeverloze Eiland

*Europe > Belgium > Ghent > Bars And Cafes > Cha Cha*

If you´re in for a cup of tea served the way it should be, this is the place to be. They have a large variety of coffee and a lot of other quality stuff. Prices are very reasonable. The ambience is different on the three floors you can choose from. The name means 'the island without shores'.
