# Cha Cha

*Europe > Belgium > Ghent > Bars And Cafes > Cha Cha 1*

With a dark-exotic interior in a small house in the Steendam, this is the place for wine lovers. They have great food too, although a bit pricy (around 15 euro). Close to the Charlatan, great for warming up (you've got to be sober to apreciate the wine anyway).
