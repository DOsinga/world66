### [Griekse Snack](/europe/belgium/ghent/eating_out/griekse_snack)

Big food for little money. Simple, quick and tasty, all you need if you aren't looking for a romantic dinner. Recommended: the Retsina menu, a Greek "sampler" with a drink included.
