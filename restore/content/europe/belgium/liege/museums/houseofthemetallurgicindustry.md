# House of the metallurgic industry

*Europe > Belgium > Liege > Museums > Houseofthemetallurgicindustry*

The museum is housed in a former industrial edifice. The focus is on the industrial development of the area. There is a reconstruction of an authentic 19th century forge as well as a portrait of a m14th century steel workshop. Different ferro items (the famous zinc bathtub of Napoleon). Another section is about the evolution of 'energy-creation': (the Marly machine, the dynamo of Zenobebebebe Gramme). There is also a separate section about information technology.
