# 23 Unforgettable Days in Bosnia and Herzegovina

*Europe > Bosniaandherzegovina > Books > 23 Unforgettable D*

The collection of over 700 photographs contained in this book was taken on my journeys through Bosnia and Herzegovina and portrays a part of the natural beauties of this interesting country and its versatile landscapes.

The photographs have been grouped into 23 sections representing 23 days, which is at least how long it would take you to repeat these journeys. In each section you will find a map and suggestions for unforgettable outings.

If, like me, you enjoy untouched nature, unusual flora and fauna, fresh air, mountains, lakes, rivers and visiting old towns, then you are sure to find something of interest in this book.

Content:
01 - Mostar
02 - Blagaj and Podvelez
03 - Stolac and Pocitelj
04 - Wine road and Medjugorje
05 - Drezanka and Rujiste
06 - Prenj
07 - Rafting on the Neretva River (Konjic)
08 - Sarajevo
09 - Sarajeva surroundings
10 - Bjelasnica (Lukomir)
11 - Nature Park Bijambare and surroundings (Olovo, Vares)
12 - Prokosko jezero (lake) and Fojnica
13 - Treskavica
14 - The region of Upper Drina (NP Sutjeska, Gorazde, Visegrad)
15 - Srebrenica
16 - Old towns (Doboj, Tesanj, Gradacac, Srebrenik, Maglaj, Vranduk)
17 - Nature Park Tajan and Kamenica
18 - Travnik and Vlasic
19 - Jajce and Plivska jezera (lakes) (Vrbas, Banja Luka)
20 - Sana and Sanica
21 - National park Una
22 - Rafting on the Una River (Bihac)
23 - Bosanska Krupa and Bosanska Otoka

This book is bilingual on English and Bosnian.
