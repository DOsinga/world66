# Getting Around in Bosnia and Herzegovina

*Europe > Bosniaandherzegovina > Getting Around*

Sarajevo documentary film

This is documentary through which you will be introduced to the
history of our town, which is over 500 years old - from times of the
Founder of Sarajevo, Isa-bey Ishakovic . Apart from the footage from the
archives, 100 years old, computerized reconstruction has also been used
of certain parts of the town that today do not exist anymore.

This film is available in following languages:

Bosnian, English, German, French and Italian.
