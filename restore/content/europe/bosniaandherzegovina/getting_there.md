# Getting There

*Europe > Bosniaandherzegovina > Getting There*

Want to visit Bosnia & Herzegovina?! No problem! If you prefer traveling by airplane, there are four international airports in Bosnia & Herzegovina: Sarajevo, Banja Luka, Mostar and Tuzla. Airports are renovated and they are very modern just like the other in London and Paris. BH Airlines is official airline of Bosnia & Herzegovina. On the other hand, if you like rail traffic, there is very good rail system in hole country. You can come by rail from every european country to Bosnia & Herzegovina. By bus you can come by really comfortable drive, from almost every european country. But a real adventure is traveling by car, especially through Bosnia & Herzegovina! Beautiful scenery that can be seen only in Bosnia & Herzegovina are breath-taking. Also good route system in this country provides you more pleasure.
