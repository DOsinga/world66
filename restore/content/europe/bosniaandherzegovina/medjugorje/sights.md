### [Medjugorje](/europe/bosniaandherzegovina/medjugorje/sights/medjugorje_2)

The name Medjugorje means (area between two mountains.)

Medjugorje is one of the villages of the Herzegovina municipality of
Citluk or you can say of historic Brotnjo.The territory of Medjugorje
has a mild Mediterranean climate and spreads out at the altitude of 150
meters above sea level.

The parish of Medjugorje was founded in 1892 and also placed under
the protection of St. James, patron of pilgrims. Five years later, the
old parish church of St. James in Medjugorje was

completed.The church was completed and blessed on 19.1.1969. So there
