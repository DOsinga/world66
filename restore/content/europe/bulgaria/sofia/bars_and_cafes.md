# Bars and Cafes in Sofia

*Europe > Bulgaria > Sofia > Bars And Cafes*

If you are visiting Sofia, you will be pleased to know that there are several **Sofia Bars and Cafes** where you can enjoy a good drink. There are over 300 bars, clubs and cafes in Sofia, all offering delicious dishes to suit different pockets. If you are looking to have a good time while in Sofia, these bars and caf&eacute;s might interest you.

**Sin City**

Sin City is the most popular club in Old Town. Comprised of three big rooms with diverse music equipment ranging from Bulgarian folk music to modern songs, this club is vibrant and always with buzzing atmosphere. This is a place where you can party and enjoy drinks till late night.

**Toba & Co Bar **

Toba & Co is one of the most popular bars in the city. It is located near the National Art Gallery. It is an ideal destination for visitors to enjoy evening drinks. Toba & Co turns into a cocktail bar at night and people can be seen dancing till late night. You can enjoy music ranging from Turkish pop to jazz and watch gypsy dance here. Most bars in Sofia are excellent places to hang out with friends and have a good time. They are elegantly designed with comfy sofas for relaxation.

**Cabaret **

Cabaret is another bar worth mentioning. This bar mostly attracts Sofia&rsquo;s youth. It has a huge dance floor with wonderful lighting arrangements. The bar has DJs playing popular music every night. You might want to step into this place and enjoy the evening dancing.
