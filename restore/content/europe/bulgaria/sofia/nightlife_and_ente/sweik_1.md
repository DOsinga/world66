# Jack's Piano Bar

*Europe > Bulgaria > Sofia > Nightlife And Ente > Sweik 1*

**Jack`s** - the new piano bar is situated just next to The Theatre of Army at Rakovski Street (the ex `Maxim Club`). It was officially opened with a VIP party on 4 March, when many famous Bulgarian stars came to have fun.

The whole bar is panelled in cherry-tree and the grand piano, placed in the centre of the bar, is a real work of art. Soft light from the matt bracket lamps, candles on the tables, nice blues or rock music, plus the favourite Bulgarian rock songs from the recent past - all these things create the unique atmosphere oj Jack`s Piano Bar. Every night the famous musicians Hristo Lambrev and Georgy Danov perform their music on the piano and guitar.

The attractive girls behind the bar and the perfectly trained waitresses, dressed in the clothes of MONDO, serve the 80 places in this cozy place, where everybody could relax and enjoy themselves with friends. Famous actors, musicians, singers, businessmen and politicians can often be seen here.
