# Practical Information in Sofia

*Europe > Bulgaria > Sofia > Practical Informat*

This **Sofia practical information **is useful, especially if you are planning a visit to this destination. Sofia is considered to be a cultural, educational and economic center of Bulgaria. There are a lot of things to see in Sofia, but knowing something about the city makes your stay especially worthwhile. Here is some of the important information that you should know when you are in Sofia.

**Climate**

Sofia enjoys oceanic climate conditions. The coldest month is January, when temperatures can drop to 2.2 degrees Celsius. The hottest month in Sofia is July, when the temperature is around 25 degrees Celsius.

**Population**

Sofia has a population of 1,249,798, and a population density of about 926.5 per km. There are slightly more men than women; the ratio between the two sexes is about 1:1.10. The entire city has a birthrate of 12.3 per square mile, which is the reason why the city&rsquo;s population is increasing in a rapid rate. Moreover, the number of immigrants in the city is also high, which also adds to the current number of Sofia&rsquo;s population.

**Language**

The language in Sofia can be challenging, since it is Bulgarian. Bulgarian is made up of the Cyrillic alphabet, which was invented by the philosopher St. Constantine during the year 855 AD. For first timers, Bulgarian can be very tricky due to its many symbols. Getting around Sofia is easy, however, as there are a lot of people who can speak and understand English and the signboards are written using the English language.
