# Public bus

*Europe > Channelislands > Guernsey > Getting Around > Public Bus*

There's a public bus line going all around the island. This is a
prefect way of seeing more of Guernsey than just St Peter Port. The
trip takes about 1.5 hours. You can find time tables at the bus stops.
