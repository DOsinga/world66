# St Peter Port

*Europe > Channelislands > Guernsey > St Peter Port*

The main town, home to 15,000 people, is the home of the island's main
historic harbour, and is the centre of the island's social life.

The view of the town front from the harbour itself is renowned as one
of outstanding beauty. On a warm week in July St Peter Port turns into
a carnival, as the Town Festival takes over.

From town it is only twenty minutes on the bus out to the west coast for ice creams and a dip in the refreshing channel waters.
