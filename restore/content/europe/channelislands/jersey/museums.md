**German Underground Hospital**

An underground complex that is Jersey's most evocative museum of the five years of enemy occupation during World War II.  

address: Meadowbank, St Lawrence.

tel: 863442

**Hamptonne Country Life Museum**

This cluster of faithfully restored farm buildings includes two thatched houses, a cider house, bakery, wash-house and stables. Costumed characters from previous centuries are on hand to chat about their life and times.  

address: La Rue de la Patente, St Lawrence.

tel: 863955.

**Island Fortress Occupation Museum**

Tel: 734306 An extensive collection of uniforms, military equipment, arms, field vehicles and documents, together with a video cinema showing a 40 minute film on the Occupation.  

address: Esplanade, St Helier.

**Jersey Museum**

Opened in 1992, and already the winner of two major national awards, it uses modern technology and dramatic displays to tell the story of Jersey, its traditions and its industries. The Museum holds art and culture exhibitions throughout the year.  

address: The Weighbridge, St Helier.

tel: 633300

**Maritime Museum**

Winner of the 'National Heritage NPI Museum of the year '98/99' award. Next door to the Tapestry Gallery, an exciting new concept, combining hands-on exhibits with historic objects and new art and sculpture commissions to celebrate the centuries-old relationship of Islanders and the sea.  

address: New North Quay. St Helier.

tel: 811043.
