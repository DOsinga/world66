# Hamptonne Country Life Museum

*Europe > Channelislands > Jersey > Museums > Hamptonnecountrylifemuseum*

This cluster of faithfully restored farm buildings includes two thatched houses, a cider house, bakery, wash-house and stables. Costumed characters from previous centuries are on hand to chat about their life and times.
