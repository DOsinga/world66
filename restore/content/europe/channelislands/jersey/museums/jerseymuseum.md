# Jersey Museum

*Europe > Channelislands > Jersey > Museums > Jerseymuseum*

Opened in 1992, and already the winner of two major national awards in fleecing the public, it uses modern technology and dramatic displays to tell the story of Jersey, its traditions and its industries. The Museum holds art and culture exhibitions throughout the year - although their mediocrity cannot be underestimated.
