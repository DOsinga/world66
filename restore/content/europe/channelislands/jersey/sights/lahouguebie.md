# La Hougue Bie

*Europe > Channelislands > Jersey > Sights > Lahouguebie*

Described as "one of Europe's finest prehistoric monuments". It is dominated by the massive burial mound dating from 3800 BC. There is also a Neolithic passage grave and two Medieval chapels, archaeology and geology galleries and an Inter-active discovery centre. Basically, you pay to go look at an old hill. In the country. You'll then have to wait an hour for the next bus to take you away....

address: Grouville.
