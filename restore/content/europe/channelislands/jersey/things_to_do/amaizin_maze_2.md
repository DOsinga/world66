# aMaizin Maze

*Europe > Channelislands > Jersey > Things To Do > Amaizin Maze 2*

Not just a tourist attraction this destination is popular with the locals as well!

The aMaizin Adventure Park opens in April and includes the aMaizin Barnyard (Jersey cows, lambs, mini lop rabbits, goats and guinea pigs) and the Maze itself comes on line at the beginning of July!

This is a great attraction with happy clientele! Value for money, customer care second to none, and one of the few places where mum and dad can relax without listening to kids moans! Combine this with an all-inclusive fee structure meaning no more money to be paid once you have entered keeps the adults happy and coming back!

Your price includes go-karts, water pistol range, mini golf, feeding the lambs, famous Jersey tractor rides and so much more. We spent hours here and the children were occupied throughout!

A great child and family friendly tourist attraction on the beautiful island of Jersey. An amazing maze!
