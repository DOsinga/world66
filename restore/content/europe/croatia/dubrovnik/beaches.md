# Beaches

*Europe > Croatia > Dubrovnik > Beaches*

Stikovica is a magical spot, fantastic scenery and the real deal compared to the touristic Dubrovnik. It's a small beach where the locals hang out, very friendly and welcoming and a great bar right on the beach. Not a wild spot, but somewhere to go if you feel like rubbing shoulders with the locals. The bar doubles as a restaurant as well and the french fish there is amazing and excellent value.
