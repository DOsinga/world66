# Eating Out

*Europe > Croatia > Dubrovnik > Eating Out*

Food choices available are mostly sea food and Italian meals. Great for lovers of pizza!

### unknown!

The only Mexican restaurant that we could find was just outside the old city walls. Leave the old city by the smaller gate (that's Ploce Gate, not the one with the drawbridge). Follow the wall around the outside of the city, going uphill to the left. Look up above the high wall to your right, you'll see it peeking over the edge. The location isn't great, but Mexican food makes a welcome change after days of Italian food.
