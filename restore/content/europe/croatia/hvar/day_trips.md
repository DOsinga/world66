SEA KAYAKING:

1 day sea kayaking (9am – 2:30pm, light lunch included)
The starting point of the kayaking tour is the town of Hvar. You set off to the group of small islands called the Pakleni. The origin of the name goes back to old days when the islands were the main source of rosin for local shipbuilders. Today, just like then, the islands are covered with thick pine woods providing refreshing shade even during the hottest summer days. Besides the well-known
beach of Palmižana, the route through narrow straits and along the islands
reveals to you small hidden pebble beaches where you can enjoy the privilege
of having, at least temporarily, a beach of your own. Returning back to Hvar
around 2pm. Lunch in a restaurant on the beach.

Sunset sea kayaking tour - a perfect ending to the day (4pm-8pm)
Kayak into the sunset, stop in beautiful bays for last swim and glass of wine.

TASTE OF HVAR (WINE TASTING TOUR 6pm-?):

According to an ancient legend, when God distributed benefits, Dalmatians got grapevine... because, at a place where sun burns the rocks, where soil is thirsty, where rocky ground prevails, hardly any crop may survive. That is exactly Dalmatia and that is where grapevine found its homeland.
For as early as the 4th ctr. BC, the Ancient Greeks brought the grape vine to this sunny island and planted it in the fertile plain of the ancient town Pharos.
Throughout the centuries of rich and turbulent history, people on Hvar cultivated their vineyards with great skill and care, blessed with the lushness of Mediterranean. Grapes and wine gave them safety, food and medicine. They grew some of the finest varieties in this area, but the challenge still remains: how to turn this richness and harmony into great wines.
Tour starts at 6pm. After 30min drive we arrive to Jelsa, small town on the north coast of the island. It used to be one of the most significant maritime, shipbuilding and trade centres of the Adriatic, starting point for ocean-going sailing vessels carrying best wines of Hvar, olive oil and salted fish to far destinations all over the world.
We visit two significant local wine producers. Besides wine tasting you will have opportunity to spend the evening in one nice restaurant, so you can stay for the dinner. Beautiful and quiet ambience, nice choice of local wines and food will make your visit to Jelsa easy, relaxing and unforgettable experience…
