# Picena cove

*Europe > Croatia > Korcula > Beaches > Picena Cove*

The small beach is enclosed by alpine trees, sheltered and hidden by a rocky promontory extending into the bay. The beach is only accessible on foot or by boat, so it's rarely crowded
