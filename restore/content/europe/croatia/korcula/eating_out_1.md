### [Fresh](/europe/croatia/korcula/eating_out_1/fresh_korcula_1)

Friendly atmosphere, great affordable food that isn’t pasta or pizza, free book exchange, and cheap beer. Finally a place in Korcula that caters to budget travelers! Fresh serves a tasty takeaway menu of vegetarian and non-vegetarian wraps & real fruit smoothies for under 5$. Located across from Korcula Town's marina & bus station next to the half-buried pirate ship.

### [Restauran Konoba Grubinjac](/europe/croatia/korcula/eating_out_1/restauran_konoba_g)

Restaurant Grubinjac is a small place located at one of the most beautiful panorama spots around [Korcula Old Town](/europe/croatia/arhitecture_korcula.htm). It is created out of the old farm house on the way from Korcula to village Zrnovo.

The atmosphere here is rustic and rural and sitting on it's terrace feels like miles away from the busy Korcula Town. ### [Zure (Lumbarda)](/europe/croatia/korcula/eating_out_1/zure_lumbarda)

Winery and restaurant in Lumbarda (day or overnight trip from Korcula or 100kn each way in taxi).

Fantastic local produce - seafood but also local meat and game. Lobster soup lovely and rich, grilled quail lovely. Try (and buy) their own wine and aperitifs. Rated by Time Out as the best restaurant in Lumbarda and would have to agree.

type: Local
