# Sights

*Europe > Croatia > Korcula > Sights*

The most famous journalists and underwater photographer: Franco Banfi, describe the underwater world in Vela Luka as the most interesting of the whole of the Mediterranean, Aegean and Adriatic !! They have published many of their images on the website of Croatia Divers based on Korcula!
Croatia Divers is a friendly and professional English & Dutch 5 star PADI Gold Palm resort, BSAC resort and Mares dive center with more then 35 dive sites including the famous Blue Hole, Pirates Cove, Rockies island, Cathedral and the Red Gorgonian Forest. Croatia divers offers scuba diving on Korcula island in Vela Luka since 1988! They offer: Nitrox, Wall, Wreck, drift, Cave, Cavern, Deep and all PADI courses from beginner to PADI Pro!
