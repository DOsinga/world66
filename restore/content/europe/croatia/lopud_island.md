# Lopud island

*Europe > Croatia > Lopud Island*

The island of Lopud is located northwest of [Dubrovnik](/europe/croatia/dubrovnik) and is a part of the
Elafiti islands. It lies between Kolocep and Šipan The town of Lopud lies on northwest
side of the bay and has a view of Šipan.

The
island has a long history. You can find buildings dating back to Greek
and Roman times as well as from the heyday of the repuplic of
[Dubrovnik](/europe/croatia/dubrovnik).
