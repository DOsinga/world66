RAB SUMMER FIERA / MEDIEVAL FESTIVAL

Open air festival held on the streets of the oldest part in town - Kaldanac during 3 days, starting on 25th, ending on 27th July. Festival is a celebration of medieval life. Enjoy in the medieval costumes, crafts, and crossbow show.
Further reading on Kristofor portal.
