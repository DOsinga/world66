# Nightlife and Entertainment in Split

*Europe > Croatia > Split > Nightlife And Ente*

**Split Nightlife** is something that you must experience while on vacation in Croatia. Split is one of the most active cities in Croatia, partly because it is the largest city on the coast, but also because it is one of the biggest transport hubs in Croatia. No matter what time of the day it is, you are sure to find something to do.

**Caf&eacute; Shook**

If you are looking for the younger crowd to hang out with, you will want to stop in at Caf&eacute; Shook. Weekends are going to offer more crowds in this place. Caf&eacute; Shook is not just a great place to grab a quick bite to eat, but it is also a nice spot to grab a drink. If Caf&eacute; Shook is too crowded for your taste, you can always go next door and check out Caf&eacute; Song.

**Split Summer**

Split Summer is only offered during the summer months, but Split Summer is still something worth checking out. Every night something new is happening, so you will have many amazing things to choose from. During Split Summer everything in town, including the city square, turns into a stage. Many different types of events take place on the stage under the evening sky, including concerts and operas, but you can also find dramas taking place at this event.

**Tropic Club Ecuador**

Clubs come and go in the city of Split, but even the ones that are around to stay change what they have to offer based on what is currently in demand. Tropic Club Ecuador is one of those clubs that is around to stay, so it has adopted the Latin style that has captured the attention of the people of Split. Here you will find fruity cocktails to sip on while dancing the night away. While taking a break from dancing, you should take a minute to take in the beautiful view.

**O&rsquo;Hara Klub**

This club is located just past Bavice, but is worth the trip if you enjoy live music. Here you are going to be presented with a beachside venue; they even have tables right along the beach, which gives you the chance to enjoy looking up at the stars or out along the coast while listening to music. During the summer you will have the opportunity to listen to a live rock or jazz band each night.

**Croatian National Theatre**

This theater is open year round, but during the winter months, you will be presented with a choice of opera or ballet. The theater was originally built in 1891, and wasn&rsquo;t restored until 1979. The architecture is amazing; in fact, many people go to a show just to see the beautifully designed building. Seats cost around $10 a person, and they can usually be bought the same day as the show, so there is no need to call ahead for reservations.
