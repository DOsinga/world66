# Getting There

*Europe > Croatia > Trogir > Getting There*

Trogir is a short ride with bus 37 from the small busstation in [Split](/europe/croatia/split). The fare is the usual amount for four zone, about three dollar.

 Another option is to take Trogir as a break on a trip from [Zadar](/europe/croatia/zadar). Just ask the driver to drop you off. The busstop is just opposite the bridge that leads to the old city, next to the market.
