# Komarna Travel Guide

*Europe > Croatia > Trogir > Komarna*

The best resource for sights, hotels, restaurants, bars, what to do and see

Komarna is small village in Dalmatia.

Situated by the Mali Ston Bay from where there is a spectacular view over reefs, cliffs and small islands to The Peljesac Peninsula.

There are not many facilities in Komarna. One small hotel
with a restaurant
that is open during the tourist season, one konoba (café) and a
mini-market.
Next to the hotel and in Villa Komarna there is a fitness centre and a
swimming pool and a pool bar.
Three
kilometres to the south is the holiday resort Klek and eight kilometres
south is Neum, the only seaside town in Bosnia-Hercegovina where there
is several big shops, restaurants
and hotels.

Komarna is also ideal as a base for sightseeing.
Many historical places and world-class tourist attractions can be
reached
by car within an hour. And close by there are several interesting and
little
known places that are well worth your visit.
