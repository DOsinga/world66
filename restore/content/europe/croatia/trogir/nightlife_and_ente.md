### [Monaco](/europe/croatia/trogir/nightlife_and_ente/monaco)

Monaco is a - at day caffe bar/at night night club in Trogir which plays mostly hits, from Hip Hop to R'n'B and House. It is open every day and is the centar of Trogir's club scene. Every Friday there's a band coming and at that day you can hear folk music. Monaco is located just across the bridge to Ciovo, which is practicly the centar of Trogir.
