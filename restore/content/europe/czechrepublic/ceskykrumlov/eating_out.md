**Restaurants**

**Barbakon**

A delightful restaurant built into what was once a gunpowder room in the old town's defenisve wall, the Barbakon is a must-see in Cesky Krumlov. Small and cozy, the Barbakon serves a nice assortment of very reasonably priced dinners cooked in a wood stove only a few feet from your table.
