# U KAMENNEHO MOSTU

*Europe > Czechrepublic > Prague > Eatingout > Copy Of Ukamennehomostu*

Open: Daily. 12.00 - 01.00. An historic restaurant with an interesting decor, great views of Charles Bridge, a large, floating terrace and excellent food. What more can you ask for? The carpaccio with pesto that I had was like butter, the meat was so tender. The service is top-notch too. (AMEX, MC, V)
