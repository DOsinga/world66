# middle east

*Europe > Czechrepublic > Prague > Eatingout > Middleeast*

**Restaurants**

**ADONIS**

Open: Daily. 11.00 - 20.00

An excellent venue for a take-away lunch. Cheap, but good choices and quality. Don't expect to sit as there are only a few chairs available. Note the early closing time.  

address: Jungmannova 21, Praha 1

tel: +42 (0)02 268 908

**QUEENZ GRILL **

Open: Mon.-Sat. 09.00 - 21.00, Sun. 11.00 - 21.00

Ideal for a lunch-time snack of light Middle Eastern dishes such as hummus and interesting salads.  

address: Havelska 12, Praha 1

**U CEDRU II **

Open: Daily. 11.00 - 23.00

A new big brother for the restaurant above. Not quite as atmospheric (except for the belly-dancer, who tries hard to make up for it), but the Lebanese menu and food is just as good and a little more extensive. (AMEX, V, DC, MC, EC) 

address: Na Kociance 3, Praha 6

tel: +42 (0)02 2431 1682

**FAKHRELDINE**

Open: Daily. 12.00 - 24.00

An elegant Lebanese restaurant with all the Lebanese staples on the menu. Everything is well cooked and presented and the serving staff are very professional. Expect to pay almost Western prices, but it is most definitely worth it. (AMEX, DC, EC, MC, V)  

address: Klimentska 48, Praha 1

tel: +42 (0)02 232 1659

**U CEDRU **

Open: Daily. 11.00 - 23.00

This has become more of a cult restaurant with diners returning again and again for U Cedru's selection of Lebanese starters. Not in the same class as Fakhreldine but with more atmosphere. Mr. Mahdi will probably be there on a barstool to welcome you like a long-los [more..](/europe/czechrepublic/prague/eatingout/middleeast/ucedru)

address: Na hutich 13, Praha 6

tel: +42 (0)02 312 2974

**CASABLANCA **

Open: Daily. 19.00 - 02.00

Not really middle-eastern as it is mainly Moroccan, but close enough. Excellent dishes that are professionally prepared and served. Interesting atmosphere (maybe the belly-dancer helps with this). (AMEX, V, MC, DC) 

address: Na prikope 10, Praha 1

tel: +42 (0)02 2421 0519
