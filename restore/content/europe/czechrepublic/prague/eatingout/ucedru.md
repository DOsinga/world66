# U CEDRU

*Europe > Czechrepublic > Prague > Eatingout > Ucedru*

Open: Daily. 11.00 - 23.00

This has become more of a cult restaurant with diners returning again and again for U Cedru's selection of Lebanese starters. Not in the same class as Fakhreldine but with more atmosphere. Mr. Mahdi will probably be there on a barstool to welcome you like a long-lost cousin. (AMEX, DC, EC, MC, JCB, V)
