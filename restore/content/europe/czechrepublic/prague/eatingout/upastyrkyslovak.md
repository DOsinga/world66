# U PASTYRKY (Slovak)

*Europe > Czechrepublic > Prague > Eatingout > Upastyrkyslovak*

Open: Daily. 18.00-01.00. Interesting, to say the least. Rustic, 'Slovak', atmosphere, with a frenetic 'Gypsy' band and meat roasting on open grills. Seats made of skins and long wooden tables. Worth at least one visit. (, EC)
