# Taxis

*Europe > Czechrepublic > Prague > Gettingaround > Taxis*

Taxis can be tough because there are a lot of rouge ones that don't use a meter. This means they charge you way too much. I suggest you either jot down numbers for taxi companies when you arrive or pop into a hotel and ask them to phone one for you. Never take them off the street unless you recognize the company name and make sure the meter is turned on.
