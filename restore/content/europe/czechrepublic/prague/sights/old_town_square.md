# Old Town Square

*Europe > Czechrepublic > Prague > Sights > Old Town Square*

The old Town Square (Staromestske namesti) is from the 12th century. Old Town Hall with Astronomical Clock (on
the right), Gothic Church of Our Lady before Tyn (in the middle) and
Church of St.Nicholas (on the left) dominate this unique heart of the
Prague.
