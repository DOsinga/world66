# Prague Botanical Gardens

*Europe > Czechrepublic > Prague > Sights > Prague Botanical G*

The garden covers 52 ha of area in total. Around 1.5 hectares from the
area is used for new Japanese garden and Turkey and Mediterranean
expositions, accessible for public from 1997. Both complexes are
interconnected by a collection of moorland plants.
