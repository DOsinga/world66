**ARoS Aarhus Museum of Art**

ARoS Aarhus Museum of Art opened it's doors 2004. It's architecture is compared with the Guggenheim Museum in Bilbao, it is rather extraordinary and definitely worth a visit - art interested or not. It displays a large permanent collection of Danish Golden Age art, modern art and f [more..](/europe/denmark/aarhus/museums/aarhusmuseumofart)

**National Open Air Museum **

The National Open Air Museum of Urban History and Culture (Den Gamle by) is located at the Old Town and well worth a visit.

address: Viborgvej 2 8000 Århus C

openinghours: 9-18
