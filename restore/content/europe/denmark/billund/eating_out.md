# Eating Out in Billund

*Europe > Denmark > Billund > Eating Out*

Although Billund is just a small community town, it contains several nice restaurants, mostly situated in local hotels. These restaurants serve a wide selection of foods, ranging from international cuisines to delectable local Dutch specialties. The much larger community of Vejle contains a larger assortment of fine cafes, while the park of Legoland has a multitude dining options including outlets of international fast food chains. The **Billund eating out guide **offers travelers information on the best places to eat in this picturesque Danish town.

**Billund Kro**

This is a rustic restaurant located in a stunning conventional Danish edifice. Billund Kro serves items from various cuisines, although their prime specialty is the Danish dishes on the menu. The portions they serve are extremely generous and reasonably priced, which makes Billund Kro a favorite eating hangout for budget travelers.

**La Famille Café and Bistro**

La Famille Café and Bistro serves a carvey buffet that includes bread, soup, three different roasts, salmon and salad. They also serve a children’s buffet that features children’s favorite treats like fries and salads. This restaurant is located in Billund FDM Camping and offers good value for your money.

**Munkebjerg Hotel**

Located in a beautiful environment, this hotel houses at least three nice restaurants. If you want to have an attractive vista of the fjord and its nearby forests, opt for the Panorama restaurant. This restaurant serves a remarkable lunch buffet and various Danish delicacies. There is also the Tree Top Restaurant, which serves authentic Danish specialties made by expert chefs. Apart from the delectable food, guests are also offered a chance to taste aged champagnes from a well-stocked wine cellar. The third restaurant is the Jackpot, which offers a gourmet carte du jour, champagne and a view of the casino.

**Cafeen Vejle**

Located in a historical prison in the community of Vejle, this fashionable café serves not just tasty beverages, coffees and a wide selection of food, but hosts various events upstairs, like live gigs, film screenings and stand-up comedy.

**Zleep Lounge**

Situated near the Billund International Airport and housed within the Zleep Hotel Billund, the chic Zleep Lounge is an ideal option for international vacationers, particularly those who are travelling with the entire family. It is an amazing breakfast restaurant, where patrons can enjoy an array of healthy foods. The restaurant also offers a broad range of drinks, refreshments and meals throughout the day.

**Caféen i Torvehallerne**

Located near Vejle, this café offers beer, cappuccino, cakes, sandwiches and Irish coffee nachos. It also features live music every Friday and Saturday nights and breakfast jazz sessions on Sundays.
