# Getting Around in Billund

*Europe > Denmark > Billund > Getting Around*

There are a few traveling options to chose from during your visit to Billund.
First, if you are staying in a hotel you can check with the hotel clerk on
traveling and expenses.

**Ask the Locals**

Some hotels offer free complimentary traveling by taxi or at a discounted
price. There are also many places to rent vehicles from cars to limousines.
Be sure to familiarize yourself with the laws and try to drive as the locals do.
You might want to also pack a map and a translation book in case you need to ask
for directions. Before you chose your mode of transportation find out where the
attraction your are wishing to visit. If it is close enough you may want to save
a few bucks and walk. This is a great way to see many details of the town and
stay in shape. You can also travel by train.

**Travel by Train**

The Billund train system, also known as the Rail, has many stops for your
convenience and is great for local travel within the city or longer distance
travel out of the city. You can also have a chance to take in many more of the
sights in a short time utilizing this travel option. They will also accept
reservations.

**Take a Tour**

Another choice that may be great for the ladies is a tour package for
shopping. This choice allows you to have a tour guide escort to all the
great shopping centers and you can take home souvenirs from your experience. There are
several different packages you can choose from.
