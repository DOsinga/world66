# When to Go in Billund

*Europe > Denmark > Billund > When To Go*

If you're planning a trip to the enchanting Danish toyland city of **Billund**, **when to go** depends on what you want to get out of your trip. Billund is a city in Jutland which is in the western part of the Danish mainland. Generally, July and August are the best times to visit to Denmark because of the pleasant climate and cultural activities. Despite of the unpredictable climate of Denmark, Billund has a moderate climate without severe rainfall or snow. March, May, June and October are the top four months for the visitors to go to Billund. Although the weather changes dramatically between summer and winter, the overall climate is pleasant.

**Weather**

Billund's normal temperatures range from -0.85 degrees Celsius to 21.65 degrees Celsius. February is the coldest month, and it usually lessens the number of visitors. The warm months are June, July and August and frequent rainfalls can be experienced in those months. Billund is wettest during the months of December and January. The overall rainfall is 544.7mm per year. Summer is the best time for traveling to this region. Off-season expeditions can be made in May and September when the weather is still warm and pleasant. Visitors should obtain prior information on the direction of the wind in the region, since it affects weather conditions significantly.

**Legoland**

The most famous place to visit in Billund is the Legoland amusement park, which is a world-class entertainment center for children and even adults. The Legoland season starts at the end of March and ends in late October. This exciting place has different opening hours for different seasons.

**Lalandia Resort**

Formerly founded as a tropical holiday center in Rodby, Lalandia is a newly opened resort in Billund with accommodations for 600, as well as indoor and outdoor activities. It's possible to make a visit to Lalandia all year round for its extensive collection of indoor activities with a cinema, a mini golf field and an ice rink. Heated swimming pools and spa pools are suitable for all seasons, but the place is at its best in summers when tourists flock here for the exciting line-up of events and activities.

**Billund FDM Camping**

Billund FDM campsite is situated next to Legoland and 24 kilometers from Vejle, a famous shopping area. It is a progressive and well-equipped campsite offering 600 pitches in a 14-hectare area. The best time to go to Billund FDM Camping is during the summer. During this season, children can indulge in fun activities like pony rides or swimming in the pools. Campers can fully relish the beauty of the nearby countryside in summer. Winter seasons will be the choice for visitors with economical plans, because the FDM provides free admission to several sites during this season.

Billund boasts of a pleasant and unpredictable climate throughout the year, butits country-side beauty is accentuated during peak summers.
