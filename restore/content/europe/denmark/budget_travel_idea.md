# Budget Travel Ideas in Denmark

*Europe > Denmark > Budget Travel Idea*

**Denmark budget travel** is a great way to explore the country without making it a costly vacation. If you plan it well, it is possible enjoy thoroughly and make it a memorable experience within your budget.

#### **Staying at a Hostel or Sleep In**** **

Denmark has several good hostel accommodations for the whole family. Most of these hostels are located in prime areas with conveniences located nearby. Many sight-seeing destinations are also located near the hostels. Shared rooms are available that cost lower than private rooms. Another low-budget option for staying in Denmark is a Sleep In. A few major cities offer this option where a dormitory is provided for sleeping and bath facilities are common. Breakfast inclusive offers are also available at the Sleep Ins.

#### **Camping on a Low Budget**** **

For adventure lovers, a camping holiday in Denmark is possible on an affordable budget. There are several hundred camp sites all over the country. All of them are cheap, while some of them are absolutely free. If you are keen to explore the scenic natural beauty of Denmark on foot or on a bike, overnight camping shelters are a great option to stay. There are also Island Camps which offer themed vacations at a low cost.

#### **Affordable Transportation**

Copenhagen has a system of free &ldquo;City Bikes&rdquo; available against a refundable deposit. After you have used the bike for as long as you want, you can return it at any of the City Bike racks located at all prime spots in the city, and get your deposit refunded. If you are with family and planning to travel extensively across the country, consider using the train. It is a cheap mode of transport in Denmark, and special discounts are available for youth, senior citizens and students. Children under 12 travel for free.

#### **Sightseeing on a Budget**** **

Wednesday is the day to explore the museums of Copenhagen while on a budget. Most museums offer free admission on this day. Museums and other tourist spots outside the city also offer free entry for one day a week in most cases. There are a number of musical concerts, events and shows which allow free admission. Opera previews of the season can be enjoyed free of cost in Copenhagen.

#### **Inexpensive Dining Options**** **

Traditional restaurants specializing in foreign cuisines such as Thai, Greek and Turkish are spread throughout the country, and they offer quality food at reasonable prices. Most cafes and museum eateries also offer nice food at cheap prices. Breakfast is often included along with accommodation which works out to be a good deal.
