# ARKEN

*Europe > Denmark > Copenhagen > Museums > Arken*

This Museum, 20 km (12 miles) south of Copenhagen, looks like an huge vessel that has stranded on the coast of Denmark. As is a trend among today’s museum builders, architect Søren Robert Lund made ARKEN the showpiece of its exhibitions. The museum was opened in 1996 and has its focal point on all forms of Danish, Scandinavian and international art from 1945 and onward. The building is organised around the imposing main room which, like a huge banana penetrates the other spaces.
