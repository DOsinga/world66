# Statens Museum for Kunst

*Europe > Denmark > Copenhagen > Museums > Statensmuseumforkunst*

This is Denmark’s largest museum on art with a scope that goes form medieval painting to modern installations and onward. Recently enlarged the exhibition space has doubled. The emphasis is still on Dutch 17th century and Danish 19th century painting, two Golden Ages. Wednesdays free admission.
