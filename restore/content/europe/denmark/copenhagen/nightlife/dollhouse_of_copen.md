# Dollhouse of Copenhagen

*Europe > Denmark > Copenhagen > Nightlife > Dollhouse Of Copen*

Dollhouse of Copenhagen is a high end nightclub mostly for gentlemen. It is situated in central Copenhagen near the central station.

This is the place to visit - if you can afford it.

They offer a lot of entertainment. Without paying any extra fees you can enjoy a floor show performed by beautiful girls from all over the world. The floor show is a combination of dance and striptease, and in the end - not much is left for your fantasy. Besides that they offer table dance, lap dance and private VIP areas where you privately can enjoy the company or dance of one or more girls.

Expensive - but worth the money. Open Mo-Sa 15.00 to 05.00
