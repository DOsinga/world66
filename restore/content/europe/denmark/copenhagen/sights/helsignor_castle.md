# Helsignor Castle

*Europe > Denmark > Copenhagen > Sights > Helsignor Castle*

This is the castle that inspired William Shakespeare's play "Hamlet". This castle is overlooks the border of Sweden... With it's unique architecture and beautiful tapestries, this is a place to visit, while you are there you can take a quick trip across the border to Sweden...
