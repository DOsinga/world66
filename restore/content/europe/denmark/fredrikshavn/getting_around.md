# Getting Around in Fredrikshavn

*Europe > Denmark > Fredrikshavn > Getting Around*

Getting around in Frederikshavn Denmark means that you need to find the best **Frederikshavn transportation** that will take you to all of the places that you want to see on your vacation. There are a few different ways that you can find to get around the city.

#### Travel by Ferry

One of the best ways to get around in the city and throughout the area is to travel by ferry. The ferry boats have many features that include restaurants and games to play while you are onboard. Most of the ferry rides take about three hours to get from Sweden to Frederikshavn, Denmark, and there are also express ferries that are a little bit more expensive but will help you reach the places that you want to see without all of the stops with the other ferries.

#### Rent a Limousine

Getting around in style can be a great way to travel and you can use one of the local limousine services in Denmark to travel to the places in the city of Frederikshavn that you want to see. The prices vary depending on what kind of trip you are going to be taking, and how many hours you want to rent the limo to use.

#### Travel using Eurail Pass

You can use the Eurail Pass to get to any of the places that you are going, and if you are going to be traveling in or around the area, you can find rail passes that will allow you to travel in an affordable way--and you will also able to see the countryside and views of the city while you are riding the rail.

#### Rent a Car

You can choose to rent a car to travel through the city. If you are more familiar with the area, you can find this to be an affordable way to travel and you can also get to where you are going without having to make all of the stops that you would on the train or the ferry.

#### Travel by Foot or Bike

When you want to make a lot of stops along the way, you may want to consider walking or renting a bike to use. The city is small enough to easily commute around and be able to stop at the many fun places to shop along the way. You can find maps and routes that make it easier to get around in the city when you are planning Frederikshavn transportation.
