# The Funen Village

*Europe > Denmark > Odense > Sights > Thefunenvillage*

An open-air museum with more than 20 rural buldings from the 18th and 19th centuries. Apart from the proper farmhouse building, there are such buildings as a smithy, a windmill and a watermill, a vicarage, a school and an almhouse. Agriculture and cattleraising are practiced in the village, using old methods. Workshop activities, especially during the summer months.
