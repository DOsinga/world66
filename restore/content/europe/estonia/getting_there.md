Getting to and from Estonia

### [Budget airlines which fly to Estonia

Use the European Low Cost Airline Guide search engine ( http://www.low-cost-airline.info](/europe/estonia/getting_there/budget_airlines_wh)) to find out which budget airlines fly into Estonian airports (Mainly Tallinn, in my opinion the most interesting place to see but its also a good place to set off to other towns from )

Often you can save many $/£/whatever by using these airlines instead of the main carriers.
