# Nordic Jet Line

*Europe > Estonia > Getting There > Nordic Jet Line*

Nordic Jet Line operates 'high speed' catamarans on the [Helsinki](/europe/finland/helsinki) - [Tallinn](/europe/estonia/tallinn) route. There are seven sailings a day (less on sundays) and travel time is 1h40. There are no ferries from January to April, due to the ice conditions at sea. Booking online is the best way to get hold of a cheap ticket.
