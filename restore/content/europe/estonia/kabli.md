# Kabli Travel Guide

*Europe > Estonia > Kabli*

The best resource for sights, hotels, restaurants, bars, what to do and see

**Kabli** is a small quiet coastal village in South West Estonia.
The beach is the biggest attraction of the village.

Kabli is also a place where the birds migrate in May and October every year - they can be observed from the bird watchtower.
**More information **about Kabli can be found on the official tourist
information website of Estonia: http://www.visitestonia.com/en/articles/destinations/the-coastline/kabli
