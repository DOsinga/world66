# Klaksvík Travel Guide

*Europe > Faroeislands > Bordoy > Klaksvk*

The best resource for sights, hotels, restaurants, bars, what to do and see

Klaksvík is the largest city in the northern islands. The population of Klaksvík is 4600, it is the second largest city/town in the Faroe Islands. Klaksvík is situated on the island Borðoy, but there is a subsea road tunnel from Klaksvík to Leirvík on Eysturoy, so it is possible to drive from the airport on Vágar island and from Tórshavn to Klaksvík.
