# Eiði Travel Guide

*Europe > Faroeislands > Eysturoy > Eii*

The best resource for sights, hotels, restaurants, bars, what to do and see

Eiði is a village to the north-west of Eysturoy. It has a museum.

Try the mountain road from here to Gjogv - at one point there is a FREE telescope to view the wonderful sea stacks of Risin and Killingin.
