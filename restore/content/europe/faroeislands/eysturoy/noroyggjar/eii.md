# Eiði Travel Guide

*Europe > Faroeislands > Eysturoy > Noroyggjar > Eii*

The best resource for sights, hotels, restaurants, bars, what to do and see

Eiði is a village in the north-west of Eysturoy, near to the impressive sea stacks of Risin and Killingin. There is a museum in the village.

Try to take the wonderful mountain road from Eiði to Gjogv.
