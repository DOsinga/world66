# Suðuroy Travel Guide

*Europe > Faroeislands > Suuroy*

The best resource for sights, hotels, restaurants, bars, what to do and see

Suðuroy or Suduroy is the southernmost of the islands in the Faroe Islands. The population is around 4900. The largest towns on Suðuroy are Tvøroyri and Vágur.
