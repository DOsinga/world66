# Bour Travel Guide

*Europe > Faroeislands > Vagar > Bour*

The best resource for sights, hotels, restaurants, bars, what to do and see

This is a delectable little village with outstanding views, even by local standards, of the uninhabited but remarkably shapely island of Tindholmur and the nearby stacks. A waterfall drops into the village near to the church.
