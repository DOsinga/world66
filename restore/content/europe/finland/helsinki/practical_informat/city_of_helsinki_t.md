# City of Helsinki Tourist Information

*Europe > Finland > Helsinki > Practical Informat > City Of Helsinki T*

City of Helsinki Tourist Information is located in the heart of the city, at the corner of Pohjoisesplandi and Unioninkatu (Pohjoisesplanadi 19). Information point sells Helsinki Card, tickets for sightseeing cruises etc. Free maps and brochures also available.
