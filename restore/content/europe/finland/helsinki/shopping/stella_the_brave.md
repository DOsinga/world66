# STELLA the brave

*Europe > Finland > Helsinki > Shopping > Stella The Brave*

A beautiful and unique boutique located at Bulevardi 11, which is the most picturesque street in Helsinki.
STELLA the brave has something for everyone. It carries leather purses, fine jewelry, costume jewelry, some special vintage pieces, clothing, shoes. The owner Leah is American and living in Helsinki and seems to offer a different shopping experience then most of the other shops. The interior is fun, warm, and welcoming. The prices are friendly and the quality is evident. Truly a fun and sassy shopping experience.
