# Olympic Stadium

*Europe > Finland > Helsinki > Sights > Olympic Stadium*

The Olympic Stadium, completed in 1938, was the main venue of the XV Olympic Games in 1952. The 72 metres high tower offers an excellent view over Helsinki.
