# Sights in Kouvola City

*Europe > Finland > Kouvola City > Sights*

**Kymmene River**, **Kuusankoski**, **Ankkapurha**, **Myllykoski Falls** and **Valkeala lake district** are most famous
attractions in Kouvola City. There is also an **International football
Stadium of Mypa FC** at Myllykoski community.
