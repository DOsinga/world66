# Sights

*Europe > Finland > Punkaharju > Sights*

Perhaps the largest and most breathtaking sites Punkaharju has to offer
is the nature. You should take the "Harjutie" whether arriving from
Savolinna or leaving Punkaharju towards Savonlinna. It is an old road
that winds on top of a 5-6 km long hill next to the water. You have to
see it to experience it. The Russian Czar declared this area a nature
reserve once he saw it (a hundred or so years ago)!
