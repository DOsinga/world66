# Nightlife and Entertainment

*Europe > Finland > Savonlinna > Nightlifeandente*

Savonlinna has a great nightlife compared to its size. In the busy summer months the city is extremely active with tourists from around the world.

**Olutravintola Sillansuu**

Located in the city centre, it is one of the best pubs in town. It has an assortment of over 100 beers and whiskeys. A very good atmosphere. Don't miss the thursday special - 2 beers for the price of 1!

**Cafe Mimosa**

Mimosa (as it is more commonly called) is located right next to Olavinlinna, the castle. It has a very cozy open air terrace with live artists in the summer time. Busiest month July. Serves food, snacks, drinks (refreshments and alcoholic).

address: Linnankatu 12

**Pub Happytime**

Pub Happytime is a westernstyle with a mix of modern disco spice -style cafe/bar. Very busy and active in the weekends.

address: Olavinkatu 35, 57130 Savonlinna

url: [www.happytimepub.fi](http://www.happytimepub.fi/index.php)

**Opera Terrace / Oopperaterassi**

The opera terrace is opened usually in May. It is located right next to the harbour with the steam boats.

**Club Reef**

A club with the latest music and hits. http://www.clubreef.net

address: Olavinkatu 40

tel: 535 5161
