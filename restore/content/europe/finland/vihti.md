**Vihti** ( IPA: /ˈʋihti/), or *Vichtis* in Swedish, is a small municipality located in the Uusimaa region of province of Southern Finland, approximately 50 km north of the capital city [Helsinki](/europe/finland/helsinki). Its seat is **Nummela**. The population of Vihti is 25,561 ( 2004- 12-31) and the town covers an area of 566.88 km² of which 44.91 km² is water. The population density is 48.97 inhabitants per km².
