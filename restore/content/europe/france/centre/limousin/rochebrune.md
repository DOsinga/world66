# Rochebrune

*Europe > France > Centre > Limousin > Rochebrune*

Tucked away in a forest, the chateau de Rochebrune has been the seat of the Lords of Chabanais since the 11th and 12th centuries.

 The castle is not far from Rouchchouard between [Limoges](/europe/france/centre/limousin/limoges) and [Poitiers](/europe/france/aquitaine/poitiers).
