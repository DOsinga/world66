# Nightlife and Entertainment

*Europe > France > Centre > Loirevalley > Angers > Nightlifeandentertainment*

Although Angers is not a big nightlife town (bars closing at 2 a.m.) there are some really nice places you can go to if you fancy a beer.

### [Le Chabada](/europe/france/centre/loirevalley/angers/nightlifeandentertainment/lechabada)

Le Chabada is a night club and a live music lounge . Their music program for pop, electro and rock is the best of the town. Prices vary depending on the band playing but they're never expensive and some of them are free. You can also join the club membership and get discounts in every concert. To check their program you can visit their site at www.lechabada.com
