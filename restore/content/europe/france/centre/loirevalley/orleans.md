# Orleans

*Europe > France > Centre > Loirevalley > Orleans*

In 1429 Orleans had its finest hour. Joan of Arc, a.k.a. the Maiden of Orleans, delivered the city form the English troops. Most of the sights in present day Orleans have a link to that event. 

In the **Cathedrale St-Croix** you find Joans pedestal on an altar carved with scenes from here life. The 19-th century stained glass windows tell the rest of the story. The **Maison de Jeanne d'Arc** on Place General de Gaulle is fun for children and has loads of additional info.
