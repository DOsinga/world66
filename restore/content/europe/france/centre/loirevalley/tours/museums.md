# Museums

*Europe > France > Centre > Loirevalley > Tours > Museums*

**all**

**Wine Museum**

located near the Loire river in the rue National this museum gives you indepth information on the wines from the Touraine.

address: 16, rue Nationale

tel: 02 47 61 07 93

**Musee du Compagnonnage**

Focus on the guilds that built the chateaux in the region. Quite interesting if you want some background info.

address: rue Nationale

openinghours: closed tuesdays

**Musee des Beaux Arts**

Good collection of paintings, Rembrandt and Hals among others.

address: Place Francois Sicard

openinghours: closed tuesdays
