# Place des Halles

*Europe > France > East > Alsace > Strasbourg > Shopping > Place Des Halles*

La Place des Halles is a shopping mall containing everything from fashion to grocery stores. This two-story mall has its own tram stop, and is easily accessible from anywhere in the city via tram or bus.
