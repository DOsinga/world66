# Cathedrale de Notre Dame

*Europe > France > East > Alsace > Strasbourg > Sights > Cathedraledenotredame*

The cathedral soars out of a huddle of medieval houses, with a spire of such delicate, flaky, lightness it seems the work of confectioners rather than masons. In the south transept the slender triple-tiered 13th century column, the **Pilier des Anges**, is decorated with some of the most graceful and expressive statuary of its age. Look also at the enormous and tremendously complicated **astrological clock** built by Schwilgue of Strasbourg in 1842, a big hit with guided tours who roll up in droves to witness the clock’s crowning performance of the day, striking the hour of noon with unerring accuracy at 12.30pm – noon.
