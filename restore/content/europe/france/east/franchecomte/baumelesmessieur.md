# Baume-les-Messieurs and Cirque

*Europe > France > East > Franchecomte > Baumelesmessieur*

There is little point in trying to describe the wonderful Cirque de Baume in words, when you only have to click on perso.club-internet.fr/esiblote/index129.htm to see some excellent photos.

Baume-les-Messieurs owes its name to a 16th century decision that its abbey should be run by secular canons. It has an outstanding situation below the Cirque and the Abbey buildings allow for a visit of some length.
