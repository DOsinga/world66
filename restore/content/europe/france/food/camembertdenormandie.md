# Camembert de Normandie

*Europe > France > Food > Camembertdenormandie*

True Camembert comes from only five departments in [Normandy & Brittany](/europe/france/normandybrittany). Its origin dates back to the 18th century. Originally, this cheese was dry and yellow-brown, but after a few modifications it became softer and more earthy. A genuine Camembert has a delicate salty taste.
