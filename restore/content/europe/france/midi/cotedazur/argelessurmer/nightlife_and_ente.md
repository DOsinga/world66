# Nightlife and Entertainment in Argeles sur Mer

*Europe > France > Midi > Cotedazur > Argelessurmer > Nightlife And Ente*

One of the biggest attractions to France is the exciting nightlife. The **Argeles sur Mer Nightlife** is no different from other places in France. In Argeles sur Mer, you can find cafés that are open all night, but you can also find the best places to go dancing until the late hours of the morning.

**Argeles Plage**

If you are on vacation with your entire family Argeles Plage is one of the best spots to go at night. The reason that this is the best spot to go for a family vacation is because the area offers something for everyone in the family. Argeles Plage offers a large variety of restaurants and bars, which includes pizzerias and local cuisine.

**Indigo **

If you are looking for a great place to go during the late hours of the night Indigo is one place to be. If you attend Indigo on a Friday night, which is highly recommended, you will be able to take part in Indigo’s foam party. During the other days of the week, you can come to Indigo to enjoy a few drinks with your friends or you can dance the night away.

**Plaza Bowl**

If you are on vacation with a family, Plaza Bowl is a great place to go visit at night. Plaza Bowl is one of the most family-friendly places in town because all ages are always welcome. If you go to Plaza Bowl on a weekend, you want to be prepared for a wait because the place tends to fill up, but it is worth the wait to enjoy a game of bowling.

**Cinemaginaire**

The nightlife in France is not always about going out to the best clubs or sitting in a café until the wee hours of the morning. One big part of the nightlife in France is the cinemas, which are usually full of culture-based shows. The Cinemaginaire is 1 of those places because it promotes the activities in Argeles sur Mer that give you an idea of the local culture.

**Salle Polyvalente**

If you are looking for a concert to go to at night this is the place to be. Salle Polyvalente offers numerous concerts throughout the year, but the best part is the price of the concerts. Some of the concerts that you can attend will be free, but others will charge a fee.
