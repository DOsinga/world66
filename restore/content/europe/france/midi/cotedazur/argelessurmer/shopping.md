# Shopping in Argeles sur Mer

*Europe > France > Midi > Cotedazur > Argelessurmer > Shopping*

If you are going to be in **Argeles sur Mer Shopping** there are some cute little shops that you cannot miss. When shopping in Argeles sur Mer you can go to the touristy areas to find some of the local stores that sell various handicrafts, but you can also go to other areas in town to find what you are looking for. What you want to keep in mind is that sometimes going off the beaten path is the best way to find that special something to take home with your from your trip.

**Palau Del Vidre**

If you are looking for anything that is made from blown glass you want to be sure to visit this quiet little shop. Not only can you shop inside the shop, but you can also have a tour of the shop, which will include a demonstration of glassblowing. The shop is open every day from 9 a.m. to 12 p.m. and then again from 2 p.m. to 7 p.m.

**Argeles Village**

This market is open all year round on Wednesdays and Saturdays from 7 a.m. to 1 p.m. At this market you are going to find about 60 locals putting their products on display. The types of products that you will find are going to vary, but everything here is made, grown, or caught locally. You will be able to find this market in the center of town.

**Argeles Plage**

All along Argeles Plage, you will find shops and restaurants that are looking for your business. In fact, you will find that there are over 100 shops located along Argeles Plage. Not only can you find the shops and restaurants located along Argeles Plage between June and September you can also find a traditional market taking place 3 mornings a week. Be sure to visit on Monday, Wednesday and Fridays between the hours of 7 a.m. and 1 p.m.

**Craft Market**

If you plan to be in Argeles anywhere from June 15 to September 15, you will want to check out the local craft market. The craft market takes place along Argeles Plage, but it does not interfere with the local market that is featured during the day. The craft market during the months of June, July, August, and September takes place every evening from 5 p.m. to 12 a.m. On average, 40 artists feature their handicrafts.

**Port Docks**

Located along the waterfront, the Port Docks market features a variety of local products. At this local market you will find about 50 stands displaying items. The products on display will range from artistic work done by local painters to jewelry and earthenware. This market only takes place during the months of June, July, and August, and can only be found on Tuesdays and Fridays from 5 p.m. to 12 a.m.
