# Travel Guide in Cagne

*Europe > France > Midi > Cotedazur > Cagne > Travel Guide*

Having a **Cagnes-sur-mer travel guide** can help you maximize the beauty of the place, which is located on the hills near Nice. The town is renowned for its breathtaking view that many artists have revered and admired.

**Hotels**

One of the most-frequented hotels in town is the Villa Estelle, which is only 10 minutes away from the Nice airport. The place is known for its beautiful and elegant 14th-century styled house. Most tourists admire the Florentine columns and Mediterranean garden that surround the hotel. If you are looking to spend money on some quality accommodations, then Villa Estelle is the perfect place to be. Other hotels in the area include Residence La Crystal and Hotel Splendid. They are very popular among tourists, but service is not as superb.

**Bed and Breakfast**

For most backpackers who do not want to go lavish on hotel accommodations, bed and breakfast places are also available around the town. Villa St. Paul is the highest rated and recommended. For a budgeted price, you get comfortable lodging, excellent service and tasty food. Other bed and breakfast rentals are Bastide des Eucalyptus and Villa Cedria.

**Landmarks**

Chateau Grimaldi is home to contemporary art kept in rooms of beautiful architectural designs like a mini museum. Here also is a very popular restaurant, Grimaldi. This local favorite serves inexpensive meals prepared individually unlike the common fast food. Next stop is the 1315 House which is about 700 years old. This popular tourist spot displays its age proudly, built in 1315. Tourists proudly take pictures on the site and images of the house are often seen on the area&rsquo;s post cards. Next, climb to Upper Cagnes and see Haut-de-Cagnes on the skyline. Here, a beautifully preserved medieval village is seen. This fortified structure encloses winding alleys and packed houses. A mini-bus service can take you up if you are not up for the steep climb.

**Food**

Every tourist loves a good restaurant. La Caruso is popular in town as it is located at the center of the St. Paul de Vence village. Les Terraillers is another famous option for tourists who like to dine is an eccentric stone restaurant. Food is very delicious and cheap and the perfect ambiance is set to compliment the town.

**Leisure**

Some tourists who would like to try the local leisure offered can go to L&rsquo;essenciel where you are greeted with impeccable service for beauty treatments. Both men and women are offered beauty plans, which they definitely enjoy.
