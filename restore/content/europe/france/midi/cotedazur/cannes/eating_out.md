# Eating Out in Cannes

*Europe > France > Midi > Cotedazur > Cannes > Eating Out*

Cannes, France is a very luxurious city and is the destination of
many tourists every year. Filled with luxury homes and hotels,
vacationers spend much of their time here various shops, museums, the beach, and of course restaurants. Cannes is the stage
to many high end and expensive restaurants serving everything from
traditional French cuisines to Mediterranean or Asian delicacies. The
most common are the restaurants that stay true to French culinary
techniques.

**High End Restaurants**

For the most time if you’re looking for a fancy high end restaurant
in France you will be able to find them in abundance. Most of them
serve traditional foods of the region and are extremely expensive. In
some places you will be spending over $150 for a meal. One restaurant
to visit for expensive tastes is La Palme d’Or. It’s a hotel restaurant
that serves as hosts to the many celebrities around the area during the
film festivals. Reservation is definitely required. The menu features
foie gras and various seafood dishes prepared in a number of ways
depending on the season. The menu changes by season, so what you’ll be
dining on really depends on when you go. Main courses here can range
from $65 to $117.

**Middle Range Restaurants**

Price isn’t always a complete reflection of quality or class. There
are many restaurants in Cannes that offer a delicious menu with a
beautiful ambience at a much reduced cost to some of the very expensive
restaurants in the area. One of these restaurants is La Mere Besson. This restaurant is dedicated to pure culinary traditions and serves
delicious plates of regional food at a fair price. Reservations are
required to be seated here as are most of the restaurants like this
one. The menu may include specialties like rack of lamb or estouffade
Provecal. Other restaurants to consider are Cote Jardin and La Broutte
de Grand-Mere.

**Dining on a Budget**

Just because you can’t afford to spend $40 or $100 on a plate of
food doesn’t mean you are unable to eat great food while in Cannes. If
you are looking for a budget friendly restaurant you will be sure to
find it in Le Comptoir des Vins. Originally a wine shop this restaurant
serves a wide variety of wines with delicious entres to accompany them.
You can get cheese sampler plates to enjoy with your wine but they also
have a variety of meat, fish and vegetarian dishes that are paired with
your wine selections.
