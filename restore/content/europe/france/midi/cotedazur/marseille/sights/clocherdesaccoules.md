# Clocher des Accoules

*Europe > France > Midi > Cotedazur > Marseille > Sights > Clocherdesaccoules*

Place Daviel – (12th, 14th and 17th centuries) The last vestige of one of the oldest churches in Marseille, it formerly contained the Sauveterre bell, wich was used to convene municipal meetings.
