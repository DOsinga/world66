# Corniche J. F. Kennedy

*Europe > France > Midi > Cotedazur > Marseille > Sights > Cornichejfkennedy*

A coast road wich formely linked the city centre to the outlying country cottages. The viaducts going over the valleys date from the Second Empire. The road comes out at the Plages (beaches) Gaston Defferre wich have been reclaimed from the sea and are now used for walks as well as providing numerous water sport facilities.
