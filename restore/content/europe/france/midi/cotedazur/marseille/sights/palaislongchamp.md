# Palais Longchamp

*Europe > France > Midi > Cotedazur > Marseille > Sights > Palaislongchamp*

Municipal water tower built at hte same time as the Marseille Canal in 1839. The complex, including the Museum of Fine Arts and Natural History, was only completed in 1869, to the design of the architect Espérandieu.
