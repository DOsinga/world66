# Pavillon Daviel

*Europe > France > Midi > Cotedazur > Marseille > Sights > Pavillondaviel*

Place Daviel – The old Law Courts built between 1743 and 1747 by the Gérard brothers on the site of the previous Law Courts. The main floor includes a superb wrought iron balcony with " ox-eye " panels created by the sculptor Verdiguier.
