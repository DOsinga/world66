# Perigueux

*Europe > France > Midi > Dordogne > Perigueux*

Situated on the bank of the river Isle, it is the administrative centre of the Dordogne.  It is a very interesting town where you will find roman ruins such as the Vésonne Tower and "les Arènes"(unfortunately converted into a fortress during the Hundred Years War) alongside some extraordinary Romanesque Churches, (Saint-Front Cathedral ).

  Périgueux is a cultural centre and offers many festivals, exhibitions and concerts especially during the Summer months.
