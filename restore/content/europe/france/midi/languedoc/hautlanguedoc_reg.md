# Haut-Languedoc Regional Nature Park

*Europe > France > Midi > Languedoc > Hautlanguedoc Reg*

Some sites describe the location for this park as Southern Cevennes but the ame seems to place it firmly in this section. An area very little known in England and not mentioned much on travel boards, this may surprise by its beauty. It encompasses the Monts de Lacaune, which have their own delicious cheese not totally dissimilar toCantal, the lovely Monts d'Épinousse, and the strange but beautiful Sidobre areas.
