**FLAUGERGUES CASTLE **

17th century building, 17thand 18th century furniture, works of art, gorgeous formal French gardens. This listed castle, that has belonged to the family since 1696, is located in the middle of the "Côteaux de la Méjanelle" vineyard (Cellar with wine-testing sessions).

**CHÂTEAU D’ASSAS**

This listed summer house was built by architect Giral. It features Regency wrought irons, a vast oval music hall decorated with paintings by Lajoue,and a collection of keyboard music instruments, among which a wonderful French 18th century harpsichord. French Formal Gardens.
