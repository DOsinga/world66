# Eating Out

*Europe > France > Midi > Languedoc > Montpellier > Eating Out*

The restaurants in our region offer courses that smell of sun and the "garrigue" : the Languedoc bouillabaisse, "supion"-, cuttle-fish- or squid-based Palavas "rouille", bull "gardianne", Sète "tielle" ( a small pie stuffed with octopuses in a tomato sauce), salmi of coots (a local aquatic bird), stuffed mussels, "lou cagaraoulat" (snail and ham stew)... but also local wines, among which excellent "Côteaux du Languedoc" (registered designation of origin) from selected vine varieties on a sandstone land.
