# CHRISTIAN CINEMA FESTIVAL

*Europe > France > Midi > Languedoc > Montpellier > Festivals > Christiancinemafestival*

Catholics, together with Protestants, organize an ecumenical Christian Cinema Festival. Because of their special look onto life, the Man, and our Society, Christians feels interested in Cinema as a means of communication. This festival is aimed at making it easier for them to meet other people and to set up a dialogue with those who share the same love for life.
