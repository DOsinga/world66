# Nightlife and Entertainment in Montpellier

*Europe > France > Midi > Languedoc > Montpellier > Nightlife And Ente*

Montpellier, France has a great nightlife, mostly due to the
university population in town. All year round students are in
residence and the city has many activities during the evening hours. The clubs are diverse from discotheques to pubs and all are usually
crowded Thursdays through to Sundays.

**Night Clubs**

You don’t have to be a student to take advantage of the nightlife in
Montpellier. You can find a night club that will match your interests
and personality. The night clubs are easy to get to in town because
there is a special bus called the Amigo bus that runs Thursday to
Sunday and takes you around the night club circuit.

**Irish Corner**

Visit the Irish Corner on Avenue de Boirargues to eat traditional
Irish food including their specialty of a beef steak called Tartare.
There is always some Irish music playing and they have a big screen TV
on which to view sporting events. This pub is closed on Sundays.

**L’Oxymore Café**

If you don’t know exactly what you prefer try going to L’Oxymore
Café. This is a music bar that is situated in the downtown area on Rue
Boussairolles and can hold 400 people encompassing three floors. The
music and place itself is very diverse and there is sure to be
something there that peaks your interest and allows you to have fun. They are open from 6pm to 1am in the winter and 7pm to 2am in the summer.

**La Pleine Lune**

Another diverse music bar is La Pleine
Lune on Rue du Faubourg Figuerolles. Music starts at 9:30 pm and keeps
going until 1am. This establishment has been in business since 1986.

**Le Fizz**

Le Fizz is a discotheque that caters mostly to the young students in
the area, but if you are looking for a great time there is no reason
you shouldn’t go too. It offers theme nights and is located on Rue
Cauzit and is open midnight to 5am except on Sunday and Monday.

**Mi Barrio**

Looking for something a little more docile? Then you will enjoy
Mi Barrio on Rue du Plan d’Agde open from 6pm to 1am and closed on
Sunday. This club has wines from the area and from around the world and
hosts wine tasting events.They provide music and also have big screen TV’s so that major sporting events can be viewed.

**Rockstore**

Rockstore near Place de la Comedie is a conglomerate of 5 bars and
clubs where rock, electronica, and world music is performed. Look for
the bright red Cadillac suspended from the entrance on Rue du Verdun.

As you can see you can certainly find something that will make the
evening fly with fun and frivolity in Montpellier making your vacation
or business trip more adventurous.
