# Top 5 Must Do's in Montpellier

*Europe > France > Midi > Languedoc > Montpellier > Top 5 Must Dos*

Montpellier is a city in France that is a popular place for people
to visit. Its main sights of interest have to do with
the rich history. There are many châteaux that give tours and
museums for all interests.
Sightseeing is a popular thing to do and there are many buildings and
landmarks that are worth a visit. Tours of the city are available to
help you visit the best places in the area but they can be
costly. Below is a list of the best places to visit to get the most out
of your visit to Montpellier without having to spend the money on a
tour guide.

**Chateau de Flaugergues**

This chateau was
renovated from 1696 to 1730 by Etienne de Flaugergues, councillon to the
revenue court of Montpellier and has some of the best architecture and
design in the area. The house includes original features and furniture as well as
beautiful lands and gardens and a winery. Here you can take a tour and
have a great wine tasting. Prices are approximately €6.50 a person but can
vary depending on the size of group you have. The Chateau is open every
day except Monday.

**Place de la Comedie**

This is the main focal point of the city of Montpellier. It is a busy
central part of the city with many beautiful buildings, cafes and some
shopping opportunities. In the center is a fountain called the "Three
Graces" which was sculpted in 1790 and is located very close to the main train
station.

**Musee Fabre**

This art museum is one of the best in the area. It is very close to
the center of the city and houses many displays of local and international art.
Displays include ceramics from Greece and other European countries and sculptures and painting from the 17th through 19th centuries. The museum offers guided tours for groups, families and individuals.

**Dining**

Montpellier is one of the best places to find exquisite food and wine. One of these places is Le
Grillardin which is hailed by many as the best place to eat in the
area. Prices are relatively high but the food is worth the price.
