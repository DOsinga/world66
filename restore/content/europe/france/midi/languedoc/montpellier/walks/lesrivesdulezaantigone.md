# LES RIVES DU LEZ A ANTIGONE

*Europe > France > Midi > Languedoc > Montpellier > Walks > Lesrivesdulezaantigone*

On both sides of the River Lez, that links Montpellier to the sea and the floods of which are now perfectly mastered, extend the new districts of Montpellier : Antigone, Port Marianne and the University Pole. Adorned with little tree-lined pedestrian bridges, the river banks, with their old dance-halls encourage adults and children to take a stroll or to learn how to drive easy and secure electrical boats.
