# LUNARET ZOOLOGICAL PARK

*Europe > France > Midi > Languedoc > Montpellier > Walks > Lunaretzoologicalpark*

Location : 50, avenue d’Agropolis Tel : 04 67 63 27 63 Free Entrance Opening Hours : Daily from 9:00 to 17:00. From September 16 to May 14,closed on Monday mornings.   To the North of Montpellier, in a thick Mediterranean "garrigue" shadowed by big pine-trees, the park is home to 145 species - 750 animals in total- scattered on a 80-ha "Réserve refuge libre" protected area.
