# THE "DOMAINE DE GRAMMONT"

*Europe > France > Midi > Languedoc > Montpellier > Walks > Thedomainedegrammont*

Location : Avenue Albert Einstein Open to the public all year round.  Near the Eastern entrance to Montpellier, this 90-ha estate houses various activities : The drama center of the "Treize Vents" theater, the Rock-music Hall, a sports and leisure activity complex, the school's outdoor centre... The former monastery of Grammont in the 12th century, its beautiful park and carefully maintained green areas (You will find a several century-old cedar-tree and numerous tree species) make it an enticing place to visit.
