# THE ESPLANADE GARDENS

*Europe > France > Midi > Languedoc > Montpellier > Walks > Theesplanadegardens*

Location : Esplanade Charles de Gaulle (place de la Comédie)  It is a well-shadowed plane-tree-lined promenade from Comédie Square to the Corum, adorned with fountains, weatherings, play areas and benches to take a little rest. To the right, the Champ de Mars, a former military area tranformed into a public garden in 1900, is now one of Montpellier inhabitants favorite places to take a stroll.
