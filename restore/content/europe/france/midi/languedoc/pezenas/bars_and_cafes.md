# Bars and Cafes in Pezenas

*Europe > France > Midi > Languedoc > Pezenas > Bars And Cafes*

No matter if you are looking for a place to grab a light refreshment or to enjoy a 3-course meal the **Pezenas Bars and Cafes** are some of the best places to go. If you are looking for a great breakfast, going to a café will save up to 50 percent when compared to what you would pay for a breakfast inside your hotel.

**Café de la Bourse**

The Café de la Bourse is located along the main car parking area and is a great spot for people watching. Whether you are sitting inside enjoying a light refreshment or sitting outside soaking up the sun you will have ample time to enjoy the various people walking by. Inside the Café de la Bourse, you will find an entertaining, but relaxed atmosphere.

**The English Shop & Tea Room**

If you are looking for a great place to grab a quick bite to eat for breakfast or even possibly for brunch The English Shop & Tea Room is a great place to go. The English Shop & Tea Room is the best place around to get a traditional English breakfast. The only thing that you need to watch out for is if you are coming for breakfast on Saturday, it can be difficult to get a table because Saturday is market day. The homemade scones come highly recommended, but so does the “Full English.”

**Brasserie Moliere**

If you are looking for a friendly place to spend the evening, Brasserie Moliere is a great place to go. If you are looking for something besides a refreshing drink or even a quick bite to eat you can come here for some great people watching because of its location, but you can also enjoy the dance floor that they provide.

**Café-Brasserie du Midi**

The Café-Brasserie du Midi is the best place to go if you are looking for a refreshing beer during the evening. The Café-Brasserie du Midi offers their customers a variety of beers to choose from, which means you will be able to find at least 1 beer that suits your tastes. This is also a great place to go if you are looking for a new beer to try because of how many different beers they have to offer.

**Le Glacier**

If you are looking for a great place for lunch, Le Glacier is an amazing place to go. One of their best dishes is the crepes, which you can get just about any style of crepe that you desire. One of the best places to go if you are traveling with children because they have a separate children’s menu, which not all cafes do even though most all welcome children.
