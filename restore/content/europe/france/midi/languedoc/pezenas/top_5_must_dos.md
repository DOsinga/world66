# Top 5 Must Do's in Pezenas

*Europe > France > Midi > Languedoc > Pezenas > Top 5 Must Dos*

**Pezenas Must Do's **are comprised of several unique places, museums, and monuments. The charming French town is most certainly not short of memorable attractions, but there are a few that stand-out.

**Musee du Jouet**

The Musee du Jouet is a museum completely dedicated to toys. The museum may be small, but it celebrates every type of toy ever created. Over 5,000 years of history are explored in nearly 16,000 toys from around the world. It has one of the richest collections of European toys. The toys are divided into different genres such as dolls, mechanical toys, construction toys and stuffed toys. There is even a section for antiquities. Operating hours for Musee du Jouetvary change with the season. Admission is 5 euros.

**Church of Sainte-Jean-de-Bebain**

The Church of Sainte-Jean-de-Bebain is actually classified as a historic monument. The Church was a significant place during the 17th century and its importance still reigns true today. The front of the church blends slightly into its surroundings, but suddenly the romantic architecture will spring out to enchant visitors. Photographers will adore taking pictures of the building. The Church of Sainte-Jean-de-Bebain is a wonderful symbol of the charming town's past and definitely appropriate for Pezenas Must Do's.

**Old Town Center**

A stroll through the old town center of Pezenas is an experience like no other. It's exactly what a traveler would expect to find in an old French town. The streets are narrow and visitors will surely get excited about the sights around the next corner. Elegant buildings from the 16th century line the tiny, winding streets. Small shops and cafes are also in this beautiful area of Pezenas. The old town of Pezenas has been well-preserved and still resembles the Medieval town that it once was. This addition to Pezenas Must Do's allows travelers to immerse themselves in the French town and explore it by foot.

**Door Museum**

Perhaps one of the most unique museums in the world, the Door museum is a must-do while in Pezenas. This unusual museum is located in the heart of the old town and is surprisingly more interesting than one would expect. The doors and their frames date back to the 15th century. The collection also includes door knockers and iron work. All pieces in the museum are from Pezenas and the surrounding area. The museum is closed on Mondays. Operating hours change depending on the time of the year.

**Molière Monument**

Moliere left a lasting impression on the town of Pezenas. His influence can be seen in theaters and artwork across the town. The most notable homage to Moliere is the Moliere Monument. The famed French sculpture Jean Antoine Injalbert was commissioned to create the monument. The sculpture depicts a bust of Moliere above a woman and a seated man. The craftsmanship of the pieces is undeniable. The clean lines and attention to detail are what makes this a must-see.
