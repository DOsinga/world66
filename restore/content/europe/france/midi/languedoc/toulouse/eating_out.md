# Eating Out

*Europe > France > Midi > Languedoc > Toulouse > Eating Out*

**Caffe Luce**
*8 place de l'Estrapade, 31300 Toulouse*
Situated on a lovely square in St Cyprien, Caffe Luce offers fresh italian food at a reasonable price served on its cute terrasse.
And don't hesitate to talk to the owner, Fefe is a really easy going guy.
