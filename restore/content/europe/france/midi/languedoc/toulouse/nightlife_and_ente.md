# Nightlife and Entertainment in Toulouse

*Europe > France > Midi > Languedoc > Toulouse > Nightlife And Ente*

Toulouse, situated in the southwest of France and not far from the
border with Spain, is an ideal place to spend a few days exploring the
charms of a quaint European city, but most people traveling in the city
look forward to experiencing the **Toulouse nightlife. **It is a
western city that is brimming with history and culture. Known
colloquially as ‘the Pink City’ (Ville Rose) because of the color of the
brickwork and intricate architecture in many parts of the city,
Toulouse is also the home of one of the richest and most diverse
cultures in France, including good music at many clubs and pubs.

**La Tantina de Bourgos**

La Tantina de Bourgos, located at 27 rue de la Garonette, is a
popular place for the younger crowd because of its striking Latin style.
If you are fond of listening to live Latino beats, then this energetic
club is the right place to satisfy your nightlife cravings while in the
city of Toulouse. On Thursdays, you will certainly be entertained with
the jazz music that fills the air. DJs are also available all
throughout the week, so expect fun and liveliness every single night.

**Monsieur Carnaval**

Being a trendy club, Monsieur Carnaval will surely keep you rocking
and rolling the night away. This famous bar schedules both live and
recorded music, so you can enjoy songs from both the bands and the DJs.
The club is situated rue Bayard, a place where you can feel the French
style of nightlife with young people 35 years old and younger.

**Le Purple**

To experience the vibrant nightlife of the city, travelers often go
to Le Purple. Being the city’s most popular disco, it plays various
genre of music that you will surely enjoy while dancing with the locals.
This discotheque offers early bookings, so why not reserve a table and
gather your circle of friends before other club goers get the dance
floor crowded?

**Le Frigo**

As cool as its name, Le Frigo--or The Fridge--is the right place where
you can see people dancing while lively music pumps out of the speakers.
Although the club decor is simple, the monthly all-nighters
still come back for fun while listening to energetic music and meeting
new friends.

**Animae Bar**

If you like thunderous house music, then the Animae Bar will surely
suit your taste. This night club always plays loud music on week nights
and jazz or pop music during the weekends. Expect the happy hours at
midnight, 7 days a week, so prepare your cool dance moves and party
all night long.
