# Top 5 Must Do's in Toulouse

*Europe > France > Midi > Languedoc > Toulouse > Top 5 Must Dos*

Discover **Toulouse must do’s**. Toulouse is a city located in
southwest France, close to the Pyrenees and at the center of the
Mediterranean and the Atlantic. This city is also popularly known as the
pink city. It has a lot of history, and in the past, Toulouse was among
the wealthiest cities in France because of its manufacture and sale of
the blue pastel which were made from woad plant extracts. Only when
Portugal began importing the color indigo to Europe did this monopoly of
Toulouse fall. Up to the present, more than 50 mansions and hotels are
witness to the earlier greatness of this city. You, too, can experience
the history and richness of culture in Toulouse with these top 5 must
do’s in this city.
**Roam the Streets of Toulouse**

The humble city of Toulouse is not very big, which suggests walking
as the best option when you want to explore all the beauty of the city.
If you want to go someplace else other than the center of the city, you
can take public transport through a system of trains and buses. The
train system basically has two routes, one is traveling from east to
west (line A) and another traveling from north to south (line B) and
vice versa. On the other hand, the bus always misses its estimated time
of arrival, but it is definitely worth the wait.
**Study in Toulouse**

Toulouse is ranked second in France to have the largest number of
students. Students come to learn engineering, aeronautics, business and
other courses through a the many universities in Toulouse. As a result,
there are a number of libraries you can visit.
**Enjoy Food in Toulouse**

Toulouse offers the best food in the region. The main specialty of
Toulouse is duck and a lot of restaurants are offering this delicacy in
their menus, especially dinner. At lunchtime, visit the Victor Hugo
market and experience local dining at very affordable prices.
**Shop at Toulouse**

Enjoy shopping in different places. Boutiques in Toulouse are open
from Monday to Saturday. You can also check out souvenirs sold by the
Airbus Company, or you can buy second-hand items through their shops.
**Sleep in Toulouse**

Toulouse offers more than 50 chains of hotels. Get a five-star
treatment from a hotel’s friendly management and staff.
