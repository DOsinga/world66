# van Gogh's Nightcafe

*Europe > France > Midi > Provence > Arles > Sights > Van Goghs Nightca*

This French cafe with it's distinctive yellow awning was painted by van Gogh in 1888. It is still there. You can find it at Place du Forum in the heart of Arles.
