# Lac des Bouillouses Travel Guide

*Europe > France > Midi > Pyrenees > Lac Des Bouillouse*

The best resource for sights, hotels, restaurants, bars, what to do and see

The Lac des Bouillouses (*Bollosa* in Catalan) and its surroundings is a *site naturel classé* (a classified natural site) since 1976. Since the 1980s, it has become a frequently visited summer destination.

The lake provides relatively easy access to the [Pic Carlit](/europe/france/midi/pyrenees/pic_carlit), the local highest peak, very popular with trekkers (*randonneurs*), as well as the Pic Péric.
