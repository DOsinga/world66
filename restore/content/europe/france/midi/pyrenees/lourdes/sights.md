**The Miraculous Medal Museum**

Reconstitution of the life of St Catherine Labouré. Life-size wax figurines dressed in genuine clothes. Historical diorama of the Miraculous Medal (light and sound display) : reconstitution of the events that lead to the striking of the Miraculous Medal in 1832. 

address: 3, rue du Porche

tel: 05 62 94 42 21

**The Panorama of St Bernadette’s life**

Audiovisual reconstitution of the life of Bernadette and the apparitions.

tel: 05 62 94 43 84

**The Nativity museum **

The nativity Museum brings you the animated crib of Jerusalem (reconstitution of Jesus’ birth), a Biblical landscape made of animated Santons, retracing Jesus’ childhood in Jerusalem.  

address: 21, Quai Saint Jean

tel: 05 62 94 71 00

**The Little Lourdes Museum**

The Little Lourdes takes visitors into the past, in a miniature world to discover the history and birth of Lourdes in 1858. Wandering in this natural environment, visitors can admire its old stone houses, its streams and its old mills, reconstituted in a pleasant flower and bonsai garden… 

address: 68, avenue Peyramale

tel: 05 62 94 24 36
