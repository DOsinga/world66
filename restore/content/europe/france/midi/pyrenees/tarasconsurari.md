# Tarascon-sur-Ariège

*Europe > France > Midi > Pyrenees > Tarasconsurari*

The town of Tarascon between Foix and the Spanish border is pleasant in itself and a good centre for a break but what makes it special is the Cave at Niaux nearby with ancient cave paintings of bisons and other animals.

http://www.showcaves.com/english/fr/showcaves/Niaux.html
