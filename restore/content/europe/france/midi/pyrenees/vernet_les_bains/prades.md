# Prades Travel Guide

*Europe > France > Midi > Pyrenees > Vernet Les Bains > Prades*

The best resource for sights, hotels, restaurants, bars, what to do and see

Situated in the
heart of the Pyrenees Orientales (a department of
Languedoc Roussillon), Prades is a small town with a weekly market and
all amenities are within walking distance. Nestled in the Têt Valley, set away from the road between
Perpignan and Font Romeu. It's a small town with a village feel and has
a magnificent church in the centre of the town.

It is an ideal base for a winter or summer holiday, being 50 minutes
away from both excellent ski resorts (Les Angles, Font Romeu,..) and
the sandy beaches of the Mediterranean (Canet Plage, Argelès-sur-Mer).

It
is 50 minutes from the Mediterranean beaches, 40 minutes drive from
Perpignan and 50 minutes drive from a choice of 9 ski resorts. There is a public swimming pool in Prades, river swimming a 5 minute drive away, and a large lake with swimming, dinghy sailing and a cafe 15 minutes away.
