# Lille Beaches

*Europe > France > Nord > Lille > Beaches > Lille Beaches*

Lille is right by the coastline in Northern France. You will find some
beautiful beaches there, and many restaurants to try out the french
cuisine. Lille has many narrow streets packed full with bars and
restaurants. French food and drink is one of the Highlights of France,
the Beaches are alright to but somtime it is very cold! But in Summer
you will be missing out by not taking a visit there.
