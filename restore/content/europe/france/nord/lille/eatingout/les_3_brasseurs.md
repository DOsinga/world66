# Les 3 Brasseurs

*Europe > France > Nord > Lille > Eatingout > Les 3 Brasseurs*

Nice restaurant with a lively atmosphere. It is situated right across
from the train station Lille-Flandres so you can't miss it. The
selection consists mainly of crepes, galettes with all sorts of
toppings to choose from. It's a brewery so the beer selection is great.
