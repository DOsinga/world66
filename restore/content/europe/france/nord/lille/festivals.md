# Festivals

*Europe > France > Nord > Lille > Festivals*

In the beginning of September come to Lille to experience the
"Braderie." The largest flea market on Europe with over 3 million
visitors and lots of shopping. It lasts all weekend when almost all of
Lille's streets are turned into pedestrian areas with lots of music and
entertainment. Be prepared to come early to stake out the best deals on
everything from antiques to LPs, books, clothes, furniture, etc. During
lunch enjoy the famous "Moules-Frites" dish. By the end of the day
you'll see mountains of "moules" stacked up on the sidewalks! Enjoy!
