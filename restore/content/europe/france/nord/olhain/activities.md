# Things to do

*Europe > France > Nord > Olhain > Activities*

**all**

**hiking**

" The Park path" (4 km waymarked walking paths ) will allow you to discover the whole Olhain site by combining the discovery of the flora and fauna and unusual sceneries. You are guaranteed a new surrounding !  

**golf **

Golf can be practised from 7 to 77. Space amateur, soft sport practice enthusiast, Olhain golf welcomes you with its conviviality : it is never too late to play golf. (training sessions and booking tel 03 21 02 17 03). 

**cross country ski**

Whenever we wonder which is the lowest cross country ski resort in France, everybody should be able to answer : OLHAIN. At more than 180 metres high, Olhain Park offers several kilometres of tracks to go cross country sking.  (rentals and booking tel 03 21 27 91 79).  

**tree hiking**

Tree hiking become "Treenaute" on planet Olhain ! On top of the Olhain Park forest, the branches of a tall beech will transport you between earth and sky, and the Great Green is there, as far as the eye can see. The soft contact on the bark, the wind rustling in the leaves... It is a travel on an [more..](/europe/france/nord/olhain/activities/treehiking)
