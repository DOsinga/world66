# Day Trips in Nantes

*Europe > France > Normandy > Nantes > Day Trips*

**Day Trip from Nantes to Carnac**

**Nantes day trips** can be filled with fun if one chooses the right destinations. Various picturesque attractions around the area provide good options for a short, memorable day visit around Nantes.

****

One of the popular excursions from Nantes is a day trip to the historic commune of Carnac. It is easy to reach Carnac by road in less than two hours. Ferry is also a popular alternative to make this trip by the sea.

**Prehistoric Exploration**

Carnac is famous around the world for its thousands of prehistoric stones that give the place a mystique similar to the pyramids of Egypt and other such ancient locations. There are several other monuments as well as museum of prehistory to be explored for those who are fascinated by ancient culture and history.

**Camping in Carnac**** **

Carnac is surrounded by scenic woods and lakes. There are several camping sites that tourists can opt for if they're looking for a day or night camping adventure.

**Windsurfing in Carnac**** **

A Carnac day trip can be wonderful for beach lovers and windsurfing enthusiasts. Saint Colomban Beach in this area is renowned for its windsurfing facilities and its windy environment.

**Lunch at Carnac**** **

The place offers a wide range of restaurants and cafeterias. Outdoor eating can be great fun here in the midst of scenic locales and sunny beaches. Most restaurants have an elaborate menu and offer affordably priced fare.

The day trip can be a good opportunity for the whole family, because there is something for everyone at Carnac.
