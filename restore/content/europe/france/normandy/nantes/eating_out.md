# Eating Out in Nantes

*Europe > France > Normandy > Nantes > Eating Out*

**French Dining in Nantes, France at its Finest**

French food is world famous for its decadently rich taste, and you will find that **Nantes restaurants** are where you will find some of France&rsquo;s finest dining. Those looking for some French food to enjoy will find everything from quaint outdoor cafes to simply breathtaking fine dining restaurants. If you are in Nantes, France and are looking for a traditional gourmet French dining experience, you need to dine at Pomme D&rsquo;Or.

**Pomme D&rsquo;Or**

One tip that you should keep in mind before you visit this standout among Nantes restaurants is not to eat during the hours before you are to dine at Pomme D&rsquo;Or. You want to make sure that you have a very healthy appetite before sitting down to eat. You can enjoy a fabulous three course meal along with delightful "cheeses and desserts." Eating at this fine dining restaurant is truly a delight for the senses. While you are waiting for your meal to be brought to the table, you can take in the gorgeous decorations that surround you while you also enjoy the sights and smells of all the food being served. When your meal is served, you will want to take the time to savor each and every bite. Top that off with a bottle of wine during dinner and an espresso to end the meal, and you have had one of the best meals that Nantes restaurants can offer you.

Now while the Pomme D&rsquo;Or is priced slightly higher than average, it will be money well spent to have a truly authentic French dining experience.
