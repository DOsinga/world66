# Getting Around in Nantes

*Europe > France > Normandy > Nantes > Getting Around*

If you are going to Nantes and want to know how to travel around the area, there are a few **Nantes transportation** choices. Some of the popular choices are bus, tram and train.

#### Check the Bus Services

The city bus service is a great way to travel around Nantes. Board the bus at any of the stops along the street and choose the route you need. The buses make many stops, so you will want to watch carefully as you approach your destination. Be sure to check the route before you board since there are many routes.

#### Speed your Travel with Trams

The trams are one of the best ways to travel in the city. The high-speed trams will take you throughout the city and are a fast way to get to where you are going. You can get tickets for the tram at any of the stations, and day passes will allow you to ride all day if you plan on doing a lot of traveling. The trams are one of the best Nantes transportation choices to see a lot of different stops.

#### Enjoy the Routes with Trains

The trains are slower to use, but they will take you on a beautiful route around the area. Use them to travel to anywhere in the area you want to see. They are affordable, and you can get your tickets and find out more about each of the train routes at the station.
