# Carnac

*Europe > France > Normandybrittany > Carnac*

Carnac is a stunning guide. The first question you will ask yourself is : why on earth did they put all these stones here 4000 years ago? I'am afraid no one has a very clear answer to that question. But the 3000 menhirs aligned in 13 parallel rows is very impressive.

 The site is a touristic highlight in Brittany and can be quite busy. Parts of it can now only be seen from a distant because some visitors wnated to touch, push etc. the stones.
