**Strassburg Villa**

Since the beginning of the century, Deauville has succeeded in establishing a Norman and neo-Norman architectural style which has contributed significantly to the city's charm. The Strassburger Villa is the most emblematic exemple of the richness of Deauville's architecture. The Villa now belongs to the City of Deauville. In an effort to promote its heritage, Deauville allows you to visit this jewel of the local architectural scene.  [more..](/europe/france/normandybrittany/deauville/sights/strassburgvilla)

**Mont-Canisy **

The quiet of nature at the doors of Deauville Mont Canisy is located 110 meters above sea level. The 13 hectares of the Mont offer a beautiful natural setting as well as a fascinating bit of history. Walking tours are availables at the tourism office. 

**Calouste Gulbenkian park **

At the doors of Deauville, the Calouste Gulbenkian park aka. Domaine des Enclos represents all by itself the beauty of Normandy's many gardens. This 24 hectares park has been the property of Deauville since 1973. It is planted with many rare varieties of plant.
