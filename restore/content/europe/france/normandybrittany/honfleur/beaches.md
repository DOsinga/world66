# Beaches

*Europe > France > Normandybrittany > Honfleur > Beaches*

The Plage Municipal of Honfleur is okay but not spectacular. The view is actually quite terrible: you look directly to the Zone Industrial of [Le havre](/europe/france/normandybrittany/lehavre). The beach is nice for children though, there is a playground and a small pool for children. Parking is free. Further down south the Cote Fleurie there are some other coastal towns with better beaches.

**all**

**Etretat**

[Etretat](/europe/france/normandybrittany/etretat) is just 10 km down the coast from Honfleur. The beaches here are good, although they are very, very small at high tide.
