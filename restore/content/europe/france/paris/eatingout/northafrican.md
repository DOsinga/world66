**Wally Saharien**

Original maghreb cooking, couscous and taboule, cosy atmosphere, menues about 200 FF, open every day till 23.30. Métro: Pont Marie  

address: 16, rue le Regrattier, Paris

tel: (01) 43 25 01 39

**Le Fouta Djalon**

African restaurant near the Republique. Service can be a bit slow, but the food is great. 

address: 27, bd St. Martin
