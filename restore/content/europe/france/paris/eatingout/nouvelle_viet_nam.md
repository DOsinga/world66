# Nouvelle Viet Nam

*Europe > France > Paris > Eatingout > Nouvelle Viet Nam*

Located in the 13eme, this restaurant has GREAT food with a reasonable price. Here you can try Laotion and Thai dishes as well as Vietnamese. There is a che (sweet bean dessert) bar in the front.

Get there early, because the line can be long and there are no reservations. There is a tea salon next door that is owned by the same people that will offer you the food.
