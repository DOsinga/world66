# Les Bains-Douches

*Europe > France > Paris > Nightlife > Lesbains*

A former Turkish bath house, was once one of the hippest clubs of Paris, playing house, rap and funk and the odd rock band. Nowadays it is still an OK club, but if you're looking for the 'to be seen' crowd, look elsewhere. Open daily 11.30pm - 6am.
