# Parks, Gardens & Zoos

*Europe > France > Paris > Sights > Parksgardenszoos*

Paris has many parks where you can sit down and relax. Most of them are typical French style parks: white gravel, few trees, hardly any grass, a fountain in the middle and benches with old men and sleeping clochards. Although this may not sound very cosy, they have a certain grandeur and are actually very nice. Here are a few highlighted ones. 

**Sights**

**Pere Lachaise**
