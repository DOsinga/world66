# Le Marais

*Europe > France > Paris > Things To Do > Le Marais*

Spend a day in Paris's most fashionable district. Located on a collection of streets north of place de la Bastille, Le Marais is home to kitschy Paris hotels, funky boutiques, trendy bars, and unique Paris restaurants and cafes.
