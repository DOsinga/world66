# Benze

*Europe > Georgia > Batumi > Eatingout > Benze*

Despite the comparatively large size – holding 150 people- the restaurant creates a cozy atmosphere. The service is of high level. The highly-qualified staff and cooks will never disappoint even the great gourmets.
“Benze” serves delicious Georgian dishes. There is only live music.
The yard is the adornment of the restaurant. There is nothing more pleasant than sitting in the yard in summer – owing to the closeness to the sea, eucalyptus and fir trees it is always cool here, and the picturesque views of the sea will lavishly spread in front of your eyes.
