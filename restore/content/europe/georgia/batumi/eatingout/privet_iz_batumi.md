# Privet iz Batumi

*Europe > Georgia > Batumi > Eatingout > Privet Iz Batumi*

A fashionable café “Privet iz Batuma” is situated in the city center, with pleasant environment, modern standards, pictures of old Batumi, sailor-suited waiters and high service. “Privit iz BAtuma” offers various pizza, pancake, salads. The café is good for ice-cream, cakes and deserts. There is also open terrace, with outdoor tables.
