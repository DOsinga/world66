# Slim Cafe

*Europe > Georgia > Batumi > Eatingout > Slim Cafe*

Slim cafe is situated in the city center. Its interior is long and thin, with tasteful style. Slim café serves wonderful pancakes with fruit and chocolate dripping, assorted sandwiches, soups, various pizza and salads. There is also open terrace, with outdoor tables.
