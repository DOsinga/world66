# Sport Bar

*Europe > Georgia > Batumi > Eatingout > Sport Bar*

The bar attracts visitors with its unique design. Dim and divergent interior illumination creates quiet and pleasant environment. The Sport bar is equipped with a projector and plasma monitor which enable the visitors watch live transmissions in all types of sports while enjoying delicious dishes of the cuisine. You can enjoy the atmosphere created by low music and high-level service together with your family, friends or business partners.
