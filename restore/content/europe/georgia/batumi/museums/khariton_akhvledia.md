# Khariton Akhvlediani Adjara State Museum

*Europe > Georgia > Batumi > Museums > Khariton Akhvledia*

The museum was established in 1908, it houses archeological materials of XII-IX cc. B.C.: engraved axes, bronze ram, the Gonio Treasury samples Intaglio with Aphrodite and Eros images, I-II cc. golden engraved phial, golden figurine of discus-thrower, golden pendants with images of Helios and decorative motifs, beads made of various materials, fragments of belts (gold, pasta), brooch, religious bronze sculpture, fragments of truck decorations with images of oak leaves and acorns; ethnographic, folk and applied art samples of the nature (25 billion old fossilized tree).

The museum collection: 183241 items.
