# Nightlife and Entertainment

*Europe > Georgia > Batumi > Nightlifeandente*

Nightlife is limited to drinking and loud music. There are a few good hangouts in Gorki kucha and a few nice places in the center.

**all**

**Privet Batumi Bar**

Hippest place in Batumi. Old photo's of Batumi on the wall, waiters are dressed as sailors. They have imported beer and real espresso. Not the cheapest of bars around.

address: Stalin kucha 39

**Heineken bar**

A bit outside of the center, but an okay place, with imported beers.

address: Gorki kucha
