# Short sightseeing to Mzkheta

*Europe > Georgia > Tbilisi > Day Trips > Short Sightseeing*

Located in 20 km. from Tbilisi City center Mtskheta is beloved place for local travellers and foreign visitors of Country. Favourite places to visit in

the City of Mtskheta are Jvari, Svetitskhoveli Cathedral and Samtavro Convent. After Lunch in the local food restaurant visit Shio Mghvime and small trekking to Zeda Zeni monastery.
