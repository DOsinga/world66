# Eating Out

*Europe > Georgia > Tbilisi > Eating Out*

The area of Tbilisi with the most restaurants is around Akhvlediani Street – still better-known by its old name Perovskaya. The area lies between two landmarks: the Philharmonia concert hall, and Republic Square (where the giant Iveria Hotel still houses hundreds of refugees from the unrest in Abkhazia).  

**Restaurants**

**East restaurant **

Opposite the Beatles Club and diagonally opposite the Philharmonia concert hall has good Chinese food. 

address: 22 Kostava Street

**Toucan Bar**

With a good range of well-cooked food at reasonable prices, this is a popular place with travalers and locals alike. 

**Mirage restaurant**

Great views and good food. 

address: Near the Sheraton Metechi Palace but on the river side of the main road.

**Sakartvelo**

Good food at reasonable prices in a luxurious setting, between the Philharmonia concert hall and the University. 

**Hip Restaurant**

Don't know the name of this place. It's accross the street from the Georgian State Art museum. Down stairs there is a restaurant with good local specialities and cheap booze. Upstairs there is a bar with a buffet and real espresso!
