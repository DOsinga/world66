# Airlines

*Europe > Georgia > Tbilisi > Gettingthere > Airlines*

Airlines currently serving Tbilisi:

- British Airways from London Heathrow
- Lufhansa from Munich
- Aeroflot from Moscow (SVO)
- Turkish Airlines from Istanbul
- Austrian Airlines from Vienna
- Air Baltic from Riga

- FlyLal from Vilnius

In addition, a Georgian airline called Airzena (www.airzena.com), has direct flights to Paris, Amsterdam, Frankfurt, Vienna, Athens, Dubai, Moscow (DME), Kiev and Tel Aviv using various series of Boeing 737’s and Yak-42D. Toll free reservation number form the US is 1-800-220-3106 and online booking is also available.
