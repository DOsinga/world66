# The Open-Air Folk Museum

*Europe > Georgia > Tbilisi > Museums > Theopenairfolkmuseum*

Nice collection of traditional houses built in various regional styles. (Don’t be put off by its ugly Soviet title of the "Museum of Ethnography".) It’s situated in Victory/Vake Park. ** Rick:** Outstanding views of Tbilisi from this vantage point. This museum is set up much like Williamsburg, Virginia with houses constructed from ancient times to relatively recently. Wine was everything and worshipped appropriately. See how early Georgians improvised their defence from an onslaught of invaders. An excellent personal tour is available.
