# Georgia – Wine Cradle

*Europe > Georgia > Tours And Excursio > Georgia  Wine C*

Approximately 8 thousand years ago our ancestors tamed the wild vine, squeezed grape, produced wine and its name was spread all over the world. It is the beginning of Georgian civilization. Vine and corns, wine and bread was the beginning of the self-affirmation of Georgia, was the statement of the national consciousness and easy thinking. Bread and wine is trouble and happiness, concomitance and wreath of dignity for Georgian people.

Georgia is the motherland of vine taming. Vine cultivating (more than 500 denominations) is insisted by not only Georgian but by foreign scientists in their archeological, historical, ethnographical and linguistic sources. For thousand centuries vineyard and wine-making was the major industry in Georgian agriculture. It was the material well-being and spiritual life for the nation. It’s same to date.
