# Peak Ushba. North peak 4694 m. Category 4 A.

*Europe > Georgia > Tours And Excursio > Peak Ushba North*

**Day 1.** Our guide will meet you in Tbilisi Airport with poster: "IRIDIUM-TRAVEL". Luggage carriers will be at your service at Airport. Formalities at the Airport (visas, passport control, luggage delivery). Transfer Airport-Hotel (15 km) by comfortable minibus. Hotel. Light lunch during check-in (cool or hot drinks, sandwiches). Rest. Small sightseeing tour to Tbilisi. Supper.

* The plan of the 1st day may be changed, according to the time of your arrival. The Company will undertake all costs for extra cultural measures and meals

**Day 2.** Breakfast at the hotel. Check-out. Set out at 9 a.m. in comfortable minibus. Travel to the province Svanei, north-west Georgia. Tbilisi-Becho (460 km, 8-10 hour). Dinner in the road. Arrival to a vil. Becho. Dislocation in guesthouse. Supper.

**Day 3.** Breakfast. Inventory of equipment and food, preparation, instructions. Dinner, Free time. Supper.

**Day 4.** Breakfast. Road to camp 1. Rest. Dinner. Arrival to camp 1. "Akhotnichi" ("Hunters'") (2800 m). Supper. Night in hunters' shelter.

**Day 5.** Breakfast. Set out to camp 2. Ushba plateau (3500 m). Camp break. Supper. Stay for a night in tents. Thorough meal according to situation.

**Day 6.** Continuation of a route. Ascend to the "Ushba Pillow" (3800 m). Camp 3. Stay for a night.

**Day 7.** Ascend to the mountain. Descend. Stay for a night in camp 3.

**Day 8.** Descend to Becho. Supper. Stay for a night in guest-house.

**Day 9.** Wake up at 4 a.m. Breakfast. Departure by minibus. Becho-Tbilisi (460 km, 8-10 hour). Dinner in the road. Tbilisi. Visit to sulfur baths, the legend about foundation of Tbilisi is connected with. Dislocation in hotel. Farewell supper.

**Day 10.** Reserve. In case the reserve day is not used during ascend to the peak, the Company will offer you historical-informational tour.

**Day 11.** Breakfast in hotel. Check-out. Transfer Hotel-Airport.

* The plans for this day might be changed, according to the time of flight. The company id responsible for arranging additional events and covering food expenses.
