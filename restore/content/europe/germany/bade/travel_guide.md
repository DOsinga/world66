# Travel Guide in Bade

*Europe > Germany > Bade > Travel Guide*

By using a **Tubingen travel guide**, you will be able to plan out every aspect of your vacation. Tubingen is a university town, and is therefore filled with pubs and cafes so that students can hang out and study, or just chat about what has been going on.

**Get Around
**
The best way to get around town is by bicycle, but keep in mind that the town is rather hilly, so you will get a workout. Buses can also be used to get around town, but at night only certain routes are available. An all day bus pass can be purchased for about $5.

**Sights

**The city center is one of the most interesting places to visit in town because of the cobblestone that lines all of the roads, but also the old churches. You can also go down to the Necker River to watch people who are punting, which the river is located right next to the city center. You can even give punting a try yourself if you happen to be in town during the summer.

**Restaurants
**
Neckarmuller is one of the best places to eat in town because you can enjoy your meal outside while looking out on the Neckar River. Zwiebelkuchen, which is an onion dish, comes highly recommended. Brezeln that is served with a regional beer is something else
to try. Mauganeschtle is a great place to eat, especially for special occasions. The best dish to try is Maultasche, which is similar to large ravioli.

**Accommodations
**
One of the best places to stay in town is the youth hostel that is located near the river and within walking distance to the city center. If you are visiting during the summer months, you can go camping along the Neckar River. If you want to stay in a hotel, try the Hotel am Schloss for about $110 a night.

**Nightlife
**
During the spring and summer months, spending an evening in the pubs is very relaxing because of the outside tables that are provided, which allows you to people watch until late at night. Schwarzlocher Hof is a great place to go; while there, make sure you try a
Mostbowle. Tangente Jour is a great place to go for some people watching and a glass of beer.
