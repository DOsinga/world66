# Baden-Baden

*Europe > Germany > Badenwrttemberg > Badenbaden*

Baden-Baden is built on a series of hills at the foot of the Black Forrest. As the name suggests Baden-Baden is most renowned for its curative baths and hot springs. It has been for centuries, all the way back to Roman Times.

 The town was spared from WW II bombings and retains the grandeur of the 19-th century, with luxurious villas, stately mansions and tree lined avenues.
