# Friedrichsbad

*Europe > Germany > Badenwrttemberg > Badenbaden > Spas > Friedrichsbad*

19-th century posh. 21 euro for a bathing program of three or more hours. Open Monday to Sunday.

Rather regimented with a suggested time for each section but a beautiful building and quite an experience. Males and females separate and leave everthing except what God gave them in a locker. An attendent provides a sheet that is used to lie on in a series of dry saunas. The sheet is taken away before being soaked and scrbbed and then moving on to the steam room. The suite of pools after this is mixed. Most are warm except for a cold central one under a painted domed ceiling. Back into the single sex section to steam again, shower, cold plunge pool and then moisturising and relaxing before rejoining the real world.

You will leave feeling relaxed and liberated.
