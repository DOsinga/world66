# Brennessel

*Europe > Germany > Badenwrttemberg > Freiburg > Eatingout > Brennessel*

The Brennnessel (german for stinging-nettle) is famous for its daily special offer from 18-19.30h: Spaghetti bolognese for only 1,80 € (you need to be early). It is located nerby the train station.
