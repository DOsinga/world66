# Getting There

*Europe > Germany > Badenwrttemberg > Heidelberg > Getting There*

Heidelberg has good railway connections to major German and European Cities, several major highways passing by, and is not far from the airport in Frankfurt. 



**By air:** 

Frankfurt (FRA): Germany's biggest airport is 80km/55mi north of Heidelberg. Make a reservation for the hls-airportshuttle (mail@hls-online.com). They charge 60,00 € (Heidelberg-Frankfurt or v.v.) for the first person and 9,00 € for each more person ( 2 pers.=69,00 / 3 = 78,00 / 4 = 87,00 / 5 = 96,00 / 6 = 105,00 / 7 = 114,00 / 8= 123,00). 



**By car:** 

Coming on A5, switch to A656 at Autobahnkreuz Heidelberg, direction Heidelberg. You can also enter Heidelberg on B3 (Bergstraße) or B37.
