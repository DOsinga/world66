# Public Transport

*Europe > Germany > Badenwrttemberg > Heidelberg > Gettingaround > Publictransport*

All parts of the city are well served by public transport. It is managed by VRN, a regional traffic organisation. At the two major stations Hauptbahnhof (railway station) and Bismarckplatz there are offices to get directions or general assistance. The one-way fare is 3,40 DM everywhere inside Heidelberg. For extensive use there are a 24h ticket (10 DM) and a 3-days ticket (20 DM).
