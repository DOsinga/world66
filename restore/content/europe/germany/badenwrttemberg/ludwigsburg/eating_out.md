# Eating Out in Ludwigsburg

*Europe > Germany > Badenwrttemberg > Ludwigsburg > Eating Out*

Ludwigsburg is in the southwestern region of Germany. Although the
downtown area only has a grid of 20 streets, there are many restaurants
in which to eat. These restaurants offer a wide variety of
Ludwigsburg’s regional favorites.

**Marktplatz**

The Market Square or Marktplatz is the main place to go downtown and
there are small food vendors where you can get local food. There are
many small vendors right out on the street and others that have seating
areas outside and in. You will pay under $10 for many meals in the
square and then you can go shopping.

**The Palace**

Ludwigsburg is the home of a large palace that is almost as big as
the downtown area. It was built in 1704 by Duke Egerhard Ludwig and
the town is named after him. This palace has several museums, gardens,
and many popular restaurants are nearby.

**Schlosstuble**

Schlosstuble in the Schloss Hotel is a restaurant in town with
German and International Cuisine. It is about 1 block from the palace
the building is about 150 years old. The Red Room is a beautiful place
in which to eat with the ornate wooden ceiling and gold baroque walls.
It is certainly elegant and is for small parties. The rest of the
restaurant is decorated luxuriously and it will feel like you went back
in time. The salads are crisp and tasty and the schnitzel with noodles
is pure heaven. The prices are very reasonable.

**Alte Sonne**

Alte Sonne provides you with a great dinner with modern German
Flair. It is a little pricy but the food is great. The ambiance is very
stylish and baroque, yet homey.

**Ratskeller Café**

The Ratskeller Cafe offers outdear seating and local cuisine. It is highly recommended for breakfast or brunch. Plates range in price from $4 (lunch) to $20 (dinner).

**Castle Restaurant**

There is a small terrace restaurant at the castle where live music
is played during the summer. Lunch is the best time to eat here. Be
sure to check out the desserts.

**Goldener Pflug**

Goldener Pflug is on the West End and is also a Hotel as well as a
restaurant. It is stylish and the food is beautifully presented. They
have a wonderful wine list, several types of beer and other non
alcoholic beverages. You will pay anywhere from $12-$40 for a dinner.

The rich German Cuisine is a feature of Ludwigsburg and seen in
almost every restaurant. Check out some of the small coffee shops along
the streets as well for cheap but very good tasting food.
