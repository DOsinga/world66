# Ravensburg

*Europe > Germany > Badenwrttemberg > Ravensburg 1*

A small, but lovely town 45 minutes trainride south of Ulm, famous for its puzzles, also known as the gaming town. It is in close proximity of [Lake constance](/europe/germany/badenwrttemberg/lakeconstance).
