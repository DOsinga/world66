**The Lonetal** - famous for its archeological discoveries. It was here that the famous "Löwenmensch" was found - scarved from the tusk of a mammoth more then 30.000 years ago, the one of the first and most impressive piece of eraly human arts. The fantastic creature of the **lion man** represents a unique relic which refers to the intellectual and religious state of mind of the humans during the last ice age - see www.lonetal.net for more information.
