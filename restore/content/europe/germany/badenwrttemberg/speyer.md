# Speyer

*Europe > Germany > Badenwrttemberg > Speyer*

Speyer, a free city of the Holy Roman Empire from 1294 until 1779, is best known for having the most impressive Romanesque cathedral in all of christendom, the Dom St. Maria und St. Stephan.
