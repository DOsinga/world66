# Ackermann's Kniepe

*Europe > Germany > Badenwrttemberg > Stuttgart > Nightlife > Ackermanns Kniepe*

Located in Stuttgart West, on the corner of Bebel and Schwabstrasse, this place is one of the hidden gems of the city, though from the crowds packing the place in the autumn through spring, maybe it's not such a secret. They have friendly staff, great food anything from traditional german fare to bbq spare ribs depending on the mood of the head chef, and most impressively, well over 100 single malt scotch varieties to sample and learn about. For you Deutch impaired American tourists, ask for Barbara Schrieber, (she studied English in America at the University Of Minnesota!) and tell her Sean sent you.....my favorite restaurant in the city, and I lived there from 1999-2002!
