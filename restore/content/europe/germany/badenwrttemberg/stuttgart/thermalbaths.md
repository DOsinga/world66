# Thermal Baths

*Europe > Germany > Badenwrttemberg > Stuttgart > Thermalbaths*

Stuttgart offers excellent possibilities to spend a day in one of the Thermal Baths. Once there, you can choose from various treatments, pools and saunas. Pamper yourself in a Turkish hammam or a sparkling hot spring whirlpool!But don’t forget to bring enough money as a whole day in there can be quite pricy.
