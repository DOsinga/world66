# Tubingen

*Europe > Germany > Badenwrttemberg > Tubingen*

Tubingen is one of the nicest university towns in germany. With 25,000 of its 85,000 inhabitants students at the University, the saying runs that Tubingen is a town on the university campus. 



The old town is very nice, with small alleys and half-timbered houses. The Holzmarkt and the Marktplatz form the heart of this town. Around them you will find the most interseting sights, such as the St. Gerog, the Holderlin Tower and Schloss Hohentubingen. 

Northeast of the Markt you will find the university quarters.
