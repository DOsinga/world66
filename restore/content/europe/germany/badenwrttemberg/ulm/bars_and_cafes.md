# Bars and Cafes in Ulm

*Europe > Germany > Badenwrttemberg > Ulm > Bars And Cafes*

When you go on vacation, the last thing you want to do is eat or drink in places that you can find at home, so you will want to find the real **Ulm Bars and Cafes**. Finding the real bars and cafes will allow you to experience the way locals of Ulm live.

**Einstein Café Restaurant Bar**

One thing about the Einstein Café Restaurant Bar is that it offers you 3 different venues in 1 location. Something to keep in mind though is that even though the place serves good food, it is a better café than restaurant. When stopping in for breakfast it is recommended that you try out 1 of the various omelets and the pancakes.

**Dolce Cocktailbar**

Perhaps the worst thing about this bar is the theme of the bar, but the drinks are outstanding. If you do choose to visit the bar you will want to get there early enough to ensure you get a seat or a booth. If you simply stand at the bar chances are you will be jostled around, which will result in spilling your drink. The crowds are worse on the weekends.

**Café 113**

One of the best features that Café 133 has is a separate smoking room, which means you won’t be breathing in any secondhand smoke will enjoying your stay. On Monday, Tuesday and Thursday, you can stop in for happy hour, but other nights offer other specials, such as Biertag. Daily entertainment includes a pool table and a foosball table.

**Henry’s Coffee World AG**

If you choose to visit Henry’s Coffee World, you will be able to access to a large selection of coffee and teas, including Espressos and Chai. If you enjoy Chai, the Power Chai is recommended. For Espressos you will want to try out the Espresso Venezia. During the Christmas holidays, you can also buy Gingerbread syrup to add flavor to any cup of coffee. Henry’s Coffee World prides itself on only serving top quality coffee; it also aims for flavors that nobody else offers.

**Café u. Bar Blaupause**

This bar is frequented by people of different ages, downstairs is where the younger group gathers. For the younger crowd this is a place to go before the party, mainly because it is located in a central location. The atmosphere is very pleasant, not very rowdy. The spirits and wine selection is amazing, especially the whiskey selection.
