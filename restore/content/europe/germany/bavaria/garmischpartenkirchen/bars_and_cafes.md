# Bars and Cafes in Garmisch-partenkirchen

*Europe > Germany > Bavaria > Garmischpartenkirchen > Bars And Cafes*

Garmisch-Partenkirchen offers a wide range of restaurants, bars and cafes.
**Nightlife **

Skiers, hikers and all tourists looking for excellent
après-ski service can find venues in a variety of settings. The following list is a sampling of places to relax after a hard day on the slopes.

Fraundorfer--a beer room with Bavarian dancingGletschergarten--the best place for après-ski before heading back down the
mountainIrish pub--a traditional pub that attracts European visitors with its party
atmosphere.Zirbelstub--live music and a good atmosphere, in Promenade 2 Berggasthof Pflegersee--selling Hacker Pschorr beer near the lakeAlmhutte--offers a large terrace overlooking the Zugspitze and serves Mittenwalder beer.

**Other Places
**

In addition to beer gardens and pubs, there are many other places
to drink in Garmisch Partenkirchen. Most hotels open to travellers will
stock beer and offer a nightlife of sorts. There are also quite a few nightclubs
in the area, allowing people to dance until early morning. Beyond the typical choices for bars and clubs, Garmisch Partenkirchen offers a few unusual clubs, including the Peaches, which also serves Pizza,
and the Igloo bar, made of snow. Try some of the following for additional fun:

The Disco Exit--open very lateThe Evergreen--a casual dress/club style venue offering dancing until dawn Karaoke night every WednesdayThe Warsteiner--a basement bar featuring bar-dancing and body
shotsThe Peaches/The Music Cafe--located in the same building, neither allows jeans or exercise clothing but the former
serves cocktails, and the latter plays music
John’s Club--more refined, typically playing 80’s music; small but quite pretty inside. The Igloo Bar--made out of snow, and cold inside; a good place to enjoy fondue
