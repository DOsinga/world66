# Marktl am Inn

*Europe > Germany > Bavaria > Marktl Am Inn*

Located near the border to Austria and near Burghausen (with
the largest Castle of Europe) and Altötting / Altoetting /
Altotting - a wellknown Pilgrimage Place

Marktl am Inn is an
idyllic village suddently become famous in 2005 because it was the
birthplace of Cardinal Josef Ratzinger (aka Pope Benedict XVI).

There
are many small Villages in this area with many small guesthouses,
Hotels, bed & breakfast, and holiday's by the Farmers.
