# Eating Out

*Europe > Germany > Bavaria > Munich > Eating Out*

If you connect Munich with beer-drinking and original German or better Bavarian food, you are right. But don't believe this is the only possibility to ease your hunger - hundreds of restaurants offer delicious international specialities and atmosphere. It's nearly impossible to describe the wide variety of bars, bistros, restaurants and cafes. From first class top cuisine to popular Beer Gardens, from Japanese to Bavarian, where both Bavarians and tourists like to spend their leisure time, you will find everything in Munich.
