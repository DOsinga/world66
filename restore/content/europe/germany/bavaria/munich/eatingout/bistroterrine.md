# Bistro Terrine

*Europe > Germany > Bavaria > Munich > Eatingout > Bistroterrine*

In the middle of a nice open air patio the Bistro Terrine offers french cuisine par excellence - which includes first class wines. Gourmet-cook Werner Licht creates delicious and often unexpected combinations. The rooms inside are fitted in very nice art nouveau style.
