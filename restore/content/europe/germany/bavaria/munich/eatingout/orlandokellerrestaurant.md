# Orlando Keller & Restaurant

*Europe > Germany > Bavaria > Munich > Eatingout > Orlandokellerrestaurant*

Right in the heart of Munich, at Platzl 4, you have the choice either to eat in a rustic cellar or in a somewhat Baroque restaurant, both are run by the Augustiner Brewery. Very popular amongst tourists, friendly service and nice atmosphere, open from 9am to 1am every day, kitchen open till midnight, main courses between 13 and 30 DM.
