# Restaurant am Chinesischen Turm

*Europe > Germany > Bavaria > Munich > Eatingout > Restaurantamchinesischenturm*

The restaurant “Chinese tower” is a traditional destination of an excursion in the English Garden. In 1992 the house has been completely renovated. Nowadays they offer a big choice of Bavarian food and original Löwenbräu beer. 120 seats inside, a 75 seat extra room, and a patio with 500 seats offer enough space, even for groups up to 200 persons (reservations: +49 (0)89 - 38 38 73 – 27)
