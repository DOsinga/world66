# Salon Zazou

*Europe > Germany > Bavaria > Munich > Eatingout > Salonzazou*

In autumn 1999 the german star-cook Hans Peter Wodarz opened his new restaurant, a real palace of Art-Deco. It's situated in an old departurehall of the airport Riem, which is now under monument protection. Here you can try Wodarz' newest creations in a really unforgettable atmosphere.
