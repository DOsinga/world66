# Tantris

*Europe > Germany > Bavaria > Munich > Eatingout > Tantris*

The Tantris is without any doubt the best-known first-class-restaurant in Munich. In every gourmet-guide you will find the Tantris at least mentioned, mostly recommended. This is first and foremost the merrit of Hans Haas, the "chef-cuisinier". Very famous is also Paula Bosch, its excellent and very pleasant Sommelière.
