# Nightlife and Entertainment in Oberammergau

*Europe > Germany > Bavaria > Oberammergau > Nightlife And Ente*

A picturesque little town in Bavaria, Oberammergau nightlife
is mainly focused around the relaxed small town atmosphere and the once-a-decade performance of one of the world's most famous passion plays.
In addition to the play, Oberammergau also attracts winter sports
enthusiasts. Although it does not have the size and intensity of other
winter sports locations in Europe, the friendly small town feel and
beautiful surroundings make it a great destination, particularly for
cross-country skiers. A village of less than 10,000 people, Oberammergau
is not going to rival the nightlife of major cities, but if
you're looking for relaxation and small town charm, this is the place
for you.

**The Passion Play**

It is impossible to talk about entertainment in Oberammergau
without talking about the Passion Play. Despite the fact that it only
happens once a decade, the Passion Play is the principal tourist
attraction of Oberammergau . Dating back to 1634, the Passion Play has
grown exponentially over the years, and the most recent productions
have incorporated as many as 2000 of the local villagers. The play
occurs at the turn of each decade, the most current being 2010.
Typically the play runs from mid-May until October and is roughly 5
hours long. The 2010 performance will be directed by ChristianStuckl of Munich's Volkstheater
. If you are interested in going to the play there are many tourist
packages that will include it, or you can just buy your tickets and
spend the rest of your vacation relaxing in Oberammergau.

**Nightlife**If you are looking for wild and crazy nightlife, chances are you will not coming to Oberammergau. Despite being a tourist destination, Oberammergau is still a small town, and part of its charm is that it still keeps with the rhythms of a typical small Bavarian village. Still, this doesn't mean that you have to go to bed at 8 PM every night during your stay here. One place to look for nightlife is in the many hotels that populate Oberammergau.
The medium sized hotels in particular tend to be more traditional and
offer the ambiance and spirits that you would expect from a small town
in Germany. There are also a couple of small bars and cafes where you
can get a nightcap. Obviously if you are in town for the Passion Play
you will probably be surrounded by more tourists than locals, but if
you do come off-season don't be afraid to go out, as the villagers are
known for their friendliness and hospitality.
